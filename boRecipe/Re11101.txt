  "Instructions": "Grease an oblong loaf or cake tin. Roll out thawed pastry into a large  oblong about the thickness of a penny. Cut off a piece of pastry to fit top  of loaf and reserve. Use remainder to line base and sides of tin.    In a mixing bowl, combine sausagemeat, onion, sage and breadcrumbs. Bind  with beaten egg, reserving a little for glazing pastry.
