Cover with plastic wrap and let rise in warm, draft-free place  until double, about one hour. Punch down. Cover and let rise again until  double, about 45 minutes. Punch down.  On lightly floured surface roll out  1/2 inch thick. With a 3 1/4 inch round cutter cut out muffins. Sprinkle  cookie sheets lightly with cornmeal. Add muffins, about 1 inch apart. With wide spatula  carefully remove muffins (do not compress or puncture or they will  collapse) to griddle.  Bake over very low heat 8 to 10 minutes on each side  until light brown.
