Cool on rack  a few minutes before eating.    *Note- I add less chips because I dont like a lot of chocolate chips. The  cookie dough tastes so good you dont need a lot of chips. Dont let them overcook, as soon as the edges  start to look brown, even if the center isnt set, they will be fine once  they are cooled. Leave them on the cookie sheet for a minute or two and  then cool completely on wire racks. When I picked up this recipe the author  had written that these cookies stay soft for a long time.
