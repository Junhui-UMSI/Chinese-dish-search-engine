Melt the remaining margarine and pour 3 tablespoons of it unto the noodles.  Add the lemon, raisins, nuts and salt.    Beat the egg whites until soft peaks form. Put half the mixture in the bottom of a greased 33x23x5 baking pan Spread  the apples on top and cover with the rest of the noodles.
