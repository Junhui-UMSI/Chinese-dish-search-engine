  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Line baking sheets with parchment. Pipe out  1/2-inch balls for profiteroles or cocktail puffs, 1-inch balls for cream  puffs, or 1/2 by 4-inch strips for eclairs. Be sure to leave enough room in  between for them to triple in size. Brush with egg glaze and bake for 15  minutes. Lower heat to 400 and open oven door briefly to let steam out.  Close the door and continue to bake another 1030 minutes according to their  size.
