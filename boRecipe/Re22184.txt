Place the carcass pieces and any left over peelings &  parings into a stock pot and fill with water. Add garlic cloves. Fill the  stock pot with water and bring to a boil. The next day remove the congealed fat from the  top of the broth.  Drain the contents through a collander into another  large pot. Return the broth to the stock pot.
