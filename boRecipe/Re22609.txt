  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cut or tear bread into pieces amd place on a  cookie sheet. Toast bread pieces in oven for about 15 mins. Place in a 2  quart buttered casserole. Meanwhile mix 3 cups milk, cream, 3 eggs and  sugar together. Pour milk mixture over bread in casserole and let bread  absorb liquid for 15 mins. Add almond extract, apricots, raisins and  almonds. Combine with ingredients in casserole dish. Place casserole dish  inside another bigger baking dish. Pour boiling water in the larger dish.  Bake for 1 hour, making sure there is always water in the bigger dish. In  saucepan mix powdered sugar, margarine and egg. Bring just to the boil.  Remove from heat and add ameretto.
