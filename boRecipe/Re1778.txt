Add wine and  stock and cook over moderate heat for 3 - 4 minutes to reduce liquid  slightly.    \r\n\r\nReturn chicken to pan and place some of the leeks mixture on top of  breasts. Halfway through the baking of the breasts, place the zucchini on a separate  baking sheet and bake until zucchini just begins to soften.
