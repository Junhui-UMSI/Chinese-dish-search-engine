  "Instructions": "1. Have pork boned, but kept intact. Wipe clean with a damp cloth; dry  with paper toweling; then rub with salt and refrigerate, covered, 2 days.       2. Wipe pork with a damp cloth to remove excess salt. Place in a heavy  pan with water. Bring to a boil; then simmer, covered, until tender (about  1-1/2 hours), turning meat several times. Drain, reserving liquid for  stock.       3. Squeeze pork snugly into a deep rectangular pan (like a cake pan) and  cover with a weighted tray.
