  "Instructions": "Roast the chiles either under the broiler or over a gas burner on all sides  until charred, but not burned. Place immediately in a plastic bag to steam  until cooled. Toss the cheeses together and press into 6 oblong shapes large enough to  fit into the chiles. Wrap each chile and the cheese, being careful that the  seams are closed.
