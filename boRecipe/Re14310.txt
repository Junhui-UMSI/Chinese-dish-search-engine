  "Instructions": "1. Soak tangerine peel.     2. Wipe duck inside and out with a damp cloth. Dry with paper toweling or  hang up to dry in a cool airy place 1 to 2 hours. Place bird on a rack over a drip pan containing several inches  of water. Roast to brown lightly (about 20 minutes), turning duck once.  Meanwhile bring water to a boil.     4.
