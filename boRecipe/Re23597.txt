  "Instructions": "Brown turkey in olive oil, drain and place in a large cooking pot (20  quart). Saute chopped vegetables and add to pot. Add remaining ingredients  (excluding cilantro and spices) to pot. Cook over medium heat for about 1/2  hour, stirring frequently. Add spices, one at a time, stirring well between  each addition. This made  about 16-17 quarts.    Serve plain or over spaghetti, baked potato or rice, with grated low-fat  cheese and chopped onions on the side. Tortilla chips or browned chunks of  Italian bread are also good on the side. The chili freezes well. Freeze in  plastic quart containers for quick 2 or 3 person meals.
