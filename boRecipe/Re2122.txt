Bake the cake until top is golden and a toothpick  inserted in the center comes out clean, about 45 minutes. Cool on a rack  for 5 minutes. Run a small knife along the edge of the cake and carefully  invert cake onto a serving platter. If apples stick to the skillet, loosen  with a knife and arrange on the cake.
