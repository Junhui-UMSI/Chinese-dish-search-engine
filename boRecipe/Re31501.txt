Heat the oil in a wok over medium heat to very hot, about 375. Deep-fry  the pork slices until they turn golden brown and float to the surface. Pour the oil out of the wok, leaving only enough to cover the bottom,  about 2 tbsp. Heat to very hot, or until the oil surface ripples. Add the  garlic and and stir-fry. Cook, stirring,  until thickened. Add the pork. Stir to coat thoroughly.
