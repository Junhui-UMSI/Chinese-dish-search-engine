Near the end of the cooking time, drizzle  spoonfuls of the mixture across the surface of the mixture. When ready, the  mixture will form a neat thread across the surface.     Assemble all ingredients and utensils before starting to cook. You will  need a large heavy bottomed aluminum pot or skillet with deep sides, a long  handled metal whisk or spoon, 2 large spoons (or an ice cream scoop with a  manual release) and a lightly buttered cookie sheet.
