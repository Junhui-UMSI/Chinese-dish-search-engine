(To do this by hand,  mix flour, confectioners sugar, cornstarch, and salt in medium bowl.  Freeze butter and grate it on large holes of box grater into flour mixture.  Toss butter pieces to coat. Refrigerate for 30 minutes, then bake until golden brown, about 20 minutes.    3.
