Cut tomatoes into quarters and remove seeds.  Puree tomatoes in  blender or processor.    Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in heavy large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cover and let stand at room  temperature 2 hours.    Heat remaining 3 tablespoons olive oil in heavy large skillet over medium  heat.  Add fennel; zucchini, green bell pepper, mushrooms and celery.
