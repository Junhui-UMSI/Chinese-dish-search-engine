  "Instructions": "Toast pine nuts in 350 Foven, 8-10 mins or until golden brown. Watch them  carefully as they burn easily. Set aside. Cut a thin slice from the top of  each potato. Remove pulp, being careful not to tear the shells. Keep shells  warm. Crumble potato pulp. Add white sauce, salt, and Parmesan; mix  thoroughly. Add all other vegs except tomatoes; mix well. It is best  to have a colorful combo, all cooked just crisp-tender.
