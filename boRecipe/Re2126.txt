Place dough in a bowl coated with cooking spray,  turning to coat top. Cover and let rise in a warm place (85 deg), free from  drafts, 1 hour or until doubled. Top with apple and raisins. Roll up tightly, starting at short side,  pressing firmly to eliminate air pockets; pinch seam and edges to seal.
