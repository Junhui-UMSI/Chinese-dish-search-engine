  "Instructions": "I want to share a really yummy recipe I adapted from Laxmis Vegetarian  Kitchen by Laxmi Hiremath. ( It was practically fatfree to begin with.) Preheat oven to 400 degrees Combine kofta ingredients and mix thoroughly,  form into 1 inch balls and bake 1 inch apart non-stick baking sheet until  golden, about 30 min. Meanwhile, blend together 1st 5 gravy ingredients in  a blender with 1 cup water.
