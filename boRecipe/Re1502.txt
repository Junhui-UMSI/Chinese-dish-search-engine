  "Instructions": "In large pot, combine split peas, bay leaves, and water/vegetable stock.  Bring to boil, skim foam, add thyme, cover and simmer 30 minutes. I use dried thyme leaves, since  the recipe didnt specify fresh, dried, leaves, or ground.
