PRUNE BUTTER FROSTING: 6 Tbsp butter or margarine 3 cups confectioners  sugar, sifted 3/4 tsp lemon juice 4 to 4-1/2 Tbsp prune juice 1 tsp grated  lemon rind salt, dash 1/2 cup prunes, cut-up, cooked, well-drained 2 Tbsp  chopped nuts Cream butter or margarine in large bowl of electric mixer.
