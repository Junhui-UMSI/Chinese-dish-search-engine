Peel  the asparagus spears using a swivel-bladed vegetable peeler, lay them flat  in a large heavy skillet, pour in boiling salted water and set over  moderate heat. Arrange the asparagus  spears like the spokes of a wheel over the bottom of the pie crust, with  their tips pointing toward the center.
