Gradually stir in the flour mixture. Add the almond extract &  the rest of the grapefruit juice, stirring well until the mixture is well  combined. Preheat oven to 275F. If the top is  browning too quickly, cover with some waxed paper. After it is cooked,  remove from the oven & place on a wire rack to cool for 30 minutes. Gently  remove cake from the tin & peel away the paper. Let cool on wire racks till  completely cooled.  Wrap in waxed paper & keep in a cool place.
