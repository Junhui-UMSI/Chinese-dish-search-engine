Beat with an  electric mixer on high speed for 2 minutes.  Mix in enough flour to form a  soft dough.  Knead for 5 to 10 minutes, until dough is shiny and elastic. Or use 11x8 pan, grease well.    FILLING: Toss apple slices, lemon juice, and 1/4 c of sugar. Spread apples  over crumbs. Drain raisins, reserving rum, and sprinkle raisins over  apples. Bake in a preheated 350 degree F. oven for 15 minutes.  Beat eggs  and sugar until thick and lemon-colored.  Stir in milk and reserved rum.  Pour custard over apples and bake for 45 to 60 minutes at 350 degrees F.  until custard is set.  Cool completely before serving. Do NOT remove  springform pan until cool.
