  "Instructions": "Hi all. This is a salsa I created for a potluck this week. I intended it  for dipping tortilla chips, etc... I bet a spoonful of this stuff could  also jazz up your potato soup, corn chowder, bean & ham soup, etc...    Chop carrots.
