  "Instructions": "Discard loose fat from rabbit. Rinse & pat dry. In a large bowl  combine carrot, garlic, bay leaves, allspice, pepper, 1 Tbls. oil,  wine, orange juice, orange rind, & rum. Add the rabbit. Chill,  covered, turning occasionally, for 24 hours.  Remove rabbit from the  bowl, reserving the marinade, & pat dry. In a large skillet heat the  remaining 2 Tbls. olive oil & brown the rabbit. Sprinkle with 1 Tbls.  flour, turn it, & brown the flour. Repeat with the remaining 1 Tbls.  flour. Transfer the rabbit to a large casserole. Salt to taste.  Deglaze the skillet with the reserved marinade. Pour over the rabbit.  Cook, covered at 350 for 1-1 1/2 hours, basting twice, till very  tender. Transfer to a heated platter, pour sauce over it, & garnish  with sliced orange.
