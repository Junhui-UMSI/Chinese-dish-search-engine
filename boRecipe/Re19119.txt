Add broccoli; cook, covered  for 4 minutes or until potato is tender and broccoli is tender-crisp; set  aside.    Meanwhile, in bowl, whisk together eggs, egg whites, salt and pepper; set  aside.    In 9-inch ovenproof skillet, heat oil over medium heat; cook onion, garlic  and tarragon, stirring, for about 2 minutes or until softened.    Arrange sweet potato mixture in bottom of skillet, pour egg mixture over  top.
