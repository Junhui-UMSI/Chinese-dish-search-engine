Turn off heat let sauce steep for at least 1/2 hour or more to let  flavors meld.Taste and adjust your spice mixture. Serve as a hot table  sauce. This is not a marinade or a cooking sauce, although you might add a  light brushing to your meat or chiocken about 15 min. before removing from  grill.    Orange-Habanero-Chipotle oil 3-4 Chipotle dried peppers, 1 dried habanero,  2-3 tbls dried orange peel minced, 2 cups canola oil. In a spice mill  combine the dry ingredients and grind fine.
