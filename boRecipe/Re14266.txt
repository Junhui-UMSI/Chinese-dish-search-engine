Sharply tap pans once on counter to  remove air bubbles. BAke at 350? for 25 minutes or until a wooden pick  inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pans 10 minutes on wire racks;  remove from pans. Cool completely on wire racks.    Place 1 cake layer on a plate; spread with half of Lemon Filling, and top  with another cake layer. Spread with remaining Lemon Filling and top with  last cake layer. Spread Fluffy White Frosting over top and sides of cake.
