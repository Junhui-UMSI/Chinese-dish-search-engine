  "Instructions": "Cook lentils with the cinnamon stick in 7 cups water until tender, about 30  minutes. Remove and discard the cinnamon stick and drain the lentils,  saving the water, if any is left.    Meanwhile, heat oil in a soup pot. Add 6 cups of water, including the reserved cooking water from lentils, if  any. Add the canned crushed tomatoes with juice and bring to a boil.
