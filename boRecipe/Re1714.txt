Brush one side with melted butter and season with  freshly ground pepper. Do not add salt, as this will extract the juices.  Put the steak, buttered and seasoned side up, under a hot broiler and cook  4-5 minutes under high heat to brown the surface and seal in the juices.  Turn over, brush with remaining butter, season with pepper. Turn the heat  down and broil the steak a further 4-5 minutes, turning it once only.
