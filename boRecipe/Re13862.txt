  "Instructions": "Wash the lettuce, shake as much water off as possible and tear into pieces,  or shred coarsely and place in salad bowl. Place bacon on a microwave  roasting rack set in a baking dish. Cover with a paper towel. Microwave at  high (100%) 4 minutes. Drain on a paper towel. Remove roasting rack from  baking dish. Stir in onions. Microwave at high (100%) until onions become  transparent, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in vinegar, water, sugar, salt and  pepper, mixing well. Microwave at high (100%) 30 seconds. Pour hot mixture  over lettuce, coating all the greens. Crumble bacon and toss lightly until  lettuce is wilted. Serve immediately. Makes 4 servings.
