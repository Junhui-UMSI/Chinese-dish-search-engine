  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 350F.  Line a jelly-roll pan with foil.    Almond topping:  In top of double boiler over hot water, combine all  ingredients. Cut in butter until  mixture is crumbly, add egg yolks.  With hands, mix into a smooth dough.  Press into prepared pan and prick with fork.  Bake for 15 minutes.  Remove  from oven and spread almond topping over crust.  Return to oven and bake  for 20 minutes longer.    Apricot Glaze:  Meanwhile, in a small saucepan melt the apricot preserves  with water over low heat.  Strain through a fine sieve.  Brush blaze over  baked almond topping imediately after pan comes out of the oven.  Cool pan  on rack...Cut into 2\" squares when cool.    Chocolate Glaze:  In top of double boiler over hot water, melt semisweet  and unsweetened chocolate together.  Drizzle on top of cookies.  Allow to  set.
