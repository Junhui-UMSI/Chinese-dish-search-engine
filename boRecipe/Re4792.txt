  "Instructions": "Heat a medium castiron and enamel casserole, or other heavy ovensafe pan  over mediumhigh heat. Cook the bacon until crisp, then add the onion. Stir  frequently for about 5 minutes, or until golden, taking care not to let it  burn. With a slotted spoon, transfer the mixture to a bowl. Let the fat in  the pan get very hot, and add the meat. Brown the meat, turning so that  each piece is evenly golden. Add the tomatoes and peppers and cook,  stirring frequently, for 2 minutes more.
