  "Instructions": "Heat oven to 250 F or 120 C. Spread 1/2 cup fresh bread crumbs on a cookie  sheet and bake 10 minutes. Remove from oven. Increase oven temp to 350 or  180.    Brush four salmon fillets (6 oz each) with 2 TBSP Dijon mustard. Combine  crumbs with 1/2 cup chopped pignoli (pine) nuts. Pat on top of salmon. Heat  1 tsp vegetable oil with 1 TBSP butter, in oven-proof skillet over  medium-high heat, until it foams; add salmon crumb-side down and cook till  golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes. Turn fish over. Transfer skillet to oven.  Bake 7 to 10 minutes more, until cooked through.    SAUCE: Reduce 2 TBSP fresh lemon juice to 1 teaspoon. Add 1/4 cup heavy  cream and boil until slightly thickened, 3 minutes. Whisk in 1/4 cup cold  butter, cut up, till smooth. Add 1/8 tsp EACH of salt and freshly ground  pepper. Serve with salmon.
