In a big frying pan or a whook, Heat well oil, add onions and fry till you  get a nice gold colour. Transfer to a small bowl. Add the other 1 Tabls  oil, Heat well , Do not lower heat . Add the mushrooms ,and toss them well  till they are well blended with the oil and begin to fry.They should not  get soggy.    Add salt and pepper, Add the onions, mix, and stir for couple of minutes.  Transfer to a bowl for later use. cool.    Final step: In a bowl beat lightly eggs, Add all other ingredients, mix.
