  "Instructions": "Peel mangoes & cut in half. Remove pit & discard. Cut flesh into 1/4\"  thick slices. Combine mango, sugar, fennel, cumin, pepper flakes,  salt, water & raisins in a saucepan & bring to a boil. Cook over med  heat, partly covered, for 6 minutes or until mango is soft &  translucent. Stir in cornstarch & continue cooking until chutney  thickens. Turn off heat. Heat the oil in a small skillet until hot.  Add mustard seeds. Let spice sizzle for 10 seconds then immediately  pour the entire contents of the pan over the chutney. Mix well &  serve warm, at room temperature, or chilled. Keeps for a day at room  temperature & 4 weeks refrigerated.    Note: This chutney can be made with ripe mangoes; just add a  tablespoon of lemon juice to cut the sweetness.
