Im not sure how you get em blackened - I cured mine  with olive oil and then I just wipe them out when Im done. They are kind  of getting blackened looking with use. Drain off the excess fluid after squashing, and spread this  over the cheese. Smidge of salt and ground pepper over the top. Chopped  garlic, fresh chopped basil and oregano.
