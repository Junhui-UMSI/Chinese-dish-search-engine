Meanwhile, with vegetable peeler, remove 2 strips  (about 5 inches long) of peel from oranges, then squeeze 3/4 cup of juice;  set aside.    5. Stir in mushrooms with their liquid and carrots; cover and simmer 20  minutes, or until carrots are tender Add salt and pepper.
