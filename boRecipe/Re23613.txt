Using small sharp knife, trim outside of base until no  dark green areas remain. Rub trimmed area with lemon half. Place in lemon  water until ready to use.    Drain artichokes. Mix oil and vinegar into orange juice  mixture in bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Mix in artichokes and onion.  Let stand10 minutes.
