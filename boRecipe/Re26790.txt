  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 350F.  To prepare fresh chestnuts, cook covered, in water,  for 20 minutes till expanded & lighter in colour.  To prepare canned  chestnuts, cook in their liquid till heated through.  To prepare dried  chestnuts, cook according to package directions.    Drain & rinse hot chestnuts under cold water.  Cut off flat part with a  sharp knife & scoop nut meat with a spoon.  In a food processor, combine  chestnut meat with soy milk, juice, 4 tb maple syrup, 1 ts cinnamon &  nutmeg.  Fold in raisins or currants.    Peel apples & remove core from top, leaving the bottom intact. Hollow out  the apple leaving a 1/2\" shell.  Brush shell with lemon juice to prevent  discoloration.  Sprinkle inside with a little cinnamon. Chop scooped out  apple & mix with the chestnut puree.    Fill apple shells with chestnut-apple stuffing.  Place in a grease proof  dish & pour wine & remaining syrup around the base of the apples. Add  margarine & cinnamon stick to the pan.    Bake 30 to 40 minutes, basting occasionally with the wine mixture. Garnish  & serve.    \"Vegetarian Times\", December, 1993",
