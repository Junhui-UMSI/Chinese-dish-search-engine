  "Instructions": "Trim the crusts from the bread and spread the slices out on a tray to dry  for about 24 hours.    In the container of a food processor, place the almonds, garlic, and cooled  potato. Process until smooth.    Mix the lemon juice, vinegar, olive oil, salt, and sugar in a small bowl  and set aside. Fill a large bowl with cool water. Drop the dried bread into  the water, 1 slice at a time. When it is soaked, squeeze about half the  water from the bread between the palms of your hands.
