  "Instructions": "To cook the sausage, slit the skin at several point with a knife to keep it  from splitting while cooking. Place in a pot of cold water and bring to a  boil. Turn down the heat and simmer for 45 minutes. Remove, allow to cool  on a cloth, then carefully remove skin with a knife. Chop coarsely. Preheat  oven to 425~. Spread puff pastry sheets as directed. Cut into 4\" squares.  Place a tb of andouille in center of each square. For variety, place a  dollor of mustard on 1/3 of the appetizers, 2 tb cheese on 1/3 and leave  1/3 plain.
