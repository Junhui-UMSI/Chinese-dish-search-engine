Meanwhile, whisk egg yolks and sugar together in medium bowl.  Gradually whisk in hot milk. Return mixture to saucepan; cook, stirring  constantly, over medium-low heat until mixture thickens and coats back of  spoon, about 6 minutes. (Do not boil.) Strain through sieve into mixer  bowl.
