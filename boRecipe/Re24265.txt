  "Instructions": "Line 8-1/2x41/2 loaf pan with plastic wrap, overlapping sides. FOR BEAN  LAYER: Mash beans in large bowl. Add lemon juice, olive oil, oregano and  garlic and blend until smooth. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Spread  bean mixture evenly on bottom of prepared pan. FOR RED PEPPER LAYER:  Combine peppers and feta in processor and blend until smooth. Spread pepper  mixture evenly over bean layer in prepared dish. FOR PESTO LAYER: Mince  garlic in processor. Add basil, parsley and pine nuts and mince. With  machine running, gradually add oil through feed tube and process until  smooth. Mix in ricotta.  Spread pesto evenly over red pepper layer. Cover  with plastic wrap and refrigerator overnight. To unmold, invert pate onto  serving platter. Peel off plastic wrap from pate. Garnish with herb sprigs  and serve with sourdough bread slices. Bon Appetit Oct. 93.
