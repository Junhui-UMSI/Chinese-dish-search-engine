  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 350 degrees; line cookie sheets with parchment paper. In a  large mixer bowl, combine the butter and almond paste. Beat on low speed,  scraping the bowl often, for 1 to 2 minutes until well mixed. Add the  powdered sugar, egg yolks, almond extract and vanilla extract. Beat on  medium speed, scraping the bowl often, for 1 to 2 minutes until well mixed.  Reduce the speed to low. Continue beating, gradually adding the flour and  scraping the bowl often, for 1 to 2 minutes until well mixed. Place the  dough in a pastry bag with a 1/2-inch opening.  Pipe onto the cookie sheets  forming 2 1/2 to 3-inch logs.  Bake for 13 to 17 minutes, or until the  edges are very lightly browned. Cool completely on parchment paper. In a  1-quart saucepan, melt the chocolate chips and shortening over low heat,  stirring often, for 5 to 8 minutes until smooth. Remove the cooled cookies  from the parchment paper and dip the ends or the tops in the chocolate and  then in the almonds. Place on waxed paper until set. Penny Halsey  (ATBN65B).
