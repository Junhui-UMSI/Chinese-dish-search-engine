  "Instructions": "Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.    Place the sweet potatoes on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.  Season the potatoes with salt and pepper and place in the oven. Roast the  sweet potatoes for 1 to 1 1/2 hours or until the potatoes are fork tender.  Remove the potatoes from the oven and cool. Remove the skin from the sweet  potatoes.    In a mixing bowl, whisk the sweet potatoes, molasses, spices, eggs, cream  and 1 teaspoon vanilla together. Pour the filling into the unbaked pastry  shell. Spread the pecan pieces evenly over the sweet potato filling. In a  mixing bowl, whisk the remaining ingredients together. Pour the topping  over the pecans. Bake for about 1 hour or until the filling and topping  set. Cool for 10 minutes before slicing.    Garnish with whipped cream, chocolate sauce, fresh mint sprigs, and  powdered sugar.    This recipe yields 8 slices.
