  "Instructions": "Mix yogurt, ginger, salt and red coloring and rub into chicken.  Let it  marinate overnight.    Place in an ovenproof dish and brush with oil. Add sour cream and chicken liquid.  Add crushed saffron, and  cream.  Cover and simmer gently for 5-6 minutes. Add chicken pieces and  adjust seasoning.  Add ground almonds.
