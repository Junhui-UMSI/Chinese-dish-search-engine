  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 350. Beat the egg slightly in a mixing bowl. Melt the  butter and pour it into the bowl. Add the cake mix and mix until the  dry lumps disappear. For the next step you will need an electric mixer or blender. Soften  the cream cheese ( I use the microwave ) and whirl away in blender  (or mixer). Add the remaining eggs and vanilla. The recipe says it takes 30-35 minutes but mine took 50  minutes. The center will sink and bubble, and the sides rise up.  Remove when the top is a light brown. Watch carefully so that it  doesnt get too brown. The cake will be very soft. Wait for it to  cool and harden before cutting into small squares and eating.
