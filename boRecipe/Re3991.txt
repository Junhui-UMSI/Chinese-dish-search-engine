  "Instructions": "In a small bowl combine 2/3 of lemon juice, all of zest and 1 1/2  tablespoons olive oil; set aside. In a bowl toss squash with remaining 2  tablespoons lemon juice, 1 teaspoon oil, herbs, shallots and salt and  pepper to taste; set aside at room temperature.    Heat vegetable oil over high heat until very hot. Season fish with salt and  cracked pepper and cook, skin-side up, for two minutes; turn and continue  to cook 3 minutes more. On 2 warm plates, mound squash mixture and top each  with a fish fillet. Drizzle with lemon sauce.    How to prepare spaghetti squash: Cut squash in half lengthwise. Place  cut-side down on a baking sheet with sides. Pour in enough water to come  1/4-inch up sides of squash.
