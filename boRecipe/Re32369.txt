Keep adding liquid, stirring frequently until  barley pearls are al dente, about 45 minutes. Meanwhile, cut eggplant into  1/2-inch cubes. Heat 4 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet over  medium-high heat. Saute eggplant, stirring frequently, until soft, about 12  minutes. Set aside.
