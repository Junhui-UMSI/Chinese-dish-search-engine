  "Instructions": "Measure water into 1 2-cup glass measure. Microwave on high 3  minutes, or until boiling. Stir in couscous, cover with plastic wrap  and let stand 5 minutes. Fluff with fork.    Peel and segment oranges over a large stainless steel bowl to catch  excess juice; reserve segments for garnish. After segmenting oranges,  squeeze juice from core and membranes into bowl. Add mango, basil,  chives, cumin, pine nuts and couscous; toss to combine. Pack mixture  into six individual 1/2 cup molds.    Arrange arugula wagon-wheel fashion on six large plates.
