Place the veal in a utensil with a cover and add 1/4 cup oil, paprika,  pepper, oregano, salt and lemon juice. COVER AND REFRIGERATE OVERNIGHT     Preheat oven to 400 Transfer veal to a baking dish and add the marinade.  Cover with foil and place the veal in the oven. Bake 1 hour, covered.
