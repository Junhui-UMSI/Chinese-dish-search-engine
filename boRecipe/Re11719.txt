  "Instructions": "Cream butter and cream cheese at medium speed of an electric mixer; add  flour, beating well. Divide dough into 4 equal portions. Wrap each in  plastic wrap, and chill.    Work with 1 portion of dough at a time; keep remaining dough chilled. Sift  powdered sugar lightly over work surface. Roll dough into a 12- x 8-inch  rectangle.    Combine raisins, preserves, walnuts, brown sugar, and coconut; spread  one-fourth of raisin mixture over dough. Roll up jelly-roll fashion,  starting at short side. Pinch seams and ends together.
