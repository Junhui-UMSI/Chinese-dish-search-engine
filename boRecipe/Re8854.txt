  "Instructions": "PRALINES: Combine first five ingredients in a large, heavy saucepan. Cook  over low heat, stirring gently, until sugar is dissolved. Remove from  heat and stir in soda and vanilla. Add butter and beat with a wooden spoon  until mixture begins to thicken. Working quickly, drop by tablespoons onto  buttered wax paper. Makes 1 1/2 - 2 dozen pralines. BUT DONT EAT THEM!  Crumble coarsely and measure out 3 1/2 cup of the crumblies for this ice  cream.    ICE CREAM: Combine sugar, flour, and salt in a Dutch oven. Add eggs, and  stir until smooth. Stir in milk and cook over medium heat until thermometer  reaches 165~, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and let cool slightly;  Chill for 3 hours. Combine whipping cream and vanilla in a large bowl and  whisk in chilled custard. Pour into freezer container of a 1 gallon ice  cream freezer. Freeze according to manufacturers instructions.
