  "Instructions": "Wash and drain 1 pound of the dried pea/bean mixture. Add 3 quarts water,  one ham hock, and herb bag (combine your own choice of herbs in a small  piece of cheese cloth and tie closed with twine). Bring to a boil, cover  and simmer for 2 1/2 to 3 hours. Remove herb bag, place pot in refrigerator  for several hours or over night. Skim congealed fat from the surface.    Return to heat. Add tomatoes, onions, celery and garlic. Cook slowly 1 1/2  hours in covered kettle. It will appear thick and creamy. Add sausage and  chicken breast.
