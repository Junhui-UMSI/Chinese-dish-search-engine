The \"Banana Salad\"    My wifes recipe is simular to the one in Smoke & Spice, page 346. although  she did not get hers from the book, it used to be her grandmothers.    Serves six.  In a small, heavy sauce pan, combine the sugar, egg yolk, and vinegar. Warm  over low heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar disolves and the  mixture thickens.
