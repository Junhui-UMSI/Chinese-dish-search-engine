COMMENTS: To make         good doilies, observe  the following 3 points: 1) The       dough should be a bit on the \"dry\"  side. Do not roll in  back and forth motion, since the edges may fold in and you will have a hard  time separating the doilies. It would also be helpful      if you have a  tortilla press.
