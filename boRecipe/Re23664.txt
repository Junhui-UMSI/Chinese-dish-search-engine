  "Instructions": "This thick and sturdy herbed omelet lets the full flavor of fresh  artichokes shine through.    Prepare artichokes (see recipe for Young Artichokes and Fava Beans  Stewed in Olive Oil) and cut them into 1/2-inch cubes.    Heat the olive oil in a medium-sized, heavy-bottomed skillet over  medium heat.  Add the garlic and saute until translucent, 2 to 3  minutes. Add the eggs, salt, pepper  and herbs.
