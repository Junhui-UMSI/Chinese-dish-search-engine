  "Instructions": "Cover the ancho chiles with warm water. Weight down with a plate to  submerge. Let stand 1 hour to soften. In a large stockpot, heat the olive  oil. The stock can be  covered and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, or frozen  indefinitely. Note: If more \"heat\" is desired, leave half the seeds in the  ancho chiles.
