Preheat oven  to 375?.  On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to 1/8\"  thickness.  Place the dough in a 9\" tart pan with removable bottom.  Trim  and crimp the edges. For Filling: Place porcini mushrooms in small bowl. Heat  cider to boiling and pour over dried mushrooms. Let stand for 30 minutes.  Preheat oven to 375?. In medium size skillet, heat oil.
