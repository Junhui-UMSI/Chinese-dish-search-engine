BLEND SHORTENING AND FLOUR TOGETHER; MIX UNTIL SMOOTH.    7.  GRADUALLY ADD SHORTENING OR SALAD OIL AND FLOUR MIXTURE TO STOCK,  STIRRING  CONSTANTLY; HEAT TO BOILING POINT.
