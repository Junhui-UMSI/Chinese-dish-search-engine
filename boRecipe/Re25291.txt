In a small bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, nutmeg, flour      and cinnamon; mix well. Arrange the apple/sugar mixture over the      bottom crust of the pie. Top with the top crust. This will be      used on the top of the pie when removed from the oven and still      hot.    6.  Remove pie from oven; loosen edge of pie. Cover top of pie with      glaze.
