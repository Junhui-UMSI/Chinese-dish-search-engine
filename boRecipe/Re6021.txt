  "Instructions": "In a large mixing bowl, blend the flour, sugar, anise seed, baking powder,  baking soda and salt.    In another bowl mix the oil and eggs. Heat on both sides until a drop of  water bounces and sizzles on an inside surface of the iron. Turn the iron  over occasionally during cooking to keep both surfaces hot. Place a ball of dough onto the center of the heated iron and flatten it  slightly by pressing it against the iron with your finger.
