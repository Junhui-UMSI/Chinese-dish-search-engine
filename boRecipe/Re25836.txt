Deglaze with 1 cup of red wine and cook over high heat for 5 to 10  minutes.         Meanwhile, in a second saute pan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Reduce over medium heat to dry.         Add 1 cup fish stock to saute pan with salmon bones.  Cook about 5  minutes.         Deglaze reduction (shallot-red wine) in the second saute pan with  about 3 cups of strained liquid from the first saute pan (salmon bones and  fish stock).         Reduce ingredients in second saucepan by two-thirds (not dry).         Add remainder of the butter, whisk, and add salt and pepper to taste.  Strain and reserve.
