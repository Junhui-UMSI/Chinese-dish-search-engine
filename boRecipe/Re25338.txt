Tortellini (usually with a cured ham filling, but spinach or other fillings  can go well too. Just dont use those dry tortellini packets you find on  the shelf; choose fresh tortellini, or those you find in the refrigerated  section of the store)    Grated parmesan (enough to make cream thick) Salt Ham, cubed Butter or  Margarine    While you cook the tortellini, mix cream, Parmesan, and salt.
