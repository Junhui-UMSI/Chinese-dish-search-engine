  "Instructions": "In a heavy Dutch oven or stockpot, fry the bacon over moderate heat,  stirring frequently, until it starts to brown. Toss in the onions, half of  the diced Poblanos, the salt and pepper and cook until slightly golden, 5  to 7 minutes. Spoon out any excess fat. Stir in the garlic and cook briefly  just to release the aroma, 1 to 2 minutes. Pour in the chicken stock, add  the potatoes and bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer and cook 20 minutes.
