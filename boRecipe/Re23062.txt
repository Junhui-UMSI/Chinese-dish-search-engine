  "Instructions": "In a large nonstick stock pot, over medium heat, render the bacon for 10  minutes. Add the onions, celery, and carrots. Season the vegetables with  salt, cayenne, and bay leaves. Stir in the chicken stock and truffle juice and bring up to a boil. Add the  potatoes and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the potatoes are fork tender.  Stir in the cream and parsley. Simmer the soup for 5 minutes. Add the hot  sauce and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer the soup for 2 minutes.
