Chop 1 lb button mushrooms  roughly and 1/2 medium onion and saute in butter until mushrooms cook down.  Chop Shitake mushrooms fine and add mushrooms AND WATER THEY HAVE BEEN  SOAKING IN  to pan & simmer 5-6 min, until liquid starts to evaporate. Salt  & pepper lightly.
