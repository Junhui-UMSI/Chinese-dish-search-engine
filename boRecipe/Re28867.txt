THAI GREEN CURRY CHICKEN WITH  BASIL Tiny pea-size Thai green eggplants are cooked with this curry. You  may substitute fresh garden peas which satisfies the visual appearance.  Allow the coconut milk to stand for one hour or until it separates. Skim  about 1 cup thick coconut cream off the top. Put coconut cream, galangal  and fish sauce into a wok or saucepan. Cook, stirring over high heat until  it becomes thick and oily. Add green curry paste and cook until it becomes  aromatic, about 2 minutes.
