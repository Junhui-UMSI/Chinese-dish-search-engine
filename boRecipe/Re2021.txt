Using spoon, scoop out  pulp from squash, leaving 1/4- inch thick shell.; reserve shells. Transfer  pulp to medium bowl. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F.    Melt butter in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add onions, saute  until very tender, about 15 minutes. Add sage; stir 2 minutes. Add rice,  squash pulp, lemon juice,; stir until mixed, breaking up squash pulp into  smaller pieces. Mix in 1/2 cup cranberries, 1/2 cup hazelnuts and parsley.  Season with salt and pepper.    Divide rice mixture among reserved squash shells. Place in roasting pan.  (Can be made 6 hours ahead. Cover and chill.)    Bake squash until filling is heated through, about 25 minutes.
