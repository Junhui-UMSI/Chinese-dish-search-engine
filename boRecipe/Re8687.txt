Pour batter over fruit in pan and bake in a preheated 350F oven for about  40 minutes, or until a tester inserted in centre comes out clean. Let cool  slightly; turn out onto serving plate. Cut in squares and serve warm or at  room temperature, with whipped or ice cream, if desired.
