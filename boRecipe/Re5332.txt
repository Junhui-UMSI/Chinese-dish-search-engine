Combine apple and  coconut, add cinnamon and chopped nuts, stir in drained pineapple and mix  well; set aside. 5. To Make Batter: Measure out a generous half cup, dice  and drain as above; set aside. 6. In a large bowl combine ggs, oil and  apple juice concentrate, beat for 1 minute. 7. Add drained pineapple and  stir; then mix in cinnamon. Add flour a little at a time, stirring after  each addition, and then beat well by hand for at least 2 minutes. 8. Stir  in baking soda quickly and then mix 28-20 beats, immediately pour into  prepared pan. 9. Using your hands, sprinkle topping over batter and then  bake 28-29 minutes or until a cake tester comes out clean. 10. Remove pan  from oven and place on a wire rack until completely cooled.
