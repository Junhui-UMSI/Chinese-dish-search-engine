  "Instructions": "set 4 bamboo skewers to soak in warm water.    Preheat the broiler or compression grill for 10 minutes.    For the sauce: Heat oil in a small saucepan over medium high heat and fry  the garlic and ginger for 2 minutes to release flavorful oils. Add 1/4 cup  of the wine, tamari, brown sugar, and Shanghai Spice Mix. Remove from the  heat and add the peanut butter, stirring until smooth. Set aside and keep  warm.    For the tuna kebabs: String 5 tuna pieces and 4 artichoke halves  alternately on each bamboo skewer, starting and ending with tuna. Brush the  kebabs with the sauce.
