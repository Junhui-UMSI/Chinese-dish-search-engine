Place the radicchio quarters in the oil 4 at a time and saute cut side down  until lightly brown on both cut sides. Set aside and repeat with the  remaining 4 quarters. Let cool.    While the pan is still hot, saute the 8 sage leaves until crisp and brown,  set aside.
