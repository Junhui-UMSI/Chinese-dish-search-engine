  "Instructions": "Slice the banana peppers in half lengthwise. Rinse and discard the seeds as  well as any interior pulp. Combine the 4 cups water and the salt. In a  large bowl cover peppers with the salt solution. Let stand overnight.  Drain;rinse well. In a large saucepan combine the 3 cups water, the  vinegar, basil, and oregano. Bring to boiling. Reduce heat; simmer,  uncovered, about 10 minutes. Meanwhile pack banana peppers in hot clean  half-pint jars, leaving 1/2 inch headspace. Add one clove of garlic to each  jar.
