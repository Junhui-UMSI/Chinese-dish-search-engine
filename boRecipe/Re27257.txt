The Grand Duke took the recipe home to Florence, where it became popular in  the English intellectual and artistic colony by the end of the nineteenth  century. Starting with the biscuits, arrange in four individual  dishes alternating layers of biscuits and mascarpone, ending with  mascarpone. Dust with cocoa powder and put into the fridge to set and  chill.    Enjoy and dont worry about the calories for the flavor is worth it.
