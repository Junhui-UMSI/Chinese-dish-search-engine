Beat butter in large bowl of electric mixer until light; beat in sugar  until fluffy. Beat in egg, vanilla and salt; beat well. Stir in flour and  nutmeg until well mixed. Refrigerate dough, covered, 1 hour.   2. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Have ungreased baking sheets ready.   3. Shape dough into 1-inch diameter balls. Place 2 inches apart on baking  sheets. Add rum extract to taste. Beat well. Fill a pastry bag fitted  with a  medium star tip with the filling. Pipe a star into the center of  each cookie.  Sprinkle with nutmeg. Chill until filling firms, 15 minutes.    This is a light and delicate cookie with a rum-flavored filling.
