Pour  into a greased 1 litre (2 pint) pie dish or baking dish and cook until set,  about 45 minutes. Remove from the oven and allow to stand for a few  minutes.  Meanwhile beat the egg whites and make the meringue with 2-4 Tbs  sugar, according to taste. Soften the jam by warming or adding a little hot  water. Spread jam on top of pudding and cover with meringue. Bake until the  meringue is lightly coloured, 15 - 20 minutes. Serve hot or cold.
