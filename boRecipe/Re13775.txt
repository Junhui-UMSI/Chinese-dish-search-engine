Allow  to soak for 4 hours. Drain, discarding the water.      Cut the ribs into 3- to 4-rib serving pieces and place on an oven  broiling rack. Bake at 450? for about 15 minutes, or until lightly browned.      Mix all other ingredients, except the onion slices and the seasoning of  salt and pepper for the ribs.      Choose a large, lidded oven-proof casserole and place 1/3 of the rice on  the bottom. Top with 1/3 of the browned ribs; season the meat with salt and  pepper and top with 1/3 of the onion slices. Repeat the layer of rice,  ribs, and onions as before. Continue until you have 3 complete layers.      Pour the tomato mixture over the pan contents and cover. Bake in a 350?  oven for about 2 hours, or until the bones are loose from the meat.      You should stir this mixture twice while it is in the oven.
