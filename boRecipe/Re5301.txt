  "Instructions": "1. Peel and finely slice shallots. Peel garlic.    2. Saute shallots and garlic in a little hot oil over medium heat for 2 to        3    minutes.    3. Deglaze pan with balsamic vinegar and reduce until vinegar almost  disappears. Add wine and thyme. Bring to boil then simmer slowly until  shallots have absorbed all the wine.    4. Add veal stock, bring to boil and simmer slowly until reduced to a third  of its original volume. With a small ladle skim off any scum that rises to  the top. Remove from heat, discard garlic and thyme.    5. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve with grilled beef or veal.  Sauce can be enriched by whisking in 1 or 2 teaspoons ( to taste ) of  unsalted butter. Makes 2 cups sauce or 4 to 6 servings.
