Cover and cook on LOW until the  vegetables are tender, 7 to 9 hours (or on HIGH for 4 to 6 hours).    Add the broccoli. Cover and cook until the broccoli is tender, about 15  minutes or longer. Spread the cubes on a baking sheet or perforated pizza pan.
