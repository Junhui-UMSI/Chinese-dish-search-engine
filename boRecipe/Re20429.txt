Its very low fat when you calculate the  nutritional content of your whole pizza (providing youve chosen a healthy,  low fat crust, sauce and filling). ~Jacqueline    Place all ingredients in a food processor or blender, and process until  completely smooth. Pour into a small saucepan and cook over medium heat,  stirring constantly, until smooth and thick. Pour or spoon over assembled  pizza, sprinkle with nutritional yeast (and optional almonds, if desired),  and heat in the oven according to your pizza recipe instructions.
