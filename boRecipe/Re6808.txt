  "Instructions": "Roll the chicken pieces in the flour, which has been well mixed with the  seasonings. Heat the oil and butter in a skillet; place chicken pieces skin  side down in skillet and cook, uncovered, for 15 to 25 minutes on each  side, turning only once.
