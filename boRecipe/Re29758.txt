In a small bowl combine the salsa ingredients.  Cover and chill until ready to use. Sprinkle a little  water on each tortilla and rub it into each tortilla  with your fingers. This will heat the tortillas with a little  steam to soften them. To serve, place a tortilla on  each plate.  Spoon some of the tempeh mixture along  the center, cover with a little cucumber salsa, then  fold and roll to enclose the filling.
