DUST your hands with the flour and press the batter into a flat cake about  1/2 an inch / 1.25 cm thick.    POKE a hole in the middle (so it looks like a big donut).    HEAT a lightly greased frying pan over high heat for 1 minute, then turn it  to low.    PLACE the bannock in the pan, cover and cook for 5 minutes.
