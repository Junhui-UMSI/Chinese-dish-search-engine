Microwave at HIGH 8 to 10 minutes or  until green pepper is crisp-tender, turning meatballs after 4 minutes.  Remove meatballs and green pepper from baking dish; drain.    Drain pineapple, reserving syrup.  Combine brown sugar and cornstarch in  baking dish.  Stir in reserved pineapple syrup, vinegar, remaining 1/4 cup  catsup and soy sauce.
