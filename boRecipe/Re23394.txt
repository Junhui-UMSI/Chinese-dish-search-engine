  "Instructions": "In food processor or blender with metal blade, drop garlic in with machine  running. Scrape down sides. Add chick peas, lemon juice, and mayonnaise to  bowl. Process until smooth. (Do ahead and refrigerate). In large mixing  bowl, whisk together egg white and milk to light foam. Add crumbs, turkey,  cilantro, raisins, onion, coriander, cumin, pepper flakes, and tumeric.  Using hands, gently mix ingredients without over-handling meat. Shape into  6 patties to fit muffins. Grease an oven grill pan. Cook patties on  med.-high, turning down once, until juices run clear. Place 1 tbs. cheese  over each patty the last 2-3 min. of cooking. Cover to melt. Toast muffins,  spread with Chick Pea Mayonnaise. Top bottom half with lettuce, patty,  cucumber slices.
