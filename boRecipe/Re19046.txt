(The vegetables are not thoroughly cooked at this  stage). Add peas. Remove from heat and keep covered until the topping is  ready    Topping: Preheat oven to 425F degrees. Best served warm, but also good at room temperature.    To serve: Score the surface lightly with a knife to indicate where to cut  for each serving.    Makes 4 to 6 servings.
