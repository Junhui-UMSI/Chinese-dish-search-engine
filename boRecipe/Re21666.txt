This is a quick dish that is not terribly  sweet, as you would expect, but very flavorful.      Cut the pork steak as instructed and marinate for 15 minutes in the soy,  sherry, and ginger. Heat a wok or large frying pan and add the oil and  garlic. Drain the marinade from the meat and saute the meat over very high  heat until done to your liking, about 6 minutes.
