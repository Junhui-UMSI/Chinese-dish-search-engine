  "Instructions": "ESSENCE OF EMERIL SHOW#EE2315    Season the rabbit with the Rustic Rub. In a large pot, heat the oil, over  high heat. Add the onions, celery, bell peppers, shallots,  and garlic. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring constantly until vegetables  are wilted. Add the salt, cayenne, black pepper, and bay leaves.
