In another mixing bowl,  combine water, egg whites, oil, and vanilla. Mix dry ingredients with  wet ingredients just until moistened. Bake for 25 minutes or until  golden brown. Cool. Meanwhile, to prepare topping, follow directions  according to package. Spread with about 1/4 of the topping over top  of first cake layer. Unmold second cake layer; carefully place on  first layer.
