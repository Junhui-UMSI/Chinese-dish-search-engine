Ive  eliminated the 2 tsp. olive oil to saute the onions - we certainly dont  need it...    Put finely chopped onions in a pan with your favorite sauteing liquid and  stir for 4-5 minutes on medium heat. Add chopped carrots and 2 cups of hot  water and bring to a boil.
