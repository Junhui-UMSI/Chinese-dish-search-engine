Add chicken  stock, if needed, to thin the puree. Pour sauce into the skillet and simmer  about 10 minutes to evaporate some of the liquid.    Carve chicken in 4 or more pieces and add to the sauce, coating the pieces  well.  If possible,let chicken sit overnight in the sauce so that the  seasoning permeates and the hotness of the chili diminishes. Reheat gently  so that sauce does not boil.
