  "Instructions": "In a small bowl combine the pork lightly with 1 teaspoon of the vinegar, 1  teaspoon sesame oil, and 1 teaspoon soy sauce and let the mixture stand for  20 minutes. In a bowl let the bean threads soak in warm water to cover for  15 minutes, drain them, and cut them into 3-inch lengths.    Heat a wok or heavy skillet over high heat until it is hot, add the  vegetable oil, and heat until it is hot. Add the garlic and gingerroot and  stir-fry the mixture for 5 seconds. Add the bean  threads, combine the mixture well, and add the stock, the Scotch, remaining  2 teaspoons vinegar, remaining soy sauce, and salt and pepper to taste.
