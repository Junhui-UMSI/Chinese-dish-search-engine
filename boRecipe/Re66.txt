SET ASIDE    IN LARGE BOWL COMBINE BUTTER AND CREAM CHEESE. FOLD INTO LIME  MIXTURE. POUR INTO PREPARED CRUST. COVER; REFRIGERATE UNTL FIRM (3 TO 4  HOURS). LOOSEN SIDE OF CHEESECAKE FROM PAN BY RUNNING KNIFE AROUND INSIDE  OF PAN. COOL COMPLETELY.
