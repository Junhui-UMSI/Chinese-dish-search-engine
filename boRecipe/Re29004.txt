Unfold the pie crusts, one at a time, place on a lightly floured surface  and roll each into a 10inch square. Cut each square into 1inch strips.  Starting at the bottom of the pear, begin wrapping the pastry strips  carefully around the pear. Overlap the strips by about 1/4 of an inch as  you cover the pear. Continue wrapping by moistening ends of the strips  until the pear is completely covered. Repeat with each of the pears. Place  pears on a baking sheet.
