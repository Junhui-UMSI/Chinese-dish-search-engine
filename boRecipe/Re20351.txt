Add 4  pieces dredged veal and cook until light golden brown on each side, about 2  minutes. Remove to plate and continue with remaining pieces. When second  batch is browned, add first batch back into pan and add chicken broth and  lemon juice and cook 2 to 3 minutes, until liquid is nearly gone.
