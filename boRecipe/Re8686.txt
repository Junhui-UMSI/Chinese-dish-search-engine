  "Instructions": "Combine onion, garlic, tomatoes, ham, and raisins. Reserve chicken neck and  giblets for other uses; rinse chicken inside and out and pat dry. Cover; cook at low setting until meat near thighbone is very tender when  pierced (7 1/2-8 hours)    Carefully lift chicken to rack of a broiler pan. Increase cooker heat setting to HIGH; cover and cook, stirring  two or three times, until sauce is thickened (about 10 minutes). Stir in  vinegar; season to taste.
