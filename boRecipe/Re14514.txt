  "Instructions": "In a large pot, add paprika, oregano, MSG, chili powder, cumin, beef  bouillon, beer and 2 cups water.  Let simmer.     In a separate skillet, brown meat in 1 lb. or 1 1/2 lb. batches with  Wesson oil or suet.  Drain and add to simmering spices.  Continue  until all meat is done.     Saute chopped onion and garlic in 1 T. oil or suet.  Add to spices  and meat mixture.  Add water as needed.  Simmer 2 hours.  Add mole,  sugar, coriander seed, hot sauce and tomato sauce.  Simmer 45 min.     Dissolve masa harina flour in warm water to form a paste.  Add to  chili.  Add salt to taste.  Simmer for 30 minutes.  Add additional  Louisiana Hot Sauce for hotter taste.
