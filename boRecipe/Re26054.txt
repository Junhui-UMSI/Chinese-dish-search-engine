  "Instructions": "Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a skillet.  Add garlic, onion, pepper, carrot &  celery.  Season with 1 tablespoon tamari. When vegetables are tender,  remove from the heat & place in a large bowl. Add mashed tofu. Cut bread  into crouton sized pieces & quick fry in oil with tamari, garlic powder &  five tablespoons of nutritional yeast. Add to tofu mixture. Season with  remaining ingredients.  Combine well & place the batter into a well greased  casserole. Bake at 350F for 35 minutes. Allow to cool for 5 minutes or so,  slide a butter knife around the edges, turn upside down on a plate. Serve  warm with a simple green salad.  It is also terrific in sandwiches cold. It  keeps pretty well in the fridge for several days. Serves 4 to 6. *When  using this amount of nutritional yeast, be sure to use a good quality  product.  I speak here from experience.
