For a clear stock, strain the liquid into  another container, pressing with the back of a spoon to extract the juices,  then refrigerate of freeze. Note: Use for Asian-style soups and stir-fries. To make a wonderful Asian  vegetable soup, discard the bouquet garni and pur=82e the broth and  vegetables in a blender.
