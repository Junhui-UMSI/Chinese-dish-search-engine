  "Instructions": "1. Boil potatoes in lightly salted water until tender when pierced with a  knife. Drain and set aside. Cut large potatoes in halves or quarters; leave  small ones whole.    2. Cut chard ribs away from leaves. Blanch leaves and ribs separately in  lightly salted water, cooking just until tender (3 to 5 minutes). Drain and  plunge into ice water to stop the cooking. Set  mixture aside.    5. At serving time, in a large skillet warm remaining butter and olive oil  over moderately high heat. Add softened shallot-garlic mixture and chard  ribs and saute quickly to heat through. Add potatoes and turnips and saute  quickly to heat through. Add chard leaves and cook 20 seconds. Stir in  mustard and creme fraiche. Add lemon juice and season with salt and pepper.
