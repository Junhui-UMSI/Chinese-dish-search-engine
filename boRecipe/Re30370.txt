  "Instructions": "Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Put the pie shell on a cookie sheet and  scatter the chocolate in the shell. To make the custard, mix together the  eggs, sugar, cream, and vanilla. Pour into the pastry shell and carefully  put it in the oven. Whip the  heavy cream, sweeten it with confectioners sugar, and flavor with vanilla.  Spread the cream over the bananas, dust with nutmeg, and chill until  serving time.
