Dissolve yeast in warm water with 1 t. honey. After it bubbles up, add remaining honey, salt, oil, and 3 c. of flour. Beat by hand 200 strokes  (this strengthens the gluten and makes the bread rise more easily). Add 1 more cup of flour and beat briefly.
