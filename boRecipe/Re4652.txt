  "Instructions": "In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, cornstarch, ginger and bouillon.  Add water; set aside. In a skillet or wok over medium-high heat, stir-fry  pheasant in 1 tablespoon of oil until no longer pink, about 3-4 minutes.  Remove and keep warm. Add remaining oil to pan. Stir-fry broccoli and  carrots for 2 minutes. Add celery, onion and peas; stir-fry until the  vegetables are crisp-tender, about 4-5 minutes. Stir in soy sauce mixture  and add to the skillet; bring to a boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes.  Return meat to pan and heat through. Serve over rice. Yield: 4 servings.    NOTES : \"I learned creative ways to prepare game while cooking for hunters  at a lodge in Alaska, where my husband was a guide. Everyone enjoyed this  savory stir-fry.
