Mix well. Then  stir in the egg yolks, one at a time. A mixer is great for this.      Whip the egg whites along with the cream of tartar and gently fold into  the pumpkin mixture. Do not overmix. Place in a buttered 1-1/2 quart  souffl? dish and bake in a preheated oven at 350? for about 30 minutes, or  until the souffl? begins to expand and brown ever so slightly on top.
