  "Instructions": "Mix the persimmon puree and the baking soda together and let it stand.  Cream together the sugar and butter, then add the egg, milk, vanilla,  (rum), and persimmon mixture. Add the remaining dry ingredients and mix.    Pour into well-buttered pudding mold or tin can (i.e., a coffee can). Fill  them only 2/3 full.
