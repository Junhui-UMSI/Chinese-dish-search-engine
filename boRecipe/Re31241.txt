In a medium-sized mixing bowl, stir mashed banana with brown sugar,  cereal, pineapple, including juice, butter, peel, and vanilla.  Let mixture  soak for 5 minutes.  Then, beat in eggs.  Meanwhile, measure flour,  skim-milk powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt into a large mixing  bowl.  Stir flour mixture with a fork until well mixed.  Then, make a well  in centre.
