While this is doing, put 1/4 cup peanut oil in a wok, and stir fry the pork  pieces, until they are well browned, set aside when all done, and keep  warm. Check for thickness of the curry mixture, and add the remaining  coconut milk, cornflour mixture, to thicken, add all of the reserved pork,  stir around, and throw in the basil and sesame oil to taste.
