  "Instructions": "Wrap cubed pumpkin in foil and bake at 350o for 30 min.  While it is  baking, saute shallots in a little white wine, water or stock. Add wine and saffron and cook, stirring  constantly until all the lquid has been absorbed. Add the pumpkin and 1 c  stock stirring constantly.
