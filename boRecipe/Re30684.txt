If using dried corn husks, soften by steaming for 3  to 5 minutes or soaking in warm water for 10 minutes. Fresh corn husks and  parchment paper are ready to use. b. Wrap the two wide  ends of the corn husk around the tamale. Fold the top edge of the corn husk  over the tamale.    To Cook tamales: a. Lay tamales on stacked steamer trays. Cover and steam  40 to 45 minutes over rapidly boiling water. Add more water to pot as  needed to create steam.
