  "Instructions": "Grease a 15 x 10 x 1 or 18\" jelly roll pan. Line with waxed paper. Grease  and flour the waxed paper. Sift together flour, baking powder, cinnamon,  pumpkin pie spice, nutmeg, and salt; set aside. Beat eggs and sugar until  thick and fluffy, beat in pumpkin and lemon juice. Stir in dry ingredients  all at once. Pour into prepared pan, spreading evenly with spatula.  Sprinkle with nuts (or mix into filling). Bake for 15 minutes at 350 or  until center springs back when lightly touched. Loosen cake around edges  with a knife. Invert onto clean tamp towel dusted with confectioners sugar;  peel off waxed paper. Trim 1/4\" from all sides. Roll up cake and towel  together from short side. Place seam side down on wire rack, cool  completely. Unroll cake. Beat last 4 ingredients until smooth. Spread on  cake. Reroll cake. Refrigerate.
