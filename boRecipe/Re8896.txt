  "Instructions": "Shell and devein the prawns, leaving the tail segment and its shell  intact. In a mixing bowl, whisk the marinade ingredients together and toss  with the prawns. Marinate for 1 hour. Meanwhile, blanch the zucchini slices  in lightly salted boiling water for 5 seconds. Remove and immediately  plunge into cold water to stop the cooking. Drain, pat dry and set aside.    Place a basil leaf on top of each prawn. Curl a zucchini slice around the  prawn, enclosing the basil leaf. Thread a bamboo skewer or rosemary branch  through the prawn and zucchini slice to hold in place. Grill or broil the  prawns approximately 3 minutes, turning once, until just done. Be careful  not to overcook.
