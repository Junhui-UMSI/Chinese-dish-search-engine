  "Instructions": "This dish is a traditional New Years Day stew.  It is thick with hominy,  beans and pork.     *  Pork should be cut into 1/2-inch cubes.    Heat oil and garlic in 3-quart saucepan until oil is hot.  Coat pork with  flour.  Cook and stir pork in oil over medium heat until brown; remove  pork with slotted spoon and drain.  Cook and stir 1/2 Cup onion in the  same saucepan until tender.  Stir in beans, hominy, carrot, celery, green  chiles, ground red chiles, and broth.  Heat to boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 10 minutes.  Stir pork, salt and pepper into vegetable  mixture.  Heat to boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 30 minutes.  Sprinkle with oregano, 1/4 cup of onion and the cilantro.  Serve with lime  wedges and tortilla chips.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/meats.zip",
