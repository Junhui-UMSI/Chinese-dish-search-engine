  "Instructions": "Directions: Heat oven to 350. Combine apple slices, flour, sugar, cinnamon,  salt ,lemon peel, and lemon juice. Toss lightly to coat fruit well. Allow  pie crust to come to room temperature. Place one prepared crust in pan,  pressing in bottom and up sides of pan. Spoon apple mixture into pie shell  and dot with butter. In a saucepan melt butter and stir in brown  sugar and half and half. Slowly bring to a boil. Remove from heat and stir  in pecans. When pie has finished baking slowly pour sauce over pie. Return  pie to oven to bake 5 to 7 minutes longer or until sauce bubbles. Cool at  least one hour before serving.    Carolyn T.
