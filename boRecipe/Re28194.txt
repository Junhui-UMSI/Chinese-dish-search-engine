  "Instructions": "Butter casseroles (custard cups work well). Press 2 to 3 tsp. bread crumbs  on bottom and sides of each. Blend remaining crumbs with 1 T. butter,  reserve. Break an egg into each dish. Mash garlic with cream cheese and  gently place over eggs. Add smoked salmon, folding long strips as needed.  Sprinkle grated cheddar over the salmon. Put 1 fat tomato slice (from  middle of the tomato) on each dish (dice the rest for saled).
