For stock: Heat a heavy bottomed pot, when smoking add shrimp shells and  cook, stirring for 6 to 8 minutes or until shells are toasted. Add wine and  reduce by half. Add 1/2 cup shrimp stock and cook for 5 minutes or  until the shrimp are no longer translucent. Set aside in a bowl. Repeat  process for other half.    Assembly: Place the mache and frisee divided among 6 plates. In a medium  size bowl, toss the shrimp with the orange sections, and scallions. Divide  shrimp among plates.
