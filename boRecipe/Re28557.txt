  "Instructions": "1. Heat oven to 375F. Cook macaroni according to package directions until  barely tender; rinse and drain.    2. Meanwhile, heat oil in small nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add  onion and cook until softened, 3 to 4 minutes. Add red pepper and cumin;  cook 2 minutes more. Set aside.    3. Spoon into shallow 2-quart  baking dish.    4. Combine bread crumbs and parsley in small bowl; sprinkle over top of  macaroni mixture. Lightly spray top with cooking spray.
