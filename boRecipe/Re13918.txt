  "Instructions": "Buy some white wine that you like.  I like Italian Sparkling wines, they  are sweet and are already carbonated so you can make a stronger cooler by  cutting back on the water. Pour in to cooler  or suitable container already full of ice. pour in the carbonated water and  stir slowly.  Garnish with chunks of fruit.
