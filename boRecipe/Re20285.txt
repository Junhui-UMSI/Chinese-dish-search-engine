Heat the butter and olive oil in a skillet and brown veal a little at a  time Tranfering them to a Dutch oven as they brown.    3. Cook onions and garlic in the same skillet until onions are soft,add  mushrooms and cook until almost all the liquid has evaporated.    4. Stir in flour and cook 2 minutes.Pour in wine and cognac, stirring to  boil.    5. Pour sauce over veal in dutch oven. Add tomatoes, peeled, seeded and  chopped, broth, bayleaf and thyme. Bring to a boil stirring constantly.  Season with salt and pepper    6. turn down heat and simmer covered for 1 hour, adding more stock if  needed.    7.Add pearl onions and continue simmering another 45 minutes, watching not  to burn.    8. Pour some boiling water over olives to remove some of the salt , drain  and add to stew.    9. Give it a boil and serve sprinkled with parsley    Serving Ideas : serve with lemon pilaff    Recipe by: Miriam Podcameni Posvolsky    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #958 by Leon & Miriam Posvolsky  <miriamp@pobox.com> on Dec 16, 1997",
