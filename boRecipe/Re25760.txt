  "Instructions": "Preparation of the meat: Make a mixture of all the above mentioned  ingredients and rub the meat with it. Brown it in a frying pan with either  butter or oil. Sauce Preparation: Cut the onion in fine rings or slices and fry them  together with the finely cut garlic in oil or margarine. How to Serve: Pour the hot sauce over the warm  sliced meat and garnish it with Chopped Vegetable Pickle and Shrimp  Crackers.
