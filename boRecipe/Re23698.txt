  "Instructions": "Cook artichoke hearts as directed on package or cook fresh artichoke hearts  in boiling water to cover until easily pierced with knife, 20 to 40  minutes. Drain and set aside. Add remaining artichokes in another layer and  top with Swiss cheese. Pour egg mixture over layered artichokes and top  with Parmesan cheese. Bake at 350 degrees 45 minutes.
