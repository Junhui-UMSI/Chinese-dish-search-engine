  "Instructions": "Wash the asparagus and snap off the white ends.  Set aside all  tops and half that number of ends.  Plunge the tops into a large pot  of salted, boiling water and cook until limp but not for too long.  (Note: Cook slightly longer than for eating.  If undercooked, they  will not process well; if overcooked, they will taste earthy.)
