  "Instructions": "Using a cookie cutter, cut 6 rounds out of each  tortilla and make a 1/2 inch cut at opposite sides of  each one.  Press the 24 rounds into mini muffin tins. Arrange tomato on one side and the jalapeno on the  other to replicate the bands of color on the Mexican  flag.    For the Monterey Jack antojitos: Place a small slice  of cheese in the bottom of 8 tortilla cups, cover with  black beans and top with the remaining cheese.    For the Queso Blanco antojitos:  Mix together the  queso blanco, cilantro, red bell pepper, and chili  powder and divide among 8 tortilla cups.
