  "Instructions": "PROVE the yeast: Put 3 tablespoons of luke-warm water in asmall bowl and  add the yeast, 1 teaspoon of sugar, & 1 tablespoon of the flour. Mix well,  cover, and sit in a warm place for 30 minutes. MAKE the batter: Put the  flour in a large bowl, add the egg, the egg yolk, the salt, 2 1/2 cups of  milk, the remaining sugar, & add the yeast mixture. Beat for 5 minutes  using a mixer. Add the butter & the corn oil. Beat those in and cover. Set  in a warm place and allow to rise for 1 hour. Warm the remaining milk and  add to thebatter. Stir it inwith a wooden spoon and cover the batter with a  cloth. Alow to sit in a warm place for 30 minutes. COOKING the bliny: Heat  the skillet over medium-low heat, grease with a small amount of butter or  corn oil. Ladle in 2 tablespoons of the batter and cook the blin. be sure  to tilt the pan so the batter covers the entire bottom of the pan. Cook for  1 minute and 10 seconds, turn it  over and cook for 30-45 seconds more.  Turn out onto a heated plate, slather with melted butter, and garnishes.  ORIGIN: Chef Mikhail F.
