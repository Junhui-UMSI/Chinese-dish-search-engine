  "Instructions": "Place bread cubes in a greased 1 -qt. baking dish. In a saucepan, heat  milk, sugar, butter and salt over low heat just until butter melts. In a  bowl, beat eggs; whisk in warm milk mixture. Stir in vanilla. Pour over  bread. Place baking dish in a shallow pan of hot water. Bake, uncovered, at  350 degrees for 40-45 minutes or until a knife inserted 1 in. from the edge  comes out clean. For the lemon sauce, combine sugar, cornstarch and salt in  a saucepan.
