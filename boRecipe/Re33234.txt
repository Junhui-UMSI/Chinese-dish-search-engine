  "Instructions": "In a large mixing bowl, combine honey, the warm water, and yeast, and stir  until yeast dissolves. Let mixture stand, uncovered, at room temperature  for 5 minutes.    Stir in 1 cup of the flour and mix well. Let batter rise for 20 minutes,  then stir down and add remaining flour, oil, and salt substitute.    Lightly flour a counter or breadboard and turn dough onto it. Knead dough  for 5 minutes, flouring lightly as needed to prevent stickiness. Dough  should be smooth and elastic.    Lightly oil a large bowl and place dough into it. Cover with a dish towel  and let rise for 30 minutes. Punch down, then divide into 4 balls and roll  into rounds.
