Combine  1 cup reserved pineapple juice, 1/2 cup pureed pineapple and 1  teaspoon vanilla. Quickly stir into dry ingredients. Beat three egg  whites until stiff but not dry. Spread  between the layers and over the top and sides of cake. Chill until  serving time.
