Cook these three items simultaneously, so they are all ready to serve at  about the same time.     To prepare pasta: Cook according to package directions. Drain. To prepare mushrooms: In separate oiled skillet over medium heat, saute  onions briefly.  Add mushrooms, cover and cook until tender.  Stir  occasionally. Remove from heat and keep warm.
