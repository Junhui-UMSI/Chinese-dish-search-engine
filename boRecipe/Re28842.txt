Use it in        Thai curries unless otherwise specified.  A little added to stir-fry        dishes or rubbed into chicken as a marinade before barbequing or        grilling will add a real Thai accent.  A teaspoon added to a meatloaf        mixture or meat balls, transforms them into something that is rather        exotic, and when combined with peanut butter and coconut milk, it        makes a tangy and aromatic sauce for Thai-style satays.          Use whole spices whenever possible.  Anchovy fillets may be        substituted for the anchovy paste.
