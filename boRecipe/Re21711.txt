  "Instructions": "Preheat the oven to 425F. Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan. Sift both flours  and the baking powder together in a medium sized bowl. Pour the dough into the  prepared pan and smooth out evenly. Lightly press the apples into the dough  in straight lines. Bake for 10 minutes at 425F. Remove the cake from the  oven.
