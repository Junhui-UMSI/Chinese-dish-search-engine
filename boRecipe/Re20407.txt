  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a small saucepan, bring potato, carrot,  onion, 1 cup water and salt to a boil over medium high heat. Meanwhile, thaw frozen broccoli in a colander under hot running  water, then set aside and drain well.    When potato mixture has finished simmering, pour into a blender and add  tofu, yeast, lemon juice and garlic. Blend until very smooth and creamy. Serves 4.    Substitution: Instead of frozen broccoli, you can use 1 pound fresh  broccoli (tops only), chopped and steamed briefly until crisp-tender, then  cooled under cold-running water and drained thoroughly.
