Dust a large oven roasting  bag with additional flour as directed on the package.    2. Place roast in bag; tie bag closed. Cut six 1/2-inch slits in top of  bag. Set in a 1 3x9x2-inch baking pan. Cover and return to oven; roast for 1 to 1  1/4 hours or till meat and potatoes are tender.    4. Arrange apple quarters, cut side up, atop sweet potatoes. Brush with the  honey mixture in the pan. If roast is tied, remove strings. Transfer roast, potatoes, and fruit to  serving platter. Makes 8 to 10 main dish servings.
