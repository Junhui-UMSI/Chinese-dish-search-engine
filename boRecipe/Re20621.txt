When the apples are left in  pieces, the compote can be served as a dessert on its own; its especially  good served hot with ice cream, sour cream, or yogurt.    Peel & halve the apples. Core & cut into thin wedges or slices.    Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add apples & saute, turning  pieces over from time to time, for about 2 minutes or until they are coated  with butter. Add lemon juice and cinnamon, cover & cook over low heat for 5  minutes, or until liquid begins to come out of the apples. Uncover &  continue cooking over low heat, gently stirring occasionally, for 15-20  minutes or until apples are tender and begin to fall apart.
