  "Instructions": "Cook pasta according to package directions; drain. Rinse with cold water.  Drain; and set aside. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan cook carrot and green  onion in a small amount of water till tender; drain.    For filling, in a medium mixing bowl mash tofu with a fork. Stir in carrot  mixture, ricotta cheese, cheddar cheese, 1/4 cup of the mozzarella cheese,  egg white, salt and pepper. Set aside.    For sauce, in a medium saucepan combine undrained tomatoes, tomatoe paste,  basil, oregano, sugar, garlic powder, and, if desired, fennel seed. Bring  to boil, reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.    Stuff each cooked pasta shell with about 1 rounded tablespoon of the  filling. Place shells in an ungreased 2 quart square baking dish. Pour  sauce over shells. Cover and bake in a 350 degree oven about 25 minutes or  till heated through. Sprinkle with remaining mozarella cheese. If desired,  serve with Parmesan cheese. Makes 4 servings.
