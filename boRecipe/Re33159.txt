  "Instructions": "1. Saute the onion and celery in the oil in a covered non-stick saute pan  for 3 to 5 minutes. Remove the cover, add the garlic, and continue to saute  until wilted and tender. Nut Stuffing: One cup coarsely chopped toasted walnuts and/or almonds may  be added to the onion with the matzo farfel.    Fruit Stuffing: Ten, drained pitted chopped prunes or apricots; 2 cups  peeled, cored and diced apples; 1/2 cup dark raisins may be added with the  seasonings and broth.
