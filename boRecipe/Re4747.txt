  "Instructions": "Grate the muenster cheese finely in a food processor or with a hand  grater. Add eggs & parsley & blend well. Remove the phyllo dough from  the freezer & gently unroll. With a sharp knife, slice dough into  thirds, lengthwise, so that each is approximately 4\" x 12\". Re-wrap  two thirds of the dough in plastic & return to the freezer. Tear a  large piece of wax paper & place on counter. Working quickly, take  one sheet of phyllo dough & brush liberally with butter. Top with two  more sheets of phyllo, brushing each with butter.
