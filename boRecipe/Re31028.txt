In a large mixing bowl beat egg whites and cream of tartar until  whites hold stiff peaks when beater is slowly withdrawn. Gently fold in  flour mixture, blending well. Bake in a preheated 325 degree oven until a cake tester  inserted comes out clean and cake springs back when touched - 1 hour and 10  or 15 minutes. At once invert pan and let stand on legs; if pan does not  have legs invert over a funnel or bottle; cool completely. With a small  metal spatula loosen edges and around tube; ease out of pan with fingers.  Leave cake bottom side up (top will have cracks) and cover with Orange  Frosting.
