  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.    Using a meat mallet, pound the veal pieces until they are 1/8 inch thick.  Season with salt and pepper and set aside. Lay the 4 veal pieces out, season with salt and pepper, and divide the pine  nut mixture among them. Roll and tie them individually and place in pan  with sauce and mushrooms.
