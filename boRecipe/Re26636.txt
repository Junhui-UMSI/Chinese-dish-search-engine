  "Instructions": "Sift whole wheat flour in bowl with 1 tsp salt. Gradually add water, make a  stiff flour. Cover with a damp cloth. Keep aside for 20 minutes. Boil eggs  and mash well. Place 1 tbsp egg filling in the centre of  each circle. Shape them into round balls again. Now roll out each ball on a  floured board to a circle of 5\" (13 cm) diameter.
