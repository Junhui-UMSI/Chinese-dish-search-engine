  "Instructions": "Heat oil in heavy pan.  Add onions and fry until golden brown. Add garlic,  ginger and all spices except salt. Fry for 5 minutes until fragrant. Remove lid and simmer till sauce reduces, usually 15-30  minutes.  This is an exceptionally hot curry, if you prefer a milder curry  reduce by half the following ingredients: chilli powder and hot paprika.
