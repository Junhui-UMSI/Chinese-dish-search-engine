Vegetarian Steak  with Mushrooms and Bamboo Shoots Mien Jing Tsao Er Dung: Shanghai    A. 3 tablespoons peanut oil B. 1 scallion, chopped C. 2 slices ginger,  chopped D. 10-oz. can vegetarian steak E. 1 cup slice bamboo shoots  F. 6 Chinese mushrooms G. 1/4 cup dry lily flowers H. 1/4 cup cloud  ears [Similar to wood ears, but white and more expensive.
