If you have more time, let it simmer even a little longer.    To make the filling: Combine ricotta, pepper, parsley and eggs in  medium bowl. Mix thoroughly. Reserve cheeses.    To assemble the casserole: Cook rigatoni according to package  directions, but reduce cooking time by two minutes. Do not overcook.    Spread sauce over the bottom of a 9x13x2\" baking dish. Top with a  layer of pasta. Top pasta with a little more sauce, then several  spoons full of ricotta mixture. Spoon together with the back of a  large spoon. Top with a sprinkling of mozzarella cheese. Top with a  layer of pasta and repeat process, ending with Parmesan this time.  Then repeat, ending with mozzarella, etc. Finish with a layer of  pasta.    Bake for 30-35 minutes, or for about 15 minutes after it begins to  bubble around edges. Ricotta will rise to top; it should be slightly  set. Allow to stand 10-15 minutes before serving.
