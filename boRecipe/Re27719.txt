  "Instructions": "Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.    Saute tortillas with garlic and epazote over medium heat until tortillas  are soft. Add onion and fresh tomato puree and bring to a boil. Add cumin,  chili powder, bay leaves, canned tomato puree, and chicken stock. Bring to  a boil again, then reduce heat to simmer. Add salt and cayenne pepper to  taste and cook, stirring frequently, for 30 minutes. Skim fat from surface,  if necessary.    Strain and pour into warm soup bowls.  Garnish each bowl with an equal  portion of chicken breast, avocado, shredded cheese, and crisp tortilla  strips. Serve immediately.    Soup may be made one day ahead and gently reheated before serving with  garnishes.
