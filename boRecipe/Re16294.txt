(approximately 5 cups)    2. Place the watermelon chunks along with 1/2 cup of freshly squeezed lemon  juice, derived from the 3 fresh lemons, and 1/4 of sugar into a food  processor. Puree the mixture until smooth.    3. Remove the top of the food processor and add 3 tablespoons of Campari  and      1/8    teaspoon of salt.    4. Pour the watermelon mixture into a chilled rectangular metal baking pan.  Place the pan in the freezer for 30 minutes or until ice crystals form  around the sides and bottom of pan.    5. With a fork, gently rake the mixture until the frozen and unfrozen  elements are evenly combined. Break up any large clumps that form, but be  careful of making a slush.    6. Return the pan to the freezer and repeat the raking process every 20  minutes until the ice has formed a uniform consistency of soft crushed ice.  The entire process will take about two hours.
