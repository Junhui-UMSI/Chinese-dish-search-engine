Starting with a short side, roll the stuffing and meat into a  neat cylinder.  Use kitchen twine to tie compactly, wrapping twine  around once lengthwise and 3 times crosswise.  Chill, covered, until  firm (at least 30 minutes.)         Preheat oven to 450 F.         Heat 6 tablespoons of clarified butter in a large oven-proof  skillet.
