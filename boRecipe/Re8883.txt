  "Instructions": "1. Wash scallop shells and place on a cookie sheet. Preheat broiler.    2. Peel and devein prawns, then coarsely chop. Quarter bay scallops. Drain and reserve cooking wine. Set scallops and prawns aside.    3. In the same pan, heat butter over medium heat and sauteed mushrooms  until they begin to exude moisture.
