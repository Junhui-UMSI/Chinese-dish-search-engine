  "Instructions": "Place the grated cheese and the 1/2 stick of butter in a small bowl. Cover  and leave out of the refrigerator for 5 to 6 hours or overnight. Steam the  asparagus until tender. Drain and cool. Mash the cheese and butter together  to form a thick, smooth paste. Add a few dashes of cayenne pepper. Trim the  crust from the bread and flatten the slices with a rolling pin. Preheat the broiler. Place rolls 3-inches  under the heat. Watch carefully to avoid burning and broil until golden  brown. Turn and brown other side. Serve hot.
