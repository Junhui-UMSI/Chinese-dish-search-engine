  "Instructions": "Peel and roast the garlic. Cool. Puree the garlic and add to cream. Place  in a sauce pan and reduce to 2/3 volume over low to medium heat. Remove  from heat and allow to cool.    Peel and thin slice the potatoes. Layer the potatoes evenly in a greased  baking pan. Sprinkle salt, pepper and garlic cream between each layer. Wrap  with foil. Bake at 350 for 1.5 hours. About every 15-20 minutes press the  top of the potatoes with a flat spatula. This is done to compact the  potatoes. When the potatoes are done, remove the foil. Service: Re-heat the remaining sauce. Cut the potatoes into 4-6 servings.  Pour 1-2 oz of the sauce over each serving.
