In a 12\"x8\"x2\" micro-safe baking  dish arrange thicker parts of chicken toward edge. Tuck under thin parts.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cover with plastic wrap and vent. For sauce, in a blender container combine, broth, tomato, onion,  seeds or nuts, garlic, sugar, salt, coriander and cinnamon. Cover; blend  till nearly smooth, scraping sides as needed. Transfer to a 4-cup glas  measure; cook, uncovered on high 1 to 2 minutes or till bubly, stirring  once. Serve with sauce and listed garnishes.
