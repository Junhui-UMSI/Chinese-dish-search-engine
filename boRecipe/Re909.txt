With vanilla, stir in sugar substitute with  sweetening equivalence of 1/2 cup sugar.    Canadian Cocoa: 1/2 teaspoon maple extract.    Microwave Single Serving: In microwave-safe cup or mug, combine 1 heaping  teaspoon Hersheys Cocoa, 2 heaping teaspoons sugar and dash salt. Add 2  teaspoons cold milk; stir until smooth.
