Cut into a shrimp to test for  doneness. The shrimp are done when the shells turn pink and the shrimp meat  has lost its raw inside color, about 6 minutes. (Alternatively, preheat the  oven to 500#161#F. Then turn the oven setting to broil and broil the shrimp  4 inches from the heat for about 6 minutes.)
