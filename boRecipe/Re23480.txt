  "Instructions": "Rinse the turkey breast and place it in a large (6-8  qt) heavy bottomed pot.  Add the broth or water, salt  if using, pepper, celery, carrot, onion, and parsley.  Bring to a boil, lower the heat and simmer, covered  for about 1 1/2 hours, until a meat thermometer  registers 170 degrees in the thickest part to the  breast, away from the bone. Meanwhile, melt the  preserves in a small saucepan over low heat.  Strain  them through a sieve, pressing with thte back of a  spoon.  Return to the ssaucepan.  Stir in mustard and  horseradish and set aside. Transfer the turkey breast  to a baking dish and remove and discard skin.  Refrigerate the cooking liquid for later use. Heat the  broiler.  Brush the turkey generously with glaze and  place 5-6 inches from the broiler for a minute or two,  just until set. Serve extra glaze on side if desired.
