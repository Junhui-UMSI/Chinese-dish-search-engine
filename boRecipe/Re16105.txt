  "Instructions": "In a large, preferably nonstick, saute pan over medium heat, cook onions  slowly in butter until onions are caramelized (takes 7-10 minutes). Note: To clarify butter, melt in microwave, let sit for a couple of minutes  until the white solids come to the top, then skim off and discard.
