Microwave at High for 5 to 7 minutes, or until mixture boils.  Stir.    3. Insert microwave candy thermometer.  Microwave at High for 7 to 11  minutes, or until mixture reaches 226F. Beat at  medium speed of electric mixer until smooth.    5. Use 2 forks to dip each layers pound cake square into frosting, coating  all sides.  Let excess drip off.
