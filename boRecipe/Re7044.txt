Grease containers. In a bowl, stir  together flour, salt, and sugar (if used) until thoroughly blended. Add  butter, milk, and eggs. With an electric mixer, beat until very smooth  (about 2-1/2 minutes), scraping bowl frequently with a rubber spatula. Pour  into greased containers, filling each about half full. In ovenproof cups of  1/3 cup size, batter will yield 12 popovers; 1/2 cup size, 10 popovers; 6  oz. size, 6 or 7 popovers.
