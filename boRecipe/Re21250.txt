Mix the chopped cabbege, the onion & garlic, the butter  from the sauteeing, bread crumbs, egg yolks, & the spices. Beat the egg  whites until they are stiff but not dry, then fold them into the mixture.  Spread the blanched cabbage leaves on a large cheese cloth.
