To make the cake, beat the eggs until frothy, add the sugar, and  continue to beat until golden and well mixed.    3.  Add all the other ingredients, one at a time, and stir them into a  batter. Remove the cake from the oven and pour the cooled syrup over it. Let  the cake stand for 2 hours before serving to allow the syrup to be  absorbed.
