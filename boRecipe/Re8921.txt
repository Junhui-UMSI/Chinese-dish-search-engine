Line a large crock  with garden soil and carefully lay coated eggs on top. Cover with more soil  and place crock in a cool dark place. Allow to cure for 100 days. To remove  coating, scrape eggs and rinse under running water to clean thoroughly.  Crack lightly and remove shells.
