  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spread pineapple in bottom of lightly  greased or sprayed 9-inch loaf pan.    In large bowl, combine ham, bread crumbs, onion, parsley, cloves and 1/4  cup brown sugar.  In small bowl, whisk eggs, 1/3 cup pineapple juice and  cider vinegar. Stir egg mixtur into ham. Stir in 2 cups pineapple juice and lemon juice. Slowly bring  to boil over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened and  translucent.  Let stand for 5 minutes.  Slice ham loaf and serve with  sauce.  Serves 6 to 8.
