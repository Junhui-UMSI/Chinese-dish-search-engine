Here is Chef Alex Patouts recipe:    Trim the pork of all excess fat and cut it into strips about 1 inch thick  and at least 4 inches long. Mix together the seasonings and place in a  shallow pan. Place the pork strips on a grill or rod and smoke  until done, 5-7 hours. Dont let the smoker get too hot. (200) Remove the  meat and let it cool completely, then wrap well in plastic and foil.
