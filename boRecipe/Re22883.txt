Add the flour and salt and blend until the  dough comes together, about 30 seconds.    2. Form the dough into 2 disks, wrap in plastic and chill overnight.    3. Remove the dough from the refrigerator and let soften slightly, for  about 10 minutes. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, with a rack in the middle.  Lightly grease a baking sheet.    4. On a lightly floured surface, roll out each disk to a circle about 9 to  10 inches in diameter and less than 1/8 inch thick.    Prepare the Filling:    5. Carefully lift the roll with your hands and transfer to the baking sheet.  With a sharp knife, slice the roll halfway through into about       12    sections.    7. Repeat the procedure with the remaining dough and filling.    8. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes, or until golden brown. Cool on the baking  sheet for 5 minutes.
