Prepare the salad dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the shallots  and the mustard. Whisk in the vinegars and then the olive and vegetable  oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer to a covered container  and refrigerate until needed.    Presentation: Toss the greens with just enough dressing to coat the leaves.  Divide equally and arrange in the center of each of eight salad plates.  Invert a cup of salad onto the greens and rap gently as necessary to  unmold. Spoon a little caviar on top, if desired. Serve immediately.    To prepare ahead: Through step 3, refrigerating until needed. (The dressing  will keep, refrigerated, for up to one week.)
