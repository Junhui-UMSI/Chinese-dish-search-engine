  "Instructions": "Cut up chicken and season with salt and pepper. Fry chicken until brown, in  its own fat, in a black iron pot. Place in soup pot. Cut up seasonings.  Pour off fat from pot. Fry sliced sausage  slowly to render fat. Add sausage to gumbo. Let cook, with lid cracked, on  Low heat until tender, 2-3 hours for a good old bird. Remove bay leaves.  Add oysters, about 10 minutes before serving, cooking just until they curl.  Add file after oysters curl and turn off heat, or pass file to be used  individually.
