When the dough holds together  (add more water or flour as necessary) turn out onto a floured board  & knead until it is elastic & soft.  Cover & let rest for about 40  minutes.  Roll out as thin as you can without breaking the dough.  Place either on a 17\" pizza pan or on a cookie sheet.  Set aside.    PIZZA TOPPINGS: Saute the onion & garlic in the olive oil until the  onions are translucent.  Add the basil & bell peppers & saute for 5  minutes.  Add the shredded tofu & cook 3 minutes or so.
