If its too dry, add a few TBs liquid at a time (I use  vegetable broth, liquid from steaming veggies, or liquid from microwaving  mushrooms, etc. but water would probably do). Add salt, tahini and lemon  juice, pulse a few seconds at a time in food processor or mash with potato  masher -- can be slightly lumpy, too smooth is pretty awful.Add green  stuff, pulse once or twice to chop coarsely and distribute or chop coarsely  and stir in.   Its an ugly color, so I try to dress it up with a lemon slice, greens and  black olives, seems to help.
