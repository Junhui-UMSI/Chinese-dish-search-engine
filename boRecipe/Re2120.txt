Also sprinkle top with sugar only  when using plums or you can leave out the sugar(I leave it out).    2. Use blueberries and blueberry preserve or peaches and peach preserve.    3. Use combination of apple and cranberries as topping.    4. Try other fruits.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by NSherma@aol.com on Aug 24, 1998, converted  by MM_Buster v2.0l.",
