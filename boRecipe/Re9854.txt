In a food processor or blender, puree vegetables and broth, pumpkin,  honey, sage, and thyme until smooth. (If making ahead, chill covered up to  2 days.) Return puree to pan; heat over medium-high heat until hot. Season  to taste with salt and pepper. Ladle equally into 8 soup bowls; garnish  with a swirl of cinnamon-maple cream and cilantro.
