Bake until the cake is golden brown and springs back  lightly when touched, about 20 to 25 minutes.    Cool the cake in the pan. Remove the cake from the pan by running a knife  along the inside edge of the pan and inverting it onto a work surface.  Carefully peel off the parchment paper.
