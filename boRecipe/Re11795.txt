  "Instructions": "STEP ONE: The Dough-- Make the pasta dough as per routine with the flour  and eggs. The dough has to be solid but not stiff. Let it rest in the  refrigerator for two hours.    STEP TWO: Prepare the Filling-- Boil the zucchine in the water with salt  and the pinch of marjoram. Drain and puree them, add salt and pepper. Add  the ricotta cheese.    STEP THREE: The Ravioli- Roll the dough in very thin sheets. With the  filling for the ravioli, assemble the ravioli in the size and shape of your  preference.    STEP FOUR: To Prepare the Sauce-- Peel and seed the tomato; chop in small  cubes. In the olive oil, cook the garlic and the shallot until they become  gold-colored. Remove them from the olive oil and add the peeled shrimp and  diced tomato. Cook for two minutes, add the grated orange peel, and remove  from the fire.    STEP FIVE: Cook the ravioli in salted boiling water. Add the chopped  parsley to the sauce. Dress the ravioli with tomato sauce and serve.
