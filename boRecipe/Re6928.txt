  "Instructions": "This dish originated during the middle ages in England and spread all over  the world with many variations. Traditionalists should use bread, but stale  cake is a good substitute. Red wine can be used instead of white.  Substitute milk for wine and you have a more recognizable dish called,  incorrectly, french toast.    Bread: Cut off crusts and cut into quarters.
