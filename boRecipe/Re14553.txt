Considering it makes about 1 1/2 cups of sauce, and that goes a long way  (ie: 2 Tbs per serving, approx), the fat content per serving should be  about 1g due to the sauce. Add the tomato juice, tamari, white pepper and cumin. Lower  heat and simmer 5 mins. In a smal cup mix 2 tsp cold water and the  arrowroot until smooth. Stir into sauce, stirring constantly.
