  "Instructions": "Boil the potatoes in their jackets until cooked. Peel, mash and set aside.    TEMPERING: Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a heavy saucepan. Add the mustard  seeds, cumin seeds, black gram dal, Bengal gram dal, halved red chili,  asafoetida powder, and a few curry leaves.    When the mustard seeds splutter, add the finely chopped green chilies,  ginger, onion and tomatoes (if used). Saute for 2-3 minutes.    Add the salt to taste, ground turmeric, and 1 cup water. Cover the pan with  a lid. Simmer for about 5 minutes, until the onions are well cooked. Add  the mashed potatoes and boiled peas (if used). Cook for another 2 minutes,  until thoroughly blended.    Garnish with finely chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with Masala Dosai  or puris.
