Lower the heat until water is bubbling slightly and simmer for about 5  minutes. Be careful not to overcook or the fish will disintegrate. Remove  the slices with a slotted spatula and drain them on a linen napkin or dish  towel. Reserve stock for use in the egg sauce. Arrange cod attractively on  a heated platter and serve with egg sauce. EGG SAUCE: Melt the butter in a  saucepan. Heat almost to the boiling point, pour into a  sauceboat and serve with the cod. If you prefer, you can simply pour melted  butter over the cod and garnish with lemon slices and parsley.
