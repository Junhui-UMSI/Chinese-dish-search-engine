  "Instructions": "\"Based on the classsic dish called larb, this appetizer is easy to put  together.\"     Be generous with the green onion. Combine first 6 ingredients in medium  bowl. Set sauce aside.     Bring broth to boil in large saucepan. Working in abatches, add cabbage  leaves. Cover and simmer until leaves are wilted, about 2 minutes. Using  slotted spoon, transfer leaves to bowl of cold water. Drain leaves; pat  dry. Add beans to broth in pan; simmer until tender, about 2 minutes. Using  slotted spoon, add beans to sauce.     Pour off all but 1/4 cup liquid from saucepan. Add chicken; stir over  medium heat until cooked through, breaking up meat with spoon, about 3  minutes. Drain chicken; add to sauce. Season with salt. Cool.     The above can be prepared about 3 hours ahead. Cover the chicken mixture  and cabbage leaves separately; chill.  Bring to room temperature before  making bundles.  No further cooking is required.  Just the final assembly.    Place 1 cabbage leaf on work surface. Mound scant 1/4 cup chicken mixture  in center of leaf. Fold in 3 sides of leaf; roll up to form bundle.
