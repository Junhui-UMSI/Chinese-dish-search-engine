  "Instructions": "Preheat oven to 425F.    Heat the butter in a dutch oven over medium heat and saute the onion,  celery, and garlic for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with flour and mix well. Bring to a boil on top of the stove. Shape  dough with your hands into sixteen dumplings and drop onto boiling stew.  Return to the oven and cook, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
