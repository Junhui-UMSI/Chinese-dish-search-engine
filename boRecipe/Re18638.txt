Add lightly  beaten egg whites with lemon rind if used and almond essence.  Mix to a  firm dough with hands.  Clean hands and rub with a little butter to prevent  dough sticking while shaping.  Break off small pieces of dough the size of  a walnut and form into pear shapes.
