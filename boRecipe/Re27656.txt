In large (5 qt) Dutch oven or stockpot (nonstick, if possible), heat a few  tsp of olive oil and saute onion and garlic a few min. until soft. Add all  remaining ingredients, except meat, together in the Dutch oven (or  stockpot). Add 1 cup water. Simmer covered about 1 hour. Cut sausage into  bite size pieces and add to pot along with turkey. Gently simmer at least  another 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Do not let thicken too much. Slight  additions of water at this stage will not hurt.
