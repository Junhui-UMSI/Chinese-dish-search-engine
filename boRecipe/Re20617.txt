Mix 1/2 cup butter with 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 tsp. cinnamon, egg, sour  cream & vanilla with mixer or metal blade of food processor until  smooth & light. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda & salt & mix  just until combined. Transfer batter to pan.  Smooth out with  spatula.
