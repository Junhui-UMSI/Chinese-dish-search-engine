Using the same nut mill, grind zucchini into fine shreds.  There  should be 1 cup, firmly packed, or 6 ounces.  Place in a sieve and  press out the liquid. Using a  large rubber spatula, lightly fold in grated walnuts and minced lemon  peel, as though mixing a souffle.  Fluff up zucchini shreds and fold  in. Peel off paper, reverse onto  rack right side up, and let cool completely.    Dust with powdered sugar before serving.    Serves 8.
