Basic Red Chile Sauce-MADE FROM RED CHILE POWDER When you dont have chile  pods, this is an excellent alternative. HEAT shortening in a stainless  steel saucepan over medium heat. STIR in flour to a paste consistency. COOK  for one minute. ADD red chile powder and cook for an additional minute. ADD  cold water gradually and stir constantly so that lumps do not form.
