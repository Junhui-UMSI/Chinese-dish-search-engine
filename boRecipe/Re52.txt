  "Instructions": "Separate the joints of the roo tail. Brown the butter on a medium heat in  pressure cooker. Quickly fry the tail in the butter until the pieces are  lightly browned.    Add the onions and carrots, and gently fry for 2 minutes, then add celery,  pepper, cloves, nutmeg, salt. Fry on the same heat for another 10 minutes.  Add soya sauce and turn heat to high for 2 minutes, stirring. Add tomatoes  with liquid and stir to crush.    Add water to cover, stir and put lid on pressure cooker. When steam starts  to come out, reduce heat and start timing. After 25 minutes take off stove  and cool down and remove lid.    If you want to thicken the soup, you can mix cornflour or coconut cream  powder with hot water and stir into soup. Simmer for another 10 minutes  without the lid, while stirring.    Serve with rice.
