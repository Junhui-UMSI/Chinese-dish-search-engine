  "Instructions": "Butter a 13- x 9- x 2-inch baking pan. Cut phyllo in half crosswise, and  cut each half to fit pan.  Discard trimmings.  Cover phyllo with a slightly  damp towel.  Layer 10 sheets of phyllo in pan, brushing each sheet with  melted butter. Combine coconut, macadamia nuts, pecans, brown sugar, and  cinnamon; stir well. Bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes or until top is browned.  Let  cool completely. Combine 1 c. sugar, water, and lemon juice lemon rind, and  honey in a medium saucepan.
