  "Instructions": "BAKED PEARS: Heat oven to 375 degrees. Mix in all ingredients except pears  in shallow glass or ceramic baking dish large enough to fit pear halves in  a single layer. Place pears in dish, cut sides down; cover loosely with  aluminum foil. Bake 15 to 25 minutes until pears are just tender.  Meanwhile, prepare Almond Filling.    ALMOND FILLING: In bowl of food processor fitted with steel blade, combine  all ingredients. Process until almonds are coarsely chopped.    TO FINISH DESSERT: Invert hot baked pear halves in dish. Fill cavities with  Amond Filling, divided equally. Continue to bake 10 minutes, then place 6  inches under broiler about 1 minute, watching closely, to lightly brown  almonds. Place pears in individual dishes with the syrup. Accompany with a  small scoop of vanilla ice cream, if desired.
