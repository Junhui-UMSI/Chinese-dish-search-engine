  "Instructions": "Sprinkle chicken with 1/4 teaspoon salt and the white pepper and set aside.  In a 4 quart pressure cooker, heat oil. Add onion and garlic. Saute for 2  minutes. Add chicken and continue sauteing for 3 minutes longer. Stir in  green pepper, tomatoes and rice. Add water, broth mix, marjoram and  saffron, if desired, and remaining 1/4 tsp. salt. Stir to combine. Close  cover securely. Place pressure regulator firmly on vent pipe and heat until  regulator begins to rock gently. Cook at 15 pound pressure for 5 minutes.  Hold cooker under running cold water to bring pressure down. Remove cover  and using a fork, stir shrimp and clams into rice mixture. Close cooker  again and cook at 15 pound pressure for 3 minutes. longer. Bring pressure  down under running cold water. Using a fork fluff rice before serving.
