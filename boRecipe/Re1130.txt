In a medium bowl, using an electric  mixer, cream the butter with the granulated and brown sugars until light  and fluffy. Add the egg and beat until thoroughly incorporated. Place the balls about 3 inches apart on the  cookie sheets.    3. Bake the cookies for 10 - 12 minutes, or until golden brown and lacy.  Let cool completely on the cookie sheets.
