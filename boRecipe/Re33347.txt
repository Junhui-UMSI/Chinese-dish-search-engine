Let soak at least 3  hours or overnight.    For tamale dough: Beat lard and salt until fluffy.  Slowly beat in masa  harina mix alternately with broth until very light and fluffy.    To assemble tamales: Drain corn husks, pat dry.  Spread 2 tablespoons dough  on center of husks forming a 3 x 2-inch rectangle and spreading copletely  to right edge.
