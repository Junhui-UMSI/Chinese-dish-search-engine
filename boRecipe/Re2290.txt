  "Instructions": "Recipe by: The Bread Machine Cookbook V - ISBN 1-55867-093-9 After both  doughs are finished, remove the first from the refrigerator and the second  from the machine. Roll both into equal, thin rectangles, the width equal to  the length of the loaf pan. Sprinkle one of the doughs with cinnamon and  place the other dough on top.
