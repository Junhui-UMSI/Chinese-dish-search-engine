  "Instructions": "In a medium-size bowl, mix together the lime juice, 1/2 cup of chicken  stock, red wine vinegar, garlic, green onions, chili powder, ground cumin,  cumin seeds, cayenne or jalapeno pepper, cilantro, and lime peel. Season to  taste with salt and pepper. Place the chicken in a large bowl or resealable  plastic bag and cover with the marinade. Marinate at least 1 hour and up to  24 hours in the refrigerator. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Place the  marinated chicken together with the marinade in a roasting pan. Add more  stock if needed to come 1 inch up the side of the chicken.
