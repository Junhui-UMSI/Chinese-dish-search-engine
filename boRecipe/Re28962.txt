Remove and shower with cold water  to stop the cooking. If using canned mushrooms, drain and rinse them with  cold water; set aside. Cut the green onions, including part of the green  tops, into 1 1/2-inch lengths. Heat a wok over medium-high heat. When hot  add the oil and garlic; toss until lightly brown. Turn the heat to high and  add the green onions and shrimp to the wok; stir-fry together until the  shrimp turn bright orange and feel firm to the touch, about 30 seconds to a  minute. Add the asparagus, mushrooms, sugar, pepper, fish sauce, oyster  sauce and chicken stock; stir-fry together until the mixture is heated  through and sauce has a light cream consistency.
