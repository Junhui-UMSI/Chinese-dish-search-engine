  "Instructions": "In a bowl, combine flour, 1/4 cup sugar, baking powder and salt. Cut in  butter until crumbly. In another bowl, lightly beat eggs and vanilla; add  to crumb mixture. With a fork, gently mix in milk to moisten. Stir until  dough forms a ball. Press half of the dough into the bottom of a greased  13x9\" baking pan. Chill the remaining dough. Toss apples with tapioca,  cinnamon and remaining sugar; place over dough in pan. On a lightly floured  surface, roll chilled dough to fit top of pan. Place over apples. Brush  with milk.
