The next day, stir in salt and 1 to 1-1/2 c  of remaining flour. Beat at  medium speed with electric mixer 6 minutes, or beat 600 vigorous strokes by  hand. Knead dough 15 to 20 minutes or until smooth, elastic, and no longer  sticky.    Place dough in floured bowl. Dust surface lightly with flour; cover with a  slightly damp towel. Let rise in a warm place, free from drafts, until  tripled in bulk, about 1-1/2 hours.    Grease 2 large baking sheets or 4 French-bread pans.    Punch down dough. Knead 2 minutes. On a lightly floured surface, shape each piece into a smooth log, gently  tapering ends. For round loaves, divide dough in half. Shape each half into  a smooth ball.    Place shaped dough on prepared baking sheets. Cover with a dry towel. Let  rise until doubled in bulk, about 1 hour.    Adjust oven racks to 2 lowest positions.
