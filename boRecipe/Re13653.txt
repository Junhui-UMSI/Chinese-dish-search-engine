FOR FILLING:  Bring 1 cup water to boil in saucepan. Add porcini; remove  from heat and let stand 30 inutes. Spoon porcini from liquid; reserve  liquid.  Coarsely chop procini. Line crust with foil.  Fill with dried beans. Bake  15 minutes.  Remove foil and beans; bake until golden, about 15 minutes.  Maintain oven temperature.    Sprinkle 1/3 cup cheese in crust.
