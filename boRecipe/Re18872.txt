Butter and lightly flour a 9\" spring pan. Pour in the entire mixture.    Bake at 325 degrees F. for 1 hour - do not open oven door. Without opening  oven door, turn off oven and let remain in oven for an additional hour.  Remove from oven; the cheesecake should be lightly browned and should have  several cracks in the center.  The center will also be slightly lower than  the edges. If you do choose to use a glaze, you will see that this  cheesecake forms its own rim. Insert knife around sides of pan to insure easy removal of spring pan  sides.
