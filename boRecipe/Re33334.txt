Then use them to make your basic stock.  (When you brown the bones and  vegetables, the natural sugar in both caramelizes on the surface, which  gives the stock a fuller taste and adds color when it dissolves in the  stock water.) Always start with cold water--enough to cover the other stock  ingredients.  Place all ingredients in a stock pot or a large saucepan.
