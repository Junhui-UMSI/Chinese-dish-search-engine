Protect  filled wontons from drying out by covering with damp paper towel.    To cook pot stickers, oil (spray or the peanut oil) may be needed to  prevent sticking.    In a large non-stick skillet, arrange pot stickers flat side down (or one  triangle side down) and cook in batches of 6-12 but don?t overcrowd the  pan. Heat carefully with oil over medium low heat until undersides are  golden brown. Then add 1/2 cup water and cover with a loose-fitting lid to  generate steam.
