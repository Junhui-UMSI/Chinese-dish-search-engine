Vevas Cranberry Cake
Half and half cream, BUTTER SAUCE, Sugar, Baking Powder, Butter, Vanilla, Butter, Whole cranberries; raw, milk, Flour, Salt, Sugar, 
So Im sending along some of favorite foods my Mom used to fix in the 40s  and 50s. Her name is Veva.    Mix in order given. Bake in a greased 9x13 inch pan for 30 minutes at 350  F. When ready to serve top with the following sauce:    BUTTER SAUCE: Heat all together on medium heat until butter is melted.  While warm, pour over a cut piece of cake and serve immediately.
