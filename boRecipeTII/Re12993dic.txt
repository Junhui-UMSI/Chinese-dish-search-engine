Zesty Shrimp - Valentines Day
Dry white wine, Dijon mustard, Garlic, Lemon juice, Dried Oregano, Jumbo shrimp, Vegetable oil, Oyster sauce, Pepper, 
Peel and devein shrimp.    In bowl, combine wine, oil, lemon juice, oyster sauce, garlic, oregano,  mustard and pepper. Add shrimp. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.  Drain, reserving 1/4C marinade.    In skillet, heat reserved marinade over medium-high heat; cook shrimp,  stirring for 4 minutes or until pink.
