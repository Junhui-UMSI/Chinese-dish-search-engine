"Little Chief" Smoked Salmon Deluxe
Sugar, Garlic powder, Soy sauce, Onion powder, Water, Pepper, Dry white wine, Non-iodized salt, Tabasco sauce, 
Mix thoroughly.  Brine salmon chunks 8 or more hours, keeping refrigerated.  Rinse thoroughly after brining.  Pat dry with a paper towell and allow to  air dry for at least one hour prior to smoking.
