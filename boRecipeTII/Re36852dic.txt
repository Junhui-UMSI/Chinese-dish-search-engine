Strawberry Rhubarb Custard Pie
Single unbaked pastry shell, Eggs, Sugar, milk, Flour, strawberries, rhubarb, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Beat the eggs, add the milk, stir in sugar and  flour; add the rhubarb and berries. Pour into the pie shell and bake for       50    to 60 minutes until custard is firm.
