Peanut Butter and Jelly Streusel Bars
Salt, Baking soda, Rolled oats --, Vegetable shortening, Light brown sugar, packed, milk, All-purpose flour, Or your favorite flavor, Quick-cooking, Creamy peanut butter, Egg, Vanilla extract, Strawberry jam -- stirred, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees; grease a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan. In a  large bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed, beat the shortening,  brown sugar, peanut butter, milk and vanilla extract until well blended.  Add the egg; beat just until blended. In a mediu bowl, combine the flour,  baking soda and salt.  With the mixer on low speed, add the flour mixture  to the shortening mixture; mix just until blended. Press 2/3 of the dough  in teh bottom of the prepared pan in an even layer. Spread the jam evenly  over the dough to within 1/4-inch of the sides.  Combine the remaining  dough with the oats; drop by spoonfuls onto the jam layer. The dough will  be stiff.  Press lightly to even out the dough layer. Bake for 20 to 25  minutes, or until the edges and streusel topping are lightly browned. Cool  completely in the pan on a wire rack.  To serve, cut into bars.
