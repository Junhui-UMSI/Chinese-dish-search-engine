Banana Nut Cake #2
Water, Box (18 1/2 ounce) yellow, Eggs, Mashed bananas, Baking soda, Walnuts; finely chopped, 
In large mixing bowl, combine cake mix and soda. Add water, eggs, and  bananas. Beat 4 minutes on medium speed of electric mixer. Fold in nuts.  Pour into greased and floured 2-qt. mold. Place on bottom of slow-ooking  pot. Cover cake with 4 to 5 paper towels. Cover pot and cook on high for 2  to 3 hours. Invert on cooking rack. Serve plain, dusted with powdered  sugar, or drizzled with a thin glaze.    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by GAdams1350@aol.com on Jul 21, 1997
