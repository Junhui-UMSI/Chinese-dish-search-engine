Banana Split Cake
Butter flavoring, Crushed pineapple; drained, Butter, Pecans, 9-X-13-inch graham cracker, Cool whip, Box powdered sugar, Bananas; sliced lengthwise, Vanilla, 
In a large mixing bowl, beat sugar, butter flavoring, vanilla, and butter,  for 10 minutes. Spread over graham cracker crust. Add in the following  order in layers. Bananas, pineapple, and cool whip. Sprinkle top with  chopped pecans and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
