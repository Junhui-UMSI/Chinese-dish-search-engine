Wine Chocolate
Milk chocolate, Salt, Egg yolks, Red wine, Water, Whipped cream, 
1 .Mix the egg yolks with the wine and the water. 2. Grate the chocolate  and add to it; add the salt. 3. Put in pot and on medium heat, stirring  constantly,     heat until it gets thick. Dont let it boil, the eggs would     curdle. 3. Fill the hot liquid into cups and garnish with whip cream.
