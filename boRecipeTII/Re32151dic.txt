Barbecued Pecans
Butter, Salt to taste, Hot sauce, Catsup, Pecan halves, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Combine first 4 ingredients. Stir in pecans & mix well. Spread pecans  evenly in a shallow baking dish. Bake at 300 for 30 minutes, stirring  frequently. Drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with salt, if desired.    E. F. SONNY KALB    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
