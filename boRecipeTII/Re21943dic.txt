Applesauce Bread
Eggs, Cinnamon, Flour, Salt, Baking soda, Applesauce, Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Nutmeg, Sugar, Raisin, 
From: pippa@parcplace.com (Pippa Dillman)    Date: 5 Aug 1994 16:34:07 -0400    Grease & flour 2 loaf pans.  Sift together dry ingredients, except sugar.  Set aside. Mix sugar, oil eggs, vanilla & applesauce. Blend together dry &  wet ingredients. Pour in pans. Bake at 350 for 40 min. Check with  toothpick. Note: makes small loaves & doesnt rise very much.
