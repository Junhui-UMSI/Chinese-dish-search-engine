Taffy
Water, Vinegar, Vanilla, Butter, Sugar, 
Cook sugar, water, vinegar and butter without stirring, to hard-ball stage.  Remove from heat, add vanilla, and pour on buttered platter. Let stand  until cool enough to pull. This may be tinted with food coloring.    From the Kitchen of: Mildred Dulany  (Alvadore Christian Church Cookbook)  Shared by: Gary & Margie Hartford, Eugene, OR (1:152/19)
