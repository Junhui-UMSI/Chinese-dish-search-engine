Tangy Grilled Chicken
Butter or margarine, Milliliters garlic; minced, Shallots; minced, White wine vinegar, Dried thyme, Chicken; cut in serving-size, Tomato paste, Finely chopped, Green bell pepper; stem and, Removed; finely, Dry white wine, chile, stem and seed, Scotch bonnet or habanero, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Combine the chile, bell pepper, thyme, and Worcestershire sauce and mix  will.     Saute the garlic and shallots in the butter until lightly browned. Stir in  the tomato paste, wine, and vinegar. Add the chile mixture, bring t a boil,  reduce the heat, and simmer for 5 minutes.  Allow to cool.     Marinate the chicken in the sauce for 3 to 4 hours in the refrigerator.     Preheat the barbecue.  Arrange the chicken on the hot grill and cook,  turning pieces often and basting with sauce.     Source:  "Heat Wave!  The Best of Chile Pepper Magazine", by Dave DeWitt  and Nancy Gerlach, 1995.  The Crossing Press.
