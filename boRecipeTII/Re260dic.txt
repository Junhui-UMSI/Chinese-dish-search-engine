Ground beef, Spaghetti sauce, Cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, Egg, Water, Mushrooms, Onion, Garlic, Oregano, Vegetable oil, Salt, Mozzarella cheese, Lasagne noodles, Vegetable oil, 
Date: Tue, 27 Feb 1996 20:02:50 -0700    From: Tracy Swartz <blackhawk@ccinet.ab.ca>  This is an awesome, easy lasagne recipe.  Brown beef with onion and garlic  in 1tb oil. Add spaghetti sauce, oregano, mushrooms, and 1/2 cup water.  Bring to boil. Remove from heat. Combine cottage cheese, egg, salt,  parmesan cheese and 2 ts oil.    Layer as follows: 1/3 sauce, noodles, 1/3 sauce, noodles, cottage cheese  mixture, noodles, sauce, mozza cheese.    Bake at 375 for 45 minutes, covered with foil, bake 15 minutes more  uncovered to melt cheese.
