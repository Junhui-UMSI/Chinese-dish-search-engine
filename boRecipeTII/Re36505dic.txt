Strawberries and Creme Fraiche
Whipping cream, Sour cream or buttermilk, Strawberries, 
Combine whipping cream with sour cream or buttermilk in a glass jar or  bowl.  Blend thoroughly. Cover tightly. Leave at room temperature for 24  hours or til serving time. Wash berries. Leave on hulls and serve creme as  a dipping sauce.
