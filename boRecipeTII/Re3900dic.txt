Pepper Poundcake
Light brown sugar; firmly, Butter or margarine, Eggs, Granulated sugar, Salt, milk, Vanilla, Allspice, Cake Flour, Baking Powder, TABASCO pepper sauce, 
Preheat oven to 350F.    In medium bowl, cream butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar; beat in  eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.    In medium bowl, sift together cake flour, baking powder, allspice and salt.  Beat flour into creamed mixture alternately with milk; beat in vanilla and  Tabasco pepper sauce. Turn into lightly buttered 9-inch tube pan. Bake 50  to 60 minutes or until cake tester inserted in center comes out clean.    Cool in pan on wire rack 20 to 30 minutes; remove from pan, cool  completely. Before serving, dust with confectioners sugar, if desired.
