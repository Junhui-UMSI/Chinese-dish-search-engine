Chile Powder Sauce
Vegetable oil, Flour, Chile Powder, Beef broth, Tomato sauce, Oregano, Cumin, Garlic, 
Heat oil and garlic in large saucepan. Stir in flour and cook 1 minute on  low heat. Stir in chile powder. Stir to remove lumps. Add remaining  ingreadients and simmer 15 minutes. Remove garlic clove.    Recipe by: Mexican Cookery/Barbara Hansen    Posted to CHILE-HEADS DIGEST by RADiMenna@aol.com on Nov 20, 1998,
