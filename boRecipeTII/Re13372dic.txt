Yang Chow Fried Rice
Cold cooked rice, Vegetable oil, Light soy sauce, Green onions, chopped, Eggs, slightly beaten, Cooked shrimp, diced, Green peas, uncooked, Dark soy sauce, Barbecued pork or ham,, 
Swirl vegetable oil around bottom and sides of a heated wok. Add onions &  eggs, quick-fry, then add peas and shrimp and blend together. Immediately  add rice & press gently to sides & bottom to separate kernels. Add pork or  ham, salt, & soy sauces. Mix together quickly & serve.
