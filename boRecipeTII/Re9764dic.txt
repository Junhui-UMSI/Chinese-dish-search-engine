Pumpkin Seeds Microwave Toasted
Butter or oil, Pumpkin seeds, Parmesan, grated, 
Clean strings from pumpkin seeds as much as possible. Toss with butter or  oil. Spread seeds in pie plate, in circle with open space in middle.  Microwave at High 5-6 minutes, stirringevery 2 minutes, till seeds are dry  and slightly crisp. Sprinkle with salt or Parmesan. Let stand 5 minutes.  Makes: 1 cup Source: The Toronto Sun
