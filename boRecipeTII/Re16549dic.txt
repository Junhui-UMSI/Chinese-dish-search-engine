After-School Mix
(chocolate chi, Semi-sweet chocolate pieces, Powdered sugar, (1/2 stick) margarine or, And Corn), Peanut butter, Vanilla, Chex cereal (Wheat -- Rice, Butter, 
Place cereal in large bowl; set aside.  In a 1-quart microwave-safe bowl,  combine chocolate pieces, peanut butter and margarine. Microwave on high 1  to 1 1/2 minutes or until smooth, stirring after 1 minute. Stir in vanilla.  Pour over cereal, stirring until all pieces are evenly coated. Place cereal  mixture and sugar in a large resealable plastic bag. Shake until all pieces  are well coated. Spread on waxed paper to cool. Store in a resealable  plastic bag or other airtight    Recipe By     :
