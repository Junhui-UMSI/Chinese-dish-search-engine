Abadoos Banana Bread
Baking Powder, Shortening, Sugar, Salt, Eggs, BANANAS(ABOUT 2 OR 3 MEDIUM), All-purpose flour, Baking soda, 
COMBINE FLOUR, BAKING POWDER, BAKING SODA AND SALT AND SET ASIDE.  IN  LARGE MIXING BOWL ADD SHORTENING, SUGAR AND BANANAS; MIX WELL.  ADD  FLOUR MIXTURE 1/2 CUP AT A TIME UNTIL WELL BLENDED.  BEAT EGGS IN A  SEPARATE BOWL; THEN FOLD INTO THE MIXTURE.  BAKE IN 350 DEGREE OVEN  FOR 50 MINUTES IN 8X4X2-1/2 GREASED PAN.  MAKES 1 LOAF.
