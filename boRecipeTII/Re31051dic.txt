Banana Pecan Muffins
Egg, Baking Powder, Flour, Chopped pecans or walnuts, Butter, Bran, Raw sugar, Very ripe bananas, milk, Baking soda, Plain yogurt, 
Sift flour, b/pd., b/soda into a bowl. Stir in bran, sugar & nuts. Make a  well in center of dry ingredients. Melt butter in a small pot or microwave.  Mash bananas. Lightly beat egg, milk & yogurt together. Add bananas to dry  ingredients. Pour melted butter & egg mixture into the well & mix with a  fork until JUST combined. Spoon into prepared pans & bake at 430oF for  15-20 min.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 211 by Lee Theis  <theis@geocities.com> on Nov 07, 1997
