Apple and Green Pear Chutney
Bell pepper, Apple cider vinegar, Mustard seeds, Ginger, fresh, Onion, Sugar, brown, dark, Apple, tart, Currants, dried, Pear, green, Salt, Mint, fresh, Lemons, 
In a large, nonreactive pot, bring vinegar and brown sugar to a boil over  high heat. Add all other ingredients and turn heat to a simmer. Cook,  stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 45 minutes. Pour into  sterilized jars and seal.
