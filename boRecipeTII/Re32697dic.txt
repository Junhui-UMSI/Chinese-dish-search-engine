Sweet Potato Apple Bake
Sweet potatoes, Orange juice, Apples, Brown sugar, Flour, Butter, Salt, 
Cook potatoes until soft. Peel & cut in half lengthwise. Peel & slice  apples.  Combine remaining ingredients. Layer ingredients in casserole  making first a layer of potatoes, then apples, then half of the orange  juice mixture. Repeat, topping with remaining orange juice mixture.  Bake in 350 oven for 1 hour.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
