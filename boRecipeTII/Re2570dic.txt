Peachy Chicken (Or Non-Peachy Chicken)
Chicken breasts, Orange juice, peach halves, Flour, Salt and pepper, Butter, Cooking oil, Vinegar, Brown sugar, Basil, Nutmeg, 
Shake chicken pieces in bag with flour, salt and pepper; brown in butter  and oil.  Place browned chicken in 3-qt. casserole dish. Combine orange  juice with vinegar, sugar, basil, and nutmeg, and pour mixture over  chicken.  Cover and bake at 375 F for 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until  tender.  Baste often. Then place peach or apricot halves between chicken  pieces, baste well, and bake uncovered for 15 minutes longer. Serve over  white rice.
