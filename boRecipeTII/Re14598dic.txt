"Lite" Apricot Stuffing
Dried parsley, Onion, Wheat bread; cubed and dried, Egg whites, Salt, Chicken broth; reduced, Dried apricots; chopped, Celery; sliced, Poultry seasoning, 
In small sauce, over medium high heat, combine celery, onion and trukey  broth; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 5 minutes or  until vegetables are tender.  In a large bowl, combine celery mixture,  bread cubes, parsley, poultry seasoning, salt, egg whites and apricots.  Spoon into lightly greased 2 quart casserole; cover. Bake at 350 for 30  minutes or until heated through.
