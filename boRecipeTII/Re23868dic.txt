Asian Smoked Chicken Waldorf Salad (Mf)
Vegetable oil, Rice vinegar, (small) fresh chives,, Dried red pepper flakes, Granny smith apples, cored, Head boston lettuce (medium, Oriental sesame oil, 1/4 pounds smoked chicken, Red seedless grapes, Walnuts, toasted and chopped, Garlic, Piece ginger (quarter size),, Salt, Mayonnaise, regular or low, 
In a mixing bowl combine the mayonnaise, oils, garlic, ginger, and vinegar,  and season with salt to taste and crushed red pepper.    Remove skin and fat from chicken breast and cut it into bite sized pieces  and add to the dressing.    Dice the apples into 3/8inch cubes; wash and dry the grapes and toss the  ingredients together; adjust the seasoning. Serve over Boston lettuce  garnished with chives and walnuts.
