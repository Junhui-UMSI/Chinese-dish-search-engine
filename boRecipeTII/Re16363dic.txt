Weatherly Chicken and Rice
Whole carrots; diced, Cream of mushroom soup, Stalk celery; diced, Chicken thighs; skinned, Uncooked rice and 1 cup, Onion; diced, Pepper, Paprika, 
Notes: By Curtis Osman, Weatherly. The Times News, PA    Mix rice, soup, water, celery, carrots, onion, pepper and place chicken on  top. Add paprika to chicken parts. Cover. Bake 375? for 65 minutes or until  done. Fifteen minutes before done, add 2 cans tomatoes and cut mushrooms.  Serves 6.    Posted to recipelu-digest by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I Canty) on Mar 7, 1998
