Pecan Salad Dressing
Light corn syrup, Mayonnaise, Pecans, (6 or 8-oz) plain yogurt, Salt, 
Stir together mayonnaise, pecans, syrup and salt. Fold in yogurt. Chill.  Serve over fruit. Yield: 1-1/2 cups.    NANCY COUCH LEE    (MRS. JAMES M., JR.)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
