Pecan Waldorf Salad
Chopped maraschino cherries, Mayonnaise, Pecans, Celery, Chopped unpeeled apples, 
Mix fruit, pecans, celery and mayonnaise in small bowl. Chill, covered, and  serve on crisp lettuce leaves. Makes 4 servings.    NOTES : Creative Cooking with Pecans  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 388 by "lbrandau"  <lbrandau@intrnet.net> on Dec 20, 1997
