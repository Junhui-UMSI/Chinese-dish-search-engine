Bacon, Lettuce and Salsa Sandwich
Mayonnaise, Washed arugula leaves, Wide slices of toasted good, Salsa, Slices of avocado, Thinly sliced or grated, Chopped cilantro, Crisp cooked turkey bacon, 
In a mixing bowl combine some mayonnaise with salsa and spread on toast.  Build up a layer of bacon, arugula, cheddar and top with avocado and  cilantro.
