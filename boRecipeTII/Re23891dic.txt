Asparagu Soup
Water, Milk; or part cream, Butter, Pepper, Asparagus, Salt, 
Take asparagus, break into 1/2 inch pieces, boil until tender in salted  water, add 1 qt good rich milk or part cream, add 2 ounces butter, season  with pepper and it is ready to serve. This is delicious.    Source: Margaret Bower, Greenford Grange, Mahoning County, OH
