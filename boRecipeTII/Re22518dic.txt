Applesauce Raisin Muffins
Vegetable oil, Flour, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Eggs, Raisins, Sugar, Cinnamon, Applesauce, Salt, 
Beat eggs slightly, add sugar, oil & applesauce & beat thoroughly. Add dry  ingredients & blend until just combined. Stir in raisins. Spoon into  prepared pans & sprinkle with brown sugar. Bake at 375F for 15-20 mins.  Makes 18-24 good size muffins.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
