Berry Patch Blueberry Muffins
Oats, Buttermilk, Baking soda, Salt, Egg, Butter, Frozen blueberries, White wheat flour, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, Fresh blueberries, 
Preheat oven to 400F. Combine oats and buttermilk in small bowl; set aside  and let stand. Combine flour, baking powder, soda, salt, brown sugar, stir  well. Add egg and melted butter to oats. Add dry ingredients and stir just  until all is moistened. Gently fold in blueberries. Spoon into muffin pan  until three-quarters full each. Bake for 17 to 20 minutes.    Recipe by Mangia!
