Apricot Cheese Pillows
Lemon juice, Prepared crepes, Orange juice, (8-oz) cream cheese;, Sugar, Lemon Peel, Butter, Vanilla, Lemon Rind, APRICOT SAUCE, Apricot jam, Butter, 
Combine cheese, butter, sugar, vanilla and 1 teaspoon lemon rind with an  electric mixer. Spread 3 teaspoons in each crepe. Fold envelope style.  Arrange in buttered dish. Bake 10 minutes at 350 degrees. Heat all  ingredients for sauce together until smooth. Serve over crepes. Serves 4.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
