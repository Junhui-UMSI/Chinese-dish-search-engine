Tofu and Vegetables with Rice
Into cubes, Sesame seeds, Low sodium tamari, Spinach -- torn into bite-, Sized pieces, Onion -- sliced, Block of firm tofu -- cut, Cooked brown rice, Mushrooms -- sliced, Green pepper -- thinly, Mung bean sprouts, Sliced, 
Saute onion and green pepper in 1/4 cup water for 10 minutes. Add tofu and  mushrooms. Cook an additional 10 minutes. Add spinach, bean sprouts, sesame  seeds and tamari. Cover and steam 5 minutes to wilt spinach. Mix in 4 cups  cooked brown rice. Heat through and serve hot.    May also be used as a topping for rice other other grains; do not add the  rice while cooking.
