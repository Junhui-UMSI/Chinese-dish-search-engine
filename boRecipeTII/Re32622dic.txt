Sweet Onion-Smothered Hamburgers
Chili sauce, Thinly sliced onion,, Chili sauce, Ground pepper, Ground round, Hamburger buns,, Instant minced onion, Vegetable cooking spray, Curly leaf lettuce leaves, Garlic, Dry breadcrumbs, Grape jelly, 
Place sliced onion in a medium nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray.  Cover and cook over medium heat 10 minutes or until onion is limp and  golden, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to low, and add 1/4 cup chili  sauce and grape jelly. Cover and cook 5 minutes or until onion is tender  and thoroughly heated.    Combine beef and next 5 ingredients in a bowl; stir well. Divide mixture  into 4 equal portions, shaping each into a 1/2-inch-thick patty. Prepare  grill or broiler. Place patties on grill rack or broiler pan coated with  cooking spray, and cook 4 minutes on each side or until done. Yield: 4  servings.    Per serving: 476 Calories; 22g Fat (42% calories from fat); 27g Protein;  41g Carbohydrate; 78mg Cholesterol; 392mg Sodium    NOTES : Line the bottom halves of buns with a lettuce leaf; top each with a  patty, 1/4 cup onion mixture, and top half of bun.
