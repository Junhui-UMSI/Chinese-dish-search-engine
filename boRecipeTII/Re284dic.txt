Layered Southwestern Pasta Salad
Shells, Vegetable oil, Ground cumin, Salt to taste, black beans, whole kernel corn, Red bell pepper, Green Onions, sliced black olives, Non-fat mayonnaise, Non-fat sour cream, Hot or mild salsa, Hot or mild salsa, fresh cilantro, 
Prepare pasta according to package directions. Drain and rinse under cold  water; drain again. Toss with oil and sprinkle with cumin. Salt to taste.    Layer pasta, beans, corn, bell pepper, green onions and olives in a 2 1/2-  to 3-qt. straight-sided glass bowl. In a small bowl combine mayonnaise,  sour cream and salsa; mix well. Spread mixture evenly over top of pasta,  sealing to edge of bowl. Sprinkle with cilantro. Cover bowl tightly and  chill overnight.    Each serving provides: 426 Calories; 20.4 g Protein; 56 g Carbohydrates;  14.5 g Fat; 15.7 mg Cholesterol; 499 mg Sodium.
