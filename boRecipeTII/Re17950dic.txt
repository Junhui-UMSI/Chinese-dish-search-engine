Amaretto & Almond Muffins
Flour, Sliced almonds, Amaretto liqueur, Sour milk, Baking Powder, Melted butter or margarine, Egg, Sugar, 
Preheat oven to 400F and grease muffin pans. Put the first 5 ingred. into  a bowl & mix well, then add the remaining ingred. & blend until just mixed.  Put into pans & bake for 15-20 mins. Makes 12.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
