Apricot Nectar Salad #3
Chopped pecans or walnuts, Miniature marshmallows, (12-oz) Cool Whip -or-, (6-oz) orange Jello, Flour, Egg, (46-oz) apricot nectar, Boiling water, Salt, Whipped cream, Sugar, 
Dissolve gelatin in water. Stir in 2-1/2 cups apricot nectar. Sprinkle  1-1/2 cups miniature marshmallows over top of gelatin mixture in a 9x13 pan  or dish. Chill for 2 hours or until set. For topping, combine sugar, salt,  flour and egg in a medium saucepan. Add 1 cup apricot nectar. Cook over  medium heat to thicken, stirring constantly. Cool 20 minutes. Add Cool Whip  or whipped cream. Spread over gelatin mixture and sprinkle with nuts.  Yield: 12 servings.    JAN GATTIS (MRS. JAMES D.)
