Pineapple-Oatmeal Cookies
Baking Powder, Rolled oats, uncooked, Ground cinnamon, Salt, Egg, DOLE Raisins, DOLE Natural Almonds,, DOLE Crushed Pineapple, Brown Sugar, Margarine, All-purpose flour, 
Drain pineapple well. reserve 1/2 cup syrup.    Beat sugar and margarine in large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in  egg, drained pineapple and reserved 1/2 cup syrup. Combine remaining  ingredients in medium bowl; blend into pineapple mixture.    Drop by 2 heaping tablespoonfuls, 2" apart, onto greased cookie sheets.  Flatten tops with back of spoon.    Bake in 350F. oven 20-25 minutes until golden. Cool on wire racks.    Makes about 2 1/2 dozen cookies.
