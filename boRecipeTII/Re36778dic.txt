Strawberry Margarita
Tequila, Strawberry liqueur, Granulated sugar, Fresh lime juice, Frozen strawberries, ice, 
Pour granulated sugar on a small plate. Rub the rim of a chilled cocktail  glass with the lime wedge and dip the rim of the glass in the sugar until  it is coated with a frosting ofsugar.    Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add tequila, strawberry liqueur, lime  juice, and frozen strawberries. Shake and strain into cocktail glass.
