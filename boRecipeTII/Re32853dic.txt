Sweet Potatoe-Pear Bisque
Chicken broth, Onions, Half and half cream, Pepper, Fresh thyme, Pear halves in juice, Pepper Croutons, Sweet potatoes, 
In a large saucepan combine 1 cup broth, onion and thyme.  Bring to  boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.  Cool slightly.  Transfer broth mixture to a blender container.  Add sweet potatoes.  Cover;blend till smooth.   Return to pan.  Add undrained pears to blender  container; cover and blend till smooth.  Add pears to pan. Stir remaining  broth, half and half to light crean and pepper into pan. Cook and stir till  heated through.  Serve with Pepper Croutons. If desired, garnish with fresh  thyme.
