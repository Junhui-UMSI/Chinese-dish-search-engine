Beef Birds in Wine Sauce
Red wine, Cloves garlic; chopped fine, Top round or sirloin steak, Onion; sliced thin, Rosemary, -(up to), Chopped parsley, Proscuitto ham, Romano cheese, 
Cut steak into 3- to 4-inch squares. Mix together garlic, parsley, cheese &  proscuitto. Place 1 teaspoon of mixture in center of meat, place onion  slice on top. Roll carefully, tie with string or fasten with toothpicks.  Brown in oil 8-10 minutes. Make gravy with drippings. Add 1/2 to 1 cup  burgundy (red) wine & a pinch of rosemary. Simmer 30 minutes.
