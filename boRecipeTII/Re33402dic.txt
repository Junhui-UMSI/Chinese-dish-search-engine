Basic White/whole Wheat Bread
Warm water, Powdered milk, White bread flour, Active dry yeast, Sugar; white, Butter or margarine; or, 
Add ingredients to bread bin in order of list (or as your manufacturer told  you to). Select white/whole wheat bread with light crust. Takes from 3-4  hours depending on your brand of machine.
