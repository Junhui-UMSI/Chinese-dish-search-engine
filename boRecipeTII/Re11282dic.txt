Ramen Pasta Salad
Finely shredded Cheddar, Thinly sliced celery, Vegetable oil, White vinegar, Diced carrot, Seasoned salt, White pepper, Ramen noodles, uncooked, Sugar, Finely shredded cabbage, Dry mustard, Paprika, 
Prepare Ramen noodles according to package directions but do not add spice  packets; drain.  In large bowl combine noodles, cabbage, celery, cheese,  and carrot. In small bowl blend oil, vinegar, sugar, seasoned salt, dry  mustard, and pepper. Add to noodle mixture; toss to coat. Chill thoroughly.  Toss gently and sprinkle with paprika before serving.
