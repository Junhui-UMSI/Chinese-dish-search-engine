Apple Raisin and Rosemary Dressing
Fresh bread crumbs, Butter, Water, Celery, Onion, Apple, Golden raisins, Baking Powder, Dried Rosemary, Dry Madeira, Salt, 
Prep Time: 15 minutes Bake Time: 35 minutes Total Time: 50 minutes Oven Temp: 225F / 110C / Gas Mark   Preheat oven.  Spread the crumbs on a baking sheet and dry and toast them in the oven 5 to 10 minutes. Melt the butter in the water in a large skillet. Add the celery  and onions, and saute until the onions have absorbed the butter and are  limp.  Dont brown them.  Add the apples, raisins, bread crumbs, baking  powder, rosemary, and the Madeira or brandy. Season with salt/pepper to  taste and toss well with a fork.    If the stuffing seems overly dry, add an  egg or a little chicken stock. Stuff a crown roast of pork or a turkey per  your pork/turkey baking times/temps or place in a casserole dish and bake  uncovered until browned, about 25 minutes.
