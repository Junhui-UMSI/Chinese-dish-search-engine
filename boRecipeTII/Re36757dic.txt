Strawberry Jello Salad (Molded)
Frozen strawberries, undrain, Strawberry Jello, Boiling water, (lg) crushed pineapple, Sour cream, large, Bananas, mashed, 
Mix Jello and 2 cups boiling water and let cool. Add mashed bananas,  pineapple, and undrained frozen strawberries. Put half the mixture into 13"  X 9" X 2" pan and chill 30-45 minutes. Spread sour cream on top. Add  remaining fruit & Jello mixture, chill, and cut into squares.  Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com (Judy E Ryder) on Feb 09,  1998
