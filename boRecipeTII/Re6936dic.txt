Poor Mans Cake #1
Sugar, Raisins, Eggs, Allspice, -(up to), Salt, Flour, Nutmeg, Baking soda, Pecans, Applesauce, Shortening, Cinnamon, Cloves, 
Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs, fruit, flour, soda, salt, spices and  nuts. Mix well. Bake at 350 for 1 hour. Bundt or stem pan may be used.
