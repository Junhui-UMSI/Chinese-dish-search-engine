Turkish Lamb\clay Cooker
Large onions, sliced, Lamb, cubed, Large Idaho potato, peeled, Cut-up green beans, Large peeled tomatoes, Bouillon cube, Salt, caraway seeds, Clove of garlic, And sliced, 
Soak lid of glazed clay cooker. Mix all ingredients in a bowl, transfer to  cooker. Cover and put into cold oven. Turn temperature to 425F and bake for  80 minutes. From Schlemmertopf Recipe Book.
