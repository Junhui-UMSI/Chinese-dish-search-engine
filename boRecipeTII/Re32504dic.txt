Sweet Creamy Salad Dressing
Plain nonfat yogurt, Sugar; or more, Nonfat mayonnaise, Red wine vinegar, 
Mix everything together in a small bowl and spoon over fruit or vegetables  salad.    *Yield 1/2 cup or 4 servings: 45 cals; 1 g fat (16%). Less than 1/2 cup  milk (exchange).    Recipe by: Fear of Frying 1996 Posted to Digest eat-lf.v097.n175 by KitPATh  <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu> on Jul 11, 1997
