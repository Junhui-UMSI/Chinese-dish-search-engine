Yellow and White Angel Food Cake
Cake flour, Water, Cake flour, Almond extract, White part:, Egg yolks, Sugar, Salt, Yellow part:, Salt, Eggs whites, Cream of tartar, Vanilla, Sugar, Baking Powder, 
Put in angel food cake pan, then stir up the yellow part. Beat egg yolks,  sugar together 3 minutes, add boiling water, vanilla, flour, baking powder,  salt, sifted 3 times or more. Pour over white part and bake like and angel  food cake. Note: Directions for white portion should instruct to whip egg  whites until frothy, add cream tartar & salt, whip until stiff. Fold in  sugar and flour which has been sifted together. Lastly add almond extract.  No time or temperature given. Put in warm oven, increase temperature every  15 minutes. Bake about 60 minutes. Source: Bernice Bell, Genoa Grange,  Ottawa County, OH
