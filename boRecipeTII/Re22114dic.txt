Turkey Macaroni Salad
Celery Stalk; Sliced, About, Frozen Green Peas; 1 Pkg, Cheddar Cheese; Shredded,, Lettuce; Torn Into Bite Size, Cooked Turkey; Cut Up, Sweet Pickle Relish, 1/2 Cup, Uncooked Elbow Or Spiral, Cholesterol Free Reduced, Green Onions; Sliced, About, 
Cook the macaroni according to the package directions and drain. Rinse with  cold water and drain again. Rinse the frozen peas with cold water to  separate and drain. Mix the macaroni, peas and remaining ingredients except  the lettuce.  Cover and refrigerate about 4 hours or until well chilled.  Serve on the lettuce.
