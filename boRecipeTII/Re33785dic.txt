Sweet and Sour Hamburgers
SAUCE, Pineapple chunks; juice of, Packed cornstarch, OTHER INGREDIENTS, Hamburger, Salt, Onion, Pineapple chunks (from, Brown sugar, Cider vinegar, Soy sauce, Water, Sliced green bell pepper, 
Fry hamburger patties smaller & thicker than for buns; brown well. Combine  sauce ingredients & cook until clear & slightly thickened, stirring often.  Add meat & simmer over low heat for 30 minutes or until tender. Add  remaining ingredients & cook for 2 minutes. Serve over hot rice.    MRS M.F.
