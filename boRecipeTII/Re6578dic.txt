Poached Shrimp
Shrimp, Water, Salt, 
1. Wash shrimp. Remove legs, leaving shells intact, then devein.      2. Bring water to a boil. Add salt, then shrimp, and simmer, uncovered,  until shrimp turn bright pink (about 4 minutes). Drain, discarding liquid.      3. Let shrimp cool; then shell and devein. Serve cold, with "Dip for  Poached Shrimp" (see recipe).    NOTE: Do not overcook, or the shrimp will toughen and dry out.
