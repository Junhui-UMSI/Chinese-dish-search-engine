Baked Lobster
Chopped parsley, Melted Butter or Margarine, Paprika, Dry white wine, Fresh Boiled lobsters, Worcestershire Sauce, Finely Crushed Ritz Crackers, 
*** 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 lb. Lobsters (split and claws   cracked) Heat oven to  375F.  Remove any tomalley   (green liver) from lobsters; mash it and  reserve.   Place lobsters, split side up, in large, flat baking   pan. In  bowl, combine crumbs, 1/4 cup of the melted   butter, Worcestershire, an d  wine. Add tomalley.   Lightly spoon mixture into lobster cavities. Sprinkl  e   exposed meat of lobster with melted butter. Bake   uncovered, 35 to 40  minutes.  Sprinkle with paprika   and parsley.
