Bacon and Cream Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms
Onion, Cream cheese, bacon, Mushrooms, Mushroom stems, 
1. saute onion and mushroom stems in bacon fat until tender. Drain off  excess fat.    2. Mix crumbled bacon, onion and stems with softened cream cheese until the  mixture is workable.    3. Stuff each mushroom and bake at 350 degrees F for 10-15 minutes. Finish  under the broiler just until tops are golden.
