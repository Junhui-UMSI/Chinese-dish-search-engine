Amaretto Butter Frosting
Butter or Margarine, Powdered sugar, Whipping cream, Amaretto, 
In a small mixing bowl, beat confectioners sugar and butter. Add 3  tablespoons cream and 2 tablespoons Amaretto; beat until smooth. Add  remaining cream and Amaretto if needed to achieve spreading consistency.  Yield: frosts 24 regular cupcakes.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Lynn Ratcliffe" <mcgrew@ntr.net> on Mar 28,  1998
