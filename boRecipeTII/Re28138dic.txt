Baked Creamed Potatoes
Potatoes, Salt and pepper, Fresh dill, milk, Heavy cream, 
Good with fish. Place potatoes in shallow baking dish; sprinkle with salt,  pepper, and dill. Combine cream and milk; pour over potatoes.
