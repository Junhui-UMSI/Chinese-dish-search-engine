Awardwinning Stuffed Quahogs
Margarine, Onions, Salt to taste, Day-old bread crumbs, Bread crumbs, Clam juice, Celery, Black Pepper, Chopped quahogs, Garlic to taste, 
Saute fresh celery and onions in margarine until soft. Put bread crumbs,  chopped quahogs and clam juice in a mixing howl. Add salt, pepper and  garlic. Put sauteed mixture into bowl and mix well. Let cool then stuff  into quahog shells. Bake in 350 oven for 30 minutes, then eat.
