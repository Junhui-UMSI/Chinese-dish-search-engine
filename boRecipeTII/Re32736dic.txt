Sweet Potato Crisp #1
Eggs; well beaten, Sugar, Vanilla, Light brown sugar, Cooked; mashed sweet, Butter, milk, Butter, Flour, Pecans, 
Combine sweet potatoes, sugar, 3/4 cup butter, eggs, milk and vanilla.  Blend in mixer until creamy. Combine brown sugar annd flour; add melted  butter; add pecans. Place sweet potato mixture in large casserole. Cover  with brown sugar mixture. Bake at 350 until heated thoroughly.
