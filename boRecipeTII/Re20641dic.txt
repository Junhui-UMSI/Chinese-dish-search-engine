Apple Cranberry Rhubarb Crisp
Lightly packed brown sugar, Slices apples, Margarine; melted, Cranberries; fresh or frozen, FILLING, Granulated white sugar, All purpose flour, Diced rhubarb; diced, Whole wheat flour, TOPPING, Oats, 
1. Combine all topping ingredients in a bowl, and mix well.    2. In a separate bowl, combine sugar and flour    3. Add fruit to flour mixture, and toss to coat.    4. Spoon fruit mixture into a 2 litre baking dish, sprinkle with topping  mixture    5. Bake at 375 for 40-50 minutes until fruit is tender.    NOTES: Serve this quick and easy to prepare dish warm or at room  temperature. For an extra special treat serve with a scoop of French  Vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
