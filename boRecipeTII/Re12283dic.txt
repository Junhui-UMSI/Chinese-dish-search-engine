Zucchini Napoleon
Margarine, pareve, Pepper, Oregano, Med.tomatoes,peel,quarter, Flour--subs. potato starch>>, For Passover., Salt, Onions, Zucchini, 
Cut zucchini into 1/2" slices.  Saute onions in margarine until tender. Add  tomatoes and cook 5 min. Then add zucchini, salt, oregano, and pepper. Cook  covered over low heat until zucchini is tender (abt. 12 to 15 min.) If a  thicker sauce is desired, sprinkle in flour and cook a few minutes longer.
