Potato and Pork Chowder
Chicken broth; ready to, milk, Water, Garlic powder, Onion, Au gratin potatoes, Celery, Pork sausage; ground, 
1. Cook sausage, onion and celery in 3-quart saucepan over medium heat,  stirring occasionally, until sausage is no longer pink; drain.    2. Stir in potatoes, Sauce Mix and remaining ingredients. Heat to boiling  over high heat, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer  about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are tender.    3. Sprinkly with chopped parsley if desired.    Recipe by: Betty Crocker    Posted to brand-name-recipes by "JoAnne Critten" <mcritten@wanweb.net> on  Mar 24, 1998
