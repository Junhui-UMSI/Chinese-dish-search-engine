Apple Pecan Crumb Pie
9" deep dish pie shell, Sugar, Butter or margarine, Ground pecans, Apples (I use Granny, Cinnamon, Flour, 
submitted by: sgjg6566@pipeline.com (Julie, Michigan, USA)  Pare apples; cut into slices and arrange in pie shell. Mix 1/2 cup sugar  with cinnamon and sprinkle over apples. Sift 1/2 cup sugar with flour; cut  in butter until crumbly. Add pecans and sprinkle over apples. Bake at 400  degrees for 40-50 minutes.
