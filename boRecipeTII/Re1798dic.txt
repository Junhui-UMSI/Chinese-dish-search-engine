Chicken Dijon
Chicken pieces, Miracle Whip salad dressing, Dijon mustard, Dry bread crumbs, 
Source: The Four Ingredient Cookbook, by Linda Coffee and Emily Cale    Combine salad dressing and mustard. Coat chicken with mixture and crumbs.  Bake in 350 degree oven for one hour.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by MUFFYMOM@aol.com on Nov 29, 1998, converted  by MM_Buster v2.0l.
