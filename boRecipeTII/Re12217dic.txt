Zucchini Casserole #2
Pepper, Oregano, Zucchini; chopped, up to 8, Salt, Onion, Eggs, American cheese; shredded, Bisquick, Vegetable oil, 
Mix all ingredients except eggs and oil in large bowl. Beat eggs and add to  mixture. Pour into large casserole or 13 x 9 inch pan. Bake uncovered for       50    minutes at 350 degrees.
