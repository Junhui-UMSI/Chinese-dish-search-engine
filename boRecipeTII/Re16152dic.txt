Warm Vegetable Salad with Truffles
Chinese pea pods, Radicchio, Celery heart, Salt, pepper, Vinegar, Mache, Fresh truffles; julienned, Carrots, Tomatoes, Shallots; finely chopped, Butter, Green beans, Chopped parsley, Vegetable oil, Turnips, Chicken broth, 
Peel and clean carrots and turnips, trim green beans and peel tomatoes.  Place prepared vegetables in water to cover with few dashes of vinegar. Cut  carrots, turnips, celery and tomatoes in julienne strips, about 2 inches in  length. Heat chicken broth along with 1 tablespoon butter. Cook cut  vegetables separately in broth, simmering 1 to 4 minutes until they are  tender but firm, depending on their type and size. Cook green beans and  peas in boiling salted water until tender but crisp. Plunge in ice water to  prevent cooking further, then drain. Decorate large platter with mache and  radicchio. Make arrangement of vegetables on lettuces. Combine shallots,  parsley, 6 tablespoons vinegar and oil in bowl. Season to taste with salt  and pepper. Sprinkle vinaigrette over vegetables and garnish with truffles.
