Wonderland Cookies
Food coloring, Butter, Eggs, Cream, Sugar, Flour, Vanilla, Salt, Baking soda, 
Mix butter, sugar & eggs. Stir in cream & vanilla. Stir dry  ingredients; blend in. Tint dough with food coloring. Chill dough 4-5  hours. Heat oven to 400. Roll dough a little at a time 1/8" thick on  well floured cloth covered board. Keep remaining chilled. Cut out  cookies. Place on ungreased sheet. Bake 6-8 minutes. Let stand a  minute before removing from sheet.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
