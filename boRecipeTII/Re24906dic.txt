Austrian Cream Cheese Brownies
GLAZE, Chocolate chips, semi-sweet, Chocolate chips, semi-sweet, Flour, Cinnamon, Pecans, chopped, Crust, Butter, Sugar, brown, Cream cheese, Flour, TOPPING, Egg, Sugar, 
Heat oven to 350-F. In large bowl, combine all crust ingredients. Beat at  medium speed about 2 minutes or until crumbly and well blended. Press  mixture into ungreased 13x9-inch pan. Bake at 350-F for 12 minutes. Remove  from oven.    In small bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, 2 Tbsp flour and eggs at medium  speed until smooth, about 2 minutes. Stir in 1 cup chocolate chips. Pour  over partially baked crust. Bake an additional 15 to 20 minutes or until  topping is almost set. Remove from oven; immediately sprinkle 1 cup  chocolate chips over top. Return to oven for 1 minute to melt chips. Gently  spread melted chips over top. Sprinkle with pecans; lightly press into  glaze. Refrigerate 1 hour. Cut into bars. Store in refrigerator.                                        --- Pillsbury Classic Cookbooks                                          per Sallie Austin Krebs Submitted  By LISE WARING <HOBBIT@MAIL.UTEXAS.EDU> On TUE, 19 DEC 1995 093839 GMT
