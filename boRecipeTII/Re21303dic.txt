Unbaked Oatmeal Cookies
Coconut, Cube butter, milk, Cocoa, PART 1, Quick rolled oats, PART 2, Sugar, Salt, 
Mix part 1 in large bowl. Put together part 2 in saucepan and bring to a  boil. Pour hot mixture over dry ingredients. Mix well and drop by tsp. on  waxed paper. Let stand about an hour until firm.
