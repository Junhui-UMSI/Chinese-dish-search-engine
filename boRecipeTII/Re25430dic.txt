Tortilla Pizzas
corn tortillas, Chili peppers, Salt, Butter or margarine, green onions, Pizza sauce, tomato, fresh, Green Pepper, Black Pepper, Cheddar, chicken, Dried oregano leaves, Vegetable oil, 
Preheat oven to 375F. Melt butter in medium skillet over medium heat. Add  chicken, oregano, salt and black pepper; cook and stir 3-5 minutes. Remove  chicken from skillet with slotted spoon; place in bowl and reserve.    Pour oil into same skillet to 1/4" depth; heat oil over medium-high heat.  Cook 1 tortilla in hot oil about 1 minute or until crisp. Remove tortilla  from skillet; drain on paper towels and place on large baking sheet. Repeat  with remaining tortillas. Spoon 1/4 of chicken mixture evenly over each  tortilla. Top each with 2 tablespoons tomato, 2 tablespoons onion, 1  tablespoon green pepper, 2 tablespoons pizza sauce and 1/4 cup cheese. Bake  5 minutes or until cheese melts. Garnish with chili peppers, if desired.
