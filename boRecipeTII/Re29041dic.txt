Baked Pork Chops
Salt, Lemon, Pepper, Butter, Brown sugar, Pork Chops, Tomato sauce, 
Brown chops with melted butter in large skillet. Place them in greased  baking dish. Place a thin slice of lemon on each. Sprinkle with salt and  pepper. Spread brown sugar over chops, then tomato sauce. Cover and bake  for 1 1/4 hours at 350 deg f. Makes 6 servings.
