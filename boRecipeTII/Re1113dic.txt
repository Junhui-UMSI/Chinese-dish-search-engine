Crab Mousse
Pimiento, Stuffed olives, stuffed, Green peper, diced, salt and pepper, Unflavored gelatin, Cold water, Boiling water, Mayonnaise, Whipping cream, Flaked crabmeat, 
Soften gelatin in cold water.  Dissolve in boiling water, then chill. Fold  in mayonnaise, whipped cream, crabmeat, pimiento, olives and green pepper.  Turn into a cold, wet 6 cup mold and chill until set.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Frances.M.Say@bakernet.com on Oct 29, 1998
