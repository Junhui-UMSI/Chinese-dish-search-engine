Lemon Poppy Seed Bread
Lemon cake mix, Eggs, slightly beaten, Vegetable oil, Instant Coconut Pudding, Hot water, Poppy seeds, 
Mix all ingredients with electric mixer for 4 minutes. Pour into 2 greased  and floured loaf pans. Bake at 350? for 50 minutes. Cool in pans before  removing    Per serving: 702 Calories; 71g Fat (89% calories from fat); 14g Protein; 5g  Carbohydrate; 362mg Cholesterol; 115mg Sodium    NOTES : Makes 2 loaves    Recipe by: Cynthia Kilian    Posted to EAT-L Digest 15 Feb 97 by Betsy Burtis <ebburtis@IX.NETCOM.COM>  on Feb 16, 1997.
