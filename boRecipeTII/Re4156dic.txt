Peppy Mini Pizza Pies
Pizza sauce, Mozzarella Cheese; shredded, Parmesan cheese, Pepperoni; sliced, Ground beef, Oregano; crushed, Hungry Jack Biscuits, 
Brown and drain ground beef. Heat oven to 400~. Lightly grease cookie  sheet. Separate dough into 10 biscuits. Press or roll each to 5" circle  forming 1/4" rim around outside edge. Spread 2 tablespoons sauce just to  edges of pizza; sprinkle each with small amount of oregno. Top with  pepperoni, ground beef and cheeses. Bake at 400~ from 12-15 minutes until  crust is golden brown and cheese is melted. Remove from cookie sheet.  Refrigerate leftovers. Makes 10 pizzas.
