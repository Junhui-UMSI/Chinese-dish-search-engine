Warrens Barbeque Sauce
Celery seed, Salt, Worcestershire Sauce, Water, Pepper sauce, Vinegar, Catsup, Sugar, Tabasco or other bottled hot, 
Combine all ingredients.  Heat to boiling.  Let simmer 30 minutes. Makes  enough sauce for basting 4 pounds loin back ribs.    Source:  BH & G Barbecue Book, 1965    Posted to bbq-digest V4 #18    Date: Sat, 26 Oct 1996 10:33:20 -0700    From: Carey Starzinger <cstarz@teleport.com>
