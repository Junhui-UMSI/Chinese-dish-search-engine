French Peas
iceberg lettuce, Frozen green peas, Granulated sugar, Green onions, Water, fresh rosemary, Garlic, Black Pepper, Butter, flour, Salt to taste, 
Melt butter in saucepan.  Slice lettuce in long shreds. Add 1/2 of the  lettuce to butter in pan.  Top with the next 8 ingredients; spread  remaining lettuce on top. Cover and bring to a boil. Lower heat; simmer for  15 minutes, stirring 2 to 3 times.  Serves 4 Typed in MMFormat by  cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Good Friends Cookbook.
