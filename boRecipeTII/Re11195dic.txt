Raisin Peanut Clusters
Sweetened Condensed Milk --, Shelled Roasted Peanuts, Salt, Seedless Raisins, (14 oz.  ), Semisweet Chocolate -- (6 oz, 
Melt chocolate in top part of double broiler over hot water. Add milk and  salt, cook for 10 minutes or until thickened. Stir constantly. Add raisins  and peanuts. Drop from tablespoon onto wax paper. Refrigerate until firm.  Makes about 20 clusters.    Great for the sweet tooth    Recipe By     : "Michelle M. Davis" <mdavis@PINEGOV. CO. PINELLAS. FL. US>
