Beef and Corn Bake
-Salt - opt, Pepper, Onion, Ketchup, Tomato soup, Ground beef, Noodles; cooked, Cheddar; grated, Oil; vegetable, Corn kernels, 
Put beef,  onion & oil in fry pan.  Stir to break up meat till it browns.  Drain off fat. Put meat mixture in bowl. Put corn, soup, salt & pepper &  ketchup in bowl. Stir & mix with meat. Cook noodles, drain & combine with  all ingredients. Pour into greased casserole. Sprinkle grated cheese on  top.  Cover & bake at 350F for 45 min. Remove cover & bake till cheese is  bubbly.    SERVES: 4 Source: _Companys Coming Casseroles_
