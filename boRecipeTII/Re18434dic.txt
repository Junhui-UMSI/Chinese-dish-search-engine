Victor Salad
Gorgonzola cheese; broken, VICTOR SALAD DRESSING, Sugar, Garlic, Basil, Oregano, Vegetable oil, Salt and white pepper, Tomatoes; (cut in 8ths), White vinegar, Lemon juice, Eggs; hard cooked and, Head iceberg lettuce, 
Core and tear lettuce. Tear with hands. Toss with eggs and tomato. Pour  dressing on salad. Directions for salad dressing: Mix all ingredients  together.    VICTOR CAFE    DICKINSON STREET, PHILADELPHIA    MONTEPULCIANO DI AQBRUZZO, DUCHI    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of Italian Recipes>.  Downloaded from  G Internet, G Internet.
