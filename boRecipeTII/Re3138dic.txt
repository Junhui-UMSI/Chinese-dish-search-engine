Peanut-Chicken Skewers
Boneless skinless chicken breast halves, parsley, Crunchy peanut butter, Garlic, Brown Sugar, cilantro, Salt, Cayenne pepper, Fresh ground black pepper, Green onions, Sweet red bell peppers, Green, chili sauce  , Lemon juice, Soy sauce, Bamboo skewers, 
1. Slice each breast half into four lengthwise strips; set aside.    2. In a stainless steel, glass, or ceramic bowl, combine peanut butter,  cilantro, chili sauce, salt, cayenne, black pepper, lemon juice, sugar, soy  sauce, green onion, and garlic. Add chicken strips, cover, and let marinate  overnight or up to 2 days.    3. Soak bamboo skewers in water for 30 minutes. Preheat broiler or prepare  a charcoal fire. Thread chicken strips on the skewers like serpents, with  pepper cubes interspersed. Broil or grill for 5 to 6 minutes, turning once.  Serve garnished with minced parsley.
