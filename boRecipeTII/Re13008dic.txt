Winter Kale Pesto
Dried basil, Fresh kale, Sunflower seeds, olive oil, Salt, Parmesan cheese, Black Pepper, Garlic, 
Ogden writes: "Toss with firm-textured whole wheat pasta, broccoli and  cheese."    Combine kale, basil, garlic, cheese and sunflower seeds in a blender.  Process to mix.  With the blender running, slowly add the olive oil. Season  to taste with salt and pepper; process to desired consistency.    Yield: 1 1/4 cups.    Recipe from "Pesto!" cookbook, pg. 73.  In "The Cooks Garden" catalog,  Vol. 9, No. 1.  Spring 1992.  Pg. 31. Posted by Cathy Harned.
