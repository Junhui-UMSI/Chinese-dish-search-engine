Potato, Egg and Onion Appetizer
Green Pepper, Potatoes, Onion, Eggs, margarin, salt and pepper, 
Boil the potatoes in their skins until tender. Cool the potatoes and peel.  Chop them together with the eggs until very well chopped. Add the remaining  ingredients and mix well. Chill thoroughly. Serve with a fresh vegetable  salad. Serves 8.    Posted to rec.food.recipes by "Joel W./Mirjam D." <yoel@brachot.jct.ac.il>  on Mar 2, 1994.
