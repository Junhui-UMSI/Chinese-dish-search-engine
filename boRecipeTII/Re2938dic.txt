Peanut Butter Orange Bar/using Basic Cookie Mix
Peanut butter chips --, Sour cream, Orange juice, Basic cookie mix, Orange peel -- grated, 
Combine cookie mix, sour cream and orange peel in a small bowl. Stir in  peanut butter chips.  Spread in a greased 9-inch square baking pan. Bake at  350 F. for 20 to 25 minutes.     Combine confectioners sugar and orange  juice; drizzle over cooled mixture.  Cut into bars.
