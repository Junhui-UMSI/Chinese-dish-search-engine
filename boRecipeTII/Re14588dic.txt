"Jammy Muffins"
Water, Whole Grain Bran Muffin Mix, AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil, (Arrowhead Mills), Egg beaten or egg replacer, Raw honey, 
Prepare Whole Grain Bran Muffin Mix according to package directions.  Before baking, spoon 1 teaspoon apple butter (or favorite flavor) into  center of the unbaked muffin.  Bake at 350 degrees F. for 25-30 minutes.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Whole Grain Muffins" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
