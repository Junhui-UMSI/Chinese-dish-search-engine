Sweet Broiled Pineapple
Flaked sweetened coconut, fresh pineapple, Brown sugar, lime zest, 
Position knife blade in food processor bowl. With processor running, drop  rind through food chute. Process until rind is minced. With processor  running, add sugar and coconut through food chute; process until coconut is  minced. Spoon mixture into a shallow dish.    Dredge one side of each pineapple spear in the coconut mixture. Arrange  spears, coated side up, on a foil-lined baking sheet. Broil 3 inches from  heat 3 minutes or until golden. Yield: 2 servings (serving size: 3 spears).    Per serving: 506 Calories; 2g Fat (3% calories from fat); 3g Protein; 130g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 23mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve warm.
