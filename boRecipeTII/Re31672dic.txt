Swiss Steak
Flour, Vegetable oil, Salt, Pepper, Onions, Round Or Chuck Steak, 
Mix flour, salt and pepper.  Pound thoroughly into steak with meat pounder  or edge of saucer.  Brown the meat and onion in hot oil.  Cover; cook over  low heat, or bake in a 350F oven until tender, about 1-1/2 hours or more.  From: Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sydsbook.zip
