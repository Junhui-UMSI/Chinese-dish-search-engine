Baked Buttered Broccoli
Salt, Broccoli, frozen, Water, 
Place broccoli, water and salt in a 1-1/2 quart casserole. Cover and bake  in a 375 degree F. oven, with the meat loaf, for 50 minutes, or until  tender. Drain. Serve with butter or margarine, salt and pepper. Makes 6  servings.    Source: Florence P. Hanfords Television Kitchen Meals, 1964.    Shared by: June Hoffman, 9/93 Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.50
