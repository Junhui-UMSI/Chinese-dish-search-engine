Sweet and Sour Cucumber Salad
Cucumbers; sliced thin, Dill pickle; chopped fine, Cider vinegar;, Salt;, Stack celery; sliced thin;, Non-nutritive sweetener;, Water, 
Boil water, vinegar, sweetener, pickle, and salt.  Place cucumbers and  celery, slices in glass jar; pour boiling liguid over; cover and  refrigerate overnight.  Drain before serving.
