Baked Sausage Patties
PORK SAUSAGE PATTIES, 
1.  SLICE SAUSAGE INTO 2 OZ PATTIES.    2.  PLACE PATTIES ON SHEET PANS (18 BY 26-INCHES); BAKE IN 350 F. OVEN 25  MINUTES OR UNTIL WELL DONE.  :    NOTE:  SAUSAGE SHOULD BE COOKED UNTIL INSIDE IS GRAY WITH NO TINGE OF PINK  REMAINING AND INTERNAL TEMPERATURE IS 170 F.
