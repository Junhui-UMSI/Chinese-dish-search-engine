Portuguese Pork Tenderloin
Onion, Parsley, Salt and pepper and paprika, Dry white wine, Pork tenderloin, Flour, Mushroom, Butter, Rosemary, Lemon juice, 
Season flour with salt, pepper and paprika. Roll tenderloin in seasoned  flour. Saute pork in butter until golden brown. Add sliced onions and  mushrooms. Saute for a minute or two. Add wine and rosemary. Cover and cook  over low heat for 45 to 60 minutes, or until done. Add lemon juice and  parsley just before serving.
