Austrian Kale
Vegetable oil, Sour cream, Potatoes, Chicken stock, Garlic, Kale, Onion, celery, 
Cut the kale leaves into 1/2-inch-wide strips. Blanch them in lightly  salted boiling water for one minute. Set aside.    Saute the garlic and onion in the oil until lightly browned. Add the  chicken stock, potatoes, celery, and blanched kale. Simmer together until  potatoes fall apart and lose their shape. Stir; season with salt and  pepper, garnish with sour cream and serve.
