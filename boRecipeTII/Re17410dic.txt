Walnut Oil Vinaigrette
Garlic; minced, Dijon-style mustard, Kosher salt and freshly, Fresh chives; chopped, Rich chicken stock or, Sherry vinegar, Fresh dill; chopped, Walnut oil, White-wine Worcestershire, Fresh parsley, 
In a blender or food processor, combine the walnut oil, garlic, stock,  Worcestershire, mustard and vinegar. Blend until fully combined and creamy.  Transfer to a bowl. Whisk in the herbs and add salt and pepper to taste.  Store the vinaigrette, covered and refrigerated, for up to 3 days. Bring to  room temperature before using.
