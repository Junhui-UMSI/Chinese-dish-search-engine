Tuna Melts
Sliced water chestnuts,,, Jar cheese spread, Mayonnaise or salad dressing, Snipped dried chives, Tuna, drained and flaked, English muffins, 
Combine 1/2 cup of the cheese spread and the mayonnaise. Stir in tuna,  water chestnuts and chives. Spread mixture onto english muffin halves.  Place muffins on the unheated rack of a broiler pan. Broil 4 inches from  heat about 3 minutes or until heated through. Top each with a dollop of the  remaining cheese spread. Broil for 30 to 60 seconds more or until cheese  melts.
