Tempeh Tahiti
Snow peas, Cooked brown rice, ginger, Barbeque sauce, Celery, Tempeh, Water, Red bell pepper, Pineapple chunks, Garlic, 
In a large saucepan, combine barbecue sauce, 1-1/2 cups water, ginger,  garlic, pineapple, pepper and celery and simmer over moderate heat until  pepper and celery are tender but still crisp, about 5 minutes. Add tempeh  and cook 10 minutes longer.  Add snow peas and cook just until bright  green, less than 5 minutes.  Serve over cooked brown rice. Serves 4 to 6  Calories per serving: 330 Grams of fat: 6.2 Percentage fat calories: 17%  Cholesterol: 0 mg. Source: Delicious!
