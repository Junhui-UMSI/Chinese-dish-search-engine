Baked Cod and Vegetables
Onion, Cucumber, Tomato, cod fillets, Marjoram, Green bell pepper, Garlic, Parsley, olive oil, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 25 min.    Preheat oven to 375F. Arrange fish fillets in an oiled baking dish.  Arrange tomato and cucumber slices over each fillet. Heat oil in a heavy  saucepan over medium heat. saute onion, garlic and bell pepper until onion  is tender. Do not brown. Remove from heat. Stir in remaining ingredients.  Spoon mixture over fillets. Bake 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily.    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 20, 1998
