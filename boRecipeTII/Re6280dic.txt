Plain Fried Rice
Green onions, chopped, Tofu, diced, White pepper, Lime, cut into wedges, Cilantro leaves, chopped, Cooked long-grain rice, Onion, Red & green chilies, chopped, GARNISH, Garlic, chopped, Soy sauce, Vegetable oil, Cucumber, sliced, 
Heat the oil in a wok over medium-high heat.  Add the onion & stir-fry for  3 minutes.  Add the garlic & stir-fry until both are golden. Add the rice &  the tofu & stir-fry for 3 minutes. Sprinkle with soy sauce & white pepper.  Remove from heat & place in a serving dish surrounded with garnishes.
