Pimento Sauce
Salt, Mushrooms; sliced, Pimento or roasted red, Brown lamb stock; or canned, olive oil, Unsalted butter; cold, Dry white wine, Shallots; finely sliced, Tomato paste, Pureed garlic, 
TAMALES WORLD TOUR SHOW #WT1A41    Heat oil in a medium saucepan over moderate heat. Cook shallots and  mushrooms until golden. Add garlic and tomato paste and saute. Add wine,  turn heat to high, and reduce by half. Add brown stock and reduce again by  a quarter. Whisk in pimento puree. Break butter into small pieces and whisk  into sauce until smooth. Adjust seasonings and strain through a fine sieve.  Serve warm with lamb.    c. 1997, M.S. Milliken & S.
