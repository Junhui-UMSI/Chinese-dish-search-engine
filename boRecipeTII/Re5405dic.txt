Pineapple Cheese Muffins
Cheddar Cheese, Muesli, Self-raising wholemeal flour, Self-raising flour, Golden syrup, Non-dairy soy drink powder, Well drained unsweetened, Butter, Egg, 
Preheat oven to 375F, prepare pans. Sift flours together retaining husks,  add muesli. Melt butter, add golden syrup, pineapple & cheese. Combine with  dry ingredients, add egg & soy powder, do not over-mix. Spoon into pans &  sprinkle tops with extra grated cheese if desired, bake for 12-15 mins or  until cooked. Makes 12 muffins. Wholemeal = whole-wheat. Recipe courtesy of  the New Idea Magazine (NZ).    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
