Strawberry Pie
Sugar, Cornstarch, Water, Lemon juice, pie shell, Fresh strawberries, 
Combine 1 cup of the berries, 2/3 cup water, and lemon juice in a saucepan.  Simmer over low heat for about 3 minutes. Strain the berries from the  juice. Mix the sugar, cornstarch, and remaining water and add to the hot  berry juice mixture. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Remove from heat  and cool. Put the other 3 cups of berries into a 10-inch baked pir shell  and pour the glaze over the top and chill. Great with or without whipped  cream.
