Sweet n Sour Pork
Pepper, (1 cn) Pineapple Chunks, Ground ginger, Med. Onion, Sliced, Lemon juice, Small Green Pepper **, (1 Pk) Frozen Pea Pods, Brown sugar, Salt, Imported Soy Sauce, Fresh Pork Shoulder *, Cornstarch, 
*     Pork shoulder should be cut into 1/2-inch cubes. ** Green pepper  should be seeded and cut into 1-inche pieces.  ~---------------------------------------------------------------------  ~--- Mix pork, onion, pineapple (with juice), brown sugar,  cornstarch, lemon juice, soy sauce, salt, pepper and ginger in 2-qt  casserole.  Cover and microwave on medium (50%) until pork is no  longer pink, 24 to 28 minutes, stirring every 3 minutes. Stir in  green pepper and pea pods. Cover and microwave on high (100%) green  pepper and pea pods are tender, 3 to 4 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
