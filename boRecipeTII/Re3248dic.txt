Pear Jam
Pears, Lemon, Sugar, Orange, crushed pineapple, canned, 
Peal and core pears. Mix pears, orange and lemon (include rinds of citrus)  in Cuisinart, until coarsely mixed. Put into pot, add pineapple and sugar.  Stir well, and bring to boil. Lower heat. Cook 20 minutes, stirring  occasionally. Pour into hot sterlized jars. Seal, and turn upside down for  5 minutes. Turn upright and cool. Makes 9 six ounce jars.
