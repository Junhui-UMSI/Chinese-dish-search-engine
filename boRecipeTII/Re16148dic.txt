Warm Tuna-Pasta Salad
Water, Rotini pasta, peas, and red, Frozen broccoli,; (1, 60%-less-salt tuna in, Nonfat sour cream, With Parmesan cheese, 
Combine water and broccoli mixture in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil;  cover, reduce heat to medium-high, and cook 6 minutes, stirring once.    Remove from heat. Add nonfat sour cream alternative and tuna to broccoli  mixture, and toss well. Yield: 3 servings (serving size: 1 cup).
