Three-Minute Oatmeal Cookies
Shortening, Raisins, Oatmeal; quick cooking, Baking Powder, Sugar, Flour, Eggs, Salt, 
Pour boiling water over raisins, let stand until rest is prepared. Drop by  teaspoonful and bake in hot oven.    Note: Combine other ingredients while raisins soak. Stir in raisins. Hot  oven is 350 - 400 F.    Source: Mrs. Hulda Bidlack, Oakleaf Grange, Paulding County, OH
