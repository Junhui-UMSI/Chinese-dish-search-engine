Herb Garden Vinegar
Bottle white wine vinegar, Rice vinegar, Few grains whole coriander, Few grains whole green, Red pepper flakes, Stalks fresh tarragon, Stalk fresh rosemary, Stalk fresh thyme, Balsamic vinegar, 
Slide whole herb stalks into bottle, stem end down. Add peppers and  coriander. Pour liquid ingredients into sterilized bottle with non-metal  cap (cork is best). Let set for four to six weeks. Strain, pour back into  bottle, replacing herbs with one fresh stalk of each.    Typed for you by Jane Stewart    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Oct 26, 98,
