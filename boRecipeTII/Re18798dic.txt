Anchovy Dressing for Salad
(2-oz) flat anchovies, Prepared mustard, Stalk celery; sliced, Lemon juice, Onion; sliced, Salad oil, Eggs, Clove Garlic; peeled, sliced, Sugar, Accent, Black Pepper, 
Put garlic, celery, onion, anchovies, mustard, lemon juice & spices in  blender & whirl for about 2 seconds. Add eggs & blend again. Add 2 cups  oil, 1/4 cup at a time, blending between additions; then blend a few  seconds more after all the oil has been added. Makes about 1 quart. Serve  on romaine, spinach or head lettuce with crisp toasted croutons.    Source: THE BLISS OF COOKING RETURNS, FT.
