Puddin Obler
Sugar, Salt, Baking Powder, Flour, Sweetened fruit, Butter, milk, 
Melt butter in 350 degree oven. Sift dry ingredients together. Stir in  milk, making a soft batter. Pour into center of butter. Do not stir. Pour  fruit & its juice evenly over top of batter. Bake uncovered for 45-55  minutes.    ANNA MAE COOKE    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
