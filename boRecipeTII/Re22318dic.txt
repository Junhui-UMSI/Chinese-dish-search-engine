Turkey-Broccoli Melt
Prepared mustard, Frozen broccoli spears, (1, Whole wheat bread, (4), Alfalfa sprouts, Deli turkey breast, (4), Reduced-fat sharp Cheddar, 
Cook broccoli according to package directions. Drain; set aside.    Spread 1 teaspoon mustard over each bread slice; place on a baking sheet.  Top each with 1 turkey slice, 3 broccoli spears, and 1 tablespoon cheese.  Broil 3 inches from heat 1-1/2 minutes. Top with 1/4 cup sprouts. Yield: 4  sandwiches.    Per serving: 86 Calories; 2g Fat (19% calories from fat); 8g Protein; 10g  Carbohydrate; 6mg Cholesterol; 167mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve immediately.
