Banana Raisin Bread
Active dry yeast, Cinnamon, Water, Salt, Raisins, Bananas; Peeled & Mashed, Bread flour, Corn oil, Flour, Egg, Honey, Walnuts; broken, 
Yields 1 Loaf    All ingredients should be at room temperature before starting. Add all the  ingredients except the raisins, broken nut meats and the final flour in the  order listed. Select" White Bread". Press "Start".    Roll the raisins in the extra flour. Add the raisins and the broken nut  meats to the pan just before the end of the final kneading, when the  "beeps" sound.    >From: Joel.Ehrlich@salata.com (Joel Ehrlich)  From: Bread-Bakers Archives: ftp.best.com/pub/reggie/archives/bread/recipe    Recipe By     : The Bread Machine Cookbook V, Donna Rathmell German  p80
