"Forgotten" Minestrone
Parmesan cheese, Zucchini, Onion, Salt, garbanzo beans, Beef bouillon, Pepper, Water, dried parsley, Ground thyme, Lean beef stew meat, tomatoes with liquid, small elbow macaroni, uncooked, 
In a slow cooker, combine beef, water, tomatoes, bouillon, onion, parsley,  salt if desired, thyme and pepper.  Cover and cook on low for 7-9 hours or  until meat is tender.  Add zucchini, cabbage, beans and macaroni; cook on  high, covered, 30-45 minutes more, or until the vegetables are tender.  Sprinkle individual servings with Parmesan cheese if desired.    Diabetic Exchanges:  One serving (without added salt and Parmesan cheese)  equals 2 vegetable, 1-1/2 starch, 1 meat.    Also, 246 CALORIES, 453mg SODIUM, 33mg CHOLESTEROL, 30gm CARBOHYDRATE, 19gm  PROTEIN, 6gm FAT.
