Peanut Brittle #1
Baking soda, Sugar, Water, Light corn syrup, Salt, Raw peanuts, 
Combine sugar, peanuts, corn syrup, water and salt. Cook on medium,  stirring continuously, until peanuts are very brown, to hard-crack stage.  Remove from heat; add soda and stir. Pour into well-buttered baking sheet.
