Banana Drop Cookies
BANANAS FRESH, Baking Powder, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, Baking soda, SHORTENING; 3LB, All Purpose Flour, IMITATION VANILLA, Salt, EGGS SHELL, 
PAN:  18 BY 24-INCH SHEET PAN                  TEMPERATURE:  400 F. OVEN    1.  PLACE INGREDIENTS IN MIXER BOWL IN ORDER LISTED.   MIX AT LOW SPEED  1 TO 2 MINUTES OR UNTIL THOROUGHLY BLENDED. SCRAPE DOWN BOWL ONCE DURING  MIXING.    2.  DROP BY TABLESPOONS IN ROWS, 4 BY 6 ONTO LIGHTLY GREASED PANS.    3.  BAKE ABOUT 12 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE.    4.  LOOSEN COOKIES FROM PAN WHILE STILL WARM.    NOTE:  5 LB BANANAS A.P. (15 BANANAS) WILL YEILD 3 LB MASHED POTATOES.
