Applesauce Spice Muffins
Vanilla, Pumpkin pie spice, Skim milk, Egg whites, Sugar, Baking Powder, Salt, Vegetable cooking spray, Vegetable oil, Brown Sugar, All-purpose flour, Unsweetened applesauce, TOPPING, Cinnamon, 
these recipies from.. <A  HREF="http://www.kelloggs.com/kitchens/book/index.html">Welcome to Kellogg  Kitchens(TM)!</A>    1.Combine flour, baking powder, salt and spice. Set aside.    2.In large mixing bowl, combine Kelloggs? Low Fat Granola cereal, brown  sugar, milk and applesauce. Add egg whites, oil and vanilla to cereal  mixture. Mix well. Stir in flour mixture only until combined. Spoon batter  evenly into twelve, 2 1/2-inch muffin-pan cups coated with cooking spray.  Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar mixture.    3.Bake at 400F about 20 minutes or until lightly browned. Serve warm.
