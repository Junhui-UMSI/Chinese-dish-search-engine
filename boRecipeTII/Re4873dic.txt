Pickled Carrots
Water, Salt, Whole mixed spices, Sugar, Vinegar, Small carrots, 
Scrape and wash carrots.  Boil until tender in water to which 1/2 teaspoon  salt has been added per quart. Pack in sterilized jars. Fill jars to within  1/4 inch of top with sirup made by boiling together vinegar, water, sugar,  and spices. Mrs. Harry R. Baer, Meyersdale, PA.
