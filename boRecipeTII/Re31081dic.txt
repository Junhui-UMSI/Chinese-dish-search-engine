Banana Puffs
Bananas, Self Rising Flour, Castor sugar, Lemon juice, Castor sugar, Cinnamon, Oil, Strawberry or rasberry jam, 
Total Time: about 15 minutes  1. Mash bananas with 1/4 pound of castor sugar and lemon juice, add flour and mix  to a thick paste.    2. Heat oil in a small pan to smoking point, then turn heat  to low.   3. Use 2 teaspoons to push one teaspoon of dough into the oil, doing  no more than 6 at a time. Turn puffs over and keep turning for 2 minutes.    4. Remove with a slotted spoon, drain and roll in cinnamon and sugar.   Serve  with jam.
