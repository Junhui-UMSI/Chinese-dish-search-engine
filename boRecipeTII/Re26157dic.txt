Toll House Chocolate Chip Pie
Semisweet Chocolate Chips, Walnuts, Pie Shell, Flour, Eggs, Butter, Brown Sugar, Sugar, 
* melted and cooled to room temperature    Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  In large bowl, beat eggs until foamy. Add  flour, sugar and brown sugar; beat until well blended. Blend in melted  butter.  Stir in semi-sweet chips and walnuts. Pour into pie shell. Bake at  325 for 1 hour.  Serve warm with whipped cream or ice cream. Makes one pie.    If using frozen pie shell, ony use deep dish style and thaw completely.  Place on cookie sheet; bake 10 minutes longer.    Recipe By     : <mandc@cascade.  net>
