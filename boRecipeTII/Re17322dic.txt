Waldorf Salad Spread
Red/green seedless grapes, (8-oz) neutchatel cheese, Lemon juice, And assorted crackers opt., Raisins, Cored red apple, Ground cinnamon, Pinch ground nutmeg, Low fat cottage cheese, Celery, Walnuts, Sugar, 
Chop half of apple; slice half. In small bowl, blend cheeses and juice.  Stir in chopped apple and next 6 ingredients. Spoon into serving bowl.  Serve with apple slices, grapes and crackers.
