Witches Hats
Unsweetened baking chocolate, Miniature marshmallows;, Puffed rice cereal, Semisweet chocolate chips;, Corn syrup, 
Preheat oven to 350. In a saucepan, melt unsweetened chocolate and  marshmallows. Add corn syrup. While chocolate is melting, place puffed  cereal in a shallow pan. Bake for 10 minutes. Then, place in prepared bowl.  Pour chocolate mixture over puffed cereal. Stir to coat cereal evenly. Form  into 8 cones, flattening large end to make brim. In a double boiler, melt  chocolate chips. Dip cones into melted chocolate to coat.  Place on waxed  paper to cool and harden.
