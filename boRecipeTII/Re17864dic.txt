Aloha Pasta Salad
Vegetable oil, Red onion,; chopped, Snow peas, Vidalia onion,; diced, Celery stalks,; diced, White vinegar, Pasta,; cooked, Heads broccoli,; cut into, Fresh pineapple,; diced, Dijon mustard, 
Rinse pasta and set aside. Steam peas and broccoli together until tender,  about 4 minutes. Allow vegetables to cool. In large bowl mix together  pasta, broccoli, peas, pineapple, onion, and celery. In small bowl combine  mustard, red onion, vinegar and oil. Whisk together to combine. Pour  dressing over pasta, stirring gently to combine. Serve chilled.
