Apple Macadamia Bake
Peeled & thinly sliced, All-purpose flour, Sugar, Butter or margarine; cut, Brown Sugar, Coarsely chopped macadamias;, Apricot preserves, Ground cinnamon, 
Combine first 5 ingredients in a bowl. Cut in margarine with a pastry  blender until mixture is crumbly. Place thinly sliced apple in a lightly  greased 8-inch square baking dish; drop apricot preserves by teaspoonfuls  onto apple slices, and sprinkle evenly with flour mixture. Bake at 375? for       35    minutes or until golden.    Makes 6 servings. Kitchen Express: Substitute 2 (12-ounce) packages frozen  scalloped apples, thawed, for fresh apples.
