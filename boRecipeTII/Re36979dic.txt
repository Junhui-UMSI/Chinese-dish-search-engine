Strawberry-Banana Squares
Eggs, Nuts; if desired, Butter, Strawberries; sliced, Cream; whipped, Vanilla wafers; crushed, Bananas; sliced, 
Blend softened butter and confectioners sugar, beating until creamy. Add  eggs, 1 at a time, beating after each. Fold in crumbs. Spread evenly in  2-1/2 quart shallow dish. Add strawberries and bananas as next layer,  spread even- ly with whipped cream and sprinkle with more wafer crumbs.  Refrigerate. Slice or cut in squares to serve. Serves 16.
