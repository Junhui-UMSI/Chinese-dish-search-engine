Barbecued Lamb
Salt, Soya sauce, Lean lamb, Garlic, Sugar, 
1. Cut the lamb into thin slices    2. Mix together the remaining ingredients. Coat all pieces of lamb with the  sauce and allow to soak 15 minutes in a dish.    3. Grill on each side for 5 minutes and serve immediately. Serves 4    SOURCE: *Kiwi Cookbook, by Alan Armstrong, Seven Seas Publishing Pty Ltd,  PO Box 1431, Wellington, New Zealand, (C. 1968) ISBN 85467 016 5 SHARED BY:  Jim Bodle 5/93
