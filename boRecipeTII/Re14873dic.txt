1990 Christmas Ale
Cascade hops (finish), Orange peel, Pale unhopped liquid extract, Flaked barley, Irish moss, Malted wheat, Chinook hops (boil), Pale malt, Kent goldings (finish), Liquid wheat extract, Gypsum, Honey, Salt, Northern brewer (boil), 
Recipe by: Chuck Cox Preparation Time: 4:00 This is a 9-gallon partial mash  recipe. Use standard procedures, brewing about 7 gallons of wort in a  10-gallon kettle, followed by  a  7-gallon primary and 2 5-gallon  secondaries, then keg (or bottle).
