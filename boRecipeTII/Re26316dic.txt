Tomato and Scallion Salad with Raspberry Vine
Raspberry Vinegar, Ripe Tomatoes, sliced, Sugar, Salt, Parsley, coarsley chopped, Ground pepper, Scallions, thinly sliced, 
Sprinkle tomatoes with salt, pepper and sugar, then with raspberry vinegar.  Toss in scallions and parsley and serve.
