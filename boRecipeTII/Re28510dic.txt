Tex Mex Chili
Green bell pepper --, Can, Can, Tomatoes with green chilis, Tomato paste, Bulk pork sausage or ground, Sour cream, Chopped, Cheddar cheese, Red kidney beans -- drained, Juice cocktail, Cumin, Celery, Mexican style, Beef, Salt, Tomatoes -- cut up, Tomato juice or vegetable, Chili powder, To 4 tsps., To 2 cans, Cloves garlic -- minced, Onions, Can, 
In skillet, cook sausge or ground beef and garlic until brown. Drain fat.  Stir in chili powder and cumin. Cook for 2 minutes longer. Meanwheil, in  crockpot, combine beans, celery, onion, green pepper, tomatoes, tomatoes  with chili peppers, juice, tomato paste and salt. Stir in browned meat  mixture. Cover and cook on Low for 10-12 hours or on High for 4-5 hours.  Ladle chili into soup bowls. Pass cheese and sour cream for topping.
