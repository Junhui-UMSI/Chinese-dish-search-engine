Pear-Custard Pie
Vanilla, Sugar, Nutmeg, Ripe pears, TOPPING, Cinnamon, Brown sugar, Flour, Egg, Sugar, Sour cream, walnut pieces, 
Place pie crust in glass pie dish (9 inch).    Beat egg lightly in bowl. Add sour cream, sugar, vanilla, flour and  optional nutmeg to beaten egg. Blend until smooth. Peel, core and slice  pears thinly. Add pear slices to custard in the bowl. Toss pear slices in  the custard until all pear slices are coated with custard. Place the coated  slices in pie dish with the uncooked pie crust. Arrange the coated sliced  pears in the crust to be more or less flat. Mix all topping ingredients,  except the nuts (if used), together. Sprinkle the nuts evenly over the top  of the pie. Pour all of topping on top of pie and smooth out the topping.  Bake at 375 F. (glass pie dish) for 1 hour and 10 minutes.
