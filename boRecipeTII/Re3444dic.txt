Pecan Clusters
Pecans, Sugar, Corn syrup, Evaporated milk, chocolate morsels, 
In saucepan, boil sugar, milk and corn syrup for 2 minutes. Stir in  chocolate morsels and pecans. Drop by teaspoonful onto waxed paper.
