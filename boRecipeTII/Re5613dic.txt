Pineapple Sherbet (Sharbatee Gulab) -- Middle Eastern
Evaporated skim milk, Rose water; or vanilla, Crushed pineapple; in its, Sugar substitute equivalent, Water, 
Combine all ingredients; stir well. Pour into ice-cream maker. Freeze  following manufacturers instructions. Or, alternatively, freeze for 30  minutes, stir well. Repeat 3 times, stirring or beating well to finely  distribute ice crystals. Serve immediately, or freeze in covered freezer  container for up to 4 weeks.
