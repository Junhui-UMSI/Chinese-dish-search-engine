Quick Buckwheat Cakes
Salt, Milk; sweet, Flour; white, Shortening; melted, Flour; buckwheat, Egg, Baking Powder, 
Sift dry ingredients. Mix egg, milk and shortening and add to dry  ingredients. Beat until smooth.  Source: Orpha Robertson, Weaver Grange, Knox County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
