Basic Bannock/scone Mixture
Baking Powder, Sugar, Butter, All purpose flour, Salt, 
In a food processor with steel blade,combine flour,baking powder,salt and  sugar for 3 to 4 seconds.Add butter and process to consistency of fine  breadcrumbs.Alternately,cut butter into dry ingredients with pastry blender  or rub in with fingers.Store in airtight container in cool,dry place.Makes  5 1/2 to 6 cups mixture.Scone recipe under separate post.
