Ambrosia Dessert Squares
Filling -- (6 servings), Can, Vanilla Pudding & Pie, Envelopes, Mandarin Oranges -- drained, Graham cracker crumbs, Bananas, Prepared (Dream Whip, Miniature Marshmallows -- or, Margarine Melted, Cream Cheese -- softened, Fruit flavored, Dessert Topping Mix --, 
1.   Heat oven to 350 F (180 C)    2.   Mix graham crumbs and margarine.  Press firmly into bottom of 13 X 9  inch pan.  Bake for 5 minutes.  Cool.    3.   Prepare pudding and pie filling mix according to package directions  for microwave or top of stove.  Beat cream cheese into hot filling. Cover  with plastic wrap and chill 40 minutes.    4.   Slice bananas over crumb crust.  Stir pudding and fold in oranges,  then spoon over bananas.  Fold marshmallows into dessert topping and spread  over filling.  Chill 3 hours.  Cut into squares.    Makes 12 servings.    From:  Kraft Kitchens    Tip: This dessert will keep in the refrigerator for at least 4 days.
