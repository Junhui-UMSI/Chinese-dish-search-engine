Apple Streusel Muffins
Sugar, Salt, Ground cinnamon, All purpose flour, Eggs, (8-oz) sour cream, All purpose flour, Margarine or butter melted, Baking Powder, Sugar, Ground cinnamon, TOPPING, Margarine or butter, Baking soda, Finely chopped unpeeled, 
From: "Ms. Martin" <kmart1@POP.UKY.EDU>    Date: Tue, 2 Jul 1996 11:44:58 -0400  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease 18 muffin cups or line with paper.    2. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking soda,  and salt.    3. In a small mixing bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the sour cream and the  melted butter. Add the egg mixture to the flour misture along with the  chopped apples. Stir just until moistened.    4. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling them 2/3 full.    5. Then in a small bowl, stir together the sugar, flour and cinnamon for  the topping. Using a pastry cutter, or fork, cut in the 2 tbsp of margarine  or butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle a portion of  the topping on each muffin.    6. Bake the muffins for 20 to 25 minutes or until the tops are golden  brown.    EAT-L DIGEST  1 JULY 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
