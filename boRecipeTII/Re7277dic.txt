Pork Chop Parmigiana
Spaghetti sauce; (about), Mozerella cheese, Boneless porkchops, Cooked spaghetti, 
1. Brown porkchops in frying pan. Place in 2 inch deep baking dish.    2. Put a slice of cheese over each chop. Cover with sauce and bake in a 350  degree oven for 45 mins (1 hour if very thick chops).    3. Serve over your favourite spaghetti with a little extra sauce.
