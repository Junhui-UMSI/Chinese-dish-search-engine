Baked Oatmeal
milk, Sugar*, Eggs, Salt, Oatmeal, Vegetable oil, Cinnamon; (up to 1), Baking Powder, 
Mix oil, sugar and eggs, add rest of ingredients, mix well. Bake 30 minutes  at 350 degrees uncovered. Serve warm with milk. * This was very sweet so I  used only half the sugar, 3/4 cup.
