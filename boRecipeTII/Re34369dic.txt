Bean and Chicken Burritos
Deli chicken breast, reduced fat cheese, Flour tortillas, Fat free refried beans, lettuce, Salsa, 
Heat oven to 400. Spread 1/4 cup refried beans evenly onto each tortilla.  Place 2 slices chicken and 2 tablespoons cheese down the center 1/3 of each  tortilla; fold sides over filling. Place, folded side down, on an ungreased  cookie sheet. Bake at 400 6-8 minutes or until throoughly heated. Serve  topped with lettuce and salsa.    Per serving: 240 cal., 15g pro., 32g carb., 5g fiber, 6g fat, 19mg chol.,  890mg sod., 180mg pot., 2 starch and 1 1/2 lean meat exchanges.
