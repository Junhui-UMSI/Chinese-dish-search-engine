Beef with Dry Spice Rub
Brown Sugar, Black peppercorns, Yellow mustard seeds, Whole coriander seeds, Garlic, Beef top round steak, Salt, 
Place sugar, peppercorns, mustard seeds, coriander seeds and garlic in  blender or food processor; process until seeds and garlic are crushed. Rub  beef with oil; pat on spice mixture. Season generously with salt.    Lightly oil hot grid to prevent sticking. Grill beef on covered grill over  medium-low briquets 16-20 minutes for medium or until desired doneness,  turning once. Let stand 5 minutes before cutting across the grain into thin  diagonal slices. Makes 6 servings.
