Underground Deli Meatloaf Sandwich
jack or  Swiss cheese, Dry mustard, Dried basil, Eggs, Dried Oregano, Garlic powder, Ketchup, Hot pepper sauce, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire Sauce, Mayonnaise, Ground beef; lean, sourdough, whole wheat bread, Horseradish, Onion, Bread crumbs; fresh, Black pepper, mustard, 
In a bowl, combine bread crumbs, mustard, black pepper, garlic powder,  basil and oregano. In a large bowl, combine eggs, ketchup, hot pepper  sauce, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard and horseradish. Mix in the  onion and ground beef, then add the bread crumb mixture. Place mixture in  9- by 5-inch loaf pan and bake in 35O degree oven for 1 hour.    To assemble, lay 1/2-inch slice of meatloaf on bottom slice of bread, and  top with cheese. If meat is cold, warm in microwave or conventional oven  with the cheese on bottom half of sandwich. On top half, spread mayonnaise,  mustard, a hint of horseradish, tomatoes, onions and lettuce. Close, cut in  half, and serve with a dill pickle. The meatloaf yields 9 or 10 servings.
