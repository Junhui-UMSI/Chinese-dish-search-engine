Purple Passion
Sugar, Can (6 oz) frozen grape, .leaves as a garnish, Purple grapes and mint-, (optional), COCKTAILS FROM CHEF FREDDYS, REALEMON lemon juice, ICE, Juice [thawed], Juice cans of cold water, Juice can Vodka, 
1)      In a pitcher, combine all of the ingredients,  except the grapes, ice and the mint leaves... Stir  until the sugar is dissolved, cover and chill... 2)  Serve over the ice and garnish if desired with the  grapes and mint leaves...
