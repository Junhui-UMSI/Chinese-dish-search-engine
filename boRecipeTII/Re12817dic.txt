Yummy Guacamole
garlic, Salt, avocadoes, Fresh lemon juice, 
1. Place the lemon or lime juice in a medium-sized shallow bowl.    2. Add the avocado. Cut the avocado and scoop out the inside, leaving the  skin. Mash the avocado to any consistancy you like.    3. Stir in remaining ingredients. Cover tightly and chill.
