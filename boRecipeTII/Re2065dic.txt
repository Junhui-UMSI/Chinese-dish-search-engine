Amish Friendship Apple Nut Bread
Amish Friendship starter, Eggs, Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Unbleached flour, Granulated sugar, Baking Powder, Ground cinnamon, Baking soda, Salt, Nuts, Apples, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease two 9-by-5-inch loaf pans.  In a  large bowl, beat together the starter, eggs, oil, and vanilla.  Add flour,  sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda and salt; blend. Fold in the  nuts and apples; transfer batter to prepared loaf pans. Bake 50 minutes and  remove from the oven.  Allow loaves to cool in pans 10 minutes, then tip  out onto a rack to finish cooling completely.
