Tri-Flavor Easter Eggs
Flaked coconut, Crunchy peanut butter, Butter or margarine, melted, Salt, Flavored compound counting, Confectioners sugar, Vanilla, Graham cracker crumbs, 
*NOTE* Can use either butterscotch or chocolate flavored compound counting.  MELTED. Mix crumbs, coconut and 1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar together in  bowl. Work in peanut butter, butter, vanilla and salt. Work in enough  remaining confectioners sugar by hand to shape centers easily. Shape into  small eggs; dip in melted compound coating.
