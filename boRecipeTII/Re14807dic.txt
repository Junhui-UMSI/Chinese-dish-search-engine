11 Minute Strawberry Jam
Sugar, Strawberries, 
Put the strawberries in a pan. Add 1 cup of sugar. Bring to a boil and boil  for 4 minutes. Add the second cup of sugar and boil again for 4 minutes.  Then add the third cup of sugar and boil for 3 minutes. Remove from stove,  cool, stir occasionally. Pour in jars and seal. busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 18, 1998
