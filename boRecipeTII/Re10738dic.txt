Quick Toffee Bars
Graham crackers; broken in, Butter or margarine, Pecans, (6 oz.) semi sweet chocolate, Sugar, 
Line a 15x10x1 inch jelly roll pan with waxed paper and grease the paper.  Arrange graham crackers in pan and set aside. In a saucepan, melt butter  and sugar over medium heat; let boil gently for 3 minutes. Spread evenly  over graham crackers. Sprinkle nuts on top. Bake at 325 for 10 minutes.  cool. Meanwhile, melt chocolate chips; spread over bars and allow to cool  again. After chocolate is set, pan can be turned over so waxed paper can be  peeled off. Bars can be frozen. Yield 4 dozen.
