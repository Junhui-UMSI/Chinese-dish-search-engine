Lemon Ice Cream
Flour, Sugar, Lemon extract, Juice from 8 lemons, whipping cream, Half & Half, lemon peel, 
Mix all ingredients together and fill container. Fill as needed with whole  milk.    Hint: grate lemon peel before squeezing them for juice.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Perry Greene <palgreen@HIWAAY.NET> on Dec 29,  1997
