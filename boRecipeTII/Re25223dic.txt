Tongdak Juk (Roasted Chicken)
Minced gingerroot, Sesame seed oil, Onion, Soy sauce, Pepper, Roasting chicken, Sugar, Garlic, Ground toasted sesame seed, 
Directions: Steam chicken for 1 hour. Combine all other ingredients to make  basting sauce. Pour some sauce over chicken. Skewer chicken and grill over  charcoal, turning and basting often, for 1 hour.  A rotisserie can also be  used, or roast on rack in preheated slow oven (325 degrees) for 3 1/2  hours, turning and basting frequently. Before serving, brush with sesame  seed oil to give chicken a good color and a special flavor.
