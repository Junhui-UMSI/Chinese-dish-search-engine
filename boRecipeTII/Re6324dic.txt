Planet Hollywoods Creole Mustard Sauce
Mayonnaise, Onion, Dijon mustard, Red wine vinegar, Water, Cayenne pepper, Salt, 1/4" pieces, Crushed garlic in oil, Horseradish, Yellow mustard, Green onions, sliced into, Worcestershire Sauce, Creole style mustard or, Cider vinegar, Finely chopped green peppers, Finely chopped celery, 
mix all ingredients together.    Serve with Planet Hollywood Chicken.    From the Miami Herald    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #108 by BobbieB1@aol.com on Apr 18, 1997
