Pureed Green Bean Soup
Sour cream, Salt and pepper to taste, Cheddar cheese, grated, Chicken broth, Beef bouillon cube, Onion, sliced, Green beans (#303) OR>>>, Frozen green beans, unthawed, Worcestershire to taste, 
Cook beans, onion and broth together, add bouillon cube. Puree in  blender while hot, season with salt, pepper and Worcestershire sauce.  Divide cheese in bottoms of 4 soup cups, add soup and garnish with sour  cream.
