5-Minute Broccoli Soup
broccoli, Salt, Nutmeg, Butter, white cheddar cheese, Onion, Flour, milk, Cayenne pepper, flour, 
Recipe by: Freshlike Products Preparation Time: 0:05 Cook broccoli  according to pkg directions, but omit salt and add onion. In a large  saucepan over medium high heat, melt butter, cook 1 minute. Add the 2 tbsp  flour, whisk 1 min. Slowly whisk in milk and salt until smooth and boiling.  Add broccoli and grated cheese, continue to whisk. Bring to a boil. Season  with cayenne and nutmeg if desired.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
