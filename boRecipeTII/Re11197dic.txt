Raisin Pear Streudel
Ground cinnamon, Salt, Raisins, Ground cloves, Phyllo dough, Sugar, Pear, Lemon juice, Margarine, 
In a bowl, toss pears, raisins, sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, cloves and  salt.    On waxed paper, arrange a sheet of phyllo dough, Brush lightly with melted  margarine, and cut into 3 long strips.    Preheat oven to 375.    Place about 1 tablespoon of mixture at the end of each strip, and begin to  roll up jelly roll style.    Place roll, seam side down, on an ungreased cookie sheet. Brush rolls with  melted margarine, and bake fro 25-30 minutes, or till golden.    Sprinkle with some powdered sugar, and serve warm.
