Barley Casserole #1
Pearl barley, Onions; chopped, Fresh parsley, Mushrooms; sliced, Pepper, Chicken broth, Butter, Salt, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    2. Melt butter in a large casserole. Saute onions 2 minutes over medium  heat.    3. Add mushrooms and saute 5-7 minutes more. Add barley. Cook until lightly  browned.    4. Add 2 cups of broth, salt and pepper, bring to a boil. Cover, reduce  heat, and bake 45 minutes.    5. Add remaining 1cup of broth. Return to oven and bake 30 minutes more.    6. Garnish with chopped parsley.
