Pistachio Whiskey Cake
Vanilla cake mix, Whiskey, 1 serving size, Vegetable oil, Water, Maraschino cherries, chopped, Chocolate chips, Instant pistachio pudding;, Walnuts, Eggs, 
Prepare cake mix as directed on package, usinf recommended amount of eggs  and oil but 1/2 c water and 3/4 c whiskey for the liquid. Blend in the  pudding mix, then fold in the cherries, walnuts and chocolate chips. Bake  in greased and floured Bundt pan for 50 to 60 minutes at 350 degrees. Let  cool before adding glaze.    Nutritional info per serving: 453 cal; 7g pro, 52g carb, 23g fat(45%)    Source: Joan F Mihalik, Wind Gap, Pa Miami Herald, 2/8/96 formatted by Lisa  Crawford, 4/22/96
