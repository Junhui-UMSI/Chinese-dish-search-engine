Quick Humgarian Goulash
Pepper, Uncooked rotini (spiral), Green bell pepper;, Sugar, Caraway seed, (8-oz) tomato sauce, Paprika, Boneless beef sirloin; cut, Water, Salt, Vegetable oil, (14-16 oz) diced peeled, Onion; coarsely chopped, 
Heat oil in 12-inch skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat until hot.  Add beef strips; cook and stir 3-5 minutes or until browned. Add all  remaining ingredients except rotini; mix well. Bring to a boil. Add rotini.  Reduce heat to medium; cook uncovered 10-15 minutes or until rotini is  tender, stirring occasionally. TIP: One 14-1/2-OZ or 16-OZ can whole  tomatoes, cut up, can be substituted for the diced tomatoes.
