Apple Crisp (With Corn Flakes)
Quaker Oats, Cinnamon, Corn Flakes, Apple Juice, or water, Flour, Stick Butter, softened, Apples, sliced and peeled, Nutmeg, Brown sugar, 
Preheat oven to 375 deg F.   Put half of the apples in a greased 9"x9" pan. Blend  together remaining ingredients, except juice, and  crumble half the flour mixture over the apples. Cover  with remaining apples and flour mixture. Pour juice  over top.   Bake 35 minutes.
