Tennessee Cream Gravy
Salt, Flour, Warm water, Pan drippings and browned bits, Evaporated milk, Black pepper, 
Heat fat moderately; stir in flour. Mixture should be thin, not cakey or  oily. Cook until medium brown, stirring constantly. Add pepper and salt.  Tehn, working very quickly, add warm water; stir thoroughly; let bubble.  Add evaportaed milk. Lower heat and cook to desired thickness, stirring  almost constantly. In necessary to thin at this point, add warm water. Best  to you, Lisa
