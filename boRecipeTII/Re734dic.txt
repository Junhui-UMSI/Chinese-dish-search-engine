Grilled Marinated Tofu
Box firm tofu, Soy sauce, Oyster sauce, Rice wine; optional, Onion powder, Green onion; chopped, White pepper; optional, 
Drain and halve tofu lengthwise.  Set aside.    Combine all remaining ingredients.  Place tofu in a 1"-deep platter and  drizzle marinade over tofu in marinade until well covered.  Fire up the  grill and grill for about 5-7 minutes on each side until browned.  You can also fry it in a non-stick pan or griddle.  Heat a teaspoon of oil  and place tofu in pan.  Cook on medium-high heat about 4 minutes each or  until browned.    Contributor:  Frances M. Say    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Frances.M.Say@bakernet.com on Aug 27, 1998
