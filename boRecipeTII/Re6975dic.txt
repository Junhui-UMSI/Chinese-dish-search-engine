Popcorn Balls
Salt, Sugar, Vinegar, Soda; pinch, Karo; white, Butter; size of walnut, 
Boil sugar, vinegar, syrup and butter with a little water until it  forms soft ball in cold water. Sift a little salt on freshly popper  corn. Add pinch of soda to syrup just before pouring over corn. Wet  handls with water before pressing each ball to prevent sticking.    Source: Mrs. Henry Bailey, Rock Springs Grange, Meigs County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
