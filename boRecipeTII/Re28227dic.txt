Baked Fish with Bacon Stuffing
bacon, Onion, butter, Seasonings, Lemon, Lemon juice, Dressed fish, Bacon drippings, Water, bread crumbs, 
Mix together all ingredients except fish, butter and lemon. Place  stuffing lightly in cavity of fish; do not skewer. Place in greased baking  pan; spread with butter. Bake in a 400 degree oven for 40 minutes or until  fish flakes easily. Baste occasionally. Serve with lemon wedges.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
