Polynesian Seafood Pasta Salad
Manderine oranges drained,, Soy sauce, (4.25 oz) shrimp (I used, Salt, Cloves garlic, crushed, Pepper, (10 oz) multi-shaped pasta, Chipotle pepper powder, Green Onions, Cheddar cheese, No fat mayonnaise, 
Directions:    1. Cook macaroni as directed on package, drain. Rinse iwth cold water,  drain.    2. Mix together remaining ingredients, add to macaroni.    3. Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours to blend flavors.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 03 Feb 97 by Wendy Harding <wharding@ODIN.WF.NET> on  Feb 6, 1997.
