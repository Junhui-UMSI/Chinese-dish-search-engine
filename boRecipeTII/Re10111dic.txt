Queens Royal Sandwich
Apple slices, Raisin bread or wheat bread, Margarine, Strawberry preserves, Cheese, Turkey slices, 
saute apples in margarine. Spread each slice of bread with preserves. Cover  with turkey, apples and cheese. Bake at 400? for 6 to 8 minutes.  Yield: 4  sandwiches.
