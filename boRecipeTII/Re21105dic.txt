Two Potato Gratin
Garlic, Butter, Onion; thinly sliced, Grated nutmeg, Fresh ground pepper; to, Chicken broth, Thyme, Blanched slivered almonds;, Grated Swiss cheese, Unpeeled baking potatoes;, Unpeeled yam; thinly sliced, 
Microwave butter in glass baking dish on high, 30 seconds, until melted.  Stir in onion and thyme; cover and microwave on high, 5 minutes. Rub a  6-cup capacity gratin dish thoroughly with garlic clove. Spread half of  each kind of potatoes in even layer on bottom of dish. Sprinkle with half  of nutmeg and a little pepper. Sprinkle with half of onion mixture, half of  cheese and half of almonds. Repeat layering with remaining ingredients  except chicken broth. Pour chicken broth over potato mixture. Cover and  microwave on high, 15 minutes. Remove from oven and allow to stand on  counter 10 minutes before serving.    Servings: 8    * To toast almonds, spread in an ungreased baking pan. Place in 350 degree  oven and bake 5 to 10 minutes or until almonds are brown; stir once or  twice to assure even browning. Note that almonds will continue to brown  slightly after removing from oven.
