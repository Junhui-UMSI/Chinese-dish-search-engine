Potato and Apple Salad
Grated onion, Salt, DRESSING, White wine vinegar, New potatoes, Black Pepper, olive oil, REST, Dry white wine, Heavy cream, McIntosh apples, peeled &, 
Mix dressing ingredients in blender or jar.    Cook pots 15-20 min in salted water, until tender. Drain, peel, slice 1/4"  thick, and toss with wine. Add dressing to apples and pots.
