Hamburger Hot Dish
Uncooked elbow macaroni, Ground beef, (28 oz.) whole tomatoes,, (15 oz.) tomato sauce, Jar (12 oz.) beef gravy, Onions, Garlic powder, 
Cook macaroni according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large  skillet, brown beef; drain.  Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, gravy, onion and  garlic powder. Drain macaroni; add to beef mixture and mix well. Transfer  to a greased shallow 3 qt. baking dish. Bake, uncovered at 350 F for 25-30  minutes or until heated through. Yield: 8 servings.
