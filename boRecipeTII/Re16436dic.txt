Welches White Grape Juice Punch
Ring ice mold made with, Ginger ale, chilled, 
When ready to serve the punch, empty White Grape Juice and ginger ale into  cold punch bowl. Carefully unmold ice ring and add to punch.    Yield: about 26 3-oz. servings.    Keep additional chilled White Grape Juice ready to add to punch. Delectable  for any occasion, light, pretty, refreshing and requiring the minimum of  preparation, is White Grape Juice Punch to be served alone or with lacy  cookies or slivers of cake, or with ice cream.
