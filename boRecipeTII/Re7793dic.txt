Portobello Pizza
Portobello mushrooms;, Baked cheese pizza crusts, Onion; sliced, Provolone cheese, olive oil, Bottled roasted red bell, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 5 min.    Heat oil in a heavy nonstick skillet over medium high heat. saute onion 3-4  minutes, until it begins to soften. Add mushroom slices and saute another  2-3 minutes, until mushroom softens. Stir in roasted peppers until hot.  Serve over pizza crusts, topped with cheese.
