Stuffed Cottage Cheese Sandwiches
Eggs, Pch nutmeg, Suggar, milk, Thin slices french bread, Cottage cheese, Vanilla, To 6 tb unsalted butter, 
Servings: 12  Make 12 mini-sandwiches using French bread and cottage cheese. Separate    eggs, beat yolks with sugar until thick.  Add vanilla, grated nutmeg, and  milk.  Beat whites to soft peak with a pinch of salt and fold into yolk    mixture.  Dip sandwiches into batter and brown on both sides in 4 to 6    tablespoons unsalted butter.
