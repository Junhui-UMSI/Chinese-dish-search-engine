Pumpkin Pecan Torta
Vanilla extract, Ground cinnamon, Ground cloves, Solid pack canned pumpkin, Ground pecans, Eggs,; separated, Sugar, Flour, Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Butter and flour a 9- inch spring form pan. In  a bowl combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and cloves. In another bowl,  beat egg whites until foamy, add in 3 tablespoons sugar; beat until stiff  peaks form. In a large bowl, beat egg yolks with the remaining sugar until  pale, about 1 minute. Add pumpkin and vanilla and beat until well blended.~  Stir in flour mixture. Fold in egg whites and pecans. Pour into the  prepared pan. Bake for 40 - 50 minutes, let cool 10 minutes, and remove  from pan. Serve each slice with whipped cream, mint, candied pecans, or  powdered sugar.
