Potatoes with Poppy Seeds
Dessicated coconut, Green chillies, chopped, Salt, Poppy seeds, ground, Potatoes, diced into 2cm pcs, Pinch of sugar, Vegetable oil, 
1. Heat the oil in a wok over a medium heat and fry the potatoes, stirring  occasionally until nearly done, about 15 minutes.    2. Add the poppy seeds, coconut, salt, sugar and green chillies and  continue cokking until the potatoes are tender. Serve with rice.    Compiled by Imran C.
