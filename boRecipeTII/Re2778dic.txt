Peanut Butter Chocolate Layer Bars
Peanutbutter sandwich cookie, Coconut, Butter, Peanuts, Sweetened condensed milk, Semi-sweet choc.morsels, 
combine crumbs and butter in small bowl. Press onto bottom of greased 13 x  9 inch baking pan. Layer peanuts, morsels, and coconut over crumb mixture.  drizzle sweeten condenced milk evenly over top. Bake in preheated 350  degrees oven for 20 to 25 min. or until coconut is golden brown. cool  completely on wire rack.    Recipe found in Nestl?s "Everday Desserts That are simply delicious"
