Tofu and Snow Peas
Strings, Snow pea pods (about 2, Soy sauce, Trim ends and remove, Ginger, Cups), Garlic powder, Into 1-inch cubes, Sesame oil, Cornstarch, Tofu, medium or firm -- cut, Sherry, Sugar, -- water, 
Place tofu cubes in a plastic bag and set bag in a small bowl. Combine soy  sauce, sherry, sugar, ginger, garlic powder, and water and pour into bag  with tofu. Marinate in the refrigerator 4 to 5 hours, turning bag over  occasionally so that al pieces of tofu absorb the marinade.    At cooking time:    Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Remove tofu  from bag, reservingmarinade. Place tofu in skillet. Cook , turning tofu so  that it browns lightly on all sides.    Pour marinade into small bowl and add cornsarch, stirring until dissolved.    When tofu is browned, add snow peas and marinade to skillet. Cook 1 minute,  stirring constantly.
