Peanut Butter Chicken Wings
Salt, Lemon juice, Black pepper, Peanut butter, Soy sauce, Chicken wings, Ginger, Durkees hot sauce, Curry powder, Garlic, 
Mix marinade ingredients in large bowl. Add wings, stir and marinade for  2 hours at room temperature. Drain and reserve marinade. Preheat oven  boiler. Place on broiling rack and broil 15-20 minutes or till browned.  Turn after 10 minutes and baste with marinate (or grill on BBQ).
