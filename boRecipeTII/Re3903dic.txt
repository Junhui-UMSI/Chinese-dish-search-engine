Pepper Relish #1
Red sweet peppers, Salt, Onions, Water, Sugar, Green sweet peppers, Vinegar, Hot pepper; or more,, 
Grind peppers and onions. Mix. Cover with boiling water. Let stand about 10  minutes. Mix salt, sugar, water and vinegar. Bring to a boil; pour over  peppers and onions. Boil 20 minutes. Pack in jars. Seal. Process 15 minutes  in boiling water bath.    NOTE:  Get a book on canning if unsure about sealing and processing.    MRS J.C.
