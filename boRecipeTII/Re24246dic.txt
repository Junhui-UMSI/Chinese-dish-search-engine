Tri - Bean Bake
Pinto beans -- cooked, Concentrate -- defrosted, Dry mustard, Tomato sauce, Onion -- thinly sliced, Garbanzo beans -- cooked, Celery -- thinly sliced, Apple juice, frozen, Lima beans -- cooked, Light molasses -- optional, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  In a 10 inch skillet, combine onion and  celery.  Cook in water over medium heat until vegetables are tender,  stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Spoon mixture into 1 quart casserole.  Cover and bake 25 to 30 minutes, or  until hot and bubbly.
