Swiss Vegetable Medley
Celery, Cauliflower), Swiss cheese -- shredded, Pepper, Cream of mushroom soup or, French fried onions, Frozen veg (broccoli, carrot, Sour cream, 
Thaw and drain vegetables.  Combine vegetables, soup, 1/2 cup cheese, sour  cream, pepper, and 1/2 can of onions.  Pour into a 1-quart casserole. Bake,  covered, in 350 F oven for 30 minutes.  Uncover; top with remaining cheese  and onions.  Bake 5 minutes more.
