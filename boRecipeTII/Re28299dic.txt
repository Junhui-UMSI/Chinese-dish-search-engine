Baked German Potato Salad
Black Pepper, Celery, Onion, Potatoes, Cider vinegar, All-purpose flour, Water, Sugar, bacon, Salt, 
Preparation time: 20 minutes. Cooking time: 50 minutes.    1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Cook bacon in a flameproof casserole until  crisp; remove to paper towels with a slotted spoon. Drain off all but      1/4    cup fat from casserole.    2. Stir in celery and onion; cook 1 minute. Sprinkle flour over and mix  well. Add water and vinegar; cook until mixture thickens. Add sugar, salt  and pepper. Add potatoes and bacon and mix lightly.    3. Cover casserole and bake for 30 minutes. Serve hot or at room  temperature.
