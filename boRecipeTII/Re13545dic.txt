Wicked Mushrooms
Fresh ground black pepper, Bay Leaves, Tomato paste, Burgundy wine, Thyme, Low sodium tamari, Onion -- chopped, Parsley flakes, -- water, Mushrooms -- quartered, Green pepper -- cut in, Chunks, 
Put 1/2 cup water in a large cooking pot. Add the chopped onion, bay leaves  and thyme.  Saute about 5 minutes.  Add chopped green pepper and mushrooms.  Continue cooking about 10 minutes.    Add the remaining water, tamari, wine, tomato paste, and parsley.  Add a  little fresh ground pepper, if desired.  Simmer slowly over low heat for at  least 60 minutes, until the liquid becomes a thick sauce.  Remove bay  leaves.  Serve very hot over brown rice or pasta.
