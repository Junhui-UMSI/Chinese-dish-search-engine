Persian Cucumber-Yogurt Salad
Yogurt, Onion, Ice cubes, Cucumber, Golden raisins, dried mint, Salt, Radishes, Cold water, Walnuts, black pepper, 
Combine all the ingredients. Adjust mint, salt and pepper to taste. If  possible, let sit overnight.    NOTES : From _The Jewish Holiday Kitchen_ by Joan Nathan.
