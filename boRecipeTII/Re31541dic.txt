Sweet-Sour Pork Chops
Green pepper, Peanut oil, Brown sugar, Pineapple juice, Onion, Cornstarch, Salad vinegar*, Soy sauce, Boneless pork chops, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 350?. Brown pork chops in oil. Place in baking dish.  Combine all other ingredients except onion and green pepper in a saucepan  and bring to boil. Cook for 2 minutes. Pour thickened sauce over pork chops  and top with onion and green pepper. Bake covered for 1 1/2-2 hours until  chops are tender. Serves 4.    *Apple cider vinegar can be used.
