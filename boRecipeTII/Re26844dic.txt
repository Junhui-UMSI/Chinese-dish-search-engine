Baked Bean Dip
ACCOMPANIMENTS, Cream cheese, Sour cream, Refried beans, Large Fritos, Taco spice, Cheddar cheese; shredded and, Onion, Tabasco suace, Tabasco sauce, 
Cobine all ingredients except 1/2 cup of the shredded cheddar cheese.    Spread into an 8x8x2 pan.  Sprinkle remaining cheese over the top. Bake at  350 for 30 minutes.  Serve with large Fritos.
