Zucchini Sandwich
Provolone cheese, cut to fit, Italian bread, toasted, Zucchini, sliced, Bacon, crisply fried and, Bacon bits, Shredded provolone cheese, Thin slice Bermuda onion, Oregano leaves, 
For each serving, top toast with cheese slice, zucchini and onion; sprinkle  with oregano, shredded cheese and bacon. Cook uncovered on ungreased cookie  sheet in 350 degree oven until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
