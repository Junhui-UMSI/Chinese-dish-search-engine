Artichoke Dip
Chopped green chilies, 14 oz. can artichokes, Monteray jack cheese;, Mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, 
Drain and chop artichokes. Add chilies, mayonnaise, and cheeses. Heat and  serve with crackers or chips.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 545 by SUNFIRE LJ  <SUNFIRELJ@aol.com> on Jan 17, 1998
