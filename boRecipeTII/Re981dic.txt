Italian Chicken Salad
Whole chicken, boiled, Onions, coarsely chopped, Wine vinegar, Oregano, Black olives, pitted, crush, Celery, coarsley chopped, Stuffed olives, sliced, olive oil, Sugar, Salt/pepper, 
Strain chicken. Reserve chicken stock for another use. Debone and remove  skin from chicken. Shred the meat. Saute onions, and celery in olive oil  for a short time. Add chicken, mix well with all other ingredients until  heated. Serve cold or hot.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  01, 1998
