Peanut Butter Frosting
Milk, Creamy peanut butter, Confectioner's sugar, Vanilla extract, Butter, 
In a large bowl with mixer at medium speed, beat peanut butter and butter  until light and fluffy. Gradually, beat in sugar, alternately, with milk  until frosting is a good spreading consistency. Beat in vanilla extract.  Makes enough frosting to frost one 13 x 9 x 2" cake. Submitted By SAM  WARING <WARING@IMA.INFOMAIL.COM> On 06 AUG 95 234147 ~0600
