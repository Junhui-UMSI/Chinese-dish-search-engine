WW Tomato-Shrimp Saute
Red pepper flakes, Garlic, Roasted red pepper,, Black Pepper, Salt, Zucchini, diced, Shrimp, shelled/deveined, Plus 1/2 tsp olive oil, Pitted black olives, sliced, Dried thyme, Plum tomatoes, sliced, 
In a large nonstick skillet, over Medium-high heat, warm 1 1/2 tsp. of the  oil until hot but not smoking. Add the zucchini and garlic, and saute until  golden, about 4 minutes. Add the roasted red pepper and the red pepper  flakes and cook for 1 minute. Add the remaining 2 tsps. oil, the shrimp,  thyme, pepper and salt. Cook the shrimp mixture, stirring occasionally,  until the shrimp turn pink and are barely cooked, 3-6 minutes. Gently stir  in tomatoes and olives and cook until the shrimp are opaque throughout and  the tomatoes are heated through, about 2 minutes. Per 12 shrimp plus  vegetables: 1 fat, 2 protein, 1 1/2 vegetables Per serving; 182 calories,  24 grams protein, 6 grams fat, 6 grams carbohydrate, 172 mg. cholesterol,  344 mg. sodium.
