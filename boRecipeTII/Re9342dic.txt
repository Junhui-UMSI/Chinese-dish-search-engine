Puffy Chili Rellenos Quiche
Stick pillsbury pie crust, Shredded monterey jack, Taco sauce; if desired, Eggs, Half and half, Pepper, Eggs separated, (4-oz) whole green chilies;, 
From: Ranger@Halcyon.com (Glenn/Sharon Gorman)    Date: 5 Aug 1994 16:47:11 -0400    Heat oven to 425 degrees. Prepare 1 pie crust stick as directed on package  for one crust 9 inch pie. Do not prick crust. Bake at 425 degrees for 5  minutes. If crust has developed bubbles, gently press down. Sprinkle 1 cup  cheese in partially baked crust. Layer with half the chilies (cut in large  pieces and laid flat); repeat cheese and chilies layer again. In medium  bowl, beat 4 eggs, cream and pepper; pour over cheese mixture. Reduce oven  temperature to 375 degrees and bake for 30 minutes. In small bowl, beat egg  whites until stiff. Fold in slightly beaten egg yolks just until blended.  Spoon egg mixture over hot filling, sealing to edge of crust. Return to  oven and bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown. Serve with taco sauce,  if desired. Serves 6    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /EGGS    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
