Ayam Bumbu Rojak (Chicken with Spicy Sauce)
Finely, Salt, Broiler or fryer, Roll shrimp paste, Candlenuts, grated, Chicken with skin 450g, Onion, finely chopped, Cloves garlic, chopped, Brown sugar, Red chilli, crushed, Vegetable oil, Sweet soy sauce, 
Cut chicken into serving portions    Fry onion and garlic in oil until golden brown. Add brown sugar, chilli,  candlenuts and shrimp paste and stir. Add chicken to this spicy mixture.    Then add tamarind juice, soy sauce, 1 cup water and salt. Cover tightly and  cook slowly until liquid is completely absorbed by chicken. Serve with  rice.    Compiled by Imran C.
