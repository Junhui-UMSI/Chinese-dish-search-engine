Strawberry Crunch Cake
Baking Powder, Fresh strawberries, Flour, whole-grain wheat, Sugar, Sugar, Flour, whole-grain wheat, Sliced, Flour, milk, Vanilla, Sugar, Whipped cream, Eggs, Lemon extract, Nutmeg, Flour, Salt, Butter, 
Combine the first 9 ingredients and mix well.  (it is quite thick) Spread  in a greased 10-inch springform pan. Sprinkle sliced strawberries and 1/2  cup sugar over top of batter. In another bowl, combine 1/3 cup sugar,  flours, butter, and nutmeg. Mix with a fork until crumbly and sprinkle over  strawberries.  Bake at 425^ 30-40 minutes. Serve warm with whipped cream.
