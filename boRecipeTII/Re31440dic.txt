Barbecue Mushrooms
Toothpicks, Bacon, Seasoned salt, Mushrooms, 
Wrap mushrooms in bacon. Skewer with toothpicks. Sprinkle with seasoned  salt Grill on barbecue pit until bacons cooked.    Serving Ideas : Melanie cooks these over charcoal--gas grill should be OK    Recipe by: Melanie Kaiser    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by BLAdams96 <BLAdams96@AOL.COM> on Apr 13, 1998
