Almond Torte Dessert
BATTER, Baking Powder, All purpose flour, Salt, Amaretto or; (1/2 tsp, Sliced almonds; (reserve), Egg, Baking soda, Butter, Water, Buttermilk or plain milk, Sugar, Vanilla, SYRUP, Sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350.    Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, egg, milk, vanilla and  butter. Spread in greased 9" spring form pan. Bake 35 mm. until lightly  browned.    Prick top of cake with fork. Top with almonds.    Combine sugar, water and Amaretto in saucepan. Boil one minute. Pour over  cake.    Place 6" under broiler until almonds are browned. Be Careful!! Will brown  quickly.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 495 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Jan 11, 1998
