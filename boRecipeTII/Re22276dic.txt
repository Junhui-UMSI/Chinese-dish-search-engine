Turkey Vegetable Burgers
mustard, Fat-free cereal, Water, Ground turkey breast meat, White pepper, Nutmeg, celery, Onion powder, Egg white, garlic, Onion, fresh parsley, 
Process crackers into fine crumbs. Preheat oven to 375. Saute onion and  garlic in 1 TAB water over med-high heat for 3-5 minutes until water  evaporats. Add celery and saute, stirring, 3 minutes to soften. If  vegetables begin to stick, add additional water as needed. In large bowl,  combine turkey meat, sauteed vegetables, onion powder, mustard, pepper, egg  white, nutmeg, crumbs and parsley. Mix well. Form turkey mixture into  patties and place on baking sheet or shallow pan. Bake at 375 for 20  minutes, or until firm and brown.
