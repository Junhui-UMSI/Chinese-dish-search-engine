Tiki Shake
Pineapple sherbet, Lime slices, Fresh lime juice, Ginger ale, 
Combine the first 3 ingredients in blender, and process until smooth.  Yield: 4 cups (serving size: 1 cup).    Per serving: 183 Calories; 2g Fat (9% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 42g  Carbohydrate; 7mg Cholesterol; 54mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Garnish with lime slices, if desired. Serve immediately.    Recipe by: Cooking Light, April 1995, page 111    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #395 by igor@digex.net on Jan 28, 1997.
