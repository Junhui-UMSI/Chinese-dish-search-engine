Beef and Cabbage Pie
Taco sauce, taco sauce, green chilies, cheddar cheese, Onion salt, potatoes, Drained, Pepper, cabbage, Ground beef, 
Combine potatoes, 1/2 cup cheese, 1/4 ts pepper and 1/8 ts. onion salt.  Press into bottom and up sides of a greased 10" pie plate. Bake at 350F. 20  minutes.    Brown beef; drain. Add cabbage, saute until crisp. Remove from heat and add  chilies, taco sauce and remaining pepper and onion salt. Spoon into crust;  bake another 20 mintues.
