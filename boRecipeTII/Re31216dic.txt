Banana-Fudge Walnut Brownies
Sugar, (6-oz) semi-sweet chocolate, Baking Powder, Mashed ripe banana, Egg, Vanilla, Salt, Walnuts, All-purpose flour, Butter, 
From: Aunt Salli <honn@icanect.net>    Date: Sat, 27 Apr 1996 02:16:16 -0400    Recipe By: Gold Medal    Heat oven to 350. Grease bottom ONLY of 8-inch square pan.  Melt in 2-quart  saucepan, over low heat, stirring constantly, the butter and chocolate  chips. Beat in thoroughly with spoon the flour, vanilla, sugar, baking  powder, walnuts, salt, banana and egg. Spread batter evenly in pan. Bake  about 30 minutes or until center is set. Cut into 2-inch squares.
