Wagon Wheel Bars
Cream cheese, TOPPING, Nuts; chopped, Butter, Flour, Powdered sugar, Crust, Brown sugar, Cool Whip, Heath candy bars; chopped, 
Mix crust ingredients and pat into 9 x 13-inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees for  15 minutes; cool. Mix topping together except for Heath bars. Pour over  cooled crust. Chop up Heath bars and sprinkle on top.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 315 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 27, 1997
