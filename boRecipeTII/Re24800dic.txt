Aunt Lillies Lake Shore Dinner
Cream of chicken soup, Green pepper, chopped, Oleo, Pimento (small can) drained, Cream of mushroom soup, Beef, cubed, Pork, cubed, Sharp Cheddar, grated, Noodles, cooked & drained, Water, 
Brown meat using oleo.  Remove meat from skillet and scrape skillet with  1/2 cup water, add water to other ingredient and mix. Add mixture to a  pyrex dish and bake at 400 degrees for 10 minutes. Reduce heat heat to 325  degrees and bake for an additional 1 hour. Top with crushed potato chips.
