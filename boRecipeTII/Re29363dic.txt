Baked Tuna Pie
Enriched Corn Meal, Frozen chopped broccoli, Evaporated milk, (Aunt Jemima or Quaker), Cold water, thawed, Vegetable shortening, Dillweed, drained, flaked, Salt, All-purpose flour, Tuna (6-1/2 or 7-oz. size), Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Heat oven to 350 F.  Combine flour, corn meal and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Cut in  shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Add water, 1 tablespoon  at a time, stirring lightly with fork until mixture forms a ball. On  lightly floured surface, roll out pastry to form 12-inch circle. Fit  loosely into 9-inch glass pie plate; trim.  Turn edges under; flute.    Place broccoli between layers of paper towels.  Pat gently to absorb excess  water.  Combine broccoli, remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt and remaining  ingredients; mix well.  Pour mixture into pastry shell. Bake 50 to 55  minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Let stand 10  minutes before serving.  Serve with lemon wedges, if desired.
