Appetizer Roll-Ups
Cream cheese, Carrot, Zucchini, Dill weed, cooked turkey, Pepper, fully cooked ham, 
1. In a bowl, combine the cream cheese, carrot, zucchini, dill and pepper.  Spread about 2 tablespoonfuls on each slice of ham and turkey. Roll up  tightly; wrap in plastic wrap. Refrigerate overnight. Slice into 1 1/2 inch  pieces. Yield: 3 to 4 dozen.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 15,  1998
