Vevas Fruit Salad
Small marshmallows, (small) cream cheese, Grated carrots, (small) crushed pineapple;, Hot water, 
So Im sending along some of favorite foods my Mom used to fix in the 40s  and 50s. Her name is Veva.    Dissolve together over hot water. Stir in a pkg. of orange jello. Cool.  Then stir in pineapple, carrots, 3/4 cup mayonnaise. Fold in 1 cup whipped  topping and pour into a mold or a dish and refrigerate till set.
