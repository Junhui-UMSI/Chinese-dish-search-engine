Baked Knockwurst with Sauerkraut
SAUERKRAUT #10, Water, KNOCKWURST FZ, 
PAN:  18 BY 24-INCH ROASTING PAN           TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  COMBINE SAUERKRAUT AND WATER.  HEAT TO SIMMER.  DRAIN OFF EXCESS  LIQUID.    2.  PLACE 1 1/2 GAL SAUERKRAUT IN EACH PAN.  ARRANGE KNOCKWURST ON TOP OF  SAUERKRAUT IN EACH PAN.    3.  BAKE 35 TO 40 MINUTES OR UNTIL WELL HEATED.    NOTE:  1.  SAUERKRAUT AND KNOCKWURST MAY BE LAYERED.  PLACE 3/4 GAL SAUER-  KRAUT IN EACH PAN, ARRANGE KNOCKWURST ON TOP; COVER WITH REMAINING SAUER-  KRAUT.    2.  OTHER SIZES AND TYPES OF PANS MAY BE USED.  SEE RECIPE NO. A-25.
