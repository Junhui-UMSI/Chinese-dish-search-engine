Asparagus and Pea Casserole
Salt, (15-oz) asparagus; drained, Cream of mushroom soup, (17-oz) small english peas;, Onion rings, Mild cheddar cheese; grated, Pepper, Hard boiled eggs; chopped, Jar (small) pimento, 
Layer 1 can asparagus in 9x9-inch casserole dish. Layer the can of peas,  then the other can of asparagus. Chop eggs over the top. Sprinkle with  cheese, pimento, salt, pepper & soup. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes. Remove  from oven & sprinkle onion rings on top & bake an additional 15 minutes.    MRS.
