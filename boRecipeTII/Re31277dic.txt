Bananas Foster #2
Brown sugar, -(up to), Cinnamon, Butter, Creme de banana (optional), Cloves, Rum, Bananas, Scoops vanilla ice cream, Brandy, 
From: "Edward J. Branley" <elendil@mintir.new-orleans.la.us>    Date: 21 Feb 1994 13:18:12 -0500    Split and slice bananas into small pieces. Melt butter over low heat, add  sugar, spices and creme de banana (if used) and bring to a boil. Heat  bananas in sauce and pour brandy and rum over mixture, then ignite. Serve  over vanilla ice cream after flames subside. Serves 6 (use 6 scoops ice  cream for one scoop person, 12 scoops for 2 per person).
