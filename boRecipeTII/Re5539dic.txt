Pineapple Mint Tulip
DOLE pineapple juice, Grenadine Syrup, Ice cubes or juice cubes, (sprig) fresh mint, FROM CHEF FREDDYS, Powdered sugar, 
1)      In a 1-qt measure, combine the juice, sugar,  and grenadine, stirring well... 2) rub the mint sprig  around the inside of a tall glass, partially fill with  ice or juice cubes and pour in the juice mixture...    from the GREAT AMERICAN Favorite Brand Name Cookbook  and Fred Goslin on CYBEREALM Bbs in Watertown NY on  (315) 785-8098
