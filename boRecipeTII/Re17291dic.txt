Wakeup Shakeup
Banana, Orange juice, Vanilla, Egg, 
Fill your glass or mug with as much orange juice as you wish. Pour this  into blender; add vanilla, egg and banana broken into small pieces. Whirl  until smooth and foamy.    Variations: Substitute apple or other juice for orange; Substitute milk for  juice; Add 1 tb strawberry jam; Add 1/2 ts instant coffee.
