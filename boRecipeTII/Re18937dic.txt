Angel Hair Pasta with Garlic
Box Angel Hair Pasta, Garlic, olive oil, Fresh parsley, 
Bring water to boil in a 2 quart pot.    Heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and cook just until soft.  Remove from heat, add parsley.    Toss pasta into boiling water, cook for 2 minutes. Drain well. Add olive  oil mixture to pasta and toss to coat.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 384 by Marie Tehas  <tehas@icarus.sfbr.org> on Dec 19, 1997
