Potato-Tomato Soup
Garlic, Salad oil, cheese, Salt, tomato sauce, canned, Onion, Potatoes, Water, 
Peel and dice potatoes, add water and salt, and cook until tender. Mash.  saute onion and garlic in salad oil; add to potatoes. Stir in hot sauce and  reheat just before serving, add cheese, blending into mixture as it melts.  Yield: 6 to 8 servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
