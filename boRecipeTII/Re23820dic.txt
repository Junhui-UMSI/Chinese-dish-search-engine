Asian Coleslaw
Pepper; cayenne, Parsley, Lemon juice, Balsamic vinegar, Red cabbage, Pepper; black, Cilantro, Maple syrup, Oil; olive, Rice vinegar, Onions, red, Salt, 
From:    LD Goss <ldgoss@METRONET.COM>    Date:    Mon, 15 Jul 1996 14:33:57 -0500  IN a large bowl, combine oil, lemon juice, vinegars, maple syrup, salt,  black pepper, and cayenne.  Toss with cabbage; let stand at room  temperature for 1 hour. Taste for seasoning and adjust if necessary, toss  in parsley, cilantro and red onion. Serve at room temperature. Makes 8  servings.
