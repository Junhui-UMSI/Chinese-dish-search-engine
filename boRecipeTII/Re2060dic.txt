Amaretto Coconut Pie
1996 AMERICAS BEST RECIPES, Butter; or margarine, softnd, Sugar, Eggs, milk, Amaretto, Self rising flour, Flaked coconut, 
Beat butter and sugar at med. speed of an electric mixer until light and  fluffy.  Add eggs; beat well. Add milk, amaretto, and flour, beating well.  Stir in coconut. Pour mixture into a lightly greased 9" pieplate. Bake at  350~ for 35 mins. or until set. Cool completely on a wire rack.    Source:  _Specialties of Indianapolis, Vol. 2_ by the Home Economists  Guild of Indianapolis, IN    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Nov 5, 1998
