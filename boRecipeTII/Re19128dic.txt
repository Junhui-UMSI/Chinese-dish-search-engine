Vegetable Hash
Peeled & diced, Cloves garlic, chopped, Zucchini, diced, Onion, diced, Peeled and diced, Water, Pepper, Vegetable oil, Large sweet potatoe, -, Large baking potatoes,-, Red pepper, diced, Chopped fresh chives, Salt, 
Heat oil in large non-stick skillet.  Add onions and garlic; cook a few  minutes until onions are softened. Add potatoes and sweet potato; cook  until lightly browned, about 10 minutes. Add water; cook until about half  the water evaporates, about 5 minutes.  Add zucchini, red pepper, yellow  pepper, salt and the pepper. Cook until vegetables wilt. Mash vegetables  lightly and sprinkle with chives.  Makes 6 to 8 servings. Source: The  Toronto Star. Oct. 9, 1996 ch.
