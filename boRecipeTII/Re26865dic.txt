Baked Beans #04
Green Pepper, Light corn syrup, (16-oz) pork & beans, Onions, Mustard, Catsup, Molasses, Black pepper, Salt, Bacon, 
Combine all ingredients except bacon. Pour into 2-quart baking dish; lay  bacon across the top. Bake at 425 for 15 minutes, reduce oven to 350 & bake  20-30 minutes longer.
