Toasty Pita Triangles
Finely chopped, Diameter), Chopped parsley or chives, Grated Parmesan, Cheese, Butter -- softened, Pita breads (7-inch, Fresh oregano or rosemary --, 
1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Blend butter, parsley, and herbs.    2. Spread butter mixture on pita breads. Sprinkle tops with cheese.    3. Cut each pita into 6 triangles. Place on baking sheets. Bake until  toasted, 10 to 15 minutes.
