Applesauce Bars with Pecan Praline Topping
Vanilla extract, Cinnamon, Golden delicious apples;, Salt, Unsalted butter, BATTER, Unsalted butter, Brown Sugar, Egg, Unsweetened applesauce;, Flour, Pecans, Brown sugar, Baking Powder, Baking soda, TOPPING, Pecans, Flour, 
OVEN: 350 Lightly butter a 13 x 9-inch baking pan.    BATTER: In a large bowl, beat together the butter and brown sugar with an  electric mixer til light and fluffy. Add the egg and vanilla; beat til  blended. Stir in the applesauce just til blended. (The mixture will look  curdled.)    Sift together the flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Stir in the dry ingredients into the butter mixture just til blended. Fold  in the apples and the pecans. Spread the batter in the prepared pan.    TOPPING: In a bowl, combine the brown sugar, butter and flour and blend  with a fork; stir in the pecans til blended. Crumble the mixture evenly  over the top of the batter.    Bake on the center rack for 30 to 35 minutes, or til the edges begin to  pull away from the sides of the pan. Cool on a wire rack before cutting  into bars.
