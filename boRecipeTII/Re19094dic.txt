Vegetable Curry
Black mustard seed, Tomatoes/ drained and, Salt to taste, 2  tsp ground cumin, Fresh ginger/minced, Cauliflower/ cut to florets, Cup frozen peas, Crushed, Large onion/ chopped, Cayenne, Water, Indian curry powder, Cloves garlic/minced, 
Cook onion over med high heat in a non stick pan. When translucent throw in  mustard seeds. When seeds pop throw in rest of spices. Cook a minute or  two.    Throw in cauliflower, tomatoes, and water. Reduce heat, cover, and cook  until cauliflower is tender. Throw in peas. Serve w/ basmati rice. (This  dish handles wide variations of spice combinations and amounts)    From: 6522@delphi.com.  Fatfree Digest [Volume 9 Issue 49] Aug. 9, 1994.
