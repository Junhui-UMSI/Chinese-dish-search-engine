Cranberry Apple Spread
Sugar, Water, (12oz) fresh cranberries, Apple, cored and finely, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, 
Boil sugar and water together 5 minutes. Add cranberries and boil gently  without stirriing for aobut 3 minutes. Add apple pieces. Continue boiling  until all cranberry skins pop open. Add cinnamon and nutmeg. Remove from  heat and cool in saucepan. transfer to a bowl. yield 3 cups or 12 to 16  servings.    The Best Bagels are made at home by Dona Z.
