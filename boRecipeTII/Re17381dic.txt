Walnut Corn Bread Twists
Egg white, (11 1/2 Oz) Refrigerated, Walnuts, 
Preheat oven to 375F. In pie plate, beat egg white. Place nuts on waxed  paper. Prepare twists as label directs. Roll twists in egg white, then in  nuts; place on greased cookie sheet. Bake 15 to 18 minutes until golden.    Recipe by: Good Housekeeping/March 1995    Posted to recipelu-digest by Rodeo46898 <Rodeo46898@aol.com> on Mar 2, 1998
