Zucchini-Pepper Pie
Whipping cream, Onions with tops; thinly, Grated Parmesan cheese, Clove garlic; minced, -or-, Garlic powder, (9 inch) pie shell, Vegetable oil, Bell pepper; chopped, Pepper, Tomato; peeled and, Salt, Zucchini (1 pound);, Basil, Eggs, 
Prick bottom of pie shell and bake at 450? for 5 to 8 minutes or until  lightly browned. Cool. Saute zucchini, onions and garlic in oil about 5  minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in tomato, bell pepper, salt, basil  and pepper. Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are  tender and liquid has evaporated (about 15 minutes). Spread vegetables  evenly in pie shell. Beat eggs and cream until well mixed. Pour over  vegetables. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake at 350? for 30 minutes.  Yield: 6 to 8 servings,    MRS. R.B.
