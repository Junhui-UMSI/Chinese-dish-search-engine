Yankee Fruit Salad
Salt, (2-oz) pimiento; chopped, Pecans, (3-oz) lemon gelatin, Celery, Cream; whipped, (3-oz) cream cheese, (20-oz) crushed pineapple, 
Bring pineapple to boil. Add gelatin and cool. Beat cheese until smooth.  Stir into pineapple. Add celery, pimiento and nuts. Fold in cream. Pour  into 2-quart mold. Chill 24 hours.
