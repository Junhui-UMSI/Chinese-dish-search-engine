Stuffed Peppers #2
Green peppers, Tomatoes; chopped, Onion, salt and pepper, Cut fresh corn, Ground beef, 
Remove tops & seeds from peppers. Parboil 5 minutes. Drain. Brown meat &  onion in skillet. Add tomatoes & corn. Salt & pepper to taste. Stuff  peppers & place in baking dish. Add small amount of water. Cover. Bake at      350    for 1 hour.    JUDY HOSEY    MARVELL, AR    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
