Yankee Pot Roast W Grainy Mustard
Onion, lean chuck roast, Leeks, salt and pepper, Fresh thyme, Carrot, Celery, Bay Leaves, Whole Grain Mustard, Potato, Beef stock, 
In a heavy cast iron pot, sear the roast on all sides over high heat. Then  add stock and herbs and simmer tightly covered for 30 minutes. Add  vegetables and simmer tightly covered another 30 minutes. Add salt and  pepper to taste and serve in a large pasta bowl with mustard on the side.    Yield: 8 servings Original Title: Yankee Pot Roast with Mustard Grain CHEF  DU JOUR JACK MCDAVID SHOW #DJ9180    Recipe by: Jack McDavid    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #816 by Holly Butman  <butma001@acpub.duke.edu> on Sep 28, 1997
