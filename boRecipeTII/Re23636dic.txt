Artichoke Casserole
Longhorn or Rat cheese;, (14-oz) artichoke hearts;, Eggs, 
Place artichokes on the bottom of a greased 8x8 pan. Top with cheese. Pour  beaten eggs over cheese. Bake at 350? for 45 minutes.  Yield: 6 to 8  servings.
