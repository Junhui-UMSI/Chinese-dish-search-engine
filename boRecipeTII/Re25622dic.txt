Avocado Vinaigrette (Gc)
Haas avocado, peeled, Jalapeno, seeded, olive oil, Sugar, Fresh lime juice, Salt, Finely chopped red onion, 
In a blender, puree the avocado, jalapeno, onion, lime juice and sugar  until smooth.    With the motor running, slowly add the oil until it emulsifies. May be  prepared up to 1 day ahead and refrigerated, covered, in a nonreactive  bowl. Bring to room temperature 1 hour before serving. Serve extra avocado  vinaigrette in a bowl in the center of the table.
