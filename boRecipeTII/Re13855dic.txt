Wilted Greens with Herb Glaze
Sprig tarragon, Chicken Stock, Peppercorns, Garlic, Pieces cheesecloth, Cold water, Spring thyme, Watercress; stems removed,, Pea shoots; washed, SACHET BAG, Bay leaf, Cornstarch, Chicken Stock, Spinach; washed, Chopped fresh shallots, Sprig rosemary, HERB GLAZE, Chopped fresh garlic, 
Make herb glaze:    Bring 1/2 gallon chicken stock, garlic and sachet bag to a boil. Simmer for  30 minutes. Dissolve cornstarch in cold water with a whisk to remove all  lumps. Slowly add dissolved cornstarch to boiling stock, whisking  constantly to avoid lumps. Set aside.    In a saucepan, sweat garlic and shallots in a small amount of chicken  stock. When the stock is simmering and the garlic and shallots are cooked,  add all the greens and stir until wilted.    Season with salt and pepper and toss with 3 teaspoons of the herb glaze.
