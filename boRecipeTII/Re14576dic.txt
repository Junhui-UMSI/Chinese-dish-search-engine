"Homemade" Potato Soup
Parsley flakes, Onions; chopped, Carrot; pared, sliced, Water, Chives, Chicken bouillon cubes, Leeks; washed, pared, cut, Pepper, Potatoes; pared, cut in, Evaporated milk (1 can), In, Stalk celery; sliced, Salt, Butter, 
Put all ingredients except milk and chives in crockpot.  Cook on low 10-12  hours or high 3-4.  Stir in milk in last hour.  Serve topped with chives.  Posted to EAT-L Digest 02 Sep 96    Date:    Tue, 3 Sep 1996 12:43:05 -0500    From:    LD Goss <ldgoss@METRONET.COM>
