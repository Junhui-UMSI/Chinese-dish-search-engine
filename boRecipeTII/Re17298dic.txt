Waldines Shrimp Dip
Shrimp; boil, peel, devein, Lemon juice, Garlic; mince, Cayenne, Onion powder; or, Onion; grate, Worcestershire, WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A, Mayo, Cream cheese; soft, Horseradish, 
Chop shrimp and mix with all ingredients. Refrigerate until time to serve.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #190    Date: 07/03/96    From: eboyd@shentel.net
