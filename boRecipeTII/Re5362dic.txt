Pineapple Bars Supreme
Salt, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Eggs, Lemon Extract, 8 oz Cans Crushed Pineapple, Flour, Baking soda, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven 350. Measure 2 tablespoons crushed pineapple for frosting and  set aside. Combine sugar, flour, soda, and salt in a mixing bowl. Add oil,  eggs, pineapple, vanilla and lemon extract. Mix thoroughly. Pour batter  into a well greased and floured 17x11 jelly roll pan. Bake 25-30 min or  until pick comes out clean. Remove from oven and immediately frost with  Coconutty Cream Cheese Frosting. Cool completely.    Coconutty Cream Cheese Frosting 1/4 c. margarine, 1- 3 oz pkg cream cheese  softened, 3 c. sifted powdered sugar, 1 tsp vanilla, 2 Tbls crushed  pineapple (reserved from cake) 1 c. flaked coconut, 1/2 c. chopped nuts.  Beat margarine and cream cheese together. Add powered sugar, vanilla and  pineapple. Beat until well blended and smooth. Stir in coconut and nuts.
