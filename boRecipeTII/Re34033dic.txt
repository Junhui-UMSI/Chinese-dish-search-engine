Basil-Mint-Orange Pesto
Walnuts, Packed fresh basil leaves, Garlic, Olive oil (or more), Packed fresh mint leaves, Orange juice concentrate; -, 
From: kmeade@ids2.idsonline.com (The Meades)    Date: Mon, 1 Jul 1996 16:55:21 -0400  In processor, blend first 5 ingredients until almost smooth.  With machine  running, gradually add 1/4 cup olive oil; process until smooth. If pesto is  dry, mix in more oil by spoonfuls.  Season with salt and pepper. (Can be  made ahead. Press plastic wrap onto surface of pesto. Cover and refrigerate  up to 2 days or freeze up to 1 week. Bring to room temperature before  using.) Makes about 3/4 cup.
