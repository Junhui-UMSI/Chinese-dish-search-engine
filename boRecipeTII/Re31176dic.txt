Banana Walnut Snack Cake
Veg oil, Egg, Buttermilk; milk or water, Walnuts, Mashed ripe banana, SNACK CAKE MIX; (see recipe), 
Preheat oven to 350F. Pour SNACK CAKE MIX into ungreated 8 or 9 inch square  baking pan.    In a small bowl, combine remaining ingredients, beating with a fork to  blend. Stir into SNACK CAKE MIX until blended. Bake 35 to 45 minutes in  preheated oven until a wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
