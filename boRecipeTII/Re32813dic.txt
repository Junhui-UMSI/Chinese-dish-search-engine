Sweet Potato Souffle #2
Eggs, Vanilla extract, Flour, Sticks margarine, Lemon extract, (small) yams, (small) pet milk, Sugar, 
Cook yams for 15 minutes on boiling temperature in saucepan, cook down  liquid to half.     Pour mixture into round casserole and decorate if desired. Bake at 375  degrees approximately 45 minutes to one hour.
