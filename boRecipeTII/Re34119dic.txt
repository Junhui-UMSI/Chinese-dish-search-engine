Batter for Fried Feggies
milk, Egg yolk; beaten, Flour, Salt and pepper, Baking Powder, Butter, 
Mix egg, milk and butter. Add flour and baking powder. Beat until smooth.  Add salt and pepper.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
