Veprove S Krenem (Piquant Pork with Horseradi
Celery root (knob celery),, Salt, Caraway seeds, OR 1 celery stalk, Pepper, Water, Carrot, Prepared horseradish, Vinegar, Butter or margarine, Lean pork, cut into 1, Onion, stuck with 3 cloves, 
Brown pork in hot butter on all sides in deep kettle. Add water and all  other ingredients except horseradish. Simmer, covered, for 1 1/2 hours, or  until tender. Transfer meat to hot platter; keep hot. Force stock through  sieve or puree in electric blender. Pour stock over meat. Top with  horseradish. Serve with boiled potatoes.    Makes 4 to 6 servings.    From: Steve Herrick Source: [Womans Day Encyclopedia of Cookery - Vol. 4]
