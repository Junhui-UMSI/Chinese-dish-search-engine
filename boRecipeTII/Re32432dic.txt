Basic Abm White Bread
olive oil, Dry yeast, Flour, Salt, Sugar, Tepid water, 
There is a range of 2 1/2 -3 cups of flour. use the smaller amount in the  winter because flour is like a sponge; it absorbs moisture from the air.  Place the 4 ingredients in the pan of your machine in the order indicated.  Place pan in machine. Press start.
