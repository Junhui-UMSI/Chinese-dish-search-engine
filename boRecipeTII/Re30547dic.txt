Tads Barbecue Potatoes
Sugar, Bacon drippings, Ketchup, salt and pepper, Texas Pete hot sauce, Yellow onions, Potatoes, 
Peel potatoes and onions and cut into large chunks. Place in large pot and  cover with water. Add remaining ingredients, stir to blend, and bring to a  boil. Reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are soft. Let potatoes simmer  over very low heat until ready to serve, stirring occassionally.
