Barbecued Pork Butt
Garlic salt, Chili powder, Ground cloves, Sugar, Allspice, Seasoned salt, Black pepper, Paprika, Ground ginger, Pork butt, Celery salt, Onion salt, 
Combine all of the dry ingredients and blend well. Prepare the pork butt as  desired. Cook with or without the bone. Trim the cap fat. Season all over  with the seasoning mixture. Rub in if desired. Cook on a covered, medium  hot grill with a water pan for 7 to 10 hours.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 655 by QueenBerta@aol.com on Jan  31, 1998
