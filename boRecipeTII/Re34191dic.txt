Bay Scallop Saute
Red bell pepper, diced, Rich, salt-free fish stock, Toasted pine nute, Yellow bell pepper, diced, Dry white wine, Cornstarch, Green bell pepper, diced, Garlic clove, chopped fine, olive oil, Bay scallops, Finely shredded fresh basil, Cooked pasta-thin strands, Black Pepper, 
In a large skillet, heat the oil with the garlic over moderate-to-high  heat. When the garlic sizzles, add the scallops and saute just until they  firm up slightly, about 30 seconds. then add the peppers and saute about 1  minute more.    Stir in the fish stock. In a small cup or bowl, stir the cornstarch into  the wine until it dissolves, then stir that mixture into the ingredients in  the skillet. Simmer until the sauce begins to thicken slightly, about 1  minute. Then stir in the basil and pine nuts and spoon over cooked pasta.  Season to taste with black pepper.
