Baked Apples with Creamy Topping
Maple syrup, Buttermilk, Sour cream, Water, Sugar, Stem end, Cinnamon, Peeled half way down from, Baking apples -- cored and, Walnuts, 
Place apples in greased shallow baking dish. Mix together nuts,sugar and  cinnamon and fill centers of apples. Drizzel syrup over apples, add water  to dish.  Bake at 425 for 25-30 minutes. Cool on a rack, basting  occasionally with pan juices.  Cover and chill. Beat together sour cream  and butter milk until well blended.  Spoon over baked apples.
