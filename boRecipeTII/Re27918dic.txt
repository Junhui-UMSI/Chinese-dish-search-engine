Thor Cakes
Demerara su, Baking Powder, Ground ginger, Fine oatmeal, Flour, Candied peel, Butter, Salt, Coriander seeds, Warmed black treacle, 
Set oven to 350/F or Mark 4. Mix all the dry ingredients together in  a bowl, rub in the butter and add the treacle. Knead together and  roll out fairly thinly on a floured sur face. Cut into large rounds,  place on a greased baking tray and bake until golden; about 20  minutes. The actual cooking time depends on the thickness and the  size of the cakes.
