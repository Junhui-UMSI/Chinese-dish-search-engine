Swiss Steak with Vegetables
Pepper, Chopped, Flour, Paprika, Tomatoes, Salt and pepper, Round steak, Nutmeg, Onion -- finely chopped, Tomato sauce, Mixed vegetables -- finely, Salt, Beef broth, Carrots,celery,green pepper, Garlic salt, 
Preheat to 300. Cut meat into 6 serving pieces, season with garlic salt,  let set in. Mix flour and rest of seasonings. Heat some oil in large  skillet. With edge of plate, pound as much flour into meat as possible,  brown on both sides. When last batch is turned to 2nd side and onion and  mixed veggies.Do  not brown veggies. Add all to a 13x9 baking dish. Season  with salt and pepper. Pour broth and sauce atop. Cover and bake for 2-2 1/2  hours.
