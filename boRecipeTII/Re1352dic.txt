Easy Fritatta
Vegetable oil, Potatoes, Eggs, Salsa, cheese, S & P, 
Preheat oven to 425. Wash potatoes & cut small cubes.Leave skin on. Saute  in pan a few minutes then cover for 10 minutes. Combine the rest of the  ingredients & pour over potatoes in pan. Cover and cook for 3 minutes.  Remove cover & cook in oven for 4-6 minutes until set.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "leah perez" <perezleah@hotmail.com> on Aug  12, 1998,
