Vegetarian Chili Texas Style
Boiling water, Salad oil, garlic, Granule burger, Onions, Water, Oregano leaves, tomato paste, Chili powder, Cumin powder, Sugar, green pepper, cooked kidney beans, Salt, whole tomatoes, 
Good slow pot recipe. Longer cooking enhances the flavor.     Soak granule burger in boiling water for 10 minutes or more.     Place oil in heavy saucepan. Combine onions, green pepper,     garlic and saute inoil. Add the granule burger to the sauteed     vegetables, cook for 5 minutes. Add the rest of the     ingredients. Simmer at least 1 hour.
