Bangkok Chicken
Onion, Flour, Beer, Chicken breasts, boned, Paprika, Salt, Tabasco sauce, Ham, cooked, Parsley, dried, Garlic, olive oil, Shrimp, 
Mix flour and salt; dredge chicken breasts.  In large skillet, brown  chicken in olive oil and remove.    Combine onion, ham and garlic and saute in same skillet over low heat until  onion is soft.  (Dont let garlic stick to skillet.) Remove. Drain all but  1 T of the oil and pour in flour left from dredging chicken; stir a lot to  make sure flour is well-blended. Return ham mixture.    Clean and devein shrimp.  To ham, add beer, shrimp, parsley, paprika and  tabasco; mix well.  Add chicken; cover and simmer for 20 minutes. Serve  with rice or garlic bread.
