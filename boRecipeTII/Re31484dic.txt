Barbecue Sauce (Microwave)
Unsalted butter, Water, Fresh lime juice, Chili powder, Onion, Salt to taste, Slice Lime Cut In Half, Ground cumin, Cider vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Catsup, Tabasco sauce, Garlic, Chopped Fresh Coriander, Diced Red Pepper, Celery, 
Microwave butter, onion and garlic in a medium microwave safe bowl, covered  with plastic wrap, on high power for 2 minutes.  Stir in celery and  microwave covered for 2 minutes.  Stir in remaining ingredients except  coriander until blended.  Microwave covered for 7 minutes, stirring halfway  through cooking.  Puree sauce in a blender bowl or food processor. Transfer  to bowl and stir in all but 1 tb coriander.  Garnish with remaining  coriander.  Serve warm or at room temperature.  Makes 2-1/2 cups. 81  calories per 1/4 cup.  From: Syds Cookbook.
