White Fudge
Nuts, Vanilla, Water, Sugar, Sugar, Corn syrup, white, Egg whites, 
Cook 1 c. sugar and water to soft ball stage. Fold into stiffly beaten  egg whites. In another kettle, cook at same  time sugar, syrup and a  little water to hard ball stage. Pour into first mixture. Add vanilla  and beat until it begins to harden. Add nuts if desired and pour on  buttered plate. Cut into squares.    Source: Mrs. Susie Woolard, Jacksontown Grange, Licking County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
