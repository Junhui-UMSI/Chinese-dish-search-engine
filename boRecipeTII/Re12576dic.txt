Yogurt and Cucumber Soup with Mint And Dill
fresh mint, Fresh Lemon Juice, Lowfat (1%) milk, cucumber, hot pepper sauce, Cucumber, , Fresh dill weed, Black Pepper, salt, garlic, Lowfat yogurt, olive oil, 
1. Put the yogurt into a cheesecloth-lined strainer and let drip over a  bowl 2 hours at room temperature, or overnight in refrigerator. Discard the  water and place yogurt in a bowl.    2. Whisk the milk into the drained yogurt until smooth. Stir in the  cucumber, garlic, olive oil, 2 tablespoons dill, mint, lemon juice, salt,  pepper and hot pepper sauce. Refrigerate 1 to 2 hours, until ice cold.    3. Garnish each serving with a little of the remaining teaspoon dill and a  slice of cucumber.
