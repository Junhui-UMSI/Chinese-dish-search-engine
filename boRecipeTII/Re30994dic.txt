Banana Nut Cake #1
Powdered sugar, Flour, Brown sugar, butter, Pecans, milk, Eggs, Buttermilk, Baking soda, Shortening, Sugar, bananas, ICING, 
Mix together shortening, sugar & eggs. Add buttermilk, soda, mashed  bananas, flour & nuts. Mix well & bake slowly at 350 until done. Makes 3  layers. When cool, top with icing. ICING: Boil brown sugar & butter until  it melts. Add milk. Cook 1 minute. Cool. Add enough powdered sugar to make  desired spreading consistency.    MRS.
