Cashew, Ham, and Pea Salad
Lemon ; Juice of, Few drops of hot pepper, (16 oz) frozen peas; thawed, Diced cooked ham; (up to, Green Onions, Roasted unsalted cashews, Mixed salad greens, Mayonnaise, Sour cream,
