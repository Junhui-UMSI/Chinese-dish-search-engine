Tomato-Corn Chowder
Tomato puree, salt and pepper, Ribs celery; chopped, milk, Potatoes; peeled & chopped, Fresh basil leaves; chopped, Corn; cooked, 
Combine milk, tomato puree, basil and celery in soup kettle. Simmer,  uncovered, 10 minutes. Do not boil. Remove from heat and strain. Return  liquid to kettle; discard solids. Cook potatoes in separate saucepan in  boiling water 15 minutes or until tender. Drain. Add potatoes and corn to  liquid in kettle, season with salt and pepper, and heat. Do not boil.
