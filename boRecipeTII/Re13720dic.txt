Wild Rice Dressing
Brown Rice, Waterchestnuts -- chopped, Granules, instant, Wild Rice -- uncooked, Dried thyme, Fresh Mushrooms -- fresh, Onion -- chopped, Sliced, Pepper, Celery, Vegetarian,, "Chicken" Bouillon --, Water, 
Rinse wild rice in a strainer under cold running water for 1 min.  In med  saucepan combine wild rice, water, onion, brown rice, bouillon granules,  thyme and pepper.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat.  Simmer, covered for 45  min.    Stir mushrooms, celery and waterchestnuts into rice mixture.  Return to  boiling; reduce heat.  Simmer, covered for 10 - 20 min more, or until  celery is just tender, stirring occasionally.    Serves 8 John Wilson, Lansing Mich.
