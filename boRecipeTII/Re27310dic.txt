To Grill Garlic
Butter or margarine, Sprigs fresh rosemary; or, Whole garlic bulbs; or heads, Oregano, 
(Prepare outdoor grill for cooking.)     Peel outer papery skin from garlic, leaving cloves and head intact. On  double thickness of heavy-duty aluminum foil, place whole garlic heads; cut  butter into bits and distribute it and the herbs on top of the garlic. Fold  up foil and seal tightly.  Place foil over hot coals and cook 40 to 45  minutes, or until garlic is softened, turning occasionally.    To serve, squeeze cooked garlic cloves from skin onto cooked meat and  vegetables or spread on bread. Or store as ingredient.
