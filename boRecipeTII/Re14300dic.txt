White-Cooked Pork with Lotus Root
Salt, Pork, Cornstarch, Pepper, Water to cover, Lotus root, Red bean cheese (see recipe), Water, 
1. Cut pork in 1-1/2 inch cubes. Peel lotus root and split open  slightly. Mash red bean cheese. Place in a heavy saucepan.       2. Add water, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, then simmer, covered, 45  minutes. Remove lotus root; slice thin and set aside.       3. Blend cornstarch and remaining cold water to a paste. Add to liquids  in pan and cook, stirring, to thicken. Return lotus root only to reheat.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
