Pineapple-Glazed Carrots
pineapple  , Salt, orange rind, Ground cinnamon, Butter or margarine, Carrots, Brown sugar, 
Cut carrots as you please. Cover and cook until tender in small amount of  water and salt. Drain. Add remaining ingredients. Place over medium heat;  toss lightly until glazed,a bout 5 minutes. Serves 5-6.    Recipe by: Home Cooking--July 1996 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #731 by  L979@aol.com on Aug 9, 1997
