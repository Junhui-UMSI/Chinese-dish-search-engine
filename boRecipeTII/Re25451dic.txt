Tortilla Soup W/ Roasted Red Peppers
Red bell peppers; roasted,, Scallions; cut in 1" lengths, Cayenne pepper; to taste;, CornCilantro Stock, Poblano peppers; seeded and, Tomatoes; peeled and seeded,, Rest of the Soup, Anaheim chili pepper; seeded, Fresh lime juice, Minced canned chipotle, Tortilla strips; see recipe,, Frozen corn or 3 ears of, Garlic, Handfuls fresh cilantro, Salt, Salt, Olive oil or other sauteing, Water, Onion; minced, about 1 1/2, 
Place the stock ingredients in a large saucepan and bring to a boil.  Partially cover, lower heat to simmer, and cook for about 30 minutes. Keep  it covered, so too much liquid doesnt evaporate.    Heat the oil, and add the onion, chiles, garlic, salt, and saute until the  vegetables are very tender.    Puree the roasted bell peppers and the tomatoes in a blender or food  processor with 2 cups of the stock. Puree until smooth, then add this to  the onion mixture. Strain in the rest of the stock, pressing every last bit  of the liquid from the cooked vegetables.    Add lime juice; taste to correct salt and add the cayenne or a small amount  of the chipotles.
