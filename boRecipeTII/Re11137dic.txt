Raisin Bulgar Pilif
Ground cumin;, Salt;, Boiling water, Bulgur;, Golden or dark raisins;, 
Place bulgur in a medium-size bowl and cover with boiling water.  Add  remaining ingredients and stir to combine.  Let stand 30 minutes until  water is absorbed. Fluff and serve warm. This was a serving for 4, but  change it becauce I wanted to frezze some, but instead the  grandchildren came and stayed for supper.
