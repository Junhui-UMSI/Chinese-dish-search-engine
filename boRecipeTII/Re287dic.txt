Lazy Daisy Cake
Vanilla, FROSTING, Butter, Brown sugar, Cream, Coconut, Eggs; well beaten, Sugar, Flour, Salt, Baking Powder, Hot Milk, Butter, 
Beat eggs, add sugar and keep beating. Sift together flour, salt and baking  powder. Add slowly to egg mixture and keep beating. Melt 1 tablespoon  butter in hot milk and add all at once to above mixture. Beat well. Add  vanilla. Pour into greased cake pan and bake 25 to 30 minutes at 350.  Frosting: Mix ingredients together and spread on cooked cake, brown under  broiler.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 350 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Dec 08, 1997
