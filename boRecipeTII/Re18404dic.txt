Vevas Cold Water Chocolate Cake
Cold water, PART D, Boiling water, Cake flour, Soda to, PART C, Shortening, Sugar, Cocoa, PART B, Egg whites; stiffly beaten, PART A, Salt, 
So Im sending along some of favorite foods my Mom used to fix in the 40s  and 50s. Her name is Veva.    Sift part "A" into a mixing bowl. Add part "B". Beat with mixer at high  speed for 2 ins. Add part "C". Beat 1 minute more. Fold in 3 stiffly beaten  egg whites. Bake in 9x13 inch cake pan until done in center. No time given.
