Heavenly Mashed Potatoes
Scallion, finely chopped, salt and pepper, Ore-Ida froz mashed potatoes, milk, Sour cream (light or reg), Light cream cheese, Real bacon bits, 
Prepare potatoes following package directions but using only one cup of  milk.  Add sour cream and cream cheese to warm potatoes. Stir untill creamy  and blended. Add bacon bits and scallion. Add salt and pepper to taste.    Source: OreIda ad    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by jbowde19@idt.net on Nov 06, 1998
