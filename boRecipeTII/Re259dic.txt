Lasagne
Ground Beef; 1" thick, Garlic, Salt, (Large) Tomato Puree, (6 Oz.) Tomato Paste, Cottage Cheese, Romano Cheese, Parsley Flakes, Eggs, Salt, Pepper, Mozzarella Cheese; sliced, Lasagna Noodles, 
Brown meat, spoon off fat and add next 5 ingredients. Cook noodles with oil  to prevent sticking. Place 1/2 noodles in dish. Mix with next 6 ingredients  and spread 1/2 over noodles, top with meat sauce then cheese. Repeat with  remaining ingredients.    Source: Recipes Out of This World Women of St. Agnes Catholic Church  Charleston, WV 1969    billspa@icanect.net    Recipe by: Judy Boggess    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Bill Spalding <billspa@icanect.net> on Mar  04, 1998
