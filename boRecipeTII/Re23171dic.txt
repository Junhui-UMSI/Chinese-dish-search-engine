Tuna Bruschetta
(1/2 to 3/4 inch thick each), (6 oz.) starkist solid white, Minced fresh parsley, Garlic, Chopped plum tomatoes, Shredded mozzarella cheese, Bibb lettuce, Coarsely ground black pepper, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Salt; (optional), 
1. In a blender or a food processor bowl with metal blade, blend garlic and  oil.    2. Brush oil mixture on one side of each piece of toast; reserve remaining  oil mixture.    3. In medium bowl, lightly combine tomatoes, cheese, tuna, parsley and  reserved oil. Add salt, if desired, and pepper.    4. Mound about 1/2 cup tuna mixture over each piece of toast.    5. Place 2 pieces of toast on each lettuce-lined salad plate.    Recipe by: StarKist?    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Barbra<barbra@pipeline.com> on Feb 13, 1998
