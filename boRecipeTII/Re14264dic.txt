White Texas Sheet Cake
Eggs, Water, Baking soda, Frosting:, milk, Confectioner's sugar, Almond extract, Sugar, Margarine, Flour, Salt, Almond extract, Sour cream, Margarine, Walnuts, 
Melt margarine & water in saucepan.  Remove from heat & stir in flour,  sugar, eggs, sour cream, extract, salt, & baking soda. Beat until smooth.  Pour into greased 15x10" pan & bake at 375 degrees 20-22 min. Cool.    Frosting:    Heat margarine & milk, then mix in remaining ingredients.    ~-
