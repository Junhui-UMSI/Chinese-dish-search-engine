Barbecued Meat Loaf
Ground beef, Kitchen Bouquet, Pork, Brown sugar, Salt, Ketchup, Dill Pickle; Chopped, Onions, Dry bread crumbs, Pepper, milk, Eggs, 
Mix all ingredients together and put in loaf pan. Bake 1 1/2 hours at 350  degrees.
