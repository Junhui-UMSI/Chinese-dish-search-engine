Peanut Butter Oatmeal Snacks
Oats, Nonfat dry milk, Honey, Crunchy peanut butter, milk, Raisins or currents, Mixed seeds or nuts, Coconut, 
Toast oats for 15 to 30 minutes in a 350 degree over. Combine milk, peanut  butter, honey and bring to a boil and remove from heat. Add nonfat dry  milk, raisins, seeds and nuts, and coconut. Press into and 8 inch square  pan and chill for at least 1 hour. Keep refrigerated. Makes 16 bars.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Ken Vaughan <kvaughan@ptialaska.net> on Feb  15, 1998
