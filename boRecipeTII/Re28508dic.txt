Tex Mex Blt
Mayonnaise mixed with, Thick slices whole wheat, Thin slices avocado, Thin slices ripe tomato, Chili powder and chopped, Washed lettuce leaves, Sprigs cilantro; roughly, Crisply fried turkey bacon, 
Mix the mayonnaise with pinch of chili powder and chopped fresh jalapeno  pepper and set aside.    Toast the bread lightly. Spread each side with mayonnaise. Pile on bacon,  tomato, avocado, cilantro sprigs and lettuce and top with other slice of  toast.
