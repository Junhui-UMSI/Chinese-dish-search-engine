Portobellos with Port
Vegetable oil, Portobello Caps; 6-Ounces, Coarsely Chopped Walnuts, Bleu Cheese; Crumbled, (1, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    Wipe the caps clean and brush them with a little oil. Place in the oven on  a cookie sheet and heat for 3 minutes. Remove from the oven and evenly fill  the caps with the walnuts and then the cheese. Return to the oven until the  cheese is melted, about 2 minutes.    NOTES : Serve as an appetizer with knives and forks, or cut into quarters  and pass as an hors doeuvre to be picked up by hand.
