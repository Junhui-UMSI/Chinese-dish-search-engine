Quick Crockpot Chicken
(16-oz) Hunts Tomato Ready, Cornstarch, Boneless; skinless chicken, Boiling water, Green pepper sliced, Onion sliced, Chicken boullion cube, Garlic powder to taste, 
From:    Jo Kinnaly <jkin@LOC.GOV>    Date:    Wed, 7 Aug 1996 19:34:56 -0400  Sprinkle chicken breasts with garlic powder and brown in skillet coated  with Pam. Place in bottom of crockpot. Top with sliced peppers and onions.    Disolve boullion cube in water.  Add cornstarch and mix thoroughly.  Add  Hunts Tomato Ready to boullion mixture and pour over chicken in crockpot.    Cook on low 4-5 hours.  Serve over rice.    EAT-L Digest  6 August 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
