Whiskey Sour Sirloin
(1pc) Round Bone Sirloin, Orange juice, Salt, Vegetable oil, Peppercorns, Med Orange, Thinly Sliced, Whiskey, Lemon juice, Small Onion, Thinly Sliced, 
When getting the steak look for a good piece  that is about 2-inches thick.  Place steak in shallow baking dish.  In small saucepan combine remaining  ingredients except orange.  Over low heat, simmer 5 minutes; pour over  meat.  Top with orange slices; cover.  Refrigerate 4 to 6 hours, turning  occasionally.  Remove meat from marinade; grill or broil as desired.  Heat  remaining marinade and serve with steak.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/meats.zip
