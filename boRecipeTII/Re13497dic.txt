Whole-Wheat Cookies
Brown sugar, Whole wheat flour, Baking soda, Nut meats, Salt; a pinch, Shortening; heaping, Sour cream, Honey; strained, Flour, Baking Powder, Raisins, Eggs, 
Bake in quite a hot oven. Makes 4 dozen drop cookies.    Note: Cream sugar, shortening, add remaining ingredients. Hot oven is 400 -      450    F.    Source: Bridgewater Grange, Williams County, OH
