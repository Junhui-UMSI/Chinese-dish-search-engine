Amazing Coconut Pie
Butter, Bisquick, Eggs, Coconut, milk, Sugar, Vanilla, 
Combine everything but coconut in blender on low for 3 minutes. Pour into  pie plate, let sit for 5 min. Top with coconut. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 580 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 23, 1998
