Strawberry-Grapefruit Drink
frozen strawberries, grapefruit juice, 
Break up strawberries. Place strawberries and 1 cup grapefruit juice in  blender. Cover and blend on high speed about 30 seconds or until smooth.  Add remaining grapefruit juice. Cover and blend until mixed. Serve over  ice. 4 SERVINGS (ABOUT 3/4 CUP EACH); 155 CALORIES PER SERVING.
