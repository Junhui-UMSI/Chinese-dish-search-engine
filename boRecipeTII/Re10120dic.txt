Quesadilla (Emeril)
flour tortillas, Fresh salsa, arugula, Oil, Ground cumin, Queso Fresco, chicken, Cayenne, Sour cream, Salt and pepper, 
On work surface lay out two tortillas. Layer chicken, cheese and arugula on  each; season with salt and pepper. Cover with remaining tortillas. Heat a  griddle or two skillets and brush with oil. Carefully place quesadillas on  griddle and cook until blistered and golden. Turn them with a large spatula  and cook until golden. Meanwhile, stir cumin, cayenne and salt, to taste,  into sour cream. Remove quesadillas and cut each into 6 pieces. To serve,  dollop with cumin cream and top with salsa.
