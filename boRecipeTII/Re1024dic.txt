Classic Pear Crisp
Brown sugar, Flour, Cinnamon, Pears, Flour, Rolled oats, Brown sugar, Butter, 
1.  In small bowl, stir 1/4 cup brown sugar with 1 tbsp flour and    cinnamon.  Toss with pears and place in 8" (2L) baking dish.   2.  Stir 1/2 cup flour with rolled oats and 1/2 cup brown sugar, and    slowly stir in 1/4 cup melted butter.  Spread over pear mixture.   3.  Bake at 350F for 35 mins or until tender and topping is golden brown.
