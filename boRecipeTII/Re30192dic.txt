Banana Bread
Baking soda, Vainilla, Salt, Buttermilk, Flour, Nuts, Butter, Baking Powder, Sugar, Eggs, Bananas, 
Cream butter and sugar, add eggs one at a time. Add  Vainilla.  Add dry  ingredients alternating with buttermilk.  Fold in Bananas Bake 350: about  50 minutes. Sprinkle with powdered sugar after you unmold it.    Posted to FOODWINE Digest 16 November 96    Date:    Thu, 14 Nov 1996 22:15:58 PST    From:    Francisco Sanchez <fransang779@CANTV.NET>
