Baked Bean Sausage Casserole
Catsup, Brown sugar, (1 Lb.) Baked Beans, Pepper, Onion, Smoked Ham; cut in 1/2", (10 Oz.) Frozen Lima Beans, Pork Link Sausage, (8 Oz) Tomato Sauce, (1 Lb.) Kidney Beans;, Salt, Mustard, 
Cook lima beans 10 minutes. Drain, mix with baked beans, drained kidney  beans, port sausage (cut each link in 3 pcs.) ham, tomato sauce and  seasonings with 3 1/2 qt. casserole. Bake uncovered in 400 degrees for one  hour. Add water during cooking if casserole seems dry.    Serves 25 at buffet.    Source: Recipes Out of This World Women of St. Agnes Catholic Church  Charleston, WV 1969    Recipe by: Claire Foster    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Bill Spalding <billspa@icanect.net> on Feb  26, 1998
