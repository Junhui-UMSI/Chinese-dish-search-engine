Quick Bbq Beef Bake
Onion, Bisquick baking mix, milk, Cheddar cheese, Eggs, Ground beef, BBQ sauce, 
Preheat to 400. Cook meat and 1/2 cup of the onion, drain. Stir in bbq  sauce. Spoon into a 13x9 baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese. Blend bisquick  ,milk, and eggs til well blended,, pour over meat. Sprinkle with rest of  the onion. Bake for 25-27 minutes. Til crust is light golden brown.    Try add some chopped green peppers, and chopped tomatoes.
