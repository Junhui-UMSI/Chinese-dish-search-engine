Strawberries Cardinal
Boiling water, Strawberries, frozen raspberries, strawberry, Raspberry-flavored liqueur, 
From: Donna Ransdell, COOKING echo. Originally from Jell-O, published again  in Arizona Republic on June 5, 1991    Dissolve gelatin in boiling water in a saucepan over low heat; stirring  constantly. Chill until slightly thickened, about 20 minutes. Dip  strawberries in gelatin to coat, while holding stem. Place on waxed  paper-lined baking sheet; chill until gelatin is firm.    Place raspberries and liqueur in a blender or food processor; puree. Spoon  about 1/4 cup sauce on 6 dessert plates. Top with strawberries. Makes 6  servings.
