Alaskan Blueberry Coffee Cake
Sugar, Cinnamon (or more to taste), Baking Powder, Salt, Egg, All-purpose flour, Firm butter (1/2 stick), All-purpose flour, Vegetable oil, Blueberries (I use fresh,, milk, Brown sugar, firmly packed, 
Appearing In: The American Country Inn and Bed & Breakfast Cookbook, Volume  One. From: Heavenly View Bed and Breakfast, Anchorage, Alaska    In a medium mixing bowl, blend together 1-1/2 cups flour, sugar, baking  powder, salt, oil, milk, egg and 1 cup blueberries. Beat thoroughly for 30  seconds and spread in a greased round 9x1-1/2-inch pan or an 8x8x2-inch  pan. Combine 1/3 cup flour, brown sugar, connamon and butter. Sprinkle over  batter and top with the remaining berries. Bake in a 375 degree oven for 25  to 30 minutes, until done. Dont overbake. Serve warm with butter or honey.  Serves 8.    Sonyas note: If I remember correctly, this takes a bit longer to bake than  what is in the instructions, but it is worth the wait!
