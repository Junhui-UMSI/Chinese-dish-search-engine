Raspberry Marshmallow Bread
Canola oil, Egg Beaters, Red raspberries;frozen in, Salt, Water; + ->, Gluten, Baking soda, Better for Bread flour, Raspberry liquid, heated, Yeast, Miniature marshmallows, 
Place the first 9 ingredients into the pan in the  order listed, set darkness control at 11 oclock.  Selectwhite bread and push "Start." Add the reserved,  well-drained raspberries (2/3cup) when the breadmaker  "beeps," 88 minutes into the cycle. (33 minutes with  Dak Turbo II) Shared by Barb Day Delightful with fruit  salad, or for a cholesterol-free fiber-flled  breakfastreat, spread toasted slices with Kaplans  Better than Butter.
