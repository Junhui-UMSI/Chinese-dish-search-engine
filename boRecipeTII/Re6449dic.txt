Plum Tomato Basil Pizza
Cornmeal, Ripe seeded Italian plum, olive oil, Shredded Stella Mozzarella, Refrigerated pizza dough;, TABASCO pepper sauce, Fresh basil leaves, 
Preheat oven to 425F. Prepare l2-inch round pizza crust according to  package directions. Dust crust with cornmeal. Distribute Stella Mozzarella  Cheese evenly over crust. Layer with tomato slices and basil leaves.    Drizzle with TABASCO sauce and olive oil. Bake l5 minutes, or until crust  is golden brown.
