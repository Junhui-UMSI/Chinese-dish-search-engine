Tapenade
Canned tuna in oil, Capers, Black olives, chopped, Anchovy fillets (opt), Shot glass of cognac, 
Using a pestle and mortar, crush olives, capers, anchovies and tuna  together. Add cognac.    Serve as spread with bread or toast points.    Nutritional analysis per serving: 142 calories; 15.0 grams fat; 6.13  milligrams cholesterol; 514 milligrams sodium; 81% of calories from  fat.
