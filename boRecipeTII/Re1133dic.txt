Cranberry Orange Muffins
Rolled oats, Plain yogurt, Vegetable oil, Brown Sugar, Egg, OR equivalent egg, Substitute, All-purpose flour,, Unbleached, Salt, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Fresh cranberries; cut in, Half, Chopped walnuts; roasted, Coconut flakes; roasted, (omit), Yield:  2 dozen., 
Cut cranberries in half so they wont explode.    LARGE BOWL:  combine oats and yogurt, soak for 10 minutes. Add oil, sugar  and egg; mix well.    Use a sifter to combine flour, salt, soda and baking powder. Sift the  mixture into the bowl. Also fold in cranberries, walnuts (and coconut)  until just blended.    Spoon into paper-lined muffin tins and bake 20 minutes at 400F.    Cooks Note: vary the 2nd dozen by adding zest from 1/2 orange or tangelo.  Vanilla yogurt may be substituted for plain.
