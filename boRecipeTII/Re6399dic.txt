Plum Chicken
chicken breasts, Dry mustard, Tabasco sauce, DAN GRUBER   (PXSM21A), Fennel seeds, Soy sauce, Lemon extract, Lemon juice, Ground ginger, Onion, Oriental plum sauce, Pepper, 
Heres a recipe that is Heart-Smart (something we should all think about  more often) Rinse chicken; pat dry and place in a 9X13 baking dish. In a  small bowl, mix together all of the other ingredients.    Pour over chicken. Bake chicken, uncovered, in a 400 degree oven until meat  is no longer pink in its thickest part, about 25 minutes.    Serve chicken with sauce on top. Hope you enjoy!
