Peanut Butter Fudge 3
Sugar, Peanut butter, Vanilla, milk, 
Put sugar and milk in pan and bring to a boil. Boil two and a half minutes.  Remove from heat and add peanut butter and vanilla. Stir just until mixed  well. Pour into greased pan. Cool and cut.
