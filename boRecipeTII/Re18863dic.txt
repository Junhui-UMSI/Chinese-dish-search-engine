Andreas Pineapple Walnut Cookies
Egg, Brown sugar, Salt, Walnuts, Lemon extract, Baking Powder, Granulated sugar, Shortening, Crushed pineapple, Flour, Baking soda, 
Cream sugars, shortening and egg. Add pineapple and then sift flour,  salt, soda, baking powder together and add to sugar mixture. Add  lemon extract and walnuts.    Drop by teaspoonsful onto a greased cookie sheet and flatten with the  bottom of a glass dipped in sugar. Bake at 350F for 12 min.
