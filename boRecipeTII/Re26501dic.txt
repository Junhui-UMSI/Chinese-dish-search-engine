Bacon Stix
Parmesan cheese, Bacon, Thin bread sticks about 8, 
Dredge one side of each bacon strip in cheese, then roll it around a bread  stick cheese-side in. Place all the sticks on a plate lined with paper  towel. Microwave 4 - 5 1/2 minutes or until bacon begins to brown. Remove  from oven and immediately roll in cheese.    These stay crisp for several hours after microwaving and are great with  pasta dishes.
