Asparagus with Nutmeg Browned Butter
(10-oz) frozen asparagus, Grated or ground nutmeg, Fresh asparagus spears or, Salt, Butter, Boiling; salted water, 
Wash and trim fresh asparagus spears. Cook fresh or frozen spears in a  small amount of boiling, salted water until just tender. Drain thoroughly.  Season lightly with salt; turn into serving dish. ln a small pan, heat  butter until it bubbles and browns slightly; stir in nutmeg. Pour butter  mixture over asparagus. Serve immediately. Makes six (6) servings.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
