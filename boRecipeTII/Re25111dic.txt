Tomato-&-Pear Chowchow
Yellow onions (as above), Cinnamon, Tart pears (peeled & finely, Apple cider vinegar, Ginger, Green tomatoes (as above), Brown sugar, Salt, Cayenne, White mustard seed, Turmeric, Chopped), 
Place first 8 ingredients in large kettle & bring to boil. Cook for 5  minutes & add remaining ingredients. Reduce heat & simmer over low  heat for 30 minutes.  Pack chowchow into sterile jars. Seal & process  in a boiling water bath for 15 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
