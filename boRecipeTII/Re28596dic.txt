rinsed and drained, Red beans or pinto beans, Hickory-smoked BBQ sauce, Rice, Smoked beef sausage, 
Brown sausage in large skillet over medium-high heat; drain and return  sausage to skillet.  Stir in rice, beans, and sauce. Cook until thoroughly  heated, about 2 to 3 minutes.    Microwave Oven Instructions: Place sausage in microproof baking dish.  Cover with waxed paper; cook on HIGH 3 minutes.  Add rice, beans and  sauce.  Cook on HIGH 4 to 5 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring  after 2 minutes.
