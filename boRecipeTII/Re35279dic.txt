Beef and Vegetable Soup #1
Vegetable oil, Boneless beef sirloin; cut, Water, (10.5-oz) condensed, Garlic, Chopped fresh parsley; if, Uncooked elbow macaroni (1, 
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat until hot. Add beef and  garlic; cook and stir 2-3 minutes or until browned.     Add remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium; simmer  15 minutes. If desired, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
