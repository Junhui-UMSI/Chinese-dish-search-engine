Almost Snickers
Sugar free Chocolate pudding, Crunchy peanut butter, Low fat ice cream; softened, Low fat Cool Whip, Grape Nut cereal, 
Mix together ice cream, Cool Whip, peanut butter and pudding. Fold in Grape  Nuts. Spread in 8x8 inch pan and freeze.    Recipe By     : Mary Ann Housman (WW leader)    Posted to EAT-L Digest 25 Sep 96    Date:    Thu, 26 Sep 1996 18:20:24 -0400    From:    Eileen & Bob Holze <beck4@ASAN.COM>
