Trout Amandine
freshwater trout, Lemon juice, almonds, Clarified butter, Pepper, Chopped fresh parsley, Salt, Lemon slices, All-purpose flour, 
Rinse fish thoroughly in cold water; pat dry, and set aside.  Combine flour, salt, and pepper in a small bowl; dredge fish in flour.  Fry fish in 2 tbs clarified butter in a large skillet 5 minutes on  each side or until golden brown.  Transfer to a warm serving dish;  sprinkle with lemon juice.    Add remaining clarified butter to skillet.  Add almonds, and saute until  golden brown.  Spoon almonds over fish.  Garnish with lemon and parsley.
