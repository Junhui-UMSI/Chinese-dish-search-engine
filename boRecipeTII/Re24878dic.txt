Aussie- Roseville Tea Sandwiches
Butter, Cucumber slices, thin, Capers, Green onion, minced, Mayonnaise, Smoked salmon, Horseradish, Pepper, Whole wheat bread, Fresh dill, chopped, 
Combine mayonnaise, onion, dill, cappers, horseradish and pepper. trim  crusts from bread; spread with butter then mayonnaise mixture. Arrange  salmon and cucumber over 4 slices of bread. Top with remaining bread; cut  each sandwich into 4 triangles or squares.
