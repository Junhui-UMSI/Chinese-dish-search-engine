Bechamel or Veloute Sauce
Flour, Salt, Light cream, Hot milk or chicken broth, Butter, Grated Swiss cheese or, 
Heat butter and add flour. Cook slowly for a minute or two. Add the hot  liquid and bring to a boil,    stirring continuously. Season to taste with salt and pepper; beat in cream.  Off heat, add cheese and    adjust the seasoning.
