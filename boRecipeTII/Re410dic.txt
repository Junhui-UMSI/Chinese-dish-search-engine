Lemon Delicious Pudding
Butter, Grated zest of 2 lemons, Sugar, Eggs, Self-rising flour, milk, Juice of 3 lemons, 
In a food processor, cream the butter with the grated lemon and the sugar.  When thoroughly creamed add the yolks. Add the flour and milk alternately  to make a smooth batter. Scrape the mixture from the sides of the food  processor and then blend in the lemon juice. Beat the egg whites until a  firm snow and delicately fold them into the batter. Pour into buttered,  individual molds and bake for 40 minutes at 350oF in a bain-marie (place  molds in a baking pan filled 2/3 full with boiling water).
