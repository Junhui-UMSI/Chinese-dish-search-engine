Bean Curd with a Deliciously Spicy Sauce
Vegetable oil, Scallions, Sesame oil, Garlic, Very finely minced ginger, Vegetable stock, Salt, very finely sliced, Bean curd, cut into 1inch cubes, peeled and minced, Cornstarch, Sugar, into rounds, including, Chinese thin soy sauce, Chili paste with soy bean, 3/4 of the green, 
Prepare the sauce.  Put the cornstarch in a small bowl.  Slowly add  the vegetable stock, mixing as you do so.  Now add the chili paste,  soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and sugar.  Mix again.  Set the sauce  aside.    Heat the vegetable oil in a wok over a medium high flame.  When hot,  put in the garlic and ginger.  Stir and fry for 5 seconds.  Put in  the bean curd. Stir and fry for 1 minute.  Turn heat to low.  Stir  the sauce and pour it over the bean curd.  Mix gently and bring to a  simmer.  Let the sauce thicken, stirring gently every now and then as  it does so.
