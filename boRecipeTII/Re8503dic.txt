Potato-Hamburger Pie
Onion, Carrots, Seasoned mashed potatoes, Flour, Ground beef; seasoned with, Butter, salt and pepper, 
submitted by: karyntag@ix8.ix.netcom.com  Cook onions and carrots in salted and boiling water; drain.  Season beef  and form into 8 balls. Brown in fat placed in casserole. To make gravy add  flour to drippings; brown. Add 1 1/2 cups liquid, using any left from  cooking vegetables. Stir until thickened. Add to the meatballs the gravy,  onions and carrots. Cover with the mashed potatoes. Bake in a moderate  oven, 350 degrees, until brown, about 20 minutes.
