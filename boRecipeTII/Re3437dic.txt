Pecan Chicken Casserole
Pecans chopped, Salt, Mayonnaise, Lemon juice, Eggs hard cooked, Pepper, Celery chopped, Water, Cream of Chicken soup, Onion chopped, Rice cooked, Cooked chicken diced, 
Combine chicken, soup, vegetables, nuts and seasoning; blend well. Stir in  lemon juice, mayonnaise and water.  Chop 2 eggs; add to mixture. Place in  greased casserole.  Bake at 400 for 15 minutes or until bubbly. Let stand  15 minutes before serving. Garnish with remaining sliced egg. Makes 4  servings.
