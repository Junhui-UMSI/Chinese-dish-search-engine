Eggplant Spread
Eggplant; 1 to 1 1/2 pounds, Fresh parsley; minced, Fresh cilantro; minced, Ground cumin, Ground coriander, Garlic powder, Salt; optional, Tabasco sauce, 
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook Preparation Time: 1:10 Preheat the  oven to 350F. Cut the stem off the eggplant and prick it all over with a  fork. Place it directly on the oven rack and bake for about 1 hour, or  until the eggplant is soft and the skin is wrinkled. Remove from the oven  and allow to cool. When it is cool enough top handle, peel and chop. Place  in a blender or food processor with the parsley and cilantro. Process until  smooth. Place in a saucepan and add the remaining ingredients. Cook,  stirring, until the mixture thickens slightly, about 10 minutes. 75  calories, 0.9 grams fat per 1/2 cup serving.
