Peaches and Cream Dessert
Salt, Peaches (20 oz.), Cream cheese (8 oz.), Sugar, Sugar, Flour, Vanilla pudding mix (3 oz.), milk, Peach juice, Baking Powder, Egg, Butter, 
Combine flour, baking powder, vanilla pudding, salt, butter, milk and egg  in a bowl; beat 2 minutes. Pour into greased pie plate. Drain the peaches,  saving the juice. MIx together the cream cheese, 1/2 cup sugar, and the  peach juice. Spread the peaches in pie plate. Spread cream cheese mixture  over peaches within 1 inch of edge. Mix 1 tablespoon sugar and 1/2 teaspoon  cinnamon. Sprinkle over pie top. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for  30-35 minutes.
