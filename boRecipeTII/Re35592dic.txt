Stringed Beef ( Ropa Vieja )
Dry wine, Vegetable oil, Red peppers, Round beef, Onion, Green pepper, Bay leaf, Garlic, Tomato sauce, Salt, 
Cook the beef in stew or soup until it falls apart. String the beef. Slice  onion and green pepper.  Crush garlic and fry in hot oil with onion. Add  green pepper and fry a little more. Add remaining ingredients, cover and  cook over low heat for 15 or 20 minutes stirring occasionally to avoid  sticking.  The red pepper may be added now or used to decorate. Serve with  rice or salad.    This recipe from:       The Cuban Flavor - A CookBook                          by Raquel Rabade Roque
