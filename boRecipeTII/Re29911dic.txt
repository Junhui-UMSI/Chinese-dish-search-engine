Teriyaki Chicken Kebabs
Squares, Skin and fat removed, Red onion cut into, Or, Cut into 16 1-inch, Chunks, drained, Mild soy sauce, And stems removed, Frozen unsweetened, 16 1-inch squares, Garlic minced,, Salt-free chicken, 8-oz chicken breast, Ground ginger, Drained pineapple juice, Peeled, grated, Can unsweetened pineapple, Or, Green or sweet red, Apple juice concentrate, Garlic powder, Fresh ginger, Dry sherry, Broth, defatted, Mushrooms, cleaned, Or, Halves,boned, with, Pepper, seeded and, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in bowl.  Cut each chicken breast half into 6  squares.  Stir chicken, mushrooms, onions, and green pepper into marinade.  Marinate at room temperature for 10 minutes. Thread chicken, mushrooms,  onions, green peppers, and pineapple onto 8 (9 to 10-inch) wooden skewers.  Alternate ingredients until all are used. Broil 3 to 4 inches from heat for  7 to 9 minutes on each side, or until chicken is done. (Or barbecue over  hot coals for 12 to 15 minutes.) Brush several times with remaining  marinade while cooking. Walt MM    :       To serve: Serve kabobs on bed of pilaf.    In microwave:  Arrange skewers on 12-inch glass baking dish and cook on  high for about 7 minutes.
