Pennsylvania Pepper Cabbage (Cole Slaw)
Bell pepper, red, finely cho, *hot salad dressing, Bell pepper, green, finely c, Salt, *or:, Cabbage, shredded, 
Mix the shredded cabbage, pepper and salt and let stand about 1 hour. Drain  off all liquid. Pour the Hot Dressing over the cabbage and mix well. Serve  at once. Source: Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary  Arts Press, 1936.
