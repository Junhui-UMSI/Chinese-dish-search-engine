Quesadilla Supreme
Ground beef, Prepared guacamole, black olives, green onions, sour cream, Refried beans with peppers, canned, Tomatoes, fresh, Taco seasoning mix, flour tortillas, Monterey Jack cheese, Cheddar cheese, 
Brown beef and season according to taco seasoning directions. Butter 1 side  of tortilla and place on a cookie sheet, buttered side down. Cover with the  following layers: refried beans, taco seasoned beef, and two kinds of  cheeses that have been mixed together. Cover with another flour tortilla  that has been buttered on the top side. Bake 5 to 6 minutes at 500?. Broil  for 2 to 3 minutes or until slightly brown and cheese has melted. Remove  from oven and top with guacamole, black olives, tomatoes, green onions and  a spoonful of sour cream. Cut into 4 wedges to serve. Yield: 4 servings.    BOOPIE PROCTER MC INNIS    (MRS.
