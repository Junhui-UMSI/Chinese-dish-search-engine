Trishas Beef Fajitas
Red pepper, cut into strips, Jalapeno pepper, chopped, Ground cumin, Beef flank steak, (8 oz) chopped tomatoes, Salsa, Chili powder, Guacamole, Onion, sliced, 8-inch flour tortillas, TOPPINGS, Green pepper, cut into, Garlic, Grund coriander, Cheddar cheese, Salt, Sour cream, Cilantro, 
It is in the December Better Homes & Gardens. (Prep time-15 minutes)    Cut flank steak into 6 portions. In any size Crock-Pot, combine all  ingredients except tortillas and toppings. Cover and cook on low for 8-10  hours or on high for 4-5 hours. Remove meat from Crock-Pot and shred.  Return meat to Crock-Pot and stir. To serve fajitas, spread meat mixture  into flour tortillas, add favorite toppings. Roll up tortillas.
