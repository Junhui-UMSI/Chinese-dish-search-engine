Baked Tomatoes
Good sized plum tomatoes, Fine bread crumbs, Dried thyme, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Cut each tomato in half and arrange cut side up on a baking dish.  Sprinkle each half lightly with bread crumbs, parmesan cheese and thyme.  Bake for 15 to 20 min.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmealnew.zip
