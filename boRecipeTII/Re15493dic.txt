4 Bean Soup
Pinto beans; (16 oz.), Pork and beans (do not, Sugar, Catsup, Hamburger, Vinegar, Dry mustard, Kidney beans; (16 oz.), Chopped onion; sauteed, Salt, Bacon, Butter beans; (16 oz), Brown sugar, 
Brown and crumble bacon. Brown hamburger. Put all of the ingredients into a  casserole and bake for 40 minutes at 350 degrees in an oven or put in a  crock-pot all day.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 438 by "Diane Geary."  <diane@keyway.net> on Jan 2, 1998
