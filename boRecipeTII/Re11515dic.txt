Raspberry Crush
Lemon-Lime Soda, Frozen Raspberries, Cold water, Sugar, Raspberry Flavored ?, Frozen Lemonade, Raspberry Sherbet, 
Combine koolaid powder and sugar. Add water; stir until koolaid and sugar  are dissolved. Stir in lemonade concentrate and raspberries. Chill. Jest  before serving, gently stir in lemon-lime soda. Serve over ice cubes. Top  with raspberry sherbet.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 350 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Dec 08, 1997
