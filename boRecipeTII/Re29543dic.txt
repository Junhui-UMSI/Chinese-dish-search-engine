Tart Sweet Dipping Sauce
Garlic; chopped, Fish sauce, Chinese chili suace, Water, White vinegar, Lime juice, Sugar, Carrot; shredded, 
This is the traditional Thai dipping sdauce used for salad rolls, spring  rolls, and most everything else.    Combine sugar, water and vinegar.    Bring to a boil and boil for 5 minutes.    Stir in garlic, fish sauce and cbnhili sauce. Simmer for 2 minutes.    Cool and add lime juice and shredded carrot
