Pennsylvania Dutch Rice Pudding
Raisins, Rice, Butter, milk, Sugar, Confectioners sugar, Eggs, Ground nutmeg, 
Beat the egg yolks and mix in the sugar and milk. Stir into the rice. Mix  in butter and raisins. Pour into a buttered 1 quart baking dish. Beat the  egg whites until frothy, beat in the confectioners sugar and spread on top  of the rice mixture. Sprinkle with nutmeg. Bake at 325oF about 30 minutes.
