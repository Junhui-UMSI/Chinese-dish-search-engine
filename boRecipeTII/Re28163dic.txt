Baked Doves in Sherry
Doves, Salt and pepper, shallots, bacon, Sherry, Vegetable oil, mushrooms, Flour, 
Salt and pepper doves; dredge with flour and brown in small amount of oil.  Place 3-4 shallots in chest cavity; wrap doves with bacon and secure with  toothpicks. Place in pan; add sherry and mushrooms. Bake at 350 for 30  minutes. Serve sauce in pan with doves. Men like this one!
