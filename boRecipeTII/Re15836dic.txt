Acadian Coush-Coush
Salt, Cooking oil or bacon grease, Cornmeal, Milk or water, Baking Powder, 
Mix thoroughly cornmeal, salt, baking powder and milk. Put oil or grease  in a skillet; heat. Let crust form. Give a good stir and lower heat to  simmer. Cover. Cook 15 minutes. Serve with milk and sugar or with syrup.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
