Basic Fruit Sauce Recipe
Fruit; fresh or frozen, Cornstarch if needed;, 
1. Cook fruit over moderate heat.    2. Puree in a blender.    3. Strain through a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove seeds &  such..    4. If sauce is too loose thicken over a medium heat with a light cornstarch  slurry (cornstarch + water)    NOTES : YIELDS 1 Pint Great for desserts or ice cream... Its just that  Easy!
