Vegetable Tuna Casserole with Pretzel Topping
Skim milk, Crushed pretzel twists or, (10 3/4 oz.) cream of celery, Uncooked wide egg noodles, Frozen broccoli florets,, (6 oz.) water packed tuna,, Finely chopped red or green, Green Onions, Dried basil, 
Cook noodles to desired doneness as directed on package. Drain; keep warm.    Heat oven to 350. Spray 1 1/2 quart casserole with nonstick cooking spray.  In medium bowl, combine cooked noodles and all remaining ingredients except  pretzels. Spoon mixture into spray-coated casserole; top with pretzels.    Bake at 350 for 25 to 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated. 4 1 1/4 cup  servings.
