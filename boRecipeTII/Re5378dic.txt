Pineapple Broiled Loin Chops
Grated orange rind, Salt, Loin lamb chops (3/4", Unsweetened pineapple juice, Red wine vinegar, Thick), Basil, 
Sprinkle lamb with salt. Combine remaining ingredients; mix well. Pour  pineapple juice mixture over lamb; chill 1 hour, turning occasionally.  Remove lamb; reserve marinade. Broil lamb 3-4" from heat 5-7 minutes  per side, basting frequently.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
