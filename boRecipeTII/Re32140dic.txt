Barbecued Meat Patties
Egg, Water and, Onion, Brown sugar,, BASTING SAUCE, Lemon juice., Hot pepper sauce., Cornstarch, Ketchup,, Soy sauce, Half-half, Tomato ketchup, Worcestshire sauce, Ground meat, 
Stir to blend well. Add dash salt and black pepper. In a large mixing bowl,  combine ground meat, egg and onion. Add soy sauce, and ketchup. Mix half  and half and cornstarch. Add to meat mixture, mixing to blend well. Form  portions of meat to even, rather thick meat patties. Place patties on  preheated barbecue grill. Broil 3 to 4 inches from source of heat, basting  with sauce and turning patties over from time to time for even browning.  Grill 12-15 minutes total or to your liking- time depends on how well you  like your meat done. Serve meat patties on hamburger buns, if you like, or  with French Fries.
