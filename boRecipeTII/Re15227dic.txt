Whipped Ricotta Topping
Apple juice, Envelope unflavored gelatin, Nonfat ricotta cheese, Sugar, Vanilla, Nonfat buttermilk, 
Cook apple juice, sugar and gelatin over medium heat about 2 minutes,  stirring occasionally, until gelatin is dissolved. Place gelatin mixture  and remaining ingredients in blender. Cover and blend about 30 seconds or  until smooth. Cover and refrigerate about 30 minutes or until almost set.
