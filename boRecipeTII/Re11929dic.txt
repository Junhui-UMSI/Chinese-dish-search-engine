Rebeccas Rice Recipe
Crackers, (8-oz) sour cream, Chicken broth, Cream of mushroom soup, Chicken breasts; boiled and, Instant rice; uncooked, Stick butter or oleo; melted, 
Mix chicken, broth, soup, rice and sour cream all together. Pour into a  casserole dish. Crumble crackers. Mix cracker crumbs with butter, sprinkle  on top of casserole. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, uncovered.
