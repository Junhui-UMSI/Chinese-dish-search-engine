Barbecued Pork Chops
-, PORK LOIN CHOP FZ, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN               TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  BROWN CHOPS 2 MINUTES ON EACH SIDE ON LIGHTLY GREASED GRIDDLE.    2.  PLACE 50 CHOPS IN EACH PAN.    3.  HEAT PREPARED BARBECUE SAUCE; POUR OVER CHOPS IN EACH PAN.    4.  COVER PANS.  BAKE 1 1/2 HOURS OR UNTIL TENDER.  IF CONVECTION OVEN IS  USED, USE 14 LB 1 OZ (6 1/4 QT) PREPARED BARBECUE SAUCE; POUR 3 QT SAUCE  OVER CHOPS IN EACH PAN; COVER PANS; BAKE 1 HOUR 15 MINUTES ON HIGH FAN,  CLOSED VENT.
