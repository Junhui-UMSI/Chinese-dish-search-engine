Amaretto Baked Bananas
Light cream, Sugar, Unsalted butter; melted, Amaretto, Firm ripe bananas; peeled, Chopped almonds, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 15 min.    Preheat oven to 350F. Butter a shallow baking dish. Arrange whole bananas  in baking dish. Combine remaining ingredients in a mixing bowl. Pour sauce  over bananas and bake 15 minutes or until bananas are softened.  Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 05, 1998
