Ann Landers Meatloaf
Eggs, Bread crumbs, Ground round steak, Ketchup, Accent, Warm water, 
Beat thoroughly. Put into loaf pan. Cover with two of strips bacon, if you  like that flavor. Pour one 8-ounce can of tomato sauce over all. Bake one  hour at 350 degrees...
