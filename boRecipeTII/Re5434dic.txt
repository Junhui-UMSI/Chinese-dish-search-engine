Pineapple Cloud Pie
Brown Sugar, Crispy rice cereal crumbs, Powdered sugar; sifted, Fat-free cream cheese;, Crushed pineapple; undrained, Corn syrup, Cool Whip? Free; thawed, 
In a mixing bowl, combine crispy rice cereal crumbs, brown sugar, and corn  syrup. Mix well. Press mixture evenly and firmly in bottom and on side of  9" pie pan coated with cooking spray. Chill in refrigerator. In a mixing  bowl, combine cream cheese, powdered sugar, and pineapple. Fold in whipped  topping. Pour mixture into chilled crust. Freeze about 3 hours or until  firm.
