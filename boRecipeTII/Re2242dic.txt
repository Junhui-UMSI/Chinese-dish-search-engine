Baked Omelet Roll
All-purpose flour, (4 oz) shredded cheddar, Salt, Pepper, Eggs, milk, 
SOURCE: Taste of Home, December 98/January 99 Issue    Place eggs and milk in blender. Add flour salt and pepper; cover and  process until smooth. Pour into greased 13" x 9" x 2" baking dish. Bake at  450? for 20 minutes or until eggs are set. Sprinkle with cheese. Roll up  omelet in pan , starting with short side. Please with seam side down on a  serving platter. Cut into 1 inch slices.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by judith trachtman  <Judith.Trachtman@cbis.ece.drexel.edu (Judy Trachtman)> on Dec 1, 1998,
