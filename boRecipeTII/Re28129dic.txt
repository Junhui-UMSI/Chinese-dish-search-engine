Baked Crabmeat
Onion, Chopped, Parsley, Butter, Bread crumbs, Garlic, French bread, Chopped, Bread, Celery, salt and pepper, Milk, Egg, Crabmeat, 
In a skillet, saute onions and celery in butter until clear and tender. Add  crabmeat, parsley, and garlic. Stir fry about 10 minutes. Add French bread  and egg. Salt and pepper to taste. Mix well. Place in a buttered baking  dish. Cover with bread crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes.  From "Jambalaya, Crawfish Pie, File Gumbo" formatted by Mary Dishongh  Bowles.
