Vegetable Soup
Onion, finely chopped, Carrot, shredded, Vegetable oil, Condensed tomato soup, Vegetable juice cocktail, Water, Green pepper, finely chopped, Celery; finely chopped, 
Heat oil.  Add vegetables and cook until tender.  Mix soup, water, and  vegetable juice cocktail in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Add vegetables.  Cover and heat to serving temperature. Serve in mugs.
