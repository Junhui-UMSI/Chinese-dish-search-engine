Bcs Lime-Honey Marinade
Honey, Or apple juice, Finely chopped gingerroot, Grated lime peel, Dry white wine, Lime juice, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Soy sauce, 
Mix all ingredients in shallow nonmetal dish or resealable plastic bag. Add  1 pound boneless beef, pork, chicken or turkey, turning to coat with  marinade. Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours but no more than 24 hours.  Remove meat from marinade; reserve marinade. Grill meat as desired,  brushing occasionally with marinade. Heat remaining marinade to boiling;  boil and stir 1 minute.    *Betty Crockers Great Grilling  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #306 by Michael McLaughlin  <mmclaughlin@micronet.net> on Nov 25, 1997
