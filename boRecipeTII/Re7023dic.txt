Popeye Red Beans and Rice
Red chili beans in chili, Chili powder, Garlic salt, Cumin, 
Cook enough rice to yield 1 c cooked. In saucepan heat beans without  letting it boil and stir in chili powder, cumin and garlic salt. When  piping hot, spoon chili mixture into 6 small dishes, adding a few tb of  hot, cooked rice to each serving. Season with Chili Seasoning Mix, if  desired. 270 cal, 5.9 gr fat, 60% fat.
