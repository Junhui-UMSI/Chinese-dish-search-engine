Porcupine Meatballs
Tomato soup, Onion, Catsup, Ground turkey, Water, Sage, Garlic powder, Salt, Rice, Egg, Tomatoe sauce, Worchestershire, 
Mix meat, rice, onion, egg and dry seasonings with catsup.  Form into  meatballs and place in pressure cooker.  Mix soup, tomato sauce, water, and  worcestershire sauce and pour over meatballs.  Cook 15 minutes with pressur  rocker rocking slowly.  Cool at once.
