Sweet Potato-Honey Balls
Marshmallows, miniature, Pecans, Honey, Potatoes, sweet; mashed,, Salt, Pepper, Butter (or marg.); melted,, 
Combine potatoes, salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons butter; stir in  marshmallows.  Chill.  Shape into balls, using 1/4 cup potato mixutre for  each.    Combine 2 tablespoons butter and honey in small heavy skillet; add potato  balls one at a time.  Using 2 forks to make handling easier, quickly coat  each with glaze.  Roll potato balls in pecans, and place in a greased  shallow baking dish.  Drizzle with remainig butter. Bake at 350 degrees for  15 to 20 minutes.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974.
