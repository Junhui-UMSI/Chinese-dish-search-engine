Sweet Peanut Soup
Brown sugar, Boking soda, Raw peanuts, Water, -(up to), 
1. Heat water to lukewarm; then stir in baking soda.    2. Add raw peanuts and bring to a boil. Then simmer, covered, until  completely softened (about 1-1/2 hours).    3. Add brown sugar and cook, stirring, until dissolved. Serve hot, in small  bowls.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
