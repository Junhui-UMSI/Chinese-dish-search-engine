Basic Cheesecake
Ricotta cheese; softened, Cream cheese, Vanilla, Box graham cracker crumbs, Butter, unsalted; softened, Sugar, Eggs, 
Crust: In a large bowl: put graham cracker crumbs. Knead in 1/2 stick of  butter. Press onto bottom of 9" prepared spring form pan. Chill for about       15    minutes before filling.    Filling: In blender combine1 tub of ricotta (16 oz) 1 brick of cream chesse  (12 oz), 1/2 tsp vanilla, 1/3 cup sugar, 2 eggs and 1/4 stick of butter.  Blend until smooth. Pour GENTLY on top of crust. Repeat for seond half of  ingredients. Bake @ 325 NO HOTTER for about an hour and a quarter. Check  frequently! It is done when center starts to firm up and sides begin to  pull away from pan. Turn off the oven and allow to cool SLOWLY. Chill for  at least 6 hours before serving.
