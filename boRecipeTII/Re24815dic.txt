Aunt Marys Hot Peppers
Italian plum tomatoes, garlic, Hot peppers, olive oil, 
Salt    Remove stem and seeds from peppers. <-(CAUTION)-> WEAR RUBBER GLOVES AND/OR  WASH HANDS AFTER.    Saute peppers and garlic in oil for about 10 minutes. Crush 2-3 tomatoes  with fork and add to fry pan. Saute for another 20 minutes. Season with  salt.
