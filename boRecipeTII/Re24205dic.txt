Treacle Pudding
Grated Rind of Lemon, milk, Shredded Suet, Self-Raising Flour, Egg, Breadcrumbs, Golden Syrup, 
Sift the flour and mix it with the shredded suet, stir  in the breadcrumbs and grated rind of the lemon.  Whisk up the egg, warm the golden syrup, then whisk  them together and stir them into the dry ingredients.  Mix well, adding a little milk as required.  Turn the mixture into a greased pudding basin, cover  it with a greased paper and steam for about two hours.  Turn pudding out and serve it with some more golden  syrup, the latter heated and served over the pudding.
