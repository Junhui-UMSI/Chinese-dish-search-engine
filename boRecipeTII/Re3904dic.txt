Pepper Relish #2
Green bell peppers, Sugar, Red bell peppers, Onions, Vinegar, Salt, 
Grind peppers & onions. Cover with boiling water & let stand 5 minutes.  Drain. Add vinegar, sugar & salt. Boil 5 minutes & pour into hot,  sterilized jars & seal. (If you want HOT relish, add 20-25 banana peppers!)    GAYLE KEIRSEY    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
