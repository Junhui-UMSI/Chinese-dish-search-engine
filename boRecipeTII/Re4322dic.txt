Persimmon Butter
Persimmon puree, Orange juice, rind of 1 orange, Honey, 
Combine all ingredients in an enameled or stainless steel kettle and cook  over medium-high heat until thick, about 10-15 minutes. Ladle into hot jars  and seal. Process pint jars for 15 minutes in boiling water bath. Makes  about 5 pints.  Try on toast or English muffins.
