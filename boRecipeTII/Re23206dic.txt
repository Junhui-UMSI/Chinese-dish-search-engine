Tuna Delight
(6 1/2 oz.) Tuna per person;, Hard-boiled egg per person;, Onion; very finely minced, Equal parts mayonnaise and, Chopped parsley (optional), Water, Juice from lemon, Knox unflavored gelatin, Toasted, slivered almonds;, Black, pitted olives per, 
Using hands for mixing and adding eggs last to keep them intact, mix the  first six ingredients.    Mix mayonnaise, sour cream and lemon juice.  Set aside.    Add gelatin to water and soak until the gelatin expands. Heat on a low  flame until just at the melted point. Pour into mayonnaise mixture and stir  like mad.    Fold in dry ingredients (first six ingredients) and stir well.    Either pour into mold or just toss and chill in bowl in fridge.    Source:  Unknown.
