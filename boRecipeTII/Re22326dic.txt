Turkey-Meatball Pitas
Grated onion, Ground cumin, Ground turkey, Low-fat bread; chopped, Plain nonfat yogurt, Romaine lettuce; thinly, Whole-wheat pitas, Cucumber; peeled and cut, Egg white, Salt, Chopped fresh cilantro OR, 
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Spray 15 1/2" by 10 1/2" jelly-roll pan  with nonstick cooking spray.    2. In large bowl, with hand, mix ground turkey, bread, onion, egg white,  cumin, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and 3 tablespoons water.    3. Shape turkey mixture into 25 meatballs. (For easier shaping, use wet  hands.) Place meatballs in jelly-roll pan and bake 12 to 15 minutes until  cooked through (meatballs will not brown).    4. Cut about 1 inch from top of each pita; reserve cutoff pieces for use  another day. Wrap pitas in foil. After meatballs have baked 5 minutes, warm  pitas in oven until meatballs are done.    5. Meanwhile, mix cucumber, yogurt, cilantro or mint, and 1/2 teaspoon  salt.    6. To serve, fill pitas with lettuce and meatballs; top with cucumber  sauce.    Each serving: About 380 calories, 28 g protein, 44 g carbohydrate, 11 g  total fat (3 g saturated), 46 mg cholesterol, 1020 mg sodium.
