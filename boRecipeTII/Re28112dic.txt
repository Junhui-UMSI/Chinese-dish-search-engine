Baked Corn En Casserole
milk, Red pepper; to taste, Bell pepper; chopped, Onion, Sugar, Pimiento; chopped, Cracker crumbs, Cheese; grated, Creamed corn; No. 303, Butter or Margarine, Whole kernel corn; No. 303, Egg; well beaten, salt and pepper,
