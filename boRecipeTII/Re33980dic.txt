Sweet Corn and Tomato Saute
Cooked whole kernel corn, Butter or margarine, Salt, Tomato, diced, Onions, Brown sugar, Green Pepper, 
In a saucepan, melt butter. Add corn, onion, green pepper, brown sugar and  salt. Cover and simmer 10 minutes. Stir in the tomato; simmer 5 minutes  longer. Yield: 4 servings.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
