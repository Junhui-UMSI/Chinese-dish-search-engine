Amish Peach Pie
CRUMBS, Salt, Tapioca, Cinnamon, Peaches; peeled and sliced, 9" unbaked pie shell, Butter or margerine; melted, Brown sugar, Flour, Sugar, 
Mix together gently, peaches, sugar, salt and tapioca. Let belnd for 5  minutes before spooning into pie shell. Mix crumb ingredients well and  sprinkle over pie shell.   Bake at 425 degrees F. for 45 to 50 minutes.    Source: The Best Of Amish Cooking by Phyllis Pellman Good Submitted by  Michael Hatala, Prodigy F&W Board
