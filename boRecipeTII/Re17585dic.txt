Almond Glaze
Salt, SUGAR, POWDER, FLAVORING MAPLE, WATER, BUTTER, 
1.  COMBINE POWDERED SUGAR, BUTTER OR MARGARINE, SALT, BOILING WATER  AND FLAVORING, ALMOND; MIX UNTIL SMOOTH.    2.  SPREAD GLAZE OVER BAKED SWEET ROLLS OR COFFEE CAKES.  COAT OR DIP  FRIED DOUGHNUTS IN GLAZE.  :    NOTE:  THIS GLAZE MAY BE USED FOR A VARIETY OF SWEET DOUGH PRODUCTS  (SEE RECIPE NO. D-G-7, GUICE FOR SWEET DOUGH MAKE-UP) OR DOUGHNUTS  (SEE RECIPE NOS.
