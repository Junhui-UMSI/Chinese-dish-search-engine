Preserved Green Chillies
Corn oil, Green chillies, Salt, Whole garlic (not clove), 
Wash chillies and slit on one side    Grind garlic and salt together and stuff into chillies    Heat oil in a frying pan and fry chillies until light brown Compiled by I.  Chaudhary  Posted to FOODWINE Digest  by Abbott <labbott@MHO.NET> on Nov 20, 1997
