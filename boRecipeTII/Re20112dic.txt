Vanilla Ice Cream and California Fruit Trio
Mint leaves, Chopped pistachio, Peaches, peeled, then, Orange zest, Pieces orange wedges in own, Orange juice, Vanilla ice cream, Pieces cherries, cut into, 
Remove stones from the poached peaches.  Arrange the orange wedges and  cherries on a plate in a circle following the edge of the plate. Place a  peach in the middle of the orange wedges and put the ice cream on top of  each peach.    Sprinkle with orange zest over the oranges.    Pour orange juice over oranges.    Decorate with mint leaves.    Sprinkle chopped pistachio nuts on top of the ice cream. Serves: 2 Typed in  MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Food & Wine the Westin Way Chef  Tadashi Katoh, Chef de Cuisine, Century Plaza Hotel, Los Angeles.
