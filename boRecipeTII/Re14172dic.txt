White Grape, Tangerine, and Asti Spumante Punch
Bottle (25.4 ounces) chilled, Strained fresh lemon juice, 24-ounce bottles chilled, Chilled club soda, Can frozen tangerine juice, Thin tangerine or orange, Brandy, 
In a punch bowl whisk together the grape juice, tangerine concentrate, club  soda, lemon juice, and brandy and chill the mixture, covered, until it is  cold. Add the Asti-Spumante slowly just before serving and serve in punch  glasses, garnished with the tangerine slices.
