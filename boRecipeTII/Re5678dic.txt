Pineapple Zucchini Bread
Vanilla, Eggs, Baking Powder, Raisins, Salad oil, Sugar, Crushed pineapple , Salt, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Drained, Zucchini, Pecans, Unsifted flour, 
In a bowl, beat eggs, salad oil, sugar, and vanilla until well mixed. Stir  in zucchini and pineapple. In a separate bowl sift together flour, baking  soda, salt , baking powder, and cinnamon. Mix dry ingredients into zucchini  mixture until just blended. Add nuts and raisins. Pour into two greased and  floured baking pans. Bake at 350 degrees for one hour or until a toothpick  comes out clean.
