Baked French Toast
Cottage Cheese; Lowfat, Vanilla extract, Bread Slices; whole grain, Skim milk, Egg white, Egg, Sugar, 
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. In a food processor or blender, whirl the  cottage cheese, milk, sugar, egg, egg white and vanilla for about 1 minute  or until smooth. Transfer the mixture to a shallow dish, place the bread in  it, and let stand for 10 minutes. Turn the bread over and let stand another  10 minutes. Meanwhile, grease a baking dish and place it in a preheated  oven for 7 minutes.    2.Remove the baking sheet from the oven, place the bread on it and bake for  6 minutes. Turn the bread over and bake 5-6 minutes more or until golden  brown. Makes 4 servings  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #831 by slhire@juno.com (Sandra L Hire) on  Oct 8, 1997
