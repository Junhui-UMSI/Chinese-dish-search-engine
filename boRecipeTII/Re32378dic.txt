Barley Soup #2
Water, Potatoes; diced, Kitchen Boquet, Salt, Parsley, Pearl barley, Celery, Worcestershire, Thyme, Onions; chopped, Carrots; sliced, Bay leaf, Vegetable oil, Beef; cubed, 
Brown beef in oil. Add celery, salt, parsley, Worcestershire, thyme, bay  leaf and water. Simmer 2 hours. Skim and add remaining ingredients. Cook  another 45 minutes. More water may be needed if soup cooks down and becomes  too thick.
