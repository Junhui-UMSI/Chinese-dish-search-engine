Polenta-Crusted Pompano Salad
salt and pepper, Polenta or corn meal; for, Extra virgin olive oil; plus, Virgin olive oil, Juice of 1/2 lemon, Capers; rinsed, drained, Black Pepper, Vine-ripened tomato, Chicory salad, washed; spun, Balsamic vinegar, Boneless fillets of Pompano,, Spicy peppers vinaigrette, 
In a large mixing bowl, toss clean chicory with 3 tablespoons extra virgin  olive oil, lemon juice and salt and pepper to taste. Divide among 4 plates,  placing mixture in center of each. In a 10-inch to 12-inch saute pan, heat  4 tablespoons virgin oil until smoking. Meanwhile, dredge Pompano fillet  and sprinkle with black pepper. Carefully place crusted Pompano in saute  pan and cook until golden brown (about 3 minutes per side). Meanwhile,  roughly chop tomato and put into blender with balsamic, capers and  remaining half cup extra virgin olive oil. Blend until smooth.    Remove Pompano fillets and place one on each plate over chicory. Arrange  peppers vinaigrette around on plate and drizzle with tomato oil and serve  immediately.
