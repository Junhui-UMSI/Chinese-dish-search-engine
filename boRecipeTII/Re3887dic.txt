Pepper Jack Ties
Salt, Water, Sugar, White bread flour, Dry milk, Pepper jack cheese, grated, Active dry yeast -OR-, Dried, crushed red pepper, Cilantro (I omit), Fast rise yeast, 
*from Electric Bread*    these are delicious and not too spicy    Add water to pan. Add rest of ingredients except yeast. Place yeast in well  on top of dry. Use dough setting.    When done, turn dough onto lightly floured surface. Punch down and divide  dough into 12 equal parts. Form each piece into a 7 inch rope and tie in  knot. Place in greased muffin tin and allow to rise for 25 minutes. Brush  with beaten egg and bake and 375 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
