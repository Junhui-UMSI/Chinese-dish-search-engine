Baked Apples
Sugar, Washed apple, top and core, Of cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 350~.    Sprinkle the inside of the apple with the sugar and cinnamon. Place in a  baking dish, with a few spoonfuls of water at the bottom. Bake until tender  or 30-45 minutes.    (NOTE: this is a good one for dessert for the whole family. Adults can    sprinkle nuts on top of their portions).    From "Make Your Own Baby Food" By Lorrie Scally.
