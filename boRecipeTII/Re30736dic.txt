Tandoori Chicken - Tandoori Murgh
Chicken, Turmeric, Plain yogurt, fresh ginger root, Cayenne pepper, Salt, Veg. oil, Garlic, Cumin seed, 
Preparation and cooking time: 2 hours for overnight marinating, plus about        1    hour    Combine grated ginger, garlic, cumin seed, cayenne pepper, salt and yogurt.    Put the chicken pieces in a foil-lined baking pan, pour over the yogurt  mixture, and use your hands to coat the meat completely. Leave the chicken  to marinate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight.    Heat the oven to 350 F. Dribble the oil over the chicken in the baking pan,  and sprinkle the chicken with turmeric. Place the pan in the oven and bake  for about 1 hour, basting frequently with the oil and yogurt marinade at  the bottom of the pan. Serve with hot pita bread and lentil dal.
