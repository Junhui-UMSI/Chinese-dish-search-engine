Crab Sandwiches
crab meat, celery, Green onions, mushrooms, Caraway seed, Mayonnaise, Salt, Bread, Bacon, Cheddar Cheese, 
Combine the crab meat, celery, scallions, mushrooms, caraway, mayonnaise,  salt and pepper in a bowl and mix until thoroughly combined. Toast the  bread on one side under the broiler. Spread the crab mixture on the  untoasted side, top with the bacon and the Cheddar cheese, and heat under  the broiler until the cheese is melted.
