Raspberry Fudge Balls
Vanilla wafer crumbs, Raspberry preserves, seedless, Cream cheese, Almonds, Chocolate chips, 
Combine cream cheese and chocolate.  Mix until well blended. Stir in crumbs  and preserves.  shape into 1 inch balls and roll in chopped almonds. Chill  several hours. Makes 30 balls. Thank you Laurie Thelen.
