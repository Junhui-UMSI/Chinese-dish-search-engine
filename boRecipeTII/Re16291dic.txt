Watermelon Gazpacho Soup
Salt and pepper, Tomatoes, Fresh watermelon juice, Scallions, Cucumber, Crostini, 
In a blender combine watermelon juice, tomatoes, cucumber, scallions. and  salt and pepper.    Serve in bowls garnished with crostini.
