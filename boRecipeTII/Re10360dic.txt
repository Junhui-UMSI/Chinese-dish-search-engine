Quick Cheesy Chicken-Corn Chowder
chicken breast, Onion, condensed cream, American cheese, Pimento, milk, Celery, Whole kernel corn  , 
1. In a saucepan combine chicken, onion, celery, and 1/4 cup water.    2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat. Cover and simmer 15 to 20 minutes.    3. Remove chicken; when cool, cut off meat and chop.    4. Return meat to broth; stir in remaining ingredients. Cook, uncovered,  for       10    minutes.
