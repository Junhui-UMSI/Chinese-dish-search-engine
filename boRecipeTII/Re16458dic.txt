Welsh Rabbit
bread, cheddar, milk, Worcestershire Sauce, American cheese, Eggs, Dry mustard, Egg, cayenne, 
For cheese sauce, in a heavy saucepan combine shredded cheese, milk,  mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and cayenne. Cook over low heat, stirring  constantly, till cheese melts. Slowly stir about half of the hot cheese  mixture into 1 beaten egg; return all to remaining mixture in pan. Cook and  stir over low heat till mixture thickens and just bubbles. Serve at once  over toast or poached eggs.  Makes 4 servings. Beer Rabbit: Prepare Welsh  Rabbit as above, except substitute beer for the milk. Top each serving with  2 slices of crisp-cooked bacon, halved crosswise.
