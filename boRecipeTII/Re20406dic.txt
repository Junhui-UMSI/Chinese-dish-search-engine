Vegan Bechamel Sauce
Cashew nuts, Cooking oil, Sea salt, Nutmeg, Unbleached flour, Soymilk, Water, White pepper, 
Blend the soymilk, water, cashews, and seasonings in a blender until  smooth. Pour into a double boiler and heat.     Meanwhile, heat the oil in a small saucepan. Add the flour to make a roux.  Then whisk it into the heated soy milk mixture to make a light, creamy  sauce. For a thicker sauce, use more roux.
