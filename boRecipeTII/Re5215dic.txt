Pilgrim Soup
Onion (about 1/2 cup),, Celery with tops, chopped, milk, Flour, Salt, Tiny whole potatoes (diced, Pepper, Paprika, Parsley, snipped, Corn, cream-style (17 oz), Bacon, diced, 
In large saucepan, fry bacon until crisp; drain. Pour all but 3 tablespoons  drippings from saucepan. Add onion and celery to drippings in pan; cook and  stir until onion is tendexture is bubbly.
