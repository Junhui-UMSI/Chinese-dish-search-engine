Baked Squash Casserole
Yellow squash, Light Whole Wheat Roll,, Brown sugar, Salt, as desired, Onion, Ener-G Egg Replacer, Green pepper, diced, Dash of pepper, 
Directions: Cut off tips of squash, cut each squash into 3-4 pieces. Drop  squash into a large saucepan with enough boiling water to cover. Return to  a boil, reduce heat and cook until tender. Mash, reserving liquid. Combine  squash and liquid with egg replacer, bread crumbs, sugar, salt, onion and  pepper.  Turn into a 3 quart casserole that has been lightly greased or  sprayed with non-stick vegetable spray. Cover with a light layer of crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees until lightly browned. Makes 10 servings. Files of  Alice in Houston, update old Family Recipe 5/11/93
