Artichoke Squares
Jars (6-oz) marinated, Salt, Sharp Cheddar cheese; grated, Oregano, Clove garlic; chopped, Fine; dried bread crumbs, Eggs, Chopped parsley, Onion, Black pepper, Tabasco, 
Drain juice from artichoke hearts and reserve the juice from one jar. Use  the reserved artichoke juice to saute the onions and garlic. Saute the  onions and garlic until limp. Chop drained artichoke hearts into small  pieces and set aside. ln a medium-sized bowl, beat eggs. Add bread crumbs  and seasonings and mix. Stir in cheese, parsley, sauteed onions and garlic.  Put into a 7x11 pan. Bake at 325? for 30 minutes, uncovered. Let cool and  cut into squares.     For hot and spicy artichoke squares, add 1 (4-oz) can green chilies,  drained and chop- ped, and 1 (4-oz) can jalape?o peppers, drained and  chopped. Follow the same direc- tions as written for artichoke squares.    NANCY COUCH LEE    (MRS. JAMES M. JR.)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
