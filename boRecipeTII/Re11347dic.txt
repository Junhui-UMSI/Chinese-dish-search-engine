Ranch-Style Turkey Kabobs
Red bell pepper, cut into, Purchased ranch salad, Zucchini, sliced 1 " thick, Turkey tenderloins, cut, 
Spray broiler pan with nonstick cooking spray.  Thread turkey cubes,  zucchini slices and bell pepper pieces onto four 12 inch metal skewers.  Brush each kabob with 1 tablespoon salad dressing. Place kabobs on  spray-coated pan.    Broil 4 to 6 inches from heat for 10 to 12 minutes or until turkey is  golden brown and no longer pink, turning once halfway through cooking.  Makes 4 servings MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Pillsbury  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Cindy Hartlin" <cjhartlin@email.msn.com>  on May 27, 1998
