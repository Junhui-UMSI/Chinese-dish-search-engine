Apricot-Sauced Baked Apples
Brown sugar, Finely snipped dried, Ground allspice, (5.5-oz) apricot nectar, Baking apples, 
Date: Mon, 10 Jun 1996 21:07:36 -0700 (PDT)    From: "Tina D. Bell" <tdbell@altair.csustan.edu>    Core apples; score sides with a fork.  Place in a 10 x 6 x 2 inch baking  dish. Fill apples with apricots. In a small saucepan, stir together nectar,  brown sugar, and allspice. Bring to boiling. Pour over apples. Bake,  covered, in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake about 30  minutes more or till apples are tender, basting occasionally with nectar  mixture.  Serve warm. Serves 4.
