Baked Garlic Bundles (Ccc)
Walnuts, finely chopped, Butter, Frozen phyllo dough, thawed, Garlic bulbs, separated, Italian bread crumbs, 
Peel garlic and set aside. Preheat oven to 350 F. Place phyllo dough on  waxed paper and cut into 2-inch-wide strips. Lay one strip of phyllo at a  time on a flat surface and brush with butter. Place one clove of garlic at  one end. Sprinkle about 1 teaspoon of walnuts along length of strip. Roll  up garlic clove and walnuts in strip, tucking in side edges as you roll.  Brush bundle with more butter. Roll in bread crumbs. Repeat with remaining  phyllo strips, garlic cloves, walnuts, butter and bread crumbs until all  but the smallest garlic cloves are used. Reserve small cloves for another  use. Place bundles on rack in shallow roasting pan.
