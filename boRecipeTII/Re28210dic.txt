Baked Fish
Seasond coating mix for fish, Fish fillets or serving, Water, Size pieces of fish, 
1. Lightly grease a shallow, 10-inch, heat-resistant, non- metallic baking  dish. 2. Empty seasoned coating mix into the plastic shaker bag. 3. Moisten  fillets with water. Shake off excess water. 4. Shake 1 or 2 fillets at a  time in the bag until evenly coated. 5. Arrange in greased baking dish. 6.  Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 7 minutes or until fish is easily flaked  with a fork. Note: If frozen fillets are used, partially defrost in  Microwave Oven and do not moisten with water.
