Tamarind-Apricot Sauce
Honey, FORMATTED BY LISA CRAWFORD, Sweetened tamarind nectar, Apricot jam, Dijon style mustard (opt), 
Bring the nectar and jam to a boil and cook until  thickened.  Stir in honey and mustard.
