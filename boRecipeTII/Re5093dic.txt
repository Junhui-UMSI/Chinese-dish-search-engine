Pico De Gallo Salsa
Tomatoes, finely chopped, Vegetable oil, Onion, finely chopped, Red wine vinegar, Finely chopped, Serrano or Jalapeno chiles,, Cups, Cloves garlic, finely, Finely chopped cilantro, 
Combine ingredients and mix well.  Allow refrigerated sauce to come to room  temperature before serving.
