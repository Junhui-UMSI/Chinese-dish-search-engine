Waites Bakery Frozen Fruit Salad Slices
Sliced Peaches, Cream cheese, Sour cream, Crushed Pineapple, Mandarin Oranges, Miniature Marshmallows, Pitted Bing Cherries, 
Mix cream cheese and sugar, add sour cream.  Fold in fruit and  marshmallows.    Return mixture to cans. Freeze. When ready to serve cut out the bottom of  the can and use the lid to push the fruit salad. Cut one inch slices as you  go. Place round slices on a lettuce covered tray and serve.
