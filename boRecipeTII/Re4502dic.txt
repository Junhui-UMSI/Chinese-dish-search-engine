Pesto Tamales
Romano cheese, FILLING-----, Garlic, Parmesan cheese, Masa Dough, olive oil, Basil, fresh, Corn husks, Pine nuts, 
To make filling: Blend basil, oil, nuts and garlic for 2 to 3 minutes in a  blender or food processor.    Add cheese and blend until smooth, about one minute. Fill and cook tamales  according to "Tamales: Basic Procedure".
