Swedish Balls
Eggs, Cocoa, Butter, Vanilla, Strong cold coffee (instant), Quick-cooking oats, uncooked, Sugar, 
Cream butter and sugar.  Blend in eggs.  Add coffee, vanilla, and cocoa.  Mix. Add oats and mix well. Chill 1 to 2 hours. Shape into one-inch balls.  Roll in sugar. Store in air tight container. Keep refrigerated.    Makes 5 dozen.    From:  Diane Horton Posted by: Karin Brewer, Cooking Echo, 9/92
