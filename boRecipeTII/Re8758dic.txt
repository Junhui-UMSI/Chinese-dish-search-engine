Poussin with Red Wine Sauce
Canned plum tomatoes,, Salt, Pearl onions, Diced carrots, Dried thyme, Pure olive oil, Diced turnips, Dry white wine, Crumbled crisp-fried bacon, Chicken Stock, Spring chickens, 12 - 16, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Heat the olive oil in a large, deep skillet.  Season the chicken halves with the salt and pepper, and brown well on both  sides before removing and placing in an ovenproof casserole. To the skillet  in which you cooked the chicken, add the onions, carrots, turnips, thyme,  and garlic and saute until the vegetables begin to wilt. Add the red wine,  plum tomatoes, and chicken stock to the vegetables, and cook 10 minutes  over medium heat. Pour the vegetable mixture over the chicken in the  overproof casserole and bake 1 1/4 hours. When the chicken has finished  baking, add the cooked, crumbled bacon to the casserole. Serve.
