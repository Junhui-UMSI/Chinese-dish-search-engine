Reasonable Crabmeat Saute (Mf)
Lump crabmeat, picked over, Carrots, grated, Finely chopped mint, Finely chopped parsley, Butter, Finely chopped chives, Fresh Lemon Juice, Finely chopped basil, White bread, thinly sliced, Grated lemon zest, Salt, Melted butter, for brushing, 
Lightly toast the bread and brush both sides with melted butter; divide  each slice in half diagonally and set on a dinner plate. Heat the butter in  a skillet. When the foaming subsides, add the carrots and saute for a  couple of minutes or until the carrots just begin to soften. Add the  crabmeat and stir for a minute just to heat up. Add the lemon zest and  juice and herbs; saute for 30 seconds and remove from heat.
