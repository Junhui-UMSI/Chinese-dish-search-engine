Tangy Tamarind Sauce
Salt, Ground cumin, Water, Jalapeno pepper, halved and, Tamarind Puree, Onions, Garlic, 
Combine all ingredients in a blender, and process until smooth. Yield: 1  cup (serving size: 1 tablespoon).    Per serving: 8 Calories; 0g Fat (3% calories from fat); 0g Protein; 2g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 68mg Sodium    NOTES : Serve this sauce with Gallo Pinto, grilled meats, chicken, or fish,  or use it as a marinade.
