Barbecue Sauce #01
Vegetable oil, Lemon; grated, including, Worcestershire, Chili powder, Brown sugar, Butter, Salt & black pepper to taste, Cayenne pepper to taste, Bottle ketchup, Onions; chopped very, Bottle A-1 sauce, Tabasco, 
Saute onion in butter and oil until tender. Add other ingredients, mix well  and cook for 30-45 minutes.    SHADDENS BARBECUE    MARVELL, AR    From the book <High Cotton Cookin>, Marvell Academy Mothers Assn, Marvell,  AR  72366, ISBN 0-918544-14-9, downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
