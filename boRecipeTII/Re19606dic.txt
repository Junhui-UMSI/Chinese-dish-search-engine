Annas Salsa
Garlic; minced, Celery; finely chopped, Salt, Roma tomatoes; fine chopped, Red cayenne pepper; to taste, Red onion; finely chopped, Serrano pepper; or to taste, Cilantro; finely chopped, Tomatoes; finely chopped, Green chili pepper;, Black pepper; to taste, Jalapeno peppers;or to taste, 
Recipe by: Ann Lyne Combine first six ingredients in a large bowl and mix  well.  Chop the peppers finely and add to large bowl. Mix salsa well. Add  the spices (I normally use about 1-2 teaspoons of the spices) and mix. Let  chill in refridgerator at least 1 hour for flavor to combine.
