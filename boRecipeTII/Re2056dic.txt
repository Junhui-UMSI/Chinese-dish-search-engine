Amaretto Cheesecake
Butter, Graham cracker crumbs, Sugar, (8 oz.) cream cheese;, Eagle Brand sweetened, Eggs, Salt, Amaretto, 
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Combine butter, crumbs and sugar. Pat firmly  in bottom of buttered 9-inch spring-form pan. In large mixing bowl beat  cheese until fluffy. Beat in Eagle Brand, eggs and salt until smooth. Stir  in Amaretto. Pour into prepared pan. Bake 50 to 55 minutes or until cake  springs back when lightly touched. Cool to room temperature. Chill. Garnish  as desired. Refrigerate leftovers.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Deb <leadfoot@alpha.ultraweb.net> on Nov  05, 1998,
