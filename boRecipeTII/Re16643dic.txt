Al Fresco Pasta
Mozzarella cheese, Basil, salt and pepper, olive oil, Tomatoes, Pasta, Onions, 
Sautee olive oil, tomatoes, onion and basil with salt and pepper to taste  until onions are translucent.    Then add pasta to skillet and saute with sauce until hot.    Take off burner and immediately add diced mozzarella    Toss and serve while hot.    Suggested Wine: Cascata Fireside Chardonnay, Riesling or Sweet Iris    Recipe by: Cascata Winery at the Professors Place    Posted to recipelu-digest by Nesb2 <Nesb2@aol.com> on Feb 27, 1998
