Pink Turkey Sandwich (By Dawn Douglas)
White bread slices, Strawberry cream cheese, Lettuce leaf, Turkey slice, 
1. Get out all of your ingredients.   2. Spread strawberry cream cheese on one slice of bread.   3. Put lettuce on other slice of bread.   4. Put turkey in between the lettuce and cream cheese.   5.
