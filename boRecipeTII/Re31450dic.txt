Barbecue Sauce
Lemon juice, Sweet pepper, chopped, Vinegar, Liquid smoke, Garlic, chopped, Mint, dried, Ketchup, Salt, Onions, chopped, Louisiana hot sauce, Lea & Perrins, Dry white wine, Parsley, dried, Honey, 
Place all ingredients in a pot that is big enough to hold  them.  Bring to a boil.  Cook, covered, on low heat for  several hours.  From Justin Wilsons "Outside Cooking with Inside Help"    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/cajun.zip
