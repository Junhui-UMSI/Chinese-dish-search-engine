Strawberries
Condensed milk, Coconut, Vanilla, (3-oz) strawberry gelatin, Finely chopped nuts, 
Add milk and vanilla to dry ingredients and stir well. Refrigerate 1 hour.  Shape into strawberries and roll in red sugar. Dip stem end into green  sugar or dye slivered almonds in green cake coloring for stems. Refrigerate  overnight. (Colored sugars may be made by putting a few drops of food  coloring in jar with sugar. Shake well. Put on waxed paper to dry.)
