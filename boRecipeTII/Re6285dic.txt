Plain Muffins (Mix)
CAKE MIX YELLOW #10, 
PAN:  12-CUP MUFFIN PAN                    TEMPERATURE:  400 F. OVEN    1.  PREPARE MUFFINS ACCORDING TO INSTRUCTIONS ON CONTAINER.    2.  FILL EACH WELL-GREASED MUFFIN CUP 2/3 FULL (1-NO. 16 SCOOP).    3.  BAKE 20 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE.  NOTE:  IN STEP 3, IF CONVECTION OVEN IS USED, BAKE AT 350F., 15 MINUTES OR  UNTIL DONE ON OPEN VENT WITH FAN TURNED OFF FIRST 5 MINUTES, THEN LOW FAN.
