Wild Rice Pilaf
White rice, Salt, Dried thyme, olive oil, Wild rice, Chopped pecans; toasted, Green seedless grapes;, Pepper, Chopped fresh thyme; or 19, 
In a large saucepan over high heat, bring 3 cups of water to a boil. Add  wild rice, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 25 mins. Stir in white  rice, cover and continue to cook for 20 mins or until rice is tender. Stir  in grapes, pecans, thyme, oil, salt and pepper. Toss to coat well.
