Barley Stuffing
Green pepper; minced, Salt to taste, Lemon juice, Pepper, Barley, Broth, Celery; minced, Garlic, Onion; minced, Dried herb of your choice, 
Saute veggies till tender. Add barley. Add the rest and bring to a boil.  Let simmer 25 min. Use to stuff chicken or whatever.    Recipe by: Annices files    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #252 by Annice Grinberg  <VSANNICE@WEIZMANN.WEIZMANN.AC.IL> on Sep 19, 97
