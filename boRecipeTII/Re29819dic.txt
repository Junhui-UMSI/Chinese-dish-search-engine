Ten Precious Soup
Vinegar, Salt, Peanut oil, Shrimp, Vegetable stock, Eggs, Sesame oil; more or less, Fish filet, Cornstarch, Sherry, Vegetable stock, Beche-de-mer, Scallion stalk, Bamboo shoots, 
1. Soak b?che-de-mer.       2. Shell and devein shrimp; cut in half if large. Slice fish fillet  against the grain. Shred bamboo shoots and b?che-de-mer. Mince scallion  stalk. Blend cornstarch and stock to a paste.       3. Heat oil very hot and stir-fry shrimp and bamboo shoots 1 minute. Add  sherry, b?che-de-mer and fish; stir-fry gently 1 minute more.       4. Add remaining stock and salt. Bring to a boil. Add cornstarch paste  and cook, stirring, until soup thickens and is smooth.       5. Stir in vinegar, sesame oil and minced scallion. Beat eggs and stir  in quickly, then remove from heat.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
