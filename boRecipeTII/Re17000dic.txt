Viennese Nut Torte
Eggs -- separated, Sugar, Grated, Cinnamon, Chocolate icing, Nuts -- ground, Bittersweet chocolate --, 
Bear yolks and sugar till light.  Add chocolate, cinnamon and nuts. Fold in  stiffly beaten whites.  Put into a tube pan and bake 45 min at 325F or till  done.    Recipe By     : My files
