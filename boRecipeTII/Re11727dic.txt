Raspberry-Pineapple-Blueberry Dessert
Pecans, Blueberry pie filling, TOPPING, Raspberry Jello, Vanilla, Crushed pineapple, sour cream, JELLO, cream cheese, 
Mix jello according to package directions; add fruit. Chill until set. Mix  topping ingredients and spread over top of jello. Chill.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
