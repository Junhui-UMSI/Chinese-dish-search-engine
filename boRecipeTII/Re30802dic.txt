Tangerine-Berry Salads
Tangerines, Blackberries, Sour cream, Blueberries, Banana, 
Slice banana into blender or food processor. Add sour cream. Cover and  blend on high speed, or process, about 1 minute or until smooth. Cut  tangerines crosswise into 1/4-inch slices. Spoon about 1/4 cup banana  mixture on individual serving plates. Top with tangerine slices,  blackberries and blueberries. 4 SERVINGS; 130 CALORIES PER SERVING.
