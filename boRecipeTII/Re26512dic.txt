Bacon Wrapped Shrimp
teriyaki sauce, Bacon slices, Shrimp, 
Cut bacon slices in half. Wrap bacon around shrimp. Secure each with wooden  toothpick. Placei n 9x13" baking pan. Pour on teriyaki sauce.Cover and  refrigerate for one hour. Bake at 400 F. uncovered for 20-25 minutes until  bacon is crisp.  NOTES : Can cut recipe in half or double, depending on the size of your  crowd.
