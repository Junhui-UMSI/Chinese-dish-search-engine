Wilted Waldorf Salad
Slivered almonds, Walnuts, Sugar, Caraway seed, Finely shredded red cabbage, Large tart apple, Garlic clove,pressed/minced, Salt, Cider vinegar, Salad oil, Large onion,thinly sliced, 
1. In a 10-12" frying pan over medium heat, stir nuts until lightly  browned, 8-10 minutes. Remove from pan; set aside.   2. In a bowl, mix 1/4 cup vinegar and the sugar. Core apple and cut into  1/2" cubes; stir into vinegar mixture.   3. Add oil, onion, garlic, and caraway to frying pan; stir over high heat  until onion is limp, 4-5 minutes. Add cabbage and remaining 2 tablespoons  vinegar; stir until cabbage barely wilts, about 2 minutes. Pour into a  shallow bowl. Top with apple mixture and nuts; mix. Add salt to taste.
