Tahini Milk
Vanilla (opt), Tahini (sesame seed paste), Water, ice cold, Sweetener (opt), 
Blend well in a blender. Good for baking. Yields 1 1/2 cups.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
