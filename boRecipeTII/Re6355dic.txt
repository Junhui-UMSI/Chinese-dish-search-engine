Planters Nut Crust
Sugar, Margerine, Melted**, Planters salted Peanuts,, 
Combine ground Nuts, margerine, and sugar. Press evenly into bottom and on  sides of a 9 inch pie plate. Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes. Cool  and Fill as desired.    ** Increase to 4 Tbs margerine for walnet or pecan crust.    Taken From Best Recipies From the Backs of Boxes, Bottles, Cans and Jars by  Ceil Dyer.
