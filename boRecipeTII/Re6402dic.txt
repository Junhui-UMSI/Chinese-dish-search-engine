Plum Conserve
Pitted plums cut in pieces, Seedless raisins, Pinch of salt, Sugar, Lemon with rind chopped, Walnuts, Cinnamon, 
Mix plums, lemon and salt. Boil until soft. Add sugar, raisins and cinnamon  and boil until almost jelled. Add nuts and cook five min. longer, Bottle  and seal while hot.
