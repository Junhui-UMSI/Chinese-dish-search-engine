Cabbage Salad
cabbage, Onion, Sugar, Vinegar, Salad oil, Dry mustard, Celery seed, Salt to taste, 
Layer cabbage, onions, and sugar in large bowl.  Heat oil, vinegar, mustard  and celery seed and salt.  Pour over Cabbage and sugar mixture. Marinate 3  to 4 hours in refrigerator.  Keeps for long time in refrigerator. If  desired you can also add green pepper and carrots.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Aug  24, 1998
