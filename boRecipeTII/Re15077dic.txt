Western Salad
Paprika, Pepper, Spinach, Lettuce, Boston, head, Vinegar, Garlic powder, Salad oil, Parsley, chopped, Salt, Egg, cooked, Onion, sliced, Dry mustard, 
Remove egg yolk and mash with garlic. Blend in salt, paprika, pepper and  mustard. Add salad oil, vinegar; Beat well. Chop egg white and parsley and  stir in. Combine lettuce, spinach and onion in salad bowl. Add dressing.  Toss lightly.
