Potato Soup #4
Butter, Onion, milk, -(up to), Salt, Celery salt, Flour, Potatoes, 
Cook onion with potatoes. Mash potatoes; scald milk. In saucepan, cook  flour and butter, stirring until smooth. Add 1 cup milk, salt and celery  salt. Blend well. Add remaining milk and potatoes and boil 1 minute. Thin  to proper consistency with additional hot milk. Serve hot.
