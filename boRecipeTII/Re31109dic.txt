Banana Spice Cupcakes
Egg, sugar, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Vanilla extract, bananas, Cinnamon, BANANA FROSTING, Salt, Cloves, bananas, Lemon juice, Confectioners sugar, Nutmeg, Crisco, Flour, 
Mix all ingredients together, spoon into paper lined muffin pan and bake 20  minutes at 375 degrees.    Cool completely and frost with banana frosting (following)    Sorry I didnt have the yield marked down.
