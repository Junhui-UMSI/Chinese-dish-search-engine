Pollo Loco Marinade
Worchestershire sauce, Chili sauce, Tabasco, juice of 1 lemon, Garlic powder, White vinegar, juice of 1 orange, Sugar, Vegetable oil, 
Just marinate chx. in this mixture for 4 to 24 hrs., grill 20 min. on each  side.  Serve with flour tortillas and fresh salsa. Hope you like it! FROM:     LESLIE BACKEN   (NGVP22A)
