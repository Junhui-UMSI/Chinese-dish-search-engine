Quick Orange Cake
Grated Orange Rind, Butter or margarine, Orange juice, Confectioners Suger, Eggs, ORANGE GLAZE, Grated Orange Rind, Orange Juice; (2 to 3), Vanilla, 
Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl; beat well, about 5 min. with  electric mixer.  Bake in a greased and floured 9-inch square baking pan 350  for about 30 min.    Combine ingredients and drizzle over warm cake.  NOTES : This recipe come from an old  "A&P Cookbook       and Shopping Guide".
