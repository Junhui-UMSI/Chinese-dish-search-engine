Teriyaki Chicken
Monosodium glutamate (opt), Sugar, Or cooking sherry, Sake (Japanese rice wine), Salt, Vegetable oil, Grated fresh ginger root, Broiler-fryer chicken,, Soy sauce, 
1. In a large bowl combine all ingredients. Marinate chicken pieces 3 to 4  hours. 2. Place chicken skin-side-down in a deep, 3-quart, heat- resistant,  non-metallic baking dish and heat in Microwave Oven 12 minutes. 3. Turn  chicken skin-side-up and heat an additional 6 minutes or until chicken is  fork-tender. Baste occasionally with sauce.
