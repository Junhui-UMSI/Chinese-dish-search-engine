Zesty Citrus Vinaigrette
Vinegar, White wine vinegar or cider, Garlic, Frozen orange juice, Dried, Dried Oregano, Powdered fruit pectin, Chopped fresh oregano or 1/4, Chopped fresh parsley or 2, Sugar, Ground red pepper (cayenne), Concentrate, thawed, Salt, Water, 
Combine all ingredients in small jar with tight- fitting lid; shake to  blend well. 2/3 cup, 1 Tbsp per serving (8 calories, 0 g fat, 0 g chol, 50  mg sodium)    From: nanette.blanchard@springsboard.org (NANETTE BLANCHARD).
