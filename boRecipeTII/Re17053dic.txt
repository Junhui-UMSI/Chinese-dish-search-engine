Vieux Carre Spinach Salad
Beef Frye--cooked crisp, Salt, Vinegar, Hard cooked egg, sliced, Dry mustard, Sugar, Salad oil, Finely grated onion, Fresh spinach, Hard cooked eggs, chopped, 
Wash spinach--drain.  Chill to crisp.  Combine oil, sugar, vinegar, onion,  salt & mustard. Beat or blend in blender until dressing becomes thick and  syrupy and sugar is thoroughly dissolved.    Tear spinach in bite-sized pcs. and place in lge. salad bowl. Crumble Beef  Frye and add with chopped eggs. Pour dressing over salad and let stand abt.  1/2 hr. Toss and garnish w/egg slices.
