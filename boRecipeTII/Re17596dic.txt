Almond Jam Bars
Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Shortening, Jam, Cloves, Flour, Almond extract, Vanilla extract, milk, Sugar, Salt, Egg, 
Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder and spices. Cut shortening into  flour mixture (like pie crust). Mix egg and milk; add to flour mixture. Add  flavoring or add flavorings to egg and milk before mixing with flour  mixture. Spread 1/3 into greased 8 inch square pan. Cover with jam  (raspberry is good). Spread remaining flour mixture over jam. Just spoon on  carefully - doesnt have to be real evenly spread. Bake at 400 degrees for       25    minutes.
