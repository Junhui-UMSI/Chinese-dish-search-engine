Quiche Lorraine #4
Sour cream, onions, Unbaked pie shell, Bacon, Swiss cheese, Eggs, Salt and pepper to taste, parsley, 
Bake pie shell, final 10 minutes covered with foil to keep from browning.  Sprinkle ham or bacon, sauted onions and cheese over the crust. Mix  together the sour cream, eggs, salt and pepper, and parsley. Pour egg  mixture over the ingredients in the pie crust. Bake in a 350 degree oven  until set but not dry, about 20 minutes.
