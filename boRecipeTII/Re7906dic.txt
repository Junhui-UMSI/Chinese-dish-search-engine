Post Thanksgiving Enchiladas
FILLING, Garlic, Chopped green onion, Chicken broth, Vegetable oil, Avocado for garnish, Cumin, Diced, cooked turkey, Sour cream or plain yogurt, SAUCE, Chili powder, Shredded jack cheese, Corn tortillas, Oil, extra cheese, Tomato sauce, Dried green chilies (4oz), Salt, if desired, Onions, 
In a bowl, combine the turkey, green onion, cheese, chilies and sour cream  or yogurt - set aside.  In a skillet or sauce pan, saute the onion in the  oil until barely softened. Stir in garlic. Cook for one minute. Add chili  powder, tomato sauce, broth, cumin and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring.  Remove from heat.    Fry tortillas in oil but just until soft-not crisp. Dip in the sauce.  Smooth some filling on each tortilla and roll up. Place seam side down in a  baking dish.  Repeat with remaining tortillas.  Spoon remaining sauce over,  sprinkle with extra cheese.    Heat at 375f for 10-15 minutes.  Garnish with avocado and serve
