Pear Cookies
Sour milk, Flour, Mace, Brown sugar, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Dried pears, Eggs, well beaten, Salt, Shortening, 
Wash pears, remove cores, and chop.  Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs  and milk.  Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder, baking soda,  salt, and mace. Add pears. Mix thoroughly. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto  well-oiled baking sheet.  Bake in hot oven (410 F) 12-15 minutes. 20  servings. The Household Searchlight
