Lasagna Filling
(8oz each) tomato sauce, Garlic, Fresh oregano or, Dried Oregano, Chopped frozen spinach, Shreaded carrot, Ricotta cheese, Yogurt lowfat, Grated Parmesan cheese, Shreaded part skim mozarell`, Frozen peas, 
Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl. If making lasagna also need 9  lasagna noodles undercooked slightly. Layer like usual. Bake at 350F for 45  min then let cool on wire rack for about 15 min.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Kendig - von Fehrn"  <TikalMe@PowerOnline.net> on Dec 8, 1998
