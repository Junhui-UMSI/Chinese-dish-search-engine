Yam-Brosia
Butter or margarine, Worcestershire Sauce, Vacuum-packed Sweet, Light corn syrup, Bananas; sliced, Salt, Orange juice, Flaked coconut, 
In a 2-quart casserole, place alternate layers of potatoes and bananas; set  aside. In a small saucepan, combine remaining ingredients except coconut.  Heat until butter is melted. Pour over potato mixture. Cover, and bake in a  preheated 375` oven for 30 minutes. Remove cover; baste with syrup by  tilting casserole. Sprinkle coconut over all. Return to oven for 10 minutes  longer.    Recipe by: Source: MSN Cooking Forum    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #982 by Judi Moseley <judi@moseleygroup.com>  on Januy,, ary 04, 1998
