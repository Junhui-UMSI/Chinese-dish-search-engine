Banana Dogs
Bananas, Lemon juice, Honey, Peanut butter, Hot dog buns, 
Mix honey with peanut butter. Spread peanut butter mixture on each bun.  Peel bananas; brush with lemon juice. Divide in half. Place banana half in  each bun.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 2,  1998
