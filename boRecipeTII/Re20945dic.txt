Apple Pancakes
Apple juice concentrate,, Thawed, Green apples, cored and, Salt, Baking Powder, Whole wheat pastry flour, Grated, Lowfat soy milk, 
Mix the flour, salt and baking powder together; mix the apple juice  concentrate and soy milk separately. Add wet ingred-ients to the dry; add  the grated apples and stir gently until well blended. Spray skillet with  vegetable cooking spray and preheat until it is moderately hot. For each  pancake, use about 3 Tablespoons of batter. Cook 3 minutes on each side or  until golden brown.
