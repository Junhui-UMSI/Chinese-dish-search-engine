Venetian Panettone
Orange Peel, All-purpose flour, Sugar, Vanilla, Sliced almonds, for 1 1/2 pound loaf, Water, Egg yolks, Lemon Peel, Butter or margarine, Active dry yeast, Salt, Golden raisins, Eggs, 
1. Add ingredients to bread machine pan according to manufacturers  directions.    2. Select sweet or basic cycle.    2. When fruit and nut signal sounds (or 5 minutes before end of last  kneading), add golden raisins and sliced almonds.    3. Remove baked loaf from pan at once. Cool loaf on a rack at least 15  minutes before slicing. Serve hot, warm, or cool.
