Wine Marinated Kabobs
Salt, Salad oil, Ground beef, Fresh Large Mushrooms; *, Worstershire Sauce, Instant Minced Garlic, Burgundy Or Other Red Wine, Catsup, Marjoram Leaves, 
*     Mushrooms should be washed and trimmed.  Shape the meat by TBLS into 24 balls.  Place in a glass bowl or plastic  bag.  Add the mushrooms.  Mix the remaining ingredients together and pour  over the meatballs and mushrooms.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 8  hours, turning the meatballs and mushrooms occasionally.  On each of 4  12-inch metal skewers, alternate 6 meatballs with 6 mushrooms.  Set oven  control to broil and/or 550 degrees F.  Broil kabobs 4-inches from the  heat to the desired doneness, about 15 to 20 minutes.  Brush occasionally  with the remaining marinade and gently push with a fork to turn.
