"Baloney" Cheese Dogs
Turkey bologna, Sticks cheddar cheese or, Mayonnaise or mustard, Hot dog buns, 
Spread inside of hot dog buns with mayonnaise or mustard. Roll 1 bologna  slice around each cheese stick. Place inside hot dog bun. Wrap each bun in  plastic wrap.
