Pink Lemonade Dessert
sweetened condensed, frozen pink lemonade, frozen whipped topping, graham cracker crumbs, 
Thouroughly blend together sweetened condensed milk (not evaporated) and  lemonade concentrate. Fold in whipped topping.      Sprinkle half of the crumbs in bottom of greased 9 inch square pan. Spread  whipped mixture over crumbs. Sprinkle remaining crumbs over the top. Chill.  Makes 9 servings.
