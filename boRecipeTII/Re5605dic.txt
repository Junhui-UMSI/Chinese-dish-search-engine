Pineapple Sausage Tidbits
Cooked), Sausages (cut in half &, Cornstarch, Pineapple juice, Pineapple chunks, 
Using toothpicks, skewer sausage & pineapple. Set aside. Mix starch  with juice.  Heat in pan until thick. Place tidbits in chafing dish.  Pour sauce over tidbits.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
