Vino Margarita
Dry Chablis wine, Lime; juice of, Salt for rim of glass, Liquid margarita mix, 
Freeze Margarita mix. Place frozen mix in blender, top with wine & lime  juice; blend until frothy. Moisten rim of glass; dip in salt & pour in  Margarita wine mixture. Serve immediately.
