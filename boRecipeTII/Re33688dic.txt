Sweet and Hot Baked Beans

Date: Thu, 30 May 1996 10:22:04 -0700 (PDT)    From: "Tina D. Bell" <tdbell@altair.csustan.edu>    Heres a different bean treatment.  Preheat oven to 250 degrees. In a large  bowl combine all ingredients.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Place  beans in a greased casserole dish and cook for 6 to 9 hours. If they become  dry, add a little well seasoned stock (chicken or vegetable). Serve hot.
