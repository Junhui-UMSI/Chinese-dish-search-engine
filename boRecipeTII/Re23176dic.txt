Tuna Cashew Casserole
Celery, (6 1/2 oz) tuna, Cashews, Cream of mushroom soup, (3 ounces) chow mein, Water, Onions; minced, 
Reserve 1/2c noodles. Flake tuna and combine reamining ingredients in 1 1/2  qt casserole. Top with reserved noodles. Bake 325, 40 mins.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1040 by Carol & Bob Floyd  <c.floyd@arnprior.com> on Jan 21, 1998
