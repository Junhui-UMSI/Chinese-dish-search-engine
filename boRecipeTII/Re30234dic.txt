Banana Bread #16
-(up to), Baking Powder, Eggs, Salt, Vanilla (optional), Baking soda, Sugar, Fully ripe bananas, Butter or margarine, Flour, Sour milk (see note), 
Date: Fri, 1 Mar 96 16:45:46 EST    submitted by: friesenh@dolphin.upenn.edu  This recipe was given to me by a friends aunt.  It is great and very easy  to prepare!    NOTE: sour milk can be made by adding a little bit of lemon to regular milk    Cream sugar and butter. Add eggs and vanilla (optional) and beat well.  Mix  flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add flour mixture alternately  with mashed bananas. Add sour milk. Bake in greased 9 inch loaf pan linked  with 1 strip wax paper at 350!F for 60 to 70 minutes.
