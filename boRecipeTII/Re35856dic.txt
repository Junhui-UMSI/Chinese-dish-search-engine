Stuffed Green Peppers
Long-Grain Rice, Tomatoes, Green Peppers, Onions, Salt, Water, Dried parsley, Spaghetti Sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Dried basil, Ground beef, 
Preheat oven to 350.    Cut tops from green peppers; discard seeds and membranes. Chop enough of  the tops to make 1/4 cup, set aside. Cook the whole green peppers,  uncovered, in boiling water for 5 minutes, invert to drain well. In a  skillet, cook ground beef, onion and the 1/4 cup chopped green pepper until  meat is browned and vegetables are tender. Drain off excess fat. Add  undrained tomatoes, uncooked rice, water, salt, Worcestershire sauce, dash  pepper, parsley, and basil. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Cover and simmer  15 minutes or until rice is tender. Stuff peppers with meat mixture. Cover  tops with spaghetti sauce and bake uncovered for 30 to 35 minutes.    Recipe By     :
