Barbecue Beef
Brown sugar, Catsup, Cloves, Ground beef, Mustard, Lge Onion; chopped, Green Bell Pepper; chopped, Wesson Oil, Vinegar, Salt, 
Brown meat in oil until crumbly but not hard. Add remaining ingredients and  cover and simmer about 30 minutes. Serve on a bun.
