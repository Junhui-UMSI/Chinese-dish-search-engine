Pineapple Rollup
Baking Powder, Sugar, Sugar, Cold water, Brown sugar, Flour, Butter, Eggs, Vanilla, Crushed pineapple in juice,, Salt, 
Melt butter in jelly roll pan. Spoon pineapple over and sprinkle with brown  sugar. Beat egg whites till foamy, gradually add 1/2 cup sugar, beat till  stiff peaks. Add rest of ings. to yolks and blend. Pour over whites and  fold in. Spread over pineapple in pan.    Bake 20-25 mi,/ at 350F. Turn out immediately onto towel dusted with  powdered sugar. Roll along short edge, ending seam side down. Cool.
