Quick Chicken Taj Mahal
almonds, Ground coriander seed, Cinnamon, juice of 1/2 a lemon, Ground fenugreek, Salt and pepper, Chicken broth, Ground turmeric, Raisins, Dried apricots, Onion, Saffron threads, Vegetable oil, Skinless boneless chicken, Cayenne pepper, 
Heat the oil in a large pan. Add the chicken and the onion and stir-fry 10  minutes, the until chicken is well browned on all sides and the onion is  soft. Stir in 1 1/2 cups of the chicken broth, the raisins, dried fruit,  saffron-lemon juice mixture, turmeric, fenugreek, cinnamon, cayenne, and  coriander seed. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 5 to 10  minutes, until chicken is cooked through and flavors are married. Add salt  and pepper to taste. Garnish with the almonds.
