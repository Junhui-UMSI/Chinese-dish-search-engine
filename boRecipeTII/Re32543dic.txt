Sweet Graham Snacks
Graham crackers, Butter, Sugar, nuts, 
Line a 15x10x1-inch baking pan with graham crackers. In a saucepan, bring  butter and sugar to a boil; boil for two minutes. Remove from heat; stir in  nuts. Spoon over graham crackers. Bake at 325F for 10 minutes. Immediately  remove from pan onto foil.  Cool. Break apart.
