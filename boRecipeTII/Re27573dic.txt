The Best and Easiest French Onion Soup
Grated gruyere cheese, 10 1/2 oz. beef broth soup, Grated Parmesan cheese, Butter, Thinly sliced onions, Holland rusk slices, 
Day before: 1. Saute onion in butter in skillet until golden, not browned -  for approx. 8 minutes. 2. In saucepan, combine broth and sauteed  onion.....bring to a boil. 3. Leave saucepan in refrigerator overnight.    To Serve:    1. Reheat in saucepan. 2. Pour soup into French Onion soup bowls. 3. Cover  with rusks and dip rusks into soup. 4. Sprinkle with cheeses. 5. Broil for  1 - 2 minutes until cheese is bubbly.    Tried and true and excellent.
