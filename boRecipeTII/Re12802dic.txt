Yummies
Rice Krispies, Sugar, Egg -- beaten, Dates -- chopped, Butter or margarine, Pecans -- chopped, Salt, Coconut (or 3) -- shredded, 
Melt butter, add sugar, salt, and egg. Cook until thick. Add dates and  continue cooking about 4-5 minutes, stirring constantly. Pour over Rice  Krispies and nuts. Stir until well blended. Drop by teaspoonfuls into a  bowl of coconut. Roll ball to cover with coconut and place on waxed paper.  Cool 30 min. Store in an airtight metal container. Cookies may be rolled in  chopped pecans instead of coconut.    Source: Fay Daugherty    Recipe By     :
