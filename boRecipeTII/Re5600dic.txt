Pineapple Sauce
Lemons, Pineapple juice, Sugar, Diced pineapple, Flour, Marshmallows, diced, 
Combine flour and sugar.  Add pineapple juice, lemon juice, and grated  lemon rind.  Cook over hot water, stirring constantly, until thick. Add  pineapple and marshmallows. Mix thoroughly. Serve with pudding or frozen  desserts.    Wilma Onstott, Gering, NE.
