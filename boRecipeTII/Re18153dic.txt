Velvet Shrimp
Medium shrimp; peeled and, Green Onions, Butter or margarine, Magic Seasoning Blends, Cooked rice or pasta;, Garlic, Heavy or whipping cream, Shredded Muenster cheese, 
** or 1/2 teaspoon each salt, dry mustard, ground red pepper and basil, and  1/4 teaspoon each garlic powder and thyme    1. Heat 10-inch skillet over high heat 1 minute. Add butter. Stir in green  onions and 2 teaspoons seasoning mix; cook 1 minute. Add shrimp ad garlic;  cook, stirring occasionally, 2 minutes. Stir in cream and remaining  seasoning mix; cook 1 minute. Remove shrimp with slotted spoon.    2. Boil cream in same skillet, whisking constantly, 2 minutes. Stir in  cheese, then shrimp. Serve with hot cooked rice or pasta, if desired.    Its the cream plus the cheese that makes the sauce velvety.    Prep time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 9 minutes Degree of difficulty: Easy  Makes 4 servings.
