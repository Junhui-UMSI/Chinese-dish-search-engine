Almond Crumble
Vanilla extract, Unsalted butter, Baking Powder, Almond extract, Sugar, Eggs, milk, Flour, almonds, Salt, Allspice, Cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 350. Grease a 9 by 5 inch loaf pan. Line bottom and along  sides of pan with waxed paper. Allow 3 inches overhang of paper at top.  Sift flour, sugar, baking powder and salt together in a large bowl. Cut in  butter until mixture resembles coarse meal. Remove 1/2 cup flour mixture to  medium bowl and set aside. Stir eggs, milk ,vanilla and almond extracts  into original flour mixture. And beat until blended. Pour about 1 cup  batter into prepared pan. Add almonds, cinnamon, and allspice to reserved  flour mixture and stir well. Sprinkle 1/3 of the nut mixture over batter in  pan. Repeat layering twice, ending with nut mixture. Bake until toothpick  inserted in center comes out clean, about 70-80 minutes. Loosen sides of  loaf pan with wide spatula. Grasping waxed paper firmly, pull loaf from pan  and cool on wire rack.
