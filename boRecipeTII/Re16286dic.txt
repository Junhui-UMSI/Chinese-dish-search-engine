Watermelon Cooler
Watermelon, Scoops lemon sherbet, Juice of 1/2 lemon, 
Remove seeds from watermelon and slice into chunks. Place on cookie sheet  and freeze. Once frozen put in blender. Add sherbet and lemon juice. Blend  until smooth. Pour into cups and serve.
