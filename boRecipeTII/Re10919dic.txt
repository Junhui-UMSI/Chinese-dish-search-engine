Rabbit Stew with Oysters
Rabbit; cut up, Beef bouillon cubes, (8-oz) oysters; with liquid, Flour; salt and pepper, Potatoes; peeled and cut in, Boiling-size onions; peeled, Carrots; peeled and cut in, Bacon drippings, 
Dredge rabbit with flour, salt and pepper. Brown in drippings. Sprinkle  some extra flour with rabbit (for gravy). Slowly add boiling water,  stirring to make gravy, until rabbit is more than covered. Add bouillon.  Cover tightly and simmer 1 hour. Add vegetables and simmer until done. Add  water if needed, but the liquid should be thick. Add oysters and simmer 10  minutes.
