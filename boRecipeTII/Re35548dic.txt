Strawstacks
Butter, Cream, Brown sugar, Non-dairy liquid coffee, Coconut, 
A fudge-type candy in different form, adding nice variety to a plate of  assorted fudges.    In a saucepan mix all ingredients except coconut; cook without stirring to  238 degrees F. Remove from heat, cool 30 minutes, and beat until mixture  thickens. Add coconut, blend in well. Drop by spoonful onto waxed paper in  the shape of strawstacks. Makes about 60 pieces.
