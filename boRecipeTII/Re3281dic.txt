Pear Tomatoes Stuffed with Boursin
Yellow pear tomatoes, Cream cheese, Fresh chives, Black olives, pitted, Garlic, Fresh dill, Red pear tomatoes, Fresh basil, 
Blend cream cheese, garlic and herbs in a food processor and blend until  smooth.    Add the olives and blend again until combined.  Set aside.    Remove the top third off the tomatoes, scoop out the seeds with a tiny  spoon or narrow utensil handle. Sprinkle a little salt inside and invert  onto a towel. Fill each tomato with Boursin.    From: The Cooks Garden catalog - Spring/Summer 1990 - page 54
