Tibetan Incense (A Dough Incense)
Juniper leaf, Frankincense, Water, Sandalwood, (Juniperus virginiana), Potassium nitrate, Patchouli, Tragacanth powder; as needed, Cinnamon, + 1 tb. powdered cedarwood, 
From Sandy Maines "Herbal Incense" article in "The Herb Companion." Dec.  1992/Jan. 1993, Vol. 5, No. 2. Pg. 38. Posted by Cathy Harned.
