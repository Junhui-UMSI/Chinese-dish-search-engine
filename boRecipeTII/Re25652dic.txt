Awabi Sakami (Sweet Cooked Abalone)
Sugar, Abalone (canned), Sake, Soya sauce, Water, 
Date: Wed, 6 Mar 1996 17:23:58 -0500    From: kmeade@ids2.idsonline.com (The Meades)    Empty the can of Abalone into a small saucepan and add water. Bring to  boil, reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes. Add Sake and Sugar  and cook for another 5 minutes, stir in Soya sauce and continnue to cook  for 2-3 more minutes. Cool to room temperature, cut the abalone into 1/2"  thick slices and serve.    MC-RECIPE@MASTERCOOK.COM    MASTERCOOK RECIPES LIST SERVER    From the MasterCook recipe list.
