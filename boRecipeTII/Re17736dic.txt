Almond Syrup
Bitter almond extract, Yogurt, Almond syrup, ALMOND COOLER, 1 SERVING, Almonds, ground, Oranges; juice of, -Cold water; 3/4 c, Sugar; up to 7 cups, -Water, 
To serve, put a few tablespoons in a glass and add cold or sparkling water  and ice.    Bring water to a boil; add almonds and return to a boil. Remove from heat  and allow to cool thoroughly. Squeeze the ground almonds in several layers  of rinsed cheesecloth. Pour almond extract into a saucepan; add sugar,  bitter almond extract, and orange juice. Stir well and bring to a boil;  cook to a thick syrup consistency. Let cool completely. If syrup if not  thick enough, boil again for 5 minutes. Pour into sterile bottles, seal and  refrigerate. Can be used immediately Serve with ice water or with club  soda.    FOR 1 SERVING ALMOND COOLER: Combine a2 tb almond syrup, 2 tb yogurt and 6  oz (3/4 cup) cold water.    MAKES: 1 QUART SOURCE: _From My Grandmothers Kitchen: A Sephardic  Cookbook_ by Viviane Bicheck Miner
