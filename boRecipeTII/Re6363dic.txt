Platts "Red, White and Blue Potato Salad"
Onion, to taste; Minced, Ripe Olives; Chopped, Mayonnaise, Blue Cheese Dressing, Red Potatoes, Chunked and, Red Bell pepper; Seeded and, Strips Bacon; Cooked and, 
Toss potato, pepper, and olives with dressing made of equal parts  of mayo  and blue cheese dressing. Chill, top with crumbled bacon before serving.
