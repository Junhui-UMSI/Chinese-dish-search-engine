Tinas Pie Crust
Egg, Water, Shortening, Salt, White vinegar, All-purpose flour, 
Cut flour into shortening with salt Beat egg, add vinegar and water. Blend  ingredients. (Should not be wet OR crumbly at this time) Form into 4 equal  sized balls. Refrigerate for 1 hour! Roll that dough out...you can get this  stuff thin!!!!    NOTES : Makes four crusts    Recipe by: T.L.
