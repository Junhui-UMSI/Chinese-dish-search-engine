Strawberry-Pineapple Salsa
Fresh jalapeno, Salt to taste, Fresh cilantro, Unseasoned rice vinegar, Fresh strawberries, Sweet onion, Fresh pineapple, 
From this past Thursdays Food section in the Miami Herald to serve with  grilled foods.    In a nonreactive bowl, combine pineapple, strawberries, onion, jalapeno and  rice vinegar. Toss and allow to rest 30 minutes but not more than 1 hour.  Add cilantro and salt. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Makes 4  half-cup servings.    Per serving: 27 calories, 0.4 grams protein, 8.2 grams carbohydrate, 0.2  grams fat, 8% calories as fat, 1.3 grams fiber, 0 mg cholesterol, 1.4 mg  sodium. Posted to EAT-L Digest by "Pamela F.
