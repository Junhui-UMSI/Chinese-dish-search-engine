Plantation Plum Pudding
Raisins, seeded, Sugar, brown, Currants, Nutmeg, Bread crumbs, Sherry or brandy, Cinnamon, Cloves, ground, Almonds; coarsely chopped, Flour, Suet; finely chopped, Egg, Baking soda, Citron; finely cut, 
Sift flour on fruit and mix. Mix eggs and brown sugar, and combine with  flour-fruit mixture. Add all other ingredients, mix well. Put in four 1 lb  containers. Tie waxed paper covers on with string, and steam on a rack set  in a large pot with the water level just below the rack for 3 hours. Flame,  garnish and serve with hard sauce or sherry sauce.
