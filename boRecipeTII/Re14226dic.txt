White Rice with Fried Plantains
Ripe plantains, Hot cooked white rice, Unsalted butter, 
TOO HOT TAMALES SHOW #TH6187    Peel the plantains and cut into 1/4-inch slices on the diagonal. Melt the  butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute the plantains until  golden brown and soft, about 2 minutes per side. Heap the rice on a platter  and top with the browned plantains. Serve hot.    Yield: 6 servings for breakfast or as an accompaniment.    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 24, 1998
