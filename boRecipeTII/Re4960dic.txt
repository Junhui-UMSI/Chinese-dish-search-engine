Pickled Mushrooms #2
Pepper, Vegetable oil, Wine vinegar, Garlic, Sugar, (12-oz) fresh mushrooms, Salt, Basil leaves, Worcestershire Sauce, Dry mustard, Paprika, Tabasco, Cayenne pepper, 
Clean mushrooms with damp paper towels. Mix all ingredients together in a  large air-tight container or ziplock bag and marinate overnight.  Serves:  10 to 12.    CHILL OVERNIGHT    BARBARA HOFFMAN    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
