Baked Brie Topped with Blueberry Chutney
Brown Sugar, Brie Cheese, Blueberries, White onions, Cider vinegar, Salt, Fresh ginger root, Cornstarch, cinnamon stick, 
Cost: $ ; Preparation Time: 15 minutes    Difficulty Level: 3 ; Servings: 6-8 as an hors doevre    1. To make chutney, combine all ingredients (except brie) in a large sauce  pan. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Boil 1 minute. Remove cinnamon  stick. Cover and refrigerate 30 to 45 minutes.    2. Preheat oven to 350F. Bake brie on an ungreased cookie sheet for 10 to  12 minutes, or until soft. Place brie on a serving plate and top with the  chilled chutney. Serve with a very mild-flavored cracker or thin slices of  slightly toasted French bread.
