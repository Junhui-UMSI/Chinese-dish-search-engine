Baked Stuff Pattypan Squash
olive oil, Fresh bread crumbs, Onion, Pepper, Chopped parsley, Summer squash, 
Set oven temp to 350F. Cut squash in half, horizonally and scoop out the  seeds. Chop onion. Crumble sausage and cook until no longer pink. Remove  from the pan with a slotted spoon and set aside. Add onion to fat in pan  and cook till soft, 2 mins. Combine sausage, onion, parsley, bread crumbs,  pepper and 2 tablespoons water. Divide mixture among squash halves. Brush  tops of the stuffing and squash with oil. Pour 1/2-inch water in a large  baking pan. Put squash in pan, cover and bake 30 minutes. Uncover and bake  until squash are tender and stufing is browned, about 15 mins.
