Randy Robinsons Chili
Ground cumin, Chili powder, (8 oz.) tomato sauce, Mild New Mexico chili powder, Granulated garlic, Cooking oil, (14 1/2 oz.) beef broth, Tabasco sauce, Chilis, drained, Jalapeno chili, chopped, PHASE 11, Ground pork, Granulated garlic, Chili powder, Ground cumin, Onions, (14 1/2 oz.@) chicken broth, Beef boneless round steak,, (4 oz.) chopped mild green, Cut up or coarse ground, Black pepper, PHASE 1, (8 oz.) tomato sauce, All-purpose flour, Brown sugar, 
Coat beef with flour. Heat oil in Dutch oven over medium heat. Cook beef  and ground pork in oil, stirring occasionally, until beef is brown; drain.  Stir in remaining ingedients of Phase 1. Heat to boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 1 1/2 hours. Uncover; stir in all ingredients of Phase 11.  Simmer uncovered about 45 minutes or until beef is tender.    FROM BETTY CROCKER INTERNATIONAL CHILI SOCIETY 1991 $25,000 WINNER.
