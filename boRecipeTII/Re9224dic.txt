Prysnac Serbia (Broccoli Casserole)
Cottage cheese, small curd, American cheese; diced, Salt, Butter, Eggs; unbeaten, Flour, Scallion; chopped, Broccoli; frozen chopped, 
Quickly thaw frozen broccoli by placing in a colander ar holding under hot  running water. Separate the pieces with fork; drain well. Combine broccoli  with all remaining ingred ents in a large bowl and beat until well blended.  Pour into greased crockpot. Cover and cook on High 1 hour, then on Low 2 to        3    hours.
