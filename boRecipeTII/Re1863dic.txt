Chicken-Lickin Nuggets
Cornmeal, Chili powder, Ground cumin, Salt, Boneless; skinless chicken, Vegetable oil; (up to 4), 
1.In a large mixing bowl, combine cornmeal, chili powder, cumin, and salt.  Add chicken and coat thoroughly.    2. In a large skillet, heat oil. Add chicken and cook 7 to 9 minutes,  turning once, until crisp. Drain on paper towels.    Makes 20 to 30 pieces.
