Pineapple Breakfast Bread
All-purpose flour, crushed Pineapple, Salt, Butter or margarine, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Buttermilk, sugar, 
Preparation : In mixing bowl, sift together flour, sugar, baking powder,  baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Cut in butter until coarse crumbs. Add  undrained pineapple and buttermilk, mix until it holds together. Turn dough  out onto lightly floured surface.  Knead for 2 minutes. Shape dough into a  6-inch circle, transfer dough to an 8-inch greased round cake pan. Score  the top in quarters.  Bake at 375 degrees for 30-35 minutes. Serve warm  with pineapple cream cheese spread.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #255      Date: Tue, 17 Sep 1996 21:52:18 -0500      From: pickell@cyberspc.mb.ca (S.Pickell)
