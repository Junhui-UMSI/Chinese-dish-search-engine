Yellow Perch Club Sandwiches
Salt, Tomatoes, Pepper, Yellow perch fillets, Buttered toast, Lettuce leaves, Boiling water, Salt, Hard-cooked eggs; sliced, Sandwich spread; divided, 
Skin fillets and place in boiling salted water. Cover and return to the  boiling point; simmer for 10 minutes or until fish flakes easily when  tested with a fork. Drain and flake.    Combine flaked fish, 1/2 cup sandwich spread, salt and pepper.    Spread 6 slices of toast with fish mixture; arrange egg, lettuce, and  remaining 1/2 cup sandwich spread between other slices.    Fasten together with toothpicks.    Cut into quarters.    Garnish with tomato wedges.
