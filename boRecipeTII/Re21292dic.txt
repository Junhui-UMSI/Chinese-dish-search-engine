Unbaked Cake
Eagle brand milk, Flake coconut, Vanilla wafers, FRUIT, DATES, OPTIONAL-----, Pecans, 
Crunch wafers real fine & mix with pecans, Coconut & fruit. Pour milk into  mixture. Mix well, press into a pan. The harder it is pressed the better.  Cool in refrigerator, cover. Cut into squares.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
