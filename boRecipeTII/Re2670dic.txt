Peanut Butter and Jelly Cookies
Sugar, Brown Sugar; Firmly Packed, Unbleached all-purpose flour, Vanilla extract, Butter or margarine, Egg, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Salt, Jam Or Jelly, Peanut butter, 
Mix the butter, peanut butter, sugar, brown sugar, egg and vanilla  together, blending well.  Blend in the flour, baking soda, baking powder,  and salt, beating until well mixed. Cover and chill for 1 hour. Heat the  oven to 375 Degrees F.  Shape the dough into 1-inch balls and place 3  inches apart on lightly greased cookie sheets. With your thumb press deeply  into the center of each ball.  Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until set but  not hard.  Let cool.  Spoon small amounts of jam or jelly into each  thumbprint
