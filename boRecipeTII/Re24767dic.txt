Aunt Esthers Porcupines
Salt, Rice, Tomato juice, Pepper, Ground beef, Onion; minced, Poultry seasoning, Dry mustard, 
Mix all ingredients, except tomato juice, with 1/2 cup water. Form in 8  balls and brown lightly in heavy kettle. Add tomato juice; cover, and  simmer about 1 1/2 hours, adding a little water when necessary.    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #231 by Martha Sheppard  <marthahs@worldnet.att.net> on Sep 12, 1997
