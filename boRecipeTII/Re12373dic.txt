Zucchini Volante
Egg; separated, whipped sep., salt and pepper, White onion, Bread crumbs, Grated Parmesan cheese, To medium-sized green, Cooking oil, 
Preheat oven to 325 F. Shred zucchini and onion. Combine all ingredients  and gently fold together. Add salt and pepper to taste. Bake in a souffle  dish in preheated oven for 15 minutes or until top is just brown. Note: A  combination of green and yellow zucchini may be used. A shredded carrot may  also be added for color. The cooking oil may include some olive oil for  best results. If desired, a little butter may also be added.
