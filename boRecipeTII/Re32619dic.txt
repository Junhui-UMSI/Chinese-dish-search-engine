Sweet Onion Shrimp Balls
Sweet onion, Shrimp, salt and pepper, Egg, Potato, 
oil for frying    Grind onions, potatoes and shrimp.  Stir in eggs, salt, and pepper. Batter  must be thick.  Drop by teaspoons into hot oil and fry until brown.    Source: Rock Springs Sweet Onion Festival Cook Book 1992
