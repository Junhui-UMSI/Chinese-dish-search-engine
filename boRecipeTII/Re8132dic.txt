Potato Chip Cookie
Softened butter or margarine, Sifted powdered sugar, All purpose flour, Finely chopped pecans, Crushed fresh potato chips, Vanilla, 
Combine butter and the 1 cup powdered sugar in a large mixing bowl. Beat  with an electric mixer on medium speed for 3 minutes. Add egg yolks and  vanilla and beat until well mixed. Gradually beat in the flout. Stir in  potato chips and pecans with a wooden spoon.    Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake in a 350  degree oven for 10 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack. While cookies are  still warm sift with powdered sugar. Cool completely. Store in airtight  containers. Makes about 40    Posted to FOODWINE Digest 24 Jan 97 by Don Barry  <donbarry@sweden-c.it.earthlink.net> on Jan 23, 1997.
