Truckers Breakfast
cheddar cheese, frozen hash browned, Bulk pork sausage, Eggs, 
Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan. Layer sausage on the bottom, then hash  browns, then pour eggs evenly over all.    Bake at 400? F for 40 minutes. If desired, after baking 30 minutes, remove  from oven and sprinkle cheese on top, then return to the oven for the last  10 minutes. Can be made ahead of time and refrigerated until time to bake.
