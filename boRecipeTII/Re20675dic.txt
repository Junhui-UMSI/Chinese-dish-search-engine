Apple Crisp #07
Sugar, Cinnamon, -(up to), Flour, Largish green or cooking, Lemon juice, Ground cloves, Water, Stick butter; not melted, 
From: bankler@sheltie.ece.cmu.edu (Brian Bankler)    Date: 4 May 1994 14:42:36 GMT    Grease casserole dish.  Peel and slice apples and put in dish (fill to  below rim of dish). Combine sugar, flour, butter and crumble together with  fork. On top of apples sprinkle cinnamon, cloves, water and lemon juice.  Cover with topping. Bake 30 minutes at 375F.
