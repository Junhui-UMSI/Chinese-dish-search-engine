Tu Bishvat Fruitcake
Sugar, Vegetable oil, Flour, Baking Powder, Baking soda., Eggs, Salt, Dried fruits (figs, dates,, Ground lemon peal, Peanuts or pecans ground to, Orange & lemon juice (or, 
Source: cakes for all seasons by nira scheuer    1. Preheat the oven to 170C / 325F.    2. Put sugar, eggs & salt in a bowl and beat until you get a white creamy  mixture.    3. Pour the oil slowly and steady into the mix while continuing to beat it.    4. Mix in a separate bowl the flour, baking powder, baking soda, dried  ruits, ground lemon peal and the peanuts or pecans. Add gradually the dry  mix and the juice. continue to stir only until you get a smooth and even  batter.    5. Pour batter into a 26cm./10.5" buttered pan and bake in oven for 65-75  min.
