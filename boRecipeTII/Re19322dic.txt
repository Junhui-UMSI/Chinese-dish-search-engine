Vegetable Stock 3
Leek, Peppercorns, Stalks celery, Mushrooms (all cut in, Bay leaf, Peeled cloves garlic, Potato, Onion, Cold water, Handful of fresh parsley, Seeded tomatoes, Carrots, Dried thyme, 
In a large pot combine 1 onion, 2 carrots, 2 stalks celery, 1 leek, 1  potato, 2 seeded tomatoes and 1/4 lb. mushrooms (all cut in chunks). Add 3  qt. cold water, 1 bay leaf, 1/2 teas. dried thyme, handful of fresh  parsley, 4 peeled cloves garlic, and 6 peppercorns. Bring to a boil, then  remove any scum. Cover and simmer gently for 1 hour. Strain. This stock  freezes well.
