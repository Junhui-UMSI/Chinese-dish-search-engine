Tuna Salad
tuna, canned, water-packed, Celery, Green onions, Lemon juice, mayonnaise, low-calorie, 
Combine all ingredients and toss slightly before serving. Serve on a  bed of shredded lettuce.  Serves 6. (From Dr. Richard Berger.)    Nutrients per serving:  Calories 131, Fat 4 g, Cholesterol 35 mg,  Carbohydrate 1 g, Sodium 413 mg.    Exchanges:  Meat 1, Fat 1.    Source:  "There IS Life after Lettuce" by Pepper Durcholz, Alberta  Gentry, and Carolyn Williamson, M.S.    Formatted for Meal-Master by Joyce Burton.
