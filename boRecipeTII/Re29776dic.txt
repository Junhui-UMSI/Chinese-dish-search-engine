Tempt Me Truffles
Vanilla, Red and green colored sugar, Powdered sugar, Cream cheese, Powdered sugar, Cocoa, Unsweetened chocolate;, 
Beat cream cheese and powdered sugar until light and fluffy. Add melted  chocolate and vanilla; mix well. Chill for several hours. Shape into 1-inch  balls. Roll in cocoa, powdered sugar, chopped nuts, or colored sugar to  coat; chill. 48 servings.
