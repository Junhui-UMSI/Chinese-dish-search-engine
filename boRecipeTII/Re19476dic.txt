Vegetarian Mulligatawny
Red chili, Salt to taste, Caroot, Ghee, Onion, Coriander, coconut, Green bell pepper, Lemon juice, Cayenne pepper, Tomato, Vegetable stock, Potato, Turmeric, Coconut milk, Cilantro, 
1. Soak, rinse & cook chick peas.  Cook for 45-60 minutes till soft,  :    depending on age of the peas. 2. In a soup pot, saute the onions in  the ghee for 5 minutes.  Add  :    chili, cayenne, turmeric & coriander.  Saute for 2 to 3 minutes,  :    stirring.  Add the stock & the vegetables.  Simmer for 10 to 15  :    minutes.  Add the coconut, coconut milk, chick peas & cook for a  :    further 5 minutes. 3. Remove from heat & let cool for a few minutes.  Blend well.  Return  :    to pot & add lemon juice & cilantro.  The longer this soup sits,  :    the better its flavour.
