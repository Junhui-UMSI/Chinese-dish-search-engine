Peanut Butter Fudge
Chopped peanuts, or other, Peanut butter, Light corn syrup, Soft butter or margarine, Vanilla, Salt, 
Servings: 2 pounds    DIRECTIONS: Mix well: peanut butter, butter, corn syrup, vanilla and salt.  Gradually stir in sugar. Knead until smooth. Gradually add nuts. Pack into  an 8x8x2" pan. When firm, cut in squares.    Source: Moms old magazine clippings- 1940s to 1970s    From: Sallie Austin Submitted By Z@FYBITS.COM (Z PEGASUS) On TUE, 27 JUN  1995 034101 GMT
