Tropical Green Slaw
Salt, Garlic, Green mangoes or papayas, Red bell pepper; seeded and, Jalapeno or serrano chile;, Fresh lime juice, Carrot; coarsely grated, Roasted peanuts; crushed, Pineapple juice, Sugar, 
Peel the mangoes and cut the flesh away from the pit. (If using papaya,  peel, cut in half and remove the seeds with a spoon.) Using a food  processor with the shredding attachment, shred the mango or papaya.  Alternatively, the mango or papaya can be finely julienned using a sharp  knife. In a large bowl combine the mango, carrot, red bell pepper, chile,  and garlic. In a small bowl combine the lime juice, pineapple juice and  sugar and pour over the mango mixture, tossing to combine. Season to taste  with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with roasted peanuts.
