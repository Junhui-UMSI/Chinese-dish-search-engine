Pecos River Burger Topping
See below, 
Two minutes before burgers are done, top with 1 oz slice Swiss,  Cheddar or Jack cheese, 2 crisp cooked bacon strips & a fresh peeled  & grilled Poblano chili or whole canned green chili.
