Arizona Forest Fire Chili (Corr)
(#10) kidney beans, (#10) tomato; diced, Jalpeno; canned; sliced, Oregano, Beef; ground, Black pepper, Celery; sliced, (#2) green chilies; diced, Cumin, Red pepper; ground, Garlic chopped, (#10) red beans, Chili powder, (#10) tomato crushed, Parsley flakes, Salt, Onion; medium dice, Green pepper; diced, White pepper, 
Combine red beans, kidney beans, diced tomato, crushed tomato, green  chilies, in large pot    (DO NOT DRAIN CANS). Add jalpeno, chili powder, chopped garlic, parsley  flakes, oregano, cumin. Brown the beef and add to the beans. Brown the  onions and celery in grease from the meat then add them to the rest. Add  the salt, black pepper, white pepper, red pepper.    This is a thick chili.    Simmer for 2 - 4 hours. Serve or refrigerate. This chili will age well.    created 3-19-1998  Contributor: Chef Keith W.
