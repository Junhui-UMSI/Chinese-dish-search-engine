Baked Chicken and Potatoes
Grated Parmesan cheese, Parsley, Basil, Vegetable oil, salt and pepper, fryer chicken, Crushed tomatoes, Potatoes, Garlic, 
Wash chicken and wipe dry with paper toweling.  Put enough oil in a large  roasting pan to cover bottom of the pan. Place chicken in p;an and place  potatoes between the chicken pieces.    Sprinkle tomatoes, parsley, basil, garlic, salt and pepper over the chicken  and potatoes.  Lastly, sprinkle with the grated cheese and the oil.    Bake in 375F oven for 1 1/4 hours, or until chicken is done. Yield: 4  servings.
