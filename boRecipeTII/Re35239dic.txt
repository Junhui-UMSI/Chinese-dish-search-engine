Beef and Noodles W/sour Cream And Paprika
Beef bouillon cubes, Chopped parsley, Sour Cream, Ground beef, Onion, minced, Uncooked wide noodles, Water, Butter, Lemon, Paprika, Garlic, salt and pepper, Mushrooms, sliced, Dry Red Wine, 
SOURCE: Natl Cooking Echo 22 Jun 90    Contributed to the echo by: Janice Norman    Beef and Noodles with Sour Cream and Paprika    Melt butter; add beef, onion, garlic, and mushrooms. Saute until meat  loses red color and onion tender, stirring with fork to break up the meat.  Add wine, lemon juice, bouillon cubes and water. Simmer uncovered for 10  minutes.
