Aunt Ruths Famous Fudge
Butter; (or margarine), (12 oz.) chocolate chips, Sugar, Vanilla, Pecans, Evaporated milk, Marshmallows, 
In large saucepan melt sugar, milk and butter together to soft boil stage.  Remove from heat and add chocolate chips and marshmallows. Add vanilla and  chopped nuts. Pour into buttered 8 or 9 inch square pan and let harden.  Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Feb 16, 1998
