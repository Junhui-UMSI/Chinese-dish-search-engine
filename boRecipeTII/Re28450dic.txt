Baked Omelette
Butter, Pepper, Mustard, Cheese, Potatoes, Salt, Egg, 
Boil and mash potatoes, mix potatoes, grated cheese, beaten egg and  butter together, season with mustard, pepper and salt to taste. If too  stiff add a little milk. Pour into a buttered dish and bake until  light brown (about 15 minutes in quick oven).
