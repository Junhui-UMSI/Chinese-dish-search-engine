Banana Gelatin
Water, Sugar, Yellow food coloring; more, Agar-agar, Evaporated milk, Banana extract, 
1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add sugar and agar-agar and cook,  stirring, until the mixture begins to thicken.    2. Stir in evaporated milk, banana extract and food coloring. Remove pan  from heat.    3. Pour mixture into a shallow rectangular pan. Let cool; then refrigerate  until set. Cut in 1-inch squares.    4. Serve in individual dessert dishes, either plain or with Chinese  preserved fruits in syrup.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4.
