Swiss Venison
Venison chops -or-, Tomatoes, Cooking oil, Venison rounds, Salt and pepper, Flour, Onion, Water, 
Salt, pepper and flour the meat.  Brown sides in cooking oil. Add onion  slices, tomatoes and water. Simmer about 45 minutes or until tender. Serve  with rice.
