Vegan Eggnog
vanilla soymilk, silken tofu, vanilla extract, Sugar, Brown sugar, rum, Nutmeg, Ground tumeric, 
In a blender or food processor, combine all ingredients except nutmeg;  blend thoroughly stopping occasionally to scrape down sides. Serve well  chilled and dusted with nutmeg.
