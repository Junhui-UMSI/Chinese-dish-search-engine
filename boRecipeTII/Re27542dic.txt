Thanksgiving Pie
Pie crust (deep dish),, Butter, Sugar, Vanilla, Pumpkin, Corn syrup, dark, Pecan halves, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Beat eggs.  Add other ingredients except  pecans and beat well.  Put pecans in bottom of pie crust and slowly pour  egg mixture over nuts. Bake 45 minutes, or until knife inserted one inch  from edges comes out clean. Let pie cool (if cut warm, the pie will be  runny) Serve with whipped cream.    NOTES:    *  A rich pie with pumpkin and pecans -- Ive been cooking this recipe for  a few years, and my people love it. It combines the best of the flavors of  pumpkin and pecan.
