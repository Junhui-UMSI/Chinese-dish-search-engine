White Chocolate Nut Cookies
Salt, Vanilla, Nuts, coarsely chopped, milk, Butter Flavor Crisco, Baking soda, Sugar, granulated, Flour, all purpose, White Chocolate baking bar, Brown sugar, firmly packed, Egg, 
Preparation Time: 20 Minutes               Bake Time: 8 Minutes    1. Heat oven to 375 F.    2. Cream Butter Flavor Crisco and milk in a large bowl at medium  speed of electric mixer until well blended. Add brown and granulated  sugar gradually. Beat in egg and vanilla.    3. Combine flour, baking soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Mix  well. Stir in white chocolate and nuts. Drop level measuring  tablespoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheet.    4. Bake at 375 for 8 minutes. Cool on baking sheet one minute. Remove  to cooling rack.    Makes 5 dozen 2 inch cookies.    Source: Butter Flavor Crisco Cookie Collection, page 27.  Shared by: David Knight    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcrisco.zip
