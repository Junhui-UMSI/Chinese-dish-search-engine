Winter Ham Soup W Blue Cheese
Butter, Water, Salt, Pepper; white, Onion, Frozen Peas, Potatoes, Marjoram; dried, milk, Blue cheese, Ham, Flour, 
In a large saucepan, melt the butter. Add the onions, potatoes, and  water. Cover and cook over low heat until the potatoes are tender, about 20  mins. Stir in the flour and slowly add the milk. Cook until thickened. Stir  in the peas, ham, marjoram, salt and pepper. Cook until the peas are  tender. Garnish with blue cheese.
