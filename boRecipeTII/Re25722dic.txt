Baba De Mo=e7a ( Egg Coconut Custard Cream )
Egg yolks, Sugar, Coconut cream or coconut, Water, A few drops vanilla, 
Stir sugar and water over medium heat until sugar is dissolved. Let cook to  make a thick syrup. Pass the yolks through a sieve. Blend yolks and coconut  cream and stir into syrup. Cook over a double boiler until it coats the  spoon. Let cool. Chill. Serve in individual bowls or on top of ice cream.    NOTES : This tastes great with ice-cream or as filling for walnut cake.
