Avocado and Crab
Avocado, Crab meat, Clove garlic -- crushed, Paprika, Lemon juice, Sea salt, Black pepper -- to taste, Spring onions -- to garnish, 
Mix together the lemon juice, garlic and seasonings.  Peel and remove the  stone from the avocado and mash the flesh with the lemon mixture. Mix in  the flaked crab meat, with a fork.  Garnish with chopped spring onions.    Recipe By     : Caribbean and African Cookery, by Rosamund Grant    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
