Candy Tree
( 13 oz ) minature candy, 12 inch styrofoam cone, Foil, (18 oz) gumdrops., 
1. Decorate candy bar miniatures as desired with decorator frosting. Let  stand about 30 minutes or until frosting is set.    2. Wrap cone in foil to cover. Attach candy bars and gumdrops to cone with  decorator frosting.    Makes 1 candy tree.    source: pillsbury festive holiday cooking    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by PMD1011@aol.com on Oct 31, 1998, converted by  MM_Buster v2.0l.
