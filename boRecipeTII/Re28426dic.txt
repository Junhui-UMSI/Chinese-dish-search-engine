Baked Mexican Rice
Onion, Garlic, olive oil, Rice, Salt, Black Pepper, Chilli powder, button mushrooms, Chorizo sausage, chicken broth, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Directions:    Saute the onion and garlic in the oil until soft.  Add the rice and  seasonings and brown th rice lightly.  Add the mushrooms, sausage and  enough boiling stock to come 1 inch above rice. Cover with tight lid and  bake in a 350 oven until liquid is completely absorbed about 30 to 35  minutes.
