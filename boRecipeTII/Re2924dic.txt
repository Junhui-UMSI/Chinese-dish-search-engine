Peanut Butter Milk Shake
TEXT, 
Place 1/4 cup peanut butter, 3 tablespoons sugar, dash of salt and 1/4 cup  sweet milk in a bowl and whip with egg beater until smooth. Add 1 3/4 cup  milk and beat until blended. Add 1/4 teaspoon vanilla.
