Apple Peanut Crumble
Butter -- softened, Nutmeg, Peanut butter, Sliced, Cinnamon, Cooking apples -- peeled and, Brown sugar -- packed, Flour, Quick-cooking rolled oats, 
Place apple slices in slow-cooking pot.  In medium bowl, combine sugar,  flour, oats, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Mix in butter and peanut butter with  pastry blender or fork.  Sprinkle over apples.  Cover pot and cook on low  for 5 to 6 hours.  Serve warm; plain, with ice cream or whipped cream.
