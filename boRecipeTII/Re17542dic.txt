Almond Crecents
Sugar, Vanilla extract, Flour; unbleached, Almond extract, Almonds; ground, Butter or margarine, 
Beat together butter and sugar until very light and fluffy.  Blend in  extracts.  Mix in flour and almonds.  Using about 1 T of dough for each,  shape into logs and bend into crecents.  Place on greased cookie sheet.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes at 350 degrees F until light brown. While warm, roll  crecents in confectioners sugar.  Cool on racks and store in a tightly  sealed container.
