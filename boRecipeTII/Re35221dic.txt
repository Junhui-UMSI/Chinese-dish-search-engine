Beef and Fruit Kabobs
Ground red pepper, Plain yogurt, Sirloin steak; boneless, cut, BILLS200896, Curry powder, Ripe mango; peel, cut into, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
Partailly freeze beef to firm; slice into 1/8" thick strips. Combine  yogurt, lemon juice, curry and red pepper. Place beef strips and marinade  in plastic bag, turning to coat. Close bag securely and marinate in  refrigerator 30 minutes, turning occasionally. Meanwhile, soak 12" bamboo  skewers in water. Peel mango and cut into 3/4" pieces. Remove beef from  amrinade; discard marinade. Thread equal amount of beef strips (weaving  back and forth) and mango pieces on each skewer. Place kabobs on grid over  medium coals. Grill 4-5 minutes, turning once. Season with salt to taste.    NOTE: Peaches, nectarines, plums or pineapple may be substituted for mango.  Peel and cut fruit into 3/4" pieces. Or, if doubling the recipe, try using  different fruits.
