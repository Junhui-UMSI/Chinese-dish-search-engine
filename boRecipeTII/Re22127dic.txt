Turkey Mexican
Cheddar cheese, Barbecued Breast of turkey, Sour cream, To 8 oz jar salsa, Sliced ripe olives, 
Source: MAINPOUL.ZIP    fresh parsley    Cut turkey into eight 3/4" slices. Place turkey in  large skillet, overlapping slices. Add salsa. Bring to  a boil; turn down heat. Cover. Simmer 10 minutes. Add  cheese and olives. Cover. Heat one minute more until  cheese melts. Place turkey on serving platter. Garnish  with sour cream and parsley. Makes 4 servings.
