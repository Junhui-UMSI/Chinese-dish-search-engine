Wacky Cake
Sugar, Unbleached all-purpose flour, Vegetable oil, Cold water, Cocoa, Vinegar, Vanilla, Salt, Baking soda, 
Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, soda and salt.  Make three wells in the flour  mixture.  In one put vanilla; in another the vinegar, and in the third the  oil.  Pour 1 c cold water over all and stir.  No need to beat.  Pour into  8 x 8-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees F oven until it springs back.    From "Trail Bosss Cowboy Cookbook" by Society of Range Management.
