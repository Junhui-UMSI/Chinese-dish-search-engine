Artichoke Spinach Dip
salt and pepper, Lemon Rind, Mayonnaise, Artichoke hearts; drained, Lemon juice, Tabasco; to taste, Bag (10-oz) spinach, 
Date: Thu, 7 Mar 1996 19:01:18 -0500    From: beck4@pipeline.com (Eileen & Bob Holze)    Chop artichoke hearts in food processor fitted with steel blade. Set aside.  Wash spinach well.  Blanch in boiling water; drain; squeeze dry and chop in  food processor. Add chopped artichoke hearts and remaining ingredients and  process just until blended.  Prepare a day in advance. Serve with vegetable  sticks.  Yield 2 1/2 cups.    MC-RECIPE@MASTERCOOK.COM    MASTERCOOK RECIPES LIST SERVER    From the MasterCook recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
