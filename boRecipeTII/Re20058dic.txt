Vanilla Cordial
Granulated sugar, Light rum, Vanilla bean, Brown Sugar, Water, 
In a saucepan, combine water and the sugars and vanilla bean. Bring to a  boil. Boil 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and let cool.    Add rum I vanilla extract (if used). Pour into 2 quart bottles with tight  fitting covers. Let stand 2 weeks, shaking occasionally. Remove vanilla  bean, if used before serving.    Makes 7 cups liquid.
