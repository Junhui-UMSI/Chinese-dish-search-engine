Lemon Mousse
Fresh Lemon Juice, Whipping cream, Lemon extract, Yellow food coloring; up to, 
Put all ing into a bowl and whip as if you were whipping the cream for a  topping. All the ing. come together to form a thick mousse. Put into  serving dishes or glasses and chill 1 -2 hours and serve. Yummy and super  quick!    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by Pat Hanneman <kitpath@earthlink.net> on Sep  20, 1998,
