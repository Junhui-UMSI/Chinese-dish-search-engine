Almond Crumble Topping
All-purpose flour, nutmeg, Sugar, Unsalted butter, almonds, 
Combine flour, sugar and nutmeg in a mixing bowl; stir well to mix and stir  in sliced almonds. Stir in the butter evenly. Set aside for 5 minutes,  then, using your fingertips, break the mixture into 1/4 to 1/2 inch crumbs.  Set aside again while you prepare the filling for Blueberry Lattice Pie.
