Raspberry Cider
Fresh Cider, Raspberries, choppe, 6-ounce packages Red, Pack, Red Star Epernay Yeast, 
Toss all ingredients into a carbouy at room temperature. Put on an  air-  lock and go away. Rack after 2-3 weeks and go away again. After  another  2-3 weeks bottle and go away for a few months! Drink in the spring, Yumm!
