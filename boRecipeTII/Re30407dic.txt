Banana Fritters
Salt, Flour, Baking Powder, Oil for frying, Egg yolk, Sugar, Bananas, Powdered sugar, Whipping cream; sweetened if, milk, 
Sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar together. Beat egg yolk slightly.  Add milk.  Combine with dry ingredients and beat until smooth. Slice  bananas in half lengthwise. Dip in batter and fry in oil until crisp and  golden brown. Sprinkle with sugar and top with whipped cream. Yield: 6  servings.    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
