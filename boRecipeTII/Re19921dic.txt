Apple Bread
Margarine, Sugar, Eggs, milk, Flour, Baking soda, Vanilla, Salt, apples, TOPPING, Sugar, Flour, Butter, Cinnamon, 
Cream together margarine and sugar. Add eggs, milk, vanilla and stir. Sift together dry ingredients and add to wet ingredients in small increments.  Add apples; stir. Pour into greased loaf pan, sprinkle with topping.    To make topping - Melt butter. Combine dry ingredients. Mix well with fork until crumbly. Bake bread at 325 degrees for 1 hour or until top is firm  when pressed. Serves 8-10.
