Banana Bread
INTO A MIXING BOWL, Baking Powder, Grated), Unsweetened pineapple, Whole wheat flour, Egg whites (or equivilent), Overripe bananas, Dairy or soy nf milk, Zest of one orange (orange, Dried currants OR 1/2_cup, 8 ounce canned crushed, INTO A BLENDER/FOOD PROCESSO, Chopped dried Apricots, Part of peel, finely, Baking soda, 
Mix the wet into the dry, stir until well mixed.  Pour into a prepared or  nonstick loaf pan, bake at 350 degrees farenheit for about 40 minutes, test  with a wooden pick.  Let stand for about 5 minutes, remove from pan, cool  on a rack.    Note: I dont like to use refined sugar and this is fairly sweet, but if  you prefer a sweeter bread, add some chopped dates or 1/4 cup sugar.    Posted by jrg14@cornell.edu (Janice R.
