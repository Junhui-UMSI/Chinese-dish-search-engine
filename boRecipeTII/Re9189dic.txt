Prune Chocolate Cup Cakes
bitter chocolate, Salt, Eggs, Flour, Cream, Sugar, Vanilla, Baking Powder, prunes, 
Pit prunes and cut in pieces.  Beat eggs until thick  and creamy. Add to cream, and sugar.  Mix well.  Sift  flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt.  Add grated chocolate.  Beat thoroughly. Add prunes and  flavoring.  Mix well.  Pour into well-oiled muffin  tins. Bake in moderate oven (375 F) about 15 minutes.  If desired, use 2 teaspoons baking powder and 1/2  teaspoon baking soda with 1 1/4 cups sour cream. The  Household Searchlight
