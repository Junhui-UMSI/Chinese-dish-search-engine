Peking Jade Salad
Carrots, shredded, Salt and pepper, Mixed salad greens of choice, Snow peas, fresh, Tomato, 6-ounce chicken breast,, Black olive, Hard-boiled egg, peeled,, Alfalfa sprouts, 
coral Isle Cafe, Disneys Polynesian Resort    Lightly season chicken breast with salt and pepper. Broil chicken  approximately 5 minutes on each side, until lightly browned and cooked.  Place mixed greens on a large salad plate or bowl. Arrange carrots, snow  peas, tomato slices, black olive, and egg around sides of plate or bowl.  Slcie broiled chicken on bias and place in center of salad. Garnish with  alfalfa sprouts and serve with your choice of dressing. Makes 1 serving.
