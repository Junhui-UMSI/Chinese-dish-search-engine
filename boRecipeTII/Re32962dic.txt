Sweet Won Ton
Pecans, Brown sugar, Vegetable oil, Granulated sugar, Powdered sugar, Won ton wrappers (about 60, Egg, Flaked coconut, 
Mix nuts, coconut, brown sugar and granulated sugar. Place one teaspoon of  this mixture in the center of each won ton square. Fold into a triangle.  Moisten edges with beaten egg to seal. Deep fry in hot oil until golden  brown, turning once. Shake in a bag of powdered sugar. Cool. Store in an  air-tight container. Yield: 50.    ANN WARRICK (MRS. ALAN F.)
