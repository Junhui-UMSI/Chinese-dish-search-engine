Sift together dry ingredients.   Combine milk, egg yolks, butter and  pumpkin puree.  Stir into dry ingredients until just blended.  Beat egg  whites until stiff and fold into batter. Pour onto hot, oiled griddle,  about 1/3 cup at a time. Cook until tops bubble and turn and cook other  side.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
