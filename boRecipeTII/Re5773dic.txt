Pink Pussycat
Lemonade, Fluid oz heavy cream, Fluid oz cherry juice, Nic cherry brandy, 
Place ice cubes in a tall glass. Pour in cherry brandy, cream and  cherry juice.  Stir well with a long spoon and fill glasses with  lemonade. Garnish with cherry and serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
