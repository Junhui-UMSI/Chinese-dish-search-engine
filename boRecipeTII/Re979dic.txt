Italian Chews
Butter, Sugar, Flour (sifted), Eggs, Light brown sugar, Walnuts, chopped, Flaked coconut, 
Cream butter then add granulated sugar and beat until light. Add flour and  mix well. Put into 9"x13" baking dish and bake at 375 degrees for 15  minutes. Remove from oven and cool.   Beat egg whites until fluffy.   Beat egg yolks and then gradually beat in brown sugar, nuts, and coconut.  Fold in egg whites and then spread over top of baked cookie. Bake for 25-30  minutes and then cool. Cut into squares.    Source: The Zaney Chefs Recipe Center    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Nov 17, 1998
