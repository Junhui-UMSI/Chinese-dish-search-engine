Artichoke Dip #3
(14-oz) artichoke hearts;, Nonfat sour cream, Grated Parmesan cheese, Clove (small) garlic;, Scallions; cut in chunks, 
Date: Tue, 5 Mar 1996 12:12:51 -0500    From: TexGQ24@aol.com  Process sour cream, Parmesan cheese, scallions and garlic in a food  processor or blender until blended. Add artichokes and process just until  finely chopped. Makes 1-3/4 cups.    Per 2 tablespoons: 18 cal, 1 g pro, 2 g car, 0 g fat, 1 mg chol, 26 mg sod.
