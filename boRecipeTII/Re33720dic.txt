Sweet and Sour Bbq Sauce
Onion -- chopped, Inch fresh ginger root, Orange, olive oil, JUICE OF:, Soy sauce, Cloves -- ground, Mustard, Cloves garlic -- minced, Lime, Lemon, Flour to thicken, Spices:, Molasses, Pods, Black pepper, Red wine, Malt, Chili sauce or tabasco, Cardamon -- ground, Capers, Allspice, Nutmeg, 
Chop the onions, garlic and ginger and slowlycook in the oil until soft.  Add flour to thicken ( thin if you want to marinade). Add everything else  and cook for half an hour.
