Pecan Loaf
Sage, Bread crumbs, dry, Pecans, Soy milk, Potatoes, Salt, Onion, Non-dairy margarine, Soy flour, 
Grind nuts and bread crumbs separately in blender.     Chop onion and potatoes in soymilk in the blender. Add to nut mixture.     Add soy flour, salt and sage; mix well. Pour into greased loaf pan. Dot  the top with small pea-sized pieces of margarine. Bake at 350 degrees fro  30 minutes. Serve with brown gravy.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
