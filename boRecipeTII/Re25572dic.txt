Avocado Moulded Salad
Mayonnaise, Avocado (Ripe & Soft); diced, (1 Lb.) Crushed Pineapple;, Whipped cream, Warm water, Lime Gelatin Powder, Lemon juice, 
Dissolve jello in water and stir in 1/2 cup pineapple juice. Cool. When it  thickens, add pineapple. Salt and add lemon juice. Fold in mayonnaise,  whipped cream and avocado. Mould.
