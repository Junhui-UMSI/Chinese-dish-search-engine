Sweet and Sour Meatballs #1
Sugar, Ground lean pork, White wine, Water, Catsup, MEATBALLS, Almonds; ground fine, Cornstarch, SWEET AND SOUR SAUCE, Pineapple juice, Ginger to taste, Nutmeg to taste, Eggs, Soy sauce, Vinegar, Soy sauce, Bread crumbs, Ground round, Salt, Cloves garlic; minced fine, Cornstarch, 
Mix all meatball ingredients well; make into meatballs & roll in  cornstarch. Fry for 10 minutes. Add balls to sweet & sour sauce & simmer.  Sweet & Sour Sauce:  Combine sugar, juice, soy sauce, wine, vinegar &  catsup & bring to a boil. Mix cornstarch & water. Add to boiling  ingredients & stir until thick.
