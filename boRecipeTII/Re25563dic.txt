Avocado Green Salad
LETTUCE FRESH, CAULIFLOWER FZ, PEPPER SWT GRN FRESH, TOMATOES FRESH, LEMON FRESH, 
1.  TRIM, WASH, AND PREPARE SALAD FRUIT AND VEGETABLES AS DIRECTED ON  RECIPE  CARD MG-1.    2.  SPRINKLE AVOCADO SLICES WITH LEMON JUICE.    3.  ADD PEPPERS, CELERY, LETTUCE, AND TOMATOES; TOSS LIGHTLY.    4.  COVER; REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.    NOTE:  IN STEP 1:  8 LB 12 OZ FRESH AVOCADOS A.P. WILL YIELD 6 LB SLICED  AVOCADOS; 12 OZ LEMONS A.P. (3 LEMONS) WILL YIELD 1/2 CUP LEMON JUICE; 2 LB  7 OZ FRESH SWEET PEPPERS A.P. WILL YIELD 2 LB CHOPPED CELERY; 7 LB 9 OZ  FRESH  CELERY A.P. WILL YIELD 7 LB SHREDDED LETTUCE; 4 LB 1 OZ FRESH TOMATOES A.P.  WILL YIELD 4 LB TOMATO WEDGES.
