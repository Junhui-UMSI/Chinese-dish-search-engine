HAM SECTIONED CURED, POTATOES FRENCH FZ, EGGS SHELL, BUTTER PRINT SURE, SHORTENING; 3LB, Onions, 
TEMPERATURE:  325 F. GRIDDLE  :    1.  SAUTE COOKED DICED POTATOES, CHOPPED, CANNED HAM, AND CHOPPED ONIONS,  BUTTER OR MARGARINE.  ADD CHOPPED PARSLEY.    2.  PLACE SHELLED EGGS IN MIXER BOWL.  USING WIRE WHIP, BEAT JUST ENOUGH  TO THOROUGHLY BLEND YOLKS AND WHITES.    3.  POUR 1/3 CUP EGG MIXTURE FOR INDIVIDUAL OMELETS ON GREASED GRIDDLE.    4.  SPRINKLE MIXTURE OVER EGGS WHEN PARTIALLY SET.    5.  FOLD OMELET IN HALF OR INTO THIRDS MAKING A LONG OVAL SHAPED  OMELET.  SERVE IMMEDIATELY.  :    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 1, 6 LB 4 OZ (5-NO. 3 CYL CN) CANNED, DEHYDRATED EGG  MIX COMBINED WITH 7 1/2 QT WARM WATER MAY BE USED FOR WHOLE EGGE. SEE  RECIPE NO. A-8.    NOTE:  2.  TO SERVE ASSORTED OMELETS, PREPARE INGREDIENTS FOR DESIRED  VARIATIONS.  SET UP INGREDIENTS IN INDIVIDUAL CONTAINERS AND PLACE NEAR  GRIDDLE.
