Quick Chicken Stir-Fry
Garlic, Carrot, Green Onions, Miracle Whip, Boneless skinless chicken, Red pepper, Broccoli flowerets, Soy sauce, Vegetable oil, Ground ginger, 
Heat oil in skillet over high heat.    Cut chicken into strips, add to skillet with garlic; stir-fry 3 minutes.    Add vegetables; stir-fry an additional 3 minutes.    Reduce heat to medium; stir in Miracle Whip, soy sauce and ginger.    Simmer 1 minute.    Serve over rice.
