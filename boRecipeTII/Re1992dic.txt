Chop Suey Casserole
Butter or margerine, Ground beef, Celery, Instant minced onion, Water, Uncooked converted rice, Salt, Condensed chicken with rice, (4 oz) slice mushrooms;, Brown sugar, Soy sauce, 
Cook and stir ground beef, celery and onion in skillet until beef is brown;  drain. Stir in remaining ingredients.    OVEN METHOD: Pour into greased 2 qt. casserole dish. Cook uncovered in 350  degree oven 30 minutes; stir. Cook until rice is tender and liquid is  absorbed, about 30 minutes longer.    RANGE TOP METHOD: Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer, stirring  occasionally, until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed, about 30  minutes.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by IceTeaLuvr@aol.com on Aug 21, 1998,
