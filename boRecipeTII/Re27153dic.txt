Tilefish with Orange Grape Sauce
Salt, Fresh Lemon Juice, Boiling water, ORANGE GRAPE SAUCE, Tilefish fillets, 
Cut fish into serving sized pieces; place in well greased 10"  skillet. Add boiling water, lemon juice & salt; cover & simmer 8-10  minutes. Carefully remove fish to warm platter; serve with Orange  Grape Sauce.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
