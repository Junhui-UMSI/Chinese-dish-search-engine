Prune Spice Cake
Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Vanilla, Pecans, Vegetable oil, Buttermilk, Salt, strained Prunes , Flour, Baking soda, Sugar, Eggs, 
Mix together - Flour, Soda, Salt, Cinnamon and Nutmeg. Mix Sugar, Oil,  Eggs and Buttermilk. Add to dry ingredients and then add Prunes, Chopped  Pecans and Vanilla. Bake at 350 for approx 45 minutes in Bundt or Angel  food pan. Toothpick test, cool on rack and enjoy!       Jeff Viets
