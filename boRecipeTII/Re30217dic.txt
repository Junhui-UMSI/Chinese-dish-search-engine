Banana Bread
To 6 bananas, Butter, Pecans, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Flour, Beaten eggs, Salt, Vanilla extract, Sugar, 
Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, & salt. Cream butter &  sugar. Add eggs & vanilla. Add flour mixture alternately with bananas. Add  nuts. Bake 1 hour 20 minutes at 350 degrees in a greased & floured 9x5x3"  loaf pan. (Dont fill pan more than 2/3 full - bake extra batter in muffin  tins; or divide batter, & make 2 smaller loaves, baking about an hour, or  until browned & bread pulls away from sides of pan.)
