7 Grain - Rice and Vegetable Salad
(Quick), cooked & cooled, Cooked AM 7 Grain Cereal, Canned peas and carrots, Chopped pimientos, drained, Celery, chopped, Onion powder, Vegetable seasoning to taste, cooled, Cucumbers, diced, AM Brown Wild Rice & Herbs, Onion, Garlic powder, 
Toss ingredients together with mayonnaise or salad dressing of choice.  Chill.  Serve on a bed of alfalfa sprouts.
