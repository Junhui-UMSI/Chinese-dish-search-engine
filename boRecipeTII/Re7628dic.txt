Pork Tacos
Bell peppers, garlic, Pork, Cheddar cheese, Tomato sauce, Taco shells, Salt, Sugar, Onions, Cheddar cheese, olive oil, Stewed tomatoes, 
Preheat oven or toaster oven at 300.  Place taco shells in oven to heat  watch carefully could brown quickly .  In a skillet, heat oil.  Add  undrained tomatoes, tomato sauce, bell peppers, garlic, onion, and sugar  until softened.  Then, combine pork, 3/4 cup cheese and salt.  Spoon about  1/4 cup mixture into each taco shell.
