Rainbow Trout with Herb Stuffing
Parsley, mushrooms, onions, Dressed trout, Fresh bread cubes, Marjoram, Lemon juice, Butter, Salt, Pimento, 
Sprinkled salt over inside and outside of trout. Saute bread in 1/4 cup  butter until brown. Stir often. Ad onions and mushrooms, Cook until  mushrooms are tender. Stir in parsley, lemon juice, marjoram and dash of  salt. Mix and stuff fish with mixture. Place in greased baking dish and  bake ate 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
