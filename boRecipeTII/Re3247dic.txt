Pear Honey #2
Oranges, Lemon, Pears, Sugar, Crushed pineapple, 
Peel and core pears. Put pears, oranges and lemon through a food chopper.  Cook with sugar for 2 hours; add pineapple and cook to consistency of jam.  Put in sterilized jars; seal.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
