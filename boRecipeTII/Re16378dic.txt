Wedding Rings
Vanilla, Powdered sugar, (Generous), Flour, Finely Chopped Pecans, Butter, 
Roll into small balls. Bake. Shake, while hot, in bag of powdered sugar,  then again when cold.    Recipe By     : Scandanavian Cookbook    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
