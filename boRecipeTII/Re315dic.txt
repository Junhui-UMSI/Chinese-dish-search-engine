Lefse
Flour, Mashed potatoes, Lard or shortening, melted, Sugar, Cream, Salt, 
Combine potatoes, lard, sugar, cream and salt. Add flour. Roll out dough as  thin as possible. Bake lefse on griddle ore electric fry pan at 400  degrees. Turn frequently. Dont grease pan. Place lefse between cloths or  waxed paper to keep moist. Cut each lefse in halves or fourths. Roll up.    Serving Ideas : butter or butter & sugar & cinnamon    NOTES : (Sweden)    Recipe by: ?
