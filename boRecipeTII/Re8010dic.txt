Potato and Broccoli Curry
Clove Garlic -- minced, Ground ginger, olive oil, Broccoli -- chopped, Dry mustard, Salt, Ground red pepper, Carrot -- sliced, olive oil, Ground coriander, Ground turmeric, Curry powder, Ground cumin, Potato -- diced (unpeeled, Ok), Water, 
Fry, stirring often to coat potatoes with seasonings. When potatoes are  almost tender, add last 4 ingredients.    Cover and simmer until broccoliand carrots are tender. Serve with rice or  pasta.    Recipe By     :
