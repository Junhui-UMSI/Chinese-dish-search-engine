Trout Almandine
Sticks of butter or, Chopped almonds, Salt, Black pepper, Flour, Fillets of trout, milk, 
Dip fillets in milk. Season with salt & pepper. Coat with flour. Melt  butter or margarine in skillet and cook fillets, browning evenly on both  sides. Remove fish from skillet and saute almonds for about 10 minutes.  Serve almonds over trout fillets.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mm-trout.zip
