Anise Hyssop Sauce for Roast Lamb
Water, Coarsely chopped anise, Sugar, Cider vinegar, 
From: arielle@bonkers.taronga.com (Stephanie da Silva)    Date: Sat, 22 Apr 1995 11:28:04 GMT  In a small saucepan combine sugar, vinegar and water.  Bring to a boil. Add  chopped anise hyssop leaves and bring to a boil.  Turn off heat and let  steep for 1/2 hour. Pour through a sieve, pressing solids to extract all  the liquid. Serve with roast lamb or veal.
