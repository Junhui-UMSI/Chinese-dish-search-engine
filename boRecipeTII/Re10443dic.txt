Quick Cranberry Bread
Pecans, Salt, cranberries, Egg, Baking Powder, milk, Orange zest, Flour, Sugar, Butter, 
Sift dry ingredients; add rind. Combine egg, milk and butter. Gradually add  flour mixture. Add cranberries and nuts. Pour into greased and floured loaf  pan. Bake at 350 for 1 hour. Cool. Wrap in foil and chill before slicing.  Makes delicious sandwiches with butter or cream cheese. Delicious buttered  and toasted.
