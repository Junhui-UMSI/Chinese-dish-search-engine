Almond Butter Cutouts
Baking Powder, Salt, Sugar, Colored sugar and frosting;, Butter; no substitutes, Ground almonds, Almond extract, Egg yolks, All-purpose flour, 
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Beat in egg yolks and extract.  Combine flour, salt and baking powder; gradually add to creamed mixture.  Stir in almonds. Cover and chill at least 2 hours. Roll out on a lightly  floured surface to 3/8-inch thickness. Cut with cookiecutters; place on  ungreased baking sheets. Sprinkle with sugar if desired. Bake at 375? for  10-13 minutes or until the edges begin to brown. Cool on wire racks. Frost  if desired.
