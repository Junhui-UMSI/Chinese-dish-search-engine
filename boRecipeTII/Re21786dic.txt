Apple-Cranberry Compote
Orange peel -- grated, Cooking apples -- peeled and, Fresh cranberries, Water, Sliced, Sour Cream, Port wine, Sugar, 
Arrange apple slices and cranberries in slow-cooking pot.  Sprinkle sugar  over fruit.  Add orange peel, water and wine.  Stir to mix ingredients.  Cover and cook on low for 4 to 6 hours or until apples are tender.  Serve  warm fruits with the juices, topped with dab of sour cream.
