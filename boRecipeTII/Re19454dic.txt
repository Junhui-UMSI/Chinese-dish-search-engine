Vegetarian Hungarian Goulash
Marjoram, Potatoes, Black pepper, Garlic, Vegetable Bouillon (cube), Peas, fresh, Paprika, Water, Caraway seeds, Safflower oil, Noodles, eggless, Onions, Tomatoes, fresh, 
In a large saucepan, combine all the ingredients, except the noodles. Cook  over medium heat, covered for 30 to 40 minutes, then serve over hot  noodles.     Vegetarian Cooking for Good Health by Gary Null/MM by DEEANNE
