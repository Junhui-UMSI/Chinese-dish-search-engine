Wine Braised Lamb Shanks
New Zealand lamb shanks, Pepper, Vegetable oil, Dry white wine, Chicken bouillon, onion, Salt, garlic, Tomato paste, carrots, 
Heat oil in large heavy skillet; brown lamb on all sides. Drain off  all but 2 Tbls. fat; add carrots, onions & garlic to kettle & saute  until onion is tender. Add wine, bouillon, salt & pepper; bring to a  boil. cover, reduce heat, & simmer 1 1/2 hours. Stir in tomato paste;  simmer 30 minutes or until tender.
