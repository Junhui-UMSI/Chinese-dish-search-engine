Apricot Balls
Eagle Brand sweetened, (8-oz) dried apricots; cut, Flaked coconut, Pecans, 
Mix all together except confectioners sugar. Shape into 1-inch balls. Let  stand 2 hours until firm. Roll in confectioners sugar, if desired. Makes 4  dozen.    MRS RICKY D.
