President Eisenhowers Old-Fashioned Beef Stew (En)
Stewing beef (Prime round),, Fresh tomatoes, Small Irish potatoes, Salt, Assorted spices in a cloth, Small carrots, Pepper, Small onions, Beef stock, 
In a pot combine meat and stock. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and  simmer until meat is tender. Add vegetables and spices. Cook until  vegetables are done, strain off one cup of stock from stew, thicken  slightly with beef roux mixture (???). Pour back into stew and let simmer  until ready to serve (about one-half hour).
