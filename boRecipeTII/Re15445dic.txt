White Chocolate and Lemon Cheesecake #1
Chocolate wafer cookie, Sugar, Crust, lemon zest, Light corn syrup, Butter, GLAZE, Unsweetened chocolate, Eggs, Heavy cream, White chocolate, Lemon extract, Bittersweet chocolate, Butter, lemon juice, Cream cheese, FILLING, 
From: mark@alexr.co.uk    Date: Sun, 25 Feb 1996 00:00:24 GMT  JAMES PRINCE   (VRVF28A)    CRUST: Combine cookies and butter and press into a lightly buttered 10-inch  springform pan.    FILLING: Melt the chocolate with the cream until mixture is smooth. Cool.  Beat the cream cheese with the sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the  cooled chocolate mixture. Add the eggs one at a time; beating well. Beat in  the lemon juice, zest an extract. Pour into crust. Bake at 350 degrees for  45 to 50 minutes. Cool an then refrigerate.    GLAZE: Combine chocolates and syrup and heat until melted and smooth.  Remove from heat and add butter. Spread warm glaze over the cake and  smooth.
