Aunt Roses Fish
Salt to taste, Saucy Susan, Garlic powder to taste, Sea bass filet; 2-3 lbs, Black Pepper, 
Place sea bass on broiler tray, and season with salt pepper and garlic  powder. Place tray under broiler Broil fish until almost fully cooked  Remove fish from oven, and put a thin layer of Saucy Susan on top of sea  bass. Return sea bass to oven and broil until Saucy Susan caramelizes...do  not leave it unattended....so babysit until the desired doneness is  achieved. Then serve. Its very good and very rich in flavor.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Dave & Jackie Schnitzer"  <djms@cyberjunkie.com> on Oct 1, 1998,
