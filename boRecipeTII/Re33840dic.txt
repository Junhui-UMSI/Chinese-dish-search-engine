Sweet and Sour Pot Roast (Tyshenoe My Sladkoe I Kucloe)
Beef pot roast, Beef brisket, Suet (for browing), Vinegar, Brown sugar, Seedless raisins, Bay leaf, Onions, crushed, Catsup, Garlic clove, sliced, Stock or bouillon, 
Brown meats in suet.Add onions and garlic. Add stock and bay leaf. Cover.  Simmer for 1 hour. Add vinegar and brown sugar. cover and simmer for 1 hour  longer. Add catsup and raisins. Cover. Simmer for 30 minutes longer. Serve  with noodles or Brown rice.    sugguested menu: Sweet and sour pot roast, noodles, peas, green salad,  custard, coffee or tea.
