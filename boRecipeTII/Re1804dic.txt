Onion; sliced, Fresh mushrooms, Garlic, Dried thyme; divided, Butter or margarine, Leftover chicken, Leftover gravy, Chicken bouillon cube;, Leftover mashed potatoes, Pepper, 
> From: srasheed@juno.com (Sam M Rasheed)    Saute onion, mushrooms, garlic and 1/4 tsp thyme in butter. Stir in  chicken, gravy, bouillon, and pepper. Spoon into a greased 3 qt casserole.  Combine potatoes and remaining thyme and spoon over chicken mixture. Bake  uncovered at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Serves 6-8.
