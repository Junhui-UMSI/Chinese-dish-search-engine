Wine Biscuits
Baking Powder, Egg white; for glazing, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Flour, Burgundy wine, 
Mix all ingredients well. Roll dough pieces as thin as a pencil and about 3  inches long. Cross ends at bottom. Brush with egg white. Bake on cookie  sheet about 20 minutes at 325 degrees.    NOTES : These wine biscuits are the best I ever tasted!    Recipe by: Paula P.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 471 by Marie Smith  <craftee@sprynet.com> on Jan 7, 1998
