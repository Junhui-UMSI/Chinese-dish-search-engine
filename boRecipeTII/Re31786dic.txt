Szechuan Bean Curd (Tofu)
Clove garlic minced, Sesame oil, 85% lean ground beef, Cornstarch, Cold water, Light soy sauce, Bean curd(tofu) 1/2" cubes, Green onions  w/tops chopped, Hot oil*, Chili sauce, Red pepper flakes, Chicken broth, 
Place ground beef, green onions, & garlic in a nonstick skillet and  cook, stirring quickly, until beef is browned. Stir in chicken broth,  soy sauce, oils, and red pepper flakes. Mix the cornstarch with the  cold water. Add to the skillet. Cook stirring continously, until  sauce thickens. Gently stir in the bean curd (tofu). Continue cooking  over medium heat for 3 minutes. * Sesame oil & hot oil may be found  in Asian markets and in cooking specialty stores.
