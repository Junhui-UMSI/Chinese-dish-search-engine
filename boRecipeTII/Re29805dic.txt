Tempura Poricini with Hoisin Sauce
Salt and pepper, Oil for frying, Hoisin sauce, Egg,, beaten, Essence, Cold soda water, Flour, Finely chopped parsley, Porcini mushrooms, Cornstarch, Sesame oil, Fresh herb salad, (mild, 
Preheat the fryer. Season the Porcini with Essence. In a mixing bowl, whisk  the egg, flour, cornstarch, and soda water together. Whisk the batter until  smooth. Season the batter with salt and pepper. Dip each mushroom in the  batter, letting any excess drip off.    Fry a couple of mushrooms at a time until golden brown, about 2 minutes.  Stir the mushrooms occasionally for overall browning. Remove the mushrooms  from the fryer and drain on a paper-lined plate. Season the mushrooms with  Essence. In a small mixing bowl, toss the sesame oil with the herb salad.  Season the salad with salt and pepper. Spoon the sauce in the center of the  plate and around the rim. Mound the tempura mushrooms in the center of the  sauce. Top with the herb salad. Garnish with parsley.
