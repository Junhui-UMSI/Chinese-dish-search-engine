Stuffed Frankfurters
Hot dogs, Potatoes; mashed, divided, Caraway seeds, Sauerkraut; drained, Cheddar cheese; shredded, 
Split 7 hot dogs lengthwise; don?t cut all the way through. Top with  8-ounce can sauerkraut, drained, seasoned with 3/4 teaspoon caraway seeds.  Top with total 2 cups mashed potatoes divided equally; sprinkle each  equally with total of 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar. Bake in baking pan in 375-F  oven 15 minutes. Makes 7 servings at 88 cents each.    Recipe by: Family Circle Magazine 8/5/97    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #943 by "Nitro_II "  <Nitro_II@classic.msn.com> on Dec 4, 97
