Quiche
coarsely shredded, Butter or margarine, Eggs, Dried beef, coarsely cut, 9-inch pastry shell, Swiss cheese, natural, Half-and-half, unbaked, Pepper, 
Preheat oven to 375 F (moderate)    Lightly cook dried beef in fat, about 2 minutes.  Cool slightly; sprinkle  into pastry shell.  Sprinkle cheese over beef.  Mix seasonings and sprinkle  over cheese.  Beat eggs and half-and-half together.  Pour over cheese and  beef.  Bake 40 minutes or until lightly browned and a knife inserted into  the center comes out clean.    NOTE: If beef is very salty, pour 1 cup boiling water over beef.  Drain  well; then cut coarsely.
