Ambrosia #1
Cream; whipped, Sugar, Grated fresh coconut, Orange sections, 
Combine orange sections, coconut and sugar in 1-1/2 quart bowl. Combine  cream and sugar; fold into fruit mixture. Refrigerate until serving time.
