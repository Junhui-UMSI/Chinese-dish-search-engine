Baby Lima Beans or Broad Beans with Italian Sausage
Baby lima beans, Ground cumin seed, Water, Black Pepper, Italian bulk sausage;, Onion, Salt, Fresh hot red pepper, 
Place the sausage and onion in a heavy casserole or sauce pan and saute  until light brown. Add the water and bring to the boil. Add the beans,  cumin seed, and hot pepper and bring back to the boil. Cover, reduce heat  and simmer 20 minutes. Season well with salt and pepper.  Yield: 4 to 6 servings  Recipe By     : Nathalie Dupree Cooks a Sunday Night Supper.
