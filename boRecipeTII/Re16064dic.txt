Warm Goat Cheese Fondue
Corn oil, Salt, Whipping cream, Pepper, Thyme, fresh, Shallot, Goat cheese, 
Cook shallot in oil in a large nonstick skillet until tender, stirring  often; stir in chopped thyme and whipping cream. Bring to a boil, stirring  constantly; remove from heat. Whisk in goat cheese, salt and pepper until  smooth. Yeild about 1 1/2 cups    Notes: This is served with Warm Goat Cheese and Potato Salad  Per serving: 364 Calories; 33g Fat (82% calories from fat); 13g Protein; 4g  Carbohydrate; 101mg Cholesterol; 415mg Sodium    Posted to FOODWINE Digest 09 Dec 96    Recipe by: Southern Living Best Recipe 1996/tpogue@idsonline.com    From:    terry pogue <tpogue@IDS2.IDSONLINE.COM>    Date:    Mon, 9 Dec 1996 13:17:28 +0100
