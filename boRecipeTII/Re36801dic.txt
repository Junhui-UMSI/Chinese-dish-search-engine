Strawberry Mold
Boiling water, Jello-strawberry, Med Bananas-mashed, Gelatin-unflavored, Sm Pineapple-crushed,canned, Strawberries-frozen (thawed), Sour cream, Walnuts-chopped, 
Soften gelatin with juice from pineapple.  Add jello and dissolve with 1  cup boiling water.  Add bananas,pineapple,strawberries and walnuts. Mix  well.   Pour half of mixture into a bowl. Refrigerate until hard. Spread sour  cream on mixture.  Cover with rest of mixture and refrigerate. Source:  Carol-1990-UAL
