Strawberry Pretzel Dessert
Cream cheese, Strawberry Jello, Sugar, Cool Whip Lite, Pretzels, Strawberries, Sugar, Boiling water, Butter, 
Mix first three ingredients together and press into bottom of 9 X 13  greased pan. Bake at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes. Let cool.    Blend the next three ingredients together and carefully spread on the  pretzel layer. Refrigerate, letting the cream cheese mixture become firm.  Dissolve the jello in the boiling water and refrigerate until it becomes  syrupy. Mix the fruit (juice and all) into the syrupy Jello and spoon over  cream cheese layer. Refrigerate until Jello layer is firm.  Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest by Carolyn <c4@groupz.net> on Apr 11, 1998
