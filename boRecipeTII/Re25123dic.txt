Tomato-Beer Cooler
See Below, 
Mix 1.5 cups tomato juice, chilled, and 2 cans (12 oz each) beer, chilled.  Pour into chilled glasses. Serve immediately with green onions for stirrers  and, if desired, with red pepper sauce, salt and pepper. 6 servings (about  3/4 cup each); 75 calories per serving.    Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
