Basil Sauce with Pasta
Cream cheese, Salt and pepper, Garlic, Chopped Basil, Butter, Parmesan cheese, Blue Cheese, olive oil, SPRIGS Parsley; Chopped, Mince Garlic And Add It To, 
Mince garlic and add it to the chopped parsley and basil. Mash butter,  cream cheese and blue cheese with a fork. Add to the olive oil, parmesan  cheese, salt and pepper. Mix with the garlic mixture and pour into a  screw-top jar. Shake well. Pour over hot pasta.    Serving Ideas : Main    Recipe by: B.
