Apple Salad
Tangerine, Container of strawberry, Navel orange, Valencia orange, Apples,; cored and diced, Tangelo, 
Place apples in a large bowl place apples. In separate bowl pour yogurt and  squeeze juice from oranges (different orange types can be substituted) a  little at a time to desired consistency. Pour yogurt mixture over apples.  Chill and serve.
