Sweet Carrot Salad
Lime juice, Cayenne, Grated carrots, Honey, 
Add carrots to honey-lime mixture Mix well and press lightly till somewhat  juicy    Serve at room temperature    Comments: Yummy! vary the amount cayenne according to the desired hotness    From: Jeanne Chappell <jchappel@tyrell.net>.
