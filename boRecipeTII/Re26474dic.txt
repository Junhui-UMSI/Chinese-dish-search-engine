Tomato Onion Dip
onion cup-a-soup, tomato cup-a-soup, Worcestershire Sauce, Tabasco sauce, Sour cream, milk, 
In small bowl blend 2 packages cup-a-soup with sour cream, milk,  Worcestershire and Tabasco. Makes about 1 1/4 cups dip. Serve with chips or  crackers.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 26,  1998
