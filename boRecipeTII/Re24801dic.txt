Aunt Linas Carrot Cake
Sugar, Butter, Baking soda, Water, Carrots; grated, Cinnamon, Cloves, Nutmeg, Seeded raisins, Walnuts, Flour, Salt, 
Put sugar, water, raising, butter, carrots, cinnamon, cloves & nutmeg into  a saucepan & boil slowly for about 5 minutes. Remove from heat & allow to  become perfectly cold. NEVER USE WHILE STILL WARM! Then add walnuts, salt,  flour & baking soda. Bake very slowly in a loaf pan for about 1-1/2 hours.    MRS W.G.
