Tabasco Classic - Avery Island Barbecue Sauce
Dry mustard, Ground allspice, Lemon slices, TABASCO pepper sauce, Bay leaf, Chopped Celery With Leaves, Garlic, Molasses, Butter or margarine, Green Pepper, Onions, Red wine vinegar, Canned Whole Tomatoes,, Ground cloves, Tomato paste, 
This classic spicy barbecue sauce has a mellow, rich flavor that is  marvelous with just about anything youd care to grill. Serve additional  sauce on the side. ~------------------------------------------------------  ~----------------- In a heavy nonaluminum saucepan over medium heat, melt  the butter and saute the onion, celery, pepper, and garlic for 5 minutes,  or until the onion is tender but not browned. Add the remaining  ingredients.  Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, until the sauce thickens,  stirring occasionally. Discard the lemon slices and bay leaf. Brush the  sauce on chicken, ribs, frankfurters, or hamburgers during grilling,  broiling, or baking. Makes 2-1/4 cup.    From: The Tabasco Cookbook.
