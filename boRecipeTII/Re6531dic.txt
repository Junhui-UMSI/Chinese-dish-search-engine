Poached Pear Halves
Cinnamon, Sugar, Pear juice made with 4 oz., Pears, peeled, halved and, One-inch piece fresh ginger,, 
In a small saucepan mix together the pear juice, sugar and cinnamon. Bring  to a boil. Add the ginger, cook for 5 minutes, then add the pears. Lower  the heat and simmer the pears for 20 minutes, turning them occasionally.  Transfer the pears to a serving platter. Bring the syrup to a boil and cook  over high heat until reduced to 1/2 cup (about 6 minutes). Pour the juice  on the pears.
