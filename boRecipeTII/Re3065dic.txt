Peanut Crumb Apple Pie
9" unbaked pie shell, Butter or margarine, Flour, Light brown sugar, Salt, (30 oz.) apple pie filling, 
Blend peanut butter, butter, brown sugar and salt into flour until mixture  is crumbly. Spoon apple pie filling into unbaked crust; sprinkle peanut  butter mixture over pie filling. Bake in 400? oven for 30-35 minutes or  until filling is hot and pastry browned. Yield: One Pie.    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Abtaxel <Abtaxel@aol.com> on Apr 18, 1998
