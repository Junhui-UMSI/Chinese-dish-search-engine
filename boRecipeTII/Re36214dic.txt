Beef Stroganoff Casserole
(small) mushroom stems and, Ground beef, Sour cream, Egg noodles, Cream of mushroom soup;, (small) black olives; sliced, 
1. Cook egg noodles according to directions and set aside.    2. Cook ground beef until it is no longer pink. Drain off grease. Add soup,  sour cream, olives, mushrooms, and noodles and cook until heated through.
