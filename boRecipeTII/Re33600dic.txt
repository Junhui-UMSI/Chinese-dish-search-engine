Swedish Meatballs #2
Onion, Soft bread crumbs, Eggs, Sugar, Ground allspice, Salt, Ground nutmeg, Gound beef, Barbecue sauce, milk, Ground pork, Butter or margarine, 
Brown onions in butter or margarine. Combine all ingredients except  barbecue sauce. Mix well and shape into small balls about 1 inch in  diameter. Brown well in baking pan in preheated 350 degree oven for 20  minutes. Arrange browned meat balls in a 2-1/2 quart heat-resistant glass  bowl. Pour barbecue sauce over meat balls and heat for 20 minutes at 350  degrees or cook in an electric skillet. Insert colorful toothpicks to  serve.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
