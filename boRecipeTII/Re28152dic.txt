Baked Custard (Diabetic)
Vanilla, Salt, Granulated sugar, Low fat milk, Eggs, 
Combine milk, sugar replacement, salt, vanilla and eggs in large  bowl. Beat to blend well.  Pour into six 1/2 cup individual baking  dishes; sprinkle with nutmeg. Set cups in large baking pan. Add 1  inch of water to pan. Bake custard at 350 degrees for 45 min. or  until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
