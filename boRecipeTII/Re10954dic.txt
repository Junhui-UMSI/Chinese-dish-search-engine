Rack of Lamb with Herbed Mustard
Mustard, Garlic, Soy sauce, Egg, Ground ginger, olive oil, Dried Rosemary, Racks of lamb, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in container of an electric blender; process 15  seconds or until smooth. Add oil to mustard mixture, one drop at a time;  process on low speed until light and creamy.    Place lamb, fat side up, on rack in a shallow roasting pan; insert meat  thermometer in lamb. Brush mustard mixture on lamb. Bake at 325 degrees for  1 hour and 20-30 minutes or until meat themometer registers 160 degrees.
