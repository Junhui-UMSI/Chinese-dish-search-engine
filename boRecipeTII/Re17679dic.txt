Almond Poppy Seed Tea Bread
milk, oil, GLAZE, Baking Powder, Vanilla, Vanilla, Sugar, Eggs, Almond extract, Poppy seeds, Sugar, Flour, Almond flavoring, Orange juice, 
Blend all ingredients until well blended. Pour into greased tins. Bake at  350 degrees for 1 hour. Makes 2 large, 3 medium or 4 small loaves. Heat all  glaze ingredients until sugar melts. Brush on cooled breads with a pastry  brush. Allow glaze to harden before wrapping. Enjoy!    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #187 by Julie Bertholf  <jewel1@ix.netcom.com> on Jul 19, 1997
