Aji Sauce #1
Heavy cream, Vegetable oil, Whole Aji peppers, seeded, Queso fresco, Or Muenster, 
Combine chiles, 1 cup queso blanco or Muenster cheese, and heavy cream in a  blender and puree until smooth. Heat olive oil and slowly add the cheese  mixture, stirring constantly. Simmer the sauce for 5 minutes or until it  thickens.
