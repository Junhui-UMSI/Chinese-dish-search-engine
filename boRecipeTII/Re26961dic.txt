Baked Cauliflower and Mushrooms
mushrooms, Lemon juice, Cauliflowerets, Olive oil, Cider vinegar, red onion, Pepper, green onions, Salt, Garlic, 
Heat oven to 350 F. Spray 9-inch square baking dish, with nonstick cooking  spray. Mix all ingredients except green onions. Spread evenly in baking  dish. Bake uncovered 40 to 45 minutes, stirring occasionally, until  vegetables are tender and golden brown. Sprinkle with green onions.
