Pudding in a Cloud
Thawed Cool Whip, non-dairy, (4 serving size) Jello, Cold milk, 
FROM:   General Foods advertisment    Spoon 1/3 cup of whipped topping into each of six dessert glasses. Using  back of spoon, make depression in center and spread topping up sides of  glass.    Prepare pudding with milk as directed on package. Spoon pudding into  glasses. Chill    Makes 6 servings.
