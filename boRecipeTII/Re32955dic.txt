Sweet Vegetable Relish
Sweet red pepper, cut in, Thinly sliced fresh ginger, Or more dried red chiles, Sugar, Salt, Carrots, peeled and slice, Bell pepper, cut in 1-inch, Cucumber, unpeeled, seeded, Vinegar, Onions, cut in 1-inch chunks, 
Prepare all vegetables.  Then combine ginger root, dried chiles,  vinegar, sugar and salt with 3/4 cup water in a saucepan.  Bring to a  rapid boil, reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes; remove from heat,  cool to tepid and add carrots.  Cool completely, stirring from time  to time.    Add bell pepper, sweet red pepper, onions and cucumber.  Pour into a  bowl and chill for at least 4 hours, or overnight in the  refrigerator.  May be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.    Makes approximately 3 pints.    From "Innards and Other Variety Meats".  Jana Allen and Margret Gin.  101 Productions.  San Francisco, 1974.    Posted by Stephen Ceideburg November 7 1990.
