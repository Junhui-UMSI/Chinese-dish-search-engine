Strawberry Yogurt Pie
Honey, Water, Envelope unflavored gelatin, Cream cheese, milk, 8" baked graham cracker shel, Vanilla, Plain yogurt, Halved or sliced strawberrie, Large egg yolks, Grated orange rind, 
In a 1 1/2 quart saucepan,sprinkle gelatin over water to soften a matter  of minutes.In a 1 cup measure beat together milk and yolks until  blended;add to gelatin mixture.Over low heat stir constantly with a wide  rubber spatula,especially around side of pan,until gelatin is  dissolved;cool at room temperature.In a medium bowl,beat together  cheese,honey,orange rind and vanilla gradually and gently beat in yogurt  until smooth.Chill until very slightly thickened.Turn into graham cracker  shell;chill to set.At serving time,cover surface with strawberries.Serves  6.
