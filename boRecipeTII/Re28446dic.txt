Baked Oatmeal
Vanilla, Salt, Cinnamon, Milk, Oats, Eggs, Brown sugar, Applesauce, Baking Powder, Raisins, 
1. Preheat oven to 350.    2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl; mix wet ingredients in another.    3. Stir wet and dry ingredients together and add raisins.    4. Spread in a 9x9 pan sprayed with nonstick spray.    5. Bake 25-30 minutes.    Calories per serving 200 fat 2 grams. Using unsweetened applesauce, egg  substitute, skim milk and 1/2c raisins.
