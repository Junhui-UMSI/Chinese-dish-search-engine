Quick Peach Ice Cream
peaches, Vanilla extract, milk, Heavy cream, Superfine sugar, 
Slice peaches and puree; refrigerate. Combine milk and cream with sugar.  Whisk 2 minutes, or until sugar dissolves. Stir in vanilla extract. Add  peach puree, stirring well. Chill. Freeze in ice cream freezer featuring  pre-chilled cannister. Makes 4 servings.    The Tampa Tribune, 07/15/93
