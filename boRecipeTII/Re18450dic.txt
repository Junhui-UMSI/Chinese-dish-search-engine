Vidalia Onion Casserole
Vidalia Onions, Club Crackers, Salt, Grated sharp chedder cheese, Eggs, Pepper, Parika, Skim milk, Butter, 
Spray 9" x 13" dish with cooking spray. Crumble pack of crackers and spread  ouut in bottom of dish. Melt half of butter and pour over crackers. Cut  onions into small pieces. Sautee onions in light butter. Spread onions  evenly over crackers. Add salt and pepper to taste. Beat 4 eggs and 1 1/2  cup milk. Pour egg and milk mixture over crackers and onion. Top with  grated sharp chedder cheese. Sprinkle paprika on top. Bake at 350 degrees  for 35 - 45 minutes until it sets.    Its so good it will make you cry.
