Peanut Butter Fudge #14
Crunchy peanut butter, Semisweet chocolate morsels, Sweetened condensed milk, 
Put chocolate chips and peanut butter into a microwave-safe dish. Microwave  on high 1-2 minutes until chips have completely melted. Add sweetened  condensed milk; stir until mixture is of a uniform consistency. Pour into a  8"x8" square pan lined with wax paper. Refrigerate until firm. Cut into 1"  pieces.
