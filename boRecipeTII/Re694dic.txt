Greek Garlic Chicken Salad
Mayonnaise, Cooked chicken, cut in 3/4", Crumbled feta cheese, Ripe black olives, pitted, Chopped fresh parsley, Dried oregano (opt.), Peeled, seeded and diced, Yogurt, Lettuce leaves, Dressing:, Garlic, minced, 
In a bowl, combine chicken, feta, olives, parsley and oregano (if using).  For dressing, combine garlic and mayonnaise; stir into chicken mixture. Let  stand for 1 hour in refrigerator to develop flavors. Just before serving,  fold in cucumber and yogurt.  Serve on a bed of lettuce.
