Phenomenal Pollo
(3-lb) broiler; cut for, Salt to taste, Onion; quartered, Sour cream; if desired, Chicken broth, Clove (large) garlic, Pickled Jalapeno juice, Leaves dark green lettuce, Parsley sprigs, Fresh Jalapenos; stemmed, -(up to), Virgin olive oil, Corn tortillas; warmed, Guacamole (see index), Fresh cilantro leaves, Ground almonds, 
Place chicken in a single layer in a large pot. Pour in broth and jalapeno  juice. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 45 minutes or until  just tender. Cool in cooking broth. Lift chicken from broth (reserve  broth); discard skin and bones and tear meat in bite-size peices. To  prepare sauce, in a food processor or blender, process onion, lettuce,  cilantro, parsley, garlic and jalapenos until quite smooth. Add about 1 cup  chicken broth, a few tablespoons at a time, until consistency of whipping  cream. Set aside. Heat oil in medium, heavy saucepan, then add almonds.  Lightly cook and stir over medium-low heat 3-5 minutes. Add sauce and  chicken and simmer 10-15 minutes. Taste & adjust seasonings, add salt if  desired. Serve in warmed tortillas or over rice, topped with guacamole and  sour cream, if desired.    From the <Hotter Than Hell!>, by Jane Butel, ISBN 0-89586-646-3  (0-89586-542-4 paperback).
