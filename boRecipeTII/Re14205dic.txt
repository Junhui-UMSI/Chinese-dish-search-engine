White Pizza
Pizza Dough, Fresh basil, Dried basil, Dried Oregano, Garlic, Fresh ground black pepper, olive oil, mozzarella cheese (about 8 oz), fontina cheese (about 8 oz), Grated Parmesan cheese, Oregano, 
1.  In a small mixing bowl combine basil, oregano, garlic, olive oil, and pepper.  Mix together well and allow to stand for flavors to blend.     2.  Preheat oven to 500F.  Prepare pizza dough and form circular crust on a baking stone, as for normal pizza, except form a higher outside edge on crust.     3.  Spread oil, garlic and herb mixture evenly over pizza crust. Then layer mozzarella cheese evenly over crust and finally top with fontina  cheese. Sprinkle Parmesan over top. Dust lightly with pinch of oregano.     4. Bake until cheese is bubbly and crust is brown around edges, 12 to 15  minutes.      Serves 2 to 4.    Source:  365 Easy Italian Recipes, Rick Marzullo OConnell
