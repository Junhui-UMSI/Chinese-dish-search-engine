Polenta Wide Mushrooms in Cream
Heavy cream, Salt, pepper to taste, Finely chopped parsley, olive oil, Lemon, Butter, Soft polenta (see Barbar, Fresh mushrooms, Shallots, finely chopped, 
Wipe mushrooms and slice thickly. Toss in lemon juice. Saute mushrooms with  shallots, covered, in melted butter and oil for 20 minutes. Add cream and  simmer 5 minutes more. Seasonto taste with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with  parsley and serve over soft polenta. Makes 6 servings.    Note: Use a blend of mushrooms if available.
