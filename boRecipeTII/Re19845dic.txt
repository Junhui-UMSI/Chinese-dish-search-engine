Apple and Cheese Pleasers
Quick cooking oats, Egg, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Butter or margarine,softened, Salt, Raisins, Chopped apple, Brown sugar, Cheddar; shredded; 1 c, Vanilla, 
Combine flour, butter, sugar, egg, vanilla, cinnamon, baking powder and  salt in large bowl; mix well.  Add oats, cheese and raisins; mix well. Stir  in apple. Drop by heaping tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheet; bake in  preheated moderate oven (375 degrees) 15 minutes or until golden brown.  Store in tightly covered container in refrigerato or in loosely covered  container at room temperature. Yield: about 2 dozen cookies
