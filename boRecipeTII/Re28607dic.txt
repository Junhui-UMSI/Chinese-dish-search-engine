Texas Bbq Sauce
Stalks celery; chopped, Cider vinegar, Onion, Salt, Butter, Garlic, Tomato ketchup, Chili powder, Water, Sugar, Paprika, Black pepper, Bay Leaves, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Combine all the ingredients and bring to a boil. Simmer about 15 minutes.  Remove from heat and strain.    Note! The celery pieces that are strained out are excellent eating.
