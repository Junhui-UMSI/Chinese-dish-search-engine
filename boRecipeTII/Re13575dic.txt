Wild Berry Freezer Jam
Blackberries, Blueberries, Sugar, Liquid fruit pectin, Strawberries, Lemon juice, Raspberries, Saskatoon berries, 
In a large bowl, crush all of the berries. Stir in sugar; let stand for 10  minutes. Combine pectin and lemon juice; add to fruit, stirring constantly  until sugar is dissolved, about 3 minutes. Pour into jars or freezer  containers, leaving 1/2 inch headspace. Cover tightly. Let stand at room  temperature until set, up to 24 hours. Freeze or store in the refrigerator  up to 3 weeks. Yield: 6 half-pints. Editors Note: If saskatoon berries are  not available in your area, add an extra cup of one of the other berries.
