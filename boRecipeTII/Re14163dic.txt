White Fudge
Salt, White Karo corn syrup, Sour cream, Chopped walnuts; more or, milk, Vanilla, Sugar, Butter, 
Combine sugar, sour cream, butter, syrup and salt. Stir over medium heat  until sugar dissolves and mixture boils. Boil 9 minutes. Let stand to cool  for 1 hour. Beat until thick. Add walnuts and vanilla. Pour into greased  pan. Slice when set and remove from pan.
