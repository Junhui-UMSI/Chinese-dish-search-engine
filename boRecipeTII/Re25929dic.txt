Bacon and Onion Appetizers
pumpernickel toast, green onions, Bacon, 
Fry bacon until very crisp. Strain and crumble. Combine bacon, onions and  mayonnaise; blend. Spread on toast and place in a 350 degrees oven to heat  through. Serve immediately.
