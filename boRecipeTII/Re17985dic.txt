Amaretto Coconut Cake
Directed, Coconut cream, Cake mix -- prepared as, Cool Whip?, Coconut, dried, Amaretto, 
Mix cake mix as directed on package  adding amaretto and 6  oz. coconut to  batter, bake as directed. When done  take out of oven  with end of wooden  spoon make holes in entire cake ,pour coconut cream over top  of cake let  stand  3 hrs or over night . frost with mixture of cool whip and remaining  coconut. keep refigerated.
