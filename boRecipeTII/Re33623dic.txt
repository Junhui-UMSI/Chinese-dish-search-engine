Swedish Pepparkaka Cookies (A Spice Cookie)
Ground cloves, Ground ginger, Baking soda, Sugar, Flour, Egg, Cinnamon, Dark Karo syrup, Orange juice, Butter, 
Cream butter and sugar together.Mix all ingredients together thoroughly.  Roll out and cut in desired shapes with cookie cutters.Bake on a buttered  baking sheet @ 350 degrees for about 10 minutes.Hints:Put a piece of wax  paper over bread board and use a cloth on rolling pin.Use flour on paper  and then you can roll them out thinner and they will not stick.After mixing  dough,put in refrigerator until chilled,at least one hour.
