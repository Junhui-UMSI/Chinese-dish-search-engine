Tomato Bread Salad with Herbs
With a pinch of salt, Vine-ripened tomatoes, cut, Basil leaves; chopped fine, Red wine vinegar, 3/4-inch cubes crusty bread, Fresh marjoram; chopped fine, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Clove garlic, minced and, Wedges, Nicoise or Kalamata olives, Vine-ripened yellow, 3/4-inch wedges, 
In a bowl whisk together vinegar, garlic paste and pepper to taste and  whisk in oil until emulsified. Add remaining ingredients and salt to taste  and toss to combine well.
