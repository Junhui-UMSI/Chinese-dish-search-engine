Welsh Cakes (Welsh)
Margarine, Currants, milk, Lard, Sugar, Mixed spice, Flour, Egg, Baking Powder, salt, 
(PICAU AR Y MAEN) Sift the flour, baking powder and salt together. Rub in  the fat and add the other dry ingredients. Beat the eggs lightly and add  with enough milk to make a firm paste. Roll out on a floured board to a  thickness of 4 inch and cut into rounds. Cook on a greased griddle for  about 3  minutes on  each side.  until golden-brown. Cool and sprinkle with  sugar. Serve alone or with butter.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #263 by "ray.watson"  <ray.watson@ukonline.co.uk> on Wed, 25 Sep 1996.
