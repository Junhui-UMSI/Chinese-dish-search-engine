Rabbit with Pasta
Cooked Pasta, Salt & Pepper, Rabbit, Prepared Spaghetti Sauce, Garlic, , Onions, Peas, 
Heat 1 Tbs Olive Oil in large Dutch Oven.  Brown Rabbit pieces, without  crowding, until golden.  Remove from pan.  Add onions and saute until they  start to sweat.  Add garlic and continue to cook for 1 - 2 mins. Return  Rabbit to pan along with sauce. Season to taste. Simmer for 1 1/2 hrs over  low heat.  Add peas and cook 15 mins. longer. Serve over hot pasta.
