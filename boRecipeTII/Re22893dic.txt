April Victorias Lavender Faerie Cookies
lavender flowers, Baking Powder, Butter, Eggs, Sugar, Vanilla, Flour, 
Preheat oven to 375 F.    Cream butter and sugar.  Beat in eggs and vanilla; stir in the dry  ingredients until well blended.    Drop by half-teaspoons on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, and  cool slightly on baking sheet before transferring to plates.    From "Where the Wild Thyme Grows" article in "The Herb Companion."  April/May 1993, Vol. 5, No. 4.  Pg. 57. Posted by Cathy Harned.
