Stuffed Lobster Tails Supreme
Cayenne, Light cream, Butter or margarine, Lemon juice, Cracker crumbs, Grated Parmesan cheese, Salt, Flour, Frozen lobster tails, Paprika, 
Cook lobster tails in boiling salted water until pink and tender or as  package directs. Drain.  With scissors, cut meat away from the shells and  dice. Rinse the shells and set aside.    Saute the lobster meat in 4 tbsp. butter in large frying pan for 2-3  minutes.  Remove the lobster from the pan. Blend in the flour, salt,  paprika, and cayenne and cook for 1-2 minutes.    In a separate pan, heat the cream and then add to the flour mixture,  stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils 1 minute. Stir in the  lemon juice. Add lobster.    Spoon filling into the lobster shells.  Mix together the cracker crumbs,  cheese, and remaining tbsp. butter and sprinkle this mixture over the  lobster. Bake at 450F for 10 minutes, or until top is golden. Yield: 4  servings.
