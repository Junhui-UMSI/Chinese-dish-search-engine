Poodle Toast
Margarine, Flour, Salt, Scalded milk, Weiners cut into 1/4 inch, Canned tomatoes, drained, Toast, Baking soda, 
Melt margarine, then blend in flour and salt. Add tomatoes, soda, and milk.  Cook 10 minutes, stirring as needed. Add wiener rounds. When hot, serve  over hot toast.    Makes 4 servings.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
