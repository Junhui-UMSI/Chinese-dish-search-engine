Almond Flavored Cake
CAKE MIX YELLOW #10, FLAVORING MAPLE, Water, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN                TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  PREPARE CAKE MIX ACCORDING TO INSTRUCTIONS ON CONTAINER. SEE  GUIDELINES FOR USING CAKE MIXES, RECIPE NO. G-G-3.  ADD 2 OZ (1/4 CP)  IMITATION ALMOND FLAVORING.    2.  POUR 7 LB 4 OZ (3 3/4 QT) BATTER INTO EACH GREASED AND FLOURED  SHEET PAN.  SPREAD EVENLY.    3.  BAKE 40-50 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE.    4.  COOL; FROST OR DUST WITH POWDERED SUGAR IF DESIRED.    5.  CUT 6 BY 9.    NOTE:  1.  OTHER PAN SIZES MAY BE USED. SEE RECIPE NO. G-G-4.    2.  IN STEP 3, IF CONVECTION OVEN IS USED, BAKE AT 300F., 35 MIN.  OR UNTIL DONE, ON LOW FAN, OPEN VENT.
