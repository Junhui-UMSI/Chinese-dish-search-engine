Uncooked Cookies
Nuts; chopped, Oats, quick cooking, Coconut, Vanilla, Cocoa, milk, Margarine, Sugar, 
Put sugar, milk and margarine together in a saucepan and bring to a rolling  boil; boil for one minutes. Mix together oats, cocoa, nuts or coconut, and  vanilla. Pour the liquid over, and mix well. Drop by spoon- fuls onto waxed  paper.  Let set until firm.
