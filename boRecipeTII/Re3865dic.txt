Pepper Casserole
Ortega diced chili peppers, Bisquick, Eggs, Cheese of your choice;, milk, 
Grease 9"x13" casserole. Cover bottom with chili peppers. Cover with grated  cheese. Mix eggs, Bisquick and milk. Pour over peppers. Bake 45 minutes at  350 degrees. This makes a good brunch dish or perhaps Sunday night supper.  You can add bits of other things too for variation.
