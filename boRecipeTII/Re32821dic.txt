Sweet Potato Stars
Freshly squeezed orange, Brown sugar, Toasted sesame oil, Sweet potatoes or yams; at, Ground nutmeg, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Place each sweet potato or yam on its side  and slice crosswise into 1/2inch rounds. Using a 3inch starshaped cookie  cutter, cut out a star shape from each round; discard the scraps. If your  star cookie cutter is packed away with the Christmas decorations, place a  paper star stencil on top of the round and use a paring knife to cut the  shape Pour the sesame oil into the bottom of a shallow ovenproof baking  dish and spread it so that the whole bottom is greased. Place the stars in  the baking dish. Pour the orange juice over the stars and sprinkle the  brown sugar and nutmeg on top. Cover tightly with aluminum foil. Bake for  30 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for 15 more minutes. Use a  spatula to remove the stars from the baking dish. Top each star with 1  teaspoon of the pan juice.
