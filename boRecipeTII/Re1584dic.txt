Butterscotch Wheat Germ Brownies
Canola oil, Light brown sugar, Eggs (use equivalent egg, Substitute), Vanilla, Wheat germ, Powdered milk, Baking Powder, Salt, Broken walnut or, Pecan meats, Yield: Makes 16 2"-squares, 
A quick-and-easy one bowl recipe    Preheat oven to 350? F. Using Microwave oven, melt shortening in the mixing  bowl. Add molasses, sugar, eggs and vanilla. Stir well. Sift in powdered  milk, baking powder, and salt.  Add wheat germ and nut meats. Stir only  enough to blend, about 20 strokes. Line pan with waxed paper, mitering  corners.  Spread dough in pan and bake 25-30 minutes at 350? F. Turn out of  pan, remove wax paper immediately, and cut into bars or squares while warm.
