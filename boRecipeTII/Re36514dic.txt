Strawberries Elegante
Light cream cheese, Orange flavored liqueur; OR, Brown sugar, Strawberry slices; fresh, Orange flavored liqueur; OR, Skim milk, 
Toss strawberries with 2 tb liqueur in small bowl. Place cream cheese  product, brown sugar, 1 tb liqueur and milk in food processor or blender  container; process until well blended. Serve over strawberries. Garnish  with fresh mint leaves, if desired.   170 calories; 7 g fat; 220 mg sodium; 20 mg cholesterol Source: No Guilt  Desserts
