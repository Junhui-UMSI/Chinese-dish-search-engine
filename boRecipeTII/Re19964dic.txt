Apple Butter Bars
Baking soda, Butter or Margarine, All-purpose flour, Sugar, Apple butter, Uncooked quick-cooking oats, Salt, 
Combine first 5 ingredients in a large bowl; stir in butter. Press half of  mixture into a greased 13- x 9-inch pan; top with apple butter. Sprinkle  with remaining crumb mixture, and press gently with back of a spoon.    Bake at 350? for 55 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool and cut into  bars. Makes 3 dozen.    Notes : Freeze extras and keep on hand for after-school snacks. Select  tart, green-skinned Granny Smiths for cakes and pies.
