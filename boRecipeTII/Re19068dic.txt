Vegetable Chowder
Diced potato, Water, Pepper, Chicken bouillon cubes, Uncooked rice, Celery, Carrots; chopped, Canned tomatoes, Salt, 
Bring to boil and simmer 45 minutes. When ready to serve, stir in 1 cup  milk (optional). 6 to 8 serving.    Diana Webster  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 475 by "bunny"  <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Jan 8, 1998
