Apple Danish
filling, Flour, Margarine, Sugar, milk, Crust, Shortening, glaze, milk, Egg yolk, Flour, Powdered sugar, Apples, peeled and sliced, Salt, Almond extract, Cinnamon, 
Mix flour and salt, cut in shortening, combine milk and egg yolk and add to  flour.  Roll out half the mixture on a floured surface to fit 11x16x1/2  inch jelly roll pan. Place in pan.     Mix all filling ingredients together and spread over dough. Roll  remaining pastry and place over filling. Brush pastry with 1 egg white,  stiffly beaten. Bake at 375F 45 minutes.     Mix together glaze, drizzle over cooled danish. Cut into bars. Source:  My mother.
