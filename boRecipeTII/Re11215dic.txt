Raisin Sauce for Ham (Easter)
Prepared mustard, Salt, vinegar, Cornstarch, Raisins, 
Simmer raisins in water to cover for 1 hour. Add mustard, sugar, salt and  vinegar. Dissolve cornstarch in small amount of cold water; add to sugar  mixture. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Serve hot with baked ham.    Posted to recipelu-digest by Horton Family <dhorton@jazz.cybermedia.net> on  Mar 25, 1998
