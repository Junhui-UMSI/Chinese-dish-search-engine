Beef and Potatoes
Pepper, Onions; finely chopped, Potatoes, Light cream, Ground beef; lean, Salt, Tomato soup, 
In large skillet brown beef; break up large chunks with fork. Pour off fat.  In a small bowl, mix together salt, pepper, onion and undiluted soup. Peel  and slice potatoes. In crockpot arange alternate layers of potatoes and  meat, with potatoes on bottom    Pour soup mixture over. Cover and cook on LOW for 4 to 6 hours. Turn  control to high. Pour cream over all; cover and cook on HIGH for 15 to 20  minutes. Makes 6 servings.    >From the recipe files of Carole Walberg    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Tracy Waltermyer <inadayz@nbn.net> on Aug  25, 1998,
