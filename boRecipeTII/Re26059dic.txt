Tofu Mayonnaise
Tamari (or soy sauce), Cider vinegar, Medium tofu, drained, Lemon juice, Maple syrup (optional), 
1. Crumble tofu into a blender or food processor.    2. Add remaining ingredients.    3. Blend until smooth.    This recipe makes about  2 cups.
