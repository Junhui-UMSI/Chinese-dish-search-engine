"Guardian Angel" Torte
Shaved chocolate, Angel food cake;, (9 or, Chocolate pudding mix, Sour cream, Heavy cream, milk, 
Combine pudding mix and milk; cok until mixture boils. Cool slightly; stir  in sour cream. Chill thoroughly. Split cake in 2 or 3 layers. Spread  chocolate mixture between layers. Cover top and sides of cake with whipped  cream, sweetened with condectioners sugar. Garnish with shaved chocolate.  Serves 10.
