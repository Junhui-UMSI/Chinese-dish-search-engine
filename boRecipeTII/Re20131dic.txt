Vanilla Peanut Butter Pie
Instant vanilla pudding, Baked pie shell, Powdered sugar, Cool Whip whipped topping, Peanut butter, 
Mix peanut butter and powdered sugar;crumble.Prepare instant pudding  according to package directions.Spread most of crumble on bottom of baked  pie shell.Pour prepared pudding over.Cover with Cool Whip. Sprinkle with  remaining crumble.Chill at least 1 hour.
