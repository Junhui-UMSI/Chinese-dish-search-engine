Baked Chicken Breast Supreme
Celery salt, Garlic, Chicken breasts; skinless,, Lemon juice, Paprika, Worcestershire Sauce, NF sour cream, Salt, Bread crumbs, Shortening, Pepper, Butter, 
1.  In large bowl, combine first 8 ingredients.  Add chicken, turning to  coat.  Store, covered, in fridge overnight.    2.  Remove chicken from marinade (dont leave too much) and roll in crumbs.  Lay in single layer in shallow baking pan.    3.  Spoon half of butter and shortening on top and bake at 350F for 45  mins.  Spoon rest of butter mixture over and bake 15 mins until tender and  nicely browned.
