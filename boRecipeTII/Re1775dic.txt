Chicken and Sausage
Parsley, Onion, Potatoes, Oregano, olive oil, Sweet Italian Sausage, Black pepper, Frying chicken, Garlic salt, Paprika, Green peppers, 
Arrange potatoes in a large shallow baking pan. Place sliced green peppers  and onions on top. Mix all of the seasonings together and sprinkle half on  the vegetables. Arrange the chicken and sausage pieces on top. Pour the oil  over the ingredients and sprinkle with the remaining seasonings. Cover with  foil and bake in 425 degree oven for 1/2 hour. Reduce the heat to 375  degrees, uncover, and bake for 45 minutes longer until chicken and sausages  are brown.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Aug  31, 1998
