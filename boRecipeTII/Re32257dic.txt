Barbecued Venison Kabobs
Fresh mushroom caps, Whole potatoes, Venison steak, Dry Red Wine, Green bell peppers, Cherry tomatoes, 
Combine meat, mushrooms & wine in shallow dish. Cover & marinate for 2-3  hours in the refrigerator. Remove meat & mushrooms from marinade. Alternate  vegetables & meat on skewers. Grill for 10-15 minutes over medium heat or  until degree of doneness is reached.
