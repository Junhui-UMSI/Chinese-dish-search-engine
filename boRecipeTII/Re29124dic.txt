Baked Rice #2
Onion, Butter, Water, Raw rice, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Saute onion & rice in butter. Add water. Transfer  rice to baking dish & bake in preheated oven 20 min., covered.    CLAM BOX    MISSION & FIFTH; CARMEL    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of American recipes>, downloaded from  G Internet, G Internet.
