Apple Mallow Crisp
Sugar, Margarine, Raisins, Water, Apple pie filling, Salt, Marshmallows, Flour, Cinnamon, 
Combine flour, sugar, salt & cinnamon. Cut in margarine until it resembles  coarse crumbs. Place apple pie filling in a baking dish; sprinkle raisins  over apples; add the water. Sprinkle crumb mixture over the apples. Bake in  preheated oven at 350 for 35-40 minutes. Cover the top with marshmallows.  Broil for a few minutes. Serve plain or with ice cream.
