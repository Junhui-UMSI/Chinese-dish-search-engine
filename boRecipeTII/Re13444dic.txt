Yellow Angel Food Cake
Sugar, rind of 1/2 an orange, Egg yolks, Salt, Cake flour, Cream of tartar, Egg whites, 
Beat egg whites with salt until foamy. Add cream of tartar & beat until  stiff. Fold in sugar. Beat egg yolks until thick & lemon colored. Fold  about 1/3 of egg white mixture into yolks. Blend well, using folding  motion, and add this to remaining egg whites. Add the grated orange rind &  fold in flour, sifted once before and several times after measuring. Bake  in greased 9-1/2 inch tube pan. Place in cold oven. Set temperature control  at 350. Bake for 50-60 minutes.    MRS G.J.
