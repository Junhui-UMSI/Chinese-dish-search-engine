Bacon Potato Soup
carrot, milk, Celery, potatoes, Onions, Butter, Chicken broth, bacon pieces, 
In 3 quart casserole, combine 1st 7 ingredinets. Cover loosely with plastic  wrap. Microwave on high 13-15 minutes or until potatoes are tender;  stirring once. Stir in milk, salt and pepper. Recover. Cook at high 8  minutes or until heated through.    Posted to recipelu-digest by "bunny" <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Feb 26,  1998
