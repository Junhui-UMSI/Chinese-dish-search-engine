Apple Fritters
Eggs, Apples, chopped, Cinnamon, Flour, Baking Powder, milk, 
Mix dry ingredients. In separate bowl, beat eggs, stir in milk and apples.  Combine with dry ingredients. Drop 1 Tablespoon at a time into hot 375 deg  F. fat. Fry until golden brown. Drain and serve sprinkled with  confectioners sugar, and/or maple syrup. Makes 12-15.
