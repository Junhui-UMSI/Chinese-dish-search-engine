Peachy Yogurt Breakfast Sundae
(fresh or frozen), Sliced peaches, (fresh, frozen or canned), Wheat germ, Raspberries or blueberries, Low-fat vanilla yogurt, OPTIONAL, 
Place peaches in small bowl; top with yogurt and wheat germ.  Garnish with  raspberries or blueberries.    Per Serving: About 190 cal, 11 g pro, 31 g car, 3 g fat, 14% cal from fat,        5 mg chol, 75 mg sod.
