Tourtiere - Pork Pie
Mashed potatoes, Allspice, Salt, Pepper, Lean pork, ground, Nutmeg, Pastry for 2 (2 crust) pies, Onion, Water, 
In a saucepan, combine the ground pork, water, nutmeg, allspice, salt,  pepper and onion.  Cook over moderate heat for 30 minutes, stirring often  to prevent sticking. Remove from heat; add the mashed potatoes; and mix  well. Cool.      Fill two pastry-lined plates;adjust top crusts, slashing pastry to allow  for the steam to escape as the pies bake. Bake in a hot oven, 425 degrees  F., For 40 minutes. Serve hot or cold.
