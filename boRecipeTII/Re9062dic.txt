Prize Fruit Chili Sauce
Onions, Bunch celery, Salt, Tin tomatoe paste, Large tomatoes, Pears, Sugar, Vinegar, Peaches, Sweet red peppers, Green peppers, Pickling spices (tied in, 
Chop all vegetables, mix together all remaining ingredients, add and simmer  slowly until thick, about 2 hours. Remove pickling spices and discard.  Store in sterilized jars.
