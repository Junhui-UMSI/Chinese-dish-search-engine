Praline Apple Crisp
All-purpose flour, Pecans, chopped, Zwieback crackers, coarsely, Tart eating apples, sliced, Almond extract, Ground cinnamon, Firm margarine, Water, Brown sugar, Yogurt Topping (recipe, 
Heat oven to 375. Spray 1 1/2-quart casserole with nonstick cooking spray.  Mix water and almond extract; toss with apples in casserole. Cut margarine  into flour, brown sugar and cinnamon with pastry blender in small bowl  until crumbly. Stir in zwieback and pecans. Sprinkle over apples. Bake  uncovered about 30 minutes or until top is golden brown and apples are  tender. Serve warm with the Yogurt Topping.
