Apple and Mint Chutney
Light brown sugar, Ground clove, Tart apples, chopped, Whiskey, Sultanas (golden raisins), Salt, Fresh peppermint, chopped, Each: mace, allspice, 
NOTE: This one also works with pears replacing apples.    GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS: Combine all ingredients in a stainless steel pot.  Bring to a boil uncovered. Then lower to a simmer and cover for for the  remaining cooking time: about 1 hour. The mixture should be thick like a  good finished jam or marmalade.
