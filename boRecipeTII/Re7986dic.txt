Potage Puree De Pommes De Terre
Cooked brown rice, Veggie bouillon, Peeled; diced celery, Splash wine vinegar, Pepper, Water, Salt or to taste, Peeled & cubed potatoes (or, Chopped leeks (white part), 
Date: Sat, 02 Mar 1996 07:46:00 GMT    From: gemini@synapse.net (Georgette & Dave Burnside)  In a 4-qt. soup pot over medium heat, saute leeks, celery and potatoes in a  little water for 5 to 7 min. Add more water as needed to prevent sticking.  Add the 6 cups of water, salt & pepper. Bring to a boil, lower heat and  simmer potatoes are very soft, about 15 min. Add cooked rice and heat  through. Puree soup 2 to 3 cups at a time in a blender or food processor  until very smooth. Return to soup pot and rewarm if needed.
