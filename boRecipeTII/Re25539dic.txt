Avocado Buttermilk Sherbet
Lemon juice, Avacados, Light corn syrup, Buttermilk, Sugar, Honey, 
Puree avacados in blender or food processor.    Combine buttermilk, sugar, honey, corn syrup and lemon juice. Beat well and  blend with avacados. Refrigerate until mixture is thoroughly chilled, then  freeze according to ice cream maker manufacturers directions.    Makes 1 quart.
