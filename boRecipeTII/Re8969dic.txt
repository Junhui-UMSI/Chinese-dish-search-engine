Presto Pesto Chicken
Breadcrumbs, Italian style, skinned, broiler/fryer, boned,, Cheese, provolone, slices, Breasts, chicken, halves,, Parsley, chopped, Pesto, prepared, 
Place the bread crumbs in a shallow dish.  Add the chicken breast  halves, one piece at a time, turning to coat.         Place the chicken in a single layer in an oiled baking pan.  Spoon the pesto over the chicken.  Top each breast with one slice of  cheese, and 1/2 tablespoon of parsley.         Bake in 350 F oven for about 20 - 25 minutes or until the chicken  is fork tender.         Cook:  Yvette T.
