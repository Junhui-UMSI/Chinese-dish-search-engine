Zucchini Dip 2
Cream cheese, Lemon juice, Zucchini, Avocado, Sugar, Garlic, Dried parsley, Onion, Salt and pepper, Wine vinegar, 
Steam or microwave the zucchini until soft. Add all ingredients into  blender and blend well.    Recipe by: Dave Posted to EAT-L Digest by "Iris E. Dunaway"  <hister@JUNO.COM> on Jul 5, 1997
