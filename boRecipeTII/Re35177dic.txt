Beef and Avocado Spiral Sandwiches
Purchased spicy guacamole, 8-inch flour tortillas, Alfalfa sprouts, Cooked roast beef; 2-oz, Chopped seeded tomato, Salsa, 
Warm tortillas as directed on package.     Spread 2 tablespoons guagamole on each tortilla; sprinkle each with 2  tablespoons tomato. Top with 1 slice roast beef and 1/4 cup sprouts. Roll  up. Secure with toothpick. Serve with salsa.
