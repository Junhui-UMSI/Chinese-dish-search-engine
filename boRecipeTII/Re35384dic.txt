Beef Chop Suey
Celery, Chicken broth;, Vegetable oil;, Low-sodium soy sauce;, Sesame oil;, onions, Bean sprouts, Beef round steak;, 
Trim any fat from steak and dice.  In a large non-stick skillet or  wok, heat oils and stir-fry meat over high heat 1 minute.  Stir in  soy sauce. Remove meat with a slotted spoon and keep warm.  Add  celery, onions and broth to the skillet. Bring to a boil, reduce heat  and cook 5 minutes. Add bean sprouts.  Cook 3 minutes more, then  return meat and stir to heat. Serve with Confitti Rice and Sesame  Spinach Salad.
