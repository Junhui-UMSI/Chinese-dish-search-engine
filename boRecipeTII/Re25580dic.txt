Avocado Pineapple Smoothie
Med.-lg ripe avocado, Honey, Coconut flavoring, Fresh pineapple, Lime juice; (opt.), Orange juice, Ice cubes, 
Peel and slice avocado and pineapple into small chunks. Combine with rest  of ingredients (except ice) and puree in blender til smooth. Add ice, if  desired, and blend again. (2 svgs.)    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by brightl@kitmailbox.com on
