Asparagus with Pesto (Spieler)
Asparagus, Pesto, 
Toss asparagus with pesto to taste, a little olive oil and minced garlic.    Grill (hot coals) until lightly charred, then serve as is, or with more  pesto.    Notes: SF Chronicle 5/13/98    Recipe by: Grilling by Marlena Spieler    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Kitpath <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu> on May 14,  1998
