Peppered-London Broil with Lime-Marinated Onions
, Black Pepper, Flour, London Broil, Red Onion, Lime juice, Tabasco sauce, Salt, 
In a small nonreactive bowl, combine onions, lime juice and Tabasco ; cover  and refrigerate for at least 2 hours but less than 6. Rub meat all over  with pepper and salt. Sprinkle half the flour on each side of the meat.  Saute over high heat in a large saute pan, with no oil, until well seared,  5 to 7 minutes on each side for rare. Remove from heat and serve in  thickish slices, topped with generous portion of the marinated onions. Per  serving: 210 calories, 5 grams fat.
