Teriyaki Drumettes
Garlic powder, Wine or wine vinegar, Drumettes; (1 1/2 to 2), Honey, Worcestershire Sauce, Teriyaki sauce, Catsup, 
Bring all ingredients to a boil. Pour over chicken drumettes. Marinate at  least 2 hours or preferably overnight. Place drumettes along with 1/2 cup  of the marinade in simmer pot. Cook 10 minutes on high and 20 to 25 minutes  on simmer or medium.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 245 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 11, 1997
