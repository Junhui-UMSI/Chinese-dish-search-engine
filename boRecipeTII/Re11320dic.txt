Ranch Potato Crisps
Ranch salad dressing mix, (1, Baking potatoes, Vegetable oil, 
Preheat oven to 400F. Wash potatoes and leave skin on. Slice potatoes into  1/4-inch thick slices. Place sliced potatoes in bowl. Add vegetable oil;  stir to coat. Add dressing mix; stir to coat. Line potato slices in a  single layer on 15-inch baking stone. Do not overlap slices. BAke 40-45  minutes or until potatoes are brown. Serve warm.
