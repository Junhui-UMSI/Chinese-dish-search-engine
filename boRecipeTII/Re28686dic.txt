Texas Poppy-Sweet Cake (Low Fat)
Egg substitute, liquid, Tub-style canola or corn, Vanilla, Flour; unbleached, Nonfat buttermilk, Baking soda, Egg whites, Nonfat sour cream, Sugar, Powdered sugar; for dusting, Poppy-seed filling; (1 can), Salt, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Coat a 12-cup bundt pan with non-stick spray. In a  large mixing bowl, beat margarine and sugar together with electric beaters.  Add buttermilk, poppy-seed filling, egg substitute, vanilla and sour cream.  Beat until well-combined. Add flour, baking soda and salt and stir with a  spoon until just combined. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until stiff  but not dry. Fold into batter. Pour batter into prepared pan and spread  evenly. Bake in preheated oven about 1 hour and 10 minutes, or until  toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pan 10 minutes, then  remove to wire rack to cool. Dust with powdered sugar, if desired. Makes 16  or more servings. Each serving contains approximately: Original recipe 379  calories, 91 mg cholesterol, 18 g fat, 359 mg sodium. Revised recipe 298  calories,  negligible cholesterol, 9 g fat, 334 mg sodium.
