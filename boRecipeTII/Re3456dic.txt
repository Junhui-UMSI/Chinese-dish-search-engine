Pecan Crumb Apple Pie
Deep-dish pie crust, Flour, , Ground cinnamon, CRUMB TOPPING, Flour, Butter, Pecans, Sugar, Brown sugar, Ground nutmeg, 
1.  In large bowl, toss together sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and apples.  Spoon into frozen crust.    2.  For crumb topping, combine brown sugar and flour; cut in butter. Stir  in pecans. Sprinkle over apples.    3.  Bake in preheated 400 degree oven on preheated baking sheet for 40-50  minutes.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
