Banana Pudding
Sweetened condensed milk, Box (4 serv size) instant, Vanilla wafers, Bananas, sliced, Water, Cartons (8 oz) whipping, 
Mix together condensed milk, water and pudding mix.    Chill 5 minutes. Whip cream until stiff and fold into pudding mixture.    Layer pudding mixture, sliced bananas and vanilla wafers, ending with  pudding layer.    Crush some of vanilla wafers and sprinkle on top.    Chill and serve.
