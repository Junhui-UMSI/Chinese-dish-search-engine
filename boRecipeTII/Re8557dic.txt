Potatoes Duchesse
Egg yolks, Butter -- melted, Black pepper, Eggs, Hot milk, Nutmeg, Whole potatoes, Salt, 
Boil potatoes until tender. Mash with 1/4 cup milk, butter, salt, pepper,  and nutmeg. Beat eggs and egg yolks together, then beat in remaining milk.  Beat egg mixture into potatoes until fluffy. Serve at once.    Recipe By     : Elizabeth Powell    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
