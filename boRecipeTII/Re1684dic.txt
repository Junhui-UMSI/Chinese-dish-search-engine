Cauliflower Slaw with Creamy Dill Dressing
Fresh lemon juice; optional, Head cauliflower; about 2, Red bell pepper; roasted,, Red onion; minced, Creamy Dill Dressing; (see, salt and pepper, 
cut the cauliflower into small florets. Steam the florets until they are  still firm and crunchy, but lose the raw taste: about 3-4 minutes. Run  under cool water to stop cooking, then drain.    Chop the florets coarsely. Add the other vegetables and enough of the  Creamy Dill Dressing to coat.
