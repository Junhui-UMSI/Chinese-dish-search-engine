Butterscotch Brownies
Flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Butter, Brown sugar, Egg, Vanilla, Nuts, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Sift flour with baking powder and salt and  set aside.  Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat, add sugar, and stir  until dissolved; cool 5 minutes.  Beat in egg and vanilla, mix in dry  ingredients and nuts.  Spoon into a greased 8x8 pan and bake 30-35 minutes  until brownies just begin to pull from sides of pan. Cool in pan on a rack  before cutting.
