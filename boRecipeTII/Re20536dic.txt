Apple Cheese Muffins
Eggs, Flour, Rolled oats, Baking soda, Margarine, Apple pie filling; chopped, Salt, Nuts, milk, Sugar, Grated sharp cheese, 
Preheat oven to 375F, prepare pans. Cream margarine & sugar. Add eggs &  beat well. Sift flour, soda & salt & stir into creamed mixture. Stir in  oats, apple cheese & nuts, mixing well. Add milk last. Spoon into pans &  bake for 20-25 mins or until done. Makes 18 muffins.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
