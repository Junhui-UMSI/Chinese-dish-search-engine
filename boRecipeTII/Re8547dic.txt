Potatoes Au Gratiin
Stick butter or margarine, Velveeta cheese, Half and half, (4.5-oz) shredded Cheddar, (2 pound) frozen hash brown, 
Melt first 4 ingredients in a saucepan. Place frozen hash browns in a 9x13  dish. Pour cheese mixture over potatoes and bake, uncovered, for 1 hour at  350?.  Yield: 8 servings.    RITA MASSEY (MRS.
