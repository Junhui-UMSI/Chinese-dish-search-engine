Strawberry Charlotte Russe
Coarsely broken pecans, Whipping cream, whipped, Egg whites, Sugar, Miniature marshmallows, Hot water, Strawberry-flavored gelatine, (10 oz.) frozen strawberries, Commercial or homemade jelly, Lemon juice, 
A slick trick--a super dessert! Be ready to serve seconds--youll have  requests.    Cut jelly roll into 1/2-inch slices. Arrange slices over bottom and around  sides of oiled 2 1/2-quart mixing bowl. Dissolve gelatine in hot water. Add  marshmallows and partially drfrosted strawberries, stirring until  marshmallows dissolve. Add lemon juice and chill until partially congealed.  Beat until fluffy. Beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Gradually beat in  sugar. Fold into gelatin mixture. Blend in whipped cream and nuts. Pile  mixture lightly into jelly roll-lined mold. Chill until firm. Unmold and  serve. Makes 1 O to 12 servings.
