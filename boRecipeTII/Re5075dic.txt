Picnic Special
Quaker Oats, uncooked, Process American cheese, cut in half, (quick or oldfashioned), Onion soup mix, Ground beef, Tomato slices, cut into 24 slices, Loaf Vienna bread, Leaf lettuce, milk, 
Combine ground beef, oats, soup mix and milk thoroughly.  Shape to form 12  flat patties.  Place in broiler or over hot coals about 5 minutes.  Turn  and cook about 5 minutes longer.    To serve, place a tomato slice, leaf of lettuce, half a cheese slice and a  hamburger between 2 slices of Vienna bread.  Reassemble loaf in bread  basket.
