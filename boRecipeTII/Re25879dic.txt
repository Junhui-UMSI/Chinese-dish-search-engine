Bachelors Buttons
Glace (candied) cherries, Eggs; well beaten, Baking Powder, Sugar, Vanilla extract, Butter or margarine, Salt, Flour, 
From: jleidy@wariat.org    Date: 30 Nov 1994 17:47:24 -0500    Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in eggs; add vanilla.  Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt; stir into creamed mixture.  Mix thoroughly. Drop by teaspoons onto greased baking sheet. Put half a  cherry on top of each "button". Bake 8-10 minutes at 425F.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /COOKIES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
