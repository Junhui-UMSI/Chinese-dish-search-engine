Raos Penne with Cabbage, Sausage, and Marinara Sauce
Marinara sauce, salt and pepper, Cloves garlic; peeled and, Freshly grated pecorino, Penne, olive oil, Italian sausage; cut into, Savoy cabbage; cooked and, 
1. Heat oil and garlic in a large saute pan over medium heat. Add sausage,  and saute until meat is cooked.    2. Add cabbage, and salt and pepper to taste, and saute for an additional  1-2 minutes. Stir in Marinara Sauce, and cook for 3-5 minutes, until  flavors have blended.    3. Meanwhile, cook penne in a large, deep pot in rapidly boiling salted  water until al dente.    4. Drain pasta, and return it to the pot with 1/2 cup of sauce. Stir for  one minute over high heat, then place sauced pasta on serving platter and  pour remaining sauce on top. Serve with a sprinkle of Pecorino Romano  cheese, if desired.    A Columbian Dish    Notes: Raos Recipes Stay tuned Tuesday for a demonstration of some  delicious dishes from the new ?Raos Cookbook.?
