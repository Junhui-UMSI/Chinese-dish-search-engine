Amaretto Parfait Dessert
Orange zest, Strawberries; sliced, Amaretto liqueur, Fat-free cream cheese, or raspberries, Fat-free ricotta cheese, Fat-free plain yogurt, 
Combine ricotta cheese, cream cheese and yogurt in a food processor or  blender and process until smooth. Stir in orange zest. Divide berries among  6 parfait or wine glasses. Top with cheese mixture and drizzle with  Amaretto liqueur.    Serves 6, 79 calories per serving.    >From Jyl Steinbacks "Recipes for Fat Free Living Desserts".
