Willie Gs Oyster Bar Trash
Peeled and deveined shrimp, White rice, Unsalted butter; clarified, Jumbo lump crab meat, Blackening seasoning, 
Heat a cast iron skillet. Add 1 - 2 oz. of clarified butter. Add shrimp and  cook until halfway done. Add crab meat and sprinkle with blackening  seasoning. Mix shrimp and crab meat together. Spoon onto a plate serve with  white rice. Serves 2.
