Peanut Butter Fudge #01
Vanilla, Sugar, milk, Jar (12-oz) crunchy peanut, Butter, Jar (7-oz) marshmallow creme, 
Combine milk and sugar and bring to a gentle boil. Boil for 12-15 minutes.  Remove from heat and add vanilla; pour over other ingredients and mix well.  Pour into buttered platters.
