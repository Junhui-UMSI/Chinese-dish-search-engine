Apple Cheese Bread
Eggs, Apple, Peeled And Chopped, Walnuts, Salt, Sugar, Baking Powder, Grated Sharp Cheddar Cheese, Flour, Baking soda, Butter or margarine, 
Preheat oven to 350F.  Cream butter and sugar, beating until light. Beat  in eggs, one at a time.  Stir in apples, cheese and nuts.  In separate  bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Gradually and  gently stir into apple mixture.  Pour into greased loaf pan and bake 1  hour.  Cool 10 minutes before serving.
