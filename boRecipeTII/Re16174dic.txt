Wasabi-Ginger Butter Sauce
Rice vinegar, Shallot; chopped, Soy sauce, White wine, Unsalted butter; cubed, Heavy cream, Wasabi, Piece (1-inch) ginger root;, 
From:    Judith Pirie <judith@ALOHA.NET>    Date:    Wed, 26 Jun 1996 15:58:48 -1000  In a small saucepan, combine shallot, ginger, wasabi, vinegar, and wine.  Cook on medium heat until liquids reduces to 1 tablespoon. Add cream and  soy sauce, continue to cook until cream mixture reduces to 1-1/2  tablespoons. Lower heat; whisk in butter cubes one at a time. Remove from  heat and strain. Makes about 1 cup.    The Electric Kitchen; Hawaiian Electric Company, Inc.
