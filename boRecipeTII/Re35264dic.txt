Beef and Snow Peas
Onion, Ginger, Beef broth, Garlic, Cornstarch, Vegetable oil, Soy sauce, Sugar, New York sirloin, Pea pods, Water, 
Cut beef in chunks, slice onion. Combine soy sauce, beef broth, water,  sugar and cornstarch. Heat 3 tablespoons oil. Stir fry beef until browned.  Take beef out of pan and keep on the side. Add oil and fry onion. Add soy  sauce mixture and heat until thick. Add meat and pea pods, heat through and  serve.
