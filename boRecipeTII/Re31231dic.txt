Banana-Oat Bread
Ripe bananas; mashed, AM Oat Bran, Unrefined vegetable oil, Pecans, Cinnamon, Honey, AM Whole Wheat Pastry Flour, beaten until fluffy, Egg whites, Non-alum baking powder, Hot milk, soymilk or water, AM Oat Flakes, 
Combine first three ingredients, set aside to cool, combine mashed  bananas, honey, vegetable oil, beaten egg whites, then stir into oat  mixture.  Mix dry ingredients together and stir into oat mixture.  Pour  batter into an oiled bread pan.  Bake at 350 F. for 1 hour or until done.
