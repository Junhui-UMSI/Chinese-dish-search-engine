Apple Pie 2
Pastry for 2 crust pie, Nutmeg, Butter, Cinnamon, Flour, Brown sugar, Thinly sliced apples, Salt, 
BAKE:  425?    Make bottom crust.  Refrigerate.  Combine sugar, cinnamon, flour and salt.  Add to apples, tossing lightly.  Turn into pie plan.  Dot with butter and  sprinkle with nutmeg.  Cover with top crust and cut slits for steam,  Bake  45 - 50 mins.    ORIGINATOR:    ?
