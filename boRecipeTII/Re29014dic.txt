Baked Pheasant
Paprika, Apple cider, ?, garlic, Worcestershire Sauce, Pheasants, Salt, Onions, cream of chicken, mushrooms, sliced, canned, 
Blend all ingredients except pheasants and paprika. Pour over pheasants and  sprinkle with paprika. Bake, covered, for 1-1/2 to 2 hours. Remove cover  the last 30 minutes of cooking. After 1 hour, sprinkle again with paprika.    CAROL LORD (MRS. ROBERT C.)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
