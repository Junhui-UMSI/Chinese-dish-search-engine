Pullus Fusilis (Chicken W/liquid Filling
Minced meat (half beef; half, Vegetable oil, Liebstoeckl, Liquamen or salt to taste, Ground ginger, Green peppercorns, Fresh chicken (approx., Ground pepper, Groats (of oat), White wine, Stone-pine kernels, Eggs, 
From: hz225wu@unidui.uni-duisburg.de (Micaela Pantke) (COLLECTION)    Date: Thu, 22 Jul 93 11:12:07 +0200    See "Ancient Roman Ingredients" for info on ingredients. Ground pepper,  Liebstoeckl, ginger, minced meat and cooked groats. Add eggs and mix until  you have a smooth mass. Season with Liquamen, add oil, whole peppercorns  and stone-pine kernels. Fill this dough into the chicken. Cook  approximately 1 hour with 220 deg C in the oven.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.
