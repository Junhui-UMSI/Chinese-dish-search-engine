Recipe Rescue Low Fat Molasses-Spice Cookies
Molasses, Baking soda, Ginger, Salt, Nonfat plain yogurt, Sugar, Cake Flour, Mace, Cinnamon, Canola oil, Cloves, 
Preheat oven to 350. Prepare baking sheets with parchment paper or nonstick  spray. Whisk together molasses, yogurt, sugar and oil. Stir together flour,  spices, soda and salt. Stir the dry ingredients into the wet ones and  blend. Drop by spoonfuls unto baking sheets and bake 15 to 20 min.
