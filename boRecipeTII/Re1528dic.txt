Broccoli Souffle
Frozen chopped spinach or, Beaten eggs, Melted butter or margarine, Flour, Salt, Pepper, Nutmeg, Lemon ; juice of, Carton small curd cottage, Brick cheese cubed, Velveeta; American, or, 
Cook spinach or broccoli until defrosted. Drain well and cool. Add all  ingredients but the cheeses and mix together. Fold in hard cheeses then  fold in cottage cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by SuperSecDD@aol.com on Oct 11, 1998,
