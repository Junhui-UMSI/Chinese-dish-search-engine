Tortilla Chip Soup
Stewed tomatoes, Monterey jack cheese --, Bell peppers -- chopped, Onion -- chopped, Chicken broth, Cumin, Chicken -- cooked, Tortilla chips -- crushed, Lemon juice, Black pepper, Shredded, 
In a large pan combine chicken, tomatoes, peppers, onion, broth, lemon  juice, and black pepper, simmer for 20 minutes  Place chips in individual  bowls, sprinkle with 1/2 cup cheese, and pour soup mixture over it.  Top  with remaining cheese.    Recipe By     : Anita A. Matejka    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
