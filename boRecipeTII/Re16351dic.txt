Watkins, Pasta with Zucchini and Fresh Tomatoes
Tomatoes -- coarsely, Chopped, (about 3 cups), WATKINS Garlic Liquid Spice, Salt, Seasoning -- as desired, Zucchini -- halved then, WATKINS Basil Liquid Spice, (about 12 ounces dry), White wine vinegar, Sliced, WATKINS red pepper flakes, Thinly sliced onions, Hot cooked ziti pasta, Fresh Lemon Juice, WATKINS Parsley, WATKINS garlic and chives, 
In small bowl, combine zucchini and lemon juice;toss well. In a large  skillet, with a cover, heat Garlic Liquid Spice over medium heat.  Stir in  zucchini and onion;saute 3 to 4 minutes.  Add tomatoes, and next 5  ingredients;bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 5 to 7  minutes.  Toss with hot pasta.  Sprinkle with Garlic and Chives Seasoning.
