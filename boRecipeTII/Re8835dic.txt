Praline Pumpkin Mousse
Butter or margarine, Chopped pecans, Brown Sugar, Pumpkin, JELL-O, COOL WHIP Whipped, Cold milk, Pumpkin pie spice, 
POUR milk into large bowl. Add pumpkin, pudding mixes and pumpkin pie  spice. Beat with wire whisk 1 minute until well mixed. (Mixture will be  thick.) Immediately stir in whipped topping. Spoon into 8 dessert glasses.    REFRIGERATE 4 hours or until set.    MIX butter, pecans and sugar in small bowl. Just before serving, sprinkle  with pecan mixture. Garnish with additional whipped topping, ground  cinnamon, fresh mint and currants. Store leftover mousse in refrigerator.
