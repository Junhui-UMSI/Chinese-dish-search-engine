Hot Cinnamon Plums with Oranges
Oranges, Cinnamon, Sugar, Sultanas, Orange juice, Water, Plums, 
Pare rind from oranges using a potato peeler.Set aside oranges and place  rind in a medium saucepan with rest of ingredients. Heat gently and simmer  covered for about 10 mins or until the plums are tender. Meanwhile remove  pith from the oranges and cut flesh into slices.Add orange slices to  saucepan heat through gently.Remove cinnamon stick and serve.
