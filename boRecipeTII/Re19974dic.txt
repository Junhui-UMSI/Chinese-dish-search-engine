Apple Butter Refrigerator Cookies
APPLE BUTTER, All-purpose flour, APPLE PIE SPICE, Sugar, CHEDDAR CHEESE, SHREDDED, Baking soda, Egg, BUTTER/MARGARINE, SOFTENED, 
MIX SUGAR, BUTTER, APPLE BUTTER AND EGG.  STIR IN REMAINING INGREDIENTS.  COVER AND REFRIGERATE AT LEAST 2 HOURS. DIVIDE DOUGH IN HALF. SHAPE EACH  HALF INTO ROLL, ABOUT 1 1/2 INCHES IN DIAMETER AND OUT 8 INCHES LONG. WRAP  AND REFRIGERATE AT LEAST 4 HOURS.  HEAT OVEN TO 400 DEGREES. CUT ROLLS INTO  1/8-INCH SLICES. PLACE ABOUT 1 INCH APART ON UNGREASED COOKIE SHEET. BAKE 7  TO 9 MINUTES OR UNTIL EDGES ARE LIGHT BROWN; COOL. ABOUT 6 DOZEN COOKIES;       55    CALORIES PER COOKIE.
