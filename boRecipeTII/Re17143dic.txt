Virginia Butter Beans
Minced bell pepper, Flour, Onion, Garlic cloves; minced or, Sugar, Bacon, Boxes frozen lima or butter, salt and pepper, Salt, Canned tomatoes; (juice and, 
Fry bacon. Remove and cook vegetables until tender. Add flour, sugar, salt,  pepper and tomatoes. Add cooked and drained beans and pour in casserole.
