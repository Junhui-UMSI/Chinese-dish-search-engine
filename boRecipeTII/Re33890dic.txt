Sweet and Sour Turkey Wings
Brown sugar, Green pepper, Vinegar, Uncooked turkey wings, Cornstarch, Chicken broth, Celery, Salt, Chili sauce, pineapple chunks, Drained, Soy sauce, 
Separate wings at joints and discard tips.  In slow-cooking pot, combine  wings, broth, celery, and salt.  Cover and cook on low for 4 to 6 hours or  until tender.  Drain, saving 1 1/2 cups broth for sauce.  In large skillet,  combine cornstarch, brown sugar, 1 1/2 cups broth, vinegar, soy sauce, and  chili sauce.  Cook and stir until thick and clear.  Stir in green pepper  and pineapple; simmer for 2 minutes.  Add drained, cooked wings. Bring to  simmer.  Serve over rice.
