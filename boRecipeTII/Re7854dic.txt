Portuguese Sausage
Cold water, Lean pork, Black pepper, Garlic, Red pepper flakes, Vinegar, Paprika, Salt, 
Combine all ingredients except pork & mix thoroughly. Pour over pork  & mix. Refrigerate for 2 days & mix occasionally. Stuff into casings  & twist into 8-10 inch links. Hang links in well-ventilated spot &  dry for 1/2 hour.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
