Triple Chocolate Cookies
Brown Sugar, Eggs, Cocoa powder --, Semi-sweet chocolate chips, Vanilla extract, Salt, Whole Milk, Regular) kisses, Baking soda, Tbspn, All-purpose flour, Granulated sugar, Stick unsalted butter --, (unsweetened), (softened), 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Beat butter, sugars, and vanilla until well  blended.  Add eggs, one at a time, and milk -- beat well. In a medium  mixing bowl, stir together flour, cocoa, salt, and baking soda. Add  gradually to butter mixture and blend well.  Batter will be very thick. Add  chocolate chips and stir with a wooden spoon.  Shape dough into 1" balls  and place on ungreased cookie sheets.   Bake for approximately 10 to 11  minutes.  Cool on wire racks about 5 minutes.  Gently press a Kiss into  center of each cookie.      Enjoy!! This is a recipe I ususally make to  bring to our annual Super Bowl parties in January.
