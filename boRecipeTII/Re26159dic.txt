Toll House Cookies
Nuts; chopped, Sugar, Choc chips, Flour; sifted, Vanilla, Eggs, Baking soda, Brown sugar, Water, Salt, Butter, 
Sift together, the flour, soda and salt.  Combine the butter and sugar and  cream until light and fluffy.   Add sugars, vanilla and water. Beat until creamy.   Beat in eggs one at a time, mixing well. Add flour mix, and stir until  well combined.  Stir in chocolate chips and nuts. Drop by teaspoon on  greased cookie sheets. Bake 10-12 minutes at 375 degrees. Makes 200 2"  cookies.
