Sweet Potato and Pineapple Pie
Pineapple juice, Well-drained pineapple; 1/4", Nine-inch pre-baked pie, Eggs, Mace, Sweet potato; well mashed, Cinnamon, Dark brown sugar, Milk or half and half, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. ln a mixing bowl, mix the pineapple and sweet  potato with the sugar. Mix the milk with the egg, then add the remaining  ingredients and mix until smooth. Pour into pie shell and bake for 25  minutes until set.
