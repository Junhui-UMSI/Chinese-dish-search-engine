Kathys Mushrooms Alfredo
olive oil, Mushroom Alfredo Sauce, Mushrooms, Onion, 
Cut Mushrooms into slices, Slice Onions.  Place Olive Oil in a large fry  pan and heat.  Place mushrooms and onions in pan and saute.  Mix in enough  Alfredo Sauce to turn Mixture to a creamy Gray color.  Heat and serve.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Your Name  <TMAINES.OUTBACK@worldnet.att.net> on Oct 22, 1998
