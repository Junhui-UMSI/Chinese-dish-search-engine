Barbecue Sauce #12
Worcestershire Sauce, (8-oz) tomato sauce, Cayenne pepper, Chopped green onion tops, Dry mustard, Salt, Paprika, Brown sugar, Vinegar, Chili powder, 
Combine all ingredients except green onion tops. Simmer for 15 minutes,  stirring occasionally. Serve hot with onion sprinkled on top. Yield: 2-1/2  cups.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
