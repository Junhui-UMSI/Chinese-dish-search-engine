Apple Almond Wheat Bread Stuffing
Onions, Chopped Granny Smith apples;, Sliced almonds, Margarine, Celery, 
Put all ingredients above in pan and saute. Stir in 1 teaspoon cinnamon,  1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and toss with 6 cups of  bread cubes. Then add 1 cup raisins, 1 beaten egg, and 1/2 cup cider or  apple juice. Put in greased 9x5 inch loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30  minutes.
