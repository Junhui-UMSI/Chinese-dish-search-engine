Prepared Pancake Flour
Baking soda; heaping, Baking powder ; heaping, Salt, Flour; pastry, Corn meal, 
Mix and sift ingredients 3 times. Keep in dry place. To use, take 1 c.  mixture, add 1/2 c. dry bread crumbs. Beat 1 egg in cup, fill with  sweet or sour milk, stir into dry mixture, add a little more milk if  necessary to form a batter. Bake on hot griddle.    Source: Mrs. Ricka Witt, Springfield Grange, Williams County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
