Tangy Potted Cheese
Butter, Crackers or melba toast, Port or sherry, Fresh parsley sprigs (opt), Walnuts, coarsely chopped, Green onions, finely chopped, Caraway seeds, Coarsely ground mustard, Cheddar cheese, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Finely grate cheese into a bowl. Add butter and mix well.    Stir in port, green onions, caraway seeds, mustard and Worcestershire  sauce. Mix thoroughly until well combined.    Spoon mixture into a serving dish. Cover with walnuts and press walnuts  down lightly into mixture. Chill at least 2 hours. Serve with crisp  crackers or Melba toast and garnish with parsley sprigs, if desired.    VARIATION: Add 1 teaspoon chopped fresh herbs and a few pinches cayenne  pepper to taste.    NOTE: This spread will keep in a refrigerator for up to 5 days.
