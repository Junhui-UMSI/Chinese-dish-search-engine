Bailey=d5s Irish Cream Bars
For Cake:, White chocolate chips, Eggs, Cream cheese frosting,, For Swirl:, Yellow cake mix, Moist, Vegetable oil, 
Preheat oven to 350?. Prepare a jelly roll pan (about 15" x 10" x1"). Mix  cake mix with eggs, Irish Cream and oil. Set aside 1/2 cup of prepared cake  batter for swirl. Pour cake batter into prepared pan.    In a small bowl, blend all swirl ingredients. Drop large spoonfuls of this  mixture over the cake batter. Swirl with a fork to make a marble effect.  Bake 25 - 30 minutes. Cool and cut into squares.
