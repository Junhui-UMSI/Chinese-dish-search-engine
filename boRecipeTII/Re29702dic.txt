Tea Punch
Tea; strong, Orange juice, Lemon juice, Raspberry syrup; or grenadin, Sugar; to taste, Club soda; chilled, Pineapple, crushed, 
Combine all ingredients except club soda in a punch bowl with a large block  of clear ice; allow to chill thoroughly. (If fresh fruit juices are used,  they should be strained.) Add club soda just before serving.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy  Coleman.
