Almond Macaroons
(8 oz) almond paste, Salt, Sliced almonds, Egg whites, Vanilla, 
Grease and flour cookie sheet. Tap off excess flour.    Break up paste into blender. Add egg whites, salt and vanilla and beat at  low speed until smooth. Slowly add sugar and continue to blend until dough  forms.    Place rounded spoonful onto greased cookie sheet and bake at 325 for 20  minutes.    Yield: 36  Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #229 by lisamontag@juno.com (Lisa Montag)  on Aug 11, 1997
