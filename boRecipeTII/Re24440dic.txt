Tropical Fruit Smoothie
Unsweetened orange, , Soft tofu, banana, 
Combine all ingredients in the container of an electric blender; cover and  process until smooth. Yield: 5 cups (serving size: 1 cup).    Per serving: 146 Calories; 3g Fat (14% calories from fat); 5g Protein; 29g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 5mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve immediately.
