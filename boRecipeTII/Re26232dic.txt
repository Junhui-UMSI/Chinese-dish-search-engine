Tomatillo Salsa
Sweet onion, coarsely choppd, Fresh tomatillas, Fresh lime juice, Packed cilantro sprigs, Salt, Fresh jalapeno peppers, 
Recipe by: Sarah Reynolds and Miriam Rubin/ womans day Preparation Time:  0:15 From: CookieTstr@aol.com    Date: Thu, 23 May 1996 17:20:13 -0400    Remove papery husk from tomatilos, then rinse.  Cut in quarters ( youll  have about 3 cups)  Halve and remove stems of jalapenos; cut both in  quarters, remove seeds from them.    Put all ingredients into a food processor and process until smooth.    Posted to Master Cook Recipes List, Digest #96
