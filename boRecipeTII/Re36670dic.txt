Strawberry Cream Soup
Half and half, Pound cake, Fresh strawberries, Powdered sugar, Grenadine syrup, sour cream, Vanilla extract, 
Slice strawberries and mix with sour cream. Process briefly with food  processor or blender. Add vanilla and powdered sugar; process until smooth.  Pour into bowl and add half and half stirring until well blended. Chill.  Serves 6.    To make croutons: Cut pound cake into cubes. Toast in the oven under the  broiler until lightly brown.
