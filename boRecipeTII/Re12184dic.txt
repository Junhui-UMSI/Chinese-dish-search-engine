Zucchini Bread #02
Salt, Vanilla, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Pecans, Eggs, Grated raw zucchini, Honey, Flour, Cinnamon, Vegetable oil, 
Combine & mix together all ingredients except nuts & zucchini. Last, fold  in nuts & zucchini. Grease 2 small or 1 large loaf pan or a 10-inch tube  pan. Bake at 350 for 1 hour until done.
