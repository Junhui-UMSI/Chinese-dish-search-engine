Pineapple and Banana Dessert
Sugar, Nuts, chopped, Sweet cream, pineapple chunks, Egg, Miniature marshmallows, Bananas, Flour, 
Drain pineapple, reserving juice.  Place juice in saucepan; add cream,  sugar, flour and egg.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Pour hot  mixture over pineapple, bananas, marshmallows and nuts.    SOURCE: 1979 New Orleans Times-Picayune Recipe Contest Cookbook Typed for  you by Nancy Coleman
