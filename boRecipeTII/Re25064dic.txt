Tomato Spinach Sauce
3 cloves fresh garlic, 6 fresh basil leaves, Each fresh dill, thyme, and, 3 cups water, Spinach, Lipton Tomato-Herb soup, Ff spagetti sauce, 
Put all sauce in pot (rinse jar & use that too). Add soup powder.  Blenderize remaining ingredients using as much water as needed. Add to the  sauce and simmer until desired consistancy.
