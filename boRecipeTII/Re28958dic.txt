Thai Shrimp and Sesame Noodles-Newmans
Fresh cilantro leaves, Chopped, Crushed red pepper, Angel hair pasta, Peanut butter, Sesame oil, Shrimp, Light Italian Dressing, Green onions, Carrot, Fresh ginger root, Honey, Vegetable oil, Soy sauce, 
* Medium shrimp, shelled and deveined. ** Newmans Own Light Italian  Dressing. In medium bowl, mix shrimp with 1/3 cup Italian dressing. Cover  and refrigerate for 1 hour. In small bowl, use whisk to combine peanut  butter, soy sauce, honey, ginger, red pepper and remaining dressing; set  aside. After shrimp has marinated 1 hour, cook pasta according to package  directions; drain. Meanwhile, in 4-quart saucepan over high heat, heat  vegetable oil and sesame oil until very hot; cook carrot 1 minute. Drain  shrimp and discard dressing. Add shrimp and green onions to carrot in pan.   Cook, stirring constantly, about 3 minutes or until shrimp turn opaque  throughout. In large bowl, toss hot pasta with dressing mixture and shrimp  mixture.Sprinkle with chopped cilantro and serve immediately.
