Peppermint Angel Food Cake
Egg whites -- whipped, Salt, Granulated sugar, Vanilla, Unbleached flour, Crushed, Baking Powder, Peppermint candies --, Peppermint extract, 
Preheat oven at 375.  Unprepared 10" tube pan.  In a mixing bowl, combine  flour, candy, baking powder, salt, and sugar.  In another mixing bowl,  combine egg whites, peppermint extract, and vanilla.  Mix dry ingredients  with wet ingredients just until moistened.  Pour mixture into unprepared  pan.  Bake for 30 minutes.    Recipe By     : Cooking Light Guilt Free Desserts    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
