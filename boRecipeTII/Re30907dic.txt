Tangy Sweet and Sour Meatballs
meat, minced, garlic, Ketchup, Matzo meal, Ginger ale, Salt, Pepper, Egg, 
Combine first 6 ingredients and form into small balls. Combine ketchup and  ginger ale in a large saucepanand bring to boil. Drop meatballs into sauce,  cover and simmer for 2 hours.    Yield: 6 servings as an appetizer, 4 servings as a main course.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #011, by alotzkar@direct.ca (Al) on Mon,       13    Jan 1997.
