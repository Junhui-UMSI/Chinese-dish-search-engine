Annas Cream Cheese Pound Cake
Eggs, Sugar, Flour, (large) cream cheese, Sticks butter (preferably, 
From:    "Thomas J. May" <Matsubayas@AOL.COM>    Date:    Wed, 31 Jul 1996 19:20:19 -0400  *All ingredients should be at room temp.    Cream butter and cream cheese till REAL fluffy.  Add sugar gradually;add  eggs one at a time,beating well after each one.  Blend in flour.    Pour into greased and floured bundt pan.  Bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours then  remove PROMPTLY.    *NO MATTER WHAT IT LOOOKS LIKE IT, WILL NOT LOOK DONE.  Let cool 15  minutes,turn out of pan.    Cake freezes well,in fact it tastes better after freezing.    EAT-L Digest 30 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
