Apricot Delight
Quick-cooking tapioca, Vanilla, Ripe apricots, Sugar, Sugar, Toasted sliced almonds*, Lemon juice, Heavy whipping cream, 
*Note: Almonds are optional. Remove pits from apricots and cut into l/4  inch thick wedges. Cut each wedge in half crosswise. Place apricots into  hig-sided 2 quart microwave-safe casserole. Add 3/4 cup sugar, lemon juice  and tapioca. Stir until apricot slices are evenly coated. Let stand l5  minutes. Cover casserole with lid and microwave on HIGH (l00% power) l2 to  l5 minutes, stirring until mixture comes to full boil. Mixture is cooked  when beads of tapioca become translucent. Let mixture stand, covered, l0  minutes. Remove lid and cool to room temperature. Cover and refrigerate l  to 2 hours, until very cold. Whip cream with remaining 3 tbsp sugar and  vanilla until stiff peaks start to form. Fill 8 wine or dessert glasses  about l/3 full with apricot mixture. Cover apricot layer with about 3 tbs.  of whipped cream. Cover cream layer with remaining apricot mixture. Top  with dab of remaining whipped cream. Garnish with sliced almonds. Serve  immediately.
