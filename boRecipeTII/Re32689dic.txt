Sweet Potato and Pear Soup
Onion, S & P, Yams, Vegetable stock, Maple syrup, Bartlett pears, celery, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, 
Saute onion and celery by whatever means. Add potatoes and seasonings. Add  pears and maple syrup if desired. Stir in stock and simmer till potatoes  are tender. Puree and heat through. Serve with a swirl of ff yoghurt.    Posted to fatfree digest V97 #024 by sharon@netwave.ca (Sharon McCleave) on  Mar 12, 1997
