Strawberry Pretzel Squares
Strawberries, Sugar, Cold water, cream cheese, margarine, milk, Jello, Sugar, Cool Whip, pretzels, Boiling water, 
Notes: By Debbie Hosler, Tamaqua. The Times News, PA    Heat oven to 350?. Mix crushed pretzels, 1/3 cup sugar and margarine in  13x9 pan. Press firmly into bottom. Bake 10 min. cool. Beat cream cheese,  1/4 cup sugar, and milk until smooth. Gently stir in Cool Whip. Spread over  crust. Refrigerate. Stir boiling water into gelatin in large bowl 2 minutes  or until all dissolved. Stir in cold water. Refrigerate 1-1/2 hrs. or until  thickened.    Mix in strawberries. Spoon over cream cheese. Refrigerate for 3 hours. Keep  leftovers refrigerated.
