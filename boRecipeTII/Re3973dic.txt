Peppered Beef Dip
Sour cream, Pecans, (3-4 oz) thin-sliced beef;, Salt, TOPPING, Black pepper, Garlic salt, Butter or margarine, milk, DIP, Cream cheese, Finely chopped bell pepper, Dehydrated minced onion, 
Date: Mon, 26 Feb 96 15:13:21 EST    submitted by: MCTYREG@baylor.edu    From the kitchen of Kay Wellbaum, Waco, TX Cream the cream cheese, sour  cream and milk together until smooth. Add the other ingredients and stir  until well mixed.  Pour into a baking dish. Mix the pecans, salt and butter  together and spread on top of the dip. Bake at 350 for about 20 minutes.  Serve warm with crackers, chips and fresh vegetables.
