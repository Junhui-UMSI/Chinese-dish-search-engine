Quick and Easy Peanut Butter Fudge
Powdered sugar, Sticks butter, Peanut butter, Vanilla, 
Notes: By Pepper Klitsch, Palmerton. The Times News, PA.    Melt butter and peanut butter on medium high heat. Mix in powdered sugar  and vanilla. Only add a small amount of the sugar at a time, while  stirring. If it becomes too hard to mix, add a little milk. Press mixture  into 8x12 inch greased pan; refrigerate for about 3 hours. Turn pan over  onto wax paper, tap bottom of pan, cut fudge into 1 inch pieces.    Posted to recipelu-digest by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I Canty) on Mar 6, 1998
