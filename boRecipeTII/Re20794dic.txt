Apple Fritters
All Purpose Flour, CINNAMON GROUND 1 LB CN, MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, SHORTENING; 3LB, Water, EGGS SHELL, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, LEMON FRESH, Salt, NUTMEG GROUND, APPLE SLICED #10, Baking Powder, 
TEMPERATURE:  375 F. DEEP FAT  :    1.  SIFT TOGETHER FLOUR, BAKING POWDER, MILK, SALT, SUGAR, NUTMEG  AND CINNAMON INTO MIXER BOWL.    2.  COMBINE EGGS, WATER, SHORTENING OR SALAD OIL, AND LEMON JUICE;  ADD TO DRY INGREDIENTS.  MIX AT LOW SPEED UNTIL WELL-BLENDED.    3.  DRAIN APPLES AND CHOP COARSELY; ADD TO BATTER; MIX LIGHTLY.    4.  USING A NO. 40 SCOOP OR WELL-ROUND TABLESPOON, DROP BATTER  INTO DEEP FAT.  FRY 4 TO 6 MINUTES.  DRAIN ON ABSORBENT PAPER.  :    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 3, 6 LB (7 LB 11 OZ A.P.) PARED, CORED AND DICED  FRESH APPLES MAY BE USED FOR CANNED APPLES.    2.  IN STEP 3, 4 OZ (1 LEMON) FRESH LEMONS A.P. WILL YIELD 2 TBSP  JUICE.    3.  IN STEP 4, AFTER FRYING, FRITTERS MAY BE SPRINKLED WITH 2 LB  (2 QT) SIFTED POWDERED SUGAR.
