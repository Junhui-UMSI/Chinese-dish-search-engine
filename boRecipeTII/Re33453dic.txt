Basil Buttered Beans
Celery, Onions, Dry, Fresh Basil -- minced, or, Salt, Green Beans -- fresh, cut, Pieces, Butter Buds. -- or butter,, Into 2" pieces, Water, Melted, Pepper, 
REVISED: the original recipe called for 2 T butter or margarine melted    In a saucepan, combine beans, onion, celery and water.  Cover and cook for  5 min or until beans are tender.  Drain.  Add the butter, basil, salt and  pepper; stir to coat.  Serve immediately.    Recipe By     : Taste of Home, June/July 1994, pg16    Posted by reggie@netcom.com (Reggie Dwork) to the Fatfree Dig.
