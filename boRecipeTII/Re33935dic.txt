Sweet Avocado Puree (Mf)
Ripe chilled avocados;, Raspberries, Fresh lime or lemon juice, Fresh mint sprigs or chopped, 
With the back of a spoon, press the avocados through a sieve into a bowl.  Add the lime juice and sweeten with sugar.    Transfer the puree to 4 chilled wine glasses and garnish with raspberries,  mint and/or pistachio nuts.
