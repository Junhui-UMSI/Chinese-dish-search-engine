Pork Cardinal Style
Pepper, Garlicpowder, Butter, Apricots,dried, Thyme, Salt, Heavy cream, Vegetable oil, Brandy, Porkfilets, Rice Cooking Bags,Instant, 
1.The day before ,cover the dried apricots with water  and cook until they are soft. 2.Mix brandy and seasons  with 1 tablespoon oil and brush the meat with it from  all sides. and let it set for 30 minutes. 3.Cook rice  according to directions. 4.Heat the leftover oil and  fry the meat in it until it is browned on all  sides;add salt;take out meat and keep it warm. 5.Add  the apricots with the juice,leftover marinade and the  butter to the fryingfat in the skillet;stir and let  cook until it is reduced a little. 6.Add the cream and  taste sauce and season more if nessesary. 7.Cut meat  into slices and serve on rice with the apricots.
