Pistachio Pineapple Delight
Pistachio Jello, Cool Whip Lite, Pineapple Crushed, 
1. Pour pineapple with juice into mixing bowl, or mixer bowl.    2. Add pistachio mix, mix 2 - 3 minutes in mixer or Combine well by hand,  fold in Cool Whip.    3. Spoon into custard cups, cover with plastic, and put in refrigerator. It  will be soft set in 5 minutes. Can Microwave 60 seconds if it is frozen.
