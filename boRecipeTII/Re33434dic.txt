Basics- Rice
Water, Salt, Rice or brown rice, 
Basics for new cooks:    in saucepan, bring water and salt to boil; stir in rice. cover and reduce  heat to low; simmer white rice for 20 minutes, brown rice for 40 minutes,  or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed. Makes 2 cups, or 2  servings.
