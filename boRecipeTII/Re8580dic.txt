Potatoes Roasted with Garlic and Rosemary
Dried rosemary, Baking potatoes, garlic, olive oil, Salt, 
1. Preheat oven to 375.    2. Cut the potatoes in half lengthwise, then crosswise into 1 inch thick  pieces.    3. IN a 13 by 9 by 2 inch baking dish, stir together the oil, garlic and  rosemary and bake uncovered for 5 minutes..    4. Add the potatoes and bake uncovered, turning occasionally until tender  and crispy brown, 30 to 40 minutes.    5. Sprinkle with salt. Serve hot or at room temperature.
