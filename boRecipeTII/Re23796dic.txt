Asbourne Gingerbread
Golden syrup, Pinch of salt, Self-raising flour, Margarine, Ground ginger, 
Pre-heat oven to 350oF Mark 4. Sieve flour, salt and ginger together. Cream  the syrup, margarine and sugar in a bowl, then stir in the dry ingredients.  Knead to form a smooth dough. Form 1 inch balls, then roll into a sausage  shape and flatten slightly on a greased baking sheet with the back of a  fork. Bake until golden brown; approx. 15 to 20 mins. Cool on a wire rack.
