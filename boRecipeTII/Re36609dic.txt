Strawberry Buckle
Egg, Cake flour, sifted, Cinnamon, Sugar, Brown sugar, Baking Powder, TOPPING, Dash nutmeg, Fresh strawberries, sliced, Salt, Vanilla, Flour, sifted, Stick butter, softened, Stick butter, softened, milk, 
In large mixing bowl cream sugar and butter until light and fluffy.  Blend in eggs and vanilla then milk.  In separate bowl sift together  dry ingredients.  Stir into liquid mixture.  Fold in berries.  Spread  in greased, floured 9 x 9 inch baking pan.    Topping:    Cream butter and sugar.  Blend in flour and cinnamon.  Sprinkle over  batter.  Dash with nutmeg.  Bake in 375F oven 30-35 minutes.  Serve  warm with cream.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcyber4.zip
