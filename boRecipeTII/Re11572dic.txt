Raspberry Lemon Smoothie
Boiling water, Lemon slices; (opt.), Pineapple juice, Lemon herbal tea bags, Raspberry sherbet, 
In a teapot, pour boiling water over tea bags; cover and steep for 5  minutes. Remove bags. Chill tea. In a blender, process the tea, pineapple  juice and sherbet until smooth. Pour into glasses. Garnish with lemon if  desired. Source: Taste of Home mag, June/July 1996  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "M. Hicks" <nitro_ii@email.msn.com> on Feb 7,  1998
