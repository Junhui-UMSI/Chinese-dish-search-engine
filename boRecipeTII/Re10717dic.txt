Quick Strawberry Doughnuts
Oil for frying, Ripe strawberries, biscuits, 
Heat oil to 365 degrees. Halve biscuits and flatten into rounds 1/8-inch  thick. Place a strawberry, coated with sugar, in center of each round.  Gather edge to the top and twist or pinch to seal. Fry in oil, a few at at  time, about 2 minutes, or until browned, turning occasionally. Drain. Sift  sugar generously over hot doughnuts and serve immediately. (Interior of  doughnut is very juicy!)    MRS N.R.
