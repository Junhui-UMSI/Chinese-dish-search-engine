Potato Momos
Green onions, sliced, Momo sauce, see recipe, Soy sauce, Mashed potatoes, chopped, Chili powder, Minced ginger, Cilantro, chopped, Garlic, Garam masala, Salt, Fresh spinach, parboiled &, Onion, diced, Ghee, Pepper, Momo wrappers, see recipe, 
Melt ghee in a small skillet.  Add ginger, garlic & onion. Stir fry for 1  minute.  Add garam masala, chili powder, salt, pepper & soy sauce. Stir fry  for 30 seconds. Remove from heat & add mashed potatoes. Add spinach when  cool, green onion & cilantro.  Mix well. Put 1 tb of filling into each  wrapper, seal the edges closed with a little water. Steam over steaming  rack & serve hot with sauce.    Adapted from Betty Jung, "The Kopan Cookbook"
