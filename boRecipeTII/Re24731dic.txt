Audreys Oriental Chicken
Cornstarch, 15 oz. can pineapple chunks, Boneless chicken breasts*, Vegetable oil, Vinegar, Strips, green pepper, Thin onion rings, Soy sauce, Brown Sugar, 
*Cut into thin strips.  In a medium bowl, combine sugar and cornstarch.  Gradually stir in vinegar  and soy sauce. Stir in pineapple, green pepper and onion, set aside.  Heat  oil in wok. Add chicken and stir fry, just until tender, and chicken has  turned white.  Add pineapple and vegetable mixture; stir until well mixed.  Cover and simmer over low heat 15 minutes.
