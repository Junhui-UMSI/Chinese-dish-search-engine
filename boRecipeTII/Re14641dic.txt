"Philly-Style" White Pizza
Asst. shredded cheeses, olive oil, Green pepper; sliced, Parmesan cheese, Onion; sliced, Mushrooms; sliced, Worchestershire sauce, Roast beef; shaved, Garlic powder, Pepper, Crushed garlic, Loaf frozen white bread, 
Saute vegetables in 1 tb. olive oil until limp; add roast beef.  Saute for  three more minutes.  Add Worchestershire sauce and seasonings; cook 2-3  minutes more and remove from heat.  Set aside.    Dust work surface with flour and roll out bread dough to fit a 10" x 15"  cookie sheet.  Grease cookie sheet; fit dough into pan, making a rim of  dough around edges of pan. Brush with remaining 2 tb. olive oil and spread  crushed garlic over entire surface of dough. Top with a light layer of  shredded cheese, then meat/vegetable mixture, distributing evenly. Top with  remaining shredded cheese, then Parmesan. Bake at 400 degrees F. for 20-25  minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Let sit 5 minutes before  cutting and serving.
