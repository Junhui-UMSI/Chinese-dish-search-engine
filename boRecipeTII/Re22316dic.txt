Turkey-Bean Chili
Cloves garlic -- minced, Tomato sauce; no salt -- (8, Ground turkey, Cannellini beans -- drained, Each), Each, Onion -- chopped, Black & cayenne pepper --, Canned, Vegetable oil, Oregano -- crumbled, Vinegar, Tomatoes, Water, Chili powder, Long grain white rice, Black beans -- drained and, Canned, 
Saute onion and garlic in oil in large saucepan over medium heat until  tender.  Increase heat to medium-high. Add turkey; cook until browned. Add  chili powder, oregano, black and cayenne peppers; cook 30 seconds. Add  tomatoes, tomato sauce and water.  Simmer, covered, 30 minutes. Meanwhile,  cook rice following package directions. Stir beans and vinegar into chili.  Remove 1 1/2 cups chili and 3/4 cup cooked rice for Chili Vegetable Soup.  Source: Family Circle 2/22/94. MM by Lyn.
