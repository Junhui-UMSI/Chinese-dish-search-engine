Tart Kebab Sauce
Salt, Cider vinegar, Raisins, Dates, pitted, chopped, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Ginger, ground, 
Mix the ingredients together then put into a blender and `puree them into  a smooth mixture. Heat the mixture in a sauce pan until thoroughly heated.  Use as a warm dipping sauce for kebabs.
