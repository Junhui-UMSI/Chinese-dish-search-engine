Apricot Nut Loaf
milk, Pecans, Orange juice, Shortening, Egg, Finely chopped dried, Grated orange rind, Flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, 
Combine sugar, shortening and egg. Stir in milk, juice and rind. Sift  flour, baking powder, salt and add to other mixture. Combine apricots and  nuts; add to batter. Pour into greased loaf pan and let stand 20 minutes.  Bake at 350 for 50-60 minutes.
