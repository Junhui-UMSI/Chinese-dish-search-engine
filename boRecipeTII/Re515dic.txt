Frankenstein Salads
(6 oz. ea.) lime gelatin, Boiling water, Bean sprouts, Orange jelly beans, Red jelly beans, Sour cream, Miniature marshmallows, Purple kale, optional, 
In a bowl, dissolve gelatin in water.  Pour into a 8 inch square pan that  has been coated with nonstick cooking spray. Refrigerate for 4 hours or  until firm. Cut into six rectangles; place each on a plate. Decorate with  bean sprouts for hair, orange jelly beans for eyes and red jelly beans for  nose.  Fill a small plastic bag with sour cream; cut a small hole in the  corner of the bag.  Pipe a jagged smile on face. Place marshmallows on side  of head for bolts.  Garnish plates with kale if desired. Yield: 6 servings.
