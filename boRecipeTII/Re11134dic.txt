Raisin Brown Bread
Salt, Baking soda, All-Purpose Flour, Molasses, Buttermilk or sour milk, Raisins, Whole wheat flour, 
Mix the flours and salt in a bowl.  Combine molasses, baking soda and sour  milk or buttermilk and add to the mixture. After beating it until smooth  add the raisins. Bake in a greased loaf pan for 1 hour at 350F. Serve with  baked beans. Source: Heritage Collection ch.
