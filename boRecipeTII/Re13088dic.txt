Wish-Bone Marinated Steak Italiano
Wish-Bone Italian dressing, Boneless sirloins; top loin, 
In shallow dish, pour 1/2 cup dressing over steak. Cover and marinate in  refrigerator 3 to 24 hours. Remove steak, discard marindade. Grill or broil  , turning once and basting with remaining dressing, until steak is done.  Serve with grilled marinated vegetables. Makes 8 servings.
