Banana Soft Serve
Vanilla extract, Sugar, Part-skim Ricotta cheese, Ripe; frozen bananas, 
Date: 06 Mar 96 13:57:04 -0800    From: Lawren.Freebody@salata.com (Lawren Freebody)  In a food processor, combine frozen bananas with remaining ingredients.  Using a steel blade, process until smooth. Divide into 4 individual serving  bowls and serve right away. "As rich as ice cream and as smooth as frozen  custard, this dessert is a real prize."    Each serving provides 109 calories, 4 g protein, 3 g fat, 18 g carboydrate,       39 mg sodium, 10 mg cholesteral.
