Plum Ginger Sauce (Served with Ecrevisse Strudel)
Butter, Ginger -- grated, Red wine vinegar, Granulated garlic, Granulated sugar, Plum preserves -- purreed, 
Heat Skillet, melt butter.  Add grated inger, saute 2 minutes.  Add  remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil. Strain.  Serve Hot. Serve with  Ecrevisse Strudel with two Sauces.
