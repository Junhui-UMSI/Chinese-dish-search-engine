Potato Soup (Cream Style)
Mrs. dash; or substitute, Celery; diced, Potatoes; peel & dice, Onion; diced, Flour, Water; * see note, Dill weed, Nonfat milk; or substitute, Chives; chopped, 
Recipe by: The Mormon Diet Cookbook Preparation Time: 1:00 Place potatoes,  onion, celery and water in a lg saucepan. Add dill weed, Mrs. Dash and  chives; simmer until potatoes are tender, about 30 min. Place milk and  flour in a pint jar. Shake vigorously to mix thoroughly. Add to potato  mixture; simmer 5 - 10 min, or until thickened.    VARIATIONS: Add 1/4 ts ground pepper, 1 ts basil leaves and/or 1/2 ts  garlic powder in addition to or instead of other herbs. Blend part of soup  mixture in blender if desired.
