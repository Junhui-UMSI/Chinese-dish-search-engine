Baked Cheese Omelette
liquid red pepper, ham, Baking Powder, Salt, Tarragon, dried, Butter, sage, dried, Thyme, Flour, Monterey Jack cheese, Eggs, Small curd cottage cheese, 
Coat 4 (1-cup) bakers with butter, using 1 teaspoon per dish. Saute ham in  remaining butter about 5 minutes. Place in baking dishes. Beat eggs with  salt, pepper seasoning, sage, thyme, tarragon, flour and baking powder.  Stir in cheeses. Spoon into baking dishes. Bake in 400 degree oven 15  minutes. Reduce heat to 325 degrees and bake 15 minutes longer or until  puffed and golden.    Makes 4 servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "bunny" <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Feb 26,  1998
