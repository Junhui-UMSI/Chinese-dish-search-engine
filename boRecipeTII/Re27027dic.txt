Three-Berry Compote
Sliced fresh strawberries, Fresh blackberries, Maple syrup, Triple sec or other, Fresh mint sprigs,, Ground nutmeg, Fresh blueberries, Vanilla extract, 
Combine first 7 ingredients in a bowl, and stir well. Let stand 10 minutes.  Yield: 4 servings (serving size: 1 cup).    Per serving: 114 Calories; 1g Fat (6% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 26g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 5mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Garnish with mint sprigs, if desired.    Recipe by: Cooking Light, Jul/Aug 1994, page 124    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #413 by igor@digex.net on Jan 28, 1997.
