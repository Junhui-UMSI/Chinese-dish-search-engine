Tuna and Cheese Swirls Casserole
milk, Cream of mushroom soup, Lemon juice, Tuna, salt and pepper, Bisquick biscuits, Onion, Flour, Crisco, 
Brown onion in Crisco; add flour and blend. Add milk and stir until  thickened. Add soup and tuna, juice and seasonings. Put into greased  casserole. Top with Bisquick biscuits. Add grated cheese to biscuits if  desired. Bake 400 degrees until biscuits are browned.
