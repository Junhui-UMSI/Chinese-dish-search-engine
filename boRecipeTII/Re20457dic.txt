Vegan- Middle Eastern Style Black Bean Salad
fresh mint, red onion, Tomato, Chopped parsley, Green pepper, Cucumber, black beans, 
Combine beans with rest of ingredients.    Salad Dressing    1/4 cup lemon juice 1 teaspoon cumin 1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste 1/4  teaspoon pepper 1/4 cup olive oil    Mix lemon juice with cumin, salt and pepper.  Beat in olive oil. Fold into  reserved salad.  Chill several hours to enhance flavor.    Enjoy!    ~Laury
