Baked Carrots
Salt, Sugar, Boiling water, Carrots, Butter, Cinnamon, 
Scrape or pare carrots and place in casserole. Cream butter, sugar, salt  and cinnamon together. Add water and blend well. Pour over carrots, cover  and bake in 350 degree oven for 90 minutes.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
