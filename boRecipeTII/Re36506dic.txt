Strawberries and Molded Devonshire Cream
Envelope (1 Tbsp) unflavored, Sugar, Vanilla, Cold water, Whipping cream, Fresh strawberries, sliced, Sour cream, at room, 
1.   In saucepan, sprinkle gelatin over water; then dissolve over low heat.  Stir into sour cream, whisking lightly to smooth out lumps. Set aside.    2.   In mixing bowl, beat cream, gradually adding sugar, until soft peaks  form.  Stir in vanilla and sour-cream-gelatin mixture.    3.   Pour into 1-quart mold, rinsed first with cold water. Chill until  firm.  Unmold on serving plate. Surround with sliced berries, sweetened to  taste, if needed. Slice mold to serve and top each serving with berries.
