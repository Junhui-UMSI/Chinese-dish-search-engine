Festive Spinach Salad
Low-sodium chicken broth;, Pre-washed spinach leaves, Canned mandarin oranges;, Ground ginger, Salt, Black Pepper, Olive oil; up to 2tbs, Sliced red beets, Cashews; chopped coarsely, DRESSING, Lemon juice, Honey; PLUS, Honey, Dijon-style mustard, 
n a bowl, whisk together lemon juice, honey, mustard, ginger, salt and  pepper. Add the broth. Slowly whisk in olive oil. Set aside.    In a large bowl, toss the spinach with the dressing. Divide among six  plates. Scatter the mandarin orange slices and the sliced beets over the  spinach. Garnish with the chopped cashews.    Counts as 2 1/2 servings toward individuals 5 A Day.
