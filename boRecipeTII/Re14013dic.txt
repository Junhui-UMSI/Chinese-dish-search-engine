White Chocolate Drizzle
See below, 
Melt together 3 oz white chocolate pieces & 1 tsp. vegetable oil in a  small saucepan over very low heat. Spoon into small plastic bag,  squeezing to one corner. Snip off corner; drizzle over frosting or  topping on bar cookies.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
