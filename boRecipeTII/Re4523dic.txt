Peta Lentil Carrot Burgers
Cooked lentils; mashed, Water, Fresh parsley, Grated carrots, Oil for frying, Bread crumbs, Onion, Tomato paste, Salt to taste, 
Cook the onion and carrot in the water until tender, about 10 minutes.  Drain off excess water, then combine the onion and carrot with the  remaining ingredients except oil. Form into patties and fry in a lightly  oiled frying pan until browned on both sides and heated through, about 10  minutes.    >From The Compassionate Cook by PETA and Ingrid Newkirk Typed by Lisa  Greenwood recipes@soar.Berkeley.EDU.
