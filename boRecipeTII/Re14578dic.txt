"Hot" Artichoke Dip
Artichoke hearts, drained, Grated Parmesan cheese, RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and, Mayonnaise, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic powder, 
Thoroughly combine all ingredients. Place in a 1-quart casserole.  Bake in a 375F. oven for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Let  cool 10 minutes before serving. Makes 2 1/2 cups dip.    From "Southwestern Sensations Starring RO*TEL", courtesy Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sw-sense.zip
