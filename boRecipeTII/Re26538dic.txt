Bacon-Oyster Bites
Herb-seasoned stuffing mix, Bacon; halved, and partially, (5-oz) smoked oysters;, Water, 
Combine oysters, herb-stuffing mix and water. Form into balls, using about  1 tablespoon mixture for each. Wrap a half slice of bacon around each and  secure with a toothpick. Place on a rack in a shallow baking pan and cook  at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes. Makes 16.    Posted to FOODWINE Digest  by Laurie Thompson <elelt@IBM.NET> on Jan 20,  1998
