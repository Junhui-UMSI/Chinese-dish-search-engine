Swiss Apple Pudding
Apples, Sugar, Flour, Cloves; ground, Baking Powder, Ginger, Egg, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Butter or margarine, Water, 
Recipe by: Sue Klapper Preparation Time: 0:40 Preheat oven to 350. Peel,  core and slice apples. Distribute apple slices evenly in pan. Combine  flour, sugar, baking powder and spices. Add egg and mix until crumbly.  Cover apples with flour mixture.  Dot with butter and sprinkle with 1/2 cup  water.  Bake for 30 to 40 minutes or until apples are tender and top is  golden brown.  Serve warm with vanilla or cinnamon ice cream, or whipped  cream. Source: The Pampered Chefs Favorites.  Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest by angstrom@juno.com (Angela  L Gilliland)  on Apr 15, 1998
