Sweet Potato Pie 2
Vanilla, Buttermilk, pie crust, Sugar, Sweet potatoes, Sticks butter, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. In large bowl mash potatoes while still hot .  Add butter and mix well. Stir in sugar and eggs. Add milk and vanilla. Stir  to combine. Pour into pie crusts. Bake for 1 hour. Serve hot or room  temperature.
