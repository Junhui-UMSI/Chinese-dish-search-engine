Whole-Egg Yellow Cake (Microwave)
Baking Powder, Flour, Sugar, milk, Salt, Eggs, Orange peel; grated (opt), 
Place all ingredients in mixing bowl.  Blend at low speed, then beat at  medium speed 2 minutes. Spread batter in 9-inch glass baking dish.  Microwave at 50 percent power 6 minutes, rotating 1/4 turn every 3 minutes.  Increase power to High. Microwave 1 to 5 minutes, or until done. Let cake  stand directly on counter top 5 to 10 minutes.     Variation:  Poppy Seed Cake;  add 2 tbsp. poppy seed t milk and mix as  directed.
