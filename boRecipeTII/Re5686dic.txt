Pineapple-Barbecued Lamb Spare Ribs
Lamb spare ribs, Vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Honey, Pepper, Salt, Garlic, Pineapple, crushed, Ginger, 
1. Place spare ribs on a rack in a shallow roasting pan; bake in preheated  325F. oven 1 1/2 hours.    2. Drain off drippings.    3. Combine remaining ingredients, mixing well; pour over lamb.    4. Bake 45 minutes, basing lamb with pineapple mixture frequently during  baking period.
