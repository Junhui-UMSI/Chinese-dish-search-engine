Chicken with Vinegar Peppers
Whole chicken, cut up, olive oil, Garlic cloves, sliced, Wine vinegar, Jar sweet vinegar peppers,, Parlsey, chopped, Bay Leaves, Oregano, Mushrooms, sliced, Salt/pepper, 
Brown chicken in olive oil and garlic. Add all other ingredients except for  peppers and reserved liquid. Cook uncovered for 45 minutes. Add peppers and  liquid. Cover and steam cook for 15 additional minutes.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  17, 1998
