Basil Mashed Potatoes
Chopped basil leaves, Garlic, Salt, Grated ginger, Lemon juice, Heavy cream, Baking potatoes,; peeled, Stick unsalted butter, 
In a large saucepan of salted water bring potatoes to a boil. Simmer about  20 minutes or until tender. Drain. Pass potatoes through a ricer into a  mixing bowl. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan combine cream and butter. Bring  to a boil and keep hot until potatoes are cooked. With a mixer or electric  beater whip potatoes on slow speed, slowly drizzling in cream mixture. Do  not overmix. Fold in lemon juice, basil, ginger and garlic.
