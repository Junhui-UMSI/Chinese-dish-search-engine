Lemon Chicken
Lemon; thinly sliced, Chicken breast halves with, Plus 2 tsp. unsalted butter;, Plus 2 tsp. lemon juice, Lemon pepper seasoning, Parsley, 
Prep: 5 min, Cook: 30 min.    Preheat oven to375F. Arrange lemon slices in bottom of a baking dish. Top  with chicken breasts. Combine melted butter and lemon juice in a bowl and  pour over chicken. Sprinkle evenly with lemon pepper and parsley. Bake  30-35 minutes, or until chicken is tender.  Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 04, 1998
