Banana Bake
Sugar, Butter, Light corn syrup, Bananas, peeled, Finely chopped pecans, Raisins, Water, Lemon juice, 
Dip bananas in lemon juice; arrange in a greased 2 qt. baking dish.  Sprinkle with raisins & pecans.    In a small saucepan, combine sugar, syrup and water and cook over medium  heat until bubby. Spoon syrup over bananas, dot with butter. Cover and bake  at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Serve with whipped cream if desired.    From the daily newspaper, the Arkansas Democrat-Gazette.
