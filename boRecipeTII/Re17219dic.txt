Vons Shrimp De Jonghe
Mace, Cooked shrimp, Fresh parsley -- chopped, Grated bread crumbs, Tarragon, Ground nutmeg, Butter, Dry sherry, Salt, Black pepper, Garlic, Green Onions, Chervil -- chopped, 
Peel the garlic and mash with a fork; work it into the butter. Gradually  add the rest of the herbs and seasonings. Mix in the crumbs and sherry.  Arrange the cooked shrimp in buttered individual ramekins and top with the  mixture. Bake in a 450-degree oven for 5-8 minutes, or until thoroughly hot  and bubbly, or run under the broiler for 2 or 3 minutes.
