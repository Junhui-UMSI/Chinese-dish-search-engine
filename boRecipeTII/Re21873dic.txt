Apple-Scallop Ramekins
Apples,Granny Smith,med-size, Lemon juice, Salt, Basil,dried, Butter or margarine, Bay scallops, Mushrooms,sliced,drained(3oz, Bread crumbs,plain,dry, Flour, milk, Onion,minced, Parsley,minced, 
1. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in large skillet.   2. Add onion; saute until tender.   3. Add scallops, apples, mushrooms, lemon juice, parsley, basil and salt;  cook over medium heat, 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.   4. Meanwhile, melt 2 tablespoons butter in a small saucepan; blend in  flour.   5. Remove from heat; stir in milk.   6. Return to medium heat; cook, stirring constantly, until thickened.   7. Add sauce to scallop mixture in skillet; mix well.   8. Spoon into 4 buttered ramekins.   9. Combine remaining butter with bread crumbs; sprinkle over scallops.   10. Place under broiler; broil 3 to 4 minutes, or until crumbs are golden.
