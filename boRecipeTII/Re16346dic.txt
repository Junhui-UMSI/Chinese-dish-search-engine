Watkins Peanut Sauce
WATKINS Garlic Powder, Creamy peanut butter, Fresh Lemon Juice, Dark molasses, Hot water, Reduced-sodium soy sauce, Granules, WATKINS Red Pepper Flakes, 
In small  bowl, whisk peanut butter into hot water.  Add remaining  ingredients and whisk until smooth.  Cover and refrigerate until serving  time. Bring to room temperature before serving.    Note: For the peanut butter do not use old-fashioned, home style or freshly  ground.
