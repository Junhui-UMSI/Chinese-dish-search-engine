Baked Stuffed Pumpkin
Sweet corn, Butter, Instant barley, Peppers; chopped, Ground beef; to 1-lb., Pumpkin, Onion, Cabbage; chopped, Brown sugar; divided, 
Preheat oven to 350~.  Cut top from pumpkin and clean. Coat inside with  butter and 1/4 cup brown sugar. Saute ground beef, onion, cabbage, and  peppers.  Add corn and barley; stuff into pumpkin. Add remaining brown  sugar on top.  Put on pumpkin top.  Place in shallow pan with 2" of hot  water.  Bake for 1.1/2 to 2 hrs., or until tender.    Source:  Home Cooking (11/96)    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Oct 3, 1998
