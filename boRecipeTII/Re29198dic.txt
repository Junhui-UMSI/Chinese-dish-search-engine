Baked Sea Scallops
Butter, Sea scallops, Fresh bread crumbs, Sherry, salt and pepper, 
Slice scallops in half and place in a buttered casserole dish. Season with  salt and pepper. Combine butter, sherry and crumbs. Pour over scallops.  Bake at 350 degs for 10-15 mins.
