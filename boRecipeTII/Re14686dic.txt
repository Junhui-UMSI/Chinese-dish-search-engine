"Tnt" Peanut Butter Cookies
Sugar, Eggs, Peanut butter, Vanilla, Baking soda, Butter, Flour, Brown sugar, Salt, 
Cream the sugars and butter together. Mix the eggs, vanilla, peanut butter  together and add to the sugar/butter mixture. Blend flour with the soda and  salt, then add to the other mixture. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased  baking sheet. Bake 350 F. for 10 minutes    Have fun!! My husband really enjoyed these cookies.
