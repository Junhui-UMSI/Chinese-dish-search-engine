Persian Cucumber Dip
Pd, Salt to taste, Cuke -- peeled and diced, Pita bread, Plain yogurt, Onion -- grated, Fresh mint leaves, EACH raisins and chopped, 
Combine all ingredients except bread and chill up to 72 hrs.  Cut pitas  into triangles and toast under broiler; use as dippers.  Makes 2 Cups dip.  Posted by SSG SPEAR    Recipe By     : Vegetarian Favorites    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
