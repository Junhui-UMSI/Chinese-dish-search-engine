Quick Beef Stew
Celery, Water, Beef, Carrots, Unbleached flour, Potatoes, Onion Soup Mix, 
*     2 to 2 1/2 Cups cut-up Cooked Beef  **    Carrots to be cut into 2 1/2-inch Strips  ***   Potatoes to be pared and cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces    Mix all ingredients in 2 1/2 Qt casserole.  Cover and microwave on high  (100%) to boiling, 10 to 12 minutes; stir.  Cover and let stand 5 minutes  and microwave until vegtables are tender,10 to 12 minutes more, stirring  every 5 minutes.
