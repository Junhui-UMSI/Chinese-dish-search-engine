Ahuvas Strudel
Chocolate spread, Cocoa; cinnamon and sugar, Flour, Baking Powder, Water, Margarine, Vegetable oil, (scant) sugar, FILLINGS, CHOOSE FROM, Prune levka; mixed with, Date spread; mixed as above, Strawberry jam, 
Mix all the ingredients together into a dough. Divide into eight balls.  Refrigerate for two hours or more, covered with saran wrap.    Roll out on a floured surface to a rectangle. Spread with choice of  fillings, and roll jellyroll fashion.    Bake in a moderate oven until golden brown. This freezes very well. Serve  sliced.
