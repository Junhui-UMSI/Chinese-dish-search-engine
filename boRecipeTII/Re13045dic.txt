Winter Strawberries
(3 oz.) size Jello, Finely chopped grated, Finely grated coconut, (14 oz.) Eagle brand, Red sugar, Vanilla, 
Combine all ingredients (except red sugar). Mix well, chill for 1 hour.  Then form mixture into strawberry shapes and roll in red sugar to coat.  Make green leaves with green icing that comes in tube or can from grocery.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 19, 1998
