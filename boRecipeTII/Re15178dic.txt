Wheres the Fat Chocolate Sauce
Evaporated skimmed milk, Cornstarch, Vanilla, Unsweetened cocoa powder, Sugar, 
In a small saucepan, stir together sugar, cocoa powder and cornstarch. Add  milk and cook, stirring over Mediu heat until thickened and bubbly. Cook  and stir for 2 minutes more. Remove from heat. Stir in vanilla. Serve the  sauce warm or cool. Note: Refrigerate leftove sauce for up to one week.  Reheat, uncovered in Microwave on High until warm.
