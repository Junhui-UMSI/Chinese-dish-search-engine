Pizza Dough #02
Yeast, Bread flour, Water, Salt, olive oil, 
Load ingredients into breadmachine pan according to manufacturers  directions. "Yeast" refers to active dry yeast. Use dough mode. Remove and  let rest covered 15 minutes. Roll or stretch to 14" circle. Top with choice  of toppings. Bake 15-20 minutes at 500o until crust is golden. From Clark,  Bread Machine Cookbook.
