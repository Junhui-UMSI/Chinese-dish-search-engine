Quick and Creamy Applesauce Dessert (Lacto)
Apple pie spice, Nonfat yogurt, Vanilla extract, Applesauce; unsweetened, Sugar, 
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mix thoroughly. Serve right away or  chill for later serving. This was altered to lofat from the original  recipe. Also to reduce the calories you can use a sugar substitute to equal  the sugar.
