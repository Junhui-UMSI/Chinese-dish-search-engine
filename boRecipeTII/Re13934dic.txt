Wine Marinade a la Maria Nocole
Beef or lamb, Lemon, Dry white wine, Bay leaf; crumbled, White pepper; freshly ground, Garlic, Dried Oregano, 
Squeeze the lemon directly into a bowl. Whisk in the remaining ingredients,  adding a liberal amount of pepper. Marinate the meat on both sides for a  few hpurws or overnight. When ready to broil, drain the meat and baste with  the reserved marinade while cooking.    Calories and nutrients are negligable. Do not have to count.
