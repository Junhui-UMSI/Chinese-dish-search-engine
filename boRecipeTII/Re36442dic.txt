Beer Garden Spread
Unsalted butter; room temp., Fresh vegetables of choice;, Capers, Pequin quebrado or cayenne, Caraway seeds, (2-oz) anchovy fillets;, Small-curd cottage cheese, Onions, Dry mustard, Fresh grated horseradish, 
From the Bierstuben of Bavaria--but enhanced with horseradish, mustard &  pequin. Serve any favorite seasonal vegetables alongside. In a food  processor, process all ingredients (except vegetables) until smooth. Place  in a crock or pottery bowl & refrigerate at least 2 hours or until firm. To  serve, place on a platter, attractively arrange vegetables around crock of  dip. Dunk vegetables in dip or spread dip on vegetables with a knife. Makes  about 2 cups.
