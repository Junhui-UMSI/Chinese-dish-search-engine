Peanut Butter Bites
Creamy peanut butter, Vanilla, Skim milk, Quick cooking oats, Diet margarine, Equal, Raisins, 
Source: The Free and Equal Sweet Tooth Cookook    In a heavy saucepan, bring milk, margarine, and peanut butter to a boil.  Remove from heat. Add raisins, vanilla, and Equal. Mix well. Work in oats,  a cup at a time. Form into small balls about 1 1/2 in. Diameter. Place on a  cookie sheet. Chill. Keep Refrigerated and covered.
