Peaches in Primitivo Syrup
Sugar, Clove, Primitivo di Gioia, a dry, Peaches, 
In a medium saucepan, combine clove, sugar and red wine and heat until  sugar is dissolved, about 10 minutes. Cool and keep covered. Peel and slice  peaches and divide among 4 wine glasses. Pour 1/4 cup syrup over each peach  and serve immediately with biscotti.
