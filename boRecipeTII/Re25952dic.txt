Bacon Cheddar Cheese Spread
Green onions with tops;, Cheddar Cheese, Crisp bacon; crumbled, Sour cream, 
Mix bacon, cheese and onion with sour cream until well blended. Serve with  Olive Bread Ring (See recipe). May also be served separately with crackers.    NANCY COUCH LEE    (MRS. JAMES M. JR.)
