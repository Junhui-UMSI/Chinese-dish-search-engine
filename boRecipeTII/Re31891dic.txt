Tabbouleh Salad
Rich vegetable stock,;, olive oil, Grated lemon zest, Fresh Lemon Juice, Minced fresh mint, Minced scallion, Fine bulgur wheat, Salt, Minced fresh parsley, Garlic, 
In a bowl, combine the bulgur, the boiling stock, and the olive oil and  cover. Allow to stand for at least 30 minutes, then uncover and fluff with  a fork. Stir in the parsley, mint, scallion, garlic, lemon juice and lemon  zest. Correct the seasoning with salt and pepper.
