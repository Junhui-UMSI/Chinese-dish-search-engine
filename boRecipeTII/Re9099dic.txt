Pronto Pup Mix
-(up to), Yellow corn meal, Bag (7-lb) Krusteaz pakcake, Cayenne (ground red pepper), Dry mustard, Baking Powder, 
Mix all ingredients together. Store in an air-tight container for future  use. For a regular batch, use 3 cups of the mix and add just enough water  to make a stiff batter. Coat wieners with batter and deep-fry in oil.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
