Chicken and Artichokes
olive oil, Tarragon Vinegar, Garlic, artichoke hearts, Parsley, Bread crumbs, seasoned, Boneless chicken breasts, Salt/pepper, 
Cut chicken into bite size pieces and roll in bread crumbs. Heat oil and  saute garlic to a golden color and remove. Add chicken, saute to a light  brown on both sides. Place chicken in a large baking dish, add sauteed  garlic, salt, pepper, and vinegar. Sprinkle with parsley. Bake at 350  degrees for 30 minutes. Add artichokes and bake 10 minutes more.
