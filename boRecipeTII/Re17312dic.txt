Waldorf Salad
Raisins, (8-oz ) container low fat, Apples, cored, cubed, Walnuts, Stalks celery, chopped, 
This slimmed-down apple lovers favorite uses a dressing of creamy low-fat  lemon yogurt to replace fat and calorie rich mayonnaise.    In large bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well. Cover; refrigerate until  serving time. 9 (1/2-cup) servings.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
