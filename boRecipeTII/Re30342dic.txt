Banana Condiment
Very ripe bananas, Lemon, Raisins, 
At least 1 day before, place bananas in fridge to blacken. Mash bananas  till almost smooth.  Add juice & raisins. In a pot, bring to a boil,  stirring constantly till mixture thickens, about 10 minutes. Cool slightly  before serving.  Can be stored in the fridge for 2 or 3 weeks. Serve with  jolof rice.  Makes 1 cup.    "Vegetarian Times" December, 1993
