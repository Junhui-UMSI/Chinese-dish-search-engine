Purple Pudding
AM Blue Cornmeal, Grape juice, Natural sweetener to taste, Plain yogurt, 
Combine juice and blue cornmeal in a saucepan, and stir over low heat with  a wire whisk until very thick.  Combine mixture with yogurt and sweetener  and beat with an electric mixer or in a blender, until creamy and smooth.  Pour into small bowls and chill.
