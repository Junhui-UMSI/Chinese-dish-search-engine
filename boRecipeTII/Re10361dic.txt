Quick Cheesy Grits
Cheddar cheese, Hominy grits, Black Pepper, Water, Garlic, Salt to taste, Unsalted butter, 
In a medium saucepan with the water boiling, add the salt. Slowly stir in  the grits. Reduce the heat and cook until grits become very thick. Remove  grits from the heat and let sit. On a cutting board, mash the garlic cloves  using the flat side of a large knife. Shred the cheese, using oil on the  grater to keep from sticking. Into the saucepan with grits, mix the garlic  butter, cheese and pepper. Stir until combine and until the cheese has  melted. Serve to taste and serve. Approximately 7 minutes.
