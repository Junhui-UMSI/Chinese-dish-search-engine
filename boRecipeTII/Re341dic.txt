Lemon Basil Butter
Butter, Lemon juice, Fresh Basil; Chopped, 
In work bowl of food processor fitted with steel knife, process butter with  lemon juice until light and fluffy. Stir in basil.    NOTES : Spread this savory butter on your favorite bread or rolls.  Its  great over vegetables, too!  Recipe by: Dierbergs Home Economists    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Jill & Joe Proehl <jpxtwo@swbell.net> on Apr  05, 1998
