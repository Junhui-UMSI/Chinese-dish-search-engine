Whipped Topping
MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, WATER; COLD, TOPPING DES & BAK, IMITATION VANILLA, 
1.  PLACE COLD WATER IN MIXER BOWL; ADD TOPPING, MILK, AND VANILLA.  USING  WHIP AT LOW SPEED, WHIP 3 MINUTES OR UNTIL WELL BLENDED.  SCRAPE DOWN  BOWL.    2.  WHIP AT HIGH SPEED 5 TO 10 MINUTES OR UNTIL STIFF PEAKS FORM.  COVER;  REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.  :    NOTE:  WHEN TOPPING IS USED FOR ICING CAKES, FOLD 1 LB (1 QT) SIFTED  POWDERED SUGAR INTO WHIPPED TOPPING.
