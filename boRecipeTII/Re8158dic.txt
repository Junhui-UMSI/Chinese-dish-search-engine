Potato Croquettes
Vegetable cooking spray, Egg white, lightly beaten, Coarsely crushed corn flakes, Baking potatoes, peeled, Nonfat margarine, Salt, & sliced, (1 oz) shredded low-fat, Swiss or cheddar cheese, Prepared or dijon mustard, Hot sauce, Nonfat cream cheese product, Chopped fresh chives, 
Cook potato in boiling water for 20 minutes, or until  tender; drain. Transfer to bowl.  Beat potato at medium speed until smooth. Add cheese,  and next 6 ingredients. Beat well. Divide mixture into  10 equal portions and shape into balls. Dip in egg  white and roll in cereal. Place on a baking sheet  coated with nonstick spray. Bake at 400F for 10-15  minutes or until crisp and golden. Yield: 5 servings  (166 calories per serving).
