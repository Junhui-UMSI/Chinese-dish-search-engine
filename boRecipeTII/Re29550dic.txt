Tartar of Smoked Salmon on Rye Barquette
Capers, Pastry, Fresh chives finely chopped, Onion, Egg white, Hand, Barquettes-boat shaped, Juice of 1 lemon only, Smoked salmon minced by, 
MIx well minced salmon, capers, lemon juice, onion, egg white and chives  and portion into 30 equal parts. Top barquettes and serve. If barquettes  are not available, use round 1 inch croutons or similar cracker as base.
