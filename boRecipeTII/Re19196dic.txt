Vegetable Pasta Salad
Mayonnaise, Frozen broccoli and, Dressing:, Sm. zucchini, thinly sliced, Jar marinated artichoke, Thawed and drained, Thinly sliced celery, Dried Italian Seasoning, Sour cream, Rotini pasta, cooked and, Bottled Italian salad, Thinly sliced carrots,, Drained and quartered, Green onions, thinly sliced, 1/4 oz can sliced ripe, Prepared mustard, Frozen peas, thawed, 
In a large bowl, combine pasta, onions, zucchini, broccoli and cauliflower,  carrots, celery, peas, olives and artichoke hearts. In a small bowl,  combine dressing ingredients; mix well. Pour over pasta and vegetables and  toss.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.    Serves:  16 From: "Taste of Home Magazine" Posted by: Debbie Carlson  (D.CARLSON - GEnie)
