Pot Roast with Sour Cream Gravy
Flour, Med. potatoes, peel, quarter, Carton sour cream (8 oz.), Med. onions, quartered, 2-3 lb. pot roast, Water, Med. carrots cut in 2" pcs., Cooking oil, 
Brown roast on all sides in hot oil in lge. roaster or Dutch oven. Add  water.  Cover and simmer about 2 1/2 to 3 hrs. Add vegetables. Cover and  simmer 30 min. or until vegs. are tender. Remove roast and vegs. to serving  dish.  Drain off all but 2 tbs. drippings, sift flour into drippings. Make  reux, stirring constantly. Add water to drained off drippings to make 1  cup.  Stir into flour and cook, stirring constantly, until smooth and  slightly thickened. Add sour cream and cook, stirring constantly, on low  heat until heated.  Serve gravy with roast.
