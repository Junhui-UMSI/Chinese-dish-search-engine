Wined and Dined Beef Roast
Salt, Vegetable oil, Chuck roast, Basil, Dry mustard, Red wine- dry, Garlic, Lemon juice, Steak sauce, 
Cook garlic in oil, but dont brown. Remove from heat and add wine, lemon  juice, basil, salt and mustard.   Prick roast on both sides with fork.  Place roast and marinade in a  plastic bag and refrigerate overnight or about 6 hours. Drain and reserve  marinade. Grill over medium coals, 25-30 minutes on each side. Add steak  sauce to marinade and > over roast while grilling. FROM: RALPH MOULTON  (BFVR93A)
