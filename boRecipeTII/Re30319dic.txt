Banana Chocolate Chip Bread
Sugar, Margarine, Bananas, Sour cream, Eggs, Flour, Baking soda, Salt, Miniature chocolate chips, 
OVEN: 350 degrees. Grease and flour bottom only of a 9 x 5 or (2) 8 x 4-inch loaf pans.    In large bowl, combine sugar and margarine. Beat til light and fluffy.    Add bananas, sour cream and eggs; blend well.    Stir in flour, baking soda and salt; blend well. Stir in chocolate chips.  Pour into pan(s).    Bake 55 to 65 minutes, or until toothpick inserted into center comes out  clean.    Cool 15 minutes; remove from pan. Cool completely. Wrap tightly and store  in refrigerator.    NOTES : Best served next day- if it lasts that long!   Freezes well.
