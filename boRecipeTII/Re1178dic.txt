Creamy Lemon Dressing
Lemon zest, Honey, Salt, Cayenne pepper, Fat-free plain yogurt, Mayonnaise, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
Whisk together until blended.    Makes about 1 1/4 cups.    PER SERVING: Calories 48.18; Fat total 3.67 g; Protein 0.86 g; Saturated  Fat 0.67 g; Carbohydrates 3.41 g; Dietary Fiber 0.05 g; Calories from fat  66%; Calories from carbs 27%.
