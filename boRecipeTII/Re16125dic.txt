Warm Smoked Pecans
Whole pecans, Curry powder, Paprika; plus 2 tablespoons, Sugar, Cayenne pepper, Chili powder, Dried thyme leaves, Ground coriander, Mansion barbecue spice mix;, Dry mustard, Black pepper, Salt, Sugar, Ground cumin, MANSION BARBECUE SPICE MIX, 
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Place sugar in a medium saucepan and add 4  cups water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Add pecans and cook for 5  minutes. Remove nuts and drain. Place nuts in a greased sheet tray and  sprinkle barbecue spice mixture. Place tray in oven and roast for 15  minutes., stirring nuts occasionally. Remove tray from oven and transfer to  smoker. Smoke for 10 minutes. Serve warm.    For mansion barbecue spice mix: Mix all of the ingredients together. Store  in a cool, dry place .
