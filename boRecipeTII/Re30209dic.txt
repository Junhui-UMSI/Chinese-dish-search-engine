Banana Bread
Sugar, Nut meats, Flour, all-purpose, Baking Powder, Eggs, Salt, Bananas, Butter, Baking soda, 
Mix well and turn in well greased loaf pan. Bake in moderate oven 350  F for 45 minutes. Remove from oven, let stand in pan a few minutes,  then remove to cool.    Source: Emma Blair, Newbury Grange, Geauga County, OH  Mrs. W. F. Work, Hardy Grange, Holmes County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
