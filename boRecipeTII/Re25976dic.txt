Bacon Layered Salad
Head cauliflower, Head lettuce; torn, (8-oz) sour cream, (10-oz) frozen peas, Bacon; cooked, crumbled, Mayonnaise, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Separate cauliflower into flowerets. Layer lettuce, cauliflower & peas in  that order in a 3-quart bowl. Combine sour cream & mayonnaise, mixing well.  Spread evenly over top, sealing to edge of bowl. Sprinkle with remaining  ingredients in order listed. Cover bowl tightly & chill overnight. Yields  8-10 servings.
