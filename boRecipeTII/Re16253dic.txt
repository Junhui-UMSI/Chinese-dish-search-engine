Watercress with Grilled Onions,peppers, Spicy Walnuts
Vegetable oil, Walnut halves, Red bell pepper; seeded, cut, Fresh watercress; trimmed, Walnut oil, White wine vinegar, Fresh Cilantro, Yellow bell pepper; seeded,, Cayenne pepper, Fresh cilantro; chopped, Sugar, Red onion; peeled, cut into, 
Heat 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat.  Add cayenne and sugar and stir until sugar dissolves. Add walnuts and stir  until coated. Continue stirring until walnuts are toasted. Using slotted  spoon, transfer walnuts to foil lined sheet and cool. Whisk walnut oil,  vinegar and 3 tablespoons chopped cilantro in medium bowl until well  blended. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Prepare barbecue (medium  high heat). Combine 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, onion wedges and pepper  strips in large bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Grill  vegetables until tender and blackened in spots. Transfer to large bowl. Add  watercress. Toss with dressing. Arrange on platter. Garnish with candied  walnuts.
