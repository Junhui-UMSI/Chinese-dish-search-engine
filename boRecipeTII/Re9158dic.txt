Provencal Vinegar
White wine vinegar (up to 2), Sprigs (each 3 in) fresh, Sprig (5 in) fresh rosemary;, Sprigs (each 4 in) fresh, 
With a chopstick or skewer, push rosemary sage and thyme into a clean 12-  to 16-ounce bottle. Pour vinegar through a funnel into bottle to fill. Seal  and store in a cool, dark place at room temperature for at least a week or  up to 4 months.
