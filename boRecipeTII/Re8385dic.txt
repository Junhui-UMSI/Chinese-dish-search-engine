Potato Skin Snack
Salt-free herbs, Oil-free Italian salad, Baking potatoes, 
Bake potatoes at 400F. for 1 hour or until done. Allow potatoes to  cool.    Cut potatoes in half lengthwise; carefully scoop out pulp, leaving a  1/4" lining in shell. Cut shells into 5 strips. Place strips skin  side down on a baking sheet. Brush with dressing; sprinkle with  seasoning blend. Broil 6" from heat for 5 minutes or until browned.  Serve warm. 11 calories each.
