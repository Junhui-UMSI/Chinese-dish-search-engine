Warm Citrus Melange with Frozen Fruit Yogurt
Papaya, peeled, seeded and, Oranges, peel and pith, Fruit frozen yogurt,, Fresh berries, preferably, Sugar, Orange juice, Sweet butter, Available in asian specialty, Banana galettes, optional,, Lemon, peel and pith removed, Mint sprigs for garnish, Fresh pineapple, peeled, and, 
In a saute pan melt butter over moderate heat and cook until golden brown.  Add the papaya, pineapple, oranges, lemon, berries, sugar, and orange  juice, stirring to combine, and cooking until liquid is reduced to a syrup.  Transfer fruit to a bowl. Roll banana galettes into cones and secure with a  toothpick or skewer. Scoop frozen yogurt into banana galettes and top with  fruit. Garnish with mint. Serve immediately.    Yield: 4 to 6 servings    Recipe by: Cooking Live Show #CL8874 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #609 by  "Angele and Jon Freeman" <jfreeman@netusa1.net> on May 12, 1997
