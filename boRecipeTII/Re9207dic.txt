Prune Pillows
Prune jam; (or thick filling, Baking soda, Margarine, Powdered sugar, Baking Powder, Water, Salt, milk, Egg yolk mixed with:, Eggs, Flour, 
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. In a large bowl, sift together the flour,  baking soda, baking powder and salt. Cut in the margarine using a pastry  blender or knives. Add eggs and milk; mix thoroughly to form a dough.    Divide the dough into quarters. On a lightly floured work surface, roll out  each quarter of dough to about a 10-by-10 inch square 1/8 inch to 1/4 inch  thick. Cut dough into about sixteen 2 1/2-by-2 1/2-inch squares. Place 1  scant teaspoon of the prune jam or other thick filling in the center of  each square. Bring the 4 corners together and pinch to seal.    Brush the Prune Pillows with the egg/water mixture. Place on a greased  baking sheet and bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until brown.    Remove from the oven and cool on a wire rack. When cool, sprinkle with  powdered sugar.
