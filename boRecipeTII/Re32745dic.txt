Sweet Potato Fries
Onion powder, Salt, Sweet potatoes, Ground red pepper, orange juice, 
Preheat oven to 450F    Line a cookie sheet with foil and spray with Pam    In a medium bowl combine orange juice concentrate, onion powder, salt and  cayanne.  Add potato sticks and turn to coat.    Arrange coated potato sticks on the oiled foil in a single layer. Bake 10  min; turn carefully, bake 10 more minutes or until tender The total batch  has 416 calories, 1 gram of fat, and tastes pretty good    From Weight Watchers Quick Meals Submitted By RUFUS@MELBPC.ORG.AU On TUE,       28    NOV 1995 171617 +1100
