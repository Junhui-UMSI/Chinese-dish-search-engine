"Thyme and Thyme Again" Dressing
olive oil, Crumbled feta or goat cheese, Minced fresh marjoram, Honey or sugar, Red wine vinegar, Minced fresh oregano, Garlic clove (or more), Salt, Finely minced fresh thyme, Dijon-style mustard, White pepper, minced or crushed, 
Place all ingredients in a jar, seal tightly, and shake vigorously  for 10 seconds.  Use immediately or refrigerate.  Shake again before  serving. If kept refrigerated, the vinaigrette should keep for up to  two weeks.
