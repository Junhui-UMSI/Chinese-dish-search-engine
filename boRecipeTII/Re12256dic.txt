Zucchini Fritters #1
Zucchini, Eggs, Salt, Pepper, Parmesan cheese, Bisquick, Butter, 
Blend the bisquick, parmesan, salt, pepper.  Add eggs. Mix zucchini into  batter.  Grease hot pan or griddle with butter.  Pour batter into pan and  fry 3 minutes on each side.  Serve warm, a delicious way of serving  zucchini.  Make 8 to 10 fritters.    The Andersons Food Garden    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Mar 22, 98
