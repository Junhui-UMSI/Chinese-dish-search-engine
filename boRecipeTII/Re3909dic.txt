Pepper Salad
Carrot; chopped fine, Mayonnaise-type dressing;, Green pepper; (red/yellow), Head cabbage;, Water;, 
Cut cabbage into small wedges.  Place half in blender along with  water, carrot, and peper.  Blend quickly; turn off and drain at once,  using liquid for blending with balance of cabbage, drain once more.  Mix with mayonnaise dressing.
