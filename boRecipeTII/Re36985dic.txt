Beeswax Wood Polish/leather Polish (Tlc)
Beeswax granules, Pure turpentine, Boiling water, Essential oil of lavender, Pure soap or soap flakes, 
1. Melt the waxes with the turpentine in a double boiler (warning low flash  point) 2. Grate the soap into a bowl and pour on the boiling water and stir  briskly to dissolve it. 3. Let the soapy water cool slightly and then pour  it into the melted wax stirring well as it makes an emulsion. 4. Put in a  small jar or container with a tight fitting lid.
