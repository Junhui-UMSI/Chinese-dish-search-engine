Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp
Hoisin sauce, Red bell pepper,, Shrimp, Chili sauce, Canadian bacon,, Teriyaki sauce, 
1. Place bacon on rack; cover with paper towel. Microwave on High 1  minutes; pat dry on paper towel. Let cool slightly. 2. Wrap one shrimp and  one pepper strip in each piece of bacon; secure with wooden pick. Place in  11x7" baking dish. 3. In small bowl, combine the teriyaki, chili and hoisin  sauces with 2 T. water; pour over shrimp. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours,  turning once. 4. Uncover and microwave on High 3-4 minutes, until shrimp is  cooked through. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
