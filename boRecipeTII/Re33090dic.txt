Basic Fat Burning Soup
Head of cabbage, carrots, Seasonings, Celery, Lipton Onion Soup Mix, tomatoes, White onions, Green bell pepper, 
Cut veggies into small pieces, cover with water. Boil fast for ten minutes.    Change to Simmer and continue cooking until veggies are just tender.
