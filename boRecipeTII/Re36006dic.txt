Beef Macaroni
Carrot; chopped, Bacon drippings, Chili powder to taste, Salt, Chopped parsley, Ground beef, (10-oz) macaroni, Cheese; grated, Poultry seasoning to taste, Onions; chopped, Rib celery; chopped, Pepper, Tomatoes, 
Saute onion in drippings. Add beef and brown. Add remaining ingredients,  except macaroni, and simmer 1 hour. Cook macaroni according to directions.  Grease large casserole and layer macaroni, sauce and cheese. Repeat. Bake  at 350 about 30 minutes. Serves 8-10.
