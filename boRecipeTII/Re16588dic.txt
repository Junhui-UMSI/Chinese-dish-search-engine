Aimees Chili
Ground beef, Tomato sauce, Corn meal, Bell pepper; diced, Salt and pepper, Tabasco sauce, Chilli powder, Water, Onion, 
Recipe by: Wayne Preston Allen Brown meat; add onion and saute well. Add  tomato sauce, seasonings, bell pepper, and chilli powder, salt, and pepper  to taste. Add water until sauce is of desired thickness. Simmer 15-30  minutes. During last 15 minutes of cooking, sprinkle in corn meal and stir.
