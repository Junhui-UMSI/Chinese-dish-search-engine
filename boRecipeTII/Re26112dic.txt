Tofu Stuffing
Minced onion flakes, salt and pepper, Ground sage, Shredded nonfat Cheddar, Tofu, sliced, drained well, Garlic powder, 
Preheat oven to 350. Place tofu in a bowl and mash with a fork. Add  remaining ingredients and stir until well blended. Place mixture in a  1-quart casserole that has been sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray.  Cover and bake 30 minutes.
