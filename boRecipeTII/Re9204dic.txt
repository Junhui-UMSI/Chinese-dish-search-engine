Prune Muffins
Prunes; cooked - well drain, Egg, milk, Flour, Sugar; granulated, Brown sugar, Nutmeg, Baking Powder, Shortening, Salt, 
Mix and bake in hot oven.    Note: Hot oven is 400 - 450 F. Time not given, probably about 25  minutes.    Source: Mrs. Jeneila Gillson, United Grange, Marion County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
