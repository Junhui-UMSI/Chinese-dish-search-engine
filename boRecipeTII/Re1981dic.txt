Chocolate Tofu Pie
10.5-oz packages Silken tofu, Chocolate chips, Graham cracker crust, Container of Cool Whip, 
Melt chocolate with 2 tablespoons of water. Keep warm. Put tofu in blender  and blend until smooth. Add melted chocolate and 1 teaspoon of vanilla.  Pour into crust and chill 1-1 1/2 hours. Top with Cool Whip.    This is not lowfat and not low-cal but is really good.    Typed by Carole 8/97    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Carole <caroleru@tp.net> on Aug 28, 1998
