10 Minute Meat Loaf
Soy sauce, Bread crumbs, Ketchup, Swiss cheese; shredded, Ground beef, Onion soup mix, Egg, milk, 
Combine all ingredients and shape into a round or oval loaf. Place in  microwave-safe dish, cover with waxed paper and microwave on high for 10  minutes, turning dish after 5 minutes, of cooking, drain and cover with  foil. Let stand 10 minutes before slicing. Makes 4-6 servings.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 18, 1998
