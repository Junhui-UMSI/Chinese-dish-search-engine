Angel Biscuits
, self-rising flour, Sugar, Baking soda, Shortening, Yeast, Warm water, Buttermilk, 
Combine flour, soda, and sugar; cut in shortening and stir in yeast and  buttermilk. Be sure to mix well. Keep covered in refrigerator at least an  hour or two before using. Roll out and cut as desired. Plae on greased pan.  Let rise for 2 hours. Bake at 450~ for about 10 minutes. NOTE: Dough will  keep in refrigerator as long as a week
