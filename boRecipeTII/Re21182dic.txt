Tzajiki
Garlic, thick yogurt, Vinegar, olive oil, Cucumber, dill weed, 
Mix together and chill for at least 1 hour. Serve as a dip, with fried  fish, or with fried eggplant or fried zucchini. Excellent with crudites. If  you run whole thing through blender it makes a delicious cold soup. It is  also great as a virtually non-fat salad dressing.
