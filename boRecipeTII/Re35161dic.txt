Bee Sting Cake
Vanilla, Sugar, milk, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, Eggs, Vanilla, Shredded coconut, Flour, Butter, Butter, TOPPING, Heavy cream, 
From: Robin Ottoson <rdottosn@csn.org>    Date: Mon, 6 Jun 1994 21:29:10 GMT  Oven at 350.  Grease and flour and 8 x 8" pan.  Mix eggs, sugar, vanilla,  flour and baking powder.  Bring milk and butter to a boil and mix with  batter mixture. Pour into pan and bake approx. 30 mins. Mix topping  ingredients and spred and cake while it is still warm. Broil until topping  is bubbly and warm. 10 servings.    Originally published in Univ.
