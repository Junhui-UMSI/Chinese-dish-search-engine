Zesty Bean Salad
Kidney beans,drained(15oz), Vegetables,mixed,drained(16, White pepper, Swiss cheese,julienne strips, Caesar dressing,prepared, Basil, Oregano, Parsley,chopped, Garlic clove,minced, Croutons, 
Combine all ingredients except croutons in large bowl. Refrigerate several  hours. Sprinkle with croutons before serving.
