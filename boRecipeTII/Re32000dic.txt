Barbecue Sauce for Fish
Pepper, Catsup, Onion; sliced, White vinegar, Butter, Liquid smoke, Cayenne (red) pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, Sugar, Thick lemon slice, Prepared mustard, 
Mix first 9 ingredients; simmer for 20 minutes then add remaining  ingredients. Remove lemon slice, baste of fish and barbecue.    DAN HIRSCHI    <VFOAO0CY@HUEY.CSUN.EDU>    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
