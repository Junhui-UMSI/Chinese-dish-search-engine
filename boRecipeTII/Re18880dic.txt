Angel Biscuits
Self-rising flour, Yeast cake, Buttermilk, -(up to), Sugar, Warm water, Shortening, 
Dissolve yeast in water. Sift dry ingredients together in large bowl. Mix  in shortening. Pour liquid ingredients into dry ingredients & mix well.  Store in refrigerator until needed. Roll out amount desired & let rise 1  hour. Bake at      400    degrees.    JUDY HASTINGS    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
