Heath Bar Cake
Dark chocolate cake mix, Sweetened condensed milk;, cool whip, Heath bar candy bar, 
Make cake mix according to package directions. While still warm, poke with  fork. Pour condensed milk over top. Let cool. Frost with whipped cream and  sprinkle crushed candy bar over top. Chill and serve.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Tonya Stinson" <babyface49@hotmail.com> on  Aug 10, 1998,
