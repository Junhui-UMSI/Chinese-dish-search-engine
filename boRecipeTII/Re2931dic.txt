Peanut Butter Muffins Ww
Eggs, Peanut butter, chunky, Milk, skim, Sugar, superfine, Flour, self-rising, Margarine, 
Preheat oven to 400F. Sift together flour and sugar; add remaining  ingredients, stir until combined. Do not beat or overmix. Spray 4 muffins  cups with cooking spray; fill 3/3 full with batter. Fill remaining cups 1/2  full of water. Bake till golden brown - about 20 minutes. Remove muffins to  wire rack, cool. Serve cooled or freeze until ready to use.     Weight Watcher Exchanges: 1 Protein, 1/2 Bread, 1 Fat, 20 Optional  Calories.     Nutritional Analysis per serving: 160 calories, 7 g.   protein, 8 g. fat, 15 g. carbohydrate, 110 mg. sodium, 69 mg. cholesterol.
