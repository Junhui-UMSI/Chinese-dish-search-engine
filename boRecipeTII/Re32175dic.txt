Barbecued Pork Loin (Prep
PEPPER BLACK 1 LB CN, Salt, -, PORK ROAST LOIN FZ, 
PAN:  18 BY 24-INCH ROASTING PAN          TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  RUB EACH ROAST WITH MIXTURE OF SALT AND PEPPER.    2.  PLACE IN PANS. DO NOT ADD WATER. DO NOT COVER.    3.  BAKE 1 1/2 TO 2 HOURS, DEPENDING ON SIZE OF ROASTS. DRAIN OR SKIM  OF EXCESS FAT. SET ROASTS ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 6.    4.  POUR 2 CUPS PREPARED BARBECUE SAUCE OVER EACH ROAST; COVER PANS.  RESERVE  REMAINING SAUCE FOR BASTING.    5.  BAKE 1 HOUR; UNCOVER ROASTS; BASTE; CONTINUE TO COOK APPROXIMATELY  30 MINUTES OR UNTIL MEAT THERMOMETER INSERTED IN MEAT REGISTERS 170 F.    6.  LET ROASTS STAND 20 MINUTES. REMOVE STRINGS AND NETTING BEFORE  SLICING. DRAIN SAUCE; REMOVE EXCESS FAT.    7.  SLICE PORK; SERVE WITH REMAINING SAUCE.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 1, 58 LB PORK LOIN, BLADES, MAY BE USED.    2.  OTHER TYPES AND SIZES OF PANS MAY BE USED.  SEE RECIPE NO. A-25.
