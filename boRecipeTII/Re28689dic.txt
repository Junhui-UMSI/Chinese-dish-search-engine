Texas Quick Dip
Seasoned salt, (8-oz) cream cheese;, Sour cream, Jar (8-oz) mild or medium, 
In a medium sized bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add sour  cream. Fold in the salsa and seasoned salt. Store in refrigerator but serve  at room temperature.     Serve with chip, or veggies. Makes 3-1/2 cups.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
