Aunt Dorabells Chocolate Cake
Buttermilk, Cocoa (heaping), Butter, Salt, Flour, Vanilla, Baking soda, Sugar, Eggs (must use medium), Boiling water, 
Cream sugar, eggs and butter. Dissolve soda in milk, add to creamed  mixture. Blend well. Sift together flour, salt and cocoa; add to creamed  mixture. Beat well; add boiling water, vanilla and mix. Bake at 350 for 45  minutes in three greased and floured 8-inch round pans. Frost as desired.
