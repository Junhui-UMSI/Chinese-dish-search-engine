Baked Green Tomatoes
Salt and pepper, Brown sugar, butter-cracker crumbs, tomatoes, Sticks butter, 
Cut tomatoes in half and arrange in an oiled shallow casserole. Season with  salt and pepper and spread each with a tablespoon of sugar. Cover with  crumbs and dot with butter.  Bake at 350 F until tender but still firm -  the time will depend on the size and greenness of the tomatoes - but will  probably range from 30 to 40 minutes.    Note: 1/2" slices may be baked in this same manner.  Bake 25 minutes.    From "The Farmington Cookbook."  Louisville, KY: Farmington, 1968. Pg. 167.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
