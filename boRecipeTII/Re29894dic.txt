Teriburgers
Ground chuck, Kikkoman Teriyaki Sauce, Lettuce leaves, Hamburger buns, Prepared mustard, Onion slices, Tomato slices, 
Combine first 3 ingredients; mix well and shape into 4 patties.  Broil or  grill to desired doneness, brushing with additional teriyaki sauce, if  desired.  Serve on hamburger buns with lettuce leaf and tomato and onion  slices.    Source: SPICE UP YOUR MEALS! with Kikkoman Teriyaki Sauces  Reprinted with the permission of Kikkoman International Inc.
