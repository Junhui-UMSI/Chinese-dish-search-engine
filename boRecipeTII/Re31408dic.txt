Barbecue Baked Beans
Garlic, Salt, Chili powder, Cider vinegar, Green Peppers; Diced, Onion; Sliced, Prepared mustard, Tomato sauce, Hot Sauce; Or To Taste, Vegetable broth, Chili Sauce, Canola oil, Pinto Beans; Rinsed And, Brown sugar, Onions; Chopped, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Heat 2 teaspoons of oil in a skillet. Saute chopped  onion, garlic and green pappers until onions are lightlybrowned, about 10  minutes. Add all the other ingredients. Lightly oil roasting pan using  remaining teaspoon of oil. Add mixture. Top with sliced onions. Bake one  hour uncovered. The beans should look nice and brown.
