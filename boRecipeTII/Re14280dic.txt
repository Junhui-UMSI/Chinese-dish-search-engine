White Wine Sangria
dry white wine, Superfine sugar, Ice cubes, Lime slices, Seltzer water, Brandy, Orange slices, Lemon, 
In a large glass pitcher, combine first 3 ingred. Add ice and stir till  sugar is disolved. Add fruit and seltzer, serve at once.    Recipe by: food and wine Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #686 by kriegz  <kriegz@bccom.com> on Jul 22, 1997
