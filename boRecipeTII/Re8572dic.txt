Potatoes Parmesan
Grated Parmesan cheese, Buttery flavored crackers,, Salt, Paprika, Butter or margarine,, Inch thick, Pepper, Garlic powder, Fresh Idaho potato, sliced, 
In medium bowl toss butter and potatoes to coat. In small bowl combine  remaining ingredients and coat potatoes with mixture. In 9-inch  microwave-safe pie plate arrange coated potatoes in single layer; sprinkle  with any remaining crumb mixture. Cook on High 4 to 6 minutes or until  potatoes are tender, turning dish once. Makes 2 snack servings.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
