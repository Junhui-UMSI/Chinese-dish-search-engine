Viennese Style Pot Roast
Rump roast or sirloin tip, White wine, Carrot; chopped, Salt, Pepper, Turnips; chopped, Dried figs; chopped, Beef stock, Gingersnap cookies; crushed, Butter, Onion, New potatoes, 
Brown meat on all sides in butter. Put carrots, turnips and potatoes in  bottom of crock pot and place roast on top. Add remaining ingredients,  except gingersnaps. Cover and cook on low 8 to 10 hours. Add gingersnaps  and cook on high, uncovered, until thickened.
