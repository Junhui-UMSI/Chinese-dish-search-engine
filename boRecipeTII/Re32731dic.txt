Sweet Potato Chips
Yams, Safflower oil, Spicy vegetable seasoning, Paprika, Garlic salt, 
Place sliced potatoes in a bowl, sprinkle with oil and mix thoroughly.  Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a non-stick baking sheet and  sprinkle with vegetable seasoning and garlic salt. Bake for 12-15 minutes  in a preheated 450F oven unitl nicely browned. Serve immediately.
