Warm Chicken Taco Salad
Taco seasoning mix, Lettuce, Green onions, Chicken breasts, boneless,, Tomato, Cheddar cheese, Olives, Miracle Whip, Tortilla chips, 
Cook chicken (about 1 1/4 lb) in 1/4 cup Miracle Whip or * Miracle Whip  Light in large skillet on medium-high heat for 3 minutes. Stir in 1/4 cup  Miracle Whip, tomato and taco mix. Cook 5 minutes or until chicken is  cooked through. Layer tortilla chips, lettuce and chicken mixture on large  platter. Top with Cheddar cheese, olives and green onions.
