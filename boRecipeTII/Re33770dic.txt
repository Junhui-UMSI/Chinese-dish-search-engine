Sweet and Sour Duck
carrot, Ketchup, red bell, Sweet & sour sauce, Sugar, White vinegar, onion, pineapple pieces, canned, green onions, SWEET AND SOUR SAUCE, Cooking oil, Cold water, broccoli, Pineapple juice, soy sauce, duck, Cornstarch, 
Preheat oven to 350. Score duck all over w/ sharp knife. Brush w/ or dip in  soy sauce. Place wire rack over roasting pan. Set duck on rack & cook in  oven for 2 hrs. Remove & set aside to cool. Remove rib cage & cut duck into  8 pieces (2 each breasts, wings, thighs, & legs). Preheat wok over high  heat w/ 2 tbs. oil. Add carrot & stir-fry 1 min. Add pepper & broccoli,  stir-fry 1 min. Add onion & duck; stir-fry 1-2 min. Stir in Sweet & Sour  Sauce & bring to fast boil. Combine cornstarch & water to make smooth  paste. Add to mixture, stirring constantly until thickened & translucent.  Garnish w/ sliced green onions. SWEET & SOUR SAUCE: Combine sugar & vinegar  in small saucepan over medium heat. Add juice & pineapple pieces,stirring  well. Stir in ketchup & simmer 7 min.
