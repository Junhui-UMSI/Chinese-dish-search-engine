Pecan-Wheat Waffles with Cherry Sauce
CHERRY SAUCE, Pecans, Whole-wheat flour, Butter, Maple syrup, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Ground nutmeg, Light-brown sugar, milk, Cornstarch, dark sweet cherries, Honey, 
Makes 5 waffles (7-inch) and 2 cups sauce for $4.65. Prep: 25 minutes.  Cook: 4 minutes per waffle.    1. Combine flours, pecans, baking powder, nutmeg and salt in large bowl.  Make well in center.    2. Whisk butter and sugar in medium-size bowl until blended. Whisk in eggs  and milk. Add to well; stir to blend. Let stand 5 minutes.    3. Preheat waffle iron. Prepare Cherry Sauce: Combine cherries, maple syrup  and honey in small saucepan. Bring to simmering. Stir in cornstarch  mixture; cook, stirring, 1 minute or until thickened. Remove from heat.    4. Coat the waffle iron lightly with nonstick cooking spray. Spoon the  batter onto the iron. Cover and cook 4 minutes or until browned. Remove  waffles; keep warm. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve with sauce.    Nutrient Value Per Waffle: 390 calories, 11 protein, 16 g fat, 53 g  carbohydrate, 410 mg sodium, 100 mg cholesterol. Exchanges:1 1/5  starch/bread,1/2 meat,1/4 milk, 3/4 fruit, 2 1/2 fat. Nutrient Value Per 2  Tablespoons Cherry Sauce: 32 calories, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 8 g  carbohydrate, 1 mg sodium, 0 mg cholesterol. Exchanges: 1/a  starch/bread,1/2 fruit.    NOTE: This is a great sauce, easy to make use over your favorite pancakes  or even pound cake or ice cream for a great dessert treat.
