Vegetarian Tacos
Bell peppers, Drained, Taco shells, Chili powder, Cheddar cheese, Onion, Taco seasoning mix, Kidney beans, 
In a skillet over low heat cook onions, and peppers.  Drain beans and place  in a skillet.  Mash with a spoon.  Add chili powder and taco seasoning mix  in skillet until thoroughly combined.  Divide bean mixture among taco  shells and sprinkle with cheese.
