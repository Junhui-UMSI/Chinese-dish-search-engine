Potato Bread 4
Potato water, Yeast, Sugar, Butter, Whole egg, Potatoes; cooked and mashed, Salt, Bread flour, 
Boil one to two peeled potatoes. Save the water to use in the bread. Then  mash potatoes, without milk or butter, and let cool to lukewarm or room  temperature.    Add ingredients to the bread machine according to manufacturers  directions.
