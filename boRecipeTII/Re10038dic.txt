Quaker Choc-Oat-Chip Cookies
(2 sticks) margarine or but, Salt, Chocolate morsels, Quaker oats (quick or old-, Coarsely chopped nuts, (optional), Granulated sugar, (1 12-oz. pkg.) semi-sweet, Fashioned, uncooked), All-purpose flour, Brown Sugar, milk, Baking soda, Eggs, Vanilla, 
Heat oven to 375.  Beat margarine and sugars until creamy. Add eggs, milk,  and vanilla; beat well.  Add flour, baking soda and salt, mix well. Stir in  oats, chocolate morsels and nuts; mix well.  Drop by rounded measuring  tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheet. (for easy bar cookies press dough  instead into bottom of ungreased 13 X 9 inch baking pan.) Bake cookies 9 to  10 mins for chewy and 12 to 13 for crispy (bake bar cookies 35 to 40 mins  until light golden brown). Cookies: cool 1 min on cookie sheet; move to  wire rack.  Cool completely.  ABout 5 dozen cookies.
