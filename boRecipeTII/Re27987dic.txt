Three Grain Muffin Mix
Unbleached flour, Egg whites, Cinnamon, Vegetable oil, Millet, Egg, Baking soda, Brown Sugar, All-Bran, Raisins; chopped or whole, Buttermilk, Rolled oats, 
Preparation :20 Cook :20 Stand :00 Total :40  Mix first 8 ingredients well. Store in air tight container in a cool, dry  place.    To make muffins: Combine wet ingredients (last 4 ingredients). Combine dry  mix with wet ingredients. Stir only until blended. Spray muffin pans with a  non-stick spray. Spoon batter into muffin cups until 2/3 full.    Bake in preheated, 400 degree oven, until firm to the touch, 18-20 minutes.  Cool slightly, invert pans and remove muffins.    For a smaller batch: Combine 4 1/2 cups of the stirred muffin mix with 1/4  cup vegetable oil, 1 egg plus 1 egg white, and 1 1/4 cups buttermilk.    Note: Freezes well.
