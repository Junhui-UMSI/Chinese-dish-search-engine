Raspberry Vinegar 3
Vinegar, Raspberries, Sugar, 
Mash the berries and add the vinegar and let stand for 4 days. To each cup  of liquid add one cup of sugar. Bring to a boil and cook for       20    minutes. Strain and bottle.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #009 by scotlyn@juno.com (Daniel S Johnson)  on Jan 8, 1998
