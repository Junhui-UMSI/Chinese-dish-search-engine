Pork Chops and Rice (P/c)
Uncooked rice, Salt, Green pepper, Fat or drippings, Pepper, Canned tomatoes, Onion, Pork Chops, 
Brown chops well on both sides in cooker in melted drippings. Place rack  under chops in cooker.  On each chop put a tablespoon of rice, a slice of  onion and green pepper, and a section of tomato. Add salt and pepper and  cover with liquid from tomatoes. Pressure cook at 15 lbs. pressure for 15  minutes. Reduce pressure immediately. Serve 4.
