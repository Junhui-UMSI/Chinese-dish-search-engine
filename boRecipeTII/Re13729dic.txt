Wild Rice Pilaf
Seasoned salt, Chopped red onion, & diced, thinly sliced, Cinnamon, nutmeg, Margarine, Finely diced celery, Tart apples, peeled & cored, Currants, Scallions, green parts only, Pecans, Wild rice, rinsed, Brown rice, rinsed, Black pepper to taste, Freshly minced parsley, Orange juice, Water, 
Stir rices into boiling water & add salt.  Simmer covered for 40 minutes.  Heat margarine in a large skillet & saute onions & celery till golden. Ad  apple & saute for 5 minutes. Stir in cooked rices with juice, scallions,  currants & spices.  Season to taste with pepper. Saute mixture for 5  minutes, stirring. Just before serving, heat through, add more liquid if  necessary & stir in parsley & nuts.
