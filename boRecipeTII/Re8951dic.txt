Pressgurka- Cucumber Salad
(optional), Dill -- chopped, Sprinkling caraway seeds, Salt, Cucumbers -- peeled, Mild vinegar, Sugar, 
Thiny slice the peeled cucumbers. Mix with the salt. Place a plate with a  weight over the cucumbers to squeeze out the liquid. Drain after one hour.  Mix the remaining ingredients and pour over the cucumbers.
