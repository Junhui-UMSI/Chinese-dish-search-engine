Zucchini Bread #03
Raisins, Salt, Corn oil, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Grated orange rind, Eggs; well beaten, Unsifted flour, Baking Powder, Grated raw zucchini; packed;, Sugar, Pecans, 
Sift dry ingredients.  Mix with remaining ingredients.  Divide batter  between 2 greased and floured loaf pans. Bake at 350 degrees F for 45  minutes to 1 hour, or until done.    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of Contemporary Recipes>.  Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
