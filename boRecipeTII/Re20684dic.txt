Apple Crisp 5
C. brown sugar (up to 3/4), Nutmeg, Oatmeal, Cinnamon, Apples, Butter, Flour, 
Grease pan.  Peel and slice apples.  Place on bottom of pan.  Mix all other  ingredients.  Put on top of apples.  Bake at 375F for 30 to 35 minutes.    Posted to FOODWINE Digest  6 November 96    Date:    Wed, 6 Nov 1996 21:17:26 -0600    From:    Rachel Hile Bassett <rbassett@EAGLE.CC.UKANS.EDU>
