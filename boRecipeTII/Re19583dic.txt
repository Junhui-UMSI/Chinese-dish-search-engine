Anns Chicken W/goat Cheese and Garlic
Whole chicken; free-range if, Salt and pepper, Cloves garlic; widely sliced, Herbs de Provence, Goat cheese, olive oil, 
Cut several small holes in the skin of the chicken and stuff with the goat  cheese.    Stuff slices of garlic all over the chicken. Rub the skin with the olive  oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper and herbs de Provence.    Bake in 350 degree oven for 1/2 an hour. Remove from oven and microwave for  7 minutes. Return to oven and continue baking for 20 to 30 minutes more.    Serve with Spinach Salad and Crusty Bread.
