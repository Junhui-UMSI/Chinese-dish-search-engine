Barmbrack
Baking soda, Salt, Washed raisins, Brown sugar, Hot strong tea, Chopped mixed peel, Chopped dates, Egg, Baking Powder, All-purpose flour, 
Pour tea over fruit and sugar and let stand over-night. Next morning stir  in egg, then flour, baking powder, soda and salt sifted together. Turn into  greased loaf pan and bake for 1 1/2 hours at 300F. Turn onto wire rack to  cool.  To serve, slice and butter. Source: Chatelaine Golden Anniv. Recipes  ch.
