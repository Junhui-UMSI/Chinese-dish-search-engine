Traffic Light Cooler
Gold Tequila, Splash Sour Mix, Orange juice, Sloe Gin, Melon Liqueur, 
Into pilsner glass filled with ice cubes, pour melon liqueur then tequila  to create a green layer. Add sour mix. Slowly pour orange juice against the  side of the glass to create the yellow layer. Add a few more ice cubes if  needed. Carefully float the sloe gin on top to form the red layer. Garnish  with a cherry and lemon and lime wheels. Stir before drinking.
