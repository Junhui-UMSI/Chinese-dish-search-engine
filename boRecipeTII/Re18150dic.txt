Velvet Noodle Pudding
Sugar, Vanilla extract, Butter, Butter, Semi-broad noodles, TOPPING, Sugar, , Whole Milk, corn flakes, cream cheese, Eggs, Cinnamon, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.    2. In a food processor or electric mixer, blend butter, cream cheese and  eggs. Add sugar, milk and vanilla. Blend well. Transfer to a large bowl and  add the cooked noodles. Blend. Pour into a greased oven-to-table 8 1/2 x  11-inch serving dish.    3. Combine topping ingredients and spread over noodles. Bake for 1 hour.    Makes 6 to 8 servings.
