Rainbow Trout with Green Onion - Ginger Mayonnaise
MARINADE, Oriental sesame oil, Fresh Lemon Juice, Soy sauce, Green Onions, Regular or Light Mayonnaise, , Rainbow Trout fillets, Oriental sesame oil, Mayonnaise:, Ginger, Lime juice, 
Marinate trout. Combine mayonnaise ingredients; refrigerate. Cook trout.  Serve immediately with mayonnaise.  Makes 2 to 4 servings.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mm-trout.zip
