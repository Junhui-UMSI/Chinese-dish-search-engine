Beer B-B-Qd Flank Steak
Lime juice, Soya sauce, Green onion; chopped, Flank steaks, Beer, Consomme, Brown sugar, Garlic, 
Combine first 6 ingredients and pour over steaks in a dish. Pour beer over  all and let marinade 24 hours. Barbecue over medium heat until done to your  liking. To serve, slice thinly across the grain. Source: Eatons Great  Canadian Recipe Challenge 1993 :  Pg. 62  From the collection of K.Deck
