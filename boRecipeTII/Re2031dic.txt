Ajam (=chicken) Abon Abon
Chicken, Coconut oil, Piece of tamarind; or a bit, Water; up to 2, Salt, 
Boil the chicken till it is well cooked. Slice the meat in long slices. Mix  the water, asem and salt. Rub the meat with it. Fry it in oil till it is  crispy.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Coby Andel en Han Mauwer"  <hanmauwer@wxs.nl> on Nov 1, 1998,
