Strawberries in Champagne
Fresh mint, Fresh lime juice, honey, light flavoured, strawberries, champagne, 
Heat the lime juice and honey until honey melts. Add two large sprigs of  mint. Remove from heat and steep for 5 minutes. Remove the mint. Put  strawberries in a bowl, add the sauce, toss gently, refrigerate at least 20  minutes. Divide the berries amoung six dessert dishes. Pour on champagne  and add a mint garnish.
