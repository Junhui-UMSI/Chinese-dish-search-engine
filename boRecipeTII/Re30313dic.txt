Banana Chip Muffins
Jiffy banana nut muffin mix, Mini-chocolate morsels, Rolled oats, Egg, Banana, milk, 
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Lightly grease muffin pan, or use muffin  cups in muffin pan.    2. Mix all ingredients well and pour into muffin pan.    3. Bake for 12 - 14 minutes, until lightly brown.    Notes: (Yield: 6 - 8 Muffins, depending on size)    Recipe by: JIFFY?
