Pest Powder
Cinnamon sticks, Sugar, Handful of lavender flowers, Dried peel of 1 orange, Crushed Coriander Seeds, Handful of nutmeg, Handful of cloves, Powdered orris root, Handful of wormwood or, 
1. Put the cinnamon sticks, cloves and nutmeg in a grinder. Add all the  other ingredients and mix in a large bowl. Fill the linen sachet with the  mixture and secure.    2. To make little spice bags simply put a small amount of mixture in centre  of handkerchief and tie up. Decorate with ribbon.
