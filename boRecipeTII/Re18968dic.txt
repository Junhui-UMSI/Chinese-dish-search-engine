Angel Pasta Paradise
Angel Hair Pasta, olive oil, Butter or margarine, Garlic, Onion, Sweet red pepper, Dried parsley flakes, Dry white wine, Shrimp, Chicken meat, Mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, 
Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and toss with olive oil.  Melt butter in skillet. Add garlic and onion and saute until onion is  tender.   Add sweet pepper. Add parsley flakes and white wine. Pour mixture  over pasta and toss until blended. Add shrimp, mushrooms and cheese. Toss  and serve immediately.
