"Land of Nod" Cinnamon Buns
Frozen Dough Rolls, Vanilla Instant Pudding, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Raisins, Butter, 
Before you put the cat out and turn off the lights, grease a 10" bundt pan  and add the frozen rolls. Mix the brown sugar, dry pudding powder and  cinnamon. Sprinkle over the rolls. Sprinkle the plumped raisins ontop. Pour  the melted butter or margarine over all. Cover with a clean, damp cloth.  (Leave out at room temperature). Turn out the lights and say Good Night. In  the morning preheat the oven to 350F and bake for 25 minutes.  Let sit for  5 minutes and then turn out on a serving plate. Now Enjoy!
