Peanut Butter Scotch Bars
Eggs, Rolled oats, Margarine, Flour, Crunchy peanut butter, Domino liquid brown sugar, Salt, Vanilla, Butterscotch morsels, 
DIRECTIONS ~------------------------------------------------------ ~---  COMBINE PEANUT BUTTER, MARGARINE, VANILLA AND LIQUID BROWN SUGAR. MIX UNTIL  SMOOTH. BEAT IN EGGS. STIR IN REMAINING INGREDIENTS. MIX WELL. SPOON BATTER  INTO A GREASED 13X9X2 INCH BAKING PAN. SPREAD SMOOTH. BAKE IN A 350F OVEN  35 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE. MAKES 36 SQUARES.
