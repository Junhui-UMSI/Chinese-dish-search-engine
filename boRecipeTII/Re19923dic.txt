Apple Bread Pudding
Butter or oleo, Cinnamon, Eggs, Pared and coarsely chopped, Cooking apple, Stale cinnamon- raisin, Sugar, Salt, milk, Vanilla, 
Heat oven to 325`. Spread butter on bread slices. Cut each slice into 1"  cubes. >> Place half of the bread cubes in a greased shallow 2-qt. baking  dish. Top with half of the chopped apples. Repeat layers, using remaining  bread cubes and chopped apple. In a large bowl, lightly beat milk, eggs,  sugar, vanilla, cinnamon and salt. Pour over bread and apples in dish.  Bake 1 hour or until knife inserted 1" from edge of dish, comes out clean.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #170    Date: Sat, 27 Jul 1996 04:04:25 -0400    From: CarolAnnn@aol.com
