Baked Catfish Parmesan #2
catfish filets, Basil, Chopped parsley, Parsley, Paprika, Pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, Oregano, Dry bread crumbs, Butter or Margarine, Lemon, Salt, 
Clean, wash and pat fish dry. Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese,  parsley, paprika, oregano, basil, salt and pepper. Dip catfish in melted  butter and roll in crumb mixture. Arrange fish in a well greased 9x13  baking dish. Bake at 375? for about 30 minutes or until fish flakes easily  when tested with a fork. Garnish with lemon wedges and parsley. Yield: 6  servings.
