Swordfish with Mango-Tomatillo Relish
Ground black pepper -- to, Taste, Red bell pepper -- seeded, Lime juice, Mango -- peeled and diced, And diced, Tomatillo -- husked, washed, Fresh cilantro -- minced, And minced, Swordfish, 
Combine the mango, tomatillo, bell pepper, cilantro and lime juice. Set  aside. Place the fish on a rack in a baking pan or use a broiler pan and  sprinkle fish with pepper. Bake in a preheated 450-dregree oven for 12  minutes per inch of thickness. Serve with the relish on the side.    Recipe By     : Rozanne Gold
