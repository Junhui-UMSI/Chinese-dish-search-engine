Aunt Tea Ms Crab Spread
Worchestershire sauce, Lemon, Crab, Philadelphia Cream Cheese, 
Mix all ingredients in an ovenproof dish.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Put in a 350 degree oven until cheese is bubbly.  Serve with warmed Ritz  Crackers.  Serves 20.  Enjo    Recipe By     : AUNT TEA M    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
