Perfect Potato Latkes
Potatoes, Pepper, Onion, Eggs, Salt, Flour, 
Grate potatoes (preferably by hand). Remove excess liquid. Mix in other  ingredients. Fry small spoonfuls until golden brown. Drain and serve hot.
