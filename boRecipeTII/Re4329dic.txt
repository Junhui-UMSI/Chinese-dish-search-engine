Persimmon Cookies
Flour, Ground cloves, Egg, Butter or margarine, Ground cinnamon, Ground nutmeg, Baking soda, Sieved persimmon pulp, Sugar, Baking Powder, Salt, Raisins and/or nuts, 
Stir soda into persimmon pulp and set aside. Cream butter and sugar  together. Beat in egg, then persimmon mixture. Sift flour with baking  powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg. Add to creamed mixture along  with raisins or nuts. Mix thoroughly. Drop by teaspoons onto greased baking  sheets and bake at 350F 8 to 10 minutes.
