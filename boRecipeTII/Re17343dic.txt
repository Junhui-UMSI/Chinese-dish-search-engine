Walnut and Herb Crusted Chicken
Dried basil leaves, Flour, Salt, Plain bread crumbs, Chicken pieces; (skin, Egg or egg white, Dried rosemary leaves, Finely chopped diamond, 
In a shallow dish, combine walnuts, bread crumbs, basil, rosemary, flour,  and salt. In a second shallow dish, lightly beat egg. Roll chicken in egg,  then in walnut mixture. Place on baking sheet.    Bake 40-45 minutes at 350? F. until crust is browned and juices run clear  when pierced with a fork.    Makes 4 servings.
