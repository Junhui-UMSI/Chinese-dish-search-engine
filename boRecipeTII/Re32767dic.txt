Sweet Potato Patch
Pineapple, crushed, canned,, Carrot; sliced, Sweet potato, Pine nuts, Cinnamon, Mung beans; cooked, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Pierce sweet potato with fork and place in  oven for 45 minutes. When potato cools, cut into 1/2-inch cubes. Steam  carrots for 8 minutes. Combine potato and carrots. Ad remaining ingredients  and mix well. Serve cool or at room temperature.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
