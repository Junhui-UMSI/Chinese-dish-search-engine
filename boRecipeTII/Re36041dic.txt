Beef Panang
Panang curry paste, Sugar, Peas, Vegetable oil, Red chili peppers, Beef, Coconut milk, Fish sauce, 
1. Stir-fry oil and curry paste until fragrent. (You may want to have the  fan turned up full in the kitchen... this gets very pungent!)    2. Add remain ingrediants and bring to boil. Reduce heat and cook for 10  min.    Garnish with chili and serve with steamed rice.
