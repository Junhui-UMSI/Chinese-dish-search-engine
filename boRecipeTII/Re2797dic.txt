Peanut Butter Cookies #04
Sugar, Egg, Salt, Chunky peanut butter, Baking soda, milk, Shortening, Flour, 
Cream peanut butter and shortening; gradually add sugar, mix well. Beat in  egg and milk. Sift flour, soda and salt together; add to creamed mixture.  Shape into balls. Flatten on ungresed cookie sheet. Bake at 370 degrees for  10-12 minutes. Cool.     To frost cookies, add enough milk to 1 cup sifted confectioners sugar to  make of spreading consistency and frost cookies, if desired.
