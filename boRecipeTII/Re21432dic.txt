Upside Down Kugel
Sugar, Salt, [16 ounce] pkg medium, Eggs beaten, Cinnamon, Sour cream, Dark brown sugar, Walnuts, Applesauce, 
Source: Faygie Spray bundt pan with no - stick spray. Pour 1/4 cup melted  butter into pan; place brown sugar over butter; place chopped nuts over  brown sugar. Cook noodles and drain. In large bowl mix remaining  ingredients together; add noodles and mix thoroughly. Pour into pan. Bake  at 350 for 1 hour or until brown. Remove from oven, turn upside down on  plate and serve.  Notes:  This recipe, not quite the traditional kugel, is delightful.
