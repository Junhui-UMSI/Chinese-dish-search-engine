Pepper Soup
Tomato paste, Tomatoes; peeled & cubed, Green chilies; seeded &, Rice; cooked, for serving, Thyme, fresh spriga; or, Beef, stewing; lean, cut in, -Water, Potatoes, new red;washed &, -Dried thyme, Onion; peeled & finely, 
"...can be made with firm skinless fish fillet instead of meat. If using  fish, cut each fillet into 3 pieces and reduce cooking time to 40 minutes."  Equipment: large-size saucepan with cover or Dutch oven, mixing spoon 1.  Put meat, water, peppers, onion, potatoes, tomatoes, tomato paste, thyme  and salt and pepper to taste in saucepan with cover or Dutch oven. Bring to  a boil over high heat and mix well. 2. Cover and cook for 1 hour or until  meat is very tender. To serve: put cooked rice into the bowls and cover  with soup.
