Wacky Cake
Sugar, Flour, Salt, Baking soda, Cocoa, 
Sift into baking pan (ungreased), make 3 holes in the mixture. Mix  together, 1 teaspoon of vanilla, 5 teaspoons of oil, 1 teaspoon of vinegar,  stir together with 1 cup of cold water, pour into the 3 holes in dry mix,  stir all together. Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Russellyn Crombie <DJJRS@AOL.COM> on Sep 10,  1997
