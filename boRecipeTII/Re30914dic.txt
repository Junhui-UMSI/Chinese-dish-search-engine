Tangy Tomato Soup
Frozen chopped onion, Fat free chicken broth, Shredded cheddar, Tomatoes, olive oil, Optional, Pasta-ready chopped, Chopped jalapeno peppers --, Jarred minced garlic, 14oz cans, Tomato sauce, 
In large dutch oven, heat oil over medium hi.  Add garlic and onion and  cook for 1 minute. Add tomatoes and sauce. Bring to a boil. Stir in broth  and peppers and bring back to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10  minutes. Just before serving, sprinkle with cheese and stir gently.
