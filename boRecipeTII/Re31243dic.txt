Banana-Pineapple Kabobs
Brown sugar, Margarine; optional, Whole vanilla bean, Bananas, Water, Pineapple, Salt; optional, 
From: Bobb1744@aol.com    Date: Tue, 23 Apr 1996 13:28:27 -0400  Slit vanilla bean lengthwise & scrape out sticky black seeds. Place seeds &  pod in small heavy bottomed pot along with remaining ingredients except  fruit. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat & simmer for 2 to  3 minutes. Stir till sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat. If glaze  hardens, reheat before using.    Prepare grill. Peel bananas; peel & core pineapple. Cut into bite-sized  pieces. Alternate bananas & pineapple on skewers. Place skewers on hot  grill & cook 5 to 10 minutes, turning once. When hot, baste with glaze, two  or three times. Turn & baste again.    PER SERVING: 139 Cal.; 0.8g Prot.; 0.4g Fat; 33g Carb.; 0 Chol.; 6mg Sod.;        2 g  Fiber.
