Baked Beans
Ketchup, Bacon, Onion, Baked beans  , Kidney beans  , Ground beef, Mustard, Butter beans  , Brown sugar, Vinegar, 
Preheat oven to 350? Cube bacon, fry and drain. Brown ground beef with  onion and drain. Combine all ingredients and pour into 2 quart baking dish.  Bake for 1 hour.    Recipe by: Kathy Libengood Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #662 by "LauraG"  <gaggini@gate.usaor.net> on Jul 9, 1997
