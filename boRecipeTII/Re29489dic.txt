Tarragon Cider Basting Butter
Tops, Pepper, Sliced green onion with, Bottled steak sauce, Apple cider or juice, Vinegar, Honey, Salt, Tarragon, 
In a 1 1/2 quart saucepan combine ingredients. Bring to boil; simmer,  uncovered 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Use as a meat marinade  or to baste chicken, beef, pork or fish last 15-20 minutes of  barbecuing. Heat & pass remaining sauce.
