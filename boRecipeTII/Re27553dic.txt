The "Best" Potato Salad
Prepared mustard, Eggs, Italian salad dressing, Sweet pickle relish, Mayonnaise, Onion, Celery seed, Celery, Potatoes, Seasoned salt, salt and pepper, 
Marinate potatoes in Italian dressing in refrigerator for several hours.  Combine with other ingredients and chill until serving time. May garnish  with hard-cooked eggs, cherry tomatoes, or green pepper rings.  Posted to recipelu-digest by GramWag@aol.com on Feb 7, 1998
