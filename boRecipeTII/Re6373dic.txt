Play Dough 5
Flour, Water, Vegetable oil, Food Coloring (as desired), Salt, Cream of tartar, 
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan.  Add food coloring one drop at a  time, stirring well, until desired color is reached.  Cook over medium  heat, stirring constantly, until dough is very stiff.    Turn dough out onto counter, allow to cool slightly, then knead until fully  cooled.    Store in airtight container.
