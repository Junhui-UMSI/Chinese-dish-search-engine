Banana Coconut Pudding
Sugar, Firm-ripe bananas, (14 oz) light unsweetened, Coarsely chopped, Small pearl tapioca (not, 
Garnish: 1 tablespoon chopped crystallized ginger, if desired    In a small bowl, soften tapioca in warm water to cover 1 hour.    While tapioca is soaking, in a small saucepan cook coconut milk, ginger,  and sugar over moderate heat, stirring, until sugar is dissolved. Remove  pan from heat and let mixture stand 10 minutes.    Drain tapioca. Peel bananas and quarter lengthwise. Cut quarters into  1/2-inch pieces.    Heat coconut milk mixture over low heat and gently stir in tapioca and  bananas. Cook pudding, stirring (be careful not to break up bananas), until  thickened slightly and bananas are heated through, about 5 minutes, and  cool. Divide pudding among six 3/4 cup ramekins and chill, covered, until  cold. Garnish with crystallized ginger.
