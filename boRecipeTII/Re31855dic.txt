Szechwan Sprout Salad
Rice wine vinegar, Sliced, Soy sauce, Mung bean sprouts, Green onion -- thinly, Sesame oil, Crushed red pepper flakes, Sugar, 
1. Rinse sprouts and pat dry with paper towels.    2. In a small bowl stir together vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and  red pepper flakes.    3. In a serving bowl combine sprouts and onion. Pour dressing over and toss  to coat.    * Timesaver Tip: Recipe tastes as good, if not better, when made up to 4  hours in advance. Store, covered, in refrigerator and serve either chilled  or at room temperature.
