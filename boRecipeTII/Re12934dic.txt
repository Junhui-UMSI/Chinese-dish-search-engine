Zesty Cilantro, Chili, Pea Dip
Cilantro, chopped, Frozen petite peas, thawed, Black Pepper, Lemon juice, Ground cumin, 1 10.5 oz. pkg Mori-Nu, 1 4oz. can green ortega, 
Zesty Cilantro, Chili, Pea Dip    Salt to taste (optional)    In a food processor or blender, combine all ingredients until smooth. Whirl  approximately 30 seconds on high. Refrigerate 1 hour. Mound over  overlapping tomato slices. Rim with fresh cilantro.
