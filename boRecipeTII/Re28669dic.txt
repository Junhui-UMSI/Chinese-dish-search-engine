Texas Hot Hash
Clove garlic,, Lean ground beef, Pepper, Can tomato sauce, Onion, thinly sliced, Green pepper, finely chopped, Instant rice, Salt, Can stewed tomatoes, Chili powder, Sharp Cheddar cheese, grated, 
1. In a deep, 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole crumble the  ground beef. Add the onion, green pepper and crushed garlic. 2. Heat,  uncovered, in Microwave Oven 6 minutes or until meat is browned. Stir  occasionally to break up the meat. 3. Add the remaining ingredients except  the Cheddar cheese.. Mix throughly to combine. 4. Heat, covered, in the  Microwave Oven 7 minutes. 5. lf desired, sprinkle Cheddar cheese over the  top of the meat mixture. 6. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 1 1/2  minutes or until cheese melts.
