Tangy Cheese Ball
Parsley Flakes; Dryed, Green Olives With Pimentos;, Onion Flakes; Dry, Green Pepper Flakes; Dried, Tabasco sauce, (8 Oz) Cream Cheese; Room, Black Olives; Diced, 
1. Blend cream cheese, green pepper flakes, olives, onion, and Tabasco  sauce with a fork in a medium size bowl.    2. Shape mixture into a round ball.    3. Roll in parsley flakes until covered slightly, pressing flakes against  ball as you roll it.    4. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before  serving.    5. Present on platter surrounded with snack crackers.    NOTES : This will keep in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.  You can add  fresh sprigs of parsley around base of ball for an added festive touch.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 304 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 24, 1997
