Apple Blossom Cake
Sugar, Pecans, Vegetable cooking oil, Nutmeg, All-purpose flour, Cinnamon, Eggs, Apples; diced and unpared, Vanilla extract, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Salt, Cloves, 
Sift dry ingredients into large bowl. Pour oil, vanilla and eggs into  mixture. Beat with spoon until mixed. Add apples and pecans. Place in  greased and floured tube pan. Bake at 325? for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Cool  cake on wire rack.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 296 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Nov 23, 1997
