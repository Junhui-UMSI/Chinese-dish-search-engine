Pineapple Beets
Brown sugar, Salt, Can pineapple tidbits;, Butter or margarine, Cornstarch, Lemon juice, 
In a saucepan, combine brown sugar, cornstarch and salt; add pineapple and  bring to a boil, stirring constantly until thick, about 2 minutes. Add the  beets, butter and lemon juice; cook over Medium heat for 5 minutes,  stirring occasionally. Yield: 4 servings.
