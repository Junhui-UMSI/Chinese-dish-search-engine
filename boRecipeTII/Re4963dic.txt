Pickled Mustard Cabbage
Mustard cabbage, Cider vinegar, Light brown sugar, Water, Sherry, -(up to), Salt, 
1. Separate mustard cabbage leaves and cut across the grain in 1/2-inch  sections.       2. Place sugar, water and vinegar in a deep pan. Bring to a boil over  high heat, stirring until sugar dissolves.       3. Add cabbage. Bring to a boil again, stirring gently. Remove from  stove and stir in salt and sherry.       4. Let cool. Then transfer to a tightly covered container and  refrigerate overnight.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
