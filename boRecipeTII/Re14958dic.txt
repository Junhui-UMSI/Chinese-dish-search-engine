From: ynnuf@yetti.amigans.gen.nz (Doreen Randal)    Date: Mon, 15 Nov 93 22:26:05 PST    (butter and milk is heated until butter melts.) Beat egg whites until  stiff, Add yolks and beat well. Pour into lined sponge tin and  bake 375 deg. F. (20 mins. 190 degs C in a ring tin.)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
