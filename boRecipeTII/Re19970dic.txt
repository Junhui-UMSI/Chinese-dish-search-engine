Apple Butter Muffins
Flour, Cinnamon, Allspice, Apple butter, Ginger, milk, Pecans, Sugar, Egg, Vegetable oil, Baking Powder, Nutmeg, Salt, Sugar, 
Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, spices and salt; mix well. Make a well  in the center. Combine egg, milk and oil; add all at once to flour mixture.  Stir just until the mixture is moistened. Line muffin tins with paper cups.  Spoon one rounded tablespoon of batter into each cup. Top each with one  rounded teaspoon of apple butter. Fill each with remaining batter. Combine  pecans with 2 tablespoons sugar and sprinkle on top of batter. Bake at 400  degrees for 20 minutes.
