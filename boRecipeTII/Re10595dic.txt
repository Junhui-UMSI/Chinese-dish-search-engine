Quick Parsley-Garlic Rolls
Loaf frozen bread dough, Flour, Butter or Margarine, Dried parsley leaves, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Garlic, 
Thaw frozen bread at room temperature about 2-3 hours. Place on lightly  floured surface and with a knife dipped into flour, cut the loaf in half  lengthwise. Then cut each half into 6 or 7 pieces. Roll out each piece to  about 1/4" thickness. Combine melted butter and garlic, mixing well. Brush  dough with the mixture. Lightly sprinkle with the parsley leaves. Roll up  each piece jelly-roll fashion. Place, end up, into lighlty oiled muffin  tins. Set in warm place to rise for 1 to 1-1/2 hours or until doubled. Bake  at 400~ for 12-15 minutes or until rolls are golden brown.
