Watermelon Sorbet
Watermelon pulp, Sugar, Lime juice, 
Put watermelon pulp in food processor or blender, and puree. Dissolve sugar  in lime juice. Combine watermelon & sugar-lime mixture, place in ice-cream  freezer, follow operating instructions. Yield: 1 quart. Optional: You may  add 2 beaten egg whites to mixture in ice-cream freezer, after it starts to  freeze. This will give you a consistency similar to a sherbet.
