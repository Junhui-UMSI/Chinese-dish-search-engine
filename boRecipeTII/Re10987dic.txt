Radish and Cilantro Relish
Onion; Chopped, 1 Med, Vegetable oil, Orange juice, Lime juice, Fresh Cilantro; Fine Snipped, Radishes; Thinly Sliced, Salt, Pepper; Freshly Ground, 
Mix all ingredients in glass or plastic bowl.  Cover and refrigerate at  least 1 hour. Makes 3 cups of relish.    Miscellaneous recipes from the collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mo-misc.zip
