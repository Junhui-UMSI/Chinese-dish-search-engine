Ramirez Hot Crab Spread
Slivered almonds, Cream cheese, softened, Chopped scallions, including, Worcestshire sauce, milk, Black Pepper, Fresh lump backfin crabmeat,, Salt, 
Beat cream cheese with milk in bowl till smooth. Add crab meat,  Worcestershire sauce, green pepper, scallions, salt and pepper; mix well.  Spoon into small baking dish; top with almonds. Bake at 350 degrees for 15  minutes or until bubbly and heated through. Serve with crackers and toasted  bread rounds.
