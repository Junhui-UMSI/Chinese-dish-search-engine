Apricot Pear Tart
Shortbread cookie or graham, Boiling water, Cinnamon stick; cut into, Ground ginger, Sugar, (4-serving size) Jell-O, Mint leaves, Cool Whip whipped topping;, Parkay margarine; melted, Cold water, (16-oz) pear halves; drained, 
MIX together cookie crumbs, sugar and margarine in small bowl. Press crumb  mixture onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.     DISSOLVE gelatin in boiling water.  Add cold water. Measure 3/4 cup  gelatin; set aside . Chill remaining gelatin until slightly thickened. Fold  in whipped topping and ginger. Spoon over crust.     CHILL measured gelatin until slightly thickened. Slice pears lengthwise,  cutting almost through stem ends; arrange on whipped topping mixture in  pan, fanning each pear slightly. Place mint leaf and cinnamon stick at stem  end of each pear. Carefully spoon thickened gelatin over pears and filling.  Chill until firm, about 3 hours. Run hot metal spatula or knife around edge  of pan before removing sides of pan.     MAKES 12 servings, Prep time: 30 minutes, Chill time: 3 hours    From <Favorite Recipes: Jell-O Easy Entertaining>.  Downloaded from Glens  MM Recipe Archive, G Internet.
