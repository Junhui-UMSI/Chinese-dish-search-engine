Balsamic Fig Sauce
Balsamic vinegar, Sugar, Fresh figs, Black Pepper, Red wine, fresh thyme, Salt, 
Combine all ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a saucepan. Cover; bring to  boil over high heat. Reduce to a simmer; cook, partially covered, until  fruit has broken down, about 20 to 30 minutes. Let cool slightly; remove  thyme sprig. Press mixture through a large-holed sieve with a rubber  spatula.
