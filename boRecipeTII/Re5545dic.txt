Pineapple Mustard Sauce for Ham
Pineapple chunks, Brown sugar, Ground cloves, Cornstarch, yellow mustard, 
Combine reserved juice and remaining ingredients except for pineapple. Cook  until it boils and thickens. Add pineapple chunks. Heat through. Spoon over  ham.    Recipe by: Dole    Posted to recipelu-digest by Peg Baldassari <Baldassari@compuserve.com> on  Mar 17, 1998
