Butternut/sweet Potato Kugel
Eggs, Sugar; ( taste to check if, Flour, Riches parve cream, Parve margarine, Vanilla essence, Rounded teaspoon baking, Sweet potatos, Butternut kugel, 
source: my neighbour Amira    Peel and cook both vegs till soft in a small amount of water. Drain off  water,cool and place in a processor with all the other ingriedence, process  till smooth. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar. Bake in a square or oblong  glass dish that has been sprayed with non-stick spray. Cover with foil  lightly. 160c for about 1 to 1 1/2 hrs till the centre is no longer soft.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Linda Shapiro <lss@coconet.com> on Oct 28,  1998,
