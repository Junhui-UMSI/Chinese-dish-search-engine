Ammonia Cookies
milk, Egg, Pastry flour, Sugar, Oil of lemon*, Salt, Shortening, Ammonium carbonate*, 
Directions: * may be purchased in a drug store. Cream shortening and add  sugar.  Add egg and oil of lemon. Mix. Sift flour before measuring. Measure  flour and sift with salt. Dissolve ammonium carbonatein a little of the  milk. Add flour and liquid alternately mixing after each addition. Chill  dough. Roll 1/4" thick. Cut in squares. Place on greased baking sheet.  Sprinkle cookies lightly with sugar. Bake 12-15 mins. in a 375 oven. Yields        8    doz. 2"squares.
