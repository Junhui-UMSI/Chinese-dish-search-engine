Pomegranate Syrup
Sugar, Pomegranate juice, 
In an enameled saucepan, bring pom- egranate juice and sugar to a boll,  stirring, and simmer the syrup for 5 minutes, skimming the froth. Let the  syrup cool and strain it through an enameled colander, lined with a double  layer of rinsed and squeezed cheese- cloth, into a nonmetallic bowl. The  syrup may be stored in a sealed jar in the refriger- ator for 2 to 3 weeks  or frozen. Makes about 2 cups.
