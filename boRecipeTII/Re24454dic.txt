Tropical Nut Bread
Chopped macadamia nuts, Flour, Baking soda, Mashed bananas, Orange juice, Butter, Baking Powder, Salt, (8.5-oz) crushed pineapple;, Sugar, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Butter a one pound loaf pan. Sift dry ingredients  together and set aside. Combine pineapple, banana and orange juice and set  aside. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and beat well. Add  a small amount of the flour mixture alternately with the fruit mixture,  mixing only enough to moisten the flour. Stir in macadamia nuts and pour  batter into prepared pan. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes.    Note: This bread improves if you wait a day or two before serving.
