Baked Cabbage
Vegetable broth, Fresh parsley, Non-fat mozzarella; shredded, Head cabbage; quarter, White wine; optional, Apple; peel, chop, Onion, Fresh dill, 
Put cabbage in casserole. Top with onion, apples, cheese and parsley. Put  broth in bottom. Cover with foil and bake 350~ until cabbage is tender.
