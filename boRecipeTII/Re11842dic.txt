Raw Vegetable Salad and Dressing
Raw Cauliflower Buds, Frozen Green Beans; cooked, Garlic, Cooking oil, Cracked Pepper, Raw Mushrooms; sliced, Water Chestnuts; sliced, Radishes; sliced, "Fines Herbes", Dressing, Bean Sprouts; drained, Head Boston Lettuce, Wine Vinegar, Paprika, 
FOR THE SALAD:    Mix all ingredients and serve with the dressing given.    FOR THE DRESSING:    Shake all ingredients in a covered jar and toss with salad.
