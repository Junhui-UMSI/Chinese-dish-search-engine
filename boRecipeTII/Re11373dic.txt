Randy Pollaks Persimmon Spice Cake
Eggs, .drops of water, Sugar, Soda, dissolved in a few, Raisins, Margarine, Cinnamon, Flour, Pecans, Cloves, Nutmeg, Persimmon pulp, Salt, 
Cream together butter and sugar. Add beaten eggs and beat well. Blend soda  mixture together and add to persimmon pulp. Then blend to cake mixture.  Sift together remaining dry ingredients and add gradually. Fold in nuts and  raisins. Pour into floured and greased 9 x 13 pan. Sprinkle top with  coconut generously and bake at 350F for 1 hour.
