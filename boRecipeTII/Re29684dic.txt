Tblisi Beef and Mushrooms
White pepper, Vegetable oil, Peppercorns, Red pepper flakes, Beef stock, SPICE MIX, Paprika, Flat noodles, Plain yogurt, Dry mustard, MAIN INGREDIENTS, All-spice, Black pepper, Salt, Beef steak 3/4 " thick, Sour cream, Onions chopped, 
Mix the spices in a bowl and set aside. Place the meat on a cutting board  and cut into 3/4" chunks. Lay these chunks flat and sprinkle with the spice  mixture. Cook the noodles and allow to remian ion the water. Take a large,  heavy pot and place it on High heat for 3-4 minutes. Add 1 T of vegetable  oil and then the onions. Saute until onions are turning transluscent. Add  the meat and the remianing spice mixture. Stir and cook for approx. 4-6  minutes. Add the stock. Scrape the pot bottom for the crusts and mix them  into the meat. Cook for 6 minutes more and reduece the heat to Medium-High  and stir in the sifted flour. Stir and cook for 3 minutes. Add the  mushrooms and cookf for 3 more minutes. Whisk in the sour cream <yogurt>  and cook for 2-3 minutes. Drain the noodles, place a serving of noodles on  each plate and sevre the meat mixture over the noodles.
