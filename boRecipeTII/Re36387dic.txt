Beer Batter
Beer, Eggs, TT salt, TT pepper, Flour, Vegetable oil, 
1. Mix flour, salt, pepper in a bowl.    2. In a separate bowl mix egg yolks, beer, & oil.    3. Mix all the above in a bowl together and let sit for 1 hour.    4. Whip egg whites into peaks and fold into the above mixture.    NOTES : Good for 1 Day.
