Pear Tartlets
Pears; cored & chopped, .  shells, .  garnish, Lemon peel; grated, Lemon juice, Corn syrup, Cinnamon, Prepared graham cracker tart, Flour; all-purpose, Nondairy whipped topping for, 
1. In medium glass bowl, combine pears, flour, cinnamon, lemon peel, juice  and corn syrup. Cover with wax paper and microwave on High 6 minutes,  stirring twice Let stand 5 minutes.    2. Divide pear filling evenly among tartlet shells. Garnish with topping,  if using.
