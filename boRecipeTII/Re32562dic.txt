Sweet Italian Hot Sausage
Ground pepper, Garlic, Dried parsley, Salt, Ground pork, Fennel seeds, Corrinder seeds, 
Using a morter and pestle or your blender, make a powder out of the fennel  seeds, corrinder seeds, dried parsley, salt minced garlic and pepper. Add  the spice mixture to the ground ;pork and lightly mix together, using your  hands. Shape the meat into a loaf and use within two days. You can make in  larger amounts if you want. Divide into pound portions, and wrap each  portion in plastic wrap, then in freezer foil. Store in freezer and use  within three months. Makes 1 pound.
