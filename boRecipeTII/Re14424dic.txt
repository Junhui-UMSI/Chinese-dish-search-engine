Whole Wheat Honey Muffins
Salt, Baking Powder, Eggs, milk, Honey, Vegetable oil, Cinnamon, Each whole wheat and white, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease muffin tins or line with paper baking  cups. Sift together flours, salt, baking powder and cinnamon. In a seperate  bowl, beat eggs. Add oil, honey and milk and mix well. Add liquid  ingredients to dry and mix. Fill each muffin tin 2/3 full. Bake at 400  degrees for 20 minutes, or until golden brown. Make 12 muffins.
