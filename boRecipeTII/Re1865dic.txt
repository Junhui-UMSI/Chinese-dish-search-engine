Chicken-Vegetable Chowder
Boneless skinless chicken, Fresh baby carrots; cut in, Sliced fresh mushrooms, Onions, Water, Garlic powder, Dried thyme leaves, (14 1/2 oz) ready-to-serve, (10 3/4 oz.) condensed cream, milk, All-purpose flour, (9 oz) Green Giant Harvest, 
1. In 3 1/2 to 4-quart Crock-Pot slow cooker, combine chicken, carrots,  mushrooms, onion, water, garlic powder, thyme and broth; mix well.    2. Cover; cook on low setting for 7 to 9 hours or until chicken is no  longer pink.    3. Drain fat from slow cooker. In small bowl, combine soup, milk and flour;  beat with wire whisk until smooth. Add soup mixture and broccoli to chicken  mixture. Cover; cook an additional 30 minutes or until broccoli is tender.    5(1 1/2-cup)servings. Per serving: 220 Cal; total fat 5g; (sat 2g); Choles  60mg; Sod 660mg; Total Carbs 17g; Fiber 3g; Sugars 5g; Pro 26g. POINTS: 4    MC Formatted by Sue B 5/31/98 and submitted to WW forum. NOTES  : Kitchen Tip:  To facilitate cleanup, spray the interior of the slow  cooker with nonstick cooking spray. It will help foods resist sticking  during the extended cooking time.
