Top Sirloin Steak Teriyaki
Soy sauce, Beef top sirloin steak, Ground ginger, Brown sugar, Garlic, 
Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger and garlic. Place steak on grill  over ash-covered coals. When first side is browned, brush with teriyaki  sauce, turn and finish cooking the second side, brushing with sauce  occasionally. Carve steak across grain into thin slices    This kitchen-tested recipe is from the National Live Stock and Meat Board.    SOMEHOW, NOTHING SATISFIES LIKE BEEF.
