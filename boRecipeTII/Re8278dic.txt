Potato Pancakes (Neighborhood Shopping)
Eggs, Salt, Pepper, Of cold water, Flour, Onion, very finely chopped, Potatoes, grated into bowl, Baking Powder, Vegetable oil, for frying, 
Soak potatoes in the water 10 minutes; drain and squeeze dry in a kitchen  towel. In large bowl combine all ingredients except oil; mix thoroughly.  Pour oil into large skillet to a depth of 1/8 inch; place over medium heat.  For each pancake, portion 1/2 cup Makes 4 to 6 servings (12 pancakes);    Recipe provided by The National Potato Promotion Board.
