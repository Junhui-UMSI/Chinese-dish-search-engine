Vidalia Onion Chili Relish
Celery seed, Allspice, Cinnamon, Vinegar, Bell peppers, Tomatoes, Jumbo Vidalia onions, Cloves, Ginger, Mustard seed, Hot peppers, Salt, Sugar, 
Tie all spices in bag. Scald and peel tomatoes. Chop onions and peppers.  Add all ingredients. Cook down. Put in jars and seal. Makes about 14 pints.
