Quakers Tantalizing Baked Chicken
Broiler-fryer chicken, Margarine, Pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, Quaker Oats, uncooked, Paprika, Salt, milk, 
Heat oven to 375 F.  Place dry ingredients in blender container or food  processor bowl; cover.  Blend or process about 1 minute, stopping  occasionally to stir.  Coat chicken pieces with oat mixture; dip into  milk.  Coat again with oat mixture.  Place in 15x10-inch jelly roll pan;  drizzle with margarine.  Bake 55 to 60 minutes or until juices run clear  when chicken is pierced with fork.
