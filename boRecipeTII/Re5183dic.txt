Pigs in Blankets
Bacon, Biscuits, Cheese, Frankfurters, 
Take each frank, wrap 1/2 slice cheese around it. Take 1 pc. bacon and  wrap over frank and cheese.  Wrap 1 biscuit around frank. Continue until  ingreds. are used up. Place on cookie sheet and bake at 450 F. until golden  brown.  Serve hot. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers BillSpalding *P CRBR 38 A
