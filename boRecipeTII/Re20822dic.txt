Apple Honey Crumble
MacIntosh apples; peeled,, Honey or a little less, Orange juice concentrate, Nutmeg, Flour, Vanilla, Cloves, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, Canola oil, Sugar, Egg, Ginger, Cinnamon, Salt, 
Notes: By Mary M. Crook, Lansford. The Times News, PA    Preheat oven to 350?. Toss apples, honey, brown sugar, orange juice  concentrate and spices. Place the mixture in a 9x13 pan and set aside. In a  separate bowl mix the oil, sugar, flour, baking powder, salt, vanilla and  egg to form a thick batter. Drop by spoonfuls onto apple mixture. Bake 45  minutes at 350? or until top is nicely browned. Makes 8 servings.    Posted to recipelu-digest by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I Canty) on Mar 6, 1998
