Perky Squash Soup
(8 oz) tomato sauce, Pepper, Dried whole oregano, Buttter; melted, Onion; coarsely chopped, Garlic, Parsley, Water, Grated parmesan, Dried whole thyme, Yellow squash; sliced 1/4", Chicken broth, Salt, 
Saute onion and garlic in butter in dutch oven til tender. Add remaining  ingredients except cheese. Cover and simmer 20min. or til squash is tender.  Top w/ cheese. Yield: about 6C.    from "Southern Living Annual Recipes"    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by "Dan/Trina D." <itisme@swbell.net> on Dec  07, 1997
