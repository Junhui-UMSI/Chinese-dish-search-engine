Yogurt Deviled Eggs
Lemon juice or dry vermouth, Parsley flakes or freeze, Worcestershire Sauce, Hard-cooked eggs, Prepared mustard, Salt, Instant minced onion, Plain yogurt, Pepper, Paprika, 
Cut eggs in half lengthwise. Remove yolks and set whites aside. mash  yolks with a fork. Blend in remaining ingredients. Refill whites  using about 1 tablespoon yolk mixture for each egg half.
