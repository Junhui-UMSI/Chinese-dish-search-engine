Baked Fish Portions
PAPRIKA GROUND, BUTTER PRINT SURE, FISH FILLETS FLAT FZ, 
PAN:  18 BY 24-INCH SHEET PAN                  TEMPERATURE:  375 F. OVEN    1.  PLACE FISH IN WELL GREASED PANS.    2.  BRUSH FISH WITH MELTED BUTTER OR MARGARINE; SPRINKLE WITH PAPRIKA.    3.  BAKE 35 MINUTES OR UNTIL LIGHTLY BROWNED.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 1, PORTIONS SHOULD NOT TOUCH EACH OTHER WHILE BAKING.    NOTE:  2.  IN STEP 1, DO NOT THAW FISH PORTIONS BEFORE BAKING.    NOTE:  3.  OTHER SIZES AND TYPES OF PANS MAY BE USED. SEE RECIPE NO.  A02500.    Recipe Number: L12400    SERVING SIZE: 4 OZ    From the <Army Master Recipe Index File> (actually used today!).
