Porcupine Beef Balls
Condensed tomato soup, Onion, Salt, Pepper, Hot water, Fat, Uncooked rice, Ground beef, 
Source: Cutco Cook Book    Combine rice, beef, salt, pepper and onion. Mix and shape into 12 balls.  Brown in fat in heavy fry pan. Add tomato soup and water. Cover and cook  11/2 hours over low heat until rice is tender.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #252 by fancymom@juno.com (Frances Kahn)  on Sep 18, 1997
