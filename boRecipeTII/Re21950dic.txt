Applesauce Cake
Baking soda, Butter, Cinnamon, Sugar, Cake flour, Salt, Cloves, Raisins, Applesauce, hot, Egg, beaten loghtly, Nutmeats, 
Cream butter and sugar. Add egg. Stir in dry ingredients. Add hot  applesauce. Bake in 350 degree oven about 1 hr and 15 mins or until  toothpick comes out clean. Keeps moist for days.    Mrs. Louis Radaj    From a book of recipes compiled by members and friends of the LONDONTOWN  PUBLIK HOUSE ASSEMBLY, EDGEWATER, MD 21037.
