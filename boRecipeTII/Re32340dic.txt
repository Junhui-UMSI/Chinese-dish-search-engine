Barley Bread
Active dry yeast, Millet flour, Barley flour, All-purpose flour;, Millet flour, Vegetable broth; or water, Active dry yeast, Vegetable broth; or water, Small Loaf, Salt, Dark brown sugar; packed, Vital wheat gluten, Canola oil, Barley flour, Egg, Large Loaf, Salt, Canola oil, Dark brown sugar; packed, All-purpose flour;, Eggs, Vital wheat gluten, 
Pour all wet ingredients into bread machine baking pan. Measure all other  ingredients except yeast into baking pan. Sprinkle yeast over dry  ingredients, being careful not to get yeast wet. Use rapid bake cycle on  your machine for this loaf.    Recipe by: More Recipes for Your Bread Machine Bakery by R. W. Langer    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #441 by "Christopher E.
