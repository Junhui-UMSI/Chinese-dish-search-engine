Pepperoni Pizza - Hamburger Pie
Green Bell Pepper, Shredded Mozzarella Cheese;, Mushrooms, Chopped Pepperoni; (2 Oz.), Sliced Ripe Olives, Dry bread crumbs, Chopped Fresh Oregano; Or, Spaghetti Sauce, Salt, Egg, Lean ground beef, 
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Mix beef, bread crumbs, egg, oregano and salt;  press evenly against bottom and side of ungreased pie plate, 9x1 1/4  inches. Sprinkle mushrooms, bell pepper, pepperoni, and olives into  meat-lined plate. Pour spaghetti sauce over toppings.    Bake uncovered 25 minutes or until beef is no longer pink in center and  juice is clear; carefully drain. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake about 5 minutes  longer or until cheese is light brown. Let pie stand 5 minutes before  cutting.    Recipe by: Betty Crockers Best of Baking, 1997, pg. 189    Posted to recipelu-digest by Jill & Joe Proehl <jpxtwo@swbell.net> on Feb  08, 1998
