Poppyseed Muffins #2
Sugar, Eggs, Flour, Vegetable oil, Poppy seeds, Vanilla, WET MIX, DRY MIX, Yoghurt, Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven to 375F, prepare pans. Combine wet mix ingredients & mix  well. Add combined dry mix to wet mix until just combined. Spoon into pans  & bake for 10-15 mins. Makes 12.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
