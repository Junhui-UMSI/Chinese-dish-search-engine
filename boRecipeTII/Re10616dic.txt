Quick Pizza
Basil, crumbled, Baked cheese pizza crust, Pepperoni slices *(optional), Grated Parmesan cheese, Purchased pizza sauce, Fat-free mozzarella cheese,, 
Preheat oven to 450.  Place curst on a large baking sheet.  Spread pizza  sause over curst.  Spread shredded mozzarella evenly over top and sprinkle  with basil.  Arrange pepperoni slices over top (if using). Sprinkle with  Parmesan.  Bake 10 minutes or until topping is bubbly. This recipe serves  4.
