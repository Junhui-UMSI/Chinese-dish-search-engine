Apple Meringue
Tart apples,peeled and cored, White seedless raisins,, Egg whites, Sugar, Salt, LORELI AGUDA WSKD49A, Butter, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
Slice apples about 1/4 inch thick. Place in a well buttered baking dish.  Pour melted butter over apples. Sprinkle with raisins, lemon juice and 1/2  of the sugar. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350 for about 20  minutes, or until apples are tender. Beat egg whites with salt until stiff.  Gradually add the remaining sugar to make a stiff meringue. Spread meringue  over apples to the edge of dish. Bake an additional 10 minutes, or until  meringue is brown. Serve warm. Cream may be added if desired.
