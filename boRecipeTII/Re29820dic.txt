Ten Spice Honey Dip
Honey, BARB DAY, Pickled Ginger juice; from, -China Moon Ten Spice; see, Soy sauce; plus 1 tablespoon, 
Makes 1 cup.     Combine all of the ingredients in a small non-aluminum saucepan. Heat,  stirring, over low heat until the honey dissolves, 3 to 4 minutes. Store,  refrigerated, in a clean container.  Warm over low heat to a liquid  consistency before using.
