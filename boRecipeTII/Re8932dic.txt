Preserving Dry Nuts (Canning)
Information, 
Spread shelled nuts evenly on a cookie sheet and dry in a 250 degrees oven  for at least 45 minutes or until dry, stirring occasionally to prevent  scorching. Pack dry nuts in pint mason jars to within 1/2-inch of top.  Cover with hot lids and screw bands on firmly. Place jars on rack in  pressure canner with 2-inches of water in the bottom. Process at 5 pounds  of pressure for 10 minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 236 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 10, 1997
