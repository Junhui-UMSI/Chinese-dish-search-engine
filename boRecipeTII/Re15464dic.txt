White Chocolate Brownies
Vanilla extract, Stick (4oz) butter, Eggs, Sugar, Baking Powder, Flour, Salt, Semisweet or bittersweet, Imported white chocolate,, Walnuts, 
1. Preheat oven to 350F. In a 2-quart glass bowl, combine white chocolate  pieces, sugar, and butter. Heat in microwave oven on High about 1-1/2  minutes, or until melted and smooth when stirred.    2. With a whisk or fork, beat in eggs and vanilla until well blended. Add  flour, baking powder, and salt and stir until well mixed. Stir in  bittersweet chocolate and walnuts.    3. Spread evenly in a buttered, foil lined 7x11 inch baking pan. Bake 20 to  25 minutes, or until golden brown. Do not overbake. Let cool, then cut into       24    pieces.
