Emerils Bbq Ribs
Plums, peeled, pitted, diced, Whole roasted shallots, Garlic, Chicken Stock, Ketchup, Molasses, Honey, PLUS 1 ts white vinegar, Soy sauce, Dry mustard, Salt and pepper, (2 racks) baby back ribs,, Trimmed of fat, Creole seasoning, Liquid crab boil, Bay Leaves, "Cilantro Potato Salad", 
Make glaze: Combine plums, shallots, garlic, stock, ketchup, molasses,  honey, vinegar, soy, mustard, 1 ts of salt and 6 turns of pepper in medium  saucepan. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium and simmer,  stirring occasionally, 15 mins. Transfer to a food processor and puree  until smooth. (Makes 2 2/3 cups). Heat oven to 375~. Place ribs in a large  stockpot and cover with 4 quarts of cold water. Add Creole seasoning, crab  boil, bay leaves and 2 ts salt. Bring to a boil over high heat and cook 10  mins. Place a rack in a large roasting pan. Drain ribs and season them on  all sides with remaining salt and 12 turns of pepper. Brush with glaze to  coat. Place ribs on rack and bake, turning and basting every 10-15 mins,  until dark brown, crusty and carmelized, about 1 hour and 25 mins. When  cool enough to handle, cut into individual ribs. Serves 5 ribs per plate.  Serve with Cilantro Potato Salad. Formatted by jayne@idt.net    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Jayne Oake <jayne@idt.net> on Aug 12, 1998
