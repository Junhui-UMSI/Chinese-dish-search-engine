Rancho California Rice
Onion, Whole green chilis (canned), Rice, cooked, Cottage cheese, Sour cream, Grated cheddar, Bay leaf, crumbled, Butter, Salt & Pepper, to taste, 
Saute onions in butter until limp. Add rice, sourcream, cottage cheese, bay  leaf, salt & pepper. Mix well. In greased cassarole dish, put a layer of  rice mixture, a layer of chilies (seeded and cut into strips), and 1/2 cup  cheddar. Repeat (save 1/3 C cheddar) ending with a layer of rice. Bake for  25 min in 375F oven. Top with remaining cheese, and bake 10 more minutes    Source: US Bi-cenntennial commemmorative cookbook Governor Ronald Reagan,  California
