Pineapple Sauce for Ham
Pineapple juice, Brown sugar, Crushed pineapple, Soy sauce, Water, Cornstarch, Vinegar, 
Mix first 4 items together in sauce pan & heat. While heating mix last 3  items together & pour into sauce pan with other ingredients. Pour over  baked ham. good good...
