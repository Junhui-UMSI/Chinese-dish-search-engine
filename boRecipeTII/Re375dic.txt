Lemon Chess Pie
Eggs, Lemon juice, Lemon Peel, Vanilla, Sugar, Cornmeal, milk, Butter, Flour, 
Lightly blend sugar, meal and flour. Beat eggs and blend in sugar mixture.  Add remaining ingredients. Pour into unbaked 9 inch pie shell. Bake at 375  for 30-40 minutes.  Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #439 by "Mike England"  <englandm@kear.tds.net> on Dec 05, 97
