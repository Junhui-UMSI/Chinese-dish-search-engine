Pineapple Upside Down
Shortening, Marachino cherries, Eggs, Butter, Salt, Vanilla extract, Pineapple, Brown sugar, Baking Powder, Sugar, Flour, milk, 
http://ecep1.usl.edu/cajun/des/10140.htm    *** Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time and beat well. Add  sifted dry ingredients alternately with milk and vanilla; mix well. Melt  butter in 9-inch square pan. Sprinkle with brown sugar; stir until melted.  Arrange pineapple slices in pan; place cherry in center of each slice. Pour  batter over topping. Bake in 3500F oven for 50-60 minutes until done.    Posted to FOODWINE Digest 30 Jan 97 by Arnold Elser <PastryWiz@MSN.COM> on  Jan 30, 1997.
