Uncle Tais Special Drink
Triple sec, Jigger dark rum, Jigger light rum, Pineaple juice, Orange juice, Orgeat almond syrup, Jigger apricot brandy, 
Combine rums, apricot brandy,almond syrup & Triple Sec. Fill glass with  equal portions orange juice & pineapple juice. Orgeat almond syrup is a  flavored sugar syrup which is available at liquor stores. This may be  decorated with miniature paper parasols.
