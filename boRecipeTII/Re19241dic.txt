Vegetable Rice
Tomato -- diced, Oregano, Cheese -- grated, Rice -- uncooked, Mushroom -- sliced, Onion -- chopped, Chicken bouillon cubes, Butter, Water, Broccoli -- chopped, Salt, 
From: Gerald Edgerton <jerrye@wizard.com>    Date: Sat, 18 May 1996 16:17:20 -0700    Cook onions in butter until golden; Add mushrooms, broccoli and tomatoes  and cook about 4 minutes. Add water and bouillon cubes and bring to a boil.  Stir in rice, salt and oregano. Cover and cook over low heat, 20 minutes or  until water is absorbed. Stir in parmesan cheese, serve hot or cold.
