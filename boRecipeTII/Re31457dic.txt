Barbecue Sauce
Tomato juice, Chili powder, Bay Leaves, Cayenne pepper, Catsup, Brown Sugar, Paprika, Cider vinegar, Salt, Dry mustard, Worcestershire Sauce, Water, 
Mix together brown sugar, paprika, salt, dry mustard, bay leaves, chili  powder, cayenne pepper, Worcestershire, vinegar, tomato juice, catsup and  water in large saucepan.     Place over high heat and bring to boil.  Reduce heat to simmer and cook  until slightly thickened, about 30 minutes. Discard bay leaves. Makes about        4    cups.
