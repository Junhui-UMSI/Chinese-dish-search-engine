Really Simple Barbeque Sauce
Vinegar, Catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, , Onion, Fat, Brown sugar, Lemon juice, Water, Tabasco sauce, 
Brown onion in the oil and add remaining ingredients.  Simmer for 30  minutes.  Transfer to a blender and throughly blend, making a smooth sauce,  ready for beef, chicken, pork, or little fuzzy lambs...
