Potato Hash with Rosemary
Baking potato; peeled and, Fresh rosemary sprigs; for, Sweet potato; peeled and, Black Pepper, Garlic, Onions, Fresh rosemary; chopped, olive oil, Salt, 
Stir together first 8 ingredients in a lightly greased nonstick skillet.  Cover and cook over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, 25 minutes or  until potatoes are tender. Garnish if desired.
