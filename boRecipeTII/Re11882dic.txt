Real French Dressing (Basic)
Garlic, Fresh ground pepper, Balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Dill weed, Dried basil, Dry mustard, Paprika, Salt, 
Put all ingredients in a bowl except the garlic and have ready the clove of  garlic. Right before serving, crush garlic and put into bowl with all other  ingredients and whisk together very well.
