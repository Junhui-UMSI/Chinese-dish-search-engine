A Good Hot Sandwich
Mayonnaise, Onions, Cubed American cheese, Spam; cubed, Chopped green bell pepper, Chopped green olives, Hard-cooked eggs; chopped, Chopped sweet pickle, 
Combine all ingredients; mix well. Put into hamburger buns and wrap each  sandwich in foil. Bake at 325F for 30 minutes.    Recipe by: Hellen Mitten    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 274 by Nancy Pallotta  <nancee@neo.lrun.com> on Nov 18, 1997
