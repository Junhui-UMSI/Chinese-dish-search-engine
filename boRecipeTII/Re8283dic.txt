Potato Pancakes or Potato Cakes
Eggs, Potatoes *, Baking Powder, onion *, Flour, Salt, Pepper, Vegetable oil, 
Soak potatoes in the water for 10 minutes; drain and squeeae dry in a  kitchen towel. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except the oil; mix  thoroughly. Pour oil into a large skillet to a depth of 1/8 inch;Place ofer  medium heat. For each pancake, pour 1/2 cup potato mixture into the pan;  flatten slightly. Cook about 6 minutes until crispy and brown, turning  once. Repeat with more oil and remaining potato mixture. Serve with sour  cream and applesauce, if desired. Makes 12 servings. * 2 pounds potatoes,  grated into a bowl of cold water. * 1 medium onion, very finely chopped.
