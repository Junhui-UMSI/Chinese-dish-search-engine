Quinoa Summer Salad
AM Unrefined Olive Oil, Lemon juice, and drained, Shelled pecans, Chopped green onion, Sliced black olives, plumped in hot water, Tamari, Raisins, Pepper, Cooked AM Quinoa, Mushrooms, 
Mix first six ingredients together in a large bowl.  In a separate  container mix the last four ingredients.  Pour the liquids over the salad  and toss gently.  For the best flavor let set in the refrigerator for  about one hour before serving.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "The Native Americans" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
