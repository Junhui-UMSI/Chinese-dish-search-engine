Whole Chicken in a Bag
Whole thyme, Whole chicken, juice of 1/2 a lemon, olive oil, Aluminum foil, Paper bag, lunch sack size, salt and pepper, Cooking parchement paper, Garlic, 
Prepare a dressing of the oil,garlic,thyme and lemon juice. Rub the bird  with this dressing, and add a bit of salt and pepper. Tear off an  18-inch-long piece of parchment paper, and carefully wrap up the chicken.  Place the bundle in a paper bag, and then wrap in alumiun foil. Place the  bundle on a rack in the oven. Bake at 375 F for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
