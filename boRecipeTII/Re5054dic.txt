Pickling Spice with Chilies (English)
Pimento [allspice]., Coriander seed, Yellow mustard seed, Zanzibar cloves, Indian black pepper, Jamaica ginger, Mace, Birdseye or Nyasa chillies, 
An English mixture of whole spices, used for chutneys, pickled fruits and  vegetables, and to spice vinegar. The spices can either be tied in a small  muslin bag and removed after pickling, or added straight to the vinegar,  depending on the type of pickle.    Spice merchants have their own versions; here are two given in Laws  Grocers Manual (1950 edition). To make manageable domestic amounts,  substitute 25 g (1 oz) or even 15 g (1/2 oz) for each pound.
