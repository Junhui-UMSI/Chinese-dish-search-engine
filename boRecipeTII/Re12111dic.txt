Zucca Brusca
Vegetable oil, for frying, Whole zucchini, sliced, Lemon juice, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt and pepper, to taste, Beef broth, Cloves garlic, chopped, 
Fry zucchini slices in deep oil until brown. Drain and add other  ingredients. Bring sauce to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes. Serve on top  of Pasta.
