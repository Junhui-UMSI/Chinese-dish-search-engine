Swedish Limpa Bread
Whole-wheat flour, Active dry yeast, Canola oil, Molasses; unsulphered, Warm water; plus 2, Vital wheat gluten, Carob, Caraway seed, Anise seed; lightly crushed, Grated zest of 1 1/2, Whey, Salt, Lecithin; liquid or granules, Rye flour, 
Put all ingredients in bread pan in order listed.  If your machine calls  for dry ingredients first, reverse order. Ssetmachine on whole grain mode,  light or medium crust. Timer OK.  Serving Ideas : Especially good with split pea, lentil or bean soups.
