From:    Cheryl Miller <csmiller@LAMAR.COLOSTATE.EDU>    Date:    Fri, 26 Jul 1996 20:24:21 +0000  Press 1 package (1o ounces) frozen strawberries, thawed, through a strainer  to measure 3/4 cup.  Heat strawberry juice, 3 cups white or cider vinegar  and 1 cup sugar to boiling, stirring frequently. Boil and stir 1 minute.  Cool. Pour into bottles and cover tightly. Use in salad dressing and as  marinades. Makes 1 qt.    EAT-L Digest 25 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
