Western Chickchunks
Cornflake crumbs*, Salt, Skinless boneless chicken, Fat-free ranch dressing, Plus, Black pepper, 
submitted by: johanssen@matnet.com  Preheat oven to 450F. Line large baking sheet with foil; spray with  non-stick cooking spray.    In small bowl, combine chicken and ranch dressing, tossing well to coat  thoroughly. On shallow plate or in gallon-size sealable plastic bag,  combine cornflake crumbs, salt and pepper. One at a time, add chicken  pieces to crumb mixture, turning chicken or sealing bag and shaking to coat  evenly.    Arrange chicken pieces on the prepared baking sheet; bake 15-20 minutes,  until cooked through and juices run clear when pierced with a fork.    *You may substitute bread crumbs for the corn flakes.
