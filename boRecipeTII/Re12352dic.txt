Zucchini Southern Style
Zucchini, Onion, Bacon, Salt and pepper, 
Wash zucchini well under cold tap water.  Cut off ends.  Slice 1" thick.  Put aside. Cut up bacon in 1" pieces. Put in 2-quart saucepan. Peel and  chop onion.  Put in saucepan.  Add zucchini. Cover pan. Put on medium heat.  Cook 15-20 minutes.  Stir around 2-3 times during cooking. Add salt and  pepper to taste.  Serve.
