Whiskey Sausage
Whiskey, Brown sugar, Ketchup, Sausage, sliced-2" pieces, 
Mix sugar, ketchup and whiskey. In a crock pot, layer with sausage, sauce,  sausage, sauce etc. Cook overnight on low. I used this recipe for deer  sausage and let it cook in the oven for about 1 hr. at approx. 325F and  served it in a big clay pot.
