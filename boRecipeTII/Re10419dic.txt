Quick Cobbler Pie
Sugar, Baking Powder, Salt, milk, Flour, 
Preheat oven to 375 Mix above ingredients. Put 1/2 c. oleo in 8 x 12 baking  dish and melt. Pour above mixture into oleo in baking dish. Add 1 medium  sized can of sweetened fruit or 3 to 4 cups of fresh fruit. If fruit is not  sweetened, add 1/2 c. sugar on top before baking. Bake about 45 min    Recipe by: Mrs. Schoonover    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #825 by Tommy & Luann Boggus  <tommylou@c-com.net> on Oct 04, 1997
