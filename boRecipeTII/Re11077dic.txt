Rainbow Trout Provencale
Onion, Fennel seeds, Bell pepper, Chopped fresh parsley, Tomato paste, Butter, Salt and pepper, Dry vermouth, Garlic, Trout, 
Combine butter and next four ingredients in microwavable dish. Cover  and microwave on full power for 2 minutes. Stir in vermouth, tomato paste  and parsley. Season with salt and pepper. Place trout fillets flesh-side  down on vegetable mixture; cover and cook for 2 minutes. Rotate dish; cook  1 to 2 minutes longer, or until fish flakes with a fork. Place trout on  serving plate and top with vegetable mixture. Garnish with additional  parsley. Makes 2 to 4 servings. About 255 calories per 4-oz serving.
