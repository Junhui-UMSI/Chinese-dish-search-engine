Banana-Orange Frozen Push-Ups
Milk; powdered, Water, Bananas, Fat-free yogurt, Orange juice; frozen, 
Thaw orange juice until soft.  Peel bananas and slice into blender or food  processor.  Add remaining ingredients. Cover and blend until foamy. Pour  into small paper cups and freeze.  To eat, squeeze bottom of cup. Or pour  into popsicle mold. from the American Heart Association.
