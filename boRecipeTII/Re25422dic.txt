Tortilla Lasagna
Black olives; pitted, Oregano, Cumin, Kidney or pinto beans,, Nutritional yeast "cheesy", Green pepper;finely chopped, Vinegar, Garlic powder, Tomato sauce, salt-free or, Onions; finely chopped, Tortillas, corn, 6-inch; cut, 
Preheat oven to 375 deg.    Spray a baking pay with nonstick spray.    Spread 1/4 cup of tomato sauce in the bottom of the prepared pan. Ina large  bowl, combine remaining tomato sauce with remaining ingredients, exept  tortillas and "cheese." Mix well.    Arrange 1/3 of the tortilla sections over sauce in pan. Spread 1 cup of  sauce over tortillas. Pour 1/3 of the "cheese" sauce over the sauce.    Repeat with another 1/3 of the tortillas, 1 cup of sauce and 1/3 of the  cheese sauce.    Repeat one more time, using remaining tortilla sections, sauce and cheese  sauce.    Cover and bake 20 minutes. Uncover and continue baking 15 more minutes.    Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
