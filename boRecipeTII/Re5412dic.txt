Pineapple Cheesecake #1
Nutmeg, BASIC MIX, Whipped cream, FRUIT MIX, (450-gram) crushed, Oranges; juice of, Boiling water, Butter, Gelatine dissolved in:, (generous) cornflake crumbs, Cream cheese, Crust, Passionfruit; pulp of, (440-gram) condensed milk, Lemon juice, 
Date: Mon, 25 Mar 96 13:05:40 EST    submitted by: thid@voyager.co.nz (Theresa, Tauranga, New Zealand)  Beat softened cream cheese till smooth & creamy. Gradually add condensed  milk, beating well then add lemon juice gradually. whip cream & fold into  mixture. Add dissolved gelatine.    Crust: Press firmly onto base only of greased 20cm spring form tin and  refrigerate.    Combine fruit.  Add 1/3 fruit mixture to basic cheesecake. Pour into  prepared spring formed tin. Allow to set. Heat remaining 2/3 fruit mixture,  thicken with a little arrowroot.  Spread over cake. Refrigerate till firm.  Decorate with cream.
