Tricolor Crudites Platter
Minced fresh basil OR, Plus 1 T red wine vinegar, Large zucchini, grated, Minced fresh chives or, Large cucumbers, peeled,, Green onions, Large raw beets, peeled,, Salt, Dijon mustard, Large carrots, peeled,, Dried basil, Minced fresh rosemary OR, Dried Rosemary, Plus 2 T olive oil, Bunch radishes, trimmed, 
Whisk vinegar and Dijon mustard to blend in small bowl. Gradually whisk in  olive oil.  Mix in basil, chives and rosemary. Season with salt and pepper.  (Dressing can be prepared 1 day ahead.  Cover and refrigerate.)    Toss cucumbers and 2 teaspoons salt in bowl.  Let stand 1 hour. Rinse and  drain well.  Place cucumbers in small bowl; add enough dressing to coat.    Place beets, carrots and zucchini in separate bowls. Toss each vegetable  with enough dressing to coat. (Vegetables can be made 4 hours ahead. Cover  and chill.)    Mound vegetables on platter.  Garnish with radishes.    Bon Appetit/May 94 Typed by Didi Pahl
