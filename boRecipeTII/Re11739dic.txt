Rats Darkness
Black Patent Malt, Cascade hop pellets, Whitebread dry ale yeast, Pack, Saaz hop pellets (boiling), John Bull Dark Extract, (finishing), Crystal Malt, 
Cracked  the grains and put them in 1.5 gallons of water, bring to  boil  and remover grains after 5 mins, add boiling hops and extract.  Cook for 1  hour,  add finishing hops for last 10 minutes. add to water  in  fer-  menter,  bring  level  up to 5 gallons. ferments out in  about  8  days,  tasted  good  right out of the fermenter, ready to drink in  about  8-10  days. Bottled with 1 cup Amber Dry Extract.
