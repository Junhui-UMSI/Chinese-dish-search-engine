Vegetable Dip #1
carrot, Celery, Lemon juice, low-fat cottage, cucumber, Onion, green bell pepper, 
Combine vegetables, mixing well. Puree cottage cheese in blender and add  lemon juice. Put in mixer and beat until fluffy. Fold in vegetables; chill  several hours. Makes 3 cups.     NOTE: For a party sandwich, spread on thinly sliced bread, top with second  slice of bread, trim off crusts and cut into 3 long strips.
