Indian Mango Cheese Cake

Also, 2 recipes from food network that Ive meant to try but havent. They  looked great on the show, Cooking Live.    Preheat oven to 325 degrees. In a large bowl, stir together cheese, bread  crumbs, sugar, salt, eggs, butter, cardamom, and almond extract. Spoon into  a greased 9- x 9-inch baking pan. Smooth top, and sprinkle with mango and  almonds.    Bake on middle rack of the oven until top is golden, about 1 hour and 15  minutes. Let cool (it will keep in refrigerator for a week). Cut into  3-inch squares and serve with mango sauce (see recipe below).    Yield: 6 servings    MANGO SAUCE: Combine all the ingredients and puree in a food processor.  Refrigerate and chill until needed.    Yield: 2 cups    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by ddmmom@popalex1.linknet.net on Aug 29,  1998,
