Honey Cake
Salt, Flour, Baking Powder, Allspice, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Honey, Coffee; warm, Sugar, Raisins;, (optional), Eggs, Vegetable oil, 
Recipe By : Mrs. Max Tofle of Congregation Shaare Emeth, St. Louis, MO    Oven at 350?. Sift together 1 c. flour, baking powder, baking soda and  spices. (Can use 3 tsp. instant coffee in 2 c. of water instead of regular  coffee.) Beat together eggs and sugar; add honey and oil. Beat well. Add  dry ingredients and coffee alternately. Add raisins and stir.  Bake in greased and floured loaf pans for 1 hour (glass pan) or 1 hour and  15 minutes (larger metal pan) or until toothpick comes out dry. Can be  frozen.
