Penne Alla Puttane Delba
Cloves garlic,; sliced paper, Young zucchini,; sliced, Tiny black olives, Juice of 1/2 lemon, Kosher or sea salt, Leaves basil,; torn in half, Overripe tomatoes, roughly, Giant capers, rinsed; of, Crushed red pepper flakes, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Dried penne pasta,, 
In a large bowl, combine zucchini, garlic, basil, tomatoes, olive oil,  capers, olives, lemon juice, pepper flakes, and salt. Allow to stand in  cool place 1/2 hour.    Meanwhile, bring 6 quarts water to boil in spaghetti pot and add 2  tablespoons salt. Drop penne in and cook according to package instructions  until ?al dente@, about 10 to 12 minutes. Drain pasta in colander and add  pasta to cool vegetables in mixing bowl. Stir gently to mix well and serve  immediately.    This dish is not served hot!!
