White Salad Dressing
Vinegar, Salt, Egg yolk, well beaten, Flour, olive oil, Cream, Mustard, dry, Sugar, 
Mix the dry ingredients together and slowly add the vinegar and oil. Stir  in the cream and beaten egg yolks and cook in top of double boiler until  thick. Source: Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary  Arts Press, 1936.
