Pineapple and Cherry Butterscotch Cake
Brown sugar, Baking Powder, maraschino cherries, sliced pineapple, Sugar, Whipped cream, Vanilla, Flour, Eggs, Salt, Butter, Shortening, milk, 
Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt; set aside. In another  bowl, cream the sugar and shortening; add eggs. Add milk alternately with  the dry ingredients to creamed mixture. Add vanilla and mix well. Grease a  10 inch round cake pan and line with brown sugar, about 1/2 inch deep. Dot  with butter. In the pan, place slices of pineapple with a amaraschino  cherry in center of each. Pour the cake batter over the mixture in the pan.  Bake at 350 for 50 minutes. Turn out onto plate and serve hot with whipped  cream topped with cherries.    Recipe by: Quick and Easy Home Cooking Magazine Posted to MC-Recipe Digest  V1 #731 by L979@aol.com on Aug 9, 1997
