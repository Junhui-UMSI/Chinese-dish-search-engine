Pound Cake with Lemon Sauce
Sugar, Cornstarch, Boiling water, Sticks butter, Lemon juice, Butter, Salt, Eggs, Box (1-lb) confectioners, Vanilla, Flour, LEMON SAUCE, Lemon rind; grated, 
Cream butter & sugar; add eggs, one at a time, beating 1-1/2 minutes after  each addition. Add flour & mix well. Pour into oiled Bundt pan or 2 loaf  pans. Bake at 350 for 1-1/2 hours (less for loaf pans). Sprinkle with  confectioners sugar or top with Lemon Sauce. Lemon Sauce: Combine sugar &  cornstarch; add boiling water. Cook, stirring constantly until thickened.  Add remaining ingredients & serve warm with pound cake.    MRS R.M.
