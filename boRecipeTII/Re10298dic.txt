Quick Bean Salad with Tuna
solid light tuna, Fresh parsley, Italian plum tomatoes, fresh, romano beans, Onion, olive oil, red onion, Lemon juice, black olives, 
Break up tuna fish and combine with beans, onions, tomatoes and olives. In  separate bowl whisk together lemon juice, parsley and oil.  Pour over  mixture.  Toss to coat and serve.  Additional oil and lemon juice may be  added if desired. Source: The Toronto Star Newspaper, July 30th, 1996.
