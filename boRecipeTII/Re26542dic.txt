Bacon-Spinach Salad
White vinegar, Sugar, Green Onion, Salt, Spinach, Bacon, Pepper, 
Cook bacon in 12-inch skillet over low heat, stirring occasionally, until  crisp. Stir in vinegar. Heat through, remove from heat.    Add spinach and onions to bacon mixture. Sprinkle with sugar, salt, and  pepper. Toss 1 to 2 minutes or until spinach is wilted.
