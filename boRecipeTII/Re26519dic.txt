Bacon, Dill, and Onion Potato Salad
Bacon, Green Onions, egg, Sour cream, Fresh dill, Green pepper, Salt, Dijon mustard, Lemon juice, Red potatoes, milk, 
1. Slice potatoes into 1/4-inch-thick slices; place in a large saucepan;  add water to cover. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer 15  minutes. Drain.    2. Combine potato, green onions, and next 3 ingredients in a bowl; toss  gently.    3. Combine sour cream and remaining 5 ingredients in a small bowl; stir  well. Pour dressing over potato mixture; toss gently. Cover and chill.    Recipe by: Americas Best Recipes 1997    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "Marie Smith" <crafty1@earthlink.net> on May  2, 1998
