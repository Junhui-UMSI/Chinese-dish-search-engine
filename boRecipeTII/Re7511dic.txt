Pork Parmesan
Vegetable oil, Mozzarella cheese, Egg, Paprika, Pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, Salt, Boneless pork loin chops;, Tomato sauce, Dry bread crumbs, 
In a shallow bowl, combine the bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper  and paprika. In another bowl, beat egg. Dip each pork chop in egg, then  coat with crumb mixture. In a skillet, cook pork chops in oil over medium  heat for 6 minutes on each side or until juices run clear. Top each chop  with tomato sauce and cheese; cover and simmer for 1 minute or until cheese  is melted.
