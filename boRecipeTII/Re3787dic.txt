Pennslyvannia-Dutch Apple Cake
Eggs, Baking soda, Sugar, Vanilla extract, Cinnamon, White sugar, apples, Baking Powder, All-purpose flour, Cooking oil, Orange juice, 
Toss the apple slices with 1/4 cup sugar and the cinnimon ; set aside. Mix  together the flour, 1 1/2 cups sugar, eggs, oil and vanilla, orange juice.  baking soda and baking powder. Beat with an electric mixer at medium speed.  (The batter will be very thick). Spread a 3rd. of the batter in a greased  10 inch tube or bundt pan, top with half the apple mixture. Repeat layers  ending with batter.    Bake in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for 1 hour. Remove pan to wire rack;  cool for about 10 minutes. remove cake from pan.
