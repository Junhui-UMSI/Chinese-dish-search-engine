Zippy Cauliflower
Low fat margarine, All purpose flour, Salt, Red (cayenne) pepper, Pepper, Skim milk, Cauliflower, Fresh bread crumbs, low fat cheddar cheese, green chilies, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Steam cauliflower just until tender; drain. Melt  margarine in a medium size saucepan over medium heat. In a jar with a lid  combine flour, red pepper, salt, pepper, and milk; shake until blended.  Slowly add to margarine in saucepan, stirring constantly until smooth.  Add cheese and continue stirring until smooth and slightly thickened. Stir  in chilies. Arrange cauliflower in a  2 quart baking dish; pour sauce over  cauliflower, then sprinkle with bread crumbs. Bake 10 to 15 min or until  bubbling.  Cal: 73 Fat 2g.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmealnew.zip
