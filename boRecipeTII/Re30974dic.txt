Taro Root and Garlic Chive Latka
Taro root, Salt and pepper, Cornstarch, Chopped garlic chives, Chopped ginger, Sour cream, olive oil, Chive points for garnish, Egg white, Salmon roe, 
Peel taro root and grate over large holes of cheese grater. In a mixing  bowl place shredded taro root, garlic ves, ginger, egg white and corn  starch. Mix and season to taste with salt and pepper. In a large skillet  over medium heat, heat the olive oil and sear 1 tablespoon of taro mix,  flatten as a pancake. Cook each side until golden brown. Drain excess oil  on a paper towel. Serve topped with mon roe, sour cream and chive points.
