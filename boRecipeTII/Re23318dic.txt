Tuna Stuffed Bell Peppers
Tomato paste, Dried Onion Flakes, Dried Vegetable Flakes, Tomato juice, Garlic powder, Chunk Style Tuna, Drained, Green Bell Peppers, Tops, 
Mix the tomato juice and the tomato paste. Set 1/3 of the mixture aside.  Mix the remaining tomato mixture with all the other ingredients except the  peppers. Fill the peppers equally with the mixture. Place in the slow  cooker. Pour the reserved tomato mixture over. Cover. Cook on LOW for 8 to        9    hours. Serve hot.
