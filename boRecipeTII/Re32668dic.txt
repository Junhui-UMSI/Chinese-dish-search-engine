Sweet Pinto Pie
Honey or molasses (optional), Sea salt (optional), Baked pie crust, Onions; chopped, Cooked AM Pinto Beans, Cinnamon, crumb or regular, (unseasoned), drained, Nutmeg, Water, 
Saute the onions in the oil over medium heat until very soft and clear.  Watch carefully so they do not brown.  Blend the cooked onions, beans,  water, and seasonings until smooth.  If more sweetness is desired, add the  honey and blend.  Spread in the baked pie crust, and bake 15 to 20 minutes  at 350 F.
