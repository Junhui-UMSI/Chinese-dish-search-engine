Banana-Coconut Bake
Bananas, Lemon juice, Brown Sugar, Shredded coconut, Margarine or butter, Orange juice, 
Heat oven to 375F. Cut bananas crosswise into halves. Cut each half  lengthwise into halves and arrange in greased 9-inch pie plate. Dot with  margarine and drizzle with orange and lemon juices. Sprinkle with brown  sugar and coconut. Bake until coconut is golden, 8 to 10 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdjaxxx.zip
