Banana Bars
Crispy rice cereal crumbs, Unbleached flour, Brown Sugar, Bananas; mashed, Baking Powder, olive oil, Pumpkin pie spice, Egg whites; whipped, Baking soda, 
Preheat oven to 400. Prepare a  9 x 9 x 2" pan with cooking spray; set  aside. In a mixing bowl, combine crispy rice cereal, flour, brown sugar,  baking powder, pumpkin pie spice, and baking soda. In another mixing bowl,  combine egg whites, bananas, and oil. Mix dry ingredients with wet  ingredients just until moistened. Spread mixture into prepared pan.
