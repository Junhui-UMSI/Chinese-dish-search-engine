Potted Shrimps
cayenne pepper, Brown toast, shrimp, Salt to taste, mace, Lemon wedges, butter, black pepper, Half and half cream, 
Melt 1/3 of the butter in a frying pan and toss the  shrimp, spices and seasoning over medium heat for  about 3 minutes.  Add cream, check seasoning and pack  into ramekin dishes.  Pour over the rest of the  butter, after clarifying.  Chill.  Serve with strips  of toast and lemon wedges. Source:  Adapted by Deborah  Kuhnen from The Hot and Spicy Cookbook by Sophie  Hale, ISBN 0-7858-0505-2.
