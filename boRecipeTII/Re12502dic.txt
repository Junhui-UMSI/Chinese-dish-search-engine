Yellow Split Pea Soups
Dried yellow split peas, Croutons, Parsnips, Cumin, Turnip, Mustard powder, Salt, Vegetable stock, Celery, Onion, Carrots, Allspice, Marjoram, Black Pepper, Potatoes, Thyme, 
Rinse split peas, bring to a boil in the stock. Coarsely chop the  vegetables & add to the water as it comes to a boil. Lower heat & simmer  for about 1 1/2 hours. Puree soup in batches until it is quite smooth. Add  spices & herbs & season with salt & pepper. Reheat gently.    "Sundays at Moosewood Restaurant"
