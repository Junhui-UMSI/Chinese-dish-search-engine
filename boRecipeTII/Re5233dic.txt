Pimento Cheese Spread
Egg, Onion, Flour, pimentos, Salt, Burger xwsf96a-, Vinegar, Butter, Sugar, Sweet cream, Cream cheese, Eggs, 
Mix first seven ingredients in a double boiler and cook until mixture  thickens. While mixture is still hot add hard-boiled eggs, cream cheese or  American cheese, onion and pimentos. Blend well and put in jars or other  containers. Cover and store in refrigerator.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #622 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  May 29, 1997
