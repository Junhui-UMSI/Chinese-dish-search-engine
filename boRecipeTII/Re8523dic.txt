Potato/cheese Casserole
Sour cream, Salt, Onion, chopped very fine, Black Pepper, Cream of chicken soup, Potatoes, Grated sharp cheddar, Melted margerine, 
Cook 8 medium potatoes in salted water with a bay leaf. Let cool. Peel and  grate coarsely. Put in greased 9x13 baking pan. Mix together and pour over  potatoes remaining ingredients.    Cover and bake 30-35 minutes. Sprinkle top with: 1/2 cup grated cheese      1/2    cup crushed potato chips Bake a few minutes more.
