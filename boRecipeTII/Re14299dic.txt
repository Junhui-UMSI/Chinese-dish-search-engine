White-Cooked Cubed Pork
Salt, Water, Sherry, Sugar, Pork, 
1. Cut pork in 1-1/2 inch cubes. Place in a heavy pan with sherry, water  and salt. Bring to a boil; then simmer, covered, until tender (about 1  hour).       2. Add sugar and simmer 10 minutes more.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
