Potato-Vegetable Casserole
Chopped scallions or green, Dijon style mustard, Peeled, boiled potatoes,, Plain low-fat yogurt, Muenster cheese, shredded, Each: cooked cauliflower and, Black pepper, 
In small bowl combine first 4 ingredients. In shallow 4 quart flameproof  casserole arrange potatoes, cauliflower and carrots; sprinkle with  scallions and top with yogurt mixture. Broil until cheese is melted and  begins to brown. >From Weight Watchers Fast and Fabulous Cookbook. Contains  356 calories per serving.
