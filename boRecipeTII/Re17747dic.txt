Almond Thatched Cottages
Brown Sugar Sweeties Recipe, 
Beat 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder into dough. Roll out; cut out  using 4" house cutter. Bake 8-10 minutes. Decorate cooled cookies  with plain Royal Icing. Decorate with sliced almonds, sliced gumdrops  for doors & windows & chocolate candies for chimneys.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
