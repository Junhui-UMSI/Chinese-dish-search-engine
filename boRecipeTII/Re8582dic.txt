Potatoes Romanoff
Salt, Margarine, Potatoes -- quartered, Onion -- sliced, Cheddar cheese -- shredded, Black pepper, Fat-free sour cream, Paprika, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a 1 1/2 quart baking dish with cooking spray.  Boil potatoes until tender.  Mash with margarine, sour cream, onions, 3/4  cup cheese, salt and pepper.  Turn mixture into baking dish. Sprinkle with  remaining cheese and paprika.  Bake uncovered, for 40 minutes.
