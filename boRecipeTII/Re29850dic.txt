Tenderloin Pita Pockets
olive oil, Green pepper; diced, Tenderloin tips; cut into, Dried oregano leaves, Onion; sliced and separated, Pita pockets; cut in half, Garlic, 
Combine oil, onion, green pepper, garlic and oregano in a non-stick frying  pan and cook for 2-3 minutes.    Add beef strips, brown all sides and season to taste.    Place 1/4 meat mixture into a pita and top with Sour Cream Dill Sauce or  another sauce of you choice.
