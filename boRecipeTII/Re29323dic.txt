Baked Sweet Potatoes with Onions and Lime Butter
sweet potatoes, Salt, Lime, Butter, Pepper, Red onions, 
In small bowl place butter. Squeeze in juice of lime, and zest from rind .  Mix well. Add salt and pepper to taste. Slice sweet potatoes, without  breaking through the base of the potato. Place onions and pats of butter  between potato slices. Wrap potatoes in foil and place on grill. Cook for  about 15 minutes over medium to low fire. Serve.
