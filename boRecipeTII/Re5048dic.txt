Pickling Marinade
Brown sugar, Rice wine vinegar, White wine, Salt, Cilantro stems, Ginger, grated, Serrano pepper, sliced, Garlic, sliced, Pickling spice, 
Combine everything and simmer 4 minutes. Refriderate overnight. Then  strain. before use over sliced cucumbers or other veggies. When adding the  ginger squeeze the juice into the mixture before adding the ginger to the  liquid.    This was used over cukes, basil, and red onions.
