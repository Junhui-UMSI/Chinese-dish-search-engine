Pecan Carmel Muffins
Pecan halves, BASIC RECIPE, Brown sugar, Soft butter or margarine, 
Mix as for original recipe. Blend 1/2 cup brown sugar with 1/4 cup soft  butter or margarine. Pat mixture evenly in greased muffin cups. Arrange  pecan halves in each. Use basic muffin recipe. bake in moderate oven 375  for about 20 minutes  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #318 by "Robert Ellis"  <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on Dec 7, 1997
