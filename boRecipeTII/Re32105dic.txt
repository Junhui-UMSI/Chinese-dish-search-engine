Barbecued Duck #3
Hot water, Cayenne pepper, Salt to taste, Black Pepper, (4-oz) mushrooms; chopped, Vinegar, Butter, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic, Ducks; well washed, Bottle (14-oz) ketchup, Onion, 
Place ducks breast down in an electric skillet. Mix remaining ingredients,  pour over ducks, cook on very low heat, covered, for 4 hours or until meat  is very tender. Add water if needed. The last hour of cooking add chopped  onion and mushrooms. This makes a good gravy. Yield: 6 servings.    KATHY COBB (MRS. THOMAS)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
