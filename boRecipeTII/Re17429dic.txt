Walnut Raisin Cookies
Walnut pieces,chopped, Packed light brown sugar, Seedless raisins, Sliced dried apples,chopped, Eggs, Butter or margarine,softened, All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Vanilla extract, 
In a small bowl,combine flour and baking soda;set aside.In a medium  bowl,using an electric mixer,cream butter,brown sugar and vanilla.Beat in  eggs,one at a time.Stir in flour mixture.Mix in walnuts,apples and  raisins.Drop by tablespoons onto greased baking sheets.Bake @ 350 degrees  for 12 to 15 minutes.Cool on wire racks.
