Unfried Fried Red Snapper
Soy sauce, Ginger root, Sesame or other vegetable, Red snapper filets, Chopped scallions, 
Boil lightly salted water in sauce pan. Boil each filet until tender, being  careful not to overcook. Drain, arrange on serving plate. Sprinkle with soy  sauce, scallions, ginger. Heat oil carefully until boiling, then pour over  fish so that it sizzles. Serve quickly.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 234 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 09, 1997
