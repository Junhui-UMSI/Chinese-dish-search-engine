From: LIR119@delphi.com    Date: Thu, 22 Feb 1996 18:04:41 -0500 (EST)    Prepare filling by combing all filling ingrdients.  Prepare phyllo by using  2 whole buttered sheets, stack and cut into 6 strips. Use a heaping  taespoon of filling on bottom of each pastry strip, use the triangle method  for folding (like folding up a flag). repeat using phyllo and filling as  instructed. Brush with melted butter and bake from 350 to to 375 15 minutes  or golden. These may be prepared and frozen prior to baking. Note: another  variation is to use 1/3 cup chopped walnuts to the filling. Both above  versions are similar in ingredients but differ a bit.
