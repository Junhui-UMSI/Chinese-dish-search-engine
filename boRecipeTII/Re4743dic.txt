Phyllis Dillers Lemon Refrigerator Cookies
Salt, Shortening, Light brown sugar, Fresh Lemon Juice, Nuts, chopped, Baking soda, Flour, Butter, Egg, Sugar, Lemon peel, grated, 
Cream butter, shortening and sugar until fluffy.  Beat in egg, lemon  juice and lemon peel. Mix well.  In bowl combine remaining  ingredients and stir in cream mixture. Cover and refrigerate for 1  hour.  Remove from refrigerator and form into two 10-inch rolls on  waxed paper. Wrap tightly and refrigerate overnight.  Preheat oven to  400 degrees. Slice rolls into 1/4-inch slices and bake on ungreased  cookie sheets 8 to 10 minutes. Makes about 80 cookies. Formatted    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
