Swedish Sticks
Flour, Vanilla, Pecans, Brown sugar, Jam or jelly, Shortening, Coconut, Egg, 
Cream the shortening and brown sugar.  Add the egg flour and vanilla. Pat  evenly into cookie sheet (moisten fingertips to prevent sticking). Bake  12-15 minutes at 350F. Immediately upon removing from the oven, spread with  the jam.  Sprinkle with the nuts and coconut then dust with 1 tablepoon  confectioners sugar.  Cut into 1"x2 1/2" strips. From Kris Bumford    Recipe By     : Concord Hospital Admitting Cookbook, Concord, NH
