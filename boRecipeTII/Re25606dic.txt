Avocado Soup
Fresh cilantro; chopped, Ripe avocado, Chicken Stock, Garlic, FOR THE SALSA, Cooked corn kernels, olive oil, Juice of 1 lime, Black Pepper, Cooked black beans, Fresh lime juice, Fresh Cilantro, Sherry, Salt, Black Pepper, Sour cream, Finely-diced red bell pepper, Salt, 
Peel and mash the avocado. Place the avocado, chicken stock, lime juice,  cilantro, and sherry into a food processor. Puree until smooth, season with  salt and pepper. Refrigerate for 1 hour.    Meanwhile prepare the salsa. In a bowl combine the corn, black beans,  peppers, garlic, olive oil, and lime juice. Season to taste with salt and  pepper.    Serve the soup in chilled cups topped with the salsa and fresh cilantro.
