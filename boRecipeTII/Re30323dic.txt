Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins
Salt, Chocolate chips, bananas, Eggs, Baking Powder, Walnuts, Brown Sugar, Butter, Vanilla, Ground cinnamon, All-purpose flour, 
Preheat oven to 350=B0. Puree bananas in blender. In medium bowl, beat  pureed bananas, eggs, sugar, butter and vanilla until well blended. In  large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Stir in  chocolate chips and nuts. Make well in center of dry ingredients. Pour in  banana mixture. Mix until just blended. Spoon into well-greased 2 1/2-inch  muffin cups. Bake 25 to 30 minutes. Remove from pan. Makes about 12  muffins.
