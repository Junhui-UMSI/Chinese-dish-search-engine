Pumpkin Apple Muffins
Apple -- peeled/shredded, Raisins, Unbleached flour, Cloves, Cinnamon, Unbleached flour, Salt, Vanilla, Pumpkin, Ginger, Baking Powder, Granulated sugar, Temperature, Baking soda, Egg white -- whipped, Nutmeg, Applesauce -- at room, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare 6 muffin tins with cooking spray and flour.  In a mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt,  cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, ginger, and sugar.  In another mixing bowl,  combine applesauce, egg white, vanilla, pumpkin, apple, and raisins.  Mix  dry ingredients with wet ingredients just until moistened.  Use an ice  cream scoop to fill muffin tins two thirds full.  Bake, 35 minutes or until  browned.
