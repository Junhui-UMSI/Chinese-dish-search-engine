Strawberry French Toast
Text Only, 
For each serving: Toast two slices frozen French toast according to package  directions. Thinly slice strawberries to make 1/3 cup. Fold in 2 TBSP  strawberry or red-raspberry flavored yogurt. Place one slice French toast  on warm plate. Top with strawberry mixture and remaining slice of toast.  Cut in half; serve immediately.
