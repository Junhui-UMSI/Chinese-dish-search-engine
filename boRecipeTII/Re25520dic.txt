Avocado and Mushroom Salad
White mushrooms, olive oil, Basil leaves, Italian bread, Butterhead lettuce, Red bell pepper, Green bell pepper, Avocado, Sherry wine vinegar, Whole grain mustard, 
In a large bowl, mix oil, mustard, vinegar, basil and bell peppers. Set  aside. Wash mushrooms and slice. Add to the dressing mixture and marinate  for 10 minutes. Serve each salad portion on a leaf of lettuce. Fan sliced  avacado on top and serve with two slices of grilled Italian bread.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdjaxxx.zip
