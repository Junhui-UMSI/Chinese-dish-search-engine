Tortellini Soup with Peas
Chicken broth (10 oz.), Grated Parmesan, Frozen cheese tortellini, Pepper, Frozen peas, Garlic, 
In large pot, bring 3 cups water and garlic to boil. Add frozen tortellini;  return to boil.  Reduce heat to medium-high; cook, stirring occasionally,  for 15 minutes.    Add chicken broth, 1 1/4 cups water and peas; return to boil. Reduce heat  to medium; cook for 5 minutes or until pasta is tender but firm. Stir in  pepper. Serve sprinkled with Parmesan. Makes 3 servings. Typed in MMFormat  by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Canadian Living  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Cindy Hartlin" <cjhartlin@email.msn.com>  on May 27, 1998
