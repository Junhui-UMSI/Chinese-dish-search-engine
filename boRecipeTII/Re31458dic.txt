Barbecue Sauce
White vinegar, Dash pepper, Water, Onion, Ketchup, Brown sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, Celery seed, Dash garlic powder, Salt, Prepared horseradish, 
1. Combine all ingredients in a 2-quart glass casserole. Cover with waxed  paper.    2. Cook in microwave oven 3 minutes at High, or until hot; stir once.    Yield: about 1-1/2 cups.    SOURCE: Adventures in microwave cooking
