Quick Spiced Peaches
Whole allspice, Brown sugar, Vinegar, (29-oz) peaches, Sticks cinnamon, Cloves, 
Drain peaches. To syrup, add sugar, vinegar and spices. Boil 5 minutes. Add  peaches and simmer 5 minutes. Chill several hours.
