Pepper Steak for Two
Soy sauce, Sugar, Garlic salt, Tomato; peeled and coarsely, Hot cooked rice, Ground ginger, Cornstarch, Round steak, Green peppers; thinly sliced, Pepper, Beef consomme, Vegetable oil, 
:    Partially freeze steak; slice across grain into 2" x 1/4" strips.  :    Cook steak in oil in a large skillet until brown.  Stir in tomato,  green pepper, soy sauce, sugar, garlic salt, pepper, and ginger; cover and  simmer 15 minutes.  :    Combine cornstarch and consomme; stir into steak mixture. Cover and  simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Serve over rice. Yield: 2  servings.    L. Floyd of Florida in November, 1981 "Southern Living". Typos by Jeff  Pruett.
