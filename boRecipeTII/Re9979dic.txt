Purloo
Thinly sliced okra*, Tomato; seeded and chopped, Salt, Red pepper flakes, Onion, Cooked ham (about 3 oz.), julienned, Bacon, Garlic, Dried thyme, Dried basil, Rice, Green pepper; chopped, 
Cook bacon over medium heat in large skillet until brown; drain fat.  Stir  in onion, pepper, okra and garlic; saute 2 to 3 minutes or until onion is  tender.  Add ham, continue cooking 3 minutes.  Add rice, tomato, thyme,  salt, basil and pepper flakes.  Heat thoroughly; serve as a side dish or a  main dish.    *Thawed, frozen okra or fresh celery may be substituted for fresh okra, if  desired.
