Wild Rice and Mushrooms
Unsalted;defatted chicken, Cloves garlic; finely minced, Low-sodium soy sauce, Wild rice, Black Pepper, Mushrooms, 
Pour broth in saucepan and bring to a boil. Add remaining ingredients Stir  and reduce heat to low. Cover witha tight-fitting lid and cook for 40  minutes, or until rice is tender. If necessary, remove lid and increase  temperature to evaporate any excess liquid. Serves 2.    This is very good with chicken or with fish.
