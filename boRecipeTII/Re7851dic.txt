Portuguese Red Pork
Jalapeno; chopped, Jar Red Pepper; peeled, Pork sirloin chops; (1 inch, Salt, Onion, Fresh ground pepper, Dry white wine, Garlic, olive oil, Parsley, 
1. In a blender of food Processor, smoothly puree jalepenos and red  peppers.    2. Rinse pork and pat dry.    3. Place an 11- 12 inch nonstick frying pan with an ovenproof handle over  med-high heat. Add oil, onion, and garlic and stir often until mixture is  lightly browned, 8 to 10 minutes. Pour Onion mixture from pan.    4. Add Pork chops to pan without crowding and brown evenly, turning once or  twice, 3 to 5 min per side. Add onion, pepper puree, and wine; stir to  blend.    5. Cover frying pan tightly and bake in a 375 F. oven until meat pulls from  bone easially, about 1 1/2 hrs. Uncover for last 10 min and stir to thicken  the sauce.    6. Transfer chops to a platter and pour sauce over them. Sprinkle with  parsley; add salt and pepper to taste.    Recipe by: Sunset Mag.
