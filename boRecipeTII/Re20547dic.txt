Apple Chicken Salad
Celery; thinly sliced, Fryer thighs; * see note, Napoleon Capers; plus 1/2, Granny Smith apples, Shredded parmesan cheese;, Split fryer breast  plus, Green onions; including tops, Best Foods mayonnaise; or to, 
*if using fresh chicken, place breast and thighs in water with 1 stalk  celery, 1/2 onion, salt and pepper.  Simmer 30 minutes.  Cool; strip meat  off and dice. Leaving skins on apples, wash, core and cut into bite-sized  pieces.  Place apple pieces in water with a little lemon juice while  completing recipe, if necessary. Combine all ingredients and mix well.  Place in glass serving bowl, garnish with parmesan cheese, and chill.
