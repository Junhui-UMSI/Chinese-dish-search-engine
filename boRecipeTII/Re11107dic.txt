Raisin and Bran Cookies
Salt, Vanilla, Brown sugar -- packed, Egg whites -- whipped, Nutmeg, Raisins, Margarine -- softened, Baking soda, Unbleached flour, Bran -- unprocessed, Cinnamon, 
Preheat oven at 375.  Prepare baking sheet with cooking spray and flour. In  a mixing bowl, combine margarine, egg whites, and vanilla.  In another  mixing bowl, combine flour, bran, sugar, baking soda, raisins, cinnamon,  nutmeg, and salt.  Mix wet ingredients with dry ingredients just until  until moistened.  Drop dough by heaping teaspoons onto prepared baking  sheet.  Bake for 14 to 16 minutes or firm to the touch.    Recipe By     : Anita A.
