Wild Rice-Turkey Salad
Chopped pecans; for garnish, Wild rice;, Sweet red pepper; Chopped, Turkey; cooked chopped, Water;, Red or white wine vinegar;, Black pepper;, Raisins;, Green Onions, Ground nutmeg;, Celery, Olive oil;, Fresh parsley leaves;, Apple; chopped, 
Cook the rice in the water until tender, about 50 minutes.  Cut the  turkey into bite-size pieces.  Combine all the ingredients ina bowl  and toss. cover and chill until ready to serve.  Sprinkle on the  pecans just before serving.    1/4 recipe = 422 calories 3 lean meat, 1 bread, 1 1/2 fruit, 2 fat  exchanges 42 grams carbohydrate, 24 grams protein, 18 grams fat 344 mg  sodium, 503 mg potassium, 54 mg cholesterol    Source:  Am. Diabetes Assoc. Holiday Cookbook by Betty Wedman, 1986  Shared but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier, Nov 93    My note:  Id probably make this with regular rice, vegetable oil,  regular vinegar (or a lower calorie dressing), 2 tsp dry parsley and  chopped almonds.
