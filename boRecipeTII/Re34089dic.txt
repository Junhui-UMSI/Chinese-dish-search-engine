Bassa Bassa Chicken
Chicken Drumsticks, Jar; (6 oz) Bassa Bassa, Seasoned salt, Chicken Thighs, 
http://www.ronainc.com/Recipes.htm    SPICY CARIBBEAN RECIPES    Wash & clean chicken, dry and pat generously with seasoning salt Bake  chicken for 30 minutes in 350 degrees oven Baste each piece with Bassa  Bassa Sauce and cook another 15 minutes    Savor the flavor of Barbados!!!
