Quick and Easy Fruit Dip
Cranberry-orange relish, Pear, sliced, Ground ginger, Nutmeg, Nonfat vanilla yogurt, Apple, sliced, 
Mix together yogurt, relish, and spices. Chill. Serve with fruit slices.    Per serving: 310 Calories; 1g Fat (3% calories from fat); 6g Protein; 73g  Carbohydrate; 2mg Cholesterol; 100mg Sodium    Recipe by: BH&G - 1/90    Posted to Digest eat-lf.v097.n037 by <ebburtis@ix.netcom.com> on Feb 7, 97.
