Apple Jelly Spread
Granulated gelatin, Artificial sweetener to subs, Lemon juice, Unsweetened apple juice, Few drops red food color, Water, Or 8 small whole cloves, 
Recipe by: Tampa Bay Online Combine fruit juices and cloves in heavy  saucepan. Bring to a boil; simmer gently 10 minutes. Meanwhile, soak  gelatin in cold water. Remove juices from heat; discard cloves; add gelatin  and artificial sweetener and mix well. Add about 3 drops red food color;  mix well. Pour carefully into two hot, clean half-pint jars. Cover lightly  until cooled. Cover tightly and refrigerate.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on May 16,  1998
