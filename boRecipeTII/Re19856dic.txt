Apple and Grape Salad
Walnuts, Sour cream, Celery, Lemon juice, Apples, Grapes, Plain yogurt, 
In large bowl, combine apples, grapes, celery, walnuts and lemon juice. Mix  yogurt and sour cream. Mix lightly into fruit mix. Refrigerate. If desired,  serve on lettuce lined plates.
