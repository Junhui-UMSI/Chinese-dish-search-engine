Picante Sauce
Salt, Jalapeno peppers, chopd fine, Can Whole Tomatoes, Garlic powder, Medium onion, chopped fine, Apple cider vinegar, 
Combine all ingredients in saucepan.  Bring to boil. Lower heat and simmer  uncovered 15 minutes.  Chop tomatoes while cooking.    Number of peppers may be varied to increase flavorful "heat".    Serve chilled with Doritos or similar tortilla chips.    Enjoy! - Jeff Duke
