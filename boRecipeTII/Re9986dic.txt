Purple Pesto
olive oil, Garlic, Sun dried tomatoes, Asiago cheese, Opal or Purple Ruffle basil, Pine nuts or walnuts, 
Combine all ingredients except oil in blender or food processor. Slowly add  oil.  Blend to desired consistency and toss on freshly cooked pasta.    (This pesto variation makes a great sauce with sauteed strips of sweet  Italian peppers and linguine.)    From: The Cooks Garden catalog - Spring/Summer 1989 - page 1)
