Perky Party Punch
Packet Kool-ade (any color, Pineapple juice, Ginger ale, (small) frozen lemonade, Sugar to taste, (large) frozen orange juice, Ice, 
Reconstitute orange juice and lemonade. Add pineapple juice and Kool-Aid.  Mix. Sweeten to taste. Just before serving add ginger ale and ice. Makes 40  cups.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
