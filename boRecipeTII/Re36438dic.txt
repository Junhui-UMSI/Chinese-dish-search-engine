Beer Cheese Spread
Tabasco sauce, Cheddar cheese; shredded &, Worcestershire Sauce, Beer, Garlic powder, Dry mustard, Monterey Jack cheese;, Salt, 
Combine all ingredients in a heavy-duty, zip-top blastic bag; push air out  of bag, and seal.    Knead bag with hands until mixture is blended and of spreading consistency.  Serve with bagel chips.    Recipe by: Southern Living    Posted to recipelu-digest by Nesb2@aol.com on Feb 23, 1998
