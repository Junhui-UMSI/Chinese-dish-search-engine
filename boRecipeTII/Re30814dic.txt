Tangy Blue Cheese Sauce
Blue Cheese; Crumbled, 3/4, Green Onion W/Top; Sliced, Sour Cream, Salt, milk, Bottled Hot Pepper Sauce, 
Put all of the ingredients into a blender container and blend until the  onion is finely chopped.  Heat through, but do not boil.  Serve over  vegetables.  NOTE: Refrigerate and reheat any unused sauce.    Makes 1 1/4 cups.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/blend1.zip
