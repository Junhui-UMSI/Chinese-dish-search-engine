Tomato and Peach Salad
Peaches -- washed, Lemon juice, Taste, olive oil, Almonds -- sliced, Fresh basil leaves, Ground black pepper -- to, Sugar, Tomatoes -- washed, 
In a small skillet combine the almonds and sugar. Toast over medium-low  heat until the almonds are golden and sugar has started to caramelize. Stir  often and watch carefully. Remove from heat and set aside. In a small jar  with lid, combine lemon juice, olive oil and pepper. Shake well. Core the  tomatoes and cut into 1/2-inch thick wedges. Cut the peaches into wedges  about the same thickness. Arrange the tomatoes and peaches in alternating  pattern on a serving platter. Drizzle with dressing and sprinkle with the  almonds.  Garnish with basil leaves.    Recipe By     : Seattle Times-posted by Mike Key
