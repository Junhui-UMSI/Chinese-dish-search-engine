Bean Stew
Northern beans, Hominy, Carrot, Pinto beans*, Salt to taste, Garlic powder; oregano,, Fresh ground pepper, Beef broth, Lentils, Onion; slice, Green chilies; chopped, 
Date: 27 Apr 96 22:23:24 EDT    From: "Lisabeth Crawford (Pooh)" <104105.1416@CompuServe.COM>  * Makes 1 1/2 cup of soup for ONE, again!!!!! No soup for BERT!!!    Boil beans and lentils in beef broth for 10 minutes, covered. Allow to  stand 1 to 2 hours or overnight. Place softened beans and remaining  ingredients in baking dish. Bake aat 350F for 45 minutes to 1 hour or until  ingredients are tender.
