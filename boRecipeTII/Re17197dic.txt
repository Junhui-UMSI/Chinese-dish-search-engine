Vodka Fruit Punch
Lemon juice, Vodka, Orange sherbet, Sugar, orange juice, Gingerale, maraschino cherries, Pineapple juice, 
Mix the above four ingredients in punch bowl (this part can be made ahead  and kept in refrigerator). When ready to serve add gingerale, cherries and  vodka. Float one pint orange sherbet on top.
