American Garlic and Parsley Bread #2
Active dry yeast, Salt, Active dry yeast, Sugar, Oat bran; ** see note, Garlic, FOR 1LARGE LOAF, Salt, Loaf:, Bread flour, Water; +1 tsp, Sugar, Bread flour, Wheat germ; * see note, Vegetable oil, Fresh parsley, Oat bran; ** see note, Water, Wheat germ; * see note, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Fresh parsley, 
Add all ingredients in the order suggested by your bread machine manual and  process on the basic bread cycle according to the manufacturers  directions. Let the loaf cool before slicing. Serve plain or toasted.    NOTES : *Note:  I omitted the wheat germ. **Note: Original recipe calls for  wheat bran.
