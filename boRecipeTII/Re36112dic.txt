Beef Sausage
Lean ground beef, Garlic salt, Morton Tender Quick Salt, Hickory Smoke Salt, Coarse Ground Pepper, 
In a large bowl combine ground beef, salts and pepper. Mix well, cover and  refrigerate.  On second day mix again. Refrigerate. On third day mix again.  Refrigerate.  On fourth day shape into 5 rolls, about 1-1/2" X 10" Spray  broiler rack with Pam.  Lay rolls on rack, place on lower oven rack. Cook  at 150F for 8 to 10 hours.
