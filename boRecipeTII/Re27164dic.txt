Times Square Special
Tomato slices, Pumpernickel bread, Green bell pepper; diced, Eggs, Pickles, Ripe olives, Bermuda onion; diced, Butter, Smoked salmon; diced, GARNISHES, Lettuce, Bermuda onion; slices, 
Saute onion and peppers in butter for about 5 minutes until onions are  golden brown and peppers cooked. Add diced salmon and beaten eggs and fry  lightly to make an omelet.    Place omelet on bread and eat as an open-faced sandwich. Decorate the plate  with lettuce, tomato slices, pickles, olives and onion. Serve.    NOTES : Recipe from the New York Sheraton Hotel, New York, New York    Recipe by: Sheraton World Cookbook    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by Roberta Banghart <bobbi744@sojourn.com> on  Jan 10, 1998
