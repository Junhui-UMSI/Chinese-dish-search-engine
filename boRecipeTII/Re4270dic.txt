Pernil Asado Estilo Chino-Cubano
Five-spice powder, Mashed cooked black beans, Garlic, Sugar, Soy sauce, Hoisin sauce, Sherry, pork, 
one-quarter cup honey, mixed with one-quarter cup boiling water, for  basting Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Rinse pork and pat dry. Place on rack  in a roasting pan and fill bottom of pan with 1 inch of water. In a mixing  bowl, combine soy sauce, sherry, hoisin sauce, black beans, five-spice  powder, garlic and sugar. Mix well. Coat pork loin with mixture, rubbing  meat all over until the mixture is used up. Roast pork 15 minutes, then  reduce heat to 325 degrees and cook 1 to 1 and one-half hours, basting  every 15 minutes with honey and water mixture. When done, remove from oven  and let cool slightly. Transfer to serving platter. Serves 6-8. Fresh tuna  is best in this dish. When grilling, look for firm tuna that will hold  together when turned, or use a fish grilling basket.
