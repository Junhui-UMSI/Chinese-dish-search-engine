Zucchini Cake
zucchini, Salt, Almond extract, Baking Powder, Cloves, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Orange juice, Flour, Orange icing, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Eggs, 
In large bowl mix flour, baking powder, soda, salt, cinnamon and cloves.  Set aside. With wisk, beat eggs. Stir in oil, sugar, juice, extract and  zucchini. Mix well. Add in flour mixture, stirring just to moisten. Pour  into greased pan or pans. Bake in preheated 350F oven.    Tube cake = 55-60 minutes 1 - 15x9x2 pan = 35-40 minutes 4 loaf pans, 6x3  foil loaf pans = 40 minutes 24 cupcakes = 20-25 minutes    ORANGE ICING: In small bowl beat together until blended: 2 T softened  butter, 3 cups confectioner sugar, 1/4 c OJ, and 1 tsp lemon juice.
