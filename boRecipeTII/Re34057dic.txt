Basmati Chaaval
Water, Salt, Unsalted butter, Basmati rice, 
Pick the rice over and put in a bowl.  Wash in several changes of water.  Drain.   Pour 3 times the water you have rice over the rice and let soak  for 30 minutes hour.  Drain thoroughly.  Combine the rice, salt, butter and the water in a pot.  Bring to a boil.  Cover  with a tight fitting lid,  turn heat to very low and cook for  20  minutes.   Lift  the lid,  mix gently but quickly with a fork and  cover  again.  Cook for 5-10 minutes until tender.    From the TWSU BBS, 316-689-3779, C.
