Tomato Relish
Grated rind 1 lemon, Lemon juice, Bottle fruit pectin, Crushed and peeled tomatoes, Sugar, 
Boil tomatoes 10 minutes in an uncovered kettle.  Stir occasionally. Add  lemon juice and grated lemon rind. Heat rapidly to full rolling boil. Stir  constantly before and while boiling. Boil hard 2 minutes. Remove from fire.  Stir in fruit pectin. Skim. This recipe requires about 2 1/2 pounds  tomatoes. The Household Searchlight
