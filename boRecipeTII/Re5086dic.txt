Pico De Gallo 1
Tomatoes; drained, chopped,, Jalapeno chile peppers;, Fresh cilantro leaves;, Onions; finely chopped, 
Mix all ingredients in large bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper.  (Can be prepared 3 hours ahead. Cover and let stand at room temperature.)
