Recipe Secrets Golden Lemon Chicken
Butter, Soup mix, Boneless chicken breasts, Hot cooked rice, Flour, Water, Beaten egg, Lemon slices, Env Golden Herb with Lemon, 
Dip chicken in egg, then flour. In 12" skillet, brown chicken in  butter. Add soup mix blended with water; place lemon on chicken.  Bring to boil; simmer covered 10 minutes or until chicken is done.  Serve over rice.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
