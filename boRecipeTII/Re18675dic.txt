Amish Rhubarb Jam
Sugar, Stawberry Jello, Rhubarb, 
Mix rhubarb and sugar together and let stand overnight.  In morning, cook  mixture until rhubarb is soft.  Remove from heat and stir in jello until it  is dissolved.  Jar andseal.
