Alisas Mexican Rice
Jalepenos; minced (with or, Tomatoes, Clove garlic; mashed with a, -(up to), (small) chiles, Plenty of black pepper, Onion; chopped fine, Bell pepper; chopped, olive oil, Rice, 
Saute the rice, onion & garlic in a little oil until the onion is soft. Put  the tomatoes in a big measuring cup and squish them into pieces.  You need  about 3-1/2 cups of liquid, so add water to the tomatoes til you get this  amount.  Bring to a boil, add the bell pepper, hot pepper & chiles. Cover &  cook 15-20 minutes.  Taste for black pepper.    NORVELLE@UGA.CC.UGA.EDU    (ALISA NORVELLE)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
