Swanson Chicken and Pasta Primavera
Carrots; sliced, Grated Parmesan cheese, Garlic powder, Cooked corkscrew or elbow, milk, Broccoli flowerets, Pepper, Swanson Premium Chunk White, 
1. In saucepan mix soup, milk, cheese, garlic powder, pepper, broccoli and  carrots.  Over medium heat, heat to a boil.    2. Cover and cook over low heat 10 min. or until vegetables are    tender-crisp, stirring occasionally.  Stir in macaroni and chicken and heat  through.  Serves 4    Prep time: 10 min.    Cook time: 20 min.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #057 by "srussell" <srussell@unix.ieway.com>  on Feb 24, 1997.
