Artichoke Fritters
Oil, peanut, Eggs, Artichoke hearts, Baking Powder, Green onions, salt and pepper, Lemon Peel, Flour, Cornstarch, 
Place artichoke hearts in a large bowl and stir in egg yolks and baking  powder.  Add green onion.  Fold in lemon peel. Mix in flour, salt and  pepper. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites and cornstarch together until  peaks form. Fold egg whites into artichoke mixture. Heat oil to 360  degrees.  With a tablespoon, drop half dollar sized dollops of fritter  batter into oil. Fry until golden brown, maintaining oil at 360 degrees.  Remove fritters with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
