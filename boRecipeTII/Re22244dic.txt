Turkey Stroganoff
Paprika, Dill weed, Fresh sliced mushrooms, Beef boullion granules, Breast, 3 cloves garlic, minced, Skim milk, Onion, sliced, - 3/4 lb ground turkey, Flour, 
saute ground turkey with onion and garlic.  add paprika, dill weed and  mushrooms. mix milk, flour, and boullion. add to meat mixture. simmer 5-10  minutes untill mushrooms and onion are cooked and mixture is thickened. add  salt and pepper to taste. stir frequently. just before serving, stir in 4  oz non-fat sour cream, serve over eggless noodles or pasta.
