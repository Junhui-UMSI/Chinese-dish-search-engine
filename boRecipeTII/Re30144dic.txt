In medium bowl beat cream and liqueur until soft peaks form. Place bottom  layer of pound cake on serving platter. Spread 1/3 of cream mixture on  layer; top with half the banana slices. Repeat with remaining layers,  ending with whipped cream. Sprinkle with almonds.
