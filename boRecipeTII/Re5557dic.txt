Pineapple Party Cake
Baking Powder, milk, (21 oz) pineapple pie, Butter, Orange juice, Grated orange ring, Whipping cream; whipped, Grated orange rind, Baking soda, Orange juice, Sugar, Flour, Eggs, Vanilla, Salt, Sugar, 
Workbasket Recipes: This issue (December 72) has so many intriguing  recipes.    contributed by Mrs CHarles Enocksen    Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time,  beating thoroughly after each addition. Add vanilla. Sift flour with baking  powder, salt and soda; add to creamed mixture in thirds alternately with  milk and 1/4 cup orange juice, beating until smooth after each addition.  Fold in 2 tbs rind. bake in 3 nine-inch greased wax paper lined pans at 350  for 35 minutes. Turn out on racks; cool.    FILLING: Combine pineapple filling, 3 tbs orange juice and 1 tsp rind.  Spread between layers and on top. Fold sugar into whipped cream for  garnish.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Cecilia <elric01@IMPERIUM.NET> on Sep 6, 1997
