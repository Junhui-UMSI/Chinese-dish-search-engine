Venison
Stalks celery, Sliced carrots, Venison shoulder, Red wine, Salt and pepper, Turnips (halved), Peeled potatoes, Horseradish, Flour and shortening, Whole onions, Tomato juice, 
Roll the shoulder in seasoned flour (salt and pepper to taste) and place in  a hot kettle, browning it on all sides in hot shortening. Add horseradish,  tomato juice, and red wine. Cover and cook slowly for 3 hours, adding water  to prevent burning. About 45 minutes before end of cooking time, add  onions, clery, carrots, potatoes, turnips and salt. Thicken the juice with  flour for good gravy.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 16 November 96    Date:    Sun, 17 Nov 1996 16:12:02 -0600    From:    Pam and KerryAnn Cobb <priss@AMARANTH.COM>
