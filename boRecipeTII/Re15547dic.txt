7 Up Pound Cake
Vanilla, Butter, Eggs, 7-up, Flour, Lemon juice, Sugar, 
From:    rayna knighton <rayna@ICONZ.CO.NZ>    Date:    Wed, 10 Jul 1996 07:16:22 +1200  Preheat oven to 350F. In large bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, one  at a time, beating well after each. Add flour and 7-Up alternately, mixing  well after each addition. Fold in lemon juice and vanilla. Bake in a  greased and floured 10-inch tube pan for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Cool on  wire rack and remove from pan. Makes 8 to 10 servings.    EAT-L Digest  8 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
