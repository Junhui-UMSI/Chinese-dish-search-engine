Westinghouse Waffles
Flour, Butter, Baking Powder, Salt, Sugar, milk, Eggs, 
Sift dry ingredients together. Beat egg yolks, add with milk to dry  ingredients. Beat until batter is smooth. Add melted butter and folk in  stiffly beaten egg whites. Preheat iron and bake 3 or 4 minutes. This  recipes makes 6 waffles.    Source: Mrs. E. A.
