Peanut Butter Muffins
Sea salt (optional), AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil, AM Whole Wheat Pastry Flour, Non-alum baking powder, AM Crunchy Peanut Butter, Unsulphured molasses, Fresh milk or water, 
Stir flour, baking powder, and salt together.  Cut in peanut butter and  oil with fork or pastry blender until the mixture is like small grains.  Add the milk and molasses; stir just until well mixed, but do not beat.  Fill oiled muffin tins or muffin cups 2/3 full.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes at      350    F.
