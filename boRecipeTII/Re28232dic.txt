Baked Fish with Orange-Chili Marinade
olive oil, Chipotle pepper; chopped, Cloves garlic; peeled and, Ground black pepper; to, Fresh orange juice, Fresh cilantro leaves;, Fish fillets; (thick), 
Combine the orange juice, cilantro, olive oil, garlic and chipotle. Place  the fish in a glass dish and cover with the marinade. Refrigerate 1 hour.  Remove the fish from the marinade and place on a rack set into a baking pan  or on a broiling pan. Bake in a preheated 450-degree oven 12 minutes per  inch of thickness.    Recipe By     : W.
