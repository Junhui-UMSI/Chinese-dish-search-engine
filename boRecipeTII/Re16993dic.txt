Viennese Coffee Mix
Ground allspice, Dry nondairy  creamer, Gound nutmeg, Sugar, Instant coffee, Cinnamon, Ground cloves, 
Combine and put in a festive airtight container.  To mix, add 4 heaping  teaspoons to 8 oz. boiling water.    *gift idea-a tin of Vinnese Coffee Mix can be packaged with some Vinnese  cookies.
