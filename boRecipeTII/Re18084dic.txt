Veggie Pie
milk, Cream of potato soup,, Pepper, Pie crusts, Veg all, drained, Thyme, 
Mix soup, Veg-all, milk, thyme and pepper together. Line deep dish pie  plate with one crust. Fill with vegetable filling. Place second crust on  top. Bake until top crust is golden brown.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 13 Feb 97 by Maggie Workman  <workman@CORIOLIS.ATMS.PURDUE.EDU> on Feb 14, 1997.
