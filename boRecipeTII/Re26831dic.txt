Baked Barbecued Hot Dogs
Allspice, Stalk Celery, Lemon juice, Paprika, Cinnamon, Tomato Sauce; <OR>, Vinegar, Sugar, Chili Sauce, Tomato Puree, Salt and pepper, Chopped Onions; as desired, 
Chop onions and celery and brown in cooking oil. Add remaining ingredients  and cook for about 20 minutes. Pour over hot dogs in bread pan., and cook  one hour in oven at 350?. Makes enough sauce for 20 hot dogs.    Yield 20 servings.    Source: Recipes Out of This World Women of St. Agnes Catholic Church  Charleston, WV 1969    billspa@icanect.net    Recipe by: Eileen Pettigrew    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Bill Spalding <billspa@ICANECT.NET> on Feb 28,  1998
