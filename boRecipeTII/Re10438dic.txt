Quick Corny Chili Rice Dinner
Water, Tomato sauce, PATTI - VDRJ67A-----, Minute rice, Onion, Green pepper, Cheddar cheese, Chili powder, Ground beef, Frozen whole kernel corn, 
Brown ground beef and onion in skillet. Add spices, corn, green pepper,  tomato sauce and water. Cover and bring to a full boil, stirring  occasionally. Stir in rice; reduce heat, cover and simmer for 5 minutes.  Sprinkle with cheese.
