Tuna Casserole #1
(small) noodles; cooked,, milk, (small) sliced mushrooms, Chopped pimento, Frozen green peas; cooked, Potato sticks, Hard-boiled eggs; sliced, (6-oz) solid pack tuna, Flour, Stick margarine, 
Make cream sauce by melting margarine over low heat & blending in flour.  Add milk & cook until slightly thick & smooth. Remove from heat & stir in  tuna, green peas, mushrooms, egg & pimento. Fold in well-drained noodles.  Turn into buttered 2-quart casserole. Bake in moderate (350 degree) oven  about 30 minutes. Scatter potato sticks over top about 10 minutes before  taking from oven. Yields 4-6 servings.    VELDA ALEXANDER    MEMPHIS, TN    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
