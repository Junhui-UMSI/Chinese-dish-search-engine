Balkan Cold Cucumber Salad
Sea salt, Dijon mustard, Plain nonfat yogurt, Garnish, Honey, Spinach, Fresh dill sprigs -- for, Thinly sliced cucumbers --, Hard-boiled eggs, Apple cider vinegar, Peeled and seeded, 
1. Cut cucumber slices into half-moon shapes. Place cucumbers in a large  bowl with sea salt. Toss well. Let marinate at room temperature for 30  minutes. Rinse well to remove salt.    2. Slice eggs in half. Separate yolks and whites. Chop whites and set  aside. Mash egg yolks with mustard, yogurt, honey, and vinegar.    3. Toss together cucumbers, egg whites, and egg-yolk mixture. Wash spinach  and remove stems. Arrange on 4 salad plates. Spoon cucumber salad on top.  Garnish with dill sprigs. Serve at room temperature.
