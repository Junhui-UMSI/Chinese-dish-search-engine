Peanut Sauce - Nam Jim Satay
Red Curry Paste, Coconut milk, Sugar, Ground Roasted Peanuts, Fish Sauce (Nam Pla), 
This richly flavored sauce is usually served with satays.  ~------------------------------------------------------ ~-----------------  Combine all the ingredients in a medium saucepan and simmer for 15 minutes,  stirring constantly. Makes 2-1/2 cups.
