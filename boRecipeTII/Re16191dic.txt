Wassail
Whole box whole cloves, Whole cans apple juice; (48, Whole can pineapple juice;, Whole cinnamon sticks, Whole family size tea bags, Sugar, 
Simmer all ingredients 10 minutes; add tea. For a stronger wassail simmer  with tea bags. Rum can be added to taste. Editors Note: Wassail can be  simmered and kept warm in crock-pot on low.
