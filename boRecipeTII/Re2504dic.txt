Peach-Strawberry Pie
Baked 9" Pet Ritz deep dish, Carton Cool Whip, thawed, Vanilla ice cream, Boiling water, (6 oz.) lemon Jello (sugar, Lite peaches, chopped, Strawberries, chopped, 
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Add ice cream by spoonfuls. Stir until  melted and smooth. Blend in whipped topping and fruit. Chill until mixture  will mound. Spoon into crust. Chill. Garnish with strawberries. A very lite  dessert. Posted to Bakery Shoppe Digest148 by Chris & Karen  <sanburns@m2.sprynet.com> on Jul 06, 1997
