7 Up Cake--Charlotte Collins
Cubes of butter, Sugar, Eggs, Vanilla, Flour, 7 UP, 
Mix all ingredients together well and put into angel food cake pan, or a  well greased and floured Bundt pan. Bake 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours at 325.    Recipe by: CHARLOTTE COLLINS    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by The Meades <kmeade@idsonline.com> on Feb 23,  1998
