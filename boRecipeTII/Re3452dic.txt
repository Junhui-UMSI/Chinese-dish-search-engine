Pecan Cream Cheese Pie
vanilla, pastry shell, Eggs, sugar, Corn syrup, Philly cream cheese, pecans, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees    Soften the cream cheese at room temp.  Cream together in a bowl with 1 egg,  4 tabsp sugar and 1 teasp vanilla.  Keep stirring and beating until well  creamed with no lumps.  In another bowl, beat together the corn syrup, 1  teasp vanilla, 3 eggs and 2 tabsp sugar.    Pour the cream cheese mix into the pie shell.  Level off and spread the  chopped pecans on top.  Pour the corn syrup mixture over the pecans. Bake  for 35 minutes.
