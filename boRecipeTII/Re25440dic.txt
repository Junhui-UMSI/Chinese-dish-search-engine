Tortilla Soup #3
Sour cream; for garnish, (32-oz) tomatoes, Chicken; cooked, Tomato puree, Chicken broth, Garlic, Limes, Avocado, Vegetable oil, Worcestershire Sauce, Picante sauce, Corn tortillas, Cilantro; (optional), Celery, Cheese, Onion, 
Date: Sun, 18 Feb 1996 18:26:21 -0500    From: gourmet@mail.utexas.edu (Cindy)    Recipe By: Flavors of Simon David, March 1991    Saute onion, celery and garlic in oil until tender.  Add all the remaining  ingredients except the tortillas, cheese, and cilantro.  Simmer 30 minutes.  Cut tortillas into strips and simmer 2 minutes.  Ladle into bowls and top  with cheese.
