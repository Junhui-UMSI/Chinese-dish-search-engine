Potato Bread 2
Whole wheat flour, Bread flour, Potato water, Yeast, Vegetable oil, Salt, Mashed Potatoes, Sugar, MEDIUM LOAF, 
This recipe is from Donna Germans Book II. She states that you may  substitute bread flour for the whole wheat flour. Also; Dick Bequette  (PKMP64A) posted privately that he cut back the whole wheat flour by 1 cup  (and added bread flour) plus added 1/3 cup of sunflower seeds. He said it  was terrific....
