Adobo Sauce [If I Understand My Chiles]

From: mariusj@best.com (Marius Johnston)    Date: Wed, 31 Jul 1996 21:29:22 -0700  Cover chiles with hot water, let sit for 15 min. Combine chiles, and 1/4 to  1/2 cup of the water they were soaking in, onion, garlic, oregano and cumin  in a blender and puree. Saute the mixture in oil for 5 min add vinegar and  sugar and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, simmer until thick about 5-8 min.
