West Indian Rice and Peas with Tempeh
Fennel, Vegetable oil, Tempeh, Scallions, Unsweetened coconut, Vegetable oil, Garlic, Chile, Water, brown rice, uncooked, Dried black eyed peas, Onion, Bay Leaves, Red bell pepper, 
Saute rice & coconut in the 2 1/2 tablespoons oil for 2-3 minutes, stirring  constantly.  Add the water & cinnamon stick. Cover the pot & bring it to a  rapid boil. Do not peek at the rice, but when the steam starts escaping,  turn the heat down.  Simmer for 40 minutes. Meanwhile, cook the black eyed  peas with the bay leaf in salted, boiling water till tender (only takes  abot 20-25 minutes).  Drain them & remove the bay leaves. Keep warm till  the rice & tempeh are ready. Saute the garlic & onion with the 1/4 cup of  oil till the onions soften. Stir in chile & bell pepper. Saute for 2  minutes.  Add fennel, tempeh, salt & pepper. Lower heat, but stir  frequently til tempeh is crisp & golden. Combine everything, mixing  together well.
