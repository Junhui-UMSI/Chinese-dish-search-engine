Whole Wheat-Banana Pancakes
All-purpose flour, Honey, Whole wheat flour, Butter, Sugar, Baking soda, Egg, Baking Powder, Milk plus whatever more you, Salt, Bananas (older bananas seem, 
I mix all the dry ingredients, then add the egg, honey, butter, and milk.  Finally, I blend in the bananas. I use a cordless beater, which is not  capable of going too fast. Makes about 20 4" pancakes, I guess. I usually  make "silver dollars" for my kids, so I dont count em.     Ive also substituted apples (2), and a tsp of cinnamon for bananas, with  good results.
