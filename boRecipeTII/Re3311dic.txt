Pears and Blue Cheese
Onion rings, Salt, Lettuce, Sour cream, Basil, fresh leaves, Pears,, French champagne, glasses of, TO SERVE, Pepper, Lemon,, Blue vein cheese, Paprika, 
Peel, halve and core the pears and marinate in the 2 glasses of wine, and  lemon juice for 30 minutes.    Mash the blue vein cheese, sour cream and season with salt and pepper.    Drain the pears, fill centres with cheese mixture, dust with paprika.    Serve on a bed of lettuce leaves with fresh basil and onion rings.    from THE EPICUREAN Jan/Feb 1982 typed by KEVIN JCJD SYMONS  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by muddy@ibm.net on May 27, 1998
