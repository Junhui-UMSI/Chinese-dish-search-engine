Applesauce Cake (Obl Dunkes Kucka)
Flour, Nutmeg, Applesauce, unsweetened, Butter, Cinnamon, Salt, Cloves, Raisins, Brown sugar, light, Baking soda, 
Mix butter, sugar, salt and spices. Add flour and applesauce in which soda  has been dissolved. Add the raisins. Pour into an oblong, well-greased pan  and bake at 350-F about 35 minutes. Source: Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book -  Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
