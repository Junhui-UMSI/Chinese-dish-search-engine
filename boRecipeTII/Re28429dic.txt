Baked Mushroom Chicken
Chicken breasts with bone, Parmesan cheese, Pancake mix, Onion soup mix, Cream of mushroom soup, Mushrooms, Milk, 
Recipe By: Uncle Don Preparation Time: 1:25    Mix slat, pepper and pancake mix. Shake chicken in mix and place in baking  dish 9x13. Place mushrooms over chicken.    Mix soups and milk and pour over chicken and mushrooms.    Cover tightly with foil. Bake 1 hour in 375 oven. Then uncover and sprinkle  with parmesan cheese. Bake uncovered 15 min. Serve.    Per serving: 76 Calories; 2g Fat (20% calories from fat); 3g Protein; 13g  Carbohydrate; 1mg Cholesterol; 683mg Sodium Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest  V1 #208 by Kris Viles <vilesk@magicnet.net> on Aug 29, 1997
