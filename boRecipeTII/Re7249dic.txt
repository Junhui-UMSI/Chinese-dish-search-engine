Pork Burritos
Green Bell Pepper; diced, Vegetable oil, Cider vinegar, Pork; cut in 1-in. cubes, Green Chilies; chopped (4, Onion, Red Bell Pepper; diced, Fresh Jalapeno Pepper;, Cumin, Garlic, Oregano, Salt, 
* or 3 canned jalapeno peppers.    Brown pork in oil.  Add rest of ingredients EXCEPT GREEN CHILIES and cook  for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.  Add green chilies before serving.
