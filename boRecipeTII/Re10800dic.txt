Quickie Jerk Pork
Chopped garlic; to taste, Chopped hot pepper; to taste, Red pepper flakes; to taste, Salt, Vinegar, Boned leg of pork, Chopped onion; to taste, 
COMBINE ALL THE INGREDIENTS except the pork in a non-reactive pan. Add the  pork and marinate for 4 days, turning frequently. Keep covered in the  refrigerator. Take out and wipe dry. Cook pork on a grid over burning coals  to get a smokey flavor.
