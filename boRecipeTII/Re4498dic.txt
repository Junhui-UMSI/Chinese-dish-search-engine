Pesto Shrimp
Basil pesto, Lemon juice, Large raw shrimp; shelled,, Dry white wine, 
1.  In large skillet over med-high, stir together pesto, wine and lemon  juice just until mixture starts to bubble.  Add shrimp and cook 2-3 mins  just until shrimp turns pink.    2.  Serve immediately.
