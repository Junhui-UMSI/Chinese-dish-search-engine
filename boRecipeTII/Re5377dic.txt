Pineapple Breakfast Shake
Banana, Vanilla extract, Skim milk, Pineapple Chunks *  , 
* Pineapple chunks in unsweetened juice.  ~-------------------------------------------------------------------------  Drain pineapple, reserving 3/4 cup juice.  Measure 1/2 cup pineapple chunks  and save the remainder for another use.  Place all ingredients in a blender  bowl and process until smooth.  Pour into glasses and serve..  85 calories  per serving.  From: Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
