Puppy Chow #2
Butter, Bag (12-oz) milk chocolate, Box powdered sugar, Peanut butter, Box (large) Crispix, 
Date: Fri, 26 Apr 1996 17:09:03 -0600    From: Shelly Crawford <Crawfs@Brain.Missouri.Edu>  Here is a recipe the whole family loves.  I have made cloth bags decorated  with paw prints etc. and have given as this snack as gifts.  Hope you  enjoy!    In a large dutch oven melt butter, chocolate, and peanut butter together  over medium heat stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.  Add Crispix.  spoon to coat. Place powdered sugar in a brown paper bag and pour in  Crispix, shake to coat. Eat and enjoy.
