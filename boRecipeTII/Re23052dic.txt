True Blueberry Jam
Blueberries; rinsed and, Sugar, Freshly grated lemon zest, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
In a large pan cook the berries over high heat, stirring constantly with a  wooden spoon and crushing them with the back of the spoon, for 5 minutes.  Add the sugar, zest and juice and cook the mixture over a low heat,  stirring, until the sugar is dissolved. Increase the heat to moderate and  boil the jam, skimming frequently, for 15 minutes, or until the jellying  point is reached. Remove the pan from the heat and let the jam stand for 10  minutes. Ladle the jam into warm sterlized jars and seal.    Makes 1 1/2 quarts    Recipe by: RED FLA    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Suzy Wert <SuzyWert@aol.com> on May 15, 1998
