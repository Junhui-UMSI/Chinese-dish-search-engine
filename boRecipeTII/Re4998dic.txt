Pickled Pumpkin
Salt, Prepared pumpkin, Lemons, Sugar, Mixed spices, 
Wash pumpkin. Remove peel and seed. Cut pumpkin as wanted. Weigh and mix  with sugar. Let stand 12 to 18 hours in a cool place. Add thinly sliced  lemons, salt and mixed spices( tied in a bag). Boil until pumpkin is clear  and syrup thick. Pour, boiling hot into canning jars and seal at once.    Ball home cooking book.
