Basil Bean Salad
Dried basil, Pepper, Red wine vinegar, Olive oil; plus 1 tsp., Water, Grated Parmesan cheese, Garlic powder, 10oz. pkg frozen green beans, Onions; very thinly sliced, Salt to taste, 
Cook beans according to directions, cooking until just tender-crisp.  Drain. Combine beans and onions in a large bowl. Combine remaining  ingredinets and pour over beans. toss until well blended. Chill several  hours or overnight, stirring occasionally. **More Lean and Luscious** Info  per serving: 78 calories 2g protein 5g fat 1mg cholesterol
