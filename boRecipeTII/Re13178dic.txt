Woodcock Legs Sauteed in Garlic Butter
Garlic, pressed, Vegetable oil, Salt to taste, Woodcock legs, Butter, Black Pepper, 
Melt butter and oil in a heavy-bottomed skillet and saute garlic for one  minute, stirring so it wont scorch. Add woodcock legs and cook two to  three minutes on each side, turning once to brown evenly. Dont cook for  much more than five or six minutes in all.
