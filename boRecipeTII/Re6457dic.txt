Plum Wonderful Chicken
Chicken dark meat; no skin,, Chili sauce, Lemon juice, Garlic, Soy sauce, Dry mustard, Onion, Frozen lemon juice, Chicken breast halves, Ginger root; grated, Plum sauce, 
In a nonstick skillet, brown the chicken over medium heat for about 10  minutes. Remove chicken and place in a 3-quart baking dish. Drain off all  but 1 tb. fat in the skillet; add onion, garlic and gingerroot. Cook until  onion is tender. Stir together remaining ingredients and mix into onion  mixture. Heat just to boiling. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 5  minutes. Spoon mixture over chicken in dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350 F. for  40 to 45 minutes or until chicken is done, spooning sauce over chicken  occasionally.
