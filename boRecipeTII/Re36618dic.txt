Strawberry Cake (Cake Mix
Water, CAKE MIX WHITE #10, DSRT PWD STRAWBERRY, STRAWBERRIES FZ, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN               TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  THAW STRAWBERRIES IN REFRIGERATOR, SET ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 4.    2.  BLEND CAKE MIX, CONTENTS OF SODA PACKET,AND GELATIN TOGETHER IN MIXER  BOWL AT LOW SPEED UNTIL BLENDED, ABOUT 1 MINUTE.    3.  ADD STRAWBERRIES AND 1 QT WATER, MIX AT LOW SPEED FOR 3 MINUTES.  SCRAPE DOWN BOWL.    4.  ADD REMAINING WATER GRADUALLY, MIX AT LOW SPEED 2 MINUTES.  SCRAPE DOWN  BOWL.  BEAT AT MEDIUM SPEED ABOUT 2 MINUTES.    5.  POUR 4 3/4 QT (ABOUT 8 LB 6 OZ) BATTER INTO EACH GREASED AND FLOURED  PAN.    6.  BAKE 45 TO 55 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE.    7.  COOL; FROST IF DESIRED. CUT 6 BY 9.  NOTE:  1.  OTHER PAN SIZES MAY BE USED.  SEE RCIPE NO. G-G-4.    2.  IN STEP 6, IF CONVECTION OVEN IS USED, BAKE AT 300 F., 30-40  MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE ON HIGH FAN, OPEN VENT.
