Pumpkin Vegetable Dish
Sugar; to taste, Butter, Meat stock, ;Salt & Pepper to taste, Pumpkin, fresh, Vinegar; to taste, Dill, fresh; finely chopped, Parsley, fresh; finely chop, 
Peel and clean pumpkin and cut into sticks. In a large skillet, melt the  butter, and braise the pumpkin sticks. Add the meat stock, bring to a boil,  and simmer for about 8 minuts, or until fork-tender. Season to taste. Stir  in dill and parsley. Serve immediately. Serve with pan-fried liver and  rice.
