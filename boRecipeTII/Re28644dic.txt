Texas Chocolate Cake
Plain flour, Sugar, Sour cream, Icing:, Cocoa, Water, Baking soda, milk, Butter, Vanilla, Powdered sugar, Eggs, Nuts, Salt, Vanilla, Cocoa, Butter, 
Sift flour, sugar and salt into bowl.  In sauce pan: 2 sticks butter, 1/4  cup cocoa, 1 cup water.  Bring to boil.  Pour over ingredients in bowl. Add  sour cream.  add baking soda, eggs and 1 tsp vanilla.  Mix well and pour  into 18X12X1 pan.  Bake 25-30 minutes at 350.  Spread with icing while cake  is still warm.    Icing:  1 butter, 1/3 cup milk, 1/4 cup cocoa.  Bring to hard boil for 1-2  minutes.  Pour over box confectioners sugar.  Add 1 tsp vanilla and 1 cup  nuts.
