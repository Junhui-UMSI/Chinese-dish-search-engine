Sweet and Pungent Chinese Sauce
White vinegar, Ground ginger, Peach preserves, Orange Peel, Safflower oil, Onion powder, Apricot preserves, Ground red pepper, 
Combine all of the ingredients in a medium-sized bowl. Store in  refrigerator in a tightly covered container. Use as basting sauce for  chicken or pork.  NOTE: Recipe can be doubled.    Makes about 2 cups.    [Womans Day  MEALS IN MINUTES  August 1985]
