Apple Harvest Rice
Carrot, Reduced fat margarine, Water, Apple juice, Lemon juice, Brown sugar, Salt, Rice, Cinnamon, Raisins, Green onion, Apple, Seasame seeds, 
Cook carrots in margarine til tender-crisp, about 5 minutes. Add water,  apple juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, salt, rice, cinnamon and raisins.  Bring to a boil.    REDUCE heat, cover and simmer til rice is tender and liquid is absorbed,  about 15 minutes. Gently stir in green onions and apples, heat thoroughly.    Turn into a serving dish and top with sesame seeds.
