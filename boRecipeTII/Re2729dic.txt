Peanut Butter Bulgur Cookies
Eggs, Peanut butter, Reconstituted bulgar wheat, Brown sugar, Salt, Water (hot), Vanilla, Baking soda, Shortening, Sugar, Flour, 
Cream shortening and sugars, Add eggs, beat well. Dissolve soda in water.  Add ingredients to sugar mixture. Add bulgar and flour, mix well. Roll in  balls, press with fork. Bake 325 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes.    From the Kitchen of: Gary & Margie Hartford, Eugene, OR (1:152/19)
