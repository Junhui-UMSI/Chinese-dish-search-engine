Artichoke Soup
Chicken Stock, Green onions, Artichoke hearts, Potatoes, Cayenne pepper, Lowfat yogurt, 
Bring stock to boil. Add potatoes, onions, artichoke hearts, salt and  pepper. Bring back to boil and cook until tender, approximately 8 to 10  minutes. Remove from heat. Fill blender half full and puree. Strain to  remove strings. Return mixture to pot and heat. Stir in yogurt. May be  served hot or cold. Yield: Serves 6    Reduce sodium by using half regular chicken broth and half low-sodium  broth. >From A Votre Sante BY: Greater Baton Rouge Health Care  Alliance-1988    Recipe by: LYNDA D.
