The Best New England Clam Chowder
Butter, potatoes, Flour, clams, Onions, Celery, Clam juice, Bouillon, Salt pork, 
Saute the onion and celery in the butter until the onion is tranlucent. Add  the flour to make a roux, stirring well. Add the heated clam juice slowly,  stirring constantly.  Add the bouillon cubes and scored salt pork. Stir in  the clams and the potatoes. Remove from the heat and let stand for 20  minutes. Remove the salt pork before serving.
