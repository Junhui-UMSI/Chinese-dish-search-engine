Poultry Stuffed Peppers
Green bell peppers, Curry powder, Parsley flakes, Pepper, milk, Eggs, Diced cooked chicken, Onion; grated, Salt, 
Prepare peppers for stuffing. Combine rest of ingredients except chicken  and cheese. Add chicken and fill peppers. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 350  degrees for 35 minutes.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
