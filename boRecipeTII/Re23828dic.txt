Asian Gingered Chicken Soup/stew
Frozen chicken, Garlic, Salt, chicken broth, Scallions, Ground ginger, Frozen petite peas, fresh parsley, Lemon juice, vegetable oil, Long grain rice, 
Heat oil in a medium saucepan. Add garlic and ginger and saute for a few  seconds to release the aroma. Add the broth and water and bring to a boil.  Add the rice and boil for 10 minutes or until tender. Add the frozen  chicken and simmer just until cooked through. Add the peas and simmer just  to heat through. Season to taste with salt, pepper and lemon juice. Remove  the soup from the heat and add parsley and scallions.
