Angel Food Pie
Pecans, Egg whites, Vanilla, Sugar, Whipped topping, 
Beat egg whites until almost stiff. Add sugar & vanilla slowly. Beat until  mixture stands alone. Pour into baked pie shell. Bake at 300 until whites  are slightly browned, about 30 minutes. Remove from oven & cool. Top with  whipped topping. Sprinkle with pecans. Keep in refrigerator.
