White Clam Spaghetti Sauce
Canola oil, Clams, Thyme, Garlic, Flour, Parsley, Pasta, cooked, 
Cook garlic in oil until softened. Add flour and mix to a paste. Add juice  from canned clams and mix until smooth. Add remaining ingredients except  pasta and cook stirring over med heat for about 5 mins. Serve over hot  pasta.    NOTES : Some white wine can be added although you will need more flour.  Serve with freshly grated parmesan cheese.  Recipe by: Carol    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #950 by Carol & Bob Floyd  <c.floyd@arnprior.com> on Dec 08, 1997
