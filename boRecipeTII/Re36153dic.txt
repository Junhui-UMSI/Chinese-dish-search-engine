From: Charlie Johnston <howknows@bigblue.oit.unc.edu>    Date: 18 Feb 1994 02:18:17 -0500    Heat small amount of oil (or cooking spray) in large skillet.  Brown beef  chunks, seasoning with salt/pepper/etc.  Place beef in bottom of Crockpot  and top with vegetable (except mushrooms) and a bay leaf or two. Add water  to barely cover. Cook all day on low. Stir in mushrooms 15 minutes before  serving.  Serve with bread or rolls.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.
