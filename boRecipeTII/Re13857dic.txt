Wilted Iceberg and Peas
Salt, Fresh green peas, Sugar, Thinly sliced iceberg, Margarine, Pepper, 
Melt margarine in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add  lettuce, and cook 3 minutes or until lettuce wilts, stirring frequently.    Stir in sugar and remaining ingredients, and cook 2 minutes. Yield: 4  servings (serving size: 1 cup).    Per serving: 100 Calories; 3g Fat (29% calories from fat); 5g Protein; 13g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 180mg Sodium    Serving Ideas :  Serve immediately.
