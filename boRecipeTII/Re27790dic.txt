The Sherwood Royale
Brut sparkling wine, Cranberry juice, Hazelnuts, Creme de Cassis, Frangelico, Sugar, Dried cranberries, 
Heres a different drink recipe which calls for Cassis. It was created at  and named for a lodge in the Muskoka tourist area of Ontario.    Combine dried cranberries, hazelnuts and sugar in a food processor and  process to a powder.    Dip rim of Champagne flute into cranberry juice, then into  cranberry/hazelnut mixture.    Pour Champagne into flute, add Creme de Cassis and Frangelico. Posted to  FoodWine Digest by Leslie Duncan <duncan@VIANET.ON.CA> on Jul 9, 1997
