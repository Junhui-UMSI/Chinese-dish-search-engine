Veal Milanese
Fresh basil, Eggs, Salt and pepper, Lemon peel, Dry white wine, garlic, Onion, Veal leg, Seasoned flour, olive oil, Dry bread crumbs, tomatoes, Chopped, Italian Sauce-----, 
Toss veal in flour; dip in egg and coat in mixed crumbs and peel. To make  the Italian sauce heat the oil in a saucepan add the onion and garlic and  cook over a low heat until soft. Add tomatoes and wine and season with salt  and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes. Puree sauce in a blender or food  processor until smooth or press through a sieve. Stir in basil and reheat  the sauce before serving. Serve with the veal cooked in hot oil.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
