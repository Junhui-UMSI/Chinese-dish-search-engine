Yummie Sesame Noodles
scallions, Warm water, Honey, fresh ginger, Soy sauce, Crunchy Peanut butter, Sesame oil, Pasta, Garlic, 
While pasta is cooking, mix all of the ingredients in a large bowl. When  the pasta is cooked, drain, and mix with sauce. Chill for a few hours  before serving. They are best cold!! Garnish with scallions just before  serving.    Serves: 4    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Mar 04, 1998
