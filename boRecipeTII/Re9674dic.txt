Pumpkin Oatmeal Cookies
Egg Beaters. 99% Egg, Oatmeal, Flour, Brown sugar, Baking soda, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Raisins, Cloves, Ground, Pumpkin Pie Filling, 
Throughly mix pumpkin and brown sugar; then beat in egg substitute.  Gradually mix in flour, soda, and spices, then mix in oatmeal and raisins.  Drop by spoonfuls on non-stick or oil-sprayed cookie sheets.  Bake at 350  deg. F  for 10 - 20 min.
