Pear Ginger Preserves
Pears -- peeled and, Lemon -- finely diced, Coarsely, Salt, Ginger root, Honey, 
Combine all ingredients except ginger root.  Cook for about 15 minutes,  stirring occasionally, until the mixture is of spreading consistency. Spoon  hot mixture into hot sterilized jars to within 1/2-inch from top. Add a  small piece of ginger to each jar.  Seal and process in a boiling water  bath for 5 minutes. Yield: 5 half-pints Posted by RED FLA.
