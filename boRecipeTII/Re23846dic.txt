Asian Noodles with Chile Shrimp and Cashews
Dried Chinese-style noodles, Sesame oil, Grated ginger root, Scallions; thinly sliced, Yellow bell pepper;, Chinese chile paste, Light soy sauce, Prepared hot sauce, Garlic, Large shrimp; peeled and, Peanut oil, Red bell pepper; julienned, Cashews; toasted, Honey, 
In large pot of boiling salted water, cook noodles, breaking up with fork,  until tender, about 3 minutes. Drain well and toss with 1 tablespoon oil.  In large skillet over medium-high heat, heat 1 tablespoon oil and cook  shrimp until they turn pink, about 3 minutes. Transfer to plate. In same  skillet over medium-high heat, heat remaining tablespoon oil and cook  garlic and ginger until fragrant, about 1 minute. Stir in scallions and  bell peppers. Cook until tender, about 3 minutes. In small bowl, combine  hot sauce, chile paste, soy sauce, sesame oil and honey until blended. Stir  into skillet with vegetables. Stir in shrimp and cashews and heat to  boiling. Remove from heat. In large bowl, toss noodles with vegetables and  shrimp.
