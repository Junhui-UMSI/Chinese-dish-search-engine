Barbara Billingsleys "Sweet Potato Casserole"
Brown Sugar, Orange juice, Cooked yams, Salt, Butter, Brandy, Lemon Peel, Eggs; slightly beaten, Cinnamon, 
Beat cooked yams with electric mixer, adding melted butter - add remaining  ingredients and beat until smooth - butter a 2 quart casserole and spoon in  the mixture - bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until set.
