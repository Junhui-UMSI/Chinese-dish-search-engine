Barbeque Sauce with Beer Robert
Worcestershire Sauce, Mince garlic, 12-oz bottle chili suace, Salt, 14-oz bottles catsup, Soy sauce, 5-oz bottle steak sauce, Brown sugar, firmly packed, Prepared mustard, Dry mustard, Black pepper, 12-oz bottle beer, 
Combine all ingredients, except garlic, in saucepan and simmer 30 minutes  over medium heat.  Add minced garlic before using. Baste meat during last       15    minutes of grilling time.
