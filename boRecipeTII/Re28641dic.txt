Texas Chili Chicken Stew
VEG-ALL Mixed Vegetables,, Green Pepper, Vegetable oil, Flour, Chopped stewed tomatoes, Chopped mild green chilies, Chili powder, Onions, Oregano, Chicken broth (12 oz), Basil, With liquid (16 oz), Garlic, Cooked chicken, diced, 
1. Heat oil in 2-quart pot. Cook onion and green pepper until tender, but  not browned.    2. Add garlic and stir in flour, chili powder, basil, oregano, chilis,  chicken broth and diced chicken.    3. Bring to a boil and simmer for 20 minutes.    4. Stir in tomatoes and VEG-ALL and cook 10 minutes longer.
