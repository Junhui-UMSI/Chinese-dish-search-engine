Quick Cheddar Bread
Unbleached flour, Eggs, Salt, milk, Cheddar; Sharp, Baking Powder, Butter, 
Combine the dry ingredients, then cut the butter into the flour until the  mixture resembles coarse crumbs, then add the cheddar cheese.  Combine the  milk and eggs then add the mixture to the cheddar mixture.  Stir until  just moistened, then spoon into a greased 9 X 5-inch loaf pan.  Bake at  375 degrees F.   hour.  Remove from the pan immediately and let cool on a  wire rack.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcheese.zip
