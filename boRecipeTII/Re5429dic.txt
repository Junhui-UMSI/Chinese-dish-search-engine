Pineapple Chicken Salad
Chopped red pepper, Vegetable oil, GREY POUPON Dijon Mustard, Fresh pineapple, Cooked; diced chicken, Cooked long-grain and wild, Celery, Red or white wine vinegar, Green Onions, PLANTERS Sliced Almonds;, Ground ginger, 
1. Cut pineapple in half lengthwise, slicing through top. Scoop out  pineapple; dice and set aside.    2. Mix chicken, rice, 1 cup reserved pineapple, celery, red pepper and  green onions in large bowl; set aside.    3. Whisk together oil, vinegar, mustard and ginger until smooth. Stir into  chicken mixture until well blended. Cover; chill several hours to blend  flavors.    4. Gently toss chicken salad with almonds; serve in pineapple shells.
