Popcorn Balls
Popped Corn, Butter, Water, Salt, Vanilla, Sugar, Corn syrup, 
*******    Combine sugar, water, corn syrup, salt and butter in saucepan.  Cook over  medium heat until mixture is 250 degrees or until hard cracked stage.  Remo  from heat; add vanilla.  Pour candy in a thin stream over popped corn  tossing constantly.  Shape into balls with buttered hands.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
