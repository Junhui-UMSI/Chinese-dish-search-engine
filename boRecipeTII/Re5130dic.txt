Pierogi (Basic)
Shortening, Flour, Whole egg, Water -- cold, Salt, Sugar, 
Make dough like pie crust: roll out onto floured board: cut into 8"x4"  squares. put a tablespoon of your favorite filling and fold dough over  filling. pinch seams to seal. Boil in salted water for 15 minutes. when  cool fry in butter.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
