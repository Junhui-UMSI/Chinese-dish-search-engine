Crock Pot Ratatouille
Zucchini, Garlic, Green Bell Peppers, Tomatoes, Dried basil, Salt, Black pepper, Fresh parsley, olive oil, Parmesan Cheese, Onions, Eggplant, 
The Cook and Kitchen Staff are heading "Back to School" and offering you  some of our simplest recipes that nearly anyone can prepare.    Todays recipe requires a Crock Pot, which is a great cooking apparatus for  your college- bound student, or for a busy working parent. Any way you  prepare it, todays recipe is a nutritious meal for anyone on the move!    Layer half the vegetables in a large crock pot in the following order:  onion, eggplant, zucchini, garlic, green peppers, tomatoes. Repeat layering  process with remaining vegetables.    Sprinkle basil, salt, pepper, and parsley over top. Drizzle with olive oil.  Cover and cook on low 7-9 hours. Cool and chop coarsely. Place in serving  bowl and sprinkle with freshly grated Parmesan cheese. Refrigerate to  store. May freeze up to 6 weeks.
