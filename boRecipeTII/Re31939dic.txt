Taco Bean Salad
Chili powder, Ground cumin, Sea salt (optional), Oregano, Tabasco sauce (optional), Cayenne pepper (optional), Celery stalk; chopped finely, Cooked AM Pinto Beans, Onion, Tomato; chopped, Unrefined olive oil, drained, Green bell pepper; chopped, Sour cream or plain yogurt, 
Soak beans overnight, then cook until tender.  Drain.  Combine all  ingredients together and mix well.  Serve with lettuce and corn chips.
