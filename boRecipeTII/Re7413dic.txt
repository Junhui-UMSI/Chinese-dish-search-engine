Pork Chops with Waldorf Rice
Celery, diced, Apple, cubed, Inch lean pork chops,, Rice, Onion, minced, salt and pepper, 
(Favorite Recipes of Home Economics Teachers)    Season chops with salt and pepper. Brown in skillet. Place chops in  casserole dish. Dissolve 1/2 teaspoon salt in 2 cups water and pour over  chops. Spread rice evenly over chops. Cover with apple, celery and onion.  Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes or until chops are tender.  Serves 4.
