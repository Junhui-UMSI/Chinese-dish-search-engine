Peppermint Creams
Peppermint, Water, Corn syrup; white, Sugar, Cream of tartar, 
Boil to soft ball. Drop on wax paper from spoon.    Source: Loleta Hales, Acma Grange, Lorain County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
