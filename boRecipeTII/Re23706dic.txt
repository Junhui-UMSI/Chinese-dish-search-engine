Artichoke Rice Salad
Marinated artichoke hearts , Hot cooked rice, Green Onions, Lettuce leaves, red bell pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, fresh mushrooms, white pepper, White wine vinegar, 
Combine hot rice, artichoke hearts, mushrooms, onions, cheese, and pepper.  Combine reserved liquid from artichokes and vinegar; shake to combine.  Drizzle over salad.  Spoon into individual lettuce-lined salad plates.
