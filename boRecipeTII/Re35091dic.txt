Bearnaise Sauce
Butter, Egg Yolks, White wine, Fresh Tarragon, Salt, Shallots, 
Combine wine, shallots and tarragon, and cook until wine is reduced to a  mere glaze. Combine glaze with egg yolks and salt in blender. Heat butter  until bubbling hot. Turn blender on and off once quickly to blend  ingredients. Turn on again and gradually pour melted butter in steady  stream until sauce thickens. Additional tarragon or parsley may be folded  into sauce. Excellent on broiled beef, fish or eggs.
