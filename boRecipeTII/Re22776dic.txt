Apricot Raisin Filling for Hamentaschen
Seedless orange, Dried apricots; (8 ounces), Sugar; Up To 4 Tbsp., Raisins, 
Steel Knife: Cut orange in quarters, but do not peel. Process half the  orange until fine, about 20 seconds. Add half the remaining ingredients.  Process until fine, about 15 seconds. Empty processor bowl. Repeat with  remaining ingredients. Yield: about 2 cups filling. May be prepared in  advance and refrigerated or frozen.
