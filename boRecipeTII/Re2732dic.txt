Peanut Butter Cake (Cake
CAKE MIX, YELLOW, PEANUT BUTTER, Water, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN                   TEMPERATUE:  375 F. OVEN  :    1.  PREPARE CAKE MIX ACCORDING TO INSTRUCTION ON CONTAINER.  ADD PEANUT  BUTTER WITH SECOND ADDITION OF WATER.  SEE GUIDELINES FOR USING CAKE  MIXES (RECIPE NO. G-G-3). ADD 2 LB 8 OZ (4 1/2 CUP) PEANUT BUTTER WITH  SECOND ADDITION OF WATER.    2.  POUR 8 LB 12 OZ (14 1/2 QT) BATTER INTO EACH GREASED AND FLOURED PAN.    3.  BAKE 40 TO 45 MINUTES.    4.  COOL.  CUT 6 BY 9.
