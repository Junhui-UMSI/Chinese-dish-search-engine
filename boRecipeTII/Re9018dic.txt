Pride of Iowa Cookies
Shortening, Oatmeal, Baking Powder, Salt, Brown sugar, Flour; sifted, Nuts; chopped, Vanilla, Sugar, Coconut; chopped, Eggs, Baking soda, 
Mix shortening, brown sugar, white sugar, eggs and coconut. Sift and add  dry ingredients. Then add oatmeal, vanilla and nuts. Mix all together, roll  into small balls. Press down on cookie sheet with fork dipped in flour or  sugar. Bake in 350 oven 8-10 minutes. Makes 5 dozen.
