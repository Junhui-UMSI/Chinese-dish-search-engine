Strawberry Cheesecake #2
Sugar, Graham crakers, Crust, Sugar, FILLING, Sugar, Mashed strawberries, GLAZE, Butter or Margarine, Water, Lemon peel; grated, Cornstarch, Vanilla, Eggs, Cream cheese, 
From: mark@alexr.co.uk    Date: Sun, 25 Feb 1996 00:00:24 GMT  CRUST: Pre-heat oven to 350f. Stir together graham cracker crumbs and  sugar. Mix in butter thoroughly and press into a 9" springform pan. Bake 10  minutes. Cool. Reduce oven temperature to 300f.    FILLING: Beat cream cheese in a large mixing bowl. Gradually add sugar;  beating until fluffy. Add lemon peel and vanilla. Beat in eggs one at a  time. Pour into shell. Bake 1 hour or until center is firm. Cool to room  temperature and then spread with the strawberry glaze. Chill 3 hours.    GLAZE: Blend sugar and cornstarch together in a small saucepan. Stir in  water and strawberries. Cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture  thickens and boils. Boil and stir for 1 minute. Cool thoroughly before  spreading over cheesecake.
