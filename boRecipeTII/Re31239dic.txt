Banana-Peach Shake
Vanilla frozen yogurt, fresh peach, Skim milk, banana, 
Combine first 3 ingredients in container of an electric blender; cover and  process until smooth. Add frozen yogurt; process until smooth. Yield: 3  cups (serving size: 1 cup).
