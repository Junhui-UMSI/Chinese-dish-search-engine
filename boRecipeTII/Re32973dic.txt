Sweetnsour Cocktail Wieners
Currant jelly(10oz), Prepared mustard(6 oz jar), Frankfurters, Vienna Sauges, 
Mix mustard and jelly in chafing dish or double boiler. Diagonally slice  frnkfuters in bite size pieces; add to sauce and heat. Makes 8 servings.    Recipe by: Martha Dixons Copper Kettle Cookbook Posted to Kitmailbox by  "Jazzbel" <jazzbel@grouper.batelnet.bs> on Apr 29, 1997
