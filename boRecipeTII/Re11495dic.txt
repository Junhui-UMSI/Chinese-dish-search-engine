Raspberry Coconut Layer Bars
Raspberry jam or preserves, (14-oz) sweetened condensed, Semi-sweet chocolate, Chopped Nestle Premier white, Graham cracker crumbs, (7-oz) flaked coconut, Finely chopped walnuts;, Butter or Margarine, 
In medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and butter. Spread evenly  over bottom of 13x9-inch baking pan, pressing to make compact crust.  Sprinkle coconut over crust. Pour sweetened condensed milk evenly over  coconut. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 25-30 minutes or until  lightly browned; cool. Spread jam over coconut layer; chill 3 to 4 hours.  Sprinkle with walnuts. Drizzle dark chocolate then white chocolate over top  layer to make lacy effect; chill. Cut into 3x1-1/2-inch bars.
