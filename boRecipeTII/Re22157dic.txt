Turkey Patties in Mushroom Wine Sauce
Fresh mushrooms, Onions, Margarine, Ground turkey, Egg, Salt, Italian herb blend, Dry Red Wine, Cracked pepper, 
Saute half the mushrooms and the chopped onion in margarine in a large  frying pan.  Combine sauteed vegetables with turkey, egg, salt, and  herbs. Shape turkey mixture into 4 patties.  Broil 3-5 minutes on  each side until cooked through.  While patties are cooking, add  remaining mushrooms, wine, and pepper to frying pan.  Simmer gently  and serve sauce over patties.
