Tiramisu
Mascarpone cheese, Biscotti pavesini or, Sugar, Bittersweet chocolate,, Eggs, Rum or brandy, Espresso, cooled, 
Dip 12 ladyfingers quickly in espresso. Arrange in a row in a shallow  platter. Beat egg yolks with sugar until pale. Add mascarpone and rum or  brandy and mix well. Beat egg whites until stiff but not dry; fold into  mascarpone mixture. Spread half of mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers.  Sprinkle with half of chocolate. Dip remaining ladyfingers in remaining  espresso. Spread with remainder of mascarpone mixture. Sprinkle with  remaining chocolate. Cover lightly with foil. Refrigerate.    *Italian ladyfingers  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #499 by nancee <nancee@neo.lrun.com> on 4 Ma,  r 97.
