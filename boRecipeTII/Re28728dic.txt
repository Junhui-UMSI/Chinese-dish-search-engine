Texas Truffles
Shredded coconut meat;, Bag (10 oz) marshmallows, Pecans, (12 oz) chocolate chips, Multi-colored sprinkles, 
Arrange marshmallows on tray and freeze for 15 minutes. Microwave chocolate  chips in glass bowl on High for 1 minute. Stir. Microwave for 1 minute.  Stir and microwave a further 30 seconds or until smooth. Using a skewer or  fondue fork, dip marshmallows into melted chocolate unitl marshmallow is  chocolate coated. Roll chocolate-coated marshmallows in coconut, chopped  nuts and/or sprinkles. Use another fork or metal spatula to place  marshmallows on wax paper lined tray. Chill or keep in a cool dry place.
