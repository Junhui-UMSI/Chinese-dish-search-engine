Beef or Chicken Stock
Meaty beef bones, thyme, Salt, sage, celery, Dried oregano leaves, Margarine, Cold water, carrots, onion, Pepper, 
In heavy Dutch oven, brown beef bones slowly in margarine over low  heat, 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add water & remaining  ingredients; bring to simmer. Simmer, uncovered, 3-4 hours, skim off  grease or scum that rises to top.  Remove meat, bones & vegetables.  Strain. Chill stock & remove fat from surface.  Let stand 24 hours.
