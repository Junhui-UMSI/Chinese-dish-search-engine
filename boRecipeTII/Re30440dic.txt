Banana Jama Bran Muffins
Walnuts, Mashed ripe bananas, Flour, Butter or margarine, Baking soda, Sugar, Eggs at room temp, Salt, Banana Jama sauce; or, (NOTE: see recipe "Banana, Wheat bran, 
mikeb@radonc.ucdmc.ucdavis.edu    Preheat oven to 375F.  Grease 24 muffin tins.  Sift first three ingredients  together, add bran and nuts.    In another bowl cream the margarine and sugar together.  Mix in the  bananas, sauce and the eggs one at a time. Stir banana mixture into the  flour mix; mix only until moistened. Pour into muffin tins, bake 15 min or  until golden brown. Let cool before attempting to remove from the tins.    CHILE-HEADS ARCHIVES    From the Chile-Heads recipe list.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
