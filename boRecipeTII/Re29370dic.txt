Baked Turkey and Rice
Rice, Butter, Flour, Turkey stock, Sauterne, Mushroom, Parsley, Instant minced onion, Powdered sage, Sharp cheddar cheese, Turkey, Salt & pepper, Paprika, 
Preheat oven to 375F.  Cook the rice as usual.   Make a sauce with next 4 ingredients on stove top.  Add  mushrooms, parsley, onion, sage, and half the cheese. Stir until cheese is  melted. Stir in rice and turkey; season with salt and pepper to taste.   Put  in shallow baking dish, and sprinkle remaining cheese and paprika on top.  Bake for about 30 minutes.  *Good with zucchini, sliced  tomatoes, apple crunch/crisp.
