Apple Noodle Kugel
Box Raisins, Apple Pie Filling; Comstock, Sugar, Sticks Margarine; melted, Eggs, Noodles; cooked & drained, Cinnamon, 
Recipe By : Betty Farber, Miami    Oven at 350?. Combine all ingredients except noodles and stir well. Add  noodles. Spray Pam in 13 x 9" pan. Pour ingredients in. Bake 1 hour.  Freezes well after baking.    NOTES : Pareve recipe.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by SuperSecDD@aol.com on Aug 30, 1998,
