Prune-Apricot Compote
Frozen unsweetened apple, Lemon, Water, Dried apricots, Brandy (optional), Pitted prunes, Orange sliced; quartered, Sticks orange zest -or-, 
From: elayne@usa.pipeline.com (Elayne Cohen)    Date: Wed, 17 Jul 1996 21:34:29 GMT  Mix all ingredients in a 3-quart glass casserole. Cover and microwave on  high for 15 minutes or until prunes and apricots are plumped and soft.  Cover and refrigerate to chill before serving.
