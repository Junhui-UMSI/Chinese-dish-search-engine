24-Hour Lettuce Salad
Red onion, chopped, Head lettuce, torn, Frozen baby green peas,, Green pepper, diced, Slices bacon, crumbled, Sugar or honey, Celery, chopped, Slightly thawed, Mayonnaise, Cheddar cheese, grated, 
Layer first 5 ingredients in a glass bowl in order of listing. Spread on  mayonnaise.  Sprinkle on sugar or honey, then cheese, then bacon. Cover  with foil and refrigerate overnight. Toss lightly just before serving.
