Yorkshire Pudding - English
Salt, Flour, Buttermilk, Eggs, Beef drippings, 
Sift flour and salt together. Form well in center of mixture. Break in  eggs. Add milk. Mix gradually. Beat until smooth.Continue beating for 5  minutes. Leave drippings from roast beef in roasting pan to cover bottom.  Pour batter into hot drippings. Bake at 400 degs for 40 minutes. Cut into  squares.
