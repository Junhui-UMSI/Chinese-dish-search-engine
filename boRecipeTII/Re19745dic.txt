Anytime Artichokes
Pepper, Salt, Miracle Whip, Fresh Artichokes *, Bay leaf, Lemon, 
* Artichokes should have stems, top 1" of cone, tough bottom leaves and  leaf tips trimmed.  ~-------------------------------------------------------------------------  Place artichokes in a large pot and add enough water to cover artichokes  halfway.  Add lemon, bay leaf and salt.  Heat to boiling over medium high  heat.  Reduce heat and cook covered until bottoms are tender when pierced  with a knife, about 20 minutes.  Drain Well.  Serve with a small dish of  Miracle Whip for dipping leaves and heart.  From: Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sydsbook.zip
