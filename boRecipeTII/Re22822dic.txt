Apricot Wild Rice Stuffing
Herb-seasoned stuffing cubes, Butter, Dried apricots; chopped, Chopped pecans; toasted, Hot water, Onion, Cooked wild rice, 
Mix all ingredients until well blended.    Lightly fill body cavities on turkey.  Spoon any remaining stuffing into an  ungreased casserole and bake, covered, with the turkey until hot.    Per serving: 293 Calories; 6g Fat (17% calories from fat); 8g Protein; 55g  Carbohydrate; 11mg Cholesterol; 1092mg Sodium    NOTES : Stuffing to fill a 18 pound turkey.
