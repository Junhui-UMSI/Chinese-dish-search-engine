Arni Me Araka (Lamb with Peas)
Salt and pepper to taste, Butter or margarine, Parsley, chopped, Scallions, chopped, Tomato paste + 2 c. water, Peas, shelled, washed, Lamb, Tomatoes, peeled,strained OR, Dill, chopped, Water as needed, 
Cut meat into 1 1/2" cubes (approx.).  Wash and drain. Brown butter in a  pot.  Add meat and brown well. Add scallions, and cook until soft but not  dark.  Add tomatoes (or diluted tomato paste), dill, parsley, salt &  pepper.  Simmer abt. 20 min.  Add peas and an addtl. 1 c. water. Cover,  simmer until all liquid is absorbed and only butter remains as a sauce.
