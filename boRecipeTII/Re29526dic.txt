Tart n Tangy Italian Dressing
Wine vinegar, Parsley flakes, Onion, Garlic powder, Sugar, Cayenne or red pepper, olive oil, Dry mustard, White pepper, Seasoned salt, Chives, Oregano, 
Combine all ingredients in jar; cover and shake vigorously. Chill 1 hour  for flavors to blend.  Shake well before serving.  Makes 1 1/2 cups.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/garlic.zip
