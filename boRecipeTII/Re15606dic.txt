A 14-Minute Maple Candy
Lemon extract, Butter, Maple sirup, Cream, Nut meats, chopped, 
Cook the maple sirup, cream, and butter for 9 minutes after the boiling  point is reached.  Remove from heat, add the nut meats and extract and stir  for 5 minutes. Pour into buttered pans and when cool, cut into squares.    From: Culinary Arts Institute Encyclopedic CookBook Shared By: Pat Stockett
