Tzimmmes Deluxe
Salt, Honey, Onion, small, Fat, Brisket or flanken, Brown  sugar, Sweet potatoes, Flour, Prunes, Water to cover, White potatoes, Carrots, large, 
Pare and cut the carrots and potatoes into thick slices. Rinse prunes. Sear  meat on all sides in heavy pan until brown. Add vegetables, seasoning,  honey, cold water to vocer and cook for 2 1/2 to 3 hrs until meat is  tender. shake from time to time so vegetables do not stick. Add water if  necessary.  Remove onion and make an einbrein by stirring the flour into  the melted fat. Cook minute or two and then add some of the liquid from the  pan.  Place all in casserole and bake, uncovered for 350 F. oven for 30  min.
