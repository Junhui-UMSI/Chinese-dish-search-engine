Peppermint Chocolate Squares
Margarine, Whipping cream, Conf sugar, Pecans, Sugar, Green food coloring, First layer, Flour, Eggs, Third layer, Margerine, Second layer, Unsweetened Chocolate, Vegetable oil, Mint & peppermint extract, Milk chocolate chips, 
POWER: High/50% .... TURN: Yes .... TIME: 11 min. per 12 servings Melt  chocolate and margarine in glass mixing bowl on high for 2 minutes. Stir in  remaining first layer ingredients. Pour into 8 in. square glass baking  dish. Microwave 5 to 6 minutes on high, giving dish a 1/4 turn halfway  through cooking. In separate bowl, mix together second layer ingredients.  Spread over first layer. Refrigerate to harden. Combine third layer  ingredients in glass bowl for about 3 minutes at 50% power. Pour over  second layer. Refrigerate and cut into squares.
