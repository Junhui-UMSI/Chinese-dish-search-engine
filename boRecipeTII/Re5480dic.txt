Pineapple Drop Cookies
Baking soda, Baking Powder, Nut meats, Sugar, Egg, Butter, Flour; sifted, Salt; a pinch, Pineapple; drained - crushe, 
Cream butter, sugar, add remaining ingredients. Mix well, drop 1/2 teaspoon  on cookie sheet. Bake in oven at 375 F.    Source: Florence Yohey, Tallmadge Grange, Summit County, OH
