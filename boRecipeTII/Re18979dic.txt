Angel Wings
Powdered sugar, Egg Yokes, Flour, Vanilla, Vinegar, Egg, Sugar, 
Use 1 1/3 c four to start. Add more for desired consistency.  Mix  ingredients together.  roll out thin 1/8" thick.  cut into long rectangles.  Cut 2" slit in center. Pull on end of dough through slit cut. Fry in 350  degree shortening (crisco). Roll in powdered sugar.
