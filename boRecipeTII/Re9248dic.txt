Pudding Brownies
Eggs, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Butter, Unsifted flour, Sugar, Chocolate pudding, Nuts; chopped, 
Combine: Flour, pudding mix and baking powder. Mix  well. Melt butter in sauce pan. Remove from heat. Beat  eggs in one at a time. Blend in vanilla and pudding  mixture. Spread in 8" square pan and bake at 350~F for  35 minutes. Do not overbake. Cool in pan. Ice or cut  into bars.
