Swabian Bread
Whole wheat flour, Vegetable oil, milk, Yeast, Flour, Salt, Water, 
Flour, water & milk should be warm. Add yeast to 1/2 cup warm milk. Combine  all other ingredients & pour yeast & milk over all. Mix well by hand &  knead about 15 minutes; let rise in a warm place for 1 to 1-1/2 hours. Form  dough into a loaf & put on buttered cookie sheet. Let rise again while oven  heats to 400. Bake for 30-40 minutes.    FRAU GUENTER (MARGRIET) SCHON    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
