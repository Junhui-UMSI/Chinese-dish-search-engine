Traditional Irish Soda Bread
Plain flour, Salt, Buttermilk, Baking soda, 
From: ELF <mirya@sk.sympatico.ca>    mix the dry ingrediants together,making sure there are no lumps in the  soda.Add buttermilk and mix well with a wooden spoon. Turn out into a  lightly floured surface and knead lightly until smooth. Placed onto a  baking sheet and with sharp knife mark the top with a cross. Bake at the  top of a hot oven for three quarters of an hour,until the bottom of the  loaf sounds hollow when rapped with the knuckle.    According to my G.grandfather from Co.
