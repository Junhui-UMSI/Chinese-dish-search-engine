Walnut-Cheese Spread
Green onions; finely chopped, Cream cheese, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Garlic powder, Walnuts; coarsely chopped, Lemon juice, 
In small bowl, stir cream cheese until smooth; stir in walnuts, lemon  juice, green onions and garlic powder. Serve with whole wheat or  pumpernickel party bread. Makes 3/4 cup.
