White and Wild Rice Casserole
Cream of mushroom soup, Chicken broth or milk, Green Pepper, salt and pepper, Cooked chicken; cut up, Cooked long grain white &, 
Mix ingredients. Bake in 350 oven in a 2-quart baking dish for 30 minutes.  Serves 8-10.    MRS GLENN H. (IRENE) PACK    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
