Timothys White Chili
Ground cloves, Chicken broth, Oregano, Garlic, Sour cream, Vegetable oil, Ground cumin, Salsa, Medium onions, chopped, Diced cooked chicken breast, Grated monterey jack cheese, Dried white beans, Canned green chilies, Cayenne pepper, 
1. Combine beans,broth,garlic & 1/2 the onions in a large pot. bring to a  boil, reduce & simmer til beans are soft - 2 hrs or more - adding broth if  necessary. 2. Saute remaining onions in oil til tender. Add chilies &  seasonings & mix thoroughly. Add to bean mixture. Add chicken & simmer 1hr.  3. Serve topped with grated cheese, salsa & sour cream.
