Zesty Chicken Dijon
Italian salad dressing, Boned and skinned chicken, Dijon mustard, 
In small bowl blend mustard and dressing. Add chicken, stirring to coat  well. Refrigerate at least 1 hour. Grill or broil chicken over medium heat  6 minutes on each side or until done.    Recipe by: Grey Poupon Recipe Flyer    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by MAMacR@aol.com on Feb 21, 1998
