Asian Vinegar
Slivered peeled fresh ginger, Rice vinegar (up to 2), Sprigs (each about 4 in), Cloves garlic; slivered, Dried small hot red chiles, 
With a chopstick or skewer, gently push the ginger, slivered garlics,  chilies and cilantro sprigs into a clean 12- to 16-ounce bottle. Pour  vinegar through a funnel into the bottle to fill. Seal and store in a cool,  dark place at room temperature at least a week or up to 4 months.
