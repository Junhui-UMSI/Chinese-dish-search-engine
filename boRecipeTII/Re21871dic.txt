Apple-Sausage Oven Pancake
Egg, Brown & serve link sausages, Ground cinnamon, Chopped pared apple, Maple syrup, milk, Bisquick, 
Heat oven 450.  Grease 8x8x2" baking dish.  Brown sausages as directed on  pkg.  Beat Bisquick, cinnamon, milk and egg with hand beater until smooth.  Stir in apple.  Pour in dish; arrange sausages on top. Bake until light  brown, 20 - 25 min. Serve with syrup.
