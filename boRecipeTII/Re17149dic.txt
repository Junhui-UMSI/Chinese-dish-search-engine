Sprinkle with 1 heaping teaspoonful nut mixture. Fold in sides 1/2 inch to  seal. Roll up as for jelly roll. Place seam side down in buttered pan.  Repeat with remaining ingredients. Brush tops with clarified butter.    Bake pastries: Place pan on center shelf of oven and bake until golden  (about 20 minutes). Transfer to wire rack. Drizzle 2 teaspoonsful honey  syrup over each pastry. Set aside to cool.    Yield: 64 pastries    NB: Baklava can be frozen for up to 6 months.
