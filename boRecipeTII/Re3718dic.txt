Penne or Rigatoni a la Vodka
Vodka, Heavy cream, Parmigiano Reggiano, Salt, Canned tomatoes, Butter, Red pepper flakes, 
~-Heat butter in large skillet over medium heat.  Stir in vodka and pepper  flakes and heat gently for 2 minutes.  Add tomatoes and salt and cook over  high heat, stirring to break up the tomatoes until thick (approx. 5  minutes). --Stir in heavy cream and cook until thick (approx. 5 minutes or  more). Stir in parmesan cheese. Toss with cooked penne or rigatoni style  pasta    Recipe By     : lroberts@41mad.com (Lauren Roberts)    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
