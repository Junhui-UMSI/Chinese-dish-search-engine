Tex-Mex Meat Loaf
RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes, Ground beef, Onion, Chili sauce, Cheddar cheese, Egg, Chili powder, Black pepper, Bread, 
Preheat oven to 375F. Combine first seven ingredients. Spread into an  ungreased 8 1/2x4 1/2x2 1/2" loaf pan. Bake 1 hour; drain excess  drippings. Spoon chili sauce over cooked loaf, then sprinkle with  cheese; bake an additional 15 minutes.    From "Southwestern Sensations Starring RO*TEL", courtesy Mike Orchekowski.
