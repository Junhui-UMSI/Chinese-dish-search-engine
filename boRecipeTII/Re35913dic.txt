Stuffed Mushrooms #1
Grated Parmesan cheese, nutmeg, Fresh mushrooms, Sour cream, Salt, Ground chuck, Oregano, Green Onions, Garlic, Fresh spinach, Monterey Jack, Cheddar cheese, 
Cook spinach briefly, drain & chop. Wash mushrooms & remove stems. Finely  chop stems. Add stems to spinach & mix well with sour cream, salt, oregano  & some of mixed cheese. (Reserve some of cheese for top). Saute ground beef  lightly with green onions & garlic. Fill mushroom caps with meat. Spoon  spinach mixture around edges & top with cheeses & nutmeg. Bake at 350 for       25    minutes.    MRS. ROBERT M. HORNOR, JR.    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
