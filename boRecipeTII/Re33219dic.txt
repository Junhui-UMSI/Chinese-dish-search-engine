Basic Pesto Recipe
Garlic, Basil leaves, Pine nuts, salt and pepper, Pecorino, Ligurian olive oil, Parmigiano reggiano, 
In a food processor pulse basil leaves, garlic, pine nuts and cheeses until  blended. With motor running, pour olive oil in a thin stream until  incorporated. Season with salt and pepper.
