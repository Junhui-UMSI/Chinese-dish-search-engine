Bean Sprouts with Tomatoes
Tomatoes; quartered, Yellow onion; quartered, Vegetable oil, Green Onions, Garlic, Cilantro; chopped, Soy bean paste, Bean sprouts, Red chili pepper; sliced, 
Heat the oil in a wok & stir-fry the garlic & bean paste for 1 minute. Add  the onion, bean sprouts, chili & tomatoes.  Cook for 2 minutes, stirring  frequently.  Garnish with green onions & cilantro.  Serve hot.    Date:    6/28/96 7:27 AM    From: dlassiter@atsva.com    MC-Recipe Digest V1 #133    From the MasterCook recipe list.
