Tajin Chicken
Canned Tomatoes -- cut up, Cloves Garlic -- minced, Lemon, Parsley -- snipped, Ground ginger, Thread Saffron -- crushed, Onions -- chopped, Ground Coriander -- .25, Olive With Pimento, Ground cumin, Broiler Chicken -- cut up, Salt, Cooking oil, Paprika, 
in a large skillet  cook onions , garlic , coriander , paprica ,cumin ,  ginger , safron , and salt in hot cooking oil until onion is tender but not  brown.  stir in chicken and undrained tomatoes. transfer mixture to a 3  quart casserole. cover; bake in a 375? oven 1.25 to hours or till chicken  is tender. skim off fat quarter lemon length wise and seed. stir lemon  parsly and olives into cassarole: heat through.
