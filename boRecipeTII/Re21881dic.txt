Apple-Stuffed Pork Chops
fresh parsley, Salt, Celery, Onion, Butter or margarine, apples, bread, Pepper, Pork loin, Cooking oil, 
In a skillet, saute onion in butter until tender. Remove from the heat; add  bread, apples, celery, parsley and 1/4 tsp salt. Cut a large pocket in the  side of each pork chop; sprinkle inside and outside with pepper and  remaining salt. Spon stuffing loosely into pockets. In a large skillet,  brown the chops on both sides in oil. Place in an ungreased large baking  pan. Cover and bake at 350? for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake 30 minutes  longer or until meat juices run very clear. Make gravy from pan juices if  desired.
