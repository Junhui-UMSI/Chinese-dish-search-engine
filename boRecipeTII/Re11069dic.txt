Rainbow Salad
Cucumber, Pepper, Vinegar, Cooked or canned green peas, Lettuce leaves, Sliced onion, Shredded American cheese, Chopped lettuce, Tomato chunks, Salt, Finely chopped green pepper, Sliced celery, Finely chopped cabbage, Sugar, Grated carrots, 
Toss all ingredients except lettuce leaves. Chill. Serve in lettuce-lined  salad bowl. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
