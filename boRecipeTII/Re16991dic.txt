Viennese Coffee
Sugar, Semisweet Chocolate, Orange Peel, Whipping cream, Hot Strong Coffee, Whipped cream, Makes 1-1/2 Quarts, 
Melt chocolate in a heavey saucepan over low heat. Stir in sugar and  whipping cream.  Beat in coffee with a whisk, 1/2 cup at a time; continue  to beat until frothy. Top with whipped cream and sprinkle with orange  peel.   From Sheila Buff & Judi Olstein, "The New Mixers Guide to Low-Alcohol and  Nonalcoholic Drinks." Published By HPBooks, Inc., 1986, ISBN 0-89586-458-4.
