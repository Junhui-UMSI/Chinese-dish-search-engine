A Christmas Surprises
Vanilla, Shortening, Flour, Baking soda, Any Flavor Jam Or Jelly, Salt, Coconut, Light brown sugar, Cream of tartar, Egg, 
Cream shortening and sugar together. Add egg and mix well. Sift dry  ingredients together and add. Add vanilla, and drop by teaspoons- full onto  ungreased cookie sheet. With your finger or a spoon, make an indentation in  the center of the cookie ball and fill with jam or jelly. Sprinkle coconut  over all and bake at 375F for 10-12 min.
