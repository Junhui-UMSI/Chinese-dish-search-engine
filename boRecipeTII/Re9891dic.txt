Punch #2
Lemonade; frozen, Soda water, Strawberries; frozen, Water (orange juice can), Water (limeade can), Water (lemonade cans), Limeade; frozen, Dry ginger ale, Sugar, Pineapple juice; unsweetened, Orange juice; frozen, 
Use large cans of pineapple juice. Mix the base of juices and sugar. Chill.  Add ginger ale and soda just before serving.    NOTE: Mint leaves may be substituted for the strawberries. This is a good  base for spiking with vodka or gin.    Taken from: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK A Collection of  Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce and Clem Kohl  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by valerie@nbnet.nb.ca (valerie) on Mar 11,  1998
