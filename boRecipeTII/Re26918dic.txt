Baked Brown Rice
Onion, Dehydrated parsley, Defatted beef broth, Brown rice, Mrs Dash, 
Brown the rice in a nonstick skillet. When golden brown, place in tightly  covered casserole. Pour over rice, hot defatted beef broth (mix as per  directions on can). Bake in a 350 degree oven for one hour. Garnish with  dehydrated parsley. Add more water if needed during cooking.    NOTES : *To remove fat, chill can of beef froth in refrigerator; lift off  congealed fat with spoon.
