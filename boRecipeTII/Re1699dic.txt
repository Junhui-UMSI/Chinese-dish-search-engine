Certified Angus Beef Marinades
PARISIAN PIZZAZZ:, Dry white wine, Dijon-style mustard, Vegetable oil, Lemon juice, Dried basil, MEDITERRANEAN MELODY:, olive oil, Lemon juice, garlic, Salt, Pepper, DELICIOUSLY BOLD:, Beef broth, Red wine vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Vegetable oil, Dried vegetable seasoning, SUNKISSED MARINADE:, frozen orange, Soy sauce, Light molasses, Ground ginger, 
These marinades will tenderize about 2 pounds of steak or a 3 to 4 pound  roast.    To marinate:  Combine ingredients.  Place beef in zipper-locking plastic  bag and pour marinade over beef. Close bag removing air; refrigerate;  marinate for about 15 minutes to 2 hours.    Note:  Extend marinating time to a minimum of 6 hours to tenderize.
