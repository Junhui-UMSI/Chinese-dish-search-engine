Wilted Cabbage
Shredded cabbage, Bacon, Onions, Vinegar, Pepper, Sugar, Salt, 
Cook bacon in large skillet until crisp; remove bacon, reserving drippings  in skillet. Crumble bacon and set aside. Add onion and next 4 ingredients,  stirring until blended. Stir in cabbage. Cook over medium heat, uncovered,  15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle with bacon. Yields 4-6  servings.
