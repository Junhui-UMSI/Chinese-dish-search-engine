Apple Yam Bake
Brown Sugar, Sweet potatoes or, Ground ginger, Apples, cored and peeled, Cinnamon, Pecans, 16 oz. yams/sweet potatoes, Butter, 
* cooked and cut into 1" slices. (Bake or peel and steam. Cool first before  slicing.    Preheat oven to 350 F    Toss apples, brown sugar, nuts and spices.  Alternate layers of apples and  yams in 9" square baking dish/pan. (Use what you have.) Dot with butter.    Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
