Taco Bake
(16 oz) whole kernel corn,, Eggs, Envelope taco seasoning mix, (16 oz) tomato sauce, Biscuit mix, Onion, Cheddar or process American, milk, Lean ground meat, 
Here are a few Biscuit recipes that came from" Betty Crocker, Bisquick  makes it Easy"    Heat oven to 325 degrees, and grease a rectangular pan, 13x9x2 inches Cook  ground beef and onion in 1- inch skillet, stirring frequently, until beef  is brown; drain. Stir in seasoning mix (dry), tomato sauce and corn. Spoon  into pan; sprinkle with cheese. Mix remaining ingredients until smooth.  Pour over beef mixture. Bake about 35 minutes or until knife inserted in  center comes out clean.Cool 10 minutes before cutting, Serve with sour  cream, chopped tomato and shredded lettuce if desired.
