The Best Ever Turnips (Mf)
Butter, Salt, garlic, 
Bring a lot of salted water to a boil and parboil the turnips for 7  minutes; add the garlic and boil 1 minute longer; drain.    Melt 8 to 12 tablespoons of butter and cook the garlic and turnips,  covered, over low heat for 5 minutes.    Transfer turnips and garlic to a food processor and puree until smooth,  adding 4 more tablespoons butter with the machine turned on. Season well  with salt and pepper and, if made in advance, reheat in a double boiler.
