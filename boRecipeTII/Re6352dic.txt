Planters Punch
Fresh Lemon Juice, Dark rum, Orange slice, Powdered sugar, Maraschino cherry, Orange juice, Cracked ice, Club soda, Grenadine, Ice cubes, 
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add rum, grenadine, orange juice,  lemon juice, and sugar. Shake and strain into collins glass filled with ice  cubes. Fill with club soda. Garnish with a cherry and orange slice.    Recipe By     : Joe Robertson
