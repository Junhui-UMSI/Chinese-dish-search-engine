Apple Cheesse Ball
1 cup shredded cheddar, Cheese, Cinnamon stick, halved, Finely chopped dried apples, Softened, Finely chopped nuts, Bay leaf, 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, Cinnamon, 
Directions;    In larage bowl, beat cream cheese, cheddar cheese and cinnamon until  well blended.  Stir in dried apples.  Shape mixture into ball; roll  in nuts. Inssert bay leaf and cinnamon stick on top to resemble stem  and leaf of apple.  Refrigerate till firm.  Can be stored in refrigerator up to 2 weeks.    source Pillsbury holiday baking cookbooks  submitted by marina  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #217    Date: Thu, 12 Sep 1996 07:03:33 -0700 (MST)    From: jmchee@goodnet.com (Marina Cheesman)
