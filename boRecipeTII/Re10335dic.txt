Quick Buttermilk Biscuits
Self-rising flour, Buttermilk, Butter or margarine, 
Cut 1/2 cup butter into flour with a pastry blender until mixture resembles  coarse meal. Add buttermilk, stirring until dry ingredients are moistened.  Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface, and knead lightly 3 or 4  times.    Roll dough to 3/4 inch thickness; cut with a 2 inch biscuit cutter. Place  on a lightly greased baking sheet.
