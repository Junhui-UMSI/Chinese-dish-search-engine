Prickly Pear Ice Cream
milk, Ripe cactus fruits; peeled, 
Mix mashed fruit and milk together in ice cream maker. Dont add sugar or  honey, since cactus fruit is sweet enough. Process according to  manufacturers instructions. For smoother ice cream, strain cactus fruits  through colander to remove seeds. Makes 1 gallon.  Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on Apr  23, 1998
