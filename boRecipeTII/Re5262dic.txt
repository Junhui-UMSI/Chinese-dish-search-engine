Pina Colada Bread Abm
Unsalted Butter; softened, Salt, Egg, Sugar, Unsweetened Flaked Coconut, Dried Pineapple; chopped, Coconut Milk; plus, Yeast, Ascorbic Acid, Bread flour, Coconut Milk; (if needed), Coconut Extract, 
Place all ingredients except coconut and pineapple in the machine. Program  for sweet or raisin bread. Add the coconut and pineapple when the Hitachi  timer reads 3:44.
