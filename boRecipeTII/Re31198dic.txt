Banana-Brownies, Lo Cal
Buttermilk, Flour; all purpose, Cocoa; unsweetened, Banana, Vanilla, Nonfat dry milk powder, Egg whites, Salt, Sugar, Baking soda, 
Preheat oven 350F. Coat 9 in. square baking pan with vegetable cooking  spray.  Combine flour, cocoa, milk powder, baking soda and salt in bowl.  Puree banana, sugar, egg whites, buttermilk and vanilla in food processor  until smooth.  Add dry ingredients and pulse just until blended. Pour into  prepared pan.  Bake 25 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Cut into        2    in. squares.
