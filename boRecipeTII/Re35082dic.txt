Bear Trap Ranch Sour Cream Apple Squares
Nuts, chopped, (opt), Flour, Vanilla, Apple, finely chopped, Cinnamon, Egg, Brown sugar, Margarine, Baking soda, Sour cream, imitation, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Combine first three ingredients and blend Stir in  nuts. Press 2-3/4 cups crumb mixture in 9 x 13 ungreased pan. Stir in  apples in remaining mix, spoon evenly over crumb "crust". Bake 35 - 45  mins. Cut in 12 - 15 squares. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream    NOTES : From -- Food Service Director, April 15, 1997    Recipe by: Bear Trap Ranch Colorado Springs, CO Posted to MC-Recipe Digest  V1 #613 by Thomas Vath <tgvath@airmail.net> on May 16, 97
