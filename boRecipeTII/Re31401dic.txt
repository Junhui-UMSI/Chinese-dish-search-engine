Barbaras Gazpacho
Cucumber, Fresh chopped parsley, Minced scallions, Tomato juice, Tarragon, Ripe tomato, Basil, Green pepper, Lime , juice of, Salt and pepper to taste, Lemon , juice of, Yellow onion, Red wine vinegar, olive oil, 
In food processor, mince green pepper and onion until they exude liquid.  Add to tomato juice. Chop cucumber and tomato. Add to tomato juice mixture.  Add all other ingredients. Mix well and chill for several hours. Serve VERY  cold. This is a refreshing soup to have on a hot summer day!!! Posted to  FOODWINE Digest 26 Jun 97 by =Mark <mstevens@EXIT109.COM> on Jun 27, 1997
