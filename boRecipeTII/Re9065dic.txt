Prize Winning Applesauce Brownies
Chocolate chips, Salt, pecans, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Eggs, Flour, Applesauce, natural sweet, Cocoa powder, Granulated sugar, 
In a large bowl, combine the applesauce and the sugar  and blend well. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Add  the vanilla and beat well. Add the flour and the cocoa  powder and beat well.Add the baking powder and the  salt and beat. Stir in the nuts and the chocolate  chips. Pour batter into a greased 13x9" baking pan and  Bake at 350 F for 30-35 minutes. Cool completely  before cutting.
