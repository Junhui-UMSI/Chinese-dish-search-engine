Carey's Sweet & Sour Sauce
Cornstarch, Water, Brown sugar, Rice vinegar, Orange juice, Ketchup, Lemon juice, Regular soy sauce, Ginger root, Hot chili oil, Hot sauce, 
Mix cornstarch and water. Add rest of ingredients. Bring to a boil.  Cook over medium heat until glossy and thickened.  May adjust some ingredients to your preference.
