Turtle Brownies
Salt, Caramels, Baking Powder, Eggs, Flour, Pecans, milk, Vanilla, Sugar, Shortening, Cocoa, Vanilla, 
Grease 9 x9 pan. Mix sugar, shortening, vanilla & eggs. Stir in flour,  cocoa, b. powder & salt.  Spread in pan. Sprinkle pecans over batter. Bake  at 350 for 20 - 25 min. or til pick comes out clean. Heat caramels & milk  over low heat, stirring frequently, til caramels are melted. Drizzle over  warm brownies. Cool completely.
