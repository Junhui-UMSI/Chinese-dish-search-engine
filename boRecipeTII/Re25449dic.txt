Tortilla Soup From El Paso
Tomato juice, Onion, Condensed beef bouillon, Water, Tomatoes, peeled and chopped, Salt, Worcestershire Sauce, Corn tortillas, cut in, Cheddar cheese, shredded, Pepper, Steak sauce, Cloves garlic, crushed, Vegetable oil, Condensed chicken broth, Ground cumin, Chile powder, Green chiles, chopped,, 
Saute onion, chiles and garlic in oil until soft. Add tomatoes, bouillon,  chicken broth, water, tomato juice, cumin, chile powder, salt, pepper,  Worcestershire and steak sauce. Bring soup to a boil; lower heat and simmer  covered 1 hour. Add tortillas and cheese and simmer 10 minutes longer.
