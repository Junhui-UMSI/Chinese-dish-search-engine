Baked Potato Soup
Baking potatoes, Butter, Flour, milk, Salt, Pepper, Scallion, Bacon, Cheddar, Sour cream, 
Bake potatoes. Cool. Scoop out pulp and set aside. Discard skins.   Melt  butter over low heat and add flour. Add milk, until thick. Add potato pulp,  salt, pepper, 2 tablespoons of green onion, 1/2 cup of crumbled bacon and  cup cheese. Cook until thoroughly heated; stir in sour cream and heat through.   Serve with remaining green onions, bacon and 1/4 cup greens as garnish.
