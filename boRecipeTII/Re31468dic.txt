Barbecue Sauce #10
Liquid smoke, Stick margarine, Brown sugar, Onion, Hot catsup, -(up to), Tabasco sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Tomato paste, Water (tomato paste can), 
saute onions in margarine until tender. Add other ingredients and bring to  a bubbling boil. To baste meat on a spit, dilute 1 cup sauce with 1/2 cup  hot water. Remainder is kept hot to serve with meal. (To use this sauce for  barbequed chicken or seafood, substitute 1/2 cup lemon juice for the  onions.)    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
