Quick Spinach with Sesame Seeds
Black Pepper, freshly groun, Spinach, small leafed, Garlic, Peanut or other nut oil, Sesame seeds, toasted, Lemon juice, Soy sauce, 
Rinse spinach carefully, but leave branches attached to roots. Drain  thoroughly and pat dry.  In small bowl, combine soy sauce, lemon juice, and  garlic. Heat oil in wok over high heat. Add spinach. Stir with pancake  turner to gently keep spinach moving. Cook about 1 minute, until tender.  Remove from heat. Sprinkle with soy mixture and toasted sesame seeds.  Season to taste with pepper.  Mix to blend.
