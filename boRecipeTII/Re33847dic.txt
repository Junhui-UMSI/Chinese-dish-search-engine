Sweet and Sour Red Cabbage
Salt, Cornstarch, Caraway seeds, Water, Cider vinegar, Fructose, red cabbage, 
Shred cabbage steam over rapidly boiling water 10 minutes, or until  crisp-tender.  Remove from heat thoroughly.  Dissolve cornstarch in  water; add remaining ingredients; cook over medium heat until  slightly thickened. Put this dish with either in large mixing bowl;  add sauce and mix well. Use this dish with either hot or cold meat.  NOTE: This recipe is provided through the courtesy of Sweet Lite  Fructose. (However, I would and shall use my sugar sub.)    Food Exchange per servings: 1/2 FRUIT EXCHANGE + 1 VEGETABLE EXCHANGE  Cal: 45    Source: Recipes for Diabetics by Billie Little.
