Wild Rice, Yellow Pepper, and Black-Eyed Pea Salad
Cooked wild rice, Dried rosemary, crumbled, Cooked or canned (drained, Fresh Lemon Juice, Red wine vinegar, Pepper, Salt, Chopped zucchini, Chopped fresh parsley, Bell pepper, Chopped jicama, Cherry tomatoes, quartered, Dijon mustard, And rinsed) black-eyed peas, Chopped yellow (or green), Garlic, 
In a large bowl, combine the wild rice, black-eyed peas, bell pepper,  jicama, zucchini, tomatoes, and parsley.    In a small bowl, stir together the lemon juice, vinegar, mustard, garlic,  salt, rosemary, and pepper.  Pour over the salad and toss.
