#1 Lemon Bars
Eggs, powdered sugar, Flour, Granulated sugar, Lemon juice, butter, All-purpose flour, Powdered sugar, 
In bowl combine flour, sugar and butter.  Pat into 9X13 pan.  Bake for 20  minutes in a 350F oven.  While it is baking, whisk together eggs, 3 TB  flour, sugar and lemon juice.  Pour over crust while crust is still hot.  Bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until top is golden brown. Cool on  a wire rack and dust with additional powdered sugar.
