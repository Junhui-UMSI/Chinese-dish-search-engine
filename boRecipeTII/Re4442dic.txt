Pesto and Noodle Salad
Salt, Cloves garlic -- minced, olive oil, Dried basil, Parmesan cheese -- grated, Cherry tomatoes, Red wine vinegar, Pepper, Sugar, Bow-tie noodles, Parsley -- chopped, 
1. Cook noodle bows according to package directions. Drain, rinse with cold  water, and drain again.    2. In blender or food processor combine vinegar, basil, parsley (if fresh  basil is used, omit parsley), garlic, salt, sugar, cheese, and olive oil.  Whirl or process until smooth and well combined.    3. Lightly mix noodle bows and dressing. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour  or longer to blend flavors. Serve sprinkled with pepper and garnished with  cherry tomatoes.    Recipe By     : the California Culinary Academy    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
