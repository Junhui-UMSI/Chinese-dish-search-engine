Pizza Monster
Boboli Italian Bread Shell, Hormel Pre-Sliced Pepperoni, Shredded Mozzarella Cheese, Boboli Pizza Sauce Pouch, Green pepper strips, Black olive, Mushrooms, 
Spread Boboli Pizza Sauce on Boboli Italian Bread Shell. Place Hormel  Pepperoni Slices on Sauce. Sprinkle with cheese. Use scissors to cut  Hormel Pepperoni and other ingredients to create monster face for your  Pizza Moster. Bake on ungreased baking sheet at 450F. for 8-10  minutes.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
