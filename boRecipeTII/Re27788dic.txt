The Sanctuarys Cumin Carrots
Ground cumin; or to taste, Carrots; peeled and, Granulated sugar, Prepared horseradish;, Fresh cilantro; optional, Water, Salt and pepper, Whipping cream, 
In a skillet, combine carrots, water and sugar; cover and simmer until most  of the water is evaporated and carrots are tender-crisp. Add cumin, cream,  horseradish, and salt and pepper to taste; simmer just until carrots are  coated. Add cilantro for garnish.    Recipe by: The Sanctuary Restaurant, Chinook, Washington    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #893 by LBotsko@aol.com on Nov 9, 1997
