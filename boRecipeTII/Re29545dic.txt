Tart Tomatillo Relish
Husked tomatillos; cut in, Salt, Red bell pepper; seeded and, Garlic clove; peeled and, Yellow bell pepper; seeded, Seeded jalapenos; cut in, Peeled shallots; cut in 1/8", Maple syrup; to taste, Juice of 4 large limes, 
Mix all ingredients together, then check seasoning.
