Pueblo Pinto Bean Soup
Black pepper, Ground coriander, Sliced dry or fresh red, Onion, salt and pepper, Garlic, Cubed cooked ham, (15 oz) pinto beans, Corn; (optional), (16 oz) chicken broth, 
In a large skillet or dutch oven, saute onion in oil or butter until clear.  Stir in all other ingredients. Simmer on medium heat, stirring occasionally  until flavors are well blended, about 35 minutes.    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Dan and Mary <4christ@bellsouth.net> on Mar  26, 1998
