Fried Squid
Squid; cleaned, milk, Eggs, Bread crumbs, Salt/pepper, olive oil, Marinara or cocktail sauce, 
Cut squid in 1"" rings. Combine eggs and milk. Combine salt/pepper and  bread crumbs. Dip squid in egg mixture, then dredge through bread crumb  mixture. Let coated squid stand while oil heats. Fry 8-10 pieces ata a time  in olive oil until light brown. Remove with slotted spoon, let drain on  paper towels. Serve with sauce for dipping.
