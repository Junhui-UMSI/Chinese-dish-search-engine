Arnauds Salad Dressing for Shrimp
Chopped sweet onion, Creole mustard, Salt, Red wine vinegar, Heart of celery, Virgin olive oil, Paprika, Parsley, 
Mix all and marinate cold cooked shrimp for several hours.    Recipe by: Courtesy of Arnauds Restaurant, New Orleans    Posted to recipelu-digest by Dogface 44 <Dogface44@aol.com> on Mar 17, 1998
