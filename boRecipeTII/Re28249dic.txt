Baked Flounder with Sauce
Lemon, Flounder, Parsley, Salt and pepper to taste, Flour, Onions, Butter, Dry white wine, garlic, 
From: Wendy Lockman <wlockman@ra1.randomc.com>    Date: Sun, 11 Feb 1996 10:35:49 -0500    Place fish in baking dish or pan. Prepare a sauce by browning garlic, onion  in melted butter. When brown, add parsley, wine and lemon juice. Blend  sauce. Pour sauce over fish; sprinkle with salt, pepper, flour. Bake  covered 10 min. at 375 Degrees. Uncover, basting with melted butter and dry  white wine. Bake (ten minutes more or until done to taste. Also for:  Flounder, mackerel, weakfish, grouper, snapper, bass etc. Suggestions: Vary  the ingredients of the sauce with other recipes.
