Quick Coconut Bread
Currents or raisins, Flour, Grated unsweetened coconut, Canned or fresh coconut milk, Or milk, Baking Powder, Salt, Vanilla extract, Sugar, Egg, (optional), Butter/margarine, melted, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Sift dry ingrediants into mixing bowl,  blend in egg, milk, butter and vanilla. Mix in coconut and  currents/raisins if used. Turn out onto floured board and knead  slightly. Shape into 2 loaves and pat into 2 buttered med.  loaf  pans. Bake 40-45 min., until a toothpick in the center comes out  clean.
