Wheat Mix
Baking Powder, Salt, Whole-wheat flour, Instant Non Fat Dry Milk, Sugar, Wheat germ, Unbleached all-purpose flour, Vegetable shortening, 
In a large bowl, combine whole-wheat flour, unbleached flour, dry milk,  salt, wheat germ, sugar, and baking powder.  Mix well.  With a pastry  blender, cut in shortening until evenly distributed.  Put in a large  airtight container.  Label as WHEAT MIX and store in cool, dry, place.  Use within 10 to 12 weeks.  Makes about 14 Cups WHEAT MIX.    From "Make-A-Mix Cookery" by Karine Eliason, Nevada Harward & Madeline  Westover. Dedicated to memory of Freddi Michael from SYSOP of The Home BBS.
