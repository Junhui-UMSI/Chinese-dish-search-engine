Raspberry-Pear Crisp
Lemon Rind, Dried currants, Cinnamon, Fresh raspberries, Sliced pears, such as Bosc, Cardamom, Lemon juice, Melted unsalted butter, Nutmeg, Maple syrup, Light honey, Safflower oil, Cornstarch, Arrowroot powder or, Uncooked rolled oats, 
1. Lightly oil a 9- by 12-inch baking pan and arrange pear slices in bottom  of pan. Cover with raspberries, then currants. Mix together maple syrup,  arrowroot, lemon juice, and rind, and drizzle over pear mixture. Preheat  oven to 375 degrees F.    2. In a large bowl combine oats, butter, honey, nutmeg, cinnamon, and  cardamom. Crumble over pear mixture. Bake crisp until pears are tender and  oats are lightly browned (30 to 40 minutes). Serve warm.
