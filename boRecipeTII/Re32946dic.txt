Sweet Tater Pie
Evaporated milk, Cinnamon, Lemon extract, Eggs, Mashed cooked sweet taters, Salt, Unbaked pie crust, Sugar, 
Scald the milk and mix everything together.  Pour into the unbaked ie shell  and bake at 425 degrees for 20 minutes, reduce to 350 degrees and continue  another 20 or so minutes.  Make sure the crust is good and brown. Cool  before serving.  Its best warm.    Cookbook:  Sinkin Spells, Hot Flashes, Fits and Cravins by Ernest Matthew  Mickler.
