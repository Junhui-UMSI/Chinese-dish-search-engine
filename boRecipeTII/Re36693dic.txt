Strawberry Dip
Cool whip Lite?; thawed, Fresh strawberries; diced, Lowfat strawberry yogurt, Orange Peel, 
Frozen thawed straberries can be used in place of fresh strawberries. Mix  topping, yogurt, strawberries and orange peel until well mixed. Spoon into  serving bowl. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Serve with assorted sliced  fresh fruit.(strawberries, grapes, kiwi, pineapple)    Source Cool Whip Light package.
