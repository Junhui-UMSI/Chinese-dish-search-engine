Tossed Salad W Red Roquefort
Black pepper, Ketchup, Onion, Mayonnaise, RED ROQUEFORT DRESSING, endive, Seasonal vegetables, Paprika, Red wine vinegar, Iceberg lettuce, Celery seed, Dijon mustard, Salt, Romaine lettuce, Sugar, 
Remove core from iceberg lettuce; hold under cold running water until  leaves separate. Wash romaine and endive. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour  before serving. Tear into bite-size pieces. Toss with Red Roquefort  Dressing. Garnish with seasonal vegetables of your choice. Directions for  Red Roquefort Dressing: Put all ingredients except cheese in mixing bowl.  Stir with paddle until combined. Add crumbled cheese and stir lightly.  Refrigerate. Note: Dressing will keep several weeks in refrigerator.    JEFFERSON AVE.
