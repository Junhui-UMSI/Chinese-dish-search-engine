Vanilla Brandy
Half lenght, Brandy, Vanilla Bean -- split in, 
Pour Brandy into 1 pint glass jar with tight fitting lid.  Add vanilla  bean. Seal jar and store for a week in a cool dark dry place. --To Serve  Stir brandy to taste into yogurt and serve with fresh fruit or use in other  vanilla recipes as called for.
