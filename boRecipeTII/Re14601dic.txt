"Long Boy" Cheeseburgers
Catsup, Crushed cornflakes, Loaves brown n serve French, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, (4oz) shredded cheddar, Lengthwise, Cheese, Bread, cut in half, Onions, Whipping cream, Salt, Xtra lean ground beef, 
Preheat oven to 350F.    Combine meat, seasonings, cornflakes and cream. Spread to edges of bread  and bake about 25 minutes until meat is cooked. Sprinkle with cheese during  last 5 minutes of cooking. Cut into slices.    Taste of Home Ground Beef Collection 1996 Edition
