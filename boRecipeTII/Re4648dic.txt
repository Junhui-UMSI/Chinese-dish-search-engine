Pheasant or Grouse over Charcoal
Vinegar, Tabasco sauce, Onion, Vegetable oil, Pepper, Garlic, Wine, white, Worchestershire sauce, Sugar, Salt, Paprika, 
Marinate the pheasant pieces in the above sauce for 2-3 hours.    Turn pieces of meat in sauce once or twice. If birds are apparently old and  tough, let them simmer in marinade sauce 1/2 hour.    Remove meat from sauce and place on charcoal grill. Brush the meat  frequently with marinade sauce until deep brown and well done. Dont place  charcoal briquets closer than 1 inch abart or the fire will be too hot.
