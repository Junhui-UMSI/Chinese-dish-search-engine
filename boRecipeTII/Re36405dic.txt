Beer Biscuits
Baking Powder, Unbleached flour, Salt, Beer, Shortening, 
Preheat Oven to 450 degrees F.  Sift dry ingredients together.  Cut in  shortening until it has cornmeal consistency.  Stir in beer, knead lightly,  roll out to 1/2-inch thickness.  Bake 10 - 12 minutes or until golden  brown.  Makes 12 to 15 biscuits.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/breads1.zip
