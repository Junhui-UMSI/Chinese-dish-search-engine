Potato Ratatouille (Lf)
Zucchini, Fresh parsley, Water, *gm fiber, pepper, PER SERVING, Potatoes, Garlic, Green peppers, *cals, *gm carbo, Fresh oregano, *mg sodium, Yellow onions, tomatoes, *gm protein, *gm fat, Fresh basil, 
Place onions and garlic in a large pot with water.   Cook and stir for about 3 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients, except  pepper. Cover and cook over medium heat for 30 minutes, stirring  occasionally. Season with pepper before serving.
