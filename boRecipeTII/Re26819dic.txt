Baked Asparagus
Salt, Fresh asparagus, 
Break off and discard the tough ends of the asparagus. Peel the stalks.  Rinse the asparagus, drain it well, and wrap it in a large sheet of foil,  crimping the edges tightly to seal the package. Bake in a preheated hot  oven (425?) for 25 to 30 minutes or until done to taste. Season to taste  with salt. Calories: 38 per serving. Yield: 6 servings.    LOW CALORIE    BEN HUSSMAN    (MRS.
