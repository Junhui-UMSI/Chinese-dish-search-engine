Banana Bread #02
Chopped pecans or walnuts;, -(up to), Eggs, Sugar, Very ripe bananas, Sifted unbleached flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Shortening, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.  Beat shortening in mixer until creamy consistency.  Add sugar and eggs,  continue beating at medium speed one minute.  Add bananas to egg mixture.  Mix until blended. Add flour mixture, beating at LOW speed about 30 seconds  or *only* until blended. Turn into buttered loaf pan and bake for one  hour...or until broom straw inserted into center of loaf comes out clean.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
