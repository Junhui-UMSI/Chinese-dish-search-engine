Autumn Punch
Whole allspice, Cloves, Whole cardamom seeds, Apple cider, Honey, Lemon juice, 3-inch sticks cinnamon, Crushed ice, Apricot nectar, Ginger ale, Cranberry juice, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in a saucepan; bring to a boil, reduce heat,  and simmer 10 minutes.  Strain and discard spices.  Chill. Combine chilled  mixture with remaining juices and ginger ale.  Serve over crushed ice.  Yield: 7-1/2 quarts.
