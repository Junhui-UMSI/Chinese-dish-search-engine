"First" Ginger Molasses Cookies
Cinnamon, Baking soda, Molasses, Sugar for rolling, Brown sugar, Flour, Min - Work Time, Unsalted Butter (marg/short), Ground ginger, Egg, Hr + chilling - Total Time, Salt, At room temp, 
Combine flour, spices, soda, & salt. With an electric mixer at  medium-low speed, beat butter & sugar, until light & fluffy. Beat in  egg & molasses. Reduce speed to low, & gradually add flour mixture  until just mixed. Chill until firm, about 1 hour. Heat oven to 350~.  Shape dough into approximately 1" balls, roll in sugar, & place about  2 " apart on a baking sheet. Bake until the edges begin to brown,  about 15 min. Cool on baking sheet 2 min, then move to wire racks.  Cool completely.
