Whole Wheat Banana Nut Bread
Honey, bananas, whole wheat flour, Eggs, pecans, Vegetable oil, Baking soda, Hot water, Salt, 
(Whole foods for the whole Family)    Sift dry ingredients together.  Cream oil and honey in large bowl.  Add  eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in bananas. Add  dry ingredients alternately with hot water, mixing well after each  addition. Fold in pecans. Pour into a greased 5x9-inch loaf pan. Bake at  325F for 55 to 60 minutes. Invert onto wire rack to cool completely before  slicing.  Freezes well. Yield: 12 servings.    APPROX PER SERVING:  (If your concerned about this kind of stuff) Cal 218;  Prot 4 g; Carbo 30 g; Fiber 3 g; T Fat 11 g; 42% Cal from Fat; Chol 35 mg;  Sod      169    mg.
