Baptist Pound Cake
Butternut flavoring, Stick margarine, Eggs, rm temp, Baking Powder, Pinch of salt, Crisco, Canilla, milk, Sugar, Plain flour, 
Cream together sugar, butter, and shortening; add eggs, one at a time. Mix  well after each. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt; add alternately with  milk and flour to first mixture. Add flavorings. Grease and flour pan.  Place in cold over. Turn heat to 350F, and bake 1/2 hour. then turn heat to  325F and bake 40 minutes more. Let stand 5 minutes after taking from oven.
