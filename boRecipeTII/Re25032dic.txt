Tomato Sauce for Moussaka
Vegetable oil, Clove garlic,, Can tomato sauce, Dried thyme leaves, Salt, Sugar, Onion, Can tomato paste minus, Pepper, Dried rosemary leaves, 
1. Place oil and onions in a deep, 1 1/2-quart, heat-resistant,  non-metallic casserole. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 3 minutes or  until onions are tender. 2. Add remaining ingredients; stir to combine  well. Heat, covered, in Microwave Oven 6 minutes or until sauce bubbles.
