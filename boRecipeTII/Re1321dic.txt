Double-Ginger Scones
All-purpose flour, Brown sugar, Crystallized ginger; finely, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Ground ginger, Salt, Unsweetened applesauce, Butter or margarine; cut up, Lowfat vanilla yogurt, Golden raisins, Egg white; lightly beaten, Sugar, 
Heat oven to 400. Lightly coat large baking sheet with nonstick cooking  spray. Combine first 7 ingredients in a large bowl. Add applesauce and  butter; mix with fingers or fork until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Stir in yogurt and raisins. Turn dough out onto floured surface. Gather  into a ball. Pat into 9-inch circle. Using sharp knife coated with nonstick  cooking spray, cut into 12 equal wedges. Arrange wedges on prepared baking  sheet, 2 inches apart. Brush with egg white; sprinkle with sugar. Bake in  400 oven for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown. Remove to a wire rack to  cool slightly. Serve warm or at room temp.
