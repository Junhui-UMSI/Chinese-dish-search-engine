Basil Jicama Fruit Salad
Basil, fresh; chopped OR, Basil, dried; crushed, Grapes, green, seedless, Basil springs, optional, Strawberries; halved, Jicama; peeled, cut in, Cantaloupe, honeydew or, Orange juice, Grapes, red, seedless, Pineapple chunks, fresh, Nectarine; cubed, Orange; peeled, sliced and,
