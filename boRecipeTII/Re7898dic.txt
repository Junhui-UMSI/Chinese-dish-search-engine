Possum on the Half Shell (Armadillo)
Lemon juice (to taste), Margarine (1 stick), Salt (to taste), Pepper (to taste), Armadillo meat, 
Season meat with salt, pepper and lemon juice; rub with margarine. Wrap in  foil; bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes. Remove foil; add more butter and  brown.
