Radicchio and White Bean Salad
Red wine vinegar, Seeded and minced, Garlic, Grainy mustard, To taste, 6-oz head radicchio, leaves, Finely minced parsley, Black Pepper, Drained again, Coarsely chopped, Minced shallot, Red bell pepper, stemmed,, Salt, Beans, drained, rinsed and, Separated, shredded, Kalamata olives, pitted,, olive oil, 15-oz cans small white, 
1. Put the shallot into a small bowl and cover with water. Let sit 10  minutes to reduce the acidity. Drain and press with paper towel to remove  excess moisture.    2. Combine the shallots with the drained white beans. Stir in the  radicchio, bell pepper, parsley and olives.    3. Put the vinegar, garlic, mustard, olive oil, salt and several grindings  black pepper into a small jar. Shake to blend. Pour over the salad,  stirring to blend. Serve at room temperature.
