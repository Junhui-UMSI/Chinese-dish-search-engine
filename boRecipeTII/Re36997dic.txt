Beet and Walnut Salad
Feta or goat cheese, Walnuts, Beets; cooked and peeled, Basic vinaigrette dressing, Tart apples; peeled & cored, Thinly sliced scallions, 
Cube the beets and the apples and mix together in a bowl wiht the cheese,  the walnuts and the scallions. Drizzle dressing over and stir to coat.  Serve on a bed of butterhead lettuce.    From: The Cooks Garden catalog, Spring/Summer 1993 (page 88)
