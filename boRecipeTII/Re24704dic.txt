Atomic Barbeque Sauce
Honey, Brown sugar, Black pepper, Salt, Chili powder, Crushed red pepper, Garlic powder, cider vinegar, Fresh lime juice, Tamarind paste, Onion, Catsup, Orange juice, Dry mustard, Tabasco, Sticks butter, 
Add the onion and 1/2 cup of the juice to a blender and puree until smooth.  Transfer to a medium-size saucepan. Add the remaining ingredients to the  saucepan and bring to a boil. Simmer for 25 to 30 minutes. Set aside to  cool. Sauce keeps two weeks in fridge.
