Pumpkin Bread #03
Nutmeg, Pecans, Shredded coconut, Flour, White raisins, Sugar, Cinnamon, Salt, Vegetable oil, Eggs, Water, Baking soda, Canned pumpkin, 
Sift together first six ingredients; add pumpkin, oil & water. Add eggs one  at a time, beating well after each addition. Add remaining ingredients &  pour into two greased & floured loaf pans. Bake for 1 hour at 350. Test for  doneness.    MRS G.M. (CARA) HAMSCHER    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
