Almond Toffee Bars
Confectioners sugar, almonds, Sweetened condensed milk, Egg, Milk chocolate covered, Almond extract, flour, margarine, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees (325 if using glass dish)    2. In a medium bowl, combine flour and icing sugar, cut in margarine until  crumbly. Press firmly on the bottom of 13X9 in baking pan. Bake 15 minutes.    3. Meanwhile, in a large bowl combine condensed milk, egg, and almond  extract    Recipe by: Borden Fast & Fabulous    Posted to brand-name-recipes by RecipeLu <recipelu@geocities.com> on Mar  16, 1998
