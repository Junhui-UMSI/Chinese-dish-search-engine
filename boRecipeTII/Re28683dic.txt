Texas Pecan Pie
See below, 
Beat 6 Tbls. butter, 1 cup sugar, 1 egg, 1/2 tsp. vanilla together  until fluffy.  Mix 1 1/2 cups cake flour, 1 tsp. baking soda, 1/2  tsp. salt together & add alternately with 1/2 cup sourdough starter,  blending after each addition. Blend in 1/2 cup milk. Pour into  buttered 8 1/2" tube pan. Bake in 350 oven for 35 minutes. Remove  from pan & cool. Pecan glaze: Combine 3/4 cup light brown sugar, 6  Tbls. cornstarch, 1/4 tsp. salt in saucepan; stir in 3/4 cup water.  Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens & boils; boil 1  minute. Add 2 Tbls. butter. Cool mixture until spreadable. Cover with  whole pecans.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
