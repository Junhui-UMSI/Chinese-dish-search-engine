Chicken Chow Mein Bake
Mayonnaise or salad dressing, Green onions; sliced, Flour, Soy sauce, Chopped cooked chicken or, (16 oz) fancy-mixed chinese, Chow Mein Noodles, 
"Stolen" from Better Homes and Gardens New Casserole Cookbook    In a 1-qt. casserole dish, combine mayonnaise or salad dressing, green  onions, flour, and soy sauce. Fold in chicken {or turkey}, and vegetables.  Bake, covered, in a 350 degree oven about 30 minutes, or till heated  through. Sprinkle with chow mein noodles and bake uncovered, 5 minutes  more.
