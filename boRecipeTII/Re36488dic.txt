Strata-Various Pie
Eggs, Ham, sliced, Spinach, frozen, Eggs, Cheese, sliced, Butter, Pie crust, Garlic cloves, crushed, Onions, chopped, 
In a large frypan, saute onion and garlic in butter. Add spinach, cover and  cook until spinach softens. Drain very well; set aside. Whisk together 3  eggs and 1 egg white. In one pie shell layer half the meat, cheese, and  spinach; cover with half the beaten egg mixture. Repeat layers. Invert  second pie shell over top; crimp edges. Brush top with remaining egg yolk  mixed with a bit of water; slit surface. Bake pie on cookie sheet 30 - 35  minutes or until golden. Serve warm or cool.
