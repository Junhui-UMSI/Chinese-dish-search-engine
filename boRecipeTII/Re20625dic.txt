Apple Corn Bread (Vegan)
Vegetable oil, Orange Marmalade, Brown sugar, Baking Powder, Milk Alternative, Apple, Cider vinegar, Cornmeal, Flour, 
* i.e. Plain Rice Milk, Lite Soy Milk, Almond Milk, etc.    Mix all ingredients in order listed. Put one half of the batter in a  greased 8 or 9 inch square baking pan. Evenly top batter with 1/2 cup jam  and top with remaining batter.    Bake at 400 F for 25 minutes. Do not overbake. Allow to cool slightly  before cutting    Recipe By     : rolyat@main.citynet.net    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
