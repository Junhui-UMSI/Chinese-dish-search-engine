Wyoming Sheepherder Chili
Oregano, Dried onions, Stewed tomatoes, Ground beef, Vinegar, Tomato sauce, Water as necessary, Chili powder, Black pepper, Garlic powder, Cumin, Worcestershire, Cayenne pepper, Salt, 
Brown the meat in a heavy pot, drain off the fat and add all the other  ingredients. Simmer for at least 2 hours, adding water as needed. Thicken  with flour if desired (preferably Masa Harina or ground-up corn tortillas).  Serve over rice.
