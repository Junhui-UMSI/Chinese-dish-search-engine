Ranch Salad Dressing (Vitasoy)
Silken tofu* *from 10.5 to, Soy OR dairy milk, SALAD DRESSING MIX, Ranch dressing packet 1-oz, 
Put the soymilk, silken tofu and dressing mix into a blender container.  Whirl until well blended, scraping down sides if necessary. Put dressing  into a jar and store in the refrigerator.    (Yield: 2-1/2 cups (10 servings). Serving size: 1/4 cup.
