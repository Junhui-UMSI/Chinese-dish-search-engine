Bacon Walnut and Feta Focaccia
Or pizza crust, Minced, (6-to-7-inch plain focaccia, Crumbled feta cheese, Strips bacon, Walnuts, Green onion, green part only, 
Fry bacon in skillet until crisp.  Drain on paper towels. Crumble and  arrange on focaccia.  Sprinkle with onion, walnuts and feta cheese. Place  on baking sheet. Bake at 400 degrees until cheese melts and ingredients are  hot, 6 to 10 minutes.  Serve hot.
