Potato Cake
Nutmeg, Butter or margarine, Eggs, grated, Walnuts, Unsweetened Chocolate, C and H Granulated Sugar, Cinnamon, Cold mashed potatoes, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, milk, 
Cream together sugar and butter.  Add eggs one at a time beating well  after each addition.  Add mashed potatoes, chocolate and nuts.  Stir until  well blended.  Combine flour, baking powder and spices.  Add alternately  with milk to creamed mixture.  Spoon thick batter into greased and floured  13 x 9-inch pan.  Bake in 350 F degree oven 45 minutes or until cake tests  done.  Cool.  Sprinkle with C & H Powdered Sugar or dollop with sweetened  whipped cream.  1 cake.  12 servings.
