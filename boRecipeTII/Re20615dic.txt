Apple Coffee Cake #4
Oil; salad, Non-stick spray (or butter);, Granulated sugar, Nuts; chopped, Cinnamon, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Coffee, Apples; peeled and sliced, Vanilla extract, Salt, Eggs; large, 
Date:    Fri, 14 Jun 1996 11:44:22 -0500    From:    TeAntae Turner <tturner@GRAPHSOFT.COM>    Source: The Disney Institute, From Studio Bakery You will need a bundt cake  pan (or other baking pan).  Mix all ingredients together in a bowl. Grease  the cake pan or use non-stick spray. Pour mixture into the pan. Bake at  350F for 60 minutes.
