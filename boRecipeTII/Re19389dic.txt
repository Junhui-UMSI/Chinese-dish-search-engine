Vegetables Cancun
(14.5-oz) tomatoes; cut up,, Parkay margarine; melted, (17-oz) whole kernel corn;, Zucchini; cut into 1/2-inch, Dried basil leaves; crushed, Velveeta Mexican pasteurized, Onion; sliced, 
Stir-fry zucchini and onions in margarine until crisp-tender.  Stir in corn  and tomatoes; heat thoroughly, stirring occasionally.  Reduce heat. Add  remaining ingredients; stir over low heat until Velveeta cheese spread is  melted.  6 servings.
