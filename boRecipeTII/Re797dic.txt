Hawaiian Flours Splendiferous Chocolate Dessert Bread
Betty Crocker Super Moist, Double Chocolate cake mix, All purpose flour, White bread flour, Whole wheat bread flour, Gluten flour, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Swirl powder *, Yeast, Egg, Liquid, Water, Mandarin oranges, walnuts, Chocolate chips **, Coconuts, Maraschino cherries, 
* Swirl powder comes in a separate envelope with the cake mix. ** Use  semi-sweet chips     Adjust the amounts of fruit, nuts, and chips to suit.    Pat oranges and cherries dry between double layers of paper towels. Add  fruits, nuts, and chips, at the beep or near the very end of the second  kneading.    Variations:  Add more fruit.  Use some juice from the oranges in place of  water portion of liquid.    Serve plain as a snack or dessert, or, add your favorite topping for added  enjoyment.  HINT:  Cut into extra thick slices.
