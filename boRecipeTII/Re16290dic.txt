Watermelon Gazpacho
Field cucumber, Cayenne pepper, Cayenne pepper, Watermelon, Salt and pepper, Jalapeno pepper, Red onion, 
Seed watermelon, cut into chunks and puree in food processor until smooth.  Discard rind.  Finely chop onion, red pepper and jalapeno pepper.  Peel  and seed cucumber, chop into cubes.  In large bowl, combine watermelon and  vegetables.  Season with salt, pepper and cayenne.  Chill 2 hours, serve.  Source:  Toronto Star Newspaper, July 2nd, 1996.
