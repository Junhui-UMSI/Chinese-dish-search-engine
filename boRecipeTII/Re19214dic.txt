Vegetable Pizza
PHILADELPHIA BRAND Cream, Green Onions, Sliced pitted ripe olives, KRAFT Natural Shredded, Italian seasoning, Chopped radishes, Chopped red pepper, MIRACLE WHIP or MIRACLE WHIP, Refrigerated crescent, 
Heat oven to 375F. Unroll dough into 2 rectangles. Press onto bottom and  1/4" up sides of 13x9" baking pan to form crust. Bake 10 minutes; cool. Mix  cream cheese, dressing and seasoning until well blended. Spread over crust.  Top with remaining ingredients. Refrigerate. Cut into squares.    PREP TIME: 20 minutes plus refrigerating.
