Pepper Cream Dressing
Whole green peppercorns, Whipping cream, White wine vinegar, Mayonnaise, salt and pepper, 
You can purchase green peppercorns packed in vinegar at gourmet food stores  and some supermarkets. MIX ALL INGREDIENTS TOGETHER. Refrigerate at least  one hour before serving to allow flavors to develop. Makes 1 1/2 Cups
