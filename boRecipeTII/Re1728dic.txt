Cheese Tortilla Roll Ups
Soft cream cheese with chive, And bacon, 6 or 8 in tortilla, Smoked cooked ham, American cheese, 
Spread one tablespoon cream cheese on each tortilla.  Top with two slices  of ham and cheese.  Roll up tightly.  Wrap each roll securely in plastic  wrap.  Refrigerate.    Cut each roll into six pieces, secure each slice by running toothpick  through middle.    Serve with chunky salsa.
