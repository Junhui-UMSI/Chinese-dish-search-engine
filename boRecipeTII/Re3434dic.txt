Pecan Cheesecake
Butter, Pecans, Whipped cream, light brown sugar, Eggs, Plus, chopped pecans, Sugar, Vanilla extract, cream cheese, Pecan halves, Graham cracker crumbs, 
From: llburnet@lesley.b23b.ingr.com (Lesley Burnette)    Date: Tue, 12 Oct 1993 15:36:19    From: Southern Living    Combine first 3 ingredients, mixing well. Press into bottom of 10-inch  springform pan; chill. Beat cream cheese with electric mixer until light  and fluffy; gradually add brown sugar, mixing well. Add eggs, one at a  time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla and 1 cup chopped  pecans. Spoon into prepared pan. Bake at 325 for 1 hour. Turn oven off;  allow cheesecake to cool in oven 30 minutes. Let cool to room temperature;  refrigerate 8 hours. Remove sides of springform pan. If desired, garnish  with whipped cream, top with additional chopped pecans and pecan halves,  and gently press additional chipped pecans onto sides of cake. Yield: 12  servings.
