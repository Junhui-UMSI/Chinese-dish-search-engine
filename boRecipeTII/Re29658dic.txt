Tater Dipped Veggies
Instant potato flakes, ranch salad, Butter, Parmesan cheese, Veggies, Celery salt, Eggs, Garlic powder, 
In a small bowl, combine potatoe flakes, cheese, celery salt, garlic and  butter. In another bowl, beat eggs. Dip veggies, one at a time, into egg,  then in potato mixture - coat well. Place on an ungreased baking sheet.  Bake 400F for 20-25 minutes. Serves 6-8. Serve with dressing or dip, if  desired.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #386 by sewin <hammer@imag.net> on Jan 24,  1997.
