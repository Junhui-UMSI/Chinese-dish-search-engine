Apple Crumb Pie
Ground cinnamon, Apple slices and mint sprig;, Apple pie filling; (21 oz), Pecans, Egg white; slightly beaten, Margarine, Honey Graham Pie Crust, All-purpose flour, Sugar, 
Brush pie crust with egg white. Bake at 375F for 5 minutes; set aside.  Spoon pie filling into pie crust. In small bowl, combine flour, sugar and  cinnamon. Cut in margarine until mixture is crumbly; stir in pecans  Sprinkle over pie filling. Bake at 375F for 25 to 30 minutes or until  topping is golden brown. Cool slightly on wire rack. Serve warm, sprinkled  with confectioners sugar if desired. Garnish with apple slices and mint  sprig.
