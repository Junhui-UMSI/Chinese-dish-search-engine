Venison Tongue Wine Sauce
Salt, Dried parsley flakes, Prepared mustard, Butter or margarine, melted, Dry white wine, Sour cream, 
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan. Cook over low heat until sauce  is warm, stirring constantly.    Yield: 1 cup.
