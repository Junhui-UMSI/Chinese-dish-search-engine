Almond Pound Cake
Salt, Sugar, Large eggs, 1 cup, Shortening, Small can evaporated milk, (3/4 cup) + water to make, Almond extract, Sticks butter, Flour, 
Cream sugar and shortening. Add eggs and salt, cream well. Add remaining  ingredients. Pour into a very lightly greased 12 cup bundt pan. Put into  cold oven; set temperature at 320F. Bake 1 hour and 30 minutes. Cool 15  minutes and remove from pan.
