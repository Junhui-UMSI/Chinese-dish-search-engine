Sweet and Sour Meatball Appetizer
Salt, Canned chili without beans, Lean ground beef, Grape jelly, Water, Breadcrumbs, Onion; grated, Egg, Black Pepper, Lemon, 
Combine first 7 ingredients and blend well. Shape into 1-inch meatballs.  Combine remaining ingredients in medium saucepan and mix well. Add  meatballs, stirring lightly to coat with sauce. Simmer about 1 hour,  stirring occasionally.    Recipe by: Cooking with Bon Appetit (Appetizers)    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1069 by Carriej999@aol.com on Feb 1, 1998
