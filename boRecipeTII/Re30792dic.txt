Tangerine Salsa (Great Citrus)
Minced green onion, Tangerines; peeled, seeded, Hoisin sauce, Lime juice, Fresh Cilantro, Minced gingerroot, Scotch Bonnet; seeded and, 
In a stainless steel bowl, combine all of the ingredients. Cover and  refrigerate for 1 hour before use.
