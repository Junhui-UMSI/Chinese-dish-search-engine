Chicken Stroganoff
Skinless boneless chicken, Sour cream, Cream of mushroom soup, Lipton dry onion soup mix, 
Mix all together and put into crockpot.  Cook on low for 4 -6 hours or  until chicken is tender.  Serve over cooked noodles or rice.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by rfalconmoors@juno.com (roseanne  m M Brown)  on Dec 1, 1998
