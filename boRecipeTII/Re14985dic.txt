30-Minute Chili
Chili powder, Tomato soup, Onion, Kidney beans  , Salt, Ground chuck, 
Brown the meat in a little butter and cook until the meat is brown - about  10 minutes. Add all other ingredients and let simmer for 30 minutes. Your  choice of hot sauce may be added to taste.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
