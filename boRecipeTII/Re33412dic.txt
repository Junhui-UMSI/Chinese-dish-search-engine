Basic Yellow Cake
Eggs, milk, Salt, milk, Flour, Baking Powder, Vanilla, Crisco, Sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350? Grease and flour 2 9 inch round pans. In bowl, combine  flour, sugar, salt and powder. Add Crisco,and 3/4 cup milk. Beat 2 minutes  at medium speed. Add eggs, remaining milk and vanilla. Beat for another 2  minutes, pour in prepared pans. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until done. Cool,  frost as desired.
