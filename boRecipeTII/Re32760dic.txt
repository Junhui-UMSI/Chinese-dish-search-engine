Sweet Potato Muffins #2
Baking soda, Nutmeg, Egg white; whipped, Unbleached flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Sweet potatoes, Cinnamon, Granulated sugar, Raisins, Skim milk; at room, Applesauce; at room, 
Date: Fri, 23 Feb 1996 08:53:16 -0800    From: Gerald Edgerton <jerrye@wizard.com>    Recipe By : Anita A. Matejka Preheat oven at 350. Prepare 12 muffin pans  with cooking spray and flour. In a mixing bowl , combine sweet potatoes,  milk, applesauce, and egg white. In another mixing bowl, combine flour,  sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, raisins, and salt. Mix  wet ingredients with dry ingredients just until moistened. Use an ice cream  scoop to fill muffin tins about full. Bake for 25 minutes.
