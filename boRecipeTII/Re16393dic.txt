Weetabix Fruit Loaf
Sultanas, Sugar, Self-raising flour, Weetabix, milk, 
From: mark@alexr.demon.co.uk (Mark Alexander)    Date: Sun, 4 Sep 1994 21:21:32 +0000    Thought people might find this recipe interesting.    1. crush the weetabix into fine crumbs    2. add sugar, fruit and flour    3. add milk and mix well    4. Pour into greased lined loaf tin    5. Bake at Gas Mark 4 / 350F for 60 to 70 minutes    6. Cool for 10 minutes    7. When cold store in an airtight tin Source: Mrs Brenda Nuttall, Halifax,  England.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
