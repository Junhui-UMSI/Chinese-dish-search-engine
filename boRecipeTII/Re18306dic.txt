Verlas Turtle Cake
Chocolate chips, German chocolate cake mix, Evaporated Milk, Pecans, chopped, Caramels, Margarine, 
Preheat Oven to 3500 Mix cake as per box directions. Grease a 9x13  pan. Bake a little less than half of the batter for 15 minutes. Melt  the caramels, margarine and milk in the microwave (about 10 minutes)  stirring a couple of times. Pour caramel mixture over cake, sprinkle  with nuts and chocolate chips. Pour the rest of the cake mix over the  top. Bake 20-35 minutes more, test for doneness.
