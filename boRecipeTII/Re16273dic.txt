Watermellon Rind Preserves
Sugar, Watermelon cubes, Water, Lime (calcium oxide), Water, Lemon, sliced, 
Contributed to the echo by: Merrilyn Vaughan WATERMELON RIND PRESERVES 1.  Soak melon in limewater for 3+ 1/2 hours. Drain. 2. Soak in clear water for  1 hour. Drain. 3. Cover with water and boil for 1+ 1/2 hour. Drain. 4. Make  syrup fo2 c. sugar and 1 quart water. 5. Add rind and boil for 1 hour. 6.  As syrup thickens add lemon [half ob. for each lb. of melon]. 7. When syrup  is thick and rind clear, pack in hot, sterilized jars,    top with syrup and seal.
