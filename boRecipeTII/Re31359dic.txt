Bar-X Bbq Sauce
Worcestershire Sauce, Sugar, Condensed tomato soup, Catsup, Vinegar, Salt, Medium onion, Sliced bacon, Water, 
Put bacon and onion through food chopper, using medium blade. Add remaining  ingredients and slowly bring to boiling.  Simmer        2    hours.  Makes 3 cups.
