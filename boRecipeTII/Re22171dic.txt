Turkey Pot Pie with Mashed Potato Crust
Celery, Egg, Cheddar cheese, potato, Turkey, Lemon juice, Mayonnaise, Peas, 
Cook & mash potatoes, or used leftovers. Stir in egg. Spread in well oiled  9-inch pie pan. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes. Combine mayonnaise and  lemon juice; stir in turkey, celery, and peas. Spoon into potato crust. Top  with cheese. Continue baking 15-20 minutes or until hot through.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 351 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Dec 09, 1997
