Raspberry Slush
Fresh orange juice, Fresh raspberries, Ice cubes, 
SOURCE:  The Joslin Diabetes Gourmet Cookbook by Bonnie Sanders Polin,  Ph.D., and Frances Towner Giedt, Copyrright 1993. ISBN #0-553-08760-6.  Formatted into MM by Ursula R. Taylor.    Place raspberries (reserve a few for garnish), orange juice, and ice  cubes in a blender.  Puree until smooth. Pour into 2 tall glasses, filling  halfway. Add mineral water to fill each glass. Float reserved berries in  drinks.    Makes 2 servings.    PER SERVING:  calories - 43, protein - 1 g, carbohydrate - 10 g, fat -  trace, calories from fat - less than 1%, dietary fiber - 2 g, cholesterol -  0 mg, sodium - 4 mg, potassium - 171 mg.
