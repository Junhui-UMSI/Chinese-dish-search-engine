Wiggly, Wicked Cut-Outs
(12-ounce) frozen cranberry, Envelopes unflavored gelatin, Apple jluice, 
Lightly grease a 13x9 inch pan. In medium saucepan, combine juices.  Sprinkle gelatin over top and allow to soften 1 to 2 minutes. Bring to a  boil over medium-high heat. Stir constantly to dissolve gelatin. Pour into  prepared pans. Refrigerate until firm. Cut into Halloween shape with cookie  cutters or simply cut into squares.
