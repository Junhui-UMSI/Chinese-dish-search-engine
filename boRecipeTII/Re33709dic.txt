Sweet and Savory Pork
Cornstarch, Garlic cloves; crushed or, Dried oregano leaves, Pitted green olives;, Salt, Black Pepper, Red wine vinegar, Pork tenderloin; sliced 1", Dry white wine, Brown sugar, Pitted prunes; coarsely, olive oil, Capers; drained, 
1. On waxed paper, combine brown sugar, salt, pepper, and garlic; use to  coat pork slices.    2. In nonstick 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat, heat olive oil. Add  pork slices and cook until lightly browned on both sides and pork just  loses its pink color on the inside, 5 to 7 minutes. Remove pork to plate.    3. In 1-cup glass measuring cup, mix wine, vinegar, cornstarch, and oregano  until well blended. Add cornstarch mixture to skillet; cook, stirring  constantly, until slightly thickened, about 1 minute. Return pork slices to  skillet; add prunes, olives, and capers; heat through.    Each serving: About 245 calories, 25 g protein, 22 g carbohydrate, 6 g  total fat (1 g saturated), 64 mg cholesterol, 840 mg sodium.
