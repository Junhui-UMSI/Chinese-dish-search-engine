Taco Quiche
GARNISHES, Lean ground beef, Sour Cream, Chili Salsa, If Desired, Avocado, Sliced, Chili powder, Eggs, Salt, Purple Spanish Onion *, Whole Ripe Olives, Ground cumin, 
* Purple Spanish Onion should be chopped.  ~------------------------------------------------------ ~-----------------  Line lightly greased 13-inch quiche dish (or one 13 x 9-inch pan or two  9-inch piepans) with tortillas, tearing them to fit, if necessary. Brown  ground beef and onion, pour off drippings.  Add garlic, chili powder,  oregano, cumin, thyme and salt, stirring to combine. Spoon Beef mixture in  pan(s). Top with cheese. Beat eggs and milk together and pour into pan(s).  Bake in moderate oven (350F) 1 hour or until set. Spread top with sour  cream; top with tomatoes avocado and lettuce. Garnish with olives. Cut into  serving size peieces; serve with salsa.
