Two Cheese Vegtable Pizza
(2 oz) shredded lowfat, Red pepper, cut into strips, Dried basil, (about 8 oz) chopped, Mozzarella cheese, MAZOLA corn oil, Garlic, Onion, Salt, Muffins, split and toasted, Mushrooms, Lowfat ricotta cheese, 
1.  In medium skillet heat corn oil over medium-high heat.  Add mushrooms,  red  pepper, onion and garlic; stirring frequently, cook until tender.  Remove  from  heat.    2.  Stir in ricotta cheese, basil and salt.  Spread over toasted muffins.  Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.    3.  Place muffins on baking sheet.  Bake in 375 F oven 10 minutes or until  cheese melts.    Makes 4 servings.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #148    Date: Fri, 12 Jul 1996 15:01:18 -0400    From: CookieTstr@aol.com    Recipe By     :Womans Day RecipesTwo
