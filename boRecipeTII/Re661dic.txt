Gluten Free Enchilada Sauce
Sea salt, Garlic powder, water, rice flour, garbanzo flour, Chili powder, Cumin powder, Ground red pepper, 
Mix all dry ingredients together.  Add to 1 quart of cold water in  saucepan.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until simmering and  thick.  USe over enchiladas or to season grain dishes.    SUGGESTIONS: Refrigerate extra sauce.  Double or triple the dry  ingredients. Store the dry mix in a tightly closed jar.  Use 3/4 cup of the  dry mix to one quart of cold water to make sauce.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Recipes for Special Dietary Needs" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
