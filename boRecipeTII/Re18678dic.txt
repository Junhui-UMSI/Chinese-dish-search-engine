Amish Sugar Cookies
Sugar, Cream of tartar, Vegetable oil, Eggs, Butter, Salt, Baking soda, Flour, Powdered sugar, Vanilla, 
From: arielle@taronga.com (Stephanie da Silva) (COLLECTION)    Date: Wed, 2 Mar 1994 23:07:59 GMT  See recipe "Grandma Olsens Sugar Cookies" for source/info on this recipe.    Mix sugars, butter, oil and eggs well.  Mix remaining ingredients well.  Blend all ingredients together, chill. With floured hands, shape into balls  and place on cookie sheet, flatten with bottom of chilled glass dipped in  sugar. Sprinkle with colored sugar. Bake 350F until edges are golden.
