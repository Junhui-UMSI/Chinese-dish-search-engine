Lemon Butter
White wine, Lemon juice, Chopped shallots, Heavy cream, Butter,; cubed, Salt and pepper, 
Reduce the wine and lemon juice with the shallots for 2 minutes. Add the  cream and reduce for 2 minutes. Using a whisk, slowly incorporate the  butter. Season. Remove the plank from the grill and place on a serving  plate.
