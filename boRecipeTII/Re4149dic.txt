Peppery Scalloped Potatoes
(10.75oz) condensed cream of, Cayenne pepper, Butter or Margarine, Salt, Thinly sliced peeled, All-purpose flour, milk, 
In a small bowl, combine the soup, milk, salt and cayenne; set aside. Place  a third of the potatoes in a greased 13x9x2-inch baking dish; layer with a  third of the butter, flour and soup mixture. Repeat layers twice. Bake,  uncovered, at 350? for 1 hour and 20 minutes or until the potatoes are  tender.
