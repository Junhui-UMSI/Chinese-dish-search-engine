Banana Split
Caramel Sauce, Evaporated milk, Banana, Whipped cream, Cherries, Ice cream, Nuts, Sugar, Sugar, Warm water, 
Cut a banana in half lengthwise. Cut each half into two. Arrange the banana  pieces in a shallow pudding bowl (preferably a boat shape bowl). Top with  two or three scoops of ice cream. Spoon sauce* over. On each mound of ice  cream, place a dollop of whipped cream. Decorate with a cherry or a nut.  CARAMEL SAUCE: Melt sugar and butter over low heat. Keep on stirring until  golden brown. Add warm water and keep on stirring until smooth. Add the  evaporated milk, and keep on stirring until smooth. Serve hot or cold.    busted by sooz  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 250 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 12, 1997
