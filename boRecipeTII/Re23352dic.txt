Tuna with Water Chestnuts
Celery, Garlic salt, Mayonnaise, Salt, Green onion, tuna  , water chestnuts  , black olives  , Chinese noodles  , lemon, 
Mix all ingredients except Chinese noodles. Just before serving, sprinkle  with noodles or serve over noodles. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.    ROBYN DICKEY    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
