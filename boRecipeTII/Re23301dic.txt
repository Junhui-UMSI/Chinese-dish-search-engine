Tuna Shrimp Delight
Cooking sherry, Frozen shrimp, White bread,, Frozen asparagus spears,, Can pitted black olives,, Can cream of mushroom soup, Can chunk tuna, drained, 
1. Place first 4 ingredients in a shallow, 1 1/2-quart, heat- resistant,  non-metallic casserole. Pour sherry over ingredients and mix gently. 2.  Arrange asparagus spears over mixture and heat in Microwave Oven 8 minutes  or until mixture bubbles. 3. Cut conventionally toasted bread into 9  squares per slice. Arrange toast squares on top of casserole filling during  last 30 seconds of cooking time.
