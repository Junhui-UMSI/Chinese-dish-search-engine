Pork Steak and Noodle Casserole
Pork steak; cubed, salt and pepper, Dried onions, Cream of mushroom soup, Dried cleery, (large) wide noodles, 
Brown meat and add celery and onions. Saute until almost tender. Add soup  and salt and pepper. Boil noodles 5 minutes in salt water. Drain and mix  with mixture. Pour into large pan. May add milk if you like more moist. Top  with Velveeta cheese slices and crushed potatoe chips. Bake at 35 degrees  for 2 hours.
