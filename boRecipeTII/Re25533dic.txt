Avocado Aperitif
Lemon, Lemon slices; -OR-, Cucumber peel, Cucumber, Parsley, GARNISH, Avocado, -Crushed Ice, 
Peel avocado and cucumber; chop in chunks and place in a blender. Add  parsley, lemon, and blend until smooth. Add the crushed ice and blend  together again. Strain into glasses. Garnish with lemon slices or cucumber  peel.    SERVES:4 SOURCE:_The Non-Drinkers Drink Book_ by Gail Schioler
