Quick and Easy Apple Butter
Ground cloves, Brown sugar; firmly packed, Ground nutmeg, Ground cinnamon, Apples; cored and finely cho, Apple cider, 
Place apples and apple cider in 1 1/2-quart micro-proof bowl. Cover tightly  with plastic wrap. Microwave on High 15 minutes. Carefully remove plastic  wrap and stir in remaining ingredients. Return to microwave and cook,  uncovered, 10 minutes more, stirring every 2 to 3 minutes. Transfer mixture  to a blender of food processor and process until smooth. Store in airtight  container in refrigerator. Yield: about 2 pints of apple butter FROM: RITA  TAULE (BTVC62A)  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #190    Date: 07/03/96    From: eboyd@shentel.net
