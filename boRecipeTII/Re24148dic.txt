Traditional Pumkpin Pie
(16-oz) pumpkin, Ground ginger, Salt, (14-oz) Eagle Brand, Ground nutmeg, Ground cinnamon, 9-inch unbaked pastry shell, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.  In large mixer bowl, combine all ingredients  except pastry shell; mix well. Pour into pastry shell. Bake 15 minutes.  Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees; bake 35-40 minutes longer or until  knife inserted 1 inch from edge comes out clean. Cool.  Garnish as desired.  Refrigerate leftovers.    FROM "EAGLE BRAND ALL TIME    FAVORITE DESSERTS WITH EAGLE    BRAND CONDENSED MILK"    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
