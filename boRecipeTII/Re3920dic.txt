Pepper Spread Appetizer
Cream cheese, Cumin, or to taste, Garlic, Finely chopped sun-dried tom, olive oil, Minced sweet onion (Vidalia,, Chopped cilantro, Sweet red peppers, roasted, Salt and pepper to taste, 
*Note: Sun-dried tomatoes should be oil-packed type. Process all in food  processor until well blended. Serve chilled with crackers.
