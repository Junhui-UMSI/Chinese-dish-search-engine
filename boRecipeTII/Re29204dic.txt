Baked Shrimp
Large cleaned shrimp, Butter, Lemon juice, Paprika, Cloves garlic (minced), Bread crumbs, Chopped parsley, 
Butterfly shrimp but dont cut all the way.  Spread and flatten. Place  shrimps in individual baking dishes. sprinkle with portion of lemon juice.  Combine butter, 5 tbl parsley and garlic in a bowl. Place some of the  mixture on each shrimp. Combine crumbs, remaining parsley and paprika and  place some on each shrimp. Bake at 400 deg for 15 minutes. Garnish with  lemon wedges.
