Cheddar Pasta And Vegetables
Green peppers, medium, Carrot, Rotini pasta, Broccoli floweretts, Cheddar cheese, shredded, Milk 2%, Can cream celery soup, Brown spicy mustard, 
Put on the pasta water to boil. When it is boiling add the sliced carrots.  Cook for five minutes. Then add the rotini and bring back to the boil. Once  it is boiling again slowly add the broccoli flowerettes. Stir and cook for  about 10 minutes until rotini is done. Drain into a colander. Return pan to  stove and turn down the heat to low. Add the remaining ingredients. Over  low heat, heat until cheese melts, stirring often. Add rotini and  vegetables back into the pot. Heat through, stirring occasionally.
