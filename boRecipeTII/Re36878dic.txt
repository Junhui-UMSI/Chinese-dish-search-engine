Strawberry Shake
Vanilla extract, Low-Fat Yogurt, Fresh Strawberries, Light brown sugar, Skim milk, 
* Strawberries should be rinsed and hulled.  Frozen strawberries can be  used and should be thawed and drained.  ~-------------------------------------------------------------------------  Place all ingredients in a blender bowl and process until smooth.  Serve at  once.  133 calories per serving.  From Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
