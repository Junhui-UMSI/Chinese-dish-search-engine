Lace Cookies
Flour, Coconut, Nuts, Brown Sugar, Corn syrup light, Butter, Vanilla, 
Mix flour and coconut.  Combine syrup, sugar and butter in heavy saucepan.   Bring to boil over medium heat, stir constantly.   Remove from heat, gradually blend in flour mixture, then vanilla.  Drop on foil-covered cookie sheet by scan teaspoons full three inches apart.   Bake 8-10 minutes at 350 degrees.   Cool 3-4 minutes on wire rack until foil peels off easily.   Place  cookies on rack covered with absorbent paper.     Recipe by:  Sonja Appleton
