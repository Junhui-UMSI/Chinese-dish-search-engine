Ranchhouse Tortilla Bake
Corn tortillas, Salsa, Flour, Butter, Sour cream, Onions, Shredded jack cheese, Cubed cooked chicken, Salt and pepper, 
Cook onions in butter until soft. Stir in flour. Add milk and sour cream,  cooking until thick. Stir in salso. Season to taste. Stir in chicken. Line  a buttered 12x8 baking pan with 6 tortillas; spoon in chicken mixture. Top  with remaining tortillas. Sprinkle cheese over all. Bake at 375F for 30-45  minutes, or until cheese melts and filling is hot. Can sprinkle with  chopped cilantro before serving, if desired.
