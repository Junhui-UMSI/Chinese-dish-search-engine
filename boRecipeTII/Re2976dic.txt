Peanut Butter Roll
Or more peanut butter, Condensed milk, Egg white, Powdered sugar, 
Beat egg white until very stiff. Mix enough sifted sugar and milk in egg  white to make consistency of pie dough. Roll about 1/4-inch thick and  spread with peanut butter. Roll like jelly roll and cut in pieces.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 22,  1998
