Pumpkin Waffles #2
Pumpkin pie spice, Half and half, Salt, Canned pumpkin puree, Granny smith apple; pared,, Eggs, Chopped; toasted walnuts, Butter; melted and cooled, All purpose flour, Brown sugar, Baking Powder, 
From: Ranger@Halcyon.com (Glenn/Sharon Gorman)    Date: 5 Aug 1994 16:44:47 -0400    Whisk eggs and brown sugar together in mixing bowl.  Add half and half and  butter; whisk until blended.  Mix flour, baking powder, salt and pie spice  in another bowl. Add to egg mixture and stir just until blended. Stir in  pumpkin; fold in apple and walnuts.  Heat waffle iron as directed.  Serve  with butter and warm maple syrup.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
