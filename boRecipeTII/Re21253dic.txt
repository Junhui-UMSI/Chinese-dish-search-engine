Ukranian Honey Cake (Medivnyk)
Flour; sifted, Currants, Pitted dates; chopped, Candied orange peel; chopped, Egg whites; stiffly beaten, Nutmeg or mace, Unsalted butter; softened, Sugar, Powdered cinnamon, Baking Powder, Salt, Dark brown sugar; firmly, Egg yolks, Powdered cloves, Walnuts or blanched almonds;, Dark honey, Gold raisins, With, Baking soda, 
Combine honey and spices, bring to a boil, remove from heat and cool. Cream  butter with baking soda and sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg yolks,  one at a time, beating well between additions. Resift flour with salt and  baking powder.  Add flour and honey to butter mixture and stir in  thoroughly.  Mix in fruits and nuts. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.  Pour into two 7- or 8-inch loaf pans that have been buttered and lined with  buttered brown paper.  Bake in preheated 325 degree oven for 2 to 2-1/2  hours, or until a tester comes out clean. Turn out of pans, remove paper,  cool and let mellow for 2 days before cutting. Makes 2 loaf cakes. From:  Visions of Sugarplums by Mimi Sheraton.
