Taiarini
Lean ground beef, Tomato soup; (10 3/4 Oz), Thin noodles; (8 Oz) cooked, (Small) sliced ripe olives;, (Small) cream style corn, (Small) mushrooms; drained, Onion, Cheddar cheese; Grated, 
Mix ground beef and onion in a 2 quart casserole. Microwave at high (100%)  8 minutes, stirring once to break up beef. Drain. Add soup, noodles, corn,  olives, mushrooms and half the cheese. Mix well. Cover with wax paper.  Microwave at high (100%) 8 minutes, stirring once during cooking time.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top. Microwave, uncovered, at high (100%)  until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes Makes 6 servings.
