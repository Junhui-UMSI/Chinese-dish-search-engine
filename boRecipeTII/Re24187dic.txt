Trampers Muffins
Shredded coconut, Chopped dried apricots, Pecans, Egg, Flour, WET MIX, Raisins, Sugar, Bran, Baking Powder, Yoghurt, Chocolate chips, Vegetable oil, DRY MIX, 
Preheat oven to 400F, prepare pans. Blend wet mix ingredients & mix well.  Add dry mix until just blended. Spoon into pans & bake for 15-20 mins.  Makes       12    muffins.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
