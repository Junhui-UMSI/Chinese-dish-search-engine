Potatos Plakey
(cilantro), Potatos cut in 1/2" rounds, Tomatos with juice, Italian parsley chopped, Tomato paste or catsup, Cloves of Garlic, Black pepper, Salt, olive oil, Water (as needed), 
Cook in pressure cooker. Cook for 10 minutes at pressure, remove from heat  then let pressure fall by itself. Cook without lid for an additional 5  minutes, or until liquid has a light sauce texture (not watery).
