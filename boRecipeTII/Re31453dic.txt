Barbecue Sauce
Chili powder, Low-sodium chicken broth, Cider vinegar, Ground clove, Ketchup, Black Pepper, Garlic, Carrot; finely minced, Worcestershire Sauce, Sprig fresh thyme; -=OR=-, Tomato paste, Celery stalk; finely sliced, -Dried thyme, Cayenne pepper, Brown sugar, Salt, Bay Leaves, Dry mustard, 
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS in a pot, place over medium heat on the stove and  cook until reduced to 3/4 cup. Strain, and use to glaze chicken, meat or  fish while grilling.    Makes 3/4 Cup    MICHAEL ROBERTS - PRODIGY GUEST CHEFS COOKBOOK
