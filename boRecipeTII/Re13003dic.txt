Winter Green Salad with Olives
Orange juice, Chopped Drained Capers, Dried Oregano, Chopped Olives, olive oil, Pomegranate Cells, SALAD, VINAIGRETTE, Lemon juice, Mixed Winter Greens, Pine Nuts, Toasted, 
Vinaigrette: Combine all ingredients in a jar; cover and shake vigorously.    Salad: Toss greens, pomegranate cells, olives and vinaigrette in a large  bowl. Sprinkle with pine nuts.
