Bean Stew
Runner beans, trimmed &, sliced, Tomatoes, chopped, Sugar, Salt, olive oil, Basil, Onions, chopped, Black pepper, Vegetable stock, Garlic, 
Heat oil in a pot & fry the onion for 2 minutes.  add garlic & fry  together.  Add the rest of the ingredients & the seasonings. Mix together  well.  Fry for 5 minutes. Add stock. Simer till the beans are tender.   Serve hot or cold.     Jack Santa Maria, "Greek Vegetarian Cookery"  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #241 by Jack Elvis <jackelvis@moonlink.net>  on Sep 10, 1997
