Beer Rye Bread
Beer; dark, Caraway seed, Dollop molasses, Self-Rising Flour, Rye flour, 
Combine ingredients, mix til doughy, drop in oiled or Pamd bread pan,  bake in pre-heated oven at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #805 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Sep 25, 1997
