Banana Republic
Ginger ale, Mango juice, Pineapple rings in juice, Sliced, Rum -- to taste, Maraschino cherry, Whole banana -- peeled and, 
Throw all the ingredients except the ginger ale/soda & cherry in a blender  and liquefy. Toss a couple ice cubes in a collins glass and fill it half  way with banana republic. Add ale/soda to the top and stir slowly. Toss in  your Cherry,  Now, put on a safari jacket, kick back in the lawn chair,  crank up the stereo and sip away.    Recipe By     : The Net
