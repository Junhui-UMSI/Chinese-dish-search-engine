Pickle Roll Ups
Smoked meat, thin sliced, Jars Pickles; dill, Cream cheese, 
Fat grams    per serving:              Approx. Cook Time: none Roll pickle  completely in cream cheese between hands. Wrap in 1 layer of meat. Chill  for at least 1 hour. Then slice rolls into wheels, the pickle will be  surrounded by the cream cheese and then the meat. from Neils mom,  originally from Florida Source: Neils mom from Florida
