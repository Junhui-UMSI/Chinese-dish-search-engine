Urulakazhangu Kaarakari (Spicy Potato Curry)
Potatoes; boiled and mashed, For Seasoning, Sprigs curry leaves; chopped, Salt, Lemon, Turmeric, Red chiles; broken, Black mustard seeds, Sambar powder, Black gram dal, Coconut oil, 
Heat oil and add mustard seeds, black gram dal and red chilies. When brown,  add turmeric and curry leaves. Fry for a minute and add mashed potatoes,  salt and sambar powder. Cook on a low fire till dry. Add lemon juice and  mix well. Serve hot.
