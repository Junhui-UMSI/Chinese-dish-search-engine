Arts Mock Crabmeat Casserole
Hard-boiled eggs, chopped, Curry powder, Green pepper, diced, Mock crabmeat, Parsley, Onion; diced, Sour creame, Paprika, Mayonnaise, 
Combine all ingredients, except paprika. Turn into greased 1 1/2-quart  casserole. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake at 350^ for 40 to 50 minutes. Serve  over hot, cooked rice. Source: Art Reed, Siver Spgs. MD
