Thick and Spicy Spaghetti Sauce Pt 1
(continued on sauce2), Cl Garlic, minced, 6-oz cans tomato paste, 16-oz can whole tomatoes, Worcestershire Sauce, Green peppers, chop, olive oil, Pounds ground beef, Fresh mushrooms, sliced, Undrained & chopped, Whole oregano, Celery, Onions, chopped, 8-oz cans tomato sauce, Dill weed, Chili powder, 
Brown ground beef with onion, green pepper, celery and garlic in hot oil in  large dutch oven.  Cook until meat is browned, stirring to crumble; drain  well, and stir in remaining ingredients. Cover, bring to boil, and reduce  heat to simmer for an additional 2 or 3 hours, stirring occasionally.  Remove bay leaves and serve over pasta of choice. Sprinkle with shredded  sharp cheddar cheese and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
