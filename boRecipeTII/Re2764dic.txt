Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies
Semisweet Chocolate Chips --, (miniature), Sugar, Egg -- lightly beaten, Peanut butter, 
In a mixing bowl, combine peanut butter, sugar and egg (batter will be  stiff).  Stir in chocolate chips. Scoop level tablespoonfuls and roll into  balls.  Place on ungreased cookie sheets and flatten with a fork. Bake at  350 degrees for 15-18 minutes or until browned.    Yield:  2 dozen.    Editors Note:  This recipe does not use any flour.
