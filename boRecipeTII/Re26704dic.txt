Bailys Irish Cream
Half & half or whipping, Vanilla, Instant coffee, Sweet & low, Eggs, Chocolate syrup, Sweetened; condensed milk, Whiskey, 
Put all ingredients in blender and blend until well mixed 1 to 2 minutes.  (DO NOT FORGET TO ADD THE SWEET & LOW) Refrigerate!
