Basil Bean Salad
Fresh Basil; Chopped, Red Bell Pepper; Chopped, DRESSING, Safflower Oil, Black pepper, Lemon juice, Cucumber; Chopped, Fresh Parsley; Minced, Cider vinegar, Great Northern Beans;, 
In mixing bowl, combine beans, cucumber and red pepper. In a small bowl or  measuring cup, combine dressing ingredients. Pour over bean mixture and  toss. Using a slotted spoon, arrange on lettuce leaves on individual  chilled salad plates. Garnish with chopped scallions.
