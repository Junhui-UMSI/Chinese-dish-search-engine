Tom Yam Goong (Hot and Sour Soup)
fresh ?, Black chili paste, Garlic, Shrimp, straw mushrooms, Green Thai chili peppers, Fish sauce, lemon grass, Lime juice, Kaffir lime leaves, Water, shallots, 
Rinse shells and place in water.  Heat water to a boil, then strain out  shells.  Add garlic, lime leaves, galangal, fish sauce, lemon grass,  shallots, straw mushrooms, and chilis and simmer for 2 minutes. Add the  shrimp and reheat to a boil and cook for 3-4 minutes until shrimp turn  pink.  Place the lime juice and chili paste in a serving bowl, pour the  soup into the bowl, stir, and garnish. Serves 4.
