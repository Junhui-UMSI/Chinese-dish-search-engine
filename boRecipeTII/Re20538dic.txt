Apple Cheese Pancakes
Salt, Flour, Cinnamon, Cottage cheese, 1/2% fat, Honey, Lemon juice, Almonds, Eggs, Apples, 
Mix all ings. but egg whites.  Beat whites till stiff and fold in.  Spray a  griddle or skillet with cooking spray.  Heat and drop batter to form  pancakes. Turn when bottom is browned.
