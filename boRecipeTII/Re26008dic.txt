Tofu Brunswick Stew
Barbecue sauce, Diced onion, Canned tomato puree, Canola oil, divided, Hot pepper sauce to taste, Firm tofu, diced, Fresh or frozen corn kernels, Tomato paste, Dry red wine or beer, Tamari or soy sauce, Celery, Garlic, 
(listed as taking 30 minutes or less to prepare)    Recipes from VEGETARIAN TIMES, November 1995.    Heat 1 teaspoon oil in a non-stick pan over moderate heat.  Add diced tofu;  brown. Remove from pan; set aside.    Add remaining 1 teaspoon oil; saute onion, celery and garlic until  well-browned.  Stir in remaining ingredients and reserved tofu; simmer  until sauce is reduced and develops a rich brown color, about 15 minutes.  Makes 4 servings.    Per serving: 154 cal.; 10G protein; 6G fat; 19G carb.; 0 chol.; 838MG  sodium; 4G fiber.  Vegan.
