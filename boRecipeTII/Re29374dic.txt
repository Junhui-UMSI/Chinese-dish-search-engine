Baked Turkey Loaf (Lf)
Ground turkey, Ground, Chopped, Onion, Skim milk, Egg white, Black pepper, Fresh parsley, Bell pepper, Garlic, Vegetable stock, Celery, Dried oregano, Whole-wheat bread crumbs, Chopped, 
Mix all ingredients until just blended.  Do not overmix. Press the mixture  into a 4 x 9-inch nonstick loaf pan. Bake at 325F for 45 minutes to 1 hour.  Serve hot with Mushroom Glaze (see recipe same name) or serve cold. Makes 6  servings.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
