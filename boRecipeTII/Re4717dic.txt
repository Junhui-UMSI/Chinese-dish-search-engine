Philly No-Bake Summer Cheesecake
Sugar, Tub (8-ounces) Cool Whip, Ready-to-use graham cracker, (8-ounces) Cream Cheese or, 
Mix cream cheese and sugar with electric mixer on medium speed until well  blended. Gently stir in whipped topping. Spoon into crust. Refrigerate 3  hours or overnight. Top with fresh fruit. Makes 8 servings. Posted to EAT-L  Digest  by Karyn Farrell <Syber12@AOL.COM> on Aug 2, 1997
