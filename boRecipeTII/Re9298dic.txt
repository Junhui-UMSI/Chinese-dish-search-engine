Puerto Rican Rice
White rice, Sofrito, ham, EILEEN LAMPARELLI, Peas, Oregano, Capers, CGDR39A-----, Water, tomato sauce, Paprika, 
In a skillet fry dry rice with ham; add sofrito, oregano, tomato sauce,  paprika and water.  Cover and let simmer until rice is almost tender. Add  peas and capers.
