Warm Apricot Crunch Sundaes
Soft butter or margarine, Brown sugar, Cashew halves, Apricot halves, drained, 
Info:  from Selections from Betty Crockers Cooking for Two cook book  posted by Perry Lowell, COOKING Echo, Dec. 91    vanilla ice cream    Set oven control at broil and/or 550 degrees.  Place apricots in 8-inch  oven-proof skillet or shallow baking dish. Sprinkle sugar and cashews on  apricots; dot with butter.    Broil with tops about 6 inches from heat until sugar and butter are melted  and sauce is bubbly, about 2 minutes.    Serve immediately on ice cream.    Two servings.
