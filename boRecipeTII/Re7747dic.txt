Portabella Mushroom Appetizer
Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Shallot, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Fresh parsley, fresh rosemary, Portabella mushrooms, Garlic, Marinade:, Salt, white pepper, Juice of 2 limes, 
Season the mushrooms with salt, white pepper and olive oil to taste and  grill over high heat 4 minutes, until soft.  Let cool.  Combine marinade  ingredients.  Marinate at least one hour in the mixture.  Chill before  serving.    Recipe By     : Phillips Mushroom Farms/AnnieMiner    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
