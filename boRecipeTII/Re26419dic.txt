Tomato Cups
Vinegar, Dried Basil Leaves; Crushed, Salad oil, (3 Cans) Tomatoes, Drained, Onion; Cut Into Rings, Dried Oregano Leaves; Crush, Green Pepper Strips; 1/2", Salt, Black pepper, Tomatoes; Chopped OR, 
Combine all of the ingredients in a large salad bowl. Toss lightly to mix  well, then cover and refrigerate until servings time.
