Warm Spinach-Chicken Salad
olive oil, olive oil, CHICKEN, DRESSING, Garlic; Chopped, Red onions; Thinly sliced, Dry white wine, Garlic; Finely chopped, Mushrooms; Sliced, Black pepper, Fresh spinach; washed, dried, White pepper, Chicken breast; sliced, Rice vinegar, MARINADE, Soy sauce, Cornstarch, Thai Sweet Black Bean Sauce, 
Combine marinade ingredients in a small bowl.     Add chicken & mix with marinade.  Cover and refrigerate 3 hours. Place  spinach in a large bowl, arrange sliced onions & mushrooms in a circle on  top.     Heat olive oil in a small saucepan over high heat.   Add garlice and saute, stirring constantly, for 3 minutes. Stir in  remaining ingredients and bring to boil; boil for 1 minute. Pour chicken &  dressing onto salad and serve.
