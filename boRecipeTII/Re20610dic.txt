Apple Coffee Cake
Apples, TOPPING, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Nuts, Eggs, Sugar, BREAD, Sugar, Brown sugar, Butter, Cinnamon, Flour, Baking soda, Sour cream, 
Cream  butter  and  sugar.    Add   eggs  and vanilla. Add dry ingredients  alternately with sour cream. Put half of batter in pan. Lay apple slices on  batter and sprinkle 1/2 of topping over them. Add remaining batter and  sprinkle  topping on it.  Bake in 350 oven 35-40 minutes.
