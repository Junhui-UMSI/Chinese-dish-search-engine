Au Pied De Cochon Re (French Onion Soup)
Vegetable oil, Dry wine, unsalted butter, French Bread Baguette, Chicken stock, Onions, 
Melt butter with oil in heavy large pot over medium heat. Add onions, cover  and cook until lightly colored, stirring occasionally, about 45 minutes.  Add wine and bring to boil, scraping up any browned bits. Cook 5 minutes.  Add browned bits.  Cook 5 minutes. Add stock and bring to simmer. Simmer  uncovered 1-1/2 hours. Season to taste with salt and pepper. (Can be  prepared 1 day ahead.  Cover and refrigerate.  Bring to simmer before  continuing.)    Preheat broiler.  Ladle soup into boilerproof bowls. Top with slices of  toasted French bread.  Sprinkle with grated Gruyere cheese. Broil until  cheese melts. Serve immediately.
