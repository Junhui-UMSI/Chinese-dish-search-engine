Yucatan Marinade
Ancho chile powder, Lime juice, Black Pepper, Fresh orange juice, Salt, Fresh Lemon Juice, Paprika, olive oil, Cayenne, Pasilla chile powder, 
In a food processor, combine all the ingredients except the olive oil and  process for 30 seconds. With the motor still running, slowly add the olive  oil and process until emulsified.
