Dried basil;, Fresh bread;, Green pepper; finely chopped, Mozzerella Cheese;, Mushrooms; finely chopped, Catup;, Dried oregano;, Tomato paste;, 
Roll bread slices flat and thin with a rolling pin or an empty quart  jar. Place on a flat pan 3" inches under the broiler; heat until  lightly toasted on one side, then remove from oven.  Turn slices over  and spread untoasted sides with a layer of each of sprinkling of  cheese.  Broil until cheese melts and browns slightly. To serve, cut  each pizza into quarters.    Food Exchange per serving: 1 STARCH EXCHANGES + 1/2 LEAN MEAT  EXCHANGES; CAL: 123; CHO: 16g; PRO: 7g; SOD: 279; CHO: 26; Low-sodium  diets: Substitute tomato paste for catup.
