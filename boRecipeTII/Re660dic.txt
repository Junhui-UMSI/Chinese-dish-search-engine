Glue for Envelopes or Stickers
White vinegar, 
Mix 2 parts white glue to 1 part vinegar.  Brush on back of stickers or on  envelopes and let dry.  Can be moistened to stick.  Corners will curl, so  do whole pages of paper when possible when making stickers.
