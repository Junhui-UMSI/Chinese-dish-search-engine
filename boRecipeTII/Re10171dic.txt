Quiche Lorraine-Carols
Grated Romano cheese, Bacon; cooked, crumbled, Black Pepper, Skim milk; evaporated, Bacon drippings, Eggs, 1% low-fat milk, Salt, 9-inch pie crust; deep dish, Bacon, Swiss cheese; lowfat, grated, Onion, 
Saute onion until tender in 1 bacon drippings. Mix eggs, evaporated milk,  milk, cheeses,salt, pepper and cooked cooled onions. Place bacon in pie  shell. Pour over mixture in pie shell. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes,  or until mixture is firm and golden.
