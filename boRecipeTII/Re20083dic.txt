Vanilla Evaporated Ice Milk
Vanilla, ICE CREAM COOKBOOK-----, Salt, Evaporated milk, Sugar, 
Dissolve the sugar in 2 quarts of milk and heat almost to the boiling  point. Remove from heat. Chill, add remaining ingredients and freeze.
