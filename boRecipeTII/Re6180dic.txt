Pizza Sauce
Cooking oil, Tomato paste, Oregano, Brown sugar, Raw onions (chopped), Black pepper, Parmesan cheese (grated), Marjoram, Water, Clove garlic (chopped), 
Combine ingredients in a 1 qt sauce pan and cook for 30-40 minutes. Makes  enough sauce for 4 12" Pizzas.    From the recipe collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/ms-pizza.zip
