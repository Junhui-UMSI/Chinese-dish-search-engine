Port Wine Marinade
Port wine, Salt, Freshy thyme, garlic, Hot sauce, Bay leaf, Low-sodium soy sauce, Pepper, 
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl; stir well. Use to marinate beef,  pork, or lamb. Yield: 3/4 cup.
