Barbecued Shrimp and Chicken
shrimp, Onion powder, Ground cumin, Butter, Boneless chicken breast, Garlic, 
Date: Thu, 21 Mar 96 09:30:51 PST    From: eboyd@shentel.net    Peel shrimp and cut chicken into 1 inch cubes.  Combine Garlic, Onion  powder, Cumin and melted Butter. Marinate shrimp and chicken while reparing  grill. String shrimp and chicken onto separate skewers. Cook about 5 inches  above hot coals, allowing 5 minutes for shrimp and 10 minutes for chicken.  Turn once or twice while cooking and baste often with remaining marinade.
