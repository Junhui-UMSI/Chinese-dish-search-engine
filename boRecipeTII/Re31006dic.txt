Banana Nut Muffins
Millet, Bananas; ripe, Salt, Walnuts, Baking soda, Egg, Flour, Dark Brown Sugar, Plain yogurt, Whole wheat flour, Vegetable oil, 
Preheat oven to 350.    Mash bananas and add egg, oil, and yogurt.  Mix dry ingredients together  and add wet ingredients to dry ones.  Fill 12 greased muffin tins and bake  about 25 minutes.
