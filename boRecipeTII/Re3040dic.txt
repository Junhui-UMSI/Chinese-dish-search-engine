Peanut Cakes
Chopped peanuts, Raisins, Coconut, Sugar, Egg, well beaten, Flour, Baking soda, Salt, Sour milk, Butter or butter substitute, Baking Powder, 
Cream sugar and butter.  Add egg.  Beat thoroughly. Sift flour, measure,  and sift with baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add alternately with  sour milk to first mixture. Add raisins, coconut, and peanuts. Mix  thoroughly.  Fill well-oiled muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake in moderate oven  (375 F) 30 minutes.  Ice with maple icing. The Household Searchlight
