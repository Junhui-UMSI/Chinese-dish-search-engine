Crock-Pot Apple Cobbler
Vegetable oil, Tart apples, Sugar, Grated rind and juice of 1 lemon, Cinnamon, Butter, Natural cereal with nuts, 
Grease side of 3 1/2 quart Crock-Pot lightly with oil. Core, peel and slice  apples; place in Crock-Pot. Add sugar, lemon rind, lemon juice and  cinnamon. Combine butter and cereal. Add to Crock-Pot; mix thoroughly.  Cover. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hou    rs or High for 2 to 3 hours.
