Teriaki Sauce Mix
CLOVES GARLIC; PEELED &, RUM, Soy sauce, Water, Sugar, GRATED GINGERROOT; OR1/4DRY, 
1. COMBINE ALL INTREDIENTS IN A PLASTIC CONTAINER WITH A TIGHT FITTING LID.  LET STAND AT ROOM TEMPERATURE FOR 2-3 WEEKS (!). POUR INTO A CLEAN BOTTLE,  SEAL WELL; STORE AT ROOM TEMPERATURE.
