Szechuan Beef
Vegetable oil, chili paste, Scallion, green peppers, Black bean sauce, MM:MK VMXV03A, carrots, Vinegar, Dry sherry, Sugar, Hoisin sauce, Beef round or chuck, 
Heat oil in the wok and stir-fry the green peppers,        carrots, and  scallion for 1 to 2 mins; Push aside.         Stir-fry the slivers of beef  for 1 to 2 mins; and recombine the vegetables. Add the remaining     ingredients. Stir and heat throughly. Serve at once        with rice.
