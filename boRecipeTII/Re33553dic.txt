Swedish Beef Roast
Whiskey, Peppercorns, Anchovies, chopped (opt), Salt, Vinegar, Onion, minced, Beef chuck or rump roast, Bay leaf, Water, Brown sugar, Butter, 
Melt butter in a heavy kettle or deep frying pan.  Add beef roast and  brown well on all sides.    Add anchovies, onion, salt, bay leaf, peppercorns, sugar vinegar, whiskey  and water.  Cover tightly and cook slowly 2 to 2-1/2 hours or until meat  is tender.    Serve roast with the pan juice.  (The pan juice may be thickened for  gravy.  One-half cup of cream added makes a delicious gravy.)    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/swedish.zip
