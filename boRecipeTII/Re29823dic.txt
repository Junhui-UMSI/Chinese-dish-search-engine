Ten-Inch Strawberry Pie
Inch pie crust; baked, Carton Cool Whip, Sugar, Whole strawberries, Water, Cornstarch, Strawberry Jell-O, 
Mix sugar, cornstarch and water. Boil until thick and clear. Cool slightly.  Add Jell-O and mix well. Fill cooled pie crust with strawberries until  level. Pour cooled mixture over berries and let set until completely  cooled. Top with Cool Whip.
