Pizza Cups
(10 ounces each), Jar (14 ounces) pizza, Garlic powder, Ketchup, Hot or mild pork sausage, 
In a skillet, cook sausage over mediun heat; drain. Stir in pizza sauce,  ketch up and garlic powder; set aside. Press biscuits into 20 well-greased  muffin . cups. Spoon 1 or 2 tablespoons of the meat sauce into each  biscuit; top with mozzarella cheese and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake  at 350! for 10-15 minutes or until golden brown.
