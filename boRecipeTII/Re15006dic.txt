West Coast Pizzas
Moon -- cut in 24 wedges, Cracked black pepper, Fat-free Monterey Jack --, Tomato, fresh -- chopped, Green onions -- sliced, Cheese, Healthy Choi, Vegetable oil cooking spray, Flour tortillas -- 10", Fresh basil -- chopped, Ripe olives -- chopped, 
Heat oven to 400 F.  Lightly spray both sides of each tortilla with  non-stick cooking spray.  Place on baking sheet and bake at 400 F., 2  minutes on each side or until crisp and golden brown. Remove from oven and  reduce temperature to 350 F. Top each tortilla with half the tomato, basil,  onion, olives, and 12 wedges of cheese. Sprinkle with pepper to taste. Bake  at 350 F. for 5 minutes or until cheese melts. Cut each pizza into 8  wedges. 8 servings, each 123 calories, 13 g protein, 13 g carbo., 3 g total  fat, .5 g saturated fat, 6 mg cholesterol, 405 mg sodium, 2 g fiber.
