Basic Hamburgers
Blue Cheese, Water, Pickle Relish, Sesame Seed, Worcestershire Sauce, Onion, Olives, Horseradish, Mustard, Prepared, Chives, Dill Pickle, VARIATIONS, Pepper, Ground beef, Salt, Nuts, chopped, 
*  You should use only enough Blue Cheese to suit your own taste.  Mix all ingredients (including those in the variations that you choose)  together.  Shape mixture into 6 patties, each about 3/4-inch thick.  Broil  or grill patties 4-inches from the heat, turning once, to desired  doneness, 10 to 15 minutes.  Nice served on toasted buns with favorite  toppings.
