Swedish Red Cabbage
Onion, Lemon juice, Vinegar, Honey (or alternative), Red cabbage, Water, Sea salt, Green peas, Black pepper, Red wine or sherry, Apple; cored, 
1. Shred cabbage, slice apple and grate the onion.   2.  Heat honey in a heavy pot, add cabbage, stirring to coat, cover and  simmer 10 minutes.   3.  Stir in rest of ingredients except peas, simmer again for 1 to 1 1/2  hours, stir occasionally.   4.  Stir in peas just before serving. This mixture will reduce to 1/2 the  original size.
