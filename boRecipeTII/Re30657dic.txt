Tamale Pie with Cornmeal Cheese Crust
Yellow cornmeal, Sugar, FF Cheddar Cheese, Skim milk, Egg Product, Vegetable oil, Ground turkey breast, Green pepper, Onion, Garlic, Jalapeno pepper, 
8      ounces        Can tomato sauce 1 cup Whole kernel corn -- drained 2  teaspoons     Chili powder 1 1/2 teaspoons Ground cumin 1/3 teaspoon  Cayenne pepper 4 ounces FF Cheddar Cheese, HC -- shredded Light or FF Sour  Cream Salsa    Heat oven to 350 F.  Combine cornmeal and sugar; stir in 1 c. cheese, milk,  egg product and vegetable oil. Stir just until moistened. Press mixture on  bottom and up sides of a 9" pie plate sprayed with non- stick cooking  spray. In 10" skillet, cook ground turkey, green pepper, onion, garlic and  jalapeno pepper over medium heat until turkey is no longer pink. Drain.  Stir in tomato sauce, corn, chili powder, cumin and cayenne pepper. Spoon  turkey mixture into cornmeal crust. Bake at 350 F. for 30 to 35 minutes.  Remove from oven; sprinkle with 1 cup cheese. Return to oven and bake until  cheese melts.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving. Serve with fat-free sour  cream and salsa to add at the table if desired.
