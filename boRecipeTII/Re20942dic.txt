Apple Pancake Puff Low Calorie
Granulated sugar, Raisins, Skim milk, Vanilla extract, Orange juice, Mac. apples, margarine, Cinnamon, Eggs, flour, 
In heavy med. ovenproof skillet, melt 2 tsp of the margarine. Add apples,  raisins, 1 T sugar, and orange juice and cinnamon. cook, stirring freq. 6-8  minutes, until apples are just tender. Remove apples to bowl. Preheat oven  to 425d. In small bowl, w/ electric mixer, beat eggs until foamy. Gradually  add milk, flour, the remaining 2 TB. sugar and the vanilla; beat 2 minutes  longer, until batter is smooth. Add remaining 2 tsp. margarine to same  skillet. Place in oven to melt, abt 1 minute. POur batter into skillet;bake  10 minutes. Remove from oven and spoon apple mixture into center of  pancake. Return to oven and bake 12-15 min. longer until puffed and golden.  Cut into quarters. Each serving: 1/2 Fa, 1/2 P, 1/2 B, 1 FR, 60 cal.
