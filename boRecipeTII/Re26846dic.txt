Baked Bean Pot Lentils
Onions, Salt, Catsup, Dark brown sugar, Molasses, Lentils, Water, Dry mustard, Bacon, 
In saucepan, combine lentils, water, onion, and salt. Bring to a boil;  cover and simmer 45 minutes. Stir in remaining ingredients. Turn into  1-quart casserole dish and bake at 350 for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Stir  occasionally.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 185 by "Karen L. M.
