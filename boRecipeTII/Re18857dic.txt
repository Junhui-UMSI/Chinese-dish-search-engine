Andreas Baked Ziti
Hamburger, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Sauce of your choice, Parmesan cheese, Ziti, Parsley, Egg, 
Mix ricotta cheese, egg, parsley and parmesan cheese together.  Careful- ly mix hamburger with this mixture. Add ziti. Mix in 3/4 of  the sauce of your choice. Spread in a pan. Put the rest of the sauce  on top. Sprinkle mozarella cheese on top. Bake at 350F for 30-35  minutes.
