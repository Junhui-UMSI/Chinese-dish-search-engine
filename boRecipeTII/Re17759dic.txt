Almond Wafers Cookies
Sugar, Currant jelly, Butter, Gr. almonds, Salt, Vanilla, Flour, 
Cream butter and sugar to fluffy. Mix in vanilla, salt and almonds.  Add flour and mix just to blend. Divide in 3 pieces. Form each into  thin rectangle and wrap in plastic.  Chill. Roll 1 dough between  plastic wrap to 1/16 to 1/8 in. Cut in wafers and place on lined  sheet. Bake 10 min or until beginning to brown on edges. When cool  spread with jelly on one and top. Frost with icing.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
