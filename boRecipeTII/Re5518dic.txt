Pineapple Jelly
Pineapple juice, Sugar, Tart apple juice, 
Peel fresh pineapple. Remove the core. Partly cover the pineapple with  water. Boil until fruit is tender. Strain juice through jelly bag.  Wash tart apple. Cut into quarters without peeling and barely cover  with water. Cook until fruit is tender. Also strain through jelly  bag. Mix 2 cups of pineapple juice with 2 cups apple and bring to  boiling point. Add 3 cups of sugar and boil rapidly to the jelly  stage. Pour into sterilized jelly jars.
