Banana Nut Cheesecake
Margarine, BODY, banana, Chocolate wafer crumbs, Cold water, BASE, Walnuts, Margarine, cream cheese, TOPPING, Sugar, Eggs, Milk chocolate, 
BASE: Combine crumbs and margarine; press onto bottom of 9-inch springform  pan.  Bake at 350, 10 minutes.   BODY: Combine cream cheese, sugar and banana, mixing at medium speed on  electric mixer until well blended. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well  after each addition. Stir in walnuts; pour over crust.   Bake at 350, 40 minutes.  Loosen cake from rim of pan; cool before  removing rim of pan.   TOPPING: Melt chocolate pieces and margarine with water over low heat,  stirring until smooth.  Drizzle over cheesecake. Chill.
