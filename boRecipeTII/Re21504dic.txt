Apple Pie (No Sugar)
Concentrate, undiluted, Apple, To 1 tsp. cinnamon, Frozen apple juice, Tapioca or cornstarch, 
Mix apples and all ingredients until well coated; pour into pastry shell  and top with pastry.  Bake at 425 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes.    Recipe By     :    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #260      Date: Sun, 22 Sep 1996 22:08:56 -0400      From: BobbieB1@aol.com
