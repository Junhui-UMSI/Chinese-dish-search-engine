Sweet and Sour Celery
Egg, Celery, diced, Sour cream, Flour, Water, Salt, Sugar, Vinegar, 
Cook celery in a little salt water until soft and almost dry. Make a  dressing of the egg, flour, sugar, vinegar and water. Bring to a boil and  when it thickens add the sour cream. Pour this over the celery, heat and  serve.
