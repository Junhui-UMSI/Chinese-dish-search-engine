Bagna Cauda (Bon-Ya Codda)
olive oil, Anchovy fillets chopped,, Chopped fine garlic cloves, Mashed, Pepper, Stick (1/4 lb) butter, 
Heat the oil and butter together in an earthenware pot over hot water or in  a double boiler. In another pan cook the garlic in a bit of the oil until  it is soft. Add the anchovy fillets, and cook until the fish dissolves into  a paste, about 5 minutes. Add all to the pan of hot oil and butter. The hot  dip is kept in the middle of the table. Celery, endive, cucumbers, green  onions, Italian bread and cooked and cooled artichokes are dipped into it.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdjaxxx.zip
