Add juice to electric blender, then add remaining ingredients. Blend until  smooth.    Servings: 1 (2-cup) or 2 (1 cup)    * To toast almonds, spread in an ungreased baking pan. Place in 350 degree  oven for 5 to 10 minutes or until almonds are light brown; stir once or  twice to ensure even browning. Note that almonds will continue to brown  slightly after removing from oven.    (PER SERVING: 354 Calories; 10 g Fat; 0 mg Cholesterol; 5 mg Sodium; 66 g  Carbohydrate; 7 g Fiber; 7 g Protein.)
