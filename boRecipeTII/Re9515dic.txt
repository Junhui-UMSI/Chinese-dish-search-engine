Pumpkin Cheese Pie (Diabetic)
Canned pumpkin, Granulated sugar, Eggs, Evaporated milk, Egg, Vanilla, Granulated sugar, Pumpkin pie spice, Softened cream cheese, 
For cheese layer, combine cream cheese, sugar replacement, vanilla  and 1 egg in mixing bowl. Stir to mix well, spread in bottom of  unbaked pie shell. For pie layer, combine pumpkin, milk, 2 eggs sugar  replacement and spices in a mixing bowl, beating to blend thoroughly.  Carefully pour over cheese layer. Bake at 350 degree for 65 to 70 min  or until knife inserted comes out clean.
