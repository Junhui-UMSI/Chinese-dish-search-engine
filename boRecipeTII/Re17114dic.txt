Vinete (Eggplant)
Salt (to taste), Onion, medium, grated, Vegetable oil, Lemon juice, Eggplant, 
Broil eggplant on all sides until well cooked. Scrape off skin and mash  well with a wooden spoon. Add salt to taste. Mix grated onion with 1  teaspoon of the oil and add to eggplant. Add remaining oil to eggplant  slowly, stirring constantly. Add lemon juice and serve as an appetizer on  crackers or bread.  Posted to recipelu-digest by GramWag <GramWag@aol.com> on Mar 18, 1998
