Irish Soda Bread Mix with Recipe
Flour, Whole wheat flour, Dried currants, Light brown sugar, Baking Powder, Baking Powder, Bread mix, Butter, Eggs, Buttermilk, 
Mix: Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.    Irish Soda Bread: Preheat oven to 350. Grease and flour a 9" springform  pan. Cut butter into mix. Add eggs and buttermilk, stir until smooth. Pour  into pan and bake 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes  out clean. Serve warm.
