Barbequed Beef Ribs
Water, Pepper, Paprika, Salt, Catsup, Beef short ribs, Margarine, Dry mustard, Onions, Sugar, Worchestershire sauce, White vinegar, 
Contributed to the echo by: BOYD NARON BARBEQUED BEEF RIBS Brown ribs.  Saute onions in margarine and add remaining ingredients. Simmer 30 minutes.  Baste ribs with sauce and bake at 350 degrees for 2 hours. Drain off fat,  baste again and bake at 300 degrees for 1 hour.
