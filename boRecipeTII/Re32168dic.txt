Barbecued Pork Chops
Lemons; juice of, Tabasco, Black pepper, Vinegar, Brown sugar, Garlic salt, Onion, (8-oz) tomato sauce, Chili powder, Butter, Water, Pork chops (or more), -(up to), Worcestershire, Vegetable oil, 
Saute onions in butter until tender. Add remaining ingredients except chops  and simmer 15 minutes. Cook chops on slow grill without basting for 1 hour  until brown. Cook 1 more hour, basting with sauce often.    N.R.
