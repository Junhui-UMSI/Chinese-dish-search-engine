Brownies for Diabetics
Oleo, (scant) loquid sweetener, Squares unsweetened, Eggs, Vanilla, Flour, Baking Powder, Nuts; ( if desired ), 
Cream oleo and sweetener until light and fluffy. Add chocolate beat until  smooth. Beat in eggs and vanilla, Add flour, baking powder and nuts. Beat  until smooth. Spread batter in greased 8x8" pan and bake 30 mins.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Deb <leadfoot@alpha.ultraweb.net> on Aug  21, 1998,
