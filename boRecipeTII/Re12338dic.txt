Zucchini Sausage Torta
Grated Parmesan cheese, Bulk pork sausage, Zucchini, Cracker crumbs, Each: thyme; rosemary,, Eggs, Onions, 
Wash and trim off zucchini ends. Dont peel. Cook whole in boiling salted  water until tender. Drain and chop coarsely. Cook sausage and onion  together until sausage browns, stirring with a fork to break sausage. Add  zucchini to sausage and onion. Add all remaining ingredients except 2  tablespoons cheese. Mix well. Turn into greased 9-inch pie plate. Sprinkle  with reserved cheese. Bake at 350 degrees about 45 minutes or until firm  and brown.
