Acapulco Jicama Salad
Oranges, Vegetable oil, juice of 1 lime, Red-leaf lettuce leaves, Jicama, Kosher salt, Cilantro, Avocado, White wine vinegar, Pepper, 
1. Mince cilantro. Whisk together cilantro, lime juice, vinegar, oil, salt,  and pepper.  2. Peel oranges, and slice into 1/4-inch-thick circles. Peel and pit  avocado; slice into 1-inch cubes. Peel jicama and slice into 1/2-inch  cubes.  3. Marinate orange slices, avocado, and jicama in lime-cilantro vinaigrette  for       30    minutes.  4. Wash, spin dry, and tear lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Arrange lettuce  on chilled salad plates. Remove orange, avocado, and jicama from marinade  and place on lettuce.  Serves 6.
