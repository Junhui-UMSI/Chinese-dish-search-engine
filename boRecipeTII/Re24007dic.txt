Tossed Salad
Head lettuce or same, Mayonnaise; sufficient to, Salt and pepper to taste, Onion; sliced thinly, Parmesan cheese; to taste, Cauliflower or broccoli, Parsley, Bacon; cooked crisp,, 
In large salad bowl, layer the ingredients. Be sure to chop or break all  ingredients very fine. End layers with bacon. Spread the top with  mayonnaise and leave in refrigerator 24 hours. When ready to serve, toss  and add salt and pepper to taste.    REFRIGERATE 24 HOURS    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
