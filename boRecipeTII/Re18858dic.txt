Andreas Brunch
Ham, sliced thin, Butter, milk, Cornflakes, Eggs, Dry mustard, Salt, Sharp cheddar cheese, Bread of your choice, 
1. Butter both sides of 16 slices of bread. Place 8 slices in a  8 x 10 inch pan 2 inches deep. Cover bread with sharp cheese and  a slice of ham on each piece of bread. Top with the remaining 8  slices of bread.    2. Mix together all the rest of the ingredients except melted butter  and cornflakes. Pour over casserole and refrigerate overnight.    3. Before baking, mix the melted butter and cornflakes. Spread evenly  over casserole. Bake at 350F for 1 hour.
