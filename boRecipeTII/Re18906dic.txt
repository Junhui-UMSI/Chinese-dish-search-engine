Angel Food Cake #2
Egg whites, Sugar, Vanilla extract (or more), Cream of tartar, Flour, Salt, Sugar, Almond extract (or more), 
Date:     Tue, 27 Feb 1996 20:21:24 -7    From: arlenes@holly.ColoState.EDU    Prepare tube pan with Pam spray; preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Beat egg  whites with cream of tartar till holds shape; add 1/2 cup sugar.  Sift  together the flour, 1 cup sugar and the salt. Fold in eggs; add extracts.  Pour into pan and bake until done.
