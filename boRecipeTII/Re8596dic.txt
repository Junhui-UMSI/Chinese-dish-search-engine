Potatoes with Chives
Mayonnaise, Snipped Chives, Garlic Wine Vinegar, Salt and pepper, Soured Cream, Washed New Potatoes, with, 
Cook potatoes in lightly salted water for 10-15 minutes until just tender.  Drain and place in a bowl.    Mix together all the other ingredients and pour over cooked new potatoes.  Chill until needed.
