Turkey Filets with Parmigiano Reggiano
Butter, Breast of turkey, Egg, Vegetable oil, Breadcrumbs, Parmigiano Reggiano, 
Dip each turkey slice on both sides in the egg and then into the  breadcrumbs. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook the turkey  slices. When done, transfer to a butter smeared baking dish, cover with  Parmigiano Reggiaino and place in an oven preheated to 400 degrees for 10  minutes. Serve immediately. Serves 4 to 6 people.
