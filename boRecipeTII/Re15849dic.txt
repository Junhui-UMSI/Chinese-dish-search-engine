Acapulco Rice
Minute Rice, Tomato sauce, Chili powder, Water, Cheddar cheese, Crushed Tortilla Chips, 
Combine tomato sauce, water and chili powder in a medium sized saucepan.  Bring to a full boil.  Stir in rice.  Cover, remove from heat and let stand  for 10 minutes.  Sprinkle with tortilla chips and cheese.  Serve with  shredded lettuce and sour cream if desired.  From: Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sydsbook.zip
