Asparagus Salad with Pecans
Red leaf lettuce, fresh, Water, Pecans, Prepared light mayonnaise, Buttermilk mayonnaise, Asparagus, fresh, 
Date: 27 Apr 96 22:21:47 EDT    From: "Lisabeth Crawford (Pooh)" <104105.1416@CompuServe.COM>  Bring large pot of water to boil. Wash asparagus and snap off tough bottoms  os stems.  When water is boiling, add asparagus and let water return to a  boil. Cook about 3 minutes, until asparagus is crisp but tender. Remove  asparagus, run it under cold water, and refrigerate to chill. At serving  time, line six salad plates with lettuce and arrange four spears on each.  Top salads ith 1 tb of Buttermilk Mayonnaise or commercial light mayonnaise  and sprinkle with 1 ts chopped pecans.
