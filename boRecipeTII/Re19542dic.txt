Anise Cookies (Radola)
Flour, Ground Anise, Sugar, Eggs, 
Beat the eggs and the sugar to fluffy cream. Add flour and anise.    With 2 teaspoons place small heaps on your baking sheet. Let them dry  overnight.    Next morning heat your oven to 180 degrees Celsius(!) Bake the cookies for  8-10 minutes. They should keep their pale colour.
