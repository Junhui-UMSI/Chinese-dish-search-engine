Tomato and Chive Topping
Shallot; minced, Tomato; finely chopped, Pepper, Fresh chives; for garnish, olive oil, Dry white wine, Chopped fresh or frozen, Lemon juice, Salt, Garlic, 
Cook shallot and garlic in oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat,  stirring constantly, 1 minute. Add wine, and cook until reduced by half.  Stir in tomato, chopped chives, and next 3 ingredients. Spoon over salmon  or other fish and serve immediately. Garnish if desired. Makes about 1 cup.
