Banana Butterscotch Bread
Nutmeg, Salt, Sugar, Flour, Butterscotch morsels, Eggs, Ripe bananas; mashed, Baking soda, Butter, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, 
Cream together butter, eggs and sugar. Add bananas. Then add flour, baking  powder, salt, baking soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg and blend together. Fold in  butterscotch morsels. Place in two greased loaf pans and bake at 350  degrees for 28 minutes.    Recipe by: Ya Got It From Lisa Lepsy    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by LizaCooks <LizaCooks@aol.com> on May 5, 1998
