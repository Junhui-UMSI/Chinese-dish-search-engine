Taco Salad
Cn garbanzo beans,drain, Pitted black olives, sliced, Head iceberg lettuce,, Avocado, peeled and sliced, Sour cream, Ground beef, Salt, Pepper, LA TIMES, Onion, chpd, Radish slices, Tortilla or corn chips, Cheddar cheese, shredded, Bottled French dressing, Cn corn, drained, Tomatoes, cut in wedges, Dried oregano, crushed, 
Brown meat and drain off fat. Add 1/3 cup French dressing, onion, salt,  pepper and oregano. Simmer 5 minutes. Combine lettuce, tomatoes, corn,  beans, radishes and enough dressing to moisten. Toss lightly. For each  salad, serve meat mixture over tortilla chips. Top with lettuce mixture,  avocado, cheese, olives and sour cream, as desired.
