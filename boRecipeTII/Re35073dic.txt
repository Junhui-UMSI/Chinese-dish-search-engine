Beans/potato Curry
Turmeric, Urad dal, Vegetable oil, Vegetarian, Salt, Mustard seeds, 
Heat oil, mustard seeds, and urad dal.  Roast for a few seconds. Add  vegetables.  Sprinkle salt and turmeric. Cook on high heat for a couple of  minutes. Add coriander powder, chili powder, and cumin powder, (and ginger  and garlic if you wish) cover and cook on low until done. For making the  potatoes more crisp, add rice powder (arshi maav) and stir it in.      Rajeev Krishnamoorthy, rajeev@twain.EE.CORNELL.EDU
