Banana Gingerbread Bars
Egg, Extra-ripe DOLE Banana,, Lukewarm water, Powdered sugar, DOLE Banana, peeled and, DOLE Slivered Almonds, Juice from DOLE Lemon, Gingerbread cake mix (14.5oz, DOLE Raisins, 
Place medium banana in blender. Process until pureed; use 1/2 cup for  recipe.    Combine gingerbread mix, water, 1/2 cup pureed banana and egg in large  bowl. Beat well.    Stir in chopped banana, raisins and almonds.    Spread batter in greased 13x9" naking pan. Bake in 350F. oven 20-25  minutes until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.    Mix powdered sugar and 3 tablespoons lemon juice in medium bowl to make  thin glaze. Spread over warm gingerbread. Cool. Cut into bars. Sprinkle  iwth additional powdered sugar if desired.    Makes 32 bars.    From Favorite All Time Recipes - Homemade Holiday Cookies, Publication  International, Ltd., ISBN 0-7853-0147-X.
