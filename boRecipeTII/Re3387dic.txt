Peasant-Style Spaghetti
Spaghetti; cooked & drained, Vegetable oil, Ground oregano, Black pepper, Garlic, Tomatoes; peeled & chopped, Grated Parmesan cheese, Prosciutto; minced, Chopped parsley, 
Saute garlic and tomatoes in hot oil in skillet until tender, about 5  minutes. Add prosciutto, pepper and oregano. Reduce heat and simmer until  ingredients are blended, about 2 to 3 minutes. Toss with spaghetti and  sprinkle with parsley. Serve with Parmesan cheese.
