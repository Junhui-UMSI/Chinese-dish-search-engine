Quick Salsa
Whole tomatoes, drained, Vinegar, Garlic, Green chiles,chop,drain, Onions, Salt, Sugar, Tabasco sauce or Louisiana, Hot sauce, 
Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor. process very  briefly until the salsa is mixed but not too smooth. Taste and adjust  seasonings. If possible allow to sit for 30 min. to allow flavors to blend.  This recipe can be made in abt. 2 min. in processor. It can be used in any  recipe calling for Salsa Cruda; best in cooked dishes. Makes excellent  enchilada sauce. Yield: approx. 2 1/2 cups
