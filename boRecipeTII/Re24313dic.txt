Triple Chocolate Coffee Brownies
walnuts, fudge brownie, Milk chocolate pieces, White chocolate  baking pieces, Cooking oil, egg, Coffee liquer, coffee, Semisweet chocolate pieces, 
In a large mixing bowl combine egg, brownie mix, oil, liquer, coffee, milk  chocolate pieces, white baking pieces, semisweet chocolate pieces and nuts.  Stir just till combined. Spread in a greased 13x9x2 baking pan. Bake in a  350* oven for 30 min. Cool completely on a wire rack. Cut into bars. Makes       36    brownies.
