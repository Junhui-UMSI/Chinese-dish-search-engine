Bean and Franks Soup
Worsterceshire Sauce, American or Cheddar Cheese,, Garlic, Frankfuaters, cut into 1", Carrots, sliced, Onions, chopped, Baked Beans in Brown Sugar, Butter/Margarine/Bacon Fat, V-8 or Tomato Juice, 
Cook carrots, onions and garlic in the butter in a 3 Qt sauce pan. Stirring  frequently until crisp and tender. Stir in the remaining ingredients except  for the cheese. Heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally until hot.  Serve with cheese.    Source: "The Yankee Kitchen" 04-06-93 [#2] Cheryl
