Strawberry Loaf Cake
Cinnamon, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Flour, Baking soda, Salt, Eggs, strawberries, frozen, 
04/23/92       10:12 PM     Combine flour, soda, salt, cinnamon and sugar; mix together well. In  another bowl, combine eggs, oil and (sliced) strawberries. Add to the dry  ingredients and mix well. Pour into 2 greased 9x5" loaf pans. Bake in  pre-heated 350 degree oven for about 1 hour.    Leave in pan to cool. When almost cool, turn onto wire rack and dust top  with confectioners sugar.    These freeze well and are very moist when thawed. If you freeze them,  dont dust with confectioners sugar until thawed.
