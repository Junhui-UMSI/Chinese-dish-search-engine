Aduki, Orzo and Pepper Salad
Crumbled goat or feta cheese, Cooked orzo pasta, Packed Italian parsley,,, Salt, Finely sliced black pitted, Finely diced crunchy fennel, Slivered roasted red peppers, Dressing made with olive, Cooked aduki beans,
