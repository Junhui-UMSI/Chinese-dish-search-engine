Quick Brown Rice Pilaf
Margarine or butter, Dried rosemary or marjoram, Uncooked instant brown rice, Chicken broth, Shredded carrot, Green Onions, -(up to), 
In medium saucepan, bring broth and margarine to a boil. Stir in remaining  ingredients. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 5 minutes.     Remove saucepan from heat; stir. Cover; let stand 5 minutes. Fluff with  fork before serving. TIP: Use amount of rice indicated on package to make 4  servings.
