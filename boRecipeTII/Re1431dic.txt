Evergreen Mint Punch
Vanilla ice cream, softened,, (1 quart) milk, Green creme de menthe, Candy canes (optional), 
In a punch bowl, combine 1/2 gallon ice cream and creme de menthe; stir  until it reaches a pourable consistency. Make scoops of vanilla ice cream  ad float on the punch. Serve immediately garnished with candy canes, if  desired.  Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
