Polynesian Prawns
Japanese plum wine or sake, Dijon mustard, Salt -- to taste, Lemon Peel, Lemon juice, Cayenne pepper -- to taste, Purple plums, Red wine vinegar, Large prawns -- boiled and, Shelled, Plum Sauce-----, Parsley, 
Arrange prawns on a serving platter. Combine parsley and lemon peel and  sprinkle over prawns. Serve with Plum Sauce and cocktail picks.    Serves 10.    Plum Sauce: Cut a cross in the bottom of each plum. Plunge into boiling  water briefly, then into a bowl of ice water. Peel the plums (skins should  peel off easily) and pit them. In a food processor or blender, combine  plums and lemon juice and process until smooth. Stir in vinegar, plum wine,  and mustard. Season with cayenne and salt.
