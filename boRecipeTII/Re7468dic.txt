Pork Loin in Green Sauce
salt and pepper, Onion, Garlic, Parsley), Green chiles - roasted;, Onion; quartered, Tomato; peeled and, Lean pork; boneless, Marjoram, Quartered, Tomatillos (or green tomato, Bay leaf, Cooking oil, Salt, Thyme, Cilantro; chopped (or, Broth from meat, 
Cover meat with cold water, Add onion, bay leaf, marjoram, and thyme. Cook,  covered, 3 hours. Add salt after 1 1/2 hours. Puree onion, garlic,  tomatillos, tomato, and chiles. Fry paste in hot oil for 5 minutes. Stir in  cilantro and broth; salt and pepper to taste. Slice the meat, add it to the  sauce, and simmer 30 minutes. Serve with hot rice or boiled potatoes.
