Champagne Mustard
Red pepper flakes, Champagne, Dry Colemans Mustard, Eggs beaten, Sugar, Butter, Each black and white pepper, Cider vinegar, 
Heat vinegar and sugar to dissolve in large heavy saucepan. Cool. Add rest  of ingredients, beating in the eggs one at a time, and put on low heat and  bring to the boil stirring with a wire whisk all the time. Boil 5 minutes.  Bottle, cool, cap and put in the refrigerator.    If you use 2 large cans of mustard, which are 4 oz. each, add one more egg  and use a whole split of champagne (small bottle).
