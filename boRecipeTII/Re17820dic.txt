Almost Maple Syrup
Water, Vanilla extract, Brown sugar, Salt, 
In saucepan dissolve sugar in water. Add salt and boil for 1 minute. Add  vanilla and mix well. Serve warm.  Yield: 3/4 cup.    ELLON COCKRILL (MRS. ROGERS)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
