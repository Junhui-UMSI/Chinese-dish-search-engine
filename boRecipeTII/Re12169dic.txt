Zucchini Bake
Canola oil, Shredded zucchini (3-4 cups), Eggs, Mix, Buttermilk, Bisquick or similar biscuit, Grated Parmesan cheese, Onion, grated, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a 9-inch pan or 11-by-7-inch baking  dish with a nonstick vegetable coating; set aside. Combine all ingredients  in a large bowl and mix well. Pour into the prepared pan.    Bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour or until lightly browned. Let  rest 5 minutes before cutting into 6 servings.
