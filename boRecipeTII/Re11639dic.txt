Raspberry Squares
Vanilla, flour, Coconut, Salt, Margarine, Egg, preserves, Sugar, Baking Powder, Pecans, 
Cream butter and sugar.  Add egg and vanilla; sift flour and baking powder  and salt; add to creamed margarine batter, then add coconut and nuts.  Batter will be very thick. Grease and flour a 9x9 inch pan. Put about 2/3  of batter in the pan, spread jam over it; sprinkle remaining batter on top.  Bake at 350F for 35-45 minutes. From Linda Martin-Bronson    Recipe By     : Concord Hospital Admitting Cookbook, Concord, NH
