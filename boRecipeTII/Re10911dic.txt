Rabbit Pie
Prepared biscuits, salt and pepper, Lg. onion, diced, Jack rabbits -or-, Cottontails, Reserved stock, Medium onion, diced, Salt, GRAVY, Sage, Pepper, Celery stalk, diced, Celery (from above stalk), Chicken bouillon, Poultry seasoning, Flour, 
Soak rabbits in salt water in refrigerator overnight. Dry, cut up and stew  rabbits with celery and onions for 2-3 hours. Add salt and pepper to taste,  then strip meat from bones and put in 26-inch pie pan, add stock or gravy  for moisture.  Cover with biscuits. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.   For gravy, take reserved stock, add bouillon, onions and celery. Bring to  a boil.  Strain.  Mix flour with an equal amount of water, add sauce to  thickening. Season to taste with spices.    Source:  N.A.H.C. Wild Game Cookbook Author: Michael D. Ferrin, Lyman WY
