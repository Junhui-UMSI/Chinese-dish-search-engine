Avocado Filling
Avocados, peeled and finely, Cilantro,, finely chopped, Sea salt, to taste, Limes, Juice of, Serrano chiles or 1 small, White onion, peeled and,, Freshly ground black, 
Mash the avocados with a fork and stir in the onions, cilantro, chiles, and  lime juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
