Egg Fried Rice
Egg beaters 99% egg, cooked rice, Frozen green peas, Salt, Black pepper, Fresh bean sprouts, Onions, Soy sauce, 
Lightly spray a large nonstick skillet or wok with cooking spray and heat  over medium-high heat. Add egg mixture; stir-fry 2 minutes. Add rice;  stir-fry 3 minutes. Add green peas, salt, and pepper; stir-fry 5 minutes.  Add bean sprouts and onions; stir-fry 2 minutes. Serve immediately. Yield:  6 Servings (serving size: 1 cup).
