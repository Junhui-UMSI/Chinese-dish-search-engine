Thai Sweet Chili Sauce
Plum sauce, All-purpose flour, red chili paste, Fresh lime juice, Tomato paste, Garlic, Thai fish sauce, Water, Sugar, Sweet paprika, Salt, Rice vinegar, serrano chili, 
Combine all ingredients and 1/2 c water in a small saucepan, and bring to a  boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for 4 minutes. Let cool. Will keep in  refrigerator for 3 days.    Shared by Michael Kean, Prodigy ID# VMXV03A.
