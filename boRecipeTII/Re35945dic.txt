Stuffed Pattypan Squash
Package frozen creamed peas, Water, Or mix veggies, Salt, Pattypan squash(light green, Summer squash), 
In slow-cooking pot, cover squash with water; add salt.  Cover and cook on  low for 6 to 7 hours or until squash is tender.  Drain.  Meanwhile, cook  peas or vegetables according to package directions.  Scoop out squash pulp  in center.  Fill with hot creamed peas or hot mixed creamed vegetables.
