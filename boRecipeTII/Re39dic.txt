John Marshs Talliwagger Southern Style Bbq Sauce
Honey, Molasses, Head garlic; broken unpeeled, Whole cumin seeds, Whole coriander seeds, Whole black peppercorns, Whole white peppercorns, Dried red chiles, Bay Leaves, Tomato paste, (16 Oz) tomatoes, White vinegar, Water, Salt, 
Combine first 9 ingredients in stock pot. Cook on medium heat 30 minutes.  Add paste and tomatoes and cook 15 minutes breaking up the tomatoes. Stir  in vinegar, water and salt. Simmer and reduce to desired consistency  uncovered for 2-4 hours. Puree in blender. Refrigerate for a day or two to  age.
