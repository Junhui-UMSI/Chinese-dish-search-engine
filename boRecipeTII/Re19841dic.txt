Apple and Cheddar Cheese Crumble
Cheddar cheese, Flour, Unsalted butter, Apples, Ground cinnamon, Brown Sugar, Granulated sugar, Rolled oats, 
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees farenheit. Grease a 10-inch square baking  dish.    2. Peel and core apples. Cut each into 8 pieces. In a medium bowl, toss  together apples, cinnamon, and brown sugar. Place in baking dish.    3. In a medium bowl, combine cheese, flour, oats, and granulated sugar. Cut  unsalted butter into dry ingredients with your fingers, a pastry blender,  or two knives. Combine until mixture holds together in large crumbs. Cover  apples with crumb mixture. Bake until apples are tender and topping is  slightly browned (35 minutes).
