Swordfish and Halibut Steak Duxelles
Green onion; chopped, Lemon juice, Parsley, Fish steaks; 1 inch thick, Mushrooms; fine chopped, Butter, 
Turbot and Salmon are also suitable. Mix butter, parsley, green onion,  mushrooms and lemon juice. Spread half of mixture in bottom of a baking  dish. Arrange 4 fish steaks on top and add desired seasoning. Cover with  remaining mixture. Cover and microwave at high 6-8 minutes. Let stand  covered 2 minutes.
