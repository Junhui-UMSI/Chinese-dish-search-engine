Porterhouse Pork Chops with Caramelized Onions and Pecans
Onion, Pork Chops, Brown sugar, Soy sauce, Butter, Pecans, Balsamic vinegar, 
Broil or grill pork chops to desired doneness. Place pecans in a large  skillet over medium heat; stir until dark and fragrant, 1-2 minutes. Remove  pecans and reserve. Melt butter in same skillet; add onion and saute until  golden brown. Add brown sugar and soy sauce; stir 1 minute. Add pecans and  vinegar; stir 1 minute. Serve onion mixture over hot pork chops.    Serving Ideas: Serve with asparagus and potatoes    Suggested Wine: Merlot    Per serving: 486 Calories; 37g Fat (67% calories from fat); 25g Protein;  15g Carbohydrate; 120mg Cholesterol; 925mg Sodium    Recipe by: Scottys Home Delivery, Palatine IL., Posted to MC-Recipe Digest  V1 #589 by Habanera9@aol.com on Apr 27, 1997
