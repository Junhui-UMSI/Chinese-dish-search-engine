Tomato Balsamic Dressing - Homemakers
olive oil, Garlic, Salt, Balsamic vinegar, Tomatoes, Pepper, Fresh basil, 
1.  In blender, chop garlic.    2.  Cut tomatoes in half and squeeze out seeds.  Add to garlic and blend  until pureed.    3.  Add vinegar, salt and pepper and blend a few seconds.  Blend in oil.  Stir in basil.    Per serving:       58    calories    Contributor:  Homemakers Apr 97    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Cathleen <catht@interlog.com> on Apr 21,  1998
