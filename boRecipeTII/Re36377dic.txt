Beer - Cheese Bits
Bisquick baking mix, Cheddar cheese, Beer, Melted butter/ margarine, Sesame/poppy seeds, 
Heat oven to 450 degrees. Mix baking mix, cheese and beer until soft dough  forms; beat vigorously; 20 strokes. Gently smooth dough into ball on  floured cloth board. Knead 5 times. Roll dough into rectangle, 16 x 10  inches. Cut into 2-inch squares, cut squares diagonally into halves. Spread  with melted margarine/butter; sprinkle with sesame seeds. Separate and  place on ungreased cookie sheets. BAke until brown, about 8 minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 580 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 23, 1998
