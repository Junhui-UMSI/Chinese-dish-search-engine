White Bean Meat Casserole
Onion, Parmesan cheese, Carrot; diced, Chicken broth; fat removed, Chicken breast; no skin, no, Cannelini beans; cooked, Whole wheat bread crumbs, Tomatoes; peeled, diced, Whole bay leaf, Garlic powder, Dried Oregano, Ground pepper, 
In a dutch oven, cook sausage, then drain well. Add beans, onions, carrots,  tomatoes, garlic powder, bay leaf, oregano, and pepper. Mix well. add stock  and bring to a boil. Then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes. Preheat  oven to 325F.    Remove bay leaf. Add chicken and pork, stirring with a wooden spoon. Turn  into a 2-quart ovenproof casserole.    In a small bowl, mix bread crumbs and parmesan cheese. Spread on top of  casserole and bake until heated through, about 30 minutes.
