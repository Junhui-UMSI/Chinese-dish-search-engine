Swedish Limpa Bread (Eb)
Water, Butter, Honey, Flat ale, Rye flour, Salt, Anise seed, Bread flour, Dry milk, Orange peel, Yeast, Molasses, Cardamom, 
Produce the flat ale by opening a dark beer and stirring until the bubbles  are dispersed. Scoop off residual foam before measuring ale.    Bake on regular cycle.
