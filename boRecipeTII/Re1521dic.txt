Broccoli Frittata
Onion, Frozen, chopped broccoli, Parmesan cheese, milk, Mozzarella, Butter, Garlic, Rice, cooked, Eggs, Salt, Pepper, 
Saute onion in butter until tender. Do not brown. Add broccoli, garlic,  rice and parmesan, mix well. Combine eggs, milk and seasonings. Stir into  rice mixture. Turn into well-buttered shallow 1 quart casserole. Top with  mozzarella cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes until set.
