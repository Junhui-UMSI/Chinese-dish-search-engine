Raspberry Sauce - Master Chefs
Raspberries, whole, Water, Almonds, sliced, toasted, and drained, Raspberries, frozen, thawed, Sugar, (opt), Juice of one lemon, Whipping cream, Raspberries, fresh OR, 
In saucepan, combine all sauce ingredients except lemon juice.  Bring to boil, then boil gently, stirring, about 7 minutes.  Reduce  heat, stir in lemon juice, and keep warm.         To garnish, remove parfait from freezer.  Carefully release  springform ring (if difficult, warm ring slightly withy your palms).  Whip 1/2 cup cream until stiff; form decorative border or rosettes  around top and bottom edge of parfait by piping whipped cream through  pastry bag fitted with star tip.  Arrange 2 raspberries and 2 or 3  almond slices on top of each serving.  Cut into wedges.  Serve with  warm raspberry sauce, if desired.
