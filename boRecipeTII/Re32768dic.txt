Sweet Potato Patties
Sweet potatoes, cut into, Pepper and nutmeg, Butter, Pecans, ground, Virgin or extra-virgin olive, Hot skimmed milk, Pecans, chopped, Salt, 
Drain sweet potatoes and mash with a fork, ricer or electric mixer. Add  oil, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and milk, beating until light and fluffy. Stir  in chopped pecans and shape into 8 flat round patties. Dip both sides into  ground pecans to coat.  Place on a lightly oiled baking sheet and dot with  butter.  Bake in a preheated 450F. oven for 20 minutes. Serve with a  mushroom gravy.
