Pfeffernuesse Cookie Mix (Spicy German Cookie
All-purpose flour, Sugar, Cinnamon, ground, Eggs, Cloves, ground, Light molasses, Nutmeg, ground, Powdered sugar, sifted, Butter or margarine, Baking soda, 
Stir together flour, sugar, baking soda, spices, and dash black pepper. In  large saucepan, combine molasses and butter; heat and stir until butter  melts. Cool to room temperature. Stir in eggs. Add dry ingredients to  molasses mixture; mix well. Cover. Chill several hours or overnight. Shape  into 1" balls. Place on greased cookie sheet. Bake in 350 degree oven for  12 to 14 minutes or until cookies are done. Remove. Cool. Roll in powdered  sugar.
