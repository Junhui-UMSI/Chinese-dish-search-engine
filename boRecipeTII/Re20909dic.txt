Apple Oat Crisp
Brown sugar, TOPPING, Applesauce, Whole wheat flour, Apples; cored & sliced, Cinnamon, Cinnamon, Lemon rind, & 1 tsp. diet margarine, Lemon juice, Quick Oats, Sugar, 
:       Mix the base mix in an 8" baking dish. Combine the topping mix and  crumble over the apples. Bake 350, 35-40 minutes. Exchanges: 1/2 bread, 1/2  fat, 1 fruit, 35 calories. Source: Weight Watchers
