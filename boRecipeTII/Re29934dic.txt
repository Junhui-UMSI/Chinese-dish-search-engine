Teriyaki Meat Balls
Eggs, (14 oz.) beef broth, Sugar, Soy sauce, Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Sake, Ginger root, Cornstarch, Flour, Ground beef, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Grease a large baking pan. Comnine  beef, eggs, flour, salt, and pepper. Mix lightly and shape into small  balls about 3/4 to 1 inch in diameter. Place in prepared baking pan  and bake for 14-17 minutes. Mix cornstarch with sugar and soy sauce.  Stir in beef broth, sake, ginger, and garlic. Cook in skillet or  chafing dish until mixture thickens slightly, stirring constantly.,  Add meat balls and serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
