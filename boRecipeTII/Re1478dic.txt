Fireworks Slaw
Pommery mustard, Dijon style mustard, Shallots, finely diced, Chopped fresh thyme, Champagne vinegar, Canola oil, Green cabbage, shredded, Carrots, julienned, Red bell pepper, julienned, Yellow bell pepper/julienned, Jicama, peeled;shredded, Red onion, thinly sliced, Beet (raw), peeled, shredded, Salt, Black pepper, 
Mix together the mustards, shallots, thyme and vinegar.  Slowly whisk in  the canola oil to emulsify the dressing.    Toss the dressing with the vegetables and season with salt/pepper to taste.    Signature Dish: Chef Becky Foulk of Fresh Fields, Vienna Va.
