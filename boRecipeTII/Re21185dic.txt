Tzatziki
Yogurt, unflavoured, Medium cucumber, peel, grat, Cloves garlic, crushed, Dried dillweed, olive oil, Salt, pepper, 
Place a piece of cheesecloth in a colander and pour in the yogurt. Drain  for several hours. Place grated cucumber in another colander and allow to  drain for 2 hours.  Mix together all ingredients, chill. Drizzle  additional olive oil on top. Serve as a dip for vegetables, or a spread  for bread.
