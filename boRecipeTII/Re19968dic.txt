Apple Butter From Brigitte Sealing
Apples (or 8 if small), Cinnamon, Cornstarch, +2 TB conc. frozen apple, Water, Water, Ground cloves, 
1. Peel, core, and thinly slice apples. Put them in a  saucepan with 1/4  cup of the    water and 1/4 cup of the  apple juice. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat  and simmer    uncovered for 1 hour. Mix cornstarch with the 2  tb of apple juice. Stir  into the mix,    and stir until it  thickens. 2. Put all ingredients in a blender and puree  until  smooth. Refrigerate. Makes about 4    cups.
