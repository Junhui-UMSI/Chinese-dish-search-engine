Bardys Cheesy Garlic Bread
Parmesan cheese, Butter, Grated mozzarella cheese, Garlic, Cheddar Cheese, Butter, Cheese wiz, Wide loaf french bread, 
Preheat oven to 350. Cut bread into twelve slices. Combine 1/2 cup butter  with garlic. Spread on one side of bread slices. Puree remaining  ingredients in food processor till smooth. Spread mixture on buttered side  of bread. Place on greased cooky sheet and bake until toasted. Cut each  slice in half. Makes 24 slices.
