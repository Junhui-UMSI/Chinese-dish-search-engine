Tortilla Beef
Garlic, Round steak, Beef broth, Tomatoes (14 1/2 oz.); peele, Carrots; chopped, Lime or lemon juice, Onion, Pepper, Marjoram, Salt, 
Spread ,minced garlic on round steak and sprinkle with salt, pepper and  lime juice. Place in the slow cooker and add remaining ingredients. Set  cooker on low and cook for 8 hours. Meat should be fork tender and shred  easily. Fill tortillas with favorite fillings and meat.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "bunny" <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Feb 26,  1998
