Pork and Corn Stuffing Bake
Onion, Pepperidge Farm Corn Bread, Spicy brown mustard, Paprika, Boneless pork chops, 3/4", Finely chopped celery, Brown sugar, 
Prep Time: 10 minutes. Cook Time: 30 minutes    1. Combine soup, stuffing, celery, onion and paprika. In a 9" greased  pie plate, spoon stuffing. Arrange chops on stuffing, pressing  lightly into stuffing.    2. Combine sugar and mustard. Spread mixture evenly over chops.    3. Bake at 400F. 30 minutes or until chops are no longer pink.  Garnish with celery leaves if desired. Serves 4    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
