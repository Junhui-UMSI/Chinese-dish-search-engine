Tatale (Ghanaian Plantain Cakes)
Palm oil, Plantains -- over-ripe -, Cayenne -- to taste, Black, Onion -- finely chopped, Vegetable oil -- for frying, Self-rising flour, Salt -- to taste, 
Peel and mash the plantains well.  Put into a bowl and add enough of the  flour to bind.  Add the onion, palm oil, salt and pepper to taste. Mix well  and leave to stand for 20 minutes.  Fry in spoonfuls in a little hot oil  until golden brown.  Drain on kitchen paper and serve hot.
