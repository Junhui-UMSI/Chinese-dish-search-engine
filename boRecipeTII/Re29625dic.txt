Tasty Duck
Slices bacon, -(up to), Onions, Salt and pepper, Soup cans water, Rice, (10-oz) cream of chicken, Onion, Sliced celery, Water, Apple; chopped, Ducks, Wine, Dry onion soup mix, 
Boil ducks, onion, apple, salt and pepper in water to cover for 1 hour.  Drain. Debone ducks and chop. In large casserole, combine rice, soups,  water, celery and onion. Add ducks; put bacon over ducks; pour in a little  wine. Cover and bake at 325 for 1-1/2 hours.
