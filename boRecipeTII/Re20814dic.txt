Apple Ham Slaw
Toasted walnut/pecan pieces, Mayonnaise, Fresh Lemon Juice, Diced cooked ham, Dijon mustard, Diced apples, Salt and pepper, Shredded cabbage, Raisins or currants, 
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Serve cold or hot.  Serves 4    Source: The Oakville Journal. August 20, 1996  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #229    Date: Fri, 23 Aug 1996 17:16:20 -0400    From: Cindy J Hartlin <cjhartlin@accent.net>
