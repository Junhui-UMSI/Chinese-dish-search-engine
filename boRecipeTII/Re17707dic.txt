Almond Rice Muffins
Ground almond, Arrowroot powder, AM unrefined vegetable oil, Honey, Almond extract, Water or soymilk, Am brown rice flour, Non-alum baking powder, 
Date: Mon, 25 Mar 1996 13:43:47 -0500    From: Walt Gray <waltgray@mnsinc.com>  Mix dry and liquid ingredients separately, then combine the two and mix  thoroughly (this is a thin batter). Fill 12 paper muffin cups 2/3 full and  sprinkle tops with almond. Bake at 350 F. for 25-30 minutes.
