Tangy Salad Dressing Mix
Sugar, Garlic powder, Salt, Skim milk powder, Cold water, Lemon juice, Mayonnaise or salad dressing, Dried basil, crushed, Dried onion flakes, Dry mustard, To Serve:, 
1. Combine all dry ingredients. Store in an airtight container. Makes about  1 cup. 2. To make up, combine 1/4 cup of dry dressing mix with 1/2 cup cold  water and lemon juice. Blend in mayonnaise or salad dressing. Shake well  before serving.    makes 1/2 cup dressing.
