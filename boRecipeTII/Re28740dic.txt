Texas-Style Barbecued Ribs (En)
Lean pork ribs, (6 to 10), Dry mustard, Barbecue sauce (recipe, White wine, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.    Boil ribs in a large pot of salted water for 5 minutes; drain well. Arrange  in a large baking pan.    Mix mustard with wine and coat ribs with the mixture, using a pastry brush.    Brush ribs with barbecue sauce. Bake on middle oven shelf, turning often  and brushing with additional sauce, for 45 minutes to 1 hour. Pork is done  when meat pulls easily from the ribs. Serve with hot beans and cold beer.
