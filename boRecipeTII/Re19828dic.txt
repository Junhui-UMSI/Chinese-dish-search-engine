Apple and Apricot Envelopes
Mixed spice, Apricots; finely chopped, Brown sugar, Light salad oil, Orange zest and juice to, Apples; peeled cored and, Sheets phyllo pastry, 
Mix apples, apricots, sugar and mixed spice together. Lay one sheet of  pastry onto a flat surface. Brush with oil and cover with second sheet of  pastry. Spoon filling into the pastry and fold into a envelope shape.  Continue in this way until all the filling is used up. Bake at 200 C for 20  mins. or until golden brown.    busted by Sarah G.
