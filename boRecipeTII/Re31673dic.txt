Swiss Steak
Lg Onion; sliced, Salt & pepper; to taste, Garlic; mashed, Vegatable oil, Med Carrot; quartered length, Celery; sliced, Flour, Steak;1" thick round steak, Tomato, 
Rub steak with garlic and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Pour 1/4 cup of  flour onto meat and pound it into the steak with a meat pounder or the edge  of a heavy saucer. Turn and prepare other side in the same manner. Heat oil  in a large frying pan and brown meat on both sides. Add remaining  ingredients. Cover tightly and simmer over low heat for 1 1/2 hours or  until meat is tender.  Cut into serving sized pieces and serve with gravy  from pan. Good with noodles, rice or mashed potatoes.
