Raisin Buglur Pilaf
Golden pr dark raisins;, Bulgur, Boiling water;, Salt;, Ground cumin;, 
Place bulgur in a medium-size bowl and cover with boiling water.  Add  remaining ingredients and stir to combine.  Let stand 30 minutes until  water is absorbed.  Fluff and serve warm.
