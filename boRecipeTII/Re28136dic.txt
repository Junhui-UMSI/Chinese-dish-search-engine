Baked Creamed Cabbage
Butter, Bread crumbs, Flour, milk, Cabbage, Salt, salted water, 
Shred cabbage and cook 9 minutes in boiling, salted water. Remove cabbage,  drain well, and place in buttered 1-1/2 quart casserole. Melt butter in  pan, stir in flour and salt; cook until smooth. Add milk gradually.  Continue stirring until thick. Pour sauce over cabbage and sprinkle bread  crumbs over top. Bake at 325 for 15 minutes or until crumbs are brown.    MRS E.J.
