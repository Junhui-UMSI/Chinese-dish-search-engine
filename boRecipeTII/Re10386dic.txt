Quick Chicken Quesadillas
Cheddar cheese, Flour tortillas, refried beans, chicken meat, Salsa, Nonfat sour cream, Lettuce, Tomatoes, Green Onions, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 5 min.    Turn on broiler. Arrange tortillas on a cookie sheet. Spread refried beans  over tortillas. Add salsa, then layer with chicken, onions, and cheese.  Place cookie sheet under broiler 1-2 minutes or until the cheese melts and  the tortillas are crisp. Serve topped with sour cream, lettuce and  tomatoes.
