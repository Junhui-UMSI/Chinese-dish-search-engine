Pork Chops in Tomato Sauce with Oregano
Plum tomatoes, Water, Salt and pepper, Loin pork chops, olive oil, Garlic, Mushrooms, Green pepper, Dry white wine, Oregano, 
1. Put the tomatoes, with the water and bring to a boil. Cook, stirring  occasionally, until reduced to about 1 1/2 cups. Add the salt and pepper to  taste, set aside. 2. Sprinkle the chops with salt and pepper. 3. Heat the  oil in the skillet and add the chops. Brown on both sides, about 5 min. to  a side. Add the garlic, mushrooms and green peppers. Coover closely and  cook about 5 min. 4. Stir in the wine, tomatoes and oregano. Cover closely  and cook 35-40 min. Serve with buttered noodles sprinkled with parm cheese.    The name for this dish:  Cotes de porc "pizzaiola"
