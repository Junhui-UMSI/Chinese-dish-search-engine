Popeyes Garden Tonic
Tomato, Cherry tomato for garnish, Stalks of asparagus, Stalks of celery, Handful of spinach, 
What better way for Olive to show her love for Popeye than with this  healing drink?    Bunch up spinach and push through hopper with celery. Juice asparagus with  tomato. Mix juices in a tall glass and garnish with a cherry tomato.    Recipe by: Cherie Calbom    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 493 by ctlindab@mail1.nai.net on  Jan 11, 1998
