Tennessee Chili
Each, cumin & salt, Butter or margarine, To 6 tbsp. chili powder, (14 1/2 oz.) can beef broth, Black Pepper, (16 oz.) can red kidney, (16 oz.) can stewed, Green pepper, chopped, Each, oregano & sugar, (8 oz.) can tomato sauce, Water, Beef chuck, cut into 1/2, Garlic clove, crushed, Cornmeal, Onion, Bay Leaves, 
Preparation : In Dutch oven melt butter or margarine over high heat. Add  beef and brown. Drain excess fat. Stir in onion, green pepper and garlic;  saute until vegetables are softened, 3 minutes. Stir in next 7 ingredients;  cook 2 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil; reduce heat,  cover and simmer 1 hour. Simmer uncovered 1 to 1 1/2 hours more. Discard  garlic and bay leaves. Makes 2 quarts, 660 calories per cup. This favorite  recipe of Governor McWherters mother, Lucille, won Honorable Mention in  the 1988 Ladies Home Journal "Great Chili Cook-Off.") From: Tracy Schell  Date: Tue, 01-0
