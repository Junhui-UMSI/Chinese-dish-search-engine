Western Chick Chunks Recipe
Fat free ranch dressing, Cornflake crumbs, Skinless boneless chicken, Salt (optional: I left out), Black pepper ( I used 2 tsp), Plus, 
from Weight Watchers Slim Ways Chicken    1. Preheat oven to 450 degree F. Line large baking sheet with foil: spray  with nonstick cooking spray.    2. In small bowl, combine chicken and ranch dressing, tossing well to coat  thoroughly. On shallow plate or in gallon size sealable plastic bag,  combine cornflake crumbs, salt and pepper. Add chicken pieces, one at a  time to crumb mixture, turning chicken or sealing bag and shaking to coat  evenly.    3. Arrange chicken peices on prepared baking sheet; bake 15-20 minutes,  until cooked through and juices run clear when pierced with a fork.    Makes 4 servings:        1 g  fat, 0g fiber, 117 calories, 17g protein, 8g carbohydrate,       41 mg cholesterol,  Posted to Digest eat-lf.v097.n056 by Jean Mulrenan  <jean.e.mulrenan.1@nd.edu> on Feb 27, 1997.
