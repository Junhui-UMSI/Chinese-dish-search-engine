Aggression Cookies
Flour, Butter, Brown sugar, Baking Powder, Oatmeal, 
Dump all ingredients in a large bowl. Mash it, knead it, pound it! The  longer and the harder you mix it, the better it tastes. Roll dough into  small balls, and bake on cookie sheet at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes,  or until lightly browned.
