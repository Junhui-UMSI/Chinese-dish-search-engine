Balsamic Marinade for Grilled Vegetables
Yellow squash, Shallots, Zucchini, Balsamic vinegar, garlic paste, Chiffonade of basil, Black Pepper, Red onion, Creole, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Salt, Asparagus spears, Radicchio, assorted wild vegetables, 
Preheat the grill.    Season the vegetables with 2 tablespoons of the olive oil, salt, and  pepper. Place all the vegetables on the grill (except for the mushrooms)  and grill for 2 minutes on each side.    In a mixing bowl, whisk the olive oil, vinegar, shallots, garlic, and basil  together. Season the marinade with salt and pepper. Remove the vegetables  from the grill.    In a glass souffle bowl, alternate layering the different vegetables. Pour  the marinade over the vegetables and allow to marinate for 12 hours or  overnight. Either serve the vegetables in the glass container or on a  platter. Garnish with parsley.    This recipe yields 6 servings.
