Zero Salad Dressing
Lemon juice or vinegar, Onion, Salt, Pepper, Oregano, Tomato juice, Garlic powder (optional), 
Yield: 1/2 C Each serving: 2 Tbsp Free Food (up to 2 servings per day)    Combine all ingredients in jar with tightly fitted top. Refrigerate  several hours. Shake well before using.    Note: This dressing may be kept for several weeks refrigerated in a  closed container.
