Swiss Baked Stuffed Mushroom
Mayonnaise; up to 2, Swiss cheese; shredded, Small shrimp; drained, Fresh mushrooms; up to 10, 
1. Cut the stems of the mushrooms and scoop out the centres. Finely chop  stems and centres.    2. Combine the crab meat and Mayonnaise    3. Add whatever seasonings you like (I use "Pinch of Herbs") to taste.    4. Add in chopped stems and centres. Add in cheese. Stuff into mushroom  cups and place under the broiler for 5-7 minutes.
