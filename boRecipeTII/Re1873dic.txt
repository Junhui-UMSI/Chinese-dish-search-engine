Chile Relleno Casserole
Jack cheese, (18 oz.) can ortega chilies,, Whole, To 8 corn tortillas, cut, Into wide strips, Tomatoes, sliced, Doz  eggs, milk, salt and pepper, Cumin, Clove garlic, crushed, 
Grease a 9 x 13 inch casserole.  Lay 1/2 chiles in pan.  Top with 1/2  tortilla strips and 1/2 cheese.  Cover with tomato slices. Repeat layering  with remaining chiles, tortillas and cheese. Combine eggs, milk and spices.  Pour over casserole.  Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes until  puffy hot.  Let stand 5 to 10 minutes before cutting. Serve with salsa.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by tbankerd@leading.net on Sep 06, 1998
