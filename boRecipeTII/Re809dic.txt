Heavenly Jam
Peaches --peeled; pitted and, Orange juice --or juice of 2, Orange rind --or rind of 1, Sugar, Maraschino cherries; cut, 
Combine all but cherries. Cook gently for 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Ladle into hot,  sterilized jars and seal with paraffin. Makes 12 baby food jars.    Recipe by: Vivian Levya    Posted to EAT-LF Digest by Katherine Levya Rodman <levya@mindspring.com> on  Sep 02, 1998,
