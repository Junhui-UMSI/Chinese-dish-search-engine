Porcupine Balls
Onion, Salt and pepper to taste, Egg, Tomato juice, Minute rice, Sugar, Ground beef, Tomato sauce, 
Combine 1/2 C. tomato sauce with rice, beef, egg, onion, salt and pepper.  Shape into balls. Place meatballs into remaining tomato mixture, baste  them, then simmer 1 hour or more on low in an electric skillet. Serve over  cooked rice.
