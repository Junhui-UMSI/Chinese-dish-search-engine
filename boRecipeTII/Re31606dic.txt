Swiss Chard
Garlic, Butter, bacon, salt and pepper, Chard, 
This is how we make it in Brazil    Shred chard as finely as possible. Melt butter in a frying pan. Add diced  bacon cook a few minutes, discard some of the fat. stir in minced garlic,  cook 2 minutes. Stir in chard, salt and pepper and cook over high heat  until all liquid has evaporated.    We serve it with black beans and rice or our most typical dish Feijoada (  made with black beans and salted pork.
