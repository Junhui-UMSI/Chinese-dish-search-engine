Peruvian Chicken (Aji De Gallina)
Vegetable oil, Bread, Chicken, Garlic, evaporated milk, Parmesan cheese, boiled rice, Boiled eggs, Potatoes, Chiles, Onion, Nuts, salt and pepper, Olives, 
Recipe By: "Peruvian Dishes", compiled by Brenuil, edited by A.B.C. S.A    Boil chicken in salted water.  Bone and break into bite size pieces.  In a  saucepan, heat oil and fry onion, garlic, and chili peppers.  Salt and  pepper to taste. Fry until golden. Add bread, soaked in chicken broth,  crusts removed. Cook slowly for 10 minutes; then add chopped nuts, grated  cheese, and chopped chicken. 2 or 3 minutes before serving, add evaporated  milk. Decorate with halved potatoes, eggs quartered lengthwise, and olives.  Serve with boiled rice. Lesley P.
