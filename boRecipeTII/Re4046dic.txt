Peppermint Hot Chocolate
Whipped topping, Peppermint Schnapps, Whole candy cane; optional, Hot chocolate; prepared, 
1. Pour peppermint schnapps into a 10-ounce mug. Stir in hot chocolate. Top  with whipped cream. Add a candy cane stirrer or sprinkle with crushed candy  cane, if desired.    NOTES : For a nonalcoholic version, just omit the schnapps and add 1/4  teaspoon peppermint extract.  Recipe by: Better Homes Christmas Cookies 1997    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 534 by "Marie Smith"  <craftee@sprynet.com> on Jan 15, 1998
