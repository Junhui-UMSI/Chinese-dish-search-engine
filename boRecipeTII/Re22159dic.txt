Turkey Patties Oriental
Dry bread crumbs, Garlic salt, Vinegar, Dash, white pepper, Cooking oil, Stalks celery, cut diagonal, Egg, Onion, cut in thin rings, Oriental Sauce, Cornstarch, Ground turkey, Green pepper, cut 3/4"pieces, Chicken broth, Soy sauce, Sugar, 
Mix first 4 ingredients.  Form into 4-6 patties, each about 3/4-thick. Heat  oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute burgers 3 mediums each  side, until browned. Mix Oriental Sauce and pour over burgers; bring to a  boil, stiring occasionaly.  Reduce heat; add celery, onion and pepper.  Simmer, covered, 6 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste.
