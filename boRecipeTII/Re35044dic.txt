Beans and Eggs
Orange -- peeled,sliced,opt, Onion -- chopped, Sausage links -- cut in 1/2", Beans, Water, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, Eggs, Avocado -- sliced, opt, Catsup, Cubes, Vegetable oil, Salt, Basil -- crushed, 
Brown sausage; remove from pan.  Saute onion in oil. Add beans, sausage,  catsup, water, and seasonings. Simmer, covered, 20 minutes. Cook eggs as  desired. Arrange bean mixtureon platter with eggs, avocado and orange.  Makes 6 servings.    Recipe By     : Idaho Bean Commission
