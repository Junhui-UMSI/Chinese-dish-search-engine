Apple Butter with Port
Bottle port 4/5 qt, Salt, Ground cinnamon, Whole cinnamon stick, Large golden delicious appls, Water, Sugar, 
Pare apples and thinly slice. Combine port with water and bring to boil  over high heat.  Add apples and simmer uncovered, stirring occasinally for  45 minutes. Add sugar and spices and cook 20 -25 minutes until it resembles  applesauce. Prepare 3 pint jars and fill.
