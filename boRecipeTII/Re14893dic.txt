1995 4th Place Friendship Cookies
Baking soda, Salt, Additional sugar for coating, Sorghum molasses, Vegetable oil, Pure maple syrup, Egg, Ground ginger, Cinnamon, Ground cardamom, Unsifted all-purpose flour, Sugar, 
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Have ungreased baking sheet(s) ready.     2. Combine all ingredients, except sugar for coating, in large bowl of an  electric mixer. Beat on low speed until combined. Refrigerate to firm dough  slightly, about 20 minutes.     3. Roll into large balls, using  1/4 cup dough each for large cookies or a  scant tablespoon for smaller cookies (dough will be soft). Roll in  additional  sugar to coat. Arrange on baking sheet, spacing them 3 inches  apart.      4. Bake until set, about 15 minutes for large cookies and 10 minutes for  smaller ones. Cool on baking sheet 1 minute before transferring to wire  rack  to cool.
