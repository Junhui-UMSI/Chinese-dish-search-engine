Apple Bites
Raisins, Pecans, Velveeta pasteurized process, Chopped apple, Cinnamon, Plain yogurt, 
Combine apples, raisins, yogurt, nuts and cinnamon; mix lightly.  Top  bagelette halves with apple mixture and Velveeta cheese spread. Broil until  cheese spread begins to melt.  24 appetizers.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
