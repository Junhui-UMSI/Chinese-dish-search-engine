Zesty Orange and Onion Rice
Dried Oregano, Garlic, Water, Water, Red pepper flakes, Fresh orange juice, Onion, Long-grain white rice, Black pepper, Ground cinnamon, 
Heat 3 tablespoons water in a saucepan and cook garlic and onion over  medium heat until just tender, 4-5 minutes.    Add remaining ingredients and heat to a boil. Cover and simmer over low  heat until the liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes.  Per serving: 209 Calories; less than one gram Fat (1% calories from fat);  1g Protein; 50g Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 100mg Sodium    Recipe by: Chicago Tribune Posted to Digest eat-lf.v097.n078 by Joanne  McAndrews Eisenman <jmca@nwu.edu> on Mar 22, 1997
