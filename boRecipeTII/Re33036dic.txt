Basic Cookie Mix
Granulated sugar, Brown Sugar, Firmly Packed, Salt, Unbleached flour, Baking soda, Vegetable shortening, 
Sift dry ingredients together into a large bowl, mixing well.  With a  pastry blender, cut shotening into dry ingredients until evenly  distributed.  Put into a large airtight container and label as Basic Cookie  mix.  Store in a cool dry place and use within 10 to 12 weeks.    Makes about 16 cups of mix.  From "Make-A-Mix Cookery" by Karine Eliason, Nevada Harward & Madeline  Westover via The Home BBS. Posted to MM-Recipes Digest #336, 9 Dec 96 by  c.simpson@worldnet.att.net
