Barbecued Onions for Goodwich
White onion; sliced, Safflower oil, 
IN a small skillet, heat oil; add onon and saute until it begins to  soften. Ad barbecue sauce and continue sauteeing, stirring frequently,  until onion is thoroughly wilted. Makes enough for 3 or 4 Goodwiches.   Leftover Barbecued Onions are delicous in any vegetable soup.     Source: Fit for Life, by Harvey and Marilyn Diamond/MM by DEEANNE
