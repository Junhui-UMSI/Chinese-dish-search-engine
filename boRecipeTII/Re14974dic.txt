30-Min- Beef Fajitas
Onion, cut in strips, Sweet green pepper, chopped, Low-fat sour cream, Garlic, Chili powder, Salsa, Dijon mustard, Vegetable oil, Lean ground beef, Zucchini, thinly sliced, Sweet red pepper, chopped, Salt, Pepper, 10-inch flour tortillas, 
In nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; cook onion, garlic,  chili powder, salt and pepper, stirring often, for 5 minutes or until onion  is softened.    Add beef and salsa; cook, breaking up beef with back of spoon, for about 3  minutes or until beef is no longer pink. Add peppers and zucchini; cook,  stirring, for 3 minutes or until tender-crisp and liquid has evaporated.    Meanwhile, wrap tortillas in foil; heat in 350F 180C oven for 5 minutes or  until warmed through. Stir sour cream with mustard. Divide beef mixture  among tortillas and roll up. Serve with sour cream mixture.    4 servings for $9.56CDN [Aug 95]    Per Serving: about 545 calories, 31 g protein, 25 g fat, 49 g carbohydrate,  good source calcium, excellent source iron    Source: Canadian Living magazine, Aug 95 Article "30-Minute Summer Suppers"  Recipe by Canadian Living Test Kitchen        [-=PAM=-]   PA_Meadows@msn.com
