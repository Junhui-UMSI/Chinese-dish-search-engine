Pueblo Indian Squash and Corn
Salt and pepper, Yellow squash; thinly sliced, Onion; minced, Ears fresh corn; kernels cut, -or-, Butter, 
Melt butter in skillet; saute onions slowly over low heat until tender; add  squash, corn and seasoning. Cover and simmer 10-15 minutes.    MRS JOHN MCDOWELL (CARMEN)    THOMPSON FALLS, MT    From the book <High Cotton Cookin>, Marvell Academy Mothers Assn, Marvell,  AR  72366, ISBN 0-918544-14-9, downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
