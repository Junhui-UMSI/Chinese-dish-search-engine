Tuna and Olive Pasta Salad
Mushrooms, Olives, black, Thyme, dried, Parsley; fresh, Olive oil, virgin, Wine, white, Garlic, Elbow pasta, Scallions, DRESSING-----, Mustard, Tuna in water, Oregano, dried, Lemon juice, 
cook pasta according to directions. Rinse in cold water and drain  well.Place macaroni, olives, tuna and parsley in a large bowl and toss  well. Put the rest of the ingredients in a jar and shake well. Pour over  pasta and gently mix well to coat all ingredients. Refrigerate for 2 hours  befor serving.    Recipe By     :
