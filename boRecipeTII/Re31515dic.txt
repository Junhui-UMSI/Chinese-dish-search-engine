Sweet-N-Sour Meatballs
Lean ground beef, Egg beaten, Soft bread crumbs, Grape or red currant jelly, Heinz Chili Sauce, Onion, milk, Pepper, Vegetable oil, Garlic, Salt, 
Combine first 8 ingrediants form into 40 bite size meatballs.Brown lightly  in oil.Cover cook over low for 5 minutes.Drain.Combine chili sauce and  jelly;pour over meatballs.heat.stirring occasionally,until jelly is  melted.Simmer 10 tp 12 minutes until sauce is thickened,basting  occasionally.
