Banana-Blueberry Cream Pie
pie shells, Bananas, Whipped topping mix, Sugar, Blueberry pie filling, cream cheese, 
Bake two 8-inch pie shells. Let cool. Slice bananas & put in pie shells.  Make whipped topping mix according to directions on the package. Add sugar,  a little at a time. Blend in cream cheese, pour into pie shells & spread  pie filling over the top. Refrigerate 1-2 hours before serving.
