Potatoes with Salmoriglio Sauce
Black Pepper, Small red new potatoes,, Salt, olive oil, Clove garlic, peeled and, Minced fresh oregano or 1, Lemon juice, 
Cut the potatoes in halves and place in a steamer basket. Steam in a  covered pan with water at a low boil. Steam about 20 minutes, until the  potatoes are tender. Drain and transfer to a bowl.    While the potatoes are cooking combine the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic,  salt, pepper, and oregano in a small jar.    Pour the sauce over the potatoes and stir well to coat. Let sit 5 minutes  and serve.    Menu suggestion: Serve this side-dish with baked chicken or a simply  prepared fish Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.50
