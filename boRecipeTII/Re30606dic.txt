Taigelach
Lemon juice, Honey, Sugar, Cinnamon, Walnuts, Ginger, Farfel, 
Bring honey, sugar, lemon juice, ginger and cinnamon to boil. Cook 15  minutes (soft ball). Add farfel, cook 5 - 10 minutes longer. Add nuts. Cook  5 minutes more. Pat cold water on board. Flatten to 1/4" thick. Let cool.  Cut into 1" squares.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Paula Miller Jacobson" <paulaj@erols.com>  on Apr 8, 1998
