Texas Spoon Bread
milk, Sugar, Baking Powder, Eggs, Yellow cornmeal, Salt, Butter or margarine, 
In a saucepan, scald the milk; stir in cornmeal. Reduce heat; simmer for 5  minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from the heat; stir in butter, sugar,  salt and baking powder. In a small bowl, beat egg yolks. Gradually stir in  a small amount of the hot mixture into yolks; return all to pan and mix  well. In a mixing bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Fold egg  whites into hot mixture until well blended. Pour into a greased 8 inch  square baking dish. Bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes or until well puffed. Use  a spoon to serve. Yield 6 servings.
