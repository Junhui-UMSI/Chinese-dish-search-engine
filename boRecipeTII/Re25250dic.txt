Tonys Salad Dressing
Black pepper fresh ground, Garlic clove minced fine, Balsamic vinegar, Grated Parmesan cheese, Fresh basil minced, Olive oil extra lite, Salt, Chablis, Oregano chopped, Soy sauce, 
Mix all ingredients into the oil. Stir well. regrifgerate for 2 days. Shake  well and pour liberally over salad. Origin: Tony DOlivo.....Tampa, fl.  cieca 1990
