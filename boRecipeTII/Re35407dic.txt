Beef Empanadas
Ground beef, Corn, frozen, Cheddar cheese, Golden raisins, Pillsbury Grands, Tomato paste, Green salsa, Egg, Dried basil, 
In large skillet brown ground beef.  Stir in raisins, corn, tomato paste  and salsa, heat throughly. Remove from heat stir in cheese and basil. Set  aside.    Preheat oven to 375F.  Separate dough into 8 biscuits. Roll orpress each to  a 5 inch circle.  Place about 1/4 cup meat mixture in center of biscuit.  Fold in half; seal edges and prick with fork. Brush with egg.    Place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake for 13 to 16 minutes or until golden  brown.    Contributor:  Pillsbury    Posted to recipelu-digest by Kelly <kelly@hci.net> on Feb 16, 1998
