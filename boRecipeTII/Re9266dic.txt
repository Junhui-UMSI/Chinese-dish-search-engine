Pudding Sauce
Water, boiling, Vanilla, Flour, Butter, Sugar, 
Combine the sugar and flour and mix well. Add the boiling water stirring  constantly to prevent lumping. Cook in double boiler until the mixture  thickens. Add butter and stir until melted. Flavor and serve. Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
