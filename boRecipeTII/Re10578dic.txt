Quick N Easy Potato Casserole
Box (small) Velveeta cheese, Onions, Hamburger, Diced potatoes, 
From:    Catherine F Ramey <rameycf@MAIL.AUBURN.EDU>    Date:    Fri, 12 Jul 1996 12:19:47 -0500  Brown 1 1/2 lb. hamburger, drain.  Make a layer of diced potatoes, layer of  hamburger, and a layer of onion in lightly greased casserole dish.  Melt  small box of Velveeta cheese (no amount given) with at least 2 1/2 cups  milk and pour over top. Make as many layers as you want. Bake at 350 until  potatoes are tender - about 20 minutes.
