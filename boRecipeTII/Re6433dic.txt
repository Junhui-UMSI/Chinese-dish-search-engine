Plum Sauce #1
Apricot preserves, Garlic juice, Red plum preserves, Honey, Applesauce, Cider vinegar, 
Combine all ingredients in saucepan; bring to boil over medium heat.  Cook,  stirring constantly for 5 minutes, until all ingredients are well blended.  Remove from heat; let cool.  To preserve, pour into hot sterilized jar and  seal at once.    ANNA KAOS    SERVE WITH ANY RECIPE CALLING    PLUM SAUCE.    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of Chinese Recipes>.
