Wild Thing
Canned stewed tomatoes, drained, prepared according to, undrained, Vegetable oil, Green Onions, Top round or sirloin steak, sliced into 1/8" slices, Long grain and wild rice mix, (4.3 to 6.25 oz.), Canned whole kernel corn, package directions, 
Brown beef in oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Add onions;  cook 1 minute longer.  Add rice, corn, and tomatoes.  Cook until  thoroughly heated, about 2 to 3 minutes.
