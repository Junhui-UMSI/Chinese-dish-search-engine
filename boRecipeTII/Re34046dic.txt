Basilgarlic Vinaigrette (Gc)
olive oil, Red wine vinegar, Fresh lime juice, Honey, Basil leaves, tightly packed, Chopped red onion, Garlic, Salt, 
Combine the basil leaves, vinegar, lime juice, garlic, onion, and honey in  a blender and puree. With the motor running slowly add the oil until  emulsified. Season to taste with salt and freshly ground pepper. May be  prepared up to 1 day ahead and refrigerated. Bring up to room temperature  before serving.
