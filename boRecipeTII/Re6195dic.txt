Pizza Sauce #3
Raw onions (chopped), Water, Brown sugar, Oregano, Tomato paste, Marjoram, Clove garlic (chopped), Parmesan cheese (grated), Black pepper, Cooking oil, 
Combine ingredients in a 1 qt sauce pan and cook for 30-40 minutes. Makes  enough sauce for 4 12" Pizzas.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Feb 06, 98
