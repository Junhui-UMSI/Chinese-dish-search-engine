Tomato and Basil Bruschetta
Toasted country bread, cut, Basil - chopped, Roma tomatoes - seeded and, Minced garlic cloves, olive oil, 
In a bowl, combine oil, garlic, basil and tomatoes. Season with salt and  pepper. Spoon about 2 Tbs. of tomato topping on each toast. Arrange on  platter and serve. For variations: Add chopped olives, pimentos or chopped  artichoke hearts.
