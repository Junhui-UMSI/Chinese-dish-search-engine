Peanut Butter Bars #1
(8-oz) milk chocolate bars, Powdered sugar, Graham cracker crumbs, Butter, Crunchy peanut butter, 
Beat butter and sugar until smooth. Add peanut butter and graham cracker  crumbs. Mix well. Pat into a 9x9 pan. Melt chocolate in a double boiler  over hot water (do not let water come to a boil). Ice peanut butter mixture  with melted chocolate. Cool. Cut into small bars. Yield: 16 squares.    SUSAN BRAINARD (MRS. JAY),    LISA CHAPMAN (MRS.
