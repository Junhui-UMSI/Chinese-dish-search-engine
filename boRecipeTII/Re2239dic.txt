Baked Mashed Potatoes
Potatoes, peeled, boiled, Cream cheese, softened, GARNISH, Paprika, Salt or to taste, Sour cream, Pepper or to taste, Chives, Clove garlic minced (OR, Butter, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    Whip first seven ingredients together and place in a greased 9 x 13  casserole.    Sprinkle with paprika and dot with butter.    Bake uncovered for 30 minutes.
