Potatoes with Oyster Sauce
Dry mustard, Garlic salt, Clove garlic; crushed --or--, Baked potatoes, Handful chopped parsley, Oysters, Butter, Lemon, Tabasco, Worcestershire, 
Bake potatoes. Mix all ingredients except oysters in saucepan. Simmer a few  minutes. Do not boil. Add oysters with juice and cook just long enough for  oysters to curl. Slice baked potatoes and put in soup bowl. Pour oysters  and sauce over. Serve hot.
