Western Style Beef Skillet
Into thin strips, Beef sirloin steak -- cut, Prepared chili sauce, Vegetables, Western Style Frozen, Vegetable oil, 16 oz. pkg Green Giant, American Mixtures, Chili powder, 
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.  add beef.  cook until no  longer pink.      Add remaining ingredients; stir to combine. cover; reduce  heat to medium. Simmer 6 to 8 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender,  stirring occassionally.    Recipe By     : Green Giant ad    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
