Zucchini "Frisbees"
Eggs, Brown Sugar, Flour, Baking soda, Sugar, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Shortening, Salt, Cloves, Peeled, grated zucchini, Pecans, Vanilla, Raisins, 
From: Donna Ransdell, COOKING echo    Sift together flour, soda, powder, salt and spices. Cream shortening with  sugars; beat in eggs and flavoring. Add dry ingredients alternately with  zucchini. Stir in raisins and nuts. Drop in 1/4 cup quantities onto Greased  cookie sheets. Spread out to 5 inches diameter. Bake at 375 for 10 to 12  minutes, or until edges begin to brown. Cool 1 minute and remove and cool  on racks. If desired, frost with powdered sugar glaze. Can make into  smaller cookies. Makes 20 big ones or more smaller ones.    Recipe from Festival Cookie Book, published by KMA Radio, 1983.
