Pork Tofu
Ground pork, Soy sauce, Sugar, Cube (1-1/2") see chin choy, Stalks green onions; chopped, Block tofu; cubed, Water, Cornstarch, Tomato; slice in wedges, Salt, 
In teflon pan, stir fry pork.  Add salt, green onions, see chin choy and  tomato wedges and cook until tomato softens.  Add tofu.  Pour gravy mixture  made of cornstarch, soy sauce, sugar and water, over tofu.  Cook until  gravy thickens.    From a book of my mothers (Judy Hosey) titled <Senator Richard Matsuuras  Favorite Recipes>, mostly Japanese and Hawaiian.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
