Turkey Enchiladas with Green Chili Salsa
Chopped cilantro, Shredded low fat Co-Jack, Canned enchilada sauce, Fat free small flour, Fat free sour cream, Pkg. Butterball Turkey, Fat free refried beans, Green chili salsa or taco, 
Heat large nonstick skillet over high heat. Spray with nonstick cooking  spray. Cook turkey strips until no longer pink, about 5 minutes. Spread a  generous tablespoon refried beans on each tortilla. Place cooked turkey  strips on top of tortillas. Top turkey strips with 1 tablespoon enchilada  sauce and 2 tablespoons cheese. Roll and place in ovenproof or  microwaveable dish sprayed with nonstick cooking spray. Top with remaining  enchilada sauce and cheese. Heat in 350 degrees F. oven about 20 minutes or  microwave on high for 5 minutes. Serve tortillas with sour cream, chopped  cilantro and green chili salsa.
