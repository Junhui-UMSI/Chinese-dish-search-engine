Bcs Lemon Herb Marinade
Dry vermouth -or-, Beef broth, Lemon Peel, Dry white wine -or-, Vegetable oil, Or 1/4 t dried sage leaves, Coarsely ground pepper, Chopped fresh sage leaves, Chopped fresh oregano leaves, Or 1/4 t dried oregano lvs, Salt, Lemon juice, 
Mix all ingredients in shallow nonmetal dish or resealable plastic bag. Add  up to 2 pounds beef, pork, chicken or turkey, turning to coat with  marinade. Cover dish or seal bag and refrigerate, turning meat  occasionally, at least 4 hours but no longer than 24 hours. Remove meat  from marinade; discard marinade. Grill meat as desired.    *Betty Crockers Great Grilling  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #306 by Michael McLaughlin  <mmclaughlin@micronet.net> on Nov 25, 1997
