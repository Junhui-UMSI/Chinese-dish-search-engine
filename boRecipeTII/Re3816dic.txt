Pennsylvania Shoo-Fly Pie
EACH ground cloves, ground, Ground cinnamon, Boiling water, Whipped cream for garnish, 9" pie crust, All-purpose flour, Egg, Butter or margarine, Molasses, Baking soda, Brown sugar, 
Great American Recipes    In a bowl, mix mollases, egg, soda and water. In a second bowl, combine  flour, brown sugar, butter, and spices. Work with your fingers until even  crumbs form. Sprinkle 1/3 of the flour mixture into pie crust. Pour on 1/2  of the molasses mixture. Repeat with 1/2 of the flour mixture and remaining  flour mixture. Top with remaining flour mixture. Bake at 450 deg F for 10  minutes. Reduce heat to 350 deg F. Bake 20 minutes more or until filling is  firm when shaken. Cool at least 30 minutes before serving. Garnish with  whipped cream and a sprinkle of brown sugar. Posted to bakery-shoppe digest  V1 Number 018 by "Lewis" <lewises@flatoday.infi.net> on Apr 10, 1997
