Quick Sesame Bread
Sesame seed, Water, Bisquick original baking mix, Margarine or butter;, 
HEAT oven to 400 degrees. Grease cookie sheet.     MIX baking mix and water with fork until soft dough forms. Pat or roll  dough into rectangle, 6x4 inches, on cookie sheet. Brush margarine on  rectangle; sprinkle with sesame seed.     BAKE 8 to 10 minutes or until golden brown. Serve hot, broken into pieces  or cut into squares. 2 servings.
