Peanut Butter/chocolate Kiss Cookies
Butter, Egg, Sugar, Chocolate kisses, Peanut butter, Baking soda, Salt, milk, Vanilla, Flour, Decorating sugar, Packed light brown sugar, 
Heat oven to 375. Mix all ingredients except kisses and decorating  sugar. Will form stiff dough. Roll into balls. Roll in decorating  sugar. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 10-12  minutes. Press chocolate kiss in center. Makes 4 doz.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
