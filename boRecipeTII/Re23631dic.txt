Artichoke Bottoms Stuffed with Spinach
Cayenne pepper, Salt, Black pepper, artichoke bottoms, frozen chopped spinach, Grated Parmesan cheese, Onion, Sour cream, Butter, 
Cook spinach according to package directions; drain thoroughly. saute  onion in butter for 5 minutes or until tender. Stir in the cooked spinach,  sour cream, peppers, salt and grated cheese. Spoon mixture onto artichoke  bottoms; place in shallow baking dish. Sprinkle cheese over the top. Add  water to barely cover bottom of dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 to 20  minutes Serves 6 to 8.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
