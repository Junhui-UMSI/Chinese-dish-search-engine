Raised Rabbits with Prunes
Beef broth, Chicken broth, Rabbit, boned, Salt & White Pepper, Flour, Prunes, Butter, 
Season rabbit pieces with salt and white pepper. Dredge in flour and shake  off the excess.  Fry in moderately heated butter. Add flour and brown  slightly.  Add prunes, beef broth and chicken broth. Cover and bake at 375  degrees until meat falls away from the bones. Serve with wild rice.    Source:  N.A.H.C. Wild Game Cookbook Author: Unknown
