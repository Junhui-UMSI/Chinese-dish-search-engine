Valentines Day Potato Salad
Onions, Eggs, hard boiled and sliced, Mayonnaise (add as desired), Apple, diced, Sweet relish, with juice, Red Rose potatoes, Boiled and cubed, with skins, Salt and pepper to taste, Mustard, Spicy, Dill, 
Combine all ingredients, folding together gently. Serve chilled.    Created and typed for you by Lawren Freebody at Salata BBS.
