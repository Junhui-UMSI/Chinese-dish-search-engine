(Sort-Of) Sweet and Sour Chicken (Also Good with Just Veg
Soy sauce, Orange juice, Brown sugar, Ground ginger, Can of pineapple chunks in, Vinegar, Cornstarch (or arrowroot, 
I usually roast a whole chicken every Sunday, then use the leftovers for  sandwiches and other meals. Heres what I made tonight:    mix all of the above (actually, use only the syrup from the pineapple) and  then heat in a saucepan until it thickens. Add water if you need more  sauce.    Then add whatever veggies you want: Pineapple, carrots, onions, peppers,  celery. Let them soften (especially the carrots: add them first). Add  chicken.
