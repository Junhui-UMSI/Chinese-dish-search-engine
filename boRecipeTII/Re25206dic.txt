Tomatoes with Basil Parmesan Dressing
Fresh Basil; Chopped, salt and pepper, Parmesan Cheese; Sliced Or, olive oil, Red Tomatoes, Red Wine Vigenar Or Balsamic, 
Cut the tomatoes into slices 1/2 inch thick. Sprinkle with the cheese and  basil. Combine the salt, pepper, vinegar and oil and pour over th tomatoes.  Refrigerate for 1 hour, or until serving time.
