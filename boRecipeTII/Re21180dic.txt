Tzadziki #1 - Stella Vallianos
Plain yogurt, Cucumbers, Parsley, Garlic, salt and pepper, olive oil, 
Dice cucumbers very fine into a small bowl and drain off the excess liquid.  In another small bowl combine the yogurt, garlic, olive oil, salt and  pepper. Stir them until they are well mixed, and gently fold in the  cucumbers, and chill. Garnish with parsley before serving.
