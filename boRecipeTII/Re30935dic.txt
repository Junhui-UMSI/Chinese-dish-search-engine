Taos Taco Salads
Frozen corn, Drained, Fresh cilantro, black beans  , Mexican Cheddar Cheese, lettuce, Corn tortillas, Salsa, Tomato, Vegetable oil cooking spray, green onion, Drained, 
Heat oven to 400 F.  Lightly spray both sides of each tortilla with  non-stick cooking spray.  Place on baking sheet and bake at 400 F. for 3  minutes on each side or until crisp. Combine beans, corn, onion and  cilantro; mix lightly.  For each salad, top tortilla with 1/2 c. lettuce,  1/4 c. tomato, 1/4 c bean mixture, 1 tb. salsa and 1 oz. cheese. 8  servings.
