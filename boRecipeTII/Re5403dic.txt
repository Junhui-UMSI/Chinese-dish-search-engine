Pineapple Cheese Loaf
Shortening; melted, Sugar, Salt, Vanilla, Egg, Flour, Cheddar Cheese, Walnuts, Crushed pineapple, Baking Powder, 
Mix ingredients in the order given. Pour into a greased loaf pan. Bake at  350 degrees for 35 mins. or until toothpick comes out clean.
