Really Hot, Hot Pepper Jelly
Ground bell pepper, Sugar, Cider vinegar, Ground hot peppers, Bottle Certo, Tea green food color, 
Remove seeds from peppers, grind and save juice.  Measure ground peppers  with juice.  Bring sugar, vinegar and peppers to rolling boil. Boil for 10  mins.  Remove from heat.  Add Certo and food color.  Pour into sterilized  jars.
