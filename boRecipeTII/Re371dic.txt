Lemon Cheese Squares
Yellow Cake Mix, Cream cheese, Sugar, Lemon juice, Egg, Vegetable oil, 
Oven at 350?. Mix dry cake mix, 1 egg and oil until crumbly; reserve 1 c.  Pat remaining mixture lightly in an ungreased 13 x 9" pan.    Bake 15 minutes. Beat cheese, sugar, juice and 1 egg until light and  smooth. Spread over baked layer. Sprinkle with reserved crumb mixture.    Bake 15 minutes more. Cool and cut into bars.
