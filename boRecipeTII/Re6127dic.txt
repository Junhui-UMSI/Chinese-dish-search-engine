Pizza Lovers Soup
Dried basil, Dried Oregano, Peeled fresh or canned, Bell pepper, chopped, Undiluted condensed chicken, Onion, sliced, Part-skim-milk mozarella, Juices), Garlic, Thin rounds Italian bread,, Water, Tomato juice, 
Preheat oven to 400F.  In a saucepan combine all ingredients except bread  and cheese.  Simmer for 30 minutes. Spoon into 4 oven-proof soup bowls.  Float bread round in each bowl and top bread with cheese slices. Place  bowls on baking tray and heat in oven until the cheese melts. Use oven  mitts to remove and serve, cautioning diners about heat of bowls.    Nutrients per bowl: Calories 222 Protein 14.7 grams Fat 6.0 grams  Cholesterol 15.7 mg Fiber 2.6 grams Sodium 1042 mg    [ MODERN MATURITY; Feb/March 1990 ]    Posted by Fred Peters.
