Banana Nut Bread
Sugar, Mashed ripe bananas, Baking Powder, Walnuts, Flour, Shortening, Salt, Eggs, Baking soda, 
Cream together shortening and sugar; add eggs and beat well. Sift together  dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture alternately with banana blending  well after each addition. Stir in nuts. Pour into well greased 9x5x3 inch  loaf pan. Bake at 350 45-50 minutes or till done. Remove from pan; cool on  rack. Wrap and store overnight    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Aug  17, 1998
