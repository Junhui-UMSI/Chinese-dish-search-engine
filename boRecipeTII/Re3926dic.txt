Pepper Steak 2
Tomatoes, Cut Into Wedges, Garlic, Minced, Beef Round Steak, Sliced, Snow Pea Pods, Hot cooked rice, Onions, Cut Into Thin Wedges, Beef broth, Soy sauce, Paprika, Green Bell Peppers, Sliced, Water, Cornstarch, Butter, 
Stir fry the garlic in a wok or very large skillet in half the butter for 1  minute.  Add the onion and green bell pepper.  Stir fry until tender (4 to  5 minutes longer). Transfer the vegetables to a bowl using a slotted spoon.  Add the remaining butter to the wok.  Stir fry the steak (in batches if  necessary to prevent overcrowding) until cooked through (2 to 3 minutes).  Return the vegetables to the wok.  Add the broth and soy sauce.  Bring to a  boil.  Stir the water and cornstarch together in a cup.  Add to the mixture  in the wok. Cook, stirring, until bubbly. Cook another 2 minutes. Add the  paprika, tomato wedges and snow pea pods.  Cook, stirring rapidly, for 30  seconds.  Serve over rice.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 30 October 96    Date:    Wed, 30 Oct 1996 09:56:05 -0800    From:    Joel Ehrlich <Joel.Ehrlich@SALATA.COM>
