Zucchini Supreme
Stove Top Stuffing Mix, New York white sharp cheddar, Zucchini, cubed, Onion, Stick butter or margarine,, Sour cream, Cream of chicken soup, 
Boil zucchini and onion in salted water 10 minutes or until done. Drain and  set aside. Toss stuffing crumbs and seasoning packet with melted butter or  margarine. Put 1/2 in bottom of 9x13-inch baking pan. Mix together sour  cream, cream of chicken soup and cheddar cheese. Add zucchini and mix well.  Spoon over stuffing mixture in pan. Top with remaining stuffing mixture.  Bake at 350F for 25 minutes.    Recipe by: Mom Posted to EAT-L Digest by Nancy Pallotta  <nancee@NEO.LRUN.COM> on Jul 5, 1997
