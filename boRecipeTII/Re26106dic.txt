Tofu Spread
Water, Tofu, Pimento, optional, Tapioca, Lemon juice, Dill weed, Salt, Cashews, Onion powder, 
Blend all ingredients till smooth.  Bring to a boil & simmer gently,  stirring, for 5 to 10 minutes or until the toapioca is clear. Chill. Use as  a dip or as a spread on bread or crackers.
