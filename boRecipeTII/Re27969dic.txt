Three Cheese Roasted Vegetables
Zucchini, cut into 1/2 inch, Recipe blend, Russet potatoes, peeled and, 1/2 inch slices, Carrots, pared and cut into, Red bell pepper, cut into, Cut into 1 in. pieces, olive oil, Pepper, Garlic minced, 1 inch pieces, 4 cheese country casserole, Salt, Pieces, Each basil and oregano, 
Directions  Place potatoes and carrots in greased 13 x 9 inch baking dish.  Drizzle with oil; sprinkle with basil, oregano, salt and pepper. Toss  lightly to coat.  bake 425 F. for 20 minutes. Add zucchini, red pepper and garlic; stir  vegetables. Return to oven and bake 25 minutes or until all  vegetables are tender.  Sprinkle with cheese; return to oven about 2  minutes more or just until cheese melts.  submitted by marina  source Sargeno Great summer recipes  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #243    Date: Thu, 5 Sep 1996 12:53:24 -0700 (MST)    From: jmchee@goodnet.com (Marina Cheesman)
