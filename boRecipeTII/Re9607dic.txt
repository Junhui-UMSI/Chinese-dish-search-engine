Pumpkin Ginger Muffins
Brown sugar -- packed, Apple juice -- at room, Baking Powder, Nutmeg, Salt, Temperature, Pumpkin, Applesauce -- at room, Unbleached flour, Baking soda, Ginger, Temperature, Egg whites -- whipped, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare muffin tins with cooking spray and flour. In  a mixing bowl, combine pumpkin, applesauce, egg whites, and juice.  In  another mixing bowl, combine sugar, flour, baking soda, baking powder,  salt, ginger, and nutmeg.  Mix wet ingredients with dry ingredients just  until moistened.  Use an ice cream scoop to fill muffin tins two thirds  full.  Bake 25 minutes.
