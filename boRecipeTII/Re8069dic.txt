Potato Bacon Pie
Grated sharp cheddar, Black pepper, Bacon,chopped, Potatoes,peeled and grated, Large eggs,beaten, Onion, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.Grease a 9 x 13" glass baking dish. Cook bacon  and onion until bacon is crisp. Drain well. Combine eggs with potatoes and  cheese. Mix in bacon. Pour evenly into baking dish. Bake about 45 minutes  or until center is set. Serves 6 to 8.  Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com (Judy E Ryder) on Feb 09,  1998
