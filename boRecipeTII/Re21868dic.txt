Apple-Rhubarb Dump Cake
Water, Small size, Sugar, Apple pie filling, Stick of butter, Strawberry Jello, Rhubarb, White cake mix, 
1)    Put rhubarb & pie filling in greased 13x9x2 inch pan... 2) Mix sugar  and Jello; sprinkle over rhubarb... 3) Sprinkle cake mix over that and  sprinkle water over all... Slice butter on top of cake mix... 4) Bake at  350 degrees for 45 minutes. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or whipped  cream. Serve cold if youre lucky enough to have any left...
