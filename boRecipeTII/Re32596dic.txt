Sweet N Sour Green Beans
Bacon -- slices, Trim beans -- cut on a s, Sugar, Trim beans, cut on a slant, Green beans, Cider vinegar, Onion -- thinly sliced, 
until just crisp and tender.  Set aside and keep warm in a serving dish.  Cook bacon in a small skillet until it is crisp. Remove, crumble bacon and  sprinkle over beans. Add onions to bacon fat in skillet, and cook until  they are the texture, color and doneness you want them. Drain off any  remaining fat. Beat or whisk vinegar and sugar together, pour over onions  in frying pan, stir all together, heat to a simmer, pour over beans and  bacon, and serve immediately.
