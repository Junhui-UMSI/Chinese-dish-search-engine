Tomato Soup
milk, Flour, Canned tomatoes, Butter, Clove, Salt and pepper, Bay leaf, Onion, 
Combine tomatoes, bay leaf, onion, and clove.  Cover.  Simmer 10 minutes.  Rub through sieve.  Combine butter and flour.  Add to tomato puree. Simmer  5 minutes.  Add slowly to cold milk.  Heat to boiling. Season to taste.  Serve at once.  4 servings.    The Household Searchlight    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #081 by Patrick & Sarah Gruenwald  <sitm@ekx.infi.net> on Dec 20, 1996
