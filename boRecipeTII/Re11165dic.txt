From: jagordon@agsm.ucla.edu    Date: 8 Nov 1993 18:05:42 GMT  Prepare cookie sheet with film of oil or spray.  Combine in blender the  sweet milk, dates, vanilla, egg and mashed fruit.  Blend on low till  smooth,    Put oatmeal in bowl, mix in blended ingredients. Combine all dry  ingredients in large bowl, add in moistened oatmeal. Mix well, stir in  raisins.    Preheat oven to 350F.  Drop by teaspoonful onto prepared cookie sheets.  Bake 12 - 15 minutes til lightly browned and toothpick comes out clean.  Cool. Store in airtight container in frige. Freezes well.    Sweet milk: Combine in jar and store in frige 3 days or at least overnight.  Strain milk to remove mushy, tasteless raisins, Use milk.
