Vanilla Ice Cream-Diabetic
Vanilla, Eggs, Evaporated milk, Sugar replacement, Whole Milk, 
Combine evaporated milk and sugar replacement. Beat well until sugar is  dissolved. Add whole milk and vanilla extract; beat well. Add eggs; beat  eggs into milk mixture vigorously. Pour into ice cream maker. Freeze  according to manufacturers directions.  Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #039 by Mindi Miller <myenta@psnw.com> on  Feb 03, 1997.
