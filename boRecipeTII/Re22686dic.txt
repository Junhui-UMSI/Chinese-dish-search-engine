Apricot Fruit Flip
Honey, Ripe banana, Apricot nectar (12 oz), Instant nonfat dry milk, Lemon Rind, Lemon juice, Ice cubes, 
Combine all ingredients, except ice cubes, in container of an electric  blender. Add enough ice cubes to measure 4 cups. Blend until smooth.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
