Beer-Basted Steaks
Brown sugar, White vinegar, Sirloin steaks, Bay leaf, Grainy mustard, Onion, Beer, 
Combine all ingredients in a pan. Bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer for       10    minutes, cool.     Pour mixture over steaks. Let stand 1 to 2 hours, turning steaks  occasionally.     Barbecue until golden brown on both sides.     Source: NSLC Consumer brochure - Summer 95    AF656@FREENET.CARLETON.CA    (PAUL A. MEADOWS)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
