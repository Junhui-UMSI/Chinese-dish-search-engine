Raisin Pumpernickel Bread
Cornmeal, Bread flour, Caraway seed, Cocoa powder, Raisins, Vegetable oil, Yeast, Molasses, Water, Salt, Instant coffee granules, Rye flour, 
*** Add raisins at the beginning of the second kneading period!!!    Add ingredients to your bread machine according to the manufacturers  instructions - Wheat, Sweet, or Basic Cycle. Maked 1 1/2 lb loaf. This is a  moist, low-rising bread.
