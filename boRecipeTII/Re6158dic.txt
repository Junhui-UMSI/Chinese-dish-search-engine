Pizza Primavera
Healthy favorites low-fat, Broccoli flowerets, Parmesan; shredded, Asparagus pieces, Frozen peas; thawed, Yellow summer squash, Fresh basil; minced, Plum tomatoes; sliced, Pita bread rounds, 
Preheat oven to 400~. Cook veggies until crisp-tender. Drain. Sprinkle 1/4  C mozzarella on each pita bread. Top with tomato slices; sprinkle with  basil. Arrange cooked veggies and peas on tomato slices. Sprinkle with  remaining mozzarella and Parmesan. Place on cookie sheet. Bake 8 minutes or  until mozzarella cheese is melted.
