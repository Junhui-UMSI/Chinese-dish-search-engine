Poppy Seed Cake
Salt, Baking soda, Eggs, Solo poppy seed filling, Butter, Sugar, Vanilla, Sour cream, Flour, 
Cream butter and sugar. Add Solo. Add egg yolks, one at a time, beating  after each. Blend in vanilla and sour cream. Sift together flour, soda and  salt. Add gradually. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Pour into greased  tube or bundt pan. Bake at 350 for 1 hr 15-20 mins. Drizzle with glaze made  with powdered sugar and milk 12:05 PM
