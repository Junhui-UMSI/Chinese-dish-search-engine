Team Tabasco Hot Pepper Pizza
Package prepared pizza crust, TABASCO pepper sauce, Green bell pepper, cut, Shredded white Cheddar, Vegetable oil, Sliced pimento-stuffed, Red bell pepper, cut, Yellow bell pepper, cut, 
Preheat oven to 450 degree F.  In 12-inch skillet over medium heat, cook  bell peppers 5 minutes or until tender-crisp.  Stir in olives and Tabasco  sauce. Spoon mixture onto pizza crust.  Sprinkle top with cheese.    Bake pizza on pizza pan or pizza stone for 10 minutes or until cheese is  melted and crust is crisp.  Makes 4 servings.
