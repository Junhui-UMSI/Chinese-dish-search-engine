Vanilla Ice Cream (See Butter Pecan Sauce)
milk, PKG VANILLA INST PUDDING MIX, Sugar, CAN EVAPORATED MILK, Salt to taste, FROZEN WHIPPED TOPPING THAWD, 
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl; beat at medium speed of an  electric mixer until well blended. Pour into freezer. Freeze according to  manufacturers instructions. Let ripen one hour. Yield 5 quarts. NOTE: May  add additional vanilla to taste.
