Quick Skillet Supper
Vegetable oil, (16-oz) stewed tomatoes;, Green Onions, (4.3- to 6.25-oz) long grain, (7-oz) whole kernel corn;, Top round or sirloin steak;, 
Brown beef in oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onions; cook  1 minute longer. Add rice, corn and tomatoes. Cook until thoroughly heated,  about 2 to 3 minutes.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 667 by "Karen Coe"  <ayla@duo-county.com> on Feb 1, 1998
