Asparagus Cake
Buttermilk, Eggs, Salt, Flour, Sugar, Asparagus puree( cooked ), Butter  or margarine, Squares melted chcolate, Baking soda, Vanilla, 
Cream butter and sugar. blend in asparagus, chocolate, vanilla. Combine  flour with baking soda. Add alterantely with buttermilk to creamed mixture.  Turn into 2, 8 inch cake pans and bake 350 for 35 minutes or tested done.  Cool in pans for 10 minutes and remove to racks to cool. Fill and frost  with a chocolate frosting.
