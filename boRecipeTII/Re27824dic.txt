The Ultimate Eggnog
Sugar, Powdered cloves and freshly, Cognac, Bourbon, Egg whites, Egg yolks, Dark rum, Quars whipping cream, 
Beat yolks until very light. Beat in sugar, cloves nutmeg. Cover let stand  overnite. Before serving beat egg whites still, beat cream until it peaks,  Fold mixtures together, stir in liquor. Serve over cracked ice. Sprinkle  with freshly ground nutmeg.    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #436 by "glenn r. goble"  <grgbob@digital.net> on Dec 04, 1997
