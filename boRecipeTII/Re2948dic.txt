Peanut Butter Pie #4
Crust, Graham cracker crumbs, FILLING, Powdered sugar; sifted, Vanilla, (3-oz) cream cheese;, Skim milk; at room, Margarine; melted, Peanut butter, Granulated sugar, Whipping cream, 
Date: Sun, 11 Feb 1996 07:57:42 -0500    From: "Lisa K." <lisabeth@shore.net>    Recipe By: Anita A. Matejka  Preheat oven at 425.  To prepare crust, in a mixing bowl, combine crumbs,  margarine, and granulated sugar.  Press into a 9" pie pan.  Bake for 10  minutes and set aside.  To prepare filling, combine cream cheese, powdered  sugar, milk, peanut butter, and cream in another mixing bowl. Turn filling  into prepared pie pan.  Chill covered for 4 hours.    Per serving: 697 Calories; 53g Fat (66% calories from fat); 11g Protein;  51g Carbohydrate; 65mg Cholesterol; 491mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Drizzle melted chocolate chips on top.
