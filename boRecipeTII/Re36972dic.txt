Strawberry-Banana Daiquiri Jell-O
Light Rum or, Light Rum, and, Dark Rum, Lime juice, Triple Sec, Box Strawberry-Banana Jell-O, Cold water, Boiling water, 
Mix the usual way. These are tasty... the 3/4 cup of liquor gives it a  little more kick. Good and tasty. Ive been making them in 2-oz. plastic  "salsa cups." See also Daiquiri Jell-O.
