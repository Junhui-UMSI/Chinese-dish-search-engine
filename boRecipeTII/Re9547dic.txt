Pumpkin Chiffon Pudding
Unflavored gelatin, Frozen whipped non-dairy topping, Evaporated skim milk, Pumpkin pie spice, Powdered sweetener, Water, Pumpkin, 
In a small saucepan combine water and gelatin.  Let stand for 5 min.  Cook and stir over low heat till gelatin dissolves.    Transfer to a medium mixing bowl.  Stir in pumpkin, milk, sweetener,  and pumpkin pie spice.  Chill till partially set (the consistency of  unbeaten egg whites).  Fold in whipped topping.    Spoon into individual dessert dishes.  Chill for 1 to 3 hours or till  firm. Makes 4 servings, each 1/3 cup.    1 serving, 79 calories 4 g protein, 10 g carbohydrate, 3 g total fat,        2 g  sat. fat, 1 mg cholesterol    Source:  Diabetic Cook Book, Better Homes & Gardens c. 1992 Shared  but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier, Oct 93.
