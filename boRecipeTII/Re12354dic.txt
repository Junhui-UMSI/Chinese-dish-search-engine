Zucchini Squash
Fresh mushrooms, Onion, tomatoes, Bell pepper, Fresh zucchini, Parmesan cheese, Monterey Jack, Garlic salt, 
Mix zucchini, onion, bell pepper, tomatoes and seasonings. Cook over medium  heat for 10 minutes. Add mushrooms and cook 10 minutes more or until  vegetables are tender. Cover top with grated Monterey Jack or Pizza cheese  and let melt on top. Before serving, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
