Banana-Sour Cream Coffee Cake
Eggs, Shortening, Salt, Flour, Vanilla, Sugar, Baking soda, Pecans, Sour cream, Bananas, Cinnamon, Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven to 350.    Combine pecans, 2 tablespoons sugar, and cinnamon.  Stir well and set  aside.  Combine shortening and 3/4 cup sugar; cream until light and fluffy.  Beat in eggs, bananas, and vanilla; stir in sour cream.  Combine remaining  dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture.  Pour batter into greased 8 inch  square pan.  Sprinkle cinnamon mixture over top and bake 50 minutes or  until cake tests done.
