Pineapple Carrot Coffee Ring
Raisins (dark or golden), Salt, Unbleached white flour, Bran, 8 oz crushed pineapple in, Its own juice, Vegetable oil, Cinnamon, Pk active dry yeast (1tbsp), Grated carrots (2 med), Ground nutmeg, Whole wheat flour, Honey, Sunflower seeds, Warm water (110-115 F), Egg, 
Sprinkle the yeast over the warm water in a bowl.  Stir to dissolve  the yeast.  Combine the egg, honey, yeast mixture, unbleasched white  flour, and the pineapple in a mixing bowl.  Beat well.  Let stand in  a warm place for 30 minutes covered with a wet towel.  Add the  carrots, raisins, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, sunflower seeds, whole  wheat flour, bran and oil. blend well.  Spoon the dough into a  lightly oiled 10 inch tube pan. Let rise unti ldoubled in a warm  place for 60 minutes. Bake in a 350 F oven for 25 minutes or until  browned. Cool in the pan for 5 minutes; then remove from the pan and  cool completely on a wire rack.
