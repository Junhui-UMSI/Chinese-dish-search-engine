Pineapple Sauce #2
Orange juice (or orange, Cornstarch, Cinnamon candies, (20-oz) crushed pineapple, 
In a medium saucepan, place cornstarch.  Drain and reserve the juice from  pineapple.  Slowly add the reserved juice to the cornstarch, stirring well  until smooth.  Add the crushed pineapple, candy and orange juice.  Place  the saucepan over high heat, and cook until thickened, stirring  *constantly*.
