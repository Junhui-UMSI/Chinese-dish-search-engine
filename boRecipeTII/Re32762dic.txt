Sweet Potato Orange Muffins
sweet potatoes, Orange juice, Eggs, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Whole wheat flour, Baking soda, Brown Sugar, All-purpose flour, Nutmeg, Allspice, Carrot, Cinnamon, 
Lightly grease 16 muffin pan cups or line with paper liners. In a medium  bowl, flours, baking powder, baking soda, and spices. In a large bowl, mash  sweet potatoes. Add brown sugar, eggs, orange juice, carrot and vanilla.  Mix well. Add dry ingredients to sweet potato mixture and stir until  combined. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full with batter and bake at 400 F for 15 to  20 minutes or until tops are brown. Let cool slightly and remove from pan.  Serve warm. Makes 16.
