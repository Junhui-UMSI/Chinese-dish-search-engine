Baked Beans #03
Ground beef, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic, Seasoned salt, Catsup, Lemon pepper seasoning, Molasses, (16-oz) pork & beans, (16-oz) barbecued beans, Prepared mustard, Green Pepper, Brown Sugar, Bacon (or more), Onion, 
Brown ground beef & drain. Combine all ingredients except bacon in mixing  bowl & mix well. Pour into 13x9x2-inch baking dish. Top with bacon. Bake at      350    for 2 hours.
