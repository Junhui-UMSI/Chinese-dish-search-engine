Apple Pie in a Brown Bag
Butter, Apples, Cinnamon, Lemon juice, Sugar, Sugar, Flour, ***TOPPING***, Flour, Pie crust, 
Place apple slices in pastry-lined pie pan.  Combine sugar, flour and  cinnamon; sprinkle over apples.  Drizzle with lemon juice.  In bowl,  combine topping ingredients until crumbly; sprinkle over pie.  Place in  brown paper bag, fold end twice and fasten.  Bake at 350 for 50 to 60  minutes. Source:  Favorite Apple Recipes.
