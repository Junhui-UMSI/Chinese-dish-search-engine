Pierogi Cooked Fruit Filling
Sugar -- optional, Lemon juice, Pitted cherries, blueberries, Dry bread crumbs, Or apples, Water, Cinnamon -- or cardamom, 
Combine fruit water and sugar in saucepan.  bring to boil.  Simmer until  fruit is tender and water is almost gone.  Remove from heat.  mash slightly  with potato masher.  Add cinnamon and lemon juice.  Cook and stir over low  heat until thick.  Stir in enough bread crumbs to further thicken.
