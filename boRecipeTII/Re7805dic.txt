Portofino Punch
Lemonade; Frozen, Club Soda; Chilled, Pineapple juice, Hawaiian Punch; Chilled, 
Combine punch, juice and lemonade in punch bowl. Just before serving, add  club soda. Garnish with lemon and lime slices or fresh mint, if desired.  (variation: the day before pour 1 can of the punch in ring mold and freeze.  Add to punch just before serving.)
