Sweet Potato Pone
milk, Dark corn syrup, Ground nutmeg, Cinnamon, Flour, Salt, Butter, Grated peeled raw sweet, Potatoes, Egg, well beaten, 
Mix sweet potatoes with egg, melted butter & milk. Stir in corn  syrup. Beat in remaining ingredients. Pour mixture into 1 1/2 quart  baking dish. Bake in slow oven of 325 for 2 1/2 hours. Stir  occasionally during first hour. Serve hot or cold. When cold, it can  be sliced & served with milk or cream.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
