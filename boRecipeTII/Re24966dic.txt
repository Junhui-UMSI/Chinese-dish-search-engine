Autumn Pork Chops
Salt, Brown bouquet sauce, Orange juice, Brown Sugar, Pork Chops, Acorn squash -- unpeeled, Orange peel -- grated, Butter, 
Trim excess fat from chops.  Cut each squash into 4 to 5 crosswise slices;  remove seeds.  Arrange 3 chops on bottom of slow-cooking pot.  Place all  squash sliceds on top; then another layer of remaining 3 chops.  Combine  salt, butter, sugar, bouquet sauce, orange juice, and orange peel.  Spoon  over chops.  Cover and cook on low for 4 to 6 hours or until done.  Serve  one or two slices of squash with each pork chop.
