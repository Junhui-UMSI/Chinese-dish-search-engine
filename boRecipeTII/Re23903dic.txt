Asparagus and Beef
Boiling water, Fermented black beans;, Flank steak; thinly sliced, Salt, Cornstarch, Vegetable oil, Garlic, In 1/2-in. pieces, Water, Chicken Stock, Sugar, Asparagus; sliced diagonally, 
Soak black beans in warm water a few minutes. Drain and mash beans with  garlic. Drop asparagus in boiling water and boil 2 minutes. Drain  immediately. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in skillet or wok until very hot. Add  beef, stir quickly, then remove from pan. Add remaining oil to skillet and  heat. Add bean mixture and stir over high heat for a few seconds. Add salt  and asparagus. Stir-fry 1 minute. Add chicken stock, cover and cook 2  minutes. Mix cornstarch, sugar and water and stir into meat mixture. Stir  until sauce is thick and smooth. Add beef and blend quickly.
