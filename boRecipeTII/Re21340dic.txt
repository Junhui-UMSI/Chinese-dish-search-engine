Uncooked Chocolate Cookies
Margarine, Cocoa, Sugar, Salt, milk, Oatmeal, Cocoanut, 
Boil first 4 ingredients to-gether for 2 min. no longer,cool and add  vanilla. In large bowl put oatmeal and cocoanut and pour cocoa mixture  over. Press in pan or drop by tsps on waxed paper. Refrigerate. Instead of  cocoanut you can use 1/2  cup peanut butter.
