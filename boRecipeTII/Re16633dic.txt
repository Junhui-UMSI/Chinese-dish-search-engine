Akin Back Farms Pasta Blue
Fresh Lemon Juice, Unsalted butter, Nasturtiums, Sprigs fresh thyme, Gorgonzola cheese; shredded, Boneless chicken breasts, Sprigs fresh rosemary, salt and pepper, skinless, Fresh spinach, Ziti, torn in bitesize pieces, 
Prepare pasta according to package directions; set aside.    Poach chicken breasts; remove meat and cube. Combine all ingredients except  nasturtiums.  Garnish with nasturtiums.    Given to me by Herman Sommerville II on 03/09/93.  He said that this recipe  from Akin Back herb nursery in Shelbyville, KY was in Alice Columbos  "Cooks Corner" column in "The (Louisville, KY) Courier-Journal." Posted by  Cathy Harned.
