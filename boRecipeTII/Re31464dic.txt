Barbecue Sauce #06
Cider vinegar, Worcestershire, Cayenne pepper, Water, Lemon, Liquid smoke, Salt, Catsup, Vegetable oil, Prepared mustard, Black pepper, Sugar, Onion, 
Mix all ingredients except catsup, Worcestershire & liquid smoke together.  Simmer 20 minutes in a saucepan. Remove from heat. Add catsup,  Worcestershire & liquid smoke & mix well.    MRS CLYDE R.
