Antipasto Pasta Salad
salami, olives, Green pepper, pimiento, Italian salad dressing, Rotini, Cherry tomatoes, Provolone cheese, 
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain. Rinse with cold water to  cool quickly; drain well. In large bowl; toss cooled pasta with remaining  ingredients until well blended. Cover; refrigerate to blend flavors;  stirring occasionally. 6 to 8 servings.    Prep Time: 10 min. Start to Finish: 20 min.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Paula M.
