Tomato Celery Chow-Chow
Brown sugar, Green sweet peppers, Onions, Nutmeg, Salt, Celery, Mustard seed, Vinegar, tomatoes, 
Scald, peel, chop, and drain tomatoes.  Add celery, onions, and peppers.  Scald and cool vinegar.  Add sugar, salt, mustard seed, and nutmeg. Combine  with vegetables. Stir all together thoroughly 3 or 4 times at intervals of  1/2 hour.  Let stand 6 weeks before using. Florence Taft Eaton, Concord,  MA.
