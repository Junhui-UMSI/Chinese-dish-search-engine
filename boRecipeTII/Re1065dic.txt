Cool Curry Chicken
Chicken breast halves, Butter, Honey, Dijon mustard, Prepared mustard, Curry powder, Lime; juice, rind, finely, Salt, Garlic, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    Arrange chicken in a single layer in a pyrex baking dish.    In a metal saucepan, combine remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.    Remove from heat and pour over chicken.    Bake for 45 minutes - Baste from time to time if necessary.    Can be served hot over rice or cold for a summer buffet with rice salad.
