Pizza Pockets
Mozarella Cheese, Black Olives, Mushrooms, ragu pizza Sauce, Pillsbury Cresent Rolls, Pepperoni, 
1. Heat oven to 375F.    2. Seperate each can of cresent rolls into 4 rectangles.    3. Firmly press perforations to seal.    4. Spoon 2t pizza sauce on center of each of 4 rectangles.    5. Add desired toppings, including cheese.    6. Take remaining rectangles and place them on top of the filled  rectangles; seal edges--be careful not to let sauce reach edges. Bake  20-25min or until golden brown.    Optional: Put more sauce on top. Cover with more cheese. Place in oven  until cheese begins to melt.
