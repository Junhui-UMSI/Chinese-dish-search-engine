Pizza Rolls
Cream Cheese with herb, Cheese, Pepperoni [chopped], Green pepper [finely chopped, & garlic, .part-skimmed Mozzarella, olive oil, [thawed], KRAFT shredded low moisture, Frozen italian bread dough, PHILADELPHIA Brand soft, Italian seasoning, 
1) Roll the dough to 15"x20" rectangle on a floured surface... Spread the  cream cheese over the dough to within 1" of the edges... 2) Sprinkle the  mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, and the peppers over the cream cheese and  roll the dough from the LONG sides, pressing the edges together to seal...  Brush the top and sides with the olive oil and sprinkle with the italian  seasoning... 3) Cover and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour... and  preheat your oven to 350o... 4) Bake 30 to 35 min or until golden brown...  Slice and serve...
