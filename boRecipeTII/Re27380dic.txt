Toasted Herb Bread
Loaf thinly sliced bread, Butter, Basil, Savory, Chives, 
Preheat oven to 325?. Remove crust from the bread. Combine softened butter  with the herbs and mix well. Spread herbed butter on both sides of slices  of bread and cut in half. Place on a cookie sheet and bake for 45 minutes  to 1 hour. Yield: 4 to 8 servings.    GEORGEA MC KINLEY GREAVES    (MRS. THOMAS G.)
