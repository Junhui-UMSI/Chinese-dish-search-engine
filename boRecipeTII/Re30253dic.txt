Banana Bread Dip
Ripe Bananas; Mashed, Sugar, Quick Oatmeal; Uncooked, *, Vanilla extract, Cinnamon; Ground, Salt, Nutmeg; Ground, Lemon juice, 
*   Do NOT use the instant packets for the oatmeal. Use the Quick Oats.  +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++ ++++++++++++++++++  Mix the bananas and lemon juice, blending well. Add all of the other  ingredients, blending well. Serve immediately. Makes about 2 cups of dip.  SUGGESTED DIPPERS:  Fresh Or Dried Fruit, Chocolate Wafers, Pound Cake  Cubes, Vanilla Wafers
