Wormy Apples
Pkg caramel apple mix, Apples, Bottle red crystal, Or more gummy worms, 
Prepare caramel apples according to package directions. As each is done,  roll it in red sugar while still warm. Place on individual plates and put a  gummy worm on each. Chicago Tribune 10/28/93.
