Barbecue Sauce - Mr Andersons
Sugar, Bottle Hot Barbeque Sauce, Liquid smoke, Pods Garlic, Mustard, Onion, Horseradish, Bottle Water, Vinegar, Ketsup, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Mix together. Cook 3 hours. Stir often. Store in refrigerator. Makes a gob.    <Editors Note:> With or without the liquid smoke, this looks like a winner  Posted to bbq-digest by RockMc@aol.com on Feb 8, 1998
