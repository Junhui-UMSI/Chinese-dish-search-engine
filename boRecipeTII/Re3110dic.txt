Peanut Sauce - 2
Red curry paste., Salt, Peanut butter, Chili paste in bean oil, Cans coconut milk, Sugar, 
Heat coconut milk until boiling. Add chili and curry pastes, and blend.  Boil again, and add the rest of the ingredients and mix thoroughly. Cook  for 5 minutes and remove from heat. This is often served with sate; its  also nice on toast or on fresh apple slices.    Recipe by: RICKERT    Posted to bbq-digest by wight@odc.net on May 16, 1998
