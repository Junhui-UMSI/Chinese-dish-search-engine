Turkey Taco Salad
Shredded lettuce, Cheddar cheese, Lean ground turkey, Tomatoes; chopped, (4.5 oz.) taco seasoning mix, (4.5 oz.) mild green, Water, Bag (8 oz.) baked tortilla, 
Saute turkey in large nonstick skillet over high heat until browned, about  5 minutes, stirring with fork to break up clumps. Drain any fat from  skillet. Stir in chilies, seasoning mix and water; cook over high heat  until liquid is evaporated.    To serve: Divide meat mixture among plates. Top with lettuce, tomatoes and  cheese. Serve with chips.
