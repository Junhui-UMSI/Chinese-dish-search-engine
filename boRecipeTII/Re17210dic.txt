Vodka Smash
Orange, Vodka, Orange juice, Lemon juice, Sugar, Lime juice, Lemon, Grapefruit juice, 
Combine the first 6 ingredients in a punch bowl. Stir until the sugar is  dissolved. Chill 1 hour to blend flavours.    Float orange and lemon slices on top of the punch.    To serve : ladle into ice-filled glasses.    Yield : approx. 1 1/2 quarts (3 liters).
