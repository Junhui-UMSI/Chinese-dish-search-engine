Anish-Nah-Be Pakwejigan (Real Indian Bread)
Blueberries -=OR=- raisins, Sunflower oil, Recipe soft bread, 
Let the soft bread dough cool to room temperature.  Mix in the blueberries & put the dough into a bowl.  Chill until it thickens. When the dough is firm, cut  it into 1/2" slices & fry until it is a golden colour.  Serve hot with maple syrup.
