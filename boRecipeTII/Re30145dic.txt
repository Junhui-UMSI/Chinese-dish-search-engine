Banana and Bacon Sandwich
Bacon slices, Salt and lettuce, Apple, Crushed bananas, 
Crush banana, chop crisped bacon and mince apple.  Mix, add salt, spread  between buttered slices of bread and add lettuce leaf.    For You From:  Jon Judd {Cooking Echo}  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 670 by GramWag@aol.com on Feb 1,  1998
