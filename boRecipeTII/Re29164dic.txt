Baked Salmon Loaf
Herb seasoned bread stuffing, (10-1/2 oz.) cream of celery, Eggs, (1 lb.) salmon (cleaned) or, milk, Whole green olives;, Parsley; (dry or fresh), Onion, Pepper, 
Mix all ingredients together; pour in greased loaf pan. Bake at 350? for 50  minutes. Unmold after 10 minutes. Heat one cup ranch dressing or ranch with  bacon pour over loaf.    Posted to recipelu-digest by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I Canty) on Mar 9, 1998
