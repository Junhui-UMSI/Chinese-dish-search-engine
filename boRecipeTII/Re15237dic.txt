Whipping Cream Biscuits
Self-rising flour, Whipping cream, 
Combine ingredients, stirring with a fork until blended. (Dough will be  stiff.) Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface, and knead 10 to 12  times. Roll dough to 1/2-inch thickness; cut with a 2-inch biscuit cutter.  Place on a lightly greased baking sheet. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 to 12  minutes. Yield: 1 dozen.    NOTES The recipe for Whipping Cream Biscuits is so simple you can remember  it by heart. Youll probably make it often once you try it.
