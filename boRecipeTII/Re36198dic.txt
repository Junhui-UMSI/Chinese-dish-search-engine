Beef Stroganoff
Flour, Onions, Salt, Mushrooms, Butter, Sour cream, Garlic, Stew meat; cubed, Water, Pepper, 
Coat meat with flour and brown in butter.  Add garlic, onion, salt and  pepper.  Cook 3-4 minutes.  Stir in water and add mushrooms.  Cover and  simmer 1-1 1/2 hours until meat is tender.  Add sour cream and heat through  but do not boil.
