Apple Tuna Sandwich
Tuna, Lowfat vanilla yogurt, Whole wheat bread, Honey, Iceberg lettuce leaves, Apple, Prepared mustard, 
Combine tuna, apple, yogurt, mustard, and honey. Spread 1/2 cup mixture on  3 bread slices. Top each with lettuce leaf and remaining bread Place on  cutting board. Cut sandwiches in half.
