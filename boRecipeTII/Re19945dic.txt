Apple Bundles
Dough, Orange, Apples, Butter flavor mix*, Refrigerated breadstick, Sugar, Cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 375.  Prepare apple slices with Apple Peeler/Corer/Slicer.   Cut slices in half.  Unroll dough; separate into 8 breadsticks.  Cut each  in half crosswise to make 16 strips.  Place 3-4 apple slices at the end of  each piece of dough; roll up. Arrange bundles in Deep Dish Baker. Zest 1  tsp. orange rind with Lemon Zester/Scorer, set aside. Juice orange. Pour  orange juice over bundles. Combine butter mix with hot water as directed on  package.  Pour liquified butter mix over bundles. Mix z est, sugar and  cinnamon; spoon over bundles.  Bake 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve warm of toped with light whipped topping if desired.
