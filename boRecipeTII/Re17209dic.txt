Vodka Slush 2
Sugar, Vodka, Water, frozen lemonade, frozen orange juice, 
Boil water and sugar and cool. Add remaining ingredients. Mix and freeze.  Stir every once and awhile as it freezes. Fill glass 3/4 full and add 7-up  to the top.
