Cashew Butter (Xmas)
Cashews, Butter, Salt, 
With metal blade in food processor add cashews to beaker of processor.  Process turning on and off rapidly, until cashews are finely ground. Add  butter and salt; process until smooth. Taste and add more salt, if desired;  process.
