Aunt Carols Oatmeal Lace Cookies
Oats, rolled, quick-cooking, Flour, Vanilla, Egg, Butter, Salt, Baking Powder, Sugar, 
Mix all the dry ingredients together, and add the liquids and mix.  Refrigerate 24 hours.  Heat oven to 325 F. Line cookie sheets with aluminum  foil, and drop dough on in small spoonfuls, leave 2" between each. Bake 11  minutes.                                            --- per Susan Stamberg
