Pound Cake #1
Flour, Sugar, Vanilla, Eggs, Lemon flavoring, Sticks butter, Salt, 
Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time. Add flour, salt and  flavoring. Pour into 10-inch tube pan and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
