Stuffed Eggplant Padovana
Celery, Salt and pepper, Plum tomatoes, Parsley, Parmesan Cheese, Green pepper, olive oil, Bread crumbs, Eggplant, Onion, 
Clean eggplant;  cut in half lengthwise.  Scoop out pulp, leaving shell 1/2  inch thick.  Cut pulp into small pieces.    Combine onion, parsley, celery, green pepper, plum tomatoes, bread crumbs,  cheese, salt and pepper with pulp. Fill eggplant halves with mixture.    Place prepared eggplant in baking dish with olive oil. Bake at 350F. for 45  minutes.  Lower heat if necessary to prevent browning.    SOURCE:  American Academy of Chefs--S.  Sabatelle, Chef
