Yogurt Tomato Cooler
Fresh mint sprigs, Garlic cloves, crushed, Chopped fresh mint, Green onions, finely chopped, Fresh ground pepper to taste, Firm tomatoes, fine chopped, Paprika, Plain yogurt, Cumin seeds, Salt to taste, Corn oil, 
Heat oil in a small saucepan. Add garlic and cumin seeds and cook very  gently 2 minutes. Remove from heat and cool.    In a bowl, combine cooled garlic mixture, yogurt, paprika and stir well.  Add tomatoes, green onions, chopped mint, salt and pepper.    Spoon mixture into a serving bowl and chill several hours. Garnish with  mint sprigs.    VARIATION: Omit tomatoes and onions and add 1 peeled seeded finely chopped  or grated cucumber.    NOTE: Serve as an accompaniment to curries or as a tasty sauce with hot  buttered Nan or pita bread.
