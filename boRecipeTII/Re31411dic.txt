Barbecue Beef Pot Roast
Onion, Worcestershire Sauce, Lean pot roast, Salt, Peppercorns, Tomato paste, 
Sprinkle salt over the roast and place in crock pot. Spread tomato paste  over meat; imbed peppercorns into paste; top with onions and worcestershire  sauce. Cover and cook on LOW 8-10 hrs. Serve meat with accumulated gravy.    Source: Crockpot Cooking from Barbara Blitz  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 624 by molony <molony@scsn.net>  on Jan 28, 1998
