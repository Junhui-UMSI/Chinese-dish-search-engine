Piquante Sauce #1
Long green hot pepper;, Hot chili peppers; finely, (no. 2-1/2) whole tomatoes, Salt to taste, Green onions; finely chopped, Ribs celery; finely chopped, 
Press tomatoes through a collander. Combine chopped ingredients with  tomatoes. Salt to taste. Store in covered jar in the refrigerator.  Excellent with dried beans or meat. May also be used as a dip with corn  chips.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
