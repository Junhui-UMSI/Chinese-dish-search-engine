Almond Creme
(180 ml.) cold water, (2 ml.) almond extract, Envelope unflavored gelatin, (125 ml.) sugar, (180 ml.) boiling water, (310 ml.) evaporated milk, (2 ml.) vanilla, Kiwi fruits, if desired, Fresh ripe strawberries, if, 
1.  Measure cold water into small bowl.  Sprinkle gelatin over cold  water. Let stand for 1 minute.    2.  Add sugar to gelatin mixture.  Stir until gelatin dissolves. Pour  boiling water into medium bowl.  Stir in gelatin mixture.    3.  Combine milk, vanilla and almond extract.  Stir milk mixture into  gelatin mixture.    4.  Divide mixture between four serving dishes.  Refrigerate until  set, about 3 hours    5.  Pare and slice kiwis.  Arrange kiwi slices and strawberries over  each dessert.
