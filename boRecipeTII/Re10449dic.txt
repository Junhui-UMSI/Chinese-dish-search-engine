Quick Cream of Spinach Soup
Onion, Half and half, Black Pepper, Frozen chopped spinach, Nutmeg, Cream of mushroom soup; *see, 
1. In a large saucepan combine all ingredients except the nutmeg; mix well.    2. Cook uncovered for for 5-10 minutes until heated through.    3. Ladle into serving bowls and sprinkle with a dash of nutmeg    Notes: * you can also use cream of chicken soup.
