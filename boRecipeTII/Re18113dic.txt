Veggies on the Grill - Super Easy
EASY -- (see below), Veggies on the Grill - SUPER, 
Any quantity of : zucchini, carrots, onions, broccoli, mushrooms, peppers -  or any other veggies you wish Marinate with garlic and your favorite  Italian dressing.  Grill on a hot griddle plate (not open grate) on your  favorite grill.  -Delicious and ezzzz.
