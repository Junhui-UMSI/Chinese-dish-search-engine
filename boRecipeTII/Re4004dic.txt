Peppered Pork Roast
2-4 Pound Boneless pork, Garlic pepper, Dried rosemary; Crushed, 
Coat roast with seasoning mixture of garlic pepper and rosemary. Place  roast in a shallow pan in a 350-degree oven and roast for 1.5 hours for  pork roast and 1 .25 hours for pork tenderloin, or until internal  temperature is 155 degrees. Remove pork from oven; let stand 10 minutes  before slicing.
