Peanut Butter Dream Pie
Powdered sugar, Graham cracker, Topping mix, Peanut butter, cream cheese, Peanuts, 
Whip cheese until soft and. fluffy. Beat in sugar and peanut butter. Fold  topping into mixture. Pour into baked pie shell and sprinkle with chopped  peanuts and chill until firm.
