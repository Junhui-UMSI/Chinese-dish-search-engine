Yorkshire Pudding
Salt, Vegetable oil, Egg, milk, Plain flour, 
Whisk the flour, egg, salt and milk until smooth and stand for at least 5  to 10 minutes. Put a teaspoon of oil into four individual tins or half a  spoonful into eight tartlet tins and heat in the oven for at least five  minutes. When they are really hot, pour in the batter. Set oven to gas mark  7 for 15 minutes until golden. Serve immeadiately
