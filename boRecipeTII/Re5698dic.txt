Pineapple-Ginger Syrup
Granulated sugar, Pineapple; peeled and cubed, Inch fresh ginger; peeled, 
In the bowl of a food processor fitted with the metal chopping blade,  combine the pineapple and ginger pieces. Process for 45 seconds, or until  the mixture is pureed. Transfer the puree to a medium, non-corrosive  saucepan. Add the sugar to the puree and cook over medium heat, stirring  constantly with a wooden spoon until the mixture comes to a boil. Lower the  heat and simmer the puree for 10 minutes. Remove the pan from the heat, and  cool the puree completely. Strain the puree through a fine-meshed sieve  into a medium bowl. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at  least 2 hours, until chilled.
