Tallahassee Lime Pie with Pecan Crust
Cold Butter, Pecans -- finely chopped, Baking Powder, Eggs, Granulated sugar, Flour, Lime juice, With rum, Salt, Grated Lime Peel, Whipped Cream -- flavored, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees    SIFT the flour, 1/4 cup confectioners sugar and salt into a bowl. Cut into  the butter with a pastry blender (or with a whirl or two in a food  processor) until the mixture resembles coarse meal. Add the pecans (they do  not have to be ground, but a food processor is excellent for chopping them  very fine. ) PRESS the mixture into the bottom and sides of a pie plate and  bake for 25 minutes or until  the crust is pale gold. Remove from the  ove(keep it at 350 degrees) and let cool while you prepare the filling.    TO DO THIS, beat the granulated sugar, eggs, lime juice and peel, baking  powder and a pinch of salt until smooth. Pour the mixture into the pie  shell and bake another 25 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool. When  cool, sprinkle the top with confectioners sugar put through a flour  sifter, and serve with dollops of rum-flavored whipped cream. Makes 8  servings.
