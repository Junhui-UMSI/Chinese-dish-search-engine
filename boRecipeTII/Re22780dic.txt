Apricot Rolls
Flour, Sugar, Eggs, Butter, Cream cheese, Apricot preserves, 
Mix cream cheese, butter, egg yolks, sugar and flour. Refrigerate  overnight. Divide dough into 4 sections and roll out in powdered sugar into  a 15x10 inch rectangle. Spread with preserves and roll up jelly roll style.  Place on greased cookie sheet, brush with reserved egg white, and sprinkle  with sugar. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until brown. Cool then  cut into slices.    NOTES : You can roll these out into individual cookies if desired.
