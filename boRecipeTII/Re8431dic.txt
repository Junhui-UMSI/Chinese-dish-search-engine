Potato Tarragon Bread
Dried tarragon leaves, Salt, Water, Butter or Margerine, Yeast*, Water, Salt, 1 1/2 1B LOAF, Sugar, Egg, Mashed potatoe mix dry, Eggs, Bread flour, Bread flour, Mashed potatoe mix dry, Butter or margerine, Dried tarragon leaves, Yeast, 2 LB LOAF, Sugar, 
Place all ingredients in order.  Select basic/white cycle.  Use medium or  light crust colour.  Do not use delay cycle.  Remove bread from pan and  cool on wire rack.  Yeast amount is correct though proportionately less  than called for in the 1 1/2 lb recipe.
