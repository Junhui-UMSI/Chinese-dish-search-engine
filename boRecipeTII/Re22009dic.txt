Turkey Chimichangas
Container Fat Free SourCream, Salsa, Ground turkey, Water, Flour tortillas (8-inch), Taco seasoning mix (1-1/4oz), 
Brown turkey; drain.  Stir in seasoning mix and water. Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from  heat; stir in 1 cup of the sour cream.    Place 1/3 cup of turkey mixture in center of each tortilla. Fold in sides  and roll up to enclose filling.    Heat large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat. Spray with no stick  cooking spray.  Brown 4 chimichangas on both sides. Repeat with additional  cooking spray and remaining chimichangas.  Serve with remaining sour cream  and salsa.
