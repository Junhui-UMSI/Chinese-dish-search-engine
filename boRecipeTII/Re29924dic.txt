Teriyaki Grilled Halibut
Honey, Sherry, Minced garlic cloves, Halibut steaks, Low sodium soy, 
In shallow baking dish combine soy, garlic, sherry & honey. Marinate  fish for 30 minutes each side. Place fish in oiled grill basket.  Grill 5 minutes, turn & baste with marinade. Grill another 5 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
