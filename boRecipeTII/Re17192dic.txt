Frozen peas, Kraft FF Honey Dijon salad, Bottle Old Dutch sweet &, FF Miracle Whip, Chopped onion (Vidalia or, Bag frozen Fiesta Blend, Bag pasta (Radiatore works, 
Date: Thu, 13 Jun 1996 12:37:54 -0400    From: CZerby@aol.com    Marinate first 3 ingredients in Sweet & Sour Dressing; stirring frequently  until veggies are defrosted.  Cook pasta according to package; rinse with  cold water. Add pasta to veggie/marinade mixture & blend well. If you have  time, let this marinate for a while longer. Add FF Honey Dijon dressing to  taste; adding small amount of FF Miracle Whip for zip. (Really needs this)  Mix well & refrigerate until serving time. Stir once in a while to blend  flavors.
