Unlikely Chicken
Apricot preserves, Yogurt, skim milk, Dijon mustard, Skinless boneless chicken, 
Heat oven to 350. Rinse chicken breasts in cold water, pat dry with paper  towel. Place in a small, shallow nonreactive baking dish in a single layer.  Combine yogurt, preserves and mustard, spread over the chicken breasts and  bake uncovered for 45 minutes.
