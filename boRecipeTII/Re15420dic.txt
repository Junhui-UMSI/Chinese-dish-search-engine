White Chili #3
Onions, Diced, Chicken broth, Garlic, Salt, Cooked Chicken Breast, Diced, Sour cream, Cayenne, Monterey Jack, Grated, Canned Green Chiles, Chopped, Cumin, Cloves, Ground, Salsa, White Kidney Beans or Great, Oregano, 
Soak the beans in water to cover overnight.  Drain.  Combine the broth,  beans, garlic, half of the onions and the salt in a large pot.  Bring to a  boil.  Reduce heat to a simmer.  Simmer until the beans are soft (about 2  hours), adding more broth or water as as needed.  Saute the remaining  onions in a skillet in oil until tender. Add the chopped green (Anaheim,  Poblano or Serrano) chile peppers and the seasonings. Mix thoroughly. Add  to the beans. Add the chicken. Simmer for 1 hour. Serve hot topped with  grated cheese, salsa of choice and sour cream.
