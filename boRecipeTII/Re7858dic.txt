Portuguese Sweet Bread #1
Bread flour, Whole egg; beaten, Salt, Sugar, Warm water, Active dry yeast, Milk; warm, LIQUID INGREDIENTS, DRY INGREDIENTS, Butter, Egg yolk; beaten, 
All ingredients should be at room temperature before starting.  Add the dry  ingredients to the pan in the order listed.  Combine the liquid  ingredients. Add the liquid ingredients to the pan. Select "White Bread".  Press "Start". --- Dorothy Ferris    JOEL.EHRLICH@SALATA.COM    (JOEL EHRLICH)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
