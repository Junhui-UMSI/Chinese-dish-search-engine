Thai Salad Dressing
Water, Fish sauce, cayenne pepper, peanuts, Sugar, coriander leaves, Lemon juice, Garlic, 
1-2     ea fresh red chilis-sliced 2-3 T chopped white onion *In a small  sauce pan, cook sugar in water over med heat until it turns into a light  syrup. Add all remaining ingredients, stir well. Let cool. Serve over  salad. FROM:    SOPIT MERRELL   (HKRT62B)
