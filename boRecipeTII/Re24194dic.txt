Transylvanian Stew
Paprika (preferably Szego), Chicken boullion cubes, Sauerkraut, Chicken (amount not, Potatoes peeled and chunked, Garlic, Hot paprika, Onion., 
Chop garlic and onion.  PAM and heat stew pot, add garlic and then onion.  Add paprika  When onions cooked, add chicken (1-2 lbs)    Add juice from Sauerkraut and enough water to mostly  cover the chicken (2 cups)    After simmering for 20 minutes, pull out chicken &  remove bones. (Alternatively use boneless chicken or  turkey) Add boullion cubes  Add sauerkraut & potatoes  Cook for another hour or two.
