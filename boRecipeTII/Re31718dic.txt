Swordfish Marinade
Oregano, Pepper, Tomato paste, Lemon juice, Orange juice, Garlic, olive oil, Parsley leaves, Soy sauce, 
Recipe by: Dale Coggins <dcoggins@COUNSEL.COM> This is the best swordfish  marinade I have ever made. Whisk everything together, marinate the  swordfish in it for an hour, and grill.
