Peas in Mint Sauce
Margarine, Chopped fresh mint, Lemon Peel, Fresh or frozen peas, Salt, Sugar, Black Pepper, 
Source: Jewish cooking class taught by Bilha Widmann    1. If the peas are fresh, cook them in boiling salt water for about 10  minutes. If frozen, let them thaw while mixing up the sauce.    2. Melt the margarine in a small pot. Add the mint, lemon peel, sugar,  salt, and pepper to taste. Mix well.    3. Add the peas to the sauce, and heat through.
