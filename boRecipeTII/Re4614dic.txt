Vinegar, Sugar, Dark blue plums, 
Wash, dry & pit plums. Combine with sugar & vinegar & let stand for 12  hours. After 12 hours, boil for 1 minute, reduce heat & simmer 6 hours  without stirring. Turn heat high & cook for 10 minutes, stirring  frequently. Pour into glasses, cover with wrap paper & tie securely. Serve  when cool.
