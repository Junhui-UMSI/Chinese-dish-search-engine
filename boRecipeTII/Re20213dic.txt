Varnished Chicken
Brown sugar, Chicken ; cut up, Dry onion soup mix, Water, Catsup, 
Place chicken pieces in single layer in large baking pan. Combine onion  soup mix, catsup, brown sugar and water and pour over chicken. Cover with  foil.  Bake at 300 degrees for 20 minutes. Uncover and bake 20 minutes  longer or until chicken is done. LA TIMES Author: Everlyn Birkby Thats it!  Simple and just right for the busy cook! Hope you enjoy it.
