Strawberries Romanoff
Nutmeg;, PER SERVING, Lowfat yogurt; put in, Strawberries; fresh or, Equal sweetner; up to 5, 
For each serving, mix yogurt with Equal and a dash of nutmeg. Then add  strawberries and stir.  Serves 1.    Nutrients per serving:  Calories 57, fat 1g, cholesterol 0mg,  carbohydrate 5g, sodium 40mg.    Exchanges:  Milk 1/2, fruit 1/3.
