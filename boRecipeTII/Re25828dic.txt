Baby Porcupine Appetizers
Salt, Celery salt, Oregano, Ground beef, Garlic, Cloves, Worcestershire Sauce, Onions, Green bell peppers, Rice, Tomato juice, 
Combine rice, ground meat, onion, green pepper, salt, celery salt and  garlic; form into balls about 1-1/2 inches in diameter.     Heat tomato juice, cloves, oregano and Worcestershire sauce in skillet.  Add meatballs. Cover tightly and simmer for 50 minutes. Remove cloves  before serving.
