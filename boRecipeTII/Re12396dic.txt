Zucchini-Crusted Pizza
zucchini, mozzarella cheese, Crust, Fresh basil leaves, Grated Parmesan cheese, Salt and pepper, Flour, Eggs, 
Salt zucchini lightly and let stand for 15 minutes. Squeeze out all excess  moisture.    Combine all crust ingredients. Spread into an oiled 9 x 13-inch baking pan.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes, until surface is dry and firm. Brush the top with  oil and broil it, under moderate heat, for 5 minutes.    Pile on all of your favorite toppings. Bake about 25 minutes.
