Carrot Ring
Margarine, Brown sugar, Egg yolk, Carrot, Water, Vanilla, Flour, Baking powder, Baking soda, Egg whites, 
Cream together egg yolk (or egg beaters) margaine, sugar, carrot, water,  and vanilla. Add dry ingredients, fold in egg whites. (no egg whites if  using egg beaters)    Generously grease and flour 10" springform pan or 9x13. Pour batter into  pan and bake 1 hour at 350 degrees.    Notes: ok to substitute egg beaters for eggs, use as yolks can substitute  matza cake meal for flour    Serving Ideas: a Rosh Hashonah tradition    Recipe by: Bubbie    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Lorrin Lewis <lorrin@ix.netcom.com> on Aug  02, 1998,
