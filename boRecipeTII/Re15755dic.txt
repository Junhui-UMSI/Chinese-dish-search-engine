Abc Vegetables N Chili
Tomato sauce, Salt, Stewed tomatoes; undrained, Chili powder, Ground chicken breast;, Water, Bell peppers; chopped, Alphabet pasta; uncooked, Frozen mixed vegetables;, Onions; chopped, Black pepper, 
In a kettle, combine chicken, onions, and bell peppers. Cook over medium  high heat, stirring occasionally, until chicken is no longer pink. Add  water, tomato sauce, stewed tomatoes, chili powder, salt, and black pepper.  Continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until chili comes to a boil. Add  mixed vegetables and pasta. Reduce heat to medium low. Continue cooking,  stirring often, until flavors are blended, 20 minutes.
