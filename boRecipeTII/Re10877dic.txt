Quinoa/potato Croquettes
Eggs, Onion -- chopped, Oregano, Subst, Quinoa, cooked (the basis, Salt, Cook 15 min), (1 cup grain , 2 c water --, Potatoes, cooked -- mashed, Recipe), W/skins on, Parsley -- chopped, Beaten -- or 1/2 cup egg, Cumin,
