Artichoke Hearts Toscana
Garlic; minced or pressed, Water, Mushrooms; sliced, Oil; from dried tomatoes, Onion, Sun-dried tomatoes,, Prosciutto; sliced, chopped, Dried sage, Artichoke hearts, Salt and pepper, Dry white wine, 
In a 10- to 12-inch frying pan over medium-high heat, combine oil, garlic,  onion, mushrooms and prosciutto.  Cook, stirring occasionally, until onion  is golden and mushrooms are brown and glazed, 15 to 20 minutes. Mix in  sage, tomatoes, wine, water and artichokes; bring to a boil. Simmer,  covered, until artichokes are tender when pierced, about 45 minutes; stir  occasionally.    Season to taste with salt and pepper.  makes 4 to 6 servings.
