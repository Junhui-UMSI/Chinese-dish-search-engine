Banana Pudding #1
Vanilla, Bananas, Sugar, Butter, milk, Eggs, Salt, Cream of tartar, Box vanilla wafers, Vanilla to taste, Flour, 
Combine sugar, flour, milk, egg yolks and butter and cook in double boiler  until thickened. Remove from heat and add vanilla. Line 9x13 baking dish  with vanilla wafers. Alternate sliced bananas, filling and wafers. Beat egg  whites with dash of salt, 1 teaspoon cream of tartar until soft peaks form.  Gradually add 3/4 cups sugar and vanilla to taste. Beat until stiff. Spread  over pudding and bake at 350 for 10-15 minutes.
