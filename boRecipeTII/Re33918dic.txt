Sweet and Spicy Meatballs
Brown sugar; (up to 1), Jar medium picante sauce, Prepared meatballs, Jellied cranberry sauce, 
Mix cranberry sauce, picante sauce and brown sugar. Stir to dissolve brown  sugar. Pour over meatballs in crockpot and cook for several hours.    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #473 by Pamela Morrisson  <pmorrsn@wnm.net> on Dec 27, 1997
