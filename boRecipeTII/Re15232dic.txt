Whipped Topping
Envelope (1.3 ozs.) whipped, Vanilla extract, Skim milk (cold), 
In small mixer bowl, stir together skim milk, vanilla extract and whipped  topping mix whipped topping mix; whip at high speed of electric mixer until  topping peaks, about 2 minutes. Continue boating 2 minutes longer until  topping is light and fluffy.    Makes about 2 cups topping.
