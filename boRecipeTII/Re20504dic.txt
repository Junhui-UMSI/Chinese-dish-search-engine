Apple Cake #1
Vanilla, Oil (vegetable; corn,, Sugar, Cinnamon, Sugar, Apples; peeled, sliced, Baking Powder, Flour, Eggs, 
Mix dry ingredients together in small bowl.  Mix with apples.  Add vanilla.  Let stand.  Beat eggs.  Blend in oil.  Gradually add sugar (These 3 steps  can be done with a mixer or by hand, but it is easier to use a mixer!).  Stir in flour until all blended. Fold in apple mixture. Pour into greased  bundt or angel cake pan. Bake until toothpick inserted comes out clean.  Bake: 375F 1 hour 15 minutes    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /CAKES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
