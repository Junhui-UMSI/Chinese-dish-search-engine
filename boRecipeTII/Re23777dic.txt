Arugula and Nectarine Salad
Raspberry vinegar, Butter lettuce leaves, torn, Arugula leaves, torn, Dijon mustard, Sugar, Ripe nectarines (up to 3), Salt, Walnuts, toasted*, Pepper, SALAD, ;cut in slices, DRESSING, Olive oil or vegetable oil, 
*Toast walnuts for 5 to 8 minutes in 300 F oven or toaster oven.    Combine dressing ingredients; mix together well. Arrange salad ingredients  in bowl.  Pour dressing over them. Sprinkle with walnuts.    Yield: 6 to 8 servings    From 1991 "Shepherds Garden Seeds Catalog," pg. 36. Posted by Cathy  Harned.
