Pierogie Casserole
such as Mrs. Weiss, Dumpling style noodles, Cheddar cheese, mashed potatoes, Servings prepared instant, Margarine, Onions, 
Melt margarine in skillet and saute onions until tender. Cook and drain  noodles. Prepare mashed potatoes according to box directions for 10  servings. Mix margarine, onions, and noodles together in 9 x 13 baking  dish. Spoon potatoes over top and spread them evenly. Sprinkle with cheddar  cheese.  Cover and bake at 350 for about 25 min. until hot and "bubbly."    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #117 by jlewis1@juno.com (Julie L Lewis) on  Apr 27, 1997
