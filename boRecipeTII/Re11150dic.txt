Raisin Coconut Treasure Cookies
All-purpose flour, Crushed graham cracker, Walnuts, (14 oz.) Eagle Brand, Margarine; softened, Baking powwder, Raisins, (3 1/2 oz.) flaked coconut, 
Heat oven to 375*F. Mix together graham cracker crumbs,flour and baking  powder. With mixer, beat sweetened condensed milk and margarine until  smooth. Add crumb mixture; mix well. Stir in raisins, coconut and walnuts.  Drop tablespoons onto lightly greased cookie sheets Bake 9 to 10 minutes or  until lightly browned. Cool 2 to 3 minutes; remove from cookie sheets.  Store loosely covered at room temperature. Makes about 3 dozen.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Alice Poe <afpoe@swbell.net> on May 7, 1998
