Swiss Chicken Enchiladas
6 oz pkg. Corn Tortillas, Cubed,cooked chicken, 10 oz can mild enchilada, (6 oz.) container frozen, milk, Green Onions, Slivered almonds, (1 cup) shredded Monterey, 8 oz pkg. cream, Sour Cream, 
Sliced radishes or almonds    Heat oven to 375 degrees.In a large bowl,combine cream cheese and milk.Stir  in chicken,onions,1/4 cup almonds and 1/2 cup Jack cheese. heat tortillas  as directed on package.Spoon about 1/4 cup filling down center of each warm  tortilla;roll up.Place seam side down in ungreased 13 x 9" baking dish.Pour  enchilada sauce evenly over tortillas;sprinkle with remaining Jack cheese.  Bake @ 375 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes or until bubbly.In small  bowl,combine avocado dip and sour cream.Spoon over warm enchiladas. Garnish  with radishes or almonds.    Makes 10 enchiladas.
