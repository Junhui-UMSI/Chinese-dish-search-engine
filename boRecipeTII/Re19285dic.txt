Vegetable Soup #4
Garlic powder, Cayenne pepper, Sugar, Stewed tomatoes, canned, tomato juice, canned, Salt, Garlic, Green pepper, Pepper, frozen mixed ?, Worcestershire Sauce, Onion powder, Soup bone, Onion, Chili powder, Bay Leaves, Stew meat, water, 
Combine all ingredients except stewed tomatoes and frozen mixed vegetables.  Bring to a boil, cover tightly, and simmer for 2 to 3 hours or until meat  is tender. Return to a rolling boil, add tomatoes and frozen vegetables,  and boil 5 minutes, uncovered. Cover, lower heat, and simmer until ready to  serve. Better the second day. Yield: 10 to 12 servings.    JO ANN DREW (MRS. TOMMY, JR.)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
