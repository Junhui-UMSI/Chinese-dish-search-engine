Waffle Batter
Baking Powder, milk, Butter, Salt, Eggs, Heavy cream, Sugar, Flour, 
Sift flour, salt, powder amd sugar. In bowl beat milk, cream, eggs and  butter until frothy. Slowly add dry ingredients to make a thick  batter. Cook in well greased waffle iron.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
