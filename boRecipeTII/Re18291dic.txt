Veras Buttermilk Cookies
Dry sugar substitute equal;, Egg; (large), Sugar;, 1/4 cup sugar;, Baking soda;, Water at room temperature, All-purpose flour;, Salt;, Margarine; (1 stick), Dry Buttermilk;, Vanilla;, 
Cream margaring, sugar, and dry sugar substitute together at medium  speed untill light and fluffy.  Add egg and vnill and mix at medium  speed for 30 seconds, scraping down the bowl before and after adding  the egg and vanilla. Stir flour, dry buttermilk, baking soda and salt  mixture, along with water, to creamy mixture, and mix at medium speed  to blend well. Drop dough by 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls onto coolie sheeet  that have been sprayed with pan spray orlined aluminum foil. Press  cookies down on the bottom. Remove them to a wire rack and cook to  room temperature.
