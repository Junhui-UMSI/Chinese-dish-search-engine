Polka Dotted Macaroni and Cheese
Cheddar cheese soup, STIR TOGETHER IN BOWL, Worcestershire sauce., Elbow macaroni, cooked as, milk, 
Heat Oven to 375F.    Spread drained macaroni in 10"x6" baking dish; add cheese sauce, stirring  to mix. Cut into penny thin slices two hot dogs. Arrange slices on top.  Bake       25    minutes.    Posted to rec.food.recipes by marcia@netcom.com (Marcia) on Oct 8, 1993.
