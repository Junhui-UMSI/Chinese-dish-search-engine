Apricot Salad #1
Marshmallows, Pineapple juice, Flour, Sugar, Egg yolk; beaten, Ripe bananas; sliced, (15-oz) crushed pineapple;, (6-oz) apricot gelatin, Butter, Boiling water, (3-oz) cream cheese;, Cool whip, Cold water, 
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Add cold water. Stir in marshmallows,  pineapple and bananas. Pour into 2 quart dish and chill until firm. Mix  sugar, flour and pineapple juice and bring to a boil. Slowly add egg yolk.  Cook until clear and thick, stirring constantly. Add cream cheese and stir  until smooth. Let cool. Fold in Cool Whip. Spread on gelatin. Refrigerate.
