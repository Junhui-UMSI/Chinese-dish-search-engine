Basque Garlic Soup
Salt, Loaf French bread, sliced, Eggs, Sweet spanish paprika, Spanish olive oil, Water, Garlic cloves, peeled, And allowed to dry out, 
Heat olive oil in dutch oven. Lightly brown garlic cloves.Add sliced bread,  paprika, salt, and enough water to cover bread. Simmer for 30 minutes.  Whisk beaten eggs into simmering soup. Serve at once.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by BobbieB1 <BobbieB1@aol.com> on Apr 16, 1998
