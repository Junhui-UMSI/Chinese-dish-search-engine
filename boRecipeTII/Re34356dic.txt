Beacon Hill Clam Dip
Lemon juice, 3 oz pkg. cream cheese, Prepared horseradish, 
Mix horseradish, lemon juice with cream cheese.  Add 1 tsp. of clam juice  from the can, blend into cream cheese mixture. Add drained clams. Add  additional clam juice if necessary to reach the desired consistency. Serve  with chips.    Shared By: Pat Stockett
