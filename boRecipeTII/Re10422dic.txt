Quick Coconut Cake
Butter, Sweetened condensed milk, Baking Powder, A lb grated coconut, Eggs, 
Here are ome recipes from a Brazilian cooking show that ive just  translated for you. I havent tried them all yet, but they looked real  yummy:    Pre heat oven to 350. Butter a tube pan and sprinkle it with a little  flour. Process or blend all the ingredients. Pour into the prepared pan.  Bake until golden.
