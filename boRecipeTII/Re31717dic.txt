Swordfish Kebobs
Lime juice, Marinade:, Chopped cilantro, Clove garlic; finely chopped, Cherry; tomatoes, Reserve 1/4 cup juice, Green bell pepper, seeded,;, olive oil, Swordfish; cut into 1 1/2 ", Quares, Salt and pepper, Pineapple chunks, canned, Red onion, peeled; cut into, 
Combine marinade ingredients and pineapple juice in a large bowl and add  swordfish chunks. Let sit for 15 minutes. Soak 8" bamboo skewers in water  for 15 minutes. Alternate swordfish and remaining ingredients on skewers.  Kabobs can be grilled or broiled for 8-10 minutes. Rotate the kabobs for  either method and baste with remaining marinade.
