Tortilla-Wrapped Sandwiches
(3-oz) cream cheese;, Shredded leaf lettuce, Salsa, 8-inch flour tortillas;, (6-oz) avacado dip; thawed, Alfalfa sprouts; optional, Roast beef and/or turkey;, Shredded Monterey Jack, 
In a small bowl, combine avacado dip and cream cheese. Blend well. Spread  each tortilla evenly with dip mixture to within 1/2 inch of the edges.  Arrange slices of meat, lettuce, cheese and sprouts over top of mixture.  Roll each tortilla, secure with 2 toothpicks, cover with plastic wrap and  refrigerate until ready to serve. Before serving, slice into 1-inch slices.  May also be served as whole sandwiches. Serve with salsa.     Makes 6 servings. May be easily increased for a larger crowd.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
