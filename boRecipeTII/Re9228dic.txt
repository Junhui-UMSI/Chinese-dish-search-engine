Psarosoupa Kakavia (Aegean Sea Chowder)
Clams (if desired), Onions; chopped, -Water, Mussels  (if desired), Baby octopus  (optional), Salt, cut into 2inch pieces, Crab  (if desired), Lobster  (if desired), Chopped mushrooms, Shrimp  (if desired), olive oil, Canned peeled tomatoes, including liquid, Scallops  (if desired), Cayenne pepper, Wine, red preferably, White fish, Celery stalks; chopped, Bay leaf, Garlic cloves; pressed, 
Prepare fish and shellfish by cleaning and cutting into bite-size pieces.  Heat oil in a large pot.  Fry onions and garlic on medium heat for 5  minutes.  Add remaining ingredients, except seafood and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and cover.  Cook one hour.    Add fish and octopus, and cook 20 minutes.  Add shellfish and simmer 5  minutes more.  Serve hot with crusty bread and crisp salad.
