Pueblo Fire
Barbeque sauce, Cooking oil, Red chili pods, Water, Round steak cut into small, Garlic salt, 
These are from the American Indian Society Cookbook. It was published in  December 1984.  If anyone wants the complete book (approximately 80 pgs/  150 recipes) the address for inquiry is                  American Indian Society of Washington, D.C.                519 - 5th Street, S.E.                Washington, D.C. 20003    Rinse chili pods in cold water after removing stems and seeds.  Tear pods  into pieces and blend smooth in a blender with the water. Brown themeat in  oil. Add chili pod mixture, garlic salt, and barbecue sauce. Cook over low  heat for 1 hour. Can be served as a soup.
