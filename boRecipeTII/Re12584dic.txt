Yogurt Bread
Plain yogurt, Salt, Graham flour, Molasses, Walnuts, White flour, Baking soda, Baking Powder, dates, 
Sift white flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add graham flour and  blend well. Mix yogurt and molasses. Add dry ingredients, then dates and  walnuts. Stir only until blended. Pour into well-greased 9"x5"x3" loaf pan.  Bake at 350F for one hour.    Posted to Recipe Page 08 Sep 96
