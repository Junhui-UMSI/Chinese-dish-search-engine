Zwieback Pie #2
Box zwieback toast; crushed, Flour, Sugar, Cinnamon, Egg whites, Sugar, Egg yolks, Sugar, Vanilla, Butter, milk, 
Mix toast (reserve 1/2 cup for top), 1/2 cup sugar, butter & cinnamon.  Press into pie pan & bake at 375 for 10 minutes. Combine 3/4 cup sugar,  flour, milk & yolks in saucepan. Cook & stir over low heat until thick. Add  vanilla & pour into crust. Beat egg whites & 6 Tbs sugar. Cover filling  with meringue, sprinkle with remaining crumbs. Bake at 400 until brown.
