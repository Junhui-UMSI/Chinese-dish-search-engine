Key Lime Pie
Egg yolks; slightly beaten, (15 oz) sweetened condensed, Lime juice, Baked pie shell; (9-inch), Egg whites, Sugar, 
Combine egg yolks and condensed milk and mix well. Add lime juice and blend  well. Turn mixture into baked pie shell. Beat egg whites until stiff, but  not dry. Gradually beat in sugar just until whites hold firm peaks. Swirl  onto pie, spreading to the edge of shell. Bake at 300 degrees until  meringue turns a pale honey color. Cool and refrigerate until ready to  serve.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Sue Albro <SALBRO@OCMVM.CNYRIC.ORG> on Feb 13,  1998
