White Chocolate and Almond Brownies
White chocolate;, Slivered almonds, Eggs, Flour, Unsalted butter, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven to 325~. Grease and flour 9" square pan.  Melt chocolate and butter in large saucepan over low  heat, stirring constantly (do not be concerned if the  white chcolate separates). Remove from heat when  chocolate is just melted. With electric mixer, beat in  eggs until mixture is smooth. Beat in flour and  vanilla. Spread batter evenly in prepared pan.  Sprinkle almonds evenly ove the top. Bake 30-35  minutes or just until set in center. Cool completely  in pan on wire rack. Cut into 2" squares.
