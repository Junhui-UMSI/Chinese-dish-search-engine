Veggie-Lo-Mein
Mushrooms, Bunc green onions, Cornstarch, (10 oz) linguine or, Vegetable oil, Soy sauce, Dry sherry; (optional), Envelope flavored bullion, Celery stalks, Roasted peanuts, Bean sprouts, Pea pods, Water, Red bell pepper, 
Stir fry all veggies except mushrooms together. Stir-fry the mushrooms  separately. Cook linguine according to directions on box.    In a cup, mix soy sauce, sherry, cornstarch, bullion, and 1/2 cup of water.  Set aside while cooking other ingredients. Add this mixture to the veggies  (except mushrooms) in the skillet and heat to boiling. Add linguine and  mushrooms, and heat through. Sprinkle with peanuts. Serves 4.
