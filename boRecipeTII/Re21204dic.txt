Tzimmes (Meat and Vegetables)
Orange juice, Onion, Potatoes, Dried prunes, Sweet potato, Carrots, Beef top round steak, Water, Dried apricots, 
Cut the vegetables into 1-inch pieces.  Cut the beef into strips. Brown the  meat in a nonstick pan.  Add the vegetables, prunes, apricots and orange  juice. Add enough water to cover meat and vegetables.    Bring to a boil, lower the heat, and simmer for 2 to 3 hours or until the  meat is tender.  Or bake in a 350 degree oven for 3 hours in a covered  dish.    1/6 recipe - 356 calories, 4 lean meat, 1 1/2 bread, 2 fruit exchanges 41  grams carbohydrate, 35 grams protein, 7 grams fat 100 mg sodium, 1113 mg  potassium, 70 mg cholesterol    Source:  Am.
