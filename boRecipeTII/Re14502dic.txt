"Better Than Sex" Cake
Jar caramel sauce, Box German chocolate cake, Container; (8 oz) non-dairy, Sweetened condensed milk, Heath candy bars; crushed, 
Bake cake in 9 by 13-inch cake pan, as directed on box. Let it cool  slightly and poke little holes in the top of the cake with a pencil.  Dribble sweetened condensed milk and caramel sauce over cake. Let stand for  over an hour until the cake has completely cooled. Frost with whipped  topping and sprinkle crushed candy bars over that. Refrigerate.
