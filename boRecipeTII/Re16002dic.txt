Warm Apricot Jewel
Dried Apricots -- firmly, Thawed, Lemon juice, Brandy -- optional, Packed, Frozen Tangerine Juice --, Apricot Nectar, Water, 
Smoothly puree dried apricots and water in blender.  Rub puree through a  fine strainer into a 3- to 4-quart pan.  Add apricot nectar, tangerine  concentrate, and lemon juice.  Stir over high heat until steaming. Add  brandy, if desired.  Pour into mugs.  Makes 8 cups, 6 to 8 servings.
