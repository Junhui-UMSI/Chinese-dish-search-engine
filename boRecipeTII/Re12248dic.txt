Zucchini Egg Casserole
milk, Mozzarella cheese, Butter, Zucchini, sweet onion, Seasoned pepper, Seasoned salt, Eggs, 
Melt butter in electric skillet at 350 degrees. Add onion adn saute about  10 minutes. Slice unpeeled zucchini into 1/2 inch slices. Add zucchini,  salt and pepper to onions, saute 6 minutes. Reduce heat to 220 degrees.  Beat eggs slightly. Add milk. Pour eggs over vegetable mixture. Bake about  15 minutes in skillet. Top with cheese slices. Bake about 5 more minutes or  until cheese melts. Serves 6.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 25 Aug 96    From:    Pam and KerryAnn Cobb <priss@AMARANTH.COM>    Date:    Mon, 26 Aug 1996 10:29:31 -0500
