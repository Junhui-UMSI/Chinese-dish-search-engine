Kolaches
White bread, Your favorite pie filling, Margarine, Powdered sugar (optional), 
Put dough in greased bowl; cover & let rise.  Punch dough down & cut into 12 pieces. Place in 13x9x2-inch nonstick baking dish.  Cover with moist towel & let rise in warm place for 25 minutes.   Make a 1-inch depression in the center of each piece.   Place a heaping teaspoon of filling in the depression.   Brush the rolls with melted margarine.   Bake in a 375 degree oven for 20 minutes or until golden borwn.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving. Yield: Makes 12 Kolaches  Per Serving: 138 Cal; 4g Fat; 17g Protein; 17g Carbohydrate; 211mg Sodium; 0mg Cholesterol. Dietary Exchanges: 1 Fruit; 1 Bread/Starch
