Raspberry Vinegar Syrup (Mf)
Sugar, Balsamic or raspberry, Raspberries, 
In a stainless steel saucepan combine the raspberries with vinegar to  barely cover them and the sugar. Bring this to a boil, slowly, skim off  frothy scum and let cool to room temperature. Strain, pressing down on the  fruit. Refrigerate the liquid. Use as an intense syrup with club soda,  gingerale; vodka as well and other fruit.
