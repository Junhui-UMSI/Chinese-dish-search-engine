Triple-Peppered Sausage Microwave
Sweet red pepper; cut into, Onion; thinly sliced and, Beer, Green pepper; cut into, Prepared mustard, Fully cooked smoked turkey, Banana pepper; seeded and, 
Cut the sausage into 2-inch pieces; slash each piece with a sharp knife to  cut through. The skin and about 1/4-inch into the meat. Place sausage in a  10 x 6 x 2-inch dish. Arrange all of the peppers and onion rings around and  over the sausage pieces. In a screw top jar, combine the beer and prepared  mustard; shake well. Pour over mixture in dish. Micro-cook, covered with  vented, clear plastic wrap, on 100% power (High) for 9 to 11 minutes or  until sausage is hot and vegetables are just tender, turning dish once.  Serve sausage and onion with a slotted spoon. Makes 4 servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
