Watkins Chicken Enchiladas
Finely chop cooked chicken, Chopped tomato, Sour cream, Cheddar cheese, (4 ounces) green chiles, WATKINS Cumin, 16 inch corn tortillas, (15 ounces) tomato sauce, Green Onions, WATKINS Garlic Liquid Spice, Onions, All-purpose flour, WATKINS Mexican Oregano, Sauce, Water, WATKINS Inferno Hot Pepper, Sour cream, WATKINS Chicken Soup base, 
The chopped tomato, sliced green onions and sour cream are for garnish, use  the amount you desire.    In medium saucepan, heat Garlic Liquid Spice over medium heat.  Add  onion;saute until tender.  Stir in flour;mix well.  Stir in tomato sauce  and next 5 ingredients.  Cook and stir until mixture begins to boil and  thicken.  Remove from heat;slowly stir in sour cream.  In large bowl,  combine 1 cup of the sauce, chicken, 1 cup cheese, and green chiles;mix  well.  Dip one tortilla at a time into remaining sacue to soften.  Place on  a plate and spoon on some of the chicken mixture;roll up.  Arrange in  greased 13-x9-inch baking dish.  Spoon any remaining sauce on top. Sprinkle  with remaining cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or until bubbly.  Garnish as desired.    Formatted for MM by Diana Eichman.
