Cranberry Conserve
Cranberries, Cold water, Boiling water, Seeded raisins, Walnuts, Orange, Sugar, 
Pick over and wash cranberries. Add water and boil until skins break. Add  raisins, orange (thinly sliced and cut into small pieces) and sugar. Bring  to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes. Add nut meats that have been cut  into small pieces. Cool slightly. Pour into jars. Seal with paraffin wax.
