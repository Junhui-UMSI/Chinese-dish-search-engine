Zucchini Pancake
Small zucchini,; coarsely, Flour, About 1/2 cup olive oil, Eggs, Salt and pepper, Lemon wedges,; for garnish, Freshly-chopped parsley, Chopped lemon zest, Garlic, 
In a bowl combine grated zucchini, parsley, lemon zest and garlic. Season  with salt and pepper. Stir in eggs and flour. In a skillet heat 1/4-inch  olive oil until it ripples. Spoon batter by tablespoons into skillet and  flatten each mound into a 3-inch pancake. Cook pancakes for 1 minute on  each side. Remove with a slotted spatula to a paper-towel lined baking  sheet. Keep warm in a 250 degree oven while you continue to prepare  remaining batter. Arrange pancakes on a platter, sprinkle with salt,  garnish with lemon wedges and serve immediately.
