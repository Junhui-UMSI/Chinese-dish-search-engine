Bean Muffins
drained, Cooked AM Anasazi Beans, Egg, Cinnamon, Non-alum baking powder, Nutmeg, Bean juice, water or milk, Molasses or maple syrup, Carob powder, Vanilla, AM Whole Wheat Flour, 
Blend beans, juice, molasses, egg, and vanilla in a blender or food  processor.  Add dry ingredients to blender and mix until smooth.  Fill  oiled muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake at 350 F. for 25 minutes, or until done.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Variety Bean Recipes" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
