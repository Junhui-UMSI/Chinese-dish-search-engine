Aloha Cake
Walnuts, Cherry pie filling, PHYLLIS SMITH, Coconut, Butter, Box white or yellow cake mix, Cn pineapple;crushed; not, 
In the order given, put all ingredients in a greased 9x13" pan. Do NOT  stir. Bake for 1 hour at 350. Source: My Famous Little Black Box  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1016 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Jan 16, 1998
