Potato Pancakes - Food Processor
Eggs, All-purpose flour, Lemon juice, Salt, Vegetable oil, Potatoes, Onion, Pepper, 
Note: KNIFE BLADE, BOWL, and DISC refer to food processor components.    Position KNIFE BLADE in BOWL; add eggs and process 5 seconds. Leave KNIFE  BLADE in BOWL.    Add onion to BOWL. Position DISC above KNIFE BLADE, Shredding Side up.  Shred potatoes and continue processing until finely chopped.    Remove DISC from BOWL; add flour, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Process 5  seconds.    In 10-inch skillet heat small amount of vegetable oil over medium high  heat.    Spoon 2 heaping tablespoonfuls of potato mixture into skillet for each  pancake. Brown on both sides until done, about 2 to 3 minutes per side.  Serve topped with sour cream.
