Tuna Salad
Celery, Paprika, Parsley, Salt, Garlic, Lemon juice, Tuna, Black Pepper, Onion, mayo, 
Drain the tuna and break it up into small sized chunks. Add all dry  ingredients; salt, pepper, garlic and, parsley. Then add the celery, lemon  juice and, onions. Fold in the mayonnaise until it is blended.    Serve a scoop on top of green salid, or between two pieces of bread or  toast. Sprinkle a little paprika on top to add a little flavor and color.  Fresh Lettuce is also a wonderful addition to any sandwich.
