Whiskey Bread Pudding
Butter, Butter, Vanilla, Egg, SAUCE, Whiskey, milk, -(up to), Eggs, French bread, Sugar, Sugar, Raisins, 
Soak bread in milk, mixing with hands until well mixed.  Add eggs, sugar,  vanilla, and raisins.  Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish with all of the  butter. Pour mixture into dish and bake at 350 deg. F. for about 1 hour or  until very firm. Cool.     Sauce: Cream butter and sugar and heat in top of double boiler until  dissolved.  Add egg and whip quickly.  Cool, and add whiskey.
