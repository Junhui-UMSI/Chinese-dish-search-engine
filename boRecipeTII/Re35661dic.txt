Stuffed Avocados
Tamari, Sprouts (any kind), Garden radishes, diced, Sesame meal (see recipe), Sea salt, Nutritional yeast, Garlic powder, Garden cucumber, cut into, Avocados, Scallions or chives, diced, 
Cut the avocados in half; remove the pits and gently scoop out the  insides, leaving the shell intact. Mash the avocados; add the scallions,  cucumber, radishes, and seasonings, and mix welll. Fill the avocado shells  withthe mixture. Top with sesame meal, nutritional yeast, and sprouts.     From _The Cookbook for People Who Love Animals_ from Gentle World
