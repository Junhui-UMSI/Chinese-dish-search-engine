Turkey Croquettes
Mozzarella cheese, Eggs, Heavy cream, Lupini beans, Fresh ground pepper, Fresh grating of nutmeg, Vegetable oil for frying, Salt; optional, Ground cooked turkey, Parmesan cheese, Pinches basil, Breadcrumbs, Parsley, 
Mix leftover (or cooked) ground turkey with cheese, breadcrumbs, mozzarella  cheese. Grind lupinin beans in food processor or power blender, or pound by  hand. Add eggs and cream, all seasonings; now mix well with hands. Have oil  very hot (375? for deep frying (electric fry pan ideal). Now form  croquettes with hands. Deep fry until golden both sides. Place on paper  toweling; serve hot.    Makes about 15 2 oz. croquettes.
