Peanut Sauce - Microwave
Shallots, Oriental Chili Sauce *, Water, Fresh lime juice, Minced Fresh Ginger, Unsalted butter, Coconut Extract, Chunky Peanut Butter, Oriental sesame oil, 
* Use oriental chili sauce with garlic.  ~-------------------------------------------------------------------------  Microwave butter and shallots in a medium microwave safe bowl, covered with  plastic wrap, on high power for 1 minute or until butter is melted. Stir in  remaining ingredients except oil until blended.  Microwave covered for 4  minutes on medium power or until mixture is well softened.  Stir in oil. If  sauce is too thick, thin with water.  Serve warm or at room temperature  with crisp cooked vegetables or broiled chicken, or as a sauce for satay (  grilled skewered meat, poultry or fish).  Makes 1-1/4 cups. 366 calories  per 1/4 cup.
