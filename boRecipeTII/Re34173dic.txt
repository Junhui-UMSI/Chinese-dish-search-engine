Bavarian Potato Salad
Vegetable oil, Lemon juice, Potatoes; *, Onion, Sugar, Pepper; as desired, Chicken broth; **, Salt, 
* Potatoes should be peeled and sliced 1/4-inch thick. ** Chicken broth may  be either home made or commercial.    Boil potatoes in broth with 1/4 ts salt for 5 to 8 minutes, until tender.  Drain. Toss warm potatoes with vegetable oil and onions. Dissolve remaining  1/4 ts salt and the sugar in lemon juice. Pour over potatoes. Marinate  salad 1 to 2 hours before serving. Serve at room temperature.
