Popcorn Squares
Popped corn, Corn sirup, Salt, Sugar, Water, Butter, 
Put popped corn through coarse knife of food chopper. Boil sugar, sirup,  salt, and water to hard ball stage (265 - 270 F). Add butter. Boil to hard  crack stage (285 - 290 F). Add popped corn;p stir until well blended.  Return to fire for a minute to loosen mixture. Pour onto well-buttered  baking sheet. Roll with rolling pin as thin as possible. Cut in squares.  Grace Viall Gray, Glen Ellyn, IL.
