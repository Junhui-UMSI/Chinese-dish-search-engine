Quick Cobbler
Evaporated Skim Milk, ; Low + 1 t cinnamon, Baking Powder, Ground Cinnamon; OR 1 pkg, Sugar; OR, Sugar; mixed WITH, Apple Pie Filling; *NOTE, Butter Buds?; not diluted, Flour, Salt; optional, 
*NOTE: your choice of filling, lemon is excellent also.  Use regular or  "lite", if using lite add 1 pkg Sweet n Low + 1 ts almond extract    Pour pie filling into a square casserole dish that has been sprayed with a  nonfat cooking spray.  Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and  skim milk.  Sprinkle Butter Buds on top of fruit.  Pour batter mix on top  of the fruit. Sprinkle cinnamon-sugar mixtur on top of the batter. Bake at  350 deg F 35 - 40 min or until golden brown.  Serve warm with nonfat frozen  yoururt or Dream Whip.    I have eaten this 2 times using apple filling and lemon filling.
