Toasted Nut Cookies
Vanilla, Salt, Semi-sweet chocolate, Toasted pecans; chopped, Cake flour, Dr Pepper, Brown sugar, Margarine; (1 1/2 sticks), 
Cream margarine, add sugar and beat until fluffy. Add flour and salt  alternately with Dr Pepper. Add vanilla, pecans and chocolate morsels.  Shape into balls, bars or crescents. Place on ungreased baking sheet. Bake  at 325 for 30 minutes. Remove from oven. Roll into powdered sugar.    Yield: 75-80 1-inch balls    Posted to recipelu-digest by GramWag@aol.com on Feb 6, 1998
