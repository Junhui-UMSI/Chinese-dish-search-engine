Jerrys Old Bay Seasoning
Celery seed, Whole black peppercorns, Sweet hungarian paprika, Mustard seed, Bay Leaves, Cloves, Ground cardamom, Mace, Allspice, Ginger, Cinnamon, Cayenne pepper, 
In a spice grinder or small food processor, combine all of the ingredients.  Grind well and store in a small glass jar.
