Baked Cranberry Sauce
Ground cloves, Brown sugar, cranberries, Yield: 1-1/2 cups., 
Place cranberries in a 1 -quart. baking dish. Sprinkle with brown sugar and  cloves. Bake, covered, at 350F for 30-35 minutes. Stir after 15 minutes    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Sep  15, 1998
