Connolas Potato And Carrot Soup
Onion; sliced, Butter or margarine, Boiling water, Potatoes; thinly sliced, Bay leaf, Cabbage; shredded, Carrots; shredded, Celery; thinly sliced, Parsley, Basil; dried, salt and pepper, 
Saute onion and remove from pan. Boil potatos and bayleaf in water until  potatos are tender. Mash potatoes and throw bay leaf away. Return potatos  to water, add onions and all other ingredients. Simmer approx. 15 min. From  Connie Rizzzo    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on Dec  06, 1998,
