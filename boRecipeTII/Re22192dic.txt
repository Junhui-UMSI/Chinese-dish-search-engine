Turkey Salad (Hot)
Lemon juice, Mayonnaise, TURKEY, CUT UP, White pepper, Celery, Cheddar cheese, Salt, HARD BOILED EGGS, CHOPPED, CRUSHED POTATO CHIPS, CREAM OF CHICKEN SOUP, 1 cn, Onion powder, SLIVERED ALMONDS, ACCENT, OPTIONAL, 
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS EXCEPT FOR THE CHEESE AND POTATO CHIPS IN A  CASSEROLE DISH. TOP WITH CHEESE AND POTATO CHIPS. BAKE AT 400 DEG F. FOR 20  TO 25 MINUTES.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #124 by L or M Babcock  <Mark_Lisa.Babcock@sympatico.ca> on May 03, 1997
