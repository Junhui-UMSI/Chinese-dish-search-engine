Herbed Corn Casserole
milk, Mayonnaise, Egg, well beaten, Whole kernel corn, drained, Herb seasoned bread stuffing, Onion, minced, Parsley flakes, Dry bread crumbs, Oleo, 
Combine milk and mayonnaise, mix well.  Add egg, corn, stuffing, onion and  parsley.  Pour into greased and floured 8 inch round baking pan. Toss bread  crumbs with melted oleo.  Sprinkle over corn mix. Bake at 350 degrees for       30    minutes.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by tbankerd@leading.net on Sep 06, 1998
