Strawberries for Remembrance Topping
Strawberry jelly, Chocolate curls,dark/light, Strawberries,fresh, 
Melt strawberry jelly in saucepan over low heat. Use 3 tablespoons to  sandwich layers together. Brush 1 tablespoon over top layer. Arrange  strawberries on top. Glaze with remaining jelly. Surround with chocolate  curls.
