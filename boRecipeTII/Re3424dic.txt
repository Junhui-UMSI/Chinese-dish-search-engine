Pecan Caramel Squares
Flour, Brown sugar [packed], PREHEAT OVEN TO 350, Brown sugar [packed], Butter [unsalted], Pecan halves, Butter [unsalted], Chocolate chips (milk choc, 
1)      Combine the flour, 1 c brown sugar, and ? c of butter in a food  processer and blend `til the mixture resembles fine crumbs, then pat onto  the bottom of an ungreased 13" x 9" pan. Sprinkle the pecan halve in an  even layer over the top of the crust and set aside... 2) In a small sauce  pan, melt the remaining butter and brown sugar together, cooking and  stirring over med heat `til smoth and entire serface is bubbly, then cook  for 1 min. longer. Pour evenly over the pecans and crust... 3) Bake in a  350? oven for 18 to 20 min. or `til entire serface is bubbling... Then  remove from the oven and IMMEDIATELY sprinlke with chocolate chips. 4)  After the chips are melted (about 2 to 3 min) lightly swirl the chocolate  around the top ... cool completely on a wire rack before cutting into  squares...
