Apple Raisin Bread Pudding
Bread cubes, Brown sugar, Hot water or skim milk, Raisins, Cinnamon, Apples; thinly sliced, Egg whites, Vanilla extract, 
Combine bread cubes, raisins, and apples in large bowl. Add water or milk  and sugar.    Mix thoroughly and let stand for 20 to 30 minutes. Add beaten egg whites  (just til foamy), cinnamon, and vanilla. Mix thoroughly. Pour into a 1  1/2-quart sprayed casserole.    Bake at 350 degrees,  uncovered for 45 minutes to 1 hour, until set and  lightly browned    Recipe By     : Internet    6-95    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V96 #039    Date: Wed, 25 Sep 1996 23:23:21 -0400    From: Linda Shapiro <lss@coconet.com>
