Pepper Blue
Colored peppercorns; (optl), Honey, Ground red pepper, Frozen limeade; Thawed, Blue cheese; crumble;, Black pepper; Whole or, Cream cheese, Toasted walnuts; chopped, Butter or margarine; soften, 
Combine first 3 ingredients in a large mixing bowl; beat at high speed of  an electric mixer until smooth. Add limeade concentrate, honey, and ground  peppers; blend well. Stir in walnuts. Cover and chill at least        1    hour.    Shape mixture into a cheese round. Sprinkle peppercorns over cheese round,  creating a wreath design, if desired. Place cheese round on a cardboard  circle. Wrap in plastic wrap. Serve with fruit and crackers. Yield: 3-1/2  cups. mm Wrenn    Southern Living Christmas 1995  Posted to recipelu-digest by "bunny" <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Feb 26,  1998
