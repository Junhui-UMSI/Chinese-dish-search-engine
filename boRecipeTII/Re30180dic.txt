Banana Blueberry Muffins No 1796
Lemon Zest, Brown sugar, Sweet Butter, bananas, Egg, Salt, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Blueberries, frozen, All-purpose flour, 
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Cream the butter and brown sugar until  light and fluffy. Mix in the banana and egg. Combine the flour, baking  powder, baking soda, salt and lemon peel. Blend into the banana mixture by  hand until just moistened. Do not over blend. Gently fold in the  blueberries. Line LARGE muffin cups with paper liners. Coat the liners  lightly with vegetable spray. Fill each cup a little more than half full.
