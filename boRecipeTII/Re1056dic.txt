Complete Contentment Squares
Butter, Miniature Marshmellows, About 40 regular, Raisins, Apricots, dried,pitted,chop, Coconut, unsweetened, Rice Crispies, 
In a large saucepan over low heat, melt butter. Add marshmallows; stir  until melted and well blended. Remove from heat. Add raisins, appricots and  coconut, then cereal, stirring until thoroughly coated. Press firmly into  buttered 9" sqaure pan. Chill until set, about 30 minutes. Cut into bars.  Best stored in refrigerator, covered.    From the back of the Rice Krispies Box    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by valerie@nbnet.nb.ca (valerie) on Nov 07,  1998
