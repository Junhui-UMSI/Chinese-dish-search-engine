Banana Cake
Baking soda, Plain flour, crushed pineapple, Sugar, Salt, Eggs, bananas, Vanilla, Powdered sugar, Vegetable oil, Lemon juice, 
Beat all ingredients together 4 minutes. Cook 1 hour in greased and floured  tube pan at 350 F. Glaze with 1/3 cup powdered sugar and add enough lemon  juice for right consistency to spread    NOTE: Makes a really moist cake.
