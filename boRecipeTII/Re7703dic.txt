Pork with Sweet Potatoes and Pineapple
Sweet potatoes, Pineapple juice, Salt, Dry sherry, Loin pork chops, Brown Sugar, Butter or margarine, Pineapple rings in juice, Pineapple juice, Cornstarch, 
*sweet potatoes can also be canned. Cut each one in half. Trim excess fat  from chops, try out in a heavy skillet. Skim out browned fat pieces, brown  chops well on both sides. Lay on paper towels to drain.    In a shallow casserole arrange the pineapple. Lay the chops on top, and the  potatoes over that.  Heat the pineapple liquid to boiling. Stir in brown  sugar, cornstarch and salt, stirring until syrup is clear and thickened.  Stir the sherry and butter into the hot syrup, pour over casserole. Bake at  350, covered, 1 hour.
