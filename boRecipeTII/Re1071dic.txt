Corn and Winter Squash with Spinach And Bacon
Turkey bacon, Onions, Butternut squash, Spinach leaves, Frozen corn kernels, fresh basil, 
~Saute bacon in large pot over medium heat until crisp, about 10 minutes.  Add onions and squash. Saute until squash is almost e\tender, about 12  minutes. Add spinach and corn. Toss until spinach wilts and corn is heated  through, about 5 minutes . Stir in basil. Season with salt and pepper.  Transfer to bowl and serve.
