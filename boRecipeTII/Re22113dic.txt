Turkey Mac Skillet
Vegetable oil, Medium tomato, chopped, Water, Tomato sauce, Ground turkey breast, (4 oz.) uncooked elbow, Macaroni, (8 oz) cans no-salt-added, Thick and chunky salsa, Thinly sliced celery, SAUCE, 
Brown ground turkey in oil in large skillet; drain. Stir in all remaining  ingredients.  Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 8-10  minutes or until macaroni is of desired doneness.
