Baked Pompano
Brandy, Whole shallots; minced, Pompano fillets; 4 fillets, Butter, Hazelnuts; chopped, 
saute shallots in butter. Place pompano fillets in a shallow buttered  baking pan. Sprinkle generously with the hazelnuts. Pour melted butter and  shallots over pompano and hazelnuts. Bake 20 minutes at 450?, basting every  5 minutes to prevent dryness. Remove from oven. Add the brandy and flame.  When flames die down, broil 2-3 minutes.    Serving Ideas : Serve with lemon wedges.    Recipe by: Elizabeth Powell    Posted to recipelu-digest by "Valerie Whittle" <catspaw@inetnow.net> on Feb  19, 1998
