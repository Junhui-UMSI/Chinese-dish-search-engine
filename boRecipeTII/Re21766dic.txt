Apple-Cheddar Muffins
Sugar; Granulated, Unbleached flour, Pecans, Eggs; Lg, Baking Powder, Oats; Quick Cooking, Cheddar; Sharp Coarse Grate, milk, Salt, Apple Slices; *, Shortening, Baking soda, Butter, Cinnamon-Sugar Mixture, Apples; Finely Chopped, 
*   You should have 12 to 15 thin slices of unpeeled red apple for this      recipe.  ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++    Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.  Cream the shortening and sugar  together and add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each  addition.  Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a  mixing bowl, mix lightly.  Gradually stir the flour mixture into the  shortening mixture.  In this order, add the oats cheddar and pecans,  mixing well after each addition.  Gradually add the milk, stirring until  all the ingredients are just moistened.  Grease the muffin pans and fill  each cup 2/3rds full of batter.  Dip the apple slices in the melted butter  and then into the cinnamon-sugar.  Press 1 apple slice into the top of  each muffin.  Sprinkle lightly with cinnamon-sugar and bake for 25 minutes  in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcheese.zip
