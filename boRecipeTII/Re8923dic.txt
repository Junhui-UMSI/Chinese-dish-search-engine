Preserved Eggs with Ham and Vegetables
Bunch watercress, Powdered mustard, Garlic, Peanut oil, Heads endive, Soy sauce, Preserved eggs, Smoked ham, 
1. Clean and shell preservcd eggs (see "HOW-TO SECTION").       2. Cut eggs in 1/4-inch slices. Cut ham in strips. Remove tough stems  from water cress. Cut endive in 1-1/2 inch sections.       3. Mince garlic clove, then combine with mustard, vinegar, soy sauce and  oil. Pour over endive and toss.       4. Transfer to a serving platter and arrange decoratively with other  ingredients.
