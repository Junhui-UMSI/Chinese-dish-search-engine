Tofu Lasagna
Mushrooms, Sliced & Sauteed, Lasagne Noodles, Garlic, Drained Tofu, Grated Parmesan, Fresh Parsley, Grated Mozz, Swiss or M.Jac, Sauce, 
Mix mashed tofu with parmesan and garlic. Oil pan -- layer noodles with  sauce, cheese, mushrooms & tofu mix.     top layer should be noodle with sauce & cheese. Bake @ 350 for 45  minutes (or 400 for 35 minutes)
