Barbecued Black Beans over Elbows (Mf)
Frozen corn kernels; thawed, Black beans; drained and, Ground cumin, Chopped fresh cilantro; for, Minced fresh jalapeno, Chopped tomatoes (14-16oz);, Barbecue sauce, Salt and pepper, Chopped green chilies, elbow macaroni, 
Bring water to a boil and cook pasta. Meanwhile simmer beans in a saucepan  with the remaining ingredients. When pasta is done spoon in bowls, ladle  beans over top and garnish with cilantro and hot pepper.
