Hushpuppies
Flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, Salt, Corn meal, Onion, egg, milk, 
Sift together dry ingredients. Add onion.. Add beaten egg and milk to dry  ingredients, stirring lightly. Drop a tablespoon of batter into hot deep  fat, frying only a few at a time until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.    Posted to bbq-digest by "James A.
