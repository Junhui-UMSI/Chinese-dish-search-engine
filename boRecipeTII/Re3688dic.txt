Penicillin Pizza
Cookie sheet, Bowl, English muffins, Jar pizza sauce, Plastic sandwich bag, Grated Parmesan cheese, Coloring, TOOLS, Yellow, green and red food, Shredded mozzarella cheese, Spoon, 
Split muffins and place on an ungreased cookie sheet, making sure they  dont touch.    Spoon a thin layer of sauce on topthen cover the sauce with a layer of  shredded cheese. Set aside.    Pour the Parmesan into a small bowl and add five drops each yellow, red and  green food coloring until its an even shade of mold green. (Use plastic  bag to prevent your fingers from staining.) Set aside.    With an adults help, broil the pizzas until the cheese has melted and  begun to brown. Remove from oven and let cool about 1 minute.    Carefully sprinkle mold on top of hot pizzas. Allow pies to cool slightly  before you slip on a lab coat and serve. Serves 6 peaked patients.    From the Book: Gross Grub by Cheryl Porter Random House ISBN 0-679-86693-0
