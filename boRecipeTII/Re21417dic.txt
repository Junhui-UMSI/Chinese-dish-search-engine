Uppu Elimachangai (Salty Lemon Pickle)
5 cm. ginger; peeled &, Salt, Green chiles, Lemons, 
Cut lemons into 2 cm. pieces. Chop chillies fine. Mix together lemons,  ginger, chilies and salt well in a pickle jar. Allow to stand for about 3  days and then use. Refrigerate.
