Winter Vegetable Chowder
Onion; diced, Sweet potato; peeled & diced, Cream; approximately, Parsnip; peeled & sliced, Celery rib; diced, Carrot; diced or sliced, Bouquet garni, Acorn squash; peeled, Potato, red; diced, Nutmeg, grated, Butternut sguash; peeled, Bell pepper, red; diced, 
In a large pot, simmer the first 6 ingredients (including bouquet garni) in  1 quart of water until the squash is almost tender. Add the next 4  ingredients and simmer until the potatoes are almost tender. Add enough  cream to achieve the desired consistency. Heat through. Grate nutmeg over  each serving.                               -- COLUMBIA MAGAZINE; Winter 1990                                Posted by Fred Peters                                Echonet RECIPE_CORNER echo
