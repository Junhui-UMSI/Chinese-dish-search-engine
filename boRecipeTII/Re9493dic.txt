Pumpkin Burgers
Pumpkin pie spice, chili sauce, pumpkin, Condensed tomato soup, Ground beef, Pepper, Salt, Onion, 
Combine ground beef and onion and brown lightly, breaking up meat  with a fork.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and let simmer  approximately 20 minutes; uncover and simmer 15 minutes longer until  mixture is thick. Serve on buns.
