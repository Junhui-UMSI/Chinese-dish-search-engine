Sweet Potato Streusel Casserole
milk, Bourbon, Large sweet potatoes, Large eggs, Nutmeg, Flour, Butter, Old fashion oats, uncooked, Salt, Cinnamon, Finely chopped pecans, Brown sugar, firmly packed, 
1). Bake potatoes until tender. Split; scoop pulp into large bowl,  discarding skins. 2). Melt 1/4 C butter and beat into potatoes. 3). Beat in  eggs, milk, 1/4 cup brown sugar, bourbon, cinnamon, salt and nutmeg. 4).  Spread into lightly greased 1 1/2 quart broiler safe casserole. 5). COmbine  remaining 1/2 c brown sugar, oats and flour in a small bowl. 6). Cut in  remaining 1/4 c butter until mixture is size of peas. 7). Stir in pecans.  Sprinkle over potato mixture. 8). Bake in 350 oven for 30 minutes or until  heated through. For crisper topping, broil 4-5 inches from heat for 3 min  or until browned.
