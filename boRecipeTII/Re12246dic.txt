Zucchini Drop Cookies
Grated zucchini, Egg, Cinnamon, Shortening or butter, Flour, Baking soda, Sugar, Salt, Raisins, Ground cloves, Pecans, 
Mix together zucchini, soda, sugar, butter, and beaten egg. Sift in flour,  cinnamon, cloves, and salt.  Stir to blend. Stir in raisins and nuts and  drop batter by the teaspoonful on greased cookie sheet. Bake in preheated  375F oven 12-15 minutes.  Makes 3 dozen.    Origin:  Hearth and Home Companion Shared by: Sharon Stevens
