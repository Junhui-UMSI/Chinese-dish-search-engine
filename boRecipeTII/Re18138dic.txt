Velvet Chicken Satays with Fresh Cilantro Chu
Egg white, Dried crushed red pepper, Chicken, Wooden skewers, Sugar, Lightly packed fresh mint, Curry powder, Plus 1 teaspoon low-sodium, Cornstarch, Cloves garlic, pressed, White wine vinegar, Lightly packed fresh, Garlic, Dry white wine, Boneless skinless chicken, Water, Sesame seeds, 
Chutney - Process first 8 ingredients in blender until herbs are chopped.  Season to taste with salt and pepper. (Can be made one day ahead. Cover and  chill.)    Chicken - Whisk first 5 ingredients together in medium bowl. Add chicken  and toss to coat.  Cover and refrigerate approximately 2 hours. Preheat  oven to 350 degrees. Thread chicken onto skewers. Transfer to baking sheet.  Sprinkle chicken with sesame seeds. Bake until chicken is just cooked  through, 10 to 15 minutes. Arrange on platter and serve with chutney. 40  calories per serving, 0 g fat, 73 mg sodium, 16 mg cholesterol. From Bon  Appetits Light & Easy Mar 93.
