Vanilla Krumkake
Vanilla extract, Flour, Sugar, Butter, Egg; beaten well, 
Add sugar to eggs and beat well. Melt butter and add to mixture, add flour  and extrac. For variation, sherry, almond, orlemon may be substituted for  vanilla. Bake as directed.
