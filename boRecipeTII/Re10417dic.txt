Quick Clam Chowder
Leek, Potato, Carrot, Margarine; diet, Littleneck clams, Thyme, Celery, Tomato; low sodium with liquid, 
In 4 qt microwave casserole, combine potato, leeks or onions,  celery,carrot, and margarine;cover with plastic wrap and zap on high for 6  min, until potato is softened. Add tomatoes with liquid and thyme,recover  with plastic wrap and zap again on high for 5 min until potato is soft and  mixture is thoroughly heated. Arrange clams hinge side out and cover  again...Zap 3 min turn... Zap 3 min.Let stand 5 min and serve. FROM: ROBYN  HENDRICKS   (SPGW99B) >>>>>>>>>>>>>>
