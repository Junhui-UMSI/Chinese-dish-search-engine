Pecan Meringues
Cornstarch, Pecans, Egg whites, Salt, Light brown sugar; packed, Flour, 
Chop the nut medium fine.  Mix the nuts , flour and cornstarch in a small  bowl.  Beat the egg whites until stiff, adding sugar gradually.  Fold in  the flour/nut mixture. Drop by spoonfulls onto parchment paper. Bake in  preheated 300 oven for about 18-20 minutes.
