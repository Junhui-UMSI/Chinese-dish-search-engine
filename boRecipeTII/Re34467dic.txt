Bean Pate
Lemon juice, Cooking liquid from beans, Minced fresh parsley, Garlic clove (juice from), Tahini, Sea salt (optional), Sliced black olives, AM 7 Bean & Barley, Cumin, Onion powder, -Chili powder, AM Olive Oil, Cayenne pepper; OR..., 
Puree the cooked beans, using the liquid as needed to make a smooth puree.  Combine the puree with the remaining ingredients except the olives.  The  mixture should be thick.  Chill, covered, for at least 1 hour. Garnish  with olives, if desired and serve as an hor deuevre with whole wheat  bread or crackers.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Homestyle 7 Bean & Barley Soup Mix" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
