Corn Chowder
Bacon; diced, Onions, Peeled; diced potatoes, Frozen whole kernel corn;, Cream-style corn, Worcestershire Sauce, Seasoned salt, Pepper, Water, 
"Stolen" from Rival Crock-Pot Cooking book    In skillet, fry bacon until crisp, remove and reserve. Add onion and  potatoes to bacon drippings and saute for about 5 minutes; drain well.  Combine all ingredients in crockpot; stir well. Cover and cook on LOW  setting for 6-7 hours.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by IceTeaLuvr@aol.com on Aug 21, 1998,
