Thai Mushroom Salad
Lime, grated rind & juice, Mixed mushrooms eg button, Mooli radish (daikon),, Seasoning, Chopped fresh coriander, Spring onions, sliced into, Light soy sauce, Cm piece fresh root ginger,, Groundnut oil, Garlic clove, crushed, For the Salad, Crisp lettuce leaves, to, For the dressing, Red chilli, deseeded fine, 
1. Mix together all the dressing ingredients in a large bowl. Add the  mushrooms, mooli and spring onions and mix well.    2. Cover and leave to marinate for at least 2 hours in the fridge. Season  to taste. Serve spooned over crisp lettuce leaves.    NOTES : Preparation: 10 minutes, plus marinating Serves 4 Not suitable for  freezing Make ahead Per serving: 136 cals, 12g fat Cost: =A31.20p per  portion  Recipe by: BBC Vegetarian Good Food, July 1997 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest  V1 #645 by Kerry Erwin <kerry@north.org> on Jun 16, 1997
