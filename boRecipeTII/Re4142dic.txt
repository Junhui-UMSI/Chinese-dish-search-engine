Peppery Pan-Fried Shrimp
shrimp, Salt, Vegetable oil, Flour, Black pepper, Juice of 1 lemon, Cayenne pepper, 
On a large plate, combine flour, black and cayenne pepper, and salt.  Lightly coat shrimp in flour mixture. Heat oil in a skillet over  medium-high heat. Add shrimp and cook until golden and curled, about 3  minutes per side. Sprinkle with lemon juice .  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "The dwarf with a spoon."  <jeffries@southconn.com> on Feb 7, 1998
