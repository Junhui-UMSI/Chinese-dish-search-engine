Very Hot Shrimp Cocktail Sauce
Pequin quebrado, juice of half a lime, horseradish, hot-pepper sauce, Ketchup, 
Super-hot and passionate, this sauce is great over any seafood. Make it as  painful as you wish by adding more hot pepper sauce or pequin. Combine all  ingredients; taste & adjust seasonings. Makes 1 cup.    From the <Hotter Than Hell!>, by Jane Butel, ISBN 0-89586-646-3  (0-89586-542-4 paperback).  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
