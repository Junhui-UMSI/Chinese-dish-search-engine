Bean and Cheese Taco
chili hot beans, canned, Garlic, Green onions, Mozzarella Cheese, Fresh cilantro, tomato, Salsa, lettuce, low-fat Sour Cream, Corn tortillas, 
Heat oven to 350 F.  In a small bowl, mash beans and stir in garlic. For  each taco, spread about 1/2 c bean mixture on half of each tortilla. Top  with 1/2 c. cheese, 1 tb. each cilantro and green onion. Fold tortilla over  filling. Place folded tortillas in 8" square baking dish sprayed with  non-stick cooking spray. Bake at 350 F. for 10 to 12 minutes or until  tortillas begin to brown and filling is hot. Serve with shredded lettuce,  tomato, FF sour cream and salsa to add at the table as desired. 4 Servings,  each (without garnishes) = 286 calores, 28 g protein, 39 g. carbohydrate, 2  g fat, 12 mg cholesterol, 426 mg sodium    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
