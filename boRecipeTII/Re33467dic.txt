Basil Garden Salad
Sugar; optional, Pepper, Red wine vinegar, Green onions with tops;, Fresh mushrooms, Fresh basil leaves; thinly, Tomato; peeled and diced, Torn leaf lettuce, Salt; optional, Torn bibb lettuce, Olive or vegetable oil, 
Toss the lettuce, onions, tomato, mushrooms and basil in a large salad  bowl. Combine remaining ingredients in a jar with tight-fitting lid; shake  well. Pour over the salad and toss. Serve immediately.    Recipe by: Taste of Home Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #730 by L979@aol.com  on Aug 9, 1997
