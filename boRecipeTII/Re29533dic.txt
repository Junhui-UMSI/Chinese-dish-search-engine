Tart Crust
Salt, Flour, Unsalted butter, cut in, Egg yolk, Baking Powder, Powdered sugar, Cold water, Pieces, 
Pulse together flour, sugar, salt & powder in bowl of food processor.  With processor running, add butter until mix is crumbly. Add yolk &  pulse 2-4 times.  With machine running add water until dough comes  together. Remove dough from processor & gather into a ball. Flatten  into a disk. Wrap in plastic & chill 30 minutes. Roll out on lightly  floured board to 10" circle. Ease into 7" tart pan with removable  bottom. Remove excess dough. Chill 1 hour.
