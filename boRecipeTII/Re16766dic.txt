Alices Oatmeal Rounds
Flour, Baking soda, Rolled oats, Sugar, Eggs, Cinnamon, Raisins, Shortening (half butter or, Salt, Vanilla, 
Mix sugar, shortening, eggs, and vanilla thoroughly. Blend remaining  ingredients into shortening mixture. Refrigerate dough 4 to 5 hours or  overnight. Heat oven to 375. Roll dough into 1 inch balls and place on an  ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten with greased bottom of glass dipped in  sugar. Bake 10 minutes. Makes about 4-1/2 dozen cookies. (55 cookies; 28  per lb.) NOTE: If using self rising flour, omit soda and salt.
