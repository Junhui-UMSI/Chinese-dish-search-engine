Sweet and Sour Short Ribs
Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, onion, Vinegar, SAUCE:, Sugar, Soy sauce, Ketchup, lean short ribs, Flour, Water, Salt, 
Flour salt and pepper ribs by shaking in plastic bag; lay in roast pan.  Cover the top with sliced onion. For sauce, mix ketchup, vinegar,  worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, sugar and water and pour over ribs. Cover  and bake at 350 degrees for 2 1/2 to 3 hours or until very tender. Ribs  will have a beautiful glaze.
