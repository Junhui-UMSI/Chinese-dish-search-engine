Beaumont Inn Smoked Sausage and Mushrooms
Fresh; ground smoked Sausage, Mushrooms; stems removed, Parmesan cheese, Cream cheese, 
Recipe by: Beaumont Inn    Cook mushrooms at 350 degrees for 7 minutes. Allow to cool. Mix cooled  sausage, cream cheese, and Parmesan. Fill each mushroom with 1 teaspoon of  filling. Bake at 350 degrees for 7 minutes.    Note: If ground smoked sausage isnt available you can add 1/2 teaspoon of  a liquid natural smoke product (like Wrights) to 1/2 pound ground pork  (not pork sausage).
