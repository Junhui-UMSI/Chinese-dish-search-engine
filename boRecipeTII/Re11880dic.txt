Real Cornbread
Vegetable oil, Yellow cornmeal, Baking Powder, Salt, Sugar, Egg, milk, Flour, 
Measure dry ingredients and stir.  Add milk, mix well.   Add eggs, beat    >> thoroughly.  Stir in oil, beat aagain until well  blended. Melt Crisco in heated cast iron skillet (mine measures about 9  inches across the bottom). Pour the batter into the hot pan. Bake in a 400  degree F. oven about 40 minutes, until top is lightly brown. ANN PUCKETT  (HGPS48A)
