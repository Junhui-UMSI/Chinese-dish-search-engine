Pumpkin and Chocolate Chip Muffins
Salt, Sugar, pumpkin, Baking soda, Chocolate chips, Baking Powder, Butter, Eggs, almonds, Garam masala, Allspice, Flour, 
Put almonds on to an oven tray & bake at 180 deg Celsius for about 5  mins or until lightly browned (this step may also be done in a  microwave).    Sift flour, allspice, garam masala, b/pdr, b/soda & salt together,  stir in sugar. In a separate bowl beat the eggs, melt the butter &  add with pumpkin to eggs. Beat, stir in almonds & ch/chips. Quickly  fold in dry ingredients. 3/4 fill greased muffin pans & bake 180deg  Celsius for 20 mins or until well risen. Makes 12.
