7 Layer Salad
Cheddar cheese, Mayonnaise, Lettuce, DRESSING, Lemon juice, Waterchestnuts; sliced, Bacon bits, TOPPING, Sugar, Tomatoes; cut up, Cucumbers, Onions, Green Peas; Frozen, Parmesan cheese, 
In salad bowl make seven layers starting with tomatoes and ending with  Parmesan cheese. Mix dressing ingredients and put on top of the seven  layers.
