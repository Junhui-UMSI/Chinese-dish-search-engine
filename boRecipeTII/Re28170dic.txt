Baked Egg Dish
buttered bread, Eggs, milk, Ham, Black Pepper, Velveeta cheese, Salt, 
Beat eggs;add milk and remaining ingredients.Pour into 9 x 13" buttered  pan.Cover;refrigerate overnight.In the morning,bake,uncovered, @ 350  degrees about 45 minutes.It should bubble and be golden brown. Serves 8 to  10.
