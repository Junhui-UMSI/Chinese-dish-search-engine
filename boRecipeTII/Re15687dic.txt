A Simple Dressing
Ketchup, (scant) salt, Vinegar, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Lemon -- juice of, Onion -- minced, Paprika, 
Beat all ingredients together with a rotary beater or wire beater. Or you  can put it in a jar, cover and shake well. this is a fine dressing to use  on lettuce and fresh tomatoes.
