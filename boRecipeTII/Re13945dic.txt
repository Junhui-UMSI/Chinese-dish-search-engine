Wine Soup
Red or white wine, Egg yolks, -hot water, Small bread crumbs, Finely grated lemon rind, Sugar, Butter, 
Pour wine and water into saucepan; cover.  Simmer for 30 minutes. Do not  boil.   Saute bread cubes in butter; add lemon rind. Cook over medium heat  until crisp and golden brown. Beat egg yolks till foamy; add sugar. Beat  until well blended.  Stir in 1/4 cup wine mixture. Return to remaining wine  mixture, gradually, stirring constantly. Cook for 1 minute; do not boil.  Place bread cubes in soup bowls; pour hot wine soup over bread cubes.
