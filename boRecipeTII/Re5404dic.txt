Pineapple Cheese Mold (Sugarless)
Plain yogurt, Boiling water, Sugar-Free Orange Jello, Crushed Pineapple in its ow, Cold water, Cottage Cheese, Pineapple juice, Sweet & Low, 
Dissolve jello in boiling water, stir in the cold water and refrigerate  until syrupee. Combine all except the crushed pineapple, blending well  until smooth. Add to gelatin mixture. Stir in crushed pineapple. Pour in  mold or pan or cups. Chill until firm, about 2 hours.    Source: "The Yankee Kitchen" 03-30-93 (#1) [Jonesy]
