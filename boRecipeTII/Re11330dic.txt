Ranch Style Baked Catfish Fillets
Catfish fillets, salt and pepper, ranch style salad dressing, Onion rings, 
Season fillets to taste. Dip fillets in salad dressing. Arrange in lightly  greased baking dish and top with onion rings. Bake at 350 until fillets  flake when tested with fork.
