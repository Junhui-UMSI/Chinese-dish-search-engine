Pork Barbecue
Chili sauce, 7-up, MARINADE, Calamansi (or lemon) juice, Soy sauce, Sugar, Monosodium glutamate (msg), Salt and pepper to taste, Pork; cut into bite-size, 
Pork pieces must be marinated overnight in a refrigerator. Arrange in  barbecue sticks. Mix marinade with banana catsup (should be available in  Asian stores, preferably UFC brand) and use to baste. Brush on pork pieces  and broil over charcoal. Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #207 by "Ria D.  Maningat" <riabenji@pworld.net.ph> on Aug 09, 1997
