Radishes with Sour Cream (Rediska So Smetanoi)
Hard-boiled egg, chopped, Sprigs fresh dill, Round radishes, washed and, Sour cream, 
Combine radish slices with egg and sour cream and mix well. Arrange on a  glass plate and garnish with dill. Serves 4    Source:  The Delights of Russian Cuisine    From: Joe Shedlock  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #343    From: BobbieB1@aol.com    Date: Sat, 14 Dec 1996 21:03:09 -0500
