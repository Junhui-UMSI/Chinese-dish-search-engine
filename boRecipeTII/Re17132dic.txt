Violet Vanishing Cream
Mineral oil, Borax (sodium borate), White beeswax, Essence of violet oil, Rose water, Lanolin, 
Place beeswax, lanolin, and mineral oil in the top of an enamel, or glass  double boiler. Heat until thoroughly melted. Dissolve Borax in warmed rose  water and add this to beeswax mixture. Remove from heat. Stir vigorously  with a wooden spoon while mixture cools and thickens. Then add essence of  violet. Continue beating until well blended. Jar and store in refrigerator  if you wish.    Source: Spoonbread and Strawberry Wine by Norma Jean & Carole Darden    Posted to EAT-L Digest 01 Oct 96    From:    "Anita M.
