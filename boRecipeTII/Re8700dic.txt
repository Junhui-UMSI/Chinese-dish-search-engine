Poulet Au Miel
Chicken breast, Prepared mustard, Curry powder, Honey, Butter, 
Mix honey, mustard, melted butter and curry powder. Remove skin from  chicken breasts. Pour sauce over breasts and cook for 1hour to 11/4 hours.  Baste often.    ~ - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -    Recipe By     : Carole Drolet.
