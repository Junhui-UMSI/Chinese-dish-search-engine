Tzatziki #2
Olive oil, Fresh dill, Wine vinegar, fresh mint, Sour cream, Salt, OR Plain yougurt AND, Plain yogurt, Garlic, Cucumber, 
Combine all ingredients in glass or earthenware bowl; chill for several  hours. Use a a dip with vegetables or as a side dish.
