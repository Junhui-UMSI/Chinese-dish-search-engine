Angel Hair Pasta and Stewed Tomatoes
Box angel hair pasta, Cloves garlic, chopped up, Basil, Thyme, Broccoli, chopped, Stewed tomatoes, Fresh oregono, 
Cook pasta.  Cook broccoli (steam or boil).  Mix pasta, broccoli and rest  of ingrediants.  Its that easy and tastes great! Ill be having it for  lunch today.
