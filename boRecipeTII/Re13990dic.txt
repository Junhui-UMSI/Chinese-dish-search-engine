Winter Fruit Crisp
Ground cloves, Fruit:, Lemon juice, Melted unsalted butter, Peeled, cored & sliced, Golden delicious apples,, Sliced, Sugar, Sugar, Pears, peeled, cored &, Cranberried, fresh or, Frozen, Flour, Cubed cinnamon raisin bread, Sliced almonds, 
Preheat oven to 375. For topping, combine bread, butter, sugar,  cloves & almonds in large bowl. To prepared fruit, toss together  sugar & flour in large bowl until combined. Add apples, pears,  cranberries & lemon juice, tossing until combined. Spoon into 13X9X2"  baking dish or oval baking dish. Sprinkle on topping, leaving 1"  border of fruit showing around rim of dish. Tent loosely with  aluminum foil. Place on baking sheet. Bake for 1 hour 10 minutes or  until bubbly & fruit in center of dish is tender when pierced with a  fork. Remove foil during last 30 minutes or baking. Cool until warm.  Serve with vanilla ice cream if desired.    Topping:    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
