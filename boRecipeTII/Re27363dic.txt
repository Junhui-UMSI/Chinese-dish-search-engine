Toasted Coconut Cookies
Flaked coconut; toasted, Eggs, Butter or margarine, Quick cooking oats, Chopped walnuts; toasted, Shortening, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, Vanilla extract, All purpose flour, Salt, Sugar, 
In a mixing bowl, cream butter, shortening, and sugars until fluffy. Add  eggs and vanilla; beat well. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and  aslat; gradually add to creamed mixture. Fold in oats, coconut and nuts.  Drop by tablespoonfuls onto greased baking sheets. Bake at 375 for 10-11  minutes or until golden brown. Cool 2-3 minutes before removing to a wire  rack. Yield about 5 dozen.    Recipe by: Taste of Home Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #684 by L979@aol.com  on Jul 21, 1997
