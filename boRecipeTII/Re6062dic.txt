Pizza Crust
Warm water, Quick rise yeast, Vegetable oil, Salt, flour, 
Stir yeast into flour.  Add remaining ingedients, stir well. Roll out  crust.  Grease pizza pan, sprinkle with cornmeal. Place crust in pan,  sprinkle top with more cornmeal. Bake pizza at 400 degrees for 20 to 30  minutes. VARIATIONS: Garlic and Herb: Add 2 t basil, oregano or rosemary  and 1 clove crushed garlic.Cornmeal: Replace 1/2 c flour with 1/2 c  cornmeal.Cheese: Add 1/4 c parmesan cheese.
