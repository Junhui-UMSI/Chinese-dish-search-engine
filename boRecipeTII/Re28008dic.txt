Baked Chicken Breasts Supreme
Salt, Garlic, Celery seed, Hungarian sweet paprika, Boneless, skinless chicken, Fine dry bread crumbs, Plain yogurt, Lemon juice, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, 
**from Taste of Home**    In large bowl, combine first 8 ingredients. Place chicken in mixture and  turn to coat. Cover and marinate overnight in the refridgerator. Remove  chicken from marinade; coat each piece with crumbs. Arrange on a shallow  baking pan. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until juices  run clear.
