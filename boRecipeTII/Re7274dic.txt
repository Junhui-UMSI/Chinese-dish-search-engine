Pork Chop Lima Casserole
Onion, Pork Chops, Pepper, Tomato catsup, Limas drained, reserve liqui, Green pepper, diced, Hot water, Salt, Vegetable oil, 
Season chops. Brown in oil with onion.  Place green pepper, limas and chops  in layers in an oven proof casserole dish. Add hot water to pan drippings  with catsup and bring to a boil. Pour over layered casserole. Add the bean  liquid as needed while baking in the oven. Bake @ 350 for 1 1/2 hrs. Hope  you enjoy it! Lisa/Cedar Rapids
