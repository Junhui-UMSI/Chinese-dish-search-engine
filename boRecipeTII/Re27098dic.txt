Thyme and Thyme Again Dressing
Red wine vinegar, Crumbled feta or goat cheese, White pepper, minced or crushed, Minced fresh marjoram, Finely minced fresh thyme, Garlic clove (or more), olive oil, Dijon-style mustard, Minced fresh oregano, Honey or sugar, Salt, 
Place all ingredients in a jar, seal tightly, and shake vigorously for 10  seconds.  Use immediately or refrigerate. Shake again before serving. If  kept refrigerated, the vinaigrette should keep for up to two weeks.    * Source: Frances Shridan Goulart, Weston, Connecticut * The Herb  Companion, August/September 1993 * Typed for you by Karen Mintzias
