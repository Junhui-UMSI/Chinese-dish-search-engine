Bake-Sale Lemon Bars
All-purpose flour, All-purpose flour, Eggs, Sugar, Lemon juice, Butter or margarine, 
Combine flour, sugar and butter; pat into a greased 13x9x2  inch baking  pan.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.  Meanwhile, in a bowl, whisk eggs, sugar,  flour and lemon juice until frothy; pour over the hot crust. Bake at 350  for 20-25 minutes or until light golden brown.  Cool on a wire rack.
