Zucchini Oven Omelet
Pepper, Chopped green bell pepper, Salt, Cooking oil, Eggs, lightly beaten, Chopped zucchini, Butter or margarine, melted, Cheddar cheese, Light cream, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
In a 10-in. ovenproof skillet, saute zucchini and green pepper in oil until  tender, about 3 minutes. Combine eggs, Parmesan cheese, cream, butter, salt  and pepper; pour over the vegetable mixture. Cook and stir gently for 3  minutes or until eggs are set on bottom. Top with cheese. Bake at 350 for  5-7 minutes or until eggs are set and cheese is melted.
