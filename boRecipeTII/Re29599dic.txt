Tasso Hollandaise Sauce
Water, Egg yolks, Salt, Stick (1/4 pound) butter,;, Tabasco sauce, About 1/4 cup finely chopped, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
In a stainless steel bowl set over a pot of simmering water over medium  heat, whisk the egg yolks with the lemon juice, salt, Tabasco, and water  until pale yellow and slightly thick. Be careful not to let the bowl touch  the water.    Remove the bowl from the pot whisking vigorously, add the butter, 1  teaspoon at a time, until all is incorporated. Add the tasso and continue  whisking for 30 seconds. Serve immediately.
