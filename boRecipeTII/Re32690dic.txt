Sweet Potato and Pecan Casserole
Orange peel -- grated, Salt, Pecans -- chopped, Sweet potatoes -- boiled, Apricot nectar, Hot water, Brown sugar, Ground cinnamon, Margarine, Cornstarch, 
Place cooked, sliced sweet potatoes in a 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan. In a saucepan  combine sugar, cornstarch, salt and cinnamon. Stir in apricot nectar, water  and orange peel. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, for two minutes.  Remove from heat; stir in margarine and pecans. Pour over sweet potatoes.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
