Whipped Jello Cake .. Magazine
Raspberry jello, Cool whip, Walnuts, chopped, Maraschino cherries, chopped, Angel food cake, Crushed pineapple, save, 
MAKE JELLO, USING JUICE FOR PART OF THE LIQUID. WHIP THOROUGHLY WHEN IT  BEGINS TO SET. ADD COOLWHIP AND PINEAPPLE. SPLIT CAKE IN HALF. SPREAD  FILLING BETWEEN LAYERS AND ON TOP AND SIDES. GARNISH WITH NUTS AND  CHERRIES. REFRIGERATE FOR AT LEAST ONE HOUR.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #544 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Mar 27, 1997
