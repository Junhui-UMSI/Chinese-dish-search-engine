Trout Almadine
Slivered almonds, Salt, Lemon juice, Butter, Dill weed, Trout fillets, parsley, Pepper, 
In 8 inch dish, place almonds and butter.  Microwave uncovered on HIGH 5  minutes, until almonds and butter are golden brown. Remove almonds and  set aside. Place fish in butter, turning to coat both sides. Sprinkle  with salt, dill, pepper, parsley and lemon juice.  Roll fillets and  leave in dish.  Cover with wax paper and microwave on HIGH 4 minutes.  Uncover, sprinkle with almonds.  Microwave covered on HIGH 1 minute,  until fish flakes with fork.  Let stand 4 minutes. Garnish with lemon  wedges, parsley springs and sprinkle of paprika. Serves 2-3.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mm-trout.zip
