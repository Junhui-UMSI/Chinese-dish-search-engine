Raisin Squares
Seedless raisins, Flour, Sugar, Butter, Eggs, well beaten, Cocoa, Pecans, Vanilla, Salt, 
Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs.  Beat thoroughly. Sift flour, measure,  and sift with salt and cocoa. Add to first mixture. Add nuts, raisins, and  flavoring.  Mix thoroughly.  Pour into shallow, well-oiled baking pan. Bake  in moderate oven (375 F) about 45 minutes. Cool. Cut in squares. The  Household Searchlight
