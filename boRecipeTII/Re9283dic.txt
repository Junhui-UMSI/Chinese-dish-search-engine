Pueblo Red Chile Stew
Red chile powder, Blue cornmeal *, Stewing beef, Water, Salt, Potatoes, 
Cut meat into 1" cubes. Cover with water and bring to a boil in a large  kettle. Reduce heat to simmer and begin cooking.    Meanwhile peel and cube potatoes, add to the meat and cook until meat and  potatoes are tender. Measure chile powder and blue cornmeal into a bowl  with enough water to make a paste. Stir slowly into the stew, mix well to  thicken the broth. Simmer for 1/2 hour and serve.
