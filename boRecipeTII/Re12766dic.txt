Yucat?n Marinade
Fresh orange juice, Fresh lime juice, Paprika, olive oil, Fresh Lemon Juice, Pasilla chile powder, Ancho chile powder, Salt, Black Pepper, Cayenne, 
In a food processor, combine all the ingredients except the olive oil and  process for 30 seconds. With the motor running, slowly add the olive oil  and process until emulsified. May be prepared up to one day ahead, covered  and refrigerated.
