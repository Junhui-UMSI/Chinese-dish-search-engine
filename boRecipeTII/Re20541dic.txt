Apple Cheese Spread
Cream cheese -- soft, Room temp, Black pepper, Or the like), Dried thyme, Grated cheddar cheese -- at, Brandy or sherry, Dried Oregano, Size apple (Granny Smith --, Dried basil, 
Combine the cheeses, and brandy.  Beat till smooth. Peel, core, and grate  the apple into the bowl. Sprinkle w/ the pepper and the herbs. Stir till  combined. Spoon into a bowl (or crock), cover, and chill about an hour.  Serve on toast or crackers.
