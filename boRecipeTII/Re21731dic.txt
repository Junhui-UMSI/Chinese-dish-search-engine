Apple Walnut Cake with Lemon Butter Frosting
Cinnamon, Flour, Water, Butter, Lemon juice, Eggs, Vanilla, Salt, Baking soda, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Walnuts, Apples, ***LEMON BUTTER FROSTING***, 
In bowl stir together apples and sugar; let stand 5 minutes. Stir together  eggs, oil and vanilla.  Sift flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Blend  in egg mixture.  Fold in apples and walnuts, mixing well. Pour batter into  greased and floured 13 x 9 baking pan.  Bake at 350 for 60 minutes or until  done.  Cool and frost with Lemon Butter Frosting. Frosting: In bowl combine  butter, sugar, lemon juice and enough water for spreading consistency; beat  well. Source:  Favorite Apple Recipes.
