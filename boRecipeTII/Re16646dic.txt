Als Cheese Dip
Can, Hormel chili (no beans), Jalapeno peppers -- to taste, Onion, Velveeta cheese, 
Preheat oven to 350 F.  Cut cheese into chunks; mix in chili.  Chop onion  finely; add to cheese.  Chop pepper finely; add to mixture and mix well.  Bake until cheese starts to melt.  Mix; return to oven.  Bake until all  cheese is melted.
