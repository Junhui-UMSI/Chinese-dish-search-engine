Watermelon
WATERMELON FRESH(SEASONAL, 
START WITH 1 EACH.  WASH IN COLD WATER AND CUT WATERMELONS INTO 3/4  TO 1" SLICES PER SERVING.  PLACE IN APPROPRIATE SERVING PAN ON COLD LINE  FOR CUSTOMER SELF SERVICE.  ENSURE TONGS ARE AVAILABLE.  REPLENISH WITH  ONE WATERMELON AT A TIME. ENSURE WEIGHTS ARE POSTED TO THE KITCHEN  REQUISTION AND RETURNS WORKSHEET.
