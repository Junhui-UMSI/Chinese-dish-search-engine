Turnips and Bacon
Salt, GREEN TURNIPS, frozen, PEPPER, BLACK, BACON, frozen, WATER, 
1.  PEEL AND SLICE TURNIPS INTO 1/4 SLICES.    2.  ADD BACON TO WATER; SIMMER 30 MINUTES.    3.  ADD SALT, PEPPER, AND TURNIPS TO BACON AND WATER.    4.  COVER; SIMMER 15 TO 30 MINUTES OR UNTIL JUST TENDER.  :  **ALL NOTES ARE PER 100 PORTIONS.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 2, 24 LB 10 OZ TURNIPS A.P. WILL YIELD 20 LB PEELED  SLICED TURNIPS.    NOTE:  2.
