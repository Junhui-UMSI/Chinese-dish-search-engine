Vanilla Cookies
SHORTENING; 3LB, Baking soda, All Purpose Flour, SUGAR; POWDER 2 LB, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, IMITATION VANILLA, BUTTER PRINT SURE, EGGS SHELL, Salt, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN                 TEMPERATURE:  375 F. OVEN    1.  PLACE INGREDIENTS IN MIXER BOWL IN ORDER LISTED, BEAT AT LOW SPEED  1 TO 2 MINUTES OR UNTIL SMOOTH. SCRAPE DOWN BOWL ONCE DURING MIXING.    2.  DIVIDE DOUGH INTO 10 PIECES. ABOUT 1 LB 4 OZ EACH.  ROLL IN POWDERED  SUGAR FORMING ROLLS 2 INCHES THICK.    3.  SLICE EACH ROLL INTO 20 PIECES. DIP TOP OF EACH PIECE IN POWDERED  SUGAR; PLACE IN ROWS, 4 BY 6, ON GREASED PANS. DO NOT FLATTEN COOKIES.    4.  BAKE 12 TO 14 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE.    5.  LOOSEN COOKIES FROM PANS WHILE STILL WARM.    NOTE:  A PASTRY TUBE MAY BE USED TO DROP COOKIES ONTO SHEET PAN.
