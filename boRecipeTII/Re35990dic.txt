Beef Jerky 3
Honey, Garlic, Pinch salt, Flank steak, Lemon juice, Soy sauce, Pinch pepper, 
Put steak in freezer for about half an hour, just until firm.  Slice steak  across grain, about 1/4-inch thick.  Combine remaining ingredients and  marinate steak strips in this for at least 2 hours.
