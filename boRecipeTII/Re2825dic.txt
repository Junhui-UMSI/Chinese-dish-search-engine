Peanut Butter Crisscrosses
Peanut butter; (can use, Sugar, Eggs, Flour, Brown sugar, Baking soda, Vanilla, Shortening, Salt, 
Cream shortening, vanilla and sugars. Add eggs; beat thoroughly. Stir in  peanut butter, then dry ingredients. Form into small balls with palms of  hands; place on greased cookie sheet. Press with back of fork to make  crisscrosses. Bake at 375 degrees for about 10 minutes. Makes 50-60  cookies.
