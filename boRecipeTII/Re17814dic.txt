Almost Instant (Secret) Broccoli Soup
Left over or freshly cooked, Salt and pepper, Broccoli, Mushroom soup, Milk -- as desired, 
Remove tiny tops of flowered ends of broccoli and save.  Dump remaining  stems into blender.  Add soup, some milk, salt and pepper.  Blend. Garnish  with saved tops.  Heat and serve.
