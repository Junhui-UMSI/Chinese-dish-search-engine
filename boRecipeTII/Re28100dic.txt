Baked Cod Provencal
Cod fillets, Tomatoes, Onion, olive oil, Salt, Capers, Oregano, dried leaf, Lemon juice, Garlic, Black Greek olives, Basil, fresh, Pepper, 
1. Preheat oven to 450F. Arrange fillets in 8x8x2" square baking dish.   2. Saute onion and garlic in oil in medium-size skillet over medium heat  3-5 minutes or until lightly softened. Add tomatoes, capers, basil, olives,  lemon juice, oregano, salt if using, and pepper. Simmer, stirring  occasionally, 8 minutes. Spoon sauce over fish.   3. Bake in preheated 450F. oven for 10 minutes or until cooked through.
