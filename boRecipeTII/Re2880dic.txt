Peanut Butter Fudge #08
Crunchy peanut butter, Sweetened condensed milk, Semisweet chocolate morsels, 
Date: Sat, 23 Mar 1996 18:57:57 -0500    From: beck4@pipeline.com (Eileen & Bob Holze)    Put chocolate chips and peanut butter into a microwave-safe dish. Microwave  on high 1-2 minutes until chips have completely melted.  Add sweetened  condensed milk; stir until mixture is of a uniform consistency. Pour into a  8"x8" square pan lined with wax paper.  Refrigerate until firm. Cut into 1"  pieces.
