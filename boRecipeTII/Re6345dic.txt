Plantation Pecan Pie
Vanilla extract, Nine-inch unbaked pie crust, Sugar, Eggs, Butter or Margarine, melted, Pecan halves, Dark corn or cane syrup, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  In large bowl, beat eggs. Stir in syrup,  sugar, butter and vanilla.  Stir in pecans. Pour in crust. Bake, uncovered,  35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted about 1-1/2 inches from edge comes  out clean.  Cool on wire rack.
