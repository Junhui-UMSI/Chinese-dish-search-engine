Quick Franks and Macaroni
Cream of chicken soup, Cheddar cheese, elbow macaroni, Dash pepper, Dash of worcestershire sauce, Turkey (or chicken) franks, Onion, Frozen corn, 
Fat grams    per serving:              Approx. Cook Time: .5 hr Directions:  Bring a large pot of water to boil.  Chop frozen franks. When water reaches  the boil, add pasta, franks, onions and corn. Cook until pasta is "al  dente". Remove from heat.  Pour off water, stir in soup and cheddar cheese  until mixture is heated through and cheese has melted. I also add a dash of  Worcestershire sauce and pepper. Serve with lettuce wedges. Total meal time  ~ 1/2 hour.
