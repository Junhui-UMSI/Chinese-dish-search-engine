Peanutty Pumpkin Spread
Solid-pack pumpkin, Honey, Ground cinnamon, Chopped peanuts, Carrot, celery sticks, apple, Peanut butter, 
Combine pumpkin, peanut butter, honey and cinnamon; mix well. Chill. Before  serving, top with nuts. Serve with vegetable sticks and apple slices. Makes  one cup. Fun to serve the spread in little individual paper cups with the  fruit and vegetables in individual zip-loc bags. Easy to transport to  parties.
