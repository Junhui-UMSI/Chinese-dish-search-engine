Apple Pudding with Lemon Sauce
Water, Stick butter or margarine, Nutmeg, Sugar, Cinnamon, Lemons; juice of, All-purpose flour, LEMON SAUCE, Egg, Sugar, Stick butter or margarine, Flour, Baking soda, Apples; peeled and diced, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees.     Cream sugar, egg and butter together. Add flour, soda, nutmeg and  cinnamon. Blend in apples. (If mixture is too stiff, add 1/2 cup water.)     Spread dough in a greased and floured 8x10-inch baking pan or cookie sheet  with 1-inch sides. Bake for 30 minutes. (Be careful not to overbake.)     LEMON SAUCE: Put water, sugar, flour, butter and lemon juice in saucepan  with the halves from one of the juiced lemons (to impart a bit of lemon  oil). Bring to a boil. Remove lemon halves. Boil for 2 minutes while  stirring.     To serve, cut pudding into squares and pour hot sauce over them. Or omit  sauce and dust with confectioners sugar.
