Baby Food Bars
Vegetable oil, 8oz pkg cream cheese, Vanilla, Baking soda, Eggs, Sugar, Cinnamon, Flour, Stick margarine, Box powdered sugar, (4 1/2 oz) jars baby food, 
Mix eggs, sugar, flour, baby food, oil, soda and cinnamon together. Spread  onto an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 for 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool.  For icing: Mix cream cheese, margarine, vanilla and powdered sugar until  smooth. Top bars with icing.
