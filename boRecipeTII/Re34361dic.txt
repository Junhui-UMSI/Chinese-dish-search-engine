Bean and Apple Casserole
Cinnamon, Firm eating apples, peeled,, Salt, (250ml) Great Northern or, Brown sugar, Red wine vinegar, 
From: Margaret Baechler, a/k/a mbaechle@lehman.com    Cook the beans in unsalted water until tender, about 1 to 1 1/2 hours.  Drain, reserving the liquid. Preheat the oven to 350F (180C). Stir the  apples, sugar, cinnamon, vinegar, and salt into the beans. Transfer to an  ovenproof dish and just cover with some of the reserved liquid. Cover with  a lid or foil and bake for 35-40 minutes.    Posted by Michelle Dick, FATFREE listowner, while list was down.    From:    KimAllen@aol.com    Date:    Thu, 10 Oct 96 04:32:43 PDT
