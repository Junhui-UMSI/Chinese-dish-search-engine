Potato Pork Chop Casserole
Mushroom soup, salt and pepper, sour cream, Potatoes, Water, Parsley, Pork Chops, 
Mix above ingredients.  Brown pork chops.  Slice 2 cups of potatoes thin.  Alternate layers of mix and potatoes.  Salt and pepper to taste. Cover with  pork chops.  Bake 1 hour and 15 minutes at 375 degrees covered. Randy Rigg    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #263      Date: Wed, 25 Sep 1996 18:29:47 -0500      From: netdir@cyberspc.mb.ca (S.Pickell)
