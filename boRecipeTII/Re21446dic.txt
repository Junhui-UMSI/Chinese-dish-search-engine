Upside-Down German Chocolate Cake
Coconut, Powdered sugar, Pecans, margarine, German chocolate cake mix, Cream cheese, 
Oil, but do not flour a 9 x 13 inch pan. Layer coconut and chopped pecans  in bottom of pan. Prepare cake mix using the directions on the box. Pour  over the coconut and chopped pecans. Mix together the remaining  ingredients. drop by spoon over cake mix. Bake 350 for 50 minutes. After  cake has cooled, turn over. Dust with powdered sugar on top, if desired.    Source: Albertsons cream cheese package    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 539 by "Noreen Welsh"  <Noreen@iname.com> on Jan 16, 1998
