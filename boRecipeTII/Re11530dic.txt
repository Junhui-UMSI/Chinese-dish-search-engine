Raspberry Dressing
Raspberry or red wine, Sugar, Plain nonfat yogurt, Raspberries*, 
Place all ingredients in blender. Cover and blend on high speed about 15  seconds or until smooth.    *Frozen unsweetened loose-pack raspberries, thawed and drained, can be  substituted for the fresh raspberries.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
