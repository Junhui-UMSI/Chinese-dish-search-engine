Tortellini with Garden Vegetables
Fresh or Frozen Peas, Carrot *, Fresh Basil or Parsley ***, Ripe Tomato **, Fresh Ground Pepper To taste, olive oil, Parmesan cheese, Zucchini; sliced thin, Tortellini or Penne, 
* Peeled and thinly sliced ** Cored, peeled and chopped *** Minced OR use      1/2    tsp each.   In a large saucepan bring 6 cups of water to a rolling boil. Add pasta and  cook 8 to 10 mins or until tender. Reserve 1/3 cup cooking liquid; drain  pasta. Meanwhile in a 12" skillet heat oil over low heat for one min. Add  the carrot and zucchini and gently saute for 5 mins stirring occasionally.  Mix in tomato and peas; cook 5 mins longer. Add the pasta, cooking liquid  and remaining ingredients. Toss to combine and heat through about one min.  Serve with additional Parmesan cheese on the side.
