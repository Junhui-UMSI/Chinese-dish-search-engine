Strawberry Cheesecake Trifle
Vanilla, Icing sugar, Whipping cream, Fresh strawberries, cream cheese, Sour cream, Strawberry Jello, Sugar, Angel food cake, 
Combine strawberries ,sugar and strawberry jello. Cream together cream  cheese and icing sugar; add sour cream and vanilla. Whip cream. Fold  whipped cream into cream cheese mixture. Add cake pieces to this. Layer  together in large glass bowl starting with cake / cheese mixture and ending  with strawberries. (Three layers of each). Cover with plastic wrap and  leave overnight. This makes a lot !    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Pat and Dan Jahns-Kraak <jahns@PANGEA.CA> on Apr  10, 1998
