Baked Pears with Kuzu-Raisin Sauce
Spring water, Apple juice, Raisins, Anjou pears; (or 1/2 pear, Rice syrup, Kuzu, 
Cut and core pears and slice in half. Put in a baking dish with apple  juice, water, and raisins. Bake 20 minutes. Then mix kuzu in cold water and  thicken the remaining liquid. Add rice syrup to taste, simmer, and serve in  serving bowl.    Formatted by suechef@sover.net    NOTES : This is a sweet, smooth, relaxing dessert.
