Potato Potage
Celery; sliced, Parmesan cheese, 8 oz package cream cheese;, Dry mustard, Margarine, milk, Potato; chopped and cooked, Salt, Pepper, Onion, Water, 
Saute celery and onion in margarine. Add cream cheese and milk; stir over  low heat until cream cheese is melted. Stir in remaining ingredients; heat  thoroughly.
