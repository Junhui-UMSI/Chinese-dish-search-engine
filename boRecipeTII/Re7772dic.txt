Porter Cake (Irish)
Flour, Bottle Guinness or stout, Brown sugar, Butter, Mixed peel, Rind of one lemon, Eggs, Bicarbonate of soda, Raisins, Mixed spice (nutmeg&cinnamon, Currants, 
Sift the flour and cut in the butter.  Add the fruit, spice, peel, etc.  Beat eggs.  Heat Guinness or other stout and pour it over the bicarbonate  of soda.  Pour stout and eggs over dry ingredients.  Mix well and beat for  15 minutes.  Bake in a well-greased pan in a moderate oven, 350F, for 2  1/2 - 3 hours.  Keep for a week before cutting.
