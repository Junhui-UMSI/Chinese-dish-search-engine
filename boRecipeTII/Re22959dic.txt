Armadillo Eggs
Chedder cheese to cover tops, (lg) whole mild(or to taste), Mild breakfast sausage, Cream cheese, 
1. Cut peppers in half and de-seed. 2. Put on a cookie sheet cut side up.  3. Cook sausage, crumbly. 4. Drain the fat, and mix the sausage with the  cream cheese* 5. Spoon this mixture onto the jalopeno halves, and top with  a little cheese. 6. Bake in a pre-heated 350 degree oven until cheese has  melted.
