Vegetable Ham Hash
potatoes, Package frozen mixed veggies, Diced frozen hasb browned, Vegetable oil, Onion, Diced ham, 
Heat oil; brown ham and onion in heavy skillet.  Add 1/2 cup boiling water  and vegetables; cover. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Simmer until  vegetables are just tender, about 6 to 10 minutes.
