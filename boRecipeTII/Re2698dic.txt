Peanut Butter Banana Breakfast Shake
Peanut butter, Ground cinnamon, Sweet cocoa powder, Vanilla extract, Fat free milk, banana slices, 
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth and creamy.  Pour into a tall glass and garnish with a sprinkle of cocoa powder, if  desired. Makes 1 serving.    Posted to brand-name-recipes by "Peggy L. Makolondra"  <pmakolon@mail.wiscnet.net> on Apr 03, 1998
