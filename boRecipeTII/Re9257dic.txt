Pudding Drops
Butter, Sugar, Evaporated milk, Unsalted peanuts, (3-oz) chocolate pudding, 
Combine all ingredients except peanuts in a saucepan. Bring to a rolling  boil. Lower heat and boil for 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from  heat and add peanuts. Beat candy with a wooden spoon until it starts to  thicken. Drop by tablespoons onto waxed paper. Makes 34.
