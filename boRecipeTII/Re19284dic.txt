Vegetable Soup #1
Onion; quartered, Sliced carrots, Cubed potatoes, (8-oz) tomato sauce, Small elbow macaroni, (8-oz) green beans, (12-oz) whole kernel corn, (8-oz) lima beans, Salt to taste, (14.5-oz) tomatoes w/juice, Stew meat, Soup bone, Water, Celery, (8-oz) english peas, 
Add half the meat and soup bone to cold water. Brown remaining meat in a  little hot oil. Add browned meat, onion and salt to cold water. Cook slowly  for 2 hours. Add vegetables, undrained, and continue cooking for 2 more  hours.    MRS E.J.
