Turkey Divan
Cream, Flour, Lengthwise, Mixed vegetable seasoning, Parmesan cheese, grated, Diced turkey, Butter, Spears broccoli, sliced, 
In a sauce pan, melt butter and gradually stir in flour to make a smooth  paste.  Slowly add cream and whisk until smooth. Place turkey in baking  dish, alternating with layers of broccoli spears. Pour sauce over all and  sprinkle on vegetable seasoning. Top with Parmesan cheese. Bake at 350  degrees for 20-30 minutes; cheese will be bubbling and slightly browned.
