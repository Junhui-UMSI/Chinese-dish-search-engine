Peanut Butter Pine Cones
Pine Cones, Wild bird seed mix, Any number, Jar smooth peanut butter, 
Coat each cone with peanut butter.  Roll in bird seed.    This is great nutrition for birds in the winter.  The trick is keeping them  out of the reach of squirrels.  Remember, bread fills up birds and will  keep them from eating food with nutrients and can lead to death for them.
