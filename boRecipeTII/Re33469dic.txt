Basil Garlic Vinegar
Pint jar; sterilized, Cloves garlic; peeled; split, Vinegar, Fresh basil leaves; coarsely, LORI NORMAN RFHH52E, 
Place 1/2 cup coarsely chopped fresh basil leaves and 2 cloves split peeled  garlic in a sterilized pint jar. Heat wine or distilled WHITE vinegar to  just below the boiling point. Fill jar with vinegar, and cap tightly. Allow  to stand 3 to 4 weeks. Strain vinegar, discarding basil and garlic. Pour  vinegar into a clean sterilized jar, adding a new sprig of fresh basil, if  desired. Seal tightly.  Use in dressings for rice, pasta, antipasto salads  or in flavored mayonnaise.
