Bean and Eggplant Stew
Cooking liquid from beans, olive oil, Dried asil, Garlic, Eggplant; diced, Spanish onion; sliced, Dried Oregano, Great Northern beans, dried, Bay Leaves, Wine vinegar, Sun-dried tomato halves;, 
Cook beans in 3 1/2 cups of water for 1 1/2 to 2 hours on the stovetop or  10 to 15 minutes in a pressure cooker. Reserve cooking liquid.     Heat oil ina large soup kettle over medium heat. Saute bay leaves, basil,  oregano and onion until onion is soft and translucent. Add garlic and  eggplant and saute for 5 minutes.     Add remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer, stirring ocasionally, until  eggplant is tender.     Serve immediately.     Per serving: 272 cal; 13 g prot; 305 mg sod; 46 g carb; 8 g fat; 0 mg  chol; 140 mg calcium     Source: Vegetarian Gourmet, Winter 93/MM by DEEANNE
