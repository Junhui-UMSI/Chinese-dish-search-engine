Barbie Pretty Pink Cake
Cake flour, Red sugar crystals, Baking Powder, Salt, Vegetable oil, Vanilla extract, milk, 
Stir together cake flour, baking powder, salt, red sugar, vanilla, oil and  milk until the batter is smooth and pink.  Pour 3 TBS. of batter into  greased and floured cake pan.  Bake 15 mins. Repeat for second layer. Makes        2    layers.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #008 by valerie@nbnet.nb.ca (valerie) on Jan  8, 1998
