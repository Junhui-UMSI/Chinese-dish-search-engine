Almond Crescents
Flour, Powdered sugar, Oats; uncooked, Butter, Almonds; finely chopped, Salt, Powdered sugar; sifted, Almond extract, 
Date: Sun, 17 Mar 1996 23:27:01 -0500    From: kmeade@ids2.idsonline.com (The Meades)    Heat oven to 325 degress F. Beat butter and almond extract till fluffy;  gradually beat in sugar. Add combined flour and salt; mix well. Stir in  oats and almonds. Shape to form crescents. Place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 15-18 minutes or until light golden brown. Sift powdered sugar over  warm crescents.    MC-RECIPE@MASTERCOOK.COM    MASTERCOOK RECIPES LIST SERVER    From the MasterCook recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
