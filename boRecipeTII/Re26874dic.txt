Baked Beans - 1000
Bacon, Bottled Bar-B-Que sauce, Onions, Brown sugar, Tart (Granny Smith) apples, (16-oz) pork & beans, Golden rasins, 
Date: Wed, 20 Mar 1996 17:04:13 -0600    From: pickell@cyberspc.mb.ca (S.Pickell)    Drain pork and beans and place into oven-proof bowl. Chop onion and apples  and add to beans. Also add Bar-B-Que sauce, brown sugar and rasins. Mix  together and lay bacon strips on top. Place in covered grill and bake at  350 degrees for 1 to 1-1/2 hours.
