Trenette with Pesto, Potatoes and Beans
New potatoes, boiled and, Fresh pesto, Young green beans, blanched,, Package Trenette pasta, 
Bring 6 quarts of water to a boil and add 2 tablespoons salt.    Place pesto, potatoes and beans in a 12 inch to 14 inch saute pan and set  aside. Cook pasta according to package instructions until "al dente" and  drain. Add pasta to pan, place over medium heat and toss until warm  through. Pour into warmed serving dish and serve.    Yield: 4 servings    Recipe by: Molto Mario    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #506 by Sue <suechef@sover.net> on Mar 08,  1997.
