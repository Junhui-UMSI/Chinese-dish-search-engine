Rathskeller Pork
Light cream, condensed cream of Asparagus soup, cabbage, Seasoned salt, Potatoes, Water, Pork steaks, Green onions, Salad oil, Seasoned pepper, 
In large skillet or slow-cooking pot with browning unit, brown chops in oil  on both sides.  Drain off excess fat.  Combine undiluted soup with onions,  seasoned salt, pepper and water.  Arrange alternate layers of meat,  potatoes and cabbagte in slow-cooking pot.  Pour soup mixture over. Cover  and cook 4 to 6 hours on low.  Turn to high.  Add cream; cover and cook on  high for 20 to 30 minutes.
