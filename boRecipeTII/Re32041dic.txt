Barbecued Beef Jerky
Brown sugar, Onion powder, Catsup, Red wine vinegar, Salt, Hot pepper sauce, Cracked pepper, Dry mustard, Lean beef, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Cut beef into strips 1/2 inch thick. Combine all marinade ingredients in a  large glass baking dish. Add strips of beef, cover and refrigerate  overnight. Drain beef slices. Dry in an electric dehydrator at 145 until  pliable. Package in home canning jars, food-grade plastic bags or  seal-by-heat food storage bags.
