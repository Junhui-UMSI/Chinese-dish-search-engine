Thai Apple Slaw
Sugar, Shredded Green Cabbage, Rice vinegar, Low-Sodium Soy Sauce, Sesame Oil; Optional, Chopped Peanuts, Thai Sesame Dressing, Toasted Sesame Seeds; * See, Gala Apples; Cored, Cut Into, Vegetable oil, Garnish, 
To make Thai Sesame Dressing, combine ingredients in jar with tight-fitting  lid. Seal jar and shake well. Makes about 1/2 cup.    In large bowl, combine apples and cabbage. Add Thai Sesame Dressing and  toss gently. Chill at least 1 hour to blend flavors or up to 12 hours.    Garnish salad with sesame seeds and chopped peanuts.    NOTES : To toast sesame seeds, place in a small, heavy, ungreased skillet  over medium-high heat.  Toast until you hear the seeds begin to pop,  shaking the pan to keep the seeds from scorching.
