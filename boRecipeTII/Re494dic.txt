Lemon Pudding Cake
Eggs, Salt, Butter, Sugar, Lemon juice, Flour, milk, 
Oven 325F.    Cream butter, gradually add sugar. Cream together until light and fluffy.  Add egg yolks, beat well. Add flour, lemon juice, rind, salt and mix well.  Stir in milk , Beat egg whites until stiff and fold into the mixture. Pour  in a loaf dish 9" x 5". Set in a pan of hot water and bake in a 325F oven  for 40 minutes. Turn oven to 350F and bake until brown (about 10 Minutes)    Source: From the River Road Cookbook    >From the kitchen of D.
