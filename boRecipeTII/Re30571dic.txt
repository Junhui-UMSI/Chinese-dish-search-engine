Tagliatelle Alfredo
Dried tagliatelle, Parmesan cbeese; grated, Creamy milk, Butter, Black Pepper, Parmesan cbeese; grated, 
Cook the dried tagliatelle or noodles in a large pan of boiling salted  water for about 6 minutes (but cook fresh for 2-3 minutes), until it is  just tender but still firm, al dente.    Meanwhile, mix the butter with the cheese and milk in a heated serving dish  and season to taste with salt and pepper. Drain the noodles thoroughly and  add to the cheese mixture. Toss well until the noodles are coated. Serve  immediately with a little extra grated cheese.    Source: Feast of Floyd
