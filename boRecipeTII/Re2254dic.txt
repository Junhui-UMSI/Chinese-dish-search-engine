Baked Rigatoni
Frozen vegetable burger, Jar pasta sauce, Dried basil, Fennel seeds, Frozen broccoli flowerets, Canned ready-cut diced, Rigatoni, Mozzarella cheese; part skim, 
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cook pasta according to package directions,  drain.    2. Combine pasta, pasta sauce, tomatoes, broccoli, burger crumbles, basil,  fennel seeds and 4 oz. of the mozzarella in a 9x13 baking dish. Top with  remaining 2 oz. mozzarella. Bake until heated through, about 20 minutes.    Posted to EAT-LF Digest by Kathy Rogers <kathyrogers@earthlink.net> on Dec  05, 1998,
