Sweet Cream Base #3- Ben and Jerrys-Adapted
No-Fat Milk, Light cream, Sweetened condensed milk, Low-Fat\No-Fat Adapted, Salt, COLD, Fat Free Condensed Skimmed, Vanilla, 
This recipe makes a less creamy, less rich ice cream. Ben likes the  slightly cooked flavor of the sweetened condensed milk. Whisk the light  cream and the sweetened condensed milk together in a mixing bowl until  blended. Makes 1 quart.
