Strawberry Dessert Cake
Sugar, Flour, milk, butter, Egg, Shortening, Baking Powder, Sugar, Fresh strawberries, Salt, Flour, 
Sprinkle 1/2 cup sugar over strawberries, let stand. Sift 2 cups flour,  baking powder, 6 Tablespoons sugar & salt. Cut in shortening with pastry  blender or 2 knives until particles are size of peas. Combine egg & milk,  stir into flour mixture. Blend. Spread dough in well-greased pan. Spoon  berries & juice over top of dough. Blend together butter, 1/4 cup sugar & 3  Tablespoons flour. Drop mixture by spoonfuls over strawberries. Bake 35-40  minutes at 400. Serve warm with cream.    MRS WILLIAM (CHERYL) MARTIN    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
