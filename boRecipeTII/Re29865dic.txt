Tentsuyu Sauce
Mirin OR, MSG; (optional), Horseradish, Ginger; grated, to taste, Lemon juice; optional, Light broth, Soy sauce, Sweet sherry OR, Honey, Daikon radish; 1" piece, 
Mix all ingredients except the horseradish, ginger and lemon juice. Bring  to a boil, and remove from heat. Grate the radish. Hydrate the horseradish  if you are using the dried product.    For serving, put warm sauce into individual bowls. Place a heap of the  radish topped with horseradish on each plate of fried food. Each person  should mix as much of this with his sauce as suits his taste. Add the lemon  juice if desired.  This sauce used mainly with Tempura.        Source:  Practical Japanese Cooking
