1981 Winning Recipe Chili
Brown sugar, Oregano, pinch, Chili powder, Beef bouillon cube, salt and pepper, Budweiser beer, Large onion, chopped fine, Garlic, Ground cumin, Lean pork, ground, Wesson oil, Tomato sauce, Diced green chilies, Water, Beef brisket, 1" cubes, Dry mustard, 
In a large kettle or Dutch oven, brown the Beef, Pork and Onions in hot  Wesson Oil.  Add Salt and Pepper to taste. Add remaining ingredients. Stir  well.   Cover and simmer 3 to 4 hours, until meat is tender and Chili is  thick and bubbly.  Stir occasionally.
