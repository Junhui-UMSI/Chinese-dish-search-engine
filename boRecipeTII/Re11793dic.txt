Ravioli Casserole - Southern Living
Zucchini, Onion, olive oil, Tomato-and-basil pasta sauce, Frozen cheese ravioli, Mozzarella, Parmesan cheese, 
Oven temp: 375F / 190C / Gas Mark 5  1. Preheat oven.  Lightly grease a 9" square baking dish.  2. Saute zucchini and onion in hot oil in a skillet until tender.   3. Spoon one-third of pasta sauce into baking dish.  Layer with half each of ravioli, zucchini mixture, and mozzarella; top with one-third of sauce. Repeat layers, ending with sauce. Sprinkle with  Parmesan cheese.      4. Bake for 10 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
