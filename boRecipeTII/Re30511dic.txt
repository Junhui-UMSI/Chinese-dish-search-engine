Taco Soup
Ground beef, Mushroom, Tomatoes, Shredded cheese, Onion, Avocado, Kidney beans, Corn, Tomato sauce, Corn chips, Sour cream, Taco seasoning, 
In keetle, lightly brown beef and onion.  Stir in tomatoes, beans, corn,  tomato sauce, mushrooms, and taco seasoning. Bring to boil and simmer 15 to       20    minutes.     To serve, garnish with choice of avocado, cheese, and sour cream.
