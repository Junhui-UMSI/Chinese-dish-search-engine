Peters Sunday Am Coffee
TOPPING, Cinnamon, Pot extra strong coffee, Almond syrup (see recipe), Sweetened condensed milk, Nutmeg, Whipping cream, 
Make pot of extra strong coffee. Pour desired amount into ice filled  pitcher. Add desired amounts sweetened condensed milk, almond syrup, and  cinnamon. Serve in individual glasses topped with whipping cream and  nutmeg.
