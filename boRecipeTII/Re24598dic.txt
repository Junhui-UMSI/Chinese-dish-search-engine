Asparagus with Mustard Sauce
Margarine, Flour, Skim milk, Dijon mustard, Trimmed asparagus, Wht pepper, Chicken broth, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
Melt margarine in med. saucepan. Stir in flour, cook 1 min. Add broth and  milk, stirring constantly w/ whisk. Bring to boil, lower heat and simmer  for 5 mins. Remove from heat and whisk in mustard, lemon juice and white  pepper.
