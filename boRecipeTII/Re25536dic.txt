Avocado Bisque
Green onion tops -- chopped, BAGGETT, Cream of chicken soup --, Salt, Pepper and Cay, Avocado, milk, 
Reserve half of 1 avocado for garnish.  Mix rest of avocados, soup, onions,  milk, and seasonings in a blender or food processor until smooth.  Refrigerate overnight or at least 2 hours before serving. Garnish with  chopped green onion and sliced avocado. This soup does NOT freeze.
