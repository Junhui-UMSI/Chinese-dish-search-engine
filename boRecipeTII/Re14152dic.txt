White Frosting
See below, 
In a saucepan, combine 1 cup sugar, 1/3 cup water, 1/4 tsp. cream of  tartar, & dash salt. Bring mixture to boiling, stirring till the sugar  dissolves. In mixing bowl place 2 egg whites; very slowly pour the hot  sugar syrup over, beating constantly with electric mixer till stiff  peaks form, about 7 minutes.  Beat in 1 tsp. vanilla.
