Pecan Cheese Salad
Mini marshmallows, Sour cream, Sugar, Water; cold, Celery; sliced thin, Pineapple in chunks; drained, Pecans, Knox gelatin, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Whipped cream, Cottage cheese, Lemon, Mayonnaise, 
Dissolve Knox gelatin in cold water. Add 1/2 cup pineapple juice which has  been heated. Mix all remaining ingredients together. Add gelatin mixture.  Mix well. Pour into container of choice. Chill until set.    Mom Anderson.
