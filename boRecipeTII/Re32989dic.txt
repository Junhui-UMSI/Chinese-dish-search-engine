Basic British Sausage
Nutmeg; ground cloves, Lean pork; such as shoulder, Pork fat, Salt and pepper, Sausage skins, Mace; thyme, Fresh breadcrumbs, Egg yolks, 
Mince the lean pork and pork fat finely.  Season generously with salt,  pepper, and at least a pinch each of nutmeg, cloves, mace and thyme. Add  the breadcrumbs and egg yolks and mix well. Refrigerate the mixture to make  it easier to handle, then stuff into the skins and twist to secure the  ends. If sausage skins are not available, coat with egg and dry  breadcrumbs.
