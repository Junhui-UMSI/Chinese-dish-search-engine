Zama De Pasole Verde (Green String Bean Soup)
Parsley, and dill,chop fine, Spring chicken, cut up, Onion, small, minced, Water, Cayenne pepper, Lard, String beans, cut, Flour, Onion,large,chop & saute, Garlic, 
Place lard, onion and flour in a pot, saute, add red pepper, water,  chicken and cook. Cook cut up green beans, garlic, and onion in 4 ounces  water separately for a half hour, then add to chicken pot and cook for 45  min or more. Add parsley and dill last.
