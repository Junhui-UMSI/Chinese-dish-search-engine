Swiss Chocolate Hazelnut Cake
Eggs, Bittersweet chocolate, ?, Flour, Sugar, Margerine, Chocolate pudding, Baking Powder, 
Beat the margerine and the sugar. Add the eggs one at a time. Add the  melted chocolate, and then the remaining ingredients. Pour into 2 loaf pans  (It wont pour exactly, as it will be quite thick). Bake at 350 degrees for  about 55 minutes. Test for doneness.  Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V96 #83    Date: Sun, 24 Nov 1996 09:29:03 GT+0300    From: "Ellen Spitzer" <ellens@nice.com>
