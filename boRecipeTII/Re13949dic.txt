Wine-Cherry-Nut Salad
Pecans, chopped, Pitted black cherries, Water, boiling, Crushed pineapple, Port wine, Black cherry gelatin, 
Drain both fruits and reserve juices. Add enough juice from both fruits to  make 2-1/2 cups of liquid. Add wine and the 2-1/2 cups of fruit juice to  gelatin. Stir well until gelatin is dissolved. Chill until slightly  thickened. Fold in pineapple, cherries and pecans. Chill until firm. Serves  8 to 10.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #512 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Mar 12, 1997
