Turkey Tortilla Stew
Low-fat tortilla chip crumbs, Ground turkey, skinless, Black pepper, olive oil, Dark red kidney beans, Tomato soup, condensed, Bell peppers, Frozen corn kernels, garlic, Water, Drained, tomatoes, Drained, Pinto beans, canned, Onions, 
In a skilllet heat oil over medium heat. Add turkey, onions, bell peppers,  and garlic. Cook until turkey is no longer pink and vegetables are tender.  Stir in water, crushed tomatoes, pinto beans, kidney beans, tomato soup,  corn  kernels, and black pepper. Heat until mixture is bubbly. Reduce heat to low  and simmer 30 minutes.
