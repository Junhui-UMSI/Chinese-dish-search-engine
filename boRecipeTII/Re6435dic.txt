Plum Sauce #3
Pimento, Plums, Vinegar, Sugar, Applesauce, Apricots, 
1. Peel and pit fresh plums and apricots; chop pimiento. Combine in a  saucepan with sugar, vinegar and applesauce.     2. Bring slowly to a boil, stirring; then simmer, covered, 1 hour.     3. Pour into jars while still warm and cap tightly. Store at least 1 month  in a cool, dark place before using.    NOTE: If the mixture is too thick, thin it slightly with water before  serving. Sweeten, if desired, with additional sugar.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
