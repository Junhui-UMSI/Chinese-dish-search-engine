Strawberry Marlow
Cream; whipping  - whipped, Marshmallows, Strawberries; ripe, Salt, 
Crush washed berries. Heat marshmallows and 2 T. juice from berries in top  of double boiler. Stire mixture with folding motion, heating only until  marshmallows are partially melted. Remove from firs and continue folding  until mixture is smooth. Cool, add berries and salt. Whip cream and fold  in. Place in freezing tray and freeze without stirring.    Source: Mrs. Ralph Orb, Newark Grange, Licking County, OH
