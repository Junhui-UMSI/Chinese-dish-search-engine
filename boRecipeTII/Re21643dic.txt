Apple Sorbet
Granny Smith Apples; peeled, Applejack, Nutmeg, Water, Cinnamon, Fresh Lemon Juice, Sugar, 
Combine sugar and water in saucepan over med. high heat and stir til sugar  is dissolved. Just before syrup comes to boil, remove from heat. Cool and  chill. Add syrup to remaining ingred. and blend well. Finish in either ice  cream maker or freezer.
