Avocado Torey "Le Ruths" Restaurant New Orleans La
Dried Oregano, Sprig fresh thyme (optional), Romaine lettuce leaves, olive oil, White pepper, Salt, Vinegar, Red wine, Avocados, Clove garlic - minced, 
Cut the avocados in half and discard the pit.  Scoop out the avocado meat  with an ice-cream dipper, and shape into little balls.  Place them on two  plates lined with lettuce leaves.  In a small bowl, whisk together the rest  of the ingredients, pour over the avocados, and serve.
