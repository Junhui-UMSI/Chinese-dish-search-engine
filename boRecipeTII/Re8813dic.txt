Praline Coffee
Hot brewed coffee, Sweetened whipped cream, Half and half, Light brown sugar, Praline liquer, 
Cook first 4 ingredients in a large saucepan over medium heat, stirring  constantly, until thoroughly heated (do not boil). Stir in liqueur; serve  with sweetgened whipped cream    Recipe by: : Southern Living, January, 1997, p. 104    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1004 by Gr8seeksM8 <Gr8seeksM8@aol.com> on  Jan 11, 1998
