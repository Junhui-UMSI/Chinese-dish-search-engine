9-Point Flan
Evaporated milk, Rum; or more to taste, Egg yolks; or up to:, -Egg yolks total, Sugar, 
In heavy skillet, slowly heat 3/4 cup sugar until melted and  caramel-colored. Pour into 5-cup baking dish and carefully swirl to line  dish with caramel. Set aside. Beat egg yolks with 1/2 cup sugar. Add  evaporated milk and rum to taste and beat well. Turn custard into prepared  baking dish. Place dish in pan of hot water and bake at 350F about 1 hour,  or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cool thoroughly at room  temperature. Unmold on serving plate.
