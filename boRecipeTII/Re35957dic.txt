Stuffed Peppers #1
Tomatoes, salt and pepper, Shredded cheese, Green peppers, Onions, Rice, Worcestershire, Ground beef, 
Brown beef and onion. Add tomatoes, rice, Worcestershire, salt, pepper and  1/2 cup cheese to meat. Seed peppers and boil in lightly salted water 5  minutes. Drain and stuff with meat mixture. Place in casserole. Put any  extra meat around the peppers. Sprinkle rest of cheese on top and bake at      350    for 25 minutes.
