Prize Winning Quick Brown Bread
Sour or buttermilk, Baking soda, Graham flour, Egg, Brown sugar, Salt, Shortening; melted, Flour; white, Baking Powder, Syrup; maple or molasses, 
Beat first four ingredients, add soda dissolved in milk. Add flour and  baking powder sifted together, then shortening. Bake in 5 x 10 inch  greased pan for 1 hour in oven 375 F.    Source: Dela Carver, Hamden Grangem Geauga County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.  Compiled by members of Ohio State Grange Home Economics Committee.
