Annes Quick Corn Light Bread
White Self-rising Corn Meal, White Self-rising flour, Baking soda, Sugar, Oil or melted shortening, Buttermilk, 
Heat oven to 375, Grease and dust lightly a Loaf Pan, with flour. Combine  all dry ingredients in mixing bowl, stir in melted shortening or oil and  Buttermilk. Pour mixture in prepared pan and let set for 15 minutes. Bake  45 minutes. Cover pan with tea towel and let set. Cool before slicing.
