Pineapple-Raspberry Cream Punch
Pineapple juice, Ginger ale, Vanilla ice cream, Raspberry sherbert, 
Here are some recipes for non-alcoholic party punches. All are for 12 four  ounce servings. All are from _The New Doubleday Cookbook_ by Jean Anderson  & Elaine Hanna.    Pour pineapple juice and ginger ale over ice cream and sherbert. Stir until  melted and blended.    Recipe by: Jean Anderson & Elaine Hanna Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #519  by "Master Harper Gaellon" <gaellon@inch.com> on Mar 16, 1997
