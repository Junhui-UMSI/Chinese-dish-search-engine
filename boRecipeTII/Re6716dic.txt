Polish Beets
Horseradish, Sugar, Salt, Parsley, Yogurt, Butter, Pepper, Flour, Onion, beets, 
Saute the onion in the butter for three to five minutes. Stir in the flour.  Add the yogurt and heat to just below the boiling point. Add the beets,  horseradish and sugar, and heat thoroughly. Season with salt and pepper to  taste and sprinkle with the parsley    NOTES : Made this for the supper with Father Mike. Everybody seemed to like  it. Especially Bill. Must make it again.
