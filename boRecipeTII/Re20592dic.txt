Apple Cinnamon Pancakes
Applesauce, Baking soda, Buttermilk, Vegetable oil, Salt, Cinnamon, Baking Powder, APPLE & CINNAMON, Sugar, Eggs, All-purpose flour, 
Heat griddle to 375? F. In large mixing bowl, combine flour, cereal, sugar,  baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon; mix well. Add remaining  ingredients, stir until moistened and batter is smooth.    For each pancake, pour 1/4 cup batter onto hot griddle. Bake about 1  minute; turn and continue baking about 1 minute more.    YIELD: 16 4-inch pancakes    * One and one-half tablespoons lemon juice or vinegar plus milk to equal 1  1/2 cups can be substituted for buttermilk.
