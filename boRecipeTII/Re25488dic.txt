Avocado Salad Souffle
Mayonnaise, (3-oz) lime gelatin, Cream; whipped, Salt, Pecans, Chopped avacado, (20-oz) crushed pineapple;, Hot water, Lemon juice, 
Dissolve gelatin in hot water; let cool. Add lemon juice, pineapple juice,  mayonnaise and salt. Blend well in mixer and allow to chill until  thickened. Pour into ice trays in freezer for a few minutes. Turn into  mixing bowl; beat until fluffy. Fold in cream, avacado, nuts and pineapple.  Pour into molds. Serves 12-16.
