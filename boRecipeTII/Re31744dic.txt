Sybil Carters Chocolate-Peanut Butter Frosting
Water,hot, Peanut butter, Vanilla, Cocoa, Salt, 
Combine cocoa and water. Add peanut butter, vanilla, and salt, beating  until smooth. Gradually stir in sugar and beat until smooth again. If  needed, add a very little hot water until frosting is of spreading  consistency.
