Wassail
Apple cider; or juice, Orange pineapple juice, Lemon juice, Cloves, Sugar, Sticks cinnamon, 
Combine all ingredients. Simmer 25 minutes and let sit 15 minutes. Remove  cinnamon and cloves. Serve warm.    NOTES : My husband likes rum in it; I prefer bourbon.  Good with no booze  at all.
