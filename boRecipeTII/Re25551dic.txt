Avocado Dip
RIPE AVOCADO (PEELED AND SEE, MEDIUM ONION (FINELY CHOPPED, Thyme, Marjoram, Rosemary, Mayonnaise, Sage, Basil, Sour cream, CRACKERS OR CHIPS, 
In a mixing bowl, mash avocado. Add onion,       mayonnaise, thyme, basil, rosemary, sage,       marjoram, and sour cream. Mix well. serve with       crackers or chips.       Yields 2 cups.
