Key Lime Pie
Lime slices & fresh mint, (8oz.) cream cheese, (14 oz.) sweetened condensed, Container (12 oz.) of frozen, Key limes (squeeze into, Baked 9-in. graham cracker, 
In a large bowl, combine the cream cheese, sweetened condensed milk and  whipped topping. Stir in the lime juice until well blended and mixture is  thick and smooth. Immediately pour lime filling into pie shell. Pie can be  served at once, but for best flavor, chill for about 2 hours. Garnish with  lime slices and mint.
