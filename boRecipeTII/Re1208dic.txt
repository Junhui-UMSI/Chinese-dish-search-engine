Crock Pot Hashbrowns
Hashbrowns, Soup, cream of mushroom, Margarine, Soup, cheddar cheese, Sour cream, 
Put hashbrowns in crock pot. Mix soups and sour cream together. Pour over  hash browns. Cut up margarine and stir into hashbrowns. Cook on low for 4  to 6 hours, stirring occasionally.
