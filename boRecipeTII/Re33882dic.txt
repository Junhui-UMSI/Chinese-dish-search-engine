Sweet and Sour Tofu
Water, SEASONINGS, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Pepper, Green bell pepper, Garlic cloves, crushed, Green onions, chopped, Tomato paste, Cornstarch dissolved in, Soy sauce, Dried shiitake, soaked, Lemon juice, Tomatoes, cut into wedges, Tofu, Chili sauce, see recipe, GARNISH, Japanese cucumbers, peeled, 
Trim the shiitakes & set aside.  Press the tofu for 30 minutes & cube.  Quarter the cucumbers lengthwise & cut into 1 1/2" lengths. Chop the bell  pepper. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu & carefully  stir- fry until the colour changes. Remove the tofu & add the garlic.  Stir-fry until the colour changes. Add all the vegetables to the wok &  stir-fry for 2 minutes.  Add all the seasonings & continue to stir-fry for  3 to 5 minutes.  Serve garnished with green onions.
