Beef Stew #1
Worcestershire Sauce, Boiling water, Celery, Paprika, Lemon juice, Salt, Tomato juice, potatoes, Onion, Pepper, Sugar, carrots, Beef stew meat, 
Brown stew meat in 1 tablespoon oil. Add water, onion, lemon juice, salt,  Worcestershire sauce, pepper, paprika, sugar & tomato juice. Simmer 2  hours. Add carrots, potatoes & celery. Cook until tender. Serves 6.    JUDY HASTINGS    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
