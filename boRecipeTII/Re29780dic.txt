Tempting Tuna Bake
Salt, (7 oz.) tuna; drained and, Butter or margarine, Shredded Swiss cheese, Green Onions, Celery, milk, Eggs; slightly beaten, White bread, 
Alternate layers of bread and cheese in well buttered 9 inch square baking  dish. Begin with bread and end with cheese. Sprinkle tuna over all. In  small saucepan, cook onion and celery in butter until vegetables are  tender. Remove from heat. Blend with milk, eggs and salt. Pour mixture over  tuna in baking dish. Bake at 350 until puffy and lightly browned. Makes 4-6  servings.    Recipe by: Finlandia Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #752 by L979@aol.com on  Aug 21, 1997
