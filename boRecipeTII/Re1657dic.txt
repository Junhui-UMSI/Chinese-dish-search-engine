Carrot Cake
carrots, White wheat flour, Granulated white sugar, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Salt, Eggs, Vegetable oil, pecans, Cream Cheese Icing for, 
Mix the sugar and oil. Add eggs, beating after each one. Add carrots. Sift  the dry ingredients together, and stir them in. Butter a 9x13 pan, pour in  the batter and bake at 325F for 40 minutes. Top with Cream Cheese Frosting  for Carrot Cake.    Recipe by Mangia!
