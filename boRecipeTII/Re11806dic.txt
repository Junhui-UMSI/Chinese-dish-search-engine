Ravioli Salad with Vegetables and Ham
olive oil, Garlic, Red wine vinegar, Dried red pepper flakes, Green Onions, Fresh cheese ravioli; fresh, Red bell pepper, Black olives, marinated mushrooms, fresh rosemary, Sugar, Grated Parmesan cheese, ham, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in large bowl and whisk to blend. Rinse ravioli  under cold water to cool; drain. Add ravioli, ham, bell pepper, mushrooms  and green onions to vinaigrette; toss to coat. Season salad to taste with  salt and pepper. Cover and chill at least 30 minutes. (Can be prepared 3  hours ahead; mix occasionally. Let stand at room temperature       30    minutes before serving.)    Arrange salad on large platter. Garnish with olives, if desired. Serve,  passing Parmesan cheese separately.          4    Servings    Bon App?tit March 1995 Tammy Moore-Worthington: Artesia, New Mexico  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by shade <liveoak@polaris.net> on Apr 5, 1998
