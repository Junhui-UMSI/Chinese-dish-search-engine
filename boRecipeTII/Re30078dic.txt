Balsamic Pork Chops From Lhj
olive oil, Shallots, Salt, Thyme, Butter or margarine, Chicken broth, Balsamic vinegar, Pork chops, Black Pepper, 
1. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat, 2 minutes. Sprinkle pork  chops with salt and pepper. Add to skillet and cook 5 minutes per side.  Transfer to platter and keep warm.    2. Add shallots to pan; cook, stirring, 1 minute. Add broth, vinegar and  thyme; cook, stirring, over high heat 5 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in  butter. Pour over chops.    Total prep time: 20 minutes Degree of Difficulty: Easy    Happy holidays from everyone at Ladies Home Journal!
