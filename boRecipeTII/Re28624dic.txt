Texas Cake
Buttermilk, Eggs, Cocoa, Vanilla, Shortening, S-r flour, Water, Sugar, Butter, 
From:    Tina Rogers <CAJUNT@UGA.CC.UGA.EDU>    Date:    Sat, 27 Jul 1996 08:47:38 EDT  Mix flour & sugar together in a large bowl; set aside.  Melt butter, cocoa,  water, & shortening in pot and bring to a boil.  Pour over flour & sugar;  mix well then add buttermilk, eggs, & vanilla. Pour into a greased 13x9 pan  and bake at 400 for 20 minutes. About 5 minutes before cake is done  baking, mix frosting and frost cake when it comes out of the oven.
