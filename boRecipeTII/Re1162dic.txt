Cream of Zucchini Soup
Water, Zucchini; chunked, Onion; chunked, Garlic, Tamari or soy sauce, Oats, (heaping) dill weed; dried, White pepper, 
Bring water, zucchini, onion, garlic, tamari sauce and oats to a boil.  Reduce heat. Add dill weed and pepper, cover and simmer 8-10 minutes.    Blend vegetables and broth in three batches in a blender until smooth and  creamy. (This is a beautiful soup!) Serve immediately.    Suggestions: Serve with garlic bread for dipping or with croutons. Garnish  with fresh dill if desired.
