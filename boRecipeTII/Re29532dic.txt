Tart Cherry Soup
Medoc, Sugar, (16-oz) tart cherries; with, Stick cinnamon; 3-inches, Medoc (red) wine, Heavy cream, 
Bring cherries, cinnamon, 1-1/3 cups of wine and sugar to boil.  Reduce  heat and simmer, covered, for 10 minutes. Remove cinnamon. Cool a bit and  puree. Stir in cream. Add additional 1/4 cup wine. Chill at least several  hours before serving. Sprinkle a little ground cinnamon on each portion  when serving.    TIME DOES NOT INCLUDE CHILLING    BEFORE SERVING.
