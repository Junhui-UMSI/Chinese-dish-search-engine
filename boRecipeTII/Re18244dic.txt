Venison Sausage
Nutmeg, Poultry seasoning, Water, Bacon, Sage, Salt, Pepper, Allspice, Venison, 
1)      Combine all of the ingredients, mixing well. 2) Shape into patties  and fry in a skillet `til brown on both sides. (sausage will be slightly  pink on the inside) *or* It may also be stuffed in casings and boiled...    Source: Randy L.
