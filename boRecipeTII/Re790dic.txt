Hash Browns
Info, 
Grate a boiled potato. Put a little margarine (today Id use butter) in a  frying pan and heat it Sprinkle onion salt and black pepper on the grated  potato. Press the grated potato down flat and put in the pan Fry until  crisp on the bottom, turn and fry until crisp on the other side.
