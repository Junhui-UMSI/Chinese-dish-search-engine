Veggie or Casserole Topper
Dry bread, cracker or, Bacon, diced, Garlic clove, finely chopped, Black Pepper, Italian seasonings, 
Place bacon in a 2-cup glass measure. Cover with paper towel. Stirring  midway through cooking, microwave on high 3 1/2 to 4 1/2 minutes, or  until bacon is crisp. Remove bacon and reserve.    Discard all nut 1 tablespoon bacon drippings. Add garlic and  microwave on high 1 minute. Add bread crumbs, seasonings, pepper and  reserved bacon; mix thoroughly. Sprinkle over cooked vegetables such  as green beans, Brussels sprouts or broccoli. Or top a casserole  during last 3-4 minutes of microwaving with mixture. Makes 2/3 cup.
