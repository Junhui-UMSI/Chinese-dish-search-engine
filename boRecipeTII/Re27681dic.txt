The Fryes Favorite Squash Casserole
Shredded cheddar (I use, Salt, Pepper, milk, Butter or margerine, Cracker crumbs (saltines), Onion, Beaten eggs, Chopped yellow squash, 
From:    "Sharon H. Frye" <shfrye@PEN.K12.VA.US>    Date:    Thu, 11 Jul 1996 18:39:47 EDT  Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Combine squash, onion, and butter in a  skillet. Saute until squash is tender, then coarsely mash with a fork. Add  Remaining ingredients. Pour into a greased casserole dish. Bake at 375  degrees until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Let sit 5  minutes before serving.    EAT-L Digest 10 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
