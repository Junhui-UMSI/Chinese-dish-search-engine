Almond Toffee Popcorn
Popcorn; popped, Water, Butter, Vanilla, Almonds; chop; toasted, Sugar, White corn syrup, 
In heavy saucepan, combine sugar, butter, corn syrup, water and almonds.  Cook over a moderate heat to 280~ on a candy thermometer. Add the vanilla.  Stir well and pour over the popped corn.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 652 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 31, 1998
