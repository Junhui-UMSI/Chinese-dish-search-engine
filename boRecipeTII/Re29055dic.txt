Baked Potato Crisp
Chopped fresh parsley;, Cranola oil or cooking spray, Baking potatoes; about, Chopped fresh chives;, Salt and freshly pepper, 
Source: Willians Sonoma (good Cooking)    Preheat an oven to 400F. Oil 2 nonstick baking sheets with spray or oil.  Cut the potatoes into slices 1/8 inch tick. Place the potatoes in a bowl  and toss with the oil to coat evenly, or coat with spray. Seasson to taste  with soat and pepper. Place on the prepared baking sheets. Bake until crisp  and browned, 20-25 minutes. Transfer to serving dish and toss with the  chives and parsley is desired. Taste and adjust the seasoning. Serve  immediately.
