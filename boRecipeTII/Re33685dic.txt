Sweet Almond Tea #3
Cornstarch, Evaporated milk, Water, Sugar, Water, Almond extract, 
1. Bring water to a boil; then stir in sugar to dissolve. Add evaporated  milk and heat, but do not boil.    2. Meanwhile blend cornstarch and remaining cold water to a paste. Then  stir in to thicken mixture.    3. Stir in almond extract and cook 1 minute more. Then serve hot, in small  bowls. VARIATIONS:    1. For the evaporated milk and water in step 1, substitute 4 to 5 cups  whole milk.    2. In step 3 add, with the almond extract, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract.
