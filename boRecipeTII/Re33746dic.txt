Sweet and Sour Chicken
Butter or cooking oil, Brown sugar, Soy sauce, Pineapple chunks, Sliced onion, Water, Cornstarch, Salt, Sliced green bell pepper, Chicken, boned and cubed, Vinegar, 
Brown chicken in butter in a large skillet. Combine onions and bell peppers  after chicken is near done. Combine other ingredients Cook until clear and  slightly thick, about 2 minutes. Add to chicken and cook about 1 hour or  until chicken is done. Serve over cooked rice.
