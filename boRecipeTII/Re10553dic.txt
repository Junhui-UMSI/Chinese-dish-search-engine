Quick Marinara Sauce
Onions; diced, peeled and pressed, pitted and chopped, Capers, Garlic cloves (or more), Kalamata olives*, EDEN Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Jar EDEN Spaghetti Sauce, 
*(Green or black olives can be substituted if necessary, but wont be  as good)    Heat oil. Saute onions and garlic until translucent. Add spaghetti  sauce. Bring to a simmer. Add capers and olives and heat a few minutes  more. Serve with any EDEN Pasta. A great meal with a salad and your  favorite bread!    Prep Time: 5 minutes  Cooking Time: 10 minutes  Yield: 5 servings    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/eden.zip
