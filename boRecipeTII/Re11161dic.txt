Raisin Custard Pie
Raisins, Butter, Eggs, Molasses, Vanilla, 
Mix together the ingredients then pour the mixture into an unbaked pie  shell and bake it at 400 oF for 10 minutes. Reduce the heat to 350 oF and  bake it 30 minutes longer.    Variations: Walnuts may be substituted for raisins.
