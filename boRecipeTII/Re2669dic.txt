Peanut Butter and Jelly Coffee Cake
Creamy peanut butter, milk, Buttermilk baking mix, Sugar, Egg, Jam or jelly -- (any, Flavor), 
Heat oven to 400?.  Grease round pan, 9 X 1 1/2 inches. Mix baking mix and  sugar in medium bowl.  Cut in peanut butter with a pastry blender or 2  knives.  (Mixture should be crumble.)  Mix in milk and egg.  Beat  Vigorously 30 seconds. Spread batter in pan. Spread top of batter with jam.  Bake 20 o 25 minutes or until light brown.
