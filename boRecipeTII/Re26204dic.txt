Toms Caesar Salad
Cubed French bread, Lemon juice, Mashed anchovy fillets, Egg, Romaine lettuce leaves --, Cloves minced garlic, Worcestershire Sauce, Herbal salt substitute, Washed and torn, Pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, Dry mustard, Olive oil -- plus 2 tsp., (optional), 
1. Toss cubed bread with the 2 teaspoons olive oil. Place cubes on a baking  sheet and bake for 20 minutes at 250 degrees F.    2. Place lettuce in a large salad bowl. In smaller bowl combine the 3/4 cup  oil, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, salt substitute, pepper,  anchovies (if used), cheese, lemon juice, and egg. Toss with lettuce  leaves. Add croutons and serve.
