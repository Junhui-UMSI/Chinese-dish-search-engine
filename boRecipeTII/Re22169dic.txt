Turkey Pot Pie for the 90s
Onion -- chopped, Pepper, Cooked low-salt turkey --, All-purpose flour, Refrigerated 9-inch uncooked, Low-sodium chicken broth, Leaf thyme -- crumbled, Carrots -- thinly sliced, Mushrooms -- sliced, Frozen corn kernels, Breast, cubed, Pie crust (1/2 of a, 
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Cook onion in 2 Tbs. of the chicken broth in  large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat until tender, about 2  minutes. Combine the flour, thyme and pepper in a small bowl. Gradually  stir in the remaining chicken broth until smooth. Stir the flour mixture  into skillet until well blended. Cook and stir over medium heat until the  mixture thickens and boils. Add carrots and mushrooms. Simmer, covered, for  10 minutes. Stir in the corn and turkey. Pour filling into a 9-inch pie  plate.  Cover filling with the piecrust. Pinch down edges around the rim.  Pierce with fork for steam vents. Bake pie in preheated 450 degree oven for  15 to 18 minutes or until the crust is golden brown. Makes 4 servings at  $2.15 each Nutritional information per serving: calories - 418, protein -  23 gm., fat - 16 gm., carbohydrates - 46 gm., cholesterol - 36 mg., sodium  ~ 870 mg.
