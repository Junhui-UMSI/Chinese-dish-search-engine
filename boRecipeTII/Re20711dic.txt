Apple Crunch #2
Stick butter or margarine;, Sugar, Sliced apples, Flour, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Salt, Egg, 
Line pan with apples and melted butter. Beat egg and vanilla together;  combine with dry ingredients. Spread over apples. Bake in preheated 350  degree oven for 45 minutes. Serve warm.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
