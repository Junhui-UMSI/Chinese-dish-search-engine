Sweetpotato Souffle
Raisins, Nutmeg, Butter, Eggs, Pecans, Sugar, Mashed sweetpotatoes, Marshmallows, milk, Salt, 
This is an OLD family favorite recipe. It brings back fond memories for me  since my mom fixed it for our family every holiday season. Scald milk and  add sugar,salt,butter,nutmeg and potatoes; beat until fluffy. Beat egg  yolks and add to potatoes. Add raisins and pecans. Beat egg whites  stiff,fold into potatoes and pour into greased baking dish. Bake in moder-  ate oven (350 degrees.) 50 to 60 minutes or until firm. Top with  marshmallows and brown in oven. Serves 8. Note: You might like to do a  flower of peach slices in the center of your sweetpotato souffle. HAPPY  HOLIDAYS!
