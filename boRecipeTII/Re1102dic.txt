Cottage Cheese and Lime Jello Salad
Lime jello, Hot water, Cottage cheese, Cool whip, Salad dressing, Celery, Onion, 
Whip jello and water until foamy.  Fold in the rest of the ingredients.  Refrigerate until firm.    from Hazel Dietrich shared with you by Peg Dietrich, 1/98    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Nov 22, 98
