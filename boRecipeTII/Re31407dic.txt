Barbecuded Pork and Apple Pizza
All-purpose flour, Semolina flour, Molasses, Yeast; quick-acting, TOPPING, Dijon mustard, Smoked lowfat provolone, Whole wheat flour, BASE, Pork tenderloin; sliced, Barbecue sauce, Tart apples; sliced, olive oil, Warm water, 
1. Preheat oven to 375F 2. Mix ingrediants for base in Bread Maker. Allow  to rise for 10-15min. 3. Punch dough down and into shape for base. Place in  oven and bake until firm. 4. In a bowl, toss pork with mustard, spinkle  with majoram and bake for 15min in oven,    5. Increase oven temperature to 450F. Brush pizza crust with olive oil and  top with barbecue sauce, pork, apples and cheese. Bake for 6-7min or until  cheese is brown.    6.
