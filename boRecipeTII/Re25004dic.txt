Tomato Rice Soup
Chicken Stock (cubes), Short-grain Rice, Onion, Tomato Puree, Crusty Bread, Boiling water, 
Put stock cubes in a pan and dissolve in boiling water. add onion and rice,  and simmer for a further 5 minutes until all the flavours have combined.  Serve with crusty bread.    Source: Emily Murray, Newton Stewart
