Pork and Sauerkraut Goulash
Bay leaf, Water, Onions, Salt, Black pepper, Pork shoulder, Tomatoes, Sweet green peppers, Hungarian paprika, Sauerkraut, Sour cream, Pork Fat, Caraway seeds, 
Directions Sauate pork in a heavy skillet in the fat or oil until lightly  browned. Add onion, green pepper and paprika.  Saute until vegetables are  just limp. Add bay leaf, water or stock, tomatoes, and sauerkraut. Cover  and simmer very slowly for 1 hr, or until meat is tender. Season with salt  and pepper.
