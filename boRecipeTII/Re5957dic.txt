Pistachio Pudding Surprise
(1 pt.) sour cream, (8 1/4 or 20 oz.) crushed, Miniature marshmallows, Flaked coconut, (4-serving size) pistachio, 
Combine sour cream and pudding mix. Add coconut, pineapple and marshmallows  and stir until blended. Spoon into an 8 x 4- or 9 x 5- inch loaf pan and  freeze until firm, at least 3 hours. A jello mold can be used. Dip pan in  warm water and unmold onto serving plate. May be chilled and served just  like pudding also.    Recipe is from a church fundraiser cookbook called _Community Cooks Come  Alive_.
