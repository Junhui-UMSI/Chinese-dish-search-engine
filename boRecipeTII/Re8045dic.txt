Potato and Pea Stew
Ginger root, Asafetida, Turmeric, Fresh or frozen peas, Water, Ghee, Potatoes, cubed, Green chilies, minced, Whole cumin seeds, Fresh coriander, chopped, Coriander, Black mustard seeds, Tomatoes, chopped, Salt, 
Heat ghee till hot.  Add ginger, chilies, cumin seeds & black mustard  seeds.  Fry for a few seconds & add asafetida. A few seconds later add the  tomatoes & fry for 3 minutes.    Add the potatoes, turmeric, coriander & water.  Bring to a boil, reduce  heat, cover & simmer for 15 minutes.    Add the peas, salt & half the chopped coriander. Continue to cook,  partially covered, till the potatoes are soft. Garnish with the rest of the  coriander & serve.    Yamuna Devi, "The Art of Indian Vegetarian Cooking"
