Tomato-And-Onion Pizza
Rosemary, Olive puree, Plum tomatoes, olive oil, Garlic, Dry white wine, Oregano, Pizza Crusts, Onions; sliced, 
PREHEAT OVEN TO 375F. Wash tomatoes and cut off the stem ends. Cut into  1/3-inch lengthwise slices. Heat oil in a non-stick saute pan and saute the  onions over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes. Add the garlic, white  wine and rosemary and reduce heat to medium; cover and cook about 10  minutes or till liquid is mostly absorbed. Stir in the olive puree. Spread  the onion-olive mixture over the pizza. Place the tomato slices on top and  brush with remaining olive oil. Sprinkle with oregano and bake. Makes 2  Pizzas
