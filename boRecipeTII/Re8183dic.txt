salt and pepper, Garlic, Finely chopped parsley, Butter, Idaho potatoes, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In a small sauce pan melt together the butter,  garlic, and parsley. Peel each potato and cut each potato crosswise into  1/4" slices without slicing all the way through the potato. Place potatoes  in baking dish and drizzle with butter mixture. Bake for 1 hour or until  golden, crispy and the potatoes have fanned out. Serve hot.
