Ranch Barbeque Sauce
Worcestershire Sauce, Tabasco sauce, Catsup, Water, Salt, Chili powder, 
Combine all ingredients.  Heat to boiling; simmer 30 minutes. If sauce gets  too thick, add small amount of water.  Use to baste ribs, etc.    Source:  Better Homes and Gardens Barbecue Book Posted to bbq-digest V2 #87    Date: Mon, 07 Oct 1996 07:14:00 -0700    From: Carey Starzinger <cstarz@teleport.com>
