Acapulco Salad
Whole Kernal Corn; Drained, Bottle Green Goddess Salad, Radishes; Thinly Sliced, Black Olives; Sliced, Garlic Salt And Pepper, Shr, Garbanzo Beans; Drained, Head Iceberg Lettuce,
