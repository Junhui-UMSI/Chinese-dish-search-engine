Upstate Minestrone Soup
(1 - lb) Italian pear, Tomatoes, chopped, undrained, Salt, Pepper, Diced onion, Italian sweet sausage, Small amount chopped parsley, Clove garlic, finely minced, Olive OR Vegetable oil, Crumbled basil, Finely shredded cabbage, Undrained, Small zucchini, sliced, 3 beef boullion cubes plus, Sliced carrots, (1 lb) great northern beans,, 10 3/4 oz beef boullion OR, 1 1/2 cups hot water, 
Slice sausage crosswise about 1/2 inch thick; brown in oil in deep saucepan  or Dutch oven.  Add onion, garlic, carrots and basil; cook for 5 minutes.  Add zucchini, tomatoes with liquid, boullion, cabbage, salt and pepper.  Bring soup to boil; reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 1 hour. Add beans  with liquid; cook another 20 minutes. Garnish with parsley.
