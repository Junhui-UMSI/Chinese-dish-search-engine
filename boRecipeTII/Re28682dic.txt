Texas Pecan Cheese Balls
Salt, Paprika, Pecan halves, Butter, all-purpose flour, Cheddar Cheese, 
Mix all ingredients well except the pecans, kneading with hands if  necessary, thats right get those hands in there. Form dough into small  balls.  Press center of each ball with them. Insert pecan half into thumb  imprint and bring dough up around pecan. Place on cookie sheet and bake at  375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes until lightly browned. Serve hot!    Randy Rigg
