Praline Toffee Sundae Sauce
Praline Liquor, Vanilla, Pecans, Lt brown sugar, Instant Coffee Powder, Sweetened condensed milk, Water, Salt, 
Combine liquor, coffee, salt, water, sugar in heavy saucepan. Heat,  stirring constantly, to boil. Cook without stirring to 230 deg. on candy  therm. Blend syrup slowly into milk. Add pecans and vanilla. Serve over ice  cream.    Recipe By     :    From:                                 Date:    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
