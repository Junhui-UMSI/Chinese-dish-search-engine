Tutti-Frutti Ice Cream
Assorted candied fruit, Recipe for vanilla ice, Cream, ICE CREAM COOKBOOK-----, 
When your favorite vanilla ice cream is half frozen, add 1 1/2 lbs. of  candied fruit--equal parts of peaches, apricots, cherries, pineapple, and  pears--all cut fine. Mix well and finish freezing.
