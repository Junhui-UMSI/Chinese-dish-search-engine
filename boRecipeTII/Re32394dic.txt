Barley-Amaranth Waffles
Amaranth Flour, Sea salt, Milk, Unrefined Vegetable Oil, Non-alum baking powder, Barley Flour, 
Mix liquids and dry ingredients separately, then beat together with a hand  mixer.  Pour onto hot, non-stick surface griddle.  Thin batter with  additional water if needed.  Makes four waffles.  May be used for pancakes  also.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Amaranth Flour" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
