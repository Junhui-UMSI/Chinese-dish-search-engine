Suzis Thang
Grated cheese, any type, Pillsbury Biscuits, any type, Parsley, Ground sausage, browned (up, Dozen eggs, beaten with, 
Heat oven to 350. Grease the bottom of 9 1/2 x 13" pan. Line with biscuits.  Add beaten eggs. Sprinkle with ground sausage and cheese on top. Bake  approx. 30-40 minutes. Garnish with parsley.    ~-- This recipe can be altered in proportion or ingredients.
