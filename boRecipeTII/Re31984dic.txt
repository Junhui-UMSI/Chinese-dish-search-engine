Taco Rice (With Beans)
Brown rice, Taco seasoning mix, Pinto beans, Tomato sauce, Oregano, Garlic powder, Corn, Water, Green pepper, 
In a medium saucepan, combine all ingredients, except rice & beans.  Bring  mixture to a boil over medium heat.  Stir in rice and beans.  When mixture  boils again, stir, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until  most of the liquid has cooked out, 45 minutes to 1 hour.  Remove from heat,  and set aside covered for 5 minutes.  Mix well.    Recipe By     : Bobbie Hinman in The Meatless Gormet    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
