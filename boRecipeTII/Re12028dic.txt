Egg; separated, Finely chopped walnuts, Baking Powder, Flour, Lemon juice, Nutmeg, Sugar, Butter or margarine, Cinnamon, Eggs, Salt, 
Mix butter, sugar, 2 whole eggs, 1 egg yolk and lemon juice until fluffy.  Stir the dry ingredients together. Blend into sugar mixture. Stir in nuts.  Roll dough, 1/3 at a time, to a 1/16 inch thickness on a lightly floured  board. Cut with a star cutter. Brush tops of cookies with remaining egg  white, beaten until frothy. Bake on a lightly greased cookie sheet at 375  for 6 to 8 minutes. Makes about 96 cookies; 43 per lb. Input: 10/29/91  Note: If you use self-rising flour, omit baking powder and salt.
