Really Ready Chili
Ground beef, Beans, 
Brown ground beef; drain.  Stir in Hunts Chunky Chili Ready Sauce and 1  (15.5-oz) can drained Hunts Chili or Kidney Beans. Simmer, uncovered, 10  minutes and serve. Source: Hunts ad, Womans Day, 12/21/93.
