Apple Nut Cake
All-purpose flour, Salt, Cooking oil, Sugar, Apples; diced and peeled, Vanilla extract, Pecans, Eggs, Baking soda, 
Mix oil, sugar, eggs and vanilla until creamy. Beat in flour, salt and  baking soda. Fold in apples and pecans. Place batter in 13 X 9 inch pan.  Bake at 350  for 1 hour.
