Strawberry Pretzel Surprise
strawberry jello, pretzels, Cream cheese, Cool Whip, Margarine, Sugar, Sugar, Frozen strawberries, pineapple juice, 
Combine pretzels, 1 tablespoon sugar and margarine. Pat into a 9x13 inch  baking dish.  Bake at 400 degrees for 6 minutes. Set aside to cool. Cream  together cream cheese and 1 cup sugar. Fold in Cool Whip. Spread over  cooled pretzel layer.  Dissolve jello in hot pineapple juice. Stir in  strawberries.  When jello has slightly thickened, spoon over cream cheese  layer.  Chill.
