Anzacs
Baking soda, Coconut, Nuts; (optional), Sugar, Butter or margarine, Rolled oats, Flour, Water, Salt, Karo Syrup, 
Mix oats, sugar, coconut, flour and salt. Heat butter, syrup and water  boiling, add soda. Combine liquid mixture and dry ingredients. Add nuts if  used and mix well. Place by teaspoonfuls on greased COOKIE sheet. Allowing  plenty of room to spread. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes at 325 degrees. Yields:  4 dozen. This is an early Australian recipe.
