Pollo Alla Cacciatora (Chicken Cacciatora)
(1-pound can) Italian, Pepper, Crushed dried oregano, Chicken (2 1/2 to 3 pounds), Garlic cloves minced, (8 ounces) tomato sauce, Celery seed, Salt, Dry white wine, olive oil, Onions sliced, Bay Leaves, 
Heat small amount of oil in large deep skillet. Brown chicken on all sides.  Remove chicken and keep hot. cook onions and garlic in oil in skillet until  tender. Add other ingredients except wine, and blend. cook for 5 minutes.  return chicken to skillet. Cover and simmer for 45 minutes. Add wine and  cook, uncovered, for about 15 minutes. Remove bay leaves, serve with  spaghetti or your favorite type of pasta. (Womans Day Cookbook) Posted to  EAT-L Digest  by Al & Diane Johnson <johnson@NEGIA.NET> on Aug 2, 1997
