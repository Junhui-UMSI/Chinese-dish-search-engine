Baked Tomatoes and Eggs
Eggs, Salt, Onions, Fresh basil, Margarine, Tomatoes, fresh, Hot pepper sauce, Black pepper, 
1. Grease 4 ramekins or serving dishes with a tablespoon of margarine. Heat  oven to 350 degrees. 2. In a heavy saucepan or skillet, melt remaining 4  tablespoons margarine. Saute the onions until tender and translucent. Add  tomatoes, basil, hot pepper sauce, salt and pepper to taste. Simmer,  stirring often, for 10 minutes. 3. Pour the mixture evenly into the  ramekins. Top each with an egg. Bake 6-10 minutes or until eggs are done to  your taste. While still hot, sprinkle 1 tablespoon shredded cheese over top  if desired. Serve immediately.    Recipe By     : Jo Anne Merrill    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
