Tofu with Hoisin Sauce
ginge rroot, Garlic, red bell peppers, Firm-style tofu, Dry sherry, Chinese sesame oil, Sliced scallions, Reduced sodium soy sauce, Vegetable oil, Hoisin sauce, 
In 1 qt microwaveable casserole combine pepper, celery, oil, gingerroot and  grlic and stir to coat. Microwave on HIGH for 2 minutes, stirring once  halfway through cooking. Add sherry, hoisin sauce, soy sauce, and sesame  oil and stir to combine; microwave on HIGH for 1 minute. Add tofu and  microwave on HIGH for 1 minute; sprinkle with scallions. 2 servings.
