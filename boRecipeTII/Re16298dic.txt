Watermelon Ice
Watermelon juice; * see note, Lemon juice, bottled, Granulated sugar, 
In a shallow pan, combine watermelon juice, sugar, and lemon juice until  sugar is dissolved; place pan in freezer. Stir watermelon mixture every 20  minutes until frozen, about 2 hours.
