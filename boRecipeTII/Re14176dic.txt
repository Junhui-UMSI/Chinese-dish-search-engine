White House Pink Fruit Punch (En)
Pineapple juice, Orange juice, Ginger ale, ice, Cranberry juice, 
In a large punch bowl, mix together fruit juices; chill.    Just before serving, add ginger ale and stir.    Add ice. ( Ice cubes make punch difficult to serve; use larger blocks of  ice instead.) Ladle punch into pretty punch glasses, and garnish with  orange slices, if desired.
