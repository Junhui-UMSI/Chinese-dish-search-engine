Peaches and Cream Rice Salad
Water, Whipped topping, coconut, shredded, Peaches, diced, canned, 
1. Cook rice accoding to package directions. 2. Allow rice to cool. 3. Fold  in diced peaches, whipped topping and coconut. 4. Refrigerate until ready  for serving. Measurements and timing are designed only for Uncle Bens Fast  Cooking Rice Pilaf, Original Rice Pilaf, Fast Cooking Spanish Rice, Long  Grain & Wild Rice Blend, and CONVERTED Brand Rice
