Tamora Pudding
Papaya, slightly unripe,, Oranges, juiced -=OR=-, peel & seeds removed, Pineapple chunks, fresh, Orange juice, 
Cut the papaya into small pieces.  Put in a blender with the remaining  ingredients.  Blend at medium speed until smooth. Pour into pudding  glasses.  Refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours or until the puddings are firm. Then  serve immediately. If keeping rhe puddings longer, cover tightly & store in  the refrigerator for 3 to 5 days.
