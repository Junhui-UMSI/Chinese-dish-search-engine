Balsamic Mushrooms
Mushrooms, Red wine, Balsamic vinegar, 
Braise the mushrooms in the vinegar and wine mix covered for a few minutes.  When mushrooms are soft, removed cover and let liquid condense down to  almost the bottom.    From: Z3MAX@ttacs1.ttu.edu.
