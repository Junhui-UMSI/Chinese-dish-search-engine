Stringy and Gooey
Dinner, Box macaroni and cheese, Chicken breast filets, Condensed, Cream of asparagus soup,, Mozzarella cheese, 
Make macaroni and cheese dinner per instructions on box, then place in  baking dish.  Fry chicken breasts, then place on top of macaroni and  cheese.  Cover with cream of mushroom soup, then cover with shredded  Mozzarella cheese.  Bake in oven at 400 F for 20 to 30 minutes.  Serve with  butter, bread, and beer.
