"Gone in a Second" Pickles
Sugar, Vinegar, Dried red pepper flakes, Salt, Accent, Cloves garlic; sliced, Ice water, Cucumbers, Boiling water, 
Cut the cucmbers in half lengthwise, then slice diagonally into 1/2"-thick  slices and place in a large bowl. Mix salt with boiling water and pour over  the cucumbers and let stand 1 hour, then drain. Mix the remaining  ingredients and pour over the cucumbers and refrigerate.
