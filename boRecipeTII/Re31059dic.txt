Banana Poppy Seed Muffins
Powdered sugar, Baking Powder, Salt, Vanilla, CITRUS GLAZE, Vegetable oil, Egg, Orange juice, Poppy seeds, Orange Peel; grated, Sugar, Orange Peel; grated, All-purpose flour, Ripe Bananas; peeled, 
Puree bananas in blender (1 cup). In medium bowl, mix bananas, egg, sugar,  oil and orange peel until well blended. In large bowl, combine flour, poppy  seeds, baking powder and salt. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until  evenly moistened. Spoon batter into greased 2 1/2 inch muffin cups. Bake in  375 degrees oven 20 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes  out clean. Remove from pan; cool on wire rack. Top with Citrus Glaze while  warm. Makes 12 muffins.    CITRUS GLAZE: In medium bowl, combine powdered sugar, orange juice, grated  orange peel and vanilla until smooth.
