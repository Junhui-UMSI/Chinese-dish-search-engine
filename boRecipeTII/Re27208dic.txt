Tips for Perfect Biscuits
Tips for perfect biscuits, 
Use your fingertips to knead dough.       Dont overwork dough.       Use shortening or butter for best texture.       Make certain baking powder is fresh.       Dont rush baking: Allow biscuits to brown nicely.       Contrary to popular belief, brush biscuits with melted butter AFTER  baking--not before.       Hint:  To save on calories and fat, spray tops of biscuits with food  release spray instead of brushing with butter--no one will ever know the  difference!! (Except the scales).
