Honey-Caramelized Bananas and Oranges
Bananas; peeled, halved, Orange; peeled and sliced, Honey, Walnuts, Brandy; optional, 
Place bananas and orange slices in small flameproof dish. Drizzle with  honey and sprinkle with walnuts. Broil 4 to 6 inches from heat source for  about 5 minutes, or until heated through and lightly browned but not burnt.  Remove from broiler. If desired, pour brandy over top and flame. Serve  immediately.    Makes 2 servings.
