Tomato-Mushroom Sauce
Dried whole oregano, Dried whole basil, Sliced fresh mushrooms, olive oil, No-salt-added whole, Salt, Garlic, Vegetable cooking spray, Chopped fresh parsley, Onion, Sugar, 
Coat a saucepan with cooking spray; add oil, and place over medium heat  until hot. Add mushrooms, onion, and garlic; saute 3 minutes. Add parsley  and remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer,  uncovered, 15 minutes, stirring frequently. Yield: 2 cups (serving size:      1/2    cup).    Per serving: 126 Calories; 3g Fat (22% calories from fat); 5g Protein; 23g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 191mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve warm.
