Citrus-Ade
Limes, Oranges, Water, Sugar, Lemons, 
Squeeze the juice from four of each fruit.  Pour into a gallon container.  Thinly slice the rem fruit and set aside for garnish.  Add water and sugar  to juices; mix well.  Store in the fridge.  Serve on ice with fruit slices.    Source:  Taste of Home magazine    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Country Mouse <mjcksn@telusplanet.net> on  17 A, ug 1998
