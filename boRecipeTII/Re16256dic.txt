Watergate Cake
milk, Coconut, COVERUP ICING, 7-Up, Pistachio Instant Pudding, Whipped Topping Mix, Nuts (Pecans), Coconut, White Cake Mix, Vegetable oil, Pistachio Instant Pudding, Eggs, Nuts, 
This cake recipe came out when Nixon was caught in the Watergate Scandal in  1976    Combine the ingredients in the order given, blending well after each  addition.  Pour into a 13 X 9-inch pan and bake in a preheated 350 degree  F. oven for 35 to 45 minutes, or until the cake tests done.    ICING: Combine the topping mix, milk and pudding. Beat until thick. Spread  on the cake, (The icing will be a light green color). Sprinkle with the  coconut and chopped nuts.    From The Food Fare Section Rocky Mountain News September 3, 1986
