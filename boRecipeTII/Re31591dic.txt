Swiss and Turkey Casserole
Onions, Chopped cooked turkey, Miracle Whip-reg or lite, milk, Kraft shredded Swiss cheese, EACH-croutons and sliced, 
Miz all ingredients. Spoon into 2-quart casserole. Sprinkle with more  croutons if desired. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes or until heated through.  Makes 6 servings.    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Abtaxel <Abtaxel@aol.com> on Mar 10, 1998
