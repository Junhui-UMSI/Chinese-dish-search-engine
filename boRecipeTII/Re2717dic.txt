Peanut Butter Bread
Flour, Sugar, Baking Powder, Peanut butter, Salt, milk, 
Sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar together. Add peanut butter  to milk and mix well, then add this to the dry ingredients, beat  thoroughly and bake in well greased load pan in slow oven 45 - 50  minutes. It it better when day old.    NOTE: Slow oven is 300 - 350 F.    Source: Florence L. Houston, Massie Grange, Warren County,  OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
