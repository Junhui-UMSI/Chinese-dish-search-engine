Plain Cake
Sugar, Flour, Salt, Baking Powder, Butter, milk, Eggs, 
Cream butter and sugar.  Beat eggs and stir in.  Sift together flour, salt  and baking powder.  Add alternately with milk. Bake in a 9 x 9" pan at 350  degrees for 30-40 minutes.    Use this recipe for strawberry shortcake.    From Mrs. Alvin Funk, Hillsboro, KS    Formatted for MM by Rosanne Troxel (D.TROXEL on GEnie)
