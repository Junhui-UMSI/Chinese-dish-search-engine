Asian Noodle Salad with Chiles and Peanuts
Soy sauce, Oriental sesame oil, Sugar, Green onions, Cilantro, Dried soba noodles, Jalapeno peppers, Dry roasted peanuts,, Balsamic vinegar, 
Cook noodles or spaghetti until just tender.  Drain.  Rinse with cold water  until cool. Drain well.  Transfer to large bowl.  Combine sesame oil, soy  sauce, sugar, vinegar and salt in small bowl.  Stir to dissolve sugar.  Add  to noodles.  Add choppped cilantro, chiles, and onions.  Mix well. Sprinkle  peanuts over individual salads. Cover and refrigerate.  Sprinkle  chopped peanuts over salad at serving time.
