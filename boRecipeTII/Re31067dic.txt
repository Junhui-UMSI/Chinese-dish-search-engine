Banana Pudding #2
Vanilla wafers, Boxes (3-oz) vanilla, (8-oz) whipped topping, Sweetened condensed milk, Or, Bananas, 
Prepare pudding as directed on box, except use 1/2 cup less milk for a  thicker pudding. Stir in sweetened condensed milk & whipped topping. Line  large bowl with vanilla wafers. Slice bananas over top of wafers. Pour  pudding mixture over all. Repeat layers of wafers, bananas & pudding. Chill  until set.    MRS. T.J.
