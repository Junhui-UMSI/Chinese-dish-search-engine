Peanut Butter-Orange Sandwich Filling
Honey, Peanut butter, crunchy, Salt, Oranges, peeled, diced, 
Thoroughly mix peanut butter, honey, and salt.  Stir in oranges.  Chill.  Use about 1/3 cup filling per sandwich.    NOTE: Filling can be made the day before using.  Store tightly covered in  the refrigerator.  Stir lightly before making sandwiches.    Calories per serving: About 285 for filling; 420 for a sandwich made with        2    slices of bread.
