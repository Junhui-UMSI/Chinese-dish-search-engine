Biscuits for Your Hearts Content - Heart Healthy
Flour, Baking Powder, Vegetable oil, Nonfat milk, Salt, , 
A light fluffy biscuit without the saturated fat.    Preheat oven to 475 degrees F. Sift flour, baking powder and salt. Measure  oil and milk into cup but dont stir. Add all at once to flour mixture.  Stir with fork until mixture leaves side of bowl and forms ball. Knead 10  times. Roll out (1/4" thick) and cut with floured glass or biscuit cutter.  Bake 10-12 minutes.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Aug  17, 1998
