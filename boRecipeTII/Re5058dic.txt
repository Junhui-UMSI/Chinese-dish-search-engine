Picnic Burritos
Tomato, fresh, Leaf lettuce, Flour tortilla, cheese, turkey, Taco seasoning mix, Mayonnaise, 
Mix mayonnaise and seasoning mix; spread on each  tortilla; sprinkle with cheese.  Layer with turkey, tomato and lettuce, leaving about 2  iches at one side covered with mayonnaise mixture only.  Beginning at side layered with turkey, roll up each  tortilla. To serve immediately, cut in half. Or,  securely wrap uncut burritos with plastic wrap and  refrigerate up to 2 hours; cut in half.
