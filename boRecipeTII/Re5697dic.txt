Pineapple-Date Dip
Unsweetened crushed, Pitted dates, chopped, Ground nutmeg, Reduced-calorie frozen, Nonfat sour cream, 
Combine sour cream and whipped topping in a medium bowl; stir well. Add  dates and pineapple; stir until well blended. Cover and chill at least 1  hour; sprinkle with nutmeg. Yield: 2 cups (serving size: 1 tablespoon).    Per serving: 34 Calories; 2g Fat (63% calories from fat); 0g Protein; 3g  Carbohydrate; 9mg Cholesterol; 4mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve with fresh strawberries and apple wedges.
