Tortilla Wrap-Ups
Tomato, Salsa, Egg, Bacon bits, flour tortilla, Cheddar cheese, 
Scramble egg. Warm tortilla in microwave or wrap it in foil and place in  oven for a few minutes. Place scrambled egg in center of tortilla. Add  cheese, salsa, bacon bits and tomato (or any additions that suit you or  child). Fold in sides, roll up. Wrap in foil to keep in warm while eating.
