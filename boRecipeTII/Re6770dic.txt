Pollo Al Ajo (Garlic and Thyme Chicken)
6-oz boneless chicken, Fresh chopped thyme, Jalape?o pepper; minced, Minced garlic cloves, Shredded ginger root, Butter, Fine dry bread crumbs, 
Here is the recipe taken for Chef Ks International Family Favorites  cookbook pictured at www.divinecuisine.com.    Wash the chicken breasts and lay flat on a baking sheet.    Preheat the oven to 350 F (180 C).    In a small mixing bowl blend the butter, garlic, ginger, thyme, jalape?o  and the bread crumbs.    With a small knife, gently lift the skin of the chicken from the meat.  Stuff equal amounts of the butter filling under the chicken skin. Skewer  the ends with tooth picks to seal the ends.    Bake the chicken in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.  Serve with honey glazed carrots, green beans and a chicken rice pilaff.
