White Bean Stew (Mf)
olive oil, Chicken broth, White wine, Dried red pepper flakes, Chopped parsley, Garlic cloves; finely sliced, Thinly julienned red bell, Dried Oregano, Cooked cannellini beans;, 
In a large skillet heat garlic in oil until golden. Add white wine and cook  about a minute until evaporated. Add the cannellini beans, broth and red  pepper flakes and oregano. Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes just to  heat through; adjust seasonings. Add parsley and remove from heat. Garnish  with red pepper.
