Peanut Butter Hard Suet Cakes
Ground  suet, Yellow cornmeal, Fine cracked corn, Peanut butter, 
Melt the suet (in the top of a double boiler), let it cool completely; then  remelt.  Remove it from the heat, add the peanut butter and mix until well  blended.  Stir in the cornmeal and cracked corn.  Spoon this mixture into  foil molds, margarine tubs or cupcake liners.  Chill.  When hard, remove  the cakes from the molds and store them in plastic bags in the freezer.    This is a favorite of downy woodpeckers and nuthatches.    Makes six 4" x 4" x 1" cakes, six margarine tub sized cakes or 13 to 20  muffin size cakes.
