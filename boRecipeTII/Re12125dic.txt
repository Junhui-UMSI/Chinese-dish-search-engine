Zucchini and Carrot Pickles
Carrots, Zucchini, Soy sauce, Dried red chili pepper, Mustard seeds, Ginger, 1/4" slice, Garlic, Rice vinegar, 
Cut Zucchini into 2X1/2 inch sticks (1 cup) Cut Carrots into 2X1/2 inch  sticks (1 cup) Combine soy sauce, vinegar, mustard seeds, chili pepper,  ginger and garlic in a small bowl.   Place zucchini and carrots in a shallow dish or bowl and pour marindated  over them.     Cover and chill at least overnight, stirring occasionally.
