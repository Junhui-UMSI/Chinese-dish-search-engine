Peaches and Cream Pie
Almond Extract -- or vanilla, 9 Inch Pie Shell, Extract, Sugar -- divided, Light cream, Ripe Peaches -- peeled and, Eggs, Unsalted butter, Halved, 
Preheat oven to 350.    Arrange peach halves, pitted side up, in unbaked pie shell. Combine cream,  extract, eggs, and 1/4 cup sugar. Pour over peaches. Dot tops of peaches  with butter; sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup sugar. Bake for one hour.    Recipe By     :
