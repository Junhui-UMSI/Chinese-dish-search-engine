Appetizer Pie
(8-oz) fat free cream, Skim milk, Fat-free sour cream, Green Onions, Chopped green bell pepper, Pepper, Jar; (2 1/2-oz) sliced dried, Chopped fresh parsley, 
Fast and Healthy Jan/Feb 95    Heat oven to 350 degrees. In medium bowl, combine cream cheese and milk;  beat until smooth. Stir in remaining ingredients except parsley; mix well.  Spoon and spread mixture in ungreased 9-inch pie pan or small shallow  baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes or until thoroughly heated.  Sprinkle with parsley. Serve immediately with crackers or cocktail bread. 2  cups. MICROWAVE directions: Prepare as directed above; spoon into 9-inch  pie pan or shallow dish. Microwave on medium for 5 to 8 minutes or until  thoroughly heated, rotating dish once halfway through cooking. Sprinkle  with parsley. Serve immediately.
