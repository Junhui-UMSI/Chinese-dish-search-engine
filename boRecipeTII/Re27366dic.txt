Toasted Coconut Pie
Baked pie shell, Evaporated milk, Sugar, Vanilla, Stick butter or margarine, milk, Eggs, Salt, Nutmeg, 
Combine first 5 ingredients & beat. Heat until butter melts. Add salt,  vanilla & nutmeg. Pour into pie crust & sprinkle with nutmeg. Bake at 400  for 5 minutes; turn oven up to 425 and bake 10 minutes more.    MRS. W.W. CAERY    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
