Twinkle Cake
Flour, Sugar, Sugar, CONKLIN(VXBD11A); FILLING, Butter or margarine, Baking Powder, Crisco, Vanilla, Eggs, Butter, Salt, Vanilla, Evaporated milk, CAKE, milk, 
Mix together and put into 13 x 9 pan or you can use a yellow cake mix. Cook  for 25 to 30 min at 350. then cut into squares and put filling on cake that  has been split and top with other piece.
