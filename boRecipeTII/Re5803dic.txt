Pinto Bean Loaf
Fresh ground pepper to taste, Bread crumbs, finely ground, Catsup or barbeque sauce, Quick oatmeal, Water, beat til frothy, Tomato sauce, Pinto Beans, cooked/mashed, Onion, Egg replacer mixed with, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine all ingredients, except catsup, in  a large bowl.  Mix well. Turn into a non-stick loaf pan, 9-1/4 x 5-1/4 x 3,  and flatten.    Spread catsup or BBQ sauce over the top.  Bake for 45 minutes. NOTE:  Makes a good sandwich spread when cold.
