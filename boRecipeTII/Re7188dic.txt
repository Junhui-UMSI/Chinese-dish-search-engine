Pork and Corn Bake
Tblsp brown sugar, Corn; whole kernel, Envelope Shake n Bake, Dry mustard, Pork Chops, 
Spoon corn into 13x9-inch baking dish. Add sugar and dry mustard to bag of  seasoned coating mix - then coat pork chops as directed on package. Arrange  chops in a single layer on corn. Sprinkle any reamining coating mix over  chops. Bake at 425 degrees for about 50 minutes. Cover with foil.
