Strawberry Cake #2
Water, ICING, Margarine, Vanilla, Corn oil, Frozen strawberries, Eggs, powdered sugar, white cake mix, Strawberry Jello, Frozen strawberries, Flour, 
Mix all cake ingredients until smooth. Bake in 9x13-inch pan for 35-45  minutes at 350. For icing, mix all ingredients until smooth. Pour over cake  while cake is still warm.    MRS J.R.
