Pecan Banana Bread
Butter, Pecans, coarsely chopped, Ripe bananas, mashed, Whole wheat pastry flour, Baking Powder, Lemon Peel, Eggs, slightly beaten, Baking soda, Maple syrup, 
In a large bowl, mix first 5 ingredients with fork; then use knives to cut  in butter until mixture looks crumbly. With fork, stir bananas, pecans,  lemon peel, and eggs into flour mixture, until flour is just moistened.  Spoon batter into lightly oiled 9" x 5" loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees for  40-50 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Cool at least 10 minutes  before slicing.
