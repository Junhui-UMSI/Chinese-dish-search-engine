Quick Sweet Potato Pie
margarine, Egg substitute, cinnamon, Sugar, Dark brown sugar, salt, Unbaked pie crust, sweet potatoes, Vanilla extract, 
Preheat oven to 400. Mix together sweet potatoes, extract, margarine, white  and brown sugars, egg substitute, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Blend well.  Pour into pie crust and bake 12 minutes at 400. Reduce oven temperature to      350    and bake until done.
