Potato Soup with Green Chiles
Cumin, Potatoes; peeled, diced, Onion, Vegetable oil, -(up to), Oregano, Black pepper, Whole roasted; peeled green, Garlic, Chicken broth, -(up to), 
Heat oil in 2 qt saucepan, add onion and garlic, cover, cook over low heat  until the onion wilts (about 5 min).  Turn up the heat to medium, uncover,  and add cumin and pepper. When the onion starts to brown (2 min?), add the  rest of the ingredients, bring to a boil and simmer for 45 minutes.  Options:    Add some chopped jalapenos with the green chiles for more heat.    Top with chopped cilantro and/or shredded cheese.    Add 1/2 C sour cream and stir well just before serving.
