Taco Pie #2
Onion, (1.25-oz) taco seasoning mix, Shredded Cheddar cheese;, (16-oz) refried beans, Baked 9-inch pastry shell, Shredded lettuce, Chopped tomato, Ground beef, Jar (8-oz) taco sauce;, Crushed corn chips, Water, 
Cook ground beef and onion in a skillet until meat is browned, stirring to  crumble meat. Drain. Add taco seasoning mix and water, stirring well. Bring  to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.     Combine refried beans and 1/3 cup taco sauce. Spoon half of bean mixture  in bottom of pastry shell. Top with half of meat mixture, half of cheese,  and all of corn chips. Repeat layers with remaining bean mixture, meat  mixture and cheese. Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes. Top with lettuce  and tomato.
