Easy Oven Fried Rice (Chicken)
Water, Onion soup mix, chicken, Mushrooms with juice, Vegetable oil, Soya sauce, Celery, Green pepper, Green onion, 
Stir all together and bake in oven a 350 without cover for 1 1/2 hours.  Stir only twice.    Recipe from http://www.canadianparents.com/canadacooks/index.htm    NOTES : This recipe is quick and easy and uses very few dishes!
