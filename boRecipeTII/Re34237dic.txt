Bbq Baked Beans (Jacks)
Molasses, Mustard, Green pepper, Water, Barbecue sauce, Red pepper, Cider vinegar, smoked meat, Bacon, Onion, Navy beans, 
In a saucepan cook beans with water for 2 hours.    In a saute pan, cook bacon over moderate heat. Add onions and peppers and  cook until tender. Add remaining ingredients, including beans and bake at  250 degrees for 45 minutes. Serve hot.
