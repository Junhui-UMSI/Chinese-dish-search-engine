Bacon Muffins
Butter, Heavy cream, Flour, Bacon, egg, Green Onions, cheddar, Baking Powder, 
Heat oven to 400. Fry bacon. Drain fat, reserving 1 Tbls.. Crumble  bacon. Sift flour and powder. cut in butter. Stir in egg and cream.  Add bacon, cheese and onions. Grease 12 muffing tins with reserved  fat. Drop batter into each cup filling 2/3 full. Bake 15-20 minutes  or until gold. Serve hot.
