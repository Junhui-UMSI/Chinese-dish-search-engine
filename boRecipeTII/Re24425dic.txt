Tropical Fruit Bowl
& dice, Kiwi fruit, peeled -- in, -inch rounds, Diagonally -- 1/2-inch, Lime juice, Diced fresh pineapple, Bananas, peeled and sliced, Ground ginger, Papaya -- peel, seed & dice, Honey-Rum Dressing -, Rum, Honey, Mango or peach -- peel, seed, Thick, 
1. Lightly mix pineapple, papaya, mango, kiwi, and their juices with  Honey-Rum Dressing in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate 2 to 3 hours. 2.  Mix in bananas and serve immediately in a glass bowl. Honey-Rum Dressing:  Pour honey into a small bowl; gradually add rum and lime juice, then  ginger. Mix until well blended. Makes about 1/2 cup.
