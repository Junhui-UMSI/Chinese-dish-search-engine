Tomatillo Salsa
Tequila, Lime juice, Tomatillos, Crushed red pepper, Onions, Minced fresh cilantro, 
1.  Remove husks and stems from tomatillos.  Position knife blade in food    processor bowl; add tomatillos and remaining ingredients, and pulse 10    times or until chopped.  Cover and set aside. Yield 1 1/2 cups; about    calories per tablespoon. ---
