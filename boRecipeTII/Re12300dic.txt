Zucchini Parmesan Bread
Baking soda, Grated onion, Peeled; shredded zucchini,, All purpose flour, Salt, Eggs, Sugar, Grated Parmesan cheese, Baking Powder, Buttermilk, Butter, 
Mix flour, zucchini, sugar, cheese, baking soda, powder and salt together;  set aside. Melt butter; stir into buttermilk. Beat eggs in medium bowl; add  butter/buttermilk and onion; stir into flour mixture. (Batter will be  thick.) Spread in greased and floured 9x5x3 inch loaf pan. Bake at 350 for  1 hour.
