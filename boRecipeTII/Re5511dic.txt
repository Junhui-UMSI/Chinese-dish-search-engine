Pineapple Icebox Dessert
Pecans, Crushed pineapple, Egg, Heavy cream, confectioners sugar, vanilla wafers, Butter, 
Grease an 8 inch square pan. Pack the bottom with half of the crushed  vanilla wafers; cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add egg, beating well.  Drain pineapple; fold in pineapple, pecans, then whipped cream. Pour in pan  evenly. Cover with rest of the crushed vanilla wafers. Refrigerate 24  hours.
