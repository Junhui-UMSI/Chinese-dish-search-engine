Raspberry Cinnamon Applesauce
Cinnamon sticks, Sugar; (optional), Cooking apples;, (10 oz.) thawed red, 
Place raspberries, cinnamon and apples in heavy pan. Bring mixture to boil.  Reduce heat to low; cover. Simmer 15-20 minutes until tender, stirring  occasionally. Stir in sugar to taste, if desired. Remove cinnamon sticks.  Serve warm or refrigerate. Makes 6 cups. Great with pork and a great way to  use all those Fall apples.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 19, 1998
