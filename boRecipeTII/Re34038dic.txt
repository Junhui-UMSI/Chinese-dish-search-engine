Basil-Sesame Dressing
Chopped Italian parsley, Grated Parmesan or Asiago, Pepper; to taste, Garlic, Chopped fresh basil, Rice vinegar, Lemon juice, Sesame oil, olive oil, 
Whisk together all ingredients.  Add pepper.    Yield: 3/4 cup.    Shepherd writes: "A delicious flavor combination for all your garden fresh  greens."    Recipe developed by Renee Shepherd and Fran Raboff. From 1996 "Shepherds  Garden Seeds" catalog.  Pg. 24. Electronic format by Cathy Harned.
