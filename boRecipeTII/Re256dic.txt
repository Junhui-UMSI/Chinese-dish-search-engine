Lasagne
Lasagne noodles; (about 9), Salt, olive oil, Ricotta cheese, Mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, SAUCE, Onion; minced, Garlic, olive oil, Ground beef or ground turkey, Can tomatoes, Can tomato paste, Salt, Cayenne pepper, Sugar, Basil, Bay leaf, Water, 
Lightly brown onion and garlic in oil in large saucepan. Add ground beef or  turkey and brown lightly. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer, uncovered,    1 1/2    hours.    Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain, add oil. Arrange in  shallow 1 1/2 quart baking dish, making three layers each of noodles,  ricotta, mozzarella, sauce and Parmesan cheese. Bake at 350F for 45  minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.    Recipe by: Dolores Blaner    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Nancy Pallotta <nancee@NEO.LRUN.COM> on Dec 17,  1997
