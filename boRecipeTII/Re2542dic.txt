Peaches in Honey Vanilla Syrup
Peaches, Lemon juice, Honey, Vanilla Bean, Vanilla nonfat yogurt, Water, Sliced, 
Bring water, honey, and vanilla bean to boil; reduce heat and simmer 5  minutes. Add peaches; raise heat to bring mixture back to a fast  simmer. Simmer until peahes are just softened, 10-15 minutes,  depending on ripeness. Remove from heat; stir in lemon juice.  Refrigerate at least 2 hours. Serve over frozen yogurt.
