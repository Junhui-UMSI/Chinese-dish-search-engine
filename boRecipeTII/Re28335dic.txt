Baked Ham Slices
Dry mustard, milk, Brown sugar, Ham; 1-inch thick, 
Cut slashes in edge of ham. Sprinkle with mustard and sugar. Place in heavy  skillet. Pour milk into pan but do not cover ham with milk. Bake uncovered  at      350    for 1-1/4 hours.
