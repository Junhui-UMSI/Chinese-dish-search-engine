Pork Chops in Adobo Sauce
Onion, Chili powder, Cumin seed, Avocado, Cooking oil, Garlic, All-purpose flour, Vinegar, Salt, Pork loin chops, Onion, Tomato sauce, Radish, Dried oregano, 
1. Place tomato sauce, vinegar, onion, garlic, flour, chili powder, oil,  salt, oregano and cumin in blender container. Cover. Blend till onion is  finely chopped.    2. Place chops in shallow baking dish. Pour sauce over.    3. Cover, refrigerate several hours, or overnight.    4. In large covered skillet, simmer chops in sauce 35-45 minutes or till  tender.    5. Arrange chops with sauce on a serving platter. Garnish with sliced  onion, radish and avocado.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 549 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 18, 1998
