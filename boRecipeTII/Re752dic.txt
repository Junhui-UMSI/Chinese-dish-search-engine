Guava Bbq Sauce
Tomato paste, Corn syrup, Guava jelly, Mustard powder, White vinegar, Cumin, Shallots; minced, Dry sherry, 
Combine the sauce ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium  heat. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 1/2 hours. Remove from heat and cool.    ( from the test kitchen of Restaurant Business)    Posted to bbq-digest by wight@odc.net on Nov 19, 1998, converted by  MM_Buster v2.0l.
