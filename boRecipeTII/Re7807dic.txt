Portorhouse Steak and Italian Vegetables
Lengthwise, Lengthwise, Strips, Porterhouse steaks -- 1", Yellow squash -- cut in, olive oil, Thick, Zucchini -- cut in half, Parmesean cheese, Half, Red bell pepper -- cut into, BARB DAY - GWHP32A-----, Italian seasoning -- dried, 
Preparation time:  25 minutes Combine cheese, oil and Italian seasoning and  brush mixture on cut sides of squash and inside of bell pepper. Place steak  and vegetables, cut side down on a rack in the broiler pan so the surface  of the meat is 3 to 4 inches from the heat.  Broil 10 to15 minutes for rare  (140 degrees F.) to medium (160 degrees F), turning once. Season steak with  salt and pepper if desired.  Carve steaks into thick slices. Substitute  boneless beef sirloin steak if desired.
