Banana Nut Wheat Bread
Pecans, Banana; mashed, Tbls Maple Syrup, Maple Extract, Whole wheat flour, Salt, Yeast, Warm water, 
Yields 1 Loaf  All the ingredients except the water should be at room temperature before  starting. Add the ingredients to the pan in the order listed. Select "Whole  Wheat".
