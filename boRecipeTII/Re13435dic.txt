Yeasted Lemon Poppy Seed Bread
Egg, Lecithin; liquid or granules, Unbleached flour, Nonfat dry milk, Lemon juice, Vital wheat gluten, Lemon peel; grated, Water, Sugar, Butter, Whey, Active dry yeast, 
Have all ingredients at room temperature.  Add ingredients in order given.  If your bread machine specifies dry ingredients first, reverse order. Set  on basic setting, light crust.  Do NOT use programmable timer as bread  contains perishable ingredients.    Adapted from a recipe in _The Ultimate Bread Machine Book_ by Tom  Lacalamita.  Serving Ideas : Drizzle with a lemon glaze, if desired.
