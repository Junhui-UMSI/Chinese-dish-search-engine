Latkes - (Potato Pancakes)
Onion, Egg, Flour, Vegetable oil, Plain Lowfat Yogurt, Unsweetened Applesauce, Baking Potatoes, 
Keywords: Vegetarian, Lacto, Breakfast    Peel and grate the potatoes onto paper towels.  Squeeze out the moisture.  Combine the potatoes and the remaining ingredients, except the yogurt and  applesauce.  Beat well.    Heat the oil in a skillet.  Drop the batter into the oil by spoonfuls. Fry  until crisp and brown on both sides.  Remove from the skillet and drain on  paper towels.  Serve warm with plain yogurt and unsweetened applesauce.    Note: One-fourth cup oil is used in frying.  Reserve rest of oil for later  use by storing in the refrigerator.  Serves 4.
