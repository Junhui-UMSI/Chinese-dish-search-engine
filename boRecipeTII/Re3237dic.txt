Pear Crumble
Flour, Salt, Flour, Cinnamon, Fresh pears, Butter, nuts, Sugar, 
On bottom of lightly greased 8 inch square baking pan, arrange sliced  pears. Combine 2 tbsp flour and half the cinnamon. Sprinkle over pears. To  make topping, combine 3/4 cup flour, remaining cinnamon, sugar and salt.  Cut in butter until crumbly.  Add nuts to topping mixture. Spoon over  pears.  Bake, uncovered, in 350 F oven for 30-35 minutes until pears are  tender.  Makes 6 servings.    Origin:  The Great Canadian Cookbook, part one, British Columbia. Shared  by: Sharon Stevens
