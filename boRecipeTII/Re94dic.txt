Kidney Bean Salad
Onion, Stalk celery, Lettuce, Cabbage, Sweet pickles, Peppers, Kidney beans, Sugar, Salt, pepper, to taste, Mayonnaise, Carrots, 
Chop all vegetables. Add kidney beans. Mix sugar, salt, pepper, and  mayonnaise. Combine with kidney beans and vegetables. Recipe from Margarete  Wolf. Formatted by Lynn Thomas. Source: The Palm Schwenkfelder Church  Cookbook, Palm, PA.
