Two Toners
Walnuts, Butterscotch Chips, Cooking oil, Mini Marshmallows, Chocolate chips, 
Melt butterscotch chips and 1 tablespoon of oil in top of double boiler  over hot, not boiling water.  Stir in walnuts.  Pour into pan.  Arrange one  layer of mini-marshmallows on top.  Melt chocolate chips and 1 tablespoon  of oil in top of double boiler over hot, not boiling water. Pour over  marshmallows.  Chill.  Use a hammer and chisel to cut.  From: Syds  Cookbook.
