Tater Tot Hot Dish
Corn , Ground beef, Taco seasoning mix, Nacho cheese soup, Tater tots, 
Brown one pound of ground beef or turkey and add taco seasoning and cook  according to package and put in bottom of casserole and top with a can of  corn and a can of nacho cheese soup and then tator tots and bake at 375  degrees for 45 minutes.
