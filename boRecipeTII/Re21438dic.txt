Upside Down Supper
1% low-fat milk, Cooked ham; cubed, Onion, Creamed corn; canned, Worcestershire Sauce, Cooked lima beans, Lowfat cheddar cheese;, Bisquick? baking mix, Egg, Cornmeal, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Mix ham, beans, corn, cheese, onion and  worcestershire sauce. Put into a greased 1 1/2 quart casserole. Cover and  bake 15 mins. Mix remaining ingredients, spoon over hot meat mixture,  spreading batter evenly to the edge of casserole. Bake uncovered 20 mins.  Cut into wedges, invert each onto plate.
