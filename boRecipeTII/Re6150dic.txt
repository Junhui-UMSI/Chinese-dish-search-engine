Pizza Popcorn
Grated Parmesan cheese, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Popped popcorn, Dry spaghetti sauce mix, olive oil, 
Place popcorn in a large bowl. Warm olive oil in microwave. Drizzle over  popcorn; sprinkle with dry spaghetti sauce powder and parmesan cheese; toss  well to coat.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 655 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 31, 1998
