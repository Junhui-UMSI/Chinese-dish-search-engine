Beef Brisket De Quarles
Worcestershire Sauce, Tastes best), Soy sauce, "Good Seasons Italian, Beef brisket; (flat cut), 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Criss-cross two pieces of foil and line bottom  of casserole dish. Sprinkle small amount of Good Seasons dressing on bottom  of foil lined dish before placing in brisket. Place brisket in dish, fatty  side up. Evenly pour worcestershire and soy sauces over brisket. Sprinkle  remaining Good Seasons dressing mix on top of brisket. Tightly fold foil  over brisket, forming a tent. Place in oven. Bake at 375 degrees for 1  hour. Bake at 325-350 degrees for an additional 2 hours (or until meat  looks "stringy"). Enjoy!
