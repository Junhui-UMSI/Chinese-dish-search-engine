Beefy Baked Beans
Syrup (maple preferred), Ketchup, Ground chuck, (20.75-oz) pork and beans, (15-oz) ranch style beans, Prepared mustard, Brown sugar, Stick margarine, Onions; chopped, 
Brown beef and onions in margarine. Add beans. Mix remaining ingredients  and add to meat and beans. Place it all in a casserole or beanpot and  refrigerate overnight. Bake at 300? for 1-1/2 hours. Yield: 10 to 12  servings.    JULIE TRUEMPER    (MRS. JOHN J., JR.)    REFRIGERATE OVERNIGHT    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
