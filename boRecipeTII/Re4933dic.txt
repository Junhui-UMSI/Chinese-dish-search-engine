Pickled Green Tomatoes
Salt, Garlic, Vinegar, Hot Pepper, Pepper, Water, green, Tomatoes, Celery, Dill, 
Contributed to the echo by: Janice Norman Pickled Green Tomatoes    Pack the tomatoes in quart jars, either whole or cut in half    Combine water, vinegar, salt, dill and cook 5 minutes. Fill jars within 1/2  inch from top and seal.    NOTE: Jars should be hot when you fill with the tomatoes as it assures the  jars will seal properly.
