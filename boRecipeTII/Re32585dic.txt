Sweet Milk Cottage Cheese
HOUSEHOLD SEARCHLIGHT, Salt, Pepper, black, Rennet tablets, milk, Cream, Paprika, Cold water, 
Heat milk to lukewarm.  Dissolve rennet tablets in cold water. Add to milk.  Let stand in warm place until set. Pour into thin cloth bag. Drain. Remove  curd from bag. Break into fine pieces. Moisten with cream. Season to taste.  The Household Searchlight    Source: HSCHEESE.TXT, downloaded from GEnie F&W software library.
