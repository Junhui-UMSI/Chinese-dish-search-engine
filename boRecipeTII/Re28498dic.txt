Tesque Pumpkin Cookies
Cooked pumpkin, Vanilla extract, Vegetable shortening, Grated nutmeg, Raisins, Walnuts, Ground allspice, Tofu, beaten, All purpose flour, Sugar, Baking soda, Salt, 
Preheat the oven to 350F.  Grease a large cookie sheet. In a large bowl,  cream together the sugar and shortening. Add the pumpkin, tofu, and vanilla  and beat until smooth. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda,  salt, nutmeg and allspice. Slowly add the dry ingredients to the pumpkin  mixture, small amounts at a time, until completely mi8xed together. Stir in  the raisins and walnuts. Drop tablespoonfuls of the dough roughly 2 inches  apart on the cookie sheet.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes, until golden brown.
