Toffee Ice-Cream Topping
Blue Bonnet margarine, Vanilla ice cream, Brown Sugar, Cheerios cereal, 
Heat margarine in 2-quart saucepan until melted; stir in brown sugar. Cook  over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar is dissolved and mixture is  smooth; remove from heat. Stir in cereal until well coated. Spread mixture  in thin layer on ungreased cookie sheet; cool completely. Break apart;  serve over ice cream with chocolate sauce if desired. 2 cups topping.    Recipe by: The Cheerios Recipe Booklet, General Mills, 1977    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1002 by NGavlak <NGavlak@aol.com> on Jan 10,  1998
