Quick Chocolate Fudge
Margarine, Water, Vanilla, (16-oz) powdered sugar, Light corn syrup, Pecans, Squares (1-oz) unsweetened, 
Grease an 8x8 pyrex pan. In a 2 quart saucepan, melt margarine and  chocolate. Stir in corn syrup, water and vanilla. Remove from heat. Add  sugar and nuts. Stir until smooth. Pour into pan, cool, and cut into  squares.  Yield: 20 pieces.    MRS. MIKE WHITE    BONO, AR
