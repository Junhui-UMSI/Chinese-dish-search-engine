(Machine) Doggie Biscuits
Margarine, Whole wheat flour, Powdered milk, Salt, Egg, Hot water, 
Mix well - roll in to small logs in your hands and bake at 325 degrees for  about 50 mins.    This was posted by C.Harold Quinn (FPHK52A)    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdog.zip
