Waikiki Appetizers
Pineapple Chinks, Maraschino Cherries, Bulk Pork Sausage, Cornstarch, Green Pepper, Brown sugar, Lemon juice, Soy sauce, 
Shape sausage into 1/2 to 3/4 inch balls.  Place in single layer in baking  pan.  Bake in 400F oven 25 minutes, or until cooked; drain on paper  towels. Meanwhile, drain pineapple, reserve syrup. Add enough water to  syrup to measure 1 cup; combine with brown sugar, lemon juice, cornstarch  and soy sauce in large saucepan. Cook and stir until sauce boils and  thickens.  Fold in green pepper, cherries, pineapple chunks and drained  cooked sausage.  To serve, turn into chafing dish. Typed by Syd Bigger.
