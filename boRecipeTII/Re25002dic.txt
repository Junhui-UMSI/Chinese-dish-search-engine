Tomato Rice
Boiling, Rice -- long grain, Onion -- chopped, Tomato paste, Margarine -- nondairy, Tomatoes, canned -- chopped, Tomato juice, Vegetable stock or water, 
Melt the margarine in a large saucepan and saute the onion until tender.    Add the rice and stir well. Add the tomatoes, tomato paste and tomato  juice, and bring the mixture to a boil. Cover, reduce the heat and simmer  for 25-35 minutes or until the rice is tender. Check on the liquid content  towards the end of cooking, and add boiling stock if necessary. Serve hot.
