Baked Stuffed Herring
Herrings, Parsley, Lemon, Salt and pepper, Fresh ground pepper, Hard cider, Egg, bread crumbs, Bay leaf, Nutmeg, 
From: Wendy Lockman <wlockman@ra1.randomc.com>    Date: Sun, 11 Feb 1996 10:35:49 -0500    First make the stuffing by mixing the breadcrumbs, parsley, beaten egg,  lemon juice and peel, and salt and pepper. Stuff the fish with the mixture.  Lay in an ovenproof dish, close together; add the cider, crumbled bay leaf  and salt and pepper. Cover with foil and bake at 350F for about 35 minutes.  Serves 4.
