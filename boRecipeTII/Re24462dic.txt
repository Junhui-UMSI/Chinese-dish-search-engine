Tropical Punch Cooler
Bottle ginger ale or 7-Up, Powdered sugar, Water, (6 oz.) cans frozen orange, Pineapple juice, chilled, (16 oz.) pkg. frozen, 
Preparation : In blender container or food processor bowl with metal blade,  combine 2 tablespoons of the powdered sugar, 3/4 cup of the water, 1  package peach slices and 1 can orange juice concentrate; blend until  smooth.  Pour into 4 quart non-metal container.  Repeat with remaining  powdered sugar, water, peach slices and orange juice concentrate. Add  pineapple juice; blend well.  Refrigerate until serving time.    Just before serving add ginger ale,; stir gently to blend. 30 (1/2 cup)  servings.    Note:  Two 16 ounce cans peach slices in heavy syrup can be substituted for  2 (16 ounce) packages frozen peaches.  Omit powdered sugar.
