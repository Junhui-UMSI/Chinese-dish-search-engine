Chicken and Dressing
Celery, Sage, Boneless chicken breasts;, Flour, Pepper, Condensed cream of mushroom, Butter or margerine; melted, Paprika, Soft bread crumbs, Vegetable oil, Salt, milk, Onions, Salt, Dried thyme leaves, Pepper, 
Heat oil in skillet until hot. Mix flour, 1 tsp. salt, paprika, and 1/4  tsp. pepper in bowl; coat chicken. Cook chicken in hot oil over medium heat  until brown, about 15-20 minutes. Place in ungreased 2 qt. casserole dish.  Pour mushroom soup over chicken. Toss remaining ingredients in bowl. Spread  mixture on chicken. Cover and cook in 350 degree oven for 1 hour.
