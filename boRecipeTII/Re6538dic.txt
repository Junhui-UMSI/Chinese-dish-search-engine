Poached Pears in Vanilla and Champagne
Champagne, Sugar, Water, Vanilla bean, Pears; peeled, (up to 6), 
ESSENCE OF EMERIL SHOW #EE2267    In a sauce pot combine the sugar and water together and boil for 2-3  minutes thus creating a simple syrup. Split the vanilla bean in half,  scraping the inside to remove the pulp. Add the champagne and vanilla to  the simple syrup. Bring to a simmer and add the pears. Poach until tender,  about 6-8 minutes. Remove and cool.    Yield: 4 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Mar 13, 1998
