Zucchini Chutney
Onions, Tamcon, Zucchini, Hing, Green chillies, 
Fry cut zucchini, onions, and green chillies. Add turmeric, salt, cook on  low flame for 5 to 10 minutes. Boil tamcon, add to mix above. Pulverize the  whole thing in blender.    Seasoning:    Thalshi Kottify with mustard seeds, urad dal.      Rajeev Krishnamoorthy, rajeev@twain.EE.CORNELL.EDU
