Penne Pasta with Sun-Dried Tomatoes and Chile
Lemon Peel, Oil from the tomatoes, Parmesan cheese, grated, Cut in slivers, Penne pasta, olive oil, Oil, pitted and halved, Chopped, Garlic, minced, Crushed red chile, Black Pepper, Fresh basil, chopped, Sun-dried tomatoes,, Black olives, cured in, Fresh Italian parsley,, 
Combine all ingredients, except the cheese and pasta, and let sit at room  temperature for a couple of hours to blend the flavors.     Cook the pasta in 4 quarts of boiling salted water until tender but still  firm - al dente.  Drain.     Toss the pasta with the sauce and cheese until well coated and serve.
