Swedish Cabbage Soup
Beef bouillon  , Pepper, Parsnips, Water, Lamb shanks, cabbage, Leeks, Salt, Celery, Whole allspice, Potatoes, Parsley, 
Place lamb shanks in slow-cooking pot with bouillon cubes, pepper, and  salt.  Tie allspice in cheesecloth.  Add allspice, leeks, parsnips,  carrots, celery, potatoes, parsley, and water to pot.  Cover and cook on  low for 7 to 9 hours or until meat is tender.  Remove all spice and meat  from bot.  Cut meat off bones; dice and return to pot.  Skim off fat from  top of soup.  Turn control to high.  Add cabbage.  Cover and cook on high  for 25 to 30 minutes or until cabbage is done.
