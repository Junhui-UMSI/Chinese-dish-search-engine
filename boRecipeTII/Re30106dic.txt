Balsmic-Shallot Marinade
Coarsely chop fresh rosemary, Coarsely chopped shallots, Coarsely chopped fresh sage, Balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Black Pepper, 
In a blender or food processor, combine the vinegar, shallots, sage,  rosemary and pepper.  With the machine on, add the olive oil in a slow,  steady stream and blend until emulsified.    Good on any kind of beefsteak, pork, lamb, chicken:  marinate 2-4 hours    Reprinted from Food and Wine Magazine - June 1996  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #190    Date: Wed, 3 Jul 1996 23:47:30 -0700    From: Julie Bertholf <jewel1@ix.netcom.com>
