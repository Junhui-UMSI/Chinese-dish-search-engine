Banana with Peanut Caramel Cream
C, Bananas, Sour cream, Cream, Brown sugar - firm packed, Lime juice, SAUCE, Oz, Smooth peanut butter, 
Prep time: 5 mins   Cook time: 10 mins   Difficulty: *    Slice bananas and toss in lime juice. Divide evenly between 6 small serving  bowls, spoon cold sauce over top and top with whipped cream.    SAUCE Melt butter in small saucepan. Add sugar and condensed milk. Stir  without boiling until sugar is dissolved then increase heat and stir for  about 5 minutes until golden brown.
