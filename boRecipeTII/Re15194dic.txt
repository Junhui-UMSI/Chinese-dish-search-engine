Whipped Cream 24 Hour Fruit Salad
Marshmallows, Lemon Juice, Eggs, Heavy cream, DRAIN ALL FRUIT, Oranges, Sugar, Marachino Cherries, Crushed Pineapple, Fruit Cocktail, 
Cook beaten eggs, sugar and lemon juice in double boiler until thick and  smooth. Set aside to cool. Add mixture to drained fruit and marshmallows.    Let mixture stand in refrig. for 24 hours. Beat cream and fold into fruit  mixture an hour or so before serving.
