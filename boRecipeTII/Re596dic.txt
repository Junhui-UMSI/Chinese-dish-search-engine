Garlic Beans Three Ways
Fresh green beans, Butter, Garlic, 
Wash and prepare beans.  Place in a saucepan of boiling water; cook for 4  to 6 minutes, until tender-crisp. Drain beans in a sieve. Quickly place pan back on low heat.  Add butter, melt.  Add garlic; then beans. Toss. Serves:  4    To Prepare beans early in day, drain beans after boiling; quickly cool  under cold water.  Drain again and pat dry. At serving time, melt butter in  a fry pan. Add garlic, then beans and cook only until beans are hot.    Add either or both of the following:    4 slices bacon, crisply cooked and crumbled.      1/4    cup pine nuts, toasted    Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Good Friends Cookbook.
