Teriyaki Salmon Steaks
Parsley, FROM CHEF FREDDYS, Peanut oil, garlic, Pepper, Salmon Steaks, Teriyaki sauce, 
1)      Mix the first 5 ingredients in a bowl... then  add the salmon steaks and let sand for 1 to 2 hours,  turning the steaks every 20 to 30 min... 2) Place the  steaks on a sheet of heavy foil... Grill for 4 to 5  min. basting with the sauce... Turn the steaks and  grill for 3 min. or until the steaks flake easily,  basting with the sauce... 3) Serve hot or cold...
