Perfectly Simple Roast Chicken
Flour, Sliced onion, Sliced celery stalks, Salt, pepper & paprika, 7 lb roasting chicken, Cooking bag, 
Preheat oven to 350. Shake flour in bag; place bag in 13 X 9 X 2"  baking pan. Place half the onions & celery in bag. Rinse chicken &  pat dry. Sprinkle chicken with salt & pepper; place remaining  vegetables inside cavity. Sprinkle chicken surface with seasonings.  Place chicken in bag with tie; cut 6 half inch slits in top. Bake  until tender, 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
