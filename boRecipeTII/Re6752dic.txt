Polish Sausage Skillet
Instant rice, Worcestershire Sauce, mushroom stems and tops  , individual tomato, Salt, celery, Boiling water, Green Onions, Pepper, Smoked Polish sausage, 
Combine smoked Polish sausage, onion and celery in large skillet. Cook over  low heat until vegetables are tender-crisp. Add mushrooms, water, salt,  pepper, soup mix and Worcestershire sauce. Heat until bubbling, add rice,  stir and cover. Remove from heat and let stand 10 to 15 minutes. Serves 6.
