Turkey and Ham Croisants
Salt, Margarine; melted, Lemon juice, CURRY SAUCE, Flour, Fresh asparagus -or-, Paprika, Thinly sliced cooked ham, Frozen asparagus (cooked and, Curry powder, Coffee cream or Half and, Sliced Swiss cheese, Thinly sliced cookey turkey, Croisants split, 
Date: Tue, 27 Feb 96 6:10:09 EST    submitted by: 102735.17@compuserve.com  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Arrange Asparagus, ham, turkey and cheese in  Crossant.  Bake for 10 - 15 minutes or until hot.  Serve with Curry Sauce  spooned over.  Sprinkle paprika over top to serve.    Curry Sauce - 1/4 cup of margarine melted, 2 tbls flour, 1/2 tsp curry  powder and 1/4 tsp of salt, add 1 1/2 cups coffee cream or half and half  and cook till thickened (about 5 minutes) Remove from heat. Slowly stir in      1/4    cup lemon juice.
