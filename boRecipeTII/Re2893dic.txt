Peanut Butter Graham Snackers
Butter flavor Crisco, Powdered sugar, Semi-sweet chocolate chips, Creamy peanut butter, Graham cracker crumbs,, Graham cracker crumbs, 
1. Combine Crisco, powdered sugar, and peanut butter in large bowl. Beat at  low speed with electric mixer until well blended. Stir in 1 cup crumbs and  chocolate chips. Cover and refrigerate for one hour.    2. Form dough into 1-inch balls. Roll in 1/2 cup crumbs, peanuts, or  sprinkles for a fancier cookie. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.    Makes about 3 dozen cookies.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 18 Sep 96    From:    Kelly Henry <henry86@SNYCORVA.CORTLAND.EDU>    Date:    Thu, 19 Sep 1996 12:25:38 -0400
