Sweet Potato Gratin with Chiles
Chipotles chiles in adobe sauce, Heavy cream, White pepper, Salt, Unsalted butter, Sweet potatoes, 
Preheat the oven to 350F. Generously butter a shallow 4 qt. casserole. Set  aside. In a blender or food processor, combine the chiles and cream and  blend til smooth. Place a layer of sliced sweet potatoes in the casserole.  Pour some chile cream over the top and season with salt and pepper.  Continue layering until all the poatoes are used, ending with cream. Bake 1  hr. or until the potatoes are tender and the top is lightly browned and  bubbling. Serve hot or at room temperature.
