Tropical Salsa

Peel and segment grapefruit and oranges. Also squeeze the juice. Combine  cut grapefruit, oranges, mango, red onion, peppers, cilantro, salt and oil.  Toss carefully.    Yields 6 servings. Serve over fish, chicken or pork.
