Baked Rigatoni with Sausage
Low-fat milk, Croutons, Italian sausage, Mozzarella cheese, Rigatoni pasta, Parmesan cheese, Dry bread crumbs, All-purpose flour, 
In large skillet, brown sausage 5 minutes; drain fat. Add milk and flour;  mix well. Bring to a boil over medium high heat, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat. Stir in pasta, cheeses, garlic salt and seasoned pepper.    In 1 1/2 quart baking dish, place pasta mixture. Bake in 350F oven,  uncovered, 25 minutes. Sprinkle with bread crumbs (or croutons); place  under broiler just to brown.
