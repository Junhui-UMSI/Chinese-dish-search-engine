Walnut Vinaigrette
Walnut Oil, Balsamic vinegar, Broth, Sugar, Canned No Salt Chicken, Garlic Minced, Pepper, Dijon mustard, Sherry Vinegar, 
Bring Chicken Broth To A Boil in A Small Saucepan. Cook About 6Min. OR  Until Reduced To 2/3 C.  Combine Chicken Broth & Remainingingredients in A  Jar; Cover Tightly & Shake Vigorously. Cover & Chill. (Fat 1.7. Chol. 0.)
