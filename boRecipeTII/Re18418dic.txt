Vichyssoise Soup (Usually Served Cold)
Peeled; cubed potato, Low-fat sour cream, Fresh chives; (optional), Ground white pepper, Onions, Dried whole basil, Salt, Canned low-sodium chicken, Evaporated skimmed milk; (12, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in a large saucepan. Bring mixture to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes or until vegetables are tender.  Position knife blade in food processor bowl; add hot vegetable mixture.  Process 1 minute or until smooth. Transfer pureed mixture to a large bowl;  stir in milk. Cover and chill 8 hours.    Ladle soup into individual bowls. Top each serving with 1 tablespoon  low-fat sour cream; garnish with fresh chives, if desired.    For a time-saving option, omit the chilling procedure, and serve this reamy  potato soup warm.        126    calories      2.1 g  fat  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 410 by "Diane Geary."  <diane@keyway.net> on Dec 28, 1997
