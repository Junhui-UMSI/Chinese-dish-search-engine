Pecan Pie
Corn meal, Pie shell, Sugar, Butter; stick, Karo; white, Salt; pinch, Egg, Vanilla, Pecans, 
Calories     per serving: 250 Fat grams    per serving: 30 Approx. Cook  Time: 1:30 Cholesterol  per serving: 20 Add all ingredients together in  bowl, and mix well. Pour into pie shell, and bake 1 hour at 300 degrees.  Allow to cool and serve.
