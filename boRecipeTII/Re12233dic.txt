Zucchini Cookies
Sugar, Butter, Pecans, Grated zucchini, Baking soda, Of flour, Cinnamon, Raisins, Cloves, Egg, Salt, 
Cream sugar, butter and egg until fluffy and well blended. Sift dry  ingredients together and add to the mixture with grated zucchini, blending  well.  Stir in raisins and nuts. Drop by teaspoonful onto greased cookie  sheet. Bake at 375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes or until done. Do not  overbake.  Makes 3 dozen cookies.    Randy Rigg
