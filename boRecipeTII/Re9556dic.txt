Pumpkin Chutney
Cloves, White vinegar, Non-nutritive equivalent to, Bell peppers; chopped fine, Ginger, Pumpkin (fresh if possible), Salt, White rasins, Allspice, 
Combine all ingredients in large pan; bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium,  cook unitl pumpkin is smooth and mixture is slighty thick (about an hour).  Spoon into three clear 1/2 pint jares and seal inmediately.
