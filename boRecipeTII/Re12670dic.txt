Yogurt-Cucumber Salad
Dry mustard, Cucumbers, Yogurt, Vinegar, white, Dill, Salt, Pepper, black, Onions, 
Coarsely chop cucumbers. Combine cucumber and onion; set aside. In small  bowl, stir together yogurt, seasonings, and vinegar; beat lightly with wire  whisk until smooth. Stir 1/2 cup yogurt dressing into pasta mixture and  toss well. Cover and chill, stirring occasionally. May add 1 c cooked  macaroni if desired.
