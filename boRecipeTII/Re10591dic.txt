Quick Overnight Sourdough Starter
Yeast; plus, Lukewarm water, Yeast, Flour, all-purpose, 
Recipe by: FWDS07A    Patricia Dwigans Dissolve yeast in a small amount of  lukewarm water. Stir flour into remaining water and add yeast mixture. mix  well and cover. Let mixture stand in a draft free area that is near 85 for  al least 6 hours or overnight. Starter is now ready to be mixed with other  ingredients for your favorite sourdough recipe
