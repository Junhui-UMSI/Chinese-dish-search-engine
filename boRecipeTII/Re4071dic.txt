Peppermint Snowballs
Finely crushed peppermint, Powdered sugar, Butter, Powdered sugar, Red food coloring, Finely crushed peppermint, Flour, Powdered sugar, Vanilla, milk, Cream cheese, 
From: arielle@taronga.com (Stephanie da Silva) (COLLECTION)    Date: Wed, 2 Mar 1994 23:07:59 GMT  See recipe "Grandma Olsens Sugar Cookies" for source/info on this recipe.    Combine room temp cheese & milk and add 1/2 cup powdered sugar slowly. Stir  in 2 tabelspoons candy & food coloring, set aside for filling. Cream  butter, 1/2 cup powdered sugar, mix in vanilla, then flour. Knead dough  into balls, make a deep well in center of each ball with handle of wooden  spoon. Fill with 1/4 tsp of filling, shape a scant 1/4 tsp of dough into a  flat round, lay on top of filling; press gently to seal. Place on ungreased  cookie sheet, bake 350F for 12 minutes. Roll warm cookies in combined 1/4  cup powdered sugar & 6 tablespoons peppermint candy. Makes 4 dozen.    Mary    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.
