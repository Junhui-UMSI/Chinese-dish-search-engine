Strawberry Surprise Muffins
Oat bran, Sugar, Flour, Baking Powder, Melted butter or margarine, SURPRISE, WET MIX, Strawberry jam, DRY MIX, Yoghurt, Sliced almonds, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 400F, prepare pans. Combine wet mix ingredients & mix  well. Add the dry mix to the wet mix until just combined. Spoon half of the  batter into each pan, put a little strawberry jam into the centre of each  muffin & top off with remaining batter. Scatter a few extra sliced almonds  on top if desired & bake for 15-20 mins. Makes 12.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
