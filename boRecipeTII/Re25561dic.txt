Avocado Gazpacho
Tabasco sauce; or to taste, Red onion, Chicken broth, Fresh cilantro sprigs; for, Fresh cilantro or parsley;, Cucumbers; peeled, Firm/ripe avocados, Lime juice, Salt, 
Cut one cucumber in chunks and put in the blender; whirl with 2 cups of the  chicken broth, cilantro, lime juice, salt and Tabasco until smoothly  pureed. Peel, pit and coarsely chop avocados. Put into a food processor and  add the pureed mixture and the rest of the chicken broth. Whirl very  briefly to coarsely puree the avocado, leaving some chunks. Cover and chill  at least 2 hours or overnight. Just before serving, finely mince the red  onion; seed and chop the second cucumber. Pour soup into 6 bowls and  garnish each with a sprig of fresh cilantro. Offer the minced onion and  chopped cucumber on the side to add individually to taste.
