Whole-Wheat Challah
Vegetable oil, Glaze:, Whole-Wheat Flour; To 13 C, Cornmeal, Warm water, Honey, Egg, Dry yeast, Coarse salt, Poppy Seeds, Eggs, 
Makes 4-6 loaves or 4 loaves and 12 rolls    	Pour water into large mixing bowl.  Add yeast and stir.  Add oil, honey  and eggs.  Stir in about 5 cups flour.    	Let mixture rest about 45 minutes to 1 hour until bubbles.    	Add salt and stir in more of remaining flour.  Knead 7-10 minutes.  Shape  into loaves and rolls. Place in pans and let rise 45 minutes to 1 hour.    	Preheat oven to 350.  Brush loaves with glaze and sprinkle with seeds.  Bake 45-50 minutes for loaves, 30 minutes for rolls.
