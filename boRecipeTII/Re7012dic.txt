Popcorn--Border Corn
Unpopped corn, Chili powder, Monterey Jack Cheese, Ground cumin, Paprika, 
Pop popcorn.  Mix spices into the shredded cheese.  Sprinkle mixture over  unseasoned popcorn and toss until well blended.
