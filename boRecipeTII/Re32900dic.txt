Sweet Relish
Sugar, Mustard seed, Celery seed, Ground cabbage, Ground onion, Red peppers; ground, Water, Green peppers; ground, Salt, Vinegar, Turmeric, 
This is one of my Grandmothers recipies that survived. It is about 100  years old.    Place veg in large kettle. Pour salt over. Add enough water to cover. Let  stand overnight. Drain and cover with water and drain again. Mix remaining  ingredients . Simmer 15 min. Pack in nars. Water bath for 10 min.
