Lasagne
Salt, Crushed basil, Cottage cheese, Grated Parmesan cheese, Salt, Crushed oregano, Lasagna noodles; cooked and, Mozzarella cheese; sliced, Lean ground beef, Lean ground pork, Small onion; chopped, Garlic, Can tomatoes; cut up, Can tomato sauce, Beef bouillon cubes; crushed, Parsley flakes, Sugar, 
In large skillet or slow-cooking pot with browning unit, cook and stir  beef, pork, onion, and garlic. Drain off excess fat. In slow-cooking pot,  combine drained beef, pork, onion, and garlic with tomatoes, tomato sauce,  bouillon cube, parsley, sugar, 1/2 tsp. salt, and basil. Cover and cook on  low for 6 to 8 hours. Mix cottage cheese, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, 1/2 tsp.  salt, and oregano. In 13 x 9-inch pan, layer half the cooked noodles,  sauce, mozzarella cheese, and cottage cheese mixture; repeat, reserving  enough sauce for layer on top. Sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Bake  in 350 F oven for 45 minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 268 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 17, 1997
