Potatoes Dauphinoise
Potatoes, Salt, pepper, nutmeg, Butter, Garlic, milk, -(up to), Gruyre cheese, Onion, Egg, 
From: Felicia Pickering <MNHAN063@SIVM.SI.EDU>    Date: Thu, 4 Jul 1996 19:05:52 EDT  Preheat oven to 350 F. Rub the interior of an ovenproof baking dish with  the cut side of the garlic clove, then generously with butter. Scald the  milk with the onion slices. Extract the onion and add the slightly beaten  egg, stirring until well incorporated. Season with salt, pepper, and a dash  of nutmeg. Peel the potatoes and slice them thinly into the vegetable dish.  Pour the milk and egg mixture over the potatoes. Cover with the grated  cheese and dot with the remaining butter. Bake 45 minutes. Serves 4.    EAT-L Digest  3 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
