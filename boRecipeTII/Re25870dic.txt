Bacardi Rum Cake From Loren Martin
Cake:, Wesson oil, Dark rum (80 proof), Pecans, 18.5 oz yellow cake mix, Butter, Sugar, Vanilla pudding mix, Cold water, 3.75 oz pkg jello instant, Dark rum (80 proof), Water, Glaze:, Eggs, 
Sprinkle nuts in bottom of bundt pan. Mix all cake ingredients together.  Pour over nuts.  Bake 1 hour at 325 F.  Cool. Invert on serving plate.  Prick top and drizzle and smooth glaze evely over top and sides. Allow cake  to absorb glaze.  Repeat until glaze is used up. Glaze: Melt butter in  sauce pan.  Stir in water and sugar. Boil 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and stir in rum.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #015 by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Jan  14, 98
