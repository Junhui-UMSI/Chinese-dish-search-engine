Tangy Raspberry Freeze
Raspberries; thawed, Sugar, Light corn syrup, Cornstarch, Evap. lowfat milk (12-oz.), Fresh or frozen unsweetened, Egg, Buttermilk, 
In medium saucepan, combine sugar and cornstarch. Stir in evaporated milk  and corn syrup.  Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture is bubbly;  remove from heat. Stir half of hot milk mixture into beaten egg. Stirring  constantly, pour egg mixture into remaining hot milk mixture. Return  mixture to saucepan; cook and stir over low heat until mixture coats a  spoon and is slightly thickened. Set aside to cool. In blender or food  processor fitted with metal blade, puree berries. Strain and discard seeds.  Add berry juice to cooked mixture; stir in buttermilk. Pour into ice cream  canister.  Freeze in ice cream maker according to manufacturers  directions.  Makes about 1 quart.    Per Serving: 150 calories - 5 g. protein - 31 g. carb. ~ 1 g. fat - 29 mg.  chol. - 171 mg. calcium.
