Black Bean Salsa
Tomatoes, Green bell pepper, Bunch of cilantro, Chili powder, Salt, Lime juice, Dried black beans, Red bell pepper, Onion, medium, olive oil, 
Soak and cook black beans per package directions.  Drain, rinse, and cool.  Chop tomatoes in 3/4" dice. Chop bell peppers and onion in 1/2" dice.  Finely chop cilantro. Mix beans, chopped vegetables, and remaining  ingredients in a large bowl. Chill in refrigerator for 24 hours. Adjust  seasoning, salt and chili powder.  Add a little hot sauce if desired. Serve  cold, either as a salad item or with tortilla chips.    Recipe by Richard Douville    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Aug 19, 98
