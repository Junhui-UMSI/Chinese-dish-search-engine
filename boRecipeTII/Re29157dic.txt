Baked Salmon
salmon steaks, Lemon juice, Dried whole dill weed, Pepper, Vegetable cooking spray, Patricia McKenna-BJDJ46B, Dried parsley flakes, 
Rinse steaks thoroughly in cold water; pat dry with paper towels. Place  steaks in a shallow baking pan coated with cooking spray; brush with lemon  juice. Sprinkle evenly with dill weed, parsley, and pepper. Bake at 350  degrees F. for 25 to 35 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested  with a fork. PER SERVING: 1 steak   calories - 92, carbohydrate - 1 g.,  protein 15 g., fat - 3 g., cholesterol - 26 mg., fiber - 0, sodium - 50 mg.
