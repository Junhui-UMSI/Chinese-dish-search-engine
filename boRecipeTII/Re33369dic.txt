Basic Wheat Bread
Yeast; bread machine type, FOR 1 1/2 POUND LOAF, Salt, Sugar, Butter or margarine, Bread flour, Whole wheat flour, Vital Gluten, + 2 T. water, Nonfat dry milk powder, 
Add ingredients to machine in order suggested by manufacturer. Process on  White bread cycle. Can do in timed baking cycle. Process on Light setting.  MC formatting by bobbi744@sojourn.com    NOTES :  Makes a good sized loaf. Can add 1/2 tsp. dried herbs with the  flour.
