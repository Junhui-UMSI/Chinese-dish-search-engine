Balsamic House Dressing
Black Pepper, Fresh rosemary; marjoram,, olive oil, Garlic, Balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, Salt, 
Blend vinegar, mustard, garlic, salt, pepper and herbs together. Whisk in  olive oil. Let stand in refrigerator for 30 mins. before serving. Will keep  for about 1 week. Makes 2 cups.
