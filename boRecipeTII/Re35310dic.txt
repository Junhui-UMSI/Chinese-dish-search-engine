Beef Bourgiunon
Red wine, Black pepper, Mushrooms; sliced, Lean beef; cubed, Bacon; diced, Flour, Garlic, Thyme, Onion; diced, Vegetable oil, 
Marinate beef in wine ,oil, thyme and pepper 4 hours at room temperature or  overnight in refrigerator. In large pan, cook bacon until soft.  Add garlic  and onion; saute until soft.  Add mushrooms and cook until slightly wilted.  Drain beef, reserving liquid.  Place beef in slow cooker.  Sprinkle flour  over the beef, stirring until well coated.  Add mushroom mixture.  Pour  reserved marinade over all. Cook on LOW 8-9 hrs.
