Sweet Smoked Bourbon Ribs
Soy sauce, Onion Power, Bourbon, MARINATE, Lemon , Juice Of, Coarse black pepper, RIBS, Brown Sugar, Slab Pork Spare Ribs, Salt, Beer, Garlic Juice, 
In a sauce pan combine all ingredients and bring to a boil. Let marinate  cool. Marinate ribs for 6 to 24 hours, the longer the better.  Take ribs out of marinate and BBQ your favorite way.
