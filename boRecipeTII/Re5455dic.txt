Pineapple Cream Pie
Plain unflavored gelatin com, Pineapple juice, Whipping cream, 3 tbsp. cold water (Let stan, Large eggs,separated, #2 crushed pineapple,straine, Sugar, 10" baked pie shell, 
Combine 1/2 cup sugar with pineapple juice and slightly beaten egg  yolks.Cook over very low heat until sugar is completely dissolved and  custard coats spoon.Do not allow to boil;just  simmer.Stir,continuously,while cooking.Add gelatin mixture to very hot  custard and keep over low heat,stirring constantly,until completely  melted.Remove from heat and pour into a large bowl. Add strained crushed  pineapple to hot custard and blend well.     Beat egg whites until they just cling to bowl,add other 1/2 cup  sugar;beat only until blended.Pour egg whites,all at once, into hot custard  and fold until well blended.     Pour into cold baked pie shell.Let stand until cool,then put in  refrigerator for several hours until cold.beat 1 cup whipping cream and put  on pie.Serves 8.
