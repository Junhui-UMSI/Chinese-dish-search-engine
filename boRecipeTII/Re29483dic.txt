Baking Powder Biscuits and Buttermilk Biscuits
All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Shortening, milk, 
BAKING POWDER BISCUITS: In a medium mixing bowl stir together flour, baking  powder, and salt. Using a pastry blender, cut in shortening till mixture  resembles coarse crumbs. Make a well in the center. Add milk all at once.    Using a fork, stir just till moistened. Turn dough out onto a lightly  floured surface. Quickly knead dough by gently folding and pressing dough  10 to 12 strokes or till nearly smooth. Pat or lightly roll dough to  1/2-inch thickness. Cut dough with a 2 1/2-inch biscuit cutter Hint: (if  you dont have a biscuit cutter, a glass works well to cut biscuits).   Place  biscuits 1 inch apart on a ungreased baking sheet. Bake in a 450 oven for  10 to 12 minutes or till golden. Remove from baking sheet; serve warm.  Makes 10 biscuits.      DROP BISCUITS: To make drop biscuits: Prepare as above, except increase  milk to 1 cup. Do not knead or roll or cut dough. Using a big spoon drop  dough into 12 mounds on a greased baking sheet. Bake as directed above.  Makes 12 biscuits.      BUTTERMILK BISCUITS: Prepare as above, except stir 1/4 teaspoon baking soda  into dry mixture and substitute buttermilk or sour milk for the milk Bake  as directed above. Makes 10 to 12 biscuits.
