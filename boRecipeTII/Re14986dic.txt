30-Minute Chili Ole
Cloves garlic -- minced, Rinsed/drained, Shredded cheese, Can stewed tomatoes, Can black beans, Garnish, Vegetable oil, Ground beef, Onion -- chopped, Can corn -- drained, Sour cream and, Chili powder, Can tomato sauce, Ground cumin, A-1 Steak Sauce, Chopped tomato -- for, 
In 6 qt. heavy pot, over medium-hight heat, saute onion and garlic in oil  until tender. Add beef; cook and stir until browned. Drain; stir in tomato  sauce, stewed tomatoes, steak sauce, chili powder and cumin. Heat to a  boil; reduce heat to low. Cover; simmer for 10 minutes, stirring  occasionally.   Stir in beans and corn; simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.  Serve hot; garnish with cheese, sour cream and tomato.
