Barbecued Beef in Crusty Rolls
Dash Pepper, French Rolls, Split, Worcestershire Sauce, Catsup, Slices American Cheese, Cut-up Cooked Beef, Dry mustard, Vinegar, Finely Chopped Garlic Clove, Brown sugar, Onion, 
Mix all ingredients except beef, rolls and cheese in 1-Qt casserole.  Cover  and microwave on high (100%) util hot and bubbly, 2 to 3 minutes; stir.  Mix beef into sauce.  Cover and microwave until beef is hot, 3 to 5 minutes  Fill rolls with beef mixture and cheese slices and serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/meats.zip
