White Polenta
White cornmeal, Water, Salt, Clove garlic, crushed, 
Combine cornmeal and salt in a large saucepan. Gradually add water and  garlic, stirring constantly with a wire whisk. Bring to a boil; reduce heat  to medium-low. Cook, uncovered, 15 minutes or until thickened, stirring  frequently. Yield: 9 servings (serving size: 1/2 cup).    Per serving: 85 Calories; 0g Fat (4% calories from fat); 2g Protein; 18g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 182mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve warm.
