Pepperoni Dipping Sauce
Homemade Pizza Sauce, Pcs Parmesan Bread Sticks, Onions, chopped, Reduced fat turkey, Italian seasoning, Garlic, Bell peppers, chopped, 
In a saucepan, combine sauce, pepperoni, onions, bell peppers, garlic, and  Italian seasoning. Mix well. Cook over medium heat until hot.    Per serving: 24 Calories; 1g Fat (21% calories from fat); 2g Protein; 3g  Carbohydrate; 3mg Cholesterol; 119mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve warm with bread sticks.    NOTES : Yields: 1 1/2 cups (twenty-four 1 tablespoon servings).    Recipe by: Pep It Up With Hormel Italian Meats Posted to EAT-L Digest 11  Apr 97 by "Anita A.
