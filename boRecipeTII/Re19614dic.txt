Anne Rosss Brunch Casserole
Bulk sausage, Eggs, milk, sharp cheddar cheese, Salt, White bread, Dry mustard, 
Crumble, saute and drain sausage.  Beat eggs with milk, salt and  mustard. Layer bread cubes, sausage and cheese in a lightly greased 9x13  pan.  Pour egg mixture over, cover and refrigerate overnight. Bake at 350  degrees for 45 minutes.
