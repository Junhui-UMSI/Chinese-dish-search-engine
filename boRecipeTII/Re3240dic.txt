Pear Fans
Vanilla sauce (see recipe), Canned pear halves; drained, Cinnamon stick; cut into, Berry cream sauce (see, Mint leaves (optional), 
SLICE pears lengthwise, cutting almost through stem ends.  Place on  individual serving plates; spread to form fans. Spoon Vanilla Sauce around  pears. Swirl Berry Cream Sauce through Vanilla Sauce to form design (see  "Fruit in Cream" for directions). Place cinnamon stick and mint leaf at  stem end of each pear, if desired.
