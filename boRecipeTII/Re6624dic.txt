Poires Flambees
Ice cream, Sugar, Shredded coconut, Butterscotch topping, Napoleon Brandy; heated, Fresh pears; peeled, halved, Butter, Eggs, Flour, 
Sprinkle pear halves with sugar, dip in flour, then beaten eggs & again in  flour. Melt butter in a large saute or presentation pan & in it lightly  brown the pears. Pears should be cooked through, but still firm. Place on  warmed serving plates. Blend butterscotch with butter remaining in the pan  until creamy. Add warmed brandy & ignite. Place 1 scoop of ice cream in  each dessert dish. Top with pear half & ladle over 1/3 cup butterscotch  sauce. Garnish with a sprinkling of coconut.    THE QUORUM    EAST COLFAX, DENVER.
