Baked Tuna Chow Mein
(10 1/2 oz. ) cream of, Accent, Onions, Tuna (reg. size), Butter, Celery, Pepper, Green Pepper, Chow mein noodles, Salted cashew nuts, 
Heat oven to 350 F. saute celery, onions, green pepper in butter. Mix in  thinned soup, tuna and 1 cup noodles, cashews and seasoning. Pour in  buttered 1 1/2 qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with rest of 1/3 cup chow mein  noodles. Bake 30 mins. Serves 4-6 Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #178 by  "William & Evelyn Hall" <wchall@nevia.net> on Aug 2, 1997
