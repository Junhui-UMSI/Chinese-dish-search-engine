Bbq Sauce
Worcestershire Sauce, Beer; cooks choice, Brown sugar, Pepper, Chili Sauce; Heinz, Vinegar, Butter; 1 stick, Onion, YOUR LINK TO THE PHILLY.INQU, Lemon; juice, Water, Garlic Clove; finely chopped, Mustard, 
In a large saucepan, saute onion and garlic in butter. When the onions  are transparent add the remainder of ingredients. Bring to a boil. Simmer  until your grill is ready, about 10 to 15 minutes or until you cant stand  the wonderful smell of the sauce any longer. Now let me tell you, this is  what I call barbecue sauce.
