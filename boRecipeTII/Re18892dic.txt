Angel Cloud Cookies
Egg whites; stiffly beaten, Dash salt, Almonds; chopped, Chocolate chips or carob chi, Sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Turn off when putting in  cookies. Fold sugar, salt, nuts and chips into egg whites. Drop by the  teaspoonful onto a well greased cookie sheet. Leave in the oven for 2 1/2  hours or overnight, but do not open oven door until time to remove.
