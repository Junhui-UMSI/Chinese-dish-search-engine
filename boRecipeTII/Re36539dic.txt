Strawberries with Molasses Sour Cream Sauce
Sour cream, Sugar, Unsulfered molasses, Strawberries, hulled, and if, Vanilla, 
In a small bowl whisk together sour cream, sugar, vanilla and 2 tablespoons  molasses until smooth. Divide strawberries between 4 bowls and top with  sour cream sauce. Drizzle remaining molasses over servings.
