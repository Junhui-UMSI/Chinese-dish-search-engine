Quick n Easy Salmon Patties
Baking Powder, Onion, Crisco, Egg, pink salmon, Flour, 
Drain salmon; set aside 2 tbsp of the juice. In a medium mixing bowl, mix  salmon, egg and onion until sticky. Stir in flour. Add baking powder to  salmon juice; stir into salmon mixture. Form into small patties and fry  until golden brown (about 5 minutes) in hot Crisco. Serve with tartar sauce  or Caesar salad dressing.
