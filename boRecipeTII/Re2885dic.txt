Peanut Butter Fudge #13
Powdered sugar, Margarine, Vanilla, Plus, Peanut butter, Salt, Light corn syrup, 
Cream margarine until soft.  Blend in corn syrup; add peanut butter, salt  and vanilla.  Add powdered sugar.  Put mixture onto cutting board and  knead, making sure it is well blended. Form mixture into a square, and cut  into small pieces. No baking needed.
