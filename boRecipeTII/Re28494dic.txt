Terrys Taco Dip
Sour cream, Lettuce -- shredded, Tabasco Sauce -- to taste, Refried Beans, Tomatoes -- diced, Chili Powder -- to taste, Cream cheese, Taco Seasoning Mix, Cheddar Cheese -- shredded, Black Olives -- sliced, 
Layer the following in a glass casserole dish, in the order given:    1.  Heat refried beans in microwave for 2 minutes.  Blend in chili powder  and tobasco.  Blended until smooth.    2.  Blended together:  cream cheese, sour cream, and taco seasoning.    3.  Shredded lettuce.    4.  Shredded cheddar cheese, diced tomatoes, and black olives.
