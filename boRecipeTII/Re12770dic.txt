Yucca Root Soup or Manioc Soup
Cover the veggies), salt and pepper, Yucca root, peeled and cut, In some of the broth, Into pieces, Water (more if needed to, Organic Gourmet veggie, Onions, chopped and sauted, Broth, 
Boil the yucca root pieces in the broth until tender (about 20 minutes)  along with the sauted onion pieces.    When tender, put into the blender a portion at a time.    It thickens by itself.  Add salt and pepper to taste.    It made about 4-5 good sized bowls of soup.
