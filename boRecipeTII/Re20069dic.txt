Vanilla Creme Filling
Eggs, Whipped topping, thawed, Vanilla, Sugar, Container (8 oz) frozen, Cornstarch, Salt, milk, 
In a saucepan, slowly bring 3 cups of the milk and sugar to a boil.  Combine the remaining 1 cup of milk, eggs and, cornstarch add to milk  sugar mixture, stirring until thicken. Mix in salt and vanilla. Cool  in refrigerator. Fold in whipped cream. Fill Puff Shells.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
