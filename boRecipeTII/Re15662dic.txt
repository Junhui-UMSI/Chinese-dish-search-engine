A la Recherche De Lorange Perdue
Orange Bitters, Vermouth, dry, white, Sweetened Lime Juice, Juice from lime, Orange liqueur, Orange Marmalade, Orange; quartered, Rum, jamaican, dark, Ice Cubes, 
Place all ingredients in jar of electric blender and blend 20 seconds.  Strain through a sieve into a pitcher. Cover and refrigerate until serving  time. Stir before serving, and pour into chilled cocktail glasses.
