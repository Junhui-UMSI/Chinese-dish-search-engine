Zupa Migdalowa
Almond extract, Almonds (blanched),, Rice, cooked, Sugar, milk, Raisins or currants, 
Heat milk just to simmering in a large saucepan.  Add all the ingredients;  stir until well mixed.  Cook over low heat 3 to 5 minutes.    Serve hot as is traditional for Christmas, or chill before serving.    NOTES:    *  Almond Soup -- This recipe could be used as part of a 12-course meal  known in Polish as Wigilia, or on its own. Wigilia is eaten after sundown  on Christmas Eve.
