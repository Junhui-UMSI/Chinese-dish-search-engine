Poultry Seasoning Butter
Fresh chopped oregano, Fresh chopped lemon thyme, at room temp., Fresh chopped rosemary, A little salt and pepper, Unsalted butter, Fresh chopped sage, To 2 pressed garlic cloves, 
Blend all ingredients thoroughly.    From Lynn Alleys "Herbs in Butter" article in "The Herb Companion."  April/May 1993, Vol. 5, No. 4.  Pg. 47. Posted by Cathy Harned.
