Banana Oatmeal Cookies
Flour, Eggs, bananas, Nutmeg, shortening, Rolled oats, Salt, Cinnamon, Sugar, Baking soda, Baking Powder, 
1. Sift together flour, baking powder, soda, salt, spices and sugar into a  bowl.    2. Add shortening, eggs and and half the bananas.    3. Beat until creamy, about 2 minutes.    4. Fold in remaining bananas and rolled oats.    5. Drop from a teaspoon onto greased cookie sheet.    6. Bake in a moderate oven (375 (F.) 12 to 15 minutes.    7. Makes about 4 dozen.
