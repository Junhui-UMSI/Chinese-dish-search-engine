Thanksgiving Fruit Salad
Sugar, Coarsely ground fresh, Finely ground orange with, (3-oz) cherry gelatin, Salt, Cold water, Pineapple tidbits, Walnuts, Hot water, Lemon juice, Orange sections, halved, Celery, 
1. Drain pineapple, reserving 1/3 cup juice.    2. Dissolve gelatin in hot water.  Stir in cold water, reserved pineapple  juice, lemon juice and salt.  Chill until partially set.    3. Meanwhile, stir sugar into ground cranberries and orange.    4. Stir ground fruit mixture, pineapple, orange sections, celery and  walnuts into partially set gelatin.    5. Pour into mold and chill until set.    Variations: Add 1 cup red grapes to the partially set gelatin.  or  Substitute strawberry gelatin for cherry.  Omit celery and use only 1/4 cup  sugar.
