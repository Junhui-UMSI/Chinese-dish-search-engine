Zesty Bread
Italian seasoning, Warm water, All purpose flour, Shortening, Margarine; softened, Salt, Parmesan cheese, Yeast, 
Directions : in medium bowl, combine all bread dough ingredients. roll  dough out kitchen counter. make the dough fairly thin. take a pizza cutter  and cut the dough into strips. then cut the strips in half. in a small  bowl, combine all ingredients of topping. take the bread strips and twist  in the middle then lay them on a cookie sheet. take a basting brush and dip  it into the topping mix and brush each piece of bread real good. place into  oven and bake for 25-30 min. at 400 degrees.
