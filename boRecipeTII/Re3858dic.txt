Pepper Beef Kabobs
Vegetable oil, Dijon mustard, Mushrooms, Beef Sirloin Steak, Bell Pepper, Dried Oregano, Fresh Lemon Juice, Water, Honey, Pepper, 
Cut steak into 1 inch pieces.  Whisk together oil, lemon juice, water,  mustard, honey, oregano, and pepper in large bowl; add beef, bell pepper,  and mushrooms, stirring to coat.  Alternately thread pieces of beef, bell  pepper, and nushrooms on each of 4 12-inch skewers.  Place kebabs on rack  in broiler pan so surface of meat is 3 to 4 inches from heat.  Broil 9 to  12 minutes for rare to medium, turning occasionally.  Season with salt, if  desired.    Kababs may also be grilled over medium coals for 8 to 11 minutes.    Yield: 4 servings.
