Baked Cocoa Custard
Eggs -- slightly beaten, Vanilla, milk, Cocoa Powder, Sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350.    In a medium bowl, combine eggs and vanilla.  In a small bowl, combine sugar  and cocoa.  Pour sugar and  cocoa mixture into eggs.  Combine with wire  whisk.  Add milk.  Pour into custard cups.  Place cups in a 13 x 9 baking  pan.  Pour water to 1 inch deep in pan.  Bake one hour or until a knife  inserted into custard comes out clean.    Makes 6.
