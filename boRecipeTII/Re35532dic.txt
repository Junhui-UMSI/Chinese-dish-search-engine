Strawberry-Rhubarb Pie #3
Sugar, Minute Tapioca, Strawberries, Rhubarb; sliced, 9" Pie Crust and lid, Lemon juice, 
Stir all ingredients together, let stand 15 min. and then place in an  unbaked 9 inch pie crust. Cover the pie with a top crust or a sweet crumb  mixture. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes; reduce heat to 350 and bake a  further 40 to 45 minutes.
