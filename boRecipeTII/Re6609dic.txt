Podina Chati (Mint Chutney)
Clove garlic, finely, Salt, Water, Mint leaves, finely chopped, Sugar, Garam masala, ground, Lemon juice, Green onions, finely, Chopped, Green chiles, finely, Chopped, Chopped, 
Seed chiles. Put all ingredients in blender or food processor and  blend until smooth.  Place in small dish. Chill. Serve with water  crackers, low fat crackers (e.g. Cracked Pepper Snackwells), low fat  white corn chips, or pappadams (crisp Indian bread).
