Sweet-Sour Carrots
Onion, lg, diced, Salt, Dry mustard, Vinegar, Sugar, Green pepper, lg, diced, Pepper, Frozen sliced carrots, Vegetable oil, Condensed tomato soup, 
Cook 1 bag of frozen sliced carrots in salted water until just tender.  Combine with green peppers and onions. Simmer remaining ingredients  together until flavors blend. Pour over carrot mixture. Serve hot, or may  be chilled and served as a relish. Will keep, covered, in refrigerator  several days.    Mrs. Harold T.
