A Ns Slow Shoulder of Lamb
White wine, Shallots, Salt, Tomatoes; quartered, Tomato puree, Black peppercorns, Branches rosemary, Haricots verts, Shoulder lamb; about 4, Onions, Cloves garlic peeled; whole, Bay Leaves, 
Put shoulder into large ovenware pot. Cover with haricots verts, the  liquid, the wine, onions and shallots roughly chopped, the tomatoes, tomato  puree, the garlic, crushed peppercorns and salt to taste. Tuck in the bay  leaves and rosemary. Cover the casserole and cook for 4 hours in a 275  degree oven, then increase the temperature to 425 degrees for 1 more hour.  Have a look at it now and then to make sure it is not drying out. At the  end of cooking the meat will fall off the bone. Serve in soup plates with  bread and salad.
