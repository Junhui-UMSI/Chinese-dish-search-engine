Texas Syyle Bbq Sauce
Pepper, Bay leaf -- crushed, Cloves garlic -- minced, Ketchup, Worcestershire Sauce, Juice, Onion -- grated, Salt, Lemon, Butter, Vinegar, Dry mustard, Water, Cayenne, Chili powder, Sugar, 
Melt butter. Add vinegar, water, ketup, worcestershire and lemon juice. Mix  mustard, chili powder, cayenne, pepper, salt, sugar and bay leaves. Add to  other mixture. Add garlic and onion. Cook together for about 10 min. makes  about 2 cups, enough to swab 4-5 pounds of meat.
