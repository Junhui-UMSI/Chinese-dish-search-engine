Baked Onions
zest of 1 lemon, Pepper, Port wine, olive oil, Cider vinegar, Raisins, Brown sugar, Red onions, 
In large saucepan, cover onions and lemon rind with water; bring to boil  and boil for 5 minutes. Drain; refresh under cold running water and drain  again. Discard rind. Peel onions and arrange in 13 x 9 inch casserole dish;  pour olive oil over.    Bake, uncovered in 375=F8F oven, basting occasionally, for 40 minutes. Add  port, vinegar, raisins, sugar, and pepper to taste. Bake for 30 minutes  longer or until juices are syrupy. Makes 12 servings. Source: Canadian  Living Magazine ch.
