1800 Beaten Biscuits
Sugar, Ice water, Shortening, Flour, Salt, 
Mix the flour and salt and sugar together. Cut in the shortening and add  enough ice water to make a stiff dough. Beat your dough with a mallet until  it is smooth, folding frequently. Takes about 20 mins then roll out the  dough, cut with a small cookie cutter or tin can rim. Bake these for 30  mins in a 375 degree oven. If desired dust these with some powdered sugar.  Makes 20 small biscuits or cookies.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 254 by "janet toomey"  <lovefd@hotmail.com> on Nov 13, 1997
