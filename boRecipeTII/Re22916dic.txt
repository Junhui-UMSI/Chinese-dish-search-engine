Arcadian Shepherds Pie
Butter, Tomato catsup, Cream, Butter, Beef stock, Potatoes peeled, boiled mash, Garlic powder, Ground beef, Eggs, Worcestershire Sauce, Onions, Minced, Parmesan cheese, 
Brown beef and saute onions in butter untill golden. Mix meat with onions,  catsup, Worcestershire, salt & pepper. Add a litter meat stock and cook,  covered, over low heat for 15 to 20 minutes. Beat egg yolks untill light,  whites untill stiff.  Beat yolks, cream, butter, and garlic powder into  mashed potatoes. Gently fold in beaten egg whites. Put meat mixture into  casserole and top with potatoes.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and bake at  350F. untill puffed and browned.
