Whiskey Hill Crab Chowder
Sprigs fresh or 1 teaspoon, Ripe medium sized tomatoes;, Butter, salt and pepper, Tabasco, Lump Crabmeat; picked, Chicken broth, Kernels from 3 ears of white, Onion chopped, Fresh chopped parsley or, 
In a large saucepan, saute the onion in butter until transparent. Add  tomatoes, cover pan and simmer for approximately 5 minutes. Add chicken  broth and thyme and bring to a boil. Stir in corn, Crabmeat and Tabasco,  cover and simmer gently for 5 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste. Simmer  uncovered for an additional 5 to 10 minutes. Sprinkle with chopped parsley  or chervil and serve immediately.
