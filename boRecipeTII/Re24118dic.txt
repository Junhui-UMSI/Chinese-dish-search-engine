Traditional Greek Salad
Cucumber, Red wine vinegar, Salt, tomatoes, Red onion, Kalamata olives, Dried oregano, Feta cheese, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, 
Decoratively arrange the tomatoes, cucumber, olives, feta cheese and red  onion on a platter. In a small bowl whisk together the vinegar, oil,  oregano, salt and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad and serve.      Yield: 2 to 3 servings
