Babes Apple Cake
Salt, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Sugar, Walnuts, chopped, Vegetable oil, Flour, whole, Baking soda, Apples, diced, Wheat germ, toasted, Brown sugar, Eggs, 
Sift together whole wheat flour, wheat germ, baking soda, cinnamon, salt  and nutmeg.  Set aside. In large bowl combine peeled and diced apples,  sugars, oil, walnuts, eggs and vanilla. Add flour to mixture. Stir gently.  Blend well. Turn into 2" deep pan.  Bake in pre- heated oven (350 deg) for  50 minutes or till cake pulls from side of pan. Cool in pan or rack. If  desired, sprinkle with confectioners sugar. Cut in 12 bars.
