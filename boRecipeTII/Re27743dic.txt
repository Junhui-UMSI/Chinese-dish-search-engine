The Original "Hot Brown" Sandwich
To 3.1/2-cups milk, Flour, Parmesan cheese, Heavy cream; whipped, Salt, Parmesan cheese, Toast, Bacon; fried crisp, Egg, Butter, Pepper; to taste, Roast turkey breast; sliced, 
To make sauce - in a skillet, melt butter, add flour and stir until  absorbed.  Stir in milk and 6 tbsp cheese; add eggs. Do not allow to boil.  Remove from heat; fold in cream with seasonings.    For each Hot Brown - place two slices toast in ovenproof dish. Cover toast  with sliced turkey and sauce. Sprinkle with additional cheese; broil until  speckled brown and bubbling.  Remove from broiler; add 2 crisp strips of  bacon on top.  Serve at once.    Source:  Old Brown Hotel in Louisville, KY (Bradenton Herald, 6/8/95)  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #334 by kdeck@epaus.island.net (Karen Deck)  Subject: on Dec 24, 1997
