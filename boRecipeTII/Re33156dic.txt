Basic Marinade for Barbecued Spareribs
Soy sauce, Sugar, Garlic, Fresh ginger root, Sherry, 
1. Crush garlic and ginger root; then combine with remaining ingredients.     2. Add to spareribs and marinate.     VARIATIONS:     1. Rub the spareribs first with 1 teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon sugar. Let  stand 1 hour; then marinate.     2. Reduce the soy sauce to 3/4 cup; increase the sherry to 1/4 cup.     3. Reduce the sugar to 1 teaspoon and add 1/2 teaspoon Five Spices. For  the sherry, substitute brandy.     4. Add 2 tablespoons honey and 2 tablespoons of either hoisin sauce or  applesauce.
