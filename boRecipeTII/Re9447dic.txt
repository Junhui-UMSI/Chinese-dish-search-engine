Pumpkin Bars #1
Each ginger & nutmeg, Salt, TOPPING, Salad oil, Baking Powder, Vanilla, Sugar, Cream cheese, Butter, milk, Flour, Baking soda, Eggs, Pumpkin, Powdered sugar, 
Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda & salt. Add sugar, nutmeg & ginger.  Add oil, beaten eggs & pumpkin. Mix well & bake in a 13x9-inch pan for 20  minutes at 350. Combine topping ingredients & ice bars.    MRS LOUIS (BAMBI) STURBOIS    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
