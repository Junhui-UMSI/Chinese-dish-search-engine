Carrot Pineapple Muffins
Sugar, Eggs, Flour, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Salt, Vanilla, carrot, Crushed pineapple  , Vegetable oil, 
In bowl combine sugar, oil & eggs. In another bowl, combine flour, baking  powder & soda, cinnamon & salt & mix well. Add dry ingredients to the first  mixture & stir to moisten. Add carrots, pineapple & vanilla. Fill greased  muffin tins to the top & bake at 375` for 20 minutes.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "leah perez" <perezleah@hotmail.com> on Oct  21, 1998,
