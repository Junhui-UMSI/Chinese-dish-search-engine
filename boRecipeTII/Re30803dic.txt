Tangerine-Salsa Chicken
Salsa, Garlic oil, Flour, Whole chicken breast halves, Oregano, Basil, Without skin, White wine, White pepper, Cayenne pepper, Whole tangerines, 
Mix together flour,oregano, basil, cayanne & white pepper. Heat garlic oil  in heavy skillet. slice chicken breasts into "pinky size" strips. Coat  chicken in flour mixture and fry in HOT oil till golden brown. Remove  chicken and discard oil. Deglaze pan w/ wine and reduce till half. Add  Salsa and mix well.Reduce again then add chichen. Just before serving  squeeze tangerines inot pan and stir & serve.    Recipe By     : John A.
