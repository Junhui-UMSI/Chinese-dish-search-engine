30-Min Beef Fajitas
Garlic, 10-inch flour tortillas, Lean ground beef, Sweet red pepper, chopped, Vegetable oil, Salsa, Onion, cut in strips, Dijon mustard, Chili powder, Pepper, Zucchini, thinly sliced, Low-fat sour cream, Salt, Sweet green pepper, chopped, 
In nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; cook onion, garlic,  chili powder, salt and pepper, stirring often, for 5 minutes or until onion  is softened.    Add beef and salsa; cook, breaking up beef with back of spoon, for about 3  minutes or until beef is no longer pink. Add peppers and zucchini; cook,  stirring, for 3 minutes or until tender-crisp and liquid has evaporated.    Meanwhile, wrap tortillas in foil; heat in 350F 180C oven for 5 minutes or  until warmed through. Stir sour cream with mustard. Divide beef mixture  among tortillas and roll up.
