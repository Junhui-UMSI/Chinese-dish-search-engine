Genovese Rice and Bean Soup
Green cabbage, chopped, Onions, chopped fine, Potatoes, peeled and diced, Water, Water, Brown rice, Salt/pepper, Spinach, stemmed and chopped, olive oil, Salt, White beans, drained, Pesto sauce, 
In a large saucepan, combine cabbage, spinach, potatoes, onion and oil with  rice and the 6 cups of water. Bring to a boil. Add salt and pepper. Simmer  for about an hour. Add beans. Continue to simmer 10 minutes longer. Remove  from heat. Stir in pesto. Let stand for at least 10 m inutes before  serving. This soup can be served hot or at room temperature for a stew-like  consistency.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  17, 1998
