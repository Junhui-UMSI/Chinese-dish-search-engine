Texas Salsa
Tomato; quartered, Purple onion; whole, halved, Canned chopped green chilies, Garlic, Fresh cilantro; loosely, Salt, 
Position knife blad in food processor bowl; add first 5 ingredients.  Process until chopped. Add chilies; pulse once or until combine.    Yield: 2 cups    Recipe by: Erin Whiteley, Annapolis Maryland    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Barb at PK  <abprice@wf.net> on Apr 17, 1998
