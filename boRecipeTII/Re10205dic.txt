Quick and Easy Chicken
(13-oz) evaporated milk, (10.75-oz) chicken gumbo, (6.5 to 7.5-oz) boned, (5-oz) Chinese noodles, (10-oz) cream of chicken, 
Mix all ingredients; place in ungreased casserole and bake at 350 for 45  minutes or until firm. Serves 4-6. This is a very easy and quick company  meal when accompanied by a nice green salad and dessert.
