Taco Salad #1
Ground beef, (6-oz) corn chips, Onion; thinly sliced, Envelope dry onion soup mix, Green Pepper, Water, Sliced ripe olives, Cheddar cheese, Head lettuce; torn into, Tomato; cut in wedges, 
Brown ground beef in skillet. Sprinkle onion soup mix over meat; stir in  water. Simmer, uncovered, 10 minutes. In salad bowl, combine lettuce,  tomato, onion, green pepper, olives and cheese. Toss well. Spoon in meat;  top with corn chips.
