Fresh Apple Bread
Shortening, Sugar, Egg, Plain flour, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Nuts, Cloves; ground, Salt, Apples, 
Cream together shortening and sugar. Add egg; beat well. Sift together  flour, soda, salt, cloves and cinnamon. Add to shortening mixture; then  stir in apples and nuts. Pour into 9" greased loaf pan. Bake 1 hour at 350?    Let stand until cool. Remove bread from pan and sprinkle with  confectioners sugar.
