Strawberry - Jalapeno Salsa
Plum Tomatoes, Seeded and, olive oil, Red Onion,Diced, Lime, Juice of, Garlic, Strawberries, Fresh, Diced, Jalapeno Pepper, Minced, 
Combine strawberries, tomatoes, onion and peppers. Stir in lime juice,  garlic and oil. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours. Great with poultry or  pork. Or with tortilla chips.
