Pound Cake with Dried Fruit and a Port Wine Reduction Sauce
Assorted dried fruits;, Unsalted butter; softened, Ground cinnamon, Whipped cream in a pastry, Orange zest, Lemon zest, Sugar, PORT REDUCTION, Shaker of powdered sugar, Fresh mint sprigs, Port wine, CAKE, Flour, Eggs, Vanilla, Baking Powder, 
ESSENCE OF EMERIL SHOW#EE2308    Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Grease and flour a 9x5x3 inch loaf pan. For  the sauce: In a small sauce pan, combine the port wine and zest together.  Bring up to a boil. Reduce to a simmer and reduce mixture until 1/2 cup  remains or syrup consistency, about 25-30 minutes. For the cake: Cream the  butter and sugar together. Beat in one egg at a time into the butter  mixture, scraping the bowl often. Combine the flour, spices, and fruit,  together. Gradually add flour mixture to butter mixture, beating on low  speed just till combined. Pour into prepared pan and bake for 55-65  minutes. Remove the cake from the pan and cool on a wire rack. Stack a  couple of slices in the center of a plate. Drizzle with the port wine  reduction. Garnish with whipped cream, powder sugar and fresh mint.    Yield: 4 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 21, 1998
