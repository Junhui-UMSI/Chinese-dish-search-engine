Vegetable Curry From Nepal
Onions, sliced thin, Ground cumin, IN A DUTCH OVEN, SAUTE, Dry mustard, Potatoes, unpeeled, scrubbed, Peanut oil for flavor, Cabbage, chopped, Ground coriander, Broccoli, cauliflower or, Tomatoes, Curry powder, ADD TO IT, And diced, WHEN ALMOST TENDER, ADD, Garlic, salt and pepper, (+-) cayenne, Ground turmeric, 
Cover and simmer until all veggies are tender. Serve over rice.    Posted by EBWATERS to AOL. [Rev. SKS.
