Striped Cat Ipa
Bullion hops (8.2 alpha), Alpha), Crystal malt, Cascade hops (4.2 alpha), Amber dry extract, Wyeast #1098, Toasted pale malt, Brewers Gold hops (7.5, Pale dry extract, Pack, Pale malt, Corn sugar for priming, Handful steamed oak chips, Gypsum 1/4 tsp. Irish moss, 
(boil Brewers Gold and Bullion).  Remove from heat and add the cascades.  Cool wort. Pitch yeast. I have made this twice and both times it turned out  fine. Nicely hoppy.
