Topping, Low Fat Whipped
Vanilla extract, Unfavoured Gelatin, Lemon juice, Sugar, Evaporated Skim Milk, Cold water, 
Chill evaporated milk.  Sprinkle gelatin over cold water in small  saucepan, then stir over low heat until dissolved.  Add to milk and beat  until stiff.  Add sugar, vanilla and lemon juice.  Use immediately, or  beat again before serving. Makes 1/2 c. 1 serving = 2T, 29 cal.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmealnew.zip
