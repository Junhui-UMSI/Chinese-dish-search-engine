Tarragon Vinaigrette
olive oil, Pepper -- to taste, Salt, White wine vinegar, Minced fresh tarragon, 
In a small bowl, combine vinegar, salt and pepper, and minced tarragon.  Whisk in oil and let stand 5 minutes. Whisk again, then taste and adjust  seasoning.    Makes 1 cup.
