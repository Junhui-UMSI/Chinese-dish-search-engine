Basic Freezer Ice Cream
(16-oz) evaporated milk, milk, Peaches or other fruit;, Vanilla, Eggs, Sugar (or less), 
Beat eggs & sugar until frothy & mixed well. Add milk & mix well. Add  vanilla. Pour into electric freezer & freeze. Yields 3 quarts plain or 1  gallon with fruit.
