Beer Beef Stew C/p
Onions; sliced, Cooking oil, Salt, Bay leaf, Flour, Thyme, Stew beef; 1" inch cubes, Pepper, Beer; not "lite" beer, Beef bouillon cubes;, 
Combine onions and cooking oil in removable liner. Place liner in base.  Cover and cook on high 30 to 40 minutes or until onions are soft. Coat meat  with mixture of flour, salt and pepper. Add meat and all other ingredients  to removable liner in base. Cover and cook on auto 6 hours; or low 7-9  hours; or high 4 hours. To thicken sauce for gravy, uncover and cook on  high 20 to 30 minutes before serving. Serve over noodles.  Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on May  12, 1998
