Whipped Cream Frosting
Whipping cream, Powdered sugar, Vanilla, 
Combine all ingredients in medium mixing bowl; beat until firm peaks form.    Kahlua Cream Frosting: Combine 2 tablespoons Kahlua, 1 teaspoon instant  coffee powder, and 1 teaspoon water, stirring until smooth. Beat whipping  cream, sugar, and vanilla until foamy; add coffee mix, beating until firm  peaks form.    Makes 4 cups Busted by Gail Shermeyer 4paws@netrax.net    Recipe by: The Southern Living Cookbook, 1987, 8487-0709-5    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest by Gail Shermeyer <4paws@netrax.net> on Apr  13, 1998
