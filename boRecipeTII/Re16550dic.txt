After-School Snack Muffins
Salt, Baking Powder, Shredded carrot, Boiling water, Whole weat flour, Baking cups, All-purpose flour, Brown sugar, Egg, Raisin bran cereal, milk, Vegetable oil, Cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 400*. Place baking cups in muffin pan; set aside. IN a  small bowl, combine cereal and water; set aside. In a medium bowl, combine  flours, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Add remaining ingredients to  cereal mixture; mix well. Add cereal mixture to flour mixture; stir just  until dry ingredients are moistened. Spoon batter into bake cups, filling  3/4 full. Bake 20-25 minutes. Serve warm. Makes 12 muffins.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Shawn Zehnder Lea <slea@HIGHTOWERSERVICES.COM>  on Sep 19, 1997
