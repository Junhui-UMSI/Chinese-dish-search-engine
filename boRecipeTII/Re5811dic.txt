Pinto Beans and Ham Hocks
Salt, Garlic powder, Ginger, smoked ham hocks, Pepper, Onion, Celery, Chives, Dry beans, 
{for vegetarians, ham-hocks are optional}    Starting the night before... Wash and sort beans. Rinse well. Pour into  crock-pot. Add ham-hocks. Cover with water to just below rim. add other  ingred. Cover and turn on high. After 2 hours, check water, fill to below  rim, if needed.  Turn to low and let cook for at least 18 hrs. Before  serving, stir well and remove ham-hock bones. Reminder= the longer it  cooks, the better it tastes.    Serve with fresh, hot cornbread, or garlic bread.
