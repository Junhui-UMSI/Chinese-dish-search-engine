Applesauce Walnut Cake
Eggs, Butter, Applesauce, Black walnuts, Cinnamon, Butter golden cake, 
Preheat oven to 375~. Grease and flour 2, 9x1 1/2" round cake pans. Put dry  cake mix, butter, applesauce, eggs and cinnamon into large mixer bowl. Beat  for 4 minutes at medium speed. Stir in black walnuts. Turn batter into pans  and spread evenly. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Frost with Mocha Frosting. (see  recipe)
