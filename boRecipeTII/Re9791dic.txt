Pumpkin Spice Cake Baked in a Jar
Salad oil, Flour; all purpose, Granulated sugar, Canned pumpkin, Ground cloves, Walnuts, Salt, Seedless raisins, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Ground cinnamon, Ground ginger, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 325~. Coarsely chop the nuts and raisins; set aside. Sift  together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cloves, cinnamon, and  ginger in a large bowl. Add raisins and walnuts; toss lightly to combine.  In another large bowl, beat eggs at high speed until thick and yellow, 2-3  minutes. Gradually beat in the sugar until thick and light. At low  speed,beat in the oil and pumpkin; blend thoroughly. Gradually stir in the  flour mixture until well blended. Divide batter among the eight canning  jars, should be slightly less than 1/2 full. Wipe the sides of the jar off,  inside/outside, in case you slop or itll burn. Place jars on a cookie  sheet or theyll tip over. Bake in preheated 325~ oven for 35-40 minutes or  until a toothpick inserted deep into the center of the cakes comes out  clean.
