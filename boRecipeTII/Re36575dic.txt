Strawberry Angel Fluff
Angel food cake, Frozen whipped topping, lg, Frozen strawberries, Strawberry Jello, lg, 
Tear the cake into walnut-size pieces and scatter over bottom of 10" X 13"  sheet cake pan. Dissolve Jello in 2 cups of boiling water and then 2 cups  cold water. Allow to jell a little. Fold in the frozen strawberries and  whipped topping which has thawed a little. Pour over cake and refrigerate  until set. Decorate with whipped cream or additional whipped topping and  fresh strawberries.    Mrs. Harold T.
