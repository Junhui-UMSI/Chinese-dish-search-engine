Strawberry Glace Pie
Fresh Strawberies, Sugar, Baked Pie Shell, Cornstarch, Water, Cream cheese, 
Bake pie shell.   Mash enough berries to measure 1 cup. Stir together sugar and cornstarch.  Gradually stir in water and crushed strawberries. Cook over medium heat,  stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils. Boil and stir 1  minute. Cool.   Beat cream cheese until smooth; spread on bottom of cooked pie shell.  Fill shell with remaining sliced berries; pour cooked berry mixture over  top. Chill at least 3 hours or until set.    VARIATIONS:  Peaches Glace Pie: Substitute 5 cups sliced fresh peaches (7 med) for the  strawberries. To prevent peaches from darkening, use lemon juice or  ascorbic acid mixture.   Raspberry Glace Pie: Substitute 6 cups fresh raspberries for the  strawberries.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmstrawb.zip
