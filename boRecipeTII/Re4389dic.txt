Peruvian Chicken with Plums
Skinned chicken; cut up, Garlic, Seeded; diced, fresh, Cooked brown rice, Low sodium chicken stock, Bay leaf, Quartered fresh California, Diced green bell peppers, Onions; chopped, Tomato; chopped, Powdered saffron; (optional), Vegetable oil, 
Saute chicken in oil in large nonstick skillet, turning often until golden  brown on all sides, about 12 minutes.  Add onion, green pepper, garlic;  saute 2 minutes longer.  Add tomato, chili, saffron, stock and bay leaf.  Bring to a boil; cover and simmer 10 minutes.  Add plums and rice; heat.  Serve with extra plum wedges, if desired.
