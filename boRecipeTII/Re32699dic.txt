Sweet Potato Bake
margarine, Margarine, Miniature marshmallows, sweet potatoes, Vegetable cooking spray, Kellogs Corn Flakes, Orange Peel, Salt, 
1.Combine sweet potatoes, 2 tablespoons softened margarine, orange peel and  salt. Spread mixture in 1 coat casserolee coated with cooking spray.    2.Arrange marshmallows over potatoes, pressing in slightly.    3.Combine Kelloggs ? CORN FLAKES? cereal with melted margarine. Sprinkle  over marshmallows.    4.Bake at 375F about 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated.    Recipe by: Kellogs?
