Ancho-Rubbed Game Hen
Ancho chilies; dried, Cilantro; minced, Boiling water, Cornmeal, Garlic; minced, Cumin, Rock Cornish game hens, Salt, Salt and pepper, olive oil, Curry powder, 
Preheat the oven to 400F. Place the ancho chilies in a large bowl, cover  them with the boiling water, and soak them for 30 minutes to overnight.  Drain the chilies well, then remove and discard the seed and stems. Place  the chilies in a blender with the garlic, salt, and cilantro. Puree and set  aside. Combine the cornmeal, curry pwoder, cumin, salt, and pepper and  spread in a jelly-roll pan. Spread the hen breasts generously and evenly  with the chili puree, then dredge them in the seasoned cornmeal. Place a  large saute pan or skillet over high heat,. add the oil and heat until it  begins to smoke. Add the hen breasts and cook 2 to 3 minutes, or until the  topping is crisp. Turn the breasts, cook the other side until crisp, then  transfer the breasts to the preheated oven to cook another 5 minutes, or  until opaque throughout.
