Todays Pot Roast
Potatoes; quartered, Boneless beef rump or round, Reynolds oven bag, Flour, Carrots; cut in 2" pieces, Pepper, Can Italian-style stewed, Envelope onion recipe soup, 
Preheat oven to 325 F.    Shake flour in oven bag; place in 13x9x2" baking pan. Add tomatoes, soup  mix and pepper.  Squeeze bag to blend ingredients. Trim fat from roast;  place roast in bag. Turn bag to coat roast with sauce. Place vegetables in  bag around roast.  Close bag with nylon tie; cut 6 half-inch slits in top.  Bake until roast is tender, 1 1/2 to 2 1/4 hours. Let stand in bag for 5  minutes.    Note: Could have used some spices.    Yield: 6 to 8 servings.    In brochure that came with package of Reynolds large (19x23.5") oven bags.  Richmond, VA: Consumer Products Division/Reynolds Metals Company, 1988.  Tried 01/08/94. Posted by Cathy Harned.
