Tofu Gumbo
Red bell peppers; diced, Funugreek, Salt and pepper, Paprika, Green bell peppers; diced, Vegetable stock, Onions, yellow; diced, Okra; sliced 1/4" thick, Yellow squash; sliced, Corn kernels, fresh, Cajun Spice, Red pepper flakes, Garlic cloves; peeled,sliced, Carrots; sliced 1/4" thick, Italian tomatoes, canned;, Tofu; pressed, cubed, 
In a large saucepan, combine the onions vegetable stock, tomatoes, carrots  and celery. Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes.     Add the okra, red and green bell peppers, yellow swquahs, corn, garlic,  Cajun Spice, paprika, fenugreek and red pepper flakes. Continue simmering  for another 10 mintues. Add the tofu and simmer an addtional 10 minutes.  Season to taste with pepper and salt.Serve hot.
