Baked Stuffed Zucchini
Onion, Clove garlic; chopped, Parsley, Tomato sauce, Zucchini; cut in half, Matzo meal, 
Date: Mon, 1 Apr 1996 22:32:01 -7    From: arlenes@holly.ColoState.EDU  Scoop out pulp of zucchini halves. Heat pulp, onion, sauce and spices in a  pan for 5 minutes. Add matzo meal to mixture and mix well. Restuff zucchini  with mixture. Place in a baking dish with a little water on bottom. Bake at  450F for 30 minutes until zucchini shells are soft.
