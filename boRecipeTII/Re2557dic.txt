Peaches with a Sunshine Smile
Lettuce leaves, To 3 cups cottage cheese, Of peach halves, 
Arrange lettuce in a bowl or plate. Spoon a layer of cottage cheese "sun  rays" on lettuce. The peach half is placed round end up. Press raisins  gently into peach to form a sunshine face. Sprinkle extra raisins on  cottage cheese.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
