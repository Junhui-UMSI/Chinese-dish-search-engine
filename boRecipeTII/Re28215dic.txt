Baked Fish Au Gratin
Flour, Cream of celery soup, Haddock fillets, Cheddar Cheese, milk, Paprika, Parsley, 
Dip fish in flour. Arrange in Pam-sprayed baking dish. Blend cream of  celery soup and milk. Spread over fish in pan. Sprinkle with grated cheese,  parlsley and paprika. Bake at 350 F. for 45 mins. until fish flakes easily  with fork.    Bobbie Banghart, Lansing, Michigan, U.S.A.    NOTES : Can do in Microwave for 6-7 minutes, until fish flakes easily with  fork.
