Bechamel Sauce (Mccartney)
Skim milk OR soya milk;, Ground nutmeg, Sea salt, Butter or margarine OR soy, Black Pepper, All-purpose flour; or less, 
Melt the butter in a small, heavy-bottomed saucepan. Gradually stir in the  flour. Add the warm milk slowly, stirring all the time until the sauce  thickens. Season to taste with nutmeg, salt and pepper and simmer over a  very low heat for 5-6 minutes.    Notes: Makes 2 cups.
