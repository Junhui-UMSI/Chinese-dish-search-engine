Baked Potatoes Elegante
Softened Butter, Sour  Cream, milk, Onion salt, Softened Cream Cheese, Salt and pepper to taste, Medium Baking Potatoes, 
Bake potatoes at 375 degrees until done. Cut slice from top of each potato  and scoop out insides.   Mash potato insides and mix with sour cream, cream cheese, butter, onion  salt, salt, and pepper. Beat until smooth and fluffy. Fill potato shells  with mixture.   Return to oven and bake 15 minutes or until potatoes are heated through.
