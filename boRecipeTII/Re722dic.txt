Grilled Chile Wings
Pineapple juice, Balsamic vinegar, Dark brown sugar, garlic, Scotch bonnet, Ground allspice, Chicken wings, Salt, Carrot and celery sticks, 
Use side burner or preheat grill. Combine all ingredients in a small  saucepan and let boil for 2 minutes. Remove from heat, pour into a large  bowl and let cool. Add chicken wings to the marinade and marinate in the  refrigerator at least 2 hours. Grill over medium heat for 10 to 15 minutes  or until cooked through. Serve with celery and carrot sticks.    * Several jalapeno or serrano chiles work well for non-Chile-Heads    Recipe by: Bobby Flay - HOT OFF THE GRILL SHOW #HG1A39    Posted to bbq-digest by wight@odc.net on Nov 27, 1998, converted by  MM_Buster v2.0l.
