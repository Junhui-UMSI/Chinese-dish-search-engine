Applesauce-Oatmeal Muffins
Baking soda, Wheat flour, Egg whites, Salt, Applesauce, Non-fat milk, Baking Powder, Rolled oats, Brown sugar, 
~ Soak oats in milk for ~1 hour.  Combine the rest of the ingredients, mix  well, and bake at 400 degrees for 25 minutes in a muffin tin.    ~ Makes 12 medium-sized muffins (guaranteed to turn you into a marathoner  ;-) Shared by Mike D.
