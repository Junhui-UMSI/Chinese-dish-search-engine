Quickie Chicken Paprikash
Shortening, Franco-American chicken, Hot buttered noodles or, Salt, Sliced onions, Paprika, Clove garlic. minced, Pepper, Chicken pieces, Sour cream, Flour, 
Dredge chicken pieces in flour; brown in hot shortening; drain off any  excess fat. Mix together gravy, sour cream, onions, garlic, paprika, salt  and pepper; pour over the chicken. Cover and simmer 45 minutes or until  chicken is tender and done.    Serve with hot buttered noodles, mashed potatoes or even rice.
