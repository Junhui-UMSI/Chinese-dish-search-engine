Baked Chimichangas
flour tortillas, green chilies, diced, black beans, cheddar , Salsa, Lean ground beef, Green onions, black olives, Lettuce, 
In a skillet thoroughly cook the meat, drain and set aside. In the same  skillet combine and saute lightly the beans, chiles, onions and olives. Add  the cooked meat to the mixture and stir. Evenly distribute the meat mixture  onto each tortilla, fold tortillas like an envelope and place folded side  down on baking sheet. Bake 30-35 minutes in a 400 degree oven. Serve  garnished with the lettuce, cheese and salsa.    Recipe by: http://www.firstcyberkitchen.com/dot.htm Posted to MC-Recipe  Digest V1 #697 by Rooby <MsRooby@sprintmail.com> on Jul 29, 1997
