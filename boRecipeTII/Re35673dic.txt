Stuffed Baked Potato
Idaho potatoes, salt and pepper, Sour cream, Bacon; cooked & crumbled, Chives, 
Bake potatoes in 400 oven for 45 min. Cut potatoes in half & scrape out  potato meal. Mix w/ sour cream, chives & bacon. Season w/ salt & pepper to  taste. Place stuffed potato halves under broiler until lightly browned.    CHESAPEAKE RESTAURANT    N.
