Poppy Seed Chicken Casserole
Cream of chicken soup, Condensed, Pimientos  , Crackers, chicken, Sour cream, Poppy seeds, Mushrooms, Margarine, Margarine, 
In a skillet, melt margarine. Saute mushrooms until tender. Stir in  chicken, soup, sour cream and pimiento; mix well. Spoon mixture into a  greased 2-quart casserole. In a small bowl combine topping ingredients (1/2  cup margarine, the crushed crackers and 2 teaspoons poppy seed). Sprinkle  over the chicken. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
