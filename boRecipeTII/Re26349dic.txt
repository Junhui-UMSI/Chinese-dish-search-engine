Tomato Basil Dressing
Lemon juice, Basil, Scallions; chopped, Dried Oregano, Sugar, Tomato juice, Red wine vinegar; or less, 
In a jar, combine all ingredients. Shake vigorously. Green salad or pasta  salad. Variation: For more tomato flavor, use only 2 tbsp vinegar.    Serves 4, 2 tablespoon each; 22 cals, 0.3 g protein, 0.1 g fat, 6 g carb,      111 mg sodium, 0 mg chol.
