15-Minute Super Sundae Pie
syrup, Pecans, cracker pie crust, Maraschino cherries, Vanilla ice cream, Keebler ready crust graham, Whipped topping or non-dairy, whipped topping, 
From: Gerald Edgerton <jerrye@wizard.com>    Date: Thu, 23 May 1996 13:35:07 -0700    In a mixing bowl, stir slightly softened ice cream until smooth. Spoon the  ice cream into the pie crust and cover with the inverted plastic lid. Place  pie in freezer for several hours, until hard.    TO SERVE: Remove pie from freezer and let stand for 10 minutes to soften.  Spoon Smuckers Strawberry Topping onto serving plate. Cut pie and palce  slice on plate. Garnish generously with Smuckers Chocolate Sundae Syrup,  and other toppings as desired. Return unused portion to freezer.     Preparation time: 15 minutes.
