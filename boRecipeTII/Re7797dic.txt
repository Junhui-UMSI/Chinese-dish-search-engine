Portobello Saute
Lime juice, Portobello mushrooms;, Grated Parmesan cheese, Unsalted butter, Balsamic vinegar, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 5 min.    Melt butter in a heavy nonstick skillet over medium high heat. saute  mushroom slices 3-4 minutes, or until softened. Stir in vinegar and lime  juice. Cover skillet and let cook 30-45 seconds. Serve sprinkled with  cheese.  Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 19, 1998
