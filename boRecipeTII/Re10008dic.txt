Pyrohy(Varenyky) Fillings- Meat or Fish
Meat or fish, Salt and pepper, to taste, Butter, Onion, grated, 
Prepare any leftover meat or fish by grinding it. Cook the onion in the  butter until it is tender, and combine it with the meat or fish. Moisten  the mixture with a rich gravy or cream sauce or egg, and them season to  taste with salt and pepper.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #373, by sewin <hammer@imag.net> on Wed, 15  Jan 1997.
