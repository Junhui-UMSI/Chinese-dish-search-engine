Wurstsalat
Vinegar, Pickles, Onion, Some Chili powder, Salt, Some Parsley, Vegetable oil, Pepper, Mortadella or similar, 
Cut sausage and pickles into stripes. Mix. Add spices, vinegar and oil.  Stir well. Store in refrigerator for 3-4 hours. Cut onion to rings. Spread  onion rings and cut parsley on top.
