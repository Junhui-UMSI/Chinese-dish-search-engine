Apricot Coffee Cake
Equiv.sweetner, Canned apricots,drain+chop, Salt, Egg, Water, Flour, Vanilla, Nonfat dry milk, Baking Powder, Ground cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 375F. In a small bowl, sift flour, salt and baking  powder.In another bowl, cream sweetner, egg and margarine on low  spped of an electric mixer. Add dry milk, water and vanilla. Beat  until blended. Stir in dry ingriedients. Stir until smooth. Spread  batter in 8" square pan that has been sprayed with a nonstick cooking  spray. Arrange apricots evenly over batter. Sprinkle liberally with  cinnamon. Bake 25 minutes, until toothpick inserted in cneter of cake  comes out clean. Cool in pan.
