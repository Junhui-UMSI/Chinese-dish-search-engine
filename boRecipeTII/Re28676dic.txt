Texas Meatloaf
Chopped, Onion, Barbecue sauce, Old-fashioned oats, No Stick corn oil Cooking spray, Green pepper, Garlic, Lean ground beef*, Egg whites, corn oil, chopped green Chilies, 
1. Spray 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 x 2 1/2-inch loaf pan with cooking spray.    2. In large skillet heat corn oil over medium-high heat.  Add green pepper,  onion and garlic; stirring frequently, cook 4 minutes or until tender.  Remove  from heat.    3. In large bowl combine ground beef, egg whites, oats, 1/2 cup barbecue  sauce  and chilies.  Stir in cooked vegetables.  Spoon into loaf pan.    4. Bake in 350 F oven for 1 hour.  Drain excess liquid.    5. Invert loaf onto serving platter.  Spoon remaining 1/4 cup barbecue  sauce  over top.  Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.    Makes 8 servings.    * Use ground round or 90 percent lean ground beef    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #148    Date: Fri, 12 Jul 1996 14:58:17 -0400    From: CookieTstr@aol.com    Recipe By     :Womans Day Recipes
