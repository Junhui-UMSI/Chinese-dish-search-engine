Pure Vanilla Extract
Vanilla beans, vodka, 
Purchase vanilla beans from health food store. With knife, make 1-inch  slits down vanilla bean on all sides. Place beans in vodka. Mark bottle  with date. Wait 3 months and you have pure vanilla extract. Leave beans in  vodka. Keeps forever.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 6,  1998
