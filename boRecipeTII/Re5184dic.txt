Pigs Ears Salad
Lemon juice to taste, Raw carrots cut in thin, Cucumber *, Salt, * peeled, seeded and cut in, Whole star anise, Plum sauce or mango chutney, Butter lettuce leaves, Green onions **, Toasted sesame seeds, Two-inch slice fresh ginger, 
** tops only, slivered lengthwise and cut in 2-inch julienne    It is important that all ingredients in this recipe be well chilled.    Prepare pigs ears according to Steps I and II in the basic  instructions. Discard water and return ears to pot with water to  cover, star anise and ginger.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover  and simmer for 45 minutes, or until ears are tender.  Remove ears,  let cool and chill.  Cut into thin strips (1/8 inch).    Combine pigs ears, carrots, cucumbers and onions and toss lightly  with plum sauce.  Season with salt and pepper and a little fresh  lemon juice if a slightly tart flavor is desired.  Serve on crisp  lettuce and sprinkle with sesame seeds.    Serves 6.    [Ill bet this would be good made with sliced duck web or jellyfish as  well...
