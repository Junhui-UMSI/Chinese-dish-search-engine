Barbecued Cornish Hen
BARBECUE SAUCE:, Vinegar, Ketchup, Garlic, Cornish game hens, Vegetable oil, Lemon juice, Onions, Worcestershire, Brown sugar, Salt, Paprika, Butter, 
Source: MAINPOUL.ZIP    Brush each Cornish hen with butter and place on vertical roaster. Mash  garlic and salt together in a bowl. Combine with remaining ingredients. Set  Cornish upright on grill over coals and cover barbecue grill. Brush with  sauce every 10 minutes for about 45 minutes or until tender.
