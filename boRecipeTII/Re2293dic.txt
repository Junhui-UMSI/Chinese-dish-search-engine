Banana-Nut Bread
Brown Sugar, Mashed bananas-very ripe;, Sifted white wheat flour, Salt, Eggs, Pecans, Lemon juice, Butter, Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Butter a large loaf pan.    Sift the flour, baking powder and salt together.    Cream the brown sugar together with the butter, then mix in the eggs.  Thoroughly mix in the bananas and lemon juice, then mix in the flour  mixture as quickly as possible.    Fold in the chopped nuts, then pour into the prepared pan. Bake for 45  minutes to 1 hour, until a toothpick inserted in the center of the loaf  comes out clean.    Recipe by Mangia!
