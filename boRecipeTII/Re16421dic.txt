Weiner Schnitzel
Salt, Breadcrumbs, Sour cream, Water, Veal escalopes, Flour, Egg, Fat, -(up to), 
Season meat with salt; dip in flour, then in egg, finally in bread crumbs.  Fry in hot fat on both sides until golden. Add water & sour cream to pan  drippings; stir well. Garnish with slices of lemon, anchovies & capers.    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
