Raspberry Rascal
Pineapple, fresh, Raspberries; fresh or frozen, -Cold water, Honey; to taste, 
Whirl raspberries and pineapple in blender. Add water and honey to taste.  Whirl again. Strain. Garnish with tiny piece of pineapple.    MAKES: 3-4 SERVINGS recipe may be halved for 2 servings    SOURCE: _The Non-Drinkers Drink Book_ by Gail Schioler
