Warm Plum Sauce
Balsamic vinegar, Sugar, Plums; pitted, diced 1/4", Garlic, Sesame seeds; for garnish, Salt, 
In a medium sauce pan, combine all ingredients except sesame seeds; cook  over high heat for 3 minutes. Reduce heat to medium high and cook, stirring  occassionally, until liquid thickens and the plums have softened and broken  down to the consistency of a chunky preserve, about 15 minutes. Remove from  heat, transfer plum sauce to a serving dish and sprinkle with sesame seeds.  Serve warm.
