Baked Pears with Chocolate
Sugar, pears, Chocolate fudge topping, Water, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 30 min.    Preheat oven to 350F. Combine sugar and water in a heavy saucepan over  medium heat. Simmer 8-10 minutes until mixture begins to thicken slightly.  Place pears, cut side down, in a buttered shallow baking dish. Pour sugar  mixture over pears and bake 10 minutes. Turn pears and bake another 10  minutes, basting occasionally. Serve pears warm with chocolate topping.    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Mar 02, 1998
