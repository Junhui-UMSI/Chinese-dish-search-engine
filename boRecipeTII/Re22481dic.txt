Tuscany Chicken
Fresh basil; minced, Tomatoes; diced, Boneless skinless chicken, Water, Fettucine or angel hair, Red wine vinegar, olive oil, Garlic, 
In a large no-stick fry pan over medium heat, warm the oil for 1 minutes.  Add the chicken and saute for 5 minutes per side. Remove to a plate.    Add the tomatoes, garlic, vinegar and water to the pan. cover and cook for  5 minutes, or until the tomatoes are softened and have given up some of  their juice. Add the chicken, cover, and cook for 5 minutes, or until the  chicken is cooked through. Sprinkle with the basil.    Serve over fettuccine or angel hair pasta.
