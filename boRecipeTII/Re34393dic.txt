Bean Bean Bean Bake
Hard cooked eggs; cut up, Hot pepper sauce, Cooked lima beans, Prepared mustard, Steak sauce, Garlic, Mayonnaise, Buttered bread; broken up, Onion; quartered, Cooked green beans, Cooked kidney beans, Lemon juice, 
In covered blender jar, blend bread piece by piece at low speed until  crumbed. Set aside. Blend mayonnaise, eggs, onion, garlic, lemon juice and  seasonings at high speed until smooth. In a 1 1/2 quart casserole, layer  half of each kind of bean and half the sauce; repeat. Sprinkle crumbs over  all. Bake at 350 F for 35 minutes until brown and bubbly. Serves 8.
