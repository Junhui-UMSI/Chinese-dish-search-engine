Peanut Butter Cream Pie
Powdered sugar, oreo cookies, FILLING, Creamy peanut butter, Heavy cream, cream cheese, Crust, Butter, Salted peanuts, Heavy cream, 
Combine cookie crumbs and butter.Press mixture into a 9" pie pan.Set in  freezer while you prepare the filling.   Combine cream cheese,powdered sugar and peanut butter.Beat until  smooth.Add the 1 cup cream and mix well.Fold in the whipped  cream,gently.Turn into the prepared pie crust.Sprinkle with chopped  peanuts.Place in the freezer for at least 2 hours.It can be made several  days in advance.Makes 6 to 8 servings.
