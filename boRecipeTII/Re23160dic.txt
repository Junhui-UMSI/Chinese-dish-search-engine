Tuna and Tomato Pasta
Onion, Tin tomatoes chopped, Tin tuna (in springwater), Mushrooms sliced, Garlic, Tomato paste, Basil, salt and sugar, Capsicum diced, Mixed herbs, 
Saute onion and garlic until transparent, then add capsicum and mushrooms  and fry until soft.  Add tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, basil, salt and  sugar, and simmer for 5-10 minutes. Lastly add the drained tuna and simmer  for a further 10 minutes.    This pasta suace can then be used on any preferred type of pasta.    ENJOY!
