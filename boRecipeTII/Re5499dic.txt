Pineapple Gelatin
Cold water, Can unsweetened cr.pineapple, Lemon juice, Cinnamon, Orange extract (optional), Unflavored gelatin, 
Sprinkle gelatin over cold water in a small bowl and allow 5 minutes  to soften.  Dissolve over hot water.    Mix remaining ingredients in blender until smooth.  Add gelatin  mixture and whip until blended.  Pour into 3 cup mold and refrigerate  until set. Garnish with whipped topping.    Adapted from Fancy, Sweet & Sugarfree by Karen Barkie, 1985 Shared  but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier Feb 94    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/diabetic.zip
