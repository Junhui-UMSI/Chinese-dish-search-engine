Pecan Cookies
Brown sugar, (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted, Flour, Coarsely ground pecans, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Egg, Vanilla, 
Heat oven to 350.  Cream butter and sugar in large mixing bowl until  well blended.  Beat in egg and vanilla until mixed.  Using a wooden  spoon, stir in flour, baking powder and baking soda until mixed.  Stir  in pecans.    Drop by spoonfuls onto lightly greased baking sheets.  Bake until golden  and firm on the top, about 10 minutes.  Cool on wire rack.    Di Note:  Good cookie to have with coffee after everyone has gone home,  the dishes are done and you finally get to sit down.
