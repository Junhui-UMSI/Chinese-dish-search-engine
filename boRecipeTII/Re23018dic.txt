Trout Menuire
Garlic powder, Green onion tops, chopped, Worcestershire, Butter, Lemon juice, Cayenne, Salt, Trout fillets, 
Combine butter, Worcestershire, green onion tops, lemon juice, garlic  powder, salt, and cayenne in a 2 cup measure.  Microwave on HIGH 1  minute.  Pour over fish.  Cover with wax paper. Microwave on HIGH 5-6  minutes, turning dish once as it cooks. Serves 4.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mm-trout.zip
