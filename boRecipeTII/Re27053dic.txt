Three-Grain Medley
Finely shredded, Chopped fresh parsley, Water, Pearl barley, Wild rice, Margarine or butter, Chicken, Uncooked wheat berries, Green onions (with tops),, Cl Garlic, finely chopped, 
Heat water and bouillon granules to boiling in 1-1/2-quart saucepan. Stir  in wheat berries. Cover and simmer 15 minutes. Stir in remaining  ingredients. Cover and simmer about 40 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.  6 SERVINGS (1/2 CUP EACH); 55 CALORIES PER SERVING.
