Atholl Brose Tart
Lemon juice; to taste, Sesame seeds; toasted, .  crushed, BASE, Chopped walnuts or hazelnuts, British measurements, Sweet oat biscuits (cookies), .  garnish, Butter, Scotch whiskey, Honey, Double (heavy) cream, FILLING, .  or fresh raspberries for, 
To Make The Base: Put the butter into a large saucepan and heat gently  until melted. Stir in the crushed biscuits and the sesame seeds.  Press the mixture into the base and up the side of a lightly oiled  8-inch flan dish or sandwich tin. Refrigerate whilst making the  filling.    To Make The Filling: Put the whiskey, lemon juice and honey into a  bowl and whisk until mixed. Add cream and whisk until softly stiff.  Spoon onto base and refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours until set. Top with  chopped nuts and/or fruit before serving.
