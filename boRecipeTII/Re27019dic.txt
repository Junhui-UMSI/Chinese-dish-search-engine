Three-Bean Salad
Salad oil, Pepper, green bell pepper, yellow wax beans, green beans, Celery, Onion, Granulated sugar, dark red kidney beans, Salt, Cider vinegar, 
Drain beans, place in mixing bowl.  Add green bell pepper, celery and  onion.  Mix oil and vinegar with sugar, salt and pepper. Pour over bean  mixture. Mix together. Transfer to large non metallic container. Cover and  refrigerate overnight or longer for flavors to blend. Occasionally stir the  mixture.    Serve with slotted spoon.
