Pear and Cucumber Salad
Cucumber; unpeeled, Lettuce, Mayonnaise; or French Drssng, Salt, Sugar, Lemon juice, Pears; peeled and sliced, 
Combine pears, lemon juice, sugar, and salt.  Slice cucumber paper thin,  and toss lightly with pears. Arrange on crisp lettuce; serve with  mayonnaise.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy  Coleman.
