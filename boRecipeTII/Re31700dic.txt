Swiss-Style Mocha Mix
Instant nonfat dry milk, Sugar, Boiling water, Cocoa, Instant coffee granules, 
Combine first 4 ingredients, and mix well. Store mix in an airtight  container. For each serving, place 1 Tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon mix in a  cup. Add 1 cup boiling water, and stir well. YIELD: about 24 servings    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #539 by Sherry Zeiss <zeiss@tab.com> on Mar  24, 1997
