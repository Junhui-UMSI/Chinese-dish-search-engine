Avocado Gazpacho
fresh dill, Salt, lettuce, Shallots, Garlic, Vodka, Cucumber, fresh dill, Green bell pepper, Juice of 1/2 lemon, Red wine vinegar, Chicken Stock, Avocado, white pepper, 
PLACE THE CUCUMBER, garlic, shallots, green pepper, lettuce, 1 tablespoon  dill, vinegar and chicken stock (in that order) in the container of a  blender or food processor. Blend until smooth. Cut the avocado in half  lengthwise. Peel and remove the pit. Roughly chop half the avocado and add  it to the mixture in the blender or processor. Blend until smooth. Transfer  to a medium bowl. Finely chop the remaining avocado and sprinkle with the  lemon juice. Stir this into the gazpacho; add the vodka, salt and white  pepper to taste. Stir gently and chill thoroughly before serving. Garnish  with remaining chopped dill.
