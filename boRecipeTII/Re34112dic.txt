Batter Baked Fillets
Pancake mix, Margarine, milk, shredded corn cereal, Frozen fish fillets, Salt, Egg, Tabasco sauce, Vegetable oil, 
Heat oven to 400 F. Combine pancake mix and salt in a bowl. Mix together  egg, oil, milk and Tabasco; add to dry ingredients and beat until smooth.  Combine margarine and cereal in a flat pan. Dip fillets in the pancake  mixture, then roll in cereal mixture. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet for 15  minutes. Turn; bake 10 minutes longer or until crumbs are lightly browned.  Makes 6 servings.
