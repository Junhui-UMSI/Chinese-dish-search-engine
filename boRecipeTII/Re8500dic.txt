Potato-Ham Bake
Ham; cubed, Cream of celery soup, Green pepper; chopped, Raw potato; peeled, sliced, Cheddar cheese; shredded, Green Onions, 
Combine all ingredients, except cheese, in 11/2 quart casserole. Cover.  Microwave at high (100%) until potatoes are tender, 12 to 14 minutes,  stirring once during cooking time. Sprinkle cheese on top. Microwave at  medium high (70%) until cheese is melted, 1 to 2 minutes. Makes 4 servings.
