Tuna Mushroom Mornay
Onions, Tuna, Mushrooms in sauce, milk, Butter or margarine, Plain flour, 
Melt butter and saute onions until soft but not brown. Add flour and fry  for a few minutes. Stir while gradually adding milk. When smooth add  drained tuna and mushrooms. Warm through and serve with rice.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 665 by "Anna Zapral/David  Sudmalis" <annadave@nettrade.com.au> on Jan 31, 1998
