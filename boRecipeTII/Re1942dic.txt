Chocolate Chip Applesauce Cake
A, Flour, Baking soda, Cloves, Salt, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, B, Shortening, Sugar, Eggs, C, Thick; cold applesauce, D, Chocolate chips*, 
* but I always put in a whole package of 12 oz. - who wants to skimp on the  chocolate?!    Put "A" above in big bowl. Add "B".    Beat for 2 minutes. Add "C".    Mix. Add "D".    Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes or until done.    This cake is always a hit.
