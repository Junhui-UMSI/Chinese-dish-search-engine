Yogurt Salad Dressing Mix (Low Fat)
Chili powder, Ground cumin, Cayenne pepper, Ground ginger, 
source:Readers Digest Great Recipes    Place all the ingredients in a 1/2 pint jar, cover tightly, and shake well  to mix. Store in a cool, dark, dry place. makes 1/2 cup    To Make Yogurt Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together 2 teaspoons of the  Yogurt Salad Dressing Mix, 1/2 cup low-fat yoghurt, 3 tablespoons water, 1  tablespoon cider vinegar, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 crushed clove garlic  and 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley. Let stand at room temperture at  least 30 minutes before using, then whisk again.
