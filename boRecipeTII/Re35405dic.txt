Beef Elegante over Rice
Seasoned flour, Dry Red Wine, Hot cooked rice, Fresh parsley, Corn oil, Beef broth, Tomato wedges, sliced mushrooms , Round steak, Onions, 
Tenderize steak; cut in 1" strips. Coat steak with flour; brown in  oil. Add onions & cook 2-3 minutes longer. Stir in wine, broth &  mushrooms with liquid; bring to boil. Cover, reduce heat, & simmer  20-30 minutes or until tender. Serve over warm rice; garnish with  tomato & parsley. NOTE: Beef broth may be substituted for wine.
