Bananas with Yogurt
Cardamom, Unsalted butter, Lowfat vanilla yogurt, Firm bananas; peeled and cut, Plus 2 Tbs. dark brown, 
Prep: 5 min, Cook: 5 min.    Turn on broiler. Arrange banana slices in a buttered baking dish. Sprinkle  with brown sugar and cardamom and dot with butter. Broil 2-3 minutes or  until top is bubbly. Serve bananas with vanilla yogurt.  Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 08, 1998
