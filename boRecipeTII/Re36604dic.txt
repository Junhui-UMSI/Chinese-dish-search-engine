Strawberry Bread with Cream Cheese
Shredded coconut, Sugar, Salt, Cream cheese softened, Baking soda, Strawberry juice (if using, Flour, Cinnamon, Vegetable oil, Beaten eggs, (10 oz) frozen berries, Pecans, 
Batter    o Mix flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon and salt o Make a well in center  of batter and add 1/2 cup of berry juice, strawberries, oil and eggs - mix  ingredients together by hand o Grease and flour 2 4x8" loaf pans o Pour  batter into pans and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour  Cheese Spread    o combine cream cheese with enough berry juice to make it spread easily o  frost breads    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #204 by Cheryl_Leibovitz@ed.gov (Cheryl  Leibovitz) on Jun 27, 1997
