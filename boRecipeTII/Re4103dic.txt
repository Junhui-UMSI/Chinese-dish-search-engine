Pepperoni Spaghetti - Southern Living
Jar Spaghetti sauce with, Spaghetti; cooked, Lean ground beef, Pepperoni slices; (3.5 oz), Onion, Grated Parmesan cheese, Shredded mozzarella cheese, Size green bell pepper;, 
Recipe by: Southern Living    Cook first 4 ingredients in a large skillet over medium high heat until  beef is browned, stirring until it crumbles; drain well. Return mixture to  skillet; stir in spaghetti sauce.    Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer, stirring occasionally, 20  minutes. Place pasta in a 13 x 9 inch baking dish, and top with beef  mixture; sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.    Broil 5 1/2 inches from heat (with electric oven door partially open) 1 to  2 minutes or until cheese melts. Remove from oven; sprinkle with Parmesan  cheese.
