Pork Chop-Rice Casserole
Parsley, Pork chops, Progresso tomatoes, Bouillon  , Onion, Corn  , Hot water, Salt and pepper to taste, Parsley, Dry, rice, 
Quickly brown pork chops in skillet. Dissolve bouillon cubes in hot  water. Spread rice in baking dish; place chopped onions over rice. Cut and  place tomatoes over onions. Also use juice from tomatoes. Sprinkle with  parsley. Place chops over all. Pour bouillon into frying pan and scrape  residue. Pour over chops. Salt and pepper to taste. Bake at 350 degrees for        1    hour. Serves    4.      Note: Corn or cut beans cut be layered between the rice and chops.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
