Barbecue Sauce #02
Liquid smoke, Sugar, (small) tomato sauce, Cumin, (small) tomato paste, Vinegar, Water, Chili powder, Red pepper or cayenne, 
Mix all ingredients in a 2-gallon cooker. Simmer on very low heat for 30-45  minutes.    GORDON WHITE    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
