Strawberry Apple Frost
Sugar, Artificial sweetener; equal, Apple juice; no sugar added, Strawberries; very ripe, Yogurt, plain, -Ice cubes, Vanilla extract, 
Combine all ingredients except reserved berries in blender container;  process until frothy. Divide into 2 stemmed glasses; garnish each serving  with a strawberry.    Per serving: 134 calories, 4 g. protein, 4 g. fat; 19 g. carbohydrate, 53  g. sodium    SERVES: 2 SOURCE: _Weight Watchers 365-Day Menu Cookbook_
