Alligator Etouffee
Onions; chopped, Alligator meat; cut in thin, Parsley; minced, Rice; cooked, Stalks celery; chopped, Rotel tomatoes, Cayenne pepper to taste, Green Onions, Garlic, salt and pepper, Sticks butter, 
Saute onions, garlic and celery in butter until soft. Add Rotel tomatoes  and simmer for 20 minutes in covered iron pot. Add alligator meat and let  cook over low heat until tender, about 1 hour. If gravy is too thick, add a  little hot water. Serve over cooked rice.
