Prune Cake 2
Prunes; cook in water 20 min, milk, Apple, Salt, Nutmeg, Allspice, Shortening, Eggs, Flour, Baking soda, Sugar, Walnuts, 
Cream shortening, sugar, and add eggs.  Sift together flour, salt, baking  soda, nutmeg, allspice.  Stir in prunes, nuts and apples.    Pour in 9 inch square pan or 3 round tins.    Bake 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
