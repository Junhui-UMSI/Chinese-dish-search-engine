Pickled Beet and Onion Sa
Onions, VINEGAR CIDER, CINNAMON GROUND 1 LB CN, JUICE FROM BEETS, CLOVES GROUND, PEPPER BLACK 1 LB CN, SUGAR; BROWN, 2 LB, BEETS #10, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, Salt, 
1.  DRAIN BEETS; RESERVE JUICE FOR USE IN STEP 2 AND BEETS FOR USE IN STEP  4.  IF WHOLE BEETS ARE USED, SLICE.    2.  COMBINE RESERVED JUICE, VINEGAR, CINNAMON, CLOVES, SALT, PEPPER,AND  SUGAR    3.  COVER; BRING TO A BOIL; REDUCE HEAT; SIMMER 10 MINUTES.  COOL.    4.  COMBINE BEETS AND ONIONS.    5.  POUR SAUCE OVER BEETS AND ONIONS. COVER; REFRIGERATE AT LEAST 3 TO 4  HOUR  BEFORE SERVING, KEEP REFRIGERATED UNTIL READY TO SERVE.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 4, 2 LB 12 OZ DRY ONIONS A.P. WILL YIELD 2 LB 8 OZ  THINLY  SLICED ONIONS.    NOTE:  2.  IN STEP 4, 5 OZ (1 2/3 CUPS) DEHYDRATED ONIONS MAY BE USED. SEE  RECIPE CARD A01100.
