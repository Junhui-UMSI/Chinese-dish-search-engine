Terre Haute Meatballs
Salt to taste, Egg, Onion; grated, Bottle chili sauce, Ground beef, Jar (medium) grape jelly, Lemon, 
Combine ground beef, egg, onion and salt. Mix well and shape into 50 to 60  small balls. Make sauce of chili sauce, lemon juice and grape jelly. Drop  meatballs into sauce; simmer until brown. Heat thoroughly. Serve from  chafing dish wilh cocktail picks. Can be made into bigger balls and served  as a main dish.    TERRE HAUTE HOUSE    MONROE, LA    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
