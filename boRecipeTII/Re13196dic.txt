Working Girls Casserole
Onions, Medium potatoes, Grated cheese, Hamburger, 
Cook and mash potatoes till creamy.  Put in a casserole and using spoon  hollow out hole in center about the size of a saucer.  Brown loose  hamburger and onions.  Fill hole in potatoes with hamburger and onions.  Cover with grated cheese.  Place in oven at 450 degrees until cheese melts  and is bubbly and youre ready to eat.  It travels good too.
