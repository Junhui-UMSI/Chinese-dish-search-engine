Tomato Gravy
Salt, Sugar, Tomatoes; peeled, 
Cut tomatoes into quarters; put in a saucepan, and bring to a boil. Remove  from heat; put through a food mill, or blend well in an electric blender.  Add sugar and salt. Return mixture to saucepan, and simmer until thick.  Serve over dried peas or beans.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy  Coleman.
