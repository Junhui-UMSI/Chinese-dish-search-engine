Barbecue Pecans
Catsup, Salt, Butter, Worcestershire Sauce, Hot sauce, Pecans; halves, 
Melt butter in a large saucepan; add Worcestershire sace, catsup, and hot  sauce.    Stir in nuts; spoon into a glass baking dish, spreading evenly. toast at  400 degrees about 20 minutes, stirring frequently.    Turn out on absorbent towles, and sprinkle with salt.
