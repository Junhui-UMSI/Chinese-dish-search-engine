Pumpernickel Pus Lumps
Pumpernickel bread, Bread knife, Toaster, Bowl, TOOLS, Colander, Spoon, Jar capers, Tub whipped cream cheese, 
Carefully drain the capers through a colander over the sink. Place the  drained capers through a bowl.    Scoop the cream cheese into the bowl of capers and mix gently.    Toast the bread. Spread each slice with a generous glop of pus lumps and  serve, open faced, immediately. Serves 4 infectous friends.
