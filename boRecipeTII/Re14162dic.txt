White Fudge
Granulated sugar, Butter, Vanilla, Candied cherries, Salt, Walnuts, White corn syrup, Sour cream, 
Combine first five ingredients in sauce pan: bring to a boil  slowly,stirring until sugar dissolves. Boil, without stirring, over medium  heat, to 236 deg. on candy thermometer, or unti a little l mixture dropped  into cold water forms a soft ball. Remove from heat and let stand for 15  minutes; DO NOT STIR. Add flavoring; beat until mixture starts to lose its  gloss (about 8 minutes). Stir in the candied cherries and walnuts and  wuickly pour into a greased shallow pan. Cool and cut into squares    Makes 1 1/2 pounds of fudge.
