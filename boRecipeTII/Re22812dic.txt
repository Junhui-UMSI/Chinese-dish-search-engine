Apricot Torte
Powdered sugar, Cooked dried or canned, Cream; whipped, Vanilla wafers, Eggs, Butter, 
Roll wafers into crumbs. Pack crumbs on bottom of 8- or 9-inch square pan,  reserving 1/2 cup for top. Cream butter & sugar, add well beaten egg yolks  & mix well. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Pour over crumbs. Spread  apricots on top. Cover with cream & sprinkle with remaining crumbs.  Refrigerate at least 12 hours. Cut in squares to serve.    MRS JOHN T.
