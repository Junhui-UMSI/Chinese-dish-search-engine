Wacky Soy Cake
DRY INGREDIENTS, Vanilla, Cocoa, All-purpose flour, Vinegar, Frosting; optional, Sugar, Lukewarm water, Salt, Baking soda, WET INGREDIENTS, Vegetable oil; plus, Vegetable oil; preferably, Soy flour, 
Stir together the dry ingredients into an ungreased 8" x 8" or 9" x 9" pan.  Make three holes in sifted ingredients. Put vanilla in the first hole,  cooking oil in the second hole and vinegar in the third hole. Pour 1 cup  lukewarm water over the whole thing. Stir well with a fork. Bake 30 to 35  minutes at 350 degrees F or until top springs back when touched slightly.    Nutritional information per serving (does not include frosting): 125  calories, 0 cholesterol, 5 grams fat, 5 grams protein, 17 grams  carbohydrate    Sources: http://soyfoods.com/SimplySoy/index.html and the U.S. Soyfoods  Directory Web site http://soyfoods.com/recipes/ Posted to Digest  eat-lf.v097.n184 by KitPATh <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu> on Jul 21, 1997
