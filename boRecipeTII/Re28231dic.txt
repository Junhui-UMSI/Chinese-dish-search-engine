Baked Fish with Hot and Spicy Spinach
Egg white, Hot pepper flakes, , Soy sauce, Garlic, Fresh clean cilantro, Fresh, spinach, fresh ginger, Dark sesame oil, 
cod, salmon, tuna or sole filets    Fit food processor with metal blade and whir all ingredients together.  Place the aove mixture on fresh filsh filets and bake in 325 F. oven until  fish flakes easily, about 15-29 minutes. Really great on salmon, tuna, cod  or sole.
