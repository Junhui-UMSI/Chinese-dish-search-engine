Asparagus Tacos
Monterey jack cheese;, Olive oil; for brushing, Pieces asparagus; grilled, Pepper, White Cheddar cheese;, Yellow corn tortillas, Salt, 
Prepare grill.    For each taco, spread 1/4 of the cheeses and 4 pieces of the asparagus on  each tortilla, Season to taste with salt and pepper. Fold in half. Brush  the outside lightly with olive oil.    Grill for 3 minutes on each side or until tortillia is crispy and cheese  has melted.
