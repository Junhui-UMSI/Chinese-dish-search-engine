Pumpkin Bundt Cake
Cinnamon, Pecans, Baking Powder, Cooked, mashed pumpkin, Eggs, Sugar, Baking soda, Vegetable oil, Salt, Flour, Chocolate chips, 
sift flour, baking soda, baking powder cinnamon and salt together  twice.  Beat eggs and sugar together. Add oil and pumpkin, then blend in  flour mixture. Fold in pecans and chocolate chips. Bake in greased and  floured bundt pan at 350F for 60 minutes. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes,  then turn out onto cooling rack.
