Tea Biscuits
Flour, Salt, Baking Powder, Sugar, Shortening, 
mix well.    Beat 2 eggs and add enough milt to make a soft dough (dont you love old  "precise" recipes??), roll out 1/2" thick, cut with a glass, and cook in a  rather hot oven about 450 for 12-13 min.    makes a lot but they freeze well (I generally eat them all before they go  anyway...freeze? like anything ever lasts that long here!!
