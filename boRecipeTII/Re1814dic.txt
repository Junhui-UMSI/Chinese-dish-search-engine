Chicken Macaroni Casserole
Sliced mushrooms , Condensed cream of mushroom, milk, Salt, Black Pepper, elbow macaroni, cheddar cheese, chicken, 
OVEN METHOD: Mix all ingredients in ungreased casserole dish. Cover and  cook in 350 degree oven till macaroni is tender, about 55-60 minutes.    RANGE-TOP METHOD: Mix all ingredients in large skillet. Heat to boiling,  stirring occasionally; reduce heat. Cover and simmer, stirring  occasionally, until macaroni is tender, about 15 minutes.
