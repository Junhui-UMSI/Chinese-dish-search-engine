Vermicelli Salad
French salad dressing, Fresh parsley -- chopped, Salt, Garlic cloves -- mashed, Caraway seed, Cayenne pepper, Poppy seeds, Vermicelli, Celery seed, Celery, Green onions -- chopped, Sweet pickle juice, 
Break vermicelli into 1" pieces, and cook according to package directions.  Mix pickle juice, French dressing, garlic, and seasonings and pour over  warm vermicelli. Chill. One hour before serving, add celery, green onions  and parsley.
