College Casserole
Ground beef, Whole kernel corn, Undrained tomatoes; cut up, Onions, Chopped bell pepper, (7 oz) corkscrew macaroni;, Sliced celery, Condensed tomato soup, 
Saute beef, onion, bell pepper, and celery until meat is brown; drain. In  large bowl, mix all ingredients and transfer to casserole dish. Cover with  foil and cook at 350 degrees for 1 hour. Remove foil last 10 minutes of  cooking.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by IceTeaLuvr@aol.com on Aug 22, 1998,
