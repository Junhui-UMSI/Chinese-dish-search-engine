Pumpkin Spice
Ground cinnamon, Ground allspice, Use 1 tsp for 3 tb total, Use 1/8 tsp for trial amount, Ground cloves, Powdered ginger, Grated nutmeg, 
Combine all ingredients (or those of your choice) and store in an airtight  jar.  Try a little in plain oatmeal cookies, white or raisin bread.    Source:  Cut Your Bills in Half - Rodale Press Shared by Elizabeth Rodier  Oct 93, tested without nutmeg and allspice
