Beehive Peanut Butter Cookies
Egg, Shortening, Peanut butter, Brown Sugar, Honey, Baking soda, Salt, Flour, 
In mixing bowl, cream shortening, peanut butter, honey and sugar. Add egg,  beat well. Stir together flour, baking soda and salt. Stir into creamed  mixture. Mix well. Cover and chill 1 hour. Using 1 level tablespoon of  dough for each cookie, shape into small balls. Place on greased cookie  sheet. Flatten with a lightly floured fork. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to  12 minutes. Let cool 1 minute. Remove from cookie sheet. Cool on wire rack.  Makes 3 1/2 dozen cookies.
