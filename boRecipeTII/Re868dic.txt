Honey Mustard Chicken
Miracle Whip Salad Dressing, Dijon mustard, Honey, Chicken breasts; boneless, 
Source: The Four Ingredient Cookbook    Combine salad dressing, mustard and honey. Brush chicken with 1/2 of  mixture and broil 8 - 10 minutes per side. After turning chicken, baste  with remaining mixture.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by MUFFYMOM@aol.com on Dec 4, 1998, converted  by MM_Buster v2.0l.
