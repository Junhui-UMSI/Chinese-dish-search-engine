Best Soft Sugar Cookies
Flour, Baking soda, Sugar, Butter, Salt, Eggs, Sour cream, 
Sift flour, soda and salt together well.    In a large bowl, mix remaining ingredients and beat 2 minutes.    Add flour mixture and stir until well mixed. Roll on floured board about      1/4    inch thick.    Bake 10 - 12 min at 400.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by ".sharon hansen" <shahan@hubwest.com> on  Aug 12, 1998,
