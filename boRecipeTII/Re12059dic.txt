Zippy Tomato Appetizer
Lemon juice, Cocktail, Can vegetable juice, Can condensed beef broth, Dried marjoram -- crushed, 
In slow-cooking pot, combine broth, marjoram, vegetable juice cocktail, and  lemon juice.  Cover and heat on low for 2 to 3 hours.  Serve hot from  slow-cooking pot.
