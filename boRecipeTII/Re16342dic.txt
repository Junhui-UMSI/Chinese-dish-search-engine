Watkins Grilled Oriental Steak
Reduced-sodium soy sauce, WATKINS Oriental Five Spice, Top Round Steak, Cider vinegar, WATKINS Ginger Garlic, Powder, Liquid Spice Blend, WATKINS Red Pepper Flakes, 
Combine the first 5 ingredients in a shallow dish;mix well.  Add meat to  marinade, turning to coat.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.  Grill meat  over medium coals 12 to 15 minutes per side for medium-rare, basting  occasionally with leftover marinade.  Let stand 10 minutes.  Slice into  very thin slices across the grain.
