Baked Fish with Potatoes
sugar, Stewed tomatoes, Bay Leaves, Filet of fish, Green pepper, Onions, Celery, Potatoes, Garlic, olive oil, parsley, 
Saute onions, pepper, celery, garlic, and parsley in olive oil. Add blended  tomatoes and bay leaves. Cook approximately for 1 hour, add sugar. Oil a  roasting pan, season the fish with salt and pepper. Place fish in pan with  cut-up potatoes. Pour sauce over potatoes and fish. Bake for approximately  1 hour. Basting every 15 minutes.
