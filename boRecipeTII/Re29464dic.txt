Baking Mix, Low Sodium
Flour, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Low-sodium baking powder, 
Sift together flour, baking powder and sugar twice in a large bowl. Add oil  slowly, using pastry blender, until mixture is texture of coarse corn meal.  Store in a tightly covered container at room temperature or in the  refrigerator. Mix will keep two months at room temperature, longer in the  refrigerator. Spoon mixture into a cup lightly and level with a knife or  spatula. Yield: 12 Cups. Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Marti Wolek <mwolek@techline.com> on Mar  09, 1998
