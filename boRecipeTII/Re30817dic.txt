Tangy Broiled Salmon
Worcestershire Sauce, Lemon wedges, Butter, Catsup, Salmon fillets, Vinegar, 
Combine butter, vinegar, catsup & Worcestershire. Place fish, skin  side down, on broiler: brush with sauce. Broil 6" from heat for 15  minutes, brushing often with sauce. Serve with lemon wedges.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
