Pecan Candy
Pecans, Vanilla, Corn syrup, Whipping cream, Baking soda, Sugar, Salt, 
Mix sugar, corn syrup & salt in saucepan. Bring mixture to a boil. Add  pecans & cook until soft ball stage (225 degrees). Remove from heat & add  soda & vanilla. Beat vigorously until candy begins to cool & thickens. Pour  into a greased 13x9-inch pan. When cool, cut into 1-inch squares.
