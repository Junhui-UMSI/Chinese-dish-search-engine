Asian Fruit Salad with Papaya-Mint Sauce
whole lychees, Sugar, Seedless green grapes;, Papaya, Seedless red grapes, Pineapple, PAPAYAMINT SAUCE, Cantaloupe, Fresh mint, Papaya, Fresh lime juice, 
Mix first 6 ingredients in large bowl. (can be made 4 hours ahead. Cover;  chill.) Spoon fruit into 6 small bowls or goblets. Drizzle Papaya-Mint  Sauce over fruit. Sprinkle with coconut. Garnish with mint.    Payapa-Mint Sauce: Puree all ingredients in processor until smooth.  Transfer to bowl. Cover and refrigerate until ready to use. (Can be  prepared 1 day ahead. Keep refrigerated.)    This sauce is also nice over vanilla frozen yogurt.
