Pudding, Bread
Sugar, Egg, Scalded milk, Butter, Stale bread crumbs, Salt, 
Soak  bread  crumbs  in  milk,  add  sugar,  butter, egg slightly  beaten,and salt.  Flavor to taste and bake in a moderate oven.    Time in oven, 1 hour.  Temp., 325 degrees.  Servings, 6.    VARIATIONS:  Melt two squares of chocolate and add to scalded milk.  Cararmelize  three  tablespoons of sugar; dissolve in hot water to make a  syrup and add to the scalded milk.    Add spice and raisins.
