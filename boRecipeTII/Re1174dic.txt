Creamy Dill Dressing
Silken soft tofu, Garlic, fresh dill, Lemon juice, Water; **, Dijon mustard, Salt, 
* For best results, use the fresh (refrigerated) tofu, not aseptic-packed.    ** Original was 3 tbsp olive oil, but water works to thin the dressing,  too.    Drain the tofu in a fine-mesh strainer for 15 minutes.    Process all ingredients in the food processor until smooth. Use immediately  or refrigerate up to 5 days.    Per serving: 208 Calories; 12g Fat (45% calories from fat); 20g Protein;  12g Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 2344mg Sodium Food Exchanges: 2 1/2 Lean  Meat; 1/2 Vegetable; 1 Fat    NOTES : This dressing is also tasty on steamed new potatoes and shredded  cabbage.
