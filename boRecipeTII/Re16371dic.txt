Wedding Chicken
Container (8-oz) sour cream, Poppy seeds, Cream of chicekn soup, Ritz crackers, Cooked chicken, Soup can of water, Stick margarine/butter, 
(I got this recipe from a high school friend named Brenda Thurman)    Mix liquid ingredients. Add chicken. Melt butter in 13- x 9- inch casserole  dish. Pour of butter. Put mixture in casserole dish. Cover with crushed  Ritz crackers. Pour butter over Ritz crackers. Top with poppy seeds. Bake  at 350 for 30 minutes.
