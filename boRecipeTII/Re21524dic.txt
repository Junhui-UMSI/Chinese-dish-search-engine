Apple Pie Filling
Flour, Cinnamon, Lemon juice, Apples, Sugar, Nutmeg, 
Wash, peel, core and slice apples. Treat to prevent darkening. Combine  sugar, flour and spices. Rinse and drain apples; stir into sugar mixture.  Let stand until juice begins to flow, about 30 minutes. Stir in lemon  juice. Cook over medium heat until mixture begins to thicken. Ladle pie  filling into canor-freeze jars or plastic freezer boxes, leaving 1/2-inch  headspace. Cool at room temperature, not to exceed 2 hours. Seal, label and  freeze.  Yield:  about 6 pints.
