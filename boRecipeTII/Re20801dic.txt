Apple Fritters - Frittelle Di Mele
Sugar, Salt, Apples; peeled, cored,, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Active dry yeast, All-purpose flour;, Egg yolk, 
Dissolve the yeast in 3/4 cup of warm water. Stir in the flour, salt,  sugar, and the egg yolk. Let the batter rest for 20 minutes.    Heat the oil until it registers 350 on a thermometer. Dip slices into  batter and deep fry until golden on both sides. Remove with slotted spoon  and blot dry with paper towels. Serve immediately.
