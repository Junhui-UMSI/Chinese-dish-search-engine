Backdoor Chili
Cayenne, Beef chuck tender, cut into, Ground cumin, ****PART 2****, White pepper, Chicken bouillon granules, Chili powder, Onion powder, Garlic powder, (8 oz.) tomato sauce, ****PART 1****, To 2 tablespoons cooking oil, 1-inch cubes, Tabasco, (14 1/2 oz.@) beef broth, Salt to taste, Beef bouillon granules, 
Heat oil in Dutch oven. Cook beef in oil, until beef is browned. Stir in  remaining ingredients of Part 1. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer  uncovered about 1 1/2 hours, adding water as needed, until beef is tender.  Stir in the ingredients of Part 2. Simmer uncovered about 30 minutes or  until desired consistancy is reached.    FROM BETTY CROCKERS INTERNATIONAL CHILI SOCIETY 1990 $25,000 WINNER.
