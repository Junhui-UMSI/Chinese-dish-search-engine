Strawberry Soda (Mf)
Superfine sugar, Water, Mint for garnish, Strawberries, washed and, 
Bring sugar and water to a boil. Add strawberries and remove from heat.  Steep strawberries until syrup is cool. Puree in a blender and pass through  a sieve. Fill half of glass with strawberry puree, then add ice and club  soda, seltzer or gingerl ale; garnish glass with mint.
