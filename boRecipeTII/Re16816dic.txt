All Purpose Marinade
Dry white wine, Garlic powder, Onion powder, Cayenne pepper, Soy sauce, 
Mix all ingredients together.  Marinate the meat (beef, pork, chicken, or  game) for 3 to 6 hours, then use the marinade as a basting sauce as the  meat cooks on the grill.
