Texas T-Bones
Black peppercorns, Dijon mustard, Margarine, garlic, Lime juice, Beef T-bone steaks, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt and pepper, 
Trim fat on beef steaks to 1/4 inch thickness.  Rub garlic on beef. Press  peppercorns into beef.  Mix remaining ingredients, except salt and pepper;  reserve.    Heat coals or gas grills.    Cover and grill beef 4 to 5 inches from medium heat 16 to 18 minutes for  medium doneness, turning once. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.    Place beef on warm platter; remove bones.  Cut beef at slanted angle into  thin slices.  Serve with reserved margarine mixture. Makes 4 servings.
