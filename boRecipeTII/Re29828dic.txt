Tender Beef Filet
Tenderloin filet steaks, Worcestershire Sauce, Chopped parsley, Garlic, Brandy, Salt and pepper, Butter, 
Pound steaks with a meat mallet until thin Melt butter in a pan. Add steaks  and sear on each side over high heat Remove steaks and keep warm    To pan juices add garlic, salt, pepper parsley and Worcestershire sauce.    Heat and simmer for 3 minutes. Return steaks to the pan; baste and simmer  for 2 minutes pour brandy over steaks; simmer 3 or 4 minutes. Remove steaks  and serve immediately with Bearnaise Sauce (optional)  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Gr8seeksM8@aol.com on Feb 14, 1998
