Zuppa Di Raffano
Dry white wine, Chicken Stock, Speck, in 1/4" dice, Spanish onions, finely, Lard, Freshly grated horseradish,, Heavy cream, Scallions, thinly sliced, Potatoes, peeled, in 1/4", 
In a heavy bottomed 6 quart soup pot, heat lard and speck until smoking  over medium heat. Add onions and cook until soft but not brown, about 8 to  10 minutes. Add potatoes, horseradish, chicken stock, cream and wine and  bring to a boil. Boil 15 minutes and lower heat to medium. Cook just below  the boil for 1 hour, until potatoes have fallen apart. Season with salt and  pepper and garnish with scallions, fresh horseradish and poppy seeds.    Yield: 4 servings    Recipe by: MOLTO MARIO Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #587 by Sue  <suechef@sover.net> on Apr 24, 1997
