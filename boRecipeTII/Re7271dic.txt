Pork Chop Dinner
Cream of chicken soup, salt and pepper, Potatoes, Butter, milk, -(up to), Pork Chops, 
From:    Kristen Klusmire <kristen@MICROLINK.NET>    Date:    Sun, 4 Aug 1996 21:19:54 -0500  Brown pork chops in butter about 5 minutes on each side.  Salt and pepper  each pork chop.  Lay sliced potatoes in greased 8 x 8 inch pan.  Mix cream  of chicken soup with milk. Pour this mixture over the potatoes. Place pork  chops on top of soup mixture.  Cover.  Bake at 350 for 1 hour or until  potatoes are done.    EAT-L Digest  3 August 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
