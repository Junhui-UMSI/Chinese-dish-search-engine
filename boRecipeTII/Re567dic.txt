Frozen Bananas
Bananas, Popsicle sticks, wooden, Orange or lemon juice, Chocolate chips, Vegetable oil, Peanuts or walnuts; chopped, Coconut, 
Cut bananas in half and insert popsicle stick lengthwise through center.  Roll in orange or lemon juice and place on waxed paper. Place in freezer  until frozen firm. Melt chocolate chips and oil on top of double boiler.  Spoon chocolate over frozen bananas and then roll in nuts and coconut. Work  fast as the chocolate dries fast.    from: The Daily Herald, 2/14/95    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Jeanne Geake <jgeake@burgoyne.com> on Oct  16, 1998
