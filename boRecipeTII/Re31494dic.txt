Sweet-And-Sour Dipping Sauce
Red wine vinegar, Hot sauce, Mango chutney, Dark brown sugar, Vegetable oil, Dijon mustard, 
Combine all ingredients; stir well, and chill. Yield: 1-1/2 cups (serving  size: 1 tablespoon).    Serving Ideas : Serve with griller shrimp or pork.    Recipe by: Cooking Light, April 1995, page 77    Posted to bbq-digest by wight@odc.net on May 16, 1998
