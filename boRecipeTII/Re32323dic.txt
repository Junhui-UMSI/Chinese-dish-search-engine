Bargain Barn Caramel Chews
Eggs; slightly beaten, Margarine, Vanilla, (16-oz) brown sugar, Pecans, Flour, 
Preheat oven to 350?. Melt margarine. Add remaining ingredients and mix  well. Mixture will be thick. Pour into a 9x13 baking pan. Bake for 25 to 30  minutes. Check for doneness after 25 minutes. DO NOT OVERBAKE. These freeze  nicely. Yield: 24 to 28 squares.    JANE FAUST (MRS. NORMAN)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
