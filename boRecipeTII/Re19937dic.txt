Apple Brown Betty with Hard Sauce
Cinnamon raisin bread, Eggs, HARD SAUCE, milk, Apples; 1/2" dice (6 to 8), Cinnamon, Butter, Moonshine; (or Bourbon), Sugar, Molasses, Vanilla extract, Powdered sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cube raisin bread, and mix with apples. Spread  evenly in a 9 x 9-inch baking dish. In a bowl mix eggs with milk, sugar,  cinnamon, molasses, and vanilla to combine well. Pour over the bread and  apples. Bake for 18 to 22 minutes until set. Remove and let cool. Combine  all hard sauce ingredients and mix well. Serve w. the Betty.
