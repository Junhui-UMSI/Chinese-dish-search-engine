Peanut Butter Broiled Frosting
Margarine, milk, Brown sugar, Pecans, Peanut butter, 
Cream margarine, sugar and peanut butter.  Add milk and stir well.  Then add nuts and scread over warm cake.  Place under broiler about 4  inches from source of heat.  Broil a few seconds or until topping  starts to bubble.    Watch carefully to avoid scorching.
