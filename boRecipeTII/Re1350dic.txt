Easy Crab Imperial
FROM CHEF FREDDYS, Crab meat, Mayonnaise, Egg whites, Salt, Pepper, Bread crumbs, 
1)      Mix the crab meat and the mayonnaise in a bowl, then fold in the  egg whites, salt & pepper... 2) Pour into a greased baking dish, and  sprinkle with the bread crumbs... 3) Bake in a 350=F8 oven for for 45 min.
