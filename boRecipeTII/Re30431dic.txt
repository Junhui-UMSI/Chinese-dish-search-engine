Banana Guava Pie
Sliced bananas, Guava nectar, Sugar, Lemon juice, Cornstarch, Salt, Cold water, Baked pie shell, 
Combine guava nectar, lemon juice, sugar and salt. Bring to boil over  low heat. Mix cornstarch and water to a smooth paste and stir into  mixture. Stir until thickened and clear. Cool. Combine with bananas  and pour into baked pie shell. Serve with whipped cream.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
