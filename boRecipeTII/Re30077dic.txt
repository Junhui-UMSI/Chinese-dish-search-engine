Balsamic Pesto
Black Pepper, olive oil, Packed fresh basil leaves, Garlic, Salt, Soft bread crumbs, Balsamic vinegar, Fresh parmesan cheese;, 
Combine basil, cheese, garlic, salt, pepper, oil and bread crumbs in food  processor. Pulse to combine. Add vinegar and process until smooth    Per serving: 88 Calories; 5g Fat (53% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 9g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 257mg Sodium    NOTES : This unusual pesto is adapted from "The Balsamic Vinegar Cookbook"  by Meesha Halm. Mary Carroll is the author of the "No Cholesterol (No  Kidding!) Cookbook" and she cut back the amount of oil by substituting  bread crumbs as a thickener.
