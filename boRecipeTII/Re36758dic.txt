Strawberry Jelly
Of salt;, Arrowroot;, Strawberries; hulled washed, Lemon juice;, Envelopes unflavored gelatin, Non-nutritive sweetener, 
Mix gelatin, lemon juice, salt, and arrowroot; add strawberries.  Boil, stirring constantly, about 3 minutes.  Remove from heat; stir in  sweetener. Fill and seal jars.
