Lemon Milk Sherbet
milk, Sugar, Rind Of 1 Lemon, grated, Lemons, juice only, 
Stir milk and sugar until sugar is dissolved. Freeze until it begins to  thicken. Add rind and juice. Freeze, stirring often.    Serves 6 to 8    Source: "Mountain Measures" -- Junior League of Charleston, WA ed. 1974    Recipe by: Mrs. Hugh G.
