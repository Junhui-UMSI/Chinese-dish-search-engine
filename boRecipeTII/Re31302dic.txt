Bandits Chili
Cider vinegar, Water, Lean ground beef, Cumin, 16-oz. red kidney beans with, 16-oz. crushed tomatoes (2, Onion, Allspice, Coriander, Garlic, Green pepper; finely chopped, Salt, Tabasco; to taste, Chili powder, 
Cook beef, garlic, onion, and green pepper in a skillet over med-high heat,  stirring frequently to break up meat. Cook until onion is soft and meat has  lost its pink color.  Add remaining ingredients, except Tabasco. Bring to  boil.  Cover and reduce heat. Add Tabasco. Simmer for 45 mins., stirring  frequently.    For thicker sauce and fuller flavor, add mesa flour. For the best flavor,  let simmer longer.  Recipe can be doubled; freezes well.    Source:  Ann Dunlap of Tampa, FL (TPA Trib, 2/24/94)  :: MM by Sue Woodward
