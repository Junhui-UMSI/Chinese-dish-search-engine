Tangy Chicken Breasts
Salt, Pepper, Worcestershire, Paprika, Sour cream, Celery salt, Bread crumbs, -(up to), Water, Lemon juice, Garlic juice, Butter, Chicken breasts, 
Coat chicken breasts with all ingredients, except butter, crumbs and water.  Place in buttered, flat baking dish. Marinate several hours. Sprinkle with  bread crumbs and place generous pat of butter on each. Bake at 350 for 1  hour 10 minutes until tender. If pan becomes too dry, add a little water.
