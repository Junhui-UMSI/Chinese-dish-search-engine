Pineapple-Pretzel Salad
Sugar, margarine, Cream cheese, pretzels, Crushed pineapple, Cool Whip, Sugar, 
Mix margarine, 3 Tbs. sugar and pretzels.  Put in 9 x 13 pan and bake at  400 degrees for 10 minutes. Take a spatula and loosen pretzels from bottom  of pan. Cool. Blend cream cheese, l/2 c. sugar, Cool Whip and drained  pineapple.  Spread on top of cooked pretzels.  Refrigerate.
