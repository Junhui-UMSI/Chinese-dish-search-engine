Weissgerbers Third Street Pier Coleslaw
Carrot; shredded, Head green cabbage; finely, Mayonnaise, White vinegar, Salt, Sugar, 
Cole slaw and potato salad are two of the fish fry go-withs on the menu at  Third Street Pier. Photo/Jeffrey Phelps    Combine cabbage and carrots in large bowl. Add salt, sugar and vinegar;  marinate 10 minutes. Add mayonnaise and mix well. Chill. Makes 6 servings.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 633 by GramWag@aol.com on Jan  29, 1998
