Veal Scallopini #2
Onion, garlic, Flour, Salt, black pepper, veal shank, -(up to), Mushrooms, portabella, Oregano, Wine, white, Olive oil, 
1.  Add oregano, salt and pepper to the flour.    2.  Dredge the veal cutlets in the flour.    3.  Brown the veal cutlets in a skillet with the olive oil.    4.  Place browned veal cutlets in a casserole.    5.  Saute the mushrooms, onions and garlic in the skillet just recently  vacated by the veal cutlets.    6.  Pour wine over mushrooms, onions and garlic and stir for just a few  seconds.    7.  Pour this mixture over the veal cutlets and place in oven 350 degrees  30 to 45 minutes.    8.  Mangia.
