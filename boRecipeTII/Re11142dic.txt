Raisin Cake From the Pantry
Water or milk, Walnuts, Sifted flour; resifted with, Ground cinnamon and ginger, Dried raisins, Vegetable shortening or, Light or dark brown sugar;, Salt, 
Combine the raisins with sugar, water, shortening and spices and simmer for  a minute. Cool to    room temperature.    Preheat the oven to 300 degrees. Lightly grease a 9inch square baking pan.  Combine cooled raisin    mixture with flour and nuts. Transfer to baking pan and bake 1 hour or  until a toothpick when    inserted in the center comes out dry. Cool in pan and cut into squares.
