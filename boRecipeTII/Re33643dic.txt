Swedish Spice Cake
Sugar, Vanilla, Ginger, Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom, Salt, Sour cream, Flour, Baking soda, Butter, Eggs, Bread crumbs, 
Grease a bundt or 10 in tube pan; sprinkle with bread crumbs; shake out  excess; set aside.  In a large mixing bowl cream butter and sugar until  light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, stir in vanilla. Sift in flour  with baking soda, salt and spices.  Add to sugar mixture alternately with  sour cream, beginning and ending with flour. Spoon batter into prepared  pan.  Bake at 325 for about 1 hour.  Cool in pan 10 minutes. Remove from  pan and cool on rack. Dust top of cake lightly with xxx sugar.
