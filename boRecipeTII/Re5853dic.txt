Piquant Chocolate Sauce
Water, Butter, Lemon, rind and juice,, Soft brown sugar, Golden syrup, Can evaporated milk, 
Measure syrup with hot spoon into pan, add sugar, butter, water, heat  gently, stirring occasionally, until sugar dissolved. Bring to boil, take  off heat, stir in milk, lemon.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #650 by Douglas Weller  <dweller@ramtops.demon.co.uk> on Jun 29, 1997
