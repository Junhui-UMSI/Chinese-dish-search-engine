Phoney Egg Salad
Cayenne, Tumeric, Salt, Green onions, sliced, FF mayo, Red bell pepper, chopped, Tofu (medium or firm),, Curry powder, 
(people should never know it isnt egg)    Mix all the above together. You might want to have a sweet chutney on the  side and up the amount of curry and cayenne.
