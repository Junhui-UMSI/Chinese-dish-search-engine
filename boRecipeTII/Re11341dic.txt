Ranch Style Pork Chops
California dried figs, Apple mint jelly, sliced, Cinnamon, Brown sugar, Lean pork steaks, Tart apple, Salt and pepper, 
Place meat in large shallow baking dish.  Season with salt and pepper.  Top with dried figs and apples.  Sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.  Dot each chop with a dab of jelly.  Bake, uncovered, for 40 minutes at 350  degrees F.  Uncover and bake an additional 20 minutes.
