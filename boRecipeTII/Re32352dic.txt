Barley Honey Muffins
milk, Barley flour, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Eggs, Honey, Brown sugar, 
Combine milk, oil, honey and vanilla. Add beaten eggs and sugar. Mix well.  Sift dry ingredients and add to wet mixture. Mixture will have consisitency  of cookie dough. Place into greased muffin tins. Bake 400 for 20 minutes.  Makes 12 Note: some rasisns or dates may be added to the mixture.
