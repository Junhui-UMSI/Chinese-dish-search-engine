Sweet Valdalia Onion Scallop
Eggs, Well Beaten, salt and pepper, Swiss Cheese, Grated, Cracker Crumbs, Pinemo, Chopped, Margarine, Melted, Butter or margarine, Vidalia Onions, Sliced, Green Pepper, Chopped, Light cream, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease an 8-inch square baking dish; set  aside. In a heavy skillet, melt 1/4 cup margarine over medium heat and cook  the onions and green peppers until tender. Stir in pimento. Place half of  the onion mixture in prepared dish. Sprinkle with half of the grated cheese  and half of the cracker crumbs. Repeat layers of onion and cheese. Beat  eggs with light cream, salt and papper. Pour over onions. Sprinkle top with  cracker crumbs; lightly dirzzle melted margarine over the top. Bake 25  minutes.
