Rabbit Fried
INGREDIENTS:, Flour, Cooking oil, Rabbit, cleaned cut into, Onion, sliced, Salt and pepper, 
DIRECTIONS: If it is a young rabbit, proceed with recipe. A older tougher  rabbit should be boiled in saltwater until tender. Lightly brown onions in  cooking oil. Dredge rabbit pieces in flour. salt and pepper. Put in hot  skillet with oil and onions and cook until brown on both sides. Remove meat  from skillet and make gravy by sprinkling a tbs of flour from dredging in  oil. Cook until brown. At 1 1/2 cups water, return rabbit to mixture and  cook slowly until gravy thickens.
