Tofu Fiesta
Ripe tomato, cubedd, Sesame seeds, toasted, Tofu, drained, Vegetable oil, Sweet green pepper, sliced, Carrots, sliced thin, Diced mushrooms, Chopped fresh parsley, Onions, Garlic, Broccoli pieces, Celery, Ricotta cheese, Ground tumeric, 
Saute the onion and garlic in the oil until the onion is transparent. Add  the broccoli, carrots, pepper, mushrooms and celery and cook until tender.  Break the tofu into chunks. Add the tofu to the vegetables with the  turmeric and cheese.  Simmer to heat thoroughly. Just before serving, add  the tomato and parsley and sprinkle on the sesame seeds.    One serving 1/2 recipe, 383 calories 25 gm. carb, 23 gm protein, 23 gm fat,  149 mg sodium, 1090 mg potassium, 31 mg cholesterol.    1 bread exchange, 2 vegetable, 2 medium fat meat plus 2 fat exchanges.  Source:  American Diabetes Association Holiday Cookbook by Betty Wedman  1986 Shared but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier Nov 93.
