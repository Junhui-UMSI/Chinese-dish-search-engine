Bulgurlu Mercimek Corbasi (Bulgar Lentil Soup)
Vegetable oil, Fresh cilantro; chopped, Diced onions, Dried mint, Tomato paste, Mild or med. red pepper, Salt, Boiling water, Bulgur, Lentils, Flour dissolved in water, 
In a 2 quart saucepan, saute the cilantro, onions, and mint in oil. Add  tomato paste, stirring to blend. Add red pepper, salt, and boiling water to  half fill the pan. Stir in bulgur and lentils and bring the mixture to a  boil. Then cook over medium heat 15-20 minutes. Dissolve flour in a small  amount of water and stir into soup. Add enough water to nearly fill pan.  Cook 30-40 minutes more.
