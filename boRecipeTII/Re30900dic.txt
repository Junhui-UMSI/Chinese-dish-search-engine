Tangy Shrimp Noodle Salad
Uncooked noodles (about 4, Coarsely chopped zucchini, Sliced celery (about I, (4 1/4 ounces) tiny shrimp,, Sliced ripe olives, 
Cook noodles as directed on package;drain. Mix all ingredients except salad  greens Refrigerate at least 2 hours but no longer than 24 hours. Spoon onto  salad greens.          4    servings.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
