Upside Down Gingerbread
Raisins, Sliced apples, Recipe of your favorite, Light molasses, Butter, 
From: <NDooley@president-po.president.uiowa.edu>    Date: Wed, 7 Dec 1994 15:49:00 GMT  A favorite childhood gingerbread treat: Make your favorite recipe of  gingerbread that fits into an 8x8x2 inch baking pan.  Melt  2 T. butter in  the pan. Add 1/2 C. light molasses and 1/4 C. raisins. Heat above  ingredients. Arrange 2 sliced apples in the molasses mixture in the bottom  of the pan. Pour gingerbread batter over and bake at 350 degrees for 50  minutes.    To serve:  While hot, cut into squares and flip servings upside down onto  individual dessert plates.  Offer whipped cream flavored with a small  amount of powdered ginger or nutmeg, or ice cream as an embellishment.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /CAKES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
