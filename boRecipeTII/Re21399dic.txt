Unsweetened Hazelnut Butter
Hazelnuts; (about 7 1/2, Vegetable oil, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    In a baking pan toast hazelnuts in one layer in middle of oven 10 to 15  minutes, or until colored lightly and skins are blistered. Wrap nuts in a  kitchen towel and let steam 1 minute. Rub nuts in towel to remove loose  skins. Do not cool. In a food processor grind hazelnuts to a fine paste.  Add oil and pulse until combined well.
