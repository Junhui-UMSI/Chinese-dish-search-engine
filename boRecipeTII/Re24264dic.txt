Tricolor Salsa
Fresh lime juice, La Carreta cilantro apple, Sugar, Minced serrano chile; with, Red bell pepper; seeded and, Yellow bell pepper; seeded, Minced shallot, Salt, Finely chopped fresh, 
Thoroughly combine all the ingredients together in a mixing bowl... Serve  with grilled steak or oysters. Yield: 1-1/4 cups Posted to CHILE-HEADS  DIGEST V4 #095 by gcaselton@ANTISPAMeasynet.co.uk (Graeme Caselton  BSc(Open)) on Aug 23, 1997
