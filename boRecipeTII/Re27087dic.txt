Thumbprint Buttercreams
Salt, Vanilla, Brown sugar, Egg yolks, Shortening, Flour, sifted, 
Mix ingredients well, then roll into small balls. Dip each ball into  beaten egg white that has been beaten stiff, then into nuts. Bake for  5 minutes at 350. Take out and press thumb in middle. Bake 8 minutes  longer. When cool, fill with buttercream icing.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
