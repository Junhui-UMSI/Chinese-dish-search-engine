Quick Chopped Barbecue
Brown sugar, Salt and tabasco sauce, Shredded cooked pork or, Hamburger buns, Beef broth, Canned tomato puree, Brown mustard, Ground cinnamon, Barbecue sauce; prepared or, 
Combine barbecue sauce, beef broth, brown sugar, mustard, cinnamon and  tomato puree and add shredded pork or ham. Slowly, over very low heat  simmer for 10 to 15 minutes, uncovered until meat is warmed through and  sauce has thickened but is still of spoonable consistency. Season to taste  with salt and Tabasco sauce and spoon over buns. Serve with salad.
