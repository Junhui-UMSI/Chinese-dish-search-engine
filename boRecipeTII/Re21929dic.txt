Applesauce
Honey, Peeled and sliced apples, Ground cinnamon; to taste, Water, 
Place apples? water and honey in a 1 quart casserole. Cover. Microwave at  high (100%) until apples are tender, 10 to 1 2 minutes. Mash to sauce  consistency. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Chill and serve.
