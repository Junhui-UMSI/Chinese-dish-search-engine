Quick Beef Bake
Skim milk; divided, Carrot slices; canned, Green peas, Garlic salt, Ground chuck, Onion, Cream of mushroom soup;, Pioneer Low Fat Biscuit Mix, 
Heat oven to 450 degrees. Cook ground beef and onion until beef is brown;  drain. Spoon into ungreased 13x9x2" baking dish. Stir in soup, 1 cup of the  milk, the garlic salt and vegetables.    Stir baking mix and remaining milk until blended. Pour over beef mixture.    Bake 27 to 30 minutes or until crust is light golden brown. Sprinkle with  parsley if desired.    NOTES : If you choose to use different vegetables or frozen vegetables, the  nutritional information will differ from this recipe.
