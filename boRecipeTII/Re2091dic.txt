Apple Cranberry Compote (Crock Pot)
Sour cream (low fat), Cooking apples, peel, slice, Orange Peel, Fresh cranberries, Sugar, Water, Port wine, 
Arrange apple slices and cranberries in crockpot. Sprinkle sugar over  fruit. Add orange peel, water and wine. Stir to mix ingredients. Cover,  cook on low 4-6 hours, until apples are tender. Serve warm fruits with the  juices, topped with a dab  of sour cream.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Sep  15, 1998
