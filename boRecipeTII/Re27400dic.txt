Toasted Pumpkin Seeds
See instructions, 
Clean pumpkin seens and dry on a paper towel. If desired, the seeds may be  soaked in salted water overnight (1 1/2 tsp. salt per 2/3 cup water). Place  dry seeds in shallow baking pan. Dot with margarine. Sprinkle with salt of  seeds were not soaked overnight. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes  until golden brown. Stir occasionally.    VARIATION: Combine 1 1/2 T. melted margarine with 1 tsp. Worcestershire  sauce. Pour this mixture over 2 cups of seeds. Mix well and bake in a  shallow pan at 250 degrees for one hour.
