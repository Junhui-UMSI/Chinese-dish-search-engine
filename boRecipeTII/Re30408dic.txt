Banana Fritters
Salt, Baking Powder, Vanilla, crushed pineapple, Egg, Unsifted flour, Powdered sugar, Sugar, butter, milk, banana, 
Mix flour, sugar,baking powder and salt. Combine egg, milk and melted  butter; add to dry ingredients. Blend in pineapple, vanilla and banana.  Drop by teaspoonfuls into deep fat (400 degrees) and fry for 2 minutes or  until crisp and brown.    NOTE: Dip spoon into deep fat between fritters; it makes batter slide off  spoon more easily.    Remove and drain onto paper towels. Dust with powdered sugar and serve at  once.    Makes 18    Recipe by: DAZEY CORPORATION    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 518 by ctlindab@mail1.nai.net on  Jan 14, 1998
