Tuna Spaghetti
Margarine or butter, Oregano, Half-and-half, Garlic, Dried basil, Thin spaghetti, Tuna, Parmesan cheese, Pimiento-stuff olives, 
Cook spaghetti as directed on package; drain.  Cook garlic in margarine  in 2-quart saucepan until garlic is golden brown. Stir in half-and-half,  basil and oregano. Heat to boiling. Stir in tuna, olives, and cheese. Boil  and stir 1 minute.  Pour over hot spaghetti. Sprinkle with snipped parsley  if desired. 5 servings: 375 calories per serving.
