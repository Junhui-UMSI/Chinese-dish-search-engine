After Thanksgiving Salad
Hard-cooked egg, Shredded cooked turkey or, Mayonnaise, Sweet pickle relish, Pecans, 
In a bowl, mash the egg with a fork.  Add turkey, mayonnaise and relish.  Cover and refrigerate until serving. Stir in pecans just before serving.  Yield: 4 servings.
