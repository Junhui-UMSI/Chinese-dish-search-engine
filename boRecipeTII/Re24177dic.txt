Trail Drivers Goulash
Grated cheese, Onion, Celery stalks, chopped, Salad oil, Red beans, drained, Package spaghetti, cooked, Chili powder, Ground beef, Tomato soup, Salt to taste, Black Pepper, 
1. Brown ground beef lightly in oil; add onion and celery and cook until     onion is yellow and celery is soft. 2. Add tomato soup; rinse cans and  add water. 3. Add beans, spaghetti and seasonings; put in 9 1/2 x 13-inch  baking     dish. 4. Bake at 350F until hot and bubbly. 5. Grated cheese may be put  on top last 15 minutes of baking.
