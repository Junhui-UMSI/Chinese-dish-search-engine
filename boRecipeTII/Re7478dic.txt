Pork Loin Supreme
Pepper, Pork loin roast; (4 lb.), Carrots, Cloves, Water, Sage, Nutmeg, Currant jelly, Dry mustard, Garlic, Salt, Onions, 
Mash the garlic in a bowl with salt, sage, pepper and nutmeg. Rub into  roast. Slice the onions, carrots and place in a shallow roasting pan. Place  the roast, fat side up, on vegetables and pour water into pan. Roast in 325  degrees oven for 1 hour and 30 minutes. Remove roast from oven and score  the fat in crisscross pattern. Combine the jelly and mustard and spread  over roast. Stud with cloves and roast for 1 hour longer. Makes 6 servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Mar 22,  1998
