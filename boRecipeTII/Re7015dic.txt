Popcorn--Sonoma Pop
Popped), Unpopped corn (8 cups, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Dried tomato bits (half of a, Jar), 
Pop popcorn.  Drizzle olive oil over the unseasoned popcorn. Sprinkle with  the dried tomatoes and toss until will blended.
