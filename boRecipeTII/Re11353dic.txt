Ranchero Chili and Rice
Onion; diced, (10.5-oz) condensed, Ground beef or ground turkey, Hot cooked brown rice, Chili powder, 
Cook beef and onion in large skillet about 5 minutes or until beef is  browned, stirring to crumble beef. Add condensed soup and chili powder;  simmer about 10 minutes. Serve over hot rice.
