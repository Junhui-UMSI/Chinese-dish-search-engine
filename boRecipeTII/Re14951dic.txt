3 Step Mini Cheesecake Baskets
Cream cheese; (8 oz), Eggs, Sugar, Licorice, Jelly beans, Vanilla, Coconut, 
Mix together the cream cheese, sugar and vanilla; add eggs. Place 12  vanilla wafers on bottom of 12 paper lined muffin cups. Pour cream cheese  mixture into muffin cups. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 mins or until centers  are almost set. Cool. Refrigerate 2 hours or overnight. Sprinkle with  coconut and jelly beans just before serving. Shape licorice to make basket  handles.    Recipe by: Philadelphia Cream Cheese ad    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by Cairn Rodrigues <cairnann@yahoo.com> on Apr  6, 1998
