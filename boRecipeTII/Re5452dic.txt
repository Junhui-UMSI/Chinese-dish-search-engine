Pineapple Cream Cheese Pizza
Flour, Sugar, (8 oz) cream cheese,, Grated peel and juice from 1, Sliced almonds, toasted, Egg yolks, (8 1/4 oz) cans crushed, Frozen bread dough (1 loaf),, Strawberry jam, 
Servings: 16    Press dough into 2 greased 12 inch pizza pans.    Beat cream cheese, sugar, flour, egg yolks, 1 tsp lemon peel and 2 tsp  lemon juice until smooth. Stir in pineapple. Spread mixture evenly over  pizzas to within 1/2 inch of edges.    Bake in 400 deg. oven for 15 minutes or until browned.    Spread each with jam and sprinkle with almonds. Bake 5 minutes longer.
