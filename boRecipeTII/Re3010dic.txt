Peanut Butter Waffles
Hot tap water, Creamy peanut butter, 
Using a fork, stir 4 tablespoons of hot tap water into a glass that  contains 4 tablespoons of creamy peanut butter. Stir into the basic batter  and call the kids!    From <The Frugal Gourmet Cooks American>.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
