Thousand Island Dressing
Salt, Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing, Onion; Cut Into Chunks, Eggs; Hard Boiled, Quartered, Green Pepper;Cut Into Chunks, Paprika, Celery Stalk; Sliced, Chili Sauce; Not Catsup, 
Put all of the ingredients in a blender container and blend until combined  and the green pepper, celery, and onion are finely chopped.    Makes 2 cups.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/blend1.zip
