Apple-Raisin Crumb Pie
Apples; pared and sliced, Flour, Egg, CRUMB TOPPING, Self rising flour, Sugar, 9 Inch unbaked pastry shell, Sour cream, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Sugar, Butter, Salt, Raisins, Brown sugar, Sprinkle of nutmeg, 
Fill unbaked pastry shell with apples and raisins mixed together. Stir  sugar and flour together and fold in cream, beaten egg, vanilla and salt.  You can substitute fresh cream, but sour is best. Spoon cream mixture over  apples and raisins. Bake at 350~ for about 40 minutes, or until pie is  golden brown. Combine flour, sugars, cinnamon and nutmeg. Blend in 1/3 cup  butter until mixture is crumbly. Spoon crumbs over top of pie and bake 15  minutes more, or until lightly browned.
