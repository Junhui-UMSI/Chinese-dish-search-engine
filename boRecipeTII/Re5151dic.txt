Pierogi Sauerkraut and Mushroom Filling
Sauerkraut, Water -- boiling, Sour cream, Pepper, Mushrooms -- sliced, Hard-boiled egg -- chopped, Onion -- chopped, 
Cook sauerkraut about 20 mins in saucepan.  In frying pan, sautee onion and  mushrooms in butter.  Stir in sauerkraut and pepper.  Fry until sauerkraut  is golden.  Add chopped egg and sour cream.
