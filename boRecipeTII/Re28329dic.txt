Baked Ham and Cheese Sandwich
Mushrooms, milk, SAUCE, Cream of mushroom soup, milk, Bread, Hard boiled eggs, Eggs, Ham; sliced, Cheese, 
Remove crust from bread. Place 8 in the bottom of a greased 9x13 pan. Put 1  slice of ham, 1 slice of cheese on each piece of bread. Put other slice of  bread on top.    Beat eggs and milk together. Pour over the sandwiches. Refrigerate  overnight. Remove in the morning for several hours before baking. Bake at  350 degrees for 1 hour. Pour sauce over sandwiches when serving.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 29, 1998
