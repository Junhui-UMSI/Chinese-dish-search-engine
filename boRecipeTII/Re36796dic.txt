Strawberry Mint Salsa
Lime, juiced, Leaves fresh mint, julienned, Strawberry puree, Strawberries, 
Cut off strawberry tops and cut up. Toss into strawberry puree then add  lime juice. Wrap and refrigerate. To serve, spoon salsa on plate and  sprinkle with mint.
