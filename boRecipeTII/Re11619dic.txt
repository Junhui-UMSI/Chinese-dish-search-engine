Raspberry Sauce
Raspberry preserves, Lemon juice; or to taste, Ingredient, Raspberries;frozen w/sugar, Kirsch, Frozen (no sugar), Raspberries;fresh or->, 
03/09/92        4:40 PM Drain raspberries, reserving syrup. Place  fresh and frozen raspberries and preserves in the bowl of a food processor.  Puree with the metal blade. Pass the puree through a food mill to eliminate  seeds. Stir in kirsch and lemon juice. Source: Bristol Farms Cooking Demo  Shared by Barb Day
