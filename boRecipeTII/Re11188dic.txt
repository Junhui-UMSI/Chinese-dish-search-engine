Raisin Nut Cereal Bars
Ground cinnamon, Raisin Nut Bran cereal, Margarine or butter, Miniature marshmallows, 
Butter square pan, 9x9x2 inches.  Heat margarine, marshmallows and cinnamon  in 3-quart saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly, until smooth;  remove from heat. Stir in cereal until thoroughly coated. Pat into pan;  cool.  Cut into 2-1/4 x 1-1/2-inch bars. Makes 24 bars.
