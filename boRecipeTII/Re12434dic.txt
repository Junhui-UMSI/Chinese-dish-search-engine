Zukini Casserole
Cracker crumbs, Layer of onion rings, Each salt & pepper, Brown sugar, Basil, Large zukini sliced, Grated cheese, Peeled diced tomatoes, 
Layer zukini and peeled and diced tomatoes and then onion rings. Sprinkle  brown sugar and salt, pepper and basil on top then cracker crumbs and  grated cheese . Cover with foil and bake 1/2 hour in 350 oven then remove  foil and continue baking 45 min.
