Persimmon Ice Cream Topping
See below, 
You will need 1 goopy persimmon per guest. Put 1 persimmon in each  cup of a muffin tin. Carefully cut out the stem end & save. Pour 2 T.  of bourbon in each persimmon. Replace the stem end. Bake until warm.  Scoop out of the skin and pour on vanilla ice cream.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
