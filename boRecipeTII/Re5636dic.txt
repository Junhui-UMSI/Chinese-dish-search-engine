Pineapple Sweet Potato Casserole,
Salt, Walnut, Pineapple spears, Marshmallows, Orange juice, Whole sweet potatoes , Butter or margarine, Crushed pineapple , 
Mash potatoes, add butter, salt, and orange juice; beat with electric or  rotary beater until light and fluffy. Add crushed pineapple. Pour into  greased 1-quart casserole. Dot with butter. Bake in a moderate oven (350~)  about 45 minutes.   During last 10 or 15 minutes of baking, top casserole with halved  marshmallows (walnut halves and pineapple spears, too, if desired). Bake  until marshmallows are melted and golden.   Serves 6.     Per serving:  Calories 401; fat 6.1 g (12.9%); cholesterol 15 mg;  carbohydrate 90.3 g (84.5%); fiber 3.6 g; protein 2.8 g; sodium 271 mg;  potassium 573 mg; calcium 62 mg; vitamin A 3035 iu.
