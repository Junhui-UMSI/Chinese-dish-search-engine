Tortellini Basil Soup - Southern Living
Condensed chicken broth, Cheese, Tortellini, Cannellini, Tomato, Fresh basil, Balsamic vinegar, Parmesan, Pepper, Fresh basil, 
Bring broth to a boil in a large Dutch oven. Add tortellini, and cook 6  minutes or until tender. Stir in beans and tomato. Reduce heat, and simmer  5 minutes or until thoroughly heated.    Remove from heat; stir in basil and vinegar. Sprinkle each serving with  cheese and pepper. Garnish, if desired.
