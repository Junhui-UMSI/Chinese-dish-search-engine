Taco Hotdish
Tomato soup, Taco chips, Taco seasoning, Tomatoes, Onion, Lettuce, NANCY HAGFORS GXDB48A, Cottage cheese, Hamburger, Cheddar cheese, 
Crush taco chips and place in lightly-oiled 9x13-inch pan. Brown hamburger  and onion; drain. Add tomato soup and taco seasoning. Mix and pour over  chips. Spread cottage cheese over meat mixture. Add cheddar cheese. Cover  with foil. Bake 40 minutes at 350-degrees.   Serve with shredded lettuce and chopped tomatoes.
