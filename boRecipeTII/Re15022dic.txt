West Indies Chicken Soup with Gouda Cheese
Green onions; minced, Gouda cheese; grated, Dried kidney beans; sorted, Garlic, Diced white chicken meat;, Pumpkin; peeled, seeded, and, Onion; cut into small dice, Light Chicken Stock, Ground coriander, salt and pepper, Ground caraway seeds, olive oil, 
The rich, golden squash paired with plump kidney beans, chunks of chicken  breast, and nutty Gouda cheese make a premium combination for cool-weather  dining.    1.Soak the beans in 6 cups of water overnight. Drain and place in a  heavy-bottomed, 6-quart saucepan. Add the chicken stock and bring to a boil  over high heat. Reduce the heat to moderate and cook, stirring  occasionally, for 2 hours or until the beans are tender.    2.Using a large saute pan, cook the onion, garlic, and spices in the olive  oil over moderate heat for 10 minutes, stirring frequently. Add to the  cooked beans and mix well.    3.Add the pumpkin and bring to a boil over high heat. Cook for 10 minutes,  stirring frequently, until the pumpkin is very tender and the mixture is  slightly thick.    4.Add the chicken, green onions, and cheese, reduce the heat to moderate,  and cook just until the cheese melts and the chicken is cooked through,  stirring constantly.    5.Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.    6.Makes about 9 cups.
