Strawberry Fruit Dip
Strawberry, Frozen Unsweetened, 
Place the strawberries in a blender or food processor, cover and blend or  process until smooth, then pour into a medium bowl. Stir in the spreadable  fruit. Cover and refrigerate for about 1 hour or until chilled. Serve with  cut-up fresh fruit.
