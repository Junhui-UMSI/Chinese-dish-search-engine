Peas with Bacon and Sour Cream
Celery, Sour cream, salt and pepper, Bacon; Fried Crisp, Green Onion; Chopped Fine, Cashews; Coarsely Cut, Pkg. Peas; Thawed, 
Mix together the peas, onions, celery, salt and pepper and sour cream. Just  before serving, add the bacon and cashews. 6 servings. Typed in MMFormat by  cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Salads.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Mar 8,  1998
