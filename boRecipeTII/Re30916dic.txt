Tangy Touchdown Casserole
Onion,chopped, Green pepper,chopped, Catsup, Pork and beans(31oz), Chili powder, Cumin, Kielbasa,sliced, Brown sugar,firmly packed, Garlic powder, Pickles,Mexican,w/1T liquid, 
1. Place all ingredients in a greased 2-quart casserole; mix well.    2. Cover and bake in preheated 350F. oven 35 minutes.
