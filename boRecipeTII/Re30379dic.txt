Banana Crunch Muffins
Egg substitute, Baking soda, Salt, Vanilla, Mashed bananas, Barley/wheat nuggets (grape, Whole wheat flour, Baking Powder, White flour, Sugar, 
In a small bowl mix all dry ingredients together. In a larger bowl mix all  wet ingredients together. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients. Bake for  20-25 minutes (or until toothpick comes out clean) in a 350 degree oven.  This recipe makes about 10 muffins.
