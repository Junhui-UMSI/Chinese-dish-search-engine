Quick Cheese and Pepper Bread
Sharp cheddar cheese; grated, Fresh pepper, Sugar, Salt, Baking soda, Buttermilk, Flour, Margarine, Eggs, Baking Powder, 
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease bottom only of 9X5 loaf pan.    2. Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup, level off.    3. In a medium bowl combine flour, cheese, sugar, baking powder, soda, salt  and pepper.    4. In a small bowl combine buttermilk, margarine and eggs; blend well and  add to dry mixture.    5. Stir ingredients until just moistened, and pour into greased pan.    6. Bake for 35-45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.    7. Cool completely on a wire rack, wrap and store in the refrigerator.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 371 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Dec 15, 1997
