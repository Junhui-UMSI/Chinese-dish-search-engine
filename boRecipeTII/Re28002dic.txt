Baked Chicken and Rice #1
Brown rice, chicken, Onion soup mix, Garlic salt, Hot water, 
Butter baking dish, add 1 cup rice. Sprinkle rice with garlic salt. Lay  chicken pieces on top. Sprinkle onion soup mix over chicken. Add 3 cups hot  water. Bake at 350 degrees for 1-1/2 hours or until done.    FROM "HAVE A RICE DAY!", THE    ARKANSAS RICE DEPOT, 1014 MAIN    LITTLE ROCK, AR 72202    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
