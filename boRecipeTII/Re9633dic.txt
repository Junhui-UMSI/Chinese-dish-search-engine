Pumpkin Loaves
Cinnamon, Sugar, Salt, Canned pumpkin, Nutmeg, Flour, Ground cloves, Eggs, Baking soda, Salad oil, Baking Powder, 
Preheat ove to 350F.  Grease well, two 9x5" loaf pans.  In large bowl, with  fork, mix flour and next seven ingredients.  Add remaining ingredients and  mix just until blended.  Turn batter into pans.  Bake one hour or until  toothpick comes out clean.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #308 by Peg Doolin <peggyvt@ibm.net> on Nov  27, 1997
