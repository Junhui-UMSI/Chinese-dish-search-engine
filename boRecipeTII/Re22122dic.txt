Turkey Meatballs
Turkey, ground, Garlic powder, Onions, Tomato sauce, Bread crumbs, Sage, Carrots, 
Preheat oven at 375.  Combine all ingredients in a medium-size bowl and mix  well.  Shape into balls and place on a non-stick baking sheet.  Bake 35  minutes until browned.
