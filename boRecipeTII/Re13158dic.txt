Wondrous Lowfat Vanilla Sauce for Desserts
Boiling water, Cornstarch, Diet unsalted margarine, Salt, Pure vanilla flavoring, Sugar, 
Combine sugar and cornstarch, then gradually stir in the boiling water,  stirring for 5 minutes on low heat. Remove from heat and add margarine,  vanilla and salt. Serve over Angel Food Cake, lowfat custards and puddings,  fat-free pastries, fruit gelatins or fresh fruit, especially bananas,  mangos, and other non-citrus fruits. Makes about 1 1/2 cups.
