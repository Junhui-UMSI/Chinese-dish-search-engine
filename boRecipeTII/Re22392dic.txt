Turnip Greens W/dumplings
Egg, Salt, Ham hocks, Well-cleaned turnip greens, Water-ground corn meal, -(up to), Boiling water, Sugar, 
In large pan, cover ham hocks in boiling water and simmer 30 minutes. If  ham is salty, pour off water and add fresh water. Simmer 40 more minutes;  add greens and cook until tender. To make dumplings, add cornmeal all at  once to 1-1/2 cups boiling water; stir vigorously until mush is formed; add  sugar & salt. Cool. Add egg and beat well. Drop by tablespoon onto floured  board. Roll into small balls, flatten and dredge lightly with flour. Drop  dumplinngs into boiling greens liquid. Lower heat and cook about 15  minutes.
