Daikon Relish- Sesame Pickled Japanese Radish
Daikon, Vinegar, Salt, Sesame seed oil, Sugar, 
Daikon Relish is pickled without heat. Recipe from Bill Sidnam, who writes  the VEGETABLES column for the Riverside Press-Enterprise.    Scrub the radish; peel (optional). Trim the ends. Thinly slice or finely  julienne. Mix well with the marinade ingredients. Refrigerate at least 2  hours, preferably overnight. Serve as a relish.
