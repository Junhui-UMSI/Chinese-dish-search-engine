Tex Mex Ranch Casserole
10 oz can, Black pepper, Turkey -- ground/cooked, Zucchini -- diced, Pinto beans --, Garlic powder, Oregano, Egg whites, Cooked/mashed, Cheddar cheese -- shredded, Mozzarella cheese, shredded, Cumin, Onion -- chopped, Squash -- diced, Cayenne pepper, Bread crumbs, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a 13 x 9" pan with cooking spray.  In a  mixing bowl, combine turkey, beans, garlic powder, cumin, oregano, and  pepper.  Coarsely mash beans with pastry blender; transfer to prepared pan.  Prepare skillet with oil; add onion.  Cook over medium-high heat 2 minutes.  Add squash, zucchini, and red pepper; cook 3 minutes longer. Spread  vegetable mixture over turkey mixture. In a small bowl, whisk together  cheeses and egg whites; pour over casserole.  Sprinkle with bread crumbs.  Bake for 30 minutes, or until golden brown.
