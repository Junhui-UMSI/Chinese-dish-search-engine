Aji Molido (Chile Paste - Red)
Oil, olive, Salt, Vinegar, Garlic, HEAT SCALE  HOT, Chiles, red, New Mexican, 
If the chiles are dried, soak in water till softened. Remove seeds and  stems. Combine all ingredient in a food processor and puree to a fine  paste. This is a HOT condiment.    This South American chile paste can be used as a substitute whenever  fresh chiles are called for. It will keep for two weeks or more in the  refrigerator. For longer storage, increase the vinegar and reduce the  amount of olive oil.
