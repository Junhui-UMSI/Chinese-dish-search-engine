Apple Crisp #05
Whole wheat flour, Ground cinnamon, Butter, Apples, Ground nutmeg, Brown sugar, 
From: avi@rix.Eng.Sun.COM (Ann Adamcik)    Date: 30 Nov 1993 19:10:58 GMT  Heres an apple crisp recipe that is very easy, very good, and does not use  oatmeal as the topping.  Amounts are approximate:    Wash, core, and slice apples.  Arrange in baking dish.  Mix together flour,  sugar, and spices.  Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly.  Sprinkle flour  mixture on top of apples.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or  until apples are tender.  Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes.  Serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /DESSERTS    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
