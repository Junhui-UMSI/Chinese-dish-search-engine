Tamale De Elote
Roasted fresh corn kernels, Chiles poblanos; finely, Salt, milk, Masa harina, Dried corn husks, Baking Powder, Jack cheese, Butter, 
Soak husks in hot water until pliable.  In a saute pan simmer the milk and  corn together over medium heat until the corn becomes soft (about 10  minutes.  Strain the corn, reserve 1 cup, and puree the remainder with the  milk.  Add the puree to the masa harina, and mix with a spoon or whisk. In  a large bowl, whip the butter, baking powder, and salt together until light  and fluffy. Incorporate the masa in 2 oz increments, whisking until light  and fluffy (about 10 to 15 minutes total). Fold in the green chile,  remaining corn and cheese.    Divide masa evenly between the corn husks, roll and tie tamales and steam  for 30 minutes.  Let cool slightly and serve.
