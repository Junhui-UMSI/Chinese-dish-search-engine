Bills Potato Salad
Yellow mustard; prepared, Green onions; optional, Hard-cooked eggs, Sweet pickle relish, Miracle Whip or mayonnaise, Russet potatoes; peeled and, 
Cook, cool and dice eggs and potatoes. Add mustard, relish, salad dressing,  and onion if available. Mix well and refrigerate until time to serve.    Recipe by Mangia!
