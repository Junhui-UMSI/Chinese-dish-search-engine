Baked Cheese Tomatoes
bread cubes, Tomato pulp, cashews, Salt, Butter, mild cheddar cheese, Onion, tomatoes, Parsley flakes, Basil, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Core tomatoes and scoop out pulp, leaving  sturdy shell. Sprinkle lightly with salt. Combine remaining ingredients.  Fill tomato shells with cheese mixture. Bake, covered, for 30 minutes, or  until cheese melts. Serves 6.    For variation, tomatoes can be cooked on a grill for 15-30 minutes  dependingon fire.
