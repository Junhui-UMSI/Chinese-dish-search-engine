Beef Chili
Cube steaks -- lean, Vegetable oil, Undrained, Whole kernel corn -- frozen, Divided,  Note: See, Onion -- chopped, Spicy Seasoning Mix --, Plum tomatoes; canned --, BARB DAY - GWHP32A-----, Recipe for Spicy Beef and --, Rice Salad for this, 
Preparation time:  30 minutes Heat oil in deep large skillet over medium  heat 5 minutes. Meanwhile cut each steak lengthwise into 1" wide strips,  then cut crosswise in 1" pieces. Sprinkle meat with 2 ts. spicy seasoning.  Stir-fry meat and onion 2 to 3 minutes.  Season with salt if desired. Add  tomatoes (break up with back of spoon), corn and remaining 2 1/2 teaspoons  of the seasoning mix.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium low and  simmer, uncovered, 18 to 20 minutes. Serving suggestions: Tortilla chips  and melon wedges.    Source: Beef Industry Council    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
