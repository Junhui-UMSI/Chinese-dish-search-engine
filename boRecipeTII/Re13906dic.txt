Wine Basted Red Potatoes (r T)
Olive Oil, Fresh Rosemary, White wine, Red Potatoes, Garlic, Salt, 
*NOTE: Original recipe used 1 1/2 T olive oil    In skillet, cook first five ingredients in hot oil over med heat, stirring  5 min. Add wine, cover and reduce heat to low. Cook 10 - 15 min or until  tender.    Work Time: 15 min Total Time: 20 min          8    servings    These were incredible!!
