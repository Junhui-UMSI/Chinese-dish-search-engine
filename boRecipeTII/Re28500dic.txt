Test Test (Peanut Butter) Cookies
Baking soda, Brown sugar, Shortning, Salt, Peanut butter, Egg, Vanilla, Sugar, Flour, 
Cream together shortning,vanilla and sugars. Add eggs. Mix together the  dry ingredients and add to the first mixture and mix well. It will be dry  at first. Form into small balls and place on greased      cookie sheet. Press with a fork which has been dipped in sugar ( to  prevent sticking). Press to make an . They will remain the same size in  diameter .. so what you see is what you get. bake at 375 for 10 minutes.
