Basil Lo-Cal Dressing
Water, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, -, PEPPER BLACK 1 LB CN, VINEGAR CIDER, BASIL SWEET GROUND, 
1.  COMBINE VINEGAR, WATER, SUGAR, SALT, ONION POWDER, BASIL, AND PEPPER;  BLEND WELL.    2.  COVER; REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.    3.  SHAKE OR BEAT WELL BEFORE USING.
