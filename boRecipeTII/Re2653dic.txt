Peanut Butter and Chocolate Fondue
Peanut butter, Pineapple chunks, Lemon juice, Seedless grapes, pound cake, Strawberries, Apple, Bananas, Marshmellows, Semisweet chocolate, 
Melt chocolate and stir in peanut butter.  Mix till smooth. Heat till hot  and put in a serving bowl.  Pat fruit dry. Brush apple and banana with  lemon juice. Arrange a selection of fruit and cake on platter around  fondue.  Dip into fondue to eat.  Makes 1 1/3 cups of fondue mix.
