Basil/balsamic Vinegar Dressing
Onion, chopped fine, Water, Black pepper, Soy sauce, Fresh basil, chopped fine, Cloves garlic, chopped fine, Balsamic vinegar, 
Combine in a jar, shake, and allow to sit, preferably overnight.  The salad  will also be improved if it is dressed and allowed to sit for several  hours, which is good, since there is a lot of it!    From:"Anne.Cox" <20676AC@msu.edu>.
