Premium Macadamia Macaroons
Egg whites, Vanilla extract, Vegetable oil cooking spray, Premium Saltine crackers, Unsalted macadamia nuts;, Sweetened condensed milk, Shredded coconut, Semi-sweet chocolate;, 
Place coconut and nuts on a 15.5x10.5x1-inch baking pan. Bake at 350?  until lightly toasted, about 10 minutes, stirring frequently. Cool.     In large bowl, combine milk and vanilla. Stir in coconut mixture and  crushed crackers; blend well. Beat egg whites until stiff, then gently fold  into coconut mixture. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto lightly greased  baking sheet.     Bake at 350? for 12-14 minutes or until golden around edges. Cool  completely. When cool, dip cookie bottoms into melted chocolate and place  on waxed paper. Refrigerate until set.
