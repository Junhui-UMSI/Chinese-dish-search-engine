Banana Bread Pudding
condensed milk, Pecans, Butter, Water, Margarine, Whole wheat bread cubes, Flaked coconut, Salt, bananas, Brown Sugar, Rum, Borden whipping cream, Eggs, BUTTER RUM SAUCE, Ground cinnamon, Vanilla extract, 
Place bread and coconut in buttered 9-inch square baking pan. In large  bowl, beat eggs and cinnamon; add remaining ingredients except nuts. Pour  over bread, moistening completely. Top with nuts. Bake at 350 degrees for  50 minutes or until knife comes out clean. Cool. Serve with Butter Rum  Sauce.     Butter Rum Sauce: In saucepan, melt 1/4 cup butter; add 3/4 cup firmly  packed brown sugar and 1/2 cup Borden Whipping Cream. Boil rapidly 8 to 10  minutes; add 2 tablespoons rum or 1 teaspoon rum flavoring.
