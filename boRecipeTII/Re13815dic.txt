Wilhelmina Salad
Heads iceberg lettuce;, Tomatoes; diced, Heads Romaine; broken, Crumbled Roquefort, -or-, Vinegar and, OIL AND VINEGAR DRESSING, Bunches fresh watercress;, Avocados; peeled & diced, Endive; pulled apart, Cups oil; mixed, 
Combine iceburg lettuce, romaine, endive & watercress in salad bowl.  Add  avocado, tomatoes & Roquefort.  Toss with just enough oil & vinegar to  moisten.
