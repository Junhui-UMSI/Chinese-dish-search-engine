Apricot Preserve Cake
Buttermilk, Apricot preserves, Pecans, Sticks butter, Sugar, Baking soda, Eggs, Flour, Cinnamon, Chopped; dried apricots, 
Cream butter. Add sugar and mix well. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well  after each addition. Add preserves and mix. Sift flour and cinnamon  together. Add soda to buttermilk. Add flour mixture and buttermilk  alternately to creamed mixture. Add pecans and apricots; pour in greased  bundt or tube pan. Bake at 325 for approximately 1 hour.
