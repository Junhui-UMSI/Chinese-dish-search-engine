Batter-Dipped Poppy Seed Crabs
Baking Powder, Soft-shell crabs, All-purpose flour, Eggs, Poppy seeds, milk, Salt, Blue cornmeal, Margarita Jalapeno Salsa, Unsalted butter, 
In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder and salt; stir in cornmeal and poppy  seeds. In another bowl, lightly beat eggs; blend in milk, then combine milk  mixture with dry ingredients and stir until well blended. Add a little more  milk, if necessary, to make a smooth bater that will cling to crabs. Melt  butter in a large, shallow skillet over medium heat. Dip crabs in batter to  coat; add to skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides, turning  once. Serve with salsa. Makes about 4 servings.    From the <Hotter Than Hell!>, by Jane Butel, ISBN 0-89586-646-3  (0-89586-542-4 paperback).  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
