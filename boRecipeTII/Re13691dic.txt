Wild Rice and Mushrooms
Salt, Mushrooms, fresh, Butter, wild rice, uncooked, Green onions, Pepper, Monterey Jack, Water, Dry white wine, 
Cook and stir rice, mushroom and onions in butter in 10" skillet over med  heat until onions are tender, about 5 min. Stir in water, wine, salt and  pepper. Heat to boiling, stirring occasionally; reduce heat. Cover and  simmer until tender, about 40-45 min.; drain if necessary. Stir in cheese.
