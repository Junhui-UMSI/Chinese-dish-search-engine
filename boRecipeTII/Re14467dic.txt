Whole Wheat Yogurt Bread - Pan-1
Salt, Sugar, Yogurt (4 1/2 oz), Whole wheat flour, Dry yeast, Dry milk, Water, XKGR41A Don Fifield, Butter, Sesame seeds, 
Bake (Rapid) mode may be used. Place all ingredients (except liquids and  yeast) inside the bread pan. Add liquid ingredients. Close cover and place  dry yeast into the yeast holder.
