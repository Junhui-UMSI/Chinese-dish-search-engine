Apricot Glazed Ham Patties
Apricot preserves, Ground cooked ham, Eggs, Prepared mustard; divided, Onions, milk, Vinegar, Soft bread crumbs, Sage, Pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Crumbled blue cheese, 
Heat oven to 350 F. Combine ham with crumbs, milk, eggs, onion, cheese,  Worcestershire, 1 tsp mustard, sage and pepper. Shape into 6 patties. Place  in shallow baking dish; bake 30 minutes. Combine preserves, vinegar and  remaining mustard; brush on patties. Bake 10 more minutes. Serves 6.    Source: "The Smart Shoppers Cookbook" by Loyta Wooding, 1972.
