From: morrissey@stsci.edu (Mostly Harmless)    Date: Fri, 29 Oct 1993 14:54:29 GMT  Cream shortening and sugar until fluffy.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating  well after each addition.  Stir in banana and vanilla.  Sift dry  ingredients together and add to banana mixture. Mix well. Pour into greased  loaf pan or 9x9x2-inch pan. Bake at 350F for 30 to 35 minutes.     Note:  1/2 cup chopped nuts can be added if desired.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
