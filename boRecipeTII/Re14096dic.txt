White Chocolate Strawberry Trifle
Granulated sugar, Egg yolk; large, Vanilla extract, Cream cheese, Whipping cream, Espresso; instant, dissolved, Pound cake, White chocolate; shaved, Strawberries; sliced, around, 
1 Mix the sugar and egg yolks in a blender for one minute. Add the vanilla  and blend one minute. Add the cream cheese and blend until smooth. Chill.    2 Beat the whipping cream until stiff. Add the cream cheese mixture and  chill.    3 Slice cake into 1 x 3-inch strips. Dip the strips in hot espresso. Layer  half the cake strips, white chocolate, cream cheese mixture, and  strawberries in a clearbowl. Repeat layers. Cover and chill until ready to  serve.
