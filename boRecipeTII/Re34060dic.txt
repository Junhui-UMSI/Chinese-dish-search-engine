Basmati Pullao (Cashew and Raisin Pilaf)
Cinnamon, Onion, Basmati rice, Raisins, Vegetable oil, Bay leaf; crumbled, Raw cashews; toasted, Cloves, 
Cook onion over low heat until tender.  Add rice, cinnamon, cloves, bay  leaf, cashews, raisins. Cook for 2 minutes. Add 2 cups of water; cover.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low.
