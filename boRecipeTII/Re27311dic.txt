take head feet and haslet of pig:boil until done.Then cut up fine making  sure to remove the bones.add black pepper salt and sage.2 onions chopped  fine,a lil red pepper,1 tsp mace,1 tsp cloves.Put it back in the same  vessel with liquor and cook well then thicken with flour.Add 2 hardboiled  eggs and one cup walnut catsup.
