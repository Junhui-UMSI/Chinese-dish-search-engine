Bbq Crab Sandwich
Tomato sauce, Green Stuffed Olives; Sliced, English Muffins, Crab, Cheddar; Md, In Small Cubes, 
Mix all the ingredients except the muffins together.  Refrigerate for at  least 1 hour to blend the flavors.  Spread on English Muffin halves and  broil until hot and cheese is melted.  Serve hot.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcheese.zip
