Baked Beans Thompson
Onion; chopped fine, Ketchup, (15-oz) chili without beans, (16-oz) pork and beans, (15-oz) kidney beans, (15-oz) Mexican corn, 
Mix together. Bake in bean pot or heavy pan at 250 for 2-1/2 to 3 hours.  Serves 12.    MRS A.B. THOMPSON (BETTY)    MARVELL, AR    From the book <High Cotton Cookin>, Marvell Academy Mothers Assn, Marvell,  AR  72366, ISBN 0-918544-14-9, downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
