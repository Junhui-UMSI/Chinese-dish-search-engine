Barbara Bushs Lemon Bars
Crust, Margarine, Powdered sugar, Sugar, Flour, Eggs, Flour, Lemon juice, Shredded coconut, Zest of 2 Lemons, Baking Powder, FILLING, 
Mix margarine, powdered sugar and flour. Spread in 15x10" jelly roll pan.    Bake at 350~ for 15 minutes until pale tan. Cool. Mix remaining  ingredients. Pour over crust. Bake for 25 minutes at 350~. Cut into bars.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 162 by minnie@juno.com on Oct  25, 1997
