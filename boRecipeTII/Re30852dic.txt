Tangy Glazed Drumsticks
Tomato paste, Chili sauce, Honey, Fresh watercress sprigs (opt, Corn oil, Onion, Garlic clove, crushed, Chicken drumsticks, Five Spice Powder, Catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, Lemon slices (opt), 
In a saucepan, combine onion, honey, garlic, 2 tablespoons of oil, catsup  and tomato paste.    Add Worcestershire sauce, chili sauce and Five Spice Powder and simmer,  uncovered, 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. In a blender or food  processor, process mixture to a smooth paste. Add remaining oil and stir  well.    Arrange chicken in a roasting pan. Brush with marinade and let stand 1  hour. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 400F. (205C.). Bake chicken in preheated  oven 35-40 minutes, turning and brushing frequently with marinade juices.  Serve hot or cold, garnished with watercress sprigs and lemon slices, if  desired.
