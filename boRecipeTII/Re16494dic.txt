Aegean Shrimp Salad
Salt; divided, Fresh Cilantro, Mesclun or spring salad, Sugar, Medium shrimp; Shelled &, Garlic, Feta Cheese; Crumbled, White wine vinegar, Crushed red pepper flakes, Olive oil; plus 2 teaspoons, 
In medium bowl, combine vinegar, cilantro, 1/2 tsp. salt and sugar. Slowly  whisk in 2 Tbsp oil; set aside. Heat remaining 2 tsp oil in medium skillet  over medium heat. Add next three ingredients and remaining 1/3 tsp salt;  cook 2-4 minutes or until opaque. Combine with dressing and Mesclun; toss  well to coat. Top with Feta.    NOTES : Feta is a Greek sheeps milk cheese that is cured in brine.  It has  a sharp, salty flavor and crumbly texture. You can make it less salty by  rinsing the cheese under cold water before crumbling.  Recipe by: First for Women    Posted to recipelu-digest by Rodeo46898 <Rodeo46898@aol.com> on Mar 2, 1998
