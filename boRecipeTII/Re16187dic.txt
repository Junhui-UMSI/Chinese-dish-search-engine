Wasp Catcher
Washing-up liquid, Marmelade, Plastic box w/lid, Sweet juice, 
Cut 4 holes in the lid of the plastic box and fill it with sweet juice and  a few drops of washing-up liquid. Place some marmelade on top of the lid. A  cheap and effectiv wasp catcher that should keep the wasps away from you.    From the Danish Consumer Institute.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #095 by "B. J. Ohms" <mohms@post3.tele.dk>  on Apr 6, 1997
