Swiss Crabwiches
Pepper, Sour cream, Garlic powder, Dry mustard, Nutmeg-ground, Bacon-cooked, drained, crumb, Mayonaise, Crabmeat, Lemon juice, Swiss cheese shredded, Rolls-kaiser or french, Almonds-slivered, Green onions with tops, slic, 
Toast almonds until lightly browned. Toast split buns. Stir next 7 items  until well blended. Stir in crumbled bacon, almonds, crabmeat, onions, and  cheese. Spread about 1/3 cup of mixture on each roll half. Place on baking  sheet and broil 6 inches from heat until golden brown. 2 open faced  sandwiches per person.
