Lemon Basil Chicken
Mayonnaise, Fresh Lemon Juice, Honey, Dried basil leaves, Broiler/fryer chicken, 
Source: Kraft Cookbook    Heat oven to 375? F.    Mix mayonnaise, juice, honey and basil. Place chicken in 13x9 baking pan.  Spread with mixture. Bake 45 minutes- 1 hour or until chicken is cooked  through. ( My oven is very slow- it takes almost 2 hours to bake).
