Real Lemonade
lemons, juice of, Sugar water, ice cubes, Cold water, Lemon, 
Squeeze the juice from 5 fresh lemons and pour it thru a coarse strainer  into a pitcher. Add 4c cold water and sugar water to taste, adding another  cup of water if the lemons are particularly juicy. Add lemon slices and ice  cubes and stir to blend and bruise the lemon a bit. Pour into tall glass  and enjoy.    Sugar water--1/2c sugar to 1c hot water.  Pour water over the sugar and  stir til dissolved.  Store in covered jar in frig.
