Puttanesca
2lb , 3 oz can Italian plum, (optional), Sliced, Sliced, Tomatoes  (4., Stuffed green olives --, Cloves garlic -- minced, olive oil, Celery rib,minced, Capers1 teaspoons  basil, Pitted black olives --, Sweet red pepper -- minced, Anchovies, chopped, Dried red pepper, 
Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Saute garlic, celery, and sweet red  pepper until soft. Press tomatoes thru a food mill and add to skillet along  with anchovies. Simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in olives, capers, basil, and  dried red pepper. Simmer, uncovered for 20 minutes. Serve with hot pasta  tossed with butter.
