Asparagus Frittata Casserole
Asparagus, 1% milk, Eggs, Salt, Monterey Jack, Birds Eye frozen, 
Pre-heat oven to 400?. Beat eggs in mixing bowl. Add milk and salt; blend  thoroughly. Pour mixture into 9 x 13 in. greased baking pan; add asparagus.  Top with grated cheese. Bake for 15 minutes or until firm. Makes 4  servings.
