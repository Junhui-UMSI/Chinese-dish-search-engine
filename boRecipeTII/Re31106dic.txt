Banana Spice Bread
Cinnamon, Cloves, Baking Powder, Nuts; chopped, Baking soda, Flour; sifted, Salad oil, Eggs; beaten well, Salt, Honey, Bananas; mashed, 
Sift dry ingredients together. Combine honey, eggs, salad oil and mashed  bananas. Add to dry ingredients and beat until smooth. Add nuts. Pour into  greased 9 x5 x3 inch loaf pan or two small greased 8-1/2 x 1/2 x 2-1/2 inch  loaf pans. Bake in a 350 degree oven, 50 to 65 minutes in a large pan or  45-50 minutes in small pans, or until toothpick inserted in center comes  out clean. Remove from pan and cool before slicing. Glaze loaf with thin  confectioners sugar icing if you like, or serve plain.    Recipe by: Unknown    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #397 by Lea <lhoover@ptialaska.net> on  Nov 18, 1997
