Swedish Meatballs W/homemade Meatballs and Sauce
Homemade white sauce mix, Beef broth, Fresh mushrooms, meatballs, Egg noodles, Ground nutmeg, Chopped, 
In a large saucepan, combine white sauce mix and beef broth.  Cook over  medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens.  Add  mushrooms and nutmeg.  Stir in meatballsand continue cooking until heated  through.    Meanwhile, prepare noodles according to package directions.  Serve the  Swedish meatballs in sauce over hot drained noodles.    Makes 4-6 servings.
