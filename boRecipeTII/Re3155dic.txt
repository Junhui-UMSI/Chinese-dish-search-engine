Peanutty Caramel Squares
Sifted all purpose flour, Few drops almond extract, Baking soda, milk, Sugar, Shredded coconut, Salt, Butter or margarine, Snicker bars or 10 from the, Butter or margarine, 
Beat first two ingredients, Sift the dry ingredients together and add. Mix  until crumbly. Reserve 1/2 cup crumbs for topping. Sprinkle remaining crumb  mixture on bottom of 9x9x2 baking pan. Pat down firmly. Bake in 350 degree  oven, 12 minutes. Melt the snickers with 2 tbsp butter and milk in a  saucepan over very low heat. Stir in the coconut and almond extract. Spread  chocolate mixture evenly over baked crumbed mixture. Bake for 12 mins  longer. Watch these you just want them golden. Cool on wire rack. Cut into  squares. Makes about 16 squares.
