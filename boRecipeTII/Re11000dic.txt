Radishes in Cream
Salt, Thinly sliced scallions, Radishes; thinly sliced, Fresh plain yogurt, "Lite" sour cream, DRESSING, GARNISH, Fresh ground pepper, Chives; minced, Chopped parsley, Prepared horseradish, Lettuce leaves, 
Combine radishes with dressing and mix together well. Serve on lettuce leaf  "cups."    From 1993 "Shepherds Garden Seeds Catalog," pg. 41.
