Ready Hamburger Mix
Oregano, Lean ground beef, Large Onion, Chopped, Garlic salt, Salt, Pepper, 
Brown Ground Beef in a heavy skillet.  Add onion and continue cooking over  medium heat until onions are golden.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cool; spoon mixture into four 1-pint containers, leaving 1/2-inch space at  top.  Label as READY HAMBURGER MIX and freeze.  Use within 3 months.  Makes about 4 pints of READY HAMBURGER MIX.    From "Make-A-Mix Cookery" by Karine Eliason, Nevada Harward & Madeline  Westover. Dedicated to memory of Freddi Michael from SYSOP of The Home BBS.
