Peanut Butter Jewels
Sugar, Strawberry jam or apricot, DUNCAN HINES Peanut Butter, Egg, Cocktail peanuts, finely, 
1. Preheat oven to 375F.    2. Combine cookie mix, peanut butter flavor packet from Mix and egg in  large bowl. Stir until thoroughly blended. Shape dough into 36 (1") balls.  Roll half the balls in sugar and half in chopped peanuts. Place 2" apart  on ungreased cookie sheets. Make indentation in center of each balls with  finger or handle end of wooden spoon. Sill each with 1/4 teaspoon  strawberry jam. Bake at 375F. for 8-10 minutes or until light golden  brown. Cool 1 minute on baking sheets. Remove to cooling racks. Cool  completely. Store in airtight container.    Makes 3 dozen cookies.    TIP: For a delicious flavor variation, try seedless red raspberry or  blackberry jam.
