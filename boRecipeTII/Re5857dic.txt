Piquant Meatloaf with Mushroom Gravy
Oregano, Flour, Onions - chopped, Cracker crumbs, SAUCE, Cream, Ground beef, Evaporated milk, Mushrooms - sliced, Thyme, Unsalted butter, Salt, Chili powder, Salt, Garlic powder, Paprika, Egg, Pepper, Onions - chopped, Paprika, Parsley flakes, Beef broth, 
Contributed to the echo by: Fred Towner Originally from: Calgary Suns Great  Meatloaf Recipe Search - Monday June 4, 1990 Submitted by Lidia Barwis  Piquant Meatloaf with Mushroom Gravy    LOAF: Sprinkle meat with salt, pepper, paprika, oregano, thyme, garlic  powder, chili powder and parsley flakes. Toss with fork to mix. Combine  evaporated milk and egg in bowl, stir in cracker crumbs and let soak for 5  minutes. Add to ground beef mixture with onions and mix well. Bake in 350F  oven for 1-1/4 hours.    GRAVY: Saute mushrooms and onions in butter until limp. Combine flour, salt  and paprika. Blend with mushrooms. Stir in broth and cream. Cook until  thick and bubbly. Serve over sliced meatloaf.    Servings: 6
