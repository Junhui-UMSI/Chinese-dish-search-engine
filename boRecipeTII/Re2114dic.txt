Apple Raisin Cookies
Butter, Shortening, Sugar, Eggs, Flour, Baking soda, Salt, Malt-O-Meal Apple Cinnamon, Raisins, Sugar, Cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 400*F. In large mixing bowl beat first four ingredients,  continue beating until fluffy. Combine flour, baking soda and salt. Slowly  add to bowl while mixing on low. Stir in cereal and raisins by hand until  blended. Combine 1/4 cup sugar with cinnamon in bowl. Using rounded  teaspoonfuls, shape cereal mixture into balls. Roll lightly in  cinnamon-sugar. Place 2-inches apart on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 8 to  10 minutes or until bottoms are lightly brown. Let set four minutes before  removing. Makes about 3 1/2 dozen cookies.
