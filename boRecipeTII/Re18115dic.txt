Vegi-Burgers
Sunflower seeds, Salt, HARROWSMITH CKBK, Bran, Sage, Wheat germ, Hamburger buns, Cheddar cheese, grated, Oats, Onion, minced, Breadcrumbs, Eggs, 
Its important to add sufficient seasoning otherwise the finished  product on any meatless burger will be bland and pasty. Top as you  would a hamburger.  Mix all; form into patties.
