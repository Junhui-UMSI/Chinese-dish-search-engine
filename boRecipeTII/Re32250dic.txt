Barbecued Tofu
Ground fennel seeds, Tamari soy sauce, Chili powder, Finely chopped onions, Tomato paste; (from 7oz can), Green or red bell pepper;, Fresh Lemon Juice, Water, Tabasco sauce; or to taste, Ground coriander, Tofu cakes; pressed and, Cider vinegar, Ground cumin, Vegetable oil, Molasses; or brown sugar, Garlic, Cayenne, SAUCE, Prepared mustard, 
Saute the vegetables and spices. Whisk together the sauce ingredients.  Combine the sauteed vegetables and the sauce and pour over tofu cubes in a  shallow baking pan. Bake covered at 350F for 30 minutes and uncovered for  another 30 minutes, stirring very gently to avoid breaking the tofu cubes  throughout the cooking period. Serve on rice with a side dish of cole slaw  or mixed greens. Or pack it into a pita to make a Dixie Pita Sandwich.
