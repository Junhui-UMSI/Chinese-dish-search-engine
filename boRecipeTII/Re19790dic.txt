Appalachian Bean Loaf
OR bacon drippings, Soft bread crumbs, milk, Beans -- mashed, Eggs -- beaten, Salt, Pepper, Butter, Onion -- chopped, 
Combine all ingredients using enough milk to moisten. Pack firmly into well  buttered loaf pan.  Bake at 350F for 1 hour or until firm. Serve with  tomato sauce.    Recipe By     : The Little Mountain Bean Bible Cookbook
