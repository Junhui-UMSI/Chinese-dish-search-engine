Baked Sole with Red Onion
Unsalted butter, Grated Orange Rind, Pepper; to taste, Wine Vinegar, Dijon mustard, Soy sauce, Salt, Orange juice, Chopped fresh parsley, Sole fillets, RED Onion; Thinly Sliced, Granulated sugar, 
Place 3/4 of the onion in oven proof 11-x6-inch baking dish. Top with sole  and remaining onion. Sprinkle with orange rind, salt, and pepper to taste.  Combine orange juice, vinegar, soy sauce, sugar and mustard; pour over  fish. Cover with foil and bake in 350F oven for 10 to 12 minutes or until  fish is opaque and flakes easily when tested with fork, set aside. Pour  contents of baking dish into skillet and bring to boil over high heat; cook  for about 5 minutes or until reduced to about 1/2 cup. Stir in any  accumulated liquid from fish; remove from heat and whisk in butter. Pour  sauce over fish; sprinkle with parsley.
