Turtle Bars
Pecan halves, All-purpose flour, Brown Sugar, Chocolate chips (I used, Topping:, Butter or margarine, Butter or margarine, Brown Sugar, 
In a mixing bowl, beat flour, sugar and butter on medium speed for 2-3  minutes.  Press firmly into an ungreased 13x9x2 inch baking pan.  Arrange  pecans over crust.  Combine butter and brown sugar in a heavy saucepan.  Bring to a boil; boil for 1 minute, stirring constantly.  Pour over pecans.  Bake at 350 for 18-22 minutes.  Spread chocolate but allow some chips to  remain whole.  Cool completely; cut into small squares.  Yield:  about 8  dozen.
