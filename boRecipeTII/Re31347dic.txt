Bar-B-Q Spare Ribs
Country style spare ribs;, Onion, Molasses, SAUCE, Soy sauce, Ketchup, Brown sugar, 
Bake ribs in pan on a rack at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Coat generously  with sauce on top and sides of ribs and bake another 15 minutes. Turn ribs;  coat top and pour rest of sauce over ribs and bake approximately 15 minutes  more. Extra sauce in roasting pan makes a delicious sauce over rice. Sauce:  Mix well; do not cook.
