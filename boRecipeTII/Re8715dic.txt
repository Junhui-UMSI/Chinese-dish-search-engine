Poulsbo (7-Grain) Bread
Salt, Sunflower seeds, Molasses, Active dry yeast, Bread flour, Whole wheat flour, Warm water, Sugar, Applesauce, 7-grain cereal mix, Powdered buttermilk, 
All ingredients except the water should be at room temperature before  starting. Add all the ingredients except the sunflower seeds to the pan in  the order listed. Select "White Bread". Press "Start". Add the sunflower  seeds just before the end of final kneading when the "beeps" sound.
