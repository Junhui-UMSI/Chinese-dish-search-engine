Potluck Chicken Casserole
Sour cream, Butter-flavored cracker, Butter or Margarine, Sweet red pepper rings, (10.75 oz) condensed cream, Celery seed, Cubed cooked chicken, Fresh parsley sprigs, 
Combine chicken, soup and sour cream; spread into a greased 13x9x2-inch  baking dish. Combine crumbs, butter and celery seed; sprinkle over chicken  mixture. Bake, uncovered, at 350? for 30-35 minutes or until bubbly.  Garnish with parsley and red pepper if desired.
