Pesto Hummus
Basil, juice of 1/2 a lemon, Chickpeas, 
Put chickpeas, basil, and some of the lemon into bowl. Puree using blender.  Add lemon juice until consistency and taste are pleasing. If still too  thick, you can add some of the leftover juice from the chickpea can. Serve  as a dip or use as a spread on fresh bread.    Variations (untested): Add some minced garlic. Use like pesto sauce on  pasta.    Posted by vmh@intellicorp.com to the Fatfree Digest [Volume 15 Issue 4]  Feb. 4, 1995.    Individual recipes copyrighted by originator.
