Asian-Style Honey Vegetable Stir Fry
Small mushrooms, Carrot; cut diagonally into, Peanut oil OR vegetable oil, Red pepper flakes; may be, Prepared stir fry sauce, Small broccoli florets, Onion; cut into wedges and, Honey, 
In small bowl, combine honey, stir fry sauce and crushed red pepper flakes;  set aside. In wok or large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; add  vegetables and toss while cooking, about 2-3 minutes. Add honey sauce, stir  until all vegetables are glazed and sauce is bubbly hot, about 1 minute.  Serve as a vegetable side dish or over steamed rice or noodles for a main  dish. Makes 4 servings.    Prep: 10 minutes/Cook 5 minutes    Nutrients Per Serving: 168 calories, 4.9 g fat (24% calories from fat), 0  mg cholesterol, 2.6 g protein, 31 g carbohydrates, 3 g dietary fiber, 74 mg  sodium.
