Pumpkin, Sweet Potato, Peanut Butter Soup
Smooth peanut butter, Canned pumpkin, sweet potatoes, Salt, Chicken Stock, fresh chives, Black Pepper, unsalted butter, 
Melt the margarine in a soup pot over medium heat.  Stir in the pumpkin,  sweet potatoes, and peanut butter.  Add the stock, pepper, and salt, and  stir well until smooth.  Reduce the heat to a simmer and cook for 20  minutes.  Before serving, garnish the soup with chives and sour cream.   8  servings.
