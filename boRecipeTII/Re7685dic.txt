Pork with Coriander
Dry white wine, Salt, New potatoes, cut into halve, Ground coriander, Pork boneless shoulder, Pepper, Mushrooms, cut into halves, Vegetable oil, 
Trim fat from pork. Cut pork into 1-inch cubes. Heat oil in Dutch oven  until hot. Cook pork over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until all  liquid is evaporated and pork is brown, about 25 minutes. Drain fat. Stir  in wine, coriander, salt and pepper. Heat to boiling. Reduce heat. Cover  and simmer 45 minutes. Stir in potatoes and mushrooms. Heat to boiling.  Reduce heat. Cover and simmer until pork and potatoes are tender, 15 to 20  minutes.
