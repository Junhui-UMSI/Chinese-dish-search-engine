Prune Ice-Cream
Lemon juice, Prunes, Few grains salt, Sweetened condensed milk, Water, Sugar, 
Wash prunes.  Cover with cold water.  Heat to boiling. Simmer until soft.  Rub through sieve.  There should be 1 cup prune pulp. Combine prune pulp,  milk, water, sugar, lemon juice, and salt. Pour into freezer. Freeze. 6  servings. I.M., Greenfield, IN.
