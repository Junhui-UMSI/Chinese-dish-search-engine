Abm Dijon Rye
Gluten, Lecithin granules, Whole wheat flour, Dijon mustard, Rye flour, Yeast, Molasses, Bread flour, Water, 
Dump yeast in machine. Dump in flours and lecithin granules Fill measuring  cup to 2/3rds with warm water. Add mustard until liquid measures a full  cup. Add molasses and stir. Dump in machine.    Set machine to basic bake.    A small dark loaf with a nice mustardy flavor. While dense, it can be  sliced quite thinly. Makes a fabulous egg salad sandwich.
