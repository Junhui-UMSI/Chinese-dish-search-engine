Tomato-Cheese Bruschetta
Green Onions, Bacon, Cream cheese, Garlic powder, Seasoned salt, Tomato, French baguette, Fresh basil, 
Preheat oven to 350 F. Slicing on an angle, cut baguette into 1/2 inch  slices. Set aside. Combine cheese, seasoned salt, garlic and basil. Add  bacon, tomato and onions. Arrange bread slices on a baking sheet and spread  with tomato-cheese mixture. Bake 15 minutes. Serve immediately.    recipe from cheese.miningco.com    Posted to recipelu-digest by LeaConklin <LeaConklin@aol.com> on Feb 28,  1998
