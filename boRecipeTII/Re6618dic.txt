Poinsettia Sipper
Orange juice, Apple juice, Cranberry juice cocktail, Sugar, Cloves, Water, Orange rind curls (optional), Cinnamon sticks, (3-inch), 
Combine first 7 ingredients in a saucepan, stirring until sugar dissolves.  Bring to a simmer; cover and cook 30 minutes. Remove cloves and cinnamon  sticks from pan with a slotted spoon. Serve warm or chilled. Garnish with  orange rind curls, if desired. Yield: 6 cups (serving size: 3/4 cup). Note:  For a Poinsettia Cocktail, add 1 tablespoon vodka each serving; the  calories go up to 150 per serving.
