Perrys (San Francisco) Roquefort Salad
Head romaine lettuce, Dry mustard, Head red lettuce, Worcestershire Sauce, olive oil, Tabasco sauce, Red wine vinegar, Fresh parsley, chopped, Head butter lettuce, Celery seeds, Dried Oregano, Roquefort cheese, crumbled, Cherry tomatoes, stemmed, Burgundy wine, Dried basil, Dried thyme, 
Combine all ingredients for Perrys House Dressing in a mixing bowl,  blender, or jar and mix well.    Wash, dry; and chill lettuce leaves until serving time.    In a large bowl, combine greens with Perrys House Dressing to taste,  tomatoes, and half of cheese. Toss well. Serve on chilled plates and top  with remaining cheese.
