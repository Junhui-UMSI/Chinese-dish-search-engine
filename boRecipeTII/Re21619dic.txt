Apple Sauce Crazy Cake (Motts)
Salt, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Vegetable oil, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Pecans, All-purpose flour, Sugar, Cloves, Vanilla, Raisins, Unsweetened Apple Sauce, 
Heat oven to 350F.    In 8 or 9-inch pan, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, baking  soda, salt cloves and nutmeg; blend well.    Stir in apple sauce, oil and vanilla; mix well. Stir in raisins and nuts.    Bake at 350F for 25 to 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center  comes out clean.    Notes: Pantry: Motts Natural Apple Sauce. Let the kids lend a hand in  measuring and mixing this quick-to-assemble cake. Just mix it in the pan  and bake.    >Hanneman/Buster 1998-Apr    Recipe by: Motts Applesauce www.motts.com    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by KitPATh <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu> on Apr 18,  1998
