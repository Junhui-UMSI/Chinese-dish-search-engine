Tasty Chicken Nuggets for Kids (Mf)
Skinless boneless chicken, Unsalted butter, seasoning, Dried bread crumbs, Salt, 
Transfer the seasoning or flavoring of choice in a shallow dish and season  with salt and pepper. Marinate the chicken in the mixture for as long as  possible. Heat the butter in a medium skillet, over medium high heat. Dip  the chicken in bread crumbs and saute slowly on all sides until cooked  through.
