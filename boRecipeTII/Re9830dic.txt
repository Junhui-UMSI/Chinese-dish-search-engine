Pumpkin Waffles with Maple Apple Syrup
Bisquick baking mix, Frozen apple juice, Brown sugar, Pumpkin pie spice, Canned pumpkin, Maple syrup, Pecans, milk, SYRUP, Eggs, Vegetable oil, 
Waffles: Beat all ingredients except pecans till smooth. Make waffles  according to your machine directions. Sprinkle with pecans and serve with  syrup. Syrup:Heat in small saucepan till warm.    Recipe by: Lisa    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #831 by MeLizaJane@aol.com on Oct 8, 1997
