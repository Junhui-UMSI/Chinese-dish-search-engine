Pork Chop Noodle Dinner
Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Sour cream, Oregano, Extra-wide noodles, Onion, Tomato paste, Poppy seeds, Pork chops, Water, 
In a large skillet, brown pork chops in hot oil;  drain fat.  Sprinkle with  salt and pepper.    Meanwhile, in a bowl, combine tomato paste, water, onion, garlic and  oregano; pour over prok chops. Cover; simmer 30 to 35 minutes or until  chops are done.    On platter, toss hot noodels with poppy seeds or parsley.  Arrange pork  chops on top; pour sauce over.
