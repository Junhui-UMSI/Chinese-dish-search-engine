Walnut Miso Condiments
Water, Roasted walnuts, -water; or up to..., EDEN Genmai or Shiro Miso, 
Roast walnuts in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Blend with remaining  ingredients into a smooth paste in a blender or food processor.  Delicious served with grains and vegetables, or as a spread.
