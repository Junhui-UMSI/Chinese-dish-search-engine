Apricot Nectar Salad #2
(3-oz) orange gelatin, Mandarin oranges; drained, Whipped topping, Miniature marshmallows, Bananas; sliced, Boiling water, Apricot nectar, 
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Stir in apricot nectar, put into a 1-1/2  quart mold. Let chill in refrigerator until syrupy. Add oranges, bananas,  marshmallows and whipped topping. Chill until firm.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
