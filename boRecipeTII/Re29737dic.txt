Teething Cookies for Babies
Wheat germ, Honey, Vegetable oil, Soy flour, Molasses, Egg yolk; beaten, Salt, Scant cup whole wheat flour;, Vanilla, 
Blend honey, molasses, oil and egg yolk. Stir in vanilla, salt, flours and  wheat germ. Dough should be stiff. If too thick, add a little milk; if too  thin, add more flour. Roll dough to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut into  rectangular pieces about 1 x 1 1/2 inches. Place on ungreased cooky sheet.  Bake at 350 F until lightly browned, about 15 to 20 minutes. Makes about 4  dozen.    Milk powder may be added to dough if desired.
