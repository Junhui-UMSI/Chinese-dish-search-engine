Toasted Garlic Bread
BREAD, FRENCH, GARLIC, DEHYdrated, BUTTER, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN                  TEMPERATURE:  400 F. OVEN  :    1.  PLACE BUTTER OR MARGARINE IN MIXER BOWL. WHIP AT MEDIUM SPEED  UNTIL CREAMY. ADD GARLIC; BLEND THOROUGHLY.    2.  SLICE EACH LOAF IN HALF LENGTHWISE. PLACE 5 HALF LOAVES ON EACH  PAN. SPREAD EACH HALF LOAF WITH ABOUT 2 OZ (1/2 CUP) GARLIC-BUTTER  MIXTURE.    3.  HEAT 10 TO 15 MINUTES OR UNTIL EDGES ARE TOASTED TO A GOLDEN BROWN.  CUT EACH HALF LOAF IN 8 SLICES.    4.  SERVE HOT.  * ALL NOTES ARE PER 100 PORTIONS.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 1, 4 TBSP (12 CLOVES) DRY GARLIC, MINCED, MAY BE USED.  SAUTE GARLIC 5 MINUTES.  ADD TO WHIPPED BUTTER.  FOLLOW STEPS 2 THROUGH 4.    NOTE:  2.  IN STEP 2, 100 HARD ROLLS MAY BE SPLIT AND USED. EACH PORTION:        2    SPLIT ROLLS.
