Diced Apple Salad
apples, Celery, Pecans, Raisins, Mayonnaise, Sugar, 
Combine apples, celery, nuts, and raisins in a salad bowl. In a small bowl,  stir mayonnaise and sugar together; beat until creamy. If needed, stir in a  teaspoon of milk to thin. Pour dressing over salad and stir to coat.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by IceTeaLuvr@aol.com on Aug 19, 1998,
