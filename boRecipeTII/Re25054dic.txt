Tomato Soup Salad
Salt, Pecans, Pickle relish, Minced celery, Tabasco, FOR SECOND DAY, Undiluted tomato soup, (3-oz) cream cheese, Plain gelatin, Worcestershire Sauce, Tomato aspic with dill, Mayonnaise, Onion, Lemon gelatin, Diced green pepper, 
Crumble cream cheese and add to soup. Heat slowly to bubbly stage. Remove  from heat and add all remaining ingredients except tomato aspic. Combine  well and pour into greased mold. Chill.     On second day; Slowly heat can of tomato aspic with dill and gently pour  over molded salad. Chill until set. Unmold on salad greens.    CHILL 2 DAYS    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
