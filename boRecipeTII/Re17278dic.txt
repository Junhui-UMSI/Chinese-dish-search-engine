Wagonwheel Layered Dip
Mayonnaise, Bean Dip, Finely Chopped Green Onions, Shredded Monterey Jack, Sour cream, Pace Picante Sauce, Drained Sliced Olives, 
Spread bean dip on a pie plate.  Pour Pace Picante Sauce over bean dip.  Sprinkle chopped onions over mixture. Blend together mayonnaise and sour  cream. Spread over onions. Top with cheese and Pace Picante Sauce. Sprinkle  sliced olives over cheese.  Makes 2-1/2 cups dip.    From: Pace booklet.
