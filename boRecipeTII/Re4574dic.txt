Petits Pots De Creme Au Cafe
Eggs, Sugar, milk, Kahlua, Strong-brewed coffee, Extra-fine instant coffee, 
Preheat oven to 275. Place eggs, sugar, and coffees in a large mixing bowl.  Mix well. Add the Kahlua and milk. Fill 4 custard cups with the mixture.  Place in a deep baking pan filled with 1 inch hot water. Bake in oven for  45 min. or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
