Banana Spicecake
Flour, Cloves, Vanilla, Nutmeg, Baking soda, Shortening, Sugar, Mashed bananas, Baking Powder, Salt, Eggs, well beaten, Cinnamon, 
Sift flour, baking powder, soda, salt and spices together 3 times. Cream  shortening with sugar until fluffy.  Add eggs and beat thoroughly. Add  sifted dry ingredients and bananas alternately in small amounts, beating  well after each addition.  Stir in vanilla. Pour into greased pans and bake  in a 350 degree oven for 35 minutes. Makes 2 (8 inch) layers. Randy Rigg  The Pinnacle Club BBS 812-963-9139  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #007 by scotlyn@juno.com (Daniel S Johnson)  on Jan 5, 1998
