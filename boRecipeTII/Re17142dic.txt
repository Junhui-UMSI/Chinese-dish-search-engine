Virginia Baked Apples
Sliced apples, Sugar, Pecan halves, (small) marshmallows, Butter or margarine, Lemon, Coconut, 
Place apples in casserole dish. Dot with butter. Sprinkle with sugar.  Squeeze lemon juice over apples. Spread coconut & pecan halves over apples.  Heat, cover with marshmallows & brown.    MRS. J.T.
