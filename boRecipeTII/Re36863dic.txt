Strawberry Rice-Cream
Sliced fresh/frzn Strawberry, milk, Uncooked Rice, Vanilla, Heavy cream, whipped    *, Sugar, Beaten Eggs, 
* whip heavy cream with additional 1/4 t vanilla     Place rice and milk in top of double boiler or in pan over boiling water.  Cook for 1 hour, or until tender, stirring occasionally. Stir in sugar.  Add a little hot rice to beaten eggs, then add eggs to remaining rice  mixture. Stir in vanilla. Mix well and continue cooking over hot water for        1    minute. Cool.   Whip cream, fold in strawberries, then fold mixture into rice.    Chill until serving.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmstrawb.zip
