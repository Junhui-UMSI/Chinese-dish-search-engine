Apple Charlotte
Butter, Water, Lemon zest, Sugar, White Bread, Apples, Cooking, 
Cut a small circle of bread to fit the base of a 1 1/2 pint pyrex bowl, and  another circle to fit the top. Reserve top circle of bread. Line bowl with  remaining pieces, first dipping each in melted butter, and overlapping  slightly where necessary. Peel. core and slice apples thinly. Cook apples,  water, sugar and rind gently in a saucepan until apples are slightly  softened but not mushy. Turn apples into lined bowl. Dip top circle of  bread in melted butter, and cover top. Bake in a moderate oven (375 F - gas  mark 4) for about 1 hour or until bread is toasted a golden brown. Cool in  the bowl for a few minutes and turn on to a serving dish.    Serve with fairly thin, pouring custard.  Recipe By     : Old Magazine clipping from England, Sixties?
