Aloha for Six Coconut Cookies
All-purpose flour, Granulated sugar, Egg yolk, Butter, Nutmeg, Unsweetened coconut, 
In large bowl, beat butter until creamy; beat in sugar until smooth, about  1 minute. BEat in egg yolk until slightly fluffy. With wooden spoon, stir  in flour, coconut and nutmeg until thoroughly combined. Wrap dough in  plastic wrap; chill until firm, about 2 hours.    Roll heaping tablespoons of dough into 3-inch long logs. Place 2 inches  apart on greased and floured or parchment paper-lined baking sheets;  flatten with back of fork to 1/4-inch thickness. Refrigerate for 30 minutes  to chill.    Bake in 325F=160C oven, 1 sheet at a time, for 25-30 minutes or until  golden around edges. Let cool in the pan, on a rack, for 5 minutes;  transfer to rack and let cool completely.    Cookies can be stored in airtight container for up to 4 days.
