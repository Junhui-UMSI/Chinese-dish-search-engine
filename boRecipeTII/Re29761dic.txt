Tempeh Mock Chicken Salad (Vegan)
Parsley, Scallions, Carrot, Tempeh, Mayonnaise, eggless, Vinegar, rice, Celery, Soy sauce, Water, 
Combine tempeh, soy sauce, water and viegar in a covered 1-quart glass  casserole. Cook on high for 5 minutes, stirring once. Remove cover and cool  to room temperature.    Add remaining ingredients to tempeh mixture. Stir well and chill. Makes 6  servings.
