Strawberry Banana Split Pie
Pureed strawberries, Vanilla crumb crust, Banana slices, Cold half-and-half or milk, (4-oz) French Vanilla or, Cool whip, 
Pour half-and-half and pureed strawberries into a large bowl. Add pudding  mix. Beat with wire whisk until well blended, 1 to 2 minutes. Let stand 5  minutes or until slightly thickened. Line bottom of crust with banana  slices. Fold cool whip into pudding mixture. Spoon into crust. Freeze until  firm, about 6 hours or overnight. Remove from freezer. Let stand at room  temperature about 10 minutes before serving to soften. Garnish with whipped  topping, maraschino cherries, and chipped nuts. Serve with remaining  strawberries, pureed, if desired.    Recipe by: More Joys of Jello Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #598 by Diana  Stephens <mdstephe@ix.netcom.com> on May 5, 1997
