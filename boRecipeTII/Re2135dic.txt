Applesauce Cake Icing
Sweet butter, Powdered sugar, Heavy cream, Vanilla extract, 
PREPARATION: In a large bowl, beat the butter with the vanilla until  lightened. Gradually beat in the confectioners? sugar and continue beating  until fluffy. Beat in cream, as necessary, to thin the icing to a  spreadable consistency. Spread icing over the sides and top of cake.    Recipe by Mangia!
