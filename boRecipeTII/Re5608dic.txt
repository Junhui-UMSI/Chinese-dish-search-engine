Pineapple Sherbet
Lemon juice, Pineapple, Sugar, Water, 
Slice pineapple in half, lengthwise. Remove pulp and place in  saucepan with the remaining ingredients. Cook 5 minutes. Place in  food processor or blender until smooth. Freeze until firm. Remove  from freezer, beat until frothy, place into pineapple shell and  freeze until ready to serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
