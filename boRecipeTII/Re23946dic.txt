Asparagus Cantonese
Asparagus, Garlic clove; crushed, Vegetable oil, Salt, pepper, 
Heat oil in skillet over high heat almost to smoking point. Stir in garlic,  then add asparagus. Cover and shake pan vigorously 3 to 5 minutes.  Asparagus should be crisp-tender. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
