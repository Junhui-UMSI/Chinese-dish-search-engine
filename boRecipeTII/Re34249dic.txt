Bbq Chicken
Chinese-style barbecue sauce, Soy sauce, Chicken, frying; cut up, Sesame oil, 
Combine all ingredients in plastic bag. Press air out; seal bag securely.  Rotate gently to coat chicken. Refrigerate 4 to 6 hours. Grill or broil 4  to 5" from heat, 12-16 minutes on each side, or until done.    Origin: Oriental Classics Cookbook - GEnie D.WEISSGERBE    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
