Pops Jerky
Garlic salt, Soy sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Honey, Round Steak, 
Combine all but meat, let rest 1 hr in frig while slicing meat cross-grain  into 1/2" strips. Stir marinate, add meat. Cover and place in frig for 24  hrs, stiring occasionally.   Preheat Dehydrator to 135 F, remove meat onto paper towels, drain well.  Place meat on rack(s), dry 24 hours or until done.
