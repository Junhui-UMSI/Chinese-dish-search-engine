Carrot Muffins
Cinnamon, Egg, Vegetable oil, Flour, Rolled oats, Raisins, Baking Powder, Salt, milk, Carrots, 
Mix flour, oats, b/pdr & salt. Stir in carrot, raisins & cinnamon. Beat  egg, milk & oil. Make a well in centre of dry ingredients, pour in liquid.  Mix lightly. 3/4 fill greased muffin pans & bake at 375oF for 15-20 mins.
