Honey-Style Roasted Vegetables
Olive or vegetable oil, Seasoned salt, Pepper, Potatoes, unpeeled, cut in, Carrots, cut in strips(about, 1 to 1 1/2 cups), Red onion, cut in 8 wedges, Dried marjoram leaves, Green bell pepper, cut in 8, 
1. Heat oven to 450 degrees. In large bowl, combine oil, seasoned salt,  marjoram, and pepper; mix well. Add all remaining ingredients; toss to  coat. Spread on ungreased 15x10x1-inch baking pan.    2. Bake at 450 degrees for 20 minutes. Turn and stir vegetables. Bake an  additional 20 to 25 minutes or until vegetables are tender, stirring once.    Nutrition Information Per Serving: 150 Calories, 4 g Fat, 230 mg Sodium    taken from Pillsbury Classic Cookbooks magazine.
