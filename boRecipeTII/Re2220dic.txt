Baked Beans with Fruit and Chutney
Chutney, Additional water, Apples; sliced, Freshly ground pepper; to, Dried navy beans; washed and, Peaches; peeled and sliced, Dried apricots, Onion; chopped fine, Brown rice syrup, Molasses, Dry mustard or 1 tablespoon, 
Drain the beans and combine with the 2 quarts water in a stockpot or dutch  oven. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, cover, and simmer 1 hour, until the  beans are tender but not mushy. Drain, reserving 1 1/2 cups of the cooking  liquid.    In a small bowl, dissolve the mustard in the bean liquid and combine with  the finely chopped onion, chutney, and salt and pepper to taste. Stir this  into the beans.    Preheat the oven to 325 degrees. Pour half of the bean mixture into a 2- or  3-quart lidded casserole or baking dish. Top with half of the sliced  apples, peaches, and apricots, then pour in the rest of the bean mixture  and top with the rest of the fruit. Combine the rice syrup and the molasses  and pour evenly over the top.    Cover and bake for 1 hour, then remove the cover and bake for another 30  minutes. Serve steaming hot.
