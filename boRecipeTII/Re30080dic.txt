Balsamic Ratatouille
Tomato (red);*, Tomato (yellow);*, Sprig fresh thyme chopped in, Black pepper; or to taste, olive oil, Clove garlic; finely diced, Eggplant;*, Zucchini (yellow) OR yellow, Kosher salt; or to taste, Onion; peeled*, Zucchini (green);*, Balsamic vinegar, 
* finely diced    Serve with Grilled Balsamic Veal Chops and Mashed Potatoes  w/Mascarpone Cheese & Roasted Garlic    1. Pour olive oil into a heated saute pan. Add garlic and cook over  medium heat, stirring, for 30 seconds. Add all diced vegetables;  cook, stirring, until tender but crisp, about 2 to 3 minutes.    2. Add thyme and balsamic vinegar. Stir just until blended. Remove  from heat. Season with salt and pepper.    Served at the Chicago 1996 Democratic National Convention.
