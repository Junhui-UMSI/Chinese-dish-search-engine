Beer Mop Sauce
Onion, Garlic, Oil canola, Beer, Worcestershire Sauce, Vinegar, barbecue spice mix, 
Stir all ingredients together in a medium bowl. Apply the sauce with a  small string "mop" for barbecue, or with a pastry brush.    Variations: Substitute stock for the beer, or inexpensive dry wine (red for  the meat or white for the poultry or fish).
