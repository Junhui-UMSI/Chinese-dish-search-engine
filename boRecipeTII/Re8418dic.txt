Potato Spice Cake
Vanilla, Sugar, Cocoa, All-Purpose Flour, Cold mashed potatoes, And allspice, milk, Lemon extract, Eggs, Raisins, Nuts, Salt, Shortening, Baking Powder, Each cloves,nutmeg,cinnamon, 
Sift together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, salt and spices. Cut  shortening into flour mixture till fine as cornmeal. Add all at once milk,  pototoes, unbeaten eggs and flavoring. Beat till smooth. Beat in nuts and  raisins.  Pour into a large well-greased angel cake pan. Bake at 350F for  70 minutes or till done. Cool about 10 minutes. Remove from pan.
