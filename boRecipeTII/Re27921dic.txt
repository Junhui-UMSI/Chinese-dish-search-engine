Those Good Potatoes
Cream of chicken soup, Potatoes, cooked til tender, Sour cream, Salt and pepper, Eggs, hard-boiled, Bread crumbs, Jar Pimentoes, Green pepper, sliced thin, Sharp cheddar cheese,, Onion, sliced thin, 
Mix sour cream and soup in 2 qt. casserole or 13 x 9 x 2 baking dish.  Mix  in everything else, saving 1 egg and crumbs for topping.    Bake 350 oven for 30 min.  If made ahead of time and kept refrigerated,  then bake for 50 min.
