Beefy Cheese Dip
Worcestershire Sauce, black olives, Tobasco, Tomatoes, Velveeta*, Ground beef, 
Melt Velveeta in a large saucepan. Add the browned and drained ground beef  to the cheese, plus all remaining ingredients. Mix well.    *Its up to you whether or not you want to use the spicy Velveeta, or half  and half.
