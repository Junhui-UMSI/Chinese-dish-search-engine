Baked Chicken Brunch Casserole
Butter or margarine, White pepper, Parsley, Butter, Onion, white bread, Onion, Salt, Eggs, milk, Flour, Paprika, Light cream, Sour cream, Sour cream onion sauce, Cheddar cheese, chicken, 
Casserole: Butter a 9x13 inch pyrex dish. Butter one side of each bread  slice and place 6 slices in the prepared dish, buttered side down. Layer  the chicken, onion and cheese over the bread. Cover the layers with the  remaining bread slices, buttered side up.    In a medium bowl, combine the eggs and milk. Pour over the casserole and  sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate overnight. Bake in a 325 ? oven for 1 to    1 1/4    hours.    Top the cooked casserole with the Sour Cream Sauce and garnish with parsley  or cherry tomatoes.    Sour Cream Onion Sauce: In a medium saucepan, cook the onion in the butter  until soft, but not browned. Stir in the flour, cream, salt and pepper.  Bring mixture to a boil, stirring constantly until blended. Stir in the  sour cream until heated thoroughly, but do not boil.    This recipe comes from The American Country Inn Bed and Breakfast Cookbook  (volume II), ISBN 1-55853-218-8 (paperback).
