Pico De Gallo Corn
Corn kernels, Salt, Jalapenos, seeded and minced, Tomatoes, cut into 1/4-inch, Juice of 2 limes, Diced red onion, olive oil, Chopped cilantro, 
Combine vegetables, jalapenos, cilantro and lime juice. Heat olive oil in a  skillet and add vegetable mixture. Cook just until corn softens.
