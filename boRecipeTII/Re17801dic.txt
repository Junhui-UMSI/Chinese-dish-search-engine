Almost Cocos Sour Cream Dressing
Garlic salt, Mayonnaise, Pepper, milk, Cider vinegar, Sour cream, Buttermilk, Parmesan cheese, 
In a mixing bowl, combine milk, buttermilk, mayonnaise, vinegar, Parmesan  cheese, pepper, and garlic salt. Mix well with wire whisk. Fold in sour  cream, leaving lumps intact. Refrigerate in a tightly closed container.  Makes 6 cups.
