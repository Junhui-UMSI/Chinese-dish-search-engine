Warm Lobster Dip
white wine, Granulated sugar, Dijon mustard, Light cream cheese, Onion, Light mayonnaise, lobster, salt & cayenne pepper, Garlic, 
Drain lobster; flake meat with fingers and set aside.    In small saucepan, melt cheese over low heat until smooth, stirring often.  Stir in lobster, wine, mayonnaise, garlic, onion, sugar, mustard, salt and  cayenne; heat through, stirring occasionally.  Serve in warmed bowl. Makes  about 2 cups.  Variation: Crab Dip: Substitute 1 pkg. (7 oz.) frozen crab  for the lobster. Yield: 2 cups Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: The Canadian Living, 20th Anniversary Cookbook.
