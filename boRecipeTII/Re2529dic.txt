Peaches and Cream Pie #3
Nutmeg, Whipping cream, Quick cooking tapioca, Pastry for 9 inch pie, Sugar, Fresh peaches; sliced, 
Combine first 4 ingredients. Let stand for 30 minutes. Prepare a pie shell  with a firm, high standing ridge. Fill shell with peaches. Pour in cream  mixture. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes or until firm in the center.  Cool  completely before serving.
