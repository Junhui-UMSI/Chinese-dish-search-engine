Pecan Cups
Pecans, (13-oz) evaporated milk, Dark brown sugar, Graham cracker crumbs, Butter, (12-oz) semi-sweet chocolate, 
Preheat oven to 350?. Soften butter to room temperature. Mix all  ingredients together. Fill miniature muffin tins to top. Bake for 10 to 13  minutes. Can be frozen. Yield: 3 dozen cups.
