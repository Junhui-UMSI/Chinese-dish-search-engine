Sweet Potatoes and Onions
Cinnamon, Vegetable oil, Parsley, Butter, Onion; peeled and sliced, Sweet potato; cook, peel,, 
Heat oil and butter, saute onions, add cinnamon. Saute potatoes until  lightly browned. Serve with parsley.    Recipe by: Frugal Gourmet Cooks American    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by LauraG <gaggini@GATE.USAOR.NET> on Jan 16, 1998
