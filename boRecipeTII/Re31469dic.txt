Barbecue Sauce #11
Ketchup, Sugar, Chili powder, Onion, Water, Salt, Vinegar, Black pepper, 
Put ingredients in a large pot and simmer until thick, about 6 to 8 hours.  Yield: 3 quarts.    JULIE ALLEN (MRS. WALLY)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
