Zucchini Chicken Spaghetti
Uncooked spaghetti, Thinly sliced zucchini, Jar (32-oz) spaghetti sauce, Vegetable oil, Cubed cooked chicken, Onion; thinly sliced,, (4-oz) mushroom pieces &, 
Cook spaghetti to desired doneness as directed on package. Drain; cover to  keep warm. Meanwhile, heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat  until hot. Cook and stir zucchini and onion for 3 minutes or until  vegetables are crisp-tender. Stir in chicken, spaghetti sauce and  mushrooms; cook until thoroughly heated. Serve over cooked spaghetti. If  desired, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese. Four servings.
