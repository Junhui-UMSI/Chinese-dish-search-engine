Water Chestnut Pudding
Water-chestnut flour, Water, Sugar, Water, 
1. Place water-chestnut flour in a heatproof bowl. Gradually stir in cold  water to make a smooth paste. Then blend in sugar and remaining water.    2. Steam 1 hour (see "How-to Section"). Serve hot or cold.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
