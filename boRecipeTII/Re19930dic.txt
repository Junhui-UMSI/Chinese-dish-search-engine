Apple Breakfast Lasagna
Granola, ham, Cheddar cheese, Brown Sugar, Sour cream, Apple pie filling, Frozen french toast, 
In small bowl, combine sugar and sour cream; cover and refrigerate. Place 6  slices French toast in bottom of greased 9 x 13 pan. Layer ham, 2 cups of  cheese and remaining 6 slices of French toast in baking pan. Spread pei  filling over top; sprinkle granola over apples. Bake in preheated 350F oven  for 25 minutes. Top with remaining 1/2 cup cheddar cheese; bake another 5  minutes until cheese is melted and casserole is hot. Serve with sour cream  mixture.
