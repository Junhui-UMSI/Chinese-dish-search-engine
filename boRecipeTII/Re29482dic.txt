Baking Powder Biscuits (Diabetic)
Milk, SKIM, Shortening, Flour, Baking Powder, Salt, 
Mix flour, baking powder, and salt together. Cut in the shortening until it  is evenly mixed with the dry ingredients. Then ADD, Milk, mixing well.    Then Knead for a a 1/2 minute and roll out to about 1-1/2 inch in  thickness. Cut with a Biscuit cutter. Place the biscuits 1-1/2 inches apart  on a greased baking sheet. The BRUSH with the Milk and BAKE at 450F oven  degrees 10-12 minutes    One biscuit may be Exchanged for:  1 bread exchange or 1 fat exchange.
