Alpine Avocado Dip
Hot pepper sauce, Avocado; peeled, seeded, Lemon juice, Onions, Plain yogurt, Garlic salt, 
In small bowl, mash avocado. Stir in remaining ingredients (mixture will be  lumpy). Chill thoroughly to blend flavors.    Serve with assorted chips.    Makes 1 1/2 Cups.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #924 by "abprice@wf.net" <abprice@wf.net> on  Nov 25, 1997
