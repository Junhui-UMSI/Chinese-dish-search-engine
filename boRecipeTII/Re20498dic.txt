Apple Cake
Nutmeg, Cloves, Unbleached flour, Vanilla, Applesauce, Buttermilk*; at room, Baking soda, Apples; shredded, Ginger, Cinnamon, Egg whites; whipped, Granulated sugar, Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a 13" x 9" x 2" baking pan lightly with  cooking spray and flour.  In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking  powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger.  In another  mixing bowl, combine applesauce, buttermilk, vanilla, egg whites, and  apples.  Mix dry ingredients with dry ingredients just until moistened.  Pour batter into prepared pan.  Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
