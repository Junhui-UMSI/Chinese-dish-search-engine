Weiner Schnitzel
Pepper, French bread crumbs, Eggs, Veal cutlets, Salt, Flour, 
Preheat oil in large frying pan.Pound meat on each side to 1/4" thickness.[  Cut into 6 pieces of its a veal steak.] Mix flour and seasonings. Dip meat  in flour then eggs, then lightly coat with the bread crumbs. Let stand for  5-10 minutes. Fry 3-4 minutes per side til nice and brown.Turn often.    Note; fry in very hot oil, but fry slowly.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
