From: Wendy Lockman <wlockman@ra1.randomc.com>    Date: Fri, 19 Apr 1996 18:03:47 -0400    In small skillet, heat oil over medium heat; add green pepper, onion,  garlic and jalapeno pepper (seeded and minced), and saute until vegies are  softened, 1-2 min. Add beans and cook, stirring frequently, until beans are  thoroughly heated, 2-3 min. Transfer to small mixing bowl; add remaining  ingredients and mix well.
