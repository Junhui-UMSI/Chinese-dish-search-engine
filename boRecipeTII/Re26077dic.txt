Tofu Provencal Club Sandwich
Yellow bell pepper, BASIL MAYONNAISE, Black pepper, Vegetable bouillon, Fresh herbs, Reduced-fat tofu, Reduced-fat mayonnaise, Red onion, Arugula lettuce, Worcestershire Sauce, Red bell pepper, French bread, whole wheat, Fresh basil, Lemon juice, Garlic powder, Black pepper, olive oil, Garlic, 
To make marinade, combine olive oil, lemon juice, bouillon, Worcestershire  sauce, garlic, herbs and black pepper in a shallow baking dish.    Slice tofu lengthwise into four steaks.  Add tofu, peppers and onion to  marinade.  Set aside 15 minutes. Turn tofu and vegetables occasionally to  coat with marinade.    Combine mayonnaise, basil, garlic and pepper in a blender or food processor  and puree until smooth.
