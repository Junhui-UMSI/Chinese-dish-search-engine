Pizza Sauce (Mitchs)
Tabasco, Makes this many pints OR, Pizzas, Good Red Wine, Salt to taste, Angela Mia crushed tomatoes;, Fresh ground black pepper, Garlic powder, AMOUNT, Oregano, Fennel, Basil, olive oil, Marjoram, Water, 
1. Combine all ingredients and slowly add to sauce while whisking.    2. Bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer over low heat for 1 to 3 hours, or  until you like the taste.    3. Makes about 6 pints or enough for roughly 20 servings. Freezes well. The  only way to buy Angela Mia tomatoes is Sams or a warehouse outlet and it  comes in 100 ounce cans.
