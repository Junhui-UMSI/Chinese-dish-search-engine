Upside Down French Toast
Eggs, Skim milk, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Salt, Unsweetened pineapple rings, Bread, Reduced calorie margarine, 
Melt margarine in bottom of 8" baking dish.   Arrange pineapple rings on  bottom of dish and sprinkle brown sugar on top. Combine milk and eggs. Soak  the bread in the egg-milk mixture, then put on top of the first  ingredients. Pour remaining milk and eggs over the bread. Can be  refrigerated overnight at this point. Bake at 375 degrees for 20-25 min. or  til browned as desired. Serve with syrup.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
