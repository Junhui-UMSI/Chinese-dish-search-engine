Trailblazin Beef Soup
Red Potatoes; halved, Pepper, Water, Onions; each cut into 8, Carrots; peeled and cut into, Cilantro; coarsely chopped, Salt, Beef stock, olive oil, Garlic powder, Garlic, Ears Corn; each cut into, Chuck Pot Roast; trimmed and, Ground cumin, Zucchini; cut into 1" pieces, 
Heat 3 tb of the oil in a large skillet. Add meat, garlic powder and cumin;  stir-fry until meat is well browned. Remove with a slotted spoon to an 8  quart stock pot.    Heat remaining 3 tb oil in th pan; add onion and saute until soft. Transfer  to the pot. Add remaining ingredients, bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat  and simmer 1 hour or longer. Serve with flour tortillas or cornbread.    Per serving: 355 calories, 22 g protein, 37 g carbohydrate, 14 g fat, 3 g  saturated fat, 41 mg cholesterol, 335 mg sodium, 4 g fiber.
