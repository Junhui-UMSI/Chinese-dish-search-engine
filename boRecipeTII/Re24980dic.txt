Autumn Sweet Potato Bake
Fresh sweet potatoes or 16, Light raisins, (16 oz) apricot halves, Shake of nutmeg, Cornstarch, Liquid from apricots; (about, Salt, SAUCE, Grated orange rind, Cinnamon, Brown sugar, COMBINE, Sherry or 2 T lemon juice, ADD, 
Spread sauce over potatoes/apricot mixture.    Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #472 by christine miller <jcm@iquest.net>  on Dec 27, 1997
