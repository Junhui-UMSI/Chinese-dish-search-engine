Pumpkin Pie Squares 2
Rolled oats, Brown sugar, Pecans, Eggs, Ginger, Butter, Cinnamon, Coconut; optional, Flour, Eagle Brand Low Fat Milk, Canned pumpkin, 
Combine flour, oats and sugar.  Cut in butter till crumbly.  Stir in  pecans. Press into 9" square baking dish.  Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.  Combine remaining ingredients,except coconut.  Pour over base. Sprinkle  with coconut. Return to oven and bake another 25 - 30 minutes or until set.  Serve warm or chilled.
