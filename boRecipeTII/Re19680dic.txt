Anthonys Moutwatering Meatballs
Crisco Natural Blend, Ground pork, Ground Veal, Garlic, Salt, Pepper, Fresh parsley, Egg, Ground beef, White Bread; Pulled Apart, Water, Romano Cheese; Grated, Breadcrumbs, 
Combine first 4 ingredients. Toss bread pieces with water and add to meat  mixture. Mix well. Add breadcrumbs to mix and then form into balls. Heat  oil in large frying pan, and add meatballs in a single layer and cook on  medium-high heat for 4 minutes. Flip. Cook for 4-6 minutes more (until  done). Drain in a paper-towel lined bowl.
