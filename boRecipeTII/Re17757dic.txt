Almond Tuna and Rice
Green Pepper, Dill weed, Slivered almonds, Fresh bread crumbs, VEG-ALL Mixed Vegetables, Tuna (12.5 oz), Rice, Mayonnaise, 
1. Drain VEG-ALL; combine liquid with mayonnaise, blending until smooth. 2.  Stir in tuna, rice, green pepper, dill and vegetables. 3. Spoon into  greased 2-quart casserole dish. 4. In small skillet, melt butter; stir in  bread crumbs and almonds, coat well and spoon over mixture in casserole. 5.  Bake at 375F. for 30 minutes or until bubbly and lightly browned.
