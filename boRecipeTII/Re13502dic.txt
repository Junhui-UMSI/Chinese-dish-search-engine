Whole-Wheat Heart Smart Cookies
Egg, All purpose flour, Salt, Vanilla, Whole wheat flour, Ground cinnamon, Baking Powder, Margarine, Brown sugar, 
1. In a small bowl combine flours, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. 2. In  a large bowl with mixer at medium speed, beat margarine and brown sugar  until well blended. Beat in egg and vanilla. With mixer at low speed,  gradually beat in flour mixture until well blended. 3. Wrap dough in  plastic wrap or waxed paper; refrigerate at least 1 hour. 4. On floured  pastry clothy with floured rolling pin, roll out dough, 1/3 at a time, to  1/8 inch thickness.  Dip heart cookie cutters into flour. 5. Place cookies  on ungreased cookie sheets. Bake in preheated 350 degree according to the  following times or until lightly browned around edges. 6. Remove and cool  on wire rack.  Decorate as desired.    Yields 36 cookies.
