Syrup Glaze
Water, SYRUP; IMIT MAPLE, #10, 
1.  IN STEP 1, OMIT FRUIT JUICE AND SUGAR, INCREASE SYRUP TO 3 1/3 CUPS,  ADD  2 CUPS WATER.  BRING TO A BOIL; BOIL ABOUT 5 MINUTES, STIRRING CONSTANTLY.    2.  BRUSH WARM GLAZE OVER ROLLS OR COFFEE CAKES IMMEDIATELY AFTER BAKING.    Recipe Number: D04501    SERVING SIZE: 2 TABLESPO    From the <Army Master Recipe Index File> (actually used today!).
