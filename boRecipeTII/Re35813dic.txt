Stuffed Eggs a la Polonaise
Fresh Lemon Juice, Ground Polish ham, Dry mustard, Salt, Mayonnaise, Chopped capers, Hard cooked eggs; shelled, 
Cut eggs in half lengthwise; remove yolks. Mash yolks of eggs with a fork.  Blend in remaining ingredients except egg whites. Refill whites with  mixture. Garnish with paprika or capers. Chill until ready to serve. Makes       12    appetizers.
