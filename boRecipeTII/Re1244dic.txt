Cucumber Dip for Smoked Fish
Pepper, Cucumbers, Sour cream, Vinegar, Crushed dried dill, Chives, Salt, 
Peel and chop cucumbers, then mix with other ingredients and chill for at least 2 hours. Slice smoked fish thinly.  Place a slice of fish on a thin buttered  slice of pumpernickel or a crisp cracker. Top with a spoonful of cucumber dip as a canape, or serve in a bowl with a plate of crackers and a plate of dipping-side pieces of smoked fish for do-it-yourself guests.    Posted to bbq-digest by Bill Mathews on Dec 04, 1998.
