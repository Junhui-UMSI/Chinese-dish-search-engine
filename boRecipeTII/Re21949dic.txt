Applesauce Cake
Applesauce, EDENSOY, Baking Powder, walnuts, Raisins, Sea Salt, Whole wheat pastry flour, Allspice, Cinnamon, Vanilla, Baking soda, Safflower Oil, Honey, Nutmeg, 
Preheat oven to 350 F. Oil two inverted torte pans or one 8"x8" cake pan.  Blend all liquid ingredients until creamy. Add baking powder, baking  soda, spices and salt to flour and mix well. Pour wet ingredients into  dry ingredients and stir thoroughly. Add nuts and raisins if using and  mix well. Bake 30-40 minutes, until center is done. If using inverted  torte pans, turn cakes over and fill with custard. Decorate with fresh  fruit if desired If using cake pan, cut into squares and pour custard  over individual pieces.
