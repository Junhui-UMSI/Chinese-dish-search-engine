Hanukkah Cookies
Butter or margarine, Sugar or 1/2 cup honey, Eggs, Flour, Whole wheat flour , Salt, Baking Powder, Vanilla; almond or orange, 
Cream butter or margarine together with sugar or honey. Beat in eggs and  mix together well. Add dry ingredients (which have been sifted together),  then extract. Form dough into a ball; chill in refrigerator at least 1 hour  before rolling out. Roll out 1/8" thickness on floured board; cut into  shapes using holiday cookie cutters (in the shape of menorahs, dreidels or  Jewish stars).Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes on ungreased cookie  sheets; let cool on wire rack. Can be decorated with blue icing, if  desired. Makes 4 dozen.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Sheryl Donner <boxermom@iowacity.net> on  Dec 04, 1998,
