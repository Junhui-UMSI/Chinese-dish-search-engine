Beef Jerky
Pinch salt, Soy sauce, Honey, Lemon juice, Flank steak, Garlic, Pinch pepper, 
Put steak in freezer for about half an hour, just until firm.  Slice steak  across grain, about 1/4-inch thick.  Combine remaining ingredients and  marinate steak strips in this for at least 2 hours. Place slices on rack in  pan and dry in oven at 150 degrees, 12 hours.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Feb 12, 98
