Walnut and Egg Spread
Mayonnaise, Walnuts, chopped fine, Paprika, Pepper, Eggs, hard boiled, Salt, 
Chop eggs and nuts until very fine.  Add salt, pepper and paprika to taste.  Mix. Add enough mayonnaise to hold mixture together. Keep chilled when not  using.    Source: Torah Prep High School for Girls Passover booklet    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #122 by Tzimmi@aol.com on Apr 16, 1997
