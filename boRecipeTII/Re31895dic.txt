Tabbouli
Dash, cayenne pepper, Green Onions, Uncooked bulgur wheat, Lemon juice, Soy sauce, Hot, Parsley, Finely chopped fresh mint, Vegetable stock, boiling, Tomato, diced, Clove garlic, finely minced, 
Soak the bulgur in the hot stock for 30 minutes: Combine the lemon juice,  soy sauce, garlic, and cayenne in a small jar with a tight-fitting lid and  shake vigorously to blend; set aside. Drain the bulgur and combine it with  the tomato, green onions, parsley, and mint. Add the dressing and toss  thoroughly.    Serve chilled on salad plates with romaine lettuce.
