From: "Andrew M. Gage" <agage@NS.MORAN.COM>    Date: Sun, 30 Jun 1996 21:08:55 -0400  Grill Directions;    1. Heat Grill. In large bowl combine all ingredients; mix well. Shape into  ~6 patties, 1/2 inch thick    2. When ready to grill, lightly oil grill rack. Place patties on gas grill  over medium heat or on charcoal grill 4 to 6 inches from medium coals. Cook  10-12 minutes or until burgers are no longer pink in the center, turn once.    Broiler Directions;    1. Prepare patties as directed above.    2. Spray broiler pan with non stick cooking spray. Place patties on  spray-coated pan. Broil 44 to 6 inches from heat for 10 to 12 minutes or  until burgers are no longer pink in the center, turning once.
