Peaches and Cream Liqueur
Almond extract, (14 oz) sweetened condensed, Vodka, Eggs, Vanilla, Heavy or whipping cream, Peach Schnapps, 
Combine all in a blender until smooth. Pour into bottles and refrigerate  overnight. Shake well. Store up to 1 month.    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #239 by Shelley Sparks  <ssparks@mailbox.arn.net> on Sep 14, 1997
