Potato Torta - Torta Di Patate
Butter or oil, Parmigiano-Reggiano;, grated, Freshly grated pepper, Fresh oregano or thyme or, Salt to taste, Fresh tomato slices;,, Flour, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Mozzarella cheese;, small, Starchy potatoes, 
Preheat oven to 475 degrees. Boil, peel and rice or mash potatoes. Work in  the flour along with a generous pinch of salt. Spread the potato mixture in  a layer 1/4 inch think in a baking pan rubbed with butter or oil. Arrange  the Mozzarella cubes over potato dough. Sprinkle on Parmesan and a pinch of  pepper along with fresh herb of choice. If desired top with fresh tomatoes  which have been peeled and seeded. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top.  Bake at 475 degrees until the surface of the torta has browned lightly and  the potato dough is crunchy. NOTES  : This torta can be served as an antipasti course or as an entree.
