Ponche De Pina (Hot Pineapple Punch)
Whole allspice, Water, Coconut milk, Pineapples, Sticks cinnamon, Cloves, Sugar, Light rum, 
Peel pineapples; chop or shread. Add water and let stand overnight. Put in  large saucepan with spices, sugar, and coconut milk. Boil for 5 minutes.  Strain liquid into large pitcher. Add rum and serve hot. Makes 8-12  servings.    Note: If fresh coconut milk is not available, pour sweet milk over shredded  coconut. Let stand for several hours. Squeeze through a cloth.
