Sweet Corn Salsa
Rice vinegar, Jalapeno pepper, Coarse salt, red onion, tomatoes, cilantro, Fresh corn kernels, Butter, Black Pepper, 
Cut raw kernels off cobs with a sharp knife.  Combine all ingredients in a  saucepan over high heat and stir while bringing salsa to a boil. When  butter has melted completely and takes on the look of a light butter sauce,  remove from heat and serve immediately. Use with chicken or lobster. Makes        3    cups.
