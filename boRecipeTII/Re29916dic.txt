Teriyaki Fish
Cooking sherry, Soy sauce, Sugar, Monosodium glutamate (opt), Sake (Japanese rice wine) or, Fish fillets, Clove garlic,, Grated fresh ginger root, 
1. In a shallow, 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic baking dish combine  soy sauce, sugar, ginger root, garlic, monosodium glutamate and sake. Stir  to combine well. 2. Place fish fillets in sauce mixture and marinate for  1/2 hour. 3. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven for 9 minutes basting  occasionally with sauce.
