Annie Wades Sweet Potato Souffle
Sweet potatoes, Brown sugar, Butter, Egg, Light rum, Cinnamon, 
Prep Time: 5 minutes (plus potato cooking time = 30 minutes) Bake Time: 50 minutes Total Time: 55 minutes (1 hour 20 minutes) Oven Temp: 350F / 180C / Gas Mark 4  Preheat oven.      Combine the mashed sweet potatoes with the rest of the ingredients. Beat  well by hand or with an electric mixer. Fold into a buttered 2 quart baking  dish. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes or until a knife inserted into the center  comes out clean and the top is firm and golden.
