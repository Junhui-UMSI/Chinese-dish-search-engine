Quakers- Peanutty Crisscrosses
Peanut butter, Water, Brown Sugar, Margarine\butter, softened, Granulated sugar, Vanilla, All-purpose flour, Egg, QUAKER Oats uncooked, Baking soda, 
Beat together margarine, peanut butter and sugar until creamy. Add water,  egg and vanilla;  beat well.  Add combined oats, flour and baking soda.  Cover;  chill about 1 hour.    Heat oven to 350 F.  Shape dough into l-inch balls. Place on ungreased  cookie sheet;  flatten with tines of fork, dipped in granulated sugar, to  form crisscross pattern. Bake 9 to 10 minutes or until edges are golden  brown.  Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet; remove to wire rack. Cool  completely.  Store in tightly covered container. ABOUT 7 DOZEN    Nutrition Information:  1 cookie Calories 70, Calories From Fat 27, Total  Fat 3g, Saturated Fat 1g, Cholesterol 2mg, Sodium 40mg, Total Carbohydrates  8g, Dietary Fiber 0g, Protein 2g
