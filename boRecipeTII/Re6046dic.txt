Pizza Bread2
Salt, Mozzarella cheese; grated, Dried Oregano, Yeast, olive oil, Whole wheat flour, Bread flour, Mushrooms; chopped, Sun-dried tomatoes;, Pizza sauce, Pepperoni; diced (optional), 
Add one or two of the optional ingredients after the first kneading period  when the dough has formed a smooth ball.    Maked a 1.5 pound loaf.
