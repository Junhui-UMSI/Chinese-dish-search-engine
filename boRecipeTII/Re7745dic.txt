Portabella Fajitas
Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Onion sliced, salt and pepper, Soft flour or corn tortillas, Green pepper sliced, Portabella caps sliced, 
Saute above ingredients until soft (about 5-6 minutes). Serve wrapped in  soft or corn tortillas with your choice of accompanients (we like it with  chopped tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, shredded lettuce, sour cream and  salsa).
