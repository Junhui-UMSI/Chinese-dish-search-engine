Bbq Veggie Brochettes
Salt, Zucchini, olive oil, Lemon juice, Red onion, Garlic, Sweet red/yellow pepper, Pepper, Ground cumin, Fresh mint, chopped, 
In small bowl, whisk together mint, olive oil, lemon juice, cumin and  garlic; set aside.    Cut zucchini, red pepper and onion into 1-inch chunks. Thread chunks onto  eight 8-inch pre-soaked wooden skewers.    Place on greased grill over medium-high heat; cover and cook, turning and  basting often with sauce, for about 15 minutes or until tender. Season with  salt and pepper.
