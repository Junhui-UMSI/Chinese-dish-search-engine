Carrots; medium-sliced, Ground coriander, Bunch green onions (both, Ribs celery; medium-shced, Corn tortillas, (10-oz) Ro-tel tomatoes, Sharp Cheddar cheese; grated, Jar (3.2-oz) Spice Island,
