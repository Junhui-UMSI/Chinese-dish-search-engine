Vietnamese Pork "Spaghetti Sauce" over Rice
Sugar, Garlic; chopped, Coriander Leaves, Vietnamese Style Fish Sauce, Lime juice, Tomatoes; seeded and chopped, Shallots; chopped, Tomato paste, Ground pork, Serrano Chiles; seeded and, Chicken Stock, Vegetable oil, Black pepper, Hot Steamed Rice, 
Pour oil into a saucepan and place over high heat. Add pork and saute until  lightly browned, about 5 minutes, breaking up lumps. Add sugar,    1 1/2 tb fish sauce, the lime juice and chiles. Cook 1 minute. Set aside  in a bowl.    Put garlic, shallots, pepper and more oil if needed into the saucepan; fry  over medium heat until fragrant. Add tomato and cook until reduced to a  slightly lumpy sauce, about 5 minutes. Add pork, tomato paste, remaining  fish sauce and chicken stock; simmer 10 minutes. Garnish with coriander.  Serve over hot steamed rice.    Source: San Francisco Chronicle Typed by Katherine Smith Kook-Net: The  Shadow Zone IV - Stinson Beach, CA
