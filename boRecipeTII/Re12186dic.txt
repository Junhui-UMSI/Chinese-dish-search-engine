Zucchini Bread #04
Eggs, Sugar, (8-oz) crushed pineapple, Grated zucchini, All-purpose flour, Vegetable oil, Cinnamon, Baking Powder, Vanilla, Pecans, Baking soda, 
Mix all ingredients together. Pour into loaf pan and bake for 1 hour at 350  degrees.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
