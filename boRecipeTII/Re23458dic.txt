Turkey and Tomato Casserole
Onion, American Cheese; Shredded, (9 oz) Frozen French-fried, (10 3/4-oz) Condensed Tomato, Catsup, (13 oz) Whole Kernel Corn;, Celery, Cooked Turkey; Chopped, Butter or margarine, 
In a large skillet, cook onion and celery in butter until vegetables are  tender, but not brown. Stir in the corn, turkey, tomato soup, catsup and  cheese. Turn mixture into a 8 x 8-inch baking dish. Arrange French-fried  potatoes over the top in a single layer. Bake uncovered in a 425-F degree  oven for 25-minutes.
