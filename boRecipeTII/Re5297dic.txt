Pinata Cups
Garlic, FILLING:, Sour cream, Minced green onions, Chopped ripe olives, Chili powder, Chopped green chiles, Egg, Salt, Shredded sharp Cheddar chees, Cumin, Lean ground beef, 
SHELLS: Combine ingredients for the shells and divide into 30 balls. Press  into miniature muffin tins forming miniature "pie shells". FILLING &  TOPPING: Combine filling ingredients and divide mixture evenly among the  meat shells. Place a heaping 1/2 teaspoon of shredded cheese on top of the  filling in each shell. Bake in a preheated 425 degree oven for 7-8 minutes.  Remove pinata cups from muffin tins and place on paper towels to drain.  Serve hot. Pinata cups may be prepared the day before serving, covered, and  refrigerated. Reheat in a 350 degree oven for 5-8 minutes. They may be  flash frozen and stored in plastic bags.Reheat frozen pinata cups in a 350  degree oven for 10 minutes or until heated through. Source: "Teasers and  Appeasers" an Hors dOeuvre Cookbook.
