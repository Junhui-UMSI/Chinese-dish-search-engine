Apricot Orange Bread
Butter, Salt, Sugar, (12-oz) Nestle Toll House, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Orange juice, Egg, All-purpose flour, Walnuts, Chopped dried apricots, Mllk, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In small bowl, combine flour, baking powder,  salt and baking soda; set aside. In large bowl, combine sugar and butter;  beat well. Add orange juice, milk and egg; beat well. Gradually blend in  flour mixture. Stir in apricots, Nestle Toll House Little Bits semi-sweet  chocolate and nuts. Spread into greased and floured 9x5x3-inch loaf pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 50-55 minutes. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pan.  Cool completely on wire rack. Makes one loaf.
