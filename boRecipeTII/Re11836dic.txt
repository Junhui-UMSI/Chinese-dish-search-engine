Raw Mushroom Appetizers
Stuffed Olives, Dry mustard, Cream Cheese, Pepper, Mushrooms, Deviled Ham  , Onion salt, Garlic salt, Pimento, 
Rinse mushrooms well.  Remove the stems.  Mix the cream cheese, deviled  ham, chopped olives and the seasonings.  Spoon or pipe the cheese mixture  into the mushroom caps.  Garnish with pimento.  Place on platter, wrap and  refrigerate until serving time.  These may be made a day in advance. From:  Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sydsbook.zip
