Whole Wheat and Honey Banana Bread
Whole wheat flour, Nutmeg, All-purpose flour, Salt, Very ripe bananas, Eggs, butter, Honey, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, 
Sift flour together, add baking powder and salt, resift.  Cream together  butter and honey, beat in eggs, then bananas. Add spices, add flour in 2 or  3 parts. Blend well. (Optional: walnuts, raisins, etc). Bake at 350F for  about an hour. Makes one loaf.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
