Pumpkin Pie 4
Envelope unflavored gelatin, Salt, Equal. sweetener, Cornstarch, Evaporated skim milk, Ground nutmeg, Pie crust, graham cracker, Pumpkin 16-ounce, Eggs, beaten or 1/2 cup egg, Ground cinnamon, Ground ginger, 
In  a large saucepan combine gelatin, cornstarch, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger  and salt. Stir in pumpkin and evaporated skim milk. Let stand 5 minutes to  soften gelatin. Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture bubbles; cook  and stir for 2 minutes more. Remove from heat. Gradually stir 1 cup hot  mixture into eggs; return egg mixture to saucepan. Cook and stir over low  heat for  2 minutes. Do not boil. Remove from heat. Stir in Equal. Pour  into baked pie crust. Cover and chill 6 hours or overnight. Makes 8  servings.
