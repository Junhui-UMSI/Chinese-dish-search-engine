Quick Chicken Dijon
milk, White pepper, Onion, Chicken breasts, Cream of mushroom soup, Fresh parsley, Chicken thighs, Butter, Garlic, Dijon mustard, 
1. In a large skillet melt the butter and brown the chicken on all sides;  about 4-5 minutes per side.    2. Remove chicken and keep warm in the over (200 degrees).    3. In the same skillet saute the garlic and onion until golden and tender.    4. Stir in soup, milk, dijon, parsley and pepper; stir until smooth and  bring to a boil.    5. Add chicken to skillet; reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or until  chicken is cooked and no longer pink.    NOTES : Serve over rice or broad egg noodle.
