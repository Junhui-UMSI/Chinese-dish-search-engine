Apple Snowballs
Red delicious apples, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Water, Allspice, Orange rind, fresh grated, Butter, FROM BRIGITTE SEALING, Pastry for two crust pie, Egg, 
Core and pare apples. In a medium bowl, cream butter with sugar,  orange rind, and spices. Spoon into core of apples. Roll out pastry,  cut into 6 inch squares, and wrap around apples. Beat together egg  and water. Place apples on a baking sheet and brush with egg/water  mixture. Bake at 425F for 15 minutes, then reduce heat to 350F and  bake 40 minutes longer, or until apples are done and pastry is brown.
