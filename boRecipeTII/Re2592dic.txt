Peachy Pork (Roblyns Recipe)
Boneless pork (tenderloin is, Prepared salsa, any brand, (heaping) peach preserves, Envelope taco seasoning mix, Oil, for browning, 
Cube the pork and place in a bowl. Add the dry taco seasoning mix, and toss  to coat. Heat oil in skillet, and add cubes. Brown, then drain off fat.    Add salsa and peach preserves to the skillet. Stir to mix well, and lower  heat. Simmer gently for 15 minutes.    Note: I serve this with rice to utilize the wonderful sauce. If any is left  over, it makes a wonderful topper for a baked potato.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 01 Feb 97 by "Sharon H. Frye" <shfrye@PEN.K12.VA.US>  on Feb 2, 1997.
