Peanut Butter Cups #1
Graham cracker crumbs, semi-sweet chocolate, powdered sugar, Peanut butter, Butter, 
Mix sugar, graham cracker crumbs, peanut butter & butter together  thoroughly. Press evenly & firmly into a buttered 13x9-inch pan. Melt  chocolate chips in top of double boiler. Spread melted chocolate over top  of first mixture. Cool. Cut into squares. Yields 1 dozen.
