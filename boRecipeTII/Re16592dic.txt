Aioli
Mayonnaise, Garlic, 
Mash 2 cloves garlic to a paste with salt and stir into 1/2 cup mayonnaise.  This Provencal classic is usually served with fish or shellfish; it is also  excellent on new potatoes, fresh tomatoes or in a vegetable sandwich.    VARIATIONs: *Use commercially made egg-free mayonnaise. *Add a little  lemon, paprika and extra virgin olive oil.
