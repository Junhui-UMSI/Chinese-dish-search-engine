Whole Wheat Peanut Sesame - Pan-1
Peanut butter (2 oz), Dry milk, Sesame seeds, Salt, Dry yeast, Water, Butter, XKGR41A Don Fifield, Whole wheat flour, 
Timer OR Bake (Rapid) mode may be used. Place all ingredients (except  liquids and yeast) inside the bread pan. Add liquid ingredients. Close  cover and place dry yeast into the yeast holder. SELECT: WHOLE WHEAT BAKE  MODE. Press start. 5:00 will show.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #213 by serge.cyr@sympatico.ca (Serge Cyr)  on Aug 14, 1997
