Tomato Cucumber Salsa
salt and pepper, olive oil, Cucumber;peeled,seeded and, Finely diced, IRWIN E. SOLOMON JJGF65A, Pepper,stemmed and seeded, Dill; finely chopped, Fresh or jarred Jalapeno, PHILLY. INQUIRER, Ripe tomatoes; cored,chopped, Onion, 
In a food processor or blender or with a knife,finely chop the  onion,tomatoes,and Jalapeno pepper. Transfer to abowl and mix in the olive  oil,cucumber and dill. Season with salt and pepper.Makes about    2 1/2    cups...or four servings.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #170 by "Griff" <wgriffin@ix.netcom.com> on  Jul 2, 1997
