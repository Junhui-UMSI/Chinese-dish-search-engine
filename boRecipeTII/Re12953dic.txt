Zesty Green Beans
Butter or vegetable oil, Red bell pepper -- 1/2-inch, Water, Clove garlic -- minced, Green beans, cut diagonally, Inch lengths, Wine vinegar, Strips, 
1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, melt butter. Add garlic and  saute briefly. Add beans, pepper, and water. Cover and steam until  tender-crisp (about 6 minutes).    2. Remove cover, add vinegar, and increase heat to high to evaporate most  of the liquid (1 to 2 minutes).
