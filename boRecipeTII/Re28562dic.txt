Tex-Mex Pasta Skillet
Canned diced mild green, Onion; diced, Cheddar cheese; or Monterey, Frozen corn, Canned stewed tomatoes, Wagon wheel or other pasta, Green bell pepper; diced, Zucchini; diced, Cilantro; chopped, Chorizo sausage; fully, 
Cook pasta in a large pan of boiling water 10 minutes, or until al dente.  Drain and keep warm. saute sausage, green pepper and onion in a heavy  nonstick skillet over medium high heat about 5 minutes, stirring frequently  until vegetables are tender. Discard any fat in skillet. Stir in next 4  ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer 10 minutes.  Stir in pasta and cilantro. Serve sprinkled with cheese.    Per serving: calories 715, fat 27.5g, 34% calories from fat, cholesterol  62mg, protein 31.0g, carbohydrates 92.0g, fiber 8.9g, sodium 1114mg.    The recommended wines are: Pinot Noir, Beaujolais, or Chianti.    MC Fortmatted and MC Busted by Barb at PK <abprice@wf.net>    Suggested Wine: Pinot Noir, Beaujolais, or Chianti.
