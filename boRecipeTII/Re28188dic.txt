Baked Eggs in Potatoes
Heavy cream, Chives, Eggs, cheese, Butter, Baking potatoes, salt and pepper, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Scrub and dry potatoes, place on a baking  sheet and rub a little oil or butter over skins. Place on baking sheets,  bake 1 to 1 1/4 hours. Remove from oven.    Cut a slice lengthwise off each potato. Scoop out insides. Mash scooped out  potatoes in a bowl with remaining butter and seasoning beat cheese into  potato mixture. Press mixture back into shells, leaving a hollow in center  large enough for an egg. Season and spoon the cream over. Return to the  oven. Bake 8 to 10 minutes until eggs are lightly set. Sprinkle the chives  and serve.    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Feb 16, 1998
