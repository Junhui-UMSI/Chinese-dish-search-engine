Lemon Ice Cream
Lemon Rind, Lemon extract, Half-and-half, milk, Sugar, Frozen lemon juice; thawed, 
Combine all ingredients in freezer container of a 1 1/2-gallon hand-turned  or electric freezer. Freeze according to manufacturers instructions. Pack  freezer with additional ice and rock salt, and let stand 30 minutes to 1  hour. Makes 1 gallon.
