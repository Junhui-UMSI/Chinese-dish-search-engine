Teriyaki Tofu Brochettes
Garlic powder, Honey, BROCHETTES, Large button mushrooms,, Zucchini, sliced crosswise, Ground ginger, Pineapple juice, Fresh pineapple, cut, Red onion, cut in six, Mirin or sherry, Red bell pepper, cut in, TERIYAKI MARINADE, Sesame oil, Red pepper flakes, Yaki or extra firm tofu,, Yellow bell pepper, cut, Tamari or soy sauce, 
Combine marinadeingredients in a shallow glass baking dish. Add tofu  and pineapple chunks and let marinate at room temperature one hour. Stir  occasionallytocoat tofu with marinade.       Prepare grill. Thread skewers with tofu, pineapple, andvegetables  leaving small spaces between pieces of food to allow for faster, more even  cooking. Brush brochettes with some of the marinade.       When coals are ready, place skewers on grill and cook 10 to 15  minutes, turning once. Brush brochettes with more marinade as they cook.  Brochettes are ready when edges become crips and begin to blacken.
