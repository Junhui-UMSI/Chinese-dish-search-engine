Turkey Stroganoff (Admiston)
Your favorite pasta; cooked, Nutmeg, Onion, Sour cream, Dry white wine, olive oil, Paprika, Ground turkey, Mushrooms, Salt and pepper, 
Heat oil in heavy skillet over medium heat. Brown turkey and onion. Mix in  mushrooms, wine and nutmeg. Season with salt and pepper. Reduce heat and  simmer 15 minutes. Add sour cream. Do not boil. Place pasta on a platter.  Pour turkey mixture over top and sprinkle with paprika.  Post and Courier, Charleston, SC, Sunday, October 20, 1996.
