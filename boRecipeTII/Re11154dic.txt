Raisin Cranberry Relish
Ground cinnamon, Tart Cooking Apple,, Salt, Seedless Golden Raisins, Whole Cranberry Sauce, Chopped Dried Apricots, Lemon, Grated Peel and, 
Combine all of the ingredients in a large saucepan. Gradually heat to  boiling, stirring often.  Simmer for 5 minutes then cool to room  temperature.  Cover and refrigerate. Serve chilled.    "The Sun Maid Cookbook"
