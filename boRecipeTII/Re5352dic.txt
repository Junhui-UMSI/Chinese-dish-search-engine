Pineapple and Strawberries with Mango Sauce
fresh mango, Fresh lime juice, Fresh pineapple chunks, fresh strawberries, 
Position knife blade in food processor bowl; add cubed mango, and process  until smooth. Combine mango puree and lime juice in a bowl, and stir well.  Cover and chill. Yield: 8 servings.      Per serving: 90 Calories; 1g Fat (7% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 23g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 2mg Sodium      NOTES : To serve, spoon 1/2 cup pineapple chunks into each of 8 dessert  compotes, and top with 1/4 cup sliced strawberries and 2 tablespoons of  mango mixture.
