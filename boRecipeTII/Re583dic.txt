Fudgy Brownies
Cocoa, Flour, Sugar, Egg whites, Applesauce, Baking Powder, Salt, Vanilla, Liqueur; (try Godiva or, Peanut butter, 
Spray 8"x 8" pan with Pam. In medium bowl, sift together cocoa, flour,  baking powder & salt.    Combine peanut butter with liqueur warmed in the microwave. It will be kind  of sticky.    In large bowl, mix this with sugar. It will be crumbly.    Mix in egg whites , applesauce & vanilla. Combine flour mixture with other  mixture. Bake 35 minutes at 350 till a toothpick in the center comes out  not quite dry. Do not overbake.    Recipe by: Net    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Linda Shapiro <lss@coconet.com> on Nov 02,  1998,
