Cranberry Waffles and Orange Sauce
Buttermilk biscuit mix, Sugar, Eggs, milk, Shortening, Cranberries, ORANGE SAUCE, Sugar, Cornstarch, Salt, Water, Butter, Orange rind, Orange juice, Lemon juice, 
Combine biscuit mix and sugar; stir in eggs and milk.  Add melted  shortening, mixing well; fold in cranberries.  Bake in a preheated waffle  baker, and serve with Orange sauce.    Orange Sauce: Combine sugar, cornstarch, and salt; add to boiling water.  Cook 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat; stir in butter  orange rind, orange juice, and lemon juice. Yield: about 2 cups.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the 1970s. Typed for you by  Nancy Coleman.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Sep 21, 98
