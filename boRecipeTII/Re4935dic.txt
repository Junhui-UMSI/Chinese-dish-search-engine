Pickled Herring
Vegetable oil, Herring fillets, To 3/4 cup vinegar, Bay leaf, Onions, sliced, Cold water, Sugar, 
Soak herring for 3 or 4 hours in cold water. Wash in cold water and cut  into cubes. Prepare a marinade with the remaining ingredients and marinate  for 2 days. Drain and serve.    From The Cookie Ladys Files    Posted by COOKIE.LADY [Cookie] on Mar 16, 1993
