Baked Indian Pudding
Yellow granulated corn meal, Black molasses, Hot milk, Eggs, Salt, Granulated sugar, Lard or butter, Baking soda, 
From: jlang@cityscape.co.uk (Jack Lang)    Date: Thu, 25 Aug 1994 04:05:35 GMT  This is from a leaflet from the famous Durgin Park Restaurant (Where your  grandfather dined a century ago),    Mix all the ingredients thoroughly with one half (3/4 quart) of the above  hot milk and bake in a very hot even until it boils.  Then stir in  remaining half (3/4 quart) of hot milk, and bake in a slow oven heat for  five to seven hours. Bake in a stone crock, well greased inside.
