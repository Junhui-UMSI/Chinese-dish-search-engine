Aloo Keema Methi (Lamb with Potatoes and Fenugreek)
Frozen Peas, Lean mince, Chilli powder, Garlic pulp, Salt, Tomatoes, Fresh red chillies, Potatoes, fresh coriander, Ginger pulp, Turmeric powder, Corn oil, Onions, Chopped, 
1. Put the minced lamb, ginger, garlic, chilli powder, salt and turmeric  into a large bowl and mix together thoroughly. Set to one side.    2. Heat the oil in a deep round-bottemed frying pan or karahi. Throw in the  onion and fry for about 5 minutes until golden brown.    3. Add the minced lamb and stir-fry over a medium heat for 5-7 minutes.    4. Stir in the potatoes, chopped fenugreek, tomatoes and peas and cook for  a further 5-7 minutes, stirring continuously.    5. Just before serving, stir in the fresh coriander and garnish with fresh  red chillies.    I.C.  Posted to EAT-L Digest 15 November 96    Date:    Sat, 16 Nov 1996 15:56:05 +1000    From:    "I.
