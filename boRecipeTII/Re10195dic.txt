Quick and Easy Apricot Bread
Shortening, Ground nutmeg, Salt, Flour, Ground cinnamon, Ground cloves, Dried apricots; cut in small, Sugar, Baking soda, Water, Eggs, 
Sift flour with baking soda; set aside. Cook apricots with water, sugar,  shortening, spices and salt for 5 minutes. Cool apricot mixture.    Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Add eggs to sifted dry ingredients to cooled  apricot mixture. Pour mixture into 2 greased 5x9 inch loaf pans. Bake in  oven for 1hour. Makes 2 loaves.
