Carrots Cooked in Old Wine
Carrots; scraped, Tablespons olive oil, Garlic, Salt, Black Pepper, Dry marsala, Sprigs italian parsley;, 
Cut carrots into discs less than 1/4-inch thick and place in bowl of cold  water for 30 minutes. When carrots are ready, heat oil in skillet over  medium heat. Add garlic and saute 5 minutes or until lightly golden;  discard garlic. Drain carrots and add to skillet. Season with salt and  pepper and saute 2 minutes, constantly stirring with wooden spoon. Add  Marsala and cook, covered 10 minutes more, stirring every so often with  wooden spoon. If by that time the carrots are not sufficiently cooked, add  a little cold water and continue until they are completely cooked but still  firm. Transfer to serving dish, sprinkle with parsley and serve hot.
