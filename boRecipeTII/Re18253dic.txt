Venison Steaks
Carrots, sliced, Black Pepper, Vinegar mixed with 1/4 cup, Onions, sliced, Pinch of ground cloves, Water, Shallots, chopped, Each, cut 1/2 to 3/4" thick, Thyme, olive oil, Dry white wine, Venison round steaks, 8 to 9, Clove garlic, chopped, Mild vinegar (3/4 cup cider, Bay Leaves, 
Place the steaks in an enamel, glass or earthenware bowl. Add the  remaining ingredients & let stand in the refrigerator twenty-four  hours. Turn the meat several times. Remove the steaks & dry,  reserving the marinade. Saute the steaks in shallow, hot fat until  brown on both sides. The steaks should be rare. Serve on a hot  platter with sauce poivrade.
