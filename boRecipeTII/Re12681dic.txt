Yogurt-Tahini Dressing
Salt, Onions, Tamari soy sauce, Green onion, Garlic, Dill weed, Lowfat yogurt, Peanut oil, Sesame butter; (tahini), Vinegar, 
Blend all ingredients except yogurt in a blender. Then add yogurt and blend  BRIEFLY. The longer you blend, the thicker it gets.    Recipe by: Common Ground Restaurant, Brattleboro, VT    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #992 by Sue <suechef@sover.net> on Jan 08,  1998
