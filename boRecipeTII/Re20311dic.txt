Veal Parmigiano
Corn flakes, Veal cutlets, Parmesan cheese, Mozzarella cheese, Tomato sauce , Sugar, Dried Oregano, Butter, onion salt, Egg, 
Preheat your oven at 400 degrees.  Melt butter in a 10x6x1 1/2 inch baking  dish.  Combine corn flake crumbs, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper. Cut  meat in seving pieces. Dip meat in egg, then in crumb mixture. Place in  baking dish.  Bake for about 20 minutes. Turn meat and bake on other side  until brown and tender, usually 15 to 20 minutes. Meanwhile, combine sauce  and rest of ingredients in saucepan. Heat to boiling point stirring  frequently.  Pour over meat, top with Mozzarella cheese and return to oven  long enough to melt the cheese. Randy Rigg
