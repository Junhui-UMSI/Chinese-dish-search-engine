Baked Halibut with Stewed Tomatoes
Onions, sliced, (including juice), Halibut steaks, Canned plum tomatoes-, 1 tsp. dried, Each salt and pepper, Butter, Chopped fresh basil or about, 
In heavy saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; cook onions, stirring  occasionally, for about 10 minutes or just until beginning to brown. Stir  in tomatoes, breaking up with fork. Sprinkle with half of the salt and  pepper; cook for about 3 minutes or until slightly thickened.    Meanwhile, cut halibut into 6 or 8 pieces; remove bones. Sprinkle with  remaining salt and pepper; place in greased 13 x 9 inch baking dish. Spoon  tomato sauce over top; sprinkle with basil. Bake in 450F oven for 10 to 15  minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with fork. Makes 6  servings.
