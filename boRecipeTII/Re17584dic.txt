Almond Ginger Sand Tarts
Almonds -- finely chopped, Flour, For Garnish-----, Almonds -- sliced, Baking Powder, Egg white, Salt, Unsalted Butter -- softened, Vanilla, Ginger -- grated, Vegetable shortening, 
Cream butter, shortening, and confectioners sugar until fluffy.  Add  vanilla, flour, ginger, salt and baking powder.  Add ground almonds and  combine well.  Chill dough for at least 1 hour.    Preheat oven to 325.    Roll dough into balls and arrange on greased cookie sheets.  Flatten each  with the bottom of a glass.  Brush tops with egg white, put one almond  slice on each, and brush almond slice with egg white.  Bake for 15 to 20  minutes, or until tops are golden.    Makes about 30.
