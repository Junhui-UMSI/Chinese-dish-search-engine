2. Wash and scrub potatoes; dry thoroughly. Rub surface with 2 tablespoons  butter. Prick skin with fork, to let steam escape durning cooking. 3. Bake  50 to 60 minutes. Potatoes are done when they can be easily pierced with  fork. 4. Slash tops in an X; then gently squeeze; with fork, remove most of  potato to large bowl, keeping skin intact. Mash potato with fork. 5. In hot  oil in medium skillet, saute onion and green pepper until tender- about 5  minutes. Heat milk. 6. Add sauted vegetables, hot milk, butter, salt,  pepper, paprika and 1 cup grated cheese to mashed potato, mixing well with  fork. Pile lightly into potato shells, mounding high. Sprinkle rest of  grated cheese over tops of potatoes, dividing evenly. 7. Return to oven;  bake 15 minutes, or until thoroughly hot and cheese is melted. Makes 6  servings Recipe From: McCalls, April 1982 Joan Johnson    Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.20
