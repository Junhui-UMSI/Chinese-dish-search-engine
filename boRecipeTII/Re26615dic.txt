Bagna Lauda
Red Bell Pepper, In1" Strips, Stalks Cardoons, Peeled, and, Yellow bell pepper, in 1", Fennel, raw, into 1" strips, Cloves garlic, sliced paper, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Anchovy fillets, rinsed, Unsalted butter, Loaf Country Style Bread, Black pepper, 
Heat oil and butter over medium heat until warm. Add garlic and 2  tablespoons cooking milk, anchovy and black pepper, and blend, using  submersion blender. Pour into fondue pot and serve vegetables on the side.    Yield: 4 servings    Recipe by: Molto Mario MB5743 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #605 by Sue  <suechef@sover.net> on May 11, 1997
