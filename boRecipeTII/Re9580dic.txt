Pumpkin Custard
Pumpkin, canned, Salt, Cinnamon, Egg, well beaten, Nutmeg, Water, warm, milk, Saccharin tablets, (1/4, 
Dissolve saccharin in warm water.  Combine with remaining  ingredients; Pour into custard cup. Place in shallow pan of hot  water. Bake in moderate (350 degree F.) 45 minutes, or until knife  inserted near edge of custard comes out clean.
