Poppy Seed Cake
Butter, Sugar, Water, Eggs, Sour cream, GLAZE, Yellow cake mix, French vannilla pudding mix, Sherry, Sweet sherry, Poppy seed, Butter, 
Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl, beat for a full 5 mins. Pour  into a greased and floured tube pan. Bake at 350 for 1 hr. Cool slightly an  then pour glaze on cake in pan. GLAZE: Mix glaze ingredients together and  bring to a boil, boil for a full 3 mins. Prick top of cake and pour hot  glaze over cake. Let cake cool in pan. Freezes great. 12:05 PM
