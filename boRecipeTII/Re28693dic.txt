Texas Red Chili 2
Masa harina or cornmeal, Cold water, Tbs. cayenne pepper, Garlic, Lean beef, coarsely cubed, Salt, Beef stock or canned beef, Chili powder (about 4 1/2, Crushed cumin seeds or, Oregano, Suet, or vegetable oil, 
from Famous Chili Recipes from Marlboro Country (1979)    Fry suet until crisp.  Then add beef, about 1 lb at a time, and brown,  stirring as it cooks.  Remove each pound after browning.  When all meat is  browned, return it to kettle and add seasonings and beef stock or broth.  Cover and simmer 2 hrs.  Skim off fat.  Combine masa or cornmeal and water  and stir thoroughly into chili.  Simmer 30 min.  Makes about 3-3/4 qts.
