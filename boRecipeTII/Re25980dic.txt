Bacon Mushroom and Swiss Meat Loaf
Corn flakes, Bacon, Onion, Egg, ground beef, swiss cheese, Ground pork, milk, mushrooms, 
In a large bowl add all ingredients. Blend well and shape into a loaf in a  baking dish. Bake at 350 for about an hour or till cooked through.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 332 by Aquasea221@aol.com on Dec  1, 1997
