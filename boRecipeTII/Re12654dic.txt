Yogurt Soup
Rice, Butter, Dried mint, Meat or chicken stock, Flour, Egg yolks, Yogurt, 
1- Boil the rice in meat or chicken broth, on low heat 2- Beat the yoghourt  with the egg yolks and flour. Stirring constanly, slowly add 2 cups of  boiling stock. Add it to the meat stock with rice. Let simmer for 10  minutes. 3- Heat the butter. Add dried mint. Leave on low heat for 1  minute. Pour it over the soup and serve.
