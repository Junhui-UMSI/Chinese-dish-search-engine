20th Century Salad Dressing
olive oil, Salt, Dry mustard, Garlic, Paprika, Wine Vinegar, Black pepper, Sugar, 
Dissolve salt, pepper, paprika, dry mustard and sugar in olive lil. Add  vinegar. Peel clove of garlic and let stand in dressing until ready to use.
