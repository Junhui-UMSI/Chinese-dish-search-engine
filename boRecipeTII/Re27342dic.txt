Toasted Almond Brittle
Toasted almonds, Sugar, Water, Butter, 
Bring sugar and water to a boil in a heavy sauce pan. Brush down sides of  pan with a water to prevent sugar from crystallizing. Cook until amber in  color and water has evaporated. Carefully stir in butter with a wooden  spoon until completely blended. Stir in almonds. Pour onto a greased  1/2-inch sheet pan. Cool.
