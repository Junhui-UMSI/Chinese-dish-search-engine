Baby Ruth Bars
Peanut butter, Special K Cereal, White corn syrup, Smooth peanut butter, (6oz) Chocolate Chips, Sugar, 
Bring Sugar and Syrup to a boil. Remove from heat. Add 1 3\4 cups Peanut  Butter and blend. Add Cereal and pour into 9x13 dish. Melt Chocolate Chips  and 3\4 Cup Peanut Butter. Spread over cereal mixture. Cool and cut into  squares.    Posted to recipelu-digest by Giz2day@aol.com on Feb 15, 1998
