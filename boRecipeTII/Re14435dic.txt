Whole Wheat Orange Anise Bread - Pan-1
Dry yeast, XKGR41A Don Fifield, Butter, Salt, Anise, Dry milk, Whole wheat flour, Orange zest, Orange juice, Water, 
Bake (Rapid) mode may be used. Place all ingredients (except liquids and  yeast) inside the bread pan. Add liquid ingredients. Close cover and place  dry yeast into the yeast holder. SELECT: WHOLE WHEAT BAKE MODE. Press  start.    5:00  will show.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #213 by serge.cyr@sympatico.ca (Serge Cyr)  on Aug 14, 1997
