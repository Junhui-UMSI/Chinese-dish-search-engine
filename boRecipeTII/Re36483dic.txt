Beer-Poached Polish Sausage
Kiebasa sausage; 1 1/4 lbs., Beer, Vegetable oil, Lemon, 
Preheat the grill. Place the beer in a skillet large enough to hold t  sausage. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Prick the sausage and gently poach  it in the beer 4 minutes per side. Drian. If using presoaked w chips or  chunks or other flavorings, sprinkle them over the hot coal o rock of a gas  grill. Brush the grid lightly with oil. Brush the saus lightly with oil.  Grill over medium-hot heat for 5 mintues per side. serve: spilt the sausage  down the center or cut into thick wedges. Sprinkle with lemon juice before  serving.
