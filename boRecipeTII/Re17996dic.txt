Vegetarian Rice and Beans (Tabasco)
Green bell pepper; chopped, Onion, Ground cumin, Vegetable bouillon cubes or, Water, Raw brown rice, Red kidney beans; rinsed and, Jar Original or Spicy, 
Put water and bouillon cubes in 4-quart heavy-gauge saucepan with lid.  Bring to a boil over high heat. Stir in rice. Return to a boil; add  remaining ingredients. Return mixture to a boil; reduce heat to low if  cooking over campfire, move to side. Cover and simmer 45 minutes or until  liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes before  serving.
