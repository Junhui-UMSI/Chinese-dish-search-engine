Quick and Easy Fruit Cobbler
Margarine; melted, (21-oz) apple pie filling, Granulated sugar, Cinnamon, Skim milk; at room, TOPPING, Low-fat baking mix, 
Date: Sat, 9 Mar 1996 18:49:29 -0700    From: matejka@bga.com (Anita A. Matejka)    Recipe By: Comstock "Makin It Easy Recipes"  Preheat oven to 400. Prepare a 8" square pan with cooking spray. Spoon  apple pie filling into prepared pan. Then, sprinkle cinnomon over top.  Meanwhile, to prepare topping, combine baking mix, milk, sugar, and  margarine in a mixing bowl. Drop by spoonfuls on top of filling. Bake for  25 minutes or until golden brown.
