Tortilla Roll-Ups
Red pepper, chopped fine, Cream cheese, 12" flour tortillas, Dry ranch dressing mix, Green onions, chopped fine, Celery, chopped fine, Can sliced black olives, 
Mix cream cheese, ranch dressing and onions and spread on tortillas. Layer  with red pepper, celery and olives. Roll tortillas up tight, wrap in  plastic wrap and refrigerate for several hours or overnight. Slice and  serve.
