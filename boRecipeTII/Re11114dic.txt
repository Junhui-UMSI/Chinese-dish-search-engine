Raisin Bran Muffins
All-purpose flour, Brown sugar, Buttermilk, Baking soda, Salt, Vanilla, Sugar, 15-ox box raisin bran, Pumpkin spice, Eggs, Melted shortening or, Vegetable oil, 
Mix al ingredients thoroughly. Store in refrigerator in tightly sealed jar.  Bake as needed in lightly greased muffin pan for 20- 30 minutes at 425  degrees.    Nutritional info per serving: 88 cal; 2g pro, 15g carb, 3g fat (26%)  Exchanges: 1 bread, .5 fat    formatted 3/24/96 by Lisa Crawford
