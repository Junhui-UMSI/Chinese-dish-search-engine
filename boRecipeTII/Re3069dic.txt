Peanut Dip
Peanut butter, Sour cream, Garlic clove;minced, Lemon or lime juice, Soy sauce, Hot pepper sauce; optional, Ginger root;fresh, minced, 
Blend all ingredients till smooth. Use as dip for vegetables, pita crisps,  chicken wings or chicken fingers. Source: _Cooking with Milk Calendar  1993_, Ontario Milk Marketing Board
