Asparagus Salad
Salt, Asparagus; drained, Vinegar, Pepper, Sugar; sugar substitute to, Paprika, Lettuce leaves, Dill, Onions, Onions, Tomato juice, Chopped parsley, 
Place the asparagus in a shallow dish and set aside. Combine the remaining  ingredients (except the lettuce) in a blender and mix well. Pour the  mixture over the asparagus and marinate for 1 hour; drain. Place the  asparagus on the lettuce leaves. Garnish with strips of pimento, if desired  and remaining marinade. Yields 6 servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
