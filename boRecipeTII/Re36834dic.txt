Strawberry Preserves #1
Water, Fresh strawberries, Sugar, 
Boil 2 cups sugar and water until mixture spins a thread; add 2 cups  strawberries and bring to a full boil. Add 2 cups sugar and allow to come  to a boil again; add 2 cups strawberries and boil 15 minutes. Cool in large  container, shaking occasionally to plump berries. Put in sterile jars and  seal with paraffin. Makes 2 pints.
