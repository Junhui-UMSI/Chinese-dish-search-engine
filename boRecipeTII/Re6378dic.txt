Plaza Martini
Dry gin (preferrably Bombay, Dry vermouth, Sweet vermouth, A splash of pineapple juice, 
Shake over ice in a cocktail shaker, and strain into a chilled cocktail  glass. Garnish with a pineapple wedge Posted to FOODWINE Digest 30 Jun 97  by Jared Brown <writers@AXIONET.COM> on Jun 30, 1997
