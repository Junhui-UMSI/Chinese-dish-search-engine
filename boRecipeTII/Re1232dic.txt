Crunchy Pea Salad
Sour cream, White vinegar, milk, Sugar, Salt, Garlic powder, (10-oz.) frozen peas; thawed, (8-oz.) sliced water, Sliced green onion, 
For dressing: combine sour cream, vinegar, milk, sugar, salt and garlic  powder in a small bowl. In large bowl combine peas, water chestnuts and  onion. Add dressing, toss lightly to coat. Cover and chill about 2 hours  before serving.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Peggy Geromette" <pegger50@hotmail.com> on  Oct 14, 1998,
