Tasty Chicken Wings
Soy sauce, ****** Ingredients ******, Paprika, Honey, Chicken wings, salt and pepper, Catsup, garlic, Brown sugar, 
Serves 10-12    ****** Directions ******    Place cleaned, disjointed wings in foil-lined 9x13-inch pan. Sprinkle with  salt, pepper and paprika. Combine honey, soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic and  catsup. Pour over wings and bake uncovered turning and basting every 15  mins with sauce. Bake @400 for 1 hour.    ~_The Crowning Recipes of Kentucky_
