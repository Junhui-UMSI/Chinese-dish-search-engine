Aunt Julies Strawberry Roll
Salt, LISA CRAWLEY, Eggs;separated, Cold water, Vanilla, Sugar, Flour, 
Whip egg whites stiff and add 1/2 of the sugar. Beat egg yolks til thick  and add rest of sugar, cold water, vanilla and salt. Fold egg yolk mixture  into egg white. Slowly add flour and baking powder and pour batter into  oblong pan.  Bake at 375 for 12-15 min. Beginning at narrow end, roll up in  a tea towel and allow to cool. Spread w/ whipped cream topped w/ fresh cut  strawberries. Flute top w/ whipped cream and decorate w/ mint leaves and  fresh strawberries... EXCELLANT!
