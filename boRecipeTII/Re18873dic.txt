Andys Moms Snickerdoodles
Salt, Flour, Cream of tartar, Shortening, Baking soda, Cinnamon sugar, Sugar, Eggs, 
From: akhurshe@pearl.tufts.edu (Arifa)    Date: Fri, 17 Sep 1993 18:22:32 GMT    Mix shortening, sugar and eggs together thoroughly.  Sift together flour,  cream of tartar, soda and salt.  Stir the two mixtures together.  roll into  ball the size of walnuts.  dip in mixture of equal parts cinnamon and  sugar. place two inches apart on lightly greased baking sheet. bake until  lightly browned but still soft (8 - 10 minutes) at 400 degrees farenheit.  makes 3 dozen.
