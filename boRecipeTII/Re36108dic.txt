Beef Satay Marinade
Sesame oil, Coconut milk, Fish sauce, Peanut oil, Soy, Red pepper flakes or, Lemon, juice of, Black pepper, Brown sugar, Garlic cloves, crushed, 
Marinate thinly sliced meat (like flank) for several hours. Put on skewers  and grill over hot coals until browned. Goes well with hot mustard, peanut  sauce or nuoc cham (thats cham--not mam).
