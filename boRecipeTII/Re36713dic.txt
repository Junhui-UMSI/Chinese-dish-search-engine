Strawberry Frozen Fruit Dessert
Banana, Crushed pineapple in juice , Frozen strawberries, 
Recipe by: McDougall Program Place the crushed pineapple and juice in the  blender. Add the frozen fruit a little at a time, blending between each  addition. Scoop 1/2 cup servings into dessert dishes and freeze for 15 to  30 minutes. (If frozen for longer period of time, remove from freezer a few  minutes before serving to soften.)
