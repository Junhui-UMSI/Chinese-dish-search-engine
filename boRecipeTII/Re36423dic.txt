Beer Broiled Shrimp
Vegetable oil, Parsley, Beer, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic, Large shrimp, in shells, 
Combine beer, oil, parsley, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt and pepper.  Add shrimp, stir and cover. Marinate for 60 minutes. Drain, reserving  marinade. Place shrimp on well-greased broiler rack; broil for 4 minutes,  4-5 inches from flame. Turn and brush; broil 2-4 minutes more or until  bright pink.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
