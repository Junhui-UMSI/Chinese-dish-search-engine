Quick Corn Chowder (Vitasoy)
Water, Bouillon powder, Dried parsley flakes, Potatoes; cut into 1/2", Salt, Pepper, Canned cream-style corn, Soymilk, Onion, 
In a medium saucepan combine the potatoes (well-scrubbed, but unpeeled if  you prefer), onion, water, bouillon, parsley, salt and pepper. Bring to a  boil, reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender (15-20 minutes).  Remove pan from heat and stir in the corn. Put two cups of the mixture into  a blender container and briefly pur e. (Do not overblend or potatoes will  become gummy.) Return the pur ed mixture to the pot. Stir the soymilk into  the pot and heat through. Taste to adjust seasonings and serve.    (Yield: 5 servings. Serving size: 1 cup.
