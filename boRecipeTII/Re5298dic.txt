Pinata Pumpkin Chili
Red pepper flakes -=OR=-, Taco seasoning, Salt, Hot pepper sauce, Ground beef, Cheddar cheese; shredded, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Onion, Water, Chili powder, Corn chips; crushed, Garlic, Solid pack pumpkin, Sour cream, 
In 3 quart saucepan, cook meat, onion and garlic until meat is brown.  Drain. Add pumpkin, water, taco seasoning, chili powder and salt. Bring to  a boil. Cover; simmer 30 minutes. Season with red pepper flakes or hot  pepper sauce. Garnish with cheese, corn chips and sour cream.    NOTE: May add 16 oz red kidney beans; drained.
