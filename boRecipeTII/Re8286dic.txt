Potato Pancakes with Juniper
salt and pepper, Potatoes, Butter or oil, Orange (grated zest only), Juniper berries (or more), 
Peel and grate the potatoes. Season with salt, pepper, the finely grated  orange zest and the pulverized juniper berries. Divide into six flattened  heaps and fry in batches in a mixture of butter and oil until golden-brown  and crisp on both sides. Drain on crumpled kitchen paper and keep the  pancakes hot (uncovered) in a low oven while you cook the rest. Source:    Philippa Davenport in "Country Living" (British), November 1988.
