Pierogi Beef Filling
Beef broth, Onion -- sliced, Margarine, Ground beef, Rice, Parsley -- chopped, Hot water, 
Saute onion in margarine.  Stir in meat and rice.  Dissolve bouillon in hot  water.  Add to meat mixture with parsley and salt & pepper to taste.    Recipe By     : Posted rec.food.recipes by Dan Eisenreich, dan@clark.net    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
