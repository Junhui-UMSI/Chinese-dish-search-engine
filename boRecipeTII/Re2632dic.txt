Peanut Brittle Triangles
Baking soda, Or 2 sticks butter or, Peanut butter, Brown sugar firmly packed, Eggs, (8 oz) of Peanut Brittle, All purpose flour, Granulated sugar, Vanilla, 
Bake time 20 mins in 375 degree oven.    Sift together first three ingredients onto waxed paper. Crush the peanut  brittle. Beat butter and peanut butter until well blended in a lg bowl with  electric mixer at high speed. Beat in the sugars,and vanilla. Add dry  ingredients slowly and 1 1/4 cup peanut brittle crushed. Spread dough into  a greased 15 x 10 x1 inch jelly roll pan. Sprinkle remaining peanut brittle  on top. Bake 375 for 20 mins. Cool in pan on wire rack. Cut into 2 inch  bars or triangles. Decorate with a vanilla frosting if desired and whole  peanuts.    **Jelly roll pan for those who are wondering is simply a cookie sheet with  sides.
