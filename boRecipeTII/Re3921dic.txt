Pepper Steak
Stew beef, Green pepper, medium cut, Onion soup mix, olive oil, Rice, Cornstarch, Water, 
In a large skillet, heat oil then add meat which has been cut into thin  slices. Cook till meat is brown, then add onion soup mix and water. Cover  and simmer about 30 minutes. Add strips of peppers and cover; Simmer about  10 more minutes or till meat is tender. Blend cornstarch with a little  water and stir into mixture in skillet. Cook while stirring till thick.  Serve with rice.
