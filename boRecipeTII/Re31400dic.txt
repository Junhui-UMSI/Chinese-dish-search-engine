Barbaras Favorite Chicken Recipe
Margarine or butter, Mushrooms; if desired, Boneless chicken breasts, Salt, Cream of mushroom soup, Garlic, Pepper, Container sour cream cooked, 
Combine all ingredients together, except chicken breasts and rice. In  casserole, layer chicken breasts and cover with sauce. Bake, covered, at  350 degrees (325 degrees for glass) for 60 minutes, or until chicken is  cooked. Serve over rice. Serves 4.
