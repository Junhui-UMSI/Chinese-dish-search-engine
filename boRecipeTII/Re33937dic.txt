Sweet Baked Yams
Pumpkin pie spice, Salt and pepper to taste, Yams, peeled and cubed, Butter or margarine, Southern Pecan Pieces, 
Boil the yams in water until soft. Drain well and mash with potato masher  in medium-size glass or pyrex casserole dish. Mix the yams with molasses,  buuter or margarine, pumpkin pie spices, and salt/pepper to taste. Top  evenly with pecan pieces. Bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes or until lightly  browned.
