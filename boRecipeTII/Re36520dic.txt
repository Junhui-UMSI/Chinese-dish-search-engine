Strawberries Into Salsa Into Avocadoes (Mf)
Olive oil, (up to 2), Salt, Balsamic or raspberry, Strawberries, rinsed, hulled, Ripe haas avocadoes, Washed boston or bibb, Honey, Finely minced red onion, Minced fresh jalapeno, Garlic, Chopped roasted cashews or, 
In a bowl whisk together vinegar, olive oil, honey, garlic, onion and  jalapeno. Season to taste with salt and pepper and combine the strawberries  and adjust seasoning again.    Peel, halve and pit the avocadoes. Spoon some strawberry salsa in avocado  hollow and serve on lettuce; garnish with peanuts.
