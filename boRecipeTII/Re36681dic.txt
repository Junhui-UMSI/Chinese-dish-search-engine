Strawberry Daiquiri
Orange slice, Strawberry, Strawberry liqueur, Cracked ice, Sugar, Syrup, Frozen strawberries -- in, Rum, Fresh lime juice, 
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add rum, strawberry liqueur, lime  juice, frozen strawberries, and sugar. Shake and strain into a chilled  cocktail glass. Garnish with strawberry and orange slice.    Recipe By     : Joe Robertson    From:                                 Date:
