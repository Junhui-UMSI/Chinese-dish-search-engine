Three Minute Microwave Fudge
margarine, milk, Vanilla, Pecans, Cocoa, Powdered sugar, 
(very rich, but fun for the kids to make)    Combine sugar and cocoa in microwave-safe bowl. Place stick of margarine on  top of sugar/cocoa mixture. Pour milk over all. DO NOT STIR. Microwave at  high for 3 minutes Stir well and add vanilla and nuts. Pour into greased 8  x 8 pan Cool 20 minutes before slicing Posted to EAT-L Digest 24 Mar 97 by  "P. J.
