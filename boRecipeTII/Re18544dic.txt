Ambrosia
Maraschino cherries; halved,, Pecan; chopped, Fresh coconut; chopped, Fresh pineapple; chopped, Powdered sugar to tast, Halved orange sections;, 
Combine all ingredients in large bowl. Cover and refrigerate. Serves 12.    SOURCE: Vicksburg Junior Auxiliarys new cookbook, Ambrosia    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Shawn Zehnder Lea <slea@HIGHTOWERSERVICES.COM>  on Dec 22, 1997
