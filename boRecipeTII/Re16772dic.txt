Alicias Flour Tortilla
Salt, Lard, Flour, Warm milk, Baking Powder, 
Mix all dry ingredients and sift. Mix in lard. Add milk gradually. Knead  til smooth. Roll in pieces. roll out flat very thin. Cook on griddle until  lightly spotted.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #241 by Walt Gray <waltgray@mnsinc.com> on  Sep 10, 1997
