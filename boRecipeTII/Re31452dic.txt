Barbecue Sauce
Lemon juice, Salad oil, Sugar, Pepper, Onions, Salt, Water, Tomato Catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, Prepared mustard, 
Cook onion until soft in oil.  Add remaining ingredients.  Simmer 15  minutes.  Good for steak, chicken or other barbecue favorites.    From "Trail Bosss Cowboy Cookbook" by Society of Range Management.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/west.zip
