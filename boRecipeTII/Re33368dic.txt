Basic Western North Carolina Barbecue Sauce
White wine vinegar, Dry mustard, Brown sugar, Water, Stick of butter, Onion, Cinnamon, Ketchup, Salt and pepper, Cloves, Garlic, Chili powder, 
Melt butter in a saucepan; add the chopped onion, and minced garlic, until  they just begin to brown. Add white wine vinegar, cinnamon, whole cloves,  dry mustard, chili powder, brown sugar and ketchup. Stir, then add water  and blend. Bring to a boil, lower heat to simmer, add salt and pepper to  taste, and simmer for about 20 to 30 minutes.
