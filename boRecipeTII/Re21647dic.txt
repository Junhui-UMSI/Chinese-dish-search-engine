Apple Spice Bread
Nutmeg, Stick butter, room temp, Salt, milk, Lemon juice, Vanilla, Eggs, All purp flour, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Dark brown sugar, Apples, peeled, chpd, 
~Granny Smith    Preheat oven 350. Lightly butter 2 8x4" baking pans. Combine milk and lemon  juice in sm bowl. Let stand 5 mins. Cream butter with sugar in med bowl.  Add eggs and vanilla and beat until smooth. Add milk mixture. Combine dry  ingreds in another med bowl and blend into butter mixture. Fold in chopped  apples. Spoon batter into prepared pans. Bake until tester comes out clean,  about 45 mins. Cool in pans 10 lmins. Transfer to rack.    Posted by:  Sherilyn Schamber (JHXX93B) - Prodigy Reposted by: Debbie  Carlson - Cooking Echo
