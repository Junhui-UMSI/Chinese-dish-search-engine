Tomatoes with Okra and Peppers
Salt, Paprika, Breadcrumbs; buttered, Green pepper; finely chopped, Onion; minced, Breadcrumbs; dry, fine, Butter (or marg.); melted, Pepper, Okra pods, Tomatoes, 
Wash okra and slice.  Parboil 5 minutes; drain.  Mix with green pepper, dry  breadcrumbs, butter, and onion. Season to taste with salt, pepepr, and  paprika.    Core tomatoes, and remove tops.  Fill tomatoes with okra mixture. Place in  a greased baking dish; sprinkle with buttered breadcrumbs, and bake at 375  degrees for 20 minutes.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974.
