Kids Fruit Salad
fruit cocktail, Miniature marshmallows, Bananas, Apple, Lemon juice, Maraschino cherries, Drained, Cool whip, 
Stir sliced apples and bananas in lemon juice to keep from turning dark.    In large bowl, combine all ingredients except coolwhip.  Gently fold in  coolwhip.  Cover; chill until served.    Kids dig into this - think it is the coolwhip they are interested in.    ++ Courtesy of Dale & Gail Shipp, Columbia Md. ++    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Oct 26, 98
