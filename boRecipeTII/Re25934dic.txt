Bacon and Potato Pie
Pepper, Russet potatoes, peeled,, Bacon, Onion, chpd, Eggs, Sharp cheddar, grated, BON APPETIT TBTC 1990, 
Preheat oven 350 F. Grease 9x13" glass baking dish. Microwave bacon,  remove. Add onion to bacon fat, microwave; remove. Combine eggs, potatoes,  cheese, and pepper. Crumble bacon and mix into egg mixture. Add onions.  Pour into prepared baking dish. Bake until center is set, 45 mins. Cut into  squares.
