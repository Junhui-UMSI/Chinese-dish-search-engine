Baja California Chicken
Chicken breasts, Tarragon Vinegar, Garlic, Dry sherry, Seasoned salt and pepper, olive oil, 
Here is an easy chicken recipe that is pretty tasty! Enjoy. Joe    Preheat oven to 350 degrees    Put the oil and vinegar into a skillet and press garlic into the mixture.  While the pan is heating up, sprinkle the seasoned salt and pepper onto the  chicken.    Saute the chicken breasts until they are "golden brown." Place chicken  into a baking pan and pour sherry over chicken. Place pan in the oven for       10    minutes.    Serve and enjoy! Copied from "The Cowboy Cookbook."
