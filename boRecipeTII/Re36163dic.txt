Beef Stew with Ale
Vegetable oil, Garlic, crushed, Onions, chopped, Grated nutmeg, Ale, Chuck steak, Bay leaf, Flour, Sprigs parsley, Thyme, Prepared mustard, Peppercorns, Salt, Sugar, 
1>. Preheat oven to 350 deg.F. Trim beef and brown in  a skilet in hot oil. 2>. Transfer beef to a casserole  and brown onions in oil. Add garlic, mustard, and  flour. Stir in ale and add the bouquet garni  ingredients, tying them in a small piece of  cheesecloth. Add salt, sugar, and nutmeg. 3>. Pour  sauce over browned beef. Cover casserole and place in  the oven for 2 hours.
