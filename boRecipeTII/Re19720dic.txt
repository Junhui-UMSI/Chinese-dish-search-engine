Antipasto Skewers
Prosciutto, Italian fontina cheese, pepper, Olive oil, Seasonal melon, Black olives, 
1. Wrap each cube of melon in a piece of prosciutto.    2. On each of 36 skewers, thread a cube of cheese, an olive, and a  prosciutto-melon cube. Place skewers in a tightly sealed container.    3. At serving time, drizzle with olive oil and dust with pepper.    Makes 3 dozen skewers.
