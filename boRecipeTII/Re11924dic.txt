Reames Cheesy Broccoli Tuna Bake
Evaporated milk, broccoli, frozen, mild cheddar cheese, Instant minced onion, Mushroom pieces, Tuna, Cheddar cheese soup, REAMES Home Style Frozen noodles, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Cook noodles in boiling water 20 minutes. Blend  soup and milk into smooth sauce. Add onions and mushrooms. Drain  noodles. Layer 1 1/2 cups noodles, 1/3 cup tuna and 1 cup broccoli in  a 2-quart casserole. Pour in 1 cup of sauce. Repeat layers. Bake 20  minutes. Then add cheese and bake 15 more minutes. Makes 6 1-cup  servings.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
