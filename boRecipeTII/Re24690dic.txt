Athens Pecan Cake (Greek Menu 1)
Baking Powder, Rind of 1 orange, Butter, Eggs, Pecans, Sugar, Brandy, Flour, 
Cream butter and sugar. Fold in beaten eggs and blend well. Add orange  rind, nuts and brandy and mix well. Add flour and baking powder, blending  thoroughly. Pour into a greased 9x13" baking pan. Bake in a 350 F. oven for  40 minutes, or until done. Makes 24 servings. MC formatting by  bobbi744@sojourn.com    NOTES : This recipe is a big hit at the Taverna Anna Restaurant in North  Filotheh, a suburb of Athens. If you like pecans, youll enjoy this cake.  Bobbies notes: I found this a bit dry. I served it with peach butter. Try  cutting cooking time by 10 minutes. Test for doneness.  Recipe by: Opaa! Greek Cooking, Detroit Style by George J.
