Picnic Loaf Dinner Sandwich
Marinated artichoke hearts, Olivada to taste, Large round Italian bread, Peeled slices of seeded, Assortment of cured meats, Slices of cheese like, Red wine vinegar or, Roasted sliced red peppers, Salt and pepper, 
Remove a little "cap" from top of bread and remove inside crumb from inside  and sides. Spread olivada inside walls of bread; sprinkle with drops of  vinegar and season each side with salt and pepper.    Layer assorted cold cuts and cheeses inside, dotting layers with more  olivada and slices of red peppers, cucumbers, and artichoke hearts.    ONE MORE USE FOR OLIVADA    Bring canned vegetable soup to a boil. Stir in olivada and freshly grated  Parmesan cheese to change the nature of the soup.
