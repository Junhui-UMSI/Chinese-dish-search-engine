Sweet and Hot Peach Relish
Red bell pepper -- cut into, Lime), Salt and pepper -- to taste, Slice, Orange juice, Lime juice -- (about 1/2, Thin strips, Choice -- minced, Ripe peaches -- pit & thinly, Molasses, Red or green chile of your, Fresh parsley -- chopped, 
This relish is fairly hot, but it also has a mellow sweetness that  contrasts nicely with the spicy, crunchy exterior of the chickent breasts.    In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well. This relish will  keep, covered and refrigerated, for about 4 days.
