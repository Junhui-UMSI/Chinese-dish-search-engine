White Chocolate Fudge
Powdered sugar, Vanilla, dried apricots, macadamia nuts, Cream Cheese, White chocolate, 
Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla in large mixing bowl at medium speed  with electric mixer until well blended. Gradually add chocolate; mix well.  Stir in apricots and nuts. Spread into greased 8-inch square baking pan.  Chill several hours. Cut into squares.    Makes 2 1/2 pounds    Prep time: 15 minutes plus chilling    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 279 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 20, 1997
