Peanut Butter Color Squares
Eggs, Candy-coated chocolate, Peanut butter, Margarine, Baking soda, Brown sugar, firmly packed, Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven to 350. In large bowl of electric mixer, beat eggs 2 minutes  at high speed. Beat in sugar. Blend in peanut butter, margarine and  vanilla. Add flour, baking powder and baking soda, mixing well. Stir in 3/4  cup of chocolate candies. Spread batter in greased and floured 9 inch  square baking pan. Sprinkle remaining candies on top. (Add a few more, if  you like!). Bake 25 minutes. Do not overbake. Cool in pan on wire rack. Cut  into squares after it has cooled completely.
