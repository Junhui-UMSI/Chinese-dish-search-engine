Tropical Kumquat Cake
Sugar, Salt, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Flour, Eggs, Vegetable oil, crushed pineapple , Pecans, kumquats, 
In a bowl, beat eggs, sugar, and oil. Fold in dry ingredients and mix well.  Add kumquats, nuts, and pineapple. Pour into greased and floured 10" tube  pan. Bake 1 hour and 15 minutes in a 350 degree oven. Cool, remove from  pan.  Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by ddmmom@popalex1.linknet.net on Feb 25, 1998
