Sweet n Sour Crockpot Chicken
Tapioca; quick-cooking, Catsup, Onion, Tapioca, cooked couscous, White Chicken; skinless, Red wine vinegar, Chicken bouillon granules, Pineapple juice; frozen, Lemon juice, Cinnamon, Brown Sugar, Soy sauce, Green Pepper, Carrots, 
For sauce, in a small bowl combine juice concentrate, catsup, and lemon  juice. Pour about half of the sauce into a crockpot. Add tapioca and  cinnamon to the cooker; stir.    Cover; cook on low-heat setting 10-12 hours or high-heat setting 4 1/2-5  1/2 hours. Discard spice bag. Transfer chicken to a serving platter. Skim  fat from sauce. Serve sauce and hot couscous with chicken.
