Apple Braid
Refrigerated crescent roll, Flour, Melted, Nutmeg, Glaze:, Powdered sugar, Dough, Sugar, milk, Cinnamon, Cloves, Butter or margarine --, Apples, 
Preheat oven to 375.  Unroll crescent roll dough and place in bottom of  9"x13" Baker.  Lightly sprinkle with flour.  Roll dough with Dough and  Pizza Roller until perforations are sealed and dough is flat.  Peel, core  and slice apples with Apple Peeler/Co rer/Slicer. Cut in quarters. Combine  sugar and spices in Batter Bowl. Add apples and toss lightly. Place apple  mixture down center of dough, approximately 2-inches wide. Cut 10 slits,  about 3/4-inch wide on each long side of dough surrounding apple mixture.  To braid, fold strips from one side ot the other.  Fold ends over to seal.  Melt butter in Microwave Saucepan just until it liquifies; do not boil.  Brush with melted butter.  Bake 30 minutes.  Cut and serve warm. Combine  powdered sugar and mi lk for glaze. Stir until smooth. Pour over warm  braid.
