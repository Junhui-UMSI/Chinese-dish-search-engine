Bacon Ranch Potato Salad
Mayonnaise, Red bell pepper, Celery, Salt, Bacon, Ranch dressing, Garlic powder, New potatoes, Pepper, Green onions, 
Cut potatoes into 1/4-inch slices; boil in water until tender (about 10  minutes); drain well. Mix mayonnaise, ranch dressing and seasonings in  large bowl until well blended. Add potatoes and remaining ingredients; mix  well. Serve immediately or refrigerate.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #643 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Jun 11, 1997
