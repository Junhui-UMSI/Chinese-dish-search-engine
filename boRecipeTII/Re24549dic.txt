Asparagus Soup #1
Onion, Cream, Pepper, asparagus, Salt, Butter or margarine, All-purpose flour, milk, 
Heat butter or margarine; add onion and simmer for about 4 minutes, or  until soft. Stir in flour and seasonings. Remove from heat and slowly add  milk, stirring until well blended. Return to low heat and cook until thick  and smooth, stirring constantly. Put asparagus through food mill, then add  to soup mixture. Reheat before serving, adding cream if desired. Yield: 8  servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
