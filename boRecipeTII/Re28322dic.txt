Baked Halibut Supreme
Halibut, Salt, Sour cream, White wine, Mayonnaise, Onions, bread, Paprika, 
Mix wine and salt, marinate fish for 1 hour or all day is best. Drain fish:  dry with paper towels. Dip both sides of fish into bread crumbs; place in  buttered baking dish. Mix mayonnaise, sour cream and onions and spread over  halibut. Sprinkle with remaining crumbs and paprika. Bake at 500 degrees  for 20 minutes or until fish flakes with fork.
