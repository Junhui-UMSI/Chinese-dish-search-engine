Vinha Dalhos
Bay leaf, Hawaiian red peppers; seeded, Salt, Sage, Vinegar, Thyme, Garlic, Boneless pork, Cloves, Salad oil, 
From: MeLizaJane <MeLizaJane@aol.com>    Cut pork into 2 X 1 1/2-inch pieces. Combine vinegar, garlic, red pepper,  bay leaf, salt, cloves, thyme and sage; pour over pork and let stand  overnight in refrigerator. Cook pork in marinate for 20 minutes; drain.  Heat oil in skillet; add pork and saute slowly for 10 to 15 minutes until  browned.    Makes 6 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com on Mar 10, 1998
