Swingers
Tomatoes, coarsely chopped, Garlic, Green Onions, Salt, Ground round, Mushrooms, sliced, Onion, coarsely chopped, Green pepper, coars chopped, Wheat germ, Cheddar cheese, cubed, 
Mix all ingredients.  Shape into patties.  Refrigerate 2 to 3 hours. Press  patties together to prevent crumbling. Cook 10 minutes on each side.
