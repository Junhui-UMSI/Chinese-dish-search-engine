Baked Chicken and Rice
Chicken, Cream of mushroom soup, Rice, Chicken broth, Dry Lipton Onion Soup Mix, 
Pour uncooked rice into a 10 inch Dutch Oven. Place cut-up chicken parts on  top of the rice. Sprinkle Lipton Onion Soup mix on top of chicken. Dilute  chicken broth with one can of water. Pour mixture over the chicken and bake  with coals on top and bottom for at least one hour.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
