Basic Fishball Soup
Fishballs, Vegetable stock, Salt, Scallion, 
1. Prepare fishballs according to recipe "BASIC FISHBALLS". Mince  scallion.       2. Bring stock to a boil. Add salt. Lower fishballs in one at a time so  they wont splash. Simmer, uncovered, 3 minutes. Add minced scallion.  Simmer 1 minute more.       NOTE: Shrimpball soup is prepared the same way.       VARIATIONS: Before step 2, add to cold stock 4 dried black mushrooms  (soaked), 1 or 2 Cakes bean curd and 1/2 cup bamboo shoots, all slivered;  bring to a boil and simmer 1 minute before adding salt and fishballs.       In step 2, add to boiling stock 1 Cup Chinese cabbage stems and 1/2 cup  celery, both slivered. Simmer, uncovered, 5 minutes before adding salt and  fishballs.       Add, with fishballs, 1/4 pound peastarch noodles (soaked).       Add, with scallion, 1/2 cup spinach or water cress, chopped.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4.
