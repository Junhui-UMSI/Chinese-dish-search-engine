Tantalizing Tempeh Dinner
Broccoli flowerets; in bite-, Onion; minced, Fresh chives; chopped, Salt, Soybean sprouts, Dulse, dry, Tempeh; cut into 1/2-inch, Soy oil, Chickpeas; cooked, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease a 4 x8 baking pan with soy  oil. Soak and rinse dulse 2 or 3 times in cold water. Combine with the  remaining ingredients and mix well. Transfer to baking pan and bake for 20  minutes.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
