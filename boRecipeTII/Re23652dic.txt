Artichoke Dip
Minced garlic to taste, Cans green chilies, chopped, Mayonaise (to 1 1/2 cups), Romano cheese, grated, Tabasco to taste, Jars marinated artichockes, Parmesean cheese, grated, 
Mix artichokes, parmesean, romano, chilies and mayonaise.  Add garlic and  tabasco to taste.  Bake at 350F for about 30 minutes, until brown around  edges and bubbly.  Serve with crackers and toasted bread.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #281 by Peg Doolin <peggyvt@ibm.net> on Oct  25, 1997
