Stuffed Eggplant #3
Sliced Mozzarella, Eggplant, Ricotta Cheese Mixed With 1, Marinara Sauce, Grated Cheese, Flour For Dredging, Eggs, olive oil, Parsley; finely chopped, 
From: beck4@nyc.pipeline.com (Eileen & Bob Holze)    Date: Mon, 1 Jul 1996 22:22:39 GMT  Recipe By: Little Mom    Wash eggplant, remove stem, peel and slice lengthwise.  Dredge eggplant  slices in flour.  Dip in egg and fry until golden brown.  Pat dry with  paper towels. Place mozzarella in middle of eggplant. Add scoop of ricotta  cheese. Roll and place seam side down in greased pan and top with marinara  sauce. Bake in 350F for 15-30 minutes.    MC-Recipe Digest V1 #136    From the MasterCook recipe list.
