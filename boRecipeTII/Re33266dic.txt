Basic Refrigerator Cookies
Flour, Eggs, Sugar, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Butter, Salt, Baking soda, 
From: tamale@aloha.net    Date: Tue, 06 Aug 1996 23:58:26 -1000  Source: LA Times    Sift together flour, salt, baking powder and soda. In bowl cream together  butter and sugar until light and fluffy.    Beat in eggs individually. Add vanilla. Gradually add dry ingredients to  creamed mixture, mixing well after each addition.    Shape dough into 1 1/2- to 2-inch rolls. Wrap rolls in foil. Chill at least  3 hours or overnight in refrigerator.    Cut into slices 1/8- to 1/4-inch thick. Bake on ungreased baking sheets at  375 degrees 10 to 12 minutes. Makes about 3 dozen.    Each cookie contains about: 77 calories; 83 mg sodium; 19 mg cholesterol; 4  grams fat; 9 grams carbohydrates; 1 gram protein; 0.02 gram fiber.
