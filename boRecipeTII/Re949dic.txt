Icebox Apple Pie
Prepared pie shell, Sliced peeled apples, Water, Butter or margarine, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Peach flavored gelatin, Cook & serve vanilla pudding, TOPPING, Graham cracker crumbs, Butter or margarine, melted, Sugar, Brown sugar, 
In a large saucepan, combine apples, water, butter, cinnamon and  nutmeg. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, simmer uncovered for 5 minutes or  until apples are tender. Stir in gelatin and pudding, bring to a boil.  Remove from heat, let stand 5-6 minutes. Pour into shell. Add topping over  filling. Chill 3-4 hours. Makes 6-8 servings.    Typed by R.
