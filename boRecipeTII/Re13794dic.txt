Wild Roasted Chicken Wraps
Frozen chicken tenderloins;, Water, Flour tortillas; (10-inch),, Ranch salad dressing, Lettuce; shredded, 
Spray large skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Add chicken, cook over  medium high heat 10 to 12 minutes, or until light brown. Add water, rice  and contents of seasoning packet. Bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat;  simmer 5 minutes, or until chicken juices run clear and liquid is absorbed.  Stir in salad dressing.    Fill each tortilla with chicken and rice mixture; top with lettuce. Place  filling across the center of each tortilla. Fold in both sides of the  tortilla. Holding in the folded sides, roll the tortilla tightly from  bottom to top, keeping the ingredients firmly packed as you go. Slice each  wrap diagonally and serve.
