Anise Pine-Nut Drops (#)
Basic Holiday Cookies; *with, 
* Prepare Basic Holiday Cookie dough, with the following changes:  Substitute 3/4 tsp. anise flavoring for vanila. Wrap in plastic and  refrigerate until well chilled. For each cookie, roll 1 round teaspoon  dough in pine nuts; youll need about 1/2 cup nuts total. Place on  ungreased baking sheets. Bake in 350 F. oven for 10-12 minutes. Cool on  wire racks. Makes 24 cookies. MC formatting by bobbi744@sojourn.com    NOTES : (#) All cookies made with the Basic Holiday Cookies mix are marked  in this way for ease in finding in cookbook.
