1850 Blackberry Pie
milk, Pie Shell, Flour, Granulated sugar, Blackberries, 
Fill shell with berries. Mix flour, sugar, and milk. Pour mixture over  berries. Bake at 350? for 45 to 50 minutes until center is set. If desired,  brown under broiler.    Serves 6 to 8    Source: "Mountain Measures" -- Junior League of Charleston, WV ed. 1974    billspa@icanect.net    Recipe by: Mrs. Charles G.
