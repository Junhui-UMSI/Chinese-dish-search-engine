Baked Apples
Orange juice, Granulated white sugar, Lemon juice, Brown sugar; optional, Water; plus, Salt, Cinnamon, Lemon zest, Granny Smith apples or Rome, SYRUP  BOIL TOGETHER, Water, Brown sugar, 
Heat oven to 350F. Mix together syrup ingredients and bring to a boil. Peel  the apples, removing only about the top two inches. Core the apples and  place them upright in a baking dish. Pour the syrup over and into the  apples. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. A fork will glide through  the apple when done. Sprinkle tops with a little extra brown sugar and  broil for 3 minutes. Serve warm with ice cream, whipped cream, creme  fraiche or plain cream.    Recipe by Mangia!
