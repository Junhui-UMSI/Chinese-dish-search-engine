Sweet and Sour Red Cabbage
Vegetable oil, Minced garlic cloves, Ground ginger, Cider vinegar, Tamari soy sauce, Red cabbage core and shred, Honey, Pepper, 
In heavy stainless steel or enamelled skillet saute cabbage and garlic 3 to  4 minutes, stirring to coat well with oil. Add remaining ingredients.  Combine well. Bake in preheated 350 degrees oven 40 to 60 minutes or until  tender.
