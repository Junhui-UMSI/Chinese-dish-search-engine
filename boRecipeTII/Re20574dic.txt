Apple Cider Salad
Raisins, Apple, unpeeled and sliced, Apple cider, Lettuce, Celery, chopped, Apples, coarsely chopped, Package orange flavored, LORELI AGUDA WSKD49A, Juice and grated rind of one, 
Dissolve gelatin in 2 cups hot cider; stir in raisins. Let cool. Add  remaining 2 cups cider; chill until consistency of unbeaten egg whites.  Stir in chopped apples, celery, lemon juice and rind. Pour into a lightly  oiled 6 cup mold. Chill until set. Unmold onto lettuce leaves and garnish  with apple slices, if desired.
