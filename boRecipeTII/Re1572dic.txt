Butternut Squash Bisque with Apple
Butternut squash; unpeeled,, Green apples; Granny Smiths, Onion; coarsely chopped, Marjoram, Rosemary, Chicken Stock, White bread trimmed of crust, salt and pepper, Heavy cream or plain yogurt;, Freshly grated nutmeg to, 
http://www.foodstop.com    Combine all ingredients except optional cream and bring to a boil, then  allow to simmer uncovered for 45 min, or until squash is tender. Season.    Scoop the flesh out of the squash and discard the skin. Return the squash  pulp to the soup and puree until smooth. Keep hot over low heat until  serving.    Serve soup in bowls and pour 2 Tb cream on top or substitute 1 Tb plain  yogurt. Sprinkle with fresh grated nutmeg.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Nov 24, 98,
