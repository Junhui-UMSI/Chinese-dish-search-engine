Tomato Vidalia Onion and Chickpea Salad
tomato, Dijon mustard, WHOLESOME HARVEST, GELLER, Salt, Vidalia, Red wine vinegar, chickpeas  , fresh basil, Pepper, Onion, 
In a large bowl, toss the tomato, chickpeas, onion, and basil.    In a small bowl, stir together the vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper. Pour  over the salad and toss.  Serve immediately or refrigerate several hours to  chill and blend flavors before serving.
