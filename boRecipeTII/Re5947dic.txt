Pistachio Nut Tea Bread
Eggs, Grated peel (no white, Fresh or reconstituted, Orange juice, Sugar, Whole pistachios, Self-rising flour, Butter, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9 x 5 x 3-inch loaf  pan. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter and sugar until well blended.  Beat in the eggs, orange juice, and peel until light. Add the flour,  alternating with the buttermilk. Fold in the pistachios. Pour into the  prepared pan and bake until a skewer inserted into the center comes out  clean, about 1 hour.
