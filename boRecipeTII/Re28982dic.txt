Thai Style Basil Dipping Sauce
Rice wine vinegar, Fish sauce (Nuoe Mam), Serrano or jalapeno chilis;, Sugar, Fresh basil leaves lightly, White wine vinegar, Garlic, Light corn syrup, 
In a non-aluminum saucepan, combine the vinegars, corn syrup, fish sauce  along with four tablespoons of water. Bring to a boil and stir. Remove from  heat and let mixture cool to room temperature. In a food processor, combine  the garlic, chilis and sugar and quickly puree. Add the cooled syrup and  process in one or two bursts just to combine. Add basil and briefly process  until basil is minced. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator for  up to one week.
