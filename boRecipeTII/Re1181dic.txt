Creamy Potato Soup
Potatoes; diced, Onion; diced, Water, Vegetable oil, Minced pasley, Salt, Pepper, 
Source: Spice and Spirit    Use: 4 Quart pot    Yields: 6 servings    Place all ingredients in a 4 quart pot. Cook uncovered over moderate heat  30 to 40 minutes, or until potatoes are soft, stirring occasionally. With  wooden spoon, press some potatoes against sides and bottom of pot to break  into small pieces.    Serve hot.
