Sweet Potato and Pecan Pie
Light brown sugar, Whipped sweetened cream, Salt, Unbaked 10-inch deep-dish, PECAN TOPPING, Black Pepper, Drizzle of olive oil, Salt, Drizzle of molasses, Ground cinnamon, Heavy cream, Warm chocolate sauce, Shaker of powdered sugar, SWEET POTATO FILLING, Eggs, GARNISHES, Vanilla, Ground ginger, Corn syrup, Pecans, Eggs, Sweet potatoes, Several fresh mint sprigs, Ground nutmeg, Sugar, 
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.    Place the sweet potatoes on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.  Season the potatoes with salt and pepper and place in the oven. Roast the  sweet potatoes for 1 to 1 1/2 hours or until the potatoes are fork tender.  Remove the potatoes from the oven and cool. Remove the skin from the sweet  potatoes.    In a mixing bowl, whisk the sweet potatoes, molasses, spices, eggs, cream  and 1 teaspoon vanilla together. Pour the filling into the unbaked pastry  shell. Spread the pecan pieces evenly over the sweet potato filling. In a  mixing bowl, whisk the remaining ingredients together. Pour the topping  over the pecans. Bake for about 1 hour or until the filling and topping  set. Cool for 10 minutes before slicing.    Garnish with whipped cream, chocolate sauce, fresh mint sprigs, and  powdered sugar.    This recipe yields 8 slices.
