Sweet Potatoes with Tangarine Sauce (r T)
Water, Salt, Cornstarch, Sweet Potatoes; *Note, Water, Frozen orange juice, Ginger, Brown sugar, 
*NOTE:  Or Yams, (1 1/2 Lb), peeled, cut into 1/2" cubes (4 C)    In large saucepan, combine 1 C water and salt. Bring to a boil.  Add sweet  potatoes; return to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer 8 - 11 min or  until tender, stirring gently once during cooking.    Meanwhile, in small saucepan, combine cornstarch, brown sugar and ginger;  stir until well mixed.  Stir in 3/4 C water and frozen juice  concentrate...(I didnt have any tangerine so I used the orange juice).  Cook over medium heat for 7 - 9 minutes or until sauce is thick and clear,  stirring occasionally.    Drain potatoes; return to saucepan.  Pour sauce over potatoes; mix gently  to coat.    Makes 6 (2/3 C) servings.    Nutrition according to magazine (using tangarine concentrate): Cal 90, Fat  0g, Carb 21g, Fib 2g, Pro 1g, Sod 190mg, Sugar 7g, CFF 0% Dietary  Exchanges: 1 Starch OR 1 Carb    Awsome is really putting it too mildly...this is truely wonderful.
