Baked Mackerel
Water, Whole mackerel fillets, Pickling spice, Cayenne pepper, White vinegar, Salt, Whole bay leaf, Whole peppercorns, Cloves, Onion, 
Place fillets in one layer in a glass or ceramic baking dish. Heat vinegar,  water, onion, herbs, and spices. Pour over fish. Allow to marinate for at  least one hour. Refrigerate if marinated longer. Bake, covered, for 45  minutes at 375 degrees .    Serving Ideas : Garnish with fresh parsley    NOTES : Works well with other strongly-flavored and/or oily fish    Recipe by: Elizabeth Powell    Posted to recipelu-digest by "Valerie Whittle" <catspaw@inetnow.net> on Feb  19, 1998
