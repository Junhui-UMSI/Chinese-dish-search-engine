Peanut Butter Meatballs
Garlic salt, Salt, Ground beef, Creamy peanut butter, milk, Onion, Egg, Pepper, 
Combine ground beef, egg, onion, garlic salt and pepper. Mix well. Shape  into 1 inch balls. Place into an 8x8-inch square pan. Set aside. In a small  bowl, add peanut butter, milk and salt. Mix well. Pour over meatballs. Bake  in 325 oven for 35 to 40 minutes.
