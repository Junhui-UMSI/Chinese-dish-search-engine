Pig Lickin Cake Recipe
Box Duncan Hines Yellow Cake, (small) can, mandarin, Crushed pineapple and its, Container r, cool whip, Butter or margarine softened, Box (small) box, instant, 
Mix the first four ingredients together and pour into greased and floured  13 x 9 baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees F oven for 30 minutes. Cool cake  completely and frost with the last three ingredients mixed together.  Refrigerate cake after frosting.
