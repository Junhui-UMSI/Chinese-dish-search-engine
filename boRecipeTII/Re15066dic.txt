Western Mac
Box macaroni and cheese mix, Medium), Can, Hamburger, Corn -- (or can use frozen), Prepared as directed, Green pepper -- chopped, Onion -- chopped(or 1, Tomato paste, 
Prepare macaroni mix according to directions.  Brown hamburger, onions, and  green peppers.  Combine macaroni, hamburger mixture, corn and tomato paste.  Simmer for 1/2 hour or so.  Salt and pepper to taste.    Note:  When you add the tomato paste, you will swear that you need more,  but just keep stirring, and pretty soon it looks OK.    from Peg Dietrich    Recipe By     :
