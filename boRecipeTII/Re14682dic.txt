"The Works" Pizza
Sliced green olives, (2 oz) shredded Mozzarella, Jar (2.5-oz) sliced, Chopped green/red bell, Onions, 
Heat oven to 425 degrees. Grease cookie sheet or pizza pan. Place frozen  pizza on greased cookie sheet; sprinkle remaining ingredients over pizza.     Bake on middle oven rack at 425 for 15-17 minutes or until cheese melts.     TIP: Do not bake this recipe directly on oven rack.
