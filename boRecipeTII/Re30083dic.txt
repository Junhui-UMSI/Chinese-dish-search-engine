Balsamic Roasted Onions
Dried thyme, Cooking spray, olive oil, Salt, Sweet onions (about 1 3/4, Dried basil, Pepper, Balsamic vinegar, 
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Peel onions, leaving roots intact; cut each  onion into 6 wedges. Place onion wedges in an 11 x 7-inch baking dish  coated with cooking spray. Combine vinegar and remaining ingredients in a  small bowl; pour vinegar mixture over onion wedges, tossing gently to coat.  Cover and bake at 450 degrees for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake an  additional 45 minutes or until tender. Yield: 6 servings (serving size 1  cup).    CALORIES 72 (33% from fat); FAT 2.6 g; PROTEIN 1.6g; CARB 11.8g; FIBER  2.5g: CHOL 0mg; IRON 0.7mg; SODIUM 102 mg; CALC 33 mg.
