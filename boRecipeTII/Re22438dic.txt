Turtlettes
Vegetable shortening, Chocolate chips, Doz  lg. pecans,, Doz  caramel candies, Doz  lg. pecans,, RECIPE, 
Microwave caramels to soften (not melt) to form turtles. Microwave 2 to 4  minutes until chips soften. Stir well. Use to dip or frost turtle candies.  Makes 2 dozen. From: Eldon Morris
