Stuffed Artichokes
Artichokes, Butter, Onion, chopped fine, Hot pepper cheese, grated, Swiss cheese, grated, Celery, chopped fine, Bread crumbs, Bell pepper, chopped fine, Eggs, well beaten, Crabnmeat, Mozzarella cheese, grated, Salt & Pepper & Red pepper, 
Wash artrichokes and cut off tips.  Boil until tender. Drain. Saute  celery, bell pepper and onions in butter. Remove from heat. Add crabmeat  and grated cheeses. Add bread crumbs and eggs. Mix well. Clean out heart of  artichokes and stuff with crab mixture. Stuff each leaf. Wrap in foil and  bake at 375 F. for 35 min.    A.F.R--Terrebonne Parish, Houma, La.
