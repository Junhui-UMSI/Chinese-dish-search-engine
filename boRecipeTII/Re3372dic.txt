Peas with Onions
PEAS FZ, BUTTER PRINT SURE, Onions, WATER; BOILING, Salt, 
1.  ADD PEAS TO BOILING SALTED WATER.    2.  BRING  TO A BOIL; COVER, COOK GENTLY 6 TO 8 MINUTES OR UNTIL TENDER.  DRAIN; SET PEAS ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 4.    3.  SAUTE ONIONS  IN BUTTER OR MARGARINE .    4.  COMBINE HOT PEAS WITH SAUTEED ONIONS.    NOTE:  IN STEP 3, 4 LB 7 OZ A.P. FRESH ONIONS WILL YIELD 4 LB FRESH CHOPPED  ONIONS.
