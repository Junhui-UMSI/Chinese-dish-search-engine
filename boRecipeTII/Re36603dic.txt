Strawberry Bread Bowl
White bread, low calorie;, Orange juice, unsweetened, Fresh strawberries; chopped, 
Line sides and bottom of 1 1/2 qt. round bowl with 12 over-lapping slices  of bread. Puree 1 c berries with orand juice and 3 packets Equal. Add  remaining berries.   Stir in remaining  Equal.  Pour into bread-lined bowl.  Top with reamaining 4 slices of bread.  Lay plate over bread and place 3-4  pound weight (such as a large can of tomatoes) on top.  Refrigerate  overnight.  To serve: remove weight, unmold onto plate and slice to serve.
