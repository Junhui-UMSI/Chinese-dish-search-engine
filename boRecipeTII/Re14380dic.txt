Whole Wheat Banana Nut Bread
Vanilla, Whole wheat flour, Sugar, Shortening; (half margarine,, Eggs, Buttermilk, Coursely chopped pecans, Mashed ripe bananas; (takes, Level teaspoon soda;, 
Mix in order given. Bake in greased loaf pans or muffin tins in slow oven  (about 300 degrees). Bake until toothpick comes out clean when inserted in  cake. (1/2 c. cracked soy may be used instead of nuts.)    Posted to recipelu-digest by Kara9718 <Kara9718@aol.com> on Mar 28, 1998
