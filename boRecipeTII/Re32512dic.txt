Sweet Dipping Sauce
Cornstarch, Soy sauce, Brown sugar, Water, Chicken broth, 
Combine ingredients. Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Cook and  stir 2 minutes more. Transfer to serving container; cover surface with  clear plastic wrap. Cool. To serve, remove plastic wrap. Makes 1 2/3 cups.    NOTES : Holiday Appetizers 1996, Better Homes and Gardens, page 97.
