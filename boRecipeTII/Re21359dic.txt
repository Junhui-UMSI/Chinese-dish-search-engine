Undercroft Dressing
Garlic powder, Buttermilk, Black pepper, (heaping) accent, (8-oz) cream cheese, Mayonnaise, Onion salt, 
Have cream cheese at room temperature. Mix well with mayonnaise. Add  buttermilk, onion salt, pepper, garlic powder and Accent. Makes about 1-1/2  quarts and keeps well in refrigerator.
