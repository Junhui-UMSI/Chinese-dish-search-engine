Sweet and Sour Green Beans #2
Strips bacon, (no. 2) tomatoes; reserve, Salt and pepper to taste, Sugar, (heaping) flour, Onions, milk, (no. 2) green beans; reserve, Buttered bread crumbs, Stick butter or margarine, 
Fry bacon until crisp & remove from grease. Add butter & flour to grease.  Blend in milk & liquid from beans & tomatoes. Cook until thick. Add beans,  tomatoes, onion, sugar, salt, pepper & crumbled bacon. Place in casserole &  cover with buttered crumbs. Bake at 350 until bubbly & light brown.    MRS.
