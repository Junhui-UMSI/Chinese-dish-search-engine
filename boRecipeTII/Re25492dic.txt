This jump-in-your-mouth loaf stars one of our all-time favorite  condiments--Tabasco sauce, which is made only one place in this country,  Avery Island, Louisiana. Accept no substitutes if you want the authentic  version of this bread. The other special ingredients are chili honey, which  is available by mail order, and pepper-flavored vodka, which is readily  available in liquor stores. You may substitute regular vodka if you wish.
