Pots De Creme Au Chocolat (Mousse)
Vanilla extract, Confectioners sugar, Heavy cream, Salt, Half and half, Semi Sweet Chocolate, Eggs, Yolks, Sugar, 
In 1 qt saucepan over medium heat, heat half and half until tiny  bubbles form around edge of saucepan (do not allow half and half to  boil). Set saucepan aside. In double boiler over hot, not boiling,  water, melt semi-sweet chocolate squares,stirring occasionally with  rubber spatula.    Remove double boiler top to work surface and, with rubber spatula,  beat egg yolks into the melted chocolate until smooth; then stir in  sugar and salt.    Gradually stir half and half into the chocolate mixture until  thoroughly blended. Replace double boiler top over simmering (not  boiling) water.    Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture coats back of spoon, about 15  minutes. Stir in 2 teaspoons vanilla extract.    Pour mixture into six 6 ounce cups or stemmed glasses. Refrigerate for  about 4 hours or until mixture has set.    In small bowl with mixer at low speed, beat cream, confectioners  sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon vanilla untill stiff; use to top pots de  creme.
