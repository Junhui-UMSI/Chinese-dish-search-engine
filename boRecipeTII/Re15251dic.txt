Whiskey Sauce
Brown Sugar, Ground cinnamon, Apple juice, Margarine, Bourbon whiskey, Water, Cornstarch, 
Combine apple juice and next 4 ingredients in a small saucepan. Cook over  medium heat, stirring frequently until sugar dissolves.    Combine the water and cornstarch; stir well, and add to apple juice  mixture. Bring to a boil, and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.  Yield: 1-1/2 cups (serving size: about 2-1/2 tablespoons).    Serving Ideas : Serve sauce warm.    Recipe by: Cooking Light, October 1994, page 95    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Marilyn Thomson <MThomson@worldnet.att.net>  on Apr 7, 1998
