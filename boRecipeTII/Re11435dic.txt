Raspberry and Walnut Vinaigrette
Walnut oil, Minced fresh tarragon, Raspberry vinegar, Sea salt to taste, Oilive oil, Honey mustard, 
In a bowl, whisk together mustard, vinegar, and salt to taste, add the oils  in a stream, whisking;whisk until it is emulsified Stir in tarragon. Makes      1/2    cup. ( Fresh fields)
