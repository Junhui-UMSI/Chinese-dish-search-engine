Baked Hush Puppies
Canola oil, Baking Powder, Egg white, Skim milk, Paprika, Yellow cornmeal, Salt, 
Source: Healthy Meals in Minutes    1.Preheat oven to 425? F. Spray a baking sheet with vegetable cooking  spray.    2.In a large bowl, combine cornmeal, baking powder, salt, and paprika. Mix  well.    3.In a small bowl, whisk together milk, egg white, and oil until smooth.    4.Add milk mixture to cornmeal mixture. Stir until batter is just combined.  Shape the batter into 1 1/2-inch rounds. Place rounds, 1 inch apart, on  prepared baking sheet.    5.Bake hush puppies until tops are firm and golden, about 10 to 15 minutes.  Serve immediately.  SERVES 6    PER SERVING: Calories 104 (26% from fat), Carbohydrates 17g, Protein 3 g,  Sodium 286 mg, Fat 3 g, Cholesterol 1mg.
