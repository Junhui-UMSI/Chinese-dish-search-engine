Tofu in Black Bean Sauce
Water, Kuzu, Dark sesame oil, , Water, Firm tofu, Soy sauce, Barley, Onions, Peanut oil, Ginger, Chinese black beans, Garlic, 
In a wok, heat oil until nearly smoking.    Add garlic and ginger, and stir-fry rapidly over high heat for 1  minute.    Add black beans, and stir for 30 seconds.  Add onions, and stir for  2-3 minutes.    Add soy sauce, sweetener, and water.  Bring to a boil.    Add kuzu.  Stir with a wooden spoon until it thickens and becomes  clear and shiny.    Stir in tofu and dark sesame oil, and simmer 2-3 minutes.  Serve on  rice with blanched Chinese greens.
