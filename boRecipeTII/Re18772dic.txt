Ancho Chili Mayonnaise
Corn oil, Salt to taste, Dijon mustard, Balsamic vinegar, Lime or to taste; juice of, Egg yolk, Ancho chilies; seeded, 
Soak chilies in hot water for about 20 minutes or until soft. Place in a  blender and puree until smooth. Add egg yolk, Dijon mustard, and balsamic  vinegar. Process until well incorporated. With blender running, slowly add  oil in a thin, steady stream. When well combined, blend in lime juice.  Season to taste. Pour mixture into a plastic ketchup bottle and set aside  until ready to use.    NOTES : I have the Planning and Pantry List for this theme. E-mail me if  you want it.
