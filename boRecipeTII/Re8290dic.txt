Potato Paprikash (Paprika Potatoes)
Salt to taste, Onions, finely chopped, Paprika, Cooking oil, Green peppers, seeded, Garlic clove, crushed, Potatoes, peeled, Caraway seeds, Water (up to 2 C), Tomatoes, peeled,, 
In a heavy-bottomed casserole, heat the oil and fry the onions and garlic.  Add the caraway seeds, paprika and water.  Add the potatoes, peppers and  tomatoes.  Simmer for thirty minutes.    NOTES:    *  Hungarian potato paprikash -- From "Cuisines of the World," Exeter  Books. A hearty dish, good as either a side dish or a vegetarian main  course. Suitable for Passover. Yield: Serves 4 as main course.    *  This makes a very good main course or side dish for Passover.    : Difficulty:  easy.  : Time:  15 minutes preparation, 30 minutes cooking.  : Precision:  no need to measure.    : Evelyn C.
