Portugese Spice Muffins
Margarine melted, Salt, Nutmeg, Buttermilk, Sugar, Baking Powder, FROM J.RS KITCHEN, Molasses, Allspice, Baking soda, Flour, Cinnamon, Ginger, Egg beaten, 
Mix dry ingredients together. Combine wet ingredients. make a well in drys  and combine liquids lightly. Spoon into muffin cups and bakee 400 15 to 20  minutes or until done.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Mar 10, 1998
