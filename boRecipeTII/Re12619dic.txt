Yogurt Fruit Dip
Ground cinnamon, Frozen orange juice, Brown sugar, Plain nonfat yogurt, 
Line a strainer with a paper coffee filter or cheesecloth; place over a  bowl. Pour yogurt in strainer; refrigerate for 8 hours. Discard liquid in  bowl. combine yogurt, brown sugar, orange juice concentrate and cinnamon;  mix well. Serve with fresh fruit.
