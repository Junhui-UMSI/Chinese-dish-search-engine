Watergate Cake
Eggs, TOPPING, Vegetable oil, White cake mix, milk, Nuts, chopped, Box (3 1/2 oz) instant, Cool Whip, Box (3 1/2 oz) instant, Club soda, Nuts (optional), Chopped, Maraschino cherries, 
Mix pistacho pudding with cake mix. Add all other cake ingredients except  nuts and beat for about 2 minutes. Add nuts. Pour into a greased 9" x 13"  pan and bake at 350 F for 30-40 minutes. TO MAKE TOPPING: Beat the instant  pistacho pudding with the milk until stiff. Fold in cool whip. Place on  cake and top cake with maraschino cherries and nuts.
