Best Chocolate Chip Cookies-
Crisco, Flour, Egg, Baking soda, Semisweet mini chocolate, Brown sugar, milk, Vanilla, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 375. Cream Crisco & sugar, milk & vanilla. Blend in egg.  Combine the dry ingredients & add to the mix. Stir in the chocolate chips.  Drop by rounded tsp about 3" apart on an UNGREASED cookie sheet. Bake at  375` for 8-10 minutes for chewy cookies. Cool on baking sheet 2 minutes.  Remove to rack & cool completely.
