Farmers Omelet
Butter, Diced, uncooked potatoes, Onion, finely chopped, Diced ham, Chopped fresh parsley, Eggs, Salt, Black Pepper, milk, Shredded Colby, OR Cheddar, 
Melt butter in a 10 inch nonstick fry pay.  Add potatoes and onion, cover  and cook over medium heat for 20 minutes, stirring now and then to brown  evenly OR until potatoes are tender and golden brown.  Add ham; heat  through, then sprinkle with parsley (if using)    Beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper together in a bowl.  Pour over the potato  mixture.  Cover pan and cook 10 minutes OR until eggs are almost set.  Uncover pan and check often and with a spatula, lift around edge of eggs  and allow egg mixture to run under omelet during cooking.    Sprinkle with cheese.    Cover and cook until cheese melts.    Cut into wedges and serve.
