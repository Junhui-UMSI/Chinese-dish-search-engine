Ram Tough Chili
Salt, Cayenne pepper, Tomato sauce (8 oz.), Oregano, Tabasco sauce, Ground cumin, Onion, finely chopped, Cloves Fresh garlic, minced, Chili powder, Beef chuck, coarsely ground, Garlic powder OR, Vegetable oil, 
Brown meat in oil in a covered 2 quart pan. Add tomato sauce, onion and  garlic powder. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add  water if too thick. Serve with side dishes of pinto beans, chopped onions  and grated cheddar cheese. Serves 6.
