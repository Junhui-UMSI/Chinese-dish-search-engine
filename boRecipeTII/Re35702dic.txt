Stuffed Burger Bundles
Worcestershire Sauce, Cream of mushroom soup, Catsup, Ground beef, Evaporated milk, Prepared Stove Top Stuffing, 
Combine evaporated milk and meat; divide into 8 patties. Put 1/4 cup  stuffing in center of 4 patties. Top with remaining meat patties. Seal.  Place in baking dish. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over meat.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
