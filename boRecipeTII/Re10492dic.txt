Quick Fruit Cobbler
Orange or Lemon Peel; grated, Butter or margarine, Can Apple, Blueberry, or, Sugar, milk, Biscuit Mix, Ground nutmeg, Ground cinnamon, 
In a 1 1/2 quart microwave-safe casserole combine pie filling, orange or  lemon peel and ground nutmeg; stir. Microwave 3-5 minutes, stirring every 2  minutes, until hot. Combine all remaining ingredients in a medium mixing  bowl; beat until moistened; then drop by spoonfuls onto hot fruit filling.  Microwave on HIGH (100%) 6-8 minutes or until no longer doughy. Let stand  on heat-proof counter top to cool slightly, then serve warm with ice cream.
