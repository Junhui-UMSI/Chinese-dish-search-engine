Unicorn Villages Lentil Chowder
Vegetable stock or water, Celery, diced, Carrot, diced, Salt and pepper, Onion, diced, Curry powder, Garlic croutons for garnish, Minced fresh ginger, olive oil, Garlic, Crushed tomatoes in juice, Lentils, Bay Leaves, 
Saute the carrot, onion, celery and spices in the olive oil for 3 to 5  minutes in a Dutch oven or soup pot over medium-high heat. Add the crushed  tomatoes, stock or water, and lentils and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a  simmer and cook about 1 hour, or until lentils ar softened and soup is  thick. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve with garlic croutons.  Makes 2 quarts.
