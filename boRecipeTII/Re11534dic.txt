Raspberry Filling
Evaporated Skim Milk, Lemon juice, Granulated Sugar Replacement, Cornstarch, Unsweet, 
Process 1/2 cup of the raspberries in a food processer or blender until  pureed. Combine the cornstarch and sugar replacement in a heavy or nonstick  saucepan; then gradually add the milk, pureed raspberries, and lemon juice.  Cook and stir over medium heat until clear and thickened. Remove from heat  and stir in remaining raspberries. Cover and cool to room temperature  before using.
