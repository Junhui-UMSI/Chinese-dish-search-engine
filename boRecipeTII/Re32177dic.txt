Barbecued Pork Ribs W/apricot-Chutney Glaze
Salt, Onion, Apricots; drained, Lemon juice, Garlic, Lemon rind; grated, Pork backrib; 4 - full racks, Chutney; finely chopped, Orange, Tomato puree, Honey, 
1) Several hours before cooking meat, cut orange in half. Rub juice on each  rack of ribs. Allow to marinate until cooking time.    2) Combine all other ingredients. Bring to simmer in a small saucepan.  Simmer for 2 minutes. Remove from heat. Set aside to cool.    3) Prepare barbecue coals. When ready brush ribs with sauce. Grill 35 - 45  minutes in all, Start ribs rounded-side up; turning ribs every 10 minutes.  Brush with sauce frequently.    4) When cooked through, and sides are crisp and glazed, remove from fire.  Serve one rack per person.
