"M&ms" Coconut Pecan Bars
Granulated sugar; divided, Eggs, /4      c  butter; divided, Vanilla extract, Finely chopped pecans;, Plus 3 tablespoons, Shredded coconut, 
Preheat oven to 350? F. Lightly grease a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan; set  aside. In large bowl combine 3/4 cup sugar, 1/2 cup flour and 1/2 cup  chopped pecans; add 1/4 cup melted butter and mix well. Press mixture into  bottom of prepared pan. Bake 10 minutes or until set; cool slightly. In  mixing bowl cream 1/2 cup butter and 1/2 cup sugar; add eggs, one at a  time, beating well after each addition. Blend in vanilla. Combine 1 cup  "M&Ms"r Chocolate Mini Baking Bits with 3 tablespoons flour; stir into  creamed mixture. Spread mixture over cooled crust. Combine coconut and  remaining pecans; sprinkle over batter. Sprinkle remaining 3/4 cup "M&Ms"r  Chocolate Mini Baking Bits over coconut and pecans; pat down lightly. Bake  25 to 30 minutes or until set. Cool completely before cutting into bars.  Store in tightly covered container. MAKES 24 TO 36 BARS.    Recipe by: M & M WEBSITE    Posted to recipelu-digest by GramWag@aol.com on Feb 4, 1998
