Turkey-Apple-Cheese Sandwiches
Fuji apples, Mango chutney, Vanilla low-fat yogurt, Vegetable cooking spray, muenster, smoked turkey, sandwich bread, 
Combine yogurt and chutney, and stir well. Spread 2 tablespoons yogurt  mixture over each of 4 bread slices; top each with turkey, apple, cheese,  and a bread slice.    Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; place over medium heat  until hot. Add sandwiches; cook 3 minutes on each side or until golden.  Yield: 4 servings.    Per serving: 384 Calories; 14g Fat (34% calories from fat); 21g Protein;  43g Carbohydrate; 59mg Cholesterol; 533mg Sodium    Serving Ideas :    NOTES : A substantial bakery-quality whole-grain bread is best for this  hearty sandwich thats a meal in itself.
