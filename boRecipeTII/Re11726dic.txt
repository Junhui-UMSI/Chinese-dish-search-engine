Raspberry-Pecan Dressing
Unsweetened applesauce, Chopped pecans or walnuts, Nakano natural rice vinegar, Raspberry all-fruit seedless, 
With wire whisk, blend all ingredients. Serve with smoked cubed turkey,  canned mandarin oranges and celery on a bed of shredded spinach.     Nutritional information for 2-tablespoon serving: 41 calories, <0.5 g  protein, 9 g carbohydrate, 1 g fat (0 g saturated), 0 mg cholesterol, 0 mg  sodium.
