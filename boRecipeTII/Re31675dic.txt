Swiss Steak
Flour, all-purpose, Tomatoes, Round steak, Salt, Butter, Onion, Green pepper, Pepper, 
Combine flour, salt and pepper. Sprinkle half the mixture on one side of  the round steak and pound on. Repeat for other side, cut meat into pieces.  Melt butter in skillet and brown meat. Cover tightly and simmer 1 hour. Add  water if necessary. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over meat. Cover  and simmer 30 minutes or until tender.
