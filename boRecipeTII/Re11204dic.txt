Raisin Pie 3
Chopped raisins, Butter, Pastry for double-crust pie, Lemon Rind, Hot water, Lemon juice, Brown sugar, Cornstarch, Egg, Salt, 
Bring the raisins, water, butter and lemon rind to a boil; cook for 5  minutes.    Cool a little then add the beaten egg and mix well.    While the mixture is cooling line a pie pan with pastry and preheat the  oven to 400F.    Pour the filling into the unbaked pie shell. Roll out the top crust; cover  and seal edges.    Bake at 400F for about 25 to 30 minutes.
