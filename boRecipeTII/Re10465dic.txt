Quick Dinner Rolls
Butter, Sugar, milk, Egg, Quick-rising yeast, Flour, 
Heat butter and milk to 120 degrees. Mix flour, sugar and yeast. Add egg  and heated butter and milk. Stir well. Let rise 30-45 minutes. Preheat oven  to 375 degrees. Spoon dough into buttered muffin tins. Let rise 15 minutes  more. Bake for 15 minutes.    HELENA DAILY WORLD, 01/18/1987,    "PHILLIPS COUNTY COOKS: JUDY    HOSEY SHARES DIFFERENT RECIPES"    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
