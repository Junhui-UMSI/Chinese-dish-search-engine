Peppered Pecans
Butter or margarine, Pecan halves, TABASCO pepper sauce, Salt, Garlic, 
Preheat oven to 250F.    In small skillet melt butter. Add garlic; Tabasco pepper sauce and salt;  cook 1 minute. Toss pecans with butter mixture; spread in single layer on  baking sheet. Bake 1 hour or until pecans are crisp; stir occasionally.    Makes 3 cups.
