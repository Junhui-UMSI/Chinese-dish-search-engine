Traditional Margarita
Tequila, Kosher salt for the rim of, Triple sec, Juice of one lime, 
With a piece of lime rim the glass getting a little juice on the entire  rim. Pour a little salt in a plate and move the lime rimmed glass in the  salt. Fill the glass with ice. Add tequila, triple sec, lime juice. Stir.    Yield: 1 Margarita    Recipe by: MEXICAN COCKTAIL BUFFET, DEAN FEARING Posted to MC-Recipe Digest  V1 #768 by Sue <suechef@sover.net> on Aug 31, 1997
