Pretzel Salad
strawberry gelatin, cream cheese, Margarine, whipped cream, pretzels, frozen strawberries, Sugar, Pineapple juice, 
With electric mixer, blend cream cheese and sugar, then set aside. Mix  pretzels and margarine and press into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Bake at 400  for 10 minutes; cool. Spread cream cheese mixtuer over cooled pretzel  crust. Spread whipped topping over cream cheese and chill.    Dissolve gelatin in hot pineapple juice. Add the strawberries. Allow  gelatin to thicken then pour over whipped topping and refrigerate until  firm.
