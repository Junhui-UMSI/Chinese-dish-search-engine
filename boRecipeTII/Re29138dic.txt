Baked Rice Pudding
Vanilla, Soy milk; "rice dream", Raisins; or currants, Honey, Brown rice; cooked, 
Preparation Time: 0:55 Combine all ingredients. Pour into a casserole,  cover and bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes. May be served hot or cold.
