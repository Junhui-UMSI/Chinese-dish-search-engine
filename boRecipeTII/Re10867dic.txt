Quinoa Vegetable Soup
Vegetable oil, Carrots, Cabbage, Green pepper, Parsley, Quinoa, Celery, Tomatoes, Water, Onions, Salt, garlic, 
Saute quinoaa, carrots, celery onions, green pepper and garlic in oil until  golden brown. Add water, tomatoes and cabbabe and bring to a boil.  Simmer  20 to 30 minutes or until tender.
