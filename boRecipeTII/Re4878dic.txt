Pickled Cherries
Sweet black cherries, with, Pickling salt, Water, Cider vinegar, Brown sugar, firmly packed, 
Wash cherries and set out on paper towels to dry.  In heavy saucepan or  preserving kettle, combine water, vinegar, sugar and salt. Bring to a boil,  stirring until sugar is dissolved. Pack cherries into hot, sterilized jars.  Ladle syrup over top, leaving 1/8 inch headspace. Seal immediately in 8oz  jars and process 10 minutes in boiling water bath. Makes about 8 cups...
