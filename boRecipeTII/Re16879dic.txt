All-Purpose Tomato Sauce
(28 oz) tomatoes; juice, Salt and ground black pepper, Garlic, Fresh basil leaves;, olive oil, Tomato paste, 
In a large saucepan, warm garlic in 2 tablespoons oil over low heat until  soft but not colored. Stir in tomato paste, then the tomatoes and their  liquid. Simmer, uncovered, until sauce coats the back of a spoon, about 25  minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper- before serving, stir in  basil and remaining 1 tablespoon oil.    Makes 2 cup.    This chunky, rustic, and pungent sauce goes well with dried pasta as well  as with fresh gnocchi and ravioli.    Cooks Illustrated, Mar./Apr. 1994, Page 26. Credit: Julia Della Croce.
