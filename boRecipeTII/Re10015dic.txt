Quadruple Chocolate Cookies
Semi-sweet chocolate chips, All purpose flour, Milk chocolate chips, Granulated sugar, Cocoa powder, Unsalted butter; softened, Baking soda, Salt, Eggs, Pure vanilla extract, White chocolate chips, 
Directions: Preheat oven to 350. Cream together butter and sugar. Add eggs  and vanilla and continue mixing until creamy. In separate bowl, combine  flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well and then add to creamy  mixture. Mix until thoroughly blended. Add all of the different chips and  fold into batter. Drop by teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 10  to 12 minutes.    George Geary/Disneyland Pastry Chef    Posted to recipelu-digest by P&S Gruenwald <sitm@ne.infi.net> on Feb 20,  1998
