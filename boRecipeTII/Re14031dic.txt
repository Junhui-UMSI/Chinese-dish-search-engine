White Chocolate Ice Cream
Vanilla, milk, Whipping cream, Egg, White chocolate; chopped, Sugar, 
In a saucepan, combine the cream, milk, and vanilla, cook over low heat  until it is just heated and remove from the heat.  In a double boiler, melt  the white chocolate, stirring until smooth.  In a bowl, beat the egg with  the sugar until the mixture is frothy.  Stir the cream mixture into the  white chocolate and stir the mixture into the egg mixture.  Strain through  a fine sieve into a bowl and freeze in an ice-cream maker.    Makes 1 pint.  THIS IS NOT A FIRM ICE CREAM.
