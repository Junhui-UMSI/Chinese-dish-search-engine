Basle Cheese Fritters
Paprika, Onions, Slices bread, Slices Gruyere or Emmenthal, Butter, 
Fry bread lightly on both sides in butter and arrange on a baking sheet.  Pour boiling water over finely chopped onions and leave for a moment. Pour  off water, and fry onions in the remains of the butter until tender. Spread  onions thinly on the bread and cover each slice with a slice of cheese.  Sprinkle with paprika and bake in a very hot oven (445 degrees F/Gas mark  8) until cheese melts. Serve at once.
