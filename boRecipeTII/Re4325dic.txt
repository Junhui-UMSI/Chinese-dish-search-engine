Persimmon Cake #2
Egg, Persimmon pulp, Cinnamon, Flour, Salt, Cloves, Pecans, Sugar, Margarine, Baking soda, 
Mix together margarine, sugar,and egg; beat with electric mixer until  creamy. Add flour, soda, salt, cloves and cinnamon; batter will be stiff.  Add persimmon pulp and pecans by hand. Bake in a greased, floured 13x9 inch  pan in a 350 degree oven for 35 to 40 minutes.     Note: Persimmon pulp may be frozen in measured amounts of 1 cup. Heat pulp  until completely thawed before adding to batter.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
