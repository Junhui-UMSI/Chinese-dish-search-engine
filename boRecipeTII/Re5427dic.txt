Pineapple Chicken - for the Holidays
Mushrooms, Soya sauce., green pepper, red pepper, Salt, pineabble chunks  , Paprika, Chicken peices, Pepper, consomme  , 
Place chicken, skin side down on pan. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and lots  of paprika. Add consomme.    Cover and bake for 1 hr. at 350 degrees. Turn skin side up. Add pineapple,  peppers, mushrooms, and soya sauce. Continue baking, covered until chicken  is fork tender. Uncover and bake until brown.
