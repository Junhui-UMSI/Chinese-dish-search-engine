Warm Potato Salad
olive oil, salt and pepper, New potatoes, scrubbed; cut, Chopped fresh parsley, White wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, Scallions; thinly sliced, 
In a large pot of boiling water, cook potatoes until they are tender.    In a large bowl whisk together the vinegar and the mustard. Season with  salt & pepper. Whisk in the olive oil a third at a time until it binds  together. Add the warm, drained potatoes and toss to coat. Let the potatoes  sit for 10 minutes or longer to absorb the flavor of the dressing. Add the  scallions and parsley and toss to coat. Season well with salt & pepper.
