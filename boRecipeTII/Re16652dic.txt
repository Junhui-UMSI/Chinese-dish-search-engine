Ala Badun
Lime juice, Potatoes, Onion, Ground mustard, Salt, Vegetable oil, Chilli powder, Cm cinnamon stick, Turmeric, 
Boil the potatoes, peel and cut into bite size pieces Slice the onion.  Place the potato in a bowl and and add the salt, turmeric, chilli powder  and paprika powder Heat the oil and when hot add the curry leaves followed  by the onion, mustard and cinnamon stick Fry until the onions are a golden  brown, then add the potato and keep tossing with the onion until browned  and heated through. Discard the cinnamon stick and sprinkle the lime juice  on top just prior to serving. From "A taste of Sri Lanka" by Indra  Jayasekera, ISBN #962 224 010 0
