Peppery Blueberry Dressing
Firmly packed light brown, Black Pepper, Balsamic vinegar, Fresh blueberries, 
In a medium bowl combine brown sugar, vinegar and pepper until smooth. Add  blueberries; toss to coat. Let stand at room temperature at least 20  minutes. To use: drizzle over mixed fruits, over ice cream or pound cakes.
