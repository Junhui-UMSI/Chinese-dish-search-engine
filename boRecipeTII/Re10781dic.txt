Quick-Cook Chili
Tomato soup, condensed, Chili powder; or as desired, Ground beef, Onion, Canned pinto beans, 
Heat beef and onion in a frypan until beef is browned and onion is tender.  Drain off excess fat.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Cover.  Simmer for  30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
