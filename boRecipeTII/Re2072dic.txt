Anise Cookies
Light brown sugar, Eggs, Vanilla extract, Whole-wheat pastry flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Whole anise seeds, Nonstick cooking spray, Butter; (or Margarine), 
Cost: $ ; Preparation Time: 25 minutes Difficulty Level: 3 ; Servings: 36  cookies    1. Preheat oven to 400F and lightly spray two cookie sheets with non-stick  cooking spray.    2. In a medium bowl, cream together butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla  and mix thoroughly.    3. In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, salt and anise  seeds. Mix the dry ingredients into the wet.    4. Using a teaspoon, drop dough onto prepared cookie sheets and bake for 5  to 7 minutes, or until golden brown.
