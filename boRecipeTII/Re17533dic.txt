Almond Cookies (Vanilla Sticks)
See below, 
Beat 4 egg whites stiff, add 1 lb powdered sugar. Beat 15 min [more  like 5 min in electric mixer]. Set aside some (about 1/4?] for icing;  add 1 lb. grated almonds, vanilla to flavor, to rest. Roll out, cut  in strips, glaze with icing; bake in moderate oven.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
