Wacky Cake #1
Vinegar, Vanilla, Stick margarine; melted, Sugar, Sugar, Cocoa, Stick margarine, Cocoa, Baking soda, ICING, Vanilla, Water, All purpose flour, milk, 
Mix dry ingredients together & add remaining ingredients. Pour into an  ungreased 8x11-inch pan. Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes. Icing: Combine  ingredients & bring to a boil. Add vanilla. Beat until it begins to  thicken. Pour over cake.    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
