Bbq Kielbasi
Brown sugar, Ketchup, Worcestershire Sauce, Skinless kielbasi, Dry mustard, Lemon juice, Chili sauce, 
Biol kielbasi in water for 30 minutes to remove grease. Combine remaining  ingredients in a crock pot and cook for about 2 hours or until done. Serve  in crock pot with toothpicks.
