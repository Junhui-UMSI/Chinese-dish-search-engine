Swedish Mushroom Sauce
Butter, White pepper, Sherry,Dry, Mushrooms, Sliced, Salt, Lemon juice, All-purpose flour, Half and half, 
Offer as a sauce or a side dish. Saute mushrooms in butter until limp. Stir  in flour, salt, and pepper. Add cream; cook, stirring constantly, until  thick and smooth. Stir in lemon juice and Sherry; salt to taste. Makes  about 2 1/2 cups sauce or 3 to 4 servings.
