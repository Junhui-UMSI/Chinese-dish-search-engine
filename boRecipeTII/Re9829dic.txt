Pumpkin Waffles From Southern Living
All-purpose flour, Brown Sugar, Baking Powder, Ground cinnamon, Ground allspice, Salt, Ground ginger, Eggs, milk, pumpkin, Butter, 
Combine first 7 ingredients in a large bowl. Combine egg yolks, milk, and  pumpkin; add to flour mixture, stirring just until moistened. Stir in  melted butter. Set waffle batter aside.    Beat egg whites at high speed with an electric mixer until soft peaks form,  gently fold into batter.    Bake in a preheated, oiled waffle iron until crisp. Serve with butter,  maple syrup and toasted pecans.      Source: Southern Living, November, 1995  Typed by J.
