Tuna Stuffed Pasta Shells
Tuna in water; drained and, Garlic powder, Pcs jumbo pasta shells;, Fat-free plain yogurt, Dill weed, Fat-free cottage cheese, Fat-free parmesan cheese, Parsley, FILLING, SAUCE, Dill weed, Lemon juice, bottled, Fat-free cottage cheese, Onions; chopped, TOPPING, 
Preheat oven to 350. Prepare a 11 x 7" baking pan with cooking spray; set  aside. Prepare pasta shells according to package directions; drain. To  prepare filling combine tuna, 1/2 cup cottage cheese, parsley, onions,  lemon juice, and  1/4 teaspoon dill weed in a mixing bowl. Stuff cooked  pasta shells with tuna mixture and place seam side down in prepared pan. To  prepare sauce, combine remaining cottage cheese, yogurt, dill weed, and  garlic powder.
