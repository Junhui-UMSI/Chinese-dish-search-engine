Tangy Gelatin Salad
Boiling water, Cottage cheese; sieved, Horseradish, Whipped cream, Pecans, (20-oz) crushed pineapple, (3-oz) lemon- or, Green pepper or pimiento;, 
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Cool.  Add sieved cottage cheese and  other ingredients. Spoon into 6-cup mold and chill in refrigerator until  firm. Yield: 6 servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
