Thai Cucumber Salad #5
Salt, OPTIONAL, Freshly minced cilantro, Cilantro sprigs, Medium-sized cucumbers, Sugar, Very finely minced red, Finely mined red or green, Cider vinegar, Red Serrano chili;, 
Date: Thu, 9 May 1996 15:58:17 -0500    From: lisa@magicnet.net (Lisa Folickman)    >Date:    Thu, 22 Dec 94 16:31:00 PST >From: "Severson, Kate"  <hpl@WSDOT.WA.GOV> from Mollie Katzens "Still Life With Menu"    1. Peel and seed the cucumbers.  Cut into quarters lengthwise, then into  thin slices.    2. Combine everything except the serrano chile and cilantro sprigs into a  medium-sized bowl.  Mix gently.  Cover tightly, and let marinate in the  refrigerator at least 4 hours.    3. Serve cold.  Top with thin strips of red serrano chili and sprigs of  fresh cilantro, if desired, shortly before serving. Yield: 4 to 6    FATFREE DIGEST V96 #129    From the Fatfree Vegetarian recipe list.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
