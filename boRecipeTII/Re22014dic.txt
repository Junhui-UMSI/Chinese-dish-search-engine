Turkey Cocktail Meatballs
Poultry seasoning, Egg, MEATBALLS, fat-free saltine crackers, Chili sauce, Worchestershire sauce, SPICY SAUCE, Vinegar, Lite salt, Brown sugar, Onion, Lean ground white meat, Chicken broth, 
Mix all of the meatball ingredients together and roll into bitesized balls.  Place meatballs on top of a broiler pan so any fat will fall through. Bake  at 375 degrees F. for about 15-20 minutes. Mix spicy sauce ingredients as a  dip for meatballs. Yields 50 Meatballs.
