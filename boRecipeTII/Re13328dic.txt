Yakitori Sauce
Sake wine, Cornstarch, Mirin wine, Sugar, Soy sauce, Water, 
Combine sake and mirin wines in a medium-size saucepan and bring to a boil.  Add soy sauce and sugar. Simmer, covered for 30 minutes. Dissolve  cornstarch in water and add to boiling mixture. Cook and stir until mixture  thickens. Serve hot over baked, broiled, or roasted chicken.
