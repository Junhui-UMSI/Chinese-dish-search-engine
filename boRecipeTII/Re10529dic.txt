Quick Italian Spaghetti
Real bacon bits (optional), Beef, ground, Worcestershire Sauce, Olives, ripe; drained and, Tomato sauce (3x8oz cns), Sugar, Cheese, Parmesan or Ramano, Salt, Pepper, Green onions; thinly sliced, Spaghetti, cooked, 
In a skillet, brown ground beef and onions.  Add next five ingredients;  cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Add olives; simmer 5 minutes longer. Spoon  over spaghetti; sprinkle with cheese and bacon bits if desired.
