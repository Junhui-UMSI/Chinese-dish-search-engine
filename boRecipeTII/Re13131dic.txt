Won Ton in Oyster Sauce (Ho Yau Gon Lo Won To
Thin soy sauce, Won ton, Water, Sesame oil, Oyster sauce, Green onion, chopped, 
In a large saucepan bring 2 quarts water to a boil.    Add won ton and boil for 5 minutes; then, remove with a Chinese strainer or  colander.    Put the cooked won ton in a deep serving bowl, and add all other  ingredients except the green onion, and mix carefully.    Garnish with the chopped green onion.    VARIATION: Heat sauce in wok; stir fry won ton briefly.    SOURCE: Chopstick, Cleaver, and wok.
