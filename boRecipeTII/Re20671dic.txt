Apple Crisp #03
Rolled oats, Sugar and cinnamon to taste, Flour, Salt, Peeled; sliced apples, Cinnamon, Stick butter or margarine, Brown sugar; firmly packed, 
Cut apples.  Add sugar, cinnamon to taste.  (1/4 - 1/3 c sugar, 1 teas  cinnamon) Arrange in buttered baking dish.  Mix dry ingredients together.  Add butter. Blend well with a fork. Spread on top of apples; press down  lightly. Bake until topping is brown and apples are tender when tested with  a fork. Bake: 350F 30-40 minutes    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /DESSERTS    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
