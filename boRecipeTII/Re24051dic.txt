Tournament Tamalie Pie
Chili powder, Gebhardts or, (8-oz) Tomato sauce, Bacon drippings, (or oil), Seasoned salt, Seasoned pepper, Garlic salt, milk, Lean ground beef, (12-oz) Whole kernel corn, Cheddar Cheese, Eggs, Cornmeal, (3-1/4 oz) Ripe olives, Onions, , chopped, 
Preheat oven to 350 deg. F. Beat eggs, add milk and cornmeal, mix together  and let stand while preparing the following mixture. Cook onions and ground  beef in fat until onions are transparent and beef loses pink color. Add  corn, tomato sauce, ripe olives and the seasonings. Mix well. Add to  cornmeal mixture and pour into greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Top with  cheese. Bake for       45    minutes.
