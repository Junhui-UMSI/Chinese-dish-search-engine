1 Egg Butter Spritz
Egg, Baking Powder, Salt, All-purpose flour, Sugar, Vanilla; almond or lemon, Butter, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Combine butter, sugar, egg and flavoring; beat  at medium speed until smooth and fluffy. Add flour, baking powder and salt,  stirring until well combined. Dough can be divided and tinted, if desired.    If dough is too soft, chill until workable. Force dough through a cookie  press onto an ungreased baking sheet. Decorate with colored sugar, cinnamon  candies, sprinkles or jimmies, etc. Bake near center of oven for 8 to 14  minutes until cookies are light golden around the edges.
