Taco Bean Bake
Chili powder, Salt, Onions, corn  , Fat free mayo, Red beans, Tabasco, non fat cheese, tomato sauce  , Egg substitute, Fat free baking mix, Nonfat milk, 
Date: Sat, 20 Apr 1996 19:53:53 -0700    From: bg381@scn.org (SCN User)    Preheat oven to 350.  Saute onion, add beans (you may want to use more than  3 c beans), salt, chili powder, tabasco, tomato sauce, and corn. Pour into  Pam sprayed 13x9x2 pan. Sprinkle with cheese. Put baking mix in bowl, cut  in fat free mayo, stir in 1/2c egg substitute, and milk. Spread over bean  mixture. Bake 35 min or until browned nicely. Serves great with lettuce,  tomatoe, and fat free sour cream.    FATFREE DIGEST V96 #110    From the Fatfree Vegetarian recipe list.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
