Turnip or Big Radish Salad
Onion medium size; cut into, Salt NO pepper., Boiled hard eggs ;cut into, Turnip or Big Radish, Tabls Oil; (up to 3), 
Source: From my mother with love & more. My mother.    Wash well and peel Turnip, If you use Radish, do not peel, In food  processor shred the Tunip.    In a skillet, Fry the onions till gold in color.    Add to Tunip, Add the eggs, Add the salt. It is up you if how much salt you  want, Mix till all is well blended. Keep in refrigerator You can have it  with other salads,like Tomatoes salad,    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest Volume 98 #010 by Zvi&Rina perry  <pzvi@netvision.net.il> on
