Tomato Kachumber
Salt to taste, Grated ginger, Chopped fresh mint, wedges, Fresh lime juice, Black mustard seeds, Tomatoes, cut into thin, Scallions, chopped, Vegetable oil, 
Combine the tomatoes, scallions, mint, ginger & lime juice in a medium  bowl.  Heat the vegetable oil in a small skillet. Add the mustard seeds.  Have a cover ready to contain the seeds as they begin to pop. Remove from  heat.  Pour over the other ingredients, add salt to taste & lightly toss.  Let marinate for 30 minutes before serving.
