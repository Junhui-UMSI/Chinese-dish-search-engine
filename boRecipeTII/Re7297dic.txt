Pork Chops a la Morella
Cumin, Loin pork chops - thick ones, olive oil, Vermouth, salt and pepper, Oregano, Water, Garlic, Chile powder, 
From: ADAMSFMLE@aol.com    Date: Thu, 15 Aug 1996 18:12:20 -0400  Recipe By     : Senora Maria Luisa Carillo de Padilla    Season chops with salt and pepper. Fry in hot oil until nicely browned.  Remove from ran.  saute onion and garlic, adding a little more oil if  needed. Stir in chile powder, oregano, cumin, and water. Return chops to  pan, spoon sauce over them, cover pan, and let simmer for 15 minutes. Stir  in vermouth just before serving.    SOURCE:  The Mexican Cookbook, by George and Inger Wallace, 1971 We thank  Senora Maria Luisa Carillo de Padilla, manager of Hotel Acueducto in  Morelia, Mexico for this excellent recipe.
