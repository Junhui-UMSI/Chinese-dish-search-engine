Bacon and Cheese Waffles
sour cream, cheddar, milk, Butter, bacon, Egg, Pancake mix, 
In a medium bowl, beat egg; add milk, sour cream and butter. Stir in  pancake mix; mix well.  Fold in bacon and cheese. Bake in a preheated  waffle iron according to manufacturers directions until golden brown.
