Raspberry Vinaigrette 4
Raspberry vinegar, Vegetable oil, Half and half, Sugar, Frozen unsweetened; 10 oz., Raspberries; thawed, 
Puree raspberries by pushing through a sieve with back of wooden spoon.  Blend vinegar and sugar in blender until smooth.  With blender running,  slowly add oil, then half and half, then raspberry puree (about 3/4 c.) Mix  well.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.  Shake well before stirring.
