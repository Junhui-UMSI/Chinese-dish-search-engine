Pumpkin Pie Bars
Powdered sugar glaze;, Sweetened condensed milk, Pumpkin pie spice, Sugar, Walnuts; finely chopped, Egg, Vanilla extract, All-purpose flour, Margarine; softened, Light molasses, Canned pumpkin, 
With mixer, beat margarine and sugar until creamy.  Stir in flour and  walnuts until mixture is crumbly. Reserve 3/4 cup crumb mixture for  topping. Press remaining crumb mixture on bottom of 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x  1-inch baking pan. Bake at 350 deg F for 15 to 18 minutes or until lightly  browned.    Meanwhile, blend pumpkin, condensed milk, molasses, eggs, pumpkin pie spice  and vanilla; pour over pastry crust. Sprinkle with reserved crumb mixture.  Bake 30 to 35 minutes more or until set. Cool in pan on wire rack. Drizzle  with powdered sugar glaze if desired. Cut into 2 1/2 x 1 1/2-inch bars.  Store covered in refrigerator.
