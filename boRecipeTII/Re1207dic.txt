Crock Pot Hamburger Soup
Lean ground beef, Pepper, Oregano, Basil, Seasoned salt, onion soup mix, tomato sauce, Soy sauce, Celery, Carrots, Macaroni, cooked, Parmesan cheese, 
Crumble beef into slow-cooking pot. Add pepper, oregano, basil, seasoned  salt and dry onion soup mix. Stir in 3 cups boiling water, tomato sauce and  soy sauce, then add celery and carrots. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8  hours. Turn control on high. Add cooked macaroni and Parmesan cheese. Cover  and cook on high for 10-15 minutes.
