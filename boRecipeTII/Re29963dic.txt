Teriyaki Steak Salad
Green onions sliced w tops, 12 oz bottle teriyaki marine, 6 radished sliced, Cherry tomatoes halved or, Top round steak, Med green bell pepper chop, Shredded mozarrela, Sweet & sour salad dressing, 
Marinade steak in teriyaki sauce in refrigerator for several hours, turning  occasionally. Remove steak and broil 3 to 5 inches from heat source for 5  to 8 minutes or medium rare. baste with marinade after turning.Cool and  slice. Combine remaining ingredients in bowl and toss with dressing.  arrange among 6 plates.Slice meat across grain crosswise and arrange atop  greens.
