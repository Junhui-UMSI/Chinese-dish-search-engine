Almond-Orange Biscotti
Salt, Eggs, almonds, Vegetable oil, zest of 1 orange, Sugar, Baking Powder, Flour, Almond extract, 
From: waring@infomail.com (Sam Waring) (COLLECTION)    Date: 12 Apr 1994 15:17:00 -0400  Preheat oven to 350 F.  Grease and flour cookie sheet.    In a large bowl, sift the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Make a  well in the center of the mixture; add the remaining ingredients and mix  well. Divide the dough in half. Shape each half into flat-bottomed  cylinders (1-inch high x 2-/12-inches wide x 8-inches long). Bake 30-35  minutes or until brown on top. Remove from oven and cool slightly. Cut into  3/4-inch slices. Return to oven with cut side down for 15 minutes or until  slices are brown. Remove from oven and cool on racks.
