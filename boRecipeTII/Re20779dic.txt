Apple Filling (Canned Apples)
APPLE SLICED #10, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, All Purpose Flour, CINNAMON GROUND 1 LB CN, MACE GROUND, 
1.  CHOP APPLES INTO 1/2 INCH PIECES.    2.  MIX TOGETHER CINNAMON, FLOUR, MACE AND SUGAR.    3.  ADD TO APPLES; MIX WELL.    NOTE:  1.  THIS FILLING MAY BE USED IN A VARIETY OF SWEET DOUGH PRODUCTS.  SEE RECIPE NO.D-G-7, GUIDE FOR WEET DOUGH MAKE-UP.    NOTE:  2.  USE ABOUT 2 TBSP (1-NO. 30 SCOOP) APPLE PIE FILLING FOR EACH  PASTRY.    Recipe Number: D00300    SERVING SIZE: 2 TABLESP    From the <Army Master Recipe Index File> (actually used today!).
