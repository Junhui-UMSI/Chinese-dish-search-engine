Applesauce Oatmeal Muffins
Vegetable oil, Baking soda, Cp skim milk, TOPPING, Cp packed brn sugar, Cp applesauce, Egg white, lightly beaten, Cp flour, MUFFINS, Cp oats, Cinnamon, Cinnamon, Cp oats, Packed brn sugar, Baking Powder, 
Heat oven to 400.  Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners.  Combine topping  ingredients and set aside.  For muffins, combine dry ingredients and mix  well. Combine applesauce, milk, oil and egg white and mix well. Add wet to  dry ingredients and mix just until dry ingredients are moistened. Fill  muffin cups almost full and sprinkle with topping. Bake 20-24 minutes or  until golden brown. Cool 5 minutes in pan on wire rack and then remove from  pan. Serve warm-makes 1 dozen.
