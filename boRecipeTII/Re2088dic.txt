Apple Chutney
Sultanas, Candied ginger, chopped, Salt, Ground cayenne pepper, Sterilized glass jars, Gr. (3 pound, 13 oz.), Lemon, washed and chopped, Apple-vinegar, Soft brown sugar (pressed, Clove of garlic, peeled and, 
Delicious with pork or ham.    About 6 cups    Bring all the ingredients to the boil in a fairly large pan. Reduce the  heat. Simmer (no lid on the pan) for approximately 2 hours. When the  mixture is thick enough, pour the chutney in sterilized jars and close them  immediately.
