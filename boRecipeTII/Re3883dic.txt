Pepper Dressing
Whipping cream, Dehydrated chives, Commercial sour cream, Paprika, Instant onion flakes, Pepper, Salt, Dried green pepper flakes, Instant minced garlic, 
Combine all ingredients in a small mixing bowl; stir well. Refrigerate  dressing overnight. Serve with fresh vegetables. Yield: 1 1/4 cups.    Recipe by: Southern Living 1980 Annual Recipes Posted to MC-Recipe Digest  V1 #760 by NGavlak2@aol.com on Aug 25, 1997
