Sweet and Sour Cabbage #1
Flour, Water, Onion; sliced, Bacon, Vinegar, Brown sugar, Shredded cabbage, Cloves, salt and pepper, 
Cook cabbage in boiling, salted water for 7 minutes. Fry bacon. Add sugar &  flour to bacon fat. Blend. Add water, vinegar & seasonings. Cook until  thick. Add onion, diced bacon & cabbage. Heat. Serves 6.    JUDY GREGORY    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
