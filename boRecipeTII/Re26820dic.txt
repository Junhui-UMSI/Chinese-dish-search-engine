Baked Asparagus
Fresh asparagus spears, , Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Fresh parsley, Garlic, Salt, Black Pepper, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Break tough ends off asparagus and place in a  baking tray so the stalks dont overlap. Add remaining ingredients and bake  uncovered until asparagus is just tender, 5-8 minutes. Serve hot.    Nutritional Info Per Serving: Protein: 4 gr. (10%); Carbohydrates: 7 gr.  (19%); Fat: 11 gr. (71%); Calories: 133; Sodium: 60 mg.; Cholesterol: 0 mg.
