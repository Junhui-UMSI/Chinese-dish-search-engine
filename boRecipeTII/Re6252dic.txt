Pizzocheri Pasta Basic Recipe
Buckwheat flour, Salt, Jumbo eggs, Vegetable oil, Unbleached white flour, 
Make pasta in regular way using well method.    Roll into sheets on thinnest setting and then cut sheets into horizontal  strips 1 inch thick. Cut across the strips in 2 inch intervals to yield 2  inch long and 1 inch tall strips. These are pizzocheri.    Yield: 4 servings    Recipe by: Molto Mario Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #517 by Sue  <suechef@sover.net> on Mar 14, 1997
