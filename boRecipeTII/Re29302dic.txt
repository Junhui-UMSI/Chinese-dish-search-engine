Baked Stuffed Sweet Potatoes
Flaked coconut, sweet potatoes, butter, orange, Salt, Butter or margarine, Sugar, 
Scrub sweet potatoes and bake at 350 degrees for one hour or until tender.  Remove from oven and cut a thin slice from each side of potato, scoop out  all pulp, being careful not to break skin. Mash pulp; add melted margarine  or butter, sugar, oranges, coconut and salt, mix well. Spoon into shells,  dot each with approximately 1 teaspoon of margarine or butter. Bake at 450  degrees for 15 minutes or until tops are lightly browned. Serves 8. Good  with pork roast or pork chops.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
