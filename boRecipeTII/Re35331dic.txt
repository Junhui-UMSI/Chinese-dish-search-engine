Beef Brisket in Teriyaki Sauce
Garlic salt, Teriyaki sauce, Lipton onion soup mix, Water, Beef brisket, Salt, Black Pepper, 
Cut most of fat off the brisket. Put brisket on rack in deep baking pan.  Sprinkle all sides of brisket with garlic salt, salt and pepper. Sprinkle  soup mix on top. Pour teriyaki sauce on top. Half of soup mixture will fall  off to make gravy. Pour 1 1/2 cups water in bottom of pan. Cover with foil  and bake at 275 degrees for 5 hours.
