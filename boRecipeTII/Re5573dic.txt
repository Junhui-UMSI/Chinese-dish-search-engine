Pineapple Preserves
Pineapple, Little water, Sugar, 
Pare, remove eyes; slice, taking out core or cut into sticks  lengthwise, or cut sticks into cubes, or grate. Put sugar and fruit  into preserving kettle with small amount of water and cook for 20  minutes. Pour into sterilized jars and seal.
