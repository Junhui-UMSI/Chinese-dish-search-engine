Basic Oatburger
Water, Oil OR, Soy sauce, Nuts, ground, Large onion, diced, Garlic salt, Flaked, Italian seasoning, 
nutriti nal yeast    Bring all ingredients to a boil. Turn down heat and add 4 1/2 c. rolled  oats. Do not stir. Cook about five minutes. Then set aside to cool. Form  patties with oiled mason jar lid. Place on oiled cookie sheet. Bake at 350  degrees for about 45 minutes, turning every 15 minutes. Serve with ketchup  or gravy. Makes about 20 ~25 medium sized burgers. (From _Vegetarian  Cooking for a Better World_, by Muriel Collura Golde, MA, MS, MLS,  published by NAVS)
