Sweet and Sour Chicken Salad
Apples; preferably Gala,, Canola oil; plus 2 teaspoons, Snow peas; trimmed, Boneless skinless chicken, Paprika, Celery seed, Vinegar; plus 2 teaspoons, Sugar; plus 1 teaspoon, salt and pepper, Celery; diagonally sliced, Red bell pepper; seeded and, 
Combine all salad ingredients. Whisk together the dressing ingredients.  Pour the dressing over the salad and serve.    Per serving: calories 238, fat 11.7g, 44% calories from fat, cholesterol  54mg, protein 20.9g, carbohydrates 12.7g, fiber 2.0g, sodium 75mg.
