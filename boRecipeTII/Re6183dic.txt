Pizza Sauce
Oregano, Chopped parsley, 8-oz  cans tomato sauce, Garlic powder, 6-oz  can tomato paste, Anise seeds, freshly ground, Pepper, Italian seasonings, 
Grind the anise seeds. Mix it all together. Spread on the dough. Top with  veggies.
