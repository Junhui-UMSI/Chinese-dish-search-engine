Swedish Pancakes (Plattar)
milk, Flour, Salt, Sour cream, Apple Sauce, Eggs, Lingonberry Preserves, 
Beat yolks until thick. Sift together flour, salt and sugar. Add to egg  yolks alternately with milk. Stir in sour cream. Beat egg whites until  stiff but not dry. Fold into batter.Heat pancake pan and butter each  depression (if you have a Swedish Pan Cake Pan <see notes>). Pour about a  tablespoon of batter into each depression and spread out evenly. Brown on  one side, turn and brown on the other side. Serve at once with preserves or  applesauce.
