Sweet and Sour Cabbage With Plantains
Ripe medium plantain; cut, Savoy cabbage; coarsely, Vegetable oil, Cornstarch, Green onions; sliced, Cider vinegar, Red bell pepper; cut into 1, Vegetable broth, Brown sugar; packed**, Red pepper sauce, 
Date: 27 Apr 96 22:30:23 EDT    From: "Lisabeth Crawford (Pooh)" <104105.1416@CompuServe.COM>  **PERSONAL NOTE from Ursula Taylor - for diabetics - use brown sugar  substitute.    Mix broth, brown sugar, vinegar, cornstarch and pepper sauce; reserve. Heat  oil in 10" nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Add plantain,  onions and bell pepper; stir-fry one minute. Add cabbage; stir-fry 2  minutes. Stir in cornstarch mixture; cook and stir 10 seconds or until  thickened.
