Pumpkin Soup #1
(8-oz) tomatoes, Green Pepper, Sprig parsley, Pepper, Salt, Thyme, (1-lb.) pumpkin, Onions, Butter, Flour, Chicken broth or stock, milk, Bay leaf, 
From:    Judy L Garrison <bluj@JUNO.COM>    Date:    Fri, 12 Jul 1996 00:53:52 PST  Melt butter in large saucepan.  Add green pepper, onion, parsley, thyme and  bay leaf.  Cook 5 minutes.  Add tomatoes, pumpkin and chicken broth.  Cover  and simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Press mixture through food  mill or wire strainer. Blend together flour and milk and stir into soup.  Add salt and pepper and cook, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a  boil. Serve immediately. Serves 6    EAT-L Digest 11 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
