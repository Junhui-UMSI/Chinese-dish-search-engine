Banana Raisin Muffins
Egg whites, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Mashed bananas, Raisins, Oatmeal flour, Unsweetened applesauce, Vanilla extract, Branflakes, Salt, Sugar, 
Sift together the dry ingredients; set aside. Beat together well the sugar,  applesauce, egg whites, and extracts. Stir in bananas, branflakes, and  raisins. Add the flour mixture and stir just until moistened. Spoon into 12  muffin cups coated with cooking spray.  Bake at 400=B0 F. for 20 minutes or  until done.    per muffin:  113 Kcal  1.0g fat (0g sat fat) 8% CFF  382mg Na  2.4g fiber  (3.4g PRO/ 1.0g FAT/ 23.9g CHO)=20 Exchanges: 1 STARCH/BREAD, 1/2 FRUIT    Recipe By     : Rosemary Winters    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
