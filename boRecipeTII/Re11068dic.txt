Rainbow Smorsels
Marshmallow creme, Sugar, Graham cracker crumbs, Brown Sugar, All-purpose flour, Butter, (9-oz) Nestle Toll House, Salt, 
Date:    Tue, 11 Jun 1996 21:11:28 -0400    From:    Linda Robinson <CatRescuer@AOL.COM>    Preheat oven to 325x F.  In mixer bowl, blend flour, graham cracker crumbs,  butter, sugars and salt until crumbly.  Press into greased 9" square baking  pan.  Bake for 15 minutes.  Spoon marshmallow creme over base; let stand  until softened. Gently spread marshmallow creme over warm base; top with  Rainbow morsels. Bake 5 minutes longer. Cool completely. Cut into 2 1/4"  squares. Makes 16 squares.    EAT-L DIGEST 10 JUNE 1996    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
