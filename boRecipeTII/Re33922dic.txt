Sweet and Spicy Popcorn
Salt; optional, Sugar, Chili powder, Cayenne pepper, Ground cinnamon, Plain popcorn; popped, 
Seeing I havent posted in quite a while, I thought that I would post  lowfat from the Taste of Home Lowfat Country Cooking. Out of 455 recipes I  only have 44 formatted. Plan on formatting the rest this week. Hope you all  enjoy these.    Place sugar, chili powder, cinnamon, salt if desired and cayenne pepper in  a resealable plastic bag or other 2-quart container. Mix. Add popcorn. Mist  popcorn with nonstick cooking spray. Close bag and shake. Repeat one or two  times until popcorn is coated.    Yield: 6 cups    Serving Size: 1 1/2 cups    Calories: 78, Total Fat: 1gm, Calories from Fat: 14%, Saturated Fat: trace,  Cholesterol: 0, Sodium: 7mg, Carbohydrate: 16gm, Protein: 2gm    NOTES : this crisp snack has a fun and different flavor.  I took some to my  neighbors one evening when we were visiting and it didnt last long!
