Potato Pudding (Kartofshka Pynding) Russia
Onion, grated, Potatoes, grated, Sour Cream, Egg yolks,beaten, Egg whites, beaten stiff, Flour, salt and pepper, 
from:the wonderful world of cooking       Combine potatoes, onion, and sour cream. Sift in flour. Mix well. add  egg yolks and seasoning. Mix well. Fold in egg whites. Turn into greased 9  inch pie pan. Bake at 350 deg F. for 30 minutes. Serve with spiced  applesauce.
