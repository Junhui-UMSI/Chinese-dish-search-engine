Turnovers
Lard or shortening, Filling, milk, Salt, Flour, Baking Powder, Eggs, 
Stir flour, baking powder & salt. Cut in shortening. Beat egg with  milk. Add to flour mixture stirring till combined. Form dough into  balls; cover & chill 1 hour. Divide dough into 16 portions. On  lightly floured surface roll each to a 6" circle. Place about 3 Tbls.  filling on each. Moisten edges with a little water, fold in half  sealing edges with fork. Fry or bake as directed. To Fry:  Fry in  deep hot oil for about 4 minutes. To Bake: Place in baking sheet.  Brush tops with a little milk, sprinkle with sugar. Bake at 400 for       15    minutes.
