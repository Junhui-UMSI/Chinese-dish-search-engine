Vanilla Refrigerator Cookies
Flour, Eggs, Baking soda, Vanilla, Shortening, Brown sugar, Sugar, Salt, 
Mix shortening, sugars, eggs & vanilla thoroughly. Blend dry  ingredients together; mix into shortening mixture. Mix thoroughly  with hands. Press & mold into long, smooth roll about 2 1/2" in  diameter. Wrap in wax paper & chill several hours. Heat oven to 400.  Cut into thin slices. Place a little apart on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 6-8 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
