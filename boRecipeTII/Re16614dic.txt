Rice vinegar, Red onion; thinnly sliced, Fresh red or green chili, Salt, Black pepper, Sugar, Cucumber; peeled, Cilantro leaves; chopped, 
In a medium bowl, stir vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper until sugar & salt  are dissolved.  Quarter cucumber lengthwise.  Remove seeds.  Cut quarters  crossswise into 1/8" thick slices.  Add cucumber, onion, cilantro & chili  to vinegar dressing; toss until combined. Cover and marinate in the  refrigerator for 3 hours, stirring occasionally.
