Crepes
Flour, Baking Powder, Eggs, Salt, milk, 
Water to right consistency    Place dry ingredients in a bowl.  Add milk slowly and beat til smooth. Add  eggs and then water til fairly runny.  Using very thin layer, fry crepes  over moderately high heat.  Use oil as needed.
