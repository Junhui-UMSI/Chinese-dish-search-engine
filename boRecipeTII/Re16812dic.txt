All Purpose Barbecue Sauce
Salad oil, Black Pepper, Bourbon, Sherry, Or Wine, Soy sauce, Garlic powder, 
Combine all ingredients and pour over meat.  Marinate in refrigerator.  Also use to baste meat as you cook it.  Good on red meat, fish or chicken.    From "Trail Bosss Cowboy Cookbook" by Society of Range Management.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/west.zip
