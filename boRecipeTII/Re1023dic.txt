Classic Meat Loaf - Lf
Vegetable cooking spray, VEGETABLES, Onion, Finely chopped carrot, Finely chopped zucchini, Garlic, SEASONINGS, Chopped parsley, Low-salt ketchup, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, Pepper, MAIN INGREDIENTS, Extra lean ground beef -, Bulgur; soaked in, Boiling water; for 20mins;, Egg substitute - or 2-lg, OPTIONAL TOPPINGS, Low-salt ketchup, 
Heat oven to 350F. Have ready a 13 x 9-in. baking dish and larger roasting  pan.    Coat a large nonstick skillet with vegetable oil spray. Add vegetables and  saute until slighty browned. Let cool. Stir in Seasonings and Main  Ingredients. Shape mixture, in baking dish, allowing a 1/2 inch gap between  the loaf and the pan. Set dish in roasting pan and add Topping. Place in  oven; add boiling water to come halfway up sides of baking dish. Bake until  center of loaf registers 160F on an instant-read thermometer. Remove dish  from roasting pan. Drain if necessary. Let loaf stand 5 minutes before  slicing.    RECIPE NOTES: Original [245 cals, 10g fat, 37%] by using 16oz beef, 1/2 cup  bulgur, whole eggs. Modified [240 cals, 7g fat, 27%cff].
