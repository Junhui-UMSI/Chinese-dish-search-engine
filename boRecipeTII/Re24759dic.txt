From: arielle@taronga.com (Stephanie da Silva) (COLLECTION)    Date: Wed, 2 Mar 1994 23:07:59 GMT  See recipe "Grandma Olsens Sugar Cookies" for source/info on this recipe.    Preheat oven to 325F, grease & flour 12 cup bundt pan. Sprinkle nuts over  bottom of pan, mix remaining ingredients & pour over nuts. Bake one hour  and cool. Prick holes in top of cake & pour 1/2 of glaze over. When cool  remove from pan & prick holes on balance of cake, pour balance of glaze.    Glaze: Melt butter, stir in water & sugar. Boil 5 minutes, stirring, remove  from heat and add rum.    Lori    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
