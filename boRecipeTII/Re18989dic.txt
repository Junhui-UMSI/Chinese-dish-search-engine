Vegetable and Herb Rice Oriental Soup
Lemon juice, Box Quick Brown Rice, (Vegetable and Herb), olive oil, Mung bean sprouts, Water, Tamari soy sauce, 
Cook contents of box according to package directions.  Add the soy sauce,  oil, and lemon juice.  Stir and let simmer 5 minutes more.  Add the  sprouts and just heat through.  More tamari soy sauce may be added to  taste.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Quick Brown Rice" tri-fold  Copyright 1987 Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
