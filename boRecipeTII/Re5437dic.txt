Pineapple Coconut Muffins
All-purpose flour, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Sliced almonds, Vanilla, Salt, Baking Powder, Flaked coconut, Egg, crushed pineapple, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.    Stir together flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, and coconut. Mix egg, oil,  milk, and pineapple together well. Add this mixture to dry ingredients.  Stir until just moistened. Fill greased or paper lined muffin pans 2/3  full. If desired, sprinkle sliced almonds over batter and press them in  lightly.    Bake for 20 minutes, or until golden brown. Makes 12 to 14 muffins.    Recipe by: "Quick Breads"    Posted to recipelu-digest by "Susan Mori" <bonaparte@email.msn.com> on Mar  15, 1998
