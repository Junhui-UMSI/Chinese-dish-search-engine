Tomato Bacon Pomodoro Sauce For Pasta
olive oil, Bacon, Onion, Garlic, Tomato, Tomato paste, Chicken broth, Basil, Oregano, Dried parsley, 
Place oil and bacon in pan. Cook bacon until brown. Add onion and garlic  and cook until soft. Stir in remaining ingredients and simmer for 3  minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve over prepared ravioli or  tortellini.
