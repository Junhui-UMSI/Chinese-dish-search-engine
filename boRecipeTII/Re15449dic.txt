White Chocolate Banana Cake
Baking soda, Vanilla, Buttermilk, banana, Eggs, Baking Powder, Icing sugar, Crisco shortening, Cake & pastry flour, Milk, Lemon juice, White chocolate, Cake:, White chocolate, Salt, Frosting:, White chocolate, Crisco shortening, banana, 
1. Preheat oven to 350F (180C).   2. Grease two 8-inch round cake pans with shortening and line with waxed  paper.   3. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.   4. Cream shortening, sugar and eggs on high speed until light and fluffy.   5. Add banana and vanilla.   6. Add dry ingredients on low speed, alternating with buttermilk, mixing  lightly after each addition.   7. Stir in grated chocolate.   8. Spread batter evenly in greased pans.   9. Bake at 350F (180C) for 30 to 35 minutes. Cool 10 minutes, then remove  from pans and cool completely on wire racks.    Frosting: 1. Beat all ingredients together at high speed until smooth and  light. Use just enough milk to make a spreading consistency.   2. Frost cake layers.   3. Decorate with chocolate curls.  Makes: About 8 servings. Freezing:  Excellent  Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #396 by angstrom@juno.com (Angela  L  Gilliland) on Nov 18, 1997
