Wanchese Crab Cakes
Mayonnaise, Worcestershire Sauce, ;salt, Egg, Cracker crumbs; for dredging, Crabmeat; flaked, Onion; minced, Hot pepper sauce, Cracker crumbs, Peanut oil; for frying cakes, Black pepper; ground fresh, Mustard; prepared, 
In a medium bowl, mix together with fork the first 10 ingredients. Form  mixture into 3-inch patties; roll in cracker crumbs.    In a skillet or deep fat fryer, heat oil to 375 degrees F; fry patties  until golden brown, about 2 minutes on each side. Drain on paper towels and  serve immediately with sauce of choice. Makes 3 servings. Source: Blue  Crab, a Cookbooklet.    Shared and MM by Judi M. Phelps. jphelps@shell.portal.com,  juphelps@delphi.com, or jphelps@best.com
