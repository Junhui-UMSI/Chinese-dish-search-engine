Witchs Brew
Lime sherbet; softened, Unsweet apple juice, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Water, Vodka; can use water instea, Club soda; chilled, Lime juice, 
Combine apple juice, water, vodka, and lime juice in a large bowl. Cover  and chill. Just before serving, spoon sherbet into a large punch bowl;  slowly pour apple juice mixture and club soda over sherbet, stirring  gently.
