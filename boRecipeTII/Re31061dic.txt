Banana Pudding
Eggs, Cornstarch, Ripe Bananas, Sliced, Salt, Butter, Vanilla, Vanilla Wafers, milk, Sugar, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Combine the sugar, cornstarch, milk and  salt in a large saucepan. Cook, stirring, over medium heat until thickened.  Beat the eggs lightly with a wire whisk. Add a little of the hot mixture to  the eggs. Blend well. Pour the eggs into the mixture in the saucepan. Cook,  stirring, for a few minutes. Add the butter and the vanilla. Blend well.  Cover the surface of the pudding with plastic wrap. Cool slightly. Place a  layer of vanilla wafers on the bottom of a (2 Qt.) casserole. Alternate  additional layers of vanilla wafers with layers of banana slices and  pudding, ending with pudding as the top layer. Bake until set (about 10  minutes).
