Banana Fudge Brownies
Baking Powder, Hodgson Mill 50/50 flour, Vanilla, Salt, Margarine, Chocolate chips, Banana, Sugar, Egg Beaters 99% egg, 
From:    charles moffat <moffats@USAOR.NET>    Date:    Thu, 3 Jan 1980 13:30:15 -0500  Recipe By: Betty Crockers Snacks, Salads & Desserts    Melt margarine & chocolate chips in a 3 qt saucepan over low heat, stirring  constantly. Beat in remaining ingredients with a wooden spoon until  thoroughly blended. Spread evenly in a 13 x 9 pan ( grease bottom only).    Bake at 350F for about 30 minutes or until center is set. Cool completely.    NOTES : 2 eggs can be used instead of EggBeaters.  2/3 cup of chopped nuts  can be added.    EAT-L Digest 13 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
