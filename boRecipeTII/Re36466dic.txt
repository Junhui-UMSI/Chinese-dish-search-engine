Beer Stew
Shortening, Sprig parsley; large bay, Dark beer, Or tea ball--pinch thyme;, Onions; chopped, Butter or margarine, Boquet garni wrapped in bag, -(up to), Potatoes; diced coarse, Lean beef; in 1/2-inch cubes, Carrots; diced, Turnip; peeled, diced, Peppercorns slightly bruised, Chopped celery; if desired, 
Prebrown flour dredged cubes of beef in hot vegetable oil. At same time,  brown onion slowly in butter. Add carrots, turnips, potatoes & celery to  brown slightly. Mix all ingredients together plus enough water to cover.  Bring to a slow simmer, cover & cook slowly for about 2 hours or until meat  & vegetables are tender. Add liquid if needed, correct seasonings, and  thicken stock with flour if necessary. Serves 6-7.    MRS R.R.
