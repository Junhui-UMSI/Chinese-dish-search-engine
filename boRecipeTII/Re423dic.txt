Lemon Herb Chicken
Lemon juice, Vegetable oil, fresh parsley, Dried tarragon, Pepper, Boneless skinless chicken, 
In a large resealable plastic bag or shallow glassdish, combine the first  five in gredients. Add chicken. Seal or cover and refrigerate for 4 hours  or overnight. Drain. discard marinade. Grill chicken, uncovered, over  Medium -Low coals for 10-15 minutes or until juices run clear, turning  several times. Yield: 8 servings. Per serving: 4 lean meat, 1/2 fat 174  calories, 66 mg. sodium, 73 mg. cholesterol, 7 grams fat.
