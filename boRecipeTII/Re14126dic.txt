White Coconut Cake
Salt, Cake flour, milk, Baking Powder, coconut, Egg whites, Butter, Sugar, Vanilla, 
Sift sugar & measure. Cream with butter until fluffy. Sift dry ingredients  3 times. Add to creamed mixture alternately with lukewarm milk & vanilla.  Fold in egg whites (not too stiffly beaten). Grease & flour 13x9-inch pan.  Line bottom with waxed paper. Bake at 325 for 25-30 minutes. Use divinity  icing. Top with fresh grated coconut.
