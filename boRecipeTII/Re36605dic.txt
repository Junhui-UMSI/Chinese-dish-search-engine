Strawberry Breakfast Sauce
Sugar, Red food coloring; optional, Sliced fresh strawberries, Orange juice, Cornstarch, 
In a saucepan, combine the sugar and cornstarch. Stir in orange juice;  bring to a boil over medium heat. Add strawberries; return to a boil. Cook  and stir for 2 minutes. Add food coloring if desired. Serve warm over  waffles or pancakes.
