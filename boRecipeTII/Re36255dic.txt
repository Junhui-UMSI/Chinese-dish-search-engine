Beef Tortilla Pizza
Tomato -- seeded and, Mozzarella cheese, Parmesean cheese, Oregano -- dried, Ground beef -- lean, Flour tortillas -- 10", Vegetable oil, Onion -- chopped, BARB DAY - GWHP32A-----, Basil leaves; fresh --, Sliced, Thinly, Salt, Chopped, 
Preparation time:  27 minutes Heat oven to 400 degrees F.  Brown ground  beef and onion in skillet over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes or until beef is  no longer pink. Pour off drippings. Stir oregano and salt into the meat.  Lightly brush tortillas with oil  Bake tortillas on 2 large baking sheets  in 400 degree F. oven 3 minutes.  Spoon beef mixture evenly over top of  each tortilla; top with an equal amount of tomato.  Sprinkle with basil and  cheeses. Return to the oven and bake 12 to 14 minutes or until tortillas  are lightly browned. Serving suggestion: Mixed green salad with Italian  dressing  Source: Beef Industry Council    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
