Porkaghetti Platter
Pork blade stks. 1/2" thick, Oregano, Garlic pwdr., Rosemary, Grated Romano cheese, Pepper, Tomato sauce (15 oz.), Lard or drippings, Salt, Instant minced onion, Mushrooms, Spaghetti (7 oz.), Flour, Water, 
Cut pork steaks into strips, 1/4" thick & 2 to 3 inches long. Combine  flour, salt and pepper; dredge pork, reserve excess flour. Add tomato  sauce, water, onion, oregano, resemary and garlic powder. Stir to combine.  Cover tightly and simmer 45 minutes or until meat is tender. Stir in  mushrooms and olives, continue to cook covered for 15 min. Cook spaghetti  according to directions, drain and arrange on platter. Serve meat and sauce  with spaghetti.  Sprinkle with Romano cheese.
