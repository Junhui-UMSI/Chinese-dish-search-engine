Tuna Spinach Au Gratin
Lemon juice, Grated Parmesan, Bread crumbs, Mayonnaise, (7 oz.) can tuna, drained, Salt, (10 oz.) frozen chopped, 
Blend first 7 ingredients and fold in the mayonnaise. Spoon       into individual shells or 9-inch pie plate. Sprinkle with       additional cheese. Bake in 350 degree oven (moderate) for 20       minutes. Serves 4.    Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.20
