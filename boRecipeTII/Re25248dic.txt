Tonys Moms Lemon Snowflakes
Lemon cake mix w/pudding, Egg, Thawed cool whip, Powdered sugar, 
1.  In a mixing bowl, combine cake mix, whipped topping, and egg.  Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until blended. Batter  will be very thick and sticky.    2.  Drop by teaspoonsful into powdered sugar; roll lightly to coat.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake at 350F for 10-12 minutes  or until lightly browned.    Yields:  5-6 dozen    Source:  Eilene Kiger - Blodgett Oregon (Tony Hammerquists mom!)    Cyberealm Cookbook, Vol 1, 4/93, Cyberealm BBS, compiled by Linda Fields.
