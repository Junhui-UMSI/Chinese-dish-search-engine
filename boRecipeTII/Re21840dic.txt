Apple-Pecan Muffins
Lemon Peel, All-purpose flour, Salt, Sugar, Green apples; coarsely, Baking soda, (about 2 ounces), Ground cinnamon, Chopped toasted pecans, Vegetable oil, Fresh Lemon Juice, Grated peeled (abt 2 lg), Egg, Vanilla extract, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 1/2-cup muffin cups. Sift all purpose  flour, baking soda, ground cinnamon and salt into medium bowl. Combine  sugar, oil, egg, lemon juice, vanilla and lemon peel in large bowl; whisk  to blend. Mix in dry ingredients, then apples and pecans. Spoon 1/3 cup  batter into each prepared muffin cup. Bake until tester inserted into  centers comes out clean, about 35 minutes. Turn muffins out onto rack; cool  slightly. Serve warm. (Makes about 12)
