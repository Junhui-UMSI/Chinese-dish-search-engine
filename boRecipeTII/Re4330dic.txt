Persimmon Cookies #2
Sugar, Ground cloves, Shortening, Walnuts - chopped, Egg, Baking soda, Nutmeg, Persimmon pulp, Salt, Raisins (dunk in wiskey), Flour, 
Cream shortening and sugar, add egg and beat. Add all the rest. Drop by  tsp. and bake @ 350 - 15 mins.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #867 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Oct 26, 1997
