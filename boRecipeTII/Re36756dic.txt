Strawberry Jello Salad
Sour cream, large, Strawberry Jello, Bananas, mashed, (lg) crushed pineapple, Frozen strawberries, undrain, Boiling water, 
Mix Jello and 2 cups boiling water and let cool. Add mashed bananas,  pineapple, and undrained frozen strawberries. Put half the mixture into  13" X 9" X 2" pan and chill 30-45 minutes. Spread sour cream on top. Add  remaining fruit & Jello mixture, chhill, and cut into squares.    Mrs. H. Alexander Perry    From a book of recipes compiled by members and friends of the LONDONTOWN  PUBLIK HOUSE ASSEMBLY, EDGEWATER, MD 21037.
