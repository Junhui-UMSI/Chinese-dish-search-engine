Barbecue Sauce for Chicken
White vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Jar (6-ounce) prepared, Butter or margarine, 
Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat. Stir in remaining ingredients;  remove from heat. Cover and refrigerate sauce until ready to use. Heat  thoroughly, and use to baste chicken when grilling.    Makes about 4 cups.
