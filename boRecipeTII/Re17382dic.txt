Walnut Cottage Pudding
Baking-powder, Egg, well beaten, Flour, Vanilla, Salt, Butter, Sugar, Pecans, milk, 
Cream butter with sugar.  Add egg.  Beat thoroughly. Sift flour, measure,  and sift with baking-powder and salt. Add alternately with milk to first  mixture. Add nuts and flavoring. Mix thoroughly. Pour into shallow,  well-oiled baking pan.  Bake in moderate oven (375 F) 30 minutes. Serve  with vanilla or hard sauce. 6 servings.    The Household Searchlight
