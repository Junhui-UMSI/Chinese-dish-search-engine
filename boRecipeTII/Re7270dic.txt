Pork Chop Casserole 1
Sharp cheddar cheese, Sour cream, Sargento 6 Cheese Italian, salt and pepper, Potatoes, Dried dill weed, Peanut oil, Water, Cream of mushroom soup, Dill weed, Pork chops, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.    Brown pork chops seasoned with dill weed, salt and pepper in oil.    Place chops in a baking dish. Top with potatoes.    Combine other ingredients, except cheddar cheese, and pour over potatoes.    Bake for 1 1/2 hours or until pork chops are tender.    Sprinkle cheddar cheese over the top and return to oven to melt and brown  cheese. About 5 minutes to melt and 5 minutes under boiler to brown cheese.
