Peanut Butter Clusters
White almond bark, Dry roasted unsalted peanuts, Mini marshmallows, Creamy peanut butter, Rice krispies, 
Microwave in large bowl bark & peanut butter for 2 minutes. Stir well. Add  marshmallows. Microwave 1 minute. Stir well. Stir in rice krispies and  peanuts. Drop by teaspoon onto waxed paper. Keep refrigerated.    Makes a ton. I usually make 1/2 a batch.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 15 November 96    Date:    Sat, 16 Nov 1996 10:25:10 -0500    From:    Susan Fields <sdfields@MAGICNET.NET>
