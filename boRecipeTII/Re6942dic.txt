Poor Mans Halibut Thermidor
Sherry, Paprika, Mushrooms, Pepper, Cooked halibut, Onions, Chicken bouillon, Bread crumbs, Butter, milk, Flour, Paprika, Worchestershire sauce, Egg yolk, Parmesan cheese, 
Saute onions and mushrooms in butter. Add flour, pepper and paprika and  blend. Remove from heat, add milk, broth and worcestershire sauce. Boil for  1 minute. Add egg yolk slowly, then sherry. Mix in the halibut and pour  into 6 individual dishes and sprinkle with parmesan cheese and bread  crumbs, and a dash of paprika. Bake at 450 degrees F. for 5-10 minutes, or  till heated through. Garnish with fresh parsley.    Source: Alaska Seafood Cookbook Reprinted by permission from the Alaska  Seafood Marketing Institute
