Hot Apple/pear Chutney
Or more green chiles, Tart cooking apples or, Green pears, Salt, Seedless Raisins, Head garlic; peeled, Inch of fresh ginger, Vegetable oil, Mustard seeds, Peppercorns, Cumin powder, Turmeric, Vinegar, Sugar, 
Seed and chop the chile peppers. Peel, core and slice the apples; sprinkle  them with the salt and set aside. Finely mince half the garlic and ginger.  Finely slice the other half. Heat the oil, put in all the ginger and garlic  and fry until fragrant. Add the other spices and the chopped chiles. Cook  gently for a few min and add the apples or pears, raisins, vinegar and  sugar. Stir and simmer on low for about 30 minutes. Cool and bottle.
