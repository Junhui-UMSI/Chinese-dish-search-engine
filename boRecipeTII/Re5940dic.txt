Pistachio Ice Cream-2
Almond Flavoring, Peaches; Frozen, Peeled,, Evaporated Skim Milk; Frozen, Sugar, Green Food Coloring, Vanilla, 
Partially thaw milk and peaches. Chop in chunks with knife and place in  chilled metal bowl. Add extract, sugar and food coloring. Mix on low speed  till smooth. Beat on high speed until volume is doubled. Place in freezer  until firm. (or you may freeze in your ice cream maker) Serves 4.
