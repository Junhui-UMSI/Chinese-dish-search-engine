Terrys Turtle Cake
Pecans, Caramel Candy, Chocolate chips, Margarine, Evaporated milk, Box Chocolate Cake Mix, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    Prepare cake batter according to box directions.  Pour 1/2 of batter into a  9 x 13" pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.    Melt caramels, margarine, and evaporated milk.  Blend well, and pour  mixture over the hot cake.  Sprinkle chocolate chips and pecans over  caramel mix.  Top with rest of cake batter, and bake for an additional 15  minutes in a 350 degree oven.    Frost with favorite chocolate frosting, if desired.
