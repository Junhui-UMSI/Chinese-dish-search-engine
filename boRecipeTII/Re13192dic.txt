Worcestershire Butter Sauce
Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, Lemon juice, Parsley, Butter or margarine, 
Melt the butter in a small saucepan and then add the remaining  ingredients.  Heat thoroughly and serve over fish or vegetables.    "More Recipes from the Backs of Boxes, Bottles, Cans & Jars", by Ceil Dyer.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sauces1.zip
