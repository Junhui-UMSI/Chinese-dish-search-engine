Trout Fricasse

EMERIL LIVE SHOW #EM1A38    Adapted from Louisiana Real and Rustic by Emeril Lagasse    Seasont the fillets with Essence. In a large saute pan, melt the butter.  Stir in the flour, stirring constantly for 5 to 6 minutes for a medium  brown roux. Add the onions, celery, and peppers. Season with salt, pepper,  and cayenne. Cook for 6 to 7 minutes, stirring constantly, or until the  vegetables are wilted. Add the water and stir until the mixture thickens, 6  to 7 minutes. Add the lemon juice and lay the trout in the pan and baste  with the sauce. Cover and cook for 6 to 7 minutes or until the fish flakes  easily with a fork. Add the parsley and remove from the heat. Serve over  white rice.    Yields: 4 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 28, 1998
