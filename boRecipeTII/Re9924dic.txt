Pure Di Olive Nere Liguriane (Spicy Ligurian "Caviar")
Paste Recipe, Red pepper flakes, Salt, to taste, Olive paste, see Black Olive, Capers, 
In a small bowl or in a food processor, blend the olive paste, red pepper  flakes & capers to make a moist & spreadable mixture.  Season with salt.  Use the pure immediately or transfer to a jar, cover with a thin layer of  olive oil & refrigerate it for up to 2 weeks. Serve spread on crostini.
