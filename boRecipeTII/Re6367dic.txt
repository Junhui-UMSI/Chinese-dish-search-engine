Play Clay for Kids
Cornstarch, Water, Baking soda, 
Mix corn starch and baking soda, add water, mix. Bring to a boil, stirring  constantly. Thicken to consistency of mashed potatoes.    WARNING:  This is NOT edible.    Form clay into desired shaps, let dry for 36 hours, color with paint or  felt-tip marker. Coat with SPRAY shellac or clear nail polish.
