Vanilla Variety
milk, Vanilla ice cream, Vanilla, Vanilla Milk Shake:, 
Place ice cream in a blender container; add milk and vanilla. Cover; blend  until mixture is smooth. Pour into glasses. Serve immediately. Vanilla  Soda: 1 to 2 c. vanilla ice cream 1 c. carbonated water, ginger ale or  lemon-lime soda, chilled Add a large spoonful of ice cream to a chilled 14  to 16-ounce glass. Add enough soda to half fill the glass; stir gently to  mix. Add another large scoop of ice cream; fill remainder of glass with  soda.
