Prune Rye Bread
Baking Powder, Egg, Salt, Cooked prunes; chopped, Sifted wheat flour, Sugar, Sifted rye flour, milk, 
Sift dry ingredients together. Combine egg and milk, add to flour mixture,  stirring only until well mixed, stir in prunes. Turn into greased loaf pans  and bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour. Makes 2 loaves.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Russellyn Crombie <DJJRS@AOL.COM> on Sep 5, 1997
