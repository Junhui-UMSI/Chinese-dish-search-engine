Pine Nut Cookies
Butter or, Superfine sugar, Flour, Heavy cream, Eggs, Pine nuts, 
Beat the eggs well, then add sugar and continue to beat until the mixture  is pale and thick; approximately seven to 10 minutes. Stir in the flour and  pine nuts, and then add the butter or cream. On a floured board, spread out  the dough by hand to avoid crushing the pine nuts, and cut it into rounds  with a glass. Place the rounds on buttered or oiled baking sheets and bake  them in a preheated 400 deg. F oven for 30 minutes, or until delicately  browned.
