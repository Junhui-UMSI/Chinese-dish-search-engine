Pineapple-Papaya-Mango Salsa
Cayenne pepper, Lime juice, Black Pepper, Small white onion, pineapple, Mangos, papaya, 
Date: Fri, 24 May 96 07:17:47 PDT    From: eboyd@shentel.net    Dice fruits and onion into small pieces. Toss together in a bowl with lime  juice. To spice up salsa, add a dash of cayenne pepper or minced jalapeno  pepper to taste. this should be done a hour before dinner to give a chance  for the flavors to meld. great way to liven up grilled or broiled meats,  and fish and chicken.
