Prune Bar Cookies
See Below:, 
Preheat oven to 400F.  In a large mixing bowl, combine: 1 1/4 cups flour  1/2 tsp. salt 1/3 cup sugar 1/2 cup butter,softened) 1 tsp. lemon zest  Blend well at low speed of mixer until particals are fine. Press into an  ungreased 8 inch square pan. Bake for 12 minutes. Remove from oven.  Sprinkle evenly with: 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips 6 oz. chopped prunes    Topping: 3/4 cup sliced almonds 1/2 cup sugar 1/3 cup half & half 1/4 cup  unsalted butter    Place topping ingredients in a small sauce pan and bring to a boil. Boil  for 3 minutes, stirring occasionall. Pour ober prune and chocolate pieces  and continue baking for 12 minutes or until lightly browned. While warm,  cut into bars.
