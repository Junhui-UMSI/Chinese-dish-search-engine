Anchoade
Garlic, Basil leafs, olive oil, Vinegar, Anchovies; 5 oz, Peanut oil, Water; if needed, 
Wash anchovies to remove salt. Place enchovies, garlic and basil in a small  food processor. Process adding oil as for mayonnaise. Add vinegar and  process 1/2 minute. If too thick add a little water. Chill    Based on Barbara Kafkas " Party Foods"    NOTES : Great with toasts or crudites.    Recipe by: Miriam P.
