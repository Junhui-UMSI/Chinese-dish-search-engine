Pineanna Nut Cake
Butter or margarine,, Cinnamon, Vanilla, FROSTING, Bananas, chopped, Cake flour, Vanilla, Pecans, chopped, Pineapple, crushed in juice, Chopped pecans for garnish, PINEANNA CAKE, Vegetable oil, Cream cheese, softened, Sugar, Powdered sugar, Salt, Baking soda, Eggs, slightly beaten, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour 3 (9- by 1 1/2-inch) round  pans. Mix flour, sugar, baking soda, salt and cinnamon in large bowl. Add  oil and eggs. Stir until all dry ingredients are moistened; do not beat.  Stir in bananas, 1 cup pecans, the vanilla and pineapple with juice. Divide  batter evenly among prepared pans. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until toothpick  inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans. Cool  completely on wire racks. For frosting, beat cream cheese and butter in  large bowl on medium speed until smooth. Beat in powdered sugar and vanilla  until light and fluffy. Fill layers and frost side and top of cake. Cut  cake using sharp knife. Refrigerate any remaining cake.
