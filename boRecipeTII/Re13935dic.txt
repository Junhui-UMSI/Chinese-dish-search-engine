Wine Marinade or Basting Sauce for Beef
Cooking oil, Soy sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Dry mustard, Salt, Fresh ground pepper, Dry red wine, Dried parsley flakes, Fresh Lemon Juice, Garlic seasoning, 
Combine all ingredients in a covered container. Marinate steaks for 3 hours  (6 hours if refrigerated). Marinade can also be used as a basting sauce  when grilling.    If you are rotissing a beef roast, marinade can be used as a basting sauce.  Place a shallow baking pan beneath the meat. Baste the roast every 20  minutes.
