Beef Topping for Cooked Rice #2
Lean beef, Pepper, Peanut oil, Fresh ginger root, Light soy sauce, Sugar, Bundle preserved turnip, 
1. Mince or grind beef. Wash and shred preserved turnip; shred ginger root.  Then combine and blend with remaining ingredients.    2. Leave the cooked rice still in its pot and spread the beef mixture over.  Cook, covered, 2 minutes more over low heat; then serve.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4.
