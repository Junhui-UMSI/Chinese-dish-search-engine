Cranberry Crunch Dessert
Quick Oats, Firmly packed light brown, Unsifted flour, Corn oil margarine, Cranberry-Pear Sauce; (see, 
Grease 8x8x2 (inches) baking pan. In medium bowl, stir together oats, sugar  and flour. With pastry blender or two knives cut in margarine until coarse  crumbs form. Place 1 1/2 cups of the oat mixture evenly in bottom of  prepared pan. Spread with Cranberry-Pear Sauce. Top with remaining oat  mixture. Bake in 250o F oven 45 minutes or until lightly browned and  bubbly. If desired, serve warm with vanilla ice cream. Makes 6 to 9  servings.
