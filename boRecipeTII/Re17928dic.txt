Amana Coleslaw
Sugar, cabbage, Celery seed, horseradish, carrots, Mayonnaise, white onion, White vinegar, Lemon pepper, celery, 
In a medium bowl, combine cabbage, carrots, celery and onion. In small  bowl, combine the mayonnaise, lemon pepper, horse- radish, sugar, vinegar  and celery seed. Add to vegetable mixture. Mix well. Cover and refrigerate  at least 1 hour. Makes 6 (2/3 cup) servings.
