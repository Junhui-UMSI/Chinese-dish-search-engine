Almond Float
Unflavored gelatin, Almond extract, Water, (16 oz) peach slices, (15 oz) lychees, Sugar, Maraschino cherries, cut, milk, Into halves, 
Soften gelatin in 1/4 cup of the water. In a sauce pan, bring  remaining 1 cup water to a boil. Add softened gelatin and stir until  gelatin is dissolved. Stir in sugar, milk and almond extract. Pour  into an 8 inch square pan and chill until firm. To serve, drain  peaches, saving the liquid. Cut peaches into smaller pieces. Put back  into liquid and add lychees and cherries. Cut almond gelatin into  diamond shapes. Carefully put into fruit mixture.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
