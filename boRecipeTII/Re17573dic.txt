Almond Fried Ice Cream
Scoops vanilla ice cream, Oil for deep frying, Sliced almonds, Tangerine sauce, 
Scoop the ice cream in advance; return to freezer until extremely hard.  Heat the cooking oil in a deep-fryer. Roll each ice-cream ball in the  tangerine sauce then in the sliced almonds, coating thoroughly. Deep-fry  for a few seconds only. Serve immediately.
