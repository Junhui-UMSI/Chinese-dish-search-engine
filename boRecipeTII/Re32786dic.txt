Sweet Potato Pie W Bourbon
Lemon juice, WENDY CERACCHE DBCP84B-----, Butter/marg., Nutmeg, Bourbon whiskey, Sweet potato -- mashed, Sugar, Eggs -- beaten, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Use an unbaked pie shell. Mix all ingredients  together and pour in unbaked pie shell. Once placed in oven, immediately  turn down the temperature to 325 degrees and then bake for 45 minutes or  until a knife inserted comes clean.
