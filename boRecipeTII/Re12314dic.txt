Zucchini Pizza Strips
Fresh basil leaves; cut into, Carrot; grated, Tomato; cored, coarsely, Zucchini; rinsed, ends, Grated mozzarella cheese, 
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F    2. Cut the zucchini lengthwise into flat strips 1/4 inch thick. Bring 1/2  inch water to a boil in a skillet. Add the zucchini strips, cover and cook  for 3 minutes. With a slotted spatula, remove the strips to a baking dish  lined with foil.    3. Sprinkle the zucchini with tomato, carrot, and basil. Top each with some  cheese. Bake 20 minutes or until the cheese has melted. Let the pizzas cool  slightly before serving    Recipe by: Baby Lets Eat, p. 64-65 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #702 by  Diana Stephens <mdstephe@ix.netcom.com> on Jul 31, 1997
