Peanut Butter n Carrot Cookies
Granulated sugar, Egg, Vanilla, All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Shredded carrots, Salt, 100% bran cereal, Chopped peanuts or, Sunflower seeds, Shortening, Peanut butter, Brown sugar, 
Cream peanut butter, shortening, sugars, egg and vanilla until light and  fluffy. Mix flour, baking soda and salt; blend into creamed mixture. Stir  in cereal, carrots and peanuts. Form into 1-inch balls; place on baking  sheet, about 2 inches apart. Flatten with fork. Bake at 350F (180C) for 10  to 12 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool on racks. Store in airtight  container.
