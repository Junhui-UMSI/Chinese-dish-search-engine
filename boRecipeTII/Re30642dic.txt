Tamale Pie
Garlic cloves; pressed, Green pepper; chopped, (Original), Chili powder, EDEN Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Sea salt, Organic whole wheat flour, EDEN Hot Pepper Sesame Oil, Onion, EDEN Organic Pinto Beans, Fresh or frozen corn, (more to taste), Cumin, Corn meal, FILLING, EDEN Spaghetti Sauce, Baking Powder, (15 oz. cans), drained, EDENSOY Or EDENSOY EXTRA, EDEN Barley Malt, Chopped olives, TOPPING, 
FILLING: Heat oil, saute onions and garlic until translucent.  Add  remaining  ingredients, saute for 2 minutes, and set aside.  Prepare topping.    TOPPING: Sift dry ingredients together. In a separate bowl, mix liquids.  Combine wet and dry ingredients. Place filling in oiled deep dish pie  plate. Pour cornmeal topping gently over top of filling. Bake at 350 F  for 40 minutes.
