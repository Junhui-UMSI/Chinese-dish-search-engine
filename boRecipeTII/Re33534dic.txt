Swamp Punch
Orange; thinly sliced, Orange sherbet; slightly, Ice cubes, Pkg lime-flavor gelatin, Lime; thinly sliced, Cold orange juice, Cold seltzer, Boiling water, 
Stir boiling water into gelatin in large bowl at least 2 minutes until  completely dissolved. Stir in orange juice. Cool to room temperature.    Just before serving, pour into punch bowl. Add cold seltzer and ice cubes.  Place scoops of sherbet and fruit slices in punch.
