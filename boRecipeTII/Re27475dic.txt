Tofu "Cheese" Sauce
Tamari, Nutritional yeast flakes, Water, Basil, dried, Garlic clove; peeled, Lemon juice,freshly squeezed, Pepper, Tahini, raw, Turmeric, Tofu, firm; crumbled, 
Put all of the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth. Serve  this sauce warm or at room temperature.     May All Be Fed by John Robbins/MM by DEEANNE
