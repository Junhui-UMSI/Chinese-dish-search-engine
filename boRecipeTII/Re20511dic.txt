Apple Cake Brownies (Ginger)
APPLE SLICED #10, NUTS MIX SHELL #10, CAKE MIX GINGER BREAD, RAISINS #10, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN                  TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  PLACE GINGERBREAD MIX IN MIXER BOWL. PREPARE ACCORDING TO INSTRUCTIONS  ON CONTAINER. SEE RECIPE CARD G-G-3, GUIDELINES FOR USING CAKE MIXES FOR  MORE DETAILED INSTRUCTIONS.    2.  ADD APPLES, NUTS, AND RAISINS TO MIXTURE. DO NOT OVERMIX. MIXTURE  WILL BE THICK.    3.  SPREAD ONE HALF OF MIXTURE INTO EACH GREASED AND FLOURED PAN.    4.  BAKE ABOUT 40 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE.    5.  COOL; CUT 6 BY 9.    NOTE: 1.  IN STEP 5, 1 LB (1/2-NO.10 CN) CANNED DEHYDRATED APPLES MAY BE  SUBSTITUTED FOR CANNED,SLICED APPLES. COMBINE APPLES AND 3 1/2 QT WATER;  BRING TO A BOIL; SIMMER 10 MINUTES. COOL, DRAIN, AND CHOP.    2.  IN STEP 5, 7 LB 14 OQ A.P. FRESH APPLES MAY BE SUBSTITUTED FOR  CANNED, SLICED APPLES. WASH, CORE, PARE, AND CHOP APPLES.    3.  IN STEP 5, 6 LB 12 OZ (1-NO.10 CN) CANNED APPLESAUCE OR 1 LB 8 OZ  (1-NO.2 1/2 CN)CANNED,INSTANT APPLESAUCE RECONSTITUTED WITH 3 QT WATER MAY  BE SUBSTITUTED FOR CANNED APPLES.
