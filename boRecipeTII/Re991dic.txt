Italian Soup
Beef Stock; Homemade/Canned, Dry Red Wine, Lean Turkey Sweet Sausage, Onion, Garlic, Carrots; Sliced, Celery, Green Bell Pepper; Chopped, Chopped Tomatoes or 16 Oz  can, Tomato sauce, Fresh Oregano, Sprig Thyme; Several, Tortellini; Spinach-Filled,, Freshly Chopped Basil;  garnish, Freshly Chopped Parsley; garnish, 
In a stock pot, heat stock and wine. Add sausage and simmer for 10 min  until sausage is cooked. Remove sausage. Add onion, garlic, carrots,  celery, green bell pepper, tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano and thyme. Cook  until vegetables are tender, about 30 min. Slice sausage into think rounds  and add to the stock with tortellini. cook an additional 20 min or until  tortellini are cooked.    To Serve: Ladle into serving bowls and garnish with basil and parsley and a  little cheese.    Quick and Easy: Use frozen packages of chopped onion, carrot, celery and  green pepper.    Serves: 8    According to book (per serving): Cal 250; Total Fat 4g; Sat Fat ~~lt1g; Sod  637mg; CFF 13%    This was very good.
