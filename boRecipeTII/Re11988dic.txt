Red and White Strawberry Salad
(10-oz) frozen strawberries, (20-oz) crushed pineapple;, Boiling water, Commercial sour cream, Crisp greens, Bananas; mashed, (3-oz) strawberry-flavored, 
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Add partially thawed strawberries. Stir  well, then add bananas and pineapple.  Place half of mixture in 13x9x2-inch  dish. Put into refrigerator to congeal. Allow other half to thicken  slightly, but remain at pouring consistency. When refrigerated half is set,  top with sour cream. Add remaining fruit mixture and return to  refrigerator. Cut in squares; serve on crisp greens. Yield: 12 to 15  servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.
