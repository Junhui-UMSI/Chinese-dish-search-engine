Another Tuna Casserole
Whole mushrooms, drained, Celery, finely chopped, Solid white tuna, drained, Green pepper, finely chopped, Can chow mein noodles, Cream of mushroom soup, Dash of garlic powder, 
Combine everything in a casserole dish. Reserve some of the chow mein  noodles for the topping. Bake at 350F for 25 minutes. Top with  remaining noodles and return to oven for 5 min.
