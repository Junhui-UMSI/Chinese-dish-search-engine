Swiss Egg Cakes
Flour -- whole-wheat, Soy powder, Swiss cheese, Salt, Sugar substitute, Milk -- nonfat (skim), Milk,non-instant -- skim*, Eggs, Oil -- sesame, 
Preparation Time: 0:15 1. Mix together flour, powdered milk, soy powder,  salt, and sugar substitute. 2. Add skim milk, swiss cheese and eggs and  beat well. 3. Put 2 tablespoons of oil in large skillet and pour in 1/4 cup  of batter. Saute on both sides until browned. 4. Use remaining batter the  same way.
