Vickis Fresh Fruit Ice Cream
Pureed fruit (choice), Food coloring - optional, Vanilla, Eagle milk (14 oz), Half & Half, 
Combine all ingredients in ice cream freezer. Freeze according to  manufacturers directions. Makes 1 1/2 quarts.    This is the best fruit ice cream. Great for picnics because you dont have  to cook it.    Source: Vicki Spurgeon Walker From the collection of: Joan Mershon
