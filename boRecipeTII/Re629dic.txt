German Potato Soup with Dumplings
Margarine, Parsley, DROP DUMPLINGS, Egg, Water, Salt, Flour, JUDY LAUSCH DGSV43A, COUNTRY WOMAN 01/93, Potatoes, Celery, Onion, Water, Salt, Pepper, 
In a kettle, combine first six ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat  and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 1 hour. With a potato masher,  puree most of the vegetables. For dumplings, combine egg, water, salt and  flour. Stir until smooth and stiff. Drop by teaspoonsful into the boiling  soup. Cover and simmer until the dumplings are cooked through , about 10-15  minutes. Serve with a pat of margarine and sprinkle with parsley. Yield: 6  servings.
