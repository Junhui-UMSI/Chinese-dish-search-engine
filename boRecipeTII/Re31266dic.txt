Bananas and Limes with Ginger Ale
Banana, Zest of 2 limes, Ginger ale, juice of 2 limes, Crushed ice, Sugar, 
Puree the bananas in a blender with the lime zest and juice and a little  ginger ale. Taste and add sugar, if desired. place crushed ice in the  bottom of each glass, pour the mixture over and top up with more ginger  ale.
