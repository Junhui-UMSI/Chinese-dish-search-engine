White Clam Sauce for Pasta
clams, Salt to taste, Butter, White wine, parsley, fresh, garlic, olive oil, Black Pepper, 
Cook garlic in butter and olive oil until limp. Add clams, wine, parsley,  and pepper, and simmer until slightly thickened. Add salt if necessary to  taste.    Posted to rec.food.recipes by jmartine@zeus.aix.calpoly.edu (Joe Martinez)  on 1994g, .
