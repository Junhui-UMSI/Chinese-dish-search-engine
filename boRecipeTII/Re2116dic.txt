Apple Spice Cake
Butter, Sugar, Cinnamon, Golden delicious, Brown sugar, Egg whites, Skim milk, Applesauce, Vanilla extract, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven to 350 F. Spray loaf pan with nonstick cooking spray and dust  with flour. Cream 2 tablespoons softened margarine and sugar at medium  speed of an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add egg whites and beat  at medium speed 2 minutes. Add milk. Combine 1 1/3 cups flour and baking  powder, and add to creamed mixture, stirring just until moistened. Spread  into prepared pan and arrange apple slices in single layer in 3  rows,pressing gently into batter. Cake will be small. In small bowl,  combine 3 tablespoons flour, brown sugar, remaining cinnamon and chilled  margarine. Mix until crumbly, and sprinkle over apples. Bake 45 minutes or  until brown. Make 6 to 8 servings.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by tbankerd@leading.net on Sep 06, 1998
