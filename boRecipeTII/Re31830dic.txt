Szechwan Chicken
Dry sherry, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Soy sauce, chicken breasts, Ground ginger, Scallions, Vinegar, Cornstarch, crushed red pepper, Salted peanuts, 
Directions: combine cornstarch and 1 T of soy sauce. Add chicken to coat.  Combine the other 2 T of soy sauce, the sherry, sugar and vinegar and set  aside.    Heat the oil in your wok, add the red pepper and cook until it turns black.  Add the chicken and cook for 2 minutes, remove the chicken. Add scallions  and ginger and stir fry for 1 minute. Return the chicken to the wok and  cook for another 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the soy sauce mixture  and stir for 1 minute then add the peanuts to the mixture and stir them in.  Serve over rice.    This dish is fast and easy and tastes GREAT!  Enjoy.
