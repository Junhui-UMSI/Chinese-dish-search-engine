Philadelphian Soft Bread Pretzels
water, All purpose flour, Dry yeast, Sugar, 
Combine water, yeast and sugar and let stand untiil yeast has dissolved and  is foamy.  Mix in flour and knead for 7-8 minutes. Let dough rise covered,  in a greased bowl in a warm place, until double in bulk. Form into sticks  or pretzels and place on greased cookie sheets. Apply a thin egg wash (1  egg yolk diluted with 1-2 tbsp water or milk) Sprinkle with coarse salt.  Allow pretzels to rise until almost double in bulk. Bake in preheated 475 F  oven for about 10 minutes. Cool and serve.
