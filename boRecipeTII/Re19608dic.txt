Annabelles Potato Chip Cookies
& 2 tbsp. flour, Nuts, Sugar, Egg yolks, Broken chips, Sticks oleo, Vanilla, Baking soda, 
Mix all together. Roll into balls, size of walnut. Press with a fork. Bake  15 minutes in 350 degree oven. Potato chips crush nicely in food processor.    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Feb 24, 1998
