Tex-Mex Pizza
Tomatoes, diced, Sour cream, Cheddar cheese, shredded, (12-inch) pizza dough, Chopped parsley, Chopped jalapeno pepper, Green onions, chopped, Garlic, Grated Parmesan cheese, Avocado, chopped, 
Spoon tomatoes over pizza dough, leaving a 1/2" border.  Top with  jalapenos, garlic and onion. Sprinkle with cheeses and season with salt and  pepper to taste.  Bake in 500 degree oven for 10 to 14 min. until bottom of  crust is golden brown.  Top with avocado, a dollop of sour cream and  parsley.  Pass extra sour cream.
