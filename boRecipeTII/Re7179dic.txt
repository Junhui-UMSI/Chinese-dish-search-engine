Pork and Chicken Adobo
Pork, garlic, Black pepper, water, Chicken livers, Native vinegar, Soy sauce, Salt to taste, Chicken, , Water, 
Cut chicken into serving pieces and the pork into 2 inch squares. Place in  a saucepan and add vinegar, black pepper, salt and garlic. Let stand for  about 1 hour. Remove chicken.    Boil and then simmer pork first in the mixture until almost tender. Add  chicken and simmer.  When this sauce mixture becomes dry add about 1/2 cup  water. Simmer for about 30 minutes or until meats are almost tender. Fish  out the garlic from the adobo sauce and fry in a little lard. Remove pork  and chicken from adobo sauce and brown. Add soy sauce and the adobo sauce  to the meat.  Continue cooking until tender. Pound the liver and combine it  with 2 tablespoons of water. Add to sauce to thicken. Simmer for about five  minutes.    From "Favorite Filipino Recipes" by Pat Limjuco Dayrit. Paul Hamlyn Pty  Limited.  Dee Why West, New South Wales. 1975.
