The Green Godess Dip
Salt And Pepper -- to taste, Sour cream, Dill -- chopped fine, Soft White Cheese (9% 5% Or, Possible, Vinegar, Lemon Juice -- fresh if, Parsley -- chopped fine, Green Onion -- chopped fine, % Fat), Mayonnaise, 
Combine all ingredients in a small bawl. Stir well. Serve chill.    Recipe By     : Yaron Blachman
