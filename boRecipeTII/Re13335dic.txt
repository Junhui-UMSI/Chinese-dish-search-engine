Yam and Cranberry Casserole
Sugar, Nutmeg, Cranberries (3 cups) --, Fresh,whole, Mace, Orange juice (or brandy), Cinnamon, Orange -- sliced, Yams -- drained, Pecan halves, 
Wash cranberries. Combine with sugar, orange slices, pecan halves, orange  juice and spices in a 2 quart casserole.  Bake, uncovered, at 375 for 30  minutes. Stir yams into cranberry mixture; bake until hot through, about 15  minutes.
