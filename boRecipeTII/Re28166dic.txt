Baked Ducks
Apple, Ducks, Butter, Onion, Orange, Cooking wine, 
Salt and pepper ducks. Stuff each with 1/4 orange, 1/4 apple and 1/4 onion;  place in roasting pan. Combine butter and wine. Pour over ducks. Bake at  350 for 2 to 2-1/2 hours or until tender, basting every 15 minutes. Serve  with wild rice, a vegetable and an orange-flavored congealed salad.
