Taboulleh
cucumber, Fresh mint, green pepper, bergo, Black pepper, Red onion, Juice of half a lemon, 
Cook the wheat in twice its volume of boiling water for 10 minutes or leave  it to stand in the same amount of just boiled water for 15 minutes until  the grains have swelled and softened. Drain and squeeze out the moisture  and place the wheat in a bowl. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
