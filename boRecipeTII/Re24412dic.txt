Tropical Cooler
Orange juice, Pineapple juice, Honey, Cran-apple juice, Maraschino cherry juice, Ginger ale, Lemon juice, 
Blend all ingredients except ginger ale. Just before serving, add ginger  ale. If desired, for added color, in each serving put 1 mint leaf, 1 red or  green maraschino cherry, or both. Serves 6.    Recipe by: Home Cooking--July 1996 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #752 by  L979@aol.com on Aug 21, 1997
