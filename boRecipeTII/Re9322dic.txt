Puff Pudding
Lemon juice, Egg whites, stiffly beaten, milk, Flour, Grape-Nuts cereal, Lemon Rind, Butter, Egg yolks, Sugar or honey, 
Heat oven to 325 deg. F.  Beat butter with sugar and lemon rind in large  bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in egg yolks. Stir in lemon juice, flour,  cereal and milk. (Mixture will look curdled.)    Fold in beaten egg whites.    Pour into greased 1-quart baking dish.  Place dish in pan of hot water.  Bake 1 hour and 15 minutes or until top springs back when lightly touched.  When done, pudding has a cake-like layer on top with custard below. Serve  warm or cold. Makes 6 servings.
