Quick Soak Method for Beans
INFO, 
for each cup of beans add 3 cups of water. slowly bring to boil and boil  gently for 2 minutes. Remove from heat, cover and let stand 1 hour. Discard  soaking water and cook beans according to recipe.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #070 by Murray Hackett  <hackett@upanet.cc.uleth.ca> on Mar 10, 1997.
