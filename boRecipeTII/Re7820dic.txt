Portuguese Bean Soup
Clove garlic -- minced, Salt pork -- cut in thick, Diced, Salt, Beef bouillon, Onions -- sliced, Slices, Can tomato paste, Water, Allspice, Dried red beans, Potatoes -- peeled and,
