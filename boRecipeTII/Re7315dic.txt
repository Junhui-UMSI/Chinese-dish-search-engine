Pork Chops and Stuffing
bread, Water, Pork Chops, onion, Butter, Vegetable oil, Poultry seasoning, Water, Mushroom soup, canned, 
Preheat oven to 350 Brown chops and place in a shallow baking dish. (  2-2-1/2 quart ) Lightly mix together bread, onion, butter, 2 T water and  poultry seasoning . Place a mound of stuffing on top of each chop. Blend  soup and 1/3 C of water, pour over chops. Bake uncovered for one hour.                               Enjoy,                               * Jeannie *
