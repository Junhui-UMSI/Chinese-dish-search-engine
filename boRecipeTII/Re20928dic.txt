Apple Onion Turkey Stuffing
Butter, Allspice, Almonds; chopped, Pippin apples; chopped, Nutmeg, Onion, diced, Lemon juice, Bread, crumbs, White wine, 
In frying pan, melt butter and saute onions until they are clear. In a  bowl, combine the bread crumbs with the onion mix. Add apples and wine.  Return to pan and cook 5 minutes longer. Add the remaining ingredients  Stuff into turkey neck and body cavities.
