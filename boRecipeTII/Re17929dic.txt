Amandas Angel Food Cake
Cream of tartar, Flour, Vanilla, Egg white, Sugar, Salt, 
Sift the dry ingredients together 4 times. Beat the egg whites until dry.  Fold dry ingredients into eggs. Add flavoring and bake at 250-F for 1 hour.  This cake should be baked in an ungreased angel food pan. Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
