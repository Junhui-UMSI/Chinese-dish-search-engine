Ambrosia Sherbet
Sugar, Coconut, (6-oz) frozen orange juice, (large) apricots; chopped, (large) crushed pineapple, Ginger ale or champagne, Ripe bananas; mashed, Juice cans water, 
Mix thoroughly all ingredients except gingerale or champagne. Freeze in 1  quart (cardboard) freezer containers with lids. Serve with champagne or  gingerale.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
