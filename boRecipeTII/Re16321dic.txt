Watermelon Slush
Cubed seeded watermelon, Mint sprigs, (optional), Frozen lemonade concentrate,, Sifted powdered sugar, 
Place watermelon in a large bowl; cover and freeze.    Place half of frozen watermelon, half of powdered sugar, and half of  concentrate in a blender, and process until smooth.    Pour mixture into individual glasses. Repeat procedure with remaining  watermelon, powdered sugar, and concentrate. Yield: 7 cups (serving size: 1  cup).    Per serving: 91 Calories; 0g Fat (4% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 23g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 3mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Garnish with mint sprigs, if desired. Serve immediately.
