Quick n Simple Souffle
Nutmeg, Eggs; well beaten, Honey, Non-alum baking powder, AM Multi Blend Flour, Oil (or non-stick spray), Vanilla extract, Milk or milk substitute, 
Preheat oven to 450 F.  Oil a 9" x 9" cake pan. Mix liquid ingredients  together.  Mix dry ingredients together.  Combine mixtures.  Pour batter  into pan and bake 15 minutes.  Serve while hot with honey or syrup and/or  fruit.  Try with fruit yogurt.  Makes 2 large or 4 small servings.
