Blueberry-Banana Pie
Sugar, (8oz) cream cheese, softened, Whipping cream, whipped, Baked 9-inch pastry shell, Bananas, sliced, Blueberry pie filling (I, 
Combine cream cheese and sugar; beat at high speed with an electric mixer  until light and fluffy. Spread evenly in bottom of cooled pastry shell.  Arrange banana slices on cream cheese layer. Spread pie filling over  bananas. Top with whipped cream, and chill.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Julie Bertholf & Simon Wood  <jewel1@ix.netcom.com> on Sep 06, 1998
