Raisin-Walnut Stuffing [For a 12-Pound Turkey]
Onion, Pepper, Seedless raisins, Powdered Sage, Celery, chopped, Granulated chicken bouillon, Salt, Cubed white bread-16 slices, Butter or margarine-1 stick, Water, Walnuts, coarsely chopped, 
1.  Simmer raisins in water in a small saucepan for 1 minute; reserve.    2.  saute celery and onion in butter or margarine until soft in a  large    skillet.  Stir in chicken broth,salt, sage, and pepper.    3.  Add too bread cubes and walnuts in a large bowl; add raisin-water    mixture; toss lightly until evenly moist.    Makes approximately 10 cups.
