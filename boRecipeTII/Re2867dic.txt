Peanut Butter Fudge
Butter, Sugar, Brown sugar, Marshmallows, Peanut butter, Evaporated milk, Vanilla, Salt, 
Cook sugar, butter, milk, and salt to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F). Add  the marshmallows and peanut butter just before removing from the fire. Do  not stir.  Cool to room temperature. Add flavoring. Beat until mixture is  creamy, thick, and will hold its shape when dropped from a teaspoon. Pour  into well-buttered, shallow pan. Cut in squares.
