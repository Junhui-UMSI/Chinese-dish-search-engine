Ratatouille, Second Time Around (Mf)
Half of protean ratatouille, Tuna fish or strips of, Slivered pitted cured black, Lemon zest and juice; to, Basil chiffonade, Salt and crushed red pepper, Washed lettuce leaves; torn, 
Bring the ratatouille out of the fridge to bring back to room temperature.  Perk up and change the flavor with lemon zest and lemon juice, salt and  pepper. On each dinner plate make a wreath of washed, torn lettuce leaves  Make an inner wreath of ratatouille, leaving some room in center of plate  for tuna or chicken. Garnish with black olives and basil chiffonade.
