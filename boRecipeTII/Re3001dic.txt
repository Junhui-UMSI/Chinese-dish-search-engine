Peanut Butter Sundae
Peanut butter, Honey, salt, Wheat germ, milk, Vanilla ice milk, 
Stir peanut butter, honey, milk, and salt together in a saucepan.  Cook  over low heat, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat when peanut butter  is melted.  Sauce should be smooth.  Cool.  Serve sauce over ice milk.  Sprinkle each serving with wheat germ, as desired.
