Potatoes with Bacon and Zucchini
Potatoes, boiled and skinned, Butter, Zucchini, sliced fairly thin, Slices bacon, salt and pepper, olive oil, 
In a skillet, fry the bacon until almost crisp. Drain most of the fat. Chop  the bacon and return to pan. Add sliced zuchini, butter and oil. Sautee  until slightly soft. In a large serving bowl, slice the potatoes about 1/2"  thick. Add bacon-veggie mixture, salt and freshly ground pepper and mix  well. Serve hot or warm. *You may also use sliced onions in this recipe.  Add them with the zuchini.
