Zucchini Bake
SMALL GREEN PEPPER, CHOPPED, ONION, SLICED, RICE (NOT MINUTE RICE), Sugar, CUBED ZUCCHINI, SLICES BACON, RIPE TOMATOS, SLICES VELVEETA CHEESE, Salt and pepper to taste, 
CHOP BACON AND FRY, THEN DRAIN OFF GREASE. LAYER IN CASSEROLE DISH,  ZUCCHINI, GREEN PEPPER, ONION, BACON, RICE, TOMATO, AND CHEESE. IN THAT  ORDER. BAKE AT 350 DEG F. FOR 1 1/2 HOURS.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by scotlyn@juno.com (Daniel S Johnson) on Mar  22, 1998
