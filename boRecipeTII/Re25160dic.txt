Tomato-Pineapple Salsa
Garlic -- peeled and minced, Lime juice, Sliced, Green onions -- thinly, Tomatoes -- seeded and, Diced, Fresh cilantro -- minced, Pineapple -- finely diced, Jalapeno pepper -- minced, 
Combine all ingredients and refrigerate until ready to serve.    Recipe By     : Seattle Times-posted by Mike Key
