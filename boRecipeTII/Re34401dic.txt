Bean Burgers
Garlic powder, Vegetarian refried beans; ca, Millet or brown rice; cooked, Salt, Onion, finely minced; or, Tomato paste, (optional), Whole wheat bread slices, Arrowroot, Oats; quick-cooking, 
Process bread, one slice at a time, in a dry blender to form soft crumbs. M  all ingredients together well. Form into 8 patties. Brown on each side n a  vegetable-oil sprayed non-stick skillet. Serve as is or on whole wheat buns  with lettuce, sprouts and ketchup, if desired.
