Peas with Mushrooms and Dill
Fresh dill, finely chopped, Frozen peas, defrosted, salt and pepper, Mushrooms, cut into slices, Vegetable broth, Onion, finely chopped, Margarine, Vegetable oil, 
Source: Jewish cooking class taught by Bilha Widman    Saute the mushrooms and onions in the oil and margarine till lightly  browned. Add the peas and dill, and salt and pepper to taste.    Add a cup of water, bring to a boil, then cook over a low heat for about       10    minutes. Serve and enjoy.
