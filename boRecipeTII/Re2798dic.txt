Peanut Butter Cookies #05
Smooth peanut butter, Egg, Vanilla, Salt, Brown sugar, Granulated sugar -and-, Granulated sugar -or-, 
Preheat oven to 325?. Mix all ingredients until well blended. Roll into  small balls. Place on an ungreased cookie sheet, 1 inch apart. Press  lightly with a fork to flatten. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes. Yield: 1-1/2  dozen.    MARY M. JENNINGS (MRS. WARREN)    DEWITT, AR    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
