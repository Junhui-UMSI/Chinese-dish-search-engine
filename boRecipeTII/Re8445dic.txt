Potato Walnut Latkes
Vegetable oil, Eggs, Walnuts, Potatos, boiled, Salt and pepper, Margarine (suet), 
Place (suet) margarine in frypan wirh oil and heat well.  Meanwhile, mash  the potatos   add the eggs and walnuts and salt and pepper and mix well.  Fry in pattiesin hot fet until well crusted and golden.  Turn over and fry  until other side is crusted also.
