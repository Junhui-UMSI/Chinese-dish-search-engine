Ramps Piquante
Ramp bulbs *, salt and pepper, Wine vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Grated sharp cheddar cheese, Broth drained from ramp, Butter, Crisp toast, 
* Boiled until tender in salted water & drained (reserve broth). Over low  heat, melt cheese in ramp broth. Add Worcestershire, vinegar, and butter.    Stir ramps into the sauce and reheat.  Add salt and pepper to taste.    Serve hot over crisp toast.    Yield:  6 servings.    Makes a nice brunch dish or lunch, for those sensible enough to try it. <g>  Typed for you by the sensible Cathy Harned
