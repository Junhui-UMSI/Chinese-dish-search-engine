Raspberry Spinach Salad
Salad oil, DRESSING, Sugar, Raspberry vinegar, Raspberry jam, seedless, Macadamia nuts, Raspberries, fresh, Spinach leaves, 
Toast macadamia nuts in 250 degree (F) oven for 30 minutes, flipping and  stirring often. Cool. Mix, spinach, nuts and rasberries in a large bowl.  For Dressing: combine all ingredients in a blender and blend til thick.  Pour dressing over salald and enjoy the taste of spring!  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "M.
