Amber Cider Punch
Sugar, Cinnamon, Allspice, Nutmeg, Apple cider, Frozen orange juice concent, Ginger ale, ===Garnish===, Orange, Cloves, 
Mix sugar and spices. Stir in cider and heat until sugar dissolves. Add  frozen concentrate and stir until melted. Chill.     To serve, add ginger ale and serve over ice in glasses with small orange  wedges or with orange slices studded with whole cloves in a punch bowl.     Makes 5 cups.
