Prairie Fire Spiced Nuts
Sugar, Prairie fire spice, Cinnamon; ground, Butter, Honey, Cloves; ground, 
Toast Pecans in a 350 degree oven for 5-8 min.  Remove from oven add butter  and honey.  Stir to coat.  Add spices.  Stir to coat.  Add half the sugar,  and toss.  Place on waxed paper to cool.  When cool toss with remaining  sugar.   Serve as is or over top Ice Cream.
