Tapioca Thai Style
Sugar, Salt, Coconut milk, Water, Tapioca, quick, 
Bring water to a boil in a 2-quart saucepan. Stir in sugar and salt. Add  tapioca and return to a boil. Stir in coconut milk. Reduce heat to low.  Stir frequently to prevent sticking. Cook for 30 minutes or until  thickened. Serve warm or cold. If serving cold, place in individual bowls  while still warm.
