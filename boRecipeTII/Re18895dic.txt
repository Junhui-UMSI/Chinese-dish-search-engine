Angel Crisps
Cream of tartar, Salt, Sugar, Vanilla, Shortening, Egg, Brown sugar, Flour, Baking soda, 
Cream sugar, brown sugar and shortening. Add vanilla and egg. Blend until  fluffy. Add the dry ingredients; blend. Roll teaspoonfuls into balls. Dip  into water and then into granulated sugar. Lay on cookie sheet, sugar side  up, then flatten with a glass. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
