Israeli Coffee Chicken
Soy sauce, Lemon juice, White vinegar, olive oil, Brown sugar, Broiler fryer, Black coffee, Ketchup, 
Mix liquids and sugar and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 5-10  minutes to reduce. Pour over chicken in a shallow baking dish and bake at  350 uncovered for 1 hour. Turn chicken pieces a few times while baking.    From " The Jewish Cooking Secrets From Here And Far"    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by Pat Hanneman <kitpath@earthlink.net> on Sep  01, 1998,
