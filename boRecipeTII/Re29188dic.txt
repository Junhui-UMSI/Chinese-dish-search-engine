Baked Scallops #1
Butter, Paprika, Butter, Catsup, Pepper, Bay  scallops, Rich, buttery-flavored ?, Green onions, Sugar, 
Date: Wed, 01 May 1996 06:55:25 -0700    From: Dan Schamber <dansch@haven.ios.com>    Rinse Scallops with cold water to remove any remaining shells. Combine  Scallops, cracker crumbs, butter, catsup, salt and pepper in casserole.  Combine green onion and butter; Place on top of scallop mixture. Bake in  Moderate oven at 350 for 25-30 minutes or until golden. Sprinkle with  paprika.
