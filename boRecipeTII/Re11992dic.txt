Red Apple Salad
Red cinamon candy, Sugar, Water, Green Pepper, Cottage cheese, Firm tart apples, Salt, 
Pare and core apples.  Add sugar, salt, and candy to the water. Put over  heat and stir until dissolved. Cook apples slowly in this sirup in covered  pan until just tender, turning occasionally to color evenly. Drain and  chill.  Mix cheese with green pepper and stuff the apples. Serve on  watercress or other dark greens.
