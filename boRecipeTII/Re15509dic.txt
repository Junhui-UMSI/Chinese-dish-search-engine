5 Minute Vegetable Soup ( in Pressure Cooker )
carrots, onions, celery, garlic, Flour, Stewed tomatoes, Tomato puree, Beef bouillon (cubes), water, Barley, Dried basil, Worcestershire Sauce, 
In a 6 qt. pressure cooker sprayed with Pam, saute the carrots, onions and  celery till limp. Dust with the flour. Add all remaining ingredients and  cook under high pressure for 5 minutes. Remove from heat, let pressure drop  naturally, remove lid carefully and enjoy!    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Cynthia Ryan <cdryan@JUNO.COM> on Nov 24, 1997
