Pineapple-Walnut Dip
Sour cream, Pineapple, Crushed, Cottage Cheese, Lemon Zest, Lemon juice, Walnuts, 
Cream the cottage cheese in a blender or food processor or with an electric  mixer.  Blend in the sour cream. Add all the other ingredients and mix  well.  Cover and chill. Makes about 2 3/4 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS:  Vanilla Wafers, Chicken, Pears, Strawberries
