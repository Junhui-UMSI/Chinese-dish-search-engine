Aunt Sarahs Apple Mint Ice Cream
Sweet milk, Bitter chocolate, Apple jelly, Whipping cream, Oil peppermint extract, Egg white, 
~- (Makes 1 1/2 quarts)    Cut chocolate into small pieces. Add to milk and heat until chocolate is  melted, beating occasionally to blend the chocolate with the milk . Cool.  Whip egg white and jelly together until stiff. Add to chocolate milk,  mixing thoroughly. Add mint Flavoring and fold in whipped cream. Stir  mixture thoroughly and chill. Freeze.
