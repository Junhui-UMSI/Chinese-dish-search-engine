Bacon-Wrapped Dried Fruit
Apricots, Dried pears, apples, or, Toothpick, Bacon (3-inch length), 
Wrap dried pears, apples, or apricots in a 3-inch length of bacon. Spear  with a toothpick and broil until bacon is crisp (2 to 3 minutes).    Recipe By     : the California Culinary Academy    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
