Raisin Chicken Santa Fe
Salt, pepper, Skinned, Into strips, Strips, Ground coriander, Garlic, Jalapeno chile, minced, Chicken breast halves,, Defatted chicken broth, Onion, Raisins, Tomato paste, olive oil, Green pepper, sliced into, Sweet red pepper, sliced, 
Cook chicken in oil over medium heat in large skillet until lightly  browned. Turn once. Add garlic, onion and peppers and saute 2 minutes. Mix  in broth, raisins, tomato paste and coriander.    Bring to boil, then reduce heat and simmer, covered, 10 minutes. Uncover  and cook 10 ninutes until chicken is tender and sauce is slightly reduced.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.
