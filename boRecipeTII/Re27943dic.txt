Three Bean and Sausage Soup
Carrots; shredded, Kielbasa; low-fat, Onions; chopped, Canned red beans, Celery, Chicken bouillon granules, Water, Canned white beans, Canned black beans, Canned tomatoes; with juice, Kale, Garlic; minced, Russet potatoes; peeled and, 
Saute sausage, onion, celery and garlic in a 5-quart pot until vegetables  are softened, about 3 minutes. Add bouillon, tomatoes and water. Bring to a  boil, breaking up tomateos with a spoon. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 15  minutes. Meanwhile, wash kale thoroughly, remove stems, and cut into narrow  strips; set aside. Prepare carrots and potatoes.    Stir potatoes and carrots into the pot; cover and simmer 10 minutes. Add  beans and kale; cover and simmer for 15 minutes more or until all of the  vegetables are tender. Mash some beans against the side of the pot with the  back of a large spoon to thicken the soup.
