Tvp-Quick Chili
(16 oz) beans (I used kidney, Salt, Water; (up to 3-1/2), Green pepper; chopped, Garlic, Fresh cilantro leaves;, Chili powder, (14 oz) of EACH diced, Textured-soy-protein, Onion-chopped, 
Combine everything in a large saucepan; heat to boiling. Reduce to a  simmer. Cook for about 15 minutes and serve.    Makes aprox 7-8 cups chili.    Posted to recipelu-digest by Abtaxel <Abtaxel@aol.com> on Mar 5, 1998
