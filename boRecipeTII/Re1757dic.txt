Chick-Pea Salad
Green pepper, julienne, Vegetable oil, Freshly squeezed lemon juice, Garlic, minced, Generous pinches of salt and, Each 19 ounce, chick peas, Chili powder, DRESSING:, Dash of hot pepper sauce, Sweet red pepper, julienne, Green onions, diagonally, 
Drain and rinse chick-peas.  Combine with red and green peppers and green  onions in a bowl.  In a separate bowl, whisk together all dressing  ingredients.  Toss salad with dressing; refrigerate until ready to serve.  Flavor improves if left to marinate overnight. Serves 6-8 Typed in MMFormat  by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Good Friends Cookbook.
