Sweet Potato Banana Pudding
(17 oz.) whole yams; well, Cornstarch, Butter, Ripe banana, Egg, Evaporated skim milk, Vanilla, Grated nutmeg, Sugar, Firm banana; sliced, 
In medium saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch and nutmeg. In food processor  or blender, puree yams and ripe banana with evaporated milk, until smooth.  Pour into saucepan. Cook, stirring constantly, over medium-high heat until  mixture comes to a boil. Gradually stir about 1/2 cup hot mixture into  beaten egg. Return this to the pan and continue cooking and stirring, until  mixture thickens. Remove from heat; stir in butter and vanilla. Cool  slightly. Spoon into dessert dishes and chill. Top with sliced banana.  Makes 4 servings.    Recipe by: Low Fat * No Fat Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #684 by  L979@aol.com on Jul 21, 1997
