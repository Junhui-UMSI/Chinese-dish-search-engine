Baked Pineapple #1
Grated Cheddar, Stick oleo, Pineapple juice, TOPPING, Flour, Stick oleo; melted, Ritz crackers; crushed, Can chunk pineapple; drained, Sugar, Slivered almonds for top, Almond flavoring, 
Mix sugar and flour in bowl; then add remaining ingredients. Spread in a 8  x 8 x 2-inch dish. Combine oleo and crackers for topping. Sprinkle on top  along with some slivered almonds. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes.  Excellent with chicken, turkey and ham. Rave reviews.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 23,  1998
