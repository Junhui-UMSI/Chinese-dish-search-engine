Tangy Scarlet Runner Bean Salad
Garlic, Prepared mustard, Red onion; thinly sliced, olive oil, Salt, Scarlet runner beans; fresh, Red wine vinegar, Paprika, Black Pepper, 
Wash the beans, snap off the ends, and French-cut into lengthwise strips.  Steam briefly, until just tender; drain thoroughly, and place in a large  bowl with the onion and garlic. Toss well.    Combine the remaining ingredients to make a dressing. Pour over the beans,  toss well, and refrigerate for an hour or two before serving to blend  flavors.    Serves 4 or 5.    Source: "Unusual Vegetables: Something New for This Years Garden" by the  editors of Organic Gardening and Farming  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #070 by Linda Place  <placel@worldnet.att.net> on Mar 10, 1997.
