Ranch Beans
Extra lean ground beef,, Vinegar (cider is best), Onion, Brown sugar, Dark red kidney beans,, Ketchup, Sugar, Bacon, cooked, drained and, Mustard, Pork and beans, not rinsed, Lima beans, rinsed and, 
Cook, drain and crumble meats separately and set aside. Mix in large  container ketchup, sugars, vinegar and mustard. Add onions. Add beans, meat  and bacon. Bake at 350 F for 45 minutes.
