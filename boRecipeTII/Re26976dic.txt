Baked Cheese Spread
Cream cheese, softened, Eggs, Thin slices French bread, Paprika, milk, Dried marjoram leaves, Camembert cheese, Salt, Green onions (with tops),, 
Remove rind from Camembert cheese and let cheese stand until room  temperature.    Mix cheeses and remaining ingredients except paprika and bread on low speed  until smooth. Pour into greased 1-quart casserole; sprinkle with paprika.  Bake uncovered in 350F oven until knife inserted 1 inch from edge comes out  clean, 30 to 35 minutes. Serve with French bread. 32 servings; 85 calories  per serving.    Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
