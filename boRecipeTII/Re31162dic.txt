Banana Sweet Potato Puff Casserole
Mashed ripe bananas (3, Egg, Salt, Curry powder, Sour cream, Mashed sweet potatoes, 
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients. Beat with electric mixer until  light and very fluffy. Turn into 1 quart casserole dish. Bake at  350-degrees F. in the oven for 20 minutes or until puffed and lightly  browned.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 262 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 16, 1997
