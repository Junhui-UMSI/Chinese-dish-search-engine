Tuna Melt Pitas
Cheddar cheese, Celery, Green onions, pitas, Paprika, Mayonnaise, white tuna, canned, Green pickle relish, 
Drain and flake tuna.  In bowl, combine tuna, celery, Cheddar, mayonnaise,  onions and relish.    Cut pitas in half to form circles; arrange on baking sheet. Top each half  with heaping tablespoon tuna mixture. Sprinkle with paprika to taste.  (Pitas can be prepared to this point, covered and refrigerated for up to 1  day.)    Bake in 350F oven for 15 minutes or until Cheddar melts. Makes 20  appetizers.
