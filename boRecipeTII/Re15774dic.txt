Abm Foolproof White Bread
Yeast, WATER; WARM, Salt, Flour, bread, Dough enhancer; optional, Sugar, Eggs, Vegetable oil, 
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in order.  Set "baking control" at 11 oclock. Select "white bread" and push Start.    In hot, humid weather, use only 1-1/8 c water.    Tested in DAK 1-1/2 lb R2D2 bread machine.
