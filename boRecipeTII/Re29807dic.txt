Tempura Shrimp
Flour, milk, Cornstarch, Vegetable oil, Eggs, Baking Powder, Salt, Prawn shrimp, 
Blend milk, 3/4 cup flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, eggs & 2  Tbls. oil into a smooth batter. Heat remaining oil to 375. Dip shrimp  into remaining flour, then into batter. Cook in oil 2 1/2-3 minutes.  Serve hot.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
