Twenty-Four Hour Slaw
Prepared mustard, Bermuda onions, Sugar, Salt, Vinegar, salt and pepper, Head of cabbage, Salad oil, Celery seed, 
Shred cabbage; thinly slice the onions. Put together in a large bowl in  alternate layers & sprinkle each layer with salt & pepper very lightly.  Combine sugar, vinegar, celery seed, mustard & salt. Bring to a boil; add  salad oil & pour over cabbage & immediately cover tightly. Keep in  refrigerator for 24 hours before serving.
