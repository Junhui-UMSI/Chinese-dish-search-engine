Raspberry Baverian Cream
Instant vanilla pudding mix, Heavy Whipping Cream, Fresh or frozen raspberries, Crisp chocolate coockies;, Individual seving size, 
Whip yougurt and pudding mix until smooth. Add cream and beat on high until  stiff peaks form. Fold in raspberries.    Serve chilled in dessert glasses with a cookie for garnish.    Recipe by: Heidi Schulz    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Tiina <4Angels@videotron.ca> on Mar 17,  1998
