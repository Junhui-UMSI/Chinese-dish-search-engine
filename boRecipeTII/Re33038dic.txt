Basic Corn Bread
Sugar, Bacon drippings, Buttermilk, Egg, Self-rising white corn meal, 
Mix corn meal, buttermilk, sugar and egg. Melt bacon drippings in small,  cast iron skillet in 500 degree oven. Pour drippings in batter and stir  thoroughly. Pour batter into hot skillet; bake at 500 degrees for 10-15  minutes. Posted to Kitmailbox by Paula Thompson <paulat@adaptsol.com> on  Jul 07, 1997
