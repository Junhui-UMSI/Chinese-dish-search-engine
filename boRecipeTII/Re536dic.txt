Fresh Orange-Cranberry Juice Salad
Cranberry juice cocktail, Lemon-flavored gelatin; 3 oz, Oranges; peeled and cut into, Sour cream, Nuts; chopped, 
Heat cranberry juice cocktail to boiling.  Remove from heat and stir in  gelatin.  Chill until the consistency of unbeaten egg whites. Fold in  orange pieces and juice, sour cream, and nuts.  Chill until firm. Yield: 4  to 6 servings.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, December, 1972. Typed for you by Nancy  Coleman.
