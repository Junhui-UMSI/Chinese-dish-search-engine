Pork Chops with Apple Rings
Pork Chops, Margarine, Brown sugar, Apples -- * see note, Cinnamon, 
* Use apples that are unpeeled, cored, and sliced into rings.    Brown chops on both sides. Place apples rings in greased baking dish.  Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon. Dot with margarine. Place pork chops on  top of apples. Cover. Bake in preheated 350-degree oven for 1-1/2 hours.
