Yummy Bread Pudding
Evaporated milk, Nutmeg, Vanilla extract, Sugar, Cinnamon;, French bread; cubed, Eggs, Raisins, Salt, Water; boiling, 
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. 2. Grease a 1-1/2 cup baking dish, and  place the bread cubes in it. 3. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs. Add the  sugar, salt and milk. Stir in 2 tablespoons of boiling water, then add the  remaining water, cinnamon, nutmeg, raisins and vanilla. Pour the egg  mixture of the bread cubes. 4. Bake the pudding until set, about 45  minutes.  Serve warm, at room temperature or cold.
