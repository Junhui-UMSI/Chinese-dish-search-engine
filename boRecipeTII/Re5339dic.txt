Pineapple Ambrosia
(20-oz) pineapple chunks in, Fat-free plain or, Miniature marshmallows, (20-oz) crushed pineapple, Flaked coconut, (11-oz) water-packed, 
In a large bowl, gently combine the marshmellows, crushed pineapple,  pineapple chunks or tidbits, oranges, yogurt and coconut. Cover and chill  in the refrigerator for at least one hour. Makes 10 side-dish servings.    Calories: 133 Fat: 1g Calories from fat: 7% Cholesterol: 0    NOTES : Leftover ambrosia keeps well in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.
