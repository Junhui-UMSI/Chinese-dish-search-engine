Peaches and Strawberries in Wine
Dry red or white wine; or, Fresh strawberries, Sliced peaches, Lemon juice, Sugar, -(up to), 
Pick strawberries carefully; wash, hull & drain. Peel peaches & slice;  sprinkle with lemon juice to prevent discoloration. Mix fruit together &  combine with sugar. Allow to stand in refrigerator for 1 hour before  serving. Cover (barely) with wine & serve.    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
