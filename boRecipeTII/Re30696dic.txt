Tamarind Dip
Plus, Chicken stock, Soy sauce, Garlic, Ketchup, Tamarind Pulp, Cornstarch, Cold water, Honey, Cilantro, Fresh, Sherry, dry, Pepper, Red, flakes, dried, 
1. Soak tamarind pulp in 1/2 cup boiling stock off the heat for 15 minutes.  Stir & mash it with a fork to help it dissolve. Strain through a fine  strainer into a bowl, pressing on the solids with the back wooden spoon to  extract all the liquid.    2. Combine tamarind liquid, remaining 1/3 cup stock, soy sauce, sherry,  honey & ketchup in a nonreactive saucepan & bring to a simmer over  medium-low heat. Slowly drizzle in the cornstarch mixture & heat, stirring,  until the sauce thickens, about 2 minutes. Off the heat, stir in the garlic  & pepper flakes, Let the sauce stand for 15 minutes, then strain into a  serving bowl & add cilantro.
