Raspberry Streusel Cake
Nutmeg, Sugar, Frozen apple juice, Applesauce, Flour; plus 2 tablespoons, Cinnamon, Multi-grain oatmeal, Baking soda, Raspberries, Brown sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Lightly spray 8-inch baking pan with nonfat cooking  spray. Combine 1 1/2 cups flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon and nutmeg  and mix well. Add applesauce and raspberries to flour mixture and mix until  dry ingredients are moistened. Spread batter into prepared pan.    In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, oatmeal and 2 tablespoons flour. Stir  in juice concentrate and blend until mixture is crumbly.    Sprinkle crumb topping over batter and bake in preheated oven for 25  minutes, until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cool for 15  minutes before cutting.    Serves 8, 168 calories per serving, Dietary fiber 2 grams.    >From Jyl Steinbacks "Recipes for Fat Free Living Desserts".    Typed by Gail Shermeyer <4paws@netrax.net>.
