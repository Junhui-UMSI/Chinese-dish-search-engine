Peanut-Sitting-Pretties
Egg; separated, Salt, Butter or margarine, Vanilla frosting;, Brown sugar; firmly packed, Nuts; finely chopped, Vanilla extract, All-purpose flour, 
1. Blend butter or margarine and sugar in a bowl; stir in egg yolk and  vanilla. Sift flour and salt into bowl; mix well. Chill 1 hour. Roll into  1-inch balls.    2. Dip into slightly beaten egg white. Roll in nuts. Place 1-inch apart on  cookie sheet.    3. Bake in moderate oven (350 degrees) 5 minutes. Press thumb gently in  center of each; bake 5 minutes longer. Cool. Fill with frosting. Garnish  with "M&Ms" Peanut Chocolate Candies.
