Stuffed Buns
Tomato sauce salt to taste;, Garlic powder to taste, Doz medium buns, Cheddar cheese; grated, (Small) chopped olives, Onion, Hamburger, Jar (Medium) pimentos, 
In a large skillet cook hamburger, onions, salt and garlic until meat is  brown. Add olives, pimentos, tomato sauce and cook on medium heat for 20  minutes. Remove insides of buns. Crumble this bread into meat mixture to  soak up any moisture. Turn heat off. Grate and add cheese and flour into  first mixture. Stuff meat into buns. Wrap in foil and freeze. Heat in oven  whenever needed for lunch, picnics, etc.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 282 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 21, 1997
