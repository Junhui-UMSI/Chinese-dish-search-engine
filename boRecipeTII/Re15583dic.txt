7-Up Salad (Aka Martian Guts)
7-up soft drink, Lime Jell-O, Miniature marshmallows, Cream cheese, Crushed pineapple, Cool whip, 
Recipe by: Cindy Fewless   Heat 7-Up and lime Jell-O together in a saucepan  until Jell-O is dissolved. Add marshmallows and stir until melted. Add  cream cheese and remove from heat. Mix with whisk until the cream cheese is  combined.  Add drained pineapple and mix well. Put in refrigerator  overnight to thicken.     Next day, stir in Cool Whip thoroughly.
