Fudgy Casserole a la Mode
+ tbs. butter or margarine,, Sugar, Eggs, Vanilla, All-Purpose Flour, Pecans, Vanilla ice-cream, 
Melt baking chocolate in top of a double boiler over simmering water.  Remove from heat.  Beat in butter or margarine, sugar, eggs and vanilla.  Blend in flour; add chopped pecans. Pour into buttered 1 or 1/2 quart  casserole.  Bake at 350F for 55 minutes.  Spoon into individual dishes and  serve warm with ice cream. Makes 6 - 8 servings.
