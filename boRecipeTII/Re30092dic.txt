Balsamic Vinaigrette
Apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, Fresh garlic, Water, Fresh parsley, Balsamic vinegar, olive oil, 
Whisk together vinegars, water, mustard, garlic, oil and parsley in small  bowl. Yield: About 1 cup.    Per serving: 280 Calories; 28g Fat (84% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 11g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 194mg Sodium    NOTES : Mary Carroll makes this delicious vinaigrette with the vinegar,  which she uses to dress spinach, endive and fresh orange salads for fall  lunches.
