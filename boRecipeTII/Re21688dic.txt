Apple Stuffed Chicken Breasts
GLAZE, Orange juice concentrate, Onion, Dried sage, apples, Walnuts, Apple jelly, butter, butter, raisins, Golden, seedless, Dry sherry, Chicken breasts, boneless, skinless, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease baking pan and set aside.  Place chicken breasts between sheets of plastic wrap and pound with meat  mallet to 1/4-inch thickness. Set aside    Place apples, raisins, nuts, onion, and sage in bowl and stir to combine.  Place 1 tablespoon apple mixture in center of each breast ad roll up,  enclosing filling completely. Secure with wooden toothpicks and place,  seam-side-down, in single layer in prepared pan. Brush with melted butter  and set aside.    To make glaze, melt 4 tablespoons butter in small saucepan and stir in  apple jelly, orange juice, and sherry. Simmer 3 minutes until jelly is  completely melted.    Bake chicken in preheated oven, basting frequently with glaze, 30-45  minutes or until no longer pink inside. Remove toothpicks before serving.
