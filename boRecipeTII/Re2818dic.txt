Peanut Butter Creams
Butter, Peanuts -- chopped fine, Powdered sugar -- sifted, Cream cheese, Peanut butter, Vanilla, 
In a mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar, peanut butter, butter, and  vanilla.  If necessary add a little more powdered sugar.  Cover and chill  for 1 to 2 hours.  Form into small balls and roll each ball in peanuts.  Place in paper candy cups, cover and keep refrigerated for up to 4 days.  Return to room temperature about 2 hours before eating.
