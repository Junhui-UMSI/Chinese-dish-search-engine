Swiss-Chicken Bake
Uncooked rice, green pepper, Chicken breasts, Water, V-8 juice, Onion, Chicken broth (pkg), Salt and pepper, Swiss cheese, 
In buttered casserole dish, place raw rice. On top of rice, place chicken  breasts and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Sprinkle onion and green pepper  on top. Pour chicken broth, hot water, and V-8 over top. Sprinkle cheese on  top. Bake at 350F uncovered for an hour, then uncover and bake for another  15 minutes. This is just easy comfort food.
