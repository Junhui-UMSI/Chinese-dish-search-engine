Bacon-Cheese Popcorn
Bacon bits, popcorn, popped, Hickory-smoked salt, Margarine, American cheese, Seasoned salt, 
Pour freshly popped corn in large bowl. Combine margarine with  hickory-smoked salt. Pour over popcorn; toss well to coat. Sprinkle with  cheese and bacon bits. Toss again and serve while warm.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 652 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 31, 1998
