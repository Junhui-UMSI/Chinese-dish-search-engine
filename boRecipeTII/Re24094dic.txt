Traditional Alfredo Sauce
Dried herbs, Onion, Flour, Butter, Nutmeg, garlic, Heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, 
Heat butter in heavy sauce pan over medium-low heat. Sautee onion and  garlic until onion is translucent. Add flour and stir constantly for 20-30  seconds. Reduce heat and slowly add cream. Stir constantly and wisk any  lumps. Add herbs and nutmeg. Slowly add cheese. When cheese has melted,  bring briefly to a boil to thicken to desired consistancy. Toss with  favorite pasta.
