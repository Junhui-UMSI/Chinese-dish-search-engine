Alsatian Cassoulet
Caraway seeds, Unpeeled apples; sliced, Cooked seitan; chopped, Pepper, Red cabbage; thinly sliced, Whole wheat flour, Heads cauliflower; cut into, White beans; cooked, Shallots; chopped, Vegetable stock or dark beer, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 450 deg.     Combine all ingredietns, except flour, in a large roasting pan or  casserole dish. Sprinkle top with flour.     Place in oven and cook, uncovered, for 30 minutes.     Reduce heat to 250 deg. Cover and cook for 20 hours, stirring  occasionally. Serve hot.     Per serving: 442 cal; 43 g prot; 132 mg sod; 64 g carb; 5 g fat; 0 mg  chol; 116 mg calcium     Source: Vegetarian Gourmet, Winter 93/MM by DEEANNE  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Mar 21, 98
