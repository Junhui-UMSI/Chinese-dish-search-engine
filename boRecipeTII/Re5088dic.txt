Pico De Gallo Burger Topping
Chopped scallions, Diced fine, Salt, Jalapeno peppers, seeded &, Cider vinegar or white wine, Vegetable oil, Vinegar, Chopped fine green tomatoes, Cilantro leaves (1/2 cup), Chooped loosely packed, 
Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Cover & refrigerate 1 hour or until  chilled. Store up to 2 days.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
