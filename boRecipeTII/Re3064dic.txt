Peanut Crisps
Vanilla, Egg, Crispie rice cereal, Sugar, Crunchy peanut butter, 
In a large mixing bowl stir together peanut butter, egg, sugar and vanilla.  When well blended, quickly stir in cereal.    Roll into 1 inch balls.  Place on a cookie sheet and flatten with tines of  a fork.  Bake at 350 F for about 10 minutes. Do not remove from pan until  slightly cooled.    Source:  Co-op grocery store advertising, September 1993 Shared and tested  by Elizabeth Rodier
