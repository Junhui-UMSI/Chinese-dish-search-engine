White Chili

From the Rival Crockpot cookbook    Put beans in medium pan and cover with water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat  and allow to simmer 20 minutes. Discard water. Cut chicken into 1-inch  pieces and brown if desired. Put all ingredients in crock-pot. Stir to mix  thoroughly. Cover. Cook on low 10-12 hours or high 5-6 hours.
