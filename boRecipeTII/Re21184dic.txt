Tzatzika
Salt, Cucumber, Clove, minced, Pepper, white, Sour cream, Onion, grated, Yogurt, plain lowfat, 
Wash cucumber and partially peel (leave about 1/3 peel for color) then  grate. Drain cuke and onion in strainer and press. Add remaining  ingredients and season. Chill well. Serve with Greek olives and pita  triangles. Makes 2 cups or 8 servings.
