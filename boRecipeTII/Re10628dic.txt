Quick Potato Soup
milk, Shredded carrot, Salt, Green Onions, Water, Pepper, Margarine or butter, Hungry Jack Mashed Potatoes, 
Melt margarine in large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onions and  carrot; cook 2 minutes or until tender. Add water; bring to a boil. Stir in  potato flakes, milk, salt and pepper. Cook until hot, stirring  occasionally.    CALORIES: 220     SODIUM: 580MG    CHOLESTEROL: 15MG    FAT: 9G    CARBOHYDRATE: 26G    SAT: 3G    From <Meals In Minutes>, a Pillsbury Classic Cookbook.
