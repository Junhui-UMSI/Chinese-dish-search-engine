Yellow Rice with Raisins
Butter or margarine, Salt, Ground turmeric, Yellow sugar, Boiling water, Seedless raisins, Uncooked, parpoiled rice, Piece of cinnamon, 
Wash rice. Add all the ingredients to the boiling water, except the  raisins, butter and sugar. Add rice slowly to boiling water and cook for  about 20 minutes over high heat. Drain the water and steamcook the rice  with the raisins and cinnamonstick for 1 hour over boiling water. Remove  the stick of cinnamon and mic in the butter and the sugar. Serve warm with  bobotie.    Posted to rec.food.recipes by japlady@nwu.edu (Rebecca Radnor) on 1995, .
