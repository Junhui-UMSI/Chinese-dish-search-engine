Tex-Mex Pasta Salad
Garlic, Sunflower seeds, Jack cheese, cubed, Yellow pepper, cubed, Chopped parsley, Cooked pasta, Green pepper,cubed, Hot pepper, chopped, Chili powder, olive oil, Tomatoes, chopped, Red wine vinegar, 
Combine vegetables in large bowl.  Combine oil, vinegar, garlic, parsley,  chili powder, salt & pepper to taste. Pour over vegetablemixture. Stir in  cooked pasta, sprinkle with cheese and sunflower seeds, toss. Chill.
