Tangy Blue Cheese Dip
Blue Cheese; Crumbled, Hot Sauce, Garlic; Minced, Fresh Chives; Finely Chopped, Dried Chives; Crumbled, *, Sour cream, 
*    Use the dried chives if the fresh is not available, otherwise omit.  +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++ ++++++++++++++++++  Blend the blue cheese and sour cream thoroughly. Add all the other  ingredients, blending well. Cover and chill. Makes about 2 cups of dip.  SUGGESTED DIPPERS:  Carrots, Cherry Tomatoes, Pineapple, Apple, Pearapple,  Cocktail Black Bread, Italian Or French Bread Chunks
