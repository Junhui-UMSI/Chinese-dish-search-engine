Pineapple Relish
Salt, fresh pineapple, red onion, Rice wine vinegar, cilantro, Jalapeno, 
Place the pineapple, onion, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and vinegar in  a mixing bowl and mix well with a plastic spatula. Season to taste with  salt and pepper.
