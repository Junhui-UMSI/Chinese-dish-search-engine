Baked Garlic-Thyme Chicken
Dried thyme leaves, Scallions; chopped, Garlic, Lemon juice, Whole chickens; rinsed and, Unsalted butter; softened, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 1:00.    Preheat oven to 350F. Combine all ingredients, except chicken, in a bowl.  Season with salt and pepper to taste. Spread seasoned butter over outside  of chicken. Place chicken, breast side down, in a baking dish. Bake 35  minutes. Turn chicken breast side up and bake another 25-35 minutes or  until tender, basting occasionally with pan juices.
