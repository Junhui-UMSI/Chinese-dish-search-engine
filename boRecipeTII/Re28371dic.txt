Baked Lamb Shanks with Tomato Sauce
Allspice, Pepper, salt, Olive oil, Nutmeg, Lamb shanks, tomatoes, Onions, 
Lightly coat lamb with oil; arrange in 13*2*9" baking dish. Bake in  450 oven for 30 minutes, turning once. Remove lamb from oven; reduce  heat to 350. Place onion slices over lamb; sprinkle with allspice,  nutmeg, salt & pepper. Pour tomatoes evenly over all. Return lamb to  oven; bake 2 hours, basting frequently.
