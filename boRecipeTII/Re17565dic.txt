Almond Filling
Powdered sugar, Almond paste, Margarine or butter, Sliced almonds, 
Heat 1 package (3 1/2 oz) almond paste, cut up, 1/2 powdered sugar and 1/4  cup margarine or butter over medium heat, stirring constantly until smooth.  Stir in 1/2 cup sliced almonds.    Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
