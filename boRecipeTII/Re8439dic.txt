Potato Tuna Chowder
Celery; sliced, White pepper, Skim milk, Tuna in water; drained and, Onions; chopped, Sage, Paprika, Carrots; sliced, Potatoes; diced, Chicken broth, Green beans, canned; drained, 
In a large saucepan, combine 1 cup potatoes, chicken broth, and onions.  Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and cover. Simmer for 10 minutes or until  potatoes are tender. Add sage, paprika, and white pepper. Mash potatoes.  Add remaining potatoes, carrots, green beans, celery, and milk to mashed  potatoes. Bring just to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes or  until vegetables are tender. Add tuna. Simmer 5 minutes more.
