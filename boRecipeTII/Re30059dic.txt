Balsamic Dressing (Lf)
Balsamic vinegar, Dried basil, Fresh parsley; chopped (opt), Capers, Water, Dijon mustard, 
Combine the ingredients. Adjust vinegar to taste, since it has a strong  flavor. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator. Makes about 1  cup. Per tablespoon: 1 calorie, 0 chol, 0 dietary fiber, 0 fat, 7mg sodium  Authors Note: If you dont have balsamic vinegar, you may substitute  another kinds. But, if this be the case, start with water and vinegar in  equal proportions.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "Martha Sheppard" <marthahs@worldnet.att.net>  on Apr 13, 1998
