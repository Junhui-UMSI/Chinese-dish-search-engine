Biscotti Con Pignoli
Pignoli (pine) nuts, Almond paste, Confectioners sugar, Egg whites, at room temp, Sugar, Honey, Vanilla, 
Cream together the  almond paste, sugars, and honey into a smooth batter.  Beat the egg whites until stiff, then gradually mix into the batter, along  with the vanilla. Spread the pignoli in a dish. Drop batter by teaspoonfuls  into the nuts, then place onto a lightly greased cookie sheet at 1"  intervals. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 12-15 minutes or until  golden. Remove carefully from baking sheet with a spatula while still warm.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  17, 1998
