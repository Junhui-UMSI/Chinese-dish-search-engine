Quick Cheese Sauce
Cornstarch, Salt and pepper, Butter, Velveeta cheese 1/2 lb, milk, 
Cut up cheese into a sauce pan.  Add butter and milk.  Heat slowly until  cheese has melted.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Dissolve cornstarch in 1/2  cup water, stir in a little at a time until sauce is of the required  consistency.    Collection of recipes complied by Don Zarzecki.
