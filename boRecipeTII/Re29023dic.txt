Baked Pineapple #3
Eggs; well beaten, Flour, Sugar, Crushed pineapple; (20 oz.), 
Mix and pour into buttered casserole or baking pan. Bake 1/2 to 3/4 of an  hour at 350 degrees. Brown 3 slices of cubed bread in butter. Place on top  of baked pineapple. Serves 6 to 8 persons.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 23,  1998
