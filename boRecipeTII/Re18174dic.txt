Venezuelan Spiced Hot Chocolate
star anise, orange ; Zest of, Brown sugar, milk, Semisweet chocolate, Aged dark rum, allspice berries, cinnamon, 
TOO HOT TAMALES SHOW #TH6349    Combine milk with spices and sugar in large heavy saucepan. Cook over  medium heat, stirring to dissolve sugar, until milk just starts to bubble  around the edges (scalding). Lower heat and cook 10 minutes. Remove from  heat and allow to steep 10 additional minutes, then strain into another  large heavy pot. Heat gently, then add chocolate and rum. Whisk briskly 5  minutes over low flame until chocolate is completely melted.    Yield: 8 cups    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 24, 1998
