Avocado with Smoked Fish
Avocados -- ripe, Pimientos -can, Red bell pepper -- or 12, olive oil, milk, Smoked fish, Lime juice, Eggs -- hard Boiled, Sugar, Salt, Vegetable oil, 
Remove the skin and bones from fish and flake the flesh with a fork.    In a deep bowl, mash the egg yolks with the milk until they form a smooth  paste.  Add sugar, salt, and 1 tablespoon of the lime juice. Then beat in  the vegetable oil, a teaspoon or so at a time.  Add the olive oil in the  same gradual manner.  chop egg whites finely and add them to the bowl,  along with the fish.  Combine thoroughly but gently.    Just before serving, cut the avocados in half, remove pits, and fill  cavities with the fish mixture.  Garnish with pepper or pimento, and pass  around the remaining lime juice to sprinkle on individual servings.
