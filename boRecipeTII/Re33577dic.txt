Swedish Dreams
Brown sugar, Flour, Butter, Crushed walnuts (I buzz mine, Beaten egg yolk, 
Cream butter, add sugar, egg yolk and flour.  Roll into balls and then roll  balls into crushed walnuts.  Make indentation in middle with thimble or  finger.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.  About halfway through the  baking, put jam into the hole and finish baking.
