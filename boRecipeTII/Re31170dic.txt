Banana Waffles
bananas, All-purpose flour, milk, Sugar, Shortening, Salt, Eggs, Baking Powder, 
Sift flour, measure and resift 3 times with remaining dry ingredients. Beat  eggs, add milk and shortening. Pour into dry ingredients, add bananas and  beat all together until smooth. Using 1/2 cup of batter for each waffle,  bake in a hot waffle iron until golden brown. Serve immediately with hot  syrup. Makes six 7-inch waffles.
