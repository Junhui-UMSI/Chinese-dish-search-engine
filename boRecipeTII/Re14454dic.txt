Whole Wheat Snickerdoodles
Ground cinnamon, Baking soda, Whole wheat flour, Salt, All-purpose flour, Butter, TOPPING, Sugar, Egg white, Sugar, Egg, 
In a mixing bowl, cream sugar and butter until fluffy. Add egg and egg  white; beat well. Combine the dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture and  beat well. In a small bowl, combine topping ingredients. Shape dough into  walnut-sized balls; roll in cinnamon-sugar. Place 2 in apart in ungreased  baking sheets. Bake at 400 for 8-10 minutes. Cookies well puff up and  flatten as they bake.    Recipe by: Jana Horsfall (ToH Collectors Edition)    Posted to EAT-L Digest 01 Mar 97 by Sean Coate <swcoate@PEGANET.COM> on Mar  1, 1997.
