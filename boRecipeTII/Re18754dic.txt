Anasazi Bean Spread
Tofu, drained, Cooked AM Anasazi Beans, Oregano, Garlic powder, Onion, Celery stalk, Basil, Chili powder, Vege-sal, AM Unrefined Olive Oil, 
Blend together in blender or food processor until smooth.  Great for dips  or sandwich spread.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "The Native Americans" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
