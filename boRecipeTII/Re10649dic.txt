Quick Red Clam Sauce
Pepper, Leaf basil, Onion, Olive oil, Garlic, clams, Salt, tomatoes, Chopped parsley, Leaf oregano, 
Saute onion and garlic in olive oil in a medium-size saucepan until soft.  Drain liquid from clams into saucepan, reserving clams. Add tomatoes, salt,  pepper, basil and oregano; bring to boiling; lower heat. Simmer, uncovered,  15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until sauce is thickened as you like it.  Mix in clams and parsley and heat 5 minutes longer. Serve over linguini or  other pasta. Posted to EAT-L Digest 11 Mar 97 by "Elizabeth A. Post"  <millefiore@FUSE.NET> on Mar 11, 1997
