Peanut Butter Fudge #12
Sugar, milk, Vanilla, Stick butter (2 oz), Salt, Heaping tb peanut butter, 
Cook sugar, milk, and salt until hard ball stage, then add butter, vanilla,  and peanut butter.  Beat until thick.  Pour into a buttered pan.
