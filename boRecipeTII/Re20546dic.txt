Apple Chicken
Lemon juice, Small onion, sliced, Chicken breasts, salt and pepper, Apple juice, Cumin, Flour, Vegetable oil, Water, 
Saute onion in oil.  Salt, pepper and flour chicken, add to oonion and  brown quickly.  Add cumin and stir. Add water and juices. Cover, reduce  heat and simmer 30 minutes. Serve with some of the sauce and onion over  chicken.  Recipe may be doubled (4 breasts), but use only HALF again the  sauce ingredients. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers BillSpalding *P CRBR 38 A
