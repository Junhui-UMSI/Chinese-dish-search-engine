Beet Salad
Onion, Lemon juice, Vegetable oil, sugar, Salt and pepper to taste, parsley, fresh, beets, 
Cook...ahem...open the cans of beets & drain well. Combine the dressing  ingredients & pour over beets in a glass bowl. Garnish with finely chopped  parsley.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "leah perez" <perezleah@hotmail.com> on Nov  01, 1998,
