Peppermint Brownies Ala Mode
Margarine, Vanilla fat free frozen, Baking cocoa, hard peppermint, Baking cocoa, Chocolate Glaze, Hot water, Egg whites, CHOCOLATE GLAZE, Powdered sugar, Peppermint extract, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Salt, Vanilla, Sugar, All purpose flour, 
Heat oven to 350. Spray square pan, 8x8x2 inches, with nonstick cooking  spray. Mix sugar, margarine, vanilla, peppermint extract and egg whites in  medium bowl. Stir in flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt. Spread in pan.    Bake 20-25 minutes until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool  completely. Prepare Chocolate Glaze; spread evenly over brownies. Sprinkle  with candies. Cut into about 2 inch squares. Serve with frozen yogurt.  Makes 16 brownies.    Chocolate Glaze: Mix all ingredients until smooth and spreadable.    Recipe by: Unknown Cooking Magazine    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #994 by L979 <L979@aol.com> on Jan 8, 1998
