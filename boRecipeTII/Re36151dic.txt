Beef Stew #4
Bacon cut into 1/2" pieces, olive oil, Tomato paste, Pepper; ground, Bouquet garni, Flour, Garlic, Orange peel, Beer, Lean stewing beef cut into, Onion, Beef stock, Coarse salt, 
From: Kattwoman1@aol.com         Date: Tue, 6 Feb 1996 23:59:38 -0500    Heat oil in heavy casserole. Add bacon. Dry beef cubes w/papertowel. Brown  the beef. Remove the beef, add the onion browning lightly. Stir in the  flour, cook for 2-3 min. (dont let the flour burn). Return the beef, with  its juices to the casserole. Add the garlic, the stock, beer, tomato  paste, bouquet, orange peel, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil. Lower heat,  simmer gently for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally. remove and discard  bouquet.  Serve warm.
