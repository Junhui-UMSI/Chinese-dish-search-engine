Banana Pudding (Bahamas)
Nutmeg, Ripe bananas, Sugar, Flour, Butter, 
submitted by: LeiG@aol.com  Mash the bananas. Add the flour, sugar, butter and nutmeg. Pour into a  buttered 8 inch square pan, . Bake in a moderate 325 deg Oven for 30  minutes or until delicately browned on top. cut into squares and serve hot  or cold, dusted with powdered sugar. If desired, whipped cream or fruit  syrup may be placed on each portion.    Recipe Archive - 29 July 96    From the RECIPEinternet: Recipes from Around the World recipe list.
