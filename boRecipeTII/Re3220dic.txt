Pear Cake
Shortening, Cloves, Granulated sugar, Baking soda, Eggs, Buttermilk mixed with, Allspice, All-purpose flour, Nuts, Nutmeg, Pear preserves, 
Mix all ingredients and bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
