Ten Flavor Shrimp
5 oz. deveined medium shrimp, Fresh bread crumbs, Minced shallots or scallions, Grated Parmesan cheese, Soy sauce, Clove garlic, mashed, Butter, Teaspoon powdered ginger, Brandy, Freshly grated ginger or 1, Parsley, 
Directions Drain shrimp. Rinse under cold water andthen soak for 10 - 15  minutesin cold water to remove preserving brine. Drain thoroughly and put  on paper towels. Heat butter in skillet until it bubbles. Add shallots and  garlic, and saute gently until golden. Add shrimp and toss for a minute or  two to warm through. Mix in brandy, soy and ginger, along with parsley.  Spoon the shrimp mixture into shells or ovenproof glass individual serving  dishes, leaving any juice in pan.  Sprinkle each serving with bread crumbs  and Parmesan cheese and then dribble pan juices over top.  Heat under  broiler until mixture is bubbling ane the crumbs are browned. submitted by  marina source Cooking Better For Less    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #230      Date: Mon, 30 Sep 1996 04:12:45 +0000      From: Marina <thecollector@worldnet.att.net>
