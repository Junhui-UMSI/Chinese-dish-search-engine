Apple Crisp
Flour, Medium-sized apples, Butter or margarine; (1, Oats, Brown sugar, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease an 8 x 8 or similar baking pan. Cut  apples in quarters. Peel (I dont peel mine) and cut out core. Slice thin;  put in pan. Mix oats, sugar and flour. Mix butter or margarine in with your  fingers. Mixture will be crumbly. Put mixture on apples. Bake about 35  minutes.    If desired, serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or some whipped  topping. Serves 6.    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #436 by Lee Ann Hamm <lhamm@premier.net>  on Dec 4, 1997
