Quinoa Corn Bread
Baking Powder, Salt, Buttermilk, chopped green chilies, canned, Quinoa meal, Corn meal, Honey, OPTIONAL, Egg, Butter, Baking soda, 
Preheat oven to 425 F.  Prepare quinoa meal by grinding raw quinoa in a  blender.  Combine all dry ingredients. Combine wet ingredients. Mix the two  together.  Bake in greased 9" x 9" pan or greased muffin tin until golden  (about 25 minutes).    Serve hot, with lots of butter.  For a spicier variation add optional green  chilies or jalapeno-and-tomato relish.    Source: Ancient Harvest Quinoa recipe leaflet Typed for you by Karen  Mintzias
