Sweet and Sour Spareribs #1
Vegetable oil, Salt, Soy sauce, Cider vinegar, Water, Garlic, Brown sugar, Ginger, Flour, Spareribs, 
Cut ribs into 1-1/2" pieces.  Sprinkle with soy sauce and flour, mix  gently. Heat oil, then add garlic and ginger. Brown ribs; drain fat. Add  remaining ingredients and simmer for 45 minutes.    From a book of my mothers (Judy Hosey) titled <Senator Richard Matsuuras  Favorite Recipes>, mostly Japanese and Hawaiian.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
