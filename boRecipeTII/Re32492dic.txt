Sweet Corn Snack
Salt, Ears of corn, Sugar, milk, 
Cut the kernels from 8 large ears of corn.  Combine with 3 tablespoons of=    sugar, 2 teaspoons of salt and 1/4 cup milk in a heavy-bottomed saucepan.  Bring to a boil, then lower the heat to simmer and cook for 15 minutes,  stirring frequently.  Pour into a shallow, greased baking pan and dry in  oven for 1-1/2 hours at 250^F, stirring occasionally.  Corn should be light  brown.    Store in a covered container for snacking.  Yield 7-8 cups.
