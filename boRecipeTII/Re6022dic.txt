Pizelles
Vanilla, Sugar, Crisco, Flour, Eggs, Butter, melted and cooled, Baking Powder, 
Combine all ingredients until well mixed. Roll into golf ball size balls  and bake in pizelle iron 45 seconds.    NOTES : To make chocolate pizelles, substitute 3/4 cup of flour with cocoa.  You can also substitute the vanilla for any desired flavoring.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #600 by "LauraG" <gaggini@gate.usaor.net> on  May 6, 1997
