Basil Stir-Fry
Carrot sliced 1/4" thick, Fresh spinach washed, Water, Corn oil, Ground beef, Onion sliced thin, Couscous, Green pepper sliced, Dijon mustard, Dried basil, Cider vinegar, 
In a small saucepan, heat water to boiling, add couscous, cover and set  aside.    Add oil to wok or heavy skillet; over hight heat, quickly brown meat; add  peppers, carrots, and onions.  Continue stirring until vegetables are  slightly cooked, about 2-3 minutes.    Add basil, mustard, and vinegar and mix thoroughly.  Reduce heat to low,  toss spinach on top, cover and allow to steam 3 or 4 minutes. Serve over  couscous.
