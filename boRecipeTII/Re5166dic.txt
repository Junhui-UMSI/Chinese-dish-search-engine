Pig in the Blanket
Long-Grain Rice; uncooked, salt and pepper, Pork Sausage, (16 Oz) Sauerkraut, Cabbage Head, (16 Oz) Tomatoes, Lean Pork Chops, 
Wash and place cabbage leaves in boiling water for 5 minutes and drain. Mix  sausage and rice; roll up in cabbage leaves; fasten with toothpick. Place  rolls in bottom of large pan in a nest of extra cabbage leaves. Brown pork  chops. Pour tomates and sauerkraut over cabbage rolls; then add browned  pork chops. (Liquid should cover it.) Salt and pepper to taste. Cook 4  hours over low heat, covered tightly.    Serves 6    Source: "Mountain Measures" -- Junior League of Charleston, WV ed. 1974    billspa@icanect.net    Recipe by: George. S.
