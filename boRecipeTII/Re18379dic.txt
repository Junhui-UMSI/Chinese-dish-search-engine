Very Light Sauce
Mayonnaise, Egg, separated, Clam broth, Lemon juice, 
Mix all ingredients except egg white together. Heat just until hot through.  Beat egg white until stiff and glossy but not dry. Fold into warm sauce.  Serve at once. *** Dental floss is handy for tying rolled fillets and also  for trussing poultry.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
