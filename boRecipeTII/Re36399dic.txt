Beer Batter Onion Rings #1
oil, Beer, Flour, Onions, Salt, 
Combine flour and salt; stir in beer and beat until smooth. Chill for 3  hours. Separate onions into rings, dip in batter and fry in oil until  golden brown on both sides. Sprinkle with salt. Keep warm in 200 oven.  Note: This batter is also very good used on banana peppers. Slice peppers  in half and remove seeds. Dip in batter and fry.
