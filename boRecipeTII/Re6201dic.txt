Pizza Sauce Recipe
Pepper, 8-oz  cans tomato sauce, Italian seasonings, Garlic powder, 6-oz  can tomato paste, Oregano, Anise seeds, freshly ground, Chopped parsley, 
Grind the anise seeds. Mix it all together. Spread on  the dough. Top with veggies.
