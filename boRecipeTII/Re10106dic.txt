Queen Royale
Parmesan, Finely chopped green onion, Lemon pepper, Melted margarine, Antarctic Queen fillets, Mayonnaise, 
In a buttered baking dish, arrange fillets in a single layer. Brush  wIth margarine & sprinkle with lemon pepper. In bowl mix mayonnaise,  cheese & green onion. Spread evenly over fish. Bake at 350 for 20  minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
