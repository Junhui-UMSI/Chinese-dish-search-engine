Abadoos Walnut Bread for Bread Makers
Bread flour, Dry yeast, Salt, NONFAT DRY MILK POWDER, All-purpose flour, WATER (150 ML), Butter, Sugar, EGG, SMALL, Walnuts, chopped, 
PLACE ALL INGREDIENTS IN THE ORDER LISTED IN THE BREAD MAKER. USE SWEET  MODE FOR THIS BREAD.  BREAD MAKER CANNOT BE PUT ON TIMER MODE.
