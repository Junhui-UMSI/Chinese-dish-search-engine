Tomato and Okra Gumbo
Margarine, Okra; fresh or frozen, #2 can Tomatoes, Salt, Bacon, 
Cut okra in 1/2" pieces, pour tomatoes in sauce pan. Add okra along with  seasoning. Cover and cook on medium heat for 20 minutes.    Recipe by: Mrs. Wilkes Cookbook    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 584 by Rodeo46898  <Rodeo46898@aol.com> on Jan 23, 1998
