Apple-Oatmeal Waffles
Maple syrup, Egg, Tart apples, Ground cinnamon, Orange juice, Maple syrup, Regular oats, Egg whites, Raisins, Flour, Nonfat milk, , Butter or margarine, Baking Powder, Salt, 
1. Spread oats on a baking sheet; toast in 350 oven, stirring occasionally,  until golden (12-15 minutes). Mix oats, flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and  if desired, salt in a large bowl. In a medium sized bowl, beat milk, syrup,  juice, egg and egg whites until blended. Stir in apple and raisins. Add egg  mixture to flour mixture; stir until evenly moistened. 2. Preheat a waffle  iron following manufacturers directions. Grease iron; fill three-quarters  full of batter. Bake until golden and crisp (6-8 minutes). Transfer to a  plate and keep warm in a 200 degree oven while making remaining waffles.  Offer with butter and syrup. Makes about twelve 4- inch square waffles.
