Quick and Easy Cake
Coconut flavoring, Salt, Sugar, Almond flavoring, Eggs, Flour, Solid vegetable shortening, 
Pr-heat oven to 325? F . Lightly grease a 13 x 9 inch baking pan with  non-stick cooking spray.    In large mixing bowl, combine sugar, shortening and 4 eggs. Beat for 2  minutes. Add flour, salt, 2 more eggs and the flavorings. Beat for 2  minutes longer.    Pour into prepared baking pan and bake for 1 hour. The top will be crusty.  Serves approximately 12 portions    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest Volume 98 #026 by Bella Parnes  <bparnes@aabgu.org> on Jan 13, 1998
