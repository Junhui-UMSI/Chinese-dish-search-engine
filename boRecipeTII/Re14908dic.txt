20-Minute Chicken Parmesan
Mozzarella cheese; shredded, Parmesan cheese, Chicken breast halves, Fresh parsley, Egg, Butter, Bread crumbs; seasoned, Spaghetti sauce, 
Using palm of hand flatten chicken to even thickness. Dip chicken into egg  then into crumbs to coat. In skillet over medium heat, in hot margarine,  brown chicken on both sides. Add Prego sauce. Reduce heat. Cover; simmer 10  minutes. Sprinkle with cheeses and parsley. Cover; simmer 5 minutes or  until cheese melts. 4 servings.    Suggested Wine: Chianti, Chardonnay    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Feb 22, 1998
