Bakewell Cream Biscuits
Tsp.Bakewell Cream *, Flour, Baking soda, Mayonaise, Salt; ( may be omitted ), milk, 
Source : The Good Sams RV Cookbook    Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Mix and sift dry ingredients together. Add  milk and mayonaise ; mix. Turn onto a floured board; knead 5 or 6 times.  Cut with biscuit cutter. Bake 10 minutes, then lower the heat to 325  degrees until biscuits are golden.    HINT: You can turn off the oven after 10 minutes; the biscuits will brown.    * If in an area where Bakewell Cream is not available, order by mail from  Byron H. Smith & Co.,Inc., 54 Perry Road, P.O.
