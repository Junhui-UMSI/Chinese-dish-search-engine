Turkey Calypso
Brown sugar, Pineapple juice or orange, Rum, Fresh pineapple or orange, Ground ginger, 
Combine all ingredients; add turkey, marinate 30 minutes or overnight.  Grill, brushing with additional marinade. Last 4 minutes of grilling top  steaks with pineapple slices or fresh orange slices.  Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by J Pellegrino <gigimfg@ix.netcom.com> on May  21, 1998
