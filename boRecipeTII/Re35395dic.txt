Beef Croquettes
Cream style corn, Handful Bread Crumbs, Salt and pepper to taste, Hamburger, Egg, Egg whites for coating, Bread crumbs for coating, Onion; diced, Flour for coating, 
Mix hamburger, egg, onion, handful of bread crumbs, salt, pepper and cream  style corn together and roll into balls or make into patties. Coat in  flour, then egg whites, then crumbs. Deep fry in hot oil until browned. So  easy and so good!    Recipe by: Found on MSN Cooking Forum    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #976 by "M. Hicks" <nitro_ii@email.msn.com>  on Jan 1, 1998
