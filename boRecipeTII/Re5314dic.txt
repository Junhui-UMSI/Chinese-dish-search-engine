Pine Nut Dressing
Coarsely ground pepper, Pine nuts, Ground beef, Orzo, Garlic powder, Onion, finely chopped, Concentrated chicken base, 
Saute beef, onion, chicken base, garlic powder, pepper and pine nuts until  onions are tender. Add mixture to orzo.    Serving Ideas : Serve with Greek Chicken    NOTES : *6 cups long grain rice may be substituted for the orzo. *If  serving with Greek Chicken, try stirring the dressing around in the pan you  cooked the chicken in before serving. Gives it a little extra flavor.  Recipe by: Michaels Restaurant - Rochester, MN    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #509 by Julie Benson <cejab@cin.net> on Mar  11, 1997.
