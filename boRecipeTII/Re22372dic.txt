Turkish Salad
Couscous, Salt, Lemon, Vegetable oil, Ripe tomatoes, Frsshly chopped parsley, Spring onions, Freshly chopped mint, Cucumber, Raisins, 
Cover the couscous with cold water and leave to soak for 30 minutes. Drain  through a sieve, pressing out the excess water. Place in a bowl.    Trim the spring onions and chop finely. Peel the cucumber if preferred, and  cut into small cubes. Finely chop the tomatoes (peel and discard the seeds  first, if preferred).    Add the spring onions, cucumber, tomatoes and raisins to the couscous with  the chopped herbs and seasoning to taste. Pour over the lemon juice and  oil. Mix lightly together, if not eating immeadiately, cover and chill  until required.    Source: Take a Break
