Applesauce Spice Muffins
Butter, Flour, Eggs, Allspice, Cloves, Applesauce, Pecans, Baking soda, Powered sugar, Cinnamon, Sugar, 
Cream butter, add sugar.  Add eggs, beating after each.  Add applesauce,  mix well.  Combine flour and next 4 ingredients.  Add to creamed mixture,  beat till smooth. Stir in pecans. Grease muffin tins (or use paper liners)  and fill 3/4 full. Bake at 350 for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick  inserted in muffin comes out clean.  Transfer from pan to wire rack.  Sprinkle muffin tops with powdered sugar.     For mini muffins: grease 1 3/4 inch muffin pans. Fill 3/4 full.  Bake at      350    for 15 minutes.
