Ann Coopers Apple Pancakes with Maple Pecan Butter
Baking Powder, MAPLE PECAN BUTTER, Maple syrup, All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Salt, Eggs, Butter, Granulated sugar, APPLE PANCAKES, Ground cinnamon, Ground nutmeg, Butter, Chopped tart red apples, Pecans, Buttermilk, 
Make Maple Pecan Butter. In small mixer bowl, beat butter on medium  speed until light. Add pecans; mix well. Gradually beat in syrup. Set  aside while making pancakes.    For Apple Pancakes: In medium bowl lightly beat eggs with wire whisk.  Add buttermilk and melted butter and whisk until smooth. Add dry  ingredients and mix only until moistened. Stir in apples.    Heat large non-stick griddle or skillet until hot. Melt a little of  the butter on griddle until bubbly but not brown. For each pancake,  spoon about 1/4 cup batter onto griddle. Cook until bubbles form and  edges are cooked. Turn and cook until second side is golden. Keep  pancakes warm on platter in 200 degree oven while preparing remaining  batter. Serve warm with Maple Pecan Butter.    Yield: 16 to 18 (4-inch) pancakes.
