"Tootie" Bread
(8-oz) Swiss cheese slices, Onion, Poppy seeds, Prepared mustard, Loaf French bread, Margarine; softened, Uncooked bacon; cut into, 
Preheat oven to 400?. Partially slice the bread into 16 pieces. Cut each  slice of cheese diagonally and place one triangle of cheese in between each  piece of bread. Place the bread on a foil covered cookie sheet. Combine  margarine, onion, mustard and poppy seeds and mix well. "Ice" the bread on  top and sides. Place small pieces of bacon on top. Bake for 20 minutes and  serve hot. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.    ANN LEEK (MRS. STEPHEN A.)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
