Washed Lentils
Lentils, Garlic, Green chili, chopped, Black mustard seeds, Chili powder, Ghee, Salt, Water, Cumin seeds, 
Wash lentils thoroughly.  Place in pot with the water & bring to a boil  over medium heat.  Cook about 10 minutes. Stir in salt, garlic, chili &  mustard.  Lower heat & simmer for 30 minutes till lentils are soft. In a  skillet, heat the ghee & toss in the cumin seeds & chili powder. Top cooked  dahl just before serving with ghee mixture.
