Poached Orange Roughy
Sliced tomato, Cider vinegar, Pepper, Chopped celery & leaves, Sliced lemon, Bay leaf, Orange roughy fillets, Sugar, Sliced onion, Water, 
Cut fillets crosswise into strips about 1/2" wide. In large skillet,  cook celery & onion in oil until tender. Arrange fish over  vegetables. Sprinkle with pepper.  Cover fish with tomato & lemon  slices. Combine water, sugar & vinegar & bay leaf. Pour over fish  mixture. Cover & simmer 8-10 minutes. Remove bay leaf before serving.
