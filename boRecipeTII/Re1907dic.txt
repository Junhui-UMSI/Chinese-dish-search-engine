Chinese Chicken Pasta
Vegetable oil, Shiitake mushrooms, fresh ginger, Garlic, Chicken broth, Soy sauce, Asian sesame oil, Black Pepper, chicken, Green Onions, Spinach, Spaghetti, 
Heat vegetable oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms;  cook 5 minutes. Add ginger and garlic; cook 1 minute more. Add chicken  broth, soy sauce, sesame oil and pepper; bring to boil and cook 1 minute.  Add cooked chicken, green onions and spinach. Toss with hot pasta.
