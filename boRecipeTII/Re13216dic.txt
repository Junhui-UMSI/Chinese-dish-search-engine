Worlds Best Chocolate Chip Cookies
All-purpose flour, 12-ounce pkg chocolate chips, Butter, Pecans, Baking soda, Vanilla, Eggs; room temperature, Salt, Granulated sugar, Brown sugar, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.  Cream the butter and sugars together in  a large bowl. Add the eggs and the vanilla and beat thoroughly.    In a separate bowl, sift together the salt, soda and flour.  Mix the flour  mixture into the liquid mixture one third at a time, to form the dough. Add  the semi-sweet chocolate chips and the nuts to the cookie dough.  Fold in  by hand until well mixed.    Drop the cookie dough onto parchment-lined cookie sheets by the tablespoon  full.  Bake in a preheated 350 degree F. oven for 10 to 12 minutes.  BAKE  NO MORE THAN 12 MINUTES! Do not brown or cookies will dry out too much!!!!!
