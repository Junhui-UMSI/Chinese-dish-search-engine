Texas Two-Step Chicken Picante
Packed light brown sugar, Dijon mustard, Hot cooked rice, Boneless; skinless chicken, Pace Picante Sauce, 
Mix picante sauce, sugar and mustard. Place chicken in 2-quart shallow  baking dish. Pour picante sauce mixture over chicken. Bake at 400 for 20  minutes or until chicken is done. Serve with rice. Serves 4.
