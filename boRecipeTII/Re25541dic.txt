Avocado Cake with Dates
Baking soda, Salt, Powdered sugar (optional), Nonfat milk, Thawed frozen nonfat egg, Ground cinnamon, Mashed avocados (about 1 1/2, Additional dates; slivered, Ground allspice, Nonstick cooking spray or, Cake flour, Granulated sugar, Ground nutmeg, Chopped dates, 
From: christi@lexis-nexis.com (Christi Wilson)    Date: 26 Oct 1995 10:00:52 -0500  Spray a nonstick 9-inch-square baking pan with nonstick cooking spray or  lightly brush with butter.    Sift together cake flour, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice, baking soda  and salt in large bowl. Blend together avocados, egg substitute and nonfat  milk in small bowl. Stir into dry ingredients along with dates until  blended. Spread evenly into prepared pan.    Bake at 325 degrees 30 to 35 minutes or until wood pick inserted in center  comes out clean. Cool in pan. Sprinkle lightly with powdered sugar. Garnish  top with a few slivers of dates, if desired.    Per serving: 217 calories, 4 gm fat, no cholesterol, 119 mg sodium.
