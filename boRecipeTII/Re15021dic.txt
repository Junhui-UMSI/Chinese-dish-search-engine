West Indian Shrimp
Angostura bitters, And peeled, Thyme, Shrimp, cooked -- drained, Saffron, Grated Parmesan cheese, Cumin seed, olive oil, Marjoram, Dried chervil, 
In an oven-safe skillet, heat olive oil until it smokes. Quickly saute  shrimp. Stir in bitters and herbs. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and broil  until lightly browned.    Recipe By     : Elizabeth Powell    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
