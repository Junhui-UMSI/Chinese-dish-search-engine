Swedish Thumb Cookies
Cubes, Sugar, Carbonate of Ammonia, Butter, All-Purpose Flour, Sifted, Egg Yolk, Unbeaten, Brown sugar, 
Cream butter, add sugar gradually and cream until light. Add egg yolk and  blend well. Crush cubes of ammonia and sift with flour. Add enough flour to  make a stiff dough. The dough should crack when thumb is pressed in. Roll  in balls and push in center with thumb. Bake in a slow oven (250 degrees)  for 30 minutes. Yield: 30 1 1/2 inch balls.
