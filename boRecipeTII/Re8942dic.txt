Presidents Rice Pudding(From the 1600s)
Sugar, Rice, Grand Marnier liquor, Crushed pineapple,drained, Heavy cream, 
Mix all with a few drops of Grand Marnier liquor. Chill well in  refrigerator. Whip heavy cream until very stiff. Fold into rice mixture.  Chill overnight.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #303    Date: Tue, 5 Nov 1996 08:53:03 -0500    From: ctlindab@usa.nai.net
