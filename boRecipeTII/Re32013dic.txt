Barbecue Soy Burgers #1
Onion; minced, Butter or margarine, Soy sauce, Sesame seeds, Fine bread crumbs, Cooked soybeans; drained, Cloves garlic; mashed, Egg, 
These nutty burgers are a barbecue favorite, great on whole-wheat onion  buns, with the traditional toppings of tomato, cheese, lettuce and so on.  Freshly ground black pepper to taste In a small pan, saute onion and garlic  in butter or margarine, and cook until translucent. Set aside. In food  processor, whirl beans, bread crumbs, egg, soy sauce and sesame seeds to a  coarse meal. Transfer to a medium bowl, add garlic- onion mixture and black  pepper, and mix well. Shape into 4 patties. Heat on grill till brown,  giving them about 5 minutes each side.    GARHOW@HPUBMAA.ESR.HP.COM    (GARRY HOWARD)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
