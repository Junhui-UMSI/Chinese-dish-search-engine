Pickled Baby Corn on the Cob
Garlic, Celery seed, Ground turmeric, Dry mustard, Fresh baby corn on the cob,, Sugar, Vinegar, 
In a large saucepan stir together sugar, vinegar, turmeric, celery seed,  mustard, and garlic.  Add corn. Bring to a full boil. Boil gently for 2  minutes. Remove from heat. Cool slightly. Tranfer corn to a moisture and  vapor-proof container.  Add enough of the cooking liquid to cover. Cover  and chill in refrigerator for up to 1 week.
