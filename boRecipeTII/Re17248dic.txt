Waffeln (Waffles)
Cinnamon, Flour, Egg whites, Sugar, milk, Vanilla flavor, Confectioner's sugar, Unsalted butter, Egg yolks, 
Cream the butter and add the sugar, vanilla flavor, cinnamon, and egg  yolks. Stir until the sugar is melted. Add the sifted flour and milk. Mix  well. Fold in the stiffened egg whites. Bake in a waffle iron until golden  brown. Sprinkle with confectioners sugar.    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 24, 1998
