Zwiebelfleisch
Salt and pepper to taste, Onions; diced, Butter, Flour, Beef filet; 1/2 lb each, 
Pound beef slices; salt & pepper & sprinkle both sides with flour. Heat  butter in frying pan; add beef & cover with onions. Brown in covered pan.  When brown, add water & steam until tender. Serve with noodles or boiled  potatoes.    FRAU WALFER (RITA) BOCK    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
