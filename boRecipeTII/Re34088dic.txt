Bass with Avocado Sauce
Fine Dry Bread Crumbs, Hot Sauce, Dijon mustard, Lemon juice, Skim milk, Bass Fillets, Vegetable cooking spray, Lime juice, Light soy sauce, Ripe Avocado; coarsely, Garlic, Lemon Rind; grated, 
Combine the first 5 ingredients in a blender; cover and process until  smooth. Set mixture aside. Combine lemon juice and next 3 ingredients in a  shallow dish; dip fillets in lemon juice mixture, and dredge in bread  crumbs. Place on a baking sheet coated with cooking spray. Bake at 450F  for 7 minutes; turn fillets over, and bake an additional 7 minutes or until  fish flakes easily when tested with a fork. Transfer fillets to a serving  platter, and top with avocado sauce.    Alternate Fish: Jumbo Cod, Orange Roughy, Grouper.
