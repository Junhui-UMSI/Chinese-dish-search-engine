Pickled Vegetables
Cauliflower, coarsely chop, Carrot, shredded, Salt, Boiling water, White vinegar, Sugar, Cabbage, shredded, Serrano chilies, stemmed &, thinly sliced lengthwise, 
Combnie water, vinegar, sugar & salt.  Stir until the sugar is  dissolved. Let the mixture cool to room temperature.    Add the vegetables to the liquid & let them marinate, covered, for 1  day at room temperature, or 2 to 3 days in the refrigerator.
