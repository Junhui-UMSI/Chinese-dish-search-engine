Quickie Scampi
Butter, Grated Parmesan cheese, Worcestershire Sauce, Frozen Shelled Shrimp, Finely Chopped Parsley, Dillweed, Garlic, Tabasco sauce, 
Thaw shrimp and squeeze out excess liquid with paper towels.  Melt the  butter in a skillet; saute? garlic lightly.  Add shrimp, Worcestershire  sauce and Tabasco sauce to taste. Cook over medium low heat until shrimp  turn pink. Remove from heat and stir in parsley and dill. Preheat oven to  400F.  Turn shrimp mixture into a baking dish, top with lots of grated  Parmesan cheese.  Bake until heated through, about 10 minutes.  Serve with  lots of French bread.  From: Syds Cookbook.
