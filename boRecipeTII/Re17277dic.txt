Wagon Wheels W/sun-Dried Tomato Sauce
Wagon wheel pasta; cook, Parsley, Dried Oregano, Lemon juice, Parmigiano reggiano or, Onion, Fresh oregano; or, Garlic, Very hot tap water, olive oil, Sun-dried tomatoes; not, 
Saute onion in hot oil in nonstick skillet until soft. Meanwhile, with food  processor on drop garlic through feed tube and mince. Combine 1/2 cup of  the tap water, as hot as possible, with sun-dried tomatoes, and add to  processor. Process to puree tomatoes. Add remaining 1-1/2 cups of water to  combine. Add tomato mixture to skillet and bring to boil. Reduce heat and  continue to simmer to thicken slightly. Add parsley and oregano to sauce  with lemon juice. Simmer over low heat. Serve drained pasta with tomato  sauce, sprinkled with cheese. (wrv)
