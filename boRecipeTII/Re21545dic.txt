Apple Pizza
Apples; pared, cored, sliced, Flour, all-purpose, Butter, unsalted, Bread dough, loaf, frozen, Sugar, granulated, Nuts; chopped, Cinnamon, ground, 
Let frozen dough set at room temp. until thawed. Roll dough into a circle  for a 12 inch pizza pan. Cover dough with the sliced apples. Combine  remainder ingredients and sprinkle over the apples. Let rise. Bake for 10  to 15 minutes at 375 degrees.    <source unknown> posted by DonW1948@aol.com Submitted By DONW1948@AOL.COM  On   12 JUN 1995 095338 +0100
