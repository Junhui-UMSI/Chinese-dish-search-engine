Trail Mix Bread
Vegetable oil, Salt, Vegetable oil, Salt, Water, Yeast, Water, Yeast *, Trail mix, Bread flour, FOR 11/2 POUND LOAF, Bread flour, FOR 2POUND LOAF, Brown sugar, Brown sugar, Trail mix, 
The next group are from the Bread Machine website --    http://members.iex.net/~dahasher/Bread_Machine.html    Measure carefully, placing all ingredients except trail mix in bread  machine pan in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Add trail mix at  the raisin/nut signal or 5 to 10 minutes before last kneading cycle ends.    Select Basic/White cycle. Use medium or light crust color. Remove baked  bread from pan and cool on a wire rack.    *Yeast amount is correct through proportionately less than that called for  in the 1 1/2 pound recipe.
