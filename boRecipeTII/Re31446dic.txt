Barbecue Sauce
Lemon; sliced, Tomato puree, Red Devil  Hot Sauce, Sugar, Celery stalk; sliced, Water, Red pepper flakes; crushed, Onion; sliced, 
Combine all the ingredients in a heavy pot and heat just till hot.  Dont  bringto a boil or the sauce will turn dark and become thin.  Cool the sauce  to room temperature, strain it, and store it in a tightly covered jar in  the refrigerator.  NOTES : Makes about 5 cups
