Pork Apple Steaks
applesauce, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, Pork steak, Sherry, 
Brown steak on both sides in frying pan sprayed with cooking spray or under  a broiler. Arrange in baking pan large enough to hold in single layer.  Combine remaining ingredients in small bowl. Stir. Spoon about 1/2 mixture  over the steaks. Cook, uncovered, in 350 oven for about 20 minutes. Turn  steaks over. Spoon second 1/2 of sauce over. Return to oven and cool for  about 30 more minutes or until tender.    Per Serving      155    calories 3 grams fat    Posted to recipelu-digest by Abtaxel <Abtaxel@aol.com> on Mar 2, 1998
