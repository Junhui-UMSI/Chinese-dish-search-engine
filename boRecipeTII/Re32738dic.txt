Sweet Potato Crunch
Sweet potatoes, Butter, Bourbon, Cornflakes, Light brown sugar, CRUNCY TOPPING, Butter, Pineapple tidbits, Nutmeg, Light brown sugar, 
Preheat overn to 350.  Mash potatoes until smooth and add to rest of the  main ingredients. Can be mixed in a food processor. Turn into souffle dish  or 2 qt shallow baking pan. Top with crunchy topping.    TOPPING: mix brown sugar, melted butter and crushed cornflakes. Toss until  well coated.  Sprinkle over sweet potato mixture. Bake uncovered for 30  minutes or until topping is brown and crisp. Serves 8 -10.
