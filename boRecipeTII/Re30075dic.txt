Balsamic Peppered Chicken
Balsamic vinegar, Lemon pepper; seasoning, Chicken broth, Butter, Cherry tomatoes; (8 to 12), Skinless boneless chicken, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Garlic, Parsley sprig, 
Flatten chicken to 1/4 inch thickness. Press lemon-pepper seasoning evenly  on both sides. In large frypan, place oil and heat to medium temp. Add  chicken and cook, turning once about 7 minutes or until fork can be  inserted in chicken with ease. Remove chicken to warm serving platter; keep  warm. In medium bowl, mix together vinegar, broth and garlic; add to  frypan. Cook over medium-high, scraping up brown meat bits, about 2 minutes  or until mixture is reduced and syrupy. Add butter; stir to melt. Place  chicken on serving dish and spoon sauce over chicken. Garnish with parsley  and tomatoes. Amount per serving Calories 278 Calories from Fat 150 (17g,  8g sat fat, 111mg chol) Fat 54%; Protein 43%, Carb 3% (no sugars); 694 mg  sodium.    Recipe by: Qty bag; Ralphs house brand.
