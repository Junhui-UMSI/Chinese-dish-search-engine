Banana Nut Pancakes
Baking Powder, Honey, Egg whites, Vegetable oil; for frying, Bananas; mashed, Water, Salt, Egg yolks; beaten, Walnuts, Vegetable oil, Rice flour, Xanthan gum, 
Combine flour, baking powder, salt and xanthan gum in medium bowl.  Stir  in water, oil, honey, egg yolks, banana and nuts.  Beat egg whites in  medium bowl until stiff peaks form. Fold into batter.  Pour batter onto  hot oiled griddle or skillet; cook on both sides until golden brown.
