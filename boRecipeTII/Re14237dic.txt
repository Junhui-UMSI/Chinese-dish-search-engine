White Sangria
Ginger ale, Fresh lime juice, Fresh Lemon Juice, Fresh orange juice, dry white wine, Navel orange, Lemon, Seltzer, 
In a large pitcher stir together citrus juices, seltzer or club soda,  ginger ale, wine, Pimms, and half of orange and lemon wedges. Add ice  cubes and serve sangria with remaining orange and lemon wedges.
