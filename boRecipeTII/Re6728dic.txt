Polish Kapusta Soup
Water, Garlic powder, Cabbage, tomato sauce, Onions, Beef chuck, Red potatoes, Sugar, Garlic, White pepper, 
Place cabbage, onions, pepper and water in a large soup kettle; cover, heat  to boiling.  Add beef and tomato sauce; cover and heat to boiling. Lower  heat; simmer for 1 hour. Add potatoes, simmer 1 hour or until meat is  tender.  Add sugar and garlic.
