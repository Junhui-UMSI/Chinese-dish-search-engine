Basil Bread
Pesto, Store bought pizza dough, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. On a greased baking sheet, roll pizza into a 9  x 13 rectangle. Brush pesto over entire surface. Roll the dough lengthwise  to form a log. Brush surface with olive oil and bake for 30 minutes. Slice  on the diagonal to show spiral pattern. Serve.
