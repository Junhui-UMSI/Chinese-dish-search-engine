Peanutty Fruit Grill
Smooth peanut butter, White bread, Cranberry orange relish, Butter, Crushed pineapple; well, 
These "Savory Sandwiches" are from the September, 1974 issue.    Butter both sides of bread. Combine peanut butter and crushed pineapple;  spread evenly on 6 slices of bread. Top peanut butter mixture with  cranberry orange relish. Close sandwiches with remaining bread slices.  Grill on both sides until golden brown. Cut in half and serve hot. Garnish  with celery sticks and carrot curls. Makes 6 sandwiches. Posted to EAT-L  Digest  by Cecilia <elric01@IMPERIUM.NET> on Sep 1, 1997
