Quick Hamburger Hot Dish
American cheese; cubed, Sliced ripe olives, (no. 2) tomatoes, Lean hamburger meat, Onion, Ripe olive juice, Dry elbow macaroni, 
Brown meat and onions together. Add other ingredients. Simmer, covered,  for about 25 minutes, or until macaroni is cooked. Serve piping hot. Serves        4    bountifully.     Great for a busy clubwoman!    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
