Vidalia Onion Muffins
Self-rising flour, Pecans, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Egg, Old-fashioned rolled oats, Chopped Vidalia onion, Grated extra-sharp Cheddar, milk, 
In a large bowl whisk together the egg, oil and milk; add the flour, oats  and the sugar; and stir the mixture until it is just combined. Fold in the  onion, Cheddar and pecans; spoon the mixture into 36 buttered 1/8 cup  muffin tins (gem tins), and bake the muffins in the middle of a preheated  400 degree oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden. Turn the  muffins out onto racks and serve them warm or at room temperature. Makes 36  muffins.
