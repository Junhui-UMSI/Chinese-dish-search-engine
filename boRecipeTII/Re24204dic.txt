Treacle and Oatmeal Drop Scones(English)
Butter, Level baking powder, Medium oatmeal, Self-raising flour, Milk (scant), Salt, Egg, well beaten, Soft brown sugar, Black treacle, 
Sieve the flour, baking powder and salt into a bowl and stir in the  sugar and the oatmeal. Gently melt the butter and black treacle  together in a pan. Add to the dry ingredients and stir in with the  well beaten egg and the milk. Heat a thick frying pan or griddle and  grease lightly. Drop teaspoons of the mixture on to the pan; as soon  as bubbles appear on the surface turn over and cook the other side.  Serve hot with butter.
