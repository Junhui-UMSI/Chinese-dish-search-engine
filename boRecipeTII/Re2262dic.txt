Baked Tomatoes
Tomato, Parmesan cheese, Basil, Butter, salt and pepper, 
Oven Temp: 350F / 180C / Gas Mark 4  1. Preheat oven.   2. Cut out stems of tomatoes, leaving small cavity.  Place tomatoes in muffin tin. Fill with cheese and sprinkle with basil.  Place 1 tablespoon of butter over each tomato and season  to taste.      3. Bake 30 min.
