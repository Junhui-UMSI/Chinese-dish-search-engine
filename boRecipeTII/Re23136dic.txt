Tuna a la King
salt and pepper, English muffins, Chopped pimiento, Green pepper; diced, Fresh mushrooms, White sauce, Tuna; drained and flaked, Dry sherry; optional, Butter, 
In a chafing dish (or heavy saucepan), melt butter and saute green pepper  and mushrooms until pepper is tender. Add white sauce, tuna, pimiento,  sherry, and salt and pepper. Heat and serve on warm muffings.    From:  The Tuna Cookbook
