Prawn and Garlic Sauce
olive oil, fresh chives, Butter, prawns, garlic, 
Heat the oil and the butter until butter is melted Add garlic and cook  gently for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally    Stir in the prawns, cook gently until heated through. Season with salt and  pepper. Stir in the chopped chives then pour sauce over hot pasta    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
