Angel Hair Pasta with Cilantro, Sun-Dried Tomatoes and Feta
Fresh cilantro, Angel hair pasta, Olive oil, "Bella" sun-dried tomatoes, Feta cheese, 
Combine first 3 ingredients and set aside. Cook pasta according to  directions and toss with the tomatoes, cilantro and feta cheese. Serve warm  or cold.    NOTES : From a recipe leaflet that came with a jar of "Bella" brand  sun-dried tomatoes.
