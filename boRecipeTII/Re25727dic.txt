Baba Ganouj (Tangy Eggplant Dip)
Chopped fresh parsley (opt), Salt, Eggplants, Lemon, Garlic cloves, pressed, Tahini, Black pepper to taste, 
Preheat oven to 300F. Pierce eggplant with a fork and bake whole until  eggplant begins to deflate (about 40 minutes). Let cool and scoop out  insides and mash with a fork.
