Applesauce-Nut Cake
Seedless raisins, Applesauce, Cinnamon, Pecans, Ground nutmeg, Eggs, Butter, Brown sugar, Flour, Sugar, Baking Powder, Ground cloves, Salt, 
Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs and applesauce; beat well. Combine dry  ingredients and stir into applesauce mixture. Add raisins and pecans and  mix well. Pour into greased 13x9x2 inch pan. Bake at 325 for 2 hours.
