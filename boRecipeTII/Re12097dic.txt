Zombie
Light rum, Sour mix, ice, Apricot brandy, Dark rum, Ice, Triple sec, Grenadine, 151-proof rum, Orange juice, Lemon, 
Recipe by: Joe Robertson Preparation Time: 0:05 Fill a mixing glass with  cracked ice. Add light and dark rums, grenadine, triple sec, apricot  brandy, orange juice, and sour mix. Shake well and strain into a Collins or  highball glass filled with ice cubes. Top with a dash of 151-proof rum and  garnish with a lemon slice.
