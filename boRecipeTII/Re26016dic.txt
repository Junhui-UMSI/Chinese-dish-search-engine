Tofu Cheesecake
Firm, Mori-Nu Lite Silken Tofu,, Vanilla, Fruitsource (or honey), 
1. in food processor or blender whip tofu until smooth    2. heat fruitsource 90 seconds in microwave (med-high)    3. pour over cocoa powder - stir until smooth    4. add vanilla    5. add choclate mixture to tofu and continue blending for 1 min.    6. pour into premade pie shell or custard cups and chill 1-2 hours.    7. garnish with banana slices    (makes 10 servings) (per serving *filling only* - 97 calories, 5.5g  protein, 16g carbohydrates, 1g fat, 62mg sodium, 24mg calcium, 0 chol)    From: Makara.Loradii@concert.net.  rfvc Digest V94 Issue #200 Sept. 17,  1994.
