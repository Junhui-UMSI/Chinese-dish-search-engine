Baked Pineapple #2
Stick oleo, Flour, Eggs, Bread, Crushed pineapple, 
Beat eggs and flour, then pineapple. Pour into greased casserole. Melt  oleo; add bread that has been cubed. Pour on top of pineapple. Bake for 45  minutes at 350 degrees. Serve hot with ham.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 23,  1998
