Extra-Rich Scalloped Potatoes
Sweet butter, Idaho potatoes, Gruyere cheese, Garlic, S&p, Light cream, Parmesan, 
Anne McNally Heather Watts & Jock Soto, Our Meals (NY: Riverhead 1997)    Oven at 350.    Grease a 9x13" baking pan with 1 T butter. Arrange: 1/3 the potatoes, 1/2  the cheese, 1/2 the garlic, season to taste. Repeat, ending with potatoes.  Pour cream over, sprinkle with Parmesan, dot with butter, and bake 90 min.
