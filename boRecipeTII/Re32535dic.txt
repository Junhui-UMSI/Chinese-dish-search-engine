Sweet Garlic Soup Crock
Rendered duck or goose fat, Gruyere cheese; grated, Head garlic; peeled and, Fresh chives; chopped, GARNISH, Flour, Egg yolks, Chicken Stock, Vinegar, salt and pepper, Several thin slices dried, Onions; peeled and thinly, 
Heat the goose or duck fat in a large saucepan. Add the onions and cook  over low heat until golden brown. Add the chopped garlic and cook for 2-3  minutes. Sprinkle the flour over the garlic, stirring with a wooden spoon.  Add the chicken stock and season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a  simmer and cook for 15-20 minutes at low heat. Remove the soup from the  heat. Beat the egg yolks with the vinegar. Temper this liaison with some of  the hot soup and pour the mixture back into the pot, stirring until well  mixed. Place the dried bread slices in the bottom of the serving bowls.  Adjust the seasoning on the soup and ladle the soup over the dried bread.    Serve with grated cheese and garnish with chives; serve immediately.
