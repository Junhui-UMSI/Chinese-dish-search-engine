Picante Bean Salad
Onion -- chopped, Mayonnaise, Lettuce leaves, Green pepper -- chopped, Mild Picante sauce, Drained, Cheddar cheese, Red beans or kidney beans --, Black olives -- sliced, 
Drain beans and combine with all other vegetables. Mix salad dressing and  Picante sauce. Combine with bean mixture. Refrigerate until time to serve.  Place on lettuce leaf lined platesand top with Cheddar cheese.    Recipe By     : Original recipe by Roberta Banghart    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
