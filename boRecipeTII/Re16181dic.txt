Washington Cookies, 3rd Place
Margarine or butter, Brown sugar, Chopped black walnuts, Baking soda, Salt, All-purpose flour, Eggs, 12 Ounce Pac chocolate bits, Rolled oats; not instant, Granulated sugar, Vanilla, 
Sift flour. Measure and sift again with soda and salt. In separate bowl,  cream margarine with both sugars. Beat eggs into margarine and sugar  mixture. Add vanilla and mix well. Stir in dry ingredients. Add nuts, oats  and chocolate bits. Stir well. Drop by teaspoon onto greased cookie sheets.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes at 350 degrees until cookies are light brown. Makes 5  to 6 dozen.    Formatted by suechef@sover.net    NOTES :  Rebecca Moore of Poquoson "I cannot tell a lie. These cookies are  so good Washington would have crossed the James River to get one."
