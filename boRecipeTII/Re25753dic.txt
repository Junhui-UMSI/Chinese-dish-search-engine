Babes Creamy Fish Chowder
Celery, Bay leaf, carrot, Fresh parsley, Northern-pike fillets, Butter or margarine, Dried oregano, All-purpose flour, Onions, milk, Instant chicken bouillon, potato, 
1. In a large saucepan, combine the bouillon and bay leaf with 1 quart  water. Add carrot, potato, celery, onion, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon  pepper, and oregano. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer 10  minutes or till the vegetables are just tender. Add fish, and cook 5  minutes more or till fish is done.    2. Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium  heat. Stir in the flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/8 teaspoon pepper till  blended. Add milk all at once, and stir till bubbly; remove from heat.    3. Slowly add the thickened mixture to the fish, stirring constantly. To  serve, remove bay leaf. Garnish bowls with fresh parsley.    Makes 4 to 6 servings.
