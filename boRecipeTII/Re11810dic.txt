Ravioli with Ham and Peas
Onion -- chopped, Evaporated skim milk, Frozen peas -- thawed, Ham -- (Virginia) julienned, 1% milk, Cheese ravioli -- fresh, olive oil, Fresh parsley -- chopped, Chicken broth, Garlic -- minced, Fresh ground black pepper, 
1.  Bring 4 qts water to boil.  Meanwhile, heat oil or melt 1 1/2 T butter  in medium skillet over medium-high heat.  Add onion and cook until  translucent, 3 minutes.  Add garlic and cook 10 seconds.  Add chicken broth  and cook 2 minutes more.  Add peas, cream and pepper.   Bring to a boil;  boil, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes.  Add ham.    2.  Meanwhile, cook ravioli according to package directions; drain.  Toss  in large heated bowl with cream sauce.  Sprinkle with parsley.
