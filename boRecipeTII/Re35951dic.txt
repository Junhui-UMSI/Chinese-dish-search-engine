Stuffed Pepper Pie
Water, Onions, Mozzarella; shredded, Egg, Butter, Instant potato flakes, milk, Green bell peppers; halved, Sausage; cooked, 
Boil peppers 15 minutes. Drain; arrange cut side up in a pie pan. Combine  onion, water and butter and bring to a boil. Stir in potato flakes, milk  and egg. Stir in sausage. Spoon into pie pan over peppers. Sprinkle with  cheese. Bake at 350 degrees 15-20 minutes.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
