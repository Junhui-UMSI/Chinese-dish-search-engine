Todays Meatloaf
Salt and pepper, Parsley; fresh or dried, Dijon mustard, Onion; diced, Green bell pepper; diced, Bread crumbs, Lean ground turkey, Garlic, Worcestershire Sauce, Eggs; or 1/2 cup egg, 
Saute onion and pepper with garlic unitl tender, set aside. Combine turkey  with remaining ingredients and mix well. |Add in onion and green peppers.  Shape into loaf and place in a shallow baking dish or loaf pan. Bake at 350  F. for 45 minutes to 1 hour. Halfway through baking, glaze meatloaf with  either honey mustard, barbecue suace or catsup.    Serves 8.
