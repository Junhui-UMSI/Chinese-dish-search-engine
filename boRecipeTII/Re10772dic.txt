Quick, Easy Chinese Chicken
Garlic powder, Chicken thighs skinned,, Low-sodium soy sauce, Chicken breast halves, Reduced-calorie catsup, White vinegar, Brown Sugar, Instant onion flakes, 75%-less-sodium seasoned, Chicken drumsticks skinned,, 
Place chicken in a 13- x 9- x 2-inch baking dish. Combine catsup and next 6  ingredients; stir well, and pour over chicken.    Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg for 1-1/2 hours or until chicken is done,  turning chicken over after 45 minutes, and basting occasionally. Yield: 9  servings (serving size: 3 ounces chicken).    Per serving: 182 Calories; 4g Fat (18% calories from fat); 19g Protein; 19g  Carbohydrate; 62mg Cholesterol; 641mg Sodium    NOTES : Low-fat cooking is important to Judy Lev, of West Bloomfield,  Michigan. She adapted one of her favorite recipes to make this nutritious  version that she serves with rice.
