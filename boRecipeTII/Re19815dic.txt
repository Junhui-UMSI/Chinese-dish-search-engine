Appetizer Wieners
Beef Wieners, Mustard, Ketchup, Worcestershire Sauce, Onion powder, Water, 
Cut the wieners into eighths and place them on a microwave bowl or paper  plate.  Combine the remaining ingredients in a small bowl. Brush half of  the mixture over the wieners.    With the microwave on HIGH, cook for 1-1/2 minutes, rotating the plate  one-half turn after 1 minute. Brush with the remaining sauce mixture.    Continue cooking on HIGH for 1 minute.  Place a toothpick in the top of  each wiener piece.
