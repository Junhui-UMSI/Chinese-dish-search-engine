Barbecued Beef and Corn Muffins
W/4" thick, ", Vegetable oil, To, Favorite, Onion -- cut into thin, Wedges, Corn muffins -- quartered, Round tip steaks -- cut, Barbecue sauce -- your, BARB DAY - GWHP32A-----, 
Preparation time: (Not including muffins) 15 minutes Cut the steaks  crosswise into 1" wide strips; reserve. Cook and stir onion in oil in large  nonstick skillet over medium-high heat 3 minutes or until lightly browned.  Remove and keep warm. Add beef strips (1/2 at a time) to skillet; cook and  stir 2 to 3 minutes. Add barbecue sauce and onions; heat until hot. Place  an equal amount of the beef mixture over corn muffins.
