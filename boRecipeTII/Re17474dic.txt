Walnut-Spice Cake
Allspice, Butter or margarine, C&H Granulated Sugar, Salt, C&H Golden Brown Sugar, Nutmeg, Walnuts, Cake Flour, Baking soda, Sour milk, -yolks, Eggs; OR..., -Whole egg, plus..., Baking Powder, 
Cream together butter and sugars.  Add eggs one at a time, beating well  after each addition.  Sift dry ingredients and stir in alternately with  sour milk.  Blend in nuts.  Spread in greased and floured 13x9x2-inch pan.  Top the batter with Bake-On-Meringue (see "Special Cake Toppings" recipe)  and bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes, or until cake tests done.
