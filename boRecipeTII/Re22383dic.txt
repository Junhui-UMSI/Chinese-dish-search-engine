Turnip and Apple Whip
Diced turnips, Onion, Granny Smith apples, Salt; or as desired, Ground white pepper, Unsalted butter, 
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350F. Peel and quarter fresh turnips. Do not bother to  defrost frozen ones. Combine all ingredients in a baking dish with a cover.  Cover and place in the oven for 35 minutes. Remove from oven, transfer  contents of the baking dish to a food processor and puree until smooth.  Spoon the puree into a serving dish. Serve immediately.
