Sweet Potato-Cranberry Butter
Sweet potatoes, peeled &, Apple juice, Fresh Lemon Juice, Tahini or sesame butter, Sea salt, Cut into 1 inch chunks, Dried cranberries or raisins, 
In a 1 quart casserole dish combine the sweet potatoes, apple juice, lemon  juice and cranberries. Cover and microwave on full power until the sweet  potatoes are tender, about 6 minutes.  Let them cool, uncovered, for about        5    minutes.    Combine the sweet potato mixture (liquids too) tahini and salt in a  processor or blender and whiz until smooth. Serve warm or chilled on  whole-wheat raisin toast or bran muffins. Stores covered and refrigerated  for up to a week; makes 1 1/2 cups.
