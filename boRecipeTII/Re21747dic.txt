1. In a small bowl combine raisins and brandy and let stand 30 minutes. In  a medium bowl combine raisin mixture, apple, cinnamon, and walnuts and stir  to blend. Stir in lemon peel, honey, and lemon juice.    2. To serve, spoon about 1/4 cup filling down the center of each hot crepe  and roll into a cylinder. Brush with melted butter and sprinkle with  Cinnamon Sugar.    Makes 8 filled crepes.    Note: To make Cinnamon Sugar, in a small bowl stir together 1/4 teaspoon  cinnamon and 2 tablespoons sugar.
