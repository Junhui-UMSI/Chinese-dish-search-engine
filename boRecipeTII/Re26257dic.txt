Tomato and Avocado Pinwheel Salad
Garlic, Sweetener, olive oil, cut into thin wedges, Lime juice, Balsamic vinegar, Tomatoes, cut into wedges, SWEET VINAIGRETTE, Avocadoes, peeled & pitted,, Onion, grated, 
Sprinkle tomato & avocado wedges with lime juice.  On a serving platter,  arrange laternate slices of tomato & aovcado in a pinwheel fashion.  VINAIGRETTE: Whisk together vinegar, garlic, salt, onion & sweetener.  Gradually pour in the oil & whisk to blend. Pour this vinaigrette over the  wedges. Serve.
