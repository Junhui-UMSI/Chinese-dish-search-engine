Poppy Seed Dressing #1
Onion; grated, Honey, Vinegar, Onion juice  --or--, Dry mustard, Sugar  --or--, Salt, Poppy seed, Vegetable oil, 
Mix all ingredients inn blender for a few seconds (or until onion is  pulverized). Excellent over fruit salad or green salad. Can be stored in  refrigerator for weeks. Makes about 1 quart.
