Teriyaki Marinade #2
Garlic salt and cracked, Garlic, Cooking oil, Bottle Kikkoman teriyaki, Ground ginger, Can pineapple juice, Sugar, 
Combine all ingredients well. Marinate meat of your choice for at least 24  hours. Cook over coals, preferably, and baste often with marinade.  Excellent for beef, chicken, ribs or shish kabobs.
