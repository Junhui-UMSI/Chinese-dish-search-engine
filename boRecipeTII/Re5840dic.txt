Piperade or Piperrada (Basque Pepper Stew)
Salt, Ripe tomatoes, peeled,, Crushed hot red pepper, Vegetable oil, Onion, Cloves garlic, chopped, Black Pepper, Green peppers, cored, seeded, 
Heat oil in deep skillet over low heat. Add onion and cook, stirring  frequently, until tender but not brown, about 5 minutes. Add green peppers  and garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until peppers become tender,  about 7 minutes. Add tomatoes, crushed hot pepper and salt and pepper to  taste. Cook, uncovered, over medium heat, stirring often, until stew is  thick, about 30 minutes. Adjust seasonings to taste. Makes 2 to 3 servings.    Posted to FOODWINE Digest 22 Jan 97 by "Ted D.
