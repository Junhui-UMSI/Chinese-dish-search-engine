Albondegas
Water, Green onions sliced, garnish, Beef consomme, Tomato sauce, Potatoes-cut into 1" pieces, Carrots-halved lengthwise &, Stalks celery-cut into 1", 
Combine first 3 ingredients - cover bring to a boil. Add potatoes and  simmer 10 minutes. Drop in prepared meatballs (below) and carrots. Cook 10  minutes.  Add celery. Simmer until vegetables are tender 10-15 minutes.  Garnish with green onions.
