Swedish Mustard
Vegetable oil, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, Granulated sugar, Cider vinegar, Boiling water, Dry mustard, 
Place all the ingredients in the container of a blender or food processor  fitted with a steel blade. Process for 1 minute. Scrape down sides with a  rubber spatula & process for 30 seconds longer. Keep in a well-sealed  container & allow to stand overnight before using.    Makes 3/4 cup.
