Trio Garlic Horseradish Mashed Potatoes
Soft butter to taste, milk, Horseradish, Salt to taste, Russet burbank potatoes, Garlic, 
Peel potatoes, cut in chunks and boil until tender. Drain, add butter and  milk to taste and mash until smooth. Add horseradish, garlic and salt, mix  well. Makes 4 servings.    Recipe by Ronald Perley, chef and owner, Trio Restaurant, Egg Harbor,  Wisconsin.
