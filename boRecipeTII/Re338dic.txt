From: karen@mit.edu (Karen E. Walrath)    Date: Tue, 19 Apr 1994 23:39:01 GMT  Heres my moms Lemon Bar recipe:    Crust: Mix like pie crust, pat into 8x8 pan.  Bake 15 minutes at 350.    Filling: Pour on baked crust and bake 20 minutes at 350.  Sprinkle top with  powdered sugar.    If you are really fond of lemon (I am), you can double the lemon juice and  add extra flour to compensate.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /COOKIES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
