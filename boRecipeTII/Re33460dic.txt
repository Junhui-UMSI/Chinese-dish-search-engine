Basil Cornbread
Granulated sugar, Baking Powder, Unbleached flour, Salt, Egg white -- whipped, Black pepper, Temperature, Skim milk -- at room, Basil, Cornmeal, Applesauce -- at room, Temperature, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a 8" square baking pan with cooking spray and  flour. In a mixing bowl, combine cornmeal. flour, sugar, baking powder,  salt, and pepper.  In another mixing bowl, combine egg white, milk,  applesauce, and basil.  Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients just until  moistened.  Spread batter into prepared pan.  Bake for 18 to 22 minutes, or  until top is light golden.    Recipe By     : Cheap Eats    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
