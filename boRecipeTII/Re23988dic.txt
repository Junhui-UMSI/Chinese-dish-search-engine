Asparagus Jewels
Cream cheese; broken into 24, Fresh asparagus, Salt, Sugar, Flour, Baking Powder, Eggs, Salad oil, milk, Cornmeal, Toasted almonds; sliced, 
Snap off asparagus end. Cut into 1/4-inch slices; reserve tips. Combine dry  ingredients and wet (except cream cheese) separately, then stir wet into  dry and mix well. Stir in asparagus. Place 1/2 portion of batter into paper  muffin cup. Add a chunk of cream cheese and cover with remainder of butter  portion. Garnish with asparagus tips and almonds. Bake at 400 degrees for       15    minutes. Yield: 24 muffins.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
