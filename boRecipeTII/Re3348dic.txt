Peas and Cream Pasta
Onion;, chopped, PASTA, (14 1/2oz.) diced tomatoes, Crushed red pepper, Packed basil leaves;,, Penne or other pasta, Salt, Frozen peas;, thawed, SAUCE, olive oil, Heavy cream, 
Cook onion olive oil over medium heat until tender. Add tomatoes, peas,  basil leaves, cream, salt and red peppers. Heat.
