Baked Frankfurters with S
SAUERKRAUT #10, Water, FRANKFURTERS, 
PAN:  18 BY 24-INCH ROASTING PAN              TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  COMBINE SAUERKRAUT AND WATER.  HEAT TO SIMMER.  DRAIN OFF EXCESS  LIQUID.    2.  PLACE 1 1/2 gal SAUERKRAUT IN EACH PAN.  ARRANGE FRANKFURTERS ON TOP OF  SAUERKRAUT IN EACH PAN.    3.  BAKE 35 TO 40 MINUTES OR UNTIL WELL HEATED.    NOTE:  1.  SAUERKRAUT AND FRANKFURTERS MAY BE LAYERED. PLACE 3/4 GAL  SAUERKRAUT IN EACH PAN; ARRANGE FRANKFURTERS ON TOP; COVER WITH REMAINING  SAUERKRAUT.    NOTE:  2.  OTHER SIZES AND TYPES OF PANS MAY BE USED. SEE RECIPE CARD A-25.    Recipe Number: L11300    SERVING SIZE: 2 FRANKFUR    From the <Army Master Recipe Index File> (actually used today!).
