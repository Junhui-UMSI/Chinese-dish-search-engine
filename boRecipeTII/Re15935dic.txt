Adelynes Cranberry Mold
Cranberries, Raspberry Gelatin Powder, Water, Cranberry juice, Apple, Walnut, Mayonnaise, 
1.  Heat and drain the cranberries, reserve the juice.      2.  Make jello with juice and boiling water.      3.  Chill until slightly molded.      4.  Add mayonnaise and beat until smooth.      5.  Add cranberries, apples, and nuts.  Chill until firm.
