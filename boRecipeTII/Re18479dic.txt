Vienna Crescents
Almonds -- or pecans,, Chopped, Flour, Butter, Sugar, 
1.  Cream butter until light.  Add sugar and blend well.    2.  Blend in flour, add nuts, and chill dough slightly.    3.  Pinch off a bit of batter,  form into crescent shape.  Place on  ungreased cookie sheet.    4.  Bake at 325 degrees for 23-25 minutes.  Cool on rack.    5.  Roll in, or sprinkle on, powdered sugar when cool    Recipe By     : Mae Fautsch    From: Love2bake@aol.Com               Date: Thu, 8 Dec 1994 14:07:56 -0500    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
