Avocado Dressing
avacado, Sour cream, milk, onion, Salt, -(up to), Garlic juice, Black pepper, Fresh lime juice, 
Peel & cup up avacado. Whirl in blender with milk & sour cream. Add  remaining ingredients & blend until smooth. Place avacado seed in middle of  dressing to avoid discoloration. Seal tightly & chill before serving.    MRS HILLIARD (CAROL) LEWIS    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
