Plum Tomato Salsa with Sherry Vinegar
Sliced thin, Sherry vinegar, olive oil, Pepper to taste., Fresh Cilantro, Salt and ground black, Plum tomatoes, peeled,, Medium shallot, sliced thin, Serrano chile, seeded and, Chopped fresh parsley, Seeded, and cut into 1/2", 1/4 ts dried, Dice, Chopped fresh thyme, or, 
Mix all ingredients together in medium bowl.  Serve within 2 hours.
