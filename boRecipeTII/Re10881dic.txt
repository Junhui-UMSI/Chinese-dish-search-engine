R-Mm-Impossible Hot Toddy Pie
Eggnog, Bisquick, Ground nutmeg, Sugar, Rum, Rum Flavoring, Eggs, 
Heat oven to 350.  Grease 9x1-1/4" pie plate.  Place all ingredients except  nutmeg in blender.  Cover and blend on high 15 seconds. Pour into plate;  sprinkle with nutmeg. Bake until knife inserted in center comes out clean,  about 40 minutes.  Cool 5 minutes. Serve with spices Whipped Cream.
