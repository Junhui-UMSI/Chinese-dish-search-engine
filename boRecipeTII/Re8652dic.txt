Pots De Creme
Egg yolks, Swiss chocolate, Warm milk, Bourbon whiskey, 
Melt chocolate in warm milk. Stir until it boils and the chocolate coats  the spoon. Remove from heat. Stir into the egg yolks and add the Bourbon.  Pour into the pots de creme cups.    Use the leftover egg whites to make small meringues to serve with the pots  de creme and a slivered orange rind for the garnish.    Yield: 8 servings    Recipe by: CHEF DU JOUR #DJ9430    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Sue <suechef@sover.net> on Mar 21, 1998
