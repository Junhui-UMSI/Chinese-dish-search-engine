Strawberry Parfait (Low Fat)
Strawberries; hulled and, Nonfat powdered milk, STRAWBERRY COULIS, Cornstarch, Nonfat milk, Granulated sugar, Confectioners sugar, Strawberries, Vanilla, 
From: Perry Greene <palgreen@hiwaay.net>    Bring milk, powdered milk, sugar and cornstarch to boiling in saucepan over  low heat, stirring constantly, until thickened, 15 to 20 minutes; then boil  30 seconds. Remove from heat. Add vanilla. Strain into bowl. Place plastic  wrap directly on surface of custard. Refrigerate until chilled and firm,  about 30 minutes. Prepare coulis: Puree strawberries and sugar in blender  or food processor. In parfait glasses or custard cups, alternate layers of  pudding, coulis and sliced berries. Chill.    Per serving: 125 calories, 1 gm fat    Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com on Mar 14, 1998
