Pennestroika Penne with Vodka and Spicy Tomato Cream Sauc
Of salt, Garlic cloves minced, Penne or similar pasta, Chopped parsley, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Heavy cream or half and, Vodka, Crushed red pepper flakes, Can diced tomatoes, 
Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly. In unheated large skillet  combine oil, garlic, peppers and pinch of salt. Stir to coat with oil. Cook  over moderate heat till garlic is golden. Stir in tomatoes and simmer for  15 minutes. Meanwhile boil water and cook pasta al dente. Add drained pasta  to skillet with tomato sauce. Add vodka and toss. Add cream and toss.
