Sylvies Salad Dressing
Brown mustard, Various herbs, Plain yogurt, Light olive oil, Salt to taste, Black Pepper, Dijon mustard, Juice of lemon, 
Mix all ingredients together. It should be the consistency of ranch  dressing. If too thick, thin with more oil.    Makes approximately 1-1/2 cups, or about 24 servings.    Nutritional analysis per serving: 72.6 calories; 7.4 grams fat; 0.9  milligrams cholesterol; 26.2 milligrams sodium; 90% of calories from  fat.
