Pineapple-Orange Yogurt Shake
Ice, orange, Yogurt, frozen orange juice, Water, Skim milk, Pineapple chunks, Vanilla, 
Recipe by: THE DESSERT SHOW SHOW #DS3026 To blender add milk, orange juice,  ice, water, vanilla, yogurt, orange and pineapple chunks. Blend until  smooth and serve immediately.
