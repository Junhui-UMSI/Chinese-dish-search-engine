Baked Shrimp
shrimp, Eggs, olive oil, Parsley, Flavored bread crumbs, Garlic, 
Peel and devein shrimp, dip in egg then roll in bread crumbs and place in a  lightly oiled shallow baking dish. Sprinkle garlic and parsley over top.  Pour oil over shrimp. Heat oven to 400 degrees, bake for 20 minutes or  until cooked. (Do not overcook.)    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  03, 1998
