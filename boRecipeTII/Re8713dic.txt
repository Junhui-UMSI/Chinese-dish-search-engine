Poulet Marengo (Chicken in Wine and Tomato Sauce)
onions, fresh parsley, Butter, CHICKEN, garlic, fresh thyme, Dry white wine, olive oil, tomato, Chicken broth, Mushrooms, Bay leaf, salt and pepper, Tomato paste, 
1. Sprinkle the chicken with salt and pepper to taste. Heat the butter and  oil in a heavy skillet and add the chicken pieces, skin side down. Cook  until golden brown on one side, about 5 min. Turn the pieces and cook on  the other side about 2 min. 2. Pour off the fat from the skillet, then  scatter the mushrooms over the chicken. add the onion,garlic,bay leaf and  thyme and cook about 5 min. 3. Add the wine and stir well. add the  tomatoes,tomato paste,chicken broth and parsley. bring to a boil. cover and  simmer for 10 min. 4. dicard the herbs before serving.
