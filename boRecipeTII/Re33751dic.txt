Sweet and Sour Chicken And Vegetables
(1-lb) Green Giant American, Sweet and sour sauce, Soy sauce, Cubed cooked chicken, Tomato; seeded, chopped, 
Prepare frozen vegetables as directed on package; drain. Stir in remaining  ingredients. Cook over medium heat until thoroughly heated, stirring  occasionally. If desired, garnish with toasted sesame seeds.
