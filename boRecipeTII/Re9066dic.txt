Prize Winning Chicken and Cabbage Casserole
Onions, Ounce can tomatoes;, Ounce can tomato sauce, Salt, Chicken, cut up, Celery seed, Vegetable oil, Seasoned salt, Shredded mozzarella cheese;, Flour, Caraway seed, Sugar, Shredded cabbage, 
In a plastic bag, mix together flour, seasoned salt and celery seed. Add  chicken, a few pieces at a time. Shake to coat. Place oil in frypan. Heat  to medium. Add chicken and cook, turning, 10 minutes until brown on all  sides. Remove from pan. Place chicken in large shallow baking dish. In same  frypan, place onion and cook over Medium heat for 3 minutes until tender.  Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, carawy seed, salt and sugar. Heat to boiling.  Add shredded cabbage. Stir to mix. Spoon cabbage mixture over chicken,  lifting chicken to put some underneath. Cover with foil. Bake at 350 F. for  1 hour or until chicken is fork tender. Remove foil. Sprinkle 1 cup cheese  over chicken, if using. Return to oven for one minute to melt cheese.
