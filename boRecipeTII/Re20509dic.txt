Apple Cake 2
Brown sugar, Flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Sugar, Cinnamon, Less 2 tbs.oil, Pecans, Sliced apples; (6 to 8), Cinnamon, Eggs, Orange juice, Vanilla; ----topping-----, 
Preheat oven to 375 deg.Combine apples,1/4 cup sugar and cinnamon,and set  aside.In a large bowl combine flour,remaining sugar,baking powder and  salt.Make a well in center of mixture and pour in oil,juice,eggs and  vanilla.Blend well.Spoon 1/3 of batter into greased and floured 10" tube  pan.Arrange 1 1/2 cups of the apples in a ring on the batter.Repeat with  layers of batter,apple and batter.Cover with topping swirling it in a  bit..Bake 1 hour at 375 deg.and an additional 10 minutes at 350 deg. or til  cake tests done..
