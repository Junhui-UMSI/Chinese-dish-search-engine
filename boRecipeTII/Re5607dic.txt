Pineapple Sheet Cake
Vanilla, Salt, Cream cheese, nuts, Crushed pineapple, Vanilla, Flour, To 1 lb., Water, Eggs, margarine, Cheese, Sugar, Baking soda, 
Mix sugar, flour, pineapple, water, vanilla, eggs, soda and salt. Pour in  greased 15x10" cak pan or cookie sheet with sides. Bake at 350 F. for 30-35  minutes. Icing: cream margarine, cream cheese, sugar and vanilla. Mix until  pourable. Add nuts, if desired. Spread on warm cake. Refrigerate.
