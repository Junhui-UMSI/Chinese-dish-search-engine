Avgolemono Soup (Egg and Lemon Soup)
Rich chicken stock, Eggs, well beaten, Rice (not instant), Strained lemon juice, 
In a large sauce-pan bring to a boil the 6 cups of broth. Add the rice and  simmer for about 20 minutes until tender. Reduce the heat and barely  simmer. in a mixing bowl beat the eggs until they are light and frothy and  stir in the lemon juice.  Slowly add 2 cups of the broth to the mixing  bowl, beating constantly. Stir the mixture gradually into the remaining  broth. Reheat the soup before serving, but do not let it boil.
