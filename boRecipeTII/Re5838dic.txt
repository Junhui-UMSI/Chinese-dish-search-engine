Piperade (Basque Scrambled Eggs)
Salt and pepper, Tomatoes, peeled and, Fried bread croutons, for, Garlic, Eggs, olive oil, Thinly-sliced onion, Green bell peppers, cut into, Chopped fresh basil, Ham, fried or grilled,, for, 
Heat olive oil in a large saute pan and cook onion until softened and  beginning to turn golden. Add peppers and cook 15 minutes, until tender.  Add tomatoes, garlic, basil and season to taste with sal t and pepper.  Simmer until tomatoes are pulpy. Thoroughly beat eggs in a small bowl, add  to tomato mixture, and stir until they begin to thicken; remove from heat  before they solidify. Serve on heate d plates, topped with fried ham slice  or slices, and surrounded with croutons.    >From Taste Show # TS4721    Recipe by: David Rosengarten Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #548 by "Master  Harper Gaellon" <gaellon@inch.com> on Apr 4, 1997
