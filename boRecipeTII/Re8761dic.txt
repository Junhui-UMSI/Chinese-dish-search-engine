Pow Bars
Cashew Butter -- *, Honey Or Molasses, Almond Butter -- *, Coconut, Dry Milk, Non-Instant, ** Options **, Raisins Or Dried Fruit Bits, Peanut butter, Chocolate chips, 
1. Kneed all ingredients together with your hands, adding enough milk  powder to form a stiff but not crumbly dough. Optional ingredients can be  kneaded in as well. 2. Shape into logs about 2-3 inches long and 1 inch in  diameter. 3. Roll in powdered milk, confectioners sugar or coconut.    Makes about 6. * May be used in place of peanut butter.    Recipe By     : "Backpackers Cookbook" by Margaret Cross and Jean Fiske
