Pumpkin Patch Pie
Softened orange sherbet, Oreo cookies, chopped, Tub thawed cool whip, Oreo pie crust, 
Place sherbet in bowl; stir in 1/2 of the cool whip until well  blended. Spoon into crust. Sprinkle with chopped oreo cookies,  pressing firmly into sherbet.  Cover with remaining cool whip. Freeze  4 hours or until firm. Let stand at room temp 10 minutes before  serving. Garnish with finely chopped oreo cookies & candy pumpkins,  if desired. Store leftover pie in freezer.
