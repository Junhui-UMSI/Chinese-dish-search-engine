Vanilla Frozen Custard
Whipping cream, Egg yolks, milk, Vanilla extract, Sugar, 
Scald milk in top of double boiler. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.  Cool. By spoonful, add ? cup hot milk to beaten eggs. Slowly pour egg  mixture into remaining hot milk in a fine stream, stirring constantly.  Return mixture to double boiler and cook, stirring until mixture thickens  slightly and coats the spoon. Remove from heat and chill thoroughly  (overnight). Add cream and vanilla and pour into ice cream maker.
