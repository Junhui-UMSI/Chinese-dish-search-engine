Xnipec Salsa
Tomatoes; diced, Purple (red) onion; cut into, Garlic salt, Habanero chiles; cored,, Red bell pepper; cored,, Cilantro; chopped, Cumin, Limes, Anaheim chiles; cored,, 
Pierce the limes.  Place in a microwave safe bowl.  Warm them in the  microwave.  Squeeze the juice into a non-metallic bowl.  Add the onions,  Habanero chiles, cilantro, cumin and garlic salt.  Let stand for at least  30 minutes. Add the Anaheim chiles, red bell pepper and tomatoes. Blend  thoroughly. Yields 8 servings.
