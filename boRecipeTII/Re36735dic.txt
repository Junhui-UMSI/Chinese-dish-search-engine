Strawberry Griddle Cakes
Baking Powder, Eggs, Butter, Salt, milk, Fresh strawberries, Flour, Sugar, 
In large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.  Combine eggs and milk and stir into dry ingredients only enough to  moisten batter. Add melted butter and strawberries.  Fry on hot  griddle. Serve with syrup and butter.  Typed for you by: Fred Goslin, Cyberealm BBS Watertown NY 315-786-1120    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcyber4.zip
