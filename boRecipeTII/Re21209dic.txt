Tziyerosarmas (Liver)
milk, Mint, chopped, Salt and pepper to taste, Dill, chopped, (or 2) Sausage casings, Raw rice, Eggs, Scallions, chopped, Butter, 
For stuffing, skin the liver and cut it into small pcs. with scissors;  place in a bowl.  Add rice, scallions, dill, mint, salt & pepper; mix well.       Wash the casing and dip it into warm water, to open well. Place the  stuffing in the casing (as you would stuff a sausage). Fold it carelully  and place on a buttered pan with the folded side down. Pour the melted  butter over this and roast in a 325 F. oven for 45 min.
