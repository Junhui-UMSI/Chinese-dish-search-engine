Balsamic Dressing (Fat Free)
Water, Capers, Fresh parsley, Dried basil, Dijon mustard, Balsamic vinegar, 
Combine the ingredients. Adjust vinegar to taste, since it has a stong  flavor. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator.
