Queen Scones
Vegetable oil, Sugar, Raisins, Medium or soft tofu, Baking Powder, Water, Wholewheat flour, Salt, 
Combine flour, baingp owder, salt & sugar in a bowl. Mix well. Stir in the  oil & the raisins. Put the tofu & water in a food processor or a blender &  blend well, until smooth.  Pour into the dry mixture & stir until well  combined. Turn out onto a floured board, roll out the dough & cut into  scones. Place on a lightly oiled baking sheet & bake at 425F for 15  minutes.
