Tropical Soup
Lime juice, Papayas,; seeded and cut, Bananas, Toasted coconut, Mint, chopped, Mangoes,; sliced, 
In a blender, puree mango, papaya, banana and lime juice. Add buttermilk  and continue blending. Once mixed, chill in refrigerator. To serve, pour  into bowls and garnish with toasted coconut and mint.
