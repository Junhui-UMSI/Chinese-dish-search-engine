Sweet and Sour Salad Dressing
Dry mustard, Salt, Pepper, Bacon, Cider vinegar, Water, Sugar, Paprika, 
Chop bacon & fry until brown. Stir in salt, paprika, pepper & mustard. Mix  vinegar with water, sugar & bacon. Cook over low heat stirring frequently  for about 5 minutes. Cool before tossing with salad greens. Makes about 1  cup.    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
