Barbecued Beef on a Bun
Chili powder, Green pepper; chopped, Paprika, Cumin, Lean ground beef, Pepper, Salt, Salt, Bottle catsup; (14 Oz), Vinegar, Onion; chopped fine, Black Pepper, Whole ribs celery; diced,, Whole sesame seeded, Prepared mustard, 
Mix ground beet, onion, green pepper and celery in a 2 quart casserole.  Microwave at high (100%) 5 minutes. Stir to break up beef. Add 1/2 teaspoon  salt and pepper to taste. Microwave at high (100%) 8 to 9 minutes, stirring  once to break up beef. Drain. Mix together the catsup, mustard, vinegar,  salt, pepper, paprika, cumin and chili powder. Stir into beef mixture.  Microwave at high (100%) 6 to 8 minutes, stirring after half the cooking  time. Reduce power to medium high (70%), and microwave, uncovered, until  sauce thickens, 15 minutes. Serve by spooning over hamburger bun halves.  Makes 8 servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 209 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 6, 1997
