Apple-Peanut Cake
Egg -or-, Sugar, Salt, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Peanut butter, Margarine or butter, Eggbeaters, Ground cloves, Ground cinnamon, Ground nutmeg, Canned applesauce, 
Cream together margarine, peanut butter, and sugar. add egg, beat well.  combine dry ingredients together. Add alternately with applesauce to  creamed mixture. Pour into greased waxpaper-lined 8 x 8 x 2 inch cakepan.  Bake at 350 degs for 40 minutes or until done. Cool 10 minutes. Remove from  pan to rack. Cut into squares. 9 squares.
