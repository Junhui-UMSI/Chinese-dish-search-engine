Baked Chicken Nuggets
Italian Bread Crumbs, Parmesan cheese, Basil; Dried, Salt, Thyme; Dried, Butter, Chicken Breasts, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.    1. Soak chicken breasts in salt water for several hours.    2. Remove all bones and cut meat into 1 1/2" pieces.    3. Combine bread crumbs, cheese, salt, and herbs and mix well.    4. Dip chicken pieces in melted butter and coat with bread crumb mixture.    5. Place on baking sheet in a single layer and bake 20 minutes or until  done.    6. Serve with dipping sauces, if desired.
