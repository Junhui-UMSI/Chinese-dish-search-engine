Argentine Corn Chicken
Virgin olive oil;, Broilier-fryer chicken;, Tomatoes;, Bay leaf;, Leaf marjoram;, Pepper to taste;, Frozen whole-kernel corn;, Garlic; minced, 
Renove skin from chicken.  Season with salt and pepper.  In a large  non-stick pan skillet, heat oil and cook chicken until tender,  turning. 15 to 20 minutes.  Remove from skillet and keep warn.  Saute  onion and garlic in skillet.  Add tomatoes, bay leaf and narhiran;  simmer 10 minutes. Add corn and chicken; heat through, mix well with  the sauce.
