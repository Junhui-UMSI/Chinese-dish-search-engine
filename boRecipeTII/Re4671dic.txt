Phils "Quick Fix" Gravy
Beef OXO bouillon cubes, Flour, Salt, Hot water, Coarse ground black pepper, Butter, 
Dissolve cubes in water. Melt butter in frying pan. Add all ingredients  into frying pan and cook, stirring when you feel the urge. Cook until  thickened (usually pre-boil). Mine usually comes out hot and lumpy (just  like Mom used to make).
