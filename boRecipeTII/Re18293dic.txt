Veras Squash Pie (1945)
Pastry for 9-inch pie pan, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Ginger, Sugar, Eggs, Mace, Steamed squash, strained, Heavy cream or evaporated, Salt, 
Add sugar, salt and spices to squash and mix thoroughly. Beat eggs, add  cream and mix with squash. Line 9-inch pie pan with pastry and pour in  squash mixture. Bake in a very hot oven (450 degrees) 10 minutes then  reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake 40 minutes longer or until knife  inserted in the center come out clean.
