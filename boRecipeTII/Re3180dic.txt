Pear and Apple Chutney
Mustard seeds, Dried cranberries, Ground cumin, Shallots, pear, Brown sugar, Granny smith apple, Cider vinegar, Salt, ginger, Red pepper flakes, Apple juice, lemon zest, 
URL : http://www.lowfatlife.com/condime.htm    In saucepan combine juice, vinegar, brown sugar, shallots, ginger, and  spices. Boil until reduced to 1 cup (about 15 minutes). Stir in apples and  pears. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until fruit is tender (about 10  minutes). Remove from heat. Stir in lemon zest and dried cranberries. Let  cool until room temperature. Refrigerate over night. Serve with poultry,  fish or pork.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 06 Sep 96    From:    "~REality" <Ausetkmt@CRIS.COM>    Date:    Sat, 7 Sep 1996 19:04:15 -0700
