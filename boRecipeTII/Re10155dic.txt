Quiche Honfleur
(9-inch) unbaked pie shell, Onion; sliced thin, Heavy cream, Salt, Butter, Cleaned; peeled shrimp, Sour cream, Cayenne; or so, Bread crumbs, Nutmeg, Gruyere cheese, Eggs, Chopped pimento, 
Spread bread crumbs lightly over bottom of pie crust. Saute onion in butter  until golden. Add shrimp & saute very quickly. Put onions & shrimp in pie  crust. Mix eggs, cream, salt, nutmeg & cayenne until lightly blended. Add  chopped pimento & pour over shrimp & onion. Bake at 350 for about 30-40  minutes or until knife inserted into center comes out clean. Combine  Gruyere & sour cream & pour over baked pie, place until broiler until  bubbly.    MRS B.S.
