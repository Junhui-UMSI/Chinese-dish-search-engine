Unbelievable Fruit Salad
Cool whip, pineapple tidbits, Seedless grapes, Hershey candy bars, Sour cream, Frozen blueberries dry pack, mandarin oranges, 
Drain canned fruit.  Grate chocolate bars and set aside. Combine whipped  cream with sour cream.  Fold in fruit and chocolate and serve immediately.  Note. All fruit except blueberries if frozen, can be combined and chilled  overnight.  You can then assemble just prior to serving. Makes 6-8  generious servings. This can also be served as a light dessert. Source:  Ester Yoell 1965, from Dottie Theriaults collection
