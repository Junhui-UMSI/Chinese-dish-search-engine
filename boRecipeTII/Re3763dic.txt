Penne with Marinara, Bacon and Herb Ricotta
Parmesan cheese, Fresh flat-leaf parsley;, Penne, Onion, Bacon, Crushed red pepper, Marinara sauce, Salt, Ricotta cheese, Black Pepper, 
1. Cook the bacon in a large skillet until crisp. Drain bacon on paper  towels. Discard all but 1 tablespoon drippings from skillet.    2. Add the onion and red pepper to skillet. Cook over medium heat, stirring  occasionally, until tender, about 5 minutes. Add marinara sauce; bring to a  boil.    3. Meanwhile, beat ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper in  large bowl with spoon until creamy. Stir in parsley.    4. Toss hot pasta with marinara sauce and bacon in serving bowl. Serve  topped with parsley-ricotta mixture. Makes 4 servings.
