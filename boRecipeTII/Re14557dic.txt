"Fried" Rice
Ginger, Water, Vegetables*, Garlic, Soy sauce., Cooked brown rice, 
Boil water garlic and ginger, add veggies *(yellow, green, red bell  peppers, broccoli, anything), cook 10 min. or however long you like and add  rice and soy sauce.
