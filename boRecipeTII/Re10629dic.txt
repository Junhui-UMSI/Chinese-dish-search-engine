Quick Potatoes
Potatoes, Vegetable oil, Paprika, 
Heat oven to 375F.  Pat potatoes dry with paper towels. Pour oil into  10x6-inch (1 1/2-qt) baking dish. Add potatoes; stir to coat potatoes  evenly with oil. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake for 30 minutes or until  lightly browned and thoroughly heated.
