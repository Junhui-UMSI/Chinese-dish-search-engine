Apple Walnut Bread
Salt, milk, Finely chopped apples, Sugar, Golden raisins, All purpose flour, Eggs, beaten lightly, Walnuts, coarsely chopped, Baking Powder, 
Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Beat the eggs lightly, add milk  and stir into the dry ingredients, blending well. Stir in raisins, nuts and  apples. Turn into 2 greased and floured loaf pans, 9x5x3 inches. Bake in  350-F oven for 1 1/4 hour or until a wooden pick thrust into the center  comes out dry with no dough clinging.
