Almond Liqueur
Lemons; the rind -- finely, Vanilla extract, Vodka, Almond extract, Water, Sugar, 
Combine first 3 ingredients in a Dutch oven; bring to a boil. Reduce heat  and simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally; cool completely. Stir in  remaining ingredients; store in airtight containers. Yield: about 6 1/2  cups.    Recipe By     :
