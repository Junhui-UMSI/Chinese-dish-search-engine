Raspberry Carousel
PATTI  VDRJ67A, Lemon jello, Nuts; chopped, Cool Whip, Cranberry juice, Mayonnaise, Water; boiling, divided, Apples; diced, Celery; sliced, Raspberry jello, 
Dissolve raspberry jello in 1 cup boiling water. Add cranberry juice; chill  until thickened (about 1 hour). Fold in apples, celery and nuts; spoon into  6 cup mold. Chill until set. Meanwhile, dissolve lemon jello in 1 cup  boining water. Chill until thickened. Combine Cool Whip and mayonnaise;  fold into lemon jello. Spoon into mold on top of raspberrry jello. Chill  unitl firm; about 4 hours. Unmold. Garnish with salad greens and sliced  apples.
