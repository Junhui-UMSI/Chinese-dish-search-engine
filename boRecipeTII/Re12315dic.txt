Zucchini Pizzas
Mozarella cheese; grated, EVA ESTES BXGT29B, Large zucchini, For each slice of zucchini, Black olives; chopped, Salt, Pizza sauce, Green onions; minced, ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~, 
Preheat broiler, placing rack about 5inches from heat source. Cut  zucchini into slices 1/4 inch thick. Salt lightly. Place slices on baking  sheet and add in order the pizza sauce, olives, green onions and cheese.  Broil until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 4-5 minutes. Zucchini should  be tender-crisp.  Yield will depend on size of zucchini.
