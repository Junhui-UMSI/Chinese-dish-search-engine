Tasty Seitan
Garlic powder, Vegetable oil, Onion powder, Paprika, Salt, Black Pepper, Whole wheat flour, (8 oz) of seitan, Water, 
Heat seitan, oil and spices over medium heat, 5 minutes. Stirring  occasionally. Add the flour and water and heat 2 minutes longer. Serve hot  over mashed potatoes, rice or pasta.    This next recipe is from The Lowfat Jewish Vegetarian Cookbook by Debra  Wasserman. It is similar to the previous recipe, but I havent tried this  one.
