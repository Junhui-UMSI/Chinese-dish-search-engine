Vege-Pate
Wheat germ or nutritional, Celery stalk, Carrot, grated, Salt, pepper, basil, Lemon juice or vinegar, Flour, Whole wheat, Potato, grated, Thyme, sage or other, Sunflower seeds, ground, Vegetable oil, Onion, fresh or fried, 
Mix it right in a 9 by 9 pan or use a food processor. Bake at 350F for  about 1 hour or until brown around the edges and firm and dry on top. Hand  grating the vegetables makes a very course pate. Using the food processor  makes a very smooth one. Frying the onions gives the pate a very different  flavour.
