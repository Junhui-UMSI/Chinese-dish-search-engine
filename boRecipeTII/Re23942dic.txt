Asparagus Braise
Asparagus, thinly sliced, Cashews (opt), Celery, sliced diagonally, Canned mushrooms, Bamboo shoots, sliced, Water chestnuts, sliced, Fresh mushrooms, sliced, Celery stalk, sliced, Vegetable oil, Salt, 
1. Clean and prepare vegetables.    2. Heat oil in wok to smoking point. Stir fry asparagus in wok for  about 2 minutes. Reduce heat to low medium, cover wok and cook  asparagus for 5 more minutes, lifting cover 3-4 times to stir it. At  the end of 5 minutes, add all the other ingredients except cashews  raise heat 1 notch, stir fry 3 minutes with cover over wok. Lift  cover at least once or twice to stir vegetables, so all will be  cooked through. Place food in platter, if desired sprinkle cashews  over all.    NOTE: A very lovely party dish, especially in spring, when asparagus  is available.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/cc-wok.zip
