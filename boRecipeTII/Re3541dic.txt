Pecan Puffs
Vanilla, Sugar, Pecans, Butter, Flour, 
Cream butter, add sugar and vanilla.  Add half flour, then pecans, and  remainder of flour.  Roll into balls.  Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes.  Roll in icing sugar when hot and again when hold.  These melt in your mouth  and also freeze well.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 23 November 96    Date:    Sun, 24 Nov 1996 09:19:46 -0800    From:    Pat Burrows <nomad@NETROVER.COM>
