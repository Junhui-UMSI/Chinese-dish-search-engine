Autumn Quick Bread
PUMPKIN, Molasses, Canned pumpkin, Pumpkin pie spice, Flour, Cinnamon, Cranberries, Salt, Sugar, Baking soda, Applesauce, Eggs, Orange juice concentrate, BANANA, PEANUT BUTTER BANANA, CRANBERRY, Nuts, bananas, Ginger, Raisins, Applesauce, Cinnamon, bananas, Vegetable oil, QUICK BREAD BASE, Peanut butter, 
Preheat oven to 325.  Gather the ingredients for the Quick Bread Base and  one of the variations.  Coat 4 small loaf pans with vegetable cooking  spray.  Combine dry ingredients; blend well and set aside. Combine wet  ingredients; blend well.  Mix together wet and dry ingredients. Do not  overmix.  Distribute batter among the 4 pans, and bake for 30 to 40  minutes.    Source: Lisa Clarke
