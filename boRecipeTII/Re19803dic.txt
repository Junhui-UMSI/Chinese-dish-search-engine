Appetizer Meatballs
Onion, Soft bread crumbs, milk, Salt, Vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, SAUCE, Ketchup, Egg, lightly beaten, Onion, finely chopped, Sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, Ground beef, 
Recipe by: TASTE OF HOME DEC/JAN 1996 Combine the first 7 ingredients; mix  well. Shape into 1" balls. In a skillet over medium heat, brown meatballs;  drain. Place in a 2-1/2 qt baking dish. Combine sauce ingredients. Pour  over meatballs. Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes or until  meatballs are done. Yield: about 3 dozen.
