Beckys Louisiana Spoon Bread
Milk; scalded, Butter, Cornmeal, Sugar, Egg whites; stifly beaten, Salt, Egg yolks; beaten, 
After scalding the milk in a saucepan over high heat, reduce to low and add  cornmeal.  Cook 5 minutes.  Add salt and cool slightly. Add the butter and  sugar then add the egg yolks, stirring rapidly and mixing well.   Fold in the egg whites.  Bake in a greased baking dish 350 degrees F,  about 45 minutes.  Serve immediately.
