Quick Peanut Butter Pound Cake
Eggs, Creamy peanut butter, Yellow cake mix, milk, Instant vanilla pudding, 
Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Blend well. Beat on medium  speed for 5 minutes. Pour mixture into a greased and floured 13x9" pan.  Bake at 350~ for 1 hour. Cool in pan 10 minurtes. Remove from pan and cool  completely.
