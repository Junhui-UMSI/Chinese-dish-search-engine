Swiss N Turkey Casserole
Onion, Croutons, Salt, Light Turkey Meat; Cubed, Celery; Sliced, Swiss Cheese; Grated, Skim milk, Fat Free Mayonnaise, Bell Peppers; Chopped, 
Preheat oven to 350. Prepare a 2-quart casserole dish with cooking spray;  set aside. In a mixing bowl, combine turkey, cheese, croutons, celery,  mayonnaise, milk, onions, bell peppers, and salt. Spoon into prepared pan.  Bake for 40 minutes or until heated through.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Mar 8,  1998
