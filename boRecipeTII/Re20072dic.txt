Vanilla Cupcakes
Sugar, Flour, Baking soda, Egg, Baking Powder, Vanilla, vanilla yogurt, Vanilla Buttercream Frosting, milk, Butter, 
Heat oven to 350?. Line 12 regular-size muffin cups with paper or foil  liners.    Beat butter, sugar, baking powder and baking soda in a medium bowl with  mixer on high speed until pale, about 1minute. Beat in egg and vanilla  until fluffy. With mixer on low speed, beat in yogurt mixture (batter will  look curdled), then flour just until blended. Spoon batter into lined cups.    Bake 15-20 minutes or until tops are light golden brown and a wooden pick  inserted in centers comes out clean. Cool in pan on a wire rack 5 minutes  before removing to rack to cool completely.    Spread tops with frosting and decorate as desired.    Recipe by: Womans Day - 3/10/98    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by The Taillons <taillon@access.mountain.net> on  Mar 05, 1998
