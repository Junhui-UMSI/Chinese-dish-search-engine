Pedernales River Chili-President Lbjs Favo
Onion -- chopped, To taste, Oregano -- dried, Salt to taste, Garlic, Liquid hot pepper sauce --, Bacon drippings, Canned whole tomatoes, String dried mushrooms, Comino seed, No. 2 1/2 cans kidney beans, Chili meat *, Hot water, Chili powder, ZIOLA-CBTN00C, 
* Chili meat is coarsely ground steak or well-trimmed chuck meat. If  specially ground, ask the butcher to use 3/4 inch plate for coarse grind.*  Place ground meat, onions, garlic, and bacon drippings in large, heavy  frying pan or Dutch oven. Cook unitl light colored. Add oregano, comino  seed, chili powder, tomatoes, kidney beans, hot pepper sauce, dried  mushrooms, salt, and hot water. Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer  about 1 hour. Skim off fat during cooking. Serve hot in individual  casseroles.    From "The First Ladies Cookbook" 1966 edition.
