Willadolls Hula Bread
Grated orange rind, Vanilla, Sugar, Flour, Mashed banana, Flaked coconut, milk, Pecans, Butter, Eggs, Baking soda, Salt, 
Sift flour, soda, and salt. Cream the butter and sugar. Add eggs and beat  it. Add mashed bananas, orange rind, milk, and vanilla. Stir it. Add dry  ingredients and mix. Add coconut, pecans and bake at 350 degrees for one  hour and 10 minutes in a greased loaf pan. (Sinkin Spells, Hot Flashes,  Fits and Cravins. 1988)  NOTES : I am to assume "soda" means baking soda.
