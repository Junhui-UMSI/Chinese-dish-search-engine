Pickled Beets #2
Allspice, Water, beets, Cloves, Sugar, Vinegar, Cinnamon, 
Wash beets & cook until tender. Dip in cold water & peel off skins. Make  syrup of sugar, water, vingar, cloves, allspice & cinnamon. Pour over beets  & boil 10 minutes uncovered. Pack beets with syrup to cover into hot  sterilized jars & seal at once.    ANNA MAE COOKE    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
