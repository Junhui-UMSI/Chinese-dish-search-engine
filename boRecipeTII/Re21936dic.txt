Applesauce - Sugar Free
Sticks cinnamon, Apples; pared,cored,quarterd, Nutrasweet Spoonful, Water; to 1/2 cup, 
Combine apples, water and cinnamon. Cover and simmer until very tender,  about 10 minutes. Remove cinnamon. Mash apples until smooth. Stir in  sweetener.    NOTES : I use a couple of dashes of ground cinnamon instead of stick  cinnamon.
