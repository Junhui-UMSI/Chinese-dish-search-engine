Kabobs
Beef sirloin, Onion, Tomato, 
Push the meet, onion, and tomato alternately on the stick.  Or, try an  apple instead of tomato for variety. Broil by holding close to hot coals,  turning constantly.  Cook until meat is brown and vegetables are tender.  Makes 1 kabob for 1 dinner From: Betty Crockers Cook Book for Boys and  Girls Shared By: Pat Stockett    From: Pat Stockett                    Date: 04-05-94    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Oct 26, 98
