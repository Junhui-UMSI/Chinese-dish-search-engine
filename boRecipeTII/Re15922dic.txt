Adas Pork Chops
Pork Chops, A smear of oil, Flour, Mushrooms, Parsley for garnish, Onion soup mix, (6 oz) (2/3 cup) evaporated, 
Trim fat off pork chops. Brown chops in oil in a heavy skillet. Drain off  any fat. Mix 1 cup water to onion soup mix. pour over the chops. Cover  skillet and simmer until chops are tender (40-45 minutes).    Add evaporated milk and mushrooms to pan. Combine flour and 1/4 cup cold  water. Blend into pan juices. Cook and stir under low heat until thick and  bubbly. Sprinkle with parsley.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 10 Sep 96    From:    Kathy Bohaty <KathysWaltz@MSN.COM>    Date:    Wed, 11 Sep 1996 21:32:17 UT
