Lemon Cheesecake
Butter, Biscuits, Soft cheese, Egg yolks, Caster sugar, rind and juice of 1 lemon, Gelatine, Yohurt, 
Mix melted butter and biscuits and spred over bottom of 8 inch cake tin  chill till set. Line tin with parchment paper first.    Filling: melt gelatine in 1/2 cup water mix all ingrediens in bleder or by  hand till smooth pour over base and chill 2-3 hrs till set.Decorate with  fruit.
