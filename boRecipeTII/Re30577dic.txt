Tagliatelle with Red Seafood Sauce
Garlic cloves, finely, Sugar, (16-oz) spinach tagliatelle, Fresh thyme sprig (opt.), Crushed red pepper, Fresh thyme leaves or, Mussels (opt.), 14 1/2-oz cans diced, Dried thyme leaves, Salt, Cod fillets, cut into 1-inch, Dry white wine, Cleaned calamari (squid),, olive oil, Large shrimp, shelled but, 
1. In large skillet, heat 2 T oil over medium heat.  Add garlic, thyme, red pepper, and 1/4 t salt; cook 2  minutes. Add shrimp and cod to skillet-saute with  slotted spoon until shrimp and cod are almost cooked  through-about 3 minutes. Cut calamari crosswise into  1/2-inch-thick rings; add to seafood mixture and cook  1 minute (do not overcook as calamari will toughen).  Remove seafood to large bowl or plate; cover to keep  wann and set aside. 2. Add remaining 3 T oil to  skillet. Stir in tomatoes with their juice, the wine,  sugar, and remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt to skillet to  make sauce; cover and simmer 10 minutes. Meanwhile,  cook tagliatelle following package directions. Uncover  sauce and cook 5 minutes longer. Remove from heat and  gently stir in reserved seafood. 3. If desired, just  before serving, steam mussels until they open. Discard  any which dont open. To serve, transfer cooked pasta  to serving platter, top with sauce and seafood mixture  and toss until well combined. Garnish platter with  mussels and fresh thyme sprig, if desired. Note:  Grippa Brand calamari, cleaned and frozen in 2 1/2-lb  packages, can be mail ordered from BG Lobster & Shrimp  Corp. Call (800) 221-7664.
