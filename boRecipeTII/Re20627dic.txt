Apple Corn Johnny Cake/muffins
Sugar, Applesauce (14 oz can), Baking Powder, Corn meal, Salt, Canola oil, Rice flour, Eggs, 
Breakfast idea without gluten or dairy.  Serve warm with maple syrup or as  a quickbread with soup or salad.    In a large mixing bowl combine cornmeal, rice flour, baking powder, salt  and sugar.  Beat egg whites until stiff.    To the dry ingredients, add egg yolks, oil, applesauce and bacon. Stir  well.    Fold in egg whites.  Pour batter into greased 8x8 inch baking pan (or  prepared muffin pan).  Bake in 425 F oven for 45 min or until an inserted  knife comes out clean.  Serves 4-5.
