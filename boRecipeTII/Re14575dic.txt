"Home Made" Potato Soup
Carrot; pared and sliced, Stalk celery; sliced, Chicken bouillon cubes, Water, Potatoes; peeled and cut, Parsley flakes, (13-ounce) evaporated milk, Butter or margarine, Chives, Leeks; washed and cut up in, Onions; chopped, Pepper, Salt, 
Put all ingredients in cooker except evaporated milk and chives. Cover and  cook on low 10-12 hours or high 3-4 hours. Stir in evaporated milk with  masher before serving. Serve topped with chopped chives.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 448 by "rudolph"  <rudolph@1st.net> on Jan 4, 1998
