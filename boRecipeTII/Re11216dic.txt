Raisin Sauce for Ham or Cabbage Rolls
Seedless raisins, Flour, Brown sugar, Water, Vinegar, Dry mustard, Sherry, 
Mix dry ingredients, add vinegar and water.  Stir in raisins which have  been standing in cold water for about 20 minutes.  Cook until syrupy, add  sherry.  May be served hot of cold.
