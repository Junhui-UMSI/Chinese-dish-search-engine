Apple Pound Cake
Baking soda, Flour, Sugar, Vegetable oil, -or-, Pecans, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Eggs, Salt, Winesap apples; chopped, 
Blend eggs and sugar. Sift together flour, baking powder and soda; add to  dry mixture alternately with oil, eggs and sugar. Blend until smooth. Add  vanilla. Fold in pecans and apples. Bake in bundt pan at 350 for 1 hour.
