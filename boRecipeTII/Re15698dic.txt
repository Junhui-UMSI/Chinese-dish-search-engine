A Tasty Turkey Loaf
Prepared mustard, Ketchup, Onion, SAUCE, Worcestershire Sauce, Ground turkey, Saltine crackers, or more, Light brown sugar; packed, Env ( 1 1/2 oz.) sloppy joe, Salt and white pepper; to, Eggs, 
Preheat the oven to 350 and lightly oil a 9x13-inch baking dish.    In a small bowl, combine the sauce ingredients and set aside.    In a large bowl, combine all the loaf ingredients and mix well. Form the  mixture into a loaf and place in the baking dish. Brush the sauce evenly  over the top. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.    Bake for 1 hour or until done.
