Angel Delight
WILSON BWVB02B, Cherry pie filling, Pkg. instant vanilla pudding, Angle food cake, milk, Carton sour cream, 
Break up angel food cake into large bowl. Mix vanilla pudding with milk and  add sour cream. Combine pudding and sour cream in bowl with cake. Let sit  in refrigerator until firm. Top with cherry pie filling and refrigerate  until cold.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1016 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Jan 16, 1998
