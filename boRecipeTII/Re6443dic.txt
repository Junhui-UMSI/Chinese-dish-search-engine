Plum Sorbet
Sugar, Fresh Lemon Juice, Cherry brandy, Plums; peeled and diced, 
Combine all ingredients in a food processor and blend until smooth.    Pour into a container and freeze for several hours. Remove from the  freezer, whip, and freeze again for 2 hours.    Let the mixture stand for 5 minutes to soften slightly before serving.
