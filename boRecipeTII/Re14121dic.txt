White Clam Sauce #1
Butter, Parsley; finely chopped, Shallots; finely chopped, Clam juice, Clams; minced, olive oil, Garlic, 
Date: Wed, 6 Mar 1996 11:53:11 -0500    From: kmeade@ids2.idsonline.com (The Meades)    Recipe By: The Gourmet Cookbook Volume II    In a saucepan heat the olive oil and butter and in it saute the garlic and  shallots until the shallots are golden.  Add the clam juice and simmer for  5 minutes. Stir in the clams and parsley and bring the mixture to a boil.
