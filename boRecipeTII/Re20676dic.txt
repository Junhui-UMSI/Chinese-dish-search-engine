Apple Crisp #08
Quick-cooking oats, Ground nutmeg, Sliced tart apples (about 4, Brown sugar, Ground cinnamon, -(up to), Flour, Margarine or butter;, 
From: lwolfgra@plains.NoDak.edu (Linda M Wolfgram)    Date: Fri, 6 May 1994 22:40:29 GMT  This recipe is from the Betty Crocker cookbook.  Its quick, easy, and  delicious!    Heat oven to 375.  Grease 8x8x2 square pan.  Arrange apples in pan.  Mix  remaining ingredients.  Sprinkle over apples.  Bake about 30 minutes or  until topping is golden brown and apples are tender. Serve warm and, if  desired, with cream or ice cream. 6 servings, 290 calories per serving.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /DESSERTS    From rec.food.cooking archives.
