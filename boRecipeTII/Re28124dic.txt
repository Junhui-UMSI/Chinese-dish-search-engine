Baked Crab Au Gratin
Butter, Onions, Diced), Grated med cheddar, salt and pepper, Green Pepper, Tomatoes (peeled, seeded &, Heavy cream, Sliced almonds, Flour, Chopped red pepper, Cooked carb meat, 
Preheat oven to 450. Saute vegetables in butter until tender.  Sprinkle with flour; stir until well blended. Add cream & simmer,  stirring, until thick. Stir in crab meat & seasonings. Spoon hot  mixture into greased shallow baking dish.  Sprinkle almonds & cheese.  Bake in oven just until cheese is golden.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
