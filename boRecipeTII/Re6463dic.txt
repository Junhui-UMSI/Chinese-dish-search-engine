Plum-Sauced Chicken
Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, Onion powder, Corn oil, Chickens,broiler-fryer,8ths, Dark corn syrup, Lemon juice, Damson plum jelly, Ginger,ground, 
1. Combine all ingredients except chicken in a large bowl; mix until  blended.   2. Add chicken pieces, turning to coat.   3. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour.   4. Place chicken pieces on a rack in a foil-lined shallow roasting pan.   5. Bake in preheated 350F. oven 45 minutes, basting chicken with plum  jelly mixture occasionally; turn once.   6. Increase oven temperature to 450F.; bake chicken, basting with sauce  twice, until well glazed, about 15 minutes.   7. Remove drippings from pan; skim off fat.   8. Serve drippings hot as a sauce with the chicken.   NOTE: Serve with hot cooked rice and cooked snow peas.
