Strawberry Sorbet #3
milk, Orange juice, Fresh rasberries; about, Honey, Strawberries; fresh -or-, Egg whites, Honey, 
From: hz225wu@unidui.uni-duisburg.de (Micaela Pantke)    Date: Thu, 12 Aug 93 09:33:46 +0200    From: Sandee.Eveland@p0.f116.n114.z1.fidonet.org (Sandee Eveland)  Remove hulls from berries. In a blender container, place berries, orange  juice, milk, and 1/4 cups honey. Cover; blend 1 minute or till smooth. (If  desired, strain rasberry mixture to remove seeds.) Pour mixture into  9x9x2-inch pan. Cover; freeze 2 to 3 hours or till almost firm.    In a mixer bowl beat egg whites with electric mixer on medium speed till  soft peaks form. Gradually add 1 T honey, beating on high speed till stiff  peaks form. Break frozen mixture into chunks; transfer frozen mixture to  chilled large mixer bowl. Beat with electric mixer till smooth. Fold in egg  whites. Return to pan. Cover; freeze 6 to 8 hours or till firm.    To serve, scrape across frozen mixture with spoon and mound in dessert  dishes. Serves 6.    Nutrient Analysis Per Serving: Calories 101 kcal, Protein 2 g,  Carbohydrates 22 g, Fat 1 g, Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 28 mg.
