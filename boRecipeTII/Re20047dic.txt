Vanilla Butter
Vanilla extract, Powdered sugar, sifted, Unsalted butter, at room, 
With a wooden spoon, an electric mixer, or a food processor, cream together  the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Gradually beat in the vanilla  until smooth. Scrape into a decorative serving dish and chill, covered, in  the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks. Let stand at room temperature for at  least 15 minutes before serving.
