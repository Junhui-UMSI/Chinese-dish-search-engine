Banana Cream Pie #2
Egg yolks; beaten, Vanilla extract, Cream, Bananas, Sweetened condensed milk, 9-inch baked pastry shell, Lemon juice, Cornstarch, Water, Butter or margarine, 
Date: Wed, 20 Mar 1996 13:05:11 -0500    From: Kattwoman1@aol.com  in heavy saucepan, dissolve cornstarch in water. stir in milk and egg  yolks. cook and stir constantly, until thick and bubbly. remove from heat.  add butter and vanilla. cool slightly. slice bananas and gently coat  bananas with lemon juice. arrange 1/2 of bananas on prepared pie crust and  pour 1/2 of custard mixture over. repeat with the remaining bananas and  custard.  cover and chill till set. top with whipped cream.    whipped cream: whip cream with 1 tb. sugar and 1 ts.  vanilla till soft  peaks form.    I have made this with Nilla wafer crumbs as the crust and it was fantastic!
