Pizza Ginger
Boneless sirloin steak, Chili sauce, Red pepper, seeded & diced, Green onions, sliced, Inch piece ginger root, Ready bake pizza crust, Dry sherry, Clove crushed garlic, Corn oil, Cornstarch, 
Cut steak into very thin slivers & place in a bowl. Add salt & pepper to  taste, sherry, ginger, garlic, oil, cornstarch & chili sauce; mix well.  Cover & refrigerate for 1/2 hour; stir occasionally. Add onions & red  peppers, to beef mixture & spread on pizza shell. Bake for 7 -10 minutes at      450    degrees.    from ERNEST & JULIO GALLO GOURMET PIZZA Booklet "Can You Top This" typos by  BUD WALL    Posted by Michael Prothro, Mikes Resort BBS on Kook-Net recipe Network
