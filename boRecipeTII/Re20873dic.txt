Apple Mint Chutney
Cayenne pepper, Fresh parsley -- chopped, Chopped, Sugar, Lemons, Fresh mint -- lightly, Raisins, Apples -- peeled and, Diced, Cider vinegar, Packed, Tomatoes -- peeled and, Onions -- chopped, Cinnamon, Salt, 
Pare outer rind from lemons and cut in thin strips. Squeeze and strain  lemons into stainless pan.  Stir in remaining ingredients; bring to boil.  Reduce heat to low; simmer for 1 1/2 hours or until thickened.  Fill and  seal jars according to Bath Basics.
