Whitefish Rolls
Bacon, Finely diced celery stalk, Whitefish fillets, Grated havarti, Finely diced carrot, Butter, Finely diced small onion, MORNAY sauce, 
Heat oven to 350. Finely dice bacon & saute. Add vegetables & sauce  until tender. Allow to cool. Combine mixture with cheese. Press  cheese mixture onto each fillet & roll. Bake in buttered pan for  15-20 minutes. Remove from oven, place on plates & cover with heated  Mornay.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
