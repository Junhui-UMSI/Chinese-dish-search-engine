Pizza Di Napoli
Kraft Healthy Favorites, Olive oil; divided, Garlic; minced, Coarse grind black peppe, Spinach; coarsely chop, Red peppers, Sweet onion; sliced, Water, Italian bread shells, 
Place peppers on cookie sheet. Broil 2 to 3" from the heat until all sides  are blackened, about 10 minutes. Place in paper bag; cool. Seed and cut  into strips. Reduce oven temnperatute to 450~. Heat 1 t olive oil. Add  garlic; cook until gragrant. Add spinach; cook and stir until spinach is  wilted. Remove from pan. Heat remaining oil in skillet. Add onions; cook  until onions begin to brown. Add water; cover and simmer over low heat  until onions are limp, about 15 minutees. Remove cover and continue cooking  until all water has evaporated. For each pizza, place onions on bread  shell; sprinkle with 1/4 c cheese. Arrange spinach mixture and red pepper  strips on shell in alternating rows. Sprinkle with 1/4 C cheese and pepper.  Place on cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Source: Sentry News (wrv)
