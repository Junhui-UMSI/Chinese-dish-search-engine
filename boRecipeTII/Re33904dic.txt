Sweet and Spicy Bean-Macaroni Skillet
(14 1/2 oz) stewed tomatoes, Chili powder, Shredded Monterey Jack, Elbow macaroni, uncooked, Water, Onions, Lean ground beef, 
In large skillet, cook ground beef and onion until meat is browned; drain.  Add beans, tomatoes, macaroni, water, and chili powder; stir well. Bring  mixture to a boil. Cover; reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes or  until macaroni is tender, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle with cheese  before serving, if desired.    Makes 6 servings    Recipe by: Bushs Baked Beans Posted to EAT-L Digest 09 Mar 97 by Sean  Coate <swcoate@PEGANET.COM> on Mar 10, 1997
