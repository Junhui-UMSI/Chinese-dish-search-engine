Pickled Herring -1
White vinegar, Black Peppercorns, Mace; (leaves), Sugar, Cloves, Onions; sliced thinly, Cinnamon Sticks, Allspice; (whole), Fresh Herring Fillets; * see, 
* skins left on and cut into 1 inch pieces.    Combine all ingredients except herring and boil for 15 minutes. Add  herring, let simmer for 15 minutes. Let cool in liquid; refrigerate.
