Barbecue Sauce #03
(12-oz) tomato paste, (15-oz) tomato sauce, Bottle (8-oz) Russian, Instant minced onions, Vinegar, Bottle (32-oz) catsup, Sticks butter or margarine, Salt, Lemon pepper, Black pepper, Box (16-oz) brown sugar,
