Wandas Bran Muffins
Nutmeg, Baking soda, Whole wheat flour, Egg whites, Skim milk with 1 tbsp lemon, Cinnamon, Brown sugar or honey, Nuts, raisins as desired, Juice, Uncooked, unprocessed bran, Apple sauce, Heinz baby food prunes, Vegetable oil, 
Preheat oven to 350. Combine milk oil, egg, sugar,  prunes, and applesauce with fork in bowl. Set aside.  Combine dry ingredients. Add liquid ingredients to  dry.  Stir just to moisten . Grease 12 muffin cups or  use paper cups. Bake 25 minutes. Let cook 5 minutes,  and remove from pan.
