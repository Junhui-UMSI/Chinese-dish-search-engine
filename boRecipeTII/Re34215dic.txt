Bayou Burgers, Lhj
Ground beef, Salt, Soft rolls; split, Thick slices tomato; (6 1/2, The Spice Hunter Cajun, 
1. Heat grill.    2. Lightly mix beef, cajun spice and salt in large bowl. Pat into six 3  1/2x1/2-inch-thick burger patties,    3. Grill burgers 4 minutes per side for medium and rolls 1 to 2 minutes  until lightly toasted. Arrange burgers on bottoms of rolls, top each with a  tomato slice, cover with roll tops.    * Available in specialty stores and supermarkets or from The Spice Hunter,  Inc., 800-444-3061.    Prep time: 8 minutes Grilling time: 10 minutes    (C) Copyright 1997, Meredith Corporation, All Rights Reserved.
