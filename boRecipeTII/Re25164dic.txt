Tomato-Rosemary Salad
Red onion, Virgin olive oil, Tomatoes, Fine chopped fresh rosemary, Chopped Italian parsley, Salt, Balsamic vinegar, 
Cut tomatoes into 1/2-inch dice.  Chop red onion into 1/4-inch pieces.  Combine tomatoes, onion, rosemary, parsley and salt to taste.     Toss with vinaigrette dressing composed of 3 parts olive oil and 1 part  balsamic vinegar.  Makes 4 servings.     Each serving contains about: 119 calories; 84 mg sodium; 0 cholesterol;  10 grams fat; 7 grams carbohydrates; 1 gram protein; 0.79 gram fiber.
