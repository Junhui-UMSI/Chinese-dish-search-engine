Vegetarian Medley
About 1 c. water, Any kind of noodle or rice, olive oil, Zucchini, Jar tomato sauce and spices, Green peppers, Cayenne pepper, Onion, Parmesan cheese, 
Slice vegetables and saute in olive oil for few minutes (5 no more than  10). Add tomato sauce and some water. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes. Add  Parmesan. Serve over pasta and noodles. Fast - delicious. Low calorie and  good for you! Mt.
