Peanutty Chicken Salad
Curry powder, Prepared mustard, Nonfat mayonnaise, Skinless boneless chicken, Spanish peanuts, (16 oz) frozen green peas, Plain lowfat yogurt, 
Cook peas according to package directions. Drain. Mix yogurt, mayonnaise,  mustard and curry powder in large bowl. Stir in peas and cooked chicken.  Refrigerate at least 1 hour. Stir in peanuts just before servings. Serves  4. MC formatting by bobbi744@sojourn.com    Recipe by: Birds Eye Peas Pkg.
