Three Fruit Cobbler Melange
Nutmeg; to taste, Bag frozen peaches; small, Pecans broken into small, Frozen raspberries or, Brown sugar, Pear; peeled and sliced, Cube butter; (not too much), Granny smith apples; peeled, Lowfat granola or museli, Cinnamon; to taste, 
Place apple slices in a small glass baking dish 9 x 12 inch to the top. Add  peaches, pear, butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Mix and bake in  microwave for approximately 12 minutes. (Until apples are soften, but not  mushy.) Remove and add raspberries or blueberries. Gently fold mixture  together. Add granola. Serve in individual dishes. Top with whipped cream  and a splash of raspberry syrup or with lowfat vanilla frozen yogurt.    Recipe by: Lisa Lepsy    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by LizaCooks <LizaCooks@aol.com> on May 2, 1998
