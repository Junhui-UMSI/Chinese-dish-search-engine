Lemon Pepper Chicken
chicken, Margarine, Soy sauce, Lemon pepper, 
Source: The Four Ingredient Cookbook, by Linda Coffee and Emily Cale    Place chicken in greased bakeing dish. Spread margarine, soy sauce and  lemon pepper on each peice of chicken.
