Puerco Filling for Tortillas
Onion, (4-oz) diced green chiles, Ground cumin, Salt, Garlic, Potato; cooked and diced, Ground pork, Ancho chile pepper; dried, Tomato; chopped, Dried Oregano, Boiling water, 
Date: Fri, 8 Mar 1996 21:26:33 +0000    From: "Diana Rattray" <diana@EbiCom.net>    Recipe By: Tortilla International  Remove seeds and veins from ancho chile.  Wash ancho and soak in boiling  water for 30 minutes.  Place the ancho, water and tomato into a blender or  food processor and putee until smooth.    Crumble pork into a skillet and cook until browned (about 20 min) over  medium high heat.  Add onion and garlic and cook until soft.  Add the diced  green chiles, oregano, and cumin.  Stir in the ancho-tomato puree, check  the seasoning, and salt to taste.    Add potato and heat, stirring occasionally, until the potato is heated  through.    NOTES : Good quick filling for sopes, tacos, flautas, burritos, and  chimichangas.    CHILE-HEADS DIGEST V2 #263    From the Chile-Heads recipe list.
