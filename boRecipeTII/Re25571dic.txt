Avocado Logs
Worcestershire Sauce, Mashed avocado, Cream cheese, Dry-roasted cashew nuts;, Paprika, Salt, Tabasco, Garlic clove; crushed and, Sharp cheddar cheese; grated, Lime juice, 
Combine all ingredients except paprika, and mix well. Cover mixture and  refrigerate 30 minutes. Divide mixture in half and shape each into roll.  Sprinkle waxed paper with paprika, turning roll to cover with paprika. Wrap  each roll in foil and refrigerate until ready to serve. Slice and serve  with crackers. May be made up to 2 days ahead of serving. Yields 2 logs.
