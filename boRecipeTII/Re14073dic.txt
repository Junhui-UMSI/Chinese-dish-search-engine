White Chocolate Pecan Pie
White chocolate baking, Vanilla, Pecan halves, Eggs, pecans, Butter or Margarine, Salt, Unbaked pie crust, Sugar, Light corn syrup, 
Bake pie crust at 450 degrees for 5 mintues. Combine butter, sugar and corn  syrup. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until sugar dissolves. Let  cool slightly. Add chopped pecans and next three ingredients; stir well.  Pour filling into prebaked pie crust; sprinkle with two-thirds of chopped  white chocolate. Top with pecan halves. Bake at 325 degrees for 50-55  minutes or until set. Cover with aluminum foil during last 10 minues of  baking to prevent excessive browning. Let cool on wire rack. Place  remaining chopped white chocolate in small heavy-duty zip-top plastic bag  in hot water until chocolate melts. Remove bag from water. Snip a tiny hole  in one corner, using scissors; drizzle chocolate over pie.
