Tuscan Summer Salad (Tabasco)
olive oil, tomatoes, Black olives, Cucumber, Balsamic vinegar, Fresh basil leaves, Italian bread, Garlic, TABASCO pepper sauce, Red onion, Arugula leaves, Salt, Red pepper, Yellow pepper, Capers, 
Tear bread into large pieces to make about 4 cups. In large bowl, combine  olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic, salt and TABASCO sauce. Add remaining  ingredients; toss to mix well. Let mixture stand 30 minutes before serving.    Makes 4 servings.
