Chilled Cantaloup Soup
Ripe; (3 lb.) cantaloupe, Sugar, Orange juice, Dry sherry, 
Makes 4 c.; 5 servings.    Cut melon in half; scoop out seeds; scoop out cantaloupe meat. In food  processor or blender, combine cantaloupe and remaining ingredients. Blend  until smooth. Turn into bowl and refrigerate, covered, until very cold.  Serve in chilled serving bowls.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by SuperSecDD@aol.com on Sep 27, 1998,
