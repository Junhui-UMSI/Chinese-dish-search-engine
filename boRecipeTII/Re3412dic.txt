Pecan Brittle
Hot water, Broken pecans, -(up to), Butter or margarine, Sugar, Vanilla, White corn syrup, Baking soda, 
Cook sugar, water & corn syrup in a 10 to 12-inch skillet to soft ball  stage, stirring until all sugar is dissolved. Add pecans. Continue cooking  & stirring until golden brown. Remove from heat & add soda, vanilla &  butter. Pour & spread on buttered cookie sheet. Break into pieces when  cool. (The secret to this recipe is not to undercook.)
