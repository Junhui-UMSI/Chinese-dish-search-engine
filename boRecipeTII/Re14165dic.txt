White Gazpacho
Med Cucumbers, (1 pk) Sour Creem, Instant Chicken Bouillion OR, Pepper, Cubes Chicken Bouillion, Lemon juice, Garlic powder, Boiling water, 
Pare and seed cucumbers.  Cut into cubes so that you have about 3 cups of  cubes.    In small saucepan, dissolve bouillion in water.  Cool completely.  In  blender or food processor, blend cucumber with 1/2 Cup Bouillon liquid  until smooth.  In medium bowl, combine cucumber mixture, remaining bouillon  liquid, sour cream, lemon juice, garlic powder and pepper; mix well.  Chill  thoroughly.  Garnish as desired.  Serve with condiments.  Refrigerate left  overs.    SUGGESTED CONDIMENTS:    Chopped Fresh Tomato  Chopped Green Onions  Chopped Green Peppers  Toasted Slivered Almonds  Toasted Croutons.
