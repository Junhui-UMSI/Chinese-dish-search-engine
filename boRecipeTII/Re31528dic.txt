Sweet-Sour Beef Stew
Water, Onion, Celery, Cooking oil, Brown Sugar, Catsup, Chuck steak, Carrots, Vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, 
Combine all ingredients, except meat and carrots. Simmer about 30  minutes.  Brown meat and remove from skillet. Pour simmered sauce into pan  drippings of skillet. Place meat in baking dish. Pour sauce over. Bake at  325 F. for abt. 30 min.  Add carrots and bake abt. 20 min. more. Garnish  with chopped parsley or sprigs, pimento or make a rice ring of 3 cups  cooked rice.
