Sweet n Sour Pork Chops
Cornstarch, Vegetable oil, -(up to), Vinegar, Brown sugar, Dried dill, Pork Chops, Fruit cocktail, -(up to), Salt, Onion, 
In skillet, brown chops in oil. Remove. Combine cornstarch and vinegar and  add to oil, stirring until thick. Add water if necessary. Add cocktail,  sugar, onion, salt and dill. Add chops. Simmer 1 hour.
