7-Grain - Rice and Vegetable Salad
Chopped pimientos, drained, Garlic powder, Onion, AM Brown Wild Rice & Herbs, (Quick), cooked & cooled, Cooked AM 7 Grain Cereal, Celery, chopped, Onion powder, Canned peas and carrots, Vegetable seasoning to taste, cooled, Cucumbers, diced, 
Toss ingredients together with mayonnaise or salad dressing of choice.  Chill.  Serve on a bed of alfalfa sprouts.
