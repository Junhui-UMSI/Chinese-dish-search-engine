Peanut Minus Cookies
Plus 3 Tab. pure maple syrup, Sea salt, Pure vanilla extract, Chopped unsalted roasted, Jar natural peanut butter, Brown rice flour, 
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Have ungreased baking sheet(s) ready. 2.  Combine peanut butter, maple syrup and vanilla in a large bowl, mixing  until smooth. Add rice flour and salt; mix well. Fold in peanuts. 3. Form  cookies, using 1 tablespoon dough for each. Place on cookie sheet, spacing  1 inch apart. Flatten gently with the tines of a fork. Bake until set, 11  to 12 minutes. Cool 1 minute on baking sheet, then transfer to a wire rack  to cool. Yield: 2 dozen cookies
