Basic Candy Popcorn
Water, Butter, Food coloring, Oil of peppermint *, White corn syrup, Sugar, Popcorn; popped, 
In a heavy saucepan put the water, sugar, corn syrup and butter. Cook over  a moderate heat to 280~ on a candy thermomter. Add the oil and the food  coloring.    Stir well and pour over the popped corn.    * You can use concentrated flavorings in place of the oil of peppermint  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 652 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 31, 1998
