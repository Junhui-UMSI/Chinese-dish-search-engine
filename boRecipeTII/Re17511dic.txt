Almond Chocolate Pie
Almond, pie crust, baked, almonds, Marshmallows, Whipping cream, milk, 
Combine chocolate bars, marshmallows and milk in top of doubler boiler and  heat until mixture is melted. Cool completely, about half and hour. Whip  cream to stiff peaks and fold into cooled chocolate mixture. Pour into  baked pie crust and refrigerate at least 4 hours, until set. Garnish with  slivered almonds or favorite dairy topping. Note: Can be made day ahead.
