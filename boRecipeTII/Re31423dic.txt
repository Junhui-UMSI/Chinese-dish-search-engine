Barbecue Chicken
Pepper, Butter, Firm packed dark brown sugar, Paprika, Can tomato sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, Onion, Broiler-fryer, cut into, Sugar, Vinegar, 
1. Wash chicken pieces and pat dry. Set aside. 2. In a deep, 2 1/2-quart,  heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole, combine remaining ingredients  except chicken. 3. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4 minutes, stirring  occasionally. 4. Add chicken pieces to sauce and heat, covered, in  Microwave Oven 12 minutes. Stir. 5. Heat, covered, in Microwave Oven an  additional 5 minutes or until chicken is tender.
