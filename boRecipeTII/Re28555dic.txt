Tex-Mex Hash
Tomatoes, whole (medium), Salt, Onions, sliced, Chili powder, Ground beef, Pepper, Green pepper, chopped, Rice, uncooked, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Pan fry ground beef until light brown in  skillet. Drain fat. Add onions and peppers and cook until onion is tender.  Stir in rest of ingredients and heat until warm. Pour in a casserole dish,  cover bake 1 hour.
