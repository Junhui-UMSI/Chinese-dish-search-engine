"Finger Lickin" Pickens Sauce
Whole celery seed, Onions finely chopped, Sage, Ketchup, Horseradish, Dark brown sugar, Salt, Beer, Juice of 2 lemons, A-1 steak sauce, Soy sauce, Garlic powder, Tabasco sauce, 
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan. Bring mixture to boil. Simmer sauce  over medium-low heat, uncovered, for 5 minutes, stirring constantly..
