Beer Bacon Bread
Water, Butter or margarine, 2 LB LOAF, Bread flour, Crumbed cooked bacon, Green Onions, Sugar, Flat beer, Salt, Yeast*, Yeast, Crumbed cooked bacon, Mustard, Bread flour, Salt, Flat beer, 1 1/2 LB LOAF, Green Onions, Mustard, Sugar, Water, 
Put all ingredients in the bread maker except bacon.  Add bacon at the  raisin/nut signal or 5 to 10 minutes before last kneading cycle ends.  Select basic/white cycle and use medium or light crust colour.  Do not use  delay cycles.  Yeast amount though proportionately less than called for in  the smaller loaf.
