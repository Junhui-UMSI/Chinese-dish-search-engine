Amaretto
White granulated sugar, Pure almond extract, Vodka, SUGAR SYRUP, Water, Sugar syrup (below), 
Make sugar syrup, cool it, add all the ingredients together, shake well.  Mature a few days and it is ready to drink. Yield: 2 cups  Sugar Syrup: Bring to a boil, and stir until all the sugar is dissolved and  the mixtue is clear. Always cool before adding to alcohol mixture; heat  caused alcohol to evaporate. If tap water is heavily cholorinated or bad  tasting, (use bottled water or) heat thesyrup a few minutes to drive off  chlorination.
