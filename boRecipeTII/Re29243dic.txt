Baked Spinach Egg "Nests"
Eggs, Canadian bacon, Salt and pepper to taste, Cheddar cheese, Butter, Fresh spinach leaves, 
Wash, dry, and remove stems from spinach. Stack leaves and roll up tightly,  then slice in narrow strips. Put 1 teaspoon butter in each of 4-4oz baking  cups. Add about 1/4 of the spinach, then about 1/4 of the bacon. Break 2  eggs on top of each nest, then top each egg with 1 teaspoon cheese. Place  baking dishes on a cookie sheet and bake in a preheated 325 oven for  approximately 15-20 minutes.
