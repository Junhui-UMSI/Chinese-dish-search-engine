Basil Dinner Rolls
Chopped fresh basil, Finely chopped walnuts, Warm water; (120-130), Salt, Sugar, All purpose flour, Grated Parmesan cheese, Melted butter or margarine, Active dry yeast, 
In a mixing bowl, combine 6 cups flour, sugar, salt and yeast. Add water;  beat until smooth. Add enough remaining flour to form a soft dough. Turn  onto a floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, about 8-10 minutes.  Place in a greased bowl, turning once to grease top. Cover and refrigerate  overinight. Punch dough down; knead several times. Cover and let rise for  10 minutes. Meanwhile, combine basil, walnuts and cheese; reserve 2  tablespoons for topping. Shape dough into 32 balls. On a floured surface,  roll each ball in a 4 inch circle; place about 1 1/2 teaspoons of the basil  mixture in the center of each. Pinch dough together over filling. Place 16  rolls each, seam side down, on two greased baking sheets with rolls  touching. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 45 minutes. Brush tops  with butter; sprinkle with reserved basil mixture. Bake at 375 for 25  minutes or until lightly browned. Serve warm. Yield 32 rolls.
