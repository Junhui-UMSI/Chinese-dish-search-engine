White Salsa Dip
Limes; juice of, Salt and white pepper; to, Scallions; finely chopped, (6-oz) pitted black olives;, Sour cream, Hot pepper sauce; or to, Mayonnaise, Garlic, Fresh cilantro; finely, 
In a medium bowl, combine mayonnaise and sour cream.  Add lime juice,  garlic, cilantro, black olives, scallions, hot pepper sauce, salt and  pepper. Taste and adjust seasonings if desired. Chill until serving.  Serve with blue corn tortilla chips.
