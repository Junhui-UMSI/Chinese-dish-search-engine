"Madison County" Spaghetti Sauce
(12-oz) tomato paste, Dried basil, Water, Garlic, Sugar, Vegetable oil, -(up to), Salt, Green bell pepper; chopped, Dried Oregano, Pepper, Bay leaf, Onion, 
Date: 24 Mar 96 22:44:06 EST    From: "Lisabeth Crawford (Pooh)" <104105.1416@compuserve.com>  Heat oil in a large pot over mediun heat. Saute the onion, green pepper,  and garlic until soft, about 5 minutes. Add the tomato paste and water, and  stir until smooth. Add oregano, basil, bay leaf, sugar, salt and pepper.  Simmer, uncovered for 1-2 hours. Serve over spaghetti.    Variation: For meat sauce, brown 1 pound Italina sausage and 1 pound ground  beef, and add with the spices.
