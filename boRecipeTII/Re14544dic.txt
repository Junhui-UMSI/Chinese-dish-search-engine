"Finger Lickin" Pickens Sauce
juice of 2 lemons, Soy sauce, Salt, Beer, Dark brown sugar, Onions, Garlic powder, A-1 steak sauce, Ketchup, Horseradish, Whole celery seed, Sage, Tabasco sauce,
