Alma Blueberry Bread
Salt, Orange rind -- grated, Boiling water, Orange juice, Orange rind -- grated, Honey, Baking soda, Fresh blueberries, Flour -- sifted, Egg, Sugar, Baking Powder, Orange juice, Butter, 
Preheat oven to 325~F.  Melt butter in boiling water in small saucepan. Add  1/2 cup orange juice and 3 ts. orange rind. Set aside. In mixing bowl, beta  egg with sugar, until light and fluffy. In separate bowl, combine flour,  baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add dry ingredients to egg-sugar  mixture alternately with orange mixture. fold in blueberries. Pour batter  into large, greased loaf pan.  Bake about 1 hour and 10 minutes. turn bread  out onto rack to cool  Mix 2 Tb. orange juice, 1 ts. rind, and t Tb. honey  together.  Pour over bread, while hot. Let stand and cool. Source: Cooking  in the New South by Anne Byrn Phillips. Published by Peachtree Publishers,  Limited.
