Vanilla Raisin Bread
Sugar, Raisins; soaked for an hour, Egg, Buttermilk; to 1 1/4 cup,, Dry yeast, Salt, Walnuts, Flour; bread, Vanilla, 
drain raisins well. Place all ingredients except raisins in bread pan.  Select light crust and press start. Add raisins and 1/2 c walnuts, chopped,  at beep. After baking cycle ends, remove bread from pan and cool on a wire  rack for 1 hour before slicing.    Note: if adding vanilla with ingredients, reduce amount of buttermilk by  2-3 tb.    add 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans; add 1 tb cinnamon; this is good  using Mexican vanilla    Recipe by: Bread Machine Magic    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by KrisE56749 <KrisE56749@aol.com> on Dec 27,  1997
