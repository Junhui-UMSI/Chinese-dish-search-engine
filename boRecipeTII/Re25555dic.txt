Avocado Dressing
Salt, Ground cumin, Lime juice, Vegetable oil, Ground red pepper, Sour cream, Ripe avocado; peeled and, Garlic, 
Combine first 7 ingredients in a mixing bowl; beat at medium speed with an  electric mixer until smooth. Add oil, 1 tablespoon at a time, beating until  blended. Cover and chill at least 3 hours.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Aquasea221 <Aquasea221@AOL.COM> on Jan 21, 1998
