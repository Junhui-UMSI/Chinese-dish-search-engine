Tamale Pie Rancheros
Cornmeal, Pepper, Mixed Vegetable, Onions, Ground beef, Basil, Vegetable oil, Green Pepper, Stewed tomatoes  , Cheddar cheese, Mild green chilis, 
1. Heat oil in heavy skillet. Add onion and green pepper; cook over low  heat until soft.    2. Add ground beef and brown.    3. Stir in remaining ingredients, except for cheese.    4. Place in a 9" pie pan and bake at 375F. for 30-35 minutes.    5. Sprinkle with cheese and bake 5 minutes longer.
