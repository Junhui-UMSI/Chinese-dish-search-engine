Pineapple Pie
Frozen whipped topping, 8 oz. size thawed, Sweetened condensed milk, Crushed pineapple, drained, Lemon juice, 14 oz. can, 20 oz. can, Graham cracker crust, 
Combine condensed milk and lemon juice.  Stir well. Fold in pineapple and  whipped topping.  Spoon mix into crust and chill before serving. Randy Rigg
