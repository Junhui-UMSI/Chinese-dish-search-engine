Toasted Olive Canapes
Mayonnaise, Green Onion, Cocktail rye bread, Black Olives, Parmesan Cheese, 
Preheat oven to 475. Combine parmesan, mayonaise and green onions. Drain  the olives and stir into cheese mixture. Generously spread the olive cheese  mixture on top of the bread, using a rounded tbsp for each. Arrange on a  baking sheet and place in the oven for about 6 minutes, until the bottoms  are brown and the tops begin to turn golden brown. Serve immediately.
