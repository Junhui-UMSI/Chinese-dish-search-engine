Abalone Soup #1
Carrot; grated, Salt, Ginger; grated, Green onion; chopped, Chicken stock (see wonton, Celery, Sliced canned abalone, 
Bring the chicken stock to a boil. Add the ginger, onion, carrot and  celery. Simmer for 15 to 20 minutes. Add the abalone and salt to taste.  Allow to heat and ladle into individual soup bowls.
