Tidbit Cookies
Egg, 1oz squares unsweetened, Pecan pieces, Shredded coconut, Miniature marshmallows, Butter, Powdered sugar, Vanilla, 
Melt butter and chocolate together. In a large bowl, beat egg and powdered  sugar until well blended. Add vanilla and pecans. Add chocolate mixture and  marshmallows and stir to combine all ingredients evenly. Form into logs on  waxed paper sprinkled with coconut, and coat all sides of the logs with  coconut. Wrap and freeze until needed. Slice and serve.
