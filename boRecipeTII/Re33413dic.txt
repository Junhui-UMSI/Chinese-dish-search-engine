Basic Yellow Cake
Salt, Baking Powder, Eggs, milk, Margarin, butter or, Almond extract, Granulated sugar, Cake flour, 
Grease and flour a 10" round pan. Preheat oven to 375F. Place all  ingredients in bowl of electric mixer. Mix on low speed until  thoroughly combined, scraping bowl frequently. Increase speed to  medium and beat for four minutes. Pour into pan; smooth top of  batter. Bake 25-30 minutes, until cake tests done. Do not overbake.  Cool on wire rack for 10 minutes, then remove cake from pan and  continue cooling on rack.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.
