Walnut Rum Meringues
Egg white, Cornflour, Rum, Rum, Dark chocolate, Castor sugar, CHOCOLATE CREAM, Walnuts, Butter, Icing sugar, 
From: ynnuf@yetti.amigans.gen.nz (Doreen Randal)    Date: Mon, 29 Nov 93 21:09:38 PST  Beat egg-white until soft peaks form, gradually add sugar, beat until  dissolved. Fold in finely chopped walnuts, sifted cornflour and rum.  Drop  half-teaspoonfuls of mixture on to well greased lightly-floured oven trays.  Allow room for spreading. Bake in slow oven 30 mins. When cold, sandwich  together with chocolate cream.    CHOCOLATE CREAM: Melt thoroughly chopped chocolate in basin over hot water.  Cool a little. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in rum  and chocolate. Refrigerate until ready to use.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
