Tomato Beef
Cornstarch, Sugar, Pepper and salt, BEEF MARINADE, Vegetable oil, Vegetable oil, Tomatoes, Water, Vinegar, Flank Steak, thin slices, Garlic clove (chopped), Soy sauce, light, Wine, Sesame oil, Tomato ketchup, Cornstarch in 1 t water, Green onions, SAUCE, 
Marinate beef slices for 30 minutes.  Heat wok with 2 T oil and garlic for  30 seconds, add beef, cook for 1 1/2 to 2 minutes, remove and drain oil.  Set aside.  Heat wok, high, add tomato wedges and stir for 2 minutes, then  add sauce mixture.  Thicken if too watery.  Return beef and stir well and  serve hot.   Serve immediately.
