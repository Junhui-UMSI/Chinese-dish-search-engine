White- and Red-Cooked Chicken
(4-5 pound) chicken, Salt, Water, Fresh mushrooms, Sherry, Soy sauce, 
1. Wipe chicken with a damp cloth and place in a heavy pan with cold water.  Bring to a boil over medium heat; then simmer, covered, 30 minutes. Pour  off liquid, reserving it for stock.    2. Stem fresh mushrooms. Add to chicken, along with soy sauce, sherry and  salt.    3. Simmer, covered, 1 hour, turning bird once or twice for even coloring.
