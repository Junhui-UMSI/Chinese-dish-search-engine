Taglarini Casserole
milk, Ground beef, Onions, chopped, Tomatoes, Whole kernal corn, Mary Bowles DNSR31A, Chili powder, Eggs, Cornmeal, Margarine, Ripe olives; sliced, 
Brown meat and onions.  Stir in tomatoes, chili powder, corn, olives, and  margarine.  Cook 10 minutes. Beat eggs, milk, and cornmeal. Stir into meat  mixture. Pour into casserole dish and bake 40 minutes at 375 degrees.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #260    Date: Sat, 26 Oct 1996 15:13:39 EDT    From: colorado-pegg@juno.com (peggy g seevers)
