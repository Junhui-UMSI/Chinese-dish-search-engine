Witches Brew
Hot coffee, Quick chocolate drink mix, Sugar, cinnamon, milk, 
Bring milk, cinnamon sticks, and sugar to simmering point in large  saucepan. Reduce heat and cook 10 minutes until cinnamon flavor is  released. Add chocolate mix to the hot milk; combine with hot coffee.  Yield: 8 servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.
