Posole Stew
Oregano, Dried red chilies; rinsed, Salt, Pkg. frozen posole (Mexican, Garlic powder, Lean pork; cubed and browned, 
Cook posole in 1 quart water until soft, 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Be sure not to  season posole until it softens. Add the browned pork, salt, garlic powder,  oregano and chilies. Allow to stew 6 to 8 hours (do not hurry it). Add more  water if necessary at any stage. Leftovers freeze well.
