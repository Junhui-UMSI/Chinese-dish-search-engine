Poppy Seed Oatmeal Bread
Butter, Flour, all purpose, Flour, whole wheat, Poppy Seeds, Honey, liquid, Egg, Oatmeal, quick-cooking, Salt, Buttermilk, Baking Powder, 
In large bowl, combine all-purpose and whole wheat flours, oatmeal, baking  powder, salt and poppy seeds.  In separate bowl, whisk together  buttermilk, honey and egg.  Add all at once to dry ingredients; mix  quickly, just until combined.  Spoon batter into greased 8 x 4 in (1.5L)  loaf pan.  Brush top with melted butter.  Bake in 350F (180C) oven 45 to  50 minutes or until cake tester inserted in centre of loaf comes out  clean.  Let cool 5 minutes in pan.  Turn out onto wire rack.
