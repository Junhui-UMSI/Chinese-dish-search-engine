Baked Fish
Grated Parmesan cheese, Panfish fillets, Dill weed, 
Place fish in a 10 inch pie plate that has been coated with nonstick  cooking spray. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and dill. Bake, uncovered, at  350 for 8-10 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork. Yield 2  servings.
