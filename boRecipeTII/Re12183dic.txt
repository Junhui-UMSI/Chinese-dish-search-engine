Zucchini Bread #01
Flour, Broken nuts, Salt, Baking Powder, Zucchinii, Raisins, Sugar, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Eggs, 
Mix eggs, oil, sugar and vanilla, beating to blend. Sift dry ingredients  together; add to first mixture and add squash. Add nuts and raisins; spoon  into slightly greased and floured loaf pan and bake at 350 for 1 hour.
