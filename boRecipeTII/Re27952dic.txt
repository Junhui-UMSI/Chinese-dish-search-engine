Three Beans in Lettuce Parcels
Flageolet beans, Medium red chilli, Chives, Garlic and herb dressing, Chickpeas, salt and pepper, Black eye beans, Chinese lettuce leaves, 
1. Heat the dressing in a wok over a medium heat. Add the beans and     chilli and stir fry for 2-3 minutes until the majority of the     liquid has evaporated. Season to taste, and add chives.    Divide the mixture between the lettuce leaves and serve.
