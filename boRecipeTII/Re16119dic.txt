Warm Saute of Winter Fruit
Brandy, Sliced, Nutmeg, Maple syrup, Thinly sliced, Ground cinnamon, Apple -- cored; thinly, Water, Raisins or currants, Butter or margarine, Firm, ripe pear -- cored;, Walnuts or pecans --, Chopped, 
1. Melt butter in a saute pan or large skillet. Stir in maple syrup and  adjust to taste.    2. Add remaining ingredients. Cook over medium heat and stir until fruits  are tender.    * Timesaver Tip: Ingredients can be prepared ahead; rub apple and pear  slices with lemon juice to prevent browning.
