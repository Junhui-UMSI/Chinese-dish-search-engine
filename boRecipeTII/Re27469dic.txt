Toffee Popcorn Crunch
almonds, Light corn syrup, SKOR English Toffee Bits, popcorn, 
Heat oven to 275 F. Grease large roasting pan (OR two 13x9x2-inch baking  pans). Place popcorn and almonds in prepared pan. In heavy medium  saucepan, combine toffee bits and corn syrup. Cook over medium heat,  stirring constantly, until toffee is melted (about 12 minutes). Pour  over popcorn mixture; stir until evenly coated. Bake 30 minutes,  stirring frequently. Remove from oven; stir every 2 minutes until  slightly cooled. Cool completely. Store in tightly covered container in  cool dry place. About 1-1/2 pounds popcorn.
