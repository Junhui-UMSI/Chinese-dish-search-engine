Spelt Kamut Buttermilk Biscuits
Spelt Flour, Bob's Red Mill Kamut Flour, Non-alum baking powder, Buttermilk, Vegetable oil, Honey, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Mix dry ingredients. Blend liquids and sweetener. Combine  mixtures. Mix gently.  Batter should be like thick oatmeal. (add 1/4 cup water if too  stiff) Drop by round 1/4 cup onto oiled and floured baking sheet or large cast iron skillet. Bake 35  minutes or until golden brown. Makes 18.    (Modified from Arrowhead Mills Quick Spelt Bisquits recipe.)  Hint:  Let the batter stand for 20 or 30 minutes for lighter bisquits.
