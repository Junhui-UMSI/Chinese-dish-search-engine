White Bean Soup
-(up to), Handful parsley, White Cannalini beans (I, Cloves garlic; slivered, Veggie soup broth, salt and pepper, 
Date: Wed, 24 Apr 1996 15:42:49 -0700 (PDT)    From: "J. Blochowiak" <jabloch@u.washington.edu>    Saute slivered garlic in broth and then add parsley.  Add drained beans  with broth, simmer for 20 minutes or so. Add salt and pepper to taste. Mash  the beans while in the pot and serve with a salad and yummy bread.
