Dixies Bar And Smokehouse Grill Baking Powder Biscuits
Flour, Sugar, Baking Powder, Salt, Baking soda, Lard, Buttermilk, 
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.    In a large mixing bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and  baking soda with electric mixer. Add lard and continue to mix.  Add the  buttermilk, mixing only until liquid is incorporated.  Do not overmix.    On a floured surface, place the dough and knead in extra flour until the  dough no longer is sticky.  Roll dough out to W-inch thickness. Cut  biscuits with a 3-inch biscuit cutter or glass.  Place on greased sheet pan  and bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes.    Makes 20 biscuits.
