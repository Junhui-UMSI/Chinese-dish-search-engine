Pike Baked in Cream
Pike fillet, swiss cheese, Bread crumbs, celery, green pepper, Butter, onion, Heavy cream, Tomato, Salt, 
Heat oven to 350. Wash & dry fish. Place in greased casserole. Heat  butter in pan. Add onion, pepper & celery; saute until tender. Add  tomato & simmer until liquid has evaporated. Spoon over fish. Add  cream & sprinkle with cheese & crumbs. Bake 30 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
