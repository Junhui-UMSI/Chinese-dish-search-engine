Apple Cranberry Dessert-Using Mix
Brown sugar -- packed, Can whole cranberry sauce**, Cooking apples -- sliced*, milk, Bake it all mix, Ground cinnamon, Salt, Orange juice, 
*3 Medium, pared and cored. **About 1-2/3 cups    Combine sugar, cinnamon  and salt in an 8 by 8 by 2-inch baking dish.  Add orange juice, sliced  apples and cranberry sauce.  Mix lightly but thoroughly. Bake in a 425 F.  oven 15 minutes. Make a well in the Mix. Add milk and stir with a fork  until all of Mix is moistened. Drop dough by spoonfuls on hot  apple-cranberry mixture. Return to hot oven for 20 to 25 minutes. Serve  warm. Yield:  6 servings    Recipe By     :    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
