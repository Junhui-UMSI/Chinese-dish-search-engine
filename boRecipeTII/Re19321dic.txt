Vegetable Stock 2
Water, Mushrooms, including stems, Bay Leaves, Celery leaves from heart of, Turnips or parsnip, trimmed, Cloves garlic; peeled, Carrots, trimmed and, Stalks celery; quartered, Tomatoes, cored and coarsely, Leek, washed, trimmed and;, Parsley sprigs, Yellow onion, peeled and;, Peppercorns, 
In a large stockpot, combine all ingredients and bring to a boil. Partially  cover pot, reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours. Add additional water if  necessary. Cool stock and strain. Discard solids.    Refrigerate or freeze until ready to use.
