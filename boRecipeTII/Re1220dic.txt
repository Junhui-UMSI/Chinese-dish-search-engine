Crockpot Beef Burritos
Chuck roast; boneless, Jalapeno chile pepper, Garlic, Beef bullion granules, Onion, Chili powder, Ground cumin, Fresh cilantro leaves, Salt, Flour tortillas, Refried beans, Cheese, desired toppings, 
Place beef in crockpot with next 8 ingredients. Cook on low 8 hours or  until tender.    Remove meat and shred. Combine with 3/4 cup cooking juices.    Spread warm tortilla with warm beans and beef. Top as desired and serve.    Serve with mexican rice and torilla chips.
