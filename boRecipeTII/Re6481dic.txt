Poached Beef Chuck Roast
garlic, Condensed beef broth, Burgundy wine, Italian seasoning, Vegetable oil, Black peppercorns, Boneless beef chuck eye, Chuck cross rib roast, Water, PEPPERED SAUCE, 
Tie boneless beef chuck roast with heavy string at two inch intervals.  Brown roast in oil in Dutch oven over medium high heat until all sides are  browned. Browning gives the attractive color achieved by roasting. Pour off  drippings. Add water, beef broth, wine, garlic,Italian seasoning and  peppercorns. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low, cover & simmer 20 to 24  minutes per pound. Temperature will register 140 degrees. Remove roast to  serving platter. Cover tightly with plastic wrap or aluminum foil and allow  to stand 10 minutes before carving. During standing, roast will rise  approximately 10 degrees to 150 degrees for medium-rare. Remove string  Meanwhile, use one cup poaching liquid to prepare PEPPERED SAUCE. Carve  roast into thin slices & serve with peppered sauce. SEE PEPPERED SAUCE  RECIPE.
