Vegetable Soup
Watercress cleaned & chopped, Vegetable oil, Black pepper, Onion, sliced, Soy sauce, Salt, Chopped mixed vegetables, Water, 
Heat oil in a soup pot & stir fry onion for 1 minute. Add vegetables, salt,  pepper & soy sauce.  Fry together for 2 minutes. Add water & bring to a  boil. Simmer till the vegetables are tender. Add watercress & serve.    Jack Santa Maria, "Chinese Vegetarian Cookery"
