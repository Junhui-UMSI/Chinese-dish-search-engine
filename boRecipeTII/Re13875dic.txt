Wilted Spring Greens
Salt, Unsalted butter, White wine vinegar, Dandelion greens, olive oil, Granulated sugar, 
In a large heavy pot heat olive oil over moderately high heat. Add greens  and toss to coat. Add vinegar and sugar, toss to combine, cover, and simmer  for 10 to 15 minutes until tender. Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Transfer to serving dish and top with butter.
