Petes Meat Loaf
Carrots; grated, Salt; coarse black pepper,, SAUCE, Any bread; soaked in milk, Green bell pepper; chopped, Catsup, Brown sugar, Honey, Onion, Prepared mustard, Ground meat, 
Mix all ingredients for meat loaf and bake at 350 deg. for one hour. Mix  sauce ingredients and pour over meat loaf during last 20 minutes of baking.  Serve with additional sauce.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by MaeWestern <MaeWestern@AOL.COM> on Dec 5, 1997
