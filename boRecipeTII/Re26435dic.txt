Tomato Garden Casserole
Salt, Dry bread crumbs, Paprika, Fresh Tomato Wedges, Green Pepper, Butter or Margarine, Shredded Cheddar Cheese; 4oz, Onion; Diced, 
In a large bowl, combine all of the ingredients. Spoon into a greased 1  1/2-quart baking dish. Bake at 300 degrees for 45 minutes or until  vegetables are tender.    Recipe by: Taste of Home Garden Fresh Recipes Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1  #764 by Aquasea221@aol.com on Aug 29, 1997
