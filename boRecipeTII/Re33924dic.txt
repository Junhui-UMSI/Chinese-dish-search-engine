Sweet and Spicy Spareribs
Tomato sauce, Salt and pepper to taste, Rosemary, Juice from 2 oranges, Oregano, Dry mustard, Cloves garlic, smashed &, Honey, Spareribs, Chopped jalapeno pepper, 
Preheat barbecue grill on low. Place the first 7 ingredients in a small  saucepan and cook for 15 minutes over very low heat. Meanwhile, blanch ribs  in boiling water for 15 minutes. Drain ribs and baste with the warm  mixture. Place ribs on hot grill and partly cover. Cook 30 minutes,  depending on size. Turn ribs frequently, baste occasionally and season  well.
