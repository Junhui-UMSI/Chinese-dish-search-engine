Peta Spanish Tofu Omelette
Oregano, Nutritional yeast, Potato, Onion, Tomato, fresh, tofu, Green bell pepper, Tumeric, salt and pepper, Flour, unbleached, Olive oil, Garlic, 
In a frying pan over med heat, heat the oil or water and saute the garlic,  onion, potato, and green pepper until the potato is cooked, about 10 mins.    In the meantime, blend the reamining ingredients in a blender until smooth.  Add the tomato and tofu mixture to the pan and cook, covered, over a very  low heat until the omelette is set, about 10 mins.    Serve with salsa.
