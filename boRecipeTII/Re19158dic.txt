Vegetable Macedoine
(17-oz) green peas, Jar (4-oz) pimiento, (16-oz) Chinese vegetables, Sugar, (16-oz) seasoned green beans, Bunch green onions; chopped, Tarragon vinegar and white, (8-oz) water chestnuts;, Celery, salt and pepper,
