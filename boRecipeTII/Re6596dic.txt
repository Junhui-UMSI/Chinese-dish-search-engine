Poblano Soup with Milk
Salt and pepper, Poblano peppers, Nutmeg, milk, Chicken broth, Butter, Flour, all-purpose, 
Peel the poblanos, grind with the chicken broth, salt, pepper and nutmeg.  Cook the milk until be warm.  In a sauce pan, melt the butter and add the  flour, mix well, add warm milk and beat until blended; then add the  poblanos mixed with chicken broth and let cook 30 min. Patricia Wriedt.
