Bonnies Tatters
Butter, Bacon, Potato, Sour cream, Salt and pepper to taste, Onion, Cheese, 
Boil the potatoes in enough water to cover until they are done.      Drain and mash with the sour cream, butter, salt and pepper. Mix in green  onion and bacon.      Place in a greased oven-proof dish, top with the grated cheese, and bake  at 350F (180C) for 30 minutes.      Serves 4 to 6.
