Popcorn Marshmallow
In a saucepan melt: 1/2 cup, Popped popcorn, Salted peanuts, Cooking oil, Baking gumdrops, Gms. white marshmallows, 
Stir constantly while marshmallows melt and mix with melted margarine and  oil mixture. Do not allow this to boil, just melt over low heat. Mix into  popped corn mixture. Press into greased tube pan.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
