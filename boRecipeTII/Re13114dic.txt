Wolf Brand Chili Pie
Corn chips, Shredded Colby or, NORMA WRENN NPXR56B-----, Onion -- chopped, Can Wolf Brand Chili, Cheddar cheese, 
Layer 2 cups of corn chips, onion, 1/2 cup cheese and chili in a 1 quart  casserole.  Top with remaining corn chips. Bake in preheated oven (375  degrees) about 20 minutes. Top with remaining cheese and continue baking 5  minutes.  Garnish with sour cream and ripe olives (optional). Serves 3-4.
