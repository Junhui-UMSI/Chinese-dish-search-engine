Baked Breast of Chicken Excelsior
Chicken breast halves, Garlic salt, Pepper, Sour cream, Lemon juice, Sherry, Butter or Margarine, Paprika, mushroom pieces, 
Preheat oven to 375.    Sprinkle chicken with garlic salt to taste. Mix together melted margarine  or butter, paprika and lemon juice. Brush chicken well with butter mixture  and place in shallow baking pan. Bake, tented with foil, about 30 minutes  or until tender, brushing with remaining butter mixture occasionally to  keep from drying. In a bowl, blend together sour cream, sherry or broth,  mushrooms, and pepper. Pour over chicken for last 15 minutes.    Yield: 4 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest by LSHW <shusky@erols.com> on Mar 21, 1998
