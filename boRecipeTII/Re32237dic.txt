Barbecued Spareribs #6
Pork or lamb spareribs; cut, Chili powder, Barbecue sauce (recipe), Lemon juice (optional), 
Recipe from Wendy Lockman  1. Place spareribs in a large, shallow, heat-resistant, non- metallic  baking dish.     2. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 7 minutes.     3. Turn spareribs over and heat an additional 7 minutes     4. Drain off excess fat.     5.  Conmbine chili powder and barbecue sauce.     6. Pour half of sauce over ribs.     7. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4 minutes.     8. Turn spareribs again.  Sprinkle with lemon juice if desired     9. Pour on remaining sauce and heat, uncovered, for 8 minutes or until  fork-tender.  Pork should always be cooked until well-done.
