Turkey Pate
crackers, Onion soup mix, Pecans, Sour cream, Mayonnaise, Minced turkey, Paprika, Parsley, Pimento, 
Combine soup mix with sour cream and mayonnaise.  Stir in turkey, nuts and  pimento.  Refrigerate for several hours for flavors to meld and mixture to  thicken. Mound on a platter, sprinkle with parsley and paprika. Serve  chilled with crackers.  Makes about 2 cups.
