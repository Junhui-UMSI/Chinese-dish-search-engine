Pudding Cake
Flour, Cream cheese, SECOND LAYER, Butter, GLAZE, Sugar, confectioners, French Vanilla pudding,, Whipped cream, Chocolate Pudding, instant, Quick Oats, Nuts; chopped, Cold milk, 
Blend butter with flour, nuts and quick oats. Press in a 9x13" pan and bake  at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Remove from oven and C O O L !!    For second layer, mix cream cheese, whipped cream and confectioners sugar.  Spread over COOLED crust.    For third layer, mix both puddings cold milk. Spread over previous mixture  evenly (Usually this part is easy.)    Frost with whipped cream and grate a bit of dark chocolate across the top  for effect.   Chill for several hours.
