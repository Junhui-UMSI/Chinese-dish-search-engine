Arkansas Brown Rice
Salt, Butter or oil, Water, Brown rice, 
Wash rice by rinsing in a pan of cool water, pick out floating particles  and any unhulled grains. Combine ingredients in saucepan with tight-fitting  cover. Bring rice to a boil over high heat; stir once or twice; cover.  Reduce heat and simmer 45-50 minutes until rice is tender and water  absorbed.     NOTE: Brown rice is raw rice which has not been polished to the white  stage normally seen.    ARKANSAS RICE DEPOT, INC.    PO BOX 808, 825 W.
