Yeast Crisps
Warm water, Salt, Sugar, Yeast, Margarine, softened, Flour, 
Mix together yeast, warm water, margarine, salt and flour. Chill for 1  hour. Form 1 inch diameter balls and roll them in sugar. Place them in a  shallow pan and chill for 30 minutes. Roll out each ball as thin as paper,  then cut in half crosswise. Let rest 30 seconds and bake in a 350 F oven  until golden brown.    http://www.lino.com/~mimi/vegetablepatch/cookiear.htm [The Vegetable Patch]  MC formatted by Brenda Adams By waterman@vt.edu (Betsey) on Apr 11, 1997
