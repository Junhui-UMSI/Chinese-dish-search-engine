Turkey Avocado Roll-Ups - Lhj
Ripe avocado; peeled and, Shredded iceberg lettuce, Thousand Island dressing, Smoked sliced turkey, Flour tortillas; (10-inch), Shredded sharp Cheddar, 
1. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add one tortilla and heat,  turning frequently, until soft and pliable. Repeat with remaining  tortillas.    2. Divide turkey among tortillas. Toss lettuce, cheese, avocado and  Thousand Island dressing in bowl. Top each tortilla with 2/3 cup lettuce  mixture. Roll up. Makes 4 servings.
