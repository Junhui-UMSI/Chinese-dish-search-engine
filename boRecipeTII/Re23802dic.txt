Asiago Cheese Bread
Shredded Romano or Parmesan, Caraway seeds (optional), Salt, Lightly packed), Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Water, Sugar, Active dry yeast, Bread flour or unbleached, Finely shredded Asiago, Yellow cornmeal, Cup), 
Place all ingredients in pan of bread machine in order listed, or in  the order recommended for your machine. (I usually toss the grated cheese  with the cornmeal before adding it to the pan, to decrease clumping.) Set  controls for Basic bread with medium crust and start machine. Makes one  1.5-pound loaf.
