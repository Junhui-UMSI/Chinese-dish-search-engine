Baked Beans for the Crockpot
Salt pork, Molasses, Dried small white beans;, Onion, Water, Salt, Brown sugar, Dijon mustard, 
In crock pot, combine all ingredients. Cover and cook on low 13 to 14 hrs.  , stirring occasionally, if possible. 6 to 8 servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 411 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Dec 28, 1997
