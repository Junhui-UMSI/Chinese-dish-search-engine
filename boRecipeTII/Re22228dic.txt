Turkey Soup with Dumplings
Refrigerated biscuits, Turnip; pared and diced, Water, Instant chicken bouillon, Onions, Turkey carcass; broken up, Salt, 10 ounce can tomatoes; cutup, Celery, Snipped parsley, Bay leaf, Diced carrot, 
1. Put all ingredients except biscuits in cooker. 2. Cover and cook on low  7 to 9 hours. 3. Remove turkey carcass pieces; separate meat from bone,  return meat to soup. 4. Separate biscuits: place on top of soup. Cover:  cook on high 15 to 20 minutes. 5. To serve, place biscuit dumpling in bowl  and spoon soup over.    Yield: 6-8 servings    Note: This recipe fits both 4 and 6 quart cooker as is.
