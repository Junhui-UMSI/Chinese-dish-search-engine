Peanut Butter Cookies-Mix
Vanilla extract, Peanut butter, Egg, Bake it all mix, Sugar, 
Combine all ingredients and shape into balls 1 inch in diameter. Place  balls on cooky sheet.  Press with fork dipped in sugar. Bake in a 350 F.  oven 10 to 12 minutes. When cool, store in a coverd container.
