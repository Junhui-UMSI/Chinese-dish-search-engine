Sweet Pickles #1
Pickling spice, Vinegar, Lime, Sugar, Cucumbers; sliced, Water, 
Soak cucumbers in water and lime for 24 hours in a glass or plastic  container or crock. Do not use metal. Drain and wash. Pack in ice for 2  hours. Combine vinegar, sugar and spices in large vessel; add cucumbers and  simmer 2 hours. Pack and seal in sterile jars while hot. Process.    NOTE:  Get a book on canning if unsure about sealing and processing.
