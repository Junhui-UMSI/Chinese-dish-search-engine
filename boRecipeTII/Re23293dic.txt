Tuna Salad Vinaigrette
Green Salad, Tuna (6 1/2 oz) each, Vinaigrette dressing, 
Place Green Salad in a large bowl.  Add tuna, and mix well.  Pour dressing  over salad.  Toss lightly to distribute.    Prep. time: 30 minutes    Source: More Cajun Cooking  :       Modern Publishing    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #090 by Mary Lu <Sexylegs@2-hot.com> on Mar  30, 1997
