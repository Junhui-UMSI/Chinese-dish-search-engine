Strawberry Liqueur Jam
Green apple, Grand Marnier, Strawberries, Juice of 1 lime, Sugar, 
Wash & hull strawberries.  Peel, core & finely chop apple. Add lime juice &  let stand covered for 30 minutes. Microwave the fruit & juice for 4 minutes  on high. Add sugar, stir & microwave 35 minutes on high, stirring every 10  minutes.  Stand five minutes, pour into warm sterile jars. Seal.    Letts, "Jams, Pickles & Chutneys"
