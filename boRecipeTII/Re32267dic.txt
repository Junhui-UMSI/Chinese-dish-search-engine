Barbeque Chuck Steak
garlic, Worcestershire Sauce, Black pepper, Boneless chuck steak, Brown sugar, Catsup, prepared mustard, Wine vinegar, Paprika, Salt, 
Cut the beef on a diagonal, across the grain into slices 1 inch wide. Add  to the crockpot. In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients. Pour  over the meat, and mix. Cover and cook on Low for 3 to 5 hours.    Source: The Best of Electric Crockery Cooking    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 624 by molony <molony@scsn.net>  on Jan 28, 1998
