Pepes Garlic Soup
Egg, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Onion, ;Water, salt, boiling, Bread, French, stale; sliced, Parsley, Paprika, 
Preheat oven to 450 F.  Place bread in with boiling water. Fry onion,  garlic and parsley in oil.  Remove garlic and add paprika then turn heat  off.  Add to bread/water mixture. Place in oven uncovered for 15 minutes.  It will form a crust.  Place in bowls and break an egg into each while soup  is still hot.
