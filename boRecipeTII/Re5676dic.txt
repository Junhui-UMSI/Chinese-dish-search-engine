Pineapple Yogurt Freeze
Dole Pineapple Juice, (8 oz) vanilla yogurt, Sugar, Vanilla extract, 
Combine all ingredients.  Spoon into 4 small dessert bowls.  Freeze. To  serve. let stand at room temperature 10-20 minutes to soften; or microwave  for 20 seconds to soften.    For frozen pops:  Spoon into 6 fruit bar molds or 3-oz paper cups. Freeze  until slightly firm, insert wooden sticks.  Freeze until firm. Makes 6.
