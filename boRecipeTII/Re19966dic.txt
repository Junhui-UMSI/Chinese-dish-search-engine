Apple Butter Cheesecake
Graham cracker crust, Eggs, (8oz) cream cheese, 
Blend softened cream cheese and apple butter until smooth. Add eggs and  blend well. Pour mixture into crust and bake at 350 F for 40 minutes. Cool  completely. Spread top with a thin layer of apple butter. If desired serve  with whipped topping and chopped walnuts.
