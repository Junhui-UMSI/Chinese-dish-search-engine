Wild Rice Waldorf Salad
Orange juice, Fresh Lemon Juice, Hot water, Canola or other vegetable, Currants or raisins, Ground cardamom, Toasted almonds or walnuts;, DRESSING, Pure maple syrup or honey, Celery, Minced red onion, Wild rice, Brown rice; preferably long, Tart apple; Crispen, G., Bell pepper any color;, Ground coriander, 
In a saucepan with a tight fitting lid, soak the wild rice in 1 cup of the  hot water for 30 minutes. Add the remaining 1 1/2 cups of water, bring to a  boil, then lower to a simmer, cover and cook for about 45 minutes, until  the rice is tender.    When the rice has cooked for about 30 minutes, dice the apple and toss with  the lemon juice in a large bowl. Add the peppers, celery, red onions  currants or raisins, and almonds or walnuts and set aside. Whisk together  all of the dressing ingredients. Add the cooked rice and the dressing to  the bowl and toss well to combine.
