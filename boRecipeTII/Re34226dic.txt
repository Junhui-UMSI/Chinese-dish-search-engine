Bayou Short Ribs
Crushed tomatoes, Thyme, Diced green pepper, Tomato paste, Bay Leaves, Short ribs, Oregano, Vegetable oil, Pepper, Brown sugar, Minced garlic cloves, Salt, Cayenne, Diced onion, Diced celery stalks, Water, Basil, Flour, 
Heat oil in skillet. Coat ribs with flour & place in skillet. Add  onion, garlic, celery & pepper; saute until tender. Add tomatoes,  paste & water; stir. Add seasonings, bay leaves & sugar. Reduce heat  & simmer 3 hours, adding more water as needed.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
