Peanut Butter Chocolate Balls (With Graham Cracker Crumbs)
margarine, Graham cracker crumbs, paraffin wax, Smooth peanut butter, Powdered sugar, Chocolate chips, 
Mix first 4 ingredients. Melt paraffin wax and chocolate chips in double  boiler. Form peanut butter mixture into 1 inch balls and dip into melted  chocolate mixture, using a toothpick. Place on waxed paper to dry. Makes up  to 60 balls.
