Tomato-Onion Soup
Water, Thyme, Parsley sprigs, Garlic, Bay leaf, Leek, Tamari soy sauce, low Sodium, Onion, Tomato, Basil, 
Saute onions, leeks, and garlic in 1/2 cup water until transparent (about  15 minutes). Add the remaining 3 1/2 cups of water, the tomatoes, parsley,  thyme, basil, and the bay leaf. Simmer, covered, over low heat for 60  minutes. Remove cover; simmer for 30 minutes longer. Add tamari just before  serving.    HELPFUL HINTS: This soup is great with fresh, homemade bread. Add a green  salad fro an interesting meal. A large can of tomatoes (28 ounces) may be  substituted for fresh tomatoes.
