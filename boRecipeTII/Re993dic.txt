Italian Tuna and Spiral Pasta Salad
spiral macaroni, tuna, artichoke hearts, Italian dressing, Chopped fresh parsley, Capers, Green onions, celery, Frozen peas, olives, 
Formatted by Millie Ellis    Cook macaroni as directed on package; drain. Rinse with cold water; drain.  Mix macaroni and remaining ingredients. Serve on salad greens if desired.  Amount of dressing may be adjusted to your taste and this recipe doesnt  call for salt and pepper which I add.
