Wonderful Veggie Burgers
Firm tofu, Soy sauce, carrots, onion, Tahini, oatmeal, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Add a mix of whatever spices/herbs you prefer Shape into patties on baking  sheets.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes, turn them over, bake for 10 more  minutes. They are great hot or cold. We add flavorings to make the spicy.  Basic recipe is bland but very good. We make them all the time.    JAPLADY@NWU.EDU (REBECCA RADNOR)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
