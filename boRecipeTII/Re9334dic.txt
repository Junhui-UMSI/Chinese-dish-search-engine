Puffed Wheat Squares
Cocoa, Puffed wheat cereal, Butter, brown sugar, Vanilla, Corn syrup, 
In large saucepan, melt butter/marg. over med. heat. Add corn syrup, sugar  and cocoa. Bring to boil, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and blend  in vanilla. Add puffed wheat cereal (if your saucepan is large enough - or  pour syrup over top of cereal in large bowl) Combine well. Spoon mixture  into well-greased 9 x 13 pan and press down firmly with spatula. Chill  until firm. Cut into squares.    This chocolate "glue" also works well with rice krispies.
