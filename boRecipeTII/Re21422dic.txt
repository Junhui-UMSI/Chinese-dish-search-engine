Upside Down Apple Tart with Orange and Oatmeal Pastry
Orange, Superfine sugar, Butter, Apples, Honey, Tablespoorns lemon juice, All-purpose flour, Fresh orange juice (up to 2), Salt, Seeds of 4-5 cardamom Pods,, Finely grated zest of, Fine-cut orange marmalade, Fine oatmeal, 
To make the pastry, put the flour, oatmeal, sugar and salt into a food  processor and process just once to mix. Cut the butter into small pieces,  add to the flour mixture in the food processor and process again only  briefly, just until the mixture resembles rough breadcrumbs. Add the finely  grated orange zest and, with the motor running. pour in enough orange  juice, processing very briefly, for the dough to begin to stick together.  Pat the pastry into a ball, cover it with plastic wrap and leave it in the  refrigerator to chill while you prepare the apples.    For the bottom and sides of a 7-1/2 to 8" cake pan.
