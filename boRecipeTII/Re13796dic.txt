Wild Stir Fry Vegetables
Celery, Garlic, Mushrooms, Grated carrots, Vegetable oil, Beansprouts, Box Wild Quick Brown Rice, Onions, 
Cook contents of box according to package directions. Saute vegetables in  oil until tender.  Gently blend rice and vegetable mixture and serve.
