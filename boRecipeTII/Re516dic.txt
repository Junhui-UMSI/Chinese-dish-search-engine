Freezer Cucumber Salad
Sugar, Cucumbers, Onions, Cayenne pepper, Salt, Celery seed, White vinegar, 
Slice and peel cucumbers.  Mix ingredients altogether and store in a cool  place.  Stir twice daily for 4 to 5 days. Fill containers and store in the  freezer.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by BobbieB1@aol.com on Aug 27, 1998
