Pork Chops Sweet Potatoes and Apples
Nutmeg, Brown sugar, Pork chops, apples, Water, Cinnamon, salt and pepper, sweet potatoes, 
In electric frypan or large skillet, brown pork chops in Pam. Salt and  pepper to taste. Move chops to one side of pan. Add sweet potatoes and  apples. Sprinkle with brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg. Add water. Cover  and cook over Low heat until apples and potatoes are tender. Spoon syrup  over all as served.
