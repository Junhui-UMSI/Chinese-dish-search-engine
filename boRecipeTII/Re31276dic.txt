Bananas Foster #1
Lemon juice, Brown sugar, Butter, Cinnamon, White rum, banana, Banana liqueur, 
saute bananas in sugar and butter until tender. Sprinkle liqueurs and rum  over the bananas; set aflame, basting the bananas until the flame dies out.  Spoon over vanilla ice cream. Serves 2.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
