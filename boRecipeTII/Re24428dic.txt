Tropical Fruit Brunch Parfait
Nonfat cottage cheese, Raisins, Nonfat plain granola, Nonfat sugar free peach, Tropical Fruit Salad, 
DRAIN tropical fruit salad. COMBINE cottage cheese, yogurt and  raisins. LAYER in 4 glasses: cottage cheese mixture, fruit salad and  granola.    Prep time: 10 minutes.
