Suzys Onion Dill Rolls
Salt, Warm water, Granulated sugar,minus 2 tbl, Dried dill, Egg, Milk, very hot, Butter,room temperature, Dried, minced onion, Bread flour + if needed only, Milk or heavy cream, Dry yeast, not fast-acting, 
Place the butter, sugar, salt and dried minced onion in AMB. Add the hot  milk, stir with a wooden spoon until sugar and butter are dissolved. Cool  to lukewarm. Add dried dill.    In a small cup, stir together the warm water and yeast. Let the yeast  mixture rest 10 minutes. Add yeast, egg and flour to milk mixture. Set ABM  for dough setting and process.    Remove dough from ABM, punch down and shape into 24 2-inch balls. Place  balls in a lightly greased 13x9-inch baking pan. Cover and let rise until  almost doubled. Brush with milk or heavy cream.    Preheat oven to 400 F. Bake until rolls are lightly browned, 20 minutes or  until golden brown.    Rolls will bake as one unit and are broken apart when served.
