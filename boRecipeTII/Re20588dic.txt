Apple Cinnamon Muffins
Bisquick baking mix, milk, Apple; peeled & finely chop, Oil; vegetable, Cinnamon; ground, Sugar, Egg, 
Fat grams    per serving:              Approx. Cook Time: :15     Preheat oven to 400F. Grease bottom only of 12 medium muffin cups. Beat  egg slightly, stir in remaining ingredients just till moistened. Divide  batter evenly among cups. Bake till golden brown, 15 to 17 minutes.
