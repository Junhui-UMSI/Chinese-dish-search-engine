Jack O Lantern Cheese Ball
Cheddar cheese, Solid pack pumpkin, Pineapple preserves, Ground allspice, Ground nutmeg, Pretzel rod, cream cheese, Dark rye bread, Cayenne pepper, Black olives, Parsley, 
Beat cheses, pumpkin, preserves and spices in medium bowl until smooth.  Cover refrigerate 2-3 hours until cheese is firm enough to shape.     Shape mixture into round pumpkin, place on serving plate.Using a knife,  score vertical lines down pumpkin. Place pretzel rod in top for stem.     Cut bread into triangles for eyes, cut red pepper into triangle , for  nose, and cut olives in half to make the mouth. Surround with parsley     Formatted for MM by Jeannie Allen    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Oct 3, 1998
