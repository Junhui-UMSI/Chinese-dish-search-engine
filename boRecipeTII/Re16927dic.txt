Almodrote De Berengena (Turkish Eggplant Flan)
Sunflower oil; plus more for, Eggplant, Matzo meal, Feta cheese, Eggs, Gruyere cheese; grated, 
1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees. Prick the eggplants repeatedly with a  fork.  Place on a baking sheet and bake, turning to prevent burning, for 45  minutes.  Cool.  Peel or scoop out the flesh into a colander.  Drain and  press out the juices, then chop the flesh with a knife and mash it with a  fork.    2. Lower the oven temperature to 350 degrees. In a large bowl, mash the  feta cheese. Add the eggs, matzoh meal, 5 tablespoons of the Gruyere and 4  tablespoons of the oil.  Beat well.    3. Add the eggplant and mix. Pour the mixture into an oiled baking dish,  drizzle 1 tablespoon of the oil over the mixture, sprinkle with the  remaining cheese, and bake for 1 hour, until lightly colored.
