Pfeffernusse
Ground cardamom, Brown sugar, Baking soda, Salt, Ground cinnamon, Ground ginger, Eggs, Baking Powder, Ground white pepper, Finely chopped almonds;,, All-purpose flour, Butter or margarine, 
These nut-sized cookies are spiced with ginger, cinnamon, cardamom, and  pepper. They are called Pepperkaker, in Norway.    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. =A02 Combine dry ingredients and set aside.  =A03 In a separate bowl, beat butter and sugar together until light. Add  eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. =A04 Stir in dry  ingredients 1/2 cup at a time. Add almonds, if desired. =A05 Roll into one  inch balls and arrange 1 inch apart on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 11 to  14 minutes. =A06 Cool and store in airtight containers for 3 days to mellow  flavors. Makes 16 dozen    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #449 by Shelley Sparks  <ssparks@mailbox.arn.net> on Dec 11, 1997
