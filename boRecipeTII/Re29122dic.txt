Baked Rice
Recipe was for 1 serving, Celery;, And I AM not going to use, Hot water;, Cube bouillion;, Green onion; chopped, Rice;, Bread crumbs; dry, 8 bouillion cubes, 
Dissolve bouillion in hot water.  Add rice, green onion, and celery;  cover. Cook for 5 minutes.  Add bread crumbs.  Pour into small baking  dish. Bake at 350 F for 25 to 30 minutes or until top i s lightly  crusted.
