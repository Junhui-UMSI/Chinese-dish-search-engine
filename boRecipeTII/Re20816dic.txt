Apple Harvest Loaf
Hazelnut extract or almond, Water, Salt, Canola oil, Whole wheat flour, Sugar, FILLING, Apple butter, Bread flour, Dried cranberries, Yeast, 
Place ingredients into bread machine in the proper order for your machine.  Bake on white bread cycle with light crust. At beeper, add the dried  cranberries.    Recipe by: Ruth G.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #314 by Ruth & Shel <fritzl@earthlink.net>  on Dec 01, 1997
