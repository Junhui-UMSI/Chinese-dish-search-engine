Peanut Butter-Bran Muffins
Baking Powder, Sugar, Peanut butter, Whole wheat flour, Lowfat Milk, Bran, Egg, Peanuts, chopped, 
Combine the flour, bran, baking powder and sugar in a bowl. Add the  remaining ingredients and blend.     Spoon into oiled muffin tins or paper muffin cups.     Bake in a 375-degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes.     Makes 8     One Muffin = Calories: 140 Carbohydrates: 12 Protein: 6 Fat: 9  Sodium: 104 Potassium: 184 Cholesterol: 35     Exchange Value: 1 Bread Exchange + 2 Fat Exchanges     Source: Holiday Cookbook, American Diabetes Association, ISBN  0-13-024894-0, by Betty Wedman, M.S.,R.D.
