Tipsy Toast
Eggs, Grand Marnier, milk, To 3, Loaves French bread, Orange juice concentrate, Butter, Jigger, 
Combine all ingredients in large bowl, except bread.  Slice French bread,  soak bread in mix for at least 15 minutes and fry in butter untiul crisp  and golden.
