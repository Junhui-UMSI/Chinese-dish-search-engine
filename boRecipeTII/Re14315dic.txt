Whitefish Fillets
White wine, Shallots, Lemon juice, White fish fillets, Cheese, salt and pepper, Bread crumbs, 
Moisten fish with lemon juice (sprinkle on). Toss. Place in a shallow dish  (buttered). Add salt & peppper, cover with shallots, crumbs, cheese. Dot  with butter. Pour on wine to about 1/3 way up fish. Bake uncovered at 450  for 25 min. until opaque
