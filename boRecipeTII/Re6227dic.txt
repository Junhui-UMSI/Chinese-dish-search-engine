Pizza with Olives and Caramelized Onions
Dried whole basil, olive oil, Anchovy paste, Coarsely chopped ripe olives, Coarsely chopped sweet onion, Vegetable cooking spray, Garlic, Dried whole thyme, Extra-dry white vermouth or, Italian cheese-flavored, Black Pepper, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish coated with  cooking spray; stir well.    Cover and bake at 375F for 50 minutes, stirring after 25 minutes. Add  vermouth and anchovy paste, stirring well. Cover and bake an additional 20  minutes or until onion mixture is lightly browned, stirring after 15  minutes.    Spread onion mixture over pizza crust, leaving a 1/2-inch border; sprinkle  with olives. Place pizza on a baking sheet.    Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes or until thoroughly heated.    Yield: 8 servings (serving size: 1 wedge).    NOTES : Cal  141.7, Fat 3.5g, Carb 22.6g, Fib 2.1g, Pro 4.7g, Sod 223mg,  CFF  22.1%.
