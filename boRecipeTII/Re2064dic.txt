Amish Batter
Sugar, Flour, milk, 
Do Not Refrigerate. DO Not use metal spoon.    Day 1 - Combine 1 cup sugar, 1 cup flour, 1 cup milk in a resealable bowl  :       or bag; stir, use wooden or plastic spoon. Day 2 - Stir Day 3 -  Stir Day 4 - Stir Day 5 - Add 1 cup milk, 1 cup sugar, 1 cup flour; stir.  Day 6 - Stir Day 7 - Stir Day 8 - Stir Day 9 - Stir Day 10- Add 1 cup milk,  1 cup sugar, 1 cup flour; Stir; then split among  :       4 containers, these are now starters. Give 3 away & keep 1. You  :       can make Friendship Bread with what is left in your container.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Aug  24, 1998
