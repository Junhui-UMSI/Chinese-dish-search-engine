Pear and Pecan Cake
Baking soda, Nutmeg, Brown sugar; firmly packed, Vegetable oil, Water, Sugar, Flour, Salt, Pecans, Cinnamon, Eggs, Vanilla, Firm peas; cored and chopped, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Butter and flour a 9" x 13" baking pan. Sift together  flour, cinnamon, baking soda, nutmeg and salt. Beat together brown sugar,  oil, eggs, sugar, water and vanilla until smooth. Blend in dry ingredients.  Stir in pears and pecans. Pour batter into pan. Bake for about 55 minutes,  cool before serving.    Recipe by: Faygie@aol.com    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #840 by Roberta Banghart  <bobbi744@sojourn.com> on Oct 12, 1997
