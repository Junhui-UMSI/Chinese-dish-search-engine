Almond Cream Caramel
Large eggs, Vanilla extract, Almond extract, milk, Sugar, Coarsely chopped almonds, Chopped almonds (optional), 14 oz can sweetened, Condensed milk, 12 oz can evaporated skim, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees    2.  Pour sugar into a 9-inch round cake pan.  Place cake pan over medium  heat.  Cook 6 minutes or unti sugar is dissolved and golden, shaking cake  pan occasionally with tongs. Immediately remove from heat; set aside.    3.  Place eggs in a medium bowl; stir with a whisk.  Stir in 1/4 cup  almonds.  Pour mixture into prepared cake pan; cover with foil; place in a  large shallow roasting pan.  Add water to roasting to a depth of 1 inch.  Place roasting pan in oven and bake for 55 minutes or until a knife  inserted in center comes out clean.    4. Remove cake pan from water; place on a wire rack.  Remove foil.  Let  cool in cake pan 30 minutes. Loosen edges with a knife or rubber spatula.  Place a serving place upside down on top of cake pan; invert custard onto  plate, allowing syrup to drizzle over custard. Sprinkle with chopped  almonds if desired.
