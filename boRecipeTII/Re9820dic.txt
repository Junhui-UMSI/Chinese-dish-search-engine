Pumpkin Tomato Bisque
Mild vegetable oil, Vegetable or chicken stock,, Salt and fresh ground black, Onion, Pam or similar nonstick, Minced parsley for garnish, Maple syrup, Butter, Whole tomatoes with their, Fresh or canned pumpkin or, 
In large soup pot, heat oil and butter together over medium heat. Add  onion, and saute until softened, about five minutes. Add stock. Heat  through. Add pumpkin, whisking in. Heat again. Add tomatoes, maple syrup,  and heat through. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve hot, with a  parsley garnish.
