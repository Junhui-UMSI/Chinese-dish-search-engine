"Gourmet" Pizza Dough
Warm water, Salt, olive oil, Flour, Warm water, Sugar, Yeast, 
Soften yeast in 1/3 c warm water for 5 minutes.  Add 2 c flour and the rest  of the ingredients.  Knead til smooth, adding additional flour as needed.  Place in greased bowl and cover and let sit til doubled. Punch down and  roll out to fit pizza pan.    Use this dough to make Pizza Nissa Socca.  Also need recipe for Tomato  Pizza Sauce.    Source:  Gourmet Magazine.    Note:  I usually increase the salt and sugar slightly.
