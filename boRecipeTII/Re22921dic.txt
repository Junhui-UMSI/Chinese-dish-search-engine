Arepas Rellenas Con Guiso De Carne
Black pepper, Ground beef, Ground cumin, Thyme leaves, Onion, Green chile, Pimento-stuffed olives, Vegetable oil, Ground pork, Tomatoes, Capers, Salt, garlic, arepa dough, Achiote powder, 
Stuffed Arepas From Venezuela    Heat oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat, add meat, onions, garlic,  green chile, tomato, achiote, cumin, thyme, black pepper and salt, and  cook, stirring occasionally, for 20 minutes, or until tomatoes have formed  a thick sauce. Stir in olives and capers.    Prepare a recipe of basic arepas, shape into 3-inch discs (3/4-inch thick),  cook. When done, split in half, fill with meat sauce and serve hot.
