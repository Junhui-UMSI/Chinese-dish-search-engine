Penn Dutch Liver Cakes
Flour, salt and pepper, Onion, finely chopped, Bacon, sliced, Egg, well beaten, Liver, ground, 
To the ground liver add the egg, flour, onion, and salt & pepper. Mix well.  Shape into round cakes and around each cake wrap a slice of bacon,  fastening each with a toothpick. Put on hot skillet and fry until well  done.
