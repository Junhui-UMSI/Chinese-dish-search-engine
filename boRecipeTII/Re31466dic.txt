Barbecue Sauce #08
Salt, Worcestershire Sauce, Liquid smoke, Prepared mustard, Catsup, Vinegar, Brown sugar, 
Heat all ingredients together slowly in a heavy saucepan until it bubbles.  Store in a jar and refrigerate. Good on hot dogs, hamburgers. Excellent to  use with cooked hamburger meat as barbeque on buns. Use on roast, whole or  chopped. Good on deer meat. Brown chicken, brush with sauce; bake in oven.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
