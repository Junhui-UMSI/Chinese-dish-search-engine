Banana Cake #1
Butter or margarine, Eggs, Baking soda, Vanilla, Salt, Pecans, Sugar, Whipped cream, ripe bananas, Flour, 
Cream shortening & add sugar. Add eggs, beating well after each. Stir in  bananas. Add dry ingredients & then nuts. Pour into greased & floured  8x8x2-inch pan. Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes. Cut into squares & serve  warm or cold. Put whipped topping on top when served. Yields 9-12 servings.
