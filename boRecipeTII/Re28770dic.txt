Thai Banana Salsa
Japanese chili spice, Fresh Cilantro, Large firm-ripe banana, Oriental sesame oil, Chopped golden raisins, Lemon Peel, 
1. Peel and halve lengthwise banana. In a nonstick 10-12" frying pan over  high heat, brown banana well in sesame oil, about 8 minutes. 2. Chop  banana. Mix with raisins, cilantro, lemon peel and Japanese chili spice.
