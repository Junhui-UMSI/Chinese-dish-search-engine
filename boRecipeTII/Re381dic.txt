Lemon Chicken
Boneless chicken breasts, Flour, Tarragon, Lemon, Butter, Salt, Chicken broth, Chopped parsley, 
Cut chicken into 10 or 12 strips. Brown chicken in butter. Sprinkle with  flour, salt and tarragon. Cook, stirring constantly,for 5 minutes. Add  broth and lemon slices. Lower heat and cook 3 minutes longer. Sprinkle with  parsley and serve over cooked rice. Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #602 by  chinkle@mail.bright.net on May 8, 1997
