Pineapple-Pear Salad
(large) crushed pineapple, Unflavored gelatin, Hot water, Lemon juice, (large) pears; chopped, Salt, American cheese, Sugar, TOPPING, Stick butter; melted, Marshmallows, (small) whipping cream, Sugar, Egg, Sugar, Lemon juice, 
Mix gelatin & hot water & let stand. Drain juice off fruit. Mix lemon juice  with it & add salt & sugar. Bring to a boil, add gelatin & cool. Add fruit  & pour into a 10-inch square pan. Let set well and add topping. Topping:  Mix egg, sugar, marshmallows, butter & lemon juice together & cook over low  heat until thick. Cool. Whip cream & add 1 cup sugar. When first mixture  has cooled, add it to the whipped cream & spread over firm gelatin.  Sprinkle with grated American cheese.
