Avocado and Pineapple Milkshake
Semi-skimmed milk, Pineapple cubes, Very Ripe avocado pear, Salt, Ice Cubes, 
Scoop the avocado out of the skin. Discard the stone and put the flesh into  a liquidiser or mash it. Add to the milk, ice cubes, ice cubes, crushed  pineapple chunks and salt, and process until thoroughly blended in and the  whole mixture is nice and frothy. Pour into glasses and serve with a  swizzle stick of celery.    Source: Michael Barry, Yes! Magazone
