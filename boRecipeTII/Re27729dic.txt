The Mother Superiors Cabbage Salad
salt, Onion, Red Apples, pepper, Sugar, Vinegar, Cabbage, salt, Sour cream, 
La salade de choux de la Mere Superieure    From Mme Benoit, "The Mother Superior at the village convent would prepare  this recipe from special guests. Many people still make this cabbage salad  with sour cream."    Mix together in a large bowl, grated cabbage, minced onion, salt and grated  apples. Mix together sugar, vinegar, salt, pepper and thick sour cream.  Refrigerate the salad and dressing for 20 minutes before serving. Then pour  the dressing over the cabbage, mix well, and salt for salt and vinegar.
