Baking Powder Drop Biscuits
Bake-it-all mix*, milk, 
*Do not pack       Make a well in the Mix.  Add milk and stir with a fork until all of  the Mix is moistened.  Drop dough by spoonfuls onto a cooky sheet. Bake in  a 450 F. oven 10 to 12 minutes. Yield: 9 to 10 biscuits NOTE: for 6  biscuits, use 1 cup Mix with about 1/3 cup milk.
