Quick Easy Chili
Ground beef, Onion, Chili powder, tomato paste, Beef bouillon, tomato sauce, dark red kidney, Hot red pepper sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Green bell pepper, Water, olive oil, 
In large skillet, brown beef with onions and green pepper; drain. Stir in  water, bouillon cube, tomato sauce, tomato paste. Add kidney beans and  season. Bring to boil. Reduce heat, simmer 15 to 20 minutes, stir  occasionally.    busted by sooz  Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Mar 07, 1998
