Bake Store Sugar Cookies
Flour, Egg, milk, Salt, Shortening, Vanilla, Sugar, Baking Powder, 
Measure ingrediants into a bowl & mix well. Shape into a ball & wrap  in plastic wrap. Refrigerate 3 hours.    Pre-heat oven to 400 F. Roll out 1/2 of the dough (leave the rest tin  the fridge for now) to 1/8" thick & cut out shapes. Place 1" apart.  Bake 6-8 minutes or until golen brown. Remove & cool completely.  Store in tightly covered containers.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
