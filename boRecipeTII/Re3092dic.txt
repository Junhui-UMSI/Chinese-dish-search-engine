Peanut Pitas
Small pita bread (8 pack), Peanut butter, Banana, thinly sliced into, 48 slices, Strawberry spreadable fruit, 
Spread inside of each pita half with one teaspoon each peanut butter and  spreadable fruit.    Fill pita halves evenly with banana slices, serve immediately.    Other variations:    Substitute Honey for spreadable fruit.    Substitute any flavor jelly for spreadable fruit and thin apple slices for  banana slices.    Substitute mayo for spreadable fruit and celery slices for banana slices.    == Courtesy of Dale & Gail Shipp, Columbia Md.
