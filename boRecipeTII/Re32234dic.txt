Barbecued Spareribs #3
Five spice powder, Salt, Hoisin sauce, Tomato catsup, Pork spareribs, Sugar, BARBECUE SAUCE, Vinegar, Honey, Sugar, Soy sauce, Salt, 
Place the ribs on a counter and remove the covering skin. Sprinkle with the  sugar and salt and rub in well; let rest 30 minutes. Brush the Barbecue  Sauce over the ribs and set aside another 30 minutes. Cook in a covered  charcoal grill or a 375 degree oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour. Cut the ribs  apart and serve. BARBECUE SAUCE: Combine all ingredients. Use as a basting  sauce for spareribs.
