Strawberry Festival Tea [Na]
Fresh strawberries, hulled,, Gallon tea, Sugar, 6-oz cans frozen lemonade, 
Sprinkle strawberries with sugar in bowl. Mix tea and lemonade concentrate  in large pitcher; add sweetened strawberries; stir. Serve over ice. This  recipe is a favorite of Antoinette Kuzmanich Hatfield, wife of Senator MArk  Hatfield.
