Petti Di Pollo Alla Bolognese
Grated parmesan, Butter, Chicken broth, 1/2 chicken breasts*, olive oil, Cooked ham, Parsley, 
*Can also be made with veal    Heat the oil and butter in a frying pan. Brown the chicken on both sides.  Put a slice of ham on each 1/2 breast. Springle with parmesan, parsley.  Pour the chicken broth in the pan. Cover let simmer for five minutes.  Submitted By MARTIN DELISLE <DELISLE@CAM.ORG> On THU, 16 NOV 1995 120742  ~0500
