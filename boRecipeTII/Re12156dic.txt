Zucchini and Squash Casserole
Zucchini, Squash, Onion, Cheddar, 
Prep time: 10 minutes Bake time: 30 minutes Total time: 40 minutes Oven temp: 350F / 180C / Gas mark 4  1. Preheat oven.  Lightly grease casserole dish.  2. Layer 1/2 of each of the above listed ingredients in order listed, then repeat.   3. Bake for approximately 30 minutes in oven (or for 15-20 minutes in microwave on high)  until vegetables are tender.   4. Once cooked, if desired, broil for  a couple of minutes to lightly brown top.
