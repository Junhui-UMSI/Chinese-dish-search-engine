Zucchini Nut Bread
Unbleached all-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Vanilla extract, Baking soda, Zucchini; Shredded, Salt, Vegetable oil, Ground cinnamon, Sugar, Eggs, Pecans, 
Sift the flour, cinnamon, baking soda, salt and baking powder together into  a bowl, blending well, and set aside. In a mixer bowl, beat the eggs, using  an electric mixer set on medium speed, until well blended. Gradually add  the sugar and oil, mixing well after each addition. Blend in the vanilla  extract. Add the dry ingredients all at once, using an electric mixer set  on low speed, and blend in well. Stir in the zucchini and pecans. Pour the  batter into a greased 9 X 5 X 3-inch loaf pan. Bake in a preheated 350  Degree F. oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until a cake tester or wooden  pick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool, in the pan, for 10  minutes then remove from the pan and cool, until cold to the touch, on a  wire rack. Sprinkle the loaf with the confectionerss sugar before slicing.
