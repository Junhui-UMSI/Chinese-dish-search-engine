Pork Chop and Corn Stuffing Bake
Corn bread stuffing, Spicy brown mustard, Boneless pork chops, Brown sugar, celery & onion, Paprika, 
1.  Combine soup, celery, onion, paprika and stuffing. In 9" greased      pie plate, spoon stuffing mixture. Arrange chops over mixture;      press lightly into the stuffing mixture.    2.  Combine sugar and mustard; spread evenly over chops.    3.  Bake at 400F for 30 minutes, or until chops are fork-tender.
