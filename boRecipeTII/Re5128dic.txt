Pierniczki (Honey-Spice Cakes)
Eggs, Water, Sugar, Baking soda, Honey-spice cake seasoning, Honey, Flour, 
from Polish Calendar    Cream 2 eggs & 1 C. sugar until white. Add 1 Tablespoon Honey-spice cake  seasoning, 1 teaspoon baking soda dissolved in 3 tablespoons water, 1 Cup  honey, & 3 Cups sifted flour. Work ingredients well into a dough, then  knead on floured board. Sprinkle with flour & roll out 1/4 " thick. Cut  into shapes. Place on greased bakinsheet and Bake in 400 degree oven about  10 minutes Store air tight and glaze before serving. ** No Glaze recipe  listed    Posted to EAT-L Digest 31 Mar 97 by Jennylyn27@AOL.COM on Mar 31, 1997
