Zesty Micro-Baked Beans
Baked beans, undrained (1lb), Prepared mustard, Thick & chunky style salsa, Onion, chopped fine, Kidney beans, drained, 
In a 1 1/2 qt. microwave safe casserole, combine all ingreds; mix well.  Cover with waxed paper.    Microwave on high for 7 to 9 1/2 min. or until onion is tender and flavors  are blended, stirring twice during cooking.
