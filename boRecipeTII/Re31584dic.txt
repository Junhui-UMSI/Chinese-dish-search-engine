Swirl Top Casserole
Hard boiled eggs, milk, Chopped olives; (adjust, Bisquick prepared according, Flour, Prepared mustard, TOPPING, Grated cheese, Butter or margarine, Chicken; cooked and cut into, Onion, Celery salt, 
Melt butter or margarine in saucepan. Mix in flour until smooth; gradually  add milk and stir until mixture boils and thickens. Remove from heat and  gently fold in remaining ingredients. Pour into 2-quart casserole dish.  Prepare biscuits; roll out to 1/4- inch thick. Sprinkle with cheese and  roll up like jelly roll. Cut into 1/2-inch slices and place on top of  casserole.    Bake at 400 degrees until brown. Serves 6 with seconds or 8 single  servings.
