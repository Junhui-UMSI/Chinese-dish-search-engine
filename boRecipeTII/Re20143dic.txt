Vanilla Refrigerator Wafers
Vanilla, Baking soda, Cream of tartar, Butter, All-purpose flour, Egg, Margarine, C and H Powdered Sugar, Salt, 
Cream together butter, margarine and powdered sugar.  Beat in egg and  vanilla. Combine flour, soda, cream of tartar and salt and add to creamed  mixture.  Mix well.  Divide dough in half.  Form each half into a 2-inch  round roll.  Wrap in waxed paper or aluminum foil.  Chill in refrigerator  or freezer.  Slice 1/4-inch thick.  Bake on ungreased cookie sheet in  350-degree oven 8 to 10 min.  Cool on rack.  Makes about 5 doz. cookies.
