Velveeta Hamburg Dip
Hamburg, Onion, Soul seasoning, Velveeta cheese, cut up, Chopped, 0r 2 Jalepeno with seeds, 
Cook meat, onions and pepper. Drain off fat. Melt cheese in crockpot and  add meat mixture, add soul seasonings to taste.  Serve warm with nacho  chips or chilled like a pate with crackers.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #019 by valerie@nbnet.nb.ca (valerie) on  Fri,       17    Jan 1997.
