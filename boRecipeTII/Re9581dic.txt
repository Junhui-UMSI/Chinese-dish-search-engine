Pumpkin Custard Bars
Vanilla, 8-ounce carton dairy sour cr, Beaten egg, 7-ounces apple-cinnamon, Finely chopped pecans, Brown sugar, Margarine or butter, melted, Canned pumpkin, Rolled oats, 
Combine 3/4 cup of muffin mix, oats, pecans, brown sugar, and margarine.  Toss to mix well. Press about 1-1/2 cups of crumb mixture evenly on bottom  of 9x9x2-inch pan. Bake in 350 degree oven 10 minutes. Meanwhile, combine  remaining muffin mix and remaining ingredients. Pour over baked crust.  Sprinkle with remaining crumb mixture. Bake in 350 degree oven 30 to 35  minutes or till set. Cool in pan on rack. Cut. Makes 24.
