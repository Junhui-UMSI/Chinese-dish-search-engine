Quick and Easy Chili
Red kidney beans; rinsed and, Ground beef, Ground cumin, Onion, Black beans; canned, Whole tomatoes with thick, Water, Bell pepper; diced, Chili powder, Salt, 
1. Cook beef, onions and green pepper in a large wide-bottom pot over  medium heat, breaking up chunks of meat with a wooden spoon, until meat is  no longer pink, about 7 minutes. Drain off any fat.    2. Add chili powder, cumin, and salt and cook 2 minutes, stirring  constantly, until spices are fragrant.    3. Add remaining ingredients, increase heat and bring to a boil. Reduce  heat and simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally and breaking up tomatoes  with a wooden spoon, until chili thickens.    4. Cool, then remove 2 cups for Huevos Rancheros recipe.
