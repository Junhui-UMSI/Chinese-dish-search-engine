"M&ms"? Chocolate Marbled Blondies
Egg, Unsweetened cocoa powder, Vanilla extract, Firmly packed light brown, Granulated sugar, M&Ms? milk chocolate mini, Cream cheese, All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Egg yolk, (1 stick) butter, 
Preheat oven to 350? F.    Lightly grease a 9 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan; set aside.    In large mixing bowl cream butter and sugar. Add egg until well blended;  stir in vanilla. Combine flour and baking soda; stir into creamed mixture.  Stir in 2/3 cup "M&Ms"(r) Chocolate Mini Baking Bits; set aside. Dough  will be stiff.    In separate bowl beat together cream cheese, granulated sugar and egg yolk  until smooth; stir in cocoa powder until well blended. Place  chocolate-cheese mixture in six equal portions evenly in bottom of prepared  baking pan.    Place reserved batter around cheese mixture and swirl slightly with tines  of fork. Pat down evenly on top. Sprinkle with remaining 1/3 "M&Ms"(r)  Chocolate Mini Baking Bits. Bake 25 to 30 minutes until toothpick inserted  in center comes out with moist crumbs. Cool completely before cutting into  bars. Store in refrigerator in tightly covered container.    Notes: Savor two great tastes...delicious chocolate cheesecake swirled into  moist, chewy blondies!
