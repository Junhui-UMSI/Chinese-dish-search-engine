Texas Style Jambalaya with Ham
Cayenne pepper, RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and, Thyme, olive oil, Diced green pepper, Celery, Ham, finely diced, Diced onion, Garlic, Bay leaf, Chicken broth, Uncooked converted rice, Kielbasa, sliced 1/4" thick, Ranch Style Brand Pinto, 
Preheat oven to 375f. In a large skillet heat oil over medium heat. Saute  onion, pepper and celery until onion is translucent; stirring occasionally  about 5-7 minutes. Add garlic, rice, kielbasa and ham. Stirring frequently,  cook 2-3 minutes to coat rice with oil. Add remaining ingredients; heat to  a boil, pour into a 9x12" baking pan. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.  Bake 18-20 minutes, or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed. Let sir  for 10 minutes before serving.    To prepare ahead: After baking, let cool and refrigerate overnight. Cover  and warm in a preheated 35F. oven for 30 minutes or until heated through.
