Woodcutters Omelet
Butter, Lemon juice, KAREN GODFREY, Parsley, fresh, chopped, Mushrooms, sliced, Bacon strips, diced, Potato, cooked and diced, Onion, Eggs, 
Melt butter in an omelet pan or small skillet. Add mushrooms, onion, and  bacon, and saute until brown. Add lemon juice to deglaze pan. Remove and  reserve mixture.    Beat eggs and add potato. Heat pan and pour in egg mixture. Cook just until  edges are firm and the center is soft.    Place mushroom mixture on top of omelet and sprinkle with parsley. Fold in  half and serve immediately.
