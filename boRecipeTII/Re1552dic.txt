Bunny Salad
Chilled pear half, Raisins, Red cinnamon candy, Blanched almonds, 
Place crisp lettuce leaf on plate.  On top of it, place pear upside down.  Make bunny, using narrow end for face.  Two raisins for the eyes, 1 red  cinnamon candy for the nose and 2 blanched almonds for the ears. The  cottage cheese ball makes the tail.
