Raspberry Vinaigrette 8
Honey mustard, Raspberry vinegar, olive oil, Minced fresh tarragon, Sea salt to taste, Walnut oil, 
In a bowl. whisk together mustard vinegar, and salt to taste, add the oils  in a stream, whisking until it is emulsified. Stir in tarragon.
