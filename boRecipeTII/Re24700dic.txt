Atole
Honey, Salt, Ground cinnamon, Atole blue cornmeal, Water, milk, 
Mix together cornmeal, cinnamon, salt and water in 3-quart saucepan. Heat  to boiling, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to low. Stir in milk and  honey. Heat to simmering, stirring constantly (do not boil). NOTE: Regular  blue cornmeal can be substituted for the atole blue cornmeal. Spread  cornmeal in ungreased shallow baking pan. Bake, at 400F, stirring  occasionally, until light brown, about 10 minutes.
