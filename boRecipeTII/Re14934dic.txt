24-Hour Slaw
Celery seed, Vinegar, Head cabbage; shredded, Sugar, Onion, Vegetable oil, 
Combine cabbage and onion. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over cabbage.  Chill for 24 hours.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
