Baby Lettuce Salad with Tart Raspberry Vinaigrette
Rich lamb stock, Black pepper; to taste, Oregano; chopped, Baby lettuce leaves, Raspberry vinegar, olive oil, Chives; chopped, 
Recipe by: Elmar Prambs of the Riverside Cafe, Austin, TX Preparation Time:  0:10 Stir together oil, vinegar, and lamb stock in a small bowl. Add herbs  as close to serving time as possible. Toss with baby lettuce and serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdjaxxx.zip
