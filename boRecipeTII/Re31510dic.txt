Sweet-And-Sour Walnuts
Red bell pepper --, Peanut oil (about 1 quart), Eggs, Rice vinegar, Arrowroot powder, Broccoli florets, Sauce, Light honey, Pineapple chunks, Cornstarch, Dark sesame oil, Low-sodium soy or tamari, Water -- plus 1-1/2 tsp., Unbleached white flour, Whole-wheat pastry or, Julienned, Garlic, For deep-frying, Walnut halves, 
1. In a wok or deep skillet over medium heat, begin heating peanut oil to  deep-fry temperature of about 350 degrees F (8 to 10 minutes).    2. In a small bowl mix arrowroot, flour, eggs, and the 1/2 cup water to  form a smooth batter. Dip walnuts in batter. When oil is hot, fry walnuts  until lightly browned (30 seconds). Line a baking sheet with paper towels  and drain cooked walnuts on towels.    3. Pour hot oil into a container and wipe wok clean. Return 1 teaspoon of  oil to wok and place over medium-high heat. Stir-fry pineapple, red pepper,  and broccoli until vegetables are bright in color.    4. In a small bowl mix rice vinegar, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic,  the 1-1/2 tablespoons water, and cornstarch. Add to vegetables and cook,  stirring frequently, until mixture thickens. Add walnuts. Toss to reheat  and serve immediately.
