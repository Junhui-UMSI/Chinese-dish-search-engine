Plum Bbq Sauce
1/2 oz, Frozen lemonade, 6 oz, Purple plums, drained,, Butter, Onion -- choppewd, Chili sauce, Dijon mustard, Pitted, -- fine chop, 
Cook  onions in butter in 2 quart pan stirring occasionally, until tender,  about 2 min.  Stir in remaining ingredients. Heat to boiling, reduce to low  heat. Simmer uncovered 15 min. stirring occasionally. Makes 2 cups.    Recipe By     : 1/4    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
