Apple Meatloaf
Lean ground beef, Apple, cored and chopped, Med Onion, chopped, Pepper, Garlic salt, 
1 or 2 slices of Bread Preheat oven to 350.   Combine all ingredients except bread. Soak bread in cold water, squeeze  out moisture, shred, and add. Place in loaf pan and bake 25-30 minutes.  Or...form into patties, roll in bread crumbs and pan fry, or broil.
