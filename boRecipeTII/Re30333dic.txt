Banana Cinnamon Toast
Brown sugar; (or equivalent, Whole wheat bread; toasted, Light tub margarine; melted, Cinnamon, Banana, 
Source: Whats For Breakfast    Slice banana half into 4 slices lengthwise and place on toast. Drizzle  melted margarine over banana. Mix together sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle  over banana. Broil until brown and bubbly.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 673 by vergie1@juno.com (Vergie  A Ewing) on Feb 1, 1998
