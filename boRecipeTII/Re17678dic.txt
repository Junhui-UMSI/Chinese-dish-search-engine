Almond Poppy Seed Muffins
Evaporated milk, Salt, Flour, Almond extract, Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Sugar, milk, Poppy seeds, Eggs, 
Preheat the oven to 325?. Line 30 muffin cups with paper liners. In a large  mixing bowl, beat together the eggs, sugar, evaporated milk, milk and  vegetable oil.  Sift together baking powder, salt and flour. GRadually add  the flour mixture to the egg mixture, beating until well combined. Add the  extracts and poppy seeds, stirring only until well combined. Using a 1/3  cup measure, pour the batter into the muffin cups. Bake for 25-30 minutes  or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
