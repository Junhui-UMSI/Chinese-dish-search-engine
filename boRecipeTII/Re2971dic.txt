Peanut Butter Refrigerator Cookies
Peanut butter, Vanilla, Shortening, Eggs, Granulated sugar, Salt, Baking Powder, Flour, 
Cream shortening, peanut butter, and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla. Add other  ingredients. Shape into rolls. Slice into 1/4 inch slices when chilled.  Bake at 375 degrees F for 10-12 minutes.    Makes 6-7 dozen  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Ken Vaughan <kvaughan@ptialaska.net> on Feb  15, 1998
