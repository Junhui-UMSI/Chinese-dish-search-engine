Beer Soup
Butter, Sugar, Egg yolks, Bottle of beer, 
Mix beer with yolks and sugar in a jug.  Pour into saucepan and beat with  rotary whisk, until it boils. Remove from heat at boiling point, add butter  and serve with a garnish of parsley and paprika.    Taken from:  Early Settlers Household Lore - Sovereign Hill Ballarat.
