Tortillia Chips
Salt, Vegetable oil for deep, Corn tortillas, Frying, 
1.  Heat oil to 380 F.    2.  Cut each tortilla into 6 wedges.  Fry in oil for about 1-1/2 min. or  until crisp. Undercooked tortillas will be soft; overcooked tortillas will  absorb grease and taste burned.    3.  Drain on paper toweling.  Salt to taste.
