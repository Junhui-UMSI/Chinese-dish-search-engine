Pistachio-Stuffed Mushrooms
pistachios, Onions, Chopped parsley, Dried marjoram, Fine dried breadcrumbs, Salt, Mushrooms, Butter or margarine, 
Preheat broiler.  Remove stems from mushrooms and chop finely. Reserve  caps.  Saute the stems and onion in 1/4 cup butter or margarine until  tender.  Stir in the bread crumbs, pistachios, parsley, marjoram, and salt,  mixing well.  Remove from heat.  Place the mushroom caps, stem-side up, on  a baking sheet.  Fill the centers with the stuffing and sprinkle with about  3 tablespoons melted butter. Broil about 6 inches from heat source for 5  minutes or until browned and heated through.
