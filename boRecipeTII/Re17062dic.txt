Vinaigrette Dressing
Plain nonfat yogurt, Acceptable vegetable oil, Garlic powder, Chopped fresh parsley, Finely chopped green onion, Dijon mustard, ITALIAN HERB VINAIGRETTE, Thyme, Salt, Black Pepper, Water, Rosemary, Balsamic vinegar, Basil, GARLIC VINAIGRETTE DRESSING, Salt-free lemon-pepper, GREEN ONION VINAIGRETTE, DIJON VINAIGRETTE DRESSING, 
Makes 1/2 cup. Serves 8; 1 tablespoon per serving. Combine all ingredients  in a small bowl and whisk until thoroughly blended. Cover and refrigerate.  For each variation below, add the listed ingredients to the above recipe.  (Omit pepper in basic recipe.)
