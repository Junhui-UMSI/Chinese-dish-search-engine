Chili-Cheese Cornbread
Eggs; slightly beaten, (16oz) cream style corn, (4oz) chopped green chilies;, Mayonnaise, Diced onion, Cheddar cheese, Buttermilk, Self-rising cornmeal mix, 
Combine eggs, corn, chilies, mayo, onion, cheese, and buttermilk in a large  bowl; stir to blend. Add cornmeal mix; stirring just until moistened. Pour  into greased 13x9x2 inch pan. Bake at 375 degrees F. for 40 to 45 minutes  or until golden. Serves 12.    Liz C.
