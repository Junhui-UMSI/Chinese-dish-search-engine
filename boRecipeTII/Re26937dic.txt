Baked Cannellini Beans
Beer, Brown sugar, Strips thick-sliced bacon;, Dry mustard, Yellow onion; chopped, Cannellini Beans Master, Salt and pepper; to t, Molasses, 
THIS IS A LEFT-OVER RECIPE FOR USE WITH THE AROMATIC CANNELLINI BEANS  MASTER RECIPE.    Combine onion and bacon in an ovenproof frying pan. Cook, stirring, until  bacon is golden, about 7 minutes. Stir in molasses, brown sugar, mustard  and beer. Add beans and bring to a boil, stirring. remove from heat and  season generously with salt and pepper.    Transfer to a preheated 375F oven and bake, uncovered, for 25 minutes.    NOTES : Serve with grilled sausages (or hot dogs) and cole slaw; follow  with apple pie a la mode.
