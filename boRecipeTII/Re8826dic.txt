Praline Ham
Maple syrup, (about 2 1/2 pounds), Sugar, Butter or margarine, Pecans, (1/2-inch-thick) ham slices, 
Source: Southern Living Nov  96    Bake pecans in a shallow pan at 325? F, stirring occasionally, 5 to 10  minutes or until toasted; set aside.    Bake ham slices in a shallow pan at 325? for 10 minutes.    Bring maple syrup, sugar, and butter to a boil in a small saucepan,  stirring often. Stir in pecans, and spoon over ham.    Bake 30 more minutes.    Nancy Woodall Bellaire, Texas "Thanks to packaged ham slices, you can  satisfy a craving without buying the whole hog. Pan fry, bake, or grill  these  1/2-inch pieces for a weeknight change of pace. Grab a slice or two  on your way home and ham it up with these fast flavor options.
