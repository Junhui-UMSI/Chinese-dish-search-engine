Teriyaki Spareribs
Honey, Pork spareribs, Brown sugar, Grated ginger root, Garlic salt, Soy sauce, Ketchup, 
Boil spareribs in large covered pot over low heat for half an hour. Drain.  Combine rest of ingredients. Marinate spareribs overnight. Grill over  charcoal or cook in broiler until nicely browned?be careful, for they brown  quickly. Serve and eat.
