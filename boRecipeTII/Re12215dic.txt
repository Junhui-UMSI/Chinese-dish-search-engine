Zucchini Casserole
Onion, Minced, Salt, Sharp Cheddar, Grated, HERB MIXTURE, Butter, Mixed Dry Herbs: Sage,, Zucchini, Grated Coarsely, Pepper, Eggs, Soft Bread Crumbs, Celery Leaves, Chopped Fine, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Drain the zucchini. Combine the zucchini,  onion, cheese, bread crumbs, eggs, butter, salt and pepper. Combine the  ingredients for the herb mixture. Re chop or rub the leaves together to  freshen the flavor. Stir into the zucchini mixture. Spoon the mixture into  a buttered 9" pie plate. Bake until golden (40-45 minutes). Let cool for 10  minutes before serving.    Joel Ehrlich on rec.food.cooking
