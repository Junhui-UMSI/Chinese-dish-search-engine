Green Beans with Basil
PRESSURE COOKER COOKBOOK, olive oil, Garlic; crushed, Green beans; ends removed, Chicken stock; or water, Salt, Pepper, Dried basil, Butter; optional, 
In pressure cooker, heat oil.  Add garlic and saute in hot oil 1 minute.  Stir in beans.  Add chicken stock, salt, pepper, and basil. Secure lid.  Over high heat, develop steam to med-high pressure. Reduce range heat to  mantain pressure and cook 3 mins.  Quickly release pressure accdg. to  manufacturers directions.  Remove lid. Stir beans, then drain in colander.  Place beans in a serving dish and dot with butter, if desired. Makes 6 to 8  servings.
