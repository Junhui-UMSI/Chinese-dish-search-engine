Peanut Butter Squares
Marshmallow creme, Granulated sugar, Evaporated milk, Peanut butter, 
Combine sugar and milk in heavy saucepan, cook to 238F on a candy  thermometer, stirring constantly. REmove from heat; add peanut butter and  marshmallow creme. Stir until smooth; spread into buttered 8" square pan.  Cool and cut into squares.
