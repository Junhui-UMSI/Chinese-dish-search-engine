President Lyndon B Johnson Barbecue Sauce
Granulated sugar, Water, Garlic, Cider vinegar, celery, Onion, Black pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Bay Leaves, Chili powder, Butter, Salt, Paprika, Ketchup, 
Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan. Bing mixture to a boil. Turn  diown heat and simmer 15 minutes. Remove from heat and strain.
