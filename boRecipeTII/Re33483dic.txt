Basil Oil2
Tightly packed fresh basil, Olive or vegetable oil, 
In a large sauce pan, blanch basil leaves for 5 seconds. Refresh in an ice  bath and squeeze out any excess liquid. Place leaves and oil in a blender  and puree.    First, strain the puree through a fine mesh sieve. Then line a small  strainer with several layers of cheesecloth and strain oil into a glass  jar. Flavored oil keeps, covered loosely and refrigerated at all times, 1  month.
