All Purpose Seasoning
Coriander;, Minced dehydrated onion;, Spearmint;, Parsley flakes;, Seasame seeds;, Bay leaf powder;, Dry mustard;, Rosemary;, Dried basil;, Spearmint;, Marjoram;, Celery seed;, Oregano;, Garlic powder;, Thyme;, Dill seed;, 
In a blender, blend dehydrated onion into finer pieces.  Add remaining  herbs.  Blend to mix well, but do not make into a powder.  To keep  herbs fresh, store in shaker top bottle with tight fitting lid.    Can be used on all cooking, and at the table too to be used instead of  salt.  This is also bottled as commerical product called "Instead of  Salt" All Purpose Herb Seasoning, if you want to save time.    Yield: 1 3/4 oz or 9 3/4 tablespoon or 29 1/2 teaspoon.
