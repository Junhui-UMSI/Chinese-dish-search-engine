Potluck Spareribs
Brown sugar, Ketchup, Ground mustard, Pork spareribs, Honey, Ground ginger, Salt, Pepper, Vinegar, Soy sauce, Garlic powder, 
Cut ribs into serving-size pieces; place with the meaty side up on racks in  two greased 13 in x 9 in x 2 in baking pans. Cover tightly with foil. Bake  at 350F for 1 1/4 hours or until meat is tender. Drain; remove racks and  return ribs to pans. Combine remaining ingredients; pour over ribs. Return  to the oven, uncovered, for 35 minutes or until sauce coats ribs, basting  occasionally. Ribs can also be grilled over medium-hot coals for the last       35    minutes instand of baking.
