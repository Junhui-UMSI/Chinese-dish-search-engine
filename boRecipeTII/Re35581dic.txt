String Bean Soup (Potage Aux Haricots Verts)
Salt or salt substitute, Green beans, cut, Potatoes, peeled amd diced, Flour, Black pepper to taste, Bacon, diced, Dairy sour cream or, Water, 
Add salt to water; bring to boil. Cook beans in boiling salted water for 40  minutes. Add potatoes. Cook for 15 minutes. Fry Bacon until crisp. remove  bacon, drain on absorbent paper. Reserve. Brown flour in bacon fat. Add  flour-fat mixture to soup. cook and stir for 5 minutes. Add pepper and  bacon bits. Garnish with sour cream.
