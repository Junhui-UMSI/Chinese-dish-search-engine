Three Bean Salad for the 90s
White wine vinegar, Salt, Zucchini, ends trimmed,, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Each cooked small red or, Dijon mustard, Each chopped fresh basil,, Red and yellow bell pepper,, Finely sliced red onion, 
In the bottom of a mixing bowl combine the olive oil, vinegar and Dijon  mustard and season well with salt and pepper. Add the beans, peppers and  red onion. Toss, adjust seasoning and chill until serving time.    Right before serving add the zucchini and herbs so they dont discolor.
