Barbecued Shrimp #1
Thawed shrimp in shell, Onion (optional), Lemons; juice of, Black pepper, Butter, Bottle (large) Italian, 
Melt butter in large pan. Add shrimp, salad dressing, lemon juice & pepper.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes. Stir frequently to bring shrimp on bottom to  top. Serve with chunks of French bread to dip in sauce. Serves 6-8.
