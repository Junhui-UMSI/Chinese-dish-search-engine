Texas Snake Cakes
Rattlesnake per person, Crab boil **, Egg, Vegetable oil, 
Gut the rattlesnake and peel the skin off.  Cook in a crab boil**. Cool and  peel off the strips of meat. Chop and combine with egg to bind, salt and  pepper and a bit of green onion. Saute in oil until brown on both sides.  Serve with tartar sauce.
