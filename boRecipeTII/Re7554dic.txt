Pork Roast with Red Cabbage
Lemon juice, Apple, Sugar, Red wine, Salt, red cabbage, Pork roast, Onion, Bay leaf, 
Soak clay pot for 15 minutes.  If the bottom of your pot is glazed, spray  it with non-stick spray and soak the top for 15 minutes. Put the cabbage in  the bottom of the pot, salt, add lemon juice or vinegar to taste, then the  sugar, onion, apple and the wine.  Place seasoned pork roast on the  ingredients.  Cover and put into COLD oven, turn temperature to 425 degrees  F. Bake for 2 1/2 hours.    This recipe is delicious!    From hatridge@syix.com Thu May 23 09:59:50 1996
