Tangy Cauliflower
Butter, Head cauliflower; about 1, Italian dressing, Seasoned bread crumbs, Pepper; To Taste, Butter, 
Break cauliflower into flowerette & Place in medium size bowl. Dot with 1  tablespoon butter. Cover with plastic wrap. Microwave at high (100%) 5  minutes. Stir. Pour dressing over cauliflower. Add pepper to taste. Cover  with plastic wrap. Microwave at high (100%) until tender crisp, 2 to 3  minutes.    Place 2 tablespoons butter in a 1 cup measure. Microwave at high (100%)  until soft, 15 seconds. Mix in bread crumbs. Sprinkle over cauliflower.  Makes 6 servings.
