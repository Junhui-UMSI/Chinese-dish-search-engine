Trail Cookies
Eggs; OR..., Margarine; melted, All-purpose flour, Finely grated orange peel, Orange juice, -Egg substitute, Brown sugar, firmly packed, Honey, Molasses, Chopped Calif. Dried Figs, Golden raisins, Whole wheat flour, Walnuts, Cinnamon, Wheat germ, Baking Powder, Vanilla, 
Combine flours, sugar, wheat germ, baking powder and cinnamon.  In smaller  bowl, blend eggs, margarine, honey, molasses, orange peel, vanilla and  orange juice with wire whip.  Add liquid to dry ingredients, whip until  smooth.  Add figs, raisins, and walnuts.  Spread in a greased 9 x 13 inch  baking pan.  Bake in a 300 F. oven 35 minutes, until it tests done.
