Poule Deau Jambalaya
Celery stalks, chopped, Garlic head, chopped, Smoked sausage, sliced, Shallots, bunch, chopped, Bell peppers, chopped, Rabbit, deboned & cubed, Salt, Bay Leaves, Parsley, fresh/chopped/sprig, Worcestershire sauce, bottle, Onions, chopped, Mushrooms, button, Rice, raw, Tomatoes, blended, Ro-Tel, 
Brown sausage in a black iron pot (10 Qt min) over medium heat for 10  minutes. Remove and put aside. Add rabbit to residual juices in pot and  cook uncovered over medium heat until well browned. Add onions and garlic,  cook covered until onions become transparent. Add Ro-Tel, Pickapepper and  mushrooms (incl. juice) and cook covered over low heat for 1/2 hour. Add  celery, shallots, bell pepper and salt to taste, and cook covered over low  heat for 20 minutes. Now add as much hot water as necessary to achieve  consistency of stew. Turn heat to high. When boiling (rolling), add bay  leaves, parsley, rice and sausage, mix well. Cook uncovered on high until  liquid reduced to level of rice. Cover and cook low for 1/2 hr. Turn mix,  continue to done. Recipe date: 12/07/74
