Baked Pumpkin Seeds
Butter, Vegetable oil, Salt, Pumpkin seeds, 
Separate pumpkin seeds from the pulp but dont wash the seeds.    Mix seeds, butter, salt, and stir.    Grease baking tray with oil and pour on buttered seeds. Gently shake the  pan to even out the seeds.    Bake seeds at 200 degrees F. for 45 mins.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by DawnBinTN@aol.com on Oct 23, 1998,
