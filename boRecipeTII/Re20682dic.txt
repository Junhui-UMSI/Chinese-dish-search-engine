Apple Crisp 2
Golden Delicious apples -, Quick-cooking rolled oats, Butter or margarine, Sugar, Chopped nuts (almonds or, Unsifted all-purpose flour, Sugar, 
Combine apple slices and 1/2 cup sugar in bottom of a 2-quart casserole.  Mix butter, oats, flour, 1/2 cup sugar and nuts. Sprinkle butter and oat  mixture over apples. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for about 1 hour  until topping is browned and apples are tender. Serve warm. This is  delicious topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
