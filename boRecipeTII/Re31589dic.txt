Swiss Almond Apple Cake
Eggs, TOPPING, CAKE, Powdered sugar, Butter, Salt, Almonds, ground, Lemon Juice, Sugar, Baking Powder, Lemom peel, Red Raspberry Preserves, Apples, GLAZE, Sugar, Lemon juice, Flour, Eggs, Sour Cream, Flour, 
1.  Heat oven to 375F.  Grease and flour 9 or 10-inch springform pan.    2.  In a large bowl, combine 2/3 cup sugar and butter; beat until  light and fluffy.    3.  Add 2 eggs and 2 tablespoons of lemon juice; beat until well  blended.    4.  In a small bowl combine 2 cups flour, baking powder, and salt;  blend    well.    5.  Add to egg mixture; beat at low speed until well blended.    6.  Spread in prepared pan.  Then spoon preserves over batter;  carefully spread to cover.    7.  Top with apple slices; slightly press into batter.    8.  In medium bowl, combine all topping ingredients; blend well and  pur over apples.    9.  Bake at 350F. for 55 to 65 minutes or until apples are tender,  edges are light golden brown and toothpick inserted in center comes  out clean.    10. Cool 10 minutes, then carefully remove sides of pan.    11. In small bowl, blend all glaze ingredients until smooth; drizzle  over cake.
