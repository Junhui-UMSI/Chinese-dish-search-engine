Aunt Lucille Wanderers Tamale Pie
SAUCE, Tomato soup, Salt, whole kernal corn, diced tomatoes  , Chili powder, butter, TAMALE PIE, Butter, Cornmeal, Eggs, Lean ground beef, Chili powder, milk, 
Tamale Pie Stir ingredients together in the order given above. Pour into  large casserole dish or 9" x 13" pan. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for  approximately 1 hour or until solid, dry and golden brown.    Sauce Blend ingredients together with a wire wisk.    Serve with sauce over top of individual servings.    OPTIONS Can be cooked in a microwave on high for approximately 20 minutes.  One half of mixed Tamale Pie ingredients may be frozen for baking at a  later date.
