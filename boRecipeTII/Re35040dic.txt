Beans n Rice Skillet
(8 oz) cans no-salt-added, Uncooked instant white rice, SAUCE, Water, Can black beans, drained, Thick and chunky salsa, And rinsed, Tomato sauce, Chopped red bell pepper, Frozen whole kernel corn, 
Combine sauce ingredients in large skillet; mix well.  Bring to a boil.  Stir in all remaining ingredients.  Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer  about 10 minutes or until rice is cooked and vegetables are tender.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Mar 21, 98
