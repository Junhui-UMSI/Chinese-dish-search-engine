Apricot Nut Bread
Baking soda, Eggs, Sugar, Salt, Apricot halves (16 oz.), Walnuts, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Shortening, 
Drain apricots, reserving juice.  Press apricots through sieve. Add  enough reserved syrup or juice to make 1 cup; set aside.  Cream sugar  and shortening together until fluffy.  Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating  well after each addition.  Combine dry ingredients and add to creamed  mixture. Add apricots.  Stir just until all ingredients are  moistened. Stir in walnuts.  Spoon batter into well greased loaf pan.  Bake at 350 F. for 50 minutes or until bread tests done. Remove from  pan and place on rack to cool.
