Sweet and Hot Chili Sauce
Fresh Chilies, Rice vinegar, Stephen Ceideburg, Soy sauce, Nam Pla, Juice of 2 limes, Sugar, 
Put all ingredients into a saucepan. Mix well, then bring to a boil over  high heat. Cook until mixture is reduced by half. Remove from heat. Cover  and store in the refrigerator until ready to use.    SOURCE: Flower and Garden Magazine Feb/Mar 92
