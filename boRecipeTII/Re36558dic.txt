Strawberry and Cream Cake
Cake flour, Vanilla, Sugar, Salt, Baking Powder, Eggs, Whipping cream, 
Preheat oven to 350. Grease two 9-by-1.5-inch layer cake pans; dust  lightly with flour; tap out any excess. Measure flour, sugar, baking  powder, & salt into a sifter; reserve. Beat cream in a medium-size  bowl until stiff; reserve. Beat eggs in a small bowl until very thick  and light; beat in vanilla; fold in reserved whipped cream. Sift dry  ingredients over cream mixture; gently fold in until batter is  smooth. Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or  until center springs back when lightly pressed with fingertip. Cool  layers in pans on wire racks for 10 minutes; loosen edges with a  knife, turn out onto wire racks, & cool completely. Put layers  together with strawberry butter-cream frosting; then frost top & side  with remainder. Garnish with strawberries.    Strawberry Butter-Cream Frosting: 1 pint strawberries 1/2 cup butter        1 lb confectioners sugar    Mash enough strawberries to measure 0.5 cup. Beat butter in a medium  bowl until soft.
