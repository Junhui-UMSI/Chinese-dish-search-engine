Tart Apples Baked with Orange Juice and Honey
Tart granny smith apples, Golden raisins, Juice of 1/2 lemon, Ground cinnamon, Orange juice, Tupelo honey, 
Spray glass baking pan with light cooking spray. Cut cone out of top of  each apple and spoon some lemon juice into each. Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Add some honey and juice to each and stuff each with raisins. Microwave,  lightly covered, until skins have split and apples are softened. Serve hot.
