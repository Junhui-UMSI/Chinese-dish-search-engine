Banana Fruity Pie
Skim milk, (4 serv) sugar free Jello, (20-oz) light Cherry pie, Cool Whip Free, Fat free cream cheese, 6-oz Reduced fat graham, 
whisk cream cheese until smooth. Gradually beat in milk until well blended.  Add pudding mix. Stir in Cool Whip Free.    Spread in pie crust. Spoon cherry pie filling over top. Refrigerate 3  hours.  Posted to EAT-LF Digest by jenk@midplains.net (Jennifer) on Mar 8, 1998
