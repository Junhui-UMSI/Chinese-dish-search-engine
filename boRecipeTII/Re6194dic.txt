Pizza Sauce #2
Brown sugar, Clove garlic (chopped), Oregano, Raw onion (chopped), Tomato sauce, Water, Cooking oil, Tomato paste, 
Combine ingredients in a 2 qt sauce pan and cook for 30-40 minutes. Makes  enough sauce for 5 12" Pizzas.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Judith Vonneumann <pooh4jvn@ix.netcom.com>  on Feb 07, 1998
