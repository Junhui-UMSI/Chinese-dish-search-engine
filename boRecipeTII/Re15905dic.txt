Acorn Squash with Creamed Onions
Acorn Squash, Frozen Onions With Cream, Salt, Paprika, 
Prep Time: 20 minutes Bake Time: 1 hour 10 minutes Total Time: 1 hour 20 minutes Oven Temp: 325F / 170C / Gas Mark 3  Wash the squash and cut in half length-wise; remove seeds from squash. Place in  shallow pan, cut side down. Bake until almost tender (45 to 50 min.).    Turn cut side up and sprinkle with salt. Continue baking until tender (15  to 20 more minutes.) Heat onions according to package directions. Spoon  into squash centers. Sprinkle with paprika.
