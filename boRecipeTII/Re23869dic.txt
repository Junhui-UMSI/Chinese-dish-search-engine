Asian Spagetti Salad
White pepper, Ginger; grated, Soy sauce, Sesame seed oil, Carrots; raw, sliced, Plum tomatoes; raw, diced, DRESSING, Scallions; raw, sliced, Garlic clove; crushed, Italian dressing, Cooked spaghetti, Black Olives; small pitted, 
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl - refrigerate for at least 2 hours -  add additional soy sauce or Italian dressing if needed.  Posted to recipelu-digest by Chtbaby <Chtbaby@aol.com> on Mar 9, 1998
