Peachy Scotch Pie
Brown sugar, Flour, Salt, Vanilla extract or almond, Peach slices (1 lb. 13 oz.);, Butter, Lemon juice, 
In saucepan, combine sugar, flour, salt, butter, lemon juice and extract;  mix well. Bring to rolling boil, over medium heat, stirring constantly.  Boil 1 minute, stir in peach slices. Pour into 9 inch pie shell. Top with  lattice crust. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes.
