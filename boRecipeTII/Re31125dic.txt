Banana Split Cake #2
(large) Cool Whip, Butter, Pecans, Graham cracker crumbs, Vanilla, -(up to), (large) crushed pineapple;, Butter, Powdered sugar, Bananas, Boxes (large) frozen, Eggs, 
Combine graham cracker crumbs with melted butter; pat into bottom of a  9x13-inch pan. Bake at 350 for 8-9 minutes. Cream together butter, powdered  sugar, vanilla & eggs. Spread over graham cracker mixture. Spread drained  pineapple on bananas, then the strawberries, then the Cool Whip. Sprinkle  with chopped nuts. Cover with plastic wrap. Chill for 6 hours before  serving. Serves 15. Variation: Use vanilla wafer crumbs for the crust; use  2 egg whites instead of 2 whole eggs; delete frozen strawberries. MRS  JOANNE HUNT.
