Apricot Horseradish Sauce
White horseradish, Salt, Freshly grated or prepared, Pepper, Fresh lime juice, Apricot jam, 
Combine the jam and horseradish in a mixing bowl and whisk until smooth.  Whisk in the lime juice and salt and pepper. If not serving immediately,  transfer the sauce to a glass jar with a nonmetallic lid and refrigerate.  It will keep for several weeks.
