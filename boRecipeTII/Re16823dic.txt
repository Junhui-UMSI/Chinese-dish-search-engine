All Shook-Up Vanilla Ice Cream
Vanilla, , Salt, , Sugar, 2% milk, Ice, 
In a pint-size bag, mix milk, sugar and vanilla. Then seal. Fill  gallon-size bag half full of ice and salt. Put sealed small bag in with the  ice. Seal large bag. Shake the bag about 5 minutes until it becomes ice  cream. Wipe the small bag off. Open and dig in!
