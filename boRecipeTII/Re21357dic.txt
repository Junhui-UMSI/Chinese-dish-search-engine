Under the Skin Turkey
Cloves garlic -- minced, Following combinations, OR, Thyme, OR, Combine with one of the, Prepared brown mustard, Plastic blade, Turkey, Fresh Lemon Juice, Softened sweet butter, Orange juice concentrate, In a food processor using, Dried sage, Dried tarragon, Lemon Peel, 
With fingers carefully loosen the skin of the turkey all around the breast  area. Using your hands, pack butter mixture under skin, spreading to cover.  Reserve a little to coat outside of bird before roasting. stuff your bird  with favortie stuffing. Place turkey on a rack in a large roasting pan  Roast uncovered at 375 degrees 15 to 20 minutes per pound DO NOT BASTE  cover with foil the last hour if skin gets to brown. Remove turkey from  oven cool 30 minutes before carving Meanwhile make gravy using pan juices.  10 to 12 servings    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
