Thai Beef with Noodles
boneless beef, Sesame oil, Dry sherry, green onion, linguine, cooked, fresh ginger, crushed red pepper flakes, Reduced-sodium soy sauce, 
Combine sherry, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, sesame oil and pepper pods.  Place beef steak in plastic bag; add marinade. Close bag securely and  marinate 15 minutes. Pour off marinade; reserve. Heat nonstick skillet over  medium heat 5 minutes. Add steak and cook 12 to 15 minutes for rare (140)  to medium (160), turning once. Remove steak; keep warm. Pour off drippings.  Dissolve 2 tsp cornstarch in reserved marinade and 1/4 c water; add to  skillet.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Stir in noodles. Carve  steak into thin slices and serve over noodles. Sprinkle with green onion.
