Zesty Coconut Cream Dressing
.70 ounce pk. italian, Cider vinegar, Coco Lopez cream of coconut, 
In small jar with tight-fitting lid or bowl, combine ingredients; mix well.  Chill to blend flavors. Stir before serving. Serve with tossed green  salads, fruit salads, or with coleslaw. Refrigerate leftovers.    Coco Lopez Cookbook
