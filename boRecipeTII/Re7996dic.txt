Potato - Dill Soup
Fresh or frozen dill, Cream (half & half), Water, Salt, Potatoes, cubed, Hard-boiled eggs, Flour, 
Boil potatoes in salt water. Combine flour and cream. When potatoes almost  cooked, add flour and cream mixture. Bring soup to a boil. Add finely  chopped dill (do not use stems of dill). Garnish with chopped egg. Season  with vinegar to taste. Clearwater, Florida Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4  #143 by LRenfroe@aol.com on May 23, 1997
