Wild Mushroom Lasagna
Butter, Salt and pepper, Mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, Italian parsley, fresh, milk, Flour, Mushroom, wild, Lasagna noodles, Garlic, Thyme, Onion, Nutmeg, Marsala wine, Fontina cheese, 
Preheat oven to 375F    In a large skillet, heat olive oil, on med. high  heat. Add garlic and  onions and saute 6 to 7 minutes. Add mushrooms, parsley and thyme, saute 5  minutes. Stir in wine. Simmer for 5 minutes and set aside.    In saucepan, make a roux with butter, flour and nutmet. Stir in broth and  milk. Collk til sauce thickens. Stir in Fontina Cheese.    In bottom of 12" x 8" baking dish arrange 1/3 noodles. Spread with 1/4  mushroom mixture and 1/4 of the sauce. Repeat, finishing with sauce.  Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan. Cover with foil and bake 30 minutes.  Remove foil, bake 10 more minutes.
