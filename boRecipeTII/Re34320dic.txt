Bbqd Baby Back Ribs
Black Pepper, Sorghum molasses, Ketchup, Cayenne, DRY RUB, Yellow onion; diced, Paprika, Salt, Black pepper, Chili powder, White vinegar, Onion powder, Water, Salt, SAUCE, 
DRY RUB DIRECTIONS: Mix in jar, cover and shake well to mix. Sprinkle rub  liberally over and into the surface.    RIB SMOKING DIRECTIONS: Prepare smoker for long, slow cooking using  hickory, mesquite is also very good. Some people prefer to do back ribs  using some of the wood of the fruit trees, apple, pear, etc.    BBQ SAUCE DIRECTIONS: Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan. Bring to  a gentle simmer.  Allow to cool for at least one hour to allow the flavors  to blend.
