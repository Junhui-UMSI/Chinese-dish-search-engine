Vegetable Minestrone Soup
Corn, Bay leaf, Celery, Barley, Garlic, Basil, tomatoes, canned, Salt, Black pepper, Carrots, peas, white beans, Onions, Leek, Red potatos, Chopped, 
Saute garlic, oniions and leek. Add tomatoes and one can water, potatos,  celery and carrots and cook 25 minutes. Add barley and cook 10 minutes. Add  remaining ingrediants and cook 20 minutes or until barley is done. Makes  about 1 gallon.    Posted by Timo <tbraam@census.gov> to the Fatfree Digest [Volume 14 Issue  13] Jan. 13, 1995.
