Bread and Butter Pickles
Pickling cucumbers; in 1/4", Sliced onion, Sugar, Vinegar, Celery seed, Dry mustard, Salt, Tumeric, 
Mix everything together in a pot and bring to a boil. Simmer for 4 minutes.  Stir and simmer for another 4 minutes.    Cool, refrigerate and enjoy!    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Kaplan Consulting Corp. (Dovid Kaplan)"  <kapcon@netbox.com> on Sep 25, 1998,
