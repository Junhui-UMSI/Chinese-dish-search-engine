Sweet-Sour Bean Trio
Brown sugar, Vinegar, Can kidney beans -- drained, Onion -- chopped, Prepared mustard, Can lima beans -- drained, Bacon, Salt, Can baked beans -- drained, Clove garlic -- crushed, 
Cook bacon in skillet or slow-cooking pot with browning unit.  Crumble  bacon.  Combine cooked baon and 2 tbs. bacon drippings with oni9on, brown  sugar, mustard, garlic, salt and vinegar.  Mix with three kinds of beans in  slow-cooking pot.  Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.
