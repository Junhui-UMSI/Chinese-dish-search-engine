Peppy Panini
8 inch focaccia (Italian, Slices pepperoni, Sliced, Tomato, thinly sliced, Green bell pepper, thinly, Shredded provolone cheese, Flat bread), Separated, Cheese, divided, Thin slices red onion,, Light garden vegetable cream, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Slice forcaccia in half horizontally. Spread each  half with 3 T cream cheese. Layer one half with tomato, bell pepper,  pepperoni and cheese. Cover with remaining bread half. Wrap tightly in  foil. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until hot. Cut into wedges.
