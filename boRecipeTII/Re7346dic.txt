Pork Chops in Wine
Dry white wine, Pepper, Onion, Pork loin chops, oleo, Salad oil, Salt, Dry mustard, Chopped parsley, 
Saute onion in hot oil in large skillet, drain on paper towels and set  aside.  Reserve drippings. Combine butter, mustard, salt and pepper. Stir  to make paste, spread on both sides of chops. Brown chops on both sides in  reserved drippings, drain.  Return onions to skillet, add wine, cover and  simmer 1 hr. or until chops are done. Sprinkle with chopped parsley before  serving.
