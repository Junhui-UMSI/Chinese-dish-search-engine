Sweet Briar Rose Hip Jam
Sugar, Water, Lemon juice, Hips; before seeding, 
Prepare hips and measure 1 cup.  Boil sugar and water 4 minutes, add hips  and lemon juice and boil, covered, 15 minutes, uncover and boil 5 minutes  more.  The berries should be clear and transparent and syrup thick. When  done, pour into hot sterilized jars and seal.    * Source: Flower Cookery - by Mary MacNicol * Typed for you by Karen  Mintzias
