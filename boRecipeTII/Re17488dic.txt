Wan Tan (Wonton)
Egg, FILLING, Ginger; finely chopped, Wonton wrappers, WRAPPERS, Peanut oil for deep frying, Ground pork, Salt, Spinach; finely chopped, Soy sauce, 
1. Mix ground pork, spinach, soy sauce, ginger and salt in a bowl.    2. Place 1/2 tbsp on wrapper and fold, seal with egg.    3. Deep fry at 375F and serve with sweet and sour sauce, or use in wonton  soup.
