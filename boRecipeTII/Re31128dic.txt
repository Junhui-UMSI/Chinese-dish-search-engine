Banana Split Cake #5
Box (16-oz) powdered sugar, (16-oz) crushed pineapple, Eggs, Strawberries (optional), Nuts to garnish, Bananas, (large) cool whip, Butter, Graham cracker crumbs, 
From:    Tina Rogers <CAJUNT@UGA.CC.UGA.EDU>    Date:    Mon, 29 Jul 1996 19:33:53 EDT  Melt 1/2 c. butter & blend with cracker crumbs; press into 13x9 pan.  Mix 1  cup butter with powdered sugar & 2 eggs; beat 15 minutes & spread on top of  crust.  Next, slice bananas on top of filling, spread pineapple &  strawberries over bananas. Top with cool whip & nuts. Refrigerate several  hours.    EAT-L Digest 29 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
