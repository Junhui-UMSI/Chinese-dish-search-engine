Turkey Sausage and Black-Eyed Pea Stew
Smoked turkey sausage,, CAJUN SEASONING, Water, Garlic powder, STEW, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Ribs celery, finely chopped, Red pepper, diced, Finely chopped parsley, Dried basil, Dried thyme, Paprika, Flour, Low-sodium chicken broth,, Can black-eyed peas, Or 1 t dried sage, crushed, Onion powder, Divided, Can peeled and diced, Tomatoes, drained, Black Pepper, Onion, Sliced 1/2-inch thick, Chopped fresh sage leaves,, Long-grain white rice, Salt, Cayenne pepper, Rice, 
To prepare the rice: Combine the water, rice and salt in a medium saucepan.  Bring to a boil and continue boiling, uncovered, until the level of the  liquid just covers the rice. Stir occasionally. Cover and reduce the heat  to low. Cook 15 minutes. Set aside, covered.    To prepare the Cajun seasoning: Combine the thyme, basil, paprika, cayenne,  onion powder, garlic powder and flour. Set aside.    To prepare the stew: In a large saucepan or skillet, preferably nonstick,  heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic; saute 3 minutes.  Add the celery, bell pepper and 2 tablespoons of the broth. Cover and cook  5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the turkey sausage and saute with the  vegetables, uncovered, 5 minutes. Then sprinkle on the Cajun seasoning and  cook 1 minute. Add the remaining broth and the tomatoes. Cook 5 minutes,  stirring occasionally. Drain and rinse the black-eyed peas; drain well  again. Put into the pan with the sage, cover and cook over medium-low heat  10 minutes, stirring often. Stir in the parsley and pepper. Adjust the  seasonings if necessary and serve over rice.
