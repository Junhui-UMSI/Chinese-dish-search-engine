Turmeric Stuffing
Onion, White basmati rice, chicken broth  , cashews, White wine, Egg, Butter, celery, Raisins, garlic, Turmeric powder, Chopped, Salt and pepper, 
1. Saute dry rice in butter (4 tablespoons) until translucent. (dont  burn!) Add the celery, garlic, onion and turmeric powder. Mix well in pan.  Cook for a couple of minutes, take of the stove and add the chicken broth,  raisins and nuts. 2. Bring the whole thing to a boil, then turn down to  simmer, tightly cover and simmer for about 20 minutes. Let rice cool down,  then fluff with a fork. 3. Break egg into small dish, mix with a fork and  add about 4 tablespoons melted butter along with the wine. Mix this into  the rice. . . . . thats it.
