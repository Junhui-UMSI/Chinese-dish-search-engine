Banana Cream Pie
Lemon juice, Whipped Cream; Optional, Sliced Bananas, Coconut, TOPPING, Stick Butter; 2 Oz., FILLING, Ice Cream; Softened Slightly, Sugar; Optional, Coconut; Flaked Or Shredded, Sugar, Rum; Optional, 
1. Melt the butter and mix with coconut and sugar, like a crumb crust.    2. Line pie pan with mixture and bake in a 375 degree oven until toasted  and brown 5 to 7 minutes.    Filling:    1. Ist layer: Sliced bananas (Which have been dipped in lemon juice first  so they wont darken).    2. 2nd layer: Sprinkling of coconut.    3. 3rd layer: Ice cream (softened slightly).    4. Repeat layers and sprinkle top with coconut. Chill in freezer.    Topping:    1. If desired, remove from freezer and top with 1/2 cup of whipped cream  that has been whipped and flavored with 1 tablespoon rum an 1 tablespoon  sugar.
