White Hot Chocolate
3" stick cinnamon, Nutmeg, Vanilla, Vanilla chocolate chips, Half n half, Almond extract, 
In saucepan combine 1/4 cup half n half, chips, cinnamon and nutmeg. Whisk  over low heat until vanilla pieces are melted. Remove cinnamon. Add  remaining half n half. Whisk until heated through.
