Potato Kugel Almondine
Eggs; seperated, Sugar, Grated rind of lemon, Salt, Chopped almonds, Lemon ; juice of, Mashed potatoes; cooked, 
Beat egg yolks and add to potatoes. Add sugar, lemon juice, lemon rind,  almonds and salt. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Place in a greased pan  which is set in a pan of boiling water. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by RedKurls <RedKurls@aol.com> on Apr 1, 1998
