Sweet and Sour Spareribs #2
Spareribs; cracked into 2, Prepared mustard, Monosodium glutamate, Soy sauce, Brown sugar, 
Have butcher cut ribs into small pieces. Bake ribs at 325? for 1-1/2 hours.  Drain on paper towels. Make sauce with remaining ingredients, then brush on  ribs. Return ribs to 300? oven for 45 minutes. Baste several times while  cooking.    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
