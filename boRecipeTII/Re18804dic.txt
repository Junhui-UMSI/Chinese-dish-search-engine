Anchovy Yogurt Dressing
Corn oil, Anchovy paste;, Red (cayenne) pepper, Salflower, Garlic, Fresh parsley, White vinegar, Plain low-fat yogurt;, White pepper, Salt, 
Excellent dressing for vegetables, pasta, fish, or salads.    Place all ingedients in a blender and process until smooth.  Refrigerate to blend flavors; adjust seasoning before serving.  Makes  about 3/4 cup.
