Pineapple and Passionfruit Muffins
Fresh passionfruit pulp -or-, (6-oz) passionfruit pulp, Sugar, Margarine, DRY MIX, WET MIX, Vanilla yoghurt, Eggs, Finely grated, Lemon; rind & juice of, Self-raising flour, Crushed canned pineapple, 
Preheat oven to 350F, prepare pans. Blend the margarine & sugar then beat  in the yoghurt & eggs. Add the passionfruit pulp, pineapple & lemon & mix  well. Fold in the flour & spoon into pans. Bake for 25-30 mins. Makes 12.  Variation: PP & Coconut: Add 1 cup macaroon coconut to the wet mix.  This  gives a firmer texture to the muffin.
