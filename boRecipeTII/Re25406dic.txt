Tortilla Crepes (Easy)
Whipped topping; For garnish, Fruit pie filling; (21, Flour tortilla; (6-7 inch), 
1. Spoon pie filling in a line down the center of each tortilla. Roll up  and place seam side down in a microwave-safe dish.    2. Microwave on high until filling is bubbly (about 3-4 minutes) Cool  slightly then spoon on whipped topping and sprinkle with confectioners  sugar. Serve warm.
