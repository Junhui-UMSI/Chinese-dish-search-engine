Tres Leches-Three Milks
Heavy whipping cream, Vanilla, Condensed milk, Eggs, Evaporated milk, pound cake, 
Slice pound cake, no thicker than 3 inches. Place in a pan that it fits  into snugly. In a blender, combine everything but egg whites. Pour over  cake. Beat eggs and sugar to taste for meringue. Pour over cake. Put oven  on broil. Broil cake only until meringue is golden brown. Then chill.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #482 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Feb 12, 1997.
