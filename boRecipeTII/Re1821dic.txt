Chicken Mushroom Fettuccine
fettuccine, Fresh mushrooms, Garlic, Butter or margarine, chunk white chicken, milk, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Cook fettuccine according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a skillet,  saute mushrooms and garlic in butter for 2-3 minutes. Add chicken and milk;  cook for 5-7 minutes or until heated through. Drain fettuccine; add to  skillet.  Sprinkle with cheese and toss to coat.
