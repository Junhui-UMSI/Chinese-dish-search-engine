Persian Kebabs
garlic, Salt, egplants, Salad oil, Pepper, Lamb, Green Pepper, tomatoes, Bay Leaves, Lemon juice, 
Prepare marinade: combine oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, pepper and bay  leaves.  Marinate meat cubes for 4 to 5 hours in refrigerator.    Thread meat, vegetables, and bay leaves on skewers. Brush with marinade.  Broil 3 minutes on each side, or barbecue 5 minutes on each side. Serve  immediately.
