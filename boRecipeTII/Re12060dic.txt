Zippy Tomato Sauce
Onion; Small, Thinly Sliced, Green Bell Pepper; Chopped,*, Tomato Sauce; 1 can, Chili powder, Worcestershire Sauce, Chili Sauce, Margarine or butter, 
*    You can use canned chopped Green Chiles or Jalapenos for a hotter       sauce.  Melt margarine in a small saucepan.  Add green peppers and onion.  Cook  and stir until the onion is tender.  (Note: If using the canned peppers,  just cook the onion alone.)  Stir in all the remaining ingredients and  heat to boiling, stirring occasionally.  Serve hot over patties.
