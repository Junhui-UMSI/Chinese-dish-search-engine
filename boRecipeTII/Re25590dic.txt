Avocado Salsa
Orange Peel, Chopped cilantro, Salt, Lemon juice, Onion, Ground cumin seed, Orange, peeled & diced, Cayenne pepper to taste, Ripe avacado, diced, Lemon Peel, 
Combine all except avacado.  Gently stir avacado into salsa and add cayenne  to taste.  Serve w chips or NAUGHTY (c*****n)!!
