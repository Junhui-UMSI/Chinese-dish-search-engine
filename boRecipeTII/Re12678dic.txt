Yogurt-Honey-Nut Dressing
Chopped, Walnuts -- toasted and, Honey, Lowfat yogurt, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
Blend lemon juice and honey into yogurt, adding more of either to reach  desired taste and consistency. Stir in nuts immediately before serving.
