Vegetable, Bean and Pasta Soup
Canned cannellini beans, Dry vermouth, Stewed tomatoes, Penne or other tubular pasta, rinsed, drained, Canned vegetable broth, (canned, Italianstyle), Zuchinni; diced, trimmed, chopped, Fennel bulb, Green Onions, Yellow crookneck squash, diced, White onion; chopped, Garlic, 
Bring broth and vermouth to boil in heavy large pot or Dutch oven over  medium heat.  Add onion, fennel and garlic.  Cover and cook until just  tender, about 10 minutes.  Add tomatoes, pasta, zucchini and squash; cook  until pasta is almost tender, about 15 minutes.  Add beans and green  onions.
