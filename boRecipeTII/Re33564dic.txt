Swedish Cheese Pie
Basic Pastry Pie Crust; 9", Crust, Eggs; Large, Almonds; Toasted, Fine Chop, Sugar; Granulated, Unbleached Flour; Sifted, CHEESE PIE, Light cream, Cottage Cheese, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.  Press the cottage cheese through a  sieve.  Place in a large mixing bowl and beat until smooth. Add the eggs,  flour, sugar, cream, and finely chopped almond and blend well. Pour the  mixture into the prepared crust and bake for about 45 minutes or until a  knife comes out clean.  Remove the pie from the oven and chill before  serving.
