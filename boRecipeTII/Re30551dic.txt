Taffy (Dulany)
Butter, Sugar, Vanilla, Vinegar, Water, 
Cook sugar, water, vinegar and butter without stirring, to hard-ball  stage. Remove from heat, add vanilla, and pour on buttered platter.  Let stand until cool enough to pull.  This may be tinted with food  coloring.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
