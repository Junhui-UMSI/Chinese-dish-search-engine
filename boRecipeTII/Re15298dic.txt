White Bean and Tuna Salad
Fresh Lemon Juice, Salt, (12-oz) white tuna, drained, olive oil, Chopped red onion, Dijon mustard, Shopped fresh basil, Lettuce leaves, (15-oz) cannellini (white, Tomato, seeded, diced, 
Combine beans, tuna, tomato and onion in large bowl. Combine lemond juice  and mustard in small bowl, whisk in oil. Add to salad. Mix in chopped  basil. Season with salt and pepper. Line plates with lettuce leaves. Spoon  salad onto lettuce. Garnish with whole basil leaves and serve.
