The Best Banana Bread
Sugar, Butter (I use butter, Vanilla, Salt, Flour, Baking soda, Eggs, Walnuts, Bananas, 
Cream butter (or crisco) with sugar; add eggs one at a time and mix well;  add bananas (mashed), vanilla; stir in dry ingredients and walnuts until  just blended (dont overmix).  Bake at 350 for 1 hour Makes (3) 8 inch  loaves or (2) 9 inch loaves    Posted to FOODWINE Digest 13 November 96    Date:    Wed, 13 Nov 1996 11:59:56 MST    From:    Suzy Waterman <suzy.waterman@LMCO.COM>
