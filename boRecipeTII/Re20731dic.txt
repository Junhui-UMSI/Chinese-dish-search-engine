Apple Dapple
Vegetable oil, Salt, Nuts; chopped fine, Coconut, Eggs, Sugar, Flour, Baking soda, TOPPING, Brown sugar, milk, Cinnamon, Vanilla, Butter, Chopped raw apples, 
Cream eggs, sugar and oil. Sift together flour, salt, soda and cinnamon.  Combine egg mixture with sifted dry ingredients. Stir in vanilla, nuts and  apples. Pour into greased 13 x 9 inch pan and bake 45 minutes at 350  degrees. Cook topping ingredients 2 1/2 minutes and pour over hot cake.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 23, 1998
