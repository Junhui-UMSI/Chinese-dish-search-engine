Glazed Sweet Potatoes
Sweet potatoes; cooked, Brown Sugar, Salt, Margarine or butter; melted, 
Cost: $ ; Preparation Time: 5 minutes    Difficulty Level: 2 ; Servings: 4 TO 5    1. Heat oven to 350 degrees.    2. Peel cooked potatoes; cut into quartersor slice.    3. Arrange in shallow baking dish.    4. Combine remaining ingredients; drizzle over potatoes.    5. Bake uncovered at 350F for 20 to 30 minutes or until glazed and heated  through.
