Barbecued Shrimp #2
Fresh jumbo shrimp in shell, Lemons; juice of, Salt, Butter or margarine, Hot sauce, -(up to), Worcestershire Sauce, Black pepper (or less), -(up to), Soy sauce, 
Melt butter in large flat pan. Add pepper, salt, lemon juice, soy sauce,  Worcestershire & hot sauce. Mix well. Add shrimp with shells left on. Mix  until shrimp are covered with sauce. Arrange shrimp in single layer in pan.  Broil about 10-15 minutes. Turn shrimp & broil until pink.    MRS.
