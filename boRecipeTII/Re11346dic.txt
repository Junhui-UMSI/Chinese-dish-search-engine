Ranch-Style Topping
Reduced-calorie ranch salad, Bacon bits; or 1 tablespoon, Idaho baking potatoes; baked, Low-fat cottage cheese, 
1. In small bowl, combine cottage cheese and ranch dressing stir until well  blended.    2. Cut baked potatoes in half lengthwise, cutting almost to base of potato.  Mash slightly with fork, leaving in skins. Spoon cottage cheese mixture  into potatoes, dividing evenly. Sprinkle with bacon bits.    Makes 4 servings. Preparation time: About 20 minutes.    Per serving: About 169 cal, 8 g pro, 33 g car, 1 g fat, 7% cal from fat, 3  mg cholesterol, 155 mg sod, 4 g fiber.
