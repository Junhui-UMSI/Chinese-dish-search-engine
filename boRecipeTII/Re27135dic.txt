Tigs Unsausage and Pasta Soup
Vegetable broth, Substitute), (or more, or another, Morninstar links, sliced, Green onions, thinly sliced, Bell pepper (your choice of, Small shell pasta, (vegetarian sausage, Thinly sliced serrano chilis, Frozen peas, Color), diced, Onion, Lemon pepper (or, some black, Pepper & a dash of lemon, Bay Leaves, Juice, I suppose), Variety, if you, Like), 
1. In a large pot, combine veg broth, links, onion, green onion, bell  pepper, bay leaves, lemon pepper, and serranos. Simmer for 20 minutes.    2. Add pasta shells and simmer another 7 to 9 minutes, until cooked tender.    3. Add peas and heat to warm. Ladle into bowls and serve.
