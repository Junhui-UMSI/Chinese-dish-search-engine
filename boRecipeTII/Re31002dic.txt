Banana Nut Ice Cream
Bananas, (13-oz) evaporated milk, Pecans, Vanilla, Half and half, Whipping cream, milk, Sugar, -(up to), Salt, 
Mash bananas. Mix with sugar, salt and vanilla. Add cream, Half and Half,  evaporated milk and nuts. Pour into ice cream freezer. Fill to line with  milk. Freeze according to freezer directions. Yield: 1 gallon.    MRS. JIM ROBASON    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
