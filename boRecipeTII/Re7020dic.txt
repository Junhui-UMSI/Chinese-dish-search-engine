Popeye Chicken
Parsley, Frozen chopped spinach;, Boneless skinless chicken, Curry powder, Cheddar cheese, Mayonnaise, Canned cream of chicken soup, Cornflake crumbs, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 35 min.    Preheat oven to 350F. Spray a casserole with nonstick cooking spray. Press  spinach in bottom of casserole in an even layer. Place chicken on top.  Combine next 3 ingredients in a bowl and spread evenly over chicken. Cover  top with cheese. Sprinkle corn flakes crumbs over top, then parsley. Bake  35-40 minutes, uncovered.
