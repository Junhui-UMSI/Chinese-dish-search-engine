Sweet Potatoes with Oatmeal Topping
Pineapple-orange marmalade, Rolled oats, Finely grated fresh, Orange juice, Brown Sugar, Topping:, Sweet potatos, Flour, Cinnamon, Stick butter; softened, Brown Sugar, Salt, Butter, 
Prick potatoes and microwave on high until soft. Scoop flesh into large  bowl and mash with butter, sugar, juice, marmalade, gingerroot and salt.  Spread mixture evenly in 2-qt. baking dish sprayed with non-stick spray.    Preheat oven to 400F    Using pastry cutter, combine topping ingredients until mixture resembles  coarse meal. Sprinkle topping over potato mixture and bake in middle of  oven until topping is lightly browned, about 25 minutes.
