Poached Yellow Delicious Apple
White wine vinegar, Sugar, Mashed potatoes, Yellow Delicious apples, Canned chestnut puree, CHESTNUT PUREE, Lemon juice, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Peel, core, and slice each apple in half  horizontally. Combine 1 quart boiling water, the lemon juice, sugar, and  vinegar in a saucepan; boil the apples in this mixture 5 minutes.  Remove  and drain. Stuff the drained apples with Chestnut Puree. Bake in preheated  oven 10 minutes. May be served warm from the oven or chilled. Chestnut  Puree:  Stir the puree and potatoes together until a smooth consistency is  reached.
