Vidalia Onion Custard
Butter, Eggs, Salt, milk, Black pepper, Egg yolk, Ground nutmeg, Vidalia onion; sliced thin, 
Cook onions in butter in a large skillet over moderate heat, stirring  occasionally, 30-40 minutes or until golden and soft.    Whisk together milk, eggs, yolk, and spices. Beat mix until well combined;  stir in onions. Transfer to a well buttered baking dish.    Bake at 325 40 to 50 minutes until lightly golden. A skewer inserted should  come out clean.    Serve hot or at room temperature.
