Basic Wild Rice
Salt; optional, Wild Rice, Butter Or Margarine; room, Water, 
Combine wild rice, water, and salt in steaming pan; cover and steam until  done, about 45 minutes. Add butter; fluff with fork before serving.    Variation: Mix steamed wild rice with sauteed mushrooms and fresh herbs.    NOTES : Especially good with game or poultry.    Recipe by: The Ultimate Rice Cooker Cookbook by Betty L.
