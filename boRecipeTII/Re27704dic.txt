The Judges Broiled Shrimp
olive oil, shrimp, -(up to), Lemon juice, Garlic salt, butter, 
Place shrimp in a large pan with sides about 1 inch high. Sprinkle with  garlic salt, lemon juice, butter and olive oil. Marinate in refrigerator at  least 1 hour. Bake at 300? until pink, 8 to 10 minutes. Then, broil until  oil and butter bubbles, about 4 minutes. Serve with French bread which is  delicious dipped in the sauce. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
