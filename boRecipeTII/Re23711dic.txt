Artichoke Salad with Parmigiano Reggiano
Parmigiano Reggiano; thinly, Raw artichokes; pared down, Olio Carli Extra Virgin, Lemon juice, 
Cut artichoke hearts into very thin lengthwise slices. Sprinkle the  artichoke slices with the lemon juice. Combine with the other ingredients  and toss.    Serves 4 to 6 people.    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 23, 1998
