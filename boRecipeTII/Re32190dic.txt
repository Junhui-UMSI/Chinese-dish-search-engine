Barbecued Potatoes
Pepper, Red potatoes; cut in, Salt; optional, Cooking spray, Paprika, 
1.  Spray a sheet of foil with cooking spray. Set the foil on BBQ grill.  start the grill. 2. Add potatoes to a covered glass casserole and sprinkle  with seasonings. Microwave on high for 5 minuted, stirring after 1 1/2  minutes.  Set aside. 3. When the grill is hot, arrange potatoes on foil.  Close hood and cook for 15 minutes, turning potatoes half way through  cooking time.
