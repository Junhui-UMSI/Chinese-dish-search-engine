Tasty Lamburgers
Seasoned salt, Fine dry bread crumbs, Ground lamb, Chili sauce, Beaten egg, Onion, Pepper, Toasted hamburger buns, 
Combine lamb, onion, egg, seasoned salt, pepper & crumbs; mix well.  Shape into 4 patties; broil 3-4" from heat. Turn & grill 4-5 minutes.  Serve on bun with chili sauce.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
