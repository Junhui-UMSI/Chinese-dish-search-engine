Applesauce Cookies #1
Vanilla extract, Pecan, Flour, Brown sugar, Eggs, Applesauce, Almond extract, Raisins, almonds, Oatmeal, Butter, Baking soda, Sugar, 
Cream together butter and sugars. Add almond extract, eggs, vanilla and  applesauce. Mix well. Blend in flour, oatmeal, baking soda and almonds. If  desired, add pecan pieces and/or raisins. Drop by teaspoons onto  Teflon-coated cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes. Yields        3    dozen cookies.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
