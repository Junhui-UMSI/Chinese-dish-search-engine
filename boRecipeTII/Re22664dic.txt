Apricot Date Nut Squares
(8-oz) crescent rolls, (14-oz) Eagle Brand, Pecans, Finely chopped dried, Powdered sugar, Finely chopped dates, 
Unroll the dough into 2 rectangles. Place in a greased 13-by-9-inch pan;  press over bottom and 1/2-inch up the side of the pan. Bake at 375 degrees  for 5 minutes. Cool for 3 minutes. Combine remaining ingredients except  powdered sugar and spoon over crust. Bake for 25-30 minutes. Cool. Sprinkle  with powdered sugar. Cut into bars. Makes 36.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
