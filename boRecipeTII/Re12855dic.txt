Zabaglione #6
Marsala wine, Fresh berries, Egg yolks, Sugar, Ice cream, 
Place egg yolks, wine and sugar in a copper rounded pan or double boiler  and whisk until mixture has a whipped creamy texture (about 5 to 15  minutes). Pour over ice cream and strawberries or raspberries in large wine  glasses. Yield: 4 servings.    LEX GOLDEN    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
