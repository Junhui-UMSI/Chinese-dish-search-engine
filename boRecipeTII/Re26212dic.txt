Toms Summer Veggies
Yellow squash, Taste, Onion, Salt (just a touch), Red tomatoes, Basil, oregano, pepper to, Veg stock for sauteing, Green zucchini, Optional, 
Saute thinly sliced onion until almost clear.  Add sliced squash & zucchini  when almost done add tomatoes wedges and herbs.  Stir and saute until  tomatoes are heated.    This is very simple, fast, & yummy as a side dish, or if you like serve it  over spiral pasta for a main dish (just add more stock for a sauce).    savour    From: "Kreider, Thomas R" <tkreider@smtp-gw.census.gov>.
