Beer Braised Beef in Crock Pot
Lean beef stew meat, Onions thinly sliced, Bay Leaves, Vinegar, Beer, Sugar, Thyme, Pepper, Beef bouillon, Mushrooms, Garlic, Salt, 
Preparation: Put the beef in the crock pot. Combine all the other  ingredients and pour over the beef. Cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high  for 4-5 hours. Before serving thicken juices if desired.
