Zombie
Apricot Brandy, Light Rum, Passion Fruit Syrup, 151-proof Rum, Juice of 1 small orange, Powdered sugar, Pineapple juice, Juice of 1 lime, Jamaica Rum, 
Put all ingredients except 151 with 1/2 cup of crushed ice into a blender  and blend at low speed for one minute and strain into a frosted highball  glass. Garnish with pineapple slice and a cherry. Serve with a straw.    Recipe by: Mr. Boston    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1007 by "jms@twave.net" <jms@twave.net> on  Jan 12, 1998
