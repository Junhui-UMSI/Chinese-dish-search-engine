Pork Chop Rhubarb Casserole
Cinnamon, salt and pepper, Pork Chops, Rhubarb; cut up, Cooking oil, Flour, Brown sugar, To 3 c. bread crumbs, 
In skillet, brown pork chops in oil; add salt and pepper.  Remove to  platter.  Mix 1/4 cup pan drippings with bread crumbs.  Reserve 1/2 cup.  Sprinkle remaining crumbs into 9x13 inch baking dish.  Combine rhubarb,  sugar, flour and cinnamon.  Spoon half over the bread crumbs. Arrange pork  chops on top.  Spoon remaining rhubarb mixture over chops. Cover with foil  and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Remove foil, sprinkle remaining  crumbs.  Bake 15 minutes longer.
