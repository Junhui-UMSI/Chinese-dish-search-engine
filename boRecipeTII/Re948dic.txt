Ill Be Dipped Bars
Sticks butter, Cocoa, Eggs, Sugar, Flour, Salt, Vanilla, Pecans, Chocolate chips, 
Melt butter and cocoa together. In seperate bowl, beat eggs and sugar. Add  flour and salt to egg mixture. Combine butter and cocoa and stir until  blended. fold in nuts and vanilla. Sprinkle chocolate chips evenly over  top. Bake 350* for 25 minutes in a 9x13 pan.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by boxersrock@juno.com (Sheryl Donner) on Aug  29, 1998,
