Virginia Corn Pudding
milk, Flour, Shoe-peg corn -or-, Salt, Butter, Sugar, Eggs; slightly beaten, Fresh corn, 
Drain water off corn. Mix dry ingredients first; then add remaining  ingredients. Mix well and pour into casserole. Place a pan of water under  pudding while cooking. Bake at 350 degrees for about 60 minutes. When  recipe is doubled, cook in two casseroles.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
