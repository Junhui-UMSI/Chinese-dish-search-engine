Beef and Rice Casserole
Rice (uncooked), Onion, Condensed cream of mushroom soup, Condensed chicken gumbo soup, Ground beef, Water, Celery, 
Combine ground beef, onion, and celery with a small amount of water  in a saucepan.  Boil until all moisture is absorbed.  Mix beef  mixture, rice, andmushroom soup; pour into a small greased casserole  dish. Add salt and pepper.  Bake at 350F for 25 minutes;  Microwave: Cook on Medium for 8 to 10 minutes;    Food Exchange per serving: 3 MEAT EXCHANGE + 2 STARCH/BREAD EXCHANGE  CAL: 380    Source: The Complete Diabetic Cookbook by Mary Jane Finsand  Brought to you and yours via Nancy OBrion and her Meal-Master    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/diabetic.zip
