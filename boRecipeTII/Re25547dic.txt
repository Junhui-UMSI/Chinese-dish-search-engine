Avocado Crab Dip
Lemon, avocados, scallion, Salt, Tortilla chips, Black pepper, Chili sauce, Sour cream, crabmeat, Mayonnaise, Capers, 
Mash avocado with lemon. Add crabmeat and mix gently. In a separate bowl,  combine next 6 ingredients and mix well. Fold in avocado-crab mixture.  Chill before serving with tortilla chips.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 16,  1998
