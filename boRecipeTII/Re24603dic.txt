Asparagus with Orange Sauce
Grated orange rind, Margarine, Orange juice, Honey, Flour, Fresh asparagus, 
Break off tough ends of asparagus and discard. Wash asparagus and place in  9" x 13" glass pan. Cover with vented plastic wrap. Microwave on high for 6  minutes or until tender crisp. Drain off any liquid.    In a bowl, combine flour, orange juice, orange rind and honey. Microwave on  hight 1 1/2 minutes until thick.    Stir in margarine and spoon sauce over asparagus.    Note: Sauce goes great with beets, too.    >from http://www.duban.com/craft/recipes.htm  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by KitPATh <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu> on Feb 27,  1998
