Tartar Sauce
Dill Pickle; Cut Into Pieces, Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing, Lemon juice, Chives; Snipped, Capers; Drained, Egg; Hard Cooked, Quartered, Onion, 
Put all of the ingredients into a blender container and blend until  chopped.  Chill.  Serve with fish.    Makes 2 cups.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/blend1.zip
