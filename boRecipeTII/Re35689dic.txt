Stuffed Bell Peppers
Bell peppers, Wild Quick Brown Rice, Grated cheese, Egg, Mushrooms, Diced onions, Black olives, 
Steam bell peppers until tender.  Cook contents of the box according to  package directions.  After the rice is cooked add one egg and the  vegetables to the rice.  Stuff the mixture into cooled bell peppers, and  cover with cheese.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Quick Brown Rice" tri-fold  Copyright 1987 Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
