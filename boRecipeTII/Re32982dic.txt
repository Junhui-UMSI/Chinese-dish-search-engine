Sweet-And-Pungent Sauce for Carrots
Cornstarch, Vinegar, Water, Salt, Sugar, 
1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan. Cook, stirring, over medium heat  until sauce thickens and is smooth.     2. Pour over cooked carrots and serve.     NOTE: This sauce also goes well with cooked cabbage.     VARIATIONS:     1. For the water, substitute pineapple juice.     2. In Step 1, also add 1 tablespoon soy sauce.
