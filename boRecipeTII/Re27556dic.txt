The $2 Little Meal, Caramelized Onions and Pa
Sugar, Vegetable oil, Dried basil leaves (or 3 tb., Water, Farfalle or bow-tie noodles, Black Pepper, Balsamic vinegar, Grated Parmesan cheese,, Onions, thinly sliced, Dried thyme leaves (or 3 ts., GARNISH, Salt, 
1. In large nonstick skillet heat oil. Add sliced onions and cook over high  heat until onions begin to brown, about 10 minutes. If they begin to burn a  little, thats o.k. too.    2. Lower the heat and add salt, sugar and spices. Cook 10 minutes, stirring  often, until onions are uniformly caramel-colored. Add water and vinegar  and cover. Simmer while the pasta is cooking.    3. Meanwhile, in a separate pot, bring 3 quarts of water to a boil. Cook  pasta al dente. Drain well.    4. Divide pasta equally into 4 heated bowls. Top with equal portions of  caramelized onions and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
