Andreas Coffee Cake
Baking soda, Flour, Pecans, Butter, Sugar, Margarine, Sugar, ===========================, Cinnamon, Eggs, Sour cream, Vanilla, FOR THE FILLING, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, 
Mix all ingredients for the cake together. Beat 3 minutes. Put 1/2  of the batter into a well greased tube pan. Sprinkle with half the  filling mixture. Pour remaining batter on top. Sprinkle with remaining  filling topping. Pour 3 T melted butter over the top of the cake and  bake at 350F for 45 min.
