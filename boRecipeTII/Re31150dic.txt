Banana Split Sundaes
Sweetened Condensed Milk, Ice Cream, Vanilla, Semisweet Chocolate, Light corn syrup, Unsalted, Roasted Peanuts, Small Bananas, Quartered, 
Place chocolate in a 2-cup measure.  Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power  for 2 to 3 minutes or till melted, stirring once.  Stir in corn syrup, and  sweetened condensed milk.  Micor-cook, uncovered, on 100% power for 45  seconds to 1 minute or till heated through.  Stir in vanilla.  Serve warm  atop ice cream.  Sprinkle with peanuts.  Arranged quartered bananas around  ice cream.    From Better Homes & Gardens "Microwave Cooking for One or Two".  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mwc-4-2.zip
