Zesty Three Bean Salad
Black beans; drained, Tomato; diced, Whole limes; squeezed, Red kidney beans; drained, Red onion; sliced thin, Salsa; chunky style, Chili powder, Salad oil, Garbanzo beans; drained, Salt, Stalks celery; sliced thin, 
Mix lime juice, salsa, salad oil, chili powder and salt. Mixed drained  beans and sliced vegetables. Toss the bean mixture with the sauce and  refrigerate for at least one hour. Serve at room temperature.    Recipe by: Chris Benoit    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Sue <suechef@sover.net> on Apr 27, 1998
