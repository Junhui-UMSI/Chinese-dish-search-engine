Vegetable Gumbo
Carrot, Water, Black pepper, Onion, Hot cooked rice, Flour, Zucchini, Squash, Tomatoes, Corn; frozen, 
Recipe by: Fresh Food Favorites Preparation Time: 1:00 Preheat oven at 400.  Sprinkle flour evenly into 8" square pan. Bake for for 10 to 15 minutes, or  until flour is deep golden brown, stirring every 5 minutes. Set aside. In a  skillet combine 1/4 cup water zucchini, squash, corn, carrot, and onion.  Cover and heat until zucchini is tender, stirring once. Stir in flour.  Blend in remaining water. Add tomatoes and pepper. Mix well. Re-cover and  heat until gumbo is slightly thickened and vegetables are tender. Serve  over cooked rice.    From: matejkrf@Picard.ml.wpafb.af.mil (Richard F. Matejka).
