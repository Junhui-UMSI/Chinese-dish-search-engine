Stuffed Chicken Breast in Crock Pot
Cream of celery soup, Stuffing, Boneless whole chicken, Paprika, 
Prepare dressing. If using package mix, 1/2 of package should be enough.  (May use applesauce instead of water in dressing. ) May also add 1/2 cup  cut up onion to dressing. Roll dressing up in breast and close with  toothpick. Brown in butter or margarine in hot pan. When brown, cover with  paprika and place in crock pot. Cover breasts with 2 cans cream of celery  soup. Turn on high for 5 or more hours.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 283 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 21, 1997
