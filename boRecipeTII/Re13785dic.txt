Wild Rice-Chicken Supreme
Chicken broth, Cubed; cooked chicken, Flour, Black pepper, Onions, Salt, Chopped pimiento, Chopped almonds, Half & half, Chopped parsley, Butter, 
(this recipe convinced literally a million Americans that Uncle Bens Long  Grain and Wild Rice was indeed worth the price)    Prepare contents of rice and seasoning packets according to pkg. Meanwhile,  melt butter in large saucepan. Add onion & cook over low heat til tender.  Stir in flour, salt & pepper. Gradually stir in half & half and chicken  broth. Cook, stirring constantly, til thickened. Stir in chicken, pimiento,  parsley, almonds & cooked rice. Place in 2qt casserole. Bake uncovered in  425?oven for 30min. Makes 6-8 servings.
