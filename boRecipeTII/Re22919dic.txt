Arepas Fritas De Queso O Chicharron
Grated Muenster cheese, Ground chicharrones, Basic arepa dough recipe, 
Fried Corn Cakes From Antioquia With Cheese Or Pork Rind    Knead dough with cheese or chicharrones until smooth. Shape into 4-inch  discs and deep-fry in hot oil until crispy. Drain on paper towels and serve  hot. These arepas can also be cooked on a griddle and served with butter.
