Stuffed Peppers Trinidad
Green peppers, Ground beef,lean, Butter or margarine, Water,boiling,salted, Paprika, Rice,cooked, Salt, Tomatoes(16oz), Onion,minced, Angostura aromatic bitters, 
1. Cook whole peppers in boiling water until just tender; drain and set  aside.    2. Melt butter in a skillet.    3. Add onion and beef; saute until well browned.    4. Add cooked rice, salt, paprika and Angostura bitters; mix well.    5. Cut tops off peppers and remove seeds; fill pepper cases with rice-meat  mixture.    6. Place peppers upright in a casserole; pour tomatoes around peppers.    7. Place casserole in preheated 375F. oven; bake 25 minutes. From: Michael  Orchekowski             Date: 10-23-94
