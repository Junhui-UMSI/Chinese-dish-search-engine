Asparagus Salad #1
Salt, (15-oz) asparagus; chopped, Sugar, Celery, Pecans, Unflavored gelatin; soften, Vinegar, Lemon, Jar (2-oz) pimiento; chopped, -cold water, 
Heat vinegar, salt, sugar and lemon juice. Add gelatin and stir. Add  asparagus, nuts, celery and pimiento. Pour into 1-1/2 quart mold and  refrigerate until firm. Good to serve with meat. Serves 8-10.    MRS A.B.
