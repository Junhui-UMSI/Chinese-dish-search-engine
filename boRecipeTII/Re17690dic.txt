Almond Praline Cookies
Sugar, All purpose flour, milk, Butter, almonds, 
Grind almonds in food processor until fine. In a medium skillet melt butter  over low heat. Stir in sugar and flour. Add almonds and milk. Cook and stir  over low heat until bubbly. Remove from heat. Drop batter by slightly  rounded tsp 4 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheet. Bake in 350 oven  6-7 minutes or until edges are golden brown. Remove from oven; let stand on  cookie sheet 1 minute. Carefully loosen and remove cookies with a wide  metal spatula. Cool completely. Store in an airtight container.
