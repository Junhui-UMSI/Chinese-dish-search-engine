Ye Olde Tuna Casserole
(10 1/2 oz.) mushroom or, Celery salt, (7 oz.) tuna fish, drained, (8 oz.) noodles, Worcestershire Sauce, Cornflake crumbs or buttered, Paprika and chives to taste, 
Cook noodles (as recipe above). In 1 1/2 quart glass casserole, flake tuna.  Mix with noodles, soup, celery salt, and Worcestershire sauce. Top with  crumbs; sprinkle with paprika & chives. Microwave (high), covered, 8-10  minutes, rotating dish 1/4 turn halfway through cooking. Rest 5 minutes.  Serves 4-6  Posted to rec.food.recipes by jills@VNET.IBM.COM on Sat, 10 Sep 1994.
