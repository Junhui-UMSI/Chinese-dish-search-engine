Barmbrack
Mixed fruit, Warm black tea, S.r. flour, Brown sugar; firmly packed, Egg, 
From: ynnuf@yetti.amigans.gen.nz (Doreen Randal)    Date: Tue, 23 Nov 93 16:27:19 PST    Place mixed fruit in the warm tea with the sugar, stir until dissolved. Let  cool. Add the well beaten egg to the cooled mixture, then fold in the  sifted flour, mix well. Place mixture into greased and lined 9"x5" loaf  tin. Bake in mod./slow oven approx. 2 hrs. or until cooked when tested.  Serve sliced buttered or plain.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.
