Under-The-Sea Salad
Pear halves, (16 ounce), Boiling water, Lime flavored Jell-O, (3, Cinnamon, optional, Cream cheese, (3 ounces, Lemon juice, 
Drain pears, reserving 3/4 cup syrup. Dice pears and set aside. Dissolve  gelatin in boiling water; add reserved syrup and lemon juice. Pour 1 1/4  cups into an 8 X 4" loaf pan. Chill until set but not firm, about 1 hour.  Meanwhile, soften cheese until creamy. Very slowly blending in remaining  gelatin, beating until smooth. Add cinnamon and pears and spoon into pan.  Chill until firm, about 4 hours. Unmold and garnish as desired.    Recipe by: General Foods Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #660 by "Bob &  Carole Walberg" <walbergr@mb.sympatico.ca> on Jul 6, 1997
