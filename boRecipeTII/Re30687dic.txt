Tamaqua Great Chicken
Salt, Garlic salt, Sour cream, Worcestershire Sauce, Red hot sauce, Chicken breasts, Paprika, Fine dry bread crumbs, 
Notes: By Heather Ramsey, Tamaqua. The Times News, PA    Mix together sour cream, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, garlic salt,  paprika and salt. Pierce chicken with fork and place in mixture. Leave in  fridge overnight. Take chicken out of the mixture and roll each breast in  bread crumbs. Put in large baking dish, and arrange in a single layer.  Cover dish and place in fridge for about 1 1/2 hours. Heat oven to 325?.  Uncover dish and bake for 1 hour 15 minutes.    Posted to recipelu-digest by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I Canty) on Mar 7, 1998
