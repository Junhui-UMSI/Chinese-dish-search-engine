Tater Tot Casserole
Cream of mushroom soup, Tater Tots, Cream of Chicken Soup, Sour cream, Cheddar Cheese, Ground Chuck, Onion, 
Directions: Brown ground chuck and chopped onion; season with salt and  pepper. Drain. Layer other ingredients into baking dish. Top with large bag  of Tater Tots. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until brown and heated  through.           Bob Hogan
