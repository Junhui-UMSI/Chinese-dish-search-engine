Penne with Squash, Leeks and Parmesan Cheese
Winter squash; in 1/2" dice,, Grated Parmesan cheese, Leeks; tough greens trimmed,, Coarsely cracked pepper, olive oil, Freshly grated nutmeg, Salt, Pasta, 
1. Heat to boil a large pot of lightly salted water. When boiling, add  pasta and cook according to package instructions. Three minutes before end  of cooking, add squash. Stir into pot. Add leeks for last full minute of  cooking.    2. Reserve 2-4 tablespoons cooking liquid. Drain pasta. Put in shallow  serving bowl. Toss with remaining ingredients, adding reserved liquid if  mixture is too dry. Adjust seasoning. Serve hot. Pass additional grated  Parmesan cheese.
