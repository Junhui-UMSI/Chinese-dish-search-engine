Autumn Pork Roast
Orange peel; grate, Cranberries; chop fine, Pork roast; (up To 4), Salt and pepper, Honey, Ground nutmeg, Ground cloves, 
Sprinkle roast with salt and pepper. Place in slow cooking crockpot.  Combine remaining ingredients. Pour over roast. Cover. Cook on LOW for 8 to  10 hours. MM Waldine Van Geffen vghc42a@prodigy.com.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1030 by "M. Hicks" <nitro_ii@email.msn.com>  on Jan 22, 1998
