Wilted Mustard Greens (Hl)
Mustard greens, freshly ground pepper, Shallots, Canola oil, White vinegar, 
In a medium-size saute pan, on medium heat or flame, put the oil in the  pan, add shallots and    saute quickly for 10 to 15 seconds. Add mustard greens and saute for 15  seconds. Swirl in the vinegar and season. Serve immediately. Total cooking  time is 2 minutes.    Yield: 2 servings    Recipe courtesy of Dirk Zephir, Executive Chef of Soul Cafe.
