Stuffed Baked Pork Chops
Chopped, Flour, loin pork chops, Golden raisins, Salt and pepper, Vegetable oil, Lemon Peel, Lemon juice, apple, 
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.    Combine the raisins, grated lemon peel, lemon juice and apple and season  with salt and pepper. Make a pocket in the side of each pork chop and stuff  some of the mixture inside.. Close each chop with toothpicks. Season with  salt and lightly dust with flour.    Heat the oil in an iron skillet. Add the chops and brown them brief on each  side. Season with fresh pepper. Cover skillet with foil and bake for 30 to       40    minutes.    Yield: 4 servings    COOKING MONDAY TO FRIDAY SHOW #MF6756
