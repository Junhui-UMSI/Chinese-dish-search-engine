Basil-Cheese Beer Bread
Grated Parmesan cheese, beer, Sugar, Fresh basil, NORMA WRENN, Self-rising flour, 
Mix all ingredients. Pour into well-greased loaf pan and bake in preheated  350-degree oven for 50 minutes. Remove fro pan, cool on wire rack and  slice. May be spiced up a bit with garlic.    Source: Venie Palasota, Abilene Reporter-News 9/17/94  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #805 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Sep 25, 1997
