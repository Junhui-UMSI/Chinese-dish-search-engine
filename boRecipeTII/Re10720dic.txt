Quick Stuffing Loaf
Substitute -- beaten, or, Cornmeal, Salt -- lite, optional, Sage -- or 3 t, Corn Kernels -- whole, rinse, Poultry Seasoning -- or 3 t, Bread -- whole wheat, cubed, Flour, All-Purpose, Baking Powder, Sugar -- or 2 t, or subst., Egg Beaters. 99% Egg, Subst, Butter Buds. -- liquid, Nonfat Milk, Butter Buds. -- liquid, or 3, Onion -- chopped, Celery -- chopped, Chicken broth, & drain, Oregano -- crushed, Vegetarian, 
Heat oven to 350 deg. F.  Spray a 9 x 5" loaf pan with a nonstick cooking  spray.  Cool onion and celery in  3 T liquid Butter Buds or chicken broth  until tender, about 5 min.  Stir in spices and mix well.  Remove from heat.  Add bread cubes and corn, mixing well.  Combine dry ingredients. Add  combined milk, liquid Butter Buds, and Egg Beaters, mixing just until  moistened.  Add veggie mixture.   Spread evenly into the prepared pan. Bake  45 - 50 min. or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in  pan 15 min.  Remove and cut into 3/4" slices.  Serve warm.
