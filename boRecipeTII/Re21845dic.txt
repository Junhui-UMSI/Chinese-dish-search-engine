Apple-Pecan Stuffing
Ground sage, Black pepper, Dried parsley flakes, Salt, Water, Diced apples, Pecans, Diced onion, Ground thyme, Ground marjoram, Diced day-old bread, Butter or margarine, Celery, 
Melt 1/4 cup butter in skillet. Add onion and celery and cook until tender.  Add to bread. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in skillet. Stir in apples. Cook  until golden. Add apples to bread mixture. Mix in salt, pepper, sage,  marjoram, thyme, parsley and pecans. Add water and mix thoroughly. Makes  enough for 10-pound turkey
