Swordfish with Mustard-Basil Butter
Vegetable oil, Unsalted butter, Fresh basil, swordfish, Fresh basil sprigs, Dijon mustard, 
Mix first 3 ingredients in a small bowl. Prepare barbecue or preheat  broiler.  Brush grill with vegetable oil. Season fish with salt and pepper.  Grill fish just until cooked through, about 3 minutes per side. Transfer to  plates.  Top each with spoonful of mustard-basil butter. Garnish with basil  sprigs and serve.
