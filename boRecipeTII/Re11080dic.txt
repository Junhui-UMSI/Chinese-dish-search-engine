Rainbow Trout with Grilled Vegetable Salsa
Fresh Lemon Juice, SALSA:, Green Bell Peppers, lengthwise, seeded, Eggplant (about 4 Oz.), cut in half lengthwise, Black Pitted Olives, Salt, Red Bell Pepper, Green onions, olive oil, olive oil, MARINADE:, peppers cut in half, Fresh Lemon Juice, Rainbow Trout Fillets, Black Pepper, 
Marinate trout. Meanwhile, brush vegetables with oil and cook over grill  until tender, about 10 minutes. Cool slightly and finely chop vevetables  and olives. Toss vegetables with oil and remaining vegetables, set aside.  Cook trout. Serve immediately with salsa.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mm-trout.zip
