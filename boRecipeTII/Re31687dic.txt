Swiss Steak with Stewed Tomatoes
Stewed tomatoes, Pepper, Onion -- sliced, Salt, Shortening, Flour, Round or chuck steak, 
Preheat to 325. Trim fat from meat, and cut into serving pieces.Combine  flour ,salt, and pepper. Sprinkle half the flour into 1 side of the meat,  pound in with edge of saucer. Turn meat and repeat on other side. Heat  shortening, brown meat on both sides. Add to a 13x9 baking pan. Add  tomatoes and onions. Cover with foil. Bake for 2 hours.
