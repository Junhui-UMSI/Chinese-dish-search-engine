Chinese Restaurant Spiced Rice
rice, uncooked, Corn oil, Onion, garlic, Scallion, green pepper, Water, Chicken Stock, Dried marjoram, Dried tarragon leaves, parsley, dill, cayenne pepper, Ground ginger, 
Bring water and stock to boil in a heavy bottomed saucepan. Add rice. Bring  to boil again. Reduce heat and cook, partially covered, for 12-15 minutes  or until all water is absorbed and rice is tender but firm.    Heat oil in iron skillet until hot. Add onion, garlic, scallion and pepper  and saute until lightly browned, turning frequently, 5 to 7 minutes. Add  rice to skillet and turn to coat with sauteed mixture. Sprinkle with herbs,  spices and seasoning.  Stir. Cover tightly and place in preheated 450  degree oven for 5 minutes. Serve immediately.
