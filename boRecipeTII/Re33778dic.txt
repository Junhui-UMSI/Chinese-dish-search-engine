Sweet and Sour Garlic Vegetables
Salt, Fresh vegetables, Vegetable oil, fresh garlic, White vinegar, Sugar, 
Pare, trim and slice vegetables.  Combine vinegar, sugar and salt in large  jar.  Add vegetables and shake to mix will. Cover and refrigerate  overnight, shaking occasionally. In another small jar, mix oil and garlic.  When ready to serve, drain vegetables and place on a serving platter. Pour  garlic oil over and serve with toothpicks. Makes about 4 cups.
