Quich Lorraine
Dry mustard, Onion, evaporated milk, Swiss cheese, Butter, Unbaked pie shell, Bacon, Eggs, Pepper, Salt, 
Brown bacon; remove and crumble. Saute onion in bacon grease. Beat together  eggs, milk and seasonings. Put bacon in bottom of pie shell. Put onions on  top of bacon and then a layer of Swiss cheese. Pour milk mixture over all  and dot with butter. Bake at 375* for 35-40 min. Let stand 15-20 min.  before serving.
