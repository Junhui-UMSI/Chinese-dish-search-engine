Puff Balls
Cheddar cheese; grated, milk, Bulk breakfast sausage;, Bisquick mix, 
Combine Bisquick and milk, and mix well. Add sausage pieces and cheese to  Bisquick mixture, and mix well. Pinch off dough to make about 1-inch balls.  Place on baking pan. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees F.    NOTE: They can be frozen. Cook an additional 5 minutes longer.
