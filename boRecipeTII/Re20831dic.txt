Apple Jello Salad
(Small) Red Jello; <OR>, Red Cinnamon Candy Drops, Boiling water, Pecans, (Small) Combination Flavor, Cold water, Grated Apples; unpeeled, 
Dissolve 3/4 cup red cinnamon candy drops in the boiling water. Add jello.  When all is dissolved and 2 1/4 cups cold water. Stir in 4 1/2 cups grated  apples and chopped pecans. Pour in mold or 9 x 13 pan. Chill till set.    Serve on lettuce leaf if desired.
