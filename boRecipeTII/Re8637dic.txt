Potluck Potatoes
Sour cream, Cream of chicken, Sharp cheddar cheese, Salt, Cream of mushroom soup, Onions, Pepper, Frozen hash browns, Butter, 
Topping: 2 cups corn flakes -- crushed 1/4 cup butter -- melted    Mix ingredients together, except topping, in a 9x13 inch pan.  In a small  bowl, mix the topping together and sprinkle over the top of potato mixture.  Bake at 350 F for 45 minutes.
