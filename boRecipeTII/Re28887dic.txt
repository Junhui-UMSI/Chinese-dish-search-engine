Thai Iced Tea #5
Half and half or sweetened, Water, Sugar, Thai tea, Ice cubes, 
Bring water to boil. Add tea and steep 5 minutes. Strain and season to  taste with sugar. Cool, then chill in refrigerator. When ready to serve,  place ice cubes in each of 6 tall glasses. Pour tea over ice, leaving room  to add half and half to taste. Created by: Krung Tep Thai, Tarzana, Calif.
