1-2-3 Vegetable Chili
Tomatoes; undrained, Salsa, Sliced zucchini halves, Chili powder, Black beans; rinsed, drained, Frozen corn, KRAFT Shredded Mild Reduced, 
BRING tomatoes, salsa, beans, corn, zucchini and chili powder to boil in  saucepan on medium-high heat.    REDUCE heat to low; simmer 10 minutes.    SPRINKLE 2 tablespoons cheese in bottom of each serving bowl. Top with  chili and additional 2 tablespoons cheese. Garnish with hot red pepper  slices.
