Broken Bow Scalloped Potatoes
Potato, Onion, Flour, Salt, Pepper, Butter, milk, Paprika, 
Oven Temp: 325F / 170C / Gas mark 3  Arrange a layer of potatoes in a greased 2-qt casserole. Cover with some of  the onion.  Sprinkle some of the combined flour, salt and pepper. Dot with  some of the butter.  Repeat layers until all are used, ending with butter.  Pour milk over all.  Sprinkle with paprika. Bake, covered for 1 hour. Uncover and bake 20 minutes longer or until potatoes  are tender.
