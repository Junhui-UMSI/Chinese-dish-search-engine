Yellow Party Cake
Flour, Lemon Filling, Sugar, milk, Salt, Seven Minute Frosting, Baking Powder, Vanilla, Eggs, butter, 
In mixer bowl cream together sugar & butter till light & fluffy  (about 8-10 minutes at medium speed of electric mixer), scraping bowl  frequently. Add eggs & vanilla, beat at medium speed about 2 minutes  or until mixture is light & fluffy. Scrape bowl frequently. Stir  together flour, powder & 1 tsp. salt. Add to creamed mixture  alternately with milk, beating at low speed after each addition. Beat  1 minute at end. Turn batter into two greased & lightly floured 9"  round baking pans. Bake in 350 oven for 30-35 minutes or until cake  tests done. Cool 10 minutes. Remove from pans & cool thoroughly on  wire racks. Fill with Lemon Filling; frost with Seven Minute  Frosting. Sprinkle shredded coconut on sides of cake, if desired.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
