Peas and Artichoke Hearts Saute
(14-oz) artichoke hearts;, Fennel seed, Salt to taste, Black Pepper, olive oil, Garlic, (16-oz) green peas; drained, Chopped parsley, 
saute garlic in olive oil until softened. Add parsley, fennel seed, salt  and pepper. Add artichoke hearts and saute for 5 minutes. Add peas and  simmer until heated through and flavors have blended. Yield: 4 to 6  servings.    JULIE TRUEMPER    (MRS. JOHN J., JR.)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
