Zucchini and Pepper Slaw
Green onions, Cayenne pepper, Banana pepper; slivered, Garlic clove; chopped, Yellow zucchini, Wine vinegar, Fresh ground black pepper, Honey, DRESSING, olive oil, Salt, Green zucchini, Dijon mustard, 
1. With shredder blade of food processor, shred zucchini into long strips  or cut by hand. Reserve in bowl. Sliver red pepper and green onions and add  to zucchini. Add optional banana pepper for a spicy flavour.    2. In a small bowl whisk together garlic, mustard and honey. Whisk in wine  vinegar and olive oil. Mixture should be quite thick. Season with salt and  pepper.    3. Just before serving, toss vegetables with vinaigrette.
