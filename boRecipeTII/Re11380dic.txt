Range Chili
Kielbasa sausage; casing, Salt, Ground beef, Diced tomatoes, Pinto beans; drained, Onion, Chili seasoning mix, Red wine vinegar, Garlic, Pepper, 
In a soup pot, combine the sausage, ground beef, onion, garlic, salt, and  pepper and cook over medium-high heat for 7 to 9 minutes, or until no pink  remains in the meat, stirring frequently. Add the vinegar, tomatoes, beans,  and seasoning mix to the meat mixture and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat  to low and simmer for 30 minutes; serve.    NOTE: Use whatever topppers you like or try some of these: shredded cheese  or chopped onions and peppers, sour cream, tortilla chips or bread.
