Bbq Dry Rub
Chile, ground, Salt, Onion powder, Cumin, powder, Black Pepper, Thyme, leaves, dried, Curry powder, Garlic powder, Allspice, Paprika, hungarian, Coriander, 
Mix all ingredients.  Rub on meat and refrigerate the night before smoking.    Comment: Consider halving the chile for a  milder rub.
