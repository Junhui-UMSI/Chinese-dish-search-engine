Pumpkin Seed Salsa
Tomato, Oregano, Toasted, White Onions, Garlic, Water, Pumpkin Seeds, Guero Chiles, Salt, 
Char chiles until blistered and black, then seed and devein.    Thickly slice onion and pan-roast until brown and soft.    Pan-roast garlic until brown and soft, then peel.    Pan-roast tomato until blistered, black, and soft.    Toast pumpkin seeds in 1 tbsp. olive oil until puffed and brown    In a blender or food processor, puree ingredients together until a chunky  salsa is formed. NOTES  : Is supposed to be great when served with broiled fresh goat cheese and  baby greens.
