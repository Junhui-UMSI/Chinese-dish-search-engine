Pecan Pie #04
Brown sugar, White Karo syrup, Pecans, Eggs, Vanilla, Butter, Unbaked pie shell, 
Cream butter and sugar; add Karo syrup and eggs. Add vanilla; mix well. Add  pecans; stir. Pour into an unbaked pie shell. Bake at 325 degrees for 1  hour or until firm. Serve cold.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
