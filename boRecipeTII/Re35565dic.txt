Streusel Cream Cheese Cake
Salt, Sugar, Sugar, Flour, Eggs, Flour, Egg, Vanilla, BATTER, CHEESE FILLING, Baking Powder, Margarine, Sugar, cream cheese, STREUSEL TOPPING, Margarine, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. For batter, cream margarine and sugar. Add  eggs and beat well. Add dry ingredients. Stir to combine. Spread 2/3 of  batter into flat bottom of springform pan. Blend cheese filling  ingredients. Spread evenly over batter. Spread remaining batter on top. Mix  the streusel topping to a coarse crumb texture. Sprinkle the streusel on  top. Bake for 45 minutes.    Recipe by: Pampered Chef Favorites Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #681 by  hister@juno.com (Iris E. Dunaway) on Jul 20, 1997
