Apple Nut Ring
Cinnamon, Raisins, Biscuits, Sugar, Butter or margarine --, Walnuts -- chopped, Apples, Refrigerated buttermilk, Melted, 
Preheat oven to 400.  Separate biscuits into 20 pieces.  Combine sugar and  cinnamon.  Dip biscuits into melted butter, then roll in sugar mixture.  Arrange biscuits in Deep Dish Baker.  Peel, core and slice apples into thin  slices using the Apple Peeler/ Corer/Slicer. Cut slices in half. Place an  apple slice between each biscuit and around outer edge of Deep Dish Baker.  Chop nuts with  Food Chopper. Mix nuts and raisins with remaining sugar  mixture and sprinkle over apples. Bake 25-30 minutes or un til golden  brown.
