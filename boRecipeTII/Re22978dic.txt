Armenian Rice Pilaf
Boiling hot chicken broth, Butter or margarine, Uncooked long-grain rice, MSG (optional), Vermicelli, Salt, 
Melt butter in heavy pan or Dutch oven. Break vermicelli in small pieces,  add to pan and cook until golden brown, stirring constantly. Add rice and  stir until rice is well coated with butter. Add boiling broth and MSG and  season to taste with salt. Cook, covered, over low heat until liquid is  absorbed, about 25 minutes. Stir lightly with fork. Let stand in warm place  15 to 20 minutes before serving.
