All Canadian Coffee
Whipping cream, Coffee; hot, black,, Maple syrup; pure, Maple syrup; pure, Rye whiskey, TOPPING, 
Topping: Whip cream with maple syrup just up until soft mounds; set aside.    Divide maple syrup and whiskey among 4 warmed heat proof glass mugs or  goblets. Pour in coffee to 1 inch of top; spoon topping over coffee.  Decorate with tiny Canadian flags if desired. MAKES: 4 Servings
