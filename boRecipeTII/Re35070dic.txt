Beans with Vinaigrette
Dijon mustard, olive oil, Salt, Red wine vinegar, Pepper, Fresh green beans, Garlic powder, Sugar, 
Steam green beans until crisp-tender. Mix remaining ingredients in jar with  tight-fitting lid; shake well. Drain beans; pour dressing over beans and  toss well.    NOTES : Carrot strips or red bell pepper strips steamed with the beans make  a colorful addition.
