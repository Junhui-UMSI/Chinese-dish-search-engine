Raspberry Bran Muffins
Flour, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Raspberry jam, Salt, Eggs, Bran flakes cereal, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Buttermilk, 
Preheat oven to 350F.  Line muffin tins with paper muffin cups. Combine  first 5 ingredients in medium bowl. Whisk buttermilk, oil and eggs in large  bowl to blend. Add dry ingredients and cereal and stir until just blended.  Spoon 1/4 cup batter into each muffin cup. Using small spoon,make well in  center of each and fill well with 1 tsp jam. Spoon remaining batter over.  bake until tester inserted into centers comes out clean, about 25 minutes.  Turn muffins out onto racks. Cool slightly. Serve warm or at room  temperature.
