Antipasto Hero
Sliced fully cooked ham, Pepperoncini peppers, (bottled Italian peppers),, Caesar dressing, Round focaccia bread, Or 5 leaves leaf lettuce, Sliced provolone cheese, (8 or 9 inches in dia.), Sliced salami, 
Cut bread horizontally in half.  Drizzle dressing evenly over cut sides of  bread.    Layer lettuce, cheese, ham, salami and peppers on bottom half. Top with to  half.  Secure loaf with toothpicks or small skewers. Cut into 6 wedges.  Makes 6 servings.
