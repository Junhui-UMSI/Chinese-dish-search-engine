Peppered Cajun Chicken
Course ground black pepper, Boneless; skinless chicken, Salt, Fresh or frozen sliced okra, Onion chopped, Cajun seasoning (see note), Stalks ceery; chopped, 
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Season each side of chicken breasts with  cajun seasoning and plenty of black pepper. Brownchicken breasts in skillet  sprayed with non-stick cooking spray. Cook just long enough to brown on  each side. Remove chicken breasts from skillet and brown onions and celery  in same skillet. Spray 9"x9" baking dish with non-stick cooking spray. Line  bottom of dish with browned onion and celery. Add okra on top of onions and  celery. Add salt and a little more cajun seasoning. Place browned chicken  breasts on top and cover and bake at 325 degrees for 30-35 minutes or until  chicken breasts are tender.  Entire recipe makes 4 servings.     NOTE: There are several different cajun seasonings on the market. All are  somewhat similar in taste. Any brand may be used.    ULTRA LOW FAT    FROM SOME COOKBOOK!
