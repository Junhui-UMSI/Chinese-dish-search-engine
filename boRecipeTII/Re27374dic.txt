Toasted Garbanzo Patties
AM Oat Flakes, Parsley, Sea salt (optional), Water, Ground pecans or other nuts, Onion; chopped finely, AM Toasted Garbanzo Flour, Savory, 
Mix the flour and water.  Let sit while preparing remaining ingredients.  Mix all well.  When mixture is quite thick shape into patties.  Fry  lightly on both sides.  Flatten patties fairly thin while cooking.  Delicious when fried in toasted sesame oil.
