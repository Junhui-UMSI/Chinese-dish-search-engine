Peanuts, Spiced
Roasted peanuts -- skins on, Water, Granulated sugar, Cloves, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, 
Boil sugar, water, and spices until syrup reaches hard crack stage (293).  Drop peanuts into syrup.  Stir until nuts are dry looking.  Pour out on to  waxed paper and let stand until cool and dry.  Makes 3 1/2 cups.    Recipe By     : Anita A. Matejka    From:                                 Date: 05/28    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
