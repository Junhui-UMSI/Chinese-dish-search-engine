Whole Wheat Grain Oat Bread
Rolled oats, Whole wheat flour, 7-grain cereal, Salt, Water, Molasses, Butter or margarine, Gluten, Yeast, Sugar, Nonfat dried milk powder, White flour, 
Place ingredients in bread machine in order given (or in reverse order if  your machine takes the dry ingredients first).    To make in bread machine: process on whole wheat cycle.    To bake in oven: process on dough cycle.  Remove dough, punch down and  shape into loaf.  Place in greased bread loaf pan and let rise until  doubled, about one hour.  Bake in 400 degree F oven for 20 minutes. Remove  loaf to cool.    Note: Instead of using 7 grain cereal, you can use 8 grain, 9 grain, 12  grain, etc. -- whatever is available where you shop.  You can usually find  this stuff in grocery stores where they have bins with grains, flours, etc.    Option: Add 1/4 sunflower seeds.
