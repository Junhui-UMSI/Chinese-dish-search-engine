Pickled Mangos
Vinegar, Chinese 5-spice, Sugar, Red food color if desired, Water, Hawaiian salt, 
Combine above ingredients and bring to boil, cool, Peel green mangoes and  cut into long slices. Dont add salt or water to them. Fill sterilized jar  with slices. Pour cool syrup over them. Add more slices after 30 minutes.  Cover jar. Leave it out for 2-3 days & refrigerate.
