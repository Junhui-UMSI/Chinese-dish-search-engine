Traditional Hopping John
Salt & pepper, Louisiana Hot Sauce, Red bell pepper, Onion, Water, Black-eyed peas, fresh, white rice, uncooked, Bacon, Bell pepper, frozen black-eyed peas, 
Dice bacon. brown in dutch oven with onion and pepeprs, until bacon is  crisp and vegetables are soft. Add peas and rice. then water. Cover and  simmer over very low heat about 20 minutes, until the rice is tender. Salt  & pepper to taste.  Add a dash of hot sauce (to taste).    This recipe came to America from Africa though the slave trade. It is now  the traditional dish served by most Southerners on New Years Day. It is  reported to bring good luck.
