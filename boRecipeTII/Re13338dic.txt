Yam Bake
Sugar, Butter, Eggs; slightly beaten, Vanilla flavoring, Miniature marshmallows, (3.5-oz) angel flake coconut, Louisiana yams, Whole milk or half-and-half, 
Peel, dice and cook yams until tender; while hot, drain. Add butter, sugar,  milk, eggs and flavoring. Beat well. Fold in coconut. Pour half into a  greased baking dish. Cover with marshmallows. Pour in another layer of yam  mixture and top with marshmallows. Cover so marshmallows dont burn. Bake  at 375 degrees for 45 minutes.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
