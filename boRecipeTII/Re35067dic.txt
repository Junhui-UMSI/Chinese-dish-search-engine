Beans with Thyme and Cashew Nuts
Green beans, Cashew nuts, Chopped thyme, Garlic clove, chopped, Sunflower oil, 
Slice beans.  Boil them in lightly salted water until they are just tender.  Drain them.    Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.  Put in the cashew nuts & stir  until they are golden brown. Remove from the skillet. Increase the heat.  Put in the beans & garlic. Stir until the garlic begins to brown. Mix in  the nuts & thyme & remove the pan from the heat.    Gail Duff, "A Book of Herbs & Spices"
