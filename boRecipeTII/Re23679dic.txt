Artichoke Hummus
Lemons, Cumin, Kosher salt, garbanzo beans, White pepper, Paprika, Virgin olive oil, Artichoke hearts, Garlic, 
Juice the lemons.  Combine all ingredients but the oil in the bowl of a  food processor, turn on, and slowly drizzle in olive oil as the ingredients  are being processed to a creamy consistency.
