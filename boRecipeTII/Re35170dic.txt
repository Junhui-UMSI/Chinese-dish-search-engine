Beef a la Brigitte
salt and pepper, Hot water, Mustard, Cooked, cubed roast beef, Stick of margarine, Flour, 
In a microwave bowl, melt margarine; add flour, hot water and milk,  and stir, microwaving on HIGH for 1 minute at a time after each  addition. Stir well after each minute until the gravy is nice and  smooth. Add the mustard and salt and pepper to taste. Heat through  well, about 3 minutes on HIGH. Add beef and microwave until warm.  Serve with mashed potatoes or noodles or rice and a vegetable.
