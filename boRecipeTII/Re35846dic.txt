Stuffed Goose
Goose (9 To 10 Lbs.);, Cooking apples; peeled and, Onion, Dried Whole Rosemary, Orange slices (optional), Egg, Rice; cooked, Pepper, Fresh parsley (optional), Raisins, Salt, 
Oven 350? F.    Remove giblets and neck from goose; reserve for other uses.  Rinse goose  thoroughly with water; pat dry.  Prick skin with a fork at 2-inch  intervals.    Combine rice and next 7 ingredients, stirring well.  Spoon mixture into  cavity; close with skewers.  Truss goose, and place, breast side up, on  rack in a roasting pan. Insert meat thermometer in thigh, making sure it  does not touch bone. Bake, uncovered, at 350? F for 2 to 2-1/2 hours or  until meat thermometer registers 185 degrees. Place goose on a serving  platter; garnish with parsley and orange slices, if desired.    Yield: 5 to 7 servings.
