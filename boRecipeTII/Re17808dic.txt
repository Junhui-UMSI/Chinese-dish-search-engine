Almost Fatfree Chocolate Cupcakes
Egg white, Baking Powder, Applesauce; cinnamon, Unsweetened cocoa powder, Salt, baking soda, Skim milk, All-purpose flour, Vegetable oil, Egg, Dark brown sugar, Vanilla extract, 
Preheat oven to 350 deg F. Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners. Combine  flour, cocoa, baking powder, soda and salt. Beat the next six ingredients  until combined, about 1 min. Stir in flour mixture alternately with milk  until just combined. Pour batter into muffin cups. Bake 22 - 25 minutes or  until toothpick comes out clean. Remove cupcakes from pan; cool on wire  rack 30 min. Sprinkle with sugar, if desired.
