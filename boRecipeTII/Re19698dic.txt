Antipasto Appetizer
Italian dressing mix, pimientos, stuffed green, Green bell pepper, DRESSING, Onion salt, Sugar, dry onion, Pepper, Onion, Vegetable oil, Garlic salt, Salt, artichoke hearts  , Fresh broccoli flowerets, Vinegar, celery, water chestnuts, button mushrooms  , Fresh cauliflower flowerets, Accent, 
Combine vegetables and set aside. Combine remaining ingredients in saucepan  and bring to a boil. Pour over vegetables. Refrigerate for 24 hours. Serve  with crackers. Keeps for weeks. Use a food processor for a different  texture.    MUST BE PREPARED 1 DAY IN    ADVANCE!    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
