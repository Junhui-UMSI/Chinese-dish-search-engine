Tropical Double Fudge Cake
Powdered sugar, Instant vanilla pudding, Raspberries, Raspberries; pureed, SAUCE, Kiwis; sliced, Light sour cream, Semi-sweet chocolate chips;, Mango; chopped, 
Make the batter for the devils food cake as directed  and mix in the melted chocolate, light sour cream,  vanilla pudding, pureed raspberries. Bake as directed.  Sauce: Cook the mango and raspberries until smooth  with sugar to taste (you can puree them). Garnish with  kiwis.
