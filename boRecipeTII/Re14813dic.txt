123 Meatloaf
HAMBURGER, Evaporated milk, ONION SOUP MIX, 
Mix all ingredients together and put in loaf pan.  Bake at 350 for about 1  hour or until done.  Top with tomato sauce or ketchup.    Variation:  Put cheese and/or mushrooms in middle of meatloaf.
