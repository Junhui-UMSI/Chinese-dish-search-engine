Beef Casserole
Mild Cheddar cheese;, Chopped black olives, Clove garlic; minced or, Size onion; chopped fine, Uncooked Minute rice, Green pepper; chopped fine, Lean ground beef, Tabasco sauce, Kidney beans; 16 oz, Water, Salt, Tomatoes; 16 oz chopped, 
Place ground beef in a 2 quart casserole. Microwave at high (100%) 8 to 9  minutes, stirring to break up beef after half the cooking time. Add garlic,  salt, onion, green pepper and Tabasco sauce. Microwave at high (100%) 3 to  4 minutes. Add undrained tomatoes and undrained kidney beans and rice. Stir  well. Cover. Microwave at high (100%) 1 5 to 20 minutes, stirring after  half the cooking time. Sprinkle chopped olives and shredded cheese over  all. Microwave at high (100%) until cheese is melted, 4 to 5 minutes. Let  stand 5 minutes. Makes 8 servings.
