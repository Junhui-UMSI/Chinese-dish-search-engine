Apricot Butter
apricots, Orange zest, Water, Orange juice, Sugar, 
Add water to apricots and cook until soft.  Press through sieve (or cheat  like I do and run em through the blender).  Measure and to each cup pulp  add 2/3 c sugar. Add juice and grated orange rind. Cook until thick (about  10-15 minutes). Pour into sterilized pint jars to within 1/2 inch of top.  Put on cap, screw band firmly tight and process in boiling water bath ten  minutes.
