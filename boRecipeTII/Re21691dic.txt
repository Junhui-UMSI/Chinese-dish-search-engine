Apple Stuffed Squash
Acorn squash, Butter, Salt, Ground cinnamon, White rice, Unsweetened applesauce, Celery, Toasted pecans, Brown sugar, Onion powder, Ground ginger, Apple wedges, 
Prep Time: 10 minutes Bake Time: 1 hour Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes Oven Temp: 350F / 180C / Gas Mark 4  Preheat oven.  Place squash cut-side down in shallow baking pan.  Bake for 30  minutes.  Turn squash cut-side up; brush with butter.  Sprinkle with salt  and cinnamon.  Combine rice, applesauce, celery, pecans, brown sugar,  onion powder, and ginger.  Fill squash evenly with rice stuffing.  Bake,  uncovered, 20 to 30 minutes.  Garnish with apple wedges.
