Banana-Chip Muffins
milk, Cooking oil, Semisweet chocolate pieces, Sugar, Flour, Chopped walnut; ( optional), Salt, Egg, Baking Powder, Mashed banana; ( about 2, 
Grease 16 Muffin cups; set aside. Preheat oven to 400. In a large bowl,  combine flour,sugar, b.powder,and salt. Add oil, milk, and bananas: stir  until just combined. Stir in chocolate pieces and walnuts. Spoon into  prepared muffin pan, filling each 2/3 full. Bake 20 to 25 minutes. Serve  warm.    Almost like cake!
