Fox Hunters Pie
15 oz. pkge. refrigerated, Butter or margarine, Sugar, Eggs, Light corn syrup, Vanilla extract, Salt, Bourbon (optional), 6 oz. semisweet chocolate, Coarsely chopped pecans, 
Fit piecrust into a 9-inch pieplate according to package directions; fold  edges under, and crimp. Freeze 15 minutes.    Bake piecrust at 425? for 6 to 8 minutes or until golden; cool on a wire  rack.    Beat butter at medium speed with an electric mixer until creamy; gradually  add sugar, beating well. Add eggs, next 3 ingredients, and, if desired,  bourbon; beat just until blended.    Sprinkle chocolate morsels and pecans in prepared piecrust; pour in  filling.    Bake at 350? for 45 to 50 minutes or until set; cool on a wire rack. Yield:  1 (9-inch) pie.
