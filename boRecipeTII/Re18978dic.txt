Angel Torte
Sour cream, Lemon juice, Vanilla, Cherries (garnish); optional, Whipped cream (garnish);, Ground nutmeg, Granulated sugar, Envelope unflavored gelatin, Egg whites, Salt, Butter; (3/4 stick) melted, Vanilla wafers; crushed, (2-ounce) whipped dessert, 
Combine vanilla wafer crumbs, melted butter and 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg; mix  thoroughly. Press into bottom of 9-inch springform pan. In saucepan,  combine gelatin, sugar and salt with 1 1/4 cups water. Cook and stir until  gelatin dissolves. Remove from heat. Chill in refrigerator until partly  set. Add egg whites, lemon juice and vanilla. Beat until very fluffy, about  5 minutes. Prepare topping mix according to package directions; fold into  gelatin mixture, along with sour cream. Pile mixture into prepared crust.  Sprinkle with additional nutmeg. Garnish with whipped cream and cherries.  Chill until firm. Yield: 12 servings.
