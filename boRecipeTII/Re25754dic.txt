Babes Pecan Pie
Dark corn syrup, Pie shell; unbaked, Eggs, Vanilla, Butter, Sugar, Pecans; coarsely chopped, 
Combine sugar and corn syrup in a small saucepan; bring to a boil. Beat  eggs at medium speed til blended. Gradually add hot sugar mixture to eggs,  beating constantly. Stir in butter, vanilla and pecans. Pour mixture into  pie shell; bake at 350 for 40 min.
