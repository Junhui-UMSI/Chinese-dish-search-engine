Banana Boat
Raisins (optional), Mini marshmallows, Chocolate chips, Banana, 
Partially peel banana.  Cut wedge-shaped section in banana. Remove wedge.  Place in hollow:  marshmallows, chocolate, and raisins. Cover mixture with  banana peel and wrap in foil. Place in coals for about 5 minutes, until  chocolate and marshmallows are melted.
