Banana Orange Muffins
Corn syrup, Vegetable oil, Baking Powder, Flour, Vanilla, Egg whites, Cinnamon, Raisins, Sugar, Orange zest, Baking soda, Salt, 1% low-fat milk, Bananas, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease muffin tins. Using a whisk combine all  wet ingredients including orange peel. In a separate bowl combine dry  ingredients, mixing well. Add dry ingredients to egg mixture stirring just  until combined. Fill muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake for about 20 mins. or until  done.
