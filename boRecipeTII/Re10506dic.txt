Quick Glazed Meat Loaf
Ground pork, Chili powder, Ground beef, (Kikkoman), Teriyaki Baste & Glaze, Bread; cubed, Teriyaki Baste & Glaze, Eggs, (Kikkoman), Onion, 
Combine eggs and 2/3 cup teriyaki baste & glaze in large bowl; stir in  bread cubes.  Let stand 5 minutes, or until cubes are soft.  Mix in ground  beef and pork, onion and chili powder until well blended.  Press firmly  into 10-inch microwave-safe pie pan.  Microwave on High 20 minutes; rotate  pan once. Brush top of meat loaf with remaining teriyaki baste & glaze;  microwave on High 5 minutes longer.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
