Barbecue Sauce Au Justin
Wine vinegar, olive oil, Bell pepper, Ketchup, Steak sauce, Celery seed, Salt, Lemon juice, Onion, Parsley, dried, Louisiana hot sauce, Garlic, Dry Red Wine, 
Saute onions and bell pepper in olive oil.  Add garlic, wine  and the rest of the ingredients.  Bring to a boil.  Cover,  then cook over a low fire for at least 2 hours.  Use on finished  barbecue, NOT AS A BASTING SAUCE.  This is garontee to warm the belly.
