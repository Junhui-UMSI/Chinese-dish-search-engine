Company Oatmeal
Water, Raisins, Salt, Old Fashioned Quaker Oats;, Apple juice, 
Spray rice cooker container with nonstick cooking spray. Add ingredients,  stir and cover rice cooker. Turn on and cook until rice cooker turns off.  Stir once during cooking. After rice cooker shuts off steam 10 minutes  before serving.    (Approximately 35 minutes if your cooker uses a timer rather than an  automatic shut off.)
