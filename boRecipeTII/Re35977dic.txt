Stuffed Pizza
Ground chuck, Water, Bisquick baking mixd, Your favorite toppings, Jar pizza sauce; (15 oz.), Shredded mozzarella cheese, Italian sausage, 
Directions : grease a rectangular baking dish, 13 x 9 x 2. cook and stir  sausage and ground chuck until brown. drain, set aside. combine baking mix  and water until dough forms. divide into 2 parts. one slightly larger. on  surface dusted with baking mix. roll larger portion of dough into rectangle  16 x 14. fold into thirds. place in dish. unfold. press on bottom and up  sides of dish. sprinkle with 1 cup of the cheese, cover with 3/4 cup of the  pizza sauce. add meat mixture and other toppings. cover with 1 1/2 cups of  cheese. roll remaining dough into rectangle 13 x 9. fold into thirds. place  over cheese in dish. unfold. press bottom and top crust edges together to  seal. make small slits in top crust. spread with remaining pizza sauce and  cheese. bake 22 to 25 min. or until crust is golden.
