Pineapple Carob Cashew Brownies
Baking Powder, Salt, Canned pineapple pieces, (Unsweetened), Vanilla extract, Carob powder, Honey or apple juice, Raw cashew pieces, Unsweetened applesauce, Whole wheat pastry flour, 
Preheat oven to 350 F.  In a large mixing bowl combine flour, carob,  baking powder and salt.    Blend honey, applesauce, juices, pineapple and vanilla.    Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and stir in nuts.  Pour batter into an  oiled 9" x 13" pan.  Bake 1 hour or until brownies pull away from sides of  pan.
