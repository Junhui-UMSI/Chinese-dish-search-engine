Tostado Casserole
Corn chips, Taco seasoning mix, (15-oz) refried beans, Lean ground beef, (15-oz) tomato sauce, Shredded cheese, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Brown ground beef in a skillet, drain off  fat. Add 1-1/2 cups tomato sauce to the taco seasoning; mix well. Line  bottom of a 2 quart, flat casserole with two cups corn chips. Crush  remaining corn chips; set aside. Spoon meat mixture over corn chips in  dish. Combine remaining tomato sauce and the beans. Spread over the meat.  Bake, covered, for 25 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese and crushed corn chips.  Return to oven and bake 5 minutes more (uncovered). Serves 6.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
