Pears with Tapioca
Water, Salt, Sugar, Tapioca, Lemon juice, Dried pears, Marshmallows, Boiling water, 
Combine tapioca, salt, and boiling water.  Cook over hot water until  tapioca is clear.  Wash pears.  Remove cores. Steam until tender. Place  pears in saucepan. Add sugar and 1/4 cup water. Cover. Simmer 5 minutes.  Combine with tapioca and lemon juice. Pour into buttered baking dish. Cover  with marshmallows.  Bake in moderate oven (375 F) 20 minutes. 6 servings.
