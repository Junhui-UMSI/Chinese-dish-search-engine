Winning Stuffing Meat Loaf
Ground beef, Stuffing mix; any flavor, Ketchup, Egg, milk, 
Preheat the oven to 350F. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef,  stuffing mix, egg, and milk; mix well. Place in a 9" x 5" loaf pan that has  been coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake for 45 minutes then pour off  any excess liquid. Spread the ketchup evenly over the top and bake for 10  more minutes, or until no pink remains in the meat.
