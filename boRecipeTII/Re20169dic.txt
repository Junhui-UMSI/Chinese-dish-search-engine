Vanilla Wafer Dessert
Salt, Heavy cream, Orange juice, Marshmallows, Vanilla wafer cookies, Unsweetened pineapple juice, 
Combine fruit juices and salt; bring to a boil. Add marshmallows and stir  until melted. Chill until slightly thickened. Fold in whipped cream.  Alternate layers of marshmallow mixture and wafers in refrigerator tray,  using wafers for bottom and top layers. Chill until firm. Cut in squares.
