Pepper Steak Au Rhum
Butter, 151 proof rum, Sirloin (cut 1 1/2" thick), Pepper, 
Rub pepper into both sides of meat; let stand several hours. Heat  butter in skillet & cook 8 minutes on each side for rare. Add rum;  set aflame. When flame dies out (because of butter flame will be  high), spoon sauce over steak, scraping off pan drippings. Carve at a       45    degree angle in thin slices.
