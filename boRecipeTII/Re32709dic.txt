Sweet Potato Biscuits Vegan
Flour, pastry, Margarine, non-dairy, Almond milk, Sunflower oil, Salt, Sweet potatoes, Baking Powder, Soymilk, 
Preheat oven to 400. Sift dry ingredients, then cut in margarine and oil.  Blend in potatoes. Add milk to make a moist (slightly sticky) dough. Drop  in 1/4-cup measures on a dry cookie sheet. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.  Yields 12 to 16 biscuits. Note: Unbaked biscuits can be frozen on cookie  sheet for several hours and then stored in freezer in airtight container  until needed. Bake frozen for 20 to 25 minutes at 400 degrees.
