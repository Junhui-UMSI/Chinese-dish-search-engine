Pretty Party Punch, Jn
Pineapple juice, Orange juice, Cranberry-raspberry juice, Seltzer, strawberries, 
In a large pitcher or punch bowl mix together the cranberry-taspberry  juice, the orange juice, the pineapple juice & seltzer. Stir in the  strawberries. Add two handfuls of ice cubes of punch isnt cold enough.    Source: Hamilton Journal News HOLIDAY COOKBOOK 1990, Ruby Gill  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Mar 10, 1998
