Three Cheese Spinach
Butter, Flour, Brick cheese; grated, American cheese; grated, Eggs, (10-oz) frozen chopped, Creamed cottage cheese, 
Cook spinach as directed. Melt butter in hot spinach. Mix cheeses, eggs and  flour. Mix all ingredients and bake at 350  for 1 hour.
