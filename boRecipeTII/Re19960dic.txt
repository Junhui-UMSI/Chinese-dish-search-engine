Apple Butter and Cream Cheese Spread
Apple butter, Vanilla extract, Light cream cheese; softened, Cinnamon, 
Place the cream cheese, apple butter, vanilla and cinnamon in the container  of a food processor fitted with a metal blade. Process for 20 seconds, or  till just smooth, stopping to scrape down the sides of the container, if  necessary. Scrape the spread into a small bowl. Cover and refrigerate till  slightly firm.
