Bs Batch
Semi-sweet chocolate chips, Plus 2 tbsp. sifted, Baking soda, Brown Sugar, Butter, Egg, Salt, Vanilla extract, Very hot water, Granulated sugar, 
Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes. Makes 3 dozen cookies.    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.    2. Sift together flour, baking soda and salt onto waxed paper.    3. Beat butter, brown sugar, sugar and egg in large bowl with electric  mixer until fluffy, about 3 minutes. Beat in hot water and vanilla.  Gradually beat in flour mixture until blended and smooth. Stir in chocolate  chips.    4. Drop dough by well-rounded measuring teaspoonfuls onto large greased  baking sheet.    5. Bake in preheated 375 degree oven for 10 minutes or until golden.    6. Cool on sheet on wire rack 1 minute. Remove to wire rack to cool  completely.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 19, 1998
