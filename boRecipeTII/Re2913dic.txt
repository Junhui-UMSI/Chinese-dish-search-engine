Peanut Butter Magic Middle Cookies
Sugar, FILLING, Flour, Egg, Peanut butter, Baking soda, Brown sugar, COOKIE DOUGH, Vanilla extract, Peanut butter, Unsweetened cocoa, Margarine, Powdered sugar, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.    Cookie Dough: In a small bowl, combine flour, cocoa and baking soda. In a  large bowl, beat sugar, brown sugar, margarine and 1/4 cup peanut butter  until light and fluffy. Add vanilla and egg; beat well. Stir in dry  ingredients. Set aside.    Filling: In a small bowl, combine filling ingredients. Roll into 30 - 1  inch balls. Flour hands if necessary. Shape 1 tablespoon of dough around 1  peanut butter center, covering completely. Place 2 inches apart on  ungreased cookie sheets. Flatten somewhat with bottom of glass dipped in  sugar.    Bake 7 to 9 minutes or until set and slightly cracked. Cool on wire racks.
