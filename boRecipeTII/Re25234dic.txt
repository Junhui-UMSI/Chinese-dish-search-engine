Tony Palermos Michigan Champ Chili
Oregano, Sweet basil, Cumin powder, Garlic -- crushed, Salt, Chili powder, (13 lbs), Crushed rosemary, Onions -- chopped, No.10 cans whole tomatoes, Thyme, Vegetable oil, Jalepeno peppers -- chopped, Tomato paste, Green peppers -- chopped, Sour cream for garnish, Tasbasco, White pepper, Ground beef, 
Brown ground beef, drain and set aside. Heat oil and saute garlic, 2  onioins, green peppers, and jalapeno peppers. Cook on medium heat for 5  mins. Add all spices. Continue to cook for about 3mins. Add whole tomatoes  and juice, tomato paste, Tabasco, and beef and cook for 2 1/2 hours.  Garnish with sour cream.
