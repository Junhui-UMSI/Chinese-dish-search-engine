Swiss Chalet Dip
Swiss Cheese; Shredded, Smoked Ham; Finely Diced, Brandy, Garlic Clove;CrushedIn Press, GARNISH, Unbleached flour, Black Pepper; To Taste, Nutmeg, Dry white wine, White Onion; Finely Diced, 
Place the wine in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Mix the Swiss cheese  and flour together and gradually add to the wine, stirring constantly,  until all the cheese is melted and mixture is smooth. Remove from the heat  and add the brandy, garlic, onion, pepper, and ham. Pour into a chafing  dish and sprinkle with nutmeg. Serve hot. Makes about 3 1/4 cups of dip.  SUGGESTED DIPPERS:  Apples, Artichoke Hearts, Crackers, French Bread  Chunks, Ham, Salami
