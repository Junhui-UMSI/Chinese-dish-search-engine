Chocolate Inside-Out Cake
Chocolate cake mix, (4 1/2 oz) instant chocolate, milk, Eggs, (12 oz) chocolate chips, 
From a friend in Vancouver    Combine everything except for the chocolate chips. Mix 2 minutes. Fold in  chocolate chips. Pour into greased or sprayed tube pan. Bake 50-60 minutes.  Cool 15 minutes in the pan. Remove from pan & cool completely.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "leah perez" <perezleah@hotmail.com> on Aug  02, 1998,
