Piperade Picante
Piperade*, Green olives -- pitted,, Coarsely cho, Wine vinegar, Capers, Salt and pepper --, (optional), 
Combine piperade, vinegar, olives and capers in heavy medium-size saucepan.  Heat, stirring frequently, until mixture is hot. Season with salt, pepper  and additional vinegar, if you wish. The mixture should be pungent. Spoon  over meat or fish and serve immediately. Yields enough for 4 servings.
