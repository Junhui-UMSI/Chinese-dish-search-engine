"Grilled" Apple Crisp
Cinnamon, Whipped topping or ice cream, Pie filling, apple or Cherry, Lemon juice, Sugar, brown; packed, Cake mix, yellow (18.5 oz), Nutmeg, Butter or margarine; cut in, 
1. Spray 9-inch baking pan with PAM.  Pour pie filling into 9-inch baking  pan.  Sprinkle with brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice.   Sprinkle cake mix over pie filling to cover.  Dot with butter slices.    2. Preheat grill on medium for 10 minutes, then turn left side of grill  off and invert a baking pan on left cooking grid. Place baking pan on  inverted pan. Close lid and bake 55-65 minutes, or until browned and  bubbly.    3. Let stand until warm.  Serve topped with whipped topping or ice cream.
