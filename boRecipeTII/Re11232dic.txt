Raisin-Almond Filling for Hamantaschen
Sugar, Water, Orange juice, Grated fresh orange peel, Flour or corn flour, Vanilla, Chopped raisins, Toasted chopped almonds, 
1.. Into a deep saucepan over a flame-tamer place the sugar, orange peel,  orange juice, water and flour or corn flour. Cook and stir over high eat.    2. When mixture starts to thicken, add the raisins. Continue to cook and  stir.    3. Add the nuts and cook until thick.    4. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla.
