Popeyes Pita Surprise
Whole wheat pita pockets, Shredded spinach, Low fat cream cheese;, Corn, 
In a bowl, combine cream cheese, corn and spinach. Cut each pita in half  horizontally and spread with 2 ounces of the cream cheese mixture. Roll up  and secure with a toothpick. Chill until firm. Slice pitas on the diagonal  and serve.    Recipe by: Cooking Live Show #CL8962    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #801 by "Angele and Jon Freeman"  <jfreeman@netusa1.net> on Sep 24, 1997
