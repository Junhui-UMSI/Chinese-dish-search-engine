Beer Sauce for Baked Fish
Cheddar Cheese, Medium white sauce, Beer, Mustard, Paprika, Capers, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Combine white sauce, cheese, Worcestershire sauce, paprika and mustard.  Stir over low heat until cheese melts. Add beer slowly while stirring; add  capers. Serve hot with baked fish fillets From the files of Al Rice, North  Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
