Strawberry and Lime Freeze
Strawberries, Crushed ice, Powdered sugar, Lime juice, 
Blend crushed ice and 1 pint strawberries in the blender or food processor  for about 30 seconds or until mixture is nearly smooth. Pour into quart or  larger pitcher.    Place remaining strawberries, powdered sugar and lime juice in food  processor or blender. Cover and blend on high speed about 30 seconds or  until nearly smooth. Add mixter to pitcher. Serve in 4 tall glasses.  Garnish with strawberries if desired. Serve immediately.    Recipe by: Old El Paso    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #839 by L979@aol.com on Oct 12, 1997
