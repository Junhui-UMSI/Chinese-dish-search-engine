Avocado Salsa Recipe
Orange Peel, Chopped cilantro, Ripe avacado, diced, Lemon Peel, Cayenne pepper to taste, Orange, peeled & diced, Onion, Lemon juice, Salt, Ground cumin seed, 
Combine all except avacado.  Gently stir avacado into  salsa and add cayenne to taste.  Serve w chips or  NAUGHTY (c*****n)!!
