Tuscan Tuna Salad
Can (13 oz) white tuna, Packed in water, drained, Can (8 oz) dark red kidney, Dried basil, crushed, Bottled Italian dressing, Shredded spinach leaves, Sliced green onions & tops, Pace Picante Sauce, Ripe olives, sliced, Beans, rinsed and drained, Tomato, seeded and diced, 
Combine tuna, beans, tomato, olives and onions in large bowl. Combine Pace  Picante Sauce, dressing and basil in small bowl, mixing well; add to tuna  mixture. Toss lightly. Chill. Arrange spinach on serving plate; top with  tuna mixture.  Serve with additional Pace Picante Sauce.
