Aloha Sweet Potatoes
Pineapple Preserves, To 5 Medium Sweet Potatoes, Butter, 
Boil sweet potatoes in their jackets until tender, about 25 minutes. Let  cool, then peel and cut into 1/2 inch thick slices.  In a large skillet  melt pineapple preserves and butter; add sweet potatoes to skillet. Cook  gently; tossing lightly until sweet potatoes are glazed.
