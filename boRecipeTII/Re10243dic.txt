Quick and Easy Skillet Dinner
Med. noodles, cooked ham, Butter, onion, green pepper, , cream of mush. soup, Prepared mustard, Undiluted Carnation 2% E., 
Cook noodles according to package directions; drain. Sliver ham. Melt  butter in large frypan. saute ham, onion and green pepper until vegetables  are almost tender. Stir in mushrooms, soup, mustard and E. Milk. Bring to  boil; simmer, uncovered 10 min to blend flavours. Add noodles, mix well.  Heat through. Makes 4 servings.
