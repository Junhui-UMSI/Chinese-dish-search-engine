Pecan Muffins
Pecans, Flour, Sugar, Eggs, milk, Shortening; melted, Baking Powder, Salt, Cinnamon, 
Sift dry ingredients together. Add pecans. Mix together milk, melted  shortening & eggs. Combine dry & liquid ingredients quickly with little  stirring. Bake in muffin tins in hot oven (425 degrees) about 15 minutes.  Makes 1 dozen.    JAMIE TUCKER    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
