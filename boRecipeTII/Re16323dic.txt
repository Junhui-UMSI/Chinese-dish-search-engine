Watermelon Sorbet
Lemon juice, Sugar, Watermelon Puree; See Note, 
In large bowl, mix watermelon puree, lemon juice and sugar until sugar is  dissolved. Pour mixture into ice cream maker. Freeze as manufacturer  directs. Spoon into freezer containers (or use in previous recipe); cover  and freeze until firm.    Note: In blender, puree chunks of seeded watermelon until liquefied.    (Makes eight cups, up to 16 servings)    (Nutritional data per serving: Calories, 132; protein, 0.51 grams;  carbohydrates, 37 grams; fat, 0.34 grams; cholesterol, none; sodium, 1 mg  milligrams.)
