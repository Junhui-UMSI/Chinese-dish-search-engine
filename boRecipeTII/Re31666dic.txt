Swiss Salad
Tart Green Apple, Red wine vinegar, Beets; Sliced, Swiss Cheese, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Salt, Ground pepper, 
Cut the cheese into 1/2" cubes. Drain the beets and cut them into 1/2"  pieces. Core the apple and cut into 1/2" cubes. In a serving bowl, whisk  together the olive oil, vinegar, salt and pepper. Add the cheese, beets and  apple. Toss gently to coat well and serve.    Recipe by: 5 in 10 Appetizer Cookbook    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Meg Antczak <meginny@frontiernet.net> on Mar  25, 1998
