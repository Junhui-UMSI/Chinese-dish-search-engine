Peanut Butter Cup Bars
Butter, milk, Graham cracker crumbs, Peanut butter, Powdered sugar, Chocolate chips, Butter, Brown sugar, 
Combine graham cracker crumbs, peanut butter, 1 cup butter and  powdered sugar. Work well with fingers and press into 9x13 pan.  Combine remaining butter, brown sugar and milk in small saucepan.  Boil for 1 minute. Add chocolate chips and stir until smooth. Spread  on top of first mixture. Let cool, cut into squares.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
