Strawberry Bonbons
FF Butter Cookie Recipe, 
Beat 1 tsp. almond extract into dough. Shape by tablespoonfuls into  strawberries; roll in red star sprinkles & sesame seeds. Bake 12-15  minutes. Decorate tops of cooled cookies with green candied cherry  pieces for leaves; press in whole cloves for stems.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
