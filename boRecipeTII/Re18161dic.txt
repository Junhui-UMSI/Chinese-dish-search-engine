Velveta Cheese Dip
Velveta cheese, Green Bell Peppers, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Onions, Tomatoes, Pimento, Banana Peppers, 
By Betty Davenport    Cook minced garlic in cooking oil, push aside.  Cook onions until soft and  then add green pepper and banana peppers. When banana pepers start to  soften, add tomatoes. Cook until soft. Then add pimento, garlic, oil, and  Velveta (cut into small pieces).  Cook over very low heat until cheese is  melted.    Serve warm with chips, crackers or tortillia chips.
