Pineapple Cake with Cream Cheese Ginger Frost
Vanilla, Butter, Crushed pineapple in juice  , Baking soda, Eggs, Flour, Sugar, Walnuts, Cream cheese, FROSTING:, CAKE:, Brown sugar, Ginger, Powdered sugar, 
Combine all cake ingredients and pour into an ungreased, 13x9 inch Pyrex  pan. Bake at 350^ for 45-50 minutes. Combine frosting ingredients and  spread on cooled pineapple cake.    Recipe By     : Berkshire Seasonings
