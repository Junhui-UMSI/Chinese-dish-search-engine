Tomato Bread
1 Lb Loaf:, Yeast, Sugar, olive oil, Dried basil, Powdered milk, Sun-dried tomatoes; finely, Salt, Water, Whole-wheat flour, White flour; Strong, Olives; finely chopped, 
Place all ingredients in bread pan in order specified by manufacturer.  Select wholewheat cycle and press start.
