Sweet Potato Taco with Smoked Yellow Pepper Sauce
Honey, Pepper, olive oil, Sweet potatoes;, Cilantro; chopped, Blue corn tortillas, 6", White cheddar cheese; grated, Salt, ; 1/4 inch thick, Monterey jack cheese; grated, 
Prepare a wood or charcoal frill and allow it to burn down to embers.    For each serving lay 3 slices of sweet potato over half of the tortilla,  sprinkle with cheeses, cilantro and honey. Season to taste with salt and  pepper. Fold over tortilla and brush with olive oil. Grill for 3 minutes on  each side or until crispy and cheese has melted. Serve hot with Smoked  Yellow Pepper Vinaigrette.
