Pumpkin Spice Soup
Light corn syrup, Nutmeg; To Taste, Pumpkin; 1 Can, Salt, Pumpkin-pie spice, Molasses, GARNISH, Evaporated Skimmed Milk; 2cn, 
Place all the ingredients in a 2 1/2 to 3-quart mixer bowl.  Beat until  well blended.  Refrigerate for several hours and stir before serving.  Sprinkle with nutmeg for a garnish.
