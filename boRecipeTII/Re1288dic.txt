Diabetic Rhubarb Strawberry Jelly
Stalks rhubarb, Water, Diet strawberry gelatin, Fresh strawberries; halved, Equal; (artificial sugar, 
Cut off leaves and stem ends from rhubarb..Wash thoroughly..Dry well. If  rhubarb is young, the skin is tender and will not need to be peeled..If  skin is tough, peel rhubarb. Cut rhubarb into 1/2- inch pieces..Place in  large saucepan with water. Bring to a boil..Lower heat and simmer, covered,  for 15 minutes. Stir in gelatin and strawberries..Remove from heat and cool  10 minutes..Stir in Equal. Spoon into sterilized jars..Chill until  set...Keep refrigerated.    Makes about 2 cups..Calories: 3 per tbs.
