Vodka Sauce #1
Clove (large) garlic; minced, (28-oz) tomato puree, Vodka, Cream, Prosciutto, salt and pepper, Onion, 
From: Gerald Edgerton <jerrye@wizard.com>    Date: Tue, 07 May 1996 13:06:33 -0700    Saute chopped onion (1 medium) and garlic (1 large clove).  Put in chopped  prosciutto (about 1/4 pound) and cook for 3 minutes.  Put tomato puree (28  ounce can) and cook for 20 minutes.  Put salt, pepper and basil to taste.  After sauce cooks, pour 1 ounce vodka and cook another 20 minutes. Pour in  1/4 cup cream and mix. Put over any pasta.
