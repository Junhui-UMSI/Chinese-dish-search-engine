Tarantula Jacks Chili
Garlic, Beef boneless top round,, (10 1/2 oz.@) condensed, (8 oz.) tomato sauce, Chicken broth, Cubed, Onions, grated, Ground cumin, Water, Tabasco, Chili powder, 
Cook beef in nonstick Dutch oven until brown; stir occasionally. Add  onions, garlic, broth and water. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and  simmer 1 1/2 hours. Stir in tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin and pepper  sauce. Cover and simmer 1 hour, adding additional water for desired  consistancy.    FROM BETTY CROCKERS INTERNATIONAL CHILI SOCIETY 1989 $25,000 WINNER.
