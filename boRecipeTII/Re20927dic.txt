Apple Onion Rye for Bread Machine
Water, Active dry yeast, Unsalted butter, Apple; chopped, Bread flour, Cracked wheat, LARGE LOAF11/2, Rye flour, Onion; minced, Caraway seeds, Sugar, Salt, Bread flour, Garlic, 
Add all the ingredeints in the order suggested by your bread machine manual  and process on the basic bread cycle according to the manufacturers  directions. Let loaf cool completely before slicing From: Best Bread  Machine Cookbook Ever, by Madge Rosenburg. Shared by June Hoffman on ECHO,  and formatted for MM by Marge Nemeth.
