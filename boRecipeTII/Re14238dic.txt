White Sangria
Curacao, Orange, Strawberries, Sugar, Lemon, Lime, club soda, -(up to), dry white wine, 
Combine wine, Curacao and sugar in pitcher and stir until sugar is  dissolved. Add fruits. Cover and chill in refrigerator for at least 1 hour  to let flavors blsnd. Befors serving, add soda and ice cubes and stir  gently to mix. Serve in wine glasses or champagne flutes. Yield: 6  servings.    LINDA DELANEY (MRS.
