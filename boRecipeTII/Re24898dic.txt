Austria - Viennese Coconut Macaroons
Vanilla, Vanilla Wafers; crushed, Baking Powder, Sugar, Heavy cream, Butter, Coconut, Flour, Cornstarch, Eggs, 
Cream butter and sugar. Add the well-beaten eggs, cream and vanilla. Sift  the dry ingredients, and add to the first mixture. Add the coconut and  wafers. Drop by teaspoonfuls, 1 inch apart, on a greased cookie sheet and  bake in a moderate oven until brown on top. 8-10 minutes.
