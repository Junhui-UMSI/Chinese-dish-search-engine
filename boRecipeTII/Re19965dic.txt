Apple Butter Bread
Salt, Flour; whole wheat, Sugar, Yeast, Vegetable oil, Apple butter, Water, Flour; bread, 
Bring bread ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in order.  Set "baking control" at 10 oclock. Select "white bread" and push Start.    Bake the apple butter right into the bread.  Try substituting any favorite  jam or jelly for the apple butter.    Apples comments: I substituted Peach Butter for the Apple Butter in this  recipe and it was delicious.  It has a very nice texture and aroma!
