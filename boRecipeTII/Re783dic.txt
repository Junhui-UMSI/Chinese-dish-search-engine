Harvest Fruit Salad
Pecan halves, Sherry vinegar, Red-wine vinegar, Walnut oil, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Salt & freshly ground pepper, Pears ( 1 1/2 lbs.), halved,, Red and green seedless, Green or red apples, such as, 
Preheat oven to 350F.  Spread the pecans on a baking sheet and toast until  lightly browned and fragrant, 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from the oven and let  cool.    In a small bowl, whisk together the sherry vinegar, red-wine vinegar,  walnut oil, olive oil, and salt and pepper to taste to form a vinaigrette.    In a salad bowl, combine the pears, grapes, apples, and pecans. Add the  vinaigrette, toss gently to coat, and serve.
