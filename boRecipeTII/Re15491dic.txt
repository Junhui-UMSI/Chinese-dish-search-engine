4 Bean Bake
Butter beans; (15 oz.), Onions; sliced, Dry mustard, Red kidney beans; (15 oz.), Vinegar, Green limas; (15 oz.), Brown sugar; (1/2 to 1), Garlic powder, Bacon, Baked beans; (27 oz.), Salt, 
Fry bacon until crisp and remove from skillet. Saute onions in bacon fat.  Add vinegar, salt, sugar, mustard and garlic powder. Cover and cook 20  minutes. Drain butter beans, green limas and kidney beans. Combine all  beans and pour onion mixture over. Mix gently. Sprinkle with crumbled  bacon. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees uncovered.
