Turtles #2
Chocolate chips, Vanilla, Pecan, Shortening, Margarine, Caramels, 
Put wax paper on baking sheet. Arrange nuts in clusters of 4 and place 1"  apart. Melt caramels and margarine. Remove from heat. Stir in vanilla til  thoroughly mixed.. Drop by teaspoons into center of cluster touching all 4  pecans to hold together. Cool.  Melt chocolate and shortening. Spread over  caramels.  Makes 18.
