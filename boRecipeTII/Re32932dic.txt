Sweet Spicy Chicken Skewers
green chilies, Garlic, Sweet green pepper, Salt, plum sauce, Onion, Soy sauce, Sesame oil, Green onion, Sweet red pepper, chicken breasts, 
Combine first 7 ingredients in a food processor; process until pureed.    Place chicken in a medium size bowl; add half of the sauce mixture.  Cover;  refrigerate for 1 hour to marinate.  Reserve the remaining half of the  sauce mixture.    Soak 16 small wooden skewers in a bowl of cold water for 15 minutes.  Heat  broiler.  Thread green pepper, onion, red pepper and chicken cubes on  skewers.  Place skewers on rack in broiler pan.  Spoon on remaining  reserved sauce.    Broil 6" from heat for 6 to 8 minutes or until chicken is cooked through,  turning once.
