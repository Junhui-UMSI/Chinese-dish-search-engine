Beef Biscuits
Salt, Onion, (8oz) tomato sauce, Ground beef, Pepper, Green Pepper, Oregano, Oil for frying, Refrigerated biscuits, 
Brown beef, onion and pepper. Stir in sauce and seasonings, mixing well.    Roll out each biscuit into a 5" circle. Place 1 rounded tablespoon meat  mixture on each; flod in half and seal with tines of fork.    Fry about 3" of oil to 375F. Deep fry each biscuit until golden brown;  drain on paper towels. Serve warm.    Taste of Home Ground Beef Collection 1996 Edition
