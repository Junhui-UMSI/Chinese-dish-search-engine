Bean Dip
Yellow onion; chopped, Refried beans; (preferrably, Container sour cream, Fresh serranos; (or 1-3, 
Turn crockpot on high. Put beans, onions and serranos in, and mix well.  Cover and stir periodically until it starts to bubble. Let simmer for 10  minutes, stirring frequently. Add sour cream, mix well, and let it come  back to a simmer. Turn down to low, and keep covered until ready to serve.  Serve hot with tortilla chips.
