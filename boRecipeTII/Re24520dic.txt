Asparagus Salad
Commercial mayonnaise, Light cream, Each, vertically packed, Homemade mayonnaise, or, Whole green asparagus,17 oz, Lettuce leaves, Hard cooked eggs, sieved, 
1. Drain asparagus, trying to keep spears whole. 2 Place lettuce leaves on  8 salad plates and arrange asparagus on lettuce leaves, or asparagus may be  placed in a vegetable bowl. 3. Place a spoonful of mayonnaise over  asparagus. 4. Garnish with hard cooked egg. 5. Chill before serving.
