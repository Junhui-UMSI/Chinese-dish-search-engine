Applesauce Muffins
Powdered sugar (optional), Ground cinnamon, Salt, Ground allspice, Chopped nuts (optional), Eggs, Baking soda, Unsweetened applesauce, Flour, Sugar, Ground cloves, Butter or margarine, 
Preheat oven to 375?. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, 1 at a time. Mix in  applesauce and spices. Sift salt, soda and flour together and add to  applesauce mixture. Mix well.  Stir in nuts. Pour into greased muffin tins  and bake for approximately 15 minutes for large muffins and 8 to 10 minutes  for smaller muffins. Sprinkle tops with powdered sugar while still warm.  Yield: 3 dozen muffins.    CAROLE LYNN SHERMAN    (MRS. WILLIAM F.)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
