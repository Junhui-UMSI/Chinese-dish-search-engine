Bean Maplenut Candy
Boiled and mashed Great, Peanuts, Maple syrup or Mapleine, Powdered sugar or amount, 
Flavor with maple syrup or Mapleine, add a few chopped peanuts. Mold with  hands or between 2 spoons; drop on wax paper to harden.    This recipe came from: America Cooks, copyright 1917    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 187 by "Bonnie"  <solomon1@kcsnet.com> on Nov 1, 1997
