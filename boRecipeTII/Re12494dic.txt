[Sort Of] Blueberry Stuffed French Toast
Fresh berries, Cream cheese, Maple syrup, Cornstarch, White bread, Water, Eggs, milk, Sugar, Butter, blueberries, SAUCE, 
from Joans Bed & Breakfast; Peabody, Massachusetts    Cook sugar, cornstarch, and water until boiling. Add berries and butter.    Break bread into small pieces. 6 slices first - place in bottom of 9x12  oven proof dish. cut creamcheese into many small pieces and spread over  bread cubes. Cover cheese and bread cubes with 1 full cup blueberries.  Place 6 (left) slices of bread cut into cubes over all. Mix milk, eggs, and  maple syrup with mixer and pour over all. Bake at 350 degrees for 1/2 hour  covered. Uncover and cook about 15 minutes.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V96 #106    From: msteinhorn@exit109.com    Date: Fri, 20 Dec 1996 10:49:33 -0500 (EST)
