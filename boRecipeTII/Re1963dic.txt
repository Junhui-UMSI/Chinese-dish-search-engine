Chocolate Mint Coffee Mix with Recipe
MIX, Nondairy powdered creamer, Sugar, Instant coffee, Cocoa, Peppermint candies, crushed, COFFEE, Mix, Boiling water, 
Mix: In blender, process all ingredients until candies are pulverized.  Store in an airtight container.    Chocolate Mint Coffee: Pour boiling water over mix in cup. Garnish with  whipped cream and stir with candy cane.
