Yummy Zucchini Bake
Gruyere cheese, grated, Salt, Paprika, Parmesan cheese to taste, Bread crumbs, Zucchini, Cheddar cheese, grated, Sour cream, Onion, Water, 
Contributed to the echo by: Janice Norman Originally from: "The Mayors  Cookbook" Yummy Zucchini Bake (Mayor Eugene B. Kunk, Springfield, Ohio)    Wash and boil zucchini about 10 minutes. Cut off ends and halve lengthwise.  Arrange in shallow, buttered casserole. Saute onion in butter; add  remaining ingredients and spread over zucchini. Top with bread crumbs mixed  with parmesan cheese. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
