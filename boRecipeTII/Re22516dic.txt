Applesauce Raisin Bread
Vegetable oil, Sugar, Baking Powder, Coconut, Applesauce, Vanilla, Salt, Nuts, Raisins, Eggs, Flour, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, 
Mix together first 5 ingredients; set aside.  Mix together the dry  ingredients in another bowl and pour thin batter into it. Grease 2 bread  tins, bake in oven for about 60 minutes, check with toothpick.
