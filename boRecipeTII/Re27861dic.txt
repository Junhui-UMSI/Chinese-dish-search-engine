Thengai Chaadam (Coconut Rice)
Rice, Coconut; grated, Cashewnuts; broken, Salt, Black gram dal, Red chiles; broken, Green chiles; cleaned &, Ghee, Black gram dal; (cleaned &, For Seasoning, Sprigs curry leaves; cleaned, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Black mustard seeds, Bengal gram dal, 
Cook rice till just done. Spread on a flat dish. Add salt and sugar, then  allow to cool. Heat oil and add mustard seeds, black gram dal, Bengal gram  dal and red chilies. When brown, add green chilies, curry leaves,  cashewnuts, soaked and drained black gram da,l and grated coconut. Fry till  coconut is pink in color and pour over rice. Add ghee and mix well and  serve.
