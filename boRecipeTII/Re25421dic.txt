Tortilla Hot Dog
Tortilla, Hot Dog; low fat or turkey, Mustard, Toothpick, 
Prepare in the morning. Broil or boil hot dog. Wrap in tortilla spread with  mustard (I used a large tortilla cut in half). Secure with toothpick and  wrap in foil for childs lunch box. taste like a sausage roll.    SOURCE: The Pennywhistle Lunch Box Book posted by Anne MacLellan
