Pepperoni Bread
Diced pepperoni, Oregano, Garlic powder, Active dry yeast, olive oil, Salt, Sugar, Basil, Bread flour, Water, Whole wheat flour, AFTER BEEP, 
Place all ingredients except pepperoni in bread machine in order suggested  by manufacturer and process on raisin bread cyle if that option is  available. At the beep add the pepperoni. If your machine does not have a  raisin bread cycle, add pepperoni about 18 to 25 minutes (depending on your  machines kneading cycle) into first kneading cycle--you need to allow 4 or  5 minutes of kneading for it to assimilate the pepperoni into the dough  properly.
