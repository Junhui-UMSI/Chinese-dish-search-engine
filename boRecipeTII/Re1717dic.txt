Chateaubriand with Bearnaise Sauce
Salt, Pepper, CENTER FILLET OF BEEF  *, Vegetable oil, BEARNAISE SAUCE, 
SPRINKLE STEAKS LIGHTLY WITH SALT AND PEPPER. MARINATE IN OIL 2 HOURS AT  ROOM TEMPERATURE. BROIL FOR 5 MINUTES ON EACH SIDE FOR MEDIUM RARE. SERVE  ON PLATTER WITH BEARNAISE SAUCE.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by BobbieB1@aol.com on Nov 23, 1998
