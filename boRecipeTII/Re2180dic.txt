Aunt Sallys Rugalach
Butter, Sugar, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Nuts; chopped, DOUGH, Cream cheese, Vanilla, Flour, FILLING, Sugar, Cocoa, 
Mix, knead, refrigerate overnight. Take out 1/2 hour before using.    Divide dough into three balls. Roll each into circle. Cut into 16 wedges; I  use a pizza cutter. Sprinkle with filling. Roll each up starting from large  end. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "dogsmom@cinci.infi.net"  <dogsmom@cinci.infi.net> on Oct 11, 1998,
