Raspberry Vinaigrette Salad
olive oil, Raspberry vinegar, SALAD, Tomatoes, Sugar, romaine lettuce, BETTY PINDER TKHN51B, Pepper, Arugula, VINAIGRETTE, red leaf lettuce, fresh dill, Dijon mustard, Mushrooms, Green onions, 
Mix first 5 vinaigrette ingredients in small bowl. Gradually whisk in olive  oil.  Combine salad ingredients in large bowl. Add enough dressing to  season to taste and toss well.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #021 by "Robert Ellis"  <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on Jan 20, 1998
