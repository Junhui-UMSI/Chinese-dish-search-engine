Queen Ranch Chicken
green chilies, Chopped, Lite margarine, Velvetta cheese, Corn tortillas, Garlic powder, Onion, Chicken, stewed tomatoes, Chicken broth, Drained, Cornstarch, Salt & pepper, 
1. In a non-stick pan, saute chicken and onion in margarine until done.    2. Add cornstarch and 1 cup broth.  Cook 5 minutes.    3. Add 1/2 cup broth, tomatoes, green chilies and cook until thick.    4. In a casserole dish layer tortillas, cheese, and chicken mixture.    5. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #153    Date: Sun, 14 Jul 1996 22:42:30 -0600    From: Shirley Jordan <shirleyj@pacificrim.net>    Recipe By     : Mary Ann Houseman - WW leader
