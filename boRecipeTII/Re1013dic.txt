Cinnamon-Cranberry Iced Tea
Cinnamon-flavored herb tea, Cranberry juice, Pineapple juice, Ice cubes, Sugar, Cinnamon stick, 
Mix the tea and juices together and pour over ice in a tall glass. Add  sugar and garnish with a cinnamon stick.
