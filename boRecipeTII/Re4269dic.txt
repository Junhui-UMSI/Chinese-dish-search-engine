Permesan Chicken Wings Oreganata
Black Pepper, Butter, INGREDIENTS, parmesan cheese, Chicken wings, Minutes cook time, Chopped parsley, Dried basil, Paprika, Dried Oregano, Minutes preparation time, Salt, 
1.  Preheat oven to 350F.  In a paper bag, combine cheese, parsley,  papriak, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Toss to mix. Pour melted butter  ina a shallow bowl.    2.  Dip chicken pieces into butter, then place in a paper bag and shake to  coat.  Place chicken on foil-lined baking sheet and bake 45 minutes. Serve  hot.    From:  365 Ways to Cook Chicken
