Bayerischer Wurstsalat (Bavarian Sausage Salad)
Sugar, Mustard; Prepared *, Parsley, Vegetable oil, Salt, Pepper, Onion; Medium, Knockwurst; Cooked / Cooled, Pickles; Small, Paprika, Capers, Vinegar, 
*  Mustard must be the strong Dijon or Gulden Type. +++ Cut the knockwurst  into small cubes.  Mince the pickles and onion. Mix together the vinegar,  mustard and oil.  Add salt, pepper, paprika and sugar. Adjust seasonings if  desired.  Add the capers; mix well. Stir in the chopped knockwurst,  pickles, and onions.  Just before serving, garnish with chopped parsley.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdjaxxx.zip
