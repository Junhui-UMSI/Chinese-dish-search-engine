Pineapple with Sour Cream
Maple syrup, (13.5-oz) pineapple chunks;, Sour Cream, Light brown sugar, 
Place pineapple in 6 small dessert dishes. In a small bowl, combine sour  cream with maple syrup until well blended. Spoon over the pineapple in the  dessert bowls. Refrigerate until well chilled -- at least 1 hour. Just  before serving, sprinkle with brown sugar.    FROM JAPAN    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
