Baked Italian Spaghetti
Garlic salt; pepper to taste, (small) tomatoes; drain and, Box elbow spaghetti, Hamburger, Kraft Colby cheese; grated, Tomato sauce, 
Cook spaghetti until done, then mix with other ingredients.  Do not fry the  hamburger.  Add a little of the juice from the tomatoes to make slightly  moist.  Mix some butter with cracker crumbs and pour over the top. Bake 30  minutes after it has started to cook.
