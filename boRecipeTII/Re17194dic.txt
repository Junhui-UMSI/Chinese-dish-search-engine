Vodka Collins
Powdered sugar, Ice, Maraschino cherry, Vodka, Club soda, Orange, Fresh Lemon Juice, ice, 
Fill a mixing glass with ice. Add vodka, lemon juice, and powdered sugar  and shake well. Strain into a Collins glass filled with ice cubes. Fill  with club soda and stir. Garnish with a slice of orange and a cherry. Serve  with a straw.    Recipe By     : Joe Robertson    From:                                 Date:
