White Pizza
Parmesan/Romano cheese;, Red hot pepper; to taste, BRADSHAW BDGM08B, Mozzerella cheese; grated, Cheddar or swiss cheese;, Oregano, Butter, Dough; or pizza shell, olive oil, Garlic, Feta cheese; crumbled, 
Ssaute garlic in butter until light brown. Spray bottom of pizza pan or  cookie sheet w/non-stick oil; knead dough to fit, or place shell...  Sprinkle cheese on top; drizzle w/olive oil, sprinkle oregano & red pepper  (if desired) on top. Bake @425 until golden brown.
