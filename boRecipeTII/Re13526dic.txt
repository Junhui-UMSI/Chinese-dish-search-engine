Whoopie Pies
Shortening, Vanilla, Powdered sugar, Marshmallow Fluff, milk, Shortening, Salt, Milk, Sugar, Flour, Cocoa Powder, Egg, Filling-----, Baking Powder, Vanilla, Baking soda, 
Preheat oven to 375.    Cream shortening and sugar.  Mix in egg, milk, and vanilla. Sift dry  ingredients together and add.  Drop by teaspoonful on a cookie sheet,  flatten with the bottom of a glass and and bake for 8 minutes. Cool and put  filling between cakes.    Makes 3 dozen pies.    Recipe By     :
