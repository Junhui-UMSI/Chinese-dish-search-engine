Lemon Custard Pudding Cake
milk, Lemon; grated peel of, Lemon juice, Powdered sugar, Flour, Butter, Sugar; divided, Eggs, 
Combine flour, butter and 1-1/2 cups sugar. Beat yolks and add to mixture  along with milk and peel. Add juice. Beat egg whites until stiff, slowly  adding rest of sugar. Fold into batter. Pour into a 2-quart baking dish.  Place in a shallow pan of hot water and bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
