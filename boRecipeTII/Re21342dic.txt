Uncooked Chocolate Squares
Egg beaten, Butter, Coffee mixed with water to, Make 1/3 cup., Chocolate chips, Pieces., Peanut butter, Icing sugar, Vanilla, Graham crackers, broken in, 
Melt chips, peanut butter, and butter together. Add coffee mixture and  icing sugar. Mix well and let cool. Add beaten egg, vanilla and graham  crackers. Spread in 8x8" pan. Cover and refrigerate.
