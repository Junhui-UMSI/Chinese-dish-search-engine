Beans a la Chara
Cilantro -- chopped, Tomato -- chopped, Garlic -- minced, Pinto beans -- dry, Bacon -- chopped, And, Chili powder, Pepper, Poblano -- seeded, Oregano, Onion -- chopped, Salt pork -- about 1/2" wid, 
??????? ea salt                          ??????? ea pepper Soak beans,  cover with water in a medium kettle for two hours.  Add salt pork and bring  to a boil over medium heat.  Reduce heat, cover and cook 1 1/2 to 2 hours.  Add salt, pepper, oregano, and chili powder and continue to simmer. Saute  bacon, onion, garlic, tomato,Poblano pepper and cilantro for approximately  10 minutes.  Add to beans and simmer an additional 30 minutes or until  beans are tender.
