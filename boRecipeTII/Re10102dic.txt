Queen of Rice Pudding
Boiling water, Uncooked rice, milk, Vanilla, Granulated sugar, Egg yolks, Egg whites, stiffly beaten, Sugar, 
Cook rice in rapidly boiling salted water. Drain and place in strainer over  heat until rice is dry fluffy. Make a custard of the milk, egg yolks, sugar  and vanilla. Pour over rice which had been places in a buttered baking  dish. Drop spoonfuls of strawberry jam over the surface and then cover with  meringue which has been made by adding the 4 tbsp. of sugar to the beaten  egg whites. Sprinkle with coconut. Bake in a slow oven at 315 F. for 20  minutes. Serve hot or cold. Serves 8.    AR/92
