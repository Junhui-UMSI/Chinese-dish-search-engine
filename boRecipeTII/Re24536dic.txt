Asparagus Sandwich
Eggs, Parmesan cheese, asparagus, cream cheese, Seasoned salt, Butter, Bread, 
Combine asparagus, eggs & cream cheese. Mash together; season with seasoned  salt. Butter outside of bread. Spread mixture in middle. Sprinkle outside  of buttered bread with Parmesan cheese & put under broiler to brown. Turn  over, butter bread & sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Brown. Cut in half  diagonally.
