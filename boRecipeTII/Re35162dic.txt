Bee Sting Cake
STREUSEL TOPPING, Flour, Cottage cheese,; mashed, Salad oil, Finely chopped blanched, FILLING, Sugar, milk, Vanilla, Butter, DOUGH, milk, Tesapoon cinnamon, Baking Powder, Butter, Vanilla, Sugar, Dash of salt, Flour, 
Preheat oven to 375. Mix cottage cheese, milk, salad oil, salt, flour,  baking powder, and vanilla together. Blend well. Spray a 10 by 15 inch  jelly roll pan with non-stick cooking spray. Roll dough mixture out and  spread on pan and set aside. Filling: combine butter, sugar, milk, vanilla,  and almonds together in saucepan. Cook until thick. Spread mixture evenly  over dough. Topping: mix together flour, cinnamon, sugar, and butter and  blend well. Sprinkle over all and bake for 20-25 minutes.
