Quick Quiche
Eggs, Swiss cheese, Pieces bacon; cooked, up to, milk, Green onions; up to 5, 
Use deep dish crust; saute onions, mushrooms and whatever you want to put  in; arrange in bottom of crust. Mix eggs, cheese and milk in bowl and warm  mixture, then place into crust. Salt to taste. Bake at 350 degrees for 35  minutes or until golden brown.
