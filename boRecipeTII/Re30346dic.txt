Banana Cranberry Cream
Lemon juice, Unflavored gelatin, Sugar, Cold water, Cranberry liqueur, Ripe bananas, Cream, whipped, 
peel and slice bananas.  Dissolve gelatin in cold water. Combine sliced  bananas, gelatin, sugar, lemon juice and cranberry liqueur, Blend in  blender until smooth. Partially freeze mixture. Fold in whipped cream and  freeze. serves 6 to 8  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #330    From: l.and-e.smith@juno.com (Lois Smith)    Date: Mon, 2 Dec 1996 09:47:28 PST
