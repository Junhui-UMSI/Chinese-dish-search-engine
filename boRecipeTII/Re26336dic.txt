Tomato Aspic with Artichokes
Vinegar, Tomato juice, Worcestershire Sauce, Celery seed, (6 Oz) Artichoke Hearts;, (3 Oz) Lemon Gelatin Powder, Onion Juice, 
Heat 1 cup tomato juice, add gelatin, and dissolve. Add remaining  ingredients. Chill until firm.    Serves 4 to 6    Source: "Mountain Measures" -- Junior League of Charleston, WV ed. 1974    billspa@icanect.net    Recipe by: Elizabeth V.
