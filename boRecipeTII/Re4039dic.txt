Peppermint Cloud
milk, (6 oz) chocolate crumb crust, Fudge sauce, Miniature marshmallows, Crushed peppermint candy, Whipping cream; whipped, (prepared), Rice, Vanilla extract, 
Combine rice, marshmallows, milk, and candy in 2-quart saucepan.  Cook  over medium heat until thick and creamy, 6 to 8 minutes, stirring  constantly.  Remove from heat and stir in vanilla; cool.  Fold in whipped  cream.  Spoon into chocolate crust.  Chill at least 3 hours.  Drizzle with  warm fudge sauce before serving.
