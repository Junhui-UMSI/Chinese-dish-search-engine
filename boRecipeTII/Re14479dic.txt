Whole-Grain Herb-Cheese Muffins
Dijon mustard, Salad oil, Honey, milk, Dry tarragon leaves, Onion, Baking Powder, Whole-wheat flour, Cheddar Cheese, green bell pepper, Yellow cornmeal, Eggs, Dry thyme leaves, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 375F, prepare pans. In a frying pan cook onion & pepper in  1 Tbsp of the oil until limp, about 7 mins, stirring often. Mix remaining  oil, honey, mustard, milk, eggs & cheese. In another bowl, stir together  flour, cornmeal, b/pdr, herbs & salt. Add the dry mix to the wet mix with  the onion mixture, stir until just combined. Spoon into pans & bake for  20-25 mins or until well browned. Makes 12. 170 calories per muffin.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
