Quick Turkey Gumbo
Chow mein noodles (3 oz.), Cubed, cooked turkey, Chopped pimiento, Soy sauce, Water, Chicken gumbo soup, Cornstarch, 
In saucepan, combine cornstarch, water and soy sauce. Add soup. Cook and  stir until mixture is thickened and bubbly. Stir in turkey and pimiento.  Heat through.  Serve over chow mein noodles.
