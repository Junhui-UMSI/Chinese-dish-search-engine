Apple
Baking Powder, Skim milk -- at room, Temperature, Cinnamon, Applesauce -- at room, Apples -- peeled/ chopped, Baking soda, Egg whites -- whipped, Ginger, Cloves, Brown sugar -- packed, Salt, Unbleached flour, Temperature, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.  In a  mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon,  ginger, and cloves.  In another mixing bowl, combine egg whites, brown  sugar, applesauce, milk, and apples.  Mix dry ingredients with wet  ingredients just until moistened.  Pour mixture into prepared pan(s). Bake  for 1 hour depending on pan size.
