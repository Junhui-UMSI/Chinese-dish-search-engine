Vichyssoise (Cold Potato Soup)
Chives, Baking potatoes, Chicken Stock, Kosher salt, Butter, Black pepper, Leeks, Heavy cream, 
1. In a large pot, melt butter over low heat, add the leeks, cover and cook       10    minutes, stir occasionally.    2. add potatos, stock, & salt. Cover & let cook until the potatos are  soft, approx. 40 minutes.    3. In a food processor or blender puree the soup in batches.    4. Place all of soup in a large container, add heavy cream & black pepper,  refrigerate atleast 2 hours before serving.    Sprinkle with chives to serve!!!!!!!!!!!
