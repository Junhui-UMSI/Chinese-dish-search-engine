Bean Sprouts and Green Onions
Low-sodium soy sauce;, Bean sprouts;, Green onion; chopped, Walnut oil;, Fresh parsley, Sunflower seeds;, 
In a wok or a large, non-stick skillet, heat oil.  Rinse bean sprout  under cold water.  Saute green onions about 3 minutes; stir in  sprouts to heat through.  Toss with soy sauce.  Garnish with parsley  and sunflower seeds.
