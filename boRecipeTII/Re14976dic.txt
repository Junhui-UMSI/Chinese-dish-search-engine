30-Min- Herbed Pork Cutlets
Egg, Fresh thyme, Dry bread crumbs, Fresh oregano, Pepper, Vegetable oil, Fast-fry pork cutlets, Salt, Fresh basil, Parmesan, fresh, 
In shallow dish, lightly beat egg. In separate shallow dish, stir together  bread crumbs, basil, oregano, Parmesan, thyme, pepper and salt. Dip pork  into egg to coat well; press into bread crumb mixture, turning to coat all  over.    In large skillet, heat half of the oil. over medium heat; cook pork, in  batches and adding remaining oil if necessary, turning once, for 8-10  minutes or until just a hint of pink remains inside. Serve with new red  potatoes and yellow beans.    4 servings for $5.37CDN [Aug 95]    Per Serving: about 255 calories, 28 g protein, 12 g fat, 7 g carbohydrate,    Source: Canadian Living magazine, Aug 95 Article "30-Minute Summer Suppers"  Recipe by Canadian Living Test Kitchen        [-=PAM=-]   PA_Meadows@msn.com
