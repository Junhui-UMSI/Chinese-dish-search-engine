Bean Threads with Minced Pork
Soy sauce, Cilantro sprigs; for garnish, Lean ground pork; or chicken, Dry sherry, Minced fresh ginger, Chicken broth, Hot bean sauce, Green onions, Peanut oil, Red/green hot chili pepper, Bean threads or chinese rice, Dried mushrooms, 
Place bean threads and dried mushrooms in separate bowls. Cover each with  hot water. Let stand 30 minutes;drain. Cut bean threads into 4-inch pieces.    Squeeze out excess water from mushrooms. Cut off and discard stems; cut  caps into thin slices. Cut chili pepper in half and scrape out seeds.    Finely mince chili pepper. Thinly slice 2 of the green onions. Cut  remaining onion into 1 1/2 inch slivers and reserve for garnish. Combine  ginger and hot bean sauce in small bowl. Combine chicken broth, soy sauce  and sherry in medium bowl. Heat oil in wok or large skillet over high heat.    Add pork or chicken and stir-fry until meat is no longer pink, about 2  minutes. Add chili pepper, sliced onions and ginger-bean sauce mixture.    Stir-fry until meat absorbs color from bean sauce, about 1 minute. Add  chicken broth mixture, bean threads and mushrooms. Simmer, uncovered until  most of the lidquid is absorbed, about 5 minutes. Garnish with onion  slivers and cilantro. Makes 4 servings.    Have a great Day! My house is a Chinese laundry today!!
