White Bean and Smoked Turkey Casserole
Garlic; minced, Dried thyme, Dry white wine, Pepper, Smoked turkey; cut in thin, Vegetable oil, Onion, (19oz) white kidney beans;, Tomato paste, Bay leaf; broken in half, 
Recipe By : The Little Mountain Bean Bible Cookbook  http://dinnercoop.cs.cmu.edu/dinnercoop/Recipes/subjects/turkey.html    Heat oil in heavy 1 1/2 to 2 qt. stovetop casserole. Add onion and garlic  and cook over medium heat until golden. Stir in turkey. Add wine and cook  over high heat until it evaporates. Add remaining ingredients and liquid  from beans. Stir gently to blend. Bring to boil, reduce heat to low, cover  and simmer 20 minutes, adding a little water if mixture becomes dry, until  hot and flavors have blended. Discard bay leaf.
