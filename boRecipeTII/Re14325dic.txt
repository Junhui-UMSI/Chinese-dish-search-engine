Whole Fish Baked in a Salt Crust
Cleaned whole fish, Sprigs tarragon, Egg white, Sea salt, Pepper, 
Preheat the oven to gas mark 5, 375F, 190?C.    Season the fish with a little black pepper.  Combine the sea salt with the  egg white and 2fl oz water. Spread half the salt mixture on a baking tray  or oven proof dish, then lay the fish on top. Put the tarragon on top of  the fish and cover with the remaining alt mixture. Bake in the reheated  oven for 20 minutes. Remove, break the top layer of salt away and serve the  fish from it dish.
