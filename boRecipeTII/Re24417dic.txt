Tropical Dip for Fruit
Macadamia nuts; chop; toast, NORMA WRENN, Carton sour cream, Flaked coconut, Apricot preserves, 
Combine all ingredients; cover and chill.  Serve with assorted fresh fruit.  Yield: 1-3/4 cups.    Source:  Southern Living 1991 Annual Recipes - Oxmoor House  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #337 by "Deborah K?hnen"  <DEBKUHNEN@classic.msn.com> on Dec 29, 97
