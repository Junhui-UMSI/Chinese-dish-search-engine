Wasabi Sauce
Wasabi, Sugar, Eggs, White or rice vinegar, 
1. Combine wasabi & vinegar, let stand overnight.    2. Combine sugar & eggs, cook over double boiler until Napp .    refrigarate & use as needed.    NOTES : wasabi is a japanese horseradish, it comes in fresh, powder, or  paste form.  Recipe by: S.C.I.
