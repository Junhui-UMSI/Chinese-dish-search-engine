Aunt Graces Sugar Cookies
Flour and 4 heaping tsp., Cream of tartar, Powdered sugar, Margarine, Eggs, Granulated sugar, Vegetable oil, Baking soda, Salt, 
Cream sugars, margarine and oil until light and fluffy.  Add eggs and beat.  Add dry infredients.  Put on greased cookie sheet.  Press with glass dipped  in granulated sugar.  Bake at 375 for 10 minutes.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 29 October 96    Date:    Wed, 30 Oct 1996 22:16:16 -0500    From:    nancanne@SOJOURN.COM
