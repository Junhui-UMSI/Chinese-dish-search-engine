Apple Berry Salsa with Cinnamon Chips
Peel/core/chop, 10-inch flour tortillas, Strawberries -- hulled &, Water, Sugar, Chips:, Sliced, Cinnamon, Orange, Salsa:, Apple jelly, Granny Smith apples --, Kiwi -- peeled and chopped, Brown sugar, 
Preheat oven to 475. Lightly brush one side of tortillas with water.  Combine cinnamon and sugar, sprinkle over tortillas.  Cut each tortilla  into 8 wedges.  Place wedges on a stone or cookie sheet.  Bake 5 - 7  minutes or until golden brown.  Remove to cooling rack. While tortillas are  baking, zest orange (about 2 tbsp.) and juice orange (about 1/4 cup).  Combine prepared fruit, orange zest, orange juice, brown sugar and apple  jelly.  Serve fruit salsa with cinnamon chips.
