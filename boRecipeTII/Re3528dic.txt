Pecan Pleasers
Granola, Baking soda, Egg, Pecans, Shortening, Vanilla, Raisins, Flaked coconut, milk, Flour, Salt, Sesame seeds, Sugar, 
Combine pecans, coconut and sesame seeds. Heat in 350 F. oven until lightly  toasted. Cream shortening and sugar, beat in egg, vanilla and milk. Sift  together flour, soda and salt. Stir into egg mixture until blended. Stir in  granola, pecan mixture and raisins. Drop by teaspoons onto greased cookie  sheets. Bake in 375 F. oven for 15-20 minutes until lightly browned.
