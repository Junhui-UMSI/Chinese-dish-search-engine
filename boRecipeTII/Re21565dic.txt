Apple Pudding
Matzo meal, Sugar, White raisins, Juice of 1/2 lemon, Eggs, Med. size cooking apples, 
Peel, core and grate apples.  Sprinkle with lemon juice. Beat yolks until  light.  Gradually beat in sugar, continuing to beat until thick. Stir in  lemon juice, apples, matzo meal and raisins. Beat egg whites in separate  bowl until stiff but not dry.  Fold whites into batter and pour into 2 1/2  qt. greased casserole. Bake at 350 F. for 40 min. Serve hot.
