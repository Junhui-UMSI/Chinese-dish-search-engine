Quick Chicken and Dumplings
Bisquick original baking mix, Frozen vegetables, milk, (10.75-oz) condensed creamy, Cut-up cooked chicken, milk, 
HEAT 1-1/2 cups milk, vegetables, chicken and soup to boiling in 3-quart  saucepan.     MIX baking mix and 1/3 cup milk until soft dough forms. Drop dough by 8  spoonfuls onto chicken mixture (do not drop directly into liquid).     COOK uncovered over low heat 10 minutes.  Cover and cook 10 minutes  longer. 4 servings.
