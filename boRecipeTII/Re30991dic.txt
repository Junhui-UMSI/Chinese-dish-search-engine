Banana Nut Bread 4
Lowfat Margarine; **Note,, Nonfat Milk, Flour, Bananas; Mashed, Baking Powder, Salt, Nuts; ***Note, Chopped, Egg Beaters? 99% Egg, Sugar; *Note, 
*NOTE:  Original recipe used 1 C    **NOTE:  Original recipe used 2 T shortening    ***NOTE:  Original recipe used the nuts...I omitted    Preheat oven to 350 deg F.    Spray nonstick baking spray in 9 x 5 x 3" loaf pan.  Mix sugar, lowfat  margarine (shortening),  egg and mashed bananas thoroughly.  Stir in milk.  Measure flour by dip-level-pour method or by sifting.  Blend dry  ingredients; stir in.  Blend in nuts.  Pour into pan.  Bake 60 - 70  minutes, or until toothpick stuck into center comes out clean. (Crack in  top of loaf is characteristic.) Cool thoroughly before slicing with a thin  sharp knife.    This was absolutely wonderful!!
