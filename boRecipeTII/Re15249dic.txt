Whiskey Sauce
Bourbon, Egg, Sugar, Butter or margarine, 
1. Place butter in a 2-cup glass measure or microwave-safe dish. Microwave  on 100% power 30 to 45 seconds to melt butter.    2. Stir sugar and egg into butter. Microwave on 50% power until sugar  dissolves and sauce thickens (2 to 3 minutes), stirring once during  cooking. Cool slightly, then stir in bourbon.    Makes 1 cup.    * Timesaver Tip: Recipe may be made ahead and reheated gently in microwave  oven or in a saucepan over low heat.
