Apple Peach Apricot Chutney
Dried apricots, Red chili pepper, Dried peaches, Golden raisins, Garlic, Wine vinegar, Green apples, Salt, Sugar, 
Combine all ingredients in sauce pan and bring to boil. Reduce heat and  simmer for 30 minutes.    Place in food processer and lightly process so that there are chunks about      1/2    inch in size.    Place in glass container and let stand in refrigerator for at least 2 days.
