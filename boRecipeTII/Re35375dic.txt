Beef Cassoulet with Herb Dumplings
Lean or regular ground beef, milk, garlic, Dried oregano leaves, Stalk celery; thinly sliced, Bisquick original baking mix, Dried basil leaves, Dried oregano leaves, Dried basil leaves, (16-oz) Navy or kidney, (16-oz) whole tomatoes;, Water, Onion; chopped (about 1/2, (14.33-oz) ready-to-serve, 
COOK ground beef in Dutch oven, stirring freequently, until brown; drain.  Stir in water, 1 teaspoon oregano, 1/2 teaspoon basil, the garlic, celery,  onion, beans, broth and tomatoes; break up tomatoes. Heat to boiling;  reduce heat.     MIX remaining ingredients until soft dough forms. Drop dough by 12  spoonfuls onto beef mixture (do not drop directly into liquid).     COOK uncovered over low heat 10 minutes. Cover and cook 10 minutes longer.        6    servings.    From <Betty Crocker: Bisquick Classics and New Favorites>.  Downloaded from  G Internet, G Internet.
