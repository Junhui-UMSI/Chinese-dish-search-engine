Poppyseed Dressing for Fruit Salad
Red wine vinegar, Poppy seeds, Dry mustard, Onion, Celery seeds, Sugar, Salt, Salad oil, 
Mix together sugar, salt, mustard, poppy seeds, celery seeds and grated  onion. Stir in vinegar. With electric beater going, add oil a little at a  time until blended and thickened. Refrigerate. Pour over fresh fruit salad.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #600 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  May 06, 1997
