Pork Chops with Lemon Dill Butter
Margarine, Rib or loin pork chops; 1", Dill weed, Lemon juice, Clove garlic; sliced, 
Place chops on grill or in broiler pan. Brush with Lemon Dill Butter. Broil  for 30 minutes, basting often.    Lemon Dill Butter: Melt margarine in Microwave pan or saucepan. |Add lemon  juice, dill weed and garlic.    NOTES : A real favorite for the grill.
