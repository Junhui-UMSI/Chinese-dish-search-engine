Chicken Pizzaiola
Chicken breast, boneless,, olive oil, salt and pepper, Oregano, Parsley, Can plum tomatoes, crushed, Garlic cloves, sliced, 
In a large fry pan qith a tight fitting lid, brown chicken in oil on both  sides, seasoning with salt and pepper.    Combine all other ingredients and pour over browned chicken breast; cover  and cook over low heat for 45 minutes, adding water if needed.
