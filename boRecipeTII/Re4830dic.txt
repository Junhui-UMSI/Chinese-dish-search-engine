Pick-Me-Up Soup
Beef or chicken stock, White dry wine (even cooking, Heavy Cream, (generous) mixed herbs, 
Heat stock and herbs and bring to boil. Add wine, quickly bring to boil and  immediately lower flame. Add cream, stirring constantly for about 2 mins.  Nice with croutons or toast. Serves 2.
