Quick Hot Fudge
Vanilla extract, Sweet butter, Light corn syrup, Evaporated milk, Sugar, Unsweetened cocoa, Sour cream, 
Combine cocoa, sugar, corn syrup, milk, and sour cream in heavy saucepan.  Heat over medium flame, stirring constantly, until mixture begins to boil.  Immediately turn off heat; stir in butter and vanilla. Serve warm. Makes  enough for 4 good-size hot fudge sundaes.    From:  "Square Meals Cookbook", Courtesy Debbie Carlson - Cooking Echo
