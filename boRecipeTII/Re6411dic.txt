Plum Duff Cake
Flour, Cinnamon, milk, Sugar, Baking Powder, Salt, Nutmeg, Wild Plums; Cooked, Shortening, Baking soda, Egg, 
Sift dry ingredients together. Set aside. Cream shortening and sugar until  fluffy. Add eggs and beat thorough- ly. Add dry ingredients alternately  with milk and plums. Bake at 350 degrees until cake tests done (springs  back when touched with finger). Cool.
