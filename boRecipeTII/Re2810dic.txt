Peanut Butter Cookies with Crunch
Margarine, Baking Powder, Sugar -- packed, Peanut Butter, Chunky, Vanilla, Flour, Whole-Grain Wheat, Granola, Eggs, 
1. Cream margarine, peanut butter, sugars thoroughly together. 2. Add eggs  and vanilla and beat well. 3. Mix in flour and baking powder, then add  granola. 4. Drop dough from teaspoon onto greased cookie sheets. Flatten  each cookie in crisscross pattern with a fork dipped in flour. 5. Bake at  350? F. until lightly browned, about 10 minutes.    1 cup of Raisins may be added if desired.
