Heath Bar Cake
Packed light brown sugar, Sugar, Unsalted butter, Egg, Buttermilk, Pure vanilla extract, Baking soda, Heath Bars, frozen and, Pecans, All-Purpose Flour, 
Grease and flour a 10-inch cake pan.  In a mixing bowl, cream the sugars  and butter.  When well mixed, beat in the egg and slowly add the  buttermilk.  Beat in the vanilla, flour, and baking soda.  Pour into the  prepared pan.  Sprinkle the candy and nuts over the top.    Bake in a preheated 350-degree oven for 45 minutes, or until a cake tester  inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool on a wire rack.    Makes 8 servings.    Nutrient Values per Serving: 542 Calories, 18 g Fat, 8 g Saturated Fat, 5 g  Protein, 63 g Carbohydrate, 68 mg Cholesterol, 154 mg Sodium.
