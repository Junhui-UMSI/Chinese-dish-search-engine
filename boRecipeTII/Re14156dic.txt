White Fruit Salad
Cream, whipped, Egg yolks, milk, Fruit juice, Lemons, juice of, Miniature marshmallows, crushed pineapple, unflavored gelatin, blanched almonds, white cherries, 
Beat egg yolks; add milk and scald in top of double boiler. Dissolve  gelatin in fruit juice and add to milk and egg mixture. Cool and add  remaining ingredients. Let sit in refrigerator for several hours. Yield: 10  servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.
