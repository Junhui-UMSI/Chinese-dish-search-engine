Potato Leek Soup 2
Thick Leeks (White And Light, Water, Chives (About 1/2 Cup);, Sprigs thyme; tied together, Bay leaf, Pepper, Yukon Gold potatoes (abt 3);, Salt, 
Place all ingredients except chives into medium saucepan and bring to a  simmer. Cook for 20-30 minutes or until potatoes can be easily smashed  against side of pot with wooden spoon. Remove thyme and bay leaf and puree  with immersion blender until very smooth. Serve in bowls with chives  sprinkled on top.
