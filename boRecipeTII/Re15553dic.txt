7-Grain Vegetable Soup
Sea salt; to taste, Green beans & juice, AM 7 Grain Cereal (uncooked), Garlic powder, Whole kernel corn & juice, INTO A LARGE KETTLE PUT, Jar sliced mushrooms, Water, BRING TO A BOIL, THEN ADD, Tomatoes & juice, Celery stalks; chopped, Celery seed, Onion powder, Squash, cubed, 
Bring to boil and simmer, covered for 1 hour.  Add 1 tablespoon of AM  Canola Oil before serving, and a handful of chopped parsley.  Any other  vegetables of choice may be added or substituted.
