Ten Minute Rice
olive oil, Long grain white rice, Salt and black pepper, 
In a large pot, bring 2 to 3 quarts of water to a boil. Add rice and boil,  uncovered, about 10 minutes. Test a grain; rice should be cooked through,  but not soft. Drain into colander. Run hot water through rice with a fork.  Mix in oil and salt and pepper to taste.
