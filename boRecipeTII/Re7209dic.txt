Pork and Rice Casserole
Sausage, Onion, Green pepper; chopped, Raw rice, (small) chicken broth, Sausage links with soy, Celery, Soy oil, 
Brown sausage; saute onion, celery and bell pepper in oil. Add rice and  broth. Put into casserole and bake 45 minutes.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
