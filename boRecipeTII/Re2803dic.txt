Peanut Butter Cookies #10
(375 mL) flour, (60 mL) margarine, (30 mL) granulated sugar, Egg, (60 mL) water, (2 mL) baking powder, (60 mL) creamy peanut butter, (5 mL) baking soda, (5 mL) vanilla extract, 
From: kyoung@prstorm.bison.mb.ca (kyoung)    Date: Thu, 26 Aug 93 10:52:09 +0200  Source: Diabetic Candy, Cookie & Dessert Cookbook by Mary Jane Finsand    Cream together margarine, peanut butter and sugar replacements. add egg,  water and vanilla, beating until fluffy. Combine flour, baking soda and  baking powder in sifter and sift dry ingredients into creamed mixture. Stir  to blend completely. Chill thoroughly, at least 2 hours or overnight. Drop  by teaspoonfuls onto lightly greased cookie sheets, 2 to 3 in. (5 to 7 cm)  apart. Press flat with a floured bottom of a small glass. Bake at 375  degrees F (190 degrees C) for 12 to 15 minutes.
