Twice-Cooked Divinity
Dash salt, Corn syrup, Water, Sugar, Stiffly beaten egg whites, Vanilla, 
Stir sugar into syrup, water and salt until dissolved; boil to medium ball  stage or 240-degrees. Slowly pour 1/3 of mixture over egg whites, beating  constantly. Cook remaining syrup to very hard ball stage or 265 degrees.  Beat syrup into candy mixture. Continue beating until mixture holds its  shape; stir in vanilla. Drop from teaspoon onto greased cookie sheet; swirl  each piece to a peak.
