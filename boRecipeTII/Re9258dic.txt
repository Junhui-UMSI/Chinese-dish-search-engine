Pudding for the Birds
Peanut butter, Lard or bacon grease, Uncooked oatmeal, 
Mix up a nice yukky mess of the lard, peanut butter and uncooked oatmeal  (as much oatmeal as you can add without making the pudding too dry).  Collect pine cones and stick them in the pudding then hang them from tree  branches.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 19, 1998
