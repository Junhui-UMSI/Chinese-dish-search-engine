Banana Breeze Pie
Granulated sugar, Crust, Corn flake crumbs, FILLING, Fat-free sweetened condensed, Lemon juice, Lemon juice, Vegetable oil, Bananas; sliced, Pure vanilla extract, Cinnamon, Fat-free cream cheese;, Bananas; sliced, 
submitted by: matejka@bga.com (Anita A. Matejka, Texas, USA)    Recipe By: The Mr. Food Cookbook  In a mixing bowl, combine oil, sugar, cinnamon, and corn flake crumbs.  Press into bottom of a 9" pie pan; chill. Meanwhile, in another mixing  bowl, combine cream cheese, sweetened condensed milk, 1/3 cup lemon juice,  and vanilla extract. Line 3 bananas onto bottom of chilled crust. Turn  filling into crust. Refrigerate for 3 hours or until firm. Dip remaining  bananas in remaining lemon juice. Arrange banana slices over top of pie.
