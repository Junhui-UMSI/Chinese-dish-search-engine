Thumbprint Cookies
Vanilla, Jelly, Salt, Shortening, Flour *, Brown sugar, Margarine, Nuts, Egg, 
*     Use Unbleached All-purpose or whole wheat flour in these cookies.  ~------------------------------------------------------ ~-----------------  Heat oven to 375 degrees F.  Mix Brown sugar, margarine, shortening, egg  yolks, and vanilla. Stir in flour and salt. Shape dough into 1 inch balls.  Beat egg white slightly.  Dip balls into egg white; roll in nuts. Place  about 1 inch apart on ungreased cookie sheet; press thumb deeply in center  of each.  Bake until light brown, about 10 minutes. Immediately remove from  cookie sheet; cool.  Fill thumbprints with jelly. Makes about 3 dozen.
