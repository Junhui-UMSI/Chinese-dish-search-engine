Peanut-Chile Sauce
Juice of 1/2 lime, Garlic, Peanut butter, Dark brown sugar, Soy sauce, Removed, roasted and peeled, Ginger, minced, Green serano chile, seeds, Piquins, or crushed red, Broth, Pepper, seeds included, Scallions, chopped,, White part only, Peanut oil, 
Saute the green onion, garlic and ginger in the oil, 3 to 4 minutes, until  soft but not browned.  (I used the micro.)    Add the stock and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and stir in the remaining  ingredients.  Simmer until thickened (5 minutes in micro, or 10 on stove).
