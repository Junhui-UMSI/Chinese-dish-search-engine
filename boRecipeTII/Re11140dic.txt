Raisin Butterscotch Pie
Sour cream, banana, Frozen whipped topping, milk, Instant butterscotch pudding mix, Raisins, Instant vanilla pudding mix, Pastry shell Baked, 
In a mixing bowl, combine pudding mixes and milk; beat until thick, about 3  minutes.  Fold in sour cream; stir in raisins.  Place banana slices in  pastry shell; top with pudding mixture.  Chill until ready to serve.  Garnish with whipped topping.  Yield 6 - 8 servings. Source: Taste of Home  ch.
