Bbq Beef Brisket
Celery seed, Ground pepper, Liquid smoke, Garlic salt, Worcestershire, Barbecue sauce, Beef Brisket, 
Preparation : Cover both sides of brisket with Liquid Smoke, garlic, onion,  celery seed and Worcestershire sauce. Wrap in foil and place in crock pot.  Cook on low for 8-10 hours. Remove and slice across grain. Return to pot,  add barbecue sauce and cook 1 hour.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 624 by molony <molony@scsn.net>  on Jan 28, 1998
