Vanilla Ice Cream #5
Gelatin, Evaporated milk; chilled, Vanilla, Hot water, Sugar equivalent sweetener, 
Dissolve gelatin in hot water.  Whisk the milk and almost cold gelatin  together and add the flavoring and sweetener.    Pour into freezing trays and freeze without stirring.  Serve with fruit.    8 servings each 60 cal, 5 g (1/2 unit) carbohydrate, 4 g protein, 3 g fat    Source:  The Diabetics Cookbook by Roberta Longstaff & Jim Mann 1984  Shared but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier July 1993    Date: Thu, 20 Jun 1996 20:07:54 -0500    From: pickell@cyberspc.mb.ca (S.Pickell)    MM-Recipes Digest V3 #172    From the MealMaster recipe list.
