Tomatillo Sauce
Serrano Chiles; Canned *, Tomatillos; Cut Into Halves, Fresh Cilantro; Snipped, Salt, Red Onion; chopped, 
*  Use 2 canned serrano chiles, rinsed and seeded or 1 fresh serrano     chile, seeded.  ~-------------------------------------------------------------------------  Place all ingredients in food processor workbowl fitted with steel blade or  in blender container, cover, and process until well blended. Makes about 1      1/4    cups sauce.    Miscellaneous recipes from the collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mo-misc.zip
