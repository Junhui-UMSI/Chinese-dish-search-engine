Broccoli Kugel
Frozen broccoli; (chopped), Rich Whip Pareve Heavy cream, Eggs, Bread crumbs, salt and pepper, Mushroom soup powder, 
Source: Ruti Rosen    Defrost and drain the broccoli well.    Beat the eggs. Add the rest, mix well and add to broccoli mixture.    Pour into greased baking pan or lined muffin tins and bake until golden on  top (approximately 1/2 hour) in a moderate oven.
