Barbequed Spoonburgers
Catsup, 6 Oz Can tomato paste, Celery; diced, Ground beef, Pepper, Onion, Paprika, Shortening, Green pepper; diced, White vinegar, Garlic, Salt, Brown sugar, Dry mustard, Worcestershire Sauce, Water, 
Brown beef in shortening; drain. Put all ingredients in slow cooker, stir  well. Cover, cook on low for 6-8 hours. Serve on toasted buns or over  noodles or rice.
