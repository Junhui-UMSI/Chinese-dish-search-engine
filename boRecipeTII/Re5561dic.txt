Pineapple Pecan Bars
Powdered sugar, Crust, Coarsely chopped pecans, All-purpose flour, Eggs, PINEAPPLE TOPPING, DOLE Crushed Pineapple in, Margarine, All-purpose flour, Brown Sugar, 
FOR CRUST, combine 2 cups flour and powdered sugar. Cut in margarine until  mixture is crumbly. Press onto bottom of 13x9" baking pan. Bake in 350F.  oven 15 minutes. Remove from oven.    FOR TOPPING, combine drained pineapple, eggs, brown sugar and 1/3 cup  flour. Stir in pecans. Pour over partially baked crust. Bake in 350F.  oven 30-35 minutes or until set. Cool completely. Cut into bars.    Makes 32 bars.    From Favorite All Time Recipes - Homemade Holiday Cookies, Publication  International, Ltd., ISBN 0-7853-0147-X.
