Vegetarian Sloppy Joes
Mexican-style pinto beans , Whole grain buns, Hickory smoke barbecue sauce, Vegetable oil, brown rice, Green pepper, Onion, 
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Add onions and green  pepepr; cook 2 to 3 minutes.  Add rice, beans and barbecue sauce. Simmer 10  to 15 minutes, until heated through. Serve on buns.    Each serving provides: * 542 calories * 19.2 g. protein * 9.4 g. fat * 96.4  g. carbohydrates * 3.2 g. dietary fiber * 17 mg. cholesterol * 1637 mg.  sodium.
