Tabuli Salad
juice of 3 lemons, parsley, Tomatoes, Salad oil, olive oil, Salt to taste, Fresh mint leaves, Sugar, green onions, Cracked wheat, 
Clean, wash & chop vegetables. Wash & soak wheat for 10 minutes. Squeeze  water from wheat. Add all vegetables, oils, lemon juice, sugar & salt. Mix  well. This recipe is better if prepared a day in advance so flavors can  melt together.    MRS C.B. (JUDY) MAPES    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
