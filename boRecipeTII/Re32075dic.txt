Barbecued Chicken (Prep B
-, CHICKEN;WHOLE FZ, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH ROASTING PAN                  TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  WASH CHICKEN THOROUGHLY UNDER COLD RUNNING WATER. DRAIN WELL. PLACE IN  PANS, COVER; SET ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 2.    2.  DIP CHICKEN IN SAUCE TO COAT WELL; PLACE CHICKEN SKIN SIDE UP IN PANS.    3.  BAKE 1 1/4 HOURS OR UNTIL DONE (180F.)    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 1, 65 LB CHICKEN, BROILER-FRYER, CUT-UP OR 82 LB  CHICKEN,  BROILER-FRYER, QUARTERED MAY BE USED.    NOTE:  2.  OTHER SIZES AND TYPES OF PANS MAY BE USED. SEE RECIPE NO.  A02500.    NOTE:  3.  IN STEP 4, IF CONVECTION OVEN IS USED, BAKE AT 350F., 30 MINUTES  OR UNTIL DONE (180F.) ON LOW FAN, CLOSED VENT.
