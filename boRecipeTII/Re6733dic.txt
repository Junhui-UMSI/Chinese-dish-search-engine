Polish Mushrooms with Sour Cream
milk, Mushrooms -- sliced, Flour -- minced, Sour cream, Butter or margarine, Onion -- chopped, Pepper, Salt, Paprika, 
In melted butter, in large skillet, saute onion till golden.  Sprinkle=20  with flour; add milk and 3/4 cup sour cream; while stirring, bring just to=  =20 a simmer. Then add sliced mushrooms, salt, pepper, and paprika.  Simmer,=20 covered, 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in remaining  sour cream ;= =20 heat thoroughly, while stirring constantly.
