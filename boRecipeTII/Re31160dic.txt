Banana Supreme Ice Cream
Heavy cream, Cold water, Banana pulp, Salt, milk, Egg, Lemon juice, Nutmeg, Powdered gelatin, 
Beat eggs and sugar until light; add salt, nutmeg, milk and gelatin which  has been softened in the cold water and dissolved over a pan of hot water.  Crush bananas with a silver fork to a fine pulp, stir in lemon juice and  add to mixture. Whip cream and add. Freeze. Makes 2 quarts.
