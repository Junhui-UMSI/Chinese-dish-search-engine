Barbaras Scotch Scones
Currants, Flour, Shortening, milk, Baking Powder, Eggs, Sugar, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 350 F. Sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Add  currants. Add shortening and mix in lightly. Beat eggs until light. Add  milk to eggs and add slowly to mixture. Roll 1/2 inch thick on floured  board. Cut in 2 inch squares and fold them over, making them 3 cornered.  Brush with milk and dust with sugar. Bake on greased tray 25 minutes.
