Zuppa Di Fagioli Con la Pasta
Dry white beans (Great, Onion; chopp, Celery; minced, Spaghetti; broken, Ham; cooked and cubed, olive oil, Salt, Water, Parmesan cheese, Garlic; minced, Pepper, 
Combine beans and water in a large pot and bring to a boil. Remove from  heat and let stand 1 hour.  Return to medium heat and add olive oil, ham,  onion, celery, garlic, salt, and pepper. Cook 3 hours. Then add spaghetti  and simmer 10 to 15 minutes until tender. Serve in soup bowl and top with  generous spoonfuls of Parmesan cheese. Serves 4 to 6.
