Wine Drops
Baking soda, Raisins, Sugar, Flour; (1 lb. 5 oz.), Molassas, Egg, Cinnamon, Cloves, Sweet milk, Salt, Shortening, 
Mix all ingredients in order. Drop by teaspoonfull to cookie sheet.    Bake at 350F. for 15 minutes.    Recipe by: Gladys Morehouse    Posted to recipelu-digest by Nesb2@aol.com on Feb 5, 1998
