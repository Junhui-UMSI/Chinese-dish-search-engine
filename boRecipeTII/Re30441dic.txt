Banana Jumbles
Salt, Old-fashioned oats, Brown sugar, Creamy peanut butter, Margarine, softened, Flour, Raisins, Baking Powder, Egg, Extra-ripe bananas, peeled, 
Mash bananas with fork to measure 1 cup.  Beat brown sugar, peanut butter,  margarine.  Stir in egg and mashed bananas; beat to blend thoroughly.  Combine oats, flour, baking powder, salt. Stir into banana mix. Add  raisins; blend well.  Drop by heaping tablespoons onto greased (or coated  with vegetable spray) cookie sheets. Shape cookies with back of spoon. Bake  at 375 degrees for 12 to 14 minutes, until browned. Cool on wire racks.  Makes 18 large cookies. Preparation time: 15 minutes Baking time: 12 to 14  minutes
