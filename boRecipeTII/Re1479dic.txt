Fish n Cuke Appetizers
Smoked fish, Sour creme, Chives, Tarragon Vinegar, Dill weed, Salt, Cucumber slices, Pimento strips; (optional, 
Recipe By : Ted Dahlen "Smoke Seafood"    Remove skin and bones from fish. Flake the fish and combine with other  ingredients, except cucumbers. Score cucumbers with fork and slice in  rounds. Chill in ice water. At serving time drain and put a dab of fish  mixture on each cuke. Decorate with pimento if desired.
