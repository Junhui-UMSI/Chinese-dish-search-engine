Beef with Ginger
Ginger root, Garlic, Canola oil, Soy sauce, Beef eye of round, Dry white wine, 
Make slashes in roast and tuck garlic or ginger into each one. Combne oil,  soy sauce and wine. Put roast in a plastic bag and cover with wine mixture.  Keep in fridge 8 hours or overnight. Remove roast from bag and place in a  greased shallow roastin pan. Place in a preheated 450 degree oven for 30  mins., 45 mins. if you are using a larger roast. Turn off heat, leaving  roast in the oven for an additional 20 mins. Remove from oven and let stand       10    mins. before slicing.
