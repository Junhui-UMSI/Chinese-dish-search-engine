Pumpkin Cake Three Rivers
nuts, PUMPKIN MIXTURE, Brown sugar, Ginger, Sugar, Ground cloves, pumpkin, Egg, CAKE MIXTURE, TOPPING, yellow cake mix reserve, Butter, Eggs, Cinnamon, Butter, Evaporated milk, Sugar, Cake mix, 
Blend butter adn egg into cake mix and press into greased and floured  9x12x2 in pan.  Use the back of a spoon to pat this mixture evenly over the  bottom and 1 " up the sides of the pan. Gently mix pumpkin, eggs, sugars,  milk and seasonings. Pour over unbaked mixture Blend reserved cake mix with  sugar and butter. Roll inot walnut sized balls and place over the pumpkin  mixture. Sprinkle with nuts if desired. Bake 350 for 55 minutes. Can be  frozen Mrs. Jame Rankin Duncan Jr. Three Rivers Cookbook 2 ( I had to add  flour to the mix to make the balls.
