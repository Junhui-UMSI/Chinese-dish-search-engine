Zucchini Scramble
Zucchini -- about 1lb, Butter or margarine, Tomato Wedges -- optional, 3 zucchini, Cheddar cheese, Salt And Pepper -- to taste, Onion -- chopped, Eggs -- beaten (6 -8), Sliced, 
In a skillet, saute zucchini and onion in butter until tender. Season with  salt and pepper. Add the eggs; cook and stir until set. Sprinkle with  cheese.  Remove from heat; cover until cheese melts. Serve with tomato wedges , if  desired. Makes 4 servings.    Submitted by Betty Claycomb, Penn.
