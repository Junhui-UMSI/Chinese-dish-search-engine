Beef Taco Salad
Tomato; fresh,chop, Ground beef, Cheddar cheese, shredded, Onion; diced, Garlic, Black pepper, Lettuce; shredded, Chili peppers; diced, Salt, 
Recipe by: Jo Anne Merrill, recipe from Del Mar Ca Brown meat in skillet  over medium heat; drain well. Put tomato, onion, garlic, salt, black pepper  and chili peppers in blender; chop fine but do not puree. Use chili peppers  of your choice, hot or mild. Add mixture to browned meat, simmer until all  liquid is absorbed; cool. Place meat mixture on shredded lettuce and add  cheese to garnish.
