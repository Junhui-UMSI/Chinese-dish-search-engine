Egg Bagels #2
Yeast, Warm water, Eggs, Salt, Sugar, Salad oil, Flour, 
Mix ingredients, then knead 10 minutes. Place in greased bowl, cover and  let rise in warm spot. When risen, punch down. Divide into 32 pieces. Let  rise 15 minutes. Drop bagels in boiling water. Boil 3 minutes. Place on  greased sheet; brush on egg yolk glaze. Bake at 425 degrees for 2 minutes.
