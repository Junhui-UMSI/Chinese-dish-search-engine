Pickled Ginger and Vegetables
Peeled onions; sliced finely, Sugar, Red beet; peeled and, Red peppercorns, White cabbage leaves, Peeled young ginger; sliced, Salt, Carrot batons, Rice wine vinegar, Daikon batons, 
In a saucepan, heat rice wine vinegar, sugar, red peppercorns and salt. Add  rest of ingredients and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let  stand until cool.
