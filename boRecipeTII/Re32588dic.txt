Sweet Milk Waffles Ii
Sugar, Sweet milk, Ready-mixed flour, shortening, Eggs, 
Sift flour, measure, and sift.  Beat egg yolks until light and foamy. Add  sugar and shortening.  Mix well. Add milk alternately with flour to egg  mixture.  Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Bake in hot waffle iron. 6  servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #131 by Tonya <imbri@earthlink.net> on May  11, 1997
