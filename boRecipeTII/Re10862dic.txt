Quinoa Tabooli
Whole lettuce leaves, Cooked AM Quinoa, Basil, Chopped parsley, Sea salt (optional), Pepper (optional), Lemon juice, AM Unrefined Olive Oil, Garlic clove; pressed, Chopped green onion, 
Mix first 9 ingredients and place in a salad bowl lined with washed  lettuce leaves.  Refrigerate for at least two hours to let flavor blend.  Fresh mint and black olives make nice garnishes for this dish.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "The Native Americans" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
