Quick Lemonaide Bread
Flour, Sugar, Eggs, milk, Shortening, Frozen lemonade concentrate, Baking Powder, 
Cream sugar and shortening, add all but the lemonade, Mix 1 Tablespoon of  the defrosted lemonade with remaining ingredients. Bake in a loaf pan at  350 for 50-60 min. When done pour 1/3 cup of the concentrate over it  allowing the concentrate to soak in. Hint: It will soak in better if you  poke holes in it with a fork.
