Pecan Pie #01
Eggs, Sugar, Pecans, Butter, Unbaked pie shell, White corn syrup, Vanilla, Lemon juice, 
Preheat oven to 350. Beat eggs lightly, then add next 5 ingredients. Place  pecans in pie shell. Pour in mixed ingredients. Bake at 350 for about 45  minutes.
