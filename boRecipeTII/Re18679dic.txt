Amish Sugar Cookies
Vanilla, Unsalted butter or margarine, Vegetable cooking oil, Granulated sugar, Eggs, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Cream of tartar, 
Combine butter or margarine, oil and sugars in a large mixing bowl; mix    well.  Add eggs, beat 1 minute until well blended.  Add vanilla, beat    well.  In separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda and cream of tartar; add to  creamed mixture, mixing well.  Drop by small teaspoon on ungreased cookie  sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes.
