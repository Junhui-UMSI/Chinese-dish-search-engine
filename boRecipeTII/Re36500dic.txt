Strawberries and Cream
Sugar, Strawberries, Cream, whipping, 
Place strawberries in a bowl.         Whip the cream and, when it begins to thicken, add the sugar.         Whip until quite thick and pipe through a pastry bag onto the  strawberries.         Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions       :    Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983       :    Chef Pierre Lacoste, Maison Pierre, Restaurant, New Orleans
