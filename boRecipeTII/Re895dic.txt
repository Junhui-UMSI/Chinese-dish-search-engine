Hopstreet Bistro Beer Cheese Soup
Yukon gold potatoes, Leek, Butter, Flour, Chicken Stock, Heavy whipping cream, Hopstreet Golden Light Ale, Cheddar Cheese, white pepper, parsley, fresh, yellow onion, Salt, 
In a large saucepan, boil potatoes til tender. Drain and set aside.    In a heavy pot or Dutch oven, saute onion and leek in butter til lightly  browned. Gradually add flour. Cook, stirring constantly, for 3 to 4 mins.  Do not allow flour to brown.    Slowly whisk in the chicken stock. Whisk in the whipping cream and ale.  Cook til thickened. Add the grated cheese all at once. Stir in the drained  potatoes and heat through. Add white pepper and salt to taste. Garnish  bowls of soup with chopped parsley.
