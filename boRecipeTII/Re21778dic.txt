Apple-Cinnamon Waffles with Cider Syrup
Bisquick original baking mix, Vegetable oil, Ground cinnamon, Sugar, Apple cider, margarine, milk, Ground cinnamon, Egg, , CIDER SYRUP, Lemon juice, Bisquick original baking mix, Sugar, 
PREPARE Cider Syrup; keep warm.  Heat waffle iron; grease if necessary.  Beat remaining ingredients except apple with wire whisk or hand beater  until smooth. Stir in apple.     POUR batter by scant 1 cupfuls onto center of hot waffle iron.     BAKE until steaming stops and waffle is golden brown. Carefully remove  waffle. Serve with syrup.  Twelve 4-inch squares.     Cider Syrup: Mix sugar, baking mix and cinnamon in 2-quart saucepan. Stir  in apple cider and lemon juice. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture  thickens and boils. Boil and stir 1 minute; remove from heat. Stir in  margarine.    From <Betty Crocker: Bisquick Classics and New Favorites>.  Downloaded from  G Internet, G Internet.
