Apple Crisp
Brown sugar, Flour, Apples(tart), sliced, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Oats, Margarine, 
preheat oven 375. Arrange apples in greased sq. pan, 8x8x2. Mix remaining  ingred; sprinkle over apples. Bake until apples tender and topping golden  brown,abt. 30 min.serve warm.    Recipe by: internet Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #635 by kriegz  <kriegz@bccom.com> on Jun 4, 1997
