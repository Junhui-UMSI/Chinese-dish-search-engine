Guasacaca Salsa
Avocado; peeled; pitted,, Tomatoes; peeled; seeded,, Onion; minced, Green pepper; minced, Hot chile; minced, olive oil, Cider vinegar, Salt, 
Chile Pepper magazine 10-96; adapted makes 1 1/4 c    Blend first 5 ingredients together to a puree. Add the oil and vinegar  alternately by spoonfuls with the blender motor running. The salsa should  have "the consistency of a milkshake just starting to melt." Correct  seasoning with salt.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Aug 09, 98,
