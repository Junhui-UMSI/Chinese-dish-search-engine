Peanut Butter-Chocolate Bon Bons
Margerine, Rice Krispies, Creamy peanut butter, Dipping chocolate, 
Blend margerine, peanut butter and confectioners sugar. Crush cereal  slightly in a plastic bag.     Add cereal to peanut butter mixture and mix well. Roll mixture into 3/4  inch balls. Chill for one hour.     Melt chocolate slowly in top of double boiler. Stir well. With a dipping  fork, dip each ball into the chocolate to coat. Place on wax paper to cool.     Makes 7-8 dozen.
