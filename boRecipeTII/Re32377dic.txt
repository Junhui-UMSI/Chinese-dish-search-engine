Barley Soup #1
Carrots; sliced, Onions; whole, Tomato juice, Fresh mushrooms, Barley, Beef; cubed, Ribs celery; sliced, 
Put meat and onions in soup kettle with water to cover. Simmer 1/2 hour.  Discard onions. Add vegetables, barley and tomato juice. Simmer until meat  is tender, about 1-1/2 hours. Brown mushroom slices in a small amount of  fat, but do not overcook. Add to soup. Salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.  Garnish with parsley. Makes 3 quarts. Freezes well.
