Strawberry Bread #1
Pecans, Flour, (small) frozen strawberries;, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Eggs; well beaten, Cinnamon, Salt, Baking soda, 
Sift together flour, soda, salt, cinnamon & sugar. Mix strawberries, eggs,  oil & nuts. Combine all ingredients & pour into 2 floured & greaswed 9-1/2  x 5-1/2 x 2-3/4 inch bread pans. Bake in 350 oven for 1 hour.    GAIL SUDDATH    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
