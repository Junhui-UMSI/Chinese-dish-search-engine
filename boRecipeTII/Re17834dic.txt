Almost Twinkie Cake
Vanilla instant pudding, Vanilla, Crisco, milk, Water, White cake mix, Eggs, Flour, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Salt, Margarine; softened, 
Combine cake mix, oil, eggs, pudding and water and 1 tsp vanilla. Beat at  medium speed for 2 minutes. Pour into a greased and floured 9 x 13 pan.  Bake at 350~ for 40-45 minutes or until cake tests done. In a saucepan mix  the flour with the milk. Cook until thick, stirring constantly; cool. Add  sugar, salt, crisco, margarine and 1 tsp vanilla. Beat until fluffy. Spread  on the cooled cake. Store in refrigerator.
