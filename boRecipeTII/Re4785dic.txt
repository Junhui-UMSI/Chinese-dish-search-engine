Picadillo De Papa Y Mostaza
Garlic, (large) mustard greens,, Mixed herbs (2 T fresh ones), Margarine (up to 3), Onion, Russet potatoes, chopped in, Hot chili pepper (optional)-, 
Saute the potatoes, garlic and the onion in margarine. Then put in the  mustard greens and cook it at low temp a while (15 min or so or until the  potatoes are cooked) with the top on, stirring occasionally.
