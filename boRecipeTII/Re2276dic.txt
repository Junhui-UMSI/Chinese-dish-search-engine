Banana Bread #3
Honey, Bread flour, Poppy seeds; (optional), Egg, Yeast, Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Salt, Wheat flour, Ripe bananas, -warm water, 
Follow the instructions for your machine as to which order you put in your  ingredients...make sure that if you set your machine on delayed timer that  you dont get your yeast wet or your bread will flop!
