Wheat Wheat Wheat Bread
Wheat germ, Yeast, Cracked wheat, Evaporated milk; (1 can), Salad oil, Boiling water, Ginger, Bread flour, Salt, Honey, Whole wheat flour, 
Ingredients should be at room temperature. Liquids (and warmed  ingredients) should be at 120-130 degrees.    Cover the 1/2 cracked wheat with the boiling water in a small bowl.  Stir  and let cool. Put the rest of ingredients in the order listed.
