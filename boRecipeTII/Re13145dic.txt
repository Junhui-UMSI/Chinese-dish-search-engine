Wonderful Layered Peach Dessert
Brown sugar, Almond flavoring, Butter, Whipped topping, Peaches, Powdered sugar, Pecans, Cream cheese, Plain flour, 
FOR CRUST: Combine flour, sugar and pecans. Add the butter. Mix well and  press mixture in bottom of 13 x 9 baking dish. Bake at 350F for 18-20  minutes. Cool. FOR FILLING: Beat cream cheese and powdered sugar at medium  speed until light and fluffy. Fold in whipped topping and almond flavoring.  Drain peaches and add to whipped topping mixture. Spoon over cooled crust  and chill for several hours.
