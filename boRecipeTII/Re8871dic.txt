Prawn and Vegetable Stir Fry
vegetables, mixed, stir fry, frozen, Sesame oil, Garlic, Ground coriander, Soy sauce, Vegetable oil, prawns, 
Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large frying pan until very hot. Add the  frozen vegetables, fry for 3 or 4 minutes, stirring frequently. Add the  prawns, garlic and coriander and cook for a further minute. Add the soya  sauce and sesame oil, stir well and cook for 1 minute. Serve immediately on  a bed of piping hot rice or noodles.
