Aunt Mamies Goulash Cake
(heaping) flour, Vegetable shortening, Butter, Light brown sugar, milk, (heaping) baking powder, Granulated sugar, Salt, Sugar, GOULASH ICING, Flour, Eggs; well beaten, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Grease and flour an 8" x 8" or 9" x 9" baking pan, or  casserole. Thoroughly mix all ingredients. Pour into prepared pan and bake  for approximately 25-30 minutes. Immediately cover with Icing (directions  follow) and place under broiler for approximately 5 minutes. Watch closely  so topping does not scorch. Remove from oven and allow to cool. Serve warm  with ice cream or whipped cream, if preferred.    Goulash Icing: Using a pastry cutter (or food processor) thoroughly mix  icing ingredients. Spread over the top of the hot cake. Place under the  broiler for approximately 5 minutes.    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #198 by "Peggy L. Makolondra"  <pmakolon@mail.wiscnet.net> on Aug 21, 1997
