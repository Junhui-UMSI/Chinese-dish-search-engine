Teriyaki Fondue
garlic, Ground ginger, Wine vinegar, Light brown sugar, Chinese leaves, Beef fillet steak, Spring onions, Soy sauce, Beansprout Salad-----, Sunflower oil, Dry sherry, Red bell pepper, Bean sprouts, 
Cut steak into think strips 1/2" wide and 4" long. Put 1 teaspoon of sugar  and 2 tablespoons of soy sauce into a bowl and set aside. In a large bowl  combine remaining sugar and soy sauce sherry garlic and ginger. Add strips  of meat and leave to marinate for 1 hour. Weave the strips of meat onto  20-24 bamboo skewer ready for cooking in the hot oil. To prepare the sald  shred the Chinese leaves and put into a bowl with beansprouts pepper  (capsicum) and spring onions. Add oil to reserved sugar and soy sauce then  whisk in vinegar and pour over salas. Toss lightly together.
