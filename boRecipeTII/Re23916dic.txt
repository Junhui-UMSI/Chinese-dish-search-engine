Asparagus and Ham Topped Potatoes
Hard boiled eggs; sliced, Hot cooked asparagus, Russet potatoes; baked, Toasted sliced almonds;, Ham; cut in julienne strips, 
Slit hot potatoes lengthwise and open by gently squeezing from the bottom.  Top hot potatoes with ham, asparagus and sliced eggs. Spoon sauce over top  and garnish with almonds.    CREAMY MUSTARD SAUCE:2 tbsp. butter 3 tbsp. flour 1 tsp. dry mustard 1/2  tsp. salt 1/8 tsp. white pepper 2 c. milk. Melt butter in saucepan and stir  in flour, mustard and salt and pepper. Mix thoroughly. Gradually stir in  milk. Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Makes 2 cups.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 16,  1998
