Apricot Pound Cake
Salt, Unsalted butter -- softened, Eggs, Vanilla extract, Granulated sugar, All-purpose flour, Sour cream, Dark rum, Apricot brandy, Baking soda, 
In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Beat until mixture is light and  fluffy.  Add eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in  sour cream, brandy, vanilla, and rum. In another bowl, sift together the  flour, salt, and baking soda.  Stir the flour mixture into the butter and  egg mixture. Pour batter into buttered Bundt pan and bake for 1 hour at  325^, or until cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool the  cake in the pan on a rack for 1 hour. Then, turn it out of the pan on a  rack to cool completely. ~Diana Rattray    Recipe By     : Dining on Deck
