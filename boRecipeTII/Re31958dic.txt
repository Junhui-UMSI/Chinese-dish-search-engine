Taco Compuesto for Diabetics
Garlic, Taco shells, Tomato, fresh, green chili peppers, canned, Taco sauce, Chili powder, Lettuce, Onion, Italian salad dressing, Low-cal Prosse cheese, Lean ground beef, Seasoned salt, 
Date: 05 Mar 1996 22:40 GMT    From: "Lisabeth Crawford (Pooh)" <104105.1416@compuserve.com>  In a large skillet cook ground beef, chopped onions, green chili pepper;  and minced garlic over medium heat till beef is brown. Drain off fat. Stir  in chili powder.    In a mixing bowl combine chopped tomato, Italian salad dressing, and  seasoned salt. ( Salt is Salt no matter what) Spoon beef mixture into taco  shells. Top with tomato mixture, shredded lettuce, shredded cheese, and  taco sauce. Makes 4 servings. Time 20 min.
