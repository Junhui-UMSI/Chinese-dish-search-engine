Apple-Mallow Yam Yums
Nuts -- chopped, Canned yams, Apples -- sliced, Butter, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, (optional), Miniature marshmallows, 
Toss apples and nuts; combine brown sugar and cinnamon.  Alternate layers  of apples and yams in 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.  Dot with butter.  Bake  at 350 F for 35 to 40 minutes.  Serves 6 to 8.    Optional:  sprinkle with marshmallows.  Broil till lightly browned.    Recipe By     : Elizabeth A.
