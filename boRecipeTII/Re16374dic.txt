Wedding Mints
Butter, Butter flavoring, Whipping cream, Confectioner's sugar, Peppermint oil, 
Leave butter at room temperature until soft. Heat cream until warm. Mix all  ingredients by hand. It will be stiff so you may have to use hands to knead  until smooth. Add color as desired and continue to mix by kneading. Press  into mint molds to form. Remove from forms. Place on wax paper and let set  overnight to dry. Turn over and let other side dry. Store in covered  container. These keep a long time and freeze well  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 344 by "Lynn Ratcliffe"  <mcgrew@ntr.net> on Dec 6, 1997
