Pear Cranberry Chutney
Fresh pears, Cider vinegar, Sugar, Fresh ginger root, Fresh cranberries, Cloves, Cinnamon, 
Mix all ingredients in a 2 quart saucepan. Heat to boiling, reduce heat to  low. Cook about 1 hour, stirring frequently until thickened. Cool slightly.    Cover and refrigerate for up to 2 weeks. Serve at room temperature.
