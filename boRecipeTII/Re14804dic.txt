100% WW Raisin Bread
Whole wheat flour, Salt, Dry yeast, Raisins, Molasses, Applesauce; Or Low Fat, Water, Nonfat Dry Milk Powder, 
Directions: Put ingredients into kettle in the order recommended by your  Breadmaking Machine. For Panasonic, drop raisins in after first kneading  while dough is resting. May be baked Timed or Rapid Bake.    NOTES : Cal  102.6, Fat 0.5g, Carb 22.5g, Fib 3.3g, Pro 3.8g, Sod 205mg,  CFF  4.1%.
