Boboli California Pizza
Avocado, peeled, sliced, Cheddar cheese, shredded, Bobolis OR 4 sm, Alfalfa sprouts, Sour cream, Ripe olives, Bacon, cook, crumble, 
Sprinkle cheese and olives over Bobolis.  Bake at 450~ for 10 minutes. Top  with avocado and sprouts. Garnish with sour cream and bacon.    From: Dan Klepach                     Date: 04-28 Cooking    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Aug 30, 98
