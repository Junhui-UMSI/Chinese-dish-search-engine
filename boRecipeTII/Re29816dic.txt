Ten Minute Dinner
Onion, Cream of mushroom soup, Cream of chicken soup, Chow mein, Beef, ground, Veg-all, drained, 
Brown beef and onion; add both soups, Veg-all and 3/4 can of chow mein  noodles. Pour into cassrole dish. Top with remaining chow mein noodles.  Bake at 400 F. for 10 min. OR until hot. SERVE AND ENJOY!!! * Golden  Mushroom with Sliced Mushrooms Soup can be substituted for the Cream of  Mushroom Soup.
