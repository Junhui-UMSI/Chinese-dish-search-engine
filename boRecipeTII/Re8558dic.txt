Potatoes Duchesse #2
Butter or margarine, egg yolks, Potatoes, Salt and white pepper, Nutmeg, 
Preheat oven 400 degrees . Pass potatoes through a food mill or ricer. Add  butter, seasonings, and egg yolks while potatoes are hot. Put the mixture  in a pastry bag with a star tube. Squeeze out onto cookie sheet into  desired shapes. You may brush lightly with egg wash for greater browning.  Bake 20-25 minutes until golden brown. Serve at once.
