Peanut Butter Marinated Ribs
Crushed red pepper, Pork ribs, Lemon juice, Garlic, Soy sauce, Minced ginger, Honey, Peanut butter, 
In bowl, combine peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, lemon juice, ginger,  garlic and red pepper; stir to blend.  Place ribs is shallow baking pan; pour marinade evenly over meat.  Cover and refrigerate 6 to 8 hours, turning meat once.  Prepare grill for cooking.  Wipe excess marinade from meat.  Cook  turning frequently, 45-60 minutes.  Source: Womans Day Meals in  Minutes.
