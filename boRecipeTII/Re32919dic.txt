Sweet Sauce for Spring Rolls
Salt, Cornstarch, Clove garlic, crushed with, Broth or water, Cold water, Soy sauce, Sugar, 
Combine sugar, soy sauce, and broth.  Bring to a boil.  Add corn starch  mixed smoothly with the cold water, and stir until the mixture thickens  some.  Simmer, stirring for 1 minute.  Stir in garlic.  Serve any  temperature.    From: williams@lanl.gov (Louise Williams). Fatfree Digest [Volume 10 Issue  5] Aug. 15, 1994.
