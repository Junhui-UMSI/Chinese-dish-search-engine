Tropical Shrimp Salad
Fresh dill; chopped, Salt & fresh ground black, Mayonnaise; (more if you, Onion, Curry powder; or to taste, Celery; diced, Flaked coconut, Fresh Lemon Juice, Green onions (tops, Large shrimp; shelled,, 
Place shrimp in boiling salted water and remove after 5 minutes. Allow them  to cool. Add all other ingredients, mix well. Serve chilled on bed of  lettuce. Garnish with sliced tomatoes. Makes 3 to 4 servings.    New York, New York    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Mar 16, 1998
