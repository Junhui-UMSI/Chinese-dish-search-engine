Raisin Spice Drops
(quick or oldfashioned), Eggs, Allspice, All-Purpose Flour, Salt, Raisins, Cinnamon, Quaker Oats, uncooked, Sugar, Baking soda, Shortening, soft, 
Pour enough boiling water over raisins to cover them.  Cover and let  stand 10 minutes.  Beat shortening and sugar together until creamy.  Add eggs; beat until fluffy.    Drain raisins, reserving 1/2 cup liquid.  Dissolve soda in this  liquid. Sift together flour, salt and spices.  Add to creamed mixture  alternately with raisin liquid; blend well.  Stir in raisins and oats.    Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased cooky sheets.  Bake in preheated  moderate oven (350 F.) 12 to 15 minutes.
