Pot Hole Potatoes
Polish sausage, Med Onion; diced, Mashed potatoes, Green pepper; diced, 
Brown Polish sausage in a saucepan,  add pepper and onion. Cook until  vegetables are soft.  Drain well on toweling. Grease muffin pans. Place  mashed potatoes in muffin pan.Scoop out center of each    and  fill with sausage mix.  Bake 425 degrees for 15 min- utes. Serve  hot.  FROM:    JEAN ALLEN    (GRDG72B)
