Apricot Mustard Glazed Chicken Breasts
Apricot preserves, Honey mustard or dijon, Ground ginger, Boneless skinless chicken, 
Blend mustard, apricot preserves and ginger. Brush some mustard mixture on  chicken. Grill or broil chicken, brushing with remaining mustard mixture  frequently until done.    Recipe by: Gey Poupon ad.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1050 by Roberta Banghart  <bobbi744@sojourn.com> on Jan 25, 1998
