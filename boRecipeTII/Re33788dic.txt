Sweet and Sour Kidney Beans
Wine vinegar, Black pepper, Kidney beans , Sugar, Garbanzo beans , Celery, Onion, Green pepper, Seasoned salt, Salad oil, 
Combine vegetables in large bowl.    Combine sugar, salt, pepper, oil and vinegar and pour over vegetables.    Stir well; cover and refrigerate overnight.    Stir occasionally to marinate thoroughly.    Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
