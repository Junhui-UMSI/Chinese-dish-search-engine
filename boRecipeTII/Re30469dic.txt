Banana Muffins
Flour, Salt, milk, Sugar, Backing soda, Mashed bananas., Vegetable oil, Beaten egg, 
In a bowl combine flour,baking powder,soda,salt and sugar.In another bowl  combine egg,milkand oil.Add egg mixture to flour mixture.Blend in  bananas.Grease 2 muffin tins liberally.Pour in batter to fill 2/3 of each  cup.Bake at 375 c for 35 minutes.Use a toothpick to test for  doneness.Remove tins to rack.Let stand for 5 min.With a greased knife cut  around each cup.Invert tap to remove muffins. Serve immediatly with  softened butter. ****Serves 6 *****  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 623 by GramWag <GramWag@aol.com>  on Jan 28, 1998
