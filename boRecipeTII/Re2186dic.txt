Avocado Bisque
Whipping cream, Zucchini, Dried mint flakes, Unsalted butter, Chicken stock, avocadoes, Celery, Onion, Whole jalapeno pepper, Ground cumin, Salt, 
1. Melt butter in the pressure cooker over medium heat. Add the celery,  onion, zucchini, and jalapeno. Cook uncovered until vegetables begin to  soften, 3 to 4 minutes. Add stock, cumin, and mint.    2. Cover and bring up to high pressure. Reduce heat to stabilize pressure  and cook 5 minutes. Release pressure.    3. Puree the contents of the cooker in a blender or food processor. Add the  avocados and blend again until smooth. Transfer to a serving container and  stir in cream and salt to taste. Serve hot or chilled.    Recipe by: The Best Pressure Cooker Cookbook Ever    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Oct 26, 98,
