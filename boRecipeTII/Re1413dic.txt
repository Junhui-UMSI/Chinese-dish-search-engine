Emerils Horseradishy Potatoes
Red potatoes, quartered, "Quick horseradish sauce", OR sour cream, Butter, Cream, Salt and pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, Finely grated horseradish, Chopped parsley, olive oil, 
Preheat broiler. Cook potatoes in salted water until tender. Drain and mash  potatoes in the pot. Add horseradish sauce, butter and cream. Season to  taste with salt and pepper. Transfer to a buttered ovenproof gratin or  baking dish. Mix together remaining ingredients and spread over top. Broil  until top is brown and crisp.
