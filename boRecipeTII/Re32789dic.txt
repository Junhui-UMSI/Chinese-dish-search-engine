Sweet Potato Pie with Pineapple
Rich milk or light cream, Cinnamon, Mashed potatoes, Brown sugar, Light corn syrup, Allspice, Salt, Unbaked pie shell, Drained crushed pineapple, Unsalted butter, Separated eggs, Ginger, 
Heat oven to 350. Mix potatoes with milk. Add butter, yolks, sugar,  salt & spices & beat until light & fluffy. Blend in pineapple. Beat  whites until stiff & fold into potato mixture. Pour mixture into  shell. Sprinkle with cinnamon & bake 45 minutes. Serve warm.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
