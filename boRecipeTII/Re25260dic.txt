Tool House Pan Cookies
Vanilla, Eggs, Baking soda, Sugar, Salt, Butter, Brown sugar, Flour, Chocolate chips, Nuts, 
Preheat oven to 375 F.  In large bowl, combine butter, sugars, and vanilla;  beat till creamy.  Beat in eggs.  Gradually add flour, soda, and salt; mix  well.  Add chocolate chips and nuts.  Spread into greased 15x10x1 inch  cookie pan.  Bake at 375 F for 20 minutes.  Cool; cut into squares. Makes       35    squares.
