Beef with Chinese Peas
Onion; sliced thinly, Chinese peas, MARINADE, Sherry, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Oyster sauce, Vegetable oil, Clove garlic; mashed, Cornstarch, Shoyu, Flank steak; thinly sliced, 
Marinate beef for 15 minutes or longer.  Heat oil in skillet and brown  garlic.  Stir fry onion and marinated meat.  Add peas and heat thoroughly.    From a book of my mothers (Judy Hosey) titled <Senator Richard Matsuuras  Favorite Recipes>, mostly Japanese and Hawaiian.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
