Baked Smoked Salmon and Feta Cheese En Croute
Finely choppedparsley, Capers, Egg, slightly beaten, (or more) poppyseeds, Feta cheese, Smoked salmon, diced, Cream cheese, softened, Scallions, topped, diced, 
Youll also need 1 frozen pastry sheet, cut into a 3" X 8" rectangle and  some melted butter.  Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. In a medium bowl,  hand mix the salmon, cream cheese, Feta cheese, egg, capers, parsle, and  scallions.  Roll out the pastry sheet to double its size. Liberally brush  it with melted butter. Spread the salmon mixture over the sheet. Roll up,  jelly-roll style, folding the ends in to seal. Brush the top of the roll  with melted butter and sprinkle with poppy seeds. Make 1/2 inch deep  diagonal slashes across the roll to allow steam to escape. Bake the roll  for 20 to 30 minutes or until golden. Serve warm.
