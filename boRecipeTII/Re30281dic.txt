Banana Cake
Mashed ripe banana, Vanilla, Chopped walnuts (my, Baking Powder, Sugar, Baking soda; dissolved in:, Soft margarine, Lemon juice; optional, Shortening, FROSTING, Water, Mashed bananas, Flour, Salt, Egg, Vanilla, 
Combine sugar, shorteing and egg. Ad mashed banana, baking soda and water.  Add flour, baking powder, salt and vanilla. Mix well. Bake in a greased and  floured 9x9" baking pan at 350 F. for 30-35 minutes or until cake tests  done. Cool. Frost with banana frosting or use lemon or orange frosting.    Banana Frosting: Mash banana. Add vanilla and margerine. Add sugar to make  correct consistency for spreading. Can add dash lemon juice to add  tartness.
