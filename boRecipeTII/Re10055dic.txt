Quakers- Golden Granola with Mixed Fruit
Brown Sugar, Honey, QUAKER Oats, Vanilla, mixed dried fruit, Margarine, 
Heat oven to 350 F.  In 15 x 10-inch jelly roll pan, combine all  ingredients except fruit;  mix well. Spread evenly over pan. Bake 20 to 25  minutes or until golden brown, stirring after 10 minutes. Cool completely.  Stir in fruit.  Serve as a snack, cereal or topping over yogurt. Store  tightly covered up to 1 week.    MICROWAVE DIRECTIONS:  In large microwaveable bowl, combine all ingredients  except fruit;  mix well. Microwave at HIGH 3 minutes; stir. Microwave an  additional 3 to 4 minutes or until golden brown, stirring after 2 minutes.  Spread onto foil;  cool completely. Stir in fruit. Serve as a snack, cereal  or topping over yogurt.  Store tightly covered up to 1 week.
