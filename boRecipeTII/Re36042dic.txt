Beef Paprika
Paprika, Caraway seeds, Butter, Onions, Beef broth, Marjoram, Garlic, Salt and pepper, Beef for stew, Brown sugar, Cornstarch, 
Cube the beef. Peel and slice the onions and put them in a casserole dish  with the butter. Heat on HIGH for 3-5 minutes. Add the beef, peeled and  crushed garlic, broth, marjoram, caraway seeds, pinch of brown sugar,  paprika and seasoning. Put a small plate on top of the meat to keep it  inder the juices and cover the casserole with a lid. Cook on HIGH for 10  minutes, then simmer for 75 minutes. Remove the cover and plate. Mix the  cornstarch to a paste with a little cold water and stir into the stew. Heat  on HIGH for 5 minutes until thickened. Source: The Cooks Color Treasury  :          edited by Norma MacMillan  :          Page 47    From the collection of K. Deck
