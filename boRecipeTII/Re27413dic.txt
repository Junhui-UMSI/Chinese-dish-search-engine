Toasted Tid Bits, Pk
Butter, Tabasco sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic powder, Pretzel sticks, Celery salt, Bite-size oat cereal rings, Shelled peanuts, Bite-size shredded wheat, 
Mix first five ingredients. Pour this mixture over other ingredients in  large bowl. Stir gently to coat all pieces. Bake in large shallow baking  pan at 300 degrees for 1 hour. Stir every 10 minutes.    Makes about 6 cups.
