Sweet and Sour Red Cabbage And Apples
Sesame oil, Garlic, Red cabbage, Granny smith apples, Ground pepper -- to taste, Apple cider vinegar, Soy sauce, low sodium, Ground ginger, Honey, 
With a food processor, shred red cabbage and mince garlic clove. Heat oil  in a large nonstick pan and add garlic and cabbage. Saute over medium heat.    Wash, core, and cut apples into bite-size chunks.  Add apples to cabbage  and stir well. Add remaining ingred. Cover and cook over low heat til  apples are tender, abt 7 min.
