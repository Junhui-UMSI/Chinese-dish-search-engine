Ambush Chili #1 No 3537
Allspice; Ground, Sour Mash Whiskey, Tomato paste, Lean Pork Shoulder; Cubed, Lean Chuck Steak; Coarse, Onions; Chopped, Green Bell Pepper; Chopped, Stewed Tomatoes; Chopped, Peanut oil, Beer; (not lite), Bay Leaves, Jalapeno Peppers; Left Whole, Jalapeno Peppers; Chopped, Sri Racha Pepper Sauce, Garlic, Red Bell Pepper; Chopped, Blackstrap Molasses, Tomato sauce, Cumin, Masa Harina, Soy sauce, Cumin, 
Saute the onions, garlic and the chopped peppers in the peanut oil. Add all  the meat. Cook until browned. Add all the other ingredients but the cumin.  Increase heat. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil for 4 minutes,  stirring steadily. Reduce heat to a fast simmer. Cook, stirring often, for  10 minutes. Stir in the first measure of cumin. Reduce heat to a moderate  simmer. Cook for one hour, stirring occasionally. Stir in the second  measure of cumin. Cook for 15 minutes. Serve hot.
