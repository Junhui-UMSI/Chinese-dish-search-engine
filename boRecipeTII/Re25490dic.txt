Avocado-Crab Dip
Avacado; peeled, cubed, Salt, Grated onion, (8-oz) cream cheese;, Sour cream, (7.5-oz) crab meat; drained, Lemon juice, Worcestershire Sauce, 
In blender or small mixing bowl combine avacado, lemon juice, onion &  Worcestershire sauce. Blend or beat until smooth. Add cream cheese, sour  cream & salt. Blend well. Stir in crab meat. Chill. Serve with assorted  crackers.    MRS. VICTOR JUENGEL, JR.    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
