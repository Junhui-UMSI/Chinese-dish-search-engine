White Chili
Boneless skinless chicken, Chopped green chile peppers;, olive oil, Oregano flakes, Black pepper, Chicken broth; (13oz), Sharp cheddar cheese; grated, Shallots; chopped, Tomatillas (18oz); drained,, Coriander seeds; crushed, Garlic, Ground cumin, Cannellini beans; drained, Fresh squeezed lime juice, Cooking oil spray, Ro*tel tomatoes*; chopped, 
*do not drain Spray a large skillet with cooking spray, add olive oil and  heat on medium high until hot. Add diced chicken and saute for 3 minutes or  until done. Remove chicken from pan. Place all ingredients, except Cheese,  in a Crockpot and cook for 8 hours. Serve with tortilla chips, sour cream,  avocado dip and Mexican beer.
