Apricot Chews
Sweetened condensed milk, Coconut, apricots, 
Heat oven to 350. Grease cookie sheets. Mix all ingredients until well  blended. Roll rounded teaspoonfuls into balls. Place 1" apart on  sheets. Bake 12 minutes or until set. Remove to wire rack to cool.  Store in airtight containers up to 2 weeks.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
