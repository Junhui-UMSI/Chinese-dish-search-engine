Tuna Mousse #2
Knox gelatin, Tuna, Cucumber; chopped, unpeeled, Onion flakes, Skim milk, Lime juice, Mayonnaise, Ice cubes, Chicken bouillon cube, 
Sprinkle gelatin over milk in pan. Let stand 2 minutes. Add chicken  bouillon; heat over low heat until dissolved. Pour into blender. Add mayo  and lime juice; blend. Add ice cubes 1 at a time. Stir in tuna, cuke and  onion. Pour into 4-cup mold or individual molds. Chill until set, 1 hour.  Unmold on lettuce leaf.
