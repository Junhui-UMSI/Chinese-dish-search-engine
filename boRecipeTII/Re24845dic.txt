Auntie Babes Cheese Cake
Sugar, Butter, Sugar, TOPPING, Sugar, Cinnamon, Sour cream, Vanilla, Vanilla, Cream cheese, Crust, FILLING, Eggs, Graham cracker crumbs, 
Combine crust ingredients and pat into bottom and sides of pan. Beat cream  cheese thoroughly; add sugar gradually; add eggs in thirds; add vanilla;  pour into prepared pan. Bake at 375 for 25 minutes; remove from oven and  add topping. To prepare topping, whip sour cream; add sugar and vanilla,  and pour over cake. continue baking at 500 for 5 minutes. Important - chill  at LEAST 24 hours.
