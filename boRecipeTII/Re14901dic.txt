2 Pungent Bbq Sauces
Red win vinegar, Tomatos peeled and chopped, Salt, Basil, Celery; minced, Red wine, Pepper, Beef stock, Tomato puree, Parsley, olive oil, Onion; minced, Green pepper; minced, Cl Garlic; chopped, Chili powder, 
Saute the garlic in the oil until browned and almost crisp. Add the onions  and cook until tender but not browned. Add green pepper, tomatos, celery,  basil, chili powder, stock and vinegar. Cover and let simmer for 40 min.,  then run the mixture through a blender or food processor. Add the remaining  ingredients and simmer for at least 10 min., the longer this sauce simmers  the better it makes it.    To serve this sauce can be used either hot or cold for basting or serving  on the table. Makes 5 1/2 cups and can be stored in the refrigerator  covered.
