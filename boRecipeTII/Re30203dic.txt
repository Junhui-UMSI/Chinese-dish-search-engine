Banana Bread
Baking soda, milk, Eggs, All-purpose flour, Pecans, Sugar, bananas, Vanilla, Salt, Margarine, 
Heat oven to 350F. Grease(not oil) bottom only of 9x5 or 8x4-inch loaf  pan. In large bowl, blend first 6 ingredients beat 1 minute at medium  speed. Stir in remaining ingredients just until dry ingredients are  moistened. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake at 350? for 50 to 60 minutes  or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean. Cool 5 minutes;  remove from pan. Cool completely.    Makes 1 loaf    Recipe by: Pillsbury Kitchens Cookbook    Posted to T.nt (sdm-marked) Recipes Digest by Marceline8@aol.com on Feb 18, 1998
