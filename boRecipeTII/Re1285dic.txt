Dhoodhi Peel Dosa
Rice, Doodhi (zucchini) peel, very finely sliced, Sour buttermilk, Coconut milk, Salt to taste, Green chillies, 1-inch piece of ginger, Bunch of coriander leaves, 
Soak the rice in water overnight.  Drain.  Grind rice until it forms a  thick, smooth paste.  Transfer to a bowl.    Finely slice chillies, ginger and coriander leaves.  Add to rice mixture  along with buttermilk, coconut milk and salt.  Mix thoroughly to form  batter.    For each dosa, cook 2 tablespoons batter on hot greased skillet or griddle,  spreading batter into thin circles and turning dosa once to brown both  sides.  Serve hot with chutney, if desired.
