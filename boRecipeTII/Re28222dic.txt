Baked Fish Steaks
Butter, Watercress or parsley, Sliced lemon, milk, Dry breadcrumbs, Fish steaks, tamure, Salt, 
1. Lay the fish in a shallow dish. Pour the milk over the steaks and add  salt and pepper. Leave to soak one hour.    2. Drain the milk away and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.    3. Lay the steaks in a buttered baking dish. Melt the butter and pour over  the fish.    4. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.    5. Serve garnished with sliced lemon and sprinkled with watercress.
