Pomegranate Tossed Salad
Onions, lettuce, Tomato, Cucumber, Pomegranate seeds, Avocado, 
Toss ingredients and top with favorite dressing. Serves 6. (Note: Garnish  your traditional Waldorf salad with pomegranate seeds; add 2/3 cup    pomegranate seeds to Fattoush - Arabic bread salad; mix in the seeds with a  variety of lettuces, such as radicchio and endive, topping with olive oil  and fresh lemon juice.)
