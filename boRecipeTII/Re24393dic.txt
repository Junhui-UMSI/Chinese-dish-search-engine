Tropical Barbeque Sauce
Water, Catchup, Water, Pineapple, crushed, Arrowroot, Brown sugar, Soy sauce, Dry mustard, 
Mix together 1 cup water and brown sugar.  Add catchup, soy sauce, dry  mustard and pineapple.  Bring to a boil; simmer ten minutes.    Dissolve arrowroot in 1/4 cup water;  add to sauce and cook until sauce  thickens.
