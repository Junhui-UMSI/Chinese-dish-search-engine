Zucchini Italian Style
Italian herb seasoning, Bottled Italian salad, Zucchini or yellow squash or, Salt, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Cut unpared squash in half crosswise, then lengthwise into fourths. Place  salt and squash lices in 1 1/2-quart casserole; sprinkle with salad  dressing and herb seasoning. Cover tightly and microwave on high (100%) 3  minutes; stir. Cover and microwave until tender, 2 to 3 minutes longer.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese, if desired.
