Baked Peanut Butter Pie
Pie shell, 9"; unbaked, Egg, Vanilla, Sugar, granulated, Peanut butter, creamy, Corn syrup, dark, 
Blend the corn syrup, sugar, vanilla, eggs, and peanut butter and pour into  the unbaked crust. Bake in a 400 degree oven 15 minutes, then lower the  temperature to 350 degrees and continue baking 30 to 35 minutes more. The  center should look "wobbly"; it will set as the pie cools.
