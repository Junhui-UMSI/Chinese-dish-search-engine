Baked Filet of Flounder
flounder fillets, Salt, Flour, mushrooms, Lemon juice, paprika & parsley, Butter, milk, shrimp, 
Cut aluminum foil in 12-inch square for each peice of fish. Arrange fish in  center of square. Divide shrimp & put on fish. Make thick white sauce with  flour, milk, butter & salt. Stir in lemon juice & pour over fish. Add  paprika & parsley. Fold foil on top so the liquid will not drain out. Place  on cookie sheet & bake at 425 for 40 minutes.    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
