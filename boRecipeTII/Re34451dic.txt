Bean Dip a la Dr. Pepper
Cheddar, Dr. Pepper, Bacon, Black pepper, Red Kidney Beans, canned, Garlic, Worcestershire Sauce, Tomato paste, Vegetable oil, Salt, Green Chilies, 
Combine all ingredients except the last 2 in saucepan; heat to boiling.  Pour into blender or electric mixer; add cheese. Blend on high speed until  well mixed. Pour into warmer dish; cover with bacon. Serve with chips,  crackers. Source: Cookin with Dr. Pepper
