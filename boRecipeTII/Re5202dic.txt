Pilaff
Condensed consomme, Onion, Butter, regular rice, Water, 
saute onion in butter until golden.    Combine consomm? and water. Bring to a boil.    In an oiled 1 1/2 quart casserole combine all ingredients.    Bake, covered, at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until rice feels tender and  all water is absorbed.    Fluff up rice with fork.
