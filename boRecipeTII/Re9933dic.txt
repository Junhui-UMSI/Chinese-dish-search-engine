Puree Mongole (Soup, Cond
WATER; BOILING, BEANS WHITE DRY 2 LB, Onions, CARROTS FRESH, WATER; COLD, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, SOUP GRAVY BASE BEEF, PEPPER BLACK 1 LB CN, Bay Leaves, SOUP TOMATO VEG #2 1/2, Water, 
1.  PICK OVER PEAS, REMOVING ANY FOREIGN MATTER.  WASH THOROUGHLY IN COLD  WATER.    2.  COVER PEAS WITH WATER.  BRING TO A BOIL.    3.  ADD SOUP AND GRAVY BASE, BOILING WATER, HAM TRIMMINGS, ONIONS, CARROTS,  BAY LEAVES, SUGAR AND PEPPER TO PEAS.    4.  BRING SOUP MIXTURE TO A BOIL, REDUCE HEAT; SIMMER GENTLY ABOUT 2 1/2  HOURS OR UNTIL MUSHY.  REMOVE BAY LEAVES.    5.  WHIP UNTIL MIXTURE IS SMOOTH.  ADD BOILING WATER, IF NEEDED, FOR A  THINNER CONSISTENCY.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 1, PEAS MAY BE COOKED 2 MINUTES, REMOVED FROM HEAT,  COVERED AND ALLOWED TO STAND 1 HOUR.  IN STEP 4, COOK 1 1/2 HOURS.    2.  IN STEP 3, 1 LB 13 OZ (1-29 OZ CN) CANNED HAM CHUNKS, DRAINED  OR 2 LB HOT ITALIAN SAUSAGE, THAWED (BROWNED AND CHOPPED) MAY BE USED  FOR HAM.    3.  IN STEP 3, 2 LB 4 OZ DRY ONIONS A.P. WILL YIELD 2 LB GROUND  OR FINELY CHOPPED ONIONS AND 1 LB 6 OZ FRESH CARROTS A.P. WILL YIELD  1 LB 2 OZ GRATED CARROTS.    4.  ONE D LADLE MAY BE USED.
