Pineapple Delights
BASIC RECIPE, Icing sugar, Sugar, Butter, BUTTER ICING, Drained crushed pineapple, Whipped fresh cream, Butter, TOPPING, Digestive biscuits, 
1. Melt butter and add to crushed biscuits and sugar. Press into  swiss roll tin and put in the refrigerator for 1/2 hour.    2. Beat together the butter icing ingredients, and spread onto  the top of the biscuits.    3. Drain the pineapple and spread ontop of the iced biscuits, and  cover the pineapple with fresh whipped cream.    4. Cut into squares.    Source: Mary Killen, Clough, Downpatrick Co. Down, N.
