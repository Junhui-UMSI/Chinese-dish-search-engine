Apple and Matzo Kugel
Matzos, broken in pieces, Apple, peeled and diced, Eggs, Shortening, 
Cover matzos with water then squeeze out as much as possible. Add apple and  fat.  Beat yolks into mixture, season with salt and sugar to taste. Beat  egg whites until stiff, fold in above gently. Pour inot pre-heated, greased  casserole.  Bake in slow oven (300 F.) about 1 hr. until top has an even  brown crust.
