Vegetable Burgers
Walnuts -- or pecans, Vegetable oil, Soy sauce, Onion, Sage, Salt, Oatmeal -- Cooked, Bread crumbs, Potato, 
Grind or chop in blender first 3 ingredients. Saute in oil until soft. Add  remaining ingredients. Mix thoroughly. Form into burger patties and fry  until brown on both sides, or bake on oiled sheet at 350 degrees    Original poster:    From the collection of Sue Smith, S.Smith34, TXFT40A@Prodigy.com    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/fatfreex.zip
