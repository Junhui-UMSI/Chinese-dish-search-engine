Artichoke Filling
See below, 
Cook 1 pack (9 oz) frozen artichoke hearts until soft following pack    directions.  Drain. Combine 2 oz Neufchatel cheese, 1/2 small chopped  onion, 2 tsp. lemon juice, 1/8 tsp. liquid red pepperseasoning &  drained artichoke hearts in food processor. Whirl until smooth &  blended.
