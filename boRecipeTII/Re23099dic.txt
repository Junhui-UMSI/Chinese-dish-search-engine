Try It, Youll Like It Lamburgers
Ground lamb, Onion, Prepared mustard, Hamburger buns (toasted &, Garlic salt, Buttered), Pepper, Salt, 
Combine lamb, onion, mustard, garlic salt, salt & pepper in bowl; mix  well. Divide meat into 4 equal parts; shape into patties. Broil 3-4"  from broiler 5-6 minutes per side. Place one patty on each bun.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
