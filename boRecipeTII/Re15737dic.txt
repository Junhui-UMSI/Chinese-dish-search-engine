Abadoos Granola
DATES, SHREDDED COCONUT; OPTIONAL, Wheat germ, SESAME SEED, CURRANTS OR RAISINS, Honey, Molasses, Rolled oats, Cooking oil, ALMONDS; CHOPPED, 
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS EXCEPT FOR FRUITS IN 13X9X2-INCH PAN.  BAKE AT 300  DEGREES FOR 45 MINUTES AND STIR EVERY 15 MINUTES.  REMOVE FROM OVEN; STIR  IN FRUITS AND REMOVE TO ANOTHER PAN TO COOL.  STIR OFTEN TO PREVENT LUMPING  WHILE IT COOLS.  STORE IN PLASTIC BAGS.  MAKES ABOUT 7 CUPS.
