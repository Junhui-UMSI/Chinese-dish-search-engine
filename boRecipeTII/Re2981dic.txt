Peanut Butter Sandwich Loaf
Salt, milk, Orange extract -- optional, All-purpose flour, Creamy peanut butter, Egg, Double-acting baking powder, Sugar, 
1. Sift flour and mix thoroughly with the salt, sugar and baking powder.  Add the peanut butter and mix in with a pastry blender. 2. Beat the egg and  add the milk and orange extract to the egg; blend. Pour this mixture into  the flour mixture and stir until thoroughly combined but do not beat. 3.  Grease a 9 x 5-inch loaf pan. Pour mixture into pan and bake in preheated  350-degree oven for 50-55 minutes. Cool before slicing.
