Potato-Beef Bake
Frozen Ore-Ida chopped, Ground beef, Finely chopped celery, Salt, Pepper, Frozen Ore-Idah golden, Chili powder; (optional), (10.5 oz) condensed cream of, Heinz tomato ketchup, 
Here are a few recipes using Frozen French Fries from: Ore-Ida  Magic....Family Favorites from Ore-Ida.    1. Preheat oven to 400?. Grease a shallow 2-quart dish.    2. In medium skillet, over medium-high heat, cook beef, celery and onions,  stirring often, until meat loses its red color and onions are golden. Spoon  off any excess fat. Stir in undiluted soup, ketchup and seasonings and heat  to boiling. Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.    3. Spoon beef mixture into baking dish and arrange frozen potatoes in  single layer across the top. bake 30 minutes or until potatoes are tender  and lightly browned.          6    servings    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Nsabatino <Nsabatino@AOL.COM> on Mar 9, 1998
