Basil Pesto Dip
Light sour cream, Cottage cheese, Grated Parmesan cheese, Basil pesto from the, 
Blend basil pesto from the supermarket deli case with 1 cup cottage cheese,  1 cup light sour cream and 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese in a food  processor. Serve with small bread sticks or vegetables.    Posted to CHILE-HEADS DIGEST V4 #279 by Jim Tidwell  <snd1jlt@snd10.med.navy.mil> on Jan 22, 1998
