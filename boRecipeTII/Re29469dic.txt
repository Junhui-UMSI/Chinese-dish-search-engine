Baking Powder Biscuits
milk, Flour, Salt, Shortening, Baking Powder, 
Sift flour, baking powder and salt. Cut in shortning. Add milk; stir with  fork until dough is formed. Turn out onto lightly floured surface, knead  about 20 turns. Pat or roll 1/2 inch thick. Note: For cheese biscuits, add  3/4 cups grated cheddar cheese. Bake at 450 for about 12 minutes. Makes 20  biscuits.
