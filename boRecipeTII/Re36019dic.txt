Beef N Cheddar Biscuits
(4 oz) shredded cheddar, Ground beef, cooked and, Drained, (4 1/2oz) refrigerated, Cheese, Biscuits, Bbq sauce, 
Preheat oven to 350F.    Press biscuits into bottom and up sides of 12 greased muffin cups. Top with  combined meat and bbq sauce. Sprinkle with cheese.    Bake 15-18 minutes or until biscuits are golden and cheese is melted.    Taste of Home Ground Beef Collection 1996 Edition
