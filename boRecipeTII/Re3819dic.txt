Pennys Beans - Adapted
Vegetable stock, Lemon juice, Beans; cooked, Onion; diced, Lemon pepper, Mushrooms, Brown rice; cooked, Vegetable oil, Garlic; crushed, 
Saute onions in oil until clear with garlic, add mushrooms (undrained),  broth, lemon juice, lemon pepper, and rice to beans. Cook over low heat for  about 1/2 hour.    Formatted by l.crawford@genie.geis. com 7/11/94
