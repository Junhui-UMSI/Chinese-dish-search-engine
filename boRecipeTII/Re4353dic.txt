Persimmon Ice Creme Pie
Silken tofu, Persimmons; ripe, Soy cream cheese or regular, Honey, Vanilla, Packed grated lemon peel, 
Put tofu, cream cheese and honey in food processor and blend till smooth.  Mash persimmons and add other ingredients, stir all together. Pour into pie  shell--I suggest the granola pie crust below. Freeze for several hours,  until set. Let thaw 1 1/2 hours before serving.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 170 by "Bonnie"  <solomon1@kcsnet.com> on Oct 27, 1997
