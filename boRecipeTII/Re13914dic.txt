Wine Cake #1
Sherry wine, Nutmeg, Instant vanilla pudding, Box yellow cake mix, Salad oil, Eggs, 
Mix all ingredients. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes in a tube pan, greased on  the sides & bottom lined with wax paper. After cooling 15 minutes, turn out  on a plate & frost with powdered sugar. Good served with fruit.    MRS CARL T. (SHERRY) SCHOOLEY    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
