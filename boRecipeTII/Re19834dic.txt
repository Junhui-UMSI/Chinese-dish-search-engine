Apple and Blackberry Sorbet
Water, Light corn syrup, Sugar, Cooking apples, frozen blackberries, 
Date: Thu, 09 May 96 09:59:39 -0700    From: Jane Colman <jane@netcom.com>    Combine apples, berries, and water in heavy saucepan.  Bring to boil over  medium-high heat.  Cover, reduce heat to medium, and simmer until fruit is  tender, stirring occasionally, about 20 minutes.  Transfer to sieve set  over large bowl. Press to force through as much fruit and juice as  possible. Discard solids in Sieve. Return puree to same pot and stir in  sugar and corn syrup. Cook, stirring, over low heat, just until sugar is  dissolved. Transfer back to bowl and chill until cold. Process in ice cream  freezer.
