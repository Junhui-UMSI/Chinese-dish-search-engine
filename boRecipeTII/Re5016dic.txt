Pickled Spinach
Green pepper; chopped, Vinegar, Oil, vegetable; to 1/2 cup, Tomato; chopped, Lettuce leaves; opt., Spinach; torn, Bacon; cooked and crumbled, 
Combine first 5 ingredients, tossing gently.  Chill at least 15  minutes. Sprinkle bacon over top; serve on lettuce leaves, if desired.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in 1981. Typed for you by  Nancy Coleman.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/diabetic.zip
