Banana Pudding Cake #1
Eggs, Vegetable oil, -(up to), Bananas; sliced, Box (18-oz) yellow cake mix, Finely chopped nuts, Box (3-oz) banana cream, Water, 
In large mixing bowl, mash bananas. Add re- maining ingredients. Beat with  mixer 2 minutes and pour into greased and floured 10-inch tube pan. Bake at  350 for 60-70 minutes. Cool in pan 15 minutes.
