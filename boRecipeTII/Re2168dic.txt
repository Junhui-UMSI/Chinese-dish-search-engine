Asian Tofu Salad
Red bell peppers, Lowfat firm tofu, Garlic, Dark sesame oil, Scallions, Fresh parsley, DRESSING, Fresh ginger root, Rice vinegar, Chili paste, Soy sauce, 
Crumble the tofu using a wooden spoon or fork. Whisk the dressing  ingredients together. Add the dressing and the peppers, onion, and parsley.  Mix well. Serve at room temperature or chilled.    This salad will keep for 2-3 days.    Serving suggestions: Tusk the salad in a pita with watercress. Or serve it  on rice crackers, or with roasted vegetables.
