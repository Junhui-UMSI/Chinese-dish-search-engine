Turkey Rolls with Stuffing
Turkey or chicken cutlets;, Butter or margarine, Dry white wine, Water, Chicken flavor stuffing mix, All purpose flour, Pepper; optional, Water, 
Combine contents of vegetable seasoning packet and 1 2/3 cup water in a  medium saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 5 minutes.  Add contents of 1 stuffing crumb packet; stir to moisten. Cover; remove  from heat and let stand 5 minutes.    Spoon stuffing evenly onto one end of each cutlet. Roll; secure with wooden  pick or tie with string. Mix flour and pepper. Coat rolls with flour and  brown in hot butter in skillet. Add wine and 1/3 cup water. Cover, reduce  heat and simmer 10-15 minutes, turning twice. Makes 6 servings.    Recipe by: Stove Top    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #996 by L979 <L979@aol.com> on Jan 8, 1998
