Basic Palacsinta
Opened), Vanilla, Butter, milk, Plain club soda (just, Sugar, Salt, Eggs, Flour, Butter, 
Sift flour & salt in bowl. Mix in sugar. Combine eggs, &  milk & beat  well. Add to flour a little at a time, beating well after each  addition with wooden spoon.  Gradually add melted butter & beat til  smooth. Beat in vanilla. Cover & refrigerate overnight. Just before  cooking, gradually stir in soda til batter is as light as cream. Melt  1 tsp. butter in a crepe pan. Heat pan til butter stops foaming. Pour  3 Tbls. batter & tilt pan. Cook til lightly browned & turn & cook  other side. Stack. Fill & bake as directed.
