Tuna Pasta Salad
White albacore tuna, Red onion, chopped, Light mayonnaise, Frozen peas, 
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain. Cook peas  according to package directions; drain. Mix all ingredients. Keep  refrigerated or in a cool, insulated lunch box until ready to eat.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
