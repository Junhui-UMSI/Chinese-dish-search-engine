Wilted Green Beans with Basil
Balsamic vinegar, for garnish, Green beans, trimmed, salt and pepper, Bell peppers, red & yellow,, Basil, minced, olive oil, 
Preheat oven to 500F.  In a large bowl, toss all the ingredients until the  beans are well coated.  Place on a non-stick baking sheet in one layer (use  more than one sheet if necessary). Roast for 10 minutes, stirring once.  Serve at room temperature garnished with the bell pepper slices.
