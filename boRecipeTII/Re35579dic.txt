String Bean Salad
Coconut milk, Green chilies, julienned, sliced, Red chilies, julienned, Soy sauce, String beans, blanched &, Lime juice, Sugar, Chili sauce, see recipe, Tofu, pressed & cubed, 
Place the beans on a large serving platter & arrange the green & red  chilies & tof cubes over the top.  In a small bowl, mix together the  coconut milk, soy sauce, lime juice, sugar & chili sauce.  Just before  serving, pour the dressing over the salad.    Adapted from Eng Tie Ang, "Delightful Thai Cooking"    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/vegan4.zip
