Zucchini Tabbouleh
Salt, Lemon juice, Fresh mint, Lettuce leaves, olive oil, parsley leaves, Scallion, Garlic, Zucchini, 
Recipe by: The Daily Breeze Food Section, July 1, 1991 Combine all  ingredients, mixing well. Refrigerate before serving.    Makes 8 servings.
