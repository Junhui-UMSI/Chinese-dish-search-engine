Almond Cookies
FLAVORING MAPLE, BUTTER PRINT SURE, Baking soda, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, SUGAR; POWDER 2 LB, All Purpose Flour, EGGS SHELL, SHORTENING; 3LB, Salt, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN                    TEMPERATURE:  375 F. OVEN    1.  PLACE INGRDIENTS IN MIXER BOWL IN ORDER LISTED. BEAT AT LOW SPEED  1 TO 2 MINUTES OR UNTIL SMOOTH. SCRAPE DOWN BOWL ONCE DURING MIXING.    2.  DIVIDE DOUGH INTO 10 PIECES, ABOUT 1 LB 4 OZ EACH.  ROLL IN POWDERED  SUGAR FORMING ROLLS 2 INCHES THICK.    3.  SLICE EACH ROLL INTO 20 PIECES. DIP TOP OF EACH PIECE IN POWDERED  SUGAR;  PLACE IN ROWS, 4 BY 6 ON GREASED PANS.  DO NOT FLATTEN COOKIES.    4.  BAKE 12 TO 14 MINUTES, OR UNTIL DONE.    5.  LOOSEN COOKIES FROM PANS WHILE STILL WARM.    NOTE:  A PASTRY TUBE MAY BE USED TO DROP COOKIES ONTO SHEET PAN.
