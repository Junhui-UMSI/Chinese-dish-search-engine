Trinity Meatballs (Sweet and Sour)
Brown sugar, Vinegar, Water, Egg, Spices, Ketchup, Cornstarch, ~~~~~~~~~SAUCE~~~~~~~~~~~~~, Bread crumbs, Lemon juice, Hamburger, 
Mix and shape into meatballs. Brown in fry pan or oven. Place in casserole  dish. Dice 1 onion and sprinkle over meatballs. Pour sauce over top and  bake 1 hour at 350. Submitted By BILL AND TRACY SWARTZ  <BIGBILL@CCINET.AB.CA>  On   SUN, 14 JAN 1996 103822 ~0700
