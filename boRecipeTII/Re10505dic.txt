Quick Gazpacho for One
Green onion, sliced, Celery, Tomatoes, peeled,seeded and, Chopped sweet green pepper, Pinch salt, Tomato juice, Half clove garlic, minced, Chopped seedless cucumber, Dash hot pepper sauce, 
In blender or food processor, combine tomatoes, tomato juice, celery,  garlic, half of the cucumber, half of the green pepper and half of the  onions, the hot pepper sauce and salt until vegetables are finely chopped  but not pureed.    Stir in remaining cucumber, green pepper and onions. Refrigerate until  chilled.  Makes 1 serving.  Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source:  The Canadian Living 20th Anniversary Cookbook.
