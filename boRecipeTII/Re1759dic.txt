Chicken Alla Spina
Oil for frying, Onions, Oregano, Red wine, Whole chicken, cut into, Salt/pepper, 
Brown chicken in oil, set aside. Lightly saute onions on same oil. Place  chicken in roasting pan. Layer onions over top and sprinkle salt, pepper,  and oregano over all. Cover pan, bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Add  wine over all. Bake and additional 30 minutes, stirring occasionally to  loosen. Serve with a loaf of Italian bread.
