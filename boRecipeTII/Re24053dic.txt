Tournedos Coliseum
Creole Meat Seasoning **, 1inch thick, Marchand de Vin **, Carrots, blanched, sliced, Butter, Pea pods, steamed, Bernaise Sauce **, Steaks, beef, tenderloin,, 
Sprinkle steaks with Creole meat seasoning and saute in butter until  done just below desired stage. Remove the steaks and keep them warm.         Allow two tournedos per serving and nap one with the Bernaise and one  with the Marchand de Vin sauce. Garnish with sliced blanched carrots and  steamed pea pods.
