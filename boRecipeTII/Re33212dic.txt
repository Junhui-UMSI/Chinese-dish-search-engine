Basic Pastry
Cold water; up to 4, Salt, Salt, Shortening; plus 1, Shortening; plus 2, Flour, Shortening, FOR 10 INCH CRUST, Flour, FOR 9 INCH CRUST, Flour, Cold water; up to 3, Salt, Cold water; up to 4, FOR 8 INCH CRUST, 
Each Makes One Pie    Combine flour and salt; cut in shortening with pastry blender until mixture  resembles coarse meal. Sprinkle cold water (1 tablespoon at a time) evenly  over surface; stir with a fork until dry ingredients are moistened. Shape  into a ball; chill.    To fit into pie plate, roll dough to 1/8 inch thickness on a lightly  floured surface. Place in pie plate; trim off excess pastry along edges.  Fold edges under and flute.    For baked pastry shell, prick bottom and sides of pastry shell generously  with a fork. Bake pastry at 450F for 10 - 12 minutes or until pastry is  golden brown.
