Almost Fat Free Brownies
Vanilla, Flour, Sugar, Unsweetened cocoa powder, Salt, Prune Baby Food, Nuts -- (optional), Egg whites, Jar, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray an 8-inch square baking pan with  cooking spray.  Combine all ingredients.  Add nuts if desired. Bake about  30 minutes or until springy to touch. Cool on rack.    Cut into 16 pieces.    Fat, about 0.3 g. per brownie.
