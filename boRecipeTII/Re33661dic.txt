Sweet n Sour Baked Chicken
Tomato sauce, Chicken, Vinegar, Lipton Onion Soup, Brown sugar, Crushed Pineapple  , Water, 
In a 12 x 8-inch baking pan, arrange chicken. Bake at 350 F for 30 minutes,  drain. In a small saucepan, combine soup mix, brown sugar, tomato sauce,  water, vinegar and pineapple. Simmer, stirring constantly, until sauce is  slightly thickened. Pour sauce over chicken and bake, basting occasionally,  for 30 minutes or until chicken is tender.
