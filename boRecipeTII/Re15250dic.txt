Whiskey Sauce
Half-and-half, Whiskey, Cornstarch, Sugar, 
In a small saucepan slowly heat half-and-half and sugar. In a small bowl  whisk cornstarch with 3 cup water. Slowly stream cornstarch into hot  mixture, stirring constantly. Cook 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Sauce  will thicken; thin slightly, if necessar. Add whiskey, to taste. Keep warm  until ready to serve.
