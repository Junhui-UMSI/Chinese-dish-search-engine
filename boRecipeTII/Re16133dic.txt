Warm Spinach Salad #2
Bacon; cut in small pieces, Vegetable oil, Garlic, Onion; sliced in rings, Lemon, Spinach; washed, Hard cooked eggs; quartered, Salt, Pepper, 
NOTE: The amount of bacon is obviously wrong!!!! [Glen]    Heat oil in lg skillet and fry bacon until crisp. Add garlic and lemon  juice. Keep bacon warm over low heat. Tear spinach into bite sized pieces  and place in a salad bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Pour bacon and oil  over salad and garnish with onion rings and eggs.
