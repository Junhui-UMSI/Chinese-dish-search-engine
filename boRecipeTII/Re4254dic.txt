Perfectly Set Cranberry Sauce
Boiling water, Sugar, Cranberries, 
1. Bring water to a boil; add cranberries and cook until berries have  popped. 2. Add sugar; cook until the sugar is completely dissolved. Pour  into a serving bowl, let cool slightly, then refrigerate until properly  set.
