Bananas Foster #4
Bananas, Sweet Butter, Vanilla ice cream, Banana Liqueur, Dark Brown Sugar, Cinnamon, Cognac, 
From:    Joel Ehrlich <Joel.Ehrlich@SALATA.COM>    Date:    Tue, 23 Jul 1996 10:37:51 -0800  Combine the butter and brown sugar in a saucepan.  Cook over medium heat  until caramelized.  Cut the bananas in quarters.  Split.  Place in the  saucepan.  Cook until tender.  Add the cinnamon and liqueur.  Blend well.  Place a generous scoop of ice cream in each serving plate.  Heat the  cognac. Pour over the bananas (do not stir). Ignite. Spoon the flaming  bananas over the ice cream. Serve.    EAT-L Digest 23 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
