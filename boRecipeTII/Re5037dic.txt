Pickled Vegetables (Tsukemono)
Turnips; Sliced, Salt, Lemon, Vinegar, Cayenne Pepper Or Shichimi, Cucumber; Sliced Lengthwise, 
This easy recipe can utilize almost any vegetable, such as daikon radish or  broccoli stems.    Sprinkle turnips and cucumbers with salt and let stand for 5 minutes. Place  vegetables in a covered container.  Add remaining ingredients and allow to  stand, refrigerated, for at least 12 hrs.    VEGAN    Makes 4 servings.
