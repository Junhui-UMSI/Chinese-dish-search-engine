Cranberry Sauce with Zip
Fresh cranberries, 1/2 onions, Sour cream, Sugar, Prepared horseradish., 
In a food processor, grind cranberries and onion. In a bowl mix sour cream,  sugar and horseradish. Manually, combine sour cream mixture with chopped  cranberry mixture Chill and serve. Can be frozen. Thaw and stir before  serving.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Sep  15, 1998
