Banana Milk Shake
Bananas, milk, Vanilla extract, Ground nutmeg, 
Mash ripe bananas with a fork in a big bowl. When soft, beat them with a  rotary beater. Add cold milk and vanilla and beat until drink is very  frothy. Dust with nutmeg. Yield: 6 servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
