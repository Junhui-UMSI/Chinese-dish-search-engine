Peanut Brittle #2
Sugar, Water, Raw peanuts, Baking soda, White corn syrup, Vanilla, Butter, 
Grease a large cookie sheet with butter or margarine. In a 4-quart  saucepan, combine sugar, corn syrup & water. Cook mixture to hard ball  stage (about 7 minutes on medium heat). Add raw peanuts. Cook until golden  brown. Add vanilla, butter & baking soda. Mix well. Pour into greased  cookie sheet, smooth evenly & let cool. Break into pieces.
