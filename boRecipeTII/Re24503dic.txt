Asparagus Omelet
Sharp cheddar cheese, cubed, Soft bread crumbs (3 slices, Salt, Cooked ham or chicken, cubed, Frozen asparagus spears, Butter, Onion, grated, milk, Eggs, 
Cut asparagus spears in small pieces and cook until tender-crisp. Combine  remaining ingredients, mix with asparagus and pour into greased 2 quart  baking dish. Bake 1 1/4 hrs. in moderate oven (300? to 325?) or until firm.
