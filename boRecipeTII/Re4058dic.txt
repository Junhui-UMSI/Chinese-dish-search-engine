Peppermint Meringues
Egg whites, Sugar, Cream of tartar, Salt, Peppermint candies, 
Arrange oven racks to divide oven in thirds. Heat to 225 deg f. Line two  cookie sheets with foil. In a large bowl, beat egg whites, salt and cream  of tartar until peaks appear. Gradually add sugar and beat at medium speed  for 5 to 6 minutes. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto cookie sheets. Sprinkle with  crushed candy. Bake 1 1/2 hours. Meringues should look dry and white. Turn  off oven. Keep door ajar and let meringues cool. Loosen from foil with  metal spatula. Store loosely covered in a dry place. Makes about 48  meringues each = 0 g fat.
