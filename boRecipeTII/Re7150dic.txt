Poppyseed Tea Loaf
Flour, Vanilla, Baking soda, Sour cream, Poppy seeds, Eggs, Butter, Sugar, Almond extract, 
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease and flour 10" Bundt pan.  In large bowl of  electric miser, cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, one at a time.  Add  poppyseeds.  Combine dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture,  alternately with sour cream.  Add flavorings and turn into baking pan and  bake 1 hour.
