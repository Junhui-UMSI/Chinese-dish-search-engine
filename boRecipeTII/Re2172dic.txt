Asparagus Gratin
Shallot; finely chopped, Aspargus; tough ends,, Salt, Freshly grated parmesan;, Fresh Lemon Juice, Chopped fresh; parsley, Plus 2 teaspoons; olive oil, Firm white bread; 2, 
Preheat oven to 400 F. In 1 quart saucepan, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil  over medium low heat. Add shallot; cook about 6 minutes, until golden.  Remove from heat.    In blender, process bread to fine crumbs; or tear bread into small crumbs.  Spread crumbs in jello-roll pan; bake 3 to 6 minutes, until golden. Set  aside.    In a 12 inch skillet, in 1 inch boiling water, heat aspargus and 1/2  teaspoon salt to boiling over high heat. Reduce heat to medium- low and  simmer, uncovered, 5 to 10 minutes, just until aspargus are tender, drain.  Place aspargus in shallow broiler- safe dish; drizzle with remaining 2  teaspoos oil.
