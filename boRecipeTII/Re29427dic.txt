Baked-On-Meringue
Cream of tartar, Brown Sugar, Walnuts; finely chopped, Egg whites, 
Beat 2 egg whites until frothy.  Add 1/8 teaspoon cream of tartar, then  gradually beat in 1 cup packed C&H Golden Brown Sugar.  Continue to beat  until stiff.  Spread over unbaked cake batter in pan.  Sprinkle with 1/4  cup finely chopped walnuts.  Bake with cake as directed.
