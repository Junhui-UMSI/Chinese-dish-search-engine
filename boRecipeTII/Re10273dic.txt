Quick Apple Torte
Cinnamon, Ice cream or cream, Baking Powder, Flour, Sliced apples, Nutmeg, Nuts; chopped, Egg, Vanilla, Sugar, 
Combine beaten egg and vanilla; to this add all dry ingredients. Add apple  slices and nuts; turn into 8 inch pie pan. Sprinkle with cinnamon and  nutmeg to taste. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes. Serve warm with  cream or ice cream. Serves 6.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
