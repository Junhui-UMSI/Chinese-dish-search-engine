Almond Joy Cake
Sugar, Coconut, Almonds; chopped, Large marshmallows, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Evaporated milk, 
Make chocolate cake according to package directions in 2 round pans. Make  favorite chocolate frosting for cake. While cake is cooling, heat  evaporated milk, sugar and marshmallows in a very large pan to avoid  spilling over. When marshmallows are melted, add coconut and almonds.  Spread this on top of the bottom cake layer. Top with other cake layer and  frost with chocolate frosting.
