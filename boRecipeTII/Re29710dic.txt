Tea Tassies
Margarine, Salt, Egg, (3 oz) cream cheese softened, Brown sugar, Vanilla, Flour, Pecans (broken), PASTRY, FILLING, Butter, 
Pastry: Combine and refrigerate 1 hour. Roll into 24 balls and press in  bottom and sides of miniature muffin pans.    FILLING: Blend all filling ingredients except pecans. Put some pecans in  bottom of pastries; add filling; sprinkle rest of pecans on top. Bake at  324 degrees for 20 minutes.
