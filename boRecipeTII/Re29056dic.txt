Baked Potato Crisps
Unsalted butter, Potato peelings, with flesh, 
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Place skins on lightly oiled or buttered  baking sheet. Dot with butter. Bake until skins are quite golden and crisp  (about 20 minutes). Drain on paper towels and serve immediately.    Makes about 6 dozen potato "crisps."
