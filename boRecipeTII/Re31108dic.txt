Banana Spice Cookies
Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Shortening, Cloves, Baking soda, Eggs, bananas, Flour, Baking Powder, Salt, 
Mix well shortening, sugar & eggs. Stir in bananas. Mix dry  ingredients & stir in. Chill 1 hour. Heat oven to 375. Drop rounded  Tbls. of dough 2" apart on lightly greased baking sheet. Bake 8-10  minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
