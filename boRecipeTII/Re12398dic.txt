Zucchini-Dill Stuffed Shells
Salt, olive oil, (35 oz.) tomatoes, Grated Parmesan, olive oil, Egg, Tomato sauce, Container; (15 oz.) ricotta, Onion, (12 oz.) jumbo shell pasta;, Salt, Minced carrot, Garlic, Black Pepper, Black Pepper, Shredded zucchini, Chopped fresh dill; (or 3, 
FROM LHJ ONLINE http://www.lhj.com    A light tomato sauce and a filling thats loaded with vegetables and herbs  give our stuffed shells a fresh, lively taste.    Prep time: 35 minutes Baking time: 25 to 35 minutes Degree of difficulty:  Easy    1. Make Tomato Sauce: Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add onion  and carrot; cook until tender, 6 to 8 minutes. Add tomatoes, salt and  pepper, breaking up tomatoes with spoon; simmer 10 minutes.    2. Heat oven to 375F. Heat oil in large skillet over high heat. Add garlic  and cook 15 seconds. Stir in zucchini, salt and pepper; cook until zucchini  is tender and liquid is evaporated, 10 minutes. Stir in ricotta, Parmesan,  egg and dill.    3. Spread 1/2 cup Tomato Sauce in shallow 4-quart baking dish. Fill each  shell with rounded tablespoonful zucchini mixture; place in dish. Spoon  remaining sauce over shells. Cover and bake 25 to 35 minutes, until very  hot. Makes 6 servings.    PER SERVING Calories 485, Total Fat 18 g, Saturated Fat 9 g, Cholesterol 78  mg, Sodium 1,049 mg, Carbohydrates 58 g, Protein 23 g.
