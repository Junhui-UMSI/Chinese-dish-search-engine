Williamsburg Scalloped Oysters
Onion, Paprika, garlic, Butter, Pepper, Flour, Worcestershire Sauce, Cayenne, Lemon juice, Fine cracker crumbs, Salt, green pepper, Oysters with liquid, 
Melt butter in saucepan; add flour, cooking & stirring until light  brown. Stir in paprika, salt, pepper & cayenne. Add onion, green  pepper & garlic; cook over low heat 5 minutes, stirring. Remove from  heat & add lemon juice, Worcestershire & oysters (which have been  picked over & heated in own juice). Pour into greased 1 1/2 quart  casserole; top with cracker crumbs. Bake in 400 oven for 30 minutes  or until bubbly.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
