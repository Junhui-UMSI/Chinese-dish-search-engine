Tomato Dressing
Miracle Whip, Clove garlic; chopped, India relish or piccalilly, Stuffed green olives;, Sugar, Green onions and stems;, Tarragon Vinegar, Salt to taste, Lemon juice, 
Place all ingredients in blender and blend until fine. Serve with fresh,  sliced tomatoes.    Recipe by: Tita Kaus    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #813 by The Taillons <taillon@earthlink.net>  on Sep 28, 1997
