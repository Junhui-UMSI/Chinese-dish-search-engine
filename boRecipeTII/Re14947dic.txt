3 Minute Bbq Sauce
Ketchup, Prepared mustard, Worcestershire Sauce, Corn syrup, Ground ginger, 
In a bowl, stir together all ingredients. Brush on meats or poultry during  last 20 minutes of grilling, turning frequently. Makes 1 1/2 cups.
