Tealas Black Bean Dip
Coriander seed, Fresh Lemon Juice, Diced tomato, Chopped jalapeno, Chopped cilantro leaves, Salt, Garlic, Ground raosted Mexican, Water, Diced onion, Dried black beans, Ground cumin, 
and yet another Houston Chronicle 1997 picks.....    Wash beans until clean; soak in warm water 6 hours, drain, cover with fresh  water & cook until soft. When done, mash beans coarsley and continue  cooking over medium heat. Add 1/2 cup water, cumin, coriander, oregano &  garlic; cook 5 more minutes. Stir in tomato, onion, jalapeno, cilantro,  lemon juice & salt. Serve as a dip with chips.    Servings: 4-5 Source: Houston Chronicle from Tealas restaurant in Houston,  TX  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 523 by jecraig@lan-inc.com on  Jan 14, 98
