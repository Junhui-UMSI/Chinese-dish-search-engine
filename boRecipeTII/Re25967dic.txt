Bacon Corn Bread
Sweet milk, Egg, well beaten, Baking Powder, Corn-meal, Salt, White flour, Sugar, Diced bacon, 
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt.  Add  corn-meal. Combine egg, milk, and bacon which has been crisped. Add  dry ingredients. Fill well-oiled muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake in hot  oven (450 F) 15 minutes. 10 minutes. Mrs. Fred Snell, Erie, PA.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #245    Date: Sat, 7 Sep 1996 19:36:21 -0400    From: BobbieB1@aol.com
