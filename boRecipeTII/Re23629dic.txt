Artichoke Balls
Garlic clove -- minced, Italian bread crumbs, Artichoke hearts -- mashed, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, 8 oz can, Egg -- beaten, 
Saute garlic in oil. Add egg and artichokes. Cook over low heat for 5  minutes. Remove from heat. Add Parmesan cheese and 1/4 cup bread crumbs.  With a teaspoon, form into balls. Roll in remaining bread crumbs. Chill 2  hours or until ready to serve.    Recipe By     : Elizabeth Powell    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
