Quick Ravioli Soup
Low-salt chicken broth, Green chili peppers, chicken, Carrot, parmesan cheese, Ravioli, cheese-filled, Fresh basil, Onion, Garlic, 
#1. In a 4- to 5-quart soup pan over high heat, bring broth, onion, carrot,  green chilies, garlic, and basil to a simmen Cover, reduce heat to low, and  simmer 15 minutes. #2. Turn heat to high and bring broth to a boil. Add  ravioli and cook until just barely tender to bite, 4 to 6 minutes. Add  chicken and cook until hot, about 2 minutes more. #3. Ladle soup into  bowls, and sprinkle wth parmesan cheese.
