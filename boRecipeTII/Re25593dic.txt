Avocado Salsa #1
VEGETABLES, Tomatoes; diced, Ripe avocado; peeled and, Olive oil; or, Green chili; finely diced,, Jalapeno pepper; fresh or, Garlic, Salad oil, Red onion; diced, Red wine vinegar, Green pepper; seeded and, DRESSING, Tabasco sauce, Salt, 
Joel Ehrlich <Joel.Ehrlich@salata.com> Combine the vegetables in a medium  bowl.  Mash the garlic with salt in a cup or small bowl. Add the vinegar,  oil and Tabasco sauce.  Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss to  combine the ingredients.  Serve chilled or at room temperature.    CHILE-HEADS ARCHIVES    From the Chile-Heads recipe list.
