Zurich Goulash
(8 oz.) tomato sauce, Dill seed, Sour cream, Worcestershire Sauce, Boneless beef chuck, cut, Hungarian sweet paprika, Pepper, Salt, Water or wine (or, Basil, Sugar, Dry mustard, Onions, chopped, Caraway seed, Finely minced garlic, Butter, 
In large frying pan or dutch oven, saute meat and onions in butter until  browned. Add remaining ingredients, except sour cream. Stir well to blend.  Cover pan and simmer slowly until meat is tender, about three hours. Remove  from heat and stir in sour cream. Serve over hot buttered noodles.
