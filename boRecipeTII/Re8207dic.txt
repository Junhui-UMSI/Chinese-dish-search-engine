Potato Griddle Cakes
Potatoes; grated, Baking Powder, Flour; sifted, Salt; level, Egg, Pepper, 
Mix and bake on greased griddle in three-inch cakes. Serve with butter  and syrup.    Source: Mrs. W. D. Mosure, Ewing Grange, Hocking County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.  Compiled by members of Ohio State Grange Home Economics Committee.
