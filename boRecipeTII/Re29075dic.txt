Baked Potato Sticks
Baking potatoes, Black Pepper, Butter, Salt, 
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Scrub and quarter potatoes. Melt butter  in a 9- by 12-inch ovenproof baking dish in oven.    2. Place potato quarters in dish and turn to coat with melted butter;  arrange potatoes cut side down and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake  until tender when pierced with a sharp knife (30 to 35 minutes).    Recipe By     : The California Culinary Academy    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
