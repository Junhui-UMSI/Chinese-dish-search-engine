Alabama Corn Bread
Commercial sour cream, Eggs, Self rising cornmeal, Vegetable oil, (8 3/4 ounce) cream style, Baking Powder, 
(This cornbread is so moist you may not want butter!)    Combine corn, sour cream, eggs and oil; beat well. Combine cornmeal and  baking powder; stir into corn mixture. Pour into greased 10 inch iron  skillet. bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until done. Yield 8  servings.
