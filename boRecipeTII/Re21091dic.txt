Two Cheese Seafood Linguine
Shrimp, peeled, deveined, Crumbled blue cheese, Whipping cream, Tomato, Medium, chopped, Dried oregano, crushed, Butter, Grated paresan cheese, Shredded swiss cheese or, Green Onions, 
1. Cook Linguine to package directions.  2. While Linguine is cooking, heat garlic, oregano and  butter 1 min in saucepan, stirring frequently.  3. Add tomato and continue to cook for 3 min or until  tomato is cooked. 4. Add shrimp, cook until just pink.  5. Add remaining ingredients.  6. Cook covered until cheese melts and sauce is hot.  7. Serve over drained linguine.        You can substitue lobster, crab or whatever you  like in place of        the shrimp. This is quick and easy to prepare  and quite good...
