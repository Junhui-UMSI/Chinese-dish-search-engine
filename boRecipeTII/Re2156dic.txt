Artichoke Pie
9" pie shell, Mozzarella, shredded, Pepperoni, diced, Artichoke hearts, frozen, Eggs, Grated cheese, 
Cook artichokes, drain and cut. Beat eggs thoroughly and add all  ingredients. Mix well and pour into 9" pie shell. Bake at 375 degrees for  45-60 minutes. May be frozen after baked. Serve as part of antipasto or as  a first course.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Aug  31, 1998
