Aunt Gins Vegetable Soup
Sugar, Celery, Flank steak cut up, Onion, Potatoes; chopped, Salt and pepper to taste, Canned tomatoes, Canned corn, Chopped cabbage, Canned green beans, 
Combine in crockpot.(you may not need all the juice from the corn & gr.  beans) Cook on low at least 8-10 hours. You can use home canned or store  bought vegetables.  Walt MM    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #263      Date: Wed, 25 Sep 1996 23:36:59 -0400      From: Walt Gray <waltgray@mnsinc.com>
