Quick Oriental Chicken and Cashews
Ready to serve chicken broth, Cashews, Soy sauce, Microwave spirals uncooked, (14-oz) chicken chow mein, 
In 2 quart microwave safe casserole, stir together pasta, chow mein and  broth. Cover; microwave at high 8-10 minutes, stirring once, or until pasta  is tender. Stir in cashews and soy sauce to taste.
