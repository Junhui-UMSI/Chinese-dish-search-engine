Aunt Lilas Snowball Cookies
Nuts, Vanilla, Sugar, Flour, All-Purpose, Butter, Powdered Sugar, 
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    2.  Cream sugar and butter until light & fluffy.    3.  Add vanilla and nuts.  Add flour gradually.    4.  Roll into small balls.  Place on baking sheet, and bake 15 to 20  minutes in a 350 degree oven.    5.  Roll baked balls in powdered sugar while still warm.
