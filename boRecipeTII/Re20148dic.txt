Vanilla Sauce
Vanilla, Nutmeg, Nonfat Milk -- or skim, Egg Beaters. 99% Egg, Flour -- or 2 tsp, Cornstarch, Substitute -- 1/4 C, Sugar -- or 1/2 C + 3 pkg, 
Boil milk and beat in sugar, flour, and Egg Beater that have been mixed  together.  Simmer until it thickens, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat  adding vanilla and nutmeg.    It is very good.
