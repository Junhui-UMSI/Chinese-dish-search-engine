Zeek Rum Balls
Crushed walnuts, Cocoa, Dark corn syrup, Rum, Crushed vanilla wafers, Powdered sugar, 
Mix all of the above ingredients in a large bowl. Roll the mixture  into small balls.  Roll the balls in powdered sugar.    NOTES You can substitute your favorite spirits for the rum.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
