Quick Miso Soup
Water, Miso paste, Carrot, corn kernels, Red cabbage, chinese egg noodles, 
Put the water on to boil. Chop up the carrot and cabbage. Throw in. When  the water boils, throw in the noodles. Add miso to taste. Stir. Throw in  the corn. Eat.
