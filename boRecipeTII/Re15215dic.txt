Whipped Cream Topping
Heavy cream, Sugar, Vanilla, 
Whip cream until almost stiff. Add sugar and vanilla and beat until  cream just holds a peak.      Sprinkle 3/4 teaspoon unflavored gelatin over 2 tablespoons milk and  stir thoroughly. Place over boiling water and stir until dissolved. Add to  cream warmed to room temperature. Chill and whip.      Tops 1 (9") pie. Source: Culinary Arts Institute
