Vegetable Potage
Diced peeled potato, Jerusalem artichokes;, Sliced carrot, Bay Leaves, Diced onion, Chopped peeled celeriac, Granny Smith apple; chopped, Salt, Leek; sliced, Garlic, Thyme sprigs, Water, 
Combine all ingredients in a Dutch oven; bring to a boil. Cover, reduce  heat, and simmer 40 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Discard thyme  and bay leaves. Place vegetable mixture in a blender in 2 batches; process  until smooth (serving size: 1 cup).
