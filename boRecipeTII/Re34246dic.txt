Bbq Burgers
Flour, Onion, Salt, pepper to taste, Vegetable oil, Walnuts, Vegetarian "bacon" bits, Rice, Barbecue sauce, Dash garlic powder, 
Process walnuts in food processor and pour into a bowl. Process rice and  pour in with walnuts. Saute onion in oil until tender and add to walnuts  and rice along with the rest of ingredients except flour. Add enough flour  to make patties. Refrigerate for one hour. Fry in a little oil for several  minutes, turning occasionally. Serve on a bun with barbecue sauce and  pickles.
