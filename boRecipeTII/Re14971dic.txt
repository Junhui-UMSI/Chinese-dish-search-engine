30-Min Tuna Burgers
Dry bread crumbs, Green onions, minced, Celery stalk, chopped, Vegetable oil, Alfalfa sprouts, Whole-wheat hamburger buns, Fresh dill, chopped, or, Canned water-packed tuna, Pepper, Dijon mustard, Horseradish, Salt, Eggs, 
In bowl, lightly beat eggs; mix in bread crumbs, dill, horseradish,  mustard, pepper and salt.    Drain tuna; mix into bowl along with onions and celery. Shape into four  1/2-inch thick patties.    In nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium heat; cook patties, turning once,  for 10 minutes or until golden brown and set.    Sandwich in buns.
