Priscillas Salad Dressing
Vinegar, Mix, Vegetable oil, Cottage cheese -- small, Curd, 
Place cottage cheese in a medium-sized bowl; set aside.  In a blender  container, combine Priscillas Salad Dressing Mix, vegetable oil and  vinegar.  Blend for 5-8 seconds.  Pour over the cottage cheese; fold  together until well blended.  Cover and refrige    Makes about 3 c .
