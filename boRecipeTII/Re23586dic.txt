Arroz Verde (Green Rice)
Salt, Parsley; fresh, chopped, Green peppers coarsely, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Brown rice; long-grain, raw, Water, Onions; yellow,, Water, 
This recipe even works with old wilted green peppers.  Recipe By: New  Recipes from Moosewood Restaurant p. 198.     Puree the vegetables in a blender with 1/2 cup of water until smooth.  Saute the rice in oil for 2 or 3 minutes, stirring to prevent the rice from  scorching.  Add the vegetable pure and cook for 5 more minutes, stirring  often.  Add 3 cups of water and the salt and bring to a boil.     Reduce the heat, cover, and cook until the liquid is absorbed, 30 to 45  minutes.
