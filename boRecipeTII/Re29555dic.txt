Tartar Sauce 1
Mayonnaise, Dry mustard, Capers; drained, Fresh parsley, Gherkins; chopped, Tabasco sauce, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
Mix lemon juice, mustard, and mayonnaise in a small bowl. Stir in chopped  pickles, parsley, and capers. Season to taste with a few drops of Tabasco  sauce. Cover and chill for 1/2 to 1 hour to blend flavors.
