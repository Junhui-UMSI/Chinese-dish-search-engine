Potato Salad
Peas, blanched, cooled & cubed, Carrots, sliced & blanched, Green bell pepper, chopped, Celery, chopped, Lemon juice, Brown sugar, Tabasco sauce, olive oil, salt and pepper, Parsley, Garlic cloves, chopped, Potatoes, peeled, cooked,, 
Put cooked potatoes & blanched vegetables with the celery & bell pepper in  a large bowl.  In a blender, combine oil, lemon juice, sugar, garlic,  tabasco sauce, coconut, parsley, salt & pepper & blend for a few seconds  till smooth.  Pour over the vegetables & gently toss till they are well  coated.  Chill in the refrigerator.    Posted by Mark Satterly in Intercook
