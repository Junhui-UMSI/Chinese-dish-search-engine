Chocolate Crunch Brownies
Butter, , Sugar, Eggs, Baking cocoa, All-purpose flour, Vanilla extract, Salt, marshmallow cream, Creamy peanut butter, Semisweet chocolate chips, Crisp rice cereal, , 
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar; add eggs.  Stir in cocoa, flour,  vanilla and salt. Spread into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in baking pan.  Bake at 350F for 25 minutes or until brownies test done. Cool. Spread  marshmallow cream over cooled brownies.  In a small saucepan, melt peanut  butter and chocolate chips over low heat, stirring constantly. Remove from  the heat; stir in the cereal. Spread over marshmallow layer. Chill before  cutting.
