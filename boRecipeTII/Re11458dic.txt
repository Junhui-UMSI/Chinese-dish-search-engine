Raspberry Bread Pudding
Raspberry-filled croissants;, Sugar, Semisweet chocolate chips, milk, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 325. In a 2-quart baking dish, place croissants. In large  bowl, combine eggs, milk and sugar. Add chocolate chips. Pour over  croissants. Bake 45-50 minutes or until kife inserted near center comesout  clean. Typed by "Essie" Ethel R.
