Asparagus Venetian
Fresh asparagus -or-, Lipton Onion Butter (see, Grated Parmesan cheese, Frozen asparagus; cooked, Diced Mozzarella cheese, 
Arrange cooked, drained asparagus in 8-inch baking dish; drizzle with onion  butter; sprinkle with cheeses. Bake 10 minutes at 450. Serves 6. Lipton  Onion Butter:  Thoroughly blend 1 envelope Lipton Onion Soup Mix with      1/2 lb butter.    MRS ROY L.
