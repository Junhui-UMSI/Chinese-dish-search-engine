Zucchini Bake
Cloves, Bay leaf, Bell pepper; chopped, Zucchini, Butter, Grated Parmesan cheese, Flour, Salt, Onion, Tomatoes, Brown sugar, 
Slice zucchini into a greased baking dish. In a skillet, melt butter; blend  in flour. Add tomatoes; add pepper, onion and seasonings. Cook for 5  minutes. Pour over zucchini in dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake at      350    degrees for 45 minutes.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
