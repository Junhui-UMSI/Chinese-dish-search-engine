Quick Cheesecake
Graham cracker crust, Carton; (8 oz) Cool Whip, Neuschafel lo-fat cream, Powdered sugar, 
Beat all together and pour in pie shell. Chill. Good alone or may top with  fruit.    Recipe by: Debbie Collins    Posted to MasterCook Digest V1 #363 by " Liz Custer" <custer@dconn.com> on  Dec 5, 1997
