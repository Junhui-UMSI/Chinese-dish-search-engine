Tater Tot and Hamburger Casserole
milk, Lean ground beef, French Style green beans, Tater tots, Cream of mushroom soup, Cheese, Onion, 
From: "mchlsn@pacbell.net" <mchlsn@pacbell.net>    Brown hamburger and onion together, until tender. Layer hamburger in bottom  of 11 x 13 inch pan. Add green beans. Cover beans with grated cheese. Mix  together mushroom soup and milk and pour over top. Top with the frozen  Tater Tots. Bake 30 to 45 minutes in a 425 degrees oven or until Tater Tots  are brown.
