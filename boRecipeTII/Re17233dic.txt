Wacky Cake
Sugar, Flour, Vinegar, Baking soda, Vanilla, Canola oil, Cocoa, Baking Powder, Salt, Lukewarm water, 
Sift dry ingredients together in an ungreased 8 inch square cake pan. Make  3 holes equally spaced in mixture. Pour vanilla into one, vinegar in  another and oil in the third. Pour water over top and stir well, until all  ingredients are mixed.  Bake at 325F for 45-50 min.    Source Co-Op recipe page, Sept 1993 ad.    Shared but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier
