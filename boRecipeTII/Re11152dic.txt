Raisin Cookie-Bar
Baking Powder, Salt, Egg; unbeaten, Cinnamon, All-purpose flour, C and H Granulated Sugar, C and H Granulated Sugar, Vanilla, Seedless raisins, Butter, 
Beat egg; gradually beating in sugar.  Stir in melted butter and vanilla.  Sift together and add the flour, baking powder and salt; mix well.  Add  raisins.  Spread in greased 9-inch square pan.  Sprinkle top with  sugar-cinnamon mixture.  Bake in 350 degree oven about 20 minutes, or  until done.  (Dont overbake.)  Leave in pan.  Cut into 24 bars.
