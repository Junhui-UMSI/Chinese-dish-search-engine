Stuffed Moose Round Steak Supreme
Burgundy, salt and pepper, Pine nuts, Butter, Ground venison, Water, Onion, Moose round steak, 
Melt 1/2 stick butter in large heavy skillet at low heat. Brown onion,  adding ground venison and pin nuts. Tenderize steak with meat tenderizer  using directions on bottle. Lay steak on floured board. Spread ground meat  mixture on round steak and roll up as a jelly roll. Tie with butcher  string.  Melt remaining butter and brown steak on all sides. Add water and  burgundy.  Cover and steam for two hours or until tender. Posted By  Susantd@aol.com On rec.food.recipes or rec.food.cooking
