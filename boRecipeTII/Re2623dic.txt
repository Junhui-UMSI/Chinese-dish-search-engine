Peanut Brittle - My Favorite
Sugar, White KARO, Spanish PEANUTS with skins;, Water, SODA; (heaping w/o lumps), 
Mix top 3 ingred together and bring to a boil Cook til thin syrup 230?. Add  Peanuts. Boil, stir til light tan color - 300?. Add soda, stir very well.  Pour onto buttered cookie sheet. DO NOT Spread.  Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by ClayCooker <ClayCooker@aol.com> on Jan 6,  1998
