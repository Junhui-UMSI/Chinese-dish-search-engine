Barbecue Sauce with Honey
Onions, finely chopped, Red wine vinegar, Chili powder, Red pepper flakes, Coriander, ground, Oregano fresh or 1 tb dried, Tomato Paste, 6 oz, Salt, Tomatoes, crushed, 28 oz, Bay Leaves, Black Pepper, freshly ground, Thyme sprig fresh or 1 ts, Honey, olive oil, Garlic, finely chopped, Tabasco sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Lemon juice, Cumin, ground, 
Servings:  8    Heat the oil in a saucepan and add the onions. Cook, stirring until wilted.  Add the garlic, cook briefly and add the remaining ingredients. Bring to a  simmer and cook, stirring often, about 30 minutes. Let the sauce cool and  use for basting. Refrigerates well. Makes 5 cups. From The Gazette,  91/08/14,typed by James Lor.
