Vanilla extract, Sweet Potatoes, Brown Sugar, Water, Lemon Rind, Fresh Lemon Juice, Ground cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 350F.    Place potatoes in a large Dutch oven, and cover with water; bring to a  boil. Reduce heat, and simmer 20 min. Drain; let cool.    Peel and slice potatoes. Layer slices in a 13x9" baking dish coated with  cooking spray. Combine sugar and next 5 ingredients; stir well. Drizzle  syrup over potatoes. Cover and bake at 350F for 40 min or until tender.  Spoon syrup over potatoes before serving.    Yield: 7 servings Serving Size: 1 C    Points: 4 Exchanges: 3 1/2 Starch    This was very good. Quite nice flavor.
