Apple Cheddar Muffins
Baking Powder, Salt, (thin) unpeeled red apple, Unbleached flour, Eggs, Baking soda, milk, Pecans, Quick-cooking oats, Apples; finely chopped, Sharp cheddar cheese; coarse, Butter, Shortening, Cinnamon-sugar mixture, Granulated sugar, 
From: lfergus!larry (Larry Moore)    Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Cream the shortening and sugar together  and add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Combine  the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a mixing bowl, mix  lightly. Gradually stir the flour mixture into the shortening mixture. In  this order, add the oats cheddar and pecans, mixing well after each  addition. Gradually add the milk, stirring until all the ingredients are  just moistened. Grease the muffin pans and fill each cup 2/3rds full of  batter.  Dip the apple slices in the melted butter and then into the  cinnamon-sugar.  Press 1 apple slice into the top of each muffin. Sprinkle  lightly with cinnamon-sugar and bake for 25 minutes in the preheated oven,  or until golden brown.    DCIEM!LFERGUS!LARRY@LSUC.UUCP    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
