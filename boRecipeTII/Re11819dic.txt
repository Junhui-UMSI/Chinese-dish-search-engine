Raw Apple Cake
Baking soda, Salt, Eggs, Walnuts,chopped, Flour, Apples (tart), Sugar, Vanilla, Vegetable oil, 
Date: Tue, 21 May 1996 10:44:08 -0400 (EDT)    From: arturo@erols.com    Peel and chop tart apple. Beat eggs with vanilla and oil. Add sugar, flour  and salt. Mixture will be stiff. Work in apples and walnuts with your  hands. Spread in 9x13 pan and bake at 350f, one hour.    Icing: 1 stick butter, 1 cup brown sugar, 1/4 cup milk. Boil 2-1/2 minutes  and spread on cooked cake while its still hot.    Posted to MealMaster Recipes List, Digest #141
