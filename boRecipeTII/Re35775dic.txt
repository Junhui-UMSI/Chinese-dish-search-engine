Stuffed Crabs
Claw crab meat, Green onions, Eggs, celery, Butter, onions, Bread crumbs, bellpeppers, Flour, Parsley, 
Saute vegetables until wilted,season to taste with salt and red pepper.  Mix bread crumbs,flour,eggs,green onions,and parsley. Combine with other  ingredients and fill crab shells. Dip in a batter of egg and milk,then in  flour and finally in bread crumbs. Fry until golden brown.
