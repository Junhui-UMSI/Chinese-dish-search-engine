Wesleys Pinto Bean Bread
Active dry yeast, Salt, Lard, Whole milk; warm, Bread flour, Sugar, Pinto beans; cooked & mashed, 
All ingredients should be at or above room temperature before starting. Add  ingredients to the pan in the order listed.  Select "White Bread".
