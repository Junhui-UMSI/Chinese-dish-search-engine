All-American Snack
Can salted peanuts, Celery salt, Garlic salt, Butter, Cheerios?, Seasoned salt, Wheat chex?, Grated Parmesan cheese, Thin pretzel sticks, 
In large mixing bowl or slow-cooking pot, mix together pretzels, cereals,  and peanuts. Sprinkle with garlic salt, celery salt, seasoned salt, and  cheese. Pour melted butter over all; toss until well mixed. Cover and cook  in slow-cooking pot on low 3 to 4 hours. Uncover the last 30 to 40 minutes.  Serve as appetizer or snack.
