Baked Applesauce
Tart apples; peeled and, Ground cinnamon, Sugar, Vanilla, 
Taste of Home LowFat Country Cooking    Place apples in a 1 quart baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray.  In a small bowl, combine suar, cinnamon, and vanilla; mix well. Sprinkle  over apples. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes or until  apples are tender. Uncover and mash with a fork. Serve warm. 2 servings.      194    cal 1 gm fat.
