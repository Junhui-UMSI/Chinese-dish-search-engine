Yogurt-Shrimp Spread
Plain yogurt, Cream cheese, softened, Prepared horseradish, Snipped chives, Salt, Small shrimp, rinsed and, 
Mix all ingredients except shrimp thoroughly. Coarsely chop shrimp; stir  into cheese mixture. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours. Serve with bran snack  toast if desired. 2 cups spread; 50 calories per tb.    Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
