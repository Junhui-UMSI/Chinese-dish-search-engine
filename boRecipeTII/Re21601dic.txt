Apple Roast Hadrosaur
Quick-cooking tapioca, Beef bouillon granules, Carrot; small chunks, Hadrosaur meat (Maiasaurus)*, Celery, Cooking oil, Salt, Frzn apple juice, Ground cinnamon, White wine, 
If Maiasaurus meat is not available, lean pork will do.    Trim any fat from hadrosaur meat. Cut in half, if necessary, to fit into  crockpot. Put carrots and celery into bottom of crockpot. Sprinkle in  tapioca and add apple juice concentrate combined with other ingredients.  Cover, cook on Low for 10-12 hours or on high for 5 to 6 hours.
