Yogurt with Cucumber and Mint (Kheere Ka Raita)
Salt, Finely chopped fresh mint, Cucumber peeled and grated, Cayenne pepper, Black pepper, Plain yogurt, Roasted cummin seeds, 
Put  the  yogurt  in a bowl.   Beat lightly with a fork or  whisk  until  smooth and creamy.   Add all the other ingredients and mix.   Cover  and  refrigerate until ready to eat.    A favourite at all Indian restaurants, this accompaniment is the perfect  partner for spicy dishes as it has a deliciously cooling effect on your  palate.    Cuisinely... Imran C.
