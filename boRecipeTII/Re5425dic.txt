Pineapple Chicken
Onion, Soft brown sugar, Cornstarch, Soy sauce, Chicken Stock, Green pepper, Chicken, deboned, Carrots, Sherry, Pineapple (drained) 16 oz., Vinegar, Salt and pepper, 
Cut up chicken into chunks and boil until done, or can be cooked in the  microwave oven.  Cut the onion, pepper, and carrot into wedges. Drop these  into a small saucepan of boiling water and cook for 5 minutes. Drain well.  Mix together in a small saucepan the cornstarch, chicken stock, vinegar,  sugar, soy sauce and sherry. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, and  simmer for 2 or 3 minutes.  Add the vegetables to the sauce, then add the  chicken and pineapple and reheat. Salt and pepper to your liking. Serve  very hot over rice or chow mein noodles.
