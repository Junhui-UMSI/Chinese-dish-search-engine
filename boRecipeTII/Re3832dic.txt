People Puppy Chow
Peanut butter, Crispix cereal, Milk chocolate bits, Confectionery sugar, 
Melt chocolate bits with peanut butter. Pour mixture over cereal and stir  gently until coated. Pour into plastic bag with confectionery cereal. Shake  until coated. Store in air tight container.    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Barbra<barbra@pipeline.com> on Feb 08, 1998
