Whole Wheat Bread
Egg, Honey, Whole wheat bread, milk, Shortening, Water, All-purpose flour; (up to 4), Dry yeast, Salt, 
Combine 1 cup all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, yeast and salt. Mix  well. Heat milk, water, honey and short- ening until warm. Add to flour  mixture. Add egg and beat. Stir in remaining flour to make a firm dough.  Knead until smooth, about 5 minutes. Place in greased bowl. Let rise in  warm place, about 1 hour. Divide into 2 parts. Let rise about 30 minutes.  Bake at 375 degrees about 35 to 40 minutes until golden brown. Remove from  pans and cool.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Bill Spalding <billspa@ICANECT.NET> on Feb 24,  1998
