Pumpkin Bread #05
Salt, Eggs, Sugar, Raisins, Allspice, Canned pumpkin, Cinnamon, Flour, Nutmeg, Shortening (margarine; oil, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Ground cloves, 
From: gnew@orion.it.luc.edu (Geraldine A. New)    Date: 7 Feb 1994 01:08:08 GMT    Ive had this recipe for so long, I cant even remember where I got it.  Sift flour, soda, powder, salt and spices.  In another bowl beat the sugar  and oil until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time. Stir in pumpkin and flour  misture.  Add raising.  Turn into loaf pan.  Bake at 350 for 60 minutes.  Makes one loaf    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
