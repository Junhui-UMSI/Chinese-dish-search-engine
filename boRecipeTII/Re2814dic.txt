Peanut Butter Cream Cheese Pie
Peanut butter, Powdered sugar, cream cheese, CoolWhip, chocolate, 
In large bowl, combine peanut butter and softened cream cheese. Mix with an  electric mixer until well-blended. Add powdered sugar and blend very well.  Carefully fold in two-thirds of the CoolWhip.    Spoon peanut butter mixture into the pie shell. Top with remaining  CoolWhip. Chill.
