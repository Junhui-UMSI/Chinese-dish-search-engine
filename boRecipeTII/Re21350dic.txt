Uncooked Puttanesca Sauce
Black Pepper, Grated Parmesan, Tomatoes; ripe, seeded,, Salt, Hot red pepper flakes, Garlic; chopped, Kalamata olives; pitted, cut, Flat-leaf parsley; chopped, Dried basil; or oregano, Capers; drained, olive oil, 
** Note: Full flavored tomatoes are very important in this recipe.    COMBINE GARLIC, tomatoes, oil, basil, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper,  olives, capers, parsley, and cheese in large serving bowl. Add drained hot  pasta and toss until combined. Serve with extra cheese and pass the  peppermill.
