A Quick Way To Decorate Cake
Green food coloring, Vanilla to taste, Box cornflakes, Red hots, Marshmallows, White angel food cake, 
Date: Sun, 25 Feb 1996 22:41:09 -0500    From: "Vernon E. Phipps Jr." <cook4u@vivanet.com>    Melt marshmallows, add food coloring and vanilla. Add the cornflakes, stir  until well coated. Spread this onto a cookie sheet, shaping into holly  leaves. Place a red hot in middle. Let dry about 5 hours. Then top a white  frosted angel cake with the holly leaves.
