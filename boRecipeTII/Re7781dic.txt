Portobella Marinade
olive oil, Red wine vinegar, Thyme to taste, Garlic clove, chopped, 
Slice Portobellinis into 1/4-1/2 inch strips.    Combine all ingredients. Place mushrooms in glass baking dish, and pour  mixture over them. Marinate for 45 minutes. Then grill on barbeque for 2  1/2 minutes per side. Can also be broiled in oven broiler for same amount  of time.    Makes a good substitute for meat.
