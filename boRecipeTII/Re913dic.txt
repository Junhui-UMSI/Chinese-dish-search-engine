Hot Fudge Ice Cream Topping
Semisweet chocolate, Sugar, Evaporated milk, Butter or margarine, Vanilla, 
I saw these in Decembers Southern Living. Thought you guys might be  interested.    *Melt chocolate in a heavy saucepan over low heat. Stir in sugar until  smooth. Gradually add milk, stirring until smooth. Bring to a boil over med  heat, stirring constantly. Boil, stirring constantly, 6 min. Remove from  heat, and stir in butter and vanilla. Store in refrigerator up to 3 weeks.  Serve warm over ice cream.
