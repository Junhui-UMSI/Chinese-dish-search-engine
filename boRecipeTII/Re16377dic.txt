Wedding Punch
pineapple, frozen lemonade, Ginger ale, Raspberry sherbet, Fresh fruit, 
Prepare lemonade according to directions on can in a large punch bowl. Add  pineapple juice and mix well. Just before serving, add ginger ale, along  with sherbet if desired. Garnish bowl with fruit. Makes about 60 4-oz.  cups.    Recipe by: Austin American Statesman 6/9/93 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1  #563 by Sherry Zeiss <zeiss@tab.com> on Apr 12, 1997
