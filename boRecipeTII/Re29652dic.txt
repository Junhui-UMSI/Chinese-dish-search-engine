Tasty Veggie Spirelli Salad
Vinegar, Pepper, Green Pepper, Salt, Tomatoes, cut in wedges, Hard-cooked eggs, sliced, Onion, Paprika, Pitted black olives, Diet mayonnaise, Leonardo Veggie Spirelli, Snipped parsley, 
Combine the onion and vinegar and let it stand for a few minutes. Then add  the mayonnaise, salt, pepper and paprika and mix well. Mix the veggie  spirelli, tomatoes, green pepper, and olives in a large salad bowl; and  pour the mayonnaise mixture over all.  Toss lightly. Chill the salad for  several hours before serving. When serving, garnish with the egg slices and  parsley.  Makes 6 servings.
