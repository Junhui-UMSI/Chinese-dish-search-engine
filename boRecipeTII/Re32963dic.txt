Sweet Yam Pudding
Red garnet yams (up to 4), Blood oranges (or other, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Fresh-squeezed orange juice, 
Individually wrap yams in heavy duty aluminum foil and bake at 350 for 3  hours. This will "carmelize" the pulp giving it a smooth, sweet flavor.  Cool yams overnight in refrigerator. Remove foil and yam skins (they should  fall off)and put pulp in food processor. Add juice, cinnamon, and nutmeg to  food processor and blend for 8-10 minutes until pudding is light and  fluffy. Chill and serve in individual dessert dishes garnished with fruit.  Posted to fatfree digest V97 #038 by Western Office <ncs0146@interpath.com>  on Mar 26, 1997
