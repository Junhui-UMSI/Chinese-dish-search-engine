Beau Monde Dip
Corned Beef, Beau Monde Seasoning, Mayonnaise, Parsley Flakes, Dark Rye Bread, Sour cream, Dill Weed, Dehydrated Onions, 
Mix all ingredients (except bread) chill for 1 hour.    Hollow bread & cube the middle to serve w/dip    Pour dip into bread shell & serve on platter.    NOTES : Beau monde spice can be found any where that sells "Spice Island  Products"  Recipe by: Mary Ryznick    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #887 by LMeyers325@aol.com on Nov 6, 1997
