Pudding Chocolate Chip Cookies
Eggs, Chocolate chips; (peanut, Butter or margarine; (2, Vanilla, Granulated sugar, Flour, Brown sugar, Box instant pudding (vanilla, Baking soda, 
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Beat butter, sugars, pudding mix (dry), eggs and  vanilla in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until light and  fluffy. Mix in flour and soda. Stir in chips. Drop by teaspoonfulls 2  inches apart, onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 10 minutes or until golden  brown. Remove from cookie sheets and cool on wire racks.
