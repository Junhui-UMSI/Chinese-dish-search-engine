"Beef" and Bean Burrito
Tortillas, flour, Taco sauce, Refried beans, Cheese, cheddar, low-fat, Natural Touch Taco Mix, 
Prepare taco mix according to package directions. Place 3 ounces of Taco  Mix and 1/4 cup of refried beans on each flour tortilla. Roll up tortilla.  Pour sauce over burritos and sprinkle with cheddar cheese. Heat in  microwave oven for 2 minutes or conventional oven for 10 minutes at 350  degrees or until hot.    From "Recipes, Natural Touch"
