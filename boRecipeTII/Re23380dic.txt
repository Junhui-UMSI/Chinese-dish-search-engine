Tunados
Avocados, Celery; chopped finely, Italian salad dressing, Tuna; draind and chunked, Chives; chopped, Cheese; grated, mild, Potato chips; crushed, Mayonnaise, 
Cut avocados in half lengthwise, remove pits, but do not peel. Combine  tuna, celery, chives, Italian dressing, and mayonnaise. Mix well. Spoon  into avocado halves and arrange on shallow baking dish. Pour 1/2 inche  boiling water into the bottom of dish. Sprinkle tops of avocados with  cheese.  Bake at 400F for approximately 15 minutes. Before serving,  sprinkle with the crushed potato chips.    From:  The Tuna Cookbook
