Two-Bean Chili
Mexican Style Chili Beans, (New Orleans Style), Tomato sauce, (Van Camps), undrained, Canned whole tomatoes, Ground red pepper, Lean ground beef, (optional), Chili powder, Cheddar cheese, undrained, Garlic powder, Onions, 
In 4-qt. saucepan or Dutch oven, brown ground beef with onion; drain.  Add  remaining ingredients except cheese.  Simmer 20 to 25 minutes or until  flavors are blended.  Top with cheese, if desired.    TEN 1 CUP SERVINGS    MICROWAVE DIRECTIONS: In 3-qt. microwaveable bowl, combine ground beef and  onion.  Microwave at HIGH 4 to 6 minutes or until meat is no longer pink,  stirring once; drain.  Add remaining ingredients except cheese.  Microwave  at HIGH 25 to 30 minutes or until flavors are blended, stirring after 15  minutes.  Top with cheese, if desired.
