Tropic Sun Spareribs
Bottle (12-oz.) chili sauce, Spareribs, or country-style, Ground giner, Dry mustard, Cloves garic, pressed, Brown Sugar, Onion, Can (20-oz.) Dole crushed, 
Rub ribs with garlic.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Arrange onion in  large baking pan.  Place ribs on top. Add 1/4 cup water to pan. Cover with  foil.  Bake in 350-degrre F. oven 1/2 hour. Combine remainig ingredients.  Spoon over ribs.  Bake, uncovered, one hour longer. S    Serves 4    SOURCE: Doles Quick N Easy Recipes.
