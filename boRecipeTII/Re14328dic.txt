Whole Fish Soup #1
Whole 2-pound bass or carp, Vegetable stock, Scallion stalks, -(up to), Salt, Fresh ginger root, Peanut oil, 
1. Have fish cleaned and left whole. Cut scallions in 1-inch sections.  Mince ginger root.       2. Bring stock to a boil. Add fish, scallions and ginger and simmer,  covered, 10 minutes.       3. Add peanut oil and salt. Simmer, covered, 10 minutes more.       NOTE: Serve the fish on a platter, the soup in a tureen.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4.
