Tater Tot Casserole #1
frozen tater tots, Onion, cream of chicken soup, cream of celery soup, Ground chuck, cheese, cream of mushroom soup, 
Brown onions and chuck in skillet. Line casserole with tater tots. Top with  meat mixture. Combine soups and add to casserole. Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake at 350 for 30-45 minutes.
