Won-Ton Soup
Soy sauce, Pepper, Chicken broth, Soy sauce, Salt, Ground pork, shallots, Egg, Won tons, 
Mix pork,egg,soy sauce,salt & pepper. Fill won - tons. Cook in boiling  water until won-tons float to top. Remove from water. Heat chicken stock  and  add won tons, ladle into soup bowls and add shallots and soy sauce.  Sandra A - Young - Chees  recipe.
