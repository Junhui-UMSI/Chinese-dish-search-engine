Potato Loaf
Egg whites -- at room, Fat-free cottage cheese, Black pepper, Shredded, Parmesan cheese -- grated, Salt, Bread crumbs, Mushrooms -- coarsely, Temperature, Onions -- sliced thin, Marjoram, Potatoes -- peeled/, Chopped, 
Preheat oven at 400.  Prepare a loaf pan cooking spray.  In a blender,  puree cottage cheese for 1 minute or until smooth.  In a mixing bowl,  combine pureed cottage cheese, mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, onions, egg  whites, bread crumbs, marjoram, salt, and pepper.  Fold in potatoes.  Transfer mixture to prepared pan(s).  Bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes or  until firm.    Recipe By     : Readers Digest Live Longer Cookbook    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
