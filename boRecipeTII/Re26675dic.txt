Baileys Irish Cream Cake
Pkg. yellow cake mix, Instant chocolate pudding, Vodka, Eggs, Water, Vegetable oil, 
Combine cake mix, pudding mix, oil, water, vodka, liqueur and eggs in bowl.  Beat until smooth. Pour into greased and floured 10 inch Bundt pan. Bake at  350~F 40 to 50 minutes or until done. Remove from pan and cool completely.  Mix a glaze of Baileys and powdered sugar to a thick consistency and  drizzle over cake.
