Potatoes, Onions and Tofu Supreme
Pepper, Water, Green onions, Potatoes, Tofu chunks, 
Saute chopped onions in water only. Steam potatoes.   Then combine chunks tofu, 3/4 cup water, 4 medium potatoes, 4-6 onions  (chopped), 1/4 tsp pepper in saucepan and simmer 10 to 15 minutes. Season  to taste.     From the files of DEEANNE
