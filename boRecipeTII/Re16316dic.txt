Watermelon Rind Preserves
Sticks ginger, Water, Sliced lemon, Watermellon, Sugar, Water, Lime, 
Pare green and pink from inch cubes of 1 melon.  Soak overnight in 1 quart  water and 1 tbsp lime  Rinse well. Boil cubes in clear water for 15  minutes. Drain. Boil 1 quart water, 2 cups sugar, 1/2 sliced lemon, 2  sticks ginger, for 5 minutes.  Add rind and cook until clear. Let stand  overnight.  Repeat, and seal in sterilized jars.
