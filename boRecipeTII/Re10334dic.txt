Quick Butter-Bagel Snacks
Banana, egg, Wheat germ, bagels, frozen, Peanut butter, Oranges, Honey, 
Heat or toast bagels according to package directions. Spread each bagel  half with 1 1/2 tablespoons peanut butter. Top 4 bagel halves with orange  slices (3 slices per bagel half). Top remaining 4 bagel halves with banana  slices. Sprinkle all bagels with wheat germ and drizzle with honey.
