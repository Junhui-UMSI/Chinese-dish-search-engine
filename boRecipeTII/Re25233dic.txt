Tony Lobiancos Orange/lemon/garlic Chicken
Shallots, mince, Oranges, Lemons, Chicken pcs, Ex-virgin olive oil, Red pepper flakes, Paprika, White wine, Milliliters Garlic, mince, Salt and ground pepper, Parsley, mince, Rosemary, 
Put chicken in a baking dish. Squeeze oranges and lemons over chicken.  Sprinkle on all remaining ingredients except wine. Top with some  slices of oranges and lemons. Bake in preheated 400~ oven for 20  minutes. Pour over white wine and broil for 15 to 20 minutes to crisp.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
