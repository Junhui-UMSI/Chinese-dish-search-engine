Avocado Chef Salad
Hard-cooked eggs; sliced, Swiss cheese; julienned, Cooked meat(s) *, Avocado; flesh cubed, Croutons, Chopped red leaf lettuce, Chopped watercress, Pitted black olives, Thinly sliced mushrooms, 
*Note: ham, turkey and/or roast beef, julienned Combine lettuce,  watercress, mushrooms, olives and croutons in large bowl. Toss lightly to  mix. Arrange avocado and eggs on top of salad. Top with Swiss cheese and  meat. Make vinaigrette.  Just before serving, shake dressing, pour over  salad and toss.
