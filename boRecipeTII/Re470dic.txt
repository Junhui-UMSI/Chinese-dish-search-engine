Lemon Pie
Flour, baked pie crust, Boiling water, Egg yolks, Sugar, rind and juice of 3 lemons, Butter, Whipped cream, 
Beat egg yolks, flour and sugar together until real light. Put in butter,  then boiling water and lemon juice. Cook until mixture thickens. Pour into  a pie shell. Garnish the top with whipped cream or Cool Whip. An old family  recipe.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
