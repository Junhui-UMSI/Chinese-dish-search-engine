Banana Apricot Bread
milk, All-Purpose Flour, Baking Powder, bananas, Butter, Powdered sugar, dried apricots, Sugar, Baking soda, Salt, Egg, 
Preheat oven to 350?. Sift dry ingredients together. Add apricots. Combine  remaining ingredients and add to dry ingredients, stirring just until well  blended. Turn into a 9x5 glass loaf pan that has been greased and lined  with wax paper. Bake at 350? for 1 hour or until done. Cool in pan for 10  minutes, then turn out on wire rack. Sprinkle with powdered sugar. Yield: 1  loaf.    NANCY DROEGE    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
