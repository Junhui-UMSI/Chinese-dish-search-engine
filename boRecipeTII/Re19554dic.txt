Anise Rolls
Anise flavoring, Anise seed as needed, Flour, Eggs; beaten until frothy, Sugar, Baking Powder, 
Sift together flour and baking powder. Mix eggs, sugar and anise flavoring.  Fold in dry ingredients.  Drop by tablespoon onto greased cookie sheet about 2 inches apart. Sprinkle  with anise seed. Bake at 350 degrees until brown around the edges, about 4  minutes. Remove quickly from sheet and roll while hot. Sprinkle with icing  sugar before serving.
