Raisin Pineapple Drop Cookies
Raisins, Baking Powder, Flour, Baking soda, Brown Sugar, Butter, Crushed Pineapple, drained, Egg, Salt, Vanilla, 
Cream butter, vanilla and sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg and cream  well. Stir in raisins and pineapple. Sift dry ingredients together. Add  gradually to cream mixture. Stir until well blended. Drop by teaspoons on  greased cookie sheets. Bake 12-15 minutes in a preheated 375oF Oven.    Source: "The Yankee Kitchen" 04-07-93 [#7] Betty from Brewster
