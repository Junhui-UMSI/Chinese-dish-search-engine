Winter Squash Cake
All-purpose flour, Cream cheese, Granulated sugar, Raw winter squash or, Cinnamon, Salad oil, Baking soda, Chopped walnuts or pecans;, Salt, Brown sugar, Vanilla, Margarine, Powdered sugar, Cream Cheese Frosting, Chopped walnuts or pecans;, Eggs, 
Beat sugars and eggs until well blended. Add salad oil and beat again. Add  dry ingredients, beating well. Add squash and nuts. Beat well again. Pour  into well greased 10 inch tube pan and bake 55 minutes (or more) at 350 F  or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes before dumping. Frost  with Cream Cheese Frosting. Top with chopped nuts if desired.    Frosting: Cream the cream cheese and margarine together. Gradually add  powdered sugar and mix well. Finally add 1 teaspoon vanilla.
