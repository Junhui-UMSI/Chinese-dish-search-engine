Apple Butter Pumpkin Pie
Apple butter, Milk, evaporated, Eggs, Pumpkin, milk, Cinnamon, Pastry shell, Salt, Brown Sugar, 
Thoroughly combine pumpkin, apple butter, brown sugar, salt and cinnamon.  Blend in eggs, evaporated milk and milk.  Turn into unbaked pastry shell.  Bake in 400 oven for 45-50 minutes or until knife inserted just off-center  comes out clean.  Cool and serve with "Cool Whip" or ice cream.
