Pineapple Picnic Salad
Cream cheese, Whipped desert topping, Vanilla pudding, instant mix, Pineapple, crushed, Pineapple chunks, 
Esther A. Middlewood of Kentwood, Michigan, brings this sweet cream  cheese-and-fruit salad in a cooler with her whenever she takes off for a  picnic at Scotts Mills Park, near Kalamazoo. She also brings along a camera  and some walking shoes.    1.  In a large bowl, gradually stir the undrained pineapple into the cream      cheese.  Stir in the pudding mix.    2.  Fold in the pineapple chunks and whipped topping. Cover and chill      till serving time.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "deborah kuhnen" <debkuhnen@email.msn.com>  on Apr 20, 1998
