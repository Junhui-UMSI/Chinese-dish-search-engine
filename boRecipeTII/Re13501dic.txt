Whole-Wheat Granola
Vegetable oil, Cinnamon, Orange juice, Sliced almonds, Salt, Whole-wheat flour, Honey or substitute, Flaked coconut, Regular oats, uncooked, Wheat germ, 
This is 1/4 original recipe.    Mix first 5 ingredients in electric mixer.  Combine oil, honey, and  juice and pour over mixture.  Mix until completely blended.    Spread evenly in a shallow pan and bake in 250 F oven for 45 minutes.  Stir in coconut and almonds and bake 30 minutes more.  Cool and stir  in raisins.    * 1/2 cup serving - 227 calories*  1 starch, 1 fruit, 2 fat  exchanges. * 27.9 grams carbohydrate, 4.7 grams protein, 11.9 gm  fat*, 4 gm fiber * 98.4 mg sodium, 189.1 mg potassium, 0 cholesterol  *NOTE: To cut calories and fat, reduce the coconut and oil by a third.
