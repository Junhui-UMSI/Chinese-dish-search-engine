Apricot-Glazed Chicken
Boneless skinless chicken, Dijon mustard, Honey, Apricot all-fruit spread, Light soy sauce, Margarine, melted, 
Coat a broiler pan with nonstick cooking spray; place chicken on pan.  Combine remaining ingredients; brush half over the chicken. Broil 5-6  inches from heat for 5 minutes. Turn chicken over; brush with remaining  apricot mixture. Broil until juices run clear.
