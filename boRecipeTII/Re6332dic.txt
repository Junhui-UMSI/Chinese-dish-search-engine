Plantain-Pineapple Saute
Butter, Ground cinnamon, Honey, Plantains, Ground allspice, Pineapple juice, fresh pineapple, 
Place plantain chunks in saucepan of water to cover. Bring to boiling.  Reduce heat. Simmer 5 minutes. Drain. In a skillet, melt the butter or  margarine. Add plantain pieces. Saute 2 minutes. Add pineapple chunks,  spices and pineapple juice or water. cover and simmer 5 minutes more. Add  honey, to taste. Serve as a dessert or side disk for ham or pork.
