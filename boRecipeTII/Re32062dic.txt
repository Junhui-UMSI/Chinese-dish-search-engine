Barbecued Catfish
Catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, Oil, vegetable, Catfish; 1 lb. each, Paprika, Vinegar, Pepper, Sugar, Salt, 
Clean, skin, and fillet fish.  Combine remaining ingredients.  Baste  fish with sauce.  Place fish in a well-greased, hinged fish basket.  Place on grill about 3 to 4 inches from coals.  Cook 7 to 8 minutes  on each side or until fish flakes easily, brushing frequently with  the sauce.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, July, 1980. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
