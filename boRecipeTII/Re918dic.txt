Hot Mulled Cider
Nutmeg, whole, Cinnamon sticks, Allspice, whole, Cloves, whole, orange, Cloves, whole, Apple cider, Brown sugar, 
In a large saucepan combine apple cider/juice, brown sugar, and nutmeg. For  spice bag, place cinnamon, allspice, and 1 teaspoon cloves in cheesecloth  and tie; add to cider mixture.  Bring to boiling. Reduce heat; cover and  simmer 10 minutes.  Remove spice bag and discard. Serve cider in mugs with  a clove-studded orange wedge in each.
