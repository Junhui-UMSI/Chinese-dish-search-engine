Apple Pie #2
Salt, Green apples, Flour, Or, Cinnamon, Sugar, Frozen Pet Ritz pie crusts, Butter; or more, 
Pare, core and slice apples. Line large pan with one pie crust. Put apples  in pan. Mix sugar, flour, salt and cinnamon. Sprinkle over each layer of  apples. Dot with butter. Cover with remaining crust; press edges together  and slash top. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes or until edges are brown.  Reduce heat to 350 and bake about 30 minutes longer. VARIATION: Substitute  peaches for apples.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
