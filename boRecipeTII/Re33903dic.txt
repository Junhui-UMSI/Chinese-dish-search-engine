Sweet and Spicy Bbq Sauce
Plus 1 t. dried thyme,, Brown sugar, Cayenne pepper, (15-ounce) tomato sauce, Cider vinegar, Salt, Onion, Dijon mustard, 
Combine all in medium saucepan. Simmer 15 minutes to blend flavors. Season  with pepper to taste. (Can be made two days ahead. Cover, chill.)    credit: Bon Appetite - Nov. 1992 Posted to bbq-digest V5 #360 by  JLipsitt@aol.com on Jul 6, 1997
