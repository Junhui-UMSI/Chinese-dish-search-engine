Rancho San Antonio Beef Marinade
Worcestershire Sauce, Black pepper, Ground cumin, Cayenne pepper, White vinegar, Salt, Water, Brown Sugar, Tomato juice, 
Combine ingredients. Marinade 4-5 lb beef in mixture in shallow pan in  refrigerator 3-4 hours before barbecueing, turning occaisionally to coat  meat with marinade. Do not shake off liquid before cooking.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/meats.zip
