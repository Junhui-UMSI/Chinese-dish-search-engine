Phyllo Chicken Bites
olive oil, Garlic, Lemon juice, Phyllo dough, Butter, Boneless, skinless chicken breast, 
Mix chicken pieces, lemon juice, olive oil and garlic together and  refrigerate overnight. Place a piece of chicken on the end of a strip of  phyllo dough. Roll up, tucking the ends to make a little pillow. Dip in a  bowl of melted butter, then bake for 15 minutes at 400 F/200 C.    Source: Eatons Great Canadian Recipe Challenge 1993 From the collection of  K.Deck
