Ranch Style Beef Stew
Frozen peas, Water, Potatoes, med. diced, Whole wheat flour (optional), Salt, Pepper, Pearl onion, canned, Lean beef, trimed, bite size, Carrots, large, sliced, Worchestershire sauce, 
Place meat in 1 quart baking dish.  Brown in slow oven at 325F for 45 min,  stirring occasionally.  Add 1 cup of water to dish. Cover, bake for 1 hr or  until meat is tender.  Meanwhile, cook carrots in remaining 2 cups water.  When partially cooked, add potatoes and cook until all tender. Add onions  and peas.  Put vegetables into beef dish. Thicken liquid from vegs. with  flour if desired.  Add worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper to liquid.  Pour over meat and vegs. Cover and return to oven for 20 min. Cal: 371, Fat  1 1/2g.
