Pineapple-Raisin Jumble
DOLE Crushed Pineapple in, DOLE Blanched Slivered, Grated DOLE Orange peel, Margarine, softened, DOLE Raisins, All-purpose flour, Sugar, Vanilla extract, 
Drain pineapple well, pressing out excess liquid with back of spoon.  Reserve juice for beverage.    Beat margarine and sugar in large bowl until light and fluffy. Stir in  drained pineapple and vanilla. Beat in flour and orange peel. Stir in  almonds and raisins.    Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls, 2" apart, onto ungreased cookie sheets.    Bake in 350F. oven 20-22 minutes until firm. Cool on wire racks.    Makes 2 to 2 1/2 dozen cookies.
