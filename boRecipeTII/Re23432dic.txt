Turkey and Broccoli Stir-Fry
Garlic, Broccoli florets, Turkey breast, Honey, sweet red peppers, Hot cooked brown rice, Boneless & skiness, Low sodium soy sauce, Vegetable oil, chicken stock, Defatted, 
Cut the turkey into strips.  In a meduim bowl, stir together the soy  sauce, honey & garlic.  Add the turkey and toss to coat.        In a wok or large frying pan over medium-high heat, heat the oil for 1  minute.  Add the turkey and stir-fry for 3 minutes, or until opaque. Remove  with slotted spoon and keep warm.        Add the broccoli and peppers to the pan.  Stir-fry for 2 minutes. Add  the stock and continue to stir-fry for 2 minutes, or until the vegetables  are crisp-tender. Return the turkey to the pan and toss briefly. Serve over  the rice.    Preparation time:  10 minutes Cooking time: 10 minutes    Per serving:  403 calories, 8.2 g. fat(18% of calories), 4.6 g. dietary  fiber, 68 mg. cholesterol, 404 mg. sodium.
