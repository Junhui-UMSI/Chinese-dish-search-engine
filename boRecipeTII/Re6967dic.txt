Pops Meatloaf
Salt, Dry mustard, Green pepper; diced, Horseradish, Hamburger, Bread crumbs; sliced & diced, Catsup, Egg, milk, Onion; diced, 
Mix, shape, put in loaf pan. Pour 1/4 catsup over the top & bake 40 min. or  until the top is crusty.    Recipe by: Joe McWilliams    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Katey Kelly-Collins <KateyKC@AOL.COM> on Sep 10,  1997
