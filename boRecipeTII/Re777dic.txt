Hapuupuu and Veggie Stew (Fish Stew)
Dried Rosemary, Bay leaf, Pepper, Garlic, Fish stock or chicken stock, Cornstarch, Water, Juice of lemons, Parsley, Vegetable oil, Butter, Large carrot, Large onion, Stalks celery, Sprigs parsley, Fresh thyme or 1/2 ts dried, 
Chop all veggies thin and cut fish to 1 1/2 inch cubes. Heat oil and butter  in large stick-free pan, adding herbs and spices to top.  cook lightly then  add stock and bring to a boil.  Add fish to boiling liquid and cover. Cook  15 mins on simmer.  Remove fish and strain veggies. Serve veggies to side  of fish.  Add cornstarch to liquid and bring to boil. Let it thicken and  pour over fish.  Add lemon juice and parsley for looks.    From: xxcarol    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Sep 14, 98
