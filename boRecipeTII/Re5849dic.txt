Pips Pumpkin Roll
Pumpkin, Lemon juice, Cream cheese, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Eggs, Sugar, Butter, Flour, Salt, Ginger, Powdered sugar, Baking Powder, 
Beat for 5 minutes the 3 eggs. Slowly add sugar,pumpkin,lemon juice, flour,  baking powder,ginger,cinnamon and salt. Grease and flour a small cookie  sheet (jelly roll pan) Put in the above mixture and bake at 350 for 20  minutes. SPRINKLE "cake" with powdered sugar and tip onto a linen towel. (  Sprinkle towel with P. sugar also).. Roll and refrigerate for 30 minutes.  Filling: Mix together -1 c Powdered sugar, softened cream cheese, butter  and vanilla Spread over "cake". Again "roll" the cake and refrigerate.  Slice & Enjoy. This recipe can easily be doubled.
