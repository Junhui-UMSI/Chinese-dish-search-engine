Baked Apples
Cinnamon, Butter, Red Cooking Apple, Brown sugar, 
Remove the core from each apple leaving a bottom in each apple. Make a  mixture of the brown sugar and butter. Fill each apple with the mixture.  Pre-heat oven to 325F. Bake apples on a rimmed cookie sheet for 45-60  minutes or until apples are done. Sprinkle each apple with cinnamon and  serve. For microwave, cook for 5-10 minutes. From: Syds Cookbook.
