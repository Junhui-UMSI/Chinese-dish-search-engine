Honey-Mustard Pork Scallopini
Crushed butter-flavored, Dry bread crumbs, Vegetable oil, Butter or margarine, Boneless pork chops (about, 4 ounces each,)trimmed, Honey, Spicy brown mustard, 
Flatten pork to 1/8 inch thickness.  Combine honey and mustard; brush over  both sides of pork.  In a shallow bowl, combine cracker and bread crumbs;  and pork and turn to coat. In a skillet, heat oil and butter. Fry pork for  2-3 minutes on each side or until crisp and juices run clear. Yield: 4  servings.
