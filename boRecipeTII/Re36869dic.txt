Strawberry Rum Cake
Vegetable oil, Rum, GLAZE, Juice of 1/2 lemon, CAKE, Stawberries, Rum, White cake mix, confectioner's sugar, TOPPING, Eggs, Vanilla, Stawberry gelatin, dry, 
CAKE: Mix all together in a large bowl.  Beat for 2 minutes at high speed;  for 1 minute at low speed. Pour into greased and floured 10" tube pan or  Bundt pan. Bake for 60 minutes at 350 degrees. Cool in pan on cake rack for  20 minutes.  Do not invert on a bottle to cool.    GLAZE: Combine glaze ingredients and bring to boil over medium heat. Simmer  gently for 3 minutes.  Prick cake with toothpick or tines of a fork and  slowly pour small amount of glaze on cake. Arrange strawberry halves  attractively on top of cake.  Pour remaining glaze over strawberries and  cake. Serve at once of refrigerate.
