Banana Bread
Sugar, Baking soda, Nuts; chopped, Eggs; well beaten, Ripe bananas; mashed, Salt, Seedless raisins, Butter or margarine, Flour, Baking Powder, 
Cream butter or margarine and sugar; add eggs. Sift together dry  ingredients and add to mixture. Add bananas and mix well; add nuts and or  raisins. Pour in well greased loaf pan; bake at 325 degrees F. for 45  minutes to 1 hour.
