Pineapple Coffee Cake
Butter or margarine, Quick coffee cake, Pineapple tidbits, Brown sugar, Ground cinnamon, Flour, 
*Drained and cut in half. Spread Quick Coffee Cake batter into a 9 by 9 by  2-inch pan, bottom rubbed with shortening. Make topping by combining flour,  sugar, cinnamon, and butter. Spread pineapple over batter. Sprinkle topping  over pineapple.  Bake in a 400 F. oven about 25 minutes.
