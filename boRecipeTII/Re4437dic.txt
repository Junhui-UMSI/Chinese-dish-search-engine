Pesto n Pasta Chicken
Vegetable cooking spray, , Pine nuts, Salt, Red pepper flakes, Plain nonfat yogurt, Garlic, Fresh spinach leaves, Grated Parmesan cheese, fresh basil, Penne pasta, Boneless, skinless Chicken breast meat, 
Slice the chicken into bite-sized pieces and set aside.  Cook the pasta  according to the package directions, omitting any salt or fat.  Drain  well, set aside and keep warm.    Combine the spinach and the next seven ingredients in the container of an  electric blender or food processor.  Cover and process until smooth.  Pour  into a small bowl and set aside.    Coat a large skillet with cooking spray; place over medium-high heat until  hot.  Add the chicken and saute until browned; stir in the reserved  spinach mixture.  Cover and simmer 5 minutes or until it is thoroughly  heated and the chicken is tender (do not boil).    Add the reserved past to the skillet.  Toss gently.  Serve immediately.
