Pounded Venison
See below, 
Pound venison steaks (If you put in a heavy plastic freezer bag it  cuts down on the mess) Flour well with flour seasoned with salt &  pepper..Fry then make gravy (a white sauce) in the pan the meat was  fried in. Serve with bread.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
