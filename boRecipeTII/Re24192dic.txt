Transylvanian Nosh
Tiny dill pickles or, Russian-style feta cheese,, Kalamata olives; halved, Paper-thin slices Hungarian, Hard-boiled eggs; sliced, Jarlsberg cheese; shredded, Loaf country white bread, Butter, 
Cut bread into 1/2-inch-thick slices, then cut each slice in half to get       16    slices.    Cut each salami slice into thirds.  Butter bread. Arrange 2 pieces salami  on each slice bread.  Add 1 egg slice to cente of sandwich, then 1 piece  feta cheese, 1 half pickle and 2 halves olives. Sprinkle jarlsberg cheese  over toppings.  Arrange on platter. Makes about 16 slices or 8 servings.
