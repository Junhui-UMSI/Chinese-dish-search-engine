Lemon Alfredo Fettuccine with Shrimp
Alfredo sauce, Lemon Rind, Fresh Lemon Juice, Shrimp, Green onion, Fettucine, 
1. In a 5 quart pan, brind 2 1/2 to 3 quarts water to a boil over high  heat. Add pasta and cook until just tender to bite, 2 to 3 minutes.    2. Meanwhile in a 10 to 12 inch nonstick frying pan over medium heat, bring  sauce, lemon peel and lemon juice to a simmer.    3. Drain pasta well. Add pasta and shrimp to sauce, stir to coat and heat  just until shrimp are hot. Sprinkle with green onion.
