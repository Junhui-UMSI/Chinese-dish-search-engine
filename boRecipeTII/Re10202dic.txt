Quick and Easy Cheese Bread Sticks
Frozen bread dough; thaw, WALDINE VAN GEFFEN, Paprika, Parmesan; grate, 
Roll out dough. Sprinkle cheese all over. Sprinkle with paprika. Cut 1"  strips with pizza cutter. Twist. Bake on ungreased sheet at 350~ for 15 to  20 minutes. Source: Yvonnes Cookbook.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #760 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Aug 25, 1997
