Quesadillas
Olives, onions, tomatoes,, Cheese, grated, Choice of: chopped black, Chilies or jalapeno peppers, (6" square) Flour tortillas, 
Spray cooking surfaces with non-stick spray or brush with margarine. Place  2 tortillas into sandwich maker. Place 2 tablespoons of mixture into each  triangular pocket. Put 2 tortillas on top. Lower lid, latch and cook 3-5  minutes or until done.  Serve with sour cream, salsa, or guacamole.
