Absinthe Suissesse
Pernod, Heavy cream, Orgeat; (almond simple, Egg white; (optional), Shaved ice, 
In freezer chill an old-fashioned glass until frosted. In a blender combine  all ingredients; process 5 seconds or until frothy.
