Uncooked Pork Filling #1
Water, Pork, Scallion stalk, Fresh ginger root, Soy sauce, 
1. Mince or grind together pork, ginger root and scallion stalk.    2. Then blend well with soy sauce and cold water.    NOTE: Eggrolls made with this filling must be steamed for 30 minutes to  cook the pork (see Cooking the Eggroll, under "Information: Eggrolls").  They may then be served as is, or deep-fried briefly to make them crisp.  (Pork with some fat is best for this filling.)
