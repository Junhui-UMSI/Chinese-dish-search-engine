Peanut-Butter Sundae Smoothie
Honey, Vanilla ice milk, milk, Wheat germ, Smooth peanut butter, 
Stir peanut butter, honey and milk together. Cook over low heat, stirring  constantly. Remove from heat when peanut butter has melted; stir in ice  milk and wheat germ; serve chilled. Serves 4.    Recipe by: Home Cooking--July 1996 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #731 by  L979@aol.com on Aug 9, 1997
