Bacon Blue Cheese Dressing
Cornstarch, black pepper, garlic, Fresh buttermilk, White wine vinegar, Salt to taste, Blue cheese, Bacon, Chicken Stock, Shallots, Heavy cream, Water, 
Julienne bacon across grain into short strips. Place in a medium sautJ pan  over medium heat and sautJ for 6 minutes or until bacon is crisp. Remove  and drain on paper towel. Pour off half of bacon fat from pan. Add shallots  and garlic and sautJ for 2 minutes. Stir in white wine vinegar. Bring to a  boil, stirring constantly, then add cream, chicken stock, and buttermilk.  Return to a boil. Dissolve corn starch in water. when dressing returns to a  boil, stir in corn starch little by little until dressing is slightly  thickened. Season with salt and pepper. Add blue cheese and bacon strips.  Keep warm.    NOTES : I have the Planning and Pantry List for this theme. E-mail me if  you want it.
