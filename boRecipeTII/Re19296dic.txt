Vegetable Soup with Lentils (Vegan)
Carrot, salt and pepper, Potato, Green bell pepper, Lentils, Thyme, stock, Savory, Celery, fresh parsley, fresh mushrooms, Fresh Lemon Juice, Bay Leaves, Garlic, Oregano, Nion, 
Bring to a boil in a large saucepan the ff chicken broth(you can use  vegetable broth) and the lentils. Add and then reduce heat and cover the  rest of the ingredients and simmer for one hour. Before serving, remove the  bay leaves and puree half the mixture in blender and add to rest of soup to  thicken.
