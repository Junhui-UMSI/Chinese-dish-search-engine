Asparagus Almondine
Asparagus, Garlic powder, almonds, Butter or margarine, 
Melt butter in a large skillet. Add almonds and garlic powder. Cook over  low heat until almonds are toasted; push to one side.    Add asparagus to the other side; cover and heat. Spoon almond butter over  asparagus and top with parsley if desired.    Recipe by: Del Monte Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #682 by L979@aol.com on  Jul 21, 1997
