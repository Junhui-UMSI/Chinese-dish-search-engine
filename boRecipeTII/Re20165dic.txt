Vanilla Wafer Cake
Coconut, Sweet milk, Vanilla wafers, crushed>>>>, (12 oz. box), Sticks of oleo, Pecans, Eggs, Sugar, 
Cream sugar and butter until fluffy, add eggs, 1 at a time, beating  after each addition.  Add milk and crushed wafers. Fold in coconut and  pecans.  Pour into prepared tube or Bundt pan and bake at 300 F. for 1 hr.  or until done.       When cake tests done, remove from oven and let cool in pan on rack for  15 min.  Turn out onto another rack (or same rack) and continue cooling.       When cake is completely cool, either glaze, dust with 10x sugar or ice  as desired.
