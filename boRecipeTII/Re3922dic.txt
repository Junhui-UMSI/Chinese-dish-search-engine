Pepper Steak #1
Salt, Garlic salt, Bell peppers; chopped, Lemon juice, Round steak, Cornstarch, Ginger, Molasses, Vegetable oil, Water, Soy sauce, Green onions w/tops; chopped, Ribs celery; cut in julienne, 
Slice steak in thin julienne strips. Saute in hot oil until brown. Add  remaining ingredients. Cook 3-5 minutes or until vegetables are clear &  crisp. Serve over rice or Chinese noodles or both. Yields 4-6 servings.    DOROTHY WENZEL    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
