Artichoke-Spinach Casserole
Marinated artichoke, Butter, Spinach, Cream cheese, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Spread artichoke hearts in the bottom of a  buttered casserole dish, in roughly one layer. Top with spinach.    Cream together the cream cheese, butter and cheese and spread evenly on  top. Bake covered for 30 minutes, then uncovered for 10 minutes.
