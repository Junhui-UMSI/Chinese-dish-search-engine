Amaretto Coffee
Amaretto, Warm water, Dessert Topping *, Instant Coffee Crystals, 
*    Dessert topping should be in a pressurized can.    In a 2-cup measure stir together water and instant coffee crystals.  Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power for about 4 minutes or just till  steaming hot.  Stir in Amaretto.  Serve in mugs.  Top each mug of coffee  mixture with some dessert topping.    From Better Homes & Gardens "Microwave Cooking for One or Two".  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mwc-4-2.zip
