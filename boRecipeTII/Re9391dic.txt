Pumpernickel Bread
Salt, Bread flour, Cornmeal, Cocoa, Molasses, Dry yeast, Dry milk, Whole wheat flour(1 1/3 oz), XKGR41A Don Fifield, Butter, Rye flour (1 oz), Water (7 1/2 fl.oz), 
Place all ingredients (except liquids and yeast) inside the bread pan. Add  liquid ingredients. Close cover and place dry yeast into the yeast holder.  Press start.
