Almond Yum-Yums
Butter, Brown Sugar, Egg, Vanilla, All purpose flour, Eggs, Brown Sugar, Vanilla, All purpose flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Flaked coconut, Chopped almonds, toasted, 
Cream softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg and  vanilla; mix well. Stir in flour. Press mixture into bottom of buttered  13x9" baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Combine eggs, sugar  and vanilla; beat until well blended. Stir in combined flour, baking powder  and salt. Fold in coconut and almonds. Spread over baked layer. Bake 20 to  25 minutes longer. Cool and cut into bars.
