Banana Fritters
Flour, Flour, bananas, Sugar, Baking Powder, Egg, Salt, milk, shortening, 
Combine egg, milk and shortening.  Add to sifted dry ingredients and blend  until smooth.  Peel and cut bananas into 1/2 inch pieces. Roll in flour and  dip into batter (above), coating banana pieces. Drop from spoon into deep  fat at 375 degrees and fry 4 to 6 minutes until evenly browned. Drain and  sprinkle with confectioners sugar. Yield: 8 servings.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
