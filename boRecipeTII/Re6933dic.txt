Poor Mans Bean Pie
Eggs, Unbaked pie crusts, Coconut, Sugar, Pecans, chop, WALDINE VAN GEFFEN, Salt, Vanilla, Cooked pintos with no,, Butter or margarine, 
Combine all ingredients and put in pie crusts. Bake in preheated 300~ for  45 to 60 minutes.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #616 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  May 19, 1997
