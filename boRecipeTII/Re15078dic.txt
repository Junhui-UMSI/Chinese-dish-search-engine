Western Sandwich (Denver)
Onions, PEPPER SWT GRN FRESH, BREAD SNDWICH 22OZ #51, Salt, LETTUCE FRESH, EGGS SHELL, PEPPER BLACK 1 LB CN, HAM SECTIONED CURED, 
1.  COMBINE HAM, EGGS, ONIONS, PEPPERS, SALT AND PEPPER; STIR TO MIX WELL.    2.  POUR 1/3 CUP MIXTURE ON LIGHTLY GREASED GRIDDLE.  COOK UNTIL BOTH SIDES  ARE LIGHTLY BROWNED.    3.  PLACE OMELET ON 1 SLICE OF BREAD; TOP WITH LETTUCE AND SECOND SLICE OF  BREAD.    4.  CUT EACH SANDWICH IN HALF.  SERVE HOT.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 1, 9 LB CANNED HAM WILL YIELD 7 LB 8 OZ FINELY CHOPPED  HAM    NOTE:  2.  IN STEP 1, 1 LB 2 OZ DRY ONIONS A.P. WILL YIELD 1 LB CHOPPED  ONIONS, AND 10 OZ FRESH SWEET PEPPERS A.P. WILL YIELD 8 OZ CHOPPED SWEET  PEPPERS.    NOTE:  3.  IN STEP 1, 1 1/2 OZ (1/3 CUP PLUS 1 TBSP) DEHYDRATED ONIONS AND  1 OZ (3/4 CUP) DEHYDRATED GREEN PEPPERS MAY BE USED.  SEE RECIPE CARD A-11.
