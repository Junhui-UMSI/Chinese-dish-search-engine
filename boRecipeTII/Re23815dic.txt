Asian Chicken Pot Pie
Chopped Chinese chives, Garlic cloves, chopped, Stalk celery, Butter, Water chestnuts, Chinese black vinegar, Chinese 5 spice, Chopped ginger, Onions, Bokchoy, salt and pepper, olive oil, Head broccoli, Cornstarch, Rosemary sprigs, 6-Ounce Boneless And, Carrot, Sheets phyllo dough, Chicken broth, 
Cut chicken into 2-inch strips. Cut all vegetables into 2-inch strips and  blanch. In a large skillet over high heat, saute the chicken strips with  the vinegar. Add in the cornstarch. Season with 5 spice powder, salt and  pepper. Add garlic, onion and ginger. Stirfry for 5 to 6 minutes. Add  chicken stock and vegetables. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes. Check seasoning.  Chill. Layer four 1/2-inch sheets of phyllo dough, brushing with butter in  between sheets and place in a four inch pie tin. Repeat the process for  four pans. Divide equally the chicken mixture on each pan. Add chives. Fold  the corners into the center. Bake in a 400 degree oven for 12 minutes.  Transfer immediately to serving plates and garnish with rosemary sprigs.
