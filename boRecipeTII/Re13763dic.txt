Wild Rice with Chili
DecilitersVegetable Broth, Parboiled Rice With Wild, Chili, Fresh Chives, Chopped, Chili Pepper -- finely, Corn oil, Rice -- (3 dl.), Onion -- finely chopped, 
Fry onions in oil until translucent. Add chili and rice, followed by the  broth. Boil 20 min. covered. Let stand for 5-10 min. and fluff with a fork.  If desired, garnish with chili and fresh chives.    Recipe By     :
