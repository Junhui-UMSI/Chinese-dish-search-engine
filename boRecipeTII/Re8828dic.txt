Praline Ice Cream Sauce
Baking soda, Light corn syrup, Buttermilk, Vanilla extract, Packed, Coarsely chopped pecans, Unsalted butter, Golden or light brown sugar, Granulated sugar, 
Combine the sugars, buttermilk, butter, syrup, and baking soda in a heavy  saucepan. Bring to the boiling point, then reduce the heat and simmer for  10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from the heat and stir in the  vanilla and nuts. Cool completely before serving. The sauce will thicken.
