Apple Butter Squares
Brown sugar, Apple butter, Quick cooking oats, Flour, Butter or margarine, 
Mix flour and sugar, cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Stir in oats until well blended. Press half the mixture (2 1/4 cups) firmly  into greased 8 inch square pan. Spread apple butter to within 2 inches of  edges. Sprinkle remaining oats mixture over apple butter. Press carefully  but firmly. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for about 40 minutes or until  lightly browned. Cool in pan on rack.
