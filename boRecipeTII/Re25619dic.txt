Avocado Veggie Burgers
Prepared mustard, Onion, Tomato puree, Sized avocado, Whole wheat bread crumbs, Cooked soybeans, Salt, 
1. Mix all the ingredients except bread crumbs together in a blender or  food processor, and blend until smooth. Put mixture into a bowl and add  bread crumbs until the mixture sticks together enough to be formed into  patties    2. Make two burger shaped patties and fry in oil until theyre browned.  Serve on buns with your favorite burger fixings!    MEANANTI@CWIS.ISU.EDU    (MEANS_ANTIGONE_M)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
