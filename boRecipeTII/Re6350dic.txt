Plantation Supper
Cream of mushroom soup, Egg Noodles, Onion, Corn, canned, Ground beef, Cream cheese, 
Brown the ground beef and onions.  Separately, prepare the noodles and then  add them to the beef/onions. Soften the cream cheese and slowly stir it in.  Then add the remaining ingredients and let it simmer for approx. 20  minutes.
