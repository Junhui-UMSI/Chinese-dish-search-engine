Vegan Chicken Broth Mix
Nutritional yeast flakes, Salt, Dried parsley, Celery seed, Onion powder, Garlic powder, A, B, Italian seasoning, 
Blend "A" to a smooth powder in a blender, shaking several times to  redistribute. Add "B".    Store in container with a tight-fitting lid. Use at ratio of one teaspoon  per cup of hot water.    Posted to fatfree digest V98 #011 by JBennicoff <JBennicoff@aol.com> on Jan  11, 1998
