Tennessee Crab Crescents
Salt, water chestnuts, Worcestershire Sauce, Pillsbury crescent rolls, Lemon juice, Onion, Swiss cheese, crabmeat, Curry powder, 
Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl except rolls. Separate rolls into  8 triangles. Spoon about 2 Tablespoons crab mixture into each triangle.  Roll up, starting at the shortest side and roll toward longest side. Bake  at 375? for 15 to 18 minutes. Serve hot. Crescent rolls may be cut in half,  lengthwise, before adding crab and before being rolled, to make smaller  appetizers.    TANDY COBB (MRS.
