Beef Pastry Pockets
Cheddar cheese, Frozen puff pastry, thawed, Pepper, Drained and cooled, Eggs, lightly beaten, And cut into 5" squares, Cottage cheese, Ground beef, browned,, 
Combine filling ingredients. In center of squares, put a heaping 1/3 cupful  of filling; moisten edges of pastry with water, fold over to form  triangles; press with fork to seal.    Bake at 400F. aabout 20 minutes or until golden brown.    Taste of Home Ground Beef Collection 1996 Edition
