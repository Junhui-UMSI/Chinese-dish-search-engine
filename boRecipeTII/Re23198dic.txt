Tuna Cookies
(8 oz.) tuna (in oil), Veg. oil, Water, Cornmeal, Flour, Salt, 
Mix all ingredients well, kneading just to combine. On floured surface,  roll out to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut out into little squares or other  shapes. PLace on greased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30  minutes. Cool on wire rack.    Store in airtight container in fridge.    Posted to Bread-bakers Digest by Marg aka MEMAW <maf@fn.net> on Mar 29,  1998
