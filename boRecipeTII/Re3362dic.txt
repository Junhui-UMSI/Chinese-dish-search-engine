Peas with a Hint of Mint
Water, Shelled fresh peas, Sprig of fresh mint, Trimmed green beans, 
Put all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer for 10 minutes. Drain,  remove the mint, and puree the vegetables in a food mill to get rid  of the indigestible husks from the peas. Add water or formula, as  needed. In addition to perking up the flavor of the vegetables, mint  is well-known carminative, and can help relieve babys painful gas or  colic.
