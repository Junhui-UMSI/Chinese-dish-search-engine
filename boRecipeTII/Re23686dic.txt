Artichoke Mushroom Casserole
(6-oz) sliced mushrooms, Jar plain artichoke hearts, Bread crumbs, Butter as needed, Jar marinated artichoke, 
Cut up artichokes and mushrooms and marinate overnight in combined liquids  from artichokes and mushrooms. When ready to prepare, place a layer of  mixture in buttered casserole alternating with bread crumbs, ending with  bread crumbs topped with pats of butter. Bake in 350 degree oven for 40  minutes. Serves 4.    OVERNIGHT MARINATE    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
