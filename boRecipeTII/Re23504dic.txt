Aromataic Cannellini Beans - Master Recipe
Cannellini beans; dried, Water, Salt, Celery ribs; finely chopped, Garlic cloves; finely, Dried thyme, Yellow onion; finely chopped, 
Rinse the beans in a colander and pick out any stones, debris, or  funky-looking beans. Place the beans in a large bowl and add cold water to  cover by 2 inches. Let soak for 8-12 hours.    OR: quick-soak by placing the picked-over beans in a large saucepan and  cover by 1-2 inches of water. Bring to a boil, cover and remove from heat;  let stand for 1 hour.    OR: Use fresh cannellini beans, cooking with vegetables just until tender,  20-25 minutes.    Combine the beans, celery, onion, garlic, thyme and the 2 quarts water in a  larage pot. Bring to a boil, cover reduce heat and simmer until the beans  are tender but notmushy, about 1 hour. Drain well, season with salt.    Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator. Yields about 8 cups  cooked beans.    Recipe by: SF Chronicle; Sep. 24, 1997 - WEEKEND COOK    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #983 by Judi Moseley <judi@moseleygroup.com>  on Jan 4, 1998
