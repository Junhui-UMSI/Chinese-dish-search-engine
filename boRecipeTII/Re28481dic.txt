Baked Pasta and Cheese Supreme
fusilli pasta, Butter, Dried oregano, Garlic, bacon, Fresh bread crumbs, Onion, Hot pepper sauce, Cheddar cheese, Tomato sauce, 
Preheat oven to 400F. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.    Meanwhile, cook bacon in large ovenproof skillet over medium heat until  crisp; drain. Add onion, garlic and 1 teaspoon oregano to skillet; cook and  stir about 3 minutes or until onion is tender. Stir in tomato sauce and hot  pepper sauce. Add cooked pasta and cheese to skillet; stir to coat.    Combine bread crumbs, remaining 1 teaspoon oregano and melted butter in  small bowl; sprinkle over pasta mixture. Bake about 5 minutes or until hot  and bubbly. Garnish, if desired.
