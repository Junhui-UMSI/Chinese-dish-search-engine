Vegetable Biriyani
Cashew nuts, Salt, Raisins, Turmeric powder, Cinnamon powder, Chilli powder, Nutmeg powder, onions, Basmati rice, Vegetables, Coriander powder, Almonds, Clove powder, Butter, 
Soak the basmati rice in water for 30 min and drain well. Melt 1 1/2 T  butter in a skillet and saute the onions and raisins and cashew nuts till  they turn golden brown. Keep aside. Melt rest of the butter in the skillet  and add basmati rice and fry till they dont stick together. Add all the  spices and mix well. Preheat the oven to 375F. Empty the basmati rice into  a baking pan. Add salt and 1 1/2 cup of water. Cook for 20 min or until the  rice is done. Add the vegetables, onions, raisins and cashewnuts to rice  and mix well.
