Baked Eel Salonika
Garlic, Parsley, Salt, Black Pepper, olive oil, Eel, Onions, Oregano, Red wine, Tomatoes, Cinnamon, 
Rinse eel with cold water.  Oil baking pan with half of the oil.  Place  slices of eel on pan. Sauce: Heat remaining oil in frying pan, and fry  onions and garlic on medium heat for 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients  and cover.  Cook 10 minutes. Pour sauce over eel. Bake 45 minutes at 375  degrees.  Serve with crusty bread and a crisp salad.
