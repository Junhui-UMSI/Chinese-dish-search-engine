Buttercup Squash Casserole
Buttercup squash, Brown sugar, Salt, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Margarine or butter, Chopped fresh chives, Nutmeg, if desired, 
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Cut squash into quarters; remove seeds. Place,  cut side down, in ungreased shallow baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 45  to 50 minutes or until tender. Cool slightly.    2. Grease 1-quart casserole. Scoop squash pulp from skin; place in medium  bowl. Discard skin. Add brown sugar, salt, cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg,  and margarine to squash. Mix with potato masher or fork until well blended.  Spoon into greased casserole. Sprinkle with chives and 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg.    3. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes or until thoroughly heated.    Nutrition Information Per Serving: 160 Calories, 7 g Fat, 210 mg Sodium    taken from Pillsbury Classic Cookbooks magazine.
