Apple Dreams
Cinnamon and sugar, Apples; sliced, Brown sugar, Brown sugar, TOPPING MIXTURE, Nutmeats, Eggs, Coconut, Salt, Flour, Butter, Flour, 
Cream first 3 ingredients and spread on the bottom of an 8x10" pan. Arrange  the apple slices evenly on dough and sprinkle with 1/4 cup sugar mixed with  1/2 teaspoon cinnamon. Bake in 375 degree oven for 30 minutes. While  baking, mix the ingredients for the topping as follows;    Sift sugar and flour. Add unbeaten eggs to the mixture. Add nuts and  coconut and spread on top of the first hot baked mixture (after it has  finished baking). Bake 20 minutes more at 375 degress. Cut into bars or  squares when cool.
