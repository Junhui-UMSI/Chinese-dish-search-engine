Pecan Balls #2
Sugar, Flour, Butter, Vanilla, pecans, 
Cream butter until soft; add sugar until creamy. Add vanilla. Stir flour  and pecans into mixture. Roll dough into small balls and put on greased  cookie sheet. Bake at 300 degrees for 25-30 minutes. Roll in powdered sugar  while hot and again when cold.
