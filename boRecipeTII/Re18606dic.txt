American Lamb Cassoulet
Tomato sauce, Lamb shoulder; cut into 3/4, Italian herb seasoning;, Zucchini; halved lengthwise, Canned diced tomatoes with, Canned Great Northern beans;, Garlic, Beef bouillon granules, Scallions; sliced, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 15 min.    Combine all ingredients, except zucchini and beans, in a microwave  casserole. Cover and microwave on high 3 minutes. Remove cover and stir.  Continue to microwave another 4 minutes. Stir in zucchini and beans and  microwave another 6-7 minutes, until meat is cooked throughout.
