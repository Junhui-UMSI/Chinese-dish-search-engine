Alfredos Noodles (Pkfr67a)
Mushrooms, Grated mozzarella, FRESH grated Romano, Light cream, Fettuccine, Salt, Garlic powder, Oregano, Butter, Pepper, 
Melt butter in a small saucepan.  Add mushrooms, salt, pepper, oregano and  garlic.  Saute for five minutes.   Add cream and cook for two minutes, stirring occasionally. Drain  fettuccine and place in a warm bowl. Add hot sauce and both cheeses and  toss gently.   Serves 4.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #157 by BobbieB1@aol.com on Jun 5, 1997
