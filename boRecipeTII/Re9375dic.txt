Pullum Frontonianum (Chicken a la Fronto
Salt, Fresh chicken (approx., Liquamen -or-, Leek, A little bit of defritum, Vegetable oil, Wine -plus-, Fresh dill; saturei,, 
From: hz225wu@unidui.uni-duisburg.de (Micaela Pantke) (COLLECTION)    Date: Thu, 22 Jul 93 11:12:07 +0200    See "Ancient Roman Ingredients" for info on ingredients. Start to fry  chicken and season with a mixture of Liquamen and oil, together with  bunches of dill, leek, Saturei and fresh coriander. Then cook approximately  1 hour with 220 deg C in the oven. When the chicken is done, moisten a  plate with Defritum, put chicken on it, sprinkle pepper on it, and serve.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.
