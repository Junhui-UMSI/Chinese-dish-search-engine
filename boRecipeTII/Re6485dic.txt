Poached Chicken Breasts
Butter, Chicken breasts, Lemon juice, Salt, 
Use as many chicken breasts as you need for dinner. Remove skin & bone,  cut into 2 parts and place in baking dish. Sprinkle with lemon juice and  dot with butter. Salt to taste. Cover closely and bake about 15 minutes.  Do not overcook; pierce with fork to be sure thickest part is done. Use  pan juices as a sauce.    Mrs. James Olfson    From a book of recipes compiled by members and friends of the LONDONTOWN  PUBLIK HOUSE ASSEMBLY, EDGEWATER, MD 21037.
