Apple Cake
Walnuts, Cinnamon, Raisins, White flour, Sugar, Whole-wheat flour, Egg whites, whipped, Margarine, Salt, Baking soda, in 2/3 c water, Ground cloves, Nutmeg, Finely chopped apple, 
Cream the margarine and sugar.  Add dry ingredients and water alternately,  then apple.  Dust the raisins and nuts with a small amount of flour and add  them to the batter.  Fold in the egg whites.  Bake in a 8 in. square  nonstick pan at 350F for 30 to 40 min. or until firm.  Cal: 295; Fat 2  1/5g.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmealnew.zip
