White Wine Tarragon Jelly
Fresh tarragon, Sugar, Dried tarragon; or 19 oz can, Tarragon Vinegar, Pouch liquid certo, Dry white wine, 
Combine wine, sugar, and vinegar in a large saucepan. Add tarragon leaves  to wine mixture. Place over high heat. Bring to a full rolling boil and  boil hard 1 min., stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in liquid  pectin. Pour through a fine seive to remove tattagon leaves. Pour into  sterilized bottles and seal. Makes 4 cups.
