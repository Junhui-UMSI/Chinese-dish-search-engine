100% Parmesan Chicken Breasts
Salt, Black pepper, Chicken breasts without skin, Dry bread crumbs, Oregano, Paprika, Parsley, Parmesan cheese, 
Recipe by: Kraft recipe Preparation Time: 0:30 Heat oven to 400F. Spray  15x10x1" baking pan with no-stick cooking spray.   Dip chicken in Shedd spread; coat with combined remaining ingredients.  Place in prepared pan.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until tender.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
