Yoghurt Mint Drink
Full Cream Yoghurt, Egg yolk, Scoop Lemon Sorbet, Mint Sprig, Lemon (The Juice), Honey, 
Place yoghurt, lemon juice, washed and chopped mint, honey, egg yolk and  sorbet in an electric blender and whip together until frothy. Serve the  drink in a longdrink glass garnished with a lemon slice. Approx 280 cal.
