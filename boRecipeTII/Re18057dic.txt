Veggie Burgers #5
Mushrooms, Garlic, Tofu, Sage, Eggs, Onion, Whole wheat bread crumbs, Garbonzo beans, Zuchinni, Carrots, 
Date: Sun, 05 May 1996 11:46:40 -0700    From: Mweb@mindlink.bc.ca (Michelle Webber)    In a food processer, chop garbonzo beans and tofu together. Place in bowl.  Chop or grate the rest of the vegetables, add to mixture and mix well.  Add  bread crumbs, egg (or egg whites) and spices, mix well.  Form into patties,  fry in pan until brown on both sides.  these freeze well, and can be  reheated when needed.    FATFREE DIGEST V96 #125    From the Fatfree Vegetarian recipe list.
