Baked Swiss Steak
Round Steak, Onion, Catsup, Celery, Carrots, Green Bell Pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, salt and pepper, Tomatoes, Horseradish, Flour, Tomato Sauce, 
Roll meat in flour and brown in fat in skillet. Place in a large, deep  baking dish. Cut onion, pepper, celery, and carrots in large pieces and add  to the meat. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over the meat. Bake,  covered, for 2 to 2 1/2 hours, at 325?, until tender.    Serves 6 to 8    Source: "Mountain Measures" -- Junior League of Charleston, WV ed. 1974    billspa@icanect.net    Recipe by: Mrs. Ronald A.
