Almond-Blue Cheese Balls
Chives, Cream cheese, Onion, Fresh parsley, Blue Cheese, Chutney, salt and pepper, Almonds, Fresh Cream, 
Blend and form in little individual balls adding drops of fresh cream to  adjust consistency of mixture. Roll in chopped almond and chill. Source:  Mountain Measures--Junior League of Charleston, WV ed. 1974    Recipe by: Mrs. C. K. Payne, III    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by kATHERINE L Smith <ksmith3002@JUNO.COM> on Nov  26, 1997
