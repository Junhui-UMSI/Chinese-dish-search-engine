Apple Cinnamon Pudding
Vanilla, Cinnamon, Water, Apple juice, Roasted pecan or other nuts, AM Rice and Shine, Sea salt (optional), (chopped), 
Bring juice, water and salt to a boil in a large saucepan.  Stir in Rice  and Shine and cinnamon.  Turn heat to low.  Simmer 5 minutes.  Remove from  heat.  Stir in nuts and vanilla.  Let set until slightly cool.  Pour into  a 9" square pan.  Refrigerate until set and cool.  Serve squares plain or  with a little fruit syrup.
