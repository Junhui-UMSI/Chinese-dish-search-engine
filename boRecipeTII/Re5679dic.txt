Pineapple, Mango and Cucumber Salsa
Salt to taste, Hawaiian chili pepper, Tomato, Green onion, Mint, Cilantro, mango, Cucumber, Red bell pepper, Pineapple, Juice of 2 limes, 
Cut all ingredients in small diced; mix well, adjust seasoning. Chill to  marinate and marry flavors.  Good with white fish.    Shared and MM by Judi M. Phelps. jphelps@shell.portal.com,  juphelps@delphi.com, or jphelps@best.com
