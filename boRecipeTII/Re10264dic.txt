Quick Apple Bread
Shortening, Pecans, Eggs, milk, Baking Powder, Vanilla, apples, Cinnamon, Salt, Flour, Sugar, 
Cream shortening, sugar, salt, vanilla until light and fluffy. Add eggs  and beat well.  Add milk, apples and nuts, beat. Stir in flour, baking  powder, cinnamon. Blend. Pour into greased loaf pan. Bake at 350 F. for  about 50 min. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers BillSpalding *P CRBR 38 A
