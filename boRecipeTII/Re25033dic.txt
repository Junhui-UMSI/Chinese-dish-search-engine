Tomato Sauce for Spaghetti and Meatballs
Salt, Italian plum tomatoes,, Hot red pepper, Sugar, Peeled whole clove garlic, olive oil, Onions, 
In a saucepan heat oil. Add onion and saute until transparent. Then add  garlic, tomatoes with their juices, salt, hot pepper, and pinch of sugar.  Cook, uncovered, for 20 minutes. Season to taste, and puree in a food mill.  Return to the saucepan, add meatballs to reheat them slightly and serve  atop your spaghetti.
