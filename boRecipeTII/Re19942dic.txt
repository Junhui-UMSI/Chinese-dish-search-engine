Apple Brownies
Brown sugar, Nuts, Eggs, Butter, Baking Powder, Flour, Apples, Powdered sugar, Salt, Vanilla, 
Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix well. Add flour, baking  powder, and salt. Stir well. Stir in apples and nuts. Bake in well greased  9 X 13 inch pan. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes at 350. When cooled, sprinkle  powdered sugar over the top with flour sifter, then serve.
