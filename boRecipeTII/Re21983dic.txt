Applesauce Cupcakes
Egg, Margarine, Salt, Nutmeg, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Vanilla, Sugar, Flour, Walnuts, Unsweetened applesauce, 
Preheat oven to 375 F.  Cream margarine until fluffy.  Beat eggs and  sugar; add to margarine and blend.    Sift together dry ingredients.  Add to margarine mixture alternately  with applesauce, mixing well after each addition.  Stir in vanilla  and nuts.    Spoon into 12 cupcake pans sprayed with vegetable pan spray or use  paper baking cups.  Bake 15-20 min.    1 cupcake - 177 calories, 1 1/2 Starch/bread exchange, 1 1/2 fat 2.7  gm protein, 9.8 gm fat, 19.8 gm carbohydrate, 207.6 mg sodium, 53.3 mg  potassium, 1 gm fiber, 23 mg cholesterol    Source:  Am.
