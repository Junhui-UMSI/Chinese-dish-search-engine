Quick Chocolate Glaze
Unsalted butter, Vanilla, Sifted dark unsweetened, Sugar, Heavy cream, 
Combine cocoa, heavy cream, butter and sugar in a saucepan. Over low heat  cook, stirring constantly, until smooth and thick, about 5 minutes. Remove  from heat, add vanilla. Drizzle glaze over slices of almond cake topped  with ice cream. Store remaining glaze in covered jar, in the refrigerator  for several weeks; reheat to boiling before reusing.
