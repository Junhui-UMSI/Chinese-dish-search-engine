Truffle Chowder
Onions, TABASCO pepper sauce, Half-and-half, Salt, Diced carrot, Chicken Stock, Finely-chopped fresh, Celery, Sliced assorted Exotic, Extra chopped parsley; for, White truffle flour, Drizzle of truffle oil, Cayenne pepper, Extra-black truffle juice, Shaved black truffles, Bacon - (abt 10 slices);, Worcestershire Sauce, Bay Leaves, Diced white potatoes, Parmesan cheese tuiles, 
In a large nonstick stock pot, over medium heat, render the bacon for 10  minutes. Add the onions, celery, and carrots. Season the vegetables with  salt, cayenne, and bay leaves. Saute for 10 minutes, or until the  vegetables are soft and tender. Stir in the flour and cook for 10 minutes,  stirring occasionally. Stir in the mushrooms and saute for 2 to 3 minutes.  Stir in the chicken stock and truffle juice and bring up to a boil. Add the  potatoes and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the potatoes are fork tender.  Stir in the cream and parsley. Simmer the soup for 5 minutes. Add the hot  sauce and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer the soup for 2 minutes.    Ladle the soup in a shallow bowl and garnish with the truffle oil, shaved  truffles, tuiles and parsley.
