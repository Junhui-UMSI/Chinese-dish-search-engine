Tomato and Dill Soup
plum tomatoes, fresh, Cayenne, Cumin, Celery, Fresh Cilantro, Vegetable stock, garlic, Onions, olive oil, green chilies, Fresh dill, sweet red peppers, 
Combine all ingredients except dill, cilantro, cumin, cayenne & salt. Bring  to a boil & simmer for 25 minutes. Let cool slightly, then puree. Stir in  remaining ingredients with some salt & pepper.  Serve warmed or chilled.    Nava Atlas, "Vegetariana"
