Strawberries with Balsamic Vinegar
Sugar, Strawberries, Balsamic vinegar, 
Combine all ingredients in a bowl; toss well. Let stand 30 minutes. Toss  well before serving. Yield: 4 servings (serving size: 1 cup).    Per serving: 55 Calories; 1g Fat (8% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 13g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 1mg Sodium    NOTES : A splash of vinegar over fresh strawberries could be served as a  light dessert.
