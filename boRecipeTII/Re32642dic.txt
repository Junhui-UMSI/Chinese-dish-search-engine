Sweet Pecans
Water, Egg white, Sugar, Cinnamon, Salt, 
Beat egg white and water until frothy. Mix dry ingredi- ents together.  Using 1 pound pecan halves, dip halves in egg white mixture, then roll in  sugar mixture. Place pecans in shallow pan; bake at 300 degrees for 30 to  40 minutes. Stir gently every 10 to 15 minutes to keep pecans from  sticking.
