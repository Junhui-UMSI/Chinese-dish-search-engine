Apple Milk Drink (Sharbat)
milk, Sugar, Rosewater, Red eating apples, 
Peel and cube the apples.  Place in the blender jar with sugar, perfumed  water and milk.  Whirl at high speed 15 seconds. Serve, with shaved ice if  desired, in small glasses.    Yield: 2 to 4 servings.    From _Couscous and Other Good Food from Morocco_ by Paula Wolfert. New  York: Harper & Row, Publishers, Inc., 1987. Pg. 326. ISBN 0-06-091396-7.  Electronic format by Cathy Harned.
