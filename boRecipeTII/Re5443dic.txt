Pineapple Congealed Salad
crushed pineapple, sour cream, lemon gelatin, 
Dissolve gelatin in 1-1/2 cups boiling water. Use liquid from pineapple  plus water. Put in refrigerator until slightly congealed. Using an  egg-beater or a fork add the drained pineapple and sour cream. Pour into  glass rings or square pan. Chill.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
