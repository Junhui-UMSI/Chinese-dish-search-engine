Vinegar Cookies
Egg, Baking Powder, STIR THESE WELL THEN ADD, Vinegar, Salt, Flour, MIX, Butter, Sugar, 
Mix well then bake at 350 degrees for about 10-15 minutes in a gas range (I  have noticed other ranges are different) . Add some nuts or raisins if you  like.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #026 by The Vinegar Man <vinegar@itctel.com>  on Jan 25, 1998
