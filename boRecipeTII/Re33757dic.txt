Sweet and Sour Chicken Wings
Black Pepper, Salt, Ketchup, Water, Sugar, Whole chicken wings, Garlic powder, Vinegar, 
Preheat oven to 350 F. Season wings with salt, pepper and garlic powder.  PLace in a shallow baking dish and bake for 15 minutes. in a small  saucepan, combine ketchup, vinegar, sugar and water. Heat, stirring until  well blended. Brush sauce over wings and continue baking for 20 to 30  minutes, or until wings are tender. Remove from oven, and serve.
