Frozen Blushing Salad
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, Granulated sugar, Crushed pineapple with juice, 10 oz. pkg. frozen straw, Whipping cream ( or 1 env., 
Have cream cheese at room temperature.  Add sugar and mix well to dissolve.  Add pineapple and strawberries. Stir and mash with fork. It will be a bit  lumpy.    Whip cream until stiff.  Fold into fruit.  Pour into ring mold or soup  cans.  Freeze. Cover to store. Unmold ring on plate. If in cans, remove  bottom end and push salad out on plate. Slice.
