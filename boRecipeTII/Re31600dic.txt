Swiss Broccoli Soup
HEINZ HomeStyle Chicken, Pepper, Salt, Frozen chopped broccoli, Butter or margarine, milk, Shredded Swiss cheese, Onion, 
In 2-quart saucepan, saute onion in butter until tender. Stir in gravy,  milk and broccoli; heat slowly, stirring occasionally. Add cheese, salt and  pepper; heat until cheese is melted, stirring frequently.    Makes 4 servings (about 4 cups).
