Quick Chicken Divan
Cream of chicken soup, Cheddar cheese, shredded, Bread crumbs, or Stuffing, Lemon juice, Whole chicken breasts, Mayonnaise, Mix., Frozen Broccoli spears, Curry powder, 
Simmer the chicken breasts in salted water until tender, about 45 minutes.    Let cool in broth.  Remove meat from bones.  In shallow casserole, place  frozen broccoli, separated, with chicken on top. Mix together the soup,  mayonnaise, lemon juice, curry, and cheese.  Pour the mixture over the  chicken and broccoli.  Top with bread crumbs and bake at 350 F. uncovered  for 45 min.
