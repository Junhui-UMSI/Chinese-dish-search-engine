Potatoes Dauphine Recipe
Salt, Eggs, Salt, pepper, nutmeg, CHOUX PASTE, Water, POTATOES, Butter, Oil, for frying, Potatoes, mashed, Flour, 
Make choux paste: Bring water, salt and butter to boil in a saucepan. Add  flour, stirring vigorously until dough leaves the sides of the pan. Add the  eggs, one at a time, stirring to incorporate each egg.(May be done in food  processor) Blend potatoes, seasonings and paste. Deep fry in small batches  until puffed and golden.    NOTES : These potatoes are delicious with any kind of meat.    Recipe by: Miriam Podcameni Posvolsky Posted to Kitmailbox by Leon & Miriam  Posvolsky <posvolsk@centroin.com.br> on Jul 02, 1997
