Baked Sole Au Gratin
Dover sole, Oregano, Bread crumbs, Salt and pepper to taste, olive oil, 
Preheat oven to 425. Season both sides of fish with salt & pepper.  Place in lightly oiled dish. In bowl combine oregano with olive oil &  bread crumbs. Spread crumb mixture evenly over surface of fish. Bake  6-12 minutes per inch of thickness, until fish is opaque throughout.  Remove from oven & place under broiler for 1 minute to brown the  crumb mixture.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
