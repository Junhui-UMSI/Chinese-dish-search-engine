Ajam Goreng Djawa (Fried Chicken with Spices)
Tamarind juice, Chicken, frying, Chilies, Coconut milk, Turmeric, Salt, Brown sugar, Coriander seed, ground, Vegetable oil, Onion, Caraway seed, ground, 
Put chicken in a pan with all the ingredients except the oil. Bring to boil  then reduce heat and simmer for 20 min. or until liquid has been absorbed.  Heat oil and deep fry chicken until golden brown.
