Turkey Potato Pancakes
Cooked turkey; finely choppe, Shredded peeled potatoes, Cranberry sauce; optional, Green onions with tops; slic, Salt, All-purpose flour, Cooking oil, Eggs, 
Recipe by: TASTE OF HOME DEC/JAN 1996 In a bowl, beat the eggs. Add  potatoes, turkey, onions, flour and salt; mix well. Heat about 1/4" of oil  in a large skillet. Pour batter by 1/3 cupfuls into hot oil. Fry 5-6  minutes on each side or until potatoes are tender and pancakes are golden  brown. Serve with cranberry sauce if desired.
