Apple Cake with Cinnamon
Cinnamon, All-purpose flour, Vanilla, Salt, Sugar, Baking soda, Vegetable oil, Pecans, chopped, Diced apples, Eggs, 
toss apples and sugar.  Add oil. mix and add nuts.  Add eggs and vanilla.  In a seperate bowl, sift together flour, soda, cinnamon and salt and pour  into other ingredients.  Pour into a 9 x 13 baking pan. Bake 350 degrees  for 1 hour.  Cool and frost.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #309    Date: Mon, 11 Nov 1996 11:52:19 -0500    From: Dotnapier@aol.com
