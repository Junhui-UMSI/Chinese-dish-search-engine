Pineapple Pork Roast
Onion, sliced, 3-pound rolled boneless pork, Cornstarch, Celery sliced, Salt and pepper, Apricot preserves, Sliced carrots, Soy sauce, Bay leaf, crumbled, Pineapple juice, 
Arrange vegetables in a greased roasting pan. Season roast with salt and  pepper; place roast over vegetables, fat side up, and sprinkle bay leaf on  top. Insert meat thermometer horizontally into one end of roast. Bake at  325 for 30-45 minutes or until browned; turn roast over and bake 30 minutes  to brown bottom side. Turn roast over again, and drain off drippings.  Combine pineapple juice and soy sauce; pour over roast and bake 15 minutes  or until thermometer registers 160 degrees. Remove roast from oven; strain  and reserve drippings from vegetables, and sprinkle vegs over roast.  Combine preserves and cornstarch; add to drippings. Cook over medium heat  until thickened, stirring constantly with a whisk or wooden spoon. Spoon  some of the glaze over roast; let stand 10 minutes. Serve with remaining  glaze.
