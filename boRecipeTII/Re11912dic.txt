Really Good Granola
Rolled rye, Honey, Apple juice, Rolled oats, Raw sunflower seeds, Cinnamon, 
Mix together first four ingredients. Stir together juice and honey until  honey is dissolved and add to dry ingredients, mixing well. Turn out into  two cake pans and bake in a 200 oven, stirring once or twice every hour  until dry and crunchy. (Takes several hours.)    Enjoy :)
