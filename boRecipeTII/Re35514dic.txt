Strawberry-Pineapple Parfait
Unflavored gelatin (1 pkg), Unsweet. crush. pineapple, Cold water, Vanilla ice milk, Unsweet. frozen strawberries, 
Sprinkle gelatin over cold water.  Place over low heat, stirring  constantly until gelatin dissolves.  Drain pineapple and thaw  strawberries.    Add ice milk and fruits.  Stir gently until mixed.  Portion into 4  individual dishes.  Refrigerate until serving time.  Do not freeze.    Source:  Am. Diabetes Assoc. Family Cookbook Vol 1, 1987 Shared but  not tested by Elizabeth Rodier Nov 93.
