Wild Mushroom Stuffing (r T)
Red Bell Pepper, Fresh Wild mushrooms, Sage, Bread, Celery, Pepper, Salt, Chicken Broth, Onions, Cooking Spray, 
*NOTE: Original recipe used low calorie stick margarine **NOTE: Shiitake,  crimini, oyster, chopped    Preheat oven to 325F.    Melt margarine in a large nonstick skillet over med - high heat. Add  mushrooms and next 3 ingredients; saute 7 min. Remove from heat; stir in  sage, salt and pepper.    Combine mushroom mixture and bread cubes; gradually add broth, tossing  gently to coat. Spoon mixture into a 13 x 9" baking dish coated with  cooking spray. Cover and bake at 325F for 30 min. Uncover and bake an  additional 20 min.    Yield: 12 servings Serving Size: 1 C    Selections: 1 FA/V, 1 FA, 1B; Points: 3    This was great!! Very flavorful and moist.
