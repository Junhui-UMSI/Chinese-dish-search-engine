Sweet Dreams Ginger Cookies
Salt, Cinnamon, Vanilla morsels, Baking soda, Pecans, Sticks margarine; softened, Eggs, Vanilla extract, Light brown sugar; firmly, Ground ginger, All-purpose flour, 
Cream together margarine, brown sugar and eggs. Mix together then add  flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and ginger. Fold in pecans, vanilla  chips, and vanilla. Form into one inch balls. Roll balls in confectioners  sugar (Dough will be gooey). Grease cookie sheet with crisco. Bake 8-10  minutes at 375 degrees.    NOTES : These cookies are another delicious recipe.
