Wild Rice Yellow Pepper and Black-Eyed Pea Sala
Cherry tomatoes, quartered, Cooked or canned (drained, Fresh Lemon Juice, Chopped fresh parsley, Pepper, Red wine vinegar, Bell pepper, Salt, Chopped zucchini, Garlic, Cooked wild rice, Chopped jicama, Dried rosemary, crumbled, And rinsed) black-eyed peas, Dijon mustard, Chopped yellow (or green), 
In a large bowl, combine the wild rice, black-eyed peas, bell pepper,  jicama, zucchini, tomatoes, and parsley.    In a small bowl, stir together the lemon juice, vinegar, mustard, garlic,  salt, rosemary, and pepper. Pour over the salad and toss.
