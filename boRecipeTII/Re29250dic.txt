Baked Squash Casserole
Salt, Onion, Butter, Pepper, Squash, Sour cream, 
Prepare and boil cubed squash. Mash squash; stir in remaining ingredients.  Turn mixture into ungreased 1 qt. casserole. Bake uncovered in 400* oven  until hot, about 20-30 min.  Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Shawn Zehnder Lea <slea@HIGHTOWERSERVICES.COM>  on Nov 24, 1997
