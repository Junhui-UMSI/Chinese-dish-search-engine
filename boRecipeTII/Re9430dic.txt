Pumpkin and Poppyseed Muffins
milk, Cooked; mashed pumpkin, Raw sugar, Poppy seeds, Self-raising flour, WET MIX, Butter, Egg, DRY MIX, 
Reheat oven to 375F, prepare pans. Cream butter & 1/2 cup sugar, add egg &  beat until light & creamy. Add milk then fold in pumpkin, p/seeds then  flour. Spoon into pans & sprinkle each muffin with the remaining sugar.  Bake for 25 mins. Makes 12.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
