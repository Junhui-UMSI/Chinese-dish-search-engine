Yellow Squash Casserole
Egg, Bell pepper; chop fine, Red pepper to taste, -(up to), Salt (to taste), Cheese crackers; crushed, Onion; chopped fine, Bacon, Fresh; yellow squash, Butter or margarine, 
Scrub squash and cut in rings. Melt butter or margarine, add seasoning and  squash and salt. Cook until tender, about 10 minutes. Add half the crackers  and beaten egg. Place in buttered casserole. Top with bacon slices and rest  of cracker crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes, or until bacon  is done.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
