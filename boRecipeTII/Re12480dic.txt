Zwieback Pie #1
Sugar, Sugar, Box zwieback crackers;, Cinnamon, Egg yolks, Sugar, Butter, Vanilla, Flour, milk, Egg whites, 
Mix crackers, 1/2 cup sugar, butter and cinnamon. Press into pie pan and  bake at 375 for 10 minutes. Reserve 1/2 cup of the crumbs for topping. To  make filling: combine 3/4 cup sugar, flour, milk and yolks in saucepan.  Cook and stir over low heat until thick. Add vanilla. Pour into crust while  mixture is hot. Make meringue of egg whites and 6 tablespoons sugar. Cover  filling with meringue, sprinkle with reserved crumbs, and bake at 400 until  brown.    MRS W.A.
