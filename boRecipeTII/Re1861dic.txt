Chicken, Lime and Tortilla Soup
Chicken Stock, Fresh lime juice, Tomato juice, Jalapeno, Cilantro, Scallions, Salt, Chicken Boned And Skinned, Corn tortillas, Oil for frying, Avocado, 
I add a little minced garlic    Place the stock, lime juice and tomato juice in a saucepan and bring to a  simmer over medium heat. Add the jalapeno, 1/2 of the cilantro and  scallions. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer, covered for 45 minutes.  Strain and reserve. Dice the grilled chicken and reserve. Julienne the  tortillas and fry in hot oil until crispy. Drain and reserve. Peel and dice  the avocado.    To serve: place the chicken in serving bowls. Pour over the soup and  sprinkle with the tortilla strips and diced avocado.
