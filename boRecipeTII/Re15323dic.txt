White Bean Sandwiches
Optional: lettuce & tomato, Stalk celery, diced, Dijon mustard, Mayonnaise, salt and pepper, Sandwich rolls, Wine vinegar, Rinsed and drained, Can small white beans,, Green onions, thinly sliced, 
Combine beans, celery, onions, mustard, vinegar, amyonnaise, salt and  pepper in a bowl.  Divide white bean filling evenly among rolls.  Top  with lettuce and tomato, if desired.
