Pizza Spirals
Sargento Light fancy, Ragu Pizza Quick Sauce, Chopped Hormel pepperoni, (10oz) Pillsbury, Green Giant frozen chopped, 
Heat oven to 425F. Grease 2 cookie sheets. Unroll dough; press into 12" x  10" rectangle. Cut into 4 (6" x 5") rectangles. Spread each rectangle with  2 tbsp pizza sauce to within 1/2" of edges. Top each with pepperoni,  spinach and cheese. Starting at longest side, roll up jelly-roll fashion;  seal edges well. Cut each roll into 10 (1/2") slices. Place on greased  cookie sheet. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Immediately  remove from cookie sheets. Serve warm. 40 appetizers.    Posted to brand-name-recipes by sadunning@juno.com (Stacy A Dunning) on Feb  25, 1998
