Apricot Betty
Bread crumbs, Brown sugar, Nonfat dry milk, Drained, Mace, Cinnamon, Ginger, Margarine, Canned apricots halves,, 
Blend first 7 ingredients. Arrange apricots in baking dish; sprinkle  with crumb mixture. Bake for 30 minutes at 375. Serve warm with milk  or cream.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
