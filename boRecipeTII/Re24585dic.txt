Asparagus with Egg, Garlic and Lemon Sauce
Hard boiled egg,fine chopped, Salt, Fresh tarragon,fine chopped, Fresh garlic,minced, Asparagus,prepared*, Black pepper,freshly ground, Butter,unsalted, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
* - stems peeled and fiberous ends removed.    1. Heat butter in saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic and saute  gently 1 minute; remove pan from heat. Reserve and keep warm.    2. Place asparagus in saucepan over medium heat. Add enough water to  cover, simmer 3-5 minutes or until just tender. Remove from saucepan,  pat dry and transfer to serving platter. Keep warm.    3. TO SERVE: Stir egg and lemon into reserved garlic butter. Pour over  asparagus, season with salt and pepper and garnish with tarragon.
