Amaranth Date Nut Bread
Eggs, Chopped dates, Honey, Baking Powder, Boiling water, Whole wheat flour, Amaranth, Butter, Pecans, Vanilla extract, 
Soak dates and amaranth in boiling water.  Beat eggs well, add honey and  butter; beat well.  Stir in all remaining ingredients mixing well. Pour  into an oiled loaf pan. Bake at 350 F for 1 hour and 15 min.    From Arrowhead Mills    Posted by Theresa Merkling.
