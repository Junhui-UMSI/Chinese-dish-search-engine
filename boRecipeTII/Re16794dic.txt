All American Muffins
Ground allspice, Granulated sugar, Salt, Egg, Unbleached all-purpose flour, (1/2 stick) melted butter or, Nonfat skim milk, Baking Powder, Fresh or frozen, 
>From Muffin Magic..and More    Line muffin wells with paper cases.Wash blueberries in a colander and blot  dry with paper towel. In a small bowl toss berries with 1/2 cup of the  flour, then set aside.    In a 2 1/2 quart bowl sift remaining flour with baking powder, salt,  allspice and sugar. Stir to blend and make a well in center.    In a 1 1/2 quart bowl whisk egg, milk and butter and add all at once to the  flour mixture. Stir lightly until flour is moistened, then fold in floured  berries. Spoon batter into prepared muffin wells, until two-thirds full.    Bake in a preheated oven at 400 degrees F. for about 20 minutes until  golden brown and a test toothpick in center comes out clean. Do not  over-bake.    Makes about 12 2 1/2" muffins.    Each muffin contains about: 143 calories, 3g protein, 22g carbohydrate, 1g  fiber, 4.5g fat, 28mg cholesterol, 227mg sodium Posted to Bakery Shoppe  Digest145 by vergie1@juno.com (Vergie A Ewing) on Jul 5, 1997
