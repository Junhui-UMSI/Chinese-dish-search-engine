Tangy Buttermilk Cheese Bread
Cheese, cheddar, shredded, Yeast, Buttermilk, powdered, Salt, Sugar, Flour, bread, Water, 
Bring bread ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in order.  Set "baking control" at 10 oclock.  Select "white bread" and push Start.    This is a tangy, tender bread.  Use extra-sharp Cheddar for the best  flavor.    Tested in DAK R2D2.
