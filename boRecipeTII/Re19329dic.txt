Vegetable Stuffed Peppers
Garlic, Sweet green peppers, -(up to), Eggplant; peeled, cubed, Vegetable oil, Salt to taste, Tomatoes; chopped, Dry bread crumbs, Grated Parmesan cheese, Parsley, 
Cut off top of each pepper; remove seeds. Cook about 5 minutes in enough  boiling water to cover; drain. Saute garlic, tomatoes and eggplant in oil  until tender. Combine eggplant mixture, breadcrumbs, salt, cheese and  parsley. Mix well. Stuff peppers with mixture. Place in a baking dish and  bake at 350 for 30 minutes.    MRS E.J.
