Potatoes Mary Margaret
Large red potatoes, Paprika, Butter, Garlic, Salt and pepper, to taste, Whipping cream, Parmesan cheese, 
Peel and slice potatoes very thin. Layer in a small buttered casserole;  potatoes, cheese, salt and pepper. Simmer cream, butter and garlic until  reduced by half. Pour over potatoes, sprinkle with paprika. Bake in a 350  degree oven for 1 1/2 hours. Enjoy, Sheryl BTW M.M. said you may need to  add more cream.
