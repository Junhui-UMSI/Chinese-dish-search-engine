Pot Luck Butter Beans
Honey, Bacon, Butter beans  , 
Fry or microwave bacon to crisp. Drain and dry bacon. Warm butter beans on  stove or 4 minutes on Medium setting in microwave. Add honey to beans.  Cover and allow heat to melt honey over beans. Sprinkle bacon pieces on  top.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 27,  1998
