Peppermint Thumbprints
Vanilla, Peppermint candy, crushed, Cream cheese, softened, Flour, COOKIE DOUGH, FILLING, milk, Softend, Powdered sugar, Powdered sugar, Margarine or butter,, 
Heat oven to 350. In small bowl, mix all filling ingredients until  well blended; set aside. In large bowl, cream powdered sugar,  margarine and vanilla. Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup; level  off. Add flour and mix well. If dough is too crumbly, add 1-4 tsp  milk. Shape into 1" balls; place 2" apart on ungreased cookie sheets.  With thumb, make indentation in center of each cookie; fill with 1/4  tsp filling. Bake 10-15 minutes or until light golden brown.  Immediately remove cookies from cookie sheets; cool.
