Asian Savoy Salad
Tabasco sauce, Carrot strips; julienned, Rice wine vinegar, Savoy cabbage, Oyster sauce, Soy sauce, Cloves Garlic; peeled,, Sugar, 
Recipe by: Jacques Pepin, Todays Gourmet II    Combine the dressing ingredients in a plastic bag large enough to hold the  cabbage. Shred the cabbage finely.  Add cabbage, toss it with the dressing  and allow the mixture to macerate for at least 2 hours. Transfer the salad  and dressing to a serving bowl, sprinkle with the carrot and serve.
