Peanut Butter Sesame Balls
Oatmeal, Skim milk powder, Vanilla extract, Boiling water, Peanut butter, Toasted sesame seets or, Chopped nuts or toasted, Honey, 
1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine peanut butter, honey, and vanilla  extract; blend thoroughly.    2. Mix skim milk powder and oatmeal together. Gradually add to the peanut  butter-honey mixture, blending thoroughly, using hands if necessary to mix  as dough begins to stiffen. Blend in the seeds or chopped nuts.    3. Add two tablespoons boiling water to mixture, blending well.    4. Shape in one-inch balls. Roll in finely-chopped nuts or toasted sesame  seeds. For variety, roll half the mixture in chopped nuts and the other  half in sesame seeds.    note: Toast sesame seeds in a preheated 200 degree oven for about 20  minutes or until lightly-browned. Yield: approximately 3 dozen balls.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 02 Sep 96    From:    Elizabeth Dye <lizdye@SOUTHWIND.NET>    Date:    Mon, 2 Sep 1996 22:53:36 -0500
