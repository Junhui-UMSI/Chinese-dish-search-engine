Watercress and Jicama Salad
olive oil, Jicama,, peeled, Watercress,, trimmed and, Salt, Freshly squeezed lime juice, Black Pepper, 
Thinly slice the jicama and then cut into 1/8-inch wide strips. Combine the  jicama and watercress in a bowl. In a separate bowl whisk together the  olive oil, lime juice, salt and pepper. Pour over the salad and toss well  to coat. Serve immediately on chilled plates.
