Watermelon with Raspberry Sauce
Raspberries in light syrup,, Thawed, Bite size chunks watermelon, Pack (10 oz) frozen red, 
Process raspberries with syrup in food processor or blender until  almost pureed but still chunky. Pour over melon in bowl. Toss gently  to coat. Refrigerate 30 minutes or until well chilled.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
