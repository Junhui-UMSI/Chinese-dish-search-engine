Sybil Carters Potato-Peanut Butter Candy
Irish potatoes, mashed*, Peanut butter crunchy, 
* - boiled and drained      Mix sifted sugar into mashed potatoes, a little at a time, by hand; do not  use electric mixer. Sprinkle waxed paper with additional confectioners  sugar; take ball of sugar-potato mixture, about the size of a baseball,  roll out as for pastry, and spread with crunchy peanut butter. Roll over  and over like jelly roll; makes enough for two rolls, each about a foot  long. Wrap in Saran or foil and chill well. Slice into 1/2" thick rounds.
