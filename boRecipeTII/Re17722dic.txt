Almond Shortbread Bars
Chopped almonds, Water, Sugar, Egg; separated, Almond extract, All-purpose flour, Butter, 
Preheat oven to 350F.  Grease 15 X 10 X 1 inch jelly-roll pan.  In large  mixer bowl, combine flour, sugar, butter, egg yolk and almond extract. Beat  at low speed of electric mixer, scraping bowl often, until particles are  fine, 2 to 3 minutes. Press on bottom of prepared pan. In small bowl, beat  egg white and water until frothy; brush on dough. Sprinkle nuts over top.  Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until very lightly browned. Cool completely in  pan on wire rack.
