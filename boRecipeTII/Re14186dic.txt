White Meat Ravioli
Parmeson or Romano cheese;, Plum tomatoes; chopped, Mace, Garlic, Shallots; finely chopped, Basil, Pasta dough, olive oil, Chicken breast OR veal OR, Ground, Parsley, Tomatoes; crushed, Black pepper; coursely, Dried leaf sage; crumbled, SAUCE, Tomato sauce, Canned tuna; chopped, Grated, Brandy or dry sherry, Scallions; thinly sliced, 
Place all ingredients, except pasta, in a large mixing bowl and combine  thouroughly.  Fill ravioli with mixture.    Heat oil over medium heat.  Stir-fry scallions and garlic for about 30  seconds.  Add tomatoes, tomato sauce and basil. Stir. Simmer for 10-15  minutes.
