Uncommon Carrot-Raisin Slaw
Raisins, Salt & pepper, to taste, Shredded carrots, Cumin seeds or ground cumin, Chopped fresh parsley, Red bell pepper, thin sliced, White vinegar, Sweetener, 
In a large bowl, combine the carrots, raisins and red pepper; toss. Add the  parsley and cumin; toss again. Add the vinegar, honey, salt and pepper;  toss one more time, and serve.
