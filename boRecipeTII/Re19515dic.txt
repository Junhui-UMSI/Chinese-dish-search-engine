Angoor Ka Sherbet
Grapes, green, seedless, -Water, chilled, 750 ml, Castor sugar; finely ground, Lemon juice, Mint, fresh; few sprigs, Cumin seeds; roasted &, -Salt, -Pepper, ground, 
Wash grapes and crush them throughly. Pour in water. Mix well. Strain  through a sieve. Add all the seasonings. Mix well. Chill. Serve, garnished  with mint leaves.    MAKES: 6 large glasses SOURCE: _Rotis and Naans of India_ by Purobi Babbar,  published in Bombay posted, not tested by Anne MacLellan
