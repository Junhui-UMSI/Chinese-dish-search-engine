Baked Ham with Orange Glaze
Brown sugar, Flour, Vinegar, Dry mustard, Frozen orange juice, (10-14 lb) ham, 
Cook ham fat side up on rack in open roasting pan. Do not add water or  cover. Bake at 325, allowing 15 minutes per pound. About 30 minutes before  ham is done, trim off skin and excess fat. Combine all other ingredients  and spread over ham. Finish cooking, basting occasionally.
