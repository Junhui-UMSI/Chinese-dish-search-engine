Tuna Patties
SOUR CREAM SAUCE, Celery, Juice of 1/2 lemon, green onion, Butter, green onion, salt and pepper, Cucumber, Sour cream, Lemon juice, CUCUMBER SAUCE, bread crumbs, Egg, Shortening, Sour cream, Tuna, garlic, 
Mix ingredients, except shortening, together and form into patties. Melt  shortening in heavy skillet and saute the patties until golden brown. Drain  on absorbent paper. Serve hot with sauce or chill for several hours or  overnight and serve cold with Cucumber sauce.    Cucumber sauce:  Mix all ingredients well and chill.    Sour Cream Sauce:  In small saucepan saute onion in butter. Lower heat and  stir in sour cream and lemon juice. Salt and pepper to taste. Serve over  hot patties.    From:  The Tuna Cookbook
