Appetizer Ham Ball
Olives, Chopped stuffed green, (3-oz) cream cheese, Softened, milk, (4.5-oz) deviled ham, Prepared mustard, Tabasco sauce to taste, 
Blend deviled ham, olives, mustard and tabasco. Form in ball on serving  dish; chill/. Combine cream cheese and milk; frost ball with mixture.  Chill; remove from refrigerator 15 minutes before serving. Trim with  parsley. Pass assorted crackers.
