Apple Syrup
Unsweetened apple juice, Brown sugar, Salt, Nutmeg, Cornstarch, Lime juice, 
Makes 2 cups.  Serves 8-10 as a topping for pancakes. Also great as a  topping for cottage cheese or frozen yogurt.    Combine apple juice, brown sugar, cornstarch, salt and nutmeg in saucepan.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and cook, stirring occasionally, 3-5 min or  until thickened. Stir in lime juice (optional).
