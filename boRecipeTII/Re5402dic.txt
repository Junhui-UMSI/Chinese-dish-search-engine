Pineapple Cheese Dream
Cornstarch, Lemon peel; grated, Lemon juice, Water, Egg yolks, Egg whites, Neufchatel cheese/low, Can unsweetened crushed, Equal? sweetener, 
Combine cornstarch and water in a medium saucepan. Stir until smooth and  add pineapple. Cook over medium heat until mixture boils and thickens,  stirring well. Remove from heat, stir in lemon juice, and beaten egg yolks.  Add cheese. Return to heat and continue cooking and stirring until mixture  bubbles. Remove from heat, add Equal, and beat with mixer to blend in  cheese. Set aside to cool. Beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Fold into  cooled pineapple mixture. Spoon into dessert dishes and refrigerate until  served.    Exchanges: 1 fruit and 1 fat.
