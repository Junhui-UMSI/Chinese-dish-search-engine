Beef-Cabbage Rolls/clay Cooker
Salt pork; ground, Salt; pepper, Large cabbage leaves, Rice; cooked, Egg, Consomme, Butter, Lean ground beef, 
Soak lid of glazed clay cooker. In a bowl mix well all ingredients. Put  into clay cooker. Cover and place in cold oven. Turn temperature to 425F  and bake for 80 minutes.
