Butter Chicken (Chicken Makhani)
Yogurt, plain, Ginger, Salt, Red food colouring, Chicken, skinless, Vegetable oil, Butter, Cinnamon, Cloves, Green cardamoms, Bay leaf, Sour cream, Saffron, Cream, Salt, Almonds, Cornstarch, Water, 
Mix yogurt, ginger, salt and red coloring and rub into chicken.  Let it  marinate overnight.    Place in an ovenproof dish and brush with oil.  Bake in a 375 degree oven  for 40-50 minutes.  Save the liquid, if any.    In a saucepan, melt butter and fry cinnamon, cloves, cardamoms and bayleaf  for 1 minute.  Add sour cream and chicken liquid.  Add crushed saffron, and  cream.  Cover and simmer gently for 5-6 minutes. Add chicken pieces and  adjust seasoning.  Add ground almonds. Dissolve cornstarch in water and add  to the chicken.    Let it thicken.  Cover and simmer for 3-4 minutes.  Remove from heat. Serve  with nan.    Serving Ideas : Nan makes a pleasant accompaniment to this dish.    NOTES : Orange food coloring can be used in place of the red.                               Indian Cooking by Lalita Ahmed, 1984    From: "Imran C."
