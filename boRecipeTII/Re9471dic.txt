Pumpkin Bread #11
Salt, Eggs, Cloves, Flour, Cinnamon, Pumpkin, Sugar, Water, Baking soda, Chocolate chips (optional I, Baking Powder, Butter, 
From: lcale@moose.uvm.edu ("Lauren T. Cale")    Date: Tue, 22 Nov 1994 09:14:14 +0000    Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, pumpkin and water. Sift flour, powder,  soda, salt and spices. Add to pumpkin mixture. Stir in chocolate chips (the  more the better!) Pour into 2 greased loaf pans (9-1/2x5x3). Bake @ 350 for        1    hr.
