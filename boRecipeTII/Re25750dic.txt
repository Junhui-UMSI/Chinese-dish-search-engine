Babe Ruth Bars
Sugar, Spanish peanuts, Corn flakes, White Karo syrup, Brown sugar, Peanut butter, 
Bring syrup and sugars just to a boil. Add peanut butter and mix well. Stir  in peanuts and cereal. Press into buttered 9 x 13-inch pan. Put candy bars  on top and place in 350 degrees oven until candy bars melt, then spread  evenly.
