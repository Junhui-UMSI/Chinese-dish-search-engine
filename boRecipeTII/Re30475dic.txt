Banana Nut Bread
Sugar, Nuts, chopped, Mashed Bananas, Eggs, Baking soda, Butter, All-purpose flour, Vanilla extract, Buttermilk, Salt, 
Cream together butter and sugar. Add banana, eggs, and vanilla. Sift  together dry ingredients. Add alternately with buttermilk. Blend well. Stir  in nuts, and pour into a greased and floured loaf pan. Bake at 325 degrees  for 1-1/4 hours.
