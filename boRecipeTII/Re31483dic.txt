Barbecue Sauce (Microwave)
Butter or margarine, Clove garlic,, Firm packed dark brown sugar, Onion, finely chopped, Finely chopped green pepper, Water, (few) Tabasco sauce,to taste, Catsup, Salt, Cider vinegar, Dry mustard, 
1. Place butter, onion, garlic and green pepper in a deep, 1 1/2- quart  heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole and heat in Microwave Oven 3 minutes  or until tender. Stir occasionally. 2. Add remaining ingredients; stir well  to combine. 3. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 2 minutes or until sauce  bubbles. Serve over meat or poultry. Makes 2 1/4 cups
