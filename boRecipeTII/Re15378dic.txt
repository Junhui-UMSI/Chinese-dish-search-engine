White Cake
egg whites, Vanilla, Shortening, Flour, milk, Sugar, Baking Powder, 
Cream Crisco and sugar -- add dry ingredients alternately with milk. --  lastly fold in beaten egg whites.    Bake in layer pans at 350 for about 30 minutes.    Ice with White Cake Icing.    Recipe by: Nannie Posted to EAT-L Digest 25 Mar 97 by Sharon  <jouet@MINDSPRING.COM> on Mar 25, 1997
