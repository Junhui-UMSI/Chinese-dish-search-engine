Apple Crisp #02
Sugar, Flour, Brown sugar, Water, Butter, Apples; thinly sliced, 
Place apples in 6x10-inch pyrex dish with water. Crumble butter in flour.  Add sugars & mix well. Sprinkle over apples. Bake at 375 for 40 minutes.    MRS.
