Tomato, Pickled Onion and Cilantro Sandwiches
Arge, Tomatoes, -- sliced, Fresh cilantro sprigs, Up, Lices, Up, Whole-grain bread, --, Up, Toasted lightly if d, Pickled onions, Mayonnaise, 
Spread bread with mayonnaise and make 4 sandwiches with tomato slices,  pickled onions, and cilantro sprigs, pressing together gently. Makes 4  sandwiches.    Recipe By     : Gourmet-August 1994    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
