Beef and Beer (Bisteces Con Cerveza)
Flour, Vegetable oil, Steaks (round; sirloin or, Salt and pepper, Onion; sliced, Glass of beer, Tomato paste, 
1. Put salt and pepper on the steaks and fry until golden.    2. Mix the beer with the slices of onion, the tomato, the flour and a  little water.    3. Add the mix to the pan with the meat. Cover the frying pan and cook for  a half hour. Serve hot.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 218 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 07, 1997
