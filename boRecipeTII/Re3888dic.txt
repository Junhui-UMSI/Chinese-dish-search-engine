Pepper Jam
Sugar, Red bell pepper, Red hot peppers, Pectin, Apple juice, Plum tomato, Cider vinegar, Pouch, 
Dice peppers and tomato. Bring all ingredients, except pectin, to full boil  in med saucepan. Add pectin, return to boil. Cook for one minute, fill jars  and seal.  -----    Recipe By     : GEPlatts    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
