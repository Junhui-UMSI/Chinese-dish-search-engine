Watermelon Ice
Water, Lime Juice, fresh, Watermelon, cubed, seeded, Granulated sugar, 
Servings:  4    In a small saucepan, combine sugar and water; bring to a boil and cook  until sugar is dissolved, about 1 - 2 minutes. Or, place sugar and water in  a 2-cup glass measure. Microwave at High for 2 - 3 minutes or until liquid  comes to a boil, stirring once to dissolve the sugar. Let cool. Puree the  watermelon in a food processor or blender until smooth; place in a bowl  along with sugar syrup and lime juice. Pour into an 8-inch square baking  dish. Freeze until nearly solid, about 2 hours. Scoop mixture into a food  processor or blender and beat until mixture is smooth, but not melted.  Spoon into dishes and serve immediately. Serves 4.
