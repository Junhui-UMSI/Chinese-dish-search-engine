Pizza Fondue
Ground beef, Basil, Onions, French bread, Pepper, Garlic powder, mozzarella, Cornstarch, Oregano, Cheddar Cheese, spaghetti sauce  , 
In large frying pan, saute beef & onion until browned. Drain grease &  return pan to heat. In a bowl, combine sauce with cornstarch, pepper,  basil, oregano & garlic powder; stir well & add to meat. Cook,  stirring, until thickened. Stir in cheese until melted. Place in an  11*7" pan & stir well. Cut bread into cubes & dip into mixture.
