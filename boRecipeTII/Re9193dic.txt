Prune Corn Bread
Flour, milk, Pitted cooked prunes, chop, Eggs, well beaten, Sugar, Baking Powder, Salt, Melted shortening, Yellow corn meal, 
Sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar together. Stir corn meal  thoroughly into flour mixture.  Combine eggs, shortening, prunes and milk;  add to dry ingredients. Mix well, pour into greased pan and bake in a 375  degree oven for about 30 minutes.  Makes 1 (8x8x2 inch) loaf.
