Asparagus-Artichoke Combo
(15-oz) asparagus tips, Chicken broth, Salt, Onion, Butter, Lemon juice, Jar (4-oz) pimiento, (15-oz) artichoke hearts, Garlic, 
Drain vegetables. In a 2-quart casserole, layer asparagus and artichokes.  Saute onion and garlic in butter; add chicken broth, lemon juice and salt.  Pour over vegetables. Decorate top with pimiento. Heat at 350 until hot  throughout and bubbling.    MRS J.L.
