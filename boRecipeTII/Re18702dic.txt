Amy Scherbers Dough Rolls
<<--NONE-->>, 
This recipe can be divided into 2 dozen pieces and shaped into rolls.    Place in a square pan with rolls touching and sprinkle with oats. Let rise  for 1 1/2 to 2 hours and bake in a preheated 425 degree oven for 10  minutes. Reduce temperature to 375 degrees and bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
