Pickled Beets (Syltede Rodbeder)
(1 lb each) sliced beets, Vinegar, Caraway seeds, Sugar, Water, 
Place a layer of beets in a bowl. Sprinkle with sugar and a few caraway  seeds. Repeat layers. Heat vinegar and water together. Pour over beets.  Chill for 24 hours. Drain and serve.    Posted to Recipe Archive - 06 Oct 96    submitted by: LeiG@aol.com    Date: Thu, 5 Sep 1996 21:04:28 -0500 (CDT)
