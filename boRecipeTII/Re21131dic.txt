Two-Hour Spaghetti Sauce
Chili powder, Pepper, Bay leaf, Can Whole Tomatoes, Can Tomato Paste, Salt, Onion -- chopped, Oregano, Cream of mushroom soup, Water, Salad oil, Ground beef, Garlic salt, Can Tomato Sauce, 
Brown onion in oil; add meal and seasonings; cook until meat is brown. Add  soup, paste, water and all other ingredients. Simmer, uncovered, for 2 to 2      1/2    hours.
