Swedish Dream Potatoes
Eggs; well-beaten, Salt, Pepper, Butter or margarine, Frozen Ore-Ida chopped, Commercial sour cream, Cheddar cheese, Bran flakes, Frozen Ore-Ida golden, 
Here are a few recipes using Frozen French Fries from: Ore-Ida  Magic....Family Favorites from Ore-Ida.    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. greae a 1 1/2 quart casserole.    2. In large skillet, over medium-high heat, melt butter; add frozen  potatoes and heat 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to medium,  add onion and cook 5 minutes, or until potatoes and onions are browned,  stirring frequently to avoid overbrowning. Remove from heat, gently toss  with bran flakes and shredded cheese. Place mixture in casserole.    3. In mixing bowl, beat eggs well, blend in well, sour cream and  seasonings; pour sauce over potatoes.    4. bake 20 minutes or until sauce is set but shiny.    5-6 servings    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Nsabatino <Nsabatino@AOL.COM> on Mar 9, 1998
