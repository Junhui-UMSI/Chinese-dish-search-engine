Pennsylvania Red Cabbage
Caraway seed, Water, Apple, olive oil, Head red cabbage, Vinegar, Salt, Sugar brown, 
Core and cut apples into quarters. Chop the cabbage in blender. Put in a  sauce pan and add caraway seed. Put 1/2 cup water and remaining ingredients  in blender and process on chop until apples are coarsely chopped. Pour over  cabbage and simmer covered for 1 hour.
