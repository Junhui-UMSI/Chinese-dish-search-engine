People Chow #2
Butter, Powdered sugar, Peanut butter, Pk chocolate chips, Crispix cereal OR Corn Chex, 
Mix butter, peanut butter and chips together in saucepan over med heat or  melt in bowl in microwave. Pour over cereal in a lg bowl. Stir to coat. Put  powdered sugar in a brown grocery bag and pour cereal into bag; shake to  coat. Cut open bag to lay flat and spread out coated cereal. Sprinkle with  extra sugar if necessary.
