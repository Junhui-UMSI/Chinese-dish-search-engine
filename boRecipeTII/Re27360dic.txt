Toasted Cinnamon Slices
Walnuts, Recipe Passover Cookie, Almonds, Cinnamon-Sugar mixture, 
OVEN: 350 degrees F Ungreased cookie sheets    Prepare one recipe of Passover Cookie Dough. ( * see recipe.)    Grind almonds and walnuts; add to dough. Roll into 2-inch loaves.    Bake for 20 to 25 minutes. Slice, sprinkle with cinnamon-sugar mixture and  return to oven.    Toast on each side for 5 minutes.    Recipe by: Something Different for Passover by Frances AvRutick  1986    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest by Linda Shapiro <lss@coconet.com> on Feb 7,  1998
