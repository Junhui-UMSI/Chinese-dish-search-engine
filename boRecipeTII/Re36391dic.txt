Beer Batter for Fried Chicken
beer, Flour, Egg yolk, Pepper, Salt, 
Combine dry ingredients & set aside. Beat egg yolk & slowly add beer.  Gradually add this to dry mixture. Moisten chicken. Dip in seasoned flour  then dip in batter. Dip in seasoned flour again. Fry. (This may also be  used for onion rings & other vegetables).    SALLY RIDENOUR    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
