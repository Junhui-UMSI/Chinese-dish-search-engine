Apple Muffins
Honey, AM Instant Oatmeal, AM Garbanzo Flour, Non-alum baking powder, Raisins (optional), Water, AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil, Apple; grated, Sea salt (optional), 
Preheat oven to 375 F.    Mix dry ingredients.  Mix liquids and stir into dry mix.  Let sit a few  minutes to thicken.  Fill oiled muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake at 350 F. for       30    minutes or until done.    VARIATION: A beaten egg may be added to the batter if fluffier muffins are  desired.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "toasted Garbanzo Flour" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
