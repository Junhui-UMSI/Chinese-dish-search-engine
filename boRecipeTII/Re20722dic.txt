Apple Crunch Pupcakes
Applesauce; unsweetened, Honey, Baking Powder, Egg; beaten slightly, Dried apple chips; ( you can, Water, Whole wheat flour, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray muffin tin with cooling spray.    Mix all wet ingredients thoroughly. Combine dry ingredients in separate  bowl. Add wet to dry slowly , scraping well to make sure no dry mixture is  left. Pour into muffin tins. Bake for 1 1/4 hours or until a toothpick  inserted into center comes out dry. Sotre in a sealed container. Makes  around 12-14 pupcakes.    Posted to Bread-bakers Digest by Marg aka MEMAW <maf@fn.net> on Mar 29,  1998
