Banana Split Pie - Very Rich!
Crushed pineapple  , PART A, Bananas, powdered sugar, oleo margarine, PART B, cream cheese, Nuts, PART C, graham crackers, Cool Whip, Maraschino cherries, 
Mix together part "A" and place in long pan or 2 pie pans. Mix part "B"  together well and spread over crust. Slice 4 or 5 bananas on top of cream  cheese mixture. Spread pineapple on top of bananas. Spread 8-oz. Cool Whip  on top and sprinkle with nuts and maraschino cherries on top.  Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #219 by Shelley Sparks  <ssparks@mailbox.arn.net> on Sep 04, 1997
