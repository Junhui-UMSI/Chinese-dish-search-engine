Peanut Butter Pie #1
Baked pie shell, Vanilla, milk, Egg yolks; beaten, Salt, Peanut butter, Powdered sugar, Sugar, Flour, Butter, 
Scald milk. Add flour, sugar & salt. Cook a few minutes, stirring  constantly. Before it thickens, add a few drops of hot mixture to beaten  egg yolks, stir well & pour into hot mixture. Add butter & vanilla. Crumble  together peanut butter & powdered sugar like pie dough. Add half peanut  butter mixture to hot mixture. Stir well. Pour into baked pie shell, top  with meringue & the other half of peanut butter mixture. Bake at 350 until  golden brown.
