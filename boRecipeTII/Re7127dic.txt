Poppy-Seed Rolls
Yeast cake; fresh, Sugar, Salt, Milk; scalded, Fat, Flour; bread, 
Scald milk, add fat and sugar. Cool to lukewarm. Add yeast dissolved  in a little warm water. Add salt and part of flour. Beat until it  forms bubbles. Add rest of flour and knead into soft dough. Let rise.  Shape into rolls, let rise again. Bake partly through, remove from  oven and brush with beaten egg diluted with water. Sprinkle with poppy  seeds and bake to golden brown.    Source: Mrs. D. E. Wilcox, Streetsboro Grange, Portage County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
