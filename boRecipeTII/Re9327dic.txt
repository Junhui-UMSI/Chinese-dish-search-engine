Puffed Chicken Wings
Dry sherry, milk, Baking Powder, Sesame oil, Gingerrroot -- minced, Flour, MARINADE-----, Egg -- lightly beaten, Garlic -- minced, BATTER-----, Cornstarch, Chicken Wings, 
Add wings to marinade for at least one hour. Combine dry ingredients, stir  in egg and milk. Pat wings dry. Dip in batter. Drip off excess. Heat oil  350. Fry few wings at a time, 3-4 minutes.
