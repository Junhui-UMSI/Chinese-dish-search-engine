Preserve Fruitcake
Butter, Buttermilk, Chopped pecans (or almonds), Apricot preserves, Flour, Pineapple preserves, Eggs, Baking soda, Vanilla, Sugar, Nutmeg, Cherry preserves, Cinnamon, 
Cream butter, sugar and eggs until light. Sift dry ingredients together;  add to creamed mixture alternately with buttermilk (to which baking soda  has been added.). Fold in remaining ingredients. Bake in 2 - 9X5X3 inch  greased and floured loaf pans or one large tube cake pan at 250 degrees for  1 & 1/2 hours. Cool in pan 15 minutes before removing.
