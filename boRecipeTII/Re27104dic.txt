Thyme-Fig Fruitcake
Sugar, Unsweetened apple juice, Dried thyme, Pine nuts, toasted, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Vegetable oil, Flour, Cornmeal, Finely chopped dried figs, Egg, 
Combine the apple juice, thyme, and figs in a bowl.  Set aside for 10  minutes.  Stir together the flour, cornmeal, baking powder, and  baking soda in a bowl.    Beat the oil, sugar and egg until well blended.  Pour the egg mixture  into the flour.  Add the pine nuts and fig mixture.  Beat well.    Pour the batter into an oiled and floured 9-inch round (or 8 inch  square) baking pan.  Bake in 350 F oven for 40 to 50 minutes.  Cool  for 5 minutes in the pan.  Remove from pan and cool thoroughly.    1/12 recipe - 190 calories, 1 bread, 1 fruit, 1 1/2 fat exchange 27  grams carbohydrate, 3 grams protein, 8 grams fat 66 mg sodium, 156 mg  potassium, 23 mg cholesterol    Source:  Am.
