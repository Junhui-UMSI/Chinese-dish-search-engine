Wilted Spinach Salad
Spinach; torn, Water, Egg, Purple onion; sliced,, Cider vinegar, Sugar, Bacon, Plus, Fresh mushrooms, Hard-cooked eggs; sliced, 
Date: Fri, 09 Feb 1996 20:23:47 EST    From: AKSC87A@prodigy.com (MARY JO KNAPPER)  Combine first 4 ingredients in a large salad bowl; cover and chill  thoroughly.    Cook bacon in a large skillet until crisp; remove bacon, reserving  drippings in skillet. Crumble bacon, and set aside. Add water, vinegar, and  sugar to skillet; stir well. Bring to a boil; remove from heat.    Gradually stir about one-fourth of hot mixture into beaten egg. Add egg  mixture to skillet, stirring until blended. Cook over low heat, stirring  constantly, until thickened. Stir in half of bacon. Drizzle warm dressing  over salad. Sprinkle with remaining half of bacon. Yield: 6 servings    Source: Southern Living Five-Star Recipe Collection.    MC-RECIPE@MASTERCOOK.COM    MASTERCOOK RECIPES LIST SERVER    From the MasterCook recipe list.
