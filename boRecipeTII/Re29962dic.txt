Teriyaki Steak for Fondue
Sugar, White wine, Ground ginger, Garlic, Sirloin steak, Soy sauce, 
Cut steak into 3/4-inch cubes. Combine other ingredients and put in a dish  with a cover. Add steak cubes and marinate for at least 30 to 40 minutes.  Spear meat with fondue fork and cook in hot oil until as done as desired.  Yield: 4 to 6 servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
