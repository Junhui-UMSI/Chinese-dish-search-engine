To Die for French Toast
Butter, apple jelly, Ground cloves, milk, French bread, Salt, Apple sauce, Corn syrup, Sugar, Vanilla, Tart apples, SPICY APPLE SYRUP, Eggs, Cinnamon, 
Cook sugar, butter and syrup until syrupy, pour into a 9 x 13 inch baking  dish.  Spread apple slices over this.  Slice bread into 3/4 inch slices and  place on top of apple slices.  Whisk together remaining ingredients and  pour over bread. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Bake uncovered for 40  minutes in a 350 degree oven. Serve with spicy apple syrup.    Spicy Apple Syrup: Combine all ingredients in small saucepan.  Cook over  medium heat, stirring consistently until jelly melts and syrup is hot.
