Wild Mustang Chili
Medium onions chopped, Round cumin, Lack pepper, Cloves of garlic minced, Whiskey, Tewed, sliced tomatos, Rum, Lemon, Omato sauce, Oney, Hopped green Bell peppers, Round sage, Alt to taste, Round cloves, Rough ground chuck beef, Fresh Jalapeno~ seeded and c, Jalapeno~ peppers whole, Omato paste, 
Saute the meat, onions and Bell peppers together. Add this to the large  cooking pot into which you have combined all the other ingredients. Bring  to a boil for 2-3 minutes, reduce heat, and cook for 40 minutes. Be sure to  stir frequently. Salt to taste. Raise heat to high and bring to a boil for        2    minutes.
