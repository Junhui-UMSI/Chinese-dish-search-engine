Peppermint Stick Punch
Bottles (32-ounce) club, Candy canes, Sugar, Lime juice from concentrate, White creme de menthe or, Vodka or water, 
In punch bowl, combine all ingredients except club soda and candy canes;  stir until sugar dissolves. Just before serving, add club soda. Hang candy  canes on edge of punch bowl or place in each punch cup for stirrer.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #935 by "Eugene Johnston"  <ejohnston@mailexcite.com> on Nov 30, 1997
