Tomato Aspic
Add:, Lemon gelatin, Tomato juice (heat), Worcestershire Sauce, Tabasco sauce, Mix above and let cool, Onion, Salt and pepper, Tiny Monarch or Richeliue, Vinegar, Finely chopped celery, Finely chopped amount of, Finely chopped green pepper, 
Pour into molds.  Makes 16 molds.    Randy Rigg
