Sweet and Sour Sesame Pork Chops
Vinegar, Soy sauce, Green Pepper; chopped, Honey, Sesame oil, Onion, Light Brown Sugar; packed, Pork Chops; about 1/2" thick, Water, Toasted Sesame Seeds, Flour, 
Combine brown sugar and flour; dredge pork chops in mixture. Arrange chops  in a 12 x 8" microwave-safe baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining flour  mixture. In a small mixing bowl combine water, vinegar, soy sauce, honey  and sesame oil. Beat with wire whisk; pour over pork chops. Add onion and  green pepper.    Cover dish with wax paper and microwave 40-50 minutes, or until pork chops  are tender, turning and rearranging chops after 20 minutes. Let stand,  covered, 5 minutes before serving. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds.    Source: Medford Mail Tribune, 20 September 1994 Typed by Katherine Smith  Kook-Net: The Shadow Zone IV - Stinson Beach, CA
