Basic Chocolate Cookie Mix
Salt, Sugar, Baking Powder, Margarine or butter, Flour, all-purpose, 
Combine thoroughly flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and salt in a large  bowl.  An electric mixer on slow speed can be used for mixing. ) Add butter  OR solid margarine; cut in with a pastry blender or with hands until  mixture resembles coarse cornmeal. Store in an airtight container in  refrigerator. NOTE:  After refrigeration, measure amount of mix needed;  bring to room temperature for easier blending. YIELD: Approximately 11 cups    Recipe By     :
