Rebeccas Corn Spoon Bread
Stick butter or margarine, Creamed corn, Eggs, Container (8 oz.) sour cream, Box (8.5 oz.) Jiffy corn, Corn, 
Preheat oven to 350. Melt the butter or margarine in a 2 qt. casserole  dish. Stir in remaining ingredients until blended. Bake at 350 for 45  minutes. Posted to EAT-L Digest 11 Mar 97 by Wendi J Grezlak  <wgrezlak@JUNO.COM> on Mar 12, 1997
