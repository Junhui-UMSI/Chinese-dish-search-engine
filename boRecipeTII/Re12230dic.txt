Zucchini Cobbler
Ground nutmeg, Crust, Lemon juice, Ground cinnamon, All-purpose flour, Cold butter or margarine, Ground cinnamon, Sugar, Zucchini (peeled) (aprox 3, Sugar, 
In a large saucepan over medium-low heat, cook and stir zucchini and lemon  juice for 15-20 minutes or until zucchini is tender.  Add sugar, cinnamon  and nutmeg; simmer 1 minute longer. Remove from the heat; set aside. For  crust, combine the flour and sugar in a bowl; cut in butter until the  mixure resembles coarse crumbs. Stir 1/2 cup into zucchini mixture. Press  half of remainig crust mixture into a greased 15-in x 10-in, x 1-in. baking  pan. Spread zucchini over top; crumble remaining crust mixture over  zucchini. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Bake at 375 for 35-40 minutes or until  golden and bubbly.
