Vanilla Kipferln
Flour, Butter, Sugar, Vanilla extract, Blanched almonds, ground, 
Combine cut up butter, sugar, flour and almonds with hands and work till  dough is smooth. Chill dough at least 1 hour Pinch off small pieces and  roll into crescents Bake 350 12-15 min. Remove and roll in sugar while hot.    Recipe by: Nanny Mizzi Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #525 by kriegz  <kriegz@bccom.com> on Mar 19, 1997
