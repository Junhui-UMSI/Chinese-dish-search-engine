Picadillo (Beef Hash)
Vegetable oil, Ground ham, Tomato sauce, Ground pork, Ground beef, Salt, Onion, Garlic, Pepper, Green pepper, Capers, Dry wine, 
Heat oil and saute minced onion, crushed garlic and minced green pepper.  Add the meat and cook a few minutes, stirring constantly to avoid sticking.  Add the remaining ingredients and cook over medium heat for about 20  minutes. Serves 6. Picadillo (beef hash)    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
