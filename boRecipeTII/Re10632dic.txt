Quick Pots De Creme
Sugar, Semi-sweet chocolate bits, salt, Egg, Rum, milk, 
Heat milk to boiling point. Place all other ingredients in an electric  blender, and add hot milk. Blend at low speed one minute. Stir with a  rubber spatula to remove bubbles. Pour into pot de creme cups and chill  several hours before serving.    Mrs. Thomas H. Morton    From a book of recipes compiled by members and friends of the LONDONTOWN  PUBLIK HOUSE ASSEMBLY, EDGEWATER, MD 21037.
