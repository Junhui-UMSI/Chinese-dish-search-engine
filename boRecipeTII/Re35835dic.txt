Stuffed French Bread
Mayonnaise, Loaf French bread; (cut in 4, Italian; (6 oz.) Salad, Chopped parsley, Cream cheese; (softened), Jar (4 Oz.) pimento; drained, 
Hollow out bread pieces, leaving 1/2 inch thick walls. Spread inside with  1/2 cup of mayonnaise; sprinkle with 1/2 cup of the chopped parsley.  Combine remaining ingredients; pack mixture into bread. Wrap in plastic;  chill several hours. Cut into 1/2 inch slices.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 24,  1998
