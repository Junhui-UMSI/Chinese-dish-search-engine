Peanut Chocolate-Chip Cookies
Salt, Crunchy Peanut Butter, Plain flour, Wholemeal Flour, Egg, Chocolate, Chopped, Soft Brown Sugar, Butter, Room temperature, 
Preheat oven to gas mark 6/200C/400F.    In a large bowl, beat together the sugar and butter until fluffy and white.  Beat in the egg, then add the peanut butter and plain chocolate. Fold in  the wholemeal and plain flours and salt. Combine the mixture thoroughly.  Put generous teaspoonfuls of the mixture on a baking sheet and flatten into  rounds with a fork. Bake cookies in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes  until golden and crisp on the outside.    Source: CHAT Magazine
