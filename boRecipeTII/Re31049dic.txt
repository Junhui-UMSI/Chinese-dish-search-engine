Banana Pecan Crunch
Butter, Sliced, Pecans -- chopped, Butter -- melted, Molasses, Brown sugar, Crosswise, Bananas -- peeled and, 
In an 11x7x1-1/2-inch baking pan, melt 2 tablespoons butter; layer bananas  and nuts in pan.  Drizzle with molasses. Sprinkle brown sugar and melted  butter atop.  Bake in a 350 degree oven for 10 minutes. Serve with ice  cream.  Yield: 6    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
