Sweet Rolls
-(up to), Salt, Warm water, All-purpose flour, Anise seed, Light corn syrup, Active dry yeast, Butter or Margarine, Sugar, Eggs; at room temp, Egg yolk, 
Date: Tue, 07 May 1996 10:55:21 -0400    From: theservs@gate.net (Thomas E. Haug)    Sprinkle yeast over water in a large warm bowl. Stir until yeast is  dissolved. Add sugar, salt, anise seed, melted butter, eggs, and 2 cups of  flour; beat until smooth. Stir in enough additional flour to make a soft  dough Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface; knead until smooth and  elastic (8 to 10 minutes). Put dough into a greased bowl; turn to grease  top. Cover; let rise in a warm place until double in bulk (about 1 hour.  Punch dough down and turn onto lightly floured surface; roll into a 12-inch  square. Cut into fourths and cut each square into 4 triangles. Allowing  space for rising, place triangles on greased cookie sheets. Cover; let rise  in warm place until double in bulk about 1 hour. Beat egg yolk and corn  syrup together until blended Generously brush over triangles. Bake at 350  10 to 15 minutes. Serve warm.
