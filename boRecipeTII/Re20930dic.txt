Apple or Grape Jelly
Sugar, Liquid Certo, juice, 
Combine juice and sugar into a large pan.  Place over moderate heat and  bring to a boil.  Stir in Certo and bring to a full rolling boil and  continue at a full rolling boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat, skim and  bottle at once.    This is from way back in the late 60s.
