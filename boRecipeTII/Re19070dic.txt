Vegetable Clam Chowder
Vegetables (such as Broccoli, Salt, Pepper, Clams, drained, Corn and Red Peppers mix), (6 1/2 ounces each) minced, Margarine or butter, milk, (16 oz.) frozen mixed, 
Place margarine and frozen vegetables in 3-quart microwavable casserole.  Cover tightly and microwave on high 8 to 10 minutes or until hot. Stir in  remaining ingredients. Cover tightly and microwave on medium- high (70%) 10  to 12 minutes, stirring after 5 minutes, until hot.
