Western Wheat Pilaf
Bouillon cubes, (chickenflavored), Water, California Dried Figs, Finely grated orange peel, Bulgur wheat, Sherry, (finely chopped), Margarine, Sesame seeds or pine nuts, Onion, Orange; juiced (about 1/4 c), 
Saute onion in margarine until soft. Stir in wheat to coat with margarine.  Add low fat chicken bouillon cubes and water.  Measure orange juice and  add enough sherry to make 1/2 cup.  Add to pilaf.  Cover and simmer over  low heat for 35 minutes.  Five minutes before serving, stir in figs,  sesame seeds or pine nuts and orange peel.  Cover to complete cooking.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
