Pineapple Coconut Shake
Coconut extract, Buttermilk, Sweetened shredded coconut, pineapple, juice packed, canned, 
Place all ingredients in blender and process for 5-10 seconds, or until  blended. Pour into tall glasses and serve. Serves 2.    Recipe by: Home Cooking July 1996 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #731 by  L979@aol.com on Aug 9, 1997
