Banana Carrot
Bananas -- mashed, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Granulated sugar, Vanilla, Applesauce -- at room, Carrots -- grated, Egg whites -- whipped, Temperature, Cloves, Salt, Unbleached flour, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.  In a  mixing bowl, combine applesauce, egg whites, bananas, carrots, and vanilla.  In another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon,  salt, baking soda, and cloves.  Mix wet ingredients with dry ingredients  just until moistened.  Pour into prepared pan(s) and bake for 1 hour  depending on pan size.    Recipe By     : The Book of Bread    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
