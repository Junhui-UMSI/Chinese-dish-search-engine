Plum and Peach Salad
Coarsely chopped walnuts,, Peaches, sliced, Red wine vinegar or, Walnut or vegetable oil, Plums, sliced, Raspberry preserves, 
Arrange plums and peaches on serving plate. Sprinkle with walnuts. Mix  remaining ingredients. Drizzle over fruit. 6 SERVINGS (ABOUT 1/2 CUP EACH);      135    CALORIES PER SERVING.
