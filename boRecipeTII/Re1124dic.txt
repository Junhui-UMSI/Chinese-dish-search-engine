Cranberry Chutney
Allspice, Are the oranges and, Apples to be, Peeled?, Orange, chopped, Apple chopped, Sized white onion, diced, Whole cranberries, Water, Can jellied cranberry sauce, Sugar, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, 
1. Place orange, apple, onion, and cranberries in a pan over medium heat.    2. Add water, cranberry sauce, sugar, and spices and bring to a boil.    3. Reduce heat and simmer until it thickens (30 to 35 minutes), stirring  occasionally.    4. Serve chilled or warm.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Sep  15, 1998
