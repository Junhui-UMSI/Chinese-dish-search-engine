Peanut Butter Buddy Bars
Sugar, Salt, Peanut butter, Eggs, Flour, Chocolate chips, Vanilla extract, Butter, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In large bowl beat peanut butter and butter  until smooth. Add sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Beat until creamy. Blend in  flour and salt. Stir in 1 cup chocolate chips. Spread in 13 x 9 inch  ungreased baking pan. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until edges brown. Remove  from oven. Immediately spread with rest of chocolate chips. After chips  melt spread evenly over top. Cool and cut.    Recipe by: Yankee Kitchen    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 307 by Yummi357@aol.com on Nov  25, 1997
