Beef and Green Chile Stew
Lard or butter, Tomatillo pulp, Garlic, Onion, Flour, Or more green chile peppers;, Dried Oregano, Round steak; cut in 1" cubes, 
Dust meat with flour. Brown with onion and garlic in lard or butter.  Combine the meat and remaining ingredients in slow cooker with 1/2 cup  water. Cover and cook on low 6 to 8 hours. Garnish with coriander and serve  with tortillas.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 406 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Dec 27, 1997
