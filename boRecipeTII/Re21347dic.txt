Uncooked Plum Pudding
Fruit juice, Corn sirup, Cooked prunes, finely, Allspice, Egg whites, stiffly beaten, Lemon juice, Cold water, Sugar, Finely shredded citron, Cinnamon, Raisins, Gelatin, Salt, Boiling water, Grapenuts or rolled graham, 
Soften gelatin in cold water.  Dissolve in boiling water. Add all  ingredients except egg whites.  Mix thoroughly. Chill until partially set.  Fold in egg whites. Pour into long shallow pan. Chill until firm. Cut in  squares with sharp knife.  Serve with whipped cream. 8 servings.    Mrs. E.B. Douglas, Anderson, IN.
