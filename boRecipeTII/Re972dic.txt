Israeli Bean Soup
Small white beans; (navy, Cold water; (for soaking the, olive oil, Onions; chopped, Stalks celery; chopped, Garlic cloves; minced, up to, Carrots; chopped, Water, Potatoes; peeled & cut in, Bay leaves; up to 2, Dried thyme; to taste, Cumin; to taste, up to 2, Tomato paste, Salt & pepper; to taste, Fresh cilantro; minced, up, (28 oz) crushed tomatoes, 
From: MealLeaniYumm by Norene Gilletz    Soak beans in cold water overnight.Drain & rinse well. Heat oil in a large  heavy-bottomed soup pot. Saute onions & celery 5 minutes until golden. Add  garlic & carrots & saute 5 minutes longer. Add water, beans, potatoes, bay  leaves, thyme & cumin. Bring to a boil, reduce heat & simmer partly covered  for 1 hour. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, salt & pepper. Cook 1/2 hour  longer, until beans are tender. Stir in cilantro.    If soup is too thick, thin with a little water. Reheats and/or freezes  well.
