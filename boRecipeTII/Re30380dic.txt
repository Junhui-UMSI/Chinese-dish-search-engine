Banana Crunch Muffins
Grape Nuts cereal, Vanilla, Bananas, White flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, Egg whites, Salt, Whole wheat flour, Baking soda, 
In a small bowl mix all dry ingrediants together. In a larger bowl mix all  wet ingrediants together. Add dry ingrediants to wet ingrediants. Bake for  20-25 minutes (or until toothpick comes out clean) in a 350 degree oven.  This recipe makes about 10 muffins.
