Apple and Raisin Noodle Pudding
Apples, Cinnamon, Butter, Eggs, Egg noodles, Raisins, 
Preheat  oven to 375F.  Cook noodles,  drain in colander & rinse well with  cold water.  Soak raisins in cup of boiling water to soften. Let stand 10  min, then drain. Combine ingredients with noodles, mix well & pour into  buttered casserole dish.  Bake for 45 min or till browned on top. Serve  with yogurt or sour cream. SERVES:4
