Tomato and Bacon Chowder
Bacon, diced, Warm milk, Shredded lettuce, to garnish, Flour, Croutons, to garnish, Butter, Onion, diced, Chicken broth, divided, Stewed tomatoes, 
Fry bacon and drain well; set frying pan aside.  Put bacon into a large  kettle; add tomatoes and three-fourths of the chicken. Deglaze frying pan  wit remaining chicken broth; add mixture to kettle. In another pan, melt  butter and saute onions until tender, but not brown. Add flour and stir  well.  Add to tomato mixture. Slowly stir warm milk into tomato mixture.  Heat well.  Before serving, top each bowl with lettuce and croutons. Serves  8 to 10  Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: The Harvest  Collection.
