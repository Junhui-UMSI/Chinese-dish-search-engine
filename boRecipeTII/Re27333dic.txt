Toadstools
Hershey bar, Large marshmellow, Peanut butter, Saltine cracker, 
Spread peanut butter on saltine cracker. Top with Hershey bar piece and  marshmellow. Microwave 20 seconds. Grown ups will love these as much as the  kids do. They are addicting!!
