Pina Colada
Pineapple Juice; 2 T, Cream Of Coconut; 1 T, Light Rum; 2 T, Crushed Ice, 
Place all ingredients in blender container and blend on high speed until  foamy, about 30 seconds.  Garnish with fruit, if desired.    Miscellaneous recipes from the collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mo-misc.zip
