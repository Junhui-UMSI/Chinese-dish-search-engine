Andys Chocolate No-Bake Cookies
Sugar, Cocoa, Vegetable oil, Skim milk, Oatmeal, Coconut, 
Mix sugar, oil and milk in saucepan. Bring to a full boil. Mix oatmeal,  cocoa and coconut in large mixing bowl. Add sugar mixture, mix together.  Spoon onto waxed paper.    NOTES : Makes 4 dozen cookies.    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Liliana Wajnberg <wajnberg@ccard.com.br> on  May 2, 1998
