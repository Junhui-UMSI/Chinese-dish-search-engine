Tangerine Peel Marinade for Roast Chickn
Garlic, Vegetable stock, Sesame oil; more or less, Sugar, Pepper, Soy sauce, Salt, Piece tangerine peel, 
1. Soak tangerine peel; then mince, along with garlic clove. Place in a  deep bowl.     2. Heat stock and add with remaining ingredients, blending well.     3. Add chicken; let stand 2 hours, turning occasionally. Drain, reserving  marinade for basting. Then roast.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4.
