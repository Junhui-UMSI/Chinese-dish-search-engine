Frisky Caesar
Chipotle puree, Your favorite Caesar, GARNISH, Toasted sunflower or pumpkin, 
By CATHY THOMAS The Orange County Register    Add one-half teaspoon chipotle puree to three-fourths cup of your favorite  Caesar dressing. Toss with romaine, using only enough to lightly coat the  leaves. Garnish with toasted sunflower or pumpkin seeds.
