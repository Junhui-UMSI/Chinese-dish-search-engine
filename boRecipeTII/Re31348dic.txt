Bar-B-Q Wing Dings
Chicken wings, Brown sugar, Sauce, Ketchup, Drops ofworcestershire, Onion, 
Cut off the small piece of the chicken wing and the 2 bone part so you have  only the meaty part. Mix the ketchup, onion cut up and brown sugar and  sauce together. Dip your wing ding in the sauce. Put on cookie sheet. Bake  at 350 degrees for about 1 1/2 hour. If you have extra sauce, cook in  saucepan until thick.
