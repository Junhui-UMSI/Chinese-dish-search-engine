Triple-Mustard Coated Potatoes
Mustard seeds, Dry mustard, Unsalted butter; cut in, Parsley, Hot Dijon-style mustard, Tiny new potatoes; scrubbed, 
Peel off a thin strip of skin around center of each potato. Cook potatoes  in boiling water until tender. Drain, return to cooking pan & add butter.  Cover pan & let stand 5 minutes or until butter is melted. Stir potatoes to  coat with butter; add pepper, Dijon-style mustard, dry mustard, mustard  seeds & mix lightly. Place in an ovenproof dish; keep warm in a low oven  until serving. Sprinkle with parsley just before serving. Makes 4 servings.    From the <Hotter Than Hell!>, by Jane Butel, ISBN 0-89586-646-3  (0-89586-542-4 paperback).  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
