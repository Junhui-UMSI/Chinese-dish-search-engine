Zesty French Dressing
Vinegar, Paprika, Corn oi, Ketchup, Lemon juice, Celery salt, Sugar, Pepper, Salt, Garlic, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Measure ingredients into a jar with a tight seal. Shake well. Chill for  several hours.  Remove garlic. Shake before serving.    Adapted from Hamilton Spectator June 9, 1993
