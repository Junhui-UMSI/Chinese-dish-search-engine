Unstuffing
Bulk Italian sausage, Salt, Onion, Butter or margarine, Chicken broth; (2 1/2 to 3), Celery, Poultry seasoning, Pepper, Unseasoned stuffing croutons, Fresh mushrooms, 
In a large skillet, brown sausage; drain. Add butter, mushrooms, celery and  onion; saute 2-3 minutes or until onion is tender. Stir in poultry  seasoning, salt and pepper. Transfer to a large bowl; add croutonsand  enough broth to moisten. Place in a greased 2 quart baking dish. Cover and  bake at 350 F. for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake for 10 minutes more.
