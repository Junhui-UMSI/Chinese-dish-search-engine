Pineapple Sweet Potatoes
Cans vacuum-packed sweet, Crushed pineapple in its, Salt teaspoon, Sliced almonds, Nutmeg, 
Crushed pineapple in its own juice takes the place of brown sugar in this  guilt-free version of the traditionally mega-calorie side dish. MiCROWAVE  DiRECTiONS: in large bowl, mash potatoes; stir in remaining ingredients  except almonds. Spoon into ungreased 1 1/2-quart microwave-safe casserole;  cover. Microwave on HiGH for 5 to 6 minutes or until thoroughly heated,  turning dish 1/4 turn once during cooking. Just before serving, sprinkle  with almonds and nutmeg. 8 (3/4-Cup) servings.
