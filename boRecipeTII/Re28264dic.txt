Baked French Toast
Dark brown sugar, Slices white bread, Eggs, Egg whites, Evaporated skim milk, Vanilla extract, Ground cinnamon, 
Lightly coat 9- by 13- by 2-inch pan with a non-stick vegetable spray.  Arrange bread slices in the bottom of the pan.     Combine all remaining ingredients and mix well using a whisk or egg  beater.  Pour mixture evenly over bread. Cover tightly and refrigerate  several hours or overnight.     To bake, preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Remove bread from refrigerator  and spray lightly with non-stick vegetable spray. Bake in preheated oven  for 30-35 minutes or until lightly browned.     Serve with hot maple syrup, powdered sugar and cinnamon.     Yield: 10 servings.
