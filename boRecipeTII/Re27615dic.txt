The Big Thaw Hot-Chocolate Toddy
Buttered toast; cut into, Ground cinnamon, Semisweet chocolate pieces, Granulated sugar, Half and half; or light, milk, Whipped cream, 
In a saucepan, combine the chocolate, sugar and 1/2 cup of the milk. Cook  and stir over medium heat until the mixture comes to a boil. Stir in the  remaining milk and cream. Heat through, but do not boil. Remove from the  heat.  If you like, beat with a rotary beater until frothy. Serve hot in  mugs.  Top each with whipped cream and a dash of cinnamon. Serve with  buttered toast for dunking.    Recipe From:  Jym Ganahl, weathercaster, WCMH-TV (channel 4), Columbus,  Ohio.  Penny Halsey (ATBN65B).
