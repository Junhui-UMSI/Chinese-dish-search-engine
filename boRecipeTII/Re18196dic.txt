Venison Chops and Rice
rice, Chicken broth, Venison Chops, onion, tomato, 
Brown chops on both sides.  Grease casserole dish well. Put 1 tb rice for  each chop in bottom of dish.   Place chops on rice.  Put slice of onion, tomato & pepper on each chop.  Cover with chicken broth.  Bake 1 hr. at 350 degrees. Submitted By  RONAMIT@NETVISION.NET.IL (RON AMIT)  On   9 JUL 1995 125532 ~0400
