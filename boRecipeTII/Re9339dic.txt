Puffy Baked Oreo Dory
Thinly sliced onion, Mayonnaise, Lemon juice, Oreo Dory fillets, Grated Parmesan, 
With paper towels, pat fillets dry. Place fish in shallow baking pan,  not overlapping; brush with lemon juice. Arrange onion on top of  fish, dividing evenly. In bowl blend mayo & cheese; spread over fish,  dividing evenly. Cover with foil & bake at 425 for 5 minutes. Remove  foil & preheat broiler. Broil 4" from heat for 3 minutes until puffy  & golden. Serve immediately. **Orange roughy, hoki or other firm fish  may be used.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
