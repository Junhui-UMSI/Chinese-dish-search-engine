Veggamole
Garlic powder, Tomato, diced, Lemon juice, (1 1/2 cups) broccoil stems, (4-ozs.) green chilies,, Ground cumin, Chopped sweet onion, 
Yields 2 cups    Here is a low-fat alternative to traditional guacamole    Over high heat, bring pot of water to a boil.  Add broccoli; cook until  tender 6 to 7 min. and drain, or microwave, covered, for 4 min. until  tender.  In a food processor, combine broccoli, lemon juice, cumin, and  garlic powder.  Process until smooth.  Transfer to a bowl; stir in tomato,  green chilies, and onion.  Chill until ready to serve.  Makes 2 cups    Posted to Recipe Archive - 10 November 96    Date: Sun, 10 Nov 96 12:23:20 EST    submitted by: gldschlgr@arn.net
