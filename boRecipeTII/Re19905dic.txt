Apple Blossom Cake
Vegetable oil, Cloves, Vanilla, Eggs, Baking soda, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Sugar, Chopped apples; unpeeled, Salt, 
Mix all ingredients except apples with mixer. Fold in apples. Bake in Bundt  pan at 350 for 1 hour 15 minutes. Grease & flour pan very well so apples  wont stick.    MRS CHARLES (PAT) COLKET    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
