Texas True Barbecue Sauce
Thyme, Kosher salt, Mustard; preferably creole, Onion; minced, TABASCO pepper sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Unsulphered molasses, Crushed red pepper, Fresh Lemon Juice, Garlic, Ketchup, Malt vinegar, 
Combine all of the ingredients in a medium nonreactive saucepan and simmer  over low heat for    10 minutes to blend the flavors. Remove from the heat and let cool.    Make ahead: The sauce can be refrigerated for up to 1 week.
