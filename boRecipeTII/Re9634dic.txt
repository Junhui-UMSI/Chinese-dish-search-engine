Pumpkin Log
Sugar, Pumpkin, Eggs, Margarine, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Filling:, Flour, Cream cheese, Vanilla, Powdered sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine eggs, sugar, pumpkin, baking soda,  cinnamon, and flour.  Spread onto greased cookie sheet.  Bake for 15  minutes.  Turn onto a towel which has been sprinkled well with powdered  sugar.  Roll up jelly roll fashion. Let cool completely.   Meanwhile  combine filling ingredients.  When cool unroll and spread on filling.  Reroll and refrigerate.    Note:  If desired, sprinkly pecan pieces over spread filling before  rerolling.  Yummy!
