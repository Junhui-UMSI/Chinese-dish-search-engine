Turkey and Leek Casserole
Margarine or butter, All-purpose flour, Chicken broth, Finely chopped fully cooked, Ounces), Shredded swiss cheese -- (4, Leeks or, Jars (2 ounces) diced pimien, Onions -- sliced, Chicken, Pepper, Hot cooked noodles, Salt, Smoked ham, Drained, milk, Ground nutmeg, Cut-up cooked turkey or --, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Heat margarine in 3-quart saucepan over medium  heat. Cook leeks in margarine about 7 minutes, stirring occasionally until  softened. Stir in flour, salt, nutmeg and pepper. Cook over medium heat,  stirring constantly, until bubbly; remove from heat. Stir in broth and  milk. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. Stir in  turkey, ham and pimiento.Spread about half of the turkey mixture in  ungreased square pan, 9 X 9 X 2 inches, or 2-1/2-quart casserole. Spread  noodles over turkey mixture.Top with remaining turkey mixture. Sprinkle  with cheese. Bake uncovered 25 to 30 minutes or until cheese is light  brown.  8 SERVINGS (ABOUT 1 CUP EACH); 315 CALORIES PER SERVING.    Recipe By     :
