Zucchini Mandarin
Nutmeg, Slivered almonds, toasted, Canned mandarine oranges, Zucchini, 
Cut the zucchini into slices.  Steam until tender.   Add the orange slices and the nutmeg.  Sprinkle with the almonds just  before serving.  From the ADA Holiday Cookbook by Dr. Betty Wedman 1  serving = 1/2 fruit exchange + 1/2 fat exchange = 60 Cal, 8 CHO, 2 PRO, 3  Fat, 1 Na, 239 K, 0 Cholest
