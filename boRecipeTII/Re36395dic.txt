Beer Batter Fried Trout
beer, Cayenne pepper, Trout, All-purpose flour, Tartar sauce, 
Mix flour, beer & cayenne in blender until smooth & consistency of thick  pancake batter. Refrigerate batter about 1 to 1-1/2 hours. Dip filets in  batter & drop into hot, deep fat (350 F.) & deep fry.  Remove from fat with  slotted spoon & drain on paper towels. Serve with CONFEDERATE TARTAR SAUCE.
