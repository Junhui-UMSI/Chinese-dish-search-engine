"Tex-Mex" Mac n Cheese
Macaroni and cheese, Bell pepper, Ground beef, Water, Onion, Mushroom, Tomato paste, Salt, pepper, Whole kernel corn, canned, 
In large skillet, brown the ground beef or turkey with the chopped pepper,  onion and mushrooms.  Stir in the tomato paste and water and corn. Cook the  macaroni and cheese according to package directions, adding milk and  margarine if required.  Add the macaroni and cheese to the beef mixture.  Mix well and serve.
