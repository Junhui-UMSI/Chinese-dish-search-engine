Strawberry Cake Supreme
Strawberries, Strawberry gelatin powder, Eggs, White cake mix, Vegetable oil, Jello, Water, Drained, 
1. Grease and flour tube pan. 2. Chop strawberries and drain over bowl to  save juice. 3. Mix cake mix with Jello, oil, water, strawberries, and eggs.  Beat for 4 minutes total while adding eggs one at a time. 4. Pour into tube  pan, bake in preheated oven at 350 degrees for 40-50 minutes. DO NOT open  oven door while this is baking or cake will fall.    ICING: Combine 1 stick margarine, softened, 1 box confectioners sugar and  juice from drained strawberries. Mix; add a liquid of your choice if not  enough juice from berries. Spread frosting on cooled cake.
