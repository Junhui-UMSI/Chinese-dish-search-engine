Teething Cookies
Vanilla, Sugar, Eggs, flour, 
Beat eggs and add sugar, vanilla and flour and mix well. Roll dough between  2 sheets of floured wax paper to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut desired shapes and  place on cookie sheet, lightly greased. Let stand overnight 10-12 hours,  then bake at 325 degrees for 20 minutes. These are hard and dissolve, but  dont break up.
