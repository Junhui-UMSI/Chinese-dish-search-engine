Toasted Zucchini Snacks
Salt, Snack rye bread, Plain yogurt, green pepper, Green onions, Hot pepper sauce, Parmesan cheese, zucchini, Mayo, Garlic, Worcestershire Sauce, 
In a bowl,toss the zucchini and salt;let stand for 1 hr.Rinse and  drain;pressing out excess liquid.Add the next 8 ingrediants;stir until  combined.Spread a rounded tsp on each slice of bread;place on a baking  sheet.375 for 10-12 minutes or until bubbly.
