Pot Roast of Boar
Water, Carrots, sliced, Sliced celery, Dry sage, Onions, sliced, Boar shoulder roast, Salted water to cover, Apple cider, 
Simmer meat in salted water to cover for 1 1/2 hours.  Drain and return to  kettle with remaining ingredients.  Cover and simmer until tender.  Slice  meat and arrange on hot platter to keep warm. Make gravy from cooking  liquid. Serve with buttered noodles.
