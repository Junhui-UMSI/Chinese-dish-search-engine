Baked Pork Chops
Butter, Pepper, Brown sugar, Pork Chops, Tomato sauce, Lemon, sliced, Salt, 
Brown chops with melted butter in large skillet. Place them in greased  baking dish.  Place a thin slice of lemon on each. Sprinkle with salt and  pepper. Spread brown sugar over chops, then tomato sauce. Cover and bake 1      1/4    hours at 350 deg.
