Artichoke Pie
artichoke hearts, Butter, fresh garlic, Onion, Grated Parmesan cheese, Whipping cream, Eggs, Mozzarella cheese, salt and pepper, Unbaked pastry shell, 
Mince garlic and thinly slice onion.  Saute in butter until soft and golden  (do not brown).  Beat eggs and add cream, cheeses, salt and pepper, and  garlic-onion mixture. Drain and cut artichokes in quarters. Add to egg  mixture and gently pour into pastry shell. Bake in hot oven (400 degrees)  about 45 minutes until set. Serve warm or cold, as entree or appetizer.  Makes 1 9-inch pie.
