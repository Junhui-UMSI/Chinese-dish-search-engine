Plain Muffins
Melted shortening, Flour, milk, Sugar, Baking Powder, Egg, well beaten, Salt, 
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt. Combine egg,  shortening, milk, and sugar.  Add dry ingredients. Beat only until smooth.  Fill well-oiled muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake in hot oven (425? F) 15-20  minutes.  10 servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941
