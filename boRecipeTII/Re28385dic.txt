Baked Lemon Pudding
Lemon rind; grated, -Salt, Eggs, milk, Flour; all purpose, Sugar;granulated, Lemon juice, Butter, 
Preheat oven to 350F. In mixing bowl, combine sugar, flour and salt. Stir  in lemon juice rind, beaten eggs yolks, butter and milk. Beat egg whites  till stiff but not dry; fold into lemon mixture. Pour into buttered 6 cup  casserole. Place in larger pan, pour in hot water to about 1" depth. Bake  in 350F oven for 40 minutes or till topping is golden brown. Serve warm.
