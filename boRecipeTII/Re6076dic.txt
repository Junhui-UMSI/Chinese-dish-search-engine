Pizza Dippers (Not Bad)
Grated Parmesan cheese, Shortening, Salt, Italian sausage, Cold water, Flour, 
Brown sausage, crumbling, and drain.  Combine flour, cheese, salt. Cut in  shortening until mixture resembles peas. Stir in the sausage. Add water a  bit at a time until the mixture can be shaped into balls. Bake 10-12  minutes @ 450~.  Serve with toothpicks and PIZZA SAUCE.
