Printemps Chowder
Raw potatoes; cut in, Finely sliced green onions;, Butter, Shelled new peas; (1 pound), Flour, Water, Quartered; thinly sliced, milk, Chopped parsley, Salt, 
Cook peas and onions in water uncovered for 10 minutes. Add potatoes and  salt. Continue cooking 6 to 8 minutes. Blend butter and flour over low  heat, add milk slowly, stirring constantly. Combine with peas, potatoes and  their liquid. SIMMER 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add radishes and  parsley just before serving. 4 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 346 by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I  Canty) on Dec 7, 1997
