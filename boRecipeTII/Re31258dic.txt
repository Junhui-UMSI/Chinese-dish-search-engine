Banana-Sweet Potato Casserole
Egg, Mashed bananas, Sweet Potato; cooked and, Salt, Sour cream, Curry powder, 
Mix all ingredients. Beat until smooth; put into greased 1 quart casserole  dish and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Feb 16, 1998
