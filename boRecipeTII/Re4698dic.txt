Phillipine Pork Inihaw
Pork chops, Hot pepper sauce, White wine vinegar, Sugar, Salt, Soy sauce, Garlic, 
Trim the pork of excess fat and wipe clean. Arrange in one layer in glass  or ceramic dish.    Combine 2 cups vinegar, 4 tablespoons garlic, sugar, salt and soy sauce and  mix well. Pour over the pork, cover tightly, and marinate overnight in the  refigerator. Bring to room temperature before grilling.    Drain pork, reserving marinade. Grill over hot coals, browning all sides  and basting as frequently as you can with the marinade; do not char.    Combine remaining 1 cup vinegar, 1 tablespoon garlic and hot pepper sauce.  Serve as a dip.
