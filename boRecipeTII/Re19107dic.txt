Vegetable Dip (Puerto Rico)
Seasoned salt, Garlic powder, Sour cream, Parsley Flakes, Hellmans Mayoniasse, Dill Seed, 
Mix all ingredients and chill. Best made day ahead. Serve with raw  vegetables: celery, carrots, cucumbers, bell peppers, califlower, etc.  (Bite size pieces)    Recipe by: PEGGY MERCHANT Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #734 by Dianne  Waller <dwaller@frontier.gulf.net> on Aug 10, 1997
