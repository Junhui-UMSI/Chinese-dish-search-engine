Aubergine and Cashews
Garam masala, Cayenne, Salt and pepper, Tomatoes  , Aubergine, Cream, light, Penne, Coriander, ground, Mushrooms, Cashews, Cumin, Turmeric, 
Chop aubergine into 1 and 1/2 cm rings then into 8 or 4 depending on size  of auburgine. Heat oil in a large frying pan. Chop mushrooms. Fry aubergine  until just soft. Add mushrooms and cashews, continue to fry until mushroms  just soften.    Add spices, stir thoroughly. Chop tomatoes and add to mix. Add a bit of  tomato puree. Stir well. Drain cooked pasta. Add cream to mix until its a  reasonable consistency. Stir well. Add pasta, mix together until thoroughly  coated. Serve with green salad.
