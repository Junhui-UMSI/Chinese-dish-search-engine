Pumpkin Frosted Dream Cake
Pumpkin; canned, Spice cake mix, Skim milk, FROSTING, Env Dream Whip whipped, BATTER, PUMPKIN FROSTING, Env Dream Whip whipped, Whole egg whites; slightly, Fat-free butterscotch, Env Dream Whip whipped, Skim milk, Walnuts, olive oil, 
Preheat oven to 350. Prepare two 9" cake pans with cooking spray and flour;  set aside. In a mixing bowl, combine cake mix, one envelope whipped topping  mix, one cup milk, egg whites, and oil. Mix until moistened. Pour evenly  into prepared pans. Bake for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, to prepare pumpkin  frosting, combine one envelope whipped topping mix, pudding mix, and  remaining milk. Mix slowly until well blended. Then, beat again for eight  minutes or until soft peaks form. Fold in pumpkin in carefully. Split  cooled cake layers and frost each layer with pumpkin frosting. Stack layers  on serving plate. Prepare remaining envelope whipped topping mix according  to package directions. Frost sides of cake. Sprinkle walnuts over top.
