Tomato Bowl
Pepper, Vinegar, cider, Parsley, fresh; chopped, Sugar, Mustard, prepared, Oil, vegetable, Salt, Tomatoes; firm, ripe, sliced, Garlic, 
Combine the first 8 ingredients, mixing well.    Place tomato slices in serving bowl, and pour dressing over all.  Cover and let stand at room temperature 20 minutes before serving.
