Quick Vegetable Beef Stew
(1-lb) Green Giant American, Water, Flour, (1.5-oz) beef stew seasoning, Cooked roast beef; 1-lb,, 
In medium saucepan, combine seasoning mix and flour; stir in water. Bring  to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring frequently. Add frozen vegetables  and beef; return to a boil. Reduce heat to medium; cover and simmer 5-7  minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender.
