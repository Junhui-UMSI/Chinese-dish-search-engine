Pizza with Rice Crust
Black Pepper, freshly ground, Green or Red Pepper, Tomato sauce, Onion, small, sliced, Egg, Vegetable oil, Mushrooms, sliced, Part-skim Mozzarella, Basil, dried, Oregano, dried, Tabasco sauce, White Rice, cooked, Seed the green or red pepper, Garlic, minced, 
Servings:  4    In a large frypan, heat oil over medium-high heat and stir-fry onion,  garlic, mushrooms and peppers until crisp-tender, about 3 - 4 minutes. Set  aside. In a bowl, combine rice, egg and 1/2 cup grated cheese. Press evenly  into an oiled 12 - inch pizza pan. Bake in an oven preheated to 400 degrees  for Sprinkle rice curst with half the remaining cheese. Stir together  tomato sauce, basil, oregano, pepper and Tabasco. Spread tomato sauce over,  then top with vegetables. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese. Bake  for about 12 - 14 minutes or until cheese is bubbly. Let rest for 5 minutes  before cutting. Serves 4.    From The Gazette, 91/05/22.
