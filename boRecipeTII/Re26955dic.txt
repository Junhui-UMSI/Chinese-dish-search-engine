Baked Catfish Parmesan #1
Skinless catfish fillets, Paprika, Dried Oregano, Dried basil, Dry bread crumbs, Grated Parmesan cheese, milk, Pepper, Vegetable oil, Chopped parsley, 
Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese and seasonings. Dip fillets in milk  and then roll in crumb mixture.     Spray baking pan with nonstick coating. Arrange fish in pan. Drizzle oil  over fish.     Bake at 450 degrees about 8-10 minutes or just until fish flakes easily.  Yields 4 servings, 231 calories each.
