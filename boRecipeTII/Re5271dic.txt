Pina Colada Dessert Squares
Cool Whip?; thawed, (8 oz.) cream cheese;, Margarine; melted, (8 oz.) coco goya cream of, Rum flavoring, Maraschino cherries; chopped, Vanilla wafer crumbs, (8 oz.) crushed pineapple;, Pecans, Flaked coconut; toasted, 
Combine crumbs, margarine, press into bottom of 9 x 13 inch pan. Gradually  add cream of coconut to cream cheese, mixing at medium speed on electric  mixer until blended. Stir in rum flavoring. Fold in whipped topping,  pineapple, cherries and nuts. Spread over crust. Sprinkle with toasted  coconut. Chill several hours or overnight.
