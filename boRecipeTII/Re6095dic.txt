Pizza Dough #08
Active dry yeast, olive oil, Bread flour, Salt, Granulated sugar, Water, 
Date: Wed, 20 Mar 1996 20:01:59 -0800    From: Francine <grenier@ncia.com>  Dough Program: Press SELECT for Dough.  Press START.  Dough will be ready  in 1 hour and 20 minutes. At the end of the program, press STOP/RESET.  Dough is ready for hand-shaping, rising and baking.    Baking: Place dough on a lightly floured surface.  Cover dough with a clean  cloth, let dough rest for 10 min.  Cut into halves.  Press each evenly into  a lightly greased 12-in pizza pan. Prick dough allover with fork. Bake in  400 degree preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, until crust is lightly  browned. Remove from oven and spread/sprinkle crust with your favorite  pizza toppings. Return to oven for 10-15 min, until toppings are bubbling  and/or melted.    Variations:    1. Substitute 1/2 to 1 c whole wheat flour for same amount of bread flour.    2. Add 1 T crushed herbs(oregano, italian spices, etc) to ingredients just  before adding oil.    3. Add 1c grated or shredded cheese(parmesan, romano, cheddar, pepper jack)  to ingredients just before adding oil.
