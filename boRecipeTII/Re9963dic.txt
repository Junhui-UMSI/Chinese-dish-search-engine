Pureed Turnips with Garlic
fresh garlic, Butter or margarine, Turnips, Salt and pepper, 
Parboil turnips 7 minutes in salted water.  Drain.  Cook turnips and  garlic gently in 8 tablespoons butter until both are soft.  Puree in food  processor or by hand, adding the remaining 4 tablespoons of butter during  process.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.
