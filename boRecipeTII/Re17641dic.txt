Almond Meringues
Sugar, almonds, Egg whites, 
Beat egg whites very well.  Add sugar gradually; beat thoroughly to form stiff meringue.  Add ground almonds.  Place teaspoonfulls on greased pan. Bake at 250 deg. F (120 deg. C), till  set.  Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Sally Wallace <scav@avsia.com> on Apr 05,  1998
