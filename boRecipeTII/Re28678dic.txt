Texas Orange Cake
Mandarin oranges with juice, Crushed pineapple & juice, Yellow cake mix, Coconut, Eggs, Vegetable oil, TOPPING, Half & half, Cool Whip, Vanilla instant pudding, 
Beat eggs, oil and oranges together. Add cake mix. Beat until thick. Bake  in greased and floured 9x13" pan for 35-45 minutes at 350~F. Cool.   Topping: Mix pudding and pineapple; beat until thick. Add half and half;  beat again. Place on cooled cake. Cover with Cool Whip and sprinkle with  coconut. Refrigerate.
