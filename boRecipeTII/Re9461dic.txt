Pumpkin Bread #01
Nutmeg, Flour, Sugar, Pumpkin, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Vegetable oil, Water, Eggs, Allspice, -(up to), Salt, 
Combine in large bowl all dry ingredients. Combine pumpkin, oil and water.  Beat eggs and add to oil mixture. Make a well in flour mixture. Add oil  mixture and stir until well mixed. Pour into 2 greased and floured 9x5 loaf  pans. Bake at 350 for 1 hour. Test with toothpick. Cool 10 minutes before  removing from pan. Cool on rack. Makes 2 loaves.
