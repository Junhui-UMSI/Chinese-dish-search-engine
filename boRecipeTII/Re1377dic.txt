Ediths Sweet Potato Souffle
Mashed cooked sweet potatoes, Eggs, Sugar; (or 1/2 cup), Vanilla, Stick melted butter, TOPPING, Stick margarine, Self rising flour, Brown sugar, Nuts; (pecan-walnuts), 
(8x8 pan)    Mix first part together and pour into pans (sprayed w/Pam).    Top with topping (Mix together with hands until crumbly).    Bake at 350 degree until top is brown and crisp    Ingredients are doubled for a 9x13 pan    Crusty    Posted to bbq-digest by Bill Mathews <billmathews@Daccess.net> on Sep 28,  1998,
