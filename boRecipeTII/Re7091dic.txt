Poppy Seed Dressing for Fruit
Lemon, Onion; grated, Poppy seed, Celery seed, Salt, Vegetable oil, Sugar, 
Mix all ingredients in a blender except the oil. Add oil slowly as you are  blending. Store in refrigerator.    MRS SAMUEL L. (MARY) HAYTON    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
