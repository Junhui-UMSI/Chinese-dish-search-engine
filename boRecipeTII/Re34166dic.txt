Bavarian Noodles with Apples
Thinly sliced apples, Margarine or butter, Dijon mustard, Caraway seed, Honey, (4 oz) uncooked medium, 
Cook noodles per package.  Drain and set aside. Melt margarine in medium  skillet over medium-high heat. Add apples and caraway seed; cook until  apples are crisp-tender, stirring occasionally. (Dont worry if the apples  break up.)  Add cooked noodles, honey and mustard; mix lightly. Cook until  thoroughly heated. Makes 6 servings.
