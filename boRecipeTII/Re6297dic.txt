Plain Pancakes #2
Water, Egg, Best-ever pancake mix (see, Butter, Nutmeg, 
In bowl and using fork, stir together Best-ever Pancake mix, water, egg  and nutmeg until blended. Heat griddle to medium-high heat; brush with  butter. Using 1/4-cup batter for each pancake and brushing griddle with  more butter as necessary, pour batter onto griddle; cook for about 2  minutes or until golden brown on bottom and bubbles break on top but do not  fill in.     Using spatula, turn and cook for about 1 minute or just until bottom is  golden brown. Makes nine 4-inch pancakes.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
