Herb Pesto
Fresh Herbs; * See Note, olive oil, Pine Nuts; Or Walnuts;, salt and pepper, Garlic, Grated Parmesan Cheese;, Water; As Needed, Fresh Thyme; * See Note, 
*Basil, parsley, cilantro, mint, spinach or and combination of 2 or 3.  *Marjoram, oregano or tarragon; optional.    Process until smooth adding water 1 tablespoon at a time. If a chunkier  pesto is desired, omit water.    Makes 3/4 cup.    Carolyn Shaw 2/25/98.
