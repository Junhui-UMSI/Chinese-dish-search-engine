Poppy Seed Chicken #1
Poppy seeds, Chopped parsley, (3-lb) broiler, Buttermilk, Pepper, Salad oil, Salt, Flour, 
Skin fryer & cut into serving-size pieces. In large skillet over medium  heat, brown chicken on all sides in oil. Remove from pan. Stir flour, salt  & pepper into oil in skillet until smooth. Stirring constantly, add  buttermilk, parsley & poppy seeds. Bring to a boil. Return chicken to  skillet & cook covered over low heat for 30 minutes or until chicken is  fork tender. Yields 4 servings.    BETTY BARROW    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
