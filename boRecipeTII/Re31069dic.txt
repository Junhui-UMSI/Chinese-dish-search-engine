Banana Pudding #4
Ripe Bananas; Sliced, Eggs, Butter, Vanilla, Salt, Sugar, Vanilla Wafers, Cornstarch, milk, 
From:    Joel Ehrlich <Joel.Ehrlich@SALATA.COM>    Date:    Tue, 23 Jul 1996 10:37:51 -0800  Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Combine the sugar, cornstarch, milk and  salt in a large saucepan.  Cook, stirring, over medium heat until  thickened. Beat the eggs lightly with a wire whisk. Add a little of the hot  mixture to the eggs. Blend well. Pour the eggs into the mixture in the  saucepan. Cook, stirring, for a few minutes. Add the butter and the  vanilla.  Blend well. Cover the surface of the pudding with plastic wrap.  Cool slightly.  Place a layer of vanilla wafers on the bottom of a (2 Qt.)  casserole.  Alternate additional layers of vanilla wafers with layers of  banana slices and pudding, ending with pudding as the top layer. Bake until  set (about 10 minutes).    EAT-L Digest 23 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
