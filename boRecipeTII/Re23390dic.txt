Tunisian Five-Spice Powder
Cloves, Freshly grated nutmeg, Black peppercorns, Ground cinnamon, Malagueta pepper, 
Grind together the cloves, peppercorns, and malagueta pepper. Add the  nutmeg and cinnamon and blend well. Store in an airtight container. Use to  flavor lamb and vegetable dishes.
