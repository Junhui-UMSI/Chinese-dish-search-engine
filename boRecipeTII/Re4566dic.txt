Petite Peas with Thyme and Garlic
olive oil, Peas, petite, frozen, Romaine lettuce, Thyme,dried, Pepper, Seasoned salt, Garlic, 
1. Heat oil, garlic and peas in a skillet. Add thyme and lettuce.   2. Mix well, then cover and steam over low heat for 5 minutes. Peas should  be bright but tender and lettuce should be wilted. Add seasoned salt and  pepper to taste.    Miscellaneous recipes from the collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mo-misc.zip
