Garlic Coleslaw
Cabbage, fresh garlic, Plain yogurt, Sour cream, Rice vinegar, Salt, Sugar, Fresh ground black pepper, 
Shred cabbage, press garlic, (quantity to taste), add other ingredients in  stainless or ceramic bowl.  Toss until well mixed. All quantities variable  so its best to start with small amounts and add to taste. Better on second  day if stored cold and well sealed From: Fred Towner Date: 06-21-96 Recipes
