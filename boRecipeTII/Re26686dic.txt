Baileys New York Egg Cream
Chocolate or vanilla syrup, Bottle seltzer water or club, milk, 
In a soda glass, stir together the Baileys, milk, and syrup until well  blended. Add the seltzer water all at once, which will produce 2-inch foamy  head. Serve immediately. Makes 1 serving. MasterCook formatted by Ethel  Snyder    NOTES : This recipe is a modern version of the 1930s New York soda  fountain classic.
