Barbecued Meat Patties Cathy Light
Ground meat, Hot pepper sauce., Tomato ketchup, Soy sauce, A, Half-half, Cornstarch, C, Water and, Egg, SNAPPETIZERSY BASTING S, Ketchup,, Onion, Worcestshire sauce and a, Finely chopped green pepper, Brown sugar,, Lemon juice., 
Stir to blend well. Add dash salt and black pepper. In a large mixing  bowl, combine ground meat, egg and onion. Add green pepper, soy  sauce, and ketchup. Mix half and half and cornstarch. Add to meat  mixture, mixing to blend well. Form portions of meat to even, rather  thick meat patties. Place patties on preheated barbecue grill. Broil  3 to 4 inches from source of heat, basting with sauce and turning  patties over from time to time for even browning. Grill 12-15 minutes  total or to your liking- time depends on how well you like your meat  done. Serve meat patties on hamburger buns, if you like, or with  French Fries.
