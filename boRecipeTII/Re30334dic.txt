Banana Cocktail
Bananas; sliced, Grand manier liquer or, Papaya puree, Vanilla essence, Any other orange liqueur, Orange juice, Fruit cocktail (without the, Fresh cream, 
Mix fruit cocktail and banana slices. Spoon into six cocktail glasses and  pour sauce over, decorate with cherries and mint leaves. Beat together the  rest of the ingredients (a smooth sauce is obtained when papaya puree is  prepared in a blender.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Turbana    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 250 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 12, 1997
