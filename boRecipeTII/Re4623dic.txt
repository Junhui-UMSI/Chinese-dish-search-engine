Phanang Curry Paste (Nam Prik Kaeng Phanang)
Shallots, chopped, Pepper corns, Coriander seeds, Galangal, Coriander roots, Salt, Dried red chilies, Stalks lemon grass, chopped, Cumin seeds, Garlic, Shrimp paste, 
Coarsely chop the chilies and soak in water for 10 minutes.  Drain.  Pound together all the ingredients to produce a fine paste.    From "Discover Thai Cooking" by Chaslin, Canungmai and Tettoni, Times  Editions, Singapore.  1987    Posted by Stephen Ceideburg April 18 1990.
