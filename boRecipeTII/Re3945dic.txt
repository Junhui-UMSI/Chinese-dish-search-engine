Peppers Tuna Casserole
Light sandwich cheese;, Onion; diced, Pepper; to taste, Water-packed tuna;, Light cream ofmushroom soup;, Salt, Egg noodles;, 
Cook noodles; drain.  Mix in all ingredients and top with the cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes to 1 hour.    Nutrients per serving:  Calories 72, fat 3g, cholesterol 5mg,  carbohydrate 3g, sodium 146mg. Exchanges: Meat 1.    Source:  "There IS Life after Lettuce" by Pepper Durcholz, Alberta  Gentry, Carolyn Williamson, M.S.    Formatted for Meal-Master by Joyce Burton.
