Peppy Tomato Dressing
Salt, Salad oil, Tomatoes; Drained, 1 Can, Onion Slice, Pepper, Celery seed, White wine vinegar, Garlic, 
Chop the tomatoes and add to the rest of the ingredients in a blender  container, blending until all of the ingredients are well blended.    Makes 1 3/4 cups.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/blend1.zip
