Carrot and Raisin Salad
Carrots, Raisins, Salt, Lemon juice, Sour cream; mayonnaise or, Chopped pecans; almonds or, 
Clean carrots, cover with ice in a bowl and refrigerate for 1/2 hour. Grate  carrots into a bowl and add raisins, (nuts if desired) lemon juice and sour  cream. Mix well and serve on a lettuce leaf.    N: If using sour cream or yogurt, you may prefer a sprinkling of sugar into  the mix.    Recipe by Mangia!
