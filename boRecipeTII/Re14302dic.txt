White-Cooked Whole Pork
Pork, Salt, Fresh ginger root, Water to cover, 
1. Place pork in a heavy pan with water and bring to a boil. Add salt  and ginger root; then simmer, covered, until tender (about 1-1/2 hours).  Drain, reserving liquid for stock.       2. Let pork cool, then refrigerate to chill. Slice thin and serve cold  with any dip for plain-cooked pork (see recipes "Soy-Sesame Dip for Pork",  "Soy-Mustard Dip for Pork", "Chile-Soy Dip for Pork", or "Hoisin Dip for  Pork").
