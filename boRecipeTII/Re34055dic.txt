Basler Mehlsuppe / Basler Flour Soup
Beef bouillon, Red wine, Sprinz (or other swiss, Onion; cut in strips, Flour, white, Salt, pepper; to taste, Margarine, 
STEP ONE:    ~ cut onion in fine strips - roast flour in a pan in the oven by great heat  until dark brown - cold dwon flour    STEP TWO:    ~ roast onion and margarine - stir in flour, mix well - cold down a little  bit - stir in consomme (or bouillon) , stir well - add red wine, boil for  one hour - carefully puree the soup in a blender at low speed until very  smooth - return to the pot and adjust the salt and pepper - serve with  shredded Sprinz  NOTES : Swiss national speciality
