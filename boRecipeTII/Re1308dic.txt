Don Martins Pork Red Sauce
Crushed tomatoes, Onion; minced, Honey, Molasses, Amber cider vinegar, Ketchup, Garlic, Lemon juice, Worcestershire Sauce, Franks Louisiana hot sauce, Chili powder, Powdered mustard, Salt, 
Bring to boil, than simmer 1 hour. Refrigerate until used. I heat it up  before I mix it into the pork.    Recipe by: BBQ List - Don Martin doncdt@tiac.net    Posted to bbq-digest by PhantomBBQ@aol.com on Aug 26, 1998, converted by  MM_Buster v2.0l.
