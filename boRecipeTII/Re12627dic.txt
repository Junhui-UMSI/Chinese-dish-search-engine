Yogurt Ice
Mixed fruit, chopped, Plain yogurt, 16 ounces, Sugar, 
Put the fruit into a blender and puree. Mix with the yogurt and sugar and  put into freezer. Stir every 15 to 20 minutes to ensure that it sets smooth  and creamy and the fruit is evenly distributed. Do not freeze solid.
