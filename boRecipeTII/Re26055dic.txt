Tofu Loaf
Walnuts, Sage, Tofu, Garlic, Rolled oats, Onion, Peanut butter, Tamari, 
Place all the ingredients in a large bowl. Mix well, using your hands if  necessary.     Press the mixture into a well-oiled loaf pan.     Run a knife around the edges of the pan and unmold th eloaf onto a serving  platter. Surround the platter with colorful ssteamed vegetables and serve  with a sauce.     From the files of DEEANNE
