Best Baked Beans
Bacon; crispy fried and, Onions, Baked beans; drained, Prepared mustard, Green Pepper, Ketchup, Hickory-smoke barbecue sauce, Brown sugar, 
Mix all ingredients in crockpot. Cover and cook on LOW setting for 8 to 12  hours. {Or on HIGH setting for 3-4 hours.}    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by IceTeaLuvr@aol.com on Aug 21, 1998,
