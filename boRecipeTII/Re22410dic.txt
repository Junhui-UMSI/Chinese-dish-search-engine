Turtle and Ham Soup
Onion, Garlic, Parsley, Lemon, piece, Thyme, Salt and pepper to taste, Turtle meat, Flour, Cloves, Bay leaf, Ham, small piece, 
Cut ham into bits; mash herbs and seasonings with it, and put them aside.  Boil turtle meat 15 minutes. Remove from heat and save the stock. Chop up  the meat. Brown onions in lard or vegetable oil; add turtle meat and brown  well. Then add ham and seasonings, stirring constantly. Add stock and 2-1/2  to 3 quarts water with salt and various peppers (to taste) and also the  lemon, chopped very fine. Cook for an hour or so stirring frequently. Types  of turtle: Softshell, snapper (Loggerhead or Alligator) or Mobilian. Also  for: Substitute Alligator meat for turtle. Recipe date: 11/30/87
