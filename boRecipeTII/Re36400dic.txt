Beer Batter Softshell Crabs
Beer, Flour, Paprika, Crabs, Salt, Baking Powder, 
Pour beer in mixing bowl, add flour then remainder of ingredients. Mix  well. Prepare batter at least 1-1/2 hours before use as it will thicken  standing. Dust crabs lightly in flour; dip singly in batter. Deep-fry at  360 degrees for 2-5 minutes depending on size. Crabs should be golden  brown. Drain and serve.
