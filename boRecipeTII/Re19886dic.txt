Apple and Sweet Potatoes
Sweet potatoes, Butter, Apple juice, Salt, Pepper, Apple, Clove, Nutmeg, 
Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes Total Time: 1 hour Oven Temp: 350F / 180C / Gas Mark 4  Place sweet potatoes in a saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a  boil and cook 30 to 40 minutes or until tender.  Remove from water,  peel and mash.  Add butter, apple juice, salt and pepper.  Stir in  apple, cloves and dash of nutmeg.  Place in an ovenproof serving dish  and warm in 350 degrees oven to minutes before serving.
