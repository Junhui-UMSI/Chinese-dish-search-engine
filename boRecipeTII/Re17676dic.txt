Almond Poppy Seed Bread
Honey; Or Maple Syrup, Yeast, Salt, Almond extract, Baking soda, Whole wheat flour, Vital Gluten; To 3 Tbs,, Poppy seeds, Buttermilk, Almonds; chopped,
