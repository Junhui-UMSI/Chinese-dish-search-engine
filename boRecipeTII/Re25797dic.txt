Baby Carrots and Onions in Cream
Cream, Pinch nutmeg, Small white onions, Baby carrots, Salt and pepper, 
Wash and trim carrots.  Peel onions.  Place in pot with 1/2 inch boiling  salted water.  Cover and simmer gently for 10 minutes.  Remove lid and boil  rapidly, shaking pot to prevent burning, until water is absorbed. Stir in  cream and add pepper and salt to taste, if necessary.  Serve with a very  light sprinkling of nutmeg.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdjaxxx.zip
