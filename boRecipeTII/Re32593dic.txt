Sweet Mustard Dip
Low sodium soy sauce, Hot mustard, Honey, Water, Rice wine vinegar, 
Combine the ingredients in a sauce pan over low heat. Cook for 3 to 4  minutes to blend the flavors. Remove from the heat, let cool for several  minutes until just warm and serve.    Recipe by: Michael Lomonaco    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on May 10,  1998
