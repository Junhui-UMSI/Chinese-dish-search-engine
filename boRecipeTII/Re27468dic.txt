Toffee Popcorn
Water, Pecans or dry roasted nuts, Popped corn, White corn syrup, Cubes butter, 
Melt 2 cubes butter in saucepan. Add: 1 cup white corn syrup and 2 Tbls.  water. Boil until the candy changes color (about 10-15 minutes). Add 3/4  cup pecans or dry roasted nuts. Pour over popped corn and stir until  completely covered. Spread out on a greased cookie sheet. Cool. Break apart  and ENJOY!
