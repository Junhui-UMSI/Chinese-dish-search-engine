Watergate Cake (Pistachio)
Pistachio pudding instant, cool whip, cherries, Pistachio pudding instant, ICING, Milk, Add:, Club soda, Cooking oil, Eggs, Pecans, cherries, Pecans, White cake mix, 
Cake:  stir together dry cake and pudding mix in large mixing bowl. Mix  together oil, eggs and ale. Add to dry ingredients and stir until well  blended. fold in pecans and cherries. Pour into 2 greased and floured round  cake pans.  Bake at 350F for 30-35 minutes. slice layers into 4 layers  after they cool by wrapping thread around middle of layer, cross and pull  through to cut. Icing: Spread icing on bottom layer, adding each additinal  layer and spreading icing until all 4 layers are iced.
