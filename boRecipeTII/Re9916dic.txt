Puppy Chow Mix (Fun Snack for Kids)
Crispix cereal, Butter, Powdered sugar, Chocolate chips, Peanut butter, 
Put 3 cups powdered sugar in large bowl with a lid or in a paper bag. Set aside.  Melt chocolate and butter.  Add peanut butter to the chocolate mixture and stir together.  Take off of heat and add cereal. Mix all together.  Put chocolate and cereal mix into the powdered sugar and shake.
