Stuffed Artichoke Hearts
Drained, artichoke hearts, Paprika, garlic, Salt and pepper to taste, Italian salad dressing, Grated Parmesan cheese, Italian bread crumbs, olive oil, Lemon juice, 
Remove the pulp from the center of the artichoke hearts & cut each in  half. In a bowl combine the bread crumbs, garlic, salt, pepper, &  cheese. Moisten the mixture with olive oil, salad dressing, & lemon  juice. Stuff the artichoke hearts with the mixture. Place the hearts  in a greased baking dish & sprinkle them with paprika. Bake the dish  at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
