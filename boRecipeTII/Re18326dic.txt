Vermont Baked Beans with Ham
Baked ham, Salt, Water, Brown sugar, Prepared mustard, Dried baby lima beans, Maple-flavored syrup, Pepper, 
Soak beans in water overnight in slow-cooking pot.  Cover and cook on high  for 2 to 3 hours or until tender but not mushy.  Drain, saving liquid.  Return beans to slow-cooking pot.  Stir in ham, mustard, brown sugar, maple  syrup, salt, pepper, and 1/2 cup liquid from beans.  Cover and cook on low  for 8 to 10 hours.  If using ham hocks, cut ham off bones.
