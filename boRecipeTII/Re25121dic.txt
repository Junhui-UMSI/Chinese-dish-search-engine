Tomato-Basil-Mozzarella Salad
olive oil, Lemon juice, Tomatoes; cut in 1/2" slices, Garnish: fresh basil sprigs, Salt, Fresh mozzarella cheese; cut, Pepper, 
Arrange tomato and cheese alternately on individual serving plates,  overlapping edges.    Combine olive oil and lemon juice; brush over top. Sprinkle with salt and  pepper; cover and chill.    Garnish, if desired. Makes 4 to 6 servings.    Recipe by: Southern Living    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1047 by Suzy Wert <SuzyWert@aol.com> on Jan  27, 1998
