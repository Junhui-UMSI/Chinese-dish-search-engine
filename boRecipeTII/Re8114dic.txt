Potato Casserole #2
Butter, Salt, Cheddar Cheese, Pepper, Potatoes; peeled, sliced and, Sour cream; at room, Green Onions, Butter, 
While potatoes are still quite warm, mix lightly with cheese and melted  butter. Mix onions and sour cream together and add to potatoes. Add salt  and pepper. Place mixture in a 9x13 pan and dot with butter. Bake at 325?  for about 20 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.    JO ANN DREW    (MRS. TOMMY, JR.)
