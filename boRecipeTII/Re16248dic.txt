Watercress Sandwiches with Chili-Lemon Butter
Whole wheat bread or, Other whole grain bread;, Fresh Lemon Juice, Jalapeno pepper; minced, Lemon peel; finely grated, Watercress sprigs; tough, Butter; room temperature, 
Mix first 4 ingredients (butter thru peel) in small bowl until well  blended. Season with salt and pepper.   Spread chili-lemon butter on 1 side of each bread slice, dividing equally.  Top buttered side of 4 bread slices generously with watercress. Cover with  remaining 4 bread slices, buttered side down, pressing to adhere. Cut  sandwiches in half and serve immediately.
