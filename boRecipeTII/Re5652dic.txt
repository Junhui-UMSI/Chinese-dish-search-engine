Pineapple Upside-Down Cake
Egg, Pineapple juice, Almond extract, Baking Powder, Salt, Vegetable oil, Vanilla extract, Flour, Sugar, Maraschino Cherries cut 1/2, Pinnapple slices, 16 oz size, Firmly packed Lt Brown Sugar, Margarine, 
1.  Cut 2 circles of waxed paper to fit bottom of microwaveable 8 inch  round cake pan. 2. Place margarine on waxed paper. Microwave on high, 45  seconds; sprinkle brown sugar on melted margarine and arrange 7 pineapple  slices on sugar, add cherries to center of pineapple slices. 3. Put flour,  granulated sugar baking powder and salt in mixing bowl. Blend together egg,  oil, pineapple juice and extracts. Pour into dry ingredients and blend with  wooden spoon. Pour batter over pineapple slices. Microwave at 50% (medium)  5 minutes. Rotate disk once. Microwave on high, 4 to 4 1/2 minutes,  rotating dish twice. Let stand in pan 5 minutes. Invert onto Serving Plate.  Peel off wax paper if it comes out with cake.
