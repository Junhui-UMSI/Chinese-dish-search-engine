Tex-Mex Crispix Mix
Crispix, Dry coriander, Vegetable oil, Crushed red peppers, Garlic powder, Corn chips, Dry taco seasoning mix, Durkee french fried onions, 
Combine taco seasoning mix and spices.  Set aside.  In a 2 gallon storage  bag, combine crispix, corn chips and onions.  Pour oil over cereal mixture,  tossing gently to toss.  Add taco seasoning mix to bag, tossing gently  until cereal mixture is coated.  Store in airtight container. Per 1/2 cup  serving: 100 calories, 4 grams fat.
