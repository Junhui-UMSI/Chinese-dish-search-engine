Pesto Dijon Pizza
Grated Parmesan cheese,, Plum tomatoes, sliced, olive oil, Parsley, chopped, Thinly sliced deli baked ham, Dried basil leaves, divided, Mozzarella cheese, shredded, Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard, Walnuts, chopped, (8 oz) small prepared pizza, 
In small bowl, combine parsley, mustard, walnuts, oil, 1 tbsp Parmesan  cheese and 1 tsp basil. Divide mixture and spread evenly onto each pizza  crust. Top each crust with 2 ounces ham, tomato slices and mozzarella  cheese. Sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese and basil. Place on baking  sheet. Bake at 450 for 8-10 minutes or until cheese melts. Cut into wedges;  serve warm.    MC Formatting by taillon@access.mountain.net    Recipe by: Grey Poupon Posted to EAT-L Digest 30 Mar 97 by The Taillons  <taillon@ACCESS.MOUNTAIN.NET> on Mar 30, 1997
