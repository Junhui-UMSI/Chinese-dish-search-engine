Banana Daiquiris
Dark rum, Frozen limeade concentrate;, Cracked ice, Medium-ripe bananas; peeled, Frozen lemonade concentrate;, 
Slice bananas in a blender jar. Add rum, limeade and lemonade. Whir until  smooth. Add ice and blend a few seconds until ice is crushed. Pour into  glasses to serve.
