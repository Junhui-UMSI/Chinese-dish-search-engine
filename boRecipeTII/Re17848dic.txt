Aloha Cake
Flour, all-purpose; sifted, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Butter, Salt, Eggs, Pinapple crushed 15 1/2 oz, Coconut, Banana; mashed, Sugar, 
Cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs and mix well. Stir in  bananas. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt together. Add to  cream mixture and mix well. Fold in undrained pineapple and coconut. Grease  and flour two 9 x 5 loaf pans. Pour batter into pans. Bale at 350 for 70  minutes until tester come out clean.
