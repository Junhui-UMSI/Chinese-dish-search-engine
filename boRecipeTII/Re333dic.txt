Lemon Bars
All-purpose flour, Sugar, Butter, Eggs, Brown sugar, All-purpose flour, Double-Acting Baking Powder, Fresh Coconut, Walnuts, Fresh Lemon Juice, Lemon Zest, Powdered sugar, 
Mix 2 cups flour with 1/4 cup sugar. Blend in butter until finely mixed.  Press into a jelly roll pan. Bake 15 minutes at 350 degrees. Beat together  eggs, brown sugar, 1/4 cup flour and baking powder. Stir in coconut and  nuts. Spread filling over crust and return pan to oven. Bake 25 minutes  more. Blend lemon juice, zest, and powdered sugar until smooth. Spread on  hot cookies. Cool and cut into bars.
