Baileys Irish Cream Bars
Duncan Hines yellow moist, Eggs, SWIRL, CAKE, Vegetable oil, Duncan Hines creamy cream, Reserved cake batter, 
Date:    Fri, 14 Jun 1996 11:20:21 -0500    From:    Suzanne ODowd <suzanne@DALLAS.NET>  Cake: Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Prepare a jelly roll pan (about 15" x  10" x 1").  Mix cake mix with eggs, Irish Cream and oil.  Set aside 1/2 cup  of prepared cake batter for swirl. Pour cake batter into prepared pan.    Swirl: In a small bowl, blend all swirl ingredients.  Drop large spoonfuls  of this mixture over the cake batter.  Swirl with a fork to make a marble  effect.  Bake 25 - 30 minutes.  Cool and cut into squares.
