Apple Crunch Muffins
Baking Powder, Sugar, Egg, Flour, Sugar, Whole wheat flour, Apples; pared, coarsely, Margarine; melted, Lemon juice, Packed dark brown sugar, Salt, milk, Cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 400 F. Grease 14-16 muffin cups. Toss apple with lemon  juice in small bowl. Stir together flour, whole wheat flour, sugar, brown  sugar, baking powder and salt until well mixed. Whisk milk, egg and melted  margarine. pour over flour mixture. Add apples. Gently stir just to  moisten. Spoon into prepared muffin pans. Combine sugar and cinnamon.  Sprinkle over dough. Bake at 400 F. for 20-25 minutes. Remove from cups.  Cool on wire rack. Reheat at 325 F . for a few minutes.
