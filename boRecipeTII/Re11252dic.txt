Raisin/caper Tapenade
Olive oil; up to 1/2, Sprigs oregano, Raisins, salt and pepper, Capers, Garlic, Branch rosemary, Pinches chile flakes, 
Combine all ingredients up to olive oil in food processor. With machine  running drizzle in oil. Season to taste.  Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by J Pellegrino <gigimfg@ix.netcom.com> on May  26, 1998
