Beefy Taco Bake
Salsa, (10.75 oz) condensed tomato, Cheddar cheese; shredded, Ground beef, milk, Flour tortillas; cut in 1 in, 
In skillet over medium-high heat, cook beef until browned, stirring to  separate meat. Pour off fat.    Add soup, salsa, milk, tortillas and half the cheese. Spoon into 2-qt  shallow baking dish. Cover.    Bake at 400? for 30 minutes or until hot. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
