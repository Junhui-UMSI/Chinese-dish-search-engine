Sweet-And-Sour Sauce (Velasco)
Cornstarch, Ketchup, Water, Sugar, Vinegar, Soy sauce, Salt, 
Put sugar, cornstarch, water, ketchup, soy sauce, salt and vinegar in small  saucepan; cook until thick and clear.
