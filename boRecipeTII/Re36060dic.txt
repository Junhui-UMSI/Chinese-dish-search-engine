Beef Porcupines
Ground beef, Salt, Cut up onions, Applesauce, Catsup, Pepper, Water, Pre-cooked rice, 
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix ground beef, applesauce, rice, onions,  salt and pepper together.  Shape meat mixture into balls. Place 1 layer  deep in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan.  Mix catsup and water together and  pour over meatballs.  Bake for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
