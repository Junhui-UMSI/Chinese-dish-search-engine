Seafood - Tuna - Tuna Supper Sandwiches
Cheddar cheese, Green onions, Red bell pepper, olives, Fresh parsley, Curry powder, Seasoned salt , Solid white tuna, Mayonnaise, french rolls, soft, 
In medium bowl, place cheese, onions, red pepper, olives, parsley, curry  powder and salt; mix lightly.  Add tuna and mayonnaise; toss lightly with  fork.  Cover baking sheet with foil; place rolls on foil. Spread about 1/3  cup mixture on each half.  Bake in 450F (232?C) oven for 10 to 12 minutes or until tops  are bubbling and beginning to brown. Cool slightly before serving.
