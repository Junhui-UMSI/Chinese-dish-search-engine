Beer Cheese Soup
beer, cheese spread, chicken broth, Worcestershire Sauce, milk, Hot sauce, cornstarch, Garlic croutons, 
Combine milk and 2 1/2 beer in large (min. 5-qt.) Dutch oven. Cook over low  heat until thoroughly heated, stirring constantly. Add cheese spread,  broth, Worcestershire sauce, and hot sauce. Cook over low heat until  thoroughly heated, stirring constantly.    In small bowl, combine cornstarch and remaining beer. Add to cheese  mixture; simmer, stirring constantly, until thickened (do not boil).    Yield: 4 quarts.    Posted to bbq-digest by Bill <wcm4@earthlink.net> on Dec 09, 1998,
