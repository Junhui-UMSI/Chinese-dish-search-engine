Quick and Easy Sloppy Joes
Chili powder, Water, Lean ground beef, Hamburger rolls or English, Onions, A.1. Steak Sauce or SWEET &, 
Preparation Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes    1. Cook and stir beef and onions in a large skillet until browned; drain.    2. Stir in steak sauce, water and chili powder; heat to a boil. Reduce heat  and simmer for 10 to 12 minutes.    3. Spoon meat mixture into rolls or English muffins to serve.
