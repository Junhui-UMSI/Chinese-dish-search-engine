Tangy Bean Soup
Dried great Northern beans, salt and pepper, Chopped hard-cooked eggs for, Small onion, chopped, Ground mustard, Brown sugar, Cold water, Medium carrot, chopped, White vinegar, Unbleached all-purpose flour, Soft margarine, 
In a large soup pot, soak the beans overnight in water to cover. Drain and  add the cold water, onion and carrot. Simmer, covered, for 2 1/2 to 3  hours, or until the beans are tender.    Puree the beans and vegetables in a blender or food processor, or force  through a metal colander or food mill. Return the bean mixture to the soup  pot.    In a small saucepan, melt the margarine and blend in the flour. Add the  flour mixture, mustard, vinegar, and brown sugar to the soup pot and cook  until slightly thickened, stirring frequently. Add the salt and pepper.    Garnish each serving with the chopped eggs and serve.
