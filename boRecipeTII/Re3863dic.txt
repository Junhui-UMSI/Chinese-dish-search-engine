Pepper Cake
Vanilla flavor, Rum flavor, Dates chopped, Sugar, Flour, Eggs beaten, Black pepper, Salt, Walnuts chopped, Butter or margarine, 
Cream butter and sugar fluffy. Beat in eggs and flavorings. Mix together,  flour, pepper and salt. Blend flour mixture into creamed mixture. Mix in  dates and nuts. Pour into a greased 8 x 8 inch pan. Bake 275 ( correct ) 45  minutes or tested done.Sift with confectioners sugar while warm.
