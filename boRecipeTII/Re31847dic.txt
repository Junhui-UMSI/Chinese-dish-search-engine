Szechwan Peanut Noodles
Chinese egg noodles (fat, Blanched carrot, cut, Soy sauce, Peanut butter, Roasted unsalted peanuts,, Cayenne, Rice vinegar, Habanero powder, Sesame oil, Chicken broth, Scallions, slivered, Snow pea pods, raw, cut, Raw bean sprouts, 
Over low heat, melt the peanut butter with the oil, soy sauce, and  vinegar. Stir in pepper powders. Thin to proper consistency with  broth. Let cool. Taste for seasoning, correcting with salt or soy.    Cook noodles in the usual way until they are done. Drain and put onto  a serving platter. Cover with sauce.    Arrange carrot, scallions, pea pods, and sprouts in a neat design  over the noodles and sauce. Strew with peanuts. Serve cool or cold.
