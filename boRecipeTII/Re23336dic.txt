Tuna Toasties
Mayonnaise, 220 g can of tuna or skinned, Curry powder, Loaf white sliced bread,, Butter, Small, finely chopped onion, 
1.    combine the first 4 ingredients, mix well.    2.    brush both sides of slices of the bread with melted butter.    3.    spread tuna filling, roll up diagonally, secure with a toothpick,  sprinkle with paprika.  bake at 200 degrees c for 10-15 minutes.    TO MAKE COCONUT MILK    soak 1 cup desiccated coconut in 1? cup warm water for 15 minutes. drain  and squeeze all liquid from coconut. use light liquid, discard coconut  yields about 1 cup.
