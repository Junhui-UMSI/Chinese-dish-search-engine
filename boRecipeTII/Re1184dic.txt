Creamy Roasted Red Pepper Sauce
Red Peppers, Buttermilk, salt and pepper, 
Puree the roasted peppers, buttermilk, and salt and pepper in a blender or  food processor until smooth.    Serve with any pasta topped with your favorite herb, or use it to dress up  Gnocchi, baked potatoes, stuffed vegetables, savory filled crepes, or  simple grain dishes. Can also be used as a marinade for steamed vegetables.    This will keep for 1 week refrigerated.
