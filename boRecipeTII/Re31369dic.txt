Barbs Corn Bread
Creamed corn; 15 ounces, Box Jiffy corn muffin mix, Butter, Egg, Sugar, 
Mix all ingredients.  Bake in greased 9 X 13 pan or 18 muffin cups for 20  to 25 minutes at 350.
