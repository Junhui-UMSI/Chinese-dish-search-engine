Apple Glazed Carrots with Bacon
Turkey bacon, Onion, Baby Carrots, Apple Cider, Brown sugar, Ground red pepper, Chives, 
*NOTE: Original recipe used regular bacon    Cook bacon slices in a small skillet over med heat until crisp. Remove  bacon from skillet, and crumble. Add onion to bacon drippings in skillet,  and saute 3 min. Add carrots, cider, sugar, and pepper, and bring to a  boil. Cook mixture 10 min over med heat or until carrots are tender. Do not  drain.    Place carrot mixture in a large serving bowl. Sprinkle with the reserved  bacon and chives.
