Prune Whip
Cooked oatmeal; cooled*, Cinnamon, Sugar, Egg whites, drained and pitted, Cooked dried prunes, 
Mash prunes; stir in oatmeal and cinnamon; set aside.  Beat egg whites  until frothy.  Gradually add sugar, a tablespoon at a time, beating  constantly until stiff and glossy.    Gently fold in prune mixture.  Spoon into 6 individual dishes or large  serving dish.  Chill several hours or until set.    *NOTE: Cook Quaker Oats (quick or old fashioned, uncooked) according to  package directions.
