Tuna and Swiss Cheese Pie
Unbaked 9-inch pastry, Dry mustard, Whipping cream, Eggs, Salt, Flour, Swiss cheese, shredded, (6 1/2 oz) tuna, drained, Ale, 
Beat eggs, salt, dry mustard and pepper until foamy. Beat in cream and ale.  Cover bottom of pastry shell with a layer of tuna. Sprinkle half of cheese  over tuna. Repeat layering. Sprinkle flour over cheese. Pour egg mixture  over top. Bake at 425 degrees for 15 minutes. Turn oven control to 300  degrees and bake 25 minutes or until a knife inserted halfway between  center and edge of filling comes out clean. Makes 6 entree servings or  10-12 appetizer servings.
