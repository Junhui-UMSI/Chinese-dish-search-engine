Potatoes with Black Pepper #2
X Potatoes; cooked, peeled,, X Chopped parsley or green, X Lots of black pepper, X Some salt, 
Heat some oil in a pan. When hot, put in the potatoes, salt, and pepper.  Keep frying them, stirring occasionally, until the outsides turn slightly  crusty. Add the parsley or coriander, stir briefly, and serve.    Recipe by: Madhur Jaffrey    Posted to recipelu-digest by Sharon Raghavachary <schary@earthlink.net> on  Feb 05, 1998
