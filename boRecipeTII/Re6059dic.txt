Pizza Chiena
Ricotta, Grated cheese, Filling, Shortening, salt and pepper, Stick pepperoni, cubed, Genoa salami, cubed, Raw eggs, DOUGH, Baking Powder, Ham, cubed, Egg, Cold water, Flour, Mozzarella, cubed, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.    If you make this filling you will need enough crust for 3 pies. This recipe  will make 2 crusts so you will need to increase the recipe. You can also  use frozen deep dish pie crusts from the supermarket.    Mix ingredients, set aside. Put crust in pie tin and pour in filling. Bake  at 400 for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 325 and bake for approximately 45  minutes. The filling will firm up as it cools.  Posted to Kitmailbox Digest  by GAdams1350@aol.com on Mar 2, 1997.
