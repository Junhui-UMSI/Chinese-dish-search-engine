Watergate Salad
Mini marshmallows, (20 oz.) crushed pineapple, Box pistachio pudding, (small) Cool Whip, Pecans, (small) cream cheese, 
Mix all ingredients, except nuts. Sprinkle top with nuts. Chill.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 366 by "Karen Coe"  <ayla@duo-county.com> on Dec 13, 1997
