White Cake
Water, Baking Powder, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, Water, EGGS SHELL, MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, SHORTENING; 3LB, All Purpose Flour, IMITATION VANILLA, Salt, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN                   TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.   SIFT TOGETHER FLOUR , SUGAR, SALT, BAKING POWDER, AND MILK INTO  MIXER BOWL.    2.  ADD SHORTENING AND WATER TO DRY INGREDIENTS.  BEAT AT LOW SPEED  1 MINUTE OR UNTIL BLENDED; CONTINUE BEATING AT MEDIUM SPEED 2 MINUTES.  SCRAPE DOWN BOWL.    3.  COMBINE EGG WHITES, WATER, AND VANILLA.  ADD SLOWLY TO MIXTURE WHILE  BEATING AT LOW SPEED.  SCRAPE DOWN BOWL.  BEAT AT MEDIUM SPEED 3 MINUTES.    4.  POUR 1 GAL    (ABOUT 7 LB  6 OZ) BATTER INTO EACH GREASED AND FLOURED  PAN    5.  BAKE 35 TO 45 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE.    6.  COOL; FROST IF DESIRED. CUT 6 BY 9.  NOTE:  1.  OTHER PAN SIZES MAY BE USED.  SEE RECIPE NO. G-G-4.    2.  IN STEP 5, IF CONVECTION OVEN IS USED, BAKE AT 300 F., 25-35  MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE ON LOW FAN, OPEN VENT.
