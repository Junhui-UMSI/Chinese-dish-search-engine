Appetizer From Black Grapes (Mantala Od Grozdja)
Sugar, Grapes (for 1 gallon juice), Lemon juice, Whole wheat flour, Nutmeg, 
Crush grapes until you have a gallon of juice; strain and cook boiling  until 1/2 liquid is left.  Add slowly 1 1/2 (cups? -KM) whole wheat flour,  stirring constantly over low flame.  Add 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg and 2 cups  sugar before flour.  Add lemon juice.  Pour into 9 x 13-1/3 x 2-inch deep  pan.  Let cool in pan 2 days.  Slice in 2x3 inch squares. Serve with  drinks.
