Avocado Dip #1
Sieved avocado, Freshly squeezed lemon juice, Celery seed, Parsley, Salt, Tabasco sauce; more or less, Garlic, Grated onion, Prepared mustard, 
Cut avocado into halves and remove seed and skin. Force fruit through a  sieve. Rub bowl with cut garlic and discard garlic. Add avocado and other  ingredients, blend well. Chill. Yield: 1 cup.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
