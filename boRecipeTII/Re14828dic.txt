15 Minute Chicken a la King
Water, Instant chicken gravy, milk, Sauteed, Jar (2 oz) pimento; sliced, Cooked; diced chicken, Swiss mushroom soup mix, Mushrooms; sliced and, Sherry or white wine, 
From: amwilson@juno.com (A Wilson)    Date: Thu, 1 Aug 1996 03:55:21 PST  Mix soup mix with water; heat to boiling, stirring constantly.  Let simmer,  covered, for several minutes.  Bring back to boiling.  Add gravy mix and  milk. Stir to blend. Cookseveral minutes. Add chicken, pimento, mushrooms,  and sherry or wine if desired. Taste before seasoning with salt and pepper.  To serve: Heat thoroughly and serve over rice. For added flavor and color,  add 2 tablespoons chopped parsley to the rice. Serves 4 to 6. Can be made  ahead. Freezes well.
