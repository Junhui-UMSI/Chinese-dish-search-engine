Tomato and Fennel Salad
olive oil, Tomatoes; cut in wedges and, Fennel bulb; core and thinly, Dijon mustard, Salt and pepper, Fennel frond or cilantro;, Red wine vinegar, 
Combine the fennel and the tomatoes. Mix together the Dijon mustard,  vinegar, and oil. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Pour over the  dressing the fennel and tomatoes. Top with the chopped fennel. See  Strawberry mouse.
