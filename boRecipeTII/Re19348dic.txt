Vegetable Tortellini Soup
Dried basil; crushed, Cheese-filled tortellini;, Garlic; minced(2tsp), Green bell pepper; diced, Onion; coarsely chopped, 14 1/2 oz beef broth;, Picante sauce or salsa;, Freshly grated parmesan;, Olive oil;, 14 1/2 oz stewed tomatoes;, 
In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, cook onion and garlic in olive oil  until tender, about 6 minutes.  Add broth, tomatoes, picante sauce  and basil; bring to a boil.  Stir in tortellini; simmer uncovered 15  minutes.    Stir in bell pepper; continue to simmer 3 to 4 minutes or until  tortellini is tender.  Ladle into soup bowls; sprinkle with cheese.    Note: Very good with Resers salsa.  Was for 6 serving, now is for 3 sweet ones.
