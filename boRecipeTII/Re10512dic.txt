Quick Haggis
Onion, Salt, Stock; (up to 8), Liver, Cayenne pepper, Suet, Oatmeal, 
Boil the liver and parboil the onion, then mince them together. Lightly  brown the oatmeal then mix all ingredients together. Place in a greased  basin and cover with foil, or a suet crust if desired and steam for 1 1/2  hours. Serves 4.    Posted to bbq-digest V5 #739 by Wiley Mixon <wmix65@arn.net> on Dec 02,  1997
