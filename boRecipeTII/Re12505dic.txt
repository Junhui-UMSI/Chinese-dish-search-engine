Yellow Squash Casserole
Margarine, Green chilies, Cheddar cheese, Saltine crackers, Salt and pepper to taste, Yellow summer squash, Onion, 
In a skillet, melt margarine over medium heat. Saute squash and onion until  crisp-tender. Remove from the heat; stir in chilies, crackers, and salt and  pepper. Spoon into a greased 1 1/2 qt. casserole. Top with cheese. Bake at      350    degrees for 15-20 minutes.
