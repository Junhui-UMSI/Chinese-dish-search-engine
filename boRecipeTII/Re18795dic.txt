Anchovies, Fried Genovese Style
Cloves garlic; very finely, Virgin olive oil, Lemons; in wedges, Salt and pepper, Eggs, Fresh bread crumbs; very, Italian parsley; finely, 
Heat oil to 375 degrees F and maintain heat.    Scale and gut anchovies, leaving heads and tails intact (or have your fish  monger do this for you). In a large mixing bowl, mix eggs, garlic and  parsley until well blended. Dip fish 3 or 4 at a time into egg mixture and  then into bread crumbs. Shake well to remove excess eggcrumb coating and  fry fish 3 to 4 at a time until crisp and golden brown, about 1 to 2  minutes. Remove and drain on a paper towel.    Serve immediately with salt, pepper and lemon wedges.
