Potage Mongol
Can condensed green pea soup, Dry sherry, Can condensed tomato soup, Soup cans water, 
1. In a deep, 2 1/2-quart heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole or  heat-resistant, non-metallic soup tureen, combine the tomato soup, pea soup  and water; stir until smooth. 2. Heat, covered, in Microwave Oven 10  minutes or until soup bubbles. Stir several times during heating. 3. Before  serving, stir in sherry.
