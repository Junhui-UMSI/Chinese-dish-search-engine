Tomato Soup Cake
Cinnamon, Tomato soup, Dates, Nuts, Sugar, Cloves, Frosting:, Shortening, Powdered sugar, Baking soda, Butter, Nutmeg, Flour, Philadelphia Cream Cheese, Cake:, Vanilla and Nuts, 
Mix all cake ingredients together and bake for 35 minutes at 350 degrees.  After cake has cooled, mix all frosting ingredients together and spread  over cake.
