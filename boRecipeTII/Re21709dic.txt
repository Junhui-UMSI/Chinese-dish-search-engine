Apple Temptation Cake
GLAZE, Baking Powder, Water, Cinnamon, Sour cream, Cinnamon schnapps, Apples; peel; slice, Sugar, Butter, Eggs, Sugar, Cinnamon schnapps, Nuts; chopped, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Baking soda, Flour, Butter, Nuts; chopped for garnish, 
CAKE: Preheat oven to 350~. Grease and flour bundt pan. Mix together the  apples, cinnamon and cinnamon schnapps; set aside. Cream butter and sugar;  add eggs and beat 2 minutes. Add remaining ingredients except apples and  nuts. Beat well. Drain liquid from apples and add to batter. Mix well. Add  apples and nuts. Bake for 1 hour. Cool 20-30 minutes.    GLAZE: Bring to a boil for 2-3 minutes the butter, sugar and water. Stir in  schnapps; pour over cake. Sprinkle with chopped nuts.
