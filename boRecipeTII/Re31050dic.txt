Banana Pecan Muffins
Baking soda, Raw sugar, Egg, Baking Powder, Very ripe bananas, Flour, Butter, milk, Bran, Chopped pecans or walnuts, Plain yoghurt, 
Sift flour, b/pdr, b/soda into a bowl. Stir in bran, sugar & nuts. Make a  well in centre of dry ingredients. Melt butter in a small pot or microwave.  Mash bananas. Lightly beat egg, milk & yoghurt together. Add bananas to dry  ingredients. Pour melted butter & egg mixture into the well & mix with a  fork until JUST combined. Spoon into prepared pans & bake at 430F for  15-20 mins.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
