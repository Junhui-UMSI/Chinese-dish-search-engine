Quesadillas
Rinsed, Ground cumin, Pinto beans/ drained and, Chopped, Jalapeno/ seeded and roughly, Powder, Fresh parsley, Red onion/ roughly chopped, Or more commercial chili, 
Throw everything in a food processor and pulse until still chunky.    Spread 1/2 inch between 2 whole wheat tortillas and heat until toasted on  hot non stick pan. Good with commercial salsa. (Dont put bean mixture in  tortillas until ready to cook)    From: 6522@delphi.com (Tom).
