Whipped Cream Apricot Sauce
Heavy cream; whipped, Pineapple juice, Pitted; unpeeled apricots, Sugar, 
Place apricots and pineapple juice in blender. Blend covered 30 seconds.  Makes about 1-1/4 cups puree. Add sugar to puree; blend. Fold puree into  whipped cream, until just blended. Refrigerate, covered, until ready to  serve. Serve as a sauce with Angel Food Cake or in Meringues. Makes 3 cups.    MRS E.J.
