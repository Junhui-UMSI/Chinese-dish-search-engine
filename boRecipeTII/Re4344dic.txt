Persimmon Gelatin
Lemon juice, Persimmons; pureed, Hot water, Egg whites; stiffly beaten, Unflavored gelatin, 
Recipe by: Time Life Books - Fruits Soften the geatin in 1/4 cup of cold  warer for 5 minutes.    Place the softened gelatin in a large bowl.  Add the hot water and stir  until gelatin dissolves.  Mix in the persimmon puree and lemon juice.  Refrigerate the mixture until it is on the verge of setting, then fold in  the egg whites.  Pour the jelly into sherbet glasses and refrigerate it  until firm and thoroughly chilled - about one more hour.
