Apricot Honey Oat Bars
Cinnamon, Wheat germ, Salt, yogurt, fat-free, plain, Vanilla, Margarine, Dried apricots, Flour, Egg whites, Rolled oats, Honey, 
1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.    2. Spray 8 inch square baking pan with non stick cooking spray    3. In a large bowl combine all ingredients; mix to blend    4. turn mixture into prepared pan, pat down and smooth top.    5. Bake until center is firm and edges are lightly browned, about 25  minutes.    6. Cool and cut into squares    Notes: Made: 03/07 and 03/20 Mom really loved these and James found them  ok. I fin them a bit too soft and will try cooking them a bit longer so  they are crisper.
