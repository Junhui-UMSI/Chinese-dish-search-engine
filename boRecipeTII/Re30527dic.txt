Taco Twist Casserole
Taco seasoning mix, Tomato sauce, Cheddar cheese, green bell pepper, Ground beef, Corkscrew macaroni, Sour cream, 
Brown meat; drain. Stir in seasoning mix, tomato sauce and green pepper.  Bring to a boil and remove from heat. Combine cooked pasta, 1/2 cup cheese  and sour cream and place on bottom of baking dish. Top with meat mixture  and remaining cheese. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
