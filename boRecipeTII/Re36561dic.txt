Strawberry and Peach Pie
Pie crust (any kind), (sm.) Cool Whip, Strawberry cream sauce, (8 oz.) cream cheese, Fresh strawberries & peaches, Powdered sugar, 
Cream cheese with the powdered sugar. Place this in the bottom of pie  shell. Cut peaches in small pieces and place on top of this. Put the  strawberries on top of peaches. Pour glaze over this. Put extra  strawberries on top. Serve the pie with Cool Whip. Posted to Bakery Shoppe  Digest148 by Chris & Karen <sanburns@m2.sprynet.com> on Jul 06, 1997
