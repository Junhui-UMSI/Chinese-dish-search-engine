Pecan Meringue Kisses
Pecans, Egg white; at room, Brown sugar; firmly packed, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F. Grease a cookie sheet.  Beat egg whites and salt until soft peaks form. Gradually add brown sugar,  beating constantly, until stiff peaks form. Fold in pecans.  Drop rounded teaspoonfuls of meringue onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake  about 15 to 20 minutes or until delicately brown and firm. Cool three to  five minutes before removing from cookie sheet.
