Alaska Paella
-Mussels in shells, Mussels in brine; drained, Garlic clove; crushed, Vegetable or chicken stock, Tomatoes; skinned,, Canned red Alaska salmon, olive oil, Long grain rice, Shelled prawns, Ground saffron; OR..., Lemon slices to garnish, Leek; cleaned and sliced, -ground turineric, Onion, Whole cooked prawns, Lemon; juiced, 
[Saffron gives this dish its traditional Paella coloring. It is however,  expensive. Turmeric can be used as a substitute.]    Drain the can of salmon, reserving the juice. Set aside.    Heat the oil in a large pan and cook the garlic, onion and leek for 5  minutes. Stir in the rice, prawns, mussels, salmon juice, stock, lemon  juice and saffron. Mix well, bring to the boil. Reduce the heat and simmer  for 15-20 minutes or until the liquid is almost completely absorbed by the  rice.    Stir the tomatoes into the rice along with the salmon, broken into large  flakes.    Pile into a serving dish and decorate with the whole prawns and lemon  slices. Serve immediately.    Serves 2.
