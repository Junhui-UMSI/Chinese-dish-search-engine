Sweet and Sour Prawns
Ground ginger, Cornflour, Vegetable oil, Cooked and Peeled Prawns, Brown sugar, Tomato Puree (optional), Crushed Pineapple (optional), Soya Sauce, Orange juice, Cider vinegar, 
Mix the corflour with the orange juice until smooth. Put all the other  ingredients except the prawns into a saucepan, add the orange juice and  cornflour and bring to the boil, stirring regularly until thick and smooth.  If you want a reddish-coloured sauce add the tomato puree and, if you like  the flavour, add the crushed pineapple, or both. Put in the prawns and heat  through for just two minutes, Dont cook them too long or theyll turn  rubbery. Serve with plenty of rice and stir-fried beansprouts.
