Quick Apple Strudel
Tart apples, Cinnamon, Egg, Brown sugar, Sugar, Non dairy margarine, Flour, Orange juice, Baking Powder, Sliced, walnuts, 
In a bowl combine apple slices, granulated sugar, cinnamon & orange  juice. Mix lightly & place in a greased 2 quart casserole. Sift flour  & baking powder together. Cut in the margarine & rub with fingertips  to a crumbly consistency.  Combine brown sugar & egg. Add to flour  mixture along with nuts. Sprinkle over apples. Bake in 350 oven for  45 minutes, or until apples are tender & crust is golden brown.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
