Bagels Galore (Roasted Bell Peppers and Onions)
Plain bagels, cut in half, (optional, I like it with, Onion  If the onion is, And without), Non-fat cream cheese, Red bell pepper, Black pepper, Small, make that 4 pieces, Inch thick slices of red, 
Cut red bell pepper lengthwise into four pieces, clean out, and place on  piece of aluminum foil into toaster oven together with the onions. At 400F  bake for about 20 minutes, or until bell pepper is soft and skin bliste  ring. Take out peppers and put bagel halfs into toaster oven. Remove skins  from red peppers. By the time you are done, the bagels are toasted .  Arrange bagels open-faced on plate. Put onions on two of the halfs. Put a  bit of cream cheese and 2 pieces of roasted bell peppers on the other two .  Sprinkle black pepper onto the bell peppers. Eat while still hot.
