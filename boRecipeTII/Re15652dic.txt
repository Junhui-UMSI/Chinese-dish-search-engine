A Good Relish
Dozen, Onions, Green tomatoes, Salt, 
Grind tomatoes and onions together.  Add salt and cover with water, let  stand 10 minutes.  Drain well. Add:    1 dozen peppers (green and red) 2 hot peppers 1 dozen pickles (canned ones  can be used) 1 tbsp. celery seed 3 1/2 lb. sugar 1 qt. vinegar Cook 15  minutes.  Seal while hot.
