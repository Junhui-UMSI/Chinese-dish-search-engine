Tutti-Fruitticake
Red candied cherries, Green candied pineapple, Sifted enriched flour, 1 lb package (3 cups) pitted, Salt, Well beaten eggs, Shelled Brazil nuts (3 cups), Or pecan halves (4 cups), Green candied cherries, Vanilla or rum extract, Sugar, . ., Red candied pineapple, Baking Powder, . ., 
Halve dates and cut pineapple in chunks. combine with the whole cherries  and nuts. Sift together dry ingredients, add to fruits and nuts, coating  well. Stir in eggs and vanilla. Line two 8 1/2x4 1/2x2 1/2-inch loaf pans  with heavy paper; then grease paper and pour in batter. Bake in slow oven  (325?) 1 hour or till done. Cool.
