Apricot Aperitif
Ground nutmeg, Angostura bitters, Sugar, Apricot nectar, Mineral water, Dry white wine, lime, 
Mix sugar and bitters in 3-quart pitcher. Stir in apricot nec- tar, wine  and mineral water. Pour over ice in each of 10 eight-ounce giasses. Garnish  each with lime slice sprinkled with nutmeg. 10 servings;110 calories per  serving.    Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
