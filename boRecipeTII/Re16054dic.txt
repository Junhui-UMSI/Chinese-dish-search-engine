Warm Crab Dip
milk, Salt, Cream cheese, Horseradish, Onion, Flake crab; picked over, Pepper, 
Mix all ingredients together by hand or with mixer. Put into shallow dish.  Sprinkle with 1/3 cup slivered or sliced almonds.  Bake at 375? for 15-20  minutes, until bubbly and almonds are golden brown.
