Wacels Relish
Texas Ruby Red or Rio Star;, Jalapeno pepper, Whole-style cranberry sauce, 
Segment the Ruby Red grapefruit. Peel the segments to remove the fibrous  parts and the skin of the individual segments. Cut the remaining flesh into  "chunks".    Remove the seeds from the jalapeno pepper and mince it finely.    Mix all three prepared ingredients together and serve chilled as a relish.
