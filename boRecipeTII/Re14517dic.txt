"Cheese" Sauce
Fresh Lemon Juice, Salt to taste, if desired, Cooked, peeled potato, Onion powder, Water, (nutritional yeast), 4 oz jar pimientos, Tablspoons cornstarch, 
Blend the potato with 1/4 to 1/3 cups of the water in a blender.  Add the  remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.  Pour into a saucepan.  Cook,  stirring, until smooth and thick, 7 to 8 minutes.    Serve at once.  The sauce will set when cool and can be reheated.    Makes 2 cups Preparation time: 10 min Cooking time: 10 min    Source: This is a recipe I found in the McDougall "Program for Maximum  Weight Loss" book.
