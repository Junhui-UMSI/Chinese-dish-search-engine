Tex-Mex Dip #2
Lemon juice, Taco Seasoning Mix packet, Tomatoes; seeded and chopped, Sharp cheddar cheese; grated, Salt, Mayonnaise, Green Onions, Avocadoes, medium size, Olives; chopped, Sour cream, Tortilla chips, Pepper, Bean dip; plain or jalepeno, 
Peel, pit and mash avocadoes in medium bowl. Add lemon juice, salt and  pepper. In separate bowl, combine sour cream, mayonnaise, and taco  seasoning. To assemble, spread bean dip on a large, shallow platter. Top  with avocado mixture. Put on sour cream and taco mixture. Sprinkle with  chopped onions, tomatoes, and olives. Cover with shredded cheese.     Miscellaneous recipes from the collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mo-misc.zip
