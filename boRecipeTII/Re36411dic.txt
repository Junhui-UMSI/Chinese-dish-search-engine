Beer Bread
Sugar, Self-rising flour*, Warm beer., From: NCBK03B, 
Place in a greased and floured loaf pan.  Bake at 350 for 1 hour. * Or  regular flour, 3 & half tsp. baking powder, and 1 & half tsp. salt This is  the only simple recipe I know and it happens to be the one you want. I  think this is one of the only breads you cant mess up on.
