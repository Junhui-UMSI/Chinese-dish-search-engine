White Chocolate Fudge Frosting
Vanilla, Frozen whipped topping,, Phillsbury Vanilla Creamy, White baking chocolate,, 
In a large bowl, beat the frosting at medium speed. Gradually add the white  chocolate. Beat at high speed for 30 seconds or until smooth and well  blended. Fold in vanilla and whipped frosting. Refrigerate.    Spread on completely cooled cake. Store cake in the fridge.
