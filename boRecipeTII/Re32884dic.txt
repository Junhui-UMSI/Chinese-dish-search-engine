Sweet Potatoes with Persimmons
Persimmons, slied, Fresh sweet potatoes, Water, Sugar, Juice of 1/2 Lemon, 
Put all ingredients into saucepan and cook, stirring occasionally, until  sweet potatoes are done and medium syrup is formed.    Posted by words@cats.ucsc.edu to the Fatfree Dig.
