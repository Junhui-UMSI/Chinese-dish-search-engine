Lemon Ice
Boiling water, Sugar, Lemon Rind, Fresh Lemon Juice, Salt, 
Combine all ingredients, stirr well. Let cool to room temperature. Pour  into a 8-inch square baking dish; cover. Freeze at least 6 hours. Bread  frozen mixture into chumks. Place chunks in food processor; process until  smooth.
