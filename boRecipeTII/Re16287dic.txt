Watermelon Dessert Salsa
Watermelon; finely chopped, Navel oranges; diced, Honey, Lemon juice, Strawberries; coarsely, Ground red pepper, 
Mix all ingredients in medium-size bowl.  Refrigerate at least 1 hour  before serving.  Serving Ideas : Spoon over frozen yogurt and serve with biscotti cookies.    NOTES : Makes 3 cups.
