Venison Shepherds Pie
carrots, Shortening, Peas, celery, Beef gravy, potatoes, Ground venison, Chili pepper, Onion, 
Slightly brown onion in shortening and then add gravy,  venison, carrots, celery and peas.  Let simmer for 2  min. and then pour into a 2-qt casserole dish.  Spread  mashed potatoes on top.  Bake at 450F for about 25 min  or until lightly browned.
