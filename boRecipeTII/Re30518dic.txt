Taco Stew
(10 3/4-oz.) condensed, Onion; chopped;, (1/2 cup), (14-oz) whole kernel corn;,, Lean ground beef, (15-oz.) pinto beans in, Envelope (1 1/4-oz.) taco, Shredded Monterey jack, Water, Coarsely crushed tortilla, 
In a large saucepan cook and stir ground beef and onion over medium heat  till meat is browned; drain off fat. Stir in soup, corn, beans, undrained  tomatoes, seasoning mix and water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Simmer  uncovered about 45 minutes or to desired consistency. Serve with tortilla  chips and cheese. Makes 6 servings.
