Peanut Butter Honey Cookies
Peanut butter, Baking soda, Egg, Brown sugar, Flour, Shortening, Vanilla, Salt, Honey, Raisins (optional),*, 
* washed and drained     Ice Box Cookies     Ice box cookies are a boon for the busy person because they can be  prepared in advance and stored in the freezer until needed. Just slice and  bake when desired, and you have a great home made cookie.     If you like, use whole wheat flour and add 1/2 cup ground peanuts to the  dough.     In a large bowl, cream the shortening and peanut butter, blending well.  Add the honey, mixing it in thoroughly. Gradually blend in sugar. Beat in  egg and vanilla.     Sift together the flour with baking powder, baking soda and salt. Stir the  dry ingredients into the creamed mixture. Stir in the raisins, if using.  Shape in a roll. Wrap in waxed paper and chill thoroughly.     Preheat oven to 350?. Cut chilled dough into thin slices and bake on  greased cookie sheets at 350? for 10-12 minutes.    Yield: 4-5 dozen. Jacqueline E.
