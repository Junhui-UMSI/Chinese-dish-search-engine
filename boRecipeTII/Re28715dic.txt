Texas Style Tofu Barbecue
Commercial barbecue sauce, Water, Hamburger buns (preferably, Salt, Garlic powder, Firm tofu; frozen, thawed, Pepper, Kitchen Bouquet (or soy, Onion, 
Squeeze excess moisture from thawed tofu; crumble in frying pan. Add  water, Kitchen Bouquet, onion, garlic, salt andpepper, stirring to marinate  tofu. Simmer for 10 minutes on medium heat. Drain and add barbecue sauce.  Simmer for 10 minutes on low heat. Serve on buns with extra sliced onions  and sweet pickle relish.
