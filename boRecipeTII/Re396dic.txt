Lemon Coconut Bars
Butter or corn oil, Plus 3 tablespoons flour;, Eggs, Sugar, Baking Powder, Salt, Fresh Lemon Juice, Grated coconut; unsweetened,, 
Heat oven to 325. Mix butter, confectioner sugar and 2 cups flour. Press  into bottom of a 10 x 15-inch pan. Bake 10 to 15 minutes or until light  brown. Beat eggs with sugar. Add 3 tablespoons flour, baking powder, lemon  juice and salt. Stir in coconut. Pour over crust. Bake 25 minutes or until  set and light brown. Cool. Cut into 2-inch squares or into fingers. Dust  with confectioner sugar.    Recipe by: RITA OERTEL--RECORD-JOURNAL 12/31/97    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 519 by ctlindab@mail1.nai.net on  Jan 14, 1998
