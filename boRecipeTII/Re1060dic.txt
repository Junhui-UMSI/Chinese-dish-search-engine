Cookie Ice Cream-A-Rounds
(18 oz) Pillsbury, Any flavor Haagen-Dazs Ice, 
1. Heat oven to 350*. Shape heaping tablespoons of well-chilled dough into  balls. Place about 4 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets.    2. Bake for 10 - 15 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool 1 minute;  remove from cookie sheets.    3. Cut foil into twelve 12 x 9-inch pieces for each sandwich desired. For  each sandwich, place about 1/3 cup ice cream between 2 cookies; gently  press together. Quickly wrap each sandwich in foil. Freeze at least 8 hours  or until firm.
