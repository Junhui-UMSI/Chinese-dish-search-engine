Avocado and Raisin Dip
Vegetable oil, Black Pepper, Lime juice, Avocados; Peeled & Chopped, Raisins, Salt, Sugar, 
Place all ingredients in blender container.  Cover and blend on high speed  until smooth, about 45 seconds.  Serve with raw vegetables, assorted  crackers or fried tortillas. Makes 1 2/3 cups of dip.    Miscellaneous recipes from the collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mo-misc.zip
