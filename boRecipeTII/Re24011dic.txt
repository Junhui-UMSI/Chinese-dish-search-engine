Tossed Salad with Tomato-Basil Dressing
White wine vinegar, Tomato juice, fresh basil, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Salt, Pepper, Hot sauce, assorted ?, Tomatoes, Cucumber, 
Combine first 7 ingredients in a jar. Cover tightly, and shake vigorously.  Let stand 15 minutes to 2 hours.    Combine salad greens, cucumber, and tomato wedges in a large bowl. Shake  dressing vigorously, and pour over salad; toss well. Yield: 12 servings  (serving size: 2 cups).    Per serving: 58 Calories; 3g Fat (43% calories from fat); 3g Protein; 7g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 181mg Sodium    NOTES : English, or hothouse cucumbers, are seedless. If they are not  available, slice a regular cucumber in half lengthwise, and scrape a spoon  down the center to remove the seeds.
