Strawberry Chiffon Squares
Graham wafer crumbs, Reallemon lemon juice, Whipping cream [whipped], Miniature marshmellows, Margarine, Eagle brand milk, Strawberry jello, Boiling water, Frozen sliced strawberries, 
Combine crumbs and margarine and pat on bottom of 9 x 13 pan.Dissolve jello  in boiling water in a large bowl. Stir in sweetened milk, lemon juice,  strawberries and marshmellows. Fold in whipped cream. Pour over crumb  crust. Chill until set, about 2 hours.
