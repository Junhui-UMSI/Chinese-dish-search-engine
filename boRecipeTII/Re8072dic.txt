Potato Bake Casserole
(8-oz) sour cream, (32-oz) frozen hash brown, Cream of celery soup, Onion, Bell pepper; chopped, Cream of mushroom soup, 
Mix together soups, sour cream, onion & green pepper. Add potatoes & mix  well. Pour into a greased 13x9-inch dish. Bake at 300 for 2 hours. Yields  15-20 servings.
