Zucchini with Peanuts
Salt, Unsalted peanuts, raw or, Zucchini, Vegetable oil, roasted, coarsely ground, 
Simmer the zucchini whole in about 1/2 c salted water until very tender,  about 10 minutes.  Combine the zucchini & oil & mash into a smooth  consistency.  Top with the nuts. VARIATION: When zucchini is tender, drain  & slice it, gently stir in the oil & top with nuts.
