Apprlkoek
Apples, Butter, Flour, Ground cinnamon, Granulated sugar, divided, Salt, Baking Powder, milk, Egg, well beaten, 
Peel & cut apples into eighths (wedges). Sift together flour, baking  powder & salt with 4 Tbls. of the sugar. Cut in butter. Combine egg &  milk & add to flour mixture. Turn batter into greased 8" square cake  pan. Press apple wedges partly into batter. Combine remaining 2 Tbls.  sugar & cinnamon, sprinkle over apple.  Bake at 425 for 25 to 30  minutes.
