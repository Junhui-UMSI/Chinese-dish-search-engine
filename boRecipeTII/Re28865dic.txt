Thai Glazed Pork Chops
Red pepper, chopped, Pork chops, 1/2" thick, Peach or apricot jam, Knorr Thai sauce, Vegetable oil, Chopped cliantro or parsley, 
In a skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; add pork chops and brown on  both sides. In a small bowl combine sauce and jam; pour over chops, turning  to coat. Stir in red pepper. Reduce heat; simmer uncovered 10 minutes or  until tender, turning chops occasionally. Serve with rice and garnish with  chopped cilantro or parsley.
