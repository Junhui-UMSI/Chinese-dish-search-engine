Apricot Nectar Salad
(14-oz) apricot nectar, (20-oz) crushed pineapple;, Box (3-oz) orange gelatin, Bananas; mashed, Box (3-oz) lemon gelatin, Pecans, 
Heat nectar. Add gelatin and stir until dissolved. Cool. Add pineapple,  bananas and pecans. Pour into 11x14 pan. When gelatin is set, cut into  squares and serve. Serves 16-20 and makes excellent accompaniment for  turkey.
