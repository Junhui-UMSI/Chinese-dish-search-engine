Sweet and Spicy Brownies
Walnuts, Butter or margarine, Eggs, Squares unsweetened, Granulated sugar, Tabasco? pepper sauce, Semi-sweet chocolate chips, All-purpose flour, 
In small saucepan, over medium-low heat, melt chocolate squares and butter  or margarine, stirring frequently.    Preheat oven to 350F. Grease 9 x 9-inch baking pan. In large bowl, combine  sugar, eggs, flour, TABASCO? pepper sauce and melted chocolate mixture  until well blended. Stir in chocolate chips and chopped walnuts. Spoon  mixture into pan. Bake 35 to 40 minutes until toothpick inserted in center  comes out clean. Cool in pan on wire rack.    Makes 16 brownies.
