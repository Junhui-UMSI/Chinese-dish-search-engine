Beef Enchiladas
Chili pepper, olive oil, Cheddar cheese, Salt, Ground beef, Tomato sauce, Vegetable oil, Onion, Tortillas, soft, Red sauce, Garlic, Egg, 
Heat 1/2 inch of oil in skillet and fry tortillas about 15 seconds each  side; remove and pat off excess oil. In separate skillet heat 2 tbl oil*  and saute onion until tender. Add chili peppers, garlic, tomato sauce,  salt and pepper. Add ground beef. Simmer uncovered 10 minutes. Dip  tortillas in egg,fill with sauce mixture, roll and place in shallow greased  baking dish.  Pour red sauce over tortillas, top with grated cheese and  bake uncovered at 350 deg for 15 minutes. *Quanitiy for standard serving  size.
