Almond Chicken Casserole
Chicken, Boned, Skinned &, Butter, Rice, Cooked, Corn Flakes, Onion, Water Chestnuts, Cream of mushroom soup, Celery, Almonds, Plain yogurt, White Pepper, Almonds, Mayonnaise, Salt, Chicken broth, Lemon juice, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Combine the chicken, mayonnaise, yogurt,  cream of mushroom soup, chicken broth, white pepper, salt, lemon juice,  onion, rice, water chestnuts, the first measure of almonds and the celery  together in a large, well buttered casserole dish. Mix the second measure  of almonds, butter and corn flakes together. Sprinkle the mixture over the  top of the casserole. Bake for 35 to 45 minutes. Serve hot.
