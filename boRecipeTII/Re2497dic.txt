Bread n Butter Pickles
Onions; thinly sliced, Green bell peppers; finely, Turmeric; ground, Salt, Cider vinegar, Cinnamon, Celery seed, Cucumbers; unpeeled-thinly, Sugar, 
In enamel or stainless-steel pot, mix cucumbers, onions and salt. Let stand  over night. Drain; discard liquid. Add remaining ingredients; bring to a  boil; reduce heat. Simmer 20 minutes. Ladle into hot sterilized jars to 1/4  inch from top; seal. Process 15 minutes in a boiling-water bath if desired.  Makes 4 pints.
