Apricot Slush
(12-oz) frozen orange juice, (12-oz) frozen lemonade, (46-oz) pineapple juice, (46-oz) apricot nectar, Vodka, Apricot brandy, 7-Up, 
Combine juices & liquors in a large bowl. Place in freezer. Stir after  about 4 hours. Freeze well, about 24 hours. Fill glass about 1/2 to 2/3  full of frozen mixture. Fill with 7-Up & serve. Wonderful summertime drink.    MADGE SABA    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
