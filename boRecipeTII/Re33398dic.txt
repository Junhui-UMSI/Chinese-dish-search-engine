Basic White Sauce and Cheese Sauce
White pepper, Arrowroot;, Evaporated Milk;, Salt, WHITE SAUCE, CHEESE SAUCE, Low-fat Cheese sauce, 
Heat butter in a small non-stick skillet, then in arrowroot.  Gradually add milk, cook stirring until sauce thickens.  Stir in salt  & pepper. VARIATIONS: THICKER SAUCE...Add more butter and more  arrowroot for each cup of milk.    CHEESE SAUCE: Add 1/4 cup shredded low-fat cheese to hot sauce; cook,  stirring, until melted.
