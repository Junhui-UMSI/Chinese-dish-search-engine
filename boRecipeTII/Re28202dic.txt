Baked Filet of Sole
Butter, Jar sliced mushrooms; (4oz), Egg; well beaten, Salt, Salt and pepper, Sole filets, Fine bread crumbs, Parsley and lemon slices, milk, Pecan pieces, 
Saute mushrooms in melted butter until mushrooms are lightly browned. Stir  in chopped pecans; sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper. Spread mixture on  filets. Roll tightly and skewer with toothpicks. Combine eggs, milk and  salt. Dip fish in egg mixture then roll in bread crumbs. Place in well  greased 8" square baking dish. Bake in preheated 400 degree oven about 20  minutes or until fish flakes easily. 4-6 serv. Garnish with parsley and  lemon slices.
