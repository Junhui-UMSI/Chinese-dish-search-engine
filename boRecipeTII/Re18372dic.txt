Very Green Casserole
Almonds; slivered & toasted, (16-oz) lima beans, (17-oz) green peas, Mustard, Green onion w/top; chopped, Mayonnaise, Hard boiled egg; chopped, French style green beans;, 
Drain vegetables, mix with remaining ingredients, cover with almonds. Bake  in 1-1/2 quart casserole at 350  until heated thoroughly and bubbling.    MRS J.L.
