Baked Beans
DPQ sauce; (recipe below), DPQ SAUCE, Basil, Salt, Catsup, Vegetable oil, Garlic powder, Pepper, Crushed red pepper or red, Dry minced onion, Dr Pepper, Lemon juice, 
Mix together in casserole dish and bake uncovered at 350? for one hour.    DPQ SAUCE: Blend together on stir speed of blender for 2 minutes. Store in  tightly sealed container and refrigerate. Shake well before using. (Will  keep for several weeks in refrigerator.)    Yield: 4 1/2 cups  Posted to brand-name-recipes by Ann_Bartholomew@dpsu.com on Feb 6, 1998
