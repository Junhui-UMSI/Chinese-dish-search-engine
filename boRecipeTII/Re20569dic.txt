Apple Cider Dressing
Apple juice; frozen, Salt, Water, Light sour cream, Dijon mustard, Cider vinegar, Finely shredded apple, Pepper, Chopped fresh parsley, 
Mix all ingredients with fork or wire whisk. About 1 cup dressing    Sources Betty Crocker New Choices Cookbook computer program    Recipe by: Betty Crocker New Choices Cookbook computer program    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #990 by Carol & Bob Floyd  <c.floyd@arnprior.com> on Jan 05, 1998
