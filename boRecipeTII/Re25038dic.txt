Tomato Sauce with Herbs
olive oil, Marjoram, Basil, Rosemary, Parsley, Cloves garlic -- minced, Ed, Oregano, Tomatoes -- cored/halved/see, Bell peppers -- chopped, 
In a skillet, heat oil over moderate heat.  Add garlic and peppers until  tender.  Add tomatoes, basil, parsley, oregano, marjoram, and rosemary.  Cook just until tomatoes juices thicken, 7 to 10 minutes.  Pour over  cooked pasta.
