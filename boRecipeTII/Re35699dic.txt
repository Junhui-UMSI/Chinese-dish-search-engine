Stuffed Breast of Veal
Hot water, Chopped Apple, Soft Bread Crumbs, Breast Of Veal, Pepper, Medium Cracker Crumbs, Bacon Slices, Onions, Sausage, Salt, 
Have the butcher bone and prepare a breast of veal. saute sausage; add  chopped apples and onion; cook until soft. Season with salt and pepper.  Place bread crumbs and cracker crumbs in medium bowl. Add hot water and mix  well. Add seasoned sausage, apples and onion. Use this mixture to stuff the  breast of veal. Lay bacon slices over the top. Bake at 325? for 2 hours.    Serves 6    Source: "Mountain Measures" -- Junior League of Charleston, WV ed. 1974    billspa@icanect.net    Recipe by: Mrs. Robert W.
