Pumpkin Soup 4
Beef, Salt, Mixed Fresh Herbs, Onion, Split Peas, Fresh Pumpkin, Fresh Parsley, Chicken Stock, Black Pepper, Fresh Thyme, 
Rinse split peas in cold water then add to stock in a large, heavy pot. Add  pumpkin, onion, beef, salt and pepper, mixed herbs and thyme. Simmer  gently, partly covered, until peas and pumpkin are tender. Season to taste  with salt and pepper and serve hot, trimmed with parsley. 6 servings.
