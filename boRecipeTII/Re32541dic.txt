Sweet Gherkins
Sugar, Vinegar, Celery seed, gherkins, Whole allspice, cinnamon sticks, White mustard seeds, Cloves, 
Brine cukes:  dissolve 1/2 cup salt in 2 quarts water, pour the solution  over cukes, and let stand for 24 hours in a cool place. Drain. Put the  cukes in a clean crock. Dissolve 1 cup of the sugar in the vinegar nd add  spices tied in a bag.  Bring the vinegar to a boil. Pour the boiling hot  syrup over the cukes. Next day, drain off syrup and add 1 cup sugar, bring  to a rolling boil.  Pour the boiling hot syrup over the cukes. Repeat the  next day adding the last cup of sugar. Next day, drain the syrup from the  pickles and pack the pickles into scalded jars. Heat the syrup to a rolling  boil and fill the jars.  Remove air bubbles. Add more syrup if necessary.  1/8" head space. 10 minutes boiling water bath.    Shared By: Pat Stockett
