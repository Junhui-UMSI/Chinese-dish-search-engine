Pickled Beets
Onions, sliced, Beets, cooked, sliced, Lemon juice to taste, Sugar to taste, 
In large glass bowl or jar, place a layer of sliced beets, then a layer of  sliced onions.  Sprinkle with sugar and lemon juice. Repeat until beets and  onions are used up. Refrigerate overnight.
