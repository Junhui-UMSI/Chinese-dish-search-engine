Wine Cooler
Ice cubes  (opt.), Wine, Lemon-lime soft drink (e.g.,, 
Mix wine and soft drink. Pour into 8 oz glass. Add ice. (750 ml wine to 2  liter soft drink = 8 wine coolers you buy.)    This recipe makes wine coolers very similar to the ones you buy.   I usually use a cheap wine with lots of berry flavor.
