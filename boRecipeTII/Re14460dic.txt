Whole Wheat Spice Bread #2
All-purpose white flour, Ground cloves or nutmeg, Ground mace or cinnamon, Ground allspice or cardamom, Vegetable oil, Vanilla; optional, Baking Powder, Walnuts, Whole-wheat flour, Sugar, Salt, Eggs, 
From: nuferv@ohsu.edu (Virginia Nufer,PC,SON)    Date: 20 Aug 1994 13:05:26 -0400    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Grease well a 9-by-5-by-3-inch bread pan.  In a large bowl, place the dry ingredients and stir to blend well before  adding any lquids.  Beat the eggs slightly; add the oil and sugar and  continue to beat until well-mixed. Combine with the dry ingredients; add  the chopped nuts and the vanilla (optional); blend well. Pour into the  prepared pan and bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Remove from the pan  and cool on a wire rack. Yields 1 loaf.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
