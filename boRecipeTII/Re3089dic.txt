Peanut Pastry
Peanut butter; smooth, Cold water, Salt; pinch, Shortening, Flour, Baking Powder, 
Sift the flour, baking powder, and salt together. Cut in the shortening and  the peanut butter with a pastry blender. Add just enough water to make it  stick together. Chill. Roll out the pastry, fit it into the pan, prick all  over with a fork and bake in a 475 def oven for 8 to 10 minutes. Cool  before filling.
