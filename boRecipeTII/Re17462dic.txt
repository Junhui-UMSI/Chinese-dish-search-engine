Walnut-Cheese Balls
Sherry, Horseradish, Butter or margarine, sharp cheddar, cream cheese, Finely chopped walnuts, 
In small mixing bowl, soften cream cheese & blend in sherry. Add butter,  cheese spread & horseradish. Mix well. Cover & chill thoroughly about 4  hours. Shape into 1-inch balls & roll in walnuts. Refrigerate until serving  time. Makes about 30 balls.    FRAN SMITH    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
