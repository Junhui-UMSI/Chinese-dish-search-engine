Quick Frozen Raspberry Dessert with Strawberries (Mf)
Rasberries, Orange juice, Fresh strawberries, Orange liqueur, 
Place frozen berries (unthawed) with orange juice and liqueur in food  processor. If you are ready to serve, then fold fresh strawberries into  this and serve in glasses. If you make this in advance, transfer mix to a  metal bowl and freeze until ready to eat; you dont want this rock hard; if  it is, reprocess in processor, then fold into berries.
