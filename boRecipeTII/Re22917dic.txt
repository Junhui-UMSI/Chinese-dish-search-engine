Ardmore Cheese Dip
MAYONNAISE RECIPE, Cornstarch, Sugar, Dry mustard, Mayonnaise, Egg, Vinegar, Worcestershire, Salt, Butter, Pecans, (4-oz) pimiento; cut fine, Tabasco, Salt, Water, Onion, (8-oz) cream cheese, 
Mix ingredients for mayonnaise and cook over low heat until thick, stirring  constantly.  Mix other ingredients and add mayonnaise.  Keep in  refrigerator and shape into ball before serving. Keeps well.
