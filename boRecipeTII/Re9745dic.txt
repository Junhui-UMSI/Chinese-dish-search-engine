Pumpkin Ring
Eggs, Margarine or butter,, (16 oz) pumpkin, GLAZE, milk, Powdered sugar, Pumpkin pie spice, milk, Bisquick baking mix, Brown sugar, Granulated sugar, Vanilla, 
Heat oven to 350.  Grease and flour 12-cup bundt cake pan or angel food  cake pan, 10 x 4 inches. Beat all ingredients on low speed for 30 seconds,  scraping bowl constantly. Beat on medium speed 3 minutes, scraping bowl  occasionally.  Spread in pan. Bake about 50 minutes or until toothpick  inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pan. Cool  completely.    Beat glaze ingredients.  Drizzle over cooled cake. Submitted By RHOMMEL  <RHOMMEL@IX.NETCOM.COM>  On WED, 15 NOV 1995 172444 ~0500
