Banana Pudding Parfaits
Vanilla wafers, Bananas, Frozen whipped topping, Wafers, vanilla instant, 
submitted by: rstewart@infobahn.icubed.com    Prepare vanilla pudding according to package directions.  Layer pudding,  crushed vanilla wafers, banana slices, and whipped topping into parfait  glasses, ending with a dollop of whipped topping. Refrigerate at least 1  hour. Garnish, if desired. Makes 6 servings.    DAVE <DAVIDG@CLAM.RUTGERS.EDU>    RECIPEINTERNET LIST SERVER    RECIPE ARCHIVE - 15 APRIL 1996    From the RECIPEinternet: Recipes from Around the World recipe list.
