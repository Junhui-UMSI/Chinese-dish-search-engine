Artichoke Sauce
Bread crumbs, Artichoke hearts; sliced, Romano cheese, White pepper, Salt, Cayenne pepper, garlic, Thyme, Black pepper, Water, olive oil, Bay Leaves, 
Place the water, olive oil, and seasonings in a large saucepan. Add the  artichokes and garlic and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in the breadcrumbs  and cheese and heat thoroughly. Reserve until the final assembly.    LE RUTHS        636    FRANKLIN ST., NEW ORLEANS    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of French Recipes>.  Downloaded from  G Internet, G Internet.
