Pepper-Cheese Spread
Green pepper; minced, (8-oz) cream cheese, Onion; minced, Tabasco, Mayonnaise, Salt to taste, Worcestershire Sauce, Lemon juice, 
Mix all ingedients well. Chill overnight.    CHILL OVERNIGHT    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
