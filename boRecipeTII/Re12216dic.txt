Zucchini Casserole
Sliced zucchini, Shredded sharp chedder, Butter, Sour cream, Pkg. herb seasoned stuffing, Onions, Can cream of chicken soup, 
Cook zucchini in amount of water until just tender, crisp. Do not over  cook-drain and combine other ingredients. Combine stuffing mix with melted  butter. Put half in bottom of buttered casserole dish. Put zucchini mixture  over this. Top with remaining stuffing mix. Bake 350 1 hour.
