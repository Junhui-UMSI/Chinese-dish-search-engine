White Sauce (Bechamel)
Bay leaf, nutmeg, Cloves, milk, Onion, -(up to), Flour, Butter, 
Melt butter over low heat.  Add and blend in flour over low heat for 3 to 5  minutes.  Slowly stir in milk. Add onion studded with cloves and bay leaf.  Cook and stir the sauce with a wirw whisk or wooden spoon until thickened  and smooth. Place in a 350-degree oven for 20 minutes to slowly cook.  Strain the sauce and season to taste. Add a grating of nutmeg.
