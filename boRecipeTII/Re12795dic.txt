Yum Yum Cake #2
(small) evaporated milk, Sugar, Sugar, Vanilla, Baking soda, Pecans, Flour, Coconut, Eggs, Crushed pineapple, Stick butter or margarine, ICING, 
From:    Pam and KerryAnn Cobb <priss@AMARANTH.COM>    Date:    Mon, 5 Aug 1996 04:44:49 -0500  Mix all ingredients together, bake in a large sheet cake pan at 350 about  20 minutes or until a golden brown.    Icing: Boil sugar, butter and milk for 2 minutes.  Remove from heat and add  coconut, pecans, and vanilla.  Mix well, pour on hot cake.    EAT-L Digest  4 August 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
