Baked Brie Spread
Bacon; fried and crumbled, Rounds brie cheese, Chutney, 
Spread chutney on cheese; top with bacon. Bake at 350 degrees for 20  minutes.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
