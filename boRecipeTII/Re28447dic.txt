Baked Oatmeal
milk, Baking Powder, Oatmeal, Salt, Chunky applesauce, Topping, Eggs, Vanilla yogurt, Brown sugar, 
Preheat oven to 325o F    In a large bowl combine the oatmeal, baking powder and salt. Mix in milk,  eggs, oil, brown sugar. Stir and pour into a greased casserole dish. Bake  at 325 for 45 minutes. Serve hot, topping each with vanilla yogurt.    Notes: A delicious hot treat on a cold morning. Convenient to prepare ahead  of time. Every time I serve it, Im asked for the recipe.
