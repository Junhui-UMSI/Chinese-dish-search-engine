Apple Brownies
Cinnamon, Baking soda, Sugar, Apples; chopped and peeled, Walnuts, Flour, Egg, Margarine, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Melt margarine in microwave or pan. Combine  other ingredients and mix with margarine. Pour into greased 8 x 8 inch  dish. Bake for approximately 40 minutes. Cool and cut into squares.    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Feb 18, 1998
