Asian Mayonnaise
Fresh ginger (optional), Scallion; thinly sliced, Non-fat mayonnaise, Rice vinegar, 5 spice powder, 
Combine all of the ingredients and season to taste with salt and pepper.  Put this on your grilled chicken or turkey sandwich or burger. Posted to  Digest eat-lf.v097.n205 by "Tina D. Bell" <tdbell@altair.csustan.edu> on  Aug 13, 1997
