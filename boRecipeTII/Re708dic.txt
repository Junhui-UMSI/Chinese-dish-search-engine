Green Chili Stew
Ground beef, Salt, Water, Potatoes, Salt, Onions, Green chili, Tomatoes, 
Fry meat and drain fat, add salt, pepper, water, onions, tomatoes, and  potatoes.  On low heat, cover and boil until potatoes are soft.
