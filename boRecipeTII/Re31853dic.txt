Szechwan Spiced Bean Curd (Mapo Tofu)
Chicken broth, Dark soy sauce, Sesame oil, Cooking oil, Hot oil, Sugar, MSG, Ground pork, bean curd, Water, Fermented black beans;, Salt, Szechwan hot pepper sauce, Cornstarch, scallion, Szechwan pepper powder, Peas, garlic, 
Heat a wok over medium high heat until hot, add the oil, wait until it is  hot, then add the meat. Stir-fry the meat until it is no longer pink. Add  the sauce and bring to a boil. Add the bean curd [and peas], bring to a  simmer, and cook for 2 minutes. Pour in the cornstarch mixture and stir  until the sauce has thickened. Turn off the heat and sprinkle with the  garnish. Serve immediately.    FWB@URSA.SIEMENS.COM    (FREDERIC W. BREHM)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
