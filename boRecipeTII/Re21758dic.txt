Apple-Banana Crunch Pie
Sugar, 9" pastry shell; unbaked, Butter or margarine; soften, Apples; cut in chunks, Bananas; cut into chunks, Cinnamon, Lemon juice, Brown Sugar, Nutmeg, All purpose flour, 
Combine fruits and lemon juice; let stand 10 minutes. Combine flour, sugar  and spices; cut in butter till crumbly. Arrange fruit in pastry shell.  Sprinkle with crumb mixture. Bake at 400~F for 35-40 minutes. Cover top  lossely with foil if crumb mixture browns too quickly. Cut in wedges to  serve.
