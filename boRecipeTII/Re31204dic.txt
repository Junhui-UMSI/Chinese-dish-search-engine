Banana-Chocolate Tea Bread
Butter -- softened, Cinnamon, Banana -- mashed, Semisweet, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Cocoa, Eggs, Miniature chocolate chips --, Sugar, Sour cream, Walnuts -- chopped, Salt, Vanilla extract, 
Cream butter; gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy. Add  eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.    Combine flour, cocoa, soda, salt, and cinnamon; sift together. Stir flour  mixture into egg mixture, blending well; add vanilla. Stir in banana, sour  cream, walnuts, and chocolate morsels.    Spoon batter into two greased and floured 7-1/2 x 3 x 2-inch loafpans. Bake  at 350 degrees F. for 55 minutes or until a wooden pick inserted in center  comes out clean.    Cool in pans 10 minutes; remove from pans. and cool completely on a wire  rack.    Yield: 2 loaves    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
