Wild Rice Casserole
Onion, sliced, (4-oz) (1/2 cup) sliced, Uncooked wild rice, Margarine, Salt, Thyme leaves, Water, 
Recipe donated by Tim Burton, California    1.Heat oven to 350F.    2. In a 2-quart casserole, combine all ingredients.    3. Bake covered at 350F for 1-1/4 to 1-1/2 hours or until rice is tender,  stirring occasionally during last half hour.
