Tofu Stuffed Shells
Jumbo shells, Jar prepared spaghetti, Fatfree mozerella cheese;, Block reduced fat tofu, 
Cook the shells as direced. Mix the tofu and cheese together and stuff each  shell with the mixture. Place in baking pan seam side down and cover with  sauce. Bake until bubbly.
