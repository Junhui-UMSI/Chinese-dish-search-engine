Two Nana Zucchini
Red wine vinegar, Minced, Zucchini -- thinly sliced, Garlic Cloves -- finely, Vegetable oil, Mint Leaves, 
1.  Fry slices, single layer at a time, until lightly browned.  Remove  slices as cooked, draining slightly, to a serving dish.    2.  As each batch is cooked, sprinkle with salt, pepper, minced garlic,  mint, and a splash of red wine vinegar. Serve warm, room temp, or cold with  crackers.  Delicious!
