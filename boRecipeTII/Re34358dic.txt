Beahms Ozark Pudding
Baking Powder, Pecans, Salt, Flour, Sugar, Can crushed pineapples*;, Egg, Chopped Granny Smith apples, Vanilla, 
Beat eggs and sugar until smooth. Mix flour, baking powder and salt, then  stir into the egg-sugar mixture. Add apples, nuts, and vanilla and  pineapple if preferred. Bake in buttered pie pan in 350 degree oven for 30  minutes. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream or frozen yogurt. busted by  sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 23, 1998
