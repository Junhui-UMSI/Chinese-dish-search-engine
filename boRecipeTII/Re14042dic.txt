White Chocolate Macadamia Nut Cookies
Vanilla extract, Sugar, Brown sugar, All-purpose flour, Macadamia nuts, Butter or margarine, Egg, White chocolate, 
Rich, slightly crunchy dropped cookies bursting with macadamia nuts and  white chocolate chunks.    1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (170 degrees C). Lightly grease baking  sheets. =A0    2. Beat butter, sugars, egg, and vanilla at medium speed of electric mixer  until fluffy.    3. At low speed, gradually beat in flour, just until blended. By hand, stir  in nuts and chocolate. =A0    4. Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls, 2 1/2 inches apart, onto lightly greased  cookie sheets. =A0    5. Bake for 17 minutes or until edges of cookies are lightly browned and  tops look dry. Allow to cool on baking sheets five minutes, then remove to  racks to cool completely. Makes 2 dozen    Posted to T.nt (sdm-marked) - Prodigys Recipe Exchange Newsletter  by  MULVEY_LYNDA@Tandem.COM on 97
