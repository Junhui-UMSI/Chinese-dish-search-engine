Kailan in Oyster Sauce
Kailan (washed & cleaned);, Cooking oil, Garlic, Oyster sauce, 
Blanch the Kailan vegetable and keep warm. Heat up wok and add cooking oil.  Fry the garlic until light brown and stir in oyster sauce. Pour the sauce  on top of the vegetable. For decoration, sprinkle with fried onions.
