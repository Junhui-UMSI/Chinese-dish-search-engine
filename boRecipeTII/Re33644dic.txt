Swedish Sprits
Salt, Vanilla Flavoring, Flour, Butter, Egg yolks, Powdered sugar, Almond Flavoring, 
Mix well. Using cookie press make various designs and decorate with colored  sugar. Bake in moderate oven about 10 minutes.
