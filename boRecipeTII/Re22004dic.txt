Turkey Chili Casserole
Bell peppers; chopped, No yolks egg noodle, Skim milk; at room temp, Prepared mustard, Fat-free parmesan cheese, Margarine; melted, Turkey light meat, skinless, Unbleached flour, Onions; chopped, Bread crumbs, 
Recipe by: The Weight Watchers Complete Cookbook & Program Basics  Preparation Time: 0:15 Preheat oven to 375. Prepare a 2-quart casserole  dish with cooking spray; set aside. In a saucepan, melt margarine over  medium-high heat; sprinkle with flour. Cook, whisking quickly and  constantly, for 2 minutes. Whisk in milk and Parmesan cheese. Cook stirring  constantly for another 2 minutes until thickened. Remove from heat. Add  noodles, turkey, onions, bell peppers, and mustard to saucepan; toss to mix  well. Spoon mixture into prepared dish and sprinkle with bread crumbs. Bake  for 20 minutes or until browned and bubbly.
