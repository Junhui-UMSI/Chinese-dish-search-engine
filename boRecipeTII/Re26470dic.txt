Tomato Mustard
Salt, Sugar, White wine vinegar, Dry white wine, Dry mustard, Egg yolks, 
Follow recipe for Homemade Mustard; adding 1 tsp paprika, 1 TBL drained and  chopped pimento, and 1/4 cup tomato paste with egg yolks. Serve with  seafood, hamburgers, frankfurters, or baked ham.
