Barbecued Spareribs #2
Salt, Tomato ketchup, Pork spareribs, Vinegar, Lemon; thin, Celery seed, Worcestershire Sauce, Brown sugar, Chili powder, Grated or sliced onion, SAUCE, Water, 
Fill flat baking pan with spareribs, fat side up. Place thin slice of lemon  on each rib. Cover all with grated or sliced onion. Bake at 450 for 45  minutes. Mix all sauce ingredients in saucepan & bring to a boil. Pour over  browned meat. Reduce oven to 350. Cover pan with foil & bake for 1 hour.  Remove foil; bake for 30 minutes. Serves 6.
