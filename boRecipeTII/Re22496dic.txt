Applesauce Loaf
Salt, Pecans, Shortening, Baking Powder, Flour, Sugar, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Eggs, Nutmeg, Sweetened applesauce, 
Mix shortening to soften. Gradually add sugar, creaming till light. Add  eggs; beat till light and fluffy. Sift together dry ingredients. Add to  mixture alternately with applesauce beating after each addition. Stir in  nuts. Pour in to loaf pan. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
