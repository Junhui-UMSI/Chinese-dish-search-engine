Venison Hash
Chopped chiles (optional), Green pepper, diced, Onions, diced, Salt, Chili powder, Red pepper, diced, 16 oz. tomatoes, Ground venison, 
Preheat over to 350F.  In large skillet cook and stir  venison, onions, and peppers until meat is brown and  vegetables tender.  Drain off the fat and stir in  tomatoes, salt, pepper, chili powder, red pepper and  chiles. Heat through and pour into covered casserole  dish. Bake 1 hour stirring a couple times while  cooking.
