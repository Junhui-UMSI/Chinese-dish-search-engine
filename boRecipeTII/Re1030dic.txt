Coca-Cola Chicken
onions, Apricot jam, Soy sauce, Ketchup, Coca-Cola, 
Source: My friend, Solange    This sauce is for about 4 - 6 chicken pieces    Cut up chicken and put it in a roasting pan. Layer the onions on the  chicken and pour over the sauce. Bake for about 1 1 /2 hour at 180.    You can serve this with rice or add small pieces of potatoes to the chicken  and bake together.
