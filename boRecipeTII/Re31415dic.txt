Barbecue Beef Sandwiches
Hamburger buns, Flour, Lemon juice, Chili powder, Garlic, chopped fine, Dry mustard, Beef instant bouillon, Brown Sugar, Tomato sauce, Beef rump roast, Onions-chopped,
