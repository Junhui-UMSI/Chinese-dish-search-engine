Barbecue Sauce
Ketchup, Cayenne pepper, Black pepper, Butter; two sticks, Sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, Mustard, Vinegar, 
Melt butter and mix in sugar, then mix all other ingredients together.  Bring to a boil, cut back heat and simmer, stirring constantly, for 8-10  minutes, or until desired consistency is reached.    Source "Nothing makes a soup moment like a rainy day" By ANN BURGER, Post  and Courier Food Editor, Sun, 08-Feb-1998.  http://www.charleston.net/pub/entertain/cookin/soup0208.htm    Notes: Linda says she has a great barbecue sauce recipe that her  father-in-law shared. If you want a milder sauce, you can cut the amounts  of red and black pepper in half.
