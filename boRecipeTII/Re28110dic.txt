Baked Corn Dish
oleo, Jiffy corn bread mix, cheese, Creamed corn  , Whole kernel corn  , Eggs, 
Melt oleo and add corns. Add 1 cup cheese, eggs and corn bread mix. Put in  greased pan (8x8) and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to an hour. Posted  to EAT-L Digest  by Phyllis Braun <pbraun@IDIR.NET> on Dec 23, 1997
