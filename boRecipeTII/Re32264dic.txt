Barbecups
Barbecue sauce, Your favorite biscuits, Brown sugar, Minced onions, Shredded chedar, Ground beef, 
Servings: makes 6 cups.    Brown groundbeef in large skilet. Drain and add barbecue sauce, onions  brown sugar. Sat aside. Separate and place 6 biscuits in muffin tins  pressing dough up sides of cups. Spoon in meat mixture. Sprinkle with  cheese. Bake at 400 for 10 to 12 min.
