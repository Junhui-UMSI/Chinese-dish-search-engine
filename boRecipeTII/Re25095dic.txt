Tomato, Corn, Basil and Pasta Salad (Hl)
frozen corn, Pasta, basil, olive oil, salt and pepper, tomatoes, vinaigrette, Red wine vinegar, Mustard, 
Combine vinaigrette ingredients in a small jar and shake to mix.    In your hot pot, cook pasta until al dente and then add frozen corn. Strain  all in colander and let sit in sink.    In another bowl, combine chopped tomatoes, basil and vinaigrette. Pour the  mixture over the pasta and toss lightly. Can be served hot or cold.
