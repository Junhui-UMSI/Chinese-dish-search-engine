Zesty Corn Relish
Bell Pepper, Chopped, Onion, Cornstarch, Corn, Whole Kernel, Drained, Sweet Relish, Celery seed, Sugar, Mustard, Prepared, Pimento, Chopped, Drained, Vinegar, 
In a medium saucepan, combine all ingredients; mix well. Bring to a boil  and boil for 1-2 minutes or until clear and thickened, stir occasionally.  Refrigerate for several hours to blend flavors. Store in refrigerator for  up to 3 weeks.  From: Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sydsbook.zip
