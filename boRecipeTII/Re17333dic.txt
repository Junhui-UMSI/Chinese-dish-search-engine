Walking Tortillas
Sliced olives, Avocado dip, Shredded mild Cheddar cheese, Diced tomatoes, Refried beans, Green chiles, Sour cream, 
Layer on serving platter in order given above.  To serve, place tortilla  chips around the edge in an upright position.    From a book of my mothers (Judy Hosey) titled <Senator Richard Matsuuras  Favorite Recipes>, mostly Japanese and Hawaiian.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
