White Chip Chocolate Cookies
White chocolate chips, Granulated sugar, Unbleached flour, Margarine, Baking soda, Brown Sugar, Salt, Egg white, 
Preheat oven to 350. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugars, baking  soda, and salt. In another mixing bowl, combine margarine, egg white,  and chocolate chips. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto unprepared  baking sheet. Bake for 12 minutes or until edges are light brown.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
