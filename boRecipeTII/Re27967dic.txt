Three Cheese Potato Fluff
Parmesan cheese, Bag Ore-Ida mashed potatoes,, Sharp cheddar cheese,, milk, Onion, Swiss cheese, shredded, Salt, Black Pepper, Scallions, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 375?. Add milk to the prepared potatoes, cheeses,  scallions, onions, salt and pepper and egg yolks. Mix together thoroughly  until well blended. In a small bowl with mixer on high speed, whip the egg  whites until stiff peak form. Gently fold beaten egg whites into mashed  potato mixture. Spoon the mixture into a greased 3" deep casserole dish.  Bake about 50 minutes, or until puffed and golden.
