Veggie Crab Cakes
Grated onion, Egg substitute or egg whites, Old Bay seasoning, Seasoned bread crumbs, Zucchini, grated (no need to, Fat-free mayo, 
Put grated zucchini on a towel and squeeze out all of the water. Mix all  ingredients together and form into patties with your hands. Cook in a  non-stick skillet ontil golden brown on both sides and done in the middle.    We had these with salsa, but you could make any kind of sauce, such as  yogurt-cucumber, and they would be just as good.
