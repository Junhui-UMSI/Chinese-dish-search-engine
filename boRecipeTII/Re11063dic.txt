Rainbow Punch
Chilled lemon-lime soda or, Chilled red fruit punch, Lemon sherbet; (or, 
Start with 6 tall juice glasses. Pour 1/2 cup juice into each glass. Place  about 2/3cups sherbet in each glass. Fill rest of glass with soda. Serve.    I have made this in larger portions by increasing recipe; preparing it in a  punch bowl; and putting scoops of sherbet about the bowl rather than one  big clump.
