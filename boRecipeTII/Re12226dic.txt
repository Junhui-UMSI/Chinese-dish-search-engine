Zucchini Chocolate Cake 2
Cocoa, Nutmeg, Sugar, Orange Peel, Vanilla, Flour, Buttermilk, Eggs, Cinnamon, Salt, Baking Powder, Zucchini, grated, Vegetable oil, Baking soda, Nuts, chopped, 
Sift together the first 7 ingredients and set aside. Beat together the  eggs, sugar and oil. Add buttermilk, zucchini, vanilla and orange peel and  mix. Add flour mixture and nuts. Bake in 9" x 13" greased and floured pan.  Bake for 50-60 minutes at 350 degrees. Frost with cream cheese frosting.    Recipe by: Stevi Posted to EAT-L Digest by "Iris E.
