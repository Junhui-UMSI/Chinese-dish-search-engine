Pizza Quiche
Italian sausage, Mozzarella cheese, milk, Ham, Grated Parmesan cheese, egg, Pie pastry, Salami, pepperoni, Ricotta cheese, Eggs, 
Line a 9 inch pie plate with half the pastry.  Dont poke holes in the  crust.  Bake in a 450 degree oven for 4 to 5 minutes.  Remove. Reduce  oven temperature to 350 degrees.  Beat Ricotta and 3 eggs.  Fold in  Italian sausage, Mozzarella, pepperoni, ham, salami and Parmesan.  Turn into partially baked pastry shell.  Foll out remaining pastry to  an 8 inch cirle and cut into 6 to 8 wedges.  Place the wedges atop  the fillng.  Bake in a 350 degree oven for 2 minutes.  Combine egg  and milk; brush over the pastry wedges.  Continue baking for 20 to 25  minutes more until golden brown.  Let cool and set 10 minutes before  serving.
