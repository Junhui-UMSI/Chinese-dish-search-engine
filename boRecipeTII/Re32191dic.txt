Barbecued Rainbow Trout
Tomatoes, salt and pepper, Lemon juice, rainbow trout, Onion, 
Fill cavity of fish with chopped onion and tomatoes. Sprinkle lemon  juice over fish, along with salt and pepper. Wrap fish in foil. Barbecue  fish approximately 10 minutes on each side....    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mm-trout.zip
