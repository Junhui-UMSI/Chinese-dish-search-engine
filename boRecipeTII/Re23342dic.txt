Tuna Vegetable Pasta Salad
Mayo, Dill, Mustard, Pepper, Tomato, Italian dressing, Cucumbers, Salt, Tuna, Rotini, Green onins, Green pepper, Celery, 
Prepare rotini. Drain. In large bowl combine pasta, tuna and veggies. In  small bowl combine dressing, mayo, mustard and spices. Add to salad  mixture. Toss to coat. Chill and serve.
