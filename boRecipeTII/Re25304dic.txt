Torrejas De Maiz Tierno (Fresh Corn Fritters)
Salt, Flour, all-purpose, Oil, vegetable, Sugar, brown, light, Eggs, Habanero hot sauce, Cheese, cheddar; grated, HEAT SCALE = MILD, Corn, sweet; cut from the, 
Gently but thoroughly combine the corn and its liquid with the flour,  sugar, salt and hot sauce. Blend in the egg and cheese. Stir the batter  well. Heat the oil to 265F and drop in the batter by teaspoonfuls. Fry  until golden brown on all sides. Drain on paper towels and serve very hot.
