Asparagus with Black-Bean Sauce (Brennan)
Vegetable oil, Sherry, Minced fresh ginger, Asparagus; diagonally cut, Cornstarch, STIR FRY, Garlic, SAUCE, Soy sauce, Fermented black beans, 
Mix together a tablespoon or two of fermented black beans, a clove of  minced garlic, a little soy sauce, minced fresh ginger, sherry and  cornstarch to make a sauce. Cut asparagus spears into 1-inch diagonal  slices.    Heat a little oil in a wok and stir- fry the mixture for a minute or two,  until the asparagus is tender, but still bright green. Pour in the sauce  and stir for a minute or two longer.
