Baked Chicken with Provencal Couscous
Chicken Breast, skinless, Zucchini, olive oil, Cannellini, canned, couscous, Near East Roasted Garlic, Parmesan Cheese, Black Pepper, Dried Rosemary, wine, Robert Mondavi Coastal, Salt, 
Place chicken breasts halves, bone sides down, in a baking dish; rub olive  oil on chicken.    Sprinkle evenly with salt, pepper, and 1 t rosemary; cover with aluminum  foil. Bake at 350 degree for 20 minutes; uncover and bake 20 minutes or  until chicken is cooked.    Combine 3/4 c water, wine, zucchini, beans, and rosemary in a large  saucepan; bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 5 minutes or until  zucchini is tender. Stir in couscous, cover, and remove from heat. Let  stand 5 minutes, add Parmesan, and fluff with fork. Serve with chicken.    Suggested Wine: Robert Mondavi Coastal Chardonnay    Recipe by: Cooking Light J/F 98, Near East Ad    Posted to recipelu-digest by Jill & Joe Proehl <jpxtwo@swbell.net> on Mar  02, 1998
