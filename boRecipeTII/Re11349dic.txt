Ranchera Sauce
Cumin, Water, Ranchera  roux, Salt, Basil, Bay leaf; ground, Tomato paste, Lard; or salad oil, Cl Garlic; pureed, Garlic powder, Brown onion, Marjoram; ground, Chicken base, Flour; all purpose, Bell pepper chopped, Lard;  or salad oil, Celery chopped, 
1. HEAT THE LARD IN A STOCK POT, ADD THE GARLIC AND SAUTE OVER MEDIUM HEAT  UNTIL LIGHT BROWN (ABOUT 5 MIN.). ADD THE WATER. 2. ADD THE TOMATO PASTE,  CHICKEN BASE, SALT AND SPICES, STIRRING UNTIL THE TOMATO PASTE IS  COMPLETELY DISSOLVED. BRING TO A BOIL AND SIMMER ON LOW ABOUT 20 MIN.  RANCHERA ROUX: WHEN THE SAUCE HAS ALMOST FINISHED SIMMERING, MELT THE LARD  IN A SAUCEPAN AND ADD THE FLOUR, STIRRING CONSTANTLY OVER MED. HEAT UNTIL  THE MIXTURE IS LIGHT BROWN (ABOUT 5 MIN.)  WHEN THE SAUCE HAS FINISHED  SIMMERING, SLOWLY ADD THE ROUX TO THE SAUCE, WHIPPING CONSTANTLY TO  DISSOLVE THE ROUX INTO THE SAUCE WITHOUT LUMPING. aDD THE VEGETABLES AND  RETURN THE SAUCE TO A BOIL.  SIMMER 20 MIN. OVER LOW HEAT. GOOD ON THE RICE  AND OTHER MEXICAN DISHES.
