White Spam and Garlic Pizza
Oven until done., Cheese  Bake in 450 degree F, Start with Boboli brand, Ricotta cheese. Mince Spam, Premade pizza crust or other, Layer on Mozzerella cheese., Sprinkle Spam and garlic, On grated Romano, Processors until pasty., Dollop on spoonfuls, Layer on Provalone cheese., And garlic in food, Paste over pizza. Sprinkle, Favorite crust.,
