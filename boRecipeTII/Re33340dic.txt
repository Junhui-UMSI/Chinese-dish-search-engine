Basic Sushi Rice
Salt, Calrose rice, Sugar, Water, Japanese rice vinegar, 
Date: Sat, 16 Mar 1996 02:31:07 GMT    From: gemini@synapse.net (Georgette & Dave Burnside)    Wash rice and rinse well. Add 3 cups water and cook in rice cooker. To cook  on stove, bring rice to a boil in a covered saucepan. Simmer for 15  minutes. Remove pan from stove and let sit for another 10-15 minutes. Mix  together vinegar, sugar, and salt, and cook over medium heat until sugar  dissolves. Cool. Place cooked rice in a large mixing bowl. Sprinkle half of  vinegar sauce over the hot rice; mix gently. Taste. Add more vinegar sauce  if desired. Makes 9 cups. All types of sushi can be made from this rice.
