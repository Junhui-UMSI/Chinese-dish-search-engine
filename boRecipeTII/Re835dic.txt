Hidden Mushroom And Bacon Wraps
7 oz. can Pillsbury Refrig., Slices bacon, cooked, cut, Green Giant Whole Mushrooms, (from 4.5 oz. jar),drained, 
Heat oven to 450?F.  Grease cookie sheets.  Press or roll each biscuit into  a 3 inch circle. Place 1 piece bacon on each biscuit; top with 1 mushroom  and 1 additional bacon piece. Wrap each biscuit to enclose; pinch edges to  seal.  Place, seam side down, on greased cookie sheet.    Bake at 450?F for 7 to 10 minutes or until golden brown. Let stand 5  minutes before serving.  Serve warm with barbecue sauce or mustard as  dipping sauce; if desired. Yield: 10 appetizers Typed in MMFormat by  cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Pillsbury Holiday Party Recipes.
