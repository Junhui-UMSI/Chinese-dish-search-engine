Swordfish Delmonico
Flour, Minced parsley (for, Garnish), Dry sherry, Cooked shrimp, milk, Snapper, salt and pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, Crab meat, Butter, Swordfish, halibut, bass,, 
Melt butter in top of double boiler directly on cooking surface; add  flour slowly, stirring constantly. Place top over boiling water; add  milk & continue stirring until sauce thickens. Add cheese, salt,  pepper & sherry, mixing thoroughly. Place fillets, crab & shrimp in  greased baking dish; pour sauce over fish. Cover & bake in 350 oven  for 30 minutes or until bubbly. Garnish with parsley just before  serving.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
