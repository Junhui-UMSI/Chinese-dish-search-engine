Baked Beans
Onion -- chopped, Navy beans -- (dry), Salt, Dry mustard, Pepper, Cold water, Molasses, Brown Sugar, Salt pork -- diced, 
Rinse beans and soak overnight.  Drain water and add 8 cups water to beans.    Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 1 1/2 to 2 hours or  until beans are tender.  Drain beans and reserve liquid.  In crockpot  combine drained beans, onion, and salt pork.  Add 1 cup drained bean  liquid, molasses, brown sugar, dry mustard, pepper, and salt.  Cover and  cook on low for 10-12 hours or on high for 5-6 hours.  Stir before serving.    Origin unknown    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #024 by lena36@juno.com (Lena P Mitchell) on  Jan 23, 1998
