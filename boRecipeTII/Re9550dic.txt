Pumpkin Chip Muffins
Can pumpkin, All-purpose flour, Vegetable oil, Salt, Baking Powder, Eggs, Chocolate chips; (12 oz .), Ground cinnamon, Sugar, Baking soda, 
In a large mixing bowl, beat egg, sugar, pumpkin and oil until smooth.  Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon andsalt; add to pumpkin  mixture and mix well. Fold in chcoolate chips. Fill greased or paper-lined  muffin cups 3/4 full. Bake at 400 F. for 16-20 minutes or until muffins  test done. Cool in pan 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack. Yield:  about 24 standard-size muffins.
