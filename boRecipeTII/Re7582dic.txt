Pork Scallopini
Marsala or Maderia wine, Posted by Stewart Hopper, Boneless pork roast, Butter, Flour, 
Dredge lightly in flour thin slices of pork roast.  Saute in butter.  When juices begin to emerge on the upper side (after about 3  minutes), turn pork and saute for 3 minutes more until pork is done.  Remove from skillet.  Deglaze the pan juices with the wine.  Pour  sauce over pork slices and serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/iowapork.zip
