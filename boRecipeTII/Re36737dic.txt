Strawberry Heaven
Skim milk, Cut into 3 horizontal layers, Frozen strawberries; thawed, Angel food cake, Cool Whip. Free; thawed, 
Place bottom cake layer on serving platter. In a mixing bowl, stir crushed  strawberries and milk into 1 cup whipped topping. Spread half of the  strawberry mixture onto cake layer. Top with second cake layer. Repeat  layers, ending with cake. Frost top and sides of cake with remaining  whipped topping. Regrigerate 1 hour or until ready to serve.    Recipe By     : Cool Whip.
