Taboule Primavera
Chopped Tomatoes, Lemon juice, Feta cheese; crumbled, Black beans; rinsed/drained, Chopped fresh basil, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Pkg Taboule Wheat Salad Mix;, Carrots; shredded, 
Prepare taboule as directed on package; cover. Refrigeratoe 30 minutes.  Stir in remaining ingredients, except cheese, cover. Refrigerate at least 2  hours. Serve on a bed of lettuce. Sprinkle with cheese. Makes 4 servins.  Prep Time: 10 minutes, Refrigerate: 2 1/2 hours.
