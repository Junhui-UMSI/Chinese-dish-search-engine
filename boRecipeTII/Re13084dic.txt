Wisconsin Potato Cheese Soup
Onions, potatoes, Croutons, Paprika, Cheddar cheese, Pepper, milk, parsley, fresh, Butter or margarine, Chicken broth, Salt, Celery, 
In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium-high heat. Saute celery and  onion until tender.  Add potatoes and broth. Cover and simmer until  potatoes are tender, about 12 minutes. In batches, puree potato mixture in  a blender (or food processor).  Return to saucepan. Stir in milk and  seasonings.  Add the cheese and heat only until melted. Top with croutons  and garnish with parsley if desired.    (Recipe from an issue of "Country" magazine about a year or two ago. It was  the midwest contest winner for Darlene Alexander of Nekoosa, Wisconsin.)
