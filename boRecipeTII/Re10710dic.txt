Quick Spoon Biscuits
Flour, milk, Miracle Whip or mayonnaise, 
OVEN TIME: 15 - 20 MINUTES TEMP: 375* F FEEDS: 3-4    All Measures are American Standard and all Temps are in degrees F.    Stir all ingredients together well. Drop by the spoonful into greased Dutch  Oven -or- greased cookie sheet. Bake until theyre golden brown    Posted by Steve Beluch to the Fidonet COOKING echo    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on May 25, 98
