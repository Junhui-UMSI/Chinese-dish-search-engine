Quick Beef Stew
Bay leaf, sliced carrots  , Salt, Vegetable oil, Celery salt, Beef gravy, Pepper, beef, Onion, green peas  , tiny white potatoes  , 
1. In a deep, 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole, heat oil in  Microwave Oven 2 minutes. 2. Add onion and heat, uncovered, in Microwave  Oven 3 minutes or until tender. Stir occasionally. 3. Add bay leaf,  carrots, potatoes, salt, pepper, celery salt, beef gravy and beef; stir to  combine. 4. Heat, covered, in Microwave Oven 6 to 7 minutes or until almost  heated. Taste and correct seasoning, if necessary, with additional salt and  pepper. 5. Remove bay leaf and discard. 6. Add peas and heat, covered, in  Microwave Oven 2 to 3 minutes or until heated through.
