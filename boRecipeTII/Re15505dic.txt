4th Graders Cranberry Bread
Raisins, Flour, Salt, Cranberry juice, Margarine; melted, Sugar, Cranberries; fresh, pulped, Egg, Baking Powder, Orange peel; grated, 
Blend all ingredients. Mixture will be lumpy.    Pour into a greased loaf pan.    Bake in a pre-heated 350 oven for 1 hour, 15 minutes, or until lightly  browned on top. - Mrs. Estudillos 4th Grade class    Flowery School, Sonoma, CA  1988    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
