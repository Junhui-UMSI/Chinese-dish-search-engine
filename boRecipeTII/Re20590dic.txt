Apple Cinnamon Muffins
Oat bran cereal, Whole wheat flour, Ground cinnamon, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Salt, Unsweetened applesauce, Apple, Honey, Vegetable oil, Egg, Pure vanilla extract, 
1. Preheat oven to 375F.     2. Coat 12 medium-size cups with vegetables oil or line with paper baking  cups. 3. In a medium bowl combine oat bran cereal, flour, cinnamon, baking powder,  soda, and salt.     4. In large bowl combine applesauce, honey, oil, egg, and vanilla. Stir in  dry ingredients; mix well. Stir in apple.     5. Fill prepared muffin cup almost full. Bake 15-20 minutes or until golden  brown. Serve warm.
