Poblano Chili Cream
Cilantro; stems included, Poblano chiles; roasted,, Tomatillos; husks removed, Juice of 1 lime, Sour cream, Chicken Stock, Black Pepper, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Coarse salt, 
In a blender, place the tomatillos and cilantro. Blend until chopped. Add  the chiles, sour cream, chicken stock, oil, lime juice, garlic, and salt.  Blend until smooth. Store refrigerated until ready to use.
