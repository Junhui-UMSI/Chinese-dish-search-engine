Tofu Burgers

Crumble the tofu into a large bowl and add the remaining ingredients.  Mix  together until consistency you wish.  (I find that I have to put my hands  in and really squeeze it all together). If it seems to be too dry, you can  add a bit more ketchup until it is suitable consistency.    The burgers can either be grilled on the barbecue, fried in a little  margarine or oil or grilled under the broiler.  Serve in a bun or with  other vegetables.
