Unfried French Fries
Potatoes, 
Salt to taste (optional) Boil potatoes 20 minutes (Peel them or leave  them unpeeled) Cut as fries.  Place on non-stick cookie sheet (or  spray cookie sheet with PAM), one layer thick. Lightly sprinkle with  salt if desired, or your favorite seasoning. Bake at 400 degrees for  45-60 minutes. Turn with spatula after 25 minutes.
