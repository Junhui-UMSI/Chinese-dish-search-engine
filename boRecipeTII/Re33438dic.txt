Basil and Spinach Salad
Proscuitto, Parmesan Cheese, Pine Nuts, Basil, fresh, Garlic, Spinach Leaves, olive oil, Salt and pepper, 
Toss spinach and basil in a large bowl.  Heat oil in a skillet over medium  heat and add garlic and pine nuts. Saute until nuts begin to brown  slightly.    Stir in prosciutto and cook another minute.  Season to taste with salt and  pepper.  Toss the salad with warm dressing and sprinkle with Parmesan  cheese.
