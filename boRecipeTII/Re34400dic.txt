Bean Burgers
Butter for sauteeing, Kidney beans, drain & mash, Cream or milk, Vegetable oil, Med size onion, finely chop, Barbeque sauce, Wheat germ, Dried Oregano, Whole wheat bread crumbs, Clove garlic,finely chopped, Cooked pinto, pea, or, Chopped parsley, Black Pepper, Egg yolks, Salt, 
1.  Heat the oil in a small skillet and saute the onion and garlic until    tender but not browned.  Combine with the beans, parsley, crumbs, egg    yolks, cream, salt, pepper, and oregano and mix well. 2. Shape into 4  burgers and coat with wheat germ. Chill for 1 hour or    longer. 3. Melt the butter in a large heavy skillet and saute burgers  until    lightly browned.  Serve with barbeque sauce.
