Double Fudge Brownie Mix with Recipe
MIX, Sugar, Cocoa (not Dutch process), Flour, Pecans, Chocolate chips, BROWNIES, Butter, Eggs, Mix, 
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.    Double Fudge Brownies: Preheat oven to 325. grease a 13X9" pan. Cream the  butter. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each. Add mix, continue  to beat until mixture is smooth. Spread into prepared pan and bake 40-50  minutes.
