Potato Bread Stuffing
Stock to moisten, Butter, Loaf white bread, Onions; chopped, Potatoes, Poultry seasoning, Celery, Salt and peper to taste, Eggs, 
Mary Anderson oBrien, who was born in Ireland and emigrated to  Lynn,Massachusetts, made this stuffing for her family all her life. Her  daughter-in-law, Bobbie OBrien, has been making it since she married into  the family 38 years ago.  "We all love it," she said. "Ive experimented  with other stuffings, but Im through experimenting." Cook to potatoes and  mash with 4 tbsp of butter. Season with 1 tbsp poultry seasoning. Add  onions and celery and mix well.  Add the bread cubes, remaining 2 tbsp of  butter, and eggs. Stir until well mixed and season with salt and peper and  at least one additional tbsp of poultry seasoning. Moisten further with  stock, if necessary.  Let sit for a few hours before stuffing the bird.  Stuffs a 12-15 lb bird.
