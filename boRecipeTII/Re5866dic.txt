Piquante Sauce #2
Finely diced celery, Finely diced jalapeno pepper, Salt, Black pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Finely diced green bell, Garlic powder, Tabasco sauce, Thyme, Ketchup, Finely diced onion, 
Thoroughly blend all ingredients several hours before using to allow  flavors to meld.    NEW ORLEANS CAFE    WEST 200TH SOUTH, SALT LAKE CITY    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of American recipes>, downloaded from  G Internet, G Internet.
