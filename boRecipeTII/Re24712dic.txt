Au Gratin Potatoes #2
Salt, Non-stick spray, Frozen hash browns; slightly, Cornflake crumbs, Pepper, Cream of chicken soup (1, Hominy (2 large cans), Cheddar longhorn cheese, Sour cream, Diced green chiles (1 can), Margarine, Onions, 
Mix all ingredients together (except cornflakes) and spoon into sprayed 13-  by 9-inch dish.  Top with cornflake crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees 45  minutes. Makes 10 to 12 servings.
