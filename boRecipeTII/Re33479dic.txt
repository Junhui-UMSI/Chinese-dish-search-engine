Basil Mashed Potatoes 3
Pine nuts, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Basic mashed potato recipe;, Kosher salt, Black Pepper, Basil leaves,; washed and, Basil Puree:, Cloves garlic,; peeled and, 
In a blender, combine the pine nuts, extra-virgin olive oil, garlic, salt  and pepper, and puree. With the motor running, add the basil leaves and  continue to puree until smooth.    Add the basil puree to the warm mashed potatoes, and over low heat stir  with a wooden spoon until the puree is completely incorporated and the  mixture is piping hot. Serve.
