Tuna Casserole "Violets"
Regular milk, Wide noodles, Evaporated milk, Cream of celery soup, Cracker crumbs, Cheddar cheese, TUNA, 
In one pan cook & drain noodles, while in another pan combine, mix  remaining ingredients & heat to a thin consistency & pour over noodles. Mix  well & top with cracker crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees 45 to 60 minutes.
