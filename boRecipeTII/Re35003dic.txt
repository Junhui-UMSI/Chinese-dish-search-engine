Bean Soup with Garlic
And, PHILLY INQUIRER-----, Taste, Directions and drained --, Other, OR, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Salt to taste, Beef broth, I.E.S.JJGF65A-----, Drained, Fresh ground pepper to, White beans -- soaked and, Parsley -- chopped, Cannellini beans -- canned, Dried cannellini -- or, Cook, Garlic -- chopped, Ed according to package, 
Put oil and chopped garlic in a soup pot and turn on the heat to medium.  Cook the garlic,stirring it,until it becomes colored a very pale gold.Add  the drained cooked or canned beans,a pinch of salt and a few grindings of  pepper.Cover and simmer gently for 5 or 6 minutes.. Take about 1/2 cup of  beans from the pot and puree them through a food mill back into the  pot,together with the broth.Simmer for 5 or 6 minutes more,taste,and  correct for salt and pepper.Swirl in the chopped parsley and turn off the  heat.Ladle into individual soup bowls.Makes 4 to 6 servings.. NOTE: This  dish can be made thin or thick by the amount of broth added..    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
