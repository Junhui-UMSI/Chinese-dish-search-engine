Apples in Cream
Rome Beauty or Winesap, Butter, Cream, Brown sugar, -(up to), 
Peel, core & quarter apples & place in shallow baking dish. Dot with butter  & sprinkle brown sugar over top. Cover & bake at 325 for 20 minutes. Pour  cream over apples. Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes or until soft.    DOROTHY MATHEWS    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
