Tartar Sauce
Capers, Onion Juice, Olives, Gherkins, Mayonnaise, Parsley, 
*    You can use finely chopped shallots or white onions in place of the     juice.    Finely chop the capers, olives, gherkins and parsley before measuring.  Fold all of the ingredients into the mayonnaise and serve cold.  Serve  with fried fish or cold seafood.    NOTE:  This is a classic recipe created by Oscar of The Waldorf Hotel many  years ago.    "More Recipes from the Backs of Boxes, Bottles, Cans & Jars", by Ceil Dyer.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sauces1.zip
