Waldorf Salad
Chopped diamond walnuts, Apples; cored and diced, Celery, Lemon juice, Lettuce, Mayonnaise or salad dressing, 
In a large bowl sprinkle diced apples with lemon juice. Add celery, walnuts  and mayonnaise or salad dressing; toss to coat. Serve on individual  lettuce-lined plates.  Posted to brand-name-recipes by Beach Princess<barbra@pipeline.com> on Jan  23, 1998
