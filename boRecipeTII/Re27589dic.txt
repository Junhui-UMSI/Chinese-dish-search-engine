The Best Chocolate Sauce in All the World
Boiling water, Squares unsweetened, Sugar, Tps vanilla, Salt, Butter, White corn syrup, 
Melt chocolate and butter in saucepan. Add boiling water very slowly.  Gradually bring to the boil. Add sugar, salt and syrup. Boil gently for 5  minutes. Cover. Cool. Add vanilla. Serve warm over ice cream. Yummy!!!    Posted to EAT-L Digest 28 Sep 96    From:    Nana Pat <cookie@CWCONNECT.CA>    Date:    Sun, 29 Sep 1996 20:00:11 -0700
