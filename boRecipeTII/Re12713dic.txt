Yorkshire Pudding
Egg white; room temperature, Flour, Eggs, milk, Unsalted butter; melted, Salt, 
In a blender, blend the eggs, salt, flour and milk for 30 seconds. Chill  the mixture, covered, for 30 minutes.    Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F, and place a muffin pan in the oven so  that it is hot when the batter is ready. In a small bowl, beat the egg  white to soft peaks, and fold into chilled mixture.    Grease the hot muffin pan with the melted butter. Pour in the batter,  filling them about half full. Bake for about 20 minutes, until the pudding  is puffed and browned.    Yield: 6 servings    Recipe by: Two Hot Tamales    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 288 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 22, 1997
