Pretzel Torte
(large) whipped topping, Whipped topping, (8-oz) soft cream cheese, (3-oz) strawberry gelatin, Melted margarine, (10-oz) frozen strawberries, Sugar, Boiling water, Crushed pretzels, Sugar, 
From: arielle@taronga.com (Stephanie da Silva) (COLLECTION)    Date: Wed, 2 Mar 1994 23:07:59 GMT  See recipe "Grandma Olsens Sugar Cookies" for source/info on this recipe.    Combine crushed pretzels, margarine and sugar. Reserve 1 tablespoon. Press  in a 9 x 13" pan and bake 10 minutes at 400F. Cool.  Mix soft cream cheese  with sugar. Blend in 1 cup topping, spread on crust and let set. Combine  gelatin & hot water. Add strawberries; stir well until partiallly set. Pour  over cream cheese. Spread remaining whipped topping on top and sprinkle  with reserved crushed pretzels. Refrigerate.    Di -- Customer Service    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.
