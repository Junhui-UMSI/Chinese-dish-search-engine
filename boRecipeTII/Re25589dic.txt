Avocado Salsa
Red onion, Fresh calintro, Fresh lime juice, Tomatoes, Salt to taste, Avocado, jalepenos, Olive oil, 
Combine 1st six ingredients (refrigerate if you want for later) and add  avocado and salt just before serving.
