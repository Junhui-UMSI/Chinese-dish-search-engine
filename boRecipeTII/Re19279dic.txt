Vegetable Soup
Green beans with juice, Bag frozen green beans, Vegetables, Optional:  1/2 cabbage,, Sliced thinly, Celery, chopped, Bag frozen okra, Bag frozen California, Onion, sliced thinly, Cans V-8 juice, Bag frozen mixed vegetables, Corn with juice, 
Add onion and celery to V-8 juice in large pot.  Cook until tender.  Add  all other vegetables.  Cook to desired consistency.  Add any seasonings you  prefer, like thyme, oregano, basil, and bay leaves.  Also, to add some  zest, you might want to try the Spicy version of V-8, using one can  regular and one can spicy.  Just a thought :)    Serves 8 Calories: 108 Fat: 0 gm, 0% fat    Posted by dmc@cherry-semi.com (Dawn Chace) to the Fatfree Digest [Volume 11  Issue 21], Oct. 21, 1994.
