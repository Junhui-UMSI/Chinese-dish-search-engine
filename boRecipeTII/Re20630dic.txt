Apple Cranberry Bars
Egg whites, Orange zest, Skim milk, cream cheese, Salt, Apples, Cranberries, Powdered sugar, Unbleached flour, Topping----, Vanilla, Vanilla, Orange zest, Margarine, Granulated sugar, Baking soda, Margarine, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Prepare a 15 x 10 x 1" jelly roll pan with  cooking spray and flour.  In a mixing bowl, combine the margarine, apples,  cranberries, egg whites, orange peel and vanilla.  In another mixing bowl,  combine flour, baking soda and salt.  Mix dry ingredients with wet  ingredients just until moistened.  Spread mixture into prepared pan.  Bake  for 30 to 35 minutes.  Meanwhile, to prepare topping, combine the  margarine, cream cheese, vanilla, orange peel, and milk.  Top when bars  have cooled.
