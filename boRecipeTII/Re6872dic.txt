Pomegranate Dressing
Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Lemon juice, Pomegranate juice, Dry mustard, Salt, Fresh mint, 
In jar with tight-fitting lid, combine pomegranate juice, olive oil, lemon  juice, fresh mint, dry mustard and salt.  Cover and shake until well mixed.
