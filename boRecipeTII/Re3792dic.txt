Pennsylvania Dutch Apple Cake
Sugar, Orange juice, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, All-purpose flour, Vanilla extract, Eggs, White sugar, apples, Sliced, Cooking oil, 
Toss the apple slices with 1/4 cup sugar and the cinnimon ; set aside. Mix  together the flour, 1 1/2 cups sugar, eggs, oil and vanilla, orange juice.  baking soda and baking powder. Beat with an electric mixer at medium speed.  (The batter will be very thick).  Spread a 3rd. of the batter in a greased  10 inch tube or bundt pan, top with half the apple mixture. Repeat layers  ending with batter.  Bake in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for 1 hour. Remove pan to wire rack;  cool for about 10 minutes. remove cake from pan. Cool completely and  drizzle the top with confectioners sugar glaze.>> (Beat together 1 1/2  cups confectioners sugar, 2 tablespoons softened butter, 1 1/2 teaspoons  vanilla extract and 1 to 2 tablespoons water until smooth)  Recipe By     : posted by Greg Pomylako    Posted to EAT-L Digest  5 October 96    Date:    Sun, 6 Oct 1996 18:00:42 -0300    From:    Betsy Burtis <ebburtis@IX.NETCOM.COM>
