Texas Candy Cake
Condensed milk, Coconut, Flour, Dates, Candied pineapple, Nuts, Candied cherries, 
Toss fruit and nuts with flour.  Mix in milk thoroughly until all is well  coated.  Press into sprayed bread pans and bake at 250 degrees 1-1/2 hours,  or till light brown. Let cool in pans until completely cool, then slice.  Best if it mellows in refrigerator about 2 weeks.   Pat Empson 05/25 09:50 am
