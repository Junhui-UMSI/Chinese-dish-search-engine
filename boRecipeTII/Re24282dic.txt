Trinidad Congo Pepper Salsa
Vinegar, Dijon mustard, Turmeric, Curry powder, Habanero or Scotch Bonnet, Mango; peeled, pitted, and, Papaya; peeled and diced, White onions; minced, 
Place all ingredients in a pan, bring to boil, lower the heat, and let  simmer for 30 minutes. Remove from heat, cool, and store in a jar in  refrigerator. Heat Scale: Extremely Hot.    CHILE-HEADS ARCHIVES    From the Chile-Heads recipe list.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
