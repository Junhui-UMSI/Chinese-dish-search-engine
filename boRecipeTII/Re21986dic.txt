Applesauce Date Cake
Salt, Allspice, Eggs, Flour (unsifted), Nutmeg, Lt. brown sugar (packd firm), Butter (softened), Cinnamon, Dates (chopped), Baking soda, Cloves, Applesauce (heated), Pecans (coarse-shopped), Butter frosting, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 F.  Grease well and flour a 9X9X2" pan. 2. Into  large bowl, sift flour with soda, spices and salt. Add eggs, brown sugar,  butter, and 1 cup hot applesauce; at low speed, beat just until the  ingredients are combined. 3. At medium speed, beat 2 minutes longer,  occasionally scraping the side of the bowl and guiding mixture into the  beater with a rubber scraper. 4. Add remaining applesauce, dates, and  walnuts; beat 1 minute. 5. Pour batter into pan.  Bake 50 minutes OR UNTIL  straw comes out clean.  Cool 10 minutes in pan.
