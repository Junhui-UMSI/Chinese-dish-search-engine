Swedish Saffron Bread
Sea salt, Active dry yeast, Canola, Sunflower or, Whole wheat flour, Safflower Oil, Oat-blend Flour, Dry Milk, Water; warmed, LISA CRAWLEY/TEASPOON, Date Sugar, Threads Saffron, Currants, Bread flour, 
Add ingred. to machine per your instructions except the currants. Add the  currants at the "beep". Program for WHOLE WHEAT mode. In my ABM100, I used  the WHITE BREAD cycle. Source: The Bread Machine Gourmet.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #294 by serge.cyr@sympatico.ca (Serge Cyr)  on Nov 12, 1997
