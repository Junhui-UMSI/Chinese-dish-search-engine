Turkey Tortilla Roll-Ups
Diced cooked turkey, Chunky salsa, Finely shredded cheddar, Chopped ripe olives, Flour tortillas (8 inches), Shredded lettuce, Sour cream, 
Spread 2 tablespoons sour cream over each tortilla. Top with turkey,  cheese, lettuce, olives, and salsa. Roll up each tortilla tightly; wrap in  plastic wrap. Refrigerate until serving. Yield: 6 servings.    NOTES : "You wont have to take along a grill to enjoy these tasty, hearty  snacks, field editor Darlene Markel of Sublimity, Oregon."
