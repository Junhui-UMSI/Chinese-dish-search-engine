Quick and Easy Seafood Newburg
Cooked peas, Frozen condensed cream of, Cooked shrimp; crab or, 
Warm undiluted soup. Fold in seafood and peas. Add sherry to taste, if  desired. Serve over rice. If preferred, omit the peas and serve over  asparagus spears, on toast.
