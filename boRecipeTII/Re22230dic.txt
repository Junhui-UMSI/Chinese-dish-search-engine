Turkey Soup with Wild Rice and Dried Corn
Turkey stock, Dried sweet corn, Vegetable oil, Fresh cilantro; chopped, Carrots; diced, Onion, Wild rice; uncooked, Lemon juice, 
1. In a large pot heat the oil over moderate heat. Add the chopped onion  and the carrots and saute, stirring, until the onion just begins to turn  golden.    2. Add the turkey stock, the wild rice, and the dried corn. Bring to the  simmer, then cook, uncovered, over low heat for 1 1/2 to 2 hours until the  rice and the corn are tender.    3. Stir in the lemon juice and the chopped cilantro. Serve very hot.
