Touchdown Chocolate Fudge
(14-oz) Eagle Brand, Vanilla extract, Salt, -(up to), Copped nuts, 
Date:    Tue, 11 Jun 1996 21:12:10 -0400    From:    Linda Robinson <CatRescuer@AOL.COM>  In heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt chips with sweetened condensed milk,  vanilla and salt.  Remove from heat; stir in nuts.  Cool 10-12 minutes.  On  aluminum foil-lined baking sheet, shape into a smooth ball then into a  football shape.  Chill 2 hours or until firm.  Place on serving plate.  Garnish as desired.  Store covered at room temperature. Makes about 2 lb.    Pan Fudge:  Do not cool.  Spread fudge into aluminum foil-lined 8" or 9"  square pan.  Chill as above.  Turn onto cutting board; peel off foil and  cut into squares.    EAT-L DIGEST 10 JUNE 1996    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
