Pistachio Inside Outside Cake
Pistachio pudding, Nuts, Club soda, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Milk; cold, Eggs, Dream Whip, nuts, CAKE, Yellow cake mix, Vegetable oil, FROSTING, Pistachio pudding, 
Preheat oven to 350~. Grease and flour a bundt pan. Blend all cake  ingredients, except nuts, and beat for 2 minutes at medium speed. Add nuts;  mix well. Pour batter into prepared bundt pan. Bake for 50 minutes. Cool 15  minutes; remove from pan, cool on rack. Split cake into 3 layers. Spread 1  cup of frosting between each layer. Spoon remaining frosting mix into cake  center. Chill. Garnish with chopped nuts.   Frosting: Add cold milk to Dream whip and dry pistachio pudding. Whip  until thickened, about 5 minutes.
