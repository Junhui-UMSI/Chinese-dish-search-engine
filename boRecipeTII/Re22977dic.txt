Armenian Rice (Great Recipes Version)
Unsalted margarine, Uncooked thin egg noodles, Black pepper, Uncooked long-grain rice, Low-sodium chicken broth, 
This version is from "Great Recipes for Good Health" by Readers Digest,  who just calls it "Noodles and Rice."     In a medium-sized saucepan melt the butter or margarine.  When bubbly, add  the egg noodles and stir until browned, about 2 minutes.  Add the rice and  cook about 3 more minutes.     Add the chicken stock/broth and spice and bring to a boil.  Reduce to a  simmer and cover.  Cook for 20-30 minutes until rice is done, stirring  occasionally.  Allow to sit for a few minutes before serving.
