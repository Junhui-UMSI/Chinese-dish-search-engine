Pork Chop Skillet Dinner
Pork Chops, 1/2" Thick, Vegetable oil, Med. Onion, Chopped, Caraway Seed, Beef Bouillon Cube, Water, Sherry, Optional, Mixed Vegetables *, 
*  Mixed Vegetables should be 1 16 oz package Birdsey Broccoli,     Cauliflower and Red Peppers.   Brown pork chops on both sides in hot oil. Add onion and caraway seed and  cook until onion is tender but not brown. Add bouillon cube, water and  sherry.    Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 minutes. Stir in  vegetables, cover and simmer 5 minutes longer or until vegetables are  heated through.
