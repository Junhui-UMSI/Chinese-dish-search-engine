Wild Pig on Sauerkraut
Pork shoulder,wild or tame, Juniperberries, smashed, Salt, Bayleaves, Canadian bacon, Butter, Juniperberries, whole, Crisco, Rolled and tied up., Wine, white, Black Pepper, Sauerkraut, Apples, 
1. Melt butter and fry pork on all sides in it. 2. Add the salt, pepper and  smashed juniperberries. 3. Heat oven to 225 C and bake the pork for 1/2  hour long,     basting it with its juices a couple of times. 4. Heat the crisco in a  pot and saute the sauerkraut shortly     in it. 5. Add bayleaves, juniperberries, the grated apples and the wine.  6. Let simmer, on medium heat, for 30 minutes. 7. Put the pork on top of  the sauerkraut and pour the juices allover it. 8. Let it bake for another  35 minutes, at 170 C. 9. Slice pork and serve on top of kraut with beer.     From " Die Actuelle " Magazine     Translated by Brigitte Sealing
