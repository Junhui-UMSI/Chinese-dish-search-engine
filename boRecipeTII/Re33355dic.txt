Basic Turkey Burger Plus
Ground Turkey, Soy sauce, Bread crumbs, Worcestershire Sauce, Onions, Garlic powder, Egg, burger buns, 
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except burger buns.    Shape meat  mixture into four patties, 1/2-inch thick.    Broil or grill burgers about 6  inches from heat, for 3 to 4 minutes per side.
