Vegetable Curry
Onions, Bunch fresh cilantro, minced, Turmeric, Cayenne, Eggplants cut into_1"_cubes, Cumin seeds, Salt, Frozen or fresh green peas, (optional), Mustard seeds, Saute medium of choice, Water, as needed, 
Heat saute medium in very large deep skillet. Add seeds and saute until  they begin to pop. (5 min.)    Add onion, salt, turmeric and cayenne. Cook, stirring occasionally for 8-10  min until onion is transluscent.    Add egplant. Cook stirring from bottom regularly for 15-20 min. until  eggplant is soft. Add water if needed. Cover pan between stirrings.    Steam peas until just tender and bright green. Serve curry over rice,  topped with peas and freshly minced cilantry. ENJOY!!!    Posted by AlisaMcG to AOL. [Rev.
