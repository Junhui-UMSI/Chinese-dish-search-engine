Tomato Rice W/herbs
Basil, Tomato paste canned, low, Aspeseta, olive oil, Salt, Water, Parsley dried, Tomato raw, Rice, basmatti, Paprika, 
chop the tomatoes into 1/2" cubes. in a deep frying pan put in the olive  oil and saute the rice and aspeteta for about 5 minutes until the rice  turns clear. in another pot boil about 1 3/4 cups of water adding the  basil, tomato paste, salt and paprika. When at a full boil added the rice  and stir well. Turn heat to low and cook covered for 15 minutes. Remove  from heat, cover and let stand another 15 minutes for rice to finish. Fold  in the tomatoes and parsely just before serving.
