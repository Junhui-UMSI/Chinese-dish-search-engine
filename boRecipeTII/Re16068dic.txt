Warm Italian Beef and Spinach Salad
Roma tomatoes, sliced 1/4", Sour cream, Prepared horseradish, Country-style Dijon mustard, Torn fresh spinach, SALAD, DRESSING, Skim milk, Sliced red onion, separated, Deli roast beef, cut in 1/8", 
In medium bowl stir together all dressing ingredients; set aside.    Spray 10- to 12-inch nonstick skillet with vegetable cooking spray, add  beef and onions. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until onions  begin to soften and beef is hot (about 2 to 3 minutes).    In a large bowl toss together beef mixture, tomatoes, spinach, and  dressing. If desired, serve with croutons or garlic toast.  Recipe by: Land OLakes    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #375, by Diane Lamere <dlamere@plantnet.com>  on Thu, 16 Jan 1997.
