Wild Rice with Sausage
Onion, Chopped almonds; toasted, Fresh mushrooms; chopped, Box (6-oz) long grain and, Hot or mild sausage, Chicken broth, Whipping cream, 
Preheat oven to 350?. Brown sausage and saute mushrooms and onion in fat.  Drain fat.  Cook rice according to directions for 20 minutes. Mix cream and  chicken broth. Combine all ingredients except almonds. Put into a 2-1/2  quart casserole and bake for 25 minutes. Top with almonds and bake 5 more  minutes. Yield: 6 servings.
