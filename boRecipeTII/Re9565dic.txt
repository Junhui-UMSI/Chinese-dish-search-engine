Pumpkin Cookies #1
Vanilla, Vanilla, milk, Baking soda, Butter, Pumpkin, Brown sugar, Flour, Egg, Salt, Sugar, Cinnamon, ICING, Butter, Baking Powder, Powdered sugar, 
Cream sugar and butter, add pumpkin and egg, then dry ingredients.  Bake  10-12 minutes at 350F.  (These are drop cookies).    Icing: Combine butter, milk and brown sugar.  Boil 3 minutes.  Cool.  Stir  in sugar and vanilla.  (When you ice, work fast -- the icing hardens  quickly).    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /COOKIES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
