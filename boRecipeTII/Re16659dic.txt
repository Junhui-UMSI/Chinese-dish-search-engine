Alabama Corn Sticks
Buttermilk, Baking soda, Salt, Baking Powder, Egg, Cornmeal, Sugar, 
Sift together cornmeal, baking powder, soda, sugar, and salt; stir in  buttermilk and egg, mixing well. Heat well greased cast iron corn stick  pans in a 400 degree oven for 3 minutes or until very hot. Spoon batter  into pans, filling two thirds full.  Bake at 400 degrees for 25 minutes or  until lightly browned.
