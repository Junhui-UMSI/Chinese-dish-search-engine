Pumpkin Bread #02
Salt, Raisins, Sugar, Pecans, Vegetable oil, Pumpkin (fresh/canned), Eggs, All-purpose flour, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Water, Baking soda, -(up to), 
Cream eggs & sugar. Add oil. Combine dry ingredients & add to creamed  mixture alternating with water. Add pumpkin, raisins & nuts. Grease & flour  pans or cans. Bake at 325 for 1 hour. Makes 2 large loaves or four 1-pound  coffee can loaves.    BRENDA RAMEY    NELLIE HOPPER    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
