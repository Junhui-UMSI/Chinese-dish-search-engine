Allisons Baked Kalamata Olives
Strip orange peel, Garlic clove; peeled and, Fennel seed; lightly crushed, Kalamata olives, Red wine; or enough to, 
San Francisco event planner Allison Rodman got the recipe for these warm  seasoned olives from a former Bay Area caterer.    INSTRUCTIONS: Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    Combine all ingredients in an ovenproof ramekin. Bake until the olives are  hot, about 15 minutes. Cool 5 minutes before serving.
