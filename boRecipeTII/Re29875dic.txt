Tequila Marinated Shrimp
Garlic, Tequila, olive oil, Lime juice, Onion, Cilantro; chopped, Salt, Fresh shrimp; medium,, 
To keep shrimp well chilled place them in a bowl nestled inside a larger  bowl of ice. Cook onion and garlic in olive oil in a large skillet over  medium heat about 3 minutes or until tender. Add shrimp and tequila; bring  to boiling. Boil gently, uncovered, 3-5 minutes or until shrimp turn pink,  stirring occasionally. Transfer shrimp mixture to a bowl. Add lime juice,  salt and cilantro. Toss to mix. Cover and refrigerate 2-24 hours stirring  occasionally. Drain before serving. Serves 10-12.
