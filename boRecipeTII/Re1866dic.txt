Chickpea and Sweet Potato Koftas
sweet potato, chickpeas, Scallions, Cumin, Ginger, Salt, Lemon juice, Black pepper, Flour, Peas, Oil for frying, 
Source: Vegetable Heaven by Mollie Katzen    Peel and cube the sweet potato and steam, boil or microwave it. Put all the  ingredients except the flour and peas into a food processor and puree. Mix  in the flour and add the peas. Form patties and fry on both sides till  golden brown.    They really look very pretty when done - all orange with green peeking out!
