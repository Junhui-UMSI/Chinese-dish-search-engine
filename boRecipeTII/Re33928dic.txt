Sweet and Tangy Cornish Hens
Lemon juice, Cornish game hens, Crushed pineapple juice, Prepared mustard, Margarine or butter, Salt and pepper, Maple syrup, Soy sauce, 
Heat oven to 350F. In 13x9-inch pan, arrange hens, skin-side up. Brush with  melted margarine; season with salt and pepper. Bake uncovered at 350F for       30    minutes.    In small bowl, combine syrup, lemon juice, mustard, soy sauce, and  pineapple; spoon over hens. Return to oven for 30 minutes or until well  browned and tender, basting occasionally.
