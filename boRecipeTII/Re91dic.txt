Kichel
Baking Powder, Flour, Sugar, Eggs; slightly beaten, Butter, milk, Evaporated milk, Cinnamon-sugar mix, 
Blend butter, sugar, baking powder and flour. Rub with hands until texture  is like cornmeal. Add eggs and milk. Divide dough into 4 parts. Roll each  into a 4 inch roll. Roll out on floured board to 1/4 inch thick and as long  as a cookie sheet. Brush with evaporated milk, then sprinkle with  cinnamon-sugar mixture. Bake at 400F for 10 minutes or until browned.
