Tomato and Sweet Corn Marinade
Corn oil, Brown sugar, Tomato & Sweet Corn Relish, Tomato juice, Cider vinegar, Apple juice, 
Mix well, place meat or tofu into marinade, let sit for 3 hours or  refrigerate overnight. Grill and baste with marinade. Leftover marinade can  be cooked and reduced over heat and used as serving sauce. source on the  Grapevine- Lesley B.
