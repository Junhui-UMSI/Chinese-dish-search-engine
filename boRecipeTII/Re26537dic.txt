Bacon-Oyster Bites
Bacon, Water, Herbed seasoned stuffing, (5-oz) oysters; chopped, 
Preheat oven to 350?. Cut bacon slices in half and cook slightly. DO NOT  OVERCOOK.  Bacon must be soft enough to roll easiiy around balls. Combine  stuffing, oysters and water.  Roll into bite-sized balls, approximately 16.  Wrap balls in bacon. Bake at 350? for 25 minutes. Serve warm.    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
