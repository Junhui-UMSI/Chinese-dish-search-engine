Wokies
Won Ton Wrappers, Mustard, Catsup, Oil For Deep Fat Frying, Hot Dogs, 
Cut hot dogs in half lengthwise. Wrap one half in won ton wrapper and seal  with egg. Deep fat fry until golden brown Dip in mustard or Catsup. These  were a huge success and requested often.
