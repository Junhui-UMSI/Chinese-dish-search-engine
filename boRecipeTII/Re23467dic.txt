Turkey Asparagus Melt
Dijon mustard, (15 oz) green giant extra, Red onion rings, Sliced cooked turkey, Dried sage leaves; crushed, Mayonnaise or salad dressing, Sourdough hoagie buns or, Shredded colby cheese;, 
In small bowl, combine mayonnaise, mustard and sage; mix well. Spread 1 to  2 tablespoons mixture on each bun half. Top each with one-sixth of turkey,  asparagus spears, onion rings and cheese.    Place sandwiches on broiler pan. Broil 4 to 6 inches from heat 3 to 4  minutes or until thoroughly heated and cheese is bubbly.          6    sandwiches    Copyright 1997 The Pillsbury Company    Recipe by: Pillsbury Solutions [pillsburysolutions@listserver.crc-inc.
