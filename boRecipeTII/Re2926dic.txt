Peanut Butter Muffins
Salt, Honey, Sunflower Seeds, Whole wheat flour, Baking Powder, Wheat germ, Skim milk, Peanut butter, 
Combine the flour, wheat germ, baking powder, and the salt.  Cut in the  peanut butter with a fork or pastry blender until the mixture is crumbly.  The dough may be mixed in a food processor.  Make a hole in the center and  add the honey, milk and the sunflower seeds.  Beat thoroughly and put into  muffin tins sprayed with a non-stick spray, (Pam or something similar).  Bake at 350 degrees F. for 20 to 30 minutes, or until done.  Cool on a  rack.    From "Make Mine Healthy" by Mimi Morgan.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/muffins2.zip
