Alt Beer
Water (up to 5 gal), Priming suger, Yeast (liquid dusseldorf ale, Wheat bran -- crushed, Hops (hallertau), Or dried ale), Irish moss seaweed, Malt extract, 
Boil wort for one hour. Ferment at slightly lower temperature to get the  proper character    Recipe By     : Northern Brewers    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
