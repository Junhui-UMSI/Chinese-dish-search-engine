Appetizers - Ham Biscuits
Margarine, Pepperidge Farm Party Rolls, Ham, Prepared mustard, Poppy Seeds, Onion, 
Mix margarine, onion, mustard and poppy seeds together. spread mixture on  top side of rolls. Cover bottom halves with ham, assemble and bake 10  minutes at 400 degrees    Recipe By     : clays@mindspring.com (Clay Stovall)    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
