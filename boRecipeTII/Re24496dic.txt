Asparagus Marco Polo
Granulated sugar, Fresh ginger root, cut into, Asparagus, Vegetable oil, Lemon juice, Salt, 
Cut off the tough stems of the asparagus.  If you wish, peel the stems  partway up with a vegetable peeler.    Cut the asparagus into pieces about 1/2 inch thick, on a sharp diagonal.    Heat the oil in a skillet.  Add the ginger and cook for a few seconds until  fragrant.    Add the asparagus, sprinkle with the salt and toss with the oil. Cook for 1  to 2 minutes.  Add the sugar, toss, cover and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.    Add the lemon juice.  Toss well and serve.  Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
