Wilted Lettuce
Bibb Lettuce, Onions, Salt, Bacon, Vinegar, Brown sugar, Water, Dry mustard, 
Put lettuce and onions in salad bowl. Fry bacon crisp and cool on paper  towel. Cool bacon grease slightly; add sugar, vinegar, water, salt, and  mustard. Pour over lettuce and onion. Sprinkle bacon on top and serve  immediately.    Serves: 6    Source: "Mountain Measures", Junior League of Charleston, WV. ed. 1974    Recipe by: Mrs. John B.
