Yum Yum Sweet Potatoes
TOPPING, Sugar, margarine, Pecans, sweet potato, Flour, Brown sugar, Margarine, Eggs, Vanilla, 
Mix together the sweet potatoes, 4 tablespoons margarine, vanilla, sugar  and eggs and put into a casserole dish. To make topping: Mix brown sugar,  flour, pecans, and margarine well together and spread on top of potato  mixture. Bake in 350 degree oven for about 20 minutes. Serves 6 to 8  persons.
