Apple Pudding with Sauce
Evaporated milk; or evap., Butter, Flour, Sugar, Margarine, Brown sugar, Grated raw apples, Cinnamon, SAUCE, Baking soda, Egg, Sugar, Nuts; chopped, 
Cream together: butter and sugar. Add egg, apple, flour, baking soda,  cinnamon and nuts. Put in greased 8x8" baking pan. Can use mold or Bundt  pan. Bake at 350 F. for 30-35 minutes. Add 20 minutes for mold or Bundt  pan. Pudding should be firm in the center. Serve with warm butter sauce.  Sauce: Heat together margarine, white and brown sugar and evaprated milk.
