60 Minute Rolls
Salt, Sugar, Yeast dissolved in 1/4 c., Warm milk, Flour, COMBINE WITH, Softened butter, Beaten egg, 
Knead and rest 15 minutes on board. Shape into rolls. When double in size,  bake at 375 for 15 minutes.    Beth bethb@lincnet.com    Recipe by: Fleischmann Yeast    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #449 by Martha Sheppard  <marthahs@ix.netcom.com> on Dec 11, 1997
