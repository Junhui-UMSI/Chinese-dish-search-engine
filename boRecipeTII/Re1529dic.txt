Broccoli Tomato Pasta
olive oil, Garlic, White wine, Dried red pepper flakes, Chopped broccoli, Chopped tomatoes; (up to 2), Halved cherry tomatoes, Mostaccioli or other noodle, 
In a 3 to 4 quart pan, bring water for noodles to a boil. Add the noodles,  and cook 12 to 14 minutes, or until done. Drain the noodles and return them  to the pot.    Add oil to a large skillet, place over medium heat until hot. Add garlic  and saute 5 minutes. Reduce heat to low. Add wine and red pepper flakes,  simmering uncovered until the mixture bubbles. Add the broccoli and cover  the pan, steaming the broccoli until it is bright green but still crunchy;  about 4 minutes. Stir in the tomatoes and heat through. Combine the  vegetable mixture with the pasta and toss thoroughly. Serve with grated  Parmesan cheese.    Recipe by Mangia!
