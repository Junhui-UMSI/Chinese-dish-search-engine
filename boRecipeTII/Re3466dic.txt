Pecan Delight Pie
Pecans, Whipped topping or whipped, Sugar, Soda crackers; coarsely, Baking Powder, Egg whites, Vanilla, 
Beat egg whites until they form peaks. Continue to beat as you add sugar &  baking powder. Continue to whip until stiff & all sugar is dissolved. Add  vanilla. Add soda crackers & pecans, gently folding in by hand. Pour into  greased 9-inch pie pan. Bake at 325 for 1 hour. Remove from oven & cool  upside down. When thoroughly cooled, top with whipped cream. Sprinkle with  pecans.    LILA HUBER    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
