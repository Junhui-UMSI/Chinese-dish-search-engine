Sweet Cinnamon Rolls
Eggs, milk, Shortening, Warm water, Flour, Salt, Dry yeast, Sugar, 
Scald milk, add sugar, shortening and salt. Cool. Prepare yeast water  in bowl.  Add milk mixture and eggs then add 3 cups flour, beat until  smooth, add remainin flour. (If dough is sticky it will make a  lighter roll). Let rise 1 to 2 hours. Divide dough in two. Roll into  rectangle.    FILLING: Mix 1 1/2 c. brown sugar, 1 level Tbsp cinnamon, 1/2 c.  chopped nuts and 3/4 c. raisins. Sprinkle oblong dough with melted  margarine and add filling. Roll up in jelly roll style. Cut into one  inch slices. Place in pans.  Rise one hour. Ice with 1 c. powdered  sugar and few drops milk. Spread over rolls while warm. Bake at 375  for 20 minutes.
