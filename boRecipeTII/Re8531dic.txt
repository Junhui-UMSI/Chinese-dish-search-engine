Potatoes
Idaho russet potatoes, Margarine or oil, Garlic, Onions, salt and pepper, 
Scrub the potatoes and bake at 425F until tender-40 to 60 minutes,  depending on size. Remove from oven and let cool completely. Peel the  onions and chop coarsely. Peel and mince the garlic. Dice the potatoes  without peeling. Preheat the margarine or oil in a skillet over medium  heat. Add the garlic and onion and cook until the onion begins to turn  translucent. Add the diced potatoes. Season with salt and pepper and mix  gently. Cook until the potatoes are heated throughout and serve at once.
