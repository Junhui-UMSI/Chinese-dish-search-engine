Apple Pork Chop Bake
Butter or oil, Apple juice; cider or beer, Seedless raisins, Vinegar, WALDINE VAN GEFFEN, Apples; core; slice, 1" pork chops, Sage, Instant flour, Molasses, Onion; slice, Salt, 
Salt and sage chops. Brown chops in hot skillet in oil or butter. Place  chops in casserole. Add onions to skillet and saute until tender. Place  onions on top of chops. In skillet put juice and flour and stir to mix. Add  vinegar, molasses and raisins to skillet. Blend well. Pour over chops. Put  apple slices on top. Cover tightly with foil and bake in 375~ oven for 45  to 60 minutes or 170~ internal temp.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #813 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Sep 28, 1997
