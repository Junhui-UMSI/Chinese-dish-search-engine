Chile Oil
Peanut oil, Dark Seasame Oil, Dried red chile flakes, Several whole dried red, 
In a saucepan, combine oils & set over med. heat for 3 min. Add chile  flakes & immediately remove from heat. Cover & let stand overnight. Next  day, Strain into sieve, then into clean bottle. Disregard flakes. Drop a  few whole dried chilies into oil for decoration and seal. Store in cool  place. Makes 1 cup.
