The Fastest Chocolate Chip Cake in the World
Water, Semisweet chocolate chips, Eggs, chocolate cake mix, Vegetable oil, instant pudding, 
Preheat oven to 350 degres.  Pour oil into a 9- by 13-inch pan to coat the  bottom.  Add to pan in order: cake mix, water, eggs, pudding mix and  chocolate chips. Mix the ingredients thoroughly with a fork. Spread the  batter evenly in the pan and bake 35 to 40 minutes, or until toothpick  inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow the cake to  completely cook before unmolding.
