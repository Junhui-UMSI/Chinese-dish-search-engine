Chicken Sticks
Flour, Boneless chicken, Eggs, Buttermilk, Oregano, Garlic powder, Salt, Poultry seasoning, Celery salt, Italian seasoning, 
Combine last 8 ingredients. Dip chicken in mixture, then in flour. Fry in  deep fat fryer for about 12 minutes.    Joan MacDiarmid in Amherst, NY    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Oct 26, 98,
