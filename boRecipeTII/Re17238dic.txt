Wacky Cake (Eggless)
Mix together:, Cocoa, Baking soda, Flour, Vinegar, pan, oil, Water, Salt, Vanilla, Sugar, Make 3 indentations in this mixture, 
Mix everything together with a fork and bake at 325 for 30-35 min.    Recipe By     : JanMary    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
