Baked Shrimp and Crab Curry
Butter, unsalted, Salt, Flour, Crabmeat, fresh, milk, Shrimp, Bread crumbs, Curry powder, Scallion, Sour cream, Pasta shells, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Melt three tablespoons butter in a saucepan over  medium heat. Cook scallions, stirring constantly about 10 minutes. Stir in  curry powder, flour and salt, mixing well. Add milk, increase heat and cook  until mixture boils and thickens. Remove from heat. Stir in sour cream,  pasta and seafood, and pour mixture into a buttered baking dish.    Melt remaining tablespoon of butter and combine with bread crumbs. Sprinkle  crumbs over seafood mixture and bake uncovered about 30 minutes.
