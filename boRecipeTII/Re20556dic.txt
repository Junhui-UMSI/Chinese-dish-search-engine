Apple Chutney
Cayenne To Taste, Pickling Salt, Sugar, Cider vinegar, Red Or Green Chili Peppers**, Hard Green Pears ***, Garlic Peeled and Chopped, Gingerroot, Peeled & Chopped, Seedless Raisins, Very Tart Apples (Sour)*, Sugar To Taste, 
*      Apples are to be peeled, quartered and cored. ** Chili peppers are  to be seeded and chopped. *** Pears should be peeled, cored and cut into  strips. ~------------------------------------------------------  ~----------------- In large preserving kettle mix the vinegar, sugar,  raisins, apples, garlic cloves, gingerroot, peppers, and salt. Bring to a  boil and simmer until the apples are mushy and transparent. If neccessary,  add a little more vinegar.  Taste and add sugar and cayenne as desired.  Then add the pears and simmer until the pears are tranparent but not  overcooked.  Spoon into hot jars and seal. Makes 8 - 10 Pints.
