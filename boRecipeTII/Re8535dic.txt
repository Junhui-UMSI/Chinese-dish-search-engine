Potatoes and Cabbage Casserole
Butter, Onions, Cabbage, Potatoes, Flour, Butter, Cheddar cheese, Chicken broth, 
In a large pan, heat butter and saute cabbage and onions until wilted.  Place half of the cabbage, onions and potatoes in 3-quart casserole.  Sprinkle with one cup shredded American cheese. Put remaining cabbage and  potatoes on top. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in same pan in which cabbage was  cooked. Add 1/4 cup flour. Cook 1 minute. Add can chicken broth. Cook until  thickened. Pour over top of cabbage. Sprinkle one cup of cheese on top  (optional). Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
