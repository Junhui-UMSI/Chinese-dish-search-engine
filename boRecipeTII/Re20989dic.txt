Tutti-Fruity Chicken Kabobs
Soy sauce, Vegetable oil, Seedless red and green, Fresh peeled and cored, Boneless skinless chicken, Oranges; peeled, quartered,, Hot cooked rice, Honey, Peeled and chopped fresh, 
In large bowl combine oil, honey, ginger, and soy sauce. Remove 1/4 cup and  reserve. Place chicken in marinade, stirring to coat. Cover and refrigerate  for 3 hours.    Heat grill or broiler. Thread chicken, pineapple and oranges onto skewers.  Cook about 5 minutes. Baste with reserved marinade and turn. Cook 5  minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink in center. Serve with rice and  garnish with grapes.    Recipe by: Parents - March 1998    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by The Taillons <taillon@ACCESS.MOUNTAIN.NET> on  Mar 14, 1998
