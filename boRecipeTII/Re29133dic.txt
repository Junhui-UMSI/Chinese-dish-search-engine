Baked Rice Broccoli
Cream of mushroom soup, Rice; cooked, Butter, Onion, Water chestnuts; drain;, Velvetta cheese; cubed, Frozen chopped broccoli;, Celery, Not cook, 
Melt butter in saucepan. Saute onion and celery in butter. Mix in a big bow  cooked rice, onions, celery, broccoli, soup, water chestnuts and cheese.  Mix all togethera nd put in a 9x13" casserole dish. Cover with foil and  bake for 25-30 minutes at 350~.
