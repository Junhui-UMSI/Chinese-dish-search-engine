Cranberry Chews
Eggs, Sugar, Lemon juice, Nutmeg, Biscuit mix, Pecans, 8-oz. jellied cranberry, Confectioners sugar; sifted, 
Beat eggs with sugar, lemon juice, and nutmeg.  Beat until fluffy. Stir in  biscuit mix.  Cut chilled cranberry sauce in cubes and fold into the batter  with the nuts. Spread in a greased 13"x9" pan. Bake at 350~ for 20 to 25  mins.  Cool.  Sprinkle with confectioners sugar. Cut into bars.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Nov 17, 1998
