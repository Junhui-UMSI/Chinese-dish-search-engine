Venison Tongue
Bay Leaves, Cayenne pepper, Venison tongue, about 2, Onion, Cloves, whole, Salt, 1/2 inch lemon slice, 
Clean tongue thoroughly, svrubbing & rinsing. Put in large pan & cover with  boiling water. Add all ingredients, cover, & simmer slowly (do not boil)  for 3-4 hours or until tender. Allow to cool slightly in stock. Drain &  remove skin & fat. Serve hot with wine sauce or sliced cold with tangy  sauce.    Serve with fluffy mashed potatoes, buttered baby beets, & a green salad.
