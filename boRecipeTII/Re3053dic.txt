Peanut Chops
Peanut butter, creamy, Eggs, Cracker crumbs, Bread, rye; 1/2" thick, Paprika, Milk, whole, Salt, 
Cut the crust from bread and divide in lengthwise strips (or trim to  pork chop shape). Spread peanut butter on both sides of each strip.     Add milk and seasonings to the eggs and beat thoroughly. Dip strips of  bread into mixture, then into sifted cracker crumbs. Put into a greased  bread pan and bake in hot oven, 450F, until golden brown.
