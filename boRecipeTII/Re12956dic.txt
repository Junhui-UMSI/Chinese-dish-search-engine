Zesty Grilled Potatoes
Italian dressing, Potatoes, Salt, Pepper, 
~Scrub Potatoes till clean.    ~In boiling water, cook potatoes in skins till just tender.    ~While still hot carefully slice diagonally into 1/2" slices. Place in  shallow glass dish and pour dressing over hot slices. Turn to coat  thouroughly & let stand 1 hour.    ~Remove from dressing and place on grill 3" from hot coals. Grill 8-10  minutes on each side till golden brown.    ~Season after turning.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #019 by dandelion@edeneast.com on Jan 19,  1998
