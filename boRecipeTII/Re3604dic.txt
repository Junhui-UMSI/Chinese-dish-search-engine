Pecan-Lemon Wild Rice
Green onion; chopped, Wild rice; uncooked, Fresh parsley, Lemon juice, Pecans; chop and toast, Butter, Chicken broth, Lemon rind; divided, 
Wash wild rice in 3 changes of hot water; drain. Combine broth, 2 1/4  teaspoons lemon rind, lemon juice, and butter in a medium saucepan. Bring  to a boil; stir in rice. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 50 to 60 minutes or  until liquid is absorbed, and rice is tender. Stir in pecans, remaining 2  1/4 teaspoons lemon rind, green onions, and parsley.    Per serving: 185 Calories; 7g Fat (32% calories from fat); 10g Protein; 23g  Carbohydrate; 6mg Cholesterol; 806mg Sodium    Recipe by: Southern Living, September 92    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by shade <liveoak@polaris.net> on Mar 13, 1998
