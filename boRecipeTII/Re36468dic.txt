Beer Up the Butt Chicken
SEE DIRECTIONS, 
Get a Chicken. Rub with Tony Chacharies or Paprika and salt Get a 16oz can  of beer. Drink about 1/2 of the beer. Put into the can, red wine, onion  powder, garlic powder, cyanne pepper, black pepper, whatever.... Slide the  chicken over the can. Stand Chicken on grill. Smoke at about 275 or so,  until the drumsticks turn easily. Usally about 5 or 6 hours.    The can of liquid boils and pushes the humidity and flavor into the bird.    Yummm.
