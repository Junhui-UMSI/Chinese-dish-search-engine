African Fruit Slush
Juice of five oranges, Sugar, Juice of three lemons, bananas, Water, Crushed pineapple, canned, 
Dissolve sugar in hot water and cool. Add all other ingredients and  freeze. (Other fruit can be added to the bananas and pineapples such  as pears, peaches, strawberries, kiwi, etc.) Spoon frozen mixture  into glass and add Sprite, 7-Up, or Ginger Ale.
