West Texas Spanish Rice
Salt & garlic powder to, (10-oz) Rotel, Onion, Uncooked rice, Bacon grease or oil, Rotel can V-8 juice, 
Saute onion in grease until clear. Add rice and stir until golden. Add  remaining ingredients and simmer, covered, 25-30 minutes. Add water if  necessary. Turn off heat and let stand 5-10 minutes.
