Tempting Almond Tea
Almond extract, Lemon juice, Bags -- regular strength, Boiling water, Vanilla, Lemon Rind -- finely grated, Sugar -- or substitute, 
Refreshing taste treat.    Place tea bags and lemon rind in lg scalded teapot or pan.  Pour in boiling  water.  Cover and steep 5 min.    Stir in sugar, lemon juice, almond extract and vanilla. Serve at once.    Serves 4 - 6.    NOTE:  1/4 C honey may be substituted for sugar.  Place a fresh mint leaf  in bottom of cup before pouring in tea.
