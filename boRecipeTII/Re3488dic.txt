Pecan Oatmeal Cookies
Margarine, Baking soda, Baking Powder, All-bran, Salt, Sugar, Brown Sugar, Vanilla, Pecans, All-purpose flour, Egg whites, Rolled oats, Water, 
Place oatmeal, cereal and pecans in a bowl and mix lightly.  Set  aside. Cream margarine and sugars together at medium speed until  light and fluffy. Add eggs whites vanilla and water, and mix at  medium speed for 1 minute, scraping down the bowl before and after  adding egg whites, vanilla and water.  Stir flour, baking powder,  baking soda and salt together and add to creamy mixture and mix at  medium speed only until oatmeal is blended into dough.  Drop by 1 1/2  tablespoonfuls onto a cookie sheet that have been sprayed with pam  spray or lined with aluminum foil. Bake at 375 for about 12 minutes;  then remove cookies to a wire rack and cool to room temperature.
