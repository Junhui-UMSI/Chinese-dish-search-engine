Ratatouille Stir-Fry
Boneless skinless chicken, Ground ginger, Carrots; sliced, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Garlic powder, Water, Onion; thinly sliced, Zucchini; cut into 1=D71/4, Cauliflower florets, Black Pepper, Soy sauce, Spinach leaves; chopped, Celery; sliced diagonally, Cornstarch, Chicken stock or water, Green beans; thawed if, Green bell pepper; chopped, 
Prep: 15 min, Cook: 15 min. Heat a wok or heavy nonstick skillet over high  heat. Heat oil and add next 4 ingredients. Stir-fry 2 minutes. Add stock,  cover pan and cook 3 minutes. Add chicken and next 8 ingredients. Stir-fry  3 minutes, uncovered. Stir in spinach and cook 1 minute. Blend water and  cornstarch in a bowl and stir into chicken mixture. Cook 1-2 minutes, or  until liquid is thick.    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 05, 1998
