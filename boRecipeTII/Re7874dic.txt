Portuguese-Style Rice
Linguica sausage, long-grain white rice, Salt, Water, fresh cilantro, 
Saute diced sausage in a heavy saucepan until browned, about 5  minutes. Drain off excess fat.  Add rice, stirring well to coat  grains. Add water and salt, and bring a boil.  Lower heat, cover pan,  and simmer for 19 minutes, or until grains are soft and water has  evaporated.  Stir in cilantro.    Serves 4.    PER SERVING:  215 calories, 6 g protein, 28 g carbohydrate, 8 g fat  (3 g saturated), 2 mg cholesterol, 316 mg sodium, 0 g fiber.    San Francisco Chronicle, 6/29/92.
