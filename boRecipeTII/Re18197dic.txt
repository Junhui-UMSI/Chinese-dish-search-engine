Venison Chops in Tomato Soup
salt and pepper, Chops, Garlic powder, Green pepper, onion, Minute rice, Tomato soup, 
Put salt, pepper & garlic powder on chops.  Brown in oil in electric  skillet.  Pour tomato soup in skillet.   Add green peppers & onions. Simmer 1 or 1 1/2 hrs.   Turn skillet off & add minute rice.  Let stand until sauce is absorbed.  Submitted By RONAMIT@NETVISION.NET.IL (RON AMIT) On 9 JUL 1995 125532 ~0400
