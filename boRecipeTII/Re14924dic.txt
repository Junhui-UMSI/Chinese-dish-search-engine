24 Hour Slaw
Head red cabbage, shredded, Celery seeds, Onions, shredded, Sugar, Vinegar, Dry mustard, Sugar, Salt, 
Pour 3/4 cup sugar over cabbage and onion and toss.    Bring celery seeds and remaining ingredients to a boil. Pour over cabbage  and onions. Let sit for 24 hours before serving.
