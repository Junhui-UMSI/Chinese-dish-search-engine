Winter-Warming Tortellini Soup
Water, Chopped fresh parsley, Stephen Ceideburg, Rib celery, chopped, Ground nutmeg, Grated Parmesan, Clove garlic, finely chopped, Onion, Chicken bouillon granules, Dried cheese-filled, Butter, Carrot, chopped, Pepper, 
In 4-quart Dutch oven over medium-low heat, cover and cook garlic, onion,  celery and carrot in margarine for 10 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in  water and bouillon granules. Heat to boiling; red heat. Stir in tortellini.  Cover and simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tortellini are  tender.    Stir in parsley, pepper and nutmeg.    Cover and cook 10 minutes. Top each serving with cheese.    Per serving: 327 calories (19 percent from protein, 56 percent from  carbohydrate, 25 percent from fat), 16 grams protein, 46 grams  carbohydrate, 9 grams fat, 50 milligrams cholesterol, 1,223 milligrams  sodium.    Exchanges: 1/2 vegetable, 3 bread, 1 meat, 1 fat.    From "Soup, Stew and Chili" by Betty Crocker.    From the Oregonians FOODday, 1/5/93.    Posted by Stephen Ceideburg
