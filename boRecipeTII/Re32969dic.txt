Sweet Yogurt Dressing (Lf)
Honey, PER 2 TBLSPOONS-----, *cals, *gm dietary fiber, Plain, nonfat yogurt, Chopped nuts; (walnuts --, Pecans, or other), *gm fat, *mg sodium, Raisins -- (golden are, Nice), 
Mix all ingredients, and chill overnight.  Serve cold. Makes about 1.5  cups. Authors Note: Fresh chopped fruit becomes a special salad or a light  dessert with this dressing.
