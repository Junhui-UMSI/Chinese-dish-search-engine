Avocado Crab Dip
cream cheese, Worcestershire Sauce, Avacado, crabmeat, Salt, Accent, onion, Sour Cream, Lemon juice, 
Combine avacado, lemon juice, onion & Worcestershire sauce in a blender &  whirl until smooth. Add cream cheese, sour cream, Accent & salt; blend  well. Stir in crabmeat. Chill.
