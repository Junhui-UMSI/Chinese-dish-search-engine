Vanilla-Coffee Liqueur
Brown Sugar, Water, Vanilla bean; split (or 2, Granulated sugar, Vodka, Instant coffee powder, 
Combine sugars and water. Boil for 5 minutes. Gradually stir in coffee.  Cool. Add vodka and vanilla and mix thoroughly. Cover and let ripen for 1  month. Remove vanilla bean.    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #223 by Shelley Sparks  <ssparks@mailbox.arn.net> on Sep 07, 1997
