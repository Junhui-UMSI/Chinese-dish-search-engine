Thin White Sauce for Soup
Butter, Salt and pepper to taste, Rice flour, browned, milk, 
Melt butter, add flour and blend until smooth. Add milk and stir until  thickened. Season. Can be made thicker if desired. Posted to EAT-L Digest  31 Mar 97 by "Elizabeth A. Post" <millefiore@FUSE.NET> on Mar 31, 1997
