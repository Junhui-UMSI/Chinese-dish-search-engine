Whipped Avocado Dessert
Powdered sugar, Avacados, peeled and cut, Whipping (heavy) cream, Lime juice, 
Whipped Avacado Dessert    This unusual dessert is popular in Brazil.    Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor. Cover and process on  high speed until smooth and creamy. Makes 3 servings. (about 340 calories  per serving)    Recipe from Betty Crockers "30 Minutes or Less" Cookbook
