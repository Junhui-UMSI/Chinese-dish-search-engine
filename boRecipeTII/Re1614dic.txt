Candied Sweet Potatoes with Peanuts
Med. sweet potatoes or yams, Boiling water, Firm-packed lt. brown sugar, Butter or margarine, Prepared mustard, Salt, Chopped peanuts, 
Peel and slice the sweet potatoes, arrange in shallow 2-qt. baking dish.  Combine water, sugar, butter, mustard and salt; pour over potatoes. Bake,  covered with foil, at 325 F for 45 minutes. Remove foil. Spoon syrup over  potatoes and sprinkle with chopped peanuts. Bake 20 minutes longer, basting  several times, until potatoes are tender and slightly glazed and peanuts  are lightly browned.    From General Lewis Inn, Lewisburg, W.
