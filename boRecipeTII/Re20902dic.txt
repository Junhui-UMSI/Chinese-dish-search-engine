Apple Nut Ring
Sugar, Refrigerator biscuits; (2, Cinnamon, Granny Smith apples, Nuts; chopped, Stick butter or margarine;, 
Preheat oven to 400oF. Separate biscuits into 20 pieces. Combine sugar and  cinnamon. Dip biscuits first into butter then sugar. Place in Deep Dish  Baker. Peel/Core/Slice apples. Place apple slice between each biscuit and  around outer edge. Top with nuts, remaining butter and sugar. Bake for  25-30 minutes.
