Apple Pandowdy
Egg, Flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Cinnamon, Vanilla, apples, Sugar, Butter or margarine, Sugar, butter, 
Place sliced apples in buttered 9x9 inch baking pan or 2 qt. casserole.  Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar mixture. Dot with butter.    Cream sugar and butter.  Add egg and beat well.  Sift together flour,  baking powder, and salt.  Mix until mixture is well combined. Add vanilla  and stir in. Drop by spoonfuls over apples and bake in 350F oven for 30 to  35 minutes, or until lightly browned  Serve warm or cold with ice cream, if  desired.  Yield: 9 servings.
