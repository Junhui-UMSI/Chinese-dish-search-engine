Thai Peanut Chicken Salad (Quick)
SALAD, roasted chicken, DRESSING, Red bell pepper, Carrot, Lime, Celery, Fresh lime juice, Salt, Light mayonnaise, Thai peanut sauce, Prepared torn lettuce, 
1. To prepare the dressing: Stir together the peanut sauce, mayonnaise and  lime juice. Set aside.    2. To prepare the salad: Dice the chicken breast finely. Combine with the  carrot, celery, bell pepper and salt. Toss with about half of the dressing.  Add the lettuce and toss gently.    3. Serve the salad with a lime wedge and the remaining dressing on the  side.    Adapted from "Pan-Asian Express" by Barbara Witt. Developed by CeCe  Sullivan of the Seattle Times food staff.
