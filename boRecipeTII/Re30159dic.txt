Banana and Raisin Muffins
Baking Powder, Raisins, Wholemeal flour, Oat bran, Vegetable oil, Granulated sugar, Vanilla, Baking soda, Egg, milk, banana, 
I dont know the fat content of normal muffins - this one is 5gram/muffin,  and healthy!    >From - The Light Hearted Cookbook (British Heart Foundation)    In bowl, combine egg, milk, oil, sugar, bananas and vanilla. Mix well, In  another bowl mix together flour, baking soda, baking powder, oatbran and  raisins. Stir into egg mixture taking care not to over mix.    Spoon into muffin tins filling each about 2/3 full. Bake in 400 F (200 C -  Gas mark6) for twenty to twenty five minutes.
