Tofu Rice Pudding
Cooked brown rice (can use, Plus 2 T. raisins; (if, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Soft tofu; (low-fat), 
In a blender container, blend tofu until smooth. Spoon into a bowl and add  the remaining ingredients. Mix well.    Chill several hours to blend flavors.    6 servings, 153 cal. per servings .. 2 g fat (from regular tofu, I think)    This is from "Lean and Luscious and Meatless."
