Tomato Sauce - Crock Pot
tomato puree  , Bay Leaves, Seasoned salt, tomato paste  , crushed tomatoes  , Carrots, Onion, Dry basil, Extra virginia olive oil, garlic, 
Heat oil in crock pot then add onion and carrot and garlic. Saute on high  until transparent. Add tomatoes and seasonings (basil and seasoned salt)  and add 1 can of water (or dry red wine) from the 18 ounce can that had the  paste in it. Cook on low all day.
