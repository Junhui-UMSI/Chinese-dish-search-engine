Apple Cider Sauce for Ham
Apple Cider; Reduced To, Makes this many cups, Reduced Cider, Mcintosh Apples Pared And, Sugar, 
In a 4 quart saucepan bring cider to boiling. Boil gently over medium heat  about 20 minutes or until cider is reduced to specified amount in recipe.  Add quartered apples and sugar; toss gently to combine. Gently simmer  apples covered until apples are soft-- about 20 minutes. Stir once. Serve  warm with ham.    See recipe for Ham baked in apple cider.    Note the reduced cider is entered so that when the number of servings is  changed the reduced amount will be accurate.
