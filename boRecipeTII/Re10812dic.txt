Quilters Rice Pudding
Raisins, White or brown sugar, Vanilla, Scalded milk, Butter, Nutmeg, Rice, Salt, Eggs, Cinnamon, 
Combine all ingredients. Pour into a lightly greased crockpot. Cook on high  for 1 -2 hours. Stir during first 30 minutes. Recipe can be doubled.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 160 by "Mimis Box"  <mimisbox@hotmail.com> on Oct 25, 1997
