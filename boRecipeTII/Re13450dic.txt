Yellow Bundt Cake W/chocolate Frosting
Eggs, Nuts; if desired, Water, German chocolate frosting, Vegetable oil, Betty Crocker plain yellow, 
Mix all ingredients together. Pour into Bundt pan and bake at 350 degrees  for       45    minutes.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
