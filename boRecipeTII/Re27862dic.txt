Thengai Chutney ( Coconut Chutney)
Black gram dal, To Grind, Black mustard seeds, Vegetable oil, Coconut, For Seasoning, Salt, Red chiles, Green chilies, 
Grind together coconut, green chilies and salt to a fine paste. Check  consistency and add water, if needed. Heat oil and add mustard seeds, black  gram dal and red chilies. When brown, pour over chutney.
