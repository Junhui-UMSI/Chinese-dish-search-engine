Persimmon Pudding Indiana Style
Baking Powder, Flour, Buttermilk, Baking soda, Sugar; white, Eggs, Sugar; dark brown, packed, Cinnamon, Allspice, Salt, Persimmon pulp, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a large bowl, measure all ingredients.  With mixer at low speed, beat ingredients just until batter is smooth. Pour  batter into two 9 x 9 inch pans; level batter with spatula. Bake for 50  minutes or until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Serve  with whipped cream.
