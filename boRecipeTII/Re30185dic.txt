Banana Bonanza Shake
Nonfat dry milk powder, Ground allspice, Skim milk, bananas, 
In blender process:    until blended and smooth.    Pour into chilled glasses; garnish with bananas slices, if desired.    Makes 2 servings    Per serving without garnish: 236 calories, 7 grams protein, 0 grams fat,       56    grams carbohydrate    Note: For super-thick shake, place in freezer 1 to 2 hours until mixture is  semi-frozen; then to drinking consistency with additional skim milk, if  necessary.
