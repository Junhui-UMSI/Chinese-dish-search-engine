Austrian Cream Cookies
Flour, Sugar, Whipping cream, Eggs, Baking Powder, Easy Creamy Icing, Salt, 
Beat eggs until light. Add sugar gradually; blend in cream. Mix flour,  powder & salt: stir in. Chill 1 hour. Heat oven to 375. Roll dough to  1/4" thick on lightly floured board. Cut into 2" squares. With knife,  make two 1/2" indentations on each side of each square. Place on  lightly greased sheet. Bake 10-13 minutes. Cool. Frost.
