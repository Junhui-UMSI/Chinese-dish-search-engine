Aubergine and Sesame Pate
Aubergine, sesame seeds, Cayenne pepper, Lemon, Flat leaf parsley, Tahini, Seasoning, Garlic, olive oil, GARNISH, 
Date: Fri, 3 May 1996 00:26:54 -0400    From: BobbieB1@aol.com  1. Preheat the oven to 200c/400f/Gas 6. Bake the aubergine for 25-30  minutes until tender. Cool slightly , then peel and puree the flesh in a  blender or processor.    2. Add the garlic, tahini and lemon juice and process until mixed.  With  the motor running, drizzle in the oil to make a smooth paste. Season to  taste. Transfer to a serving dish, garnish and serve cold with pitta bread.
