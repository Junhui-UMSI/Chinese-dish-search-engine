Swiss Onion Bake
Toast points, (Cream of Chicken Soup), Butter or margarine, French bread slices, toasted, Sliced onion, Hard cooked eggs, sliced, 10 3/4 oz. can condensed, milk, Prepared mustard, Shredded Swiss cheese (6 oz), OR, 
In a large skillet melt butter.  Add onion; cover and cook 10 minutes or  till tender.  Spread onion in bottom of a 10x6x2 inch baking dish. Top with  sliced eggs; sprinkle with shredded cheese. In a saucepan combine soup,  milk, and mustard; heat and stir till smooth. Pour over casserole. Bake,  covered, in a 350F oven for 30 minutes or till hot. Serve spooned over  toasted bread.  Sprinkle with paprika, if desired. Makes 6 servings.  Source: Better Homes & Gardens.
