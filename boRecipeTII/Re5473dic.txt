Pineapple Dessert
Sweetened condensed milk, Pecans, crushed pineapple  , Cool  Whip, Lemon juice, Graham cracker pie shells, 
Mix pineapple and sweetened condensed milk .  Fold in cool whip until  thoroughly combined. Add lemon juice and fold in pecans.  Pour into two pie  shells and chill about 4 hours. Serves 16  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #191    Date: Sat, 10 Aug 1996 06:44:46 -0400    From: Eileen & Bob Holze <beck4@asan.com>  NOTES : This is an exquisite dessert that is relatively easy to make.
