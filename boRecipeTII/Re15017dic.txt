West Indian Pepper Sauce
Honey, Mango, Cider vinegar, Garlic, Scotch bonnets, Water, ginger root, Yellow onion, Salt, Coriander, Cumin, Turmeric, Dry mustard, 
Puree first 10 ingredients in blender. Transfer mixture to bowl. In non  reactive saucepan, bring vinegar, water, and salt to boil. Pour over mango  mixture and stir well. Allow to cool before bottling. Refrigerated, sauce  will keep approx 6 weeks.
