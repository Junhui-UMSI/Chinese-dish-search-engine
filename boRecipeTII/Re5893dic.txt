Pisaladiere
Tiny genovese olives, Salt-packed anchovy fillets;, basic pizza bread, Basic tomato sauce, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Onion, Capers, 
Preheat oven to 415 degrees F. Lightly oil a large (16-inch by 30-inch)  cookie sheet, or 2 smaller (11-inch by 17-inch) sheets. Using a rolling pan  or your hands, flatten dough to fit in your cookie sheet. Using finger  tips, poke indentations across entire surface. Spread a thin layer of  tomato sauce over surface. Sprinkle with olives. Lay anchovy fillets out  into a pattern. Sprinkle with onions, capers, and drizzle with extra virgin  olive oil. Place in oven and bake 15 to 20 minutes, until golden brown on  bottom. Remove and serve hot or cold.    Yield: 4 servings    Notes: Recipes for #ME1A09 Copyright Mario Batali 1997.
