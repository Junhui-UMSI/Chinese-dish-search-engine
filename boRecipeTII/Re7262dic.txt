Pork Chop and Potato Casserole
Golden mushroom soup, mixed, 6 pork chops, 
Preparation : Season chops with salt and pepper and brown well. Place chops  at bottom of casserole. Top with layer of potatoes, seasoned with salt and  pepper, and layer of onions.  Pour soup mixture over top. Cover and bake in  350 degree oven for 1 1/2 hours.  Uncover and bake additional 30 minutes or  until potatoes are soft.
