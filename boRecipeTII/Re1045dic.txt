Cold Pasta Primavera
clear Italian dressing, any pasta, Frozen assorted vegs., 
Cook pasta al dente (not too soft); drain. Cook fro vegs also not too  mushy; if has big pieces in bag cut up to bite-size When each are cool and  well-drained, mix and pour on dressing; mix and serve. Is better if done an  hour or so before serving and refrigerated.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Adina Mishkoff <adina@amcha.org> on Aug 25,  1998,
