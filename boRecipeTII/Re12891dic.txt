Zebra Brownies
Eggs, Sugar, Brownie mix, Evaporated milk, Vegetable oil, Cream cheese, White chocolate, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease 13 x 9 x 2 in. pan. Combine brownie  mix, 3/4 cup evaporated milk, oil and eggs.  Mix well. Pour into pan. In  small bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar and remaining milk until light and  fluffy.  Beat in 1 cup grated white chocolate.  Drop cream cheese mixture  by spoonfuls on top of brownie mixture.  Using knife, swirl cream cheese  mixture through brownies, going all the way to the bottom of the pan and to  the edges.  Bake for 35-38 minutes.  Remove from oven, cool 15 minutes then  sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup white chocolate.  Cool completely before  cutting into 24 brownies.
