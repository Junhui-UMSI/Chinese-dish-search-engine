Wonton Soup
Wontons, Vegetable stock, on the diagonal, salt and pepper, Peanut oil, Bok choy, Scallions, thinly sliced, 
In a soup pot, saute the bok choy in the oil for 1 minute. Add the stock &  bring to a boil.  Lower the heat & simmer till the bok choy is just tender,  about 5 minutes. Season.    In a separate pot, bring about 2 quarts of water to a boil. Drop the  wontons into the boiling water & cook until tender. Drain & add to the  soup.  Serve garnished with scallions.
