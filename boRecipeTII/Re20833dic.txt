Apple Jelly
Or 2 tablespoons unflavored, Unsweetened apple juice, Lemon juice, Food coloring; if desired, Liquid artificial sweetener, 
In a saucepan, soften gelatin in apple and lemon juice. Bring to a rolling  boil, dissolving gelatin; boil 1 minute. Remove from heat. Stir in liquid  sweetener and food coloring. Pour hot into hot jars. Adjust caps. Store in  refrigerator up to three weeks.
