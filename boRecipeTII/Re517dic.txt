Freezer Slaw
Celery seed, Vinegar, Cabbage, Green bell pepper, Small onion, Sugar, Salt, 
Grate vegetables.    Mix remaining ingredients in saucepan and bring to boil. Pour over  vegetables. Mix, pack and freeze.    Thaw in refrigerator overnight. Remains crisp and is delicious.    Origin unknown.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by BobbieB1@aol.com on Aug 27, 1998
