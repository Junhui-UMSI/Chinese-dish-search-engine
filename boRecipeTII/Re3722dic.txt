Penne Pasta with Tuna
olive oil, Walnuts, Worcestershire Sauce, (7 oz) tuna; drained, Chopped parsley, Penne pasta, optional, Fresh basil leaves, Pepper, Salt, 
Put tuna, walnuts, lemon rind, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, basil, oil,  salt, and pepper into a food processor or blender. Cover and process or  blend until smooth. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain. Stir  in tuna mixture, coating pasta with sauce. Serve immediately. Makes 4 to 6  servings.
