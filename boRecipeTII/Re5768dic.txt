Pink Party Salad
Nuts, cottage cheese, Marshmallows, crushed pineapple, Strawberry Jello, Whipping cream, 
Heat juice from pineapple with marshmallows and Jello. Cool. Mix whipped  cream, pineapple, cottage cheese and nuts. Add first mixture and fold in.  Chill overnight.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Mar 16, 1998
