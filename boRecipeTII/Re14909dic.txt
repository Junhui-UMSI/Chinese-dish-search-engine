20-Minute Hamburger Skillet Stew
Dried thyme, Dry red wine OR tomato juice, 240 with broth and wine, THE REGISTER, Ribs celery, sliced wafer-, Salt OR garlic salt, All purp flour, Cal 230 with water OR, Onion, sliced wafer-thin, Boiling water, Bay Leaves, Carrots, sliced wafer-thin, Potatoes, halved, sliced, Pepper, Lean ground beef^, Beef extract OR bouillon, PER SERVING, 
Use melon baller and shape meat into tiny meatballs. Brown in skillet.  Discard any fat. Stir onion into skillet and cook 1 min. Add boiling water  or broth and extract or bouillon. Add remaining veggies, seasonings. Cover,  cook over low heat, 15 mins until vegs are tender. Combine wine or water  and flour in covered jar. Shake and stir into skillet. Cook and stir until  sauce is thick, 4 mins. S: The Slim Gourmet    Posted to MealMaster Recipes List, Digest #149    Date: Thu, 23 May 1996 19:35:21 -0700    From: Dan Schamber <dansch@haven.ios.com>
