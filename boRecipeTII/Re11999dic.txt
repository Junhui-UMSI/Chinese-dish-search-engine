Zesty Steak Salad From Lhj
Red pepper flakes, Soy sauce, Shredded daikon radish or, Grated fresh ginger, Trimmed watercress, Soy sauce, Garlic, Wasabi powder*, Julienned green onions, Vegetable oil, Black Pepper, Rice wine vinegar, Snow peas; trimmed, WASABI DRESSING, Salt, Beef flank steak, Sliced red radishes, SALAD, Thinly sliced romaine, Thinly sliced Napa cabbage, 
1. Prepare grill or heat broiler. Combine soy sauce and red pepper flakes  in pie plate. Add steak, turning to coat. Grill 5 minutes per side. Let  stand 5 minutes. Slice diagonally, very thin. Cook snow peas in boiling  water 30 seconds; rinse with cold water. Halve diagonally.    2. Make Wasabi Dressing: Meanwhile, puree ingredients in blender. Transfer  to large bowl.    3. Add all vegetables to dressing and toss to coat. Spoon onto plates and  top with sliced steak. Makes 6 servings.    *Wasabi powder is available in Asian markets or by mail from Mo-Hotta  Mo-Betta, 800-462-3220.
