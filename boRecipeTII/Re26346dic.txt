Tomato Basil Cheese Spread
cream cheese, milk, Garlic powder, Fontina cheese, fresh basil, sun-dried Tomatoes in oil, 
Directions;    In food processor bowl with metal blade, combine milk, cheeses and  garlic powder.  Process until smooth.  By hand, stir in tomatoes and  basil. Spoon into serving bowl. Serve with crackers.
