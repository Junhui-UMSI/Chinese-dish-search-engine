Awesome Pizza Dough
Sugar, Italian seasoning, Yeast, Salt, Fresh garlic, Bread flour, Water, olive oil, 
Date:    Sun, 23 Jun 1996 20:43:14 -0400    From:    Kari Bittner <TomBDoom@AOL.COM>  Enter ingredients into bread machine in the order listed.  Put bread  machine on dough cycle for large white loaf. When dough enters Rise cycle,  take out, flour lightly, place in large bowl and cover with very warm, damp  cloth. Set in warm place to rise. Let rise for about 1 1/2 hours.    Spread out, brush with olive oil, add toppings as desired.  (Final sprinkle  of Parmesan Cheese adds a great touch no matter what toppings you use.)    Bake for 10-12 minutes on pizza stone pre-heated for 20 minutes in 475  degree oven.    EAT-L DIGEST 22 JUNE 1996    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
