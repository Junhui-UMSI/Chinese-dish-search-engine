Annes Cheese Cookies
Stick butter; softened, Flour; sifted, Garlic salt and paprika to, Sharp cheddar cheese; grated, 
Combine cheese and butter.  Add remaining ingredients slowly.  Roll into  log. Wrap and chill. (Do not freeze!) Slice and bake at 350 degrees about  10 minutes. This will keep in refrigerator for 3 months!
