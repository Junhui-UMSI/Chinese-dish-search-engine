Zucchini and Tomatoes Basil
Zucchini(3/4 pound), Vegetable oil, Tomatoes, peeled and chopped, Dried basil, Salt, Pepper (up to 1/4), Diced onion, 
Cut zucchini into 1/4 inch slices; set aside. Saute onion and basil in oil  over medium heat 1 minute. Add zucchini, and cook 5 minutes, stirrring  occasionally. Add tomatoes, salt and pepper; cook 3 minutes or until  tender. Serve immediately. Makes 4-6 servings. Posted to EAT-L Digest by  Pam & KerryAnn Cobb <priss@AMARANTH.COM> on Jul 6, 1997
