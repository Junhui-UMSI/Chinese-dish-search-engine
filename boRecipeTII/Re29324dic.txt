Baked Sweet Potatoes with Sauteed Squash and Peppers
Yellow squash, olive oil, Pepper, Salt, Sweet potatoes, Cayenne pepper, Sour cream, Zucchini, 
Dice the zucchini, squash and red pepper. In large non-stick saute pan,  heat the olive oil. Add the diced vegetables. Slice sweet potatoes in half  and scoop out some filling. Place vegetables in sweet potato shell. Season  with salt and pepper. Serve with sour cream.
