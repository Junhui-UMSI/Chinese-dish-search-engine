Bayou Bean Soup
Onions; chopped, Lemon juice, Stalks celery, Mixed dry beans, Whole tomatoes; cut in, Green pepper, 
Soak beans overnight. Cook in 2 quarts water beef bone and ham. Add smoked  sausage and cook until done. Salt to taste. Serves 6.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 438 by "Diane Geary."  <diane@keyway.net> on Jan 2, 1998
