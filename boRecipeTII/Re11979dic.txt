Red and Green Coleslaw
Thinly sliced red onion, Celery seeds, Dried cranberries, DRESSING, Vegetable oil, Cider vinegar, Red cabbage; finely sliced, Sugar, Green cabbage; finely sliced, 
Combine cranberries, red and green cabbage and red onion in large bowl.  Toss with Dressing.  Cover and chill about 3 hours before serving. Stir and  drain off all liquid before serving. Makes 6 servings.     Each serving contains about: 173 calories; 8 mg sodium; 0 cholesterol;  13 grams fat; 17 grams carbohydrates; 1 gram protein; 0.69 gram fiber.  DRESSING:     Combine vinegar, oil, sugar and celery seeds and mix well to combine.  Makes about 1 cup.
