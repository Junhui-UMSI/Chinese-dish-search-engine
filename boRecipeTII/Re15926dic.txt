Adams Favorite Dessert - Kolacky
Additional powdered sugar, Flour, Solo fruit filling, Yolks, Egg, Almond extract, Vanilla extract, Unsalted butter, 
Cream butter and sugar.  Add egg yolks, one at a time, beating well and  flavorings.  Gradually add flour, dough will be stiff. Roll tablespoon size  into balls. Place well apart on greased tin. Make thumb print in each ball  and fill with Solo filling. Sift powdered sugar over when done.    Recipe By     :
