Tomatillo Salsa #1
Fresh cilantro; chopped, Salt; (optional), Jalapeno or Serrano chile;, Lime juice, Tomatillos; husks removed, Chicken broth, 
Rinse tomatillos; arrange in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast in  a 500 oven until slightly singed (about 15 minutes). Let cool. In a blender  or food processor, whirl tomatillos with cilantro and chile. Stir in broth  and lime juice; season to taste with salt, if desired. Makes 3 cups.  arielle@taronga.com (Stephanie da Silva) (rec.food.cooking)    CHILE-HEADS ARCHIVES    From the Chile-Heads recipe list.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
