From "Babys First Helpings" by Chris Casson Madden ISBN: 0-385-19143-X    This is another wonderful first food.  Remember: the riper the banana the  easier on babys digestive tract.    A little mothers milk or formula    Mash thoroughly and slowly mix in the milk.    As baby gets older you might want to try: milk, oatmeal, wheat germ, cooked  egg yolk or yogurt mixed in with the banana.
