Walnut Barley
Water, Walnut; chopped OR, Minced fresh parsley;, Pearl Barley;, Celery;, salt and pepper, 
Rinse barley under warm running water.  Let drain.  In a medium-size  saucepan, bring to a boil and gradually add barley.  Cover and cook  20 to 25 minutes, Return barley to saucepan with celery, nut, salt  and pepper; heat through.  Garnish with parsley.
