Polenta Taragna
Butter, plus 3 T, Polenta, quick cooking,, Buckwheat flour, Crescenza cheese, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.    Butter an oven proof dish with 3 tablespoons butter and set aside. In a 6  quart sauce pan, heat 8 cups water to a boil. Whisking furiously, slowly  drizzle in mixed flours until all are in. Pull pan to front of burner and  switch to a wooden spoon. Cook until texture of molten lava and add butter  and cheese. Stir though and pour into baking dish. Place in oven 8 to 10  minutes, remove and serve immediately.
