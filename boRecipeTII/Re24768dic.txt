Aunt Evelyns Carrot Cake
Sugar, Ginger, Vanilla, Salt, Flour, Cinnamon, Vegetable oil, Beaten eggs, Walnuts; finely chopped, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Nutmeg, Buttermilk, Grated carrots; tightly,

Pour batter into a greased and floured 9" x 13" baking  pan, and bake at 350 F. degrees for 45-60 minutes.
