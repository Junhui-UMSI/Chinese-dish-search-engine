Wraparounds
Soy sauce, Sugar 2; (5 oz.) cans water, Bacon; cut in half crosswise, Pitted dates, 
Combine soy sauce with sugar. Marinate chestnuts in mixture for  approximately 30 minutes. Wrap chestnuts with bacon. Secure with a wooden  toothpick. Place on broiler rack. Bake at 350 for 30-35 minutes or until  bacon is crisp.    Variation: Marinate water chestnuts in teriyaki sauce. Stuff pitted dates  with water chestnuts. Wrap with bacon and broil.
