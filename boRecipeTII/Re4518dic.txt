Pesto-Tomato Bread
Yeast, coarse chop, soft *, Water, Sugar, Salt, Water, Pesto sauce, Sun-dried tomatoes; (not, Bread flour, 
Measure carefully, placing all ingredients in bread machine pan in the  order recommended by the manufacturer.     Select basic/white cycle.  Use medium or light crust color.  Do not use    delay cycles.  Remove baked bread from pan and cool on wire rack.
