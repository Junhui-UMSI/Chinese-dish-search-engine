Lemon Muffins
Granulated sugar plus 1, ; divided, Ground cinnamon, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Butter or margarine, ; (1 stick) softened, Eggs, Lemon, Grated peel of 1 lemon, 
Recipe by: St. Louis Post-Dispatch 3/17/97 Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Combine 1 tablespoon sugar and cinnamon. Set aside.    Sift together flour, baking powder and salt.    In a large bowl, beat butter until light; gradually add 1/4 cup sugar,  beating until light and fluffy.    In a separate bowl, beat egg yolks with an electric mixer until thick and  light in color; stir into butter. Add dry ingredients alternately with  lemon juice.    In another bowl, using clean beaters, beat egg whites until foamy;  gradually add remaining 1/4 cup sugar, beating until soft peaks form.  Gently fold egg whites and lemon peel into batter. Spoon into 8 paper-lined  2 1/2-by-1 1/4-inch muffin cups, filling about three-quarters full. Top  with cinnamon sugar. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes, until lightly browned.    Yield: 8 muffins.
