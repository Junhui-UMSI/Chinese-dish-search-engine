Apple Raisin Walnut Cake
Walnuts, Ground cloves, Finely chopped apple, Baking soda, in 2/3 c water, Nutmeg, White flour, Cinnamon, Raisins, Whole-wheat flour, Salt, Egg whites, whipped, Sugar, Margarine, 
Cream the margarine and sugar.  Add dry ingredients and water alternately,  then apple.  Dust the raisins and nuts with a small amount of flour and add  them to the batter. Fold in the egg whites. Bake in a 8 in. square nonstick  pan at 350F for 30 to 40 min. or until firm. Cal: 295; Fat 2 1/5g.
