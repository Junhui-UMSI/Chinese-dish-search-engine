Tomato Sauce
Onion, Butter, Salt, Tomatoes (28 oz), Cloves, Black pepper, Flour, 
In saucepan, cook tomatoes with onion, cloves, pepper and salt 20  minutes. Remove onion and cloves. Push tomato through strainer with  back of wooden spoon, or puree in food processor.    Melt butter in saucepan. Stir in flour and continue stirring until  golden and frothing. Stir in tomato pulp and bring to boil. Keep warm  until ready to serve.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
