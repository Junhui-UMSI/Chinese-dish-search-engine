Curried Pumpkin Soup
Onions, Garlic, Margarine, Curry powder, Salt, Ground white pepper, Chicken broth, (15-ounce) pumpkin, (12-ounce) skim evaporated, 
Place onion, garlic and margarine in a 2-quart glass measure or casserole.  Cover with vented plastic wrap or lid; microwave on high 3 minutes. Stir in  curry powder, salt and pepper; microwave on high 1 minute.    Blend in broth and pumpkin; cover and microwave on high 10 minutes. Stir in  milk. Pour half of soup into a blender and pur?e until smooth. Repeat with  remaining soup. Makes 8 (scant 1 cup) servings, each: 83 calories.
