Tomaot and Hot Green Pepper Salad, Essaouira Style
Tomatoes, peeled, seeded &, olive oil, Cumin, Cucumber, peeled, centre, Lemon jiuice, core removed & diced, salt and pepper, Green bell peppers, grilled,, cubed, Chili peppers, seeded & dice, Parsley, chopped, Garlic cloves, chopped, seeded & diced, 
Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl & blend gently. Refrigerate  one hour before serving.    Paula Wolfert, "Couscous & Other Good Food From Morocco"  Posted to FOODWINE Digest 12 Dec 96    From:    Joell Abbott <abbott@ZIP.COM.AU>    Date:    Fri, 13 Dec 1996 10:29:59 +0000
