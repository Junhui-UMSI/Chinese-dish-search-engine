Quick Shrimp Etouffee
Salt and pepper to taste, Butter, parsley, All-purpose flour, Shrimp, Garlic, tomatoes, canned, 
Blend tomatoes including liquid until fine.  In skillet, make roux of  flour and butter.  Add garlic and continue browning until tender. Add  tomatoes, shrimp, salt and pepper. Simmer for 15 to 20 min. Add chopped  parsley before removing from heat.  Serve over hot fluffy rice. Courtesy  Telephone Pioneers  :          BillSpalding *P CRBR 38 A
