Tomato-Meat Sauce
Lean ground beef, Garlic, Red wine, Salt, Sugar, Fresh tomatoes; peeled,, Basil leaves, Onion, American Heart Association, 
In large skillet, combine beef, onion and garlic; cook until beef is no  longer pink. Set aside. In blender, combine remaining ingredients; process  for 30 seconds. Add tomato mixture to meat and vegetables; simmer for 20  minutes. Refrigerate leftovers.
