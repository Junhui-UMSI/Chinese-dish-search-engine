Quick Crustless Fudge Pie
Eggs, Semisweet chocolate chips, Brown sugar, Sweet butter, Dark or amber rum, Flour, Chopped walnuts for topping, Coarsely chopped walnuts, Instant coffee powder, 
Date:    Tue, 11 Jun 1996 21:12:20 -0400    From:    Linda Robinson <CatRescuer@AOL.COM>    Preheat oven to 350x F.  Grease a 9" glass pie plate.  In a 2-quart glass  bowl, combine 4 Tblsp. sweet butter and 12 oz. semisweet chocolate chips.  Heat in a microwave oven on high 1 1/14 to 1 3/4 minutes, until mixture is  melted and smooth when stirred.  Stir in 1/4 c. dark or amber rum, 2 tsp.  instant coffee powder, 3/4 c. packed brown sugar, 3 eggs and 1/4 c. flour.  Mix until smooth and well blended.  Stir in 1 c. of coarsely chopped  walnuts. Turn batter into prepared pie plate. Sprinkle another 1/2 c. of  chopped walnuts on top. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until puffed. Pie should  still be moist inside. Do not overbake. Let pie cool to room temperature  before serving, topped with whipped cream. Makes 8 servings.    EAT-L DIGEST 10 JUNE 1996    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
