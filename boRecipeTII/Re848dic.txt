Homemade Vanilla-Bottled Gifts
High quality bourbon OR, Fresh vanilla beans, 
Mix some medium good to high quality BOURBON with a few FRESH VANILLA  BEANS. Cut open beans length-wise (slit them). Put in a big crock or jar  with boubon (lots). Let age for 2-3 months. Transfer to smaller bottles.  Decorate with cute label and red/green ribbon. Good homemade vanilla.  Include a recipe using Vanilla (cookies, french toast, etc.) * Some people  like to make vanilla using vodka instead of bourbon.
