Corn n Salsa Meat Loaf - Lf
Vegetable cooking spray, VEGETABLES, Finely chopped red bell, SEASONINGS, Finely chopped fresh, Chili powder, Salt, Pepper, MAIN INGREDIENTS, Extra lean ground beef -, Prepared polenta; see pantry, Corn kernels; drained, Tomato salsa, Egg substitute; or one-large, OPTIONAL TOPPINGS, Tomato salsa; approximately, SIDE DISHES, Potatoes; sliced and boiled, Cooked lima beans, Applesauce; unsweetened, 
Heat oven to 350F. Have ready a 13 x 9-in. baking dish and larger roasting  pan.    PANTRY NOTE: Prepared polenta can come in 12 and 16-oz ounce chubs. Remove  whats needed for this recipe and cut it into 1/4-inch cubes. Chubs may be  kept in the refrigerator for about 5 days.    Coat a large nonstick skillet with vegetable oil spray. Add vegetables and  saute until slighty browned. Let cool. Stir in Seasonings and Main  Ingredients. Shape mixture, in baking dish, allowing a 1/2 inch gap between  the loaf and the pan. Set dish in roasting pan and add Topping. Place in  oven; add boiling water to come halfway up sides of baking dish. Bake until  center of loaf registers 160F on an instant-read thermometer. Remove dish  from roasting pan. Drain if necessary. Let loaf stand 5 minutes before  slicing.    RECIPE NOTES: Meatloaf with 1 large egg: 262 cals, 12g fat (41% cff).
