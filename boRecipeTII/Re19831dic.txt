Apple and Bean Slaw
Red Delicious apples; in, Salt, Red kidney beans; drained, Mayonnaise, Catsup, Lemon juice, Shredded cabbage, 
At least 20 minutes before serving or a day ahead, for garnish remove 6-8  outer leaves of cabbage and reserve. Shred 2 cups of cabbage from sm. head.  In large bowl, stir mayonnaise, catsup, lemon juice and salt. Add cabbage,  beans and apple. Toss gently until well coated. Cover and refrigerate. To  serve, line platter or bowl with reserved leaves and fill with slaw  mixture.
