Pesto-Stuffed Steaks
Finely shredded Parmesan, Finely shredded Parmesan, Olive or vegetable oil, Beef rib eye steaks, 1 1/2 ", Pesto, 
Heat coals or gas grill.    Make a horizontal cut in side of each beef steak, forming a pocket (do not  cut through to opposite side).    Mix pesto and 2 tablespoons cheese; spread evenly on insides of pockets;  press pockets closed.  Drizzle oil over beef. Roll beef in 1/3 cup cheese  until well coated.    Cover and grill beef 4 to 5 inches from medium heat 12 to 14 minutes for  medium doneness, turning once.    To serve, cut each beef steak into thick strips. Makes 4 servings.
