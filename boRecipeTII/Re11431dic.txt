Raspberry and Fig Gratin
Sugar, brown, Cream, sour, thinned with, 2 tb milk, Raspberries, fresh, Figs, peeled, halved, 
Layer the raspberries in a gratin dish.  Pour the sour cream over the  raspberries and top with figs.  Sprinkle generously with brown sugar.         Run under a broiler to brown, then serve warm.         Source: Great Chefs of San Francisco, Avon Books, 1984         Chef:   Jeremiah Tower, Santa Fe Bar and Grill, Berkeley, CA    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mm-gc-sf.zip
