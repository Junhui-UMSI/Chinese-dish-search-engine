Yam-Yam Sweet Potato Casserole
Sweet potatoes, Butter, Butter, Vanilla, Flour, Brown sugar, TOPPING, Eggs, Nuts, Granulated sugar, 
Mix sweet potatoes, 1/2 cup butter, granulated sugar, eggs & vanilla  together & pour into buttered casserole dish. Mix topping ingredients with  fork until crumbly. Sprinkle on top of casserole. Bake at 350 degrees for       20    minutes.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 25 November 96    Date:    Tue, 26 Nov 1996 12:57:27 -0500    From:    Helen Jukes <NJukes@AOL.COM>
