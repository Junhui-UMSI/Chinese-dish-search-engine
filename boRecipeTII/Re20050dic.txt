Vanilla Buttercream Frosting
1% milk, Vanilla, Butter, 
Beat all ingredients in a medium bowl with mixer on medium-low speed until  blended. Increase speed to high; beat until smooth and fluffy.    Lemon Variation: Prepare vanilla frosting with the following changes: Omit  milk and vanilla extract; add 1 tsp grated lemon peel and 1/4 cup lemon  juice. (To give frosting a more lemony color, beat in 3 drop yellow food  color.)
