Ancho Ranchero Sauce
Juice of 3 limes, Ancho peppers, Whole ear of corn, cut, olive oil, Onions, Cilantro,; tied together, Vegetable or chicken stock;, Tomatoes, Corn tortillas,; cut into, Salt, Garlic, Jalapeno,; seeded and, Chopped carrots, Maple syrup, 
Seed Ancho peppers, soak in hot water until soft, then puree in a food  processor or blender. Roast garlic and tomatoes in a 350 degree oven until  softened and darkened in spots. Heat oil in a medium saucepan and saute  onions until golden. Add carrots and saute until tender, about 4 minutes.  Add roasted garlic and tomatoes, jalapeno, corn, tortilla strips, cilantro  and Ancho puree. Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove and  discard corn and cilantro. Strain sauce.
