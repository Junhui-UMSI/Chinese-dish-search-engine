Vanilla Whipped Cream
Lengthwise, Heavy cream, Vanilla bean -- split, 
Over medium heat, scald cream and vanilla bean; remove from heat. Cover and  steep for 10 minutes. Scrape seeds into cream; discard pod.    Strain cream into a bowl set in an ice-water bath. Let stand, stirring  occasionally, until cold, 10 to 15 minutes.    Transfer to a large chilled metal bowl, add sugar and whip by hand into  soft  peaks, about 5 minutes.  Makes 1 3/4 cups.    Total prep and cooking time: 30-45 minutes.
