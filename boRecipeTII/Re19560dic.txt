Aniseed Cookies
Eggs, Baking Powder, Powered sugar; sifted, Pinch salt, Aniseed; ground, Flour, all purpose, 
Grease and flour baking sheets.   Beat egg yolks, powdered sugar and salt in a large bowl until pale and  creamy. Beat egg whites until very stiff; fold into egg yolk mixture.  Siftflour, baking powder and aniseed onto egg mixture; fold in quickly but  thoroughly. Fill a pastry bag fitted with a plain nozzle with cookie  mixture. Pipe in small rounds onto prepared baking sheets. Let stand  overnight to dry out.   Preheat oven to 325. Bake cookies 20 minutes. Cool on rack.
