Tabor City Yam Bread
Ingredients alternately, Milk; (1/3 to 1/2), = creamy consistency:, Butter, Baking Powder, Sift together:, All-purpose flour, Salt, Beat in thoroughly:, Cinnamon, Add:, Pecans, Sugar, Add to mixture:, Ground cloves, Eggs, Cooked/ mashed sweet, Nutmeg, To wet mixture add dry, =blend together to, Baking soda, 
Recipe by: Inn recipes Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Butter 8 x 8 loaf pan.  Pour into loaf pan and bake for 50-55 minutes. Top with caramel icing if  desired. Freezes well. Microwave to reheat. May also be baked in lined  muffin tins at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes or, in madeline pan at 400  degrees for 10 minutes.JM. From The Yam Capital of the World!
