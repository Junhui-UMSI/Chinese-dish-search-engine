Turkey Tortellini Soup
Cheese-filled tortellini, Gingerroot, turkey, Water, bok choy, Green onions, White wine vinegar, Soy sauce, Enoki mushrooms, Rice wine vinegar, Chicken broth, Ground ginger, Condensed chicken broth, 
Recipe by: Betty Crockers Cookbook Cook tortellini as directed on package;  drain. Heat water, vinegar, soy sauce, chicken broth and gingerroot to  boiling in 3-quart saucepan; reduce heat. Stir in bok choy stems (reserve  leaves), turkey and onions. Simmer 15 minutes. Stir in bok choy leaves and  mushrooms. Simmer just until leaves are wilted.   4 SERVINGS (ABOUT    1-1/2    CUPS EACH); 350 CALORIES.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #135 by novmom@juno.com (Angela Gilliland)  on May 15, 1997
