Basic White Bread
Bread flour (16 oz), Dry milk (1/3 oz), Butter (3/5 oz), Sugar (9/10 oz), Salt (1/3 oz), Water (11 fl oz), Dry Yeast*, 
*If Fleishmans yeast, add 2  tsp yeast. Add flour, milk, salt, butter ,  sugar then the water. Best when cooked on Basic; light crust cycle. Make 1  lb. loaf in the Panasonic.    **From Panasonic Manual SD-BT65P
