Swiss-Tuna Grill
Onion, Pepper, Finely chopped celery, Rye bread, Sour cream, Shredded swiss cheese, Mayonnaise, Tuna; drained & flaked, Butter or margarine, 
Combine tuna, cheese, celery, onion, mayonnaise, sour cream and pepper,  stirring well. Spread mix on 6 slices bread, top with remaining bread  slices. Spread butter on outer sides of bread, and grill over medium heat  in a large skillet until browned, turning once.
