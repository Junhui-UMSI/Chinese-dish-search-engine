American Farm Style Sausage #1
Water, Ground nutmeg, Ground mace, Medium ground pork butt, Ground cloves, Medium ground veal, Fine, dry bread crumbs, Salt, Pepper, Ground sage, 
Mix all ingredients together and stuff into sheep casing or make into  patties. SOURCE: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry Predika Collection  of Clarence Fontish    Submitted By JIM BODLE   On   11-10?94
