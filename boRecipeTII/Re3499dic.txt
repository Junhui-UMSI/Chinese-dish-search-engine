Pecan Pie
(firmly packed), Eggs, Vanilla, Unbaked 9" pastry shell, C and H Golden Brown Sugar, Salt, Corn syrup (light or dark), Butter or Margarine, Pecans (halves or pieces), 
In mixing bowl combine sugar and eggs; beat well.  Beat in syrup, salt,  vanilla and butter.  Stir in pecans.  Pour into unbaked pastry shell.  (Garnish with pecan halves, if desired.)  Bake at 375 F for 40 to 45  minutes, or until filling is firm in center when gently shaken.  Cool.
