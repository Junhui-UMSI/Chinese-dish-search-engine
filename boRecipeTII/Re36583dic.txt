Strawberry Avocado Parfait
Orange juice -- frozen, Strawberries, Avocados; seed, peel --, Cube, Strawberry yogurt, Shredded coconut, Brown sugar, Concent, 
Preparation Time: 0:10 Place strawberries in a bowl; lightly mash with  fork.  Add avocado cubes and gently mix together. Spoon strawberry/avocado  mixture into four long-stemmed wine glasses. Combine yogurt, orange juice  concentrate and brown sugar in a small bowl. Drizzle yogurt mixture over  strawberries and avocado and sprinkle with coconut.
