Zucchini Bread 1
Walnuts, optional, Vegetable oil, Salt, Baking soda, Grated zucchini, Eggs, Flour, Cinnamon, Vanilla, Sugar, Baking Powder, 
Mix wet ingredients, then add dry ingredients. Pour into loaf pans (2).  Bake for an hour at 350.    Recipe from Charlo Zauss, El Cajon.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on May 26, 98
