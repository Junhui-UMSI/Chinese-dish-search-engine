Picnic Rice Salad
Tomato, fresh cut wedges, Can tuna fish, shredded, Any leftovers, Salt, Eggs, hard-boiled, quartered, Rice, cooked, still firm, Olive or salad oil, Vinegar or lemon juice, Mustard, Celery, diced, FRENCH DRESSING, Pepper, 
Beat the seasonings and oil for the dressing in a bowl (with a fork) for  one minute then add vinegar and beat until mixed, or shake dressing  ingredients in a small jar.    Toss the salad ingredients with the dressing and store in a jar in the  refrigerator with screw-top lid.    Source:  The I Never Cooked Before Cookbook by Jo Coudert c. 1963 Shared by  Elizabeth Rodier 6/93
