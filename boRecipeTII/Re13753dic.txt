Wild Rice Tuna Skillet
Sliced green onions; if, (6.5-oz) solid white tuna;, Sour Cream, Water, Mayonnaise, Butter or margarine, 
Combine mayonnaise and sour cream; set aside. Remove and reserve bread  crumb packet from package. Combine water, butter and contents of rice and  seasoning packets in 10-inch skillet. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover  and simmer 5 minutes. Sitr in tuna. Remove from heat and stir in contenst  of reserved bread crumb packet. Spoon mayonnaise over center of tuna  mixture. Sprinkle green onions over top. Cover and let stand 5 minutes, or  until water is absorbed. Makes 4 servings.
