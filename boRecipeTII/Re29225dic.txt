Baked Spaghetti Casserole
Salt, Sesame seeds, Potato, Sesame oil, Garlic, Tomato, fresh, Scallions, Spaghetti, cooked, Thyme, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Lightly grease 4 x 8 baking pan with sesame  oil. Bake potato for 40 minutes. When cooled, cut into 1/2-inch cubes.  Combine all ingredients together. Lower heat to 375 degrees.  Transfer to a  baking pan and bake for 15 minutes.  Serves 2.
