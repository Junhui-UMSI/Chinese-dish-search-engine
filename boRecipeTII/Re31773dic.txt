Syrup Cake
Eggs, Sugar, Flour, Boiling water, Cane syrup, Baking soda, Ginger, Ground cloves, Nuts; optional, Cooking oil, 
Mix soda in boiling water; add syrup. Mix dry ingredients together; combine  the two mixture; add oil. Add beaten eggs last; nuts optional. An electric  mixer is not necessary. Pour mixture into a greased and floured loaf pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
