Peanut Butter Cereal Treats
Nonstick cooking spray, corn oil, Light corn syrup, Sugar, Reduced-fat peanut butter, Crispy rice cereal, 
Spray 8- or 9-inch square baking pan with cooking spray. Pour cereal into  large bowl.    In 2-quart microwaveable bowl, combine sugar, corn syrup and peanut butter.  Zap on high (100 percent), stirring twice, 3 1/2 to 4 minutes or until  mixture is smooth and sugar is dissolved.    Pour over cereal; stir to coat. Press evenly into prepared pan.    Cool about 15 minutes. Invert onto cutting board. Cut. Makes about 36 (1  1/2-inch) bars.    Hint: Slip a plastic bag over your hand before pressing mixture into pan.
