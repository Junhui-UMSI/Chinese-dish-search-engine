Quickie Eggs Benedict
Poached eggs, Cheddar cheese soup, Canadian bacon; fried, English muffins; halved, 
Warm muffins. Place one thin slice of Canadian Bacon on top of muffin. Top  with poached egg and warmed cheddar cheese soup, diluted somewhat with  milk.
