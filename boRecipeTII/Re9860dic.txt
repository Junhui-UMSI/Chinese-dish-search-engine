Pumpkin-Date Bread
Cinnamon, Eggs, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Water, Baking soda, Salt, Pumpkin, Nutmeg, Flour, Walnuts, chopped, Baking Powder, Dates, 
Sift dry ingredients. Mix oil, water, pumpkin & eggs. Blend into dry  ingredients. Fold in dates and nuts. Pour into greased and floured loaf  pan. Bake at 325 degrees for 1 1/2 hours.    Posted by Judy Garnett/Raleigh NC - pjxg05a
