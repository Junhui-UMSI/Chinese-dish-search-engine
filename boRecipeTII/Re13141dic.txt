Wonderful and Easy Potato Casserole
Cream of chicken soup, Onion, Cheddar cheese -- shredded, Crushed cornflakes, Browns -- thawed, Sour cream, Handfuls, Crushed garlic, Bags frozen shredded hash, 
Saute onion and garlic in butter until soft.  In large bowl mix, hash  browns, soup, sour cream and 3/4 of cheese.  Add onion mixture and mix  well. Put in 9x13 pan sprayed with Pam top with remaining cheese and  cornflakes. Bake 35 minutes at 350. I got 3 proposals after serving this at  a superbowl party.  Guys really like it. Feeds 10-12.
