Avocado and Grapefruit Salad
Poppy seed Sauce, avocado, Red onion, Pink grapefruit, 
Slice avocado into 8 wedges.  Arrange equal amounts of avocado and  grapefruit on 4 salad dishes and top each with 2 onio slices.  Dress  each salad with 1 tablespoon sauce.
