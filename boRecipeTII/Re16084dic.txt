Warm Parsnip Salad
Garlic, Dijon mustard, Salt, White wine vinegar, Julienne-cut parsnip,, Julienne-cut red bell, Leaf lettuce leaves, Thinly sliced green onions, Julienne-cut celery,, Pepper, Vegetable cooking spray, 
Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray, and place skillet over  medium-high heat. Add parsnip, celery, red bell pepper, and green onions;  saute 4 minutes or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.    Combine vinegar and next 4 ingredients, stirring with a wire whisk, and add  to skillet. Cook 30 seconds, stirring constantly. Yield: 3 servings  (serving size: 1 cup).    Per serving: 210 Calories; 2g Fat (9% calories from fat); 12g Protein; 40g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 236mg Sodium    NOTES : Spoon the parsnip mixture onto 3 lettuce-lined salad plates, and  serve warm.
