Beefy Tofu Enchiladas
browned and drained, Ground beef, Thick tomato salsa, Dried cilantro, Cheddar cheese, 8-inch soft flour tortillas, Chopped green chilies (4oz), Firm tofu; mashed, Diced tomato, drained, Cumin seed, Onions, Garlic, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with  non-stick spray. In bowl, combine all ingredients except tortillas,  tomato salsa and cheese. Place 1/2 cup of mixture in center of each  tortilla and roll. Place in baking dish, seam side down. Pour salsa over  enchiladas. Sprinkle with shredded cheese. Cover pan with aluminum foil  and bake for 25 - 30 minutes.    Yields 8 servings.
