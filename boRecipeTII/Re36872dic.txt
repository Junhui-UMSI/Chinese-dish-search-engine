Strawberry Salsa
green pepper, Fresh strawberries, jalapeno pepper, Fresh lime juice, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Fresh cilantro, yellow bell pepper, Red onion, red bell pepper, Fresh orange juice, Sliced, Salt and pepper, 
Place all the ingredients in a large mixing bowl and toss to combine.  Cover  and refrigerate at least 2 hours or up to 4 hours.  Fifteen minutes before  serving, remove the salsa from the refrigerator, so it loses some of its  chill.
