Baked Apples W/ Raisin Co
WATER; HOT, BUTTER PRINT SURE, Salt, COCONUT SWEETNED PRE, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, APPLE FRESH, RAISINS #10, CINNAMON GROUND 1 LB CN, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN                    TEMPERATURE:  375 F. OVEN  :    1.  SCORE APPLES ONCE AROUND MIDDLE TO PREVENT BURSTING.  PLACE 33 APPLES  ON EACH PAN.    2.  MIX RAISINS WITH PREPARED, SWEETENED, FLAKED COCONUT.  FILL CAVITY IN  CENTER OF EACH APPLE WITH 1 TBSP MIXTURE.    3.  MIX SUGAR, CINNAMON AND SALT THOROUGHLY.    4.  COMBINE WITH WATER AND BUTTER OR MARGARINE.  POUR 1 1/2 QT SYRUP OVER  APPLES IN EACH PAN.    5.  BAKE 45 MINUTES OR UNTIL TENDER, BASTING OCCASSIONALLY.    6.  SERVE EACH APPLE WITH 2 TBSP (1-AA LADLE) SYRUP.  :    NOTE:  IN STEP 1, USE 40 LB FRESH APPLES A.P. WILL YIELD 33 LB 14 OZ CORED  APPLES.
