Tomatoes and Okra
Fresh tomatoes; peeled,, Okra; cut in 1/2-inch pieces, salt and pepper, Garlic, Onion, Bell pepper; chopped, Bacon, 
Fry bacon until crisp. Remove from grease. Cook okra in drippings and add  green pepper, onion and garlic to drippings. Stir in tomatoes and cooked  bacon. Add salt and pepper as needed. Cover skillet and cook 15 minutes.
