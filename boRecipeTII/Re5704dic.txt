Pineapple-Lime Punch
Lemons; juice of, Oranges; juice of, Limes; juice of, Sugar, Unsweetened pineapple juice, Ginger ale, Plain soda water, Green food coloring, 
Combine fruit juices and sugar. Chill thoroughly. Just before serving, add  ginger ale and soda water. Tint a delicate green, if you wish. Yield: 35  cups.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
