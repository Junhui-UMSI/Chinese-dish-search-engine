Tuna Luau
Cider vinegar, MSG or lemon juice, Cornstarch, Prepared mustard, Brown sugar, Green pepper, cut in strips, Rice, Tuna; drained and flaked, Water, Pineapple tidbits (reserve, 
Put tuna into skillet.  Add liquid from pineapple, sugar, cornstarch, MSG,  vinegar and mustard, and water. Cook, stirring, until thickened. Simmer 10  minutes, then add pineapple and green pepper strips, and simmer another 10  minutes.  Serve over hot rice.    From:  The Tuna Cookbook
