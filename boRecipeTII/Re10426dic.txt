Quick Coffee Cake (Biscui
BISCUIT MIX #10, All Purpose Flour, IMITATION VANILLA, BUTTER PRINT SURE, EGGS SHELL, SUGAR; BROWN, 2 LB, CINNAMON GROUND 1 LB CN, BUTTER PRINT SURE, IMITATION VANILLA, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, WATER; BOILING, Water, SUGAR; POWDER 2 LB, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN             TEMPERATURE:  375 F. OVEN    1.  COMBINE EGGS,VANILLA, AND WATER IN MIXER BOWL; BLEND AT LOW  SPEED.    2.  COMBINE BISCUIT MIX AND SUGAR; ADD TO EGG MIXTURE; MIX AT  MEDIUM SPEED UNTIL WELL BLENDED.    3.  POUR AN EQUAL AMOUNT OF BATTER INTO EACH GREASED AND FLOURED PAN.  SET ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 5.    4.  COMBINE FLOUR, CINNAMON, SALT, BROWN SUGAR, AND BUTTER OR  MARGARINE; MIX UNTIL MIXTURE RESEMBLES COARSE CORNMEAL.    5.  SPRINKLE AN EQUAL AMOUNT OF TOPPING OVER BATTER IN EACH PAN.    6.  BAKE ABOUT 30 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE.    7.  COMBINE BUTTER OR MARGARINE, POWDERED SUGAR, VANILLA, AND WATER;  MIX UNTIL SMOOTH .    8.  DRIZZLE AN EQUAL AMOUNT OF GLAZE OVER EACH BAKED CAKE WHILE CAKES  ARE STILL WARM.    9.  CUT 6 BY 9.
