Barbecue Sauce with Mustard
Vinegar, Salt, Vegetable oil, Molasses, Ground thyme, Prepared mustard, Cayenne pepper, Ground oregano, Pepper, Sugar, Cornstarch, Ketchup, 
Combine first seven ingredients in a small saucepan.  Stir in enough  vinegar to make a paste.  Combine molasses, ketchup, mustard, oil and  remaining vinegar; add to herb paste. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes. Remove from heat; cool completely. Pour  into a glass jar; cover tightly.  Store refrigerated for up to 3 months.  Baste over chicken, turkey, ham or hot dogs.
