Asparagus Mold
Asparagus liquid, Cream cheese; (8 oz.), Canned asparagus, Cream of asparagus soup; (10, Grated onion, Mayonnaise, Celery, Envelopes unflavored gelatin, Sliced green olives, 
Soften gelatin in asparagus liquid. Warm soup, add gelatin and other  ingredients. Mix well. Chill in oiled mold.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
