Potatoe Wedges
Mrs. Dash Seasoning, Unsalted margarine/butter, Mrs. Dash Seasoning, baking potatoes, cheese, Sour cream, 
Sour Cream Dip: Combine sour cream and Mrs. Dash for dip, cover and chill.   Potatoes: Preheat oven to 375 F. Scrub potatoes, pat dry and cut into 8  wedges. Melt butter in a pan, add remaining Mrs. Dash. Dip potatoe wedges  into butter, coat all sides. Place in a single layer in a baking dish. Cut  side down. Spoon remaining butter over potatoes. Bake 20-25 minutes or  until brown. If desired, sprinkle shredded cheese over potatoes and bake  anotheer 3-5 minutes until cheese is melted.   Serve with sour cream dip or steak sauce.
