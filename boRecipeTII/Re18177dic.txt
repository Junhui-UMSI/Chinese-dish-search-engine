Venison (Deer-Elk-Etc) Swiss Steak
Beef bouillon, Beer, Brown sugar, Black pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Onion, Carrot, Salt, Bacon grease, Stewed tomatoes, Round steak, Flour, Ground marjoran, Salt and pepper to taste, 
Dredge your meat pieces in salted and peppered flour with the dry mustard  sprinkled over it and brown pieces in bacon grease. Put in covered  casserole or crock pot and cover with a mixture of all the other  ingredients. Add enough liquid(stock or water) to make sure pieces will be  under. Bake covered at 300 degrees F. for 2 1/2-3 hrs until meat is tender.  Take off cover and bake until sauce is good and thick.
