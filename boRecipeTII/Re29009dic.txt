Baked Penne and Sun-Dried Tomato Sausage with Ricotta
Penne pasta, Washed spinach; coarsely, salt and pepper, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Links Aidells sun-dried, Fresh tomatoes; coarsely, Low fat ricotta cheese, 
Preheat oven to 350? F. While pasta is cooking to al dente, heat olive oil  in a skillet. Saute sausage 2 to 3 minutes. Add spinach and cook until  wilted.    In a large mixing bowl combine sausage, spinach, tomatoes and ricotta. Mix  pasta with other ingredients in a bowl. Adjust seasoning to taste. Place in  a shallow baking dish and bake for 20 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
