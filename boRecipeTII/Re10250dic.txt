Quick and Easy Valentine Cake
2-Layer Cake Mix, Chocolate Candy Hearts, White Fluffy Frosting, 
Make cake according to directions on box. When cool, frost with your  favorite white frosting. Decorate with chocolate-shaped candy hearts.
