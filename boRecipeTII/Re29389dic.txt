Baked Vidalia Onions
Medium sized Vidalia onions, Buttered bread crumbs, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Peel onions.  Cut in half, crosswise.  Arrange with cut sides up in a  greased 1 1/2 quart casserole. Add just enough wine to cover bottom of  casserole. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cover and bake for 30 minutes at  375 degrees F. Top with bread crumbs mixed with cheese. Bake uncovered for  15 to 20 minutes longer or until onions are brown and tender. Makes 6  servings.
