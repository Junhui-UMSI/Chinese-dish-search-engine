Blueberry Muffins
Granulated white sugar, Granulated white sugar, milk, Baking Powder, TOSS TOGETHER, Fresh blueberries, White wheat flour, Salt, White wheat flour, Butter, Egg, 
Preheat the oven to 375F. Grease muffin tin. Mix flour, baking powder,salt  and sugar in a large bowl. Add the egg, milk and butter, stirring only  enough to dampen the flour; the batter should not be smooth. To add  blueberries: mix together second measure of sugar and flour, sprinkle it  over blueberries, toss gently and stir them into batter last. Spoon into  the muffin pans filling each cup about 2/3 full. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes.    Recipe by Mangia!
