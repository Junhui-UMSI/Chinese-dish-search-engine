Quick Chicken Mole in Tortillas (Mf)
Canned green chiles; chopped, Cloves, Chopped cilantro for garnish, Cloves garlic minced, Chicken broth, Smoked skinless chicken, olive oil, Ground cumin, Sour cream; for garnish, Golden raisins, Onions; finely chopped, Tomatoes; chopped, Ground cinnamon, Dryroasted unsalted peanuts, Boneless skinless chicken, Dried Oregano, Sesame seeds, Unsweetened chocolate;, Flour tortillas, Ground chili powder, 
Saute the onions and garlic in the oil for about 10 minutes or until  tender. Add the tomatoes, chicken broth and green chilis and simmer,  uncovered for 15 minutes, stirring frequently. Meanwhile in a dry iron  skillet toast the sesame seeds and peanuts for a few minutes or until they  begin to smell roasted, then add the cinnamon, cloves, cumin, oregano and  chili powder and toast for a few seconds. Remove and cool slightly, then  puree with the raisins to form a paste.    Add the chicken to the sauce and simmer 3 minutes to cook through; add the  smoked chicken and simmer another 3 minutes to warm through. Add the  chocolate and simmer a minute until melted. Stir in the paste and simmer  until thickened to the proper consistency. Meanwhile warm the tortillas on  the oven.    To assemble, put some spoonfuls of chicken and sauce in the middle of a  warm tortillas and roll up, top with more spoonfuls of sauce, sour cream  and cilantro.
