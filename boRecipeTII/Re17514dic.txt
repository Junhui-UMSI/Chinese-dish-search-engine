Almond Coconut Christmas Balls
Almond extract, Shredded coconut, Cream, Green food coloring, Red food coloring, Butter, 
Mix together the confectioners sugar, 1 cup coconut, creama, almond  extract and melted butter. Stir until all are well combined.    Now divide the mixture into three equal parts. color one pink to red, one  green and leave the other one white. Shape into 3/4 - 1 inch fat balls. Now  roll the bals in rest of coconut and let stand about 24 hours to firm up.    Makes 2 to 3 dozen balls.,    Recipe by: Army Times 1978    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "abprice@wf.net" <abprice@wf.net> on Apr 13,  1998
