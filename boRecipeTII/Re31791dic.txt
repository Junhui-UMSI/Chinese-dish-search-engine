Szechuan Broccoli
Crushed red pepper; (up to, Garlic, Sesame seeds, Soy sauce, Rice or cider vinegar, Sugar, Coarsely chopped broccol, Vegetable oil, Peeled; minced ginger, 
Combine soy sauce, rice vineger, and sugar in a small bowl; set aside. Heat  a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add sesame seeds; cook 1 minute or  until browned. Remove seeds, and set aside.    Add oil and next 3 ingredients to skillet; stir-fry 30 seconds. Add  broccoli; stir-fry 1 minute. Add soy sauce mixture; stir well. Cover and  cook 2 minutes or until broccoli is crisp-tender. Sprinkle with sesame  seeds.
