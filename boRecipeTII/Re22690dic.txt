Apricot Ginger Conserve
Cold water, Sliced, candied ginger, Grated orange rind, Lemon juice, Dried apricots, Sugar, Orange juice, Lemon Rind, Pecans, 
Wash apricots.  Add water, ginger, orange and lemon juice, grated rind, and  sugar.  Stir until well mixed. Simmer slowly, stirring frequently, until  thick and clear. Add nuts and cook 5 minutes. The Household Searchlight
