Applesauce Cookies
All-Purpose Flour, Allspice, Salt, Raisins, Shortening, soft, Egg, Baking soda, Quaker Oats, uncooked, Brown sugar, firmly packed, Sweetened canned applesauce, (quick or oldfashioned), Cinnamon, 
Beat shortening and sugar together until creamy.  Blend in egg and  applesauce.  (Mixture may separate but it will blend evenly when dry  ingredients are added.)  Sift together flour, soda, salt and spices.  Add to creamed mixture; blend well.  Stir in oats and raisins.    Drop by heaping teaspoonfuls onto greased cooky sheets. Bake in  preheated moderate oven (375 F.) 12 to 15 minutes.
