Apple Pie Spice
Allspice, Cinnamon, Ground ginger, Nutmeg, 
Makes 1/2 cup.  Stir into applesauce.  Stir into crumb topping for  coffeecake.  Heat with apple cider.  Pour through coffee filter before  drinking.  Simple Spice Cookies: sprinkle over sliced refrigerator sugar-  cookie dough before baking.    Buy spices in bulk from a natural foods store.
