Poulet Au Champignon
Salt, Chopped tarragon leaves, Flour, Chunks, The Perdue Chicken Cookbook, Butter or margarine, Bacon -- cut into small, Fresh mushrooms, Pepper, Chicken stockj or water, Perdue chicken,cooked --, Brandy, Onion -- chopped, Pieces, 
Melt butter or margarine in fry pan. Brown bacon in butter or margarine,  add chicken, mushrooms, onion, salt and pepper and brown lightly. Add flour  and stir. Add chicken stock and stir.
