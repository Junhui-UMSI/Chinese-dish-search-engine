Quick Spanish Hamburger Soup
Ground beef, Salt, Pepper, (8 oz.) tomato sauce, Carrot, sliced, (2 oz.) mushroom stems and caps, Onions, Sliced pimento stuffed olives, Sugar, Water, Dry Red Wine, Grated parmesan cheese, 
In saucepan cook ground beef till browned; drain off fat. Stir in salt and  pepper. Add tomato sauce, carrot, mushrooms, onion, olives, and sugar. Stir in water and wine. Cover; simmer for 30-35 minutes or till carrot is  tender, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle individual servings with parmesan cheese, if desired. Makes 2 servings.
