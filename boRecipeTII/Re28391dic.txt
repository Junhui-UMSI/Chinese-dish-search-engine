Baked Lima Beans
Molasses, Butter, Salt, Dry mustard, Sour cream, Lima beans, Brown sugar, 
Drain lima beans & put into baking dish. Cut butter over the beans. Mix  brown sugar, dry mustard & salt. Sprinkle this mixture over the beans. Stir  in molasses & then pour sour cream over all. Bake 1 hour at 350.    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
