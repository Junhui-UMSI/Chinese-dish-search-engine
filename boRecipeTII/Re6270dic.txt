Evaporated skim milk, CUSTARD, Sugar, Egg, Sugar, CARAMEL, Vanilla, Cold water, 
1.  To make caramel:  In glass custard bowl dissolve       sugar in water.  Microwave on HIGH (100% power)       1 to 2 minutes.  (Watch carefully and remove as       soon as sugar turns color.)    2.  To make custard:  In small bow, whisk together all       custard ingredients.    3.  Pour custard over caramelized sugar.    4.  Microwave on DEFROST (30% power) 2 minutes, or       until pudding is just set.    5.  Refrigerate 1 hour or longer.
