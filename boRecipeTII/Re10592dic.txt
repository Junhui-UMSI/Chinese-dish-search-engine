Quick Paella
Can minced clams (drained), Margarine or butter, Cloves minced garlic or 1/4, Servings, Minced onion or 2, Instant rice (uncooked), Cans tomato sauce, Can cooked chicken, Minced green pepper, 
Heat margarine in large skillet and lightly brown rice, onion, green  pepper, and garlic. Add remaining ingredients along with 1-1/2 cups water,  and mix well. Bring to a boil.  Lower heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
