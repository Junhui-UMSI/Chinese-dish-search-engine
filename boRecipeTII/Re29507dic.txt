Tarragon Salad Dressing
Medium onion, finely chopped, Mayonnaise, Sprigs of parsley, Vegetable oil, Freshly ground white pepper, Chicken consomme, Tarragon leaves, Red wine vinegar, Salt, Worcestershire Sauce, Tabasco sacue, 
Place all ingredients except the oil, consomme, and mayonnaise in    a blender and mix at high speed.  Empty into a bowl. Slowly mix    in the consomme and oil.  When the mixture reaches the consistency    of a loose puree, add the mayonnaise a little at a time, beating    constantly with a wire whisk.  Chill and serve.    Makes 2 quarts
