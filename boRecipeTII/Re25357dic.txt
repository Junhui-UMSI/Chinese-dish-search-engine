Tortellini Pesto Salad
Tortellini, cooked, Minced garlic cloves, Parmesan cheese, Walnuts, milk, Julienne zucchini, Crushed basil leaves, Chopped parsley, Cherry tomato halves, Julienne carrots, Mayonnaise, 
Combine first six ingredients.  Add remaining ingredients except walnuts.  Chill.  Add walnuts before serving. Makes 4 servings.
