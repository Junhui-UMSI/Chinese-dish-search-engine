Tea Loaf
Raisins, Tea, Sultanas, brown sugar, Self-rising flour, Egg, 
1. Steep fruit and sugar in cold tea ovrnight. Add egg and flour.    2. Put in a 1 lb. loaf tin and cook for 1 hour 45 minutes at 350F  or gas mark 4.    Source: Mary Killen, Clough, Downpatrick Co.
