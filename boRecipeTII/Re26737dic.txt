Bake-A-Roux
All-purpose flour, Cooking oil, 
Mix flour and oil together in a heavy ovenproof container. Place on center  shelf in preheated 400? oven.  Bake at this temperature for 1-1/2 to 2  hours. Set timer, and stir roux every 15 minutes. Roux should be a caramel  color when done. Remove from oven, cool, transfer to containers with  tight-fitting lids and store in refrigerator until need ed. Roux may also  be frozen. Yield: enough roux for 4 to 6 pots of gumbo.    MAKES 1 RECIPE, ENOUGH FOR 4-6    POTS OF GUMBO!
