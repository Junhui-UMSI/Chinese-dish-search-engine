Basic Miso Soup
Carrot, EDEN Wakame, Green onions, Water, , Onion, EDEN Miso, Water, 
Rinse Wakame. Cover with water and soak  for 5 minutes. Remove rib and dice. Meanwhile, bring 4 cups water to a  boil. Add onions, simmer for 3 minutes. Add carrots and Wakame, simmer  for 5 more minutes. Remove from heat and add dissolved miso, stirring in  well. Do not boil once miso is added. Garnish with green onions and  serve.
