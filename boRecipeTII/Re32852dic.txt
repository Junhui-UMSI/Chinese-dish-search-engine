Sweet Potatoe Souffle
Butter, Sweet potato, Salt, Brown sugar, Vanilla, Nutmeg, 
Mix and place in lightly buttered 2 qt. casserol. (Or scooped out orange  halves) and bake at 400 degrees until browned on top.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 01 Dec 96    From:    Kris Milliron <gkmilliron@NT1.DIDO.COM>    Date:    Mon, 2 Dec 1996 18:13:26 +0000
