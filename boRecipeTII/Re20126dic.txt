Vanilla Muffins
butter, Flour, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Sweet milk, Eggs, Sugar, 
Beat sugar & eggs together. Add flour, milk, baking powder, butter &  vanilla. Mix thoroughly & bake at 350 in hot, well-greased muffin pans for  12-15 minutes. Makes 36 muffins.
