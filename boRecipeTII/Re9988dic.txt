Purple Plum Pie
Sugar, Ground cinnamon, butter, All-purpose flour, Ground nutmeg, Salt, Unbaked deep-dish pastry, Ground cinnamon, Lemon juice, Fresh plums, All-purpose flour, TOPPING, Sugar, 
In a bowl, combine the first six ingredients; pour into the pastry shell.  For topping, combine sugar, flour, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a small bowl;  cut in butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle over  filling. Bake at 375 degrees for 50-60 minutes or until bubbly and golden  brown. Cover edges of crust during the last 20 minutes to prevent  overbrowning. Cool on a wire rack. Yield: 8 servings.    NOTES : "I can never resist a tart, tempting slice of this beautiful pie.  Its a down-home dessert that makes any meal special.  This pie is a  terrific way to put bountiful summer plums to use."
