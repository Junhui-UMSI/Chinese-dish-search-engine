Pumpkin Muffins
All-purpose flour, Chopped golden raisins, Baking Powder, Egg whites, lightly beaten, Canola oil, Walnuts, Sugar, Canned pumpkin, Skim milk, Vanilla, Salt, Eggs, lightly beaten, Ground cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Place 24 paper baking cups in muffin tins. Mix  together flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon and salt.    In another bowl, mix eggs, skim milk, oil, pumpkin and vanilla; add to  bowl with dry ingredients. Stir until barely moistened. Fold in  raisins and walnuts and fill cup two-thirds full.    Bake 20 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out  clean. Remove from tins; cool on rack.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.
