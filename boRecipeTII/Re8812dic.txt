Praline Chunk Cookies
Chopped, Skor candy bars coarsely, Golden Crisco, Eggs, All-purpose flour, Lightly packed brown sugar, Coarsely chopped pecans, Salt, Baking soda, Semi-sweet chocolate chips, 
Beat Crisco, brown sugar and eggs with fork. Stir in remaining ingredients  except Skor bars. Drop from heaping tablespoon measure onto ungreased  baking pan. Place several pieces of Skor bar on top of each cookie. Bake at  375F (190C) for 8 to 10 minutes or until cookies are golden brown around  the outside and a little soft in the centre. Cool.
