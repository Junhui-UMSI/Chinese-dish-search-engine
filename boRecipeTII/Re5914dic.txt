Pistachio Cake
Pistachio pudding mix, Eggs, White cake mix, Salad oil, Club soda, 
Combine all ingredients, beat 4 mins.  Pour batter into greased, floured  tube pan.  Bake at 350 for 45-50 mins. Cool. For PISTACHIO FROSTING: 2  envelopes whipped topping mix (Dream Whip); 1 1/2 c cold mild; 1 pistachio  pudding mix.  combine and whip until thick and creamy.
