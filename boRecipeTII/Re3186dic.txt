Pear and Cottage Cheese Salad
Orange or Lemon rind, ,, Carton (8-oz.) Cottage, Salad greens, (large) Pear halves, (1 lb,, Lemon juice, Grapes, (opt.), 
Drain pears, reserve 1/4 cup juice. Blend reserved juice, grated rind and  lemon juice into Mayonnaise. Arrange pear halves, cottage cheese and grapes  on salad greens. Serve dressing on the side.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #507 by Carolyn <c4@groupz.net> on Mar 9,  1997.
