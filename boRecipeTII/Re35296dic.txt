Beef Barley Skillet
Worchestershire sauce, Sugar, Tyme, Water, Celery, Hamburger, Onions, Pearl barley, salt&pepper, Green Pepper, Chile sauce, Canned tomatoes, 
Saute beef, onion, celery and green pepper. Pour off fat and stir in rest  of the ingredients and bring to a boil then turn down heat and simmer until  barley is cooked.
