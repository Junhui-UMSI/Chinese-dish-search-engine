Pick-Me-Up Breakfast
Banana, Orange juice, Apple juice, White of 1 egg, 
Put ingredients in blender. Blend well. Drink for 7 to 10 days in morning  for breakfast with whole grain toast. Should feel better in 3 to 5 days.  Can be taken twice a day if you are really wiped out!  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 2,  1998
