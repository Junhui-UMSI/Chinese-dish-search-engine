Almost No-Cal Red Beans and Rice
Celery, finely chopped, Plum tomatoes, seeded & finely chopped, Hot pepper sauce, Dry red kidney beans, Onions, finely chopped, Bay Leaves, Green bell peppers,, 
Cover beans with water (2-inches above beans).  Let soak all day or  overnight.  Drain, then replace water just to cover the beans.  Throw in  all the vegetables.  Bring to boil, reduce heat to simmer for about 45  minutes.  When beans start cracking and showing white) theyre done.    Serve with cooked rice, white, or brown.  Add hot pepper sauce to taste.    Makes 8 servings.    Recipe by Sara David on AT&Ts Interchange, Posted by David Pileggi  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Mar 24, 98
