Thai Chicken and Noodles
Red pepper flakes (up to, Boneless, skinless, chicken, Cooked oriental noodles or, Water, Smooth peanut butter, Garlic, Bottled teriyaki marinade &, (385 mL) Carnation 2%, Corn starch, divided, Chopped peanuts, Vegetable oil, Green Onions, 
Cut chicken into 1/2 inch pieces. In medium bowl, combine 2 TB of the corn  starch, teriyaki sauce, garlic and red pepper flakes; add chicken and toss  to coat. Let stand 15 min. Combine remaining 1 TB corn starch and peanut  butter; gradually stir in e. milk and water; set aside. Heat oil in wok or  large frypan. Add chicken and stir fry 6 to 8 min. or until chicken is  cooked. Add milk mixture to wok. Cook and stir over med. heat until sauce  mixture boils and thickens. Sprinkle with peanuts and green onions. Serve  with hot cooked Oriental noodles or Vermicelli. Makes 6 servings.
