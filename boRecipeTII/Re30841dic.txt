Tangy Flounder or Sole (Hl)
Fresh parsley, Balsamic vinegar, Dried mustard powder, Lemon slices, Yellow onion, Lemon juice, Flounder, 
Rinse and pat dry fish fillets. Place fish in 10-inch by 8-inch oven-proof  glass or stainless steel baking dish. Combine vinegar, lemon juice, dried  mustard powder, and chopped onion in a small mixing bowl. Pour over  fillets.    Cover dish and marinate fish in refrigerator for approximately 2 hours.    Preheat broiler. Remove cover from dish and place dish in broiler for  approximately 4 to 6 minutes, or until fish is white and flaky throughout.  Garnish with parsley and onion slices.
