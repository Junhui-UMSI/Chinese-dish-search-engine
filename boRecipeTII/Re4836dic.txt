Pickle Relish
Sugar, Whole allspice, Cloves, sweet red peppers, cucumbers, Canning salt, sweet green peppers, White vinegar, Water, Ice, Mustard seed, Turmeric, Onions, 
Yield: About 9 pints    Procedure: Add cucumbers, peppers, onions, salt, and ice to water and let  stand 4 hours. Drain and re-cover vegetables with fresh ice water for  another hour. Drain again. Combine spices in a spice or cheesecloth bag.  Add spices to sugar and vinegar. Heat to boiling and pour mixture over  vegetables. Cover and refrigerate 24 hours. Heat mixture to boiling and  fill hot into clean jars, leaving 1/2-inch headspace.    Adjust lids and process according to the recommendations in Table 1.    Table 1. Recommended process time for Pickle Relish in a boiling-water  canner.    Style of Pack: Hot.  Jar Size: Half-pints or Pints. Process Time at  Altitudes of 0 - 1,000 ft: 10 min.                         1,001 - 6,000 ft: 15 min.
