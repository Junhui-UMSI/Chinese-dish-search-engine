From: Julie Gettys <thegettys@postoffice.worldnet.att.net>    Date: Wed, 31 Jul 1996 12:16:15 -0700  Cut cuke in half lengthwise, then cut each half in crosswise paper-thin  slices.  In a medium bowl make the dressing by combining coriander, lime  juice, onion, vinegar, sugar and red pepper.    Add cuke slices to liquid mixture and toss gently.  Cover and refrigerate  for about four hours.  Serve on a bed of a leaf lettuce.    fatfree digest V96 #211    From the Fatfree Vegetarian recipe list.
