Provolone-Ham Celery Stuffing
Worcestershire sauce, dash, Butter, *Ingredients:*, Provolone cheese, grated or, Ham, minced, 
*Directions:*    Blend all ingredients and pile lightly in celery. Makes enough to fill 15  to 20 cut sections of celery.    From: A Salute to Cheese, by Betty Wason, Hawthorn Books, Inc., 1966.    Shared by: June Hoffman, 7/93
