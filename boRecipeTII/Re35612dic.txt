Stromboli 2
Frozen bread dough, ham, hard salami, Dried whole basil, Dried whole oregano leaves, Provolone cheese, mozzarella cheese, Butter, cornmeal, 
Place bread dough on a lightly greased baking sheet. Pat to a 15x10 inch  rectangle. Arrange ham slices lenghtwise from center. place salami on top.  Sprinkle with 1/4 tsp. basil and and 1/4 tsp. oregano. Arrange provolone  cheese over herbs and mozzarella cheese atop that. Top off with remaining  herbs. Moisten all edges of the dough with water. Bring each long edge of  dough to center. Press edges together securely to seal. Brush dough with 1  tbsp. butter and sprinkle with cornemal. Carefully invert. Brush top with  remaining butter. Bake at 375 for twenty minutes. Serve with heated  marinara sauce alongside.    Posted to Kitmailbox  by GAdams1350~~at;aol.com on Apr 30, 1997
