Autumn Apple Muffins
Sugar, 2% yogurt OR, Rolled oats, Baking Powder, Wholewheat flour, Butter, Egg, slightly beaten, Apple, grated, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Cinnamon, Raisins, soaked, 2% milk, DRY INGREDIENTS, 3/4ts lemon juice + 2% milk, Bran, Salt, 
In large bowl, combine the first nine ingredients. Sift dry ingredients  together.  Add to bran mixture, stirring until moistened. Spoon into muffin  cups. Bake at 425 F about 20 minutes or until golden brown. Makes 12 small  muffins.    1 muffin contains 114 calories, 1 starch choice, 1/2 fruit, 1/2 fat    Source:  Enjoy B.C. Fruit the Diabetic Way by Elaine Johnston c. 1982  Shared and tested by Elizabeth Rodier Sept 93
