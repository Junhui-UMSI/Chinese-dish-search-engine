Pork Chops with Corn Dressing
Butterfly (boneless) pork, Soft bread crumbs, Water, (10-3/4 ounces) condensed, Egg, (17 ounces) whole kernel, Cooked whole kernel corn, Soup can milk, Worcestershire Sauce, Green Pepper, Onion, Cooking oil, 
In a bowl, combine egg, bread crumbs, corn, water, green pepper, onion and  Worcestershire sauce; set aside. In a large oven proof skillet or a Dutch  oven, heat oil over medium-high. Lightly brown pork chops on both sides.  Season with salt and pepper. Top with corn dressing mixture. Add enough  water to cover bottom of pan. Bake, uncovered, at 350! for about 1 hour or  until pork is tender. Add additional water to pan if necessary. Remove pork  chops and dressing to a serving platter; keep warm. Add soup and mi lk to  pan drippings. Cook and stir over medium heat until hot and bubbly. Serve  with pork chops. Yield: 6 servings.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
