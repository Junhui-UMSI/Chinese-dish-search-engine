Sweet and Pungent Beets
Cooked; sliced beets, Honey plus, Cider vinegar, Honey, Salt to taste, 
In small saucepan, combine all ingredients. Cook over medium-low heat until  heated through. Serve hot or refrigerate and serve cold. Use these in a  tossed salad for added color and flavor. Makes 4 servings.
