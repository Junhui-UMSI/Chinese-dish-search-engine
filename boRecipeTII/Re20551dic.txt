Apple Chocolate Fudge Cake with Apple Fluff Frosting
Flour, Shortening, Vanilla, Salt, Eggs, Dream Whip, Canned apple sauce, Milk - divided, Cold milk, Vanilla, Baking soda, APPLE FLUFF FROSTING, Squares unsweetened, Cold canned applesauce, Sugar - divided, 
Mix chocolate, 1/2 cup milk and 1/2 cup sugar in saucepan; cook and stir  until thick and smooth. Stir in apple sauce and vanilla, cool. Combine and  sift dry ingredients. Cream shortening with remaining 1 cup sugar; blend in  eggs one at a time. Alternately add dry ingredients and remaining 3/4 cup  milk to creamed mixure. blending well after each addition. Blend in  chocolate mixture.    Turn batter into 2 greased and floured 8" layer pans. Bake at 350 for 40  min. or until done. Split each cooled layer to make 4 layers.    Apple Fluff Frosting: Beat topping mix with milk. When mixture begins to  stiffen and form peaks, add applesauce and vanilla. Beat until firm enough  to spread. Makes about 3 cups.    Apple Cream Frosting:    Beat together 1 large pkg (8 oz) cream cheese, 4 cups 10X sugar and 1 Tbs.  grated lemon rind. When mixture is fluffy, stir in 2 to 3 Tbs. canned  applesauce; chill. Spread between and on top layers    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest by travelers4now@juno.com (Gary & Doris  McCoy) on Mar 30, 1998
