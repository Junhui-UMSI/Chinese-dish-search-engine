Bean Dip with Chorizo
refried beans, Onion, Chorizo, Cheddar cheese, green chiles, Chili powder, monterey jack, 
Crumble chorizo in a large heavy skillet and cook just until the fat starts  to gather in the pan. Add onion and cook until onion is transparent; drain.  Add remaining ingredients and cook until all is hot and the cheeses are  melted. Serve hot with tortilla chips or corn chips.
