Turkey Florentine Pasta and Vegetables
Margarine or butter, Green giant pasta accents, Uncooked turkey breast, Garlic pepper or lemon, 
1. Prepare frozen vegetables and pasta according to package directions.    2. Meanwhile, melt margarine in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Sprinkle turkey breast slices evenly with garlic pepper. Cook turkey slices  in margarine 2 minutes on each side or until no longer pink.    3. Place turkey on serving platter; top with vegetables and pasta. Serve  immediately.
