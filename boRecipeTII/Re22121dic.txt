Turkey Meatball Spaghetti
Salt, Onions, fresh basil, SPAGHETTI, Pepper, Garlic, MEAT BALLS AND SAUCE, Dry bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, Skim milk, spaghetti, Ground skinless turkey, spaghetti sauce, carrot, 
In medium bowl, combine ground turkey, carrot, onion, bread crumbs, basil,  milk, salt, pepper and garlic; mix well. Shape turkey mixture into l-inch  balls.    In large saucepan, combine meatballs and sauce. Cover; cook over medium  heat for l0 to 15 minutes until meatballs are no longer pink in center,  stirring occasionally.    Serve with cooked spaghetti or spaghetti squash. Top with Parmesan cheese.    4 to 5 servings.    MICROWAVE DIRECTIONS: Prepare meatballs as directed. in 2-quart  microwave-safe casserole, combine meatballs and sauce; cover.    Microwave on HIGH for 10 to 13 minutes until meatballs are no longer pink  in center, stirring twice during cooking. Serve with cooked spaghetti or  spaghetti squash. Top with Parmesan cheese.
