Preserved Eggs
Preserved eggs, 
1. Clean and shell preserved cggs (see "HOW-TO SECTION").       2. With a sharp knife, cut each egg crosswise in 1/4-inch slices, or  lengthwise in quarters.       3 . Serve with any of the following sprinkled over the top:          1 teaspoon fresh ginger root, minced, combined with either 2  teaspoons vinegar or 2 teaspoons soy sauce          1 teaspoon sugar, 1 tablespoon vinegar and afew drops of sesame oil,  blended together          A few drops of Tabasco, Worcestershire, soy or oyster sauce          Or serve eggs plain with sweet-and-sour pickles or subgum ginger  (both cut up) or pickled scallions and red ginger (sliced).
