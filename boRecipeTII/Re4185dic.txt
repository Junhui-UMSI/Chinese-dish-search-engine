Perfect Apple Butter
Sweet apples, peeled, sliced, Apple cider, Ground cinnamon (optional), 
Place apples and cider in heavy saucepan over medium heat. Cook, stirring  frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.  Lower heat and simmer,  stirring frequently, for about 1 hour, or until apple slices have  disintegrated and butter is thick.  Remove from heat. Stir in cinnamon, if  desired.   Pour into hot sterilized jars, leaving 1/4 in. headspace.  Cap  and process in 10-minute boiling water bath.  Makes 4 1/2 pint jars.
