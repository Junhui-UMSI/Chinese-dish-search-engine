Tortilla Dressing
Chili powder, Thirds and then across, Dried corn bread crumbs, Ground coriander seeds, Flour tortillas, cut into, Chopped fine, Diced roasted red pepper, Finely chopped fresh cilanto, Corn tortillas, cut the same, Egg, Chicken broth, Or 2 jalapeno peppers, Finely chopped fresh sage, Ground cumin, Into 1/4 inch strips, Onion, diced, 
In a medium-size skillet, add 1/4 inch of oil.  Heat over medium-high  until hot.  Add corn tortilla strips, a few at a time and fry until  crisp, about 45 seconds.  Remove with a slotted spoon and place on a  double layer of paper towels to drain.    Combine fried corn tortilla strips with remaining ingredients except  salt in a large bowl.  Refrigerate for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.    Preheat over to 325 degrees.  Season dressing with salt.  Grease a  casserole dish and add dressing.  Cover and bake 45 minutes, serve  warm.
