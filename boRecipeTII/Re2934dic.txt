Peanut Butter Noodles
Soy sauce, Sugar, Vinegar, Onion, Salt, Peanut butter, linguine, Garlic, Sesame oil, 
Cook linguine according to package instructions in boiling water; drain.  In large bowl, mix all the remaining ingredients together. Add linguine to  sauce and toss to coat well. Refrigerate. good to pack for kids lunches    Source: _The Pennywhistle Lunch Book_ posted by Anne MacLellan    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
