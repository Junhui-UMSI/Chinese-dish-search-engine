Wedding Cakes
Stick margarine (not reduced, Vanilla, All-purpose flour; sifted, Nuts; finely chopped, Salt, Stick butter; room, 
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Cream butter, margarine, confectioners sugar,  and vanilla thoroughly. Stir in flour and salt. Dough will start to cling  together like pie crust dough. mix in nuts. (May chill dough at this point)  Roll into 1-inch balls. Place on ungreased baking sheet. Bake about 10  minutes. Cookies should not be brown on top. Bottoms will be lightly  browned. Let cool on baking sheet for 2-3 minutes. Roll in confectioners  sugar and place on wire rack. Cool. Roll in confectioners sugar one or two  more times.
