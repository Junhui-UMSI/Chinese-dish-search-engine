Quick Corn Chowder
Hot water, Can cream-style corn, Pepper, Raw bacon, diced, milk, Onion, Finely chopped fresh parsley, Salt, 
1. Place bacon and onion in a deep, 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic  casserole. 2. Heat, covered with paper toweling, in Microwave Oven 3  minutes. 3. Add remaining ingredients, except parsley. Stir to combine. 4.  Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5 minutes or until soup is heated  through. Stir occasionally. 5. Allow soup to stand 2 to 3 minutes before  serving. 6. Garnish with parsley.
