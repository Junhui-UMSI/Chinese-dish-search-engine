Fruited Brown Rice Muffins
Whole wheat pastry flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Wheat germ; (I used 1/4 c, Raisins, Rice or soy milk; (could, Egg whites beaten, Cooked brown rice, Unsweetend applesauce, 
Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt, and stir in wheat germ (or  oat bran). Add raisins, milk, egg whites, rice, and applesauce and blend  briskly. Fill greased muffin tins 2/3 full and bake at 425 for 30 minutes.    Makes 15 Nice, Moist Muffins.    Posted to fatfree digest by "Ross, Lynne" <Lynne.Ross@Den.Galileo.com> on  Nov 19, 1998,
