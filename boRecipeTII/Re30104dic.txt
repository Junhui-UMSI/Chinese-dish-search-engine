Balsmic Glazed Steak
Balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Top round steak; 1 1/2", Garlic, Cracked black pepper, 
In a plastic zip-top bag, combine all ingredients; seal and shake to coat  steak. Chill at least 2 hours, turning once. Heat broiler. Place steak on a  broiler rack. Broil 20 mins. for med-rare, turning once. Let stand 10 mins.  before slicing.
