White Cake (Microwave)
Salt, Baking Powder, Shortening, Egg whites (1/4 cup), Sugar, milk, Flour, Vanilla or almond ext., Egg, 
Makes one layer.     Place all ingredients in mixing bowl.  Blend at low speed, then beat at  medium speed 2 minutes. Spread batter in 9-inch round baking dish.  Microwave at 50 percent power 6 minutes, rotate 1/4 turn every 3 minutes.  Increase power to High. Microwave 2 to 5 minutes or until done. Immediately  sprinkle with graham cracker crumbs if desired. Let stand directly on  counter  5 to 10 minutes.
