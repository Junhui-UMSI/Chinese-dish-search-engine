Quesadillas with Brie and Plum Salsa
; 1/4 ts chili flakes, Red onion; chopped, Salt, Flour tortillas (10-inch), Ripe plums; diced, Butter; for frying, SALSA, Brie or camembert cheese, Fresh coriander; chopped or, ; basil leaves, Lime juice, Vegetable oil, Jalapeno pepper; diced or, Pepper, OTHER REQUIREMENTS, 
1. To make salsa: heat oil in skillet on medium high heat. Add onion and  saute until softened, about 2 minutes. Add jalapeno and plums and stir  together with onions. Stir in lime juice, bring to boil and remove from  heat. Stir in coriander, season with salt and pepper and cool.    2. Cut brie into strips. Place 1/4-cup brie and 2 tb salsa on one half of  tortilla. Season to taste. Fold over pressing edges together.    3. Heat remaining butter in skillet on high heat. Fry tortillas about 2  minutes per side until browned. Place in oven to keep warm. Alternatively,  place quesadillas on oiled cookie sheet and oil tops. Place in 400F(200C)  oven and bake for 10 minutes or until browned. To serve cut each tortilla  into three.    These can be make ahead and reheated when needed in 350F(180C) oven until  cheese melts.
