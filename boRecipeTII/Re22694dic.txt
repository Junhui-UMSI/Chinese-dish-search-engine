Apricot Glaze (Peggy Cullen)
Apricot jam, Water (approximately), 
Place the apricot jam and water in a small saucepot and warm over medium  high heat until it dissolves. Just before boiling, remove from heat. Dilute  with a little more water if necessary until it is thin enough to brush  evenly. Apply immediately with a soft, flat pastry brush.
