Aunt Ilos Butterscotch Dumplings
milk, Boiling water, Salt, Butter, Butter, DUMPLINGS, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, Sugar, Flour, 
Simmer brown sugar, butter and water in a 9 x 13 pan while you mix up  dumplings.    Mix dumplings and drop by spoonfuls into boiling syrup. Bake 20 minuter at  350 degress F or until golden brown.    Serve hot with whipped cream or ice cream.    NOTES : Aunt Ilo is the oldest sister of my grandmother Leah Petersen.
