Yemenite Charoset
Grated, Sesame seeds toasted, Dried figs,quartered, Wine, Lime or lemon rind,finely, Dried apricots, Ground coriander, Honey, To 4 Passover sweet white, Slivered almonds, 
Process almonds and apricots coarsely in food processor. Transfer to  small bowl.  Process figs to fine consistancy. Stir into almond apricot  mixture. Add coriander, rind, honey and enough white wine to bind  ingredients.  Refrigerate for 1 hour.  Roll into balls, 1" in diameter,  roll in the sesame seeds and place each in miniature paper cups, or shape  into a pyramid and press sesame seeds into sides. Makes about 24 balls...  ~--
