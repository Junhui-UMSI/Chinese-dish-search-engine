Baked Tomatoes
Pepper, Basil leaves, chopped, Butter, Salt, Clove garlic, chopped, Tomatoes, 
FROM MY GARDEN SHOW #5511    Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Cut around stem of tomato and remove as a  plug. Set aside. Sprinkle in basil and garlic into the tomato. Drizzle in a  touch of butter. Add salt and pepper to taste. Replace plug and bake for  about 30 minutes. Serve on a bed of rice.
