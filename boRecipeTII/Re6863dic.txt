Polynesian Teriyaki Sauce
Apricot-Pineapple preserves, Cornstarch, Garlic powder, Ginger, Water, Soy sauce, 
Combine first four ingredients in saucepan and bring to a boil slowly.  While sauce is cooking, combine cornstarch and water and add to sauce. Cook  until sauce thickens.
