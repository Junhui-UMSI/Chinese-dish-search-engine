Whole Wheat Honey-Walnut Bread - Pan-1
Whole wheat flour, Dry yeast, Butter, Walnuts (1 oz); chopped, Salt, Dry milk, XKGR41A Don Fifield, Water (7 1/2 fl.oz), Honey, 
Timer OR Bake (Rapid) mode may be used. Place all ingredients (except  liquids and yeast) inside the bread pan. Add liquid ingredients. Close  cover and place dry yeast into the yeast holder. SELECT: WHOLE WHEAT BAKE  MODE. Press start. 5:00 will show.  Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #187 by serge.cyr@sympatico.ca (Serge  Cyr) on Aug 14, 1997
