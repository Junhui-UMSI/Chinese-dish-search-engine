White Chip Orange Dream Cookies
Flour, Baking soda, Brown sugar, Butter, Salt, Orange peel; grated, White chocolate chips, Egg, Sugar, 
Beat butter, sugar, and brown sugar in large bowl until creamy.  Beat in  egg and orange peel.  Gradually beat in flour, baking soda and salt.  Stir  in morsels.  Drop by teaspoons onto greased baking sheets.  Bake at 350    for 10-12 minutes.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #338    From: sarahpreston@juno.com    Date: Tue, 10 Dec 1996 10:29:36 PST
