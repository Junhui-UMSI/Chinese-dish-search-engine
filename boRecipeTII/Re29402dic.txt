Baked Winter Squash - Acorn
Maple syrup, Acorn squash, butter, Salt, 
Cut the squashes in half and scoop out the seeds. Sprinkle the cut sides  with salt. Place cut side down on a greased baking dish and bake at 200 C /  400F / Gas mark 6 for about 30 to 40 minutes until the squash is pierced  easily with a fork. Turn cut sides up and score the cut side with a fork.  Spread with the butter, drizzle with maple syrup and return to the oven for  a few minutes to glaze. Serve hot.
