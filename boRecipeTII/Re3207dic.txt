Pear and Raisin Charoses
Dried apricot halves;, Lemon juice, (2 Tb.) sweet red wine, Honey, Pears; cored and coarsely, Raisins, Cinnamon, 
In medium bowl, combine pears, apricots, raisins, honey, lemon juice and  cinnamon; mix well.  Stir in wine. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour  before serving.    Each serving provides:  1/2 FR, 10 C.  Per serving: 43 cal, 0 g fat, 11 g  car, 1 mg sod, 0 mg chol.    Source:  "Weight Watchers" magazine, April 1992.
