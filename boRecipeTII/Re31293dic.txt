Bananes Flambees
Orange; juice of, Bananas; sliced in half, Sugar, Grand Marnier liqueur, Cognac, An orange skin (whole), Butter, 
1. Melt butter in a pan. Add sugar stirring and scrubing orange peel  against bottom of pan to caramelize. 2. Place bananas in pan. 3. Add orange  juice and turn down heat cook until sugar has dissolved. 4. Flambe with  cognac (optional) 5. Stir in Grand Marnier liqueur 6. Arrange 1 scoop ice  cream on each individual plate. 7. Top with whipped cream 8. Sprinkle  cinnamon on top of cream 9. Divide bananas among the plates and pour sauce  on top.
