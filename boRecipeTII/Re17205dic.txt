Vodka Sauce #3
Vegetable oil, Butter, Shallots; chopped, Vodka, Egg yolks, Salt & pepper (pinch of, Chopped fresh parsley, Heavy creamy, Grated cheese, Marinara sauce, 
From: Gerald Edgerton <jerrye@wizard.com>    Date: Tue, 07 May 1996 13:06:33 -0700    Saute shallots in oil until brown.  Drain oil.  Add butter, salt, pepper,  parsley and vodka.  Let mixture sizzle in pan 2 minutes.  Add marinara  sauce, heavy cream and egg yolks. Saute to blend on low flame 5-10 minutes.  Serve over your favorite pasta.
