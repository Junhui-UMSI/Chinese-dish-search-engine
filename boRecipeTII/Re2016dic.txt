A Sweet Potato-Crab Chowder
olive oil, tasso, shallots, Garlic, sweet potato, Light broth, Heavy cream, milk, Flour, Crabmeat, Chives, Salt, Black Pepper, 
In a large soup pot over medium heat, heat the oil. Add the tasso and cook,  stirring for 1 minute. Add the shallots and cook for 3 minutes. Add the  garlic, sweet potatoes, and water and bring to a boil. Cover and cook for  15  minutes or until the potatoes are tender.    In a medium bowl, whisk together the cream, milk, and flour. Add to the  soup and cook until the mixture begins to thicken. Add the crabmeat and the  chives. Season with the salt and pepper to taste.    This recipe yields 4 entree portions, or 6 to 8 side portions.
