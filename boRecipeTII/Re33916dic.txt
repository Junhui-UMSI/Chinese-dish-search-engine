Sweet and Spicy Glazed Chicken
Chicken, boneless, skinless, Onion soup mix, French dressing, Apricot preserves, 
Preheat oven to 375 Arrange chicken in baking dish Blend remaining  ingredients & pour over chicken Bake for 50-60 minutes, basting once or  twice I served this over Uncle Bens long grain & wild rice. MMMM MMMM  good! Let me know if you like it! FROM:    KIM FRANCIS (DRSH57B)
