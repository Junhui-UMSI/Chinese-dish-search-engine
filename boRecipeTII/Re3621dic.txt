Pedernales River Chili
32 oz. can stewed tomatos, c, TAbsco sauce*, Medium or 1 large onion coar, Bacon grease, Salt, Lean round steak, coarse gro, FResh ground cumin, Boiling water, Cloves of garlic, finely cho, Chili powder, DRied, ground oregano, 
Melt the bacon grease in a large skillet over a med-high heat. Add meat and  brown. Add onions and garlic then cook until the onions aregetting  transluscent. Stir in the tomatos, chili sauce, oregano, cumin, & water.  Stir, cook, and add in the Tabasco sauce. Stir every few minutes while it  cooks for 30-45 minutes on low heat.
