Pineapple Cheesecake
Drain and reserve juice, Margarine; melted, Pure vanilla extract, Granulated sugar, filling, topping, Granulated sugar, softened, Crushed pineapple, Corn flake crumbs, Granulated sugar, Cornstarch, Fat-free cream cheese, Egg whites; whipped, Crust, Vegetable oil, 
Recipe by: Southern Living Magazine Preparation Time: 0:15 Preheat oven to  350. To prepare crust, combine corn flake crumbs, 2 tablespoons sugar, and  oil in a mixing bowl.  Press into bottom of 2-9" pie pans; set aside. To  prepare filling, combine cream cheese, 1 cup sugar, egg whites, and  vanilla.  Bake for 50 minutes.  Let cool to room temperature on rack. Then,  refrigerate several hours. Meanwhile to prepare topping, add water to  pineapple juice to make 1/2 cup .  Mix remaining sugar and cornstarch with  reserved pineapple juice. Cook over low heat until thick. Add pineapple and  margarine, cook 2 minutes.  Cool and spread on cheesecake.
