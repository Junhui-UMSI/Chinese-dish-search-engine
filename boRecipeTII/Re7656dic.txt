Pork Tenderloin with Mustard Sauce
Pork Tenderloin; 2 pieces, Dijon Style Mustard; PLUS, Salt, Unbleached flour, Water, Onion, Green Pepper; Chopped, Dijon Style Mustard, 
Place the pork tenderloin in a 24-oz shallow casserole. Sprinkle with salt  and onion.  Cover with vented plastic wrap and microwave, on medium (50%)  power for 4 minutes; rotate the casserole 1/4 turn.   Microwave 4 minutes longer.  Mix the remaining ingredients; spoon over the  pork. Cover with vented plastic wrap and microwave, turning the casserole  1/4 turn every 3 minutes, until pork is done, 4 to 6 minutes.
