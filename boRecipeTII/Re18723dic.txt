An Old Recipe for Oven Pone - Call It Delmarvalous
Sifted cornmeal, Flour, Salt, New Orleans molasses, Sugar, Cold water, 
Add enough cold water to cornmeal to make a soft batter. Scald well,  mashing the lumps. Add New Orleans molasses, sugar, salt, and flour. Grease  Dutch oven well, and put in pone mixture. Put in oven of wood cook stove  and let bake from 5 to 6 hours with a good fire. Then open your stove and  let the pone remain until morning. Before you go to bed, or about 11  oclock, put another stick of wood on.    Reprinted with kind permission from "Call It Delmarvalous" by Virginia  Tanzer, EPM Publications, Mclean, VA, c. 1983.
