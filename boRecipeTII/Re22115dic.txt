Turkey Mandarin Salad
Onion, finely chopped, Mayonnaise, Whipping cream, whipped, Toasted almonds, optional, Cooked turkey, cubed, Seedless red grape halves, Salt, Mandarin oranges, drained, Slivered almonds, Celery, diced, Cooked macaroni, 
In a large bowl, combine turkey, onion and salt; mix well. Add grapes,  celery, oranges and macaroni; toss lightly to mix. Cover and refrigerate.  Just before serving, combine mayonnaise and whipped cream; fold into salad  along with almonds. Top with toasted almonds, if desired. Yield: 6-8  servings.
