Peanut Butter n Honey Cookies
Egg, Baking soda, Peanut butter, Salt, Shortening, Honey, Brown sugar, All-purpose flour, 
Cream shortening, honey and sugar together till light and fluffy. Add  well-beaten egg. Add peanut butter and salt. Stir in flour and soda sifted  together and mix well. Form into small balls of dough and place on greased  cookie sheet. Press with fork. Bake at 350F for 8 to 10 minutes.
