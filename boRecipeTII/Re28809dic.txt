Thai Chicken with Fresh Basil
Serrano chilies, Water, Basil, thinly sliced, Chicken breast (boned), Nuoc Nam (fish sauce), Vegetable oil, Garlic; coarsely chopped, Sugar, 
Skin chicken and cut into 1.25 X 1 in strips.  Remove stems from chilies  and cut lengthwise into thin strips (discard the seeds). Heat oil in wok or  large skillet over high heat. Add garlic and stir until golden brown, about  10 seconds.  Add 0.5 cup of basil and the chilies and stir-fry just until  basil wilts, about 1 minute. Add chicken and stir-fry about 3 minutes. Add  nuoc mam, water and sugar and stir-fry until sauce bubbles and thickens  slightly, about 2 minutes.  Add remaining 0.25 cup of basil and stir-fry  until just wilted, about 5 seconds. Serve immediately with steamed rice...    From: clay@panix.com (Clay Irving)
