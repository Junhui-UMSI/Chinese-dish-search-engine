Apple Face Mask
Essential oil of Marigold, Wheatgerm Oil, Apple flesh, Lemon juice, Egg white, Almond Oil, 
1. Peel and core the apple. Remove any discoloured parts and dice. 2. Place  in a blender add all the other ingredients and blend for a minute at low  speed and a minute at high speed.    Depending on the amount of juice in the apple, the cream may be too wet or  too dry. If so, add a teaspoon of rice flour or more lemon juice and blend  for another minute.
