Zippy Shrimp Spread
Ground red pepper, Grated onion, Catsup, Prepared horseradish, Water, Cream cheese, softened, Worcestershire Sauce, Unpeeled medium-size fresh, 
Bring water to a boil; add srimp, and cook 3 to 5 minutes or until shrimp  turn pink. Drain well, rinse with cold water. Chill. Peel and devein  shrimp. Reserve 3 shrimp for garnish; coarsely chop remaining shrimp. Set  aside.    Combine cream cheese and remaining ingredients in a mixing bowl; beat at  medium speed of an electric mixer until blended. Stir in chopped shrimp.  Spoon shrimp mixture into a small serving bowl; cover and chill for at  least 2 hours.    Garnish with reserved whole shrimp. Serve with assorted crackers.
