Zucchini and Tomatoes
Onion; cut into 1/4", Water, Cooked pasta; optional, Vegetable oil spray, Tomatoes; wedges, Zucchini; cut into 1/4", Garlic powder, Black pepper, 
Using a 9-inch skillet sprayed with vegetable oil, cook the zucchini and  onion over medium heat. Add water as the zucchini and onion start to stick  to the pan. Add the remaining ingredients and cook on low heat for 3  minutes or until the zucchini has reached a desired tenderness. Stir  constantly to prevent sticking.    Serve as a side dish, or over cooked pasta.    Each of the 4 servings contains only 52 calories and 1 gram of fat.
