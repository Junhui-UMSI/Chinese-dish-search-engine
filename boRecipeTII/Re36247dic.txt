Beef Teriyaki and Sauce
Dry sherry, Vegetable oil, green onion, Teriyaki Marinade:, Boneless lean beef, Soy sauce, Teriyaki Marinade, Hot cooked rice, Sugar, 
Cut beef across the grain in 1/8-inch thick slanting pieces. Prepare  Teriyaki mixture. Then add beef, mixing to coat meat well. Let stand 15  minutes to marinate, then drain. Place wok (or electric skillet) over high  heat. When wok is hot, add 1 tablespoon of the oil. When oil is hot, add  half of the meat mixture. Stir-fry until browned. Remove to a bed of rice.  Repeat, using 1 more tablespoon oil and remaining meat. Garnish with onion.  Teriyaki Marinade: Mix together all ingredients.
