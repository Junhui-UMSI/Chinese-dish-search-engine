Perky Turkey
Diced cooked turkey, Lettuce leaves, Rice, Dijon mustard dressing, Diced pimientos; drained, Sliced celery, 
Combine rice, turkey, celery, pimientos, and dressing in large bowl.  Serve over lettuce leaves.    Note: For a new sandwich twist, line pita bread halves with lettuce  leaves; fill with 2/3 cup rice salad.
