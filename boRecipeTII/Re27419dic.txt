Toasty Cheese Bake
Extra lean ground beef, Soft margarine, Black Pepper, Bread, 1% low-fat milk, Salt, Onions, Salt, Dry mustard, Shredded cheddar cheese; 4, Celery, Egg, Prepared mustard, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Toast bread and butter both sides. Cook and  stir meat, onions, celery, prepared mustard and 1/2 teaspoon salt until  meat is brown and onion is tender. Alternate layers of toast, meat mixture  and cheese in a greased 9"x9" pan. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over  layers in the pan. Bake uncovered 30-35 mins.
