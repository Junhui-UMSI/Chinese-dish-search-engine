Tropical Citrus Sangria
Lemon slices, Orange slices, Additional lemon, lime, and, Lime slices, Club soda; (10-oz)  chilled, Sugar, Dry white wine; chilled, Cognac or brandy, 
Recipe by: Cooking Light, April 1995, page 110 Preparation Time: 0:06  Combine the sugar and cognac in a 1-quart glass measure. Microwave at HIGH  1 minute or until sugar dissolves, stirring after 30 seconds. Add wine and  fruit slices; stir well. Cover and chill.  Yield: 3 cups (serving size:      3/4    cup).
