Potato-Bacon Cups
Skim milk, Turkey bacon; crumbled, Potatoes; cooked & mashed, Rosemary; crushed, Salt, Cottage cheese; low fat, Garlic powder, Black pepper, 
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Spray 4  6oz custard cups with  nonstick cooking spray.  Place the cups on a baking sheet.    2. In a medium bowl, combine all the ingredients. Divide evenly among the  prepared cups.  Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, until the tops are golden brown.
