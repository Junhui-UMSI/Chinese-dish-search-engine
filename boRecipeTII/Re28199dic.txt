Baked Fiesta Spinach Dip - Hot
Vegetable oil, Salsa, Pecans, Light cream, Pecans, Frozen spinach, Monterey jack cheese, Black olives, 
Preheat oven to 400?.  Sauteonion til tender.  Stir in salsa and spinach  and cook two minutes more.  Transfer to 1 1/2 qt baking dish.  Stir in two  cups jack cheese, cream cheese, cream & olives.  Sprinkle with pecans. Bake  about 30 minutes until hot & bubbly; cover with foil last 15 minutes to  prevent burning.  Top with remaining Jack and serve hot.
