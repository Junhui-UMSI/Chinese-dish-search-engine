Thousand Island Dressing #2
Worcestershire, Paprika, Dry mustard, Garlic, Onion; grated, Water, Ketchup, Chili sauce, Black pepper, Vegetable oil, Mayonnaise, Tabasco, 
Mix all ingredients in a quart jar and shake well. Store in refrigerator.    From the book <High Cotton Cookin>, Marvell Academy Mothers Assn, Marvell,  AR  72366, ISBN 0-918544-14-9, downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
