Beef with Green Peppers
Soy sauce, Salt, Green peppers, Wine, Corn starch, Water, Vegetable oil, Water, Round steak, Round steak, 
1. Carefully slice beef into thin bite size pieces, across the grain.  Marinate beef in soya sauce, wine, and salt. Prepare and shred green  peppers.    2. Place oil in wok and heat to smoking point. Quickly stir fry beef  in it, no more than 3 minutes. Remove beef to a platter and reserve.    3. Add green peppers to wok, also 4 tablespoons water. Stir fry green  peppers about 3 minutes, uncovered, in wok.    4. Add beef back into wok, stir up corn starch solution and pour into  wok. Mix everything up. Gravy should be on the thin side. Place in  platter.    NOTE: If desired, 2 cloves of garlic could be minced and stir-fried  along with beef to give dish a zestier taste.
