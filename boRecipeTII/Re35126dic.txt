Beaver Club Banana Bread
Salt, Buttermilk, Very ripe (read black), All-Purpose Flour, Eggs, Cooking oil, Backing soda, Sugar, 
Peel very dark skinned bananas and mash thoroughly.  Mix in sugar combined  with baking soda and salt.    Add a the flour and eggs, alternating between the two, mixing well after  each addition.  Then mix in the buttermilk and oil.    Turn into two well greased 8" x 4" loaf pans.  Let stand for 25 minutes.  Bake in a very slow over, 250 degrees, for about 2 hours.  Cool before  turning from pan.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
