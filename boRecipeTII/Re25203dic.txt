Tomatoes Vinaigrette
Garlic, Pepper, Dried basil, Tomato; sliced, Vinegar, Parsley, Salt, olive oil, 
Cut tomatoes in med. slices. Place in a bowl, sprinkle w/ parsley. Mix  garlic, oil, vinegar, salt, basil and pepper. Pour over tomato and parsley.  Cover and chill 3hrs or overnight.    Recipe by: Laurie Shanks    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Laurie <mshanks@ibm.net> on May 16, 1998
