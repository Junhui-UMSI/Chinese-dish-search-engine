Baked Chicken with Artichokes
Paprika, Flour, garlic, Chicken pieces, Salt, Chicken broth, White pepper, Onion, Dried rosemary, artichoke hearts, Mushroom, Dry sherry, 
Soak top and bottom of clay cooker in water about 15 minutes; drain. Place  mushrooms, onion and garlic in cooker. Coat chicken pieces with mixture of  flour, salt, paprika, rosemary and pepper. Arrange chicken pieces, skin  side up, in even layer over vegetables. Pour in chicken broth and sherry. .  Place covered cooker in cold oven. Set oven at 425 degrees. Bake just until  chicken is tender, about 1 hour. Gently stir in artichoke hearts. Bake  covered until artichokes are hot, about 10 minutes.. Remove cover; bake  until chicken is crisp and brown, 5 to 10 minutes.    Recipe by: Clay Cookery    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "johndavid@prodigy.net"  <johndavid@prodigy.net> on Apr 19, 1998
