West Coast Wonder
Garlic, Dijon mustard, Hoist sauce, Ground Anise seeds, Rice vinegar, Soy sauce, Fresh ginger root, minced, 
Combine the ingredients together in a saucepan and warm them over low heat  for about 10 minutes. Serve the sauce hot or at room temperature. It keeps,  refrigerated, for several weeks.    ~ SOURCE: SMOKE AND SPICE    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #232 by Michael McLaughlin  <mmclaughlin@micronet.net> on Sep 01, 1997
