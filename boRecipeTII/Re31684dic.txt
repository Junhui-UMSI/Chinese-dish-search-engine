Swiss Steak W/veg. and Grav
Leaf BAY LEAVES, Onions, All Purpose Flour, PEPPER SWT GRN FRESH, Salt, Cloves GARLIC DEHY GRA, SHORTENING; 3LB, Water, BEEF SWISS STEAK, TOMATO PASTE #2 1/2, PEPPER BLACK 1 LB CN, 
1. DREDGE STEAKS IN MIXTURE OF FLOUR , SALT, AND PEPPER, SHAKE OFF EXCESS.    2. BROWN STAEKS ON WELL GREASED GRIDDLE.    3. OVERLAP ABOUT 50 STEAKS IN EACH PAN. SET ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 5.
