Yogurt-Cucumber Sauce
cayenne pepper, seedless cucumber, fresh mint, Garlic, olive oil, Kosher salt, Plain low-fat yogurt, Honey, , 
In a medium bowl, combine the cucumber, yogurt, honey, mint garlic, olive  oil and cayenne.  Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.  Season  with salt just before serving.
