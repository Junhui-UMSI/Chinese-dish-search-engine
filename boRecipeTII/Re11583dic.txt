Raspberry Mirepoix
Diced Carrots, Cornstarch, Celery, Finely Chopped Green Onions, Minced Gingerrot, Unsweetened Orange Juice, Water, Chicken Bouillon Granules, Raspberry Vinegar, 
Combine Celery, Carrots, Green Onions, Water & Bouillon Granules in A  Saucepan.  Bring Mixture To A Boil; Cover, Reduce Heat & Simmer 5 Min. OR  Until Vegetables Are Crisp-Tender. Combine Cornstarch, Gingerroot, Orange  Juice & Raspberry Vinegar; Stir Into The Vegetable Mixture. Cook Over  Medium Heat, Stirring Constantly, Until Mixture Boils. Cook An Additional  Minute, Stirring Constantly. Maybe Used To Enhance Flavor Of Meat, Fish &  Shellfish. Fat 0, Chol. 0.
