Barley Pancakes with Banana
Skim milk, Whole-Wheat Flour, Egg whites, Maple syrup, Vegetable oil, Baking Powder, Bananas, Barley, 
In a medium bowl sift together the flour and baking powder. Stir together  the flour and baking powder. Stir in the barley.    In a small bowl whisk together the milk, banana, egg whites and maple  syrup.    Pour the milk mixture into flour. Combine quickly; dont over-mix.    Heat a well-seasoned cast-iron skillet or non-stick frying pan over  medium-high heat. Add half the oil. spoon in 1/4 C of the batter for each  pancake. Cook until bubbles form on top of each. Then flip and cook the  other side.    Repeat with remaining oil and batter. Serve immediately.    NOTES : Cal 321.9g, Fat 2.8g, Carb 65.2g, Fib 11.1g, Pro 12.7g, Sod 186mg,  CFF 7.5%.
