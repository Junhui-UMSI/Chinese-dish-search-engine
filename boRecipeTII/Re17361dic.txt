Walnut Cake
Orange peel; grated (option, Eggs, Vanilla, Salt, Powdered sugar, Matzo cake flour, Walnuts, 
Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form; set aside. Beat egg yolks and  powdered sugar. Sift flour; add to egg yolk mixture. Add vanilla, salt and  orange peel, if desired. Stir in walnuts. Fold in egg whites. Spoon into  10" tube pan. Bake @ 350 degrees for 50 minutes. Invert and cool before  removing from pan.
