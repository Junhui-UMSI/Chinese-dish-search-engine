Baked Herb Rice with Pecans
Water, Chopped; toasted pecans, Chopped shallots, Long grain rice, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt & freshly ground pepper, Chopped fresh parsley, Bay Leaves, Broth, Dried thyme, Chopped pecans; toasted, Dried Rosemary, Pimentos; chopped & drained, Butter, Tabasco to taste, Sliced fresh mushrooms, 
Date: Sun, 25 Feb 1996 22:41:09 -0500    From: "Vernon E. Phipps Jr." <cook4u@vivanet.com>    Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Melt butter in heavy oven-proof skillet. Saute  mushrooms and shallots until tender. Add rice and pecans and stir until  coated with butter. Add remaining ingredients and bring to rolling boil.  Remove from heat, cover and bake 1 hour or until rice is tender. Remove bay  leaves; garnish with pecans and serve hot. 4-6 servings.
