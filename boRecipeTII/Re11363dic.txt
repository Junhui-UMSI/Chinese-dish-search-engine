Rancho Mashed Potatoes
Salt, Butter or margarine, Pepper, Garlic, Chopped parsley, Melted butter or margarine, Heavy cream, Peeled medium potatoes, Egg yolks, Can sliced mushrooms, 
From:    Dot & Tim McChesney <jrjet@WHIDBEY.NET>    Date:    Mon, 8 Jul 1996 14:15:33 -0700  Cook potatoes with garlic clove until tender.  Drain and remove garlic.  Mash potatoes with butter (1/4 cup).  Blend in yolks, cream, salt, and  pepper. Cook mushroooms in 2 T butter - add with parsley to potatoes and  mix. Place in a 3 quart casserole oven dish and bake at 375 F for35  minutes.  8 servings.    EAT-L Digest  7 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
