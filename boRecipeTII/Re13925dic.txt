Wine Fruit Cup
White Wine; Dry, Sugar, Lemon juice, Anise Seed, Salt, Cinnamon Stick; Small, Raisins; Golden Seedless, Purple Plums; Sliced, Nectarines, Sliced, 
In an enameled or stainless steel saucepan, combine the wine, sugar, lemon  juice, anise seed, salt and cinnamon stick and bring to a boil. Turn off  heat and cool to room temperature. Combine the raisins, plums, and peaches in a bowl and strain the cooled wine syrup over them. Cover and refrigerate  for several hours, stirring occasionally. NOTE: This fruit cup can be  served as a dessert also.
