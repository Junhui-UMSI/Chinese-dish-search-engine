Artichoke Salad
salt and pepper, olive oil, Mushrooms, Water packed artichoke, Mayonnaise, Wine vinegar, Green onions, Water chestnuts, 
Wash artichoke hearts and drain. Put in salad bowl. Slice water chestnuts  as thinly as possible and add to bowl. Add mushrooms and slice green onions  including tops, into bowl.     Make dressing of oil, wine vinegar, salt and pepper. Add mayonnaise, beat  well and pour over salad.     Must be made 12 to 24 hours before serving.     Will keep in refrigerator several days.
