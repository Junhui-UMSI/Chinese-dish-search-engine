Sweet and Sour Shrimp
Plums; pitted, Pepper; to taste, Salt, Apricot preserves, Dijon mustard, Shrimp; cooked and shelled, Nectarines; pitted, Lemon, Dried chili flakes; finely, 
1. In food processor puree nectarines, plums, preserves, mustard, chili  flakes, salt, pepper and lemon juice. Transfer to large bowl.    2. Add shrimp to bowl, tossing to coat. Serve cold as an appetizer or hot  over rice as an entree.    Makes 4 servings. Preparation time: About 20 minutes. Cooking time: About5  minutes.    Per serving: About 234 cal, 25 g pro, 32 g car, 4 g fat, 13% cal from fat,  173 mg cholesterol, 550 mg sod, 4 g fiber.
