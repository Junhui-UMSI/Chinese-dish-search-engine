Basic Honey Taffy
Honey, 
Cook honey to hard crack stage (285F) stirring constantly. Pour onto  buttered marble slab. As soon as outside edges cool enough to touch,fold to  center and make long roll. Start stretching or pulling while hot. Pull  until honey becomes light and porous and small strings develop. Cut into  short pieces. Place in paper lined metal cans for two days to soften.
