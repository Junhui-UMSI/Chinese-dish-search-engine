All Star Veggie Burger
mozzarella cheese, Rolled oats, Egg whites, Zucchini, White onion, Garlic, Shiitake mushrooms, Red pepper, 
RECIPE FOR HEALTH SHOW #RHG217 (Courtesy of Ex. Chef Michael Cutler,  Official All Star Cafe)    Combine all ingredients in a food processor. Pulse the on/off switch to  roughly combine ingredients.    Do not over mix. Final mixing can be done by hand. Form into four ounce  patties.    In a skillet add a small amount of olive oil. When pan is hot add pattie.  Cook one minute per side.    Dress with your favorite bread and toppings.    Yield: 4 patties    Calories: 389    Calories from Fat: 10.9 percent    Posted to Digest eat-lf.v097.n189 by "Tina D. Bell"  <tdbell@altair.csustan.edu> on Jul 26, 1997
