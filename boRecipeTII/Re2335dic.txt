Beannut Butter
Garbanzo beans; canned,, Peanut butter; reduced fat, Honey, 
Mash beans well and mix with peanut butter and honey. Makes about 3/4 cup.    Per Tbsp: about 1.7 g fat and 49 Cal (29.8% cff)    Recipe by: Prevention Magazine, adapted    Posted to EAT-LF Digest by Jenny Herl <jlherl@uiuc.edu> on Nov 25, 1998,
