Pimento-Onion Relish
Vinegar, Water, Whole pimentos, quartered (4, Sugar, Fine herbs, Vidalia onion, thinly sliced, 
Combine vinegar, fine herbs, sugar and water. Add pimentos and onion;  marinate overnight. Drain; serve with meat.    SOURCE:* The Orignal Vidalia Onion Cookbook, compiled by Pam McIntyre,  Published by: The Vidalia, Georgia Chamber of Commerce. POSTED BY: Jim  Bodle 5/93
