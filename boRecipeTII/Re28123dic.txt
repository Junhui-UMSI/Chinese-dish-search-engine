Baked Crab and Shrimp
Green Onions, Mayonnaise, Tabasco sauce, Green pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Lemon juice, bread crumbs, Lump crabmeat, shrimp, Celery, 
Combine all ingredients in a buttered 1-1/2 quart casserole or 6 buttered  seafood shells. Top with buttered bread crumbs. Bake in a 350 degree oven  for 25 to 30 minutes or until bubbly hot. This may be made early in the day  and cooked at the last minute. Serves 4 to 6.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
