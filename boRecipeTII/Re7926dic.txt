Sour Cream, Cooked noodles -or-, Beef (chuck, blade or round), Bacon drippings, Black pepper, Garlic clove ,sliced, Lemon Rind, Potatoes cooked and cubed, Diced onion, Salt, 
from: The wonderful World of Cooking    Brown meat in drippings Add seasonings, peel, onion and garlic. Pour sour  cream over. Cover. Simmer(but do not boil ) for 3 1/2 hours, basting  occasionally.
