Banana Holiday Quickbread
Shortening, Mashed bananas; about 3, Baking Powder, Flour, Candied fruit; mixed, Eggs, Walnuts, Sugar, Salt, Raisins, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 5"x8"x2" loaf pan. Combine shortening  and sugar, beat until light and fluffy. Add eggs and beat well. Sift  together dry ingredients. Add to batter alternately with the mashed  bananas. Stir in nuts, candied fruit and raisins. Pour batter into prepared  pan. Bake for 55-60 mins. or until tests done with a toothpick.
