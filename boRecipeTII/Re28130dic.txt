Baked Crabmeat and Avocado
Lumb crabmeat, Anchovy filets, VGHC42A-----, Avocados; peel -- slice, Salt and pepper, Butter -- melted, Bread crumbs, WALDINE VAN GEFFEN, Cream of mushroom soup, Butter, Evaporated milk, 
Melt butter, add soup and simmer 5 minutes. Stir in crabmeat, milk, salt  and pepper and simmer 5 minutes longer. Line a baking dish with avocado  slices and pour crabmeat mixture over avocado. Sprinkle with bread crumbs  and brush with melted butter. Heat until brown, approximately 15 minutes at  350~. Before serving sprinkle with a few drops of anchovy oil and garnish  with anchovy strips.
