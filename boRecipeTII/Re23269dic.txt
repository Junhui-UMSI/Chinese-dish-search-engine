Tuna Pie
Butter, (5-oz) noodles; cooked, milk, (16-oz) green beans, (10.75-oz) cream of mushroom, Onion; sliced thin, Grated cheese or biscuits, Salt, (9.25-oz) tuna; drained, Flour, 
Melt butter; stir in flour and salt. Add milk and stir until thickened. Add  beans, soup, tuna and noodles. Place in buttered casserole and top with  onion and cheese, or biscuits. Bake at 425 for 20 minutes.
