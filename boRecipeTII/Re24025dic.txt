Tostadas De Frijol (Bean Tostadas)
Cream cheese; cubed, Beans, black; cooked very, Tostadas, small, Chile, chipotle; cut in, Onions; minced, Avocado; cut in strips, 
Heat the beans with the cream cheese, but do not allow the mixture to  boil, as the cheese and beans should remain seperate. Spread a layer of the  mixture on the tostadas, followed by a layer of onion, avocado and chile.
