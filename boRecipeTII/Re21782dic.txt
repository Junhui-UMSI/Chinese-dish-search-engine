Apple-Cranberry Bake
Bisquick? Original baking, Margarine or butter; *, Ground cinnamon, Bisquick Original baking mix, Sliced peeled all-purpose, Granulated sugar, Fresh or frozen; (thawed), Brown sugar, Pecans, 
1.Heat oven to 375?    2.Stir together apples, cranberries, granulated sugar, 2 tablespoons baking  mix and the cinnamon in medium bowl. Spread in ungreased square baking  dish, 8x8x2 inches.    3.Stir together remaining ingredients; sprinkle evenly over apple mixture.    4.Bake 35 to 40 minutes or until topping is brown.    *If using spread, use only stick that has more than 65% vegetable oil.    ?Reg.
