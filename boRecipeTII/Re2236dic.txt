Baked Hush Puppies 2
Pepper, Yellow cornmeal, Sugar, Baking Powder, Vegetable oil, All-purpose flour, milk, Eggs, lightly beaten, Onions, Salt, 
from Erin Dillon    Combine first 6 ingredients in a large bowl.  Make a well in the center and  set aside. Combine the remaining ingredients. Add to dry mixture. Stir  until moistened. Coat miniature- muffin pans with cooking spray. Spoon 1  tablespoon batter into each cup.  Bake at 425F for 15 minutes. Remove from  pans and serve immediately.    Yield:  3 dozen    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Aug  17, 1998
