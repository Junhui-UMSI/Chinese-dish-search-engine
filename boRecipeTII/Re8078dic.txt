Potato Biscuit Crust
Shortening, All-purpose flour, Instant mashed potato, Salt, Hot water, milk, Baking Powder, 
Mix potatoes and water in medium bowl. Let stand until water is absorbed.  Mix in shortening. Stir in flour, baking powder and salt. Stir in just  enough milk to make a stiff dough. Knead on floured surface until dough is  smooth, about 10 times. Pat into desired shape.    Posted By pgl@iglou.com (Don Thomas) On rec.food.recipes or  rec.food.cooking
