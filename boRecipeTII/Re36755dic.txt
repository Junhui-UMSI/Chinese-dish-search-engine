Strawberry Jello Mold (M_c-Tx)
Pineapple crushed, Sour cream, Walnuts, Frozen strawberries, strawberry Jello, cranberry sauce, 
Drain pineapple.  Make Jello with 1-1/2 cup hot water, no cold.  Add  cranberry sauce.  Add sour cream.  Add strawberries and pineapples and  nuts.  Chill at least 4 hours.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #296 by "Deborah K?hnen"  <DEBKUHNEN@classic.msn.com> on Nov 14, 97
