Baked Overnight French Toast
Loaf (12 3/4 inch) french, Powdered sugar, Butter; room temperature, Maple-walnut syrup (recipe, Eggs, Salt, milk, Sugar, Maple syrup, Vanilla, 
Spread butter over bottom of heavy large baking pan with 1 inch sides.  Arrange bread sliced in pan. In large bowl beat eggs, milk, sugar, syrup,  vanilla and salt to blend. Pour mixture over bread. Turn bread slices to  coat. Cover with plastic and refrigerate overnight. Preheat oven to 400  degrees. Bake French bread 10 minutes. Turn bread over and continue baking  until just golden, about 4 minutes longer. Transfer cooked toast to plates  and sprinkle with powdered sugar. Serve at once passing Maple-Walnut syrup  separately.
