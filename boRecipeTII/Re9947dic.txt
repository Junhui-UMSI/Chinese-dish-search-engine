Puree of Turnips
Whipping cream, Potato; peeled, cut into, Turnips; cut in 1-inch, -(up to), Butter, White pepper, Salt, 
Place the turnips & potato in a saucepan. Cover with water, sprinkle with  the salt, and simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 25 minutes.  Drain the vegetables and mash well. Return to the saucepan. Add the pepper,  cream and butter. Stir to combine and heat gently until the butter melts.  Serve hot.
