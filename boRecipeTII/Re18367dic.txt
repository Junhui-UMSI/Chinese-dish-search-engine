Very Cranberry Muffins
Baking Powder, Sugar, Vanilla, Egg whites, Ww flour, Whole berry cranberry sauce, Skim milk, Chopped walnuts (optional), Quick-cooking oats, 
Combine the oats and milk and set aside for 15 min.  Combine the flour,  baking powder and sugar and stir to mix well.  Add the oat mixture and the  remaining ingredients and stir just until the dry ingredients are  moistened. Fill muffin tins 3/4 full and bake at 350 for 18 min. Let sit 5  min.
