Williamsburg Chicken
Mayonnaise, Envelope unflavored gelatin, Celery, diced, Leaf thyme, Pecans, chopped, Vegetable oil, Chicken, cooked, diced, Eggs, hard cooked, chopped, Stuffed olives, sliced, Canned peas, drained, Salt and pepper to taste, Green grapes for garnish, Lemon juice, Chicken broth, 
Oil a 10" X 13" pan. Soften the gelatin in a little cold water or  chicken broth. Place over low heat and stir until it dissolves. Cool  and stir in lemon juice, salt, pepper and thyme. ix well into the  broth and mayonnaise. Add all other ingredients (except grapes) and  pour into pan. Place in refrigerator overnight or for several hours  until set. Decorate with halved grapes and serve over salad greens.
