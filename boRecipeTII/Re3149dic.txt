Peanuts, Chocolate or Butterscotch
Chips -- melted, Skins on, Semi sweet or butterscotch, Roasted shelled peanuts --, 
Melt chocolate or butterscotch chips in a double boiler over hot (not  boiling) water.  Add peanuts and stir to coat.  Turn out on waxed paper,  separate with fork.  Allow to cool.
