A-1 Zesty Lemon Steak
beef shell, Garlic, A-1 Steak Sauce, black pepper, Lemon Peel, 
In samll bowl, combine steak sauce, lemon peel, garlic and pepper; brush on  both sides of steaks. Grill steaks over Medium heat for 15 minutes on each  side or until done, brushing with sauce occasionally. Serve immediately.  Serves 4.
