Strawberry Pie
Strawberries, Sugar, Water, Baked pie shell, strawberry jello, Cornstarch, 
Combine 3/4 cup sugar, 2 T cornstarch and 1 1/2 cups water.  Bring to  boil and stir until thick and clear.  Add 1 3 oz package of  strawberry jello. Cool slightly.  Put the 4 cups sliced strawberries  in baked pie shell. Pour the cooled jello mixture over strawberries  and refrigerate at least 3 hours.
