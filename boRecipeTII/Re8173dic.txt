Potato Dinner Rolls
Flour, Powdered Milk, Butter, Yeast, Flour, Sugar, Water, Salt, Instant Potatoes, 
Make sure that the water is 115? F.  Mix yeast in warm water, add sugar,  potatoes, and then melted butter. Mix powdered milk, 3 C. flour, salt in  large bowl. Add yeast mixture. Stir until well blended. Add Additional  flour (up to 2 3/4 C.) Knead for no longer than 10 Minutes. Place in well  greased large bowl. Cover. Let rise until double. Roll out and form rolls  as desired. Brush with egg. Let rise until double. Bake at 350? F. for 8-10  minutes.
