Bayou Boeuf Jambalaya
Sprig thyme, Salt and pepper to taste, Tomatoes, Garlic, Flour, Bay leaf, Uncooked long grain rice, Green pepper, minced, Onion, sliced, Sprig parsley, minced, Kosher smoked sausage., Kosher salami, cubed, Tomato juice, Shortening, 
Melt shortening in heavy saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour, salami,  and green pepper.  Simmer 5 min, stirring constantly.    Add remaining ingredients except rice. Bring to boil. Add rice to liquid.  Cover and simmer for 40 min. until all liquid is absorbed.
