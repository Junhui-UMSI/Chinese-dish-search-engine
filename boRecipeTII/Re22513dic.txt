Applesauce Pound Cake
Salt; (optional), Baking Powder, Cake flour, Applesauce, Skim milk, Cinnamon and allspice, Nutmeg, Raisins, Chopped apples or dates, Baking soda, Brown sugar, Canola oil, Chopped walnuts; (optional), 
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. In a large bowl, blend the milk, raisins,  applesauce, sugar, oil and spices thoroughly. In another bowl, mix the  flour, baking powder and baking soda. Combine with the wet ingredients.  Fold in the apples or dates. Use Bakers Joy in a 9x4-inch non-stick pan.  Pour in the batter and bake for 1 1/4 hours. Cool on a rack, slice and  serve.    Serves 8-10    Per Serving: Cholesterol - Trace, Calories - 214, Sodium - 193mg, Total Fat  ~ 0, Saturated Fat - .5g.
