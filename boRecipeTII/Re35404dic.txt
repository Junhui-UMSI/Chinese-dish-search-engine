Beef Eggplant Casserole
Stick margarine, Eggplants, Gound round beef, Sliced celery, Clove garlic, chopped, Rice, Onion, chopped fine, Chopped bell pepper, Fine bread crumbs, 
Peel and boil eggplant in salted water until tender. In a skillet with a  small amount of shortening, brown ground beef, bell pepper, celery, onion,  garlic, salt and pepper to taste. To the skillet mixture add rice and  eggplant; mix well.  Turn into casserole, cover with bread crumbs and dot  with butter.  Bake in a      350    oven until brown.  Serves 6.
