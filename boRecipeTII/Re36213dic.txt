Beef Stroganoff - Crock Pot
Beef round steak, Sour cream, , Mushrooms, Salt, Dry mustard, Pepper, Dry white wine, condensed beef, Onions, Flour, Flour, 
Trim all excess fat from steak and cut meat into 3 inch strips about 1/2  inch wide. Combine 1/2 cup flour, the salt, pepper and dry mustard; toss  with steak strips to coat thoroughly. Place coated steak strips in crock  pot; stir in onion rings and mushrooms. Add beef broth and wine; stir well.  Cover and cook on low setting for 8-10 hours. Before serving, combine sour  cream with 1/4 cup flour; stir into crock pot. Serve stroganoff over rice  or noodles.
