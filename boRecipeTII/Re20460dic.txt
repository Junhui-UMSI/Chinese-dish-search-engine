Vegan- Ratatouille for Two
Parsley, olive oil, Onion, Japanese eggplant, Tomato, Dried leaf basil, Black Pepper, Mushrooms, Salt, Garlic, Sweet red peppers, Zucchini, 
Het a 10-inch skillet over medium heat.  Add 2 Tablespoons olive oil,  onion, garlic, eggplant, zucchini, sweet pepper and mushrooms. Saute until  onion is translucent adding more oil if necessary. Add tomato, parsley,  basil and season to taste with salt and pepper.    Cover and simmer 10 to 15 minutes.  Remove cover, bring to slow boil and  cook until juices evaporate and oil reappears in bottom of pan. Serve hot,  at room temperature or cold. Makes 2 servings.
