Bbq Meatbaalls
Egg, SAUCE, Lean ground beef, milk, Salt, Pepper, Dry bread crumbs, fresh parsley, Worcestershire Sauce, MEATBALLS, chili sauce, grape jelly, Onion, 
Combine meatball ingredients. Brown meatballs in large skillet. Drain off  fat and remove meatballs.    Combine sauce ingredients in same skillet and heat, stirring until jelly  melts. Return meatballs and heat through. Serve warm. Makes about 5 dozen.    Taste of Home Ground Beef Collection 1996 Edition
