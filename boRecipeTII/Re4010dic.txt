Peppered Sauce
Coarse ground black pepper, Bay leaf, Beef poaching liquid *, Cornstarch, 
Dissolve cornstarch in one tablespoon poaching liquid. Combine cornstarch  mixture, remaining poaching liquid, bay leaf & pepper. Cook & stir over  medium heat until clear & slightly thickened, about 3 to 5 minutes. Remove  bay leaf. (Yield: 3/4 cup) * strained & divided. ** Sauce for POACHED BEEF  CHUCK ROAST.
