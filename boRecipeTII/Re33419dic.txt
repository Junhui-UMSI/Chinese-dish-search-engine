Basics Couscous
Vegetable or chicken stock, Couscous, Salt, 
Basics for new cooks:    In saucepan, bring water and salt to boil; stir in couscous. Remove from  heat; cover and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with fork. Makes 3 cups, or        4    servings.
