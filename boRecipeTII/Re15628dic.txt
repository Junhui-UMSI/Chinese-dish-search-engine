A Butter for Kids To Make
Whipping cream, Clean baby food jars, 
For each 4 children:    Take whipping cream out of refrigerator 2 hours before using to get to room  temperature. Divide evenly into jars, put on lids and let children shake,  shake, shake. When butter forms, pour off liquid (taste this!) and spread  on crackers or bread . (Add a touch of salt if desired.) Option: Put 1 pint  of cream into a mayonnaise jar and take turns shaking.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 25, 1998
