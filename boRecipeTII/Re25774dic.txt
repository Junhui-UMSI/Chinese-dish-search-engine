Baboli Pizza Crust
Pepper;ground, olive oil, Water;warm, Olive oil;+ extra for pan, TOPPINGS, Salt  (Optional), Yeast, Flour, Water;warm, SEA SALT;OR KOSHER SALT, Water, Chopped Rosemary and Thyme, 
Mix together first six ingredients and let rise 30 to 0 minutes. Divide  dough into 3 parts and place in 3 olive-oiled pie pans. Dimple dough with  fingers. Mix olive oil and water, brush tops with this mixture. Sprinkle on  top: sea salt, pepper, rosemary and thyme. Let rise 50 to 60 mins. Bake at  350 degrees F. for 25 minutes. May dip in olive oil. Peggy also says if the  herbs are dried to soak in water 10 minutes and then pat dry with paper  towels.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Julie Bertholf <jewel1@ix.netcom.com> on  Feb 07, 1998
