Peanut-Cereal Bars
Peanuts, Corn syrup, Sugar, Water, Peanut butter, Corn flakes; or bran flakes, Chocolate chips, Eggs, Wheaties, 
Mix peanut butter, sugar and eggs in a pot.  Cook over medium heat till it  bails and pulls away from the sides.  Stir in nuts and cereal.  Spread in  greased 9" square pan.  Combine rest of ings., and heat till smooth.  Drizzle over cookie mixture in a zigzag pattern. Cut into bars when cool.
