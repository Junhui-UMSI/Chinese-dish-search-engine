Cinnamon Rolls With dried Cranberries and Pecans
Roll Dough, Dried cranberries , Pecans or walnuts , Almonds toasted until golden brown and chopped , Maple syrup , Ground cinnamon , Egg, 
Lightly oil a 9-inch-by-13-inch  baking sheet.   On floured board or counter, roll out dough into a  16-by-12-inch rectangle.  Mix cranberries, nuts and maple syrup in small  bowl; spread evenly over dough, leaving a 1/2-inch margin on all sides.  Sprinkle cinnamon evenly over filling.   With long side facing you, roll up  dough jellyroll fashion; pinch edges and ends together. Slice into 12  sections with sharp serrated knife.    Place cinnamon rolls, cut sides down, on prepared baking sheet. Cover with  clean dish towel and let rise in warm place 45 minutes.      Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Brush tops of rolls with beaten egg.  Bake rolls 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown.    Yield: 12 rolls.
