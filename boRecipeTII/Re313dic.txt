Leeks and Carrots
BERRY (CWBJ78A, Leeks, Carrots*, Juice of 1 to 2 lemons; salt, Raw rice, Peanut oil**, Sugar, Water, Tomato: chopped, 
*peeled and sliced in 1/4 in. rounds    **or kosher for Passover olive oil    Cut off roots from bulb end of leeks. Remove tough outer leaves and trim  tops. Slice lengthwise and wash thoroughly under running water. Cut into  1-in. pieces. Saut carrots and rice in the oil for 5 minutes. Add remaining  ingredients and cook; covered, on low-medium heat until tender.  Refrigerate. Serve at room temperature as an appetizer or a salad.
