Pumpkin Streusel Squares
15oz can pumpkin, Cinnamon, Pecans, Sugar, Sweetened condensed milk, All-purpose flour, Brown Sugar, Eggs, Salt, Cold butter; cut up, Allspice, 
Heat oven to 350F. In a medium bowl combine flour and sugars. Cut in butter  till coarse crumb texture. Stir in nuts. Reserve 1 Cup of mixture and press  remainder firmly into the bottom and half way up the sides of a 12x7"  baking dish. In a large mixing bowl, combine remaining ingredients. Mix  well and pour over crust. Top with reserved crumb mixture. Bake 55 minutes  or till golden. Cool.    NOTES : Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or some whipped topping if  desired.
