Sweet and Salty Snack Mix
Raisins, Peanuts, Pretzel sticks, Cinnamon, Butter, Honey-flavored graham crackers, bear-shaped, Margarine, popcorn, popped, Sugar, 
In a large bowl, combine pretzel sticks and peanuts.  Pour melted margarine over mixture; sprinkle with  sugar substitute.  Toss gently to coat. Add popcorn  crackers and raisins; sprinkle lightly with cinnamon.  Toss mixture gently.  Store in airtight container.  9  (1-cup) serving.
