The Best Passover Brownies Ever!
Eggs, Chocolate chips, Butter, Sugar, Salt, Matzo cake meal, 
Melt chocolate chips and butter in double boiler. Beat eggs and sugar. Add  cake meal. Add chocolate/butter mixture.    Bake in greased and (matzo cake meal) floured 8" square pan--350o for 25 to       30    minutes. Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #135 by  PBFinkel@aol.com on Apr 27, 1997
