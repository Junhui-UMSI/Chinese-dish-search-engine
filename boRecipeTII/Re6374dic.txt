Playdough 4
Flour, Lemon, Water, Vegetable oil, Salt, Cream of tartar, 
Cook over medium heat until mixture pulls away from sides of pan and  becomes dough-like.  Knead until cool.    Keeps 2-1/2 to 3 months unrefrigerated.    Makes enough dough for 3 to 4 children.
