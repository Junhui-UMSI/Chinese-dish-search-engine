Amish Frienship Bread Starter
yeast, Sugar, Warm water, All-purpose white flour, Active dry yeast, milk, 
Day 1:  Sprinkle yeast over warm water.  Add one tablespoon of the sugar  and let stand a few minutes until yeast is active and swelling. Commbine  remaining sugar, flour and milk in a 4 to 6 quart glass, plastic or pottery  bowl or container.  Do not use metal bowls or utensils as these my retard  starter growth.  With a wooden spoon, stir in active yeast mixture.  Cover  loosely with a towel so it can breathe.  Then start with day 2 (you know  the rest).
