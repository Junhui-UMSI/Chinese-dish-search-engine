Beef and Mozzarella Bake
Garlic powder or 1 clove, Cream of mushroom soup, Pepper, Tomato soup, Basil, Cooked macaroni, Mozzarella; shredded, Ground beef, Water, 
In 10" skillet over medium-high heat, cook beef, basil, pepper and garlic  until meat is browned. Stir in soups, water and 1 c. cheese. Add macaroni.  Pour into a 2-quart baking dish and cover with remaining cheese. Bake at  400F for 25 minutes.    Recipe by: Found on MSN Cooking Forum    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #976 by "M.
