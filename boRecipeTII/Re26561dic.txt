Badhapu Malu (Fried Fish)
Litre oil, Fish steaks, Btack pepper, Turmeric, Warm water, Chilli powder, Satt, Tamarind, 
Cut the fish steaks into six pieces. Soak the tamarind in water, remove the  seeds  and  add  to  the  fish together with the turmeric, chilli powder,  salt and pepper.Leave to marinade for 10 minutes.Heat the oil and fry fish  until it turns  brown.  Remove  and drain well. From: "A taste of Sri  Lanka" by Indra Jayasekera, ISBN 962 224 010 0
