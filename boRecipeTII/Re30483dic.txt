Banana Nut Bread
All-purpose flour, Margarine, Pecans, Eggs, Salt, Sugar, Baking soda, bananas, 
Cream margarine and sugar; add egg yolks (only) and salt. Add flour and  blend. Combine soda with the mashed bananas. Fold in well-beaten egg  whites. Add nuts. Pour into 2 small or 1 large greased loaf pans. Bake at  350 degreees for 30 to 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center  comes out clean. Source: Greenville, SC Jr. League Cookbook Judy Garnett
