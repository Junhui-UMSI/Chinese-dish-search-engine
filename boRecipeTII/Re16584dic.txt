Aidells Lemon Chicken Sausage Pasta
Into strips, Aidells Lemon Chicken, olive oil, Fresh parsley -- minced, Parmesan cheese -- grated, Salt and pepper -- to taste, Angel hair pasta, Leaves fresh basil -- cut, Lemon juice, fresh, Tomatoes, red ripe -- diced, Sausage -- sliced, 
While pasta is cooking to al dente in salted boiling water, saute sausage  in oil for 4-6 min. until heated through.  Add tomatoes, lemon juice,  basil, and parsley.  Drain pasta and toss with mixture until well coated.  Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle with grated parmesan cheese.
