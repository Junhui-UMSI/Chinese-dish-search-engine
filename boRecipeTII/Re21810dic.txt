Apple-Honey Custard Pie
apples, Yogurt, Honey, pie shell; unbaked, Eggs, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Salt, 
Date: Mon, 11 Mar 1996 21:00:37 -0800    From: johanssen@matnet.com (Johansseh, Jenny)  Spread apple slices evenly over pie shell.  Combine remaining ingredients  in the blender, and turn at high speed for several seconds. Pour custard  over apples in pie shell. Sprinkle on some nuts (walnuts or almonds  recommended), if desired. Bake 45 minutes in 375 F oven, or until sold when  jiggled. Sprinkle top of pie with chopped nuts (optional). Cool at least to  room temperature before cutting.
