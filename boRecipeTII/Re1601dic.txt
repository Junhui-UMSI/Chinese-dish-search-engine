Calamari Sauce
Garlic cloves, chopped, olive oil, salt and pepper, Can plum tomatoes, crushed, Mint leaves, chopped, Bottle clam juice, Whole squid w/ tentacles, 
Saute garlic in olive oil with salt and pepper just until garlic turns pale  in color. Add tomatoes, simmer 20 minutes. Add mint leaves, clam juice, and  squid. Simmer gently 15 minutes until squid is tender.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Aug  23, 1998
