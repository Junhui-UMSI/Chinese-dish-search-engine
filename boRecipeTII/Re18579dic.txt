American Beef Sausage
Water, Parsley, Bread crumbs, Eggs, Cayenne, Lean ground beef, Sage, Salt, Black Pepper, 
MIX ALL INGREDIENTS THOROUGHLY AND STUFF INTO HOG CASINGS. PUT INTO BOILING  WATER, BEING SURE TO COVER COMPLETELY WITH WATER, AND BOIL FOR ABOUT 1/2  HOUR. TAKE FROM POT AND ALLOW TO COOL, THEN REFRIGERATE. T O SERVE, CUT  MEAT INTO THIN SLICES AND BROIL SLOWLY UNTIL BROWN ON ALL SIDES.
