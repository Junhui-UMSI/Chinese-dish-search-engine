Beef and Cheese Burritos
Low-fat cottage cheese, Garlic, minced, Cornstarch, Salsa, Flour tortillas, 6 inch, Green onions, sliced, Mozzarella cheese, shredded, Ground beef, lean, Dried oregano. crushed, 
Cook ground beef, onions, and garlic in a small saucepan till beef is no  longer pink and onion is tender. Drain off fat. Combine 2T of the salsa,  the cottage cheese, cornstarch, and oregano. Add to meat mixture in  saucepan. Cook and stir till thickened and bubbly. Cook and stir for 2  minutes more.  Divide meat mixture between tortillas; roll up. Cover and  keep warm. In the same saucepan, heat remaining salsa. Pour over burritos.  Top with cheese.  3P, 1/2V, 1B. 293cal, 11gm fat.
