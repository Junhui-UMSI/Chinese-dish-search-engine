Amaretto Strawberries
Fresh strawberries, Amaretto liqueur, Vanilla ice cream, Slivered almonds, 
Wash and hull strawberries; drain. In a large bowl, marinate strawberries  in Amaretto liqueur overnight. Serve over ice cream and sprinkle with  slivered almonds.    Makes 4 servings. Posted to EAT-L Digest by Shawn Zehnder Lea  <slea@HIGHTOWERSERVICES.COM> on Sep 4, 1997
