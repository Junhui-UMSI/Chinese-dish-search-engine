Wilderness Hash
Bacon, Onions; diced, (no. 2-1/2) tomatoes, Ground beef, Tomato sauce, Salt; pepper, tabasco sauce, (no. 2) pinto beans, 
Dice bacon and fry until brown. Remove bacon; add onions to bacon fat and  stir until wilted. Remove onions. saute meat in bacon fat until it loses  its pink color. Then put all ingredients together; season to taste. Add  other ingredients; simmer for 30 to 45 minutes. Serve in bowls. Yields 20  servings and freezes well.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
