Sweet Potato
Brown sugar -- packed, Cinnamon, Temperature, Mashed/cooked, Allspice, 12 oz c, Unbleached flour, Nutmeg, Vanilla, Baking soda, Cloves, Orange juice -- at room, Baking Powder, Ginger, Egg white -- whipped, Sweet potato --, Applesauce, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.  In a  mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon,  nutmeg, ginger, allspice, and cloves.  In another mixing bowl, combine egg  white, applesauce, orange juice, vanilla, and sweet potato.  Mix dry  ingredients with wet ingredients just until combined.  Pour mixture into  prepared pan(s).  Bake for 15 to 20 minutes depending on pan size.
