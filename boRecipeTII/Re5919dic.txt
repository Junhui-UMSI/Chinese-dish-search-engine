Pistachio Cheese Spread
Chopped pistachios, Brandy or apple juice, Grated sharp Cheddar cheese, Green onions or chives, Cayenne pepper, (8-oz) cream cheese;, Garlic, Chopped pistachios; for, 
Combine all ingredients except nuts for garnish. Beat until thoroughly  blended (or process in food processor 30-45 seconds).     Refrigerate cheese mixture; when firm, shape into 4 balls and roll in  chopped pistachios. Or spoon into four 3-1/2x2-1/2-inch crocks; top with  chopped pistachios.
