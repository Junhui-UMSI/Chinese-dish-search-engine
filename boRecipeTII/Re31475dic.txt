Barbecue Sauce #17
Lemon juice, Worstershire sauce, Vegetable oil, Finely packed brown sugar, Butter or margarine, Chili powder, Clove garlic; finely chopped, Crushed cumin seed, Catsup, Vinegar, Prepared mustard, Crushed celery seed, Onions finely chopped, 
Saute onions and garlic in vegetable oil til golden and tender (about 10  minutes).  Stir in the chili powder, cook one minute.  Stir in remaining  ingredients (except butter).  Bring to boil.  Lower heat, simmer uncovered  for 30 minutes, stirring often. Stir in butter. Makes about 4 1/2 cups.  Keeps covered in fridge for months.
