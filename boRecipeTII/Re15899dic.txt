Acorn Squash W/ Apple Filling
Walnuts, Apples, chopped, Orange Peel, Brown sugar, Margarine, melted, Acorn Squash, COMBINE, 
Preheat oven to 350 deg F. Cut squash in half lengthwise and scrape out  seeds. Place cut side down in baking dish and bake for 25 minutes. Mix the  other ingredients. Turn squash cut side up, fill with apple mix and  continue baking for 20 minutes, or until squash is tender.  Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com (Judy E Ryder) on Feb 04,  1998
