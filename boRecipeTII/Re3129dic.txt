Peanut Tall Chips
milk, Shortening, Egg, Granulated sugar, Salt, Baking soda, Dark chocolate chips, Milk chocolate chips, All-purpose flour, Powdered sugar, Real vanilla extract, Packed light brown sugar, Creamy Peanut Butter, 
Heat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.    In large mixer bowl, beat shortening and peanut butter until well blended.  Add 1/3 cup granulated sugar and brown sugar; beat until light and fluffy.  Add egg, milk and vanilla; beat well. Add chocolate chips (both light and  dark. Stir together flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to peanut  butter mixture. Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Roll in granulated sugar;  place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly  browned.
