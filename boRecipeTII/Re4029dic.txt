Peppermint Cake
Unflavored milk;, Egg;, Granulated sugar replacement, Skim milk, Vanilla extract;, Vegetable shorting; softened, Peppermint oil;, Baking soda;, Cake flour; sifted, Baking chocolate; melted, 
Sift flour, baking soda and salt together.  Cream shortening and sugar  replacement unitl light and fluffy.  Add egg and beat well.  Add  chocolate, peppermint oil and vanilla and blend thoroughly.  Beat in  yogurt.  Add flour mixture and milk alternately in small amounts.  Beat well after each addition.  Pour batter into two well-greased  9-in. pans or one 9" X 13" pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40  minutes or until cake tests done.
