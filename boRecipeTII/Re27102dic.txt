Thyme Potatoes
Unsalted butter, Garlic powder, Onion, Red wine vinegar, olive oil, Potatoes; boiled, Salt and pepper, Thyme, 
Saute onion in 1/2 cup oil and butter.  Add thyme, garlic powder, salt and  pepper, and red wine vinegar.  Dice potatoes and pour sauce over them. Add  additional oil if necessary.    Recipes sent to me from Bill, wight@odc.net
