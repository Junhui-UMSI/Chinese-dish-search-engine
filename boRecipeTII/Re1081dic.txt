Corn Pudding
Eggs, Butter, Flour, Salt, Scalded milk, Small onions, Whole- kernel corn, 
Heat oven to 350 F. Butter a 2 quart casserole Put eggs into blender  container and process at" stir" until beaten. Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and process at mix only until corn is thoroughly mixed into batter.  Bake 1 hour and 10 minutes or until firm    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Cozinha@aol.com on Aug 21, 1998, converted  by MM_Buster v2.0l.
