Peppercorn-Sour Cream Mashed Potatoes
White potatoes, Sour cream, Black Pepper, Butter, Salt, 
1. Peel the potatoes and cut into quarters. Put into a pan and cover with  water. Bring to a boil, cover and reduce the heat. Simmer about 15 to 20  minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.    2. Drain the potatoes in a colander. Put into a large bowl and mash with  the sour cream, butter, pepper and salt. (You can use an electric mixer or  potato masher.) Serve hot.    NOTES : From "Great Women Chefs" by Julie Stillman.
