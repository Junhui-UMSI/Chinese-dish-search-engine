Turkey Cutlet
All Purpose Flour, POULTRY SEASONING GR., BREAD SNDWICH 22OZ #51, PAPRIKA GROUND, TURKEY BNLS RAW FZ, EGGS SHELL, PEPPER BLACK 1 LB CN, 
TEMPERATURE:  350 F. DEEP FAT    1.  COOK TURKEY ACCORDING TO STEP 2, RECIPE NO. L-147 EXCEPT SIMMER TURKEY  3-4 HOURS OR UNTIL TENDER.  DRAIN.  COOL. REMOVE NETTING.    2.  SLICE TURKEY INTO SLICES, ABOUT 4 OUNCES EACH. DREDGE SLICES IN MIXTURE  OF FLOUR, PEPPER, AND POULTRY SEASONING; SHAKE OFF EXCESS.    3.  COMBINE BREAD CRUMBS AND PAPRIKA.    4.  DIP FLOURED SLICES INTO BEATEN EGGS. DREDGE IN SEASONED BREAD CRUMBS  UNTI  WELL COATED; SHAKE OFF EXCESS.    5.  FRY 2 MINUTES OR UNTIL EVENLY BROWNED.    6.
