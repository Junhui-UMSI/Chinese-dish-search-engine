"Oreo" Frozen Dessert
Oreo Cookies, Margarine, Vanilla ice cream, Cool Whip, 
From:    Joel Ehrlich <Joel.Ehrlich@SALATA.COM>    Date:    Tue, 30 Jul 1996 09:44:15 -0800  Crush the cookies.  Stir in the melted margarine.  Press the crumb crust  into a 9" pie plate.  Slice the ice cream.  Place on the crust.  Pour the  fudge over.  Frost with Cool Whip.  Sprinkle more cookie crumbs over.  Freeze. Remove from the freezer 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
