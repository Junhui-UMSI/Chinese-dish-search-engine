Potatoes Romanoff - Main Line Classics
Paprika; garnish, Shredded sharp cheese, Potatoes; cooked, peeled and, Sour cream, Pepper, Salt, Green onions; cut finely, 
Boil potatoes in skins. Cool, peel and grate them. Mix all ingredients  except 1/2 cup of cheese. Put in greased 2-quart casserole; sprinkle with  reserved cheese and paprika. Refigerate 6 hours or overnight. Allow  casserole to reach room temperature before baking (about 1 hour). Preheat  oven to 350 degrees. Bake 30-45 minutes.    Yield: 8 servings    Taken from "Main Line Classics," edited by Gwen Fields Gilmore, published  by Junior Saturday Club, c. 1982.
