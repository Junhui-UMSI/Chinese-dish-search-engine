Anise Chicken
Chinese 5 spice powder, Anise seeds, Chicken broth, Uncooked rice, Worcestershire Sauce, Chicken tenders, Sesame oil, 
Cut chicken into 1-inch chunks, and fry in sesame oil over high heat, until  no longer transparent. Add chicken broth, Worcestershire sauce, anise,  Chinese 5 spice, and rice. Bring to a boil, and then lower heat. Cook over  medium heat until liquid is all absorbed.    Makes 2 servings.    Source: Lisa Rodriguez    * The Polka Dot Palace BBS 1-201-822-3627.
