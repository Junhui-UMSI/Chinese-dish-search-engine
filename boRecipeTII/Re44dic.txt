K.C. Masterpiece Sauce
Onion powder, Pepper, Paprika, Garlic, Water, Corn syrup, Tomato paste, Vinegar, Molasses, Brown sugar, Liquid smoke, Salt, 
1. Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan over high heat and whisk  until smooth.    2. Bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 45 to 60  minutes or until mixture is thick.    3. Cool then store in a covered container in the refrigerator overnight so  that flavors can develop.    Posted to bbq-digest by muddy@ibm.net on Sep 13, 1998, converted by  MM_Buster v2.0l.
