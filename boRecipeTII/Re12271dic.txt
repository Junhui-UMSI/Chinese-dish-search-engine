Zucchini Latkes
Salt, or to taste, Generous pinch of pepper, Whole wheat flour, sifted, Eggs, well beaten, Garlic powder, Cloves garlic, minced, -or-, Oil, for frying, Zucchini, shredded, Wheat germ or matzo meal, 
>From _Classic Kosher Cooking_ by Sara Finkel    Combine all ingredients, mixing well.  Drop by tablespoonful into hot oil  in a skillet and fry on both sides until golden brown.  Yields 10-12  latkes.    Posted to FOODWINE Digest 13 October 96    Date:    Mon, 14 Oct 1996 13:11:55 +0300    From:    Anat Levi <anat@OPTICS.JCT.AC.IL>
