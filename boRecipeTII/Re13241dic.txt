Wowchos
pepper Jack cheese, cilantro, Vegetable oil, fresh garlic, red onion, pimento-stuffed olive, green onion tops, chopped green chiles, 
Coat garlic cloves with oil and bake in 375-degree oven for 30 minutes, or  until soft and golden.  Cover metal baking pan (approximately 9 x 12  inches) with overlapping tortilla chips.  Distribute garlic, onion, chiles  and olives evenly over the chips.  Cover with cheese and bake at 400  degrees for 5 minutes or until cheese melts.  Top with cilantro and green  onion and serve.
