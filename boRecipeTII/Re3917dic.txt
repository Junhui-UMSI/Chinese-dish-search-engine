Pepper Slaw
Sweet green peppers; cored, Cabbages; cut into, Celery seed, Mustard seed, Vinegar, Pickling salt, Sweet red peppers; cored and, Onions; large, Sugar, 
Date: Mon, 26 Feb 1996 13:07:36 EST    From: AKSC87A@prodigy.com ( MARY JO KNAPPER)    Day before put all vegetables through coarse blade of a meat grinder.  Sprinkle with salt; mix well; let stand overnight. Next day, drain off  juice from vegetables; add mustard seed, celery seed and sugar; mix well.  Add vinegar just to cover. Pack in hot sterilized jars; seal at once. Will  keep for one year if stored in cool place. Makes 14 pints.
