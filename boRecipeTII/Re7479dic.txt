Pork Loin To Perfection
Water, Salt, Carrots, Shortening, Cream of mushroom soup, Potatoes, Paprika, Pork loin rib roast, Bay leaf, Whole small onion, Pepper, 
In a large heavy pan, brown meat in shortening; pour off fat. Add soup,  water, onions and seasoning. Cover; cook over low heat 1 hour and 15  minutes. Stir occasionally. Add carrots and potatoes. Cook 1 hour more or  until done. Remove bay leaf. Thicken sauce, if desired. Makes 4 to 6  servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Mar 22,  1998
