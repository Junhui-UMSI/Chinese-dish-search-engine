Pears Melba
Water, Raspberries, pureed with 2, Sweet wine (Marsala or, Vanilla, Sugar, Dry wine, Firm ripe pears, preferably, Strip lemon peel, Ice cream (i.e. vanilla,, 
Peel, core and halve pears.  Combine sugar, water, lemon peel,  vanilla and wines and bring to a boil.  Boil 3 minutes.  Add pears  and slowly poach in the sauce until tender and infused with sauce.  Turn pears occasionally with tongs.  Remove from sauce.  Reduce sauce  until very syrupy. Cool.    Place a spoonful of sauce in the bottom of an individual serving bowl.  Place a 2-ounce scoop of ice cream on the sauce and top with the  poached pear.  Drizzle with the pureed raspberries and serve.    Serves 8.    PER SERVING:  225 calories, 2 g protein, 36 g carbohydrate, 6 g fat  (4 g saturated), 22 mg cholesterol, 29 mg sodium, 4 g fiber.
