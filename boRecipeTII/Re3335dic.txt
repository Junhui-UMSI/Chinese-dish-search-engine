Pears with Ginger Cheese
Walnuts, Chilled, Fresh Lemon Juice, Fresh lettuce leaves, Pack (8oz) cream cheese, (16oz ea) Bartlett pears,, Ground ginger, 
Drain pears, reserving 2 Tbls. syrup. Whip cheese in small bowl until  creamy; whip in syrup & lemon juice. Stir in walnuts & ginger.  Arrange pear halves on lettuce leaves & fill centers with cheese.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
