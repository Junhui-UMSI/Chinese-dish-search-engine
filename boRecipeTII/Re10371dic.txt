Quick Chicken and Noodles
(14.5 oz.) Swanson chicken, Garlic powder, Skinless boneless chicken, Paprika, Frozen broccoli,, Dry wide egg noodles, Pepper, Dried basil leaves; crushed, 
In a medium nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, cook chicken 10 minutes  or until browned.  Sprinkle with garlic powder and paprika.  Set chicken  aside.    Add broth, basil, pepper and vegetables.  Heat to a boil.  Stir in noodles.  Return chicken to pan.  Reduce heat to low.  Cover and cook 10 minutes or  until chicken is no longer pink.  If desired, garnish with fresh basil.
