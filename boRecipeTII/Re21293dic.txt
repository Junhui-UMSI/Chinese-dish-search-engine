Unbaked Caramel Cookies
Butter or margarine, Sugar, instant butterscotch pudding mix, evaporated milk, Quick-cooking Oatmeal, 
Combine sugar, butter and evaporated milk; stir over low heat until  mixture comes to a full rolling boil. Remove from heat and add pudding mix  and oatmeal. Stir thoroughly. Cool 15 minutes, the drop by rounded  spoonfuls onto waxed=paper lined cookie pans.    These are almost like candy, too.    From - THE LADIES AID COOKBOOK by Beatrice Vaughan. Pub by The Stephen  Greene Press, Brattleboro, Vermont. 1971    Shared by Robert Rostrup
