Bavarian Pork Roast with Skin
Onion; sliced, Flour, Salt and pepper to taste, Pork shoulder roast with, Carrot; sliced, Water, Cloves, 
Score skin on roast in 1/2-inch squares; salt & pepper to taste. Place  cloves in cuts of skin. Cover with onions & carrots. Place in pan with  water; bake in 325 oven for 2 hours or until skin is crisp. Shake flour,  salt & pepper & mix with a little water. Stir into pan liquid to make gravy  & serve with German country-style potato salad (see index for recipe).
