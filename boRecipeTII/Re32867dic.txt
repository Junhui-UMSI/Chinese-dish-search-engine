Sweet Potatoes in Applesauce
Sweet potaotes; 16oz, draind, Applesauce; 8 oz., Brown sugar; firmly packed, Salt, Nutmeg, Butter (or marg.), 
Place sweet potatoes in a 1 quart casserole.  Sprinkle with salt. Spoon  applesauce over potatoes.  Sprinkle with brown sugar; dot with butter.  Sprinkle with nutmeg. Bake at 375 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy  Coleman.
