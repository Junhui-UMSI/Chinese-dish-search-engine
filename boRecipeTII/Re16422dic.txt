Weingelee (Dessert Wine Jelly)
Medium dry white wine, Boiling white grape juice, Sugar, Sweetened whipped cream, Strawberries, Cold water, Unflavored gelatin, 
Sprinkle gelatin on cold water to soften. Stir in sugar and grape juice;  stir until gelatin is dissolved. Stir in wine. Pour into 6-cup mold.  Refrigerate until firm, at least 4 hours. Unmold and serve with whipped  cream. Garnish with strawberries.
