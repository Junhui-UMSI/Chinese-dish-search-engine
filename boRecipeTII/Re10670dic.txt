Quick Senegalese Soup
Lemon, Cream of chicken soup;, Curry powder, Light cream, 
Combine soup, cream and curry powder and mix until smooth; use a blender if  desired. Serve hot or cold garnished with lemon slices.    Makes 4-6 servings.    Naomi Arbot & June Turner, GOURMET ON THE GO, Ideals Publishing Corp.,  11315 Watertown Plank Rd., Milwaukee, WI 53226, 1974, p. 8.
