Peanut Cheese Loaf
Lemon juice, Cheese, grated, milk, Green Pepper, Fine bread crumbs, Onion, Salt, Egg, Cooked oatmeal, Chopped salted peanuts, 
Combine all ingredients.  Put mixture into a greased loaf pan and bake in a  350 degree oven for about 1 hour. Serve hot with mushroom or tomato sauce.
