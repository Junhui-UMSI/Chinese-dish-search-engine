Barbecue Sauce (Star)
Vegetable oil, Salt to taste, Honey, White pepper to taste, Lemon juice, Fresh ginger, Garlic, Tomato paste, 
HEAT OIL IN A SMALL PAN. ADD GARLIC AND GINGER. SAUTE FOR TWO MINUTES. ADD  HONEY, LEMON JUICE, AND TOMATO PASTE. SEASON TO TASTE WITH SALT AND WHITE  PEPPER. SIMMER FOR FIVE MINUTES. MAKES 1/2 CUP SAUCE.  Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Sarah Gruenwald <sitm@EKX.INFI.NET> on Sep 18,  1997
