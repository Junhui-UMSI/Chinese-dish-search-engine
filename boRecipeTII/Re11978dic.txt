Red and Green Cheese Enchiladas
(sm.) green chili sauce, Grated cheddar cheese,, Red enchilada sauce, divided, Corn or flour tortillas, Chopped black olives,, Green pepper, chopped, Fresh green onions, chopped, 
Warm tortillas with butter until soft; fill with some of the cheese and  olives, all peppers, onion and green chili sauce. Pour 1/2 red sauce on  bottom of Pyrex casserole dish and roll tortillas. Cover with remaining red  sauce, cheese and olives. Warm and serve. Posted to Bakery Shoppe Digest145  by Chad Alan Bumgarner <chadalan@softcom.net> on Jul 05, 1997
