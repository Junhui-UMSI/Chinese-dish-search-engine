Bacon Wrapped Smoky Links
Dark brown sugar, Bacon, Cocktail smoky links, 
1. Cut bacon slices into thirds.    2. Wrap a piece of bacon around a smoky link. Secure bacon around smoky  like with a toothpick.    3. Put wrapped bacon slices with toothpicks into a casserole dish. Cover  with brown sugar and refrigerate overnight.    4. Bake at 350 degrees F. for 45 to 60 minutes.
