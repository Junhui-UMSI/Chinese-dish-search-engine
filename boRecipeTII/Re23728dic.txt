Artichoke Supreme
Cooked shrimp, Bottle (large) Italian, Button mushrooms; drained, Canned artichoke hearts;, Onion; sliced thin, 
Place all ingredients in a deep casserole dish & pour dressing over all.  Marinate in refrigerator for 24 hours. Serve as hors doeuvre, in a salad  separately or on a lettuce leaf.
