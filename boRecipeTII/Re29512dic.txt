Tarragon Tempeh
Corn oil, Fresh chives; minced, Brazil nuts; chopped, Tempeh; cut into 1/2-inch, Salt, Tarragon, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease baking sheet with corn oil.  Saute tempeh in corn oil for 3 minutes. Blend Brazil nuts in blender until  finely ground. Mix nut meal with chives, tarragon, and salt, as well as one  oz water. Dip tempeh in this batter and place on baking sheet. Place in  oven for 15 minutes.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
