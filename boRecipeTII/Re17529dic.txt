Almond Cookies #1
Slivered almonds, Egg yolk for painting the, All-purpose flour, Butter, Butter plus, Sugar, 
Mix flour, butter & sugar with electric beater until dough resembles grated  cheese. Finish mixing dough with hands until dough forms a compact ball.  Refrigerate 30 minutes. Roll dough out until it is 1/4-inch thick. With  sharp knife square the dough edges & cut the dough into 1-inch squares.  With pastry brush paint the dough with beated egg yolk. Place almonds on  each cookie & dush with confectiners sugar. Place cookies on ungreased  cookie sheet & bake for 15 minutes at 350. Remove cookies while still hot.  Makes about 36 cookies.
