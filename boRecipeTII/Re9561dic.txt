Pumpkin Coffee Loaf
milk, Baking Powder, Canned pumpkin, Ginger, Shortening, Baking soda, Pumpkin seeds, chopped, Eggs, Whole wheat flour, Flour, Cinnamon, Ground cloves, Brown Sugar, Salt, 
Cream shortening and sugar. add eggs, one at a time, beat well after each.  Stir in pumpkin and milk. Stir together dry ingredients. add to pumpkin  mixture. Beat 1 minutes with electric mixer. stir in seeds. Bake in greased  loaf pan at 350 F for 55-60 minutes. Cool 10 minutes. remove from pan.  Cool. Wrap, store overnight. Makes 1.
