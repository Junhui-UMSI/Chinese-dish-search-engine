Basting Sauce for Roast (For Company)
Garlic salt, Kitchen Bouquet, Vinegar, Onion salt, Mayonnaise, Dry mustard, Salt, Chuck Or Arm Roast, Pepper, Celery salt, 
Place unseasoned Roast in Roaster with 1 inch water.  Cover and cook 3-4  hours depending on sixe of roast.  Add sauce and baste.  Continue to cook  covered til tender.  Thicken liquid with flour.    From "Trail Bosss Cowboy Cookbook" by Society of Range Management.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/west.zip
