Triple Treat Rice Cereal Bars
Butter, marshmallows, 
1.Spray a 13 x 9 x 2 inch standard flat cake pan with a vegetable cooking  spray like PAM .    2.Melt the butter and marshmallows in a large microwave-safe bowl for 2  minutes on HIGH. Stir, and heat again for 1 minute on HIGH. Stir well. (You  may also melt the butter and marshmallows in a pan on the stove over LOW  heat).    3.Stir in the cereal and 1/2 Cup of the M&Ms  .    4.Spread mixture in the pan and smooth with a buttered spatula or a piece  of waxed paper.    5.Scatter the remaining 1/4 Cup of M&Ms over the top of the bars, and  press them in gently.    6.Allow the bars to cool. Cut to the size that you want.    Recipe by: Judith Tucker, N.P. Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #554 by Sherry  Zeiss <zeiss@tab.com> on Apr 05, 1997
