Strawberry Romanoff
Strawberries, Heavy Cream, Sized Dar Chocolate Easter, Grand Marnier or Cointreau, Icing Sugar, Sifted, Strawberry or Mint leaves, TO DECORATE, 
Halve the strawberries and place in a bowl. Sprinkle with the sugar and  pour over the liquer, then leave to soak for 30 minutes.    Set aside some of the strawberries for decoration. Whip the cream until it  holds its shape the carefully fold in the strawberries and their juice.    Spoon the cream mixture into the easter egg halves and decorate with the  reserved strawberries and strawberry or mint leaves.    Source: Sunday Mirror Magazine
