Strawberry Sodas
Strawberry ice cream, milk, frozen strawberries, club soda, 
In a blender, blend milk strawberries and milk on high for 15 seconds. Pour  into five 12 oz. glasses. Add a scoop of ice cream and pour club soda on  top. (Strawberry soda can be used in place of club soda.)  Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by dsabraham@juno.com (Deena Abraham) on Jun  3, 1998
