Beef Stew 2
Pepper, Onions, Sliced, Celery seed, Unbleached flour, Sugar, Salt, Shortening, Mustard, Dry, Water, Rump Roast, Boneless, Tomatoes, (1 can), 
Melt shortening in a Dutch oven.  Add the meat and brown on all sides.  Place the onions on top of the meat.  Mix the flour and seasonings with 1/4  c water.  Blend with the tomatoes and add the misture to the dutch oven.  Bake at 325=F8F, about 2 hours, until the meat is fork-tender. Serve with  oven-browned potatoes.
