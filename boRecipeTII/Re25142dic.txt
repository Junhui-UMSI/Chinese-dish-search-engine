Tomato-Fish Cossack Stew
Garlic cloves minced, Tomato sauce, Lemon juice fresh, Black pepper, Fish boneless & cubed 1/2", Dill freshly chopped, Potatoes, Thyme leaves, Onions, chopped, Bay Leaves, Vegetable oil, Canned stewed tomatos, Tomatoes peeled, Parsley chopped, 
Cook onion and garlic in oil till tender.  Cut up tomatoes. Peel and chop  potato. Stir in undrained tomatoes, tomato sauce, potato, dill,bay leaves,  thyme, and pepper. Bring  to boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer 30  minutes Stir in fish, lemon juice,& parsley. Bring to boil, reduce heat,  cover & simmer 3-5 minutes more.
