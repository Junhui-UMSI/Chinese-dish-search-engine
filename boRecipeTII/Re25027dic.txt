Tomato Sauce #1
Onion, Salt, Italian Plum or whole pack, Fresh Basil, Sugar, Tomato paste, olive oil, 
Heat the olive oil in a stainless steel saucepan.  Add the onion; cook over  moderate heat until soft but not brown.  Add the tomatoes, tomato paste,  basil, sugar, salt, and a sprinkle of pepper.  Reduce heat and simmer,  partially covered, for approximately 45 mins., stirring occasionally. Press  through a fine sieve into a bowl or pan. Season to taste.  Serve hot.
