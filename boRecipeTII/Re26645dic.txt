Baileys - # 4
Chocolate syrup, Eggs, Irish whiskey, Vanilla, Cream of coconut, 
Method: Combine all ingredients in a blender. Blend 3 to 7 minutes. That  Bordens stuff is really gooey and takes a while to incorporate. From Tom  Morstead. Suggestion: put everything else in the blender and add the milk  through the hole in the lid while its running.
