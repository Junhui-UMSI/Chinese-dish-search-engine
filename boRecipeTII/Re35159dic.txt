Bee Chee
Black pepper, Soy sauce, -1/2 tsp crushed red pepper, Bacon; cut up, Sugar, Water, Cornstarch, Small head cabbage; chopped, Garlic powder, Onions; chopped, Ginger, Toasted sesame seeds, 
Cook bacon in large skillet until not quite crisp. Reserve 2 T. bacon fat  in pan. Drain and reserve bacon. Add onions and cabbage to fat in pan.  Stir-fry for 3 minutes. Add bacon and cook for 1 minute more. Combine  water, soy sauce, cornstarch, garlic powder, sugar, ginger, black pepper,  crushed red pepper and sesame seeds. Pour over cabbage. Heat well.
