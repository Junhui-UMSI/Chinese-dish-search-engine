Sweet Pareve Noodle Kugel
Vanilla extract, Sugar, Lemon juice, Wide egg noodles, Margarine, Apricot preserves, Raisins, Salt, crushed pineapple in its juices, Sugar, Apples, corn flakes, Eggs, Cinnamon, 
Grease a 9-by-13-inch baking dish.  Preheat oven to  350 degrees F.  Cook noodles in boiling salted water  for 10 minutes; drain well. Place hot noodles in a  mixing bowl; add margarine and mix well until melted.  In another bowl, stir together the eggs, sugar, salt,  vanilla, lemon juice, apricot preserves and crushed  pineapple with juice.  Add to noodles along with  raisins and apples.  Mix well.  Pour into prepared  pan. Sprinkle with corn flakes; stir together cinnamon  and sugar and sprinkle on top.  Bake for 45 minutes to        1    hour, until lightly browned.
