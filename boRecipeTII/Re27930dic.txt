Thousand Island Dressing (Canyon Ranch)
Chili sauce, Fresh lemon juice; to taste, Salt, Fat-free mayonnaise, Black Pepper, Sweet pickle relish, Nonfat plain yogurt, Red wine vinegar, Sugar, 
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix thoroughly.    It will keep, tightly covered, in the refrigerator for 1 week.    *REF -- Canyon Ranch Cooking: Techniques & Tips, by Jeanne Jones    Notes: Makes 28 Tbs. or 1- 2/3 to 3/4 cups.
