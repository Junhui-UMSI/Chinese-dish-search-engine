A Christmas Potpourri
Bay Leaves, Assorted pinecones,, Drops rose oil, Cinnamon chips, Drops pine oil, Three-inch cinnamon sticks, Whole dried rose blossoms, Fresh juniper sprigs, Cloves, Drops cinnamon oil, Orrisroot chips, Red rosebuds, 
Combine the juniper, rosebuds, bay leaves, cinnamon chips, cloves, and  cones in a big ceramic bowl. In a separate dish, mix the oils with the  orrisroot. Stir this mixture into the first one and put into a closed  container to mellow for a few weeks, stirring occasionally. Place the  potpourri in a dish and scatter the roses blossoms and cinnamon sticks on  top.
