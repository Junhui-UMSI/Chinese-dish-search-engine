Barbecued Meat Balls #1
Catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, Onion, Water, Egg, Ground beef, Soft bread crumbs, Vinegar, milk, Lemon, salt and pepper, Chili powder, 
Soak crumbs in milk. Add beef, egg, onion, salt and pepper. Shape into  balls and put in baking dish. Combine remaining ingredients in a pan; bring  to a boil and cook several minutes. Pour over balls and bake at 350 degrees  for 1 hour.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
