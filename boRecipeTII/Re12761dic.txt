Yucatan Style Swordfish with Spicy Mango Salsa (Gc)
Swordfish fillets; 6 ounces, Spicy mango salsa, Yucatan marinade, 
About 1 hour before grilling, brush the fish steaks on both sides with the  Yucatan Marinade, cover, and refrigerate.    Prepare a wood or charcoal fire and let it burn down to embers.    Grill the steaks for 3 minutes on each side, until medium. Serve topped  with 2 tablespoons Spicy Mango Salsa.
