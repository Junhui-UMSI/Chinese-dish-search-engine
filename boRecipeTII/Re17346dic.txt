Walnut and Veggie Burgers
Buns, Garlic, Breadcrumbs, Swiss cheese, Mustard, FIXINGS, MEALS WITHOUT MEAT, TV FOOD, Red onion, Cooked brown rice, Red bell pepper, Walnuts, Salt and pepper, celery, Carrot, Eggs, Red onion, Veg oil, Avocado, Catsup, 
Saute onion celery, carrots and red bell pepper in 1t oil until soft.  Cover if desired. Garlic may be added if desired. Add nuts, crumbs,  and rice. Form into patties. Fry in 1t oil until golden.
