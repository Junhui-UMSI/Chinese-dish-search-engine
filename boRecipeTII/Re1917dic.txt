Chinese- Egg Rolls
Enough oil to deepfry, Diced roast pork, Cooked and diced shrimp, Water chestnuts, Shredded bamboo shoots, Chopped bean sprouts fresh, Green onion finely chopped, Fresh mushroom chopped, Sugar, Soy sauce, Sesame oil, salt and pepper, Package egg roll skins, Egg, 
Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet.~ Quickly cook the bean sprouts over  high heat stirring constantly. Add mushrooms, water chestnuts, bamboo  shoots and cook approximately 2 minutes. Add meat, shrimp, green onion,  salt & pepper, soy sauce, sugar Remove from heat and set aside to cool.  Place 2 tablespoons of the mixture in center of egg roll skin. Take the two  opposite ends and fold in. Moisten other 2 edges with beaten egg. Roll into  a log shape. Preheat oil in either a deep pot or wide deep skillet. Fry the  egg rolls until golden brown turning every so often to get an even  browning. Remove from oil and place on absorbent paper to drain and serve  very hot.
