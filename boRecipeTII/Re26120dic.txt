Tofu Whipped Cream
Soy milk, Soft tofu, Vanilla extract, Sugar, 
Combine tofu, vanilla, and sugar in a blender or food processor and process  until smooth.  WIth the machine running, gradually add the soy milk through  the feeder cap.  Refrigerate until serving time.  It will keep for a week,  but can also be frozen.    Posted to rec.food.veg by sxeveg@delphi.com Tue, 8 Nov 94.
