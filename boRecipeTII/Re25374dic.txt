Tortellini with Shiitake
Shiitakes, Garlic, Tortellini, Fresh herbs of your choice, Cream, White wine, 
Cook tortellini according to package directions. Set aside. Saute shiiakes  with garlic, fresh herbs, and white wine for 3 minutes. Slowly add cream  and reduce to sauce. Add tortellini and heat to serve.
