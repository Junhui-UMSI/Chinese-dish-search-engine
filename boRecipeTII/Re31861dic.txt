Szegediner Gulasch
Sour cream, Butter, Pork shoulder, Flour, Onions, Cayenne pepper, Sauerkraut, Salt, Pepper, Paprika, sweet, Caraway seeds, 
1.Saute the onions in half of the butter;add the  sauerkraut and saute.   Add a little water and cover and simmer about 1  hour.Stir once or twice. 2.Season the meat and brown  in the rest of the butter;add flour,paprika and  cayennepepper and caraway seeds and mix all under the  sauerkraut. 3. 3. Cover and simmer for 1 hour, as long  as meat needs to be tender. 4. Mix a little paprika  with the sour cream and heat in the microwave or  waterbath untill warm. 5. Put gulasch on warm plate  and pour the sour cream all over.Serve.    By:"Sendung von Radio DRS 1 "Baff-Chuchi" Traslated by  Brigitte Sealing    Cyberealm BBS Watertown NY 315-786-1120
