Anns Ruby Chicken
Vegetable oil, Orange juice; frozen, Salt, Onion; medium, chopped, Cranberries, Orange rind; grated, Pumpkin pie spice, Sugar, Chicken pieces, 
Brown chicken pieces and onion in oil and salt. Add remaining ingredients  and cook 30 minutes. Serve with hot cooked rice. Crockpot: Add browned  chicken and onions and other ingredients to crockpot. Cook till done. This  was one of Ann Sharpes favorite recipes.
