Balsamic Marinated Onions
Stephen Ceideburg, Salt, Capers, Pepper, Virgin olive oil, Red onions, Balsamic vinegar, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Cut the onions in half, peel off the outer  layer of skin and slice thinly. Toss in a medium-size pan with olive oil,  balsamic vinegar, salt and pepper. Cover the pan tightly and bake for about  45 minutes, or until the onions are tender. Stir in the capers and season  to taste with additional salt and pepper.    Yields about 2 cup    PER TABLESPOON: 15 calories, 0 g protein, 2 g carbohydrate, 0 g fat, 0 mg  cholesterol, 72 mg sodium, 0 g fiber.    From Woodwards Garden restaurant.
