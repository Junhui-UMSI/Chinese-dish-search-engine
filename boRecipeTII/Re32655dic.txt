Sweet Pickle Sticks
Vinegar, Turmeric, Mustard seed, Sugar, Celery seed, Cucumbers; cut in strips, Salt, 
Use fresh, firm, medium-sized cucumbers. Pour boiling water over cucumbers  and set stand 5 hours. Drain and pack solidly into sterile pint jars.  Combine viengar, sugar, salt and spices. Boil 5 minutes. Pour over  cucumbers; seal and process in boiling water bath 5 minutes. Makes 6 pints.    NOTE:  Get a book on canning if unsure about sealing and processing.
