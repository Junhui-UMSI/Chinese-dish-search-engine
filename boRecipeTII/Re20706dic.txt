Apple Crumble
CRUMB TOPPING, Margarine, FILLING, Flour, Sugar, Brown sugar, Lemon juice, Cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 350* peel and slice about 8 large baking apples. Mix with  other filling ingredients. Place in bottom of pan (9x13" or for a higher  dessert 9" round). Cream margarine and sugar and flour till mixture is  coarse crumbs. Put topping on top of apple mixture and bake for 1 hour. You  can serve this warm with a scoop of parve ice cream or just by  itself...Yummy!!    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Aliza Blizinsky" <alizab@hotmail.com> on  Feb 17, 1998
