Frosty Lime Sherbet
lime-flavored gelatin, Hot water, Sugar, milk, evaporated milk, Fresh lime juice, lime peel, Green food coloring, 
Recipe by: Home Cooking Magazine, April 95 In a medium bowl, dissovle  gelatin in hot water. Add sugar and stir until dissolved. Stir in milk,  evaporated milk, lime juice, peel and food coloring. Pour into an 8-inch  square dish or 2 refrigerator trays. Freeze 2 to 3 hours, or until firm.     When completely frozen, break into chunks with wooden spoon and turn into  a chilled bowl. Beat with an electric mixer, on high, until fluffy and  smooth. Return quickly to cold dish or trays and freeze firm. Cover with  foil when frozen. This will keep several weeks. Serve plain or with fruit.   Serves 6.     Typed for you by Marjorie Scofield       4/16/95    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Aug 30, 98
