Tomato Salsa
Chopped cilantro or parsley, Salt, Ground cumin, Scallions, chopped, Diced ripe tomatoes, Juice of lemon, Can mild green chiles, drain, Or 2 jalapeno peppers, chop, 
Combine the ingredients in the container of a food processor. Pulse on and  off until the mixture is a coarse puree. Store in airtight container in  refrigerator.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmealnew.zip
