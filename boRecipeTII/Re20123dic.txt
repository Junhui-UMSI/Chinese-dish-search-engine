Vanilla Milk Shake
Water, MILK SHAKE ??, 
1.  STIR DEHYDRATED MIX INTO WATER. MIX THOROUGHLY WITH WIRE WHIP OR  MIXER. COVER CONTAINER.    2.  CHILL 4 TO 24 HOURS IN REFRIGERATOR TO 35 F. TO 40 F.    3.  STIR UNTIL SMOOTH.  POUR SUFFICIENT AMOUNT OF MIX INTO TOP HOPPER  OF MILK SHAKE MACHINE (ACCORDING TO MANUFACTURERS DIRECTIONS); FREEZE  TO A TEMPERATURE OF 27 F. TO 30 F.
