Pumpkin Pie Cheesecake
Eggs, Soft Cream Cheese, Cinnamon, Ginger, Pumpkin  , Egg yolk, Graham cracker pie crust, Sugar, 
Mix 2-8oz. Soft cream cheese, 3/4 C. sugar & 2 eggs, beat till smooth. Add  16 oz. C. pumpkin, 1/2 t. Ginger & 1 1/2 t. cinnamon. Brush the pie crust  with egg yolk & bake 350F for 5 minutes. Pour combined ingriedients in pie  crust & bake 350F 40-50 minutes. Cool & put in Refrigerator for a few hours  to firm. (The reason for the 5 minute bake on the pie crust is so it wont  get soggy)..
