Strudel Filling - Cheese
Butter, Vanilla extract, Seedless, golden raisins, Cottage cheese, drained, Cream cheese, Granulated sugar, Sour cream, 
Beat the cream cheese and cottage cheese together until smooth. Add sugar,  sour cream, and vanilla. Add the raisins. Mix well.    Drizzle the butter over the dough and then spread with the cheese mixture.  Proceed as directed in "Strudel Dough"    From the recipe files of suzy@bestweb.net  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by SUZY <suzy@bestweb.net> on Mar 27, 1998
