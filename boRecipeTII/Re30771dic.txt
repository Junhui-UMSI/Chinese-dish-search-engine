Tangelo Blossom Desserts
Cinnamon, Sugar, Tangelos, Nutmeg, Heavy cream, 
Peel tangelos. Carefully separate tangelos into 4 sections, sunburst  fashion. Combine cream, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in small bowl; whip  until stiff. Spoon into centers of tangelos. Serve immediately.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 413 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Dec 29, 1997
