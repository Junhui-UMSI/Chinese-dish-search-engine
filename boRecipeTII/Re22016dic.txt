Turkey Corn Chowder
Whole Kernel Corn , Potato, Evaporated Milk, Vegetable oil, Boiling water, Fresh Ground Turkey, Sugar, Onion, Salt, Pepper, Tomatoes  , 
Saute onion in oil in a large kettle until transparent. Push to one side.  Add ground turkey and cook, stirring, until lightly browned. Add remaining  ingredients, except canned milk. Stir well. Cover. Bring just to a boil,  reduce heat, and simmer 30 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Just  before serving, remove from heat and slowly stir in canned milk.
