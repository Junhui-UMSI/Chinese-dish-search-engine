Peanut Blossoms Cookies
All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Salt, milk, Egg, Chocolate candy Kisses, Sugar, Brown Sugar, Solid shortening, Vanilla, Peanut butter, 
Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup, level off. Combine all ingredients  (except kisses) in large mixing bowl. Mix at low speed until dough forms.  Shape dough into small ball. Roll balls in additional granulated sugar.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 for 10-12 minutes. Top each  hot cookie with candy kiss, pressing down so cookie cracks around edge.
