Sweet Potato, Oatmeal, and Cashew Loaf
Sweet potato; grated, Onion, Cashews, Tamari, Rolled oats, Nutritional yeast, Tofu, soft, Water, Garlic cloves; pressed, 
Grind the cashews in a blender.     Place the ground cashews in a large bowl. Add the remaining ingredients  and mix well.     Generously oil a loaf pan and sprinkle it with additional rolled oats to  keep the loaf from sticking.   Pack the loaf mixture into the pan.     Bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes, or until firm and brown.     Serve with your favorite sauce a salad and a steamed vegetable.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
