Apple Cider Stew in Crock Pot
Salt, Beef stew meat, Carrots, Thyme, Onion, Potatoes, Apples, Apple cider, 
Place carrots, potatoes, and apples in crock pot. Add meat and sprinkle  with salt, thyme, and onion. Pour cider over meat and cover. Cook on low  heat 10-12 hours. Thicken gravy.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 406 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Dec 27, 1997
