Asparagus with Cheese- Bacon Sauce
Asparagus, Flour, Salt, , Bacon, Joan Holden wgmb33a, sharp cheddar, Butter, milk, Pepper, 
In heavy saucepan stir flour into melted butter until smooth;cook over low  heat 3 minutes ( do not brown ).Add milk; continue cooking over low heat,  stirring constantly, until nearly thickened. Add cheese;stir until cheese  is melted and sauce is bubbling. Add salt, pepper, nutmeg and crumbled  bacon. Serve over hot drained asparagus. 6 serv.
