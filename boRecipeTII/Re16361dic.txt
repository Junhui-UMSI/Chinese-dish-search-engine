We Deboes Mothers Brownies
Sticks oleo, Flour, Sugar, Vanilla, Cocoa, Pecans, Eggs, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Melt the oleo and mix with sugar and cocoa.  Add the rest of the ingredients, finally stirring in the chopped nuts. Pour  into a 9x13-inch greased and floured pan. Bake 30 to 35 minutes.
