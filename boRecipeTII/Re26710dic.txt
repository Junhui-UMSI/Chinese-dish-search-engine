Baioli Sauce
Egg yolks, olive oil, Lemon juice, Salt, Fresh basil leaves, Cloves fresh garlic, peeled, Dijon-style mustard, 
In blender jar, place 4 to 6 cloves fresh garlic, peeled, 2 egg yolks, 3  tablespoons lemon juice, 1 tablespoon mustard and 1/2 teaspoon salt.  Cover and blend smooth.  With blender running, remove cover and very  slowly pour in 1 cup olive oil in a very thin stream.  Cover blender, turn  off and scrape down sides.  Adjust seasoning.  Add 1/2 cup fresh sweet  basil leaves, and blend briefly, until coarsely chopped.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/garlic.zip
