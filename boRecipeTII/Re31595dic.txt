Swiss Baked Scallops
Fresh parsley, Celery salt, Fresh bay scallops, Lemon, Swiss cheese, Paprika, White pepper, Butter, 
Place scallops in individual baking dishes. Season with lemon juice, white  pepper, celery salt and melted butter. Bake in 350? oven for 15 minutes.  Remove from oven and pour off a portion of the excess butter and lemon  juice. Place slices of cheese on top of scallops and place under broiler  until cheese bubbles. Ganrish with sprigs of parsley and a sprinkle of  paprika.
