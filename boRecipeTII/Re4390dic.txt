Peruvian Garlic Chicken
Garlic, Onions; chopped, Cumin seed; crushed, Low-fat plain yogurt, Salt, Vegetable oil, 4-pound chicken; poached,, Rocoto chiles; seeded and, Cinnamon, Roasted peanuts; chopped, Black Pepper, Parmesan cheese, Basil, Boiled potatoes for garnish, 
Heat the oil in a large saucepan and saute the onions and garlic until the  onions are soft. Add the chiles, cinnamon, cumin, basil, peanuts, cheese,  and the chicken meat to the saucepan and fold together gently. Cook to heat  through. Two or three minutes before serving, stir in the yogurt and  correct the seasonings.    Recipe by: Garry Howard    Posted to bbq-digest by wight@odc.net on Feb 15, 1998
