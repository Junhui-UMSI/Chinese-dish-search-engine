Apple Muffins
All-purpose flour, Egg,lightly beaten, Baking Powder, Vegetable cooking spray, Sugar substitute to equal, Ground cinnamon, Skim milk, Salt, Reduced-calorie margarine, Minced apple, Ground nutmeg, Melted, 1 tb sugar, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in a medium bowl.Combine milk,margarine,and  egg; add egg mixture to dry ingredients,stirring just until dry ingredients  are moistened.Fold in apple.Spoon batter into nonstick muffin pans coated  with cooking spray,filling two-thirds full.Bake at 400 degrees for 25  minutes or until lightly browned.
