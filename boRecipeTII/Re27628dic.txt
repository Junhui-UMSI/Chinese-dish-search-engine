The Champs Sausage Without Guilt
Egg white, Dried Apples; Finely Chopped, Seasoned bread crumbs, Sea salt, Ground Turkey; Breast Meat, Onion, Ground nutmeg, Black pepper, Cayenne pepper, Fresh Parsley; Snipped, Ground Sage, 
Combine all ingredients except turkey. Mix well. Add turkey. Shape into  patties. >From the recipe book included with George Formans Lean Mean Fat  Reducing Grilling Machine.
