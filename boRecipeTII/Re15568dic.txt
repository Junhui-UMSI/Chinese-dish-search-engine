7-Up Apple Dumplings
biscuits, Butter, Sugar, apples, Nutmeg, 7-up, Cinnamon, Butter, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, 
In saucepan combine first group of sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, butter and the  7- up. Heat until melted, set aside. Peel and core apples, separate dough  from 1 can biscuits. Separate and roll out each biscuit individually and  place 1 apple in each. Combine second group of sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg.  Sprinkle 1 tsp. of this mixture in center of each apple along with 2 T.  butter. Fold biscuit around apple and pinch dough. Put dumplings in 9 x 13"  pan.  Pour sauce over each dumpling. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes.
