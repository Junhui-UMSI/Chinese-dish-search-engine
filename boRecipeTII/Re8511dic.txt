Potato-Paprksh - Hungarian Potato Paprikash
Caraway seeds, Potatoes, peeled and, Onions, finely chopped, Green peppers, seeded and, Of water, Cooking oil, Paprika, Tomatoes, peeled,seeded, Clove of garlic, crushed, 
1  In a heavy-bottomed casserole, heat the oil and fry the onions and  garlic. 2 Add the caraway seeds, paprika, and water. 3 Add the potatoes,  peppers, and tomatoes. 4 Simmer for thirty minutes.    Difficulty: easy.  Time: 15 minutes preparation, 30 minutes cooking.  Precision: no need to measure.    A hearty dish, good as either a main course or a side dish.    Contributed by: Evelyn C.
