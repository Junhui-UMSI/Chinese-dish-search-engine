Aunt Elsies Hot Dog Bar B Que Sauce
Onions, Vinegar, Fat, Pickle relish, Sugar, Salt, Black Pepper, Catsup, 
Saute onion in fat until tender.  Combine rest of ingredients in skillet  with onion.  Cut hotdogs 3 or 4 times but not all the way through (about a  fourth of the way). Place in sauce and simmer until plump and hot.
