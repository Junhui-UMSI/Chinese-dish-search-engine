Praline Sauce for Ice Cream
White Karo, Butter, Heavy cream, Vanilla, Lt brown sugar, 
Praline Sauce for Ice Cream    Pecans    Melt sugar, Karo, and butter on low fire until smooth. Cool.    Add vanilla and heavy cream.  Stir in really well. Serve over ice cream  with pecans on top of sauce.    Makes 1 qt.    Keeps 1 yr in refrigerator.    Enjoy!    *Ellen*
