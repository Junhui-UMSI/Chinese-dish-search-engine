Barbequed (In a Skillet) Franks
Water, Sugar, Soy sauce, Ketsup, Chili powder, hot sauce, frankfurters, Salt, 
Mix all, except the franks, well and put in a skillet and bring to a  boil. Add franks and simmer 15-20 minutes. Tastes great and is fast.
