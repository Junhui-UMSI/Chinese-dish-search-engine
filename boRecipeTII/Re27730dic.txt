The Mr. Turkey 300-Calorie Sandwich
Tomatoes, Alfalfa sprouts, Mr. Turkey 96% fat free, Light wheat bread, Mr. Turkey 85% fat free, Mr. Turkey 98% fat free, Lettuce, Low-calorie Swiss Cheese, 
Place all meat and cheese slices and fillers between bread slices;  slice, serve.    NOTE: Calorie count is based on full sandwich; does not include  calories in lettuce, tomatoes and alfalfa sprouts, which are minimal.  Contains nearly half the calories of a sandwich made with regular  lunchmeat, cheese and bread.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.
