Stuffed Cinnamon Apple Salad
Dates (pitted and chopped), Pineapple diced, Apples, Sugar, Water, Salad dressing, Red cinnamon candies, Broken pecan pieces, 
Pare and core apples. Cook candies and sugar in water until dissolved; add  whole apples and cook slowly until transparent but not soft. Chill. Combine  remaining ingredients; stuff apples with this mixture and serve on lettuce.  Sprinkle with cinnamon if desired.
