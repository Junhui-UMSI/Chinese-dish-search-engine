Vegetable Noodle Casserole - Southern Living
Frozen whole kernel corn;, Cream of mushroom soup;, Sliced almonds;, Salt, Pepper, Frozen broccoli; cauliflower, Mayonnaise, Green onions; sliced, Shredded four cheese; (8 oz), Lemon juice, milk, Egg noodles; (16 ozs) cooked, 
Recipe by: Southern Living    Whisk together 1 cup cheese, mushroom soup, and next 5 ingredients in a  large bowl; stir in noodles and next 3 ingredients. Spoon into a lightly  greased 13- x 9-inch baking dish.    Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes; add remaining 1 cup cheese and, if  desired, sliced almonds. Bake 5 more minutes.
