Pear Relish #3
Sugar, Pears; ground, Green bell peppers, Red bell peppers, Mustard seed, Onions; ground, Vinegar, Celery seed, Salt, 
Mix sugar, vinegar, celery seed, mustard seed and salt in a large boiler  and bring to a boil. Add pears, onions, and peppers which have been ground  together; simmer for 35 minutes. Put in sterilized jars and seal.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
