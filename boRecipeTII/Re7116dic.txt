Poppy Seed Tea Cake with Cardamom Streusel
CAKE, Sugar; (plus 2 tablespoons), STREUSEL, Sugar, Baking Powder, Cake flour, Ground cardamom, Sour cream, Ground cinnamon, Eggs, Vanilla extract, All purpose flour, Baking soda, Salt, Unsalted butter; (1/2, Ground cardamom, Orange Peel, Orange juice, Poppy seeds; (about 2, Unsalted butter; room, 
For Streusel: Mix all ingredients into small bowl until moist clumps form.    For Cake: Preheat oven to 350?. Butter and flour 12 cup angel food cake pan  with removable bottom. Sift first 5 ingredients twice into medium bowl.  Using electric mixer, beat butter and sugar in large bowl until well  blended. Beat in eggs 1 at a time. Mix in sour cream, juice, peel and  vanilla. Beat in dry ingredients to blend. Mix in poppy seeds. Transfer  batter to prepared pan (batter will fill less than half of pan).    Bake cake 25 minutes. Sprinkle streusel over top of cake. Continue to bake  until tester inserted near center comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Cool  in pan on rack. Using small knife, cut around sides of pan and center tube  to loosen cake. Push cake up. Cut cake free from pan bottom. Transfer cake  to platter.
