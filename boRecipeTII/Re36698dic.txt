Strawberry Duet
Frozen strawberries; thawed, Egg white, Low calorie strawberry, Dish of sugar, 
Drain liquid from strawberries into a 2 cup measure. Add water to make 2  cups. Pour into a small saucepan and bring to a boil. Put gelatin in a  medium-sized bowl and pour hot strawberry juice over it. Stir until gelatin  is dissolved. Refrigerate, stirring occasionally, until the mixture is the  consistency of an unbeaten egg white, about 1 hour and 15 minutes. Gently  fold drained strawberries into gelatin mix- ture. Dip rims of 5 stemmed  glasses into egg white, and then into sugar. Spoon strawberry dessert into  glasses. Refriger- ate for several hours or until firm. Makes 4 servings,       82    calories each.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 22,  1998
