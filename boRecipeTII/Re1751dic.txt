Cherry Noodle Kugel
Cinnamon, Noodles, Eggs, Sugar, Apple, Salt, Cinnamon, Sugar, Maraschino Cherries, Crushed Pineapple, Fruit Cocktail, canned, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven to 350?. Combine all ingredients together, except last two.  Pour into greased 13 x 9" pan. Sprinkle on the sugar and more cinnamon on  top.    Bake 35-40 minutes on top rack of oven. Freezes well.  NOTES : A pretty, pink kugel.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by SuperSecDD@aol.com on Aug 30, 1998,
