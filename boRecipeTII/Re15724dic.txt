A.j.foyts 500-Mile Chili
Stewing beef; cubed, Jalapeno pepper; peeled,, Cayenne, Onions; diced, Salt, Water, olive oil, Mexican beer, (15-oz) tomato sauce, Chili powder, Chorizo (Spanish sausage), Garlic, 
Date: Sat, 20 Apr 96 08:43:56 PDT    From: eboyd@shentel.net    Brown Meat, onions, garlic and jalapenos together in olive oil. While  mixture is browning, add cayenne and chili powder. Stir often. Add tomatoe  sauce, salt, water and beer. Cover and cook on low heat for 30 minutes,  stirring often. Uncover and simmer for 2 hours. Serves 6-8.
