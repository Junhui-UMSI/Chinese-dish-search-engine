Turkey and Walnut Tacos
Onion -- chopped, Lettuce -- shredded, Cream cheese -- cut in small, Raisins, Walnuts -- chopped, Ground cinnamon, Vegetable oil, Taco shells, Cooked turkey -- diced, Cherry tomatoes -- halved, Pieces, 
In a large nonstick skillet, over medium-low heat, heat oil.  Add onion;  cover and cook until tender about 5 minutes.  Uncover, add walnuts and cook  until toasted, 1 to 2 minutes.  Stir in turkey, cream cheese, raisins and  cinnamon.  Stir until cheese mixture has melted. Divide mixture among taco  shells.  Top each taco with some lettuce, tomatoes and chopped scallions.
