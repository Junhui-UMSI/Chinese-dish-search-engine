Beef, Black Beans and Rice
Lean ground beef, Mustard, prepared, Brown rice, Tomato sauce, Lemon juice, Black beans, Cayenne pepper, Soy sauce, Vegetable oil, Onions, Garlic, peppers, green, Chili powder, 
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add beef, onions, green pepper,  and garlic and saute until tender, stirring frequently.    In a small bowl combine lemon juice, mustard, chili powder, soy sauce, and  cayenne with a small amount of tomato sauce and whisk until thoroughly  blended. Stir in remaining tomato sauce, then add to meat mixture. Add  beans and cook for 20 minutes, or until flavors are well blended. Serve  spooned over hot rice.    NOTES : This would be good with ground turkey or turkey kielbasa.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 466 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Jan 06, 1998
