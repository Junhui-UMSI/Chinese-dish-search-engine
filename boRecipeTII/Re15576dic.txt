7-Up Dessert
TOPPING, Pecan pieces, Can crushed pineapple, Sugar, Egg, slightly beaten, Butter, Diced bananas, Flour, 7-UP, Pineapple juice (if there, Pkg lemon jello, BASE, Cool Whip or Dream Whip, Boiling water, Juice of 1/2 a lemon, Can Angel Flake Coconut, 
Mix the jello and boiling water. Cool. Add the 7-UP. When mixture is about  to congeal, add the pineapple. Add the bananas which have been soaked in  lemon juice. Pour into a 9x13 inch container and let chill until firm.    To Make Topping: Mix the suagr and flour. Add the beaten egg and butter.  Add the pineapple juice. Cook over low heat until thickened, stirring as it  cooks, until mixture is thick. Let cool Fold in whipped topping and  coconut. Spread this topping over the firm chilled base. Sprinkle with  pecan pieces.    Posted By rubyd@wam.umd.edu (teresa marie Cannon) On rec.food.recipes or  rec.food.cooking
