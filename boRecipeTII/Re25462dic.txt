Tortilla Torte
Powdered sugar, Sour cream, Chocolate chips, (8 inch) flour tortillas, 
Date: Tue, 21 May 1996 08:08:06 -0500    From: pickell@cyberspc.mb.ca (S.Pickell)    Melt 1 package chocolate chips and mix with 2 cups sour cream. Let cool and  spread 1/3 cup on each tortilla (layer them).  Finish with tortillas. Do  not put chocolate on top.  Chill.  Mix 1 cup sour cream with 1/4 cup  powdered sugar and ice cake.  Chill.  Before serving, garnish with fruit  jam.  Keep refrigerated.  Flavor improves if allowed to stand 1 to 2 days.    Posted to MealMaster Recipes List, Digest #141
