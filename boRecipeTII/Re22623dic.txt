Apricot Chews
Water, Powdered Sugar or Frosting, Baking soda, Eggs, Powdered sugar, Baking Powder, Butter, Dried Apricots; finely, Sugar, Crushed Pineapple; Drained, Nuts; chopped (optional), Raisins, FROSTING, Lemon juice, APricot Preservesp, Flour, 
Cook apricots and raisins over low heat in water until water is absorbed.  Cool slightly and add remaining ingredients except powdered sugar or  frosting. Blend well and spread in a greased 9" pan. Bake at 350F for 35-40  minutes. Cut into bars while warm and sprinkle with powdered sugar or  spread with frosting.    Blend well.  Spread on bars.
