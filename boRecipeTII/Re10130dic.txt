Quesadillas Casserole
Shredded Cheddar cheese;, Vegtable Cooking spray, Ground cumin seed, Garlic powder, Chili powder, (8 oz.) Tomato Sauce, (4 oz.) Chopped Green, Onion powder, 8-in Flour tortillas, (16 oz.) Refried Beans, 
In a small bowl, combine first 7 ingredients. Place 1 tortilla in an 8-ins  round cake pan sprayed with cooking spray. Spread about 1/4 of bean mix  over tortilla. Repeat with 2nd tortilla & bean mix. Sprinkle remaining 1  cup cheese over top.    Cover & refreigerate.    Bake uncovered in preheated oven 350 for 35 to 40 min. til cheese is  bubbly.          8    Servings    NOTE: Can be given as a gift! Give the recipe with it.
