Apple Duckling
Salt, Calvados, Cooked wild rice, Dried apples, Cashews, Duck, Cinnamon, Apple juice, Sugar, 
Preheat oven to 500. Rub outside of duck with salt. Roast in oven for  10 minutes. Reduce heat to 375. Baste with apple juice. Remove after  20 minutes. Mix apples, cashews, cinnamon, wild rice & half of the  Calvados. Bake 40 minutes. Pour remaining Calvados over duck. Ignite  carefully. Serve while flaming.
