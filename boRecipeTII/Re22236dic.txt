Turkey Steaks with Sausalito Salsa
Diced, seeded tomato, Fresh Cilantro, To 3 - tsp pared, minced, Salt, Thinly sliced green onions, To 2 - Tbsp butter or, 4 oz can diced green chilies, 1/2" thick turkey breast,, Peeled, diced orange, 
Source: MAINPOUL.ZIP    Cilantro sprigs    Cut each turkey slice into 2 pieces; cover and set  aside. Combine tomato, orange, chilies, onions,  cilantro, gingerroot and salt in medium bowl; set  aside. Melt butter in large skillet over medium- high  heat.
