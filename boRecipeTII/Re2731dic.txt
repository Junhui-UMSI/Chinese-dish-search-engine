Peanut Butter Cake
Sugar, Cake flour, Eggs, milk, Shortening, Peanut butter, Baking Powder, Salt, 
Cream sugar and shortening, add peanut butter. Sift dry ingredients and add  alternately with milk. Add the beaten eggs last. Put in greased and floured  pan and bake 30 to 40 min. in 350 oven. ICING: 2 cups icing sugar 2 tbs  margarine, 1 tblsp. peanut butter and enough hot water to make it  spreadable.
