Beef Spinach Pasta
PATTI  VDRJ67A, Cream of mushroom soup, Ricotta, Noodles; cooked, Ground beef, Fried onions  , frozen chopped spinach, 
Micro spinach on high 3-4 minutes. Remove and break into pieces, drain  well. Brown ground beef, drain well. Combine spinach, soup, cheese and salt  and pepper to taste. Mix with hamburger. Add noodles and half the can of  onions. Micro on high 5 minutes. Stir. Sprinkle with remaining onions.  Micro on high for 5-7 minutes.
