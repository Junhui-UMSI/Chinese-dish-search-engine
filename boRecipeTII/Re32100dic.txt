Barbecued Cube Steak
Flour, Vinegar, Brown sugar, Catsup, Water, Pepper, Cubed steak, Worcestershire Sauce, Onion, Lemon juice, Salt, Salt and pepper, Fat, 
Flour and season steak and brown in fat.  Add chopped onion, brown lightly.  Mix ingredients, pour over steak. Cover slightly. Cook slowly 35 minutes,  or until steak is tender.    From Peggy Matheny in _A Taste of West Virginia: A Book of Favorite  Recipes_.  Leawood, KS: Circulation Service, Inc., 1991. Pg. 31. Electronic  format by Cathy Harned.
