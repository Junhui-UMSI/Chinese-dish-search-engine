Tosa Joyu (Soy-Based Dipping Sauce for Sashimi)
Soy sauce, Wasabi, MSG, Sake, Katsuobushi, GARNISH, 
Place all ingredients in a small saucepan.  Bring to a boil, uncovered,  stirring constantly.  Remove from heat, strain, ans set aside to cool.  Divide into serving dishes and garnish with wasabi.    From "Recipes-The Cooking of Japan" from Time-Life International, c1965.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/japan1.zip
