Amaretto Cheesecake From Loren Martin
Sugar, Amaretto liquor, Stick butter, melted, Vanilla, Cream cheese, Eggs, lightly beaten, 1/2 c graham cracker crushed, Salt, Ricotta cheese, 
Great topped with strawberry jam. Combine crumbs of graham crackers  and butter; press over bottom and sides of greased 9 inch spring-form  pan. Chill.  Preheat oven to 325. Beat together ricotta and cream  cheese until smooth.  Pour mixture into pan; bake 1 hour and 15  minutes, or until firm in middle.  Cool 30 minutes in pan before  removing. Cool completely (preferrably in refrigerator overnight)  before serving. Top with fresh fruit, or jam.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcyber4.zip
