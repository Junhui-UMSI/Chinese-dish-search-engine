Artichokes with Flair
Bacon; cooked and crumbled, (8.5-oz) artichoke hearts;, (8-oz) cream cheese, (10-oz) frozen chopped, Butter, Parmesan cheese, 
Quarter drained artichoke hearts and spread over bottom of an 8x8  casserole. Spoon spinach over artichokes. Melt butter and cream cheese in a  small saucepan. (Cream cheese will be lumpy.) Pour and spread over spinach.  Sprinkle crumbled bacon over cream cheese mixture. Sprinkle well with  Parmesan cheese. Bake at 350? for 30 minutes or until bubbly. Yield: 4 to 6  servings.
