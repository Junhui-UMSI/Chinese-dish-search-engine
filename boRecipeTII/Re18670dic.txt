Amish Oatmeal Pie
Cinnamon, Eggs, Butter, orange rind, Salt, Vanilla, pie shell, Sugar, Flour, Light corn syrup, Quick Oats, 
Mix sugar, flour, cinnamon and salt.  Beat 4 eggs until frothy. Add dry  mixture.  Add corn syrup, melted butter, orange rind, vanilla and the quick  oats.  Mix well.  Pour into shell.  Bake at 350 F for 45 minutes.    Source: Amish Cooking in Quilt Country TV show
