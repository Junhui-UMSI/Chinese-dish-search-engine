Pork Stuffed Cabbage
Chopped cabbage, Onions, Raisins, Chopped red bell pepper, Water, Herbed stuffing mix, Leaves cabbage, Ground pork, Egg, 
ds Pepper Arrange cabbage leaves in bottom and up sides of 1 1/2-qt.  casserole; set aside. Place pork in a colander suspended over a casserole.  Stirring midway through cooking, microwave for 5 minutes or until meat is  no longer pink. Set meat aside. Discard fat and place chopped cabbage,  onion and bell pepper in same casserole. Cover and microwave on high 3  minutes. Stir in water, egg, meat, stuffing mix, raisins and pepper.    Place meat mixture in cabbage bed and re-cover. Rotating dish midway  through cooking, microwave on high 7 to 8 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1025 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Jan 20, 1998
