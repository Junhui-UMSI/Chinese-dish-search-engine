Pumpkin Chiffon Torte
Skim milk, Frozen light whipped topping, Crust:, Pumpkin, Ground cloves, Sugar, Fat Free Margarine, Gingersnaps, Cinnamon, Unflavored gelatin, Ginger, Thawed, Salt, Filling:, Can, 
Chop ginersnaps.  Combine gingersnaps with margarine.  Press mixture onto  bottom of springform pan; set aside.  Microwave milk milk until 180 F.  Whisk in gelatin. Whisk until dissolved; whisk in sugar. Add remaining  ingredients; whisk until smooth.  Pour filling into Springform Pan.  Refrigerate 25 - 30 minutes or until set.
