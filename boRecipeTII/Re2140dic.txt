Apricot (Or Peach) Chicken
Whole chickens; cut into, Black Pepper, Lemon juice, Apricot; (or peach), Soy sauce, Canned apricots; (or, Brown sugar, Prepared mustard; (either, Garlic, 
Arrange chicken in roasting pan. Mix all ingredients except the canned  fruit.] Pour mixture over the chicken. Bake for 1 hour or until chicken is  done. Shortly before chicken is ready place fruit around chicken and return  to oven.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Kaplan Consulting Corp. (Dovid Kaplan)"  <kapcon@netbox.com> on Sep 14, 1998,
