Tropical Fruit Bread
Whole wheat flour, walnuts, Baking soda, Flour, Sugar, dried apricots, Shortening, Salt, Eggs, bananas, Baking Powder, Buttermilk, 
Preheat oven to 325F. Grease a 9x5x3-inch loaf pan. Cream sugar and  shortening; mix in eggs thoroughly. Combine bananas and buttermilk. Sift  dry ingredients together; add alternately with banana mixture to creamed  mixture. Stir in whole wheat flour, apricots and walnuts; pour into greased  pan. Bake about 50 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out  clean. Crack in top of bread after baking is characteristic.
