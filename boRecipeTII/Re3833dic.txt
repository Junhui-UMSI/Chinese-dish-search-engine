Pepe Salad
Pineapple juice, Box Acine de Pepe, Maraschino cherries, Nuts (optional), chopped, (16 oz) pineapple chunks,, Sugar, Mandarin orange sections,, (16 oz) miniature, Salt, Flour, Egg yolks, Cool Whip, 
Combine the sugar, flour and salt. Add pineapple juice and egg yolks. Cook  over medium heat, stirring constantly until thick. Cool completely. Boil  the Acine de Pepe in salt water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain, rinse and cool  completely. Add the Acine de Pepe to the pineapple sauce. Add the mandarin  orange section, pineapple chunks and marshmallows. Add nuts and maraschino  cherries. When ready to serve add the Cool Whip.
