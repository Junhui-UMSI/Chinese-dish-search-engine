Real German Potato Salad
No Fat Miracle Whip, Pickle juice, Apple, Onion, Spicy mustard, Eggs, Sweet pickle relish, Boiled potatos, Salt, pepper & paprika, 
Mix everything together carefully and cook for at least 2 hours or  preferably overnight.    German Macaroni Salad: The same as above, just substitute 1/2 large  package cooked and cooled macaroni for the potatoes and add 1 Tbsp.  ketsup.
