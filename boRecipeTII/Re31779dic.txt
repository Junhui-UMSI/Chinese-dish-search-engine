Syrup Pie
Dark cane syrup, Salt, Eggs, (10-inch) unbaked pie shell, Sugar, Lump of butter; the size of, 
Beat eggs, add sugar and beat. Add salt, syrup and butter; mix well. Pour  into unbaked pie shell. Bake in hot oven about 10 minutes. Lower oven heat  to 350 degrees and bake until firm, about 40 minutes. (Test to see if knife  comes out clean.) This makes a large pie.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
