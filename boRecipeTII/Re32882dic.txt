Sweet Potatoes with Maple Ginger Cream
Ground giner, Sour cream, Sweet potatoes, Pure maple syrup, Salt, Chopped pecans, toasted, 
Source: Southern Living Magazine 1/97    PIERCE sweet potatoes several times with a fork, and place on a baking  sheet.    BAKE at 375 degrees F. for 1 hour.    STIR together sour cream and next 3 ingredients. Spoon over split baked  potatoes, and sprinkle with pecans.
