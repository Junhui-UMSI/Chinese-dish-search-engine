Waldorf Salad #1
Pecans, Mayonnaise or salad dressing, Celery, Lemon, Lettuce leaves, Diced apples, 
Put diced apples into large bowl; sprinkle with lemon juice. Add celery and  nuts, and toss. Add mayonnaise or salad dressing to hold together, and  serve on lettuce leaves. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
