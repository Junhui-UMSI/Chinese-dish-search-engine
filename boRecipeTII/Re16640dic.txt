Al and Tipper Gores Chinese Chicken with Walnuts
Salt, Walnut Halves, Dry sherry, Green onions diagonally, Water, Green peppers cut into, Grated fresh ginger, Reduced-sodium soy sauce, Cornstarch, Crushed red pepper, Peanut oil, Sugar, Chicken breast halves, 
[NAH Editors note: Serve this over rice and youll cut the percent of  calories from fat. ]    DIRECTIONS: =========== Cut the chicken into 1-inch pieces and set aside.  Mix the soy sauce and water, then blend into the cornstarch; stir in the  sherry, sugar, ginger, red pepper, and salt.    Preheat a wok or large skillet over high heat; add 2 teaspoons of peanut  oil. Stir-fry the green peppers and onions for 2 minutes and remove. Add  the walnuts and stir-fry for 1 to 2 minutes until golden brown. Remove. Add  the rest of the oil and stir-fry half of the chicken for 2 minutes.    Return all the chicken to the wok and stir in the soy mixture. Cook and  stir until bubbly. Stir in the vegetables and walnuts, cover and cook for 1  minute.
