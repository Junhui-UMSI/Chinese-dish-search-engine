Praline Sauce
Skim milk, pecans, Vanilla, Cornstarch, Low-calorie margarine, dark brown sugar, 
Combine sugar, cornstarch and milk in a saucepan. Cook over medium  heat stirring constantly until mixture thickens and comes to a boil.  Cook an additional 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Add margarine,  vanilla, and pecans. Stir until margarine melts. Serve warm.
