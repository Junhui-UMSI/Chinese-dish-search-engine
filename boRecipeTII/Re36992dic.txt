Beet and Horse-Radish Relish
Lemon juice, Beets; home-cooked or, Sugar, Horse-radish; grated, Vinegar, 
Combine all ingredients. Cove and let stand overnight. Serve with meat and  fish. Makes about 3 1/2 cups.
