Tomato Basil Mozzarella Salad - Southern Living
Tomatoes; cut into 1/2 inch, olive oil, Salt, Lemon juice, Pepper, Fresh mozzarella cheese;, Garnish: fresh basil sprigs, 
Recipe by: Southern Living Magazine    Arrange tomato and cheese alternately on individual serving plates,  overlapping edges. Combine olive oil and lemon juice; brush over top.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper; cover and chill. Garnish, if desired.    Makes 4 to 6 Servings.
