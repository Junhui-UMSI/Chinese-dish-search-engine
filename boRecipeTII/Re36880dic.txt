Strawberry Sherbet
To 10 oz frozen, sweetened, Cinnamon, milk, Orange juice, 
Puree strawberries and strain through a sieve to remove seeds. Combine them  with remainder of ingredients and freeze as directed. Makes about 3 quarts.    This sherbet has a delightful fresh strawberry flavor. Try a scoopful in a  glass of gingerale for a special treat.    From: Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat Stockett
