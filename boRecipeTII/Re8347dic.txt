Matejka)    Date: Mon, 1 Jul 1996 09:56:20 -0500  Recipe By : Better Homes & Gardens, New Cookbook    In a covered saucepan, cook potatoes in boiling water for 20 minutes. Peel  and cube them. Let chill overnight. In a covered saucepan, cook eggs for 15  minutes. In a mixing bowl, combine, egg whites, celery, peppers, onions,  mayonnaise, vinegar, mustard, relish, salt, and black pepper until  throughly combined. Then, add potatoes. Chill for 6 to 24 hours before  serving.
