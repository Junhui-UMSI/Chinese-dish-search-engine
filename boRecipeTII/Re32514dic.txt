Sweet Dough (Sweet Dough
YEAST BAKER 2 LB, ROLL MIX #10, 
PAN:  18 BY 26" SHEET PAN                       TEMPERATURE:  400F. OVEN    1.  PREPARE ACCORDING TO INSTRUCIONS ON CONTAINER.    2.  PUNCH: DIVIDE DOUGH INTO 4 PIECES, 4 LB 12 OZ EACH; SHAPE  INTO A RECTANGULAR PIECE. LET REST 10 TO 20 MINUTES.    3.  MAKE UP: SEE GUIDE FOR SWEET DOUGH MAKE-UP (RECIPE CARD D-G-7).    4.  PROOF:  AT 90 F TO 100 F UNTIL DOUBLE IN SIZE.    5.  BAKE: 15 TO 20 MINUTES FOR SWEET ROLLS OR 25 TO 30 MINUTES  AT 375 FOR COFFEE CAKES.    6.
