Apple Oatmeal Bread Abm
Honey, Dried, diced apples, Active dry yeast, Bread flour, Oats, Salt, Corn oil, Unsweetened applesauce, Cinnamon, 
All ingredients should be at room temp before starting. Add all ingredients  except diced apples in order listed. Select white bread or sweet bread.  Press start. Add the apples toward the end of the final kneading when the  beep sounds.    Posted by Joel Erlich, Kook-Net conference participant.  U/l to NCE by Burt  Ford 12/97.
