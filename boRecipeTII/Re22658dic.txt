Apricot Crispy Treats
Rice Krispies, Low-fat margarine, Miniature marshmallows, Dried apricots, 
Heat margarine and marshmallows in a pot on low heat. Stir in apricots and  Rice Krispies. Pat into 9 by 13-inch pan and chill. Cut into squares or  bars.
