Peanut Rice
Celery; finely chopped, Rice, Salted Skinless Peanuts;, 
Mix together rice, celery, and salted peanuts in nonstick pan. Cover and  steam until hot, about 5 minutes. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons water if too dry.    NOTES : The popular Southern name goober is from an African word for  peanut, nguba. Delicious with chicken or ham.  Recipe by: The Ultimate Rice Cooker Cookbook By Betty L.
