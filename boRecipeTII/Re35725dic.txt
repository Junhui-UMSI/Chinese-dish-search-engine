Stuffed Cap Mushrooms
Almond slivers, Lemon juice, Onion flakes, Salt, Margarine, Bread crumbs; seasoned, Water, Mushroom caps; fresh, 
From:    Barbara Schmidt <smgin2@SPRYNET.COM>    Date:    Thu, 18 Jul 1996 10:27:04 -0700  Recipe By: bjs    Wash mushrooms and remove stems. Arrange caps, hollow side up in shallow  glass baking dish, set aside.  In small mixing bowl, combine margarine and  almond slivers. Cook uncovered on MEDIUM for 2 minutes or until golden  brown. Add onion, salt, bread crumbs, water, and lemon juice to almond  mixture. Mix well. Spoon into mushrooms. Cook, covered with wax paper, 2  minutes on HIGH or until hot. Serves 6 to 8.
