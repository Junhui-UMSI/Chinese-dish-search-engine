Beef Fondue and Mustard Sauce
Worcestershire Sauce, Beef Filet, MUSTARD SAUCE, Dijon mustard, Butter, Olive oil, Butter, 
Twenty minutes before serving, heat butter & oil in saucepan. When mixture  bubbles, pour into a 2-quart fondue pan. (Oil should not be more than  2-inches deep.) When oil begins to bubble, spear meat with fondue fork &  lower into hot oil. Fry for 2-3 minutes to taste. Dip in sauce. Yields 6  servings. Mustard Sauce: Combine mustard, butter & Worcestershire in  saucepan. Stir until butter melts. Serve hot, but do not allow to boil.  Yields 1-1/4 cups.
