Autumn Pear Mini Muffins -Disney
All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Egg, pear slices, Salt, Buttermilk, Vegetable oil, Brown sugar, Allspice, 
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Line the cups of a mini muffin tin with  paper liners. In a medium-size bowl, sift the flour, baking soda, cinnamon,  allspice and salt.    2. In a large bowl, blend the buttermilk, oil, egg and brown sugar. Add the  flour mixture to the wet ingredients, stirring until combined. Fold in the  pears. Fill the cups 2/3 of the way up. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes, or until  golden. Makes 24 muffins.    Recipe from FamilyFun Magazine, a Disney company. Check  http://family.disney.com for more recipes. see also www.kraftfoods.com for  great recipe and food ideas.
