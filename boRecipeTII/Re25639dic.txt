Avocado-Shrimp Aspic
Pimiento, minced, Gelatin, Lemon juice, Tabasco, Shrimp, cooked and shelled, Or 2 teaspoon, Boiling water, Cold water, Sieved avocado, Salt, 
Soften gelatin in cold water, add boiling water and stir to dissolve.  Add avocado, seasonings and pimiento.  Chill until mixture begins to  thicken, then add shrimps.  Turn into cold wet mold, chill until  firm, and unmold on lettuce.    From "Watkins Salad Book" by Elaine Allen, The J.R.  Watkins Company,  1946.    Posted by Stephen Ceideberg; May 6 1993.
