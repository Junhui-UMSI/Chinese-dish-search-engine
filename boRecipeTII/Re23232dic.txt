Tuna Macaroni Bake
Dry Milk; Instant, Nonfat, Dry Milk; Instant, Nonfat, Salt, Pimento; Chopped, EASY MUSHROOM SAUCE, Sliced Mushrooms; Undrained, Cream Of Mushroom Soup; *, Tuna; Drained, Flaked, 1 Cn, Water, Macaroni; Cooked, Drained, Onion, Eggs; Lg, Cheddar; Md, Shredded, Pepper, 
*  The can of soup should be condensed Cream Of Mushroom Soup.  +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++ ++++++++++++++++++    Beat the eggs, nonfat dry milk, water, salt and pepper together, blending  well.  Stir in the macaroni, tuna, cheddar cheese, onion, and pimento. Pour  into a buttered 1 1/2-quart shallow baking dish. Bake in a preheated 350  degree F. oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a knife inserted near the  center comes out clean. Serve hot with hot Mushroom Sauce.    EASY MUSHROOM SAUCE:    Drain the can of mushrooms, reserving the liquid.  Add enough water to the  liquid to make 1/3 cup and stir the liquid into the nonfat dry milk. Stir  in the mushroom soup and heat on medium heat until hot. Serve over Tuna  Macaroni Bake.
