Waltys Dressing
Garlic, Fresh pepper, to taste, olive oil, Red wine vinegar, Fresh thyme, minced, Salt, Chili sauce, 
*Heaping teaspoons.    Mash garlic and salt to a paste with a mortar and pestle.  Combine  with other ingredients.  Let mixture stand a few hours or overnight  in the refrigerator to blend flavors.  Stir before using.    Ted Waltersdorf "The Kitchen Table: Where Herbs and Spices Make a  Difference" in "The Herb Companion."  October/November, 1992. Posted  by Cathy Harned.
