Baked Rice Pudding
Sugar, Rice, milk, Butter or marg., Strip of lemon peel, 
Wash rice and drain well. Put into 1 1/2 pint buttered heatproof dish and  stir in milk. Leave rice to soak and soften for 30 mins.Add sugar and lemon  peel and stir well. Sprinkle top with grated nutmeg. Dot with butter. Put  into center of oven (300 degrees) and bake 2 hours. Stir in skin 2 or 3  times during first hour of cooking to increase creaminess.
