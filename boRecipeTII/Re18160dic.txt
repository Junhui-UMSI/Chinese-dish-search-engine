Velvet Waffles
Baking Powder, Wesson oil, milk, Eggs, Flour, Salt, 
Sift dry ingredients together. Beat egg yolks until light, stir milk into  egg yolks, then add oil. Gradually stir this mixture into the dry  ingredients and beat until smooth. Beat egg whites until stiff, but not  dry, and fold them into flour mixture. Bake on a hot waffle iron. Yield: 6  waffles.    CAROL VICK GROSS    (MRS.
