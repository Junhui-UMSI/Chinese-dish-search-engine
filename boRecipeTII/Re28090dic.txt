Baked Clams with Black Bean Sauce
Fermented black beans;, Clams, Garlic; minced, Red pepper flakes, Ginger, fresh; finely grated, Sesame oil, Wine, white, Scallion; chopped white, Soy sauce, 
Shuck the clams and place them on a baking sheet. Set aside.    Combine the remaining ingredients in a small saucepan and bring to a boil  over medium heat.    Spoon the mixture on top of the mollusks.  Bake for 5 to 7 minutes at 450F,  or until cooked but not tough. Serve immediately.
