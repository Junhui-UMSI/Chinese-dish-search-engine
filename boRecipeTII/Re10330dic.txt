Quick Brunch Bake
Cheddar cheese, Eggs, Salt, Onions, Bisquick baking mix, milk, Pork sausage, hash browns, frozen, 
Heat oven 400? Spray 8 inch baking dish. Cook sausage and onion until  sausage is fully cooked. drain. Mix sausage, potatoes and cheese in baking  dish. Stir remaining ingredients in medium bowl, using fork, until blended,  pour into baking dish. Bake about 40 minutes or until knife inserted in  center comes out clean.    NOTES : You can add a can of chopped chilies, pimentos, pecan pieces , ect.  Really a good breakfast or brunch dish.
