Prize Coffee Cake
Sugar, Salt, Skim milk, Egg, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Flour, Baking Powder, Margarine, 
Mix together thoroughly sugar, margarine, egg.  Add flour, baking powder  and salt. Add milk. Stir together and pour into 9-inch square pan.  Make  topping by combining  brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle over cake batter.  Bake at 375 degrees F for 25 to 35 minutes.
