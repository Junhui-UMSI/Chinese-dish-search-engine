Brownies
Butter, Unsweetened Chocolate, Granulated white sugar, Egg, White wheat flour, Salt, Pecans, Vanilla, Powdered sugar; optional, 
Heat the oven to 300 xF. Grease a square 8- inch pan and sprinkle with  flour. (You can also line the pan with greased and floured wax paper.) Melt  the butter and chocolate together on low in a stovetop pan. Blend them by  stirring well. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in this order: the  sugar, salt, egg, vanilla, flour and the nuts. Pour and spread the mixture  into the baking dish and bake for 30 minutes. Cool, dust with powdered  sugar and cut into squares.    Recipe by Mangia!
