Szechaun Shrimp
Dry sherry, Salt, Soy sauce, Shelled, and deveined, Minced fresh ginger, Peanut oil, Catshup, Garlic, finely minced, Chilli Sauce, Minced green onion, Red pepper flakes, Extra-large raw shrimp, Sugar, 
Directions; Heat oil in wock or large skillet. Add shrimp green onion, and  garlic. Stir fry till shrimp are pink.  Add sherry, soy sauce, sugar, and  salt. Stir well and blend in catsup, chilli sauce, and red pepper flakes.  Serve with a bed of hot rice.  source unknown submitted by marina    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #258      Date: Fri, 20 Sep 1996 14:13:41 -0700 (MST)      From: jmchee@goodnet.com (Marina Cheesman)
