Pineapple-Papaya Salsa
Diced peeled papaya, Salt, Chopped green onion, Jalapeno chili, minced, Fresh Cilantro, Tomatoes, L 1/2 tablespoons lemon, Diced peeled pineapple, 
You could serve this with grilled fish.    MAKES ABOUT 3 CUPS    Blanch tomatoes in saucepan of boiling water 2 O seconds. Transfer to bowl  of cold water using slotted spoon. Drain. Peel off skin using small sharp  knife. Seed and dice tomatoes. Transfer to large bowl. Add all remaining  ingredients and toss gently. Let salsa stand 3 hours before serving.
