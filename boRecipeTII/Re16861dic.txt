All-In-One Chicken
Dry white wine; vermouth,, Bay leaf; broken in half, Potatoes, Onion, Boneless skinless chicken, Carrots, Frozen green beans, 
Preheat oven to 350 F. Wash the potatoes but do not peel. Cut in quarters.  Wash carrots and cut into 2 inch chunks. Peel onion and cut into wedges. In  an ungreased 8x12" or 9x13" baking dish, arrange the chicken, potatoes,  carrots and onion. Pour the wine or other liquid over the chicken. Add the  bay leaf. Cover baking dish with foil. Bake the chicken for 1 hour. Uncover  the chicken and add the green beans. Continue baking for 10-15 minutes or  until the chicken is thoroughly cooked and vegetables are tender. Makes 4  servings.
