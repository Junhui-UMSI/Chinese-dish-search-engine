A Slightly Different Sauce Bolognese
(small) marjoram and oregano, Pepper and garlic powder, Medium-sized onion, Tomato ketchup, Corned beef, Each dried herbs, or, Good grade olive oil, 
submitted by: paradise@math.tau.ac.il  Empty corned beef and tomato ketchup into bowl and stir thoroughly or mix  with electric mixer. Do not mince or blend!  Grate onion on coarse grater  or chop finely, fry in oil till transparent. Add beef, ketchup, herbs,  pepper and garlic, stir, cover and simmer over low flame for 10 mins. Pour  into glass jars, cover tightly and when cool, put in refrigerator. Keeps 2  months in unopened jars, 1 week in opened jars. Fine over spaghetti, rice  or what have you.
