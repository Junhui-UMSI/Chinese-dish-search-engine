Banana Beer (Aka Jungle Juice)
bananas, juice from 5 lemons, juice from 5 oranges, Sugar, Water, 
Mix together and freeze.  Fill large glass 1/3 full (or more) with frozen  mixture and add 7-Up, Sprite, Gingerale, etc.    Contributed by Lawrence & Cindy Kellie    Both Cindy and Lawrences family has served this for years during the  holiday season. From: Lawrence Kellie
