Avocado Bread
Salt, ripe avocados, All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Eggs, Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Baking Powder, 9- x 5-inch loaf pan, Sugar, Cinnamon, Walnuts, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease the loaf pan and set aside.    Mash the avocados and mix with the eggs, oil, and vanilla. Blend well. Add  the flour, sugar, cinnamon, salt, baking soda, and baking powder. Fold in  the nuts. Bake for 1-1/2 hours. Cover the top loosely with foil after the  first 30 minutes.    * Source: Keys Cuisine - by Linda Gassenheimer * Typed for you by Karen  Mintzias Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.40 ---
