Tomates a la Provencale
Garlic, Handful fresh parsley, Fresh bread crumbs, Salt, Black Pepper, Firm ripe tomatoes,, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Cored and halved crosswise, 
Arrange the tomatoes, cut side up, in a baking dish. Do not seed or drain.  Season generously with salt and pepper. Slice the garlic into thin chips  and sprinkle over the tomatoes. Combine the breadcrumbs and parsley and  scatter over the tomatoes. Drizzle on the olive oil.    Bake, uncovered, at 400#161#F, until the tomatoes are soft, browned and  sizzling, about 1 hour.
