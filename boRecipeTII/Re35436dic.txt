Beef Gravy W/homemade Beef Gravy Mix
Beef gravy mix, Cold water, 
Pour water into a small saucepan.  Use a whisk to stir beef gravy mix into  water.  Stir constantly over medium heat until gravy is smooth and slightly  thickened.  This will take about 2-3 minutes.  Makes about 1 c .
