Tin Roof Pie
Chocolate syrup, heated, Unsalted peanuts, chopped, Light corn syrup, Vanilla ice cream, softened, Cornflakes, crushed, Peanut butter, 
In a 9" pie plate, combine peanut butter and corn syrup. Add cereal and mix  until well combined. Press into bottom and sides of plate. Chill. Spoon  softened ice cream into shell. Sprinkle with chopped nuts. Freeze. Remove  from freezer 1 hour before serving. Serve with warm chocolate syrup. Serves  6 - 8    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #267 by Marge McInnes  <mcinnes@islandnet.com> on Sun, 29 Sep 96.
