Bean Burger
Kidney beans, Chili powder, Catsup, Ground beef, Onion, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic powder, Salt and pepper, 
Brown ground beef and onion.  Add catsup and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer  for 20 minutes.  Add kidney beans, garlic powder, chili powder and salt and  pepper to taste.  Simmer for 30 minutes.  Serve on toasted bun and tip with  cheese. Randy Rigg
