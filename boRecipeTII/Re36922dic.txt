Strawberry Spread
Orange juice, Packets Equal, Strawberries, Coriander, Orange Peel Slivers, Unflavored gelatin, Mashed OR Pureed Fresh, 
In Small Saucepan, Sprinkle Gelatin Over Orange Juice.  Let Stand 1  Min. Heat Over Low Heat Until Gelatin Is Dissolved & Mixture Comes To  A Boil. Remove From Heat And Stir Into Strawberries.  Add Remaining  Ingredients And Stir To Blend.  Refrigerate Until Firm, 3 To 4 Hours.  Best When Used Within 1 Week. (Makes 1 Cup OR 16 Servings, 1 T. Each.)
