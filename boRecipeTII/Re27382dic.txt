Toasted Marshmallow Cake
Nuts, Marshmallows, Brown Sugar, Devils food cake mix, 
Heat oven to 350~. Prepare cake mix as directed on package. Pour batter  into greased and floured baking pan, 13x9". Cut marshmallows in half  crosswise and arrange in rows over batter in pan. Mix brown sugar and nuts;  sprinkle over top. Bake 40-45 minutes. (If marshmallows become too brown,  cover cake with aluminum foil.)
