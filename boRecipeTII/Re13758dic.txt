Wild Rice Waldorf Salad
Seedless grapes, Chopped dry roasted peanuts, Mayonnaise, Celery, Plain lowfat yogurt, Wild rice, cooked, Apple, chopped, 
Combine all ingredients except nuts in a bowl.  Toss to mix.  Cover  and chill thoroughly, about 3 to 4 hours.  Sprinkle the nuts on top  just before serving.    1/4 recipe - 157 calories, 1 bread, 1/2 fruit, 1 fat exchange 20 grams  carbohydrate, 3 grams protein, 7 grams fat 138 mg sodium, 193 mg  potassium, 4 mg cholesterol    Source:  Am. Diabetes Assoc. Holiday Cookbook by Betty Waldman, 1986  Shared but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier, Nov 93    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/diabetic.zip
