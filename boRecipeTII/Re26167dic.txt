Toll House Pound Cake
Baking soda, Sour cream, All-purpose flour, Sugar, Vanilla extract, (12-oz) Nestle Toll House, Butter, Salt, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In medium bowl, combine flour, salt and baking  soda; set aside. In large bowl, combine sugar and butter; beat well. Add  eggs, 1 at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in sour cream and  vanilla extract. Gradually blend in flour mixture. Stir in Nestle Toll  House semi-sweet chocolate morsels. Pour into greased and floured 10-inch  tube pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 70-75 minutes. Cool 15 minutes on wire  rack; remove from pan. Cool completely on wire rack. Makes one tube cake.
