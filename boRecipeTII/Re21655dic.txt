Apple Spiced Chicken
Apple jelly, Lemon juice, Allspice, Vegetable oil, Chicken, 
Brush chicken pieces with oil; season with salt & pepper. Place in  broiler pan, skin side down. Broil 5-6" from heat for 20 minutes or  until lightly browned. Turn; broil 15-20 minutes or until done.  Meanwhile, melt jelly; stir in juice & spice. Brush chicken with 1/2  of the glaze; broil 1-2 minutes more. Remove to platter; brush with  remaining glaze. ** Serve with Honeyed Apple Rings.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
