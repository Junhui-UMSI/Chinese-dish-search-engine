Pepper Steak in a Crock Pot
Black pepper, Bell pepper, Beef bouillon, Onion, Pepper steaks, mushroom soup, Flour, Gravy master, Cooking oil, Fresh mushrooms, 
Roll pepper steaks in flour and pepper. Fry in cooking oil over medium heat  until lightly browned. Place in crockpot, pour soup over meat. Add the  Gravy Master and bouillon cubes. Arrange vegetables on top. Sprinkle with  more pepper, cook on low for 6 to 8 hours or until meat is tender.
