Raspberry Brandy Cordial
***  NON   NE *****, 
For Raspberry Brandy Cordial, substitute 2 3/4 cups brandy for the vodka in  Raspberry Vodka Cordial and use only 1 pint red raspberries. Or try our  favorite raspberry liqueur, derived by mixing 2 parts Raspberry Vodka  Cordial with 1 part Raspberry Brandy Cordial.
