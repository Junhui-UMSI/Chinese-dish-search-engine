Peanut Butter Crunch
Crunchy peanut butter, Crisp rice cereal, Light corn syrup, Granulated sugar, 
In a 2 quart microwavable container, stir together syrup and sugar;  cook on High 100% power about 1 1/2 to 2 minutes, until sugar is  dissolved. Stir in peanut butter until well blended.  Mix in cereal.  Pour into an 8 x 12 inch butter pan.  When mixture is cool, cut into  squares.
