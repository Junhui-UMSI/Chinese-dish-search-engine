Pumpkin Cookies 3
Nuts; chopped, Salt, Sugar, Allspice, Pumpkin; cooked, Egg, Flour; sifted, Cinnamon, Vanilla, Shortening, Raisins, Baking Powder, Baking soda, 
A spicy cookie with a mealy, rather unusual texture. Preheat oven to 375 F.  Cream together shortening and sugar.  Add and mix well the pumpkin, egg,  and vanilla.  Sift together and add to the above mixture flour, baking  powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and allspice.  Stir in raisins and  nuts.  Drop cookies onto a well-greased cookie sheet and bake about 15  minutes.
