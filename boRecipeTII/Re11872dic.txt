Ready Whenever Bran Muffins
Sugar, Flour, Buttermilk, All bran, Boiling water, Eggs, Plus, Baking soda, Shortening, Salt, 
Pour water over bran. Let stand 15 minutes. Sift flour. Add soda and salt.  Cream shortening. Blend in sugar and eggs, beating until fluffy. Stir in  bran. Add flour and buttermilk alternately. Store batter in covered  container in refrigerator, baking as many muffins at a time as desired. To  cook, fill well-greased muffin tin about 2/3 full. Bake at 400 for 20-25  minutes. Makes 3 dozen.    MRS IRENE SMITH    From the book <High Cotton Cookin>, Marvell Academy Mothers Assn, Marvell,  AR  72366, ISBN 0-918544-14-9, downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
