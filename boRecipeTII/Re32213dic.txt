Barbecued Shrimp #3
Lots of black & red pepper, Several cloves of garlic, Salt, Lemons; cut in half, Raw shrimp (with shell), Thyme, Sticks butter; melted, olive oil, Season all, 
Spread shrimp in shallow baking pan (about 1-1/2 inches deep); pour  melted butter and olive oil over shrimp. Squeeze lemons over shrimp and put  peels in the shrimp. Add other ingredients. Broil for 5 minutes on each  side.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
