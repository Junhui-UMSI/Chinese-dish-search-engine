Three-Bean Salad
Cooked green beans; drained, Cooked AM Red Kidney Beans, drained, ADD, Cooked AM Whole Grain Wheat, Wine vinegar or lemon juice, -wine vinegar or lemon juice, Cooked AM Garbanzo Beans, drained, Green bell pepper; chopped, Red onion; sliced thin, Raw honey, Cooked AM Medium Brown Rice, Chopped pimiento, Lentil sprouts, Celery, Unrefined vegetable oil,
