Avocado Torey
Red wine, olive oil, White pepper, Salt, Avocados, Romaine lettuce leaves, Cl Garlic; minced, Fresh thyme; (optional), Dried Oregano, Vinegar, 
Recipe by: "Le Ruths" Restaurant, New Orleans, LA. Cut the avocados in  half and discard the pit.  Scoop out the avocado meat with an ice-cream  dipper, and shape into little balls. Place them on two plates lined with  lettuce leaves.  In a small bowl, whisk together the rest of the  ingredients, pour over the avocados, and serve.
