Quakers- Garden-Fresh Turkey Burgers
Onion, hamburger buns, whole wheat, QUAKER Oats uncooked, red ?, Ketchup, Ground turkey breast meat, sour cream, zucchini, Lettuce, Garlic, Salt, Tomato, 
Lightly spray rack of broiler pan with no-stick cooking spray or oil  lightly.  Combine first 8 ingredients; mix well. Shape into six burgers.  Place on rack of prepared pan or over medium-hot coals on outdoor grill 6  inches from heat.  Broil 7 to 9 minutes on each side or until golden brown.  Serve on buns with rounded tablespoon sour cream per serving. Garnish with  lettuce and sliced tomato, if desired.
