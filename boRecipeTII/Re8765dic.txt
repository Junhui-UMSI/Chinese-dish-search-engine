Power Bars
Whole wheat flour, Unsalted sunflower seeds, Eggs, Raisins, Skim milk powder, Vegetable oil, Baking Powder, Peanut butter, Brown sugar, Wheat germ, Molasses, 
In bowl, combine flour, sugar, skim milk powder, wheat germ and baking  powder; stir in raisins and sunflower seeds. Combine eggs, oil,molasses and  peanut butter. Add to dry ingredients, blending well. Spread in greased 9  inch square cake pan. Bake in 350 F oven for 35 minutes or until browned  and firm to the touch. Let cool completely. Cut into24 bars. Bars can be  stored in air tight container for up to 5 days.    Origin: Canadian Living, June 1992. Shared by: Sharon Stevens    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
