Almost Grandmas Sausage and Peppers
Onion, Red peppers, Salt, Hot Italian sausages, Green pepper, Tomato paste, Garlic, White pepper, Fennel seeds, Dry white wine, olive oil, 
HEAT THE OIL in a casserole or large skillet and cook the sausage for 2  minutes on each side. Pour off most of the fat, add the onions, garlic,  peppers, tomato paste, wine, fennel seeds, salt and pepper. Cover and cook  over low heat for 25 minutes.
