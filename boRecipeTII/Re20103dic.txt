Vanilla Ice Cream #1
Light cream, Salt, Eggs, Sugar, Vanilla, milk, 
Beat eggs until light. Add sugar gradually, beating until mixture thickens.  Add remaining ingredients. Mix thoroughly & freeze in ice cream freezer.  Makes 1 gallon.
