Pork and Vegetable Alfredo
Glazed Pork Roast (about 3, Salt, Chopped broccoli, Mushrooms, Vegetable oil, Hot cooked fresh fettuccine, Bottled light alfredo sauce, Onions, Thinly sliced Apricot-, Ounces), (about 4 oz uncooked pasta, Pepper, 
Heat oil in large nonstick frypan on med-high heat. Add mushrooms, broccoli  and onion; saute 5 minutes or until tender. Add fettuccine, pork, salt and  pepper saute 2 minutes. Stir in alfredo sauce, cook 1 minute or until  thoroughly heated. Makes 2 (l 1/2 cup) servings.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #257 by "Deborah K?hnen"  <DEBKUHNEN@classic.msn.com> on Sep 27, 97
