Pollys Fiesta Quiche
Onion, minced, Cayenne, Jack cheese, shredded, Unbaked pie crust, Canned chiles, diced, Nutmeg, Whipping cream, Tomatoes, minced, Cheddar cheese, shredded, Eggs, to 6, Salt, 
* Pollys, Huntington Beach! Combine eggs, salt, nutmeg, cayenne, and  cream. Whip until smooth and light in consistency. Spread onions, tomatoes,  and chiles on bottom of pie crust. Pour egg mixture over filling. Sprinkle  with cheeses. Bake 375 F, 60-70 mins or until toothpick inserted near  center comes out clean. Allow to cool 10-15 mins before cutting.
