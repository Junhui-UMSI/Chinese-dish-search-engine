Banana Cream Pie with Chocolate
Coffee-flavored liqueur;, FOR THE TOPPING, Sugar, Fresh nutmeg; grated, Bittersweet chocolate;, Light cream, Vanilla extract, Orange juice, 9-inch pie shell; unbaked, FOR THE CUSTARD, Bananas; ripe, Vanilla extract, Eggs, Heavy cream, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Put the pie shell on a cookie sheet and  scatter the chocolate in the shell. To make the custard, mix together the  eggs, sugar, cream, and vanilla. Pour into the pastry shell and carefully  put it in the oven. Bake until the custard is nearly set in the middle,  about 40 minutes. Let the custard cool, then cover and chill.    Right before serving, peel and slice the bananas, and toss them with the  orange juice and coffee liqueur. Spread the slices on the custard. Whip the  heavy cream, sweeten it with confectioners sugar, and flavor with vanilla.  Spread the cream over the bananas, dust with nutmeg, and chill until  serving time. Dont let the completed pie wait long.    Recipe by: A Feast Of Fruits    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1047 by "Marie Smith" <craftee@sprynet.com>  on Jan 27, 1998
