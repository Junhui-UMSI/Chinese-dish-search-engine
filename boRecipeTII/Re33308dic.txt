Basic Spinach Pasta Dough
Eggs, Frozen spinach (10 oz pkg), All-purpose flour, 
Recipe by: Northern Italian Cooking Cook spinach according to package  instructions.  Drain thoroughly. Squeeze spinach to remove as much moisture  as possible.  Chop spinach very fine.  Put flour on a pastry board and make  a well in the center. Break eggs into well; beat with a fork. Add chopped  spinach and beat to combine. Continue as for Basic Egg Pasta Dough.
