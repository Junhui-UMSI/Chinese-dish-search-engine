Stuffed Peppers/jambalaya
Garlic; minced, Chopped parsley, Crushed tomatoes; #2-1/2, Bread crumbs, Cooked crawfish tails, Rice, Bell peppers; halved, Worcestershire Sauce, Vegetable oil, Smoked sausage, olive oil, Tabasco, Salt and pepper, Ribs celery; diced, Bell peppers; diced, File gumbo powder, Tomato sauce; 8oz, Bay Leaves, Onions; diced, Green onions; sliced, Fresh crab meat, 
Heat oil. Add sausage, veggies and bay leaf. Cook 5 minutes. Add file,  green onions, parsley and tomatoes. Simmer 20 minutes. Add remaining  ingredients and mix well. Put mix into bell peppers. Top with crumbs.  Drizzle oil on top. Bake at 400~ for 20 to 25 minutes. Can be served with a  tomato sauce on top. (NOTE-This seems like it would make a lot more  stuffing than for 3 peppers)
