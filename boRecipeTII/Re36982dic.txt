Strawberry-Cantaloupe Soup
banana, Cantaloupe, Unsweetened pineapple juice, Non-fat vanilla yogurt, Fresh strawberries, 
Place all ingredients except yogurt in a blender or the work bowl of a food  processor fitted with a metal blade. Cover and process until smooth. Add  yogurt. Cover and process until well combined. Serve immediately or cover  and chill until serving time.    NOTES : Raspberries can be used in place of strawberries.    Also consider whipping up this sweet treat any time you want to relax and  refuel with a fruity drink packed full of nutrients.
