Crockpot Chicken Cacciatore
Onions, Chicken, Garlic, Tomatoes, Tomato sauce, Salt, Pepper, Dried oregano, Dried basil, Bay leaf, Dry white wine, Spaghetti, 
Place onions in slow cooker.  On top of onions place chicken pieces,  garlic, tomatoes, tomato sauce, salt, pepper, herbs and wine. Cover pot.  Cook on low 6 to 8 hours, or high 3 to 4 hours. Serve chicken with its  sauce on spaghetti which you have prepared. Leisure Living Guide to Slow  Cooking.
