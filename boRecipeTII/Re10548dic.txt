Quick Macaroni and Cheese
bread crumbs, Tuna , Tomatoes, Macroni/cheese , 
Combine tuna and macaroni and cheese and pour into a greased casserole. Top  with buttered bread crumbs and bake at 350F for 30 minutes or unitl  bubbling.  During the last 5 minutes of baking time, arrange the tomatoes  in pinwheel fashion around the top and continue baking.    From:  The Tuna Cookbook
