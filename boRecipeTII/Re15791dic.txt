About Couscous (3 Variations)
SEE DIRECTIONS ON PACKAGE, 
LIME: Add 1 tablespoon lime juice and 1/2 teaspoon grated lime peel to  water when preparing couscous.    MOROCCAN: Add 1/4 cup golden raisins, 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/4  teaspoon ground turmeric, and 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin to water when  preparing couscous.    SUN-DRIED TOMATO & GREEN-ONION: Add 1 medium green onion, sliced, and 5  sun-dried tomato halves, chopped, to water when preparing couscous.
