Pork, Eggplant, and Tomato
Canned tomatoes, Salt, Soya sauce, Vegetable oil, Eggplant, sliced, Pork, shredded, Onion, sliced, Sugar, Eggplant, sliced, 
1. Prepare ingredients. Marinate pork in salt and soya sauce for 5  minutes.    2. Heat oil up in wok to smoking point. Add pork and onions. Stir fry  pork until browned, about 5 minutes. Add eggplant, tomatoes, and  sugar. Cover wok. Cook ingredients for 15 minutes. Lift cover  occasionally to stir ingredients.    NOTE: This could be made into a purely vegetarian dish by omitting the  pork. Stir fry the eggplant and onions first, then add the tomatoes.
