Banana & Bran Muffins
Orange rind, TOPPING, cinnamon &, Golden syrup, Eggs, WET MIX, DRY MIX, Flaky bran, Milk, Baking soda, Margarine, Sugar, Mashed banana, Self-raising flour, Nutmeg, 
Preheat oven to 400F & grease muffin pans. Beat well, all of the wet mix  ingredients & gently fold in the previously combined dry ingredients. Place  in the muffin pans, sprinkle with topping and bake for 20-25 mins. Makes  12-14.     Variation: 1/2 cup mixed dried fruits & nuts may be added to the wet mix  as desired.
