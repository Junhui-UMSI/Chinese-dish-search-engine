Peppery Pecan Pie
Pecans; coarsely chopped, Prepared pie crust for one, Dark brown sugar, Whipped Cream; optional, Dark corn syrup, Eggs, Butter or Margarine, TABASCO? pepper sauce, 
Preheat oven to 425?:F. Place pie crust in 9-inch pie plate; make  decorative edge. In large bowl, beat eggs slightly. Add dark corn syrup,  dark brown sugar, melted butter and TABASCO? pepper sauce. Place pecans in  prepared pie crust; pour filling over pecans. Bake pie 15 minutes.    Reduce oven to 350F. Bake pie 40 minutes longer or until knife inserted 1  inch from edge comes out clean. Cool pie on wire rack. Serve with whipped  cream if desired.
