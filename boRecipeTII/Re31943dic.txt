Taco Bell Meat Seasoning
Seasoned salt, Onion salt, Sugar, Dry mustard, Ground tumeric, Coriander; ground, Instant tea powder, Cilantro leaves, Dry parsley, COMBINE WITH, Black pepper, Cumin, Chili powder, Garlic salt, Paprika, 
Combine first group of ingredients and sift together 3 times. Funnel into  bottle with tight fitting cap. Keep out of direct sunlight and use within 6  mos, or refrigerate to use in one year. Makes 3T of seasoning.    For taco seasoning, use 2T of last group of ingredients <mixed together>  added to the 3T of above seasoning mixture for 1-1 1/2 pounds ground beef.
