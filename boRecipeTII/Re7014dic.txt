Popcorn--Cock-Trail Mix
Popped), Unpopped corn (8 cups, Butter, Raisins, 
Pop popcorn.  Melt butter.  Drizzle butter over unseasoned popcorn and toss  until well blended.  Add the raisins and toss until well blended.
