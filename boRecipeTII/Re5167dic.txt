Pig Lickin Cake
Box Yellow Cake Mix, Butter, Crushed pineapple in juice, CAKE, Cool Whip?; thawed, FROSTING, Box instant vanilla pudding, Mandarin oranges in juice, 
Mix cake ingredients and pour into a greased and floured 9x13 pan. Bake at  350F for 30 minutes. Cool completely and frost. Keep refridgerated.    Per serving: 191 Calories; 9g Fat (40% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 28g  Carbohydrate; 11mg Cholesterol; 245mg Sodium    Recipe by: Eat-L Posted to EAT-L Digest by Betsy Burtis  <ebburtis@IX.NETCOM.COM> on Sep 3, 1997
