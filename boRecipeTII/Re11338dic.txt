Ranch Style Dressing Mix
Salt, Dried minced garlic, Instant minced onion, Black pepper, Sugar, Paprika, Parsley flakes, 
Combine spices. Store tightly covered.       Uses: Sprinkle directly on salads to season,      Or: Add 1 Tablespoon mix to 1 cup mayonnaise and 1 cup buttermilk. Mix  with spoon. Refrigerate.      Or: Add 1 Tablespoon mix to 8 ounces of plain yogurt and use as dip with  fresh vegetables.      Or: Combine 2 Tablespoons water with 1/4 cup vinegar. Stir in 2 teaspoons  mix, then 2/3 cup salad oil. Shake well.       Yield: 1/2 cup mix.
