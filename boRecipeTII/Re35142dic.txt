Bechamel Sauce
Half-and-half, White pepper, Grated onion, All-purpose flour, Dash ground thyme, Butter or margarine, Chicken broth, Salt, 
1. Melt butter in a 1-quart glass measuring pitcher in microwave oven  (about 30 seconds at High).  Add onion and flour; mix well. Add broth and  half-and-half gradually, stirring constantly. Cook uncovered 5 minutes at  High, or until thickened; stir after 2 minutes, then every 30 seconds.    2. Remove from oven; stir in seasonings.    Yield: About 1 cup.    SOURCE: Adventures in Microwave Cooking.
