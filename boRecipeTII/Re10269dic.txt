Quick Apple Muffins
Cinnamon., Butter, Light brown sugar, 
In a small mixing bowl cream together:    Split 6 english muffins, toast, then spread each muffin half with 1 Tbl of  the butter-sugar mixture. Mix remaining butter-sugar mixture with a 20 oz  can drained, sliced pie apples and distribute over muffins. Broil 4 min to  heat apples and melt butter mixture.
