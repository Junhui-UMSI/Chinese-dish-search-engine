Walnut Rounds
All purpose flour, Salt for the tops, optional, Potato flour, Walnuts, finely chopped &, Iced water, Margarine, softened, Salt, toasted, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Mix flours & salt together in a large bowl. Cut in  the margarine until the mixture resembles coarse breadcrumbs. Add walnuts &  stir until they are evenly distributed in the flour. Blend in enough water  to form a cohesive ball. Add more water if necessary. The dough should be a  little sticky. Wrap dough in waxed paper & chill for at least 1/2 hour.  Divide into 2 equal portions.  Roll out to 1/8" thickness. If desired,  sprinkle tops with salt & roll over with rolling pin. With a round cookie  cutter, cut into 2" rounds. Place onto a lightly greased cookie sheet &  prick with a fork. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool  on a rack. Serve with spreads.
