Persimmon Cookie
Salt, Cloves, Sugar, Cinnamon, Rasins, Baking soda, Walnuts, Soft margarine, Whole egg, Nutmeg, Mashed persimmon, Flour, 
add soda to mashed persimmons, and set aside. cream  margarine,sugar,egg. mix flour and spices to creamed margarine and  add nuts and raisins. add thickened persimmon mix well. drop on to  cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees 15min.
