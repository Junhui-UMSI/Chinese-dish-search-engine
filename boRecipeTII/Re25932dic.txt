Bacon and Pepper Sauce
Cloves garlic chopped, olive oil, Plum tomatoes chopped, Red pepper diced, Bacon, Green pepper diced, Butter, Hot pepper halved and seeded, salt and pepper, White wine, Onion chopped, 
Sautee bacon until almost crisp. Drain. Add butter and oil. Sautee onion  and garlic.Add peppers and sautee 5 min. Add tomatoes and wine, cook 15  min. REMOVE HOT PEPPER. Season with salt and pepper and serve over pasta.
