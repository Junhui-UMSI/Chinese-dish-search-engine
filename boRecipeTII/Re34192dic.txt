Bay Scallop Scampi
Penne pasta, Garlic; minced, Dry vermouth, Lemon juice, Unsalted butter; melted, Plus 2 tsp. olive oil, Shallots; chopped, Parsley, Bay scallops, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 5 min.    Cook pasta in a large pan of boiling water 8-10 minutes, or until al dente.  Drain and keep warm. Turn on broiler. Combine remaining ingredients, except  scallops, in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well.  Arrange scallops in a broiler proof dish. Pour butter mixture over scallops  and toss. Broil 3-4 minutes. Turn and broil 2-3 minutes. Serve over pasta.  Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 20, 1998
