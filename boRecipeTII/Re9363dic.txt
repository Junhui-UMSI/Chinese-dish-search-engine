Pulitzer Pudding
Pecans, Shredded cocoanut, Double yellow cake mix, Stick butter or margarine, Pie filling (my family, 
Pulitzer Pudding     serves 6    Place  pie  filling  in  9 x 12  baking  dish,  spread evenly. Sprinkly  cake mix (right out of the box) evenly over filling. Take pats of butter or  margarine and place evenly over cake mix. Sprinkle with chopped pecans.  Bake at 375 for approx.  30 minutes. As mixture starts to brown on top,  sprinkle  cocoanut over it and bake until cocaonut just starts to brown.  Let  cool for 20 min. and ENJOY!!
