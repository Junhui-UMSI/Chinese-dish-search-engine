Plantain Casserole
Peanut oil, Lemon juice, Tomato; peeled and choped, Green Plantains; sliced 1", Salt & Freshly Ground Black, 
Peel and slice plantains 1 inch thick. Dip in lemon juice and saute them in  oil over low heat, stirring constantly. Add tomato and seasoning and  transfer to an oiled casserole dish with a lid. Bake at 275? for 1 1/2  hours.    Source: "Vegetarian Food for All" by Annabel Perkins.
