Ancho Sauce
Dried Oregano, Ground cumin, Garlic, Boiling water, Tumeric, Dried ancho chilies, Peeled; seeded and finely, Salt; (or to taste), 
Seed and stem chilies under cold running water. Break into one-inch pieces.  Place in medium bowl. Add boiling water and soak one hour.    Strain chilies through sieve, reserving liquid. Add tomatoes, garlic, salt,  oregano, cumin and tumeric, and blend well. Pour mixture into heavy small  saucepan. Add one cup reserved soaking liquid. Bring to boil, stirring  constantly. Reduce heat and simmer until sauce thickens and coats spoon,  ten to fifteen minutes. Serve at room temperature. From Bon Appetit, Dec  85.    Makes 2 cups  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1061 by shade <liveoak@polaris.net> on Jan  29, 1998
