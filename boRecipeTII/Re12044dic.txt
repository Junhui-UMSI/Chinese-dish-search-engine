Zippy Cheese Biscuits
Plus, Self-rising flour, To (up To ), (about 2 Ounces) shredded, Cayenne pepper, Pecans, milk, Shortening, 
Christmas. Combine flour and shortening; cut in shortening with a pastry  blender until mixture resembles coarse meal. Add cheese, milk, pecans, and  red pepper, stirring until dry ingredients are moistened. Turn dough out  onto a lightly floured surface, and knead lightly 4 or 5 times.Roll dough  to 1/2-inch thickness; cut with a 2-inch biscuit cutter. Place on an  ungreased baking sheet. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes. Yield:  about 1 dozen.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #805 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Sep 25, 1997
