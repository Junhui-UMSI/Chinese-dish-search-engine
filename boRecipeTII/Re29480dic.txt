Baking Powder Biscuits
Ready-mixed flour, milk, Shortening, 
Sift flour, measure, and sift.  Cut in shortening with 2 spatulas. Add  milk.  Turn onto lightly floured board. Knead lightly and pat into sheet  1/2 inch thick. Cut with floured cutter. Bake in hot oven (450? F) 12  minutes.  16 servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941    Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.20
