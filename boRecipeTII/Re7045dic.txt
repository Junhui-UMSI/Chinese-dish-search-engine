Popover with Hot Turkey Salad
Cheddar cheese; shredded, Celery; diced, Cooked turkey; diced, All-purpose flour, Salt, Onion powder, Tomato wedges; optional, Crushed potato chips, Eggs; room temperature, Milk; room temperature, milk, Sliced ripe olives; drained, Mayonnaise or salad dressing, Pepper, 
Recipe by: TASTE OF HOME DEC/JAN 1996 In a mixing bowl, beat eggs until  lemon-colored and foamy. Add milk, flour and salt; beat just until smooth  (do not overbeat). Pour into a greased 10" glass pie plate. Bake at 400  degrees for 35-40 minutes or until deep golden brown. Immediately prick  with a fork in the center to allow steam to escape. Combine the next 8  ingredients in a saucepan; cook and stir over low until heated through.  Stir in potato chips. Spoon into popover. Garnish with tomato wedges if  desired. Serve immediately.
