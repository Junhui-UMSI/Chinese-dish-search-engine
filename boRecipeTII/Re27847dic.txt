The Wedge of Light
olive oil, Potatoes, unpeeled, scrubbed, "Cajun Spice" seasoning, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.    Cut each potato lengthwise into 4 slices.  Then cut each slice into 2  wedges.    Place potatoes in a large bowl.  Add olive oil and Cajun spice.  Toss well  to coat potatoes evely with oil and seasoning.    Transfer potatoes to a large baking sheet that has been sprayed with  on-stick spray.  Spread evenly ini a single layer.  Bake for 20 minutes.  Turn wedges over bake another 20 to 25 minutes, until golden brown on the  outside and tender on the inside.
