Toffee
Walnuts (chopped), Choc chips (semi-sweet), Butter, Graham crackers, Brown sugar (well packed), 
Place crackers on bottom of buttered 12 x 7 glass pan. Set this aside.  Combine butter and brown sugar in a 4 cup glass measure cup and micro on  Roast (Med Hi) for 2 minutes or until butter is melted. Stir in nuts and  pour this mixture over the crackers. Micro on Roast for 4 - 5 minutes or  until bubbly. Top with Choc chips and continue cooking on Roast for 1 1/2  minutes until chocolate is softened. Spread across the top. Chill 30 min.  and cut or break into eating pieces.
