Aruba Chicken
Fryer chicken,, Crushed red pepper, Cut into 1/2" slices, Green onions, sliced, Sweet potatoes,pared,, Coconut, shredded, I/2 ts thyme, Onion, sliced, Bananas,, Cloves, (1 lb)cut into, Can(8oz)tomato paste, Salt, Bay leaf, olive oil, Smoked pork butt,, 
Heat oil in a heavy Dutch oven and saute onion about 5 minutes until  golden, stirring frequently. Remove From pan. Add chicken, half at a time,  and brown on all sides (adding more oil if needed). Return onion to pan and  add pork Stir in water, tomato sauce, coconut and salt.    Tie pepper, cloves, bay leaf and thyme into a small cheesecloth square  (poultice) and add to stew. Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer For 15  minutes, stirring occasionally. Add sweet potatoes and simmer covered For  35 to 40 minutes longer, or until sweet potatoes are tender. Taste for  seasonings and correct if necessary. Add bananas and cook for an additional        5    min.  Discard cheesecloth poultice. Arrange on a serving platter and garnish with  green onions and more coconut, if desired.    Serves 6.
