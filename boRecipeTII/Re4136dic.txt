Peppery Grilled Garlic Bread
Ground pepper, unsalted butter, French bread, olive oil, garlic, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
Mix first 4 ingredients in small bowl. Season with salt. (Can be made  ahead. Cover and refrigerate 2 days or freeze 1 month. Bring to room  temperature before using.) Prepare barbecue (medium-high heat). Lightly  brush both sides of bread with oil. Grill until slightly crip and light  brown, about 2 minutes per side. Spread both sides of bread with garlic  butter.    HARTMANS@IX.NETCOM.COM    (JACK AND KAY HARTMAN)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
