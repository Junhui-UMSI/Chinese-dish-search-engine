Autumn Days Pilaf
Vegetable oil, Ground cumin, Raisins, Onions, Chicken Stock, Tomato paste, Unsalted sunflower seeds, Dried Lentils, Chicken; cooked and chopped, Uncooked brown rice, Shredded carrots, 
Heat oil in skillet until hot. Saute onions until tender. Add rice and stir  until lightly browned. Add chicken stock and next 3 ingredients; stir well.  Cover, bring to a boil, reduce heat, simmer 30 minutes. Stir in chicken,  carrots, sunflower seeds, and raisins. Cover and cook 10-15 minutes or  until all liquid is absorbed. Serves 6.
