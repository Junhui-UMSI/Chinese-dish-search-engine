Strawberries n Cream Bread
Granulated sugar, Baking Powder, Unbleached flour, Temperature, Cinnamon, Strawberries -- chopped, Egg whites -- whipped, Vanilla, Applesauce -- at room, Salt, Fat-free sour cream, Baking soda, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a loaf pan with cooking spray and flour.  In  a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and  cinnamon.  In another mixing bowl, combine applesauce, egg whites, sour  cream, vanilla and strawberries.  Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients  just until moistened.  Pour mixture into prepared pan.  Bake 60 to 65  minutes.    Recipe By     : Sharon Tyler Herbst    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
