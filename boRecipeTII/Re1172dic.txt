Creamy Chicken Divan
Spears, (10oz) frozen broccoli, Cream of chicken soup,, Undiluted, Chopped, cooked chicken, Poultry seasoning, (8oz) sour cream, (4oz) shredded cheddar, Cheese, divided, 
Place broccoli spears in a shallow 2-qt. baking dish. Cover tightly with  heavy duty plastic wrap and fold back a small corner of wrap to allow steam  to escape. Microwave at high 3 minutes. Rearrange spears; cover and  microwave 3-4 minutes. Drain and set aside.    Combine soup, chicken and poultry seasoning in a microwave safe bowl; cover  and cook 2-3 minutes. Stir in sour cream and 1/2 cup cheese. Cook an  additional 2 minutes.    Spoon chicken over broccoli and sprinkle with the rest of the cheese. Cook        1    minute.    Note:    To reduce fat and calories, substitute reduced fat soup, cheese and sour  cream.    Americas Best Recipes Carolyn Shaw 8-95.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by q591b4@ilos.net on Oct 26, 1998
