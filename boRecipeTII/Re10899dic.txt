Rabbit Curry
Chicken broth, Young rabbits, cut into, Flour, Pieces, Orange peel, grated, Onion, Curry powder, INGREDIENTS:, Sour cream, Stick of butter or, Tart apple, chopped, Margarine. melted, 
DIRECTIONS: Brush rabbit pieces with melted butter. Dredge in flour. Salt  and pepper pieces. Bake on rack, turning at least one, until rabbit is  tender. Cool and debone rabbit.    Saute onion and apple in remaining butter or margarine. Add 1/4 cup of  flour. Stirring until lumps are smoothed out. At chicken broth and sour  cream slowly, stirring constantly. Simmer over well heat until blended. Add  lemon peel, curry powder, deboned rabbit and heat thoroughly. Serve over  rice.
