Chinarcha Sharbat (Rhubarb-Pomegranate Refresher)
Rhubarb; washed; trimmed,, Water - 2 c, Sugar - 1 c or to taste, Pomegranate juice - 2 c, Strained orange juice or, 
makes 1.5 L    Yamuna Devi    Stew rhubarb with water and sugar for about 15 min. Strain juice; discard  solids. Add juices and cool. Chill well and serve over ice.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Sep 28, 98,
