Vanilla Brownies
Vanilla, Eggs, Chocolate chips, Sugar, Baking Powder, flour, Salt, Butter, Brown sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350.  In small bowl combine flour, baking powder and salt.  Set aside.  In large bowl beat butter, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla until  creamy.    Add eggs, one at a time.  Gradually beat in flour mixture.    Add chips (if desired)    Spread evenly into well greased 15 x 10 x 1 baking pan (approximate size of  pan)    Bake 350 35 - 40 min.  Cool in pan on rack.   When completely cool cut into  pieces.    ==  Courtesy of Dale & Gail Shipp, Columbia Md.  === Converted by MMCONV  vers. 1.50
