Vickis Grandmas Date Squares
dates, Butter or margarine, Sugar, Rice Krispies, 
Combine first 3 ingredients in a saucepan. Bring to the boil over low heat.  Stir for 3 minutes only while still boiling. Take off heat. Pour heated  mixture over Rice Krispies in a large bowl. Stir well.    Press firmly into a greased 12 X 12" dish or cake tin. Place in  refrigerator to set. Before completely cold mark into squares to cut.    NOTES : Can be iced with either coconut or chocolate.    Recipe by: Vicki Stavropolous, Adelaide Aus. & Jubail, KSA Posted to  MC-Recipe Digest V1 #746 by Creedenite@aol.com on Aug 17, 1997
