Tarragon Roast Chicken
Tarragon Sauce, (see sauces), Salt and pepper, to taste, Tarragon, to taste, -3 pound chicken, Clove garlic, cut in half, Hot rice for 4, Kitchen Bouquet, 
Rub cut garlic clove on all sides of chicken. Rub lightly with Kitchen  Bouquet. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and tarragon to taste. Place in open  Microwave dish, breast-side down. Microwave on High for 7 minutes. Turn  dish 1/2 turn. Microwave on High for 7 minutes longer. Let stand for 10  minutes while making Tarragon Sauce. Serve sauce over meat with rice.
