Strawberry Margarita Mousse
Whole strawberries, hulled, Tequila, Triple sec or other, Sugar, Plain nonfat yogurt, Boiling water, Unflavored gelatin, 
Place strawberries in a blender, and process until smooth. Pour into a  large bowl; stir in sugar. Cover and let stand 30 minutes, stirring  occasionally.    Combine boiling water and gelatin in a small bowl; let stand 5 minutes or  until gelatin dissolves, stirring constantly. Add the tequila and triple  sec, and stir well. Stir the gelatin mixture into the strawberry mixture.    Cover and chill for 10 minutes or until the mixture begins to thicken. Add  yogurt (at room temperature), stirring with a wire whisk until  well-blended. Divide the mixture evenly among 5 margarita glasses or large  stemmed glasses; cover and chill at least 4 hours or until set. Yield: 5  servings.    Per serving: 292 Calories; 1g Fat (2% calories from fat); 6g Protein; 60g  Carbohydrate; 2mg Cholesterol; 83mg Sodium    Recipe by: Cooking Light, May 1995, page 138    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #415 by igor@digex.net on Jan 28, 1997.
