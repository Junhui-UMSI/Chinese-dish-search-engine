Angel Food Cake Topping
(10-oz) frozen strawberries,, Sugar, Egg white, 
*(you may use red raspberries) Combine all ingredients. Beat in electric  mixer for at least 30 minutes. Makes a delicious topping for a white cake.  But 30 minutes?
