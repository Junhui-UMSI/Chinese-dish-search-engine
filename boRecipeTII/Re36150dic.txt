Beef Stew #3
Diced celery (optional), (1-lb) tomatoes, -(up to), Very lean beef, Onions; diced, Water, Vegetable oil, Garlic salt (optional), Carrots; cut into 2-inch, Or, salt and pepper, Potatoes; cut into, 
Cut beef into cubes; brown with diced onions in vegetable oil in a heavy  Dutch oven. Add about 2 cups boiling water, and simmer until meat is  tender. Add other ingredients, and cook until vegetables are tender. Yield:        6    servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
