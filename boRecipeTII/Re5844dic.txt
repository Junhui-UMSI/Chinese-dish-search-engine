Piperies Orektiko (Pepper Salad Appetizer)
Sweet peppers, Karen Mintzias, Salt and pepper to taste, Onion; coarsely chopped, olive oil, Oregano, Vinegar (more if desired), 
Bake peppers in hot 450 F oven for about 20 minutes or until wilted and  soft.  Remove seeds and outer skin.  Cut in pieces and place in a bowl. Add  onion, salt and pepper.  Mix vinegar and olive oil and add to peppers.  Sprinkle with oregano.  Adjust seasonings if necessary.   Serve as  appetizer.
