Baked Chicken Dijon
Fine fresh bread crumbs, Boneless skinless chicken, Breasts -- about 2 pounds, Pepper, Dried marjoram, Dijon mustard, Salt, Nonfat yogurt, Salt and pepper -- optional, 
Preheat oven to 350=B0F.  Lightly coat 1 or 2 large baking sheets with  cooking spray. Trim all visible fat from chicken and sprinkle lightly with  salt and pepper. Stir yogurt and mustard in a small bowl. Spread mixture on  bottom sides of chicken. Place on baking sheets, coated sides down, leaving  space between pieces. Spread mixture over top. Combine bread crumbs,  marjoram, salt and pepper. Sprinkle over chicken. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes  until golden brown and no longer pink in= center.
