Poppy Seed Chicken #2
Chicken, sour cream, Poppy seeds, Cream of chicken soup, Stack, 
From:    Julie Sterchi <sterchi@WABASH.NET>    Date:    Wed, 31 Jul 1996 22:52:05 -0700  Pick chicken of the bone and tear into small pieces.  Place in greased  casserole.  Mix soup and sour cream; pour over chicken and mix.  Sprinkle  poppy seeds over, and then cracker crumbs.  You may drizzle with some  melted butter, if desired. Bake at 350 deg. for about 25 minutes.    EAT-L Digest 31 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
