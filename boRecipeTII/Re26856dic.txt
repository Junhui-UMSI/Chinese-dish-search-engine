Baked Beans
Light molasses, Cooked brown beans; drained,, Catsup, Prepared mustard, Onions, Chopped green chilies, 
Combine all ingredients in bean pot or 1 3/4 quart casserole. Bake at 350,  covered, for 1 hour. (Can prepare in microwave on HIGH for 20 minutes.)  Yield 4-6 servings.    Recipe by: Taste of Home Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #730 by L979@aol.com  on Aug 9, 1997
