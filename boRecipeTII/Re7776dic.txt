Porterhouse Steak with Hoisin Barbecue Sauce
Honey, Hoisin sauce, Dark brown sugar, Ketchup, Ancho chile powder, Pasilla chile powder, Worcestershire sauce, Porterhouse steak, Red onion, Soy sauce, Garlic, Salt, Cayenne, Rice wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, Unsalted butter, Plum tomatoes, fresh, SAUCE, 
Season the steak with salt and pepper. Combine all sauce ingredients.    Grill the porterhouse for 5 minutes on each side while basting with the  sauce. Serve with grilled scallions and cast iron potato salad.
