Potato Salad W/chive Blossom Mayonnaise
Mayonnaise, Cloves garic; crushed, Salt and pepper, Rice vinegar, Onions, Small red potatoes, Dry mustard, CHIVE BLOSSOM MAYO, Chive blossoms florets and, Lemon rind, Sugar, Chopped bell pepper, Chopped parsley, 
From: arielle@bonkers.taronga.com (Stephanie da Silva)    Date: Sat, 22 Apr 1995 11:28:04 GMT  Boil whole potatoes until tender.  Let cool, then peel and slice.  Heat  vinegar with mustard and sugar and pour over potatoes.  Mix in onion.  Toss  well and cool.    Combine remaining ingredients and pour over potatoes; mix well.  Season to  taste with salt and pepper.  Garnish with whole chive blossoms and serve.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
