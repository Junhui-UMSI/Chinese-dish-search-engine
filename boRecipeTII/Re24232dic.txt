Trees and Raisins
Sugar, Mayonnaise, Raisins, broccoli, bacon, red onion, Vinegar, 
Combine broccoli, bacon, raisins and onion in a large bowl. Blend together  mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar. Pour over broccoli mixture. Stir to coat  well. Cover and marinate at least 1 hour. Serves 6.    This recipe is from Sassafras! The Ozarks Cookbook by the Junior League of  Springfield, Missouri Posted to EAT-L Digest by "Tracie K. Graham"  <JRCTracie@AOL.COM> on Sep 3, 1997
