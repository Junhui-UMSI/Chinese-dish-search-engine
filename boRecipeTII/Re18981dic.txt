Angels Kiss
Brandy, Sweet cream, Dark creme de cacao, Creme Yvette, 
Pour ingredients slowly, in order given, into a pony or pousse-cafe glass,  being careful not to let ingredients mix. This produces a multi-colored  "striped" effect of the liquors.For best results pour each ingredient  slowly over a teaspoon held bottom-side up over the glass. You can achieve  the same effect by inserting a glass stirring rod into the glass and then  slowly pouring each ingredient down the rod.    Recipe By     : Joe Robertson
