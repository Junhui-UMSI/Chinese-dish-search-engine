Picante Pork Stew
(28-oz) stewed tomatoes, Picante sauce, Potatoes, cut in chunks, Carrots, sliced, Thyme leaves, Bay Leaves, Pork, Onion, Salt, Black pepper, olive oil, 
Brown pork with olive oil. Add onion;cook 2 minutes. Add salt and pepper.  Add tomatoes,picante sauce,thyme and bay leaves. Bring to a boil. Add  potatoes and carrots. Cook until veggies are tender.
