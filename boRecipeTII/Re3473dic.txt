Pecan Fudge Squares
Eggs; well beaten, Brown sugar, Pecans, Vanilla, Flour, Baking Powder, Butter, 
Dissolve brown sugar in melted butter; allow to cool slightly. Add eggs,  flour and baking powder. Add nuts and vanilla; mix well. Pour into well  greased 9 x 14-inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes. Allow to  cool slightly before cutting. These squares can be frozen in 1 long piece  or 2 large squares and cut into desired sizes when ready to serve. Makes  about 48 squares.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 315 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 27, 1997
