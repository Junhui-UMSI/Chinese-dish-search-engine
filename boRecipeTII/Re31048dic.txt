Banana Pecan Bread (Muffins)
Baking soda, Eggs, Sugar, Salt, Bananas; mashed, Pecans, Vegetable oil, Flour, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    Cream sugar and oil. Add bananas and eggs. Add dry ingredients. Mix  thoroughly and pour into two greased loaf pans or muffin tines. Bake about  45 minutes for loaves; 30 minutes for muffins.    Makes 20 small muffins. Bake for 30 minutes.    Recipe by: ???
