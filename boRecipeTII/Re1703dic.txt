Ceviche
Fresh lime juice, Shredded lettuce, Chili sauce, Catsup, Fresh red snapper fillets, Fresh Lemon Juice, Fine chopped onion, Lime slices, Sauce, Lemon, Of Tabasco, Horseradish, Jalepeno, minced, 
Combine lime juice, lemon juice onion and pepper in shallow glass dish (do  not use metal).  Add fish fillets, stirring to coat.  Cover and refrigerate  for 12 hours.  Drain fish and place on bed of lettuce; garnish with lime  slices.
