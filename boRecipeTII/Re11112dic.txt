Raisin Bran Cookies
Whole wheat flour, Baking soda, Granulated sugar, All-purpose flour, Eggs, Margarine, softened, Brown sugar (firmly packed), 
In large bowl, mix together raisin bran, flours, and soda; set aside.   In large mixing bowl, beat margarine and sugars until light and fluffy.  Add eggs; beat well.  Stir in cereal mixture, thoroughly combining. Drop by  scant 1/4 cup measure onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in preheated, 350  degree F oven about 14 minutes or lightly browned. Makes 18 cookies.   Nutrition Information Per Cookie: Calories 190, Dietary Fiber 2g, Fat 9g.
