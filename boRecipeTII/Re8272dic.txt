Potato Pancakes #3
Pepper, Salt, Onion; quartered, Nutmeg, Baking Powder, FIRST SET, Flour, Potatoes peeled and cut, Parsley, Worchestershire sauce, SECOND SET, milk, Eggs, 
Put first set of ingredients in blender in order given.  Mix.  Put in  potatoes.  Blend until finely chopped but NOT PUREED.  Stop periodically to  clean sides of blender with spatula.  Cook in 2 tbsp hot oil in electric  skillet at 350 F.  Add 1 tbsp batter per pancake and spread out. Cook about  2 minutes for each side. Store on platter in warm oven while cooking rest.
