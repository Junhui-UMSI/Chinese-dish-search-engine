Aloha Pineapple and Banana
Drained, Pineapple rings in juice --, Cream of tartar, Maraschino cherries, Banana, Cinnamon-sugar, Lemon juice, Egg whites, Sugar, 
Arrange pineapple slices on greased baking sheet.  Place a banana slice in  the center of each. Sprinkle with lemon juice and 1/2 of the  cinnamon-sugar.  Make meringue with egg whites, cream of tartar and sugar,  and spread over each pineapple slice.  Sprinkle with r emaining  cinnamon-sugar.  Bake 10 min. at 400F till golden.  Garnish with cherries.  Serve warm or cold.    Recipe By     : My files    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
