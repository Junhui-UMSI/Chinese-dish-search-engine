Beef and Salsa Burritos
Flour tortillas; warmed, Chili powder, Chunky salsa, Cheddar cheese; shredded, Ground beef or ground turkey, 10 oz pkg frozen chopped, Ground cumin, 
In large nonstick skillet, brown ground beef over medium heat 10-12 minutes  or until no longer pink, stirring occasionally.  Pour off drippings. Season  beef with chili powder, cumin and 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon  pepper.  Stir in spinach and salsa; heat through.  Remove from heat; stir  in cheese.  Spoon 1/2 cup beef mixture in center of each tortilla. Fold  bottom edge up over filling; fold sides to center, overlapping edges.
