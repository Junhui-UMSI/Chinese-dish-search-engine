Baked Salmon with Garlic and Herb Crust
garlic, Salt, Salmon fillets; skinned, Ground pepper, Fresh bread crumbs, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Flat leaf parsley, Dried Marjoram, 
1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees.    2. With a mortar and pestle crush the garlic to a paste. In a small mixing  bowl combine the garlic, marjoram, parsley, breadcrumbs, olive oil, and  salt.    3. Grind in a little black pepper and stir until you have a paste.    4. Cut the fillet crosswise into four pieces, lightly grease a baking dish  with about 2 Tsp. olive oil, arrange the fillets with at least 1 inch  apart.    5. Bake the salmon for about 5 minutes, remove from the oven, spread the  paste mixture over the fillets and bake the salmon for another 5-10  minutes.
