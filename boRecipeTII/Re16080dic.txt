Warm Mushroom and Roasted Pepper Loaf
Loaf (8 ounces) Italian or, Fresh shiitake mushrooms,, Garlic, French Bread, Trimmed and sliced, Salt, Black Pepper, Provolone cheese, thinly, Sliced, Thickly sliced, Dry, Fresh white mushrooms,, Jar (7 oz) roasted red, Peppers, drained and patted, Butter, Sliced baked ham, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Halve bread horizontally. Howwow out each  half, leaving 1/2 inch thick shells. In large skillet, melt butter; add  mushrooms, garlic, salt and black pepper. Cook about 6 to 8 minutes,  stirring frequently, until mushrooms are tender and liquid evaporates; set  aside. Arrange ham in bottom half of bread shell; top with mushroom  mixture, red peppers and cheese, cutting to fit shell if necessary. Cover  with remaining shell. Place bread on baking sheet; cover loosely with foil  tent. Bake about 7 minutes, until cheese melts.
