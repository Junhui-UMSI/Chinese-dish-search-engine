Apple-Orange Fritters
Orange -- rind and juice, milk, Margarine, Egg -- beaten, Vanilla, Sugar, Cake flour, Apples -- coarse chop, Baking Powder, Salt, 
Beat egg. In a mixing bowl, combine the milk, egg and melted margarine. Add  the orange juice, rind, chopped apples (skins can be left on) and vanilla.  Sift together the flour, salt, baking powder. Stir into milk mixture with a  spoon until blended. DO NOT OVERMIX. Preheat oil in a skillet to 350~. Drop  off end of tablespoon into hot oil. Fry to a golden brown. Turn so they  brown evenly. Allow to cool.    Recipe By     :    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
