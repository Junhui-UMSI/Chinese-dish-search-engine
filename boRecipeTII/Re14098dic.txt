White Chocolate Toasted Almond Fudge
Dried cherries, Mini marshmallows, vanilla milk chips, Almond extract, Sweetened condensed milk, Toasted almonds, 
Grease 9x9x2 inch pan.  Heat chips, marshmallows and condensed milk over  medium heat until melted and stirred smooth.  Stir in extract, almonds, and  fruit.  Immediately pour into pan.  Cover and refridgerate 4 hours or until  firm.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 15 November 96    Date:    Fri, 15 Nov 1996 20:46:38 -0800    From:    Dot & Tim McChesney <jrjet@WHIDBEY.NET>
