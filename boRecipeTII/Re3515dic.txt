Pecan Pie Bars #1
butter, Sugar, Butter, Eggs, Salt, Sugar, Pecans, Vanilla, Flour, Corn syrup, 
Mix flour, butter, sugar and salt until mixture is in fine crumbs. Press  into a large casserole and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Mix  remainder of ingredients, except pecans. Stir in pecans. Spread in pan and  bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Cool completely. Cut in bars.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
