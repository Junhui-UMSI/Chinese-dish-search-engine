Beef Parmigiana
Beef; thinly sliced, Egg, Bread crumbs, Tomato sauce, Mozzarella cheese; sliced, Parmesan cheese, milk, olive oil, 
Combine egg with milk.  Dredge thin beef slices through egg mixture, then  coat with bread crumbs.  In skillet heat oil until a light haze forms.  Add  beef and brown them quickly on both sides.  Transfer to a baking disk.  Top  with mozzarella and cover with tomato sauce.    Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Bake at 400 for 10 minutes or until sauce  bubbles.  Serve at omce.
