Randy Pollaks Uncooked Persimmon Pudding Rol
Sugar, Coconut, Marshmallows, cut up, Graham crackers, Nutmeats, Persimmon pulp, 
Save enough graham crackers to roll your pulp. Mix together all ingredients  and roll together in crumbs. Wrap with waxed paper and refrigerate  overnight. Slice and serve. Good with Cool Whip (tm) or ice cream.    Posted by Randy Pollak, Kook-Net Recipe Network
