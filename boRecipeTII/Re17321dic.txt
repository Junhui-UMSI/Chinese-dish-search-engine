Waldorf Salad Mold
Salt, Cold water, Diced red apples, Mayonnaise (optional), Vinegar, Boiling water, Walnuts, LORELI AGUDA WSKD49A, Lemon gelatin, 3 oz., Finely diced celery, 
Dissolve gelatin and salt in boiling water. Add cold water and vinegar.  Chill until very thick. Fold in celery, apples, walnuts and mayonnaise.  Spoon into individual molds or a 1 quart mold. Chill until firm. Unmold and  serve with cream cheese or whipped topping.
