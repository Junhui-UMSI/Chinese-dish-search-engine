Yucatanstyle Chicken Skewers W/papayatomatillo Salsa (Gr)
Jicama, julienned, Six-inch skewers, soaked, Skinless and boneless, Papaya tomatillo salsa, Yucatan marinade, 
Rub the marinade into the chicken thighs. Cover the chicken and refrigerate  for 4 to 6 hours or overnight. Prepare a wood or charcoal fire and let it  burn down to embers.    Thread each piece of chicken onto 2 skewers so that the meat stays flat on  the grill. Grill for about 4 minutes on each side or until done to taste.  Serve with PapayaTomatillo Salsa.
