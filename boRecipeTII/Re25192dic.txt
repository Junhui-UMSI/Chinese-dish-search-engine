Tomatoes Provencale
Salt, Firm tomatoes, olive oil, Fresh rosemary or basil --, Chopped, Garlic, Pepper -- to taste, 
1. Slice tomatoes horizontally into 4 slices, each 1/2 inch thick.    2. Peel and split garlic; mash slightly with the side of a knife. Rub  garlic on cut surfaces of tomatoes.    3. Arrange tomatoes on broiler pan. Sprinkle with herbs, salt, and pepper,  and drizzle lightly with oil.    4. Broil until surfaces are blistered. Serve immediately.    Variation: Sprinkle tomatoes with 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan or Romano  cheese. Broil until cheese is lightly browned and bubbly.
