Apple-Raisin Drop Cookies
Cinnamon, Apple; Coarsely Shredded, Powdered sugar, Pillsbury Moist Supreme, Raisins, Nutmeg, Sour cream, Eggs, 
1. Heat oven to 350*F. Grease cookie sheets. In large bowl, combine cake  mix, cinnamon, nutmeg, sour cream and eggs; blend well. Stir in apple and  raisins. Drop dough by heaping teaspoonfuls 1 inch apart onto greased  cookie sheets. 2. Bake 10 to 14 minutes or until edges are golden brown.  Immediately remove from cookie sheets. Cool 5 minutes or until completely  cooled. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, if desired.          4    dozen cookies.
