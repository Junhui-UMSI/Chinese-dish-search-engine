Poor Mans Shrimp
Salmon [cut into 1" cubes], Cold water, FROM CHEF FREDDYS, Salt, 
1)      Add enough salt to the cold water to float an  egg with the shell on and stirr in an additional ? cup  of salt... 2) Bring the water to a boil and add the  salmon cubes... bring to a boil again, drain and serve  with your favorite sauce...
