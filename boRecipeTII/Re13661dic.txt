Wild Mustard Seed Mayonnaise
White pepper, Wine vinegar, Lime; juice of, Garlic, Tarragon, Tumeric, Light miso, Crushed hot pepper flakes, (16-oz) silken tofu, olive oil, Wild mustard seeds or, Lecithin granules, Flaxseed, Fresh parsley, 
Grind the mustard seeds, white pepper, tarragon and flaxseed in a spice  grinder or coffee grinder. Finely chop the garlic and parsley in the food  processor. Add the spices and remaining ingredients to the food processor.  Process until smooth.
