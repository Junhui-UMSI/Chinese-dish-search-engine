Swiss Sausage Casserole
Prepared mustard, Half and half, Eggs, Ground nutmeg, Bread, Swiss cheese; (6 oz.) sliced, Pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, milk, Salt, Bulk sausage; (or less), 
Arrange bread in a lightly greased 13 x 9-inch pan. Saute sausage; drain  thoroughly. Combine sausage and mustard. Sprinkle over bread. Place cheese  slices over sausage. Combine remaining ingredients. Beat well. Pour mixture  over sausage. Refrigerate overnight. Allow to come to room temperature and  bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes. Serves 8.
