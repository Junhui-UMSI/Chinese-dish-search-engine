Barbecued Beef Brisket
Coarse cracked black pepper, Beef brisket, Water, Cider vinegar, Mustard, Ketchup, Red potatoes, unpeeled,, Carrots, peeled, cut in, Salt, Prepared horseradish, Onion, Onions, sliced, 
1. Combine ketchup, water, onion, vinegar, horseradish, mustard and pepper.  Place brisket in a shallow glass baking dish. Pour marinade over; cover and  refrigerate overnight.    2. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Scatter onions on top of meat. Cover and bake  2 1/2 hours. Add carrots and potatoes; cover and continue baking until meat  and vegetables are tender, about 1 hour. Add salt and pepper to taste. Cut  meat into thin slices. Use the pan juices as sauce.    Posted By japlady@nwu.edu (Rebecca Radnor) On rec.food.recipes or  rec.food.cooking
