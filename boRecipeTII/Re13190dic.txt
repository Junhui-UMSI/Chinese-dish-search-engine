Worcester Broiled Halibut Steaks
Salt, Worcesterhire Sauce, Orange peel; traged, Butter (or marg.), Halibut steaks, Orange juice, 
Arrange fish on rack in broiler pan; set aside. Combine orange juice and  peel, Worcestershire sauce and salt. Brush lighlty over fish. Dot with  butter. Place under pre-heated hot broiler 10 to 12 minutes or until fish  flakes when tested with a fork, basting with remaining Worcestershire  mixture.    SOURCE: Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce Advertisement in the February,  1973 issue of Southern Living Magazine. Typed for you by Nancy Coleman.
