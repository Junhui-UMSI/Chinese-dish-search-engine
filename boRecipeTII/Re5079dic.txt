Pico De Gallo
Garlic cloves; finely minced, Finely minced red onion, Lime juice, olive oil, Serrano chiles, Diced tomatoes, 
PLACE CHOPPED tomatoes in a bowl. Combine olive oil, garlic and chiles in a  small pot over medium heat and cook, stirring, 3 minutes. Add to tomatoes,  and add the onion and lime juice. Chill before serving.    Makes 1 Cup    MICHAEL ROBERTS - PRODIGY GUEST CHEFS COOKBOOK
