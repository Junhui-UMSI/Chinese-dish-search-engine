Sweet and Sour Turkey Bake
Brown sugar, Cornstarch, Dried basil, Cider vinegar, Cinnamon, Clove garlic, crushed, salt and pepper, Skinless turkey light meat,, Orange juice, Chutney, mango, Ground ginger, Nutmeg, 
Combine juice and cornstarch, cook until thickened. Remove from heat and  add rest of ingredients except turkey. Can be made ahead to this point.  Heat oven to 375 degrees. Place skinless turkey in oven proof casserole.  Spread sauce over turkey and bake, basting occasionally until done about 1  hour.
