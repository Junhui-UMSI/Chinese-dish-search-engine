Turkey Roll-Ups
Sharp cheddar cheese, Green onion, Dijon mustard, Cream cheese, Flour tortillas, Cooked deli turkey breast, Fresh spinach leaves, 
Combine first 4 ingredients in a bowl; stir well. Spread 3 tablespoons  cheese mixture over each tortilla. Top each with 2 slices turkey, 2 spinach  leaves, and 4 cucumber slices; roll up.
