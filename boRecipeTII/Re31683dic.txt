Swiss Steak W/veg
BEEF SWISS STEAK, Onions, SHORTENING; 3LB, PEPPER SWT GRN FRESH, PEPPER BLACK 1 LB CN, WATER; COLD, Bay Leaves, TOMATO PASTE #2 1/2, Salt, GARLIC DEHY GRA, All Purpose Flour, 
1. DREDGE STEAKS IN MIXTURE OF FLOUR, SALT AND PEPPER, SHAKE OFF EXCESS.    2. BROWN STEAK ON WELL GREASED GRIDDLE.    3. OVERLAP ABOUT 50 STEAKS IN EACH PAN. SET ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 5.    4. COMBINE GARLIC,ONIONS, PEPPERS, TOMATO PASTE, BAY LEAF, CELERY SALT,  AND STOCK OR WATER.    5. POUR AN EQUAL AMOUNT OF SAUCE OVER STEAKS IN EACH PAN. COVER PANS.
