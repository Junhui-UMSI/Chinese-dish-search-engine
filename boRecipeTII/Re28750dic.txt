Texas/ontario Picante Sauce
Salt, Black pepper, Garlic, Sugar, Cumin, Onions, Ontario Fresh Tomatoes, Jalepeno pepers, White vinegar, Alum, Chili powder, 
Put all ingredients in food processor and chop ( note less chopped- thicker  , more chopped-thinner ); Pour into a large pot and bring to a boil. Let  cool enough to pour into pint jars, then place jars in canning bracket and  immerse in water to about 1 inch below cap. Boil jars for 20 minutes. Let  cool, Serve as condiment for all sorts of dishes, perfect with Mexican  food.
