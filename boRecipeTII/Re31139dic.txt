Banana Split Moon Pie
Chilled banana moon pie., Chocolate syrup, Banana, Ice cream, 
Split your chilled Moon Pie. Place thin slices of bananas on both sides..  Place them on a plate and cover with a thin coat of chocolate. Sprinkle  with nuts and add a cherry to each half.    Recipe by: Moon Pie?    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Barbra<barbra@pipeline.com> on Feb 18, 1998
