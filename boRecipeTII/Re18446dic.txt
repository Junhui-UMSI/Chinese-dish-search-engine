Vidalia Onion and Apple Casserole
Vidalia onions; sliced into, Apples; peeled and sliced, White bread;, cubed, Cooked bacon; crumbled,, Chicken broth;, preferably, 
Layer onions, apples and bacon in a 2 quart casserole. Pour chicken broth  into bottom of casserole. Cover and bake at 350 for 45 minutes. While  baking, saute bread crumbs in 2 tablespoons bacon fat. Uncover casserole  and dot with bred crumbs and bake for an additional 15 minutes. Serves 4.
