Vegetables, Chicken, and Cellophane Noondles
Cellophane noodles, Whole mushroom, Cloves garlic minced, Salt, optional, Sugar, Whole zucchini, Skinned and boned chicken, Scallion, Sesame oil, Whole carrot, Chinese cabbage, 4 leaves, olive oil, Soy sauce, 
Cover cellophane noodles with warm water and set aside. Steam chicken  breasts in a vegetable steamer. When cooked through (about 10 minutes), cut  them into bite size pieces. Set aside. Cut mushrooms into think slices. Cut  carrot and zucchini into juilienne strips. Cut green onions into 1 inch  pieeces. Set aside. Remove the green leaf from the cabbage. Cut into  bite-size pieces. Julienne the white part of the leaf. Set aside. Drain  water from cellophane noodles. Saute garlic in both oils. Add vegetables  and stir-fry until tender but crisp. Stir in chicken Stir in soy sauce,  sugar, salt, and noodles. Mix to distribute sauce evenly. Serve over rice.
