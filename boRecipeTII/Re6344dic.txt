Plantation Hot Sauce #2
Molasses, Vinegar or lemon juice, Bottled hot pepper sauce, Prepared mustard, Salt, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Blend molasses and prepared mustard; stir in remaining ingredients. Heat to  boiling.  Makes enough sauce for basting 4 pounds loin back ribs or  spareribs.
