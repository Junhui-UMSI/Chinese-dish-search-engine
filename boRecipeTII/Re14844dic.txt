1948 Original Barbecue Sauce
Butter or Margarine, (14 1/2-ounce) whole, Molasses, Dry mustard, Green Pepper, White vinegar, Fresh celery leaves; chopped, Onion, Celery, Garlic, Salt, Lemon slices, (8-ounce) tomato sauce, Ground allspice, Bay leaf, Hot sauce, Ground cloves, 
Saute onion and garlic in butter in a saucepan until tender. Stir in  tomatoes and remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and  simmer, uncovered, 30 minutes; stir occasionally. Remove and discard bay  leaf and lemon slices. Process mixture through a food mill or in a food  processor, if desired. Use sauce for basting, or serve with chicken.    Yield: 3 cups.
