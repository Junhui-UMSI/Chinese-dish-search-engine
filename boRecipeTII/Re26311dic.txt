Tomato and Pepper Relish
Green tomatoes, Vinegar, Sugar, Water, Turmeric, Onions, Green or green & red peppers, Salt, Mustard seed, Celery seed, 
Chop onions, tomatoes and peppers. Add salt. Let stand overnight. Drain.  Put other ingredients in a pot and bring to a boil. Add drained vegetables  to liquid and bring to a boil. Cook 10 minutes, then simmer 10 minutes. Put  in airtight jars. No processing necessary.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 26,  1998
