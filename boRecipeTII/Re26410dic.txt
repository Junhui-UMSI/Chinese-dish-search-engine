Tomato Clam Linguine
Canned baby clams, Canned tomato sauce, Salt, Fresh Parsley, chopped, Onions, chopped, Granulated sugar, Linguine, Pepper, Vegetable oil, Garlic, 
In a large skillet, beat oil over medium heat; cook garlic and onions,  stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until softened.    Drain clams, reserving 1/3 cup juice. Add juice to skillet along with  tomato sauce, half of the parsley, the sugar, salt and pepper; cook for 5  minutes or until slightly thickened. Add clams; cook for 1 minutes.    Meanwhile, in lare pot of boiling salted water; cook pasta for 8-10 minutes  or until tender but firm; drain well and return to the pot. Add sauce and  toss to coat.    Serve sprinkled with remaining parsley.
