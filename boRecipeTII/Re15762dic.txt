Abgushte Miveh (Dried Fruit Soups)
Limu omani (dried lime);, Lean lamb or beef stew meat, To 1/2 cup brown sugar, Turmeric, Prunes; pitted, Water, Dried apricot halves, Lamb or beef soup bones, Black Pepper, Onion, Ghee or butter, Dried peaches; chopped, To 2 T. lemon or lime juice, 
Cut meat into small cubes and place in a soup pot with bones, water, and  dried lime if available.  Bring slowly to the boil, skimming when  necessary.  Add salt and pepper, cover and simmer gently for 1 1/2 hours or  until meat is almost tender.  Remove bones and dried lime if used.    In a frying pan, heat ghee or butter and fry onion until transparent, stir  in turmeric, and cook until lightly browned. Add to soup with dried fruits  (these may be washed if necessary, but do not require soaking). Cover and  simmer for 30 minutes.  Add sugar and lemon or lime juice to taste so that  soup has a pleasant sweet-sour flavor. Serve hot. Source: The Complete  Middle East Cookbook by Tess Mallos.    Shared and MM by Judi M.
