Strawberries with Creme Sauce
Strawberries; washed, hulled, Cottage cheese, Vanilla extract, Plus 1 tsp. honey, Chocolate fudge topping, 
Prep: 10 min.    Process cottage cheese, vanilla and honey in a food processor 1-2 minutes  or until very smooth. Serve sauce over strawberries and drizzle with  topping.    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 23, 1998
