Turkey Dressing
Fresh parsley, Onion, Cornbread stuffing, Butter, Chopped, White pepper, Soft bread crumbs, celery, Thyme, Baking Powder, Eggs, Salt, Sage, Bacon fat, Turkey giblets stock, 
Combine salt, sage, thyme, baking powder, pepper, bread crumbs, and eggs.  Melt butter with bacon fat in skillet on low heat; add onion, celery and  parsley. Simmer for about 15 minutes. Mix together the butter and crumb  mixtures. Soften to your taste with stock made from stewed giblets.
