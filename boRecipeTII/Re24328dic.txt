Triple Fudge Brownies
Vanilla ice cream, Instant chocolate pudding, Semisweet chocolate chips, Powdered sugar, Chocolate cake mix, 
Prepare pudding according to package directions. Whisk in cake mix. Stir in  chocolate chips. Pour into a greased 15x10x1-inch baking pan. Bake at 350?  for 30-35 minutes or until the top springs back when lightly touched. Dust  with powdered sugar. Serve with ice cream, if desired.
