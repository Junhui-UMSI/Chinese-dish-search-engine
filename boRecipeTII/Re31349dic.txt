Bar-B-Qd Lentils
Molasses, Tomato sauce, Water, Vinegar, Lentils; rinsed, Ketchup, Brown sugar, Minced onions, Liquid barbecue smoke.; opti, Dry mustard, Worcestershire sauce; or veg, 
Recipe by: USA Dry Pea & Lentil Council Add lentils to water, bring to a  boil and simmer for 30 min or until tender but whole. Add remaining  ingredients to the cooked lentils and bake at 350 deg F for 45 min.    This is EXCELLENT!    Typed and tested for you by Reggie Dwork reggie@reggie.com
