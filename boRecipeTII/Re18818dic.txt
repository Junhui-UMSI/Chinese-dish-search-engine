Anda Miso (Oil Miso)
Grated ginger root, Ground pork, Sugar, Bottle (13-oz) miso, Green Onions, 
In a skillet brown pork with ginger.  Add green onions; lower heat and  cook, stirring constantly, about 2 minutes. Stir in sugar and miso; mix  well. Continue cooking on low heat, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes.  Serve over hot rice. Makes 3 cups.    From a book of my mothers (Judy Hosey) titled <Senator Richard Matsuuras  Favorite Recipes>, mostly Japanese and Hawaiian.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
