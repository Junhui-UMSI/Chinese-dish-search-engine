Pollo Loco Chicken
Onions, minced, Mexican oregano, Lemon juice, Chicken 2 1/2-3 lbs halved, Garlic, finely minced, Or 2 drops yellow food color, Corn oil, Butter, Orange juice, Annato powder, Ground cumin, 
Combine ingredients in a large shallow pan.  Add chicken halves, turning to  coat.  Cover and marinate sevearl hours or overnite Remove chicken from  marinade.  Grill or broil until browned on both sides. Baste frequently.  Cook approx. 25 min. Cut chicken into pieces. Serve with beans and rice,  corn or flour tortillas and fresh salsa.
