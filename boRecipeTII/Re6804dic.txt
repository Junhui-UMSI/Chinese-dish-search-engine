Pollo En Salsa De Mani
Fresh coriander, Vegetable oil, Sweet red pepper, cut in, Tomatoes, Chicken breasts, skinless, Peanut butter, Salt and pepper, Garlic, Onion, finely chopped, Tomato paste, 
Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, fry  them in oil for a few minutes on both sides, until  pale brown. In a separate frying pan, fry the onion,  sweet pepper and garlic, adding the fresh tomatoes,  tomato paste, coriander and the peanut butter.  Add the chicken to the sauce, cover, and let cook  completely until it is tender, 30 minutes at low to  medium heat. Stir occasionally. Serve with Arroz con  leche de coco.
