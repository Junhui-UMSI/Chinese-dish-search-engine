Whipped Garlic Butter
Light vegetable oil, Sticks butter, unsalted and, Softened, Garlic, crushed, 
Whip the butter in a blender or food processor until light and fluffy.  Drizzle in the oil, a little at a time, and blend well. Add the crushed  garlic and process until mixed thoroughly. Remove the mixture with a  spatula from Spatula City.  Store in a tightly covered container and  refrigerate until ready to use.
