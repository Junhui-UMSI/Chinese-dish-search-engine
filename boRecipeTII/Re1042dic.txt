Coke Roast
Beef Brisket or Roast, Dry onion soup mix, 12-oz jar chili sauce, (12oz) regular Coke, 
Source: The Four Ingredient Cookbook    Mix soup, chili sauce and coke together. Pour over roast, cover and bake a  350 degrees for 30 minutes per pound. DO NOT USE FOIL WITH THIS BECUASE OF  COKE.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by MUFFYMOM@aol.com on Dec 3, 1998, converted  by MM_Buster v2.0l.
