Quick Garlic Wedges
Black pepper to taste, Garlic; chopped, Pillsbury pizza crust, olive oil, Dried parsley flakes, 
1. Heat oven to 425 degrees. Grease a 12 inch pizza pan or 13X9 in flat  pan.    2. Unroll dough and place in greased pan. Starting at the center, press out  with hands.    3. In a small bowl, combine all the remaining ingredients and brush over  the pizza crust.    4. Bake for 13-18 minutes or until crust is golden brown.    5. Cut into wedges.
