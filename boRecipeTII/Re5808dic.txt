Pinto Bean Tacos
Tomato sauce, Taco seasoning mix, Taco shells, 15 oz can, Onion -- chopped, Cumin, Drained, Chili powder, Bell peppers -- chopped, Cheddar cheese -- shredded, Oregano, Pinto beans -- rinsed/, 
Heat taco shells according to package directions.  Meanwhile, in a saucepan  combine pinto beans, tomato sauce, peppers, onions, taco seasoning mix,  chili powder, oregano, and cumin.  Bring to a boiling. Reduce heat; simmer  occasionally.  Mash beans slightly.  Spoon mixture into taco shells. Top  with cheese.
