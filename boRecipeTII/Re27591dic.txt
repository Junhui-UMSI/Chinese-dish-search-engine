The Best Dressing
Fat-free mayonnaise, Fat-free yogurt, plain, Sugar substitute, to taste, Lemon juice, optional, 
Mix together, chill and serve over salads.    NOTES : This has become a favorite recipe for all kinds of salads, fruit  salad, pasta salad, chicken salads.  It is great. Very tasty and very  healthy.  Posted to Digest eat-lf.v097.n037 by loyaz@juno.com on Feb 08, 1997.
