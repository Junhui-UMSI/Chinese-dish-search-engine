Barbeque Meatballs
Sauce, Bread crumbs, Salt, Egg, Evaporated milk, Ketchup, Chili sauce, Margarine, Onions, Vinegar, Ground beef, 
1. Mix and shape into balls.  2. Brown meatballs in 1 Tbsp . cooking oil. Place  into baking dish.     3. Mix sauce and pour over meatballs. Bake at 375 for about 20 minutes.
