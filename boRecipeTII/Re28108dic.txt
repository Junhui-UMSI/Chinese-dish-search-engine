Baked Corn Casserole
Onion -- chopped, Jar pimiento -- chopped, milk, Egg -- beaten, Whole kernel corn, Bell pepper -- chopped, Cracker crumbs, Creamed corn, Cheddar cheese -- grated, Butter -- melted, Red pepper -- to taste, Salt and pepper -- to taste, 
Combine all ingredients in order given; mix well. Pour into greased 2-quart  ovenproof casserole.  Bake at 350F for 1 hour.    Recipe By     :
