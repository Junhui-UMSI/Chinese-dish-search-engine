Quinns Sweet N Sour Sauce
Peanut oil, Green onion; thin bias cut, Grenadine, As needed cornstarch slurry, Fresh ginger; brunoise, Yellow bell pepper; brunoise, Sugar, Red bell pepper; brunoise, Rice vinegar, 
1. Saut peppers and ginger in hot oil, remove from heat and steep.    2. Combine vinegar & sugar and reduce by 1/3 over medium heat.    3. add grenadine.    4. thicken with cornstarch slurry.    5. Garnish with sesame seeds, peppers, green onion, & ginger.
