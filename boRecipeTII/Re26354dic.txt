Tomato Basil Salad
SALAD, Rice vinegar, tomatoes, Bell pepper, Red onion, Fresh Sweet Basil, DRESSING, Sugar, 
In large Salad bowl, add: Then toss salad ingredients.    Dressing: Dissolve sugar in vinegar and pour over salad, toss. Grind fresh  pepper on top, let stand half an hour for best flavor.
