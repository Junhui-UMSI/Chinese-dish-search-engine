Chocolate Pudding
Cornstarch, Sweetener; (sugar or, Unsweetened cocoa, Vanilla soy or rice milk*, 
Mix the dry ingredients in the saucepan. Over medium heat, gradually add  the liquid, stirring constantly, mixing thoroughly. Continue to stir until  the mixture comes to a boil. Boil for one minute, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and transfer to custard cups.    *If using unflavored soy or rice milk, add 1 teaspoon of vanilla to the  mixture immediately after removing from heat and prior to filling the  custard cups.
