Barbecued Spareribs
Lemon, Worcestershire Sauce, Cayenne pepper, Salt, Black pepper, Sugar, Vinegar, Thyme or sage, Celery; finely chopped, Chili powder, Paprika, Chili sauce, Several bay leaves, Onions; finely chopped, Lean ribs; (6 to 8 lbs.), Pods garlic; chopped, Tomato paste, Cumin, Mustard, 
Cut ribs into small pieces. Sprinkle with salt, paprika, black pepper,  garlic and lemon juice. Let stand several hours or overnight. Prepare sauce  by Sauteing onions and celery in vegetable oil until tender and golden. Add  mustard, Worcestershire sauce, chili sauce, chili powder, cumin, cayenne  pepper, bay leaves, tomato paste, vinegar and sugar. Marinate ribs on both  sides in sauce. Cook over low fire on grill or in oven under broiler at 350  degrees, turning and basting every 15 minutes, for about 2 hours.
