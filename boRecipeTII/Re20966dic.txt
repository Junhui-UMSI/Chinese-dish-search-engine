Apple Peanut Butter Sandwiches
Granny Smith apple, Brown sugar -- packed, Creamy peanut butter, Soft white or wheat bread, Cinnamon, Loaf, Cream cheese -- softened, 
Peel, core and slice apple with Apple Peeler/Corer/Slicer.  Chop sliced  apple with Food Chopper.  Combine all ingredients except bread in Batter  Bowl.  Place a scant tablespoonfulof filling in center of a piece of bread.  Cover with another slice.  Cut and seal the 2 layers of bread with 3-inch  Cut-N-Seal.
