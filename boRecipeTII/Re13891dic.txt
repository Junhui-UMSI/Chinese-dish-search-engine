Winds in the Willows
Season salt, Paperika, Tomato soup, Hash browns, Hot dogs, Refried beans, Hamburger, Mixed vegs, 
Method:  :       In a casserole dish put browned hamburger on bottom. Then the  refried beans , then sliver hotdogs length wise and place on top of beans.  Then add can of mixed vegs and a can of tomato soup. Then put hash browns  on top and sprinkle with season salt and paperika. Bake at 350 " F for  25-30 min.    "YOU WILL HAVE WIND IN THE WILLOWS"
