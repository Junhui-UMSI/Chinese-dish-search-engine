Poires Au Vin Rouge (Pears Poached in Red Wine)
Cinnamon stick,2", Dry Red Wine, Juice of lemon, Small pears,cored/halved, Sugar, 
Dip pears into lemon juice. Heat wine, sugar and cinnamon stick in  10-inch skillet, stirring constantly, until sugar is is dissolved and  mixture boils; reduce heat and add pears. Simmer uncovered until  pears are soft but not mushy when pierced with sharp knife, about 15  minutes.   Cool pears in syrup until lukewarm; discard cinnamon stick. Remove  pears to dessert dishes with slotted spoon. Spoon syrup over pears.  Serve warm or refrigerate until cool.     File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
