Planked Red Snapper
Vegetables as desired, Salt and pepper to taste, Lemon juice, Red snapper fillet, Plank board per each, Paprika, olive oil, 
Season fillets with salt and pepper, lemon juice and a dash of paprika. Rub  with olive oil and broil slowly for 10 minutes. Remove from the broiler,  place on a plank and decorate with vegetables (onion rings, peas, mushroom  caps, asparagus etc.) Place the whole board with fish and vegetables  beneath the broiler again. Serve sizzling hot. Also for: Cobia, trout,  King, Spanish mackerel, Dolphin, Grouper, Amberjack, mullet. Recipe date:  11/28/87
