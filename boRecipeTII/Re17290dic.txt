Wakes Cakes
Flour, Egg well beaten, Baking Powder, Sugar to sprinkle, Currants, Grated rind of 1 lemon, Caraway seeds, Butter, Sugar, 
Set oven to 375/F or Mark 5. Cream the butter and sugar together in  a bowl, add the beaten egg and mix in all the other igredients to  make a firm dough. Roll out thinly on a floured surface, cut into  rounds with a 2 1/2 inch cutter sprinkle with sugar and place on a  greased baking tray. Bake for  10-15  minutes until lightly browned.
