Baked Lemon Chicken with Vegetables
Oregano, Black pepper, Zucchini squash, White wine, Boneless skinless chicken, Lemon juice, Garlic, Green pepper, Low sodium chicken broth, 
Place chicken in a casserole dish. Top with the garlic, other seasonings,  and the vegetables. Pour in broth, lemon juice and wine.    Cover tightly and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until chicken is  cooked through.
