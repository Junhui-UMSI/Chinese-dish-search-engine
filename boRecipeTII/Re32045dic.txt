Barbecued Beef Ribs
Ketchup, Prepared mustard, Salt, Brown sugar, Water, Hot cooked rice; optional, Vinegar, Boneless beef ribs, (6oz) tomato paste, 
Preparation Time: 8:00 Put all the ingredients, except the cooked rice,  into a crock pot and mix well. Cook on low for 8 hours. Serve over hot  rice, noodles, etc. if desired.
