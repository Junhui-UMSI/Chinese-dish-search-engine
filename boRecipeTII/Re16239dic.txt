Watercress Finger Sandwiches
Bunch watercress, salt and pepper, Mayonnaise, White bread, 
Trim crust off bread, then butter each slice. Wash watercress, then chop  very fine. Add mayonnaise and seasoning to watercress; mix thoroughly.  Spread watercress mixture on two slices of the bread, and top with  remaining slices. Cut sandwiches in half, serve garnished with watercress.
