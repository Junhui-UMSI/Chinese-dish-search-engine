Chicken Tetrazzini
Spaghetti, Butter, Flour, Salt, Pepper, Chicken broth, Half-and-half, chicken, Sliced mushrooms, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Cook spaghetti pieces till just tender; drain. Heat butter in 3 qt.  saucepan until melted. Stir in flour, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring  constantly , until smooth and bubbly; remove from heat. Stir in broth and  half-and-half. Heat to boiling; stirring constantly. Boil and stir for 1  minute. Stir in cooked spaghetti, chicken, and mushrooms. Pour into  ungreased casserole dish. Sprinkle with cheese. Cook, uncovered, in 350  degree oven until top is golden brown, about 30 minutes.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by IceTeaLuvr@aol.com on Aug 21, 1998,
