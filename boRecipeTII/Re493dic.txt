Lemon Pudding Cake
Eggs separated, Sugar, Flour, Butter, Lemon Peel, Lemon juice, milk, Salt, 
Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form set aside.  Beat egg yolks and blend  in lemon peel juice and butter and milk.  Combine sugar, flour and salt and  add to egg milk mixture beating until smooth.  Fold into beaten egg whites.  Spoon into crockpot.  Cover and cook on high for 2 to 3 hours From Mable  Hoffmans Crockery cooking    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #082 by BobbieB1@aol.com on Mar 21, 1997
