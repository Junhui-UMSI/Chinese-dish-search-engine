Twinkie Cake
Pudding mix, instant chocolate, Twinkie snack cakes, Cool Whip, Heath candy bars, 
Prepare the pudding according to package directions.  Break up Twinkies  into bite size pieces into an oblong dish.  Spread with pudding, sprinkle  with 3 crushed Heath bars.  Cover with topping and remaining 3 crushed  Heath bars.  Chill and serve.  Delicious!
