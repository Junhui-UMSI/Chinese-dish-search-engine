Peaches and Cream Pie #1
Flour, Peach, Cream, Sugar, Salt, Cinnamon, Crust Pastry, 
Place peaches rounded side up in pastry lined pan. Mix the sugar, flour,  salt, cinnamon and cream; pour over peaches. Bake 35 to 45 minutes at 400  degrees until crust is nicely browned and filling is set.
