Zesty French Dressing
Onion; chopped fine, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Prepared mustard, Catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, Vinegar, Paprika, Celery seed, Salt, Garlic powder, 
In a blender or food processor, process all ingredients until smooth and  thickened. Cover and refridgerate at least 1 hour. Shake well before  serving.    NOTES : Yield: 1-1/2 cups  -- Rocky Hurley    Recipe by: LaVonne Hegland, Pg.53,April/May97,Taste of Home Serving    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by kATHERINE L Smith <ksmith3002@JUNO.COM> on Jan  21, 1998
