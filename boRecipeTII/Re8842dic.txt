Praline Sweet Potatoes
Sugar, Butter, Sweet potatoes, Flour, Vanilla, Pecans, Heavy cream, Brown sugar, TOPPING, Eggs, 
Mix ingredients above well. Spread evenly in buttered casserole dish.    Topping: Mix these ingredients until crumbly. Sprinkle over sweet potato  mixture and bake in 350 degree oven until warmed (topping should be a  little bit bubbly)    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #422 by mdrinkwater@juno.com (Margaret A  Drinkwater) on Nov 29, 1997
