Pickled Blackeyed Peas
Red onions, thinly sliced, Cooked blackeyed peas,, drained, Red wine vinegar, Canola oil, Peanut oil, DRESSING, Garlic, Salt, Cayenne pepper, 
While peas (either freshly cooked or canned) are draining, mix  dressing. Toss with peas and cover tightly.  Refrigerate overnight.  Remove garlic. Refrigerate for several days to enrich flavor before  serving.  Serve with a slotted spoon, to mound on crackers or toast  points as an appetizer.
