Basil Cherry Tomatoes #1
Butter, Dried basil -or-, Cherry tomatoes, Fresh basil; chopped, 
Rinse, stem and pat dry tomatoes. In a large skillet, melt butter over  medium heat. Add basil. Cook, stirring, for 2 minutes. Add tomatoes and  cook, tossing them, for 5 minutes or heated through. Serve immediately. 10  servings.     NOTE: Be careful not to overcook as tomatoes will become mushy.
