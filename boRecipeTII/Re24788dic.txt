Aunt Irenes Date Balls
Dates; cut up, Rice Krispies?, Pecans, Stick margarine, Egg; well-beaten, Shredded coconut meat, Sugar, Vanilla, 
Combine first five ingredients, boil 10 minutes over low heat, stirring  constantly. Remove from heat.    Add vanilla. Cool to handle. Pour over Rice Krispies. Stir well, shape into  small ball, roll in shredded coconut.    Place on cookie sheet in refrigerator to chill. The balls will then hold  their shape.
