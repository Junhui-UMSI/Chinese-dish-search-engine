Zesty Jambalaya
Vegetable oil, Uncooked; converted rice, Fresh chopped thyme OR 1, Carrot; coarsely chopped, Scallions; cut into 1/2 inch, Smoked ham; cut into thin, Chopped parsley, Chicken broth, Red pepper flakes, Ribs celery; sliced, Cloves garlic;, minced, Box (10oz) frozen green peas, Onions; coarsely chopped, 
Heat oil in heavy saucepot over medium heat. Add onions, cook, stirring,  8-10 minutes until well-browned. Add rice, celery, carrots, scallions,  garlic, thyme and red pepper flakes. Cook 2 minutes. Add chicken broth.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 15 minutes. Add ham  and peas; cover and cook 5 minutes until liquid is absorbed and rice is  tender. Stir in parsley.
