Barbequed Oysters and Razor Clams with Spicy Vinegar
Razor clams, Red onion, Red wine vinegar, Sugar, Mint leaves, oysters, Salt, Fresh jalapeno peppers;, 
Preheat grill or barbecue.    Scrub and clean oysters and razor clams and drain.    In a small mixing bowl, place chopped peppers, vinegar, sugar, salt, onion  and mint and stir together. Place shellfish on grill and cook until shells  open. remove and place on platter covered with rock salt. Place dipping  sauce in center and serve with cocktail forks.
