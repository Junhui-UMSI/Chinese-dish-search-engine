Tuna Fish Salad
Celery, Mayonnaise, Eggs, Salt, Lettuce, sweet pickles, tuna fish, Onions, 
Drain tuna. Break into pieces. Combine next six ingredients and mix  lightly. Serve on lettuce. Yield: 6 servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
