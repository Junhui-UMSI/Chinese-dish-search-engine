Veal Valdostana
Fontina cheese, shredded, Pepper, All-purpose flour, Egg, beaten with 2 TBL water, Veal cutlets (4 oz each), Plain dried bread crumbs, Reduced-calorie margarine, Salt, 
Garnish:  8 lemon wedges    Spread bread crumbs on a sheet of wax paper or a paper plate; set aside. In  bowl combine flour, salt, and pepper; dredge cutlets in flour, coating all  sides. Dip each cutlet into beaten egg, then into bread crumbs, thoroughly  coating all sides.    In 12-inch nonstick skillet heat margarine over medium heat until hot and  bubbly; add cutlets and brown on both sides. Transfer cutlets to jelly-roll  pan or cookie sheet; top each with 1 ounce cheese and broil just until  cheese melts.  Serve each portion garnished with 2 lemon wedges.    Makes 4 servings.
