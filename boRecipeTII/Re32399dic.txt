Barley-Vegetable Pilaf
Boiling water, Instant chicken bouillon, Medium pearl barley, Hot red-pepper sauce, Each; medium: red bell, Onions, olive oil, Fresh mushrooms, 
Notes: From Houston (Texas) Chronicle.    Combine onion, mushrooms and oil in a 2-quart glass measure. Cover with  vented plastic wrap. Microwave on high 3 minutes. Add barley, bouillon,  boiling water and pepper sauce. Re-cover and microwave on high 2 to 3  minutes, or until water returns to a boil. Then microwave on medium (50  percent) 15 minutes.    Add bell pepper and zucchini; re-cover. Microwave on medium 10 minutes. Let  stand 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.    Makes 4 (1-cup) servings, each: 221 calories, 4 grams protein, 4 grams fat,  43 grams carbohydrates, 6 grams fiber, no cholesterol, 78 milligrams  sodium.
