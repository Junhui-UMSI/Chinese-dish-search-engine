Zucchini Pie
Zucchini (less rind and seed, Cream of tartar, Warm water, Flour, Butter, Salt water, Brown or white sugar, STREUSEL TOPPING, FILLING, Sliced 1/4" thick, Cinnamon, Sugar, Unbaked pie shell, 
Parboil prepared zucchini in salt water until tender.Drain; put in  bowl.Add warm water,sugar,cinnamon and cream of tartar. Place in unbaked  pie shell.Bake 1/2 hour @ 350 degrees.Top with Streusel Topping  mixture.Return to oven for 1/2 to 3/4 hour.Tastes just like apple pie.
