Toms Chipotle Beans
Smoked salt pork, Salt, Onion, Jalapeno, Worcestershire Sauce, Smoked Boston Butt, Roasted black mustard seed, Paprika, Tomato paste, Marjoram, Garlic, Roasted cumin seed, Dried pinto beans, Water to cover, Chipotle peppers, 
Wash and pick over beans; put in mixing bowl. Cover w/cold water and soak  overnight. Next morning, put beans and water into Dutch over and bring to  boil; reduce heat. Cover and simmer 1 hour. Stir in remaining ingredients;  cover and simmer 3 hours or until tender. Add more water if necessary.
