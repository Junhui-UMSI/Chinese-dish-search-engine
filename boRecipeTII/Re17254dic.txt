Waffle Turtle Cookies
Vanilla, CREAM TOGETHER, Sugar, Flour, ADD AND MIX WELL, Eggs, Oleo, Vanilla, unsweetened chocolate, ICING (OPTIONAL), chocolate, Oleo, 
Heat waffle iron. Drop batter by tablespoons on waffle iron. Close and bake  1 minute or little less. Open lid and wait 1 minute; remove lightly with  fork. Cool and frost if desired with icing.
