Pumpkin Bars
Canned pumpkin, Pecans, Eggs, Butter, Sugar, Orange Peel, spice cake mix, salt, 
Reserve 2/3 cup dry mix. In large bowl combine  remaining cake mix, butter, and 1 of the eggs; mix  well. Pat into well-greased 13x9x2-inch baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees F. for 15 minutes. Combine  reserved cake mix, pumpkin, sugar, 2 eggs, peel and a  dash of salt. Beat at medium speed of electric mixer 1  to 2 minutes. Pour over partially baked layer. Top  with nuts. Bake at 350 degrees F. till set, 15 to 20  minutes. Cool; cut into bars. Refrigerate.  Makes 32  bars.
