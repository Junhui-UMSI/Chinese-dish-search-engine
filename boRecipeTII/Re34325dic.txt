Bcs Costa Rican Marinade
lime peel, Fresh Cilantro, Garlic, Lime juice, Tomato juice, Or parsley, Red pepper sauce, Vegetable oil, Salt, 
Mix all ingredients in shallow nonmetal dish or resealable plastic bag. Add  up to 3 pounds chicken, pork or beef, turning to coat with marinade. Cover  dish or seal bag and refrigerate, turning meat occasionally, at least 8  hours but no longer than 24 hours. Remove meat from marinade; discard  marinade. Grill meat as desired.    *Betty Crockers Great Grilling  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #306 by Michael McLaughlin  <mmclaughlin@micronet.net> on Nov 25, 1997
