Strawberry Cake #1
Vegetable oil, Flour, ICING, -(up to), Box (3-oz) strawberry, Eggs, Water, Butter, Strawberry juice, Box (18-oz) white cake mix, Boxes (10-oz) frozen, 
CAKE:  Combine all ingredients. Pour into sheet pan and bake at 350 for 1  hour. Punch holes in cake with toothpick. Ice while still hot. ICING:  Combine all ingredients and pour over cake.
