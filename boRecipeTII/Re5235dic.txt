Pimento Rice
Sugar, Vegetable oil, Rice, Soy sauce, Pimento, Scallion, 
1. Boil or steam rice (see recipes).    2. Dice pimento and mince scallion. Then add to cooked rice, along with soy  sauce, oil and sugar. Mix well to blend; then serve.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
