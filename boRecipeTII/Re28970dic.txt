Thai Spicy Pork Chops
Thai fish sauce, Black pepper, Thai sweet black bean sauce, Thai chili fish sauce, olive oil, White pepper, Honey, serrano chilies, Garlic powder, cilantro, Garlic, Ground coriander, pork chops, 
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except pork chops and Thai chili  fish sauce, and whisk to blend well. Add pork chops and rub gently to coat.  Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 6 hrs, preferably  overnight.  On a charcoal grill, cook pork chops for about 7 minutes on  each side.  (Or bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 12 to 14 minutes.)  Serve with Thai chili fish sauce.    Recipe by Tommy Tang, New Yorker Magazine, 5/25/92. MM:MK VMXV03A.
