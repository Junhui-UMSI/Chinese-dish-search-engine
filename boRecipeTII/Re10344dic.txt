Quick Candied Yams
Yams, Caramel topping, Butter, Marshmallows, 
Put drained yams into a baking dish.  Pour caramel topping over the yams.  Add the butter in slices over the top of the caramel topping. Bake at 350  degrees until hot and bubbly. Add marshmallows and bake until the  marshmallows are brown and melted.    Taken from: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK A Collection of  Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce & Clem Kohl
