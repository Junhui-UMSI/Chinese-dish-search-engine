Worlds Largest Pot of Irish Stew
Onions, Celery, Burgundy wine, water, Potatoes, Carrots, Garlic, Spices, Lamb, 
Use an 80-gallon pot. Stew is stirred with a boat paddle. Slow cook for 5  to 7 hours. Serves approximately 500 people.    Serve with Irish soda bread, coleslaw, brownies and beverage for lunch.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 622 by molony <molony@scsn.net>  on Jan 28, 1998
