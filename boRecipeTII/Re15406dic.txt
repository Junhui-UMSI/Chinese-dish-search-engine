White Chili
Garlic cloves: minced, Leeks; cleaned and diced, Red bell peppers; diced, Flour, Diced green chili peppers, Celery; sliced, Ground coriander, Oregano, Red onions; diced, Chili powder, Sugar, Ground cumin, Cannelini beans, Ground turkey, Chicken broth, 
In a skillet, brown the ground turkey with the garlic and onions.  Drain off the fat.  Place the turkey mixture in a stock pot over medium  heat.  Add the next 6 ingredients and stir until the flour is smooth-  cooked.  Add the stock and stir until the mixture is thickened. Add the  remaining ingredients and simmer for 1 hour.
