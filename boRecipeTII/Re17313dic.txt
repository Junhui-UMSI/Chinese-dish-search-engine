Waldorf Salad
Cheddar cheese, Walnuts, Mayonnaise, apples, Celery, Red grapes, Raisins, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
In a large bowl, toss all the ingredients and stir well to coat with the  mayonnaise and lemon juice. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Serves 6 to  8.    Recipe By     : Deanna Cook, 1996. This salad dresses up a plate nicely. Use fresh ingredients and  toss well to coat the apples and prevent them from browning.
