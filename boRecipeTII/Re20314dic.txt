Veal Piccata
Butter, Lemon juice, salt and pepper, Dry white wine, Garlic, Veal scallops; pounded thin, Green onions; sliced, Capers, Mushrooms; fresh; sliced, 
Dredge veal slices in flour. Saute veal in butter, add wine, lemon juice,  garlic, salt & pepper. Simmer one minute. Remove meat slices and place in  casserole; top with sauce, uncooked mushroom slices, capers and green  onions. You can bake at 350~ for 30 minutes or cover and refrigerate and  cook later. Bring to room temperature before baking. Garnish with fresh  parsley.
