Baked Fish Fillet
Fillet of fish, salt and pepper, Butter, Paprika, Parmesan cheese, Dry white wine, Lemon juice, 
Salt and pepper fillets. Put butter in shallow baking dish in hot oven  400-500 degrees, until browned. The browned butter gives the distinctive  flavor. Place fillets in hot butter, baste with lemon juice and white wine  and return dish to oven for 10-15 minutes. Remove from oven for basting.  Turn fish over and baste with lemon juice and wine. Sprinkle with cheese  and paprika. Return to oven for approximately 5 minute to melt and brown  cheese slightly.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Aug  23, 1998
