Fruited Couscous
Hulled and halved, Peach chunks, Plum plum chunks, Fresh mint leaves, Hot water, Salt, Uncooked couscous, Frozen oj concentrate;, White wine vinegar, Sugar, Orange Peel, Blueberries, 
Just out in Sept issue of Vegetarian Times Recipe Collection is this -    In a 1 quart saucepan, combine water and salt, bring to a boil. Stir in  couscous and cover and remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes.Fluff  couscous lightly with fork. place in medium mixing bowl and set aside. In a  1 cup measure, combine juice concentrate, vinegar, sugar, and peel.    Blend well with a wire whisk. Add half juice mixture to couscous, mix well.  Cover and chill at least 1 hour.    Add remaining juice mixture and remaining ingredients to couscous. Toss  gently to combine. Line serving bowls with lettuce, if desired.
