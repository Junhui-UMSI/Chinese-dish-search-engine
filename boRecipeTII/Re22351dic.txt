Turkish Barley Pudding
Salt, Walnut meats, Pearl barley, milk, Raisins, Water, Cinnamon, Sugar, 
1. wash barley. Place in a saucepan with cold water and salt. Bring to a  boil; then simmer, covered, 1 hour.    2. Transfer cooked barley to the top of a double boiler. Add sugar and  milk; cook 3 hours more over low heat.    3. Blanch walnuts and chop coarsely. Add with raisins to barley mixture;  cook 10 minutes more. Sprinkle with cinnamon and serve hot.    NOTE: This is customarily eaten as a dessert with Mongolian Grill-cooked  meats.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
