Apple Raisin Cobbler Pie
Butter or Margarine, Walnuts, Raisins, Brown sugar, Ground nutmeg, All-purpose flour, Apple pie filling; (20 oz., Ready Crust Shortbread Pie, 
Heat oven to 375 F. Combine apple pie filling, raisins and nutmeg. Spoon  into crust. Combine flour and sugar; cut in butter until crumbly. Stir in  walnuts; sprinkle over filling. Bake 35-45 minutes or until topping is  golden. Makes 1 pie.
