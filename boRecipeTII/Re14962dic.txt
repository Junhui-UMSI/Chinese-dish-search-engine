3-Step Cheesecake Recipe
Vanilla, Eggs, Graham cracker crust, 8 oz pkgs cream cheese;, Mini semi-sweet choco chips, Sugar, 
* You can substitute "light" cream cheese Beat cream cheese, sugar and  vanilla at medium speed until well blended. Blend in 2 eggs. Stir in  chocolate chips then pour batter into graham cracker pie crust. (you may  sprinkle 1/4 c mini semi-sweet chocolate chips on top if you desire) Bake  at 350F for 40 min, or until center is almost set. Cool. For best results  refrigerate for 3 hours.
