Vickis Noodles and Mushrooms
Grated cheese, Vegetable oil, Creamed corn, Green pepper; diced, Tomato soup, (4-oz) mushrooms; drained, Onion; diced, (5-oz) noodles; cooked as, (3-oz) olives; chopped, 
Saute onion and pepper in oil until clear. Mix all ingredients, except  cheese, and pour into casserole. Chill. Sprinkle cheese on top. Bake at 350  for 1 hour. Note: Browned ground beef may be added before baking.
