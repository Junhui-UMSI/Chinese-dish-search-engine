Tanaka Traditional Noodles
Grated fresh ginger, EDEN Tamari Almonds, sliced, Chinese cabbage or bok choy, Onion; sliced, or mustard greens,, EDEN Udon, Carrot, EDEN Organic Shoyu, Jinenjo, Buckwheat or, EDEN Toasted Sesame Oil, EDEN Shiitake Mushrooms, Mugwort Soba, (optional), -hot water for 20 minutes), finely chopped, 
Prepare pasta. Rinse, drain and mix with 1 tablespoon toasted sesame  oil. Set aside. Reserve shiitake soaking liquid, discard stems and slice  shiitake mushrooms caps. Heat oil, saute onion, add carrots, shiitake  and soaking liquid. Simmer 5 minutes. Mix in greens, add pasta, shoyu,  ginger and almonds. Serve.
