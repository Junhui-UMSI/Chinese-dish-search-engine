Beef Short Ribs Pacific Style
Beef ribs *, Brown sugar, Catsup, Cider vinegar, Onion, Butter, Soy sauce, 
Place short ribs on a broiler pan.  Broil until well-browned to remove  excess fat.  Transfer to crockery pot . Saute onion in butter until limp  and golden.  Pour in catsup; soy sauce, vinegar and brown sugar. Heat until  blended. Pour over ribs. Cover. Cook on LOW 8 hours. Makes 4 servings.
