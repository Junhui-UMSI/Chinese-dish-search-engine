Zesty Bean Salad
Red bell pepper, cored,;, Fresh cilantro sprigs, olive oil, Freshly squeezed lime juice, Salt, Black Pepper, Cooked black beans, Yellow bell pepper, cored,;, Chopped red onion, Chopped cilantro, Cooked yellow or white sweet, Cooked red kidney beans, Jalapeno peppers,; seeded, Ground cumin, Cooked lima beans, 
In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, cilantro, cumin,  salt and pepper. Add lima beans, black beans, red kidney beans, red and  yellow bell peppers, onion, corn and jalapeno peppers. Toss gently. Chill  until ready to serve. Serve garnished with cilantro sprigs.
