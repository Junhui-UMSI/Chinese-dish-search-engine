Warys Bean Tarts
Brown sugar, Butter or margarine, Currants, Cooked great northern or, Ground nutmeg, Eggs, Prepared 3-inch tart shells, Light corn syrup, Vanilla extract, 
Mash or puree beans. Beat eggs; add to beans. Add the next five ingredients  and beat well. Place a few currants in each shell. Pour in the filling.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until nicely brown. (Variations: Maple  flavoring can be substituted for vanilla extract, and pecans or walnut for  currants.)
