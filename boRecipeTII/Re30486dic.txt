Banana Nut Bread #3
Eggs, Pecans, Bisquick, Sugar, (8-oz) cream cheese;, Ripe bananas; mashed, 
Preheat oven to 350?. ln a bowl cream together the sugar and cream cheese  until light and fluffy. Beat in mashed bananas and eggs. Add Bisquick and  pecans. Pour into a greased 9x5 loaf pan. Bake for 1 hour or until done  (may be done after 50 to 55 minutes). Note: Cover with foil the last 20  minutes of cooking time if the bread browns too quickly. Cool thoroughly  before slicing. Yield: 1 loaf.    MARILYN FINCHER (MRS. EDWARD)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
