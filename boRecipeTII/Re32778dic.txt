Sweet Potato Pie #1
Evaporated milk, Sugar, Cinnamon, pie crusts, Sticks butter, Eggs, Vanilla, Sweet potatoes, Salt, 
Boil sweet potatoes 30-40 minutes. Peel. Beat potatoes, butter and salt  with mixer; add other ingredients, beating after each addition. Pour into  crusts and bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until firm.
