Baked Eggs on Rice Au Gratin
Salt and pepper to taste, Flour, Parsley, Rice, Sherry wine, Grated Parmesan cheese, milk, -(up to), Hard cooked eggs, Butter or margarine, 
Melt butter and stir in flour; add milk and cook, stirring constantly,  until mixture is thickened and smooth. Continue cooking and stirring for  another minute or two. Add sherry and parsley; season to taste. Place rice  in the bottom of a greased shallow baking dish. Half eggs; arrange on top  of rice. Pour the cream sauce over all; sprinkle with grated cheese. Bake  in a moderate oven -- 350 degrees -- about 20 minutes. Serves 6.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
