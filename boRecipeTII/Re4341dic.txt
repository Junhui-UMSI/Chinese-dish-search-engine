Persimmon Freezer Jam
Persimmon, Sugar, Lemon juice, Lemon peel, 
Mix all ingredients together & boil at low temperature for  approximently 20 minutes or until thickened. Pour into sterilized  jars & seal. Store in refridgerator or freezer.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
