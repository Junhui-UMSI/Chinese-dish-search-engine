Baked Squash
Butter, Eggs, Onions, Yellow summer squash, Salt, Cracker meal or bread crumbs, Sugar, Black pepper, 
Wash & cut up squash. Boil until tender. Drain thoroughly & mash. Add all  ingredients except 1/2 of butter to squash. Melt remaining butter. Pour  squash mixture into baking dish & spread melted butter over top & sprinkle  with cracker meal or bread crumbs. Bake at 375 for about 1 hour or until  brown on top.
