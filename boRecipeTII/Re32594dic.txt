Sweet Mustard Sauce
Mirin, Oil, vegetable, Mustard Dijon, Vinegar rice wine, 
Place mustard in a small bowl. Gradually whisk in the vinegar, then the  mirin and finally the oil. Whisk until smooth. Makes about 2/3 cup. Will  keep a short time if refrigerated. Serve with chicken, poached fish,  grilled flank steak.
