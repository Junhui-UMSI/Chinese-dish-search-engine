A Cauldron of Beans
Tofu dogs, sliced, Zucchini, finely diced, Potato, finely diced, Black beans fifteen ounce, Water, Catsup, Carrot, finely diced, 
In a soup pot, cook zucchini, carrot, and potato in water over medium high  for 10 minutes or until vegetables are soft. Add beans, tofu dogs, and  catsup and cook for 15 minutes or until thouroughly heated. Per serving:  254 calories(11% from fat). 15g protein, 3g fat, 43g carbohydrate, 246 mg  sodium, 12g fiber.    Weve made this several times now and it is very tasty. A nice cold winter  evening recipe.    From Veggie Life November 1995.
