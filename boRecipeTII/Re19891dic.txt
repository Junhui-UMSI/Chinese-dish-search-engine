Apple Bagel Broil
Cinnamon raisin bagel, Butter or margarine, Apple, Cinnamon sugar, American cheese, 
Lightly toast bagel and spread with butter or margarine while still hot.  Top each bagel with apple slices and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Place cheese slices on each bagel and broil until cheese is bubbly. Makes 2 half  bagels.
