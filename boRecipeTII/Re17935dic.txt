Amaranth Baking Powder Bread
IF USING RICE FLOUR, AM Unrefined Vegetable oil, Egg whites; beaten, AM Brown Rice Flour, Honey, Non-alum baking powder, Sea salt (optional), AM Amaranth Flour, Milk, soymilk, or water, 
Mix dry and liquid ingredients separately; beat egg whites, then combine  all ingredients.  Pour batter into well-oiled 8" x 4" pan and bake at 350  F. about 45 minutes.  Cool 10 minutes before removing loaf to rack.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Amaranth Flour" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
