Banana Bread
Stick butter; softened, Ripe banana; mashed, Pecans, Honey, Baking soda, Salt, Brown sugar, All-purpose flour, Egg, 
Soften butter till it spreads easily. Mix the butter, sugar and honey; beat  for a couple of min. Add the eggs and throughly mix in the bananas. Sift  together the flour, soda and salt; blend throughtly into mixture. Finally  fold in nuts. Butter loaf tin and pour in batter. Bake at 350 degree oven  for 1 hour or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
