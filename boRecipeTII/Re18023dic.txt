Vegetarian Supper Dish
Oregano leaves, Zucchini, Onion, Canola oil, Pepper, Garlic, Green bell pepper, Tomatoes, kidney beans, regular rice, 
Recipe contributed by Rachel Rudel, RD, from her acclaimed cookbook  "Cooking Healthy & Fast."    Cook rice as directed on package, omitting salt. In a large skillet, saute  onion and garlic in oil until onion is tender. Add zucchini, green pepper,  oregano, and pepper; cook vegetables until crisp-tender, about 5 minutes.    Add tomatoes and beans; cover and heat thoroughly. Spoon hot rice onto  serving platter and spoon vegetable mixture over.
