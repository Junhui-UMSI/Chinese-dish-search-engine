Very Easy Banana Pie
Baked pie crust, bananas, plain gelatin, Water, vanilla pudding, Splenda, Ground cinnamon, Ice cubes, Low fat ricotta cheese, Sugar free chocolate, 
Put gelatin in blender with 2 tablespoons water. Heat remaining water to  boiling and pour into blender. Blend until mixed well.  Add ice cubes,  pudding mix and ricotta cheese, blend until smooth and well mixed.  Mix Splenda and cinnamon together, set aside Place  first layer of bananas in pie crust, sprinkle with 1/2 cinnamon/ Splenda  mixture. Pour 1/2 pudding mixture into pie. Repeat process: bananas,  Splenda  and remaining pudding.   Chill 2 hours before serving. Garnish with sliced  bananas dipped in melted chocolate.  Add mint leaves immediately before  serving, if available.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #067 by patricia smith  ~~ltpjsmith~~at;galesburg.net> on Mar 6, 1997.
