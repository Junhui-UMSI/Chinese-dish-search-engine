To Roast Individual Cloves of Garlic
Head of garlic, Or 3 thyme sprigs, olive oil, Bay leaf, Salt & freshly ground pepper, 
Preheat oven to 250 F. Break garlic head into cloves; leave inner skins on  the cloves.  Place garlic in a small oven-proof dish and add the olive oil,  thyme and bay leaf.  Season lightly with salt and pepper. Bake garlic until  it is very tender, about 40 minutes. Turn the cloves over from time to  time.  Remove from the oven and let garlic come to room temperature.
