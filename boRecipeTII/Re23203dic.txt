Tuna Crunch
Dry mustard, Evaporated milk, Lemon juice, Salt, Black Pepper, Can tuna in brine, Butter, Self-rising flour, Fresh breadcrumbs, 
Make white sauce with flour,1oz butter and milk. Drain and flake tuna, add  lemon juice, mix. Add to white sauce. Put mixture in lightly greased  casserole. Melt remaining butter and toss breadcrumbs in it. Add mustard,  salt and pepper, sprinkle on top of casserole. Bake mod. oven approx. 30  mins. Can be made with canned salmon.
