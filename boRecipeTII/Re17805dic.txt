Almost Fat-Free Chocolate Cupcakes
dark brown sugar, Cinnamon applesauce, Egg, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Vanilla extract, Skim milk, Salt, Vegetable oil, Powdered sugar, Egg white, Unsweetened cocoa powder, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners. Combine flour,  cocoa, baking powder, soda and salt. Beat the next six ingredients until  combined, about 1 minute. Stir in flour mixture alternately with milk until  just combined.  Pour batter into muffin cups. Bake 22-25 minutes or until  toothpick comes out clean. Remove cupcakes from pan; cool on wire rack 30  minutes.  Sprinkle with sugar, if desired. from Womans World November 28,  1995
