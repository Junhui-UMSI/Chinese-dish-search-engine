Sweet Fried Bananas
Orange Peel, Bananas, Flour, olive oil, Water, Olive oil, Sugar, Lemon juice, Rum, Egg white, 
Cut bananas into halves lengthwise. sprinkle with sugar, lemon juice,  orange peel, and Rum.  Let stand for 2 hours. Make a batter of flour,  water, and 1 tablespoon oil, mixing well, and blend in stiff egg white.  Drain bananas, dip into batter and fry in hot olive oil until just golden  in color. Sprinkle with sugar and serve hot.
