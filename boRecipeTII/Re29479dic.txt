Baking Powder Biscuits
Salt, Baking Powder, Flour, milk, Shortening, 
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt. Cut in  shortening with 2 spatulas.  Add milk. Turn onto lightly floured board.  Knead lightly and pat into sheet 1/2 inch thick. Cut with floured cutter.  Bake in hot oven (450? F) 12 minutes. 16 servings. The Household  Searchlight - 1941    Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.20
