Pecan Stuffed Mushrooms
Minced chives, Medium-sized mushrooms, Pecans, White wine, Butter, Cooked, crumbled bacon, Cream cheese, softened, Italian style bread crumbs, 
Wash and dry mushrooms. Gently separate stems from mushroom caps. (Save  stems for use in another recipe.) Brush caps with melted butter. Fill each  cap with a mixture of cream cheese, bacon, pecans, bread crumbs, chives,  and wine. Broil stuffed mushrooms for 3-5 minutes. Serve hot. Stuffed  mushrooms may be assembled the day before the party, covered, and broiled  before serving. Source: "Teasers and Appeasers", an Hors dOeuvre cookbook.
