Quick Raisin Bread
Sugar, Baking soda, Shortening; melted, Egg, Salt, Cereal flakes, Buttermilk, Flour, Baking Powder, Raisins, 
Dredge raisins in small amount of flour, sift dry ingredients together  and add raisins and cereal. Beat eggs slightly, add milk and melted  shortening, blend well with first mixture but do not overmix. Bake in  greased loaf pan 350 F. about 1 hour.    Note: Type of cereal not given, corn or bran could probably be used.    Source: Ruth Hutchinson, Marathon Grange, Clermont County, OH  Originally published in Ohio State Grange Cook Book, 21st ed., Apr 1966.
