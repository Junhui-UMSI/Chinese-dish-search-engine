Persimmon Spice Cake
Persimmon pulp, Salt, Eggs, well beaten, Sugar, Allspice, Cinnamon, Flour, Baking Powder, Corn oil, Baking soda, 
Cream sugar, oil, eggs and persimmon.  Add dry ingredients gradually. Pour  into 2 well oiled 9 inch pans. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes at 350 degrees.  Ice with carmel icing. Randy Rigg
