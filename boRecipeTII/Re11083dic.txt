Rainbow Trout with Roasted Garlic Mayonnaise
Fresh lime juice, Rainbow Trout, Vegetable oil, Cumin, Garlic, Regular Mayonnaise, Fresh Lemon Juice, Cumin, Mayonnaise, MARINADE, 
Marinate trout. Meanwhile,break garlic bulb into cloves, leaving  skins intact. Place on cookie sheet in 350 F. degree oven for about  15 minutes, until tender. Remove skins and mash garlic with fork:  blend with mayonnaise ingredients. Refrigerate. Cook trout. Serve  immediately with mayonnaise.    Makes 2 to 4 servings    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mm-trout.zip
