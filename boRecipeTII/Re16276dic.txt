Watermelon and Ginger Sharbat
Fresh lime juice, Watermelon; chilled, Crushed ice; to serve, Fresh ginger root; pureed,, Sugar; to taste, Water, 
Cut the watermelon in wedges, remove and discard the rind and seeds. Blend  the flesh in a food processor with the ginger and lime juice. Add water if  the mixture is too thick. Add extra sugar, if needed. Serve over crushed  ice.    Printed in the Atlanta Journal/Constitution, 7/10/97    >From: Ellen C.
