Tomato and Hot Green Pepper Salad, Essaouira St
cubed, Lemon jiuice, Parsley, chopped, Green bell peppers, grilled,, Tomatoes, peeled, seeded &, Chili peppers, seeded & dice, olive oil, Cumin, core removed & diced, seeded & diced, salt and pepper, Garlic cloves, chopped, Cucumber, peeled, centre, 
Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl & blend gently. Refrigerate  one hour before serving.
