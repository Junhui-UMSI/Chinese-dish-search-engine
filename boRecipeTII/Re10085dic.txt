Quebec Tourtiere
Onion; medium, chopped, salt and pepper, Cloves; ground, Water; boiling, Pork; lean ground, Savory, Pastry for 9"pie (lard is tr, 
Mix meat, onion, spices in a saucepan. Add boiling water. Simmer, uncovered  for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Skim off any fat. Preheat oven to  375F. Roll out half of the pastry and line 9" pie plate. Place filling in  pie plate and cover with the remaining pastry. Prick with a fork. Bake at  375F for 30 minutes or till golden. Serve piping hot topped with homemade  ketchup or chili sauce. SERVES 4-6 traditionally this is eaten hot after  midnight mass on Christmas Eve. Source: Harrowsmith Cookbook 1
