Pecan Delights
Vanilla extract, (14-oz) sweetened condensed, (6 oz) semisweet chocolate, Salt, Whole pecans (toasted; if, (6 oz) milk chocolate chips, Shortening, Brown sugar, Butter, Light corn syrup, 
In a large saucepan combine the first four ingredients. Cook over medium  heat until all sugar is dissolved. Gradually add milk and mix well.  Continue cooking until candy thermometer reads 248F (firm-ball stage).  Remove from the heat; stir in vanilla until blended. Fold in the pecans.  Drop by tablespoonfuls onto a waxed paper-lined cookie sheet. Chill until  firm. Melt chocolate chips and shortening in a microwave-safe bowl or  double boiler. Drizzle over each cluster. Cool.    Yield: approximately 4 dozen.    Source: Linda Jonsson, Marion, Ohio (Probably 12/96 Taste of Home?)    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #417 by "Peggy L.
