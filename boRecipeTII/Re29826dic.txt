Tender n Tangy Ribs
Pork spareribs, Pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Ketchup, Garlic, To 1 cup vinegar, Ground mustard, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Paprika, To 1 tsp. salt, 
Combine the first nine ingredients in a slow cooker. Cut ribs into  serving-size pieces; brown in skillet in oil. Transfer to slow cooker.  Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or until tender. Yield 2-3 servings.  Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Taste of Home Quick Cooking  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #027 by "Henry Ackerman2"  <henacker@shaw.wave.ca> on Jan 27, 1998
