Warm Spiced Cranberry Cocktail
Freshly grated nutmeg,, Cloves, Orange slices, 1/4-inch, Cranberry juice cocktail, Unsweetened orange juice, 
Combine first 3 ingredients in a medium saucepan; bring to a boil.    Reduce heat, and simmer, uncovered, 7 minutes. Discard cloves. Yield: 1-1/2  quarts (serving size: 1 cup).    Per serving: 185 Calories; 2g Fat (9% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 44g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 26mg Sodium    NOTES : Pour into mugs; garnish with orange wedges and nutmeg, if desired.    Recipe by: Cooking Light, Nov/Dec 1992, page 72    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #395 by igor@digex.net on Jan 28, 1997.
