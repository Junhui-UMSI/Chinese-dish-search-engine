Turkey Salad Sandwich
CELERY FRESH, TURKEY BNLS RAW FZ, LETTUCE FRESH, BREAD SNDWICH 22OZ #51, SALAD DRESSING #2 1/2, Salt, PEPPER BLACK 1 LB CN, RELISH PICKLE SWEET, 
1.  COMBINE TURKEY, CELERY, PICKLES, SALT, AND PEPPER; MIX TOGETHER LIGHTY.    2.  ADD SALAD DRESSING TO TURKEY  MIXTURE; MIX WELL REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY  TO USE.    3.  SPREAD 1 SLICE BREAD WITH 1/3 CUP (1-NO.  6 SCOOP) FILLING; TOP WITH  LETTUCE LEAF AND SECOND SLICE OF BREAD.    4.  CUT EACH SANDWICH IN HALF.  SERVE IMMEDIATELY OF REFRIGERATE UNTIL  READY  TO SERVE.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 1, 5 LB 8 OZ FRESH CELERY A.P. WILL YIELD 4 LB CHOPPED  CELERY.    NOTE:  2.  IN STEP 3, 4 LB 5 OZ FRESH LETTUCE A.P. WILL YIELD 4 LB  TRIMMED LETTUCE.    NOTE:  3.  IF THINNER SANDWICH IS DESIRED, REDUCE RECIPE FILLING QUANTITY  IN HALF.    NOTE:  4.  IN STEP 3, 13 LB 8 OZ ROUND TOP SLICED BREAD MAY BE USED FOR  SANDWICH SLICED BREAD.
