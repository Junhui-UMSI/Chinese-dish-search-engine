Kichlach
Eggs, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Flour, Salt, Poppy seeds; (optional), 
Beat eggs until light, then beat in oil, sugar, flour and salt. Beat until  very smooth. Stir in poppy seeds, if you desire. Drop by the teaspoon onto  a greased baking sheet, leaving about 3 inches between each ( they spread  and puff while baking.) Bake at 325? for 15 minutes or until browned on  edges.
