Tortilla Dip
(6-oz) ripe olives; chopped, Garlic salt, (4-oz) chopped green chiles, Salad oil, Vinegar, Green onions; chopped w/tops, Tomatoes; peeled and, 
Mix olives, chiles, onions & tomatoes. Add oil, vinegar & garlic salt. Stir  & chill. Serve with chips. Best with ripe, homegrown tomatoes.    JEANNIE TURLEY    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
