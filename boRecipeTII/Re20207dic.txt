Varicose Veins on a Leaf
Iceberg lettuce, Red food coloring, French-style green beans;, Almonds; sliced, 
Recipe by: Creepy Cuisine, Lucy Munroe       Heat the beans in their liquid according to the directions on the can.  Wash and dry the lettuce and arrange the leaves on a serving platter. Pour  the beans and liquid into a glass bowl. Add red food coloring, drop by  drop, until the liquid turns the color of blood. Place the bowl in the  refrigerator for 15 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, remove the veins and  place them in a veinlike pattern on top of the leaves. Sprinkle almonds  over the beans for and added bony crunch and flavor. Penny Halsey  (ATBN65B).
