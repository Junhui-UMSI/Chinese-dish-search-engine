Uptown Supper Salad
blue cheese, red onion, Red wine vinaigrette, potatoes, Chicken breasts, Romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, olives, 
In 3-quart saucepan over medium heat cook potatoes, covered, in 2  inches boiling water 5 minutes.  Add chicken; bring to boil, reduce  heat, cover and cook about 10 minutes until potatoes are tender and  juices run clear when chicken is pierced.  Drain  thoroughly.  Add  remaining ingredients to potatoes and chicken except lettuce and  cheese.  Toss gently over low heat just until warm.  Line platter  with lettuce; spoon mixture onto lettuce.  Top with cheese.  (Salad  also can be served chilled.)    Menu:  Sourdough Rolls, Honeydew Melon with Lime Wedges    Nutritional Information Per Serving:  280 calories; 24 g fat; 55 mg  cholesterol; 1240 mg sodium; 31 g carbohydrate; 4 g fiber; 23 g  protein.
