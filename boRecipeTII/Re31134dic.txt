Banana Split Dessert
Pecans, Sugar, Cool Whip; 9-oz, Cinnamon, Bananas; sliced, Sticks butter, (20-oz) crushed pineapple;, Chopped maraschino cherries, Graham cracker crumbs, Eggs, 
CRUST:  Combine 1-1/2 sticks butter, 1/4 cup sugar, cinnamon and graham  cracker crumbs. Press into a 9x12 dish or 2 9-inch pie plates. FILLING: Put  2 sticks butter, eggs, confectioners sugar in large mixing bowl. Beat on  highest speed for 10 minutes. Pour into crust. Layer sliced bananas and  pineapple over filling. Cover with thawed Cool Whip. Sprinkle with nuts and  cherries. Chill overnight.
