Raos Lemon Sauce
Salt and pepper; (to taste), Fresh Lemon Juice, Dried Oregano, Garlic, olive oil, Red wine vinegar, 
Whisk together juice, oil, vinegar, garlic, oregano, and salt and pepper.  Cover and refrigerate until ready to use. Whisk or shake vigorously before  using.
