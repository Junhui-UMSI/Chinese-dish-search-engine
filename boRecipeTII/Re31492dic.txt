Sweet-and-sour Chicken Soup
Lowfat chicken stock, Rice vinegar, Low sodium soy sauce, fresh ginger, garlic, Salt, white pepper, shiitake, Sliced, chicken, Spinach leaves, Somen, 
Combine first 8 ingredients in a large Dutch oven; bring to a boil. Cover,  reduce heat, and simmer 30 minutes. Stir in chicken, spinach, and noodles;  cook 5 minutes.
