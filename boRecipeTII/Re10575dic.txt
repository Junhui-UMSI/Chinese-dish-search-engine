Quick Mousse Lorange
Sugar, Orange extract, Unflavored yogurt, (6 ounces) orange juice, Ice cubes, Hot water, Envelopes unflavored gelatin, 
In blender, mix together hot water and gelatin. Add sugar and blend. Add  orange juice, yogurt, extract, and ice and blend on high until ice is  crushed. Pour into parfait glasses and chill.
