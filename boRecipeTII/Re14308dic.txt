White-Simmered Duck with Wine
Onion, Dry white wine, Salt, Duck; 6 to 7 pounds, Yellow bean paste, 
1. Wipe duck inside and out with a damp cloth and dry well with paper  toweling. Rub duck cavity with yellow bean paste. Slice onion and place  inside cavity.     2. Bring wine nearly to a boil in a large heavy pan. Add salt. Then lower  bird in gently and simmer, covered, over the lowest possible heat until  done (about 4 hours). Use an asbestos pad to keep heat to a minimum.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
