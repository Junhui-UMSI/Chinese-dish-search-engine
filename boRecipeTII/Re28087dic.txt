Baked Clams Casino
garlic, Lemon, Fine soft breadcrumbs, olive oil, clams, Paprika, Chopped, bacon, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Toss together bread crumbs, paprika, olive  oil and garlic.  Place clams in a flat baking dish.  Mount some of  the bread crumb mixture on each clam. Cut bacon slices into quarters  and place a piece on each clam.  Bake 25 minutes, or until bacon is  crisp. If desired, the clams may be served on a bed of heated rock  salt.
