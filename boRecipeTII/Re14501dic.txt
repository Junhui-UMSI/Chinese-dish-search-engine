"Better Than Maries" Cornbread
Yellow cornmeal, Sugar, milk, Eggs, Margarine, Buttermilk Baking Mix, 
Combine baking mix, cornmeal and sugar in a mixing bowl. Add eggs and milk.  Beat until well blended. Carefully stir in melted margarine (it may  splatter). Pour into greased 9 in. square pan. Bake at 350 for 35-40  minutes or until done. Serves 6.    Posted to T.nt (sdm-marked) Recipes Digest by Lisa <byhisgrc@lightspeed.net> on Mar 24,  1998
