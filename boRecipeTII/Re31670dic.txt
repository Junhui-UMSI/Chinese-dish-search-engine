Swiss Spaghetti
Pepper, Spaghetti, cooked, Green pepper, chopped, Ground beef, Swiss cheese, cubed, Tomatoes, canned, Salt, Onion, medium chopped, Swiss cheese, grated, Butter, 
In saucepan melt butter and saute onion and green pepper, about 5 minutes.  Add beef and cook over high heat until brown. Add tomatoes, salt and  pepper. Cover and simmer 1 hour. Add cubed cheese and cook 5 minutes. Add  spaghetti and toss. Serve with grated cheese.
