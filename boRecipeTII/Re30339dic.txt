Banana Coconut Rum Cream Liqueur
Ripe bananas; mashed (about, Coconut extract, Sweetened condensed milk, Rum, Cream of coconut, Vodka, Evaporated milk, 
Mash bananas and blend in blender with coconut extract, rum, and vodka. Add  milks and blend at low speed for one minute. Add cream of coconut or  coconut milk and pulse stir for one minute (use lowest speed on blender and  turn on-off eight times.) Makes about four cups.
