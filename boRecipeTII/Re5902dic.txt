Pissadella
Onion, sliced paper thin, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Tiny genovese onions, Capers, rinsed and drained, Recipe basic pizza bread, Basic tomato sauce, Salt packed anchovy fillets,, 
Preheat oven to 415 degrees F. Follow instructions for 1 large pizza, but  form dough into large rectangle.    Lightly oil a large (16 inch by 30 inch) cookie sheet, or 2 smaller (11  inch by 17 inch) sheets.    Using a rolling pin or your hands, flatten dough to fit in your cookie  sheet. Using finger tips, poke indentations across entire surface. Spread a  thin layer of tomato sauce over surface. Sprinkle with olives. Lay anchovy  fillets out into a pattern. Sprinkle with onions, capers, and drizzle with  extra virgin olive oil. Place in oven and bake 15 to 20 minutes, until  golden brown on bottom. Remove and serve hot or cold.    Yield: 4 servings    Recipe by: MOLTO MARIO    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #506 by Sue <suechef@sover.net> on Mar 08,  1997.
