Baked Sea Bass, Portuguese Style
onions, Sea bass fillets, yellow mustard, Salt, garlic, Fresh Lemon Juice, White wine vinegar, Ground coriander, Dry white wine, Flour, olive oil, Dried parsley, 
Sprinkle fish with salt; lightly dust with flour. Arrange fish in  shallow 8*12" baking dish. Saute onions in olive oil in skillet  until limp; place over fish.  Combine wine vinegar, garlic, oregano,  mustard, parsley, coriander & lemon juice; mix well & pour over fish.  Pour wine around fish; bake, uncovered, in 350 oven for about 45  minutes. Pollock or halibut may be used.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
