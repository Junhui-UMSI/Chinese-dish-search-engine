Tomato Pork Chops
Rice, Water, Onion, Porkchops, Tomatoes, 
Take a 9 x 13 glass baking dish and cover the bottom with the rice. Lay the  pork chops on top of the rice.  Slice the Onion and Tomato and lay on top  of the porkchops.  Pour the water into pan. The water should cover pork  chops.  Cook at 350 F until the pork chops are done (about 30 min).    ~- nell@is.rice.edu These statements do not reflect Rices opinions. Just  mine.
