Sweet Mutton Hot Pot W/dumplings
Turnip; chopped, Carrots; chopped, Parsley, Vegetable stock, broth or, DUMPLINGS, Redcurrant jelly, . water, Mushrooms; sliced, Parsnip; chopped & blanched, Neck of mutton or lamb, Flour; self-rising, Onions; chopped, Suet; shredded, 
Set oven to 375 ?or Mark 5.  Put the pieces of meat in Ihe bottom of a  large casserole.  Spread them with the redcurrant jelly and place in the  oven for 15 minutes.  Remove and add the chopped vegetables and a little  salt and pepper. Stir the tomato puree into the stock or water. Pour over  the meat and vegetables.  Return to the oven. Reduce the heat to 350F or  Mark 4 and cook for about 1 1/2 hours until the meat is tender.    Dumplings: mix together the flour, suet, parsley and seasoning with enough  water to form a stiff dough.  This should make about six small dumplings.  Add the dumplings to the hot pot for the last 30 minutes of cooking. Serve  with buttered, mashed potatoes and a green vegetable. Serves 4 - 6.
