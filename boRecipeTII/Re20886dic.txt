Apple Muffins #3
Brown sugar, Vanilla, Vegetable oil, Diced apples, Sugar, Salt, Pecans, Butter, Buttermilk, Baking soda, Egg, Flour, 
From:    Tina Rogers <CAJUNT@UGA.CC.UGA.EDU>    Date:    Sun, 11 Aug 1996 07:01:21 EDT  Combine brown sugar, oil and egg.  In another bowl combine buttermilk,  soda, salt and vanilla. Add this mixture to sugar mixture and alternate  with flour. Mix well after each addition. Fold in diced apples and chopped  pecans. Pour into muffin tins and sprinkle with 1/3 cup sugar combined with  1 tbsp butter. Bake at 325 for 30 minutes. Yields: 2 dozen    EAT-L Digest 10 August 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
