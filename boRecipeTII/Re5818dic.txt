Pinto Pizza Ole
Tomato sauce, Cooked Idaho Pinto Beans, Chopped onion, divided, Salt, Frozen bread dough; thawed, Red pepper flakes, Guacamole; (recipe below), Chili powder, Green Pepper, Ground beef, divided, Cumin, to taste, Grated Monterey Jack, 
To make this pizza meatless, omit the beef and increase the beans  to 3 cups, mashing 2 cups.    Mash 1 cup of the beans; set aside remaining cup. Brown beef with 1/2  cup onion in large skillet. Stir in mashed beans, tomato sauce,  chili powder, cumin and salt. Simmer 10 minutes. Pat or roll dough  to cover bottom of greased 14-inch pizza pan. Spread with bean-beef  mixture. Top with remaining onion, green pepper, reserved whole beans  and cheese. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes. Bake in pre-heated  400-degree F oven for 15 minutes or until crust is  browned.  Cut into wedges. Top each serving with a dollop of guacamole.    Guacamole: Mash 1 medium avocado with 1 clove minced garlic and 1  tablespoon lemon juice. Add liquid red pepper sauce and salt to  taste. Stir in 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes. Cover with plastic and chill  until ready to serve.
