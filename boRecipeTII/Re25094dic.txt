Tomato, Chicken, and Mushroom Soup
Dressing mix, Envelopes, Good seasons italian salad, Mushrooms -- sliced, Tomato sauce, Butter, Minute rice, Chicken broth, Cooked chicken -- diced, Stewed tomatoes, 
Cook and stir mushrooms in hot butter; gradually stir in broth, then add  chicken, tomatoes, tomato sauce, and salad dressing mix.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer 10 minutes.  Stir in rice; cover and remove  from heat.  Let stand at least 5 minutes.    Recipe By     : Ed from McPherson    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
