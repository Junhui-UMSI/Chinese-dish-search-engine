Anglesey Cake
Mixed fruit, milk, Sugar, Egg, Self-raising flour, Pinch salt, Butter, 
TEISEN SIR FON Cream the butter and the sugar. Beat the egg and beat into  the butter and sugar mixture. Sift the flour and salt together and fold  into the mixture. Add the fruit and enough milk to give a soft dropping  consistency. Place in a greased tin in a moderately hot oven (375/F. or  Mark 5) for 45 minutes.
