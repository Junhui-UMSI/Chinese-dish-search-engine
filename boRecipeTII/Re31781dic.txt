Szagoleves (Sage Soup)
Cinnamon, Lemon rind; grated, Sugar, granulated, Lemon juice, Sage; minced, Wine, red, dry, 
Boil the sage and lemon rind in 3 cups of water for about 30 minutes.  Strain. Add sugar, lemon juice to taste, and wine. Bring to a boil.    Put the cinnamon stick in a soup tureen and pour soup over. Serve in  cups.      Note: This is a spiced drink as much as a soup, from Gyula Vasvary,  master chef of the 1820s.
