Apricot Glaze
Sugar, Salad oil, Apricot halves,drained(17oz), Salt, White vinegar, Cloves,ground, 
1. In blender at medium speed or in food processor with knife blade  attached, blend apricot halves, sugar, oil, vinegar, salt, and cloves until  smooth. Pour into small bowl.
