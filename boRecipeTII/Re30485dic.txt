Banana Nut Bread #2
Eggs, Bananas; mashed, Chopped nuts (walnuts or, Stick butter or margarine, (scant) sugar, Flour, Baking soda, 
Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs and blend well.  Sift flour and soda. Add  to egg mixture. Stir in mashed bananas and nuts. Pour into greased loaf pan  and bake at 350 for one hour and 15 minutes.  Cool at least 20 minutes  before removing and slicing.    A NO-YEAST BREAD THAT IS    QUICK AND EASY.    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of Contemporary Recipes>.
