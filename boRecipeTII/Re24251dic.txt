Tri-Level Brownies
Egg, Oats, rolled (raw) -- quick, Unsweetened baking chocolate, All-purpose flour, Brown sugar, Hot water, Margarine -- melted, Cooled, Walnuts -- chopped, Baking soda, milk, Unsweetened baking chocolate, Squares, Cooking, All-purpose flour, Powdered sugar -- sifted, Margarine -- melted, Squares -- melted and, Baking Powder, Fudge Frosting-----, Vanilla, Margarine, Sugar, 
For bottom layer, stir together first 4 ingredients and 1/4 teaspoon salt.  Stir in 6 tablespoons melted margarine.  Pat in 11x7-1/2x1-1/2-inch baking  pan.  Bake at 350F for 10 minutes. For middle layer, combine granulated  sugar, 1/4 cup butter, and chocolate; add egg. Beat well. Stir together 2/3  cup flour, baking powder, and 1/4 teaspoon salt; add to chocolate mixture  alternately with a mixture of the milk and vanilla, mixing after each  addition.  Fold in nuts. Spread batter over baked layer. Continue baking at  350F for 25 minutes. Cool. Frost with Fudge Frosting. Cut into bars. Top  with walnut halves, if desired. Fudge Frosting: In small saucepan melt one  1-ounce square unsweetened chocolate and 2 tablespoons butter or margarine  over low heat; stir constantly.  Remove from heat; stir in 1 1/2 cups  sifted powdered sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla.  Blend in hot water (about 2  tablespoons) to make almost pourable consistency.
