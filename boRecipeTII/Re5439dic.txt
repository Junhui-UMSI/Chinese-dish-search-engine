Pineapple Coconut Pie (Amish Cooking)
Cornstarch, Light corn syrup, Sugar, Eggs, crushed pineapple, Shredded coconut, Butter, 
Beat the eggs slightly, then add the remaining ingredients. Blend the  mixture well, then pour it into a 9-inch unbaked pie shell. Bake the pie at  350 oF for 45 to 50 minutes or until it is slightly set.
