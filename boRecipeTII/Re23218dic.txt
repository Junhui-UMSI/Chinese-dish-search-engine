Tuna Hamachi Caviar Spoons
ahi tuna, Salt and pepper, olive oil, Platter, Hamachi, Caviar, GARNISH, teaspoons, chives, TABASCO pepper sauce, 
In two chilled bowls, mix each fish separately with half the amount of  Tabasco pepper sauce, chives and oil. Season with salt and pepper. On the  chilled spoons, use a small amount of each mixture to build a yin/yang  effect. Top with the caviar. Serve on the platter with rock salt.
