Turkey Strips with Artichokes
Salt, Noodles, cooked, Flour, Turkey breast, sliced, Paprika, Pepper, Onion, sliced, Chiken broth, Butter, And pounded, Grated Parmesan cheese, Lemon juice, Marinated artichoke hearts, Sour cream, 
Combine flour, salt, pepper and paprika.  Coat meat slices with it. Pour  artichoke liquid into a frying pan and cook on medium heat until bubbly.  Add meat slices and brown 3-5 minutes; remove from pan. Add onion and  butter to pan. Saute 5 minutes. Add broth and stir in lemon juice, cheese,  and sour cream. Add turkey and heat through. Serve over noodles. Garnish  with artichoke hearts.
