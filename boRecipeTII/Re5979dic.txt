Pistou with Pepitas
Bunch fresh basil; washed,, Pumpkin seeds; lightly, Garlic, olive oil, Grated Parmesan cheese (Or, 
With the machine running, drop garlic through the feed tube of a food  processor. Add pumpkin seeds and basil leaves and process until pureed. Add  a little water if necessary to get the mixture going. Turn off machine, add  cheese, then turn machine on and pour olive oil in slowly through feed tube  until oil is thoroughly incorporated and a smooth paste is formed. Serve on  top of soup.
