Asparagus Casserole
Cheese slices, Crackers; crushed, Asparagus with juice, Evaporated milk; add more if, 
Directions : pour asparagus with juice into a pie plate, add crushed  crackers and 1/2 cup of evaporated milk. cook at 350 degrees for 30 min.  stir twice during the 30 min. then place cheese slices on top and put back  into oven until cheese melts  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 460 by Bunny  <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Jan 6, 1998
