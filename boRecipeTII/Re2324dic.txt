Basil Tofu and Vegetable Sandwich
extra firm tofu, basil, Garlic, Tomato, Romaine leaves, green onions, Salt, black pepper, Whole-wheat bread, Red onion, carrot, Alfalfa sprouts, Lemon juice, 
Published in the Los Angeles Times on September 2, 1998, in the Low Fat  Cooking column written by Donna Deane.    1. Drain tofu and pat dry with paper towel. Mash tofu with fork in bowl.  Stir in basil, green onions, garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper.    2. Spread 2 tablespoons tofu mixture on each of 6 slices bread. Top each  with 2 tomato slices, 1 red onion slice, 1/2 cup shredded carrot, 2 romaine  leaves, 1/2 cup alfalfa sprouts and l slice bread.    Each Serving 202 calories; 484 mg sodium; 2 mg cholesterol; 4 grams fat; 34  grams carbohydrates; 12 grams protein; 2.02 grams fiber    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Harriet Neal <queenbe@earthlink.net> on Sep  07, 1998,
