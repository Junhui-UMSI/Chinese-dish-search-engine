Chocolate Chiffon Cake
Flour, Cream of tartar, Baking soda, Sugar, Cocoa, Vegetable oil, Eggs; divided, Water; minus 2 Tbs, Vanilla, Salt, 
Source: Marcy Sher    Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Sift together the flour, 2 tsp cream of  tartar, baking soda, sugar and cocoa in a large bowl. Make a well in the  dry ingredients. Add oil, egg yolks, water and vanilla. Mix well. In a  chilled bowl beat egg whites, salt and 1/2 tsp cream of tartar until stiff.  Fold into the mixture in large bowl. Fill ungreased chiffon pan. Bake 65  minutes.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Linda Shapiro <lss@coconet.com> on Sep 10,  1998,
