Portuguese Sweet Bread 2
Butter or margarine, Bread or all purpose flour, Water, Lemon extract or, Instant potato flakes (or, Vanilla extract, Salt, Dry yeast, Sugar, Rind of 1/2 lemon; grated, Egg, Egg white; lightly beaten, Sweetened condensed milk, Nutmeg, 
Place all ingredients, except egg white in machine on basic dough cycle per  manufacturers instructions. After Dough Cycle beep, remove dough to  floured cutting board. Grease 8 or 9 inch pie tin. Gently roll dough into a  30 inch rope and starting at one end, gently twist the roll to the opposite  end to form a coil. Place one end in center of pie tin and carefully wrap  coil around itself, from the center, filling the pan. Cover and let rise in  warm oven for 1 hour or until doubled. (Turn oven to 150F for 2 to 5  minutes and turn it off. Place covered dough on low rack, in center to  rise). Remove bread from oven and preheat to 325F. Brush dough with egg  white. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes until brown. Remove and cool.
