Apple Fritters
Self-rising flour, Sugar, milk, Apple,finely chopped,, Vanilla, Orange juice, Egg, Hot oil, 
From "Hill Country Cookin and Memoirs" by Ibbie Ledford, Pelican  Publishing Company.    Beat egg. Add sugar. Slowly stir in milk and orange juice. Blend in  apple, vanilla and flour. Drop mixture by teaspooonfuls into hot oil.  Fry until brown on both sides, about 3 minutes per side. Drain in  colander or on paper towels. Dust with confectioners sugar. Makes 4  dozen.
