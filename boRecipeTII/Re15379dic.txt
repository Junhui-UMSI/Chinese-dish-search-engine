White Cake
Eggs, Vanilla, Salt, Sugar, Shortening, milk, Flour, Baking Powder, 
Mix dry ingredients together. In a seperate bowl mix shortening, sugar and  eggs; add vanilla. Add dry ingredients and milk alternately and beat until  smooth. Pour into a 8" x 8" well greased cake pan. Bake for 45 minutes at      350    F.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 13 Feb 97 by Janine Vandenberg <jvandenb@KENT.NET>  on Feb 14, 1997.
