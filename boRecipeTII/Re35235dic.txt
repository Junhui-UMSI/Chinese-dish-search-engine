Beef and Mushroom Roll
Parsley, Flour, Salt, Green pepper, Celery, Ground beef, Baking Powder, Mushrooms, garlic, Crescent rolls, CRUST ROLLS, Poultry seasoning, Onion, 
~-------------------------------------Pastry:---------- ~-- Place the  ground beef in a skillet.  Cook, stiring with a fork until brown. Add salt,  pepper, mushrooms, onion, garlic, parsley, celety, green pepper. Blend well  and simmer with 1/3 cup water for about 10 minutes. Cool. Make dough or get  rolls ready.  Roll out ans for a jelly roll, place meat on 1/3 and roll,  pinch end edges together. Place on greased shallow pan and brush with milk.  Bake at 425 for 30 minutes.
