Cornish Hen Stuffing
Cornish hens, Half cooked rice, Soaked raisins, Pinola nuts or pine nuts;, Chopped parsley or cilantro, salt and pepper, Rosemary, Egg, 
mix all together with 1 egg as binder. stuff birds. smear birds with a  mixture of oil, paprika, garlic powder, white pepper (salt if not kosher  birds). cook 350 for an hour or till golden brown and crispy. thats all    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by eddie strulovits <wesfire@yahoo.com> on Oct  21, 1998,
