Pinion Candy
Water, Brown sugar, Sugar, White corn syrup, Evaporated milk diluted with, Pinons (pine nuts), shelled, Vanilla, 
Mix all of the ingredients but the nuts. Cook to the soft ball stage. Cool.  Add the nut meats and beat until it is soft and creamy. When the mixture  begins to stiffen drop rapidly from spoon onto a buttered sheet. Press into  patties. Candy should stiffen immediately.
