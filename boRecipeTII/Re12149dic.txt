Zucchini and Parmesan Pie
Chopped parsley, Vegetable oil, zucchini, Salt, Pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, Nutmeg, onion, Bisquick, Eggs, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease (with butter) a 9x13 inch pan. Combine all  ingredients except eggs and oil. Beat eggs and oil together, then mix into  zucchini mixture. Pour into pan and bake for 25 minutes or until center  sets. Cool 10 minutes before cutting.
