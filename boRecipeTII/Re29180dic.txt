Baked Sausage and Macaroni Casserole
elbow macaroni, Butter, Tomatoes, Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, Flour, Onion, Green sweet pepper, Venison sausage, milk, celery, 
Notes: By Curtis Osman, Weatherly. The Times News, PA    Brown sausage in water, dice. Add butter, onion, pepper, and celery; saute.  Add milk, flour, cheddar cheese. Saute all. Add to cooked macaroni and 2  cans tomatoes and sausage. Top with Parmesan cheese. Bake at 350? for 25 to       30    minutes.
