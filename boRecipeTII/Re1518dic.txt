Broccoli Cheddar Cheese Soup
beef broth, Cream of celery soup, milk, White wine, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic powder, Salt, Black pepper, broccoli, Cheddar Cheese, parsley, 
Add first 8 ingredients into pan. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and  simmer for 20 minutes or until broccoli is beginning to become tender. Make  sure that broccoli is cut into bite size pieces. When broccoli is done to  proper tenderness, slowly add cheddar cheese, sting constantly until each  bit is completely blended into soup.    Before serving, garnish with a sprinkle of parsley or dried or fresh dill,  alongside some crusty French bread.    Recipe by Mangia!
