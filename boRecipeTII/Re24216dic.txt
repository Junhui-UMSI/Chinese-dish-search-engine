Treasured Toffee Bars
Margarine, Flour, Salt, Brown sugar, Vanilla, Shortening, M&Ms Plain Chocolate Candy, Egg yolk, 
Beat together margarine and sugar until light and fluffy; blend in egg yolk  and vanilla. Add flour and salt; mix well. Spread dough onto bottom of  greased 15 x 10 jelly roll pan. Bake at 350 for 25 to 30 minutes or until  lightly browned. Melt shortening in 2 qt saucepan; add candies. Cook over  very low heat, stirring constantly with back of spoon to break up. Spread  over warm bars. Chill about 30 minutes to set chocolate. Cool at room  temperature; cut into bars.
