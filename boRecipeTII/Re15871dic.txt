Achiote Marinated Shrimp Salad
Reserved shrimp shells, Diced onion, Head frisee, Toasted ground cumin, Tomato, diced, Toasted ground coriander, Oranges, supremed, (21 to 25 pieces) shrimp,, Garlic, Toasted almonds, Shallots, Lime juice, Water, Scallions, sliced on, Ginger, Vegetable stock, Ancho chili powder, Mache, Olive oil, plus 2, White wine, ACHIOTE PASTE, Almonds, slivered, Red pepper, chopped, Ground annatto seeds, 
For the paste: In a blender or food processor, puree first 9 ingredients  and then slowly add the lemon juice and 1/4 cup oil.    For stock: Heat a heavy bottomed pot, when smoking add shrimp shells and  cook, stirring for 6 to 8 minutes or until shells are toasted. Add wine and  reduce by half. Add onion, tomato, and water bring just to a boil and then  reduce to a simmer. Simmer slowly for 45 minutes and strain.    Cooking shrimp: Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a skillet. Mix 2 tablespoons  achiote paste into hot oil. Add 1/2 of shrimp to hot pan and stir to coat  evenly with the paste. Add 1/2 cup shrimp stock and cook for 5 minutes or  until the shrimp are no longer translucent. Set aside in a bowl. Repeat  process for other half.    Assembly: Place the mache and frisee divided among 6 plates. In a medium  size bowl, toss the shrimp with the orange sections, and scallions. Divide  shrimp among plates. Top with scallions and toasted almonds. Shrimp can be  served hot or cold.
