Pepper Balls
Eggs, Allspice, Ground, Black Pepper, As Desired, Baking Powder, Cloves, Ground, Flour, Unbleached, Unsifted, Mace, Butter, (No Margarine), Vanilla, Cinnamon, Water, Honey, 
Sift flour, baking powder and spices together.  Heat honey and butter until  butter melts.  Cool to lukewarm and beat in eggs. Add flour mixture. Chill  dough 1/2 hour. Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Place on greased cookie  sheet.  Bake at 350F. for 15 minutes. Cool Cookies on wire racks. Mix  confectioners sugar, vanilla, and water to form a thin glaze. Dip cookies  in glaze and place on wire rack to dry. Store cookies in airtight tins.  Makes 4 dozen cookies.
