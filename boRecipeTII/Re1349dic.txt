Easy Cornbread
(8.5 ounce) packages corn, Muffin mix, 8 ounce can cream-style, Corn, Eggs, OR 4 egg whites, OR equivalent egg, Substitute, Plain, nonfat yogurt, Shredded, reduced fat, Cheddar cheese, To 1/2 cup low fat milk, 
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and stir until just moistened. Pour into  a lightly greased 13"x9"x2" pan. Bake at 400F for 17-20 minutes or until  golden.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Aug  17, 1998
