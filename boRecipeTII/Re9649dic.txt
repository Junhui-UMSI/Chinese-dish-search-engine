Pumpkin Mousse with Gingersnap Crumbs
Box silken tofu; (soft, Ground ginger, Lemon zest; grate, Fresh lemon juice; or more, Ground cloves, Can solid-pack pumpkin or, Vanilla extract, Cinnamon, Salt to taste, 2" gingersnaps; crush, Fresh; cooked pumpkin, Maple syrup, 
*Available in the refrigerated section of supermarkets. Place the tofu,  maple syrup, lemon zest, lemon juice, vanilla, cinnamon, ginger, cloves and  salt in a blender. Puree until smooth. Add half the pumpkin and puree  again. Transfer to a medium-sized bowl and beat in the remaining pumpkin  with a whisk until the mixture becomes creamy. Taste to see if it needs  more lemon juice. Cover tightly and chill for several hours or overnight,  so the flavors combine. To serve, spoon the mousse into bowls and sprinkle  with gingersnap crumbs. Serve immediately. 242 cal; 7 gr fat; 24% fat.  Source: Mollie Katzens Vegetable Heaven, by Mollie Katzen. The Miami  Herald. MM Waldine Van Geffen vghc42a@prodigy.com.
