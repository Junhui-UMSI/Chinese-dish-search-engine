Peanut Pie
Whipped cream, Vanilla extract, Sugar, Eggs, Dark corn syrup, Salted peanuts, Peanut butter, Cream Cheese Pie Pastry,, 
From "A Taste of the South" by Terry Thompson (HP Books; 1988).    Beat eggs, corn syrup, sugar, peanut butter and vanilla until smooth  and thickened, about 4 minutes. Stir in peanuts and blend.    Roll out pastry to fit 9" pie pan. Pour in filling. Bake at 400F. 15  minutes, then reduce heat to 350F. and bake until knife inserted in  center comes out clean, 30-35 minutes. Cool completely before  serving. Top each slice with whipped cream. Makes 8 servings.    Each serving contains about: 583 calories; 118 milligrams sodium; 122  milligrams cholesterol; 34 grams fat; 62 grams carbohydrates; 13 grams  protein; 1.38 grams fiber.
