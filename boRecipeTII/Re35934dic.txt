Stuffed Okra 2
Mango powder (amchur), Ground ginger, Vegetable oil, Paprika, Okra, Chilli powder, Salt, 
Wash the okra and dry on absorbent kitchen paper. Cut off the stalks and  slice the okra in half lengthways.    Mix together the spices and salt.    Stuff the okra with the spice mix and leave them to stand in a cool place  for at least 30 minutes.    Heat the oil and fry the okra for about 4 minutes, turning carefully to  cook on all sides    Source: The Complete Indian Cookbook  Compiled: Imran C.
