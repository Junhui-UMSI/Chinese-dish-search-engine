Pepperoni Pizza Dip
Mozzarella cheese, Garlic powder, Green onion, PATTI  VDRJ67A, Pizza sauce, Cream cheese, Sour cream, Oregano, Pepperoni, 
Beat together cream cheese, sour cream, oregano and garlic powder. Spread  in a 9 or 10" pie plate. Spread pizza sauce over cheese mix. Sprinkle with  pepperoni and green onions. Bake at 350~ for 10 minutes. Top with Mozarella  Cheese. Bake for 5 more minutes. Serve warm.
