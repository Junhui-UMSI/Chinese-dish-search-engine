Beans with Tuna
Chopped Tomatoes, salt and pepper, olive oil, Sprigs basil; finely chopped, Garlic, Small white beans; soaked, (6-1/2oz) white tuna in, 
Saute garlic in oil until brown; discard garlic. Combine oil with beans and  1 1/2 quarts water in crock pot. Cover and cook on high 2 hours. Turn heat  to low, cover and cook 8 hours. Add remaining ingredients, cover and cook  on high for 30 minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 403 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Dec 26, 1997
