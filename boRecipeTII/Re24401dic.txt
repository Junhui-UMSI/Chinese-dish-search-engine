Tropical Bulgar
Romaine lettuce leaves;, Red (cayenne) pepper, Bulgur;, Fresh lime juice;, Chopped fresh parsley;, Green Onions, Ground cumin;, Red bell pepper, chopped, Dried leaf oregano;, Vegetable oil;, Boiling water;, Salt;, 
Place bulgar in a small bowl and cover with boiling water.  Let it  stand 30 minutes until all liquid absorbed.  In a small bowl, mix  oil, lime juice, cumin, oregano, salt and cayenne.  Add to bulgur and  mix with remaining ingredients except lettuce; fluff with a fork.  Refrigerate until chilled. Serve on bed of lettuce leaves.
