Popeyes Red Beans and Rice
Van geffen vghc42a, Cumin, Red chili beans in chili , Chili powder, garlic salt, 
In saucepan heat beans without letting it boil and stir in chili powder,  cumin and garlic salt. When piping hot, spoon chili mixture into 6 small  dishes, adding a few tb of hot, cooked rice to each serving. Season with  Chili Seasoning Mix, if desired. 270 cal, 5.9 gr fat, 60% fat. Source:  Gloria Pitzer, Bob Allisons Ask Your Neighbor Newsletter (wrv)
