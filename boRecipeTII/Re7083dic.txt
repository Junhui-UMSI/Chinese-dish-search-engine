Poppy Seed Cookies
Baking Powder, Flour (or more), Poppy seeds, Vegetable oil, Grated orange rind, Sugar, Eggs, Juice of 1 orange, 
Cream sugar and eggs.  Add oil and juice and then poppy seeds. Mix  thoroughly.  Add dry ingredients gradually, mixing well. Roll out a small  amount at a time on a well floured board, to 1/8th inch thickness. Cut with  cookie cutter or glass into desired shapes. Place on greased cookie sheet  and bake in a 350 F. oven for 10 to 15 min or until nicely browned.
