Teriyaki Almond Nuts
Brown sugar, Salt, Ginger, Vegetable oil, Garlic powder, Nuts, Soy sauce, Lemon juice, Onion powder, 
Place nuts in a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan. Bake 5 minutes at 325 degrees.  In a bowl, combine soy sauce, lemon juice and brown sugar. Stir until sugar  is dissolved. Stir in oil, ginger, salt, garlic and onion powder. Add  heated almonds. Toss to coat nuts. Return nuts to baking pan. Bake 10  minutes longer, turning once. Cool and store in an airtight container.
