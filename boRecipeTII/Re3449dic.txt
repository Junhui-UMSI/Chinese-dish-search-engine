Pecan Cookies
Flour, Water, Butter, Powdered sugar, Pecan pieces, 
Soften butter and mix with electric mixer all ingredients except the  pecans.  Add pecans and spoon onto unbuttered cookie sheet. Bake at 350  degrees for 20 minutes. Frosting: Using powdered sugar, milk and a little  vanilla, mix for a drizzling consistency. Drizzle over cookies. Randy Rigg
