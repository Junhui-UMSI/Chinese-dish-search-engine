Sweet and Sour Puff Batter And Sauce
Green pepper, Pinapple chunks, Flour, Sugar, Sweet and Sour Sauce:, Salt, Scallions, Cornstarch, Cold water, milk, Carrot, Baking Powder, Veg oil, Pinaple juice, 
Dip chunks of chicken or fish in batter and fry in hot oil until golden.  Drain and set aside. For the sauce combine all ingredients except peppers,  cornstarch and water. Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer 10 min. Add  green pepper and simmer 2 more min. Combine water and cornstarch and  thicken mixture stirring well. Pour over chicken or fish. Serve with rice.
