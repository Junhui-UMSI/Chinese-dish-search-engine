Apple Sauce (Cooks Choice)
Macintosh apples, Sugar, Water, Butter, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, 
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and cook over medium heat until  apples become very tender, about 30 minutes. Applesauce may be left lumpy  or pureed with a handblender to become smoother.
