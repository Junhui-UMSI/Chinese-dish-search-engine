Peanut Butter Fudge Cookies
Eggs, Fudge brownie mix, Semisweet chocolate chips, Vegetable oil, Chunky peanut butter, Water, Chopped unsalted peanuts, 
Preheat oven to 350. In a large bowl, beat peanut butter, oil and eggs. Add  brownie mix and water; stir until moistened. Stir in chocolate chips. drop  Tbsp of dough onto an ungreased baking sheet. Place 1/2 tsp peanuts on each  cookie. Bake 12 to 14 minutes. Cool completely. Store in airtight  container. Source: Quick Gifts of Good Taste    Recipe by Weston F.
