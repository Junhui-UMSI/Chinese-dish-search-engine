Sweet Spice Mix
Dried orange/lemon peel, Cinnamon, Ground cloves, Ground allspice, Ground ginger, Nutmeg, 
Orange peel should be grated.  Sprinkle on plain toast, French toast,  waffles, pancakes, oatmeal, vanilla pudding, cut-up apples or oranges.  Sprinkle over muffins before baking and apple pie before warming.    Buy spices in bulk from a natural foods store.
