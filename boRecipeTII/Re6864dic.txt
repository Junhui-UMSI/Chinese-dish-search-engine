Polynesian Tuna
Water, Tuna, Pineapple chunks, almonds, Flaked coconut, Cornstarch, Pineapple juice, Fluffy cooked rice, 
In a heavy saucepan, combine pineapple juice, water, and cornstarch (when  youve mixed with a little of the water first). Cook over low heat until  thickened. Add pineapple chunks and tuna and reheat and simmer for 5 to 10  minutes.  Serve over fluffy rice and top each serving with a sprinkling of  coconut and slivered almonds.    From:  The Tuna Cookbook
