Peanut-Oatmeal Drops
Slat, Peanut butter, Oats, Quick-cooking Rolled, Brown Sugar, Flour; Unbleached, Baking soda, milk, Vanilla, Peanuts; Chopped, Sugar; Granulated, Shortening, Eggs; Large, 
Cream together shortening, peanut butter, and sugars until light and  fluffy; add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each.  Blend in milk  and vanilla.  Thoroughly stir together flour, soda, and salt; stir into  creamed mixture.  Stir in oats and peanuts.  Drop from teaspoon 2 inches  apart on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees f. for 10 to 12  minutes.    Makes 6 dozen.    From Better Homes & Gardens Homemade Cookies Cookbook, via The Home BBS.
