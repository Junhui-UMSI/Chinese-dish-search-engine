The Chefs Special Butter
Salt, Parsley, Lemon juice, Butter, White pepper, 
1. Cream butter until fluffy.    2. Beat in next 3 ingredients; add lemon juice drop by drop.    3. Store in covered jar in the refrigerator.    4. Add a teaspoon butter to each steak after broiling.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 200 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 4, 1997
