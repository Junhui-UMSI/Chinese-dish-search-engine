Veal Paprika
Chicken broth,divided use, Onion,finely chopped, Sour cream, Salt, Dash caraway seed, Pepper, Paprika, Boneless veal stew meat, Fresh mushrooms,sliced, Flour, 
In a 2 qt. casserole,combine veal,mushrooms,1/2 cup broth,onion,  paprika,salt,pepper and caraway seeds.Cover with lid or plastic  wrap.Microwave on high 7 minutes;stir.Stirring midway through  cooking,microwave on 50% (medium) 21 minutes. Blend flour with remaining  1/2 cup broth until smooth.Stir into casserole.Microwave on high 2 to 3  minutes or until sauce thickens. Blend in sour cream;let stand 2  minutes.Serve over cooked noodles and sprinkle with additional paprika or  chopped fresh parsley.Makes 4 servings.
