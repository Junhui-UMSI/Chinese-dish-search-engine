Potluck Baked Beans
Brown sugar, Ketchup, Green bell pepper, ground beef, Honey baked beans  , baked beans  , Liquid Barbecue Smoke, Onions, White vinegar, kidney beans  , 
Mix all ingredients together and cook in a crockpot on low for two hours.  This serves a small army and is excellent!
