Peanut Butterscotch Squares
Flour, Light corn syrup, Butter or oleo, Brown sugar, Chunk-styled peanut butter, Corn flakes, 6 oz pkg butterscotch pieces, Sugar, 
Cream oleo or butter and brown sugar. Stir in flour (mixture will be  crunchy..will have to use your hands. Press in ungreased 13x9x2 in. baking  pan. Bake in 350F oven for 15 minutes. In saucepan, combine sugar and corn  syrup; heat to boiling. Remove from heat. Add butterscotch pieces and  peanut butter; stir till chips are melted, Stir in corn flakes. Spread over  baked layer. Cool; cut into squares.
