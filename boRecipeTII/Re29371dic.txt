Baked Turkey Chiles Rellenos
Green chili, Monterey jack cheese, Turkey, Flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Skim milk, Eggs, Cheddar cheese, 
Drain and rinse the chiles. Cut them up one side, seed them and open them  flat. Arrange chiles in a shallow baking dish lightly coated with vegetable  cooking spray.   Cut the cheese and turkey into 1/2-inch strips. Fill each  chile half with cheese and turkey strips. Fold over edges of chiles and  place seam-side down in dish.   In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking  powder and salt. In a small bowl, whisk the milk and eggs. Slowly add egg  mixture to flour mixture, beating until smooth. Pour over prepared chiles.    Bake at 450F for 15 minutes. Remove from oven and turn off heat.  Sprinkle cheddar cheese over top and return to oven 1 minute to melt  cheese.
