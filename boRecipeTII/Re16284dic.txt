Watermelon Compote
Fresh strawberries;, Fresh blueberries, Bing cherries; halved &, White wine; chilled, (10-oz) frozen strawberries;, Lime juice, Watermelon; seeded and cubed, 
In blender, combine strawberries and lime juice. Cover and puree. Pour into  a small jar. Chill until ready to serve. In a large compote dish, place  watermelon, cherries and blueberries. Chill at least three hours before  serving. Just before serving, drizzle compote with strawberry/lime juice  puree and white wine. Serves 6 to 8.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
