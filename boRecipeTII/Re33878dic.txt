Sweet and Sour Texas Black-Eyed Pea Salad
Green Bell pepper, chopped, White vinegar, Sugar, Pimientos, chopped, Worcestershire Sauce, Peanut oil, Onion, Garlic, Black-eyed peas cooked, Carrots cooked & diced, Condensed tomato soup, 
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Chill for 4 hours or more.  Serve on a bed of lettuce. Keeps well in refrigerator for 3-5 days.    Marjorie Luckenbach, Texas, Circa 1970    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/vegan4.zip
