Pesto with Bowties or Angel Hair Pasta
red, green, yellow pepper, garlic, Black olives, marinated artichoke, Angel hair pasta, Onions, pesto, Margarine, 
Saute peppers, onions, & garlic in margarine in heavy skillet until lightly  browned. Stir in artichoke hearts & black olives until heated through. Cook  pasta til done. Stir prepared pesto into hot skillet mixture, then toss  with pasta.    (For a meat dish, add a cutup chicken breast to peppers, onions & garlic in  heavy skillet & brown.)
