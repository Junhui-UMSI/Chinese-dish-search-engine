Tomato and Herb Bruschetta
Chopped fresh basil leaves, Chopped fresh oregano leaves, Fresh Lemon Juice, olive oil, Balsamic  vinegar, Garlic, Plum tomatoes, halved,, 1/2 to 3/4-inch-thick slices, 
Mix first 7 ingredients in bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper.  (Tomato mixture can be prepared 2 hours ahead. Let stand at room  temperature.) Cut toasted bread in half. Spoon tomato mixture atop bread  and serve.    Bon Appetit/July/94  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
