"Loudlys" Seasoned Salt
Onion powder, Garlic salt, Pepper, Cumin powder, Parsley, Dry marjoram leaves, Curry powder, Chili powder, Chicken bouillon powder, Onion salt, Salt, Paprika, 
Mix all ingredients together thoroughly, or put all ingredients in 1 qt.  mayonnaise jar with tight fitting lid, shaking until blended well. Keep at  room temperature. Use within 3 months. Makes about 1 cup.
