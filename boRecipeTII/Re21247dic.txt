Ukrainian Potato Pancakes
Salt, Cream, Onion, Black pepper, Sour cream, Flour, Eggs, Potatos, 
In a large bowl use a mixer to puree the ingredients except the sour cream  & cream. You may do this in a food processor as well or a blender. Heat oil  in a skillet and when hot drop large spoonsful of the micture. Cook until  browned on one side. Turn and repeat. When done remove, drian, and place in  a warm oven. Mix the sourcream & cream together. Serve warm with a large  dollop of the the cream mixture! This a staple in Ukrainian homes and these  pancakes will store well in the refrigerator for 2-3 days. In many homes  preserves or jam is also served on these delicious pancakes.
