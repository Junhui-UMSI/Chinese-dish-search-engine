Baked Apples
Light brown sugar, Powdered sugar, Water, Butter or margarine, Baking apples; (to 8),, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, 
Fill centers of apples with sugar; place in crockpot. Sprinkle with  cinnamon and nutmeg and dot with butter. Add water, cover and cook on low  for 7 to 9 hours or on high for 2 1/2 to 3 1/2 hours.    When ready to serve, whip cream until it is stiff and holds soft peaks. Add  powdered sugar, beating in well. Remove apples from cooker and serve each  with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkling of nutmeg, if desired.    Variation: 2 tb. each of raisins and nuts can be mixed with brown sugar to  fill the centers of the apples.
