Tropical Pineapple Banana Shake
Buttermilk, Honey, Ginger, Pineapple, Coconut extract, Banana, 
In a blender, combine pineapple, banana, buttermilk, honey, ginger, and  coconut extract.  Whirl for 1 minute or until smooth.  Pour into glasses.
