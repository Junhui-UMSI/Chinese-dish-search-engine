Almond-Pear Coffee Cake
Mayonnaise, Brown sugar, Almond extract, Finely chopped almonds, Pears; cored - peeled and, Unsifted flour, milk, 17oz Pound Cake Mix, Eggs, Ground nutmeg, 
Grease 13x9x2 inch baking pan. In medium bowl stir together first 4  ingredients. Stir in mayonnaise and extract until mixture resembles coarse  crumbs; set aside. In large bowl with mixer at low speed beat cake mix,  milk and eggs just until blended. Increase speed to medium; beat 2 minutes.  Spread in prepared pan. Arrange pears in one layer on top of batter;  sprinkle with mayonnaise mixture. Bake in 325 degree oven 50 to 60 minutes  or until cake tester inserted in center comes out clean. Cool slightly in  pan. Serve warm.    Great with sliced peaches too.    From That Amazing Ingredient: Mayonnaise! Cookbook Best Foods / Hellmans  Mayonnaise (1979)
