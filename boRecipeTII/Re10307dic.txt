Quick Beef Pie
Pepper, Chedder cheese, Onion, Condensed tomato soup, Potatoes, Ground beef, Salt, green beans  , 
Crumble beef into large fry pan.  Add onion and salt, and cook until  browned.  Drain off excess fat.  Add soup, green beans and pepper. Simmer  for 5 minutes. Pour into a greased 2 quart casserole. Spread mashed  potatoes over the top.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20  minutes. Randy Rigg
