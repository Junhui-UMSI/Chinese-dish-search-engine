Banana Chip Bars (Cake)
Margarine, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, Vanilla, Bananas, mashed, Salt, Flour, Egg, Chocolate chips, Sugar, 
Cream margarine and sugars; add egg and vanilla. Stir in other ingredients  except the chips. Then add the chips. Place mixture in a greased 9x3 pan.  Bake for 35 minutes at 350 degrees.
