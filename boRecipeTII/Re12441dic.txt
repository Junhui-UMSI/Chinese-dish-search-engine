Zuni Vegetable Stew
Yellow Squash; Thinly Sliced, Clove Garlic; Finely Chopped, Pinto Beans; Drained, 1 can, Squash; Cubed ***, Salt, Zucchini; Thinly Sliced, Pepper, Coriander; Ground, Chiles; Medium Size, **, Whole Kernel Corn; Drained, Vegetable oil, Chicken Broth; 2 cans, Onion, Red Bell Pepper; Large, *, Jalapeno Chile; Seed & Chop, 
*    Bell pepper should be seeded and cut into 2 X 1/4-inch strips. **  Chiles should be either poblano or Anaheim and should be seeded and *** Use  either hubbard or acorn squash.  (about 1/2 pound)  ~-------------------------------------------------------------------------  Cook and stir onion and garlic in oil in 4-quart Dutch oven over medium  heat until onion is tender.  Stir in bell pepper, poblano and jalapeno  chiles.  Cook for 15 minutes.  Stir in Hubbard squash, broth, salt, pepper  and coriander.  Heat to boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer until  squash is tender, aobut 15 minutes. Stir in remaining ingredients.  Cook  uncovered, stirring occasionally, until zucchini is tender, about 10  minutes.
