Tropical Truffles
White chocolate, Egg yolk, La Grande Passion, Macadamia nuts, Butter, Finely-chopped, 
Soak fruit in booze. Melt chocolate, add booze, fruit, egg. Beat with mixer  (they say it will curdle then go back together - didnt happen with me).  Chill, make little balls, roll in nuts.
