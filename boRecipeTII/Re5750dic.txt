Pink Colada
Sparkling water, Frozen limeade, Grenadine syrup, Coconut cream, 
Mix limeade concentrate, coconut cream and grenadine syrup; add sparkling  water. Serve over ice and garnish with lime slice and maraschino cherry  ifdesired. 24 servings (about 1/2 cup each);120 calories per serving.    Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
