Waldorf Astoria Stew
Garlic, Potatoes, large diced, Tomato soup, Tapioca, Carrots, chopped, Sugar, Stew meat, cut small, Celery, chopped, Onions, chopped, Salt and pepper to taste, 
Mix, cover and bake 5 hours @ 250 degrees    Options:You can use a can of stewed tomatoes and a can of mushrooms and  cook for a shorter period of time in a 325 degree oven. Submitted By  RHOMMEL <RHOMMEL@IX.NETCOM.COM> On FRI, 24 NOV 1995 080640 ~0500
