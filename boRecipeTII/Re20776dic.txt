Apple Filled Cookies
apples, Rolled oats, Sugar, Water, Vanilla, Flour, Eggs, Salt, Baking Powder, Flour, Margarine, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Baking soda, 
Cream margarine and sugar together.  Add all other ingredients. Mix well  and chill for several hours. Roll and cut into circles with a small biscuit  cutter.    FILLING:  Mix ingredients well.  Cook over low heat until thick.    Put 1 teaspoon filling over circles.  Cover with another circle. Press  edges together with a fork. Bake at 375*F. for 12 minutes.
