Praline Eggnog Pie
Rum, Egg yolks, Prepared eggnog, Whipping cream, whipped, Ground nutmeg, Envelops unflavored gelatin, Praline liqueur, 9" graham cracker pie crust, Whole-berry cranberry sauce, Sugar, Vanilla extract, 
In medium saucepan, mix gelatin with sugar. Beat egg yolks with 1 cup  eggnog. Blend with gelatin and sugar. Let stand for 1 minutes. Stir over  Low heat until gelatin is completely dissolved, about 5 minutes. Stir in  remaining eggnog, liqueur, rum and vani    Recipe By: The Anderson House Cookbook
