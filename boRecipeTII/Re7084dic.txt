Poppy Seed Dressing
Lemon juice, Salt, Poppy seed (add more if you, Salad oil, Cider vinegar, Sugar, Dry mustard, Onion powder, 
Combine sugar, salt, dry mustard, onion powder, cider vinegar and lemon  juice.  Stir until sugar dissolves.   Add oil slowly, beating well.  Dressing thickens as oil is added. Stir in  poppy seed.  Store in refrigerator. Just before serving, shake to mix well.   Especially delicious with fruit or avocado salad.   Makes about 1 2/3 cups. Enjoy!!
