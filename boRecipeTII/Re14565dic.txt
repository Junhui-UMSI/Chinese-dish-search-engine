"Good As Gold" Chicken (Like Kfc and Bostons)
Honey, Vegetable oil, Lime juice, Paprika, Chicken breast halves, 
Mix ingredients (except chicken) in a saucepan and warm just to melt honey.  Arrange chicken breast-side-up in a square baking dish or pan and bake  uncovered at 400 degrees about 35 - 40 minutes, basting pieces without  turning them, 3 or 4 times during baking. Immediately upon removing from  oven, seal baking dish or pan tightly in foil and let stand 15 to 20  minutes before serving.
