Baked Peaches
Rum extract/flavoring, 16 oz. can peach haves, Peach syrup (reserve from, Canned peaches), Flaked coconut, Honey, 
Heat oven to 400F.  Drain peaches reserving 1 T of syrup. Arrange peaches  in a shallow baking dish with cut sides up. Combine coconut, honey, extract  and syrup in small bowl. Blend well. Fill each peach with 1 T of mixture.  Bake 5 minutes.  Cool before serving. May be served as a light dessert with  a dollop of whipped topping. Serves 4-6. Source: Dottie Theriaults  collection
