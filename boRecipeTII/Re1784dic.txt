Chicken Breasts with Prosciutto and Cheese
Flour, Fontina, Boneless chicken breasts, Salt and pepper, Butter, Chicken Stock, Vegetable oil, Prosciutto, Parmesan cheese, 
Cut chicken breasts in half and pound lightly. Mix flour, salt and pepper.  Dredge chicken into seasoned flour. Heat olive oil and butter, add chicken  and saute quickly to a golden brown. Place chicken in a buttered baking  dish and layer with prosciutto and cheese slices. Sprinkle grated cheese  and chicken stock over all. Bake uncovered for ten minutes until cheese is  melted and lightly browned.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  03, 1998
