Baked Barley and Bean Casserole
AM Raw Wheat Germ, Chili powder, Cooked & mashed AM Beans, Pre-cooked AM Bits-O-Barley, AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil, Onion powder, AM Soy Flour, Sea salt to taste (optional), (of choice), Honey, Catsup of choice, Garlic powder, Prepared mustard, Onion, minced and sauteed, Soy milk, 
Preheat oven to 350 F.  Mix all ingredients together.  Let stand for 30  minutes.  Turn into an oiled casserole dish and bake for 40 minutes.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Bits-O-Barley" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
