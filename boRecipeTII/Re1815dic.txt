Chicken Marsala with Artichoke Hearts
olive oil, Marinated artichoke hearts, Onion, Mushrooms, Salt and pepper, Fresh parsley, Chicken broth, Marsala wine, Boneless chicken breasts, Egg, Bread crumbs, 
Pound chicken breast until thin.    Dip chicken into egg then bread crumb both sides. Saute until golden brown  in olive oil. Place breaded chicken side by side in oiled baking pan.    On top of chicken, place artichokes, onion, mushrooms, salt, pepper, and  parsley. Pour broth and wine over all. Cover pan with aluminum foil. Bake  at 450 degrees for 15 minutes. Remove foil. Lower heat to 350 degrees and  bake an additional 15-20 minutes.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  17, 1998
