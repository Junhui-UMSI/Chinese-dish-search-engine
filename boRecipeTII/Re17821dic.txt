Almost Marie Callendars Potato Cheese Soup
Onions, Water, Celery, Half-and-half, Potatoes, Salt, Butter or margarine, sharp cheddar, 
Place potatoes, onions, celery, and salt in the 4 c of water in a large  pot. Simmer about 15 minutes until vegetables are tender. Put in blender  and puree until chunky. Return soup to pot and add half-and-half, butter,  and cheese. Simmer until hot. Do NOT Boil.
