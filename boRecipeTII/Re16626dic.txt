Ajilimojili
Malt Vinegar, Juice of One Lime, Quartered, Chopped, Red Peppers, Seeded and, Scotch Bonnet Peppers,, Green Pepper, Seeded and, olive oil, Quartered, Seeded, Salt, Head Garlic Separated Into, Sprigs Fresh Coriander,, Black Pepper, Cloves, 
Puree the red pepper, green pepper, Scotch Bonnet peppers, garlic,  coriander and lime juice in a food processor. Add the vinegar, salt, black  pepper and olive oil and process again. Bottle the sauce in hot sterilized  jars.
