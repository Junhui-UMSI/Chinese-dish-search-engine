African Vegetarian Stew
Kohlrabies, Zucchini, Garbanzo beans, Ground coriander, Ground cumin, Sweet Potatoes, Raisins, Onion, Tomatoes, Ground ginger, Couscous, Ground turmeric, Ground cinnamon, Water, 
Combine all the ingredients in a large saucepan.  Bring to a boil, lower  the heat, and simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 30 minutes.    Note: Serve the couscous separately, if desired.  Parsnips may be  substituted for the kohlrabi.
