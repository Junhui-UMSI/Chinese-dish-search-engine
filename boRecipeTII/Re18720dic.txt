An Authentic Pesto
Pine nuts, Basil leaves, Walnuts, shelled, Grated Parmesan cheese, Butter, (or 5) black peppercorns, Grated Romano cheese, olive oil, Garlic clove(s), Coarse salt, 
Place all ingredients except 1 cup black peppercorns in food processor and  grind very fine. Add remaining olive oil and blend a few seconds until very  smooth.
