Raspberry Delight #1
Crushed pineapple, Whipped cream, Boiling water, Pineapple juice, Dozen marshmallows, Raspberry gelatin, Salad dressing, Nuts; chopped, (3-oz) cream cheese, 
Mix together the gelatin, marshmallows, water and pineapple juice. While  mixture is cooling, in another bowl, combine the cream cheese, pineapple,  nuts and salad dressing together. Then add to the cooled gelatin mixture.  Place in refreigerator and chill until thick (set to hold). Fold in the  whipped cream and chill.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
