Vegan Cheese
Oregano, dried, Firm tofu, Tahini, Umeboshi paste, Water, Garlic powder, 
Blend all of the ingredients until smooth, creamy, but thick. For a  creamier cheese, use up to a full cup of water. The creamier cheese will be  a little runny.
