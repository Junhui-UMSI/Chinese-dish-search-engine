Tomato Herb Toasts
Black Pepper, Ripe tomatoes,preferably a, Chopped fresh herb (basil,, Finely chopped red onion, Salt, Chopped fresh herb (basil,, Balsamic vinegar, Large slices bread,cut into, Red wine vinegar, Fruity olive oil, 
1. Macerate the chopped onion in 1 tablespoon each vinegar with a  little salt for at least 30 minutes.  2. Blanch tomatoes in boiling water for about 20 seconds. Cool, peel,  seed and coarsely chop. Season lightly with salt; drain.  3. Prepare a vinaigrette with olive oil, onion, and vinegars, salt and  pepper and some herbs.  4. Just before serving, dress the tomatoes with the vinaigrette and  adjust seasoning.  5. Grill bread on both sides. Spoon tomato mixture onto toasts and  garnish with herbs.
