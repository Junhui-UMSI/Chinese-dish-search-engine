Tortilla Chip Wreath
PATTI  VDRJ67A, Vanilla candy coating; cut, Shortening, Red & green gumdrops, Triangle shape tortilla chip, 
Line cookie sheet with waxed paper. Draw 6" circle on paper. In large  micro-safe bowl, place candy coating and shortening. Microwave on MEDIUM  for 6-7 minutes or until well melted, stirring once halfway through  melting. Stir until smooth. Dip chips, one at a time, in candy coating;  remove excess with a spoon. Arrange coated chips randomly around outside of  6" circle. Continue to overlap and stack chips in wreath shape. Decorate  with gumdrops. Let stand until set.
