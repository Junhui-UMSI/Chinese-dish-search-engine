Purim Poppyseed Candy
Poppyseeds, Raisins (opt), Honey, Salt, Sugar, Nuts, 
Wash poppyseeds throughly in boiling water and drain well. Cook all  ingredients except nuts and raisins to soft ball stage. Add nuts and  raisins and cook for 5 min. Pour onto wet board and spread with spatula to  about 1/4" thick.  Allow to cool for a few minutes and cut into square or  diamond shapes.  When cool, remove from board. Makes 36 pieces.
