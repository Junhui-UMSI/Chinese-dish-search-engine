Asparagus with Blue Cheese and Walnut Relish
Chopped parsley, Walnuts -- chopped, Asparagus, Salt, Grated Lemon Zest, (scallions)**, Crumbled, Chopped Green Onions --, Blue Cheese -- w/o rind &, 
* such as Roquefort or Danish Blue ** including 2 inches of green stems    Place blue cheese, walnuts, onions, parsley and lemon zest in a bowl and  mix well.  (Blue cheese relish can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and  refrigerate. Bring to room temperature 30 minutes before serving.) To  prepare asparagus, cut off and discard tough, woody ends of stalks. Bring  enough water to cover asparagus to boil in a large deep saucepan or  skillet.  Lightly salt water and add the asparagus.  Simmer until just  tender, 3-5 minutes.  Remove asparagus and drain well.  Taste and season  with more salt if needed.    To serve, arrange asparagus on heated platter or in a shallow serving bowl.  Top with blue cheese relish.  Serve hot.    Recipe By     : "Marien, Stacey" <maris.ils@MHS.UNC.EDU>    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
