Poached Fish with Ginger
Garlic, Water, Soy sauce, Flounder, lemon, Flat leaf parsley, Ginger root, celery, 
Arrange the celery sticks in a layer in the bottom of a covered saucepan.  This is your "steaming rack." Add the next 5 ingredients. Bring to a boil  and simmer 5 minutes. Lay the fish on the celery and cover the pan. Steam  about 5 minutes, or until the fish is firm and flakes with a fork. Sprinkle  with chopped parsley. Serves 4.    NOTES : Todays recipe features a basic fish cooking technique that works  with many other combinations of bottom-layer vegetables and seasonings. Yo=  u can serve the "steaming rack" vegetables, or use tough outer leaves or  stalks and discard them.
