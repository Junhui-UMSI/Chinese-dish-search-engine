Pecan Sugar Cookies
Cinnamon, Baking soda, Egg, Honey, Flour, Pecans, Sugar, light brown, Allspice, Water, 
In mixing bowl combine brown sugar, water, honey and egg. Beat about 10  seconds with mixer.  In a separate bowl combine flour, pecans, cinnamon,  allspice and baking soda,baking powder, mixing well. Add to wet ingredients  and stir.  Drop batter by the teaspoonfuls on greased cookie sheet. Bake at  375 degrees for 12 minutes. Makes about 3 dozen cookies. Let cool well  before storing.  From Chef Paul Prudhommes Louisana Kitchens.
