Wild and Exotic Hollandaise

In a stainless steel bowl, whisk the egg yolks, lemon juice, salt, hot  sauce, and water, together. Set the bowl over a pot of simmering water.  Whisk the mixture until pale yellow in color and frothy. Slowly whisk in  the melted butter, 1 tablespoon at a time, until all the butter is  incorporated and the sauce is thick. Fold in the sauteed mushrooms and  reseason if necessary. Place a cake in the c enter of a plate. Lay the  poached egg on top of the cake. Spoon the Hollandaise over the egg and  around the sides.
