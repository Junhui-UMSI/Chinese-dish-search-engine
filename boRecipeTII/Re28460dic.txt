Baked or Grilled Onions
Black Pepper, Onions, Butter, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.    Halve the onions and arrange them in a greased baking dish or on a greased  grill rack, dot with butter, and cook until tinged with black and brown,  nearly charred, for 45 minutes to 1 hour and 15 minutes, brushing with more  butter or oil as necessary to keep moist. Season with salt and pepper.  Serve hot or chilled.
