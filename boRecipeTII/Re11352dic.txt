Ranchero Casserole
chopped green peppers  , Old El Paso Spanish, Corn, Sour cream, Mexican cheese blend;, Italian plum tomatoes;, chicken, Fresh cilantro, Black beans  , 
1. Heat oven to 375F. Spray 13x9-inch (3 quart) baking dish with nonstick  cooking spray. Prepare Spanish rice as directed on package.    2. In large bowl, combine cooked rice, chicken, sour cream, chiles, half of  the beans and half of the corn; mix well. Spread mixture evenly in sprayed  dish. Top with remaining beans, corn, tomatoes and cheese.    3. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Sprinkle with  cilantro before serving.
