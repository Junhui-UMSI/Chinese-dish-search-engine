Apricot Delight Salad
Pecans, Cream cheese (8 oz.), Apricot jello, Water, Sugar, Jars apricot baby food, Condensed milk, chilled, Crushed pineapple, undrained, 
Combine jello, sugar and water, bring to a boil and dissolve jello.  Remove from heat, stir in fruit; set aside to cool. Combine milk and cream  cheese, beat until smooth. Stir in jello mixture, add nuts. Pour into mold  or bowl and refrigerate.    S.K.A.--Metairie, La.
