Tangerine, Grapefruit, Lemon and Orange Digestivi
Sugar, Bottles Nandini grappa, Grapefruit zested; in wide, Oranges zest; in wide pieces, Tangerines zested; in wide, Water, Lemons zest; in wide pieces, 
Make simple syrup of 10 cups sugar and 10 cups water by heating both  together until clear. Allow to cool    Arrange 8 quart bottles and place 1/2 of each zest in each bottle. Divide  simple syrup among bottles and top each with grappa.
