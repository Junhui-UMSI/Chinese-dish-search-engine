Poached Scallops
Quartered onion, Lemon juice, Dry white wine, Water, Bay leaf, Fresh bay scallops, Oregano, Thyme, 
Combine water, wine, onion & herbs; bring to boil. Add scallops; cook  until firm & white. Sprinkle with lemon juice; serve at once.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
