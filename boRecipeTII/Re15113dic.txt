Whats in the Fridge? Ham Glaze
Lemon juice, Apple jelly (or apple-mint), Mustard, brown, Strawberry jam, Soy sauce, 
In a small saucepan, warm the jam and the jelly until they have melted.  Stir in the mustard, soy sauce and lemon juice. Heat gently until blended;  use as a sauce or glaze over ham.
