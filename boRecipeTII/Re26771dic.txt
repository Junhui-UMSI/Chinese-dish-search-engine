Baked Apple Slices
Apple cider, Ginger, Walnuts; -or-, Raisins, Vanilla ice cream, Sugar, Cinnamon, Butter or margarine, Baking apples; peeled,, Nutmeg, 
Place apples in a greased 1 quart baking dish.  Combine sugar, cinnamon,  nutmeg, ginger and apple cider; pour over apples.  Dot with butter.  Sprinkle with nuts or raisins. Bake, uncovered, at 350 for 45 - 60 minutes  or until apples are tender.  Serve warm over ice cream.  Source:  Taste of  Home Magazine.
