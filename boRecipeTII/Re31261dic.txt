Banana-Walnut Cookies
All-Purpose Flour, Mashed bananas, milk, Baking soda, (quick or oldfashioned), Salt, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Walnuts, Shortening, soft, Egg, Brown sugar, firmly packed, Quaker Oats, uncooked, 
Sift together flour, baking powder, soda, salt and cinnamon into bowl.  Add sugar, shortening, egg, milk and bananas.  Beat until smooth,  about 2 minutes.  Stir in walnuts and nuts.    Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased cooky sheets.  Bake in preheated  moderate oven (375 F.) 12 to 15 minutes.
