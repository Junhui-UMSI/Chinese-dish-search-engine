Potato Pepperoni Dish
Onion, Salt, Butter or margarine, Pepper, 8 oz can tomato sauce, Tomatoes; diced, Unpeeled potatoes; cut in, Shredded mozzarella cheese, 3.5 oz pkg sliced pepperoni, 
In a 12-inch nonstick skillet, melt butter; remove from the heat. Arrange  potatoes on the bottom and up the sides of the skillet; sprinkle with  onion, salt and pepper. Cover and cook over low heat until the potatoes are  tender, about 20 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese; layer with half of the  tomato sauce, all of the pepperoni and tomatoes, and the remaining tomato  sauce. Cover and cook on low until the cheese is melted and the tomatoes  are heated through.
