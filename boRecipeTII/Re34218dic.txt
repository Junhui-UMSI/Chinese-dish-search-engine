Bayou Catfish Balls
Salt, Pepper, Cooking oil, Eggs, Catfish fillets, Hot sauce, Cornmeal, 
Rub fish with hot sauce, salt and pepper.  Set aside for 1 hour or  more. Cut fish into bite-size pieces and dip into eggs.  Roll in  cornmeal. Fry quickly in deep fat for 2 or 3 minutes, or until golden  brown. Drain.    NOTE: Serve hot.
