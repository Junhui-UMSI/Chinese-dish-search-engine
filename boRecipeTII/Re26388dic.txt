Tomato Butter Dill Sauce
Minced dill fronds, Butter, Plum tomatoes,; seeded and, 
Heat butter until golden, saute tomatoes just to warm; add dill off heat,  season with salt and pepper.    To assemble hot meal: Place a bed of rice on a plate; top with salmon steak  and spoon any steaming juices over salmon. Garnish with warm tomato sauce.
