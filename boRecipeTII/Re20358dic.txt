Veal Seasoning Mix
Salt, Black pepper, Ground bay leaves, Celery seed, Coriander, Onion powder, Garlic powder, Dry hot mustard, Paprika, Cayenne pepper, File powder, Rosemary, White pepper, 
Mix all ingredients together well. Store in a tightly  covered glass jar. This is an excellent seasoning for  veal, but you may use it on other dishes if you like.  Do not add extra salt to the dish. Lagniappe: You can  make larger amounts of this seasoning and store it for  use as you need it. About 4 calories per teaspoon.
