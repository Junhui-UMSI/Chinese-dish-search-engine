Tomato Citrus Salsa
Fresh tomatoes (2 medium), Orange Peel, Sliced green onion, Chopped fresh cilantro or, Minced fresh or jarred, Salt, 
Use tomatoes held at room temperature until fully ripe. Core tomatoes; cut  in halves crosswise; squeeze out and discard seeds and juice. Chop  tomatoes. In a large bowl combine tomatoes, green onion, cilantro,  jalapeno, salt and orange pee. Use as a dip, in tacos, with hamburgers,  fish, etc. 1-1/2 cups.
