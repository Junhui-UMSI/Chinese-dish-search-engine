Welches Pretty Frosting
Hot water, White frosting mix, 
Empty mix into small mixer bowl. Add hot grape juice and water, a little at  a time, beating at high speed until spreading consistency is achieved (5 to  7 minutes). If necessary, add a little more liquid.
