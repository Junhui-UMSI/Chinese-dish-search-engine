Texas Zesty Black Eyed Peas
Cold water, Cooked diced jalapeno;, Black pepper (add to taste), Smoked ham hocks, Green bell pepper; diced, Dry black eye peas; cleaned, Garlic; finely diced, Onion; diced, Salt to taste, 
Put all of above in an electric crockpot. Set on low setting for 6 hours or  overnight. Remove bones and skins, scrape fat off skin and put meat and fat  back into pot. Cook on low for 1 hour and serve.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 282 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 21, 1997
