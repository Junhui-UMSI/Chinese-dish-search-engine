Layered Soup Mix in a Jar
Pearl barley;, Dry split peas, Beef bouillon granules, Dry lentils, Dried minced onion, Italian seasoning, Uncooked long grain rice, Alphabet macaroni or other, ADDITIONAL INGREDIENTS, Ground beef, Water, Diced tomatoes undrained, 
Yield: 1 jar    *can be put in a small bag to make it easier for the recipent to get out of  jar )    In a 1-1/2-pint jar, layer the first eight ingredients in the order listed.  Seal tightly. Yeild: 1 batch.    *Instructions to attatch to jar*    ***Friendship Soup Mix***    To prepare soup: Carefully remove macaroni from top of jar and set aside.  In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, brown beef; drain. Add the water,  tomatoes and soup mix; bring to a boil Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 45  minutes.    Add reserved macaroni; cover and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes or until  macaroni, peas, lentils and barley are tender. Yeild: 16 servings ( 4  quarts)    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by valerie@nbnet.nb.ca (valerie) on Sep 30,  1998,
