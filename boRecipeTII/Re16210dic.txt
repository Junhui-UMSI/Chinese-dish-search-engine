Wasserschnalle
Butter, Water or beef broth (3 cups, Onion, Pepper, Fat (not quite 3 Tbsp), Thyme, Marjoram, Salt, Caraway seed, To 3 sl leftover bread, cut, 
This is a traditional recipes that is rarely encountered today.    Brown the onion in the fat until golden brown, then add thyme and marjoram.  Stir in the bread cubes, add water (or broth) and bring to a boil. After a  few minutes, add salt and pepper to taste. Serve with a small slice of  bread in the hot soup.    Serves 4.    From:  DSCHWAEBISCH KUCHE by Aegidius Kolb and Leonhard Lidel, Allgaeuer  Zeitungsverlag, Kempten. 1976. (Translation/Conversion: Karin Brewer)  Posted by: Karin Brewer, Cooking Echo, 8/92
