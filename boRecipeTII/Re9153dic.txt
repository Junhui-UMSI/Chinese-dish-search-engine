Provencal Roast Beef Salad Sandwich
Slivers roasted red pepper, Thinly sliced roast beef;, Olive oil; to taste, Sprig parsley, Hard rolls, Chopped pitted black olives;, Dijon mustard, Fresh lemon juice; to taste, 
Split rolls in half and score them with a sharp knife to make craters for  dressing to seep into.    Make dressing with olive paste, Dijon mustard, lemon juice and olive oil.  Season with olive oil.    Spoon dressing into rolls, top with roast beef, sliced tomato and sprigs of  parsley. Drizzle on more dressing and top with pimiento.
