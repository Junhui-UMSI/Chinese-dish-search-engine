Vidalia Onion Salad
Eggs;hard boiled, Lemon-pepper seasoning, Salt to taste, DRESSING, White vinegar, Mayonnaise, Vidalia onions;thinly sliced, Mustard, Lettuce;torn in bite size-, 
In a seperate bowl, mix the dressing ingredients. To assemble: make layers  of lettuce, onions, eggs and dressing. Repeat and end with dressing. Cover  and chill for at least 3 hours. This is great with steak and potatoes!
