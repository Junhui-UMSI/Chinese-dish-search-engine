Yum Yum Cake #3
Cocoa, Evaporated milk, All-purpose flour, Vanilla, Cocoa, Crisco (solid vegetable, Pecans, Sugar, Pecans, FROSTING, Butter or Margarine, Eggs, Salt, Vanilla, 
From:    Catherine F Ramey <rameycf@MAIL.AUBURN.EDU>    Date:    Mon, 5 Aug 1996 14:40:54 -0500  Cream sugar, shortening.  Add eggs.  Sift flour, cocoa and salt.  Add to  mixture.  Add vanilla and nuts.  Pour into greased and floured pan.  Bake  at 300 for 30-40 minutes or until done. While still warm, spread with mini  marshmallows.  Put back in oven to let marshmallows melt.    Frosting: Stir together sugar and cocoa.  Mix with melted butter.  Add  milk, vanilla, nuts. Spread on cake while it is still warm. Mix slightly  with marshmallow. Let stand for 2 hours.    EAT-L Digest  4 August 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
