Angus Barn Chocolate Chess Pie
unsweetened chocolate, Vanilla, Sticks butter, NO CAROLINAS HISTORIC REST, Eggs, pie crust, Sugar, Salt, 
Melt butter and chocolate over boiling water.  Mix sugar, eggs, salt and  vanilla; add to chocolate mixture. Pour into pie shell and bake at 375 deg.  for about 35 minutes. From: "North Carolinas Historic Restaurants"
