24-Hour Fruit Salad
Can or orange juice; frozen, Bananas; sliced, Mandarin oranges; drained, Cans of pineapple tidbits, Instant lemon pudding, Pears; 2 1/2 lb., Can of apricots, Can of peaches, 
From: kmeade@ids2.idsonline.com (The Meades)    Date: Fri, 24 May 1996 12:13:11 -0400    Drain pineapple and dissolve orange juice in pineapple juice.  Mix in juice  instant pudding, sliced bananas, pears, apricots and peaches (in bite  sizes.)  Add drained oranges and pineapple.  Mix and let stand for 24 hours  in refrigerator.
