Sweet Balsamic and Red Onion Marinade
Sugar, Vegetable or walnut oil, Balsamic vinegar, Finely chopped red onion, Pepper, Salt, Dry mustard, Water, 
1. In small bowl, combine all ingredients. With wire whisk, beat until  smooth and sugar is dissolved.    2. Pour mixture over slightly steamed and cooled asparagus spears, or  sliced fresh tomatoes or cucumbers. Let stand at room temperature for 15  minutes to marinate before serving.
