Pita Pizza Snacks
mozzarella cheese, Pepperoni, Whole wheat pita breads, Green Pepper, Mushrooms, tomato sauce, 
Directions : split pita breads in half, toast top each half with tomato  sauce, cheese, green pepper, pepperoni and mushrooms. place on paper towel  lined serving plate and microwave 1 1/2 to 2 min. or until cheese melts,  rotating plate after half the time.
