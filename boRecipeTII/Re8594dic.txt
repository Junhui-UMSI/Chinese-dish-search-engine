Potatoes with Black Pepper
Potatoes, boiled, cooled &, Cilantro, chopped, Salt, Vegetable oil, diced, Black Pepper, 
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  When hot, put in potatoes &  stir-fry them for a minute or so. Sprinkle in the salt. Cover & let the  potatoes heat through gently for 5 minutes, reducing heat as necessary. Add  pepper & mix gently.  Cook for a few more minutes, stirring every now &  then.  Sprinkle in the cilantro, mix & serve.
