Apfelschaum (Apple Mousse)
Tart apples, peeled, cored, Butter (2 Tbsp), Sugar, Grated peel of 2 lemons, Sugar, White wine (1 cup plus 1, For baking:, Egg whites, 
Central Swabia    In the wine, stew the apples until tender and mushy. Force through a sieve.  Add the sugar and lemon peel. Meanwhile, beat the egg whites to stiff peaks  and then carefully fold into the apple mixture. Fill the whole into a  buttered ovenproof dish (Jena Glass*), dust with 2 tablespoons sugar, and  slowly bake at 250 degrees F for about half an hour.    Serves 4.    ((*Note:  Jena Glass is similar to Pyrex.  K.B.))    From:  DSCHWAEBISCH KUCHE by Aegidius Kolb and Leonhard Lidel, Allgaeuer  Zeitungsverlag, Kempten. 1976.
