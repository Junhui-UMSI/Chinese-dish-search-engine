Aleecha
Potatoes; cut like french, Corn oil, Tumeric; ground, Queman; see note, Fresh hot green peppers, Cabbage; coarsely sliced, Onion; sliced, Salt, Garlic; sliced thin, Carrots; sliced thin, Water, Tomato paste, 
Aleecha is a mixed vegetable stew.  Quemam contains an ingredient not found in the US.  Id substitute dry  berebere.  In dry pan over moderate low heat, stir fry onion, garlic, and  carrots for 2 mins.  Add 1/2c of the water and cook  5 mins longer.  Add the oil and continue to simmer.    Add the tumeric, chilies, and cabbage.  Cover the pan and steam to  reduce the bulk for 2 mins.  Stir well and add the quemam, tomato  paste, salt and the potatoes.    Cover the pan and cook for 5 mins.  Add the remaining water and  simmer for 5 minutes more to soften the potatoes and thicken the  sauce somewhat.    Serve at room temperature with Injeera.
