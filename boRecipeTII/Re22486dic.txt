Tutti-Fruitti Ice Cream
Philadelphia cream cheese, Fruit salad, Maraschino cherries; a jar, Cream; whipping, Sugar; powdered, 
Drain all juice from fruit but 1/4 c. Cream sugar and cheese and add diced  fruit, cherries and 1/4 c. juice. Fold in whipped cream and freeze.    Source: Mrs. William Erf, Lyme Grange, Huron County, OH
