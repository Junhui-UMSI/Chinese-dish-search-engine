Tasty Orange Roughy
Orange Roughy Fillets, Chicken Bouillon, Parsley, Black Pepper, Whole Lemon, Paprika, Margarine, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.    Lightly butter cookie sheet.  Rinse fillets, and dry completely.  Place  single-layered on cookie sheet.    On each fillet - in this order - put:  Lemon juice, powdered bouillon,  black pepper, paprika, then bits of butter or margarine.    Bake until fish flakes lightly, and is slightly golden on top.  Place fish  on serving plate, and pour pan juice over all.    May be garnished with parsley sprigs, and thinly sliced lemon, if desired.
