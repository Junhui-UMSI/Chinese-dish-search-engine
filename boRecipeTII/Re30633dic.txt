Tamale Casserole
Meat, Sliced, Tomatoes, fresh, Cottage cheese, Vegetable oil, Egg, Oregano, green olives, canned, Yellow cornmeal, Milk, Onion, 
Mix cornmeal, milk and egg in large bowl; set aside. In skillet, brown meat  in oil with onion, tomato and oregano. Pour off fat and add olives and  cottage cheese; simmer 5 minutes. Stir into cornmeal mix; turn into  casserole dish; bake at 350 degrees, 1 hour.
