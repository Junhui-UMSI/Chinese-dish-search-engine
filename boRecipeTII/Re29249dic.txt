Baked Squash and Yams (Vegan)
Lb(400g) garnet yams, peeled, (240ml) tomato sauce, (240ml) Lite soy milk, (15ml) garlic chili paste, And sliced, Butternut), (400g) winter squash, peeled, And sliced (I used, 
Layer the squash and yams in a baking dish.  Mix the remaining ingredients  together and pour over the veggies. Bake covered @ 350 F (175 C) for ~ 45  minutes.
