Upside Down Berry
Sugar, Strawberries; for garnish, Egg whites, Vanilla extract, Strawberries, Pie crust (9 inch), Cream of tartar, Cornstarch, Whipping cream, Sugar, 
Prepare pastry. Line a 9 inch pie plate. Trim to 1/2 inch beyond edge.  Flute edge; prick pastry. Bake in 450 degree oven for 10 to 12 minutes.  Cool. For meringue, beat egg whites, vanilla and cream of tartar till soft  peaks form. Gradually add 1/4 cup sugar; beat till soft peaks form. Spread  on bottom and sides of pastry. Bake in 350 degree oven 12 minutes; cool  Mash berries; add water to measure 2 cups. Combine 1/2 cup sugar and  cornstarch; add mashed berries. Cook and stir till bubbly. Cook and stir 2  minutes more. Cool; spread over merigue. Chill. Whip cream; spread over  pie. Top with sliced strawberries. Cover; chill to store.
