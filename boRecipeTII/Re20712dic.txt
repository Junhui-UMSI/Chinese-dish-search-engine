Apple Crunch #3
Flour, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Light brown sugar, Crust, Sugar, Butter, Light brown sugar, Whipped cream or vanilla ice, Oatmeal, -(up to), Tart apples, 
Core, pare and thinly slice apples. Blend crust ingredients. Arrange apples  in the bottom of a 9 inch square glass baking dish. Sprinkle apples with  the 2 sugars and top with a layer of the crust. Repeat layers, ending with  a heavy 1/4 inch crust. Press layers gently. Bake 50 to 60 minutes at 350?.  Serve with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream. Yield: 8 servings.
