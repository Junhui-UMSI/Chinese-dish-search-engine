Tangy Tomato Sauce
28-oz can crushed tomato, Diced onion, Apple cider, Or more cloves minced, Lemon juice, 
Saute onion and garlic. Add crushed tomato. Simmer 5 minutes. Add apple  cider and lemon juice. Simmer 5 more minutes. Its done.
