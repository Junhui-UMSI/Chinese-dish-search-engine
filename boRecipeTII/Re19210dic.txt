Vegetable Pie with Cheese Crust
Frozen chopped broccoli, Ready made pizza dough (in, Salt, Chicken broth (use canned, Corn; drained, Seasoned salt, Onion, Frozen sugar peas or snow, Garlic powder (for crust), Fat free cheese; shredded, Black pepper to taste, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Remove Ready Made crust from canister and  use only half the dough provided. Spray 9" pie plate with non-stick cooking  spray. Use 1/2 cup shredded cheese and sprinkle cheese over dough. Press  cheese into the dough with your fingers.Place dough in pie plate. Shape  dough to fit bottom and edges of pie plate. Sprinkle crust with garlic  powder.     Layer vegetables next and season each layer with salt, pepper and a small  amount of seasoned salt. In small bowl add just a few teaspoons of chicken  broth to 1-1/2 tablespoons flour. Keep adding broth to make a thin paste.  Pour this mixture over vegetables and top with remaining shredded fat free  cheese. Bake 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes.  Entire recipe makes 8  servings.    ULTRA LOW FAT    FROM SOME COOKBOOK!
