Preventions Little Ears Pasta Salad with Herb Sauce
olive oil, Pasta; orecchiette, Red pepper; dried chile, Fresh herbs; assortment, 
1/> Cook pasta in boiling water with the hot pepper. Drain. Discard pepper.  2/> Use fresh herbs.  Rinse. Towel dry.  Try parsley, basil, mint, chives;  add  a little thyme, rosemary, oregano. 3/> Place herbs in a large bowl. In  small saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat until just very warm, not  smoking. Pour oil over fresh herbs and add pasta. Toss gently. Serve as a  salad at room temperature.
