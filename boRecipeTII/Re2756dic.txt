Peanut Butter Chips and Jelly Bars
All-purpose flour, Cold butter or margarine, Egg, Grape jelly, divided, Sugar, Baking Powder, 
Heat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease 9-inch square baking pan. Stir  together flour, sugar and baking powder; with pastry blender or fork, cut  in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add egg; blend well.  Reserve half of mixture; press remaining mixture onto bottom of prepared  pan. Spread jelly evenly over crust. Sprinkle 1 cup peanut butter chips  over jelly. Stir together remaining crumb mixture with remaining 2/3 cup  chips; sprinkle over top. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned.  Cool completely in pan on wire rack. Cut into bars. About 16 bars.
