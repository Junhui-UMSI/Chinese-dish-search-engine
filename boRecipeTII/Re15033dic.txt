West Nc Bbq Sauce! Davidson County Version!
Catsup, Onion minced, Texas pete, Some people put melted, Worcestershire Sauce, Apple cider vinegar, Brown sugar, Black cracked pepper, Lemons ; juice of, Pepper flakes, 
Whisk and Microwave on high 7 min. Use as both mop sauce and serving sauce.  Dont knock it until you try it! Longer it sits the better it gets!!!!!    The recipe came from my ex mother in law in Davidson Co NC. She used the  butter version. I dont!    Posted to bbq-digest by "H. WHITED ENGRAVING" <engraver@usit.net> on Mar  30, 1998
