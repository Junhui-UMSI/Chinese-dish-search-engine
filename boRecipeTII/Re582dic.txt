Fruited Tongue
Beef tongue, Salt, Gingersnaps, Brown sugar, Seedless raisins, Prunes; seeded and chopped, Cloves, Whole allspice, 
Place tongue in pan; cover with water. Add salt. Cover; simmer for 3 to 4  hours or until tender. Remove from liquid; cool. Remove skin, cut off roots  and gristle. Cut tongue diagonally into 1/4 inch slices. Combine  gingersnaps, brown sugar, raisins and prunes. Strain 3 cups of tongue  stock; stir into gingersnap mixture. Add cloves and allspice. Cook until  smooth and slightly thickened. Add tongue slices, simmer. Yield: 8 to 12  servings    Happy cooking, Marie    Posted by Marie Culver to the Fidonet Cooking echo 12-94    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Dec 06, 98,
