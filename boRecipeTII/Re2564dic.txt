Peachy Berries and nanas
Fresh strawberries, hulled, Peach slices, fresh  or, Raspberries - frozen,, Banana, Raspberry sherbet, Brown sugar (up to 1/3 cup, 
Liquify the berries and banana in the blender.  Add the peach slices,  sherbet and brown sugar and blend on high until liquified.  Serve  immediately.    From Sylvia Smith and Company.
