Vidalia Onion Chicken Casserole
Broccoli;frozen, Paprika, Cheddar cheese;grated, Vegetable oil, Rice;cooked, Water, Vidalia onions;chopped, Eggs;slightly beaten, Mayonnaise, Vegetable oil, Ch.breasts;boned & skinned, Mushrooms;sliced, 
Cook broccoli according to directions.  Drain and set aside. Saute onions  and mushrooms in 2 T. of oil until limp. Drain and combine with broccoli.  Set aside. Combine cheese, eggs, mayonnaise, water and rice. Set aside.  Brown chicken breasts in 3 T. of oil. To Assemble: Combine broccoli mixture  with cheese mixture and pour into greased, shallow baking pan. Place  chicken breasts on top and sprinkle with paprika. Bake uncovered in a 350  oven for 45 minutes. NOTE- You may cut fat and cholesterol from this recipe  with the following substitutions: light skim milk cheese for cheddar, 4 oz.  of egg substitute for the 2 eggs, light mayonnaise for reg. mayonnaise and  non-fat cooking spray for browning chicken.
