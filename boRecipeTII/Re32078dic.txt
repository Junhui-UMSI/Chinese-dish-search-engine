Barbecued Chicken and Corn Bread Stuffing
Margarine, Skinless boneless chicken, Stalk celery; chopped, Paprika, Pepperidge Farm Corn Bread, Barbecue sauce, Boiling water, 
1. Mix water, margarine, paprika and celery. Add stuffing. Mix lightly.  Spoon into 2 qt. shallow baking dish. Arrange chicken over stuffing. Spoon  barbecue sauce over chicken.    2. Bake at 400 degrees fro 35 minutes or until chicken is done. Serve with  additional barbecue sauce, if desired.    *note-You can also use one package of Pepperidge Farm Honey Pecan corn  bread top of stove stuffing mix instead. Mix vegetable/seasoning packet  with water, margarine, paprika and celery.    Recipe by: Pepperidge Farm    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 500 by PLK1028 <PLK1028@aol.com>  on Jan 12, 1998
