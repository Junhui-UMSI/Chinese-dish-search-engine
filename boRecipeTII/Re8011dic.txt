Potato and Broccoli Supreme
Cream cheese, softened, Egg, (10 oz) pkg Frozen Broccoli, Margarine, S & P, Onions, Cooked and drained, Hot mashed potatoes, Cheddar cheese (shredded), (2.8 oz) Can French Fried, 
Whip first 4 ingredients together until smooth. Season to taste with salt  and pepper. Fold in 1/2 of the onions. Spread mixture over bottom and up  sides of a buttered 8 X 12 baking dish to form a shell. Bake, uncovered at  350 for 25-30 minutes. Arrange hot broccoli in shell. Sprinkle with cheese  and remaining onions. Bake, uncovered, 5 minutes more.
