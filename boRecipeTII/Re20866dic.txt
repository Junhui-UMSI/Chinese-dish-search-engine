Apple Meatloaf
Prepared mustard, Salt, Horseradish, Onion, apples, Lean ground beef, Eggs, Herb seasoned stuffing mix, Ketchup, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Mix all ingredients together well  in a large bowl. Pack into a large loaf pan. Bake about 1 hour and 15  minutes, or until cooked through. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.    Recipe by: BUSY COOKS - http://busycooks.miningco.com    Posted to recipelu-digest by Lynn Nelson <lynnn@erols.com> on Feb 16, 1998
