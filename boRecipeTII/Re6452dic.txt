Plum Tomato-Feta Pasta
Feta cheese; crumbled, Salt, Plum tomatoes; finely diced, olive oil, Radiatore or corkscrew, Red onion; chopped, Black Pepper, Fresh parsley, 
Combine tomatoes, onion, oil, parsley, salt and pepper in large bowl. Add  feta and hot pasta; toss. Makes 6 servings.    Total prep time: 20 minutes Degree of difficulty: Easy    NOTES : The flavors of tomatoes and onion, parsley and oil mingle while the  pasta cooks.
