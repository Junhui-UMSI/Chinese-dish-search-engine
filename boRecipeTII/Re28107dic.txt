Baked Corn Casserole
chopped green, whole kernel, cream cheese, Onions, cream-style, french-fried, Butter or margarine, 
In a large bowl, beat together butter and cream cheese. Stir in the kernel  corn, cream-style corn, chilies and onion; mix well. Pour into a greased  8-in. square baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350! for 15 minutes. Remove  from oven; stir in half of the fried onions. Sprinkle remaining fried  onions on top. Bake 15 minutes longer. Yield: 8-10 servings.
