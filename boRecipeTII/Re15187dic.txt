Whipped Buttercream Icing
Meringue powder, Powdered sugar, Granulated sugar, Crisco, Cold water, Butter flavor, 
In grease free bowl, put 1/2 cup cold water and 1 tablespoon meringue  powder. Beat until very stiff. Add 3/4 cup granulated sugar, slowly. Beat  until glossy. Set aside. In another bowl beat 1 1/2 or 2 cups Crisco, 1  pound powdered sugar and 1/2 teaspoons butter flavor. Beat well. Gradually  add meringue mixture. Beat until fluffy.
