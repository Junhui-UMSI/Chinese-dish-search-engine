Beef Seasoning Mix
Garlic powder, Thyme, Finely ground black pepper, Onion powder, File powder, Paprika, Dry mustard, Cayenne pepper, White pepper, Ground bay leaves, Salt, Sweet basil, 
Mix all ingredients together well. Store tightly  covered in a glass jar for use as needed. This is an  excellent seasoning for beef dishes. Use as you would  any seasoning mix, and do not add extra salt to the  dish.   Lagniappe: This seasoning mix lends itself to beef,  but you may use it as you like. (Be sure to label the  jar so you wont forget what it is!) About 4 calories  per teaspoon.
