Velmas Caramel Popcorn
Pecans; chop; toasted, Popcorn; popped, White corn syrup, Salt, Cream of tartar, Butter, Brown sugar, Baking soda, 
Put the brown sugar, corn syrup, butter, cream of tartar and salt in a  heavy saucepan. Melt over medium heat and bring to a boil. Boil 5 minutes.  remove from heat and add the baking soda. Stir well. Pour over the popped  corn and pecans. Stir well. Put in a baking pan and bake at 250~ for 30  minutes, stirring occasionally.
