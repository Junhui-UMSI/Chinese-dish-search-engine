Zesty Pesto Tomato Bread
Tomatoes softened not oil, Yeast*, 1 1/2 LB LOAF, 2 LB LOAF, Salt, Pesto, Bread flour, Sugar, Packed, Packed, Plus 2 tb water, Pesto, Sugar, Coarsely chopped sun dried, Water, Coarsely chopped sun dried, Salt, Tomatoes softened not oil, Yeast, Bread flour, 
Place all ingredient in pan in order.  Select basic/white cycle and use  medium or light crust colour.  Do not use delay cycles.  Yeast amount is  proportionately less than called for in the smaller loaf. To soften  tomatoes soak in 1 cup of very hot water for 10 minute or until softened  and drain.
