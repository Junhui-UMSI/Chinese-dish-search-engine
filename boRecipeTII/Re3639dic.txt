Peggys Beef Stew
Onion soup mix, Potatoes, peeled and chunked, Vegetable oil, Cut up boneless chuck, Or more beef stew meat or, Carrots, sliced, Onion, diced, 
Brown the meat in the oil and onion soup mix  combination. Add 1 1/2 - 2 cups water, and cook in a  pressure cooker until done, about 40 minutes. Add  onion, carrots, and potatoes. (you can add celery as  well). Simmer slowly until vegetables are done, adding  water as necessary.  Thicken gravy and serve.
