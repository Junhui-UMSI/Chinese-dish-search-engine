Baked Potato Topping
Mayonnaise, Tarragon To Taste, Basil To Taste, Thyme To Taste, Oregano To Taste, Garlic Powder To Taste, Rosemary To Taste, 
Blend together the mayonnaise, tarragon, garlic powder, thyme, oregano,  basil and rosemary.  Top baked potato and blend in well with a fork. From:  Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sydsbook.zip
