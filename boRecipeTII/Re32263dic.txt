Barbecued-Pork Sandwiches, Lhj
Coleslaw, Boneless pork roast, barbecue sauce, Salt, Black Pepper, Soft hamburger rolls, 
1. Heat oven to 325F. Sprinkle pork with salt and pepper. Heat a Dutch  oven 1 minute over medium-high heat. Brown pork on all sides, about 4  minutes per side.    2. Add barbecue sauce to Dutch oven; cover and bake 1 1/2 hours until pork  is fork-tender.    3. Transfer pork to cutting board. Slice into thin pieces. Return to Dutch  oven and cook 5 minutes over medium heat. Place rolls on serving plates.  Divide pork mixture and spoon into each roll..    Prep time: 20 minutes Baking time: 1 1/2 hours    (C) Copyright 1997, Meredith Corporation, All Rights Reserved.    Serving Ideas : Serve with coleslaw if desired    NOTES : Heres serious barbecue, southern style: pork slow-roasted until  fork-tender and piled on a soft roll to soak up the juices. Prepared  barbecue sauce comes in a variety of flavors: hot, mild, sweet or spicy;  choose your favorite for a custom-tailored taste.
