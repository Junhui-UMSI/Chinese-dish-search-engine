Asian Grilled Chicken
Rice vinegar, Minced fresh ginger root, Minced scallions, Garlic, Black bean paste, Peanut oil, Soy sauce, Liquid hot pepper sauce, Boneless skinless chicken, Dry mustard, White wine, 
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Cover and chill for 2-4 hours or  overnight. Remove chicken from marinade, drain well.    Grill 5 minutes per side or until cooked or, bake @ 375 for 30  minutes(until juices run clear when pierced with a fork). Remove to a  serving platter. Heat marinade until boiling and reduce a little. Simmer 3  minutes. Serve over grilled chicken. Garnish with cilantro leaves, if  desired.
