Tropical Delight
Salt, (8oz.) crushed pineapple, Butter, Sugar, Flour, (3.5 oz.) coconut, Eggs; slightly beaten, Frozen pie shell, 
Mix all ingredients together and pour in pie shell. Bake for 45 minutes in  a 350F oven.    Source: Egg Recipe Contest Winners leaflet by Georgia Egg Commission    Nutritional analysis per serving: calories 293, protein 3 g fat 21 g (15 g  saturated, 6 g unsaturated), cholesterol 96 mg, sodium 308 mg, calcium 16  mg.
