Barbecue Sauce #14
Honey, (4-oz) of sliced mushrooms, Basil, Oregano, (16-oz) of no-salt tomato, Generous amount of chopped, Chili powder, (at least) of any other, 
Mix well and add to meat before you cook it.  It also works really well  for baked chicken.
