Pie Crust
Other shortening, Flour, Salt, Water, ice, Lard, *or:, 
Add salt to the flour, then rub flour and shortening together, using hands  or a pastry blender. Add enough cold water to make a dry paste. Roll out  and fit in pit pan. Dont stretch pastry when fitting into pan, as this  will cause shrinkage in finished product. Source: Pennsylvania Dutch Cook  Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
