Baked Fresh Ham
Beer, Dried Rosemary, garlic, Ground thyme, Fresh ham, olive oil, 
Trim excess fat from ham. Mix oil with herbs and rub all over ham. Bake at  350 degrees for 30-35 minutes per pound of meat, until meat thermometer  registers 185 degrees. Baste occasionally with beer.    Recipe By     : Elizabeth Powell    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
