Arugula, Red Onion and Shaved Parmesan Salad
Fresh Lemon Juice, Salt, olive oil, Red onion, peeled, cut into, Shaved parmesan, Arugula leaves, trimmed,, DRESSING, Dijon-style mustard, 
In a large bowl whisk together the lemon juice and mustard, seasoning with  salt and pepper to taste. Drain the red onion and pat dry. Just prior to  serving the salad, add the arugula and red onion to the dressing and toss  to coat. Taste and adjust seasoning with additional lemon juice, salt and  pepper if necessary. Sprinkle the salad with the shaved Parmesan and serve  immediately.
