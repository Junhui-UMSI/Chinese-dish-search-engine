Susiecue Pie
Brown sugar, Sugar, Butter, Pecan or hickory nuts;, Eggs, Vanilla, milk, Flour, 
Mix together the sugars and flour, then add the eggs, vanilla, milk and  butter. Beat the mixture until it is thick, then pour it into an unbaked  pie crust. Bake it at 375 oF for 20 minutes, then sprinkle the nuts over  the top. Continue baking the pie until the filling has set.
