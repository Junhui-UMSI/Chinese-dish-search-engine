Apple Dumpling Dessert
Sugar, apples, Salt, milk, Cinnamon, Water, Cinnamon, Margarine, Flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, Shortening, 
Combine sugar, water, and cinnamon in med. saucepan and bring to a boil.  Remove from heat. Add margarine adn set aside.    Sift together flour, baking powder, salt and then mix with shortening and  milk.  Roll dough onto floured surface about 1/4 inch thick. Spread apples  over surface of dough. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar. Roll up. Slice  into 12 1 inch rolls. Put in 9 x 13 baking pan. Pour syrup over.
