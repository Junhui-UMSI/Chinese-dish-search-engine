White Bean and Herb Sandwich Spread
Pepper, Fresh Lemon Juice, Lettuce, Beans, Cucumber, peeled and thinly, Snipped chives, Favorite bread (recipe, Using canned beans), Dried dill), Salt (can eliminate this if, Chopped fresh dill (or 1, Non fat cream cheese, Chopped fresh parsley, Recommends dark rye), Cooked and drained white, Sprouts, Sliced, 
Combine first 8 ingredients in a food processor and process until smooth.  Taste for seasonings and add more herbs or lemon juice if necessary. Spread  mixture on bread, top with cucumber, lettuce and sprouts. Put sandwiches  together and slice.    From my "Best of Vegetarian Times" collection.
