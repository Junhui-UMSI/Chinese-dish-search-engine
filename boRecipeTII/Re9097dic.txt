Pronto Picante Chicken Tacos
Cheddar cheese, Vegetable oil, Boneless chicken breasts;, Plus 1 Tbs. sour cream, Prepared salsa, Taco shells, Shredded lettuce, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 15 min.    Heat oil in a heavy nonstick skillet over medium high heat. saute chicken 2  minutes, or until no longer pink. Stir in salsa and reduce heat to medium.  Simmer 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally. Heat taco shells as directed  on package. Fill each taco shell with about 3 tablespoons chicken. Top with  lettuce, cheese and sour cream.
