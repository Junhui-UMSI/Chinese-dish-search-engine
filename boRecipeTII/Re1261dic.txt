Czech Potato Soup
Mushrooms, fresh, Water, Celery stalk, Carrot, Onion, Caraway seeds, Marjoram, Salt, Potatoes, Flour, Butter, Pepper, 
Stir mushrooms into boiling water, add celery, carrots, onion, caraway  seeds, marjoram, salt and pepper, and cook slowly for 1 hour. Add potatoes;  simmer 40 minutes more. Make thickening by slightly browning flour and  butter in skillet. Add to soup and bring to boil before serving.    Yield: 4 to 6 servings.    Variation: Pierce (to drain grease) and brown chunks of kielbasa and add to  soup.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Dec 06, 98
