Versatile Salad Dressing
All-purpose flour, Mayonnaise, Ground mustard, Eggs, lightly beaten, Vinegar, Sugar, Water, Salt, 
In a saucepan, combine sugar, flour, salt and mustard; stir in eggs.  Gradually stir in vinegar and water until smooth. Bring to a boil over  medium heat, stirring constantly; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Cover and  refrigerate. Just before serving, combine desired amount of base with and  equal amount of mayonnaise. Serve as a dressing for potato salad, coleslaw  or salad greens. Refrigerate leftovers.
