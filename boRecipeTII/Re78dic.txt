Key Lime Pie
Graham cracker crumbs, Sugar, Margarine; melted, FILLING, Sugar, Cornstarch, Salt, Eggs, Key lime juice, Grated lime rind, Margarine, 
In a med. bowl combine graham cracker crumbs, 2 tablespoons of sugar and 4  tablespoon melted margarine. Press into bottom and sides of a greased 9"  pie pan. Chill for 15 mins. Meanwhile in a med. saucepan, stir in reaining  sugar, cornstarch and salt. Whisk in eggs and 1 cup of water. Over med-low  heat, bring to a boil 1 min., stirring constantly or until mixture  thickens. Remove from heat and stir in juice, peel and remaining margarine.  Pour into prepared pie shell. Refrigerate 2 hours or until firm. Garnish  with whipped cream if desired.
