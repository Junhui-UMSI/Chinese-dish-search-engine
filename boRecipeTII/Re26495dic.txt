Bacon Roll-Ups
Bacon, white bread, Cream of mushroom soup, Toothpicks, 
Cut bacon in thirds. Trim edges off bread & cut in three strips. Spread  each strip with undiluted soup. Roll up & wrap bacon around each. Secure  with a toothpick. Place on cookie sheet. These may be frozen at any time.  Bake at 450 degrees until crisp & brown, or broil.    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
