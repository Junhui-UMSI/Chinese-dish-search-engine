Amish Bread
Baking soda, Cinnamon, Quick Oats, BREAD STARTER-----, Nuts, Eggs, Baking Powder, Flour, Yeast, Vanilla, Sugar, Brown sugar, COFFEE CAKE BATTER-----, Cinnamon, STREUSEL TOPPING-----, Butter, Nuts, Sugar, Warm water, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Flour, Salt, 
STARTER: Mix all in ingredients well and place into a GLASS bowl. Cover  loosely with a lid or plate. Let stand overnight in a warm place. Stir each  day( Use no metal spoons!!)until the 5th day, then add 1 cup flour, 1 cup  milk, and 1 cup sugar.Stir the 6th, 7th, 8th and 9th days. On the 10th day  add 1 cup flour, 1 cup milk and 1 cup sugar. Then remove 3 cups of    starter.(give one cup to each of two friends with the instructions on  feeding, ect..) To the remaining starter add the ingredients for the coffee  cake batter. Combine all the batter ingredients, mixing well. Pour half the  batter into a greased 9x13 inch pan.Mix together the streusel topping  ingredients. Pourhalf into pan. Cover with the remaining batter. Top with  the rest of the streusel. Bake at 350 F for 40-50 minutes, or until cake  tests done. From *Prodigy* Food and Wine Bulletin Board.
