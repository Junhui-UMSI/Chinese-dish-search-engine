Bakewell Tart
Almond extract, Raspberry jam, Sugar, Egg, Cake crumbs, unbaked pie crust, milk, Butter, almonds, 
Cream butter & sugar; add egg & beat well. Add cake crumbs, almonds,  extract & milk; mix thoroughly. Spread jam over pie crust & fill with  mixture. Bake at      400    for 30 minutes.    MRS K.R.
