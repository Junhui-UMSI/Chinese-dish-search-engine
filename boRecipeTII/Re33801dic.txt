Sweet and Sour Meatloaf
Brown sugar, Onion, Tomato sauce, Mushrooms, Ground beef, Vinegar, Pepper, Crushed crackers, Mustard, Egg, Salt, 
Combine first 4 ingredients to make sauce.  Combine remaining ingredients,  kneading together with hands. Add 1/2 tomato sauce mixture. Put in loaf  pan. Pour remaining sauce over loaf. Bake at 400 degrees for 45 minutes.
