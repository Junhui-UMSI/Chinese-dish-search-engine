Bachelor Dinner
Popcorn bowl, Bag Pop Secret with butter, 
Open microwave door. Remove plastic wrapping from popcorn bag. Place in  microwave "this side up". Program microwave 4 minutes, high power. Press  start button. Listen for popcorn to stop popping or smell of burnt popcorn.  Press stop button. (If burnt, repeat step one with new bag.) Serve in  designated popcorn bowl.
