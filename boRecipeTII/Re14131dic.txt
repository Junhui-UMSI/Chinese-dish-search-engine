White Corn Salad
Chopped cucumber, Chopped tomato, White wine vinegar, Chopped green bell pepper, Pepper, Garlic, Frozen shoepeg white corn,, Dijon mustard, olive oil, Salt, 
Combine first 4 ingredients in a bowl; toss. Combine vinegar and next 5  ingredients in a jar. Cover; shake vigorously. Pour over corn mixture; toss  well. Yield: 3 servings (serving size: 1 cup).    Per serving: 433 Calories; 7g Fat (14% calories from fat); 11g Protein; 86g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 242mg Sodium    Recipe by: Cooking Light, June 1994, page 105    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #395 by igor@digex.net on Jan 28, 1997.
