Ziti Casserole
Sliced mushrooms (5 c.), Oregano, Ziti, Pepper, Salt, Onions, Spaghetti sauce (1 qt.), Red wine, Ground beef, Garlic, Mozzarella cheese, shredded, Sour cream, 
Cook pasta, reducing cooking time by 5 minutes. Meanwhile, saute beef and  onions until beef is well browned. Use 2 large skillets or brown half at a  time. Add mushrooms and garlic and cook 3 minutes. Drain ziti. Combine meat  mixture, sauce, wine, oregano, salt and pepper. Add ziti and mix. Pour into  2 greased 13 x 9 inch baking dishes and sprinkle with cheese. Cover with  foil. Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes. Serve with sour cream. ]]]]]    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #186 by Julie Bertholf  <jewel1@ix.netcom.com> on Jul 18, 1997
