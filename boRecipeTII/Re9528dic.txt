From: hammond@odin.scd.ucar.edu (Steve Hammond)    Date: Thu, 14 Oct 1993 19:40:17 GMT  Graham cracker crust: mix and press into bottom and sides 10" pie plate or  9" spring form bake at 350 for 10 min.    Filling: cream cheese and sugar, beat in eggs 1 at a time, add pumpkin,  spices and brandy mixing well.  Spoon into crust and bake at 325 for 50 -  60 min.
