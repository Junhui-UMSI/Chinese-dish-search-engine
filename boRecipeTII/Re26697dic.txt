Baileys Fudge Cake
Butter, Eggs, Baileys, Sour cream; room temperature, Flour, Baileys, Instant coffee crystals; opt, Pecan halves, NORMA WRENN, Chocolate syrup, GLAZE, Pecans, Brown sugar, Semi-sweet chocolate, 
Preheat oven to 350~. Grease a 9" round cake pan. With mixer, cream butter  and sugar, blend in eggs. Add chocolate syrup, Baileys, coffee crystals and  flour, mixing until well blended. Fold in chopped pecans. Pour into  prepared pan. Bake 55-60 minutes, until center is firm and a toothpick  inserted in center comes out clean. Remove from pan onto wire rack to cool.  Dip one end of pecan halves halfway into melted chocolate morsels to coat,  place on waxed paper; chill to set chocolate.    To remaining melted chocolate add sour cream, 1 tablespoon at a time, and  Baileys. Spread over top of cooled cake that has been placed on a serving  platter, letting some chocolate drizzle down side of cake.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "M.
