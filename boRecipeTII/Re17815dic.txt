Almost Instant Mushroom Sauce W/fettucci
Fresh white mushrooms;, Salt, Fettuccine (uncooked), olive oil, Lowfat ricotta cheese, Diced tomato, Chopped fresh basil or, Dried basil; crushed, Green Onions, Garlic, 
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain, reserving 1/4 cup  cooking liquid; return pasta to pot. Meanwhile, in large skillet heat oil  until hot.    Add mushrooms, green onions and garlic; cook, stirring frequently, until  mushrooms are tender, 5 to 6 minutes. Stir in tomato, basil and salt; cook  until tomato is just warm, about 1 minute. To pasta in pot stir in ricotta  cheese and enough cooking liquid to make a creamy sauce. Add mushroom  mixture; toss. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and ground black pepper,  if desired.
