Sweet Pea Salad
Petit pois green peas;, Plain gelatin, Sweet pickle relish, Roll garlic cheese; cubed, Cold water, Mayonnaise, Hard boiled eggs; chopped, Minced celery, Onion, 
Dissolve gelatin in cold water. Mix all ingredients together and place in  ring mold. Chill overnight. Serve on a bed of lettuce. This is also good  when ham salad is placed in center of ring.    REFRIGERATE OVERNIGHT    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
