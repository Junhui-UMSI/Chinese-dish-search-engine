Apple Muffins
Vanilla, To be small pieces or the, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Chopped apples (these need, Muffins, Tend to fall apart)., BS, Egg white, beaten, Flour, Salt, Apple sauce, Sugar, 
Add apples at the end and fold in gently.  Can add raisins if you like.  Bake at 350 for 20 min.  Makes 8 muffins.
