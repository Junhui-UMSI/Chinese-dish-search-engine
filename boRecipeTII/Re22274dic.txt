Turkey Tostadas
(8 Oz.) Tomato Sauce, C +2 T. Plain Low Fat Yogurt, Dried Oregano, Dash Of Hot Sauce, Onion, Thyme, Ground turkey, (6 in.) Flour Tortillas, Minced, (1 1/2 Oz.), Garlic Minced, + 2 t. Mozzarella Cheese, Jalapeno Pepper, Seeded &, 
Place Tortillas On A Baking Sheet, Bake At 350 F. For 7 To 10 Min. OR Until  Crisp.  Set Aside. Combine Turkey, Onion, Garlic & Jalapeno Pepper in A  Large Skillet Coated With Cooking Spray; Cook Over Medium Heat Until Meat  Is No Longer Pink, Stirring To Crumble Meat. Add Tomato Sauce, Oregano,  Thyme & Hot Sauce; Stir Well. Simmer 10 Min. OR Until Thickened. Spread  About 1/3 C. Turkey & Tomato Sauce Mixture Over Each Reserved Tortilla;  Spinkle Each With 1 T. Mozzarella Cheese. Broil 8 Inches From Heat Until  Cheese Melts. Top Each Tostada With 1 T. Lowfat Yogurt. Garnish With  Jalapeno Pepper Slices If Desired.
