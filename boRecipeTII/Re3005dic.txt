Peanut Butter Thumbprint Cookies
Vanilla, Cinnamon, coconut, Baking soda, PEANUT BUTTER FILLING, Water, Brown sugar, Peanut butter morsels, Peanut butter morsels, Flour, Egg, Salt, Sugar, Honey, Butter, 
Preheat oven to 400 F. In top of doulbe boiler over hot ( not boiling)  water, melt 1 1/2 cups peanut butter morsels, set aside. In small  bowl, combine flour, salt and baking soda, set aside. In large mixing  bowl, combine butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, egg and vanilla,  beat at medium speed of electric mixer until creamy. Blend in melted  peanut butter morsels. Gradually add flour mixture. Using rounded  teaspoonsfuls, shape into 1 inch balls. Place 2 inches apart on  ungreased cookie sheet. Make thumbprint in center of each cookie.  Fill each cookie with 1/4 teaspoonful of Peanut Butter Filling(  recipe follows)Bake 5-7 minutes. Cool 2 minutes, remove from cookie  sheet and cool completely on wire rack. In small bowl, combine  coconut, honey, water and cinnamon, mix well. Stir in remaining 1/2  cup unmelted peanut butter morsels. Put inside thumbprint. Makes  about 5 dozen.
