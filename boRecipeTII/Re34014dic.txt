Basil Vinaigrette
olive oil, Fresh Basil, Green onions; finely chopped, Pepper, Salt, White wine vinegar, 
Process all ingredients in a food processor or blender, stopping once to  scrape down sides. Cover mixture, and chill. Stir well, and toss with mixed  greens. Makes 1 1/3 cups.    Recipe by: Southern Living    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1051 by SuzyWert@aol.com on Jan 25, 1998
