Potluck Special
Onion, Rice, Green pepper; chopped, (28 oz) tomatoes with, Sauerkraut; rinsed,drained, Ground beef, 
In a skillet, brown the ground beef and onion; drain. Add remaining  ingredients; transfer to a 2-quart baking dish. Cover and bake at 350? F.  for 1 hour.    NOTE: Reta says "Its delicious for sauerkraut lovers and easy to double  for a larger crowd".    Recipe by: Reta Christensen    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #981 by "M.
