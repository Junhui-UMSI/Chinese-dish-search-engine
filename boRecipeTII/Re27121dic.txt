Tibetan Potato Soup
Turmeric, Chopped green onion, Chili powder, Chopped cilantro, Diced tofu, Water, Diced red onion, Garlic, Kopan masala, Mashed potato, Spinach leaves;chopped, Soy sauce, Salt, Ginger root, Black pepper, Butter, White vinegar, 
Date: Tue, 23 Apr 1996 20:12:34 -7    From: arlenes@holly.ColoState.EDU  Melt butter in large saucepan over medium heat. Add ginger, garlic and  onion and stir-fry over medium to medium-high heat for 1/2 to 1 minute. Add  turmeric, chili powder and masala. Stir-fry 1/2 minute longer.  Add potato  and mix. Cook and stir 3 minutes. Add water 1 cup at a time, stirring  constantly with wire whisk to prevent lumps from forming.    Stir until mixture is smooth. Add tofu and spinach.  Mix well and bring to  boil. Add vinegar, soy sauce, salt and pepper. Simmer 5 minutes. If soup is  too thick, add water. Add green onions and cilantro and mix well. makes  about 8 cups.
