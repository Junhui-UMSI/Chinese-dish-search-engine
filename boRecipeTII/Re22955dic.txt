Arlenes Strawberry Pie
Sugar, (heaping) cornstarch, White Karo, Strawberries, Salt, Baked 9-inch pie shell, Water, Strawberry Jell-o, 
Notes: Arlene Kresge, McMichaels    Mix together water, sugar, cornstarch, corn syrup and salt. Cook until  clear in appearance and you are unable to taste the cornstarch. Remove from  heat and add 1/4 tsp. red food coloring and Jell-o and stir. Pour over  strawberries, stirring gently. Pour into cooled 9-inch pie shell and  refrigerate. Cool until set. If desired, top with Cool Whip or any other  whipped topping before serving.    Posted to recipelu-digest by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I Canty) on Mar 6, 1998
