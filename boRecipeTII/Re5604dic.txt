Pineapple Sausage Oven Pancakes
Baking Powder, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Sugar, (8 oz.) sliced pineapple;, Margarine; melted, Flour, Egg, Sausage; (8 oz) cut, Salt, milk, Sugar, 
In mixing bowl combine eggs, milk and margarine. Stir together flour, the  first tablespoon sugar, the baking powder, salt and nutmeg. Stir into  liquid ingredients just until moistened. Stir in pineapple. Spread in  greased 12x9 inch baking dish. Arrange sausage stop, sprinkle with  remaining sugar and cinnamon. Bake 400 oven for 18 to 20 minutes or until  done. Cut in squares. Serve hot. Makes 6.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Mar 16, 1998
