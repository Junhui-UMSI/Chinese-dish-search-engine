Raspberry Currant Syrup
Raspberries; 500 g, Currants; 500 g, Sugar, white; 2 kg, -Cold water, 
To serve, add a few tablespoons to cold or sparkling water    Put sugar and water in a large pot, bring to a boil and skim it. Add the  raspberries and currants and boil gently about twenty minutes. Strain  through a sieve, cool and bottle. Keep in a cool place.    MAKES: 1 BOTTLE SOURCE: _The Non-Drinkers Drink Book_ by Gail Schioler
