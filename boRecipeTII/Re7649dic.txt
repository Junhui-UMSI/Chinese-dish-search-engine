Pork Tenderloin with Bourbon Sauce - Chi-Chis
Pepper, Honey, green chilies, Bourbon, Onions, Pork tenderloin, Worcestershire Sauce, Soy sauce, Dijon mustard, 
Combine all marinade ingredients in recloseable plastic food bag.  Mix  well.  Add the pork tenderloin.  Seal bag and turn several times to  coat the meat.  Place in refrigerator for 8 hours or overnight,  turning occasionally.    Preheat broiler.  Remove meat from marinade; reserve marinade.    Place meat on broiler pan, broil 7 to 8 inches from heat source for  approximately 7 to 9 minutes on each side.    In small saucepan, bring remainder of marinade to a boil; boil one  minute. Serve with the meat.    Chi Chis Booklet  == Courtesy of Dale & Gail Shipp, Columbia Md. MMMMM
