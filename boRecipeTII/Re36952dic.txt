Strawberry Waldorf Salad
Boiling water, apples, Walnuts, strawberry gelatin, Sour cream, celery, strawberries, Sugar, 
Dissolve gelatin mixed with sugar in boiling water. Cool. Add strawberries,  apples, celery, walnuts and mix well. Chill until almost jelly-like. Add  sour cream. Pour into 5 cup ring mold and chill until firm. Center may be  filled with chicken salad.
