All American Barbecued Beans
Prepared mustard, Equal Spoonful sweetener, White vinegar, Salt, Fat-free turkey bacon, Onions, Water, Ketchup, Black pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Great northern beans, 
Recipe By : Sweet Ideas, Great Recipes From Equal Sweetener Serving Size  : 4 Preparation Time :0:00 Categories  : Bean    Cook bacon pieces in medium saucepan over medium-high heat four minutes.  Add onions and cook until bacon is crisp and onions are tender. In a small  bowl, combine ketchup, white vinegar, water, mustard, worcestershire sauce,  salt, black pepper, and beans. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until  flavors are blended, 20 minutes. Add sweetener.
