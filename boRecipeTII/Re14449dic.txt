Whole Wheat Raisin Scones
Salt, Egg, Whole wheat flour, Baking soda, milk, Sugar, Flour, Margarine, Nutmeg, Raisins, Cinnamon, Baking Powder, 
Combine 2 T. sugar with remaining dry ingredients. Cut in margarine until  crumbly. Beat eggs and reserve 1 T. Stir remaining eggs, milk, and raisins  into crumb mixture. Knead dough 5 times. Divide in half & pat into 5-inch  circle. Cut each into 8 wedges and place on greased cookie sheet. Brush  reserved egg over wedges and sprinkle with reserved sugar. Bake at 425  degrees for 17 minutes or until browned. Serve warm. Makes 16 scones, 130  calories each.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #095 by "Mark " <mviers@inmind.com> on Apr  6, 1997
