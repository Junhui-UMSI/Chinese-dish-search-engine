Arkayagan Abour (Meatball Soup)
Cooked rice, ground wheat, Eggs, Salt, pepper, Ground twice, Lean venison or lamb,, Finely chopped parsley, Onion, Condensed chicken broth, Water, Or bulghour, (10-1/2 ounces each), Lemon juice, 
Combine first four ingredients.  Shape into 3/4-inch balls. Heat broth and  water to the simmering point. Add meatballs; simmer 15 to 20 minutes. In a  soup tureen, beat lemon juice and eggs until smooth. Gradually beat in hot  broth.  Add meatballs last. Season to taste with salt, pepper.    Adaption from recipe by George Mardikian, Omar Khayyams Restaurant  Campbells Great Restaurants Cookbook, U.S.A.
