Sweet Corn Bread
Yellow cake mix; (18.5 oz.), Corn bread mix; (15 oz.), 
Mix cake and corn bread batter according to package directions. Blend  together and turn into 2 greased (9-inch) square baking pans. Bake at 350  degrees for 30 to 35 minutes. Makes       32    servings.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 297 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 23, 1997
