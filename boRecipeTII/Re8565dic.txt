Potatoes Lyonnaise
Salt, Fresh Idaho potatoes, cut, Pepper, Butter or margarine, Garlic, Paprika, Onions, sliced, 
In 3-quart microwave-safe casserole combine all ingredients. Cover loosely  with plastic wrap; cook on High 15 to 20 minutes or until potatoes and  onions are tender, stirring several times. Let stand 5 minutes. Makes 6  servings.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
