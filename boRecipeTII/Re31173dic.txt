Banana Walnut Bundt Cake
bananas, Walnuts, Plain low-fat yogurt, Baking Powder, Vanilla extract, Eggs, Salt, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Sugar, Shortening, 
Preheat oven 350 degrees. Grease and flour a 10-inch bundt pan. In  large bowl, with mixer at low speed, beat sugar and shortening just  until blended. Add flour and remaining ingredients except walnuts and  confectioners sugar. Beat until well mixed, constantly scraping bowl  with rubber spatula. Increase speed to high; beat two minutes,  occasionally scraping bowl. Stir in walnuts. Spoon batter into bundt  pan. Bake 65 -70 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center of  cake comes out clean. Cool cake in pan on wire rack 10 minutes;  remove from pan. Cool completely on rack.
