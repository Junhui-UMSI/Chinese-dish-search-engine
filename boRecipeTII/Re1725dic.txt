Cheese Scalloped Potatoes
Butter, milk, Onion, paprika, fresh parsely, Cheddar cheese soup, potatoes, pepper, 
Combine soup, milk, parsley, and pepper.  In 1 1/2qt casserole arrange  layers of potatoes, soup and onion. Dot with butter. Sprinkle with paprika.  Bake at 375~F for 1 hour covered. Uncover, and bake 15 minutes more or  until done. Makes 3 1/2 cups. Bake until done. If not quite done after  removing the foil, and its getting too brown on top, replace the foil for  a while longer.
