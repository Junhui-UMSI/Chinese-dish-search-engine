Sweet-And-Sour Beef and Pork Meatballs
Green pepper; slivered, Instant minced onion, Sherry (optional), Cooking oil, Ground beef, Drained pineapple chunks, Hot cooked rice, Cornstarch, Onion thinly sliced, Soy sauce, Beef bouillon cube, Sugar, Ground lean pork, (3 ounces) chopped mushrooms, Vinegar, 
Drain mushrooms, reserving liquid. Add enough water to liquid to make 1 cup  and set aside. Mix meats, mushrooms, sherry, instant onion, and 2  tablespoons soy sauce. Shape into 12 to 16 balls and brown on all sides in  hot oil. Drain off fat. Add 1 cup reserved liquid and bouillon cube. Cover  and simmer for 30 minutes. Add next 3 ingredients. Blend 2 teaspoons soy  sauce and remaining ingredients. Stir gradually into hot mixture. cover and  cook for 10 minutes, stirring frequently. Serve on rice.
