The Ultimate Chewy Oatmeal Cookie
Walnuts; coarsely chopped, Quick oats;  uncooked, Salt, Flour, milk, Egg, Vanilla, Butter flavor Crisco, Light brown sugar; packed, Cinnamon, Raisins, Baking soda, 
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.   Grease a baking sheet with butter flavor  Crisco    Combine the Crisco, sugar, egg, milk, vanilla in a large bowl. Beat at  medium speed with a mixer until blended.    Combine the oats, flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. Mix into the  creamed mixture at low speed until just blended. Stir in the raisins and  nuts.    Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet.    Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until lightly browned. Let cool.
