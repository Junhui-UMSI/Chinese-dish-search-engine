Ugly Duckling Cake
Eggs, Yellow cake mix, Evaporated milk, Butter or oleo, Coconut, Firmly packed brn. sugar, Fruit coctail (16 oz.), Granulated sugar, 
Combine cake mix, fruit cocktail with syrup, 1 cup coconut and eggs in  lge. mixer bowl.  Blend, then beat at med. speed for 2 min. Pour into  prepared 13 x 9 x 2 sheet cake pan. Sprinkle with brn. sugar. Bake at 325  F. for 45 min. or until cake springs back when lightly touched. Bring  butter, granulated sugar and milk to boil in saucepan. Boil 2 min. Remove  from heat, stir in remaining coconut. Spoon over hot cake in pan.
