WW Brownies
Baking Powder, Eggs, Chocolate Alba Shake Mix, Baking soda, Applesauce, Sugar Twin, Cocoa, 
Pour all ingredients into a blender or food processor and mix well.  Spray 2 mini loaf pans with non stick cooking spray and pour batter in.  Bake 350 degrees for 18 minutes. Do not over bake, this should be chewy.  1P, 1B, 1/2M, 14 OPT CAL.
