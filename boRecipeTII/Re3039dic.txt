Peanut Cake
Vanilla, Ground Spanish peanuts, Eggs, Egg yolk, Baking soda, Sugar, Sour milk, Baking Powder, Shortening, Flour, 
Cream shortening and sugar; add eggs. Mix thoroughly. Sift together flour,  baking powder and baking soda. Add to creamed mixture, alternating with  sour milk. Beat 3 minutes. Stir in vanilla and peanuts. Mix thoroughly.  Bake at 350 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes in prepared 9 x 13-inch pan.  Frost, then sprinkle with reserved peanuts.
