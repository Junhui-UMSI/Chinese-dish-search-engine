Alfredos Noodles
Unsalted butter; melted, Salt to taste, Heavy cream; warmed, Grated Parmesan, Black Pepper, Noodles; 1/4 inch wide, 
Have a large bowl warmed and ready before you cook the noodles. Drain the  cooked noodles and put them into the bowl. Quickly add remaining  ingredients, tossing briskly to coat all the noodles, and serve at once.    Recipe is from _The Fannie Farmer Cookbook_, 13th edition.
