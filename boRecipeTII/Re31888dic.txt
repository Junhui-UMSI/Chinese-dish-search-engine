Tabbouleh (Lemon-Parsley Salad)
Hot water, Ripe tomatoes; chopped, Salt, Chopped (about 4 c), Medium yellow onion; grated, Lemon juice, Lg bunches parsley; finely, Fresh mint; chopped, Black Pepper, Separated into leaves, olive oil, Bulgar wheat, Head iceberg lettuce;, 
1. In lg bowl mix bulgar with hot water; let stand 1 hr. Pound onion with  pestle of back of heavy spoon into bulgar. 2. Mix chopped parsley,  tomatoes, and mint into bulgar. 3. Combine lemon juice, olive oil, salt,  and pepper and beat with fork until well blended. Pour over parlsey mixture  and mix well. 4. To serve, place Tabbouleh, surrounded by lettuce leaves,  on platter. To eat in the Lebanese manner, scoop up some Tabbouleh with  lettuce leaf and eat with your fingers.
