Venision Stew (Crockpot Style)
Carrots, Water, Potatoes, Seasonings, Venison stew meat, Beef bouillon , Onion, 
This is a cooks delight. In the morning, before work, place all  ingredients in crock pot & season. When you return home in the evening,  your stew will be ready. Add water before serving, if desired.    TERRY GRIFFITH    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
