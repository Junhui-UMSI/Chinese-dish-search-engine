Asparagus Supper Casserole
Fine dry bread crumbs, Sauterne, Seasoned salt, Flour, Chives, Butter or margarine, Fresh asparagus or 1; (10, milk, Tomato; thinly sliced, Hard-cooked eggs; sliced (4, Cheddar Cheese, Butter, 
Cook asparagus until tender, drain and cut into bite size pieces. Place  asparagus in 1 1/2-quart casserole. Place sliced, hard-cooked eggs over  asparagus. Melt butter in saucepan over very low heat. Blend in flour.  Gradually stir in milk and cook until thickened, stirring occasionally.    Blend in Sauterne, seasoned salt, chopped chives and grated Cheddar cheese.  Pour sauce over the eggs in casserole. Arrange thinly sliced tomato over  sauce. Combine and sprinkle bread crumbs and melted butter over top. Bake  at 375 degrees. Serves 4.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane&at;keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
