Autumn Pumpkin Bars
Salt, FROSTING, Cream cheese, Powdered sugar, Pumpkin; canned, Sugar, Cloves, Orange peel, Nutmeg, Orange juice, Flour, Vegetable oil, Raisins, Eggs, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Pecans, Margarine, Baking Powder, 
Grease 15x10" baking pan. In large bowl, blend all ingredients except  raisins and nuts. Blend 2 minutes. Stir in raisins and nuts. Spread evenly  in pan. Bake at 350~ for 25-30 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes  out clean. Cool. Frosting. In small bowl, beat cream cheese, margarine and  orange juice. Gradually beat in powdered sugar until smooth. Spread over  cooled bars.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #836 by Lynda Beaugez <beaugez@bellsouth.net>  on Oct 10, 1997
