Turkey and Rice Quiche
Medium tomato, seed & diced, Temp, (2 oz) Shredded Mozzarella, Green Onions, Seasoned salt, Chopped cooked turkey, Finely diced green pepper, Ground red pepper, Vegetable cooking spray, Cooked rice, cooled to room, Eggs, beated, Chopped fresh basil or, Dried basil, Skim milk, 
1.  Combine rice, turkey, tomato, onions, green pepper, basil, salt, red      pepper, milk, and eggs in 13x9x2" pan coated with cooking spray. Top      with cheeses. 2. Bake at 375F for 20 minutes or until knife inserted  near center comes      out clean.  To serve, cut quiche into 8 squares, cut each square      diagonally into 2 trianges. ~--
