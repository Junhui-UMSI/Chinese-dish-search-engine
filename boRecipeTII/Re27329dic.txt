Toad in the Hole (Ellen Cleary)
Baking Powder, Butter, Flour, Eggs, milk, Salt, Link sausages, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Prick sausages with fork.  Place in baking  pan and bake about 8 mins, long enough for fat to run out, grease pan and  let pan get hot.    Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.  Beat eggs, milk, and butter. Add to  dry ingredients and beat until bubbles rise to the surface Pour over  sausages and bake 30-35 mins longer, until batter is light brown and  puffed.          4    servings  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #006    From: BobbieB1@aol.com    Date: Sat, 4 Jan 1997 22:31:34 -0500
