Apple Butter Barbecue Sauce
Tomato sauce; 8 ozs, Pickapeppa sauce; or, Apple butter, 
For sauce, in a small saucepan combine all ingredients. Bring just to  boiling. Brush sauce onto burgers to last 10 mins of grilling. Heat any  remaining sauce just til bubbly; serve with burgers. Makes about 1 1/2 cups  sauce, enough for 1 to 2 lbs of meat.    Recipe by: Better Homes & Gardens  7/96    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by Cairn Rodrigues <cairnann@yahoo.com> on May  21, 1998
