Sweet and Sour Green Beans #1
(16-oz) green beans; drained, Vinegar, Bacon, QUICKIE VERSION, Green beans; drained, Onions, Vinegar, Almonds, REGULAR VERSION, Sugar, Onion, Sugar, Almonds, 
Fry bacon, crumble & save drippings. Combine sugar, vinegar & drippings.  Heat until sugar dissolves. Layer beans, onion & almonds in large  casserole. Pour sugar mixture over beans. Cover & bake at 350 for 1 hour.  Sprinkle with crumbled bacon before serving. Quickie version: Heat all  ingredients in small saucepan with a small amount of water for about 30  minutes.
