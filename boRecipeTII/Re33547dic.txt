Swedish Apple Pie
Vanilla, Sugar, Walnuts; chopped (optional), Egg whites, Flour, Baking Powder, Apple; finely chopped, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  In a large bowl, mix first ingredients; the  mixture will be thick.  Stir in chopped apple.  Spray a pie pan with no  stick spray.  Put mixture in the pan and bake for about 35 minutes.
