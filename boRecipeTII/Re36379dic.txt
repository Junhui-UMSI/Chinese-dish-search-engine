Beer and Cheese Dip
Catsup, Sharp Cheddar Cheese, Beer, 
Put cheese through a meat grinder or grate into a large bowl. Mix cheese  with catsup and beer until mixture is right for a dip. Refrigerate. Take  out an hour before the party so it will be ready to serve.    Serves 20 to 25    Source: "Mountain Measures"--Junior League of Charleston, WV ed. 1974    Typed for you by: billspa@icanect.net    Recipe by: Mrs James Cabell    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "Bill Spalding" <billspa@icanect.net> on May  3, 1998
