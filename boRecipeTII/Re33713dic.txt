Sweet and Simple Honey Mustard
Flour,all-purpose, Sugar, Mustard powder, Turmeric,ground, Cider vinegar, Water, Honey, 
1. Combine flour, sugar, mustard powder and turmeric in small bowl. Stir in  water. Let stand for 2 hours. 2. Combine mustard mixture, honey and vinegar  in top of double boiler. Cook, whisking often, over boiling water until  mixture just begins to thicken, about 3 minutes. Cook 2 minutes longer,  whisking constantly, until slightly thickened. (Mixture will thicken  completely on standing.) turn into clean storage jar. Cover and refrigerate  at least overnight or for up to 1 month. MUSTARD PICKLE RELISH: Combine 1/2  cup finely chopped dill pickles and 1/4 cup Sweet and Simple Honey Mustard  in small bowl. Makes about 3/4 cup relish.
