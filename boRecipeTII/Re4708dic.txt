Philly Cheesecake Brownies
Egg, Sugar, brownie mix, Vanilla, Cream cheese, 
Prepare brownie mix as directed on pkg.Pour into greased 13 9 baking pan.  Beat cream cheese with mixer until smooth.Add sugar,mixing until  blended.Add egg and vanilla.Pour cream cheese mixture over brownie  mixture;cut through batter with knife several times for marbled effect.  Bake at 350 for 35 to 40 minutes or until cream cheese mixture is lightly  browned.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 418 by "rudolph"  <rudolph@1st.net> on Dec 30, 1997
