Banana Bread #09
Sugar, Eggs, Baking Powder, Flour, Baking soda, Salt, milk, Chopped nuts (optional), Mashed ripe banana, Butter, 
From: c_walker@cc.colorado.edu    Date: 24 Mar 1994 11:18:15 -0500    Oven: 350.  Grease 8x4x2 bread pan.  Stir together first four ingredients.  Set aside.  In a bowl beat sugar and butter until light and fluffy,  scraping sides often. Add eggs one at a time. Add milk, beating until  smooth.  Add flour mixture and banana alternately, beating smooth after  each addition. Fold in nuts. Turn into pan and bake for 60 to 65 minutes or  until cake tester comes out clean. Cool in pan 15 minutes, remove and cool  completely.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
