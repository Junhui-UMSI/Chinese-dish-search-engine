Walnut Basil Coated Chicken
Cloves garlic, peeled, Walnut pieces, Parmesan cheese, grated, Fresh basil leaves, tightly, Red wine vinegar, olive oil, Boneless skinless chicken, 
Place basil in food processor. With motor running, drop in garlic and  process until basil and garlic are finely chopped, about 15 seconds. Add  walnuts, cheese, vinegar and oil. Process to make a rough paste, about 20  seconds. Smear evenly onto chicken breasts. Grill.
