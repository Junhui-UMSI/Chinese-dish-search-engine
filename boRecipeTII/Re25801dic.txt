Baby Carrots with Ginger and Sage Butter
garlic, Fresh sage, Fresh ginger, Unsalted butter, dried sage, Baby carrots, Brown Sugar, juice of 1/2 a lemon, Salt, 
Poach carrots in simmering water or chicken broth until just tender. Drain.  Melt butter and add brown sugar, ginger, lemon juice, salt, garlic and  sage; then add carrots and gently saute 2 to 3 minutes to blend flavors.
