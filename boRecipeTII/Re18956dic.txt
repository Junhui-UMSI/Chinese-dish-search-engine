Angel Hair Tomato Basil Toss
Salt, olive oil, Chopped cooked chicken or, Tomatoes; peeled and diced, Chopped fresh basil, Coarse ground black pepper, Chopped cooked turkey, Green Onions, VARIATIONS, Garlic, Grated Parmesan cheese, Chopped cooked ham or, Angel hair pasta; uncooked, Cooked, crumbled bacon or, 
In large skillet, heat oil.  Add garlic and green onions, stir-fry for one  minute.  Stir in tomatoes, basil, salt and pepper; cook 2 minutes, stirring  frequently.    Prepare pasta according to package directions; drain. Toss tomato mixture  with hot pasta, adding any cooked meats at this time. Serve immediately.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Refrigerate leftovers.    Yield: 4 to 6 servings.    Recipe on back of 1 lb. box, Creamette brand angel hair pasta.  Creamette/Borden, Inc./428 N. First St./Minneapolis, MN 55401.
