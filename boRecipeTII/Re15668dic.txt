A Mold of Roquefort
Envelope unflavored gelatin, Cream cheese, Heavy cream, Salt to taste, Water, Scallions, Black olives; chopped, Roquefort cheese, Pine nuts, 
Date: Sun, 10 Mar 1996 23:26:36 -0800    From: "sharon@sols.com" <sharon@sols.com>    Recipe By: Adapted from Dr. Atkins New Diet Cookbook  Note: grams noted in Notes section are for carbohydrate grams only.    Sprinkle gelatin over water and let stand. Push Roquefort cheese through  strainer. Add cream cheese and cream to Roquefort cheese. Mix well. Add  gleatin, scallions, pine nuts, olives and slat to cheese mixture. Mix well.  Spray a 3-cup ring mold with cooking spray and pour mixture into mold.  Chill, gently stir occasionally until set.
