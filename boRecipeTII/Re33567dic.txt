Swedish Christmas Crescents
Powdered sugar mixed with, Flour, DENISE BRADSHAW, Powdered sugar, BDGM08B-----, Vanilla, Salt, Almonds -- ground, Cinnamon, Butter, 
Cream butter and sugar.  Beat in vanilla, salt & ground almonds. Gradually  blend in flour.  Shape dough into crescents using a rounded teaspoonful for  each. sprinkle with cinnamon-sugar mixture & bake on ungreased cookie  sheets in 325 deg. oven 15-18 minutes.
