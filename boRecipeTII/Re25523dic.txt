Avocado and Pine Nut Salad
Rice vinegar, Dark sesame oil, Honey, avocado, pine nuts, Pink grapefruit, Low-sodium tamari, white-meat chicken, Lettuce, Ginger root, Herbal salt substitute, 
1. Wash and dry lettuce, then tear into bite-sized pieces. Place in a large  salad bowl. Add avocado, grapefruit sections, pine nuts, and chicken.    2. Toss together vinegar, honey, tamari sauce, gingerroot, oil, and salt  substitute. Pour over salad and mix well. Serve immediately.
