Amaretto Almond French Toast
Margarine, Amaretto, Eggs, French Bread, , Ice Cream, Slivered Almonds, Maple syrup, 
Melt ice cream; add eggs and beat until well-blended.  Add liqueur.  Dip  bread slices into mixture, coating well and pressing almonds from the ice  cream into slices; add extra almonds if desired.  In electric skillet,  grill or frying pan, melt margarine to 350 deg F and fry bread until  golden.  To serve, slightly overlap 3 slices, dust with confectioners  sugar and sprinkle with almonds.  Serve with maple syrup.    Recipe By     : Home Cooking, Oct 1994, pg 7    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
