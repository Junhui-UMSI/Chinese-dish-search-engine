Waldorf Chicken Sandwich
Apple, finely chopped, Celery stalk, finely chopped, Underwood Chunky Chicken, Cream cheese, softened (3oz), Raisins, Whole grain bread, 
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well. Cover and  refrigerate 1 hour. Serve on whole grain bread.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
