Barbecued Tempeh (Vegan)
Tamari, Vegetable oil, Molasses, Mustard powder, Cider vinegar, Lemon juice, Tempeh, cut to small cubes, Cumin, Fennel, Cayenne pepper, Onion, Garlic, Green pepper, chopped, Tabasco sauce, Tomato paste, Chili powder, Coriander, Water, 
Saute onions, garlic & spices till onions soften.  Add peppers & tempeh  chunks & continue to saute for 5 minutes or so. Transfer to a shallow  baking pan.    Whisk together the sauce ingredients (tamari, lemon juice, molasses,  vinegar, mustard, paste & water). Add to the vegetables. Bake for 30  minutes at 350F for 30 minutes covered & then for 30 minutes uncovered.  Stir frequently throughout.    Serve on rice with coleslaw or stuff into pita.
