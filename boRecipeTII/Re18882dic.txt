Angel Biscuits
Biscuit baking mix, Shredded coconut, Whipping cream, Margarine; melted, 
Preheat oven to 450.    Combine baking mix and coconut till well-blended.  Add whipping cream till  well-blended.    Turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead about one minute.    Using a rolling pin, roll out to about 1/2 inch thickness.    Cut into dough, place 2 inches apart on a greased baking sheet and brush  tops with melted margarine.    Bake 7 to 10 minutes, or till light brown.    Can be made ahead of time.    To reheat:  preheat oven to 350, cover biscuits and bake 3 to 5 minutes.
