Tomato Broccoli Chicken
Onions, Broccoli florets, Boneless skinless chicken, (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, Mozzarella cheese, shredded, olive oil, 
Brown chicken and onion in olive oil in skillet, about 3 minutes per side.  Add tomatoes to skillet. Bring to boil; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, 5  minutes. Add broccoli; simmer 5 minutes more or until chicken is done and  broccoli is tender.    Top with cheese during last 2 minutes. Serve over pasta or rice, if  desired.
