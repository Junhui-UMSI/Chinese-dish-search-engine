Baked Turkey Loaf
Egg white, Skim milk, Onion, Bell pepper, Ground turkey, Dried oregano, Whole-wheat bread crumbs, Black pepper, Celery, Fresh parsley, Garlic, 
Mix all ingredients until just blended.  Do not overmix. Press the mixture  into a 4 x 9-inch nonstick loaf pan. Bake at 325F for 45 minutes to 1 hour.  Serve hot with Mushroom Glaze (see recipe same name) or serve cold. Makes 6  servings.
