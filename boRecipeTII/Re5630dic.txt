Pineapple Stuffed Celery
Ground ginger, Salt, low-fat cottage, Crushed pineapple, celery, Sugar, 
Wash celery; trim and separate into ribs. Cut into 2 1/2 inch lengths.  Drain cottage cheese, if necessary. Mix cottage cheese, pineapple, sugar,  ginger and salt; spoon cottage cheese mixture into celery ribs, mounding  slightly. Garnish with paprika and celery leaves, if desired. Yield:    2 1/2    cups filling.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
