Amish Bread with Oatmeal
Oatmeal (quick), Baking Powder, Nutmeg, Sourdough starter, Vegetable oil, Light brown sugar, Baking soda, Flour, Cinnamon, Eggs, 
Mix all ingredients together.  Bake at 350-degrees for 40 minutes.    Recipe By     :    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
