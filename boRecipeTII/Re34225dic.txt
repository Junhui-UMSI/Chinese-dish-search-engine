Bayou Salad
Green pepper; diced, Ripe olives; sliced, Celery; sliced, Fat-free Italian salad, Crawfish; cooked and peeled, Lettuce leaves, Rice, Cherry tomatoes; quartered, 
Combine all ingredients except lettuce in large bowl. Serve on lettuce  leaves. Yield: Serves 6    Recipe by: RICE COUNCIL OF AMERICA    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #857 by Emily Griffin  <emgriffi@ix.netcom.com> on Oct 21, 1997
