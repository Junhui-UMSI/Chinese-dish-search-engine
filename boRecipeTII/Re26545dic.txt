Bacon-Wrapped and Cheddar Stuffed Dogs
Frankfurters, Wooden toothpicks, Hot dog rolls, Thick-cut bacon, Cheddar cheese; cut into 1/2, 
1. Preheat the broiler. Pat the frankfurters dry. In a skillet cook the  bacon for 3 minutes or until still pliable and not crisp.    2. Transfer to paper towels to drain.    3. Cut a 1/2-inch slit lengthwise from end to end of each frankfurter.  Stuff each frankfurter with the cheese.    4. Wrap the entire length of each frankfurter with 2 slices of the bacon  and secure with toothpicks.    5. Place the prepared frankfurters on a rack over a baking pan or on a  broiler pan. Broil the frankfurters until the bacon is crispy, about 2 to 3  minutes. Serve frankfurters in rolls.
