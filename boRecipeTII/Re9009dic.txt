Preventions Roasted Eggplant
Eggplant, 
PREPARATION: 1. Roast it, skin and all and preferably over a wood or  charcoal fire, until it completely collapses. 2. Slit or halve it, and  scrape the pulp into a strainer.  Allow the potentially bitter juices to  drain out for two to three hours or overnight (refrigerated). USE:  Substitute for meat when stuffing vegetables, or making baba ghannouj, or  add to soups and stews.    BABA GHANNOUJ - puree of roasted eggplant flavored with sesame tahini,  olive oil, lemon juice and garlic.
