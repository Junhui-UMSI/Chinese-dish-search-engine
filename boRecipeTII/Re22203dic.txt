Turkey Saltimbocca
Turkey cutlets; 3/8" thick, Muenster cheese, Prosciutto; folded in half, 40 mg cholesterol, 128 calories, 8 g fat, 295 mg sodium, Sage leaves, fresh, juliennd, 0 carbohydrate, Unsalted butter, HUTRITIONAL INFORMATION/SERV, 11 g fat, 
In large skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat.  Sprinkle cutlest with  1/8 t pepper; add to hot butter, pepper side down.  Cook 1 minute. Turn  over; top with prosciutto, cheese and sage.  Cover cook 1 minute or until  cheese melts.
