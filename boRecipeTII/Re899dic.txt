Hot Anchovy Dip (Bagna Cauda)
Butter, Garlic cloves, crushed, Anchovies, olive oil, Black pepper, Raw vegetables, Italian bread, 
Combine butter, olive oil, black pepper, and anchovies. Cook on low heat 10  minutes until the anchovies have dissolved. Pour into a Fondue pot or  chafing dish, to keep warm. Surround with slices of artichokes and raw  vegetables. Any leftovers may be used as sauce on pasta.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  01, 1998
