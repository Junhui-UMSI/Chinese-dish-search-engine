Peanut Butter Mug Puddle
Peanut butter, Chocolate syrup, milk, 
Put the peanut butter and the chocolate syrup into a nonmetal mug. Use a  small spoon to stir until mixed. Stir in the milk. Do not cover. Cook on  High for 1 minute. Stir and enjoy!  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 2,  1998
