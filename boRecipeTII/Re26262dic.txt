Tomato and Basil Salad
Cracked black pepper, Basil leaves, Avocado, sliced, Tomatoes, sliced, Balsamic vinegar, Goat cheese, crumbled, Sea salt, olive oil, 
Arrange tomatoes on serving platter. Sprinkle with sea salt and pepper to  taste. Tuck basil leaves between tomato slices. Drizzle with olive oil and  vinegar. Sprinkle with goat cheese and top with avocado slices. Formatted  by Lynn Thomas dcqp82a. Source: Los Angeles Times 4-16-97.6 servings.
