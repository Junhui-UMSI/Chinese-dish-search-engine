The Only Guacamole Recipe Youll Ever Need
Basil, Ripe avocadoes,, peeled and, Fresh Lemon Juice, Cloves garlic,, minced, Sour cream, optional, Salt,, or to taste, Salsa, Cilantro, 
Peel and mash one avocado. Mix with lemon juice, garlic, salt, basil and  cilantro. Dice the remaining avocado and fold into the mixture with the  salsa. Garnish with sour cream if desired. Makes about 2 cups.    NOTES : Serve with tortilla chips and/or fresh vegetables.
