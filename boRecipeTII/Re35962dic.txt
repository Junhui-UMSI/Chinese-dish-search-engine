Stuffed Peppers (Pa Dutch Style)
Grind together:, Accent, Green pepper ; lengthwise,, Med Onion, Cheese ;  colby long horn c, Corn ; can of (not creamed, 
mix  well  spray baking pan with Pam or Mazola spray to prevent sticking.  Place peppers in pan cut side up. Fill withthe filling. Bake at 350 until  cheese is bubbly and melted. NOTE: This mixture makes a good casserole by  itself or with cut up peppers.
