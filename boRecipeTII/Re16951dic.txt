Almond Biscotti
Whole almonds;, Veg oil, Baking Powder, Anise flavoring or vanilla, Sugar, Eggs, Unbleached flour, Grated zest of 1 orange;, Salt, 
Heat the oven to 350 and toast the almonds on a baking sheet for 10-15 min,  until they emit an aroma, but havent turned brown.    Put the flour, sugar, salt, powder, and grated zest in a bowl and combine  on med speed.    In a separate bowl, lightly beat w/ a whisk, eggs, oil and anise flavoring.    With the mixer running, pour the egg mixture into the flour mixture. When  almost mixed, reduce speed to low and add almonds and mix till dough just  comes together. Do not overmix.    Dump the dough onto a lightly floured surface ( or use confex sugar) and  knead by hand any remaining dry ingredients from the bottom of the bowl.    Divide the dough into 3 parts. Roll into a 10 inch logs (about 2 inches  around). Place the logs 4 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets. Bake at  350 for 35-45 min, until light brown. Let logs cool 15 min on baking sheet,  then slice diagonally into biscotti.    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by GEPlatts <GEPlatts@aol.com> on Jan 20, 1998
