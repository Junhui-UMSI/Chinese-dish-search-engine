Hot Cocoa Mix
Nonfat dry milk powder, Sugar, Powdered non-dairy creamer, Salt, 
In large bowl, combine all ingredients; blend well. Store in tightly  covered container. 3-3/4 cups mix (About fifteen 6-ounce servings).    Single Serving: Place 1/4 cup mix in heatproof cup or mug; add 3/4 cup  boiling water. Stir to blend. Serve hot, topped with marshmallow, if  desired.
