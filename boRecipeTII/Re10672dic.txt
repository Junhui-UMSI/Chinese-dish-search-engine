Quick Sesame Ginger Chicken
Honey, fresh ginger root, skinned, boned chicken breast, Sesame seeds, Vegetable cooking oil, soy sauce, Low-sodium, 
Combine first 4 ingredients.    Place chicken between 2 sheets of heavy duty plastic wrap and flatten to  1/4" thickness, using meat mallet or rolling pin.  Brush half of soy sauce  mixture over chicken, coating both sides.    Coat grill rack with cooking spray, place on grill over med-hot coals.
