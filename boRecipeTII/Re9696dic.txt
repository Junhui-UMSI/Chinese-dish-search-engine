Pumpkin Pie
Ground ginger, Eggs, Can cooked pumpkin, Dark brown sugar, firmly, Baked 9-inch pastry shell, Ground allspice, Can evaporated milk, Ground nutmeg, Flour, Sugar, Salt, Ground cinnamon, 
1. In a large mixing bowl combine all ingredients, except pastry shell, and  beat until well combined. 2. Pour pumpkin mixture into pastry shell. If  there is too much filling for the shell, the remaining filling may be  cooked in cups. 3. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4 minutes or until  edges begin to set. 4. Carefully move the cooked portions toward the  center. 5. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven an additional 6 minutes or  until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean Cool before serving.  Variation: Heat 3 or 4 custard cups of filling, uncovered, 3 to 4 minutes  or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
