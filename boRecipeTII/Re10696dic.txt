Quick Spaghetti Sauce
Water, Brown Sugar, Minced Onion Flakes, Garlic salt, Tomato Sauce; 3 cn, Marjoram, Mushrooms;Sliced,Drained,1cn, Ground Beef; Lean, Oregano, Basil, 
Saute the ground beef in a skillet and drain off the excess fat.  Add the  remaining ingredients and heat to boiling.  Simmer for 10 minutes,  stirring occasionally.  Serve over hot cooked spaghetti or other pasta.    "More Recipes from the Backs of Boxes, Bottles, Cans & Jars", by Ceil Dyer.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sauces1.zip
