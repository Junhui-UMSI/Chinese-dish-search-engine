Quick Cranberry Salad
Miniature marshmallows, Pecans, (8 1/2 oz) crushed, Sugar, Whipping cream, Whole; fresh or frozen, 
Put cranberries in food processor and chop coarsely(frozen berries chop  best). Add sugar and chill for at least 2 hours. Then add marshmallows,  pineapple, and nuts. Whip cream and fold into cranberry mixture. (can use  whipped topping like Cool Whip). Spoon into bowl. Cover and refrigerate or  freeze. Serves 6-8    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 349 by "Lloyd W. Schempp"  <schempp@sisna.com> on Dec 8, 1997
