Banana Cream Pie
SHORTENING; 3LB, BANANAS FRESH, DSRT PWD VANILLA, Salt, WATER; COLD, WATER; COLD, All Purpose Flour, MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, 
PAN:  9-INCH PIE PAN  :    1.  SEE RECIPE NOS. IG001 AND I00100.    2.  COMBINE MILK AND WATER IN MIXER BOWL.    3.  ADD DESSERT POWDER TO MILK AND WATER.  USING WHIP, BLEND AT LOW SPEED  15 SECONDS OR UNTIL WELL BLENDED.  SCRAPE DOWN SIDES OF BOWL; WHIP AT  MEDIUM SPEED 2 MINUTES.    4.  SLICE BANANAS.  TO PREVENT DISCOLORATION, SLICE BANANAS JUST BEFORE  ADD TO FILLING.  POUR ABOUT 3 1/2 CUPS FILLING INTO EACH BAKED PIE SHELL.    5.  REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.    6.  CUT 8 WEDGES PER PIE.
