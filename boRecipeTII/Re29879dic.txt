Tequila Sunrise (Tequila Madrugada)
Orange juice, Ice cubes, Tequila, Grenadine, 
Fill a highball glass with ice. Add tequila and fill with orange juice;  stir. Slowly pour in tequila and let it settle. Before serving, stir very  gently once, to create the "sunset" effect.    Recipe By     : Joe Robertson    From:                                 Date:
