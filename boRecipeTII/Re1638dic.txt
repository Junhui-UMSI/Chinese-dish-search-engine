Caramelized Onions
Onions, olive oil, Butter, Cider vinegar, Brown sugar, 
Slice onions in thin rings, melt butter into oil and swirl together. Add  onions and cook over low heat until browned and translucent (about 15  minutes). Add vinegar and brown sugar and stir until well-mixed. Boil the  mixture on medium-high heat and then slow simmer, uncovered and stirring  occasionally, for about 10 minutes, or until the liquid has evaporated and  the onion has a deep color.
