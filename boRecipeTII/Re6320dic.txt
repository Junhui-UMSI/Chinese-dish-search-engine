Planet Hollywoods Chicken Crunch
Honey, Onion powder, milk, Mayonnaise, Oil; for frying, Salt, MUSTARD SAUCE, Cream-style horseradish, White pepper, Egg, Yellow mustard, Dijon mustard, Boneless skinless chicken, Corn flake crumbs, Garlic powder, 
Heat the frying oil to 375F degrees. Smash the Capn Crunch cereal into  crumbs with a food processor and combine with the corn flake crumbs, onion,  powder, garlic powder, salt and white pepper. In a separate bowl, combine  the egg and milk. Dip each piece of chicken into the milk mixture, then in  the cereal mixture. Fry the chicken for 4-6 minutes until dark golden  brown. Drain on paper towels.    For the sauce: Combine all ingredients and refrigerate.    MasterCook formatted for your cooking pleasure by Pamela Creeden.
