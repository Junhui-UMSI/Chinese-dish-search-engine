Anne-Maries Blueberry Glace Pie
Sugar, Blueberries, Garnish, Lemon juice, Whipped cream -- for, 9 inch baked pie shell, Butter, Cornstarch, Cinnamon, Water, Salt, 
Simmer 1 cup less-than-perfect berries with water for 4 minutes. Add mixed  cornstarch and sugar, other ingredients except whole berries,  pie shell  and  whipped cream. Cook until thick and clear. Mix gently  with whole berries  and  pour into baked 9" pie shell. Chill and top with whipped cream.
