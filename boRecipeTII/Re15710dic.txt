A Very Special Brown Betty
Tart apples, Honey, Cinnamon, Raisins, Wheat germ, Honey, Apple juice, All-purpose flour, Brown sugar, sunflowr seeds, TOPPING, Whole wheat flour, Butter or Margarine, Quick-cooking rolled oats, 
From: amberlyn@comtch.iea.com (Amberlyn)    Date: 19 Oct 1994 06:09:30 -0400    (clipped from a magzine several years ago) Combine apples with juice,  raisins, 1/2 cup honey, brown sugar, flour and cinnamon in a large bowl.  Turn into an 11 by 7 1/2 by 1 1/2 -inch baking pan. Combine topping  ingredients and mix well; spread the topping over the apple mixture. Bake  in a 350 degree oven for 45 to 50 minutes. Makes 8-10 servings.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /DESSERTS    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
