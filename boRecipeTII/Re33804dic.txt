Sweet and Sour Mustard Sauce #1
Dry mustard, Cream, Flour, Egg yolks, Sugar, Vinegar, 
Combine the sugar, flour and dry mustard in the top of a double boiler over  ~NOT IN- boiling water. Mix the cream and the egg yolks. Gradually add to  the mustard mixture. Cook until thickened.
