Radicchio and Greens with Hot Dressing
Butterhead lettuce, Arugula, Sized head radicchio, olive oil, Balsamic vinegar, Shallot; minced, 
In a salad bowl, tear lettuce and arugula into bite sized pieces. Tear  radicchio into slightly larger pieces. In a saucepan, heat oil and vinegar  and shallot. Pour hot dressing over greens and toss.    Recipe developed by Ellen Ogden.  In "The Cooks Garden" catalog,  Spring/Summer 1994, Vol. XI, No. 1. Pg. 12. Typed for you by Cathy Harned.
