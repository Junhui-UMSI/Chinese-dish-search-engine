Pheasant in Spiced Sour Cream
Water, Worcestershire sauce f/taste, Pheasant, cut up, Carton sour cream, Dash rosemary, Flour, Salt and pepper, Few drops Tabasco sauce, Dash sweet basil, Bay Leaves, Butter, 
Dust pieces of pheasant with flour and brown in butter. Mix sour cream and  water.  Add remaining ingredients to sour cream mixture. Pour sour cream  mixture in a covered roaster and add pheasant.  Steam for several hours at  325F until tender.
