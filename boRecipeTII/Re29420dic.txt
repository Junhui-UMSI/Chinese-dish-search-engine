Baked Ziti with Three Cheeses
Black pepper, Chopped parsley, Ziti, Parmesan cheese, Mozzarella cheese --, Clove garlic -- minced, Ricotta cheese, Ground nutmeg, Egg -- lightly beaten, Shredded, 
Cook pasta following package directions; drain. Preheat oven to 350F.  Grease 2-quart casserole.  In lg bowl, blend ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella,  egg, parsley, garlic, pepper & nutmeg; stir in ziti. Spoon into prepared  casserole & sprinkle with remaining mozzarella & Parmesan. Bake until top  is golden, about 30 min. Makes 8 servings. (The way my family eats it does  not make 8 servings!!)
