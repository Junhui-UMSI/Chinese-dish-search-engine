All-Time Favorite Barbecue Sauce
Condensed Tomato Soup, Brown sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic powder, Vegetable oil, Vinegar, Louisiana-style hot sauce, 
In small bowl, combine soup, vinegar, oil, brown sugar, Worchestershire  sauce, garlic powder and hot sauce; set aside. Use sauce to baste ribs,  chicken, hamburgers or steak during broiling or grilling. Makes about 1 1/3  cups sauce.
