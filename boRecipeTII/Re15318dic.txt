White Bean Relish
olive oil, Garlic; minced, Cooked white navy beans, Essence, Shallots; minced, Andouille sausage; diced, Salt and pepper, Green Onions, Celery, Italian plum tomatoes;, 
In a saute pan, heat the olive oil.  When the pan is smoking hot, render  the sausage for 1 minute.  Add the onions, celery, red peppers, shallots  and garlic for 1 minute. Season with Essence. Stir in the beans and  tomatoes.  Saute 1 minute and season with salt and pepper. Remove from the  heat.  Spoon the sauce onto the platter. Mound the relish in the cneter of  the sauce. Place prawns around the relish. Granish with fried spinach,  cheese and peppers.
