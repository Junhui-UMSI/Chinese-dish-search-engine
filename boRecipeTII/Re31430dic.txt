Barbecue French Bread Pizza
Olive oil or oil, Barbecue sauce, 16-oz loaf French bread, Thinly sliced cooked pork;, Garlic, Onion; thinly sliced,, Shredded Muenster cheese;, 
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Cut bread in half lenthwise. Place, cut side up,  on ungreased cookie sheet. In small bowl, combine oil and garlic. Brush on  cut surface of bread halves.     In medium saucepan, combine pork, onion and barbecue sauce. Cook 5-8  minutes or until mixture is hot and onion is tender, stirring frequently.  Spoon pork mixture evenly onto each bread half; top with cheese.     Bake at 375 for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted.
