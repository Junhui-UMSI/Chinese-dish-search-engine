Pumpkin, Orange and Raisin Muffins
Ginger and nutmeg, Raisins, Baking Powder, Egg, All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Each salt, cinnamon,, Pumpkin puree, Orange juice, Vegetable oil, Coarsely grated, Packed brown, Sugar, Orange rind, 
In large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda,  salt, cinnamon, ginger and nutmeg; stir in raisins. In separate bowl, beat  egg; blend in pumpkin, oil, orange rind and juice. Pour all at once over  dry ingredients; stir just enough to blend. Spoon into large greased or  paper lined muffin cups, filling to tops. Bake in 375 degree F (190 degrees  C) oven for about 25 minutes or until golden and tops are firm to the  touch. Let stand for 5 minutes before removing from pans to cool on racks.    Makes 12 muffins.    From: Canadian Living Magazine    September 1992
