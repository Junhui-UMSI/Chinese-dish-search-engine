Grilled Flatbread with Ricotta,tomatoes,roasted Garlic
flat bread, ricotta, Plum tomatoes, Basil chiffonade, parmesan, olive oil, garlic, 
Place oil and garlic in a blender and blend until smooth. Strain. Set  aside. Flatten dough, brush liberally with olive oil and through on the  grill. Grill on one side until golden brown. Turn over and spread with  ricotta, tomatoes and basil. Remove and top with shaved parmesan and  drizzle with garlic oil.
