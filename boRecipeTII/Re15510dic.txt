5-Bean Salad
EDEN Extra Virgin Olive Oil, chopped fine, drained, EDEN Brown Rice Vinegar, EDEN BarleY Malt, cut in lengthwise strips, and sliced, cut in half and sliced, drained, Can EDEN Black Kidney Beans, Oregano, thyme, basil, mint, drained, Bunch fresh parsley, Green onions, OPTIONAL, Can EDEN Navy Beans, EDEN Shoyu, Can EDEN Garbanzo Beans, Stalks of celery; sliced, Red radishes, sliced diagonally, Cucumber; peeled, Can EDEN Pinto Beans, drained, Green pepper; diced, 
Prepare a marinade of vinegar, olive oil, barley malt, and shoyu by  pouring ingredients into ajar and shaking vigorously or mixing in a  blender. Mix thoroughly with vegetables, beans and herbs. Marinate in  refrigerator for 4 hours or longer. desired and serve chilled.
