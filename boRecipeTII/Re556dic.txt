Friendship Bread
Amish Batter, Eggs, Flour, Sugar, Cinnamon, Salt, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Vanilla, Fresh or canned fruit,, Vegetable oil, 
Mix all ingredients together. Pour in 2 greased loaf pans. Bake at 350  degrees for 50-60 minutes.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Aug  24, 1998
