Sweet Kugel (Low Fat)
Cooked egg noodles; (about, 1% low fat cottage cheese, Salt, Sugar, 8 ounce cartons egg, Cinnamon, ; coarsely chopped, Vegetable cooking spray, Raisins, Reduced- calorie stick, Ounce can unsweetened, Coarsely crushed cornflakes, Low fat sour cream, 
OVEN: 350 Coat a 13 x 9-inch baking dish with vegetable cooking spray.    Combine first 9 ingredients in a large bowl; stir well.    Add noodles, toss gently to coat.    Sprinkle cornflakes over noodle mixture; cover and bake for 30 minutes.  Uncover and bake an additional 10 minutes.
