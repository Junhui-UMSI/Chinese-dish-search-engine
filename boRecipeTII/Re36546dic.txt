Strawberries Yogurt San Remo
Plain yogurt, GARNISH, Brandy, Sliced Almonds, Chocolate shavings/sprinkles, YOGURT TOPPING, Sugar, Fresh Strawberries   *, Egg yolks, Sugar, Vanilla, Nutmeg, 
* hulled, cleaned, and halved     Combine strawberries and sugar. Cover; let stand 15 minutes.   Prepare topping. Beat together yolks, 1/3 cup sugar, vanilla, nutmeg, and  brandy until smooth. Fold in yopurt.   Spoon strawberries into 4 sherbert dishes. Cover with topping; garnish  with almonds and chocolate. Yield 4 servings.
