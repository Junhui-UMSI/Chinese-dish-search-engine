Tuna Steaks with Cracked Peppercorns (Wash)
Butter, pink salt, olive oil, Fresh Lemon Juice, Lemon zest, Salt, Tuna steaks, black pepper, 
***Note: You may use halibut in place of the tuna.    In a small bowl put butter, lemon juice, zest and salt. Whisk and put  aside.    Pour olive oil on 1plate. Put pepper on another plate. Dip tuna in oil,  then press peppercorns on both sides. Sprinkle a little salt on each steak.  Cook on hot grill or in a hot pan on the stove with a little added olive  oil.    Put the lemon butter over top of each steak. Serve.    Source: Barbara Williams, Coasting & Cooking Bk #4 -- Washington & Oregon,  1997.
