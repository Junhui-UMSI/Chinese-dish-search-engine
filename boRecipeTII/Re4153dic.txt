Peppy Cocktail Nuts
Salt, Tabasco sauce, Salad seasoning, Butter or margarine, Garlic salt, Pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Walnut or pecan halves, Jim Vorheis, 
In a large pan melt butter and add remaining ingredients except nuts. Stir  until well blended.  Add nuts and toss to coat. Cook covered over low heat  for 20 minutes. Stir occasionally. Drain on brown paper. Store in an  air-tight container.    Colorado Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
