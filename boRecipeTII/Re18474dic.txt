Vieiras a la Gallega - Scallops in Mustard-Ol
Dijon-style mustard, Seasoned fresh bread crumbs, Garlic, Green olives stuffed,, Sea scallops, Whipping cream, Unsalted butter, olive oil, Small capers, drained, 
Cooks notes: Green olives stuffed with anchovies are available at stores  that specialize in imported foods, Cost Plus.    Preliminaries: Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Procedure: Heat olive oil in  medium skillet. Add garlic and scallops and cook on medium heat for 5  minutes. Add chopped olives (and anchovies if using them separately),  capers, mustard, cream and butter. Cook over medium heat for 8 to10 minutes  or until reduced by half, stirring occasionally. Spoon scallops into  scallop shells or onto small ovenproof dish. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and  bake in a preheated 350-degree oven until top is nicely browned, about 2 to  3 minutes, watching carefully to prevent burning.
