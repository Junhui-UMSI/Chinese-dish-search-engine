Gefulte Fish Dip
Lemon juice, White pepper, Cream cheese, Salt, White Horseradish, Gefulte Fish, 
Drain fish and mash with fork. Put in remaining ingredients and stir. Can  put into a mold by first lining the mold with plastic wrap. Refrigerate.  Unmold. Serve with crackers or matzo.    Recipe by: Debbie Dalin    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Lenore Gould <lenoreg@wwnet.com> on Oct 08,  1998,
