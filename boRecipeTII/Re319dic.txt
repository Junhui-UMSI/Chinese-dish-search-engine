Lefse
Butter, Salt to taste, Potatoes; fresh, milk, Flour, 
Run potatoes through food grinder. Heat butter and milk, pour over  potatoes. Cool mixture enough to handle, add enough flour to make soft  dough. Roll out a small amount at a time into very thin circles. Heat a  heavy skillet very hot. Do not grease. Fry circles on each side. Serve hot  with butter or jelly.
