Strawberry Stuffed French Toast
Cream cheese, Vanilla, BATTER, Strawberry jam, French bread, Pecans, milk, Beat above together, Eggs, 
from Blue Mountain Mist; Sevierville, Tennessee    Mix cream cheese, jam and nuts. Cut Italian or French bread into diagonal  pieces about 1 inch thick.    Cut pocket in each slice. Stuff with mixture. Dip into batter, coating both  sides. With skillet onmedium heat, brown both sides in margarine.
