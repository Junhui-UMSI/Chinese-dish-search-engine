Apricot-Almond Bread
Honey or real maple syrup, Dried apricots, Grated fresh orange rind, Water, Salt, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Butter, Chopped almonds, White flour, Vanilla, Beaten egg, Whole wheat flour, 
Cook apricots in water for 10 minutes (covered, low heat). Cool completely  and add butter, syrup and salt. Beat in egg and vanilla. Sift together dry  ingredients. Fold everything together and spread into buttered loaf pan.  Bake 35-40 minutes at 375 degrees. Remove bread from pan 10 minutes after  it comes out of the oven.
