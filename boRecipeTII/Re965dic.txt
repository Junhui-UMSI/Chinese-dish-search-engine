Indoor Smores
Chocolate chips, mini marshmallows, Butter, Light corn syrup, Vanilla, Golden Grahams, 
Melt 5 cups marshmallows (reserve 1 c. from bag), chocolate chips, butter  and corn syrup in 3 qt. saucepan over low heat, stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat; stir in vanilla. Grease a 13"x9"x2" pan.    Pour cereal into large mixing bowl. Pour marshmallow mixture over cereal;  stir until evenly coated. Stir in remaining marshmallows.    Press mixture into prepared pan with buttered back of spoon. Cool until as  firm as youd like. Cut into bars. Store covered loosely at room  temperature.
