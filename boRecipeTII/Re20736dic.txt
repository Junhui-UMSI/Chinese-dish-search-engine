Apple Date Torte
Baking Powder, Whipped cream; whipped, Butter, Nuts, Flour, Egg, Vanilla, Apples; unpared/diced, Dates; cut up, Sugar, 
Combine all ingredients except whipped cream in order given. Stir but do  not beat. Pour into greased 8" square pan. BAke at 400 F. for 40 minutes.  Serve with whipped cream, topping or ice cream.
