Vietnamese Egg Rolls
Ground pork, Garlic, Whole carrot; shredded, Whole eggs; beaten, Egg roll skins; large size, Whole green onions; diced, Soy sauce, Sugar, Bean sprouts; broken up, salt and pepper, 
Place l tablespoon in each wrapper. Fry in medium-hot oil about 20 minutes.    Recipe by: Barbara Hong    Posted to FOODWINE Digest  by Chris or Dick Marksberry  <chrism1@MAIL.PHOENIX.NET> on Sep 4, 1997
