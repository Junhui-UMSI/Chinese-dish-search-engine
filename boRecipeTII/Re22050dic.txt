Turkey Divan 2
Margarine, melted, Dry bread crumbs, Thick slices cooked turkey, (10 oz) condensed cream of, (10-oz) of frozen broccoli, Mayonnaise, Lemon juice, 
Heat oven to 350F. Grease 12x8-inch baking dish (2 qt). Cook broccoli as  directed on package until almost done (crisp-tender); drain. Arrange  broccoli in prepared baking dish. Lay turkey slices on top.    In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, lemon juice and soup; pour over  turkey. Combine bread crumbs and margarine; sprinkle on top. Bake uncovered  at 350F for 25 to 35 minutes or until heated through.
