Sweet Potato Cheesecake Bars
Pecans, milk, nuts, Sugar, All-purpose flour, Cream cheese, Butter, Pumpkin pie spice, sweet potatoes, Sugar, Egg, Graham cracker crumbs, Sour cream, 
Contributed by: ThePoet102    Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Stir together crumbs, flour, 1/2  cup nuts, and 1/4 cup sugar. Add butter mix well. Pat into ungreased  9x9x2-inch baking pan. Bake for 12 minutes. In small bowl combine sweet  potatoes, cream cheese, 1/2 cup sugar, and spice, mix well; add egg; beat  until combined. Stir in sour cream and milk just until blended. Pour over  baked layer, sprinkle with 2 Tablespoon nuts. Bake for 20 minutes or until  knife inserted off-center comes out clean. Cool and cut into squares. Makes       36    bars.
