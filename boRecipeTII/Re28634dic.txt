Texas Cheese Wafers
Flour, Cheddar cheese, Cayenne pepper, Salt, rice cereal, Butter, 
Mix butter, flour, cheese and seasonings and stir  lightly. By teaspoons, roll into balls and put on a  cookie sheet. Dip fork tines into flour and and press  into each wafer in a crisscross pattern. Bake 15  minutes or until lightly browned. Store in airtight  containers till ready to serve!
