Poppy Seed Almond Bread
Vanilla, Orange juice, Salt, Poppy seeds, BREAD, Baking Powder, 7 tbsp. sugar, Flour, Butter, Vegetable oil, Almond extract, Sugar, Almond extract, Vanilla, GLAZE, Eggs, milk, 
Mix dry ingredients in small bowl. Set aside. Mix other ingredients in  large bowl. Add flour mixture to this. Bake in 4 small loaf pans or 2 large  pans at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes (or more). Heat and brush glaze over  bread while warm.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #187 by Julie Bertholf  <jewel1@ix.netcom.com> on Jul 19, 1997
