Toffee Crunch Brownies
English toffee bits, Reynolds Wrap Everyday Heavy, Fudge brownie mix; (1 lb., Crispy rice cereal, 
Preheat oven to 350F degrees. Line a 13 X 9 X 2" baking pan with foil;  spray with nonstick cooking spray and set aside. Follow package directions  to prepare brownie mix. Spread batter evenly in foil-lined pan. Sprinkle  cereal evenly over batter; press lightly. Sprinkle toffee bits evenly over  cereal. Bake until brownies are set, 30-35 minutes. Cool in pan on wire  rack. Lift foil to remove brownies from pan to cutting board. Fold foil  back to cut brownies.
