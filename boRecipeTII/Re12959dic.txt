Zesty Herb Seasoning
Salt + potassium chloride *, Paprika, Grated peel of lemon, Ground mace, Dried rosemary leaves, Dried basil leaves,, Ground cloves, Ground allspice, Dried thyme leaves, crushed, Ground cinnamon, Ground nutmeg, Pepper, 
NOTE: * salt and potassium chloride mixture listed above is a product with  half the sodium of table salt.       Combine all ingredients. Refridgerate in covered container. To use,  sprinkle as desired over meat, poultry, or fish before broiling or baking.  Makes about 1/2 cup.    NOTE: About 47 mg sodium per teaspoon. About 5 calories per teaspoon.
