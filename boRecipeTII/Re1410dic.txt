Emerils Fried Plantains
plantains, Oil for frying, Salt, Black Pepper, 
Preheat the fryer. Peel the plantains and slice thinly on the bias. Fry in  batches until golden brown, about 2 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove  from the oil and drain on paper towels. Season with salt and pepper.    From: Emeril Live on Food TV Entered by: Roy Olsen  http://www.indy.net/~roy/    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Aug 12, 98
