Apple Pie Dublin Style
Honey, Lemon Rind, Butter, Cornstarch, Salt, Sugar, Apples, 9 inch pie shell, (1 pound 4 oz. each) sliced, 
Combine undrained apples and all except butter.  Mix lightly.  Spoon  ingredients into pastry shell.  Dot with butter.  Bake at 425 for minutes.  Serve warm or cold. This is an American version of "Apple Nianih Chinn Oir"  ~ Irish for "Noomh of the Golden Tresses", a beautiful maiden in Irish  mythology   who took her man to the land of eternal youth.
