Wartime Cake
Raisins, Shortening, Salt, Water, Flour, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Ground cloves, Dark brown sugar, + 2 tsp, Nutmeg, 
In saucepan, mix sugar, 1 1/4 cups water, shortening, raisins, nutmeg,  cinnamon & cloves. Boil 3 min.; cool.  Dissolve salt & soda in 2 tsp water.  Add to mixture. Blend in flour mixed with baking powder. Pour into a  greased & floured 8" square pan. Bake 325 for 50 min. Delicious uniced or  top with a white frosting. This is eggless, milkless & butterless & was  perfect for if you didnt have a lot of points left.
