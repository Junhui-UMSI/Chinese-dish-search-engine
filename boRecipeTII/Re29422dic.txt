Baked Zucchini Boats
olive oil, Egg, Onion; yellow, chopped, Ground beef; lean, Spaghetti sauce;canned *, Clove garlic; chopped, Dill; fresh, chopped, Zucchini, Bread crumbs, Parsley; fresh, chopped, Parmesan cheese, Rice;long grain cooked, 
Split zucchini lengthwise, scoop out seeds. Saute ground beef, onion,  garlic, dill, and parsley in olive oil. Remove from heat. Add cooked rice,  beaten egg, 1/4 c bread crumbs and mix well. Fill zucchini with mixture and  place in a baking dish. Cover with spaghetti sauce, sprinkle cheese and  remaining bread crumbs on top. Cover and bake at 350 for approximately 45  minutes, until zucchini is tender but firm.
