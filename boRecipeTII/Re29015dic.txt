Baked Pinapple
Flour, Bread; cubed, Sugar, (16-oz) crushed pineapple;, Eggs, Butter, 
Mix pineapple, eggs, sugar and flour together and pour into a 1 quart  casserole. Melt butter and stir in bread cubes gently to coat. Put buttered  cubes on top of casserole and bake, uncovered, at 350? for 30 minutes.  Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
