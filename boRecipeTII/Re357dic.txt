Lemon Butter
Butter, Lemon juice, Lemon Rind; Grated, Salt, 
1. In a small bowl blend all ingredients. 2. Shape into square and chill.  3. When ready to serve, cut into slices.    NOTES : variations: Lemon Parsley butter: Add 1/4 cup chopped parsley to  lemon butter. Lemon Caper Butter: Stir 2 tablespoons chopped drained capers  into the lemon butter.
