Apple Sausage Cheddar Quiche
French bread, Cheddar cheese, Link  sausages, Swiss cheese, Granny Smith apple, Eggs, milk, 
Tear French bread into 1-inch pieces. Cover the bottom of an ungreased  quiche dish with bread pieces. Add the sliced sausage and apple. In a  medium bowl, whisk the eggs and milk together and pour over the bread  mixture. Bake at 350? F for 40 minutes. Cool 10 minutes before slicing.  Makes 6 servings.
