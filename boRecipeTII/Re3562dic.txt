Pecan Souffl Tassies
Sugar, milk, Pecans, Flour, Margarine; melted, Pecan halves; for garnish, Eggs, Vanilla, 
Place sugar, eggs, milk, margarine, flour, vanilla, and 1 cup chopped  pecans in a blender. If blender will not contain the entire mixture,  reserve some of the milk to add later. Pour mixture into greased miniature  muffin pans, about half full. Place a pecan half on top of each pie. Bake  in a preheated 300 oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until a knife in the center  comes out clean. SOURCE: Great Desserts of the South (pg. 75)  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "M.
