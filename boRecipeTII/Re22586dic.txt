Apricot Bars #2
Brown sugar, Quick-cooking oats, Apricot spreadable fruit;, Nisquick original baking mix, Stick margarine or butter;, 
HEAT oven to 400 degrees. Grease square pan, 9x9x2 inches.  Mix baking  mix, oats and brown sugar. Cut in margarine with fork until mixture is  crumbly.     PRESS half of the crumbly mixture in pan. Spread fruit on top to within  1/4 inch of edges. Top with remaining crumbly mixture; press gently into  fruit.     BAKE 25 to 30 minutes or until light brown; cool. Cut into bars. 2 dozen  bars.
