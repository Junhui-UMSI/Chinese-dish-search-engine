Vidalia Onion and Carrot Salad
Carrots; grated, Black pepper; to taste, finely chopped, Fresh Lemon Juice, dressing:;, olive oil, Cumin seed, Wine vinegar, Fresh mint, Vidalia onions, 
Recipe by: Jo Anne Merrill Preparation Time: 0:10 From:  kmeade@ids2.idsonline.com (The Meades)    Date: Fri, 24 May 1996 12:13:11 -0400    * Use Vidalia onions or other sweet onions such as Walla Walla, Maui, Imper    1. Grate carrots coarsely; place in serving bowl along with chopped onions.    2. In a small bowl combine olive oil, lemon juice, wine vinegar and black  pepper to taste. Mix with wire whisk until blended. Add chopped fresh mint  and mix well.    3. Pour dressing over carrots and onions and toss well.    Posted to Master Cook Recipes List, Digest #96
