Halibut with Summer Vegetable Sauce
olive oil, red onion, Plum tomatoes, Zucchini, Fresh corn kernels, basil, Halibut fillet, Fresh lime juice, 
Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add onion; saute 5  minutes. Add tomatoes; saute 3 minutes. Stir in zucchini, corn, 3  tablespoons basil and parsley. Cover and simmer until zucchini is  crisp-tender, about 3 minutes.    Add halibut fillets to sauce in skillet. Sprinkle fish and sauce generously  with salt and pepper. Cover, simmer until fish is opaque in center, about 5  minutes. Remove from heat. Drizzle lime juice over.    Using spatula, transfer fish to plates. Spoon sauce over. Sprinkle with  remaining 2 tablespoons basil and serve.    Recipe by: Bon Appetit Magazine August 1998 p. 115    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Linda Shapiro <lss@coconet.com> on Aug 19,  1998,
