Apple Crisp
Salt, Sliced tart apples, (5 to 6 apples), All-purpose flour, Cinnamon, C&H Golden Brown Sugar, Nutmeg, Butter or margarine, 
Mix sugar, flour, spices, and salt.  Cut in butter until mixture is  crumbly.  Spread apples in bottom of buttered 8- or 9-inch square baking  pan.  Sprinkle crumbly mixture over fruit; pat lightly with fingers.  Bake  uncovered at 350 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes, or until apples are tender.  Serve warm with cream or ice cream.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
