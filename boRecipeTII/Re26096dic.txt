Tofu Sesame Sauce
Granulated sugar, Silken-style tofu, Cider vinegar, Toasted sesame tahini, Low-sodium soy sauce, Honey, 
Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor, along with 1  tablespoon of water. Puree to a smooth consistency. Serve immediately  or keep in the refrigerator for several days. Spoon about 1  tablespoon over each side-dish vegetable serving.    Good over steamed broccoli, cooked bulgur wheat.    Makes 16 (1 cup) servings. Per serving: 11 calories, 1 gram fat.
