Thai Chicken Hamburger (Light)
Egg whites, Grainy mustard, Chili paste, Lean ground chicken, Salt, garlic, black pepper, Quick-cooking oats, Cilantro, Fresh ginger root, Lime, green onions, 
1. Combine the ground chicken, green onions, cilantro, ginger, garlic,  mustard, chili paste, egg whites, oats, salt and pepper. Form into 6  burgers.    2. In a large nonstick skillet, cook 3 burgers at a time for about 4 to 5  minutes per side on medium heat. Check to make sure burgers are cooked  through. Hold in a warm oven while cooking the remaining burgers.    3. Serve with a lime wedge.    >From "The Olives Table" by Todd English.
