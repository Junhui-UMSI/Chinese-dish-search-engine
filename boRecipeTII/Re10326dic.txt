Quick Brown Rice Fritters
Baking Powder, Flour, Nutmeg, Cooked lundberg short grain, Salt, Sugar, Vanilla, Eggs, 
Combine rice, eggs, vanilla, nutmeg and mix well. Sift dry ingredients  together and stir into rice mixture. Drop by spoonfuls into hot deep fat  (360) and fry until brown. Drain on absorbent paper, sprinkle with powdered  sugar and serve hot. Makes 18 to 20 fritters.
