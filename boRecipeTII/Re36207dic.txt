Beef Stroganoff
Salt, Beef tenderloin, Dijon mustard, Butter, Sour cream, Beef stock, Pepper, Onion, Flour, 
SPREAD BEEF STRIPS on a baking sheet, sprinkle with salt and pepper, top  with onion slices and let stand at room temperature for 2 hours. Melt 2  tablespoons butter in a skillet and stir in flour. Mix in stock and heat,  stirring until thickened. Blend in mustard and remove from heat. In another  skillet, melt remaining butter, add beef and onion and saute until browned.  Add the beef (but not the onion) to the sauce and simmer 15 minutes. Remove  from heat and stir in sour cream. Serve onions separately or reserve for  another use.
