Aunt Phyllis Good Sour Cream Coffee Cake
Sour cream, Eggs, Butter or margarine, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Nuts, Sugar, TOPPING, Flour, Brown sugar, 
Mix soda with cream. Add rest of ingredients except topping. Put batter in  2 bread pans. Mix topping ingredients and sprinkle on top.    Bake a 350F for 1/2 hour.    Recipe by: Aunt Phyllis    Posted to recipelu-digest by Nesb2 <Nesb2@aol.com> on Mar 2, 1998
