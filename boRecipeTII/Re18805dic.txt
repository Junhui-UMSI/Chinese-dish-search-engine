Anchovy Zuppetta, Rowboat Style
Jalapenos, seeded and,, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Dry white wine, Red onion, finely chopped, Scallions, thinly sliced, Sliced almonds, Anchovies or sardines,, Basil leaves, chiffonade, Basic tomato sauce, Mint leaves, chiffonade, 
In a 12 to 14-inch saute pan, heat oil until smoking. Add jalapenos,  almonds and onions and cook until softened and lightly browned, about 2 to  3 minutes. Add white wine and tomato sauce and bring to a boil. Add  anchovies and return to boil. Lower heat to simmer and cook through, about  8 to 10 minutes. Pour into warm bowl, sprinkle with scallions, basil and  mint and serve with grilled bread drizzled with good oil.
