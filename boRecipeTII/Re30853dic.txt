Tangy Glazed Meatloaf
Catsup, Bread crumbs, Salt, Poultry seasoning, Turkey, ground, lean, milk, Catsup, Sugar, brown, Onion, GLAZE-----, Mustard, dry, Pepper, Nutmeg, 
Combine breadcrumbs and milk in a bowl.  Add turkey, egg, onion, seasonings  and catsup.  Mix well and pat into a small meatloaf pan. Bake at 350  degrees for 40 minutes.  Combine glaze ingredients and spoon over meatloaf.  Bake 20 additional minutes.
