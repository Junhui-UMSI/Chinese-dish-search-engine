Strata
Bisquick baking mix, Eggs, butter, Small curd cottage cheese, milk, Monterey jack cheese,, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Combine all ingredients; mix well. Pour into greased  9x13-inch pan. Bake at 350F 35-40 minutes. Cut into squares to serve.    NOTES : Can be made ahead.  Combine all ingredients except Bisquick. Cover  and refrigerate.  Mix in Bisquick just before baking.
