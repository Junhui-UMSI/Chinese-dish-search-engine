Tomato Garnish (Mf)
olive oil, Sherry wine vinegar, Snipped chives, Capers; drained, Vine ripened tomatoes, Salt, 
Peel, seed and dice the tomatoes; season them to taste with capers and  vinegar and just a touch of oil; season with salt and pepper. Place a bed  of the diced tomatoes on each diners plate, leaving enough room in the  center for the shad. When the shad is out of the oven, center it in the  middle of the tomatoes and garnish with chives.
