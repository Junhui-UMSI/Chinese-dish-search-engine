Rajun Cajun Ribs
Pepper, Cayenne pepper, Pork spareribs, Ketchup, Garlic powder, RAJUN CAJUN SAUCE, Chili powder, 
Cut ribs into easy to handle portions.  Cook in  boiling water until tender, about 1 hour.  Drain,  Cool. Rajun Cajun Sauce:  Combine all 5 ingredients  together in small bowl. Brush over ribs.  Place ribs  on grill over medium-hot heat. Turn and baste often,  about 10 minutes until well glazed and hot. Serves 4.  More heat can be added to suit your taste.  Just add  more cayenne pepper.
