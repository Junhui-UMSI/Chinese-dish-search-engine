All Purpose Pickling Solution
Bay leaf, Washed Pickling Cucumbers, Salt Per Quart Of Water, Garlic, Bud Of Dill, Stalk Of Celery, Sliced, Carrot, Sliced, Spice Balls, Hot Chili Pepper, 
Combine all ingredients, except cucumbers, in a pan and bring to a boil.  Then prick the cucumbers with a fork and add to pickling solution. Put in  jars and seal. Let stand for 3 weeks before eating. Typed by Syd Bigger.
