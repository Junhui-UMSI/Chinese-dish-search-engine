Ratatouille Bread Abm
Yeast, BEST BREAD MACHINE CB EVER, Garlic, Bread flour, Bell peppers, WATER; WARM, Zucchini, Sugar, Salt, fresh basil, Summer squash, Plum tomatoes, olive oil, Wheat bran, 
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in order.  Set "baking control" at 10 oclock. Select "white bread" and push Start.    In hot & humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.    This bread makes a wonderful cold steak or grilled lamb sandwich.    Tested in DAK R2D2.
