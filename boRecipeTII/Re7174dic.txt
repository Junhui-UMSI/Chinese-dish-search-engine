Pork and Beans Casserole
Brown sugar, Ground turkey, Baked beans, Tomato sauce, Yellow mustard, Onion, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, 
Chop onion. Cook turkey until it changes color; breaking apart with a fork  (about 3 minutes). Add onion and heat thoroughly. Add remaining ingredients  and heat until mixture begins to simmer. Put in a 2-quart casserole; cover.  Bake in 350 degree F. oven 30 minutes. Serves 6. Freezes well.    Recipe by: Found on MSN Cooking Forum    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1066 by "M.
