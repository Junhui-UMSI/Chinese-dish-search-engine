Apple-Dapple Loaf
Baking soda, All purpose flour, Eggs, loreli aguda wskd49a, Baking Powder, Shortening, Nuts; chopped, Salt, Raw apples; grated, Sugar, 
Beat shortening, sugar and eggs together until light and fluffy. Sift dry  ingredients together; add alternately with grated apples. Add nuts. The  batter will be stiff. Turn into greased and floured loaf pan. Bake at 350~  for about 1 hour.
