Easy Tex-Mex Hash
Lean ground beef, Vegetable oil, Frozen potatoes with onions, Salsa, Chili powder, if desired, Shredded colby-monterey jack, 
1. In large nonstick skillet, brown ground beef. Remove ground beef from  skillet; cover to keep warm. Discard drippings.    2. In same skillet, heat oil over medium heat until hot. Add potatoes; cook  and stir 8 to 10 minutes or until browned.    3. Stir in salsa, chili powder and ground beef. Cook 5 to 8 minutes or  until thoroughly heated, stirring occasionally.    4. Sprinkle with cheese. Cover; cook 2 to 4 minutes or until cheese is  melted.    Nutrition Information Per Serving: 460 Calories, 28 g Fat, 720 mg Sodium.
