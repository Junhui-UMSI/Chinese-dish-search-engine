Barbeque Pork Roast
Ketchup, Beef roast, Dry mustard, Salt, Water, Sugar, Paprika, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, Cider vinegar, Onions, 
Mix all ingredients but meat in saucepan over medium heat. Simmer for 3-5  minutes. Pour over roast in roasting pan. Cook in 325~ oven for 3 hours.  Baste frequently. Shred roast after it has cooled. Serve with rolls or on  noodles. The roast will just fall apart - juicy and tender.
