Uncooked Chocolate Roll, Mrs. Lyons Recipe
Cherries, Semi-sweet chocolate, Walnuts, Colored marshmallows, 
Melt the chocolate and add the cherries & marshmallows. Mix well and put on  waxed paper and roll up like jelly roll. On another piece of waxed paper  put some cocoanut and roll the roll in it. Refrigerate and slice as needed.
