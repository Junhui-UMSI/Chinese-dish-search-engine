Winter Vegetable Soup
Green cabbage-shredded, Thyme, Salt and pepper to taste, Onions-chopped, OPTIONAL, Cooked pasta shells or, Chicken broth, Stalks celery-sliced, Carrots-diced, Turnip-peeled & diced, New potatoes-peeled & boiled, Vegetable oil, 
Acquired these from the latest issue of Modern Maturity, a publication of  the AARP.    Bring the broth to a boil. Meanwhile, saute the onions, carrots, celery and  turnip until they are lightly browned. Add them to the broth and simmer  uncovered for 20 minutes. Add the cabbage and thyme, and continue cooking  for another 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, add pasta or potatoes,  if desired and serve.
