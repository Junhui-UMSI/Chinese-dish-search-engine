Apple Tapioca
Peeled, sliced apples, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Boiling water, Salt, Instant tapioca, Lemon, 
In medium bowl, toss apples with brown sugar, cinnamon, salt and tapioca  until evenly coated.  Place apples in slow cooker. Pour lemon juice over  top.   Pour in boiling water.  Cook on high for 3-4 hours.
