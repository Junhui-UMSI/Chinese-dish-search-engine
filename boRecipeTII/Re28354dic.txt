Baked Hush Puppies
Salt, Egg white; beaten, Granulated sugar, Celery flakes, Skim milk, Yellow cornmeal, Unbleached all purpose flour, Vegetable oil, Baking Powder, Finely chopped scallion;, Cayenne pepper, Nonstick cooking spray, 
Heat oven to 425 degrees. Using nonstick cooking spray, coat miniature  muffin pan ( 1 3/4 inch cups). In medium sized bowl, combine cornmeal,  flour, baking powder, sugar, salt, celery flakes, and cayenne pepper; stir  until well blended.    In a small bowl, combine egg white, milk, and vegetable oil; stir well. Add  liquids to dry ingredients; stir just until blended. Stir in scallion.  Spoon about 1 tablespoon batter into each muffin cup. Bake until hush  puppies are golden brown, about 15 to 20 minutes. Remove immediately; serve  warm.
