5-Bean Soup
Water, EDEN Spaghetti Sauce, Bay leaf, EDEN Shoyu, Can EDEN Navy Beans, Garlic, Celery stalks; diced, EDEN Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Can EDEN Pinto Beans, Can EDEN Kidney Beans, Corn, fresh or frozen, Onion; diced, Barley, Can EDEN Black Beans, Carrots; diced, Can EDEN Aduki Beans, Dried Oregano, Dried basil, Potato; diced, 
Heat oil and saute garlic and onion until onion is translucent. Add  celery, carrots, potato, barley and water. Simmer 30 minutes. Add beans,  corn, spaghetti sauce, shoyu, and herbs. Simmer 5-10 minutes. Serve.  Great the next day!
