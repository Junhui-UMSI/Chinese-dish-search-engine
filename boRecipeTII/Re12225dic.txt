Zucchini Chocolate Cake
CREAM CHEESE FROSTING, Unsweetened Chocolate, Baking soda, Zucchini; Coarsely Grated, Vanilla extract, Utter, at room temp, Butter at room temperature, Eggs, Flour; Unbleached, Sifted, Salt, 
1. Melt chocolate and oil over very low heat. 2. Cream butter until light.  Add sugar, eggs, and vanilla; beat well. 3. Add melted chocolate to butter  mixture and mix well. 4. Sift dry ingredients together, then add them  alternately with the buttermilk to creamed mixture, beating well after each  addition. 5. Mix the zucchini and nuts into the batter. 6. Pour batter into  2 greased and floured 9" pans. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 40  minutes or until cake tests done.   Cool completely; frost with whipped cream or other desired frosting. CREAM  CHEESE FROSTING: Cream together the butter and cream cheese until light and  fluffy. Slowly add confectioners sugar until well blended. Use more sugar  if necessary, to get a spreading consistency.
