Bacon and Egg Bake
Cream of mushroom soup, Hard cooked eggs -- sliced, Bacon, Onions -- sliced, English muffins -- split, Salt and pepper, milk, Split and toasted, Cheddar cheese -- shredded, 
Heat oven to 350~.  Fry bacon until crisp; remove from skillet and drain,  reserve 2 Tb fat.  Saute onion in the reserved bacon fat. Stir in soup,  milk, eggs, cheese, and seasonings. Pour mixture into 9x13 inch baking  dish; top with bacon. Bake for 20 minutes. Serve over muffin halves.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
