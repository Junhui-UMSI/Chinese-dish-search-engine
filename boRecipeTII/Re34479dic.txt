Bean Salad #1
(16-oz) whole kernel corn, Ribs celery; chopped, (16-oz) French green beans, Sugar, (16-oz) small lima beans, (17-oz) English peas, Jar (2-oz) pimiento, Bell pepper; chopped, Vinegar, Salt, Onion, Vegetable oil, 
Drain beans and peas; mix with celery, onion and bell pepper. Heat sugar,  oil, salt and vinegar to boiling. Pour over salad and mix. Refrigerate.
