Tofu Crisps
Firm tofu (rinsed and, Cracked black pepper, Soy sauce, Nutritional yeast, Whole wheat flour, Apple cider vinegar, Each: basil, paprika, garlic, 
These seasoned tofu cubes are baked at a high temp. so they are crispy on  the outside and tender on the inside. Theyre great as croutons on salads,  or in pastas, stews and soups.    In a bowl combine vinegar and soy sauce. Add tofu cubes and stir to coat.  Marinate for at least 10 min. Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Place remaining  dry ingredients in a one gallon ziplock. Seal and shake. Drain tofu and put  cubes, in two batches, into ziplock. Seal and shake to coat. Place coated  cubes on a cookie sheet and bake 20 min or til browned.    Makes one pound crisps.
