Raspberry Fruit Fondue
Raspberries, Pkge cream cheese (8 ounces), Sugar, 
Fresh fruit for dipping, such as strawberries, melon balls, fresh pineapple  chunks, or peach sections.    1.  Gently wash the raspberries; drain. 2. In a covered blender container,  blend the berries and sugar. Strain through a medium sieve. 3. Beat the  cream cheese until smooth.  Beat in the raspberry pulp until well blended  and smooth.  Refrigerate. 4. Arrange the fruits around the bowl of dip.  Serve as an hor doeuvre or summer dessert.    [Raspberry Recipes from Larriland Farms, Lisbon, MD]
