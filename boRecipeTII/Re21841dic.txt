Apple-Pecan Pound Cake
Vanilla, Granny Smith apples; peel,, WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A, Sugar, Baking soda, Pecans; chop, Flaked coconut, Vegetable oil, Eggs, All-purpose flou8r, Salt, 
Mix sugar and oil, add eggs and beat well. Combine         flour, soda and  salt. Add flour mixture to beaten egg      mixture. Stir in vanilla,  apples, coconut, and pecans.     Mix well. Spoon butter into a greased tube  pan. Bake      at 325~ for 1 hour and 20 minutes. Allow cake to cool      to room  temperature before removing from pan. Source:      Louisiana  Conservationist.
