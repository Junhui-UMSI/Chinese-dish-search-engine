Quick Cheese and Bacon Muffins
Flour, Tasty grated cheese, Baking Powder, Sugar, milk, Butter, Crisp bacon pieces, Egg, 
In a large bowl place flour, baking powder, sugar & bacon. Beat egg  lightly, add butter & milk. Add to dry ingredients, add cheese & mix to  just combine. 3/4 fill greased muffin pans & bake 400F for 20-25 mins.  Makes 12.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
