Prune Bums
Dried prunes, Peanut butter, 
Place prunes in bowl. Cover with hot water and soak for 30 minutes. Slit  one side and lift out seed. Stuff each prune with peanut butter. Press to  close. Yield: 1 dozen.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
