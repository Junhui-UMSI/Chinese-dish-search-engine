Peanut Butter Rolls
Lukewarm water, Eggs, Peanut butter, compressed yeast, Salt, Nutmeg, flour, Milk, shortening, Sugar, 
Soften yeast in water.  Add milk, sugar, salt, shortening, eggs, nutmeg,  and peanut butter. Beat well. Add flour to make a dough barely stiff enough  to be kneaded. Knead until smooth on lightly floured board. Cover and let  rise until double in bulk; work down. Form into rolls. Place on well-oiled  baking sheet. Cover and let rise until treble in bulk. Bake in hot oven  (450? F) about 15 minutes. 16 rolls.
