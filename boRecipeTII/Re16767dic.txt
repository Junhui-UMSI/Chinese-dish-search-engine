Alices Pickles
To 1 1/2 c. white vinegar, To 1 1/2 c. sugar, Cucumbers -- slice, Green beans, yellow, Celery seeds, Fresh vegetables - onion --, Salt, 
Boil for 3 minutes 2 tablespoons celery seeds, 1 to 1 1/2 cups white  vinegar and 1 to 1 1/2 cups sugar and cool. Soak in 2 tablespoons salt for  1 hour cucumbers, then drain. Mix boiled mixture and all vegetables in  large jar and refrigerate.    Recipe By     :
