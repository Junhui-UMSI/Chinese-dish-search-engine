Vegetable-Couscous Salad
Boiling water, Couscous, uncooked, (15 Oz) Kidney Beans, rinsed, (8 Oz) Garbanzo Beans,, Green Onion, thinly sliced, Pepper, Pesto sauce, Lemon juice, Salt, Tomato, chopped and seeded, Broccoli Spears, cooked, 
Place couscous in medium bowl. Add boiling water and salt; stir well. Cover  and let stand 5-7 minutes or until water is absorbed. Stir in remaining  ingredients except broccoli. Serve over broccoli.  Posted by Michelle Dick, FATFREE listowner, while list was down.
