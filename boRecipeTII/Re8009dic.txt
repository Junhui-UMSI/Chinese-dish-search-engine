Potato and Bologna Scallop
Cheddar cheese soup or cream, Frozen peas, Mushroom or celery, Dried sage, Onion -- grated, Potatoes -- sliced, milk, Cheddar cheese -- grated, Of, Bologna -- cubed, 
Combine bologna and potato in shallow 6 cup casserole.  Cook frozen peas,  add soup, milk, onion and sage.  Bring to boiling, stir in cheese.  Pour  over bologona and potatoes.  Bake at 375 F for 30 minutes or until bubbly  hot.  4 servings.
