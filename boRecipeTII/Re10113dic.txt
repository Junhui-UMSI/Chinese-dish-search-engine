Queensland Cabbage
Vegetable oil, Onion, sliced, Mushrooms, sliced, Parsley, fresh chopped, Thyme, Tomato, chopped and seeded, Chicken broth, Salt & Pepper, to taste, Butter, Butter, Cabbage, shredded, 
Melt butter with oil in large pot.  Add cabbage, onion, broth and thyme.  Simmer, covered 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add mushrooms, tomatoes,  salt & pepper. Stir, cover, simmer until just tender (approx 10-15 min).  Add 1 Tbsp. butter and parsley.
