Texas Enchilada Gravy
Chili powder, Salt, Lard, Flour, Water, 
Melt lard in a skillet, stir in the flour and make a light roux. Add the  chili powder, water and salt; cook until thick.                                      -- The Only Texas Cookbook                                       Linda West Eckhardt Submitted By SAM  WARING <SAM.WARING@382-91-12.IMA.INFOMAIL.COM> On MON, 06 NOV 1995 105637  GMT
