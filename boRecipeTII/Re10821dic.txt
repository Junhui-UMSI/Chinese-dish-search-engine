Quinces in Syrup - Ayva Tatlisi
Quinces, Water, Sugar, To 3 cloves, Lemon Juice, Clotted cream (kaymak) *, 
Rinse and peel quinces. Cut into quarters and remove cores.  Combine water, sugar, cloves and lemon juice in a mall pot. Bring to a simmer a stir until sugar is dissolved. Add the quinces to the pot. Simmer for about 45 minutes, or until the quinces are tender. Cool completely. Discard cloves. Arrange quince slices on a serving platter.  Spoon clotted cream over quinces.   * NOTE: Clotted cream is available at many gourmet shops as French creme  Each serving contains about: 171 calories; 3 mg sodium; 0 cholesterol; 0 fa  Presented by: Rose Dosti, L.A.
