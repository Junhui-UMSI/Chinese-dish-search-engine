Baked Apple Doughnuts
Sugar, Apples, TOPPING, Cinnamon, Salt, Sugar, Shortening, Flour, Eggs, Baking Powder, Nutmeg, Pecans, milk, Butter, 
Date: Tue, 19 Mar 1996 18:30:18 -0500    From: "Vernon E. Phipps Jr." <cook4u@vivanet.com>    Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, salt, and nutmeg. Cut in shortening until  mixture looks like crumbs. Add eggs, milk, apples, nuts and mix thoroughly.  Fill well greased tiny muffin pans 2/3 full. Bake in preheated 350-degree  oven for 20-25 minutes. In a plastic bag mix sugar and cinnamon. Dip  doughnut tops in butter and shake in bake to coat.    MM-RECIPES@IDISCOVER.NET    MEAL-MASTER RECIPES LIST SERVER    MM-RECIPES DIGEST V3 #83    From the MealMaster recipe list.
