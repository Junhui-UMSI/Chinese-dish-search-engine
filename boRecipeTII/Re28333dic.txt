Baked Ham Slice in Sauterne
American sauterne, Center cut ham, Water, Raisins, AMERICAN HEART ASSOC. CKBK, Cornstarch, Margarine, Brown sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350~.  Place ham in a shallow baking pan. Set aside.  Combine sugar and cornstarch in a saucepan. Stir in water and margarine.  Place over med-high heat and cook, stirring constantly, 5 mins. Remove pan  from heat and stir in raisins and wine. Pour the mixture over ham. Bake  uncovered 45 mins. or until tender.    Per serving - 291 cal, 24 g pro, 31 g carbo, 60 mg chol, 1391 mg sod, 8 g  tot fat (2 g sat, 1 g poly, 4 g mono)    Note - one serving of this dish provides most of the recommended daily amt.  of sodium.
