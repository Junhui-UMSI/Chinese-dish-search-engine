Peanut Raisin and Chocolate Clusters
Raisins, Egg white, Raisins, Sugar, Chocolate chips, 
Beat egg white till frothy, add sugar gradually; continue beating till  light. Stir in rasins, peanuts and chocolate chips. Place in mounds on  cookie sheet lined with parchment (or buttered and floured). Bake in  preheated 375F oven 8 to 10 minutes or till browned. Cool. makes: 24  candies Source: Lucy Waverman in the Toronto Star
