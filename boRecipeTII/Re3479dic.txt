Pecan Logs
Water, Flour, Pecans, Salt, Butter, Sugar, Powdered sugar, Vanilla, 
Mix all ingredients together except powdered sugar. Form into logs  (about the 2" long and 1/2" dia.). Bake at 325-350 degrees for 12-15  min., or just until beginning to brown around edges (you dont want  them brown). Remove from baking sheet and cool. Roll in powdered  sugar.
