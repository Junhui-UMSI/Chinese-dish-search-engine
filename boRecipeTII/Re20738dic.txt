Apple Delight
Equal, Flour, Nutmeg, Walnuts, Diet margarine, Cinnamon, Peeled; sliced, Delicious, Diet whipped topping, 
Source: The Free and Equal Sweet Tooth Cookook    Place apples in bottom of greased baking dish. In a mixing bowl, combine  flour and spices. Cut in margarine until crumbly. Add walnuts. Bake for 30  to 40 minutes at 375 degrees. Remove from oven. Sprinkle Equal on top.  Serve with whipped topping.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by TraceyDJ <TraceyDJ@AOL.COM> on Apr 13, 1998
