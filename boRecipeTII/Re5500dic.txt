Pineapple Ginger Sauce
Sauce, Minced ginger, Cornstarch dissolved in 1, Cold water, Garlic, Juice, Low sodium tamari or soy, No-sugar-added pineapple, 
Saute garlic and ginger in a little water in a medium non-stick skillet.  After about 3 minutes add juice and soysauce.  Heat on medium high until it  boils, then stir in cornstarch.  Heat until it gets to your desired  consistency.    From: Lucinda Rasmussen <lrasmuss@PICA.ARMY.MIL>. Fatfree Digest [Volume 10  Issue 16] Aug. 26, 1994.
