White Bean Salad
Tightly packed shredded, Diced pimiento, (1 jar), Clove garlic, crushed, Drained capers, olive oil, Navy beans, (1 can) drained, Red wine vinegar, Coarsely ground pepper, 60%-less-salt tuna in water,, Dried whole rosemary, 
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl, and toss well. Yield: 3 cups  (serving size: 1 cup).    Per serving: 630 Calories; 7g Fat (10% calories from fat); 50g Protein; 94g  Carbohydrate; 18mg Cholesterol; 260mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve at room temperature.    Recipe by: Cooking Light, Jan/Feb 1994, page 73    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #410 by igor@digex.net on Jan 28, 1997.
