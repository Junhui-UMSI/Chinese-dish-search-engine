Tagine of Swis Chard
Sweet paprika, salt and pepper, Onion, Swiss chard, finely chopped, Raw rice, Cilantro, chopped, Salad oil, 
Place chard in the casserole with the onion, cilantro, oil, paprika, salt,  pepper & 1/4 c water.  Cook, covered, 30 minutes. Add the rice & continue  cooking until all the liquid has been evaporated, about 20 minutes. Serve  warm.
