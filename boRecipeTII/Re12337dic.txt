Zucchini Santa Fe
Cheddar or mj cheese, shred, Garlic powder, Onions, Chopped green chilies, drain, Tomato, Pepper, Sliced zucchini, Cooking oil, Salt, optional, 
In a large skillet, saute the zucchini and onion in oil for 3-4 minutes.  Add chilies, tomato, sald if desired, pepper and garlic powder. Cook and  stir for 3-4 minutes.  Spoon into a serving bowl and sprinkle with cheese.  Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Sarah Gruenwald <sitm@EKX.INFI.NET> on Jul 5,  1997
