Apricot-Carrot Muffins
Avocado Oil, Whole Raisins, Salt, Baking Powder, Sugar, All-purpose flour, Eggs, Whole wheat flour, milk, Carrots, coarsley grated, Walnuts, coarsley chopped, Dried Apricots, chopped or, 
In a large bowl mix flour, baking powder and salt. In another bowl whisk  sugar and eggs. Whisk in milk and oil. Mix in carrots, apricots, and nuts.  Add to flour mixture. Mix until just blended. Spoon into 12 2 3/4" muffin  tin cups greased with additional avocado oil. Bake in a 375o oven for about  20 minutes, until just springy to the touch. Serve warm.
