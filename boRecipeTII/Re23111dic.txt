Tu B Shvat Fruitcake
Baking Powder, Eggs, Dried fruit, Brown sugar, Mixed nuts: almonds;, Flour, 
Source: Jewish Communication Network URL:  http://www.virtual.co.il/city_services/holidays/tubshvat/recipes.htm    1. Mix all ingredients together.    2. Put in a greased loaf pan.    3. Bake at 350 degrees F preheated oven until brown.    4. Stick a toothpick in the center to check if the cake is done. If it  comes out clean, you can let it cool, then enjoy!
