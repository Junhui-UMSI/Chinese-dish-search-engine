Tangy Sangria
Fresh lime juice, Bottle; (750ml) red wine, Lemon; thinly sliced, Brandy, Orange; thinly sliced, Sugar, Fresh orange juice, Bottle sparkling water, Triple sec, Tabasco? pepper sauce, 
In pitcher, combine red wine, brandy, triple sec, lime juice, orange juice,  sugar, Tabasco pepper sauce and fruit slices. Chill.    To serve, fill large wine glass with ice. Pour 2/3 full with sangr?a and  top with sparkling water.
