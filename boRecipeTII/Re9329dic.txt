Puffed Pancake
milk, Butter, Flour, Eggs, Almond extract, 
In a bowl, beat eggs until thick and pale.  Add milk gradually, then flour,  beating until smooth.  Stir in extract.  Melt butter in medium cast iron  frying pan in preheated 425 degree oven.  Pour into frying pan and bake for       20    minutes or until brown    Recipe By     : Maui Queen    From:                                 Date: 05/30    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
