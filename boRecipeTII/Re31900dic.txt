Tabbouli #05
Medium-sized onion -or-, Medium-sized tomatoes; cut, olive oil, Parsley; finely chopped, Lemon juice, Fresh chopped mint, Scallions; or more, finely, Romaine lettuce hearts;, Salt, Fine bulgur; soaked in cold, 
Drain the bulgur and squeeze out as much moisture as possible with your  hands. Combine the bulgur, onion (or scallions), parsley and mint in a  bowl. Sprinkle with oil, lemon juice and a little salt, and mix thoroughly.  Taste and adjust the seasoning. Cover and chill in the refrigerator.    To serve, mound the salad in the center of a plate. Decorate with the  lettuce leaves and tomato wedges. The lettuce leaves may be used to scoop  up the salad.    One may also add to the salad: finely chopped tomatoes, cucumber or green  pepper.    We made this salad last summer with the parsley and mint from our garden.    DEMERS@ERE.UMONTREAL.CA    (DEMERS SERGE)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
