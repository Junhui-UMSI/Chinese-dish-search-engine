Tomato Bean Soup
Stick celery, Tomato puree, Canned tomatoes, Onion, Red or green capsicum, Carrot, Zucchini, 
Chop vegies and steam 4 -5 minutes. Combine with puree, tomatoes, 60g  kidney beans and herbs and seasoning to taste. Simmer 15 minutes.       60 g  fresh wholemeal bread        1/2    cup fruit salad    Here are a few light meals, high in fibre, ideal for lunches, from the  Audtralian Weight Watchers.
