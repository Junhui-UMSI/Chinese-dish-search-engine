Raspberry Puree
Frozen raspberries --, Cherry-flavoured brandy, 10 oz pkgs, Defrosted & drained, Kirsch or other, Superfine sugar, 
Rub the raspberries with the back of a large spoon through a fine sieve set  over a mixing bowl.  Stir in the sugar and kirsch, cover tightly with  plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve.    Recipe By     : Time-Life Foods of the World Russian Cooking
