Bar-B-Que Beef Spoonovers
Lean ground beef, Onions, Tomato sauce; or catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, Hamburger buns, Salt, Pepper; or to taste, Chopped green bell pepper, 
Saute ground beef with onion and green pepper until lightly browned. Drain  off excess fat.  Add all remaining ingredients.  Mix well and simmer for 15  minutes.  Spoon onto hamburger buns.
