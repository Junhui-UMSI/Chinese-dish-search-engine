Andouille #2
Inner lining of pork stomach, Pepper, Ground cloves, Pork (2 lb fat ; 2 lb lean), Salt (not iodized), Parsley, Large onions, Minced marjoram, Minced thyme, Cayenne pepper, Cloves of garlic, Bay Leaves, Chili pepper, Ground mace, Ground allspice, 
(you can use an extra pound of pork instead of the tripe.) - Chop, do not  grind the meat.  Mix with seasonings.  Stuff into casings. Age at least  overnight and then smoke several hours using hickory, hackberry or ash. (Do  not use pine.)  Throw anything sweet, such as cane sugar or syrup, raw  sugar, molassess, sugar cane or brown sugar on the wood before lighting.
