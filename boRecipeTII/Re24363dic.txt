Triple-Grain Banana Raisin Muffins
Maple syrup, Baking soda, Salt, Cooked brown rice, Unsweetened applesauce, Vanilla extract, Tsp baking powder, Orange juice, Whole wheat flour, Raisins, Ripe bananas, mashed, Rolled oats, ground, Cinnamon, 
Recipe by Lisa Rivero, Outpost Exchange Columnist, Printed in the Milwaukee  Journal/Sentinel, October 20, 1996    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 12 muffin muffin cups with vegetable  spray and set aside.    In medium bowl, whisk together dry ingredients.  Set aside.    Combine juice, rice, syrup, applesauce and vanilla in blender container.  Blend on high speed until very smooth.  Pour blended mixture into dry  ingredients.  Add mashed banana and raisins and stir until just combined.    Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.  Bake in preheated oven 20-25  minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.  Cool 10 minutes, then transfer  to cooling rack.
