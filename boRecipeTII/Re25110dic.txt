Tomato, Walnut, Blue Cheese Salad W/vinagrette
romaine lettuce, Balsamic vinegar, Garlic, blue cheese, tomatoes, parsley; oregano, basil, olive oil, Walnuts, 
Chop the lettuce into bit size pieces. Add the walnuts. tomatoes and blue  cheese. To make the vinaigrette: add olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic,  oregano, parsley and basil. Mix all ingredients together and toss.
