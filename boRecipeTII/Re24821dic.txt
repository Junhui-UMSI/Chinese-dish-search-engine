Aunt Nellies Sour Cream Raisin Pie
Eggs, Cinnamon, Raisins, Sour cream, Sugar, Pie crust, 
Pour boiling water over raisins and let stand 15-20 minutes. Pour  off. Pour sugar and cinnamon over raisins. Mix well. Add eggs and  sour cream.    Pour into bottom crust and make a lattice top crust. Bake for 15  minutes at 375F or until crust is brown.
