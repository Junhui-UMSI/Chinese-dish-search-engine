Stuffed Cabbage Rolls #2
Salt, Tomato soup, Bread crumbs, Onion, Cabbage, Ground beef, Eggs, Pepper, Garlic salt, 
Cook leaves in boiling water for 5 minutes; drain. Combine beef, bread  crumbs, onion, eggs, and seasonings. Shape meat mixture into 8 rolls; wrap  cabbage leaves around securely. Pour 1 can condensed tomato soup into  skillet. Add cabbage rolls. Cover, bring to boil. Simmer 35 minutes,  spooning sauce over rolls occasionally. Mrs. Robert F.
