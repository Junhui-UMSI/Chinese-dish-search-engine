Tostada Soup
Sour cream, (8 ozs) tomato sauce, Water or tomato juice, Onions, (15 ozs) pinto beans,, Ground cumin, Drained, (16 ozs) whole tomatoes, Pound ground beef or pork, Picante sauce, Shredded lettuce, Tortilla chips, crushed, Cheddar cheese, Toppings:, 
Brown meat with onion in 3-quart saucepan; drain well.  Drain and chop  tomatoes, reserving juice.  Add tomatoes and juice to saucepan with  beans, tomato sauce, water (or tomato juice), picante sauce and  cumin. Bring to a boil; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 15 minutes.  Ladle into soup bowls, and top with tortilla chips, cheese, lettuce,  sour cream and additional picante sauce as desired. Makes about 6  cups of soup.    Modified from Pace Picante Sauce 40th Anniversary Recipe Collection by  Loren Martin, Big Cabin, OK    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcyber4.zip
