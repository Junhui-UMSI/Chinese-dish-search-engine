"Champagne"
Club soda or seltzer, Frozen white grape juice, 
Stir together club soda or seltzer and grape juice concentrate. Pour into  two soda bottles. Cap tightly and chill. Serves 8 to 10.     Per serving: 30 cal; 0 prot; 0 fat; 7 g carb; 0 chol; 29 mg sod; 0 fiber;  vegan     Source: Vegetarian Times, Jan 94/MM by DEEANNE
