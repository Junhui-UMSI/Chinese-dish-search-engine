Vegi Spagetti
Sliced olives, Pack of spinach noodles, Garlic salt, Onion, diced, Zucchini, cubed, Fresh mushrooms, sliced, Grated Parmesan cheese, Jar spaghetti sauce, Oregano, Green pepper, chopped, 
Put everything except noodles in a crock pot for 6-8 hours on med. Pour  over cooked noodles.
