Banana Nut Muffins
Vanilla, LIMA sea salt, EDEN Safflower Oil, Baking soda, Baking Powder, EDENsoy, Baking soda, Walnut pieces, Raisins, Cinnamon, EDEN Barley Malt or honey, Organic whole wheat flour, Bananas, mashed, EDEN Brown Rice Vinegar, 
Sift dry ingredients into large bowl. In separate bowl, combine liquids,  add to dry ingredients and stir. Fold in bananas, walnuts and raisins.  Spoon into oiled muffin tin. Bake in preheated oven at 350 F for 20  minutes.
