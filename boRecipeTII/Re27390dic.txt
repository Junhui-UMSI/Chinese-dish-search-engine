Toasted Orzo Salad
Chopped cilantro, Minced jalapeno pepper, Red wine vinegar, Juice of 1 lime, Red onion,; diced fine, Dried orzo, Tomatoes,; coarsely chopped, Nicoise olives,; halved and, Minced fresh garlic, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Toss 1/2 of the orzo with 1 tablespoon of olive  oil. Spread on cookie sheet and toast in oven for just a few minutes or  until lightly browned.    Cook toasted and untoasted orzo in boiling salted water for 10 minutes.  Remove it from heat, rinse and drain.    In a large mixing bowl, add all the remaining ingredients and mix well.  Cover and refrigerate until 1 hour before serving.
