Apple-Glazed Roast Pork
Apple juice, RIVAL CROCKPOT COOKING, Apples, Sugar, brown, Ginger, ground, Pork loin roast, 
Rub roast with salt and pepper.  Brown pork roast under broiler to remove  excess fat; drain well.    Core and quarter apples.  Place apple quarters in bottom of crockpot. Place  roast on top of apples.  Combine apple juice, brown sugar, and ginger.  Spoon over top surface of roast, moistening well. Cover and cook on Low  10-12 hours, until done.
