Baked Tofu Casserole
Vegetable oil, Butter, Soy Sauce; divided use, Black pepper to taste, French beans; (cut in, Cakes of firm tofu, Vegetable oil, Onion, Garlic Butter, Mushrooms; sliced, Soy sauce, Ham; sliced into fine strips, Black pepper, Parsley; finely chopped, Salt, Garlic, Unsalted butter; melted, 
From: MeLizaJane <MeLizaJane@aol.com>    Pat dry the tofu with paper towel and cut into 12 even slices. Heat oil and  fry both sides of tofu until browned. Add 2 T of soy sauce and black  pepper, set aside. Boil French beans with a pinch of salt. Heat oil and  stir-fry onion, mushrooms and ham. Add cooked beans and 2/3 T of Soy Sauce.  Pepper to taste. Grease baking dish with butter and place tofu in it. Cover  tofu with the stir-fried ingredients and sauce. To make garlic butter, mix  unsalted butter with garlic, parsley and soy sauce. Pour garlic butter over  dish. Bake at 96C/205F in pre-heated oven for       10    minutes.    Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com on Mar 29, 1998
