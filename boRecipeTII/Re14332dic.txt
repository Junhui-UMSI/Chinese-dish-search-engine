Whole Fruit and Nut Cookies
Whole pecans, Butter or margarine, Whole pitted dates, Whole Brazil nuts, Flour, Baking soda, Whole candied cherries (red, Whole pieces candied, Salt, Vanilla, Sugar, Whole walnuts, Brown sugar, Eggs (added one at a time), 
Gather the fruits and nuts together to have ready to add to the batter when  ready. Cream the butter, sugars and vanilla together; mix well. Then add  the 3 eggs, one at a time, beating well. Add the flour, baking soda and  salt. Then the batter is ready for the fruits and nuts.    Divide the dough into 4 parts and wrap each in aluminum foil and shape the  rolls into the size cookie desired. Chill several hours or will keep for  several weeks. Slice when desired using a real sharp knife. Bake at 350  degrees for 10-12 minutes. Or watch carefully to brown.
