Baked Italian Sausage Links
POLISH SAUSAGE, Frozen, WATER, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCHES SHEET PAN                TEMPERATURE:  400 F. OVEN    1.  PLACE ITALIAN SAUSAGE LINKS IN SINGLE LAYERS ON 4 SHEET PANS (18 BY 26  INCH).  PIERCE EACH SAUSAGE.  POUR 1 CUP HOT WATER OVER SAUSAGES IN EACH  PAN.  COVER; BAKE IN 400F. OVEN 20 MINUTES.  REMOVE COVER; BAKE 15 MINUTES LONGER  OR UNTIL BROWNED.
