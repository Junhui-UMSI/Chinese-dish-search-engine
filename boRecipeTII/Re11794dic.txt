Ravioli Cheese Filling
cream Cheese, Ricotta cheese, salt and pepper, fresh sweet, Lemon zest, Nutmeg, Basil leaves, Parmesan Cheese, egg yolks, 
Combine the 3 cheeses in a bowl & cream with wooden spoon. Beat in  yolks. Add zest & spices. Cover & refrigerate until ready to use.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
