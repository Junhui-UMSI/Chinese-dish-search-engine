Veal Scallopini #1
Sherry, Butter, Water, Salt, Flour, Pepper, Mushrooms, Veal, 
Pound veal very thin; sprinkle lightly with a mixture of flour, salt and  pepper. Brown veal quickly on all sides in 3 tablespoons butter.  Place  veal in a shallow casserole. saute mushrooms in remaining butter until  golden. Add water and wine; scrape bottom and sides of pan. Simmer for 3  minutes. Pour mushroom sauce over veal; cover and bake at 350 degrees for  30 minutes. Baste if necessary. Serves 6.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
