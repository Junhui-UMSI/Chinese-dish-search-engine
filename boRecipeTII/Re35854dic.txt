Stuffed Green Peppers
Egg, Salt, thyme, Onion powder, Tomato sauce, OPTIONAL GARNISH, Grated Parmesan cheese, Green peppers, Rice, Black Pepper, Garlic powder, Lean ground beef, Beef bouillon granules, 
Place pepper halves in shallow 3-quart microproof baking dish.  Cover and  cook on HIGH (maximum power) 4 minutes.  Combine beef, rice, seasonings,  egg, and 1/2 cup tomato sauce in large mixing bowl.  Mound beef mixture in  pepper halves.  Cover and cook on HIGH 10 minutes, rotating once.  Combine  remaining tomato sauce and bouillon granules.  Pour over stuffed peppers.  Cook uncovered on HIGH 5 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes.  Garnish with  Parmesan cheese, if desired.
