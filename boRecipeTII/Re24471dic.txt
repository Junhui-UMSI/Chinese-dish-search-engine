Tropical Smoothie
Pineapple juice, unsweetened, Strawberries, Papaya, Mango, Banana, 
* If using mangos or papaya, make sure they are ripe.    1. Peel and dice the fruit. 2. Have the pineapple juice well-chilled. 3.  Combine all ingredients in a blender. Puree until thick and very smooth.  Serve in a glass garnished with a whole strawberry and perhaps a mint  sprig.
