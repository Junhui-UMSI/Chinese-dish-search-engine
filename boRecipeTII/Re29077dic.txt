Baked Potato Wedges
Potatoes, Butter, Garlic powder, Parsley, salt and pepper, 
Scrub potatoes. Do not peel. Cut each potato lengthwise in quarters. Stir  together melted butter, garlic powder and parsley. Arrange potatoes skin  side down on a shallow baking pan. Brush seasoned butter over cut surfaces  of potatoes. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees  for about 1 hour or until golden brown and tender when pierced.
