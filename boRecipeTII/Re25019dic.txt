Tomato Salsa Cruda
Onion,, chopped, Black Pepper, Italian Roma tomatoes,,, Lime, Juice of, Serrano chiles,, chopped, Sea salt, 
In a blender, place the tomatoes, chiles, onion, lime juice, salt, and  pepper. Blend briefly, making sure the salsa remains rough. Store in a  covered container, in the refrigerator, no more than 1 day.
