Baked Spare Ribs and Sauerkraut
Baking Powder, milk, Sauerkraut, Flour, Spare ribs, Egg, 
Cut spare ribs into portions and place in the bottom of a roasting pan. Add  the sauerkraut, cover the pan and bake at 350-F for 1-1/2 hours. Make  dumplings by combining the flour, baking powder, milk and egg. Drop by  spoonfuls on the sauerkraut. Cover pan tightly and finish baking at 350-F  for 30 minutes. Source: Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes,  Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
