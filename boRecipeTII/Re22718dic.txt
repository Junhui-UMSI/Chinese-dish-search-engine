Apricot Kolachky Filling
Dried appricots, Water, Sugar, Cornstarch, 
Contributed to the echo by: Leti Labell Originally from: "The Best of  Boris" by the St. Mark Womens Guild of Bethesda, Md. Apricot Kolachky  Filling #1 Wash apricots. Combine apricots and water; cook until soft,  about 1/2 hour, stirring constantly to keep from scorching. Stir in sugar  and cornstarch and cook for one minute. Cool. use a teaspoonful of filling  for each square. Makes 8 dozen.
