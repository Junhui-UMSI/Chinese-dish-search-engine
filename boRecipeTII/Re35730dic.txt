Stuffed Celery Sticks
Mayonnaise or salad dressing, Finely chopped fully cooked, Stalks celery, Shredded Swiss cheese, Prepared mustard, 
Cut celery into 3-inch pieces (make sure celery is completely dry). Mix  remaining ingredients; spread about 1 tb in each piece of celery. Cover and  refrigerate 1 hour. About 14 appetizers; 65 calories per appetizer.    Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
