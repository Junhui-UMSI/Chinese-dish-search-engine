Artichoke Linguine
Stick melted butter, Salt, Clove garlic crushed, Minced fresh parsely, Fresh Lemon Juice, Linguine; cooked al, (14-oz) artichoke hearts;, Flour, olive oil, Salt and pepper to taste, Grated Parmesan, Butter, olive oil, Minced Prosciutto or other, Capers; rinsed and drained, Grated Parmesan, Chicken broth, -(up to), 
Date: Sun, 24 Mar 1996 09:10:13 +0300    From: dweinsaf@lib.drury.edu (Dianne Weinsaft)  Melt butter with oil in small saucepan over medium heat.  Add flour and  stir until smooth about 3 minutes. Blend in broth, stirring until  thickened, about 1 minute. Reduce heat to low and add garlic, parsely,  lemon juice, salt and pepper and cook about 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Blend in artichokes, cheese and capers. Cover and simmer about 8 minutes.    Melt remaining butter in large skillet over medium heat.  Stir in remaining  oil, cheese and salt.  Add linguine and toss lightly.  Arrange pasta on  platter and pour sauce over.  Garnish with prosciutto.
