Apple Cider Bread or Muffins
Cinnamon, Apples, peeled -- cored and, Chopped, Eggs, Baking Powder, Flour, Cinnamon, Butter, Sugar, Apple cider, Salt, 
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.  Grease and flour 2 loaf pans or 18 muffin  cups. Cream the butter and 1 1/4 cup of the sugar together in large bowl.  Add eggs and cream thoroughly. Sift the dry ingredients together.  Add them  to the egg mixture, alternating with the apple cider. Stir in the chopped  apples. Divide the batter between the prepared loaf pans. Combine the  remaining 1/4 cup of sugar with the cinnamon in a small bowl, and sprinkle  it over the batter. Bake at 375 degrees for 50 to 70 minutes for bread, or  for 30 to 40 minutes for muffins, or until a toothpick inserted in the  center comes out clean. Yields 2 loaves or 18 muffins.
