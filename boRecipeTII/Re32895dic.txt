Sweet Red Lentils
Red lentils  [dried - I, Green chili, chopped [or, Vegetable stock, Parsley to garnish, chopped, Salt, Bay Leaves, Chopped fresh ginger, Canned lentils], Chili powder, Small, Garlic clove, chopped thin, Onion, sliced thin, Hot water, Cinnamon sticks, Lemon, Fresh coriander leaves or, Cayenne pepper], 
[Using vegetable bouillon is easier than cooking the broth yourself.]    Fry onions and garlic until soft, add cinnamon, lentils, chili, and ginger  and cook for a minute.  Add stock, hot water, chili powder, and bay leaves.  Boil for 10 minutes.  Squeeze the lemons into the pot and then throw in the  lemon rinds.  Cook for another 50 minutes. Salt to taste, and then remove  the lemon rinds.    From: narad@nudibranch.asd.sgi.com (Chuck Narad).  rfvc Digest V94 Issue  #183 Aug. 30, 1994.
