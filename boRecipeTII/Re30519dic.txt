Taco Stew (Corrected)
Hamburger; browned & drained, Taco seasoning mix, Uncooked rice, (15 oz) red kidney beans;, Onions, (24 oz) V8 juice, Corn (Green Giant), (small) sliced olives, 
Cook hamburger and onion. Drain. Put everything in Dutch oven. Simmer for 2  hours or more. Add taco chips  Posted to EAT-L Digest  by cd <poppi@PRIMENET.COM> on Sep 20, 1997
