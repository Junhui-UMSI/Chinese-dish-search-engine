Zesty Rice
Chicken Bouillon Base, Sliced mushrooms; (4 oz), Onion, Butter or margarine, Grated Parmesan cheese, Green pepper; chopped, Sliced ripe olives, Jar pimentos; (2 oz) diced, Celery; diced, Uncooked rice, Chicken broth; or 2 cups, 
Place chicken broth, butter, onion, pepper, celery and rice in a 2 quart  casserole. Mix well. Cover with plastic wrap. Microwave at high (100%)  until rice is tender, 18 to 20 minutes, stirring twice during cooking time.  Stir in olives, mushrooms, pimentos and cheese. Microwave at medium high  (70%) until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes. Serve in place of potatoes.  Makes 6 ample servings.
