Quiche Lorraine
Egg, Onion, minced, Sugar, Bacon slices, cooked, Swiss cheese, shredded, Cayenne, Cream, light, Pie shell, deep frozen, Salt, 
Sprinkle bacon, cheese and onion in bottom of frozen pie shell. Beat  together eggs, cream and seasonings. Pour egg mixture into pie shell. Bake  in preheated 425 deg oven for 15 minutes on bottom shelf. Reduce  temperature to 325 deg and continue baking 20 minutes or till knife  inserted comes out clean. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting.
