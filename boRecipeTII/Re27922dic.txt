Thousand Island Dressing
Pitted green olives, minced, Green pepper; finely diced, Mayonnaise, Green onion, thinly sliced, Salt to taste, EDEN Crushed Tomatoes, 
Combine crushed tomatoes and mayonnaise in a blender or food processor.  Remove and combine with green pepper, green olives, green onion, and  salt. Serve on tossed salad.
