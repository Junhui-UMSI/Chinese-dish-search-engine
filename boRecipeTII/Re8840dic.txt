Praline Squares
Salt, Vanilla extract, Egg, Pecans, Margarine, Baking Powder, Light brown sugar, Flour, 
Melt margarine, remove from heat. Blend in sugar and cool. Stir in egg. Mix  flour, baking powder and salt togther. Stir into mixture. Add vanilla and  nuts. Spread into greased and floured 8 inch square pan. Bake 350 degrees  for 20 minutes. Do Not Overbake! Cool before cutting.
