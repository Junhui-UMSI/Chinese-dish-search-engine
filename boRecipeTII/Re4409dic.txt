Pesach Mandlen
Salt, Eggs, Matzoh meal, Vegetable oil, Pepper, Water, 
Bring oil, water, salt and pepper to a boil. Add matzoh meal and stir until  mixture leaves sides of pan. Remove from heat; beat in eggs, one at a time.  Grease hands; shape dough into tiny balls. Bake at 400 degrees F. on a  greased cookie sheet for 1/2 hour. Serve in hot soup or eat as a snack.
