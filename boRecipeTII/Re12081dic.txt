Ziti with Creamy Herb Sauce
Oregano leaves, Parsley -- chopped, Salt, Chicken bouillon cube, Pasta -- ziti, Cheese, parmesan -- grated, Water -- boiling, Pepper, Cheese -- ricotta, Basil, fresh -- chopped, 
Prepare pasta as directed on package; drain. Meanwhile, stir cheeses with  parsley and seasonings. Dissolve bouillon cube in boiling water; stir into  cheese mixture.  Pour over pasta; toss to coat.
