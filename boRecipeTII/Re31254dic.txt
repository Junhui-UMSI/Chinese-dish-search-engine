Banana-Strawberry Frappe
Bananas, Cold milk, Lemon juice, Strawberry yogurt, 
* Use a blender or food processor for this recipe.       Put all ingredients in blender and turn to puree mode until of desired  consistency. (Make only about 2 servings at one time so the blender will  not be overfilled.) If using a food processor, use the steel knife to  process; 8-10 seconds will be sufficient.    Serve in a tall glass.
