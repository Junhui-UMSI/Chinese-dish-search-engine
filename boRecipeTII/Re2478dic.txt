Bourbon Breast of Chicken
Paprika, Vegetable oil, (10 ounce) tomatoes, Salt, Onion, Dried chervil, (4 ounce) mushrooms;, Salt and pepper, Chicken breasts halves, Parsley, Sugar, Butter, Flour, Bourbon, 
Dredge chicken in flour which has been mixed with paprika and a little  salt. Heat butter and oil in a skillet and saute chicken on both sides  until lightly browned. Stir in onion, parsley and chervil and cook a  moment. Remove from heat. Place chicken in crock cooker. Combine remaining  ingredients and pour over chicken. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.  Serve with noodles of rice.
