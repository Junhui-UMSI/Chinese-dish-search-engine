Sweet Corn Ravioli
Cream, Salt, Water, Round wonton wrappers, Ears fresh corn, kernels, Raw shrimp,; shelled and, Chives, Eggs, Pepper, 
With a knife, roughly chop shrimp. In a food processor blend shrimp, corn  and cream. Season with salt, pepper and chives. Lay out wanton wrappers and  brush with an egg wash. Place 1 tablespoon of the shrimp mixture on one  side of each wrapper. Fold over each wrapper and seal with a fork. Bring  salted water to a boil and place ravioli in water. Poach for about 4  minutes.
