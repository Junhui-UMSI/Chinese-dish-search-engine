Double Chocolate Cheesecake Brownies
Egg, Kosher Brownie Mix; 22.5 oz., Cream cheese, Sugar, Vanilla, Chocolate chips, 
Recipe By : Jacqueline Glasgow, Beaufort House B & B, Asheville, NC    Preheat oven to 350?. Grease a 9 x 13" baking pan.    Make the brownie mix as directed and pour into pan. Beat the cream cheese,  eggs, sugar and vanilla with an electric mixer in a separate bowl until  well combined. Drop the cream cheese mixture by tablespoons on top of the  brownie mixture. Swirl the mixture together with the end of a knife.  Sprinkle the chocolate chips over the entire mixture.    Bake for 20-30 minutes or until cream cheese is slightly golden. Let cool  for 20 minutes. Place in the refrigerator for 1 hour to chill before  cutting into squares.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by SuperSecDD@aol.com on Sep 7, 1998,
