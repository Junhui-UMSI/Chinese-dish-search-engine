Angel Sandwiches
Sweetened Whipped Cream, Shortending (part butter), Raisins, Flour, Cut up dates, Sugar, Topping, Confectioners sugar, 
Heat oven to 350. Mix shortening & sugar. Stir flour into shortening  mixture. Press & flatten with hand to cover bottom of ungreased  oblong pan. Bake 15 minutes. Blend fruit, sugar & water in saucepan.  Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until thickened, 10 minutes.  Cool both crust & filling. Spread filling over crust; spread topping  over filling. Chill before cutting in 1" squares. Store in  refrigerator.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
