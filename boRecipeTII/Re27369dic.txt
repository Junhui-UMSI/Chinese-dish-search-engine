Toasted Country Bread
Thick Slices crusty country, Extravirgin olive oil, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Brush bread slices lightly with olive oil on  both sides. Place on rack placed on a cookie sheet and place in oven. Toast  for 10-15 minutes, or until just beginning to brown. Spoon tomato salad on  top and serve.
