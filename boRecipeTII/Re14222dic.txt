White Radish Relish
Daikon; coarsely shredded, Soy sauce, Canola oil, Rice vinegar, Toasted sesame oil, Sugar, Red chili pepper; seeded,, Salt, Green onions; thinly sliced, 
This relish has a real kick; serve it with Korean Barbecued Beef and other  mild foods. Combine daikon, onion and chili in a bowl. In another bowl  combine remaining ingredients until sugar is dissolved. Pour over daikon  mixture. Cover and chill up to 24 hours. Makes 16 servings.
