Cannoli for Kids
Vanilla extract, Minature chocolate chips, Sugar ice-cream cones, Part-skim ricotta cheese, Orange Peel, 
In a large bowl with electric mixer at low, beat ricotta cheese, sugar,  orange peel, and vanilla just until smooth. Stir in chocolate chips. Cover  and refrigerate 30 minutes.    To serve, spoon mixture directly into ice-cream cones or into decorating  bag without tip and then pipe into cones.    Prep time: 10 minutes; Total time: 40 minutes.
