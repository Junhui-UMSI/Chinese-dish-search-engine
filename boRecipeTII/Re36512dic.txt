Strawberries Dipped in Chocolate and Biscotti
strawberries, Bittersweet chocolate, biscotti, 
Melt the chocolate in a bowl over hot water or using the microwave melting  directtions on the package. Turn the biscotti into crumbs by taking a  rolling pin to it after youve placed it in a heavy-duty plastic bag.  Holding onto the hull, dip wach berry into the melted chocolate to cover  helf the berry, then into the crumbs. Set on a sheet of waxed paper until  the chocolate is firm.
