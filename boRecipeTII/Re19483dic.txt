Vegetarian Pizza
Part-skim mozzarella cheese, Dried basil leaves, 12 inch Boboli crust, (8oz) shredded low-moisture, Halved, Broccoli flowerets, Red onion rings, Tomato, cut into thin wedges, 
Heat oven to 350F. Place crust on cookie sheet. Top with vegetables. Bake  10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted and topping thoroughly heated.     --
