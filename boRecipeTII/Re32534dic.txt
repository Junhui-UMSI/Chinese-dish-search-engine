Sweet Garden Peas with Mint
Butter, Water, Shredded fresh mint, Scallions, minced, Pepper, Salt, Frozen tiny peas, thawed, Lettuce leaves, shredded, 
Place lettuce in a heavy medium pan. Add scallions, butter, salt, and  pepper. Cover and cook on high until steam starts to rise. Add peas and  water. Cover again, bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer 10 to 15  minutes or until peas are tender but still retain their bright color. Stir  in mint, cover, and steam 1 minute. Serve immediately.
