Watercress Bisque (Haas)
Water, Watercress; coarsely chopped, Lemon wedges; to garnish, Onion, Dry white wine; optional, Carrot; finely chopped, Light miso; or to taste, 
Simmer onion and carrot in wine or 1/2 cup water until onion is limp and  transparant. Add watercress and simmer for 20 minutes. Puree with miso.  Serve with a lemon wedge.    See DIET FOR ALL SEASONS, by Elson M.
