Peaches Italian Style
Wine, Port, Wine, white, Peaches, Sugar, Vermouth, sweet, Juice, lemon, Juice, orange, 
Peel the peaches.  Slice them into medium-sized pieces.  Place  them in a bowl and sprinkle with sugar.  Add lemon juice, orange  juice, Port, and white wine.  Mix well, (try not to bruise the  peaches) and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.         Serve in chilled glass cups.         Source: Great Chefs of San Francisco, Avon Books, 1984       Chef:   Adriana Giramonti, Giramonti Restaurant, San Francisco, CA    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mm-gc-sf.zip
