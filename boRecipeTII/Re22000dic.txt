Turkey Chili
Water, Crushed red pepper (opt.), Vegetable juice cocktail, Celery, Dried minced onion, Lean ground raw turkey, Dried basil, crushed, Beef bouillon granules, Red kidney beans (15.5oz cn), Tomatoes, cut up (16 oz can), Ground cumin, Bay leaf, Non-stick spray coating, Tomato sauce (8oz can), Garlic powder, 
Spray a cold large skillet with non-stick coating. Add turkey and  celery.  Cook until turkey is no longer pink, stirring to break up pieces.   Stir in undrained tomatoes, beans, tomato sauce, juice cocktail, bay  leaf, onion, basil, bouillon granules, cumin, garlic powder, red pepper, if  desired, and water.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, for  20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Discard bay leaf. Makes 4 servings.  Nutrition Information per Serving:  380 cal., 12 g fat, 33 g protein, 36 g  carbo., 926 sodium.
