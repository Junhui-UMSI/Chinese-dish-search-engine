Baked Fish
Salt, Fish Filets, Lemon, Pepper (fresh ground is, Basil, 
Place fish in a pan and salt, pepper, lightly put basil, and squeeze lemon,  on top. Turn over and do again. Put in oven and bake on 300degrees for  about 45 min. Posted to Bakery Shoppe Digest149 by Sandy Courrege  <sandy@aisp.net> on Jul 07, 1997
