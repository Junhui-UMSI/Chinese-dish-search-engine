Aloo Dam
Chili powder, Coriander powder, Yogurt, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Bay leaf, Salt, Potatoes, Cumin seeds, Onion, Garlic, Capsicum (pepper), Ginger, Cumin seeds, Tomato, Turmeric, 
Heat oil, add bay leaf and onion.  Fry for 3-4 minutes.  Add ginger and  garlic and fry for another minute.  Add mustard and cumin seeds. The  potatoes should be sliced, and the tomatoes and capsicum cut up. Add these,  mix well, and cook for 4-5 minutes, continuously stirring. Sprinkle with  turmeric, coriander and chili powder. Beat the yogurt and blend into a  smooth mixture. Add yogurt and salt. Mix gently, cover and cook for about       10    minutes on low heat.
