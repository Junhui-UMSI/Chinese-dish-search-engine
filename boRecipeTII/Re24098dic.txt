Traditional Baked Beans
Water, Molasses, Brown sugar, Salt pork, in 1" cubes, Salt, Onion, Dijon mustard, Dried small white beans, 
Combine all ingredients.  Cover and cook on LOW 13-14 hours, stirring  occasionally if possible.    For added nutrition, serve with rice or bread or cornbread.    Crockery Favorites, Mable Hoffman    Typed by E.Marie Campbell
