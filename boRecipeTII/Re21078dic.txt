Twinkies
CAKE, Flour, Cold Milk, Cream of tartar, Baking Powder, Flour, Butter & Crisco mixture, Egg yolks, FILLING, Vegetable oil, Cold water, Vanilla, Sugar, Salt, Vanilla, Egg whites, Sugar, 
*** Cake ***    In a large bowl, mix the first 4 ingredients. Make a well and add the next  4 ingredients. Beat until smooth with a spoon and set aside. Beat the egg  whites with the cream of tartar until stiff peaks form. Pour over egg yolk  batter and fold in until well beated. Pour in an ungreased 10x14 pan. Bake  45-50 minutes in a 350oF preheated oven. Cool in pan. Invert in pan with  cups under each corner. Cool completely and run a knife around the edge.  Remove and cut in half.    *** Filling ***    Combine the first 3 ingredients and beat on high for 5 minutes, gradually  adding the milk and vanilla. Beat 5 more minutes on high. Spread between 2  layers. Cut to size (3x1 inch) and wrap separately    Source: "The Yankee Kitchen" 03-26-93 (#1) [Helen]
