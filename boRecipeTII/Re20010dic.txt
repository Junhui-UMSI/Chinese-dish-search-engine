Valentine Sugar Cookies
Almond extract, Vanilla extract, Softened, Butter or margarine, Egg, Flour; all purpose, Red decorators sugar ( opt.), 
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add egg and extracts. Stir in  flour; mix well. Chill for several hours. On a lightly floured surface,  roll dough to 1/4 inch thickness; cut with a 2 1/2 or 3 inch heart shaped  cookie cutter. Place on ungreased baking sheets; sprinkle with red sugar,  if desired. Bake at 375F for 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.
