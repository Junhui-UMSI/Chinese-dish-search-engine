Fiesta Chicken And Black Beans
Gound cumin, Chili powder, Onion, Green pepper, tomatoes  , tomato paste  , Boneless chicken breast, corn  , black beans  , 
Combine all ingredients; mix well. Cover and cook on low for 5 to 6 hours.  Garnish with shredded cheese, if desired. Serve with warmed flour  tortillas, or over rice. Serves 4.
