Kidney Bean Salad
(15-oz) kidney beans;, Hard boiled eggs; chopped, American cheese; chopped, -(up to), Size sweet pickles;, (4-oz) Spanish olives;, Mustard, salt and pepper, Salad dressing, 
Combine all ingredients. Add sufficient Miracle Whip salad dressing to mix  well. Salt & pepper to taste. Mix well. Serve chilled.    MRS C.B. (JUDY) MAPES    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
