Garlic Oil
Head garlic, olive oil, 
Separate garlic into cloves and peel. Place in jar or bottle. For  decorative effect, thread garlic cloves on bamboo skewer. Fill with oil and  tightly seal. Let stand overnight before using, then store in refrigerator.  Use for dressings, marinades and sautes.    Recipe by Totally Garlic.    Makes 3 cups.
