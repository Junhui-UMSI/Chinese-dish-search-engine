Tavern Soup
Green pepper -- finely, Temperature, Chopped, Onion -- finely chopped, Butter, Salt, Canned chicken broth, Pepper, Flour, Carrot -- finely chopped, Can light beer -- at room, Grated sharp cheddar cheese, Celery -- finely chopped, 
Combine celery, carrot, green pepper, and onion in slow-cooking pot.  Add  broth, butter, salt, and pepper.  Cover and cook on low for 5 to 6 hours.  Strain mixture; puree vegetables in blender and return to pot with broth.  Turn control to high.  dissolve flour in small amount of water; add to  broth.  Add cheese, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until blended.  Pour in  beef.  Cover and cook on high 15 to 20 minutes.
