Winkler Sugar Cookies
Almond extract, Sugar, Flour, Vanilla, Baking soda, Butter, Nutmeg, Cream of tartar, Eggs, Lemon extract, Salt, 
Date: Thu, 12 Aug 93 09:33:35 +0200    From: hz225wu@unidui.uni-duisburg.de (Micaela Pantke) (COLLECTION)    lafauss@aplcen.apl.jhu.edu (Laura Faussone) (makes approx. 8 dozen) Cream  butter and sugar in a large mixing bowl.  Add eggs one at a time. In a  separate bowl, combine flour, cream of tartar, salt, baking soda and  nutmeg. Beat extracts into butter mixture and gradually incorporate flour  mixture, blending thoroughly. Place dough in greased bowl and refrigerate  overnight. Before baking, preheat oven to 350F. Roll dough out on floured  pastry cloth. Cut with cookie cutters and bake on greased cookie sheets for  10-15 minutes until golden brown.
