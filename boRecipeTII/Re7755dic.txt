Portabella Sandwich
Beef stock or broth, Beefsteak tomato, Chopped fresh garlic, Salt, Spinach leaf chopped into, Portabella msuhrooms (caps, Fresh chopped basil, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Pesto sauce, Marsala wine, Foccacia (4 inch diameter), Fresh chopped rosemary, Sugar, Mayonnaise, 
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Reduce stock to 1/2 cup. Combine mayonnaise  and pesto and refrigerate. Place mushroom caps, gill side down, on cookie  sheet and bake for 8-9 minutes. While mushrooms are baking, combined reduce  stock, wine, garlic, rosemary, and oil in saucepan. Bring to a boil for 1  minute. Slice focaccia in half and toast in broiler for 2 minutes or until  light golden brown. Add baked mushrooms to beef stock and keep warm. Season  tomatoes with salt, sugar, and basil. Assemble by placing bottom of  focaccia on plate, top with pesto-mayonnaise, spinach, mushroom, tomato and  focaccia top. Remaining liquid can be reserved for additional sandwiches.
