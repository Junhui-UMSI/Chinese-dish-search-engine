Tofu Pizza
Fresh Mushrooms Sliced, Grated Parmesan, Green Pepper, Tomato & Garlic Sauce,, Drained, Into Six Rounds, Pita Breads, Split, Tofu, Pressed, 
Preheat Oven To 375 F.    Place Pita Bread Rounds On Cookie Sheet & Spread With Tomato & Garlic  Sauce.  Crumble Tofu Over Each Pizza Crust.  Top With Green Pepper &  Mushrooms. Sprinkle With Cheese.  Bake 15 Min.     (Fat 6.6.  Chol. 1.0.)    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
