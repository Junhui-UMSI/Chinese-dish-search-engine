Apple and Honey Scones
Wheat germ, milk, Wheat germ, Salt, Ground nutmeg, Topping, Baking soda, Honey, Tart apples; peeled and chop, Ground cinnamon, Sugar, Baking Powder, All-purpose flour, Ground cinnamon, Butter or margarine; cold, 
Recipe by: Country Woman March/April 1997 In a large bowl, combine  flour,wheat germ, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, salt and nutmeg.  Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. combine the  apple, milk and honey; add to dry ingredients just until moistened. Turn  onto a floured surfac, knead gently 5-6 times. Gently patt dough into a  9-in. circle, 1/2 in. thick. Combine topping ingredients; sprinkle over  dough.   Cut into eight wedges and place on a greased baking sheet. Bake at      400    degrees for 15-18 minutes.  Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest by angstrom@juno.com (Angela  L Gilliland)  on Mar 31, 1998
