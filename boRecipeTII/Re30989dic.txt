Banana Nut Bread 2
milk, Vanilla, Onions, Mashed bananas (about 2, Eggs, Sugar, Bisquick Original or Reduced, Vegetable oil, 
Heres whats in my Bisquick Best Recipes booklet..    Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease bottom of loaf pan, 9x5x3 inches.    Mix bananas, sugar, milk, oil, eggs and vanilla in large bowl. Stir in  baking mix and nuts. Pout into pan.    Bake 50 to 60 minutes or until tooghpick inserted in center comes out  clean. Cool 10 minutes; loosen loaf from sides of pan. Remove from pan;  cool completely before slicing.         24    slices.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 16 October 96    Date:    Thu, 17 Oct 1996 21:53:07 -0500    From:    Julie Benson <cejab@CIN.NET>
