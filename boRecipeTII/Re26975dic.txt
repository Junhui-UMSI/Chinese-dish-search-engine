Baked Cheese Potatoes
Egg, Clove garlic; split, Nutmeg, Salt, Sharp cheddar cheese; grated, milk, Pepper, Salad oil, Potatoes; grated, 
Pour oil into a 2-quart baking dish and rub oil around dish with garlic.  Combine remaining ingredients and pour into the baking dish. Bake at 325  degrees for 1 hour and 30 minutes.
