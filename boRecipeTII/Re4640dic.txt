Pheasant Deluxe
Ritz Crackers; crushed, Mushroom Soup; undiluted, Salt, Onion Chopped, Pheasant; dressed and, Pepper, Broth, American Cheese; grated, Celery, Eggs, 
Simmer pheasant in salted water until tender; remove bones. Cut meat into  small pieces, set aside. Beat eggs, add broth and soup. Add onion, pepper,  salt, celery, cheese and crackers. Add pheasant meat (can also use Chicken  meat) and place in 9x13 inch baking dish/pan. Bake at 350 F. 1 hour. Makes  9-10 servings    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Bill Spalding <billspa@ICANECT.NET> on Dec 20,  1997
