Fish and Vegetable Stew
Vegetable oil, Flour, Root vegetables, Potatoes, Tomatoes, Onions, Garlic, vegetables, salt and pepper, Water, fish, 
cooking time: about 1 hour.    dice the root vergetable, the tomatoes, the potatoes and the onions. Chop  te other vegetables. Put all the vegatbles, salt and pepper and water into  a saucepan and cook until potatoes almost are tender. Cut fish up into  serving pieces, brush white oil and roll in flour. Put the fish on the  vegetables. Continue to cook for another 30 minutes, basting the fish with  the sauce. If you like, the fish can be put in whole at the beginning, and  vegetables can be arranged and cooked in an ornamental pattern. A Rumaniah  dish.
