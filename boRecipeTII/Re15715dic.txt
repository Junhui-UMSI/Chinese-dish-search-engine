A Working Womans Chili
Green pepper, diced, Black olives (extra large),, Garlic, Celery, diced, 11 ounce stewed tomatoes, Brown sugar, Oregano vinegar, Ground round beef, Water, Onion, Cumin, Chili pepper, minced, 15 ounce kidney beans, Chili powder, 
1.  Saute ground round in a little olive oil, drain, set aside. Saute bell  peppers, diced celery, onion, garlic till tender. Add remaining  ingredients, except the cup of water.    2.  Let simmer for approximately 15 minutes, add water and continue to  simmer another 15 minutes.  (Can adjust amount of water to whatever  consistency preferred.    NOTE:  The oregano vinegar is made by steeping fresh oregano in white  vinegar for at least 10 days, longer is better.    NOTE:  Chili Pepper; From a can of Chiles Chipotles Adobados plus 1  tablespoon sauce. From: Michelle Bass Date: Fri, 01-2
