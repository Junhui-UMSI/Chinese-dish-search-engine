Corn Pudding
Can golden crushed corn, Eggs, slightly beaten, Flour, Sugar, milk, Salt and pepper to taste, Lots of butter, 
Mix all ingredients together.  Place in buttered casserole.  Bake in slow  oven at 300 degrees for one hour.    From: "Amish - Dutch Cookbook"  By: Ruth Redcay  Published By: Ben Herman  Dutch Books (1960)    Entered for you By:  Bill Webster <thelma@pipeline.com>    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Fred Goslin" <fjgoslin@northnet.org> on  Sep 9, 1998
