Tomato-Herb Dressing
Garlic, Minced fresh parsley, Red wine vinegar, Black pepper, Cayenne pepper, Low-sodium tomato juice, Sugar, Dried oregano leaves, Chopped scalllions, 
Here are a few wonderful dressing recipes from Sarah Schlesingers cookbook  "500 Fat Free Recipes."    Place all ingredients in a jar witha tight-fitting lid and shake until  thoroughly mixed. Makes 6 servings of 3 Tbsp. each. 10 calories per  serving. 0 g fat.    Posted to fatfree digest V97 #013 by ReddHedd@aol.com on Mar 1, 1997.
