Tofu-Tahini Dressing
Salt, Lemon juice, Vegetable oil, Cl Garlic, crushed, 4 To 6 dr Red pepper sauce, Green onion (with top), chop, Water, Tahini (sesame seed paste), Ounces tofu (about 1/2 cup), 
Tahini is a popular food from the Middle East made from sesame seeds. Place  all ingredients in blender or food processor. Cover and blend on high  speed, or process, until mixture is smooth. ABOUT 1-1/3 CUPS DRESSING; 60  CALORIES PER TABLESPOON
