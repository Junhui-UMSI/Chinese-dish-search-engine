Peaches and Pork Stew
olive oil, Boneless pork loin, Black Pepper, Cinnamon, Sweet red pepper, Flour, Nutmeg, Allspice, Salt, Whole baby onions, Green pepper, Beef broth, Peach nectar, peaches, 
Heat oil in 5 qt Dutch oven over medium high heat. Sprinkle pork with salt  & pepper. Saute pork in batches for 4 minutes or until browned. Remove to  paper toweling to drain. Reduce heat to medium. Add onions, peppers and  more oil if needed; saute 8-10 minutes or until onions are slightly golden.  Whisk flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and allspice into broth until smooth. Add to  pot along with nectar and pork. Cover; simmer 8 minutes. Add peaches; cook  4 minutes or until pork and peaches are tender.
