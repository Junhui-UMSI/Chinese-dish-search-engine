Plantation Herb Muffins
Baking Powder, Grated Parmesan cheese, Flour, Oregano, Marjoram, Basil, Eggs, Salt, Vegetable oil, Cold cooked brown rice, milk, 
Preheat oven to 400F, prepare pans. Combine rice, oil, eggs, milk, cheese  & herbs. Sift dry ingredients & add to wet mix until just combined. Spoon  into pans & bake for 20-25 mins. Makes 12 very tasty muffins.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
