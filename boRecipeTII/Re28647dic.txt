Texas Crab Grass
Spinach, Onion, Butter, Parmesan, Crabmeat, 
Thaw spinach.  Squeeze water from spinach and set aside. Preheat oven to  350.  Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat. Chop onion. Add  spinach and onion and saute until onion is translucent, stirring  frequently, about 5 minutes.  Add crabmeat and Parmesan, stirring well to  blend.  Transfer spinach mixture to 1-qt casserole and bake 15-20 minutes.  Or transfer mixture to crockpot to keep warm, stirring occasionally. Can be  served hot or cold with crackers. Makes 1/2 cup per "serving"
