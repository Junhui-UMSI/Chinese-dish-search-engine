Wilted Lettuce
Radishes; sliced, Bacon; diced, Onion; sliced, Brown sugar, Leaf lettuce; torn, Vinegar, Pepper, Dry mustard, Salt; (up to 1/2), 
In a large salad bowl, toss lettuce, onion and radishes; set aside. In a  skillet, cook bacon until crisp; remove with a slotted spoon to drain on  pper towel. To the drippings, add vinegar, brown sugar, mustard, salt and  pepper; bring to a boil. Pour oover lettuce and toss; sprinkle with bacon.  Serve immediately. Yield: 4-6 servings. Submitted by Rosemary Falls,  Austin, Texas who says this is a favorite from her childhood.
