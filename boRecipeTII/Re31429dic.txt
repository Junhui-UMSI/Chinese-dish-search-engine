Barbecue Dip (Sauce)
Sugar, Apple cider vinegar, Salt and pepper, Dried red pepper flakes, Catsup, 
Combine all ingredients except the salt and pepper in a small saucepan.  Bring to a simmer. Remove from heat, season with salt and pepper. May be  served hot or cool. May be refrigerated up to 4 days.    NOTES : This is quick to make and is great with minced or sliced BBQ.    Recipe by: Craig Claiborne Cooks Mag.
