Poached Salmon with Yogurt Dill Sauce
Onion, Black peppercorns, fresh dill, Salt, Bay Leaves, Dill, Dijon mustard, Salmon steaks, YOGURT DILL SAUCE, Lemon juice, Dry white wine, Lemon, TABASCO, Water, TABASCO, Plain yogurt, 
In large skillet, combine water, wine, lemon, onion, bay leaves,  peppercorns, dill, TABASCO Sauce and salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to  low; simmer, covered, 15 minutes. Add salmon to skillet. Simmer, covered,  12 to 15 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with fork. Serve  warm or chilled with Yogurt Dill Sauce. Yogurt Dill Sauce: In small bowl,  stir together ingredients until well blended. Makes about 1 cup. Makes 4  servings.
