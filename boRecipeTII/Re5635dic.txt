Pineapple Sweet Bread Abm
Salt, Dried Pineapple; chopped, Sugar, Unsalted Butter; cut into, Bread flour, Sour cream, Yeast, Warm water, Eggs, 
Place all the ingredients except dried pineapple in the machine, program  for white bread and push start. Add the dried pineapple when the timer on  Hitachi reads 3:41. The finished bread is feathery light with a solid  texture.
