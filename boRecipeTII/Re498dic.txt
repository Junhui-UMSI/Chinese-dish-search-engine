Lemon Pudding Cake
Sugar, Salt, Whipped cream; optional, All-purpose flour, Lemon juice, Lemon Peel, Egg; separated, milk, 
In a mixing bowl, beat egg yolk. Add sugar, milk, flour, lemon juice, peel  and salt; beat until smooth. Beat egg white until stiff peaks form; gently  fold into lemon mixture. Pour into two ungreased 6-oz. custard cups (cups  will be very full). Place the cups in an 8-in. square baking pan. Pour  boiling water into pan to a depth of 1 in. Bake at 325 degrees for 40-45  minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean and top  is golden. Serve with whipped cream if desired. Yield: 2 servings.
