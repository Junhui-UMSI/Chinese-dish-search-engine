Tempting Turtle Pie
Chocolate ice cream, Butter, Vanilla ice cream, Caramel topping, Oreo cookies, crushed, Nuts, 
Mix crushed cookies with 1/4 stick butter for crust. (Cookies can be pulsed  in food processor or crushed by rolling pin.) Spread and press mixture into  pie pan. (Pie crust does not need baking.)    Fill crust with layers of vanilla ice cream, carmel topping, chocolate ice  cream, caramel topping and nuts.    After assembling, place in freezer at least 6 hours.    Slice and serve.
