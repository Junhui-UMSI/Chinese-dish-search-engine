From:    Julie Sterchi <sterchi@WABASH.NET>    Date:    Thu, 1 Aug 1996 08:47:18 -0700  In large skillet, brown meats.  Add garlic, 2 tblesp. parsley flakes,  basil, oregano, onion powder, and sauces. Simmer while lasagna noodles cook  according to package directions.  Combine cottage cheese, eggs, pepper, 2  tblesp. parsley flakes, and Parmesan cheese.  In 9 x 13 in.  pan, layer: 1)  noodles, 2) meat sauce, 3) cottage cheese mixture, 4) mozzarella cheese.  Use 1/3 each time to make 3 layers. Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes. Let  stand about 15 minutes before cutting and serving.
