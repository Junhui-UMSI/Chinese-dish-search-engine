Lemon Meringue Pie (Pie Filling)
WATER; BOILING, WATER; COLD, WATER; COOL, All Purpose Flour, SHORTENING; 3LB, Salt, 
PAN:  9-INCH PIE PAN                            TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN  :    1.  SEE RECIPE NO. IG001 AND I00100.    2.  COMBINE CUPS COOL WATER WITH LEMON PIE FILLING MIX, READY-TO-COOK.  ADD MIXTURE TO BOILING WATER; MIX WELL.  COOK AT MEDIUM HEAT STIRRING  CONSTANTLY UNTIL MIXTURE COMES TO A BOIL.  COOL SLIGHTLY.  POUR 2 3/4  TO 3 CUPS FILLING INTO EACH BAKED PIE SHELL.    3.  PREPARE MERINGUE (RECIPE NO. I00500).  SPREAD 2 1/2 CUPS COMPLETELY  OVER WARM FILLING (122 F.) IN EACH PAN.  MERINGUE SHOUDL TOUCH INNER EDGE  OF CRUST ALL AROUND AND COMPLETELY COVER TOP OF PIE.  LEAVE MERINGUE  SOMEWHAT ROUGH ON TOP.    4.  BAKE 15 TO 20 MINUTES OR UNTIL LIGHTLY BROWNED.    5.  REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.    6.  CUT 8 WEDGES PER PIE.
