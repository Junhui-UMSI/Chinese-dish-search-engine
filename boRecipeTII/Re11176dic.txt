Raisin Loaf
Shortening, Salt, Eggs beaten, Hot water, Sugar, Flour, Raisins, Baking soda, 
Put the raisins and soda in a bowl and pour the hot water over and let sit  till cool. Cream the sugar and shortening and add the beaten eggs. Add the  raisin mixture and then the flour & salt. Bake in loaf pan 1 hour in 350  oven.
