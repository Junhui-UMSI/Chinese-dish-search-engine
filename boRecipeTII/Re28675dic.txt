Texas Meat Skillet
Bell peppers -- chopped, Undrained, Crushed, Salt, Grated, Fat-free cheddar cheese --, Ground Meat Mix, Water, Crushed tomatoes, Long-grain rice -- cooked, Dark red kidney beans --, Low-fat tortilla chips --, Chili powder, 
In a skillet, combine Ground Meat Mix, and water. Cover and cook over low  heat for 15 minutes. Break up meat with a fork. Stir in crushed tomatoes,  kidney beans, rice, bell peppers, chili powder, and salt. Bring mixture to  boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring  occasionally.
