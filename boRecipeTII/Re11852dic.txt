Raymond Andersons Original Okra Supreme
Onion -- chopped, Tomato -- chopped, Cheddar cheese -- grated, Egg, Okra -- cut into 1/2", To 3 Ritz crackers --, Bacon drippings, Crushed, Rounds, 
Using med. heat, saute okra and onion in small amount of bacon drippings  until crisp-tender.  Add chopped tomato; stir, cooking, about 5 mins.  Sprinkle on crushed crackers. Blendlightly. Cook about 5 mins.    Reduce heat.  Break egg onto the mixture.  Blend lightly until egg begins  to congeal. Shape into a flat mound. Sprinkle grated cheese on top and slip  under broiler until cheese melts.    Source:  Emma Anderson (Mrs. Raymond F) of Christ Episcopal Church of New  Bern, NC    Recipe By     : Pass the Plate
