Strawberry Cheese Tart
Fruit jelly, Fresh strawberries, Ricotta cheese, Lowfat strawberry yogurt, Prebaked  pastry, 
Strawberry Cheese Tart    Mix together cheese and yogurt until smooth.  Do not overmix. Pour into  cooled baked crust.    Cut off stems from strawberries and place stem ends down into filling. Melt  jelly and 1/2 tsp water and brush over strawberries. Serves 8.    Per serving:  152 calories, 7 g fat, 7 mg cholesterol, 140 mg sodium.
