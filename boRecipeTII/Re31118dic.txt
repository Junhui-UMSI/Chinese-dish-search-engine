Banana Split Cake
Cool whip, Pudding, instant; french, Bananas, Cake mix; yellow, Milk, Cream cheese, Pineapple, Nuts, 
Bake cake according to package directions, cool.  Mix dry pudding mix and  cream cheese until well mixed. Add milk, a little at a time, until smooth.    Spread pudding over cake, cover with drained pineapple.  cut bananas  lengthwise and place over pineapple. Cover this with cool whip and top with  cherries and nuts.  ****Bake cake in a large pan and leave in the pan in order to keep all the  toppings on thecake.
