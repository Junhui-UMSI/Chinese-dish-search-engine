Tomato, Cucumber and Guacamole Sandwiches
whole wheat bread, tomato, fresh, Avocado, Fresh lime juice, jalapeno chili, red onion, cucumber, Garlic, 
These easy-to-make meatless sandwiches are nice for a picnic. Wrap them in  plastic, and transport them in a cooler.    Using fork, mash avocado, lime juice, garlic and jalape?o in small bowl  until almost smooth. Season guacamole with salt and pepper.    Divide guacamole among all bread slices, spreading evenly (guacamole will  be thinly spread). Place 5 tomato slices atop guacamole on each of 4 bread  slices. Top each with 4 cucumber slices, then 2 onion slices, then  cilantro. Top with remaining 4 bread slices, guacamole side down, pressing  lightly. Cut sandwiches in half. (Can be prepared 3 hours ahead. Wrap in  plastic; chill.)    Makes 4 servings.    Per Serving: calories, 220; total fat, 8g; saturated fat, 1g; cholesterol,  0mg.
