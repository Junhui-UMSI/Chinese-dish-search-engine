Apple Pickles
Sugar, vinegar, apples, cinnamon, Cloves, 
Combine sugar, vinegar, and spices.  Boil 5 minutes. Add fruit and cook  until tender.  Pack in sterilized jars. Fill to within 1/4 inch of top with  sirup. Other fruits such as peaches, pears, and crab-apples may be  substituted for apples. Mrs. G.E. Mayo, Sherburn, MN.
