Beef-Pot Roast Tex-Mex Style
Cumin seeds, Worcestershire Sauce, Lime or lemon juice, Can spicy tomato juice, To 2 jalapeno peppers;, Prepared mustard, Chili sauce, To 5 lb beef bottom roast;, Garlic, Fresh cilantro or parsley;, 
Put meat in shallow plastic dish just large enough to hold it. Combine  Worcestershire, lime or lemon juice, mustard, garlic. Spread mixture over  meat.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.    Roast meat in preheated 325 degree oven for 1 hour. Combine remaining  ingredients; pour over meat.  Cover and roast 3 to 4 hours more at 275  degrees until meat is very tender. Baste occasionally with pan juices (thin  with a little boiling water if necessary) Slice thinly to serve.    Makes about 6 to 8 servings.  Approximately 200 calories per 3-1/2 oz.  serving.
