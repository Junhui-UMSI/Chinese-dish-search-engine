Chocolate Pecan Chewies Cookie Mix in a Jar
Sugar, brown sugar, Cocoa powder*, Pecans, Flour, Baking soda, 
Yield: 1 jar    *(clean inside of jar with a paper towel after this layer)    Layer ingredients in order given in a 1 quart wide mouth canning jar. Press  each layer firmly in place before adding the next ingredient.    Directions given to recipient:    Empty jar into large mixing bowl, use your hands to thoroughly mix. Add 1  stick soft butter, 1 egg slightly beaten and 1 tsp vanilla (optional).Mix  until completely blended--you will need to finish mixing with your hands  Shape into balls the size of walnuts Place 2" apart on sprayed cookies  sheets Bake 375 for 11-13 min til edges are lightly browned -- cool 5 min  on baking sheet and remove cookies to racks to cool completely.
