Sweet and Sour Purple Cabbage
Water, Pepper, Onions, purple cabbage, Apples, Margarine, Vinegar, Dijon mustard, Salt, Brown sugar, 
Saute onion in cooking spray and margarine for 1 minute. Add cabbage and  apples and saute for 3 minutes.     Add the remaining ingredients and stir well. Cover. Cook over low heat  20-25 minutes.     Sprinkle with carraway seed if desired.     Yield: 6 side-dish servings
