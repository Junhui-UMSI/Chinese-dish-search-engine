Almost Like Alans Potato Salad
Mayonnaise, Sugar, Cider vinegar, Hard-cooked eggs, sliced *, Russet potatoes, Kosher salt to taste, 
* Hard-cooked sliced eggs are optional.  ======================================================= ==================     Cook potatoes in their skin until tender but still firm. Peel potatoes  while hot and cut into chunks; mix in vinegar. Add mayonnaise, sugar and  eggs and celery, if desired. Season with salt to taste.     Printed in the July 2, 1992 issue of the Los Angeles Daily News.
