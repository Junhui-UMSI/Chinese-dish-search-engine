Pecan Pie #03
Dark corn syrup, Sugar, Pecans, pie shell, Butter, Salt, Flour, Eggs, Light corn syrup, 
Thoroughly mix eggs, corn syrups, butter, sugar, salt & flour. Add pecans &  pour into pie shell. Bake at 350 for 1 hour or until filling is set. Use  sharp knife to cut pie.    MRS R.J.
