Penne Larrabiata
Cloves garlic; minced, up to, Penne pasta, Diced Roma; (plum) tomatoes, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Dried chile peppers; up to 6, Finely cut basil, Italian sausage; up to 10, 
1. Bring 1.5 gallons of salted water to a boil; add pasta, cook until al  dente (do not rinse!)    2. In a medium saucepan, saute the minced garlic in olive oil until golden    3. Add diced tomatoes, crushed red peppers, and crumbled sausage and saute  for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally    4. Add the finely shredded basil and serve on top of the penne pasta    5. Top with grated parmigiana reggiano (optional)    Notes: serves two; recommended wine is a southern Italian chianti  Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by "Bright W.
