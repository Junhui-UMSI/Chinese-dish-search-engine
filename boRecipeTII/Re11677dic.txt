From:    Randy and Colleen Jones <rcjones@METHOW.COM>    Date:    Tue, 9 Jul 1996 18:31:54 -0700  Place berries in a glass jar and sprinkle with sugar.  Stir one minute to  release a bit of juice.  Pour vinegar over berries, cap jar and let sit on  a dark shelf for 3-4 weeks. Pour mixture through a fine strainer to remove  berries and seeds.  Transfer strained vinegar to decorative bottles, label,  tie with ribbons.    EAT-L Digest  8 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
