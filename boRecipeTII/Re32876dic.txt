Sweet Potatoes with Apricots
Sweet potato, Orange rind, Margarine, Walnuts, Pitted apricots, Liquid from apricots, Brown sugar, 
Arrange potatoes and apricots in layers with brown sugar.    Melt the margarine in apricot liquid; add orange rind.  Pour over the  potatoes.    Bake at 375 uncovered for 45 minutes.    Sprinkle with nuts before serving.  Recipe By     : Janet Baker    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #302    Date: Thu, 14 Nov 96 22:49:59 -0500    From: Janet Baker <jbaker@cnmnet.com>
