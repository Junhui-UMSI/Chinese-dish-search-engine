Potato Stefado
Garlic, crushed, ... garnish, Cumin, ground, Crumbled Feta, fresh parsley, Shallots, quartered, Cinnamon, Vegetable stock, ... chopped walnuts for, Bay leaf, Oregano , dried, Red wine vinegar, Cauliflower flowerets, Tomato paste (6 oz), ... 1 1/2 inch chunks, Mushrooms, quartered, Parsley, chopped, salt and pepper, Round red potatoes, cut into, olive oil, 
Preheat oven to 350 Combine all ingredients in a flameproof casserole.  Bring to a boil, then remove from the heat. Cover tightly and bake for 1 to  1 1/4 hours. Garnish each serving with some Feta, Parsley, and Walnuts.
