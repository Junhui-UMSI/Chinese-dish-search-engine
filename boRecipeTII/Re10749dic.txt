Quick Tuna Casserole
noodles, cooked, milk, cream of chicken soup, tuna, American cheese, 
Place noodles in casserole. Mix tuna, milk, soup and cheese; pour over  noodles. Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes. Variations: 1.  Add 1 cup diced  celery, 1 cup diced onion, parsley, pimineto and 1 4-oz can mushrooms,  drained. Top with cracker crumbs instead of cheese.  (MRS W.E. Beard, Jr.,  Clarendon, AR)    2.  Add 1 14-1/2 oz can cut asparagus. (MRS Watt McKinney (Daisy), Marvell,  AR    3.  Add 1 cup chopped celery, 1/3 cup chopped onion, 1/4 cup chopped bell  pepper, 2 tablespoons chopped pimiento and 1/2 cup salad dressing.
