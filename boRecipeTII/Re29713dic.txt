Tea Time Tassies (Nut Cups)
Egg, almonds, Brown sugar, FILLING, Vanilla, Crust, Flour, cream cheese, Salt, butter, butter, 
Notes: By Stacy Wolfe, Lehighton Area HS. The Times News, PA    Mix ingredients for crust. Divide dough into 12 equal balls. Put 1 in each  divider of cupcake pan. Shape with fingers into little crusts.    Mix ingredients for filling. Place filling in the little crusts and bake  approximately 30 minutes at 350?. Makes 1 dozen cupcake size nut cups.
