Adrak Sharbat (Lime Ginger Ale)
Peeled ginger root - 1 oz, Peppercorns - 1 t, Water - 1 pt, More water - 1 pt, Sugar - 18 oz, Limes; juice of, Water or sparkling water - 1, 
makes 2 qt or 2 L    Yamuna Devi    Make a paste of the ginger. Add this paste and the peppercorns to 500 mL  water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, simmer 5 min. Strain. Set liquid aside  and put solids back in the pan with more water. Bring to a boil, reduce  heat, simmer 5 min. Strain. Discard solids. Combine both liquids together  with sugar and lime juice. Chill.    To serve, dilute with an equal quantity of still or sparkling water and  serve over ice.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Sep 28, 98,
