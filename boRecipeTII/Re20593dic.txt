Apple Cinnamon Pops (Motts)
Cold drink cups; (3-oz.), Wooden sticks, Carton; (8 ounces) plain, Apple Sauce; *see note, Honey, Cinnamon, 
In medium bowl, combine yogurt, apple sauce, honey and cinnamon; blend  well. Fill each cup with about 1/3 cup yogurt mixture.    Place cups in freezer until partially frozen, about 1 hour.    Insert sticks; freeze until firm, about 2 hours. To serve, remove drink  cups.    Notes: Pantry: Motts Natural or Cinnamon Apple Sauce. Either plastic  popsicle molds or ice cube trays can be used to make these refreshing  cinnamon-flavored yogurt pops.    >Hanneman/Buster 1998-Apr    Recipe by: Motts Applesauce www.motts.com    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by KitPATh <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu> on Apr 18,  1998
