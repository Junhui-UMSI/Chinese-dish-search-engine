Upside Down Coffee Cake
Pineapple slices -- well, Drained and halved, Packed, Reserve liquid, Refrigerated Biscuits, Brown sugar -- firmly, Maraschino cherries, Reserved pineapple juice, 
Heat oven to 375~. Arrange pineapple slices and cherries in ungreased 8"  round pan. In small saucepan, heat brown sugar and pineapple juice to  boiling, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat. Separate dough into 10  biscuits; place over pineapple slices. Pour brown sugar mix over biscuits  in pan. Bake at 375~ for 17-25 minutes or until golden brown. Cool 10  minutes in pan. Invert on serving platter. Serve warm or cool.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
