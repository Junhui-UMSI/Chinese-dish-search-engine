Tolees Pecan Bread
Eggs, Self-rising flour, Pecans, Sugar, Jar junior baby food prunes, Corn oil, Ground nutmeg, Ground cinnamon, Ground allspice, 
Mix sugar and oil. Add eggs and beat on high speed for three minutes. Sift  flour and spices and add to mix. Add prunes and mix well. Fold in pecans.  Pour into greased and floured tube pan or loaf pan. Bake at 325 degrees for  1 hour and 20-30 minutes or until toothpick pushed into center comes out  clean and bread begins to pull away from the sides of pan.
