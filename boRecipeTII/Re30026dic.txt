Balado Tahu Telor (Bean Curd & Eggs in Fried Pounded Chillie
Garlic, Salt and sugar to taste, Shallots, Big tomato, slice in 8, Boiled eggs, remove the skin, Oil for deep frying, Block tahu, slice in, Gr skined prawns, Fresh red peppers (you can, Belacan ( shrimp paste ), 
However, I did find the recipe below by following the links at:  http://www.euronet.nl/users/tampopo/foodpage/index.html I hope this is  close to what you wanted.    Method: Deep fry the eggs ( till the skin is change to golden brown colour)  and tahu. Pound/grind the peppers, shallots, garlic, belacan not too fine.  Fry the paste with hot oil in a wok. Add the shrimps and cook it for about  3 minutes. Add the fried boiled eggs and tahu. Finally add the tomatoes,  salt and sugar. Cook again for about 3 minutes.
