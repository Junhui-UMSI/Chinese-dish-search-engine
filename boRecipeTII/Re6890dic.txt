Pomme Frites/patatine Fritters
Russet potatoes; peeled in, Salt and pepper, Virgin olive oil, 
Cut potatoes into finger-sized slices of equal size and place in new cold  water. Heat oil to 385 F in a pot double the volume of oil. Add potatoes  one handful at a time and cook until golden brown. Remove and drain on  paper, season with salt and pepper and serve with mayonnaise (French) or  plain (Italian).
