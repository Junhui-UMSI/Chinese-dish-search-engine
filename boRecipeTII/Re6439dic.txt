Plum Sauce for Barbecue Ribs
17 oz can, Frozen lemonade concentrate, 6 oz can, Onion -- chopped, Worcestershire Sauce, Thawed, Soy sauce, Ground ginger, Chili sauce, Prepared mustard, Purple plums, 
Drain plums, reserving syrup; remove pits and discard. Put plums and syrup  in blender and blend until smooth. Add remaining ingredients and simmer  uncovered about 15 minutes. Brush sauce on ribs when they are nearly done.  Serve remaining sauce with ribs.
