Wild Basmati Rice with Orange-Soy
Butter flavored canola oil, Dry sherry, SAUCE, Roasted, Chopped pecans -- pan, Fresh orange juice, Soy sauce, low sodium, Water, Hot pepper sauce, White wine, Fresh Cilantro, Basmati rice, 
Use steamer to steam rice in the liquids listed.  Roast nuts in a dry pan,  add the oil, and the wine.  Stir in the cilantro and stir in cooked rice.  Stir to mix.  Serve with milder meat entrees and/or salads.
