Quick Sour French Bread
Vinegar, Baking Soda; or more, Salt, Sour cream, milk, Yeast, Bread flour, Sugar, 
Put into breadmachine according to your manufacturers directions. This  turns out GREAT in the breadmachine or baked in a conventional oven. To  bake in conventional oven, preheat to 400 degrees. Form loaves and let rise  a second time. Slash the loaves 5 or 6 times with a sharp knife and brush  with cold water. Bake in preheated 400 degree over for 15 minutes and then  lower the temperature to 350 degrees and bake for 5 to 10 minutes or until  golden brown and bread sounds hollow when tapped. This bread turns crispy  on the outside and moist with a sourdough texture on the inside. Med. for    1-1/2    lb. loaf.
