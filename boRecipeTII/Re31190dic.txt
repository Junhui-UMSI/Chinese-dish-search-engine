Banana-Apple Loaf
All-purpose flour, Brown sugar, Ground cinnamon, Apples, Vanilla extract, Granulated sugar, Margarine, bananas, Baking soda, Eggs, Black walnuts, Sour cream, Baking Powder, 
* Use stick margarine, not the soft kind, or use butter.       1. Cream together margarine and sugars, beat in eggs thoroughly. Blend  in sour cream, banana and vanilla.     2. Combine flour, baking powder, soda and  cinnamon. Gradually add to    butter mixture.     3. Gently fold in chopped apples and nuts. Pour into greased and floured  loaf pan.     4. Bake about 1 hour at 375 degrees. Cool before slicing.
