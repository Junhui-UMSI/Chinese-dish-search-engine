Pina Colada Fool
Sweetened shredded coconut, Drained unsweetened crushed, Whipping cream, Coconut liqueur or rum (opt), Mint sprigs (opt), 
In blender or food processor, puree half of the pineapple; add to remaining  pineapple.  In separate bowl, whip cream; fold in pineapple, coconut and  coconut liqueur (if using).    Divide among 6 long stemmed glasses.  Chill for 1 hour. Garnish with mint  (if using).  Makes 6 servings.
