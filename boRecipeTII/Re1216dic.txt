Crock-Pot Chili Con Carne
Ground beef, Shortening, Onions, Garlic, Chili powder, Beef bouillon, Paprika, Oregano, Ground cumin, Cayenne pepper, Beef stock, Tomatoes , Tomato paste , Red kidney beans , 
Heat shortening in skillet and brown beef, discard fat, Combine all  ingredients in removable liner, stirring well. Place liner in base.
