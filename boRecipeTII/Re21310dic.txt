Uncle Billys Bread Pudding
milk, Bread; pinched up, Eggs, Sugar, Vanilla, (large) cream, 
Stir real good. Let soak for 10 minutes. Melt 1/4 stick of butter or oleo,  then pour in mix. Put in oven for 35 minutes at 450 degrees. Let cool and  serve.    BX794@FREENET.CARLETON.CA    (JAMES A.
