Tuna Noodle Casserole
Frozen Peas, Paprika, milk, Dry Medium Noodles, Tuna; Drained And Flaked (6, Cream Of Celery Soup, Shredded Cheddare Cheese, 
1. Prepare noodles according to pakage directions. Add peas for last 5  minutes of cooking time. Drain 2. In 8-cup casserole, combine soup, milk,  tuna and half of cheese. Stir in noodles and peas. Top with remaining  cheese. Sprinkle with Paprika. 3. Bake at 400 F 20 to 25 minutes or until  bubbling and hot.    Makes 4 servings.
