Baked Fish (Psito Psari)
Lemon -- sliced, (RBCK34E), MARY SUE DAY, STUFFING-----, Juice of 2 lemons, -water, Whole grouper -- about 3 lbs, olive oil, Onions -- finely chopped, Salt and pepper to taste, Garlic -- finely chopped, Parsley -- chopped, Oregano., 
Clean fish & slit down center. Sprinkle inside w/lemon juice. Salt & pepper  and let stand 20 min. Drain & place in baking dish & stuff w/parsley &  oregano. Saute onions & garlic lightly 3 min. in olive oil. Pour over fish.  Arrange sliced lemon on top of fish, add water & bake in oven 375F. for 45  ~ 50 min. or until fish is tender. Baste occasionally w/pan juice.    Formatted by Elaine Radis BGMB90B; October, 1993 Posted April, 1993    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
