Tomatillo Salsa
Lg cloves garlic, unpeeled, Fresh Cilantro, Chili peppers, seed & dice, Fresh lime juice, Scallions, finely chopped, Salt, Fresh, prepared, 1/4" dice, 
1.  Preheat oven to 400F.  Loosely wrap garlic cloves in foil and roast      for about 25 minutes, until very soft.  Squeeze garlic pulp from the      skins into a small bowl and mash to a paste. 2. In a medium bowl,  combine all remaining ingredients.  Add garlic pulp      and mast to a paste. 3. In a medium bowl, combine all remaining  ingredients.  Add garlic pulp      and stir to blend.
