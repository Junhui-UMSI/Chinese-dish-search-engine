Taffy Apple Cookies
Flour, Egg yolks, Plus, Powdered sugar, Butter, Salt, Vanilla, 
Cream butter and add powedered sugar, yolks, vanilla and salt. Mix well.  Add flour, shape into balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet one inch  apart. Bake at 375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes. Cool and place toothpick  in center.    MELT: 1 bag Kraft caramels in 1/2 cup water.    Dip each cookie into melted caramel and roll in chopped nuts. Place on  waxed paper and let set for an hour. Do these when you have some extra  time. They take a while but they are cute and taste great.
