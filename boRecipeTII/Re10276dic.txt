Quick As an Elf Key Lime Pie
Whipped topping (garnish), (14oz) sweetened condensed, Grated lime peel, Lime juice, Coloring, milk, Graham cracker pie crust, Eggs, Few drops green food, 
In large bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form; set aside. In  small bowl, beat egg yolks. Mix in milk. Gradually add lime peel &  juice. Stir to blend well. Fold into egg white mixture; add food  coloring. Pour into crust & freeze 3 hours. Let stand 15 minutes at  room temperature before serving. Serve with whipped topping.
