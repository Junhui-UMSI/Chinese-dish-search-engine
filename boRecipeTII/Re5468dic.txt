Pineapple Curry Sauce
Pineapple juice, Ground coriander and cumin, Brown sugar, Each cardamom, turmeric,, Each ground cloves & nutmeg, Paprika, Fresh ginger, grated, Garlic, Pineapple, finely diced, 
Stir spices together in a small skillet over low heat for a minute or two.  Add garlic, ginger, sugar, and pineapple juice and cook over medium heat,  stirring frequently, for 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from heat and add diced  pineapple. Stir. Makes eight 2-Tablespoon servings    Lowfat Vitality Digest 9/96 Typo by Bobbie Beers  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #033 by BobbieB1@aol.com on Jan 31, 1997.
