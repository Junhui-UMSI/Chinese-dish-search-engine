Tequini (Tequila Martini)
Tequila, Bitters, Chili pepper, Dry vermouth, 
Fill a mixing glass with ice. Pour in tequila and dry vermouth. Stir (or  shake) and strain into a martini glass. Top with a dash of bitters. Garnish  with a small chile pepper, such as a chile piquin (if desired).    Recipe By     : Joe Robertson    From:                                 Date:
