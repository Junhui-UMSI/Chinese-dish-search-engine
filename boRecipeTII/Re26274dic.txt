Tomato and Cheese Molded Salad
Celery; finely chopped, Grated onion (if using plain, Cream cheese with chives, Envelopes Knox gelatin, Tomato soup, (small) cream cheese, or, Mayonnaise, Green pepper; finely chopped, Stuffed olives; sliced, 
Heat soup in top of double boiler, add cheese & stir until smooth. Add  gelatin, previously softened in 1/2 cup cold water. Fold in remaining  ingredients. Mold & chill.    MRS CYRUS Q.
