Pudding Rich Strawberry Ring Cake
Strawberry Frosting, GLAZE, Strawberry Cake Mix, Eggs, Vanilla Instant Pudding, Vegetable oil, Water, 
Heat oven to 350. Generously grease and flour 12 cup bundt pan. In large  bowl, combine cake ingredients at low speed until moistened. Beat 2 minutes  at high speed. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake at 350 for 45 to 55  minutes. Cool 30 minutes; invert onto serving plate. Cool completely. Heat  frosting in small pan just until melted. Spoon glaze over cake.
