Teriyaki Marinade
Light brown sugar, Sake, Garlic cloves, crushed, Rice vinegar, Ginger, grated, Soy sauce, Safflower oil, Dark sesame oil, 
Combine all the ingredients in a small container.  Let stand until the  sugar is dissolved.  When brushing onto the foods, swirl the mixture  often to keep it well combined.    Use to marinade cut & pressed tofu, marinade for 1 hour, or marinade  cubed tempeh for 1 hour or cubed seitan.  Aternatively, brush over  grilling tomatoes.
