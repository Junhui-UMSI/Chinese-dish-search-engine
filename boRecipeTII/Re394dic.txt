Lemon Chiffon Pie
Lemon Rind, pastry, Whipped cream, Envelope unflavored gelatin, Water, Eggs, Sugar, Lemon juice, Salt, 
Soften gelatin in cold water. To slightly beaten egg yolks, add 1/2 cup  sugar, lemon juice and salt. Cook in double boiler over hot, not boiling,  water, stirring constantly until a custard consistency is formed. Add  softened gelatin and stir until dissolved. Cool; add stiffly beaten egg  whites to which a 1/2 cup of sugar has been added. Fold into the custard  mixture. Turn into crust and chill until firm. Top with whipped cream.
