Three-Day Cole Slaw
Onion, Cooking oil, Vinegar, Green pepper; chopped, Sugar, Honey, (3-oz) pimiento; chopped, Salt, Head cabbage; chopped, 
Combine chopped cabbage, onion, pepper, and pimiento. Set aside. Combine  other ingredients and bring to a hard boil. Pour hot sauce over chopped  vegetables and mix well. Pack into containers and cover. Let sit in  refrigerator for 3 days without removing cover. Yield: 12 servings.    CHILL FOR 3 DAYS    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
