Tofu Au Gingembre
Sunflower oil, Firm Tofu, Tamari, Ginger root, grated, 
Cut the tofu in slices and place them side by side in a shallow oven-proof  dish. In a small bowl combine ginger, oil and tamari. Pour over the tofu.  Let marinate for 1 - 3 hours. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake for 25 - 30  minutes. Serves 4. From The Gazette, 91/01/16
