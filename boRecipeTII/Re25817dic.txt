Baby Kays Shrimp Roumalade
Shrimp, fresh, Garlic fresh, Salt to taste, Parsley, fresh, Horseradish, Lemon juice, Creole Mustard, Mayonnaise, Tabasco, 
*Creole Mustard such as Zatarains brand can be found  in well stocked supermarkets and gourmet food stores.  :       Mix all ingredients except shrimp together and  refrigerate. Serve with shrimp.
