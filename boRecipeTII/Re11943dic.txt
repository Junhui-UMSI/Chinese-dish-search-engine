Recess Peanut Butter Cups (Reeses)
Peanut butter, Peanut butter, 
Melt 1 cup peanut butter in measuring cup set in a pan of boiling water.  Cut cupcake papers down to about the size for the Reeses cups and place in  cupcake tin. Put about 1 T. of melted choc mixture, let set for 20 min then  place 1 T peanut butter on choc. let set for 10 minutes then place 1 more T  of choc on top and let set.
