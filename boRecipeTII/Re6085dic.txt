Pizza Dough
Yeast, water, Salt, olive oil, High gluten flour, 
mix together till makes a ball...  if feels to wet add flour.. place in  bowl with plastic ontop.  let rise 4-6 hours make pizza....cook 450 for  15-25 min.    Note:  I also add garlic, rosemary, herbs to dough at start... makes things  tasty...    Patti
