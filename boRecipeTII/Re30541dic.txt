Tacos De Jocoqui Al Horno (Baked Sour Cream Tacos)
Vegetable oil, Sauce:, Tortillas, Salt, Salsa Jalapena or your, Monterey Jack cheese, cubed, Oil for frying, Rubbed oregano, Solid pack tomatoes, Chopped, Onion, Sour Cream, salted, 
For sauce, wilt onion in hot oil.  Add tomatoes, oregano, salsa, and salt.  Cook for 15 or twenty minutes.  Set aside.  Fry tortillas lightly in oil,  put some of the sauce and a piece of cheese on each one and roll. Arrange  in a buttered shallow glass baking dish.  Pour remaining sauce over tacos,  top with cheese and spoon sour cream over all. Bake at 325 F. for 25 to 30  minutes.  Serves 6 to 12.
