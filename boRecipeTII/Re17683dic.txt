Almond Popseed Muffin
Baking Powder, Eggs, Salt, Almond extract, Vegetable oil, milk, Flour, Poppy seeds, Sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350 grease or paper line two 12 cup muffin tins - combine  flour-sugar-baking powder-salt in bowl; mean while whisk  oil-milk-eggs-extract in another bowl til smooth Add the dry ingredients  mixing til moist - Very gently stir in all the poppy seeds. Fill muffin  tins, and bake for appro 30 minutes. Serve warm or cold - Made these last  summer and they were very moist and very flavorful. Hope this is what you  are looking for. Barbara A.
