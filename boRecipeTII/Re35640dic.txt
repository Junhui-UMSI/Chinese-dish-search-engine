Stuffed Anaheim Chiles with Oregano Vinaigret
(cut one up for this), Garlic powder, Pkgs. garlic and herbs, Anaheim chile, minced, Imported romano, grate fresh, Coarsely ground black pepper, Dion mustard, Bread crumbs, seasoned, Seeded and pith removed., Oregano, crumbled, Pencil-eraser-sized pieces, Celery heart, minced, Black pepper, Shallots, minced, Anaheim chiles, halved,, Cheese spread, room temp, Anchovy paste, Anchovy paste, VINAIGRETTE, Dried cilantro, crumbled, Red wine vinegar, X-virgin olive oil, 
Preheat oven to 375F. Mix all ingredients well except those for  vinaigrette. Grease large baking sheet with Pam or olive oil. Stuff chile  halves with mixture, mounding slightly. Bake 30 minutes on baking sheet  till slightly browned. Vinaigrette: mix all ingredients together. Stir  before serving. Place 2 halves on each plate and drizzle with vinaigrette;  serve immediately. Serves 4.
