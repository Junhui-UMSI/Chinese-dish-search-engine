Windsor Canadian Goose
Sour cream, Apple; sliced, Salt, Currant jelly, SAUCE, Prunes, dried, Buttermilk, Butter; as needed, Flour, Bacon, Goose; 6-8 lbs., Butter, Beef or chicken broth, 
Soak goose in buttermilk at least 4 hours in refrigerator. Wash bird and  drain.  Stuff with prunes and apples. Truss and wrap in bacon. Cover  tightly with heavy duty foil. Bake until tender at 325 degrees F. for about  3 hours.  Baste with butter to brown. Sauce Melt butter in sauce pan. Stir  in flour with whisk. Add broth and heat stirring constantly. Just before  serving add sour cream and jelly.  Heat and serve. Note: Remove fruit from  goose before serving and arrange on platter around it.
