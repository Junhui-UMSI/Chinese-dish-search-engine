Barbecued Chicken Pizza
Shredded deli rotisserie, Prebaked pizza crust, Monterey jack cheese;, Italian plum tomatoes;, Barbecue sauce, 
Heat oven to 450F. In small bowl, combine chicken and barbecue sauce; mix  well. Place bread shell on ungreased cookie sheet; spread chicken mixture  over shell. Arrange tomatoes over chicken; sprinkle with cheese. Bake at  450 degrees for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and shell is browned.  Sprinkle with cilantro.
