Peppermint Cocoa
(4 1/2") peppermint sticks, Powdered nondairy creamer, Unsweetened cocoa, 
Place peppermint sticks in a airtight bag, beat to a powder with a mallet.  Combine peppermint with sugar, creamer and cocoa. Add 1/4 cup mix to 3/4  cup boiling water for each serving. Store remaining mix in a jar or  airtight bag.  Serves 8  Source: Quick & Easy Cooking ch.
