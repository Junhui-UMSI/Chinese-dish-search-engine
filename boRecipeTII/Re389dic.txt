Lemon Chicken
Margarine, Lemon juice, Salt and pepper, Lemon, Chicken breasts; split, Big Chief Brown Sugar, Poultry seasoning, Flour, 
These recipes are from http://www.monitorsugar.com Monitor Sugar Company  Recipes:    Place chicken in baking dish. Mix together Big Chief Brown Sugar, poultry  seasoning, flour, margarine, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Spread over top  of chicken, then slice lemon. Place 1 slice of lemon on top of each piece  of chicken. Bake 1 hour at 350 F. Baste occasionally.    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by Cairn Rodrigues <cairnann@yahoo.com> on May  21, 1998
