Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies #2
Peanut butter, Semisweet chocolate chips, Packed light brown sugar, Eggs, Baking soda, Granulated sugar, Pure vanilla extract, All-purpose flour, unsalted butter, 
From: japlady@nwu.edu (Rebecca Radnor)    Date: Thu, 15 Dec 1994 01:35:12 GMT    Yield: About 4 dozen cookies    1. Heat oven to 325 degrees. Have ungreased baking sheet(s) ready. Sift  together flour and baking soda.    2. Beat butter and both sugars in large bowl of electric mixer on high  speed until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes. Add eggs and vanilla; mix  well. Stop the mixer and add peanut butter. Mix on low speed just until  combined. Fold in the flour mixture and chocolate chips.    3. Using a scant tablespoon for each, drop cookies onto baking sheet,  spacing them 2 inches apart. Bake until lightly browned, 12 to 14 minutes.  Transfer to a wire rack to cool.
