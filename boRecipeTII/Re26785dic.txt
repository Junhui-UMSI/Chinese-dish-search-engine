Baked Apples - Crock Pot
Orange juice, Cinnamon, orange rind, Cooking apples, Lemon rind, Brown sugar, Rose wine, Whipped cream, 
Remove core from apples and place in crockpot. Mix together all other  ingredients except whipped cream. Pour over apples. Cover pot and cook on  low for about 3 1/2 hours or until apples are tender. Cool and serve with  whipped cream.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 408 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Dec 27, 1997
