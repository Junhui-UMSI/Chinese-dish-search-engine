Sweet Cinnamon Wontons
Cinnamon equal (1/3 cup, Warm water, Wontons, cut in half,, 
Combine cinnamon equal and water.  Brush wonton halves with cinnamon  mixture. Microwave on paper towel at high about 2 minutes or until puffed  and lightly browned. Makes 24 cinnamon wontons.    PER 4 WONTON SERVING: 37 calories, 1.4 g protein, 0.3 g fat, 7.3 g carbo    Origin: Equals 10 th Anniversary Recipe Book, Collectors Edition. Shared  by: Sharon Stevens.
