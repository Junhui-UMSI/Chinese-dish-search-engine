Pecan Delight
Baking Powder, Egg whites, Vanilla extract, Sugar; fine, Round buttery crackers; roll, Finely chopped pecans, Whipped cream, 
Grease 9" glass piepan.  Beat egg whites til stiff; fold in sugar, baking  powder, and vanilla extract.   Beat until stiff.  Fold in cracker crumbs & chopped pecans. Pour mix into  piepan. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 mins. Top with==>> whipped cream. Yield:  6-8 servings. Source: Southern Living - Our Best Recipes (1973 edition).  FROM:    SHARRON CLIVE   (TNPN54A)
