Kens Spicey Texas Brisket Rub
New Mexico Chili Powder, Garlic powder, Oregano, Rosemary, Cumin, Paprika, Sage, Cayenne pepper, Mustard, Black peppercorns, 
I know it says brisket and that is what I made this one up for, but I have  used it on brisket, port, venison, elk, buffalo, chicken, turkey etc.    I then smoke normally for bout 30-45 minutes per lb.
