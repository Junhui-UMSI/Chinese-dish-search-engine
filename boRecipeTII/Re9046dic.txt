Princess Jennifers Hot Fudge Dessert Cake
Honey, Topping-----, Brown sugar, Vanilla, milk, Unsweetened cocoa, Unsweetened cocoa powder, Baking Powder, Salt, Flour, Vegetable oil, 
Sift the first four ingredients together. Mix the next four ingredients  together. Combine the two and place in a 9 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan. Mix the  topping ingredients and sprinkle over the top. Pour 1 3/4 cups hot water  over  the top of the pan, do not stir. Bake at 3350 degrees for 40 minutes.  Remove  from oven and cool.
