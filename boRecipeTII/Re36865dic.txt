Strawberry Ring with Sour Cream Dressing
Water, boiling, Bananas, ripe, diced, Ginger, ground, Strawberries, frozen, pkgd., Pecans, chopped, Salt, Pineapple, crushed, Lemon juice, Sugar, SOUR CREAM DRESSING, Sour cream, dairy, Sour cream dressing, Strawberry gelatin, 
Dissolve gelatin into boiling water.  Add berries, stirring occasionally  until thawed.  Stir in pineapple, pecans, banana and lemon juice. Pour into  mold (6 1/2 cup).  Chill until firm.
