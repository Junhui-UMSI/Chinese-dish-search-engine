Sweet and Sour Chicken Crock Pot
Carrot, Dark brown sugar, Pineapple chunks , Red wine vinegar, Onion, Tapioca, Chicken bouillon, Dried cilantro, Chicken breasts, boned, Green pepper, Ginger-root, fresh, Soy sauce, Rice, Garlic powder, 
Date: Tue, 21 May 1996 19:43:45 -0500    From: pickell@cyberspc.mb.ca (S.Pickell)    For crockpot cooking -Put vegs.in bottom of crockpot. Sprinkle tapioca over  vegetables. Place chicken atop vegs. Combine all other ingreds. except rice  in a small bowl. Pour over chicken. Cover crockpot and turn to low and cook  for 8-10 hours. Before serving make rice. Serve over rice. Leftovers can be  reheated in microwave. To cook in oven Put vegetables in bottom of a  greased pan. Sprinkle vegs. with tapioca. Add chicken to pan. Combine all  other ingreds. except rice in a small bowl. Pour over chicken. Cover pan  tightly with foil. Bake in 300 deg. oven for 2 hours. Before serving make  rice. Serve over rice. Leftovers can be reheated in microwave. *You can  also use 2 T. chopped candied ginger.    Posted to MealMaster Recipes List, Digest #143
