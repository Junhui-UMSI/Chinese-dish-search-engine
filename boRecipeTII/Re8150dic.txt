Potato Crab Cakes
green onion, Butter or margarine, potatoes, All-purpose flour, crabmeat, Cheddar Cheese, Dry powdered mustard, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, Black pepper, Water, Cooking oil, 
1. Mix crabmeat, cheese, onions, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt and  pepper. Set aside.        2. In a pan, bring butter and water to a boil. Remove from heat; add  potatoes and flour and mix until it doesnt stick to the side of the pan.      3. Add to the cheese and crabmeat mixture. Roll and press into 3 inch  patties.      4. Over medium heat, heat oil in pan and cook patties until golden brown,  turning once or twice.
