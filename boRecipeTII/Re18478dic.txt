Vienna Coffee
Powdered milk or creamer, Instant coffee, Sugar, Cinnamon, 
Mix well in blender.  Put 2 rounded teaspoons in your favorite coffee cup  and fill with boiling water.  Relax and enjoy.   It should yield about 20  cups of coffee. Aunt Tea M.
