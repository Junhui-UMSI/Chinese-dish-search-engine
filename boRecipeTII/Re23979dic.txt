Asparagus Gratin (Brennan)
Bechamel sauce, Fine breadcrumbs, Thyme, Garlic, Salt, Butter, Grated Parmesan cheese, Pepper, olive oil, Steamed asparagus spears, Baguette slices, 
Line a gratin dish with a layer of toasted, garlic- rubbed baguette slices.  Drizzle with a little olive oil. Top with steamed asparagus spears seasoned  with salt, pepper and thyme. Pour bechamel sauce over all and top with dots  of butter, fine breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese. Bake at 400 degrees  for about 20 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly. Finish under a  broiler if desired.
