Zucchini and Peppers
Tomato paste, Green Pepper, Onions, olive oil, Zucchini; unpeeled, cut into, salt and pepper, Dried Oregano, Garlic powder, 
Heat oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add onions and  green pepper. Cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently.     Add zucchini. Cook 5 minutes. Add a small amount of water, if necessary,  to prevent drying. Reduce heat to low. Stir in remaining ingredients. Heat  through.
