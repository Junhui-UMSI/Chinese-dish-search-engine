Weight Watchers Crock Pot Spaghetti
Black pepper and oregano, Season salt, Onion, celery, Hamburger, Green pepper, Garlic salt, mushrooms, tomatoes, Bean sprouts, 
Brown hamburger with onion, celery and green pepper. Drain well. Add  tomatoes to crock-pot with other ingredients. Add mushrooms, garlic salt,  oregano and bean sprouts. Cook in crock-pot at least 10 hours. Can add  cheese to top of dish. Yields 8 to 10 servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 279 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 20, 1997
