Sweet Cajun Shrimp
Vinegar, Black pepper, Hot sauce, Cayenne pepper, Onion powder, Honey, Thyme, Shrimp, Catsup, Brown sugar, 
Combine all ingredients except shrimp in a sauce pan. Heat thoroughly. Cook  shrimp in boiling water for 5 minutes or until shrimp turns pink. Brush  sauce over shrimp. Serve over rice with leftover sauce. Serves 4.
