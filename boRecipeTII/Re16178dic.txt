Washington Apple Cake
Cinnamon, Salt, Walnuts, Eggs, Vanilla, Oil; (Wesson), Thinly sliced pared tart, All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Sugar, 
Beat eggs with a mixer until thick and light. Combine sugar and oil; pour  into eggs with mixer on medium speed. Stir together flour, cinnamon, soda  and salt; add to egg mixture with vanilla; beat to mix. Stir in walnuts.  Spread apples in a buttered 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan. Pour batter over apples,  spreading to cover. Bake in a 350 degrees oven for 1 hour. Remove from oven  and cool. Cream Cheese Icing: Mix two 3-ounce packages cream cheese, 1/4  cup melted butter, 2 cups powdered sugar and 1 teaspoon lemon juice. Spread  on cake.
