Turkey Terrific Tips (Part 2)
HOW TO DO A PERFECT TURKEY, 
TIMETABLE FOR ROASTING TURKEY:    Weight      Total Cooking Time     Internal Temperature WHOLE TURKEY,  STUFFED     6-8 lbs      2-1/2 to 3-1/2 hours         180~     8-12 lbs     3-1/2 to 4-1/2 hours         180~     12-16 lbs    4 to 5 hours                 180~     16-20 lbs    4-1/2 to 5-1/2 hours         180~     20-24 lbs    5 to 6-1/2 hours             180~ TURKEY BREAST     2-4 lbs      1-1/2 to 2 hours             165~     3-5 lbs      1-1/2 to 2-1/2 hours         165~     5-7 lbs      2 to 2-1/2 hours             165~ Based on oven temperature  of 325~. For whole turkeys that    are not stuffed, roast turkey about 1/2 hour less than times given above  for whole turkey stuffed. HANDLING LEFTOVERS: 1). As soon as possible after  serving, remove all stuffing from the turkey.   2). Refrigerate turkey meat, stuffing and gravy promptly after eating.   3). Serve leftover stuffing and gravy within 1-2 days. Reheat well before  serving.   4). Serve leftover turkey meat within 2-3 days. Frozen it can be kept up  to three weeks.
