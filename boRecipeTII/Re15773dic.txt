Abm Foolproof Rice Bread for the R2 D2
Sugar, Salt, Yeast, Bread flour, Rice; cooked, Vegetable oil, Water, 
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in order.  Set "baking control" at 10 oclock. Select "white bread" and push Start.  For a richer bread, use 1 egg and 3/4 c water instead of 1 c water. In hot,  humid weather, use 1/8 c less water. WARNING: the dough will look a little  "wet." DO NOT ADD EXTRA FLOUR.
