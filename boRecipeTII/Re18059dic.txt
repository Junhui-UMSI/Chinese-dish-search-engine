Veggie Casserole (Opt. Lacto)
Onion, diced, Grain of choice, To 3 cups frozen vegetables., Chick peas, drained. (same, Green pepper, chopped, (the mid-sized can), Cooked couscous or other, Size), Red kidney beans, drained., (I used corn), To 1 cup grated ff cheese, ..16_oz/_500_mL, I think), (optional), Garlic cloves to taste, Tomatos (larger sized, 
Combine all ingredients in large casserole dish, and bake in 400F oven for       45    mins.    You may add 1/2- 1 cup grated ff cheese the last ten minutes of cooking,  but I dont think it really needs it.    Serve over couscous (or any other grain you like)    It was rather liquid, next time, I think I will drain the tomatoes,  reserving liquid, and adding until it "looks right"    Source: improvised    Posted by Cathybear <clar6341@mach1.wlu.ca> to the Fatfree Digest [Volume       16    Issue 25] Mar. 29, 1995.
