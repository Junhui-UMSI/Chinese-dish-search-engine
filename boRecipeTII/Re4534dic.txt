Peter Pan Rice Casserole
Peanut butter, Sunflower oil, Sunflower seeds, Salt, Thyme, Brown rice; cooked, Shallots; chopped medium, Tarragon, 
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Lightly grease 4 x 8 baking dish with  sunflower oil. In a blender, combine peanut butter, oil, thyme, tarragon,  salt and 2 ounces water. Blend until mixture achieves sauce consistency.   Combine with rice, sunflower seeds, adn shallots. Mix well. Transfer to  baking dish and bake for 20 minutes in oven.
