Beef and Brown Rice Stew
Vegetable oil, Uncooked long grain brown, Can (28 oz.) crushed, Allspice, Beef bouillon cubes, Chili powder, Pkg. (10 oz frozen green, Onion; thinly sliced, Beef chuck, visible fat, Pepper, Water, 
Heat oven to 350.  Heat oil in a 4-quart Dutch oven over medium heat.  Add  onions and cook, stirring often, 7 minutes until translucent.  Add spices  and meat. Cook 5 to 6 minutes stirring 2 or 3 times. Stir in water,  tomatoes, rice and bouillon cubes. Bring to a boil. Cover and bake for 1 to  1 1/4 hours until rice and meat are tender. Stir in green beans. Cover.  Bake 10 more minutes until green beans are tender.
