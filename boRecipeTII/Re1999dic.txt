Chunky Pecan Chocolate Chip Cookies
All- purpose flour, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Salt, Heath bars, semisweet chocolate, pecans, Unsalted butter, Brown Sugar, sugar, Eggs, Vanilla, 
Beat butter at medium speed with an electric mixer until creamy. Gradually  add sugars, beating well. Add eggs and vanilla, beating well.    Combine flour and the next 3 ingredients, stirring well. Add to butter  mixture, beating at low speed just until blended. Stir in Heath bars,  chocolate chunks, and nuts.    Drop by heaping tablespoons 1 1/2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie  sheets. Bake 350 for 10 minutes, or until lightly browned. Let cool  slightly on baking sheets: remove to wire racks to cool complete;ly. Makes  about 3 1/2 dozen.
