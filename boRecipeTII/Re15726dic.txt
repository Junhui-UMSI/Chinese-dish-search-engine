Aam Lhassi
Rich milk; chilled, Clear honey, Garden rose petals, optional, Diced fresh mango, Orange juice; chilled, 
Combine mango, orange juice & sweetener in food processor. Blend for 1 1/2  minutes.    Pour milk into processor & process till it has expanded & become frothy.  Add the mango puree. Process for about 1 minute. Pour into chilled glasses.  Garnish with the rose petals if so desired. Serve immediately.    From: MARK SATTERLY
