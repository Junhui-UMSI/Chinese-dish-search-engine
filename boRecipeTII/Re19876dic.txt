Apple and Raisin Bread Pudding
Egg Beaters Real Egg, Nutmeg, Apples, Skim milk, Vanilla extract, Sugar, French bread, Raisins, Liquid Butter Buds, Cinnamon, 
In a 2 quart casserole that has been sprayed with a non-fat cooking spray,  combine bread cubes, apples, and raisins; set aside. Combine milk, Egg  Beaters, sugar, liquid Butter Buds, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg; pour  over bread mixture. Bake 350 degrees for 45 - 50 minutes or until knife  inserted in center comes out clean. Serve warm with Dream Whip or non-fat  frozen yogurt on top, if desired.
