Zippy Beef Casserole
(7 ounce) uncooked elbow, Ground beef, Condensed cream of mushroom, milk, Catsup, Salt, Potato chips; crushed, Cheddar cheese; shredded, 
(Marilyn Gierkes Recipe)    Heat oven to 350 degrees. Cook and stir meat until brown , drain. Cook  macaroni as directed on box, drain. In an ungreased 2 quart casserole dish,  mix all ingredients except chips. Cover and bake for 40 minutes. Uncover,  sprinkle with chips and bake 5 minutes longer.
