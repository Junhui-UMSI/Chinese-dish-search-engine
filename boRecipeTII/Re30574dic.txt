Tagliatelle with Caviar and Cream
Grated cheese, A couple of sage leaves or, Heavy cream, Jar caviar, Thin noodles, Pref. fresh, Butter, Cognac, 
Pour cream into saucepan, add caviar and simmer over low heat 10 min. DO  NOT BOIL!Add butter,sage and cognac and cook for 2 min on low. Serve over  hot cooked pasta with cheese on the side for topping. This is a great  starter for a fish dinner or used as a side dish.
