Pound Cake Cookie Cutouts
Frozen Sara Lee pound cake, Chopped nuts, chocolate, Chips, red hot candies,, Cookie decorations:, Frosting, Coconut, sugar crystals, 
Heat broiler. Slice cake into 4 horizontal layers. Place cookie  cutters very close together to cut the most cookies. Place on baking  sheet; toast in broiler until golden. Before serving, frost &  decorate.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
