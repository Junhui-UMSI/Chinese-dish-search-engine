Tomato Salsa
Salt, Haas avocado, pitted, peeled, Snipped chives, olive oil, Vine ripened tomatoes,, Jalapeno pepper, seeded and, White wine vinegar, 
Combine all of the ingredients and spoon over sliced Wiener Schnitzel.    Yield: about 1 1/2 cups.
