1989 Ics World Championship Bowl of Red
Chicken broth; 10 oz ea, Tomato paste, Water, Tabasco sauce, Garlic, Top round steak; cubed, Ground cumin, Gebhardt chili powder, Onions; grated, 
Saute beef in  skillet and put  in your favorite chili pot.   Simmer,  covered,  with  onion, garlic (as much as you wish), water and  broth  for  1  hour  and  30 minutes. Add tomato paste, chili powder, cumin  and Tabasco pepper sauce; stir and let cook on low for about another hour.  Add HOT water as needed. After three hours from when you started, serve  with another dash of Tabasco pepper sauce to taste.
