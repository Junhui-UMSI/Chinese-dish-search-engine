Artichoke Sunburst Cups
Box frozen puff pastry, Ricotta cheese, Green Onions, PASTRY, Egg, FILLING, Artichokes hearts (16, Chopped fresh parsley, Land O Lakes? Swiss Cheese;, Coarsely ground pepper, 
Heat oven to 400F. In large bowl stir together all filling ingredients; set  aside. Spray 12-cup muffin pan with no stick cooking spray. Cut each sheet  into 16 (2 1/2-inch) squares. Lightly press 1 square into each muffin cup;  spoon about 1 tablespoon filling over each square. Bake for 12 to 15  minutes until lightly browned. Cool 1 minute; remove from pan.
