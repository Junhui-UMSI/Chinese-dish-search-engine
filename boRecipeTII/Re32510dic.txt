Sweet Date and Coconut Cookies
Safflower oil -- for coating, Chopped almonds, Honey, Baking Powder, Pan, Egg, Grated fresh coconut, Egg white, Raisins, Unbleached white flour, Dates -- chopped & pitted, Whole-wheat pastry or, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly oil an 8-inch-square baking pan.    2. In a large bowl combine honey, egg, egg white, dates, coconut, almonds,  and raisins. In separate bowl sift together flour and baking powder.  Combine contents of two bowls, mix well, and pour into prepared pan. Bake       30    minutes.    3. Let cool, then slice.    Makes about 16 squares.
