Apple Crumb Pudding
Lemon juice, Sugar, Apples -- sliced thin, Margarine -- melted, Ginger, Cinnamon, Cubes, Sponge cake -- in small, 
Mix apples with lemon juice, sugar and spices.  Toss cake cubes with  margarine. Put 1/3 of cake in greased 8"x12" dish, top with hal f of  apples. Repeat.  Top with remaining cubes.  Cover and bake 30 min. at 375F.  Uncover and bake 30 Min. more.
