Swiss Steak for the Crock-Pot
Zesty tomato soup, Round steak, Worcestershire Sauce, Sliced onions, Stewed tomatoes, Seasoned flour, 
Pound flour into steak. Cut into serving size pieces (I cut into very small  pieces sometimes). Brown in skillet. Put in crock-pot and add remaining  ingredients. Cover. Cook 4 to 6 hours on high or 10 to 12 hours on low.  Serve with cooked rice or noodles. Serves 4 to 6.
