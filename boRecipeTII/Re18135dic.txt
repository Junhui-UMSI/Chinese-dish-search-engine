Velveeta Hamburger and Noodle Casserole
milk, Ground beef, Cream of mushroom soup, Salt & pepper, Noodles; dry, Onion, Velveeta cheese, 
Cook noodles al dente. Brown ground beef and onion. Drain grease. Mix  together with noodles. Add the rest of the ingredients. Cheese may be mixed  in or sprinkled on top. Bake 1 hour at 350~.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1054 by "M. Hicks" <nitro_ii@email.msn.com>  on Jan 29, 1998
