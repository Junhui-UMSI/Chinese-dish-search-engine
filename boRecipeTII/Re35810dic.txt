Stuffed Eggplants #1
olive oil, Eggplants, Oregano, Parmesan cheese, Fresh tomato, Basil, Garlic salt, Thyme, 
Halve eggplants. Scoop out pulp and dice. Fill shells with pulp. Sprinkle  with garlic salt, thyme, basil and oregano. Place tomato slice on top, then  small amount of oil. Place in pan in 1/2 inch of water. Bake at 350 for  1-1/2 hrs or until tender. Sprinkle with cheese and broil until brown.
