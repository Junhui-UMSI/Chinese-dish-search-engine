Tasty London Broil
whiskey, Jack Daniels, Soy sauce, Teriyaki sauce, White pepper, London Broil, Ground oregano, Fresh Lemon Juice, Fresh ground peppercorns, 
2-4 ea   cloves garlic minced    Mix the spices and the sauces in a small bowl. Take a meat fork & puncture  holes allover the top surface of the steak. Mix the Lemon juice, the  whiskey & 1 tspn of water into the sauce/spice mixture. Spread this mixture  all over the top surface of the steak. Be sure to rub it in well. Cover the  steak and refrigerate for 6-24 hours. Broil, either in the oven or on the  grill. Serve sliced into 1/4" slices. Origin: Kitchen of Dom Houston
