Bear Sausage
Pork fat, cubed, Dried savory, Dry Red Wine, Dried thyme leaves, Bear meat, trimmed of all, Salt, Black pepper, Celery seed, Feet medium hog casings, 
Prepare casings. Combine ingredients and grind through the coarse disk.  Grind through the fine disk, stuff casings, and tie off into 3" links. Age  in the refrigerator for two days. Cook as for fesh pork sausage.    Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8 Typed  by Carolyn Shaw 12-94.
