Baked Chicken Breasts with Horseradish Cream Sauce
White), Butter, Flour, Chicken Breast Halves --, Horseradish -- (prepared, With bone, Dijon mustard, Sour cream, Sugar, Salt and pepper, milk, White wine vinegar, 
Preheat oven to 350.    In a medium-size saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.  Stir in flour and  cook, stirring, for 1 minute without browning.  Whisk in milk, bring to a  boil, and cook, stirring, until sauce is thick and smooth, about 3 minutes.  Stir in horseradish, sour cream, sugar, mustard, and vinegar. Season with  salt and pepper to taste.    Arrange chicken breasts, skin side up, in a baking dish.  Spoon half the  horseradish sauce over chicken.  Bake 45 minutes until chicken is tender.  Broil chicken for 5 minutes to brown.  Reheat remaining sauce and serve  over chicken.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
