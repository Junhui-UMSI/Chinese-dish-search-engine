Wheaten Milk Biscuits
Butter-flavored shortening, Wheat germ, All-purpose flour, Egg, Powdered milk, Whole-wheat flour, Brown sugar, Water, 
Preheat oven to 325F.  Combine dry ingredients.  Cut in shortening until  crumbly.  Beat egg and combine with brown sugar. Add to flour mixture.  Gradually add water until a stiff dough is formed. Knead until smooth and  pliable.  Roll dough to 1/2 inch thickness and cut into the classic bone  shape.  Bake for 30 minutes. Store in refrigerator.
