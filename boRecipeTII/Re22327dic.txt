Turkey-Orange Salad
Seedless grapes;, Orange;, Mayonnaise/salad dressing;, Celery, Shredded coconut; toasted, Salt, Curry powder, Turkey; chopped, cooked, 
Combine the turkey, celery, salt, and curry powder in a bowl.  Peel  and chop the orange.  Add the orange, grapes, and mayonnaise to the  turkey. Toss gently to mix.  Sprinkle on the coconut just before  serving.    1/4 recipe = 217 calories, 3 lean meat + 1 fruit exchange 12 grams  carbohydrate, 21 grams protein, 10 grams fat 228 mg sodium, 391 mg  potassium, 58 mg cholesterol    Source:  Am.
