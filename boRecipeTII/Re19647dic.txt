Annies Apple Bread
Chopped apples, Salt, Lemon juice, Butter or margarine, Sugar, Baking Powder, Chopped black walnuts or, Eggs, Flour, 
Preheat oven to 350. Cream butter, sugar, eggs and lemon juice. Stir in  flour, baking powder and salt. Fold in apples and nuts. Bake in a greased  and floured 9x5x3" loaf pan for 45-55 minutes.
