Szechwan Spiced Beef Shred
Vegetable oil, Garlic, Chinese red wine vinegar, Water, MARINADE, Sugar, Cornstarch, Flank steak, Pale dry sherry, Green bell pepper, Salt, Ginger root, bamboo shoots, Scallions, MSG, Dry red chili peppers, Water chestnuts, Water, Cornstarch, Sesame oil, 
Thinly slice steak against grain, and cut into matchstick size strips.  Marinate for 2 to 4 hours. Heat 4 tsp. oil in wok to 350 F. Add beef  mixture and stir to separate pieces. Blanch briskly until beef just loses  its redness. Remove to a bowl. Heat 2 tsp. oil in wok. Add ginger, garlic,  scallions and chili peppers. Stir-fry about 10 seconds. 4. Stir in sherry,  vinegar and -if desired- MSG. Cook untill it bubles gently. Add water  chestnuts, bamboo shoots and green bell pepper. Return beef mixture to wok  and blend all together. Pour in water or chicken broth. When it begins to  boil, stir in cornstarch mixture and continue stirring until sauce  thickens. Add sesame oil, mix well and serve.
