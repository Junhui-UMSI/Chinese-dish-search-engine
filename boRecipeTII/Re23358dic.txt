Tuna, Potato, and Green Bean Salad Nicoise
Water-packed tuna --, Drained, Diced red potatoes -- skins, olive oil, Diagonally cut green beans, Red bell pepper -- sliced, Apple cider vinegar, Lettuce, Intact, Greek olives -- pitted, Herbal salt substitute, Ground pepper, 
1. In a large pot steam potatoes until tender. Place olives, olive oil,  cider vinegar, salt substitute, and pepper in a blender and puree. Pour  over potatoes and let marinate 40 minutes.    2. Wash and dry lettuce and arrange on a platter. Toss tuna, beans, and  bell pepper with potatoes and marinade, then spoon over lettuce. Serve at  room temperature.
