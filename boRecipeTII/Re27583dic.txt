The Best Bread Pudding Youll Ever Eat
Cream, Salt, PUDDING, Eggs, Sugar, SAUCE, Evaporated Milk, Vanilla extract, Vanilla extract, margarine, Flour, Sugar, milk, Bread, 
Break bread in large pieces, place in baking dish. Combine all ingredients  and pour over the bread. Mix and sprinkle over above: 1 teaspoon Cinnamon  and 1/4 cup sugar. Dot with butter and bake at 375? for one hour. This is  best served warm. (See Sauce recipe below)    Mix ingredients for Sauce and cook until thickened.
