Hot Cranberry Cider
Cranberry juice cocktail, (32 oz. bottle), Apple cider, Apricot nectar (12 oz), Cinnamon sticks, Nutmeg, grd., Rum, Butter or margarine, 
In sauce pan combine cranberry juice, cider, nectar, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Bring to boil, simmer 10 minutes.    Remove from heat, add rum and butter, stirring until butter melts.    Great for Halloween and Thanksgiving!    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Renate S." <redgrapes13@hotmail.com> on  Oct 08, 1998
