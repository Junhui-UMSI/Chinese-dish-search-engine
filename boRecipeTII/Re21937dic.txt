Applesauce Banana Bread
Ripe Bananas, Sugar, Applesauce, Vanilla extract, Eggs, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Salt, Flour, 
Preheat oven to 350 F. Place bananas in a large bowl and mash with electric  mixer. Stir in sugar and let stand for 15 minutes.      Add applesauce and eggs and beat well. Add remaining ingredients and mix  thoroughly. Pour into 9"x5" loaf pan coated with nonstick vegetable spray.  Bake for 45 minutes, or until a wooden toothpick inserted in the center of  the loaf comes out clean.      Remove from oven and let stand 10 minutes before removing from pan. Cool on  wire rack.
