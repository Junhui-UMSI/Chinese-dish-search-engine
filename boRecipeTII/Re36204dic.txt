Beef Stroganoff
Garlic, Stew meat; cubed, Mushrooms, Onions, Sour cream, Butter, Flour, Salt, Water, Pepper, 
Coat meat with flour and brown in butter. Add garlic, onion, salt and  pepper. Cook 3-4 minutes. Stir in water and add mushrooms. Cover and simmer  1-1 1/2 hours until meat is tender. Add sour cream and heat through but do  not boil.    Serving Ideas : Serve over hot, cooked noodles.
