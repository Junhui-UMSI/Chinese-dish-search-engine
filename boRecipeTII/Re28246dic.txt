Baked Flounder Teriyaki
Fish, Onions, Lemon juice, garlic, Ginger, olive oil, Apple juice, Honey, Black pepper, 
Preheat oven at 375.  Prepare a 13 x 9 x 2 pan lined with foil, with  cooking spray. Place fillets in pan.  Mix remaining ingredients; spoon over  fish.  Cover with foil and refrigerate 1 hour.  Bake covered for 15 to 20  minutes or until fillets flake easily with fork.
