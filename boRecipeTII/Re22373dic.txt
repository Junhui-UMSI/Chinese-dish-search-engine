Turkish Sea Bass
Garlic, Fresh grnd. black pepper, olive oil, Sea bass fillets, Crumbled dried oregano, Salt, Crumbled dried basil, Vegetable stock -OR-, Parsley, Pitted black olives, 
Combine garlic, olives, oregano, basil and parsley in small bowl.  Season to taste with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a large baking dish  at 425 degrees F. for 1 minute. Spread olive mixture evenly over  bottom of baking dish. Arrange sea bass, skin side up, on top. Pour  vegetable stock around fillets.  Bake, basting occasionally with  juices, until done. To serve, arrange fillets & olive mixture on  heated serving plates. Origin: Chef Hassan Emre, Topkapi Hotel,  Instanbul, Turkey    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/diabetic.zip
