Wild Rice Turkey Chili
Ground cumin, Vegetable oil, Monterey Jack -- shredded, Diced green chilies -- 4oz, Onion -- chopped, Chicken broth, Garlic -- minced, Can white corn, VGHC42A-----, Hot pepper sauce to taste, Turkey; cooked -- cubed, Can Great Northern beans --, Drained, Ea, Cooked wild rice, WALDINE VAN GEFFEN, 
Heat oil in large pan over medium heat; add onion and garlic. Cook until  tender. Add turkey, rice, beans corn, chilies, broth and cumin. Cover and  simmer over low heat to serving temperature.
