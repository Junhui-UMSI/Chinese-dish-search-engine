Asparagus Almandine
Butter, Salt, Lemon juice, Cooked, Slivered Almonds, Fresh Asparagus OR 2 bx froz, LISA CRAWLEY  TSPN00B-----, 
Drain cooked asparagus.  Melt butter in sm. skillet. Cook almonds over low  heat until golden brown, about 5-7 min.; stir constantly. Remove from heat.  Add salt and lemon juice; pour over hot asparagus. Yield: 6 servings.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
