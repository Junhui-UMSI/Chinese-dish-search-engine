Whipped Cream Frosting with Raspberry Sauce (Decadence Cak
Frozen raspberries in syrup, For Raspberry Sauce:, Vanilla, Cornstarch, For Whipped Cream Frosting:, Heavy whipping cream, Powdered sugar, 
DIRECTIONS: Whipped Cream Frosting: Whip cream with sugar and vanilla or  liqueur. Spread over top of one layer cake. Serve with raspberry or fudge  sauce.    Raspberry sauce: Thaw raspberries. Thicken by cooking with the cornstarch.  Chill and serve over (or along side) cake with whipped cream.
