Wills nana Bread
Bananas, Baking soda, Pecans, Sugar, Flour, Salt, Egg, Butter, 
Mash bananas with sugar (or mix in bowl of Kitchen-Aid mixer). Sift dry  ingredients together in a bowl. Mix egg with cooled melted butter. Add egg  mixture to banana and sugar mix. Stir in dry ingredients and nuts. Pour  into loaf pan. Bake at 325 for about 1 hour or longer. Test for doneness  with toothpick.
