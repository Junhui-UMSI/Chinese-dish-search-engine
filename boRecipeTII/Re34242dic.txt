Bbq Beef on a Bun
Chili powder, Honey, Hot sause, Worcestershire Sauce, Cider vinegar, roast beef, Ketchup, Water, Lemon juice, Plus, Brown sugar, Dijon-style mustard, 
(1) In skillet, combine all ingredients except for roast beef (2) Cook over  medium heat until heated through; approx 1 minute (3) Add roast beef and  stir to combine, until beef is heated through; 3-4 minutes. (4) Serve on  hamburger rolls. busted by sooz    Recipe by: WW Quick Success Program Cookbook (1990), Pg 245:    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 168 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Oct 26, 1997
