Bacon-Wrapped Meatballs
Cold water, Bacon; cut in half crosswis, Seasoned pepper, Salt, Ground beef, Minced onions, 
Combine first 5 ingredients, mixing well; shape into 8 meatballs. Roll  bacon pieces aroung meatballs, and secure with toothpicks. Saute over  medium heat until bacon is crisp and brown; drain off fat. If meatballs are  not done, cover and simmer an additional 5 to 7 minutes.    Sourse: Mrs. Marian Jenkins, Savannah, Georgia; Souther Living Magazine,  April 1979.
