Barbequed Beef Sandwiches
Crock Pot Roast, Pickles, Vegetable oil, Tomato ketchup, Hamburger buns, Onion, 
Saute onions in oil in a skillet until brown and translucent. Stir in beef  and leftover gravy. Add catsup and let simmer slowly for about 10 minutes.  Salt and pepper to taste. Spoon mixture onto hamburger buns and serve with  pickle slices.    Recipe by Mangia!
