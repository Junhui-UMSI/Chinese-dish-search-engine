Szechuan Beef and Snow Peas
Salt, Hot cooked rice, Pepper, Cornstarch, red pepper flakes, Soy Sauce, Tomato, Dry sherry, Water, Garlic, Boneless tender beef steak, Vegetable oil, Fresh snow peas, Onion, 
Slice beef across grain into thin strips.  Combine 1 tablespoon each  cornstarch and soy sauce with sherry and garlic in small bowl; stir in  beef. Let stand 15 minutes. Meanwhile, combine water, remaining 1  tablespoon cornstarch, 2 tablespoons soy sauce and red pepper; set aside.  Heat 1 tablespoon oil in hot wok or large skillet over high heat. Add beef  and stir- fry 1 minute; remove. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon oil in same  pan. Add snow peas and onion; lightly sprinkle with salt and stir-fry 3  minutes. Add beef, soy sauce mixture and tomato. Cook and stir until sauce  boils and thickens and tomato is heated through. Serve immediately with  rice. Makes 2-3 servings.    SOURCE:  Great American _Favorite Band Name_ Cookbook: Collectors Edition.
