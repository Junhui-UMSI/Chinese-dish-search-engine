Guava Bbq Sauce
Guava nectar; (11.5 oz), Onion, Guava jelly, Dry sherry, Molasses; (mild-flavored -, Red wine vinegar, Tomato paste, Garlic; chopped, Ground cumin, Dry mustard, 
Whisk all ingredients in a large saucepan. Boil mixture, whisking until  jelly dissolves. Reduce heat and simmer until sauce is reduced to 3 cups.  Cool.    NOTES : Sauce can be prepared 3 days ahead. Keep refrigerated.    Recipe by: Bon Appetit 9/98    Posted to bbq-digest by Bill Ackerman <ackerman@cordless.com> on Aug 24,  1998,
