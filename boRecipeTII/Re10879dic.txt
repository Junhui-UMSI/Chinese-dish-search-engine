Quinoa- the Basic Recipe
Water, Quinoa, 
Rinse quinoa thoroughly. Drain.  Place in small  (1 1/2 qt) saucepan .  Bring to a boil. Reduce to simmer, covered about 15 min. or until all water  is absorbed. Grains will turn from white to transparent and the spiral-like  germ will be separated.    Recipe By     : SASSYJO    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
