Sweet Lassi #3
Ice cubes, Plain yogurt, Lemon juice, Sugar, Cumin seeds; crushed, Ice cold water, Ice cubes, Lemon slices, Fresh mint sprig (opt), 
In a blender or food processor, blend yogurt, 4 ice cubes and ice cold  water       30    seconds.    Add lemon juice and sugar and blend mixture again until thoroughly  combined.    Pour mixture over ice cubes in glasses and sprinkle with crsuhed cumin  seeds. Garnish with lemon slices and mint sprig, if desired.
