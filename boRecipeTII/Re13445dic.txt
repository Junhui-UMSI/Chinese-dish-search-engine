Yellow Beans Berlin
Chicken broth, Prepared mustard, Yellow or wax beans, Green Onions, Salt, 
Combine all ingredients in pressure cooker. Close cover securely. Place  pressure regulator on vent pipe. Cook for 3 minutes. Cool pressure cooker  at once. Thicken sauce, if desried. Serves 6-8.
