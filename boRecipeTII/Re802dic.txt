Hearty Chicken Soup
tomatoes, canned, chicken, carrot, celery, mushrooms, Instant chicken bouillon, Bay leaf, Dried thyme, Water, noodles, 
Source: Better Homes and Gardens Crockery Cookbook    In crock pot combine undrained tomatoes, chicken, carrot, celery,  mushrooms, bouillon, bay leaf, and thyme. Stir in water. Cover and cook on  low-heat setting for 6 to 8 hours. Turn to high-heat setting; stir in  cooked noodles. Cover, heat through, about 10 minutes. Remove bay leaf.
