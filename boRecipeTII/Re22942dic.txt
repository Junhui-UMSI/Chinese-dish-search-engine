Arizona Skillet Dinner
Salt, Garlic, Chili powder, Corn; canned, Macaroni; elbow, Onion, Kidney beans; canned, Tomatoes; canned, Green bell pepper; chopped, Cumin, 
Saute onion, green pepper, garlic, chili powder, salt, and cumin until  vegetables are tender. Stir in tomatoes, breaking with spoon. Add kidney  beans and corn; bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes, stirring  occasionally. Toss with elbows.    Recipe by: Net    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Annice Grinberg  <VSANNICE@WEIZMANN.WEIZMANN.AC.IL> on May 12, 98
