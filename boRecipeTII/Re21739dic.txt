Apple Yam Crisp
Cinnamon, Sugar, Water, All-purpose flour, Apples, Salt, Lemon juice, Butter or margarine, Louisiana yams, 
Alternate layers of apples and yams in 8 x 8 x 2-inch baking pan. Sprinkle  each layer with cinnamon and salt. Pour water and lemon juice over yam  mixture. Sift flour and sugar together; cut in butter or margarine.  Sprinkle crumb mixture over other ingredients. Bake in moderate oven (350  degrees) 40 minutes, or until apples and yams are tender. Serve warm with  Lemon Hard Sauce.    To make Lemon Hard Sauce: Cream 1/3 cup butter or margarine until light and  fluffy. Gradually add 1 cup confectioners sugar; beat well after each  addition. Add 1 tablespoon light cream and 1/4 teaspoon lemon flavoring;  mix well.    NOTES : This booklet was prepared by the Louisiana Sweet Potato Commission.
