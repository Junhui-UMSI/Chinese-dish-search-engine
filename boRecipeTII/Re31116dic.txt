Banana Split Bread Abm Kooknet
Gluten, Active dry yeast, Egg, Pineapple; crushed, Instant Nonfat Dry Milk, Banana; sliced, Bread flour, Walnuts; broken, Baking soda, Pineapple Juice; heated, Vanilla, Chocolate chips, Frozen Strawberries; with, Sugar, Salt, Vegetable oil, Maraschino Cherries; chopped, 
Place ingredients, except chocolate chips and cherries, in pan in order  listed.  Select white bread setting and press "Start". Add chips and  cherries at beep, just before end of kneading.    Source: DAK Gourmet Gazette, Vol 1, Issue 2.    Posted on Kook-Net by Katherine Smith
