Cinnamon and Sugar Cookies
Sugar, Butter, Egg, All Purpose Flour, Cinnamon, Sugar, 
1. In large bowl, combine 1 cup sugar and butter, beat until light and  fluffy. Add egg; blend well. Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup, level  off. Stir in flour and 1 teaspoon of the cinnamon, mix until well blended.  Cover with plastic wrap; refrigerate 1 hour for easier handling.    2. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease cookie sheets. In small bowl, combine  2 tablespoons sugar and remaining 2 teaspoons cinnamon, mix well.    3. Shape dough into 1 1/2 inch balls, roll in sugar cinnamon mixture. Place  2 inches apart on greased cookie sheets. With bottom of glass, flatten each  ball of dough until about 1/4 inch thick.    4. Bake at 350 degrees F. for 11 to 15 minutes or until firm to the touch.  Immediately remove from cookie sheets.    Makes 18 cookies    Source: Pillsbury Festive Holiday Baking    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by PMD1011@aol.com on Oct 31, 1998, converted by  MM_Buster v2.0l.
