Beef and Mushrooms
Boneless beef, Carrots, mushrooms, tomato juice, Cooking oil, Onions, Salt, Prepared mushrooms, 
Source: Better Homes and Gardens Crockery Cookbook    In a large skillet brown beef quickly in hot oil. Place carrots, onion and  drained mushrooms in crock pot. Add browned meat; sprinkle with salt.  Combine tomato juice and mustard. pour over meat and vegetables in cooker.  Cover and cook on low heat for 8 to 10 hours. Stir the soup well before  serving. Ladle into soup bowls.
