Peanut Brittle #3
White corn syrup, Vanilla, Water, Sugar, Butter, Spanish peanuts, Baking soda, 
Cook sugar, corn syrup & water over low heat until mixture threads from  spoon when lifted from pan. Add butter & peanuts; cook until golden brown &  remove from heat. Add baking soda & vanilla which were first mixed  together. Stir swiftly & pour into buttered jelly roll pan. Dont stir or  smooth as it goes into the pan or bubbles will break and the peanut brittle  will be tough.    MRS CLYDE R.
