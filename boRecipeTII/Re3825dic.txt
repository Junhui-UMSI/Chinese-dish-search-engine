Penuche Frosting
Brown sugar, Vanilla, Nuts, Shortening, milk, Salt, 
Mix all ingredients, except vanilla and nuts in saucepan & slowly heat to a  boil, stirring constantly. Boil 1 min, a full rolling boil. Remove from  heat and beat until lukewarm Add vanilla and beat until thick enough to  spread. Spread on cake and sprinkle with nuts.
