Carrot Cookies
Whole-wheat flour, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Salt, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Quick-cooking oats, Raisins; (optional), Walnuts, Shortening, Grated Raw Carrots, Honey, Egg Beaters, All-purpose flour, 
In a large bowl, cream shortening. Stir in carrots, honey and egg  substitute. Gradually stir in remaining ingredients.    Drop from teaspoon on greased or sprayed baking sheets and flatten  slightly. Bake at 350? for approx 10 minutes or until lightly browned.    Makes about 60 cookies.
