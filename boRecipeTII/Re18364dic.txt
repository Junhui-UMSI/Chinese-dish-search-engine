Very Blueberry Cheesecake
Lemon Peel, Frozen Whipped Topping, Cream Cheese, Cold water, Lemon juice, Margarine, Blueberries, Marshmallow Creme, Vanilla Wafer Crumbs, Unflavored Gelatin, 
Combine crumbs and margarine, press onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.  Chill.    Soften gelatin in water, stir over low heat until dissolved.  Gradually  add gelatin to cream cheese, mixing at medium speed on electric mixer until  well blended.  Blend in juice and peel.  Beat in marshmallow creme; fold in  whipped topping.  Puree blueberries; fold into cream cheese mixture.  Chill  until firm.  Garnish with additional frozen whipped topping, thawed, and  lemon peel.    VARIATIONS:    Substitute Neufchatel cheese for cream cheese.  Substitute strawberry slices for blueberries  Substitute raspberries for blueberries    Recipes from Nestles Toll House cookbook and Kraft Philidelphia Cream  Cheese Cookbooks.
