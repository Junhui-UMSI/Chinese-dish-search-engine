Quick Stir-Fried Rice
Long-grain white rice, Reserved beef marinade, Red onion, diced, Salt, Sesame oil, 
Bring a large pot with 2-3 quarts of water to a boil. Add rice and boil,  uncovered about 10 minutes. Test a grain; rice should be cooked through,  but not soft. Drain into a colander in the sink. Run cold water through  rice and stir with a fork.    Add oil to wok and heat to smoking. Add onion ad stir-fry 1 minute. Add  rice and peas. Toss 3-4 minutes. Add marinade. Stir-fry another minute. Add  salt and pepper to taste. Serves 2.    Recipe by: American Statesman 2/5/97 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #518 by  Sherry Zeiss <zeiss@tab.com> on Mar 15, 1997
