Sweet Onion Saute
Red onion; cut into thin, Margarine, Salt, Scallions; cut diagonally, Balsamic vinegar, Sweet onions; 1/4" slices, 
In a large skillet over med. heat, cook first 2 ingredients in hot  margarine, stirring for 10 mins. Add scallions and salt. Reduce heat to  low; cover and cook 5 mins. or until tender. Remove from heat; stir in  vinegar. Good served with Balsamic Glazed Steak.
