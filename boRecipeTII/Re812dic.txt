Helen Peagrams Freezer Zucchini Bread Batter
Zucchini, Eggs, Vegetable oil, Salt, Brown sugar, Vanilla, Flour, Cinnamon, Nuts, TO BAKE, Baking soda, Baking Powder, 
Put all in a processor and blend well.  Line loaf pans (9x5) with plastic  and fill loaf pan 2/3 full.  Freeze.  Remove from pans and wrap in plastic.    To bake, remove plastic and thaw out.  Add 1/2 tsp soda and 1/2 tsp baking  powder per loaf.  Grease pan and pour in batter.  Bake at 350 approx 1  hour.  Delicious!
