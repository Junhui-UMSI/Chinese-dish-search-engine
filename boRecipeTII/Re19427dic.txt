Vegetarian Chili Con Carne
Garlic, Carrots, Green beans, stewed tomatoes , red and green peppers, Chili powder, Cumin, Onion, Tomato paste, Water, Kidney beans, 
Recipe by: Complete Taste Of Life Preparation Time: 0:15 Preheat oven at  350. Place onion, garlic, carrot, green beans, and peppers in a casserole  dish. Add water, chili powder, cumin, tomatoes, and tomato paste to  mixture. Bake for 20 to 30 minutes.    From: matejkrf@Picard.ml.wpafb.af.mil (Richard F. Matejka).
