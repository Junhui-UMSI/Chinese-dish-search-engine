Potato Latkes
Pepper, Salt, Baking Powder, Eggs, Vegetable oil, Sour cream, Applesauce, Potatoes; peeled and, Onion; finely grated, Flour, 
Soak potatoes in ice water. Squeeze dry. Combine potatoes and onion in a  large bowl. Mix in eggs, flour, baking powder and seasonings. Drop by 2 tb  into lightly oiled, hot skillet, spreading to flatten into discs with tines  of a fork. Cook until golden on both sides. Drain on paper towels. Serve  with applesauce and sour cream.
