Pine Nut and Orange Wild Rice
To 4 Tb Olive Oil, Ground pepper, Fresh Parsley -- chopped, Orange juice, Grated Orange Rind, Golden raisins, Long-Grain And Wild Rice --, Uncooked, Pine Nuts -- toasted, Water, 
Combine raisins and water in a small saucepan.  Bring to a boil; cover;  reduce heat, and simmer 3 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool. Drain and  set aside. Cook rice (with 2 cups water for 20 minutes). Combine rice and  remaining ingredients.    Recipe By     :
