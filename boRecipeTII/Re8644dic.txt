Potosian Tacos
Sour cream, Onion, Grated cheese, Lettuce leaves, Cilantro Epazote Or Mint, Salt, Potatoes, Sour cream, Parlsey, Soft tortillas, Green beans, Oregano, Pickled chili (in vinegar), Leaves of lettuce, Chicken breast, Tomatoes, Carrots, Vegetable oil, ANY OF THE FOLLOWING SPICES:, 
1. Cook the chicken in water with salt and pick any remaining hairs off.    2. Cut the beans, potatoes and carrots, boil or steam them, drain them, and  fry them lightly.    3. Toast the tomato and peel it. Liquify it with onion, salt and the  spice(s).    4. Dip the tortillas in the salsa and fry them in oil.    5. Put a little chicken on each tortilla and roll them up into tacos. Place  three tacos on each plate.    6. Top each of the tacos with the fried vegetables and sprinkle with  cheese. Add a tablespoon of sour cream and garnish with pickled chilis and  shredded lettuce.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 218 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 07, 1997
