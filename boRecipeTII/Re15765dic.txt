Abilene Texas Chili
Cayenne pepper, Cilantro, tomatoes, Lean ground beef, Cumin seed, Pinto, V-8 juice, Onions, Bay Leaves, Oregano, Brown sugar, Thyme, Garlic powder, Salt, Paprika, 
ABILENE TX ANNUAL CHILI SUPER BOWL    Brown beef and drain fat. Add all ingredients and cover. Cook for 4 hours  or so on medium. Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #132 by  info@yourbestimage.com (Best Image) on May 12, 1997
