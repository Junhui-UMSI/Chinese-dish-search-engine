Quick Beef Tortilla Soup
Hidden Valley Ranch, Onion, Corn, Ground chuck, Water, Pinto beans, Taco seasoning, Rotel tomatoes with chiles, 
Brown the meat and drain. Mix rest of ingredients. Simmer 45 minutes. Serve  topped with grated jack cheese and sour cream.    Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com (Judy E Ryder) on Mar 03,  1998
