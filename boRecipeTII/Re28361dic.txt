Baked Indian Pudding
Egg, milk, Cinnamon, milk, Corn Meal, Ginger, Sugar, Salt, Molasses, 
Scald milk. Mix together meal and molasses and stir into hot milk. Cook  until it thickens stir constantly. Remove from heat, add sugar, egg,  butter, salt, ginger, and cinnamon. Mix thoroughly. Pour into buttered  baking dish and bake 1/2 hour at 300 degrees. Pour over it one cup of milk  and continue baking for 2 hours. Serve with cream or ice cream.    SHARED BY:Jim Bodle 3/92
