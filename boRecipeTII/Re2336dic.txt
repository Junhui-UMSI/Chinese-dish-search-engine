Beans and Salsa on Potatoes
Pinto; kidney, or black, Salsa, Potato; baked, 
Microwave potato until done. Meanwhile put beans and salsa in a small  saucepan and heat. Serve over potato. You can top with some low or fatfree  sour cream if you want.    You can also serve this over rice, couscous, bulgar or polenta.    Recipe by: Low Fat Mailing List #74, 3/97    Posted to fatfree digest by Kathleen <schuller@ix.netcom.com> on Oct 18,  1998,
