Ranch Potato Skins
Sour cream, Baked Potatoes, (1oz) Hidden Valley, Cheddar cheese, 
Quarter 4 baked potatoes. Scoop out potatoes and combine with 1/4 cup sour  cream and 1 oz. package Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing Mix.    Fill skins with mixture and top with shredded Cheddar Cheese.    Bake 12-15 minutes at 375F.    Recipe by: Hidden Valley Website    Posted to recipelu-digest by Kelly <kelly@hci.net> on Feb 16, 1998
