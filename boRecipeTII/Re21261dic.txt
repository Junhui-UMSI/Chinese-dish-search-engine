Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookies
milk, Salt, Butter Flavor Crisco, pecans, Egg, Vanilla, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Semi-sweet chocolate chips, Brown Sugar, 
1. Heat oven to 375 degrees. 2. Combine Butter Flavor Crisco, brown sugar,  milk, and vanilla in large bowl. Bet at medium speed of electric mixer  until well blended.  Beat in egg. 3. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda.  Mix into creamed mixture at low speed until just blended. Stir in chocolate  chips and nuts. 4. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough 3 inches apart onto  ungreased baking sheet. 5. Bake at 375 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes for  shewy cookies (they will look light and moist-do not overbake), 11 to 13  minutes for crisp cookies. Cool 2 minutes on baking sheet. Remove to  cooling rack...if they get that far.
