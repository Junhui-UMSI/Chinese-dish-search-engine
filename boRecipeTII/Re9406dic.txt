Pumpernickel Stuffing
Water, Onions, chopped (1 1/2, Pepper, Drippings from ducklings, Slightly dry, Salt, Slightly dry white bread, 
1.  Saute onions in drippings just until soft in a large frying pan. Add  bread crumbs, water, salt, and pepper; toss lightly to mix. Spoon into  6-cup baking dish. 2. Bake with ducklings in a slow oven (325F) 1 hour, or  until crisp on top.
