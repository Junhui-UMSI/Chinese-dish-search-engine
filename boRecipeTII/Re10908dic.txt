Rabbit in Sour Cream Sauce
Garlic powder, Water, Containers sour cream, Pepper, Onion powder, Rabbit, Salt, Butter, 
Melt butter in skillet & quick brown rabbit. Sprinkle with onion &  garlic powders. Stew rabbit in water until tender. Drain & mix sour  cream & 1 cup water. Pour into skillet. Mix well & brown with pan  drippings. Return rabbit to skillet & stew until done. Coat well &  sprinkle with salt & pepper.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
