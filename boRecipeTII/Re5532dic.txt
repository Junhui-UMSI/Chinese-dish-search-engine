Pineapple Melon Delight
See below, 
In blender puree 1 medium sized cantaloupe, cubed, and one 16 oz can  crushed pineapple in heavy syrup, until smooth. Put ice cubes in tall  glasses. Pour fruit mixture in to fill 2/3. Fill to top with club  soda. Stir gently.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
