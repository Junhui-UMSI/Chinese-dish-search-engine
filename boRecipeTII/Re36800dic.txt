Strawberry Mold
Cold water, Crushed strawberries, Whipped cream, Diced marshmallows, Gelatin, Sugar, Thin slices angel cake, Vanilla, 
Soften gelatin in water.  Dissolve over boiling water. Cool. Combine  berries, sugar, and gelatin.  Fold in cream. Add vanilla. Pour into mold  lined with slices of angel cake. Cover with marshmallows. Chill until firm.  Unmold.  Garnish with whole strawberries. 8 servings.    Grace E. Kelly, Creston, IA.
