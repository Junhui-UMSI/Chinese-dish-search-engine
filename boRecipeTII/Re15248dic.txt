Whiskey Pound Cake
Granulated sugar, Whiskey, Almond extract, Pecans, Flour, Egg yolks, Egg whites; stiffly beaten, Butter, Vanilla, 
From: kdeck@epaus.island.net (Karen Deck)    Date: Mon, 9 Jan 1995 16:49:32 GMT  Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.  Add egg yolks one at a time,beating  well after each addition. Combine vanilla, almond extract and whiskey.  Add  alternately with flour to creamed mixture.  Beat egg whites until stiff and  fold into batter.  Put one half of the pecans on the bottom of a 10 inch  tube pan that has been greased and lined with wax paper. Add all of the  batter, and put the remaining nuts on the top of the batter. Bake at 350F  for 1-1/2 hours. This will keep for several days. Serves 12-14.
