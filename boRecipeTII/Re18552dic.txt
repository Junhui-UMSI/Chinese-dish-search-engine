Ambrosia Cookies
Vanilla extract, Chopped dates (or raisins), Lemon Rind, Eggs; well beaten, Grated orange rind, Quick-cooking or regular, Pecans, Brown Sugar, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, Salt, Butter or margarine, Baking soda, Flaked coconut, 
From: popielarz@ll.mit.edu (June Popielarz)    Date: Wed, 26 Jan 94 11:20:06 -0500    Cream butter in a large mixing bowl; gradually add sugar, beating well. Add  eggs, beating well; stir in vanilla.  Combine flour, baking powder, baking  soda, salt, coconut, oats, pecans, dates, lemon rind, and orange rind in a  medium mixing bowl; mix well.  Add to creamed mixture; blend well.  Drop  dough by teasoonfuls 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake at  375 F for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove to wire racks to cool. Store in airtight  containers. Yield: 4 dozen    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /COOKIES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
