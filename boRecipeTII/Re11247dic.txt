Raisin-Spice Coffee Cake
Baking Powder, Ground cinnamon, Ground nutmeg, Salt, Sugar, Raisins, Margarine or butter,, milk, Egg, Streusel, All-purpose flour*, Ground allspice, 
Heat oven to 350F. Grease square pan, 9 x 9 x 2 inches. Beat all  ingredients except raisins and Streusel on low speed 30 seconds. Beat on  medium speed, scraping bowl occasionally, 2 minutes. Stir in raisins.    Spread half of the batter in pan; sprinkle with half of the Streusel. Top  with remaining batter; sprinkle with remaining Streusel. Bake until wooden  pick inserted in center comes out clean, 40 to 45 minutes. 1 coffee cake (9  servings); 460 calories per serving.    Streusel Coffee Cake: Omit cinnamon, allspice, nutmeg and raisins in  batter.    *If using self rising flour, omit baking powder and salt.    Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
