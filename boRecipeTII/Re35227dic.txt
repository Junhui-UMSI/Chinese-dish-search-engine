Beef and Lotus Root Soup
Salt, Fresh lotus root, Soy sauce, Chinese red dates, Water, Short ribs of beef, 
1. Chop beef ribs in 11/2-inch sections. Peel lotus root; cut in  1/4-inch slices.       2. Place beef and lotus root in a saucepan with red dates and cold  water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat at once, skim surface to clear, then  simmer, covered, 45 minutes.       3. Stir in soy sauce and salt. Simmer another minute.       VARIATION: Add with beef, etc., any or all of the following: 1 leek  stalk, cut in sections; 2 slices fresh ginger root; 1 piece dried tangerine  peel (soaked).
