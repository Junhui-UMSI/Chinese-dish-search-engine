Caramel Dip for Apples
(14 oz) caramels, Half and half, Cream cheese, 
Unwrap carmels; place with half and half in double boiler or microwaveable  bowl. using a microwave at 50 percent power, stir often until melted. Using  a souble boiler, cook over simmering heat, stirring often until melted. Let  caramel cool. Using a whisk or electric mixer, beat cream cheese into  caramel mixture. Serve with apple slices or sugar cookies.    Recipe by: Cincinnati,Oh Enquirer 9/28/98    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Linda Shapiro <lss@coconet.com> on Sep 27,  1998,
