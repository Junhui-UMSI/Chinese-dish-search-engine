Strawberry Banana Cake
Biscuit mix, Butter, Banana, milk, Fresh strawberries, PHILLY.INQUIRER, Egg, Milk chocolate, 
Combine biscuit mix,milk,egg,melted butter and chocolate.Mix to combine  thoroughly.In a blender,combine strawberries and banana.Blend a few  seconds,until semi liquid.Add to cake mixture and mix thoroughly again.Pour  into a 9" square baking pan and bake at 350 deg.for about 30 minutes,until  a toothpick stuck in the cake center comes out clean.(It is an extremely  moist cake,so toothpick will always have some moisture on it.) Serve warm  or cold..
