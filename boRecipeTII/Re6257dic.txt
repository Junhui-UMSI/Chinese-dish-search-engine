Pi?a Martinique
Crushed strawberries, Crushed pineapple, Crushed ice, Coconut cream; such as Coco, White rum, Whole strawberries; for, Cointreau, 
Combine all the ingredients except the garnish in a blender, and process at  high speed until mixed, 1 minute. Pour into 2 tall glasses. Garnish with  whole strawberries.    Makes 2 drinks    NOTES : This rosy-hued variation on the basic pi?a colada is enhanced by  the subtle orange flavor of Cointreau and sweetness of crushed fresh  strawberries.
