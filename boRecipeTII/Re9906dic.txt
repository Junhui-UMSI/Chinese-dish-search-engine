Pungent Pumpkin Pie
Brown sugar, (16 oz) pumpkin, (12 oz) evaporated milk, Ground ginger, Ground nutmeg, Whipped cream, Eggs, Ground cinnamon, Prepared pie crust for one, Pecans, TABASCO? pepper sauce, 
Place pie crust in 9-inch pie plate. Make decorative edge.    Preheat oven to 400F. In large bowl, combine pumpkin, evaporated milk,  eggs, brown sugar, TABASCO? pepper sauce, cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger. With  electric mixer at medium speed, beat ingredients until well mixed. Pour  pumpkin mixture into prepared crust. Bake 40 to 45 minutes until knife  inserted 1-inch from edge comes out clean. Cool pie wire rack.    To serve, top pie with a large dollop of whipped cream; garnish with  chopped pecans.
