The Devils Chili
Tomatoes; chopped, OR, Olive or other salad oil, Bay leaf; crushed, Mild or hot curry, (15 oz) peeled tomatoes, Scallions; minced, Water, Shallot bulb; minced, Basil, Onions; grated, Bell pepper; chopped, salt and pepper, Paprika, Garlic, (15 oz) tomato sauce, Kidney, pink or pinto beans;, Miso paste, Chili powder to taste, 
In a large saucepan, saute onions, garlic, shallot, scallions, and green  pepper in oil. Add water, beans, and seasonings. Simmer for 1 1/2 hours.   Add tomatoes and tomato sauce. Simmer for 1 hour more or until  fork-tender. For spicier chili, use 2 or more cloves of garlic. Serve  piping hot.    Serves 6 From DEEANNEs recipe files
