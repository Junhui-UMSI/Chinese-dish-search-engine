Tostado Dip
Sliced black olives, Cheddar cheese, Taco seasoning, Mashed avocados, Chopped green chilies, Chopped tomatos, Onions, Montery jack cheese, Sour cream, (8 oz) refried beans, Alfalfa sprouts, 
There are 9 layers to this dip so use a large dip platter or shallow bowl  and spread in order given. Refried beans mixed with taco seasoning mix.  Sour cream mixed with mashed avocados. Chopped onion. Sliced black olives.  Chopped and drained green chilies. Grated montery jack cheese. Grated  cheddar cheese. Chopped tomatos. Alfalfa sprouts to cover top. Surround or  serve with round tostado chips.
