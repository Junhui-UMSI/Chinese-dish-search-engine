Alfredo Sauce (Lowfat)
Lowfat cottage cheese, Cornstarch, Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder, Parmesan cheese, Skimmed milk, 
Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor and blend until  smooth. Pour mixture into a small saucepan and cook over medium heat until  heated through and smooth, stirring occasionally. Serve with hot, cooked  fettuccini. Makes 4 serving.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Wayne T. Jones" <waynej@mail.austasia.net>  on Aug 30, 1998
