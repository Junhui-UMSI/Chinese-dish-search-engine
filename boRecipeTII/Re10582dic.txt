Quick Onion Gravy (Soup D
SOUP GRAVY BASE BEEF, SHORTENING; 3LB, All Purpose Flour, WATER; BOILING, 
1.  SPRINKLE FLOUR EVENLY OVER DRIPPINGS AND FAT IN BOTTOM OF PAN.  USE  BROWN  PARTICLES REMAINING IN PAN.    2.  COOK OVER LOW HEAT ON TOP OF RANGE OR IN 375 F OVEN 30 MINUTES UNTIL  FLOUR  IS A RICH BROWN COLOR.  STIR FREQUENTLY TO AVOID OVER BROWNING.    3.  UE BOILING WATER COMBINED WITH CANNED, DEHYDRATED ONIO SOUP; SIMMER 10  MINUTES.  ADD ROUX TO SOUP MIXTURE, STIRRING CONSTANTLY.
