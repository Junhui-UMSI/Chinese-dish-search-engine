Pecan Pie #07
Eggs; well beaten, Unbaked regular size (not, Sugar, Dark corn syrup (KARO) (not, Pecans, Butter, 
Combine sugar, corn syrup, and melted butter in medium size bowl.  Add  beaten eggs and pecans to sugar mixture.  Mix thoroughly.  Pour mixture  into pie shell. Bake 375 degrees 45-50 min. Cool on rack. Serves 8.    Courtesy of Dale & Gail Shipp, Columbia Md.
