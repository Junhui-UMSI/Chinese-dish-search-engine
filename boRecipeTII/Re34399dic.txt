Bean Burgers
Garlic powder, Onion -- chopped fine, Sage, Kidney beans, Ketchup, Thyme, Oregano, Salt and pepper - to taste, Brown rice -- cooked, 
Combine everything in a large bowl and mash well. Form into 6 patties and  cook in a non-stick pan until browned on both sides.    I consider a serving to be 3 patties. Each serving has 1019 kcal, 6g fat,  206g carbs, 24g fiber(!!!), and 37g protein.    Posted by "R. S. Koss" <rskoss@bronze.coil.com> to the Fatfree Digest  [Volume 15 Issue 22] Feb. 22, 1995.
