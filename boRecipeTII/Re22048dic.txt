Turkey Dinner-In-A-Dish
milk, Flour, Salt, Cooked; chopped turkey, Pepper, Diced pimiento, Butter, (4-oz) mushrooms; drained, (10oz) frozen mixed, (16-oz) frozen French fries, 
Combine butter, flour, salt and pepper. Stir over low heat until bubbly.  Add milk; continue to stir until mixture is smooth and thick. Add remaining  ingredients. Pour into 2-quart casserole. Bake at 400 for 20 minutes.
