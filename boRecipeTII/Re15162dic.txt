Wheat-Flour Applesauce Doughnuts
Eggs, Buttermilk, Baking soda, Mace, Flour; unsifted, Salt, Vanilla, Applesauce, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Shortening, Sugar, Wheat flour, 
Measure flour and wheat flour, baking powder, soda, salt and spices onto  wax paper. Stir well to blend. Cream shortening, sugar,eggs and vanilla  throughly. Stir in applesauce and buttermilk, mixing well. Add blended dry  ingredients, one cup at a time beating until smooth after each addition.  Refrigerate dough for 2 hours.    Preheat oil to 375 degrees. Turn dough out onto a well floured board with  additional flour for handling. Cut with floured 2 1/2-inch cutter. Remove  centers.  (jar tops make good cutters) Fry doughnuts a few at a time about  1 minute on each side. Drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with sugar. Makes 4  to 4 1/2 dozen.    Recipe by: Marion Hunter, Lincoln, Maine.
