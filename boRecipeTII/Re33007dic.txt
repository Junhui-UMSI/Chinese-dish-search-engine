Basic Cake Mix
Vegetable shortening, Salt, Sugar, Cake flour, Baking Powder, 
In a large bowl, sift together cake flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Mix well With a pastry blender, cut in shortening until the mixture is as  fine as cornmeal.  Put in a large airtight container.  Label as Basic Cake  Mix and store in a cool dry place.  Use with 10 to 12 weeks.    Makes about 16 cups of mix.    From "Make-A-Mix Cookery" by Karine Eliason, Nevada Harward & Madeline  Westover. Dedicated to memory of Freddi Michael from SYSOP of The Home BBS.
