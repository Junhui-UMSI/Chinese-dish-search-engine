Pumpkin-Walnut Bread
All-Purpose Flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, Canned pumpkin, Butter or margarine; (1/2, Walnuts, Eggs; well beaten, Salt, Milk; canned condensed (not, Baking soda, Ground nutmeg, Ground cinnamon, 
Sift flour, baking powder, soda, nutmeg, cinnamon into a bowl.    Beat eggs, milk, sugar, pumpkin and butter into large bowl until well  blended.  Stir in the dry ingredients until mixture is smooth. Fold in  nuts.  Spoon into a greased 9 x 5 X 3 inch loaf pan.    Bake in a moderate oven 350 for about 55 minutes or until a wooden tooth  pick inserted into the center of th eloaf comes out clean.  Cool 10  minutes.  Remove from pan,when completely cooled,wrap in plastic and  refrigerate.
