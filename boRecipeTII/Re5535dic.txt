Pineapple Microwave Bread Pudding
Crushed Pineapple, Whipped cream, Ice cream, Sugar, Eggs, Butter, milk, Bread cubes, Vanilla, 
In medium size mixing bowl beat together butter and sugar. Add beaten eggs,  milk, vanilla and drained pineapple. Fold in bread cubes. Pour mixture into  1.5 l (1 1/2 qt) casserole. Cook on Med (50%) 15-20 minutes or till firm.  Top with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream. SERVES: 4-6
