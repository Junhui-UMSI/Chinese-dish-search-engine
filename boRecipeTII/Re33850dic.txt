Sweet and Sour Red Cabbage #2
Salt, Sugar, Red wine vinegar, Cloves, Sized red cabbage (2-3, Water, Onion, 
From:    Pam and KerryAnn Cobb <priss@AMARANTH.COM>    Date:    Mon, 15 Jul 1996 15:51:57 -0500  Wash and trim cabbage.  Cut in 6 or 8 wedges, combine vinegar, sugar,salt  and water in stain- less steel or enameled kettle.  Bring to a boil. Insert  cloves in onion.  Add onion and cabbage wedges to pot.  cover.  Simmer  25-30 minutes or until cabbage is cooked to taste. Remove from liquid.  Discard onion. Drain cabbage well before serving. 6-8 servings.  priss@amaranth.com    EAT-L Digest 14 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
