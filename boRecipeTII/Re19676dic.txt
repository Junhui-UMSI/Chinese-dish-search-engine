Another Zucchini Dish
Cheddar cheese; shredded, Jar pimentos; 2 1/2 oz,, Butter or margarine, Eggs, Zucchini; cubed 1/2 ", French fried onion rings 3, 
Place zucchini and butter in a 2 quart casserole. Cover with plastic wrap.  Microwave at high (100%) until tender crisp, 4 to 5 minutes. Stir in egg,  pimentos and cheese. Blend well. Cover with plastic wrap. Microwave at  medium (50%) until eggs are set, 10 toll minutes. Sprinkle onion rings on  top. Microwave at medium (50%) until onion rings are heated, 2 to 2 1/2  minutes. Makes 6 servings.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 217 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 7, 1997
