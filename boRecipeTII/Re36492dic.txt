Straw and Hay Soup
Beef or chicken stock, Salt -- to taste, For garnish, Cut in 6-inch length, Fresh spinach fettuccine --, Fresh egg fettuccine -- cut, Grated Parmesan cheese --, In 6-inch length, 
1. Bring stock to a simmer in a large pot over medium-low heat. In a  separate pot, bring a large amount of salted water to a boil over  medium-high heat. Add both spinach and egg fettuccine to water and cook  until just wilted (20 to 25 seconds). Drain and run under cold water to  remove any starch. Transfer pasta to the simmering stock and simmer until  pasta is just tender. Season broth with salt.    2. Ladle soup into warm serving bowls and top each serving with grated  Parmesan.
