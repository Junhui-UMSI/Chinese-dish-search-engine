Baked Provencal Ziti Provolone
Black pepper, ziti, Skim milk, Dijon mustard, Egg whites, green onions, Evaporated skimmed milk, Parmesan cheese, Salt, Provolone cheese, 
Preheat oven to 325F. Spray 9" square baking pan with nonstick cooking  spray.    Cook pasta according to package directions; drain. Place in bottom of  prepared pan.    Meanwhite, combine evaporated milk, skim, milk, egg whites, mustard and  salt in food processor or blender; process until smooth. Sprinkle green  onions over pasta. Pour egg mixture over top. Sprinkle with pepper and top  with Provolone cheese. Bake 35 minutes or until heated through. Remove from  oven. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.  Garnish, if desired.    Recipe by: Favorite Brand Name Recipes--Casseroles & Country Favorites    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #990 by "creedenites"  <creedenites@sprintmail.com> on Jan 7, 1998
