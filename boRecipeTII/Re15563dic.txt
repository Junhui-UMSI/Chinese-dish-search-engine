7-Minute Boiled Crawfish
Ice cream salt, boxes, Cayenne, lge stirring spoons, Crawfish, live, Onions, medium, Oranges, halved, Liquid crab boil, Bay leaves, whole, Honey, Celery, ribs, Lemons or 1 tsp lemon oil, 
Fill a 60 qt pot 1/2 full. Add all ingredients except crawfish and bring to  a boil. Add crawfish. When it boils agains, time for 7 minutes. Remove from  fire, add one bucket cold water. Let it soak for one hour. Source:  Times-Picayune/States-Item 15 Mar 84 Recipe date: 03/15/84
