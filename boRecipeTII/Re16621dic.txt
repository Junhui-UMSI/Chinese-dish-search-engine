Aji Sauce #2
Aji chiles, Water, Onion, Vegetable oil, Chopped parsley, 
<Laurie Robertson> lroberts @ csc.com    This recipe comes from a recipe book distributed by the Embassy of Bolivia  at a course on South American cuisine sponsored by the Smithsonian:    Fry finely chopped onion in oil.  Add chopped parsley and ground aji.  Add  a little water and cook until onion is tender.    CHILE-HEADS ARCHIVES    From the Chile-Heads recipe list.
