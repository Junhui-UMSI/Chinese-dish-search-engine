Tangy Dressing
Dijon mustard, Orange juice, Red wine vinegar, Nonfat mayonnaise, Prepared horseradish, Honey, 
Combine all of the ingredients in a small bowl, and stir with a wire whisk  until blended. Yield: 1/2 cup (serving size: 1/4 cup).    Per serving: 54 Calories; less than one gram Fat (4% calories from fat); 0g  Protein; 14g Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 353mg Sodium    Recipe by: Cooking Light, Nov/Dec 1994, page 187    Posted to recipelu-digest by Terry Pogue <tpogue@idsonline.com> on Mar 1,  1998
