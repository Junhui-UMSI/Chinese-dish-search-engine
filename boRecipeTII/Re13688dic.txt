Wild Rice and Mushroom Soup
Parsley, chopped, Vegetable stock, Wild rice, washed & drained, Onion, finely chopped, Green bell pepper, diced, Button mushrooms, sliced, salt and pepper, Red wine, 
Put the stock into a soup pot.  Add the chopped onions, bell pepper &  parsley.  Bring to a boil, cover & simmer for 15 minutes.  Add the  washed wild rice & continue to simmer for another 40 minutes.    Add the mushrooms & the wine.  Season to taste.  Cover & simmer for a  further 15 minutes.  Serve hot.
