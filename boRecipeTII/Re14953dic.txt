3-Bean Bake
Brown sugar, Ground beef, Kidney beans; (1 to 2), Bacon, Ketchup, Onion, Cooking oil, (Medium) baked beans; (2 to, Butter beans, 
In large skillet, brown bacon until crisp; drain. Cool and crumble. Set  aside. Brown ground beef with medium onion; drain. Add together the ground  beef and bacon. Mix together baked beans, kidney beans, butter beans,  ketchup and brown sugar. Bake in casserole or Dutch oven at 375 degrees for  45 to 60 minutes. Let sit for 15 to 20 minutes before serving. Medium to  large casserole: Add 2 medium cans baked beans, 1 can kidney beans and 1  can butter beans. "Crowd size" casserole: Add extra can each type of beans,  then extra brown sugar and ketchup to taste.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 27,  1998
