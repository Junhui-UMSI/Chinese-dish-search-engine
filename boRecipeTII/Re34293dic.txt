Bbq Rum Ribs
Dry mustard, Spareribs, Brown sugar -- packed, Cloves garlic -- crushed, Soy sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Chili sauce, Ketchup, Dark rum, Pepper, 
Wrap ribs in double thickness of foil and bak for 1 1/2 hours at 350 .  Unwrap and drain drippings. Combine all ingredients and pour over ribs.  Marinate at room temperature for 1 hour. Bake at 350 for 30 min., basting  with sauce, or grill 30 min., turning and basting.    Recipe By     :
