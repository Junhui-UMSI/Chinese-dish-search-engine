Beer Bread #2
Honey, Baking Powder, Flour, Salt, Beer, 
From: Joel.Ehrlich@salata.com (Joel Ehrlich)    Date: 16 Nov 1994 16:28:56 -0500    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease a (9"x5"x3") loaf pan. Mix the  flour, salt and baking powder together in a large bowl. Add the beer and  honey. Stir together until well mixed. Spread the batter in the prepared  loaf pan(s). Bake until a wooden pick inserted in the center comes out  clean (about 45 minutes). Turn out on a cooling rack. Cool completely  before slicing.
