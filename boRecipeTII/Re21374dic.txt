Union Pie
Sugar; scant, Sour cream, Buttermilk, Baking soda, Nutmeg, Flour, Eggs, Molasses, 
Mix together all the ingredients, then pour the mixture into 4 unbaked pie  shells. Bake the pies at 400 oF for 10 minutes, then at 325 oF for 20 to 25  minutes or until a knife comes out clean.
