Raspberry Coulis-Martha Stewart Living
Sugar, Frozen raspberries, 
In a small saucepan over medium heat, bring raspberries and sugar to a  boil. Boil for 10 minutes, or until mixture thickens. Strain through a  sieve and add water, a tablespoon at a time, until coulis is the  consistency of a sauce. Refrigerate until ready to use.
