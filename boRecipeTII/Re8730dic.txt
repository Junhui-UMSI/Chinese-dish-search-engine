Pound Cake #2
Whipping cream, Sugar, Lemon juice, Flour, Vanilla, Sticks butter, Eggs, 
Cream butter & sugar. Add eggs, one at a time. Add vanilla & lemon juice  alternately. Add whipping cream (unwhipped) & flour, ending with flour.  Pour into greased & floured tube or bundt pan. Place in cold oven. Set oven  at 325 & bake 1 hour & 15 minutes. (Do not use angel food removable tube  pan.)    MRS. CHARLES WARFIELD    MANCY B.
