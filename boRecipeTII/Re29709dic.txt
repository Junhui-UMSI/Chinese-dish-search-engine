Tea Syrup
Bags, Sugar, Cold water, 
Place tea bags in boiling water; simmer 5 minutes. Remove tea bags and add  sugar. Store in airtight container in refrigerator. To make a glass of tea,  pour about 1-inch of syrup in glass, add ice and water. Enjoy!  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
