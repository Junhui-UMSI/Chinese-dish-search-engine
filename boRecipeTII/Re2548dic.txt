Peaches in Wine Sauce
Sugar, Water, White wine, Peaches, 
PEACHES IN WINE SAUCE    Scald and peel skins off peaches; leave fruit whole. Combine sugar and  water in skillet; cook 5 minutes. Add peaches; simmer 5 minutes. Add wine;  simmer 5 minutes. Baste 3 times while cooking. Transfer cooked peaches to  bowl; cover with syrup. Refrigerate; serve when chilled.
