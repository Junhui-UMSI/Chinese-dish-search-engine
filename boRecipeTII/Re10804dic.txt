Quickie Salmon-Potato Casserole
Milk,evaporated, Red salmon(16oz), Black pepper, Celery salt, Butter, Potato soup,condensed(10.5oz, Shredded wheat biscuits, 
1. Drain salmon; remove dark skin and bones.    2. Break salmon into 1-inch pieces.    3. Heat potato soup with milk, celery, salt and pepper; add salmon.    4. Pour mixture into a well-greased casserole.    5. Cover with shredded wheat; dot with butter.    6. Place under broiler a few minutes to brown.
