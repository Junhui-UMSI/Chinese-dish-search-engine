Pineapple n Ham Sandwich Roll-Ups
Corn or flour tortillas, Package thinly sliced ham, Lettuce leaves (6 to 12), Container cream cheese w/, Pineapple, softened, (about 18 slices), Cinnamon, Pumpkin pie spice or ground, Chopped walnuts or pecans, 
In small bowl, combine cream cheese, nuts and spice.  Mix well.  For  each roll-up, briefly cook tortilla on both sides in nonstick skillet  (about 1 minute).  Do not brown.  Cool slightly.    Spread about 1/6 of the cream cheese mixture over tortilla to with-in  1/2 inch of edge.  Lay 3 thin slices of ham, overlapping across  center of tortilla.  Top with 1 or 2 lettuce leaves.
