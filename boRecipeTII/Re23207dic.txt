Tuna Dip #1
Dried parsley flakes, (6-oz) cream cheese, Pepper, Cayenne pepper, (6-oz) tuna, Mayonnaise, Onion; sliced, (8-oz) mushrooms; chopped, 
Put all ingredients, except mushrooms and parsley, in blender and blend 10  minutes. Saute mushrooms in butter.  Add mushrooms and parsley flakes to  tuna. Refrigerate.
