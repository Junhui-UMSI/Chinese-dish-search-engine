Venison Sausage Stick
Peppercorns, Mustard seed, crushed red pepper, Quick salt, Regular salt, Liquid smoke flavoring, Pepper, Ground venison, Garlic salt, 
Mix all ingredients together and knead for 10 minutes.  Place in a mixing  bowl; cover and seal.  Refrigerate for 24 hours.    Remove and knead for another 10 minutes.  Seal and refrigerate another 24  hours.    Divide into five 1 pound portions.  Roll each into a log shape.  Place on a  broiler pan (for drainage) and bake at 150 F for 8 hours.  Cool, wrap in  foil, and store in refrigerator or freezer.  To serve, slice think for  crackers or sandwiches.
