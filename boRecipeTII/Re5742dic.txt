Pinewheels
Unsweetened chocolate;, Sugar, Butter, Salt, Egg, Baking Powder, Flour, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven to 400 degres, grease cookie sheet. In large bowl beat butter  until light and fluffy. Gradually add sugar, egg and vanilla while  continuing to beat. Sift flour into small bowl with salt and baking powder.  Add butter to mixture. Mix well. Divide dough in half and mix the melted  chocolate to 1 portion of the dough. Wrap both halves in plastic and  refrigerate for several hours. Roll out plain and chocolate dough  separately into oblong strips about 1/8 inch thick. Place dark dough on top  and roll jelly roll fashion, refrigerate overnight. Cut into 1/8 inch  slices and bake 8 minutes until golden brown. Yields 3 dozen.
