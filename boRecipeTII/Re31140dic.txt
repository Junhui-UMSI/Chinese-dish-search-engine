Banana Split Pie
Crust, Graham cracker crumbs, Pineapple preserves, Butter, Banana thinly sliced, Chocolate sauce, Strawberries; sliced, Whipped cream, Macadamia nuts, Sugar, Vanilla ice cream; softened, Macadamia graham cracker, MACADAMIA GRAHAM CRACKER, 
Prepare crust as described below.  Combine softened ice cream and pineapple  preserves; mix well. Spoon into macadamia graham cracker crust. Freeze. Cut  into wedges and serve with bowls of chocolate sauce, sliced strawberries,  sliced bananas and whipped cream.    Macadamia Graham Cracker Crust: Finely chop the macadamia nuts. Combine  macadamia nuts, graham cracker crumbs, butter and sugar; blend well. Press  into 9-inch pie plate. Chill. Makes one 9-inch pie crust.
