Russian tomato sauce, Onion onion lg., Red wine vinegar, Carrots, peeled, olive oil, Sugar, Beets, fresh & peeled, 
Grate the bbets, carrpts and onions together. Heat the olive oil in a large  skillet. Add the sgredded vegetables and saute over medium heat for 10  minutes. Stir in the tomato sauce, vinegar, & sugar. Continue to cook over  medium heat5 srirring until the moisture evaporates, 20 minutes approx. be  sure to continue to stir so it wont stick and burn. Remove from heat,  allow to cool to room temp then refrigerate for 3-6 hours.
