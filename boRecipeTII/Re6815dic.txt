Pollo Raphael
Salt and pepper as desired, Zucchini, cut into 1/2", Oregano, crushed, Vegetable oil, 8 oz can tomato sauce, Whole chicken breasts,, Picante sauce, Ground cumin, Garlic salt, Onions, cut into 1/2" wedges, 
Source: MAINPOUL.ZIP    Combine tomato sauce, picante sauce, cumin, garlic  salt and oregano; mix well. Cut chicken into 1"  pieces; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cook chicken  and onions in oil in large skillet over medium heat,  stirring frequently, until chicken is lightly browned  and almost cooked through, about 5 to 7 minutes. Stir  tomato sauce mixture into skillet; mix well. Stir in  zucchini. Cover and simmer 5 minutes. Uncover; cook  and stir about 1 minute to thicken sauce, if  necessary. Serve with additional picante sauce. Serves  4.
