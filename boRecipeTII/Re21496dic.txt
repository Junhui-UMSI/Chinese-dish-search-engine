Apple Pie #3
Nutmeg, Margarine, 2 crust pie shell, Sugar, Cinnamon, Flour, Lemon juice, Sliced apples, 
Thaw pastry according to directions. Mix sugar, flour,and spices. Combine  sugar mixture with apples and lemon juice. Fill pastry shell with apple  mixture. Dot with butter. Place top pastry over top Seal and flute. Slash  top for steam to escape. Bake on lower oven rack at 450 for 10 minutes.  Lower to 350 and bake for 40-50 minutes more. Until crust is golden brown.
