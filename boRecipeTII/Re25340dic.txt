Tortellini and Fruit Salad
milk, Halved green grapes, Blueberries, Orange Peel, Pkg, Cheese-filled tortellini, Plain yogurt, Salad, Cubed cantaloupe, Sugar, Dressing, Slivered almonds, 
In a small bowl, combine all dressing ingredients; Blend until smooth. Cook  tortillini to desired doneness as descrived on package. Drain; rinse with  cold water until cool. Drain well. In large bowl, combine cooled tortellini  and remaining salad ingredients; toss gently to combine. Pour dressing over  salad;    stir gently to combine.
