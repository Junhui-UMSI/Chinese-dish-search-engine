Banana Pie
Pineapple juice, Sugar, Butter or margarine, Sliced bananas, Cinnamon, Flour, Pastry for 2 crust pie, 
Soak bananas in pineapple juice for 20 minutes. Preheat oven to 400  degrees F. Line a 9 inch pie plate with pastry. Drain bananas, saving  3 tablespoons of the juice.  Place bananas in pie shell. Combine  sugar, flour and cinnamon; sprinkle over bananas.  Sprinkle with the  3 tablespoons of pineapple junice. Dot with butter, cover with top  crust. Bake for 30 to 45 minutes or until crust is browned.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
