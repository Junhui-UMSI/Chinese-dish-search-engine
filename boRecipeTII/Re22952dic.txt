Arkansas Goulash
Cheese, Onion, Salt, Green pepper, Tomato soup, Ground beef, (8-ounce) spaghetti, Pepper, 
Brown ground beef. Chop onion and green pepper. Mix onion and pepper with  beef. Cook till onion and pepper are soft. Cook and drain spaghetti.  Combine with beef mixture. Add tomato soup and one can water or milk. Cube  cheese. Add salt and pepper; mix well. Bake at 350 40 minutes.  Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Monica Boggs <wiggles@SHELL.PLANETC.COM> on Sep  16, 1997
