Randy Pollaks Persimmon Bread
Sugar, Salt, milk, Eggs, Cinnamon, Flour, Baking soda, butter, Baking Powder, Nutmeg, Persimmon pulp, Pecans, 
Sift the first 6 ingredients together. Mix the persimmon, sugar, eggs and  milk. Add the dry ingredients and butter. Mix until well blended. Stir in  the nuts. Spread into a well-greased loaf pan, 9 x 5 x 3 inch. Bake at 350F  for 45 minutes or until done. This bread freezes well.    Posted by Randy Pollak, Kook-Net Recipe Network
