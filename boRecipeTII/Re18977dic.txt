Angel Surprise Dessert
(2 oz.) dessert topping mix, (3-3/4 Oz.) Instant Vanilla, (3 oz.) cream cheese;, (13-14 Oz.) Crushed, Angel cake cubes, milk, 
In a small mixer bowl, gradually blend milk into cream cheese, beating  until smooth. Add pudding mix and beat at low speed of electric mixer until  smooth and slightly thickened, about 2 minutes. Pour pudding mixture over  angel cake cubes, stirring to coat. Turn into a 8x8x2 inch baking dish.  Spread drained pineapple over top. Prepare dessert topping mix according to  package directions. Spread over all. Chill several hours or overnight.  Serves 6.    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Feb 22, 1998
