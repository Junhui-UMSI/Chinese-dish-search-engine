Sweet Bbq Chicken
Vegetable cooking spray, Ground red pepper, Dried whole thyme, Salt, Chicken leg quarters, (12), Pepper, Frozen apple juice, Barbecue sauce, Chili powder, Brown Sugar, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in a saucepan; stir well. Cook over medium heat  for 5 minutes or until thoroughly heated; set aside.    Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.    Coat a grill rack with cooking spray; place on grill over medium-hot coals.  Place chicken on rack; cook 10 minutes on each side. Brush chicken with  barbecue sauce mixture, and cook an additional 15 minutes or until chicken  is done, turning and basting frequently. Yield: 12 servings (serving size:        1    chicken leg quarter).
