Banana Butter Cream Frosting
Salt, BUTTER PRINT SURE, LEMON FRESH, SUGAR; POWDER 2 LB, BANANAS FRESH, 
1.  CREAM BUTTER OR MARGARINE IN MIXER BOWL AT MEDIUM SPEED 1 TO 3 MINUTES  OR UNITL LIGHT AND FLUFFY.    2.  SIFT TOGETHER POWDERED SUGAR AND SALT; ADD TO CREAMED BUTTER OR  MARGARINE.    3.  PEEL AND MASH BANANAS; ADD MASHED BANANAS AND LEMON JUICE.    4.  SPREAD IMMEDIATELY ON COOLED CAKES.  :    NOTE:  1. FOR SIX 9-INCH (2 LAYER) CAKES:  SPREAD ABOUT 1 3/4 CUPS (1 LB)  FROSTING ON EACH CAKE.    NOTE:  2.  FOR 13 DOZEN CUPCAKES:  SPREAD ABOUT 1 TBSP FROSTING ON EACH  CUPCAKE.
