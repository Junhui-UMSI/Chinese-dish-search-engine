Apple Raisin Drop
Nutmeg, Water, Flour, Baking soda, Cloves, Apple; unpeeled (finely, Sugar, Vanilla, Eggs, Seedless raisins, Shortening, Salt, Cinnamon, Baking Powder, Quick cooked rolled oats, 
Heat raisins and water to boil, simmer 5 minutes. Cool water and raisins.  Cream sugar, shortening and vanilla. Add eggs and beat well. Sift together  flour, baking powder, soda, salt and spices. Add this mixture with raisin  water and sugar mixture. Add oats, raisins and apple. Chill several hours.  Drop by teaspoon on cookie sheet. Bake at      350    degrees.
