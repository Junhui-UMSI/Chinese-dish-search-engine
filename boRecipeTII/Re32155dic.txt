Barbecued Pork
Lean pork roast (fat trimmed, Salad oil, Cayenne, Soy sauce, Ground cloves, Ground cinnamon, 
Heres a recipe from The Diabetic Gourmet your husband might like.    Cut pork into bite-size pieces. Combine ingredients in plastic bag and  marinate 1 to 2 hours. Remove meat from bag and discard marinade. Put meat  on small rack or skewers and bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour. Serves 4.    Total recipe contains: 6 grams carbohydrate, 87 grams protein, and 74 grams  fat.    One serving (3 ounces) contains: 21 grams protein, 15 grams fat, and 225  calories.
