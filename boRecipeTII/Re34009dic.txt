Basil Tomato Salad
Cooked rice, cooled, Tomatoes, chopped, Cider vinegar, Basil, chopped -=OR=-, Salt, Red onion, chopped, Cucumber, seeded & chopped, Dried basil, Pepper, olive oil, 
In a small bowl, whisk together oil, vinegar, basil, salt and pepper.  Add rice, tomatoes, cucumber and onion; toss.  Serve chilled.    "Country Woman Magazine"  March/April, 1993.  Posted by Jim Bodle    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/vegan4.zip
