Vegetable Pizza
Cauliflower, Carrots, Cheddar cheese, Tomatoes, fresh, Mayonnaise, Hidden valley ranch salad dressing, Broccoli, Crescent rolls, 
Pat crescent rolls into pizza pan.  Bake 7-10 minutes, or until golden  brown.    Mix softened cream cheese with mayonnaise and dried salad  dressing.  Spread cream cheese mixture on cooked dough and top with  vegetables and cheese.  Press down lightly, and chill for at least 1 hour.  Cut into pieces. FROM:    NANCY CHRIST (KRXW62B)
