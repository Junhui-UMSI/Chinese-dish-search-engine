Watercress and Leek Soup
salt and pepper, Margarine, Cornstarch, sliced, Vegetable stock, Watercress, Leeks, 
Fry leeks & watercress in margarine in a large pot over a low heat for 15  minutes.  Keep a lid on the pot, but stir occasionally, dont let it brown.  Add stock, bring to a boil & simmer for 10 minutes. Dissolve cornstarch in  a little cold water & add to the soup. Blend till smooth, reheat & serve.
