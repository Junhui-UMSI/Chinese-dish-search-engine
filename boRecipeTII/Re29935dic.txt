Teriyaki Meat Loaf
Chili powder, Onions, Celery, Bread, Ground beef, Teriyaki sauce, Eggs, 
Mix. Press into a 9 x 5-inch loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees for one hour.  Baste top of loaf with 2 tablespoons more Baste and Glaze Teriyaki sauce.  Bake 15 minutes longer. Makes 6 servings.
