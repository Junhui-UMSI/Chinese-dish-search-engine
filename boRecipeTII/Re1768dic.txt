Chicken and Peppers
Green pepper, olive oil, Boneless chicken, tomatoes, crushed, Parmesan cheese, Onion, Garlic, Salt and pepper, Fettuccine, parsley, fresh, 
Saute chicken in a 6 quart casserole with oil until borown; add minced  garlic, parsley, salt and pepper and saute for 3 minutes. Add crushed  tomatoes and bring to a boil. Cover pot and simmer on low heat for 45  minutes. Add onions and peppers. Saute lightly for 15 minutes. Add more  salt and pepper to taste. Serve over fettuccine with parmesan.
