Quick Beef Minestrone
Vegetables, Water, Condenced tomato soup, Shredded cabbage, Dried basil, Uncooked elbow macaroni, Grated Parmesan cheese, Frozen Italian mixed, Ground beef, 
In a soup pot, cook beef until well browned. Drain off fat. Stir in frozen  vegetables, undiluted soup, water and basil. Heat to boiling, stirring  occasionally. Simmer 15 minutes. Stir in cabbage and elbow macaroni. Cook  until tender. Sprinkle with grated cheese. SERVES 6.
