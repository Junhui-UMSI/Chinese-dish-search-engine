Zucchini/courgettes with Peanuts
Garlic cloves; up to 2, Lemon juice, Zucchini;courgettes, sliced, Margarine, Peanuts; toasted and, 
To begin, simmer the sliced zucchini/courgettes in very little boiling  water for 5-10 minutes until they are very tender. Drain well and put them  in a bowl. Now add the garlic, lemon juice, margarine, salt and pepper and  mash all the ingredients with a fork. Add more lemon juice if required.  Spoon the mash into a bowl and scatter roasted peanuts on top before  serving with millet or rice.
