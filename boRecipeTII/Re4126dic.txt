Peppery Blue Cheese Sauce
milk, Pepper, Salt, Flour, blue cheese, Butter or margarine, 
Melt butter/margarine over med. heat; stir in lour till well blended,  Gradually add milk and cook, stirring constantly, till mixture is  thickened. Remove from heat, stir in cheese and remaining ingredients.  Makes 1 cup to serve over grilled steak or beef patties.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 668 by on
