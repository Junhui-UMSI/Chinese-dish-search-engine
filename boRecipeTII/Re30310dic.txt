Banana Chip Bars
Margarine, Flour, Salt, Brown sugar, Bananas; mashed, Baking Powder, Sugar, Egg, Chocolate chips, Vanilla, 
Cream margarine and sugars; add egg and vanilla.  Stir in bananas and flour  mixture.  Add chips.  Place mixture in greased 9x3" pan. Bake 25 minutes at  350-degrees.  Posted to EAT-L Digest 27 Jul 96    Date:    Sun, 28 Jul 1996 12:08:06 -0500    From:    LD Goss <ldgoss@METRONET.COM>
