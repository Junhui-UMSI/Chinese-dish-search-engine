Albanian Lamb Stew with Okra
Okra; fresh or frozen, Green peppers; diced, Water, Cloves garlic; pressed, Thyme, Roasted lamb; in bite size, Paprika, salt and pepper, Celery; diced, Onion; diced, Lemon Rind, 
Cook okra until liquid evaporated in skillet or Microwave dish, stirring  occasionally for 5 minutes. Add green pepper, celery, onion, garlic, salt  and pepper to taste. Cook for 10 minutes. Stir to prevent vegetables from  sticking. Add water, lamb and thyme and simmer, covered, over Low heat for  20 minutes until heated through. Sprinkle with paprika and garnish with  lemon rind.
