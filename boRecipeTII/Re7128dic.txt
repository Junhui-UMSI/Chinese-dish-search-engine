Poppyseed and Nut Filling for Hamantaschen
Poppy seeds, Butter, Raisins, Pecans, Corn syrup, milk, Chopped citron, Vanilla, 
Put washed and drained poppy seeds through finest blade of food chopper and  mix with rest of ingredients EXCEPT vanilla. Let mixture cook over slow  heat until thick, stirring often. Cool and add vanilla.
