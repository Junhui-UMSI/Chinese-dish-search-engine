Wieners in Butter and Beer Sauce
Wieners, all-meat, Buns, wiener, Butter, Beer, Onion, 
Melt butter in saucepan; add beer and onion.  Cover and cook over low heat  30 minutes.  Transfer saucepan to grill. Heat wieners on grill. While they  grill, use tongs to dip them into beer sauce four or five times at 5-minute  intervals.  Serve wieners in buns; spoon cooked onion and sauce over  wieners.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the 1970s.
