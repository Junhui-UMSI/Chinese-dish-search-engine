Uncle Joes Spaghetti Sauce
Generous splash sherry or, (6-oz) tomato paste, Oregano, Salt to taste, (12-oz) tomatoes, Onion; coarsely chopped, Black Pepper, Thyme, Cooking oil, Ground beef, Cloves garlic; cbopped, Coarsely chopped green, Basil, (10-oz) beef bouillon, Hot Italian link sausage;, Mushrooms; chopped, 
In a deep 10 inch skillet or Dutch oven, heat oil and brown meat. Add green  pepper, onion and garlic and cook, stirring until tender. Add all remaining  ingredients. Reduce heat and cook slowly for 1 hour. Serve over spaghetti  and top with Parmesan cheese. Yield: 4 to 8 servings.    JESS ASKEW    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
