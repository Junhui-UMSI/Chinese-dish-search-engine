Toffee Candy
Sleeve Saltine crackers, Chocolate chips, Butter, Sugar, 
Line a jelly roll pan with foil. Lay crackers flat in pan. In a sauce pan,  bring sugar and butter to a boil. Simmer for 10 minutes. Pour mixture over  crackers. Bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees. Take out of oven and pour the  bag of chips on top. Spread chocolate chips when melted. Freeze for one  hour. Then peel foil off and break into pieces.
