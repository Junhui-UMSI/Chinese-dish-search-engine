Amish Friendship Spicy Topped Cake
Uncooked, Nuts; chopped, BATTER, Amish Friendship Starter, Eggs, Vegetable oil, Unbleached flour, Granulated sugar, Baking soda, Salt, TOPPING, Butter, Granulated sugar, Brown sugar; firmly packed, Quick-cooking oatmeal, Ground cinnamon, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9-by-13-inch pan; set aside.    TO PREPARE BATTER In a large bowl, beat together the starter, eggs and oil.  Add flour, sugar, baking soda and salt; mix. Set aside.    TO PREPARE TOPPING In a small bowl, combine butter, granulated and brown  sugars, oatmeal, nuts and cinnamon.    TO ASSEMBLE Put half the batter in prepared pan. Sprinkle with half the  topping; cover with remaining batter and sprinkle on the rest of the  topping.  Bake 35 to 40 minutes.
