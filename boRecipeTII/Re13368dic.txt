Yams for Health
Brown sugar, Orange juice, Salt, Allspice, orange peel, Yams, Cinnamon, 
In a large skillet, bring 1" of water to a boil. Add yams. Cover and  simmer for 20 minutes or until they are tender. Drain. In a small  bowl, mix orange peel, orange juice, brown sugar, salt, cinnamon and  allspice. Pour over yams in skillet. Cook and stir until bubbly.  Simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes, spooning sauce over potatoes  occasionally to make a glaze.    Per serving: 135 calories, 0 fat.
