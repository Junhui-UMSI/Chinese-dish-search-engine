Pecan Cake
Heavy cream, whipped, Eggs, Vanilla, Flour, Ground pecans (reserve some), Baking Powder, Granulated sugar, 
Beat egg yolks, gradually adding sugar.  Sift dry ingredients together, add  to egg mixture.  Stir in 1/4 of beaten egg whites, and pecans, then fold in  remaining whites.  Line 2 layer cake pans with wax paper, butter and flour  them.  Fill and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Cool upside down on  racks. Frost layers with whipped cream. Sprinkle with ground pecans.
