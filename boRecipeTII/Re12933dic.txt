Zesty Chili Sirloin Steak
garlic, Boneless beef top sirloin, salsa, Chili powder, 
Combine garlic, chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper;  press evenly into both sides of beef steak.    Heat large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot. Place steak in  skillet; cook 10 to 13 minutes for medium rare to medium doneness, turning  once.    Trim fat from steak. Carve steak crosswise into slices. Serve with salsa.
