Vegetables in Foil
Sliced celery ribs, New potatoes (or quartered, Parsley flakes, Heavy foil, Onion; thin, Vegetable oil, Tomatoes; quartered, Basil, Thyme, Garlic powder, salt and pepper, Whole green beanns, 
On large square of foil, layer potatoes, beans, onions, celery and  tomatoes. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, basil, garlic powder, thyme and  parsley flakes. Sprinkle with oil and cover tightly. Grill on low fire  about 1-1/2 hours or until tender.
