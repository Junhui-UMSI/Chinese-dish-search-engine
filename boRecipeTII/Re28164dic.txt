Baked Dried Corn
Salt, Eggs, Dried corn, Butter, Milk; heated, Sugar, 
To the 2 cups or corn add 4 cups of hot mil and let stand for 1 hour. Add  the rest of the ingredients and bake 1 hour at 300 degrees in a 9 x 12 pan.  (Corn can be ground in a food chopper if desired.
