Zucchini Bars
Flour, GROUND ZUCCHINI (UNPEELED), Salad oil, Salt, Sugar, Cinnamon, Eggs, Baking Powder, Baking soda, 
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS IN A LARGE BOWL UNTIL WELL BLENDED. PRESS EVENLY  INTO A JELLY ROLL PAN. BAKE AT 350 DEG F. FOR 25 TO 30 MINUTES.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by scotlyn@juno.com (Daniel S Johnson) on Mar  22, 1998
