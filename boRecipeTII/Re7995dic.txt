Potato n Ham Bake
Bisquick original mix, Smoked ham; cut-up, Water, Pk hash brown potatoes, WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A, Cream of mushroom soup, Frozen peas and carrot, Cheddar; shred, 
Heat oven to 400%. Grease 13x9x2 pan. Mix baking mix and water in pan until  moistened; spread evenly in pan. Sprinkle potatoes over dough. Spread soup  over potatoes. Sprinkle remaining ingredients over soup. Bake 23 to 25  minutes or until edges are golden brown and cheese is melted. Cal 345; Fat  16 gr; 41.7% Fat. Source: Bisquick Makes It Easy.
