Strawberry Lime Whip
Sweetened sliced, Hot water, Sugar, Lime Jell-o, Cream whipped, Lemon juice, 
strawberries    Dissolve Jell-O in hot water. Add lemon juice and chill until slightly  thickened. Then place in bowl of ice and water, and whip until fluffy and  thick. Add sugar to whipped cream and fold into Jell-O mixture. Pile  lightly in sherbet glasses. Chill until firm. Cover with strawberries and  serve. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
