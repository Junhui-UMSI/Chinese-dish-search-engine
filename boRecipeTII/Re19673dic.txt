Another Tortilla Appetizer Recipe
Cream cheese, Picante sauce, green chilies, MIX TOGETHER, 
Spread mixture on 6" flour tortillas (room temperature) Sprinkle with  cheddar cheese and sliced black olives. Roll up and refrigerate, cut in  1/2" slices.    Posted to CHILE-HEADS DIGEST V4 #279 by Judy Howle <howle@ebicom.net> on  Jan 22, 1998
