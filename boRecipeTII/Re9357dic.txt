Pulao Rice
Salt, Peas, Hot water, Ghee Or Butter, Long grain rice, Clove Garlic, Crushed, Onion, Sliced, 
Clean, wash and soak rice for a half hour.  Heat the ghee and fry the  onions until golden.  Add the drained rice, garlic, salt and peas and  continue to cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Gradually add the hot  water, blending thoroughly, and bring to a boil. Simmer, covered, for 20 to  25 minutes or until all the liquid has been absorbed and the rice is  tender.    NOTE:  Any of the following can be added:  Cloves, Cinnamon, Caraway Seeds,  Tumeric, Ginger, Red Pepper, Coriander, Parsley, Garam Masala or Cumin.    From How To Make Good Curries by Helen Lawson Copyright 1973
