Tangy Short Ribs
Short Ribs, Ground cumin, Salt, Onion, Steak Sauce, Pepper, Lemon juice, Garlic, Chile Powder, 
*    Short Ribs should be cut into 2-inch pieces.    Place beef ribs in 4-Qt casserole or bowl.  Mix remaining ingredients; pour  over ribs.  Frfrigerate about 1 hour, turning occasionally.  Cover tightly and microwave on medium (50%) until ribs are tender, 1 1/4 to  1 1/2 hours, rearranging ribs every 30 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/meats.zip
