Tuna and Pasta Salad
Celery, sliced, Lemon juice, bottled, SALAD, Onions, chopped, Fat-free mayonnaise, Frozen green peas, thawed, DRESSING, Black pepper, Bell peppers, chopped, Thyme, Salt, Rotini pasta, uncooked, Tuna in water, drained and, 
Cook rotini according to package directions. Rinse with cold water and  drain. To prepare salad, combine tuna, rotini, peas, celery, bell peppers,  and onions in a mixing bowl. Mix well. To prepare dressing, combine  mayonnaise, lemon juice, thyme, salt, and black pepper. Mix well. Gently  stir mixture into salad. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour.    Per serving: 197 Calories; 1g Fat (4% calories from fat); 16g Protein; 31g  Carbohydrate; 13mg Cholesterol; 599mg Sodium    Recipe by: Land O Lakes Collector Series, Pasta!
