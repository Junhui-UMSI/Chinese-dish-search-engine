American Indian Fry Bread
Warm water, Powdered milk, Salt, Flour, Vegetable oil, Baking Powder, Lard, 
Mix dry ingredients. Cut in 2 T. lard until crumbly. Add water & mix  until a soft dough forms. Knead until dough is smooth & springy in  texture. Make into 12 balls. Melt 1 Tbls. lard & brush on each ball  of dough. Set aside for 30-45 minutes. On a lightly floured surface,  roll each ball to a 4" circle, then stretch to 7-8" in diameter. Poke  hole in center. Fry in oil at 365 degrees until lightly browned,  turning once. Serve with butter, honey or jam, or use as the bottom  layer of an Indian taco.
