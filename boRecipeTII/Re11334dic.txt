Ranch Style Brunch
Shredded old chedder, colby, milk, Frozen hash brown potatoes, Slices side bacon chopped, Eggs, Thinly sliced green onions, 
Cook bacon in large frypan until crisp. Drain, reserving 3 tablespoons of  drippings. Saute potatoes in drippings until browned, about 10 minutes.  Drivide potatoes among 4 individual greased 1 cup shallow baking dishes.  Sprinkle with cheese, then bacon. Beat toghether eggs, milk and onions;  pour into dishes. Bake at 350 degrees F oven 20 to 25 minutes for until  set. Garnish.  Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com (Judy E Ryder) on Feb 08,  1998
