Three-Seed Pork Loin Roast
Poppy seeds, Coarsely ground pepper, Teriyaki sauce, Single pork loin; (up to 2), Sesame seeds, 
On a sheet of waxed paper, combine seeds and pepper. Brush pork roast with  teriyaki sauce and roll in seed mixture to coat well on all sides. Center  roast vertically on spit. Close door and cook approximately 35 to       45    minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 258 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 15, 1997
