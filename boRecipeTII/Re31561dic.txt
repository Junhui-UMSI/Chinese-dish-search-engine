Sweetened Condensed Milk 4
Powdered milk (dry), Boiling water, Butter flavor Crisco, Sugar, 
Combine all ingredients in blender container.  Blend until smooth. Pour  into a quart jar and store in refrigerator. It will thicken as it sets.  Makes 3 cups.  1 1/8 cups equal the 14 oz. can that most recipes call for.  Keeps up to 8 weeks in the frig.
