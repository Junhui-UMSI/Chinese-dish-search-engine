Strawberry Margarita Crisp
Cornstarch, Triple Sec, Tequila, Cooking spray, Crispy coconut macaroons, Margarine, melted, Sugar, Medium strawberries, Sliced almonds, Sliced strawberries,, Graham cracker crumbs, 
Preheat oven to 350?.    Place graham cracker crumbs and macaroons in a food processor; process  until macaroons are finely ground. With food processor on, slowly pour  margarine through food chute, and process until well-blended.    Combine strawberries and next 4 ingredients (strawberries through liqueur)  in a medium bowl; stir well. Spoon strawberry mixture into an 11x-inch  baking dish coated with cooking spray. Sprinkle with macaroon mixture and  almonds. Bake mixture at 350? for 25 minutes or unitl sauce is thick and  bubbly. Garnish with sliced strawberries, if desired.
