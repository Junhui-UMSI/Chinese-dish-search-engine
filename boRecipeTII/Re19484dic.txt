Vegetarian Posole
Hominy, canned, Carrots; sliced, Onion, Squash, winter; chopped, Tomatoes, canned, Salt, Mushrooms; quartered, Oregano, Black pepper; to taste, Cilantro, fresh; to taste, Garlic clove; chopped, Cumin, Green beans, 
Brown the onions and garlic in a little oil. Add the vegetables to the  browning onions and garlic. Add the tomatoes and cook until vegetables  start to soften (about 10-15 minutes). Add the seasonings. Add the hominy.  Cook until all vegetables and the hominy are soft (another 10-15 minutes).                                      from the rec.food.recipes archives
