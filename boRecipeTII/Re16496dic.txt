Aepfelkratzet (Apple Scramble)
To 4 eggs, Sugar to taste, Butter (7 Tbsp), Milk (1 1/2 cups plus 1 1/2, Sugar for dusting, Flour (2 cups plus 3 1/2, Salt, Apples, peeled, cored, and, 
From the Allgaeu area.    Combine the flour, eggs, milk, and a bit of sugar and salt, and stir into a  smooth dough.  In a skillet, melt the butter and lightly saute the apples.  Pour the dough over the apples. Constantly stirring the whole with an egg  turner, cook until the Kratzet has browned all around. Dust with sugar,  and serve.    Serves 4.    From:  DSCHWAEBISCH KUCHE by Aegidius Kolb and Leonhard Lidel, Allgaeuer  Zeitungsverlag, Kempten. 1976. (Translation/Conversion: Karin Brewer)  Posted by: Karin Brewer, Cooking Echo, 9/92
