Watermelon Cake
Dr red food coloring, Egg whites, Butter, Sweet milk, Baking Powder, Raisins, Sugar, Lemon juice to taste, Flour, sifted, 
Take a little more than 1/2 of the mixture and to it add 1 teaspoon liquid  cochineal (modern: few drops of red food color) and 1/2 cup raisins. Put  the red part in the centre and bake. Cover with a frosting colored green  with spinach (suggest:  green food color).    Source:  Blue Ribbon Cook Book, 1905 Winnipeg Manitoba Shared by Elizabeth  Rodier
