Banana-Orange Daiquiri
orange juice, banana, sweet-and-sour sauce, Ice, Orange, Light rum, 
Place first 4 ingredients in blender, and process until smooth. With  blender on, add ice cubes, 1 at a time, and process until smooth. Yield: 5  cups (serving size: 1 cup).    Serving Ideas : Garnish with an orange slice, if desired.    NOTES : Serve immediately.    Nutr.
