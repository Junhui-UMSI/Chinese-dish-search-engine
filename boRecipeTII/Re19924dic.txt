Apple Bread Pudding
Brown sugar, Raisins, Cinnamon, Egg whites, Water; or skim milk (hot), Bread cubes, Apples; sliced thin, Vanilla, 
Combine bread cubes, raisins, and apples in large bowl.  Add water or milk  and sugar.  Mix thoroughly and let stand for 20-30 min.  Add beaten egg  whites (just til foamy), cinnamon, and vanilla.  Mix thoroughly.  Pour into  a 1 1/2-quart sprayed casserole.
