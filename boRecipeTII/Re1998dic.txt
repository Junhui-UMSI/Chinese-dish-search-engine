Chunky Chicken Mustard Salad
Red wine vinegar, Prepared mustard, Dijon mustard, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Each salt and pepper, Diced cooked chicken breast, Cucumber; unpeeled and diced, Chopped red onion, Chopped dill pickles, Celery stalks; sliced, Mixed salad greens, 
Taken from the cookbook "365 Easy One Dish Meals" {These are the 3 recipes  that I have prepared, and I recommend them for any picnic, church luncheon,  or just a quick and light supper.}    In a large bowl, combine vinegar, mustard, Dijon mustard, garlic, oil, salt  and pepper. Mix well. Add chicken, cucumber, red onion, pickles, and  celery. Toss to coat with dressing. Refrigerate at least 2 hours. Serve on  salad greens.
