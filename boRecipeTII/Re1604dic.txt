Calico Beans
Dry mustard, Tomato ketchup, Vinegar, (1-lb) pork and beans, (1-lb) red kidney beans;, (1-lb) butter beans with ham, Frozen baby lima beans, Bacon; cut in 1" pieces, Bacon fat, Thinly sliced onions, Brown sugar, Garlic salt, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Cut bacon into 1 inch pieces, fry, drain fat and  reserve appropriate measure of drippings in the pan. Stir in onions, brown  sugar, garlic salt, dry mustard, and ketchup. Simmer 20 minutes. Stir in  beans and half of the measure of bacon. Spoon into casserole dish. Sprinkle  with remaining bacon. Cover and bake for 50 minutes. Remove cover and bake       10    minutes longer. Serve hot.    Recipe by Mangia!
