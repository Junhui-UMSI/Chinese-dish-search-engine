Tomato Relish
Canned tomatoes, Ground ginger, Cumin seed, Garlic, Onion; sliced, Mustard seed, Chili powder, 
Recipe by: McDougall Preparation Time: 0:40 Place onions, garlic, ginger,  chili powder, mustard seeds and cumin seeds in a small amount of water and  cook and stir for 3 minutes. Add tomatoes and cook, covered, over medium  heat for 10 minutes. Then reduce heat and cook for an additional 10  minutes. Serve either hot or cold, as a side dish or on toast as a spread.
