Potatoes Obrien
Salt, Worcestershire Sauce, Onion, Beef broth, Red potatoes, Sweet red pepper, Green pepper, Cooking oil, 
In a skillet over medium heat, saute the onion, peppers and potatoes in oil  for 4 minutes.  Combine broth, Worcestershire sauce and salt; pour over  vegetables.  Cover and cook for 10 minutes or until potatoes are tender,  stirring occasionally.  Uncover and cook until liquid is absorbed, about 3  minutes.
