Aromatic Green Casserole
Sage, Walnuts, Brussels sprouts, Broccoli, Cayenne, Snap beans, Soy oil, Fresh dill, Juice of 1/2 lemon, Salt, 
Steam beans, brussels sprouts, and broccoli for 8 minutes. Combine beans  and walnuts with remaining ingredients and 2 ounces water. Transfer to  blender and puree until smooth. Pour sauce over vegetables.   Serve hot or cold.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
