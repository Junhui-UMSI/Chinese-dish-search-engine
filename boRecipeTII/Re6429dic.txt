Plum Pudding
Cinnamon, Suet, finely chopped, Bread crumbs, soft, Lemon rind, finely chopped, Orange juice, Salt, Cloves, milk, Sugar, Flour, Raisins, Nuts, chopped, Egg, well beaten, Currants, Orange rind, finely chopped, Mace, Baking soda, 
Mix the suet, sugar and bread crumbs together. Add eggs and beat. Sift  together all the dry ingredients. Combine the raisins, currants, nuts and  fruit peel and sprinkle with 1/2 cup of the sifted dry ingredients. Add  oraange juice to suet mixture; then add the milk and rest of dry  ingredients alternately. Mix thoroughly. Stir in the fruits. Fill greased  pudding molds about 1/2 full; cover tightly and steam for 3 hours. Serve  with any desired pudding sauce.    Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts  Press, 1936.
