Baked Liver with Apples
Beef liver; sliced, Water; or beef bouillon, Onion, Bacon; cut in pieces, Salt, Pepper, Apples; chopped, 
Place liver in greased shallow casserole.  cover with apples, onion, salt,  and pepper.  Top with bacon pieces and add liquid.  Bake, covered, at 325  degrees for 1-1/2 hours, removing cover during last 20 minutes of baking.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, October, 1972.
