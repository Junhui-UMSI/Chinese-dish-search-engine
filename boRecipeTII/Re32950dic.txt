Sweet Tomato Pachadi (Seasoned and Sweet Pureed Tomatoes)
Black mustard seeds, Pinch salt, For Seasoning, Tomatoes; pureed, Jaggery, Red chiles; broken, Ghee, 
Boil together tomato puree, jaggery and salt for about 10 minutes. Heat  ghee and add mustard seeds and red chilies. When brown, pour over Tomato  Pachadi and serve.
