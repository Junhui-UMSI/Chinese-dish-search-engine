Tess Nice Breath Biscuits
Whole wheat flour, Corn meal, Chopped fresh mint, Water, Chopped parsley, Safflower oil, 
Preheat oven to 350.  Combine flour, corn meal, mint and parsley in  large bowl.  Add oil and water and mix thoroughly. Roll out to 1/4"  thickness on a floured surface and cut into desired shapes with  cookie cutters. Bake 40 min., or until lightly browned.  Turn heat  off and let biscuits dry out in oven several hours. Store in an  airtight container in fridge.    From the Montgomery Advertiser.
