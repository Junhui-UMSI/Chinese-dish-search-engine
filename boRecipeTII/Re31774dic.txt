Syrup Cookies
Brown sugar, Salt, Soda water; DISSOLVED IN, Lard; melted, Hot water, Allspice, Butter, Ginger, White syrup, Nutmeg, Flour; sifted, Cinnamon, 
Combine the melted shortening, sugar and syrup. Sift the salt and spices  with the flour, and add alternately with the soda and water. Chill in  refrigerator for 2 hours. Roll very thin, cut in favorite shapes with  cookie cutters and bake on cookie sheet.    Recipe by: Grandma Ks Recipe Book    Posted to recipelu-digest by Nesb2@aol.com on Feb 5, 1998
