Vegetable and Bean Pitas
Balsamic or red wine vinegar, salt and pepper, Broccoli Florets And Stems;, Mushrooms, Fat-Free Cream Cheese, Dried dill weed, Insed, Reduced-Fat Shredded, Cans (15 Oz Each )Red, Sliced green onions; with, Nonfat Plain Yogurt, Whole-wheat pita breads;, 
Preheat the oven to 325?.  In a 1 1/2-quart casserole, beat cream cheese  and yogurt until smooth.  Mix in cheddar cheese, vinegar and dill weed. Mix  in beans broccoli, green onions, and mushrooms.  Season to taste with salt  and pepper. Bake, covered, 20 to 30 ins, until hot. Spoon bean mixture into  pita halves.
