Terrys Vegetable Casserole
Ritz Crackers -- crushed, Frozen Mixed Vegetables --, Sticks butter, Packets, ** NOTE, Velveeta Cheese, 
** NOTE:  Use any vegetable you prefer (broccoli, carrots, cauliflower,  etc.)    1.  Cook vegetables until soft but not completely done.  Lay them in bottom  of a 13 x 9 x 2" oblong pan (glass preferred, for serving).    2.  Cover with Velveeta cheese slices.    3.  Melt butter in pan.  Add Ritz Cracker crumbs, and blend until moistened  and butter is absorbed.  Pour evenly over vegetables and cheese.    4.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes, or until lightly browned.
