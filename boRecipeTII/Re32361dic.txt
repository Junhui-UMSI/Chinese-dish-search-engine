Barley Nut Muffins
AM Barley or Buckwheat Flour, Non-alum baking powder, Egg, Fresh milk or water, AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil, Pecans, Sea salt (optional), Raw honey, AM Whole Wheat Flour, 
Combine dry ingredients.  Mix liquid ingredients thoroughly.  Add dry  ingredients.  Stir until moist, but not smooth.  Stir in pecans.  Fill  well-oiled muffin cups 2/3 full.  Bake at 350 F. for 15 minutes.    (Yield: 24 small or 12 large muffins)    Source: Arrowhead Mills "Whole Grain Muffins" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
