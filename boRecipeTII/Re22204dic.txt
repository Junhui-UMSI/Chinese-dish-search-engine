Turkey Sandwiches with Berry Cream Cheese
Thinly sliced cooked turkey, Whole-wheat bread,, Curly leaf lettuce leaves, Seedless raspberry jam, Chopped walnuts, toasted, Nonfat cream cheese, 
Combine first 3 ingredients in a small bowl; stir well. Spread 2  tablespoons cream cheese mixture over each of 4 slices of bread. Top each  with 1 lettuce leaf, 2 ounces turkey, and remaining bread slices. Yield: 4  servings.    Per serving: 422 Calories; 8g Fat (17% calories from fat); 27g Protein; 63g  Carbohydrate; 36mg Cholesterol; 617mg Sodium    NOTES : The berry cream cheese is also good spread on bagels.
