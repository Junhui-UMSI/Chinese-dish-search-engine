Amaretto Cookies
Almonds, Butter, Amaretto liqueur, Lemon rind, Pastry flour, whole wheat, Molasses, Eggs, Almond extract, 
Cream together butter and molasses.  Add eggs, ground almonds, lemon rind  and almond extract; mix well. Stir in Amaretto alternately with flour. Drop  by teaspoonsful onto unoiled cookie sheets. Bake at 350 degrees for 12-15  minutes.  Cookies will be golden brown when done.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #281    Date: Sun, 13 Oct 1996 23:28:22 -0400    From: Walt Gray <waltgray@mnsinc.com>
