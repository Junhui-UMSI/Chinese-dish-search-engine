Pealing Bells
Vanilla, Flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, milk, Orange Peel, Maraschino cherries; halved, Butter or margarine, Shortening, Egg, 
Combine flour and baking powder. In a large mixer bowl beat butter and  shortening till butter is foftened. Add sugar and beat till fluffy. Add  egg, milk, orange peel, ;and vanilla and beat well. Add flour mixture and  beat till well mixed. Cover and chill about 30 minutes or till easy to  handle. Shape into two 8" rolls. Wrap and chill for several hours or  overnight. Cut into 1/4 inch slices. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Let  stand 8-10 minutes to keep dough from cracking. Place a cherry onthe bottom  half of each slice for the bell clappper. Fold in sides of slice,  overlapping at top and slightly covering cherry. Pinch in sides to resemble  bell shape. Bake in 350 degree oven for 12 -14 minutes or till done. Remove  and cool. Makes about 64.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 287 by "lbrandau"  <lbrandau@intrnet.net> on Nov 22, 1997
