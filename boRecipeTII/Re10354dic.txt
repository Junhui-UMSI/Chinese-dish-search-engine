Quick Cheddar Cheese Soup
Water, Chicken bouillon (cubes), Cheddar Cheese, cream potato soup, Butter, Cornstarch, milk, Pepper, Dried onion flakes, 
Melt butter in saucepan, add onion, cornstarch pepper, bouillon cubes and  water. Bring to boil, stirring frequently. Reduce heat, add potato soup and  milk. Cook slowly 10-15 minutes. Stir in cheese and simmer until cheese  melts.
