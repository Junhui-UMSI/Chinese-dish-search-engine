Quick Beef Dinner
Broccoli, Water chestnuts, Tomato, Water, Lean ground beef, Carrot, Soya sauce, Onion, Romain beef flavoured, 
1. brown ground beef and drain any fat (about 7 min)    2. add water, soya sauce and vegetables    3. bring to boil    4. break noodle block into 4 and add to vegetable meat mixture    5. cook until noodles are soft (about 3 min)    Serve with tomatoes and onions on top. Enjoy!    Posted to recipelu-digest by GramWag@aol.com on Feb 6, 1998
