Watermelon Salsa
Red onion, finely diced, Salt, Minced jalapenos, Parsley, flat leaf, chopped, Ginger, grated (optional), Cilantro, chopped, Finely diced watermelon, Pepper, 
Carefully mix all ingredients and let sit for 30 minutes to blend flavors.  Best when used within a few hours of making. Excellent on seafood.
