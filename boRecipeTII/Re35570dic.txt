Streusel Pound Cake - Crockpot
nuts, Cinnamon, Flour, Pound cake mix, Brown sugar, 
Mix cake mix according to package directions. Pour batter into well greased  and floured 2 pound coffee tin. Combine sugar, flour, nuts and cinnamon and  sprinkle over cake batter. Place can in crockpot. Cover top of can with 8  layers of paper towels. Cover pot and bake on high 3 to 4 hours.
