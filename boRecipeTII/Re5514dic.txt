Pineapple in Brown Sugar Rum Sauce
Rum, Fresh pineapples, Brown sugar, Water, 
Peel pineapple, remove eyes, and core.  Cut into chunks. Combine water and  brown sugar.  Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes, stirring  occasionally.  Add pineapple; simmer in syrup until heated through. Remove  from heat; stir in rum.  Pack hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4 inch head  space. Remove air bubbles. Adjust caps. Process 15 minutes in boiling water  bath.  Yield: About 4 pints.    From: Ball Blue Book Shared By: Pat Stockett
