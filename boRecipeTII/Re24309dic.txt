From:    charles moffat <moffats@USAOR.NET>    Date:    Tue, 9 Jul 1996 21:11:56 -0400  Combine all ingredients.  Mix by hand until well blended, about 2 min. Pour  into a greased and floured 10" tube or bundt pan.    Bake 350 for 50-55 min. or until cake springs back when lightly pressed  with finger. Do not overbake.    Cool 15 min. in pan;  remove from pan and continue cooling on rack.    If desired, dust with powdered sugar.    EAT-L Digest  8 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
