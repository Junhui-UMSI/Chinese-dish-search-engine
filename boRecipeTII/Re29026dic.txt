Baked Pineapple Pudding
Bread, Sugar, Salt, Eggs, Flour, (Large) Crushed Pineapple, 
Cut 5 slices of bread in cubes. Fry in 3/4 cup of butter until crisp. Mix  all ingredients and put in greased casserole. Bake at 350 degrees for 1/2  hour.    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by James and Susan Kirkland <kirkland@gj.net> on  Feb 24, 1998
