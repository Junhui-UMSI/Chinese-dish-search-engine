Tex-Mex Cheese Ball
Tortillas chips, Reduced-fat Cheddar cheese, Butter, Fresh parsley, Light nacho cheese-flavored, Onion powder, Green chiles, Chopped, Light cream cheese, Garlic powder, Drained, 
Food Processor Directions: In food processor bowl with metal blade or  blender container, combine Cheddar cheese, cream cheese, margarine,  onion powder and garlic powder; process until smooth. Add green  chiles; process with on- off pulses until just combined. Cover;  refrigerate 1 to 2 hours or until firm. Shape into a ball; roll in  crushed tortilla chips and parsley. Serve with tortilla chips.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
