Artichokes
Chopped, Fresh rosemary sprig,, Hearts, Red onion, sliced VERY thin, Cloves garlic, whole, 10 oz frozen artichoke, Juice of lemon, divided, 
Put everything (except some reserved lemon juice) in a lidded casserole.  Bake covered for about an hour. Uncover, add reserved lemon juice, and bake  another 5 minutes. You will have a HARD time believing its fat free  because of what the garlic does - it gets all gooey!
