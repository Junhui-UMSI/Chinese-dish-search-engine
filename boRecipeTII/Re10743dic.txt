Quick Tomato Sauce
Chopped Tomatoes, Basil, Apple juice concentrate, Oregano, Garlic powder, Chicken bouillon, Pepper, Onions, Thyme, 
Cook onions in bouillon until soft.  Add remaining ingredients.  Bring to  boil, cover, simmer 30 to 45 min.  Makes 3 1/2 cups. Cal: 10 per 1/2 c.  Fat: trace.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmealnew.zip
