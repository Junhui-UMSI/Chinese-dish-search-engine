Vanilla Soft Serve Yogurt
YOGURT NON FAT L/C 8 OZ, 
1.  POUR MIX INTO TOP HOPPER OF SOFT SERVE ICE CREAM FREEZER; START  DASHER MOTOR; TURN ON REFRIGERATION ACCORDING TO MANUFACTURERS DIRECTIONS.    2.  FREEZE TO A TEMPERATURE OF 18 F. TO 22 F. (APPROXIMATELY 10 MINUTES)  OR UNTIL PRODUCT CAN BE DRAWN WITH A STIFF CONSISTENCY THAT WILL HOLD A  PEAK.
