Apple Marmalade
Apple sauce, Lemon, Bottle fruit pectin, Sugar, 
Pare and slice apples.  Add just enough water to prevent sticking. Cover  and cook until apples are soft. Add grated rind and juice of lemon, and  sugar. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.
