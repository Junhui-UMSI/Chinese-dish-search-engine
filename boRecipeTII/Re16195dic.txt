Wassail #1
Sugar, Orange slices, Orange juice, Cinnamon sticks, Allspice; whole, Cloves; whole, Lemon juice, Apple cider, Lemon slices, Vodka or rum (optional), Cranberry juice, 
Tie cinnamon sticks, cloves and allspice together in cheesecloth. Combine  juices and sugar.  Simmer 15 minutes with spices. Float fruit slices on  top. Remove bag of spices. Add vodka or rum, if desired. Serve hot. Yield:  8 to 12 servings.    POLLY BREWER (MRS. LARRY)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
