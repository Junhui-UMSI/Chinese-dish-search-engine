Tomato and Chicken Stir-Fry
Garlic, Green onions & tops, cut into thin wedges, Minced fresh ginger root, Teriyaki Baste & Glaze, Boneless chicken, Vegetable oil, Tomatoes, 
Cut chicken into thin slices; combine with 1 Tbsp. teriyaki baste & glaze.  Let stand 30 minutes.  Heat oil in hot wok or large skillet over high  heat. Add garlic and ginger; cook until fragrant.  Add chicken and whites  of green onions; stir-fry 3 minutes.  Add tomatoes, green onion tops and  remaining 3 Tbsp. baste & glaze.  Cook, stirring, until vegetables are  thoroughly heated and coated with glaze.
