Pie Pastry
Flour, Salt, Unsalted butter, Lard, Ice water, 
Mix flour and salt. Cut in butter and lard with pastry blender until  mixture resembles coarse cornmeal. Sprinkle the cold water over flour  mixture and quickly beat in, using a fork. Gather dough into a ball, wrap  in wax paper and chill for 30 minutes.    Recipe By     : Elizabeth Powell    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
