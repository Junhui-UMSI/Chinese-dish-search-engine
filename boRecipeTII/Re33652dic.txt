Swedish Style Steak
Dill weed, Beef bouillon cubes --, Water, Salt and pepper, Sour cream, Onions -- sliced, Boneless round steak, Water, Flour, Crumbled, 
Cut steak into serving-size pieces.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Place  in slow-cooking pot.  Add dill, onion, bouillon cube and 1/2 cup water.  Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.  Remove meat.  Thicken juices with  flour dissolved in 1/4 cup water.  Turn control to high; cook 10 minutes or  until slightly thickened.  Stir in sour cream.  Turn off heat.
