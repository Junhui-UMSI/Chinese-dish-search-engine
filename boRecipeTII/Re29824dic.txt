Ten-Layer Casserole
Corn, whole kernel --, Tomato sauce -- canned, Canned, Drained, Pickle relish -- sweet, Onions -- chopped very fine, Rice, brown -- cooked, Packed, Garlic powder, Mustard -- dry, Brown sugar -- firmly, Kidney beans -- rinsed and, 
Preheat oven to 350.    Lightly oil a 1 3/4 quart deep sided baking dish or casserole, or spray  with a nonstick cooking spray.    In a small bowl, combine tomato sauce, brown sugar, relish, garlic powder,  and dry mustard. Mix well.    Spread one-half of the rice in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Top  with one-half of the corn. Spoon one0haf of the beans evenly over the corn.  Top with one-half of the onions, and then one-half of the sauce.    Top with remaining, rice, then corn, then beans, then onions, then  remaining sauce.    Cover and bake one hour.
