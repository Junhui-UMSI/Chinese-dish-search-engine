Baked Eggs
milk, Eggs, Onion, green bell pepper, ham, mild cheddar cheese, Bread, 
Mix bread, cheese, ham, bell pepper and onion; spread in a 13x9x2 inch  baking dish treated with nonstick spray. Whisk eggs and milk, and pour over  top.  Cover dish with foil and freeze.    To prepare for serving, thaw dish, and bake uncovered in a preheated 375  degree oven for 45 minutes.  Makes 8 to 10 servings.
