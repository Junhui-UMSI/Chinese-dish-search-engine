Pepper Cheese Muffins
Cheddar cheese, sharp,, Buttermilk, Flour, Butter, Salt, Egg, Black Pepper, Sugar, Baking Powder, 
Combine the flour, baking powder, salt, pepper, sugar, and cheese. Mix the  buttermilk, beaten egg, and melted butter. Add to the dry ingredients. Stir  just long enough to combine. Divide among 12 greased muffin tins and bake  in a preheated 400 F oven about 20-25 minutes.
