Wild Rice and Apple Bread
Dried thyme leaves, Cooked wild rice or brown, Salt, Molasses, Gold Medal Whole Wheat, Gold Medal Better for Bread, Chopped; unpeeled apple, Vegetable oil, Water, FOR 11/2# LOAF, 
(from Gold Medal Flour)    Measure all ingredients carefully and place into bread machine pan in order  directed by the manufacturer. Add apple at the "add-ins" signal or 5 to 10  min. before the last kneading cycle ends. Select Whole Wheat or Basic White  cycle. Use medium or light crust color. Do not use the dealy cycle or rapid  cycle. Remove baked bread from pan, and cool on wire rack.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 423 by Abtaxel <Abtaxel@aol.com>  on Dec 30, 1997
