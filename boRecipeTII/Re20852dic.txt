Apple Kugel for Pesach
Chopped nuts; (optional), Potato starch, Sugar, Lemon; (3 tablespoons) ,, Yellow apples; peeled, (up, Eggs seperated, 
(from Spice and Spirit- kosher Passover Cooking)    Beat egg whites until stiff. Grate apples on large holes of grater.Beat egg  yolks with remaining ingredients. Mix in apples. Fold in egg whites. Bake  in greased 8"x8" pan at 350* for 1 hour.    variation: you can put a layer of jam in the middle.
