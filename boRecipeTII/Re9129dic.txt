Proscuitto Escargots
Butter, Canned snails, Garlic, Lemon juice, Recipe COURT BOUILLON, Proscuitto, Teapoon parsley, 
Preheat oven to 500. Drain & wash snails. Simmer 10 minutes in  bouillon. Drain & cool. While snails simmer slice proscuitto into  strips of 4. Soften butter & blend in garlic, parsley & juice. Wrap  each snail with proscuitto. Skewer with toothpick. Place one skewer  in each cavity of an escargot dish. Drop a little garlic butter on  each snail. Bake 5 minutes.
