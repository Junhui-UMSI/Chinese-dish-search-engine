Pureed Lentil Soup
Onions; chopped, Cumin, Salt, olive oil, Water, Hot pepper; finely chopped, White rice; uncooked, Ground coriander seeds, Split lentils; rinsed, Pepper, Saffron, Lemon juice, 
From: Bulldogfla <Bulldogfla@aol.com>    Heat oil in a saucepan and saute onions and hot pepper over medium heat for  10 minutes. Add remaining ingredients, except lemon juice, and bring to  boil. Cover and cook over medium heat for 25 minutes. Puree; then return to  saucepan and reheat. Stir in lemon juice; then serve.
