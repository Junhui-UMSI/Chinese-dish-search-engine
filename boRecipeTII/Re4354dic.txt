Persimmon Jam
Sugar, Lemon juice, Powdered pectin, Large), Ripe persimmons - (medium to, 
Wash persimmons, cut stem ends & force fruit through food mill or  coarse strainer. Measure 4 cups pulp into deep saucepan. Add lemon  juice & pectin to persimmon pulp & mix well. Heat to boiling,  stirring constantly. Boil without stirring 4 minutes. Remove from  heat & alternately stir & skim 5 minutes to cool. Spoon into hot  sterilized jars & seal.
