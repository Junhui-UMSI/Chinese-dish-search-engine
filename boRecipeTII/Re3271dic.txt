Pear Salmon Paprikash
Salmon steaks, Flour, Butter, Lemon, salt and pepper, Dry white wine, Bartlett pears, fresh parsley, Paprika, 
Cut pears lengthwise into halves; remove cores with melon baller or  1/2 tsp.  measuring spoon. Set aside. Marinate salmon in wine for 15  minutes; turning & marinate 15 minutes longer. Dry steaks, reserving  marinade, & shake with flour; coat both sides with salt & pepper;  sprinkle with paprika. Heat butter in skillet; add steaks & brown  quickly on both sides. Add reserved wine marinade.  Arrange pears  around salmon in skillet; simmer, covered, about 10 minutes.  Sprinkle with parsley & garnish with lemon.
