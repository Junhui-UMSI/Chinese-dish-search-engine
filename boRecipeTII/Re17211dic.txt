Vodka Tomato Sauce
Crushed tomatoes with juice , Chopped parsley, Basil, Oregano, celery, olive oil, Half and half, Sugar, Prosciutto, Garlic, Vodka, Onions, 
Saute onion, celery, and garlic in olive oil. Add prosciutto, vodka,  tomatoes, parsley, basil, and oregano and simmer for about 30 minutes. Add  half and half and warm, but do not boil. Serve over pasta.    Recipe by: Carol Mann Posted to Kitmailbox Digest by wsmann@juno.com  (William S Mann) on Mar 12, 1997
