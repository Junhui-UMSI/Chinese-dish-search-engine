Quick Pizza Casserole
Ground beef, Pizza sauce, Mozzarella cheese;, Basil, Pepperoni, Onion, Parmesan cheese, Macaroni, 
Cook macaroni for 5 minutes and drain. Combine all ingredients and pour  into 13x9 casserole dish. Bake 30 minutes or until hot.    Serving Ideas : Serve with extra parmesan cheese if desired    Recipe by: BNR List/Pillsbury Classics    Posted to MasterCook Digest by Nsabatino@aol.com on Feb 25, 1998
