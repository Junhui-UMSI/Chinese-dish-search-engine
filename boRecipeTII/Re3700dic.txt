Penne Alla Bolognese
Grated Parmesan cheese, Cloves of garlic, depending, Chopped beef, olive oil, Salt and pepper to taste, Butter, Carrots, peeled and sliced, Onion, (16 oz.) whole tomatoes w/, Chopped veal or lamb, Red wine, Penne pasta, 
In a large sauce pot, brown the chopped beef and (veal or lamb), drain fat  and reserve meat for later. In the same pot, saute onion and garlic in the  olive oil & butter until the onions are translucent. Deglaze the pan with  wine and reduce by half. Puree the whole tomatoes and add them to the pot  along with the sliced carrots and reserved meat. Simmer for about 1 1/2  hours. (If the sauce become too dry add a little water or chicken stock) In  a big pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta al dente. Serve sauce over  pasta with grated cheese.    Of course youll want to have plenty of French or Italian bread to sop up  any leftover sauce and be sure to enjoy a great bottle of red wine with  this meal. Enjoy!
