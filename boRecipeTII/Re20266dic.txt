Veal Francaise
Unsalted butter, Veal cutlets (boneless), Dry white wine, Ground pepper, Heavy cream, Salt, Egg, Chicken broth, fresh parsley, Lemon, All-purpose flour, Fresh Lemon Juice, Butter, 
Place veal cutlets between 2 sheets of wax paper; flatten to 1/4"  thickness, using a meat mallet or rolling pin (or have butcher cut veal for  scallopine). Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dredge in flour. Dip veal in  beaten egg, dredge again in flour.    Saute veal in 1/2 cup melted butter in a large skillet over medium-high  heat for 2-3 minutes on each side. Remove veal, reserving drippings in  skillet; set aside and keep warm.    To the skillet add chicken broth, wine, 1/4 cup melted butter, lemon juice  (heavy cream if desired =optional=); stir well. Bring just to a boil over  medium heat, stirring frequently. Return the reserved veal to the skillet;  reduce heat, and simmer, uncovered, 5 minutes.    Remove veal mixture to a serving platter placing lemon slices either on or  around the veal. Sprinke with minced parsley.    Serve immediately.
