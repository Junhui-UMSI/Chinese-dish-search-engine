Fat-Free Pumpkin Pudding
Fat free vanilla pudding, Skim milk, Pumpkin puree, Pumpkin pie spice, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Fat free cool whip topping, 
Prepar the pudding per direction and add the spices and pumpkin, whisk  together. Spoon into 4 dessert dishes. Garnish with topping Very good,  satisfying and if you are also watching calories, this is great. If not,  you could use the pudding mix with sugar.
