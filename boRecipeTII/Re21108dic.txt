Two Tone Muffins
Cocoa powder, Beaten egg, Orange Peel, Orange juice, Salt, Almond oil or vegetable oil, Flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, Roasted diced almonds, 
Preheat oven to 400F, prepare pans. In large bowl, combine flour, sugar,  baking powder & salt. Stir in almonds, reserving some for garnish. In small  bowl, combine orange juice, oil & egg. Add dry mix to wet mix until just  combined.  Transfer 1/2 of the batter into another bowl & stir in cocoa.  Stir orange peel into remaining 1/2 of batter. Carefully spoon orange  batter into one side of each cup. Spoon cocoa batter into other side.  Sprinkle tops with almonds. Bake 20 mins or until done. Serve warm. Makes       12    muffins.
