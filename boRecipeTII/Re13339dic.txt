Yam Bake a la Orange
California Dried Figs, Walnut pieces, Honey, Oranges; peeled & sliced, into fourths, Orange Peel, Margarine; melted, Yams (1 lb. each); boiled,, sliced crosswise, 
Arrange yam slices, oranges and figs in an 8" round baking pan.  Sprinkle  with walnuts.  Combine melted margarine, honey and orange peel.  Pour over  all. Cover with foil; bake in a 350 F. oven for 30 minutes, until bubbly.
