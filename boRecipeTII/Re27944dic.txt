Three Bean Casserole
red kidney beans, baked beans in tomato, Bacon, Onion, Worcestershire Sauce, lima beans, Parmesan cheese, cheese, Catsup, Brown sugar, 
Lightly brown bacon and onion in large skillet; remove from heat. Stir in  all ingredients except the Parmesan cheese. Put mixture in well-greased  casserole. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake in 350 degree oven for 30  minutes.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
