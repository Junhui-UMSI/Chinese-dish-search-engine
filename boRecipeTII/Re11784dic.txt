Rave Reviews Coconut Cake
Yellow cake mix, Coconut, Water, Walnuts, Vanilla instant pudding mix, Eggs, Vegetable oil, 
Blend cake mix, pudding mix, water, oil and eggs in large mixer bowl. Beat  at medium speed for 4 minutes. Stir in coconuts and nuts. Pour into 3  greased and floured 9" layer pans. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes, Cool  in pans for 15 minutes; remove and cool on rack.    NOTES : Fill and top with Coconut-Cream Cheese Frosting.
