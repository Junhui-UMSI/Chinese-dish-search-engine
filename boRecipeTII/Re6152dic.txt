Pizza Porcupine Patties
Boiling water, Uncooked regular rice, Canned chillies -- chopped, Salt, Instant beef bullion, Shredded pizza cheese, Tomato sauce, Garlic salt, Ground beef, Dried oregano leaves, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Reserve 1 cup of the tomato sauce.  Mix  remaining sauce, the ground beef, rice, chilies, salt, garlic salt and  oregano.  Shape into 6 patties, 1/2 inch think.  Place in ungreased baking  pan, 13x9x2 inches.  Bake uncovered 15 minutes.    Remove patties from oven; drain.  Dissolve instant bullion in boiling  water; stir in reserved tomato sauce.  Por tomato sauce mixture over  patties.  Cover and bake 35 minutes.    Spoon tomato sauce over patties; sprinkle with cheese.  Bake uncovered  until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
