Peruvian Lomo Saltado
Potato, Tomato, Red Bell Pepper, Sirloin Steak, Jalapeno Pepper, Chopped, Fresh Oregano, Salt And Pepper, Onion, Garlic, Vegetable oil, 
Heat oil in a non-stick pan and saute onion, garlic and bell pepper. Add  potato and cook, stirring occasionally, until potato just begins to brown.  Add chile and steak. Saute a few more minutes. Stir in tomato, oregano,  salt and pepper.    Recipe By     : Jennifer Viegas - Arte Culinaria del Sol
