Streussel Topping
BUTTER, All Purpose Flour, Salt, SUGAR; BROWN, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, CINNAMON GROUND, 
1.  PLACE CINNAMON, FLOUR, SALT, AND SUGAR IN MIXER BOWL; BLEND  THOROUGHLY AT LOW SPEED.    2.  ADD BUTTER OR MARGARINE TO DRY INGREDIENTS; BLEND AT LOW SPEED  UNTIL MIXTURE RESEMBLES COARSE CORNMEAL.    3.  SPRINKLE OVER SWEET ROLLS AND COFFEE CAKES BEFORE BAKING..
