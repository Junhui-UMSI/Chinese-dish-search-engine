Peaches Supreme
Apricot preserves, Peaches or nectarines --, Halved, pitted, Pecans, Fresh mint leaves -- if, Cream cheese -- softened, Can, Pineapple tibdits -- in, Juice,drained, Desired, 
In small bowl, combine cream cheese and apricot preserves; blend well. Stir  in pineapple.  Slice a small portion from the base of each peach half so  that the fruit sits flat; reserve peach slices. Arrange peach halves on  serving plates. Spoon 1/4 of pineapple mixture into each peach half;  sprinkle with pecans.
