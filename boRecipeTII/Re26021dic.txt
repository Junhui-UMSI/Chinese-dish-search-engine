Tofu Chili
Yellow onion, (14oz) can Stewed Tomatoes, Garlic, Your taste), (cut into 1/2-3/4 "cubes), (14oz) can Red Kidney Beans, (14oz) can Chili Beans, Cake LowFat Extra Firm Tofu, 4 stalks celery, (14oz) can Tomato Sauce, To 3 Tbls  Chili Powder (to, Green pepper, 
Saute pepper, onion,celery and garlic with a little vegetable. stock (or  water) in a large pan (I use my Dutch oven). Then just add all the canned  item juice and all to the pot along with the tofu. Bring to a slow boil and  simmer covered for at least 45 minutes. (I like to make it in the morning  and simmer all day makes the house smell good)    Posted by frankm@extendsys.com (Frank I.
