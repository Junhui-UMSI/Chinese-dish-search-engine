Anchovy Steak
Beef filet steaks, salt and pepper, Anchovies; washed, Vegetable oil, Pitted olives; chopped, Butter, Lemon juice, 
Combine olives & anchovies with butter, lemon juice & pepper. Stir steak in  oil & quickly grill on each side; season with salt & pepper & serve with  olive mixture.
