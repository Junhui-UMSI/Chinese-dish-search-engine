Peas n Salmon Loaf
Salmon,drained(15.5oz ea), Salt, Lemon juice, Onion,chopped, Peas,drained(8.5oz), Eggs,beaten, Cracker crumbs,fine, Tomato catsup, 
1. Combine in a mixing bowl salmon, eggs, onion, lemon juice, salt and  cracker crumbs.   2. Carefully fold in peas.   3. Pack into a greased 1 1/2-quart loaf pan; bake in a preheated 350F.  oven 30 minutes.   4. Spread loaf with catsup; bake 5 minutes longer.
