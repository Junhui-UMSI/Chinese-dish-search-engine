Vivs Raisin Pie
Cornstarch, Cinnamon, Cold water, Orange juice, Raisins, Salt, Brown sugar, Pie pastry, top & bottom, Orange rind, Water, Butter, Vinegar, 
Boil raisin, first amount water, and juice, for 5 min in a covered pot.  Combine the following:  brown sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, orange rind,  salt (dissolved in second amount of water). Add to cooked raisins. Bring to  boil, stirring.  Remove from heat.  Add vinegar and butter. Pour into  unbaked pie shell.  Top with other pastry. Bake in hot oven (425 F) for 25  min or until done.    From: Vivian Hillier, Martensville SK
