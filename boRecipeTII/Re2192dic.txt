Awesome Breakfast Casserole
Eggs, Velveeta, Bacon, Cream of Potato soup, Hash Browns, Cheddar, Salt and pepper, 
Cook the hash browns with the onion till crisp. Fry the meat. Mix in a 13 X  9 pan. Add the cheeses, soup, beaten eggs, salt and pepper. Bake at 350 for  30 to 50 minutes.
