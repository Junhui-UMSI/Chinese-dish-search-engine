Strawberry Ginger Soup
Sparkling apple cider <<or>>, Strawberry juice; *see notes, Yogurt, Champagne, Mixed blueberries and red, Mint sprigs for garnish, Ripe banana, Ginger juice (or to taste), 
Combine juices and banana in a blender (or use an immersion blender) and  blend until smooth. Add sparkling cider and ladle into chilled soup bowls.  Serve with a dollop of yogurt, a scattering of fresh berries and a mint  sprig or two.
