Portobello Mushroom Filling
Butter, Portobello mushroom; diced, Parmesan cheese, Salt, Dry Red Wine, 
1. Melt butter in nonstick pan. Add diced mushroom and cook until soft,  about 8 minutes. Sprinkle salt over mushroom and raise the heat to high.  Add red wine and let it evaporate.    2. Transfer the mushrooms to food processor and process to paste.    3. Add cheese.
