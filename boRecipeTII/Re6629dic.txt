Pokara Dipping Sauce - Tamarind
Salt, Red chile powder, Tamarind pulp, Sugar, Water, 
Soak Tamarind in hot water for about one hour. Drain the tamarind and grind  it in a mortar and pestle.    If its already ground, then just mash it and mix other ingredients.    Recipe by: Jasmines Kitchen - Karachi Posted to CHILE-HEADS DIGEST V3 #349  by wight@odc.net on Jun 8, 1997
