Szechwan Bean Curd
Garlic; minced, 85% lean ground beef;, (light) Soy sauce reduced, Chile sauce;, Hot oil; (optional; see, Red pepper flakes;, Cornstarch;, Chicken broth;, Green onions; with tops, Seasame oil;, Water, Bean curd(tofu); cut into, 
Place ground beef, green onions, and garlic in a nonstick skillet and  cook, stirring quickly, until beef is browned.  Stir in chicken  broth, soy sauce, chili sauce, oils, and red pepper flakes.  Mix  cornstarch with cold water. Add to skillet.  Cook. stirring  constantly, untill sauce thickens. Gently stir in bean curd. Contiune  to cooking over medium heat 3 minutes.    NOTE: Seasame oil and hot oil are avalable in Oriental sections of  food markets and in specialty stores.    Food Exchanges per serving: 2 LEAN MEAT EXCHANGES + 1 VEGETABLE  EXCHANGE    CHO: 9g; PRO: 15g; FAT: 7g; CAL: 149;  Low-soium diet: This recipe is not suitable.
