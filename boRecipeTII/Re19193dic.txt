Vegetable Pasta Casserole
Salt and pepper, Vegetable oil, Zucchini, Carrots, All purpose flour, Paprika, Green bell pepper, Plain yogurt, Water, Cauliflower, Onion, tomatoes, Pasta shells, 
1. In a saucepan, heat oil.    2. Add onion, cook until soft    3. Stir in flour and paprika    4. Cook, stirring, 1 minute.    5. Add tomatoes with juice and water. Bring to a boil.    6. Stir in cauliflowerets, carrots, bell pepper, zucchini and pasta.    7. Season with salt and pepper. Cover pan and simmer for 40 minutes or  until pasta is tender.    8.
