Quick Low-Fat Burritos
Rice, Tortilla, Lettuce, Beans, Cilantro pesto, Monterey Jack Cheese, low-fat, 
Top tortilla with beans and rice; warm in the microwave. Top with cilantro  pesto or salsa, shredded lettuce and a little grated low-fat cheese. Roll  up and serve.
