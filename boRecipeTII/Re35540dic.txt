Strawberry-Wine Sorbet
Lemon juice, Chablis or other dry white, Basic sugar syrup, Coarsely chopped, Honey,

Thomasson)    Date: 5 Jun 1995 21:35:23 -0600    SOURCE: Cooking Light YEAR: 1994 ISSUE: June PAGE: 81    Note: Basic Sugar Syrup recipes can be found in several cookbooks.    Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan, and bring to a boil.  Reduce  heat, and simmer, uncovered, 4 minutes. Remove from heat, and let cool.  Pour cooled mixture into an 8-inch square baking dish; cover and freeze for  at least 8 hours or until firm.    Remove mixture from freezer; break into chunks. Position knife blade in  food processor bowl; add frozen chunks, and process until smooth. Serve  immediately, or spoon mixture into a container; cover and freeze for up to  1 month. Yield: 5 cups (serving size: 1/2 cup).
