Banana Bread-Moms
Sugar, Lemon juice or vinegar, Nuts optional, Mashed banana - 3, Salt, Eggs, milk, Butter, Flour, Baking soda, Vanilla, 
Sift flour, and salt set aside. Cream together butter, sugar, eggs and  vanilla. Add lemon OR vinegar to milk;stir. Add to creamed mixture  alternating between milk and flour mixture. Add bananas and nuts. Pour into  loaf pan, may be sprayed with Pam. Bake at 350F for 35 to 40 minutes or  until toothpicks comes out clean.
