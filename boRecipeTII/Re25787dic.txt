Baby Back Ribs with Mustard Sauce
Salt to taste, Mustard-your choice, Baby back pork ribs,separatd, Honey, MUSTARD SAUCE, Dry white wine (opt)or stock, Cayenne pepper to taste, 
Preheat oven to 300 F.  Sprinkle salt and pepper on the ribs, the place  them in a large baking pan, and bake in oven until tenter, about 1 hr.  Drizzle with the mustard sauce.    Yield: 10-20
