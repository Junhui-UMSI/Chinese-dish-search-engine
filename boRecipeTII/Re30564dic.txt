Tagine of Kefta (Meatballs in Shermula Sauce)
Coriander, Tomato paste, Onion, finely chopped, Coriander, chopped, Ground beef, White pepper, Cumin, Onions, Parsley, chopped, White pepper, Garlic, Cumin, Salt, Salt, Parsley, chopped, Tomato, diced, olive oil, Paprika, 
Combine beef, onion, corinader, parsley, salt, pepper, cumin and paprika.  Roll into meatballs. Brown and boil in shermula sauce until done. For  shermula sauce, combine remaining ingredients, bring to a boil and reduce  sauce until it reaches desired thickness.
