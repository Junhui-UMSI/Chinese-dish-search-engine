Wild Rice - Tuna Stuffed Tomatoes
Ripe tomatoes, tuna, Celery, Cooked wild rice, Diced onions, 
Combine rice, tuna, onion, and celery in a bowl with enough mayonnaise to  hold ingredients loosely together. Season to taste and chill. Prepare  tomatoes by turning stme end down, cut down, but not quite through, making  a total of 6 wedges. Spoon stuffing into tomatoes. Garnish and serve.
