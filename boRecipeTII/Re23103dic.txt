Tsatziki Sauce
Dried dillweed, garlic, olive oil, Cucumber, Salt & freshly ground black pepper, Fresh yogurt, unflavored, olive oil, 
Place a piece of cheesecloth in a colander and pour in  the yogurt. Allow the yogurt to drain for several  hours. Place the grated cucumber in another colander  and allow to drain for 2 hours.  Mix together all ingredients exept the additional  olive oil and chill. Place in serving dishes and  drizzle additional olive oil on top. Serve as a spread  for bread or as a dip for vegetables. This is  generally served as a first course. Note: Store this  dish covered in the refrigerator and it will keep well  for 2 or 3 days.
