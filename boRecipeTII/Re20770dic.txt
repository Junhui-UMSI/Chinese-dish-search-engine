Apple Eggnog
Ground nutmeg, GARNISH, Vanilla ice cream, Eggs, Apple Juice; Chilled, Ground cinnamon, Ground ginger, 
Put the first 4 ingredients into a blender container and blend until well  mixed.  Spoon in the ice cream and blend until smooth. Pour into glasses  and top with the nutmeg, if desired.    Posted by: Rich Harper From: Better Homes And Gardens Blender Cookbook  Copyright 1971
