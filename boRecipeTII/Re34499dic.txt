Stuffed Portobellos
Green pepper, chopped finely, Stalk celery, chopped finely, Garlic, Finely shredded swiss, Portobello mushroom caps,, Breadcrumbs (italian), Turkey sausage, sweet, Green onions, sliced finely, Egg white, 
Mix all and stuff the mushroom caps-bake at 425 for about 20 minutes, until  mushroom starts to look a little shrivelled. These are really good!!  Posted to Digest eat-lf.v097.n042 by NFRITZ@nssdca.gsfc.nasa.gov on Feb 13,  199.
