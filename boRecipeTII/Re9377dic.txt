Pulut Hitam
Thick coconut milk, Black glutinous rice; soaked, Pandan leaves, Granulated sugar, Thin coconut milk, Salt to taste, Pieces palm sugar, 
From: rabmalek@skat.usc.edu (Razali Ab-Malek)    Date: Sat, 23 Apr 1994 03:14:06 GMT  Here is the recipe for pulut hitam (black glutinous rice with coconut  cream).    Wash and drain black glutinous rice.  Add salt, thin coconut milk and  pandan leaves. Boil till mixture is thick and grains are tender. Add palm  sugar and granulated sugar. Stir till dissolved. Add water if preferred.  Remove from heat. Serve hot in individual bowls. Pour thick coconut milk on  top.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /DESSERTS    From rec.food.cooking archives.
