Radish and Orange Salad
Navel oranges;, Radishes; trimmed, Salt;, Orange flower water;?, Lemon juice;, Sugar or sugar sub:, 
Shred radishes in a food processor or "grate" with a blender.  Allow  excess liquid to drain of and add salt to taste.  Chill. Peel and  slice oranges.  Just before serving.  Arrange all together and dust  with cinnamon.    Food Exchanges per serving: 1 FRUIT EXCHANGE.
