Asparagus Casserole #5
Butter, Mushroom soup, asparagus spears  , Almonds, Cheddar Cheese, Cracker crumbs, 
Grate cheese and mix well with cracker crumbs. Add liquid from asparagus to  mushroom soup. Place a layer of cracker crumbs and cheese mixture in a  well-buttered casserole dish, add a layer of asparagus and a sprinkling of  nuts, cover with mushroom soup. Repeat until all ingredients are used. Put  a layer of cracker crumb-cheese on top. Place pats of butter over this and  decorate with nuts. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Serves 6.
