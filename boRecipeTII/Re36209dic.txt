Beef Stroganoff
Catsup, Clove garlic,, Onion, Can sliced mushrooms,, Pepper, Broiled in butter, drained, Salt, (few) Tabasco sauce, Boneless beef sirloin, cut, Envelope instant beef broth, Vegetable oil, Flour, Worcestershire Sauce, Commercial sour cream, Hot water, Dry sherry, 
1. In a heat-resistant, non-metallic, 10-inch skillet place 1/4 cup oil and  heat on top of a conventional range. 2. Add crushed garlic and chopped  onions to hot oil. 3. While onion is browning, combine flour, salt and  pepper in a paper or plastic bag and coat beef slices. 4. Brown coated beef  slices in hot oil with onion and garlic. 5. When beef slices are browned,  add all remaining ingredients, except sour cream, to beef mixture. Stir to  combine. 6. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5 minutes or until beef is  tender. 7. Add sour cream to beef mixture, stirring constantly. 8. Heat,  uncovered, in Microwave Oven an additional 2 minutes or until heated  through.
