Triple Chocolate Zucchini Cake
Baking soda, Sugar, Milk chocolate chips;, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Buttermilk, Eggs, Lightly packed brown sugar, Flour, Grated zucchini; about 3, Cocoa powder, Canola oil, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. In a large bowl, beat eggs, oil, white sugar and  brown sugar until foamy. Reduce speed to low and gradually beat in flour,  cocoa, cinnamon, baking powder and baking soda, alternating each addition  with the buttermilk. Fold in zucchini and 1 cup of chocolate chips. Spoon  into a greased 10" bundt pan. Bake 65 mins. or until toothpick comes out  clean. Cool in pan on a wire rack for 15 mins. Remove from pan to cool  completely. In a small saucepan over low heat, melt remaing chocolate  chips, stirrring , just until smooth. Pour over cake.
