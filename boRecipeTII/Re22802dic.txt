Apricot Spice Butter
Cinnamon, Cloves, Sugar, Juice and grated peel of 1, Apricots, 
Wash, peel and pit apricots; cut into small pieces. Cook in their own  juices over a very low heat until fruit is soft. Measure apricot pulp; add  1/2 cup sugar for each cup of apricot pulp. Add remaining ingredients and  cook until thick. Ladle into sterilized jars and seal.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 19, 1998
