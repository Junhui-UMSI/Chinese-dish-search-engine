Banana and Mango Bread
Golden raisins, Eggs, Self-raising cake flour, Salt, Cinnamon, Brown sugar, Butter, Ripe mango; peeled,, Walnuts, Grated fresh nutmeg, Mashed ripe bananas, 
Date: Sat, 16 Mar 1996 22:01:07 GMT    From: gemini@synapse.net (Georgette & Dave Burnside)    From The Gazette, 91/02/13. In a bowl, cream the butter with sugar until  fluffy; beat in eggs, one at a time, until incorporated. In another bowl,  combine self-raising flour with salt, cinnamon and nutmeg. Combine mashed  bananas with mango puree.  Mix the dry ingredients and banana mixture,  alternately, into the creamed mixture until batter is just combined; fold  in raisins and nuts. Pour batter into 2 greased 8 1/2 by 4 1/2 inch loaf  pans. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 - 60 minutes or until cake tester inserted  in the centre tests done. Leave in pan for 10 minutes; remove form loaf  pans and let cool on racks.  Makes 2 loaves.
