Bacardi Sauce
Bacardi rum, Cornstarch, Tomato ketchup, Lemon juice, Chicken Stock, Salt, White pepper, Butter, 
In a saucepan, pour bacardi rum. Add tomato ketchup, chicken stock and  reduce by half. Add diluted cornstarch and simmer for 5 minutes. Add butter  an whisk into mixture, season with salt and pepper to taste. Typed by  jazzbel@batelnet.bs.
