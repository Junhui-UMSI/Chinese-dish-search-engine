Vinaigrette Dressing #3
Salt, Vegetable oil, olive oil, Black Pepper, Dijon mustard, Tarragon Vinegar, Hard-boiled egg; separately, Dry mustard, Lemon juice, Clove garlic; bruised, Capers (optional), 
In a bowl, mix salt, pepper, dry and Dijon mustards, lemon juice, vinegar  and stir well. Slowly add oils and beat until creamy consistency. Add  garlic. After tossing salad, dust with eggs. You may add capers to  dressing. Yield: 3 bottles.    JUDY BURROW (MRS. LARRY)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
