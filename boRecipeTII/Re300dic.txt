Leche Flan
Hot water, Evaporated milk, Egg yolks, Caramel syrup, Lemon rind, CARAMEL SYRUP, Sugar, 
Scald the evap. milk in double boiler for 15 minutes. Beat egg yolks. Add  the sugar, milk and flavoring. pour into 1 quart mold. Place this in large  pan half-filled with water. Steam or bake for about 1 hour or until mixture  becomes firm. Cool before removing from mold. (If desired, line mold with  the caramel syrup before pouring in mixture for steaming or baking.    To prepare the caramel syrup, melt sugar in heavy metal container. As soon  as sugar is golden brown, add hot water to dissolve caramelized sugar and  form syrup. May be stored in covered bottle for future use.  Recipe by: Nora Daza cookbook    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #370, by Caroline Chua Pimentel  <carol@asti.dost.gov.ph> on Mon, 13 Jan 1997.
