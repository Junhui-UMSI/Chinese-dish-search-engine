Baked Cajun Chicken
To 1/4 ts grd white pepper;, Nonstick spray coating, To 2 lb chicken; meaty, Dried thyme; crushed, (breasts, thighs, drumstick), (I perfer real onion), Nonfat milk;, Onion powder;, Garlic salt;, To 1/4 ts grd black pepper;, 
Remove skin from chicken. Rinse chicken, pat dry.  Spray a 13 by 9 by  2 inch baking dish with nonstick coating.  Arrange the chicken, meaty  sides up, in dish.  Brush with milk. In small bowl mix onion powder,  thyme, garlic salt, white pepper, red pepper, and black pepper.  Sprinkle over chicken. Bake in a 375 degree oven for 45 to 55 minutes  or till the chicken is tender and no longer pink.
