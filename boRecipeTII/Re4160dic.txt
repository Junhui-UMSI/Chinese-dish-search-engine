Peppy Pizza Muffins
Shredded mozzarella cheese, English muffins; (12 oz.), Pizza sauce, Sliced pepperoni; (3 or 4, 
Directions : heat oven to 425 degrees. spread pizza sauce on cut side of  each muffin. sprinkle cheese over sauce. top with pepperoni slices. place  on baking sheet. bake 12 to 15 min. or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
