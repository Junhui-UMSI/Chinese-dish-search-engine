Baked Ditali with Sweet and Hot Italian Sausages
Ditali; boiled until barely, olive oil, Tomato sauce or pasta sauce, Ricotta cheese, Sweet Italian sausages, Grated Parmesan, Hot Italian sausages, 
From: Bulldogfla <Bulldogfla@aol.com>    In a saucepan, heat the oil and brown the sausages evenly. remove the  sausages; cut them into thin slices. pour the oil out of the pan and  discard it, but leave the brown residue. Add the tomato sauce to the pan  pan; add the sausage slices. Stir, cover and simmer for 20 minutes. Butter  a baking dish and spoon a layer of the boiled, drained ditali evenly across  the bottom; cover with a layer of sausage slices in the tomato sauce. Then  spread with a layer of ricotta and sprinkle it with Parmesan cheese. Repeat  the layering until all of the pasta, sausages and cheese are used, ending  with a layer of sauce and cheese. Bake uncovered, in a preheated 350 degree  oven for 25 minutes, or until the sauce is bubbling. serve in warmed  individual plates. Serves 6    Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com on Mar 10, 1998
