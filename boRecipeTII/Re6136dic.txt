Pizza Muffins
Flour, Fresh basil; finely chopped, (2 Oz.) sliced black olives;, Coarsely chopped pepperoni, Canned mushrooms; sliced, Baking Powder, Fresh oregano; finely, Shredded mozzarella cheese, Sugar, olive oil, Chopped tomatoes; drained, Egg, milk, Parmesan cheese, 
Preheat oven to 375.    Combine flour,sugar,baking powder,oregano and basil in a mixing bowl. Stir  in tomatoes, pepperoni, olives, mushrooms, mozzarella cheese, egg, milk and  oil. Stir just until blended. Spoon into 8 well-greased 2 1/3 to 3-inch  muffin cups. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake for 35 minutes or until  browned.
