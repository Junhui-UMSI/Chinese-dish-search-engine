Apple Crisp
Apple pie filling, Brown sugar, Nutmeg, Flour, Cinnamon, Butter, Rolled oats, 
Recipe By : WASHINGTON COUNTY FAIR KITCHEN (OKLAHOMA)    Spread two cans apple pie filling in bottom of 9 x 9 pan. Blend remaining  ingredients until crumbly - then spread over apples.
