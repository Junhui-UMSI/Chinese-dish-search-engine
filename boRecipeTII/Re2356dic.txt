Beef Stew in a Crock Pot
Carrots, Frozen peas, Raw roast beef, Potatoes, Onion, salt and pepper, Cream of mushroom soup, 
Place all ingredients in a crock pot and cook on high for 4 hours, stirring  occasionally; or bake uncovered at 275 degrees for 5 hours.    >From the recipe files of Carole Walberg    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Tracy Waltermyer <inadayz@nbn.net> on Aug  25, 1998,
