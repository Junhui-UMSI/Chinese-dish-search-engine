Baked Scrapple With Scalloped Potatoes
Soup Stock OR, Allspice, Leftover Gravy, Philadelphia Scrapple, LISA CRAWLEY/TSPN, Horseradish Sauce, Potatoes, Dutch Sauce, 
Butter a baking dish and line it w/ sliced potatoes. Cover them w/ Dutch  Sauce or Horseradish Sauce.  Mix the scrapple w/ allspice and soup stock or  gravy. Heat this and then spread it over the potatoes. Bake slowly for 45  minutes until scrapple is brown and potatoes are cooked. SOURCE: Pa. Dutch  Cook Book.
