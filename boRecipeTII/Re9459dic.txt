Pumpkin Bread
Sugar, Salt, Ginger, Eggs, Canned pumpkin, Cloves, Vegetable oil, Flour, Nutmeg, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Water, 
Preheat oven to 350 F (moderate)    Grease two 9- by 5- by 3-inch loaf pans.    Mix dry ingredients thoroughly in a large bowl.    Beat pumpkin, oil, eggs, and water together.  Add to dry ingredients. Stir  just until dry ingredients are moistened. Do not overmix.    Pour one-half of the batter into each loaf pan.    Bake 1 to 1-1/4 hours or until toothpick inserted in center of loaf comes  out clean.    Cool on rack.  Remove from pans after 10 minutes.    Calories per 1/16 loaf slice: About 200    Source: FOOD -- by U.S. Department of Agriculture Typed for you by Karen  Mintzias
