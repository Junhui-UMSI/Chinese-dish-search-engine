Tutti-Fruitti Squares
Sugar, Chopped candied orange peel, Melted shortening, Baking Powder, Flour, Eggs, well beaten, Salt, Pecans, Chopped dates, Diced candied pineapple, 
Cream shortening and sugar.  Add eggs and beat until light. Sift flour,  measure, and sift with baking powder and salt. Add fruits and nuts  alternately with dry ingredients to first mixture. Mix thoroughly. Pour  into shallow, well-oiled pan.  Bake in slow oven (325 F) about 30 minutes.  Cool.  Cut in squares and dip in powdered sugar. 12 servings. Mrs. C.E.  Rundquist, Orchard, NE.
