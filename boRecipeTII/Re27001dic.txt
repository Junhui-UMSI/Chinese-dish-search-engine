Three Nuts Apricot Bread
Egg, Blanched almonds; chopped,, Salt, Pecan halves; or pieces, Walnut halves; or pieces or, Pecan halves; or pieces, Honey, Dried apricots; quartered, milk, Large Loaf, Egg, Small Loaf, Walnut halves; or pieces or, Unprocessed wheat bran, Active dry yeast, All-purpose flour;, Walnut oil; or butter, Walnut oil; or butter, Vital wheat gluten, Dried apricots; quartered, Unprocessed wheat bran, Honey, milk, Salt, Active dry yeast, All-purpose flour;, Vital wheat gluten, Blanched almonds; chopped,, 
Pour all wet ingredients into bread machine baking pan. Measure all other  ingredients except yeast into baking pan. Sprinkle yeast over dry  ingredients, being carefull not to get yeast wet.
