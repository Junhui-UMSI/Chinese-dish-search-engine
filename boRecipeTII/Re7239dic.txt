Pork Barbecue and Noodles
(2 cups) Boneless Pork Butt,, Catsup, Onion, Beef or chicken bouillon, Cooked, shredded, Dry mustard, Chili powder, Finely chopped green pepper, Cider vinegar, Wide egg noodles, cooked,, Worcestershire Sauce, Drained, Corn oil, Pepper, Sugar, Ground cinnamon, 
In large skillet heat corn oil over medium heat. Add onion and green  pepper. Cook, stirring, 1 minute. Stir in next 10 ingredients. Bring to  boil. Reduce heat; simmer 30 minutes or until thickened. Toss with noodles.
