Waldorf Pomegrante Salad
Honey, Prepared mustard, Pomegranates, Paprika, Apples or pears; diced, Nuts; chopped, Citrus Dressing, Lemon or lime juice, Salad oil, Peel of one lemon or lime;, Celery, Seedless grapes; halved, Salt, 
Mix the first four ingredients. Blend with chilled citrus dressing. Spoon  into crisp lettuce cup. Halve pomegranate. Spoon out flesh-covered seeds  and sprinkle over salad.    Recipe by: Hurricane Heritage Cookbook    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by "Iris E. Dunaway" <hister@JUNO.COM> on Sep 20,  1997
