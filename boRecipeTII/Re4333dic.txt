Persimmon Cookies Fresno-Style
Flour, Sugar, Egg, Walnuts, Shortening or butter, Cloves, Baking soda, Salt, Raisins, Persimmon pulp (2-3 soft;, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, 
From: japlady@nwu.edu (Rebecca Radnor)    Date: 21 Dec 1994 17:58:50 -0500    From Madeline Davidsons "Cooks Notes". Cream the shortening and sugar  until smooth. Add egg and beat well. Sift the flour with the dry  ingredients and add to the sugar mixture, alternately, with the persimmon  pulp. Stir in raisins and nuts. Bake at 350 degrees 20 minutes.
