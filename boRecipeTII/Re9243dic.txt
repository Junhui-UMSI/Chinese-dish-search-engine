Puckerbrush Road Salad
Green pepper, sliced into, (11-ounce) mandarin oranges,, Sour cream, Sliced, fresh mushrooms, Chopped parsley, Red leaf lettuce, Bermuda onion, sliced and, DRESSING, Avocado, sliced, Dill pickle juice, Avocado mashed, Chives, Mayonnaise, Dill weed, 
Mix dressing ingredients ahead and refrigerate.  Mix salad ingredients and refrigerate. Toss with above dressing just before  serving. This salad is great with steak.
