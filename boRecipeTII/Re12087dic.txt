Ziti with Mexican-Style Meat Sauce
Enchilada-sauce mix(1 1/2oz), Ground beef, Tomato paste(6oz), Ziti macaroni(16oz), Water, Pimientos(2oz jar), Green pepper,medium,diced, Onion,small,diced, 
1. Drain and dice pimientos. In 12" skillet over medium-high heat, cook  ground beef, green pepper, and onion, stirring occasionally, until meat is  browned and pepper and onion are tender. Stir in water, tomato paste,  pimientos, and sauce mix; over high heat, heat to boiling. Reduce heat to  low; cover; simmer 15 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare ziti as label directs.  Drain. Serve meat sauce over ziti.    Miscellaneous recipes from the collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mo-misc.zip
