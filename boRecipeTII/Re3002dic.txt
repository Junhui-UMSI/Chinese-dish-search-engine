Peanut Butter Sundaes
Chocolate nonfat frozen, Sugar, Plus 2 tsp. creamy peanut, Plus 2 tsp. water, 
Combine sugar and water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil and boil 1 minute or  until sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter.  Transfer to a blender and blend on high until smooth. Cool slightly and  pour over frozen yogurt. Refrigerate any unused sauce.    Per serving: calories 227, fat 5.3g, 21% calories from fat, cholesterol  0mg, protein 5.6g, carbohydrates 39.9g, fiber 0.7g, sodium 2mg.
