Apricot Nut Bread
Flour, Salt, Egg, Grated orange peel; plus, Walnuts; finely chopped, Baking Powder, Canola oil, Sugar, Orange Peel, Orange juice, Dried apricots; chopped, 1% low-fat milk, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 2 loaf pans. Measure all ingredients into  a large mixer bowl. Beat on med. speed for 1/2 min. Pour into pans. Bake  55-65 mins or until toothpick comes out clean. Variation: Prune Nut Bread  Omit orange peel, orange juice and apricots. Add 3/4 cup prune juice and i  cup chopped cooked prunes.
