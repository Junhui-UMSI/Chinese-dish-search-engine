Anchovy Toast
Anchovies, Garlic, Onions, olive oil, Italian bread, Parmesan cheese, Mustard powder, Sliced, Sweet red pepper, Capers, 
With a mortar and pestle, mash anchovies, garlic, mustard, onions, and  capers. Turn into a mixing bowl and vigorously beat in olive oil  (or  combine these ingredients in food processor or blender and blend until  anchovies are mashed). Slice loaf of Italian bread lengthwise. Spread  anchovy mixture on top of one slice of bread. Lay slices of red pepper over  anchovy mixture. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Top with second slice of  bread. Wrap in foil, place in preheated 400 oven for 10 minutes. Slice and  serve.
