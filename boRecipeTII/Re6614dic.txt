Poi Nut Bread
Nuts, Salt, Vegetable oil, Poi, Cinnamon, Flour, Water, Baking Powder, Vanilla, Sugar, Eggs; slightly beaten, 
First: Mix poi and water together and let stand in bowl. Next: Mix  together flour,sugar,cinnamon,baking powder,salt in another bowl. Combine  the above mixtures with the remainder of the ingredients and place in  gresed loaf pans. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Optional: Add 1/2 cup  raisins.
