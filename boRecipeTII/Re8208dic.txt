Potato Gulasch
Extra lean ground beef, Onion, Bay Leaves, Potatoes; sliced, Salt and pepper to taste, Margarine or butter, Water, 
Saute onion in margarine; add meat & fry until browned. Add potatoes, bay  leaves, water, salt & pepper to taste. Do not stir. Cover & simmer about 20  minutes.
