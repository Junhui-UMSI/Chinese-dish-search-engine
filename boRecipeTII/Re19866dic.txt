Apple and Oat Bran Muffins (Ovo Lacto)
ADD, Brown sugar, Salt, Low/non-fat buttermilk, Oil replacement (applesauce,, Shredded, peeled apple, Whole wheat flour, COMBINE, Ground nutmeg, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Prune puree, etc), MIX & SET ASIDE, Oat bran, Ground cinnamon, 
Combine dry ingredients. Mix wet ingredients in a small bowl. Add to wet  ingredients to dry ingredients until just moistened.    Mix apple into batter.    Spray muffin cups with non-stick spray and spoon about 1/4 cup batter into  each muffin cup.  Bake in a 375 F oven for 18-20 minutes or until a  toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. 12 Muffins.    NOTE:  can store batter, tightly covered, in the fridge for up to 5 days.
