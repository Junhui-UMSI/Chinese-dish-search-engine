Armchair Quarterback Crunch
Roasted pepitas or hulled, Mini pretzels, Grated Parmesan cheese, Regular or light margarine, Mexican seasoning, Popped popcorn, 
Place popped popcorn, pretzels and pepitas in large bowl.  Melt margarine  in small pan. Add Parmesan and Mexican seasoning. Drizzle over pop corn and  toss lightly. Makes about 3 1/2 quarts.
