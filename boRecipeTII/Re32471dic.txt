Basic Braised Brussels Sprouts
Brussels sprouts, Salt, 
Bring sprouts, 1/2 cup water, and salt to boil in 2-quart saucepan over  medium-high heat. Lower heat, cover, and simmer (shaking pan once or twice  to redistribute sprouts) until knife tip inserted into a brussels sprout    center meets no resistance, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain well and serve.    Cooks Illustrated, Nov./Dec. 1995, Page 21. Credit: Phyllis M. Carey.    Nationality: USA Course: vegetable side dish Season:any Method: braised    Start to Finish 15 minutes Preparation 5 minutes Attention 10 minutes  Finishing a minute or less    Converted from Mangia!, Cooks Illustrated 1993-1995 Cookbook    NOTES : Serves 3 to 4. Serve these tender brussels sprouts seasoned simply  with ground black pepper and butter.  Recipe by: Cooks Illustrated, Nov./Dec. 1995, Page 21.
