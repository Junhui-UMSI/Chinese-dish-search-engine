Traditional English Bread Pudding
Eggs, white bread, Vanilla-flavoured milk, Sultanas, rind of 1 lemon, caster sugar, 
Remove crusts, cut bread into 1 inch squares. Place in lightly buttered  overproof dish, with alternate layers of sultanas mixed with the grated  lemon rind. Beat eggs lightly w/2 Tbsps of sugar and all the milk. Pour  custard over bread, sprinkle remaining sugar over the top and bake in a  preheated oven at 350? for about 30 mins. Let cool slightly and enjoy! With  this English style dessert you do not need any other sauce.    Processed by Recipe Buster.
