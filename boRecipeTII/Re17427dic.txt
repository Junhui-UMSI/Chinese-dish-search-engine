Walnut Quick Bread
milk, All-purpose flour, Whole-wheat flour, Salt, Unsalted butter; melted, Walnuts, Baking Powder, Orange; zest of, Egg, Pure maple syrup, 
From: dz234@cleveland.freenet.edu (Helen B. Greenleaf)    Date: 29 Nov 1994 21:56:45 -0500    In a large bowl mix walnuts, flours, baking powder, salt, and orange zest.   In another bowl beat egg, add milk, syrup and butter and beat until  smooth. Add milk mixture to dry ingredients and stir quickly to combine. Do  not over mix. Pour into a buttered and floured loaf pan and bake in  preheated 350 F oven for about 40 - 45 minutes or until well risen and  golden and toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pan for  about 3 minutes then turn out onto rack and cool completely.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
