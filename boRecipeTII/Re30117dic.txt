Bama Brunswick Stew - Sl 1/87
Onions, Chuck roast, catsup, Paprika, Brown sugar, broiler-fryer, Hot pepper, Pepper, Potatoes, Water, boneless pork, tomatoes, Red wine, Lemon juice, cream-style corn, Ground red pepper, Dried red pepper flakes, Dry sherry, 
Combine chicken, roasts, and water in a large Dutch oven, cover, and  bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer 1 hour or until meat is tender.  Remove meat from broth, reserving broth. Cool meat completely. Remove meat  from bones. Grind meat in food processor or grinder.      Add potatoes and onions to broth; cook over medium heat 20 to 25 minutes  or until tender. Add meat and remaining ingredients; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, 2-1/2 hours, stirring often. Add water for  a thinner consistency, if desired. Makes 1-1/2 gallons.    Recipe by J.A. Christian in January, 1987 "Southern Living". Typed for  yall by Jeff Pruett.
