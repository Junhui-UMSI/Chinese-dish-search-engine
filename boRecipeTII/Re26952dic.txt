Baked Catfish Dijon
Dijon mustard, Mayonnaise, salt and pepper, Tabasco, Butter, onion, Lemon juice, Catfish fillets, Chopped parsley, 
Preheat oven to 350. Arrange fish in shallow baking dish. Salt and pepper  liberally; cover with sliced onions and dot with butter. Bake 20 minutes or  until fish flakes easily. Mix mayonnaise, mustard, lemon juice, and  tobasco. Pour over fish and broil 2 to 3 minutes until brown and bubbly.  Sprinkle with parsley and serve hot with lemon wedges.
