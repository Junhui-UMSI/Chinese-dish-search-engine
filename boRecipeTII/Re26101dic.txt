Tofu South of the Border Enchiladas
Garlic, Diced tomatoes, drained, Onion, Green peppers, chopped, Dried cilantro, Silken firm tofu, drained, Cumin seed, Green mild chilies, chopped, Thick tomato salsa, 8" whole wheat tortillas, Low fat cheddar, shredded, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Lightly spray a 9"x13" baking pan with  no-stick vegetable spray. In bowl, combine all ingredients except  tortillas, tomato salsa and cheese. Place 1/2 cup of mixture in center of  each tortilla and roll. Place in baking dish, seam side down. Poru salsa  over enchiladas. Sprinkle with shredded cheese. Cover pan with aluminum  foil and bake for 35-30 minutes.
