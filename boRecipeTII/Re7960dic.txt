Pot Stickers #3
Garlic, Soy sauce, water chestnuts, Green onions, Ground pork, 
1. Combine ingredients in bowl.    2. Place 2 tsp filling in centre of wrapper. Brush edge with water. Fold  wrapper over filling to form triangle Pleat edges and press to seal. 3.  Heat 1 Tablespoon vegetable oil in heavy skillet over med-hi heat.    4. Stand stickers in pan and cook 5 minutes til bottoms are crisp & brown.    5. Add enough water to just cover bottom of pan, cover and steam 8 minutes.
