Winter Fruit Compote
Drained, Cornstarch, Allspice -- ground, Sugar, light brown, Pineapple chunks -- drained, Chicken broth -- (College, Lemon juice, Cherries, dark sweet pitted, Inn), Peaches, sliced -- drained, Apricots -- dried, 
In 3-quart oven-proof casserole, stir together broth, brown sugar, lemon  juice, cornstarch and allspice; add fruits, stirring well. Cover; bake at  350 degree for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve warm as a side dish  with poultry or pork or as a dessert.
