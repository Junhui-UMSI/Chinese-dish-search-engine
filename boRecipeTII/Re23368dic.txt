Tuna-Celery Chowder
Celery -- chopped, Paprika, Water, Fine, Onion, small -- chopped, Cream of celery soup, Salt, Tuna -- preferably chunky, 
Boil celery and onion together in water until tender. Add cream of celery  soup, stir well. Drain and add tuna, paprika and salt. Heat together under  low flame for 5 minutes.    Recipe By     :
