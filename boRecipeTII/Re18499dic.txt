Amaretto Fruit Dip
milk, Amaretto, Sour cream, Vanilla pudding, Cool whip, 
You can use instant or cooked pudding. If using cooked pudding, mix pudding  mix with milk and cook over med heat until thickened. Cool. If using  instant-mix pudding with milk and add the rest of the ingredients. You can  use up to 1/3 c of ammaretto if desired. Serve with your favorite fruits. I  use strawberries, cantaloupe, bananas, pineapple, grapes, peaches, apples,  just about anything!
