Traditional Chutney
Garlic, Fresh orange juice, Light brown sugar, Apple cider vinegar, Dried red pepper flakes, Fresh Lemon Juice, Grated fresh ginger, White raisins, Yellow onions, Whole mustard seed, Ground cinnamon, Lime, seeded and chopped, Ripe mango, Ground cloves, 
Mango: Hard, ripe, peeled, seeded and sliced.  Place all ingredients in heavy saucepan over medium heat. Bring to boil.  Lower heat and simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring frequently. Remove  from heat, cover, and let stand for about 12 hours.  Again bring to a  boil, lower heat, and cook for 15 minutes, stirring frequently. Remove  from heat and immediately pour into hot sterilized jars. Vacuum seal.  Makes: 4  1/2 pint jars.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmealnew.zip
