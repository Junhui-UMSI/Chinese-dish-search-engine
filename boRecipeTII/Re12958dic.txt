Zesty Herb Roasted Potatoes
Water, Seasoned salt, Each dried rosemary; garlic, Small red potatoes quartered, Miracle Whip, 
Heres a good one from the makers of Miracle Whip! Please forgive the  format.    Mix Miracle Whip, seasonings, and water in large bowl. Add potatoes; toss  to coat. Place potatoes on greased cookie sheet    Bake at 400 degrees for 30 - 40 minutes or until golden brown, stirring  after 15 minutes. Makes 8 servings Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #754 by  Lisa Minor <lisa@i1.net> on Aug 21, 97
