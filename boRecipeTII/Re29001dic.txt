Baked Pears
Lemon juice, Powdered Ginger, Water, Pears, Honey, Maple syrup, 
1. Leave stems on pears, but cut a slice from the bottom end to form a flat  surface so pears will stand up in baking dish. 2. Mix all ingridients and  pour over pears. 3. Bake uncovered in preheated oven at 325 F, for 1 hour  and 20 min. 4. Baste with the syrup while baking.     Out of Have a natural Christmas 79     typed by Brigitte Sealing
