Peanut Butter Kisses
Butter, Salt, Peanut butter, milk, Hersheys chocolate kisses, Vanilla, Egg, Baking soda, Brown sugar, Flour, Sugar, 
(have kisses unwrapped before removing cookies from oven) Cream sugar,  butter, egg and vanilla. Add peanut butter and milk, mix well. Add in dry  ingredients. Spoon out by spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at  375 for 10-12 minutes. Remove from oven and top each cookie with a kiss  while still hot.  = Tip = if you like crunchy cookies replace butter with  shortening, but other wise the butter makes them nice and chewy.
