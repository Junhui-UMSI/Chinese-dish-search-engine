Chocolate Chip Kamish
Egg, Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Flour, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Coconut, Chocolate chips, Raisins, Hazelnuts or almonds or, 
Beat eggs and sugar well, add vanilla then dry ingredients.    Refrigerate several hours or overnight.    Form into logs 1-2 inches in diameter. Wet hands help keep dough from  sticking to hands. Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes. Remove from oven,  allow to cool for 10 minutes then slice cookies 1/2 inch thick. Toast at      300    degrees until very dry.
