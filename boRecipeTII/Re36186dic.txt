Red bell pepper, Cumin, Beef; tenderloin, White wine vinegar directio, Sesame oil, Garlic powder, Green bell pepper-(2 1/2" x, Red bell pepper, Ginger, Yellow   "  " (2 1/2 in. x, Soy sauce, Onion; slivered, 
DIRECTIONS: Trim fat from (*I used beef) pork, and cut crosswise into 1/4  inch slices. Heat oil in a large non-stic skillet over high heat until hot.  Add pork, soy sauce, and next 3 ingredients; stir fry 3 minutes or until  pork is done. Remove pork mixture from skillet; set aside and keep warm.  Place skillet over medium-high heat; add onion and bell peppers, and stir  fry 5 minutes. Return pork mixture to skillet; add vinegar, and cook 1  minute. Yield: 4 servings.
