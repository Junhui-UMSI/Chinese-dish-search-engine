Banana Pudding
Sugar, Salt, Vanilla, milk, Cornstarch, Eggs, Bananas; diced, Vanilla wafers; (about), 
Combine first 5 ingredients in top of a double boiler and beat with an  electric mixer until smooth. Place over hot water and cover. Cook, stirring  occasionally, until thick, then cool. Stir in the vanilla. Place alternate  layers of vanilla wafers, bananas and custard in a 1 1/2-quart casserole.  Cover and chill, if desired. Serves 6 (Recipe is from SOUTHERN LIVINGS THE  DEEP SOUTH COOKBOOK.)  Posted to FOODWINE Digest  by "Joanne L. Schweikj" <SCHWEIKJ@FREDONIA.EDU>  on Dec 4, 1997
