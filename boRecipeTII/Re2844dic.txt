Peanut Butter Delight
Graham wafers;crushed finely, Margarine, Chocolate; semisweet, Vanilla, TOPPING, Peanut butter, Margarine, 
NB. Use the finest grain powdered sugar for this recipe and graham cracker  crumbs should be ground to a fine powder    Cream margarine, peanut butter & sugar until smooth. Blend in graham wafer  crumbs & vanilla, mix well. Spread evenly in 9" square pan and chill. Melt  the chocolate and margarine and pour over. Chill until it looks dull (about  30 minutes). Score and cut with sharp knife.    Source: Gail Teichmann posted by Anne MacLellan
