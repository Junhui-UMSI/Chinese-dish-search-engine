Basil Mashed Potatoes 2
Butter, Heavy cream, Salt, Potatoes,; peeled, Pesto, 
Boil potatoes until tender. With masher, mash potatoes. Add butter and  salt. Slowly add cream and stir until combined. Fold in 1 cup of pesto.  Serve.
