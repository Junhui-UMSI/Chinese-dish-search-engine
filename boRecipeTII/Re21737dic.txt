Apple Wine
Sugar, All-purpose wine yeast, Campden tablet, Apple cider; untreated, 
Mix all ingredients. Ferment under fermentation lock. Rack when  fermentation slows & sediment forms in bottom of jug. Allow fermentation to  finish & rock until wine is clear. Age as long as possible. Normally  drinkable after 3 months, better after 6 months, outstanding after 12  months. Makes a medium dry wine. For a sweet wine, use 4 cups of sugar.    COL R.M.
