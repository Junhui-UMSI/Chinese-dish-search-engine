Pizzelle Cookies
Eggs, Baking Powder, Sugar, Butter, Flour, Vanilla, 
Cream sugar and eggs. Add melted butter and vanilla with dry ingredients.  Dough will be stiff. Drop 2 cookies on pizelle iron. Sprinkle browned  cookies with powdered sugar. Makes 80  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 306 by "Sandy West"  <rwwest@execnet.net> on Nov 24, 1997
