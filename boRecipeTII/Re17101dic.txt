Vinegar Icing
Sugar, Egg whites, stiffly beaten, Vinegar, Vanilla, Cream of tartar, Brown sugar, Water, 
Combine all ingredients except flavoring and eggs. Boil to soft ball stage  (236 F).  Pour slowly, beating constantly, onto egg whites. Add flavoring.  Continue beating until thick and creamy.    Priscilla Gross, Fountainville, PA.
