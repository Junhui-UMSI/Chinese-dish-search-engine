Banana Stuffed French Toast
Bananas, Cinnamon, Eggs, Vanilla, Sugar, Half and half, sourdough bread, 
Cut a pocket in each slice bread; divide the bananas and stuff in the  bread. Whisk together the eggs and half and half. Saturate each side of  bread slices. Grill bread slices in oil or butter on both sides, until  golden. Combine the sugar, cinnamon and vanilla, sprinkle over the top of  the the French Toast.    Enjoy, Sheryl
