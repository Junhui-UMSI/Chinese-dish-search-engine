Tarragon Dressing for Fresh Spinach Sala
Sugar, Salad oil, Wine vinegar, Fresh or dried tarragon, Salt, Pepper, 
Combine all ingredients in a small jar. Shake well, then refrigerate for  several hours.  Dressing will keep in refrigerator for months.  Yield:    1-1/8    cups.
