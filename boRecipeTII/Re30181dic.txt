Banana Blueberry Nut Bran Muffins
Eggs, Baking soda, Frozen blueberries, Unsifted flour, Sugar, Buttermilk, Oat bran, Ripe bananas; pureed, Vanilla, Cup chopped walnuts, 
Makes approximately 2 dozen muffins. Combine first 8 ingredients. Add  remaining ingredients. Fill MUFFIN tins 2/3 full and bake at 375 F. for 30  minutes.
