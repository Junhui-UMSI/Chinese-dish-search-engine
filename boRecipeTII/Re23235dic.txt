Tuna Macaroni Loaf
Processed cheese, Black Pepper, (6-oz) Tuna, Beaten eggs (large), milk, Diced dark rye, Grated onion, Butter, Jar (4-oz) pimento bits, Boullion powder, Cooked maraconi, Nestles Maggi Sauce -or-, 
From: "Maxine L. Wolfson" <MLW@MATH.AMS.ORG>    Date: Sun, 14 Jul 1996 20:14:40 -0400 (EDT)  Mary Lee Taylor  NY News, 1949    Melt cheese with milk and bread.  Remove from heat, and add pimento, onion  tuna, eggs, macaroni, pepper, and sauce.  Melt butter in a loaf pan.  Pour  in the mixture, then spoon the melted butter (from the corners) over the  top. Bake 1 hour at 350F.    JEWISH-FOOD digest 258    From the Jewish Food recipe list.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
