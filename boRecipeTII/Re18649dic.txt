Amish Cookies
Butter, Baking soda, Cream of tartar, Eggs, Vegetable oil, Flour, Powdered sugar, Granulated sugar, 
Mix together, beat well and add 1 teaspoon vanilla. Drop by teaspoon onto  baking sheet (slightly greased). Bake 10 minutes in 375 degree oven. Makes  about 65 cookies.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 648 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 31, 1998
