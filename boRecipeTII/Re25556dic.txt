Avocado Fabulous First Course
Canned consomm?; chilled and, Avocado, Sour cream, Lemon juice, Capers, 
Allow 1/2 avocado and 1/3 can of consomm? per serving. Dice avocado and  toss with lemon juice. Place in bottom of balloon wine glass. Cover with  consomm?, sprinkled with a little additional lemon juice. Add a dollop of  sour cream and sprinkle with capers. Serve as appetizer or first course.  Can be made ahead about 1 hour before company arrives and kept in  refrigerator.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 16,  1998
