Tuna Club
Lettuce leaves, Low-fat bacon; cooked and, Recipe naked tuna salad, Fat-free mayonnaise, Drained, 7 grain bread, 
Recipe by: The Tuna Fish Gourmet Preparation Time: 0:10 Toast bread as  desired. Lay 2 slices of bread on work surface. Spread with mayonnaise.  Divide tuna salad mixture between the 2 slices, spreading evenl over the  surface of the toast. Arrange 3 strips of bacon on top of each. Spread a  little more mayonnaise on 2 more slices of the toast and place a slice,  mayonnaise-side down, on each serving of bacon. Spread a little more  mayonnaise on top of the 2 slices and divide lettuce over the tops. Spread  more mayonnaise on the remaining 2 slices of bread and invert one toast ont  either sandwich. Invert 4 toothpicks into each sandwich and cut into  quarters.
