Amocha Sponge Cake
Cake meal, Salt, Eggs, Finely chopped nuts, Potato starch, Strong coffee, Cocoa, Sugar, 
Pour hot coffee over finely chopped nuts.  Cool.  Add 2 tsp. cocoa. Beat  whites with salt until they stand in soft peaks. Add half of sugar in  gradually beating until stiff. Beat yolks well, add rest of sugar beating  until thick.  Add coffee mixture to yolks, blending well. Add sifted meal  and starch and blend. Fold into beaten egg whites. Place in tube pan and  bake in 325 F. oven for 1 hr. or longer.
