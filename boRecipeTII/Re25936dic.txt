Bacon and Sour Cream Omelet
Sour cream, Scallions; chopped, Butter, Eggs, Water, Bacon, 
Using a fork, beat the eggs and water until barely mixed. Fry bacon until  crisp. Drain, reserving the grease. Crumble the bacon into the sour cream.  saute the scallions in a bit of bacon grease and stir into sour cream  mixture. Melt butter in an omelet pan. Pour in egg mixture. When omelet is  set, spoon sour cream mixture along the center and fold omelet out onto a  warm plate. Yield: 2 servings.    CATHY MALLARD    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
