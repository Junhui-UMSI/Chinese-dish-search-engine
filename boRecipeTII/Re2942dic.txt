Peanut Butter Pie
chocolate shavings, Cream cheese, Crunchy Peanut Butter, Graham Cracker Crusts, Whipped Topping, Sifted Powdered Sugar, 
Combine cream cheese and peanut butter in a large mixing bowl; beat at  medium speed of an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Gradually add  whipped topping and powdered sugar, and continue beating until smooth.  Spoon filling into prepared crusts. Cover and freeze at least 8 hours.  Garnish, if desired. Source: Special Holiday Issue; Southern Living; Home  For The Holidays, 89
