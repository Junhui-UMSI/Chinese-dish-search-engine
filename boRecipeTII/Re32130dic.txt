Barbecued Lamb Shanks
Brown sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, Vinegar, Water, Ketchup, Onion, Dry mustard, Lamb shanks, TANGY SAUCE, Salt, 
Mix all ingredients for the TANGY SAUCE together. Brown the lamb shanks in  a small amount of fat. Turn down the heat and pour the sauce over the lamb  shanks. Cover and simmer for 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Serve with sauce from the  pan.    Goes well with rice. Posted to KitMailbox Digest by sadrod  <pax@pacbell.net> on Aug 20, 1997
