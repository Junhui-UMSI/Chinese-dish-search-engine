Watkins Oriental Vinaigrette
WATKINS Ginger-Garlic, Seasoned rice wine vinegar, Vegetable oil, Soy sauce, Toasted, WATKINS Chinese Mustard, Peanut oil or other, Sugar, WATKINS Sesame Seeds, Liquid Spice Blend, Powder, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in a small bowl.  Mix well with wire whisk.  While stirring, add peanut oil in a steady stream until well blender. Serve  on a salad of fresh spinach, sliced red bell peppers, and Chinese-sliced  scallions.    *To toast sesame seeds, place on a dry skillet over medium-high heat,  shaking frequently, until brown and fragrant.
