Tickuh (Roasted Chunks of Meat)
Large onions, Allspice, Salt, Garlic, Pepper, Large bell peppers, Beef of Lamb, 
:       Cut beef or lamb into 2in. chunks and place in a bowl, add  seasonings and garlic, then mix and set aside to marinate. Cut up onions  and bell peppers into large pieces and set aside too. To load the skewer,  begin with a piece of the bell pepper then meat and onion, continue this  way until skewer is full. Grill Tickuh to desired doneness, place on  serving plate with warmed pita bread. Serve with salad (salata) , rice  (risa) or Bulgur (gur-gur), and pickled vegetables (turshi).    Enjoy,    ~ Steve -
