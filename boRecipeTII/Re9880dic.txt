Pumpkin-Raisin Bread
Eggs, Sugar, Whole wheat flour, Baking Powder, Unbleached enriched wt.flour, Ground cinnamon, Vegetable oil, Raisins, Cooked or canned pumpkin, Baking soda, Salt, Lowfat milk or orange juice, 
Beat together the oil, sugar, eggs, and pumpkin until light and  fluffy. Combine the flours, baking powder, baking soda, salt,  cinnamon and raisins in a bowl.  Stir into the creamed mixture with  the milk or orange juice.    Pour into an oiled 9 x 5 inch loaf pan.  Bake at 350 F for 40 to 45  minutes.    1/15 recipe - 153 calories, 1 bread, 1/2 fruit, 1 fat 23 grams  carbohydrate, 3 grams protein, 6 grams fat 122 mg sodium, 117 mg  potassium, 37 mg cholesterol    Source:  Am.
