Lamb Kebabs with Lemon and Mint From Lhj Online
MARINADE, Vegetable oil, Lemon Peel, Fresh Lemon Juice, Garlic, Dried mint, Salt, Black Pepper, KEBABS, Boneless, trimmed leg of lamb, Green onions, Fresh mint leaves, Lemon, 
1. Make marinade: Combine oil, lemon peel, lemon juice, garlic, mint, salt  and pepper in large bowl. Set aside.    2. Add meat, tossing to coat. Cover and refrigerate 4 hours or overnight.    3. Remove meat from refrigerator 30 minutes before grilling. Heat grill.  Thread 6 skewers alternately with meat, mint leaf, green onion, beginning  and ending with meat. Grill over medium-hot heat 12 to 15 minutes for lamb,  12 minutes for beef, turning frequently and basting every 3 minutes with  remaining marinade. Garnish with lemon wedges.
