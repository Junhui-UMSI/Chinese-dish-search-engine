Taffy Apple Pizza
Creamy peanut butter, Brown sugar, Cookie dough, Vanilla, Caramel topping, refrigerated sugar, Lemon lime soda, Cinnamon, cream cheese, Granny smith apples, peanuts, Flour, 
ppreheat oven to 375 degrees. remove 1/2 cupof cookie dough and reserve for  another use. add flour to remaining dough and knead with hands to combine.  press dough into 14 inch circle on 15 inch baking stone. bake for 15-20  min. or until cookie is lightly browned. let cool for 10 min. then loosen  from stone. cool completly. blend cream cheese, peanut butter, brown  sugar,and vanilla together until smooth. spread on cooled cookie. peel,  core and slice apples. dip apple slices in lemon-lime soda to prevent  browning. arrange apple slices on cream cheese mixture. sprinkle lightly  with cinnamon. heat caramel topping slightly and drizzle over apples.  sprinkle chopped nuts over pizza. cut and serve.
