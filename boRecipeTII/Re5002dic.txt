Pickled Red Beets
Salt -- to taste, Vinegar, Brown sugar, 
Cook beets till tender, peel and cut into cubes or slice. Bring the  remaining ingredients to a boil. Pack the beets in hot sterilized jars,  cover with liquid and seal at once.
