Pineapple Salsa #2
Fresh Cilantro, minced, Fresh Pineapple, diced, Green Bell Pepper, minced, Fresh lime juice, Jalapeno, seeded and minced, Lime Peel, grated and minced, Red Onion, minced, Red Bell Pepper, minced, 
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl. Season to taste with salt. Serving  Ideas : Serve with broiled Salmon brushed with light soy
