Pistachio Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting
White cake mix, Eggs, milk, Water, Vegetable oil, instant pistachio pudding, Chopped walnuts , 
Combine all ingredients. Bake at 350(F) for 45-60 minutes in greased tube pan.  Remove from oven; let cool and remove from pan.  CREAM CHEESE FROSTING: 3  tsp butter or margarine, softened; 6 oz. cream cheese, softened; 1 1/4 cups  powdered sugar; 1/4 tsp vanilla.  Combine all ingredients and frost cooled  cake.
