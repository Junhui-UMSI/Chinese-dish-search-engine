Atlanta Waffles
Flour, Baking Powder, milk, Tsp. salt, Well egg, beaten yolks, Stiffly egg, beaten whites, Butter, Sugar, 
Sift dry ingredients together. Stir in milk gradually. Stir in yolks &  butter.  Gently fold in whites. Preheat waffle maker. Cook according  to manufacturers directions, 4-5 minutes each.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
