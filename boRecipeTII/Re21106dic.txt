Two Squash Casserole
Yellow squash; cut into 1/2, Onion, (8-oz) tomato sauce, Zucchini; cut into 1/2 inch, Italian Breadcrumbs, Bacon drippings, (4-oz) chopped green chiles, (1/4 pound) shredded Cheddar, 
From:    Patricia Moore <PCPJMOOR@IHC.COM>    Date:    Thu, 11 Jul 1996 17:46:26 -0700  Cook squash and zucchini separately in sm amts boiling salted water 5 min,  drain.  Saute onion in bacon drippings until tender but not brown.  Combine  tomato sauce, chiles and salt, stir well.  Place yellow squash in a 1 1/2  qt deep casserole, add onion. Pour on 1/2 of sauce, add zucchini, pour on  remaining sauce.  Sprinkle with cheese and top with breadcrumbs.  Bake 350  for       30    minutes or until bubbly.
