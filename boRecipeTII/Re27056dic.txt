Three-Herb Popcorn
(24 cups) popped popcorn, Dried thyme, Butter or margarine, Dried basil, (12 ounces) mixed nuts,, Dried chervil, 
Place popcorn in a large container or oven roasting pan. Salt to taste and  set aside. Melt butter in a small saucepan. Remove from heat; stir in  basil, | chervil and thyme. Drizzle butter mixture over popcorn and toss  lightly to coat evenly. Stir in the nuts if desired.    Yield: about 20 cups.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
