Potato-Nut Patties
Filberts;ground in a blender, Whole wheat pastry flour, Miso, Sage, Oil as needed, Onion; minced, Whole wheat bread crumbs, Parsley, Savory, Unseasoned mashed potatoes, 
Make sure the nuts are ground into small pieces. Place the potatoes,  onion, ground filberts, bread crumbs sage, savory, miso and parsley in a  large bowl. Mix well.     Shape the mixture into hamburger-sized patties of about 1/2-inch  thickness.     Dredge both sides of each patty in the flour, and brown the paties on both  sides.     These patties are good plain or accompanied by a sauce.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
