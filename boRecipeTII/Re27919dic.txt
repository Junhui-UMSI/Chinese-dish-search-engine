Thornrose House Bircher-Muesli
Chopped fruit of the season, Whole oats, Golden raisins, Lemon, Spoonfuls of whipped cream, Apple; grated, 
Soak oats and raisins in just enough milk to cover - store overnight Add  apple, juice and fruit Fold in whipped cream or yogurt    Recipe by: Thornrose House at Gypsy Hill, Staunton, Virginia    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by SuzyWert@aol.com on Feb 12, 1998
