Wok Cake
9" cake pan, Pkg cake mix, 
Preheat wok over medium heat. Divide package cake mix in half and mix  according to recipe. Lightly grease and flour one 9" cake pan. Pour cake  mixture into pan. Set the pan in the wok. Cook for the time suggested in  the recipe. DO NOT LIFT LID WHILE COOKING. Cook one layer at a time.
