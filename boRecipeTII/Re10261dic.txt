Quick and Spicy Chicken And Rice
Jar; (8 oz) pasteurized, Uncooked instant rice, Water, Salt, Cooked chicken breast; cubed, Frozen broccoli cuts, 
Recipe By : Easy Weeknight Meals    In large saucepan, bring water and salt to a boil. Add rice and broccoli;  return to a boil. Boil 1 minute. Remove from heat; cover and let stand 5  minutes.    Stir in cheese sauce and chicken; cook over low heat for 2-4 minutes or  until thoroughly heated, stirring occasionally.
