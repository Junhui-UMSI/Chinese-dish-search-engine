Strawberries a la Colony
Strawberries, Sugar, Cointreau or Grand Marnier, Almond extract, Heavy cream, Vanilla ice cream, 
1. Gently wash berries in cold water. Drain on paper towels; hull. Turn  into serving bowl. Sprinkle with sugar and Cointreau; toss. Chill 1 hour,  stirring occasionally.    2. Let ice cream soften in refrigerator about 1 hour.    3. Beat heavy cream just un~ stiff. Fold in almond extract.    4. Gently fold whipped cream and softened ice cream into strawberry  mixture. Serve at once. Makes 8 servings.
