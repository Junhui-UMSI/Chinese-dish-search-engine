Pressed Chicken
Flour, Hard Dry Toast, Carrots, Water, Whole Chicken Breasts, Chicken Bouillon Cubes, Stalks Celery, Chicken Legs And Thighs, 
Stew chicken, vegetables, and bouillon until done and almost dry. Remove  chicken and bone it. Grind chicken, vegetables and toast in food grinder.  Mix 1/4 cup water and flour to make a chicken gravy with residue from  stewing chicken. Mix with ground chicken until moist. Pat into a loaf dish  and press down firm and solid. Place in refrigerator. Slice when cold and  serve.    Serves 8    Source: "Mountain Measures" -- Junior League of Charleston, WV ed. 1974    Recipe by: Miss Virginia Kelly Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #689 by Bill  Spalding <billspa@icanect.net> on Jul 25, 1997
