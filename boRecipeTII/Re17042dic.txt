Vietnamese Salad
Chicken breasts; boned, Cucumber; seeded & sliced, Sprigs fresh mint leaves, Ribs celery; thinly sliced, Bunch scallions, Sugar, Teriyaki sauce, Carrot; grated, Sprigs fresh coriander or, Garlic, Fish sauce (bottled), Head green cabbage; grated, Lemon juice, 
Place thinly sliced cabbage, carrot, cucumber, scallions, celery, mint  leaves and coriander or parsley in large salad bowl. Cut chicken breasts in  small cubes and soak in teriyaki sauce for about 5 minutes. Saute in wok or  skillet for about 5 minutes until cooked. Add to salad mixture. In a  separate bowl mix lemon juice, fish sauce (from oriental food store), sugar  and crushed garlic. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Toss salad with dressing  and allow to stand for 1/2 hour before serving.    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of Contemporary Recipes>.  Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
