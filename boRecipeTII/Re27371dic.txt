Toasted Cumin Sauce for Fish
Juice of 1 1/2 oranges, Dried Oregano, Sea salt, black, Cumin seeds, Juice of 1 1/2 limes, olive oil, garlic, 
In a small pan, over a medium heat, warm the olive oil. Add the cumin seeds  and toast them 1 to 2 minutes. Add the garlic (cook a minute to release  oils) then add, oregano, salt and pepper and cook another 2 to 3 minutes.  Remove from the heat and add the orange and lime juices.    The sauce is best used immediately, but will keep for a few days in the  refrigerator.    Use as a finishing sauce for grilled seafood, such as halibut or salmon.
