Zesta Dessert
Of salt, Sugar, Powdered sugar, Pie filling (blueberry,, Butter, Egg whites, Vanilla, Whipping cream, Crushed), 
Combine crackers with butter and press on bottom of a 9x13" baking pan,  reserving 1/4 cup for topping. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form, then  gradually add sugar, salt, and vanilla. Spread egg white mixture over  cracker crumbs and bake in 325 degree oven 10 to 15 minutes; Cool. Spread  pie filling over top. Beat whipping cream until stiff and add powdered  sugar. Spread over top of pie filling and refrigerate. Cut into squares to  serve.    From: The Budget, October 2 nd 1991
