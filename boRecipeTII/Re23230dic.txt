Tuna Mac
Canned tuna in water;, Water, Elbow macaroni; uncooked, milk, Frozen vegetable blend;, VELVEETA Process Cheese, 
Bring water to a boil in a saucepan; stir in macaroni. Reduce heat to  medium low; cover with a tight fitting lid. Simmer 8 to 10 minutes or until  macaroni is tender. Add Velveeta, vegetables, tuna & milk. Stir until  Velveeta is melted & mixture is thoroughly heated.    Recipe by: Kraft Velveeta Box    Posted to recipelu-digest by "Christopher E.
