Pear Honey #1
Sugar, Lemon; grated rind & juice, Crushed pineapple, Ripe pears; about 3 lbs, 
Wash, core and pare pears. Slice before measuring. Put through food chopper  using fine blade. Measure 9 cups of ground pears. Combine all inngredients.  Cook in large vessel over low heat for 20 minutes. Pour into sterile jars  and seal.    NOTE:  Get a book on canning if unsure about sealing and processing.
