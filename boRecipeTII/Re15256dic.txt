Whiskey Sour
Lemon juice, 7 Crown whiskey, Powdered sugar, 
Shake with cracked ice and strain into whiskey sour glass.  Decorate with  half slice orange and maraschino cherry. Top with splash of soda if  desired. Also served on the rocks in an old-fashioned glass.    MILITARY FAMILY MAGAZINE    JANUARY 1996    Downloaded from G Internet, G Internet.
