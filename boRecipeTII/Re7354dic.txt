Pork Chops on Rice
Orange juice, Pork chops, Minute rice, Chicken with rice soup, 
In skillet, brown pork chops on both sides, season with salt and pepper.  Place rice in 12x7" baking dish, pour orange juice over rice. Arrange pork  chops on rice. Pour chicken soup over all. Cover and bake at 350~ for 45  minutes. Uncover and bake for another 10 minutes. Serves 6.
