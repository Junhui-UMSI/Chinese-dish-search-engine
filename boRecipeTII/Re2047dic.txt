Almond Dumplings (For Soup)
Eggs, salt and pepper, Chopped parsley, Minced almonds, Baking Powder, 
Flour    Beat egg yolks very light. Add salt, pepper, parsley, almonds, baking  powder, & enough flour to make a VERY stiff batter. Add beaten egg whites.  Test a teaspoonful in boiling water - if it boils apart, add more flour.  Drop from teaspoon into boiling soup 10 minutes before serving. This is  from The Settlement Cookbook.
