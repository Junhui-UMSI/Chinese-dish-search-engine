Wild Rice Stuffing
Salt, Skinless breakfast sausage, Green bell pepper;, Onion, Wild rice; cooked and, Black Pepper, Tarragon, 
From:  Wayne Pridgen <pridgen@pond.com>    Date: Thu, 16 May 1996 20:41:44 -465800    Recipe by: Sylvia Woods of the World Famous Sylvias Restaurant (NYC)  Crumble the sausage into a large heavy skillet.  Cook over medium heat  until the sausage renders its fat and beginsto brown. Add the green  peppers and onions and continue cooking until they are tender and the  sausage is brown and no trace of pink remains, about 10 minutes. Season  with the salt, pepper, and tarragon. Toss well with the wild rice. Let the  stuffing stand a few minutes and adjust the seasonings.
