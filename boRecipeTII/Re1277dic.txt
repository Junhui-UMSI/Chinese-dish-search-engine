Deep Fried Ice Cream
vanilla ice cream, COATING, Bread crumbs, Almond flour, Egg, Flour, GARNISH, Mint leaves, 
Freeze scoops of vanilla ice cream    Beat egg. Combine bread crumbs and almond flour. Roll ice cream scoops in  flour, then beaten egg, and finally in almond/breadcrumb mixture. Fry the  ice cream in hot oil for 20 seconds. Put the fried ice cream in bowl. Dust  with confectioners sugar and garnish with mint leaf.
