Zingy Chili Dip
Tortilla chips, olive oil, Fresh mushrooms -- chopped, 4-oz. diced green chilies --, Drained, 8-oz. tomato sauce, Salt, 4-oz. black olives --, Vinegar, Chopped, 28-oz tomatoes; drained --, Milliliters, Mashed, Garlic -- finely diced, Green onions -- chopped, 
Redrain tomatoes, removing as much juice as possible. Mix with all other  ingredients.  Allow to chill for several hours to blend flavors. Serve with  tortilla chips. Note - will keep for up to two weeks in frig if tightly  covered.    Author - Junior League of Norfolk-Virginia Beach, VA Typed by Sue Woodward    Recipe By     : Tidewater on the Half Shell
