Poppy Seed Noodles
Poppy seeds, egg noodles, Butter, 
Prepare egg noodles according to package directions; drain and set aside.  Add butter to pasta pot.  Return noodles to pasta pot, cover with poppy  seeds and stir until butter is melted and noodles are covered with poppy  seeds and butter.
