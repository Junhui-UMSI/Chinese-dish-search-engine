Potato Brunch Eggs
Potatoes Obrien, Chopped parsley, Eggs, Cream Of Celery Soup, White pepper, Dried Onions, Salt, Carnation Evaporated Milk;, 
Run hot water over potatoes to bring them to room temperature; drain well.  Spread over bottom of buttered 8" square baking pan. Beat eggs in medium  bowl. Stir in remaining ingredients; blend well. Pour over potatoes. Bake  at 350 for 25 to 30 minutes. Megs notes: Made 4/98.
