Apricot Ice Cream
(8-oz) crushed pineapple;, Cream, (17-oz) apricots; chopped, milk, Sugar, Lemons, 
Mix apricots, pineapple, juice of lemons and sugar. Let stand a few  minutes. Gradually add milk and cream. Freeze in a 4-quart ice cream  freezer.
