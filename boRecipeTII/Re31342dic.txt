Bar-B-Barn Ribs
Garlic powder, Lemon juice, Paprika, Brown sugar, Apple Sauce, Cinnamon, Ribs, Parboiled, Pepper, Salt, 
In a heavy saucepan, mix apple sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice, salt,  pepper, paprika, garlic powder and cinnamon. Over medium-high heat, bring  the sauce to the boiling point and continue boiling for one to two minutes.  Place parboiled ribs in an ovenproof dish and brush liberally with the  sauce. Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees. Turn ribs and bush underside  with remaining sauce. Continue baking for 45 minutes. Serves four. From The  Gazette, 90/12/19.
