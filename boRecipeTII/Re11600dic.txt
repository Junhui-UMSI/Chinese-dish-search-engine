Raspberry Poppy Seed Cookies
Butter, Poppy seeds, Egg, Salt, Powdered sugar, All-purpose flour, Vanilla, Fruit (raspberry; cherry,, 
Recipe by: The Dessert Show, TVFN     Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Beat powdered sugar and butter until  lightand fluffy. Add vanilla and egg. Blend well. Stir in flour, poppy  seedsand salt. Mix well. Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls 1 inch apart  onungreased cookie sheets. With finger, make an indentation in center of  each. Fill each with about 1/2 teaspoon of preserves. Bake until golden  brown, about 20-25 minutes. Immediately remove fromcookie sheets.
