Pecan Oatmeal Bread -Abm
[rounded] salt, Wheat bran, Water, [rounded] yeast, Wheat germ, Vegetable oil, All purpose white flour, Coarse ground pecans, Gluten, Fine ground oatmeal, Whole wheat flour, [heaped] brown sugar, 
Grind rolled oats into meal in a blender. Add liquids, flours, other dry  ingredients and yeast in the order recommended by the machines  manufacturer. Set cycle to whole wheat, medium crust.    Jim Weller  Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #453 by Jim Weller <jweller@ssimicro.com>  on Dec 14, 1997
