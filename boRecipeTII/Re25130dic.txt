Tomato-Cilantro Salsa
Fresh Cilantro, Shallot, Red Bell Pepper, Clarified Butter, Red wine, Salt And Pepper, Roma Tomatoes, Garlic, 
Saute shallot, garlic and bell pepper in butter until soft. Add red wine  and half of the cilantro and simmer until reduced by half. Add the tomatoes  and simmer slowly for about 15 minutes or until slightly thickened. Add the  rest of the cilantro, blend mixture until smooth. Season with salt and  pepper to taste.
