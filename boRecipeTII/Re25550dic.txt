Avocado Curry Soup
Curry powder, Salt to taste, Whipping cream, Avocados, peeled and cubed, Chicken Stock, White pepper to taste, 
Blend the avocados with 1 cup of the chicken stock in a food processor  until smooth. Stir in the remaining stock and the remaining ingredients.  Chill.  Garnish with sliced or cubed avocados when ready to serve. Serves 4  Bon appetit, Hope--Sunnyvale, CA
