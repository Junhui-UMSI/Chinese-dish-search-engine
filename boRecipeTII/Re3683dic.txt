Penchards Favorite Low-Fat Cream Sauce
Nutmeg, Non-dairy creamer, Egg substitute, liquid --, Non-fat cheese -- optional, Optional, Flour, Skim milk, Nonfat or low fat margarine, Salt, 
Cook flour and margarine together, stirring constantly. Add milk and  continue whisking until mixture thickens. Add salt and nutmeg. Just before  serving, add non-dairy creamer. This gives it a rich creamy flavor. For  cheese sauce, simply stir in non-fat cheese. For Bechamel sauce, add egg  substitute.
