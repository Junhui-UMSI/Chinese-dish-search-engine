Beer Cheese Bread #1
Beer, Cheddar cheese, Sugar, Fine dry bread crumbs, Self-rising flour, -(up to), Butter, 
From: Elaine Hacklaender <hack@ncrclm.clemsonsc.ncr.com>    Date:  Tue, 1 Mar 94 15:15:47 -0500    From: _A Treasury of Southern Baking_    Note:  This easy-to-make bread uses only 6 simple ingredients. Preheat the  oven to 350F.  Grease a 9x5 loaf pan and sprinkle it generously with the  dry bread crumbs.  Shake out any excess bread crumbs.  In a large mixing  bowl, combine the flour, sugar and cheese.  Stir to mix.  Add the beer and  mix well.  Pour the stiff batter into the prepared pan.  Bake for 45  minutes.  Brush with the melted butter.  Bake for an additional 10 to 15  minutes, until golden.  Serve warm, with butter.  Serves 8.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
