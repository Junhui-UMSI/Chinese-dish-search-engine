Strawberry Meringue Pie
Pecans, chopped, Vanilla, Sugar, Egg whites, room temp, Strawberries, (8 oz) container cool whip, Sugar, Crust, Ritz Crackers, crushed, FILLING, 
Crust:  Beat egg whites until fluffy.  Add sugar gradually, then vanilla.  Fold in Ritz cracker crumbs and pecans. Place in buttered 9 inch pie pan.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes. Cool    Filling:  Fold sugar and berries into whipped cream and place in cooled pie  shell.  This may be served immediately, or refrigerated for a short time  before serving.
