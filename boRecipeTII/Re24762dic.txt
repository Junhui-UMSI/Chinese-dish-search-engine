Aunt Effies Special
And pieces, Garlic powder, 15 oz can stewed tomatoes, Beans, Pepper/tomatoes, Italian seasoning, Can Rotel diced, 15 oz can mushrooms stems, 15 oz can French-cut green, 
Mix it all together in a big saucepan, bring to a boil and simmer for half  hour or so.  Serve it over spiral pasta (fusilli) and top with FF parmesan.  Delicious!
