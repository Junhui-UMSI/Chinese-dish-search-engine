Turkey and Eggplant Romano
Turkey stock; or chicken stk, Parsley; sprigs chopped (2-3, Turkey; cooked-1/2" cubes, Eggplant; peeled/diced-med., Onion; minced, Italian seasoning, Tomato paste, Romano cheese; grated, Garlic, 
Combine all ingredients except turkey and romano cheese in a large  non-stick skillet. (or other skillet, sprayed with Pam) Simmer uncovered,  stirring frequently, until eggplant is tender and most of the liquid has  evaporated (10 to 12 minutes). Stir in turkey and heat through. Sprinkle  with cheese and serve immediately. 175 calories per person From The Daily  Breeze - Food Section
