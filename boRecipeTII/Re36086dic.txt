Beef Rib Roast W/horseradish & Walnut Sa
Beef rib roast, Horseradish, SAUCE, BEEF, Whipping cream, Lemon juice, Walnuts; toasted, chopped, Sugar, Hot English mustard, Kale (optional), 
From: gemini@synapse.net (Georgette & Dave Burnside)     Date: Wed, 07 Feb 1996 04:06:07 GMT     FOR SAUCE: Beat cream, horseradish, lemon juice and sugar in bowl to  medium peaks. Season with salt and pepper. Continue beating until firm  peaks form. Fold in walnuts. Cover and chill.     FOR BEEF: Preheat oven to 425F.  Place beef in roasting pan.  Spread 3  tablespoons mustard over.  Roast 45 minutes.  Cover loosely with foil;  continue roasting until it reaches 125F.  for rare, about 1 hour 5 minutes.  Remove from oven. Loosely tent foil over roast.  Let stand 30 minutes.  Transfer roast to platter. Garnish with kale, if desired.  Serve, passing  horseradish sauce and additional mustard.
