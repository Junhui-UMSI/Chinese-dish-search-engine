Pepper Steak--Old Bay Seasoning Version
Sliced green pepper, Salt, Baltimore Spice Old Bay, Coarsely chopped celery, Mushrooms, Butter or margarine, Soy sauce, Sliced onion, Cornstarch, Round steak; cut in thin, Water, 
In skillet, brown round steak slightly in butter or margarine. Add  vegetables, salt and Baltimore Spice Old Bay Seasoning. Stir fry about 5  minutes. Mix corn starch with water and soy sauce. Pour over vegetables.  Stir until sauce is thick and slightly transparent. Serve over rice. Makes        6    servings    Recipe by: Old Bay Seasoning Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #731 by  L979@aol.com on Aug 9, 1997
