Banana Fudge Topping
Margarine, banana, Light corn syrup, Cocoa, 
In blender, puree banana, peeled (to yield 1/3 cup). Add light corn syrup,  cocoa and melted margarine; process until smooth.    Recipe by: BestRecipes (April, 1993)    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #894 by "Eugene Johnston"  <ejohnston@mailexcite.com> on Nov 09, 1997
