Pesto-Alla Mario
Pine nuts, Fresh basil leaves, Ligurian extra virgin olive, Freshly grated parmigiano, Sea salt, Garlic, Freshly grated pecorino, 
In a large stone mortar, place pine nuts, basil, garlic and salt and grind  with a pestle until paste. Add cheeses and drizzle in olive oil, beating  with a wooden spoon. Store in jars, topped with extra virgin olive oil.    Recipe by: Molto Mario    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #506 by Sue <suechef@sover.net> on Mar 08,  1997.
