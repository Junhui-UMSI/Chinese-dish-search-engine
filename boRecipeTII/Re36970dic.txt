Strawberry-Banana Breakfast Treat
Ice cubes, Fresh strawberries; hulled, Orange juice, Banana; cut into pieces, Nonfat dry milk powder, 
In food processor or blender, combine all ingredients except ice.  Cover  and blend on high speed.  With machine running, drop in ice, one cube at a  time.  Blend about 10 seconds or until thick and frothy.  To serve, pour  into glasses.    Per serving: About 60 cal, 2 g pro, 13 g car, 0 g fat, 0% cal from fat, 1  mg chol, 19 mg sod.
