Thai Eggplant Relish - Country Living
Clove garlic; finely chopped, Light soy sauce, Finely chopped fresh, Oriental sesame oil, Sugar, Fresh lime juice, Fish sauce; (see Note) OR, Round green-striped Thai, 
1. In medium howl, combine lime juice, fish sauce, sesame oil, garlic  jalapeno, and sugar.    2. Rinse eggplants and pat dry. Cut off each stem end and a thin slice of  bottom. Cut trimmed eggplants crosswise into 1/4@-inch-thick slices. Add  eggplants to lime mixture; toss well. Refrigerate, covered, 1 hour to  marinate before serving.    Note: Fish sauce-also called nam pla, nuoc mam, and petis-is a clear  light-brown seasoning sauce prepared from salted, fermented fish. Used in  Southeast Asian cooking, bottles of fish sauce can be purchased in Asian  markets.    Nutritional information per serving-protein-. .7 gram; fat: 3 grams;  carbohydrate: 6 grams; fiber: 2 grams; sodium: 173 milligrams; cholesterol:  0; calo- ries: 56.
