Vanilla Sauce #1
Salt, Heavy cream, Sugar, Flour, Egg yolks, Vanilla extract, Scoops vanilla ice cream, 
Combine the cream and sugar in a 2-quart saucepan and bring just to a boil.  Remove from the heat. In a bowl, beat the egg yolks, flour, vanilla and  salt. Stir in a little of the hot cream. Add this mixture to the rest of  the hot cream. Return the sauce to the heat and cook over low heat,  stirring constantly with a wooden spoon until just thickencd. Do not let  the mixture get too hot or the egg yolks will coagulate and curdle. Remove  from the heat and add the ice cream, stirring until melted. Strain the  sauce through a fine sieve. The sauce may be served hot or cold. Makes 1  quart. SERVE with "Plantation Bread Pudding" (see recipe in this cookbook).
