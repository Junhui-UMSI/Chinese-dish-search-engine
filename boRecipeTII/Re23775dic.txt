Arugula & Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese Sal
25 year old balsamic vinegar, Proscuitto di parma; thinly, Salt and pepper, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Arugula; cleaned and stemmed, Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, 
in a small mixing bowl, whisk the olive oil and vinegar together. Season  with salt and pepper.  Toss the greens with the dressing. Lay the  proscuitto on the platter. Mound the greens in the center of the  proscuitto.  SHave the Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese over the greens. Garnish  with a couple of turns of black pepper.
