Porter Beef (Irish)
Seasoned flour, Guinness or other stout, Onions, Stock or water, Pinch of nutmeg, Salt and pepper, Mushrooms, Rib steak, Fat for frying, 
Cut meat into fairly large chunks and roll in seasoned flour.  Brown in  hot fat and remove from pot.  Chop onions roughly and fry gently for 2-3  minutes.  Return meat to pot.  Add all other ingredients.  Bring to the  boil, reduce heat and simmer gently for 2 hours or until meat is tender.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/irish.zip
