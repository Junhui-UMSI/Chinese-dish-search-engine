Vidalia Onions with Cheese Sauce
Worcestershire, Salt, Hard-boiled eggs; grated, Grated cheese, Butter, Vidalia onions, Flour, milk, Paprika and parsley to taste, 
Peel and wash onions; cook in boiling, salted water until tender. Drain  and cool. Place onions in serving dish. Set aside.     In top of double boiler, blend butter and flour until smooth; add liquid  gradually, stirring constantly. Add grated cheese and Worcestershire sauce.  Cover tightly and place over hot water until ready to use. Pour over  Vidalia onions. Garnish with grated eggs, parsley and paprika, and serve.
