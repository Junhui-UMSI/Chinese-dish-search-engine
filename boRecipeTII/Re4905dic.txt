Pickled Eggs (Crocker)
Salt, Shredded greens, Beet liquid, Cloves, Onion, Hard-cooked eggs, Granulated sugar or packed,, Cider vinegar, 
Place peeled eggs in bowl or jar. Mix vinegar, beet liquid sugar, salt,  onion and cloves; pour over eggs. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 days.  Slice eggs; serve on greens. 6 servings;145 calories per serving.    Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #492 by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on  Mar 02, 1997.
