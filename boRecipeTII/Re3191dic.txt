Pear and Ginger Crisp
Rolled oats, TOPPING, Butter; cut into small, (16-oz. each) sliced pears, All-purpose flour, Packed light brown sugar, Ground ginger, Minced. crystallized ginger, 
Heat oven to 400. Arrange pears in a shallow 1 1/2-quart baking dish;  sprinkle with crystallized ginger. Combine, oats, brown sugar, flour and  ground ginger in food processor bowl. With motor running, gradually add  butter and process until mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle topping mixture  evenly over pears. Bake in center of oven until topping is lightly browned  and crisp, 20 to 25 minutes. Serve with vanilla ice cream or frozen yogurt.  Serves 6.
