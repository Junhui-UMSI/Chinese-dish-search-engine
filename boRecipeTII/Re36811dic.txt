Strawberry Nut Drops
Almond extract, Butter or margarine, Baking soda, All purpose flour, Egg, Sweet Strawberries, mashed *, Walnuts, Baking Powder, Sugar, 
* or 10 oz pkg sweetened strawberries, mashed     Remove 2 T of the crushed strawberries with juice and set aside.   Cream sugar, butter, and almond extract until creamy. Beat in egg. Mix  flour with baking powder and soda and add to creamed mixture alternately  with crushed strawberries. Stir in walnuts.   Drop by Tablespoons onto lightly greased, or Teflon coated cookie sheets,  2" apart.   Bake in 375 deg F oven for 12-14 minutes. Mix reserved berries with about  3/4 c confectioners sugar to make a thin glaze and spread over warm or  cold cookies.   Makes 40-45.
