Pineapple Marshmallow Col
SALAD DRESSING #2 1/2, VINEGAR CIDER, MUSTARD PREP. 1 LB JAR, Salt, MARSHMALLOW; WHITE, MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, PINEAPPLE SLICED #10, CABBAGE WHITE FRESH, WATER; WARM, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, 
1.  TRIM, WASH AND PREPARE CABBAGE AS DIRECTED ON RECIPE CARD M00001.    2.  RECONSTITUTE MILK; AND SALAD DRESSING, MUSTARD, SALT, AND SUGAR; MIX  WELL.    3.  ADD VINEGAR GRADUALLY BLEND WELL.    4.  COMBINE FINELY SHREDED CABBAGE AND CANNED, DRAINED PINEAPPLES CHUNKS OR  TIDBITS.  POUR DRESSING OVER CABBAGE AND PINEAPPLE; TOSS LIGHTLY UNTIL WELL  MIXED.    5.  COVER; REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.  JUST BEFORE SERVING, ADD  MINIATURE MARSHMALLOWS.    NOTE:  IN STEP 1, 12 LB 8 OZ CABBAGE A.P. WILL YIELD 10 LB FINELY SHREDDED  CABBAGE.
