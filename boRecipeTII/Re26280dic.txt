Tomato and Courgette Soup
1 lb courgettes, 1 tsp sugar., Handful of basil, chopped., 2 tsp sunflower oil, Ground black pepper., Cloves garlic, crushed., Three quarters pt water., 1 tbsp wine vinegar., Onion, peeled and chopped., 14 oz can of tomatoes, 2 tbsp tomato puree, 
Heat the oil in a large saucepan and cook the onion and garlic until soft,  stirring frequently, about 5 to 8 minutes. Add the tomatoes, courgettes,  water, tomato puree, sugar, basil, vinegar and black pepper. Simmer for 20  to 25 minutes until all the vegetables are tender. Puree the soup in a  blender and serve.    88 Calories 366 kj 4,1 g protein 12,5 g carbohydrate of which 7,0 g sugar.  3,0 g fat of which 0,3 g saturates. Trace of sodium 1,8 g dietary fibre.
