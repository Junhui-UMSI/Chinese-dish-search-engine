Cauliflower Snowman
Cauliflowerets, Diced white onions, Butter or margarine, All-purpose flour, milk, (4 oz.) shredded Swiss, Salt, Garlic salt, White pepper, Whole ripe olives, Baby carrot, 
In a large saucepan, cook cauliflower in a small amount of water for 6-7  minutes or until crisp-tender; drain well. In a medium saucepan, saute  onion in butter for 2 minutes. Stir in flour until blended; gradually add  milk.  Bring to a boil; boil for 2 minutes, stirring in cheese until  melted.  Add salt, garlic salt and pepper. Place half of the cauliflower in  a greased 2 1/2 quart baking dish; top with half of the sauce. Repeat.  Bake, uncovered, at 350F for 20 minutes or until bubbly. Add olives for  eyes and mouth and a carrot for the nose. Yield: 6-8 servings Typed in  MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Quick Cooking Nov/Dec 98.
