Pirates Chocolate Pie
Nine inch pie shell, Vanilla extract, Eggs, Margarine, Melted chocolate, Flour, Salt, Bourbon, Minced walnuts, Sugar, 
Melt margarine and chocolate together in stainless steel bowl. Mix all  other ingredients together in a large bowl.    Add melted margarine and chocolate to other ingredients. Pour into pie  shell and cook for 50 minutes at 375 degrees F.    Chill and serve with whipped cream.
