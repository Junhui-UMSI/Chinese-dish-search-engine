Backpacker Bars
Whole almonds, Wheat germ, Shredded coconut, Eggs;  lightly beaten, Butter, Whole wheat flour, Quick cooking oats, Chopped dried apricots, Brown sugar, White flour, Chopped dates, Chocolate chips, Orange Peel, 
Preheat oven to 350. Cream butter with 1 cup brown sugar. Stir in oats,  wheat flour, white flour, wheat germ, and orange peel. Press mixture into  bottom of an ungreased 9 x13-inch baking pan. Combine eggs, almonds,  chocolate chips, dates, apricots, coconut and remaining 1/2 cup. brown  sugar. Mix gently, but thoroughly. Pour over butter mixture. Spread evenly.  Bake 30-35 minutes and cool before cutting into bars.
