Almond Apricot Coffee Cake
Baking Powder, Eggs, Almond extract, Butter, Sugar, Slivered almonds, All-purpose flour, Salt, Jar apricot preserves, Sour cream, 
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, sour cream and extract;  mix well. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Add to creamed mixture  and mix well. Spread half of the batter in a greased and floured 12-cup  fluted tube pan. Sprinkle with half of the almonds. Spread half of the  preserves to within 1/2 inch of the edges. Cover with remaining batter.  Spoon remaining preserves over batter to within 1/2 inch of egdes. Sprinkle  with remaining almonds. Bake at 350 for 55-60 minutes. Cool in pan for 15  minutes.
