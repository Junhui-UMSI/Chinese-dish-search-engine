Arnauds Sweet Potato and Andouille Soup
Andouille sausage; medium, Praline liqueur, Onions; medium dice, Cream, Brown sugar, Whole butter; divided, Chicken Stock, Salt, Sweet potatoes; medium dice, Dried whole thyme, 
1. Render Andouille sausage in 1 oz. butter.    2. Add onions, saute until translucent.    3. Add dried thyme and sweet potatoes, saute for 5 minutes.    4. Deglaze pan with praline liqueur.    5. Add chicken stock and brown sugar.    6. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes.    7. Puree in a blender, finishing with heavy cream, 1 oz. whole butter and  salt. Serve hot.    Notes: Yield:6 servings (6 cups)    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 179 by jecraig@lan-inc.com on  Oct 29, 97
