Sweet Biscuit Ring
milk, Margarine or butter; melted, Glaze; see recipe, Margarine or butter;, Ground cinnamon, Brown sugar, Sour cream, Bisquick baking mix, Finely chopped nuts, 
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Grease 9" round pan.    Mix baking mix, sour cream and milk until soft dough forms.    Gently smooth dough into a ball on cloth-covered board dusted with baking  mix. Roll into rectangle 12x6 inches. Spread with 2 Tbsp. softened  margarine.    Mix brown sugar, nuts and cinnamon; sprinkle over dough.    Roll up tightly, beginning at 12" side. Seal well by pinching edge of dough  into roll. Cut roll into twelve 1" slices.    Place in circle in pan, overlapping slices.    Drizzle with 2 Tbsp. melted margarine,    Bake until golden brown, about 15 minutes. Cool 30 minutes.    Glaze.
