To Bake Peaches
Honey, Coriander, Lemon juice; or lime, TOPPING, Ground ginger, Melted margarine, 
Blend. Brush the mixture over 3 halved, pitted and peeled peaches. Sprinkle  with the spicy topping. Bake at 325 degrees F until tender, about 20  minutes.
