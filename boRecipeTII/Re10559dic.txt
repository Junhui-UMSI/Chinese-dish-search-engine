Quick Meat Sauce for Spaghetti
Clove Garlic, Pressed, Ground beef, Beef stock, Parmesan cheese, Bay leaf, Tomato paste, Basil and/or Oregano, olive oil, Pepper, Lean Pork, Ground, Salt, Plum Tomatoes, Mushrooms, Sliced, 
Heat olive oil in a medium sized pot. When hot, add garlic, meat, tomatoes,  tomato paste, stock, salt, pepper and the bay leaf. Simmer uncovered for at  least one hour. Add the mushrooms during the last 15 minutes. Season with  basil and/or oregano and serve over pasta. Grate parmesan cheese over each  serving.
