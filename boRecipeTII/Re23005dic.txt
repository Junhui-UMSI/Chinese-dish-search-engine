Trout Elmere
Seasoned bread crumbs, Romano cheese; grated, Tomato; minced, Celery; minced, olive oil, Trout filet, Strips anchovies, 
saute minced celery in olive oil. Melt anchovies; add seasoned bread  crumbs. Simmer until almost dry. Add grated Romano cheese. This is the  Elmere dressing. Salt and pepper trout filet. Spread Elmere dressing on  trout; roll. Broil in lemon butter sauce, adding minced tomato.    DELMONICOS       1300    ST. CHARLES AVENUE    NEW ORLEANS, LA    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
