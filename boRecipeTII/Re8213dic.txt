Potato Hominy Soup
Green chiles; peeled and, Cilantro; minced, Onion; thinly sliced, Garlic, Vegetable broth, Cumin, olive oil, Red potatoes; peeled and, Yellow hominy; canned, 
Heat oil in a medium size pot, and saute onion and garlic for 3 minutes.  Add cumin, stir well, then add potatoes and green chilies. Cook over medium  heat, stirring occasionally, another 5 minutes. Add vegetable broth and  hominy. Simmer the soup, covered, over low heat for 45 minutes. Stir in  fresh cilantro. Serve hot.
