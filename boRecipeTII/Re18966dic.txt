Angel of Death Cheese Spread
Cheese cloth, Garlic, Salt to taste, Walnuts, Fresh sage leaves, Ricotta cheese, Gorganzola or Bleu cheese, 
Date: 14 Mar 1996 21:41 GMT    From: Mark Alexander <mark@alexr.co.uk>    Chop garlic. Add to 1/4 C of water in a small saucepan. Reduce to 2  tablespoons.  Beat cheeses together. Add garlic mixture and salt, if  desired. In a double layer of cheesecloth - put sage leaves in a pattern in  the center. Sprinkle nuts on top. Put cheese mixture on top of nuts. Gather  cheesecloth and form into a ball. Tie together. Put the cheeseball in a  strainer over a dish and let sit in the fridge overnight to drain. Unwrap  and serve!
