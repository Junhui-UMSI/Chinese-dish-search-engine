Apple Fantasy Dessert
Egg whites, Salt, Vanilla, Sugar, Flour, Apples, cut fairly small,, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, Skins on is fine., 
Beat eggs, add sugar and vanilla and beat well.  Add dry ingredients and  mix.  Dump in apples and stir until evenly distributed.  Bake in a deep  baking dish,  45 minutes at 350.  Serve with custard.    Posted by skill@qucis.queensu.ca (David Skillicorn) to the Fatfree Digest  [Volume 11 Issue 25], Oct. 25, 1994. FATFREE Recipe collections copyrighted  by Michelle Dick 1994.
