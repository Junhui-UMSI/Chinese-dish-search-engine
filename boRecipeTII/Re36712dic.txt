Strawberry Frosty
Strawberry Flavored Gelatin;, Cold Milk, Strawberry Ice Cream, ;Boiling Water, Fresh Strawberries;, GARNISH, 
Put the strawberry flavored gelatine and the boiling water into a blender  container and blend at low speed until the gelatin is dissolved. Pour 1/2 c  of the gelatin mixture into a glass measuring c and set aside. Add half the  milk to the remaining mixture in the blender and blend on low speed until  mixed.  Add half the strawberry ice cream and blend until smooth. Pour into  tall glasses. Repeat with the reserved gelatin mixture, milk, and ice  cream.  Garnish each glass with a fresh strawberry, if desired
