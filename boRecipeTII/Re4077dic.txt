Peppermint Stick Sauce
Peppermint candies; finely, Whipping cream, Marshmallow cream, 
In a medium saucepan, combine all ingredients.  Stirring constantly with a  wooden spoon, cook over medium hear until smooth.  Remove from heat.  Pour  into an airtight container and refrigerate.    Serve chilled over ice cream or cake, or a dipping sauce for cookies or  fruits.
