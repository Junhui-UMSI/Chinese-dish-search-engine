3 Bean Salad - Mexican Style
Green beans, Salt, Kidney beans, Sugar, Cider vinegar, Green pepper; chopped, Pepper, Red onion; chopped, Garbanzo beans, Salad oil, Taco seasoning mix, 
Drain canned beans, combine with green pepper and onion. Set aside. Combine  remaining ingredients, except taco seasoning. Pour over vegetables, mixing  well. Cover and marinate in refrigerator overnight or 24 hours, stirring  occasionally.
