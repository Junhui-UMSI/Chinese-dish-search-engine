Almond Crescents
Powdered Sugar,sifted, Salt, Oats,uncooked, Powdered sugar, Butter, Flour, Almonds,finely chopped, Almond extract, 
Heat oven to 325 degress F. Beat butter and almond extract till  fluffy;gradually beat in sugar. Add combined flour and salt;mix well. Stir  in oats and almonds. Shape to form crescents. Place on ungreased cookie  sheet. Bake 15-18 minutes or until light golden brown. Sift powdered sugar  over warm crescents.
