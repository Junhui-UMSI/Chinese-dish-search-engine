Thai Iced Coffee
Water, Sugar To Taste, Ice Cubes, Ground Coffee, Evaporated milk, Cardamom, 
Using amounts specified, brew coffee in a drip-style coffee maker or  percolator.  Fill four 10 to 12 ounce beverage glasses to the rim with ice  cubes; then fill two thirds full with coffee. Into each glass, stir about 2  tablespoons evaporated milk; add a dash of ground cardamom and sweeten to  taste with sugar. Typed by Syd Bigger.
