Tuna-Egg Patties
Paprika, Salt, Celery salt, Butter, Onion, Pepper, Corn Flakes, Eggs, Parsley, Flour, Water, Pimiento, milk, Tuna, 
Grease 8x8x2 inch baking dish.  Melt butter in pan and blend in flour, salt  and pepper.  Cook 2 minutes. Stir in milk and cook until thick. Stir in  tuna, pimiento, onion, parsley, salt, paprika. Spread in dish. Refrigerate  1 hour.  Cut in 9 squares. Coat with flour. Shape into patties. Beat eggs  and water. Dip patties into egg. Coat with crushed flakes. Fry in hot oil  for 5 minutes on each side over medium heat. Serve with hot chili sauce.  From Gourmet, formatted by Mary Dishongh Bowles.
