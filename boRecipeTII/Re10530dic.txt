Quick Jambalaya
Chicken-flavor rice mix, Butter, Onion, -canned luncheon meat; diced, Salt, Green pepper; diced, Cooked ham; diced or, Water; hot, Pepper, Tuna; drained, Celery; diced, 
Saute rice in butter until lightly browned.  Add comtents of spice packet  that comes with rice mixture and the hot water. Stir to blend. Add  remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer over low heat approximately 15  minutes.
