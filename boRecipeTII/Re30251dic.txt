Banana Bread 8
Sugar, Mashed very ripe bananas (2, Tap salt, Eggs, Bakinbg powder, Flour, Shortening, milk, Baking soda, Chopped nuts (I like walnuts, 
In large mixer bowl, combine 1 cup of the flour, sugar, baking powder,  soda, and salt.  Add mashed bananas, shortening, and milk.  Beat on low til  blended and then on high for 2 minutes.   Add eggs and remaining flour and  beat just til blended.  Stir in nuts.    Pour batter into a greased 8x4x2-inch loaf pan.  Bake at 350F for 55 to 60  minutes.  (Done when tester comes out clean when inserted near center.)    Cool in pan on rack for 10 minutes.  Turn out of pan and cool completely on  rack.  The recipe says its best when wrapped and stored overnight, but  These usually either get stored in the freezer til gift time, or they get  cut into as soon as they are cool enough to hold onto and cut.
