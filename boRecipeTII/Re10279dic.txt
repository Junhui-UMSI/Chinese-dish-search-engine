Quick Bacon-Spinach Dip
Dried Dill; Crushed, Mayonnaise, Cayenne pepper, Imitation Bacon Bits; OR, Crisp Bacon; Crumbled, **, Frozen Chopped Spinach; *, Garlic powder, Fresh Dill; Chopped, OR, 
*    Thaw and drain one 10-oz package of frozen, chopped Spinach ** 6 or 7  slices of bacon cooked until very crisp should give you this       amount. +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++  ++++++++++++++++++ Squeeze as much water as possible from the spinach. Mix  the bacon and spinach and add the other ingredients, blending well. Cover  and chill. Makes about 2 3/4 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Apples,  Fennel, Zucchini, Monterey Jack or Cheddar Cheese Sticks, Toast Triangles,  Pears
