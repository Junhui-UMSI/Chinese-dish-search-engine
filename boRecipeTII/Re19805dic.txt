Appetizer Pizza
Extra Virgin Olive Oil, garlic, mozzarella cheese, tomatoes, Pita breads, Fresh basil, 
Separate each pita into two. Spread 1 minced garlic clove onto each pita  round. Arrange 4-6 basil leaves over garlic. Top with 3 slices of tomatoes.  Sprinkle about 2 tablespoons of shredded cheese on each and drizzle 2  teaspoons of olive oil on each.    Bake on cookie sheet in 400 degree oven for about 15 minutes or until  cheese melts and pizzas are bubbly. Serve immediately.    Makes 4 small pizzas    You found this recipe on 1st Travelers Choice Internet Cookbook.
