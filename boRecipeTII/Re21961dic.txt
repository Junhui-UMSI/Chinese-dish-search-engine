Applesauce Cake 1
Cinnamon, Salt, Flour, Cloves, Baking soda, Applesauce; unsweetened, Butter, Brown sugar; light, Nutmeg, Raisins, 
Recipe By : Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book    Mix butter, sugar, salt and spices. Add flour and applesauce in which soda  has been dissolved. Add the raisins. Pour into an oblong, well-greased pan  and bake at 350-F about 35 minutes.  Suggested Wine: Obl Dunkes Kucka    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #472 by fundwell@excel.net on Dec 27,  1997
