Apple Crisp
Cloves; (more if you like, Apples; sliced, (no need to, Sugar -or-, Oatmeal, Flour, Honey, Cinnamon; (more if you like, Margarine; melted, 
Pile apple slices in bottom of greased 9" pan. Mix remaining ingredients;  sprinkle over apples. Bake at 375o for 1 hr. Freezes well. Serve warm or  room temp.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Adina Mishkoff <adina@amcha.org> on Aug 25,  1998,
