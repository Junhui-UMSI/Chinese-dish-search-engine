Aloha Cheesecake
Macadamia Nuts, Toasted, Med Kiwi Peeled, Sliced, Sugar, Cream Cheese, Softened, Crushed Pineapple, Drained, Margarine, Melted, milk, Vanilla Wafer Crumbs, Large Eggs, 
Combine crumbs and margarine; press onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.  Bake at 350 degrees F., 10 minutes.    Combine cream cheese, sugar and milk, mixing at medium speed on electric  mixer until well blended.  Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well well after  each addition.  Stir in nuts; pour over crust.  Bake at 350 degrees F.,  45 minutes.  Loosen caek from rim of pan; cool before removing rim of pan.  Chill.  Before serving, top with fruit.
