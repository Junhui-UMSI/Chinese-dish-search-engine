Andouille Cornbread Stuffing
Green Onions, green bell peppers, fresh garlic, Salt, Black Pepper, Celery, Chicken Stock, Bayou Blast, corn, olive oil, yellow onions, andouille sausage, 
* Note: See the "Bayou Blast - {Emerils Creole Seasoning}" recipe which is  included in this collection.    Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add yellow onions and sausage  and saute 1 minute. Add green onions, celery, bell peppers and garlic and  stir-fry 1 minute. Stir in corn muffins, stock and Bayou Blast, season to  taste with salt and pepper and cook, stirring and shaking the skillet,  about 2 minutes. Remove from heat and serve with roast chicken, turkey or  pork.
