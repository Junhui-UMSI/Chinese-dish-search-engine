Quick Vegetable Medley
Garlic; minced, Fresh dill; minced, Fresh Lemon Juice, White wine, salt and pepper, Frozen mixed vegetables;, 
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and cook over medium high heat for  5-6 minutes. Remove the vegetables with a slotted spoon and keep warm.  Bring the liquid remaining in the pot to a boil. Lower the heat and reduce  by 1/3. Pour over the cooked vegetables and serve.    Per serving: calories 83, fat 0.2g, 2% calories from fat, cholesterol 0mg,  protein 2.8g, carbohydrates 12.9g, fiber 3.5g, sodium 35mg.    Exchanges: 1 Vegetable.
