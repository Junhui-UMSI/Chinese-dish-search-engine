Swiss Green Beans
Butter, Minced; dried onion, Flour, Crushed Ritz crackers, Sugar, (10-oz) frozen French cut, Pepper, Sour cream, Salt, Grated Swiss cheese, 
Over low heat melt 2 Tablespoons of butter. Add flour, salt, pepper, onion  and sugar. Stir in sour cream and green beans. Put in a 2 quart greased  casserole. Cover with cheese.  Melt remaining butter and mix with crackers.  Spread over cheese. Bake at 350? for 30 minutes.  Yield: 6 servings.    ANNE ARNOLD (MRS. SCOTT A.
