Apple-Mint Jelly
Lemon juice, Powdered fruit pectin, Apple juice, Fresh mint leaves, Sugar, Green food coloring, 
Recipe by: Sue Klapper In a Dutch oven, combine apple juice, mint leaves,  lemon juice, and pectin over medium-high heat. Bring to a rolling boil. Add  sugar. Stirring constantly, bring to a rolling boil again and boil 1  minute. Remove from heat; skim off foam. Strain mixture; discard mint  leaves. Tint jelly green. Pour into heat-resistant jars with lids. Store in  refrigerator. Yield: about 3 pints jelly. Source: Leisure Arts: Quick &  Tasty Christmas Gifts.
