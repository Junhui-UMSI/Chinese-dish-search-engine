Baked Pork Chops in Mushroom Burgundy
Butter, Burgundy, Flour, Egg yolk, milk, pork chops, Onion, fresh mushrooms, Sour cream, 
From:    Patricia Moore <PCPJMOOR@IHC.COM>    Date:    Thu, 11 Jul 1996 18:14:31 -0700  Saute pork in butter until brown, remove from skillet and place in shallow  buttered baking dish.  Saute onion and mushrooms, sprinkle with flour and  salt and pepper. Blend in burgundy until thickened. Pour over pork, cover.  Bake 350 degree oven 1 hour or simmer med heat 45 min. Remove pork and keep  warm. Blend into sauce the egg yolk, sour cream and milk. Cook and stir  without boiling until thickened. Pour over pork.
