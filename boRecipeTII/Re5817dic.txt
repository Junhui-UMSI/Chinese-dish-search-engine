Pinto Party Pate
Tabasco sauce, Cream cheese; (3 oz.), Chopped parsley, Minced green onion, Garlic salt, Worcestershire Sauce, Mashed cooked Idaho pinto, Chopped green Chilies, Salt, 
Combine pinto beans with cream cheese, onion, chilies and seasonings. Chill  In a bowl lined with wax paper. Shape into a ball. Roll In chopped parsley.  Serve as a spread with crisp crackers. Yields: 8 to 10 servings  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 456 by "Diane Geary."  <diane@keyway.net> on Jan 5, 1998
