Blueberry Barbecue Sauce
seeded and chopped, Dark brown sugar, olive oil, Fresh blueberries, Dijon mustard, Onions, Rice wine vinegar, Water, 
Saute the onions and jalapeno in olive oil over medium-high heat in a small  saucepan until limp, 2 to 3 minutes. Add the remaining ingredients and cook  at a low boil for 15 minutes, stirring often. Puree the sauce in a blender  or food processor until smooth.
