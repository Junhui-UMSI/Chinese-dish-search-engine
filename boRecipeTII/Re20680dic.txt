Apple Crisp (W/graham Crackers)
Granny Smith apples, Cinnamon, Allspice, Butter, Flour, Graham cracker, Brown sugar, Rolled oats, 
Prepare enough apple slices to fill 8" microwavable dish 3/4 full. Finely  chop graham crackers. Stir together crumbs, sugar, oats, flour and spices.  Add butter; mix well. Spoon mixture over apple slices. Microwave on High  12-15 min. or until apples are tender, turning dish after 6 min. Cool  slightly. Serve with whipped cream or ice cream.    Oven variation: Preheat oven to 350 ; bake for 30-35 min. or until apples  are tender. Serves: 6-8    Source: Pampered Chef    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #459 by Joan Kozik  <kozik@mail.utexas.edu> on Dec 18, 1997
