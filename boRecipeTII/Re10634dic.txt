Quick Prawn Toasts
Sesame seeds., fresh coriander, Egg, prawns, Sesame, fresh coriander, bread, Salt and pepper., Spring onion, 
1. Beat the egg in a shallow dish and add the spring onion, prawns and  coriander. Season the mixture with salt and pepper. 2. Place the slice of  bread in the egg mixture and allow it to soak for 2 to 3 minutes turning it  over once so that the egg is absorbed. Scoop the chopped prawns onto the  top surface, spread out evenly and sprinkle with the sesame seeds. 3. Heat  the sesame or vegetable oil in a small frying pan. Using a fish slice, flip  the slice of bread into the pan so that the topping faces downwards in  order to set it. Turn over after 1 minute and then cook the other side. 4.  Sere hot cut into triangles and garnished with sprigs of fresh coriander.  Preparation 10 minutes, cooking 3 minutes.
