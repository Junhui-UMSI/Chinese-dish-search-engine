Anna M. Stamplecoskies Potato Dumplings
Flour, Water, Salt to taste, Cream, Raw potatoes, 
Grate potatoes finely.  Squeeze dry in a clean cloth. Add flour and mix  well with potatoes.  Bring water to the boil and add salt. Make little  balls of the flour/potato mix. Drop into the boiling water and boil for 16  minutes.  At the last minute, add cream.
