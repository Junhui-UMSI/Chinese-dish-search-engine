Beef Yakitori
Sugar, Green onions(finely chopped), Vegetable oil, Lemon juice, Soy sauce, Ginger (ground), Garlic clove (crushed), Sesame seeds, Beef sirloin(finely chopped), 
in 9x5 inch loaf pan, combine soy sauce, lemon juice, garlic, ginger,  oil, sesame seeds and onion. Thread meat on 18 to 20 six inch bamboo  skewers, pushing the skewer in and out as though sewing. Place skewered  meat in marinade, turn to coat all sides. Cover and refriderate 4 hours;  drain.  Arrange on broiler pan and broil 5 to 8 inches from heating  element.  Broil 1.5 to 2 minutes; turn to broil 1 minute longer. Serve hot.
