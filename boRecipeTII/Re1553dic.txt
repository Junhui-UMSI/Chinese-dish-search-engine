Burdock Frittata
Eggs, Parsley, Roamano cheese, Cardoon, Garlic, Salt/pepper, 
Clean and wash cardoon thoroughly, cut into 2" pieces. Boil until tender,  drain well.    Put olive oil in fry pan, add 1 clove garlic and saute for a few minutes,  remove garlic. Add cardoon and sate about 10 minutes. Mix eggs, parsley and  cheese. Pour mixture over cardoon. Fry as an omelet, then flip, using a  round dinner plate to brown on other side. Cut in wedges. Serve with crusty  Italian bread.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  03, 1998
