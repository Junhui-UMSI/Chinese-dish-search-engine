Pecan Pie Bars
Pecans, Light corn syrup, Butter, Salt, Butter, Eggs, Sugar, Flour, Sugar, Vanilla, 
CRUST: Beat Flour, 1 cup butter, 1/2 cup sugar and salt until mixture  resembles crumbs.  Heavily grease bottom and sides of 15"x10" baking pan at  least 1-1/2" deep. Press mixture firmly into pan and bake at 350 degrees  for 20 minutes. FILLING: Blend eggs, corn syrup, 1-1/2 cups sugar, 3  tablespoons butter and vanilla. Stir in chopped pecans. Spread over hot  crust. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or until set. Cool, cut into  bars.
