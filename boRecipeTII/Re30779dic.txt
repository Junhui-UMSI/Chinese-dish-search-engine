Tangerine Chicken
Butter, Salt and pepper, Water, Orangejuice, frozen concent., Flour, Cinnamon, Skinless, boneless chicken, .breast halves, Tangerine peel, grated, Tangerines,peeled & sectiond, 
In a large skillet, melt butter. Add chicken, salt and pepper. Brown  chicken on both sides until chicken in thoroughly cooked. Remove and set  aside.    Add flour to the skillet. Cook over low heat about 1-2 minutes. Add orange  juice concentrate, water and cinnamon. Simmer, stirring until sauce is  thickened.    Return chicken to skillet, spooning sauce over it. Cover and simmer 5  minutes or until heated through. Add tangerine sections and zest. Stir and  serve.
