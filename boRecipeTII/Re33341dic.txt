Basic Sweet Dough
Egg, Salt, Warm water, All purpose flour; (up to 3), Butter, Active dry yeast, Sugar, Milk; scalded, 
Soften yeast in warm water. In mixing bowl, combine sugar, butter, salt and  milk. Cool to lukewarm. Stir in egg and yeast. Gradually add flour to form  a stiff dough.    No-Knead Method: Cover; let stand in warm place until doubled, about 1 to    1-1/2    hours. Toss on well-floured surface until no longer sticky.    Knead Method: Knead on floured surface until smooth and satiny, 5 to 8  minutes. Place in greased bowl, turning dough to grease all sides. Cover;  let rise in warm place until doubled, about 1 to 112 hours.    Turn out on floured surface. Shape as desired for one of the following  recipes.
