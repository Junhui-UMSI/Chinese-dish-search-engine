Vermicelli with Meat Sauce and Feta Cheese
Can Tomato Paste, Fresh Basil -- chopped -or-, Vermicelli, Onion -- chopped, Salt and pepper, Feta Cheese -- crumbled, olive oil, Ground beef, Sugar, Can Italian Plum Tomatoes --, Dried basil, 
Saute the onion in the olive oil over medium heat, then add the ground beef  and saute until meat is browned and broken up. Add the tomatoes and tomato  paste.  Mix well, then add the salt, pepper, sugar, and basil and let  simmer.  Add the cheese to the sauce just before serving.    Recipe By     :
