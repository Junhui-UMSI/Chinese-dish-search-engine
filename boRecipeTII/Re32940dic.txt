Sweet Success Rice Pudding
Bag success rice, cooked, Thawed whipped topping, milk, Sugar, Vanilla, 
Prepare rice according to pack directions. Place rice in med pan. Add  milk & sugar; stir. Bring to a boil over med high heat. Reduce heat  to medium; simmer until thick & creamy, about 15 minutes. Remove from  heat. Pour into med bowl.  Stir in vanilla; cool.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
