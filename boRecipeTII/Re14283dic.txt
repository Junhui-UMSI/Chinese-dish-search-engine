White Wine Sauce
Pepper, Green onions, Lemon juice, White wine vinegar, Celery salt, Heavy cream, Sticks butter, Salt, Dry white wine, 
In a small, heavy saucepan, combine vinegar, wine, and onions.  Simmer  until it is almost a glaze. Remove from heat. Add cream. Return to low heat  and whisk in butter, 1 piece at a time. Add each additional piece of butter  before previous one has completely melted, stirring constantly. Do not let  sauce get too hot or it will liquify.  Add salt, pepper, lemon juice, and  celery salt.  Pour over warm seafood and serve immediately.  Makes 1 cup.  Source:  Jambalya; The Junior League of New Orleans, La.
