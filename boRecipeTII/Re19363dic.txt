Vegetable-Meat Casserole
Butter, Ground beef, Onion, (16-oz) mixed vegetables, Toast; crumbled, (10-oz) cream of chicken, Grated cheese, 
Saute onion and meat in butter. Add vegetables and soup. Put in buttered  casserole. Top with cheese and bread crumbs. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
