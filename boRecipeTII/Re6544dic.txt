Poached Pears with Cinnamon
Dry red wine, Hard pears, Cinnamon, Sugar, 
Peel pear 2/3 way down from stem, leave upper 1/3 peel to stem and trim  pear bottoms so they will sit flat. Mix together sugar and cinnamon in  medium saucepan over medium heat and stir to dissolve sugar. Set pears in  pan so they arent touching and bring liquid to boil. Reduce heat to  medium-low and cover pan. Simmer until pears are soft. When soft, carefully  remove with slotted spoon. Also remove cinnamon stick. Reduce liquid until  syrupy.
