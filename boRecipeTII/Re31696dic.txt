Swiss, Ham and Spinach Bake
Boiled ham, diced or 1 cup, Onions, French-fried onions, Smoked, pressed ham, Salt, Pkg frozen chopped spinach, Chopped pimento, Dry mustard, Eggs, Shredded Swiss cheese, Dried basil, crushed, Butter, milk, 
Cook spinach and drain well.  Stir in butter until melted. In a medium  bowl, combine the eggs, milk, onion, pimento, mustard, basil and salt. Stir  in ham, cheese and spinach. Pour mixture into a buttered 10x6x2-inch baking  dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes or until knife inserted comes  out clean. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving. Sprinkle top with  French-fried onions.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Apr 23, 1998
