Piri Piri I
Fresh jalapeno peppers;, Fresh poblano peppers;, Garlic, Finely-chopped fresh, Salt, olive oil, Freshly-ground black pepper, Kosher salt, Crushed red pepper, 
In a saucepan, combine the olive oil, peppers, crushed red pepper, salt,  and black pepper. Bring the liquid up to a boil and reduce the heat. Simmer  the sauce for 4 minutes and remove from the heat. Stir in the garlic. With  a hand held mixer, puree the sauce until smooth. At this point, allow the  sauce to sit for 1 week under refrigeration before serving. Stir in the  cilantro and kosher salt.
