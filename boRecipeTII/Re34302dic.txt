Bbq Sauce for Pork
Maple syrup, Soy sauce, 
Half a cup of soy sauce, half a cup of maple syrup and about a  quarter of cup of Colemans mustard++maybe with a little vinegar.  Apply to racks of pork ribs.  BBQ.  Enjoy...    Posted by Stephen Ceideberg; April 19 1992.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/cberg2.zip
