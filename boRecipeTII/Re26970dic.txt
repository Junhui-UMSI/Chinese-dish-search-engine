Baked Cheese Dip
Monterey Jack cheese; shred, Garlic, Mayonnaise, Artichokes; chopped, 10" round sourdough bread, Cheddar cheese; grated, Hollowed out, Onion; chopped fine, Parmesan cheese, 
Mix all the ingredients together and hollow out a round loaf of French  bread.  Put the mixture into the bread, put lid back on and bake for 1-1/4  hours at 350~.
