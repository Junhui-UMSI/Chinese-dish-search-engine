Pizza Meatballs
Pizza sauce; (10 1/2 ounce), Cubes Monterey Jack or, Pressed garlic, Egg, Onion, milk, Salt, Jar marinated artichoke, Ground beef chuck, Soft bread crumbs, 
Drain artichoke hearts and reserve oil. Place onion, garlic and 1  tablespoon reserved artichoke oil in a one cup measure. Cover with plastic  wrap. Microwave at high (100%) until onion is tender; 2 to 3 minute&  Combine with egg, crumbs, milk, salt and mix well. Add beef and mix  thoroughly. Shape into 16 balls about 11/2 inches in diameter, shaping each  around a cube of cheese. Place in a single layer in a 1 2 x 8 rectangular  dish, or a 10 inch square casserole. Microwave at high (100%) 6 to 8  minutes, rearranging after half the cooking time. Drain. Pour pizza sauce  and remaining artichoke oil over meat balls. Cover. Microwave at high  (100%) 6 to 8 minutes, rearranging after half the cooking time. Add  artichoke hearts, cover. Microwave at high (100%) 3 minutes. Makes 4  servings.
