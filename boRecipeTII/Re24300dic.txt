Triple Cheese Dip
Red Pepper Sauce, Seasoned salt, Fresh Parsley -- snipped, Softened, Cottage Cheese, Cream Cheese (8 Oz Each) --, Blue Cheese, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Combine all ingredients in large bowl. Mix until thoroughly blended. Chill  several hours before serving. Serve with fresh vegetables and crackers.
