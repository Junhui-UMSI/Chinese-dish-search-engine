Raw Carrot Relish
Salt, red cabbage, Onions, Celery seed, Allspice, Carrots, Mustard seed, Green peppers, Red peppers, White wine vinegar, Sugar, 
Put all vegetables through the coarse blade of a food chopped. Mix well &  pack into hot, sterilized jars. (Color may be dark. The hot vinegar will  restore good color.) Mix vinegar with remaining ingredients in saucepan.  Bring to a boil & simmer 10 minutes. Pour boiling vinegar mixture over  vegetables, covering completely. Cover jars. Store in refrigerator. Makes 4  quarts.    SHARYN STEPHENS    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
