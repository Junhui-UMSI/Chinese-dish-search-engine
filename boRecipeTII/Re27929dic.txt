Thousand Island Dressing #5
Catsup, Tomato puree, Dill pickle relish, Black pepper, Mayonnaise, Salt, Dry mustard, Buttermilk (or more), Hard-cooked egg; chopped, Minced pimiento, MSG (optional), Chopped green olives, Minced chives, 
Combine egg, catsup, tomato puree, mayonnaise, relish, olives, chives,  pimiento, salt, pepper, mustard and MSG and beat. Gradually beat in  buttermilk until desired consistency is reached. Makes about 4 1/2 cups    Created by: Fiasco, Marina del Rey, Calif.
