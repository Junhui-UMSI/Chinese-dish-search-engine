Bayou Grilled Tuna
Tuna steaks, MARINADE, Golden Dipt Cajun Style, 
http://www.gortons.com/recipes/grill_recipe2.html    Pour Cajun Style Seafood Marinade evenly over fish, let stand 20 to 30  minutes, turning several times. Cook on an open grill over medium-hot  coals. Baste and turn once. Fish is done when flesh is opaque.    "10 MINUTE RULE": Cook fish approximately 10 minutes for every inch of  thickness.    Serve with Tossed salad, green beans, and French bread. Recipe by Golden  Dipt/Old Bay Products  Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <<molony@scsn.net> on Feb 19, 1998
