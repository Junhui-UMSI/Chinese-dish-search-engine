Texas State Fair Chili
Onions, Paprika, Tomato sauce, canned, Flour, Black pepper, Salt, Garlic, Chili meat, Powdered cumin, Chili blend, BEST OF SHOW, 
Combine meat, flour, salt and pepper together until all flour is worked  into meat.  Heat 2 T. oil in heavy pan. Add meat mixture and saute slowly,  stirring so meat will cook thoroughly but will not get too brown. Add  onions and garlic.  Let cook together until onion and garlic are soft.   Add 2 quarts hot water and simmer for 1 hour.  Add last 5 ingredients and  simmer for about an hour, stirring often. Add additional seasoning to  taste.   When reheating, do not add water, warm slowly over low heat. Margaret  Garland    Source:  Prize Winning Recipes from the State Fair of Texas, 1976.
