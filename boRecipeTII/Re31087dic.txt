Banana Raisin Walnut Muffins
Baking soda, +2 T. buttermilk, Brown sugar; firmly packed, Golden raisins; plumped, Nonfat dry milk powder, Walnuts, +2 T. flour, Banana; ripe, mashed, Baking Powder, Nutmeg, Sweet whipped butter, melted, Egg, Less 2 t. vegetable oil, 
Preheat oven to 400. Spray 12 muffin cups with nonstick spray. In medium  mixing bowl cmbine flour, milk powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda  and nutmeg; stir to combine and set aside. In small bowl, combine  buttermilk, egg, oil, and butter and stir until blended; stir in dry  ingredients. Add banana, raisins, and walnuts and stir to combine (mixture  will be lumpy). Fill each baking cup with an equal amount of batter and  bake for 15 minutes (until golden brown and a toothpick inserted in center  comes out dry). Transfer muffins from pan to wire rack and cool. Makes 12  servings, 1 muffin each. One muffin equals 1/4 protein, 1/2 bread, 1 fat,  1/2 fruit, 1/4 milk, 30 optional calories. From WW Meals in a Minute  Cookbook.
