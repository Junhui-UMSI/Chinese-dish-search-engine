Bavarian Mint Coffee Creamer
Non-dairy coffee creamer, Confectioners sugar, Peppermint extract, Dutch process cocoa; (we, 
Combine all ingredients in a container with a tight fitting lid. Shake well  to blend. Store in airtight container and give creamer with the recipe for  Bavarian Mint Coffee. Yields 15 servings. To make Bavarian Mint Coffee: In  a mug, combine 2 tablespoons of creamer with 6 ounces of coffee.    Posted to recipelu-digest by Lynn Nelson <lynnn@erols.com> on Feb 08, 1998
