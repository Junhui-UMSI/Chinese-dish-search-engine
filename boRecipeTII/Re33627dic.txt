Swedish Pot Roast in Pressure Cooker
Cinnamon, Pepper, Water, Bay Leaves, Ginger, Onions, Red wine, Beef pot roast chuck, garlic, Vinegar, Shortening, Brown sugar, Salt, Nutmeg, 
Method: Combine nutmeg, cinnamon, ginger, salt and pepper and rub into  meat. Heat cooker and add shortening. Brown meat well on all sides. Add  onion, garlic, brown sugar dissolved in wine, water and bay leaves. Close  cover securely. Place pressure regulator on vent pipe and cook 40 minutes  with pressure regulator rocking slowly. Let pressure drop of its own  accord.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1053 by Herbert A Morisson  <huggyb@golden.net> on Jan 25, 1998
