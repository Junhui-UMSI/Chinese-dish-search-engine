Vada
Baking soda, Coriander, Green chili, Onion, Ginger, Moong dal, Urad dal, Chili powder, Salt, 
Soak urad dal and moong dal for 2-3 hours. Grind dals into a coarse paste.  Add finely chopped onion, salt, coriander powder, chili powder OR green  chili, ginger and soda. Mix well and set aside for 4-5 minutes. Fry small  spoonfuls of the mixture in hot oil.      Rajeev Krishnamoorthy, rajeev@twain.EE.CORNELL.EDU
