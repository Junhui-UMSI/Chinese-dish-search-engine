Western Omelet
BUTTER PRINT SURE, HAM SECTIONED CURED, PEPPER SWT GRN FRESH, SHORTENING; 3LB, Onions, EGGS SHELL, 
TEMPERATURE:  325 F. GRIDDLE  :    1.  SAUTE CHOPPED ONIONS, CHOPPED GREEN PEPPERS IN BUTTER OR MARGARINE  UNTIL TENDER; DRAIN THOROUGHLY. CHOP OR GRIND CANNED HAM.  COMBINE  SAUTEED ONIONS AND GREEN PEPPERS WITH CHOPPED HAM; MIX THOROUGHLY.    2.  PLACE SHELLED EGGS IN MIXER BOWL.  USING WIRE WHIP, BEAT JUST ENOUGH  TO THOROUGHLY BLEND YOLKS AND WHITES.    3.  POUR 1/3 CUP EGG MIXTURE FOR INDIVIDUAL OMELETS ON GREASED GRIDDLE.    4.  SPRINKLE 3 TBSP MIXTURE OVER EGGS WHEN PARTIALLY SET.    5.  FOLD OMELET IN HALF OR INTO THIRDS MAKING A LONG OVAL SHAPED OMELET  SERVE IMMEDIATELY.  :    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 1, 6 LB 4 OZ (5-NO. 3 CYL CN) CANNED, DEHYDRATED EGG  MIX COMBINED WITH 7 1/2 QT WARM WATER MAY BE USED FOR WHOLE EGGS.  SEE  RECIPE NO. A-8.    NOTE:  2.  TO SERVE ASSORTED OMELETS, PREPARE INGREDIENTS FOR DESIRED  VARIATIONS.  SET UP INGREDIENTS IN INDIVIDUAL CONTAINERS AND PLACE NEAR  GRIDDLE.
