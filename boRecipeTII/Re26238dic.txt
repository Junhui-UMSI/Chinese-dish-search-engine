Tomatillo Salsa 2
Fresh lime juice, Honey, olive oil, Tomatillos, Salt, jalapeno, cilantro, red onion, 
Combine the tomatillos, onion, jalapeno, lime juice, cilantro, olive oil  and honey in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Refrigerate covered, for  up to 1 day. Bring to room temperature before serving.    Yield: 1 1/2 cups.
