Avocado Wild West Salad
Lettuce, Kidney beans; (15 ounce), Tomatoes, Cheddar Cheese, Bag corn or tortilla chips;, Hot sauce to taste, Ground beef, Salt, Onion, Avocado, French or Thousand Island, 
Chop the onion, the tomatoes and the head of lettuce. Toss with the cup of  grated cheese and the dressing, and add the hot sauce to taste. Crunch and  add the chips. Slice and add the avocado. Crumble ground beef in a 2 quart  casserole. Microwave at high (100%) 6 to 7 minutes, stirring once to break  up the beef. Add drained kidney beans and salt. Microwave at medium high  (70%) 4 minutes, stirring once during cooking time. Mix into the cold  salad. Makes 6 servings.
