Canned Clam Fritters
Egg; well-beaten, Salt, Black pepper, White wheat flour, Baking Powder, Canned clam broth or milk;, Butter, Minced canned clams;, Oil or clarified butter; for, MIX TOGETHER FOR TOPPING, Sour cream or yogurt, Dill; tarragon or thyme, 
Gently mix all ingredients together, adding the clams last. Drop 2 heaping  tablespoonfuls per fritter onto a hot greased griddle or an iron skillet.  When bubbles break, turn the fritters.    Serve warm with a dollop of herbed sour cream, yogurt or tartar sauce.    Recipe by Mangia!
