Barbecue Sauce #19
Garlic, Ketchup, Pepper, Sugar, Prepared mustard, Vegetable oil, Lemon juice, Vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, Onion, Water, 
Fry the first three ingredients in the oil until clear.  Add remaining  ingredients.  Stir all until mixed and simmer approx. 1/2 hour.  This is an  old family recipe.  approx. 50 years old it is delicious with any type of  meat.    MNEA28A@PRODIGY.COM    (MICHAEL MAJESKY)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
