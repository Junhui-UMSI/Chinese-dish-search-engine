Turnip Casserole
Eggs, Salt, Boiled, mashed turnips, Black Pepper, Sugar, Soft bread crumbs, Butter, 
Mix turnip, sugar, salt, pepper, butter, 3/4 cup of crumbs and beaten eggs.  Place in greased casserole. Sprinkle with rest of crumbs. Bake at 350  degrees for 25 minutes.
