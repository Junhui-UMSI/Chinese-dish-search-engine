Banana Lemon Sauce for Pancakes
Bananas; diced, Honey; or to taste, Lemon, Orange, Butter, Cinnamon, 
Grate orange to to produce grated rind. Squeeze juice from orange and 1/2  lemon. In small saucepan, melt butter. Add bananas. Simmer till bananas are  soft. Add remaining ingredients and serve hot.  from _The Berton Family Cookbook_  posted by Anne MacLellan    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcorr.zip
