Tuna Casserole #2
Cream of mushroom soup, Cream of chicken soup, (6.5-oz) tunafish, Bell pepper; chopped, Butter, Onions; chopped, Celery, (12-oz) egg noodles, Hard boiled eggs; chopped, Black pepper to taste, 
Melt butter in a baking dish. Cook and drain noodles. Mix all ingredients  and place in the baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Serves 6.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
