7-Layer Salad
Celery -- diced, Lettuce, To 5 tbsp. sugar, To 6 slices crisp bacon --, Grated Parmesan cheese (or, To 1 c. onion -- diced, Crumbled, Peas -- well drained, Canned), 
Mix sugar and mayonnaise together.  Make layer of each ingredient in  order listed. Top with sprinkles of crumbled bacon. Let set in  refrigerator 3 to 4 hours. Do not let sit overnight.
