Apple Knockers
Lemon juice, Ground nutmeg, Cloves, Apple cider, (3") sticks cinnamon, broken, Orange juice, Sugar, 
Place cinnamon sticks and cloves in a large tea ball, or tie in a  cheesecloth bag. Set aside.    Combine sugar and nutmeg in a large Dutch oven, stirring well. Gradually  add apple cider, orange juice, and lemon juice, stirring well. Add tea  ball. Bring mixture to a boil; reduce heat, and simmer 15 mins. Remove tea  ball; discard spices. Serve beverage warm. Yield: 2 1/2 qts.
