Bay Scallops Saute
Butter, Chopped fresh parsley, garlic, Bay scallops, Black Pepper, 
In skillet melt butter & add garlic. Cook until garlic is golden  brown. Add scallops & parsley. Cook 2-3 minutes, stirring often.  Sprinkle with pepper & serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
