Vegetable Pickle (Terkaree Ka Achar)
Cauliflower, Brinjal (Aubergine), Lemon juice, Vegetable oil, Fenugreek Leaves, White cumin seeds, Mustard seeds, Dried red chillies, Tomato, White cumin seeds, Potatoes, Turnips, Salt, (A), Carrots, Coriander seeds, Spinach Leaves, Onion seeds, Whole garlic, Red chilli powder, Peas, 
Wash all the vegetables and cut into pieces. Dry on a cloth.    When all the vegetables are dry, mix salt and place in large bowl. Set  aside for about 2-3 days or until the vegetables become soft..    Mix in the lemon juice and set aside again.    Using 2 cups of the oil, fry all the first group of spices (A) until light  brown. Remove from the oil and grind to a smooth paste.    Heat the rest of the oil in a large saucepan. Add 4 dried red chillies, the  2 tsp. white cumin seeds, and the whole garlic which has been peeled and  separated into cloves. Fry until light brown.    Add all the vegetables and all the ground spices and fry together for about  5 minutes. Remove from fire and allow to cool completely until room  temperature before placing in airtight containers. This way it can be  stored for 6-8 months.    Compiled by I. Chaudhary  Posted to EAT-L Digest 26 October 96    Date:    Mon, 28 Oct 1996 00:00:46 +1000    From:    "I.
