A-1 Caribou Steak
Brandy, Butter; divided, Onion, Chives, salt and pepper, Sherry, Chopped parsley, Caribou steak, Worcestershire, A-1 steak sauce, 
Cook onions in 2 tbsp butter until golden.  Pound steak to tenderize  and sear over high heat in butter & onion, about 2 min on each side. Add  sherry, A-1, 2 tbsp butter, Worcestershire, chives, parsley, pepper and  salt.  Reduce heat to med and cook for 3-4 mins per side. Add brandy, bring to the  table, flame and serve.
