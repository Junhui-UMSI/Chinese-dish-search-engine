Sweet and Sour Pork #4
Pineapple juice, Cornstarch, Soy sauce, Stripped pork, Water, Vinegar, Onion; sliced, Brown sugar, Salt, Vegetable oil, Green pepper, (no. 2) pineapple chunks, 
Brown pork. Drain pineapple, saving juice. Combine in order vinegar,  sugar, cornstarch, soy sauce, pineapple juice and onion. Cook until clear  and thick (about 2 minutes). Add pork; cook covered over low heat for 1  hour or until done. About 30 minutes before serving reheat, if  refrigerated. Add remaining ingredients and cook until tender. Served over  rice, this makes a nice Oriental dish. Serves 6 to 8.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
