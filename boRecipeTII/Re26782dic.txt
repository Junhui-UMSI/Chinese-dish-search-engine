Baked Apples #2
Cinnamon, Salt, Flour, Butter, Baking apples, Sugar, 
Wash and core apples; do not peel. Place in shallow baking dish: add water  to 1/2 depth. Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. Remove from oven. Mix  flour, sugar, cinnamon, salt with enough water to make a smooth paste. Fill  core cavity with butter, pour sauce over apples and bake another 20  minutes.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
