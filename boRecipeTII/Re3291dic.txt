Pear-Applesauce with Amaretto
Cubed peeled Bartlett pear, Brown sugar, Cubed peeled Granny Smith, Water, Amaretto, 
Combine the first 3 ingredients in a large saucepan; cover and cook over  low heat 40 minutes or until very tender. Mash to desired consistency; stir  in sugar and amaretto. Yield: 4 servings (serving size: 3/4 cup).    Per serving: 127 Calories; 0g Fat (3% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 31g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 4mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve at room temperature or chilled.
