Planet Hollywoods Crunch Chicken
Garlic powder, Fingers, Corn flakes, 1-0z chicken tenderloins or, Flour, Black pepper, Chicken breasts cut into, Eggs, Onion powder, milk, 
Place cereal in a food processor.  Mix well until crumbs are formed. (There  should be some chunks of cereal in the mixture.)  Transfer to a mixing  bowl.  Combine flour, onion and garlic powder and pepper in a second mixing  bowl.  Combine eggs and milk in a third mixing bowl.  Place chicken in  seasoned flour, one at a time, dip and coat evenly in egg wash, then coat  evenly with Crunch mixture.  Using a candy thermometer, heat oil in large  frying pan until temperature reaches 325f.  Fry tenders for 3 1/2 minutes  or until golden brown.  Remove and drain on paper towels.
