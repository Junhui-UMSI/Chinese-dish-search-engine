Almond Shortbread
Butter, Salt, 
BAKE at 325 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes. MAKES about 2 dozen. Sift  together flour, confectioners sugar, and salt. Cream butter. Blend in the  dry ingredients; mix thoroughly. Stir in almond extract and ground or  finely chopped blanched almonds. If desired, chill for easier handling.  Shape dough into balls, using rounded teaspoonful for each. Place on  ungreased baking sheets; flatten into circles about 1/2 inch thick. Flute  edges to form scallops. Bake in slow oven (325 degrees) 20 to 25 minutes  until light golden brown. Remove from baking sheets immediately; cool. *For  use with Pillsburys Best Self-Rising Flour, omit salt.
