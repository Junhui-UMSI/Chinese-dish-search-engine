Honey Roasted Red Onions
Fresh orange juice, Red onions (2- 1/4 pounds);, Honey, Red wine vinegar, Butter, Salt, Paprika, GARNISH, Snipped chives, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Pour 1/4 cup orange juice into shallow  baking dish large enough to hold all onions in a single layer. Place  onions, cut sides down, in baking dish with orange juice; cover with foil.  Bake for 30 minutes.    2. In small bowl, combine remaining juice and remaining ingredients except  garnish. Uncover onions, turn cut sides up. Spoon 1/2 of honey mixture over  top. Bake uncovered, 15 minutes. Spoon remaining honey mixture over onions.  Bake 15 minutes, until tender.    3. To serve: Spoon some of the honey mixture in bottom of dish over onions.  Garnish.  McCalls Magazine October 1998 p. 150 Article titled: "Honey Bunch" Recipes  by Gina Noonan and Frank P.
