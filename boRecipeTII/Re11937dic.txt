Rec- Apple Kugel
Cinnamon to taste, Nutmeg (optional), Eggs -or-, Egg whites, Sugar, Salt, Oil (more for a richer, Apples, depending on the, Bag egg noodles or no yolk, 
Source: My own    Boil noodles according to package directions. (about 10 minutes) Add salt  and oil. Add apples, sugar, and spices. In a separate bowl, beat eggs, and  add to mixture. Bake in greased (or sprayed with non-stick spray) pan at      350    degrees until golden brown.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #050 by Unkap@aol.com on Feb 13, 1997.
