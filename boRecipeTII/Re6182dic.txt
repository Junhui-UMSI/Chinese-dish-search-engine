Pizza Sauce
Oregano, Tomato puree, Black pepper, Raw onion (chopped), Glove garlic (chopped), Brown sugar, Basil, Water, Cooking oil, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Combine ingredients in a 2 qt sauce pan and cook for 30-40 minutes. Makes  enough sauce for 5 12" Pizzas.    From the recipe collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/ms-pizza.zip
