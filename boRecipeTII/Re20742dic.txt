Apple Dilly Cake
Apple; coarsely chopped, Butter, Baking soda, Walnuts, Cloves, Cold Coffee, Raisins, Egg; well beaten, Flour, Allspice, Sugar, Salt, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, 
Cream butter and sugar; add egg. Sift dry ingredients. Add alternately with  liquid. Stir in apples, nuts and raisins. Turn in well greased and floured  11 x 7 1/2 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees about 35 minutes. Cool thoroughly  in pan.
