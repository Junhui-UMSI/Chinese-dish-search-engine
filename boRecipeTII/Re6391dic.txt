Plum and Wheatgerm Muffins
Baking Powder, Butter, Very ripe plums, Egg, Wheatgerm, Flour, milk, Raw sugar, 
Preheat oven to 400F, prepare pans. Roughly chop the plums & remove  stones. Sift flour & baking powder, add sugar & wheatgerm, mix. Melt the  butter. Beat egg & milk together. Add butter & egg mix to the dry  ingredients. Mix lightly with a fork. Gently stir in the plums. Spoon into  pans & bake for about 15 mins or until golden & well risen. Makes 6 large  muffins.
