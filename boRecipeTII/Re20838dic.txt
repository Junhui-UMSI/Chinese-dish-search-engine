Apple Jolly Boys
Cinnamon, Buckwheat pancake mix, Molasses, (2 c) corn muffin mix, Diced, Canned or fresh cooked, Cold water, Apple, Egg -- well beaten, Sugar, 
Combine mixes with sugar and cinnamon; blend well. Combine egg, molasses  and water; stir in gradually.  Stir in diced apples. Drop by heaping  tablespoonsfuls on hot greased griddle; flatten with back of spoon. Cook  slowly, turning often, until well browned on both sides and cooked through.  Set finished cakes in 300F oven to keep warm while remainder are cooked.  Serve with butter and syrup or jam.
