Pesto Muffins
Eggs, milk, DRY MIX, Self-raising flour, Vegetable oil, WET MIX, Finely sliced spring onions, Chopped fresh basil, Cheddar Cheese, Wholemeal flour, Pesto sauce -or-, 
Preheat oven to 400F, prepare pans. Thoroughly combine the wet ingredients  in a bowl. Combine the dry ingredients & thoroughly but gently work this  into the wet mix. Spoon into pans & bake for 20-25 mins. Makes 12 muffins.  Variations:    1. Pesto & Pine Nut: Add 1/2 cup toasted pine nuts to the mixture.    2. Pesto & Prawn: Add 1/2 cup frozen cocktail prawns to the mixture.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
