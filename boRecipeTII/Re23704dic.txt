Artichoke Quiche
Paprika, Egg substitute, olive oil, Grated Sapsago or Parmesan, Dried thyme, Dry-curd cottage cheese, Cooked artichoke hearts, Whole wheat bread, 
Pulverize the bread in a blender or food processor. Transfer to a medium  bowl. Add the oil, thyme and paprika. Press into the bottom and up the  sides of a 9-inch pie plate. Bake at 400 degrees F for 7 minutes.    In a food processor, puree the cottage cheese, egg substitute and Sapsago  or Parmesan. Transfer to a large bowl.    Pat the artichokes dry with paper towels. Cut the hearts into bit-size  pieces. Stir into the cheese mixture. Pour into the pie shell.    Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes, or until a knife inserted in the  center comes out clean. To serve, cut into thin slices. Serves 8.    Posted to recipelu-digest by Sandy <sandysno@pctech.net> on Mar 22, 1998
