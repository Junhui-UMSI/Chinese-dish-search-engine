Yogurt Dressing
Plain yogurt, White pepper, olive oil, Dijon mustard, Salt, Fresh Lemon Juice, garlic, 
In a bowl, mix mustard, garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Gradually  whisk in olive oil. Stir in yogurt. Makes 1-1/2 cups. Serve on Spinach  Salad (see recipe).    STARS & STRIPES, 6 DEC 92    FROM CITY CUISINE BY SUSAN    FENIGER & MARY SUE MILLIKEN    Downloaded from G Internet, G Internet.
