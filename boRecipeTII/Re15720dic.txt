A-2 Steak Sauce
Paprika, Cinnamon, Tabasco sauce, Red wine, Vinegar; white, Mustard Dry, Coarse salt, Tamarind extract, Garlic powder, Ginger, Dark molasses, Kitchen Boquet, Green Onions, Pepper; black, Cloves; ground, Cardamom seeds, Rhine wine, Caramel coloring as desired, Cayenne, Fenugreek (Greek Hay), 
Run all non liquid ingredients through a spice blender until they are a  fine powder. Place over low heat with half of vinegar and simmer 1 hour.  Add the rest of the vinegar a little at a time. Stir in tobasco, wines,  kitchen bouquet and coloring. Cook 3 minutes to dissolve. Remove from heat.  Pour into crock and let stand covered for 1 week. Strain through  cheesecloth six times.
