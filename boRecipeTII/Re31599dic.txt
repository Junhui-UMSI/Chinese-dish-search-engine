Swiss Breakfast Parfait
nonfat or lowfat, Sliced strawberries, undrained, Crushed pineapple in juice, Sliced almonds (optional), Vanilla yogurt, fresh or frozen, Quaker Oats, uncooked, (quick or oldfashioned), 
In medium bowl, combine oats, yogurt, pineapple and almonds; mix well.  Cover, refrigerate overnight or up to 4 days.  To serve, layer oat mixture  and strawberries in 4 parfait glasses.  Garnish with additional  strawberries, if desired.  Serve chilled.
