Poached Shrimp W/ham and Bean Curd
Smoked ham, Vegetable stock, Sprigs Chinese parsley; more, Shrimp, Cakes bean curd, 
1. Shell and devein shrimp; dice in 1/2-inch sections. Dice ham and bean  curd in 1/2-inch cubes. Place in a saucepan.    2. Pour stock over. Bring to a boil; then simmer, uncovered, until shrimp  turn bright pink.    3. Serve hot, in a deep bowl, garnished with Chinese parsley.
