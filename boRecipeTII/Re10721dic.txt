Quick Succotash
tomatoes, green bell pepper, Salt, Frozen whole kernel corn, Water, Frozen baby lima beans, Dried whole summer savory,, 
Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add beans; cook, uncovered, 5 minutes.  Add corn and next 3 ingredients; cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat, and stir in tomato. Yield: 5 servings (serving size:  1cup).    Per serving: 142 Calories; 1g Fat (5% calories from fat); 7g Protein; 30g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 251mg Sodium    Recipe by: Cooking Light, Sept 1994, page 138    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #403 by igor@digex.net on Jan 28, 1997.
