Alfie and Archies Garlic Treats
Meat drippings, Ice water, Garlic powder, Beaten egg, Brown sugar, Salt, Whole wheat flour, Powdered dry milk, 
1.  Preheat oven to 350.  Lightly oil a cookie sheet. Combine flour, dry  milk, salt, garlic powder and sugar. Cut in meat drippings until mixture  resembles corn meal. Mix in egg. Add enough water so that mixture forms a  ball.  Using your fingers, pat out dough onto cookie sheet to half inch  thick.  Cut with cookie cutter or knife and remove scraps. Scraps can be  formed again and baked. 2. Bake 25-30 minutes. Remove from tray and cool on  rack. from: _Cookiemania_
