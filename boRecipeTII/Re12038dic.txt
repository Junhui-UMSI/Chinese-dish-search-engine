Zippy Beef Casserole
Cream of mushroom soup, cond, Ground beef, Crushed potato chips, Shredded sharp Cheddar chees, Uncooked elbow macaroni, Green Pepper, Instant minced onion, milk, Salt, Catsup, 
Preheat oven to 350. Cook and stir meat until brown; drain off fat. Cook  macaroni as directed on package;drain. In ungreased 2 qt. casserole, mix  all ingredients, except potato chips. Cover, bake 40 minutes. Uncover;  sprinkle with crushed potato chips and bake 5 minutes longer. 4-6 serv.
