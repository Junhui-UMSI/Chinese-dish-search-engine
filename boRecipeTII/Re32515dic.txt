Sweet Dough Bread
Butter, Sugar, Eggs, milk, Flour, Salt, Cakes yeast, 
Blend yeast cakes with sugar until it becomes watery. Add eggs & beat well.  Add salt, mixing thoroughly. Bring milk to scald, add butter to melt, cool  to lukewarm. Alternate milk with sifted flour & beat well. Knead on floured  board until smooth & satiny. Let rise in warm place until double in bulk.  Roll out on board & make 2 loaves by braiding. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes.    MRS EUGENE (ANNE) BROILLET    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
