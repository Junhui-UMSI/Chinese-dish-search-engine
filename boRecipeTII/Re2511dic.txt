Peaches n Cream Ice Cream
Fresh Lemon Juice, Vanilla extract, Salt, fresh peaches, Whipping cream, freestone peaches, Sugar, Sweetened condensed milk, -(up to), Almond extract, Red food color, milk, 
When using fresh peaches, sweeten to taste. Combine peaches and lemon  juice. In a very large mixing bowl combine milk, whipping cream, sugar,  vanilla and almond extract, salt. Add peaches to the mixture; stir in food  color, if,desired. Chill. Put into ice cream freezer and churn. Yields 1  gallon.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
