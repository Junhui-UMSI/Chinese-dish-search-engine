Banana Pudding
Pinch of salt, Cold milk, Carton of sour cream (4 oz.), Vanilla wafers, Vanilla, Bananas, Carton Cool Whip (4 oz.), Instant vanilla pudding mix, 
Beat the pudding with cold milk.  Fold in the sour cream, Cool Whip, salt  and vanilla.  Layer vanilla wafers and bananas. Pour pudding mixture over  bananas and wafers. Refrigerate overnight. Randy Rigg
