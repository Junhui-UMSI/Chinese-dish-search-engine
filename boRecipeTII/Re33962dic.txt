Sweet Cherries Romanoff Style
Halved sweet cherries, Favorite butter cookies, Orange flavored liqueur, Brown sugar, Sour cream, 
Combine cherries, liqueur & sugar; chill several hours. Gently stir 2  tsp. thickened mixture from cherries into sour cream. Spoon cherries  into 2 individual stemmed goblets. Spoon sour cream mixture over  cherries. Serve with cookies.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
