Zippy Crisp Chicken Bake
Miracle Whip Salad Dressing, Corn flake crumbs, Kraft Grated Parmesan Cheese, Salt and pepper, 
2,3     lb chicken;* *cut up & skinned/I use all breasts 1. Preheat oven to  350 2. Brush chicken with salad dressing: coat with combined crumbs and  cheese. Sprinkle with salt and pepper 3. Place in 13 x 9 baking dish 4.  Bake 1 hour or until tender. Makes 3 to 4 servings This is good and has a  little "zing" to it. Hope you all enjoy it.                Sherri/SCPF53A
