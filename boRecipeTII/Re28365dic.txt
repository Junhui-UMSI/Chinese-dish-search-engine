Baked Italian Zuchinni
ONION, Oregano, Pepper, Dried basil, Salt, ZUCCHINI, Tomato sauce, TOMATO, BUTTERED *, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
*      USE 1/2 CUP BREAD CRUMBS INSTEAD OF CUBED BREAD *  ~------------------------------------------------------------------- *  LAYER IN A ONE QUART BAKING DISH, ZUCCHINI, ONION AND TOMATO, IF DESIRED  SEASONNING EACH LAYER WITH OREGANO, PEPPER, BASIL AND SALT. POUR TOMATO  SAUCE TO BARELY COVER VEGETABLES. COVER AND BAKE AT 350 DEG F. FOR 45  MINUTES. UNCOVER AND TOP WITH BREAD CUBES OR CRUMBS AND GRATED CHEESE. BAKE  UNCOVERED FOR 10 MINUTES.
