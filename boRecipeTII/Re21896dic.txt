Apple-Yam Casserole
Apples, Wheat germ, Water, Water, Apple juice, Honey, Cornstarch, Yams, 
In a pot with a tight-fitting lid, steam yams in 1/2 cup water until  tender; 15-20 minutes. Peel and slice them lengthwise in 1/2" thick slices.  Layer them in a casserole.    Peel and core apples, slicing them 1/2" thick. Lay apple slices on top of  yams.  Heat apple juice to the boiling point. Combine cornstarch and water  and add to juice, cooking until sauce is clear and thickened. Add honey.    Spoon sauce over apples, then top with wheat germ. Bake in a 350 degree  oven until apples are tender, 30 minutes to an hour. Submitted By SAM  WARING <SAM.WARING@382-91-12.IMA.INFOMAIL.COM> On MON, 13 NOV 1995 132319  GMT
