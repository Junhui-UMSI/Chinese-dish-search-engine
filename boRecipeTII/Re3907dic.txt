Pepper Roasted Salmon with Mustard- Herb Cream Sauce
Garlic clove; peeled, Salmon; (3 pound) fillet, olive oil, Rosemary sprigs; (optiional), Mustard, Fresh Lemon Juice, Chives, Olive oil-flavored cooking, Light tofu; drained, Minced fresh dill, Ground peppercorns divided, Fat-free sour cream, Salt, 
Recipe By : Iara Lewin Serving Size  : 8 Preparation Time :0:12    Tofu gives this sauce its rich, creamy texture. Make sure the fillet is one  piece fish.    Place tofu and garlic in a food processor, and process until smooth. ADd  sour cream, mustard and lemon juice, process until blended. Stir in clives,  dill and 1/2 tsp ground peppercorns. Cover and chill. Preheat oven to 450.  Place the salmon, skin side down, on a baking sheet coated with cooking  spray. Brush fillet with oil, sprinkle with 1 1/2tsp ground peppercoens and  salt. Bake at 450 for 12 min or until the fish lakes easily ehen tested  with a fork. Serve sauce with salmon. Garnish with rosemary sprigs, if  desired.
