Raspberry Apple Parfait (Motts)
Fresh raspberries OR frozen, Sugar-free gelatin raspberry, Apple Sauce; *see note, Plain or raspberry yogurt, Fresh raspberries; if, Boiling water, 
In medium bowl, dissolve gelatin in bowling water; stir in apple sauce  until well blended. Stir in raspberries.    Pour into parfait glasses. Refrigerate until firm.    To serve, alternately spoon layers of gelatin and yogurt into parfait  glasses or dessert dishes. Garnish with yogurt and fresh raspberries.    Notes: Pantry: Motts Natural or Chunky Apple Sauce. Use any glass with a  stem or a clear tumbler to serve this colorful, low calorie parfait. Top  with yogurt and fresh raspberries.    >Hanneman/Buster 1998-Apr    Recipe by: Motts Applesauce www.motts.com    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by KitPATh <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu> on Apr 18,  1998
