Prizewinning Brisket
Water, Beef brisket, Beer, Onions, sliced, Chili sauce, Stalks celery, sliced, 
Place onions and celery on beef in open roaster.  Pour chili sauce over  roast.  Add water.  Roast at 325 F. until browned, basting frequently.  Cover and continue roasting until almost tender. Total cooking time to this  point should be abt. 2 1/2 hrs.  Pour beer over brisket, recover, and  continue roasting until tender, about 1 hr. Do not overcook. Remove fat  from gravy, slice meat and return slices to sauce to serve.    Serves 8-10.
