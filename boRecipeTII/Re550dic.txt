Fried Onion Rings
Hot red pepper sauce, ONION RINGS, Oil for frying, Onion, BATTER, Water, milk, White cornmeal, Vegetable oil, Seasoned salt, Sugar, All purpose flour, 
Combine all batter ingredients in small mixing bowl. Stir until smooth.    Heat 3 inches of oil in deep fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees. Dip a  few onion rings in the batter let excess batter drip back into bowl.    Fry a few at a time for two to three minutes OR until golden brown. Drain  on paper towels.    Repeat with remaining onion rings.    Serve immediately OR keep warm in 175 degree preheated oven.
