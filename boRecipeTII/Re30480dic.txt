Banana Nut Bread
Salt, Nuts; chopped, Sugar, Vanilla, Eggs, Flour, Baking soda, Bananas; mashed, Vegetable oil, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Makes 3 regular loaves. Cream sugar and oil.  Add bananas and eggs. Add dry ingredients. Mix thoroughly and pour into  three greased loaf pans. Bake about 45 minutes.    It will make 8 mini loaf pans. Bake for 30 minutes.    Convection Bake - 325 degrees - 30 minutes.
