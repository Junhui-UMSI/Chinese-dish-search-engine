Apple Cobbler #1
milk, Sugar, Sugar, Flour, Sliced apples, Flour, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Water, Salt, Salt, Butter or margarine, Shortening, 
Combine sugar, flour, cinnamon & salt. Mix with apples. Pour into baking  dish. Sprinkle with water & dot with butter or margarine. Cover with foil &  bake at 400 for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, sift flour, sugar, baking powder &  salt. Cut in shortening. Stir in milk. Drop by spoonfuls onto hot apples.  Bake uncovered for 25-35 minutes. Makes 6 servings.    MRS.
