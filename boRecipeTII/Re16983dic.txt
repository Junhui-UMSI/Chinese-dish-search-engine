Almond Butter Cookies (Sandwiched with Jam)
Sugar, Salt, Water, allpurpose flour, Unsalted butter, almonds, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Combine ground almonds and sugar. Blend in  butter. Add dry ingredients. Add water and blend well. Shape into a  flattened ball. Wrap in parchment and chill at least 1/2 hour. Roll out  onto lightly floured board until approximately 1/4-inch thick. Cut into  desired shapes, and place on parchment covered baking sheet about 2 inches  apart. Bake until golden brown. Cool. Sandwich with strained or seedless  jam (raspberry is great!) or chocolate ganache.
