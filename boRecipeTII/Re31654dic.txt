Swiss Mix Sandwiches
Pecans, Raisins, Whole-wheat or white bread, Shredded Swiss cheese, Shredded carrots, Mayonnaise or salad dressing, 
Combine cheese, carrots, nuts, raisins & enough mayonnaise to moisten. Mix  lightly. For each sandwich, spread 2 bread slices with mayonnaise & fill  with cheese mixture. Makes 6 sandwiches.
