Zesty Turkey and Rice
turkey, Frozen peas, stewed tomatoes, canned, Garlic powder, Chicken Stock, Hot pepper sauce, long grain rice, uncooked, Dried basil leaves, 
1. Combine first 5 ingredients in a saucepan over medium high heat. Bring  to a boil. Stir in rice. Reduce heat to low.    2. Cover and cook 20 minutes. Stir in peas and turkey.    3. Cover and cook 5 minutes more or until rice is done and most of liquid  is absorbed.    Per serving: calories 332, fat 4.4g, 12% calories from fat, cholesterol  53mg, protein 28.1g, carbohydrates 42.5g, fiber 2.3g, sodium 608mg.
