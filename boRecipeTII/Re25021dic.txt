Tomato Sar
Mustard seeds, Salt, Chopped coriander leaves;, Tomatoes; finely cut, Onions; finely cut, Jeera; (cumin) powder, Flakes garlic (to 8);, Pepper, Chilli powder, Sprig curry leaves, FOR SEASONING, Vegetable oil, 
Cook the tomatoes and onions with 7 to 8 cups of water and salt to taste.  When it is cooked, season with oil, mustard seeds, curry leaves, garlic and  jeera, pepper and chilli powders. Cover and cook for another 5 minutes.  Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.    Recipe by: Mangalorean Cuisine by Saranya S.
