Apple Salad and Tofu-Honey Cinnamon Dressing
Water, Granny Smith apples; sliced, Pineapple tidbits; drained, DRESSING, Ground cinnamon, Plain low-fat yogurt, Soft silken tofu, Walnuts, coarsely chopped, Celery stalks, Fresh Lemon Juice, Honey, Mint sprigs, Gala apples; sliced, 
Place sliced apples in mixture of lemon juice and water. Add more water  if needed to cover apples. At serving time, drain liquid from apples.  Combine apples, celery, pineapple and nuts. For the dressing: Combine  yogurt, tofu, honey and cinnamon in a food processor and blend until  smooth. Chill. Combine apple mixture with dressing and toss gently.  Serve on a bed of fresh greens. Garnish with mint sprigs. Serves 8.
