Tomato and Cheese Tart
Parmesan or Swiss cheese, Tomatoes, peeled; seeded, Sheet puff pastry, Eggs, Salt and pepper, Cream, Melted, Oregano; chopped, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Roll out puff pastry to fit inside a 10-inch  shallow tart pan. Crimp edges and thoroughly prick bottom of shell with a  fork. In a small bowl lightly beat eggs, add cream and mix well. Add  remaining ingredients and adjust seasonings to taste. Pour into tart shell  and bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until firm.
