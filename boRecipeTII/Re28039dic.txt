Baked Chicken Reuben
Pepper, Swiss Cheese, Salt, Prepared Thousand Island Dressing, Boneless Chicken Breasts, Sauerkraut, 
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Place chicken in single layer in greased  baking pan. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Spoon sauerkraut over chicken.  Arrange cheese slices over sauerkraut. Pour dressing evenly over top. Cover  pan with aluminum foil. Bake about 1 1/2 hours or until fork can be  inserted into chicken with ease.
