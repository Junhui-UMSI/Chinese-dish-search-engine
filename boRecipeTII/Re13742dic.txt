Wild Rice Soup
Dry sherry, Pepper, Almonds, Salt, Unsalted butter, Chicken broth, Carrots, Wild Rice, cooked, Onion, Flour, Ham, Half and half, 
Melt butter in saucepan, saute onions until tender. Blend in flour,  gradually add broth.  Cook stirring constantly until mixture thickens  slightly.  Stir in rice, ham, carrots, almonds, and salt; simmer about 5  minutes.  Blend in half and half and sherry; heat to serving temperature  and season with pepper.    Recipe By     :
