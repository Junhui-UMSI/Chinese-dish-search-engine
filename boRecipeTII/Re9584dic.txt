Pumpkin Date Nut Bread
Yeast, Flour; whole-grain wheat, Walnuts*; coarsely chopped, Milk; skim, Salt, Cinnamon, Egg, Ginger, Allspice, Brown Sugar, Dates*; chopped, Pumpkin pie filling, Butter, Nutmeg, Bread flour, Vital wheat gluten, 
Add ingredients to the machine in the order listed with the exception of  walnuts and dates.  Bake on fruit and nut cycle.  When addition beep  sounds, add walnuts and dates. Makes a 1 1/2 pound loaf.  Recipe By     : Bill Camarota    Posted to Digest bread-bakers.v096.n049    Date: Fri, 18 Oct 1996 21:53:07 -0400 (EDT)    From: Bill Camarota <gfx4tv@acy.digex.net>
