Almond Roca
Milk chocolate chips, Water, Granulated sugar, Corn syrup, Butter, Almonds; blanched & slivered, 
In heavy skillet, combine sugar, water, syrup, butter and almonds and cook  to hard crack or until the almonds are toasted light brown. Stir  constantly. Pour into a greased baking pan. While warm, sprinke the milk  chocolate chips over mixture and spread when softened. Let cool, remove  from pan, and break into pieces. Yields 9x13-inch baking dish.
