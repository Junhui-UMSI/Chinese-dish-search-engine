Potato Latkes
Potatoes, Onion, Matzoh meal (or flour), Egg whites, salt and pepper, 
~  Peel potatos and shred in food processor    ~ Remove potatos & squeeze water out of shredded potatos.    ~ Shred onion in food processor.    ~ Add all ingredients ot processor and process until well mixed using steel  knife.    ~ Fry in nonstick pan until golden brown using a small amount of cooking  spray.    ~ Serve with applesauce and fatfree sourcream.
