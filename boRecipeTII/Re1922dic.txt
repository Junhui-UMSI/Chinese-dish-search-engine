Chipotle Tomato Sauce
Onions, Garlic, Whole oregano leaves, Ground cinnamon, olive oil, Fresh or canned chopped, Dry white wine, Minced chipotle chile in, Fresh Cilantro, 
Saute the onions, garlic, oregano and cinnamon in the olive oil over  moderate heat until soft. Add the tomatoes, wine and chipotle and cook  uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes until thickened. Stir in the cilantro.    This recipe yields about 3 cups of sauce.
