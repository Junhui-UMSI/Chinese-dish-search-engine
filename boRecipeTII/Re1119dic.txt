Cranberry Banana Flip
Chilled sliced ripe banana, (1 small), Vanilla nonfat frozen, Yogurt, Chilled cranberry juice, Cocktail, Skim milk, Yield: 2-1/2 cups, (serving size: 1-1/4 cups)., 
Combine all ingredients in container of an electric blender; cover and  process until smooth.    Serve immediately.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Sep  15, 1998
