Apple Cider Cream Sauce
Salt, Apple cider, Flour, Sage, Cayenne pepper, Butter, Heavy cream, Calvados Brandy, 
Melt butter.  Add flour until blond roux forms.  Stir in mixture of heavy  cream and apple cider; reduce by half.  Stir in red pepper, salt, and sage  to taste.  Cook until thickened, stirring frequently. Add Brandy to  mixture.  Bring to a gentle simmer and serve with Oyster-Stuffed Pork Loin  Source: Cajun Revalations: Chef: Brit Shockley    Recipe By     : Rhonda Guilbeaux    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
