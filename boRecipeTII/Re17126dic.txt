Violet Jam
Flowers, make sure there, Boiling water, Lemon juice, Pectin, Sugar, 
Pour water over flowers let sit overnight. Strain and discard flowers, add  lemon juice and pectin. Bring to boil, add sugar. Boil for 1 1/2 minutes.  Pour in to sterilized jars and seal.    NOTE; Try using leaves in soups as a thickener (like you would use Okra).  Blossoms are great in salads too. Posted to Kitmailbox Digest by House of  Engraving <caengrav@aimnet.com> on Apr 3, 1997
