Zucchini Cake
Corn oil, Sugar, Salt, Baking Powder, Eggs, Baking soda, Zucchini, Flour, Nuts, Raisins, Cinnamon, 
Beat eggs until they become light yellow. Add next 3 ingredients. Add to  egg mixture and blend well. Add nuts and raisins. Bake in a well greased  tube pan for 1 1/2 hours or a 9 x 13" pan for 1 hour @ 350 degrees.
