Tulsa Fire Department Chili
Chili powder, (10 1/2 oz) beef broth, Onion, Canned pinto beans, (8 oz) tomato sauce, Ground cumin, Flour or more, Ground beef, Garlic, Salt, Water, 
Brown the meat and onion and pour off grease.  Add chili powder, cumin,  salt, crushed garlic, broth, and tomato sauce. Cover and simmer for 45  minutes.  Add pinto beans and simmer 15 minutes more. Thicken with a small  amount of flour paste (dissolving 1 tablespoon flour in 1/4 cup water).  Serve over cooked spaghetti or plain with crackers.     NOTE: FOR HEART-HEALTHY CHILI, SUBSTITUTE GROUND TURKEY FOR BEEF IN THE  ABOVE RECIPES. Formatted by Mary Wilson, BWVB02B, San Diego, Jan 1994.
