Worlds Best Bean Soup
Tomatoes, canned, Garlic, Onion, Chili powder, Salt, Lemon juice, Ham hock, Pepper, Water, 
Wash the Soup Mix and place in a Dutch oven.  Cover with water and soak  overnight.  Drain the beans and add the water, ham hock, salt and pepper.  Cover and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 1 1/2 hours until the  beans are tender.  Add the remaining  ingredients.  Simmer 30 minutes more,  stirring occasionally.  Remove ham hock from the soup.  Take the meat from  the bone, chop the meat and return it to the soup.
