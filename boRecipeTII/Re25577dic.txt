Avocado Orange Soup
Fresh orange juice, Salt, Ripe avocados; pitted, Plain yogurt, TABASCO pepper sauce, Orange slices, 
In food processor or blender, blend avocados and orange juice. Add yogurt,  TABASCO sauce and salt. Blend until smooth. Refrigerate until ready to  serve. Garnish with orange slices.
