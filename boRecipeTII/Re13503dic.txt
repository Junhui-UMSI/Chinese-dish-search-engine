Whole-Wheat Herbed Bread Twists
Sugar, Yeast, Whole-wheat flour, White flour, Salt, Shortening, WATER; WARM, Milk solids; (powdered), 
Mix yeast and sugar with warm water; let sit for 3 to 5 minutes. Melt  shortening and add to yeast with milk and salt. Stir in flour. Mix with  hook for 10 minutes until dough forms ball. Cover with plastic and proof  until doubled in bulk. Punch dough down; roll into 1/2" rope. Cut into 6  equal pieces. Prepare bread seasoning. Roll bread twists in bread  seasoning. Proof until double in size. Bake at 350oF for 10 minutes or  until golden brown. Bread seasoning: Mix 1 tbsp. water and 1 tbsp. corn  syrup together. Brush each piece of dough with mixture. Combine 2 tbsps.  herb mix with 1/2 tsp. garlic salt.    Recipe by: MAILING LIST 1995    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 442 by ctlindab@mail1.nai.net on  Jan 3, 1998
