Grilled Flatbread with Fillet of Beef, White Bean Puree and
Honey, Recipe flatbread; (see, 8 ounce beef tenderloin, olive oil, Head garlic; skin removed, Cooked white beans, Cloves garlic; smashed, olive oil, Lemon juice, Fresh thyme, Salt, Sundried tomatoes; in oil,, Roasted shallots; sliced, olive oil, Balsamic vinegar, 
Marinate the beef overnight in the olive oil and garlic, 4 hours or  overnight. Grill on both sides for 3 to 4 minutes, for mediumrare. Let rest  and slice thinly. Puree the beans, olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, thyme in  a food processor until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix  together the tomatoes, shallots, olive oil, vinegar and honey and season  with salt and pepper to taste. Flatten dough, throw on grill. Grill on one  side until golden, turn over, spread with white bean puree, sliced beef and  tomato chutney. Serve.
