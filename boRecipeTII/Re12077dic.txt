Ziti Sausage Bake
salt and pepper, Links Italian sausage, hot, Grated Fontina cheese, Half and half, Diced green pepper (opt.), Grated Italian cheese, Ziti cooked according to, 
Cook ziti according to directions and drain. Remove sausage from casing,  crumble and brown. Drain well. Add to pasta along with diced pepper, 1 cup  half and half, 1 cup of Fontina and grated cheese. Mix well. Pour into  buttered 13 by 9 " pan. Cover and bake at 350 for 20 min. Uncover and top  with remaining half and half and Fontina. Bake another 10 min. Let stand 5  min. before serving.
