Poppy Seed Cookies
Flour, Vanilla flavour, Baking Powder, Poppy seed, Vegetable oil, Eggs, Sugar, 
Beat eggs and sugar; add oil, poppy seeds and flavoring. Mix in flour  thoroughly.  Roll out on a floured board to 1/8th inch thickness. Cut into  desired shapes and place greased cookie sheet. Sprinkle tops with a little  sugar and bake at 375 F. for abt. 10 min.
