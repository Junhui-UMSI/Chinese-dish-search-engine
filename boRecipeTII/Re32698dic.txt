Sweet Potato Apple Casserole
Pecans, Sweet potatoes, Apples, butter, Brown sugar, 
Peel and slice apples and sweet potatoes. Butter bottom of casserole dish.  Make layer of potatoes and apples. sprinkle with sugar and four or five  pats of butter. Continue layers until full. Sprinkle with pecans and bake  for 30 to 40 minutes or until done.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 26,  1998
