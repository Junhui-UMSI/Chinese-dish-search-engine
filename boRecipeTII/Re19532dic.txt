Anise Carrots
Anise; crushed, Carrots; peeled, sliced, Onion, Orange juice, Butter, 
1.  Cook carrots in salted water until tender. Drain.    2.  Saute onion in 2 tbs. of butter until soft.    3.  Return carrots to saucepan. Add remaining butter, anise and orange  juice. Season with salt and pepper.    4.  Cook over medium heat until butter has melted and ingredients have  blended.  Stir occasionally.  Serve hot.
