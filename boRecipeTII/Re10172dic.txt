Quiche Lorrainne Verte
Nutmeg, Swiss cheese, grated, Half & half, Butter, Eggs, Salt, Pimientos as garnish, Pepper, Flour, Bacon, cut up, Unbaked piecrust, Green onions, inc. tops*, 
* chop green onions (inc. tops)    Fry bacon until cooked, but not TOO crisp.    Place in bottom of unbaked pie crust.    Place swiss cheese strips in a crisscross pattern on top of bacon.    Combine rest of ingredients (except pimientos) in blender, until     foamy. Pour mixture into piecrust.     Top w/pimentoes.  Bake at 375 for 45 minutes.
