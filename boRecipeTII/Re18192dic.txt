Venison Chili Ala Fred
(15? oz) kidney beans, Catsup, Pepper, Onions [chopped], Venson [ground], Salt, Tomatoes [canned & chopped], 
1)      Combine the venison, onions, salt and pepper, and brown in a  skillet, stirring `til crumbley... 2) Add the remaining ingredients, and  simmer for 45 min or `til it is of the desired consistancy...
