Warm Tea Punch
Orange slices and additional, White grape juice, Cloves, Cinnamon sticks, Cranberry juice, Water, Orange herbal tea bags, Brown sugar, 
In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil. Turn off heat; add tea bags,  cloves and cinnamon sticks. Cover and steep for 5 minutes. Remove tea bags.  Stir in the juices and brown sugar; heat through. Remove spices. Garnish  with clove-studded orange slices if desired.
