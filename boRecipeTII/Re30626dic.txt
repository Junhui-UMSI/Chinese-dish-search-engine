Taltys Red Sauce
Celery, Salt, wine, white, Carrots, Onion-Vadalia, Sugar, Pepper-white, Chickin broth, Plum Tomatoes Whole  , Garlic, Olive oil- Virgin, Herbs- Oregano, tomato paste, 
1. Place olive oil in large pot, turn heat to med.  , cook garlic until  light brown. 2. Add onion, celery & carrots & saute Al-Dente. 3. Add  tomatoes & simmer for 1-2 hours. 4. Add sugar, salt & pepper. 5. Cook until  sauce is rich & well blended.  [ Tighten with tomatoe paste/or thin with  chicken broth. 6. One half hour before serving add the herbs. 7. Ten  minutes before serving add the wine [ should be excellent wine, drink the  rest while cooking the sauce to improve the flavor and your attitude].  ***** Serve over your favorite Pasta with excellent Parmesan Cheese and  crusty bread.    !!!!!! This keeps very well in the freezer for 3-4 months. Place 2 or more  cups in a zip lock freezer bag to freeze. The recipe can be halved or  doubled with excellent results.
