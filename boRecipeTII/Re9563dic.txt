Pumpkin Cookies
Baking soda, Brown sugar, Nuts, Cinnamon, Shortening, Vanilla, Flour, Mashed pumpkin, Icing sugar (optional), Raisins, Baking Powder, 
Cream shortening and sugar, then add pumpkin, raisins, nuts and  vanilla and mix well.  Sift together flour, baking soda, cinnamon and  baking powder and add to creamed ingredients. Mix well, then drop ty  teaspoonsful onto greased cookie sheet.  BAte at 350F for 8 to 10 minutes.  When cool, dust with icing sugar if desired.
