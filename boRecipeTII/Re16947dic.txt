Almond Berries
Toasted almond slivers, Fresh blueberries, Fresh strawberries, Amaretto liqueur~, 
Mix berries together in bowl. Pour in 1 cup Amaretto. Toss well. Cover and  chill. Spoon into dessert cups to serve. Sprinkle on toasted almonds.
