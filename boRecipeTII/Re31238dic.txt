Banana-Passion Fruit Sorbet
Banana pulp, Unsweetened passion fruit, Honey, 
Notes: From Hawaiian Electric Kitchen.   Combine all ingredients and pour into ice cube trays or a shallow pan;  freeze until almost firm.   Put cubes or chunks of fruit mixture into food  processor or blender container.   Process or blend until fluffy, but not  thawed.   Pour into a container and freeze until firm.
