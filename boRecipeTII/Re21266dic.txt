Ultimate Leftover Bread Pudding
Sugar, Eggs, Shredded cocunut, Raisins, Pecans, Nutmeg, Stale bread (any kind), torn, Cinnamon, (1 stick) unsalted  butter,, Milk (3 c. if using fresh, 
Grease a 9x13 inch pan. Set oven rack in lower third of oven. Preheat oven:  350 degrees. Mix all the ingredients, may be soupy. Pour into baking dish.  Bake 1 hour 15 minutes until top is golden brown.    From: Now Youre Cooking by Elaine Corn Posted to Kitmailbox by bryce  upshaw <kbryceuj@dixie-net.com> on Apr 25, 1997
