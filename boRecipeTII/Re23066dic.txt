Truffle Mashed Potatoes
Potatoes, Truffle Butter, Salt, Heavy cream, white pepper, truffle oil, 
* Note: See the "Truffle Butter" recipe which is included in this  collection.    Cook the potatoes in salted water until tender. Mash the potatoes until  smooth. Stir in the Truffle Butter and cream and mash until incorporated.  Season the potatoes with a drizzle of truffle oil, salt and white pepper.    This recipe yields 2 cups.
