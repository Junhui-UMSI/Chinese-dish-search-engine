Pink-Grapefruit Gelatin
Sugar, Pink grapefruit juice, unflavored gelatin, 
Pour 1 cup grapefruit juice into a bowl. Sprinkle gelatin over and let  stand  to soften, about 5 minutes.  Bring sugar and 1 1/2 cups water to a boil  over  medium-high heat, stirring to dissolve sugar. Pour hot syrup over gelatin;  mix  well to dissolve gelatin. Stir in remaining juice.  Pour into a 9x13 inch  Pyrex pan, cover and refrigerate until set, about 4 hours.  When ready to  serve, cut gelatin into 1-inch cubes and serve in tall glasses.
