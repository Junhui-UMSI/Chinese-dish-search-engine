Stuffed Honeyed Sweet Potatoes
Honey, Cardamom, Walnuts -- chopped, Butter, Salt, Light cream, Sweet potaotes, Dark rum, 
Wash potatoes; drain excess water.  Place damp potatoes in slow-cooking  pot.  Cover and cook on low 5 to 6 hours or until done.*  Cut off the top  third of each potato lengthwise and scoop out, leaving a 1/4-inch shell.  Mash potato pulp with butter, cream, honey, rum, cardamom, and salt. Return  mixture to shell.  Top with walnuts.  Arrange in shallow baking sheet. Bake  in 425 F oven for 15 minutes.
