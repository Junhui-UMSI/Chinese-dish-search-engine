Tangy Honey French Dressing
Black pepper, Celery seed -- optional, Paprika, Sugar, Dry mustard, Salad oil, Salt -- to taste, Honey, Fresh Lemon Juice, Garlic clove -- optional, Lemon zest, 
Place all ingredients into a screw-type jar. Shake vigorously. If using the  garlic: cut clove in half, add to dressing and chill for 12 hours or more  before using. Remove garlic clove before serving.    This makes about 1-1/4 cup dressing.
