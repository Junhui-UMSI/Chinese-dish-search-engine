Unsloppy Joes
drained and rinsed, undrained and crushed, No-salt-added tomato paste, Vinegar, Garlic, Pepper, Can no-salt added tomatoes, Onions, Kaiser rolls (2-1/2 oz ea.), Vegetable cooking spray, Chili powder, Diced green pepper, Diced carrot, Celery, Red kidney beans (canned), 
Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; place over medium-high  heat until hot.  Add the onion, celery, carrot, green pepper, and garlic;  saute until tender.  Stir in the tomatoes and next four ingredients.  Cover, reduce the heat, and simmer ten minutes.  Add the kidney beans and  cook an additional five minutes or until thoroughly heated.    Cut a 1/4" slice off the top of each kaiser roll; set it aside.  Hollow  out the center of each roll, leaving a 1/2"-thick shell; reserve the  insides of rolls for other uses.  Spoon the bean mixture evenly into the  rolls; cover with the top slices.  Serve immediately.
