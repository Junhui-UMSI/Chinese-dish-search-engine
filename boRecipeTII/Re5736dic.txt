Pineapple-Walnut Muffins
Brown sugar, milk, Egg, Ground nutmeg, (8-oz) crushed pineapple in, Walnuts, Vegetable oil, Bisquick original baking mix, 
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Grease bottoms only of 12 medium muffin cups,  2-1/2x1-1/4 inches, or line with paper baking cups. Beat egg slightly in  large bowl. Stir in remaining ingredients just until moistened. Divide  batter evenly among muffin cups. Bake 15 to 18 minutes or until light  golden brown. 1 dozen muffins.
