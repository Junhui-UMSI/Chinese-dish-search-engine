Pilau Rice
Onion, Chicken Stock, Garlic, Cloves, Butter, Cinnamon, Cardamom pods, Curry leaves, Salt, Basmati rice, 
In a strainer, thoroughly wash rice under running water. In a large  saucepan melt butter over medium heat. Saute onion and garlic 5 minutes,  until tender. Add rice and stir to thoroughly coat with butter mixture. Add  spices and mix well. Add chicken stock, 2 cups water and salt, to taste.  Bring to a boil, cover pan tightly and reduce heat to very low. Cook 15  minutes, until rice is tender and all liquid is absorbed. Using a fork,  fluff rice and serve.
