Tomato Macaroni and Cheese
Macaroni; uncooked, Onions; finely chopped, Skim milk, Lowfat creamed cottage, Garlic, Cheddar cheese; or 2 oz., Tomato sauce, 
(Prevention Magazine May/1997)    Serves 4    Cook macaroni in a large pot of boiling water and drain well. Rinse with  hot water and drain again. Transfer to a large bowl.    In a saucepan, stir together the tomato sauce, onions and garlic. Cook over  meium-low heat, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes, or until the onions  are tender. Pour over the macaroni and toss until coated.    In a small bowl, stir together the milk, cottage cheese and pepper. Add to  the macaroni mixture and stir well.    Transfer half of the macaroni mixture to a 1-1/2 qt. casserole. Sprinkle  with 1/4 c of cheddar. Top with the remaining macaroni mixture and the  remaining 1/4 cup cheddar.    Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
