Albacore Tuna with Mushroom Caper Sauce
Fish stock, flat parsley leaves, Garlic, Tomato, fresh, Tuna steaks, olive oil, Dry white wine, Vegetable oil, Capers, Butter, Salt, shitake mushrooms, 
Brush tuna with vegetable oil, season with salt and pepper and set aside.  Heat olive oil in a saute pan over medium heat. Add mushrooms and saute  for 2 minutes. Add garlic and continue cooking until garlic begins to  brown. Add wine and bring to boil, scraping up browned bits. Add stock and  capers and return to boil, then reduce heat, add butter rolled in flour and  simmer until slightly thickened. Add tomato and season with salt and  pepper. Set aside in a warm place.    Grill tuna over high heat, being careful not to overcook. Arrange on a  serving plat- ter. Spoon sauce over and sprinkle with chopped parsley Serve  at once.
