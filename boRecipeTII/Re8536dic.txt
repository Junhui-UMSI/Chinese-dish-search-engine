Potatoes and Chicken Dijon
Barbecue Sauce, Honey Dijon, Chicken Breast Halves, Boned, Potato, Sliced 1/4" Thick, Dried Tarragon, 
While potatoes cook, in large skillet sprayed with cooking spray, toss and  brown chicken over high heat for 5 minutes. Add potatoes; saute and toss  until potatoes are lightly browned. Add barbeque sauce and tarragon; toss  until heated through.    Recipe by: The Potato Board    Posted to MasterCook Digest V1 #210 by susan@zim.com (Susan Rappaport) on  Feb 8, 1997.
