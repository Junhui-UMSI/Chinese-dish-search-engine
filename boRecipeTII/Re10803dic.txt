Quickie Peach Cobbler
Egg, Walnuts, Butter, Flour, all-purpose, Baking soda, Salt, Peaches, Sugar, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, 
Preheat 325F.  Cream butter and sugar.  Add egg and beat well. Add peaches.  Sift together flour, soda, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt. Stir into peach  mixutre.  Pour batter into buttered 9-inch square pan. Sprinkle nuts on  top. Bake 35-40 min or until center is set.    Recipe By     :
