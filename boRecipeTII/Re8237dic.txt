Potato Leek Chicken
Chicken thighs, Potatos, sliced, Water, Leek, sliced, Leek soup, packet, 
Combine potatoes and leek. Place on bottom of slow cooker. Arrange chicken  over potatoes.    In small bowl, combine leek soup mix and water. Whisk until well mixed.  Pour over chicken. Cover and cook on LOW 5 to 6 hours or until chicken  tender.    Recipe by: Crockery Favorites - 194 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #633 by  Vickie <ec628964@ix.netcom.com> on Jun 03, 1997
