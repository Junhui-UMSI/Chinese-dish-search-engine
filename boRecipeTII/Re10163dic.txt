Quiche Lorraine #1
Grated Swiss cheese, Light cream, Salt, Grated Parmesan cheese, (10-inch) unbaked pie shell;, Squeeze margarine, Bacon; cooked, Eggs; slightly beaten, Butter, Flour, Nutmeg, 
Spread butter on pie shell & bake at 450 for 10 minutes. Crumble bacon into  baked pie shell. Top with grated Swiss cheese. Add cream to flour & add to  eggs. Add seasonings & mix. Pour custard into shell. Bake at 450 for 10  minutes. Reduce heat to 325 & bake 25 minutes longer. Remove from oven &  sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top & squeeze margarine over top. Bake 10  minutes more.    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
