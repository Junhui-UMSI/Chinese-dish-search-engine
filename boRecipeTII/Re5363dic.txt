Pineapple Bavarian
Boiling water, Whipping cream, Sweetened lemon flavored, Few grains salt, Crushed pineapple, Sugar, 
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water.  Add sugar and salt. Mix thoroughly.  Cool until partially set.  Beat until light and fluffy. Fold in pineapple.  Fold in stiffly whipped cream. Pour into mold. Chill. 8 servings.    The Household Searchlight
