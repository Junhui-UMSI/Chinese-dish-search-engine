Baked Hash Brown Casserole--Corrected
cheese, Onions, Whipping Cream, corn flakes, Cream of mushroom soup, Pepper, Frozen hash browns, margarine, Salt, 
Mix together 1/4 cup margarine, salt, pepper, onion, sour cream or whipping  cream, soup and cheese. Blend well.    Butter 9 1/2 x 13 pan and place hash browns in bottom. Pour liquid mixture  over hash browns.    Mix 1/4 cup margarine and then corn flakes and put on top of potato  mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
