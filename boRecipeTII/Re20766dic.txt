Apple Dumplings (Ebl Dumpling)
Baking Powder, Apple, Salt, Cinnamon, Flour, Shortening, milk, Sugar, 
Pare and core apples. Sift flour, baking powder and salt; cut in  shortening, add milk and mix to smooth dough. Turn onto floured board and  divide into six portions. Roll each portion large enough to cover one  apple. Place an apple on each piece of dough; fill with cinnamon and sugar;  wet edges of dough and fold over apple. Place on greased baking sheet, and  bake at 350-F until apples are tender (about 40 minutes). Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
