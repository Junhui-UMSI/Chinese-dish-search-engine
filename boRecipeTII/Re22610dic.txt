Apricot Burritos
Brown Sugar, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, 6 inch tortillas, Water, Granulated sugar, 8oz pkg dried apricots; cut, 
Bring first 6 ingredients to a boil. Simmer uncovered 10 minutes or until  fruit is tender and mixture thickened. Place 1 tablespoon of mixture on one  edge of tortilla. Roll up. Fry in hot oil until golden brown, turning once.  Drain. Serve hot or cold.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #236    Date: Tue, 8 Oct 1996 20:15:00 -0400    From: Ken Raymond <kraymond@mpls.k12.mn.us>
