Broccoli Rice Casserole
Onions, Butter or margarine; divided, Hot cooked Minute? Preium, Condensed cream of mushroom, Frozen chopped broccoli (10, Jar Cheez Whiz? pasteurized, Fresh bread cubes, 
COOK AND STIR onion in 1 tablespoon butter in large skillet over medium  heat until tender Add rice, soup, broccoli and Cheez Whiz; stir    SPOON into 1 1/2 quart casserole dish. Melt remaining 1 tablespoon butter  and toss with bread cubes; sprinkle over casserole.    BAKE at 350F for 30 to 35 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Makes 4 to 6  servings    To Make Ahead: Prepare as directed except for baking; cover. Refrigerate  overnight. Bake, uncovered, at 350F for 50 to 55 minutes or until  thoroughly heated.
