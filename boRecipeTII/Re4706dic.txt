Philly Cheese Steak Sandwich
Flank Steak, Red Onions, Hard Rolls, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic powder, Pasteurized Process Cheese, Black Pepper, 
Preheat broiler. In a small bowl combine Worcestershire sauce, garlic  powder and black pepper. Place steak on a rack in a broiler pan; brush  sauce mixture over sides of steak. Top steak with onions. Broil 4 to 5"  from heat until onions are nearly crisp-tender, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove  onions from meat, placing to one side of broiler pan. Turn steak; broil  until done as desired,, 3 to 5 minutes longer. Place meat on cutting board;  thinly slice diagonally across the grain. Place bottom halves of rolls on  broiler pan, cut sides up; top with beef slices and onions. Spoon cheese  spread over onions. Broil until melted, about 30 seconds.
