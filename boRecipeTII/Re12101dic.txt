Zoo Keepers Chicken
Whole chicken breast,, Nutmeg, Nonfat milk, Sherry, Cornstarch, salt and pepper, 
Brown chicken breast in nonstick skillet that has  been sprayed with vegetable cooking spray.      While chicken is browning, combine milk,  cornstarch, sherry, salt and pepper and nutmeg.  Stir  till well combined. When chicken is thoroughly cooked,  stir milk/cornstarch mixture into skillet, stirring  constantly until the sauce is thickened and bubbly. To  serve sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired. You  can also stir in chopped, cooked broccoli at serving  time, and then just add a salad and a starch.
