Garfield Cafeterias Vegetable Cream Cheese
Philadelphia Cream Cheese, Diced carrot, Diced radishes, Diced scallions, Diced celery; red or green, Minced combined minced dill, Chopped scalliion greens;, 
Let the cream cheese come to room temperature. Fold in the vegetables.  Arrange on a serving dish and garnish with scallion greens. Makes 2 1/2  cups.    Recipe by: The Brooklyn Cookbook    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Joyce Rosenfield <dogsmom@cinci.infi.net>  on Nov 16, 1998,
