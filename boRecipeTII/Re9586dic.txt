Pumpkin Delights
Vanilla, Sugar, Pecans, Canned pumpkin pie mix, Vanilla ice cream; soft, 
Stir pumpkin pie mix. sugar and vanilla into the ice cream. Refreeze. Shape  into balls; freeze. then roll in chopped nuts. Place in a pan, cover with  foil. Keep frozen until ready to serve. Makes 9 servings.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
