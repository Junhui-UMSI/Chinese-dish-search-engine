Beef Casserole #1
Ground beef, (large) tomato sauce, (17-oz) sweet corn, Green bell pepper; diced, Onion; sliced, Salt and pepper to taste, (17-oz) sweet peas, Macaroni & cheese, 
Cook beef, breaking apart into small pieces. Drain off fat. Add tomato  sauce, peas, corn, onion & green pepper. Cook over low heat. Add salt &  pepper to taste. Prepare macaroni & cheese according to package directions.  Put in casserole & mix all ingredients thoroughly. Bake at 325 for 15  minutes. Serves    4.
