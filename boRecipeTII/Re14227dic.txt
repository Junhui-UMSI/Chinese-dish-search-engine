White Rice with Raisins
Of light or dark raisins, Raw regular white rice, Butter, 
Cook rice as package label directs; drain if necessary. Meanwhile  cover raisins with boiling water; let stand 10 minutes; drain. With  fork, stir raisins into drained cooked rice along with butter.    Makes about 8 servings.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/diabetic.zip
