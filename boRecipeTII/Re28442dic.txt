Baked Oatmeal
Applesauce, Grated lemon zest, Salt, Raisins -or-; (up to 1), Vanilla, Eggs worth Ener-g egg, Soy milk, (12 oz) frozen blueberries;, Oatmeal, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Dehydrated lemon peel -or-, Brown sugar; (I use Demerera, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl. Mix the  wet ingredients in another bowl. Stir the wet and dry ingredients together  and add the raisins or blueberries. Spread in a 9x9 pan, lightly sprayed  with nonstick spray. Bake 25-35 minutes. Serve as-is, or top with some sort  of milk.
