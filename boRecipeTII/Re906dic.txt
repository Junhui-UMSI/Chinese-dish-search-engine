Hot Chicken Salad
Cubed cooked chicken, Celery, Pecans, Salt, Grated onions, Green Pepper, Mayonnaise, Chopped pimento, Lemon juice, Grated American cheese, Crushed potato chips, 
Mix all ingredients except cheese and chips. Put into a 2-quart casserole  dish. Mix cheese and potato chips and spread over top of first mixture.  Bake at 350?F for 25 minutes. Serves 8.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Magda <antigone@easynet.fr> on Oct 15,  1998,
