Bcs Rosemary-Wine Marinade
Sugar, Dry red wine or grape juice, Vegetable oil, Or 1 t dried rosemary, Salt, Garlic, Pepper, Chopped fresh rosemary, 
Heat oil in 1 1/2-quart saucepan over medium heat. Cook rosemary and garlic  in oil, stirring frequently, until garlic is tender; remove from heat. Stir  in wine, sugar, salt and pepper.    *Betty Crockers Great Grilling  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #306 by Michael McLaughlin  <mmclaughlin@micronet.net> on Nov 25, 1997
