Tgi Fridays Soy Dressing
Honey, Prepared hot mustard, Soy sauce, Water, Green Onion stems, Peanut oil, Cider vinegar, 
Combine all ingredients in a jar with a tight-fitting lid; shake the jar  vigorously to combine ingredients thoroughly. Keep refrigerated and covered  to use within a few weeks.
