Whitefish Salad
Stalks celery; peeled and, Non-fat sour cream, Lowfat mayonnaise, Smoked whitefish to yield 2, Lemon juice, White pepper; to taste, 
Combne all ingredients except fish and pepper. Add fish. Add pepper to  taste.    Recipe by: Ann Brody(revised-low-fat) Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #232  by Judith Sobel <jcs@mindspring.com> on Aug 17, 1997
