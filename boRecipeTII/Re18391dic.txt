Very Special Southern Batter Bread
Egg whites; beaten well, White cornmeal, Butter, Sugar, Flour, Salt, Baking Powder, Egg yolks; well beaten, Milk; scalded, 
Add butter to scalded milk. Whip yolks until light and add dry ingredients,  alternating with hot milk. Fold in beaten whites. Butter a baking dish and  place bread in 350F oven 30 minutes or longer until browned on top and  sides. Be sure it is completely done. It will be a velvety texture. Serve  hot. It is delicious for breakfast with maple syrup.    Source: Square House Museum Cookbook    Happily scanned and MC formatted, using Buster, by Pamela Creeden 5/7/98    NOTES : This recipe, in Rachel Allens handwriting, noted that it was  dictated to her by Judge James Christopher Paul, who founded the first  commercial bank on the Plains of Texas and whose home was the Square House.
