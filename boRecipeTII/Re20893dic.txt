Apple Nut Bake
Ground almonds, Orange juice, Egg yolk, Cooking apples, 
1.  Peel, core and slice the apple, and cook with the orange juice until  soft.    2.  Mash or puree, stir in the egg yolk and almonds, and bake at 350F for  15 min or until set.  Serve hot  or cold.    To vary: Use other fruit in season.    From "The Best Healthy Baby Cookbook" By Carol Hunter.
