Pineapple-Coconut Chess Pie
All-purpose flour, Crisco, , Butter flavored, Vanilla, Unbaked 9-inch Classic, Yellow cornmeal, Coconut, , flaked, Sugar, FILLING, Eggs, (8 1/2 oz) Crushed, 
Heat oven to 325 Deg. F.    For filling, combine sugar and Butter Flavor Crisco in large bowl. Beat at  medium speed of electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a  time, beating well after each addition. Add cornmeal, flour and vanilla.  Mix well.    Combine undrained pineapple and coconut. Fold into creamed mixture. Pour  into unbaked pie shell. Bake at 325 Deg. F for 45 to 50 minutes or until  knife inserted in center comes out clean. Makes one 9-inch pie.
