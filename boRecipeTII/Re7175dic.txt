Pork and Beans in Cream
Celery rib; chopped, Heavy cream, Egg yolk; beaten, Salt pork; rinsed and diced, Butter, Bay leaf, Fresh grated nutmeg, Onion, salt and pepper, Pink beans (or kidney, 
Saute the salt pork and onion in butter for 5 minutes. combine with the  beans, celery and bay leaf and 1 1/2 quarts water in crock pot. Cover and  cook on high 2 hours, then on low 10 to 12 hours. combine cream, egg yolks,  salt and pepper and nutmeg and add to pot. Cook on high, uncovered, 5  minutes or until thickened. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 282 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 21, 1997
