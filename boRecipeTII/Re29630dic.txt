Tasty Grilled Chicken
White wine, chicken broth, Salt, pepper, Red wine vinegar, Chicken legs, olive oil, Garlic, Lemon, Soy sauce, 
Pat chicken quarters with pepper, rubbing into pieces. In medium bowl, mix  together chicken broth, soy sauce, lemon, olive oil, red wine vinegar,  white wine and garlic. In large bowl, place chicken and pour sauce over  all; let marinate overnight. Place chicken on prepared grill, skin side up,  about 8 inches from heat. Cook, turning and basting with sauce every 10  minutes, about 55-65 minutes or until fork can be inserted with ease.  Sprinkle salt on chicken before serving, if desired. Makes 4 servings.
