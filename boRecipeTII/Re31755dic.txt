From: sstier@usa.pipeline.com (susan stier)    Date: Sun, 28 Jul 1996 14:05:26 GMT  Cream eggs and sugar together. Add oil, mix well . Add vanilla and almond  extract.  Add baking powder to flour,  then add dry mixture a little at a  time to the egg and sugar mix. Add nuts, cherry and/or chocolate chips.  PLACE  IN REFRIGERATOR AT LEAST 2 HOUR    Turn out on well floured board.  With floured hands, divide batter into 6  or 8 strips. Put on greased cookie sheets. Bake 40-45 minutes at 350  degrees, until top is light brown. Remove from oven. Slice while still hot,  into 1/2inch slices. Put back into oven, with heat off for 10-15 minutes.  Remove and let cool. Sprinkle tops with powder sugar.    These cookies store well in a tightly covered tin or plastic container.    JEWISH-FOOD digest 284    From the Jewish Food recipe list.
