Swiss Steak #1
Rice, Vegetable oil, Round steak, Water, Flour, Bell pepper, tomato sauce, salt and pepper, tomatoes, Onion, 
Cut steak into small bite-size pieces. Salt & pepper. Roll in flour & fry  in a small amount of oil until brown. Remove steak from pan & saute bell  pepper & onion in remaining oil until tender. Drain oil. Add cut-up  tomatoes, tomato sauce & water. Put steak back in & simmer for 30 minutes.  Serve over rice.
