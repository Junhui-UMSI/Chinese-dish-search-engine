Raisin Nut Pie
Thin outer rind of 1/4 lemon, Unbaked 9 inch pie shell, Walnuts, Cloves, Sour cream, Cinnamon, Egg yolk, Sherry, Eggs, Seedless raisins, Sugar, 
In covered blender container, blend all ingredients except pie shell at  high speed until nuts are chopped. Pour into pie shell. Bake at 400 F for  15 minutes; reduce temperature to 350 F and bake an additional 35 to 40  minutes until set. Serve slightly warm.
