Italian Flag Cookies
Active dry yeast, Warm water, Sugar, Egg, Flour, Red and green colored sugar, Butter;, (4 sticks) softened, 
Dissolve yeast in warm water. Cream together butter and sugar. Beat until  light and fluffy. Beat in egg. Blend in dissolved yeast. Gradually stir in  flour. Chill dough for about 2 hours. Shape into 3/4" balls. Dip one side  in red sugar, the other in green sugar, leaving a clear stripe down the  center. Place on buttered cookie sheets. Bake in pre-heated 375 degree oven  for 10-12 minutes, or until lightly browned. Remove from oven immediately  to wire racks to cool. Can be frozen.
