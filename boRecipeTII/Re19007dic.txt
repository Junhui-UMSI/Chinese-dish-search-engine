Vegetable Beef Barley Soup
Celery, chopped, Salt, Onion, Carrots, thinly sliced, Pepper, Pearl barley, Mushrooms, chopped, Tomato sauce, Lean beef round, 1 cubes, 
Spray saucepan with non stick, add beef and onion. Saute 5-7 mintues. Add 3  quarts water adn bring to a boil. Stir in celery, carrots,and mushrooms.  Reduce heat to medium-low, cover partially, and simmer 1 hour.    Add remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer 1 hour. Stirring occassionally.
