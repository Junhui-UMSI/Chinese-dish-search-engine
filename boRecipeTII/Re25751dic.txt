Babe Ruth Bars
Butterscotch chips, Light corn syrup, (6 oz.) chocolate chips, Sugar, Peanut butter, Crispy rice cereal, 
Combine sugar and syrup in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Remove from heat  and add the peanut butter and crispy rice cereal. Spread into a  well-greased 9x9 inch baking dish. Melt chocolate and butterscotch chips in  top of a double boiler and spread over the mixture in the pan. Cool and cut  into bars.
