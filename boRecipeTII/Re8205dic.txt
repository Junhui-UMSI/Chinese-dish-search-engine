Potato Gratin
Margarine, Dried thyme leaves, Whipping cream, Salt, Nutmeg, Pepper, Potatoes, peeled and sliced, Onions, sliced, 
Saute onions in margarine. In shallow baking dish arrange potatoes and  onions in layers. Sprinkle with garlic and spices in between layers. Bake  at 350 for 40 minutes. Add whipping cream all over top. Increase temp to  400 bake for another 10-20 minutes.
