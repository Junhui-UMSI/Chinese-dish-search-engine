Aromatic Vinegar
Cinnamon stick, approx 3, Red chili, dried, Ginger, 1 inch slice, fresh, Cloves, whole, Allspice, whole, Coriander seeds, whole, Vinegar, red wine or cider, 
Place all ingredients in quart jar or bottle; seal. Store in dark, cool  location for at least 3 weeks.    Use to make tangy salad dressings or to make meat marinades. Also good to  flavor sauteed fruits.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Judith Vonneumann <pooh4jvn@catlover.com>  on Nov 17, 1998
