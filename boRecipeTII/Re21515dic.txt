Apple Pie Bars
Baking Powder, Butter, apple juice, Sugar, Tart Cooking Apples, Eggs, Ground nutmeg, Sugar, Cornstarch, All-purpose flour, Ground cinnamon, 
FOR FILLING: In a large saucepan, combine apples, apple juice, cinnamon and  nutmeg. Cook over medium high heat until boiling, stirring occasionally.  Combine cornstarch and the 1/2 cup sugar. Stir into boiling mixture, cook  and stir until thickened and bubbly. Remove from heat; cool slightly.    FOR CRUST: In a large mixer bowl stir together flour, the 1 cup sugar,  baking powder and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Add butter and eggs. Beat on low speed  until mixture is blended and crumbly. Press 2/3 of crumb mixture (3 1/2  cups) into a greased 15 x 10 x 1 inch pan. Spread apple mixture evenly over  crust. Sprinkle remaining crumbs over apples. Bake at 375 degrees for 25 to  30 minutes or until golden brown. Cool, cut into bars. Makes 48.
