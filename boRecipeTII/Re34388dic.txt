Bean and Sesame Seed Spread (Hummus)
Salt, (15-oz) garbanzo beans, Sesame seed, Snipped parsley, Lemon juice, Clove garlic; cut into, Pocket bread; crackers or, 
Date: Thu, 25 Apr 1996 11:57:41 -0500    From: dbailey@ssi.parlorcity.com (David Bailey)    Place reserved bean liquid, the sesame seed and garlic in blender  container. Cover and blend on high speed until mixed. Add beans, lemon  juice and salt; cover and blend on high speed, stopping blender to scrapes  sides if necessary, until of uniform consistency. Garnish with parsley.  Serve as spread or dip with wedges of pita bread. Yield: 2 c. spread.
