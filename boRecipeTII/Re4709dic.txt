Philly Chocolate Sauce
milk, Unsweetened Chocolate, 8 oz pkg. Cream Cheese, Vanilla, Sifted powdered sugar, 
Combine cream cheese, milk and chocolate; stir over low heat until smooth.  Blend in remaining ingredients. Serve over poached pears, ice cream or  cake.    Note this sauce can be refrigerated and then reheated.    Makes 2 cups    From Favorite Recipe Philadelphia Cream Cheese
