Ranch Style Chicken Fried Steak and Gravy
salt and pepper, milk, Fat, milk, Round Steak, milk, STEAK, Unbleached flour, GRAVY, Unbleached flour, 
Tenderize round steak.  Dip in milk and then flour, salt and pepper to  taste.  Fry in deep fat at approcimately 375 Degrees F. until golden brown.  After steak is done, pour off fat leaving about 4 T in pan. Add 4 T flour.  Stir until smooth.  Add 1 qt milk. Stir and cook until until thickened for  steak gravy.
