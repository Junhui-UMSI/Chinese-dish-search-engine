Peanut Dressing
Rice vinegar, Fresh cilantro leaves, Hoisin sauce, Mandarin orange juice, Sesame oil, Fresh ginger root, Lite soy sauce, Garlic, Smooth peanut butter, 
Houston Chronicle again...    In a food processor bowl fitted with metal blade and with motor running,  drop garlic and ginger through feed tube; process until minced. Add  cilantro & process 45 seconds or until finely chopped. Add vinegar,  mandarin arange juice, peanut butter, soy sauce, oil & hoisin; process  until smooth. Place dressing in a small bowl; cover & refrigerate until  ready to serve.    Servings: 6    Each: 335 calories, 25 grams protein, 15 grams fat, 28 grams carbohydrates,  47 milligrams cholesterol, 497 milligrams sodium.
