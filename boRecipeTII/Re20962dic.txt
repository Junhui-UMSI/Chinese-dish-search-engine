Apple Pastry
Margarine, Sweet DOUGH MASTER RECIPE, Apples, Cinnamon, Brown sugar, 
Using the Sweet Dough recipe, pat 1/2 of dough into a greased 9-inch pie  plate. Brush with melted margarine. Arrange apples on top of dough and  sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon. Let rise and 1 1/2 hours. Bake at 375  degrees for 12 minutes.
