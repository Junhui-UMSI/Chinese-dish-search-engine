Hungarian Green Beans
frozen green beans, Onions, Vinegar, Sugar, Flour, Fresh dill, Butter, 
Cook beans in salted water until tender, not soft. Drain and set aside.  Heat oil; add onions and saute until limp; add dill. Add enough flour to  make a roux. ( thick paste ) Add 1 cup of water , sugar and vinegar and  stir while the sauce gets thick. Add drained beans, and mix. If too thick,  add a little more water.
