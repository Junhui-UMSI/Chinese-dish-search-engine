Ass Kickin Horseradish Cheese Log
Walnuts, cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, Horseradish Hot Sauce, 
In your food processor, blend cream cheese, cheddar cheese, hot sauce and  1/2 chopped nuts, mix until smooth.  Divide in half, place onto plastic wrap  and roll into a log shape. Refrigerate.  Before serving, remove log fom  plastic wrap and roll into the remainder of the chopped nuts.   Serve with  your favorite crackers and top with more hot sauce for spicier snack!
