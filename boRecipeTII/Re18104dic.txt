Veggie-Bean Sloppy Joes
Underneath, 16 oz. cans Mexican style, Barbeque sauce, Onion chopped, Cooked brown rice, Buns, rolls, corn bread -, Green pepper chopped, Pinto beans, 
Saute onions and green peppers until tender crisp.  Add rice, beans, and  barbeque sauce.  Simmer 10-15 minutes until heated through.  Serve over  your choice of rolls/bread.
