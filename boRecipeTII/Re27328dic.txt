Toad in the Hole
Eggs, Butter, Flour, Salt, milk, Pork sausages, milk, 
Preheat oven to 425. Sift flour and salt into bowl. Beat to smooth batter  with egg, milk, and melted butter. Stir in milk. Arrange sausages in small  shallow baking tin and bake 10 minutes. Remove from oven, pour batter over  sausages, and bake another 30 minutes. Reduce temperature to 400 and bake  another 15-20 minutes. Serve immediately.
