White Sangria Slush
(6 oz) frozen orange juice, Bottle (10 oz) club soda;, 750 milliliter bottle, (12 oz) frozen lemonade, Water, 
Combine first 3 ingredients in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan. Freeze until firm. To  serve, spoon slush mixture into a large pitcher. Add wine and club soda;  stir gently until slushy. Garnish. Yield: about 2 quarts.    formatted and busted by RecipeLu <recipelu@recipelu.com>    Garnish with thin slices of lemon, lime or orange.
