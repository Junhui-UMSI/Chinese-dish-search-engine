Warm Lentil Salad
Salt, Chicken broth, Each finely diced radish,, Red lentils,; picked over, Mustard vinaigrette, Chopped flat leaf parsley, 
Bring the broth to a boil. Add the lentils and bring them back to a boil.  again. Lower the heat and simmer, uncovered just until the color of lentils  changes, about 6 or 7 minutes only.    If there is excess water, then drain it. Transfer the lentils to a bowl and  add the diced radish, and differently colored bell peppers. When at room  temperature, but still somewhat warm, dress with vinaigrette and season to  taste with salt and pepper.    Right before serving, toss with the parsley and serve as a bed for the  chili scallops.
