Ramen Noodle/chicken Salad
cabbage, Ramen noodles seasoning, Rice wine vinegar, chicken, Chopped, Ramen noodles, Canola oil, Onion, Sesame oil, Pepper, Sesame seeds, Slivered almonds, Sugar, 
Chop cabbage, add finely chopped onions and cubed cooked chicken. Add  chopped almonds and crushed ramen noodles. Mix remaining ingredients and  them pour over salad. Mix well. Serve chilled.    Recipe By     : Bonnie
