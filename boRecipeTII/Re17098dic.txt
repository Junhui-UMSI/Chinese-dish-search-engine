Vinegar Candy
Sugar, Vinegar, Nuts; Chopped, Butter, 
1. Combine sugar, vinegar and butter in a heavy saucepan.    2. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture reaches 270 degrees on a candy  thermometer or to the hard crack stage.    3. Fold in nuts and pour onto a buttered cookie sheet.    4. Mark into squares while warm.    5. This is an excellent hard candy.
