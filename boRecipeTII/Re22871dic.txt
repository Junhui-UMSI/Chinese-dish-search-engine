Apricot-Pecan Loaf
Baking Powder, Baking soda, Cooking oil, Egg, Salt, Pecans, chopped, Sugar, Flour, Dried apricots, chopped, Apricot nectar, 
Apricot-Pecan Loaf    Sift together flour, sugar, baking powder, soda, & salt. Add egg, oil, &  apricot nectar & blend well. Add apricots & pecans. Put into greased loaf  pan & bake at 325 degrees for about 1 hour. Cool & slice.         10    servings.    From the New Orleans Times-Picayune Cooking Contest, 1980.    Gail
