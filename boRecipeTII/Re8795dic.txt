Prairie Oyster
Catsup, Cayenne pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Vinegar, Egg yolk, Brandy, Coarse pepper, 
Shake all except egg yolk over ice and strain into old fashoned glass over  two ice cubes. Carefully add yolk without breaking. Should be gulped in one  shot!    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #127 by roy@indy.net (Roy) on May 7, 1997
