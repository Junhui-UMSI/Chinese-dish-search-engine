Baked Tomatoes
Butter, Parmesan cheese, Pepper, Dry mustard, Oregano, Tomatoes, Dry bread crumbs, 
Peel tomatoes & cut into 1/4-inch slices. Lay one layer of slices in a  shallow baking dish. Melt butter & blend remaining ingredients into melted  butter. Sprinkle half this mixture over tomato slices. Add another layer of  tomato slices & use remaining crumb mixture. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.    MRS. EDWARD W.
