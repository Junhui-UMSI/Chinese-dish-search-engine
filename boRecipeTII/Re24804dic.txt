Aunt Lizs Teacakes
Baking soda, Nutmeg, Shortening, Sugar, Salt, Eggs, Flour, Baking Powder, Butter, milk, 
Cream sugar, shortening, butter, eggs and milk together, then add all dry  ingredients. Roll out on floured and sugared board and cut out with cookie  cutters. Preheat oven to 350F oven and bake for 10 to 12 minutes. You can  add food coloring, decorate with sugar, little candies, or frosting, or you  can add flavor extracts such as vanilla, lemon, orange, almond, etc.    Recipe by: Roxannes Aunt Liz    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 399 by Sewgoode  <Sewgoode@aol.com> on Dec 24, 1997
