Almond Liqueur (Ersatz Amaretto)
Water, Vodka, Lemon extract, Sugar, Glycerine (optional), Almond extract, Vanilla extract, 
Makes 1 1/2 pints    Combine sugar and water in saucepan and bring to boil. Simmer, stirring,  for 5 minutes. Let cool to room temperature.    Add remaining ingredients and funnel into bottle(s). Let mature for at  least one week before using (longer is probably better).
