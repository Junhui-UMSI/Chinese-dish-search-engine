Apple/oatmeal Muffins
Oats, Skim milk, Oats, Unsweetened applesauce, Topping, Egg white, Margarine, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, All purpose flour, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Vegetable oil, 
Heat oven to 400^.  Line or grease 12 muffin cups.   Combine oats, flour, cinnamon, baking powder, and baking soda. Add  applesauce, milk, brown sugar, oil, and egg white: mix just until dry  ingredients are moistened. Fill muffin cups almost full. For topping,  combine ingredients:  sprinkle evenly over batter. Bake 20-22 minutes or  until deep golden brown. FROM: TARA LEE (BCGP03B)
