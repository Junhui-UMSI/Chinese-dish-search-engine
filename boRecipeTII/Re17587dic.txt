Almond Granola Bars
Butter, Sweetened condensed milk, canned, almonds, Chocolate chips, Granola, Shredded coconut, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Melt butter in 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan in heated  oven. Sprinkle granola over melted butter and bake 10 minutes. Remove pan  from oven and sprinkle chocolate chips, almonds and coconut over granola  mixture. Pour sweet- ened condensed milk on top, evenly. Bake for 20 to 25  minutes. Cool. Cut into squares.
