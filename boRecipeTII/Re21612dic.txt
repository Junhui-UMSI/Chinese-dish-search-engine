Apple Sauce n Fruit Yogurt Sundae (Motts)
Chunky Apple Sauce, Toasted coconut, Granola, Plain yogurt, Cinnamon, Chopped peanuts, Cut-up fresh fruit; *See, 
Place 1/2 cup of apple sauce in each of 4 individual dessert dishes.    Top each with 1/2 cup fruit and 1/4 cup yogurt.    Sprinkle with cinnamon and coconut.    Notes: FRESH strawberries, bananas, blueberries, pineapple, apples, pear or  any combination. Motts Chunky Apple Sauce. Tempt Breakfast skippers with  this appetizing alternative to typical breakfast fare. For company, set out  the ingredients in bowls and invite guests to create their own yogurt  sundaes.    >Hanneman/Buster 1998-Apr    Recipe by: Motts Applesauce www.motts.com    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by KitPATh <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu> on Apr 18,  1998
