Quick Caesar Salad
Grated Parmesan, Or 2 small bunches romaine;, Caesar dressing, Caesar or garlic-flavored, Black Pepper, 
1. Pour dressing into large salad bowl. Add romaine; toss until coated with  dressing.    2. Sprinkle with croutons, cheese and pepper; toss.    1 Serving: 130 calories (90 calories from fat); 10 g fat (2 g saturated); 5  mg cholesterol; 370 mg sodium; 8 g carbohydrate (2 g dietary fiber); 4 g  protein.    ? Betty Crocker, 1997    NOTES : Instantly turn this favorite into a main dish by topping servings  of the salad with a grilled boneless chicken breast half, leftover pieces  of cooked steak, cooked chicken tenders or cooked and peeled shrimp.
