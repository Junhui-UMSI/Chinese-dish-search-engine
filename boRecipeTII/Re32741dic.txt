Sweet Potato Daisy Biscuits
Bisquick original baking mix, (3-oz) cream cheese; cubed, Orange Peel, Orange marmalade, milk, Mashed cooked sweet potatoes, 
HEAT oven to 450 degrees. Mix baking mix and orange peel. Cut in cream  cheese until mixture resembles coarse meal.  Stir in sweet potatoes and  milk just until dry ingredients are moistened.     TURN dough onto surface dusted with baking mix; gently roll in baking mix  to coat. Shape into ball; knead 4 or 5 times. Roll 1/2 inch thick. Cut with  2-inch round cutter dipped in baking mix. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased  cookie sheet.     MAKE six 1/2-inch cuts around edge of each biscuit to form petals, using  sharp knife dipped in baking mix (cut completely through edge). Make  indentation in center of each biscuit with thumb.  Fill indentation with  1/4 teaspoon marmalade. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. About       20    biscuits.
