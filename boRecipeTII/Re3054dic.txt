Peanut Chutney Roll
Toasted almonds; chopped, Unsalted dry-roasted, Dry mustard, (8-oz) cream cheese;, Curry powder, Chutney; minced, 
Combine first 5 ingredients. Shape into a log. Wrap in waxed paper and  chill 1 hour. Roll log in peanuts and chill overnight.    FROM ELLEN HIRSCH    CHILL OVERNIGHT    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
