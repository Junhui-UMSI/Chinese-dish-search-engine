Babas Angel Wings
Sugar, Cream, Salt, Eggs, Butter, Vanilla, Flour, 
1. Beat egg yolks; add sugar, butter, cream and vanilla beating well.    2. Add enough flour to make a soft dough and knead until smooth.    3. Roll out dough until thin and cut into 3 inch strips.    4. Make a slit in the middle of each stip and pull one end through; twist.    5. Fry in deep hot oil until light brown. Drain on paper towels; sprinkle  with powdered sugar and serve.  Posted to recipelu-digest by RecipeLu <recipelu@geocities.com> on Feb 18,  1998
