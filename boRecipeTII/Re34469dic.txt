Bean Pot Limas
Sliced Onion; (1 1/2 tsp), Water, Dry mustard, Salt, Dark Molasses, Chili Sauce, Hot Bean Liquid, Bacon; Sliced into squares, Baby Lima Beans, Vinegar, 
Soak beans in 6 cups water overnight.  Simmer beans 30 minutes.  Drain,  reserving liquid.  Combine all ingredients.  Pour into 2 quart casserole.  Cover and bake in 300F oven for 2 1/2 hours.  Uncover for last 30 minutes  of baking.
