Apple Pancakes, Or Waffles
Flour, Wheat germ, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Salt, Sugar, Cinnamon, Eggs , Buttermilk, Vegetable oil, tart apple, 
Recipe by: HOOD RIVER VALLEY APPLE RECIPES       Stir together all dry ingredients.  Mix eggs, buttermilk, and oil; add  to dry ingredients and mix gently. Fold in grated apple. Cook on lightly  oiled griddle. For waffles, double ingredients and freeze any leftover  waffles; reheat on low setting in toaster. (One of my kids favorite quick  breakfasts!)
