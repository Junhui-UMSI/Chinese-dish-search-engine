Sweet and Sour Pot Roast
Raisins, Pot roast, Garlic, Clear stock, Bay leaf, Dark brown sugar, Ketchup, Large onions, Vinegar, 
Brown beef on all sides in some fat in heavy pan.  Add onions and garlic,  diced, and brown.  Add stock and bay leaf and simmer 1 hr , covered  tightly.  Add more stock if necessary. Add ketchup, vinegar and sugar and  simmer unil tender, adding raisins the last 15 minutes.
