Zucchini and Tomato Casserole
Large tomatoes, sliced, Black Pepper, Dried leaf basil, Snipped chives, Low fat margarine, Zucchini, cubed, Shredded low fat cheddar, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Steam zucchini until crisp tender. Using a slotted  spoon, transfer zucchini to a shallow 2 quart casserole. Stir pepper and  chives into margarine and pour over zucchini. Cover with tomato slices;  sprinkle with basil and cheese. Bake, uncovered, 30 min. Cal 54; Fat: 2 g.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmealnew.zip
