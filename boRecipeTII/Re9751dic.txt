From: law3@lehigh.edu (LISA A WERLEY)    Date: Wed, 23 Nov 1994 14:45:44 +0000  Beat eggs in sugar for 5 minutes.  Stir in pumpkin, flour, soda and  cinnamon.  In jelly roll pan, lined with wax paper, greased and floured,  spread batter.  Put chopped nuts on top if desired.  Bake at 375 for 15  minutes.  Take a linen towel sprinkle with powdered sugar.  Roll cake in  towel and cool for 1/2 hour.    FILLING: Unroll and spread with filling.  Reroll without towel.  Wrap in  aluminum foil and refrigerate.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar when serving.
