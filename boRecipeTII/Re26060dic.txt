Tofu Mayonnaise
Yogurt;plain, nonfat, Vinegar, Soft tofu, Dijon mustard, Vegetable oil, Garlic, -Salt, 
Use whenever regular mayonnaise is called for. In food processor, combine  all ingredients except for oil until smooth. with motor running, slowly  drizzle oil through feed tube until mixture is creamy. Refrigerate in  covered container. Mixture thickens when chilled. MAKES: 2 cups
