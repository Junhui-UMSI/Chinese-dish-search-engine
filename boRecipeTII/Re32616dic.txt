Sweet Onion Relish Vidalia
Mustard Seeds, Coriander, ground, Celery seed, Onions, sweet; chopped, Vinegar, white, Sugar, Brown, Ajawein Seed, Pepper flakes, red, Allspice, ground, 
Mix together all ingredients. Pack into a 1 quart glass or plastic  container. Refrigerate for 1 week before using.    Nutrition per 1/4 cup calories 35 protein 0 grams fat 0 grams         sodium 3 m_grams carbohydrate 8 grams    cholesterol 0 m_grams        fiber  0 grams    Notes and Credits To retain maximum crunch, this relish is not  heat-sterilized, so it must be kept refrigerated.    If you are looking for the sweetest onion, always select the flattest  shape. The more rounded or globular it is, the hotter it generally is.    Sometimes the tops of the onions almost seem to be slightly depressed in  the center. They will have the thickest layers and the sweetest flavor.
