Ancini Di Pepe
Cooked -- drained & rinsed, Can Crushed Pineapple, Eggs, Well, Sugar, Ancini Di Pepe Macaroni,, Conatiner Cool Whip, Flour, Can Mandarin Oranges, Salt, 
Cook juice from crushed pineapple and mandarin oranges with sugar, flour,  eggs, and salt until thick. Pour over macaroni and fruit mixture. Cool 4  hours or overnight. Add 1 small conatiner of cool whip. Mix well and  refrigerate    Recipe By     : Kathy Weeks <KWEEKS@OA.ITHACA.EDU>
