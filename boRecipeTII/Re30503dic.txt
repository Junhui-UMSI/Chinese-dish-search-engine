Taco Sauce
Chili powder, Salt, Cayenne pepper, Tomato paste, Cider vinegar, Corn syrup, Water, 
Combine all ingredients in a large sauce pot.  Bring to a boil. Reduce heat  and simmer about 1 hour or until thick. Stir frequently as mixture thickens  to prevent sticking. Pour hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4 inch head space.  Adjust caps.  Process 30 minutes in boiling water bath. Yield: about 6 half  pints.    From: Ball Blue Book Shared By: Pat Stockett
