Apple Nut Coffee Cake
Topping:, Baking Powder, Cinnamon; ground, Eggs, Apple; finely chopped, Sour cream, Nuts; chopped, Flour, Shortening, Sugar, Vanilla, Baking soda, -Salt, Brown Sugar, Butter, 
Fat grams    per serving:              Approx. Cook Time: :40    Preheat oven to 350F. In mixing bowl, cream together shortening and  sugar. Add eggs and vanilla, beat well. Combine flour, baking powder,  baking soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture, alternating with sour cream.  Fold in apple. Spread batter in greased 13 X 9 X 2" baking pan or large  greased bundt pan. Combine nuts, brown sugar, cinnamon and butter. Sprinkle  nut mixture evenly over batter. Bake 350F for 35-40 minutes.
