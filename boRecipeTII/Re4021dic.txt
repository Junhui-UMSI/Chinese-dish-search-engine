Pepperidge Farm Party Rolls
Pepperidge Farm Party Rolls, Worcestershire, Sticks butter, room, Poppy seeds, Onion, very finely chopped, Prepared mustard, Good quality ham, finely, 
Thoroughly blend the first 6 ingredients. Take rolls out of package and,  without breaking apart, cut in half horizontally. Spread the insides of the  rolls with the mixture and replace the tops. Heat at 400 degrees for about  10 minutes. Separate into individual rolls to serve.    Posted to EAT-L Digest 19 Sep 96    From:    "H.
