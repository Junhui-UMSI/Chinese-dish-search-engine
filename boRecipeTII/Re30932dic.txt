Tao Hoo Yod Sai (Stuffed Tofu with Ground Pork)
Salt, Tofu, Coriander, Pepper, white, Oil, vegetable, Pork, ground, Egg, Cornstarch, Garlic, Scallions, 
Cut tofu into 1" cubes, scoop center of each cube out, set aside to outer  shells. Mix scooped out tofu, pork, garlic, scallion, coriander, salt,  pepper, cornstarch and egg. Stuff back into tofu shells. Heat oil in wok  over medium heat, deep fry the stuffed tofu until golden brown. Drain on  paper towels. Serve with Nam Chim Priew Waan (recipe).
