Aunt Evelyns Divinity
Vanilla, Sugar, Walnuts, Water, Egg whites, beaten, White Karo Syrup, 
Boil sugar, water, anbd Karo syrup unti it forms a soft ball whien treid in  cold water.  Beat egg whites till stiff and slowly add 1/2 of syrup  mixture, beating constantly. Cook remainder until if forms a hard ball in  cold water and add slowly to first mixture. Beat until stiff. Add vanilla  and walnuts. Pour onto buttered pan or platter. Cut when cool. Cook in  clear weather -- low humidity.    Aunt Evelyn often made this as a treat for the girls. Bumgarner Family  Recipes sumbitted by the Aunt Evelyns daughters    posted by Bud Cloyd
