Sweet and Spicy German Mustard
Salt, Tarragon, Tumeric, Brown sugar, Cider vinegar, Garlic, Mustard seed, Dry mustard, Cold water, Cinnamon, Honey, Allspice, 
Combine seeds and mustard with cold water.  Allow to sit refrigerated  overnight.    Combine all remaining ingredients except honey. Simmer, uncovered 10-15  minutes, reduce volume by one half. Pour this mixture through a strainer,  into mustard and seed mixture. Blenderize thoroughly. Cook in double boiler  until consistancy of gravy.  Add Honey. Will keep refrigerated several  years.
