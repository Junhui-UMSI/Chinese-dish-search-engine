Apple, Sausage Omelet
French or plain omelet, Sausage drippings, Bulk pork sausage, Applesauce, 
Cook sausage in 10-inch omelet pan or skillet until thoroughly cooked and  browned. Drain reserving 1 tablespoon drippings. Crumble sausage. Reserve  1/4 cup. Combine remain- ing sausage with applesauce, set aside. Prepare  French omelet using reserved sausage drippings in place of butter. While  top is still moist and creamy looking, spread sausage-applesauce mixture  over half of omelet. With pancake turner fold omelet in half or roll  turning out onto platter with a quick flip of the wrist. Sprinkle remaining  sausage over top. Makes 2 servings.
