Avocado Salsa
Salt, Lime juice, Tomatillos; roasted & peeled, Avocados; peeled, Garlic cloves; minced or 2, Vegetable oil, White pepper, 
Saute onion in oil. Cut tomatillos in half and add to onion, lime juice and  seasonings. Cook 2 minutes, then set aside to cool. Peel and put avocados.  Add tomatillos with onion mixture to food processor. Puree until smooth.  Allow sauce to stand 1 hour before serving.
