Apple-Walnut Drop Cookies
Light brown sugar; packed, Apple juice or milk GLAZE, Salt, Dark raisins; chopped, Ground cloves, Apple; chopped finely,, Ground cinnamon, Ground nutmeg, All-purpose flour; sifted, Eggs, Vanilla extract, Confectioners sugar; sifted, Walnuts, GLAZE, Salt, Butter or margarine, Baking soda, milk, Soft shortening, 
1. Preheat oven to 400 F. Lightly grease several cookie sheets.    2. sift flour with baking soda. Mix shortening, brown sugar, salt,  cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and eggs until well blended.    3. Stir in half of flour mixture, then nuts, apple, and raisins. Blend in  apple juice or milk, then remaining flour mixture.    4. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto cookie sheets, 2 inches apart.    5. Bake 11 to 14 minutes, or until done. While cookies are still hot,  spread thinly with Glaze. Decorate with bits of apple as pictured, if  desired. Makes 3 1/2 to 4 dozen.    GLAZE:    1. In small bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well.    2. Use to glaze Apple-Walnut Drop Cookies.
