Waldorf Salad
Celery, Walnuts, LORELI AGUDA WSKD49A, Salad dressing, Lemon juice, Raisins, Winesap apples, 
Peel and dice apples. Sprinkle with lemon juice; add celery and raisins and  toss together. Cover and chill. Just before serving, add nuts and salad  dressing. Toss lightly to distribute dressing. Serve on crisp lettuce cups  or from salad bowl.
