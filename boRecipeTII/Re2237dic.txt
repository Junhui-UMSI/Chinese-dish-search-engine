Baked Lemon Pudding (Diabetic)
Eggs, Lemon juice, Skim milk, Low fat butter, Salt, Liquid sweetener, Flour, 
Combine egg whites, salt and sweetener. Beat well until peaks form. Combine  yolks with remaning ingredients. Fold egg yolks into whites. Pour into  quart casserole and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hr. Cool before serving.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Deb <leadfoot@alpha.ultraweb.net> on Aug  26, 1998,
