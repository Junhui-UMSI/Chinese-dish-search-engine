Vegetable Dip Recipe
Salt, Sour cream, Dill weed, Cream cheese, Cucumber; peeled, seeded,, Dry mustard, Onion, Hot sauce, 
Peel and remove seeds from cucumber.  Shred or grate them fine.  Dice the  onion fine.  Combine and mix well with all ingredients.    Refrigerate.    Good as small sandwich spread also.  Or as base for a cold vegetable pizza.    5-27-98 entered Contributor: Chef Keith Cochran Preparation Time: 00:30    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Keith Cochran" <kwcten@hotcom.net> on May  27, 1998
