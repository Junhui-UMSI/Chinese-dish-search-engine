Twenty-Four Hour Potato Salad
Bell pepper; cut in rings, Mayonnaise, Onion; chopped or in rings, Cabbage; shredded, Cooked potatoes; thinly, Boiled eggs; thinly sliced, 
This salad is in layers. Start with cabbage. Spread mayonnaise on top.  Layer ingredients putting thin layer of mayonnaise between each layer  (cabbage, mayo, potatoes, mayo, eggs, mayo, onion, mayo, etc.) Refrigerate  for 24 hours or overnight.
