Wheat Free Cupcakes
Argo cornstarch, Sugar, Margerine, Baking Powder, Vanilla, C, A, milk, Egg, B, 
Grease 8 cupcake pans Preheat oven to 375    Sift "A" together in bowl. Stir "B" together in another bowl until blended.  Then beat in "C".    Add cornstarch mixture and mix smooth. Turn into prepared cup cake pans.  Bake for 12 to 15 minutes at 375    Serve with whipped cream and fruit Posted to EAT-L Digest 02 Apr 97 by Jean  Jones <bruja@DPLUS.NET> on Apr 2, 1997
