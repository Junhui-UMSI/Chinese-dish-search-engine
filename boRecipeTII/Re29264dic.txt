Baked Stuffed Catfish
Stalks celery chopped, Chilies, Whole catfish, cleaned and, Can tomatoes with or without, Head removed, Bell pepper chopped, Green onion tops chopped, Butter, To taste salt, black pepper, And cayenne pepper, Parsley chopped, Made), Onions chopped, Shrimp and crab stuffing, Tomato sauce (canned or home, (recipe follows), 
Saute onion, celery and bell pepper in butter until onions are  transparent; add remaining ingredients except fish and stuffing and  simmer 20 - 30 minutes Season fish with salt, black pepper and  cayenne pepper, then stuff cavity with shrimp and crab stuffing.  Place fish in foil lined baking dish and pour tomato mixture over  top; close foil over fish tightly and bake at 350 for about 30 to 45  minutes.
