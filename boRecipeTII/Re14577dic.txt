"Hot Dogs"
Garlic; minced, Egg replacer equal to 2 eggs, Chickpeas (1 lb), Slices white bread; trimmed, Pepper, Oil for deep frying, Ground red peppers; diced, Salt, 
In blender grind chick peas and bread together. Mix in egg substitute,  garlic, red peppers, salt and pepper.   Shape into finger length rolls and chill 1 hour. Heat the oil to 370 deg  in deep fryer and carefully drop the "hot dogs" into it. Fry until browned;  drain.   Serve with mustard or hot chili sauce. Makes about 20.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
