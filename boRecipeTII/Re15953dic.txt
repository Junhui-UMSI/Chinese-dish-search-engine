Adobo Marinade
Lime juice, Ground cumin, Garlic, Pepper; to taste, 
Blend cumin, garlic and lime juice until smooth.  Taste and season with  pepper.  Marinade fish (chicken or meat) in the refrigerator for 1 hour.    ~-[mcRecipe / patH 24 Au 96 ]  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #216    Date: Sat, 24 Aug 1996 12:36:38 -0700 (PDT)    From: PatH <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu>
