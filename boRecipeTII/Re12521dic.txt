Yemenite Charoset
Matzo meal, Passover Concord grape wine, Sesame seeds, Red chile, minced OR pinch, Ginger pinch coriander, Dried figs -- chopped, Pitted dates, chopped, 
Combine dates, figs and wine in large bowl. Add sesame seeds, ginger,  coriander, chile and matzo meal and blend thoroughly. Roll into 1-inch  balls or serve in bowl. Formatted by Lynn Thomas dcqp82a. Source: Los  Angeles Times 4-16-97. Makes 12 balls. Each ball: 75 calories; 2 mg sodium;  0 cholesterol; 1 gram fat; 16 grams carbohydrates; 1 gram protein; 0.62  gram fiber.    Recipe by: Los Angeles Times 4-16-97 Sent to me by "Jack C.
