Angel Food Cake #1
Vanilla, Sugar, Cake flour, Sugar, Egg whites, Salt, Cream of tartar, 
From: jbalt@netaccess.on.ca (Jack Balt)    Date: Wed, 24 May 1995 17:59:13 GMT    Sift flour with 1 c sugar four times. Set aside. Beat whites with salt till  foamy, then add cream of tartar and beat till stiff. Add the 3/4 c sugar 1  T at a time, beating into egg whites. Add vanilla. Sift flour and sugar  mixture over top of whites a little at a time and fold in. Bake in an  ungreased 10" tube pan at 325 for 65-75 minutes. Invert pan to cool. Serves  12.
