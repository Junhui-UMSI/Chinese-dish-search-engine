Popcorn Nut Munchy Mixture
Peanuts, Butter, Popped corn, Honey, 
1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees. 2. Mix popcorn and nuts together in a very  big  bowl.  Warm honey and butter together over low heat until the butter  is melted.  Pour the honey-butter mixture over the popcorn-nut mixture.  Toss to mix well. 3. Spread mixture in a large baking pan and bake for 20  minutes. stirring once or twice during baking.  Cool and  break into chunks  before serving or storing in an airtight container. Mixture will keep for 2  weeks on the pantry shelf. enjoy    Recipe By     : Winebert    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
