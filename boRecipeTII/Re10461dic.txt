Quick Curry
Dill weed, Salt, Shrimp, milk, Curry powder, Pineapple tid-bits, Flour, Butter, Rice, 
Melt butter in saucepan. Remove from heat; Stir in flour and seasonings.  Add a little milk and stir to blend. Gradually stir in the remaining milk  Cook and stir until mixture boils and thickens. Add shrimp (or clams or  chicken) and pineapple tid-bits. Heat through. Serve on rice. If desired,  serve with condiments such as chopped peanuts, coconut and chutney.
