Trout Stuffed with Garlic and Spinach
Garlic cloves; peeled and, Lemon; cut in 4 wedges, Salt and black pepper, Flour, Spinach; rinsed and trimmed, Butter, Trout fillets; (bones and, olive oil, 
Blanch the garlic slivers in boiling water 3 minutes. Drain, rinse with  cold water, and rinse again. Wilt the spinach leaves in a large dry pot  over high heat, turning occasionally, 3 minutes. Remove from heat, squeeze  out excess water and roughly chop. Combine in small bowl with garlic  slivers, salt, and pepper. Preheat oven to 400F. Season inside of trout  with salt and pepper. Divide spinach mixture and tuck inside fish. Tie with  string around center of each to hold stuffing. Dust the outsides with flour  and season with salt and pepper. Melt butter and oil in 2 large skillets  over high heat. Fry the fish about a minute per side. Transfer to oven and  cook 4 minutes, turning after 2 minutes. Serve hot with lemon wedges.    Recipe from THE TOTALLY GARLIC COOKBOOK, by Helene Siegel and Karen  Gillingham (C) 1994, Ten Speed Press.
