Raisin Cookie Pie
Baking soda, Vanilla ice cream, Butter, 9 inch pie crust, Walnuts -- chopped, Raisins, Salt, Brown Sugar, Flour, Sugar, Egg, Vanilla extract, 
Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt. In a large bowl, cream butter  with sugars. Beat in egg and vanilla. Gradually add flour mixture and mix  well. Stir in raisins and nuts. Spread into pie crust. Bake for 20 to 25  minutes at 375 degrees. Cool and serve with ice cream.
