Pork Sausage Noodle Casserole
Celery, Pork sausage, American cheese, Onions, Cream of mushroom soup, Egg noodles, milk, 
Most sausage has enough pepper and salt; no more is needed. Fry sausage;  break into small pieces. As sausage browns, add onion and celery. Cook  until tender. Cook egg noodles as directed on package. Mix soup with milk  and add to sausage. In casserole layer half of the noodles, half of sausage  mixture and half cheese, broken in pieces. Repeat layers. Cover and bake at      350    degrees for 30 minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
