Portuguese-American Stuffing
Black pepper, Eggs, Onion, portuguese bread, Chopped parsley, Turkey liver and gizzard, Garlic, Chourico sausage, olive oil, Paprika, 
Soak bread in water until soft.  Squeeze dry and break up. Set aside.  Simmer sausage, liver, and gizzard in water until tender. Drain and set  aside.  In a large frying pan saute onion in oil until soft. Add Garlic and  parsley.  Stir in spices and meat.  Add beaten eggs. Return all ingredients  to frying pan and cook until mixture is slightly dry before stuffing the  turkey.  Stuff a 12 pound turkey.
