Bananas with Green Split Peas
Ripe bananas, Onion, thinly sliced, Dried green split peas, Palm oil, Salt, 
Cook peas in enough water to cover until tender, about 1 1/2 hours. Peel  bananas & lay them whole on top of the peas without mixing. Cook for 10  minutes.  Add water if necessary to prevent sticking. Meanwhile, heat the  oil & brown the onions.  With a slotted spoon, remove the bananas & peas &  add to the onions. Sprinkle in the salt & cook over medium heat, stirring  gently but constantly, until the oil has been abosrbed. Serve hot.
