Ramons Crab and Shrimp Casserole
Butter, Onion, Finely chopped Green Bell, Cloves Garlic, finely, (10.75 oz) Cream of Mushroom, Cooked Crab Meat, Cooked Shrimp, Rice, Chopped Pimientos, Finely chopped Italian, "Louisiana" Cajun Seasoning, Breadcrumbs, 
A) Start the rice.    B) Saute onion, green pepper, celery, and garlic until limp.    C) Add soup and stir well.    D) Add crab meat, shrimp, hot rice, pimientos, and parsley. Mix well.    E) Pour into greased casserole dish and top with breadcrumbs.    F) Bake 20 minutes in pre-heated 400 degree oven.
