Vinegar Cookies
Flour, Margarine, White vinegar, Baking soda, Sugar, 
In a large bowl with mixer on low speed beat margarine and sugar until  blended. Increase mixer speed to high and beat for 3 minutes until light  and fluffy. At low speed, beat in flour, vinegar, and baking soda until  mixed. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour.    Drop dough by rounded teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheet, bake 17 to 20  minutes in a 350 degree oven until edges are golden. Makes about 4 dozen  cookies.
