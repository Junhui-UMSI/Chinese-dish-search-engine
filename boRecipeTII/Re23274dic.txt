Tuna Pizza with Caponata and Prosciutto
Crumbled feta cheese, (7 1/2 oz.) caponata, (6 oz.) starkist solid white, (1 oz.) prosciutto, olive oil, Plum tomatoes; sliced 1/4,, Shredded mozzarella cheese, Crushed red pepper, 12-inch italian bread shell, 
1. Place bread shell on foil-lined baking sheet; brush to edge with oil.    2. Spread caponata to within 1 inch of edge.    3. Top with tuna, prosciutto, tomatoes, feta and mozzarella cheeses.    4. Bake in a 450 degree F oven 10 to 12 minutes or until cheeses are melted  and pizza is heated through. Cool 1 minute before slicing.    5. Serve with crushed red pepper, if desired.
