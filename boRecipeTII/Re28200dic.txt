Baked Fig Pudding
RESIFT WITH, Salt, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Butter, Molasses, ADD, Broken black walnut meats;, Nutmeg, Eggs, Baking Powder, BEAT UNTIL SOFT, Finely chopped figs, Lemon Rind, SIFT BEFORE MEASURING, Buttermilk, All-purpose flour, ADD AND BEAT UNTILL FLUFFY, 
Source: Joy of Cooking Irma S. Rombauer - Marion Rombauer Becker    Preheat oven to 325    One teaspoon ginger may be substitued for the cinnamon and nutmeg. Stir the  sifted ingredients into the pudding mixture. Bake in a greased 9-inch tube  pan for about 1 hour. Serve hot with: Brown Sugar Hard Sauce, Sabayon Sauce  or Hot Wine Sauce    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #329 by "Wajnberg"  <wajnberg@ns1.centroin.com.br> on Dec 19, 1997
