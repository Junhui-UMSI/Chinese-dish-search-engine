Banana Chocolate Muffins
Salt, All purpose flour, Semi-sweet chocolate chips, milk, Cooking oil, bananas, Baking Powder, Granulated sugar, Egg, 
Measure first five ingredients into large bowl. Mix thoroughly and make a  well in center. Beat egg until frothy. Mix in oil, milk and banana. Pour  into well. Stir only to moisten. Batter will be lumpy. Fill greased muffin  tins 3/4 full. Bake in 400 degree oven for 20 to 25 minutes. Yield 12 - 24  muffins (depending on size).
