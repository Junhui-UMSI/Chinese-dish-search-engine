Western Chili Casserole
Cheddar cheese, Ground beef, Celery, chili without beans, canned, Corn chips, Onions, Pepper, 
Brown meat, add celery and 3/4 cup of onions; saute until just tender.  Drain off excess fat. Add chili and pepper and stir over heat. Place a  layer of chips in an ungreased 1-1/2 quart casserole. Alternate layers of  chili mixture, chips, and cheese, reserving 1/2 cup chips and 1/4 cup  cheese for trim. Sprinkle center with remaining cheese and onions. Cover  and bake at 350~ for 10 minutes or until piping hot. To serve, border  casserole with corn chips.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
