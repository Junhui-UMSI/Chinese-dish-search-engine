"Ketch" a Clam Dip
minced clams, Garlic powder, Heinz tomato ketchup, cream cheese, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
Blend ketchup gradually into cream cheese. Add lemon juice, garlic powder  and clams. Cover and chill. Makes about 1 1/2 cups.    Recipe by: More Favorite Brand Name Recipes Cookbook    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #888 by "Crane Walden <cranew@foothill.net>"  <cranew@foothill.net> on Nov 7, 1997
