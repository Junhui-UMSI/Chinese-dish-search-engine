Chocolate Peanut Butter Cookie Mix in a Jar
Brown Sugar, Firmly packed powdered, Flour mixed with, Baking powder and, Salt, 
Yield: 1 Jar    *(clean inside of jar with a paper towel after this layer)    Layer ingredients in order given in a 1 quart wide mouth canning jar. Press  each layer firmly in place before adding the next ingredient.    Directions given to recipient:    Empty jar into large mixing bowl, use your hands to thoroughly mix.Add 1  stick soft butter, 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter, 1 egg slightly beaten and  1 tsp vanilla (optional).Mix until completely blended--you will need to  finish mixing with your hands Shape into balls the size of walnuts Place 2"  apart on sprayed cookiessheets Bake 350 for 9-11 min til edges are lightly  browned--cool 5 min on baking sheet and remove cookies to racks to cool  completely Makes 3 dozen cookies    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by valerie@nbnet.nb.ca (valerie) on Sep 30,  1998,
