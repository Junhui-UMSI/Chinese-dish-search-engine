Tomato Pizza Sauce
Italian plum tomatoes, Black Pepper, Garlic, olive oil, 
In a large heavy saucepan cook the garlic and the pepper in the oil over  moderately low heat, stirring, for 2 minutes, or until the mixture is  fragrant. Add the tomatoes, squashing them in your hand,    with the juice, bring the mixture to a boil, and cook it over moderately  low heat, stirring occasionally, for 20 to 30 minutes, or until the sauce  is thickened and reduced to about 2 cups. Season the sauce with salt    . The sauce keeps, covered and chilled, for 1 week.
