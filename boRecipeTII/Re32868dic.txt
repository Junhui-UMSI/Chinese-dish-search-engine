Sweet Potatoes in Orange Cups
Ground cloves, Butter at room  temperature, Pepper, Oranges, Cooked sweet potatoes, Orange juice, Paprika, Salt, Dark brown sugar, Ground nutmeg, 
Halve and squeeze oranges, save juice and orange cups. Put orange cups in  large buttered baking dish. Combine in a bowl, using mixer, the sweet  potatoes, orange juice, butter, brown sugar, nutmeg, cloves, salt, and  pepper.  Mix until smooth.  Fill the orange cups with sweet potato mixture.  Can be frozen at this point or bake in a 350F oven, uncovered for 20 to 30  minutes.  Sprinkle with paprika before serving.      Yield: 16 servings.
