Zucchini Tomato Medley
Tomatoes; cut in wedges, Chopped fresh basil, Pepper, Zucchini; sliced, Salt, Onions, Margarine or butter, Dried basil leaves -or-, 
Melt margarine in large skillet. Add onion and zucchini; cook and stir  over medium-high heat until crisp-tender.     Add remaining ingredients; cook until tomatoes are thoroughly heated.
