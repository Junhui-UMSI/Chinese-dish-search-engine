Tropical Tea Warmer
Honey, Unpeeled, Orange juice, Tea bags, Pineapple juice, Orange -- sliced and, Boiling water, Sugar, 
Pour boiling water over tea bags in slow-cooking pot.  Cover and let stand  5 minutes.  Remove tea bags.  Stir in sugar, honey, orange juice, pineapple  juice, and orange slices.  Cover and heat on low for 2 to 3 hours; serve  from pot.
