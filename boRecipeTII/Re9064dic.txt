Prize Pumpkin Pie
(14oz/398mL) Pumpkin Puree, Eggs, Salt, Condensed Milk, sweetened, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Ginger, ground, Cloves, ground, Brown Sugar, 9" (23cm) Pie Shell, Unbaked, 
In large bowl, beat eggs; blend in pumpkin, condensed milk, sugar,  cinnamon, salt, cloves, ginger and nutmeg. Pour into pie shell. Bake in  425F (220C) oven for 15 minutes; reduce heat to 375F (190C) and bake for  about 35 minutes longer or until pastry is golden and knife inserted into  centre comes out clean.  Let cool on rack. Make 8 servings.
