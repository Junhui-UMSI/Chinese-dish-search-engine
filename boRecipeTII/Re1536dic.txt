Brown Rice Muffins
Brown rice; cooked, cold, Skim milk; or substitute, Egg white; beaten-or, Honey, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Whole wheat flour, Baking Powder, 
Combine rice, milk, egg whites, honey and vanilla. Add dry ingredients and  stir just enough to moisten. Pour into non-stick muffin pans or pans  lightly misted with cooking spray. Bake at 400 deg F for 25-30 minutes.
