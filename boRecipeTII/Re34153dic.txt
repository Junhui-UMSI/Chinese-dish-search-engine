Bavarian Beef Sandwich
Lettuce, Sub buns, Pkg soft cream cheese, Roast beef slices, Sour cream, Rye bread or, Horseradish, Red onion slices, 
Combine cream cheese, sour cream, and horseradish, mixing until well  blended. For each sandwich, spread bread with the the cream cheese mixture,  add the meat, lettuce and onions.
