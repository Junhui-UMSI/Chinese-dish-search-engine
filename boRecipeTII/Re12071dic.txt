Ziti Casserole
Water, Mushrooms, Ground turkey, Onions, Salt, Chopped parsley, Cooked ziti, Lean ground chuck, Vegetable cooking spray, (6 oz.) tomato paste, Pepper, (14 1/2 oz.) whole tomatoes,, Garlic, Fresh grated Parmesan cheese, Green Pepper, Italian seasoning, 
Cook ground chuck and turkey in a Dutch oven over medium heat until  browned, stirring to crumble. Drain and pat dry with paper towels; set  aside. Coat pan with cooking spray; add onion, mushroom, bell pepper and  garlic; saute 4 minutes until tender. Return turkey mixture to pan, add  water and next 6 ingredients. Bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer,  uncovered, 25 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and cool  slightly. Add pasta; stir well. Spoon into 13"x9"x2" baking dish, cover  with heavy aluminum foil; cut 3 slashes in foil. Bake at 375 degrees for 50  minutes or until thoroughly heated. Uncover; top with cheese. Cover and let  stand 5 minutes.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #186 by Julie Bertholf  <jewel1@ix.netcom.com> on Jul 18, 1997
