Power Punch
Fifth Of Soda Water, Fifth Of Whiskey, Fifth Of Champagne, Oranges, Lemons, Fifth Of Brandy, Fifth Of Sauterne, Fifth Of Sherry, 
1. Mix and chill the first four ingredients overnight.    2. At serving time, add chilled champagne and soda water.    3. Stir. Into each glass place a slice of orange and slice of lemon.    Makes 40 (4-ounce) servings.    One or two servings is all you need per person. Not suggested for young or  weak at heart.    One of the pleasant traditions of the old Army.
