Bacon & Cheddar Sandwich
SALAD DRESSING #2 1/2, RELISH PICKLE SWEET, BREAD SNDWICH 22OZ #51, CHEESE CHEDDER, -, PIMENTOS 7 OZ, 
1.  PREPARE BACON ACCORDING TO DIRECTION ON RECIPE CARD NO. L00200 OR  L00300.    2.  COMBINE BACON AND CHEESE; ADD CHOPPED, CANNED PIMIENTO, SWEET PICKLE  RELISH, AND SALAD DRESSING.    3.  SPREAD 1 SLICE BREAD WITH 1/4 CUP (1-NO. 16 SCOOP) MIXTURE; TOP WITH  SECOND SLICE OF DRESSING.    4.  CUT EACH SAND WITH IN HALF; REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.
