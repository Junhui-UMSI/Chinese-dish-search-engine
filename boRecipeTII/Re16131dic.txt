Warm Spinach and Rice Chicken Salad
Italian salad dressing, Pitted ripe olives; halved, Fresh spinach; chopped, Plum tomatoes; chopped, Frozen chicken breasts;, 
Prepare rice according to package directions. Brush chicken with 2  teaspoons salad dressing. Grill or broil 20 to 25 minutes, or until juices  run clear, turning once.    Combine hot cooked rice with remaining salad dressing, spinach, tomatoes  and olives; stir until spinach is lightly wilted. Place rice mixture on  individual serving plates, top with sliced chicken.
