Green Bean Bake a la Campbells
milk, Soy sauce, Black pepper, Frozen green beans, French fried onions, 
Preheat oven to 350?F. In a 1 1/2 quart casserole, mix soup, milk, soy  sauce, and pepper until smooth. Add the beans and half the onions. Bake  uncovered 25 minutes. Stir. Top with the remaining onions. Bake 5 to 10  minutes more, until onions are golden brown.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Magda <antigone@easynet.fr> on Oct 15,  1998,
