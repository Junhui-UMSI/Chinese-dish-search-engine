Chicken Parmesan
Boneless Skinless Chicken, Seasoned bread crumbs, Grated Parmesan cheese, Dried Italian Seasoning, Black pepper, All-purpose flour, Egg Whites; Lightly Beaten, Cooked Spaghetti; Hot, Ultimate Quick-And-Easy, Shredded Mozzarella Cheese;, Basil Sprigs, olive oil, 
Pound chicken to 1/4 inch thickness.    Combine breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, and pepper in a  shallow dish. Dredge chicken in flour. Dip in egg whites; dredge in  breadcrumb mixture.    Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken;  cook 5 minutes on each side or until done.    Place 1 cup spaghetti in each of 4 gratin dishes. Spoon 1/2 cup Sauce over  each serving. Top each with one chicken breast half. Spoon 1/4 cup sauce  over each serving. Sprinkle with 1/4 cup mozarella cheese.    Preheat broiler. Place gratin dishes on a baking sheet; broil 3 minutes or  until cheese melts. Garnish with basil lsprigs.    Yield: 4 servings.
