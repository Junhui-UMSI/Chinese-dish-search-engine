Yolk-Free Mayonnaise
Dry mustard, Egg white, Sugar, Pepper, Melted Becel Margarine;, Vinegar, 
In a blender or food processor, blend egg whites, vinegar, sugar, mustard  and pepper on high for 30 seconds. With motor running slowly add the melted  margarine; blend until thick and creamy. Refrigerate until ready to use.    Makes 1/2 cup.    Recipe by: Lipton Shortcut Cookbook    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Barbra<barbra@pipeline.com> on Feb 09, 1998
