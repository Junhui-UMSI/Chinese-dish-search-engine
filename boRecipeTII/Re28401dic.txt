Baked Macaroni and Cheese
milk, Bread crumbs, Butter, Salt, Flour, Butter, elbow macaroni, Cheddar cheese, Pepper, 
contributed by: PammieRom    Preheat oven to 350F. Butter 1-quart baking dish. Cook macaroni according  to package directions; drain.    Melt 2 tablespoons butter in heavy saucepan; blend in flour. Cook for 1  minute, stirring constantly. Pour in milk gradually and cook, stirring  constantly until mixture thickens. Add seasonings and cheese, stirring  until cheese is melted. Add macaroni and transfer to prepared baking dish.  Combine bread crumbs and melted butter; sprinkle over macaroni. Bake for 30  minutes. Makes 6 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 494 by Giz2day <Giz2day@aol.com>  on Jan 11, 1998
