Pineapple Icing
Sugar, Pineapple juice, crushed pineapple, Flour, 
Mix all ingredients together in saucepan. Cook over low heat, stirring  constantly, until thick. Let cool. Spread on cool cake.    BOBBIE BUCK    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
