Amaretto Coffee Creamer
Ground cinnamon, Confectioners sugar, Non-dairy coffee creamer, Almond extract, 
Combine all ingredients in a container with a tight fitting lid. Shake well  to blend. Store in airtight container and give creamer with the recipe for  Amaretto Coffee. Yields 12 servings. To make Amaretto Coffee: In a mug,  combine 2 tablespoons of creamer with 6 ounces of coffee.    Posted to recipelu-digest by Lynn Nelson <lynnn@erols.com> on Feb 08, 1998
