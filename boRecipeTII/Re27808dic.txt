The Tomato Sandwich
Ripe tomato slices; (or more, Salt; & pepper, White bread, Mayo, 
A survey of our Southern Living staff brought these suggested sandwich  additions. Pimiento cheese Thinly sliced cucumber Chopped fresh parsley,  garlic salt, and a squeeze of lemon Raw onion and dill pickles Iceberg  lettuce and bacon Green onions on the side Toasted with green bell pepper  slices    Notes: Many Southerners believe one of summers best treats is the tomato  sandwich. "White bread, very ripe tomato slices, mayo, salt, pepper, and a  sink," define this favorite for Vicki Weathers, Southern Living Editorial  Coordinator.
