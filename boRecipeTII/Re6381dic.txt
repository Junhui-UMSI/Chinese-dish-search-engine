Plinys Pear Butter
Water, Honey, Pears, peeled, cored, and, Minced, Sweet wine, 
1) Combine all ingredients in a saucepan, bring to a boil, and simmer  for 30 minutes to 1 hour, until all the liquid has disappeared.       2) Let cool, and transfer into a jar.  Add a little more honey if you  desire a sweeter taste.
