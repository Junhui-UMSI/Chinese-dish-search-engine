Apple Crunch Dessert
Butter or margarine, LORELI AGUDA WSKD49A, milk, Ginger, Brown Sugar, Whole bran cereal, Quick cooking rolled oats, Baking soda, butterscotch pudding, Cinnamon, Whole wheat flour, Nutmeg, Apple pie filling, lemon peel, Lemon juice, 
Mix oats, cereal, flour, sugar, soda and spices. Cut in butter or margarine  until crumbly. Pat half the mixture into a 8x8x2 inch baking dish. Bake in  350~ oven 10 minutes. Prepare pudding mix according to package directions  except use the 1 1/2 cups milk. Pour over crumb layer. Mix remaining  ingredients; spoon over pudding. Top with remaining crumbs. Bake,  uncovered, in 350~ oven 30 to 35 minutes. Serve warm or cool with ice  cream, if desired.
