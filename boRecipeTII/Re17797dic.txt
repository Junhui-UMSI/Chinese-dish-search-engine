Almost Alfredo Sauce
Linguini, Skim milk, Low fat cottage cheese, Coarsely ground pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
1. Cook linguini until desired doneness    2. Meanwhile, place cottage cheese, skim milk and parmesan cheese in  electric blender or food processor. Whirl until smooth.    3. Toss linguini with sauce in serving bowl. Season with pepper and serve  at once.
