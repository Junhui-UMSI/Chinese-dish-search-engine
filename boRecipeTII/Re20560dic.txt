Apple Chutney --- Seb Ki Chatni
Peaches -- apricots and, White-wine vinegar, Pears, Cloves garlic -- chopped, Salt, Chopped dried fruit such as, Finely chopped fresh ginger, Peeled, cored, and c, Tart cooking apples --, Golden raisins, Sugar, Cayenne pepper, 
In a heavy saucepan, combine all ingredients and mix well. Bring to a boil  over medium heat. Reduce heat and simmer for about 45 mins, stirring  occasionally or until mixture is thick. Remove saucepan from heat and cool  chutney to room temperature.    Pour chutney into a non-metallic, covered container and refrigerate until  ready to use.
