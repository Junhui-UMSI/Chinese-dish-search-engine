Eggplant Caponata 4
Eggplants, in small cubes, Oil for sauteing, Onions, chopped, Can crushed tomatoes, Sugar, Wine, Bunch celery, diced and, Green olives, chopped, 
Put eggplant in pot and sprinkle with salt. Stir occasionally and let sit  for 2 hours. Drain eggplant, squeezing to remove excess water. Saute  eggplant in oil until tender. Remove from pan and set aside. Saute onions  in oil slightly, add tomatoes. Simmer about 30 minutes. Add sugar, wine,  eggplant, celery, and olives. Stir and simmer 5 minutes. Let cool and  refrigerate. Serve cold with bread or crackers.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  17, 1998
