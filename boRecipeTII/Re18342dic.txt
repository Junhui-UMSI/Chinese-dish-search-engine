Vermont Sweet and Sour Dressing
White vinegar, Garlic powder, Maple syrup, Salt, Salad oil, Pepper, Eggs, Sugar, Celery salt, 
In a blender on high speed, beat eggs, add spices and drizzle in 2-3 oz of  the oil until thickened. Add remaining oil and vinegar alternately. Beat  beeat in the syrup. Keep refregated. Makes 7 cups.    Source: "The Yankee Kitchen" 03-19-93 (#5) [Jill]
