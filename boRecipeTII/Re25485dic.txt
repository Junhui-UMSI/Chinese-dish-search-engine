Tortoni (Frozen Custard)
Dry white wine, Egg whites, Ground dry macaroons, Powdered sugar, Whipping cream,whipped, Egg yolks, Vanilla, Ground dry macaroons, Maraschino cherried,chopped, 
Beat egg yolks and sugar in small mixer bowl until very thick and lemon  colored, about 5 minutes. Stir in wine and vanilla.   Beat egg whites in small mixer bowl until stiff but not dry. Fold egg yolk  mixture into egg whites. Fold in whipped cream. Stir in 3/4 cup macaroons  and the cherries. Pour mixture by 1/3 cupfuls into paper liners in muffin  cups. Sprinkle with 1/4 cup macaroons. Freeze until firm, about 3 1/2  hours.
