Baked Apple Rings
Brown sugar, Water, Sweetener, Powdered Cloves, Baking Apples, Cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 400 F.    Core and slice apples into 1/2" rings. place in shallow baking dish. In  saucepan, combine and heat remaining ingredients. Pour over apples and bake  15 minutes or until tender, turning to baste once or twice. Variation: Very  good with red-hot candies put in the center of each ring.
