Almond Torte (Mozurek)
Flour, Eggs, Sour cream, Vanilla, Almonds; chopped, Butter, Sugar, 
Mix almonds with sugar. Cream butter well; add beaten eggs. Add to sugar  mixture and mix well. Stir in flour, sour cream and the vanilla. Pour into  two 8 or 9 inch square baking pans and bake at 325 F. for 1 hour. Frost, if  desired.
