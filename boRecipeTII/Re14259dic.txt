White Sour Milk Cake
Shortening, Sour cream, Baking soda, Sugar, Baking Powder, All-Purpose Flour, Salt, Eggs (beaten well), Vanilla, 
Sift flour, baking powder and salt together.  Cream shortening and sugar,  and well beaten eggs and vanilla and mix well. Dissolve baking soda in  mixture alternately with sifted dry ingredients. Bake in greased 8 inch  layer cake pans 20 to 25 minutes at 350F. Source: Heritage Recipes ch.
