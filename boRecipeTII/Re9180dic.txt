Prune Bread
Dried prunes, Salad oil or melted crisco, Egg, Salt, Boiling water, Flour, Baking soda, Sugar, 
Soak prunes in cold water 2 hours; drain. Pit prunes; chop. Add boiling  water; let stand 5 minutes. Sift to-gether flour, sugar, salt and soda. Add  prune mixture. Beat egg and add. Add salad oil or melted crisco. Pour into  greased loaf pan and bake in 325 oven 1 hour. Remove from pan and cool on  rack.
