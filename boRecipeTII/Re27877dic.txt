Thick Johnnycakes
Salt, Vegetable oil <<or>>, milk, Cornmeal, Boiling water, 
Mix cornmeal, salt and boiling water and let stand for 5 minutes. Stir in  milk. Fry 2 1/2- inch cakes on a medium hot well oiled skillet until  browned on both sides.
