Quick Penuche
Brown sugar, -(up to), milk, Vanilla, Butter, 
Melt butter & add sugar; boil over low heat 2 minutes, stirring constantly.  Stir in milk & bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Sift confectioners  sugar & gradually add to butter mixture. Beat until thick enough to put in  pan (for candy) or spread on cake. If too thick, add a small amount of  water.    MRS BERNARD B.
