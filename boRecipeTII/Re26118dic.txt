Tofu Vegetable Crepes
Safflower oil, Mushrooms; sliced, Crepes (see recipe), Tofu; baked; cubed, Zucchini; sliced, Shiitake Mushroom Gravy, 
In a large saucepan, saute the zucchini and mushrooms in the hot oil over  medium-high heat. Add the tofu and half the gravy. Mix well, then divide  the mixture between the crepes, roll up, and pour the remaining gravy over  them. Serve hot.     Vegetarian Cooking for Good Health by Gary Null/MM by DEEANNE
