Cajun Meatloaf
Butter, Onion, Bell pepper, Salt, Cayenne, Thyme, Pepper, Cumin, Lean beef; ground, Egg, Bread crumbs, Catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Preheat oven to 375x. Melt butter in heavy skillet over medium-low heat.  Add onion, bell pepper & spices; cook, stirring frequently, for 10 minutes  or until onions and bell peppers begin to soften. Combine ground beef, egg,  bread crumbs, 1/4 cup of the catsup & the worcestershire sauce. Mix well.  Add sauteed onions & bell peppers. Form into a loaf. Bake for 20 minutes.  Spread remaining catsup on top; bake for       40    more minutes.
