Baked Pears
Sliced orange, Juice and zest of 1 lemon, Port wine, Orange marmalade; up to 3, Bosc pears, Lemon, Orange rind, Water, Sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Put all ingredients,except pears, in a  saucepan and cook down until strongly flavored and syrupy. Pour the mixture  over the pears which have been placed upright in a pyrex dish, just large  enough to hold them. Bake until tender, basting often.
