White Bean and Vegetable Soup
Parmesan, olive oil, White wine, Dried rosemary, Vegetable stock, Dried white beans, Dried thyme, Chopped parsley, Onions, Celery, Bay leaf, Garlic, Red potatoes, carrots, 
In a large pot heat olive oil over moderately high heat and cook onion,  celery, bay leaf, thyme, and rosemary, stirring occasionally, until onion  and celery are translucent. Add the carrots, red potatoes, beans, stock and  5 cups of water and bring soup to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer soup,  covered, for 1 1/2 to 2 hours, or until beans are tender. Stir in the wine  and additional stock or water to achieve desired consistency. Remove bay  leaf. In a blender puree 2 cups of the soup until smooth and stir into  soup. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper and stir in parsley. Serve soup  with Parmesan.    Yield: 10-12 cups  Recipe by: Cooking Live Show #CL8798    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #376, by Angele Freeman  <jfreeman@netusa1.net> on Fri, 17 Jan 1997.
