The Ultimate Butter Tart
Salt, Raisins, Brown sugar, Corn syrup, Pastry, Vanilla, Egg, 
Prepare pastry. Roll out thinly on floured surface. Cut into rounds with 4"  round cutter. Fit into medium-sized muffin cups. Combine all ingredients  except raisins; mix well. Put raisins into pastry shells, dividing evenly.  Fill 2/3 full with syrup mixture. Bake on bottom shelf at 425F for 12 to 15  minutes or just until set. DONT OVERBAKE. Overbaking makes them runnier!  Cool on wire rack, then remove from pans. SERVINGS: 12
