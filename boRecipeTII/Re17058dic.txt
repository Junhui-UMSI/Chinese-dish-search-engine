Vinaigrette
Pinches freshly ground white, Plus 1 tablespoon corn oil, Sherry vinegar, Plus 1 tablespoon olive oil, Dijon mustard, Red wine vinegar, Fine sea salt, 
In a mixing bowl, whisk together mustard, salt, pepper and vinegars.  Whisking constantly, very slowly drizzle in the olive oil and then the corn  oil. Store tightly covered, in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.    Yield: 1 1/3 cups    NOTES : Recipe courtesy of Eric Ripert, Le Bernardin    Recipe by: Cooking Live Show #CL9054    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "Angele and Jon Freeman"  <jfreeman@comteck.com> on Feb 7, 1998
