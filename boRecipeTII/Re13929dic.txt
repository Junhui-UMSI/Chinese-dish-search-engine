Wine Jelly
Sugar, Sweet wine, juice of 1 lemon, Cold water, Boiling water, unflavored gelatin, 
Soak the gelatin in cold water.  Pour boiling water over and add sugar. Mix  to thoroughly dissolve gelatin and sugar. Add wine and lemon juice. Chill  in a pretty bowl (cut or pressed glass is traditional). Serve a bowl of  aforementioned custard sauce on the side so it can be added at the diners  discretion.
