10 K Cereal Bars
Walnuts, Vanilla, Honey, Granola cereal, Salt, Egg, Butter or margarine, 
Place all ingredients in large mixing bowl. Blend well. Pat into greased  8-inch square baking dish. Bake at 350? F 18 to 20 minutes or until lightly  browned. Cool and cut into 16 bars.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #748 by Creedenite@aol.com on Aug 19, 1997
