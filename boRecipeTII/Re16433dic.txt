Welches Hot Mulled Punch
Lemon juice, Cloves, Cinnamon, Pineapple juice, Honey, Orange juice, 
Orange and lemon slices, studded with cloves Microwave: In 2-quart baking  dish, combine juices, honey, cloves and cinnamon. Cover with waxed paper or  plastic wrap. Heat in microwave oven at high for 8 minutes or until punch  comes to a boil. Strain into warmed punch bowl. Float fruit slices on  punch.    Conventional: Place all ingredients except fruit slices in saucepan. Heat  over medium heat until punch comes to a boil. Strain and serve as indicated  above.
