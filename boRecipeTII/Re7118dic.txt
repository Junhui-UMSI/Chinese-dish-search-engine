Poppy Seed-Almond Cookies
Poppy seeds, Baking soda, Almond extract, Sugar, Egg white, Butter, Light corn syrup, Egg substitute; liquid, All-purpose flour, 
Cream sugar and margarine at medium speed of a mixer until fluffy (about 3  minutes). Add corn syrup, almon extract, egg substitute and egg white; beat  well. Stir together flour, poppy seeds and baking soda. Add to creamed  mixture, beating well.    Drop dough by rounded tablespoons onto baking sheets coated with cooking  spray. Bake at 350F for 10 minutes or until lightly browned; leet cool on  pans 1 minute. Remove from pans, and let cool completely on wire racks.
