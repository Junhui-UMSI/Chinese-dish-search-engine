Pork Chops Bonne Femme
Onion; sliced, Center-cut pork chops;, (4-oz) mushrooms; and juice, White wine (chablis or, -(up to), Water, Salt, Salt and pepper, Bay leaf, Butter, Garlic, Sprigs parsley, 
In skillet, brown chops in butter. Remove. Brown onion and garlic. Add wine  and boil a few minutes. Add chops, mushrooms, juice, water, thyme, bay  leaf, parsley, salt and pepper and simmer until tender. Serve over rice or  noodles.
