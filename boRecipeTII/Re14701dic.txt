$20,000 Prize-Winning Chili
Unsweetened cocoa, Warm water, Ground cumin, Ground coriander, Budweiser beer, Water, Cornmeal, chuck, lean, ground, Garlic, Onion, Flour, pork, ground, lean, Oregano leaves, Paprika, Sugar, Chili powder, Louisiana hot sauce, 
In large saucepan or Dutch oven, brown half the meat; pour off fat. Remove  meat. Brown remaining meat; pour off all fat except 2 Tbsps. Add onion,  garlic; cook and stir until tender. Add meat and remaining ingredients  except flour, cornmeal and warm water. Mix well. Bring to boil; reduce heat  and simmer covered 2 hours. Stir together flour and cornmeal; add warm  water. Mix well. Stir into chili mixture. Cook covered 20 minutes longer.  Serve hot. Makes 2 quarts.    From: Bill and Becky Pfeiffer. 1980 World Championship Chili Cookoff  Winners.
