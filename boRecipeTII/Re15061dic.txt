Western Jerky
Salt, Cayenne pepper, Liquid smoke, Water, Chili powder, Cut meat, Pepper, Garlic powder, NORMA WRENN NPXR56B, Round, flank steak or other, Onion powder, 
Mix marinade ingredients together in a bowl.  Dip meat slices into  marinade. Place dipped meat in layers in a bowl or dish. Pour remaining  marinade sauce over meat. Cover tightly and let marinate in refrigerator  for 6 to 12 hours. Rotate layers of meat occasionally.    Place in dehydrator until dry.  While meat is drying, blot excess oil with  paper towel.  Makes 1/2 pound jerky.    Source:  Dehydrated & Delicious, by Ron Popeil and John May  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Deborah K?hnen"  <DEBKUHNEN@classic.msn.com> on Feb 13, 98
