Anise Tea
2 tsp. fresh leaves, Dried anise leaves or, Honey, Lemon slice (optional), Boiling water, 
Place anise leaves in cup and pour boiling water over them. Let steep 5 - 8  minutes.  Stir in honey and serve with lemon slice. Source: Quick & Easy  Cooking.ch  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #303    Date: Tue, 05 Nov 1996 00:17:37 -0500    From: Cindy J Hartlin <chartlin@total.net>
