Thai Shrimp and Vegetable Pasta
Frozen mixed vegetables, Medium shrimp; Peeled and, Cappellini; (extra thin, Thai peanut sauce, Chopped cilantro, 
1. Bring 8 cups water to boil in a large, deep skillet. Add pasta and cook  5-7 minutes until firm tender. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup cooking water.    2. Meanwhile, bring vegetables and 1/4 cup tap water to boil in a large  sauce pan. Cover, reduce heat and simmer 3 minutes.    3. Stir in shrimp, cover and simmer 4 minutes or until vegetables are  tender and shrimp are opaque at centers. Stir in reserved pasta water and  peanut sauce. Remove from heat and stir in cilantro. Serve over pasta.    Recipe by: Womans Day 11/18/97    Posted to recipelu-digest by Rodeo46898 <Rodeo46898@aol.com> on Mar 2, 1998
