Praline Party Mix
small pretzels, Butter, Savannah Praline Mix, , Brown sugar, Baking soda, Pecan, 
Mix 6 cups any combination of square cereals such as Chex, Oat Squares,  Crispix, etc. Add pretzels and nuts. Spray a 9 X 13 inch pan. Melt butter,  brown sugar, Savannah Praline Mix and baking soda. Heat until boiling and  gently cook for about 2 minutes. Pour over cereal mixture. Stir to coat  well. Bake at 250 degrees for one hour, stirring once during baking. Cool  thoroughly before placing in a pretty holiday tin.    Note: Savannah Praline Mix can be found in specialty shops and gourmet food  stores.
