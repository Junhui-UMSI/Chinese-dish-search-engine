Quick Southwest Skillet Dinner
Water, red wine, Kidney beans  , Green pepper, Salt, Hot sauce, Tomatoes  , Corn, frozen, Chili powder, Whole wheat elbow macaroni;, Cumin, Onion, Garlic, 
Saute onion, green pepper, garlic, chili powder, salt and cumin in water or  any red or white wine until vegetables are tender.  Stir in tomatoes,  breaking with spoon.  Add kidney beans and corn;  bring to a boil. Reduce  heat and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.   Toss with pasta.    1/4 of recipe= 390 cal, 2.6g fat, 82g carb, 18g pro, 4.6g fiber, 5.6 cal  from fat.
