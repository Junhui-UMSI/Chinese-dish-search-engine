Zuchini Relish
Nutmeg, tumeric, and cornstarch, Red pepper, Celery salt, Fresh zuchini, onions, White pepper, Kosher salt, White vinegar, Sugar, Green pepper, 
Mix zucchini, onions and Kosher salt together a let stand overnight; drain  and rinse in cold water. Add remaining ingredients.    Boil and simmer for 30 minutes. Then can your favorite way.    This stuff is wonderful on everything you would use pickle relish but  better.
