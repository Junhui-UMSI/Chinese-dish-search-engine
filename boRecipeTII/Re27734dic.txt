The Next Best Thing To Robert Redford Cake
Grated chocolate candy bar;, Cold milk, Flour, Butter of margarine;, (6 3/4 oz) instant chocolate, Finely chopped pecans, Container (8 oz) Cool Whip;, (8 oz) cream cheese;, (6 3/4 oz) instant vanilla, Sugar, 
Prepare bottom crust by mixing together flour, butter and pecans until  crumb-like. Press mixture into greased 13x9 baking pan. Bake at 350 deg  15-20 min. until lightly golden. Cool.    Beat Cream Cheese with sugar until smooth. Fold in half of whipped topping.  Spread mixture over cooled crust. Combine vanilla and chocolate pudding  mixxes. Beat in milk until smooth and thickened. Spread over cream cheese  layer.    Spread remaining whipped topping over top. Sprinkle with grated chocolate  candy bar. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Makes 16 servings.
