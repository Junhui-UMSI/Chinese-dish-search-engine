Almond Butter Strips
Sugar, Salt, All-Purpose Flour, Cinnamon, Sugar, Canned; toasted, diced, Butter or margarine, Egg white; slightly beaten, Almond extract, 
In large bowl with electric mixer at medium speed beat softened butter with  1/4 cup sugar & almond extract until light & fluffy. Beat in flour & salt  until well mixed. Refrigerate dough 2-3 hours or until firm. On floured  surface roll dough with flour-covered rolling pin to a rectangle 12x8x1/4  inches. Then with pastry wheel, cut dough into 1x3-inch strips. Place  cookie strips on ungreased cookie sheet. Bruns with egg white & sprinkle  with sugar, cinnamon & almonds. Bake in 325 oven for about 15 minutes or  until golden in color.
