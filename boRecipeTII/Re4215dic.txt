Perfect Microwave Corn
See below, 
Place one ear of unhusked corn on paper towel. Microwave on high for 2  minutes; turn over. Microwave 1 1/2 to 2 minutes more. Let stand 1  minute. Carefully remove husk & silk.  For 4 ears: Microwave corn on  high 4 minutes. Turn ears over & switch posotions; microwave 4 more  minutes. Let stand 1 minute.
