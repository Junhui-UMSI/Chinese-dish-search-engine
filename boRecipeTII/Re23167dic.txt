Tuna Boats
(7oz,) tuna, drained, Heaping T mayonnaise, Green pepper, 
Mix tuna with mayo and any additions you like (seeds, shredded carrots,  chopped celery, relish, etc.). Cut green pepper in half or quarters (for  small child) lengthwise. Remove seeds and membranes. Fill with tuna mix.  Pack a triangle of cheese for child to add as a sail!    Posted to EAT-L Digest 25 Aug 96    From:    Maureen Riley <MeauxR@AOL.COM>    Date:    Mon, 26 Aug 1996 01:23:16 -0400
