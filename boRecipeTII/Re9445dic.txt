Pumpkin Banana Nut Bread
Salt, Mashed very ripe bananas, Baking Powder, Ground cinnamon, Baking soda, All purpose flour, Walnuts, Granulated sugar, Ground ginger, (15-16 oz) solid pack, Eggs, Brown sugar, Vegetable oil, 
Combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, ginger and salt in  medium bowl. In large bowl, beat eggs, pumpkin, granulated sugar, brown  sugar, bananas and oil; mix well. Beat flour mixture into pumpkin mixture;  stir in nuts.    Spoon into 2 greased and floured 9x5 inch loaf pans. Bake in preheated 350  oven for 55 to 60 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in centers comes  out clean. Cool for 10 minutes. Remove to wire racks to cool completely.  Makes 2 loaves.
