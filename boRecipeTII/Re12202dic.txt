Zucchini Bread with Pineapple and Nuts
Sugar, Salt, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Vanilla, Baking soda, Flour, Eggs, Zucchini; unpeeled and, Nutmeg, Pecans, Vegetable oil, Crushed pineapple; drained, Raisins, 
Preheat oven to 350?. Mix eggs, oil, sugar and vanilla together. Add the  zucchini and pineapple. Combine the dry ingredients and add to the zucchini  mixture, mixing well.  Add nuts and raisins and blend. Pour into 2 (9x5)  greased and floured loaf pans. Bake for 1 hour. Cool 10 minutes in the pans  and then turn on wire racks. Also may be cooked in small foil loaf pans and  given as gifts. If so, reduce cooking time to 35 to 40 minutes.  Yield: 2  (9x5) loaves.    CHERRY SINGLETON LAUGHLIN    (MRS. R.B., JR.)
