Veggie Pate
Nutritional yeast, Smoked salt (I skipped this, Vegetarian "Beef" bouillon, Mushrooms, Few dashes Vegetable, Vegetable oil, Fried onions (Durkees or, 
Saute 1 lb mushrooms in a bit of oil, until lightly browned. Put in blender  or food processor. Add remaining ingredients. Mix thoroughly. Serve with  crackers. Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #103 by "Phil Gurnicht"  <gurnicht@hotmail.com> on Mar 28, 1997
