Purple Salad
(8-oz) crushed pineapple;, (15-oz) blueberries;, Pecans, (3-oz) raspberry gelatin, Dream whip, Hot water, 
Mix gelatin with 3 cups hot water and 1 cup liquid from blueberries and  pineapple. Remove 3/4 cup gelatin mixture and keep at room temperature.  Pour remaining gelatin mixture into 9x12 dish and refrigerate. When mixture  partially congeals, add pecans, blueberries and pineapple. Let congeal.  Prepare Dream Whip as directed on package, omitting vanilla. Add 3/4 cup  cold gelatin to Dream Whip. Spread on top of congealed gelatin. Chill to  congeal. Cut into 2-inch squares and serve on lettuce.
