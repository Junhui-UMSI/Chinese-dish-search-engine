Zucchini Health Bread
Eggs, Grated unpeeled zucchini, Wheat germ, Vanilla, Pecans, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Whole wheat flour, Baking Powder, Vegetable oil, Bran, Raisins, Honey, 
Mix in order given.  Bake in 2 greased loaf pans at 325 degrees for 60 to       70    minutes.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on May 26, 98
