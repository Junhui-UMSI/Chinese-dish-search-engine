Veggie Topping
Sugar (can substitute, Or so of Balsamic or red, Fresh basil minced (up to 1), Medium-large sweet, Garlic cloves chopped or, 
Here is a great recipe that I learned from in-laws from Brazil. It is good  on any steamed veggies, and especially enjoyed on Broccoli, asparagus,  green beans.    Combine the above.    Mix together and let it sit for a while at room temp. Spoon it onto fresh  steamed veggies and enjoy! Posted to fatfree digest V97 #046 by  michael@newton.crisp.net on Apr 03, 1997
