30-Second Smoothie
Ice cubes, Orange juice, Nonfat yogurt, USA WEEKEND 9/24/1994, 
Whip in blender until frothy.    Variation: for 1 c orange juice, substitute 1 banana or 1 c blueberries,  strawberries, raspberries, pineapple, or sliced peaches. Or substitute  frozen fruit for the ice.
