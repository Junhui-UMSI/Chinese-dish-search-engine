Peachy Pork Stir-Fry
Peach nectar, Lean boneless pork loin, Ginger; peeled -- minced, Sliced frozen peaches --, Trimmed, Ground white pepper, Low-sugar apricot spread, Cornstarch, Thaw, Fresh snow pea pods --, Salt, Vegetable cooking spray, 
Trim fat from pork.  Partially freeze pork; slice diagonally across grain  into thin strips.  Sprinkle with pepper and salt; set aside. Combine  apricot spread, peach nectar; and cornstarch in a small bowl; stir well,  and set mixture aside. Coat a large nonstick skillet or wok with cookin  spray; place over medium-high heat until hot. Add ginger; stir-fry 30  seconds.  Add sliced pork; stir-fry 3 minutes. Add reserved apricot  mixture; cook 1 minute or until mixture is thickened. Add peaches and snow  peas; toss gently. Cover and cook 1 minute or until thoroughly heated.  Yield: 6 servings (about 204 calories per 1-cup serving).
