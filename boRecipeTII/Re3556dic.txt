From: rmd1@tntvax.ntrs.com (Reggie Drahnak)    Date: 4 Jan 94 19:01:40 -0600    Cream butter and shortening. Gradually add sugar. Cream again. Sift dry  ingredients together. Gradually add to creamed mixture. Stir in nuts. Chill  1/2 hour. Shape into 1" balls and place 2" apart on greased cookie sheet.  Flatten balls to 1/3" thick with glass dipped in sugar. Bake at 350 F for  12-15 minutes.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /COOKIES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
