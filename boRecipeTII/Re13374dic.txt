Yangtze Chicken
Dry white wine, Boneless skinless chicken, Marinade:, Soy sauce, Skewers, Orange marmalade, Honey, Ketchup, Pineapple; cut in 2-inch, 
Mix marinade ingredients in large bowl. Add chicken and mix well to coat.  Marinate for at least 1 hour in refrigerator. Alternate chicken and  pineapple pieces on skewers.
