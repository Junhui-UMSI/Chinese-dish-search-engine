Pork Fillet a Lalsacienne
Apple, Pepper, Powdered Thyme, Parsley, Pork Fillet, Condensed Mushroom Soup, GARNISH, Shallots, Onion, Celery Flakes, 
Trim any fat from pork fillet and cut in 1/2 inch slices. Place in layers  in a buttered casserole or oven dish, sprinkling each layer with shallots  or onion, apple, celery flakes, thyme and pepper. Add undiluted soup.  Smooth top. Cover dish with a lid or foil and cook in a very moderate oven  (325 F, gas mark 2) for about 2 hours or until pork is very tender.  Sprinkle with chopped parsley before taking to the table.  Recipe By     : Old Magazine clipping from England, Sixties?
