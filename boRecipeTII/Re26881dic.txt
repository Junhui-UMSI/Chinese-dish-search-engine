Baked Beans Supreme
Water, Garbanzo beans; (15 oz.), Ketchup, Garlic, Brown sugar, Lima beans; (17 oz.) drained, Dijon-style mustard, Worcestershire Sauce, Sliced water chestnuts; (8, Onion; chopped (1 c.), Butter beans with molasses, Pepper; (1/4 to 1/2), Green pepper; cut into, Red kidney beans; (15 1/2, 
In a 3 quart microwavesafe casserole, combine onion, green pepper, garlic  and water. Microcook, covered, on 100% power (High) for 4 to 5 minutes or  until onion and pepper are tender; drain. Stir in lima beans, undrained  butter beans, kidney beans, garbanzo beans, water chestnuts, ketchup, brown  sugar, mustard, Worcestershire sauce and pepper. Cook, covered, on High for  10 to 12 minutes (low watt oven, 20 to 22 minutes), stirring after every 5  minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 27,  1998
