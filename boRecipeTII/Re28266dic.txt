Baked French Toast (Ovo Lacto)
Skim milk, Cottage cheese, lowfat, Whole grain bread, Vanilla extract, Egg white, Sugar, 
:      Perheat the oven to 425. In a food proscessor or blender, whirl the  cottage cheese, milk, sugar, egg, egg white, & vanilla for about 1 minute  or until smooth. Transfer the mixture to a shallow dish, place the bread in  it, and let stand for 10 minutes. Turn the bread over and let stand another  10 minutes. Meanwhile, grease a baking sheet & place it in the perheated  oven for 7 minutes.  :      Remove the baking sheet from the oven, place the bread on it, and  bake for 6 minutes. Turn the bread over & bake 5 to 6 minutes. Turn the  bread & bake 5 to 6 minutes longer or until golden brown.  : Cal 149, fat 3g, sodium 369mg, cholesterol 55mg, Cal from fat 18% From:  Readers Digest Live Longer Cookbook  :    D/L from Prodigy 12-14-94.
