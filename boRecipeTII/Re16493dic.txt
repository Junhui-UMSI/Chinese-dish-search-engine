Aegean Sea Chowder (Psarosoupa Kakavia)
Clams (if desired), Celery stalks; chopped, Scallops (if desired), Cloves garlic; pressed, Cayenne pepper, Onions; chopped, Mussels (if desired), Lobster (if desired), Shrimp (if desired), Baby octopus (optional), Canned peeled tomatoes;, Water, olive oil, Salt, Chopped mushrooms, Crab (if desired), Bay leaf, Wine; red preferably, White fish; cut into 2-inch, 
Date: Thu, 28 Mar 1996 19:27:29 -0500    From: Walt Gray <waltgray@mnsinc.com>  Prepare fish and shellfish by cleaning and cutting into bite-size pieces.  Heat oil in a large pot. Fry onions and garlic on medium heat for 5  minutes. Add remaining ingredients, except seafood and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and cover. Cook one hour.    Add fish and octopus, and cook 20 minutes. Add shellfish and simmer 5  minutes more. Serve hot with crusty bread and crisp salad.
