Strawberry-Pecan Pie
Pastry for (9-inch), Sugar, Ground nutmeg, -(up to), Pecans, Butter or margarine, Chopped fresh strawberries, All-purpose flour, Ground cinnamon, 
From:    J Martin <jmartin@GLEN-NET.CA>    Date:    Wed, 17 Jul 1996 00:24:23 -0400  In a bowl, combine sugar, flour, nutmeg and cinnamon. Add strawberries and  pecans; toss gently. Line pie plate with bottom crust. Add filling; dot  with butter. Bake at 375 degrees for 50 minutes or until crust is golden  brown.    EAT-L Digest 16 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
