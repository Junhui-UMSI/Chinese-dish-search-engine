Vickis Country Cornbread
Baking Powder, Flour, Egg, Milk, Sugar, Cornmeal, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees    Beat first three ingredients until frothy. Add the next three ingredients  mix until moistened.. do not over mix.    Melt one tbsp of shortening in an iron skillet while you are preheating the  oven. Carefully pour the mixture in your hot skillet, Bake for 20-25  minutes or until golden brown    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #202 by saracen2@juno.com (v. j. redding)  on Aug 26, 1997
