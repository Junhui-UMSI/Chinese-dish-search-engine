Quick Bread Sticks
Low-fat refrigerated biscuit, Skim milk, Rice cereal, coarsely crushe, Salt, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
cut each biscuit into thirds.  Roll each piece into a 4 inch stick. Combine  cereal and salt.  Roll each stick in milk and then in cereal. Place on  baking sheet and sprinkle with cheese.  Bake at 400 degrees for 8-10  minutes or until golden brown.    exchanges: 1 starch, 1/2 fat Source: First Place Favorites  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #031 by Dotnapier@aol.com on Jan 30, 1997.
