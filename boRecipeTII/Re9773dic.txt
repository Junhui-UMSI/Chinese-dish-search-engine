Pumpkin Soup (American)
Light cream, Onion, Nutmeg, pepper, cinnamon, Butter, Carrot, pumpkin, Chicken broth, Water, Salt, 
Melt 3 T butter and saute 1 large finely chopped onion, 1 medium carrot -  finely chopped, till golden, about 8 minutes. Add 1 can chicken broth and 1  cup water. Bring to a boil and simmer, covered, 15 minutes. You can use two  cups vegetable broth.    Puree in blender and and return to pot. Add 1 lb. can of pumpkin, 1 tsp  salt, 1/4 tsp each pepper, cinnamon, ginger and 1/8 tsp nutmeg. Heat while  wisking until smooth. Simmer 10 minutes. Slowly stir in light cream or half  and half and reheat but do not boil. (The recipe doesnt say how much  cream, just use your own judgement).
