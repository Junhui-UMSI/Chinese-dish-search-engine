Prissy Pecans
Sugar, Salt to taste, Pecan (walnut) halves, Instant coffee granules, Cinnamon, Water, 
Bring coffee, sugar, cinnamon, water, salt, and pecans to a boil in a  saucepan over medium heat. Boil for 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Spread  on waxed paper, separating the pecan halves.
