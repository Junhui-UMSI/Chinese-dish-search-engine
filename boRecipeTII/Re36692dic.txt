Strawberry Dessert Crapes
Salt, FILLING, Light brown sugar, milk, Strawberries, Flour, Sour Cream, Strawberries, Powdered sugar, Eggs, Butter, 
Crepes:    1. In a bowl, beat eggs well with fork.    2. Add flour and salt, beating until smooth. Gradually beat in milk and 1/4  cup water, and continue beating until smooth. Refrigerate, covered, until  ready to use after 30 minutes.    3. To cook crepes, slowly heat 8" skillet with sloping sides, until a drop  of water sizzles and rolls off.    4. For each crepe, brush pan lightly with butter. Pour in about 1/3 cup  batter, rotating pan quickly to spread batter completely over bottom of  skillet.    5. Cook until lightly browned on bottom, about 30 seconds. Turn and brown  lightly on other side. Turn out onto serving plate and keep warm in oven.    6. Fill each crepe with 1/2 cup strawberries. Top with 3 tablespoons sour  cream. Fold over crepe. Sprinkle with powdered sugar. garnish with more  sour cream and strawberries. Serve at once.
