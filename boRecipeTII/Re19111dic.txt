Vegetable Eggplant Soup
Finely chopped, Oregano, Thyme, Medium turnip, finely, White pepper, 14 1/2 ounce can tomatoes, Basil, Medium eggplant, peeled, Chicken stock, water or a, Ribs celery, finely chopped, Medium carrots,, Coarsely chopped, including, Salt to taste, Finely chopped, Garlic, finely chopped, Combination, Medium onion, finely chopped, Juice, And finely chopped, 
In a heavy large pot over low heat, cook the garlic, onion, carrots, celery  eggplant and turnips until they begin to soften, about 5 minutes. Do not  brown.    Add stock or other liquid.  Over high heat, bring liquid to a boil and skim  off any gray foam that rises to the surface. Reduce heat and simmer until  vegetables are crisp-tender. 7 to 10 minutes.    Add tomatoes and simmer 5 minutes longer. Add herbs, pepper and salt.
