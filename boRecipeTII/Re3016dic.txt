Peanut Butter, Apple, and Bacon Sandwiches
Bacon; cooked until crisp, Creamy or chunky peanut, Crisp apple; (such as Royal, Slices whole-wheat bread;, Alfalfa sprouts, 
Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.    Spread peanut butter evenly on bread slices. Layer bacon, apple slices, and  sprouts on 2 bread slices and top with remaining bread slices.    Makes 2 sandwiches.    Gourmet April 1996    Posted to recipelu-digest by Sandy <sandysno@pctech.net> on Mar 16, 1998
