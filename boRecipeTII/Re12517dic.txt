Yema with Toasted Peanuts
Egg yolks, Peanuts, Caramel syrup, Butter, Vanilla, Condensed milk, 
Mix together condensed milk and egg yolks in double boiler. Stir constantly  until thick. Add butter, vanilla and nuts. Remove from flame when cooled,  form into balls or roll and cut into 1.5 inch pieces. Dip in caramel syrup  and cool    Recipe by: the Nora Daza cookbook    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #401 by Caroline Chua Pimentel  <carol@asti.dost.gov.ph> on Jan 29, 1997.
