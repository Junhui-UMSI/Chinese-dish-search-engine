Pineapple 7-Up Cake
Margarine, Instant vanilla pudding mix, Eggs, Box Duncan Hines Pineapple, ICING, Sugar, Crushed pineapple in juice,, 7-Up, 
Mix cake & pudding mixes and 7-up.  Beat well.  Add eggs one at a time,  beating well after each.  Pour into greased 9x13 pan, bake at 350 degrees  40-50 minutes, until the cake tests done.    Bring all ingredients to a boil, pour over cake.  Poke holes in cake with  cake tester to help in absorbing icing.    This cake is so moist its juicy...and delicious!
