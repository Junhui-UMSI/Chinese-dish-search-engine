Bavarian Cabbage
Water, CABBAGE WHITE FRESH, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, VINEGAR CIDER, SALAD OIL; 1 GAL, Salt, Onions, 
1.  BRING WATER, SALT, AND CABBAGE TO A BOIL; COVER; SIMMER 10 MINUTES OR  UNTIL TENDER.  DO NOT OVERCOOK.    2.  DRAIN; RESERVE 1 QT OF LIQUID.    3.  RECONSTITUTE ONIONS (SEE RECIPE NO. A01100); DRAIN; SAUTE IN SALAD  OIL.  COMBINE ONIONS AND CABBAGE WITH SUGAR, AND VINEGAR.    4.  COOK 5 TO 10 MINUTES OR UNTIL BLENDED.
