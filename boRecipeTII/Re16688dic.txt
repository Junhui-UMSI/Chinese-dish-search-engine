Alaska Seafood Pizza
Mozzarella cheese, grated, White cheddar cheese grated, Pizza crust mix, Tiny shrimp, Crab meat, Crushed pineapple, Mushrooms; sliced, Canned sliced black olives, Diced green pepper, 
Spread pizza dough on to a pizza pan. Top with crab, shrimp, pineapple,  mushrooms, olives, green pepper, mozzarella and cheddar cheese. Bake at 350  degrees F. for 30 minutes.    Trinity, Haines, Alaska    Source: Alaska Seafood Cookbook Reprinted by permission from the Alaska  Seafood Marketing Institute Meal-Master compatible recipe format courtesy  of Karen Mintzias
