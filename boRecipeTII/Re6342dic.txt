Plantation Green Beans
Salt, Toothpicks, Fresh long Pole or green, Bacon grease, Cider vinegar, SAUCE, Paprika, Chopped parsley, Bacon; partially cooked, Tarragon Vinegar, Grated onion, 
Partially cook beans in salted water until tender. Drain. Secure 10 beans  in a bundle wrapped with bacon and secured with a toothpick. Place on foil  covered cookie sheet and bake at 400? until bacon is done on all sides (10  to 15 minutes). Pour sauce over beans and serve hot. To make sauce, boil  ingredients over medium heat for 5 minutes. Pour 1 to 1-1/2 Tablespoons  over each bundle. Yield: 8 servings.    ROSIE RATLEY (MRS.
