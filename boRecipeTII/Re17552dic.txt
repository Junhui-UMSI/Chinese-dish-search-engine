Almond Crisps
Brown sugar, Almonds, Rolled oats, Vanilla, Butter, Coconut, 
Mix brown sugar and melted butter.  Add rolled oats, coconut, almonds and  vanilla.  Stir.  Press into a greased 8 inch square pan. Bake at 350F for  20 minutes. Cut in small squares while warm, remove when cooled. Source:  Heritage Recipes ch.
