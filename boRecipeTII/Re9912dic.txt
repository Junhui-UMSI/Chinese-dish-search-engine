Punkys Cranberry Butter
Unsalted butter, softened, Whole cranberries, Sugar, 
Combine cranberries with sugar in small saucepan. Cook over medium heat 10  minutes or until berries have popped and mixture forms a sauce. Cool.    Blend cranberry mixture into softened butter with wooden spoon. Pack into  crocks or molds. Chill. Makes three cups.
