Pickled Figs
Mace, Figs, Cinnamon, Vinegar, Allspice, Sugar, Cloves, Water, 
Blanch figs 5 to 10 minutes. Heat sugar and water and add vinegar and  spices (either ground or whole). Boil 5 minutes. Remove spice bag. Add figs  and cook until the fruit can be pierced with a toothpick and until  penetration of syrup is complete. (This may take a half hour at a slow  boil.) Fill sterilized jars and seal at once.                                Arizona Highways magazine                              per Sandee Eveland
