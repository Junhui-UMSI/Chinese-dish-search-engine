Banana Split Cake #1
Jar (small) Maraschino, Graham cracker crumbs, (no. 2) crushed pineapple;, Eggs, Sticks butter or margarine, Powdered sugar, (large) whipped topping, -(up to), Bananas; sliced thin, Pecans, 
Melt 1 stick butter & mix with graham cracker crumbs. Spread in 9x12-inch  pyrex dish. Beat eggs & combine with powdered sugar & other stick softened  butter. Beat 10 minutes or until light & fluffy. Pour this over crumb  crust. Slice bananas on top & put pineapple on top of this. Spread on  whipped topping & sprinkle with chopped nuts & cherries. Keep refrigerated.
