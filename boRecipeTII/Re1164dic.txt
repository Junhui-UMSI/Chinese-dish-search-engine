Creamed Dried Beef
Butter, milk, Dried beef, Flour, 
Melt butter in heavy frying pan.  Add dried beef and cook a few minutes.   Blend in flour.  Stir thoroughly.  Take from heat.  Add milk and blend.   Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.
