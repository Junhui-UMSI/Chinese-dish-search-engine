Banana Burrito Ww
Raspberry spread; * see note, 6" flour tortilla, Creamy peanut butter, Banana, Shredded coconut; (otional), 
* Use the red low calorie (8 calorie per spoon) raspberry spread.    1. Lay tortilla on flat surface; spread evenly with peanut butter and  raspberry spread. Sprinkle with coconut, if desired. 2. Place banana on  edge of tortilla; roll up to enclose. Wrap loosely in paper towel.  Microwave on High 35 seconds. This serving provides: 1 fat; 1 protein; 1  bread; 1 fruit; 20 optional calories. (Add 5 optional calories if coconut  is used.) Per serving: 232 calories, 7 g protein, 10 g fat, 31 g  carbohydrate, 49 mg calcium, 216 mg sodium, 0 mg cholesterol, 3 g dietary  fiber.
