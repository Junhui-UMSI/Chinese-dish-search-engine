Watkin Wynne Pudding (Welsh)
Egg, Chopped suet, Peel, Breadcrumbs, Sugar, Currants, milk, 
(PWDIN WATCYN WYNNE) Mix the dry ingredients. Beat the egg and add to the  milk.  Bind the pudding together with the liquid. Cover and boil for 3  hours. Serve with  Madeira Sauce  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #263 by "ray.watson"  <ray.watson@ukonline.co.uk> on Wed, 25 Sep 1996.
