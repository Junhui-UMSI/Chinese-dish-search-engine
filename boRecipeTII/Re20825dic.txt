Apple Honeydew Ice (Motts)
Ginger, Green food coloring; or more, Apple Sauce, Sugar-free lemon-lime, Honeydew melon; seeded, rind, 
In food processor bowl with metal blade or blender container, combine all  ingredients; process until smooth.    Pour into 8 or 9-inch non-metal square pan. Cover; freeze until firm.    Scoop frozen mixture into 5-oz. drink cups or spoon into dessert dishes.    Yields 10 (1/2-cup) servings.    Notes: Pantry: Motts Regular Apple Sauce. Use a well-ripened melon for  optimum flavor in this refreshing low calorie ice.    >Hanneman/Buster 1998-Apr    Recipe by: Motts Applesauce www.motts.com    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by KitPATh <phannema@wizard.ucr.edu> on Apr 18,  1998
