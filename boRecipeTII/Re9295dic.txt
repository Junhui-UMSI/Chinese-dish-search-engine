Puerto Rican Egg Nog (Coquito)(Easy Version)
Bacardi white rum, Cinnamon, Vanilla extract, Condensed milk, Ground cloves, Nutmeg, Cream of cocoanut, 
Mix all ingredients in a blender or food processor.  Store refrigerated.  Remove from refrigerator a half hour prior to serving and warm slightly.
