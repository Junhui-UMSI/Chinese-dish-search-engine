Basics- Barley
Salt, Pot or pearl barley, Vegetable or chicken stock, 
Basics for new cooks:    In saucepan, bring water and salt to boil over high heat; stir in barley.  Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer for 40 minutes or until tender and  liquid has evaporated. Makes about 3 cups, or 4 servings.
