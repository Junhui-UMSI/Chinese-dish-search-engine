Swiss Oat n Apple Cereal
Natural or chunky applesauce, Quaker Oats (quick or old, Sliced almonds, Quaker Oat Bran hot cereal,, Cinnamon, Unsweetened apple juice, Raisins, 
Swiss Oat n Apple Cereal    Combine all ingredients; mix well. Cover; refrigerate overnight. Store in  refrig. up to one week. Serve cold or hot with skim milk or low-fat yogurt  if desired.    To heat one serving, place 3/4 cup cereal in a 2-cup microwavable bowl.  Microwave on HIGH 1 minute 30 secondsto 2 minutes or until heated through.  Stir well. (about 345 calories per 3/4 cup serving)    Recipe from the Quaker Oat Bran Cookbook    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
