Venison Hor Doeuvres
Wish bone Italian Dressing, Oil, GARNETT PJXG05A, Venison, Red wine, 
Cut venison into finger-sized strips. Pour wine over meat and let marinate  1 hour. Drain and then cover with Italian dressing. Let soak at least 2  hours or overnight in refrigerator. Drain. Season with salt, pepper and  garlic. Put flour in brown paper bag. Drop in strips and shake to coat. Fry  strips in hot oil until brown and crisp. Drain on paper towels and serve  hot.
