Strawberries with Walnut Mascarpone
Mascarpone, Coffee liqueur, Walnuts, Sugar, Strawberries, 
Process walnuts with sugar in food processor until mixture becomes a paste.  Scrape the workbowl and add mascarpone and coffee liqueur, and process  until smooth. Serve as sauce with berries. Source: Fifty Ways to Cook Most  Everything (Andrew Schloss) Posted by Linda Davis
