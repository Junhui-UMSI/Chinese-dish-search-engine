Tomatillo Relish
Butter, Tarragon, fresh, Garlic, Salt and pepper to taste, Tomatoes, peeled,, Tomatillos; diced, Cognac or brandy, Shallot; chopped, 
Saute garlic & shallots in butter till transparent. Add tomatillos &  tomatoes, saute 5 minutes.  Add cognac, tarragon, salt & pepper. Simmer 8  minutes. Serve over fish dishes.
