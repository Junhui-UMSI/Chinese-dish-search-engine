Peter Pumpkin Delight
Milk, canned, Pumpkin, Eggs, Sugar, Cake mix, white, Sugar, brown, Salt, Pumpkin pie spice, Butter, Pecans, 
Preheat oven to 350.  Mix pumpkin, eggs, sugar, milk, and seasonings. Pour  into buttered 9x13" pan. Sprinkle cake mix over top and push down into  pumpkin mixture, but do not mix. Drizzle melted butter over top, then  sprinkle with pecan pieces.  Bake 45-60 minutes. Serve warm or cold with  whipped topping.    Sylvias comments: Michie is a co-worker who brought this to work.
