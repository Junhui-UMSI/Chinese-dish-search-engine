Persimmon Cream Candy
Dark corn syrup, Half and half, Sugar, Salt, Persimmon pulp, Rounded teaspoon butter, 
Mix together sugar, corn syrup, cream, and salt; let stand about 20  minutes. When sugar is dissolved, place on heat and bring quickly to a  boil. When almost at the soft-ball stage, remove from heat and stir in  persimmon pulp and butter. Return to heat and boil, stirring  constantly until it reaches the soft-ball stage. Remove from heat and  leave until partly set, then beat until mixture shows signs of  hardening. Pour onto buttered plate and mark into squares when cooled.
