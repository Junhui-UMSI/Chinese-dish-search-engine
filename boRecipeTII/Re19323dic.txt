Vegetable Stock, Home Made
Carrots -- roughly chopped, Stalks Celery -- roughly, Onions -- roughtly chopped, Sprigs Thyme, Cloves Garlic -- chopped, Shallots -- chopped, Mushrooms -- 120g, roughly, Chopped, Chop, Water -- 2L/64 fl oz, 
In a large stockpot over high heat, bring the onions, carrots, celery,  mushrooms, garlic, shallots, thyme and water just to a boil.  Reduce the  heat and simmer for about 1 hr, until the stock has a rich full flavor.  Strain through a fine sieve into a bowl and use immediately or allow to  cool to room temp before refrigerating.    This stock keeps in the refrigerator for up to one week and can be frozen.
