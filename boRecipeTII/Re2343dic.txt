Beef Barley Soup
parsley, beef, Green Pepper, Barley, Beef bouillon  , Salt, Dried basil, Catsup, Carrots, Celery, Onion, 
Layer in crock pot:    1.  Vegetables and meat  2.  Barley and spices  3.  Cover with 5 cups  water.    DO NOT STIR.  Cook on low 10 hours; high 6 hours, or combination of above.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by rfalconmoors@juno.com (roseanne  m M Brown)  on Dec 1, 1998
