Raisin Oat Muffins
Vegetable oil, Vegetable oil, Raisins, Vanilla extract, Baking Powder, Egg whites, Raisins, Brown Sugar, Rolled oats, Skim milk, Lemon juice, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly oil 10 muffin cups or spray with a  nonstick cooking spray. Place oats in a blender container and blend until  it has the consistency of flour. Place in a large bowl and add baking  powder. Mix well. Stir in raisins. Place milk in a medium bowl. Add lemon  juice and let stand 1 minute. Add remaining ingredients. Beat with a fork  or wire whisk until blended. Add to dry mixture, mixing until all  ingredients are moistened. Divide batter evenly into prepared muffin cups.  Bake 20 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin  comes out clean. Remove muffins to arack to cool.
