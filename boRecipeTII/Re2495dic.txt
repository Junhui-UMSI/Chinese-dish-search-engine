Bran Muffins
Bran, Eggs, Butter, Brown sugar, White wheat flour, milk, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 375F. Mix together bran and milk and let stand for 10  minutes. Grease muffin tins. Stir eggs, vanilla and melted butter into bran  mixture. Mix together dry ingredients and add to wet mixture, stirring only  enough to mix all together. Spoon into each muffin tin until about  three-fourths full. Bake for about 20 minutes.
