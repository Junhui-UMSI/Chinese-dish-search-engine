Picadillo (Spicy Beef)
Med. onion,chopped fine, Almonds, blanched, slivered, Cloves, Cumin, ground, Seedless raisins, Ground cinnamon, Sugar, Salt, Fresh tomatoes,peel,chop, Clove garlic, crushed, Vinegar, Lean beef, chopped coarse, 
Brown meat over med. heat.  After meat begins to cook and some he fat  begins to cook out, add the onion. Cook until onion is translucent. Drain  off excess fat, if any.    Add all remaining ingreds. except almonds.  Simmer for abt. 30 min. Add the  almonds just before serving; use as garnish for top of dish.
