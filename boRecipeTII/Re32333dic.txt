Barley and Tomato Salad
Fresh parsley, Canola oil, Water, Basil, Soy sauce, Green onions; sliced, Tomatoes; diced, Barley; uncooked, Lemon juice, 
1. In a sauce pan, boil barley in water for 15 minutes; drain. Return to  pot, replace lid and steam barley for 10 minutes more; cool.    2. Add parsley, basil, and green onions; let sit to blend flavours.    3. In a small bowl beat together, lemon juice, oil and soy sauce; add  tomatoes; toss with barley mixture.    4. Chill and serve.    >From the Recipe Files of RecipeLu <recipelu@recipelu.com>  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by RecipeLu <recipelu@geocities.com> on Mar 08,  1998
