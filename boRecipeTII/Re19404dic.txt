Vegetables with Cream Cheese Sauce
Flour, milk, Frozen vegetables (broccoli), Salt, Butter, Cream cheese, Pepper, Parmesan cheese, 
1. Cook vegetables until almost done; drain and place in casserole dish.    2. Melt butter in saucepan. Stir in flour until smooth; add milk, cream  cheese, salt and pepper, stirring until thick.    3. Pour sauce over vegetables and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.    4. Bake at 350? for 30 minutes. Yield: 4 servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 16,  1998
