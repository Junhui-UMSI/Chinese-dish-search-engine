Aros Con Pollo
Pepper, Tomato sauce, Cumin, Water, Rice, Garlic chopped, Salt, Fryer, 
Cut up fryer, brown in shortening. Remove chicken, brown rice in same  shortening. Mix together, add 3 cups water. Stir in other ingredients,  cover and simmer till rice is tender.    >From Wimberley Hill Country Cook Book    Recipe by: Odelia Nevarez    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by c4@groupz.net on Feb 12, 1998
