Pork and Rhubarb Ragout
Vegetable oil, Black Pepper, Carrots; cut in julienne, Onions, Lemon juice, Salt, Boneless stewing pork, Rhubarb; cut in 2 cm pieces, Nutmeg, Chopped fresh parsley, Water, Cinnamon, Sugar, 
Brown pork with onion in oil. Add remaining ingredients. Cover and bring to  boil. Reduce heat and simmer 1 hour, stirring often. Serve over noodles and  sprinkle with chopped parsley, if desired.
