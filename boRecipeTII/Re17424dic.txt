Walnut Praline Cheesecake
Vanilla, Chopped toasted walnuts, (14-oz) sweetened condensed, Cream cheese, Walnuts; toased, finely, Brown Sugar, Firmly packed dark brown, Graham cracker crumbs, Whipping cream, Margarine; melted, Eggs, TOPPING, 
From: Bobb1744@aol.com    Date: Thu, 29 Feb 1996 21:35:08 -0500    Recipe By: Eagle Brand recipe/Bob b1744  Preheat oven to 300 f. Combine crumbs, walnuts, sugar and margarine. Press  into a 9" springform pan. In large mixer bowl, beat cheese until fluffy.  Gradually beat in sweetened condensed milk, then eggs and vanilla until  smooth. Pour into pan. Bake for 55 minutes or until center is set. Cool.  Top with Walnut Praline Topping. chill. Refrigerate leftovers.    Walnut Praline Topping: In saucepan, combine 1/3 cup each fimrly packed  dark brown sugar and whipping cream. Cook and stir until sugar dissolves.  Simmer for 5 minutes or until thickened. Add 1/2 cup chopped toasted  walnuts.    MC-RECIPE@MASTERCOOK.COM    MASTERCOOK RECIPES LIST SERVER    From the MasterCook recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
