Amaretto Slush
(6-oz) frozen orange juice, Amaretto, Sugar, Boiling water, Bottles (33.8-oz) ginger, Regular tea bags, Water, (6-oz) frozen lemonade, 
Here are some recipes for holiday season drinks, copied from Southern  Living Magazine a few years ago:     Pour boiling water over tea bags; cover and let stand 5 minutes.  Remove  tea bags and discard.  Transfer tea to a 2-quart freezer container.  Stir  in 3 1/2 C. water and next 4 ingredients. Cover and freeze until firm. To  serve, partially thaw until slushy. Pour equal amounts of slush mixture and  ginger ale into serving glasses. Yield: 1 gallon.    NDOOLEY@PRESIDENT-PO.PRESIDENT    .UIOWA.EDU    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
