Quick Baked Monster Shrimp
Cocktail sauce; to serve, Melted butter OR olive oil, Seasoned bread crumbs, Shrimp; peeled and deveined, 
Roll peeled and deveined shrimp, tail-on, in melted butter or olive oil -  then in seasoned bread crumbs. Bake quickly in a really hot oven - serve  with a hot and spicy cocktail sauce. The coating will puff up to make the  shrimp look really big.    *REF: Shrimp Recipes 11-Apr-98 WEBstationONE from San Diego, Ca, USA.
