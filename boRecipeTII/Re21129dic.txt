Two-Grain Pilaf With?
Chicken broth, 14.5oz fat, Celery, sliced, Wild rice, Tomatoes, low sodium,, Water, Salt, Yellow onions, diced, Dry white wine, Carrots, sliced, Pearled barley, uncooked, Dried basil, Pepper, Garlic, Chicken breasts without skin, 
1. Preheat oven to 350deg.    2. Trim any fat from meat. Coat frying pan or Dutch oven with cooking  spray. Add chicken and cook until brown on both sides. Remove from pan and  set aside. Re-coat pan with cooking spray and add carrots, celery, onion,  and garlic to pan; cook over medium heat for 5 minutes or until tender. Add  wine, scraping pan to loosen browned bits. Return meat to the Dutch oven,  or place meat and vegetables in a casserole. Add all remaining ingredients,  cover and cook 1 1/2 to 2 hours, until all liquids are absorbed and rice is  tender.    3. Remove meat and shred with forks, return to pan and serve.    NOTES : You can make a version of this recipe with beef shank.  Sliced  mushrooms are also a great addition!
