Ziti Noodles
Pepper, Ziti noodles, uncooked, Egg, Chopped parsley, Ricotta cheese, Salt, spaghetti sauce, mozzarella cheese, Ground beef, Parmesan cheese, 
Prepare ziti according to package. Brown beef. Remove from heat and stir in  ricotta cheese and next five ingredients. Add 1/2 of sauce and mix well.  Add ziti and toss to cover. Put in 9 x 13 inch pan. Spread balance of  spaghetti sauce on top of mixture, sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Bake at      350    degrees for 20 minutes.
