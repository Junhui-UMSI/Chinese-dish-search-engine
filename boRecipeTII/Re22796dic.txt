Apricot Sorbet
Frozen for at least twelve, Canned apricot halves; in, Amaretto; optional, 
Place the can of apricots in hot water for about 1 minute. Remove the lid  and pour any liquid into a food processor. Remove the bottom of the can,  slide the fruit out, and chop into 1-inch pieces.    Place in the food processor with the amaretto and process until smooth,  about 1 minute.    Serve at once or transfer to a container and place in the freezer.
