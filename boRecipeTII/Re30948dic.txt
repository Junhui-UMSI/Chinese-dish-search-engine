Tapioca Fruit Salad
fruit cocktail, tapioca pudding mix, Green grapes, canned, Pineapple chunks, canned, Mandarin oranges, canned, Whipped cream, marshmallows, 
Drain fruits, reserving liquid. Measure 3 cups of juice & add pudding mix.  Cook until clear & thick; cool. Add fruit & marshmallows. Fold in whipped  cream. Chill overnight.
