Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal
Tart apple, diced, Dash cinnamon, Apple juice, Skim milk, Rolled oats, Water, 
Place water, juice and apples ina medium saucepan and bring to a boil. Sitr  in the rolled oats and cinnamon.  Return to boil, reduce heat and simmer  until the porridge is thick.
