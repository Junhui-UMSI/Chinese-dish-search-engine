Barbecued Loin-Back Ribs
Meaty spareribs, sawed in 2, (to 4) loin back ribs or, Lemon juice, Brown sugar, Salt, Salad oil;, Tomato sauce, Bottled steak sauce, Strips about 4 inches wide, Onion, Water, Pepper, 
From: Gerald Edgerton <jerrye@wizard.com>    Date: Fri, 24 May 1996 12:36:03 -0700    For sauce, cook onion in hot oil until tender but not brown, add the next 7  ingredients. Simmer uncovered for 15 min or until of nice sauce consistency    Salt and pepper the ribs, lace in accordion style on spit, securing with  holding forks. Put  a drip pan under ribs. Attach spit to motor and lower  bbq hood. let ribs rotate over slow coals 1 hour or till meat is well done.  Last 30 min., brush ribs frequently with sauce and add dampened wood  (hickory) chunks to coals for smoke flavor.    Posted to Master Cook Recipes List, Digest #96
