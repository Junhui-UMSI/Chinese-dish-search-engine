Pimento White Rice
FORMATTED BY LISA CRAWFORD, Salt and pepper, Diced pimento, Long-grain white rice, olive oil, 
Place rice ina strainer and run cold water through it  to wash it.  Bring a large pot with 2 to 3 quarts  water to a boil.  Add rice and boil, uncovered, about  10 minutes.  Test a grain.  Rice should be cooked  through but not soft.  Drain.  Place in serving bowl  and add piment o and olive oil.  Toss well.  Add salt  and pepper to taste.    Nutritional info per serving: 215 cal; 9g pro, 38g  carb, 5g fat(16%), .5g fiber. 6mg sodium.
