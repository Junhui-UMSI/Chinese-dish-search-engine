Strawberry Bread #3
Flour, Butter or margarine, Eggs, Vanilla, milk, Walnuts, Sugar, Strawberries, Baking soda, Salt, 
Date: Tue, 12 Mar 96 6:01:52 EST    submitted by: bmarks@pipeline.com (Bev)    From SI Advance 96    Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Melt margarine in microwave and set aside.    In mixing bowl, add eggs to the sugar and mix.  Pour in melted margarine,  milk and vanilla. Add strawberries. Add flour, baking soda and nuts and  blend with a spoon.    Pour into greased loaf pan, sprinkle with sugar and bake for 1 hour.
