Banana Nut Cheesecake
Eggs, Margarine; melted, Walnuts, Chocolate wafer crumbs, Milk chocolate chips, Water, Mashed ripe bananas, Margarine, Cream cheese softened, Sugar, 
From: mark@alexr.co.uk    Date: Sun, 25 Feb 1996 00:00:24 GMT    Combine crumbs and margarine; press onto the bottom of a 9-inch springform  pan.  Bake at 350F.; 10 minutes. Combine cream cheese, sugar and banana,  mixing at medium speed on electric mixer until well blended. Add eggs, one  at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in walnuts, pour over  crust. Bake at 350F., 40 minutes. Loosen cake from rim;cool before  removing rim pf pan. Melt chocolate pieces and margarine with water over  low heat, stirring until smooth. Drizzle over cheesecake.
