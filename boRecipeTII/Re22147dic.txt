Turkey or Chicken Stuffing
Bread, Onions, poultry seasoning, celery, bulk sausage, Bacon, Parsley flakes, chicken giblets, Celery, Raisins, Salt and pepper, Eggs, Loaf, Chopped, Poultry seasoning, OPTIONAL:, 
Soak bread in water very briefly and squeeze, slice by slice, breaking in  1/8s in large bowl. Add bacon, onion, celery, salt, pepper, parsley, eggs  and poultry seasoning. Mix well. Can add raisins, apples, sausage and/or  cooked giblets. Stuff 1 turkey or 2 chickens. Put extra stuffing in loaf  pan and add for last 40 minutes of cooking time. Oyster Stuffing: To  stuffing above: substitute 1 pkg. sm. oysters, drained for bacon. May add  1/2 cup light cream or evaporated skim milk. Can make half regular and half  oyster for stuffing neck cavity.
