Pickled Beets #3
Sugar, Cloves, Cinnamon, Vinegar; 5% strength, Small; young beets, Allspice, Water, 
Wash beets and cook until tender. Dip in cold water and remove skins.  Slice. Make a syrup of the remaining ingredients, pour over the sliced  beets and boil slowly for 10 minutes, uncovered. Pack beets with syrup to  cover into hot, sterilized jars. Seal at once.
