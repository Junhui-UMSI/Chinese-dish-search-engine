Tuna Tapenade
Chopped, Pitted black olives (8oz), Lemon juice, Tuna in oil (7oz), drained, Cloves garlic; coarsely, Dark rum, olive oil, Fresh ground pepper, Capers; (or less), 
Place all ingredients except olive oil) in a food processor and blend until  smooth. Slowly add olive oil. Serve with fresh vegetables.
