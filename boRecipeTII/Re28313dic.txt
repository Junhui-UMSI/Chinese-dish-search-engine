Baked Grits
Butter, Regular or quick-cooking grits, Sharp cheddar cheese, Eggs, milk, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Cook the grits according to package  directions.  Regular girts require 25 to 30 minutes of slow cooking;  quick-cooking grits will take 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in the cheese, butter,  eggs, and milk and pour into a buttered 3-cup baking dish. Bake 40 minutes  or longer, or until set.
