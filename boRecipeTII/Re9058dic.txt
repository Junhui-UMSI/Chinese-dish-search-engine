Pritikin Muffins
Non fat yoghurt, Baking Powder, Oatbran, Skim milk, Apple juice concentrate, Apples, Cinnamon, Wholemeal flour, Sultanas, WET MIX, rind of 1 lemon, Rolled oats, Egg whites, DRY MIX, morello cherries, 
Preheat oven 350F & prepare pans. Thoroughly beat together the egg whites,  milk, yoghurt & apple juice, then mix in the apples, rind, cherries &  sultanas. Combine the dry mix & then fold into the wet mix. Spoon into pans  & bake for 25-30 mins. Makes 12.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
