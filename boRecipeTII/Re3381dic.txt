Peasant Soup
Head cabbage; shredded, Nutmeg, Dijon mustard, Bacon; diced, Heavy cream, Egg yolks, Onion; diced, Red apples; chopped, Flour, Cans chicken broth, Garlic, Cinnamon, 
Saute bacon and drain leaving 1-2 T. fat in pan. Saute onions and garlic..  Add cabbage and apple. Saute. Stir in flour. Cook for 3 minutes. Slowly add  chicken broth. Add pepper, nutmeg and cinnamon. Stir. Add bacon. Bring pot  to boil. Simmer, stirring occasionally, about 25 minutes. Just before  serving, stir in cream, mixed with egg yolks and some hot soup. Pour into  pot. Add mustard. Serve immediately.
