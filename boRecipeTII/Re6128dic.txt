Pizza Magic
Whole pita breads, Canned artichoke hearts;, Mozzarella cheese; shredded, Garlic flavored olive oil, Rehydrated sun-dried tomato, Basil; chopped, 
Heat a baking sheet in a very hot oven. Lightly brush pita breads on both  side with oil. Top each with tomato halves, artichokes. Sprinkle with basil  and cheese. Bake on hot sheet about 5 minutes until bread is crisp and  cheese is melted. Source Sonoma Sun-dried Tomatoes (wrv)
