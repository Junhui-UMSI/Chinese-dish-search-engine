Banana Fudge Walnut Brownies
Egg, Pkg.; (6 oz.) semi-sweet, Chopped walnuts; ( optional), Vanilla, Salt, Flour, Margarine, Sugar, Mashed banana; (1 small), Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven to 350. Grease bottom only of 8" square pan. Melt margarine  and chocolate pieces in saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly. Mix  all remaining ingredients together, add chocolate mixture, beat thoroughly  with a spoon. Spread batter evenly in pan. Bake 30 minutes or until center  is set.
