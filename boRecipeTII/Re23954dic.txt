Asparagus Casserole #1
Shredded velveete cheese, (15-oz) asparagus, salt and pepper, (17-oz) English peas, Hard boiled eggs; chopped, (10.75-oz) cream of mushroom, (3-oz) fried onion rings, Jar (4-oz) pimiento, 
Mix all ingredients except onion rings toget- her and pour into 1-1/2 quart  casserole dish; bake at 350  until bubbly. Remove from heat and pour onion  rings on top. Bake for 10 more minutes.
