Zesty Garlic Bread
Butter, Garlic, Hot Red Pepper, flakes, Parsley, French Bread, 
Preheat the broiler. Melt the butter in microwave on high for about 1  minute. Stir in the parsley, garlic and hot peper. Slice the bread in half  horizontally. Brush or spoon the garlic butter over the cut sides of the  bread. Place the bread on a baking sheet and broil 3 to 4 inches from the  heat about 3 minutes, until nicely toasted. Cut into 1" pieces and serve.    Recipe by: 5 in 10 Appetizer Cookbook    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Meg Antczak <meginny@frontiernet.net> on Mar  17, 1998
