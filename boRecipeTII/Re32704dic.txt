Sweet Potato Biscuits
Flour, Cooked sweet potatoes or, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Baking Powder, Salt, Wheat flour, Butter, milk, 
Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.  Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder  and salt; cut in the butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Stir in  the milk and sweet potatoes.  Knead gently (about 10 times).  Roll out  dough until about 1/2 inch thick.  Cut out biscuits using either a biscuit  cutter or a floured glass rim.  Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Bake for 10-12  minutes.  This makes about 10 biscuits, but the recipe can be doubled.
