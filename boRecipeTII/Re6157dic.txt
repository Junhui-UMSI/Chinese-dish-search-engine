Pizza Presto
Black olives; pit, slice, Margarine or butter, Yellow onion; sliced, Oregano, Pepperoni; sliced, Mozzarells; cut thin strips, Ground meat; cooked, Black pepper to taste, Cheddar cheese; grated, Or 4 sm Boboli shells, Green pepper; cut in strips, 
Preheat oven to 450~. Saute onion until tender. Add green pepper and  pepperoni, stir frequently for several minutes. Remove from heat and add  olives and cooked beef. Mix combination together with oregano and pepper  and spread on Boboli. Sprinkle grated cheese and mozzarella over top. Heat  for 6 to 8 minutes. Serve piping hot. (wrv)
