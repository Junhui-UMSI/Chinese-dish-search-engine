"Second Time Around" Couscous Salad
Chopped fresh parsley, Thawed frozen peas, Dijon mustard, Rib celery,; finely diced, Smoked or regular cooked, Cooked couscous, Blue cheese, Mayonnaise, Rice vinegar to taste, Scallion,; thinly sliced, 
In a mixing bowl combine the couscous, scallion, celery, chicken and peas.    In another mixing bowl combine blue cheese, mayonnaise, mustard and rice  vinegar to taste; season with salt and pepper.
