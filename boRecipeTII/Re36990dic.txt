Beet and Buttermilk Soup
White vinegar, Beets; (1-1/4 lb.), Cucumber; (diced unpeeled), Granulated sugar, Green Onions, Light sour cream, Chopped fresh dill or, Buttermilk, Fresh dill or coriander, Salt, 
This glorious pink soup is delicious with traditional dill or fresh  coriander.    In saucepan of boiling salted water, cover and cook beets until tender and  skins slip off easily, about 25 minutes. Drain and let cool; slip off skins  and cut into 1/4-inch (5 mm) dice. Cover and refrigerate until chilled.  (Beets can be refrigerated for up to 3 days.)    In large bowl, whisk together buttermilk, 1/2 cup (125 mL) of the onions,  sour cream, dill, sugar, vinegar and salt. Cover and refrigerate until  chilled or for up to 6 hours. Taste and adjust seasoning.    Ladle buttermilk mixture into serving bowls. Swirl in beets and cucumber.  Garnish with remaining green onions and dill or coriander sprigs.    Makes 6 servings.
