Pillsburys Original Tunnel of Fudge Cake
Fudge Buttercream Frosting, Walnuts, Sugar, Pillsbury Two-Layer size, Butter, Mix, Double Dutch, Eggs, 
Cream together the butter with the eggs, one egg at a time.  Gradually add  the sugar and beat until light and fluffy. Stir in the flour by hand. Add  the frosting mix and incorporate into mixture until well-blended. Add  walnuts. Pour into well-greased bundt pan and bake at 350#161# for 60-65  minutes.
