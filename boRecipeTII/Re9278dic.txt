Pueblo Green Chile Stew
Water, Blue corn meal, Peeled,, Green chiles -- roasted and, Stew meat -- cubed, Garlic powder, Salt, 
Cover meat with water in a large pan, bring to a boil and simmer for 4  hours.  Then add the green chiles, chopped, salt and garlic powder. Mix the  cornmeal in a little col;d water and stir in rapidly. When the mixture has  thickened, simmer for about 45 minutes.
