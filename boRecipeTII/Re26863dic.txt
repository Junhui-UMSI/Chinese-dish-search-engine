Baked Beans #02
Brown sugar, Chili, Bell pepper; chopped, Bacon, Onion, Barbecue sauce or tomato, (large) pork and beans, 
Mix all ingredients except bacon & place in a long pyrex dish. Place bacon  on top. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.    JEWELL GRIFFIN    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
