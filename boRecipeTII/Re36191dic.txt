Beef Stock #1
Bay leaf, Onion; coarsely chopped, Beef bones, Garlic, Stalk celery; coarsely, Peppercorns, Tomato paste, Thyme, Medium-size carrots;, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Bake bones until browned, about 1 hour.    2. Place bones in large stock pot. Add remaining ingredients & enough water  to cover. Bring to boil; boil about 2 minutes.    3. Remove from heat & strain. Discard bones & vegetables. Return the stock  to pot & cook over medium heat until reduced by 1/2.    MARIOS    TURTLE CREEK VILAGE, DALLAS. USE    IN LASAGNE VERDE PAVAROTTI    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of Italian Recipes>.  Downloaded from  G Internet, G Internet.
