Quinoa Garden Cakes with Lemony Yogurt
Quinoa, Water, Red onion, Garlic, carrots, yellow squash, spinach, zest of 1 large lemon, Unbleached flour, Baking Powder, Egg, Salt, Black Pepper, Dill, Canola oil, Yogurt sauce:, Nonfat plain yogurt, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
PREP: Rinse quinoa well. Bring quinoa and water to a boil, simmer 20  minutes or until all liquid is absorbed. Cool. Combine all ingredients with the exception of 1 C yogurt, lemon juice, canola oil. Preheat oven to 400  degrees. Rub oil on cookie sheet. Divide quinoa into 8 balls. Flatten each ball onto cookie sheet to make a 4-inch cake. Bake 10 minutes, flip cakes over. Bake 10 minutes more. Combine yogurt and lemon juice, serve with cakes.
