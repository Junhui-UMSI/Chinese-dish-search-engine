Bacon Bits for Dogs (Not For Humans)
Graham flour, dry milk powder, Wheat germ, Bacon fat, Cornmeal, Eggs, bacon, Water, 
Contributed by Sandra Strassman-Sundy  Mix ingredients with a strong spoon; drop heaping tablespoonfuls onto a  greased baking sheet. Bake in a 350 oven for 15 minutes. Turn off oven and  leave cookies on baking sheet in the oven overnight to dry out.    Yield: about 4 dozen dog cookies.    Posted to Bread-bakers Digest by Marg aka MEMAW <maf@fn.net> on Mar 29,  1998
