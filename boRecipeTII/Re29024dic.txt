Baked Pineapple #4
Stick margarine; melted, Pineapple (1 tidbits and 1, Sugar, Flour, Roll Ritz crackers; crushed, Grated cheese; (Cheddar), 
Pour both cans pineapple in 8 x 12-inch dish (one drained, one not  drained). Mix together flour and sugar; sprinkle over pineapple. Spread  shredded cheese over flour sugar mixture. Pour crushed crackers over all.  Pour melted margarine over crackers. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30  minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 23,  1998
