Peachy Bran Muffins
Baking Powder, (1 pound) peaches packed in, Wheat bran, Egg whites, Ww flour, Chopped dried peaches or, Sugar, 
Combine the flour, wheat bran, sugar and baking powder and stir to mix  well. Set aside. Drain the peaches, reserving the juice, and puree in a  blender. Add enough juice to the pureed peaches to bring the volume up to 1  1/2 cups. Add the peach puree and the egg whites to the flour mixture and  stir just until dry ingredients are moistened. Fold in the dried peaches or  pecans. Fill muffin tins 3/4 full and bake at 350 for 15 to 17 min. Let sit        5    min.  Posted to FATREEE list members by Michelle Dick while the list was down.
