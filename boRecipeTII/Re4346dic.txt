Persimmon Holiday Cookies
Persimmons, Ground cloves, Ground nutmeg, Granulated sugar, Butter, Dates, Egg, Baking soda, Pecans, Chocolate chips, All-purpose flour, 
Using a food processor or mixer thoroughly process the persimmon pulp,  butter, baking soda and sugar until well blended and creamy. Add the whole  egg, beat well.    In another bowl sift flour with spices, then add the nuts and dates  blending by hand. Add the processed pulp and mix well.    Drop by teaspoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet. Space mounds 1" apart.  (They remain mounded and do not overly spread when baked).    Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown. Cool on racks.    Melt chocolate chips in a plastic squeeze bottle that is placed in hot  water. When melted drizzle the chocolate over the cookies or dip one half  of each cookie into melted chocolate.
