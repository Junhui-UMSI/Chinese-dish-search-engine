Picnic Coleslaw
8 Bacon slices; cooked,, 1 t            Salt, 2 c            Carrots;, 1/2 c            Cider, 1/3 c            Vegetable, 12 Green onions with tops;, 1/3 c            Sugar, 6 c            Cabbage;, 
Recipe by: Sue Klapper    In a bowl, combine cabbage, carrots, bacon and onions. In a jar with  tight-fitting lid, mix vinegar, oil, sugar and salt; shake well. Just  before serving, pour dressing over cabbage mixture and toss.    From:  Taste of Home Magazine.
