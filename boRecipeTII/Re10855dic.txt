Quinoa Soup with Corn
Quinoa, Cayenne pepper, parsley, fresh, vegetable broth, Garlic, Water, corn kernels, Fresh lime juice, 
Place broth, rinsed (IMPORTANT - to take away bitterness) quinoa, water,  cayenne and garlic in a small soup pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to  medium and cook 10 minutes. Add corn kernels and cook 3 minutes. Add  parsley or cilantro and cook for 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in the  lime juice. Serve hot or cold.
