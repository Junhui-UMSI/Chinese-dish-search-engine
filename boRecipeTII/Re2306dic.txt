Barley, Tomato, and Cucumber Salad with Yogurt Dressing
Tomatoes, Kalamata olives, Dill-Yogurt Dressing, Scallions, Black Pepper, BARLEY, pearl barley, Salt, Cucumber, 
Make the Dill-Yogurt Dressing in this file.    For the salad, bring 1 quart water to boil in a medium saucepan. Add barley  and 1 teaspoon salt; return to boil and simmer until barley is tender,  about 30 minutes. Drain barley, then transfer to a large nonreactive bowl;  cool slightly. Then add remaining ingredients, including Dill-Yogurt  Dressing, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper; toss to combine. Chill.  (Can be covered and refrigerated up to 4 hours.) Serve.
