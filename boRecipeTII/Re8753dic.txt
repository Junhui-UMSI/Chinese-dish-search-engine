Pour in the Pan Pizza Pancake
Flour, mozzarella, tomato sauce, Oregano, Eggs, Salt and pepper, mushrooms, Salt, milk, 
In a bowl, mix flour, eggs, seasonings. Pour batter into a greased 12 inch  round pizza pan. Bake in a 400 degree oven to form " crust ". It should  appear set. Remove from oven and spread on sauce, cheese and mushrooms.  Return to oven to bake 20 to 25 minutes more.
