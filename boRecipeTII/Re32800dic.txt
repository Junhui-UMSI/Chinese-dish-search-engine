Sweet Potato Raisin Cookies
Walnuts, chopped, Sweet potatoes, Non stick veg cooking spray, Allspice, Baking soda, Vanilla, Salt, Butter or margarine, Unprocessed bran flakes, Raisins, Cinnamon, Flour, Nutmeg, Egg, 
Preheat oven to 350~. Soak raisins in hot water to cover 5 minutes; drain.  Cream butter, then add sweet potato, egg, & vanilla; beat till creamy. Mix  flour, allspice, salt, nutmeg, baking soda, baking powder and cinnamon. Add  to creamed mixture and mix well. Add raisins, nuts and bran flakes. Drop  onto cookie sheet sprayed w/ veg cooking spray. Bake 12 min or until done.    Source: Cincinnati Enquirer, Shared by ROBBIE WARINNER, Prodigy ID#  TPBD20B.
