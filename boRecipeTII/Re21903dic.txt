Applejack Apples
Lemon juice, Apple cider, Apples -- medium size, Unsalted butter, Applejack -- * see note, 
* Use Calvados, a French apple brandy, or regular brandy if preferred. Red  wine may also be used with excellent results.    1. Arrange the apple slices in a glass baking dish. Sprinkle with lemon  juice and Applejack or your choice of brandies or wine. Dot with the  butter. Add enough apple cider to fill the dish to a depth of 1 inch. 2.  Bake in a preheated 375-degree oven for 20 minutes.
