Beef Salad
Red wine vinegar, Salt, Dill weed, Julienne cut cold roast beef, Lemon juice, Sour cream, Water, Sugar, White pepper, Torn salad greens, Onion; thinly sliced, 
Simmer vinegar, water, lemon juice, sugar, dill weed, salt and white pepper  10 to 15 minutes. Cool. Toss with beef and onion. Chill. Drain, reserving  marinade. Place greens in bowl. Top with beef and onion. Combine sour cream  with reserved marinade. Toss with greens and beef.
