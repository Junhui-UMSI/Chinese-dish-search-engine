Eggnog Pudding
Cold milk, (3.4 oz.) instant vanilla, Ground nutmeg, Rum extract, Additional ground nutmeg,, 
In a bowl, combine the first four ingredients.  Beat for 2 minutes. Spoon  into individual dishes. Sprinkle with nutmeg if desired.
