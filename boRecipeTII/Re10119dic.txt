Quentins Fantasy Whip
Instant chocolate pudding, Pecans, Stick butter, melted, Cool Whip, Cheese Cake, 
Spread butter and pecans on bottom of a pyrex dish.  Use any simple cheese  cake recipe; pour on top.  Mix instant chocolate pudding and pour on top of  that.  Spread Cool Whip over the top and sprinkle extra nuts on top. Keep  in refrigerator.  You may make this a day ahead.    Penny Dastugue (Rep.
