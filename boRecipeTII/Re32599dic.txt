Sweet Noodle Kugel
Broad noodles, Crushed pineapple, Sugar, Vanilla, Drained, Butter, Eggs, Drained, Cinnamon sugar, Dried apricots, Sour cream, 
Grease 9 by 13 inch-pan. Combine all ingredients except cinnamon sugar.  Pour into prepared pan. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Bake at 350F for 45  minutes.    Shared by: Dan Klepach, Nov/95 From: Intercook
