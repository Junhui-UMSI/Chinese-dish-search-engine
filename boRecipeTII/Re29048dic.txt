Baked Pork with Pecans
Dry breadcrumbs, Pecans; toasted, Cornmeal, Garlic, Sage; or 1-1/2 Tbs. fresh, Cayenne pepper, Egg white, Pork tenderloin chops; cut, Dijon mustard, Water, 
Prep: 5 min, Cook: 40 min.    Preheat oven to 400F. Combine first 6 ingredients (dry bread crumbs, pecans, cornmeal, garlic, sage, cayenne pepper) and salt to taste in a bowl. Whisk in egg white. Place pork slices between two sheets of wax paper and pound lightly with a mallet or other heavy flat object to flatten slightly. Combine mustard and water in a bowl. Dip pork slices in mustard  mixture. Dredge in breadcrumbs, shaking off excess. Press breadcrumbs to  hold. Place pork in a single layer on an oiled baking pan. Bake 40 minutes  or until coating is browned and crispy.  Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Mar 02, 1998
