Thousand Island Dressing (Hellmanns Mayo)
8-oz cup Dannon Plain Yogurt, Hard-cooked egg, finely, Chili sauce, Chopped, Olives, Grated onion, Chopped pimiento-stuffed, Chopped dill pickle, 
Stir together first 6 ingredients.  Fold in yogurt. Chill. This is from the  Hellmanns advertising back in the 70s, and was taken from the 1979  edition of "Best Recipes From the Backs of Boxes, Bottles, Cans, and Jars".  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Apr 21, 98
