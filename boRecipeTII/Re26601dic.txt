Bagels and Jam
Bagels -- halved, Strawberry jam, 
Preparation Time: 0:02 This is a simple, fast breakfast for busy days. Corn  Butter (in this file) can be used instead of jam if you like. Those who are  trying to lose weight should choose rice cakes rather than bagels, which  are high in calories.    Toast the bagels or rice cakes, if desired, and spread with jam.
