Taco Dogs
Taco sauce, Hot dogs, Tortillas; (1 for each dog), Cheddar cheese, Shredded lettuce, 
Cook hot dogs on a grill or broil in oven. Warm the tortillas. Put hot dogs  on tortillas. Add lettuce, cheese and taco sauce. Roll up and eat.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 403 by "Diane. Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Dec 26, 1997
