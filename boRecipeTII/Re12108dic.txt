Zraziki Po Krakowsku (Beef Roll-Ups)
Butter, Salt and pepper, Water, Round steak, Chopped parsley, Egg, Bread crumbs, Onion, grated, 
Cut steak into thin pieces, about 4 1/2 by 2 inches. Score, sprinkle with  salt and pepper. Combine bread crumbs, egg, onion and 4 T of the melted  butter, salt and pepper. Spread the dressing on each piece of meat, roll up  and fasten with a skewer or a toothpick. Roll in flour and brown in  remaining butter. Add 2 cups of water (meat stock if you have it) and  simmer 1 1/2 hours. Remove toothpicks. Pour gravy over meat. Garnish with  chopped parsley and serve with mashed potatoes.
