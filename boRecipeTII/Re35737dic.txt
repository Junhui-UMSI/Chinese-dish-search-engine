Stuffed Cheese Pizza
Salt, Warm Water (110 to 115, Dried Oregano, Cooked Fresh Broccoli,, Water, Package Frozen Broccoli, Part Skim Mozzarella Cheese,, (or 1 tablespoon) Active Dry, Whole wheat flour, Vegetable oil, Pizza Sauce, 
To make the crust, dissolve the yeast in the warm water in a large bowl.  Add the flour, salt, oregano, oil and 1 cup water. Mix well. Knead the  dough on a lightly-floured surface for about 2 minutes, or until the dough  is smooth. Place the dough in an oiled bowl. Cover with a damp towel and  let rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in bulk.    Punch down the dough.  Divide into two parts.  Press half the dough onto  the bottom of a 10-inch oiled pie pan., Sprinkle the cheese and broccoli  over the dough. Press the other half of the dough into a 10-inch round. Set  on top of the cheese and broccoli. Crimp the edges together. Make several  slashes in the top crust.    Bake in a 400-degree oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until golden brown.  Remove from the oven.  Spread on the pizza sauce. Bake for 5 minutes.
