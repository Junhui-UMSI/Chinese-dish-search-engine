Bbq Chicken
Italian dressing, Fryer-broiler chicken, Barbecue sauce, 
Mix sauce and dressing. Place chicken, skin-side down, on greased grill  over low coals. Grill uncovered 50 to 60 minutes or untilk tender, brushing  frequently with sauce during last 30 minutes, turning occasionally.
