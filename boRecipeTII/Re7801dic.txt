Portobellos & Roasted Peppers with Soy-Balsamic Vinaigrette
Minced fresh rosemary, Minced fresh sage, Soy sauce, Red bell pepper, Portobello mushrooms, olive oil, olive oil, Yellow bell pepper, Garlic, Mixed baby greens, SOYBALSAMIC VINAIGRETTE, Dried crushed red pepper, Balsamic vinegar, Green bell pepper, 
Roast peppers over gas flame or in broiler until skins are black all over.  Place in a covered bowl or paper bag and let stand 10 minutes.  Remove and  discard peels and seeds from peppers.  Slice peppers 1/4 inch thick. (May  be prepared up to 1 day in advance and refrigerated until ready to  proceed.)    In a large skillet, heat oil on medium high and saute mushrooms and garlic  until tender.  Stir in rosemary and sage, and cook for another 30 seconds.  Let mixture cool to room temperature.    Place mushrooms and peppers over greens on a large platter.  Dress with  Soy-Balsamic Vinaigrette (below).    SOY-BASALMIC VINAIGRETTE:  Whisk together vinegar, soy sauce and red pepper  in a medium bowl.  Whisk in oil a little at a time.  Add salt and pepper to  taste.  May be prepared 1 day in advance, and stored in the refrigerator in  a covered container.  Allow dressing to come to room temperature, and whisk  again before serving.
