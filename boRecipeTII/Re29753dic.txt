Tempeh and Vegetable Chili
Paprika, Cumin, Water or tomato juice, Kidney or pinto beans, olive oil, White or blue dried corn, Green bell pepper; seeded, Garlic; minced, Tabasco style pepper sauce, Tomatoes; peeled and stewed, Tempeh (8oz), Oregano, Red bell pepper; seeded and, Onion, Chili powder or paste from, 
Soak the beans and corn overnight, then cook until done. Cut the tempeh  into small cubes and brown with the onions in oil. (Alternately you can  grate the tempeh) Add bell peppers and garlic and saute 5 to 10 minutes.  Stir in the tomatoes, beans, corn, and herbs and spices. Add water or  tomato juice.  Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for an  hour., stirring often to prevent sticking and burning. Serve with lime  wedges squeezed over each serving or with grated vegan cheese.    Total calories per serving: 408   Fat: 10 grams    Source: Vegetarian Journal, Jan/Feb 1995 Poohs Recipe dbase  (lisa_pooh@delphi.com) 2/4/96
