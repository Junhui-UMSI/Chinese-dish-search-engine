Pretzel Cream Salad
Envelope Dream Whip or 2 c, Frozen strawberries, Crushed pretzels, Prepared whipped topping, Canned pineapple juice, Butter, Cream cheese, softened, Powdered strawberry gelatin, 
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Mix first three ingredients and spread into a  13-by-9-inch baking pan. Bake in preheated oven 10 minutes.    Prepare whipped topping (if Dream whip is used) as directed on package. Add  cheese and sugar. Spread over cooked mixture. Bring pineapple juice to  boil, remove from heat and stir in gelatin to dissolve. Add berries,  stirring vigorously with fork until separated. Refrigerate.    When gelatin begins to set, spread it evenly over cheese mixture.  Refrigerate several hours until completely set.
