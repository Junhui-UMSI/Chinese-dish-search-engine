Tomatoes with Avocado Dressing
Lemon or lime juice, Mayonnaise, Onion juice, Lettuce leaves, Chives, Tomatoes, Worcestershire Sauce, Ripe avocado, Salt to taste, 
Put avocado in blender or food processor. Mix with mayonnaise, add lemon or  lime juice, onion juice, worcestershire sauce, and salt. Peel 3 large  tomatoes, slice and arrange on lettuce leaves. Pour dressing over tomatoes  and sprinkle with chopped chives. Serves 4.    Recipe by: Calavo    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #839 by L979@aol.com on Oct 12, 1997
