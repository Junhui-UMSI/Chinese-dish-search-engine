Stuffed Artichokes (Mf)
Whole milk ricotta cheese, Salt, Egg, lightly beaten, Artichokes cooked, chokes, Grated Parmesan cheese, Black forest ham, in thin, Butter, Dry bread crumbs, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Blend the ricotta, egg, Parmesan and  season to taste with salt and pepper. Blend in the ham. Stuff the  artichokes and set them in a baking pan. Add some water to the bottom of  the baking pan. Sprinkle breadcrumbs and melted butter over the top and  bake for 30 minutes or until the top is golden.
