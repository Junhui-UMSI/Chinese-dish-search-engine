Battered Onion Rings
Egg white, Salt, Flour, Cayenne pepper, Water, Vegetable oil, 
BY: The Mining Company Southern American Cuisine    Cut onion into rings and cover with ice water. Refrigerate while making the  batter. Combine batter ingredients, folding in stiffly beaten egg white  last. Drain the chilled onion ring slices, dip in the batter and fry in  batches in hot oil (375?) for 3 to 5 minutes or until golden brown. Drain  on paper towels.
