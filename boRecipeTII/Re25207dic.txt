Tomatoes with Basil, Peppers and Blue Cheese
Tomatoes; coarsely chopped, Red wine vinegar, olive oil, salt and pepper, Basil leaves; chopped, Onion, Pepper, hot or sweet;, Blue cheese; crumbled, 
Put vegetables in bowl.  Mix dressing ingredients and pour over vegetables.  Chill in refrigerator for at least one hour.
