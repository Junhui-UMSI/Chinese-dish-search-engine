Sweet Potato Pie 4
Vanilla extract, Sweet potatoes; diced, Sugar, 9 inch pie crust; unbaked, Stick unsalted butter, Eggs, Buttermilk, 
Preheat oven to 325. Place the potatoes in cold water, bring to a boil and  cook until tender, 15-20 minutes. Drain, and mash the potatoes while hot  with the butter and sugar. Let this mixture cool slightly and stir in the  beaten eggs, buttermilk and vanilla.
