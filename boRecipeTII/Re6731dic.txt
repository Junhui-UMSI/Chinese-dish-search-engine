Polish Mistakes
Loaves, Salt and pepper, Velveeta cheese, Hot sausage meat, Worcestershire Sauce, Ground beef, 
Brown meat separately and drain well.  Combine with seasoning in skillet.  Add cheese until melted.  Cool and then spread mixture on bread on a cookie  sheet.  Place the cookie sheet in the freezer until frozen.  Remove the  breads from the cookie sheet and put in bags.  When ready to serve, bake in  350 F oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
