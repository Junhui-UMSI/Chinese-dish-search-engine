Poppy Seed Biscuits for Two
milk, Poppy seeds (approximate), Baking Powder, Salt, Shortening, Flour for rolling out, Sour cream (approximate), All-purpose flour; UNsifted, 
Source: Cooking For Two When Minutes Matter Working    Yield: 8-9 biscuits - serves 2-3    1. Turn oven to 450 degrees F.    2. In a 1-qt. bowl stir together flour, baking powder, and salt. With a  pastry blender cut in shortening until evenly distributed.    3. With a fork stir milk into dry mixture and form into a ball. On a  floured surface knead mixture a few strokes, then roll out to 1/2-inch  thickness. Cut out 4 to 5 two-inch biscuits. Reroll dough and cut out  remaining biscuits.    4. Spread top of each biscuit with about 1/3 tsp. sour cream and sprinkle  with 1/4 tsp. poppy seeds. Place on a small baking sheet and bake in upper  third of oven until lightly browned, 10-12 minutes.
