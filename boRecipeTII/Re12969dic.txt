Zesty Mashed Potatoes
Minced green chilies, Potatoes, baked or boiled, Salt, Fresh parsley for garnish, Ghee, Water or soy milk, Black pepper, 
Mash the potatoes sans their skins.  Add all the ingredients. Use warmed  water or soy milk.  Whisk till creamy with a fork. Serve immediately or  keep warm in a double broiler.    Adapted from Yamuna Devi, "The Art of Indian Vegetarian Cooking"
