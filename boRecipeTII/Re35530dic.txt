Strawberry-Rhubarb Pie #1
rhubarb, strawberries, Granulated sugar, Cornstarch, Lemon juice, Cinnamon, Pastry, Egg, 
From: dan@clark.net (Dan Eisenreich) (COLLECTION)    Date: 4 May 1994 02:46:47 GMT    In bowl combine first 6 ingredients. On lightly floured surface roll out 1/2 of the pastry and line pie plate. Spoon in filling. Brush pastry rim  with egg. Roll out top pastry. With a pastry wheel or a knife, cut into 1"  wide strips. Gently weave strips over pie to form lattice. Trim and flute  edge. Brush with egg. Bake on a baking sheet at 425F for 15 min. Reduce  heat to 375F and bake 50-60 min, or until rhubarb is tender, filling thickened, and crust golden. Makes 8 servings; per serving: 365 calories, g  protein, 14 g fat, 59 g carb.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /FRUIT    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
