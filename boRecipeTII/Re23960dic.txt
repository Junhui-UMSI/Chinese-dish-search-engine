Asparagus Casserole #6
Hard-boiled eggs; thinly, Flour, Black Pepper, (12-oz) asparagus, Salt to taste, Margarine, CREAM SAUCE, Juice of canned asparagus, Grated sharp Cheddar cheese, Crushed Ritz crackers, milk, 
To make cream sauce, melt margarine and add flour. Mix well. Add milk and  juice of asparagus. Cook in a saucepan, stirring constantly, until thick.  Add Cheddar cheese, salt and pepper. In a casserole layer 1/2 can  asparagus, 1 egg, 1/2 cream sauce. Repeat. Top with Ritz cracker crumbs and  heat at 350? for 20 to 30 minutes. Yield: 4 servings.    CINDY WAGE (MRS.
