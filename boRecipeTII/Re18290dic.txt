Veprovy Gulash (Pork Goulash)
Lard, Caraway seeds, Salt, Flour, onion, Paprika, Fresh pork shoulder, Water, 
Fry onion in lard.  Add paprika, meat, caraway seeds, and salt; brown  well; add 1/2 cup water; simmer in a covered pan until meat is tender  (about 1 hour). Dust drippings with flour; stir until brown. Add remaining  water, and simmer for 10 to 20 minutes. Serves 4.
