Pizza Alla Ferrovia
Sheet pan, Crushed red pepper flakes, Recipe bread dough, Italian tuna, drained of, Pitted Gaeta olives, Basic tomato sauce, Fresh mozzarella, thinly, 
Preheat oven to 424 degrees F.    Dust a clean work surface lightly with flour. Place entire dough on  surface, spread it out, and flatten with fingers or rolling pin until you  form a large rectangle (about 12 inches by 15 inches, and 1/4 inch to 3/8  inch thick). Dust sheet pan with flour and place dough on pan, stretching  it to reach all sides. Using a kitchen spoon, smooth a thin layer of tomato  sauce over pizza dough all the way to the edges. Sprinkle with olives.  Place mozzarella and crumbled tuna over entire rectangle. Sprinkle with hot  pepper and bake 20 minutes, or until golden brown on bottom and cheese is  melted. Remove and serve immediately.    Yield: 4 servings    Recipe by: Molto Mario    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #476 by "suechef@sover.net"  <suechef@sover.net> on Feb 7, 1997.
