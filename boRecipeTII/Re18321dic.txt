Vermicelli with Shellfish
Littleneck clams; shucked, Vermicelli or other thin, Chili pepper flakes, Sea scallops; rinsed and, Plus 2 tsp. olive oil, Crushed tomatoes; peeled, Fresh Italian parsley;, Garlic, 
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 20 min.    Heat 1 Tbs. plus 1 tsp. oil in a heavy nonstick skillet over medium high  heat. saute garlic 1-2 minutes or until golden. Reduce heat to medium. Add  tomatoes and chili pepper flakes and simmer 15 minutes, stirring  occasionally. Discard garlic. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted  water 5-7 minutes, until al dente. Drain well. Meanwhile, heat remaining 1  Tbs. plus 1 tsp. oil in a heavy nonstick skillet over medium high heat.  Stir in scallops and saute 2 minutes. Add chopped clams with their liquid,  and the tomato sauce. Cook about 3 minutes, until liquid is slightly  reduced. Stir in parsley. Serve sauce over pasta.    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 20, 1998
