Apple Cider Chicken
Cored, Unsalted butter, 1/2 & 1/2, Apple cider, Salt, Parsley, red onion, fresh thyme leaves, Whole chicken breasts, Flour, Pepper, Granny smith apple, 
In 12" nonstick skillet, melt 3 Tbls. butter. Add onion & thyme; over  medium-low heat, saute 12-15 minutes, until onion is soft & begins to  brown. Stir in apple & cider; over med heat, saute 8 minutes, until  apple is soft. Remove from skillet; keep warm. On waxed paper, coat  chicken with flour. In skillet, melt remaining 1 Tbls. butter; over  med heat, cook chicken 4 to 5 minutes each side. Remove to platter;  keep warm. To pan, add apple mixture, half & half, salt & pepper;  when hot, spoon over chicken. Sprinkle with parsley.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
