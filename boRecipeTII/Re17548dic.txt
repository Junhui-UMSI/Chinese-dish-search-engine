Almond Crescents
Almond extract, Butter, Flour, Salt, Powdered Sugar,sifted, Almonds,finely chopped, Powdered sugar, Oats,uncooked, 
Heat oven to 325 degress F. Beat butter and almond extract till  fluffy;gradually beat in sugar. Add combined flour and salt;mix well.  Stir in oats and almonds. Shape to form crescents. Place on ungreased  cookie sheet. Bake 15-18 minutes or until light golden brown. Sift  powdered sugar over warm crescents.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcyber3.zip
