Quick and Easy Dill Mustard
olive oil, Fresh Dill, Dried Dill Weed, Dijon mustard, 
Combine Dijon mustard with fresh chopped dill or dried dill weed and olive  oil.    Serve with pate or fish; or use as a sandwich spread. Also good added to a  salad dressing.    NOTE: Homemade mustards must be stored in the refrigerator.    SOURCE: Gifts from The Kitchen SHARED BY:Jim Bodle 8/92
