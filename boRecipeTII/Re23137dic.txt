Tuna Almandine
Noodles with almond sauce mx, Onion, Tuna; drained, flaked, Mushrooms; chopped, drained, Boil water, 
Put noodles (just as they come from box) into buttered casserole and  sprinkle with sauce mix.  Stir in water, mushrooms, tuna, and onion. Comver  and bake at 375F for 25 minutes. Sprinkle with almonds that come in the  package over top just before serving.    From:  The Tuna Cookbook
