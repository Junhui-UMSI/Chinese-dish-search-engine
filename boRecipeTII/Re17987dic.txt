Amaretto Coffee
Amaretto, Dessert Topping -- * see, Warm water, Instant Coffee Crystals, Note, 
*    Dessert topping should be in a pressurized can.    In a nonmetal mug stir together water and instant coffee crystals.  Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power about 1 1/2 minutes or just till  mixture is steaming hot. Stir in Amaretto.    Top with pressurized dessert topping.    Recipe By     :
