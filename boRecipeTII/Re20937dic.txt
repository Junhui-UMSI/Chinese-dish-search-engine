Apple Pan Dowdy
Tart apples, peeled and, Light molasses, Whipped cream, Refrigerated biscuit dough, Unsalted butter, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, 
Preheat oven to 375.  Arrange apple slices in a buttered shallow 9 inch  baking pan or dish.  Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Drizzle molasses over and dot  with butter.  Unroll dough and place over apples.  Cut dashes in dough to  allow steam to escape.  Bake 30-35 minutes or until bubbly. Serve hot with  whipped cream sprinkled with nutmeg. This recipe serves 8.
