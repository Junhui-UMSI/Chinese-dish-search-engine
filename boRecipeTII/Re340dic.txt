Lemon Bars Deluxe
Flour, Sifted powdered sugar, Butter or margarine, Eggs, Granulated sugar, Lemon juice, Flour, Baking Powder, 
Sift together 2 cups flour & powdered sugar. Cut in butter until mixture  clings together. Press into 13x9x2-inch baking pan. Bake for 20-25 minutes  at 350 or until lightly browned. Beat eggs, granulated sugar & lemon juice.  Sift together 1/4 cup flour & the baking powder. Stir into egg mixture.  Pour over baked crust & bake for 25 minutes in 350 oven. Sprinkle with  powdered sugar & cool; cut into bars. Yields 30 bars.    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
