Peanut Butter Dip
Peanut butter, Sesame seeds, Jam (flavour is at the, Molasses, Vinegar, 
Mix all ingredients, save the seeds, together until as smooth of a  consistency possible is achieved.  Add the seeds and stir in until  well-blended.    This recipe is best served with Bretton crackers, I find, but any dipable  cracker/chip will suffice.
