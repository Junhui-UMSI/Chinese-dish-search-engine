Banana Daiquiri
Triple sec, Banana, Heavy cream, Lime juice, ice, Light rum, Banana liqueur, 
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend at high speed until smooth.  Pour into a sour, champagne, or cocktail glass.    Recipe by: Joe Robertson Posted to EAT-L Digest by LDGOSS  <LDGOSS@PRODIGY.NET> on Jul 7, 1997
