Perfect Salad
Sugar replacement, Salt, Carrots;, Cucumbers;, Cream cheese;, Unflovored gelatin, Onions;, Cold water;, Hot water, Lo-cal Mayonnaise;, Lemon juice;, 
Dissolve gelatin in cold water.  Add gelatin mixture, sugar  replacement, and salt to hot water; stir until dissolved.  Add lemon  juice, cucumber, carrots, and onion.  Beat cream cheese with  mayonnaise until smooth. Blend into vegetable mixture. Pour into mold  and chill.
