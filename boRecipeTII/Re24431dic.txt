Tropical Fruit Dessert
Kiwi; peeled and sliced, Sugar, Grand Marnier, Star fruit or carambola;, Storebought fruit salad, Fresh mint leaves, 
Drain off any excess liquid from storebought fruit salad. In a large bowl  toss together all of the fruit, sugar and the grand marnier. Serve mixed  fruit with mint leaves as garnish.
