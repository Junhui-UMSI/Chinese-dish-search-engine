White Chili with Chicken
Frozen white corn(16oz), Vegetable oil, Oregano, Boneless, skinless chicken, Green chilies(4oz), Garnishes as desired, Tomatillos; husks and stem, Canned white beans, Garlic; minced, Cumin, Chicken broth, Chopped cilantro, Juice of 1 lime, Sweet onion; diced, 
Place beans and their liquid in a large pot over low  heat.  Heat the oil in a skillet and saute the onion  and garlic until golden.  Add the chicken and saute  until lightly browned. Stir the chicken mixture into  the bean pot along with the tomatillos, corn, cumin,  oregano, green chilies and chicken broth.  Simmer  uncovered 20 minutes.  Just before serving, stir in lime juice and cilantro.  Serve, if desired, with garnishes such as chopped red  onion, grated Monterey Jack cheese, sliced jalapeno  peppers and chunks of avocado.
