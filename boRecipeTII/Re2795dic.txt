Peanut Butter Cookies #02
White corn syrup, Peanut butter, Post Toasties cereal, Sugar, 
Boil syrup & sugar about 15 seconds. Remove from heat & stir in peanut  butter. Pour over Post Tosties in a large bowl. Mix. Drop by tablespoons  onto wax paper. Stir mixture only long enough to mix well.    MRS C.C. (KAREN) FLACK    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
