Baked Halibut Limone
Butter, Halibut, Paprika, Lemons, Seasoned bread crumbs, fresh parsley, salt and pepper, Garlic, Lemons, 
From: Wendy Lockman <wlockman@ra1.randomc.com>    Date: Sun, 11 Feb 1996 10:35:49 -0500  Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Sprinkle both sides of fish with salt and  pepper and paprika. Place fish in a buttered shallow baking dish and  sprinkle with garlic, parsley and breadcrumbs. Place lemon slices on fish  and add water almost to top of fish.    Bake uncovered 20-30 minutes until fish is firm and crumbs are golden  brown. Remove lemon slices, dot with butter and place under broiler until  browned. Garnish with thin lemon slices. Serves 6.
