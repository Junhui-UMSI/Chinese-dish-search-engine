Zucchini Bread
Natural applesauce, -3/4 c. chopped nuts, Medium), Baking soda, Egg substitute or egg, Prune baby food), Salt, Flour, Cinnamon, Homemade Wonderslim (or, Vanilla, Grated raw zucchini, (unpeeled packed -- 2 to 3, Baking Powder, Sugar, Replacer to equal 3 eggs, 
Beat egg substitute until light and fluffy.  Add sugar, Wonderslim,  applesauce, and vanilla.  Blend well.  Stir in zucchini.  Add dry  ingredients to creamed mixture.  Fold in chopped nuts.  Pour into two  lightly sprayed loaf pans (9x5").  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hr.    From: BETTE IDE <IDE@uwyo.edu>. Fatfree Digest [Volume 9 Issue 35] July 26,  1994.
