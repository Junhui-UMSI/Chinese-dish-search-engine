Trout Temptation
White toast; cubed, Salt, Dehydrated onion flakes, Tarragon Vinegar, Lemon juice, Trout fillets; (up to 2), Pepper, Chicken bouillon, 
(Friends and Neighbors Cookbook)    Place trout in baking dish. Cover with lemon juice and vinegar. Marinate  for 2 to 3 hours. Mix together bouillon, toast, salt and pepper. Pour over  fish. Sprinkle onion flakes on top of the fish and garnish with lemon  slices. Bake in 400 degree oven for about 20-30 minutes or until fish  flakes.
