Peanutty Peanut Butter Fudge
Undiluted Carnation, Salt, Mini marshmallows, Finely chopped peanuts, (300g) peanut butter chips, Sugar, Squares semi-sweet chocolate, Butter, 
Line 13x9x2 inch baking pan with foil. Sprinkle 1 cup of the peanut on  bottom of pan; set aside. Combine sugar, evaporated milk, butter and salt  in large saucepan over med. heat, stirring occasionally. Bring to full  rolling boil. Cook 4 to 5 min. stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add  marshmallows and peanut butter chips; stir vigorously 1 min, until  marshmallows melt and blend. Pour over peanuts in pan. Sprinkle remaining  peanuts over fudge; press down lightly. Chill until firm (about 2 hours).  Cut into diamond or rectangular shapes. Drizzle with melted chocolate, if  desired. Makes about 2 lbs.
