Arugula Pesto
Arugula leaves, Garlic, Parmesan, olive oil, Pine nuts, 
In blender or food processor place arugula, garlic and cheese and blend until smooth.  Slowly add olive oil and nuts. Blend until desired  consistency.     Spread on a pizza crust, top with cheddar cheese, onions and fresh tomato slices.  Or, simply toss with cooked pasta.     Makes about 1 cup Copyright Ellen Ecker Ogden From: The Cooks Garden catalog - Spring/Summer  1990 - page 20
