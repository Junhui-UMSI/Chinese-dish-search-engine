Zucchini Rice Bake
Chopped parsley, Butter, Green onions, Zucchini, salt and pepper, Eggs, Cheddar cheese, Cooked brown rice, 
*You can use up to 1 1/2 cups cooked brown rice. Combine rice, zucchini,  cheese, onion and parsley in bowl and mix well. Stir in eggs, butter, salt  and pepper. Spoon into lightly greased 9" baking dish; sprinkle with  paprika. Bake at 350 for 1 hour. This recipe is easily doubled. Use at  least 2 lb zucchini and can take up to 3 cups cooked brown rice. Use about  4 or 5 large green onions,a nd 4 cups cheese. You can also use a  combination of Cheddar and Jack cheese if you like. If you double this,  bake in a 9 X 13 casserole.
