Apple Cake in a Jar
Water, Flour, Nutmeg, Pecans, Cinnamon, Shortening, Sugar, Baking Powder, Eggs, Grated; peeled apple, 
Grease 8 wide mouth pint jars. Fill them 1/2 full with mixture of all  ingredients and bake on wire rack of oven for 45 minutes at 325 degrees. As  soon as cake is done, take one out at a time and put on hot lid. Screw band  down after wiping rim. Important: Do not add any other ingredients. Good  for Christmas gifts.
