Tank Walbanger Cake
Vegetable oil, Vodka, White corn syrup, GLAZE, Confectioners sugar, Eggs, (3 to 3-3/4 oz) instant, Vodka, Galliano, Orange juice, Galliano, NORMA WRENN, (2 layer size) orange cake, Orange juice, 
Evenly grease baking ring or spray with vegetable oil. In a large mixing  bowl, combine all ingredients. Beat for 4 minutes on medium speed with  electric mixer. Remove 1-1/2 cups batter; reserve for cupcakes. Pour  remaining cake batter into prepared baking ring. Microwave on 2/3 power  (medium high) for 2 minutes, 1/2 power (medium) for 20 to 30 minutes or  until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Rotate once during  cooking time, if necessary. Batter will run over sides of pan slightly. Let  stand in pan for 10 minutes. Turn out on plate and glaze while warm.    Glaze: Combine ingredients in a small bowl. Stir until smooth and drizzle  over warm cake.    Source: Inetha Anderson; Cook Along With Desk & Derrick Club; Abilene,  Texas; A Second Helping  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Brenda H.
