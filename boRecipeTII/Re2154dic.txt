Artichoke Bean Dip
salt and pepper, navy beans, artichoke hearts, artichoke hearts, garlic, green onion, Paprika, Lemon juice, 
Combine all ingredients EXCEPT the 7 oz of minced artichokes in a food  processor and blend until smooth. Stir in the minced artichoke pieces for  texture and enhanced flavor.
