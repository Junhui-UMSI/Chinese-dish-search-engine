Apple Glazed Pork Chops Ii
pork center loin, Flour, Reynolds Oven, onion soup mix, apple pie filling, canned, Water, 
Preheat oven to 350. Shake flour in oven bag; place in 9x13inch baking  dish. Add soup mixand water to bag; squeeze bagto blend in flour. add pork  chops and pie filling to the bag. turn bag to coat the pork chopswith  sauce. Arrange pork chops in single layerin bag. Close bag with nylon tie;  cut 6 1/2 inch slits in top of bag. Bake 35 minutes or until pork chops are  tender. to serve, stir sauce and spoon over pork chops.
