Tuna Oriental Casserole
milk, Celery -- chopped, Macaroni -- cooked, Pimiento, Soy sauce, Cream of celery soup, Tuna, Chow Mein Noodles, Water Chestnuts -- sliced, 
Combine all ingredients except Chow Mein Noodles in a 2 qt casserole dish.  Top with the noodles.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
