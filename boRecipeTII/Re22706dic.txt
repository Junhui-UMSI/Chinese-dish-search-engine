Apricot Grilling Sauce Base
Vegetable oil, Onions, Salt, Apricot halves, garlic, Pepper, 
To make the grilling sauce base, puree apricot haves with their liquid in a  blender or food processor; reserve. Saute onion and garlic in oil until  translucent. Stir in reserved apricot puree.    Yields 2 1/2 cups.
