Swiss Steak in Tomato Gravy
Drippings from meat, (#10) tomato sauce, Ground beef, TOMATO GRAVY, Tap pepper, Diced onions, Browning sauce, milk, Flour, Bread crumbs, Salt, Salt, Pepper, 
1. combine ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, salt, pepper and milk.    2. shape into 2 1/1 oz. portion patties unsing #10 scoop. or make 2 rows of  meat mixture in a 2 in pan.    3. bake at 325 degrees until brown.    4. place in steam table pan and cover with gravy.    Tomato Gravy:    1. combine drippings and flour. Brown slightly    2. add bottled browning sauce, salt peper and tomato sauce    3. Blend well and pour over patties  1 serving provides 2 oz cooked meat    Posted to EAT-L Digest 19 Sep 96    From:    Ruth L Rivera <riverar@JUNO.COM>    Date:    Thu, 19 Sep 1996 22:23:59 EDT
