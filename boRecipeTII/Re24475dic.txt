Tropical Stuffed Pears
(8oz.) sour cream, Pecans, (8oz.) crushed pineapple,, Flaked coconut, Miniature marshmallows, Lettuce leaves, optional, (29 oz.) pear halves,drained, 
In a bowl, combine the sour cream, pineapple, marshmallows, coconut and  pecans.  Place pears on lettuce if desired; spoon pineapple mixture into  the center of each. Yield 6-7 servings.
