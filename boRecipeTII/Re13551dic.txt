Widows Kisses
Granulated sugar (1 3/4 oz), Baking wafers, Citron, finely diced (1 3/4, Egg whites, Nuts, coarsely ground (4 1/4, 
Over steam, beat egg whites and sugar to firm peaks. Add nuts and citron.  Put baking wafers on cookie sheet, putting a small heap (about a teaspoon?)  of the mixture on top of each wafer. Let dry in oven.    From:  Maria Baumgartner (Karin Brewers mother), Vienna, Austria Posted  by:  Karin Brewer, Cooking Echo, 7/92
