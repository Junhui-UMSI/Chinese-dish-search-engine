Battered Leeks
Dry milk pwd; instant, Pepper, Parsley sprig, Leeks; washed & diced + grn., Tomatoes; cherry, Eggs, Salt, 
Spray nonstick skillet with cooking spray and set over medium heat; add 2  cups leeks, cover skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until leeks are  softened, 3-4 min. In small mixing bowl beat together 2 eggs, 1/3 c milk  powder, half of the salt and pepper.  Pour into skillet, making sure egg  mixture thoroughly coats leeks. Cook until bottom of omelet is golden, 1 to  2 minutes; using pancake turner, carefully turn omelet over. Cook other  side just until set, about 1 minute; transfer to serving platter and keep  warm.  Repeat procedure; garnish omelets with tomatoes and parsley.
