Bacon-Flavored Dip Mix
Instant Beef Bouillon, Instant Bacon Bits, Garlic, Instant minced onion, 
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl; blend well.  Spoon mixture onto a  6-inch square of aluminum foil and fold to make airtight.  Lable as  Bacon-Flavored Dip Mix.  Store in a cool, dry place and use within 6  months.    Makes 1 package (about 3 T) of mix. Bacon Flavored Dip: Combine 1 cup of  sour cream and 1 package of mix. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.    Makes about 1 cup of dip.    VARIATIONS:  Substitute 1 cup yogurt, 1 cup cottage cheese or 1 (8 oz)  package of softened cream cheese for sour cream.  From "Make-A-Mix Cookery" by Karine Eliason, Nevada Harward & Madeline  Westover via The Home BBS. Posted to MM-Recipes Digest #336, 9 Dec 96 by  c.simpson@worldnet.att.net
