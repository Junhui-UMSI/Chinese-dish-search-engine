Black Velvet Beef Roast
Caynenne, Fiesta chili powder, Sysco Imperial beef base, Water, Shots Black Velvet whiskey, olive oil, 
Simmer together and let cool. Injected this mixture into roasts. Coated  roasts in olive oil then sprinkled Hot chili powder and Johnneys seasoning  salt all over. Wraped and refrigerated for 24 hours . Will mop with same as  injection mixture,but will add a little more oil to it. Will smoke these  with apple and mesquite wood. Have a good Q day.
