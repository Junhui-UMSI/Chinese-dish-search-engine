White Chocolate Cheesecake #1
Sour cream, White chocolate; chopped, Vanilla, Super fine sugar, Egg yolks, Cream of tartar, Egg whites, Cream chesse; room temp, 
Date: Mon, 19 Feb 96 07:44:59 PST    From: eboyd@shentel.net    Melt chocolate; set aside. Beat cream cheese until fluffy-beat in egg yolks  1 at a time and mix well. Mix in sour cream, vanilla, chocolate and set  aside. Beat egg whites w/tartar until peaks. Gradually add sugar, beat till  stiff. Stir 1/3 of the whites into chocolate mix (to lighten) then fold in  remaining whites. Spoon into a pan w/a gramham cracker crust and bake in a  water bath till center is set.
