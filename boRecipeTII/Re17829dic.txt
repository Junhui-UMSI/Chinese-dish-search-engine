Almost Sicilian Splash
Jiggers white grape juice, Cubes ice, Jigger orange soda, Lemon slice, garnish, Sparkling water, 
Tall fluted glass. Blend in order given. Serve with lemon slice and a  straw.    NOTES : Server at Olive Garden (Aug 96)    Recipe by: Olive Garden Restaurants Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #615 by  louiseh@juno.com on May 18, 1997
