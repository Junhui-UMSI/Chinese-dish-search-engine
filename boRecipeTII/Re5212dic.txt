Pilgrim Pie
Margarine, Eggs, Vanilla, Sugar, Oats, Baking coconut, Salt, 
Cream together eggs, sugar and margarine and then add rest. mix well. Pour  into your favorite unbaked pie shell. Bake at 450 for 10 min. and then  reduce heat to      350    and bake for 30 min.
