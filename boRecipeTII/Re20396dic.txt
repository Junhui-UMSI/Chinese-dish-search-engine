Veau Braise Au Sani Bon
Mushrooms, sliced, Cubed veal shoulder, Onion, diced, Dry white wine, Chopped parsley, Garlic, olive oil, Prosciutto, diced, Salt, pepper, 
Saute veal, prosciutto, onion and garlic in olive oil until golden. Add  wine, mushrooms, salt and pepper to taste and parsley. Simmer over low heat  45 minutes or until meat is tender. Makes 6 to 8 servings.    Posted to FOODWINE Digest 22 Jan 97 by "Ted D. Conley"  <tedconley@EARTHLINK.NET> on Jan 22, 1997.
