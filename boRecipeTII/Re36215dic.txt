Beef Stroganoff Crepes
Chives, Ground cumin, Marjoram, Onion, Red wine, Garlic, Butter, Salt, Black pepper, Sour cream, Tomato sauce, Rump steak, Crepes, Mushrooms, Beef stock, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Make basic crepes. Saute onion & garlic in butter until the onion is  soft. Slice the mushrooms thinly & and to pan. Cook five minutes. Cut  steak into thin strips & add to pan along with cumin, marjoram,  worcestershire & tomato sauce. Stir frequently & cook until the meat  browns. Add the wine, stock, salt, pepper & cook until meat is  tender. Add sour cream & heat just until warm. Now fill each crepe  with stroganoff mixture. Fold over & put into shallow buttered baking  dish. Bake at 350F in oven for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with chives &  serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
