Sweet n Spicy Onion Glaze
Env. Onion Soup Mix, (1 Jar) Apricot Preserves, (1 C) Salad Dressing *, 
*     Choose one of the following Salad Dressings: Russian or Sweet n        Spicy French.    In small bowl, blend all ingredients.  Use as a glaze for chicken,  spareribs, kabobs, hamburgers or frankfurters.  Brush on during the last  half of cooking.    Makes about 2 1/2 C glaze    From the "Lipton Creative Cookery Cookbook", from Rich, Sysop, New Cook BBS  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/soup.zip
