Quick Fix Bbq Drumsticks
Prepared mustard, Chili powder, cooking bag, Flour, chicken drumsticks, Barbecue sauce, Garlic powder, 
Preheat oven to 350. Shake flour in bag; place in 13 X 9 X 2" baking  pan. Add remaining ingredients except chicken into bag. Squeeze bag  to blend ingredients. Place chicken in bag. Turn bag to coat chicken  with sauce. Arrange chicken in even layer. Close bag with tie; cut 6  half inch slits in top. Bake until tender, 35-40 minutes.
