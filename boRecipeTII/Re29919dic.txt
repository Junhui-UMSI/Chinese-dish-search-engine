Teriyaki Flank Steak Barbecue
Clove garlic, minced fine, Ground ginger, Soy sauce, Honey, Scallions or chives, Cider vinegar, Vegetable oil, 
Mix all the ingredients together. Marinate a flank steak for four hours,  turning once. Grill over a charcoal fire. Slice thinly and serve.    Posted By MCTYREG@baylor.edu (George McTyre) On rec.food.recipes or  rec.food.cooking
