"M&ms"? Brownie Turtle Cookies
Granulated sugar, Baking Powder, Pecan halves, Prepared chocolate frosting, All-purpose flour, Solid vegetable shortening, Unsweetened baking chocolate, Vanilla extract, Eggs, Salt, M&Ms? milk chocolate mini, 
Preheat oven to350F. Lightly grease cookie sheets; set aside. Heat  chocolate and shortening in2-quart sauce pan over low heat, stirring  constantly, until melted; remove from heat. Mix in sugar, vanilla and eggs.  Blend in flour, baking powder and salt. Stir in 2/3 cup "M&Ms"(r) Milk  Chocolate Mini Baking Bits. For each cookie, arrange 3 pecan halves, with  ends almost touching at center, on prepared cookie sheets. Drop dough by  rounded teaspoonfuls onto center of each group of pecans; mound the dough  slightly. Bake 8 to 10 minutes just until set; do not over bake.  Immediately remove cookies to wire racks to cool completely. Top each  cookie with frosting; press remaining "M&Ms"(r) Milk Chocolate Mini Baking  Bits into frosting to create turtle shell..    Notes: These chewy little turtles are as fun to eat as they are to make!
