Velvet Ice Cream
Heavy cream, whipped, Egg white, stiffly beaten, Egg yolk, beaten, Salt, Cornstarch, milk, Brown sugar, Maple flavoring, 
Heat the milk to scalding. Mix the sugar with the cornstarch and egg yolks.  Gradually add the scalded milk and cook until the mixture begins to  thicken, stirring constantly. Remove from stove, add salt and flavoring,  and set aside to cool. Fold egg whites and whipped cream into the cooled  custard and freeze. Makes 2 quarts.
