Venison Liver and Onions
Venison liver, Pepper, Bacon, Onion, Flour, Salt, 
Salt and pepper the liver and dredge with flour. Fry bacon in skillet until  brown; remove from skillet and set aside. Slice onion, separate into rings  and saute in drippings; remove from pan and set aside. Brown liver on both  sides. Return onions and crumbled bacon to skillet. Pour in a little water  and simmer for 5 minutes.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
