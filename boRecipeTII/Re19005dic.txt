Vegetable Bean and Pasta Soup
White onion; chopped, Penne or other tubular pasta, Zuchinni; diced, Canned cannellini beans, trimmed, chopped, Stewed tomatoes, Dry vermouth, Yellow crookneck squash, rinsed, drained, Green Onions, Canned vegetable broth, Fennel bulb, (canned, Italianstyle), diced, Garlic, 
Bring broth and vermouth to boil in heavy large pot or Dutch oven over  medium heat.  Add onion, fennel and garlic. Cover and cook until just  tender, about 10 minutes. Add tomatoes, pasta, zucchini and squash; cook  until pasta is almost tender, about 15 minutes. Add beans and green onions.  Cook until pasta and vegetables are tender and soup is thick, about 10  minutes.    * Source: David Badal, Houston TX * Published in: Bon Appetit - June 1993 *  Typed for you by Karen Mintzias
