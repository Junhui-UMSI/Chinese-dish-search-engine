Two-Minute Smoked Salmon Spread
Plain yogurt, Fresh Lemon Juice, Smoked salmon chopped,, Grated lemon zest, Green onion (white & green, White pepper, Sweet or hot paprika, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, 
In bowl of food processor fitted with metal blade, combine half of salmon  with remaining ingredients except green onion. Process to smooth puree.    Stir in remaining salmon and the white part of the green onion.    Transfer to serving dish, cover with plastic wrap and chill 30 minutes.  Sprinkle with green part of onion before serving. Makes 1 1/2 cups.    Recipe by: Diane Rozas Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #635 by  Aquasea221@aol.com on Jun 4, 1997
