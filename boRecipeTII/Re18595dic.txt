American Fried Yams
Louisiana yams, Butter, Sugar, Shortening, 
Parboil yams in skins or use left-overs. Peel; slice in rounds about  1/8-inch thick. Melt fat in large skillet. Brown slices quickly on both  sides. Turn off fire; add butter; sprinkle with sugar. Serve with bacon  curls and spiced crabapples.    NOTES : This booklet was prepared by the Louisiana Sweet Potato Commission.
