Thai Squid Salad
Salad greens, Mint, Fish sauce, Squid, Garlic, Ginger root, Juice of 1 lime, Scallion, Lemon grass, Red chili, Cilantro, Chiles, 
Clean squid & rinse throughly. Slit open body pouch & score inside surface  in criss cross pattern. Cut into 2x1/2" pieces. Bring saucepan of water to  boil. Add squid & simmer 1min, til opaque. Drain. Mix w/lime juice, fish  sauce, chili, garlic, gingerroot, lemon grass & scallion. Cover & marinate  1Hr or longer if refrigerated.   Just before serving, stir in cilantro & mint. Arrange greens on plate, top  w/squid, garnish w/chili rings & serve. Source: Book of First Courses,  pp102
