Pecan Balls #1
Sugar, Flour; sifted, Pure vanilla, Finely chopped pecans, Sticks butter; softened, 
Cream butter well & add sugar slowly while beating constantly until light &  fluffy. Add vanilla & flour. Mix well. Add pecans. Shape into 1-inch balls  & place on greased cookie sheet. Leave about 1-1/2 inches between cookies.  Bake at 300 for 25-30 minutes or until done.    BECKY WEAVER    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
