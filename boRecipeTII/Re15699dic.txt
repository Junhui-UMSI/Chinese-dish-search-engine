A Texas Breakfast Casserole
Eggs, Cream of onion soup, Crescent rolls, Sausage, Fresh mushrooms, Monterey jack cheese, 
Line a 13x9 inch Pyrex dish with rolls, sealing perforations. Cover with  sausage, mushrooms and half the cheese. Mix eggs with soup and pour over  casserole.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top. Chill overnight. Bake at 350  for 1 hour.
