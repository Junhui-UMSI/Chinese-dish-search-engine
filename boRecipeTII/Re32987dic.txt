Basic Bread Stuffing
Water or milk; (optional), Celery, Butter, Day old bread crumbs, Thyme or sage, Onions; chopped, Salt, Pepper, 
Cook celery and onions in melted butter for about 10 minutes or until  tender. Add the celery and onions and seasonings to the bread crumbs and  mix thoroughly. If dressing seems very dry, add 2 tablespoons water, milk  or fish stock to moisten.
