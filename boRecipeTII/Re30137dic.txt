Banana & Walnut Muffins
orange zest, Baking Powder, milk, Nutmeg, Butter, Salt, Egg, Bananas, Sugar, Vegetable oil, Walnuts, Lemon Rind, Flour, 
Melt butter, mash bananas. Sift flour, b/pdr, nutmeg, sugar & salt into a  bowl. Add melted butter, milk, egg, oil, nuts & rinds to bananas. Make a  well in centre of dry ingredients & gently stir in banana mixture. Spoon  into prepared pans & bake at 400F for 15-20 mins. Makes 18.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
