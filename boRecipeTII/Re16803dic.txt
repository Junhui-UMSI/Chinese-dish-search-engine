All Day Barbecue Beef
Barbecue sauce, Chuck roast, Chili sauce, Onion; sliced, 
Placed sliced onions in bottom of roasting pan.  Place roast on top of  onions.  Mix sauces together and spread over roast.  Bake, covered, all day  (at least 5 hours) at 225 degrees or in a crockpot for six hours, or until  tender. One half hour before serving, shred meat, remove bones and put back  in sauce. Continue cooking for 1/2 hour. Serve on buns. Venison can also be  used.    From Mom    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by KrisE56749@aol.com on Sep 2, 1997
