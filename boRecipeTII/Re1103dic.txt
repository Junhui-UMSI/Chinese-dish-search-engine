Cottage Cheese Salad
Cottage cheese, Chopped radishes, Sliced green onion or chives, Mayonnaise (optional), Salt, Pepper, Paprika sprinkle, Lettuce cups, 
Arrange lettuce cups on one large or several small plates. A bowl may be  used.    Combine cottage cheese, radishes, onions, mayonnaise, salt and pepper.  Spoon into lettuce cups or bowl.    Sprinkle with paprika.  Serve. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Companys Coming Salads    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Cindy Hartlin" <cjhartlin@email.msn.com>  on Sep 7, 1998
