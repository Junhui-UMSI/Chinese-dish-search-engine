Baja Chicken-Pasta Salad
Green onions (with tops),, Yoplait Original plain, Mayonnaise or salad, Cut-up cooked chicken, (6 ounces) diced dried, Uncooked Orzo or ring, Cubed jicama, Ground red chilies, Salt, 
Orzo is tiny pasta that resembles the shape of rice.    Cook Orzo as directed on package except stir fruit into boiling water with  Orzo. Rinse Orzo and fruit with cold water; drain.    Mix chicken, Orzo, fruit, jicama and onions. Mix remaining ingredients;  toss with chicken mixture. Cover and refrigerate about 2 hours or until  chilled.          6    servings.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
