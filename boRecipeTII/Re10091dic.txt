Queen Elizabeth Cake (Mrss Lyons)
Boiling water, Nuts, Butter, Vanilla, Beaten egg, Chopped dates, Salt, Baking soda, Sugar, Flour, 
Pour the water over the dates and soda. Let sit while mixing the other  ingredients then add the date mixture. Pour into prepared 8 " pan and bake  in 350 oven 35 min. ICING: 5 tblsp. brown sugar, 5 tblsp. cream, 2 tblsp.  butter. Put in saucepan and bring to boil and boil 3 min, remove from heat  and cool. I thicken this with icing sugar to spread.
