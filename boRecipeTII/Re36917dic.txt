Strawberry Sparkle Punch
Water, Strawberries, Cranberry Juice Coctail, Ginger Ale, Strawberry Jell-O, Cold water, Lemonade, 
Puree strawberries in blender; place puree in large punch bowl. Strain, if  desired, to remove seeds. Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; stir in  lemonade concentrate. Add mixture to punch bowl. Add cranberry cocktail and  cold water.  Slowly add ginger ale.    Recipe By     : Herald Journal, Logan, UT    From:                                 Date:
