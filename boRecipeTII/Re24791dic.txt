Aunt Jeans Golden Pound Cake
Vanilla, Sugar, Eggs, Salt, Baking Powder, Butter, Cream Cheese, All-Purpose Flour, 
Beat butter, cheese, and vanilla until light and fluffy.    Gradually add sugar, beating until smooth.    Add eggs, one at a time, beating thoroughly after each addition.    Sift flour with baking powder and salt; add to creamed mixture in thirds,  beating until smooth after each addition.    Turn into a greased and lightly floured 9 x 5 x 3" loaf pan.  Bake at 325  degrees for 1 hour and 10 minutes, or until done.    Recipe By     : Jean Aniolowski    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
