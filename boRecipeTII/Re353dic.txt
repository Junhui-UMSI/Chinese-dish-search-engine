Lemon Bread
All purpose flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Sugar, Butter, Lemon extract, Eggs, milk, Pecans, Grated rind of a lemon, 
Preheat oven to 350? F. Beat together sugar, butter and lemon extract for  one minute. Beat in eggs (one at a time). Add flour, baking powder and  salt. Add milk while beating mixture. Fold in lemon rind and nuts. Pour  into greased bread pan. Bake in oven at 350? F. for 60 minutes. Lemon Glaze  Ingredients 1/3 cup sugar 1/3 cup lemon juice, fresh squeezed Very simple.  Mix together. Pour on bread or anything when hot. Or warm in sauce pan. Let  cool a little and drizzle with lemon glaze while bread is still warm.
