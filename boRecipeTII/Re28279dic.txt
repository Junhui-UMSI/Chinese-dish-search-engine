Baked Fruited Popcorn
Cooked Popcorn, Pecans pieces, Baking soda, Vanilla, Candied red cherries; cut up, Brown Sugar, Butter or margerine, Light corn syrup, 
Remove all unpopped kernals from popcorn. In a 17x12x12 " baking pan,  combine popcorn, pecans and cherries. In a 1 quart saucepan combine brown  sugar, butter and corn syrup. Cook and stir over medium heat until butter  melts and mixture comes to a boil. Cook over low heat 5 minutes more.  Remove from heat. Stir in baking soda and vanilla. Pour mixture over  popcorn; gently stir to ccoat popcorn mixture. Bake in 300~ oven for 15  minutes;stir. Bake 5-10 minutes more.
