Vegetable Meatloaf
Milk Or Water, Lean ground beef, Eggs, Frozen Onion; Thawed, Uncooked Oats, (1.5 Oz) Meatloaf Seasoning, (10 Oz) Frozen Chopped, 
Preheat oven to 375. Lightly grease loaf pan. Put all ingredients in large  bowl. Mix with hands. Press mixture in pan. Bake 1 hour covered with foil.  Let stand 10-15 minutes. Drain and serve.
