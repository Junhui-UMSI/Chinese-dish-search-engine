Apple Poppy Seed Muffins
All purpose flour, Poppy seeds, Ground cinnamon (I like, Vanilla extract, Baking Powder, Applesauce, Vegetable oil, Salt, Brown Sugar, Whole wheat flour, Eggs (or egg substitute, 
Combine applesauce, eggs, oil, and vanilla in small bowl; mix well. In a  seperate bowl mix flours, brown sugar, baking powder, salt, cinnamon and  poppy seeds. Add wet ingredients to dry ones; stirring until just  moistened. Spoon batter into greased muffin tins, filling 2/3 full. Bake at  400 F for 12-15 minutes.
