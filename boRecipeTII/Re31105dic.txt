Banana Sour Cream Waffles
Baking soda, Flour, milk, Sugar, Butter, Baking Powder, Banana, Salt, Sour cream, Egg, 
Sift together dry ingredients.  Set aside.  Mix together beaten egg yolk,  sour cream, milk, butter and banana. Add to flour mixture, mixing well.  Fold in whipped egg white, and pour into oiled waffle iron or oiled pan.  Cook each side until brown.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #131 by Tonya <imbri@earthlink.net> on May  11, 1997
