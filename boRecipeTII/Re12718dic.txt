Yorkshire Pudding #1
Salt, milk, Roast beef drippings, Flour, Eggs, 
Mix flour and salt; gradually stir in mixture of eggs and milk. Beat until  smooth. Pour into hot, shallow pan with drippings. Bake at 400 for 25-30  minutes. Cut in squares and serve immediately.
