Tortellini With Smoked Chedder Cheese and C
Broccoli florets; blanched, Red bell pepper; diced, Heavy cream, Tortellini; cooked al dente,, Vegetable oil, White wine, Shallot; chopped fine, Chipotle chiles in adobo, Smoked Chedder cheese; diced, Salt and white pepper; to, Cloves garlic; chopped fine, 
Tortellini with Smoked Chedder Cheese and Chipotle Pepper Cream Heat the  oil in a large saucepan until hot.  Add garlic, shallots, saute for 2 min.  Add wine, simmer until almost completely reduced, about 2 min.  Add cream,  stir and simmer 10-15 minutes until reduced by 1/2.  Add chiles and cheese  and stir until cheese melts.  Add tortellini, broccoli, red pepper and salt  and pepper; mix well and serve.  Serves 6-8.
