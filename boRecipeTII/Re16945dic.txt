Almond Bars
Eggs, well, beaten, Vanilla, Salt, Baking Powder, Sugar, Flour, Shortening, Powdered sugar, Grated rind 1 lemon, Honey, Finely chopped blanched, 
Cream shortening and sugar.  Add honey, eggs, and lemon rind. Mix  thoroughly.  Sift flour, measure, and sift three times with baking powder  and salt.  Add chopped nuts and flavoring. Mix thoroughly. Pour into  shallow, well-oiled pan.  Bake in moderate oven (375 F) 12-15 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Cool.  Turn onto board well dusted with powdered sugar.  Cut in oblong bars. Roll in powdered sugar. 16 servings. Mrs. William  Stevens, Columbus, IN.
