Strawberry Cheese Spread
Pinch salt, Lemon juice, Crushed strawberries, Granulated sugar, Cream cheese - softened, 
Whip cheese until light.  Combine sugar, salt and strawberries. Let stand  for 10 minutes; drain well. Blend strawberries and lemon juice with cheese.  Makes about 1 cup * Great snack with crackers or plain bread.
