Apple Puffed Pancake
Salt, milk, Flour, Eggs, Sugar, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Butter or Margarine, Apples, Brown sugar, 
PREHEAT OVEN 425. MIX EGGS, MILK FLOUR , SUGAR, VANILLA, SALT AND CINNAMON  IN BLENDER UNTIL WELL BLENDED. MELT BUTTER IN 13X9X2 INCH PAN IN OVEN. PUT  APPLE SLICES IN PAN . RETURN TO OVEN. WHEN BUTTER SIZZLES, DO NOT LET  BROWN, REMOVE DISH FROM OVEN. POUR BATTER OVER APPLES. SPRINKLE WITH BROWN  SUGAR. BAKE ON MIDDLE RACK OF OVEN FOR 20 MINUTES OR UNTIL PUFFED AND  BROWNED.    NOTES : Bobbies Notes: Recipe can be cut in half using 8 or 9 inch square  baking dish. If using 8 inch dish, may need to cook about 35 minutes to  cook through and brown.
