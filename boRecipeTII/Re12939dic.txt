Zesty Coleslaw Dressing
Lemon juice, Celery seeds, Cider vinegar, Prepared horseradish, Pepper, Nonfat sour cream, Sugar, olive oil, Plain low-fat yogurt, Salt, Dijon mustard, 
Combine all ingredients in a bowl; stir with a wire whisk until blended.  Cover and chill. Yield: 1 cup (serving size: 1 tablespoon).    Per serving: 250 Calories; 17g Fat (60% calories from fat); 8g Protein; 17g  Carbohydrate; 12mg Cholesterol; 819mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : Serve over shredded cabbage.
