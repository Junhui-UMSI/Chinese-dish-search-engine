Barbecued Confetti Vegetables
Cherry tomatoes, Corn, Red bell pepper, Green pepper, Onion, Fresh Basil, Lemon Rind, Salt & pepper, Butter, unsalted, Butter, unsalted, 
Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Total Time: 40 minutes  1. Combine all ingredients except butter in a large bowl; toss gently to mix  well.    2. Divide vegetable mixture in half. Place each half in center of a 12"x 12" piece of heavy-duty aluminum foil. Dot vegetables with butter. Bring  corners of foil together to form a pyramid; twist to seal.   3. Grill foil  packets over medium hot coals for 15 to 20 minutes, or until vegetables are  tender.  Serve immediately.
