Walter Jettons Texas Cole Slaw
Dill pickles; chopped, Mayonnaise, Salt, Fresh cabbage, Sugar, 
Shred up the cabbage and add all the other ingredients. Mix well and let  stand a while before serving so that the flavors blend.    Walter Jetton was the caterer to the LBJ ranch.
