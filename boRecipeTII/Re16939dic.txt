Almond Bark Candy
Peanut butter, Almond bark, Rice krispies, marshmallows, Dry roasted peanuts, 
Melt almond bark in MICROWAVE. Stir in peanut butter, then rest of  ingredients. Spoon on wax paper and let cool.    Recipe by: Joanne    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by "Iris E. Dunaway" <hister@JUNO.COM> on Sep 16,  1997
