Pear Cheese Salad
Dry mustard, DRESSING, White wine vinegar, Pears; thinly sliced, Lemon juice, Shredded Swiss or Cheddar, Bibb lettuce, Vegetable oil, Pecans, Salt, SALAD, 
In small jar with tight-fitting lid, combine all dressing ingredients;  shake well.     In large bowl, gently toss pears with lemon juice. Add cheese and pecans.  Pour dressing over salad; stir gently. Serve in lettuce-lined bowl.
