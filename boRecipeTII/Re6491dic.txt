Poached Chicken Plus Stock
Carrot, cut in 1" pieces, Sprigs parsley, Water, Whole black peppercorns, Onion, quartered, Tomato, quartered, Chicken, whole (3 lb.), 
1. Combine all ingredients in the pressure cooker. Cover and bring up  to low pressure. Reduce heat to stabilize pressure and cook 12  minutes. (If your cooker has a fixed pressure regulating weight, cook  at high pressure for 9 minutes.) Turn off heat. Leave the pressure  cooker locked for 35 to 40 minutes.    2. Strain the stock. When the chicken is cool enough to handle,  remove meat from the bones. Discard skin; return bones and liquid to  the pan.    3. Cover and bring up to high pressure. reduce heat to stabilize  pressure and cook 10 minutes. Release pressure. Strain stock.  Refrigerate until the fat solidifies. Skim off fat before using.
