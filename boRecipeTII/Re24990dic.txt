Tomato Potato Chicken
Pepper, Salt, Meat seasoning, Chicken; cut up, Water, Potatoes; peeled and sliced, Garlic powder, (16 oz) tomato sauce, 
Put chicken pieces in a large pot. Place potatoes on top of chicken.  Combine all remaining ingredients and pour on top of chicken and potatoes.  Cook over a medium flame for an hour. (Or bake in a large baking pan at 350  degrees for an hour and 15 minutes.)    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by dsabraham@juno.com (Deena Abraham) on Apr  16, 1998
