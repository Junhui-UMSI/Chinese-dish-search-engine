Popeyes Dirty Rice
Spicy breakfast sausage, clear chicken broth, Dry onion, Long-grain rice, 
Brown sausage in skillet untiil pink color disappears, crumbling with fork.  Stir in broth, rice and minced onion. Simmer gently, covered, 18 to 20  minutes or until rice is tender and most of broth is absorbed.    Source: Gloria Pitzers Make Alike Recipes.
