Perfect Peppermint Patties
Shortening, semisweet chocolate chips, Vanilla extract, Evaporated milk, peppermint extract, Butter, 
In a bowl, combine first four ingredients. Add milk and mix well.  Roll into 1-in. balls and place on a waxed paper-lined cookie sheet.  Chill for 20 minutes. Flatten with a glass to 1/4 in.; chill for 30  minutes. In a double boiler or microwave-safe bowl, melt chocolate  chips and shortening. Dip patties; place on waxed paper to harden and  freeze for a short while.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
