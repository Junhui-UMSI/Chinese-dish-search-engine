Picnic Pasta Salad
Mayo or lite mayo, Crumbled feta cheese, Cooked chilled salad pasta;, Sliced pepperoni; optional, 
In large bowl toss pasta with mayo until coated lightly. Add entire jar  Sweet & Hot Vegetable Salad and pepperoni. Toss to mix. Refrigerate until  chilled through. Add crumbled feta and toss just before serving.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 625 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Jan 28, 1998
