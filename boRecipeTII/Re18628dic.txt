Americana Peanut Cheesecake
Vanilla extract, (3 ounces each) cream cheese, Creamy peanut butter, (14 ozs) sweetened condensed, Lemon juice, Chopped roasted peanuts; div, Graham cracker crumbs, Chopped roasted peanuts; div, Butter or margarine, melted, (4 ozs) frozen whipped toppi, 
In small bowl, combine 3/4 cup chopped peanuts, graham  cracker crumbs and melted butter; mix well. Press  crumb mixture into bototom and 1" up sides of 9-inch  springform pan. Refrigerate crust 20 minutes or until  set. In large bowl with electric mixer at medium  speed, beat softened cream cheese and peanut butter  until fluffy. Gradually beat in condensed milk and mix  well. Add lemon juice and vanilla; blend well. Fold in  whipped topping. Pour mixture into prepared crust;  garnish top with remaining 2 tablespoons chopped  peanuts. Refrigerate 2 to 3 hours, or until well  chilled.
