Wild Rice
SALAD OIL; 1 GAL, SALAD OIL; 1 GAL, SOUP GRAVY BASE CHICKEN, PEPPER SWT GRN FRESH, WATER; COLD, Onions, 
1.  COOK RICE WITH SOUP AND GRAVY BASE, CHICKEN.  SEE RECIPE CARD A-12.    2.  COVER TIGHTLY; SIMMER 20 TO 25 MINUTES.  DO NOT STIR.  REMOVE FROM  HEAT.  SAUTE CHOPPED SWEET PEPPERS, AND CHOPPED DRY ONIONS IN SALAD OIL OR MELTED  SHORTENING.  ADD SAUTEED VEGETABLES TO COOKED RICE.  MIX WELL.  PLACE EQUAL  AMOUNTS INTO EACH SERVING PAN.  (18 BY 24").  COVER; BAKE AT 350F. FOR 15  MINUTES.  TOSS LIGHTLY BEFORE SERVING.  LIGHTLY BEFORE SERVING.
