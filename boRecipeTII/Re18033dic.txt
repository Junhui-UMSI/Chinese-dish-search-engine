Vegetarian Unstuffed Cabbage Rolls
chopped tomatoes, cardamom, Cooked rice, Raisins, Ground cloves, corn, Allspice, Whole wheat couscous, Brown sugar, Fresh Lemon Juice, tomato sauce, Cabbage, 
Slice cabbage diagonally. Pour hot water over it to make it limp. Drain.  Mix rice, couscous, raisins and corn and set aside. Mix tomatoes with other  ingredients and spread in bottom of a large ceramic casserole dish lightly  coated with non-stick spray.    Cover with layers of the cabbage and the rice mixture. Top with the rest of  the tomato mixture and cover with foil. Bake at 325 degrees F for 30-45  minutes, until bubbly. Serve hot.
