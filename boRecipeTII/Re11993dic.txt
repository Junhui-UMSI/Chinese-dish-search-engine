Red Basil Vinegar
Fresh sweet basil leaves, Red wine vinegar, 
Strip basil leaves from stalks and pack into a wide mouth quart jar. Heat  the vinegar to the boiling point, then pour it into the jar. Cover and let  stand in a cool place, out of the sun, for 10 days to 2 weeks. Strain into  bottles. Drop one or two fresh basil stalks into each bottle (for  decoration), then stop with cork.
