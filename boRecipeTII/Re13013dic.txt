Winter Party Mix
Seasoned salt, Corn Chex cereal, Bran Chex cereal, Worcestershire Sauce, Salted mixed nuts, Rice Chex cereal, Butter or margarine, Wheat Chex cereal, 
Workbasket Recipes: Todays recipes are a sort of "mixed Bag". They are  from the October 1979 issue.    Preheat oven to 250. Heat butter in 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan in oven  until melted. Remove. Stir in Worcestershire and seasoned salt. Add Chex  and nuts. Mix until all pieces are coated. Heat in oven 1 hour. Stir every  15 minutes. Spread on absorent paper to cool. Makes about 9 cups.
