Bean and Garlic Dip
Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing, Pepper; Dash of, Pinto Beans; *, Clove Garlic; Finely Chopped, Red Chiles; Ground, Salt, 
*  Pinto beans can be home cooked or canned.  ~-------------------------------------------------------------------------  Mix all ingredients.  Cover and refrigerate 1 hour.
