Thengai Thirattipaal (Coconut Cake)
Cardamon pods; powdered, Ghee, Coconuts; grated, Jaggery; powdered, 
Grind grated coconut to a very fine paste, adding a little water if needed.  Take jaggery, ground coconut and ghee in a heavy pan and cook on a medium  flame for about 15 minutes, stirring continuously. Reduce to low flame and  stir once in a while. Cook till mixture becomes thick and starts leaving  the sides of the pan. Add cardamon powder, mix well and serve.
