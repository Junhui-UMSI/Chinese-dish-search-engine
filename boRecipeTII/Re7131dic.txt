Poppyseed Cake
Sugar, Sour cream, * D.H. BUTTER CAKE MIX, Vegetable oil, Eggs, POPPYSEED, 
PREHEAT OVEN 350 DEGREES. IN MIXING BOWL PUT IN DUNCAN HINES BUTTER CAKE  MIX, SUGAR, OIL, & EGGS (ADD ONE AT A TIME BEATING BY HAND),FOLD IN SOUR  CREAM, & REST OF INGREDIENTS. GREASE & SPRINKLE BUNDT PAN WITH SUGAR. BAKE       55    MINUTES.
