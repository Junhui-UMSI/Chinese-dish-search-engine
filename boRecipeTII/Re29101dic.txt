Baked Pumpkin Puree
Salt, Pumpkin, 
Preheat oven to 375. Halve pumpkin crosswise and scoop out seeds and  strings. Place pumpkin halves in a large baking dish, shallow side down.  Bake, uncovered, for two hours, or until fork tender. Remove from oven and  cool. Scrape pulp from shells and puree in a food processor. Add salt. Use  immediately in any recipes calling for pumpkin puree or freeze for future  use.
