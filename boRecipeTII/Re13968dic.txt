Winning Sugar Cookies
Salt, Sugar, Flour; sifted, Vanilla extract, Cooking oil, Butter or margarine, Eggs, Baking soda, Cream of tartar, 
Cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a  time, beating well after each addition. Blend in oil and vanilla. Sift  together flour, baking soda, cream of tartar and salt. Gradually add dry  ingredients to creamed mixture; mix well. Chill dough in refrigerator 8  hours or overnight. Shape dough in balls the size of a walnut. Place balls  about 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets. Bake in 350 degree oven 10  to 12 minutes or until done. Remove from baking sheets; cool on racks.    NOTES : Rich buttery sugar cookies that melt in your mouth.
