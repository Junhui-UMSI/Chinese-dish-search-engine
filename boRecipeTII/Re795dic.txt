Hawaiian Chicken
Prepared mustard, Molasses, Vinegar, Soy sauce, crushed pineapple, ginger, Brown sugar, Chicken breast halves, 
Combine all ingredients, except chicken, in saucepan and cook over low heat  for about 10 minutes. place chicken in 12x7 baking dish and pour mixture  over it. Cover with foil. Bake for 1 hour at 350 F. Remove foil and bake  for 15 minutes.
