Barbecued Spareribs
Ketchup, Paprika, Dry mustard, Water, Spareribs, Brown sugar, Onions; sliced, Worcestershire Sauce, Vinegar, Salt, 
1.  Cut ribs into serving pieces and brown in large baking pan on stove.    2.  Combine all remaining ingredients, and pour over ribs.  Cover and bake  at 350F for 1 hour and 45 mins, spooning sauce over ribs occasionally. Bake  uncovered 15 mins extra.
