Baked Custard
Sugar, Sprinkles of Nutmeg, Vanilla, Eggs; slightly beaten, milk, 
Mix all ingredients together, pour into 1qt casserole or custard dish.  Sprinkle with Nutmeg. Set baking dish into pan of water. Bake at 425 for  45 minutes. Let cool (or eat warm) Refidgerate.    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #458 by R & B Stambek  <rstambec@centuryinter.net> on Dec 17, 1997
