Lemon Chicken
SAUCE, Conrnflour, Lemon, Juice & rind of, Stem ginger; (chopped, Clear honey, Sesame oil, Light soy sauce, Chicken Stock, salt and pepper, CHICKEN, Chicken breasts, about, Cornflour, Lemon, Grated rind of, Vegetable oil, Black pepper, GARNISH, Spring onions, Lemon peel; (cut into thin, 
In a saucepan, blend cornflour, lemon juice & rind to make a smooth paste.  Stir in honey, ginger,    oil and soy sauce. Pour on boiling stock and bring to the boil again,  stirring until thickened. Season    to taste & simmer for 10 minutes. Cut chicken breasts into 1 inch strips.  place in a bowl and    sprinkle with cornflour, lemon rind and black pepper. Heat oil in wok or  frying pan and add    chicken pieces. Stir fry for about 10 minutes over a high heat until  chicken is golden brown.    Transfer chicken onto warm plate and spoon over lemon sauce. Garnish with  spring onions and    lemon peel.    Posted to recipelu-digest by LSHW <shusky@erols.com> on Marcay,, h 17, 199
