Whiskey a Go Go Sauce
Cloves garlic -- crushed, Soy sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, Molasses, Lemon juice, Parsley -- dried, Vinegar, Ketchup, Tabasco sauce, Dry mustard, Burbon -- to taste, 
In a pot mix dry mustard and a little of the ketchup to form a paste.  Slowly add the rest of the ketchup, stirring constanly. Add everything  else. bring to boil over medium heat then simmer 10 min.
