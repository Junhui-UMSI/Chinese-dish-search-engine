Watermelon Fire and Ice Salsa
watermelon, cilantro, Jalapeno peppers, Green bell peppers, green onion, Garlic salt, Lime juice, 
* Use up to 2 TBS. chopped jalapenos, depending on taste.     Combine all ingredients.  Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. Serve on  sliced oranges or cheese-filled manicotti. Or, top 1 cup sour cream  with 1 cup salsa and serve with chips. PER SERVING: 1/2 cup =  calories ~ 31, protein - 1 g., fat - .4 g., carbohydrates - 7 g.,  cholesterol - 0, sodium - 156 mg. FROM:  Light & Easy Meals from  Family Circle Magazine issue August 1992. NOTE:  lower sodium figure  by using garlic powder in place of garlic salt and adding lite salt  to taste.
