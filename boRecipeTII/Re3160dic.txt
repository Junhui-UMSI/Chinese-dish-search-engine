Peanutty Crisscrosses
Egg, Vanilla, Margarine or butter, Brown sugar, firmly packed, Quaker Oats, uncooked, Peanut butter, Granulated sugar, Baking soda, Water, All-purpose flour, softened, (quick or oldfashioned), 
Beat together margarine; peanut butter and sugar until creamy.  Add water,  egg and vanilla; beat well.  Add combined oats, flour and baking soda; mix  well.  Cover; chill about 1 hour.    Heat oven to 350 F.  Shape dough into 1-inch balls.  Place on ungreased  cookie sheet; flatten with tines of fork dipped in granulated sugar to  form crisscross pattern.  Bake 9 to 10 minutes or until edges are golden  brown.  Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet; remove to wire rack.  Cool  completely.  Store tightly covered.
