Quick Creamy Alfredo Sauce
Cheese, Parmesan fat-free, Pepper, Butter Buds; liquid, Flour, Garlic, Evaporated milk, skim, Chicken broth, fat-free, Salt, lite, 
In a small saucepan over low heat blend flour, Butter Buds, milk and  chicken broth.  Add garlic, salt and pepper. Continue to cook over  low heat until the sauce thickens. Stir in Parmesan cheese and serve  immediately. Serve with cooked fettuccine (10-12 ounces, cooked).
