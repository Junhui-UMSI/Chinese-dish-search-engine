Swordfish Cote-D`azur
olive oil, Lemon, Saffron, Tomatoes, peeled and, Salt, Peppercorns, Bay leaf, Dry white wine, Swordfish steaks (4-6, Thyme, Fennel seed, Clove garlic, crushed, 
1. Oil a large skillet and place over medium heat. Place fish in the  skillet. Add tomatoes, fennel, thyme, bay leaf, garlic, peppercorns, salt,  and saffron. Stir in the wine and bring to a boil.    2. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes, or until fish is well cooked but still  firm.    3. Cool fish in cooking liquid. Serve fish cold with 4-6 tablespoons of  cooking liquid. Garnish with thin slices of lemon and serve.    *This recipe also works well with other firm-meated fish such as tuna and  salmon.
