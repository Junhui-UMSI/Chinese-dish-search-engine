Potato Chowder
Chicken bouillon, low-sodium vegetarian, Flour, all-purpose, Salt, Pepper, Carrot, Paprika, Onion, Potatoes, Dried parsley flakes, Skim milk, 
Recipe by: The Mormon Diet Cookbook Preparation Time: 1:00 In lg Dutch oven  or kettle, combine potatoes, onion, carrot, salt, pepper, parsley flakes  and bouillon.    Add enough water to just cover veggies; cook until veggies are tender,  about 15 - 20 min.    Do not drain.    Measure 1 1/2 C milk and add flour to milk, stirring with wire whisk.    Stir until blended.    Simmer for 15 min on low heat and thicken.    Garnish with paprika.
