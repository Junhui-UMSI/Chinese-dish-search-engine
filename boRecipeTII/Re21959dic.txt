Applesauce Cake (Mix)
CLOVES GROUND, Baking soda, CINNAMON GROUND 1 LB CN, RAISINS #10, SALAD OIL; 1 GAL, Water, APPLE SAUCE #10, CAKE MIX YELLOW #10, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN (2 PANS)     TEMPERATURE:  375 F. OVEN    1.  COMBINE CAKE MIX, CINNAMON, CLOVES, BAKING SODA, SALAD OIL. AND  WATER IN MIXING BOWL.  BEAT AT MEDIUM SPEED 3 MINUTES; SCRAPE DOWN BOWL.    2.  ADD APPLESAUCE; BEAT AT LOW SPEED 3 MINUTES.  SCRAPE DOWN BOWL ONCE  DURING BEATING.    3.  FOLD RAISINS INTO BATTER.    4.  POUR 1 GAL (ABOUT 7 LB 3 OZ) BATTER INTO EACH GREASED AND FLOURED PAN.    5.  BAKE 35 TO 40 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE.    6.  COOL; FROST IF DESIRED. CUT 6 BY 9.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 2, INSTANT APPLESAUCE MAY BE USED. MIX 11 OZ (1/2-NO.  2 1/2 CN) CANNED INSTANT APPLESAUCE WITH 1 1/2 QT COLD WATER; ALLOW TO  STAND 10 MINUTES BEFORE USING.    NOTE:  2.  OTHER PAN SIZES MAY BE USED. SEE RECIPE CARD G-G-4.    Recipe Number: G00300    SERVING SIZE: 1 PIECE    From the <Army Master Recipe Index File> (actually used today!).
