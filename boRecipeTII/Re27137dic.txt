Tiger Butter (Microwave Candy)
Chocolate chips, Peanut butter, Almond bark, 
Melt half of bark. Mix with peanut butter and spread on cookie sheet. Melt  remaining bark and chips. Mix until smooth and spread over peanut butter  layer. Cool and break into bite size pieces.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 2,  1998
