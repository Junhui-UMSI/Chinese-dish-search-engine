Peanut Soup #1
Uncooked rice, Salt, Carrots; unpeeled, sliced, Yellow onion; peeled,, Cayenne pepper or more; to, Peanut butter, Chicken soup stock (see, 
This recipe is to be found in cookbooks from all over the country . . .  and since about 1850. When you tell your kids that you are making  peanut-butter soup, get ready for some wisecracks . . . and make plenty  since they will love it.      In a 2-quart covered saucepan simmer the chicken stock, onion, and  carrots, covered, for 30 minutes. Pur?e all in a food blender or food  processor and return to the pan. Add the rice, salt, and cayenne pepper;  cover and simmer for an additional 20 minutes, or until the rice is tender.  Stir in the peanut butter and serve.
