Potato Pancakes
Egg, Potatoes, peeled, Flour, Oil (or lard), Onions, chopped fine, Salt, 
Grate the peeled raw potatoes with a fine grater. Squeeze dry in a towel.  Mix the potatoes with all other ingredients (except the oil). Leave it for  a while.    Heat the oil or lard in a frying pan.  Form pancakes from about 2 T of  batter, press flat and fry until both sides are golden brown.    Serve the hot pancakes with any kind of stewed fruit.    NOTES:    *  German Kartoffel Puffer -- A traditional German potato recipe. Not for  people with weight problems.    *  Be sure you make enough pancakes.
