Pintos and Corn Salad
Celery seed, Garlic powder; to taste, (12-oz) corn; drained, Red bell pepper; seeded, Balsamic vinegar, (15-oz) pinto beans; drained, Onion; chopped fine, Water, 
From: mormaker@ix.netcom.com    Date: Thu, 08 Aug 1996 20:39:04 -0600  Heres my attempt to copy a salad that I had at TGIFridays.  I hope you  like it.    Mix pintos, corn, pepper, and onion in medium mixing bowl.  Mix water,  balsamic vinegar, celery seed, and garlic powder in large mixing cup.  Pour  vinegar mixture over beans and corn.  Marinate several hours stirring  occasionally.  To serve pour off vinegar mixture or use a slotted spoon.    fatfree digest V96 #220    From the Fatfree Vegetarian recipe list.
