Raspberry Mallow Pie
Marshmallows, milk, (8 oz.) frozen whipped, Graham cracker crust (9"), (10 oz.) frozen raspberries, 
In a large microwave-safe bowl, combine marshmallows and milk. Cook on high  for 1-2 minutes; stir until smooth. Stir in raspberries. Fold in the  whipped topping. Pour into crust. Refrigerate or freeze. Yield 6-8  servings.  Note: This recipe was tested using a 700 watt microwave.
