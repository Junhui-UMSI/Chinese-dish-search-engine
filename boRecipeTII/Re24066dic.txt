Tourtiere
Salt, Each tyme & sage, Potato Boil and mash/save w, Minced pork, Each cloves and dry mustard, Garlic clove minced, Onion chopped, 
Boil and mash potato and save the water it was cooked in. In large fry pan  combine pork, onion, garlic and seasonings and potato water. Simmer 25 min.  Stir in mashed potato.Cool mixture and put in pastry lined pie plate and  cover with top crust. Bake in 450 oven 10 min. then turn heat down to 350  for 20 min.
