Beef and Chinese Lettuce Soup
Sugar, -(up to), Peanut oil, Soy sauce, Lean beef, Salt, Stock (see recipe), Chinese lettuce, 
1. Slice beef thin. Combine soy sauce and oil. Add to beef and toss. Let  stand 30 minutes, turning meat occasionally.       2. Bring stock to a boil. Meanwhile cut lettuce in 2-inch sections.       3. Add lettuce to stock with salt and sugar and simmer, uncovered, until  nearly done (about 10 minutes).       4. Add beef and simmer, uncovered, 3 to 4 minutes more.       VARIATION: For the Chinese lettuce, substitute either alone or in  combination: celery, shredded; Swiss chard or spinach leaves, cut in half;  or water cress, cut in 2-inch sections. Reduce cooking time in step 3 to  about 2 or 3 minutes.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
