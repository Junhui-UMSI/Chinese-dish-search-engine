Basil Grilled Chicken
Garlic powder, Fresh Basil, Butter, Fresh Basil, Salt, Chicken Breast Halves, Fresh Basil, Grated Parmesan cheese, Pepper, Pepper, Butter, 
From: kmeade@ids2.idsonline.com (The Meades)    Date: Sun, 30 Jun 1996 18:54:22 -0400  Recipe By: Kimberly Long <siberia@USIS.COM>    Press 3/4 teaspoon pepper into meaty sides of chiken breast halves. Combine  1/3 cup melted butter and 1/4 cup chopped basil; stir well.  Brush chicken  lightly with melted butter mixture.    Combine 1/2 cup softened butter, 2 tablespoons basil, Parmesan cheese,  garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.  Beat at low speed of an  electric mixer until mixture is well blended and smooth.  Transfer to a  small serving bowl; set aside.    Grill chicken over medium coals 8 to 10 minutes on each side, basting  frequently with remaining melted butter mixture.    Serve grilled chicken with basil-butter mixture.  Garnish with fresh basil  sprigs, if desired. Serves 4.    MC-Recipe Digest V1 #135    From the MasterCook recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
