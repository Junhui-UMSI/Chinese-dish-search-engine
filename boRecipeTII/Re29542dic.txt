Tart Shells
Iced water, Flour, Shortening, Salt, -(up to), 
From: ynnuf@yetti.amigans.gen.nz (Doreen Randal)    Date: Thu, 11 Nov 93 13:20:58 PST    Sift flour and salt into a bowl. Blend in shortening. Add water 1 Tbs at a  time until dough begins to hold together.  Gather into a ball and shape  into a flattened round on a floured board. Roll out and cut into circles  about 1" larger than tart pans. Place dough in pans, trim edges and prick  bottoms thoroughly. Bake at 475 degs F. for 8-10 mins. When cool remove  from pans and fill with your favourite fillings.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /MISC    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
