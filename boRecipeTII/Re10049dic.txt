Quakers- Chewy Cocoa Cookies
Brown Sugar, Granulated sugar, Egg whites or 1 egg, Margarine, softened, 
l/2 c  skim milk        l ts vanilla        2 c  QUAKER Oats uncooked  :          -(quick or old fashioned)        l c  all-purpose flour        1 c  raisins      l/3 c  unsweetened cocoa    l l/2 ts baking powder    Heat oven to 350  F.  Lightly spray cookie sheet with no-stick cooking  spray.  Beat margarine, sugars and egg whites until fluffy. Add milk and  vanilla;  mix well. Stir in combined oats, flour, raisins, cocoa and baking  powder.  Drop rounded tablespoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheet. Bake 10 to  12 minutes or until just set. Cool l minute on cookie sheet. Remove to wire  rack;  cool completely.  Stir in tightly covered container.
