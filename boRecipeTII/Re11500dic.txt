Raspberry Coulis (Bon Appetit)
(1/2 pint) baskets, Plus 2 T powdered sugar, 
Puree raspberries and powdered sugar in blender or processor until smooth.  Strain.  (Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.    Goes with Hazelnut Cake With Chocolate And Raspberries    Bon Appetit/May 94    Typed by Didi Pahl
