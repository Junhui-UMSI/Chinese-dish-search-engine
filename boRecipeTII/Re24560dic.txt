Asparagus Swiss
Butter, All green asparagus spears, (heaping) flour, Bread crumbs, Rich sweet milk, Grated Swiss cheese, Juice from asparagus, 
Melt butter in saucepan. Add flour and stir until smooth. Add liquid slowly  and cook until thickened. Add grated cheese, set aside. Cut and arrange  spears in dish, layer with bread crumbs. Pour sauce over asparagus; top  with remaining bread crumbs. Bake 15 minutes in 400 degree oven. Serve hot.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
