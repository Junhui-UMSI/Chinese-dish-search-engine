Pumpkin Chiffon Pie #2
Brown sugar, milk, Egg whites, pumpkin, Egg yolks, Nutmeg, Whipped cream, unflavored gelatin, Salt, Cold water, Granulated sugar, 
Combine egg yolks, brown sugar, pumpkin, salt and spices; cook in double  boiler until thick, stirring constantly. Soften gelatin in cold water; stir  into hot mixture. Chill until partly set. Beat egg whites, add granulated  sugar and beat until stiff. Fold into pumpkin mixture. Place in a prebaked  pie shell and chill. Garnish with whipped cream before serving.
