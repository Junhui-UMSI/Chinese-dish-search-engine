The Mills Veggie Chili
Chili powder, Bell pepper, kidney beans, Vegetable protein, Cumin, tomato paste, Onion, Garlic powder, pinto beans, Mrs. Dash, water, Canned tomatoes, 
Sautee chopped peppers and onions in skillet treated with non-stick cooking  spray.  Mix in beans, tomatoe paste and water.  Add veggie protein and  spices.  Heat on low until thoroughly heated.
