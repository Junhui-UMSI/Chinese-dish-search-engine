Baked Radicchio with Herbs
Crushed garlic, Thyme; fresh or dried, olive oil, Head of radicchio, Fresh, crusty French bread, To 12 slices goat cheese*, 
*Use 2 to 3 slices of goat cheese per serving, for a total of 8 to 12  slices.    Preheat oven to 350 F.    Chiffonade one head of radicchio and divide evenly among 4 individual oven  proof plates.  Put goat cheese slices on top of each radicchio serving.  Drizzle with olive oil mixed with lots of garlic and thyme. Bake for 10  minutes.  Serve hot with French bread.    Recipe developed by Ellen Ogden.  In "The Cooks Garden" catalog. Vol. 8,  No. 1.  Spring/Summer 1991. Pg. 15. Typed for you by Cathy Harned.
