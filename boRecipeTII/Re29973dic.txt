Teriyaki Wings
Wings, Bottled teriyaki sauce, Garlic, Pineapple juice, Black pepper(preferably, 
Mix mairnade ingredients well in a large bowl. Add wings, stir, and  marinate for 2 hours at room temperature. Drain and reserve marinade.    Preheat broiler. Place wings on broiling rack and broil 15-20 minutes or  until cooked through. Turn after 10 minutes and baste with marinade. Eat.
