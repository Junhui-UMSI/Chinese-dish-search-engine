Chicken Croquettes
Margarine, All-purpose flour, Non-dairy creamer, Salt, Pepper, chicken, Egg, Non-dairy creamer, All-purpose flour, bread crumbs, Vegetable oil, 
> From: srasheed@juno.com (Sam M Rasheed)    Melt margarine in a heavy saucepan over low heat; add 1/4 cup flour,  stirring until smooth. Cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Gradually add 1  cup creamer; cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened  and bubbly. Stir in salt and pepper. Remove from heat; stir in chicken.  Covered and chill about 1 hour. Shape chicken mixture into 6 balls or logs.  Combine egg and 1 tbsp creamer; mix well, and set aside. Dredge croquettes  in 1/3 cup flour; then dip into egg and milk mixture, and coat each  croquette with bread crumbs. Fry croquettes in deep hot oil 370 degrees for  3-5 minutes or until golden brown. Drain croquettes on paper towels. Yield        6    servings.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on Nov  25, 1998,
