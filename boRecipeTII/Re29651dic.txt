Tasty Vegetable Stew
To 6 slices salt pork OR, Medium onion, chopped, Chopped turnip, Chopped parsnip(optional), Water, Large potatoes, cubed, Pepper, Chopped carrot, Bacon ends, Chopped cabbage, 
Render pork slices in a heavy saucepan.  Cut slices in sereral pieces and  return to the pan with the onion and pepper. Stir and cook slowly for 5  minutes.  Add cabbage, carrots, turnips and parsnip. Cook for 1/2 hour  stirring occasionally with a wooden spoon.  Stir in potatoes and water.  Cover closely and let steam for about 1 hour. Serve with freshly baked  rolls.  Makes 4 generous servings. Source: Heritage Collection ch.
