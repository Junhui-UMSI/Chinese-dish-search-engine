Ported Stuffed Figs
California dried figs, Port (or sherry), Whole natural almonds, (about 24 figs), Ricotta cheese, toasted, 
Cover figs with water in a saucepan.  Bring to boil; cover and simmer for  20 minutes.  Drain; place figs in small bowl along with port.  Cover and  chill overnight.  Drain.  Remove stems; slice figs partially through in an  X pattern.  Fill with small spoonfuls of ricotta cheese.  Top with a whole  almond, pressed halfway into cheese.
