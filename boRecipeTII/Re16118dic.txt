Warm Saute of Summer Fruit
Cherries -- sliced, Sugar, Blueberries, Peeled kiwi -- sliced, Unsalted butter or, Margarine, Peaches, nectarines, or, Strawberries, or pitted Bing, Kirsch -- or to taste, 
1. Melt butter in a saute pan or large skillet. Stir in sugar.    2. Add fresh fruits and liqueur. Adjust liqueur to taste.    3. Cook over medium heat, basting with sauce, until fruits are warmed  through. Do not overcook.
