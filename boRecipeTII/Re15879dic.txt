Acorn Pie
Egg whites, beaten stiff, Sugar, Soda crackers, (coarsley broken), Vanilla, Baking Powder, Pecans, chopped, 
Beat egg whites until stiff; add baking powder and beat more. Add sugar and  vanilla; beat again.  Fold in crackers and pecans. Put in buttered pie  plate and bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes. Let cool and top with Cool  Whip and chopped pecans. Randy Rigg
