Baked Pear Gratin
Date sugar, Bosc, Winter pears -- such as, Lime juice, Cinnamon, Dark rum, Ground almonds, Safflower oil, for coating, Baking pan, 
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Lightly oil a large baking dish.    2. Peel, core, and thinly slice pears and layer in bottom of baking dish.  Sprinkle with lime juice and rum.    3. Combine almonds, cinnamon, and date sugar. Sprinkle over pears. Cover  pan with aluminum foil and bake for 40 minutes. Remove aluminum foil and  place pan under broiler. Broil to a light golden brown (3 to 5 minutes),  and serve immediately.    Recipe By     : the California Culinary Academy    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
