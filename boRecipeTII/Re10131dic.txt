Quesadillas De Huitlacoche
Serrano chili; as desired, Onion; diced, Huitlacoche *, Fresh tortillas, Vegetable oil, Cloves garlic diced, Salt, Sprig epazote; chopped, 
*(if huitlacoche is not available, squash flower - flor de calabaza - is  often substituted)    1. Wash the huitlacoches well.    2. Toast and dice the serrano chilis    3. Fry the onion and garlic. Add the huitlacoche, chili, the epazote and  salt and cover the pan. Leave on high heat until the huitlacoches are well  cooked.    4. Fill the tortillas, and fold them to form quesadillas. Cook on both  sides.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 218 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Nov 07, 1997
