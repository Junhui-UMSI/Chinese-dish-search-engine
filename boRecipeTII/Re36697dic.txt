Strawberry Dressing
Lemon juice, Powdered sugar, strawberries, fresh, Sour cream, Mayonnaise, 
Combine first 3 ingredients. Then add sour cream and mayonnaise. Chill.  Serve over fully ripened fruit. Yield: 3 cups.    MRS. MORIN M. SCOTT    AUSTIN, TX    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
