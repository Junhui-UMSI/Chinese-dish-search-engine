White Castle Turkey Stuffing
Celery, black pepper, Pickle removed, Ground thyme, White Castle hamburgers, Chicken broth, Ground sage, 
In a large mixing bowl, tear the White Castle hamburgers into pieces and  add diced celery and seasonings. Toss and add chicken broth. Toss well.  Stuff cavity of turkey just before roasting. Makes about 9 cups (enough for  10-12 lb bird). NOTE: Allow 1 White Castle hamburger for each pound of  turkey, which will be the equivilent of 3/4 cup stuffing per pound.
