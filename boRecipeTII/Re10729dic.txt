Quick Taco Salad
Rice-A-Roni, Chili powder, lettuce, monterey, Onions, tortilla chips, salsa, Tomato, Ground beef, 
1.In large skillet, brown ground beef and onion; drain. Remove from  skillet; set aside.    2.In same skillet, prepare RICE-A-RONI as package directs.    3.Stir in meat mixture, salsa and chili powder; continue cooking over low  heat 3 to 4 minutes or until heated through.    4.Arrange lettuce on serving platter. Top with rice mixture, tomato, cheese  and tortilla chips.    Recipe by: RICE-A-RONI?    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Barbra<barbra@pipeline.com> on Feb 21, 1998
