Pickled Pink Eggs
Beets, Whole onion, Cider vinegar, Pickling spice, Kosher salt, Eggs, Sugar, Drained, Water, 
Empty beets and their liquid into a small saucepan. Add onion, vinegar,  water, sugar, salt, and pickling spice. Heat just until the sugar  dissolves. Cool to room temperature. Pour the beet mixture over the eggs.  Cover and marinate 2-3 days in the refrigerator before serving. Stir  occasionally.      To serve, spoon beets, eggs and some liquid into a  serving bowl and top with a dollop of sour cream.
