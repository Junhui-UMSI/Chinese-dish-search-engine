Tuna Buns
Cheddar cheese, Prepared mustard, Hardcooked Eggs, Chopped, Green Pepper, Hamburger Buns, Split, (1 cn) Tuna, Drained, Mayo or Salad Dressing, Onion, 
Mix eggs, tuna, cheese, green pepper, onion, mustard and mayonnaise.  Fill  buns with tuna mixture.  Place on serving plate.    Microwave uncovered on high (100%) until filling is warm, 1 1/2 to 2 mins.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/microwav.zip
