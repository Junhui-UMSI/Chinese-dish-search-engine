Pineapple Boat Aloha
DRESSING, Rum, Pineapples, Strawberries, sliced, Brown sugar, Lime juice, Vegetable oil, Salt, Bananas, sliced, Mustard, 
Cut Pineapples in half lengthwise.  Scoop out pulp, leaving shells 1/2 inch  thick; set shells aside. Cut pineapple pup into bite-size pieces,  discarding core. Add strawberries and bananas; toss gently. Spoon into  pineapple shells; spoon dressing over fruit. Dressing: Combine first 5  ingredients in container of electric blender; process on high speed 1  minute. Gradually add oil, and continue to blend.
