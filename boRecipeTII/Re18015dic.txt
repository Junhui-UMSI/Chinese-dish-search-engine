Vegetarian Stir Fried Crab Meat
potato, Rice vinegar, carrot, Snow peas, bamboo, Salt, Peanut oil, Sugar, Chinese mushrooms, 
Soak mushrooms for 15 minutes, drain and slice into thin strips.  Chop tips  off snow peas, then slice thinly. Heat oil, Stir fry carrot and potato  until crispy, about 4 minutes. Add bamboo, mushrooms and peas, stir fry for  2 minutes.  Add sugar and salt, mix well.
