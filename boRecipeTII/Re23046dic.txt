Troys Grilled Tuna Sandwiches
Dry cilantro, Garlic, Lime, Tuna, Thyme, dry, California chile pepper, mild, Vegetable oil, 
Heat oil over medium heat.  Slice tuna steaks till 1/4" thick. Put garlic  in oil. Place tuna over garlic.  Sprinkle pepper, cilantro and thyme over  fillets. Brown one side then turn over. Put juice of lime over fillets and  complete cooking.  Serve as a sandwich with mayo and slices of avocado on  top.    Measurements are approximate.
