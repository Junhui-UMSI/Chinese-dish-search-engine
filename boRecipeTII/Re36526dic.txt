Strawberries Romanoff #1
Firm ripe strawberries;, Heavy whipping cream; well, Vanilla, Grand Marnier (can use, Grand Marnier, Sugar, CHANTILLY ROMANOFF, Chopped pistachio nuts, Sour cream; chilled, 
submitted by: sgjg6566@pipeline.com (Julie, Michigan, USA)  Combine berries and liqueur in large bowl and sprinkle with sugar. Mix  gently with spoon. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate 4 hours,  spooning syrup over berries once or twice. One hour before serving, divide  berries and syrup among 6 sherbert or balloon wine glasses. Refrigerate.  Just before serving, spoon Chantilly Romanoff over berries and garnish with  reserved strawberries. Sprinkle each serving with pistachio nuts.    CHANTILLY ROMANOFF: Beat cream in large mixing bowl until slightly  thickened. Add liqueur and vanilla and continue beating until soft peaks  form. Fold in sour cream, then beat until mixture is thick and holds its  shape. Refrigerate until ready to use.    This recipe can be doubled.
