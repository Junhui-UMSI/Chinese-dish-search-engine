Hazelnut Fig Baklava
FILLING, Fresh orange juice, Calamyrna figs; (9 ounces), Coarsely ground toasted, Ground cinnamon, Nutmeg, Cardamom, Unsalted butter; melted, Phyllo dough (1 pound);, HONEY SYRUP  1 1/2 c  honey, Water, Grated orange zest, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
1. Honey Syrup: in medium saucepan, bring to a boil honey, water and zest.  Reduce heat to low, simmer 20 minutes or until mixture is reduced to 2  cups. Remove from heat; stir in lemon juice. Let cool.    2. Filling: Meanwhile in large bowl, combine orange juice and figs. Let  stand 30 minutes, stirring once or twice, until figs have absorbed juice.  Add remaining filling ingredients.    3. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Brush 13 x 9 x 2- inch baking pan with  some melted butter. Unroll phyllo, cut in half crosswise. Keep covered with  waxed paper and damp towel while working with sheets. Place 1 sheet phyllo  in bottom of buttered pan; brush with butter. Repeat with 9 more sheets  phyllo, brushing each layer with butter and stacking in pan. Scatter with  1/2 cup nut mixture over top of phyllo.    4. Top with 2 sheets phyllo , buttering each. Repeat layers of 1/2 cup nut  mixture and 2 sheets buttered phyllo until nut mixture is used up (about 9  more layers.)    5. Finish top of baklava with 5 sheets buttered phyllo. With knife, cut  phyllo layers crosswise into 6 (2- inch wide) strips. Cut each strip on  diagonal to form 4 equal "diamonds" (youll have a small triangle at ends  of each strip.)    6. Bake 1 hour or until light golden and crisp. Remove baking pan to wire  rack; from saucepan spoon over cooled honey syrup over top. Let cool at  least 1 hour.
