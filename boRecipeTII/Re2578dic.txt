Peachy Dump Cake
Coconut, Sliced Peaches , Brown sugar, Margarine, PATTI  VDRRJ67A, Nuts, Yellow or white cake mix, 
Heat oven to 350~. Arrange peaches with the juice in botttom of a greased  and floured 9x13" pan. Sprinkle cake mix over top. Sprinkle nuts mixed with  coconut and brown sugar on top. Drizzle melted margarine over all. Bake  35-40 minutes. Serve with Cool Whip or ice cream.
