Tangy Apple Cheddar Salad
Apples, red, small --, Thinly, Salt, Cheese, cheddar -- cubed, Lettuce, Mayonnaise -- light, Lemon juice, Sliced, Pepper, Celery -- thinly sliced, 
Place apples in small bowl; toss with lemon juice.  Add celery and next 4  ingredients; toss to coat.  Serve on lettuce-lined plate.
