Yogurt Cilantro Sauce
Salt, Honey, Fresh Cilantro, Lime juice, fresh garlic, Plain yogurt, 
In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients and mix to blend. Season to taste  with salt and pepper. May be made one day ahead, covered and stored in the  refrigerator.
