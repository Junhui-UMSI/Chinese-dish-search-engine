Venison Steaks with Mushroom Game Sauce
Wild mushroom game sauce, Salt, Butter, Ground juniper berries, 
Pound the steaks to 1/2-inch thickness. Sprinkle lightly with the  juniper berries & salt. Saute the steaks with the butter for about 3  minutes on each side. The meat should be done no more than med rare.  Heat the wild mushroom game sauce, adjust for salt, pour over the  steaks, & serve.    4 venison steaks cut from loin, 6 to 8 oz each    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
