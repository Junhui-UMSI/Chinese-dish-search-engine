Apple Zucchini Slaw
Chopped apples, SALAD, Zucchini; shredded, Raisins, -(up to), Unsalted shelled sunflower, Orange Peel, DRESSING, Lowfat vanilla yogurt, 
In small bowl, combine dressing ingredients; blend well. In large bowl,  combine all salad ingredients. Pour dressing over salad; stir gently to  coat.
