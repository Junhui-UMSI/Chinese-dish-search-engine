Tasty Cheese Cake
Eggs, Stick butter; melted, Egg, Cream cheese; (8 ounce), Vanilla, Box yellow cake mix with, 
Mix first four ingredients (cake mix, melted butter, vanilla and 1 beaten  egg) and pat into 9x13 inch glass baking dish. Then mix 3 eggs and cream  cheese well and add sugar a little at a time. Pour second mixture over  first in baking dish. Bake 50 minutes at 350 ?. Cuts better when cool.    Submitted to cookbook by Joyce Leslie.
