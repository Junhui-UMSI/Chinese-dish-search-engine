Zucchini-Cheese Casserole
Flour, Sour cream, Cheddar cheese, garlic, Salt, Evaporated milk, Cubed zucchini, Butter, Onions, 
Cook zucchini, onion and garlic in butter until crisp-tender, stirring  frequently. Remove from heat. Sprinkle with flour and salt and toss well.  Stir in evaporated milk, sour cream and cheese. Turn into a shallow 1-quart  casserole. Bake at 375F for 20 minutes.    Recipe by: Mom Posted to EAT-L Digest by Nancy Pallotta  <nancee@NEO.LRUN.COM> on Jul 5, 1997
