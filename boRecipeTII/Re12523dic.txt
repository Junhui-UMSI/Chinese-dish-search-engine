Yemenite Haroset
Raisins, Fresh dates, Almonds, Pomegranates, peeled and, Cumin, cardamom, cloves &, Walnuts, Mixed spices (equal parts, 
In a food processor, chop all the fruits, including the pomegranate seeds  and juice and the nuts. Add the spices, adjusting each to your taste.    Makes 7 cups.    NOTES : _The Jewish Holday Kitchen_, Joan Nathan. Schocken Books, New York:  1988.
