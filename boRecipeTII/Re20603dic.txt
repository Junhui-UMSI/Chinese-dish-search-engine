Apple Cobbler
Butter, milk, Self rising flour, Water, Sugar, Apples, Shortening, Cinnamon, 
Melt butter and put in a 9x13 inch dish.  Heat sugar and water until sugar  melts.  Cut shortening into flour. Add milk and form into soft ball; roll  out dough. Sprinkle apples with cinnamon; place on dough and roll up like  jelly roll.  Cut in slices and place in pan with melted butter. Pour sugar  syrup around rolls and bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes. Randy Rigg
