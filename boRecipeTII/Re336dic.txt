Lemon Bars
All-purpose flour, Sugar, Margarine, Egg Beaters Healthy Real Egg, Lemon Peel, Lemon juice, Powdered sugar, 
1.Mix 1 cup flour and 1/4 cup sugar in medium bowl. Cut in margarine until  mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Press mixture onto bottom of 9-inch square  baking pan.    2.Bake at 350F for 15 minutes or until golden brown.    3.Mix Egg Beaters, lemon peel, lemon juice, remaining flour and remaining  sugar; pour over prepared crust.    4.Bake for 15 minutes more until set. Cool slightly; sprinkle with powdered  sugar if desired. Cool completely in pan on wire rack. Cut into bars to  serve.    Nutrition Information per bar (without powdered sugar): 60 calories, 2 g  total fat, 0 g saturated fat, 0 mg cholesterol, 21 mg sodium.
