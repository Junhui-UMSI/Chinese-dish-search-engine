Whole Wheat Waffles
milk, Eggs, shortening, Salt, Whole wheat flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, 
Beat egg yolks well;p add milk and shortening. Combine salt, sugar, and  flour.  Add to first mixture. Beat until smooth. Fold in stiffly beaten egg  whites. Sift baking powder lightly over the mixture and fold in quickly.  Bake in hot waffle iron. 6 servings.
