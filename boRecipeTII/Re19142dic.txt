Vegetable Kurma
Corriander powder, Turmeric powder, Onions  cut length-wise, Inch Cinnamon, Salt, Vegetables, Cloves, Cardamom, Length-wise, Coconut powder, Khus-Khus (poppy seeds), Garlic, Ginger, Green-Chillies cut, 
1. Put a reasonable sized vessel on the range and heat oil. 2. Put  cinnamon, cloves and cardamom and fry for 2-3 minutes. 3. Add onions and  green chillies and fry till onions turn brown. 4. Add garlic + ginger paste  and fry for a minute or so. 5. Add vegetables and fry for about 3 minutes.  6. Add Water (about a cup or two). 7. Let the vegetables + turmeric powder  cook. 8. If you are using canned or frozen vegetables skip the above step.  9. Add coconut paste, khus-khus, salt and wait till cooked. (note: Cook on  a low fire)    Recipe By     : Somesh Rao
