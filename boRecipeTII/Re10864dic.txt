Quinoa Tabouli Salad
Eden Quinoa, Fresh parsley, Tomato, Cucumber, Green onion, Fresh mint, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Plum vinegar, Garlic, 
Prep Time: 30-45 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes  Cook Quinoa according to package directions.  Cool.  Combine Quinoa,  parsley, tomatoes, cucumbers, garlic, green onions, and mint.  Mix well.  Combine olive oil, ume plum vinegar and garlic and mix into salad.
