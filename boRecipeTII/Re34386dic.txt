Bean and Sausage Casserole
Frozen Lima Beans; 10oz, Pepper, Brown sugar, Ketchup, Salt, Baked Beans; 21oz, Kidney Beans; Drained, Dry mustard, Smoked Sausage; Cut 1" Piec, Onion, Waldine Van Geffen, Oz, Drained, Great Northern Beans; 15-1/, 
Heat oven to 400~. Rinse frozen lima beans with cold water to separate. Mix  lima, baked, kidney and northern beans, onion and sausage in ungreased  2-1/2-quart casserole. Mix remaining ingredients; stir into bean mixture.  Cover and bake 40 to 50 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
