Tomato Cajun Chicken Salad
Thinly sliced green onion --, Salt, Pepper, Diced green bell pepper, Thyme, Celery, Cloves garlic -- minced, Chicken -- cooked, cubed, Thinly sliced, Non-fat plain yogurt, Tomatoes -- seeded and, Cayenne pepper, Lemon juice, Diced, 
Combine lemon juice and next five ingredients. Beat in yogurt. Gently fold  chicken and next 3 ingredients into dressing. Chill.  Just before serving,  fold in (3 cups) tomatoes.
