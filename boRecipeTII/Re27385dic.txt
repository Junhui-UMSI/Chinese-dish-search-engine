Toasted Nuts
Assorted nuts (pecans, Celery salt, Pepper, Unsalted butter (melted), Salt, Chili powder, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spread nuts evenly in a large, flat baking  pan. Lightly dribble the butter over all. Roast for 15 minutes, shaking pan  several times to coat all nuts evenly. Turn nuts onto absorbent paper  toweling. Flavor nuts with combination of all the spices and salts  specified. Serve warm.
