Thai Beef and Chiles with Orange
Thai palm sugar, Onion, Thai fish sauce, Cumin, Oranges -- peeled and, Extra lean ground beef, Finely chopped raw peanuts, Lettuce leaves, Serrano or jalapeno chiles, Chopped, Garlic, Seeded and minced, Safflower oil, Chopped cilantro, 
1. In a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat, saute onion and garlic  in oil until soft (about 5 minutes). Add beef and continue cooking for 2  minutes.    2. In a small bowl mix together fish sauce, chiles, and palm sugar until  smooth. Pour into wok and cook until liquid evaporates (5 to 7 minutes).  Stir in cilantro, cumin, and peanuts. Cook 2 more minutes.    3. Remove from heat and stir in oranges. Place a generous helping of beef  mixture on each lettuce leaf, roll lightly, and serve immediately.
