Sweet Potato Browns
Onion, Brown sugar, Ground allspice, Granny Smith apple; peeled, Margarine, Butter flavored cooking, Unsweetened apple juice, Sweet potato; peeled and, 
Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray, add margarine. Place over  medium high heat until margarine melts. Add potato, apple and onion; saute  5 minutes. Add brown sugar, allspice and apple juice; cook stirring  constantly, 5 minutes or until potato is tender, Serve immediately.
