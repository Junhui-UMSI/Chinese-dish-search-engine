Pumpkin Bread #08
Cinnamon, Pureed cooked pumpkin, Corn oil, Sugar, Baking soda, Nuts (optional), Flour, Eggs, Salt, Nutmeg, Water, 
From: pippa@parcplace.com (Pippa Dillman)    Date: 5 Aug 1994 16:34:07 -0400    Grease & Flour 2 loaf pans.  Stir together in large bowl flour, sugar,  baking soda, salt, cinnamon & nutmeg. Set aside. In second bowl mix  together pumpkin, oil & water. Add eggs one at a time mixing well after  each egg. Blend together dry & wet ingredients. Pour in pans. Bake at 350  for 1 hour. Check with toothpick.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
