A Really Good Brine for Smoking Beef or Pork
Salt, Cayenne pepper, Water, Brown sugar, garlic, Cumin, Basil, 
Combine all ingredients and let salt and sugar dessolve. Add meat and place  in refrigerator for up to 2 days. Prepare your smoker as per manufactures  instructions. Smoke meat until done. Normally a 5 lb. roast will take about  6 hours and should be at about 170*.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 621 by molony <molony@scsn.net>  on Jan 28, 1998
