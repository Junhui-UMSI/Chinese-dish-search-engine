White Mushroom Salad with Parmesan Shards
Cloves garlic; finely minced, Sliced roasted red bell, Lemon juice, olive oil, Fresh parmesan cheese shards, Balsamic vinegar, Sliced white mushrooms, Sliced celery, 
Combine the mushrooms, celery, and pepper in a salad bowl. Whisk together  the vinegar, lemon juice, oil, and garlic. Pour over the salad, and let the  salad sit for 1/2 hour.    Serve the salad on individual plates. Top each serving with Parmesan  shards.
