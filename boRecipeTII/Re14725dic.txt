(Irish) Potato Casserole
Cream of chicken soup, sharp cheese, Sour cream, Onions, margarine, Frozen hash brown potatoes, salt and pepper, 
Thaw potatoes about 30 min., then mix all ingredients in a large bowl.  Place in a 9 X 13 baking dish. Bake at 350 for one hour.
