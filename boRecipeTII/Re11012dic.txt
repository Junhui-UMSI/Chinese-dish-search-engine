Raggmunk (Swedish Potatoe Pancakes)
Skim milk, Flour, Potatoes, Salt, Egg, Oil, 
Whip together the flour and the salt with 1/2 of the milk. Add the egg and  the rest of the milk. Grate the potatoe and add. Stir. Melt the margarine  in a frying pan, put a thin layer of the mixture in the frying pan and fry,  when light brown turn and fry the other side. Serve with unsweetened  lingonberry jam and some vegetables. Some of the potatoe may be substituted  with carrots. This is a Weight Watchers recipe. Posted by Annica Lindgren  in Fidonet Intercook
