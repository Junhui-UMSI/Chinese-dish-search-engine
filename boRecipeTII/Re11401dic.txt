Ras El Hanout (Moroccan)
Crushed dried lavender, Fennel seeds; crushed, Black peppercorns; crushed, Ground nutmeg, Crushed malagueta pepper, Ground allspice, Cloves; crushed, Ground ginger, Turmeric, Ground mace, Ground cardamon, Crushed dried rosebuds, Ground cinnamon, Dried ground galangal, Hot red chile powder, 
Place all the ingredients in a spice mill and process to a fine powder.  Store in an airtight container. Use to spice up stews, rice and couscous.    A World of Curries by Dave DeWitt and Arthur Pais ISBN 0-316-18224-9 pg 180    Recipes sent to me from Bill, wight@odc.net
