Pineapple Vegetarian Fried Rice
Carrots, Cilantro leaves, Tomato ketchup, Green beansm, Rice, cooked, Cucumber, Vegetable oil, Garlic, Green onion, Pineapple chunks, Salt, Yellow onion, 
In a wok, heat the oil over medium heat & stir-fry the onion & garlic until  golden brown.  Add the carrots & green beans & stir-fry for 2 minutes  longer  Add the pineapple, rice, ketchup & salt. Mix well & stir-fry for  another 3 minutes. Arrange the cucumber around the serving platter. Pour  the rice into the centre. Garnish with green onion & fresh cilantro leaves.
