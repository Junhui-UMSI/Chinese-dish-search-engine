Baking powder, Heavy cream, Berries, Granulated sugar, Flour, Butter or margarine, semisweet chocolate chips, Granulated sugar, milk, Granulated sugar, Salt, Unsweetened cocoa powder, 
Date:    Tue, 11 Jun 1996 21:11:37 -0400    From:    Linda Robinson <CatRescuer@AOL.COM>    Sprinkle 2 Tblsp. sugar over fruit.  Set aside.  Stir together next five  dry ingredients. Cut in butter slowly till mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Stir in chocolate chips. Add milk and stir till moistened. On a floured  surface, roll dough 1/2" thick. Cut into 3" circles. Bake on a greased  cookie sheet at 450x F for 10 minutes, or till puffed. Beat heavy cream and  1/4 c. sugar. Top shortcakes. Yield: approximately 8.
