Real Red Chili Sauce
Onions, Salt, Medium-size tomatoes, Mixed pickling spice, Sweet red peppers, Vinegar, Sugar, Tablesppon mustard seed, Finely chopped preserved, Celery seed, 
1. Wash five preserving jars in hot soapy water; rinse. Cover with hot  water until ready to fill. Cover two-piece jar lids with very hot water in  a separate pan.    2. Wash and core tomatoes; cut into eighths. Put tomatoes, onions, and red  peppers through coarse blade of food grinder.    3. Combine ground vegetables with sugar and salt in large kettle. Simmer 30  minutes, stirring frequently.    4. Stir in vinegar, celery seed, mustard seed and ginger. Tie pickling  spices in cheesecloth; push down into vegetables. Cook, stirring  frequently, until thickened, about 40 minutes. Remove pickling spice bag.    5. Ladle into hot jars; fill to within 1/2-inch of top. Wipe rims; seal  jars.    6. Process 15 minutes in boiling water bath; cool; label. Store in cool dry  place. Posted to EAT-L Digest 11 Mar 97 by "Elizabeth A. Post"  <millefiore@FUSE.NET> on Mar 11, 1997
