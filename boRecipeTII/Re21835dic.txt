Apple-Peach Chutney
Jars whole onions (15 oz, Sliced peaches (15-16 oz, Brown Sugar, Cider vinegar, Ginger, Raisins, Golden delicious apples, Cinnamon, Ground cumin, 
1.Bring all ingredients to a boil in a large pot. Boil gently, uncovered,  about 1 hour stirring occasionally, until thickened.    2. Cool slightly (mixture will continue to thicken as it cools). Pour into  jars, cook, cover and refrigerate.    Makes 7 cups. Age for 3 days. Refrigerate up to 2 months.
