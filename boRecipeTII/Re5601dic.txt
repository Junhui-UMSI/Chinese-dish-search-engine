Pineapple Sauce #1
Cornstarch, Vanilla extract, crushed pineapple in juice, canned, Water, Sugar, lemon zest, 
In a 1-1/2 quart microwave-safe dish, combine sugar and cornstarch.  Mix  well with a fork. Drain and reserve the syrup from pineapple. Slowly add  the reserved syrup to the sugar/cornstarch mixture, stirring well until  smooth. Stir in the crushed pineapple, water, lemon zest and vanilla. Cover  the dish (leave a small vent if you cover with plastic wrap) and microwave  on HIGH (100% power) for 4-1/2 to 6-1/2 minutes, or until mixture thickens.  Stir every 2 minutes.
