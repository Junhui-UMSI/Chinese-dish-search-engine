Baked Lentils
Golden corn syrup, Dry mustard, Lentils; sorted and rinsed, Ketchup, Molasses, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic powder, Onions; chopped, Water, Salt, 
Combine lentils and water in a large kettle and bring to a boil. Cover and  simmer 30 minutes. Add rest of ingredients; combine well and pour into 5  greased 9"x13" baking pans (4 qt capacity]. Cover and bake for 1 1/4 hours.  Uncover and bake another 45 minutes. Add water if necessary.    Optional: top with bacon that has been cut into small pieces, sauteed  slightly and well-drained.
