Basic Chicken Stock
Celery stalks; chopped, olive oil, Head of garlic; cut in half, Bouquet garni, Salt and pepper, Carrot; peeled and chopped, Raw chicken bones; rinsed in, Cold water, Onion; peeled and quartered, 
EMERIL LIVE SHOW #EMlA36    In a large stock pot, over high heat, add the oil. When the oil is hot, add  the onions, carrots, and celery. Saute for 2 to 3 minutes. Add the  remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and  simmer for about 2 hours. Remove the stock from the heat and skim off any  scum that is on the surface. Strain the stock through a large fine-mesh  sieve. Discard the bones and vegetables.    Yield: 3 quarts  Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 19, 1998
