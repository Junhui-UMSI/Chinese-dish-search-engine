Top-Dog Hot Dogs
Green pepper, Hot dog buns, mozzarella cheese, hot dog, Tomato, Hot dogs, Onion, 
Cook hot dogs according to package directions. Place in buns, top with  relish or chili sauce, green pepper, onion and tomato. Sprinkle with  mozzarella cheese. Makes 8 servings.
