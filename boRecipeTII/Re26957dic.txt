Baked Catfish Supreme
catfish fillets, Mayonnaise, White pepper, Parsley, Paprika, Salt, Lemon, 
Sprinkle fish fillets with satl and pepper; place in greased baking  dish. Add lemon juice and parsley to mayonnaise and spread over  fillets. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, or until fish flakes  easily when tested with a fork.  Sprinkle with paprika before serving.
