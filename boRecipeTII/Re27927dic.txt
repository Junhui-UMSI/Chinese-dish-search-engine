Thousand Island Dressing #3
Sweet pickle relish, Grated onion, Chili sauce, Hard-boiled egg; riced, Mayonnaise, Lemon juice, Finely chopped green pepper, Paprika, 
Mix all ingredients together. Store in refrigerator. Serve over lettuce.  Good served over an open-faced chicken or turkey sandwich with Swiss cheese  & bacon.
