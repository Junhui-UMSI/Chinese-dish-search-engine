Barbecue Shrimp with Angel Hair Pasta
Bay leaf, White wine, Light margarine, Hot pepper sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Paprika, Dried Rosemary, Garlic, Dried Oregano, Dressing, Dried thyme, Juice of 1 Lemon, Shrimp, Angel hair pasta, Fat free italian salad, olive oil, Parsley, Garlic, 
In a large heavy skillet, melt marg.  Add remaining ingred (before pasta)  except shrimp and wine.  Cook over medium heat until sauce begins to boil.  Add shrimp and cook for about15 min. Add wine and cook another 10-12 min.    Cook pasta according to package directions, omitting salt and oil. Drain  and set aside.    In a small pan, combine remaining ingre and saute for a few min. Pour over  pasta and toss.    Recipe By     : Shape - April 1995
