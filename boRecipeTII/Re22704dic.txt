Apricot Granola Bars
Nature Valley granola, All-purpose flour, Granulated sugar, dried apricots, Powdered sugar, Vanilla, Salt, All-purpose flour, Margarine, Eggs, Flaked coconut, Baking Powder, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a square pan (9 x 9 x 2-inch- es). Mix 1  cup flour, margarine and powdered sugar until flour is moistened. Press in  pan. Bake 10 minutes. Beat eggs. Stir in remaining ingredients,. Spread  over baked layer. Bake until golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes. Cool and cut  into bars. (Makes 36 bars.)
