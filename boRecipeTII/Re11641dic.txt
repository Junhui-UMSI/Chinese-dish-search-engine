Raspberry Streusel Coffee Cake
Eggs, TOPPING, Butter, Raspberry jam, milk, Flour, Salt, butter, CAKE, Sugar, Cinnamon, Vanilla, Flour, Brown sugar, Baking Powder, 
Here is a recipe that I got from this months HomeMakers magazine. It looks  good and I cant wait to try it soon.    Topping: In a bowl, mix together dry ingred. cut in butter to make fine  crumbs    Cake: In large bowl combine first 4 ingred; mix thoroughly, add remaining  ingred. Beat at low speed on electric mixer until moistened, then at med.  sped for 1 minute. Spoon batter into paper muffin things about 2/3 full.  Smooth top of batter evenly. Sprinkle with topping and dot with jam. Bake  in 350 oven for 20 to 25 min or until tester comes out clean. Cool.
