Bean and Sausage Soup
Dry small white beans, sliced 1/2 inch thick, drained, Water, Chopped parsley, Canned garbanzo beans, Ground cumin, (navy or great northern), Chicken bouillon granules, Canned cream-style corn, Onions, Pepper, Hot sausage, soaked, Bay leaf, Salt, 
Drain and rinse soaked beans. Saute onion and garlic in oil until  tender. Add water, onion and seasonings. Simmer, covered, about 30  minutes or until beans are almost tender. Add sausage, garbanzo beans  and corn. Return mixture to boil; simmer 30 minutes or until beans are  tender.  Remove bay leaf. Garnish with parsley. Makes 4 or 5 servings.
