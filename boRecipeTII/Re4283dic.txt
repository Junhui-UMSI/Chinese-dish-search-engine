Perrys (San Francisco) House Dressing
Worcestershire Sauce, Red wine vinegar, Fresh parsley, chopped, Dried Oregano, Burgundy wine, Dried basil, Celery seeds, Dried thyme, olive oil, Tabasco sauce, Dry mustard, 
Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl, blender, or jar and mix well.    Makes about 1 1/2 cups.
