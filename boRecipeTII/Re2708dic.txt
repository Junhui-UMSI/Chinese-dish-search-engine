Peanut Butter Bars for a Crowd
Sugar, Baking Powder, Shortening, Eggs, Peanut butter, Salt, Brown sugar, All-purpose flour, Vanilla extract, 
Cream the eggs, sugars, peanut butter, shortening and vanilla until smooth.  Combine flour, baking powder and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture and  mix well. Press into 3 greased 15"x10"x1" baking pans. Bake at 350 for  20-25 minutes or until top is golden brown. Cool. Dust with confectioners  sugar.
