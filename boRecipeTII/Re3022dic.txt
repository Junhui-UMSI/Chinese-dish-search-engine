Peanut Butter-Chocolate No Bake Cookies
Sugar, Water, Peanut butter, Oatmeal, Coconut, Cocoa, Butter, 
Mix butter, sugar, water and cocoa together, bring to a boil for 30  seconds. Remove from heat and add peanut butter, oatmeal or coconut. Drop  on wax paper and let harden.
