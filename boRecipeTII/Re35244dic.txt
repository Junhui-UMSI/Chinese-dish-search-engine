Beef and Pasta Primavera
Crushed red pepper, Cherry tomatoes; cut in half, Lightly pakced fresh basil, Uncooked farfalle; (bowtie, Freshly rated Parmesan, Salt, Garlic, Boneless beef top sirloin, Ready-to-serve beef broth, Frozen vegetable mixture;, olive oil, 
Cook pasta according to package directions. Keep warm. Meanwhile trim fat  from beef steak. Cut steak lengthwise in half and then crosswise into 1/8  inch thick strips. In large nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium-high  heat until hot. Add beef and garlic (1/2 at a time) and stir-fry 1-2  minutes or until outside surface is no longer pink. (Do not overcook).  Season with salt. Remove to a large bowl; keep warm. In same skillet,  combine vegetable mixture, beef broth and red pepper; cook 3-4 minutes or  until vegetables are crsip-tender. Add vegetable mixture, pasta, tomatoes  and basil to beef; toss to combine. Sprinkle with cheese; serve  immediately. Makes 4 servings.
