Baked Garlic-Stuffed Antipasto Bread
fresh garlic, frozen; chopped, dried basil, Garlic salt, Anchovy fillets, Butter, olive oil, KBTHOMPS / SUSAN FORTNEY, Dried tarragon, black olives, Crusty, unsliced loaf French, Swiss cheese, Coarsely ground pepper, mozzarella cheese, Capers, parsley, artichoke hearts, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    Peel and coarsely chop garlic, set aside. Slice top off French bread  lengthwise. Carefully scoop out inside of loaf, tearing bread into small  pieces, and place in large bowl.    In large skillet, melt butter and all but 1 tablespoon of the olive oil  together until bubbly. Add garlic and stir for 30 seconds. Add spinach,  artichokes, parsley and anchovies. With a wooden spoon, gently combine  ingedients and cook about 1 minute. When thoroughly mixed, add to bread  pieces and toss well to mix. Add cheeses, black olives, capers and  seasonings. Toss well to mix.    Pack into hollowed out bread, replace top and wrap in foil. Bake for 30  minutes at 350 degrees F. Unwrap bread, drizzle with 1 tablespoon olive oil  and bake for additional 5 minutes. Slice into 1 1/2 inch slices. Serve  warm.
