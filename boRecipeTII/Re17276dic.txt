Wagon Wheel Vegetable Salad
Each of paprika and salt, Cherry tomatoes, cut, Mayonnaise, Garlic, Brocoli florets, Green onions, sliced, Chopped fresh parsley, Prepared mustard, Wagon-wheel shaped Pasta, Cucumber, sliced, Sour cream, Stalk of celery, thinly, 
Cook wagon wheels according to package directions, adding brocoli during  the last 4 minutes; drain. Rinse in cold water to cool; drain well. In  large bowl, combine wagon wheel-brocoli mixture with celery, green onions,  cucumber and parsley. In separate bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream,  garlic, mustard, paprika and salt; mix well. Pour over pasta mixture; toss  to coat. Cover and chill 2 hours or overnight. Just before serving, add  tomatoes and toss gently. Submitted By A4GY@JUPITER.SUN.CSD.UNB.CA (N.
