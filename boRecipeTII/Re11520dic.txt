Raspberry Custard Kuchen
TOPPING, Whipping cream, Butter or margarine, All-purpose flour, Salt, Eggs, Granulated sugar, Vanilla extract, All-purpose flour, divided, Granulated sugar, Whipping cream, Fresh raspberries, 
Source: Taste of Home Magazine Aug/Sep/96    In a bowl, combine 1 cup flour and salt; cut in butter until the mixture  resembles coarse crumbs.    Stir in cream; pat into a greased 13"x9"x2" baking pan.    Combine the sugar and remaining flour, sprinkle over crust. Arrange  raspberries over crust.    For topping, combine sugar and flour. Stir in eggs, cream and vanilla  extract; pour over berries.    Bake at  375 degrees F. for 40-45 minutes or until lightly browned.    Serve warm or chilled. Store in refrigerator.    Yield: 10-12 servings    From the recipe files of suzy@gannett.infi.net    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #106 by Suzy <suzy@gannett.infi.net> on Apr  15, 1997
