Penne with Grilled Salmon, Asparagus and Lemon Butter
Fresh Lemon Juice, Salmon, ginger, fresh, Asparagus, Soy sauce, Garlic, Penne, lemon zest, Butter, 
Cook pasta until al dente, about 12 minutes. Brush salmon with soy sauce on  both sides. Grill or broil until almost cooked, about 3 minutes each side.  Cool slightly and pull into flakes, discarding bones. Steam asparagus until  crisp, tender, about 4 minutes. Drain pasta and melt butter in the large  pot. Add ginger and garlic, saute       30    seconds. Add lemon juice and zest. Toss the pasta, asparagus and  melted butter, then gently toss in the salmon.
