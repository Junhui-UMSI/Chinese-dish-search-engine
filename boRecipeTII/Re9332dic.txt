Puffed Wheat Balls
Corn syrup, Sugar, Brown Sugar, Butter, Puffed wheat cereal, Evaporated milk, 
Place cereal in a large bowl; set aside.  In a heavy saucepan, bring brown  sugar and corn syrup to a boil. Add butter. Combine evaporated milk and  sugar; add to boiling mixture; add to a boiling mixture and continue  cooking until a soft ball forms when liquid is dropped into cold water  (240F on a candy thermometer).  Pour over cereal and stir to coat. Shape  into 2-inch balls.    Grandma Hunt   Panguitch, Utah Taste of Home Dec/Jan/94
