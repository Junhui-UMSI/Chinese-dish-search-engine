Pocket Meals on the Run
Water, Pita breads, chicken tenderloin, frozen, olives, Oil and vinegar, Lettuce, Plum tomatoes, Feta cheese, 
Cut frozen tenderloins into 1 inch pieces. Spray large skillet with  nonstick cooking spray. Add chicken, cook over medium-high heat 12 to 15  minutes, or until light brown and juices run clear. Add water. Bring to a  boil; add rice. Cover, remove from heat; let stand 5 minutes, or until  liquid is absorbed. Stir in salad dressing and cheese.    Fill pita bread halves with lettuce and chicken rice mixture. Top with  tomatoes and olives.
