Taco Scramble
Popcorn, Butter or margarine, taco seasoning, Corn tortilla chips, 
In a large bowl, mix popped corn and tortilla chips; set aside.    In a small saucepan over low heat, melt butter. Stir in seasoning mix until  blended.    Pour seasoned butter over popped corn and chips. Gently stir until well  coated. Cool slightly before eating.    Store in a large airtight container.
