Baked Scallops
Lemon juice, Thyme, Dry white wine, Butter, Scallops, 
Heat butter wine lemon juice and thyme pour over scallops. Marinate for  15-20 minutes at room temperature. Bake at 450 for 5-6 minutes. Do not  overcook.    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by Brandy Chicoine <bnb86@alice.icanect.net>  on Nov 23, 1997
