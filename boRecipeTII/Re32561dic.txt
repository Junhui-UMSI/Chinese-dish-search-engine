Sweet Irish Cream Bread
PLUS, Eggs, Mix, Powdered sugar, (16.6oz) date quick bread, Water, To 3 milk, Sugar, (3oz) cream cheese,, Vegetable oil, Softened, 
Preheat oven to 350F.    Grease and flour only the bottom of a loaf pan.    Beat cream cheese, sugar and one of the eggs until smooth; set aside.    Combine bread mix, water, 1/3 cup Baileys, oil and remaining egg. Mix  until moistened.    Pour 1 cup batter into pan. Carefully spoon cream cheese mixture over and  pour remaining batter over. Swirl with a knife to marble. Bake 65-75  minutes until bread tests clean. Coolin pan 15 minutes, then remove to a  wire rack to finish cooling.    Blend powdered sugar, remining Baileys and enough milk to reach a  drizzling consistency and pour over cake.
