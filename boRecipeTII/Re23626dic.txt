Artichoke Appetizer
Jane harris DRKD50A, Jars marinated artichokes;, Salt and pepper to taste, Cheddar cheese; grated, Dash of tabasco, Green onions; chopped fine, Eggs, Garlic, Soda crackers; crushed, 
Saute onions and garlic in artichoke oil; then drain.  Mix all ingredients and spread in greased 8x8" pan.  Bake at 325 for 30 min. Can be baked, frozen and re-  heated. This recipe was a great success at a party  Christmas Eve. Hope you like it. Let me know. Happy  New Year to you!
