Sweet Anise Mustard
Cardamom seeds, Mustard powder, Anise seeds, Molasses, Balsamic vinegar, Canola oil, Brown sugar, 
Grind the seeds in a coffee grinder & transfer to a food processor. Add the  rest of the ingredients & blend until smooth. Pour into a double boiler &  cook for 10 minutes, or until the mixture is reduced by half.    Spoon into a container, cover & refrigerate for up to        3    days before serving.
