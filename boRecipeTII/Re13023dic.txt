Winter Spinach Salad-Martha Stewart Living
Scallions, thinly sliced, Seedless red grapes, cut, Green apple, cored and, Bunch spinach, preferably, Dried sour cherries, (about 2 oz) crumbled goat, Apple-cider vinegar, olive oil, Salt & freshly ground pepper, Walnuts, coarsely chopped, 
Combine spinach, cherries, scallions, apple slices, grapes, and walnuts.  Whisk together vinegar and olive oil; season to taste with salt and pepper.  Pour vinaigrette over salad; toss in cheese and arrange on salad plates.
