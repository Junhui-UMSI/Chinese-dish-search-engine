Peachy Orange Drink
peaches, orange juice concentrate, Water, Sugar, ginger ale, 
Puree 5 cup frozen unsweetened sliced peaches in blender. Pour into large pitcher or bowl. Stir in two 6 oz cans frozen orange juice concentrate, 4 juice cans of water and 2/3 cup sugar. Stir until  sugar is dissolved. Stir in 2 1/2 cups ginger ale. Immediately pour  into tall glasses and garnish with orange slices and mint sprigs.
