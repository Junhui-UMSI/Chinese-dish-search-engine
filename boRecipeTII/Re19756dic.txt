Anzac Biscuits
Butter, Sugar, Golden syrup, Hot water, Rolled oats, Flour, Baking soda, Coconut, 
This recipe is from the Edmonds Cookery book - the cooking bible on 3 1/2  million kiwi bookshelves. Id love to know if they were the same as the  ones you remembered. Please write and tell me.    Melt butter and sugar together in a large saucepan. Cool. Mix sugar,  coconut, rolled oats, and flour together. Stir into saucepan. Dissolve soda  in water and mix in. Place rounded teaspoonfuls on a greased oven tray.  Bake at 350 F (180 C) for 15 minutes. Makes 36. These are chewy and a vry  popular cookie here.
