Western Kentucky-Style Barbecue Sauce
Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, Ground red pepper, Garlic salt, catsup, Water, Dry mustard, Onion powder, Paprika, 
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan. Cook over medium heat 20 minutes.  Use to baste chicken or ribs.
