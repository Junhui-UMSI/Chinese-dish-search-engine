Raspberry Delight #2
Sugar, almonds, Egg whites, Frozen raspberries, Whipped cream, Pie crust, Almond flavoring, 
Line pie crust with almonds. Bake and cool. Beat all other ingredients  except whipped cream until thick; fold in whipped cream. Pour into pie  crust and freeze.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
