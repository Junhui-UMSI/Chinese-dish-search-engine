Bean Casserole
Pork and beans, Water, Onion chopped fine, White lima beans, drained, Red beans, drained, Syrup, Mustard, Bacon drippings, Cloves garlic, chopped, Vinegar, Salt and pepper to taste, 
Fry onion and garlic in bacon fat until limp.  Mix with remaining  ingredients.  Pour into casserole dish; bake 1 hr. at 350 F.
