Sweet and Sour Meatballs
Onions, Pepper, Grape jelly, Worcestershire Sauce, Bread crumbs, Chili sauce, Egg, Ground beef, Salt, 
DIRECTIONS -   MIX TOGETHER HAMBURG, ONIONS, EGG,BREAD CRUMBS, SAUCE, SALT  AND   PEPPER. MAKE INTO BALLS, BAKE AT 350 FOR 20 MIN SAUCE; HEAT JELLY AND  CHILI SAUCE TOGETHER, POUR OVER MEATBALLS.
