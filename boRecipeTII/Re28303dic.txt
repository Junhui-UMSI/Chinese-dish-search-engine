Baked Goats Cheese Log with Prawns and Basil
Sheets Jus-rol Fillo, Fresh basil leaves, finely, Butter, Pine nuts, Grated Parmesan, Prawns, chopped, Grapeseed oil, Clove of garlic, crushed, 
Mix the garlic and basil with a pinch of salt. Pound the nuts in a pestle  and mortar; add the oil, basil and garlic mixture. Stir in the Parmesan and  prawns. Cut each sheet of fillo in half and place a cube of goats cheese  in the centre. Spoon over the basil mixture. Brush edges of pastry with  melted butter and wrap up to form a parcel. Place on a baking sheet, brush  with butter and bake at 200C (400F) mark 6 for about 20min. Serve warm with  salad leaves.    Source: Jus-Rol: Pastry for Today
