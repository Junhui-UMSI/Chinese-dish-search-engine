Tofu Burgers
Whole wheat flour, Nutritional yeast, sesame seeds, potato, garlic powder, Vegetable bouillon powder, Black Pepper, Tofu, Soy sauce, Onion, 
Grind the tofu, potato and onion (if using) in a food processor. Dont let  it get too fine. Mix in remaining ingredients, shape into burgers and bake  at around 350 degrees on a very lightly oiled cookie sheet for 15-20  minutes, turning once. They can also be fried.     This recipe was the Judges Choice award winner in the "Build a Better  Burger Contest" sponsored by the North American Vegetarian Society.     Submitted by Helen Dessart, Otterburn Park, Quebec     From _Vegetarian Voice_, Vol. 19, No. 1
