Aunt Rubys Fresh Tomato Relish
Tomatoes, peeled, seeded,, Sugar, Pepper, Salt, Celery seed, Vinegar, Onion, Green pepper, chopped, 
1.  In a medium bowl, combine tomatoes, onion, and green pepper.    2.  In a small bowl, combine sugar, vinegar, celery seed, salt, and      pepper.  Stir to dissolve the sugar.  Pour over tomato mixture; toss.      Cover and chill till serving time.        Makes 4 cups.    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #780 by Bill <thelma@pipeline.com> on Sep 13,  1997
