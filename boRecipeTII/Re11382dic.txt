Ranger Cookies #1
Shredded coconut, crisp wheat cereal, Flour, Granulated sugar, Quick-cooking oatmeal, Brown sugar, Baking soda, Shortening, Baking Powder, Eggs, Salt, Vanilla, 
Date: Sun, 3 Mar 1996 16:37:07 -0500    From: PMCiesla@aol.com    Cream shortening, add sugars gradually and cream thoroughly.  Blend in  well-beaten eggs.  Sift flour once before measuring.  Sift flour, baking  powder, soda and salt together and add to the creamed mixture.  Blend in  the vanilla, oatmeal, cheat cereal and coconut. Drop by teaspoonfuls on  well-greased baking sheet and bake in 375 F oven 12 to 15 minutes.  Makes  about 5 dozen cookies.
