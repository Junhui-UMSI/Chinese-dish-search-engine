American Chop Suey
Water, Cream of chicken soup, Chop suey noodles, Celery, Ground beef, Soy sauce, Rice, Onion, Mushrooms, 
Brown beef, and add celery and onion and simmer. Add mushrooms, soup, and 1  1/2 cup water, soy sauce, and rice. Bake for 1 hour. Put Chop Suey noodles  on top and bake for 15 minutes more. busted by sooz  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 170 by James and Susan Kirkland  <kirkland@gj.net> on Oct 27, 1997
