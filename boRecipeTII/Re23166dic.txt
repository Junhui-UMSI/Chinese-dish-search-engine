Tuna Balls in Wine Sauce
Egg, Dry white wine, Hardboiled egg; finely, Flour; for dusting, Chicken broth, Parsley, olive oil, Black Pepper, Bread crumbs, Albacore Tuna; water packed,, Salt, 
In a bowl, moisten the bread crumbs with 3 T of chicken broth and 1  tablespoon of the wine.  Mix in the flaked tuna, hard boiled egg, beaten  egg, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper. Form the tuna mixture into 1 1/2  inch balls and dust with flour. Heat the oil in a skillet and brown the  tuna balls on all sides. Add the remaining five tablespoons of wine, the  remaining 7 tablespoons of chicken broth and salt. Cover and simmer for 30  mins.  (the tuna balls seem to absorb a lot of liquid during cooking, so  add more chicken broth if necessary) May be prepared ahead.    We cooked this recipe and found it to be wonderfull.
