Quick Orange-Date Bread
Baking Powder, Flour, Sugar, Pecans, Salt, Orange juice, Baking soda, Orange rind, Egg, Butter, dates, 
Combine juice, rind, butter, dates, sugar, egg and nuts. Sift dry  ingredients and add to orange mixture. Mix well and pour into greased loaf  pan. Bake at 350 for 50 minutes. Makes a great Christmas gift!
