Stuffed Lychees
Salt, Sherry, (1 lb, 4 oz size) lychees, Chopped crystalized ginger, (8 oz) cream cheese, Chopped macadamia nuts, 
Drain lychees. In small bowl of electric mixer, beat cream cheese with  sherry and salt until mixture is smooth. Stir in remaining  ingredients. Stuff lychees.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
