Leek Balls - Albondigas De Prasa
Salt, Pepper; to taste, Flour; for coating, Beaten egg; for coating, Leeks, Ground beef, Egg, 
source: a Turkish Sephardic cookbook    Peel the leeks and wash them well. Chop the leeks into small pieces and  boil them. Strain liquid from leeks, then squeeze them to remove any  remaining liquid. Add ground beef, egg, salt, and pepper and mix very well.  Form into balls. Roll the balls in flour and in beaten egg. Deep fry in hot  oil.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by dsabraham@juno.com (Deena Abraham) on Nov  10, 1998,
