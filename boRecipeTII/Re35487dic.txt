Strawberry-Cream Crepes
Sugar, Dessert Crepes, fresh Strawberries, Heavy cream, Sweetened Condensed Milk, Strawberries, Lemon juice, 
Sprinkle sliced strawberries with sugar; set aside.   Beat milk with lemon juice until thick. Fold in strawberries and whipped  cream. Divide among crepes; fold. Garnish with additional whipped cream  and a strawberry centered on the cream. Yields 6 servings.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmstrawb.zip
