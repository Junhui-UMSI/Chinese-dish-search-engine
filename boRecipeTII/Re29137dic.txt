Baked Rice Pudding
Salt, Raisins, Sugar, Butter, Nutmeg, Rice; raw, milk, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven to 300 degrees, lightly butter cassarole. Combine rice, milk,  salt, vanilla and nutmeg in casserole and mix well. Bake, uncovered for 1  hour, stirring occasionally.
