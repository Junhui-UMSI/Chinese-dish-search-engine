From: arielle@taronga.com (Stephanie da Silva)    Date: Mon, 30 May 1994 10:53:41 GMT    Cook water and sugar in small saucepan over low heat until sugar dissolves.  Boil 5 minutes.  Pour into bowl.  Cool syrup to room temperature.  Blend  tamarind pulp and lemon juice into sugar syrup.  Strain.  Transfer to ice  cream maker and process according to manufacturers instructions. Transfer  to airtight container. Freeze at least 2 hours before serving.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /DESSERTS    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
