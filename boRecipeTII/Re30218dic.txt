Banana Bread #01
Salt, Vegetable oil, Walnuts or pecans, Eggs; well beaten, Sugar, Baking soda, Flour, Buttermilk or sour cream, Very ripe bananas; mashed, 
Cream oil & sugar. Add eggs & salt & mix well. Add flour & soda combined  with buttermilk alternately. Add bananas. Fold in nuts. Grease 2 loaf pans.  Pour in mixture & bake about 45 minutes at 350. Makes 2 loaves.    DONITA ALEXANDER    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
