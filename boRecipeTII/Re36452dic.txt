Beer Rice
Green Peppers; chopped, Ground Thyme, Cubes Chicken Bouillon Cube, Boiling water, Butter, Beer, Rice; uncooked, Salt, Pepper, Onions, 
saute onion and green pepper in butter until tender.    Dissolve bouillon in boiling water; add to onion and green pepper mixture.    Stir in the beer and seasonings.    Cover and simmer over low heat 30 to 40 minutes or until all liquid is  absorbed.
