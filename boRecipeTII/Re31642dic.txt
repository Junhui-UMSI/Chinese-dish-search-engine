Swiss Fondue
Of ground nutmeg, (1 lb) shredded aged Swiss, Salt, Sauterne, All purpose flour, Clove garlic, halved, Worcestershire Sauce, 
From: Arizona Cookbook    Toss together cheese and flour. Rub inside of saucepan with garlic. Discard  garlic. Add sauterne and heat until bubbles rise. Over low heat add the  cheese, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until cheese is melted after each  addition. Add salt, Worcestershire sauce and nutmeg. Transfer to fondue pot  and serve with cubes of French bread.
