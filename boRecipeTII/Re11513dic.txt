Raspberry Cream-Cheese Bars
(8 oz.) cream cheese, Butter, Raspberry cake mix, Red food coloring, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In 1 bowl mix butter and cake mix, blending  well. Press mixture into 9x13 inch greased pan.    In another bowl cream together cream cheese, confectioners sugar and eggs.  Blend until creamy. Check bottom of bowl to get all lumps blended in  thoroughly. Mix in 3-5 drops red food coloring. Pour over cake mix. Reduce  oven temperature to 325 degrees. Bake for 35 minutes or until light brown  on top. Cool completely, cut and enjoy. Makes 48.
