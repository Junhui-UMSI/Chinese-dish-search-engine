Tuna Fish Curry
Vegetable oil, Hot cooked rice, Apple juice, Curry powder, Tomato sauce, Tuna, Apple, 
Heat oil in wok . Add apple and stir fry over medium heat, 1-2 minutes.  Stir in curry powder. Add tomato sauce. Bring to a boil, add juice and  tuna. Stir till heated. Serve over hot cooked rice. Source: _Wok Cookery_  by Ceil Dyer posted by Anne MacLellan    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
