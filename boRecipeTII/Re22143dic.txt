Turkey Onion and Tomato Pita Pockets
Iceburg lettace thin slice, Fruity olive oil, Light brown sugar, Red onion, peeled & thin, Balsamic vinegar, 6 in diameter pita bread, Salt and pepper to taste, Cherry tomatoes, quartered, Shredded fresh basil, Turkey, cooked coarsy chopd, 
In a large bowl, toss together the onion, tomatoes, turkey, lettace, and  basil.  Sprinkle with the brown sugar and drizzle with the vinegar and  olive oil. Toss well and season with salt and pepper. Cover and let  marinate in the refrigerator for at least two hours. To make the sandwich,  mound the mixture into the pita pockets and serve at once.
