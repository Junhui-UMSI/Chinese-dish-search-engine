Turkey Skillet Ratatouille
Zucchini; small, diced, Garlic, Onion; halved/thinly sliced, Eggplant; small/pared, Turkey; roasted/cubed, Oregano, Tomatoes; broken/w juice, Salt and pepper to taste, 
Spray skillet with cooking spray.  Combine all ingredients except turkey.  Cover and simmer 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until vegetables are  tender-crisp.  Stir in cubed turkey,  Cook and stir uncovered until nearly  all the liquid is evaporated and the turkey cubes are white (about 3 to 4  minutes). Calories 120 per serving.
