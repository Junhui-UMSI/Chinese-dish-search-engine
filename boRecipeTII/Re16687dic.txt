Alaska Scallops in Cream Sauce with Spinach Fettuccine
Parsley, Shallots, Lemon juice, Dry vermouth, Butter, Pepper, Garlic, Spinach fettuccine noodles, Heavy cream, Salt, Alaska scallops, Cream sauce, CREAM SAUCE, Butter, Mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, Cayenne pepper, 
saute mushroom in butter about 1 minute. Add Alaska scallops and continue  cooking 2 to 3 minutes just until flesh is opaque. Add cream sauce; bring  to simmer and cook 1 minute. Arrange well-drained noodles on serving plate  and top with creamed scallops. Garnish with cheese and parsley.    Cream Sauce: saute shallots and garlic in butter until transparent. Deglaze  pan with vermouth. Add cream; bring to simmer and reduce by 1/3 or until  slightly thickened. Add lemon juice and season to taste with salt and  pepper. Hold warm for service.
