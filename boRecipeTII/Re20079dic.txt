Vanilla Custard Ice Milk
Salt, Sugar, ICE CREAM COOKBOOK-----, Eggs, Milk plus 2 cups, Envelopes clear gelatin, Vanilla, 
Put one cup of the cold milk aside in a bowl and sprinkle the gelatin on  top of it and stir thoroughly. Make a custard of the remaining milk, yolks  of the eggs, sugar, and salt. Add gelatin-milk mixture to the hot custard  and stir until dissolved. When cold, add flavoring. Just before freezing,  add egg whites, beaten stiff.
