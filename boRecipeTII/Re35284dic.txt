Beef and Wild Rice Casserole
Onions, Water, Chicken noodle soup, wild rice, Ground beef, Cream of mushroom soup, 
Brown beef and onion. Drain; place in a 3 qt. casserole. Combine remaining  ingredients and mix well. Cover and bake 1 hour 20 minutes at 375F.,  stirring occasionally.    Taste of Home Ground Beef Collection 1996 Edition
