Toasted Cheese Fingers
Cheese, Mayonnaise, Butter, Worcestershire, Mustard, salt and pepper, bread, Durkees sauce, 
Mix cheese and mayonnaise; add seasonings and mix well. Remove crusts from  bread and cut each slice into 3 strips. Spread one strip with cheese  mixture; top with another strip. Brush with butter. Bake at 400 degrees for        5    minutes.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
