Pork with Cabbage
Vegetable oil, Water, Pork, shredded, Cabbage, shredded, Salt, Soya sauce, 
1. Shred pork and cabbage.    2. Place oil in wok and heat to smoking point. Brown pork in it  (about 3 minutes cooking time). Add cabbage, salt, and soya sauce.  Stir everything well. Add 1/2 cup of water, cover wok and cook all  ingredients 8-10 minutes, lifting wok cover from time to time to stir  mixture.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/cc-wok.zip
