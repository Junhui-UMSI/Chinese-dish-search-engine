The Perfect Tuna Casserole
Cream of mushroom soup, Eggs, Peas, milk, Tuna, Potato Chips, 
*  As this is an old recipe, it calls for tuna packed in oil. ** Slightly  crumble the potato chips.  +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++ ++++++++++++++++++  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.   Blend soup and milk in 1-quart casserole. Stir in tuna, eggs, and peas.  Bake 20 minutes.  Top with chips; bake 10 minutes longer.
