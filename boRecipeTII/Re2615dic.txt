Peanut Brittle
Water, Vanilla, Corn syrup, Raw peanuts, Butter, Baking soda, Sugar, 
Cook sugar, syrup and water to soft ball stage.  Add the peanuts and  continue cooking until the syrup is light brown and gives the hard crack  test.  Remove from fire and add van and soda, mixing well. Pour onto oiled  sheet spreading as thin as possible.  When nearly cooled, wet hands ~--
