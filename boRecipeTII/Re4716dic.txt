Philly Jalapeno Cream Cheese
Sun dried tomatoes (in oil), Cut up peppers (to taste), Cream cheese, 
I take sun dried tomatoes (in oil) & drain them on paper toweling Then,  using scissors, cut up about 1/4 cup [drained] tomatoes (in pieces) cut up  peppers (to taste) mix in w/ 8oz cream cheese put into a nice dish & serve  w/sliced baguettes or crackers    Posted to CHILE-HEADS DIGEST V4 #082 by "Rund, Sharen"  <sharen.rund@lmco.com> on Aug 14, 1997
