Beer Soup
Water, Lemon juice, Vanilla sauce powder, Beer, Sugar, Cold water, -(up to), Egg yolk with, Stick cinnamon, Sugar, Egg white with, milk, 
Add cinnamon to milk & bring to a boil. Remove from heat, add vanilla  powder blended with water; bring to a boil again. Add beer & a little lemon  juice, season to taste with sugar & reheat without boiling. Beat egg yolk  in water; add to soup & whisk lightly over low heat. Beat egg white until  stiff & combine with sugar. Serve hot or cold with snowball garnish.
