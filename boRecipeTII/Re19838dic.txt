Apple and Carrot Cake
Whole wheat flour;, Walnuts, Raisins;, Fresh orange juice;, Margarine;, Dessert apples; peeled cored, Cinnamon;, Carrot; grated, Grated peel of 1 orange, Eggs;*, 
Line the base and grease of a loaf pan with 4 cup capcity. Preheat  the oven to 350 degrees. Place the flour in a bowl and mix well, then  cut in the margarine and stir in the cinnamon. Stir in the walnuts,  raisins, apples and carrot. Add the orange peel, eggs and juice, then  beat well until thoroughly combined. Spoon the mixture into the  prepared pan, smooth the top and bake for about 1 1/4 hours, or until  the cake is well risen and firm to touch.  Turn out on a wire rack.
