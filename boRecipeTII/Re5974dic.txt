Pistacio Nut Dessert
Cream cheese, Pecans, Instant pistacio pudding, Flour, Butter, milk, milk, Cool Whip, LAYER 2, LAYER 3, CRUST LAYER 1, 
Mix CRUST LAYER 1 ingredients until crumbly. Press into pan and bake at      350    for 20 to 25 minutes.Cool.    LAYER 2: Beat and spread on cooled crust.    LAYER 3: Mix and put on top of LAYER 2.    Top with cool whip and nuts.    Recipe by: A FRIEND    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 581 by ctlindab@mail1.nai.net on  Jan 23, 1998
