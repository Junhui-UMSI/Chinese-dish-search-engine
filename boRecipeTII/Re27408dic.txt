Toasted Salted Almonds
Unblanched almonds, Unsalted butter, Cumin, Hot pepper sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Pickling salt, 
In saucepan, melt butter over low heat.  Stir in Worcestershire sauce,  cumin and hot pepper sauce; simmer gently for 5 minutes. Stir in almonds  and coat well.    Spread nuts on baking sheet and bake in 325=F8F oven, stirring  occasionally, for 15 to 20 minutes or until toasted. Sprinkle with salt and  serve warm. Makes 1 1/2 cups. Source: Canadian Living Magazine ch. *Nuts  can be cooled and stored in airtight container for up to 2 weeks. Reheat in  325=F8F oven for about 4 minutes or just until warm.
