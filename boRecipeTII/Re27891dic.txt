Thin Linguine with Escarole, Pine Nuts and Chee
Salt, Garlic cloves, large, sliced, Thin linguine, Escarole head,, Grated cheese, Pine nuts, olive oil, Cayenne pepper, 
Cut escarole into 1" slices.    Prepare pasta as directed on package; drain. While pasta is cooking, heat  a large skillet and add oil. Add pine nuts and stir until light brown;  remove from oil. Add garlic and cook until golden (dont burn). Add  escarole, salt and pepper to oil. Cover and cook just until escarole wilts,  about 3 minutes, stirring once. Mix drained pasta with cheese and escarole  mixture, tossing to coat. Sprinkle with pine nuts and serve with additional  cheese  --
