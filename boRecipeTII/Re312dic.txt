Leeks a la Grecque
Leeks; white And pale green, Water, olive oil, Fresh marjoram Or 1 teaspoon, Cinnamon, Red wine vinegar, Fresh oregano Or 1/2, Salt to taste, Diced tomatoes, Calamata olives; pitted and, Feta cheese; to 1/2 lb,, Minced fresh marjoram And, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Make 2 lengthwise slits in leek and rinse it  in a bowl of water. Arrange leeks in a gratin pan. In a sauce pan combine  water, olive oil, marjoram, cinnamon, red wine vinegar, oregano and salt to  taste. Bring liquid to a boil and simmer 10 minutes. Pour liquid over leeks  to just cover. Arrange a piece of foil over dish and bake 35 minutes, or  until tender. Transfer leeks to a serving plate. Reduce cooking liquid to  1/2 cup and strain over leeks. Chill, covered, 2 hours or overnight. Before  serving, garnish with tomatoes, olives, cheese and fresh herbs.
