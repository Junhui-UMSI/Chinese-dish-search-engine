Watercress or Spinach and Potato Soup
Chicken or meat stock, Salt, Grated cheese, Milk or cream, Potatoes; grated, Washed chopped watercress or, Pepper, 
Heat stock to boiling, add grated raw potato and SIMMER gently for 10 to 15  minutes, stirring occasionally. Just before serving, add milk, cress or  spinach and seasonings. Serve hot with a sprinkling of grated cheese on  each servings. 5 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 352 by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I  Canty) on Dec 9, 1997
