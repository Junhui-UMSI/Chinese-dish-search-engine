Walnut-Raisin Baked Apples
Water, Unsalted butter, Raisins, Honey, Walnuts, Apples, Apple juice, 
Fill cavities of prepared apples with raisins and chopped walnuts. Place  apples in 8-inch square baking dish.  Pour water and apple juice over all.  Brush apple tops with honey and dot with butter. Bake according to  directions in Basic Baked Apples recipe.    FOR GROWN-UPS: For a romantic touch, pour * cup warmed Calvados or  applejack over baked apples and ignite with a match.  Serve with vanilla  ice cream or frozen yogurt.    Source: Mommy Made and Daddy Too by Martha and David Kimmel with Suzanne  Goldenson.
