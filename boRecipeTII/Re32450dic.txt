Basic Bean Curd Soup
Bean curd cakes, Salt, Vegetable stock, 
1. Cut bean curd cakes in thin slices or 1-inch cubes.       2. Bring stock to a boil. Add bean curd and simmer, covered, only to  heat through. Season with salt.       VARIATIONS: Add with bean curd any of the following vegetables:       BAMBOO SHOOTS: 1/2 cup, sliced thin. Simmer, covered, 3 minutes.       BEAN SPROUTS: 1/2 cup. Simmer, covered, 2 minutes.       CHINESE CABBAGE: 1 Cup, cut in 1-inch Sections. Simmer, uncovered, 3  minutes.       MUSHROOMS, FRESH: 1/2 cup, sliced thin. Simmer, covered, 3 minutes.       MUSHROOMS: 8 dried black (soaked), sliced. Simmer, covered, 10 to 15  minutes.       MUSTARD CABBAGE: 1 Cup, cut in 1-inch SectionS. Simmer, uncovered, 3 to        5    minutes.       SPINACH: 1 Cup, cut in 1-inch sections. Simmer, uncovered, 1 to 2  minutes.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4. Downloaded  from G Internet, G Internet.
