Baked Potatoes W/broccoli and Chee
WATER; WARM, BUTTER PRINT SURE, MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, Water to cover, Salt, CHEESE CHEDDER, BROCCOLI FZ, Salt, All Purpose Flour, POTATOES FRENCH FZ, 
1.  PREPARE 1 RECIPE BAKED POTATOES, RECIPE NO. Q-44. SET ASIDE FOR USE  IN STEP 4.    2.  PREPARE 1 1/2 RECIPES CHEESE SAUCE, RECIPE NO. O-1-2, AND 1 RECIPE  BROCCOLI, RECIPE NO. Q-G-3. CHOP COOKED BROCCOLI INTO 1-INCH PIECES;  SET ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 4.    3.  SPLIT POTATOES LENGTHWISE TO 1/2 INCH FROM EACH END; FLUFF WITH FORK.    4.  PLACE 1/2 CUP BROCCOLI INTO POTATO; COVER WITH 1/4 CUP (1-A LADLE)  CHEESE SAUCE TOPPING.  :    NOTE:  ONE B LADLE MAY BE USED FOR SERVING TOPPING. SEE RECIPE NO. A-4.  EACH PORTION:  1 POTATO (6 3/4 OZ), 1/2 CUP (2 1/2 OZ) CHOPPED BROCCOLI,      1/4    CUP (2 1/4 OZ) CHEESE SAUCE.
