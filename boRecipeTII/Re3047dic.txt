Peanut Chicken on a Stick
Water, Creamy peanut butter, Cloves garlic, peeled, Low sodium soy sauce, Arrowroot, Sugar, Rice vinegar, Crushed red pepper, Boned and skinned chicken, 
In blender, combine all. Blend til smooth, transfer to small saucepan. Heat  over medium heat, stirring constantly til thickened, do not boil. Cool.  Pour half of sauce into sealable plastic bag, setting remainder aside. Add  chicken to bag. Seal and marinate 1-2 hours. Soak 4 6-inch bamboo skewers  in water.    Thread chicken on skewers - preheat broiler.    Broil close to heat, 1-2 minutes each side or until cooked through. Heat  reserved sauce. Serve in a bowl for dipping.
