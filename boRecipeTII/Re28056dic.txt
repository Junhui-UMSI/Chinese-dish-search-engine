Baked Chicken with Crab
Surimi seafood, Chablis wine, olive oil, Boneless skinless chicken breast, Margarine, Chicken Stock, White bread, Celery, Onion, Pepper, White pepper, Salt, Poultry seasoning, DRESSING, 
Cut bread into small cubes.    Saute onions and celery in margarine until transparent.  Combine with  bread.    Mix chicken base, poultry seasoning and pepper together.  Add to bread  mixture.  If mix is too dry, add a bit more stock.    Add surimi to dressing mix.    Flatten chicken breast to 1/4 inch; season with salt and pepper.    Spread surimi mix on one side of the chicken.    Roll the chicken into a roll and secure with skewers, toothpicks, or  string.    Brown in oil, then transfer to a pan.  Pour wine over chicken and bake in a  375 oven for 15 - 20 minutes, or until chicken and stuffing are thoroughly  cooked.
