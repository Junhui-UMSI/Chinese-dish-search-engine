Raspberry Ring
Vanilla ice cream, (6 oz) raspberry jello, Boiling water, Pecans, Frozen red raspberries;, Frozen pink lemonade;, 
Drain raspberries, reserve syrup. Dissolve jello in water. Add ice cream by  spoonfuls, stirring until melted. Stir in lemonade and raspberry syrup.  Chill until partially set, fold in raspberries and pecans. Put in 6 C. mold  and chill.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Paula Miller Jacobson" <paulaj@erols.com>  on Apr 1, 1998
