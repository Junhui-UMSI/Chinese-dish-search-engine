Sweet Holiday Yams
Salt to taste, -(up to), Sugar, Cornstarch, Orange juice, Brown sugar, Butter, Sweet potatoes, 
Mix sugar, cornstarch, orange juice, butter and salt. Simmer over low heat  until slightly thickened. Place yams in greased casserole. Pour sauce over  and bake at 350 for 30-45 minutes.
