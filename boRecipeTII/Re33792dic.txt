Sweet and Sour Macaroni Salad
Peeled; seeded cucumbers,, Tomato soup, Vinegar; (wine is good), Green peppers; chopped, Onion, Sugar, Small shell macaroni, Jar diced pimentos; drained, Vegetable oil, 
Put first four ingredients in blender and blend well. Cook macaroni and  rinse well. Mix all ingredients and marinate for 24 hours in refrigerator  before serving, stirring occasionally.
