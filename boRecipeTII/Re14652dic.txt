"Russian" Tea Mix
Ground allspice, Instant tea with sugar and, Lemon Rind, Grated orange rind, Ground cinnamon, Orange breakfast drink, Ground cloves, 
Combine all ingredients, stirring well. Store mix in an airtight container  To serve, place 3 to 4 teaspoons mix in a cup. Add 1 cup boilin water, and  stir well. Yield: about 25 servings.NOTE: Keep Instant Spiced Tea Mix on  hand to stir up when guests drop in during the winter. Its a tasty  warmer-upper, and it makes your kitchen smell good, too. For a thoughtful  gift, package Instant Spiced Tea Mix in an attractive container labeled  with mixing instructions.
