Tostaditos
Chili powder blend,, or to, Vegetable oil, Sea salt,, or to taste, Stale 12-inch tortillas,, 
In a cast iron skillet, heat the oil to the smoking point, then add the  tortilla pieces and fry them. Turn them often, until they are crisp and  evenly colored.    Drain the oil and lower the heat. Sprinkle the chili powder and salt over  the tortilla pieces. Cook, 1 to 2 minutes, tossing constantly.    Cool on paper towels to absorb any excess oil.
