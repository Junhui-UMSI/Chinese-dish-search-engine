Taters Italia
Sugar, Cheddar Cheese, Salt, CYBEREALM BBS 3157858098, Frozen tater tots (16 oz), Stewed tomatoes (16 oz each), Basil leaves, crushed, FROM BRIGITTE SEALING, Pepper, 
1. Preheat oven to 425F. Grease a 2 quart casserole.  2. In large skillet over high heat, combine tomatoes and seasonings  until well mixed. Heat to a full rolling boil.  3. Pour into casserole and arrange frozen tater tots in a single  layer on top. Bake 30 minutes or until bubbly in the center. Remove  from oven and sprinkle with the grated cheddar cheese.
