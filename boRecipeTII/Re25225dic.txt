Tongue Pollaaise
Sliced, cooked tongue, Brown sugar to taste, Blanched almonds, Lemon juice or vinegar, Water, Ginger snaps, crumbled, Chicken fat, Raisins, Salt to taste, Onion, diced, 
>From a National Council of Jewish Women, Asbury Park Chapter cookbook,  circa 1947.    Brown onion in chicken fat.  Add water, lemon juice, brown sugar and salt.  Bring to a simmer.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer until the mixture  thickens.
