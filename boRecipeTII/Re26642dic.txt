Baileys (Extra Rich) - 7
Blended Irish whiskey, Evaporated milk, Instant coffee, Eggs, Pure vanilla extract, Sweetened condensed milk, 
Method: Combine whiskey, vanilla, and coffee in blender. Add milks and  blend for 1 minute. Add eggs and pulse stir (use lowest speed on blender  and turn on-off about eight times) just until smooth and not thick. Makes  about 4 cups. From Alan Burgstahler.
