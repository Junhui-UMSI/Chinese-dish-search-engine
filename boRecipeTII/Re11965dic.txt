Recipe- Brocolli Strata (Lacto)
Teaspoon(s) Paprika, Cup(s) Egg Sub, Second, Ounce(s) Cheddar, Healthy, Tablespoon(s) Onion flakes, Choice FF, Slice(s) White bread, Oz pkg, Cup(s) Milk skim, Teaspoon(s) Mustard dried, Nature  (8 eggs), Tablespoon(s) Parsley fresh, Ounce(s) Broccoli frozen, 10, 
Cut bread into 1/2" cubes.  layer bread cubes, brocolli, & cheese in 12 x 8  Pam-sprayed pan.  combine eggs, mlk, onion, mustard, and cheese. pour over  bread & broc. cover and chill over night. sprinkle with paprika and bake,  uncovered @ 325F 55-60 minutes. decorate with parsley. serve hot.    Nutritional Analysis    Calories  ( 320 cal) Protein (34.5 g ) Total Fat ( 2.5 g )    Calories from  Protein: 43 %  Carbohydrate: 50 %  Fat: 7 %    Posted by tkreider@census.gov (Tom Kreider) to Fatfree Digest [Volume 13  Issue 12] Dec. 12, 1994.
