Killer Brownies
Butter, Unsweetened Chocolate, Eggs, Sugar, Vanilla, All-purpose flour, Semisweet chocolate chips, Pecans, 
Melt the butter and chocolate, combining well- set aside and cool. Combine  eggs and sugar beating until fluffy. Add to chocolate mixture. When  thoroughly combined, gently fold in the flour. Pour into greased or sprayed  9 X 13 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.    NOTES : Instead of butter use low fat margarine Instead of eggs use 4 egg  whites, beaten until frothy Instead of pecans use walnuts    Recipe by: Home and Family 4/8 - 4/12 1996    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #909 by "Dorothy Tapping"  ~~ltunicorn41~~at;geocities.com> on Nov 14, 1997
