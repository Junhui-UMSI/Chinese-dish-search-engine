Walnut and Wild Rice Chicken Salad
Lemon Peel, Shredded cooked chicken, Lemon juice, Pepper, White and wild rice blend, Green Onions, Plain yogurt, Salt, olive oil, Chopped Diamond? walnuts, 
Cook rice according to package directions. Cool. In a large bowl, combine  yogurt, green onions, olive oil, lemon peel, salt, lemon juice, and pepper.  Stir in rice, chicken and walnuts. Refrigerate until serving.    Recipe by: Diamond?    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Barbra<barbra@pipeline.com> on Feb 19, 1998
