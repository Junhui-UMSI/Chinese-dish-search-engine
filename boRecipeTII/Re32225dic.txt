Barbecued Spare Ribs
Brown sugar, Lemon juice, Vinegar, Water, Fat, Onion, Chili sauce, Spare ribs, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Place ribs in baking dish and bake in 350 oven 30 min.Chop onion;brown in  fat or salad oil. Add juice, vinegar, worcestershire sauce, brown  sugar,water, chili sauce, salt and pepper if you wish. Cook slowly 20  min.Pour over ribs; continue baking 1 hour.
