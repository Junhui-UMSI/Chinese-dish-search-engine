Whoopie Cup Cakes
Crisco, Baking soda, Flour, Vanilla, Sour Milk, Cocoa, Sugar, Flour, Crisco, Salt, FILLING, Butter, milk, CUP CAKE, Vanilla, Sugar, 
*** Cup Cake ***    Sift dry ingredients into a mixer bowl. Add crisco, milk and vanilla. Beat  hard for 2 minutes. Pour into paper lined muffin tins. Bake 20-25 minutes  in a preheated 350oF oven. Makes 12-16.    *** Filling ***    Mix flour and milk and cook until pasty, cool. Combine sugar, vanilla,  crisco and butter. Add to the flour mixture and beat until fluffy for 10-15  minutes. With a sharp knife, cut off the top to make a cone shape 1" in  diameter. Fill with filling and replace the top.    Source: "The Yankee Kitchen" 04-08-93 [#3] Stella
