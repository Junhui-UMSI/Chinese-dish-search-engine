Turkey Shortcakes
Chicken broth or milk, Process cheese, Pepper, Salt, Cubed cooked turkey *or*, Minced onion, instant, Canned boned turkey (opt.), 
Place cheese and broth in double broiler.  Cook over boiling water until  cheese is melted, stirring occasionally to blend. Add remaining  ingredients, seasoning to taste.    Serve over hot toast, hot biscuits or corn bread.
