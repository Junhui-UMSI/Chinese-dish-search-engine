Beef Ziti Casserole
Dried basil leaves, crushed, (16 oz.) DelMonte cut green, (11 oz.) Green Giant Niblets, Ground beef, Garlic powder, Ziti macaroni, uncooked, (14 1/2 oz.) DelMonte stewed, Pepper, Shredded sharp Cheddar, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cook ziti according to package directions;  drain. Return cooked ziti and vegetables (green beans and corn) to cooking  pot used to cook ziti. Meanwhile, in 10 inch skillet over medium heat, cook  beef until browned, stirring to separate meat; drain fat. Stir in soup,  tomatoes, basil, pepper, and garlic to meat. Heat thoroughly. Add soup  mixture to ziti mixture; mix well. Spoon into greased 13 x 9 inch baking  dish. Cover with foil. Bake 15 minutes. Uncover; sprinkle with cheese. Bake  5 minutes more or until cheese melts.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #186 by Julie Bertholf  <jewel1@ix.netcom.com> on Jul 18, 1997
