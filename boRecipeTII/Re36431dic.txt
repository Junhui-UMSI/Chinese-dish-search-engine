Beer Cheese
Sharp cheddar cheese grated, Onion minced, Stale beer, Cayenne pepper, Jar creamed horseradish, Garlic, Tabasco sauce, 
1.  Combine onion, grated cheese, 5-6 dashes tabasco sauce, horseradish, a  shake of red pepper, and garlic to taste. Add stale beer until you get the  consistency of a cheese dip.    2.  Usually served with crackers, nacho chips, and/or celery.    Difficulty    : very easy. Precision  : no need to measure.
