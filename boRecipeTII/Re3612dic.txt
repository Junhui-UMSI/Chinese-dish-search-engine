Peche Alla Piemontese
Butter, Ripe peaches, Italian macaroons, crushed, Sugar, Egg yolk, Heavy cream, optional, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.    Rinse, halve and stone the peaches. Puree 2 of the peaches in a food  processor. Place the puree in a bowl add the sugar, crushed macaroons and  the egg yolk. Cover each peach halve with this filling and place the peach  halves in a buttered baking dish. Sprinkle the remaining butter over the  peaches and bake for about 45 minutes. Serve hot or cold, with or without  heavy cream.    Recipe by: Cooking Live Show #CL8903 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #651 by  "Angele and Jon Freeman" <jfreeman@netusa1.net> on Jun 30, 1997
