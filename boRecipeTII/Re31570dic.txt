Sweetheart Fudge
Salt, Butter or margarine, milk, Baking cocoa, Vanilla, Sugar, 
In a heavy saucepan, combine sugar, cocoa and salt. Stir in milk; bring to  a rapid boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Cook, without stirring,  unitl a candy thermometer reads 234 degrees (soft-ball stage). Remove from  the heat; add butter and vanilla (do not stir). Cool to 110 degrees. Beat  with a spoon until fudge thickens and just begins to lose its gloss.  Immediately spread into a buttered 8-in. square pan. Cool. Cut into 1-in.  squares.
