Baked Stuffed Apples
Golden seedless raisins, Frozen orange juice, Concentrate, Orange peel -- grated, Water -- very hot, Red apples -- tart, Butter -- softened, Light brown sugar, 
Wash, core and stem the apples, but dont peel them.  Stand them in a  buttered mold and stuff them with 2/3 cup of the brown sugar, the raisins  and the orange peel.  fill the tops of the core cavities with butter and  sprinkle the remaining sugar over the tops.  Place the mold in the slow  cooker and pour the hot water into the cooker.  Sprinkle the orange juice  concentrate over the apples.  Cover the cooker and cook on Low for 3 to 5  hours, or until the apples are tender.
