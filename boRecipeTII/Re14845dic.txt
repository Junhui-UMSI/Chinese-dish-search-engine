1948 Original Bbq Rauce
Tabasco sauce; to taste, Molasses, Onion, Clove; ground, Tomato paste, Butter, Salt, Lemon, Allspice; ground, Can whole  tomatos (14 1/2oz, Bay leaf, Celery, Cl Garlic; minced, Dry mustard, Green pepper; chopped, Vinegar, Fresh celery leaves; chopped, 
Saute onion and garlic in butter in a saucepan until tender. stir in  remaining ingredients; bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, 30  minutes; stir occasionally. Discard bay leaf and lemon slices. Process  through a food processer if desired. Use sauce for basting  and as a side  dish for dipping. Yield 3 cups.
