Sweet Kugel
Dry curd cottage cheese, Eggs, Cream cheese, milk, Nutmeg, Cinnamon crisp crackers;, Sugar, Egg noodles; (cooked &, Sour cream, Margarine, Vanilla extract, 
Beat until creamy, cottage cheese, cream cheese, butter & sugar. Add Eggs  one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Add vanilla to milk & blend  in along with sour cream, & nutmeg. Add Noodles, & pour into a deep  casserole dish that has been sprayed lightly with cooking spray. Top with  crushed cinnamon crackers to completely coat the top. Bake@ 350? 3/4 to 1  hour.
