Ziti Casserole
Salt, ziti, Pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, Egg, Tomato sauce, Ricotta cheese, 
Cook ziti and drain. Combine Ricotta or cottage cheese, Mozzarella,  Parmesan, egg, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Layer ziti, cheese mixture  and tomato sauce in 13 x 9 x 2 inch casserole, starting and ending with  sauce. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
