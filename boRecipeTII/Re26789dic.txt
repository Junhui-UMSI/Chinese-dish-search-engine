Baked Apples a Lorange
Cooking apples, Lemon juice, (4-inch) stick cinnamon;, Reduced-calorie orange, Water, Unsweetened apple juice, Cloves, Gingersnap cookies; crushed, 
Core apples to within 1/2 inch from bottom; peel top third of each  apple.  Place apples in a 12" x 8" x 2" baking dish; add apple juiceand  next 4 ingredients.  Bake, uncovered, at 350F for 30 minutes, basting often  with cooking liquid.  Remove apples from oven; spoon equal amounts of  marmalade into center of each apple, and sprinkle with gingersnap crumbs.  Bake an additional 5 minutes or until apples are done.    Yield: 6 servings.
