Pesach Brownies
Vanilla sugar, Matzo cake meal, Sugar, Cocoa, Salt, Margarine, 
Melt margarine, add cocoa  stir till dissolved.  Cool.    Beat eggs till light, beat in sugars.  Beat in cocoa mixture. Mix in matzo  meal.    Put into greased 9"x13" pan.  Bake 25-30 min. at 350F.    Recipe By     : Annice
