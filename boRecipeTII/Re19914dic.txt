Apple Brandy Butter
Fresh unsweetened applesauce, Ground cloves, Orange blossom honey, Ground cinnamon, Sugar, Calvados, 
Place all ingredients in heavy saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat,  stirring frequently. Cook for about 15 minutes, or until mixture begins to  thicken. Pour into hot sterilized jars, leaving 1/4 inch headspace. Vacuum  seal. Makes 6 1/2 pint jars.   From Gourmet Preserves, Judith Choate.
