Sweet-And-Sour Sauce
Chinese white rice vinegar, Fresh ginger, shredded, Orange marmalade, Ginger marmalade, Salt, Hot water, 
COMBINE ALL THE INGREDIENTS in a small bowl. Be sure to mix them  thoroughly. Transfer the mixture to a small dish if it is to be used at  once, or put in a jar and refrigerate until needed.    This is a subtle and tasty sweet-and-sour sauce which can be used for any  deep-fried foods, such as the Fried Wonton. It keeps well in a tightly  sealed jar in the refrigerator. Makes 1/2 Cup
