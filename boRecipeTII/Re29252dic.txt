Baked Squash with Rosemary and Honey
Honey, finely chopped, cut in 6 serving pieces, Softened butter, Butternut squash, cleaned, Fresh rosemary, 
Preheat oven to 375 F.    Place squash pieces cut side down in a greased baking pan. Bake 35 minutes  until softened.  Turn squash over.    Combine butter, honey and rosemary and spread a spoonful of the mixture  over each squash piece.  Bake for 20 minutes longer, until bubbly and  tender.    Shepherd writes:  "Rosemary, honey and butter make the squash both sweet  and savory, a delicious flavor combination."    Recipe developed by Renee Shepherd and Fran Raboff. From 1992 "Shepherds  Garden Seeds" catalog.  Pg. 40. Posted by Cathy Harned.
