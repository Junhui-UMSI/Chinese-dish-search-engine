Quick To Fix Southwestern Rice
Jar; (8-oz) chunky salsa, (11-oz) corn with red and, (15-16 oz) black beans;, Water, Tortilla chips; (optional), Quick-cooking rice; uncooked, 
Combine water and salsa in large skillet over medium-high heat; bring to a  boil. Stir in rice, beans and corn. Remove from heat; cover and let stand  10 minutes. Fluff with fork; serve immediately with tortilla chips if  desired.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 667 by "Karen Coe"  <ayla@duo-county.com> on Feb 1, 1998
