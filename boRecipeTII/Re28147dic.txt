Baked Custard
WATER; WARM, Salt, EGGS SHELL, MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, NUTMEG GROUND, IMITATION VANILLA, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, 
PAN:  12 BY 20 BY 2 1/2-INCH STEAM TABLE PAN    TEMPERATURE:  325 F. OVEN    1.  RECONSTITUTE MILK. HEAT TO JUST BELOW BOILING. DO NOT BOIL.  SET ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 3.    2.  COMBINE EGGS, SUGAR, SALT, AND VANILLA IN MIXER BOWL; BEAT THOROUGHLY.    3.  GRADUALLY ADD MILK; BLEND THOROUGHLY.    4.  POUR 1 GAL OF MIXTURE INTO EACH UNGREASED PAN.    5.  SPRINKLE TOP OF MIXTURE LIGHTLY WITH NUTMEG.    6.  BAKE ABOUT 45 MINUTES OR UNTIL CUSTARD IS FIRM. COVER; REFRIGERATE  UNTIL READY TO SERVE.
