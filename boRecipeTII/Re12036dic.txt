Zippy Bbq Sauce
Vegetable oil, Dry Red Wine, Worcestershire, Bottle (12-oz) chili sauce, Crushed rosemary or oregano, Cider vinegar, Sugar, Onion; minced, Tabasco sauce, Clove (large) garlic; minced, Salt, 
From:    Cheryl Miller <csmiller@LAMAR.COLOSTATE.EDU>    Date:    Sun, 28 Jul 1996 22:48:56 +0000  In a small saucepan saute onion and garlic in oil until tende.  Stir in  remaining ingredients.  Simmer, uncovered, stirring every 15 minutes or  until slightly thickened. Turn into hot, sterilized wide-mouth jars. Cover  tightly.  Great as a basting sauce for pork or beef.  Keeps refrigerated up  to 4 weeks. Makes 2 1/2 cups.    EAT-L Digest 28 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
