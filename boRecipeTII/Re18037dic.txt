Vegetarian- Risi E Bisi [A Velvety Rice Dish]
Short-grain Italian rice, Fresh parsley, Chicken Stock, Smoked ham, Garlic, Parmesan cheese, Onion, Frozen green peas, Butter, Pepper, 
For best flavor, make this Italian rice with peas risotto recipe with  prosciutto ham and Parmigiano Reggiano cheese.    In saucepan, add 2 cups of water to stock; bring to simmer and maintain  simmer.    In large saucepan, melt butter over mediumheat; cook onion and garlic for 4  minutes. Stir in rice; reduce heat to medium-low. Add chicken stock, 1/2  cup at a time; cook, stirring, until each addition is absorbed before  adding the next, for about 30 minutes or until rice is tender but still  firm.    Lightly toss peas, Parmesan cheese, ham and parsley with rice. Season with  pepper to taste.    Transfer to serving platter. Per serving: about 330 calories, 15 g Protein,  8 g fat, 48 g carbohydrate good source of calcium    Source: Canadian Living [magazine] Nov 94 also appears: "Light & Easy  Multicultural Cooking" Mighton House 1994, by Kay Spicer      [-=PAM=-]         PA_Meadows@msn.com
