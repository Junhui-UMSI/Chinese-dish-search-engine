Albacore Vegetable Pilaf
Can chicken stock, Each,diced red pepper, green, Lemon juice, Florets and corn  OR, Pepper, zucchini, broccoli, Can white tuna, Uncooked white rice, Thawed frozen vegetables, Sour Cream, Pepper, Water, Dill, Garlic powder, Salt, 
1. In heavy 1 1/2 qt. saucepan (with a tight lid) combine rice chicken  stock, water, lemon juice, dill, salt, pepper and garlic powder. Bring to a  boil, cover, reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes. 2. Stir in vegetables,cover  and continue cooking 5 more minutes or until all the liquid is absorbed. 3.  Blend in sour cream and Starkist Albacore. Serve hot or cold.
