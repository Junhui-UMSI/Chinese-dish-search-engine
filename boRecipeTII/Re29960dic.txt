Teriyaki Steak Appetizers
Steak, sirloin, Salad oil, Onion, Garlic, Honey, liquid, Soy sauce, Ginger, 
Cut steak into long 1/16 inch strips across the grain. Combine remaining  ingredients in large flat pan; add steak strips. Marinate strips for  several hours. Lift strips out of marinade; shake to remove excess  marinade.  Thread strips on metal skewers.  Place on grill; broil over  mediaum heat just until well browned, turning and brushing with marinade.  Serve hot.
