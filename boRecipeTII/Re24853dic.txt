Auntie Ruths Wash Day Beans
Ham bone, Worcestershire Sauce, Onion; sliced or chopped, Canned tomatoes, Clove garlic; finely chopped, 
This recipe for "Auntie Ruths Wash Day beans***(NJMH36B) should appeal to  many on the Forum and is a very flexible recipe. By trial & error you can  fix the amounts of each ingredient to suit your own taste. Wash dried beans  (my family likes baby limas or navy) Cook according to package directions.   Then add: Simmer all day, adjusting ingredients to taste. Serve with  chopped onions and corn bread for toppings. Nothing fancy-just Midwest  good! FROM: ALICE JOHNSON (NJMH36B)
