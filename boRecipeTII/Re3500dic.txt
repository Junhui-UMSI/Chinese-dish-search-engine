Pecan Pie #09
Vanilla, Pecan halves, Butter or margarine, Large Beaten Eggs, Pecan Pie Pastry Shell, Dark Corn Syrup, Unbleached flour, Sugar, 
In a small nonmetal bowl micro-cook butter or margarine, uncovered, on  100% power for 30 seconds to 1 minute or till melted.  Stir in beaten  eggs, corn syrup, sugar and flour.  Micro-cook, uncovered, on 50% of power  about 5 minutes or till slightly thickened, stirring every minute.  Stir  in vanilla.  Turn into pecan pie pastry.  Arrange pecan halves atop pie.  Micro-cook, uncovered, on 30% power for 6 to 7 minutes or just till set,  rotating the dish a quarter-turn every 2 minutes.  Cool before serving.    From Better Homes & Gardens "Microwave Cooking for One or Two".  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mwc-4-2.zip
