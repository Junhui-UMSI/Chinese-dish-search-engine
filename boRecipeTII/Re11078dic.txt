Rainbow Trout Teriyaki
Brown sugar, Sesame seeds, Garlic, Ginger, fresh, Lemon juice, MARINADE, Vegetable oil, Soy sauce, Lemon peel, Trout, 
Combine marinade ingredients and pour over trout. Cover and refrigerate  about 30 minutes. Remove trout from marinade. Broil 4 inches from heat  source for about 3 to 5 minutes or until fish flakes with a fork. Remove  from oven and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Serve immediately.    About 226 calories per 4-oz serving.    * Or use 1/3 cup bottled teriyaki sauce for marinade.    Source: Best Recipes     May/June 1991
