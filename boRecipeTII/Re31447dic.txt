Barbecue Sauce
Worcestershire Sauce, Dry mustard, Lemon juice, Vinegar, Cayenne pepper, Chili sauce, Onion, Chili powder, Butter or margarine, melted, Brown Sugar, 
Saute onion in butter until tender; stir in remaining ingredients,a nd  simmer 15 minutes. Use to baste pork when grilling.    Yield: 3 cups    Recipe by: Louise Pittenger, Winchester,TN (Tennessee Magazine 5/97) Posted  to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #612 by Dorothy Tapping <ctapping@USIT.NET> on May  14, 1997
