Vlf Lasagna
Oven-ready lasagna noodles, (10-oz.) of LF silken tofu, Oregano, A few black olives, sliced, Nutritional yeast, TVP granules, Zucchini, chopped, Jar (32-oz.) of your, Garam-masala*, FF grated parmesan cheese, 
*garam-masala: Mine consists of black pepper, coriander, cumin, clove,  cardamom, and cinnamon. I grind in a coffee grinder (reserved for spices,  of course) and keep it on hand. Not an essential ingredient, but my family  likes it (its been my "secret" ingredient in spaghetti sauce for as long  as Ive been married--31 years!).
