Peppered Bread Sauce
Roughly crushed, Whole black peppercorns,, Fresh white breadcrumbs, Bay leaf, Onion, peeled, Salt & freshly ground black, Single cream, Butter, Cloves, Pepper, milk, 
Here are some recipes from the Safeway magazine that Ive formatted into  meal master.    Stud the onion with the cloves.Place in a pan with the bay leaf, milk and  peppercorns. Gently bring to the boil and then remove from the heat. Cover  and stand for 20 minutes. Strain the milk into a clean pan and stir in the  breadcrumbs. Simmer gently for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until  thickened, then stir in the cream and butter. Season to taste and spoon  into a serving bowl.
