Penne with a Spicy Creamy Tomato Sauce
olive oil, Penne pasta, Cloves minced garlic, Heavy cream, Crushed dry red pepper, Fresh parsley, Vodka, Canned crushed tomatoes, 
Saute garlic and pepper flakes over a medium heat until golden brown. Add  tomatoes. Once the sauce comes to a boil, lower the heat and simmer for 15  minutes. In another pot, you should boil the penne to al dente. Drain and  put back into the hot pot. After the 15 minutes is up on the sauce, add the  cream and Vodka. Bring to a boil and heat through for one minute. Add sauce  to the pot with the penne. Add parsley and stir.
