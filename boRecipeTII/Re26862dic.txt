Baked Beans #01
Worcestershire to taste, (16-oz) pork and beans, Bell pepper; chopped, Ketchup, Brown sugar, Barbecue sauce, (16-oz) tomatoes, Onion; chopped fine, Mustard, 
Mix all except beans with 3/4 cup water. Add beans. Bake 4 hours or until  desired thickness. Begin cooking at 200  for 2-3 hours. Bake at 300-350 for        1    hour.
