Warm Cherry Tomato Salad
Garlic, Black Pepper, Rosemary sprigs, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Chopped fresh rosemary, Balsamic vinegar, GARNISH, Assorted cherry tomatoes;, 
In a large non-stick skillet heat vinegar, oil, and garlic over moderate  heat until just simmering. Add tomatoes, pepper, and salt to taste and  cook, tossing, until slightly tender and heated through, 1 to 2 minutes.    Toss tomatoes with chopped rosemary and garnish with rosemary sprigs.
