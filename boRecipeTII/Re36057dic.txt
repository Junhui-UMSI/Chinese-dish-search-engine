Beef Porcupine Balls
Rice-A-Roni beef, Ground beef, Egg, Water, Vegetable oil, 
1.Combine rice-vermicelli mix, ground beef and egg. Shape into twenty  1-1/4-inch meatballs.    2.In large skillet, over medium-high heat, brown meatballs in oil; drain.    3.Slowly stir in water and Special Seasonings. Bring to a boil. Cover;  reduce heat to low. Simmer 30 minutes or until meatballs are cooked.    Recipe by: Serves 4    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Barbra<barbra@pipeline.com> on Feb 04, 1998
