Cheese Garlic Biscuits
milk, Cheddar cheese, shredded, Butter, Chives, dried, Crushed garlic, Bisquick, 
Preheat oven to 450F. Melt butter with garlic. Set aside and keep warm. Mix  baking mix, milk, chives and cheese until a soft dough forms and beat  vigorously for 30 seconds. Drop dough by spoonfuls on ungreased baking  sheet.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven.  Immediately, brush garlic butter mixture over warm biscuits before removing  from baking sheet.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Oct 04, 98
