Barley Soup
Sm Parsnip, sliced thin, Shredded carrot (opt), Chicken Stock, Pearl Barley, Rib Celery, chopped, Salt (opt.), Small Onion, sliced in rings, Small strip Kombu *, Dried basil, Ground white Pepper, Bite-size cooked Chicken mea, 
* Available in health stores, it make beans and peas more digestible.  Combine all ingredients except salt and carrot and simmer until the barley  is cooked, about 45 minutes. Season to taste with salt if needed and  garnish with shredded carrot if desired. Note: Barley soup can be a  soothing and digestible meal for convalescents. Omit the chicken to make it  easier to eat. This soup is rich in protein, carbohydrates, and fiber.
