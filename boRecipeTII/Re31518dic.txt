Sweet-Potato Fritters
106 mg cholesterol, NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION/SERV, Eggs, 5 g protein, 157 calories, 5 g fat, Flour, Vegetable oil, Sweet potatoes, Onion, 217 mg sodium, 23 g carbohydrate, 
1.  Spread potatoes and onion on clean dish towel; roll up, twisting towel  to extract excess moisture from vegetables.    2.  In bowl, whisk eggs, flour, 1/2 t salt and 1/4 t pepper until smooth    3.  In large nonstick skillet, heat 1/4 cup oil over medium-high heat.  Ad  vegetables to batter; mix.  Spoon mixture into skillet, allowing heaping 1  T for each fritter; with back of spoon, flatten slightly.  Fry, six  fritters at a time, 4 minutes.  Turn; cook 2 to 4 minutes.  Drain on paper  towels; keep warm while frying remaining fritters, adding more oil if  necessary.
