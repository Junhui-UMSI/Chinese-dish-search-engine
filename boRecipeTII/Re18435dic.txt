Victoria Fudge
Chopped candied pineapple, Sugar, Chopped figs, Sweet cream, Almond or rose flavoring, Butter, Chopped candied cherries, Coconut, 
Combine cream, butter, and sugar.  Boil to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F).  Remove from fire.  Cool to room temperature. Beat until creamy. Add chopped  fruit and coconut. Add flavoring. Pour into well-buttered shallow pan. Cut  in squares. Mrs. Martin Nelson, Homestead, MI.
