Zabaglione Con Frutta
ZABAGLIONE, Fresh rasberries, Sugar, Fresh strawberries, Crushed Italian macaroons, Marsala wine, Egg yolks, 
1. Clean and hull berries. Divide into four wine goblets.    2. Pour zabaglione over fruit and garnish with crushed macroons. Directions  for Zabaglione: Mix eggs, sugar, and Marsala. Cook in a double boiler,  whipping continuously until soft peaks form.    LELLO    EAST 54TH STREET, MANHATTAN    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of Italian Recipes>.
