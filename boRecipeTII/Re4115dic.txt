Peppers and Squash
Green zucchini, cut into, olive oil, Salt, Tomatoes, cut up, Vinegar or lemon juice, Basil leaves, Pepper, Green peppers, cut into, Yellow zucchini, cut into, Capers, Sugar, Oregano leaves, 
In a medium skillet, cook green peppers and squash in oil for 5 minutes or  until tender. Add remaining ingredients; mix lightly. Simmer, covered, 5  minutes or until tender.
