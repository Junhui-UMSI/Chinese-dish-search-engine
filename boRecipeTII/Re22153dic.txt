Turkey Pasta Florentine
Fat free parmesan cheese, Jar fat-free spaghetti, Leftover turkey, chopped, Chopped spinach, (10 oz.), Egg white, lightly beaten, Fat-free shredded mozzarella, Fat-free ricotta cheese, Jumbo pasta shells, cooked, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    2. Mix spinach, cheeses and egg until well-blended. Fold into chopped  turkey.    3. Fill each shell with heaping tablespoon of mixture. Place in 12 X 8-inch  baking dish. Spoon sauce over shells and cover with foil. Bake 30-40  minutes.
