Baked Cheddar Olives
Flour, Cayenne pepper, Butter, Cheddar cheese, olives, pimiento-stuffed, 
1. Drain olives and pat dry with towels. Combine cheese and butter in a  bowl.    2. Add flour and cayenne and blend until well mixed.    3. Drop the dough by tablespoons onto wax paper and wrap or mold each piece  of dough around an olive, covering it completely.    4. Bake the wrapped olives on a baking sheet in the middle of a preheated  400f oven for 15 minutes or until golden.
