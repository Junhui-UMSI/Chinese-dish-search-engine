Angel Hair Pasta with Lemon and Garlic
Freshly squeezed lemon juice, Grated parmesan, Olive oil from jar of, Dry white wine, Chopped sun-dry tomatoes(the, Freshly ground black pepper,, Garlic cloves, peeled and, Chopped fresh basil, Angel harpist, cooked, kept, 
PREPARATION: Saut garlic in olive oil, but be careful not to burn it.  Remove pan from heat to add wine. Return to heat and reduce by half. Stir  in lemon juice and tomato.  Toss with pasta, adding basil, parmesan and  black pepper.  Serve immediately.   Serves 4.
