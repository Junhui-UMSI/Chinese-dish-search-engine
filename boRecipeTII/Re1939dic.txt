Chocolate Cake To Live for With Ultimate Chocolate Icing
Baking soda, Sea salt, Cinnamon, Unsweetened dutch cocoa, Unsweetened dutch cocoa, Maple sugar; or evaporated, Vanilla soy milk, Water, Canola oil, Maple syrup, Apple cider vinegar, Vanilla extract, Almond extract, ULTIMATE CHOCOLATE ICING, Unsweetened dutch cocoa, Maple sugar, Sea salt, Boiling water, Vanilla extract, Kudzu; dissolved in, Water, Or 2 tsp arrowroot with 2, Whole wheat pastry flour, Unbleached white flour, Baking Powder, 
Line two oiled 9-inch round cake pans with parchment paper.    Sift the dry ingredients into a large bowl. Whisk to mix.    Mix the wet ingredients with a wire whisk until foamy.    Pour liquid ingredients in to dry ingredients and mix gently but thoroughly  until batter is smooth. This is a VERY THIN batter. Pour batter into pans,  dividing evenly. Bake on center rack of preheated oven 25-30 minutes or  until center of cake springs back when lightly touched.    Cool cake layers in pans for 10 inutes, then turn layers out of pans onto  cooling racks. Wrap the layers tightly in plastic wrap and refrigerate  before assembling the cake.
