Polish Cheesecake #2
Sour cream, (21-oz) pineapple pie, Cornstarch, Vanilla or almond extract, CHEESE BATTER, COOKIE DOUGH, Eggs, Sugar, milk, Cottage cheese, Margarine, Sugar, Baking Powder, Cream cheese, Flour, Eggs, Butter, 
From: ramiska@joyce.oit.umass.edu (Richard A Miska)    Date: 6 Jun 1995 12:02:16 -0600  Cookie Dough: Mix flour, baking powder, sugar and margarine.  Work as for  pie dough.  Then add beaten eggs and milk.  Pat in pan which has been  greased on bottom and sides. Cover dough with pineapple pie filling.    Cheese Batter: Beat all ingredients well.  Pour over dough.  Bake at 350  degrees for about one hour.  Test with knife in center to see if done.  Use  9x13 inch pan or 11x17 inch pan depending on how high you want the cake.
