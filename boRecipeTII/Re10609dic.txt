Quick Picante Salad Toss
Freshly shredded cheddar, Ripe olive slices, Bottled Italian dressing, Pace Picante Sauce, Torn romaine lettuce, Corn chips, Crisply cooked bacon slices,, Freshly shredded Monterey, Tortilla chips, Tomatoes, cut into thin, 
Combine lettuce, tortilla chips, tomatoes and olives in large bowl. Combine  Pace Picante Sauce and dressing in screw-top jar or small bowl; shake to  mix well. Pour over salad; toss lightly. Sprinkle with cheese and bacon.  Serve immediately with additional Pace Picante Sauce.
