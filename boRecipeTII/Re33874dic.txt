Sweet and Sour Steamed Cabbage
Butter, Sugar, Salt, Cabbage, cabbage, Vinegar, cabbage, 
until tender.  Stir in butter.              Combine sugar and vinegar. Pour  over cabbage and heat thoroughly. Makes 6 - 8 servings.
