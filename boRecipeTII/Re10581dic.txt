Quick Onion Cheese Bread
Scallion; chopped, Sesame seeds, Sugar, Cheddar, stale; grated, Baking Powder, Beer, Egg, Flour, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Combine flour, baking powder, sugar, cheese,  and scallions.  Stir beer in gently to form a thick, sticky batter. Pour  batter into greased loaf pan, 8-1/2 x 4-1/2 inches. Brush top of loaf with  beaten egg and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Bake in oven for 1-1/4 hours or  until loaf browns. Remove from oven and cool on a wire rack.
