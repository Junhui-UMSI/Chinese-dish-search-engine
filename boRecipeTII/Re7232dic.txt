Pork and Winter Melon Soup
Winter melon, Stock (see recipe) or water, Salt, Lean pork, Egg, 
1. Cut pork in 1/2-inch cubes. Cut winter melon in 1-inch cubes,  discarding rind and seeds.       2. Place pork, winter melon and stock in a saucepan. Bring slowly to a  boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 30 minutes.       3. Stir in salt. Beat egg, and stir in quickly. Remove from heat.       VARIATIONS: For the pork, substitute raw chicken or smoked ham, diced,  or      1/4    cup dried shrimp (soaked).       In step 2, add 3 dried black mushrooms (soaked), diced; or 5 Water  chestnuts, diced.       In step 2, bring stock to a boil first; then add pork and winter melon.  Simmer, covered, 10 minutes.
