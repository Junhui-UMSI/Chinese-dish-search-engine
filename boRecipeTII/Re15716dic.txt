A&w Root Beer
; (McCormick is best), Root beer concentrate, Hot water, Cold selzter water, Root beer concentrate, Granulated sugar, 
1.  Dissolve the sugar in the hot water. 2.  Add the root beer concetrate  and let cool. 3.  Combine the root beer mixture with the cold seltzer  water, drink  immeidately, or store in refrigerator in tightly covered  container. Makes 5 cups.
