Apple Pie Cake #1
Salt, Nutmeg, Hot water, Egg, Flour, Baking soda, Diced apples, Butter, Sugar, Vanilla, Pecans, Cinnamon, 
Combine ingredients in order given. (Batter will be thick). Bake in a  greased 9-inch pie pan for 45 minutes at 350F. Serve warm with whipped  cream or ice cream. Delish.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /CAKES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
