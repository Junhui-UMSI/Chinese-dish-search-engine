Allisons Cookies
Sugar, Margarine; cut in pieces, Vanilla extrace, milk, Cocoa, Pecans, Peanut butter, Uncooked oats, 
In a microwave-safe dish, stir together the sugar, milk, cocoa and  margarine. Microwave on HIGH for 2 minutes, stirring once. Add peanut  butter, oats, nuts and vanilla. Mix well and drop by heaping teaspoonfuls  onto wax paper. Let dry several hours and store between layers of waxed  paper in an airtight container.
