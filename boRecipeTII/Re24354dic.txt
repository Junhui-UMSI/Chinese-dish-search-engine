Triple-Chocolate Candy-Cane Kisses
semisweet chocolate, candy canes, chocolate bars, 
Melt the 3 kinds of chocolate in 3 separate bowls.  Stir half of the  crushed candy into the semisweet chocolate.  Reserve a bit for sprinkling;  stir the remainder into the milk and white chocolates.  Drop teaspoons of  the semisweet onto a parchment-lined tray.  Top with 1 dollop each of the  milk and white chocolates.  Sprinkle with crushed candy.  Let harden.    Reprinted from Food and Wine Magazine - January 1997.
