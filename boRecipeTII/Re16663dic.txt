Alabama-Style Shrimp Bake
Butter, Hot sauce, Fresh rosemary, Worcestershire Sauce, Dried Rosemary, Lemon juice, Ground red pepper, Garlic, Lemons, Onion, Coarsely ground pepper, shrimp, Salt, 
Combine first 9 ingredients in a small bowl; set aside. Rinse shrimp with  cold water; drain well. Layer shrimp, lemon slices, and onion slices in an  ungreased 13x9x2-inch baking dish. Pour butter mixture over shrimp. Bake  uncovered, at 400 for 20 to 25 minutes or until shrimp turn pink, basting  occasionally with pan juices. Garnish with fresh rosemary sprigs, if  desired.
