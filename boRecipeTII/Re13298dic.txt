Xmas Candied Nuts
Water, Ground cinnamon, Ground allspice, Pecans, Sugar, Unsalted roasted cashews, Blanched almonds, 
Combine sugar, water, cinnamon and allspice in large, heavy bottomed  saucepan. Bring to boiling; boil over medium heat until reduced to 1/3 cup.  Add nuts all at once; stir quickly to coat all nuts. (You have to work  fast.) Spread candy on baking sheet, breaking up large clumps of nuts with  spoon. After candy cools, break apart into individual nuts. Store in  airtight tin at room temperature.
