Babi Ketjap (Pork in Soy Sauce)
Onion, large, Ginger, fresh (1"), Ketjap manis, Water, Pork loin, Garlic, Thai chile, 
Calories     per serving: 344 Fat grams per serving: 16 Approx. Cook Time:  2:00 Cook the meat in one piece in a covered pan in a moderate oven (350 F)  until done.  Remove from the oven and cool. When cool anough to handle, cut  very fine (1/8" by 1/2"). Grind together the onion, garlic, ginger and  pepper in a food pro- cessor. Fry in meat drippings until onion is golden  (approximately 15 to 20 minutes).  Add ketjap manis, water, and diced meat.  Simmer about 20 minutes.
