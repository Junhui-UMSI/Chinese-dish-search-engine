Peanut Butter Milk
Nonfat dry milk solids, Sugar, Peanut butter, Cold water, 
Combine milk solids and sugar; add cold water, and shake or beat until  smooth. Add a small amount of mixture to peanut butter and mix until  smooth. Continue adding milk until all the mixture is used. Chill  thoroughly. Yield: 6 servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
