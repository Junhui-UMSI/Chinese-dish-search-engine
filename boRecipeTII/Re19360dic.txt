Vegetable-Bulgur Pilaf
Dried tarragon, Garlic, Celery, Onion, Zucchini, Carrots, Red bell pepper, Fresh mushrooms, Salt;, Chicken/beef/ vegetable, Bulgur wheat, Cooking oil, 
In a 2-quart saucepan cook chopped onion, chopped celery, sliced  mushrooms, and garlic in hot oil about 5 minutes or till vegetables  are tender but not brown.  Stir in sliced carrots, sliced zucchini, chicken, beef or vegetables  broth, red or green pepper strips, bulgur, dried tarragon, and salt.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes  or till vegetables are tender.  Makes 4 servings.
