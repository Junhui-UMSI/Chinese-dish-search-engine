Sweet Potato Shepherds Pie
Egg yolks, Ground cinnamon, Garlic, Tomato paste, Raisins, Crushed red pepper flakes, Bread crumbs, Ground beef, Sweet potatoes, bacon, Salt to taste, Spanish onions, crushed tomatoes, canned, 
Beat mashed potatoes with yolks until smooth; set aside. Brown ground beef,  drain if needed, and add onions and garlic. Cook for 5-6 min. Add tomatoes  and paste and cook 2-3 more min.  Remove from heat and add bread crumbs,  raisins, and spices, mixing well. Turn meat mixture into a lightly greased  pan.  Top evenly with potatoes. Bake for 1 hr. at 375 degrees or until top  is browned.  Cut into even squares and sprinkle each with bacon or chopped  scallions.
