Beckys Favorite Rum Cake
;Water, cold, Eggs, Vegetable oil, Rum, dark (80 proof), Sugar, granulated, Butter, GLAZE, Pecans, Water, CAKE, Rum, dark (80 proof), Yellow cake mix, 
Preheat oven to 325F. Grease and flour 10" tube or 12-cup Bundt pan.  Sprinkle nuts over bottom of pan. Mix all cake ingredients together. Pour  batter over nuts. Bake 1 hour. Cool. Invert on serving plate. Prick top.  Spoon and brush glaze evenly over top and sides. Allow cake to absorb  glaze. repeat till glaze is used up.    Glaze: Melt butter in saucepan. Stir in water and sugar. Boil 5 minutes,  stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Stir in rum.    Optional: Decorate with border of sugar frosting or whipped cream.
