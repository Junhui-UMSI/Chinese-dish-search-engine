Pepper Shrimp From Lhj
Water, Minced fresh scotch bonnet;, PEPPER BUTTER, Salt, Green Onions, Small shrimp in shells, Butter, Allspice berries; crushed, Crumbled dried habanero*, Fresh scotch bonnet chile;, Habanero chile; or, 
1. Combine water, green onions, chile, salt and allspice in large saucepan.  Bring to boil; boil 10 minutes. Add shrimp and cook 3 minutes more.    Drain. Serve with Pepper Butter to dip.    2. Make Pepper Butter: Heat butter with minced chile in small saucepan  until melted.    *Wear rubber gloves while handling and chopping these extremely hot chiles.
