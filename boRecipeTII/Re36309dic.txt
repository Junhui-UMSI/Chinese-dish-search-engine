Beef with Green Peppers
Bell pepper cut in thin, Soy sauce, Cornstarch, Sugar, Beef; cut in thin strips, Soy sauce, Grated fresh ginger -or-, Ground ginger, Salt, Sherry, Shortening or oil, 
Dredge beef with cornstarch, sherry, 1 Tbs soy sauce & ginger. Heat oil &  saute beef strips. When beef changes color, remove to a plate & in  remaining oil saute bell peppers for 1 minute; add salt. Combine beef,  peppers, 1 Tbs soy sauce & sugar. Heat well. Serve on rice.    MRS R.W. (ANN) NIETHAMER    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
