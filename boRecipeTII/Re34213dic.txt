Bayou Blast - (Emerils Creole Seasoning}
Garlic powder, Salt, Dried Oregano, Black pepper, Onion powder, Paprika, Cayenne pepper, Dried thyme, 
Combine all of the above ingredients thoroughly. This recipe yields 2/3 cup  of Emerils famous "Bayou Blast" Creole seasoning.
