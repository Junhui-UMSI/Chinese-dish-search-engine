Tomato Rarebit
Finely grated., 30 g mature cheddar cheese, Cayenne pepper., 1 oz 30 g wholemeal bread., Sprig of parsley to garnish, Worcestershire sauce., Tomato sliced., 
1. In a small bowl mix together the cheese, Worcestershire Sauce and  cayenne pepper. 2. Toast the bread lightly and arrange the sliced tomato on  top. Spoon the cheese mixture over the surface and grill until bubbling and  golden. 3. Garnish with a sprig of parsley and serve at once. Preparation 2  minutes, cooking 5 minutes.
