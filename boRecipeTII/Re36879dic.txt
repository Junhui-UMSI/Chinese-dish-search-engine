Strawberry Shake
Honey, Cold Milk, Strawberries, Strawberries, Plain yogurt, 
Puree 1/2 cup strawberries and honey in blender or food processor. Add  milk and yogurt; blend until smooth. Pour into glasses; garnish each with  a whole strawberry. Yield about 2 cups.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmstrawb.zip
