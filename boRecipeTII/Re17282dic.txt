Wainwright Inn Puffed Pancake with Black Cherries and Almond
Sliced almonds, Flour, Eggs -- fresh, Thawed, Powdered sugar, Frozen black cherries --, Vanilla, Juic, Lemon -- grated rind and, milk, Stick unsalted butter, 
Preheat oven to 475 degrees.  Whisk together eggs, milk, lemon rind and  vanilla.  Add flour and beat until batter is smooth.  Place butter in  greased baking dish and place in oven until butter melts.  Pour in batter  and bake 20 minutes until puffed and golden.    Remove from oven and sprinkle with powdered sugar and lemon juice. Offer  black cherries, almonds and additional powdered sugar as toppings.    Makes 8 servings.
