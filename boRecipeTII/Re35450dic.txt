Beef in Rubesco Wine
Tomato juice, Of fat cut from some parma, olive oil, Chump or braising Steak, cut, Rubesco Wine, Cloves garlic, unpeeled, Chopped Fresh Sage or, Few Sprigs sage and parsley, Salt and pepper, TO GARNISH, Wild Mushrooms, roughly, 
Preheat oven to 180C/350F/Gas Mark 4.    Heat olive oil in a large frying pan and add meat, sealing and browning  over a high heat. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer meat to a large  casserole dish, adding a little more olive oil. Pour in wine and add tomato  sauce. Pop in sage, parsley and garlic cloves. Cover and cook over a low  heat or in preheated oven for 90 minutes or until the beefs really tender.    Towards the end of the beefs cooking time, saute the Parma Ham to render  it down. Add mushrooms and saute briskly for 4 to 5 minutes. Season with  salt and pepper and add a pinch of chopped sage and parsley.    Add to beef, stir well and serve with fresh pasta.    Source: Floyd on Italy
