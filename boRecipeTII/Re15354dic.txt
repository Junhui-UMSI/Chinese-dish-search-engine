White Beans with Tomato, Basil, and Parmesan
Garlic, Tomato, Lemon juice, Great northern beans, olive oil, Basil, Parmesan cheese, 
In a skillet, heat oil over low heat.  Add garlic and cook, stirring  occasionally, for 4 minutes or until lightly colored and fragrant.  Stir in  the beans, tomato, and basil and cook for 7 minutes or until heated through  and the tomato has released its juices.  Stir in cheese and lemon juice.
