Banana Oat Bread
Bananas; mashed, Bread flour, Whole wheat flour, Water, Oats; old fashioned rolled, Brown sugar, Vegetable oil, Boiling water, Salt, Yeast, 
Stir the oats into the boiling water and let sit for 10 minutes. Place in  bread pan and add remaining ingredints.  Bake on basic setting. 122  calories and 2 grams fat.
