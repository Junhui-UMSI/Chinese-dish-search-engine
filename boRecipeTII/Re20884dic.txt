Apple Muffins #1
Baking Powder, Peeled; chopped apples, Nutmeg, -(up to), Flour, Sugar, Salt, Cinnamon, Egg, Vegetable oil, milk, 
Combine dry ingredients. Combine egg, milk and oil; add to dry mixture;  stir just to moisten. Add apples and mix. Fill greased muffin cups 2/3  full. Do not use cupcake papers. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes. Serve with  honey. Add more sugar if not served with honey. Makes 12.
