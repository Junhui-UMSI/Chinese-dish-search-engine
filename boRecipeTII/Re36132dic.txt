Beef Spread
Dry sherry, Green olives; finely diced, Cream cheese, Loaf cocktail rye bread, (3-oz) smoked sliced beef;, Mayonnaise, Dried; (instant) minced, 
In a small bowl, soak dehydrated onion in sherry. In another bowl, blend  cream cheese with mayonnaise. Add onion. Stir in smoked beef and green  olives. Chill. It tastes better if made ahead. Serve on a good cocktail rye  bread.    Source: Per Roberta Brisson, my girlfriend, Cindy C.    Posted to recipelu-digest by QueenBerta@aol.com on Feb 3, 1998
