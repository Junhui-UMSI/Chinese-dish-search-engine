Sweet Potato Sandwich Loaf
Applesauce, Orange zest; grated, Active dry yeast, Salt, Wheat bran; or flour, Sweet potato; or yams,, Water; *NOTE, Bread flour; or all purpose, 
Makes a nice, soft sandwich loaf.  This is for a 1 pound loaf. *NOTE add 1  to 2 tablespoons as needed for Welbilt/Dak On Basic Bread Cycle: Add  ingredients to machine according to manufacturers directions.
