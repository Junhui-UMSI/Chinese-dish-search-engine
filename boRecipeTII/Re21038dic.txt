Twice Baked Cheese Potatoes
Chives, Beans -- of choice, Cheddar cheese -- shredded, Onion -- chopped, Fat-free sour cream, Spinach -- chopped, Potatoes* -- baked, Temperature, Fat-free sour cream, Egg whites -- at room, Chives, Fat-free cottage cheese, 
Preheat oven at 375.  Prepare a 13 x 9 x2" pan with cooking spray.  Bake  potatoes 1 to 1/4 hours or until tender.  Cool just until easy to handle.  Cut potatoes lengthwise into halves; scoop out pulp, leaving thins shells.  Place potato pulp and beans in blender; cover and blend until smooth. Mix  potato mixture and remaining ingredients except 2 tablespoons sour cream  and 2 tablespoons chives.  Divide mixture among potato shells.  Bake  uncovered 15 to 20 minutes or until hot and light brown.  Top with sour  cream and chives.    Recipe By     : Anita A.
