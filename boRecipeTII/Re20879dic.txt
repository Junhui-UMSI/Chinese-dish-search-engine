Apple Muffins
AM Sunflower Seeds, Cinnamon, Eggs or egg replacer, Allspice, AM Whole Wheat Flour, Sea salt (optional), Honey, Water, Non-alum baking powder, Apples; grated (with peel), -to..., Vegetable oil, Nutmeg, Vanilla, 
Beat honey, oil, eggs and water.  Mix dry ingredients and add to honey  mixture.  Fold in apples, vanilla and nuts.  Fill oiled tins 2/3 full.  Bake at 400 F. 12 to 15 minutes.
