In saucepan, stir together 2 cups Arm & Hammer baking soda & 1 cup  cornstarch, add 1 1/4 cups water. Cook over medium heat, stirring  constantly, until mixture is consistency of moist mashed potaotes.  Turn out on a plate & cover with a damp cloth until cool enough to  handle. Roll to 1/4" thickness. Cut shapes with cookie cutters. Press  hole near top of each ornament with toothpick. Allow to dry on flat  surface & harden overnight. Paint, decorate & protect with a shiny  glaze.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
