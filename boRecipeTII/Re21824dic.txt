Apple-Nut Pancakes
milk, Sugar, Cinnamon, Flour, Walnuts, Peeled apple/cubes, Egg, Baking Powder, Salt, Cloves, Oil or melted margarine, 
Preheat griddle, 350F (180C) or medium high. Put all ingredientes in the  process at MIX until batter is almost smooth.    Bake on lightly greased griddle until nicely browned. Turn only once.  Yield: 10-12 pancakes.
