Swiss Hazelnut Crescents
Ground hazelnuts; (or, Confectioners sugar, Butter or margarine, Karo light or dark corn, Vanilla, CINNAMON COATING, Ground cinnamon, Unsifted flour, 
In large bowl with mixer at medium speed, beat margarine until softened.  Slowly beat in corn syrup and vanilla. Stir in flour and nuts. Chill one  hour. Shape dough by rounded teaspoon into 2-inch long rolls. Roll in  Cinnamon Coating. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet, forming  crescents. Bake in 350 degrees oven for 15 to 18 minutes or until bottoms  are slightly browned. Remove from cookie sheet; cook on rack. Makes  approximately 4 dozen. Cinnamon Coating: Stir together confectioners sugar  and ground cinnamon.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
