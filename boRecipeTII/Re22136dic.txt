Turkey Noodle Bake
Chopped Green Bell Pepper, milk, Soft Bread Crumbs, Butter or margarine, Chopped Pimentos, Cooked Turkey; Cubed, Eggs, Condensed Cream Of Mushroom, Cooked Noodles, American Cheese, 
Blend milk into soup. Stir in the eggs. Add rest of ingredients, mix, and  turn into a 12 x 71/2 x 2 inch baking dish and bake at 350 degrees for  30-40 minutes or until a knife inserted off-center comes out clean. Cut  into squares to serve.    Serving Ideas : I would put some peas into the recipe also.    NOTES : This is a great recipe for left-over turkey.
