Peachy Keen Coffeecake
egg, Baking soda, Flour, Wesson oil, Sugar, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Peach pie filling, Buttermilk, salt, Baking Powder, Vanilla, 
In large bowl mix together flour, oil, sugar, cinnamon & nutmeg until  crumbly.  Remove 1/2 cup mixture; set aside. Add remaining ingredients  except pie filling.  Stir until just mixed. Pour batter into oiled &  floured 8" baking pan. Spoon filling evenly over top of batter.  Sprinkle with reserved crumb mix. Bake 50 minutes or until cake tests  done with wooden pick. Serve warm.
