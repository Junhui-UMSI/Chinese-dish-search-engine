Turbo Margarita
Lime juice, Scoops lime sherbet, Lime slice (for garnish), Tequila, Triple sec, Sweet and sour mix, Crushed ice, 
Blend ingredients in heavy duty blender on high for 60 seconds. Serve  in a large stemmed glass with a lime slice.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
