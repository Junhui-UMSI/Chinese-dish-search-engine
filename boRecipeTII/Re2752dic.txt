Peanut Butter Chip Brownies
Baking soda, Vanilla, Cocoa, Buttermilk, Prunes -- 1 jar of baby, Whole-wheat flour, Food, Sugar, All-purpose flour, Peanut butter chips, Egg whites, Boiling water, 
Preheat oven to 350F.  Beat egg whites until foamy. Stir in buttermilk,  prunes and vanilla.  Set aside. In a large mixing bowl, combine cocoa and  baking soda; ass boiling water and stir until mixture thickens. Stir in  egg-white mixture until smooth.  Add sugar and flours; blend completely.  Stir in peanut butter chips. Pour batter into a 13x9 inch baking pan  lightly ocated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake for 30 min. Let cool  before cutting.    Recipe By     : Shape - Dec 1994
