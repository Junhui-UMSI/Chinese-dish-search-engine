Beef Stew with Vegetables and Potatoes
Round steak, Celery, Dry Red Wine, Onion, Crushed red pepper, Green olive oil, Italian herb seasoning, Garlic, Carrots, Fresh parsley, Fresh mushrooms, tomatoes  , Onion, bay leaf, Potato, 
In a large skillet, saute the chopped onion, garlic, celery, potatoes,  carrots, crushed red pepper, and mushrooms in the oil for 5 minutes. Add  the steak and stir often to brown lightly.    Add the wine and cook for 3 minutes. Add Italian herb seasoning, parsley,  bay leaf, crushed tomatoes, and onion rings, and cook for 10 minutes. Taste  and season with black or cayenne pepper for a more piccante taste.    Remove the bay leaf. Sprinkle with chopped fresh Italian parsley before  serving.
