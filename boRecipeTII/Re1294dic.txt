Dijon Flank Steak
Flank steak, Dijon mustard, Fresh ground pepper, 
Rub flank steak with Dijon mustard. Marinate 6 to 8 hours in the  refrigerator.    Coat both sides of steak with fresh ground black pepper.    Grill 6 to 8 minutes per side or until desired degree of doneness.    Slice across the bias of the grain.
