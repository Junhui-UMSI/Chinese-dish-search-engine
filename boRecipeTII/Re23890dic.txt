Asparagi Parmigiana
Salt and pepper, Stalks asparagus; cleaned, Butter, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 F.    2. Cook asparagus in boiling, salted water for 5 to 7 minutes, or until al  dente. Drain.    3. Melt butter in ovenproof pan. Add asparagus and saute for 5 minutes.  Sprinkle with cheese, add salt and pepper, and bake for 2 minutes.    4. Transfer to boiler and gratinee for 2 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve. Note: Use freshest asparagus available.
