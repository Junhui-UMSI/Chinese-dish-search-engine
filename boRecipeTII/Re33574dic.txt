Swedish Creamed Cabbage
milk, Dry white wine, Butler, Size cabbage; shredded, Flour, Curry powder, salt and pepper, 
Place cabbage and wine in a 2 quart casserole. Microwave at high (100%)  until tender crisp, 6 to 7 minutes.    Place butter in a small bowl. Microwave at high (100%) until melted, 30 to  45 second& Stir in flour. Blend in remaining ingredients. Microwave at  medium high (70%) until thickened, 2 to 3 minutes, stirring twice during  cooking time. Pour over cabbage. Microwave at medium high (70%) until  heated, 2 to 3 minutes. Makes 6 servings.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 219 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 8, 1997
