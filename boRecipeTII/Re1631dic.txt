Caramel Corn
popcorn, Sticks butter, Brown sugar, Light corn syrup, Baking soda, Vanilla extract, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 200F. Boil together butter, brown sugar and corn syrup. Add  baking soda and boil for 5 full minutes. Be careful to stir this  constantly, it will want to boil over the sides of the pan. Remove from  heat after 5 minutes and add vanilla extract and salt. Line a pan with foil  and bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. When done, pour out onto  another piece of foil or cooking parchment and allow to cool When cool,  break into pieces.
