Turkey (Or Beef) and Corn Dumpling Casserole
Salt, milk, Sage, Vegetable oil, Beef broth, Flour, Egg, A green pepper, chopped, Dried Rosemary, Baking Powder, Salt, Vegetable oil, Garlic, (8 ounces) tomato sauce, DUMPLINGS, Ground turkey (or lean, Onion, Corn meal, 
In a heavy skillet brown the turkey (or beef) in oil, breaking up meat with  spoon, about 5 minutes or til lightly browned.  Add remaining ingredients,  except the dumplings.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover and, stirring  occasionally, simmer for 15 minutes.  Meanwhile, mix all the ingredients  for dumplings in large bowl. Drop by tablespoonfuls into the meat mixture.  Cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until the dumplings are firm.  Makes  about 4 servings.
