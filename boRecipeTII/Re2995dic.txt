Peanut Butter Spritz Cookies
Salt, Butter, Peanut butter, Powdered sugar, Cake Flour, Shortening, Almond extract, Egg yolk, 
Mix ingredients together. Spoon onto a cookie sheet and bake at 375 degrees  for 8 to 10 minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 394 by "Diane. Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Dec 22, 1997
