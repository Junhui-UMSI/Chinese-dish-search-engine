Apples and Celeriac W Honey Mustard-Dressing
mustard, Green apples, Spinach, Canola oil, Celeriac root, Red onions, Radish, Black pepper, Apple-cider vinegar, Honey, Shallot, Lemon juice, Nonfat yogurt, 
Place the vinegar, mustard, honey, shallots, and pepper in a blender  container. Process on high speed until well mixed. With the blender  running, slowly pour in the oil to form an emulsion.   Trim and wash the celeriac. Cut into a fine julienne and place in a lg  bowl.  Add the lemon juice and combine well to keep the celeriac from  discoloring. Mix in the apples, yogurt, onions, and dressing.   Serve on a bed of spinach with the radishes.   The Healing Foods Cookbook
