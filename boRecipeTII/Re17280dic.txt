Waikiki Meatballs
Onion, Salt, Cracker crumbs, Shortening, Green pepper, Ground beef, Brown Sugar, Pineapple, Vinegar, Cornstarch, Milk, Ginger, Soy sauce, Egg, 
Mix meat, crumbs, onion, egg, salt, ginger and milk.  Shape into balls  .Melt shortening, brown and cook meatballs.  Remove meat and keep warm  ,remove fat.  Mix cornstarch and sugar.  Stir in reserved pineapple syrup  vinegar, and soy sauce until smooth.. Cook stirring until boiling.  Boil  and stir one minute.  Add meatballs pineapple and green pepper, heat  through.
