Swamp Sticks
Green food coloring, (2 pound) carrots; scraped, Container (15 1/2 ounce), 
These are from Southern Living 1995 Annual Recipes.    Cut carrots lenghtwise into thin strips. Gather strips into a bundle;  secure with a rubber band. Set bundle aside. Combine dip and coloring,  stirring well. Spoon onto plate. Place carrot strip bundle upright in  middle of plate. Tie raffia around bundle to conceal band. (18 appetizer  servings)    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Suzanne Blosser <SHBlosser@AOL.COM> on Sep 17,  1997
