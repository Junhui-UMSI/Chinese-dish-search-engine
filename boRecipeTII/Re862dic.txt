Honey Dijon Deviled Eggs
Hard-cooked eggs, Purchased honey Dijon salad, Minced green onions, Pepper, if desired, 
Remove shells from eggs; halve lengthwise or crosswise. Carefully remove  yolks and place in small bowl. Mash yolks with fork. Add salad dressing and  green onions; mix well.  Lightly spoon or pipe mixture back into egg white  halves.  Sprinkle with pepper. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Yield 12  servings.
