Quick Chicken Pie
Cream of chicken soup, Broth, Boiled eggs; sliced, Flour, milk, Chicken, Stick butter; softened, 
Boil chicken until done. Debone and reserve 2 cups broth. Layer in oblong  baking dish. Add sliced eggs. Combine cream of chicken soup and broth.  Bring to a boil and pour over chicken and eggs. Mix flour, milk and butter.  Pour evenly over layers. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 minutes.
