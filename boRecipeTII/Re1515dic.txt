Brisket
Onion Soup Mix, Parsley, Garlic powder, Honey, Vegetable oil, Brisket, Wine; Kosher Sweet, 
Recipe By : Sharon Mart    Preheat oven to 325?. Put meat in roasting pan. Pour wine in a 2 c.  measuring cup. Add honey and stir well. Sprinkle parsley, soup mix and  garlic powder over meat. Pour on wine mixture.    Bake for 2 hours. Baste. Add water or wine if needed. Cool a little and  slice.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by SuperSecDD@aol.com on Sep 27, 1998,
