Vegetarian Mince Pies
Lemon rind, grated, Fresh ripe pears, chopped, Mixed dried fruit, Nutmeg, Candied peel, Glace cherries, Allspice, Flaked almonds, Whisky or other liquid, Dates, chopped, Powdered ginger, Orange rind, grated, 
Mix all the ingredients together & let stand for 1 to 2 hours. Store in  air-tight jars.  Use as regular mincemeat.    Rose Elliot, "The Vegetarian Christmas Cookbook" Sent by Mum.
