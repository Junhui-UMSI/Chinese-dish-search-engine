Turkey Crunch
Jar sliced pimento, drained, Butter, Cream of mushroom soup, Diced cooked turkey, Chow mein noodles, Onion, Mushrooms, Celery, chopped, milk, Poultry seasoning, Green pepper, chopped, 
Combine turkey, mushrooms, celery, onion, green pepper, pimento and  poultry seasoning into 2 quart casserole greased with butter. Blend  together mushroom soup and milk and pour over the turkey mixture. Sprinkle  chow mein noodles over top. Bake in slow (325 degree) oven for 30 minutes.    Mrs. William W. Prentice    From a book of recipes compiled by members and friends of the LONDONTOWN  PUBLIK HOUSE ASSEMBLY, EDGEWATER, MD 21037.
