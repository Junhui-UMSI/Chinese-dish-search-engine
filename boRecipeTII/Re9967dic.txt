Purely Pork Chops
Salt, Paprika, CHOP MOP (OPTIONAL, SOUTHERN SUCCOR RUB, Center Cut; Bone-In Pork, Smoked habanero (my, Dry mustard, Black Peppercorns; Ground, Brown sugar, Vinegar; Cider Or White, Cayenne, Bbq Sauce, 
Recipe By: Cheryl and Bill Jamison (Smoke and Spice)    Combine the rub ingredients in a small bowl. Massage the chops with the rub  and place in a plastic bag in the frige for 2 to 4 (or more) hours. Get  your smoker ready - 200 to 220 degrees. Remove the chops 30 minutes before  youre ready to smoke them and let them come to room temperature. Warm the  Chop Mop. Sear the chops in a heavy skillet and transfer to smoker. Cook  for 55 to 65 minutes, basting with the Chop Mop and turning as appropriate  for your smoker. (Bring to an internal temperature of 160 degrees.)
