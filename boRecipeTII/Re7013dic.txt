Popcorn--Cajun Corn
Unpopped popcorn (8 cups, Butter, Lemon pepper, Onion powder, Popped), Paprika, Garlic powder, Ground cayenne pepper, 
Pop popcorn.  Melt butter and add the spices.  Drizzle over the unseasoned  popcorn and toss until well blended.
