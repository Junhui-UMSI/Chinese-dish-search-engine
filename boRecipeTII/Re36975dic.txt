Strawberry-Banana Jell-O Salad
Banana/strawberry Jello mix;, Instant banana pudding mix;, Cool whip; defrosted in, Fruit cocktail; drained, Walnuts or pecans; chopped, 
Prepare the Jell-O as directed on the package. Refrigerate for 3 or 4  hours.  Then add all other ingredients (pudding is dry). Stir, leaving  small chunks of Jell-O. Put back in the refrigerator unti ltime to serve.    Orginal Posters Note: This appeared in one of the food columns in the Food  Section of the Orange County Register Today.
