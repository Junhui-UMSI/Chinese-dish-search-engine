Pickled Pickerel
White vinegar, Whole allspice, Sugar, Capers, Onions, sliced, Pickerel, Lemon, sliced, Whole black pepper, 
"The Complete Book of Pickles and Relishes" by Leonard Louis Levinson.    Cut fish into small fillets; cover with water. Add onions, pepper,  allspice, sugar and lemon. Boil until fish is nearly done; then add vinegar  to equal amount of liquid remaining. Boil till done; add capers and seal in  hot, sterilized jars. Pike or crappies may be used instead of pickerel.
