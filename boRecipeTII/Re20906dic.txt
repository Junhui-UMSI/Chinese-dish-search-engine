Apple Nut Squares
Baking soda, TOPPING, Butter, Evaporated milk, Vanilla, Brown Sugar, Pared; chopped apples, Pecans, Eggs, SQUARES, Salad oil, Flour, Sugar, 
Squares: Combine sugar, oil, eggs, soda, flour, apples, vanilla and nuts.  Mix well; batter is stiff. Spread evenly in a greased 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan.  Bake for 1 hour in a preheated oven at 325 degrees. Cake can be iced while  still warm. Topping: Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan. Cook over  low heat. Bring to a boil and cook for 2 minutes longer. Spread topping on  squares. Cool cake squares completely before cutting.
