Baked Chicken #2
Black pepper, Soy sauce, Chicken; sliced, Thyme, 
submitted by: edmon@cc.xu.edu.ph  I noticed that recently a lot of chicken recipe had been submitted here. We  have this recipe that is very easy to prepare and is always loved by  guests.    Put the pieces of chicken in a Pyrex Rectangular dish. Rub with Pepper and  Thyme and Pour Soy Sauce over it. Let it stand for 1 hour.  Preheat oven at  375 degrees and bake for 45 minutes.
