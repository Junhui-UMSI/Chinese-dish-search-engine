Tuna Surprise
Salt to taste, Shortening, Crushed dried red pepper, Fluffy rice, Tuna; drained and flaked, Garlic clove; crushed, Rhubarb; sliced, unsweetened, Coconut milk, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Bay leaf, 
Make coconut milk by bring 1 cup milk and 1 cup water to a boil, add 1 cup  shredded coconut and let stand for 5 minutes and strain.    Heat shortening in heavy skillet, add red pepper and bay leaf, and cook 2-3  minutes.  Add tuna, cinnamon, garlic and cook 5 minutes. Stir in coconut  milk, rhubarb, brown sugar, and salt to taste. Simmer for 10 minutes or  until sauce has thickened.  Serve over hot fluffy rice.    From:  The Tuna Cookbook
