Hearty Beef Stew / Goulash
stew meat, onion, Carrots, mushrooms, Paparika, Pepper, Ketchup, Vegetable oil, 
Brown meat and saute onions in oil. Add seasonings and ketchup and simmer  covered over low heat for 1 hour. Add carrots and mushrooms and continue to  simmer for 1 hour.    Serve over thick noodles.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Annice Grinberg  <VSANNICE@WEIZMANN.WEIZMANN.AC.IL> on Oct 26, 98, converted by MM_Buster  v2.0l.
