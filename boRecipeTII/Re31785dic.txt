Szechuan Barbecue Pork and Vegetables
Honey, Pork tenderloin; sliced (1/2, Whole baby carrots, Fresh snow pea pods, Barbecue sauce (I prefer, Crushed red pepper flakes, Soy sauce, 
Date: Sat, 2 Mar 96 9:58:23 EST    submitted by: talara@azstarnet.com From: Tucson, Arizona, U.S.A.  Combine barbecue sauce, honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes together.  Set aside.  Spray 12-inch nonstick skillet or wok with cooking spray.  Heat  skillet over medium heat.  Add pork slices, and cook for about 5 minutes  (or until no longer pink), stirring occasionally. Add sauce mixture and  carrots. Cook for about 10 minutes. Add pea pods, and cook an additional 6  minutes (or until pea pods are bright green, and mixture is hot), stirring  occasionally. Serve over angel hair pasta or white rice.
