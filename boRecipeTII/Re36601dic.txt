Strawberry Bread #2
Strawberry jam, Sugar, Flour, Baking soda, Salt, Sour cream, Butter or margarine, Eggs, Nuts (optional), Vanilla, 
Mix sugar, vanilla, butter & eggs, one at a time. Add dry ingredients. Mix  well. Add remaining ingredients. Pour into 2 greased loaf pans. Bake at 350  for 50 minutes.
