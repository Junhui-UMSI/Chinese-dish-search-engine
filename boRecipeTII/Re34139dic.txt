Batter-Fried Zucchini Sticks
Flour, flour, Egg, Vegetable oil, Baking Powder, milk, Zucchini, Salt, Oil for deep frying, 
Sift flour with baking powder and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Combine egg, milk, and  2 tablespoons oil. Slowly add to dry ingredients, beating until smooth. Pat  zucchini sticks very dry and if desired dredge lightly in flour. Dip  pieces, one at a time, in batter. Fry in deep oil heated to 375 degree  until golden brown, 1-2 minutes. Drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with more  salt, if desired.    NOTES : From:  Faith91946    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #723 by Aquasea221@aol.com on Aug 5, 1997
