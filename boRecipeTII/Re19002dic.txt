Vegetable Barley Soup with Mint
Celery, Parmesan cheese, optional, Beef broth, Italian seasoning, Dried rosemary, crushed, Uncooked barley, Diced peeled potatoes, Sliced carrots, Onions, Fennel seed, Garlic, minced, Lemon-pepper seasoning, V-8 Juice (46 ounces), Water, Dried mint, 
Souce: A Taste of Home Magazine Jun/Jul 1996    In a large kettle or Dutch oven, combine all ingredients except the cheese;  bring to a boil. Reduce the heat; cover and simmer for 3 hours. Top each  serving with cheese if desired.    Yield:12-14 servings (3 1/4qts.)
