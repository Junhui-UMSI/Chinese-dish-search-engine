Aunt Ginnys Banana Nut Bread
Sugar, Buttermilk, Eggs, Soda., Vegetable oil, Bananas (mashed), Plain flour, Lemon juice, Pecans (chopped), Salt, 
Dissolve soda in milk.  Mix all ingredients in bowl.  Beat with mixer  well.  Add nuts that have been floured just enough to cover.  Bake at 350  degrees for 45 min. to 1 hr. or until done.
