Athenian Artichoke Dip with Baked Pita Chips
Whole wheat pita bread, Almonds, bell pepper, garlic, Green onions, Feta cheese, Mayonnaise, BAKED PITA CHIPS, Dried oregano leaves, ATHENIAN ARTICHOKE DIP, Artichoke hearts , 
Preheat oven to 350F. Using food chopper, chop artichoke hearts, cheese  and bell pepper; place in 1 quart batter bowl. Using garlic press, press  garlic into batter bowl. Add mayonnaise, green onions and oregano; mix  well. Spook into 8" mini-baker. Top with almonds if desired. Bake 25 - 30  minutes or until golden brown and bubbly. Serve with baked pita chips, if  desired.    Yield: 2 1/2 cups    Baked Pita Chips: Preheat oven to 400F. Using pizza cutter, cut each pita  bread round horizontally in half. Cut each half into 8 triangles. Arrange  pita triangles in single layer on flat baking stone. Bake 8 - 10 minutes or  until lightly browned and crisp.
