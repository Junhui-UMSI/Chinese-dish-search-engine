Sweet and Sour Whitefish
Green pepper, Brown sugar, carrots, Vegetable oil, Tomato sauce, Lemon juice, Onion, Celery, 
This recipe does not exact measurments, You have to adjust the amounts  according to how much fish you are using.    Saute onion, celery, diced carrots, green pepper in 1 tablespoon oil until  tender. Add tomato sauce, lemon juice and brown sugar. Stir till blended.  Pour on top of fish placed in a pan. Cover tightly with foil. Bake 350* for       45    minutes.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Aliza Blizinsky" <alizab@hotmail.com> on  Feb 20, 1998
