Green Chili Beef
Stew meat or steak cut into, (6oz) of Ortega Diced, Onion chopped, Clove garlic minced, Faita seasoning, Salt, 
Place all in crock pot cover with lid and cook all day...serve one warm  tortillas or rice. Top with sour cream, guacamole, and fresh chopped green  onion.
