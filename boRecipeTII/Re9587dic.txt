Pumpkin Dessert
Butter (sweet), Evaporated milk, Cinnamon, ground, Salt, Whipped cream, Ginger, ground, Sugar, granulated, Cake mix, yellow, Eggs, Nuts, Cloves, ground, Pumpkin  , 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Mix together sugar, eggs, pumpkin,  cinnamon, ginger, cloves, salt and milk. Line a 9x13-inch pan with wax  paper and pour the mixture in.    Sprinkle the dry cake mix on top, then sprinkle the nuts. Pour melted  butter evenly over the cake mix and nuts. Bake at 350 degrees F. for 50-60  minutes.    Cool (very important).  Flip over and remove from pan. Remove the wax  paper. Top with whipped cream (which would dissolve if the cake werent  cooled first).
