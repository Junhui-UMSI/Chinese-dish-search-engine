Pseudo-Mexican Coffee
Dark brown sugar, Vanilla bean, Whipping cream, dark roast coffee, Water, Coffee liqueur, cinnamon, 
Place cinnamon in coffee pot.  Scrape in vanilla seeds and add pod.  Brew  coffee with 10 cups water.  Whip cream to soft peaks.  Add sugar and beat  until stiff peaks form.  Pour coffee into cups; add liqueur to taste.  Top  with cream.    ARIELLE@BONKERS.TARONGA.COM    (STEPHANIE DA SILVA)    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.
