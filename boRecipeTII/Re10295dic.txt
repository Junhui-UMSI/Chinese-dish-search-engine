Quick Bean and Potato Soup
Or 3 medium potatoes; peel, Bulgur wheat, Several cloves garlic;, Salt and pepper as required, Onion, Ground cumin, Chopped pickled jalapeno, Water, (16 oz) stewed or whole, Cooked beans (we like pinto;, Oregano, (or more) chili powder, 
Place in pot. Cook until spuds are tender.    Add frozen corn, or canned hominey or whatever sounds good.    Posted to fatfree digest V97 #283 by Jan Gordon <jrg14@cornell.edu> on Dec  2, 1997
