Bacon on Breadsticks
Bacon, Breadsticks; Salted Or, 
1. Wrap a slice of bacon around each breadstick.    2. Place wrapped breadstick in a microwave baking dish. Small microwave  ovens may require making several batches. Times will very according to your  oven and the size of the dish you are using; small batches take about 5  minutes, large ones, about 10 minutes.    For the conventional oven, you can precook the bacon and then wrap around  breadsticks just before warming them up.    Servings: 2 per person.
