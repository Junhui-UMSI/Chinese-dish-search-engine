Zucchini and Lemon Pie
Caraway seed, Sugar, Custard, Eggs, Butter, Flour, Sugar, Pie crust -- unbaked, Lemon rind -- grated, Zucchini -- thinly sliced, Butter, Zucchini filling, Lemon juice, Egg yolks, Lemon juice, 
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Melt the 2 tablespoons of butter in a  skillet and saute the zucchini and remain filling ingredients over high  heat until the zucchini loses it color. Place in the pie shell and bake for  15 minutes. Then reduce the oven temperature to 375 dregees. While this is  baking make the custard by creaming the 6 tablespoons of butter with the  sugar.  Add the 2 whole eggs and 2 extra egg yolks alternately with the  flour and mix well. Then add the lemon juice, dont worry if it looks  funny.
