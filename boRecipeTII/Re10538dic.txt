Quick Lemon Rice Soup
Fresh kale; chopped, Tumeric, Vegetable broth, Garlic powder, Lemon juice, Nutritional yeast, Pre-cooked rice, Onion or 3 scallions;, Water or vegetable broth, Black pepper, Tamari or soy sauce, 
In a large pot, saute onion, kale, and seasonings in 1/3 c vegetable broth  over medium heat for 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 10  minutes longer. Serve warm.
