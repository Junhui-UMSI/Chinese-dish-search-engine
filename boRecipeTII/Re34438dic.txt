Bean Curd with Ginger
Pads tofu, Finely chopped garlic, Hot chilies; chopped, Sesame oil, Tree ears, Chopped scallions, Peanut oil, Sugar, Red wine vinegar, Finely chopped ginger, Cooked fresh peas, 
Cut tofu into 1/2 inch cubes.  Soak tree ears in warm water till soft.  Drain & chop coarsely.  Heat oil in a wok & when very hot add ginger,  garlic & scallions. Add tofu, stirring quickly. Add tree ears, chilies &  peas. Stir. Add sugar & vinegar & toss. Spoon mixture into a serving dish &  sprinkle with sesame oil.  Serve over rice.
