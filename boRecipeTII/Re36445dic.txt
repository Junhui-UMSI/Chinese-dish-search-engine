Beer Marinade for Beef
Wine vinegar, olive oil, Prepared horseradish, Salt, Onion powder, Lemon juice, Garlic powder, Beer (12 oz or 10 oz cans), Ground cayenne pepper, 
Mix all ingredients together and use as a marinade. Then use as a basting  sauce for the meat while it cooks.
