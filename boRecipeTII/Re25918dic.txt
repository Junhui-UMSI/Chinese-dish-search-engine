Bacon and Chive White Sauce
Butter or margarine, milk, Salt, Chives, Bacon, All-purpose flour, 
1. Prepare bacon and set aside.    2. Melt butter in a 1-quart glass measuring picher in mirowave oven (about  30 seconds at High).  Add flour; mix until smooth. Add milk gradually,  stirring constantly.    3. Cook uncovered in microwave oven 4 minutes at High, or until thickened;  stir after 2 minutes, then every 30 seconds.    4. Add crumbled bacon, chives and salt; stir.  Serve over fresh vegetables.    Yield: About 1 cup.    SOURCE: Adventures in Microwave Cooking
