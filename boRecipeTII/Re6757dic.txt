Polk Salad Saut?
Onion; diced, Polk salad; fresh, Baking soda, Bacon fat, Eggs, 
Note: Polk Salad is a spinach type vegetable that grows wild in the woods.  Dissolve the baking soda into a pot of boiling water. Add Polk salad and  cook for 3? minutes. Drain and discard the water. In a FRESH pot of boiling  water, cook the Polk salad until it is limp and looks like cooked spinach.  Heat the bacon fat in a skillet, add onions, the Polk salad, and the eggs,  and saute until eggs are done.
