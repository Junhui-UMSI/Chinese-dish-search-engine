Angel Hair Pasta with Tomato Cream Sauce
Frozen peas, Light sour cream, Angel hair pasta, Marinara sauce, basil, 
Bring a covered large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and  cook according to package directions. Drain. Meanwhile bring marinara sauce  to a boil in a saucepan over med-low heat. Stir in peas, cover and simmer 2  mins. or until peas are tender. Reuce heat to low, add sour cram and stir  until blended. Toss pasta with sauce. Top with basil.
