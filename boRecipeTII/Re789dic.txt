Hash Brown Casserole
Light shredded cheddar, Sour half and half, Salt, Onions; grated, (10 3/4 oz) Healthy Request, Pepper, Corn flakes, Frozen shredded hash brown, 
Mix potatoes, cheese, sour half and half, salt, onion, soup and pepper/ Put  into a 2 qt casserole dish. Top with crushed corn flakes and spray with I  Cant Believe Its Not Butter spray. Bake at 350 for 1 hour 20 mins.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Deb <leadfoot@alpha.ultraweb.net> on Aug  20, 1998,
