1-2-3 Cookies
Mix-ins, Baking soda, Vanilla, Sugar, Flour, Softened butter, Brown sugar, Salt, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 375. Mix flour, soda & salt on wax paper. Mix butter,  sugars & vanilla in bowl until creamy. Mix in eggs. Gradually stir in  flour mix. Add mix-ins; stir to combine. Drop batter by  tablespoonfuls, 2" apart, onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 10  minutes or until golden. Cool on wire rack. Mix-ins: chopped dried  fruits or dates, chocolate chips, raisins, candy coated chocolate,  peanut butter pieces.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
