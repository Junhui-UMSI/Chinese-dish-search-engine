Pork Vindalho
1-inch piece of ginger, Pork, cubed., Cloves of garlic, Cloves, Vinegar (coconut if you can, Dried red chillies, 1-inch piece of cinnamon, Peppercorns, 
Goa is the home of vindalho (vindaloo/vindallo etc.), that much basterdized  dish.  Grind the spices, ginger and garlic in the vinegar.  Marinate the pork in  the mixture for 2 days in the fridge.    Cook, covered, until meat is tender. Do not add water during cooking.  At  end of cooking reduce sauce if necessary - you are aiming for a dryish  consistency.
