Banana Bread
Eggs, Ripe bananas; mashed (up to, Sugar, Baking Powder, Salt, Butter or oleo, Flour, 
Cream oleo and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time. Add bananas, then dry  ingredients which have been sifted together. Pour into well greased loaf  pan. Cook approx. 1 hour at 350 ?. You can also use muffin pans to bake  this, just shorten the cooking time.    Submitted to cookbook by Lurline Braud.
