Barbecued Ribs
Sherry or pineapple juice, Garlic, Honey, Honey, Soy sauce, Porkloin back ribs; (3 to 4), 
Place meat in glass dish. Mix soy sauce, juice, 2 tablespoons honey and  garlic. Pour over meat. Cover dish with plastic wrap. Refrigerate 8 hours.  Remove meat from marinade. Keep marinade. Lace ribs on spit rod. Secure  with fork. Place foil drip pan under spit area. Cook ribs on rotisserie 1  1/2 to 2 hours or until done, basting frequently with re- served marinade.  Brush ribs with 1/2 cup honey last 30 minutes of cooking. Makes 4 servings.
