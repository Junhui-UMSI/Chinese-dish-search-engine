Australian Shrimp on the Barbie
(1 stick) butter;melted, Medium large shrimp;, Spinach leaves, olive oil, Lemon slices, Fresh Lemon Juice, Minced fresh herbs, Cloves (large) Garlic;, Minced shallot, Salt and pepper; freshly, 
Combine first 8 ingredients in large bowl.Mix in shrimp.Marinate at room  temperature 1 hour or in the refrigerator 5 hours,stirring occasionally.  Prepare barbecue with medium hot coals.Thread shrimp on narrow  skewers.Grill until just opaque,about 2 minutes per side.Line platter with  spinach.Arrange skewers on platter.Garnish with lemon and serve.
