Lemon Drop Cookies
WATER; WARM, EGGS SHELL, MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, All Purpose Flour, SYRUP; IMIT MAPLE, #10, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, SHORTENING; 3LB, IMITATION LEMON FLAVOR, Baking soda, Salt, 
PAN:  18 BY 26-INCH SHEET PAN                TEMPERATURE:  375 F. OVEN    1.  SIFT TOGETHER FLOUR AND SUGAR, SET ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 4.  2.  CREAM SUGAR, SYRUP, SHORTENING, SALT, EGGS, AND ADD LEMON FLAVOR AT  LOW SPEED 5 MINUTES OR UNTIL LIGHT & FLUFFY.    3.  RECONSTITUTE MILK; ADD SODA; THEN ADD TO CREAMED MIXTURE. BLEND  THOROUGHLY.    4.  ADD DRY INGREDIENTS TO MIXTURE; MIX ONLY UNTIL INGREDIENTS ARE  COMBINED.  DO NOT OVERMIX.    5.  DROP BY TABLESPOONS, OR THROUGH SIZE 10 PLAIN PASTRY TUBE, IN ROWS 5 BY  7, ONTO LIGHTLY GREASED PANS.    6.  BAKE 14 TO 16 MINUTES OR UNTIL LIGHTLY BROWNED.    7.  LOOSEN COOKIES FROM PANS WHILE STILL WARM.  Recipe Number: H01503    SERVING SIZE: 2 COOKIES    From the <Army Master Recipe Index File> (actually used today!).
