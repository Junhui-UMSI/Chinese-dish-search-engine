Baked Stuffed Yams with Sausages
Lemon; juice and grated rind, Sugar, Louisiana yams; baked, Cinnamon, Pork sausages; cooked, 
Scoop yam pulp into mixing bowl and blend with next three ingredients. If  desired, substitute juice of small orange or 1/2 cup pineapple juice. Whip  fluffy; refill yam shells. top with little pork sausages and return a few  minutes to a hot oven (450 degrees) just before serving.
