Tamale Pie
Pepper, green, Cornmeal, Corn kernels, Cumin, ground, Chili powder, Water, Egg replacer, Olives, black, Tomato paste, salt-free, Beans of choice, Tomato sauce, Oregano, dried, Salt, 
Combine beans, corn, pepper and olives in a 2- to 3-quart glass casserole.  In a small bowl, combine tomato sauce, tomato paste, cumin, chili powder  and oregano. Pour over bean mixture.    In a large glass bowl, combine cornmeal and water. Microwave, uncovered, on  high for 6 minutes. Remove from microwave and stir well. Add egg replacer  and salt. Stir again. Spread over bean mixture; cover and microwave on hgh  for 10 minutes. Let cool slightly before serving. Serves 4.    From the files of DEEANNE
