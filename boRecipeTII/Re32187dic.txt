Barbecued Pot Roast
Salt, Lean pot roast, Onion, Tomato paste, Peppercorns, Worcestershire Sauce, 
From: hz225wu@unidui.uni-duisburg.de (Micaela Pantke) (COLLECTION)    Date: Mon, 6 Sep 93 09:30:43 +0200    From: ynnuf@yetti.amigans.gen.nz (Doreen Randal)    Source: Crockpot Cooking from Barbara Blitz Sprinkle salt over the roast  and place in crock pot. Spread tomato paste over meat; imbed peppercorns  into paste; top with onions and worcestershire sauce. Cover and cook on LOW  8-10 hrs. Serve meat with accumulated gravy.
