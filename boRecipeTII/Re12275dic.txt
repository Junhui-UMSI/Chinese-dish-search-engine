Zucchini Meat Loaf
Basil, Zucchini -- grated, Onion -- chopped, Turkey -- ground, Combined red and green, Bread crumbs, Tarragon, Parsley, Egg white, Cheddar cheese -- grated, Black pepper, Pepper -- chopped, Tomato paste -- (6 oz), 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare loaf pan with cooking spray.  In a mixing  bowl, combine zucchini, turkey, onion, peppers, bread crumbs, egg white,  tomato paste, parsley, tarragon, basil, and black pepper.  Press mixture  into prepared pan and cover with foil.  Bake for 1 1/4 hours.  Sprinkle  cheese over the top.  Return to oven and bake another 10 minutes.
