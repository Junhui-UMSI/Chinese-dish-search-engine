Baked Onaga W/pancetta and Herbs
Carrots, julienne, White wine, Piece parchment paper, Salt, pepper to taste, Leeks, julienne, Whole red snapper, 10 oz or, Sprig fresh thyme, olive oil, Pancetta, sliced, Sprig fresh oregano, 
Clean and remove scales from snapper.    Cut parchment paper large enough to wrap fish.  Place fish on oiled paper,  season with salt and pepper.    Place sliced pancetta and vegetable julienne on fish. Drizzle wine and  olive oil over fish, place herb sprigs on fish and close parchment paper by  folder ends. Bake at 400F unti done. Serve snapper in paper.
