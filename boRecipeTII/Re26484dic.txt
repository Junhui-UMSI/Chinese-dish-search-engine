Tomato Pilaf
Beef broth, Salt, Uncooked long-grain white, Fresh tomatoes (3 large), Sugar, Vegetable oil, Oregano leaves; crushed, Crushed garlic, Black Pepper, 
Use fully ripe tomatoes. Core and chop tomatoes; set aside. In a large  saucepan heat oil until hot. Add garlic; cook until golden, about 1 minute.  Add tomatoes, oregano, salt, and black pepper; cook, uncovered, until  tomatoes are slightly softened, about 4 minutes. Add broth; bring to a  boil. Stir in rice and sugar; simmer, covered, until rice is tender, 15-18  minutes, stirring occasionally. Reheat with additional beef broth if  needed. Serves 4.
