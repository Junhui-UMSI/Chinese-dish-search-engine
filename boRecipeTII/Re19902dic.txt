Apple Black Walnut Cake
Vanilla, Cooking oil, ICING, Unpeeled chopped apple, Black walnuts; chopped, Salt, Eggs, Baking soda, Mrs. Roberta Johnson, Sugar, Flour, Butter, Vanilla, 
Mix sugar and oil.  Add eggs, beat until fluffy.  Sift dry ingredients  together and add to sugra, oil, and egg mixture. Blend in vanilla. fold in  black walnuts and chopped apples. Pour into a well greased and floured loaf  pan.  Bake at 350~F for 40 minutes. ICING: Soften butter and blend with  powdered sugar. Add vanilla. Use cream to blend to a spreadable  consistency.  Spread on cake. NOTE:  For holiday gift giving, I like to  bake this recipe in four miniature loaf pans, wrap in plastic wrap, and tie  with a bow. This is nice with a cup of hot tea. Happy cooking.
