Strawberry Daiquri Punch
Rum to taste (optional), Ginger ale, Cherry sherbert, Can frozen lemonade, Frozen strawberries, 
Partially thaw strawberries.  Put in blender or food processor; puree. Add  lemonade and sherbert; blend. Put in punch bowl and add ginger ale. Top  with strawberries.
