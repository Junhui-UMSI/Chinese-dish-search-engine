Quick Saucy Chicken Casserole
Chicken/turkey; cook; dice, milk, Frozen mixed vegetables, Garlic salt, Poultry seasoning, Bisquick Original or, Cream of mushroom soup, Parsley flakes, 
Heat oven to 450~. Mix chicken, soup, vegetables, poultry seasoning and  garlic salt in ungreased 13x9x2" baking dish. Stir baking mix and milk  until blended. Pour over chicken mixture. Sprinkle with parsley. Bake 30 to  32 minutes or until crust is light golden brown.
