Barbecued Chicken with Glaze
Crushed dried oregano, (8 oz) can tomato paste, Orange juice, Dry mustard, Salt, olive oil, Peppercorns, Honey, Vinegar, Chicken, Minced garlic clove, 
In large screw top jar combine paste, oil, orange juice, vinegar,  salt, peppercorns & garlic. Cover & shake vigorously to blend. In a  shallow dish pour sauce over chicken. Cover & marinate 2 hours at  room temperature or overnight in refrigerator, turning occasionally.  Drain, reserving marinade. Grill chicken over med coals for 45-50  minutes, brushing with marinade & turning frequently. Just before  serving, brush with a mixture of honey & mustard to glaze.
