White Mountain Basic Vanilla Ice Cream
Vanilla, Sugar, Eggs, Heavy whipping cream, milk, Salt, 
Separate the eggs and make a soft custard of the milk, egg yolks and sugar.  Beat the egg whites stiff and over them pour the hot custard and mix  thoroughly. Add the vanilla and salt, when cold, the whipped cream. Freeze  according to your freezer instructions. Makes 2 quarts.
