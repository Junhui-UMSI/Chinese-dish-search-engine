Peachy Lemon Crisp
Firmly packed light brown, Seedless raisins, (3 oz) pkg lemon pie filling, (29 oz) can sliced peaches, Margarine, Graham cracker crumbs, 
Strain peaches, reserving 1 1/3 cups syrup.  In 1 1/2 quart microwaveable  bowl, blend reserve syrup and pie mix. Microwave on high (100%) for about 4  5 minutes or until thickened. Stir in peaches and raisins. In bowl, combine  cracker crumbs and sugar, cut in margarine until mixture resembles coarse  crumbs. Sprinkle crumbs over fruit mixture. Microwave for 2-3 minutes or  until heated through. Serve warm.    From Best Recipes Magazine May/June 1992
