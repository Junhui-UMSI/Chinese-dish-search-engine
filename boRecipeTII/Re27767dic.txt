The Postmodern Mind
Creme de banana, Creme de cassis, Grand Marnier, Midori (melon liqueur), 
In a well-chilled martini glass, layer liqueurs in order given by trickling  each across the back of a spoon whose tip is held against the back of the  glass (its tricky; layers will be fuzzy). Or you can just mix them all up  and serve chilled. Serves 1.    Per serving: 115 calories, 1 grams fat (7 percent fat from calories), 0  milligrams cholesterol, 0 grams protein, 14 grams carbohydrate, 2  milligrams sodium.
