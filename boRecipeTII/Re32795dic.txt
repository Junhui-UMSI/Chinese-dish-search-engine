Sweet Potato Pudding
Evaporated milk, Cinnamon, Butter, Sweet potatoes, Nutmeg, Salt, Sugar, Flaked coconut, Eggs, milk, 
Combine eggs and sugar in a large mixing bowl, beating well; stir in  remaining ingredients.  Pour into a greased 1-1/2 quart casserole. Bake at      350    degrees for 1 hour.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, October, 1980. Typed for you by Nancy  Coleman.
