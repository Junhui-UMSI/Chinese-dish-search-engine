Quick Bread Mix
Sugar, Salt, Shortening, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Salt, 
Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl; mix well. Divide flour  mixture and shortening in half. Cut 1 cup shortening into half of flour  mixture with a pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse meal. Repeat  with remaining shortening and flour mixture. Combine mixtures. Store in an  airtight container at room temperature up to 6 weeks. Refrigerate or freeze  for longer storage. Yield:  13 cups
