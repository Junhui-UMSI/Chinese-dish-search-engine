Turkey Salad in Curried Mayonnaise
Tomatoes, Lemons, quartered, Shallot, chopped, salt and pepper, Mayonnaise, Flour, Chutney, Lemon juice, Butter, Paprika, Shredded cooked turkey, Curry powder, Coconut, Watercress, Mixed cooked veggies, Turkey stock, 
Cool turkey and cooked vegetables. Quarter tomatoes; remove seeds, strain  and reserve any juice that comes from tomatoes. Wash watercress and dry  thoroughly. Melt butter in frying pan. Add shallot and saute until  translucent. Add curry powder and saute 1-2 minutes more. Sprinkle in  fllour and saute 3-5 minutes. Add stock, blending well. Bring to a boil,  stirring constantly. Stir in coconut, chutney, and 2 teaspoons lemon juice;  simmer 10-15 minutes. Strain and cool. Season with salt and pepper. Season  cooked vegetables with salt and pepper; sprinkle with remaining lemon  juice. Place in salad bowl. Arrange turkey in center of bowl. thin  mayonnaise with a small amount of reserved tomato juice. Spoon carefully  over turkey, sprinkle with paprika. Arrange tomatoes and watercress sprigs  alternatley around edge of bowl. Garnish with lemon quarters and serve.
