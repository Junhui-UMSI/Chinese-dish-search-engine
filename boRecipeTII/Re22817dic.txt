Apricot Whip
Sugar, FROM BRIGITTE SEALING, CYBEREALM BBS 3157861120, milk, Almond extract, Cottage cheese, creamed, Dried apricots, Apricots, 
Place the cottage cheese, dried apricots, sugar and almond extract  into a blender. Blend about 1 1/2 minutes until all is smooth.  Pour into chilled glasses. Garnish with canned apricots.    Source: Womens Day magazine    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcyber4.zip
