Swiss Steak #2
Black pepper, All-purpose flour, Garlic powder, Vegetable oil, Cube steak, stewed tomatoes  , Onions, 
In bowl, combine flour, garlic powder and black pepper. Dredge steak in  flour mixture. In skillet, add steak to hot oil; brown steak on both sides;  add onions and continue cooking until onions are tender. Add stewed  tomatoes and simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes or until steak is cooked  through. Serve with hot noodles. Serves 4.
