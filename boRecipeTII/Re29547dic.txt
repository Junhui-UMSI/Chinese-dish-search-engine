Tart Vegetable Stuffing for Fish
Ground onions, Celery, Ground carrots, Salt, Lemon juice, Soft bread crumbs, Butter, 
Mix all ingredients well. Use as stuffing for any large fish or with fish  steaks. This dressing is especially good with salmon.    Marlene C. Dryer, Bloomington, Ind.
