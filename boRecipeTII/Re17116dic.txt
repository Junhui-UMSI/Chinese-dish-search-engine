Vineyard Spam Salad
Seedless grapes, Peapods, cut in half, SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed, Dry mustard, Mayonnaise or salad dressing, Lime juice, Thinly sliced red onion, 
In skillet, saute SPAM over high heat 2 minutes, stirring constantly; set  aside. In small bowl, combine mayonnaise, lime juice, and dry mustard. In  large bowl, combine grapes, SPAM, peapods, and onion. Tos with mayonnaise  mixture. Cover and chill 1 hour.
