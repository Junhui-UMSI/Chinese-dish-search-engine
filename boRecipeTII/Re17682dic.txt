Almond Poppyseed Muffins
Vegetable oil, Poppyseeds, Salt, Almond extract, Flour, Sugar, milk, BETTY HAHN (RTKD98B, Eggs, Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease or use muffin papers to line 2 12 cup  muffin tins.  Combine flour, sugar baking powder, and salt. In another bowl  whisk oil, milk, eggs and extract till smooth. Add dry ingredients mixing  till moist.  Then gentily stir in all the poppyseeds. Fill muffin tins and  bake 30 minutes. Liz Jones 8/92.
