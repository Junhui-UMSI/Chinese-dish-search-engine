End of Summer Stew
Beef stew meat, trimmed and, Cooking oil, Fresh tomatoes, peeled and, Tomato juice or water, Onions, chopped, Garlic, Pepper, Salt, Potatoes, peeled, quartered, Carrots, sliced, Cut corn, Fresh cut green beans, Peas, Celery stalks, sliced, Summer squash, Snipped fresh parsley, Sugar, 
In a Dutch oven, brown meat in oil over medium heat. Add tomatoes, tomato  juice or water, onions, garlic, pepper, and salt. Bring to a boil; reduce  heat and simmer for 1 hour. Add potatoes, carrots, corn, green beans, peas  and celery. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes. Add squash; simmer 10-15  minutes longer or until meat and vegetables are tender. Stir in parsley and  sugar. You can substitute or leave out any of the vegetables, or use canned  or frozen, instead of fresh.
