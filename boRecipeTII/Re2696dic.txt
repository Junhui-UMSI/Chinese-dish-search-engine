Peanut Butter Banana Bread
Flour, Peanut butter, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Eggs, Baking soda, bananas, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Spray 2 8x4 loaf pans with PAM. Combine dry  ingredients, set aside, Combine wet ingredients, blend well. Mix together  with dry ingredients, do not over mix. Pour into prepared pans. Bake 30-40  minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.    Recipe by: Laura Gaggini    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest by "LauraG" <gaggini@gate.usaor.net> on Apr  9, 1998
