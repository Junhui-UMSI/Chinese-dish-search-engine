Ahi Carpaccio
Bunch arugula, Wht or blk truffle; shaved, Fresh tuna, paper-thin, Freshly cracked pepper, Grated Parmesan cheese, Salt, Vinegar & Oil, 
Note: May use a greater amount of truffle if desired. Arrange tuna in  overlapping slices on platter. Sprinkle with salt and drizzle with oil and  vinegar to taste. Sprinkle with pepper. Surround tuna with wreath of  arugula. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over all. Top with truffle  shavings.
