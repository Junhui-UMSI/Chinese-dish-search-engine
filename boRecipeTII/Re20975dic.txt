Apple Pecan Muffins
Vanilla, Butter, Sugar, Baking soda, Granny smith apples; peeled, Pecans, Flour, Salt, Cinnamon, Eggs, Baking Powder, Nutmeg, 
Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla; beat until fluffy. Thoroughly  mix all dry ingredients. Slowly add to butter-sugar mixture, mising only  until moistened. Hand stir in pecans and apples. (mixture may be a little  dry; however, apples will provide enough moisture.) Fill greased or lined  muffin tins with 1/4 cup of mixture. Bake at 350 F. for 20 minutes or until  muffins test done.    Yield: 18 to 24 muffins    Hint: these muffins make great gift basket fillers.    Recipe copied from Baylor University Alumni Association, Waco,TX Homecoming  cookbook.
