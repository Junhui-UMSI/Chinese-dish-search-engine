Vegetable Soup with Tortellini
Grated Parmesan cheese, Celery stalks; diced, Dried basil, Leeks, white only; diced, Roma tomatoes; chopped, Carrots; peeled and diced, Frozen tortellini(12oz), Zucchini; diced, Chicken broth, Onion; diced, Oregano, Salt and pepper, 
Heat the broth in a large saucepan and add the onion,  carrots, celery, leeks, zucchini, oregano, and basil.  Simmer gently covered for 10 minutes.  Add the tortellini and cook until al dente.  The last  5 minutes of cooking, add the chopped tomatoes.  Season to taste with salt and peppers. Allow to cool  before refrigerating.  Supply parmesan cheese to  sprinkle over soup when served.
