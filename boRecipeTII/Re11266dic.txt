Rajma
Kidney beans, In Ginger, Water, Turmeric, Salt, Onion, Cilantro, bunch, Garam masala, Tomatoes, Vegetable oil, 
Wash beans and boil for 2 - 3 hours or 1/2 hour in pressure cooker. In the  meantime make masala of onions, garlic, ginger and tomato as in ? curry.  Add to the beans and cook again till most of the liquid dries up and the  beans are soft and thoroughly cooked. Garnish with coriander leaves and  serve.
