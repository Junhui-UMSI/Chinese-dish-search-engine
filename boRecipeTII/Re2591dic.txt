Peachy Pork
Peach preserves, Taco seasoning mix, Salsa, Cubed boneless pork, Vegetable oil, 
Coat cubed boneless pork with taco seasoning mix, brown in a little  oil in a skillet. Add an 8 oz. jar of salsa and 4 tablespoons of  peach preserves to skillet, stir to mix well, cover and lower heat.  Simmer gently for 15 minutes.
