Peanut Butter Treasures
Flour, Baking soda, Whole Milk, Vanilla extract, Peanut butter, Peanut Butter Cups, Egg, Vegetable shortening, Salt, Peanuts, Brown sugar, 
Heat oven to 375 degrees F. Combine peanut butter, shortening, brown sugar,  milk and vanilla in a large bowl. Beat at medium speed until well blended.  Add egg, beat just until blended. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda. Add  to creamed mixture at low speed. Mix just until blended. Shape into 1-1/2  inch balls. Roll in chopped peanuts. Press center with teaspoon. Bake 7 to  8 minutes. Press mini peanut butter cup into depression.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 486 by Kara9718  <Kara9718@aol.com> on Jan 10, 1998
