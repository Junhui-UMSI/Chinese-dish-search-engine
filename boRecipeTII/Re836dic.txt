Hidden Valley Wraps
Hidden Valley Original Ranch dressing, Cream cheese, flour torillas, Turkey breast, Monterey Jack, Avocados, Alfalfa salad sprouts, Tomatoes, 
Blend together dressing and cream cheese. Spread evenly on tortillas.  Evenly layer turkey, cheese, avocados, tomatoes and sprouts on tortillas,  leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Fold the bottome edge toward the  center and firmly roll away from you until completely wrapped. Place seam  side down and cut in half diagonally. Serve.
