Apple Banana Bread
Butter, Brown sugar, Granulated sugar, Eggs, Sour cream, Bananas, Vanilla, Flour, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Apple, Walnut, 
Prep Time: 15 minutes Bake Time: 1 hour Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes Oven Temp: 350F / 180C / Gas Mark 4  Preheat oven.   Cream butter and sugars, and beat in eggs. Stir in sour cream, banana and  vanilla.   In separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, soda and cinnamon.  Gradually add to butter mixture. Gently stir in apples and nuts. Spoon into  greased bread pan and bake 1 hour.
