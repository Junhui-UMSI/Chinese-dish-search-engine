Banana Apple
Vanilla, Unbleached flour, Brown Sugar, Granulated sugar; room temp, Applesauce; at room temp, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Bananas; mashed, Cinnamon, Egg whites; whipped, Fat-free sour cream, Apples; chopped, 
Recipe by: Jo Anne Merrill Preparation Time: 0:15 Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a pan(s) with cooking spray and flour. In a mixing bowl, combine  applesauce, egg whites, sour cream, bananas, vanilla, and apples. In  another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugars, baking powder, baking soda, and  cinnamon. Mix wet ingredients with dry ingredients just until moistened.  Pour into prepared pan(s).  Bake about 1 hour depending on pan size.
