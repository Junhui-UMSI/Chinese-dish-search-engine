Peanut Butter Pudding
Cornstarch, Sugar, Salt, Half and half cream, Creamy peanut butter, Vanilla, Whole Milk, 
In a saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch and salt. Slowly wisk in milk and  cream and bring to a boil over med. heat. Cook and stir constantly for 2  min. Remove from the heat and stir in the peanut butter and vanilla. Pour  into serving dishes and refrigerate atleast 4 hours. You can garnish this  with whip cream, but its pretty rich all on its own.    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by JTS13 <JTS13@aol.com> on Jan 4, 1998
