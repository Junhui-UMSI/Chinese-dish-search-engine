Quick Bacon Rolls
Toothpicks, Ketchup, Bacon, Brown sugar, Sugar, Water chestnuts; whole, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Cut bacon strips in thirds. Cut water  chestnuts in half. Wrap each bacon piece around a water chestnut. Secure  with a toothpick. Bake for 20 minutes or until bacon starts to crisp. Drain  grease.    Combine ketchup and sugars. Pour over bacon rolls and continue baking for  15-20 minutes.
