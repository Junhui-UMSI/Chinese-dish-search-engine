Puff Pastry Dough
water, Salted butter, flour, Salt, Baking soda, 
Place the flour in a bowl. Make a well in the center; into it place butter,  salt, baking soda and ice water. Mix by hand until dough forms walnut size  pieces. Refrigerate until ready to use.
