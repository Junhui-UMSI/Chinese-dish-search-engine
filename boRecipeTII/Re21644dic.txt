Apple Sorbet
Granny Smith apples, cored &, Cinnamon, Sugar, Unsweetened apple juice, quartered, Water, Lemon, 
Combine apples with 1/4 c apple juice in a non-corrosive pot. Bring to a  boil & simmer 5 minutes. Remove from the heat & let cool. Puree the apples  in a food processor or blender.    Combine the pureed apples, syrup, rest of juice, cinnamon & lemon juice.  Freeze following the instructions for making Lemon Sorbet.    VARIATION:  Calvados Currant Sorbet. Soak 1/4 c dried currants in 1/4 c  calvados or applejack for 4 hours or overnight. Drain & add to the sorbet  mixture before freezing.    Jim Tarantino, "Sorbets!"
