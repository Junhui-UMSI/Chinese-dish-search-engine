Baby Food Cake
Baking soda, Margarine, Eggs, Jar applesauce baby food, Jar apricot baby food, Cinnamon, Pecans, Sugar, Flour, FROSTING, Jar carrot baby food, Vanilla, (8 oz.) cream cheese, Box (16 oz.) powdered sugar, Salad oil, 
Combine eggs, sugar and salad oil. Add baby food. Sift flour, cinnamon and  baking soda and add to baby food mixture. Mix in pecans. Bake in 9x13x2  inch baking pan at 350 for 35 minutes. For frosting, combine margarine,  cream cheese, powdered sugar and vanilla. Frost cake when cool. MasterCook  formatted by Ethel Snyder.
