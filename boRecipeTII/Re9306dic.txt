Puerto Vallarta Dip
Salt to taste, Green onion -- sliced, Canned tuna -- drained, Mayonnaise, Chopped, Sprigs cilantro, or to taste, Lemon or lime juice to, Tortilla chips, Hot chile salsa, Taste, 
In a bowl stir together tuna, onion, salsa, mayonnaise (water-pack tuna may  require more mayonnaise), and cilantro. Season to taste with lemon juice  and salt; adjust other seasonings to taste. Serve with chips.    Makes about 1-1/2 cups.    * Timesaver Tip: Recipe can be prepared in processor. Cut green onion into  1-inch lengths and put in processor fitted with steel blade. Add cilantro  sprigs and process for 3 to 5 seconds. Add tuna, salsa, mayonnaise, lemon  juice, and salt; pulse a few times to combine. Taste, adjust seasoning, and  pulse one or two times more. Recipe can be made 4 or 5 hours ahead and  stored, covered, in refrigerator. Remove from refrigerator about 30 minutes  before serving.
