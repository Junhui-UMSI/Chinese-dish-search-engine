Quick Scalloped Potatoes
Pepper, Onions, milk, Salt, Margarine, Flour, Paprika, Potatoes, 
Preheat oven to 400. Peel and slice onions.  Peel and slice Potatoes. Cover  with hot water and add 2 tsp salt. Bring to boil and cook 5 minutes. Drain  and put in a greased casserole. Mix marg and flour in microwave safe glass  measuring cup.  Microwave 1 minute. Add Milk slowly whisking to keep lumps  from forming. Add 1 ts salt, pepper and paprika. Microwave stirring  occasionally til cooked.  Makes fairly loose sauce. Pour over potato/onion  mixture in casserole and bake for 35 minutes.
