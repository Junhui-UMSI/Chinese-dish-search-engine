Chicken and Rice
Sliced mushrooms or mixed, Onion soup mix powder, Raw rice, Chicken; cut in 8ths, Water, 
Put the rice in a large pan. Sprinkle some onion soup mix on top. Add  veggies on top. Add chicken on top. Add water. Sprinkle more onion soup  mix. Bake covered about 1 hour.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "leah perez" <perezleah@hotmail.com> on Oct  11, 1998,
