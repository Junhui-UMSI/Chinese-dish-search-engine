Lemon Chicken
Chicken pieces; skinned, Onion flakes; chopped, Fresh mushrooms, Honey; or more to taste, Soft margarine, Prepared mustard, Lemon juice.; or more to, Curry powder; optional, 
Mix all ingredients except chicken. Pour over chicken parts in a greased  flat pan. Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour. Baste as needed. You can  add or subtract chicken as needed.
