Toasted Almond Truffles
Undiluted evaporated milk, Sugar, Milk chocolate morsels, almond extract, almonds, 
In a small, heavy saucepan, combine evaporated milk and sugar. Over  medium heat, bring mixture to a full rolling boil; boil 3 minutes,  stirring constantly. Remove from heat; stir in chocolate morsels and  almond extract until morsels melt and mixture is smooth. Cover  mixture and refrigerate 45 minutes. With hands, shape mixture into  1-inch balls; roll in almonds. Refrigerate about 1 hour or until  ready to serve. Makes about 30 truffles.
