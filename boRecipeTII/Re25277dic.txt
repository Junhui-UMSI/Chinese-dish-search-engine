Top Secret Ihop Pancakes
Sugar, baking powder, Egg, Nonstick spray, Flour, Baking soda, Cooking oil, 
Preheat a killet over medium heat.  Use a pan wiht a nonstick surface or  apply a little nonstick spray.  In a blender or with a mixer, combine all  of the remaining ingredients until smooth.  Pour the batter by spoonfuls  into the hot pan, forming 5" circles.  When the edges appear to harden,  flip the pancakes.  They should be light brown. Cook on the other disde for  same amount of time, until light brown. SOURCE: Todd Wilburs Top Secret  Recipes    Recipes sent to me from Bill, wight@odc.net
