Apple Meat Loaf
Prepared mustard, Eggs, stuffing mix, Salt, horseradish, Ketchup, apples, Onion, Lean beef, ground, 
Combine all ingredients; mix thoroughly. Pack into greased loaf pan 8" x 5"  x 3". Bake at 350 F for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
