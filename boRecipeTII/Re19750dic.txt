Anytime Cucumber Slices
Sugar, Minced fresh dill or 1 tbsp, Salt, Cucumbers, sliced, Vinegar, Onions, thinly sliced, Water, 
In a bowl, combine cucumbers, onions and dill. In a saucepan, combine  sugar, vinegar, water and salt; bring to a boil. Pour over cucumber  mixture. Cover and chill for 3 hours or overnight.
