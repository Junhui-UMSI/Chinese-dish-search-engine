Apricot Nut Supreme Brownies
Almond extract, Margarine, Slivered almonds; divided, All-purpose flour, White chocolate OR white, Baking Powder, Dried apricots; quartered, Sugar, Eggs, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 350. In small saucepan, melt white chocolate and margarine  over low heat. Stir constantly until mixture becomes a curdling  consistency; remove from heat. Add sugar, eggs, and almond extract; stir  quickly to blend. In a seperate bowl, combine flour, baking powder and  salt. Stir into white chocolate mixture; add half the apricots and almonds  to the batter. Pour into a greased 10 1/2 x 7 1 1/2 inch pan, and sprinkle  top with the remaining apricots and almonds. Bake 25 minutes, or until  golden and brownies begin to pull away from the edge of the pan. Drizzle  the top of brownies with melted white or dark chocolate, if desired. Makes  18 brownies.
