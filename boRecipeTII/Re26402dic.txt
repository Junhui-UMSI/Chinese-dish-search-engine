Tomato Chicken Curry
olive oil, Frozen peas, Garlic, Curry powder, Boneless; skinless chicken, Chicken broth, tomato sauce  , Black pepper, All-purpose flour, Ground red pepper, Linguine, carrots, Salt, Onions, 
In large plastic food bag, combine chicken and flour. Shake to coat chicken  thoroughly.    In large nonstick skillet, cook and stir chicken in oil until chicken just  begins to brown; remove and set aside.    In same saucepan, cook and stir onions, carrots, and garlic until onions  start to soften. Add cooked chicken and remaining ingredients except pasta;  mix thoroughly.    Simmer, uncovered, 5 minutes until sauce thickens. Serve immediately over  hot pasta.    Makes 4-6 servings.    source: http://www.kittensplayground.com/tomchickcurry.html    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Maria Ilagan <maria@DOTCOM.BC.CA> on Apr 7, 1998
