Hearty Vegetable Soup
Cross-cut beef shanks, Salt, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, Dried parsley flakes, Onions, Celery, Sliced; peeled carrots, Peeled; diced potatoes, Cut-up tomatoes, Whole kernel corn; drained, Frozen cut green beans, 
"Stolen" from Rival Crock-Pot Cooking book    Combine all ingredients in crockpot. Add water to cover. {About 2 cups}  Stir well. Cover and cook on LOW setting for 12 hours. {Or on HIGH setting  for 5-7 hours.} Before serving, remove bones, cut meat into bite-size  pieces, and stir into soup.
