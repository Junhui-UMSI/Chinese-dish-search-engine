Tequila Lime Caesar Salad Dressing
Worcestershire Sauce, Red wine vinegar, Cuervo gold tequila, Parmesan cheese, Dijon style mustard, Anchovy fillets, olive oil, Pureed garlic, 
In a blender or food processor, puree garlic, cheese, lime juice, tequila,  anchovies, worcestershire sauce, mustard and 1/2 cup olive oil.    Add vinegar to the paste and pulse, slowly drizzling in remaining oil.  Season with freshly cracked black pepper.
