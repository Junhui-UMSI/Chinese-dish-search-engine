Anything Muffins
Brown sugar, Flour, DRY INGREDIENTS, D, Baking Powder, banana, Applesauce, Low sugar marmalade, granny smith apples, FRUIT COMBINATION (PICK, banana, Low sugar strawberry, Vanilla, Fresh blueberries, Salt, C, WET INGREDIENTS, B, banana, Mandarin orange, Eggs, A, E, 
(make up your own, just make sure the is some fluid in the ingredients  here)    Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix dry ingredients thoroughly, set aside. Mix  wet ingredients in a large bowl, add dry ingredsients, mix until just  blended, add fruit. Bake for 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in  center of muffin comes out clean.
