Port Wine Soaked Fig Bread
Plain yogurt, Salt, Cornmeal, Bread flour, Whole-wheat flour, Nonfat dry milk powder, Butter, Honey, Yeast, Port, Dried figs;cut into quarters, 
Soak the figs in the port for at least one hour or more. When the figs have  absorbed a good deal of the liquid, add whatever port is left to the bread  pan.   Reserve figs for later.    Add rest of the ingredients to the pan, and bake according to  manufacturers instructions.  Add the figs during the raisin-bread cycle or  five minutes before the final kneading is finished. Makes 1-1/2 lb. loaf.    SOURCE:  The Bread Machine Cookbook by Melissa Clark
