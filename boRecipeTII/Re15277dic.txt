White Asparagus in Ham Sauce
milk, Flour, Cooked Lean Ham --, White Asparagus 14 1/2 oz, LISA CRAWLEY  TSPN00B-----, Julienned, Butter, Asparagus, Salt, Reserved Cooking liquid from, Nutmeg, Ea, 
Drain asparagus; reserve 1/2 c liquid.  Heat butter in saucepan. Add flour;  blend.  Gradually pour in asparagus liquid and milk; stir constantly over  low heat until sauce thickens and bubbles. Add ham and seasonings. Gently  stir in asparagus; heat through, do not boil. Serve in preheated serving  dish.
