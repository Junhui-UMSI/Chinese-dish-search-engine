Sweet Potato Cocoons
Flour, Water, Sticks margarine; minus 1, Pecans, Sweet potatoes; mashed, Vanilla, 
Recipe By : The Sweet Potato Cookbook!    Cream margarine with sugar; add flour, vanilla, water, potatoes and pecans.  Mix well. Roll pinches of dough into small oblong rolls. Bake on cookie  sheet in 300F oven for 30 minutes. Let cool completely and roll in  confectioners sugar.
