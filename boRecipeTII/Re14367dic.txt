Whole Tenderloin with Mushrooms
Beef tenderloin, Cracked black pepper, olive oil, Butter, Sea salt, Mushrooms, quartered, 
Put tenderloin in roasting pan and drizzle with olive oil. Rub oil over  roast and season with salt and pepper to taste. Wipe mushrooms with damp  paper towel to remove dirt and cut into quarters. Scatter mushrooms around  meat in roasting pan. Roast meat at 500 degrees 20 to 25 minutes or to 140  degrees on meat thermometer for medium rare. Remove meat. Add butter to  roasting pan and heat on top of stove to deglaze pan, stirring mushrooms in  butter and drippings. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cut tenderloin  into 6 slices and arrange on serving platter. Spoon mushrooms around meat.  Formatted by Lynn Thomas dcqp82a. Source: Los Angeles Times 4-16-97. 6  servings.
