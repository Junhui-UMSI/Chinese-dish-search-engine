Zucchini Pineapple Muffins
Egg Beaters, Wheat flour; whole-grain, Baking soda, Brown Sugar, Salt, Nutmeg, Crushed pineapple in juice;, Cinnamon, Zucchini; unpeeled, shredded, Flour, Baking Powder, Walnuts, Vanilla, 
Add sugar to Eggbeaters. Stir in zucchini, pineapple and vanilla. Measure  remaining ingredients in another bowl. Stir to distribute - add to wet  ingredients and stir to moisten. Add enough reserved pineapple liquid to  attain batter consistency (drops in blobs from s poon). Pour into muffins  cups in 12 cup tin. Bake at 400F for about 24 minutes until they pass the  toothpick test. Cool for 1 minute then turn onto rack to further cool.    NOTES : Nuts may be sprinkled on top instead of mixing into the batter.
