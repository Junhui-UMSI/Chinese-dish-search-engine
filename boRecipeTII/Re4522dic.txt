Pet Party Mix
Dog treats, Imitation bacon bits, butter, Cheerios, Chex, Shredded Wheat, Dry gravy mix, american cheese, Powder, 
Preheat oven to 250. Pour melted butter/margerine into a 13x9 baking  pan. Stir in cheese, bacon bits, & gravy mix. Add cereal & stir until  all pieces are coated. Heat until crisp, approx. 45 min. Let cool &  store in tightly sealed container.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
