Big Jims Jalapeno and Cheddar Hushpuppies
White selfrising cornmeal, Fine chopped onions, Cheddar cheese, Fine chopped japaleno, Eggs, Salt, Orange juice, 
Mix all ingredients until well blended. Add orange juice until it makes a  stiff moist batter.    Drop by teaspoon (like you stir tea with) into 350 degree oil and fry until  golden brown. drain on paper towels and eat.    Posted to bbq-digest by "James A.
