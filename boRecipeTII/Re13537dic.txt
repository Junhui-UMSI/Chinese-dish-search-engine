Whores Pasta
Cream, Shallot (finely chopped), Frozen Peas, Fresh mushrooms, Butter, Prociutto, Parmesean Cheese, 
Sweat the shallot in a small amount of butter. Clean and dry the mushrooms.  Slice thinly. Add to pan. Saute till just done. Add the cream and reduce.  Then add the peas. Cook gently for 8-10 mins. Just before you are ready to  serve add the Procuiotto (diced) into the sauce. Then add the cheese. Be  sure to stir well, the cheese will stick. Serve over Angel Hair Pasta.
