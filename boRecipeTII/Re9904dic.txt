Pungent French Roll Salad Sandwich
olive oil, Pitted black olives; chopped, Cucumbers; peeled and diced, Ripe tomatoes; peel seed, Chopped fresh parsley, Capers, French rolls, Anchovy fillets; cut, Green bell peppers; diced, Red onions; chopped, Mayonnaise, 
Split the rolls almost through and brush inside well with olive oil. Mix  remaining ingredients, except mayonnaise. Moisten with mayonnaise and fill  rolls. Wrap each sandwich individually. Serve on a plate to catch filling  as it falls. Takes the place of bread and potato salad at the picnic. (c)  1966 by Womans Day Encyclopedia of Cookery. NY: Fawcett, Inc.
