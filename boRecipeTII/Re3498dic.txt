Pecan Pickups
Vanilla, Flour, Butter, Finely chopped pecans, Egg, (3-oz) cream cheese, Dark brown sugar; packed, FILLING, Butter, 
Mix cream cheese, 1 stick butter & flour until smooth & chill. Divide into  24 balls slightly smaller than walnuts. Put into muffin cups & press evenly  around sides & bottom. Filling: Mix all ingredients together well & put 1  teaspoon into each pastry-lined cup. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
