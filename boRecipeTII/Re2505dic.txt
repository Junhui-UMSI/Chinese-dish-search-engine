Peach-Toffee Shortcakes
Margarine, Brown sugar, Whipping cream, -(up to), Peaches, PEACH TOFFEE TOPPING, Bisquick original baking mix, Powdered sugar, milk, Caramel ice-cream topping, ?, 
HEAT oven to 425 degrees. Mix baking mix, milk, brown sugar and margarine  until soft dough forms. Turn dough onto surface dusted with baking mix;  gently roll in baking mix to coat. Shape into ball; knead gently 8 to 10  times. Roll 1/2 inch thick. Cut with 3-inch round cutter dipped in baking  mix. Place on ungreased cookie sheet.     BAKE 8 to 11 minutes or until golden brown. Cool 10 minutes. Prepare  Peach-Toffee Topping.    SPLIT shortcakes horizontally. Fill and top shortcakes with topping.  6  servings.     Peach-Toffee Topping: Beat whipping cream, ice-cream topping and powdered  sugar in chilled large bowl until stiff. Stir in candy and peaches.     NOTE: Place toffee bars in freezer for several hours to make them easier  to crush.     NOTE: 1 package (16 ounces) frozen sliced peaches, thawed, drained and cut  into 1/2-inch pieces, can be substituted for the fresh peaches.
