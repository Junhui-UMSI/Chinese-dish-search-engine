Swiss Cabbage Pie - Ethel Buffington
Swiss Cheese; Grated,, Cabbage; shredded, Pepper, Cream Or Half And Half, Bacon, Salt, Eggs, Loaf Unbaked Bread (Can Use, 
* Heat oven to 350 F.    1. Roll out the bread dough about 1/4-inch thick and place it in a 9-inch  pie pan. Crimp edges, as would for a pie.    2. Cut the bacon into small pieces and brown them in a large heavy skillet.  Pour off all but 1 Tbs of grease. Stir in the shredded cabbage and cook  over moderate heat, stirring, for 1 min.    3. Over low heat, add salt and pepper, eggs, cream and the cheese. Cook for  3 to 4 mins, stirring. Put mixture in the crust and bake for 30 mins, or  until filling is set and the edges are golden brown.    >From the EUREKA COUNTY FAIR of Eureka, NV.
