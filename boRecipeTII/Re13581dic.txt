Wild Card Chili
Pepper, Garlic salt, Molasses, Tomato sauce - 8 oz., Refried beans - 16 oz., Water, Red beans - 16 oz., Onions, Salt, Chili powder, Chopped beef, Chopped hot red peppers, Cayenne pepper, 
Brown beef with onions in a Dutch oven; pour off fat. Add remaining  ingredients; cover and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Makes 6  servings.                              Marlboro Country Cookin Brochure                                        1992
