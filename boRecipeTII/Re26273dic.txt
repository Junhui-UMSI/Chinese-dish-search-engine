Tomato and Cheese Melt
Light butter; softened, 3/4-inch diagonal slices, Tomato, Dried basil leaves, Chedarella cheese, Light or no fat sour cream, Avocado wedges, 
Heat oven to 350. In small bowl stir together light butter and basil  leaves; spread over one side of each bread slice. Top each bread slice with  tomato slice; place on cookie sheet. Bake for 5 minutes. Top each tomato  with cheese slice. Continue baking for 3 to 5 minutes or until cheese is  melted. Garnish with sour cream and avocado. Yield 6 sandwiches.
