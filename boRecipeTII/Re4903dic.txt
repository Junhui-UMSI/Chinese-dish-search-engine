Pickled Eggs #3
water, Cayenne pepper, vinegar, Eggs, garlic, Texas Pete peppers, Worcestershire sauce, Dillweed, Salt, 
Hard boil eggs and peel. Mix all desired ingredients in one gallon  container. Let eggs set for at least 2 days, the longer they set the  stronger the flavor. When the eggs get to the desired flavor, remove from  mixture and place in a covered jar and cover with water.
