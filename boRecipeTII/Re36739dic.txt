Strawberry Ice
Sugar, Strawberries, Orange juice, Lemon juice, Water, 
Combine sugar and water in medium saucepan. Heat, stirring, until sugar  dissolves, then boil 5 minutes. Cool. Force berries through food mill or  blend in blender or food processor. Strain to remove seeds, if desired.  Blend strawberry puree and lemon and orange juices into syrup. Pour into  freezer trays and freeze. Remove from freezer 20 minutes before serving.  Turn into bowl and stir with wooden spoon until smooth.    To make an especially attractive dessert, present scoops of ice in  cantaloupe or honeydew melon halves. Makes 8 to 12 servings
