Triple Gingerbread
Baking soda, Clove, Ginger, Salt, Vegetable oil, Flour, Cinnamon, Sugar, Unsulfured light molasses, Water, Eggs, Ginger, minced, Ginger, crystallized, 
Preheat over to 350F. Butter and flour 10" diameter spring form pan. Sift  first 6 ingredients into medium bowl. Combine sugar, oil, molasses, water,  eggs, and fresh ginger in large bowl; wisk to blend. Mix in crystallized  ginger. Stir in dry ingredients. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake until  tester inserted into center of cake comes out clean, about 1 hour. Cool  cake in pan on rack 1 hour (cake may fall in the center) Run small sharp  knife around pan sides to loosen cake. Release pan sides from cake. Serve  slightly warm or at room temperature. Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Wrap in  foil and refrigerate. Bring to room temperature before serving.
