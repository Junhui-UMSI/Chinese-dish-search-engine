Pickled Dill Eggs
White vinegar, Mustard seed, Dillweed, garlic, Onion, Eggs, Salt, Water, 
Pack eggs in jar. In saucepan bring remaining ingredients to a boil. Reduce  heat to low and simmer 5 to 7 minutes. Pour over eggs to cover completely.  Refrigerate and allow flavors to meld. Posted to EAT-L Digest by "Sheltraw,  Norris" <NSHELTRAW@METROPO.MCCNEB.EDU> on Jul 8, 1997
