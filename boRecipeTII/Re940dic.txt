Hungarian Pot Roast
Pepper, Vegetable oil, Water, Bay leaf, Onion; sliced, Lean; (3-4 lb) chuck or rump, Paprika, Salt, Carrots; peeled and sliced, Garlic, Onion salt, Sour cream {optional}, 
Taken from the book, "BEST RECIPES From The Backs Of Boxes, Bottles, Cans,  And Jars"    Trim excess fat off meat. Sprinkle with paprika, salt and pepper. Brown in  oil in Dutch oven over medium heat. Add water and bay leaf; simmer,  covered, 2 hours, or until meat is almost tender. Place onions and carrots  around meat. Add Hunts tomato sauce, garlic and onion salt. Cover; simmer  50 minutes longer until meat and vegetables are tender. Just before  serving, remove from heat and gradually stir in sour cream, if desired.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by IceTeaLuvr@aol.com on Aug 22, 1998,
