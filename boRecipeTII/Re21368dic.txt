Unicorn Village Spinach Cakes
And cooled, Garlic, Additional bread crumbs, Fresh rosemary, Cooked and chopped, salt and pepper, Olive oil spray for browning, (1 cup) grated Parmesan, Eggs, Onion; minced, Potatoes (2 medium) mashed, Fresh bread crumbs, Spinach (about 6 cups), 
Preheat oven to 375. Combine the spinach, onion, olive oil, garlic,  potatoes, eggs, Parmesan, rosemary and bread crumbs. Add salt and pepper to  taste. Pat into small round cakes, about 3 inches in diameter. Dredge the  cakes in the additional bread crumbs. Brown in a nonstick or sprayed pan.  Reserve. To serve, place browned cakes on a cookie sheet and bake at 375  until hot inside, about 20 minutes.
