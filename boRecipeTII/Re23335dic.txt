Tuna Toast
Butter, soft, Peel of a Lemon, Pickles,cubed finely, Whole Wheat Bread, Tuna in Water,drained, Salt, Anchovies, chopped finely, 
1 .Mix the anchovies, butter, lemonpeel and tuna together and mix     very good. 2. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 3. Toast the bread  and spread the tunamix on it. 4. Cut toast diagonally in half and garnish  with pickles.     Out of "DGuchi"magazine     Typed by Brigitte Sealing
