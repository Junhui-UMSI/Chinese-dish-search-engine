Vegetarian Quiche Spud
Swiss cheese, Salt, Parsley, Baked potatoes, Onion, Sour cream, Eggs, Butter, STUFFED SPUDS, Pepper, Leftover vegs, 
Cut a thin slice from the top of each potato. Remove pulp being careful not  to tear shels. Place potato pulp in mixing bowl; mash; cover and set aside.  Set shells aside. heat butter in skillet; saute onion until tender, low  heat, 10 mins. Add sour cream, eggs, salt and pepper, stirring constantly.  Add mashed potatoes; mix well. Add cheese and vegs; mix lightly. Heap  mixture into shells. Bake 350 F, 45 mins. Garnish with parsley.
