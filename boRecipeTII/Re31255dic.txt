Banana-Strawberry Shake
Very ripe banana, Whole strawberries, Skim milk, Instant nonfat dry milk, Ice cubes, Few drops vanilla extract, 
In blender, mix together ice cubes, skim milk, banana, strawberries, nonfat  dry milk and extract. Blend on high speed until ice is crushed and mixture  is slushy and serve immediately.
