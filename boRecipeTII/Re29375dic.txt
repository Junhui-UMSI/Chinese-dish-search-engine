Baked Turkey Salad
Cooked turkey breast; cut, Light sour cream, Onion, Celery; thinly sliced, Slivered almonds; toasted, Mushrooms; sliced, Mayonnaise type salad, Lemon juice, Seasoned breadcrumbs, olive oil, 
1. Preheat oven to 325F.    2. Heat oil in a large heavy nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add celery  and onion and saute 4 minutes, or until tender.    3. Remove from heat and stir in next 3 ingredients and salt and pepper to  taste until well blended.    4. Add turkey, almonds and mushrooms. Toss to coat well. Transfer to an  ovenproof casserole.    5. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and bake 25-30 minutes until bubbly.
