Cucumber, Tomato, olive oil, Green Onions, Cracked wheat, Chopped parsley, -(up to), Mint, -(up to), Lemons, 
Wash wheat until water is clear. Leaving some water in bowl, let wheat set  1 hour until softened. Squeeze lemons and mix juice with olive oil, adding  a little water. Mix tomato, cucumber, onion, parsley and mint with wheat.  Pour lemon juice mixture over vegetables. Salt and pepper to taste. Serve  on lettuce leaves or grape leaves. (Grape leaves needed are a special kind  which do not bear grapes. They may be bought in stores which carry gourmet  foods.)
