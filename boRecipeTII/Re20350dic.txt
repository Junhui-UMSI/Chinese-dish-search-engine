Veal Scaloppine with Lemon
olive oil, fresh parsley, Veal scaloppine, salt and pepper, All-purpose flour, Juice of one lemon, Lemons, Butter, 
Pound veal scallops between 2 sheets of waxed paper until they are about  1/8 inch thick. Blot meat with paper towels, then lightly dust with flour  and shake off excess.    Heat 1 tablespoon oil and half the butter in a large skillet over medium  heat. When butter and oil are sizzling, brown veal slices, a few at a time,  on both sides. Remove and keep warm. Add butter and oil between batches as  needed.    Remove skillet from heat and add lemon juice and any remaining butter,  scraping browned bits from bottom of pan.    Add parsley and veal and season to taste. Cook over medium heat about 5  minutes. Remove veal to platter, pouring juices over all and garnishing  with lemon slices. Serves 6.
