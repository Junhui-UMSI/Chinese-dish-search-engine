Baked Winter Squash
Applesauce, Molasses, Ginger, ground, Butternut squash, Maple syrup, Orange juice, Cinnamon, ground, 
Peel squash. Cut squash into slices about 1/2-inch thick. Lay in baking  dish. Mix remaining ingredients.   Pour over squash. Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes, covered.     From the files of DEEANNE
