Sweet Potatoes Southern S
WATER; WARM, CINNAMON GROUND 1 LB CN, MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, POTATO SWEET #2 1/2, Salt, NUTMEG GROUND, BUTTER PRINT SURE, SUGAR; BROWN, 2 LB, 
PAN:  18 BY 24-INCH ROASTING PAN                 TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  RECONSTITUTE MILK IN MIXER BOWL.    2.  ADD SWEET POTATOES; BEAT AT LOW SPEED UNTIL SMOOTH.    3.  ADD SALT; BLEND AT MEDIUM SPEED.    4.  SCRAPE DOWN BOWL;  BEAT AT MEDIUM SPEED FOR 2 MINUTES.    5.  PLACE ABOUT 2 GAL POTATOES IN EACH GREASED PAN; SPRINKLE AN  EQUAL QUANTITY OF BROWN SUGAR OVER POTATOES IN EACH PAN. CUT BUTTER OR  MARGARINE INTO SMALL PIECES; SPRINKLE AN EQUAL QUANTITY OVER BROWN SUGAR  IN EACH PAN. DO NOT COVER.    6.  BAKE IN OVER 375 F FOR 45 MINUTES OR UNTO BROWN SUGAR AND BUTTER  OR MARGARINE MELTS.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 3, 22 LB 8 OZ COOKED FRESH SWEET POTATOES (28 LB 2 OZ  A.P.) OR DRAINED, CANNED SWEET POTATOES (24-NO. 3 SQUAT CN SYRUP PACK OR  19-NO. 2 1/2 CN SYRUP PACK) MAY BE USED.    NOTE:  2.  OTHER SIZES AND TYPED OF PANS MAY BE USED. SEE RECIPE CARD  A02500.
