Salt, Oatmeal *, Chocolate chips, Hershey Bar, Sugar, Eggs, Flour, Baking soda, Vanilla, Butter, Pecans, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, 
*Blended Oatmeal: Measure and blend in a blender to a fine powder. Cream  butter and both sugars.  Add eggs and vanilla. Mix together with flour,  oatmeal, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Add chips, candy and nuts.  Roll into balls and place two (2) inches apart on a cookie sheet. bake for  six (6) minutes at 375 degrees. Makes 112 cookies, but recipe can halved.
