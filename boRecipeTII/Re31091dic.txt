Banana Rice Pudding
Rice milk, Bananas, Nutmeg, Short grain rice, 
Preheat over to 350F.  Mix all together well and pour into a 1 1/2 qt  casserole.  Bake for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally, until rice is  tender.  Makes 4 cups.    Posted by Lucinda J.
