Tropical Touch
(16-oz) frozen pound cake;, (3.125-oz) vanilla pudding, milk, (20-oz) crushed pineapple in, Whipping cream; divided, Pineapple juice, Cream sherry, Raspberry jam; melted, Maraschino cherries, 
Prepare pudding according to package directions using milk and pineapple  juice as liquid. Cool in saucepan over ice water, stirring.     Whipe 1 cup cream and fold into cooled pudding. Cut pound cake in half  lengthwise. Drizzle sherry over cake. Cut into bite-size chunks. Spoon 1  cup pudding into a 2-1/2 to 3-quart glass serving bowl. Top with half of  chunked cake and half of jam. Reserve 1/2 cup undrained pineapple for  garnish. Spoon half of remaining undrained pineapple into bowl. Spoon on  half of remaining pudding. Repeat layering, ending with pudding mixture.  Refrigerate overnight. Whip remaining 1 cup cream and spread on top. Drain  reserved pineapple and use as a garnish with cherries, if desired. Serve  chilled. Serves 8.
