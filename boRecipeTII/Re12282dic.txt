Zucchini Muffins 2
Flour, Granulated sugar, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Baking soda, Salt, Egg white, Applesauce, Vanilla, Zucchini, 
Prep time: 10 minutes Bake time: 20 minutes Total time: 30 minutes Oven temp: 350F / 180C /  Gas mark 4  1. Preheat oven. Prepare muffin pans with cooking spray and flour.   2. In a  mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, cinna mon, baking soda  and salt.   3. In another mixing bowl, combine egg whites, applesauce, vanilla,  and zucchini. Mix in dry ingredients with wet ingredients just until combined. Use an ice cream scoop to fill batter evenly among pans .   4. Bake 20  minutes.
