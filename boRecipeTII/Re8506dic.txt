Potato-Meat Patties
Vegetable oil, Pepper, meat, milk, Onion, potatoes, Egg yolks, 
Mix potatoes, meat, onion, egg yolks, milk, and pepper.  Shape into 6  patties, using about 1/2 cup potato mixture for each.    Heat fat in frypan over moderate heat.  Cook patties until lightly browned  on one side; turn and brown the other side.  Serve hot.    Calories per serving: About 175    Source: FOOD -- by U.S. Department of Agriculture  Typed for you by Karen Mintzias    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/usdafood.zip
