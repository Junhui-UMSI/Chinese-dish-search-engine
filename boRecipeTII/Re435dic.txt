Lemon Italian Ice
Water, Light corn syrup, Sugar, Lemon juice, 
In heavy, medium sized saucepan, stir together water, corn syrup and sugar.  Heat to boiling over medium high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer,  uncovered, 5 minutes. Remove from heat; cool for 30 minutes. Stir in lemon  juice. Pour into 9 inch square baking pan. Cover surface with plastic wrap.  Freeze 3 to 4 hours, or until frozen but still soft in center. Spoon into  blender container; cover. Blend on high speed until mixture becomes white  and creamy. Turn back into 9 inch square pan. Cover with plastic wrap.
