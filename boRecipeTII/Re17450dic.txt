Walnut Studded Green Beans
olive oil, Turkey bacon; cooked crisp, Thinly sliced red bell, Large pieces diamond walnuts, Salt, Garlic, Green beans; halved, 
Toast walnuts: spread on baking sheet. Bake 8-10 min. at 350o. F, turning  often. Place beans and salt in skillet with 1/4 cup water. Cover and cook  over medium high heat 3-4 minutes or until beans are tender-crisp. Drain.  Add garlic, olive oil, turkey bacon, bell pepper, and walnuts. Cook  uncovered, stirring frequently 2-3 minutes.    Makes 4 to 6 servings.  Posted to brand-name-recipes by Beach Princess<barbra@pipeline.com> on Jan  23, 1998
