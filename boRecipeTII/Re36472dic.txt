Beer-Batter Fried Shrimp
Salad oil, Flour, all-purpose, Shrimp, Beer, Salt, 
1. In small bowl, mix flour, beer, and salt.   2. Shell and devein shrimp; rinse with running cold water and pat dry with  paper towels.   3. In 2-quart saucepan over medium heat, heat 2 inches salad oil to 375F  on deep-fat thermometer (or heat oil in deep-fat fryer set at 375F).   4. Dip shrimp, one at a time, into batter and drop into hot oil. Fry shrimp  until lightly browned, turning shrimp once, about 1 minute. Drain shrimp on  paper towels.
