Aunt Poos Peanut Butter Pie
(8-oz) cream cheese, (9-oz) Cool Whip, (9-inch) graham cracker pie, Jar (12-oz) crunchy peanut, milk, 
Mix first 4 ingredients with mixer. Fold in Cool Whip. Pour into crust and  chill until firm. Usually ready to eat in an hour!    MRS N.R.
