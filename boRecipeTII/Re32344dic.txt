Barley Casserole
Onion, Pearl barley; rinsed, Envelope French onion soup, Butter, Chicken broth, (10 oz.) mushroom pieces;, 
From: becktech@txdirect.net    Melt butter in frying pan. Add onions and barley. Cook until onions are  transparent and barley is browned. Add chicken broth, mushrooms and onion  soup mix. Bring to a boil. Turn into 2 quart casserole. Cover and bake at  350 F for 30 minutes. Remove cover and bake another 30 minutes.    Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com on Mar 21, 1998
