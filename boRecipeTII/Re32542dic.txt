Sweet Glazed Chicken ( Meat)
Horseradish, Apricot (or other) preserves, Stick pareve margarine,, salt and pepper, Whole Chicken breasts ,, 
source: Naomi Sanders    Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, etc. and broil. After broiling,  season with mixture of melted margarine, horseradish, and preserves. Bake  at 300 degrees for 10 minutes, basting twice. Remaining glaze may be heated  and brushed on before serving. Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #084 by  alotzkar@direct.ca (Al) on Mar 13, 1997
