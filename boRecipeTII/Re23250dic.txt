Tuna Noodle Bake
Noodles; cooked, Sour cream, milk, Salt, Tuna, Sliced mushrooms, Pepper, Parmesan cheese, Butter, Bread crumbs, 
Mix cooked noodles, tuna, sour cream, milk, mushrooms, salt and pepper, and  pour into an ungreased 2 qt casserole. Mix bread crumbs, cheese and butter  and sprinkle over casserole. Bake uncovered in 350? oven for 35-40 minutes  or until bubbly.
