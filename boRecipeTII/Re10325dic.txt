Quick Broccoli and Rice Casserole
Whole Milk, Parmesan, Chicken, Frozen Broccoli with Cheese Sauce, Rice, Quick-Cooking, 
From: kmeade@ids2.idsonline.com (The Meades)    Date: Sun, 19 May 1996 15:30:17 -0400    NOTE: Cooking times are based on the use of a 625-750 watt microwave oven  and food quantities for 2 servings. Adjust cooking times as required.    Follow package directions for frozen Broccoli w/Cheese Sauce.    Turn into a (1 Qt.) casserole. Add the chicken, ham or turkey. Add the  rice, milk and cheese. Blend thoroughly. Cover. Microwave on high until the  rice is tender (about 6 minutes), stirring once.
