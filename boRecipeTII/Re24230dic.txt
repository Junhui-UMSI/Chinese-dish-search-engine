Tree-Mendous Chicken Soup
Onion -- chopped, Garnish with chopped fresh, 12-oz pkg, Chicken broth, Parsley -- (optional), Chicken breasts -- skinned, Carrots -- chopped, Thyme, Tree-shaped colored pasta, Black pepper, Celery -- chopped, Water, Cloves fresh garlic, Salt, 
Place all of the ingredients except for the pasta trees and parsley in a  soup pot. Bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 45 minutes, stirring  occasionally to avoid scorching. After 45 minutes, remove chicken breasts,  cool slightly, and remove the meat from the bones. Bring the soup to a boil  and add the past a tress. Cook 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until  the pasta is done to your liking. Add the chicken meat back. Pour into  bowls and garnish with parsley if desired. Serve hot.
