Tropical Fruit Dip
milk, Instant vanilla pudding;, 6 oz size-thawed, Sour cream, Frozen orange juice, Fruit; * see note, 
Combine thawed orange juice concentrate, milk and pudding mix. Beat with  mixer until smooth, about 2 minutes. Stir in sour cream. Chill 2 hours or  longer. * Cut a fresh pineapple in half lengthwise. Scrape out fruit,  leaving shell intact. Use pineapple shell to serve dip. Cut the pineapple  fruit in chunks to be used in dip.     Bananas, marshmallows or angel food cake chunks may also be used.
