Pot Roast, Korean Style
Chuck arm pot roast (2 1/2-3, Vegetable oil, Carrots (cut in chunks), Sesame seeds, Cloves minced garlic, Sugar, Pepper, Beef broth, Noodles, Dark sesame oil, Lb), Onion (quartered), Salt, 
Mash sesame oil, garlic, salt, pepper & sugar to make sticky paste.  Rub into both sides of meat, being sure to get mixture into crevices  between meat & bone.  Marinate in refrigerator for 2-12 hours. Coat  pan with vegetable oil. Heat. Add beef & brown on both sides.  Surround beef with carrots & onions. Pour in broth.  Cover & bake in  350 oven for 1 1/2-2 hours or until tender. Sprinkle with seeds.  Serve with noodles, spooning pan juices over meat & noodles.
