Apple-Cinnamon Baked French Toast
Skim milk, Butter, Vanilla extract, Sugar, Apples, Ground nutmeg, Ground cinnamon, French bread, Eggs, Nonstick cooking spray, 
1. Slice bread into 1 1/2-inch slices. Coat a 9-by-13-inch pan with  nonstick cooking spray and tightly pack bread into pan.    2. In a large bowl, stir together eggs, milk,  1/2 cup sugar and the  vanilla. Pour half of the egg mixture over the bread slices.    3. Evenly distribute apple slices over bread. Top with remaining  egg  mixture.    4. In a small bowl combine remaining  1/2 cup sugar, the cinnamon  and  nutmeg. Sprinkle over apples. Dot with butter. Cover and refrigerate  overnight.    5. The next day, uncover pan and bake in a preheated 350-degree oven for 1  hour. Remove from oven and let stand for 10 to 15 minutes. Cut into squares  and serve warm.    from Joseph Stefanic, Oak Park, Illinois    Source: Chicago Sun Times, August 7, 1996  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #296    Date: Mon, 28 Oct 1996 16:20:21 +0000    From: Linda Place <placel@worldnet.att.net>
