Pineapple Sandwich Salad
Pineapple slices, Cherry Juice, Cream cheese 8 oz., Chopped maraschino cherries, 
Add the cherry juice to the creamed cheese, blending until smooth. Add the  chopped cherries, mix well. Place 4 pineapple slices each on a bed of  lettuce on a serving plate, Spread each slice with some cheese mixture and  place another slice of pineapple on top of each. In center of each put a  spoonful of miracle whip and garnish with a cherry wedge.
