Beef Jerky
Soy sauce, Ginger powder, Fresh ground pepper, Beef, Brown sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, Salt, 
Cut beef into 1/4" thick strips.  Mix all ingredients together, making sure  that all surfaces are coated.  Marinate in a covered dish in the fridge for  at least 12 hours.  Lay on a rack on a cookie sheet - do not overlap. Bake  in a 150 F. oven for 6 hours, then turn and bake for another 4 hours. Store  in a sealer in the fridge.  Will stay fresh for about 2 weeks.
