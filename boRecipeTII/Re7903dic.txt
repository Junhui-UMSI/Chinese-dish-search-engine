Post Grape-Nuts Low Fat Crust
Frozen apple juice, POST GRAPE-NUTS Brand Flakes, 
PLACE cereal in food processor container fitted with steel blade; cover.  Process until finely crushed.    MIX cereal crumbs and thawed apple juice concentrate in small bowl until  well blended. Press onto bottom and up side of 9-inch pie plate.    BAKE at 350F for 5 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool on wire rack.
