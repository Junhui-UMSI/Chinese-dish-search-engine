Vinaigrette Dressing #2
Wine vinegar, Paprika, (scant) olive oil, Pepper, Lemon juice, (scant) cooking oil, Dry mustard, Cayenne pepper, Garlic, Salt, 
Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly. (Note: Liquid ingredients are  in the same proportions as the "Good Seasons" bottles, substituting lemon  juice for water and olive oil for 1/2 of the oil.) Yield: 1 cup.
