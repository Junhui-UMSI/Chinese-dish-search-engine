Zesty Stuffed Peppers
Rice, Onions, Ground beef, tomato sauce, Salt, Pepper, whole kernel corn, Steak sauce, Green peppers, 
In a large kettle over medium-high heat, blanch peppers in boiling salted  water for 3-5 minutes. Drain and rinse in cold water; set aside. In a  skillet over medium heat, brown beef and onion; drain. Add remaining  ingredients and mix well. Loosely stuff into peppers. Place in a 9-inch  square baking pan. Bake, uncovered, at 350 for 30-35 minutes or until  peppers are done and filling is hot.
