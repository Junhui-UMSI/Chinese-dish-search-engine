Wild Mushroom and Pea Bolognese over Fresh Pasta
Minced onions, Salt, Frozen green peas, Cooked fresh pasta; hot, Garlic, olive oil, Black Pepper, Fresh grated, Recipe of Bolognese; warm, Finely chopped fresh parsley, Sliced assorted wild, 
EMERIL LIVE SHOW #EMIB13, FOOD OF EMILIA ROMAGNA, ITALY    In a saute pan, heat the oil. Add the onions. Season with salt and pepper.  saute for 1 minute. Add the mushrooms and continue to saute for 2 minutes.  Season with salt and pepper. Stir in the garlic and peas. saute for 1  minute and remove from the heat. Turn the mushroom mixture into the  Bolognese and mix well. Toss the pasta with the sauce. Mound the pasta into  shallow bowls and garnish with cheese and parsley.    Yields: 4 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 27, 1998
