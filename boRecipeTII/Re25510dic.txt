Avocado n Everything Pizza
Shredded mozzarella cheese, Sliced ripe olives, Chopped green onion, (8-oz) tomato sauce, Avocado; seeded, peeled and, Fresh basil leaves; optional, olive oil, Hot water, Sliced muchrooms, Tomato; sliced, Buttermilk baking mix, 
From: arielle@taronga.com (Stephanie da Silva) (COLLECTION)    Date: Thu, 22 Jul 1993 18:23:06 GMT    Heat oven to 425F. Stir together buttermilk mix and water with fork in  small bowl. Pat or roll into 12-inch circle on ungreased baking sheet or  pizza pan. Mix together tomato sauce and green onion; spread over pizza  dough. Top with cheese, mushrooms, olives and tomato slices. Drizzle olive  oil over top. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until edge of crust is golden brown.  Remove pizza from oven and arrange avocado slices over top. Garnish with  basil leaves and serve.
