Autumn Dump Cake
Sliced apples, undrained, Stick margarine or butter,, Box spice cake mix, Pecans, 
by Shelley Sparks    In a 9x13 inch cake pan, dump in apples and spread evenly. Sprinkle with  dry spice cake mix. Pour melted margarine all over dry mix. Top with  chopped pecans. Bake at 375 for about 40 minutes or untill bubbly. Serve  warm with vanilla ice cream.
