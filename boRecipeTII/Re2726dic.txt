Peanut Butter Brownies
Margarine, Vanilla, Flour, Eggs, Peanut butter, Sugar, Brown sugar, Salt, Chocolate chips, peanuts, Baking Powder, 
Combine all but chocolate chips and peanuts.  Spread in greased jelly roll  pan. Bake 25 min. at 350F. Sprinkle with chips. When they soften, spread  them over to cover cookies. Sprinkle with peanuts, if desired. Cool and cut  into bars.
