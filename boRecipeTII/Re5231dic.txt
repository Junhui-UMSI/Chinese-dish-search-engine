Pimento Cheese Sandwich Filling
Processed American cheese;, Chopped olives (optional), Grated onion (optional), Pickles (optional), (5.125-oz) evaporated milk, Salt, (3-oz) pimientos, 
Put milk into top of double boiler.  Add diced cheese, and keep hot until  melted. Drain pimientos and mash. Add to cheese and milk with the salt.  Pickles, chopped olives, or grated onion may be added, if desired. Yield:  1-3/4 cups or filling for 10 sandwiches.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
