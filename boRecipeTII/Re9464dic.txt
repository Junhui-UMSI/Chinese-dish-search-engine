Pumpkin Bread #04
Water, Ground cinnamon, Salt, Honey, Xanthan gum, Eggs, Pureed cooked or canned, Bean flour, Baking soda, Nutmeg, Rice flour, Corn oil, 
From: Tammy Schmidt <SFF@dean.watstar.uwaterloo.ca>    Date: Wed, 21 Jun 1995 10:46:15 PDT  These recipes are a sample of what is available in "Going Against the  Grain", a great source for recipes for those who cannot eat  wheat/dairy/sugar     Grease and flour 2 loaf pans.  Stir together flour, sugar, baking soda,  salt and spices.  Stir together pumpkin, corn oil and water, add eggs one  at a time. Make a well in center of flour mixture, add pumpkin mixture and  stir. Pour into prepared pans and bake for 1 hour at 325F.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
