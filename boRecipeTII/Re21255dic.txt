Ultimate Brownies
Eggs, Milk chocolate bars; divided, Cold water, MIRACLE WHIP Salad Dressing, Fudge brownie mix, 
PREHEAT oven to 350F.    MIX together salad dressing, eggs and water until well blended. Stir in  brownie mix, mixing just until moistened.    COARSELY chop two chocolate bars; stir into brownie mixture. Pour into  greased 13x9-inch baking pan.    BAKE 30 to 35 minutes or until edges begin to pull away from sides of pan.  Immediately top with one chocolate bar, chopped. Let stand about 5 minutes  or until melted; spread evenly over brownies. Garnish with walnut halves,  if desired. Cool. Cut into squares.
