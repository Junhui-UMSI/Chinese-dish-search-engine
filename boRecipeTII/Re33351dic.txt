Basic Tomato Sauce
Garlic, Plum tomatoes, Sugar, salt and pepper, Onion, olive oil, 
Date: 18 Mar 96 13:06:45 EST    From: "Lisabeth Crawford (Pooh)" <104105.1416@compuserve.com>  Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.  SAute the onion and  garlic until soft, about 5 minutes.  Add the tomatoes, sugar, salt, and  pepper to taste. Simmer for 30 minutes, until thickened, stirring often to  break up the tomatoes.
