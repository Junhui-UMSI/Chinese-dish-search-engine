White Brownies
Eggs, (12 oz) vanilla chips, Pecans, Butter or margarine, Vanilla, All-purpose flour, Salt, Baking Powder, Squares white baking, Sugar, 
In a double boiler or microwave, melt chocolate and butter; cool for 20  minutes. In a mixing bowl, beat eggs and sugar until thick and  lemon-colored, about 4 minutes. Gradually beat in melted chocolate and  vanilla. Combine flour, baking powder and salt; add to chocolate mixture.  Stir in chips and pecans. Pour into a greased 15x10x1-inch baking pan. Bake  at 350? for 40-45 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on a wire rack.
