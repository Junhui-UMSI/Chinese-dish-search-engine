Vegtable Dip/ Salad Dressing
Sugar, Mayonaisse; (I use Hellmans), Vinegar; (I use apple cider, 
This might be an addition to your "mayonaisse" dips. It is the dressing for  a brocolli, bacon, onion, sunflower seed salad that I make frequently.
