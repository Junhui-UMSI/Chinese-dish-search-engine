Applesauce Barbecue Sauce
Salt, Butter or margarine, Thinly sliced onion, Lemon juice, Catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, Applesauce, 
1. Place onions and butter in a deep, 1 1/2-quart, heat- resistant,  non-metallic casserole. 2. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 2 minutes or  until onion is tender. 3. Add lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, catsup,  applesauce and salt. 4. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 7 minutes or  until sauce is bubbly. Makes 3 cups
