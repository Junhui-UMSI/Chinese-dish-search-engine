Apple Nut Bread
Baking soda, Vanilla, Flour, apples, Salt, Brown sugar, Egg, Sour milk, Cinnamon, Pecans, Salad oil, 
Stir ingredients together in order listed. Pour into 2 greased 8" loaf  pans; sprinkle with 1/2 cup sugar mixed with 1 Tbsp melted butter. Bake at  350 degrees for 60 minutes or until done    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Beth Ide" <rev_ide@email.msn.com> on Aug  17, 1998
