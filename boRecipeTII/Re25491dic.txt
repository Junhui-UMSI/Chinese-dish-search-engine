Avocado-Lime Jello Salad
Onion; chopped fine, Mayonnaise, Avacado; mashed, (small) lime jello, Boiling water, Green pepper; chopped fine, Salt and pepper to taste, (small) cream cheese, Pimentos; chopped, Celery; chopped fine, 
Dissolve jello in water. Soften cream cheese with mayonnaise. Mix together  & then add vegetables to jello. Pour into 9 small molds or 1 large mold.  Chill until firm.    MRS C.R.
