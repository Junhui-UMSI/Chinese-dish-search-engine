White Bean, Black Olive and Basil Salad
Olive oil, extra virgin, Radicchio heads; cut into, Vinegar, red wine, Olives, black; pitted,, Vegetables stock, salt and pepper, Basil, fresh; chopped, Shallots; finely chopped, Tomatoes, vine-ripened;, Beans, cannellini (19-oz ea), 
In a medium sized bowl, combine beans, shallots, basil and olives. In a  small bowl, whisk together vinegar, oil and vegetable stock. Pour over the  bean mixture and toss until all ingredients are coated. Season with salt  and pepper. Divide the radicchio equally among 4 plates, top with the bean  salad and garnish with tomato wedges.    Serves 4.    Per serving: 467 cal; 26 g prot; 9 g fat; 74 g carb; 239 mg sodium; 0 mg  chol.    Adapted from a recipe in Eating Well/July-Aug 1993/MM by DEEANNE
