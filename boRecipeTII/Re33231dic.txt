Basic Pizza Dough
All-purpose flour, active dry yeast, Salt, water , 
Makes two 12" pizza crusts. 8 Servings each.    1.  In a large bowl, combine 2 cups flour, the yeast and salt. With  electric mixer, slowly beat in water; beat 2 minutes, occasionally scraping  bowl with rubber spatula. With mixer on medium speed, beat in 1/2 cup  flour; beat 2 minutes, until dough is stiff. Stir in 1 1/2 cups flour. 2.  Sprinkle clean work surface with 1/4 cup flour. Knead dough
