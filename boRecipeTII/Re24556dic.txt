Asparagus Stir Fry
Vegetable oil, Asparagus, Sliced garlic, Salt, 
Rinse asparagus in cold water. Snap off tough ends. Slice asparagus into  1-inch pieces diagonally. Heat oil in wok over high heat 30 seconds.  Stir-fry garlic until golden (30 seconds). Add asparagus and salt. Stir-fry  3 minutes or until asparagus is crisp-tender. Serve hot from wok. Serves 4.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary." <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 4,  1998
