Quick Crescent Pecan Pie Bars
Vanilla, FILLING, Sugar, Margarine or butter; melted, Crust, Egg, Pillsbury refrigerated, Pecans, Corn syrup, 
Heat oven to 375 F. Unroll dough into 2 long rectangles. Place in ungreased  13x9-inch pan; press over bottom and 1/2 inch up sides to form crust.  Firmly press perforations to seal. Bake at 375 F. for 5 minutes.    Meanwhile, in medium bowl combine all filling ingredients. Pour over  partially baked crust. Bake an additional 18 to 22 minutes or until golden  brown. Cool completely.
