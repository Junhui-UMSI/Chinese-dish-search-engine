Raspberry Ice Cream
Fresh raspberries, Cream, vanilla ice cream, ICE CREAM COOKBOOK-----, Sugar, 
Add the additional cup of sugar to the vanilla recipe. Crush berries in  collander. Add berries to vanilla ice cream mix just before freezing. For a  redder color, add a few drops of red food coloring.
