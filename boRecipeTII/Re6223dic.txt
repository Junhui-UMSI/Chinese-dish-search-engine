Pizza Wiches
Ripe olives; chopped, Garlic salt (or plain salt), Oregano, Ground beef, Cheddar cheese, Tomato soup, 
Brown beef & then let cool. Shred cheese. Mix all ingredients together &  add meat. Let stand at least 4 hours in refrigerator before using. You can  use a little at a time, just keep the rest refrigerated. Spread on half a  hamburger bun, English muffin or French bread. Broil until cheese is melted  & bubbly.    MRS R.R.
