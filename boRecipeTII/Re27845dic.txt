The Very Best Home Fries
Vegetable oil, pepper and salt, Potatoes, Garlic, Salt, Butter, Green onion, 
Scrub potatoes.  In pot of boiling salted water, cook potatoes for 15  minutes or until tender but firm; drain well.    Refrigerate until cold.  Cut into 1/2 inch cubes.    In nonstick skillet, heat oil and butter over high heat; cook potatoes,  turning to coat all, for 3 to 4 minutes.    Cook for about 8 minutes longer, turning often, or until crisp and golden.  Remove from heat; stir in onion, garlic, salt, pepper and paprika. Makes 4  servings.
