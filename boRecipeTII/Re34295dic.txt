Bbq Sauce
Dried red New Mexican;, Catsup, Brown sugar, Onion; minced, Bacon fat or veg. oil, Dry mustard, Garlic; chopped, Tomato sauce; canned, Pickle relish; optional, Paprika and, Garlic powder, Chili powder OR, Cumin; and, White vinegar, Worcestershire, Dried tepins; pequins, or, 
Cover the chiles with hot water and let soften for 15 min. Put them in a  blender and whirl until smooth with 1 c of the hot water. In a saucepan,  saute the onion and garlic in the fat. Add the blended chiles and all the  rest of the ingredients. Bring to a boil and lower the heat to low. Simmer  1/2 hour. Cool and then whirl in a blender until smooth.
