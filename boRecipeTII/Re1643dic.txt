Cardamom Carrots
Carrots, peeled and, ( 1/4" thick), Unsalted butter, Freshly grated orange rind, Cardamom pods, hulled and, (or 1/2-1 tsp. ground, Salt to taste, 
Steam carrots for 5 to 7 minutes or just until tender. Melt butter; add  grated orange rind and cardamom. Toss carrots in butter mixture. Add salt  to taste. Place in serving dish. May be prepared ahead and reheated over  low heat.  Serves 8  Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Good  Friends Cookbook.
