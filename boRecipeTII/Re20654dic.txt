Apple Crisp
Cinnamon, Salt, Egg, Apples, Sugar, Sugar, Water, Baking Powder, Flour, 
Slice apples into greased baking dish.  To make topping:  Combine  ingredients, add to well beaten egg and mix until crumbly.  Spread over  apples.  Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar mixture.  Dot with butter and  pour 1/4 cup water over top.  Bake at 350F for about 50 minutes or until  top is browned and apples are done.  Serve with cream, or vanilla ice  cream.  From: Syds Cookbook.
