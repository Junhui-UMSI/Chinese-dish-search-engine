Peanut Butter Muffins Sallie
Flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, Salt, Water, Egg, Peanut butter, Butter, 
Sift together flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Beat eggs; add peanut  butter, milk, water and butter; beat well. Add peanut butter mixture to  flour mixture, beat only until blended. Grease muffin tins and fill 2/3  full. Bake in 400 degree oven for about 20 minutes. Serve with butter and  grape jelly!!!!Friend who gave this to me says theyre great.
