Layered Pizza Dip
Light sour cream, dried basil and oregano, mozzarella cheese, Green onions, pepperoni, black olives, pizza sauce , light cream cheese, 
In bowl, stir together cream cheese, sour cream, basil and oregano; spread  on bottom of 10 inch pie plate. Spread pizza sauce over top, leaving 1/2  inch border. Sprinkle with mozzarella. Top with green onions, then  pepperoni and olives.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or until  chilled.  (Can be refrigerated for up to 1 day.)    If serving warm, heat in 350F oven for 10 minutes or until cheese is  melted.
