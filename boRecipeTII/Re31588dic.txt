Swiss n Chicken Casserole
Cooked chicken; chopped, milk, Chopped walnuts; optional, Natural swiss cheese; shred, LARRY LUTTROPP FVKC70A, Miracle whip or light, Celery; sliced, Onion, Croutons, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Mix all ingredients. Spoon into 2-quart  casserole. Sprinkle with chopped walnuts, if desired. Bake 40 minutes or  until thoroughly heated. To make ahead: prepare as directed except do not  top with walnuts and do not bake; cover. Refrigerate overnight. When ready  to serve, microwave on high 20 minutes or until thoroughly heated, turning  dish after 10 minutes. Top with toasted walnuts if desired. Source: Kraft  mailer, 1995.
