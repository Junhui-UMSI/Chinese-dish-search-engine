The Pie
Prepared graham cracker pie, (medium-size) whipped, Chocolate candy bar, Chocolate-covered toffee, 
Melt chocolate candy bar in top of double boiler. Cool. Mix thoroughly with  whipped topping & pour into pie shell. Grate chocolate-covered toffee candy  bars & sprinkle onto pie. Refrigerate, serve & enjoy!    SHIRLEY STOREY    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
