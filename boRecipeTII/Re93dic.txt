Kickin Asian Chicken Salad
Shallots; chopped, Dark sesame oil, Salt, Pepper, Firm tofu, Beets, Sweet potato; 1 inch thick, Shiitake mushrooms, Salad greens; (gourmet), Chicken breast; no skin, no, Prepared wasabi powder;, Honey, Red wine vinegar, 
*chicken breast should be cooked, ready to eat, such as Tyson.    Preheat oven to 400 degrees.    Place first 8 ingredients in a blender; process until smooth. Set aside.    Leave root end and 1 inch of stem on beets; scrub with a brush. Place beets  and sweet potatoes in a shallow baking dish. Bake at 400 degrees for 45  minutes. Add mushrooms and bake an additional 15 minutes. Cool to touch.  Peel beets and potato; cut into 1/2 inch cubes. Thinly slice mushrooms.    Combine beets, potato, mushrooms, greens, and chicken. Drizzle dressing  over salad; toss well. Serve immediately.    Yields 4 servings of 2 cups each. Calories 337 (15% from fat), Fat 5.6 g.,  Protein 31.2 g,Carb 41.4g,Fiber 4.2g,Chol 72 mg., Iron 3.1 mg,Sodium 366  mg, Calc 60mg.    NOTES : Note: Regular horseradish may be substituted.
