Syrian Chicken and Rice
Ground beef -- sirloin or, Nutmeg, Pine nuts -- toasted -OR-, PATTI - VDRJ67A-----, Cinnamon, Walnuts -- chopped, Chicken; cooked -- diced, Allspice, Roud, Burgandy wine, salt and pepper, Rice, Chicken broth, 
Brown beef and drain all fat. Add rice, broth, wine, allspice, nutmeg,  cinnamon, salt and pepper. Simmer 20 minutes or until rice is done. Add  nuts and cooked chicken. Stir and simmer 5 minutes or until chicken is  heated. Can be frozen.
