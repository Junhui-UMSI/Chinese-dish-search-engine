Wafer Wrapped Prawns
Sheets filo pastry, Chopped, Sesame oil, Oyster sauce, SAUCE, Tomato sauce, Prawns -- peeled & chopped, Chilli sauce, Green chile -- seeded and, 
In a bowl mix together prawns, chilli and oyster sauce.    Cut filo pastry into 4 inch squares. Place a heaped teaspoon of prawn  filling in the centre of each square, draw the corners of the pastry  together and twist to form little bundles. Place on a floured serving plate  and chill until required.    To make sauce, pour tomato sauce, chilli sauce, 2 tablespoons water and  sesame oil into a saucepan and heat gently for 3-4 minutes. serve hot with  the prawn bundles cooked in hot oil.
