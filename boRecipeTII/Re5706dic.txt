Pineapple-Mandarin Salad
Mandarin oranges in light, Unsweetened pineapple, Fat-free honey-Dijon, Torn Boston lettuce, 
Divide lettuce among plates. Divide oranges and pineapple evenly among  salads; drizzle with dressing. Yield: 4 servings.    Per serving: 126 Calories; 3g Fat (20% calories from fat); 2g Protein; 26g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 99mg Sodium    Recipe by: Cooking Light, Sept. 1995, page 74    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #400 by igor@digex.net on Jan 28, 1997.
