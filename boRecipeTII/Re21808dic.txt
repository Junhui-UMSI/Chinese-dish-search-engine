Apple-Habanero Chutney
Salt, Fresh apples; peeled and, Water, Diced yellow onion, Pepper, Fresh haberno chile; diced, Cinnamon, 
Saw these on CBS morning show. Todays recipes and archived recipes can be  found at cbs.com under Recipes on a Shoestring.    Lightly saute onions, add apple and chile, cook for about 5 minutes, add  water and cinnamon, reduce until liquid gels, season and chill.
