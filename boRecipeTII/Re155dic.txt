Kraut Salad
Sauerkraut, Celery, Green pepper, Onion, Pimento, Salt, Sugar, White vinegar, Water, 
Combine sauerkraut, celery, pepper, onion and pimento in bowl.    Mix salt, sugar, vinegar and water in saucepan.   Cook mixture over low heat, stirring until sugar is dissolved.    Cool.    Add 1/4 c. vegetable oil.   Mix into vegetables and refrigerate overnight.    From a book of my mothers (Judy Hosey) titled <Senator Richard Matsuuras Favorite Recipes>, mostly Japanese and Hawaiian.
