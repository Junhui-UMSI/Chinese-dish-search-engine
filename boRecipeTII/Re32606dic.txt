Sweet Onion Cake
Granulated sugar, Salt, Butter, Shortening, milk, Chopped sweet onions, All purpose flour, Baking Powder, Butter, Eggs, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour two 9 inch round pans.    Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt in large bowl. In heavy  skillet, melt 4 tablespoons butter. Saute onions until lightly crisp but  not brown.  Pour in mixing bowl to cool. Cream sugar with 1/2 stick butter  and shortening.  Beat until fluffy.    Add eggs, beat slowly, then add flour mixture and milk. Beat for 3 minutes.  Add sauteed onions.  Stir thoroughly. Pour into pans.    Bake about 30 minutes or until wooden stick comes out clean. Cool in pans  about 10 minutes; turn out on wire rack and cool completely. Frost with  Creamed Icing.    Source: Rock Springs Sweet Onion Festival Cook Book 1992
