"Houstons" Honey Mustard Dressing
Honey, Mayonnaise, olive oil, Cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, Garlic salt, 
Mix all ingredients together with wire whisk until mayo cant be seen.  Refrigerate several hours.    Great also as a dip with chicken fingers and as a spread on ham or turkey  club sandwiches.    FROM:    BARBARA WINEMILLER   (HDJF80A)
