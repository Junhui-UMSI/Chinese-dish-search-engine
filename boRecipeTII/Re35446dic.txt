Beef in Mushroom Gravy
Env dry onion soup mix, Water, 10.75oz can condensed cream, Boneless round steak, Mashed potatoes; optional, 
Cut steak into six serving-size peices; place in a slow cooker. Combine  soup mix, soup and water; pour over beef. Cover and cook on LOW for 7-8  hours or until meat is tender. Serve with mashed potatoes if desired.    Recipe by: Quick Cooking - Premiere Issue    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by The Taillons <taillon@ACCESS.MOUNTAIN.NET> on  Jan 24, 1998
