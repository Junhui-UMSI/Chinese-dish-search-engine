Chocolate Mint Brownies
Sugar, Butter or margarine, Egg, Flour, Salt, Vanilla, Butter or margarine, Creme de menthe, Chocolate chips, Butter or margarine, 
Recipe By : TANTE ERNAS    1. Preheat oven to 350    2. Grease 9 X 13 inch pan    3. Mix first 7 ingredients together    4. Pour into pan, bake for 30 minutes & cool completely    5. Mix next 3 ingredients & spread over cold cake    6. Let harden completely    7. Melt the next 2 items in the top of a double boiler & spread over the  creme de menthe layer    8.
