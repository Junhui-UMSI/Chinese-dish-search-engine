Tuna or Salmon Salad
Hard boiled eggs; chopped, -(up to), Dill pickle; chopped, (7-oz) tuna --or--, Mayonnaise, (7.75-oz) salmon, Ribs celery; chopped fine, 
Mix all ingredients together, adding more mayonnaise if necessary. Chill.  Use to fill tomato cups, as a sandwich spread or atop lettuce leaves.    MRS N.R.
