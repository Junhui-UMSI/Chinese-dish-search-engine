Zesty Pineapple Ham Glaze
Crushed pineapple, Dijon mustard, Ground cloves, 
Combine ingredients in a small bowl. About 30 minutes before ham is done,  remove from oven. Score surface; spoon glaze over entie ham. Return to  oven, basting occasionally during the last 30 minutes of baking. Yield: 1/2  cup.
