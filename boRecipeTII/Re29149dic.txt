Baked Rigatoni with Ham, Tomatoes and Feta Cheese
Mozzarella cheese; grated, Whipping cream, Feta cheese; crumbled, Diced ham, Thyme; dried, Rigatoni, Plum tomatoes; chopped, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Butter 13x9x2 inch baking dish.Cook pasta in  boiling salted until just tender. Drain. Return to pot and mix in ham,  tomatoes, feta and mozzarella cheeses, and thyme. Pour cream over. Mix well  and pour into prepared baking dish.    Cover with foil and bake 15 minutes. Uncover and stir to distribute cheeses  evenly. Cover again with foil and bake another 30 minutes.    NOTES : Quick and very good.    Recipe by: Bon Appetit    Posted to FOODWINE Digest  by Dan Taylor <dant69@IDT.NET> on Dec 7, 1997
