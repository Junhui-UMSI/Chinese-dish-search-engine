Prosciutto-Black Pepper Bread
Salt, olive oil, WATER; WARM, Sugar, Bread flour, Black pepper, Yeast, Prosciutto, chopped, 
Do not trim fat from prosciutto before chopping.  Bring all ingredients to room temperature.  Pour all  ingredients into bakery except prosciutto, in order.  Set "baking control" at eleven oclock. Select "white  bread" and push Start.  Add prosciutto at beep, 88  minutes into cycle.
