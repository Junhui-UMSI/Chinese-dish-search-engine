White Bean Turkey Chili
Vegetable oil, Dried oregano leaves, Yellow bell pepper; chopped, salt and pepper, Flour, Dried cumin, Cubed turkey, Chopped medium tomato, Onions, (15 ounces) Great Northern, (141/2 ounces) chicken broth, Chili powder, Jalapeno chili; finely, Garlic, Green Pepper, 
More LA Times recipes:    Saute onion, yellow bell pepper, jalapeno and garlic in oil in large  saucepan until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in flour and cook 1 to 2  minutes longer, stirring constantly. Add chicken broth and herbs to  saucepan; heat to boiling. Boil, stirring constantly, until thickened. Stir  in beans and turkey and cook 3 to 4 minutes; season to taste with salt and  pepper. Spoon chili into bowls; top each with 1 tablespoon chopped green  pepper and tomato. Makes 4 servings. Per serving: 262 calories; 9 g fat  (1.9 g saturated fat; 31 percent calories from fat); 28.5 g carbohydrates;  39 mg cholesterol; 1,293 mg sodium.
