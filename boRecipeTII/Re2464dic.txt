Bohemia Rice Stuffing
Onion, Rice, Giblets, Chopped parsley, Egg, Butter, salt and pepper, Chicken or duck, 
Combine all ingredients except onion, giblets and duck. Boil onion and  giblets until almost tender; grind in food grinder. Add to rice mixture and  stir. Pack chicken or duck with stuffing as roast as usual. Left-over  stuffing can be baked in a buttered casserole.
