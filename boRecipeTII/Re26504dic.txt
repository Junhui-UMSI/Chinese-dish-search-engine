Bacon Vinaigrette
Cider vinegar, Vegetable oil, Bacon; diced, Cloves garlic; mashed, Brown sugar, Salt, Black peppercorn; cracked, Shallot; minced, 
To make vinaigrette: Render the bacon.  Remove the diced bacon from the pan  and reserve. Add the shallots and garlic to the bacon fat and sweat. Blend  in the brown sugar to melt. Whisk in the cider vinegar and then oil. Season  to taste with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
