Butterscotch Pumpkin, Squash or Sweet Potato Pie
Margarine; melted, (1/4), Honey; (1/4), Dark Karo syrup; (3/4), up, Eggs, Vanilla extract, Salt, Mashed pumpkin, Pecans - optional, 
If you dont use the nuts you can put in a bit more pumpkin.    Mix all ingredients and turn into a pie shell. Bake at 375 F. for 1 hour or  until firm.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on Oct  28, 1998,
