Quick Sauerbraten
Beef gravy (10 3/4 oz.), Cider vinegar, Sugar, Water, Gingersnaps, crushed fine, Cooked pot roast (thin sl.), Cloves, 
Combine gravy, water, vinegar, gingersnaps, sugar and cloves in large  skillet.  Bring to boil, stirring. Simmer and stir abt. 5 min. or until  thickened and smooth. Add beef slices. Cook until meat is hot, basting with  sauce.  Serve with mashed potatoes.    C.C.--Lake Charles, La. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers BillSpalding *P CRBR 38  A
