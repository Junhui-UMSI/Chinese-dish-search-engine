Barley Basil Pilaf
Green onion; thickly slice, Fresh basil; minced, Chicken broth, Pearl barley; uncooked, Ground nutmeg, Vegetable oil, Salt, 
1. Heat oil in a 1 wuart saucepan over medium heat. Add barley and cook for  5 minutes; stirring to coat well.    2. Add chicken broth and salt, bring to a boil; cover and reduce heat.    3. Simmer for 30 minutes or until barley is tender and liquid is absorbed.    4. Remove from heat, stir in basil and nutmeg.    Notes: Use in place of any rice side dish. You can substantially reduce the  sodium in this by using low sodium chicken broth or homemade.
