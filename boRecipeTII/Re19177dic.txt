Vegetable Mix Casserole
Frozen lima beans, Sour cream, Salt and pepper to taste, Parmesan cheese, Diced onion, Frozen peas, Frozen cut green beans, Mayonnaise, 
Cook frozen vegetables according to package directions. Drain vegetables.  Saute onion until tender. Stir in sour cream, margarine, salt and pepper.  Fold in vegetables. Put in greased casserole. Top with Parmesan cheese.  Cook 20 minutes at 350 degrees.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 26,  1998
