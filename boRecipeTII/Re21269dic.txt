Ultimate Oatmeal Cookies
All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Raisins, Coarsely chopped walnuts, Brown Sugar, Cinnamon, milk, Butter Flavor Crisco, Egg, Salt, Quick Oats, not instant or, 
1. Heat oven to 375 degrees. 2. Combine Butter Flavor Crisco, brown sugar,  egg, milk and vanilla in large bowl. Beat at medium speed of electric mixer  until well blended. 3. Combine flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. Mix  into creamed mixture at low speed just until blended to make cookie dough.  4. Stir into cookie dough, one at a time, oats, raisens and nuts with  spoon.  Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto baking  sheet. 5. Bake at 375F for 13 minutes or until set. Cool for 2 minutes on  baking sheet.  Remove to cooling rack. Makes about 30 cookies.
