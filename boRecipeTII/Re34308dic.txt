Bbq Shrimp
Thyme, Chopped parsley, Large shrimp, Lemon juice, Cayenne, Minced garlic cloves, Salt, olive oil, Teapoon basil, Butter, Paprika, Worcestershire Sauce, Oregano, Hot pepper sauce, 
Place shrimp in large mixing bowl. Add remaining ingredients to pan.  Combine well & heat with out boiling, then chill. Pour over shrimp.  Marinate 30 minutes, stirring from time to time. Preheat oven to 325.  Pour shrimp & sauce on baking sheet; bake 8-10 minutes. Serve at once.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
