Tofu Poke
Tofu, firm, Onion, sliced thinly, Tomatoes, julienned, Ginger, fresh minced, Soy sauce, Sesame seeds, toasted, Lemon juice to taste, 
Drain and rinse the tofu. Cut it into 1/2 inch cubes and place the cubes in  a mixing bowl. Add the tomatoes and onions. In a separate container, mix  the ginger, soy sauce, sesame seeds and lemon juice. Pour this mixture over  the tofu and vegetables. Turn gently and allow to marinate in the refrigerator for a few hours before serving. Serve over couscous or pasta.
