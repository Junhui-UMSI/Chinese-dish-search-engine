Baked Apples - Crockpot Style
Raisins, Baking apples; washed and, Butter, Cinnamon, Sugar, 
Notes: By Pepper Klitsch, Palmerton. The Times News, PA.    Mix raisins and sugar; fill center of apples. Sprinkle with cinnamon and  dot with butter. Put in crockpot; add 1/2 cup water. Cover; cook on low 7  to 9 hours. (High: 3 to 4 hours.)    Posted to recipelu-digest by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I Canty) on Mar 6, 1998
