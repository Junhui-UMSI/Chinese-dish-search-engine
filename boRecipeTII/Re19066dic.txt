Vegetable Chop Suey (Microwave)
Onions, Pepper, Water, Butter, Soy sauce, Instant beef bouillon, fresh mushrooms, Cornstarch, celery, Sugar, chinese vegetables, 
Mix celery, onion, mushrooms and butter in 2 quart casserole, cover.  Microwave on high 6-8 minutes, or until tender-crisp. Set aside. In 2 cup  measure, combine water, soy sauce, cornstarch, bouillon and sugar.  Microwave on high 2 minutes or until thickened, stirring twice during  cooking.    Mix sauce and remaining ingredients with celery mixture. Microwave at high  4 6 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring after half the time. Serve  over rice or chow mein noodles.
