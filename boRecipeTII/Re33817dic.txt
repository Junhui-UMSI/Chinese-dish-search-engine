Sweet and Sour Pork
Bacon dripping, Brown sugar, Sliced onion, Cornstarch, Soy sauce, Number 2 can pineapple, Vinegar, Sliced green pepper, Lean pork, Water, 
Cut the pork in 1-1/2 inch strips and brown the pieces in bacon drippings.  Drain the pineapple and reserve the juice. Stir the cornstarch into the  water and combine it with the sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, and pineapple  juice. Cook this until it is clear and slightly thickened, about 2 minutes.    Add the sauce to the pork and cook for about 1 hour, covered, over low heat  or until the pork is tender. Chill it overnight. Before serving, heat it  over a low flame until it is hot, then add the pepper, onion, and pineapple  pieces. Cook for 2 minutes and serve with wild rice or a mixture of white  and wild rice.
