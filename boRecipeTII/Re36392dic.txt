Beer Batter for Shrimp and Vegetables
Beer, ACCOMPANIMENT, Zucchini strips, Shrimp; peeled and deveined, Flour, Oil for frying, Seasoned flour for dredging, Broccoli florets, 
In a bowl with the flour, whisk in the beer, a small amount at a time. Add  more beer as necessary. Pour the batter through a strainer and let sit for  an hour. Check for desired consistency and add more beer if needed.    In a deep pot, heat the oil to 360 degrees. Dredge the item to be fried in  seasoned flour and then dip it into the beer batter. Fry until golden.  Remove to a paper towel lined plate. Serve immediately.
