"Rotel Tomatoes"
Peeled, chopped tomatoes;, Salt, Jalepeno or hot chili, 
Place in 4 quart saucepan, and simmer until reduced to 1 quart.  (measure 1  quart water into saucepan in advance, and mark on outside of pan, to help  you judge)    Freeze in 1 cup portions.    This way you can adjust the hot peppers to suit yourself, and making small  batches is so easy!
