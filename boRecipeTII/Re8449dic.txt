Potato Wedges
Salt, PARSLEY FLAKES, BUTTER, MARGARINE, MELTED, Grated Parmesan cheese, CORNFLAKE CRUMBS, Pepper, MEDIUM BAKING POTATOES, 
PREHEAT OVEN TO 400 DEG. F. CUT CLEANED POTAOTES INTO 8 LENGTH-WISE PIECES.  IN A PIE PLATE, COMBINE CORNFLAKE CRUMBS, PARMESAN CHEESE, PARSLEY FLAKES,  SALT AND PEPPER. DIP POTATO WEDGES INTO MELTED BUTTER, THEN IN SEASONING  MIXTURE. PLACE POTATOES ON A BAKING SHEET.
