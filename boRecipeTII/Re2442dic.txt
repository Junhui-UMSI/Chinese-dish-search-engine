Lettuce leaves, Tomatoes, Mayonnaise, Salt, bacon, Black pepper, Bread, 
Fry bacon slowly and drain thoroughly. Spread toasted bread slices with  mayonnaise. Lay tomato slices on every other slice of bread. Sprinkle  tomatoes with salt and pepper. Top with bacon, lettuce leaves and the other  bread slice. Cut on the diagonal and serve with soup, chips or french  fries.    Recipe by Mangia!
