Beef n Beer
salt and pepper, Beer, Prepared mustard, Onions, lg, thinly sliced, Catsup, Boneless chuck, Green peppers, lg, sliced, Mushrooms, cut into chunks, 
Salt & pepper meat and brown lightly in heavy skillet. Add onions, pepper  and mushrooms and brown lightly. Pour beer over all, stir in catsup and  mustard. Cover tightly and simmer slowly for 2 to 2 1/2 hours or until  meat is tender.    Mrs. Harold T. Cook    From a book of recipes compiled by members and friends of the LONDONTOWN  PUBLIK HOUSE ASSEMBLY, EDGEWATER, MD 21037.
