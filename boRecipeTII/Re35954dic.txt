Stuffed Peppers
Chopped parsley, salsa, No-fat refried beans, Ground cumin, Chili powder, red onions, bell peppers, Garlic powder, Whole kernel corn, brown rice, cooked, 
Cut peppers in half from top to stem to bottom. Discard seeds and  membranes.  Blanch peppers in boiling water for 3 mins. Drain. Set aside.  Mix rest of ingredients in sauce pan and simmer until hot and bubbly. Place  peppers in 13 X 9 X 2 baking dish; stuff peppers with bean mixture. Bake  covered at 350 for 35 to 40 mins.
