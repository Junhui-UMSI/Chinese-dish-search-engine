White Cake Icing
Cream of tartar, Water, egg whites, Sugar, 
Put all ingredients in a pan with a candy thermometer (cover with foil  until it is boiling good).    Have 6 egg whites beaten stiff when syrup is at 246 on the thermometer.  Pour syrup slowing into egg whites (while still beating) 1/3 at a time.    Note: if decorating with a pastry bag, add 6 Tbs. powdered sugar.
