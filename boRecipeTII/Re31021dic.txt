Banana Oatmeal Cookies
Vanilla, Flour, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Sugar, Butter, Lemon juice, Salt, Eggs, bananas, Rolled oats, Nuts, 
* Do not use instant oats. ** Use pecans, walnuts, or your choice of nuts,  chopped.       Sift flour, soda, baking powder and salt together. Cream together sugar  and shortening. Beat in well beaten eggs. Stir in the vanilla, lemon juice,  and banana.       Add rolled oats and nuts to flour mixture, alternately with banana  mixture, mixing well after each addition. Stir in nuts.       Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheet and bake in preheated  350-degree oven 15 to 18 minutes, or until golden.  Makes about 6 dozen cookies.
