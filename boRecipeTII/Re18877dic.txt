Anges Apple Bread
Cinnamon, Vegetable oil, Baking soda, Eggs, apples, All purpose flour, Sugar, Vanilla extract, Nuts, Salt, 
Grease 2 loaf pans. In a large bowl beat together the sugar, oil and eggs.  In a separate bowl sift together the flour, slt, baking soda, and cinnamon.  Add the dry ingredients to the sugar mixture. Add the vanilla, apples, and  nuts. Pour the batter into the prepared pans. Bake in a 325F over for 1  hour.
