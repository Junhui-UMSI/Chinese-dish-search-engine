Apple Cookies
Salt, Butter, Apples (any kind); chopped, Egg, Nuts (walnuts or pecans are, Cloves, Cinnamon, Flour, Baking soda, Brown sugar, Raisins, Nutmeg, 
submitted by: sgjg6566@pipeline.com (Julie, Michigan, USA)    Sift together flour, soda, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg and salt. Cream the  butter and brown sugar; add egg and beat til fluffy. Add dry ingredients  and mix alternately to creamed mixture. Add the fruit and nuts. Drop by  teaspoonfuls on a greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for about 8-10  minutes.
