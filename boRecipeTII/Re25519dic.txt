Avocado and Mozarella Cheese Stuffed Tomatoes
Garlic chives, DRESSING, Dijon mustard, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Mozarella, Garlic, White wine, Balsamic vinegar, Red leaf lettuce, Avocados, Tomatoes, vine ripened, Basil, 
Cut tomatoes in half and scoop out insides. (Can be used in place of tomato  concasse to make Tomato-Basil Cream Sauce.) Set each half on top of lettuce  garnished plate. In a blender, add garlic, basil, balsamic vinegar, oil and  white wine. Blend and adjust according to taste. Toss with Avocado and  Mozarella cubes until completely coated. Stuff tomatoes with mixture and  garnish with garlic chives or other long thin garnish stuck into tomatoes.  Looks terrific and tastes unbelievable!
