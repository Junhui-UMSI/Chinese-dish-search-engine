Turkey Hash
Cooked diced potato, Diced cooked turkey (no skin, Shred.low-fat proc.Am.cheese, Paprika, Onions, Reduced-cal margarine, Cooked green peas, Can cream celery soup, 
1.  Saute onion in margarine in nonstick skillet until tender. 2. Add  turkey, soup, potatoes and peas. 3. Place mixture in 1-quart nonstick  casserole. 4. Top with shredded cheese and paprika. 5. Bake at 350 F for 30  minutes.    1/2 cup serving, 1 bread exchange, 1 medium-fat meat    Source:  Cookbooks for Diabetics and Their Families, U of Alabama 1984  Shared by Earl Shelsby on Cooking Oct 93.
