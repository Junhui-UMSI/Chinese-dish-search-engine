Wakavaki Indian Soup
Salt, Zucchini, Cabbage, Green beans, Short ribs, Ears corn; (cut into thirds), Carrot, Garbanzo beans; (15 oz. can), Garlic, 
Cook short ribs in 20 cups of water for 1 hour over medium flame. Add  carrots, corn and garlic and then cook for 1/2 hour. Next add cabbage and  10 cups of HOT water and continue cooking for 15 minutes. Next add garbanzo  beans, green beans, zucchini, 2 tablespoons of salt and then cook 1  additional hour. Serve each bowl with 2 cups of soup and include 1 piece of  corn and 2 short ribs in the bowl. Serve the soup with a tortilla.
