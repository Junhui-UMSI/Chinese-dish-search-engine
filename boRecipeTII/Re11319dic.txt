Ranch Potato Casserole
cheddar cheese, Sour cream, Red potatoes, Parsley, Cheddar cheese, TOPPING, Lightly crushed corn flakes, Prepared ranch salad, Bacon bits, Butter, 
Cook potatoes until tender. Quarter (leave skins on) and set aside. Combine  sour cream, dressing, bacon, parsley, and 1 cup cheese. Place potatoes on  greased 9 x 13 inch baking dish. Pour sour cream mixture over potatoes and  gently toss. Top with 1/2 cup of cheese. Combine corn flakes and butter.  Sprinkle over casserole. Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes. 8 servings.
