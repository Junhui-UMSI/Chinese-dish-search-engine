Waffles #1
All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Eggs, milk, Pecans, 
Mix flour, baking powder, salt, egg yolks & milk. Beat egg whites until  stiff & fold into above mixture. Pour into heated waffle iron. Close & bake  until steaming stops, about 4-6 minutes. Yields 8 servings. Recipe may be  halved. Batter will not keep.
