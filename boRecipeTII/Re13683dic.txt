Wild Rice and Fennel
Rain, Hours in cold water and dra, Ined, 4 cup chicken broth canned, Nise, About 3 cup or celery and a, Long grain rice, Onions, Wiold rice soaked for 2, Fennel/anise seeds, Or from bouillon cubes, Fennel bulbs chopped fine, Or parboil 10 minutes and d, Salt or to taste, Butter/margarine, 
In a medium saucepan, heat the butter until foamy, add the onion and cook 2  minutes until soft. DO not brown. Add the fennel and cook 2 minutes to  soften. Add the drained wil and white rice, fennel seeds, salt and chicken  stock. Stir well. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover pan and simmer 20  minutes before lifing lid. Fluff rice up from the bottom with a fork. If  any water remains in the pan, remove lid and cook over high heat a few  minutes to evaporate. Taste for adequate seasoning.    .
