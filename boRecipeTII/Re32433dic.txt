Basic Adobo
Peppercorns, crushed, Butt, Chicken thighs or 3 lb pork, Vinegar, Salt., Garlic, Soy sauce, Bay leaf, crushed, 
Cut chicken pieces in half if thighs are large. If using pork, cut  pork into 1 1/2 inch pieces. In a saucepan, combine all ingredients.  Let stand for 1 to 3 hours.  Bring to a boil. Cover, lower heat, and  simmer for 30 minutes (45 minutes for pork).  Remove cover and simmer  15 more minutes or until liquid evaporates and chicken or pork is  lightly browned.
