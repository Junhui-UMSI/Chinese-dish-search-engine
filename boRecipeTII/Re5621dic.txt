Pineapple Sour Cream Pie
Egg yolks, (no. 2) crushed pineapple, Egg whites, Sugar, Sugar, Lemon juice, All-purpose flour, Sour cream, Salt, 
Combine first three ingredients; add pineapple, sour cream and lemon juice.  Cook until thick. Add egg yolks and cook some more. Let cool and pour into  a baked pie shell. Beat egg whites stiff aod add sugar. Spread on pie and  bake until meringue is brown.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
