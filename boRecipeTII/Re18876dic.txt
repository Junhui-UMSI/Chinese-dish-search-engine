Andys Skillet Supper
Onion, Chili powder, Canola oil, Oregano leaves; crushed, Cumin, Chicken breasts, Canned kidney beans, Chicken broth, V8 juice, 
Preparation time = 30 minutes Chicken breasts should be skinned and boned.  Any suitable oil can be substituted for canola oil. Chicken broth may be  purchased as instant and mixed with water. V8 juice is a brandname  vegetable juice. 1. Cut the chicken into 1/2-inch pieces. 2. In hot oil,  cook chicken, onion, chili powder, cumin and oregano until the chicken  turns white. 3. Stir in broth and juice, heat until boiling, then reduce  heat to low. Simmer 10 minutes. 4. Dump in beans, liquid and all, stir,  cover and simmer for another 10 minutes. Stir occasionally. Serve over  rice.
