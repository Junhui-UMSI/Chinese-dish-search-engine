Beer Batter Fried Sole
Baking Powder, Flour, White pepper, Eggs, Fillet of sole, Beer, Onion powder, 
The batter for this fried fish dish is light and crisp with a delicate beer  flavor. Other fish fillets may be substituted for sole.    Pat fish dry with paper towels. Cut each piece in half lengthwise. Combine  dry ingredients. Mix beer with eggs and 2 TBS oil and add to dry  ingredients. Stir just until moistened. Heat 1/4 inch oil in a skillet. Dip  each piece of fish in batter, coating well. Fry until golden brown on both  sides. Serve with tartar sauce. Make 6-8 servings. From the files of Al  Rice, North Pole Alaska. Feb 1994
