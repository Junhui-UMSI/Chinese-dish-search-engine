Avocado Salsa Cruda
Chopped Tomato; Peeled, Salt, Red Onion; Diced, Bell Pepper; Finely Diced, Avocado; Finely Diced, Jalapeno; Finely Diced, Fresh parsley, Sugar, 
Chop each vegetable by hand or with food processor. Mix together in a  nonaluminum bowl. Add the sugar and salt to taste. Adjust the sesonings if  necessary. Let the salsa sit for at least 1 hr. Stir well before serving.    Yield: Approx. 2 cups    Posted to bbq-digest by PhantomBBQ@aol.com on Aug 16, 1998, converted by  MM_Buster v2.0l.
