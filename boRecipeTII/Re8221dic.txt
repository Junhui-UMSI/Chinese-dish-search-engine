Potato Kugel
Grated, drained potatoes, Pepper, Potato flour, Melted chicken fat, Grated onion, Eggs, Grebenes, Onion salt, Baking Powder, 
Beat eggs. Add remaining ingredients. Put into 1 1/2 qt. greased baking  dish and bake at 325F for 1 hour. TO MAKE GREBENES: Take 1 pound of chicken  fat and fry in a fry pan until very crispy. Reserve 4 T melted chicken fat.  Take the remaining crispy skin and dice. The grebenes is actually crispy  chicken fat.
