Praline Popcorn Crunch
Popped popcorn, Margarine, Firm-pack brown sugar, Baking soda, Honey, Pecan halves, Vanilla, 
Place popcorn in greased 15x10 baking pan.  In a 1 1/2 quart saucepan,  combine brown sugar, margarine and honey. Cook over medium heat until  boiling, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon. Continue cooking at a  gentle boil for 5 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Stir in baking soda and  vanilla.  Slowly pour mixture over popped corn. Add pecans and stir gently  until pecans and popcorn are evenly coated.    Bake at 300 F for 15 minutes.  Stir and bake 5 minutes more. Turn out on  waxed paper.  Cool, break into chunks and store in tightly covered  container.  Makes about 12 cups.    From: Texas Dept of Agriculture Posted by: Sheila Exner - October 1991
