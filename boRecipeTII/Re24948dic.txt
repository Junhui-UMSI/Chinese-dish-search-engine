Autumn Corn Bake
Finely chopped green pepper, Parkay margarine; melted, (10-oz) frozen corn; thawed,, Velveeta pasteurized process, Cornmeal, Pepper, Egg, 
Combine ingredients; mix well.  Spoon into 1-1/2-quart casserole; cover.  Bake at 350 degrees, 45 minutes. 6 servings.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
