Lamb-And-Veggie Burgers with Feta Spread
Uncooked bulgur, Boiling water, Diced baking potato, Water, Lean ground lamb, Finely diced zucchini, Onions, Chopped fresh parsley, Garlic, Salt, Black pepper, Cooking spray, Hamburger buns, FETA SPREAD, Nonfat sour cream, Diced seeded peeled cucumber, Dried dill, Garlic, Crumbled feta cheese, GARNISH, Tomato slices; 1/4-inch, 
FETA SPREAD: Combine and set aside.    BURGERS: Combine bulgur and boiling water in a large bowl. Cover and let  stand 30 minutes.    Meanwhile, combine potato and 3 cups water in a saucepan; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, simmer until tender, 5 to 10 minutes, and drain. Cool.    Stir together bulgur, potato, lamb, zucchini, onion, parsley, garlic, salt,  and pepper in a large bowl. Divide mixture into 5 equal portions and shape  into 1/2-inch-thick patties.    Place a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray over medium-high  heat until hot. Cook patties 4 minutes on each side. Place patties on  bottom halves of buns; cover each with 2 tablespoons Feta Spread, 1 tomato  slice, and top of bun. Serves 5.    [PER SERVING: Calories 406 (25% from fat), Fat 11G (4 g saturated), Protein  21 g, Carbohydrate 55g, Fiber 4g, Cholesterol 61 mg, Iron 3 mg, Sodium 572  mg, calcium 113mg]    Notes: Serves 4. Time: 20 min Prep; 20 mins cook. *Recipe from "HEALTHY  COOKING: The lighter side of meat," by Sheridan Warrick in Health, October  1998.
