Apricot-Pecan Cookies - Wday
Ground cardamom, Basic Cookie Dough I;, Dried apricots; finely, Pecans; finely chopped, 
1.  Mix cardamom and apricots into dough.  Roll dough into 8" log and slice  into 16 pieces.  Roll each into ball and coat with chopped pecans.  put  balls 1" apart on ungreased cookie sheet and bake 12-14 mins until bottoms  are browned.
