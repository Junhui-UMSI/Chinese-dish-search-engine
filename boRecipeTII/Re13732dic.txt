Wild Rice Quiche
Bulk pork sausage, cooked, Cheddar cheese, Wild rice, cooked and cooled, milk, Buttermilk biscuit mix, Eggs, 
In a greased 10-in. pie plate, layer sausage, rice and cheese. In a bowl,  combine milk, baking mix and eggs; beat until smooth. Pour evenly over  cheese. Bake at 400 for 30-35 minutes or until a knife inserted near the  center comes out clean. Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.
