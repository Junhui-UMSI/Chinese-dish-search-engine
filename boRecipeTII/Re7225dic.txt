Pork and Turkey Sausages or Burgers
Half level tsp black pepper, Or 1 lb turkey meat., Level tsp paprika, To 3 garlic cloves, Or 1 lb lean pork., Ground cloves., 
Place all ingredients in a food processor or liquidiser and process until  smooth. Shape into sausages or burgers. Dry fry slowly, grill or bake in an  oven at 200 c, 400 f, or gas mark 6.
