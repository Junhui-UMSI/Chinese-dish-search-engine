Bacon and Onion Muffins
milk, Onions, Sesame seeds, Sour Cream, Bacon; diced, Unbleached flour; sifted, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Eggs; slightly beaten, Salt, 
1. Fry bacon until crisp in skillet. Remove with slotted spoon and drain on  paper towels.    2. Saute onion in 1 tbsp. bacon drippings until tender (do not brown). Set  aside to cool.    3. Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in large mixing  bowl.    4. Combine eggs, milk and sour cream in small bowl; blend well. Add all at  once to dry ingredients, stirring just enough to moisten. Stir in bacon and  sauteed onion.    5. Spoon batter into greased 2 1/2-inch muffin-pan cups, fill 2/3rds full.  Sprinkle with sesame seeds.    6. Bake in 375 degree F. Oven 18 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.
