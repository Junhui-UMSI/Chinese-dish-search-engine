Vegetable Platter - Dia Rau Song
Fresh Asian or regular basil, Ripe mango, Star fruit, Ripe bananas, Individual leaves, Head of Boston or other soft, Ripe papaya, Of scallions, cut into 2, Coriander leaves, Mint leaves, Fresh bean sprouts, Apples, Cucumber, peeled in, Sliced thinly crosswise, 
On a large platter, decoratively arrange all of the ingredients in separate  groups. Use in recipes where required.    From "The Foods of Vietnam" by Nicole Rauthier. Stewart, Tabori & Chang.  1989.
