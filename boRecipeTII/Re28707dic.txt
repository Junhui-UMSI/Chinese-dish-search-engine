Texas Steak "Pot Roast"
Salt and pepper, Lime juice, Onions, sliced, Garlic, Beef stock, Ground cumin, Hot pepper flakes, olive oil, Outside round steak, trimmed, Bottled salsa, 
In Dutch oven or deep ovenproof skillet, heat oil over medium heat; brown  steak all over.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Transfer to plate and set  aside.    Stir stock into pan; bring to boil, stirring to scrape up brown bits. Stir  in salsa, lime juice, onions, garlic, cumin and hot pepper flakes; bring to  boil.    Return meat to pan.  Cover tightly; bake in 375F oven, turning meat every  30 minutes, for 2 to 2 1/2 hours or until very tender. Makes 4 servings.
