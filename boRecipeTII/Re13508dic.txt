Whole-Wheat Pizza
Water, Part Skim Mozzarella Cheese,, Cheddar Cheese, Mushroom, Tomato Sauce, Caraway seeds, Whole wheat flour, Dried Oregano, Vegetable oil, Garlic powder, Broccoli, Salt, Black Pepper, Whole Wheat Flour, Honey, Active Dry Yeast, 
Combine the water, yeast, honey and the 1 cup of whole wheat flour in  a large bowl. Beat 100 times until mixture is smooth. Let rise in a  warm place for 15 minutes. Add the salt, oil, Cheddar cheese, pepper,  caraway seeds, garlic powder and 1-1/4 to 1-1/2 ups whole wheat  flour. Mix well and let the dough rest for 5 minutes.    Pat out the dough onto a baking sheet for 1 large pizza or divide the  dough in half and make two medium-size pizzas.    Top with the pizza sauce, oregano, vegetables and mozzarella. Bake in  a 400 degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
