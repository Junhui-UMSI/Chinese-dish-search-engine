Basic New Mexican Red Chile Sauce
Flour; (cook a minute to, Oregano; optional, Dried RED New Mexican chiles, Salt to taste, Water &, Clove garlic; cook in the, Oil; (we used olive oil., 
STEW FOR A WHILE AND THEN BLEND SMOOTH. PLACE IN ICE CUBE TRAYS, FREEZE &  BAG UP.  Posted to CHILE-HEADS DIGEST by Pam <mglory@ix.netcom.com> on May 31, 1998
