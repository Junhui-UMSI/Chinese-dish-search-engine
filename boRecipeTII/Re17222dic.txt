Voodoo Jerk Chicken
Molasses or maple syrup, Slather  Sauce, *Uncle Billys VooDoo Jerk, Split broiler chickens, 
Slather Chicken, marinate at least one hour. Bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours or  cook on BBQ    *The sauce contains: Peppers, onion, garlic oil, honey, molasses, cider  vinegar & spices.
