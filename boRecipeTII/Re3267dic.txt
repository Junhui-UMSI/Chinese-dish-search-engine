Pear Relish #2
Ground pears, Ground green or red peppers, Hot pepper sauce, Ground dill pickles, Turmeric, White wine vinegar, Sugar, Dry mustard, Flour, Salt, Ground hot pepper -or-, Ground onion, 
Mix all ingredients & stir well. Bring to a rolling boil over medium high  heat. Cook 15 minutes. Pack in jars & seal. Process in boiling water bath  for 5 minutes. Makes 7 pints.
