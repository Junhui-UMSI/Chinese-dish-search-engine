Am Delight Muffins
Sliced almonds, Peeled apples; chopped, Baking soda, Eggs, Carrots; grated, Vanilla extract, All-purpose flour, Vegetable oil, milk, Salt, Sugar, Ground cinnamon, Raisins, Flaked coconut, 
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. In  another bowl, beat eggs; add oil, milk and vanilla. ix well; stir into dry  ingredients just until moistened. Fold in the remaining ingredients. Fill  greased of paper lined muffing cups three-fourths full. Bake at 375 degrees  for 20-25 minutes or until muffins test done.
