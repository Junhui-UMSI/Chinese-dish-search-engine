Peanut Butter Smores
chocolate covered, Graham cracker, Marshmallows, Peanut butter cups, 
place1 peanut butter cup on each of 4 crackers. spear marshmallows on long  fork or clean stick, toast over campfire coals or over grill on low heat.  place toasted marshmallow on top of each peanut butter cup. top each with  cracker. press together and hold for a few seconds to melt chocolate
