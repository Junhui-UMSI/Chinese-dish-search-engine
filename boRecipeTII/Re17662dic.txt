Almond Peach Chicken Salad
Diced chicken, Green grapes; halved, Toasted almonds, White pepper, Salt, Sour cream, Mayo or salald dressing, 
Combine sour cream and mayo, and add to diced chicken till thourougly  mixed.    Add grapes, being careful not to smash them.    Then add almonds, salt and pepper, and refrigerate.
