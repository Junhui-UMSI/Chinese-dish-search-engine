Barbecued Beefburgers
bread crumbs, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, Vinegar, Salt, Onions, Sugar, Ground beef, Fat, Tomato sauce, milk, 
Mix beef, milk, crumbs, salt and pepper.  Form patties and brown in 1T fat.  While they brown, saute onion in remaining fat.  Add the remaining  ingredients to the onions. Pour sauce over burgers, simmer gently for 15  minutes.
