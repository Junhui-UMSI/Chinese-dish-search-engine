Triple-Chocolate Fondue - Jeanne Elledge
BAR SEMI-SWEET CHOCOLATE, Heavy cream, TRIPLE SEC, SEMI-SWEET CHOCOLATE CHIPS, OR CHUNKS, 
MELT IN DOUBLE BOILER, MICROWAVE, OR OVER VERY LOW HEAT STIRRING FREQUENTLY  TO PREVENT SCORCHING. SHOULD BE VERY CREAMY BUT NOT SOUPY.    MAY DIP ALL TYPES OF FRUIT, POUND CAKE, OR WHATEVER YOU DESIRE.    RECOMMEND APPLE, BANANA, STRAWBERRIES, PINEAPPLE, KIWI, ORANGES, ETC. POUND  CAKE, LADY FINGERS, ARE GREAT.
