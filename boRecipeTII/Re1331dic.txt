Drunken Wild Rice Mix with Recipe
Dried currants, Wild rice, Chicken bouillon cubes,, Pecans, Water, Butter, Rice mix, Brandy, 
Mix: Blend and store in an airtight container.    Drunken Wild Rice: Bring water and butter to a boil. Add rice, reduce heat  to simmering, cover and cook 35 minutes. Add brandy and continue to cook  until liquid is absorbed. 6 servings.
