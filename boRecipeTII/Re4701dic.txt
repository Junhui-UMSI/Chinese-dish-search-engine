Philly 3-Step Cheesecake
Philadelphia, Vanilla, Sugar, Keebler ready-crust graham cracker pie crust, Eggs, Cream cheese, 
1. Mix cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla at medium speed with elec-  tric mixer until well blended. Add eggs, mix until well blended.    2. Pour into crust    3. Bake at 350F 40 minutes or until center is almost set. Cool.  Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight. Top with fresh fruit; drizzle with        2    Tb. melted strawberry jelly.
