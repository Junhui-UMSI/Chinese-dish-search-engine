Asparagus Casserole #4
Pkg green peas, Mushroom soup, -(up to), Cheddar Cheese, Water chestnuts, Bread crumbs, Asparagus spears, (6.5-oz) sliced mushrooms, Salt and pepper to taste, 
In a well greased casserole place a layer of well drained peas (if frozen  peas are used, cook ahead), a layer of asparagus, a layer of mushrooms, a  layer of water chestnuts (sliced) and a layer of mushroom soup. Repeat.  Place a layer of grated cheese and the bread crumbs on top. Bake until  bubbly in 350 degree oven (about 20 to 30 minutes).    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
