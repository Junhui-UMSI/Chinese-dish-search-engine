Sweet Pea Guacamole
Fresh lime or lemon juice, Crushed red pepper flakes, Garlic, Rice vinegar, Ground cumin, Cilantro, washed & trimmed, Frozen peas, thawed, Soy sauce, 
Combine all ingredients in food processor or blender and process until  fairly smooth.  Variation: For a chunky version, stir in one chopped tomato  and four chopped scallions.
