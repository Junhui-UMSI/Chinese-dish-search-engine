Picadillo (Rice and Beef Hash/filling)
Raisins, Pitted black olives, sliced, Diced onions, Each salt and pepper, Cooked ground beef lean, Apple, pared and chopped, Sliced canned jalapeno, Large stuffed olives, sliced, Garlic, Tomato chopped and seeded, olive oil, Dash each cinnamon & cloves, 
1. In a 10" skillet heat oil over medium heat; add ground beef and  cook, breaking up large pieces with a wooden spoon, until crumbly.  Add onion and garlic and saute until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir  in the remaining ingredients. Reduce heat to low and cook, stirring  occasionally until flavors are well blended, about 20 minutes.    2. Serve over rice or as a filling for burritos or tacos.
