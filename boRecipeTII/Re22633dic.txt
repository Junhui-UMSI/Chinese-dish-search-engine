Apricot Chocolate Bread
Pecans, Flour, Sugar, Cocoa, Cut up dried apricots, Eggs, Veg. oil, Baking soda, Baking Powder, milk, 
Mix first 5 ingredients in a large bowl. Stir in the rest until just  moistened. Pour into greased loaf pan and bake for 50-60 mins in a  pre-heated 350 degree oven. Cool for 10 mins. and remove from pan.
