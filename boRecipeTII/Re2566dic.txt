Peachy Bread
Raisins, Walnuts, Sliced peaches, Dates, Prepared mincemeat, Apricot nectar, Brown sugar, Mixed candied fruit/peels, Sweetened condensed milk, Flour, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Ground cloves, 
In large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, cloves, and brown  sugar.  Stir in candied fruit, raisins, dates, and walnuts.   Add sweetened condensed milk, mincemeat, peaches, and nectar.   Combine well.  Pour into 2 greased and floured 9 x 5 inch loaf
