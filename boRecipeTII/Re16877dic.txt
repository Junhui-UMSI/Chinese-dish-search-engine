All-Purpose Salad Dressing
Dried parsley, Dijon mustard, Vinegar, Dried Oregano, olive oil, Wine, Garlic, 
In a small bowl whisk together wine, vinegar, and mustard until blended.  Slowly pour in olive oil, whisking constantly. Whisk in oregano, parsley,  and garlic. Use for a dressing on mixed greens.
