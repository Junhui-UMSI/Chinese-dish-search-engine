Vanilla Ice Cream
Whole Milk, Evaporated milk, Sugar replacement, Eggs, Vanilla, 
Combine evaporated milk and sugar replacement. Beat well until sugar  is dissolved. Add whole milk and vanilla extract; beat well. Add  eggs; beat eggs into milk mixture vigorously. pour into ice cream  maker.  Freeze according to manufacturers directions.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/diabetic.zip
