Kamillas Spice Bread-In-A-Jar
Baking soda, Baking Powder, Salt, Cinnamon, Cloves, Walnuts, Raisins, Shortening, Sugar, Applesauce, Water, Eggs, 
Cream shortening and sugar in mixer bowl until light and fluffy.  Add  applesauce, water and eggs; mix well.  Add mixture of flour, soda, baking  powder, salt and spices; mix well.  Stir in walnuts and raisins. Spray 7  wide-mouth 1 pint jars with nonstick cooking spray. Fill jars 2/3 full.  Bake at 325 degrees for exactly 45 minutes. Remove jars from oven one at a  time and seal quickly with 2 piece lids, pressing bread down if necessary.  Let stand until cool.  Give as gifts. Use only jars suitable for canning.    Approx Per Servings:  Cal 120; Prot 1.6g; Carbo 19.7g; T Fat 4.3g; Chol     19.6    mg.
