Banana Cream Cheese Frosting
Banana, nutmeg, Butter, Vanilla, Cream cheese, Sour cream, 
Beat the butter, cream cheese, and sour cream together in a large bowl  until light. Add the banana, vanilla, nutmeg, and sugar, mixing well until  fluffy. Spread over cooled cake if using as a frosting. Keep refrigerated  until serving time.
