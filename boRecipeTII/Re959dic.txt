Indian Soup
Kosher cut "round steak", Potatoes; peeled and chunked, Yellow onions; peeled and, salt and pepper, Water; (up to 3), 
I got this recipe from my husbands grandma. It is easy and sooo good.    Boil meat and onions together til meat is tender, then add potatoes cook  til potatoes are done. Serve with fry bread.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Joyce Rosenfield <dogsmom@cinci.infi.net>  on Oct 25, 1998,
