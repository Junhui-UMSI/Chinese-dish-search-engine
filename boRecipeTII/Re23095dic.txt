Truly Tabouli
Lemon juice, Tomatoes, Salt, Wish-Bone Italian Dressing, Bulgur, Parsley, Green onions, Cucumber, Green bell pepper, 
In large bowl, combine all ingredients. Cover and marinate in refrigerator,  stirring occasionally, at least 8 hours.
