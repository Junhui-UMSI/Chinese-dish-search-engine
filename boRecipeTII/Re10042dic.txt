Quaker Omelet
Cornstarch, Butter, Salt and pepper, Eggs, milk, 
Put heavy frying pan with close fitting cover on to heat. Separate eggs and  beat yolks until thick, then add cornstarch. Beat whites stiff, add to yolk  mixture and finally add milk and salt and pepper to taste. Melt butter in  hot pan, pour in mixture. Cover closely and cook over medium heat for 6 or  7 minutes. Remove cover and with spatula fold omelet in half. Do not lift  cover until cooked.  Source: Heritage Recipes ch.
