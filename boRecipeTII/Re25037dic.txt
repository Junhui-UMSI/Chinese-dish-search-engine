Tomato Sauce with Ground Beef
Garlic, olive oil, Italian Tomatoes, Salt to taste, Ground beef, Dried Oregano, Tomato sauce, Italian parsley, Whole Bay Leaf, Dried basil, Tomato Paste, Onions, Black pepper, 
Heat oil in a large heavy saucepan; add onion; saute over low heat,  stirring, until tender and golden, about 5 min. Add the garlic; saute 1  min. Meanwhile, brown the ground beef in a skillet, stirring to crumble  meat; drain off fat; add to the onion mixture. Add the tomatoes, tomato  sauce, 1 tomato sauce can filled with water, the tomato paste, parsley, 1  tsp salt, the basil, oregano, pepper, and bay leaf. Heat over medium-low  heat to simmering. Simmer, uncovered, over low heat, stirring occasionally  until sauce is thickened and flavorful, about 1 hour and 15 minutes.
