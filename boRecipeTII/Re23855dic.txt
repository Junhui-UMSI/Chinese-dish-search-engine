Asian Rice Pilaf
Fat skimmed from top, Rice, Frozen peas, Water, Powdered ginger, chicken broth, Sesame oil, Soy sauce, Uncooked white long-grain rice, 
1. In a large pan, combine the broth and water; add the rice and bring to a  boil.  Continue boiling until the level of the liquid just covers the rice.  Turn the heat to low, cover and cook 15 minutes. 2. While the rice is  cooking, combine the sesame oil, soy sauce and ginger. Stir into the cooked  rice with the peas. 3. Serve half of the recipe hot for a side-dish. Store  the remaining in the refrigerator to use as a base for rice salad.
