Arm and Hammer Chocolate Chip Banana Bread
Baking Powder, Salt, Eggs, Walnuts, Flour, unsifted, Arm & Hammer Baking Soda, , Mashed ripe bananas, Mini semi-sweet chocolate Chips, Sugar, Butter or margarine, 
Directions: Lightly greast the bottom only of a 9-1/4 by 5-1/4 by 2-3/4  inch loaf pan. Set aside.  In large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients  except small chocolate chips and walnuts; blend well on medium speed. Stir  in small chocolate chips and walnuts. Bake at 350 for 60 to 65 minutes or  until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes;  remove from pan. Cool completely.
