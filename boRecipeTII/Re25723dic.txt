Baba Gannouj
Eggplants, Black pepper, Finely chopped parsley,, Onions, Virgin olive oil, green peppers & green, Salt to taste, Juice of 1 1/2 lemons, Allspice, Cinnamon, Pita bread, 
Wash eggplant & pierce at intervals with a fork.  Set on a baking dish &  broil close to heat for 20 minutes. Peel & sprinkle quickly with half the  lemon juice. Cool & chop finely. Using a small bowl, combine the remaining  ingredients.  Stir into eggplant & refrigerate. Can be frozen at this  point.  Serve trimmed with parsley, green peppers & green onion with pita.    "The Hamilton Spectator", July 1993
