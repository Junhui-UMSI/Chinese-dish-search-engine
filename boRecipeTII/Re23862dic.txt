Asian Sesame Noodles
Sesame oil, Garlic, Cayenne pepper, Soy sauce, Asian egg noodles, sesame seeds, Vegetable or peanut oil, 
1. Cook noodles as per directions on package. Rinse well and drain. Place  noodles in a large bowl and set aside.    2. Combine vegetable or peanut oil, soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic and red  pepper. Mix well and pour over noodles. Mix well with hands and let sit.    Toast sesame seeds. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds on top of noodles and  serve.
