Taco Skillet Supper
Chicken bullion, Quick cooking oats, Envelope taco seasoning mix, Egg, Ground turkey, Hot water, Skim milk, Thinly sliced potatoes, Small onion, chopped, Salsa, divided, 
Coat a 2 qt baking dish with non stick spray. Add potatoes, and 1 T onion.  Dissolve bullion in water, pour 1/2 c over potatoes. Combine turkey, taco  seasoning mix, milk, 1/4 c salsa, oats, egg, and remaining onion, mix well.  Spread over potatoes. Combine remaining salsa and chicken broth, pour over  turkey mix. Bake uncovered at 350 for 1 hour, or until potatoes are tender.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #338    From: l.and-e.smith@juno.com (Lois Smith)    Date: Tue, 10 Dec 1996 14:10:12 PST
