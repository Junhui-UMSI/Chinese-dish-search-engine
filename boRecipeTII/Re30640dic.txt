Tamale Pie
Pepper, Unbleached flour, Vegetable oil, Dash Salt, Cornmeal, Ground beef, Baking Powder, TOPPING, Clove Garlic, Chopped, Salt, (1/2 7oz can) Green Chilies, Sugar (Optional), Oregano, milk, Tomatoes, Other Half Green Chilies, Grated Mild Cheese, Med. Onion, Chopped, 
Brown meat in skillet.  Add remainging ingredients and simmer 20 minutes.  Pour into a 4-qt casserole.  Top with cheese. Mix rest of ingredients  together and pour over the top of meat and cheese.  Bake at 375 degrees F.  for 30 minutes (or until topping is done).
