Zucchini Quesadillas
Green onions, Jalapeno chile, Zucchini, Black olives, Flour tortillas, Corn niblets, 
Preheat oven to 400F.  Place one tortilla on an ungreased oven baking sheet  & layer with half the remaining ingredients.  Top with a second tortilla &  the rest of the filling.  Finish with the final tortilla. Bake about 10  minutes, till the tortilla are browned around the edges. Cut into quarters  & serve hot.
