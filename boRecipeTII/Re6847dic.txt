Polynesian Glaze
Dry mustard, Pineapple; crushed, Ground ginger, Vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Jar apricot preserves, Catsup, 
Combine preserves, pineapple, catsup, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce,  ginger, and mustard in small saucepan. Heat to boiling, stirrying  constantly. Lower heat. Simmer 1 minute.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /FRUIT    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
