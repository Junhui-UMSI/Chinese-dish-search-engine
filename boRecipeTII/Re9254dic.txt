Pudding Chip Cookies
Eggs, Flour, Baking soda, Box (4 serving size) Jell-O, Vanilla, (2 sticks) butter; softened, Walnuts, Granulated sugar, Brown Sugar, (12 ounces) semi-sweet REAL, OPTIONAL, 
Heat oven to 375. Beat butter, eggs & vanilla in large bowl on medium speed  until light & fluffy. Mix in flour and baking soda. Stir in chips and  walnuts. Drop by teaspoonfuls, 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets.    Bake 10 minutes, or until golden. Cool on wire racks.    Makes about 6 dozen cookies.
