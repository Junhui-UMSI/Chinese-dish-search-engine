Stuffed Celery 2
Salt to taste, Couple squirts tabasco, Finely minced onion, Couple good dashes, Garlic, Jar Kraft Roka blue or, Cream cheese, Lots of ground pepper, 
Blend together till smooth and luscious and spreadable. Slather it on  crispy, crispy celery and serve with your favorite olives and assorted  crudites.    Posted to FOODWINE Digest 24 October 96    Date:    Thu, 24 Oct 1996 11:29:00 MDT    From:    Suzy Waterman <suzy.waterman@LMCO.COM>
