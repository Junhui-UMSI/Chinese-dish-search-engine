Venison with Spinach Fettuccine
Fettuccine, Venison with Spinach, 
Brown cubed venison (Approx. 1/2 pound) in 1/3 cup butter Then; Add 1/3 cup  chopped green onions 2 cloves chopped garlic 1tbs worcester sauce 1/2 tbs  soy sauce Approx. 1 to 2 tbs fresh parsley salt and pepper to taste (hot  pepper optional) simmer for 5 minutes. Then; add 1/2 cup can black beans,  drained approximately 8 chopped black olives 2 medium tomatoes, chopped And  simmer for an additional 10 minutes Meanwhile;    Prepare one ounce spinach fettucine according to package instructions Serve  venison sauce over the pasta and top with fresh parmesan cheese to taste.
