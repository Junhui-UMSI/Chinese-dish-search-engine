Pickled Pepper Salsa
Finely chopped, Tomatoes,seeded,peeled>>>, Some pickling juice, Pickled jalapenos including>, Green onion,finely chopped, Onion, finely chopped, Cilantro, finely chopped, 
Chop each ingredient by hand, or separately in food processor. Combine all  the ingredients, adding salt to taste. Allow to stand for abt. 1 hr to  allow flavors to blend. Stir well before serving. Yield: Approx. 5 cups
