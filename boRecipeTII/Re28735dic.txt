Texas Whoppers
Green Pepper, Hot Sauce, Frankfurters, Cheddar Cheese, Onion, Sweet Pickle, Kidney Beans, Rolls, Sour cream, 
Chop FRANKS. Add kidney beans, onion, green pepper and pickle. Mix with  sour cream and hot sauce. Chill. Spoon mixture on bottom half of roll;  sprinkle with cheese; replace top roll. Serve with dill pickles.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
