Bean Dip
Tortilla chips; --serve, Tabasco sauce, Bean dip; (1 can), Cream cheese, Grated cheddar cheese; --or, Taco seasoning mix; (1, Sour cream, Green Onions, 
Soften cream cheese. Mix all ingredients until smooth. Ccover with cheddar  cheese. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 - 30 minutes. Serve with tortilla  chips. Yields 3 cups.    Recipe by: Lisa Dugoni    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #972 by Shavonne Bauer <ssbauer@erinet.com>  on Dec 26, 1997
