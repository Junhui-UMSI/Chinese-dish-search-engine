Green Bean and Turkey Casserole
Servings mashed potatoes, Cubed cooked turkey, Can green beans, drained, Can cream of mushroom soup, milk, Shredded cheddar (1 cup), Canned french fried onions, 
1. Heat oven to 375 degrees. In medium saucepan, combine turkey, green  beans, soup, and milk; mix well. Cook over medium heat until mixture is  hot, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat. Add cheese; stir until  melted. Pour into ungreased 2-quart casserole. Top with prepared mashed  potatoes.    2. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes. Sprinkle with onions; bake an  additional 3 to 5 minutes or until mixture is bubbly and onions are warm.    Nutrition Information Per Serving: 430 Calories, 24 g Fat, 1150 Sodium    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Aug  13, 1998
