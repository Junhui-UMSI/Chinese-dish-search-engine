Sweet Tamales
Dried corn husks, Masa harina mix, Warm water, Lard, Pecans, Baking Powder, Salt, Cinnamon, Brown Sugar, Nutmeg, Raisins, 
Soak husks according to directions in Basic Tamales (see recipe).  For  tamale dough: Beat lard and salt until fluffy.  Slowly beat in masa mix  alternately with water until very light and fluffy.  Add brown sugar,  baking powder, cinnamon and nutmeg, mixing until well blended.    To assemble tamales: Drain corn husks; pat dry.  Spread 2 tablespoons dough  on center of husks, forming a 3 x 2-inch rectangle and spreading completely  to right edge. Spoon 1 teaspoon each pecans and raisins lengthwise down  center of rectangle.    To enclose and steam tamales, proceed according to directions in Basic  Tamales.  Makes 32.
