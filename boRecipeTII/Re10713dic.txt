Quick Stir-Fry
(soaked in..., Green onions, EDEN Shiitake Mushrooms, EDEN Kuzu; dissolved in..., Pea pods, chopped, Red pepper; chopped, EDEN Organic Shoyu, Head broccoli; chopped, EDEN Hot Pepper Sesame Oil, Any EDEN Udon Soba, EDEN Mirin, EDEN Brown Rice Vinegar, -water for 20 minutes), -water, 
Cook pasta, set aside. Reserve shiitake soaking liquid, discard stems,  slice tops. Cook shiitakes in reserved liquid for 15 minutes. Heat oil,  saute broccoli and pea pods for 2 minutes. Add red pepper, saute 1  minute more. Add shiitakes, reserved liquid, mirin, brown rice vinegar  and shoyu, bring to boil. Add dissolved kuzu until mixture thickens and  becomes clear. Serve over pasta, garnish with green onions.
