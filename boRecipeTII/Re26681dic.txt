Baileys Irish Cream Truffles
Butter, Egg yolks, Semisweet chocolate, Heavy cream, 
Melt chocolate, Baileys and heavy cream tog over very low heat.  Whisk in  yolks, one at a time, mixture will thicken.  Whisk in butter. Refrigerate  overnight, or until firm.  With spoon make small balls.
