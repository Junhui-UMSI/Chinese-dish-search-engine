Zucchini Ragout
Onions, thickly sliced, Peeled and seeded, cut into, Fresh basil or 1 tablespoon, Garlic, Peppers, sliced in strips, Zucchini,sliced in 1/2-inch, Butter, Tomato paste, Of celery, sliced, salt and pepper, Parsley, olive oil, 
Lightly brown zucchini in olive oil.  Remove from pan.  Add to pan butter  and onions.  Saute onion until soft.  Add green pepper and celery and cook  mixture, stirring, for five minutes.  Add tomatoes, basil, parsley, garlic,  tomato paste, salt and pepper.  Simmer mixture, covered, over moderately  low heat for twenty minutes.  Add zucchini. Serve in heated shallow  vegetable dish.  Sprinkle with fresh parsley.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Sarah Gruenwald <sitm@EKX.INFI.NET> on Jul 5,  1997
