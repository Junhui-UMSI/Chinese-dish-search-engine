Watergate Wheels
Grated white american cheese, Mozzerella cheese grated, English muffins, Jar favourite pasta sauce, Deli pepperoni slices, 
Smooth sauce on muffins, add cheese and pepperoni. Place on greased cookie  sheet. Bake in preheated oven, about 350 degrees until cheese or muffins  start turning brown.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "bunny" <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Mar 1,  1998
