Provencal Chicken Saute
Pepper, Salt, Italian Plum Tomatoes, Dried thyme, All-purpose flour, Chicken Breasts, Unsalted butter, Fresh Parsley, PROVENCAL SAUCE, Red Bell Pepper, Black Olives, salt and pepper, Dry white wine, Capers, Garlic, 
* Chicken breasts should be skinned, boned and split.  ~-------------------------------------------------------------------------  Mix flour, salt and pepper on a plate.  Lightly coat chicken with seasoned  flour.  Heat butter in a medium skillet over medium heat.  Add chicken and  saute?, turning once, until cooked through, 8-10 minutes.  Remove to  serving plate and keep warm. Sauce: Pour wine into skillet and increase  heat to medium high. De glaze skillet by heating the wine to boiling and  scrape loose browned bits on bottom of pan. Add tomatoes, bell pepper,  garlic, parsley and thyme; cook, stirring frequently, until thickened, 4-6  minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Spoon sauce over chicken and sprinkle  with olives and capers. Serve hot. From: Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sydsbook.zip
