Baked Cheese Grits #1
Margarine or butter, Salt, Quick-cooking grits, Flour, Water, Eggs, cheese, 
Heat oven to 350 F.  Grease 1 1/2 quart baking dish.     Bring water and salt to boil in medium saucepan.  Slowly add grits,  stirring constantly.  Reduce heat to low; cook 5 minutes, stirring  occasionally.  Remove from heat.  Mix in flour and margarine.  Gradually  add cheese, stirring until melted.     Beat eggs in small bowl.  Stir small amount of hot grits into eggs.  Stirring constantly, slowly pour egg mixture into hot grits.  Pour into  prepared dish.     Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until firm.  Makes 6 servings.
