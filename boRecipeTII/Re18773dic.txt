Ancho Chili Paste
Chipotle chili; chopped, Rich chicken stock, Serrano chili; chopped, Bunch fresh cilantro, Ancho chilies; stem and, Clove garlic; peeled and, Jalapeno; chopped, Onion; peeled and chopped, 
Place all ingredients in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a  boil, then simmer for 10 minutes. Pour into a blender and puree until  smooth. Set aside until ready to use.
