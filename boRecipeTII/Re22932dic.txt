Ariels Spicy Indian Potatoes
Dried Green Mango Powder, Crushed Black Peppercorns, Vegetable oil, Large Potatoes, (Amchoor or Umchoor), 
1) Cut potatoes into shoestrings (ie thin fries).    2) Pan fry in oil. Remove to serving platter.    3) Sprinkle liberally with crushed black peppercorns and green mango     powder.    From the kitchen of Peggy and Bruce Travers,Cyberealm BBS Watertown NY  315-786-1120
