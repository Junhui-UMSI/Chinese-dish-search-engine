Aloha Bread
Brown sugar, Dry milk, Reserved pineapple juice, Shredded coconut; toasted, Bread flour, Salt, Pecan or walnut halves, Yeast, Reserved pineapple juice, Dry milk, Margarine or butter;, Pecan or walnut halves, Yeast, Shredded coconut; toasted, (8 ounces) crushed pineapple, Bread flour, (8 ounces) crushed pineapple, Plus 1 teaspoon Packed brown, Water, Salt, FOR 2 POUND LOAF, Margarine or butter;, FOR 1 1/2 POUND LOAF, Water, 
This bread machine recipe is delicious and one of my favorites    Measure carefully, placing all ingredients in bread machine pan in the  order recommended by the manufacturer.    Select Sweet or Basic/White cycle. Use medium or light crust color. Do not  use delay cycle. Remove baked bread from pan and cool on a wire rack    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 423 by "rudolph"  <rudolph@1st.net> on Dec 30, 1997
