Perfect Jasmine Rice
Fat, Protein, Calcium, Sodium, Water, Jasmine rice, Fresh cilantro, NUTRITIONAL INFO PER SERVING, Carbohydrate, Calories, Cholesterol, 
Rinse rice in cool water and drain.     In a deep saucepan, bring rice and water to a boil over high heat. Cover,  reduce heat to low and simmer until rice is tender, about 20 minutes.  Remove from heat.     Fluff rice with a fork, cover and let steam for 10 minutes. Garnish with  cilantro and serve hot.
