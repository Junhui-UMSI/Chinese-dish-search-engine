Sweet Potatoe Crunch
marshmellows, sweet potatoes, Butter, Sugar, Brown sugar, Flour-plain, nuts (pecans), Butter, TOPPING, 
May use can mashed sweet potatoes.  Season with vanilla (1 t), salt (1/2  t), cinnamon 1/2 tsp.  Whip all together and pour in greased casserole. Mix  topping ingredients on top and bake for 30 minutes in 350 degree oven.
