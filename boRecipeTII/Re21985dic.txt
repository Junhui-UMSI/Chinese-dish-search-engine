Applesauce Custard Pie
Eggs, Cinnamon, Applesauce, Sugar, 9-inch unbaked single pie, milk, 
Beat eggs, sugar, cinnamon and milk. Mix in applesauce. Pour into shell.  Bake at 450 F. for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 F. for 30 minutes.
