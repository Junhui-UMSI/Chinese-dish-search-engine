Quick Cake Collins
Vegetable oil, Unsweetened Chocolate, Semi-sweet chocolate, Baking soda, CHOCOLATE ICING, Water, Buttermilk, Butter, Flour, milk, Semi-sweet chocolate, Pecans, Sugar, Salt, Eggs, Butter, Unsweetened Chocolate, -(up to), 
CAKE:  Boil together the first 5 ingredients until chocolate is melted. Mix  the next 6 ingredients together. Combine 2 mixtures and pour into greased  and floured 9x13-inch pan and bake at 400 for 20-30 minutes. Ice cake while  hot. CHOCOLATE ICING: Melt butter and chocolates together. Pour into  confectiners sugar and milk. Blend well. Add pecans.
