Amandari Smoked Salmon Angel Pasta
Shallots, zest of 1 orange, , Dried angel hair, smoked salmon, fresh, plum tomatoes, capers, Eggs, Black Pepper, 
1. In a bowl, combine the tomatoes, shallots, and capers. Add the tarragon,  orange zest, and salmon, then the 1/4 cup olive oil. Gently fold the  ingredients together. Season with the pepper. Cover and let rest at room  temperature for about 1 hour.    2. Shortly before serving, bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the  remaining tablespoon of oil and the pasta and cook until just tender. Drain  the pasta and divide among 4 shallow pasta bowls. Top each portion evenly  with the tomato and salmon mixture. Sprinkle each portion with chopped egg  and serve immediately.
