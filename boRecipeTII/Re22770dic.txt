Apricot Pork Medallions
Pork tenderloin medallions;, Green onions; sliced, Dried mustard, Buttered noodles, Apricot jam, Cider vinegar, Butter; plus, Butter, 
Flatten medallions slightly. Heat 1 Tablespoon of butter over Medium-High  heat in a skillet. Saute pork about 2 minutes on each side. Remove from  pan. To juice, add 1 tsp. buitter, apricot jam, green onions, mustard and  cider vinegar. Cover and simmer for 3-4 minutes. Add prok to heat through.  Serve with buttered noodles. and green salad.
