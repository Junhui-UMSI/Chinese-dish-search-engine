Pumpkin Mincemeat Pie
Brown sugar, Mincemeat, 9-inch unbaked pie shell, Each ground cloves and salt, (14 oz) pumpkin puree, Light cream, Eggs, Ground ginger, Cinnamon, 
"The addition of a mincemeat layer gives a delicious, tangy flavor and  nutty texture."    In bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, eggs, light cream, brown sugar,  cinnamon, ginger, cloves and salt. Spread mincemeat over bottom of pie  shell; top with the pumpkin mixture. Bake in preheated oven at 375 F. about  45 minutes, or until pumpkin is just set in center.
