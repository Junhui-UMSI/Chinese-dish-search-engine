Bacon and Swiss Waffles
baking mix, milk, Eggs, Swiss cheese , Bacon, 
HEAT waffle iron; grease if necessary.  Beat baking mix, milk and eggs  with wire whisk or hand beater until smooth. Stir in cheese and bacon.     POUR batter by scant 1 cupfuls onto center of hot waffle iron.     BAKE until steaming stops and waffle is golden brown. Carefully remove  waffle. Twelve 4-inch squares.
