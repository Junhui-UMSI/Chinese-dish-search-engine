Perfect Apple Pie
Nutmeg, Crust uncooked pastry, Tart apples,pared --, Flour, Cinnamon, Nemeth-GNFK05B, Cored(6cup, Butter, Sugar, 
If apples arent tart. sprinkle with 1 tablespoon of lemon juice. Thinly  slice apples. Combine sugar, flour, spices, salt and mix with apples. Line  9 inch pie plate with pasry. Fill with apples, dot with butter. Adjust top  crust. Cut slits for escape of steam in top of crust. seal. Sprinkle with  sugar. Bake at 400 F for 50 minutes or until done.
