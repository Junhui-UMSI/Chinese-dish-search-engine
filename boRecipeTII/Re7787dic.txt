Portobello Burgers
Kaiser rolls; sliced in half, Provolone cheese, Portobello mushrooms;, Pesto sauce, Italian dressing, Bottled roasted red bell, Bottled roasted red bell, 
Prep: 10 min, Marinate: 2:00, Cook: 5 min.    Combine mushrooms and Italian dressing in a bowl. Cover and marinate 2  hours. Prepare grill or turn on broiler. Season mushrooms with salt and  pepper to taste. Grill or broil mushrooms about 1-1/2 minutes per side.  Spread 1 Tbs. pesto over cut side of rolls.
