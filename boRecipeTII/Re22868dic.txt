Apricot-Orange Tea
Cloves, Ground cinnamon, Apricot nectar, Orange juice, Instant tea, FROM LOIS FLACK, Lemon slices, Sugar, Water, A COLD-WEATHER WARMER, 
1.  Combine apricot nectar, orange juice, water, sugar, and cinnamon  in a medium-size sauce-pan.  Insert cloves into each lemon slice; add  to sauce-pan.    2.  Heat just to boiling; reduce heat; cover. Simmer 5 minutes.  Stir  in tea.    Serve hot.
