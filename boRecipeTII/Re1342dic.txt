Easy Apple Kugel
Sugar, Flour, Eggs; (I used egg beaters), Apple pie filling, Stick melted margarine; (I, Lemon juice; (I used, Cornflake crumbs, 
Source: Amit Women/Efrat Chapter Cookbook (Chicago Area)    Melt margaine; set aside 3 TB of melted margarine for topping. Mix  remaining ingredients and pour into a greased 9 x 9 inch pan (I used  spray). Cover with cornflake crumbs and pur 3 TB margarine over top. Bake  at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to one hour.    Note: I only partially baked it before shabbat and it sat in a 225 degree  oven for two hours uncovered and it was fine.
