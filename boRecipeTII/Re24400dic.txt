Tropical Broiled Chops
Ground cloves, Garlic, Ground nutmeg, Brown sugar, firmly packed, Unsweetened pineapple juice, Chicken broth, Ground ginger, Red pepper flakes, crushed, Pork loin chops, Lime juice, 
In a large bag with a tight fitting seal, combine all ingredients in order  listed. Seal bag and place it in the refrigerator for 24 hours or  overnight, turning bag occasionally to distribute marinade. Preheat  broiler. Remove chops from marinade and discard marinade. Place chops on  the unheated rack of a broiler pan. Broil about 4 inches from the heat for  4 minutes. Turn chops and broil 3 to 5 minutes more or until done.
