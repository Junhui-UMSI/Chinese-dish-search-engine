Banana Orange Frost
ADDITIONAL, Ripe banana, peeled, Cold milk, Pineapple sherbet, Pineapple sherbet, ADDITIONAL, Orange juice, Orange sherbet, Pineapple juice, Ripe banana, peeled, Cold milk, Orange Sherbet, VARIATION, 
Mash banana in small bowl with a fork.  Stir in juice.  Beat mixture with a  mixer until smooth.  Stir in milk and one cup sherbet.  Beat until smooth.  (or use blender)    Pour into two tall glasses.  Top each glass with several scoops or sherbet.    Decorate each glass with an orange slice if you like.    ++ Typed but not tested ++ ++ Courtesy of Dale & Gail Shipp, Columbia Md.  ++    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Oct 26, 98
