Creole Beans and Rice
Red beans; Louisiana Style, Bacon, Hillshire Farms Lean &, Onions, Garlic, Chopped parsley, Cooked rice; keep hot, 
Cook bacon. Remove from pan, reserving a little bit of the drippings. saute  sausage in drippings then add onions and garlic and crumbled bacon,  stirring occasionally and cooking until translucent. Combine with the  cooked beans and add the parsley. Serve over hot rice.
