Sweet Corn and Anaheim Chile Pepper Relish
Red Bermuda onion -- diced, Small, Red Anaheim chili peppers --, Bell pepper -- diced small, Fluid ounces  white vinegar, Small, Diced small, Green Anaheim chili peppers, Head frisee lettuce, Cucumber, peeled, seeded --, Sugar, Thin,few seeds, Fluid ounces  olive oil --, Fresh lime juice, Cumin, % Extra Virgin, Thin,few seeds, Sweet corn, Red bell pepper -- diced, Tabasco sauce, White onions -- diced small, Salt -- to taste, 
Combine all the ingredients and allow them to set at room temperature for  one hour before using.    CHEFS NOTE: Refrigerate overnight and the flavor builds.    Spoon the relish onto the well-washed leaves of Frisee lettuce. Serve as  part of "Grilled Grouper Savoy Grill" (see recipe of that name in this  cookbook).
