Antoines Oysters Rockerfeller
Tabasco, Oysters, Fresh chopped spinach, Celery, Pernod liqueur, onion, Salt, Parsley, Bread crumbs, frozen spinach, Butter, 
Melt butter; add crumbs and saute 1 minute, stirring constantly. Combine  with other ingredients, except oysters, in blender until smooth. Arrange  oysters in shells (or individual dishes). Top each with 1 tablespoon  spinach mixture and broil 3-5 minutes or until lightly browned.
