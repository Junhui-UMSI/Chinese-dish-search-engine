Tuna Quickie
Tuna in oil, Onion, Mushrooms; chopped fine, Cheese; grated, Egg, 
Dont drain oil off the tuna. Mix all ingredients together and put in a  pan. cook over low heat until the egg and cheese is cooked.    Recipe by: Nancy Posted to EAT-L Digest by "Iris E. Dunaway"  <hister@JUNO.COM> on Sep 3, 1997
