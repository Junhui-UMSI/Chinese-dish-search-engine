Ultimate Cheese Bread
?, Butter or margarine, French Bread, Swiss cheese, Garlic powder, 
Cut bread diagonally into 1 1/2 inch slices to within 1/2 inch of bottom.  Repeat cuts in opposite direction. Cut cheese into 1-inch squares. Place  one cheese and one mushroom if desired into each slit. Combine butter and  garlic powder; spoon over the bread. Place on an ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 350? for 8-10 minutes or until cheese is melted.    Recipe by: Quick Cooking - March/April 1998    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by The Taillons <taillon@access.mountain.net> on  Mar 29, 1998
