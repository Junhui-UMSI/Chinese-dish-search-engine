Texas Barbecue Ribs
Dried Oregano, Chili powder, Dried cumin, Garlic powder, Beef back ribs; trimmed and, Dried thyme, 
Preheat the oven to 375F. In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients  except the ribs. Line a large roasting pan with aluminum foil, then place a  roasting rack on the foil. Rub the seasoning mixture over all sides of the  ribs, then place on the roasting rack. Roast, uncovered, for 60 to 65  minutes, or until no pink remains.    NOTE: This dry seasoning rub can also be used on chicken, turkey and meats  of all kinds!
