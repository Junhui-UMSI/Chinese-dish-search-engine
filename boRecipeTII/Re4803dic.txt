Picante De Huevos (Egg and Avocado Appetizer)
Mangoes; halved, seeded, Cilantro, Chile, habanero; seeded,, HEAT SCALE = HOT, Onions; chopped fine, Avocados; peeled, pitted,, Salt, Eggs; hard-cooked, chopped, Parsley; chopped fine, Vinegar, 
Place all the ingredients except the mangoes and cilantro in a food  processor or blender and blend at medium speed until very well combined and  fairly smooth. Chill for several hours. Pour the sauce into the hollowed  out section of the mango halves, and garnish with cilantro.
