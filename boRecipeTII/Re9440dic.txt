Pumpkin Aple Streusel Muffins
Butter or margarine soft, STREUSEL, Sugar, Flour, Pumpkin pie spice, Salt, Baking soda, Flour, Eggs, Pumpkin, Vegetable oil, Finely chopped; peeled apple, Sugar, Ground cinnamon, 
In a large bowl, combine first five ingredients; set aside. In another  bowl, combine eggs, ppumpkin and oil. Stir pumpkin mixture into dry  ingredients just until moistened. Stir in apples. Spoon batter into greased  muffin tins, filling 3/4 full. Combine streusel ingredients and sprinkle  over batter. Bake at 400 degrees for 20-22 minutes or until toothpick comes  out clean. Yield 2 dozen.    NOTES : Notes: A Christmas to Remember  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 312 by "lbrandau"  <lbrandau@intrnet.net> on Nov 26, 1997
