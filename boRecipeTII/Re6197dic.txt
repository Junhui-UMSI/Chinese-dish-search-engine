Pizza Sauce #5
Grated Parmesan cheese, Raw onion (chopped), Glove garlic (chopped), Water, Black pepper, Tomato puree, Basil, Oregano, Cooking oil, Brown sugar, 
Combine ingredients in a 2 qt sauce pan and cook for 30-40 minutes. Makes  enough sauce for 5 12" Pizzas.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on Feb 06, 98
