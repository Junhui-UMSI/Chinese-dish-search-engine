Yorkshire Pudding with Roast Beef
Eggs, Salt, 5 to 7 lb. rump roast, Flour, milk, 
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.Cook rump roast in 9 x 9" pan for 25 to 30  minutes or until internal temperature in meat is 150 to 170 degrees. Mix  and sift flour and salt.Add milk to make a smooth paste.Then add eggs,beat  2 minutes in electric mixer. Cover bottom of 9 x 9" pan about 1/4" with  drippings from roast. Pour mixture 1/2" deep on drippings in pan.Bake 20 to  30 minutes. Delicious when served.
