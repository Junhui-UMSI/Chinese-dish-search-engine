Creamy Irish Coffee
Irish cream liqueur, Freshly brewed coffee, Heavy cream, slightly, Sweetened and whipped, opt., 
Divide the liqueur and coffee among two mugs. Top with poufs of whipped  cream if desired. Serve at once.
