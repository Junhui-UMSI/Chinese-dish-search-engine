Banana Bread
Sunflower Oil, Dried Mixed Fruit, Bananas, Self-Raising Flour, Butter, Eggs, Salt, 
Mix all the ingredients, except the dried fruit, together in a food  processor. When the bananas are completely mashed, add the dried fruit. Mix  well and pour into a buttered non-stick 2 pound loaf tin, leaving an inch  from the top. Bake for 90 minutes at gas mark 4, 350F, 180C. Cover the top  with foil if you think it might burn. Leave in the tin for a minute or two  then turn on to a rack.
