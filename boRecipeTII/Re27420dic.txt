Toasty Cinnamon Popcorn
PATTI  VDRJ67A, Cinnamon toast flavor cereal, Margarine or butter, Salted popped popcorn, 
Heat oven to 300~. Heat margarine in 13x9" pan until melted. Stir in cereal  and popcorn until evenly coated with margarine. Bake 5 minutes; stir. Bake  5 minutes longer. Cool and store in airtight container. Makes 12 cups.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 655 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 31, 1998
