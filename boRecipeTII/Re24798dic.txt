Aunt Lauras Chocolate Cake
Squares chocolate, Eggs; well beaten, Butter, Baking soda, Salt, Flour, Boiling water or coffee;, Square chocolate; melted, Sugar, Buttermilk, -(up to), Vanilla, 
Melt 3 squares chocolate in water. Cool. Combine eggs, sugar, salt and  butter. Add chocolate mixture, vanilla, buttermilk and soda. Add flour.  Pour into three 8- or 9-inch pans. Bake at 375 about 30 minutes. Cool and  ice with Colonnade Icing (see Index). Dribble 1 square chocolate over top  and sides.
