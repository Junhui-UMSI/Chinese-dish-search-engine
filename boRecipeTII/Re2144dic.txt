Apricot Beef Bake
Onion, White vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, Beef stew meat; R-T-C, large, Canned apricot halves; with, Brown sugar, Ground allspice, Salt, Tomato sauce, Ground ginger, 
Place stew meat in 3 quart casserole dish. Sprinkle onion over top. Bake,  uncovered, in 400F oven for 10 minutes. Stir. Bake for 10 minutes.    Reduce oven temperature to 325F. Add remaining 9 ingredients. Stir. Cover.  Bake for 2-1/2 to 3 hours until very tender. Serves 8.    1 serving: 151 calories; 19 g protein; 3.5 g total fat; 11 g carbohydrate;      443 mg sodium.    Note: The apricot flavor is mild but noticeable. An easy one-dish stew. Can  be made ahead and frozen.
