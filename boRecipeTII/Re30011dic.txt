Baklava (Lebanese)
Drawn butter, walnuts, lemon juice, Butter, sugar, BASIC SYRUP:, Sugar, Rose water, Water, Filo dough, Rose water, 
Preparation : Combine nuts, sugar and rose water. Filo dough may be spread  in a buttered 10 x 14 inch pan, brushing each layer with butter. Half way  through the layering, place nut mixture in 1/2 to 3/4 inch layer. Then  continue layering buttered filo on top.  Cut in diamond shaped pieces. Bake  at 300 degrees for one hour or until golden brown. Pour syrup over baklava  making sure the dough is well saturated. Combine sugar, water and lemon  juice in saucepan.  Boil over medium heat for 10 to 15 minutes or until  slightly viscous (225 degrees). Before removing from heat, add rose water  and let come to a boil. Remove from fire and cool.
