Basic Chocolate Fondue
Milk chocolate (bar or bulk), Cream, Kirsch; Cointreau, or, 
Date: Fri, 22 Mar 1996 15:24:22 -0800    From: Gerald Edgerton <jerrye@wizard.com>    Warm cream in saucepan over low heat.  Stirring constantly, add the  chocolate, broken into small pieces. Continue stirring until chocolate is  melted and well blended. Add liquor or brandy, if desired. Serve in fondue  dish over candle or very low heat. Offer a choice or fruit to dunk.
