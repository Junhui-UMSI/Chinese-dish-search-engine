Garlic Cheese Biscuits
Bisquick, milk, Garlic powder, Butter, OR 1/4 cup olive oil, Shredded sharp Cheddar, Cheese, Makes 1 dozen., 
Preheat oven to 425F. Combine Bisquick and 1/4 teaspoon of the garlic  powder; mix in cheese. Add milk, stirring just until dry ingredients are  moistened. Roll or pat dough on a lightly floured surface to 1/2"  thickness; cut with a 1 1/2-inch cutter.   Place on a lightly greased baking sheet.  Combine melted butter and the  remaining 1/8 teaspoon of the garlic powder; brush on top of biscuits and  bake for 10 minutes or until golden.
