Three-Cheese Rigatoni
Colby Cheese, milk, Margarine, Uncooked Rigatoni, Fresh Basil, Sour Cream, Brick Cheese, Carrot, Celery, Grated Parmesan cheese, Seasoned bread crumbs, 
Cook rigatoni as directed on package; drain. Mix rigatoni and remaining  ingredients except bread crumbs and margarine. Place rigatoni mixture in an  ungreased 2-qt. casserole. Mix bread crumbs and margarine.    To serve now: heat oven to 375 degrees. Sprinkle bread crumb mixture around  edge of casserole. Bake uncovered 25-30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.    To Store in Refrigerator: Cover casserole tightly, cover bread crumb  mixture tightly and refrigerate no longer than 48 hours.    To Cook from Fridge: About 1 hour before serving, heat oven to 375 degrees.  Sprinkle bread crumb mixture around edge of casserole. Bake uncovered about  50 minutes or until hot and bubbly.    Recipe by: Betty Crockers Do-Ahead Cookbook, 1994, pg. 72    Posted to recipelu-digest by Jill & Joe Proehl <jpxtwo@swbell.net> on Feb  08, 1998
