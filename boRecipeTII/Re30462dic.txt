Banana Mocha Ice Cream
Eggs; clean, uncracked, Bananas, extra ripe; peeled, Vanilla, Sugar, Chocolate syrup, Ground cinnamon, Salt, PATTI VDRJ67A, Whipping cream, Half & half, Instant coffee crystals, 
Slice bananas into blender; process until pureed (1-1/2 cups). Add eggs;  process to blend. Pour banana mixture and remaining ingredients into ice  cream freezer container. Stir until sugar dissolves and mixture is blended.  Freeze according to manufacturers directions. Makes 1-1/2 quarts.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by lena36@juno.com (Lena P Mitchell) on Apr 5,  1998
