American-Style Strata
Dry mustard, Whole-grain bread -- halved, Mozzarella or monterey jack, Sourdough bread -- or, Black Pepper, Carrot or zucchini -- shred, Crumble, Dried basil leaves --, Cut bite-size, Dried thyme leaves --, Eggs, milk, Salt, Cooked beef -- lamb or pork, Worcestershire, Crumble, 
In a 10x6x2 baking dish, layer bread slices to cover bottom of dish.  Sprinkle cheese, meat and veggies over bread. In a bowl, stir togeteher the  eggs, milk and seasonings till well mixed. Pour carefully over ingredients  in baking dish. Cover. Let stand 1 hour at room temperature or in fridge  for longer periods. Uncover. Bake 325~ for 50 to 60 minutes or until center  is nearly set. Let stand 10 minutes. Cut into squares.    Recipe By     :    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
