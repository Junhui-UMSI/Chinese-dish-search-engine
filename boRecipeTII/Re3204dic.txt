Pear and Pineapple Soup
Muscat wine, Pineapple; peeled, Lemon, Chamomile tea, Honey; to taste, Pears; peeled, 
Recipe by: CHEF DU JOUR GALE GAND SHOW #DJ9164 Peel pineapple and pears  then cut up. In a blender or food processor puree fruits. Strain through a  fine sieve. Season to taste with tea, wine, honey and lemon juice. Serve  cold garnished with pineapple leaves.    Yield: 6 to 8 servings CHEF DU JOUR GALE GAND SHOW #DJ9164    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "M.
