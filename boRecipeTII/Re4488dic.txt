Pesto Salmon and Sea Scallops with Lemon/garlic Beurre Bla
Herbed bread crumbs, Salmon fillets, Sea scallops, Garlic Beurre Blanc, Basic pesto, 
Brush the Basic Pesto on top of each salmon fillet.  Sprinkle the herbed  bread crumbs on top. Preheat the oven to 375F. In an oiled baking dish  place the salmon and the scallops. Bake them for 12 to 15 minutes, or until  they are just done. Turn them over once. On each of 4 individual servings  plates place the Lemon Roasted Garlic Beurre Blanc. Place a salmon fillet  and two sea scallops on top of the sauce.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
