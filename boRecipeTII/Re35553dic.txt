Street Chalk
Plaster of paris (do not, Liquid tempera paints, any, About 1/3 to 1/2 cup cool, 
Pour plaster into a disposable container (i.e. margarine container or large  paper cup.) Stir in most of the water. Add 2-3 tablespoons of liquid  tempera, mixing well, especially at the bottom. Add a little more water as  the mixture thickens. Stir well and pour into containers. Three-ounce paper  cups work well. Peel paper off when the chalk is dry.
