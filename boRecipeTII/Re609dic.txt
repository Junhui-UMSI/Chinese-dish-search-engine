Garlic-Hot Pepper Vinegar
White vinegar, garlic, Fresh cayenne, 
In a small stainless steel saucepan, heat vinegar to boiling. Meanwhile  thread garlic and hot pepper on wooden skewer. Place in clean pint jar.  Carefully pour hot vinegar into jar. Cover; let stand several days at room  temperature to blend flavors. If desired, strain into decorative jar and  add fresh garlic and peppers. Store in cool, dark place. Makes 1 pint.
