Aloha Cheesecake
Macadamia nuts; toasted, Margarine; melted, Cream cheese, Crushed pineapple; drained, Eggs, Vanilla wafer crumbs, Sugar, Kiwi; peeled, sliced, milk, 
From: mark@alexr.co.uk    Date: Sun, 25 Feb 1996 00:00:24 GMT    Combine crumbs and margarine; press onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.  Bake at 350F., 10 minutes.  Combine cream cheese, sugar and milk, mixing  at medium speed on electric mixer until well blended. Add eggs, one at a  time, mixing well well after each addition. Stir in nuts; pour over crust.  Bake at 350F., 45 minutes. Loosen caek from rim of pan; cool before  removing rim of pan. Chill. Before serving, top with fruit.
