Apple Pie with Rum Raisin Sauce
Sugar, All-purpose flour, Ground cinnamon, Rum Raisin Sauce, Pastry For Double-Crust 9", Dark Rum, Butter, Raisins, Water, Cornstarch, Fuji Apples; Peeled, Cored,, Granulated sugar, Orange juice, 
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. In large bowl, toss the apples with sugar,  flour and cinnamon. Roll out pie pastry and line bottom of a 9" pie pan.  Fill with apple mixture. Place top crust over apples; seal and crimp edges.  With knife, cut eight 1-inch-long vents in top crust. Bake 15 minutes;  reduce temperature to 350 degrees and bake 40 minutes longer.    Prepare Rum Raisin Sauce. In small bowl, combine raisins and rum; set aside  20 minutes. In small saucepan, over medium heat, combine sugar and water;  stir until mixture comes to a boil. Boil 5 minutes. Remove from heat and  stir in rum and raisin mixture, orange juice, butter and cornstarch. Heat  to boiling, stirring constantly until mixture thickens. Remove from heat  and cool until warm. (Makes 1 cup)    Cool pie at least 20 minutes. To serve, cut pie into eight wedges; top each  wedge with 2 Tablespoons warm Rum Raisin Sauce.
