Variations on Rice Krispies Marshmallow Squares
Marshmallow Sqs, 
VARIATIONS: Use Cocoa Krispies instead of Rice Krispies. Melt 2  squares of unsweetened chocolate with the marshmallows. Add 1/4 cup  peanut butter to marshmallows.
