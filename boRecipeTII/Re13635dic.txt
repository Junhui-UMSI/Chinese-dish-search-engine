Wild Mushroom Pumpkin Soup
Vegetable stock, Dried mushrooms, (porcini and shiitake), Onion, finely chopped, Sliced fresh mushrooms, Minced fresh chives, Salt, -=OR=-, Black pepper, Nutmeg, Cooked or canned pumpkin, Hot water, Tamari, olive oil, Minced fresh parsley, 
Soak dried porcini and shiitake mushrooms in hot water for 20  minutes. Chop rehydrated mushrooms and reserve soaking liquid.  Saute  chopped onion and sliced fresh mushrooms and soaking liquid and  vegetable stock.    Add pumpkin, nutmeg, salt or tamari and black pepper.  Cook over  medium heat and stir until heated through.  Garnish with parsley and  chives.
