Vegetable Rice Pancakes
Grated carrots, Vegetable oil, Cooked brown rice, Snipped fresh parsley, Salt, Finely chopped onions, Garlic, Whole wheat flour, Eggs, Black Pepper, 
Combine all ingredients except oil.  Form into 12 thin patties, pressing  firmly with hands.  In large skillet, cook patties in heated oil until  brown, turning once.  Serve with dairy sour cream or yogurt, if desired.    Each serving provides:  * 243 calories  * 5.7 g. protein  * 11.6 g. fat  * 29.6 g. carbohydrate  * 390 mg. sodium  * 91 mg. cholesterol    NOTE: Optional ingredients are omitted from the nutritional calculations.  When ingredient options appear in a recipe, the first ingredient choice is  used for calculation.
