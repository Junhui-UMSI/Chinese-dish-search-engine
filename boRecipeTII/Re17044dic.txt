Vietnamese Spicy Beef Stew (Bo Ham Gia Vi)
Garlic, Daikon, Peanut oil, Tomato paste, Fish sauce, Water, Lemon grass, Vietnamese curry powder, Star anise, Onion, Sugar, Carrots, Chili paste, cinnamon, Stew beef, Ginger, Potatoes, 
1. Mix the beef, lemon grass, ginger, fish sauce, sugar, curry powder and  chili paste in a bowl. Set aside for one hour.    2. Heat wok. Add peanut oil and beef with marinade, stir fry 2 minutes  until beef loses its pink color. Remove to a bowl.    3. Heat wok. Add peanut oil, onion and garlic. Stir fry 1 minute until  fragrant.    4. Add tomato paste and stir fry 2 minutes.    5. Add beef and marinade, water, cinnamon and star anise.    6. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 1-1/2 to 2 hours, until beef  is tender.    7. Add carrots, potatoes and daikon, cook until tender. Remove cinnamon  stick and star anise before serving.
