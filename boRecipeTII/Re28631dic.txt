Texas Caviar 2
Blackeyed Peas, Picante Sauce, Tomatoes, Garlic, Cilantro, Green onions, Green Pepper, 
Chop vegetables. Combine all ingredients. Serve with chips. Best if made  well ahead of time so all the flavors can blend.
