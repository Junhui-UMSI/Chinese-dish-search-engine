Baked Chicken with Garlic and Apples Salt Free
Paprika, garlic, Pepper, Butter, McIntosh apples, chickens, 
Directions: Sprinkle the chicken with paprika and pepper. Do not peel the  garlic, but remove any obvious tough outer layer. Cut the apples in fourths  and core. Lay the garlic and apples among the chicken pieces. Dot with  butter and bake as long as you like chicken to bake. The apples will have  cooked down, and the garlic cloves can be eaten skin and all.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdjaxxx.zip
