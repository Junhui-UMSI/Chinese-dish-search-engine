49er Christmas Bread
Sugar, milk, Sugar, Flour, Eggs;beaten, Currants;or raisins, Cinnamon, Starter, Salt, Mace, 
add salt, sugar, milk, eggs, melted butter, currants and spices to the  culture and mix well. Add the flour a cup a a time until too stiff to mix  by hand. Then turn onto a floured board and knead in remaining flour Divide  in half form two loaves place in greased loaf pan and let rise 1-1/2 to 3  hours til the dough rises 1 to 2 inches above lip of pan. bake in preheated  oven 400 for 55 to 60 minutes cool on racks.
