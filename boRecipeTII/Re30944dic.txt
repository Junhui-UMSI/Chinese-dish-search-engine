Tapenade-Molto Mario
Anchovy fillets, soaked in, Pitted olives (Gaeta,, Dijon mustard, Anchovy paste, Capers, roughly chopped, Red onion, roughly chopped, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Red wine vinegar, 
In a food processor, blend all ingredients until a smooth, homogenous paste  is formed (about 2 minutes). Tapenade may be refrigerated several weeks,  well wrapped.
