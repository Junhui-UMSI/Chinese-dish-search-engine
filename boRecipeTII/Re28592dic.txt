Tex-Mex Turkey Tenderloins
Turkey tendeloin steaks;, (1) can diced green chili;, Nonstick spray coating;, Pepper; ground, Tomato; chopped seeded, Salt;, Grd cumin;, Slicied green onions;, Fresh chili peppers, Peppers;, Zucchini; chopped, 
Rinse turkey; pat dry.  Stir cumin, salt, and pepper; sprinkle on both  sides of turkey tenderloin steaks.    Spray a cold large skillet with nonstick coating.  Preheat over medium  heat.  Cook turkey in skillet for 10 to 12 minutes or until tender  and no longer pink, turning once.  Transfer turkey to serving  platter. Cover with foil to keep warm.    Add tomato, zucchini, green onions, and diced chili peppers to  skillet. Cook and stir over high heat for 1 to 2 minutes or until  vegetables are crisp-tender.  Spoon vegetables over turkey.  If  disired, garnich with fresh chili peppers.  Makes 4 servings.
