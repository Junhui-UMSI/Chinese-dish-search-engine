Wreath of Rice
Green Onions, Rice, butter, mushrooms, Salt, Beef consomme, water, 
Combine all ingredients; spoon into greased 3-quart ring mold. Cover with  foil. Bake at 450 degrees for 1 hour. Let stand for 10 minutes. Remove  foil; press rice mixture down with spoon. Invert carefully on serving  platter.  Garnish with holly leaves if desired.
