Lamb Chops with Orange Sauce
Lamb chops; rib, Vegetable oil, Orange juice, Honey, Salt, Cornstarch, Orange peel; grated, 
In skillet, brown lamb chops in oil; drain well. Thoroughly combine orange  juicew, honey, salt, cornstarch and grated orange peel. Brush browned lamb  chops with orange mixture and place in Crock Pot. Cover and cook on Low  setting for 6 to 8 hours. If a thicker sauce is desired, remove chops  before serving and turn on High setting; stir in a mixture of 2 tablespoons  cornstarch and 1/4 cup water. Cook, stirring, until the sauce is  transparent.    Recipe by: PRK    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on May  12, 1998
