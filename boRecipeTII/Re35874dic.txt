Stuffed Jalapenos
Egg whites, beaten, Cornmeal, Pieces Cheddar cheese, Pickled jalapenos, Flour, 
oil for deep-frying    Slit the sides of the peppers, and scrape out the seeds. Stuff with  triangular pieces of cheddar cheese cut to fit the individual peppers.    Dip peppers in an egg wash then into a mixture of half cornmeal and half  flour.  Repeat, and allow to dry for about 15 minutes.    Deep fry in hot fat until the peppers are lightly browned, and the cheese  is just melting.  Drain on absorbent paper, and serve.    You can also use a crabmeat or shrimp salad stuffing for these.
