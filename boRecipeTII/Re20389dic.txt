Veal with Pasta Salad
Salt to taste, DRESSING, Plain yogurt, Dried basil, Small; thin slices of cold, Tomatoes; thinly sliced, Green pasta shells or green, Fresh basil leaves -or-, Finely chopped celery leaves, Raw asparagus tips, Parmesan cheese, Ricotta cheese, 
Combine veal, tomatoes, asparagus and chilled pasta. Chill. Mix all  dressing ingredients together. Salt to taste. Chill. To serve, divide salad  into 4 bowls and top with dressing. Calories: 260 per serving. Yield: 4  servings.    LOW CALORIE    BEVERLY MOORE    (MRS. RICHARD N., JR.)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.
