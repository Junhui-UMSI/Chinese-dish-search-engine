Tuna Noodle Casserole Very Simple
Cream of celery soup, Bag wide egg noodles, Half cup grated cheddar, Cheese, Can tuna, Cream of mushroom soup, Grated onion., 
Cook noodles and drain tuna. Mix all ingredients together in a buttered  casserole. Top with a little of the cheese and some cracker crumbs. Bake at  350 for 40 minutes or until bubbly.
