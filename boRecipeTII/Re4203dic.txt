Perfect Corn on the Cob
Ears Fresh Sweet Corn, Pepper, Salt, Butter, 
Husk and remove silk from ears of corn.  Place in a large pot and add just  enough water to cover.  Heat covered over high heat to boiling, then boil  2-3 minutes.  Drain.  Serve hot with butter, salt and pepper.  77 calories  per ear of corn.  From: Syds Cookbook.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sydsbook.zip
