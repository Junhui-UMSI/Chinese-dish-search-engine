Apple, Raisin, and Rosemary Dressing
Onions, Brandy, Celery, Chopped, peeled apples, Golden raisins, Dry Madeira or, Baking Powder, Fresh bread crumbs, Dried Rosemary, Salt, Water, Butter, 
Preheat the oven to 225 F Spread the crumbs on a baking sheet and dry and  toast them in the oven 5 to 10 minutes. Melt the butter in the water in a  large skillet. Add the celery and onions, and saute until the onions have  absorbed the butter and are limp. Dont brown them. Add the apples,  raisins, bread crumbs, baking powder, rosemary, and the Madeira or brandy.  Season with salt/pepper to taste and toss well with a fork. If the stuffing  seems overly dry, add an egg or a little chicken stock. Stuff a crown roast  of pork or a turkey per your pork/turkey baking times/temps or place in a  casserole dish and bake uncovered at 325 F until browned, about 25 minutes.
