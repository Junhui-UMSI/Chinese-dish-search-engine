Apple Cranberry Cookie Cobbler
(16 oz) whole berry, Granny Smith Apples; peeled,, Ground cinnamon, Brown sugar, (20 oz) refrigerated sugar, All-purpose flour, 
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Peel, core, and slice apples. Cut apples in  half; place in deep dish baker. Combine cranberry sauce, sugar, flour, and  cinnamon in bowl; mix well. Pour over apples; mix well. Cut cookie dough in  half; reserve one portion for another use. Slice cookie dough into 1/4-inch  thick slices. Arrange evenly over apple mixture. Bake 30-35 minutes or  until apples are tender and top is golden brown. Yield: 10 servings.
