Apple Butter Scones
Egg, Apple butter, Sugar, Butter or margarine, Additional apple butter; for, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Vegetable cooking spray, Raisins, 
Preheat oven to 425F. In a large bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder  and baking soda. Cut in butter or margarine with pastry cutter or fork  until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add raisins. In a separate bowl  combine apple butter and egg. Add flour mixture, stirring just until dry  ingredients are moistened. Spray 2 1/2 inch muffin tins with vegetable  cooking spray. Fill almost full with batter. Bake at 425F for 14 to 16  minutes or until lightly browned. Cool slightly. Serve warm with additional  apple butter.
