Swedish Meatballs in Sourcream
Bread cubes, Flour, Kitchen Boquet, Sour cream, Nutmeg, Garlic salt, milk, Onion, Paprika, Pepper, beef consomme, Eggs, Butter, Tomato paste, Salt, Ground beef, 
Soak bread in milk. Squeeze out milk. Add bread to beef, onion, salt,  paprika, eggs, nutmeg and pepper. Shape into 48 meatballs. Brown in butter  in large skillet. Add garlic and mustard to taste. Drain on paper towels.  Blend flour, paste, Kitchen Boquet and consomme in saucepan. Stir over low  heat until thick. When ready to serve, stir in sour cream gradually.  Combine meat balls and sauce.
