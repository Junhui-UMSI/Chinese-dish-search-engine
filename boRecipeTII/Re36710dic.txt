Strawberry Frost
Plain low-fat yogurt;, Part-skin ricotta cheese;, Garnish, Pure vanilla extract;, Frozen orange juice;, Frozen strawberries; (no, Extra strawberries for, Few sprigs fresh mint;, 
In a blender or a food proccessor fitted with metal blade, place  yogurt, ricotta, strawberries, vanilla and orange juice; process  until smooth. Spoon into parfait glasses and garnish with berries and  a mint sprig, if disired.  Serve at once.
