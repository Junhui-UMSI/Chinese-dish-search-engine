Strawberries with Homemade Mascarpone
Sugar, Egg yolks, Strawberries, Mascarpone, Pure vanilla, Half and half, 
MOLTO MARIO SHOW#MB5625 STRAWBERRIES WITH HOMEMADE MASCARPONE 4 servings  Divide fruit among 4 large wine glasses. Set aside. In a blender, combine  mascarpone, vanilla, sugar, half and half and egg yolks. Blend until  smooth, about 2 minutes. The texture should be like a thick pudding. Spoon  over the fruit and serve.
