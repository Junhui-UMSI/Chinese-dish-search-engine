Lemon Curd
Egg yolks, Lemon juice, Sugar, 
Mix the yolks with the sugar and juice in a non-reactive metal bowl. Place  the bowl over simmering water and whisk until the mixture has thickened and  turned a uniform yellow color. Remove from heat and cool. (served w. J.  McDavids gingerbread).
