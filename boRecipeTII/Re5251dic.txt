Pimiento Spread for Sandwich Filling
Salt, Sugar, Pimientos; drained and, Firm tofu; crumbled, Tofu, Apple cider vinegar, Sweet pickle relish, Garlic powder, Vegetable oil, Black pepper, 
Combine first 7 ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth and  creamy. Combine in bowl with remaining ingredients. Best if refrigerated  overnight.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
