Turkey Sloppy Joes
olive oil, Bell peppers; chopped, Onions; chopped, 8 oz can tomato sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, Ground turkey, Sloppy joe seasoning, 
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add turkey, onions, and bell  peppers until meat is no longer pink and vegetables are tender. Then add  seasoning mix, tomato sauce, and Worcestershire sauce; simmer for 15  minutes. Serve on toasted buns, if desired.
