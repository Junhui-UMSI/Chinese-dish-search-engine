Tomato and Zucchini Gratin
Dried bread crumbs, Ripe plum tomatoes, Grated Parmesan, Fruity olive oil, Chopped fresh thyme, Chopped fresh oregano, Salt, Chopped fresh flat leaf, Firm zucchini, 
Slice the tomatoes and zucchini into 1/4-inch thick round slices, as  uniformly as you can. On a cookie sheet or ovenproof platter, overlap the  tomato and zucchini in a decorative pattern, like red an d green shingles  on a gingerbread house. Blend all the dry ingredients together, then  scatter them evenly over the tomatoes and zucchini. Sprinkle the olive oil  on top and bake in a 375 degree oven u ntil golden and crusty, about 20  minutes. Present the platter or sheet at the table, using a cake server or  metal spatula to slide several overlapping pieces onto each diners plate.    >From Michaels Place Show # 1A23    Recipe by: Michael Lomonaco Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #533 by "Master  Harper Gaellon" <gaellon@inch.com> on Mar 21, 1997
