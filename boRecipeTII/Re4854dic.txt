Pickled Beets #1
Small beets; boiled, skinned, Vinegar, Artificial sweetner, Sugar --or--, Salt, Allspice, Stick cinnamon; 3-inches, Cloves, Water, 
Combine vinegar, water, sugar, spices and salt. Bring to boil and simmer 15  minutes. Add beets and simmer 5 minutes. Pack beets into sterile jars.  Bring syrup to a boil; pour over beets and seal. Note: By using artificial  sweetener this can be used for diabetics or dieters.    NOTE:  Get a book on canning if unusure about sealing and processing.
