Asian Style Braised Ham Steak (Mf)
Butter or vegetable oil, (up, Ham steak, 1 inch thick, Water, Finely sliced green onions, Dry sherry wine, Minced fresh ginger, Light soy sauce, Salt, 
Remove rind and trim off all but 1/4-inch of outer fat. Score the fat edge  at 1-inch intervals so the steak does not curl during cooking and divide  ham steak into 2 serving pieces. Marinate ham in soy sauce and sherry wine.  Remove ham from marinade. Reserve marinade.    Dry the ham steak and lightly brown in butter for a minute or 2 on both  sides. Add ginger, water and reserved marinade to the skillet and bring to  a boil. Cover and cook for 5 minutes or until heated through. Remove ham to  a serving platter. Boil the liquid down until syrupy and season to taste  with salt and pepper. Stir in the scallions and spoon sauce over steak.
