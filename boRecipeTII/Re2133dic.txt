Apples, Berries, and Yams (Low Fat)
OR olive oil, Apples, , Yams  , Whole-berry cranberry Sauce  , Orange marmalade, Butter or margarine, Ground nutmeg, Yield: 8-10 servings., 
Apples, Berries And Yams    In a skillet, melt butter (or heat oil) over medium heat. saute apples  until crisp-tender. Place apples and yams in a greased 3-qt. casserole.  Sprinkle with the nutmeg. Combine cranberry sauce and marmalade. Spoon over  yams. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes.
