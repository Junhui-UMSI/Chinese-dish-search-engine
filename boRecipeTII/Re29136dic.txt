Baked Rice Pudding
Salt, WATER; WARM, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, RAISINS #10, EGGS SHELL, IMITATION VANILLA, RICE 10LB, WATER; COLD, BUTTER PRINT SURE, MILK; DRY NON-FAT L HEAT, 
PAN:  12 BY 20 BY 2 1/2-INCH STEAM TABLE PAN     TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN  :    1.  COMBINE RICE, WATER, AND SALT. BRING TO A BOIL, STIRRING OCCASIONALLY.  REDUCE HEAT, COVER TIGHTLY; SIMMER 15 TO 20 MINUTES OR UNTIL WATER IS  ABSORBED.  SET ASIDE FOR USE IN STEP 3.    2. RECONSTITUTE MILK; ADD EGGS, BUTTER OR MARGARINE, SUGAR, AND VANILLA;  BLEND THOROUGHLY.    3.  ADD RICE AND RAISINS; BLEND THOROUGHLY.    4.  POUR ABOUT 1 GAL OF MIXTURE INTO EACH GREASED PAN.    5.  BAKE 40 MINUTES. STIR AFTER 10 MINUTES TO DISTRIBUTE RAISINS.    6.  COVER, REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.    7.  CUT 4 BY 6.
