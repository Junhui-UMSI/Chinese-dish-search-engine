Lefse
Potatoes; riced, Whipping cream, Butter, Salt, Flour, 
Peel potatoes. Cook, drain. Rice potatoes in a ricer *you might wish to  rice twice* Add butter, cream, and salt.    Let stand until cold.    Mix in flour and roll into balls. Keep on a plat in fridge and remove one  at a time for rolling and baking. 500 degrees on grill.
