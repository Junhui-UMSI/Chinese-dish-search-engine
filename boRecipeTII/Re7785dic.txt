Portobello Appetizer
roasted red bell pepper, bottled, Italian dressing, Provolone cheese, Portabello mushrooms, 
Combine mushrooms and Italian dressing in a bowl. Cover and marinate 2  hours. Prepare grill or turn on broiler. Season mushrooms with salt and  pepper to taste. Grill or broil mushrooms about 1-1/2 minutes per side. Top  with slices of peppers and cheese.  Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 19, 1998
