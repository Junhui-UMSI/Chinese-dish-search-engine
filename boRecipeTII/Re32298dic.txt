Barbequed Ham
Catsup, Mustard with horseradish, Worcestershire Sauce, Green pepper; chopped, Margarine, Onion, Brown sugar, Ham; sliced, 
Mix all ingredients except ham. Simmer sauce for 15 minutes. Add ham and  simmer 1 hour. Serve on buns. Serves 10-15.    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 29, 1998
