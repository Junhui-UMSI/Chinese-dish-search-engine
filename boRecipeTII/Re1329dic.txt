Dried Tomato And Rosemary Bread
Salt, Bread flour, Nonfat dry milk powder, Granulated sugar, Dried rosemary, crushed, Paprika, Bread machine yeast, Water, Snipped dried tomatoes,, Unsalted, not packed in oil, Olive or vegetable oil, 
Make according to machine directions, but add the tomatoes with the water.    Per serving: 106 cal., 4g pro., 19g carb., 2g (14%) fat, 1 mg chol.,      199 mg sod., 0g fiber.    Adapted from a recipe in: Womans Day Holiday Baking 1995 Shared and to be  tested by Carolyn Shaw 11-95.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Sep 07, 98
