Pineapple-Scotch Bonnet Mojo
Fresh orange juice, Scotch bonnet or habanero, Fruity Chardonnay-style wine, Large; ripe pineapple,, 
Place the pineapple, wine and orange juice in a food processor or blender,  and puree. Transfer to a mixing bowl and stir in the chile. Keep  refrigerated for up to 2 days or so. Transfer to a saucepan, stir and warm  before serving. Makes about 3 cups.    Posted to CHILE-HEADS DIGEST by Alex Silbajoris <72163.1353@compuserve.com>  on Apr 6, 1998
