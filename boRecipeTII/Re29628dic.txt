Tasty Fish Puffs
milk, Cooking oil, Fish; cooked, Eggs, Salt and pepper, Self-rising flour, Parsley, 
Flake fish with a little of the liquid it was cooked in, add flour,  seasoning and eggs. Add sufficient milk to make a soft consistency. Heat  oil until hot, drop in Tbs. of the mixture. Fry until golden brown. These  will puff up really well. Serve very hot with mashed potatoes and peas.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #804 by Roberta Banghart  <bobbi744@sojourn.com> on Sep 24, 1997
