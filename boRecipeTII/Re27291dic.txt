Title Grilled Squid Salad with Black Beans and Mango
Cilantro, chopped, Rice wine vinegar, Olive oil, to brush on squid, salt and pepper, Honey, Fresh mint chiffonade, Whole squid, skinned and, Diced mangos, Handfuls of arugula, olive oil, Chipotle puree, Cooked black beans, 
Brush squid with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.    Grill squid for 1 1/2 minutes on each side and cut into 2inch pieces.    While the squid is still warm, toss the squid with the remaining  ingredients and serve on a platter.
