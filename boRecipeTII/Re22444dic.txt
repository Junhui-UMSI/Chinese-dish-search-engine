Tuscan Bean Soup with Turkey
Celery, thinly sliced, Thyme, dried, Bay leaf, Garlic, Marjoram, dried, Pepper, Turkey, diced, cooked, Tomato juice, (15 oz) cannellini beans,, (14 oz) chicken broth, low, Basil, dried, Carrots, thinly sliced, Onion, 
PREPARATION: In large saucepan combine broth, carrots, celery, onion,  herbs, pepper, garlic and bay leaf. Simmer, uncovered, 15 minutes or until  vegetables are tender. Add remaining ingredients; simmer until hot and  bubbly.    NOTES : Even better the next day.
