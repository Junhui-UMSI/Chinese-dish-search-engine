Barbequed Sauerkraut
Tomatoes, Polish Sausage, Sauerkraut, Butter, Onions, Bacon bits, Brown sugar, Bacon, 
In a small skillet, saute onion with bacon. In a 2 quart casserole dish,  combine kraut, tomatoes, brown sugar, bacon and onion (add polish sausage  if  desired). Bake uncovered, for 1-2 hours in a 350 degree oven. Casserole  should  look caramelly around the edges. Serves 6-8.
