Zucchini Coleslaw
Shredded cabbage, Ground cumin, Carrot; shredded, Light mayonnaise, Coarsely shredded zucchini, Mild picante sauce, Green onions; sliced, Thinly sliced radishes, 
Here is a recipe from Taste of Home that is absolutely delicious.    Drain zucchini by pressing between layers of paper towels. Place in a large  bowl and combine with cabbage, carrot, onions and radishes. In a small  bowl, combine remaining ingredients. Pour over vegetables and toss well.  Cover and chill at least 1 hour. Yield: 8 servings. Posted to KitMailbox  Digest  by vergie1@juno.com (Vergie A Ewing) on Jul 24, 1997
