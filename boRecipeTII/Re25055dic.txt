Tomato Soup Serendipity
Garlic powder, Instant minced onion, Black Pepper, Cold chicken broth, Salt, Paprika, Paprika, Thinly sliced cucumber, Parsley flakes, olive oil, (28-oz) tomatoes, 
Combine tomatoes, broth, minced onion, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and  pepper in container of electric blender. Blend well. Add oil; beat 1 second  longer.  Serve thoroughly chilled, garnished with parsley flakes and sliced  cucumber rolled in paprika. Yield: 4 cups.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
