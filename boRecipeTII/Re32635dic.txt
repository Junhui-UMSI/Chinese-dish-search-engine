Sweet Pea Guacamole
Virgin olive oil, Jalapeno pepper; seeded, -OR, Cilantro, Red onion; finely diced, Frozen peas; defrosted, Fresh lime juice, Ground cumin, Salt, -Serrano peppers, seeded, 
COMBINE OIL, LIME JUICE, cilantro and pepper in a blender or food processor  and blend until cilantro and peppers are roughly pureed. Add peas, cumin  and salt and blend until smooth. There will still be some lumps, but this  adds to the textural interest of the guacamole. Scrape into a mixing bowl  and add the diced red onion.    MICHAEL ROBERTS - PRODIGY GUEST CHEFS COOKBOOK
