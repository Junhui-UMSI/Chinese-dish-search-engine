Apple-Date Cream Cake
Cool whip, Salt, Vanilla, Flour, (21-oz) apple pie filling, Vegetable oil, Cinnamon, Sugar, Baking soda, Allspice, Chopped dates, Eggs; slightly beaten, Pecans, 
Combine first 6 ingredients; stir gently. Combine next 4 ingredients; mix  well and stir into dry ingredients. Stir in dates and nuts. Pour into a  greased and floured 13x9-2-inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.  Cool. Top with Cool Whip or whipped cream.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
