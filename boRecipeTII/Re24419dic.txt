Tropical Drop Candies
Pkg (2 cups) semi-sweet, Chopped banana chips OR, Chopped macadamia nuts, Shredded coconut, Choco chips, Dried pineapple, 
Line cookie sheet w/waxed paper. Melt chocolate chips over low heat;  stirring constantly. Stir in remaining ingredients; blend well. Drop  by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper lined cookie sheet. Refrigerate till  set, about 30 min. Store in airtight container.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
