Thyme and Lemon Crusted Pork Loin
Garlic, Black Pepper, Zest of 1 lemon, Dried thyme, Boneless pork loin, Fresh thyme leaves, Salt, olive oil, 
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Combine lemon zest, thyme, salt, garlic and  pepper in small bowl; stir in olive oil until blended.     Rub the entire surface of the pork to coat with the herb mixture. Place  roast top-side up in small roasting pan. Roast 30 minutes. Turn top side  down; roast 15 minutes. Remove from oven and let stand 15 minutes.     Carefully remove strings and cut into thin slices. Spoon pan juice over  slices. Serves 8.
