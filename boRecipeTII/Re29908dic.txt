Teriyaki Chicken Breasts
Split chicken breasts; about, Teriyaki sauce, Pineapple juice, 
Rinse chicken in cold running water. Drain, and pat dry with paper towel.    Combine teriyaki sauce and pineapple juice.    Spray inside of rotisserie basket with nonstick vegetable cooking spray,  place on spit and fit unit on hub of drip tray. Arrange chicken upright,  meaty side out, around outer edge of rotisserie basket. Brush with sauce.  Close the rotisserie door. Plug in?be certain unit rotates freely. Cook  until juices run clear, approximately 40 to 45 minutes. Brush with sauce  every 15 minutes during cooking. Open rotisserie door. Grasp end of spit  and basket with pot holders and place on a heat proof work surface. Remove  chicken to a warm platter to serve.
