Baked Apples - Crock-Pot
Raisins, Sugar, Apples, to 8, washed/cored, Cinnamon, Butter, 
Mix raisins and sugar; fill center of apples. Sprinkle cinnamon and dot  with butter. Put in Crock-Pot; add 1/2 c water. Cover; cook on Low 7 to 9  hours [High: 2 1/2 to 3 1/2 hours].
