Braised Celery
Unsalted butter, Beef broth, Dijon mustard, Celery; sliced, 
Blanch the celery, drain and cool.    When ready to serve, combine broth, butter, mustard and bring to a simmer.    Add celery and heat through. Salt and pepper to taste.    NOTE:    You may need as much as 3 cups broth for this. Chicken stock works too, or  a combination .
