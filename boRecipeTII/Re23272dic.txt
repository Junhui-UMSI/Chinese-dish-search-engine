Tuna Pitas with Yogurt and Mustard
Pita Breads, Halved, Celery Stalk Diced, In Water, Drained & Flaked, 6 1/2 Oz. Solid White Tuna, Dillweed, Tomatoes Sliced Thickly, Lettuce leaves, Plain Lowfat Yogurt, Coarse Grained Mustard, salt and pepper, Crosswise, 
Combine First Five Ingredients in Medium Bowl. Season With Salt & Pepper.  Line Bread Halves With Lettuce Leaves & Tomatoes. Divide Tuna Mixture Among  Bread Pockets. Serve Immediately.
