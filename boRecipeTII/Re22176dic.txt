Turkey Quiche
Half and half, Poultry seasoning, Swiss Cheese, Mushroom Pieces, Pie Shell, Eggs, Salt, , Mustard, 
Sprinkle turkey, cheese and mushrooms into pie shell. Beat together half  and half, eggs, mustard, salt and seasoning.  Pour over turkey mixture.  Bake on cookie sheet in preheated 375 degree F. oven 35 to 45 minutes or  until knife inserted in center comes out cleand and top is golden brown.  Serve hot.    Nutritional Info per serving: 384 calories; 46 g pro; 3 g car; 20 g fat;      263 mg chol.    Source Womans Day Mag. 11/25/80 - shared by trishexel 10/8/96.
