Pearls and Chocolate Cookies
White chocolate chips, All purpose flour, Baking soda, Sugar, Eggs, Vanilla extract, Cocoa, Butter, Brown sugar, Salt, 
Preheat oven to 350.  Combine cocoa, flour, soda and salt in a small bowl.   Set aside.  In a large bowl, beat butter, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla until creamy.   Add eggs one at a time beating well after each.  Gradually add flour    mixture.  Stir in chips.  Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased    cookie sheets.  Bake 9-10 minutes until edges are lightly browned.  Let stand 2 minutes    before removing from cookie sheets, cool completely.
