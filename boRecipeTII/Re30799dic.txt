Tangerine Tart
Stick unsalted butter;, Graham cracker crumbs, Vanilla, Shaker of powdered sugar, Brandy, Sugar, Cornstarch, Fresh mint sprigs, Spiced whipped cream, Sliced tangerines; rind, 
ESSENCE OF EMERIL SHOW#EE2311    For the crust: In the bottom of a 9-inch pie pan, mix the crumbs and butter  together. Press the mixture into the pie pan to form a crust. Bake for 7 to  8 minutes or until golden. Remove from the oven and cool. For the filling:  In a sauce pan, combine the tangerines, sugar and the vanilla. Stir until  the sugar dissolves. Dissolve the cornstarch and brandy together forming a  slurry. Add the slurry to the tangerine mixture. Cook, stirring  occasionally, until the mixture thickens, about 10 minutes. Pour into the  prepared pie crust and refrigerate for 6 hours, or until the filling sets.  Place a piece of the pie on a plate. Garnish with spiced whipped cream,  fresh mint sprigs, and powdered sugar.    Yield: 1 pie    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 28, 1998
