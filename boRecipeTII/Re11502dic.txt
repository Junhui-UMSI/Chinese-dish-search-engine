Raspberry Cranberry Holiday Ring
Can (8 oz) crushed pineapple, Lemon; sliced for garnish, Lemon zest, (16 oz)  whole cranberry, Raspberries; fresh or frozen, Pkg (3 oz)  raspberry jello, Boiling water, Cranberries; for garnish, Lemon juice, Walnuts, 
Dissolve jello in boiling water in medium bowl.  Stir to dissolve. Drain  pineapple, reserving juice, and set fruit aside.  Stir pineapple juice,    lemon juice, and lemon zest into jello mixture.  Chill til just set (about  one hour)  Stir in Cranberry sauce, raspberries, crushed pineapple, and    walnuts.  Pour into 4 1/2 C. ring mold sprayed with vegatable oil or    lightly    greased.  Chill at least 4 hours or over night.  Unmold and garnish edges  of salad with lemon slices alternating fresh cranberries.
