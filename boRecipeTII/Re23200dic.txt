Tuna Corn Salad
Lemon juice, Frozen whole kernel corn;, Salt, Vegetable oil, Zucchini; peeled and, Chopped parsley, Green onions, Tuna packed in water; (6 1/2, Tomatoes; peeled and chopped, Cider vinegar, Dried leaf basil, TABASCO pepper sauce, 
In large bowl, mix all ingredients. Cover and chill several hours. Serve in  bowl lined with salad greens.
