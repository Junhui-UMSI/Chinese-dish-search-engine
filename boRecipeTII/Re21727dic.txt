Apple Waffles
Flour, Tart apple, Salt, Eggs, Cinnamon, milk, Vanilla, Sugar, butter, baking powder, 
beat eggs until thick. Beat in milk, butter, and vanilla. Sift together dry  ingredients.  Sift into egg mixture & beat well. Add grated apple and stir  until evenly blended.  Bake per your waffle irons directions.
