Toasted Omelette Sandwiches
Eggs, Butter, Vegetable oil, Leg ham, Bread, cheese, Fresh herbs, 
From: becktech@txdirect.net Becky    1.Heat oil in a pan, add half the egg mixture, tilt pan so eggs cover base,  cook until set then remove from pan. Repeat with remaining egg mixture.  Roll up omelettes, cut into 5 millimetre thick slices.    2.Spread butter over toast. Top with ham, omelette spirals and cheese.    3.Cook under a hot grill until cheese is melted.    4.Serve garnished with fresh herbs.    Serves two    Posted to recipelu-digest by jeryder@juno.com on Mar 23, 1998
