Potato Carrot Soup
Cream, Celery salt, Carrots, Potatoes, Cayenne, Salt, Boiling water, water, Onions, 
Dice potatoes, carrots, and onions, and turn into kettle. Add salt and  boiling water. cover and cook gently until tender. (about 15 minutes). Mash  the vegetables thoroughly in their liquid. Combine with cream, heat just to  scalding. Add cayenne and celery salt, reheat and serve at once. Add a dash  of paprika on top of each serving. 5 servings.
