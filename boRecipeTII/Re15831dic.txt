Acacpulco Melt
Chopped; peeled avocado, Kraft real mayonnaise, Soft Parkay margarine, Crisply cooked bacon;, Hard-cooked eggs; chopped, Velveeta Mexican pasteurized, Whole-wheat bread, 
Combine avocado, eggs, bacon and mayonnaise; mix lightly. For each  sandwich, cover one bread slice with Velveeta Mexican cheese spread,  approximately 1/4 cup avocado mixture and second bread slice. Spread  sandwich with margarine. Grill until lightly browned on both sides. 4  sandwiches.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
