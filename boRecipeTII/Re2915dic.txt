Peanut Butter Marble Cake
Flour, milk, Salt, Powdered sugar, Cocoa, Peanut butter, PEANUT BUTTER BUTTERCREAM, Vanilla, Peanut butter, Milk, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Vanilla, Sugar, Eggs, 
Heat oven to 350F. Grease and flour 9x13 inch pan. Beat all ingredients  together on low speed for 30 seconds. Beat on high 3 minutes. Pour 2/3s  (about 3 cups) into pan. Stir cocoa and baking soda into remaining batter.  Drop cocoa batter by generous tablespoons in mounds on top of peanut butter  batter. Pull knife thoruhg in "s" shaped curves to marble. Bake 40-45  minutes or until toothpick test is clean or cake springs back when touched  in the center. Cool. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting, peanut  butter frosting below, or even marble the two together.    Peanut Butter Buttercream Frosting: Mix sugar and peanut butter, stir in  vanilla and milk. Beat until smooth. Frosts a 9x13 inch cake.
