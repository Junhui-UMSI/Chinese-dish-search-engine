All-In-One Quiche
sharp Cheddar cheese, Eggs, bacon, milk, Bisquick mix, Butter, Green Onions, salt, sliced mushrooms , 
Combine the first 5 ingredients in the blender and mix well. Turn into 9 or  10 inch pie plate. Add the remaining ingredients. Bake at 350 degrees for  45 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.    Recipe by: Bisquick Site - Found By Brenda Whimsey Stephens    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Tiina <4Angels@videotron.ca> on Mar 04,  1998
