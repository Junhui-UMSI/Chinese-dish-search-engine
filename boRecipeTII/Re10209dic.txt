Quick and Easy Chocolate Cake
Boiling water, Eggs, Sour Cream, Sugar, Salt, Butter or margarine, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Vanilla extract, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease and flour 13x9x2-inch baking  pan. In large mixer bowl, combine chocolate, butter and water; with spoon,  stir until chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth. Add flour, sugar,  sour cream, eggs, baking soda, salt and vanilla; beat on low speed of  electric mixer until smooth. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake 35 to 40  minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool  completely in pan on wire rack. Frost as desired. 12 to 15 servings.
