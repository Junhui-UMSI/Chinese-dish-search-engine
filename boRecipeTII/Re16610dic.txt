Ajs Southwestern Meatloaf
Plain bread crumbs, Monterey Jack cheese,, Onion, Green pepper, chopped, Black olives, sliced, Eggs, Green chilis, chopped, Lean ground beef, Salt, Frozen whole-kernal corn, Green onions, chopped, Pace Picante Sauce, Chili sauce, Cayenne pepper, 
** I used 1 teaspoon of Schilling Mexican seasoning as a substitution for  the Ajo Picante seasoning -- it tasted just fine. **    Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef,  onion, green pepper, garlic, salt, cayenne pepper, frozen corn, green  onion, Ajo Picante seasoning, bread crumbs, eggs, picante sauce, green  chilies, cheese, olives and chili sauce. Mix well with your hands. Place  into a greased 9- by 5-inch loaf pan and bake for about 1 to 1 1/2 hours,  until meat is no longer pink and juices run clear.    Chef Ernest Muniz Treets Deli, Phoenix, Arizona. (c) Arizona Republic,  6/5/96.    Hunt and pecked by John Blackwell, Taylorsville, Utah,  scooter@xmission.com.
