Anitas "Hot Salsa"
Oregano -- dash, Salt and pepper as you like, Onion -- your choice, Tomatoes, To 4 jalapeno peppers, 
In saucepan boil tomatoes and peppers.  Drain water and remove skin from  tomatoes.  put in blender with remaining ingredients and blend for a minute  or until smooth, unless you prefer your salsa chunky.    Serve with tortilla chips.  Makes great "Juevos Rancheros". Or make  Guacamole by adding salsa to a mashed avocado.    Recipe By     :
