Apricot-Pineapple Noodle Kugel
Lemon Peel, Salt, Boiling water, Eggs, Chopped dried apricots, Medium egg noodles, Sugar, (8 oz.) crushed pineapple, Sour cream, 
In large saucepot, cook noodles; drain. In medium bowl, soak apricots in  boiling water for 15 minutes. Preheat oven to 350. In large bowl, beat eggs  and sugar with electric mixer until pale yellow and thick. Add sour cream  and beat on low speed until smooth. Drain apricots well. Stir in apricots,  pineapple, lemon peel, salt and noodles into egg mixture. Pour into well  greased 13 x 9 inch pan. Bake 30-35 minutes Cut into squares.
