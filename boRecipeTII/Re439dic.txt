Lemon Lime Jello Salad
Lemon jello, Lime jello, Crushed pineapple  , Cottage cheese, Nuts, 
Mix jello according to directions and let partly set up, then add remainder  of the ingredients. Can be topped with whipped cream or mayonnaise and nuts  on top.    Recipe by: Dorothy Frazier Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #517 by  hister@juno.com (Iris E. Dunaway) on Mar 14, 1997
