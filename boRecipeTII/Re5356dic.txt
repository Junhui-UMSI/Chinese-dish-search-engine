Pineapple Au Gratin
Self-rising flour *, Butter flavored crackers;, Unsweetened pineapple, Shredded cheddar cheese; 4, Sugar, Butter or Margarine, 
In a bowl, combine flour and sugar. Drain pineapple, reserving 1 1/2 cup  juice. Add juice to flour mixture; mix well. Stir in pineapple and cheese.  Pour into a greased 13x9" baking pan. Cover and bake at 350 F. for 30  minutes. Uncover and stir; bake 10 minutes longer. Combine melted butter  and cracker crumbs; sprpinkle over pineapple mixture. Return to the oven  for 10-15 minutes or until a knofe inserted near the center comes out  clean. Yield: 16-20 servings. *Editors Note: as a substitutefor each cup of  self rising flour, place 1 1/2 tsp. baking powder and 1/2 tsp. alt in a  measuring cup; add all purpose flour to equal 1 cup.
