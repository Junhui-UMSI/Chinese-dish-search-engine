Vegetable Sauce
Mixed vegetables; cooked, Flour, Salt, Butter or margarine, milk, 
Melt butter.  Stir in flour and salt.  Add milk slowly.  Cook until  thickened, stirring constantly.  Stir in vegetables.  Heat to serving  temperature.
