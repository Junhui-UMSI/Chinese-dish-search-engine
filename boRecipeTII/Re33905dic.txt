Sweet and Spicy Beans And Rice
Vegetable oil, Uncooked instant white rice*, Jars or cans B&M? Original, Old El Paso? Taco Seasoning, Sour cream; if desired, Shredded cheddar cheese (1/2, Old El Paso? Chopped Green, Red onion; chopped, 
*Cooked according to package directions.    1. While rice is cooking, heat oil in medium saucepan over medium-high heat  until hot. Add onion; cook and stir 1 minute. Reduce heat to medium. Add  beans, chiles and taco seasoning mix; mix well. Cook just until mixture  comes to a boil, stirring occasionally.    2. Add cooked rice; mix well. Spoon into serving bowl; serve with sour  cream and cheese. Makes 8 (3/4-cup) servings.    Recipe by: Pillsbury Quick & Easy Bake-Off?
