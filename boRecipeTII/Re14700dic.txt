$100 Chocolate Cake
Miracle Whip light, Cocoa, Vanilla, Flour, Sugar, Cold water, Baking soda, 
Add all ingredients as listed. Beat until well mixed. Bake in greased  9-inch layer cake pans for 30 minutes at 350 degrees, or until cake springs  back when touched.
