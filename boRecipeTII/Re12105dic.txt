Zorros Dip Sauce
olive oil, Red pepper powder, Black pepper, Fresh jalepeno pepper finely, Texas onion, Tomatoes; (14oz.), Garlic, Salt to taste, Tomato paste, Cummin powder, 
Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan and simmer for ten to twelve  minutes. Remove and let cool. Puree in food processor until smooth and  even. Return to sauce pan and let simmer for 15 minutes.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "bunny" <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Mar 1,  1998
