Turkey - Asparagus Casserole
French-Fried Onion Rings, Cream Of Mushroom Soup,, Plus 2 Tbsp Slivered, Cooked Turkey, chopped, Can Cream Of Celery Soup,, Frozen Asparagus Cuts,, Chopped Pimiento, drained, 
Oven 350~.    Combine first 4 ingredients in a bowl; stir well. Spoon half of mixture  into a greased 8-inch square baking dish. Combine asparagus and pimiento;  stir gently. Sprinkle over mixture in baking dish. Spoon remaining turkey  mixture on top. Cover and bake at 350~ for 35 minutes; uncover. Sprinkle  with onion rings, and bake, uncovered, an additional 10 minutes. Yield: 4  servings.    From Helen Goggans of Arkansas, in November, 1986 "Southern Living". Typos  by Jeff Pruett.
