Harvest Pumpkin Bars with Cream Cheese Frosting
Sugar, Soy vegetable oil, Eggs, solid packed pumpkin, Flour, Soy flour, Cinnamon, Salt, Baking soda, Baking Powder, CREAM CHEESE FROSTING, light cream cheese, Butter or margarine, Vanilla, Powdered sugar, pecans, FORMATTED BY TRISH MCKENNA, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray a 10 x 15 x 1-inch jelly roll pan  with non-stick spray. In large mixing bowl, combine sugar, eggs and oil.    Add pumpkin and mix thoroughly. Combine flours and spices and add to the  pumpkin mixture. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.  Cool and frost. Makes 30 bars.      *All-purpose flour can be used for the entire amount of flour called for  in the recipe.     Cream Cheese Frosting    Combine all ingredients and mix until smooth. Sprinkle with pecans, if  desired.       No nutritional information available.       FROM: http://www.agricountry.com/kitchen.htm Formatted to MM online by  Trish McKenna on 7/20/98.
