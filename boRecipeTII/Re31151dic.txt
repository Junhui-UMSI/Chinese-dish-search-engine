Banana Split Sundaes ( Mw )
Light corn syrup, Unsalted, Roasted Peanuts, Vanilla, Sweetened Condensed Milk, Ice Cream, Small Banana, Quartered, Square Semisweet Chocolate, 
Place chocolate in a 2 cup measure.  Micro-cook,  uncovered, on 100% power for 1 1/2 to 2 minutes or  till melted, stirring once.  Stir in corn syrup and  sweetened condensed milk.  Micro-cook, uncovered, on  100% power for 30 to 45 seconds or till heated  through.  Stir in vanilla.  Serve warm atop ice cream.  Sprinkle with peanuts.  Arrange quartered banana  around ice cream.
