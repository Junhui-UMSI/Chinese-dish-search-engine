Tomato Salad with Basil-Buttermilk Dressing
Green onions; thinly sliced, Salt, Fresh basil springs, Bunches fresh basil leaves, Buttermilk, Mayonnaise, Black Pepper, Firm ripe tomatoes, 
Blend fresh basil, buttermilk, sliced green onions and salt in processor  until smooth. Transfer to a bowl. Add mayonnaise and pepper and whisk until  dressing is smooth. Let stand 30 minutes. Season to taste with salt and  pepper. (Can be made 8 hours ahead. Cover tightly and refrigerate.)    Core and slice tomatoes. Arrange on a platter. Spoon enough dressing over  tomatoes to coat.
