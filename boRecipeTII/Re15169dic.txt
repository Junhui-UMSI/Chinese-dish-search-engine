Wheatable Snack Mix
PATTI  VDRJ67A, Garlic salt, Peanuts, Popped popcorn, Onion salt, Margarine, Pretzel sticks, Wheat snack crackers, 
Mix popcorn, pretzels and peanuts in large bowl. Drizzle with margarine.  Stir in crackers. Sprinkle with onion salt and garlic salt; toss. Store in  airtight container. Makes 10 cups.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 655 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 31, 1998
