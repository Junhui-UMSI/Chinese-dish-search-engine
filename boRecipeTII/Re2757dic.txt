Peanut Butter Choco Chip Squares
Eggs, Brown sugar, FLOUR (UNSIFTED), Sugar, Butter, Vanilla, Baking soda, Salt, PEANUT BUTTER CHIPS, Chocolate chips, 
CREAM BUTTER, BROWN SUGAR, SUGAR,EGGS AND VANILLA UNTIL LIGHT AND FLUFFY.  COMBINE FLOUR, BAKING SODA AND SALT; ADD TO CREAMED MIXTURE. COMBINE MILK  CHOCOLATE CHIPS AND PEANUT BUTTER CHIPS; STIR INTO BATTER. SPREAD MIXTURE  EVENLY INTO GREASED 15 X 10 X 1 JELLY ROLL PAN. BAKE AT 350 DEG F FOR 20 TO  25 MINUTES OR UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN. COOL IN PAN. CUT INTO SQUARES WHEN COOL.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #197 by BobbieB1@aol.com on Jul 29, 1997
