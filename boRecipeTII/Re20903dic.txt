Apple Nut Ring 2
Cinnamon, Refrigerator biscuits; (8, Granny Smith apples, Sugar, Stick butter or margarine;, Nuts; chopped, 
Preheat oven to 400F. Separate biscuits into 20 pieces. Combine sugar and  cinnamon. Dip biscuits first into butter then sugar. Place in Deep Dish  Baker. Peel/Core/Slice apples. Place apple slice between each biscuit and  around outer edge. Top with nuts, remaining butter and sugar. Bake for  25-30 minutes.
