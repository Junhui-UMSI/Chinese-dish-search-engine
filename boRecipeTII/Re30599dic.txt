Tahitian Punch
Sliced banana, Sliced peeled papaya,, Sliced peeled mango, chilled, Crushed ice, Carton (8-ounce) plain, Orange juice, chilled, Sliced strawberries, chilled, 
Directions: Place all ingredients in a blender, and process until smooth.  Serve immediately. Yield: 1-1/2 quarts (serving size: 1 cup).
