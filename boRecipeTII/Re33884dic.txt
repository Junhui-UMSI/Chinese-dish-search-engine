Sweet and Sour Tomato Sauce (Mf)
Tomato sauce, Scallions, minced, Salt, to taste, Cider vinegar, Fresh coriander, minced, Sugar, Flour, Red pepper flakes, 
In a saucepan combine vinegar, sugar, tomato sauce, flour, red pepper and  salt. Bring mixture to a boil. Simmer for 2 to 3 minutes, or until slightly  thickened. Transfer to a bowl and stir in scallion and coriander.
