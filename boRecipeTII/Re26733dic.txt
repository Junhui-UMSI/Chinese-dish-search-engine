Bake Cherry Cheese Pie
9" graham cracker crust, 14 oz. sweetened condensed;, Milk ( not evaporated), Cherry pie filling, Vanilla extract, Concentrated lemon juice, 8 oz softened cream cheese, 
In a large mixer bowl,beat cheese until fluffy. Beat in Condensed milk  until smooth. Stir in lemon juice and vanilla. Pour into crust. Chill 3  hours or until set. Top with desired amount of e filling before serving.  Refrigerate leftovers.
