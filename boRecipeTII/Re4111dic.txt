Peppers and Pasta
Onions, (14 oz) tomatoes; chopped, Grated Parmesan cheese, olive oil, Fresh basil leaves; chopped, Chopped fresh thyme or 1/4, Garlic, Penne pasta, Chopped fresh rosemary or, Tomatoes; chopped OR, Red and yellow bell peppers;, 
Cook pasta as directed on package. Drain well.    Meanwhile, heat oil in a large nonstick skillet. Add peppers, tomatoes,  onion, garlic, rosemary, thyme and half the basil. Saute 8-10 minutes until  vegetables are tender. Toss with pasta. Sprinkle with remaining basil and  Parmesan cheese.
