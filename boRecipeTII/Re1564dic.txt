Buttercream Icing
Water, Confectioner's sugar, Shortening, Clear vanilla extract, Butter extract, 
Start with 4 cups of sugar, alternately add shortening and remaining sugar  until well blended.  Add flavorings.  Beat at low speed until smooth.    NOTE:  This icing can be stored for up to 2 weeks in an airtight container  in the refrigerator.
