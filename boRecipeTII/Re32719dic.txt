Sweet Potato Casserole
Flour, Pecans, Eggs, Salt, Vanilla, Cooked mashed sweet potatoes, Margarine, Brown sugar, Softened butter, Sugar, 
Mix first 6 ingredients to-gether. Put in 2 qt. casserole. Melt butter or  margarine in small saucepan, add brown sugar, flour and nuts. Stir well &  msprinkle on top of potatoes. Bake uncovered 30 min. in 350 oven. You may  add 1/2 tsp. each of cinnamon and nutmeg to first 6 ingredients. Hot or  cold you will think you are eating dessert. Delicious!
