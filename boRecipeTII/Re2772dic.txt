Peanut Butter Chocolate Delight
(12-ounce) frozen whipped, (3.9-ounce) chocolate, Butter or margarine, melted, Finely chopped roasted, (3.4-ounce) vanilla instant, milk, Sifted powdered sugar, All-purpose flour, Crunchy peanut butter, (8-ounce) cream cheese,, 
Combine first 3 ingredients; press into a 13-x 9-inch pan. Bake at 375? for  15 minutes; cool. Combine cream cheese, 1 1/2 cups whipped topping, sugar,  and peanut butter, stirring until blended. Spread over prepared crust, and  chill. Beat pudding mixes and milk at medium speed with an electric mixer 2  minutes. Spread over cream cheese layer. Spread remaining whipped topping  over pudding layer, and chill. Makes about 15 servings.    Bonus: For Chocolate Delight, omit peanut butter, and substitute chopped  pecans for peanuts.
