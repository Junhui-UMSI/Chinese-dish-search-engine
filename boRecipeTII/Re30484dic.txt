Banana Nut Bread #1
Salt, Sugar, Mashed ripe bananas, Flour, Eggs, Baking soda, Pecans, Shortening, Baking Powder, 
Cream sugar and shortening. Add eggs and beat well. Add sifted dry  ingredients with nuts. Add bananas. Pour into a well-greased loaf pan. Bake  at 350 for 45-50 minutes.
