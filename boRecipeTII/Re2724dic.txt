Peanut Butter Brownie Cups
Betty Crocker Fudge, Up to 18 Reeses Mini, 
Make brownie mix per directions.  Fill paper-lined muffin cups 2/3 full.  Unwrap peanutbutter cups & press into the center of each muffin until the  batter meets the top edge of the candy. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 25  minutes.  Makes 15 to 18.    ~--
