Baked Yams with Lime and Honey
Water, Honey, Unsalted butter, Sour cream, Salt, Black Pepper, Yams, juice of 4 limes, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.    Wash the yams and place in a baking dish with the water. Bake until the  potatoes are soft and the skins puffy, about 1 1/2 hours. Set aside to cool  slightly (leave the oven on).    Peel the yams and place in a medium baking dish. Add the honey, butter,  lime juice, salt and pepper. Stir and mash well with a potato masher. Cover  with aluminum foil and return to the oven for 15 to 20 minutes, until  heated through. Sprinkle the top with sour cream and serve hot.
