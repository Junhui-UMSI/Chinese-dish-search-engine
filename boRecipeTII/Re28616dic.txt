Texas Blue Cheese Slaw
Blue cheese, green bell pepper, Herbal salt substitute and pepper, Honey, White wine vinegar, fresh dill, green onions, Sour cream, plain yogurt, nonfat, savoy cabbage, 
1. In a large bowl combine cabbage, onions, bell pepper, and dill.    2. Blend together yogurt, sour cream, vinegar, honey, and blue cheese. Toss  dressing with cabbage mixture. Add salt substitute and pepper to taste.
