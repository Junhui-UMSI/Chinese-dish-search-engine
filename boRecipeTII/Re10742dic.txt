Quick Tomato Salsa
Plum tomatoes; chopped, Jalapeno; seeded and chopped, Red onion; chopped, Cilantro; chopped, Salt and pepper, Juice of one lime, olive oil, 
In a medium bowl combine tomatoes, onion, jalapeno and cilantro. Stir in  lime juice and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.
