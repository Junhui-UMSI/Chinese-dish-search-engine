24 Hour Cole Slaw
Mustard seed, Cabbage, Celery seed, Onions, Sugar, Salt, Salad oil, Vinegar, 
Chop cabbage and onion and toss together. Heat oil, vinegar, salt, sugar,  seeds. Bring to boil. Pour over cabbage - onion mixture. Chill 24 hours.  Stir and serve. busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 18, 1998
