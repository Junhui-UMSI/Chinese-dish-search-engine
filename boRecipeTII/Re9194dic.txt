Prune Danish
Recipe Danish Pastry Dough, (12 ounce) prune filling, or, Corn syrup, Egg, slightly beaten, 
Roll pastry on floured surface to 2 20" x 15" rectangles. Trim edges  evenly. With a sharp knife cut into 12 5" squares. Spoon a rounded  tablepoon prune filling onto center of each square; bring 2 opposite  corners over filling to overlap about 1 inch. Placwe on cookie sheets 2  inches apart. Let rise in a warm place until double in bulk, about 30  minutes. Brush with beaten egg. Place in hot oven (400F); lower heat  immediately to 350F, then bake for 20 minutes. Warm corn syrup slightly in  small saucepan; brush over pastries; bake 5 minutes more. Remove to wire  rack; cool. You may substitute cherry or apple pie filling, or apricot  preserves for the prune filling.
