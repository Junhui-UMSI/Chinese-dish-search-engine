Pickled Beets
Horseradish, grated, Lemon juice, Beets, cooked and sliced, Water, Sugar, Salt, 
Place sliced beets in a glass bowl or jar.    In saucepan, combine water, lemon juice, sugar and salt. Bring to a boil;  stir in horseradish. Pour over beets. Refrigerate overnight.    Source: Torah Prep High School for Girls Passover booklet. 1962    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #122 by Tzimmi@aol.com on Apr 16, 1997
