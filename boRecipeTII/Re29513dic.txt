Tarragon Trout
olive oil, Dijon mustard, Water, Tarragon leaves, Salt, 8-10 oz. trout; with heads o, Black pepper, White vinegar, Shallots; minced, 
Combine tarragon leaves and vinegar in a saucepan and bring to a boil.  Cool, and add the remaining ingredients.  Pour the marinade over the trout.  Refrigerate for 3-4 hours.  Cook the trout on a charcoal grill for 3 to 4  minutes brushing occasionally with marinade.    Serves 6.    .    Recipes sent to me from Bill, wight@odc.net
