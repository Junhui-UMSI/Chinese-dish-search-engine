Ponzu Sauce for Dipping
Mirin, rice, Dark soy sauce, Tamari sauce, giant kelp, Dried bonito flakes, Juice of 4 lemons, 
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and let stand 24 hours. Strain through a  cheesecloth and mature 3 months in a cool, dark place, or in the  refrigerator.    Yield: 2 1/2 cups    c.1997, M.S.
