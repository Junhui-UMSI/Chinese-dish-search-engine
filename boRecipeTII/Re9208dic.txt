Prune Pudding
Eggs, well beaten, Sugar, Cinnamon, milk, Dry bread crumbs, Baking-powder, Thick prune pulp, Cloves, Flour, Ground suet, Salt, 
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking-powder, salt, and spices. Prepare  prune pulp by rubbing cooked prunes through sieve. Combine prune pulp,  crumbs, suet, sugar, milk, and eggs. Add dry ingredients. Mix thoroughly.  Pour into well-oiled 1 pound cans. Cover. Steam 2 hours. Serve hot with  hard sauce or with caramel sauce. 8 servings.    Grace Viall Gray, Glen Ellyn, IL.
