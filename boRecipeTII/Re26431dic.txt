Tomato Florentine Soup
tomato puree  , Dried parsley flakes, Quick cooking barley, Onions, carrots, Celery, Water, parmesan cheese, frozen spinach, Dried Oregano, bow tie pasta, Chicken broth, Dry white wine, Salt, Dried basil, Brown Sugar, Garlic, Dried  red pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, olive oil, 
Saute carrot, celery, onion and garlic in oil in a large Dutch oven over  medium heat until tender. Add tomato puree & next 14 ingredients; stir  well. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat and simmer one hour and 25  minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in pasta and barley. Simmer uncovered,  20-30 minutes, or until pasta and barley are tender. To serve, ladle soup  into individual bowls and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
