Baked Applesauce
Water, Nutmeg, Apple juice, Cooking apples; cut in 1", Te cinnamon, Vanilla, 
Wash, peel and core apples.  Cut in 1 " cubes.  Combine all ingredients.  Place in a glass loaf dish (apple stay moist in a loaf pan) and pour juice  mixture over the top. Bake, uncovered, in a 325 F preheated oven for 45  minute.  Remove from oven and cool. Store in the refrigerator tightly  covered.  May be frozen. Makes 2 cups.
