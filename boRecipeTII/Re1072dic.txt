Corn Bread
All-Purpose Flour -- sifted, Cornmeal, (Stone-Ground Is, Preferable), Baking Powder, Salt, Sugar, Egg -- well-beaten, Milk Or Buttermilk, Melted Margarine Or Veg Oil, 
Sift together the flour, cornmeal, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.  Combine the sugar, egg, milk, and margarine or oil. Add to the dry  ingredients, stirring just enough to moisten the dry ingredients. Place in  a greased 8 X 8" pan and bake in a 400 F oven about 20 mins, or until  golden. Serve hot with butter and apple butter. You can also make a dozen  muffins or corn sticks. Test after 15 mins.    from my kitchen to------------------------------->yours.....
