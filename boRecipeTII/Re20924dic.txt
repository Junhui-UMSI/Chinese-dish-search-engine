Apple Oatmeal Raisin Bread
Yeast, Oat bran, Apple juice, Oats, Bread flour, Nuts -- chopped, Brown sugar, Raisins, Cinnamon, Apples -- diced, Salt, Butter, 
Add all ingredients but last 3 to bread machine and turn on. Add raisins,  apples and nuts at the beep.    Recipe By     : "The Bread Machine Cookbook," Donna German    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #222 by Suzy <suzy@bestweb.net> on Aug 22,  1997
