Bakers Chocolatey Banana Muffins
Flour, Cinnamon, Salt, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Baking soda, Eggs, Bananas, Bakers Chocolate Chips, 
Preheat oven to 350. In a mixing bowl, whisk together oil, sugar and eggs;  stir in bananas and chocolate chips. Combine flour, soda, salt and  cinnamon; stir into mixture just to moisten. Spoon into 24 greased muffin  cups. Bake 15 - 20 minutes.    NOTES : 1 cup chopped nuts can be added to the batter.
