Beef n Noodles in Sour Cream
Noodles, Ground beef, Onions, Sour cream, Salt, Pepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Fat, Tomato juice, Celery salt, 
Cook onion in hot fat until tender but not brown. Add meat; brown lightly.  Place noodles in layer over meat. Combine remaining ingredients except sour  cream; pour over noodles to moisten all. Bring to boiling; cover, and  simmer over low heat 30 minutes, or until noodles are tender. Stir in sour  cream, heat just to boiling. Season to taste. Garnish with onion rings, if  desired. Makes 6 servings. Submitted by Suzy Lewis    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest by Suzy-Q Lewis <lewises@flatoday.infi.net>  on Feb 10, 1998
