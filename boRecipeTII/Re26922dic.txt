Baked Bulgur with Pecans
Bulgur wheat, Boiling water, Dried basil, Salt, Pepper, Pecans, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    Lightly oil a 1 quart baking dish or spray with a nonstick cooking spray.  Place bulgur, basil, salt and pepper in prepared baking dish. Add boiling  water and mix well.    Cover tightly and bake 20 minutes.     Fluff with a fork, add pecans, and mix well.     Serve hot.
