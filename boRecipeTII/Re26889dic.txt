Baked Beef Stew
Salt, Lean beef stew meat; cut in, Pepper, Water, Potatoes; peeled and, Quick-cooking tapioca, Onion; cut in chunks, (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes;, Celery ribs; cut in 3/4", Carrots; cut in 1 in chunks, Sugar, Bread; cubed, 
In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, water, tapioca, sugar, salt and  pepper. Add remaining ingredients; mix well. Pour into a greased  13x9x2-inch or 3-qt. baking dish. Cover and bake at 375? for 1 3/4 to 2  hours or until meat and vegetables are tender. Serve in bowls.
