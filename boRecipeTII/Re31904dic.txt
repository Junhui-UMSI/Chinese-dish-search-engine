Tabbouli Salad
Green onions & tops, minced, Mint, dry crumbled, Salt, Parsley, chopped, Lemon juice, Tomatoes, chopped, Pepper, Bulgur wheat, uncooked, Vegetable oil, Boiling water, Mint, fresh chopped or,, 
Pour boiling water over wheat in a bowl.  Let stand one hour. Drain well  and return to bowl.  Add remaining ingredients and blend well. Chill at  least two hours. Serve on a bed of lettuce.
