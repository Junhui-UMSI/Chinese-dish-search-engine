Pork Chop Supreme
(19-oz) whole tomatoes, Onion; thin, Salt, -(up to), Uncooked rice (not instant, Lean pork chops; 1-inch, Black Pepper, 
Trim off excess fat. Season chops. Brown in lightly greased skillet. Top  each chop with slice of onion, 1 tablespoon rice & top with a whole tomato.  Add any remaining tomatoes & juice to skillet. Season with salt & pepper.  Cover tightly & simmer in skillet 1-1/2 hours or put in covered baking dish  & bake at 350 for 1-1/2 hours. Yields 4 servings.
