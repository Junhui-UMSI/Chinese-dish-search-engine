Amelia Bedelias Sweet and Sour Carrots
Vinegar, Cornstarch, Margarine, Sugar, Cooked carrot slices;, 
Place carrots in buttered casserole. Mix sugar, vinegar and margarine. Heat  until it begins to boil. Dissolve cornstarch in water and stir into the  first mixture. Pour this sauce over the carrots in the casserole dish. Bake  at 400 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes. Serves 4 to 6.
