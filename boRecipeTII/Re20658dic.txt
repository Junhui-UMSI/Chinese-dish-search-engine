Recipe by: Betty Crocker Preparation Time: 0:35 Heat oven to 375. Arrange  apples in greased square 8x8x2 pan.  Mix remaini ingredients; sprinkle over  apples.  Bake untl topping is golde brown and apples are tender, about 30  minutes.
