Thai Dip
Cherry tomatoes, cut in half, Ground pork, Fish sauce, Vegetable oil, Red curry paste, Palm suger, Dried new mexico chili, *, 
* soften in cold water and minced    1. Stir-fry oil, red curry paste and chili until fragent.    2. Add pork and stir-fry on high heat until cooked.    3. Add fish sauce and sugar then tomatoes and bring to boil.    4. Reduce heat to medium and cook 5 minutes.    NOTES : This dish goes well with your favorite vegetable dish, such as  lettuce or cabbage leaves.
