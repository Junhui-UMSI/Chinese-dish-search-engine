Taos Honey Lemon Dressing
Unseasoned rice vinegar, Pure red chile powder, Lemon, Honey, Peanut oil, 
Zest the lemon, being careful not to include any of the bitter white part.  Finely mince the zest. Squeeze the lemon juice into a bowl, and add the  zest  and remaining ingredients.
