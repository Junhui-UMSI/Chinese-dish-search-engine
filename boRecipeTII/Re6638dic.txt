Pol Sambol (Coconut Sambol)
Onion, Salt, Lime juice, Coconut, Chilli powder, Paprika, 
Grate  the  coconut  and  chop  the onion.  Place the coconut in a bowl and  add the onion, chilli powder salt, lime juice and paprika powder. Mix well.    Note : If fresh  coconut  is  not  available,  mix dessicated coconut with  3 tsps milk and use as above. From "A taste of Sri Lanka" by Indra  Jayasekera, ISBN #962 224 010 0
