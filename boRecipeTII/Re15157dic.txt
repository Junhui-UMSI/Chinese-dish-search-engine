Wheat Waffles
Egg, milk, Vegetable oil, Orange juice, Whole wheat flour, Sugar, Grated orange peel, Baking Powder, Salt, 
In a small mixing bowl, beat the egg white until stiff peaks form; set  aside. In another small mixing bowl, beat egg yolk, milk, oil and orange  juice. Combine flour, sugar, orange peel, baking powder and salt; stir into  milk mixture. Fold in egg white. Bake in a preheated waffle iron according  to manufacturers directions until golden brown.
