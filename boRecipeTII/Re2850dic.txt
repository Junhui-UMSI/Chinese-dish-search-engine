Peanut Butter Dressing
Peanut Butter; Chunky, Dark Corn Syrup; OR, milk, Honey, 
Stir the peanut butter and syrup together in a small bowl until well  blended.  Add the milk and then cover.  Refrigerate until serving time.  Serve over fresh fruit and fruit salads.    Makes about 1 1/3 cups.    "More Recipes from the Backs of Boxes, Bottles, Cans & Jars", by Ceil Dyer.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/sauces1.zip
