Achiote Sauce
Unsalted butter, Cracked black pepper, Chicken Stock, Onion,; roughly chopped, Salt, White vinegar, Tomato paste, Achiote paste,; crumbled, Garlic cloves,; minced, 
In a medium saucepan, over medium-low heat melt the butter. Cook the onions  until soft and translucent, 12 to 15 minutes. Stir in the garlic and tomato  and Achiote paste and cook an additional 3 to 5 minutes, stirring  frequently. Add the chicken stock, salt and black pepper. Bring to a boil,  reduce to a simmer and cook, stirring and skimming frequently, 12 to 15  minutes. Add the vinegar and cook at end for 2 to 3 minutes. Puree in a  blender or food processor. Store in refrigerator 2 to 3 days.
