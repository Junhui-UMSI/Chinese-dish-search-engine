Applesauce Oatmeal Muffins
Egg, Flour, Salt, Applesauce, Brown sugar, Nutmeg, Vegetable oil, Rolled oats, Cinnamon, Baking Powder, milk, 
Preheat oven to 375F, prepare pans. Mix dry ingredients well with a fork.  Beat egg, then add oil & milk, stir in applesauce. Add the dry mix to the  wet mix until just combined. Spoon into pans & bake for 20 mins or until  cooked.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
