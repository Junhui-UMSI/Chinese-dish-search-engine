Amish Vanilla Pie
Granulated sugar, Molasses, Brown sugar, Baking soda, Lard, Water, Flour, Vanilla, CRUMBS, Egg, Flour, unbaked pie shell, Brown sugar, Baking Powder, 
Combine all the above ingredients, except the vanilla, in a saucepan. Bring  to a boil and continue boiling until thickened. Allow to cool and stir in  vanilla. Pour into unbaked shell.    CRUMBS Melt shortening. Stir in dry ingredients. Crumble mixture over pie  shell.   Bake at 375 degrees F. for 50 to 60 minutes.    Source: The Best Of Amish Cooking by Phyllis Pellman Good Submitted by  Michael Hatala, Prodigy F&W Board
