Pineapple Muffins
Whole-grain wheat flour, Ground allspice, Baking Powder, Well mixed in:, Crushed pineapple in juice, Substitute, Egg Beaters? 99% egg, Molasses, Honey, Sunflower seeds, Water, Ground cinnamon, Applesauce, 
Combine the dry ingredients together in a large bowl. Combine wet  ingredients in a separate bowl. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients.  Stir to mix. Spoon into muffin cups (use non-stick pan, if possible). Fill  cups only to about 3/4 full. Bake at 375 degrees for 15-20 minutes.    HELPFUL HINTS: Do not use paper muffin cups--since there is no oil in these  muffins, they will stick to the papers.
