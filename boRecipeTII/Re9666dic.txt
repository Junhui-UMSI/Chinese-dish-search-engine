Pumpkin Nut Bread
Flour, Pumpkin, Pecans, Sugar (or 2 cups, works, Eggs, Ground cinnamon, Water, Salt, Baking soda, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Sift dry ingredients into a large bowl.  Make a well and add sugar, eggs, pumpkin and water. Beat well until smooth.  Add nuts, and mix into mixture. Pour into three greased and floured loaf  pans. Bake at 350 degrees F for one hour.    These freeze very well.    From:  Betty Schutz Posted by: Karin Brewer, Cooking Echo, 7/92
