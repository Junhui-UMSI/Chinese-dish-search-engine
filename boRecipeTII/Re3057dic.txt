Peanut Clusters Crock Pot
Almond bark; (16 sq.), Chocolate chips; (12 oz.), Unsweetened chocolate; (1, Dry roasted peanuts, 
Melt almond bark, chocolate chips and unsweetened chocolate in crock-pot,  stirring occasionally. Add peanuts and drop by iced teaspoon on waxed  paper. As you drop them, the peanuts will go to the bottom, so stir up  occasionally. (Pecans may be used instead of peanuts.)
