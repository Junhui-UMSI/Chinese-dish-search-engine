Beef Chilly Fry
Salt to taste, Tamarind concentrate, 1-inch piece ginger, Salt, Green chillies, Onions, Corriander leaves, Beef cubed (whichever you, Peppercorns, Mint leaves, Garlic, salt and pepper, 
Boil the meat, peppercorns, one onion and salt for 20 minutes, drain and  reserve the water.  Slice the remaining onions and finely chop the ginger,  garlic, chillies, corriander and mint.  Fry the onions until translucent.  Add the remaining chopped ingredients and meat. Pour in about 1 cup of the  reserved water and simmer until the meat is done.  Add the tamarind and  continue cooking, uncovered over a high heat until the moisture has  evaporated.
