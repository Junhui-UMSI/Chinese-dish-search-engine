Texas Golden Punch
(46-oz) apple juice, Bottles ginger ale, (12-oz) frozen orange juice, (12-oz) frozen lemonade, (46-oz) pineapple juice, 
Combine orange juice & lemonade using only half as much water as directed  on the cans. Add pineapple juice & apple juice. Mix well & freeze. Remove  from freezer 3 hours before serving. It should be slushy when ready to  serve. Add ginger ale. Serves about 125.    SUE CARTER    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
