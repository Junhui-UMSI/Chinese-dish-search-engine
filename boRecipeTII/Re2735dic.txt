Peanut Butter Candy Bars
Powdered sugar, Honey graham cereal, Creamy peanut butter, Margarine, Milk chocolate chips, Shortening, 
Mix peanut butter and margarine; stir in cereal. Mix in powdered sugar, 1/3  at a time. Press mixture firmly in ungreased square pan, 9 x 9 x 2-inches.  Heat shortening in 1 quart saucepan over low heat until melted. Add  chocolate chips. Heat over very low heat, stirring constantly, until  chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth. Spread chocolate mixture over  cereal mixture. Refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour. Remove from  refrigerator 10 minutes before serving. Cut into bars, about 2 1/4 x 1  1/2-inches. Refrigerate only remaining bars. Yield: 24 bars.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 315 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 27, 1997
