Vegetable and Herb Quiche
(Vegetable and Herb), milk, Grated cheese, Sliced fresh mushrooms, Onion; thinly sliced, Box Quick Brown Rice, Eggs, 
Cook contents of box according to package directions.  Preheat oven to  350 F.  In a large bowl, beat the eggs and milk and stir in the remaining  ingredients.  When the rice is done, add it to the egg mixture.  Stir.  Pour the entire mixture into an oiled quiche pan or 8" x 8" baking dish.  Bake 45 minutes or until done.  Serve immediately.
