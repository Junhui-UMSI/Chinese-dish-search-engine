Barbara Fireys Cheese Bread
Butter or margarine, Loaf French bread, (heaping) finely chopped, Miracle Whip, Sharp Cheddar cheese; grated, Mayonnaise, 
Preheat oven to 400?. Split the loaf of bread lengthwise and spread with  the softened butter. Combine cheese, mayonnaise, Miracle Whip and onions.  Spread on the buttered bread. Place on a cookie sheet and bake for 15 to 20  minutes until the cheese begins to bubble. Do not let it brown! Slice each  half into 8 pieces. Yield: 8 servings.    JANE MILLER (MRS. G. R.)
