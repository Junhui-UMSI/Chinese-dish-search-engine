Amish Sugar Cookies
Egg; (large), Powdered Sugar;, Vegetable oil, Cream of tartar;, Water, Salt, All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Sugar;, Margerine; (1/2 stick), Vanilla, Lemon or alomond flavoring, 
Place sugars, margarine and oil in a mixer bowl and mix at medium  speed until creamy.  Add egg, banilla, flavoring and water, and mix  at medium speed for 30 seconds, scraping down the bowl before and  after adding these ingredients. Stir remaining ingredients together  to blend well; add to creamy mixture and mix at medium speed to  blend. Form dough into 24 balls using 1 tablespoon dough per ball.  Place balls on cookie sheets that have been sprayed with pan spray or  lined with aluminum foil. Press balls down evenly to 1/2 with the  back of a tablespoon dipped in water. Bake at 375 for 12 to 14  minutes, or until cookies are browned on the bottom and lightly  browned around the edges. Remove cookies to a wire rack and cool to  room temperature.
