Bruces Sweet Potato Pie
Egg beaters or other, Lite Evaporated Skimmed Milk, Unbaked 9" deep dish pie, 
1. Preheat oven to 425 F    2. Beat egg replacers lightly in a large bowl, stir in sweet potato pie  filling and lite skimmed evaporated milk    3. Blend well and pour into pie shell(s)    4. Bake for 15 minutes at 425 F. Reduce heat to 350 F and bake for 50 to 60  minutes    Posted to fatfree digest by "Donald E. Stowe" <destowe@pipeline.com> on Dec  3, 1998,
