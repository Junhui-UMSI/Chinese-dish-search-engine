Baked Vidalia Onions, Sherry Cream Sauce
Sherry, Pre-cooked Vidalia onions, Jar (small) mushrooms;, Light cream, Salt, Butter, Pepper, Grated sharp Cheddar cheese, Pimento, 
Drain onions, arrnage in a shallow baking dish. Combine sherry, cream,  salt, pepper, pimento and mushrooms. Pour over onions, dot with butter.  Sprinkle with grated cheese, cover and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.  You may substitute sour cream for light cream, if desired.
