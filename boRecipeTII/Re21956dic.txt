Applesauce Cake #1
Cocoa, Chopped pecans (other fruits, Baking soda, Cloves, Applesauce, Allspice, Shortening, Raisins, Flour, Nutmeg, Chopped dates, Eggs, Cinnamon, Salt, Sugar, 
Sift flour, sugar, soda, salt, cocoa, and spices into mixing bowl. Drop in  shortening. Add applesauce and eggs. Add fruit and nuts. Mix thoroughly.  Pour into greased 10-inch tube pan. Sprinkle with topping. Bake at 250 for  1 hour. Topping: 1/4 cup chopped nuts, 2 Tbs sugar.
