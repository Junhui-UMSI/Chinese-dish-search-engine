Aberffraw Cakes
Flour, Castor sugar, Butter, 
TEISENNAU ABERFFRO Melt the butter in a basin and beat in the sugar.  Gradually add the flour, mixing well with a warm spoon, Roll out thinly on  a floured board and cut into rounds. Cook in a moderate oven (375/F. or  Mark 5)  for 15 minutes or until golden-brown. Serve sprinkled with sugar  and with whipped cream and raspberry jam.
