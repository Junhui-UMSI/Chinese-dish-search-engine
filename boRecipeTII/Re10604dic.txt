Quick Peanut Butter-Chocolate Swirl Fudge
Butter, Semisweet chocolate chips, peanut butter chips, sweetened condensed, 
Line an 8-inch square pan with foil; grease foil. Stir milk and butter in a  medium saucepan over low heat until butter melts and mixture is smooth. Put  1/3 cup into a small bowl. Add chocolate chips and stir until melted and  smooth. Add peanut butter chips to mixture in saucepan; stir until melted  and smooth, returning saucepan to heat if needed.    Spread saucepan mixture in lined pan. Drop teaspoonfuls of chocolate  mixture over the top. Using a small rubber spatula, lift some of the  mixture from bottom of the pan over the chocolate mixture, creating a  decorative pattern. Refrigerate 3 hours or until firm. Invert pan, peel off  foil, invert fudge and cut in 1-inch squares.    Recipe by: Womans Day - 1/6/98    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #971 by Carol Taillon <taillon@earthlink.net>  on Dec 25, 1997
