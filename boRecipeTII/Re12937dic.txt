Zesty Cod Fillets
Cod, whole tomatoes , Salt, Black pepper, Garlic, Chicken broth, Fresh spinach, Onion, Dried thyme, Dry white wine, olive oil, 
Heat oven to 350.    Heat oil in nonstick skillet. Add onion and garlic; cook until onion is  very soft, 5 minutes. Add tomatoes, broth, wine, thyme, 1/4 teaspoon salt,  an 1/4 teaspoon pepper, simmer, uncovered, until sauce is reduced and  thickened slightly, 5 minutes.    Season fish with remaining salt and pepper. Place in 13x9 inch baking pan.  Add sauce; cover.    Bake in 350 oven 14 minutes. Add spinach to sauce around fish. Bake 5  minutes or until fish flakes.    Recipe by: Unknown Cooking Magazine Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #684 by  L979@aol.com on Jul 21, 1997
