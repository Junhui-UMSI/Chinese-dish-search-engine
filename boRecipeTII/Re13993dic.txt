White Chocolate Chip Cookies
Margarine, Vanilla, Brown sugar, Skim milk, Egg whites, Salt, Granulated sugar, Baking soda, Unbleached flour, Vanilla chocolate chips, Margarine, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare baking sheets with cooking spray.  In a  mixing bowl, combine margarine, milk, and egg whites.  In another mixing  bowl, combine flour, sugars, baking soda, salt, and chocolate chips.  Mix  wet ingredients with dry ingredients just until moistened.  Drop  by  rounded tablespoonfuls onto prepared baking sheets.  Bake for 12 minutes.
