Zucchini Parmesan
Pepper, Oregano, Onion, Parsley, chopped, olive oil, Salt, Zucchini, medium sliced, Rosemary, Cheese, grated, Tomatoes, peeled, Garlicclove, large, 
Heat oil in large skillet. Add zucchini, onion, parsley, garlic, salt,  pepper, oregano and rosemary. Saute mixture over medium heat, stirring  often, until zucchini is tender (20 minutes). Toss in chopped tomatoes and  continue to saute until tomatoes are thoroughly heated (5 minutes). Turn  mixture into a serving dish; Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
