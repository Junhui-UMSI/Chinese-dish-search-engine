Tatar
Salt and pepper to taste, Lettuce & sliced pickle, Finely chopped small onion, Egg yolk, Ground premium beef; very, 
Mix beef, egg yolk, onion, salt & pepper. Serve on lettuce topped with  pickle. Very good on buttered Swedish rye bread with a beer. Variation: Add  cayenne pepper, paprika & 2 Tablespoons Worcestershire sauce.
