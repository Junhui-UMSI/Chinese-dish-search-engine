Asparagus with Lemon Grass and Shallot Vinaigrette
Shallots; minced, Pepper, Lemon grass; minced, Mustard, Dijon, Vegetable oil, olive oil, Red wine vinegar, Salt, Asparagus stalks, 
Peel the asparagus if necessary and cook the stalks in lightly salted water  for 4 to 8 minutes, depending on the thickness, or until fork tender but  still firm. Drain under cold water and set the stalks aside in serving  dish. Combine the remaining ingredients and whisk them vigorously. Taste  for seasoning and add more vinegar or salt and pepper if needed. Pour the  dressing over the asparagus and let it rest for at least 15 minutes before  serving. Serve at room temperature.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdjaxxx.zip
