Peppery Grilled Potatoes
Pepper, Olive oil spray, Salt, Sweet potatoes, Baking potatoes, 
1. Prepare grill or heat oven to 425.    2. Peel baking and sweet potatoes; cut into 3/4-inch-thick wedges. Toss    baking potatoes with 1/2 teaspoon each salt and pepper in large bowl. Coat  with cooking spray.    3. Place baking potatoes on sheet of heavy-duty foil or meshed grill rack  placed directly on top of grill rack.    4. Grill or roast 20 minutes, turning.    5. Meanwhile, combine sweet potatoes, remaining 1/2 teaspoon each salt and  pepper in same bowl. Coat with spray. Add to baking potatoes. Grill or  roast 20 minutes longer or until fork-tender.    NOTES : Olive-oil cooking spray makes this fat free.
