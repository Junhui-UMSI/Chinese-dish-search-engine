Tomato and Herb Salad
Pepper;, Non-nutritive sweetener, Tomatoes; peeled, Oregano;, Seasoned salt;, Malt vinegar;, Lettuce leaves; torn up, 
Chop 1 1/2 tomatoes fine; comine with oregano, sweetener, vinegar and salt.  Slice remaining tomatoes, place on top of lettuce leaves; top with vinegar  mix; refregerate until ready to serve.
