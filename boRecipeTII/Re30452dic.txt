Banana Liqueur
Sugar, Vanilla Or One 2" Vanilla, Bananas; Peeled, Vodka, 
1. Mash the bananas and add the vanilla and cooled sugar syrup to the  vodka.    2. shake gently. Steep 1 week. Strain and filter. Et may be consumed now,  but a 2 to 3 month maturing period will result in a richer flavor. Makes 4  cups.    One of the nicest gifts you can give anytime!
