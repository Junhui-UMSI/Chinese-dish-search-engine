Pumpkin Pieless
Nutmeg, (16 ounces) pumpkin, Baking soda, Cinnamon, Vanilla, Whole wheat flour, Egg whites, (13 ounces) evaporated, Granulated sugar, Allspice, Baking Powder, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 10 x 1/2 inch pie plate with nonstick  coating. Combine all ingredients, except egg whites. Blend until smooth.  Fold in egg whites. Pour into pie plate. Bake 45-50 minutes or until a  knife inserted in center comes out clean.    Posted to fatfree digest V97 #274 by kalvisc@juno.com (K A Cannon) on Nov  23, 1997
