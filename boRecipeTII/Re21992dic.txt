Turkey Burritos
Turkey -- ground/cooked, Cheddar cheese -- shredded, Corn -- frozen, Tomato sauce -- (8 oz), Corn tortillas, Green pepper -- chopped, Chopped, Tomatoes -- (7 1/2 oz), Onion -- chopped, Mushrooms -- sliced, Clove garlic -- chopped, Italian seasoning, Flour, Chili powder, 
Preheat oven at 375.  For filling, in a skillet cook turkey, onion, and  garlic until meat is brown.  Drain off fat.  Stir in flour, green pepper,  corn, mushrooms, tomato sauce, tomatoes, chili powder, and Italian  seasoning.  Cook and stir until thickened,  bubbly, and vegetables are  tender.  Spoon about 1/3 cup of the filling into each tortilla  and top  each with 2 tablespoons cheese.  Bake, uncovered, for 15 to 25 minutes.    Recipe By     : Anita A.
