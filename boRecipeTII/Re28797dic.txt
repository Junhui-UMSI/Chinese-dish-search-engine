Thai Chicken and Sesame Noodles
Crushed red pepper, cilantro, Carrot, Boneless skinless chicken, Honey, Thin spaghetti, Sesame dressing, Green onions, Chunky peanut butter, 
Pour 1/3 cup of the dressing over chicken in medium bowl; toss to coat.  Cover. Refrigerate 1 hour to marinate.    Mix remaining dressing, peanut butter, honey and pepper; set aside.    Cook chicken and marinade in large skillet on medium-high heat until cooked  through. Mix spaghetti, chicken, carrot, onions and cilantro in large bowl.  Add peanut butter mixture; toss to coat. Serve immediately. NOTES  : Preparation time also includes marinating time  Recipe by: Kraft    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #950 by Creedenite <Creedenite@aol.com> on  Dec 8, 1997
