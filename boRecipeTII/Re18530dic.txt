Amazing Cookies
Peanut-butter, Sugar, Egg, 
Beat the egg.  Dont let your neighbours find out, though. Mix everything  together.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.    Place blobs of cookie dough on a greased cookie sheet in the size you like,  and flatten with a fork to make the traditional peanut-butter cookie  pattern. Put in oven and bake for 8-10 minutes.    NOTES:    *  These are very simple peanut-butter cookies -- They turn out a little  moist, but are yummy.    *  These cookies are a bit moist, so dont worry about that.
