Pigs in a Blanket
Crescent rolls, Hot dogs, American cheese, 
Split hot dogs lengthwise.  Add small strips of cheese on each hot dog.  Wrap hot dog and cheese in crescent roll, rolling wide end first. Bake on  ungreased pan according to crescent roll package, or until golden brown.  Randy Rigg  The Pinnacle Club BBS  812-963-9139
