Turkey Noodle Soup Mix with Recipe
SOUP, Celery seeds, Carrots, diced, Cooked diced turkey, MIX, Chicken flavored bouillon, Dried whole thyme, Soup mix, Water, Bay leaf, Black Pepper, Garlic powder, Onion, Stalks celery, diced, Fine egg noodles, uncooked, 
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.    Turkey Noodle Soup: Combine soup mix and water. Add vegetables and bring to  a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 minutes. Discard bay leaf, add  turkey and heat through. 8 servings.
