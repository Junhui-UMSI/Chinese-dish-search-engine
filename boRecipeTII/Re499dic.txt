Lemon Rice
Garlic, Basmati rice, Pepper, Lemon zest, Fresh dill, Dry dill, Unsalted butter, Chicken broth, 
Heat the broth and garlic in a saucepan to boiling.  Stir in the rice,  cover and simmer until the liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes. Remove  from heat.  Stir in the lemon zest and let stand covered for 5 minutes.  Remove the garlic.  Gently stir in the dill and butter. Season to taste  with pepper.    Recipes sent to me from Bill, wight@odc.net
