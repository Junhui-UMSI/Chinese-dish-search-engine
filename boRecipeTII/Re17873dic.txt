Aloo Gobi
Coriander powder, Red chili powder, Garlic, Garam masala, Turmeric, Tomatoes, Cumin seeds, Potatoes, Vegetable oil, Cauliflower, (1-inch) fresh ginger, 
From: Wendy Lockman <wlockman@ra1.randomc.com>    Date: Sat, 10 Feb 1996 00:15:24 -0500    Cut cauliflower into flowerets. Cube potatoes. Heat oil and saute cumin  seeds for about a minute. Add garlic and ginger, stir and add potatoes.  Bhoona, add turmeric and chili powder, and bhoona again. Add tomatoes and  simmer for about 5 minutes. Add cauliflower and high heat for about a  minutes. Lower heat, cover and let simmer for about 15 minutes. Curry  should be damp-dry.
