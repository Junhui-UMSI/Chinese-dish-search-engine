Thymely Marinated Lingcod
Dried Rosemary, Lemon juice, Oil, Garlic, Dried thyme, Pepper, Salt, Onion, Cod Fillets, Dried Oregano, 
*NOTE: Original recipe used 2 T oil **NOTE: Original recipe used lingcod.    Combine all ingredients in a bowl and marinate lingcod for 30 minutes.  Broil or barbecue until fish flakes when tested with a fork.    Makes 8 servings.    Substitutes: cod, halibut, orange roughy    This was incredibly good!!
