Pound Cake Brownies
Eggs; Large, Butter Or Margarine;Softened, Sugar, Unsweetened Chocolate; *, Vanilla, Flour; Unbleached, Salt, Baking Powder, Nuts; Chopped, 
*    Chocolate should be melted and cooled.  ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++    Cream butter and sugar; beat in eggs.  Blend in chocolate and vanilla.  Stir flour with baking powder and salt.  Add to creamed mixture; mix well.  Stir in nuts.  Spread in greased 9 X 9 X 2-inch baking pan.  Bake at 350  degrees F for 25 to 30 minutes.  Cool.  If desired, sift opwdered sugar  over top.  Cut into bars.    Makes 24 brownies.    From Better Homes & Gardens Homemade Cookies Cookbook, via The Home BBS.
