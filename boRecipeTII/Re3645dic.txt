Peggys Moms Chocolate Cake
Cocoa, Baking soda, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Flour, Eggs, Vanilla, Salt, Water, 
1) Sift sugar, flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt  together. Add oil, eggs,     vanilla and water all at once and mix well.    2) Bake in a 13x9x2 inch pan at 350 F for 25 to 30  minutes.    Top with your favorite icing.    From the kitchen of Peggy and Bruce Travers,Cyberealm  BBS Watertown NY 315-786-1120
