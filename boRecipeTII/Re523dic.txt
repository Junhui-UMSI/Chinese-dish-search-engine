French Pear Pie
Unbaked 9-inch pastry shell, Peeled and sliced pears, Lemon juice, Sugar, All-purpose flour, Ground ginger, Light corn syrup, All-purpose flour, Brown Sugar, Butter or margarine, 
Combine pears and lemon juice; toss gently.  Combine sugar, 1/3 cup  flour, and ginger; add sugar mixture and corn syrup to pears.  Spoon  mixture into pastry shell.     Combine 1/3 cup flour and brown sugar in a small bowl; blend well. Add  butter; mix until crumbly.  Sprinkle on pear mixture.  Bake at 450 degrees  F for 15 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees F, and bake 30 minutes  longer.  Yield: One 9-inch pie.    From Ethel C.
