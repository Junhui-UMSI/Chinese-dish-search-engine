Almond Mousse Au Chocolate
Envelope Unsweetened Gelatin, Blanched Almonds -- toasted, & chopped, Sugar, Powder, Semisweet Chocolate Square, Cold water, Heavy cream, Almond extract, Squares, milk, Unsweetened Baking Chocolate, 
Soften gelatin in water. Melt chocolate in milk over boiling water. Add  sugar and stir until it dissolves completely. Let it cool. Whip cream until  it stands in soft peaks. Stir almond extract and chopped almonds into  cooled chocolate. Fold 1/3 of the whipped cream into chocolate mixture.  Then fold in remaining cream. Pour into an attractive serving bowl and let  set in refrigerator, covered with plastic wrap, for at least 2 hours or  overnight. Decorate with whipped cream, candied violets, grated sweet  chocolate or toasted almonds before serving.    Serves 6.    Recipe By     :
