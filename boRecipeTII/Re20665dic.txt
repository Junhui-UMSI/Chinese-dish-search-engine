Apple Crisp
Brown sugar, Ground ginger, Walnuts, (5 C) apples; peel and, Quick-cooking oats, Flour, Cinnamon, Margarine, 
Place the apples in a greased 8x8x2 baking dish. In a bowl mix the flour,  brown sugar, cinnamon, and ginger. With a pastry blender cut in the  margarine until coarse crumbs form. Stir in the oats and walnuts. Sprinkle  over the apples. Bake in 350 degree oven 35 minutes until apples are tender  and crumb mixture is crisp.
