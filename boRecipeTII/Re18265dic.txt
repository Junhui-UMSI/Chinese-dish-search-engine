Venison Suey
Soy sauce, Sliced green pepper, Sliced onion *, Cornstarch, Drained mushrooms, Vegetable oil, Water, Sliced celery, Steak, cut in thin strips, 
In skillet, brown venison steak in oil. Add vegetables, soup & soy sauce.  Cover, cook over low heat 20 min. or until meat is tender. Stir now & then.  Blend cornstarch & water then stir into meat & vegetables. Cook, stirring  until thickened. Serve with hot rice. * onions can be substituted with 1  can beef or onion soup for every 1/2 cup onions Submitted By  RONAMIT@NETVISION.NET.IL (RON AMIT)  On   9 JUL 1995 125532 ~0400
