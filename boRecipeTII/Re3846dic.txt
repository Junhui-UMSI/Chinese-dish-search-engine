Pepper Amd Lemon Sauce
Lemon juice, Hot chilies; fresh or dried, Salt, Garlic, Onion; quartered, 
To make 1/2 cup of sauce for hearty stews and casseroles: Puree chillies,  salt, garlic and onion. Add lemon juice and allow to stand for 1-2 hours.  The sauce can be kept in a covered jar in the refrigerator for a short  period, but will ferment if allowed to stand too long.  1979. Reprinted in 1986.
