White Bean, Radish and Red Onion Salad
Clove garlic, finely minced, Sliced radishes, Lemon juice or sherry, (19 oz) white kidney beans,, Red or mild white onion,, Salt and pepper, to taste, Thinly sliced or diced, Olive oil, or vegetable oil, Chopped fresh parsley, Red leaf lettuce, or other, 
Drain beans, rinse and drain again. Place in salad bowl. Add onion,  cucumber, radishes, garlic and parsley. Toss. Add lemon juice or sherry  vinegar, oil, salt and pepper. Serve on red leaf lettuce.
