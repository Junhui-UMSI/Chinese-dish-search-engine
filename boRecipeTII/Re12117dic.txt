Zucchini (Apple) Pie
FILLING, PART B, Cinnamon, Peeled and sliced zucchini, CRUMB CRUST, Sugar, Lemon juice or vinegar, Sugar, Margerine, PART A, Flour, 
Cook "A". Boil until tender, about 10 minutes. In the meantime, prepare the  crumb crust mixture below. Add "B". Simmer 1 minute Add 1/2 cup of the  crumb mixture (this thickens the mixture) Pour this mixture over the bottom  crust. Sprinkle remaining crumbs over the top. Bake at 350 F for about 30 -       35    minutes. Chill    Crumb Crust: Crumble together and press 1/2 into a 9 x 13 inch pan.
