Bacon-Onion Croutons
Olive oil or vegetable oil, Firm white bread, Poppy seeds, Bacon drippings, Onion powder, Or 6 slices French bread, 
Cut crusts from 5 slices firm white bread or 6 slices French bread. Dice  bread into 1/2-inch cubes. In a large frying pan over medium heat, heat 2  tablespoons bacon drippings with onion powder and poppy seeds. Add bread  cubes, stirring well until coated with mixture. Transfer bread cubes to a  rimmed baking sheet and spread in a single layer. Bake in a 300? F oven  until lightly browned and crisp (25 to       30    minutes). Cool.    Makes about two cups.
