Strawberries Devonshire
Whipping cream; whipped, Orange; juice of, Lemon, Sour cream, Brown sugar, Fresh strawberries; rinsed &, Curacao, Grand Marnier, 
Combine the sour cream, 3/4 cup of the whipped cream, the brown sugar,  Grand Marnier, Curacao, and orange and lemon juice; combine thoroughly.  Place the strawberries in six compote glasses. Pour a little of the sauce  over. Top with additional whipped cream, if desired, and pass the remaining  sauce in a sauceboat.    ORION ROOM    NICOLLET MALL, MINNEAPOLIS    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of American recipes>, downloaded from  G Internet, G Internet.
