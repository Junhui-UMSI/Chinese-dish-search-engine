Key Lime Cheesecake
Cream cheese, Eagle Brand condensed milk, Key lime juice*, Prepared graham cracker, Crust, 
Beat cream cheese until fluffy. Add condensed milk. Fold in lime juice.  Pour into graham cracker crust. Chill until firm. May be served with  whipped cream or dessert topping or with fresh fruit. * about 4 large fresh  limes    NOTE. For a lemom pie, use 1/2 cup lemon juice.
