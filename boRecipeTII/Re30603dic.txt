Tahoe Brunch
Italian Sausage, Cheddar Cheese, White Bread, Butter, Eggs, Butter, Onions, Dry mustard, milk, Mushrooms, Nutmeg, Salt And Pepper, Dijon mustard, 
Butter bread, set aside.    Melt 1/2 cup butter, saute onions & mushrooms.    Grease 13 x 9 casserole.  Layer 1/2 bread, mushrooms & onions, sausage, &  cheese.    Mix eggs, milk, mustards, nutmeg, salt, & pepper.  Pour over casserole,  cover and refrigerate overnight.    Bake uncovered in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour.
