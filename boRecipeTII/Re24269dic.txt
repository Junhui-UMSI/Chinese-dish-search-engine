Trifle #1
(10-oz) frozen strawberries, (6-oz) strawberry gelatin, Pecans, (4-oz) Cool Whip, (6-oz) instant vanilla, Angel food cake, 
Tear cake into pieces and place in 9x13 pan. Mix gelatin according to  directions and pour over cake. Add strawberries and pecans. Layer with  vanilla pudding, prepared according to directions; then layer with Cool  Whip.
