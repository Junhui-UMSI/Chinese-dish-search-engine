Wild Rice and Seafood Casserole
Crab meat -or-, (5-oz) crab meat, Onion; chopped fine, Celery, (7-oz) mushrooms; chopped, Bell pepper; chopped finely, salt and pepper, Cooked wild rice, Vegetable oil, Shrimp -or-, Cooked white rice, (5-oz) shrimp, Cream of mushroom soup, Jar (4-oz) chopped pimento, 
Sauce celery, bell pepper & onion until tender. Stir in remaining  ingredients & bake in 3-quart casserole uncovered for 1-1/2 hours at 350  degrees. (You may prepare casserole the day before & refrigerate, however,  allow to stand at room temperature 1 hour before baking).    MRS.
