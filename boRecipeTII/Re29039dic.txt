Baked Pork Chops
lemon, Pepper, Tomato sauce, Pork Chops, Salt, Brown sugar, Butter, 
Date: Thu, 02 May 1996 21:14:00 -0400    From: Walt Gray <waltgray@mnsinc.com>    Brown chops with melted butter in large skillet. Place them in greased  baking dish. Place a thin slice of lemon on each. Sprinkle with salt and  pepper. Spread brown sugar over chops, then tomato sauce. Cover and bake    1-1/4    hours at 350F.
