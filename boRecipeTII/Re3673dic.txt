Pelee Wings Early Bird Casserole
Bacon; fried crispy and, Dozen eggs; scrambled, Pork sausage; fried, drained, Cream of mushroom soup, Old cheddar cheese; grated, Frozen hash browns; browned, Mushrooms (stems and, 
From:    Pat Belanger <cookie@CWCONNECT.CA>    Date:    Sun, 18 Aug 1996 20:24:04 -0700  After all ingredients are prepared, assemble in layers in following order  in a 9" x 13" baking dish: hash browns, eggs, sausage, soup, mushrooms,  cheese, bacon. Cover with foil and refrigerate from 1 hour for up to 2  days. Bake at 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes until cheese melts and  casserole is heated through. It can be frozen before baking - just increase  cooking time and check to make sure its heated through. Serves 8-10.    EAT-L Digest 17 August 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.
