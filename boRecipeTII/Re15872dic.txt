Achiote Paste
Cumin seeds, Sea salt, Lime juice, Allspice berries, Garlic, Annatto seeds, Oregano, 
Combine the annatto seeds, cumin seeds, oregano, allspice berries, and salt  in a spice mill or coffee grinder. Grind to a powderlike consistency. In a  small bowl, mix the powder with the garlic and lime juice. Store in an  airtight container, in the refrigerator.    Use for Cochinita Pibil or any grilled seafood.
