Quickie Pasta Bake
Oregano, dried, Oil, Cheese, Pasta, Onion, Tomatoes, crushed, 
Preheat oven to 325F. Cook on pasta on stove and drain. Meanwhile, heat  oil in saucepan. Add onion and garlic, saute till soft (about 5 minutes).  Add tomatoes, oregano, salt and pepper, cook till heated through, about 5  minutes. Combine cooked pasta, sauce and 1 /2 cups grated cheese in  ovenproof dish. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top. Bake 15-20 minutes at  350F.  omit onions and garlic for even quicker rendition from The Toronto Sun  posted by Anne MacLellan    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmcorr.zip
