Peanutty Snack Mix
(3 1/8 ounces) vanilla, Raisins, Salted Spanish peanuts, Dried banana chips, if, Peanut butter, Kix cereal, Honey, 
Lightly butter jelly roll pan, 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1 inch. Mix cereal,  peanuts, rasins and banana chips in large bowl. Mix pudding and pie filling  (dry) and honey in 1-quart saucepan. Heat to boiling over medium heat,  stirring constantly; reduce heat. Boil and stir 1 minute; remove from heat.  Stir in peanut butter.    Pour over cereal mixture. Toss until evenly coated. Spread in pan.  Refrigerate 30 minuts. Break into bite-size pieces. Store in covered  container at room temperature or in refrigerator. Makes 10 cups.
