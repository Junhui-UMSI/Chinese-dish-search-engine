Beef Casserole
Golden mushroom soup, Cream of celery soup, Water, Onion soup (not creamed), Beef stew meat, 
Put all 3 soups, can of water and stew meat in covered casserole. You do  not have to brown meat before baking. Cook 4 to 5 hours at 325 degrees.  This makes a delicious gravy without adding anything else.
