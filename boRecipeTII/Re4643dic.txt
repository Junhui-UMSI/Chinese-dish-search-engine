Pheasant in a Bag
Onions, Cloves fresh garlic, minced, Butter, Salt and pepper, Pheasant, 
Salt and pepper pheasant inside and out.  Rub butter on outside of  pheasant and add garlic, onion and a little butter to cavity of bird.  Put  bird in a brown bag and tie end.  Set on cookie sheet and bake 1 1/2 hours  at 350 degrees.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/garlic.zip
