The Real Mccoy New York Italian Cheesecake
Sour cream, Sugar, Ricotta cheese, Flour, Lemon juice, Eggs, Butter, Cornstarch, Cream cheese, Vanilla, 
All ingredients should be at room temperature. Combine ricotta cheese and  sour cream in mixing bowl. Beating slowly, add cream cheese, sugar and  butter. Increase speed to medium and add eggs, flour, cornstarch, vanilla  and lemon juice. Beat on highest speed possible without splattering for 5  minutes. Pour into 10 inch springform pan. Bake in preheated 350 degree  oven for 1 hour. Turn off heat and leave in closed oven for 1 more hour.  Cool on rack.
