Wurst and Potatoes Augratin
Pepper, milk, New Braunfels Pork and Beef, Cheddar cheese; grated, Butter or margarine, Potatoes; sliced thin, Golden mushroom soup, Green Onions, Salt, 
Combine soup, milk, onions, salt, and pepper. Blend well.    Place half of the sliced potatoes into a two quart casserole. Add half of  the wurst, and cover with half of the soup mixture. Repeat layers and spat  with butter or margarine. Cover casserole and bake at 350F for about 1 hour  and 15 minutes. Remove cover and sprinkle with cheese. Bake uncovered for  10 minutes longer, or until potatoes are tender.    Serve and enjoy!
