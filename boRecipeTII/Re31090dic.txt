Banana Rice Bread (Gluten Free)
NORMA WRENN, Bananas, Rice flour, Sea salt, Pecans, Honey, Baking Powder, Eggs, Butter, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Generously butter a 9-inch loaf pan.    Combine rice flour, salt, and baking powder and set aside. In an electric  mixer cream the butter and honey. Add the eggs and beat well. Blend in the  bananas and the dry ingredients. Stir vigorously and add the nuts. Pour  mixture into the pan and bake 1 hour, or until the bread test done.
