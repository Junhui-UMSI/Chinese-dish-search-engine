Quinoa Olive Medley
Green onions; finely chopped, Eden Quinoa; rinsed, Eden Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Green olives; cut in half, cut into thin strips, -to..., Eden Ume Plum Vinegar, Cayenne pepper, Pine nuts; lightly roasted, Garlic cloves; finely minced, Parsley; finely chopped, Black olives; cut in half, 
Cook Quinoa according to package directions.  Cool, then mix in remaining  ingredients, adding Ume Vinegar last and seasoning to taste.  Delicious as  a salad or side dish.
