Tortellini and Veggies (r T)
Lf Sweet Italian Tortellini, Zucchini; Grated, Dried Rosemary; Smashed, Dried thyme, Dried Oregano, Dried basil, Carrot; Grated, Stalks Celery; Grated, 
Cook tortellini according to package instructions in boiling salted water.    In large nonstick fry pan heat margarine, add the seasonings and vegetables  and saute about 5 min.    Drain tortellini and toss with the vegetables.    This was very, very good. Also very quick and easy to make.    NOTES : Cal 188.8, Fat 5.4g, Carb 28.2g, Fib 2.8g, Pro 6.7g, Sod 205mg, CFF  25.8%.
