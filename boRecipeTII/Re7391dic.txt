Pork Chops with Honey and Garlic
Honey, Garlic; minced, Pork center loin chops, Lemon juice, Vegetable cooking spray, Soy sauce; low sodium, 
Spray a large skillet with vegetable oil. Place over medium high heat. Add  pork chops to hot skillet and brown for 1 minute on each side. Reduce heat  to medium and cook about 8 minutes; turn and cook 5 minutes more or until  done. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, stir together remaining ingredients. Set  aside. Remove chops from skillet when they are done. Keep warm. Add honey  mixture to skillet. Cook 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Pour over pork  chops.
