Tropical Slush
Bottle ginger ale, Orange juice, Mashed bananas, Crushed pineapple; undrained, Sugar, Lemon juice, Jar cherries; 6 oz., 
Mix all ingredients (except ginger ale). Freeze until firm. Serve part  thawed. Break apart and add ginger ale.    Recipe by: Mops Cookbook    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 353 by Laurie Campbell  <lauriec@flash.net> on Dec 9, 1997
