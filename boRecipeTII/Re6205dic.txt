Pizza Souffle
Water, Garlic powder (optional), Eggs, Butter for the pan, (10 1/2 ounces) tomato sauce, Onion powder (optional), Matzos, milk, American or mozzarella, 
Lightly butter a 9 x 13 inch pan. Wet the matzos lightly with water. Fit 2  1/2 matzos on the bottom of the pan. Cover the matzos with half of the  tomato sauce and arrange the slice cheese over the sauce. Cover the cheese  with the remaining matzos and pour the remaining sauce over the whole  thing. Sprinkle the top with onion and garlic powder. Beat the eggs with  the milk and pour this over the matzos. Bake in a preheated 375 degree F  oven for 30 minutes or until the custard is set. Let rest for 10 minutes  before cutting into serving portions. Serves 6-7.  Posted to rec.food.recipes by yoel@brachot.jct.ac.il on Mar 7, 1994.
