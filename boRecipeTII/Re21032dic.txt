Twenty-Four Hour Fruit Salad
Red cherries, Vinegar, Butter, White grapes; halved, Eggs, Pecans or almonds, Pineapple chunks; drained, Miniature marshmallows, Sugar, Whipped cream or whipped, Mandarin oranges; drained, 
In top of double boiler combine eggs, vinegar, sugar & butter. Cook until  thick. After mixture has cooled, fold in whipped cream. Add grapes,  pineapple, oranges, marshmallows, nuts & cherries. Refrigerate 24 hours.    LYNDA BRASEL    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
