Broccoli Salad
Granulated white sugar, Vinegar, Cheddar Cheese, broccoli, Bacon, red onions, Tomato, Mayonnaise, 
Blend the first three ingredients for the dressing. Reserve the tomato.  Toss the remaining items with the dressing and let sit overnight. When  serving garnish with the tomato wedges.    Recipe by Mangia!
