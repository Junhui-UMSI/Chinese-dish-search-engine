Baked Rice #1
Soup can water, Onion, Butter or margarine, Consomme, Slivered almonds; optional, Raw rice, Salt, 
Saute onion with butter & almonds. Do not let almonds get too brown. Add  rice, salt, consomme & water. Pour into 2-quart casserole dish & bake in  preheated oven at 350 for 1 to 1-1/2 hours or until done. Cover dish for  the first 20 minutes. Serves 8-10.    MRS W.A.
