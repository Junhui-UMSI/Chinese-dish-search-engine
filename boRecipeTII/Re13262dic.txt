WW Apple and Cheddar Corn Muffins
Sugar, Eggs, Chopped..wt before cutting, Corn oil, Flour, Uncooked yellow corn meal, Cinnamon, Flour, Nutmeg, Cheddar;reduced fat,shredded, Cherri Langley   RVSN34B, Baking Powder, Corn oil, Apples;cored,pared,finely, Corn;thawed frozen, 
1.  Preheat oven to 400 F.  Line 12 muffin pan cups with paper baking cups.  2.  In large bowl combine flour, cornmeal, and baking powder. Add apples,  corn, cheese, sugar, cinnamon, nutmet, stirring to combine. In small bowl,  beat eggs and oil; stir into flour mixture (do not overbeat...the mixture  will be stiff). 3.  Fill baking cups and bake in center of oven for 15  minutes. Makes 12 muffins. ONE MUFFIN= 1 1/2 F, 1/2 P, 1 B, 1/4 F, 25  Optional cal. Cherri Langley said the recipe is from Simply Light Cooking  cookbook she obtained at a Weight Watchers meeting. Said cookbook was just  great!
