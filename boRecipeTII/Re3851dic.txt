Pepper and Herb Marinade
Tabasco sauce, Vegetable oil, Chicken bouillon, Sugar, Instant minced onion, Dried thyme, Oregano dried, garlic, Lemon juice, 
In a shallow dish or plastic bag, combine ingredients.  Add chicken, beef  or pork.  Cover.  Marinate in refrigerator 4 hours or overnight, turning  occasionally.  Remove meat from marinade.
