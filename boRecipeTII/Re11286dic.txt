Ramons Mexican Vegetable Soup
Stewed Tomatoes, Mixed Vegetables, Hambuger, lean, Garlic, Zuchinni, Frozen Corn, Kidney Beans, Cilantro, dried, Chile Powder, Onion, Carrots, Beef Stock Base, Water, 
Brown meat, drain off fat.  Cook onion and garlic until limp.  Add  remaining ingredients. Bring to boil; simmer 20 minutes or until vegetables  are tender. Serves 6-8.  Posted to Recipe Archive -  3 November 96    Date: Sun, 3 Nov 96 13:20:43 EST    submitted by: rbrokaw@qualcomm.com
