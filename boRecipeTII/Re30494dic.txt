Taco Salad #4
Hamburger, Cheddar Cheese, Round onion; minced, Pitted olives; sliced, Taco seasoning mix, Water, Head iceberg lettuce, Ripe tomatoes; cubed, 
Brown hamburger in skillet; drain.  Add taco seasoning mix and the water;  mix well. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes. Set aside.  Wash and break lettuce into bite-sized pieces. In a large salad bowl, put  lettuce, olives, onion, tomatoes and taco chips.  Just before serving, pour  taco mixture over vegetables and top with cheese.  Makes 12 servings.    From a book of my mothers (Judy Hosey) titled <Senator Richard Matsuuras  Favorite Recipes>, mostly Japanese and Hawaiian.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
