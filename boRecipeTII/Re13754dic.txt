Wild Rice Turkey Casserole
Soft bread crumbs, (10 3/4 oz.) cream of, (4 oz.) mushrooms; drained,, Soy sauce, Onions, (6 oz.) long grain and wild, (8 oz.) water chestnuts;, Cubed; cooked turkey, Melted butter or margarine, Celery, 
Preheat oven to 350. Add water to reserved liquids to make 1 cup. Combine  all ingredients except bread crumbs and butter. Spoon into a 3 quart baking  dish. Sprinkle with mixture of crumbs and butter. Bake, uncovered, about 1  hour. Makes about 8 servings.    Recipe by: Swift Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #752 by L979@aol.com on Aug  21, 1997
