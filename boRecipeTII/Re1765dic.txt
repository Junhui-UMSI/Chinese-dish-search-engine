Chicken and Fresh Vegetables
cauliflower, Fresh parsley, Boiling water, Chicken breast halves, Lemon juice, Pepper, Onion, Chicken broth, Carrots, Tomatoes, Basil, 
Break the cauliflower into small pieces. Combine vegetables in 3 quart  baking dish.    Sprinkle with 1 Tbs. parsley, 2 tsp. basil, and the pepper. Pour the  chicken broth over vegetables. Make paste of the remaining parsley and  basil, garlic powder and lemon juice. Place chicken over vegetables. Spread  the paste on chicken, cover and bake at 350F for 1 1/2 hours or until  chicken and vegetables are tender.
