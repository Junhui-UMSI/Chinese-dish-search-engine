Chicken Waikiki
Boneless chicken pieces, Flour, Vegetable oil, *Sauce*, Sliced Pineapple; 16oz, (in, Sugar, Cornstarch, Vinegar, Soy sauce, Ginger, Chicken Bouillon cube; OR, Granulated Chicken Bouillon, 
Flour and brown 1-1/2 pounds of boneless Chicken pieces in a small bit of  Vegetable Oil.    Drain pineapple, pouring juice into a 2 cup measuring cup. Add water to  make 1-1/4 cups total. In saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch, pineapple  juice, vinegar, soy sauce, ginger and bouillon cube/granules, bring to boil  and boil 2 minutes. Pour mixture over chicken pieces which have been  arranged in a 9 x 13 inch cake pan. Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes at 350  degrees F, without pineapple. Add pineapple to top and bake 30 minutes  longer. Serve hot, over rice.
