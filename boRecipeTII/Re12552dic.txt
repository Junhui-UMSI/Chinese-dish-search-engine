Yet Another Praline
Water, Vanilla, Cream, Broken pecans, Butter, Sugar -- raw or turbinado, But brown will work, 
Caramelize 1/2 cup sugar and very slowly add 1/2 cup water.  When sugar has  melted, add rest of sugar and boil about 5 minutes.  Add cream, butter, and  pecans.  Boil until syrup reaches softball stage.  Remove from fire and add  vanilla.  Allow to sit a little while, then beat until creamy. Working  quickly, drop from serving spoon onto marble slab.  (that never works for  me- I always use wax paper) "My roommate used to bring us her Memeres  pralines after a visit home. She grew up and raised a family on a sugar  cane plantation and this is her recipe.
