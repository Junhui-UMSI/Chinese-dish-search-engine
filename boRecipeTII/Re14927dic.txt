24-Hour Cabbage Slaw
Cabbage, Onion, Salad oil, Vinegar, Sugar, Salt, Celery seed, Dry mustard, Sugar, 
Shred cabbage fine, then shred onion fine. In a bowl, put one  layer cabbage and a layer of onion alternating until all is used. Cover  with 1 cup sugar.      Bring to a rolling boil vinegar, salad oil, celery seed, dry mustard, salt  and sugar.  Pour rapidly boiling mixture over cabbage very slowly.  Refrigerate for 24 hours.      When ready to serve, stir well.
