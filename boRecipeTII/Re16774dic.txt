Alii Artichoke Casserole
Garlic powder, (10 oz.) frozen chopped, Tabasco, Butter, Lemon juice, (14 oz.) artichoke hearts, Worcestershire Sauce, Italian-seasoned bread, Salt, Pepper, Cream cheese, Cayenne, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cook spinach and drain well. Melt the butter  and cream cheese. Add seasonings, lemon juice and spinach, mixing after  each addition. In a greased 2 quart casserole place a layer of artichoke  hearts, then a layer of spinach mixture and repeat. Top with bread crumbs.  Bake for 30 minutes. Serves 8.
