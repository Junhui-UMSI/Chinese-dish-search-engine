Boboli (Focaccia) Italian Bread
Warm water, Yeast, Water, Oil, Flour, Salt, 
Dissolve yeast in 1/4 c water.  Combine with warm water, oil, salt and  flour.  Mix together thoroughly and let rise 30 to 40 minutes or until  doubled in size.  Punch down and divide dough into 3 parts and place in 3  oiled pie pans.    Let rise 50 to 60 minutes or until doubled in size, brush tops lightly with  oil.  Bake at 350 degrees 50 minutes or until done.    Source: Oregonian FoodDay Typed by Dorothy Flatman, 1994    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by OldMommy@aol.com on Aug 15, 1998
