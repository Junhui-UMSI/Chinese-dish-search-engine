Beef Spaghetti Sauce
olive oil, Red wine (optional), Crushed garlic cloves or, Garlic, Leaf oregano, Onions chopped, Can Tomato sauce, Dried leaf basil, Lean ground beef, Sweet green pepper, 
Heat oil in a large saucpan set over medium heat. Add ground beef to onions  and garlic. Saute for 2 min. Add remaining ingredients. Cover and bring to  a boil, stirring often. Then, reduce heat and simmer, stirring often for at  least 5 min.
