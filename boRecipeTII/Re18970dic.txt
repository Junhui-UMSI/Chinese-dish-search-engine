Angel Pie
Egg whites, Sugar, Lemon juice, Sugar, Lemon; grated rind from, Egg yolks, Cream of tartar, 
MERINGUE: Beat egg whites until foamy. Add Cream of Tartar then beat until  the whites stand in peaks. Add sugar a little at a time, beating well  after. Beat for a minute or two after adding all the sugar. Butter & flour  pyrex pie plate. Spread the meringue in the plate. Bake 1 hr & 10-15  minutes in a 275 degree oven, then raise temp to 300 degrees and bake for  additional 20 minutes.    FILLING: Beat egg yolks well. Add sugar, juice and rind. Cook in double  boiler until thickened. Cool.    Whip 3/4 cup of whipping cream and sweeten with 2 T powdered sugar. Cover  meringue with half of cream then lemon filling and then the rest of the  cream. Leave in refrigerator overnight.
