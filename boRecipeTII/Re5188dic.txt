Pikanter Wurstssalat - Spicy Sausage Salad
Tomatoes; peeled and, Parsley, Pepper, Bell peppers, green, Presskopf - a type of, Salt, Vegetable oil, Onion, Thyme, Vinegar, 
Cut the Presskopf and bell peppers in slices, cube the tomatoes and mix  both with the onion cubes, chopped parsley, thyme, salt, pepper, vinegar  and oil and garnish with parsley.
