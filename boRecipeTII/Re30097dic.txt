Balsamic Vinegar Chicken Salad
Onion, Pine nuts, Garlic, Parmesan cheese, Salt and pepper, olive oil, Dijon mustard, Mixed greens, Balsamic vinegar, Boneless, skinless chicken, Honey, 
Heat oil in large non-stick skillet and cook onion until tender. Stir in  garlic and chicken. Cook chicken until golden brown. Stir in the balsamic  vinegar and let simmer for 1 minutes. Stir in honey and Dijon and toss to  coat. Add pine nuts and season with salt and pepper. Top mixed greens with  chicken mixture and fresh grated Parmesan.
