Victorian Funeral Cakes
Granulated sugar, milk, Butter -- melted and cooled, Eggs -- beaten, Confectioners sugar, Baking Powder, All purpose flour, Vanilla extract, 
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Gresae and flour 18 ,  2 1/2" muffin- tin  cups. 2. In a medium-sized bowl ,mix sugar,flour and baking powder.In a  small bowl,mix eggs,milk , melted butter and vanilla. Add to dry  ingredients, stirring just until mixed. 3. Fill muffin cups halfway. Bake  on the middle level of oven for 12-15 minutes. 4. Immediately remove the  cakes from cups and let coolon racks for    15 minutes. Sift Confectioners  sugar over each cake.
