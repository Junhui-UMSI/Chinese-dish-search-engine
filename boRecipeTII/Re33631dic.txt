Swedish Potato Sausage
Potatoes, pared & cut up (6, Lean boneless pork, Salt, Non-fat dry milk, Water, Black Pepper, Allspice, Lean boneless beef, Onion, medium size, cut up, 
Grind all the meat, potatoes, and onions thru a 3/8" grinder plate and  place in a mixer.  Add all the other ingredients with the water and mix  well.  After this procedure, regrind thru the 3/8" grinder plate again.  Stuff into hog casings 38-40 MM. This sausage is a very perishable product.  It is best kept when frozen. This sausage may be served fried, baked, or  broiled. When kept in refrigerator, sausage should be placed in container  and covered with water.
