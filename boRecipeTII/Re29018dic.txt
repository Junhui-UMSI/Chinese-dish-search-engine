Baked Pineapple
Water, Ground cinnamon, Vanilla extract, Sugar, Butter or margarine, Cornstarch, (15 1/4 oz.) crushed, Eggs, 
Combine cornstarch and water; stir until smooth. Add eggs, and blend well.  Add pineapple, sugar, and vanilla; mix well. Pour into a lightly greased 1  1/2-quart casserole; dot with butter, and sprinkle with cinnamon. Bake at  350 for 1 hour. Yields 6 servings.
