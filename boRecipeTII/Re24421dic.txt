Tropical Frozen Yogurt
Plain low-fat yogurt, Bananas, sliced, Sugar, Fresh strawberry halves, Frozen pineapple juice, 
Process first 4 ingredients in a food processor until smooth, stopping once  to scrape down sides. Add yogurt, and pulse 3 or 4 times. Pour into an  8-inch square baking dish, and freeze until firm. Process frozen mixture in  food processor until smooth. Return to dish; cover and freeze.    Per Per 1/2 cup: Calories 134, Fat 0.6g.
