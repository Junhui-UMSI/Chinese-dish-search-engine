White Sangria Punch (Nonalcoholic)
Pink grapefruit, Club soda, Pink grapefruit juice, Lime juice, White grape juice, 
In large pitcher, combine grape, grapefruit and lime juices; refrigerate.  Just before serving, add soda water and grapefruit slices.
