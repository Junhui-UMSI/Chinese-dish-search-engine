Alabama Fizz
Powdered sugar, Club Soda, Juice of 1/2 lemon, Gin, 
Shake well with cracked ice and strain into highball glass over two ice  cubes. Fill with club soda. Garnish with two sprigs of fresh mint.    Recipe by: Mr. Boston    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1006 by "jms@twave.net" <jms@twave.net> on  Jan 12, 1998
