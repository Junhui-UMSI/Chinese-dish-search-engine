Adobe, Mexican Smoked Chili Marinade
juice of 3 oranges, Garlic, Wine vinegar, Salt, Chopped, PUNKY SMCD91F, Oregano, Chipotle chiles  , Cumin seeds, Black pepper, juice of 1 l ime,
