Quick-Quick Couscous
Diced Mexican-spiced, Black bean flakes, Quick-cooking couscous, 
Directions: boil 2 cups of water in a saucepan, add couscous and boil for a  couple of minutes, turn off heat, throw in bean flakes and tomatoes, cover  and let sit for a few minutes, then eat!  Posted to fatfree digest by Kathleen <schuller@ix.netcom.com> on Apr 12,  1998
