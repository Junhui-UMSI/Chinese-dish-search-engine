Baked Tomatoes with Orzo and Olives
Cooked orzo, Chopped basil; or parsley if, Finely minced garlic, Grated Parmesan cheese, Yellow tomatoes; halved,, Chopped olives, Toasted pine nuts, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.    Halve the tomatoes, hollow and place in an oven to table baking dish. Mix  the rest of the ingredients , except the cheese together in a small bowl  and season with salt and pepper to taste. Carefully stuff the tomatoes and  sprinkle with the cheese. Bake for 20 minutes and serve immediately.    TIP: ROASTING TOMATOES    Heat a dry cast iron skillet until almost smoking, and add your whole  tomatoes. Or place the whole tomatoes on a broiler tray. Turn them  frequently, until they are blackened and charred on all sides. Peel off the  blackened skin, and use in place of regular tomatoes for your salsas or  tomato sauces.
