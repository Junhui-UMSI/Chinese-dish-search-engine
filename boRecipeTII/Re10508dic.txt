Quick Graham Bread
milk, Cold water, Baking soda, Salt, Brown sugar, shortening, White flour, Graham flour, 
Dissolve sugar in water; add shortening and milk. Sift white flour,  measure, and sift with salt and baking soda. Add to first mixture. Add  graham flour. Mix thoroughly. Bake in well-oiled bread pan, in moderate  oven (375 F) 1 hour.  1 loaf. The Household Searchlight - 1941
