Apple-Raisin Soup
Inches stick cinnamon, Brown sugar, Lg. cooking apples, peeled, Apple juice or cider, Brandy, Raisins, 
In 2-quart saucepan combine first 4 ingredients.  Cover, simmer 15 minutes or till apples are tender.  Stir in sugar and brandy. Cover; chill. Remove stick  cinnamon before serving. Makes 3 to 4 servings.
