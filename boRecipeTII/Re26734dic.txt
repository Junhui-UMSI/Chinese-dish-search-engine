Bake Sale Lemon Bars
Granulated sugar, Flour, powdered sugar, Powdered sugar, Margarine, Flour, Eggs, Lemon juice, 
Combine 1-1/2 cups flour, sugar and butter; pat into a greased 13x9x2"  baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Meanwhile in a bowl, whisk  eggs, sugar, 3Tbsp. flour and lemon juice until frothy; pour over the hot  crust.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until light golden brown.  Cool on a wire rack. Dust with powdered sugar. Cut into squares.
