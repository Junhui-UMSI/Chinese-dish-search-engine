Tamarind Chipotle Sauce
Juice of 1 lime, Garlic, Chipotle chiles in adobo, Water, Dark Brown Sugar, Tamarind paste, mint leaves, 
Add the Tamarind paste to the water and heat gently, stirring until smooth.  Place in a blender and puree together with the sugar, garlic, chipotles,  and adobe / (adobo) sauce. Then add the lime juice and blend in. Serve  slightly warmed. This sauce goes very well with any pork dish, especially  barbecued pork, or with seafood such as shrimp and soft-shelled crab,  especially when combined with a little mint.
