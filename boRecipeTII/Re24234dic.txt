Tregaron Grannys Broth (Cawl Mamgu Treagon) Welsh
Carrots, Large leek, Small swede (Turnip), Oatmeal to thicken, Potatoes, Bacon, White cabbage, Shin beef, Parsnips, 
Use a large saucepan and see that all the meat and vegetables are covered  by water.  Boil ingredients, except leek, together, and leave to simmer as  long as you wish.    Put the leek in 10 minutes before serving and let the cawl boil.    Cawl was the dish most commonly served for dinner on the farm during the  winter months, in the counties of South and West Wales.    The broth itself would be served in basins or bowls, with bread, and the  meat and vegetables served as a second course.    Tregaron is a small market town in the heart of Ceredigion now famous for  its poney trekking.    Croeso Cymreig.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/welsh.zip
