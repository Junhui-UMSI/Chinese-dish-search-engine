Toasted Almond Eggnog Ice Cream
Vanilla, Cinnamon, almonds, Brown sugar, Heavy cream, Eggnog, Nutmeg, 
Combine all ingredients in bowl and mix thoroughly. Stir in almonds. Pour  into ice cream maker. Makes a quart and works great in the Krups Ice Cream  Maker.
