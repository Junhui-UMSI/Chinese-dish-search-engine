Vinegared Ginger Slices
Vinegr, Ginger, Salt, Sugar, 
Peel and slice ginger as thin as possible. Combine the vinegar, sugar and  salt to taste. Heat the liquid mixture to a boil, stirring for 1 minute, to  make sure the sugar has dissolved. Place ginger in a bowl and pour hot  vinegar on top. Cover and allow to cool. Chill before serving.    Tjis recipe will keep refrigerated for several weeks.
