Pound Cake #6
Cake Flour, Salt, Eggs, Butter, Sugar, Grated lemon, Baking Powder, milk, Lemon juice, 
From:    Randy and Colleen Jones <rcjones@METHOW.COM>    Date:    Tue, 9 Jul 1996 18:24:26 -0700  Combine the flour, baking powder and salt.  Beat the butter, sugar and eggs  for 3 minutes and not with a willow switch!  Add the dry ingredients  alternately with the milk, grated lemon and lemon juice into the egg  mixture. Place in a 9x5 loaf pan that has been greased and floured. Bake at  350 F for 1 hour.    EAT-L Digest  8 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
