Almond Cake
Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Baking Powder, Sugar, Butter, Sliced almonds, Sugar, TOPPING, milk, DOUGH, Sour cream, Flour, Vanilla, milk, 
Mix all dough ingredients together; spread on cookie sheet.  Melt butter in  saucepan; add sugar, vanilla & milk. Stir in almonds & mix all topping  ingredients well. Spread over dough. Bake about 20 minutes in low oven.    FRAU PETER (BRIGETTE) LEMM    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
