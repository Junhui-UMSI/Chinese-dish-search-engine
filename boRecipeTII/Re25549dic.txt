Avocado Cream Sauce
garlic, Light cream, Fresh cilantro, Fresh chives, Butter, Fresh tarragon, Avocados, Juice of 1/2 lime, 
In food processor fitted with chopping blade, puree avocado with garlic,  lime juice, and herbs. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat. Stir in  avocado puree and cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until heated  through. Add cream and continue to heat, but do not allow to boil. Salt to  taste and serve.
