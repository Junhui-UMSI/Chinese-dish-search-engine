Basic Spritz Cookies
all purpose flour, Vegetable shortening, Sugar, Baking Powder, Butter or margarine, Vanilla, Egg, Salt, 
With electric mixer at high speed, cream butter, shortening til soft.  Add sugar gradually, continuing to cream about 5 minutes til light  and fluffy. Add egg, vanilla; beat well. Sift flour, baking powder,  salt together; add in 3 additions by hand or with electric mixer, mix  well after each addition, dough will be stiff. Shape dough into 2  rolls, wrap in plastic film, foil or waxed paper, refrigerate at  least 1 hr. Then dough is ready to put into cookie gun. Bake 7-8  minutes at 375 degrees or til just starting to turn light golden  around edges, dont overbake!    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
