Asparagus Salad
Asparagus, Tomatoes, Black Olives, Red wine vinegar, Corn, olive oil, Dijon mustard, Pepper, Yellow Bell Pepper, Artichoke Hearts, Salt, Paprika, Tabasco sauce, 
Recipe By : Karen Mack    Heat enough water to cover asparagus and then add asparagus. Reduce heat to  medium and simmer 2 1/2 minutes. Drain and rinse under coll water. Pat dry.  Cut into 1 1/2" pieces.    Mix oil, wine vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, paprika and Tabasco in a jar  with a lid and shake it to mix.    Put all vegetables together in a bowl and pour dressing over. Toss gently.  Cover and refrigerate. Can make 1 day in advance.    NOTES : Use fresh asparagus or green beans.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by SuperSecDD@aol.com on Nov 29, 1998,
