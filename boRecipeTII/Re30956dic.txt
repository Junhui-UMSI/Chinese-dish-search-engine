Tar Heel Pie
Unbaked pie shell, Eggs, Butter, Plain flour, Chocolate chips, Brown sugar, Pecans, Sugar, Vanilla, 
Pour warm butter over chocolate chips and stir. Blend all remaining  ingredients (other than pie shell) and stir into chocolate chip mixture.  Pour into pie shell and bake in 350 F oven for 30-40 minutes. Tar Heel Pie  (from the kitchen of Mary Baker)    From: Tom Kreitzberg <tak@bushrat.jpl.nasa.gov> on rec.food.recipes
