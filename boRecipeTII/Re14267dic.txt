White Truffle Aioli
White pepper, Lemon juice, Egg yolks, Garlic, Salt, olive oil, White truffle oil, 
Recipe by: Richard Chamberlain, Restaurant at the Little Nell, Preparation  Time: 0:10 In food processor, using medium speed, add egg yolks, garlic,  and lemon juice. Slowly add truffle oil and olive oil to form an emulsion.  Season and refrigerate.    Use to top "Slow Smoked Beef Tenderloin" (see recipe in this cookbook).    CHEFS NOTE: You may substitute sauteed and chilled domestic mushrooms for  the white truffle oil, if necessary.
