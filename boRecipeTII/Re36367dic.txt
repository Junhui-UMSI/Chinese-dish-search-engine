Beefy Stuffed Mushrooms
Garlic, Ground beef, White wine, Pepper, Mushrooms, fresh, Chives, Horseradish, 
(Pre-heat the oven to 350?)    1)      Thoroughly mix the meat, horseradish, chives, garlic, and pepper in  a med. bowl... 2) Remove the stems from the mushrooms then stuff the caps  with the beef mixture... 3) Place the stuffed mushrooms in a shallow baking  dish & pour the wine over the top. Bake the mushrooms in the pre-heated  oven `til meat is browned, about 20 min. Serve warm...
