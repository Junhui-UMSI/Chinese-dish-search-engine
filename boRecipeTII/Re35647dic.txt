Stuffed Artichoke Bottoms
Neufchatel, Marinated artichoke bottoms, Crumbled bleu cheese, Lowfat mayonnaise, Paprika -- for garnish, Lowfat cream cheese or, 
In a small bowl, mix together cheeses and mayonnaise. Spoon cheese mixture  into artichoke bottoms and garnish with a dusting of paprika. Serve at room  temperature.    Makes 1 dozen stuffed artichoke bottoms.    Recipe By     : the California Culinary Academy    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
