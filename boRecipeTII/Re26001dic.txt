Tofu Anti-Pasto
Garlic clove, chopped, Mushrooms, quartered, Oregano, Black olives, pitted, Balsamic vinegar, Celery, sliced, Tomatoes, diced, Green onions, sliced thinly, olive oil, Red bell pepper, julienned, Yellow bell pepper, julienne, Tofu, diced, Salt & pepper, to taste, 
Combine the vegetables & tofu in a large mixing or salad bowl.  Add  the rest of the ingredients & mix gently to combine.  Marinate at room  temperature for 30 minutes.  Check the seasoning before serving.    "Tofu: A Recipe Booklet Produced by the Ontario Soybean Growers  Marketing Board"    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/vegan4.zip
