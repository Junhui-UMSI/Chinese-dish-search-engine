Peanut Butter Marbles
Chocolate chips, Pecans, Crunchy peanut butter, Bar paraffin, Butter, 
Mix peanut butter, sugar, butter and nuts; roll into 1-inch balls and  freeze. Melt chocolate and paraffin. Dip frozen balls into chocolate  mixture; place on waxed paper to harden.
