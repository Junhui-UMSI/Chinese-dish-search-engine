Asparagus Roll-Ups
Margarine, Asparagus Spears, Paprika, Egg, Mayonnaise, Cream cheese, Blue Cheese, Roquefort Cheese, Sandwich Bread, 
Mix cheeses, egg and mayonnaise with an electric mixer. Flatten each piece  of bread with a rolling pin until almost paper thin. Spread cheese mixture  on each side, lay one spear of asparagus on each piece, and roll up. Melt  the margarine and add paprika for color. Dip each roll in the margarine,  and place on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350? for 15 minutes.    Serves: 10 to 12 as an hors doeuvre.    Can be frozen and reheated.    Source: Mountain Measures--Junior League of Charleston, WV ed. 1974    Recipe by: Mrs. Charles Q. Gage Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #610 by Bill  Spalding <billspa@icanect.net> on May 13, 1997
