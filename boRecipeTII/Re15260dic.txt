Whiskey Treats (Pareve)
Finely chopped nuts, Cocoa, Light syrup, Jiggers whiskey (hic), Vanilla wafers rolled fine, 
source: National Council of Jewish Women, cookbook (Canada)    Mix all ingredients together. Let stand 15 minutes covered. make into balls  the size of marbles . Roll in 1 cup powdered sugar. If these are placed in  a sealed jar, they can keep indefinitely.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #058 by alotzkar@direct.ca (Al) on Jan 18,  1904.
