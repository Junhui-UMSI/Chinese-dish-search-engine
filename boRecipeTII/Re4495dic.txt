Pesto Sauce Deluxe
olive oil, Fresh basil, Garlic cloves -- minced, Black pepper, Black walnuts -- chopped, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
* The cheese is optional but, if using, use a hard cheese and grate. Use a  food processor for easiest preparation.    Chop basil leaves in food processor or blender until finely chopped. Mix  the basil with chopped walnuts, minced garlic and cheese in the processor  or blender. While machine is running, slowly pour in a thin stream of oil.  Continue blending until desired consistency. Add black pepper.    Recipe By     : Jo Anne Merrill    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
