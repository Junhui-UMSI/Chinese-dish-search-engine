Peanut Butter Fudge #2
Jar (7 Oz) Marshmallow Creme, Light brown sugar, Jar (12 Oz) Peanut Butter, Chocolate Chips; optional, Sugar, milk, 
In saucepan, stir sugars and milk. Bring to a boil and boil for exactly 2  minutes. Remove from heat and add peanut butter and marshmallow creme. Beat  until smooth and pour into a greased 13 x 9 pan. Cut when cool. (You may  add chocolate chips on top)  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 179 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Oct 29, 1997
