Lamb with Okra (Syrian Style)
Lamb shoulder, Tomato sauce, Meat stock, Cooking fat, juice of 1 lemon, Parsley, salt and pepper, Garlic, Steamed rice, Dried okra, 
Simmer lamb in water to cover until tender, about 2 hours. Boil okra in  salted water 20 minutes, then drain and brown in hot fat with parsley and  garlic. Cut cooked lamb into small pieces and add to okra mixture with  tomato sauce, stock and lemon juice. Serve hot with steamed rice. Serves 6.
