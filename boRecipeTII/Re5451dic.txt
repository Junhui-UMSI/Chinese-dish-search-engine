Pineapple Cream Cheese Pie
pineapple, Butter, Dream whip, Flour, Sugar, Eggs, pie shell, Cream cheese, 
from:Burnice Owens, Indianpolis, IN    Mix first 5 ingredients and stir while cooking over low heat until thick.  remove from heat and add cream cheese. Let cool and add 2 cups whipped  Dream whip. Mix well. Pour into baked pie shell. top with remaining Dream  whip. Keep refridgerated until ready to serve.
