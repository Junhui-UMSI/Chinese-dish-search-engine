Angel Cookies (Corrected)
Bag dried fruit; finely, Box angel food cake mix, Water, 
Preheat oven to 400 degress F. Combine cake mix and water; stir in the  fruit. Cover cookie sheet with foil; drop cookies by rounded teaspoonful  onto foil. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until cookies become puffed and golden.  Remove from oven; cool completely. Cals: 76 Fat: 1g Carbs:18g Note: There  is no exact amount of fruit to add, so I put in as much as I liked to the  batter.
