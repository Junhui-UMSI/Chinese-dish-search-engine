Winter Spinach Salad
Apple cider vinegar, Scallions -- thinly sliced, Sliced, Green apple -- cored and, Pepper, Walnuts -- coarsely chopped, Seedless red grapes --, Halved, olive oil, Goat cheese -- crumbled, Dried sour cherries, Salt, Spinach -- roughly chopped, 
Combine spinach, cherries, scallions, apple slices, grapes and walnute.  Whisk together vinegar and olive oil; seasion to taste with salt and  pepper.  Pour vinaigrette over salad; toss in cheese and arrange on salad plates.
