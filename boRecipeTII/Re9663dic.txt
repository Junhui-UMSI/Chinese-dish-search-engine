Pumpkin Muffins #5
Baking soda, Baking Powder, Ginger, Cinnamon, Brown Sugar, Egg white; whipped, Nutmeg, Molasses, Fat-free sour cream, Pumpkin, Unbleached flour, Cloves, 
Date: Wed, 3 Apr 1996 09:38:28 -0600    From: matejka@bga.com (Anita A. Matejka)    Recipe By: The Fannie Farmer Baking Book  Preheat oven to 350. Prepare 8 muffin pans with cooking spray and flour;  set aside. In a mixing bowl, combine sour cream, molasses, pumpkin, and egg  white. In another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking soda, baking  powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger. Mix wet ingredients with dry  ingredients just until moistened. Use an ice cream scoop to fill muffin  pans two thirds full. Bake for about 15 minutes.
