Potato Soup Ii
White pepper, Onion, Salt, Caraway seed, Flour, Parsley, Boiling water, Potatoes; chopped, Stalk celery; chopped, Butter or olive oil, 
Heat 1 tablespoon butter and add the onions and celery. Let sizzle in  butter for 10 minutes. Add potato, cover and cook 2 minutes. Add the water  and boil for one hour. Add more water if it evaporates. In a saucepan, melt  the remaining butter, brown the flour in butter, add some potato liquid and  cook until thickened. Combine with the potato mixture and serve hot, topped  with croutons.    Posted to recipelu-digest by Eeyore <efalt@zianet.com> on Feb 25, 1998
