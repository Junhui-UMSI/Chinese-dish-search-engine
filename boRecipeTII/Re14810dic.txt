12 Hour Salad
Pecans, Whipping Cream; whipped, Pineapple; diced, Diced Marshmallows; as, Vinegar, Egg Yolks; beaten, Sugar, 
Cook first 3 ingreds. until thick. Add marshmallows. Melt. Remove from heat  and cool. Add last 3 ingreds. Cover and refrigerate till firm.
