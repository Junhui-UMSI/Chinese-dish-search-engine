Source: _The Jewish-American Kitchen_ by Raymond Sokolov (KFP, Pareve)    Cook the carrots and sweet potatoes in boiling water to cover for 10  minutes, until barely tender. Drain.    Preheat the oven to 350:F.    Combine the carrots and sweet potatoes with all the remaining ingredients  except the butter or margarine. Turn the mixture into a shallow 3-quart  baking dish.
