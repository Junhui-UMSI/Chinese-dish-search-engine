Eggless White Cake
All-Purpose Flour, Sugar, Baking Powder, Egg Beaters? 99% egg, Almond extract, Grated chocolate; optional, Additional grated Chocolate;, Chopped walnuts; optional, 
In a large bowl, with electric mixer at medium speed, beat flour, sugar,  baking powder, margarine, Egg Beaters and almond extract for 3 minutes. Do  not overbeat. Mix in grated chocolate (optional for chocolate chip cakes.)    Grease a 9 x 9 x 2- inch baking pan with Pam and dust with flour. Spread  batter in prepared baking pan.    Sprinkle grated chocolate on top to act as an icing.    Sprinkle chopped walnuts on top of chocolate. (optional)    Bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes or until done.
