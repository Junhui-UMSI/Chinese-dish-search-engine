Apple Crumb Muffins Ww
BATTER, Flour, all-purpose, Eggs, Sugar, granulated, Baking Powder, Sugar, granulated, Flour, all-purpose, Apple pie spice, Apple, Delicious, Golden, Margarine, CRUMB TOPPING, Cinnamon, ground, Milk, skim, Margarine, 
Batter - preheat oven to 400F. Line 12 muffins cups with paper baking  cups. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder and apple pie spice. Combine  milk, apple, margarine and egg; pour into dry mix. Stir until combined - do  not overmix. Fill each cup about 2/3 full.     Topping & Baking - Combine topping ingredients, stir until thoroughly  combined; sprinkle equal amount of topping over each muffin, bake for 15 to  20 minutes - or unitl toothpick comes out dry. Remove to wire rack and  cool.     Weight Watcher Exchanges: 1 Bread, 1-1/2 Fat, 50 Optional Calories.     Nutritional Analysis per serving: 183 calories, 4 g.   protein, 6 g. fat, 28 g. carbohydrate, 88 mg. calcium, 186 mg. sodium, 23  mg. cholesterol.
