Poppy Seed Dressing #2
Salt, Dry mustard, Poppy seed, Onion juice, Vinegar, Sugar, Salad oil, 
Mix sugar, mustard, salt & vinegar at medium speed. Add onion juice & stir  thoroughly. Add oil (not olive oil) slowly, beating constantly & continue  to beat until thick. Add poppy seeds & beat for a few minutes. Store in  refrigerator, but not near freezing element. Serve over watermelon,  canteloupe & honeydew melon balls. Makes about 3-1/2 cups.
