Promiscuous Prawns
Parsley, Parsley, olive oil, STEP TWO, prawns *, green onions, Lemon, STEP ONE, Sauterne, garlic, butter, juice of 1/2 a lemon, 
Heat oil in large frypan over medium flame.   Saute` garlic and onions until tender.  Add prawns and saute` until pink,  approximately 3 minutes. Add remaining ingredients, butter last. Stir  briefly to give sauce a creamy appearance. Remove to warmed au gartin  dishes and garnish with lemon and parsley.   Source:  Cooking In The Nude--Quickies, c1984
