White Chocolate Chip Orange Dream Cookies
Sugar, Brown Sugar, Unsalted butter, Baking soda, White chocolate chips, Salt, Flour, all-purpose, Egg beaters 99% egg, Orange zest, 
Combine flour, baking soda and salt in a small bowl. Beat butter, sugar and  brown sugarin a large mixing bowl until creamy. Beat in egg substitute and  orange peel. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in white chocolate  chips. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheet.    Bake in preheated 350F oven for 10 to 12 minutes or until edges are light  golden brown. Let stand for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool  completely.    Recipe by: Nestles Ad    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Sharon Barbour <sharonb@TRITON.MAYFIELD.HP.COM>  on Dec 15, 1997
