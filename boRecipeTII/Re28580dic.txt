Tex-Mex Strata
Salt, Hot Italian sausage, Firm white bread;quartered, Chili powder, Eggs, Red peppers; roasted, drain, Monterey Jack cheese, Green peppers; fried, milk, 
In medium skillet; saute sausage 10 minutes or until  lightly browned. Cool slightly; cut crosswise into  thin slices. Place in bowl. Add peppers; toss to  combine. Preheat oven to 350~. Generously grease 2 qt.  shallow baking dish. Reserve 6 bread quarters; place  remainder in prepared dish. Sprinkle with cheese and  the sausage mixture; arrange reserved bread on top.  Set aside. In medium bowl, beat eggs with milk, chili  powder and salt until mixed; pour over bread in dish.  Bake 1 hour or until center is set. Let stand 10  minutes before serving. Makes 6 servings.
