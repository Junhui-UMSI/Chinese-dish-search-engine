Alaskan Seafood Marinade
Honey, Liquid smoke flavoring, Brown sugar; (packed), Fresh Lemon Juice, Unsalted butter or margarine, Dried Rosemary, TABASCO brand Pepper Sauce, 
In medium saucepan, over medium heat, combine all ingredients. Cook,  stirring until smooth, 5 to 7 minutes. Cool to room temperature. Marinate  seafood 30 minutes prior to grilling.
