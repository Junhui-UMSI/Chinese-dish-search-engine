Uses of Chocolate Syrup
NONE  SEE BELOW, 
* CHOCOLATE ICE-CREAM SODA: Stir 1/2 cup milk, 3/4 cup seltzer and 2 Tbsp  chocolate syrup to blend in tall glass. Add 2 medium scoops chocolate ice  cream.    * Add 1 Tbsp liqueur to 1/4 cup chocolate syrup for an ice cream or souffle  sauce    * THE QUINTESSENTIAL ADULT MILK SHAKE: Combine 3 Tbsp Kahlua or  coffee-flavored liqueur, 2 Tbsp chocolate syrup and 3/4 cup milk or half  and half cream in tall glass. Cover top, shake well and add ice.    * COCOA RUM: Combine 2 Tbsp rum, 2 Tbsp chocolate syrup, 1 Tbsp creme de  cacao and 3/4 cup milk in tall glass; add ice.    * CHOCOLATE MARTINI: Combine 1 1/2 ounces vodka (3 Tbsp), 1 tsp white creme  de cacao and 1/2 tsp white creme de menthe in a shaker or covered glass  with 3 ice cubes. Shake and pour into a chilled martini glass.  Recipe by: Ladies Home Journal (Feb 97) Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #618  by Terilynn Sanford <terily@tenet.edu> on May 23, 1997
