Sweet Potato Puff
Vanilla, TOPPING, Fresh (cooked) sweet, Pecans, Sugar, Butter, Flour, Egg whites, FILLING, Flour, Brown sugar, 
Source: Deana Hannah, Tennessee  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees    2. Apply vegetable oil cooking spray to 1-1/2 qt. casserole dish.    3. Using a food processor or mixer, smooth together all sweet potato  mixture ingredients and pour into dish.    4. To make topping, crumble flour, brown sugar, and butter in small bowl,  stir in pecans. Sprinkle over sweet potato mixture.    5. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown. Serves 10    Posted to Recipe Archive - 08 Dec 96    submitted by: kelsben@tricon.net    Date: Sun, 8 Dec 96 2:31:52 EST
