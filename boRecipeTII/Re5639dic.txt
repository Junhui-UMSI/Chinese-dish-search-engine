Pineapple Swirl Coffee Cake
Walnuts, (20 oz) crushed pineapple;, Margarine; melted, Yellow; (18 1/4 oz) cake mix, Light sour cream, Brown sugar, Eggs, 
Directions: Mix 2/3 cup of dry cake mix with brown sugar, walnuts and  margarine until crumbly; set aside. Beat eggs with sour cream; mix in  remaining cake mix. Spread half of butter in 9x13 baking pan(sprayed with  vegetable oil). Top with half of the pineapple and sprinkle with half the  brown sugar mixture. Spoon and gently spread remaining batter over brown  sugar mixture. Topw with remaining pineapple and sprinkle with remaining  brown sugar mixture. Bake for 55 - 60 minutes in preheated 350 oven. Serve  warm with ice cream or frozen yogurt if desired. Delicious room temperature  also. Yield: 12 servings    Posted to recipelu-digest by P&S Gruenwald <sitm@ne.infi.net> on Feb 20,  1998
