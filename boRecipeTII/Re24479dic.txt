Tropical Temptation
Fresh ginger,, Pineapple, diced, Papaya, diced, Fat-free caramel fudge, Mango, diced, Pineapple sherbet, 
Place papaya, mango, pineapple, sherbet, fudge and ginger in a blender.  Blend on high speed until everything is completely smooth and  incorporated.    To adjust texture, if mixture is too thick, add some of the juices of  the  fruit youve cut up, or some of the pineapple juice.    From Sylvia Smith and Company.
