Baked Peach Pudding
Cinnamon, milk, Sliced, Sweet butter, Salt, Fresh peaches -- thinly, Eggs -- beaten, Bread crumbs, Dark brown sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350.  Scald milk, stir hot milk into bread crumbs.  Add  butter and mix well.  Add salt, brown sugar, eggs cinnamon, stir into bread  crumb mixture.  Fold in peaches.  butter a casserole dish, pour in pudding  mixture and bake 1 hour.  serve with whipped cream, sour cream, ice cream,  whatever your heart desires. (serves 6).
