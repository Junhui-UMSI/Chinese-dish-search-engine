Pear Cardamom Relish
Peeled, cored, and c, Poppy seeds, Ground cardamom, Sea salt, Minced fresh ginger root, Sugar, White wine vinegar, Ground cinnamon, Firm, ripe pears, Pear nectar, 
Combine all ingredients in a nonreactivepan. Bring to boil, reduce heat to  low, simmer for about 25 minutes, until thickened. Cool. Serve with grilled  or roasted vegetables.
