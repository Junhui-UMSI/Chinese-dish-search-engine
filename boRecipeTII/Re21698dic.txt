Apple Tapioca
Nutmeg, Sugar, Water, Pearl tapioca, Butter, Apples, Cinnamon, 
Soak tapioca according to box and then drain. Bring water to a boil and add  tapioca.  Boil until clear and thick, stirring often.    Cut apples into eighths and place in a 8x8" casserole. Sprinkle with sugar,  cinnamon and nutmeg.  Dot with butter.  Pour hot tapioca over all.  Bake  1/2 hour at 350? or until apples are done.
