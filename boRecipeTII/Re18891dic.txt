Angel Cake Surprise
FROSTING, Maraschino Cherries; for, Vanilla, Crushed Pineapple; drained, Sugar, Marshmallows; cut in eighths, Cream; whipped, Sugar, 10" Round Cooked Angel Food, Pineapple Chunks; for, Heavy Cream; whipped, Maraschino Cherries;, Walnuts, 
Combine cream, sugar and vanilla. Fold in maraschino cherries, crushed  pineapple, marshmallows, and chopped walnuts. Chill. Cut with serrated  knife, 1 inch slice from top of a 10 inch round angel cake. Cut out a ring  2 inches wide and 2 inches deep. Spoon in chilled filling. Replace slice  cut from top.    Frosting: Beat cream and sugar together with mixer.    Frost top and sides of cake. Chill. Decorate with pineapple chunks and  maraschino cherries.
