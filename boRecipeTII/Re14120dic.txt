White Clam Sauce
Chopped parsley, To 3 cloves garlic,, Canned chopped clams,, olive oil, Dried oregano leaves, 
1. Place oil and garlic, to taste, in a small, heat-resistant, non-metallic  bowl and heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 3 minutes. 2. Remove garlic  pieces. 3. Add remaining ingredients and heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven  4 minutes or until heated through. Stir occasionally. 4. Serve over  spaghetti.
