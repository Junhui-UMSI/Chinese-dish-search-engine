Zorbas Pocket Sandwich
Low calorie Italian salad, Oscar Mayer New England, Cucumber; thinly sliced, Lettuce leaves, Pita breads ( 6 to 7 inch, Onion; thinly sliced, Oscar Mayer Cotto Salami, Pitted ripe olives; sliced, 
Combine salad ingredients. Cover; refrigerate at least 1 hour. For each  sandwich, place 2 meat slices and 1 lettuce leaf in pita pocket. Add salad  mixture.
