Cranberry Chocolate Fudge
Sugar, Marshmallow cream, Evaporated milk, Butter or margarine, (6 oz) semisweet chocolate, Van, Walnuts, Fresh or frozen Cranberries;, 
In a large-size sauce pan combine sugar, marshmallow creme, evaporated milk  and butter. Place over medium heat, stirring constantly until mixture  boils, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in chocolate chips, until  melted. Add vanilla, walnuts and cranberries. Pour into a greased 8-inch  square baking pan. Cool. Cut into 1 inch squares    Recipe by: Loyal Family Circle Cookbook (Joyce Douglas)    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Goldtag1@aol.com on Aug 3, 1998, converted  by MM_Buster v2.0l.
