Balsamic Peppered Chicken
Skinless boneless chicken, Chicken broth, Balsamic vinegar, Lemon pepper; seasoning, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Garlic, Parsley sprig, Butter, 
Flatten chicken to 1/4 inch thickness. Press lemon-pepper seasoning evenly  on both sides. In large frypan, place oil and heat to medium temperature.  Add chicken and cook, turning once about 7 minutes or until fork can be  inserted in chicken with ease. Remove chicken to warm serving platter; keep  warm. In small bowl, mix together vinegar, broth, and garlic; add to  frypan. Cook over medium-high, scraping up brown meat bits, about 2 minutes  or until mixture is reduced and syrupy. Add butter; stir to melt. Place  chicken on serving dish and spoon sauce over chicken.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1033 by -- Mom <clubok@nersp.nerdc.ufl.edu>  on Jan 23, 1998
