Quick Party Eggnog
Light rum, Eggnog mix, chilled, Nutmeg, Heavy cream, 
Here are some recipies for party punches that include alcohol. Some are  wine-based, others liquor-based. All are for 12 four ounce servings, and  all are from _The New Doubleday Cookbook_ by Jean Anderson & Elaine Hanna.    Mix eggnog mix and rum. Whip cream to soft peaks and fold in; cover and  chill 1 hr. Pout into large punch bowl and dust with nutmeg.    Recipe by: Jean Anderson & Elaine Hanna Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #519  by "Master Harper Gaellon" <gaellon@inch.com> on Mar 16, 1997
