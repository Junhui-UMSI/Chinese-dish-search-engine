Tongue Ragout
Konigsberger, Salt, Sugar, Beef stock, Plain flour, ox tongue, Butter or margarine, Lemon juice, Red wine, 
Melt butter; add flour & cook until medium brown. Add cold stock; stir  constantly; simmer 10 minutes on low heat. Season with wine, sugar & salt.  Add tongue & Konigsberger meatballs (see index for recipe).    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
