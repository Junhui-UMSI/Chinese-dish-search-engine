Banana Fruit Cup
PINEAPPLE SLICED #10, BANANAS FRESH, APPLE FRESH, ORANGES FRESH, 
1.  COMBINE PINEAPPLE WITH FRESH, PEELED, THINLY SLICED BANANAS; MIX  THOROUGHLY.    2.  ADD ORANGES.    3.  QUICKLY (TO PREVENT DISCOLORATION) COMBINE APPLES WITH FRUIT MIXTURE;  ADD FRESH, PEELED, THINLY SLICED BANANAS; MIX THOROUGHLY.    4.  COVER, REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.  :    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 3, 4 LB 4 OZ FRESH ORANGES A.P. (11 ORANGES) WILL YIELD  3 LB PEELED ORANGES.    2.  IN STEP 4, 4 LB 2 OZ FRESH APPLES A.P. (11 APPLES) WILL YIELD  3 LB 8 OZ CORED APPLES.    3.  IN STEP 2, 17 LB 12 OZ FRESH BANANAS A.P. (44 BANANAS) WILL YIELD  11 LB 8 OZ PEELED, THINLY SLICED BANANAS.
