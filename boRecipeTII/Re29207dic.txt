Baked Shrimp for Thirty
Tube anchovy paste, White or wild rice, Buttered bread crumbs, Cream of mushroom soup, Cream, Cooked jumbo shrimp, 
Thin soup with cream; add anchovy paste. Pour over shrimp, top with bread  crumbs. Bake for 10 minutes at 350. Serve over rice.    MRS J.R. (FAYE) GOLSTON    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
