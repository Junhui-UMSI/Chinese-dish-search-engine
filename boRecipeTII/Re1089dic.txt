Cornbread Salad
Jiffy cornbread mix, cooked, Bell pepper, chopped, Tomatoes, chopped, Onion, Cooked ham, chopped, Sweet pickle relish, Sweet pickle juice, Mayonnaise, Eggs, hardboiled, Bacon, cooked, 
Prepare mixture of bell pepper, tomatoes, onion, ham, pickles, juice, and  mayonnaise. Crumble cornbread. Alternate layers of cornbread and a layer of  mixture. Crumble bacon over last layer and top with sliced boiled eggs.  Refrigerate overnight.
