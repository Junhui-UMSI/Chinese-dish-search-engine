Willinghams Cajun Bbq Seasoning Mix
Brown sugar, Garlic powder, MSG, Salt, Black pepper; freshly ground, Cinnamon, Cayenne pepper; (or more), Lemon pepper, Mild chili powder or use hot, Dry mustard, 
Combine ingredients and keep in a glass jar with a tight-fitting lid. Store  in a cool dark place.    Recipe by: John Willingham    Posted to bbq-digest by wight@odc.net on Apr 29, 1998
