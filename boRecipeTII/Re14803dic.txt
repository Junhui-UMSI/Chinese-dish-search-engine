100% Whole Wheat-No Oil Pizza Crust
Gluten, Hot water, Sugar, Salt, Whole wheat flour, Rapid rising yeast, 
Put all ingredients in bread machine. Stop machine after first kneading.  Let dough rest 15 minutes, then spread out in pizza pan. Add sauce and  toppings. Use only plant food. Remember also olives are 99% fat free. If  making without bread machine, knead full 15 - 20 minutes by hand. Let dough  rest 15 minutes at least, then spread out in pizza pan. Add sauce and  desired toppings. Bake at 450 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes.
