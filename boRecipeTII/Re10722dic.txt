Quick Sugar and Cinnamon Coffee Cake
milk, Cake Flour, Sugar, Butter, Sugar, Topping:, Cinnamon, Salt, Vanilla, Egg, shortening, Baking Powder, 
Sift dry ingredients together. Add remaining ingredients. Beat  altogether in mixing bowl on #3 speed about 2 minutes. Scrape sides &  bottom of bowl while beating. Pour batter into 8" square baking pan  or deep 9" layer cake pan. Bake 350 for 25 minutes. Remove from oven  & put on topping. Topping: Brush warm cake with butter. Mix sugar &  cinnamon together. Sprinkle over top. Return cake to oven & bake 5  minutes longer.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
