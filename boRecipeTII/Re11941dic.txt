Recess Cups
Peanut butter, Hersery bar, 
melt chocolate and 1/2 cup of peanut butter together then melt peanut  butter in another pan. place cupcake liners in muffin tins put in 1  tablespoon of chocolate peanut butter mix in each tin let sit 30 minutes  add some melted peanut butter on top of each let sit 30 minutes then top  with another tablespoon of chocolate mix.
