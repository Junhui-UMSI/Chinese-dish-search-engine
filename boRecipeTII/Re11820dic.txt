Raw Apple Cake
Eggs, Vanilla, Stick of margarine, Cinnamon, Sugar, Raw apples, chopped, Sugar, Topping, Condensed milk, Pecans, Vanilla, Self rising flour, Vegetable oil, 
Mix all ingredients together until well blended.  Pour into a greased 9x13  inch pan and bake at 300 degrees fro 1 hour. Topping: Mix in a saucepan and  cook until thick. Remove cake from oven and cut into squares while still  hot.  Pour topping over hot cake and let cool. Randy Rigg
