Thai Rice Black Bread
Caraway seed, Fennel; or anise seeds, Malt; Or Molasses, Cumin seed, Bread Flour; Or All Purpose, Dry yeast, Flour, Salt, Rice; Black, Water, Rye; oat or whole wheat, Vegetable oil, 
Simmer rice in water until just tender. Cool and pour into transparent  measure. Add water to bring the total measure up to 11 ounces. Add to  mixing bowl with the remaining ingredients except the yeast. Place yeast in  proper place according to the directions for your machine.    from the Narsai David sheet    From: <amy@cup.portal.com>  From: Bread-Bakers Archives: ftp.best.com/pub/reggie/archives/bread/recipe
