Beefy Spaghetti Soup
Medium onion, Salt, Frozen cut green been, Can sliced mushrooms, Broken spaghetti (uncooked), olive oil, Clove garlic minced, Parsley flakes, Pepper, Bay leaf, Beef stock, Oregano, Ground beef, crumbled, Can tomato sauce, 
In  large  casserole  combine  ground  beef,  onion,  garlic,  and  oil.  Microwave  at  high until meat is no longer pink,  stirring once  during  cooking.  Drain.    Add remaining ingredients.   Cover.  Microwave at high till spaghetti is  done (around 20 to 25 minutes).  Stir occasionally.    Posted to the TWSU BBS (316-689-3779), compiled by the Sysop.
