Vanilla Sugar Cookies
Salt, Sugar, Unsalted butter, Flour, Vanilla, Egg, Baking Powder, 
Stir together flour, powder & salt in med bowl. Beat butter, sugar,  egg & vanilla in bowl until well blended. Stir in flour mixture.  Gather dough together & shape into ball, wrap & refrigerate several  hours or overnight. Preheat oven to 350. Lightly coat baking sheets  with nonstick spray. Roll out dough with lightly floured rolling pin  on lightly floured surface to 3/8" thickness. Cut into cookies with 2  1/2" holiday shaped cookie cutters. Place cookies on prepared cookie  sheets, spacing 1 1/2" apart. Decorate with colored sprinkles, sugars  & gum drops if you wish. Bake in oven 10-12 minutes or until lightly  browned around edges.
