Baked Chicken
Cinnamon, White or rose wine, Oregano, Butter or margarine, Lemon juice, Flour, Thyme, Paprika, Chicken broth, Boneless chicken breasts, Rosemary, Tomatoes, chopped, Fresh basil leaves, chopped, olive oil, Salt/pepper, Garlic powder, 
Combine flour with salt, rosemary, oregano, thyme, paprika, and pepper.  Dust chicken with seasoned flour and brown in olive oil and butter. Place  in baking dish and add tomatoes, wine, chicken broth, garlic powder, fresh  basil, cinnamon, and lemon juice.    Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes or until chicken is fork tender and  brown.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  03, 1998
