Ranch Style Beans
Vinegar, Tomato sauce, Catsup, Lean ground beef, Mix, Envelope green onion dip, Prepared mustard, Cans pork and beans in, Can kidney beans -- drained, 
In large skillet or slow-cooking pot with browning unit, cook meat until  red color disappears.  Drain off excess fat.  In slow-cooking pot, combine  beef, dry dip mix, undrained pork and beans, drained kidney beans, catsup,  mustard, and vinegar.  Cover and cook on low for 3 to 4 hours.
