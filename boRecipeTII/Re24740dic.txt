Aunt Alices Curried Four Fruit Bake
Curry powder, Pear halves; canned, Lg, Maraschino cherries, Peach halves; canned, Brown sugar, Margarine, 
Heat oven to 325 degrees.    Melt margarine. Add sugar and curry. Drain and dry fruit in 1 1/2 quart  casserole. Add margarine mixture. Bake 1 hour. Serve warm from baking dish.    Good with ham, lamb, chicken or turkey. Flavor will be even better if dish  stands covered in refrigerator and the reheated.    Recipe by: Alice Hale    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "Barbara E.
