Quick Dill Pickles in a Crock
Dill seed, Cucumbers, Garlic, Cider vinegar, Peeled and sliced, Salt, Water, 
Mix all ingredients together and bring to boil.  Pour over washed  cucumbers and store in jar or crock.  Ready to eat in 2 days. PAUL  MONTGOMERIE   (NZQM86A)    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #123 by novmom@juno.com (Angela [lynn]  Gilliland) on May 01, 1997
