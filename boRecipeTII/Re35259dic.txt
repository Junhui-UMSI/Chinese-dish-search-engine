Beef and Rice Skillet
Pepper, Ground beef, Minute rice, Tomatoes; 1 lb. 12 oz., Green pepper, Butter, Salt, Onion, Yellow mustard, 
1. Lightly brown meat in butter in large skillet. Season with 1 tsp. salt  and the pepper.    2. Thinly slice onion and green pepper and add to skillet. Stir in minute  rice right from box. 3. Saute over high heat until rice is lightly browned.  Add tomatoes, mustard and remaining salt. Mix well. 4. Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and let simmer 5 minutes uncovered.
