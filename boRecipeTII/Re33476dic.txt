Basil Marinated Tomatoes
tomatoes, red onion, fresh basil, olive oil, Red wine vinegar, Sugar, Salt, 
Place the tomato slices in a shallow dish. Top each tomato slice with an  onion slice. In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients; stir until  well blended, then drizzle over the tomatoes and onions. Cover and chill  for 2 hours before serving.
