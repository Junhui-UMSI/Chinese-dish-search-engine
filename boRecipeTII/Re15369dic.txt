White Bread for Bread Machine
Sugar, Salt, Bread flour, Egg, Yeast, Nonfat dry milk, Water, Margerine, 
Note: This is for a Large (1.5 lb) loaf, adjust # of servings to 4 for  medium loaf, 3 for small loaf.     Put ingredients in your machine in the order suggested by the manufacture  and engage warp engines on regular bread cycle.     This makes a very nice (rich) white bread.    From: The Bread Machine Cookbook (vol I) by Donna Rathmell German    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #213 by serge.cyr@sympatico.ca (Serge Cyr)  on Aug 14, 1997
