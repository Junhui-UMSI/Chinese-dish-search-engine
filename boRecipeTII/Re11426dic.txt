Raspberry Almond Kiss Dessert Pizza
Dessert Pizza Shells, Almond paste, Almonds, Chocolate chips, Raspberries, TO SERVE, Egg whites, Raspberry preserves, TOPPING, Whipped cream, 
Beat egg whites in mixer with paddle until foamy; add almond paste and  cream until smooth. Add raspberry preserves and mix until fully  incorporated. Reserve raspberry mixture.    Top each unbaked pizza shell with 1/2 cup of the raspberry/egg white  mixture, leaving 1/2-inch border. Bake in 400 degree oven for 7 minutes.    Remove from oven, and top each pizza with 6 oz. whole raspberries, 3/4 cup  toasted almonds and 4 oz. chocolate chips. Return to oven and bake for an  additional 7 minutes.    Remove and let cool for fifteen minutes. Serve warm, dusted with  confectioners sugar and topped with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.    Source: Pizza Today, January 1993.
