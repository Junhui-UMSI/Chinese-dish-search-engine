Green Bean Casserole
String beans, Sour cream, Mushroom soup, Crackers, almonds, Velveeta cheese, 
Put beans in bottom of casserole dish.  Sprinkle with garlic salt. Layer  almonds over beans.  Slice cheese over almonds.  Mix soup and sour cream.  Cover beans with soup mixture and sprinkle with cracker crumbs moistened in  butter. Bake 30 minutes at 375 degrees.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by tbankerd@leading.net on Sep 06, 1998
