Haggerty
Potatoes, washed and peeled, Onion, finely sliced, Chopped bacon, Salt and pepper to taste, Grated Irish or white, 
Slice peeled potatoes paper thin and pat dry with paper towels. Fry the  bacon in a heavy skillet. Remove the bacon and 1 tablespoon of bacon fat.  Add to the skillet the potatoes, onion, bacon and cheese in alternate  layers, ending with potatoes. Dot top with remaining bacon fat.  Cook over  medium heat until potatoes are tender and bottom is crisp. Slide the  Haggerty out onto a plate.  Return to pan, top side down. Continue cookie  until browned. Cut into wedges and serve hot.    Formatted on May 20, 1998 by Jamie Yates    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Oct 15, 1998
