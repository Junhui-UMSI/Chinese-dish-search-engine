Sweet Milk Doughnuts
Salt, Ginger, Melted shortening, Flour, Vanilla, Sugar, Lemon flavoring, Nutmeg, Eggs, slightly beaten, Cinnamon, Baking Powder, milk, 
Combine eggs and sugar, blend carefully.  Sift flour, measure, and sift  with salt, baking powder, and spices. Add alternately with combined milk,  shortening, and flavorings to first mixture. Chill dough. Turn onto lightly  floured board and roll in sheet 1/3 inch thick. Cut with floured cutter.  Fry in deep fat (365 F) until brown. Drain on crumpled absorbent paper.  Sprinkle with sugar.
