Asian Gazpacho with Scallop Satays
Size red onion; chopped, Basil, Thin soy sauce, Rice wine vinegar, Limes; juice of, Mint, GARNISH, Sambal, Ground coriander, Size cucumbers; peeled and, Eight ounce can whole roma, Salt, Garlic, Canola oil, Minced ginger, Serrano chilies; stem, Sesame oil, Cilantro, Sliced scallions, Pepper, 
Using a food processor or a blender, completely puree all the vegetables,  in batches. Add the oils, soy sauce, vinegar, lime juice and sambal. Blend  and season with salt and pepper. Add the herbs and ground coriander, blend  and taste for seasoning again. A little sugar can be added if it    tastes too tart. Ideally, this should be made a day in advance so the  flavors have time to meld    together and develop.
