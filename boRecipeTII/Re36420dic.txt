Beer Bread and Biscuits
Sugar, Beer, Sugar, BREAD, BISCUITS, Self rising flour, Bisquick, Beer, 
BREAD: Preheat oven to 375~. Grease a 9x5" loaf pan. In large bowl, mix  together the flour and sugar; add beer; mix well. Form into loaf; place in  pan. Bake 1 hour; let cool. Makes 1 loaf. BISCUITS: In large mixing bowl  combine Bisquick, sugar and beer; beat 1 minute. Place in greased muffin  tins and bake at 375~ for 20 minutes. Makes 1-1/2 dozen.
