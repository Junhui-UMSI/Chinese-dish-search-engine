7-Grain Oatmeal Cake
Cold water, Lemon Rind, Egg whites, Non-alum baking powder, Cooked AM 7 Grain Cereal, AM Unbleached White Flour, Maple syrup, Soy milk, AM rolled Oats, Pecans, Raisins (optional), AM Canola Oil, Cinnamon, AM Soy Flour, Vanilla, Sea salt (optional), 
Mix well and fill oiled cake pan 3/4 full.  Bake 350 F. until light golden  brown.  This is not a very sweet cake.  May be served with apple butter,  honey, etc.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "7 Grain Cereal Recipes" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
