Quick Pear Crumble
Walnuts, Pear halves in juice, Golden raisins, Brown sugar, Ground ginger, Quick-cooking oats, Ground cinnamon, Butter or margarine,melted, 
1. Place 8 pear halves, cut-side up, in broiler-proof pan. Combine  remaining ingredients. Crumble over pears.   2. Broil 6" from heat 3 minutes, until browned.
