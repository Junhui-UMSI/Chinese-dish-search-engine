Turkey Pasta Supreme
Cl Garlic; minced, Butter or margarine, Whipping cream, Uncooked turkey breast, Fresh basil or 2 tsp dried -, Grated Parmesan cheese, Pepper, Hot cooked pasta; 3-4 cups, 
Recipe by: TASTE OF HOME DEC/JAN 1996 Cut turkey into 2x1/4" pieces. In a  skillet, saute turkey and garlic in butter until turkey is browned and no  longer pink, about 6 min. Add cream, basil, Parmesan and pepper; bring to a  boil. Reduce heat; simmer for 3 minutes, stirring freq. Stir in pasta and  toss to coat. Serve immediately.
