Baked Garlic-Thyme Chicken2
Salt, Scallions, Lemon juice, Chicken, Black Pepper, Dried thyme, Garlic, Butter, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In small bowl, blend together butter,  scallions, garlic, lemon juice, thyme, salt, and pepper. 2. Arrange  chicken, skin side up, in a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish. Spread seasoned  butter over chicken. Bake 50 minutes, or until tender, basting occasionally  with pan juices.
