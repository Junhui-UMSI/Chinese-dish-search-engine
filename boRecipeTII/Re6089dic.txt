Pizza Dough #01
Yeast, Warm water, Salt, Hot water, Vegetable oil, Flour, Sugar, 
Soften yeast in warm water. Add hot water to oil, salt and sugar; cool to  lukewarm and add to yeast mixture. Add most of flour and beat until smooth.  Add remaining flour and knead about 2 minutes. Set in greased bowl,  covered, in a warm place to double in bulk, about 45 minutes. Punch dough  down, divide into 2 equal parts. Makes two 12-inch pizza crusts.
