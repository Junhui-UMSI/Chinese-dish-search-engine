Potato Farmers Breakfast
Eggs, Bacon, Potatoes; cubed, Onion, Cheddar cheese; grated, Pepper, Fresh chives; chopped, Salt, 
1. Fry the bacon in a large frying pan, or microwave it on a microwave    bacon tray.  Drain the grease.    2. Add the cubed potato, onion and chives to the pan with the bacon. Cook  the mixture on the stove for 5 minutes.  Stir in the beaten eggs, salt and  pepper.    3. Sprinkle the grated cheese over the mixture.  Cover the pan and cook  over medium heat for 5 more minutes, or until the eggs are set.    4. Remove the pan from the heat and cut the dish into wedges.  Serve hot.
