Beer-Boiled Shrimp
Budweiser Beer, Garlic, Bay leaf, Celery, Salt, Shrimp, peppercorns, onions, Lemon, 
Pour Budweiser in large pot and add onion, garlic, bay leaf, peppercorns,  celery, and salt. Bring to a boil; simmer 10 minutes. Add lemon and shrimp  to Budweiser beer. Simmer 12 minutes. Remove from heat, let cool, and  drain. Peel off shells and chill well.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #249 by "Deborah K?hnen"  <DEBKUHNEN@classic.msn.com> on Sep 19, 97
