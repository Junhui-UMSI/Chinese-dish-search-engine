Apricot Candy Popcorn
Jelly or jam, Popcorn; popped, Almonds; toasted, Dried apricots; cut small, Coconut; toasted, Brown sugar, Butter, 
In a heavy saucepan, put the butter, jelly and brown sugar. Cook over  moderate heat to 235~ on a candy thermometer. Pour over the popped corn,  coconut, almonds and apricots.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 652 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 31, 1998
