Popeyes Sweepea Soup
(1 1/2 lb) dried green, Cloves garlic, pressed or, Finely chopped fresh, Red onion, diced, Water, Stalks chopped celery,, Crumbled basil leaf, SPIKE to taste, Light molasses, to taste, Diced yam, unpeeled, Shelled fresh peas or 1 10, Tamari, 
In 6 or 8 qt. pot bring water, split peas, onion, yam, and celery to a  boil. Reduce heat and simmer covered about 30 min. Add spinach, garlic and  basil. Bring to boil again. Reduce heat and simmer 25-35 min.    Note: Im not sure where this recipe came from, but the original called for  3 Tbs. olive oil to be added to the water in the very beginning. The actual  name is POPEYES SWEEPEA WITH OLIVE OYL ...and it is said to contain all  of Popeyes favorite - sweet peas, yams ( " I yam what I yam"), olive oil  and of course spinach.  :)
