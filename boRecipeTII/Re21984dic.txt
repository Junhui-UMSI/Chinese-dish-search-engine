Applesauce Cupcakes/low Sugar/low Fat
Unsweetened applesauce, Walnuts, Sugar, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Egg, Flour, Salt, Margarine, Baking soda, Vanilla, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Cream margarine until fluffy. Beat eggs and  sugar; add to margarine and blend. Sift together dry ingredients. Add to  margarine mixture alternately with applesauce, mixing well after each  addition. Stir in vanilla and nuts. Spoon into 12 cupcake pans sprayed with  vegetable pan spray. Bake for 15-20 minutes.    Recipe by: MARITA BLESSING   (HWCF65A)    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #774 by "jana@cablenet-va.com"  <jana@cablenet-va.com> on Sep 7, 1997
