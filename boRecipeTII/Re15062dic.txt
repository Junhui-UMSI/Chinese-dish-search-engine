Western Kentucky Pork Bbq Sauce
Cayenne pepper, Garlic powder, Onion powder, Paprika, Dry mustard, Ketchup, Worcestershire Sauce, Black pepper, Water, Salt, 
Mix all ingredients and boil for five minues, stirring frequently.
