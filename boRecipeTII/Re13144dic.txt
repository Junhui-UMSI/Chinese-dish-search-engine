Wonderful Ham Sauce
Brown sugar, Lemon juice, Horseradish, 
Bring the ingredients to a boil, then remove from heat. May be used to pour  over ham while baking, or cooking in a crockpot, or served on the side at  mealtime.  Yield: 1-1/2 cups.
