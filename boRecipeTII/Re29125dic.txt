Baked Rice #3
Garlic powder, Green Onions, Lemon juice, Fat free cheese; shredded, Rice, Canned corn; drained, Salt & black pepper to taste, Egg whites; slightly beaten, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine all ingredients in large bowl. Spray  round or square baking dish with non-stick cooking spray. Pour mixture in  dish & bake at 350 deg for 25-35 minutes.  Entire recipe makes 4 servings.    FAT FREE    FROM SOME COOKBOOK!    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
