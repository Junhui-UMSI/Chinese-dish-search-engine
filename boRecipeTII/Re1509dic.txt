Breakfast Black Eyed Peas
Red onion; chopped, Green pepper; chopped, Yellow pepper; chopped, Garlic, Black-eyed peas; drained, Roma tomatoes; diced small,, Salsa, Whole wheat tortillas, 
In a nonstick pan, saute the onion, garlic, tomatoes, and peppers in 1/4  cup of vegt broth until soft. Stir in black-eyed peas and cook on medium  heat until vegetables are cooked through. Stir occasionally to keep from  burning. Stir in salsa and simmer for 5 minutes more. Warm tortillas and  roll up some of the black-eyed peas mixture into each one.
