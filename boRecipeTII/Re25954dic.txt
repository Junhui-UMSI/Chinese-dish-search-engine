Bacon Cheese and Tomato Sandwiches
Large Tomato, Sliced, Slices Rye Bread, Toasted, Mayo Or Salad Dressing, Dried Dill Weed, Slices Swiss Cheese, Slices Bacon, 
Place bacon on microwave rack in glass dish.  Cover loosely and microwave  until crisp, 2-1/2 to 3-1/2 minutes. Spread toast with mayonnaise; sprinkle  with dill. Place toast slices on serving plate; top with tomato and cheese  slices.  Crumble bacon and sprinkle over top. Microwave uncovered on high  (100%) until cheese begins to melt, 1 to 1-1/2 minutes. From: Syds  Cookbook.
