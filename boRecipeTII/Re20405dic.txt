Vegan Almond Cheesecake
Honey, Vegetable oil, graham cracker crust, Lemon juice, Almonds, Almond meal, Brown sugar, Almond extract, Tofu, salt, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.    Blend all ingredients except crust and almonds in a food processor until  smooth and creamy.    Pour into unbaked pie shell, top with slivered almonds, and bake for about  45 minutes or until cracks start to form on the edge of the filling.
