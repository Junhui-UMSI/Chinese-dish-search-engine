Ultimate Pineapple Cheesecake
(8-oz) cream cheese;, Salt, Sugar, PINEAPPLE GLAZE, Butter, Cornstarch, (8.75-oz) crushed pineapple;, Graham cracker crumbs, Sugar, Beaten egg, Vanilla, Sugar, Butter, Eggs, 
From: llburnet@lesley.b23b.ingr.com (Lesley Burnette)    Date: Tue, 12 Oct 1993 15:36:19 GMT    From:Southern Living.  Combine crumbs, 2 TBS sugar, butter.  Mix well, press in bottom of 9 inch  springform pan.  Beat cream cheese with mixer until fluffy.  Gradually add  1 c. sugar, mixing well. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well. Stir in  vanilla and salt.  Bake at 350 for 50 minutes.  Let cool to room  temperature on rack. Refrigerate several hours. Yield: 10 to 12 servings.    Pineapple Glaze: Drain, reserve juice of pineapple.  Add water to juice to  make 1/2 cup.  Mix sugar and cornstarch with juice.  Cook over low heat  until thick. Add egg, butter, cook 2 minutes. Cool and spread on cheesecake  and refrigerate. Yield: 1 1/2 cups.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /DESSERTS    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
