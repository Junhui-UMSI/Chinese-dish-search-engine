Asparagus with Orange Dipping Sauce
salt and pepper, Orange juice, Asparagus, SAUCE, olive oil, Salt, Orange rind; finely grated, Sesame oil, 
Find a saucepan thats wide enough to hold the asparagus spears  lengthwise and set it over medium-high heat. Fill about three quarters with  water, add the salt and bring to a boil. Plunge the spears into the boiling  water, return to a boil and cook until the spears are just tender when  pierced with a fork, about 2 to minutes depending on their thicknes (do not  overcook). Rinse under cold running water to stop the cooking, then drain  on a clean kitchen towel.   To prepare the sauce, whisk together the orange juice, orange rind, olive  oil sesame oil, and coarse salt and pepper until well blended. Arrange the  asparagus on a large serving platter with dipping sauce on the side. Serve  warm or cold.
