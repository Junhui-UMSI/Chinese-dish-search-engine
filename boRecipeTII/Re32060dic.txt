Barbecued Burger Beans
Brown sugar, Catsup, Frozen lima beans, Water, (16-oz) kidney beans, Brown sugar, Vinegar, Cooked white onions, Onion; minced, Salt, Pepper, Ground beef, Salt, Butter, Shortening, (16-oz) baked beans, Pepper, Prepared mustard, Dry mustard, 
Cook lima beans until tender; drain. In 4-quart casserole, combine all  beans with 1/4 cup brown sugar, 2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper & prepared  mustard. Bake at 400 for 45 minutes. Meanwhile, combine ground beef with 1  tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper, 1 minced onion & 1/4 cup water. Shape into balls  & brown in shortening. Remove from skillet; add catsup, vinegar, brown  sugar, dry mustard & butter; heat & add meatballs; simmer 30 minutes.  Arrange meatballs & small onions on top of beans; spoon sauce on top. Bake  at 400 for 15 minutes. Serves    10.    MRS GERARD (BETSY) HEIMER    MRS JAMES R (JAN) GLICK    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
