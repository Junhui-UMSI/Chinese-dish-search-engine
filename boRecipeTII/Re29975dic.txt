Teriyaki-Glazed Pork Medallions
Brown sugar, Orange juice, Garlic powder, Ginger, Pork tenderloins, Margarine or butter, Reduced-sodium soy sauce, 
Melt margarine in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add pork;  cook and stir 6-8 minutes or until lightly browned and no longer pink. In  small bowl, combine brown sugar, orange juice, soy sauce, garlic powder and  ginger; blend well. Pour over pork; cook and stir until mixture thickens  and coats pork.
