Apple-Raisin Cake
Eggs, Vegetable oil, Flour, Apples; peeled and diced., Sugar, Vanilla, Salt, Baking soda; (bicarbonate), Raisins; up to 1, Chopped nuts; (optional), 
Recipe by: Merle Gutkin (my sister in London)    (Preheat oven to 325 deg. F.)    1. Beat eggs, sugar & oil.    2. Add baking soda, flour, salt.    3. Stir in pre-soaked raisins and apples. (soak raisins in boiling water or  liquer of your choice for about 1/2 hour, then drain.)    4. Pour into greased, shallow pan, bake at 325 deg. F. 45 minutes - 1 hour.
