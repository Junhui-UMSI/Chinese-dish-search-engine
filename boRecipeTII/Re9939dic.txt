Puree of Fava Beans
Cumin seeds, oregano or marjoram, olive oil, Dried fava beans, soaked, Garlic, Salt, 
Drain & skin the soaked fava beans.  Cover with fresh water & cook gently  with the garlic & cumin seeds until the beans are tender. Drain. Puree the  beans.  Stir in enough olive oil & a littel water, to give the puree a  soupy consistency.  Season with salt & beat well. Heat just before serving  with a little more olive oil & a sprinkling of the zaatar. Serve with Arab  bread & a bowl of mixed spices (ground cumin, cayenne & salt).
