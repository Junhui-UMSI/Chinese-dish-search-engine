"No Bake" Cookie Crumble Cheesecake
Milk, heated to boiling, Your favorite cookies,>>>>>, Cream cheese, 8 oz. ea., Mini-chocolate chips, Deep dish graham cracker>>>>, Crust (9oz. size)., Envelope plain gelatine, Vanilla extract or flavor, Coarsely crushed., Cold milk, Sugar, 
In blender, sprinkle gelatine over cold milk; let stand 2 min. Add hot  milk and process at low until dissolved, about 2 min. Add cream cheese,  sugar and vanilla and process until blended. Arrange chocolate in bottom of  crust.  Pour in gelatine mixture; sprinkle with crushed cookies. Chill  until firm, about 2 hrs.       Yield: 8 to 10 servings
