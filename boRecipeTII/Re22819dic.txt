Apricot White Chocolate Scones
Vanilla, White Chocolate Chips, Butter, Eggs, Salt, Dried Apricot halves, Cream, Flour, Sugar, Baking Powder, Pecans, dry-roasted, 
1) Sift the dry ingredients together.    2) In a large mixing bowl, cut the butter into the flour.    3) In a small mixing bowl mix the cream, eggs, and the vanilla together,  pour into the flour and mix until it is incorperated.  4) Stir in the Chocolate, Pecans and the apricots, and mix to combine.    5) Roll out the dough to about 1/2" thick, and cut with a cutter or make  triangles.  6) Bake In the oven for 25-30 minutes at 375f.
