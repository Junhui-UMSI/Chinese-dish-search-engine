Turkey Burgers
Unsalted butter, Mayonnaise, Chopped fresh sage leaves, Mushrooms; thinly sliced, Ground turkey, Toasted hamburger buns, Bacon, -Dried sage leaves, 
COARSELY CHOP THE BACON, place in a food processor and process until  smooth. Add the ground turkey and sage and process just until well mixed.  Scrape into a bowl and form the mixture into 4 patties. Place in the  refrigerator, covered to chill for 20 minutes. Heat the butter in a medium  skillet over medium heat. Add the patties and cook 4 minutes. Turn, add the  mushrooms and continue to cook 5 to 7 minutes more. Remove the patties from  the heat and place on hamburger buns. Garnish with some sauteed mushrooms.  Serve immediately and offer mayonnaise.
