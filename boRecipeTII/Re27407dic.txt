Toasted Salad Topping
Blue Bonnet margarine;, Grated Parmesan cheese, McCormick/Schilling onion, Cheerios cereal, 
Heat margarine in 10-inch skillet until melted; stir in onion salt and  cereal. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until cereal is coated,  about 5 minutes. Turn onto paper towel. Sprinkle with cheese; toss until  coated. Cool completely. Sprinkle over tossed green salad or marinated  vegetable salad. About 2 cups topping.
