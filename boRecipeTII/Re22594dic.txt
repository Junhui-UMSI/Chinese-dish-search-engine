Apricot Blossoms
FF Butter Cookie Recipe, 
Beat 1 Tbls. grated lemon peel into dough; using cookie press fitted  with star disk, press out into 2" stars. Bake 10 minutes. Brush while  still warm with apricot preserves; decorate with finely chopped dried  apricots.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
