Apple-Glazed Roast Pork- Crockpot
Brown sugar, Pork loin roast, Salt to taste, Apple juice, Apples, Black Pepper, Ground ginger, 
From: Gerald Edgerton <jerrye@wizard.com>    Date: Wed, 22 May 1996 08:37:49 -0700 Procedures: 1. Rub roast with salt  and pepper. Brown port roast under broiler to remove excess fat; drain  well. 2. Place apple quarters in bottom of crockpot. Place roast on top of  Apples. 3. Combine apple juice, brown sugar, and ginger. Spoon over top  surface of roast, moistening well. 4. Cover and cook on Low setting for 10  to 12 hours or until done.    Posted to Master Cook Recipes List, Digest #94
