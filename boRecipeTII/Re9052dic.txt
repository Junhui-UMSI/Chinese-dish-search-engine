Priscillas Salad Dressing Mix
Poppy seeds, Dried minced onion, Granulated sugar, Salt, Dry mustard, 
Combine all ingredients in a small until evenly distributed.  Put mixture  in a foil packet or small glass jar.  Label with date and ingredients.  Store in a cool, dry place.  Use within 6 months.    Makes 1 packet, or about 1/3 c ,  Priscillas Salad Dressing Mix.
