Pineapple Tarragon Chicken Breast
Pineapple juice, Pepper, Salt, Margarine, Tarragon, Honey, Chicken breast, 
Mix pineapple juice, honey, tarragon, salt and pepper together in a  saucepan. Let simmer till hot. Set aside.    Grill chix breasts till just brown. Place in a pan and pour sauce over  chix. Cover and bake in a 350 oven till done.
