Barley Casserole
Beef broth, Slivered almonds, toasted, Butter, Onion, finely chopped, Fresh mushrooms, Salt and pepper, Barley, 
Saute mushrooms and onions in butter. Dont let onions brown. Mix in  barley, salt and pepper. Pour into buttered 1 1/2 qt casserole. Cover with  broth to 1/2 inch above barley. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 45  minutes, until liquid is absorbed and barley is tender. Garnish with  almonds.    SOURCE: National Cooking Echo 04/19/90 Contributed to the echo by: Ellen  Cleary
