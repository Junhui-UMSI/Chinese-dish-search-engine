Vegtable Rice Pilaf
corn oil, frozen mixed vegetables, brown rice, cooked, Dried thyme, Onion, Pepper, almonds, Garlic, 
1.  In large skillet heat corn oil over medium heat.  Add almonds; cook,  stirring frequently, 1 minute or until lightly toasted.  Remove with  slotted  spoon; set aside.    2.  Add onion and garlic to skillet; stirring frequently, cook 3 to 4  minutes.  Add vegetables, salt, thyme and pepper; cook, stirring, 2  minutes.    3.  Stir in rice and toasted almonds; heat through.    Makes 5 cups.
