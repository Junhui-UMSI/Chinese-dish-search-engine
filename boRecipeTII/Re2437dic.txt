Black Pepper Cake
Sugar, Lemon juice, Salt, Ground nutmeg, Sticks softened margarine, Eggs, Vanilla extract, Black pepper, Flour, Cream of tartar, 
Source : "Cooking America" by Arnold Sable    1. Heat oven to medium (170 C). Grease and flour tube pan, or 22 pan.    2. Beat margarine with sugar. Add eggs one by one and beat well after each  additional egg.    3. Sift flour with cream of tartar, salt, black pepper, and nutmeg. Add to  egg mixture and beat well. Add lemon juice and vanilla.    4. Pour into pan and bake for 35 mins, until a toothpick comes out dry.    5. Cool cake in pan. Remove from pan, and leave for one day, before  serving.    Serving suggestion: Serve with ice cream.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Raya Tarab" <tarab@netvision.net.il> on  Sep 11, 1998,
