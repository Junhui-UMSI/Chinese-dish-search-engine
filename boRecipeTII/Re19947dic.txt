Apple Bundt Cake
Salad oil, Eggs, apples, walnuts, Vanilla, Sugar, Cinnamon, Flour, Baking soda, Salt, 
1. Sift dry ingredients.    2. Beat eggs well, add sugar and oil and beat.    3. Add flour mixture, vanilla, apples & nuts.    4. Mix well. Spray bundt pan with Pam.    5. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees or until done. Or cook 50 min. Convection  Bake    6. Cool in pan 10 minutes. I use 4 apples.    GLAZE: 1 stick of oleo. 2 tsp. of milk 3. 1/2 cup light brown sugar    Combine above and boil 2 minutes, stirring continuously.
