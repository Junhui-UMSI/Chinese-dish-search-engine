Peanut-Chile Soup (South African Soup)
Onion, Salt, black pepper & cayenne, okra, Fresh hot chiles, Leeks, long-grain white rice, Chicken broth, Red bell pepper, Roasted salted peanuts, roasted red pepper, 
Reserve 1/2 cup leeks. In a heavy 2 to 3-quart saucepan over high heat,  bring broth, onion, and the remaining leeks to a boil, then reduce heat to  low and simmer until vegetables are tender (25 to 30 minutes). Remove from  heat and puree in a blender. Return soup to saucepan, reserving 1 cup. Add  rice, chiles, bell pepper, and reserved leeks and bring to a boil. Reduce  heat to simmer and cook for about 15 minutes. Meanwhile, in a blender puree  reserved soup and peanuts. Stir into soup on stove and continue cooking for  the remainder of the 15 minutes. If using fresh okra cut into rings and  boil until tender (about 5 minutes). Drain and rinse thoroughly. If using  canned okra drain and rinse. Add okra to soup, stir, and remove from heat.  Season to taste with salt, pepper, and cayenne.    NOTES : To serve, ladle into a heated serving dish and lightly sprinkle top  with chopped peanuts. Yield: 6 servings (serving size: 1 cup.)
