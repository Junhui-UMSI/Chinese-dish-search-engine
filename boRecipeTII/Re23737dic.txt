Artichoke-Fish Salad
Reduce fat french dressing, Reduce fat mayonnaise or, Lemon juice, Good dash of ground pepper, Canned artichoke hearts,, Salt, Finely diced celery, Flaked crabmeat or tuna, Lettuce, 
Marinate the artichoke hearts in the French dressing. Combine all  remaining ingredients save lettuce and artichoke hearts. Add the shredded  lettuce and toss till well blended or serve salad separately on a bed of  lettuce. Garnish each portion with 2 artichoke hearts.
