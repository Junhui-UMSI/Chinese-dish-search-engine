Pumpkin Cake Bars
Cooked pumpkin or, Eggs, well beaten, Cinnamon, Cloves, Sugar, Butter, Ginger*, Pecans, (16 ounces) pumpkin, Salt, Yellow cake mix, 
Mix eggs, pumpkin, sugar, salt, spices all together. Pour into a very  lightly sprayed 13x9 inch pan. Sprinkle dry cake mix on top. Drizzle melted  butter over mix; spread chopped nuts over all. Bake at      325    for 1 hour and 20 minutes.** Cool completely and cut into  squares. Yield: 24    *I only used nutmeg and cinnamon and didnt measure it, just went by looks.    **Cover with foil loosely to keep from browning too soon for the first half  of cooking time.
