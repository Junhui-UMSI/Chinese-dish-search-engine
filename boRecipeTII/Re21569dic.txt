Apple Pudding - Lighter
Eggs, Cinnamon, Sugar, Flour, Apples; peeled and diced, Baking soda, Walnuts, Hot water, Lighter Bake, Nutmeg, 
Cream sugar & shortening together. Add eggs and mix at low speed until  blended. Stir flour, nutmeg and cinnamon together and add to egg & sugar.  Mix. Stir in apples & nuts. Dissolve baking soda in hot water, then stir  into batter. Pour into greased & floured square baking dish. Bake at 375  degrees until knife inserted at center comes out clean. Approx baking time  35 - 45 minutes.
