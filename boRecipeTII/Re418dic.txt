Lemon Glaze
WATER, BUTTER, SUGAR, POWDER, IMITATION LEMON FLAVOR, Salt, 
1.  COMBINE POWDERED SUGAR, BUTTER OR MARGARINE, SALT, BOILING WATER,  AND FLAVORING, LEMON.  MIX UNTIL SMOOTH.    2.  SPREAD GLAZE OVER BAKED SWEET ROLLS OR COFFEE CAKES.  COAT OR DIP  FRIED DOUGHNUTS IN GLAZE.  :    NOTE:  THIS GLAZE MAY BE USED FOR A VARIETY OF SWEET DOUGH PRODUCTS  (SEE RECIPE NO. D-G-7, GUIDE FOR SWEET DOUGH MAKE-UP) OR DOUGHNUTS  (SEE RECIPE NOS.
