Strawberry Angel Delight
Pecans, Flour, Brown sugar, Jar; (7 oz) Kraft, Lemon juice, Whipping cream; whipped, (16 oz) frozen strawberries;, Butter, 
Combine flour and sugar. Cut in butter; add nuts. Press into 9-inch spring  pan    Bake 350 F. for 20 minutes. Cool.    Gradually add lemon juice to Kraft Marshmallow Creme, mixing until blended.  Stir in strawberries; fold in whipped cream. Pour over crumb crust.    Refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Take out and let stand a few minutes  before serving.
