Teriyaki Stew
Cooking oil, Boiling water, Bunch small carrots; cut in, salt and pepper, Bottled Brown Bouquet sauce, Bunch celery; cut in, Whole small onions, Cooking sherry (optional), Fresh peas, Instant blending flour for, Whole stalk celery, (10-oz) frozen peas -or-, Lean stew beef; cut in, Carrot, Potatoes; cut in, Onion, Bay Leaves, Fresh mushrooms; cut in, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Brown beef in cooking oil in skillet. Put in deep pot and cover with  boiling water; add seasonings, whole stalk celery, carrot, and onion. Cook  slowly until meat is tender (at least 1 hour).     Remove bay leaves, celery, and carrot.  Add cut celery, carrots, potatoes,  and small onions. Cook about 20 minutes or until onions are tender.  Additional water may be added with the vegetables if necessary. Add  Worcestershire sauce, sherry, mushrooms, and frozen peas; cook several  minutes. Mix flour with enough water to make a thin and fairly clear gravy;  add to mixture and stir until thickened. Serve hot over rice. Yield: 6 to 8  servings.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.  Downloaded from Glens MM Recipe  Archive, G Internet.
