Chili Nawlins
Salt, Oregano, Red wine vinegar, Grown sugar, (16 oz) red kidney beans, Corn chips, Head lettuce; shredded, Sharp cheddar cheese;, Ground round beef, Ground pork, Onions, Garlic, Chili powder, Chopped green chili peppers, (16 oz) crushed tomatoes, Tomato paste, Bay leaves; crumbled, 
The Culinary Connection Newsletter, August 21, 1998 http://www.culinary.com    In a large, heavy pot, brown the meat with the onions and the garlic. Drain  off the fat and stir in the chili powder (depending on quality, you may  want more). Add chilies, tomatoes, tomato paste, bay leaves, salt, oregano,  vinegar, and brown sugar. Cover the pot and cook for 2 hours over low heat.  Add beans and cook, uncovered, 30 minutes more. Serve with side dishes of  corn chips, shredded lettuce, and shredded Cheddar cheese.
