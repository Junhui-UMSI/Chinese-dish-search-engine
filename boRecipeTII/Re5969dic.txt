Pistachio-Almond Pudding Cake
pistachio pudding, yellow cake mix, Almond extract, Green food coloring, Eggs, Water, Vegetable oil, 
Combine all ingredients in large mixer bowl. Beat 4 minutes on medium. Pour  into greased and floured 10-inch bundt pan. Bake at 350 for 50-55 minutes.  Cool 15 minutes in pan. Sprinkle with sugar.    MRS BILLY F.
