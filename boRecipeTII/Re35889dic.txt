Stuffed Lychee
Cream cheese, Crystallized ginger, macadamia nuts, whole seedless lychee, 
Combine cream cheese and ginger. Stuff mixture into lychee with small  spoon, butter knife or a pastry bag. Chill. Garnish and serve. Makes 3  dozen.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 24,  1998
