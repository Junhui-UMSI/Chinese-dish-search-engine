Amaretto Freeze
Vanilla ice cream; softened, Amaretto, Brown sugar, 
Combine amaretto and brown sugar, stirring until sugar dissolves. Process  amaretto mixture and ice cream in a blender until smooth, stopping once to  scrape down sides. Pour into 6 individual freezer-proof serving dishes;  cover and freeze overnight. Makes 6 servings. This can also be spooned into  a 2-quart baking dish and frozen. http://www.pathfinder.com/ ?
