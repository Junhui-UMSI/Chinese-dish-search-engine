Avgolemono Sauce Ala the Dumpling Cookbook
salt and pepper, Dill, fresh, chopped, Egg yolks, Cornstarch, Chicken broth, heated, or, Cayenne pepper, Lemon, Juice of, 
1. Combine the egg yolks and cornstarch in the top of a double boiler. Beat  with a whisk until the mixture is light and frothy.    2. Add the hot broth or water, beating with a whisk all the time. Cook over  simmering water until the sauce starts to thicken. Never let this sauce  come anywhere near boiling-it will curdle.    3. Add the lemon juice and cayenne. Correct the seasoning.    4. Garnish with dill and serve.
