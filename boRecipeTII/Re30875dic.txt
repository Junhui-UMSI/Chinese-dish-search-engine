Tangy Mustard Cauliflower
Cheddar cheese, Head cauliflower, Sour cream, Prepared mustard, Onions, 
Cook cauliflower and drain. Combine sour cream with onion and mustard.  Spread on hot cauliflower. Sprinkle with cheese. Cover; let stand in  saucepan until cheese melts, about 3 minutes. Serve whole, right side up in  serving dish.    NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING 188 Calories; 5g Protein; 5g  Carbohydrate; 18g Fat;      236 mg Sodium    Posted to Recipe Page 06 Oct 96
