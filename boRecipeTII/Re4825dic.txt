Pick a Sauce Grilled Chicken
Salt, Cut-up broiler- fryer, Pepper, 
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. Grill the chicken, bone sides down 5  inches from medium coals 20 to 30 minutes; turn chicken. Grill 30 to 40  minutes longer, turning and brushing frequently with one of the sauces,  until done.          6    servings.    Sweet-and-Sour Sauce:    Heat l/2 cup chili sauce and 1/4 Cup plum or grape jelly over low heat,  stirring constantly, until jelly is melted.    French Sauce: Mix 1 cup French dressing and 2 teaspoons celery seed    Hot Pepper Butter Sauce: Mix l/2 cup margarine or butter softened, and l/2  teaspoon red pepper sauce.
