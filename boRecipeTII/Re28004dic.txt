Baked Chicken Breast Supreme
Pepper, Boneless; skinless chicken, Paprika, Butter, Salt, Nonfat sour cream, garlic, Shortening; melted, Celery salt, Bread crumbs, Worcestershire Sauce, Lemon juice, 
In large bowl, combine sour cream with lemon juice, Worcestershire, celery  salt, paprika, garlic salt and pepper. Add chicken to sour cream mixture,  coating each piece well. Let stand, covered, in refrigerator overnight.  Next day, preheat oven to 350 degrees. Remove chicken from sour cream  mixture. Roll in crumbs, coating evenly. Arrange in single layer in large  shallow baking pan. Melt butter and shortening in small saucepan. Spoon 1/2  over chicken. Bake chicken uncovered 45 minutes. Sppon rest of butter  mixture over chicken. Bake 10 to 15 minutes longer or until chicken is  tender and nicely browned. Formatted for RecipeLu by Ramona Cooper.
