Basic Pepper-Salt Mix
Salt, Szechwan peppercorns, 
1. Heat salt and peppercorns in a dry skillet oyer medium-low heat. Stir  or shake the pan constantly until the salt begins to brown and the odor of  pepper is released (about 5 minutes).     2. Remove mixture from pan and let cool slightly. Crush peppercorns with a  rolling pin; then strain through a sieve.     3. Store mixture in a tightly capped jar. Use as a table condiment or dip  for deep-fried and roasted foods.     NOTE: These mixtures are also known as Fried Salt or Cantonese Salt.     VARIATION: For the peppercoms, substitute either 1 teaspoon ground black  or white pepper; or 2/3 teaspoon Chinese red pepper.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4.
