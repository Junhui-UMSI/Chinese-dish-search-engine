Bread Pudding
Slices of stale bread, Vanilla, Eggs, milk, Sugar, 
Butter bread slices and cut into squares. Put in small casserole dish. Mix  milk, vanilla, and sugar in saucepan and heat. Beat eggs in bowl, add  warmed milk mixture slowly. Pour over bread and sprinkle with nutmeg. Bake  in 350 deg. F. oven for 45 minutes.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Sharon Wills" <willsfam@vaxxine.com> on  Oct 28, 1998
