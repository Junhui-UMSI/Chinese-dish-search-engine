Bavarian Breakfast Rolls
Butter, All-purpose flour, Coconut, Sour cream, Jar (16-oz), 
Blend flour, butter & sour cream as you would pie dough. Wrap in waxed  paper. Refrigerate overnight. Divide dough in half. Roll each half into a  thin rectangle. Spread each half with jam & sprinkle with coconut. Starting  with shorter end, roll up each piece. Pinch ends together & seal seam. Bake  on greased cookie sheet 40-45 minutes at 350. Slice & serve. (This may be  wrapped in foil & reheated or may be frozen. Great for morning meetings.)    MRS.
