Peppermint Stick Ice Cream
Cream, ICE CREAM COOKBOOK-----, Peppermint candy, milk, Recipe for vanilla ice, 
Crush or grind 1 1/2 lbs. peppermint stick candy. Soak in the refrigerator  overnight in 2 cups of milk. Substitute this milk-candy mixture for 2 cups  of milk in your favorite vanilla ice cream recipe.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
