Almond Tart
Stick of butter, Crushed crackers or cookies, Sugar, Egg yolks, Egg whites, milk, Sugar, Yeast, Almonds, Butter, Orange, zest of, with a, Flour, Vanilla, 
Madame Lerner, *translated by Lori Siegel*    Make a dough with 200g butter 500g flour, 200g sugar, 1 large tablespoon of  milk 2 teaspoons of yeast , a little vanilla, 4 egg yolks.  Let sit.    Prepare separately:  200g almonds, 200g sugar, 2 egg whites, 1 full  Tablespoon of butter, 1 zest of orange with a little juice, 3 tablespoons  of crushed crackers or cookies. Mix all.    Stretch a layer of dough to 6 to 9 mm  thick on the pie (tart mold), then a  layer of the the almond mixture.  Put in the oven.
