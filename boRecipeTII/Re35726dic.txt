Stuffed Capon with Persimmon
Chicken Stock, Fresh cranberries, Chipotle chilies; minced, Apples; diced, Plums; diced, Spelt, Ten pound Capon, Raisins, Teaspoon rosemary; minced, Bread; diced, Lemon sage; minced, salt and pepper, Ripe persimmons, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bring chicken stock to boil, add spelt and  simmer covered for ~ hour. (Holly notes: I assume this means "approx. 1  hour). Add fruit and chipoltes, simmer 15 minutes. Remove from heat and  toss with herbs, bread and seasonings. Stuff capon with mixture while still  hot. Roast stuffed bird for 70 minutes. Let sit, then arrange on a serving  platter and garish with sliced persimmons.
