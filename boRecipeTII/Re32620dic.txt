Sweet Onion, Tomato and Corn Salad with Basil
Chopped fresh basil, White balsamic vinegar, Tomatoes, thinly sliced, Basil leaves, Fresh white corn kernels, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Vidalia or sweet onion,, Dijon mustard, 
Combine first 4 ingredients in a bowl; stir well and set aside.    Combine basil and remaining ingredients; toss well. Drizzle vinegar mixture  over salads, and toss gently.
