Amish Sour Cream Apple Pie
Salt, Vanilla extract, Sour cream, Egg, All-purpose flour, Brown Sugar, pie shell, Butter, Apples, CRUMB TOPPING, Ground cinnamon, Sugar, All-purpose flour, 
Recipe by: Jo Merrill Use dairy sour cream for this recipe-not a sour cream  substitute. Beat sour cream and egg together; add sugar, flour, salt and  vanilla. Mix until smooth. Stir in apples. Place mixture into pie shell and  bak in preheated 400 degree oven for 25 minutes. Meanwhile mix topping  ingredients until crumbly-- Remove pie from oven and sprinkle the crumb  topping over. Bake 20 minutes longer.    Formatted & Busted by RecipeLu <recipelu@recipelu.com>  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 653 by RecipeLu  <recipelu@geocities.com> on Jan 31, 1998
