Strawberry Relish
Honey, Balsamic vinegar, Red pepper flakes, Walnuts, chopped, Strawberries, sliced, Raisins, Orange Peel, Orange juice, Dijon mustard, 
Combine first 6 ingredients and whisk to blend thoroughly. Add remaining  ingredients and toss. Serve this chutney-style accompaniment with grilled  fish or chicken.    Source:  California Strawberry Board, printed in Sun newspaper June 28/93  Shared but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier, Calgary Alberta
