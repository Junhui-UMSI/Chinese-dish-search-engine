Tomato Chutney #1
Raisins, Salt, Vinegar, Dry mustard, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Cayenne pepper, Allspice, Green tomatoes, Apples; chopped, Prepared mustard, Onions; chopped fine, Ground cloves, Garlic, 
Chop tomatoes, apples & onions fine. Add remaining ingredients & cook until  thick & clear, stirring occasionally. Seal in hot sterilized jars. Makes 10  pints.
