Baked Onions
Clarified Butter, Onions; peeled and sliced, 
From: ltsilver@borg.com (LT Silverman)    Date: Wed, 10 Jul 1996 14:49:57 -0400  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. (moderate).    2. Stir butter into onions in a 9 X 13-inch baking pan.    3. Bake for 1 1/2 hours, or until golden brown in color. Stir occasionally.    4. Cool. Refrigerate until needed. (May be stored in refrigerator for up to        2    weeks.) Makes 1 quart.
