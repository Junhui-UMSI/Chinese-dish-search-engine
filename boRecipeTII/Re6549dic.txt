Poached Raspberry Pears
Lemon peel, Bosc pears, Seedless raspberry, Apple juice, Lemon juice, 
Mix all ingredients except pears in 10-inch skillet. Add pears. Heat to  boiling; reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer uncovered 30 minutes, spooning  juice mixture over pears and turning every 10 minutes, until pears are  tender. Serve warm or chilled.
