Pumpkin Bread 3
Butter/margarine, Cinnamon, Pumpkin puree, Sugar, Baking Powder, Baking soda, Whole wheat flour, Apple juice, Cloves, Brown sugar, Eggs, Unbleached flour, Allspice, 
Preheat oven to 350.  Cream sugars and butter together.  Add eggs one at a  time, beating well.  Mix the flours, baking powder, soda, salt and spices.   Add alternately to creamed mixture with cider and pumpking.  Beat well.  Turn into 2 greased loaf pans.  Bake for 1 hour until bread tests done.
