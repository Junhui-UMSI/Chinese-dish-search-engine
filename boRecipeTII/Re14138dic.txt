White Cut Chicken Breast
Scallion, Soy sauce, (large) chicken breast, Vinegar, Sesame oil; more or less, Fresh ginger root, Salt, Sherry, Tabasco sauce; more or less, Or, Sugar, Water, 
1. Place chicken breast in a saucepan. Boil water and pour over chicken.  Simmer, covered, 10 minutes.    2. Drain, reserving liquid for stock. Let chicken cool; then bone. With a  sharp knife, cut in 3/4-inch strips. Arrange, skin-side up, on a serving  dish.    3. Mince scallion and ginger root and sprinkle over chicken. Combine soy  sauce, sherry, sugar, vinegar, salt and sesame oil. Pour over chicken. Then  sprinkle with Tabasco Sauce and serve.    From <The Thousand Recipe Chinese Cookbook>, ISBN 0-517-65870-4.
