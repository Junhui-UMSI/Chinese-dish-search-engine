Raspberry Gratin 2
Sugar, Baskets fresh raspberries,, Framboise (raspberry eau de, RASPBERRY LAYER, Egg whites, Salt, Flour, Egg yolks, Sugar, BATTER, 
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees and set rack on lower third. Butter a  2-quart gratin or other baking dish.    Scatter the raspberries evenly in the dish and sprinkle with the sugar and  the eau de vie.    For the batter, whip the whites with salt until they hold a very soft peak.  Beat in the sugar very slowly until the whites hold a firm peak. Whisk the  yolks until liquid and fold into the whites with the flour.    Spoon the batter over the raspberries in 8 to 10 separate mounds. Bake  about 10 minutes, until well risen and golden brown. Sprinkle with  confectioners sugar and serve immediately.
