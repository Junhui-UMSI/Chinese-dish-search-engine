Pesach Rolls
Salt, Water, Eggs, lightly beaten, Vegetable oil, Sugar, Matzoh Meal, 
Combine matzoh meal with salt and sugar. In a deep saucepan bring oil and  water to a boil. Add matzoh meal mixture and mix well. Beat in eggs  throughly, one at a time. allow to stand for 15 minutes. With oiled hands,  shape into rolls and place on greased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 F for 50  minutes or until golden brown Yields 10-12 rolls.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #054 by XXAZ71A@prodigy.com (MRS NAOMI R  SANDERS) on Feb 17, 1997.
