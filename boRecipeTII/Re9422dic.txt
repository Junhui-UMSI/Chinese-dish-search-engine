Pumpkin and Coconut Curry
Chilli powder, Pumpkin, Fresh green chillies, Dried shrimp paste, Ground turmeric, Black Pepper, Garlic, Salt, Onion, Hot water, Coconut cream, 
Place all ingredients, except pumpkin, into a saucepan and bring to a boil.  Lower heat and simmer for 5 minutes, Then add pumpkin, cover and simmer  until tender. Serve with boiled rice.
