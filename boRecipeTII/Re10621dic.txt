Quick Polenta
Water, Instant polenta (13 oz), Coarse salt, 
Bring water to vigorous boil and add salt. When polenta returns to  boil, pour polenta into pot in steady stream, stirring with wooden  spoon. Cook, stirring continuously 5 minutes or until mixture is  solid but still soft.    Pour onto oiled, 10 1/2x15 1/2" baking sheet. With wet hands or wet  spatula, pat polenta into smooth, flat rectangle. Let cool about 10  minutes or until firm.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.
