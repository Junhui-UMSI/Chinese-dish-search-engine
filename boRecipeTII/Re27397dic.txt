Toasted Pecan-Mushroom Pate
Jalapeno peppers, seeded &, Red bell pepper, roasted &, Mushroom caps, sliced, sliced, coarsely chopped, Salt, Lemon juice, Toasted pecans, Extra-firm silken tofu, Bread, quartered, 3/4" piece ginger, sliced, Chopped cilantro, 
Pre heat oven to 350F.  With food processor running, drop ginger & chilies  through feed tube & mince.  Add bread & pulse till it is reduced to crumbs.  Add mushrooms & pecan & puree. Add tofu, salt & lemon juice & blend well.  Add cilantro & red pepper & pulse until the pepper is finely chopped. Place  in an oiled 4 cup loaf pan & bake for 1 hour. Serve warm or at room  temperature.
