Almond Pumpkin Bread
Eggs, lightly beaten, Solid pack canned pumpkin, Almond extract, All-purpose flour, Baking soda, Granulated sugar, Undiluted evaporated milk, Baking Powder, Salt, Vegetable oil, Pumpkin pie spice, Toasted almonds, divided, 
Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, pumpkin pie spice and salt  in medium bowl.  Beat eggs, pumpkin, sugar, evaporated milk, extract and  oil in a medium bowl.  Add flour mixture and 1/2 cup almonds. Mix until  moistened.    Pour into greased 9x5 inch loaf pan and top with remaining nuts.  Bake at  350 degrees for 60 to 65 minutes.  Cool for 10 mintes and remove to wire  rack to cool completely.
