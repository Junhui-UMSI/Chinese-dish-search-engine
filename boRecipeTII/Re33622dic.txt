Swedish Pecan Balls
Salt, Sugar, Butter, Powdered sugar, Ground pecans, Vanilla, 
Combine all ingredients except powdered sugar. Mix well. shape into 1 inch  balls. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 300 degrees 25 to 30  minutes, but do not brown on bottom. Cool 1 minute. Roll in powdered sugar.  when cooled, roll in powdered sugar again.    Recipe by: Julie Evans    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #506 by JulieLPTA@aol.com on Mar 8, 1997.
