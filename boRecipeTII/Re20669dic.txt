Apple Crisp #01
Sliced apples, Powdered milk, Flour, Sugar, Salt, Cinnamon, Butter, Sugar, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, 
Mix 1/2 cup sugar with 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, pour over sliced apples in  1/2-quart casserole. Combine remaining ingredients and work together until  crumbly. Put on apples and bake at 325 for 45 minutes.    MRS J.C.
