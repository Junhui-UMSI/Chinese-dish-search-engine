Baked Cheese Cake
GARNISH, Granulated sugar, Chocolate sauce; strawberry, Vanilla extract, Lemon juice, Graham cracker crumbs, Salt, Eggs, Cream cheese, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spread butter lightly in a 10-inch - 12- inch  springform pan and sprinkle some graham cracker crumbs around the mould.  Mix the cream cheese and sugar together and beat it, add in the eggs one at  a time. Add vanilla extract, lemon juice and salt. Mix together. Pour  ingredients into buttered pan and place in roasting pan in hot water bath.  Bake about 60 to 75 minutes. Decorate with chocolate sauce, strawberry  sauce and fruits.
