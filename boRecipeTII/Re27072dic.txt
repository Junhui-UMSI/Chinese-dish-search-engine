Three-Pepper Pork Cutlets
Pork tenderloin, Salt, Dried whole rosemary,, Ground red pepper, White pepper, Vegetable cooking spray, Garlic, Black Pepper, Dried whole thyme, Dried whole oregano, Sweet hungarian paprika, olive oil, 
Trim fat from pork. Cut pork crosswise into 8 slices. Place between 2  sheets of heavy-duty plastic wrap, and flatten each slice to 1/4"  thickness, using a meat mallet or rolling pin. Combine paprika and next 9  ingredients; stir well. Rub pork with paprika mixture. Place on a rack  coated with cooking spray; place rack on a shallow roasting pan. Broil 5  inches from heat for 5 minutes. Turn pork over, and broil and additional 4  minutes or until done. Yield 4 servings.
