White Bean Florentine Soup (Manzolini)
Carrots; chopped, Onions; sliced, Celery, Water, Fresh basil, Parsley; to garnish, Spinach; coarsely chopped, Fresh Oregano, Small white or navy beans;, Tamari; to taste, 
In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, bring water to a boil and cook beans over  medium heat until they are soft and creamy, about 40 minutes. Add celery  and continue to cook for 20 minutes. Add more water if soup becomes too  thick.    Add herbs and cook 10 more minutes. Add spinach, season to taste with  tamari and garnish with fresh parsley.    *Serves 4-6.
