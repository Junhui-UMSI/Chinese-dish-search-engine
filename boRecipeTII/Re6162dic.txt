Pizza Rice
Parsley, Water, Basil, Quick Brown Rice, Parmesan cheese, Garlic, olive oil, Tomatoes; chopped, salt and pepper, Onion, Mushrooms; sliced, Oregano, Olives, sliced, (green or black), 
Saute vegetables and seasonings in olive oil.  Stir rice and vegetables  into a rectangular casserole dish, and pour water on top.  Bake at 400 F.  for 45 minutes; remove and sprinkle with cheese and olives; cover and  return to oven for an additional 10 minutes.
