Teriyaki Tempeh and Pineapple Salsa
Tamari, Mirin or sherry, Pineapple juice, Fresh ginger, Garlic, Red pepper flakes, Tempeh burgers, PINEAPPLE SALSA, Fresh pineapple, Red onion, Green bell pepper, Tomato, Garlic, Jalapeno pepper, Fresh cilantro, Ground coriander seed, Ground cumin, Salt, 
To make marinade, combine tamari, mirin, pineapple juice, ginger, garlic  and red pepper flakes in a shallow baking dish. Place tempeh burgers in  marinade and set aside 15 minutes. Turn burgers occasionally to coat with  marinade. Combine pineapple, red onion, green pepper, tomato, garlic,  jalapeno, cilantro and spices in a small bowl. Refrigerate until serving  time. Remove tempeh burgers from marinade and grill over medium-hot coals 5  minutes each side.  Serve immediately with Pineapple Salsa.
