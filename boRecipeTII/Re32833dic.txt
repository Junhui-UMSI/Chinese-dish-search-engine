Sweet Potato Yummy
Butter, Butter, Topping, Sweet milk, Brown sugar, Sweet potato, Pecans, Eggs, Sugar, Vanilla, Flour, 
Cook and mash sweet potatoes. Mix and put in a baking dish. For topping,  mix brown sugar, flour, 1/3 cup butter and pecans, sprinkle on top of sweet  potatoes. Bake at 350? for 25 minutes.
