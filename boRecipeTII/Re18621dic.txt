American-Oriental Chicken Soup
Cooked Chicken;, Hoisin sauce, Black Pepper, Fresh Ginger (2"); julienned, Salt, Sliced water chestnuts (8oz), Chicken Stock, Celery stalks, Soy sauce, Green onions, 
PUT THE CHICKEN STOCK in a soup pot and set over high heat. Add the ginger,  green onions and celery. Bring to the boil, reduce heat, and simmer 10  minutes. Add the chicken, soy sauce and hoisin sauce and simmer 5 minutes.  Add the water chestnuts. Taste for seasoning and add salt and pepper. Serve  hot. May be made ahead a day. This freezes fine for a leftover. Dont  freeze it for making an impression.    Makes 6 to 8 Servings    NATHALIE DUPREE    PRODIGY GUEST CHEFS COOKBOOK
