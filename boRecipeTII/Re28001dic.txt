Baked Chicken and Rice
Onion -- chopped, Pepper -- chopped, Chicken bouillon cubes, Cube, Butter or margarine, Rice -- uncooked, Green, Celery -- chopped, Water, Piece chicken, 
Bring water to boil in Dutch oven and then add bouillon cubes. Mix the  other ingredients, except chicken, with bouillon and water.   Season the chicken with seasoning salt and lay pieces on top of the rice  mixture. Cook 1 1/2 hours at 400 degrees.    Recipe By     :
