Quick and Easy Peach Cobbler
Peaches, canned, Peaches, canned, liquid only, Flour, milk, butter, Sugar, Egg, 
Drain peaches. Put peaches in baking dish with the liquid from one can of  peaches. Mix the other ingredients together and pour over peaches. Bake at  350 degrees until crust is brown.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
