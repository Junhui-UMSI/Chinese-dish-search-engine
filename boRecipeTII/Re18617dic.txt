American Style Pilsner
Saaz hops -- finishing, Vierka lager yeast, Hallertauer hops -- boil, Superbrau light malt, 45 min, Priming sugar, 
Boild one gal of water, add syrup, stir until dissolved.  Return to heat.  Add 3 oz hallertauer hops and boild for 30-45 min.  In last 5-10 minutes of  boil, add the saaz hops.  Add to 4 gal of cold water.  When cooled to below  80 degrees, pitch yeast. Fermentation at 45-55 degrees should go as  follows: 10-14 days in primary fermenter, 2-3 weeks in secondary and 6  weeks in the bottle.
