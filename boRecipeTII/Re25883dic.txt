Back Country Ham Slice for 6
Fat, Salt, Ham slices; 3/4 inch thick, Paprika, Prepared mustard, Brown sugar, Water, 
Fry ham in fat. Remove ham slice(s0 from skillet. Pour all the other  ingredients into skillet, cook 5 minutes, place ham slice(s) back into  sauce and cook for 5 minutes more. Serve. Serves 3.
