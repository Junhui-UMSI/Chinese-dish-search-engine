Asian Tuna Steaks
Sesame seeds, Fresh ginger, Lite soy sauce, Sesame oil, Orange juice, Scallions, Tuna steaks, 
In a stainless steel bowl or plastic resealable bag, combine first 6  ingredients. Add the tuna and let marinate for 20 minutes. Broil or grill  the tuna 6 inches from the heat source for 4-5 minutes per side. Cook until  done as desired (some people prefer their tuna more rare than others do).    Per serving: calories 221, fat 10.7g, 45% calories from fat, cholesterol  43mg, protein 27.3g, carbohydrates 2.4g, fiber 0.2g, sodium 354mg.    Exchanges: 3 Lean Meat Exchange.
