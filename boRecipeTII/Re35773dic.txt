Stuffed Crab
Crabmeat, Sherry, Garlic, Salt, Fresh Lemon Juice, Butter, Freshly gground pepper, Seasoned breadcrumbs, Butter, Half-and-half, Chopped ;onion, Hard-cooked egg; mashed, Chopped green onion, Minced fresh parsley, Hot pepper sauce, 
Preheat oven to 400*F. Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add  onions and cook until soft. remove from heat and add egg, crab,halfand  half, parsley, garlic,salt pepper, pepper sauce and half the breadcrumbs  and stir well. Return to heat and cook for about 5 minutes. Remove from  heat and stir in Sherry. Stuff into 4 crab shells or divide among  ramekins). Adjust seasoning. Sprinkle with remaining breadcrumbs and lemon  juice. Top each with a pat of butter. Bake until tops are golden brown.  Serve hot or cold. Makes 4 servings.
