Tortellini Al Brodo
Parmesan cheese, Veal-stuffed tortellini, Chicken stock (see index), Chopped fresh parsley, 
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add the tortellini, stir,  and boil until tender and doubled in size, about 15 minutes. Drain and  rinse in cold water.    2. Place the Chicken Stock in a 2-qt. saucepan and heat to simmering.    3. Add the drained tortellini to the broth.  Allow to come back up to heat.    4. Ladle into individual bowls. Sprinkle the parsley and Parmesan cheese  over.    AMBROSINOS    NORTH SCOTTSDALE RD., SCOTTSDALE    WINE:  AMARONE    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of Italian Recipes>.
