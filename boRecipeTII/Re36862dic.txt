Strawberry Rhubarb Tart
Unsalted butter; cut into, Sheet frozen puff pastry, Rhubarb; thawed if frozen,, Cinnamon, Cornstarch, Sugar; more if rhubarb is, All purpose flour, Strawberries; hulled and, Nutmeg, 
Preheat oven to 400F. Bake puff pastry sheet according to package  directions. Combine strawberries and rhubarb in a mixing bowl. Combine next  5 ingredients in another bowl. Fold dry ingredients into strawberry and  rhubarb mixture. Stir in butter and toss. Transfer fruit mixture to a  buttered deep 9 inch baking dish. Bake 40-45 minutes or fruit is bubbly.  Spoon fruit mixture onto baked puff pastry sheet and serve. This recipe  serves 6 people. Because this recipe is for a particular size pan, it  adjusts the number of servings only in multiples of 6.    Per serving: calories 513, fat 17.6g, 30% calories from fat, cholesterol  16mg, protein 4.3g, carbohydrates 87.1g, fiber 4.1g, sodium 199mg.
