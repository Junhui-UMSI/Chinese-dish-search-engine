Friendship Tea
Instant tea, Powdered orange drink, (1 qt.) pkg. dry lemonade, Ground cloves, Sugar, Cinnamon, 
Mix all and store in a jar. To serve: 1 1/2 - 2 teaspoons to 1 cup boiling  water. This makes a large amount so you will want to share with a friend.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by mark@alexr.co.uk on Oct 28, 1998
