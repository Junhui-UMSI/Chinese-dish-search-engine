Grand Marnier, Butter, Heavy cream, Semisweet chocolate, 
Recipe by: Mimi Markofsky From: AUNTIEFIFI@aol.com    Date: Wed, 8 May 1996 23:32:40 -0400  In a heavy saucepan, reduce cream over a medium heat by 1/2.  Add the Grand  Marnier and chocolate; stir until melted. Remove pan from heat and stir in  butter.  Place in a shallow glass dish and refrigerate until firm, 2-4  hours (may be done to this point, covered with plastic wrap and  refrigerated 3-4 days in advance).    Scoop with #100 scoop and roll into cocoa, nuts or tempered chocolate.  Refrigerate to firm again before serving.
