Artichoke Oyster Bisque
Celery, Butter, (14-oz) artichoke hearts;, (10.75-oz) cream of mushroom, Half and half, Jars (8-oz) fresh oysters;, Garlic salt, Chopped fresh parsley, Chopped green onion tops, Gumbo file, Fresh sliced mushrooms, Onions, (14-oz) artichoke hearts;, (14-oz) artichoke hearts;, 
In the butter, saute onion, 1 can of chopped artichoke hearts,  mushrooms,celery, parsley, green onion tops and seasonings for 10 minutes,  stirring often. In a large pan, combine mushroom soup and Half and Half.  Add onion-artichoke mixture and simmer over low heat for 30 minutes. Add  quartered artichoke hearts, oysters and gumbo file and continue to heat for  10 more minutes. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.    MARY LORRAINE WYATT    (MRS. GEORGE COX)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
