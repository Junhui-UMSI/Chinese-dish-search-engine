Barbecue Chicken and Andouille Pasta
Bell pepper; cut in strips, Salt, Black pepper, Andouille sausage, Chicken breast; cut into, Cilantro leaves, Cayenne pepper, Green Onions, Red onions; cut in strips, Marinara sauce, Corn oil, Cumin, Provolone cheese, Smoked gouda cheese, GARNISH, Rigatoni pasta, Garlic, Hickory smoked barbecue, 
1. Season chicken with salt, black pepper, cayenne and cumin.    2. Satu? chicken and andouille in corn oil until chicken loses raw color.    3. Add bell pepper, onion and garlic. Cook until vegetables are limp.    4. Add marinara and barbecue sauce. Bring to a boil.    5. Place cooked rigatoni pasta in a bowl. Pour Satu?ed mixture on top of  pasta.    6. Top with Provolone and Gouda cheeses. Garnish with green onions and  cilantro leaves.
