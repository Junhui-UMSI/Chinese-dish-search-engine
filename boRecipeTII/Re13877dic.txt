Wilton Buttercream Icing
confectioner's sugar, Butter extract, Milk, Solid vegetable shortening, 
From:    Julie Sterchi <sterchi@WABASH.NET>    Date:    Sun, 18 Aug 1996 00:05:55 -0700  Cream shortening with electric mixer.  Add butter extract.  Gradually add  sugar, one cup at a time, beating well on medium speed.  Scrape sides and  bottom of bowl often.  When all sugar has been mixed in, icing will appear  dry.  Add milk and beat at medium speed until light and fluffy.  Keep icing  covered with a damp cloth until ready to use.  For best results, keep icing  bowl in refrigerator when not in use.  Refrigerated in an airtight contain,  this icing can be stored 2 weeks.  Rewhip before using.  Yield: 3 cups.  **Add 3-4 tblesp. light corn syrup per recipe to thin if needed.
