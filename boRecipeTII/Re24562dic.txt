Asparagus Tip Pasta
black olives, Fresh asparagus tips, Fusilli pasta, Fresh Lemon Juice, black pepper, olive oil, Sweet butter, Kasseri, Salt, 
Cook pasta according to directions. While cooking, melt butter in saute pan  over medium high heat. Add asparagus tips and cook briefly, about 4  minutes. Drain pasta and toss with olive oil. Add asparagus, butter,  olives, mustard, lemon juice and salt and pepper. Top with freshly grated  cheese.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 16,  1998
