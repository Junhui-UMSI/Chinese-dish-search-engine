Pollo Con Marsala E Funghi
Light cream, Thyme leaves, Chicken Stock, Sliced Portobelo mushrooms, Salt, 4-oz boneless; skinless, Green peppercorns, Flour, Marsala wine, Butter, 
Chicken with Sweet Italian wine and Mushrooms    Here is a version of it from Chef Ks Divine Cuisine Cards, check out the  web site. WWW.DIVINECUISINE.COM    Place the chicken breasts in a sauce pan. Cover with the wine, stock and  the seasonings and simmer gently for 20 minutes. Remove and reserve hot.  Increase the temperature under the sauce pan and bring the broth to a boil,  reduce to 1 cup (250 ml). Strain through a cheese cloth.    Heat the butter in a 2nd sauce pan, saute the mushrooms until all the  moisture has evaporated. Add the flour, cook for 2 minutes over low heat.  Add the strained broth along with the cream. Simmer for 5 minutes. Serve.    SERVES 6    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by RKalenuik <RKalenuik@AOL.COM> on Dec 5, 1997
