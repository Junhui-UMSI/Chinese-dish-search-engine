Baked Stuffed Clams
Parsley flakes, , Oregano, Old Bay Seasoning, Garlic powder, hot sauce, Paprika, Fine dry breadcrumbs, Chowder clams, Butter, Lemon & pepper seasoning, 
Shuck and chop clams, strain and reserve liquid.  Melt butter. in  pan. Add crumbs, parsley, lemon pepper seasoning, garlic powder,  oregano, and hot sauce; mix well.  If mix is too dry, moisten with  clam liquor. Fill 12 clam shells with clam mix.  Sprinkle paprika and  crab spice over top. Bake at 400 degrees for 12 - 15 min.
