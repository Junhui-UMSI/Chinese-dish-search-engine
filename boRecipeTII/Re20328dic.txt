Veal Roll Stuffed
White wine, Veal fattless meat, Cooked ham, Dutch cheese sliced, 
lay the cheese, the ham and the spinach inside the veal meet, the roll it  and tied it with sone string. in a sucepan put a little olive oil and on  medium fire cook in every part the roll, it should not take more the a few  minutes, at that point pour the wine and let it cook for 1 hour with the  lid on, checking from time to time the cooking.
