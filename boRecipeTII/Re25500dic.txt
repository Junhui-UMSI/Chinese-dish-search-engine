Avgolemono Sauce
Warm chicken stock or, Eggs, Lemon juice, 
Beat eggs in top of a double boiler. Gradually blend in juice and stock.  Cook over hot water until thickened. Beat until foamy. Serve hot over  vegetables. Calories: 9 per Tablespoon. Yield: 2 cups.    LOW CALORIE    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
