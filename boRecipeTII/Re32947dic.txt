Sweet Tidbits Snack Mix
Honey roasted peanuts, Butter, Banana chips, Multi bran chex cereal, Flaked coconut, optional, Seedless or golden raisins, Honey, 
combine butter and honey in large microwave-safe bowl. microwave on high 1  to 1 1/2 min. or until melted. mix well. gradually add cereal, banana  chips, coconut, and peanuts stiring until all pieces are evenly coated.  microwave on high 3 to 4 min. stiring every min. stir in raisins. spread on  waxed paper to cool. store in airtight container. recipe from "Ralston  kitchens"
