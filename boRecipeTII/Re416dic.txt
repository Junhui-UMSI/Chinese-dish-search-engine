Lemon Filling for Cream Puffs
Egg yolk; beaten, Sugar, Lemon juice, Lemon zest, Speck salt, (rounded) cornstarch, Cold water, Water; hot, 
submitted by: tpogue@ids2.idsonline.com  Beat eggs. Beat in lemon juice, zest, salt and sugar. Combine well using  hand held mixer. Add cornstarch mixture (the cold water and cornstarch)  then add the hot water Cook over double boiler for 20 minutes til  thickened.  An instant read will register 160 degrees. Put plastic wrap on  top of hot custard and cool    Recipe Archive - 29 July 96    From the RECIPEinternet: Recipes from Around the World recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet, G Internet.
