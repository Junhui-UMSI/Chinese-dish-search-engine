Warm Chicken and Spinach Salad
Cherry tomatoes, Fresh spinach, rinsed and, Bottled red-wine vinegar and, Thinly sliced onion, Water, Strips bacon, breast halves (15 oz), oil dressing, tough stems removed, Boneless/skinless chicken, 
Cook bacon in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until crisp, 5 to  6 mintues.  Drain on paper towels, then crumble bacon.  Pour off all but 2  teaspoons drippings.    Add tomatoes and onions to skillet and stir-fry 2 to 3 minutes until  tomato skins just start to split and onions are crisp-tender.  Transfer to  a large serving bowl and add the spinach.    Add chicken to skillet and cook 7 to 8 minutes, turning once, until golden  brown and opaque at center.  Remove to a cutting board.    Add dressing and water to skillet, bring to a simmer and remove from heat.    Cut chicken crosswise in 3/4" wide slices; add to bowl.  Add hot dressing  and bacon.  Toss to mix and coat.    Per serving: 239 cal, 10g fat, 28 g pro, 411 mg sod, 67 mg chol.    Reprinted from Womans Day Magazine, Sept. 1, 1996.
