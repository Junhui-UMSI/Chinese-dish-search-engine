Almond Chocolate Mousse
Heavy cream, Semisweet chocolate; chopped, Almond extract, Sugar, 
1. Scald 1/2 cup of the heavy cream with the sugar, stirring to dissolve  the sugar. Pour the hot sweetened cream over the chopped chocolate in a  large bowl and stir until the chocolate is melted and the mixture is  smooth. Set aside to cool completely.    2. When the chocolate mixture is cool, whip the remaining 1 1/4 cups of  heavy cream with the almond extract just until stiff; do not overbeat. Fold  the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture. Use as soon as possible.  Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Chef K K <chef-kk@JUNO.COM> on Nov 19, 1997
