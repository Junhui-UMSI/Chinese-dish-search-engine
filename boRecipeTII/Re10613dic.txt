Quick Pigs-In-A-Blanket
Water, cabbage, Onions, Ground beef, Salt, Pepper, Parmesan cheese, rice, uncooked, Tomato soup, 
Chop cabbage into medium size pieces. Spread in a 9x13" pan. Brown ground  beef and onion. Drain. Stir in rice, salt and pepper. Spoon over cabbage.  Heat tomato soup with water. Pour over all. Sprinkle with grated cheese.  Cover with foil. Bake for 1 1/2 hours at 350 F. Fluff with fork. Can  Microwave on High for 20-30 minutes, covered with plastic wrap.
