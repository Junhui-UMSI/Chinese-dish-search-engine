Polonaise Butter
Dry bread crumbs, Salt and pepper -- to taste, Hard-boiled eggs -- chopped, Fine, Butter -- See Recipe, Fluid ounces  meuniere, 
Brown the bread crumbs slightly in the butter. Add the eggs and season with  salt and pepper if needed. Pour over vegetables or pasta.    Recipe By     : Locke-Ober, Boston, MA    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
