Peanut Patties
Raw peanuts, Butter, Vanilla, Red food coloring, White Karo Syrup, milk, Sugar, 
Combine sugar, milk, Karo and peanuts together; bring to a boil. Cook on  low heat for 1 hour and 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from  heat; add butter, vanilla and food coloring. Beat until thick and holds  shape.  Drop from spoon onto greased waxed paper.    YIELD: 50 patties    SOURCE: 1979 New Orleans Times-Picayune Recipe Contest Cookbook Typed for  you by Nancy Coleman
