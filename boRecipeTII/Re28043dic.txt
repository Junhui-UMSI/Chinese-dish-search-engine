Baked Chicken Salad
Almonds; blanched, Mayonnaise, Onion, Cheddar cheese cubes, Potato chips; whole, Celery; diced, Chicken; cooked, diced, Lemon; sliced peeled, 
Heat oven to 375F. Grease a 2-quart casserole. Put chicken and celery into  casserole. Blender - chop nuts and add to chicken. Put 2 cups of potato  chips into blender container, cover and process 4 cycles at (stir). Empty  onto wax paper and set aside. Repeat with remaining chips. Put remaining  ingredients into container, cover and process at (blend) until smooth. Add  to chicken and mix well. Sprinkle potato chip crumbs over top and bake for  30 minutes. A cycle is defined as a 1 second pulse operation with a pause  for food to settle before repeating.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1057 by "M. Hicks" <nitro_ii@email.msn.com>  on Jan 29, 1998
