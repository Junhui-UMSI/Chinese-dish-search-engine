Pineapple Waldorf Salad
Red apple, Celery, Lemon peel, Brown sugar, Tart apple, Vanilla, Walnuts, Cinnamon, Plain yogurt, pineapple, 
Drain pineapple. Combine with apples, walnuts and celery. Combine remaining  ingredients and stir into pineapple mixture. Chill.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Julie Bertholf <jewel1@ix.netcom.com> on  Feb 24, 1998
