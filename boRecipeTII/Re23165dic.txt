Tuna Au Poivre
Lime juice, Soy sauce, Sugar, Through a press, Fresh tuna, Clove garlic; peeled and put, To 3 teaspoons coarsely, Black Pepper, 
1. Combine the soy sauce, lime juice, garlic and sugar. Put the tuna into a  glass dish and pour the marinade over the fish. Marinate 1-2 hours in  refrigerator. 2. Remove the fish from the marinade, discard marinade and  place on a rack (or broiling pan) in a foiled lined pan. Sprinkle the  pepper on top of the fish, patting gently to adhere. Bake in a preheated  450-degree oven until cooked through..
