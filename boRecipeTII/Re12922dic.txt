Zesty Broccoli Casserole
Butter, Cheddar cheese, Egg, Cracker crumbs, Frozen chopped broccoli, Cream of mushroom soup, milk, Mayonnaise, Horseradish, 
Cook broccoli according to package directions. Drain. Combine soup, cheese,  egg, mayonnaise, milk & horseradish. Stir in broccoli. Spoon into greased  2-quart casserole. Combine melted butter & cracker crumbs. Sprinkle over  top. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
