Toilet Bowl Cleaner
Water, to 10c, CHEAPER AND BETTER, Bleach, 
Mix. Let sit in bowl 30 mins with lid CLOSED.  (PETS) Keep away from  ammonia products. 16-18 oz    I keep a spray bottle with bleach and water handy for countertops and  sinks. Works great.
