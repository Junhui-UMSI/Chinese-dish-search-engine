Baked Catfish Ala Meuniere
Lemon juice, Hot pepper sauce, Green onions,chopped, MEUNIERE SAUCE, Lemon and herb seasoning, White pepper, Catfish fillets, Egg, Salt, Parsley,minced, Cracker meal, Worcestershire Sauce, Water, Parmesan cheese, Butter or margarine, 
1. Thaw frozen fish according to package directions.   2. Beat egg and water.   3. Combine cracker meal, cheese and seasoning; dip fish fillet in beaten  egg, then roll in crumb mixture.   4. Pour Meuniere Sauce in shallow baking dish; place fillets in dish,  turning once or twice to coat both sides well.   5. Bake in preheated 350F. oven 40 minutes, or until fish flakes easily.   *** MEUNIERE SAUCE ***   1. Combine all ingredients and simmer 5 minutes.
