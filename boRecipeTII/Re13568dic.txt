Wild About Pumpkin Orange Muffins
Canned pumpkin, Ground cinnamon, GARNISH, Mace, Walnuts; halved, Sugar, Grated rind of, DRY STUFF (SMALL BOWL, Vegetable oil, Apple juice; or, Orange, Pecans, WET STUFF (BIG BOWL, Eggs, Apple cider, Baking Powder, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Salt, Ground cloves, Chopped walnuts; or, 
Preheat oven to 400F (200C) and and prepare muffin tin.    Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet (larger bowl) until just  mixed.    Spoon into prepared pan and decorate top of each muffin with a walnut or  pecan half.    Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
