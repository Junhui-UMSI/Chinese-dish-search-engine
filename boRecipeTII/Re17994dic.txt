Vegetarian Reuben
Sauerkraut, Swiss cheese, rye bread, Dijon mustard, Russian salad dressing, Ripe tomatoes, 
Preheat the oven to 350. Toast the rye bread and spread one side of each  slice with mustard. Place the slices mustard side up in a single layer on a  cookie sheet. Layer each slice of rye with the following in order: 1 slice  tomato, 1 slice Swiss cheese, 1/4 cup sauerkraut and 1 tablespoon Russian  dressing. Bake for 20 minutes or until sauerkraut is hot. Top each sandwich  with a slice of tomato and a half-slice of cheese. Bake for another 5 to 10  minutes or until the cheese is melted.    Recipe By     : Claires Corner Copia  in New Haven Conn.
