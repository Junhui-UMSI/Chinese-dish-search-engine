The Worlds Simplest Candy
Semisweet chocolate chips, Walnuts, 
Place the chips in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave on high for 1 to 2  min., or until melted, stirring halfway through cooking. Stir in the nuts  or fruit (or a combination). Drop by teaspoonful onto a waxed paper-lined  baking sheet and refrigerate until firm.    Copyright credit: 1996 by The Walt Disney Company ? 1996 Lifetime  Entertainment Services.
