Apple Cake (Flourless)
Dry yeast, Sugar, nuts, Raisins, , apples, Egg yolks, Juice of one lemon, 
Mix all ingredients.  Beat egg whites to stiff peaks and add them carefully  to the mixture.  Bake as any cake in a floured, oiled pan.    Recipe By     : suzana@amhost.amcham.com.br    From: Ladies Home Journal- August 1991    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
