Ultimate Chocolate Chunk Cookies
Vanilla, Baking soda, Semi sweet chocolate, Egg, Brown sugar, Gran. sugar chunks, Flour, Softened butter, 
Preheat oven to 375. Mix dry ingredients in small bowl (except chips). In  large bowl mix moist ingredients, then add dry ingredients. When blended  add chips. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto UNgreased cookie sheets 2 inches  apart. Bake 8 to 10 minutes. Makes 36 to 48 really good cookies.
