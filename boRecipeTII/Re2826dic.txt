Peanut Butter Crumble Cake
Sugar, Egg, Cinnamon, Salt, Soured milk **, Chunky peanut butter, Baking Powder, Butter, Baking soda, Raisins, Ground ginger, Flour, Light brown sugar, 
Heat oven to 350. Put flour, sugars & cinnamon in 8-9" ungreased  baking pan.  Stir with fork to mix well. Add butter & crumble. Set  aside 3/4 cup. Add baking powder, soda & salt. Stir to blend. Add  milk & egg & stir until blended. Stir peanut butter into reserved  crumb mixture. Sprinkle over batter. Bake 25-30 minutes. Cool in pan.  ** To sour milk: 2 tsp. lemon juice in glass measure. Fill with milk  to 3/4 cup mark.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
