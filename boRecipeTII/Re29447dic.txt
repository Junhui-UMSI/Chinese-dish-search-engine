Bakers Truffles
Egg yolk, Vanilla, Unsalted butter, Nuts; finely chopped OR, 
Melt chocolate in saucepan over very low heat, stirring constantly; cool.  Cream butter with egg yolk. Gradually add sugar, blending well. Stir in  chocolate and vanilla. Chill until firm enough to handle. Shape into balls  about 1" in diameter.  Roll in nuts; chill. Store in refrigerator. Makes  about 30 candies. I havent tried this recipe.
