Pitchers Mounds
8-ounce paper or plastic, (4-serving size) Jell-O, Cold milk, -(up to), Cool Whip whipped topping;, (16-oz) chocolate sandwich, 
PREPARE pudding mix with milk as directed on package. Let stand 5 minutes.  Fold in whipped topping and 1/2 of the crushed cookies.     PLACE about 1 tablespoon crushed cookies in each cup. Fill cups about 3/4  full with pudding mixture. Top with remaining crushed cookies. Chill until  set, about 1 hour. Place toy sports figure in center of each "mound," if  desired.     MAKES 8 to 10 servings, Prep time: 15 minutes, Chill time: 1 hour    From <Favorite Recipes: Jell-O Easy Entertaining>.  Downloaded from Glens  MM Recipe Archive, G Internet.
