Pumpkin Seed Bread
Honey, Salt, Bread flour, Yeast, Water, Yeast*, Olive or Vegetable oil, FOR 1 1/2 POUND LOAF, Unsalted raw pumpkin seeds, FOR 2 POUND LOAF, Honey, Plus 2 tablespoons Water, Salt, Bread flour, Olive or Vegetable oil, Unsalted raw pumpkin seeds, 
Measure carefully, placing all ingredients in bread machine pan in the  order recommended by the manufacturer.    Select Basic/White cycle. Use medium or light crust color. Remove baked  bread from pan and cool on a wire rack.    *Yeast amount is correct through proportionately less than that called for  in the 1 1/2 pound recipe.
