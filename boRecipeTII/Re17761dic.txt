Almond, Bacon and Cheddar Sandwich
Finely chopped toasted, Crisp bacon, Salt, Wheat bread, Chopped green onions or, Grated Cheddar cheese (pack, Mayonnaise, 
Finely chop almonds, crumble bacon, and add rest of ingredients and blend.  Spread generously on wheat bread.  Yield: 3 cups filling.    ANN EVANS (MRS. THOMAS)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
