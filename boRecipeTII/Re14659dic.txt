"Sinful Cherry" Liqueur
Sugar, One quart jars, Fifth V.O., Bing cherries, Lemon, 
Fill each jar half full with cherries. Add to each one slice lemon and one  tablespoon sugar. Then fill to the top with V.O. Put the lid on tight,  shake and put in cool place for 6 months.
