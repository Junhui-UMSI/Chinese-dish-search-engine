Baked French Onion Veggies
Some whole mushrooms, French onion soup mix, Onion, Approx 1 1/2 cups water, Carrots, Parsnip, Sweet potato, Garlic, Any kind of beans (green;, Mixed herbs, Potatoes, 
Preheat oven to fairly hot, chop all veggies and put in an oven bag. Mix  soup packets with water, pour onto veggies and seal bag. Put in oven for  approx. 30 mins or until veggies are tender.    Serves: 4  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Bunny" <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Mar 20,  1998
