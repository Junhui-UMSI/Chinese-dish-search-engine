Quick Kringles
Canned Pastry filling, Butter, Sour cream, Flour, 
Blend flour and butter with hand blender as for a pie crust. Add sour  cream. Chill. Cut dough into 4 pieces. Roll out into rectangle shapes in  plenty of flour between waxed paper. Cover center third of each with  filling and fold 1/3 on each side over. Place on greased cookie sheet. Bake  350 degrees F. for 30 to 35 minutes. When cool, dribble with icing. Icing:  2 Tb soft butter , 1 1/2 c Powdered sugar, Milk Mix butter and sugar. Add  just enough milk for dribbling consistency.    Note: Canned pastry filling comes in flavors like almond, apricot, poppy  seed and quite a few more in the baking section of your grocery store.
