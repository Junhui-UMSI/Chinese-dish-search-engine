Asian Salad Dressing
Soy sauce, Lemon, Ginger root, Garlic, Water, Vinegar, Vegetable oil, Onion, Tomato paste, 
Date: Fri, 22 Mar 1996 13:55:39 +1100    From: sherae@zeta.org.au (Sheri McRae)  Soak ginger root in cold water for a few minutes before removing outer  skin. Remove skin and cut in chunks. Combine all ingredients in food  processor with steel blade and blend until smooth. Refrigerate.    Often in Japanese restaurants this dressing is served over finely shredded  cabbage along with the lettuce and other salad ingredients. It is  delicious.    Per serving: 383 Calories; 41g Fat (94% calories from fat); 2g Protein; 5g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 1487mg Sodium    NOTES : This is the salad dressing served at the Mitsukoshi Restaurant at  EPCOT Center.
