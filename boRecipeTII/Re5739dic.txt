LEMON FRESH, JUICE; PINEAPPLE #3, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, WATER; COLD, ICE, WATER; HOT, 
1.  DISSOLVE SUGAR IN WATER.  COOL.    2.  ADD LEMON AND PINEAPPLE JUICES AND WATER TO SUGAR SOLUTION.  MIX THOROUGHLY.  COVER AND REFRIGERATE.    3.  ADD ICE JUST BEFORE  SERVING.  :  ** ALL NOTES ARE PER 100 PORTIONS.    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 2, 12 OZ LEMONS A.P. (3 LEMONS) WILL YIELD 1/2  CUP JUICE.    NOTE:  2.  IN STEP 2, 2 TBSP FROZEN CONCENTRATED LEMON JUICE AND        6    TBSP COLD WATER MAY BE USED.
