Raspberry-Fig Breakfast Bar Recipe (And Fig Puree)
BREAKFAST BAR RECIPE, FIG PUREE, Brown sugar, Oats, Raspberry jam, Butter or margarine, Baking Powder, Water, California dried figs, Honey, Flour, 
FIG PUREE: Combine figs, water and honey in a saucepan; bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes or until softened.  Using knife  blade in food processor bowl; add fig mixture.  Process 10 seconds or  until smooth.    Preheat oven to 250 F.  Mix all ingredients except jam.  Form into  "crumbs".  Press half of mixture into pan sprayed with PAM.  Spread jam on  crumb crust.  Form top layer with remaining mixture.  Bake 50-60 minutes.
