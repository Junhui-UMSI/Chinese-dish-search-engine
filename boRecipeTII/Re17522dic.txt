Almond Cookies
Vanilla extract, Confectioners sugar, Salt, Stick margarine, Flour, Slivered almonds, 
preheat oven to 350. cream sugar and margarine,add rest of ingrediants till  just crumbly.shape into crescents and place on greased cookie sheet. bake  for 20 minutes. sprinkle with confectioners suger. I always need to double  or triple this recipe ( they go too fast in my house!)  Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #326 by "Aliza Blizinsky"  <alizab@hotmail.com> on Dec 16, 1997
