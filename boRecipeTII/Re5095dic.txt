Pico De Gallo Salsa Puerto Nuevo
Cilantro, Serrano peppers, Tomatoes, garlic, Lemon, Onion, 
~ CILANTRO: "1 handful (no more than that)" - Combine chilies, tomatoes,  onion, cilantro and garlic. - Mix and squeeze lemon halves over mixture,  making sure no seeds get in salsa. - Serve ar room temperature.
