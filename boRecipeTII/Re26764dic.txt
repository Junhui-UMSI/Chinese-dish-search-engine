Baked Apple Pancake
Sugar, Salt, Vegetable oil, Lemon, Sweet butter, Egg whites, apples, Granny Smith, Beer, Ground cinnamon, Light brown sugar, Sugar, All-purpose flour, Egg yolks, 
Sift together the flour,1 1/2 tsp. sugar and the salt in a large mixing  bowl.Add beer and stir until smooth.Beat in one yolk,then the other.In a  separate bowl,beat whites with 1 tbsp. sugar until they form peaks.Fold  into the batter.Mix together the remaining sugar, light brown sugar and  cinnamon,reserving 2 tsp.of this mixture. Squeeze lemon over the apples and  toss with sugar-cinnamon mixture until coated.Heat the butter and oil in a  9 to 10" oven proof pan or skillet.Pour in half of the batter and cover  with all apples.Pour in the rest of the batter and bake in a preheated 350  degree oven,on the middle rack,for 60 minutes or until the apple pancake is  puffed up and golden.To serve,loosen sides and bottom with a metal spatula,  sliding onto hot plate.Sprinkle with remaining sugar-cinnamon mixture. Cut  into wedges.Serves 2 generously.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #141 by "John Weber" <hdbrer@ibm.net> on May  20, 97
