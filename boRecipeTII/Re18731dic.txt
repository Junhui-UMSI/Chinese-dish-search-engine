Anadama Bread
Yeast, Yellow cornmeal, Butter, Boiling water, 1 1/2 Lb Loaf:, Salt, Bread flour, Molasses, 
Place cornmeal into a bowl. Carefully pour boiling  water into cornmeal,  stirring to make sure it is  smooth. Let stand for about 30 minutes. Stir  in molasses, salt and butter. Place yeast into the abm pan, bread flour,  then cornmeal mixture. Select white bread and push start. NOTE: An early  American recipe.
