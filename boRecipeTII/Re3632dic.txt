Pegs Pimiento Pigs
Nutmeg,ground, Parsley sprigs(opt), American cheese,shredded, Flour, Salt, Pimientos(7oz), Butter or margarine, milk, Pork sausage links(12oz), 
1. Brown sausage links in a skillet.    2. Wrap each browned link in a strip of pimiento; arrange in a buttered  shallow baking dish in a single layer.    3. Melt butter in a saucepan; stir in flour to blend.    4. Add milk, stirring constantly; cook until slightly thickened and smooth.    5. Add salt, nutmeg and cheese.    6. Pour sauce over sausage; bake in preheated 350F. oven 15 minutes.    7. Garnish with sprigs of parsley if desired.
