Apricot-Date Jam
Pitted dates, Unsweetened pineapple juice, Lemon juice, Dried apricots, 
Soak apricots in pineapple juice overnight Blenderize all ingredients Serve  as is on toast or thin with more pineapple juice to make a softer spread  for waffles or pancakes    Makes about 3 cups **Natures Pantry Cookbook**
