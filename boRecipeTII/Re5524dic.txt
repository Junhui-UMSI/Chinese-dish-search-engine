Pineapple Limeade
Pineapple juice, Sugar, Lime juice, Sparkling Water; Chilled, 
Mix all ingredients except sparling water; refrigerate until chilled. Just  before serving, stir in sparkling water.  Serve over ice.  Garnish with  lime slices, if desired.
