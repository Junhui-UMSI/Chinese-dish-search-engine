President Clintons Pound Cake
Butter, Buttermilk, Eggs, Flour, Vanilla, Sugar, 
Cream butter and sugar by beating til smooth in a large bowl. Add eggs, 1  at a time, beating well after each egg. Add flour, 1/3 at a time,  alternating w/ 1/3 of buttermilk. Add vanilla. Pour batter into greased and  floured tube pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hr and 25 mins.
