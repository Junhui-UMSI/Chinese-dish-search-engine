Apple Raisin Stuffed Pork Chops
Spicy-brown mustard, Boneless pork chops, Boiling water, Margarine, Brown sugar, Peppridge Farm Apple & Raisin Stuffing Mix, Margarine, 
Source: Pepperidge Farm Ad    1. Mix boiling water, 2 T. margarine and contents of seasoning/fruit pouch  until margarine is melted. Add contents of stuffing pouch. Mix lightly. Let  stand 5 minutes.    2. Cut a side pocket in each of the chops. Spoon stuffing into pockets.  Place chops in 2-qt. shallow baking dish. Cover.    3. Bake at 350 F. for 25 minutes. Uncover.    4. Mix melted margarine, sugar and mustard. Spoon over chops and continue  baking uncovered or until chops are done.
