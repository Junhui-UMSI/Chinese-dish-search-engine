Tomato and Peanut Butter Bouillon
Worcestershire Sauce, Peanut Butter, Smooth, Lemon juice, Sherry, Tomato juice, Beef Bouillon, 
Combine and warm all ingredients but the sherry in a saucepan. Blend  thoroughly. Once thoroughly blended, cook slowly for about 30 minutes,  stirring frequently. Stir in the sherry and serve.
