White Jello
Sugar, Almond extract -- OR, Vanilla, Combine, Cream cheese, 
Gradually add 1/2 cup milk.  Dissolve 1 envelope unflavored  gelatin in 1/4  cup of water.  Add boiling water to make one cup.Cool slightly.   Stir  jello into cheese mixture. Chill until a little thick and then fold in 1  cup of cool whip (whipped Cream). Chill in 11/2 quart mold.
