Avocado Sauce
Coarse salt, Red onion, Jalapeno chiles, Avocados, Juice of 1 lime, Black pepper, Tomato, olive oil, Fresh cilantro leaves, 
Cut the avocados into quarters. Remove the seeds, peel and place in a  mixing bowl. Mash with a potato masher or fork. Add the remaining  ingredients and beat with a fork to mix thoroughly.
