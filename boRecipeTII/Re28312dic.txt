Baked Grits
Uncooked grits, Water, Garlic salt, Roll garlic cheese; chopped, Eggs; slightly beaten, Butter, 
Cook grits according to package directions. While hot, add cheese, butter  and eggs. Place in a buttered casserole at 400 to 450 degrees for 1 hour.  Serve hot. May be prepared several hours ahead but baked just before  serving.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
