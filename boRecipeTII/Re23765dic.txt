Artillery Pie
Butter, Lemon juice, fresh, Cinnamon, Slices bread, Ginger, Sugar, Golden delicious apples, 
Cut apples into quarters. Place in a large pot, and add just enough  water to prevent scortching. Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer  covered until apples are soft, about 30 minutes. Put through a sieve,  discard skins and cores. Stir in sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon and  ginger.    Trim the crust from the bread, spread with butter and toast in the  oven at 500F for about 5 minutes or until lightly browned. In a 2  quart baking dish, layer bread and apple mix. Bake at 350F for 30 min.  Serve warm.
