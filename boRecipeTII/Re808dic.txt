Heath Bars
Butter, Brown sugar, firm pack, Egg yolk, Vanilla, Flour, Heath Bits, divided, Finely chopped pecans, 
Preheat oven to 350~F. In large bowl, with electric mixer, cream butter  well; blend in brown sugar, egg yolk and vanilla. By hand mix in flour, 1  1/2 bags Heath Bits and nuts. Press into ungreased 15 1/2 x 10 1/2in. jelly  roll pan. Bake 18 to 20 minutes, or until browned. Remove from oven and  immeadiately sprinkle remaining 1/2 bag Heath Bits over top. Cool slightly;  cut into bars while warm.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Cindy Hartlin" <cjhartlin@email.msn.com>  on Aug 10, 1998
