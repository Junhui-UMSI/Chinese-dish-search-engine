T-3 Miracle Soup
Soup mix, onion, Bell peppers, Cabbage head, Onions, Celery stalk, Tomatoes, 
Cut up all vegetables in a large pot, cover with water, add onion soup mix,  and boil for 10 minutes. Cover and lower heat and simmer until vegetables  are soft.
