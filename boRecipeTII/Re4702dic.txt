Philly 50th Anniversary Double Chocolate Layer Cheesecake
Sugar, chocolate, Vanilla, Eggs, Philadelphia Cream, 
Mix cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with mixer on medium speed until  blended. Add eggs; mix until blended. Stir melted chocolate into 1 cup of  the batter. Pour chocolate batter into crust. Top with plain batter. Bake  at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until center is almost set. Cool.  Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight.
