Basic Mousse
Spices; raisins, and nuts, Unflavored gelatin, Honey, VARIATIONS, Cup; water, cold, milk, Frozen fruit; up to 1, Tofu; mashed, Vanilla, Fruit preserves; up to 1, 
Soften the gelatin in the cold water. Stir together the milk and honey and  bring to a boil. Add the gelatin mixture and stir until the gelatin is  dissolved. Cool. Add vanilla to the cooled mixture. Combine the above  mixture and the tofu in a blender and process at high speed until very  smooth. Cool the mixture in the refrigerator until almost set. Remove, and  beat by hand or with an electric mixer until until light and fluffy. Freeze  the mixture in a mold or in a refrigerator tray for about 1-2 hours,  depending on size of the mold. Serve before it becomes too hard. Serves 6-8    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "M. Hicks" <nitro_ii@email.msn.com> on Feb 6,  1998
