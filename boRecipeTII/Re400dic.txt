Lemon Cookies
Duncan Hines Lemon Supreme, Eggs, Lemon Peel, Cooking oil, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Combine all ingredients and mix well. Drop from a tsp  onto an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 F for 10-12 minutes,until  golden. Cool on cookie sheet about 1 minutes, then remove to rack to finish  cooking.  Posted to brand-name-recipes by The Taillons <taillon@access.mountain.net>  on Feb 21, 1998
