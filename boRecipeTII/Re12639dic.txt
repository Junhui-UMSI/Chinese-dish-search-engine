Yogurt Raita (Cucumber and Mint Raita)
cucumber, Natural yogurt, Fresh ground black pepper, fresh mint, Cayenne pepper, Salt, roasted cumin seeds, 
Put yogurt in a bowl. Beat lightly with a fork or whisk until smooth and  creamy. Add all other ingredients and mix. Cover and refrigerate until  ready to eat.    Note: This is served alongside Indian dishes to cool the palate from the  spiciness of the dish. The recipe is from Madhur Jaffreys "Indian  Cooking."
