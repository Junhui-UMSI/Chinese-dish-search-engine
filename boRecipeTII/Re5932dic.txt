Pistachio Drops
FF Butter Cookie Recipe, 
Beat additional one large egg & 1 tsp. almond extract into dough.  Drop by teaspoonsfuls onto cookie sheets; sprinkle with chopped  pistachio nuts. Bake 10 minutes. While warm sprinkle with  confectioners sugar.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
