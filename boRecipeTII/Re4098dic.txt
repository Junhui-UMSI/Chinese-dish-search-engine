Pepperoni Pizza Burgers
Pepperoni -- slices, Green pepper -- chopped, Hamburger buns -- split, Mozzarella cheese --, Make own, Pizza sauce -- (15 oz) or, Mushrooms -- sliced, Shredded, Turkey -- ground/cooked, Oregano, Onion -- chopped, 
In a skillet, brown turkey; drain.  Stir in pizza sauce and oregano.  Mix  onion, pepper, and pepperoni to beef mixture.  Simmer 20 to 25 minutes  Spoon onto buns, top with cheese and mushrooms.  Serve immediately.    Recipe By     : Jo Anne Merrill    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
