Quakers- Banana Orange Oatmeal Muffins
Salt [optl), Nonfat plain yogurt, Brown Sugar, Baking soda, All-purpose flour, Vegetable oil, QUAKER Oats uncooked, Chopped dates, Baking Powder, Mashed banana (2 medium), Ground cinnamon, Egg whites or, 
l l/2 ts grated orange peel    Heat oven to 400  F.  Spray bottoms only of 12 muffin cups with no-stick  cooking spray.  Combine dry ingredients including dates; mix well. Add  combined yogurt, banana, egg whites, oil and orange peel, mixing just until  dry ingredients are moistened.  Fill muffin cups almost full. Bake 20 to 22  minutes or until golden brown. Let muffins stand a few minutes; remove from  pan.    * Or substitute 3/4 cup skim milk combined with 2-1/2 teaspoons lemon juice  for yogurt.  Let milk and juice stand 5 minutes or until slightly  thickened.  Proceed as recipe directs.    Nutrition Information:  l muffin Calories 160, Calories From Fat 27, Total  Fat 3g, Saturated Fat 0g, Cholesterol 0mg, Sodium 120mg, Total  Carbohydrates 29g, Dietary Fiber 2g, Protein 4g
