All Corn Muffins
Corn oil, Sour cream, Baking Powder, Salt, Creamed corn, Baking soda, A, B, Cornmeal, Eggs, 
Preheat oven to 400 and prepare pan    In a large bowl combine "A" well. Add "B" and combine well. Spoon into  prepared pan. Bake for 25 min. Check for doneness via the toothpick test.  Remove from pan and cool on wire rack. For bread, let cool in pan 10 min.  before moving to rack.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 414 by Abtaxel <Abtaxel@aol.com>  on Dec 29, 1997
