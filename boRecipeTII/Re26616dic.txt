Baguette Melts
Cheddar cheese -- shredded, Pimientos -- diced, Green onions -- finely, Baguette -- sliced, Ripe olives -- chopped, Chopped, Mayonnaise, Diagonally, Prepared horseradish, 
1. Mix together cheese, olives, pimientos, green onions, mayonnaise, and  horseradish.    2. Spoon about 1 tablespoon cheese mixture onto each baguette slice.    3. Broil until cheese starts to melt.    Makes 3 to 4 dozen.    * Timesaver Tip: Cheese topping can be made up to 2 days ahead,  refrigerated, and used as needed.
