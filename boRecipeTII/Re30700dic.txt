Tamarind Puree
Wet tamarind pulp, (1, Hot water, 
Combine tamarind and hot water. Let stand 1 hour; then using your fingers  or a fork, break tamarind into small pieces while it is still in the water.  Let tamarind soak an additional 3 hours.    Press tamarind mixture through a sieve, reserving liquid; discard seeds and  fibers. Yield: 1-1/2 cups.    Per serving: 271 Calories; 1g Fat (2% calories from fat); 3g Protein; 71g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 39mg Sodium    Recipe by: Cooking Light, April 1995, page 124    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #398 by igor@digex.net on Jan 28, 1997.
