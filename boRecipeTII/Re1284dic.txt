Deviled Swiss Steak
Flour, Salt, Pepper, Dry mustard, Worcestershire Sauce, Mushrooms, drained, Round steak (3 lb.) thick, Cooking oil, Water, 
Combine flour, salt, pepper and dry mustard.  Sprinkle mixture over steak  and pound into the meat with your trusty meat mallet.  Using a heavy  skillet brown meat in hot oil.  Combine water and worcestershire and add to  browned meat.  Cover tightly and cook in 325 degree oven for 1 hour or  until tender.  Heat mushrooms in a small amount of butter and serve on  steak.
