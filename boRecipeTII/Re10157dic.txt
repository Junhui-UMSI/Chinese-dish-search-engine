Quiche in Tart Shells
Eggs, Salt, (3-inch) unbaked tart shells, Bacon; cut in 1/4-inch, Sour cream, (12-oz) shredded Swiss, Pepper, Chopped white onion, Chopped chives (optional), 
Fry bacon until crisp. Drain on paper towel. In bacon grease saute onion  until tender but not brown. Drain off fat. Combine in bowl eggs, sour  cream, salt, pepper, cheese, onion & bacon. Pour into tart shells. Bake at  375 for about 25 minutes or until knife inserted half-way between outside &  center of filling comes out clean. Garnish with chopped chives.
