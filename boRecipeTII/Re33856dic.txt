Sweet and Sour Roast
Brown sugar, Vinegar, Garlic, Salt, Ketchup, Water, Onions sliced, 
Brown the meat in a heavy pot. Add rest of ingredients. Simmer on top of  stove until tender. (depending on size of roast- usually 1 1/2 hours)    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Aliza Blizinsky" <alizab@hotmail.com> on  Apr 01, 1998
