Rainbow Popcorn Balls
Light corn syrup, popcorn, popped, Sugar, fruit-flavored gelatin, peanuts, salted, 
Combine syrup and sugar and bring to a boil; remove from heat. Add gelatin,  and stir until dissolved. Add peanuts and pour over popcorn, mixing well.  Shape into balls. Yield: about 4 dozen (1-1/2 inch) balls.    From <The Progressive Farmers Southern Country Cookbook>, by the editors  of the Progressive Farmer Magazine.
