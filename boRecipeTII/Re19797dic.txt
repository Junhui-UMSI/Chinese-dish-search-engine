Appetizer Bean Con Queso
Cheese, Cheese, cheddar, Refried Beans, Que Bueno Precooked, Tortilla chips, Green chiles, diced, 
1.  Combine refried beans and diced green chiles. Place 1 cup bean mixture  in oven-proof serving dish. Top with 1 ounce grated cheese. 2. Place under  broil or in microwave over until cheese is melted. Serve immediately with  crisp tortilla chips. May be gaarnished with sour cream, chopped green  onions or sliced ripe olives, if desired.    Makes 21 appetizer servings. Each appetizer serves 2.    Note: The above ingredients may also be used for burrito filling. To  prepare, place 1 cup heated bean mixture in large warm flour tortilla. Top  with 1 ounce grated cheese. roll and fold envelope style. Serve. Makes 21  burritos.    Source: Carnation Company     -- ---
