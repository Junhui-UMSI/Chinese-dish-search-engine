Bbq Butter Beans
Dry mustard, Ketchup, Molasses, Dried butter beans, Brown sugar, Sugar, 
Soak beans overnight. Rinse and boil with salt and pepper until tender. Mix  remaining ingredients in bowl and add small amount of water from beans. Mix  beans and sauce. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until done.  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Diane Geary" <diane@keyway.net> on Feb 27,  1998
