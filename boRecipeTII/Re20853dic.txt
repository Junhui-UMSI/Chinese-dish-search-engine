Apple Kutchen
Margarine, cold, Granulated sugar, All-Purpose Flour, sifted, Margarine, softened, TOPPING, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, to taste, Sugar, Whole Egg, milk, Brown sugar, All-Purpose Flour, sifted, Vanilla, Apples, 
Mix first six ingredients till soft dough forms. Dump into greased jelly  roll pan, With buttered hands spread till covers pan evenly. Peel and slice  apples to cover dough. Use lots. For topping: mix remaining ingredients and  crumble on top. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
