Perrys Chicken Wings
Ranch Or Blue Cheese, Garlic powder, Chicken Wings (Second And, Pepper, Celery Sticks For Garnish, Peanut Oil For Frying; no, Vinegar, Butter; No Substitutions, 
Preheat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees. Deepfry wings 10 to 12 at a  time, until golden brown, approx. 10 to 12 minutes. Place wings on paper  towels to drain. In a medium saucepan, melt butter. Add red hot sauce,  vinegar, garlic powder and pepper. Bring to a simmer and add chicken wings.  Toss wings in sauce for a few minutes. Serve with ranch or blue cheese  dressing with celery sticks for garnish.    If you like your wings hotter add additonal sauce (to taste) to the butter.
