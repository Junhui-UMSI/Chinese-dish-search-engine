Tomato and Basil Salsa
Salt, Tomato; coarsely chopped, Minced seeded jalapeno, Fresh basil chiffonade, olive oil, Balsamic vinegar, Finely diced red onion, 
Combine the tomato, onion, jalapeno, vinegar, basil and olive oil. Add salt  and pepper to taste. Cover and refrigerate for up to 1 day. Bring to room  temperature before serving. Serve any extra salsa in a bowl in the center  of the table.
