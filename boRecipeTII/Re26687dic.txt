Baileys Original Irish Cream
(14-oz) Eagle sweetened, Instant coffee, Almond extract, Light cream, Irish Whiskey, Vanilla, 
Combine all the ingredients in a blender set on high speed for 30 seconds.  Bottle in a tightly sealed container and refrigerate. The liqueur will keep  for at least 2 months if kept cool.  Be sure to shake the bottle well  before serving. Makes 4 cups.
