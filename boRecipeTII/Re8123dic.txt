Potato Cheese Croquettes
bread crumbs, Egg, potatoes, Cheddar cheese, Salt to taste, milk, 
Break up potatoes. Beat egg in blender until frothy. Add potato pieces  gradually, beating at medium speed until blended.    Stir in cheese, salt, and Accent. Shape as desired. Dip into crumbs, them  milk, and again into crumbs.    Fry in shallow hot fat until golden brown on all sides.    Source ...... Mary Margaret McBride ENCYCLOPEDIA OF COOKING Editor ......
