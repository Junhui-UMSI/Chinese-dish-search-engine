Quick Cookies
Sugar, Butter, Salt, Flour, 
Cream Butter, (with beater) gradually add sugar & when it is light add  flour & salt. Beat until dough is a good consistency. Divide dough in two  pieces. Roll each piece until thin , cut for cookies & place on a cookie  Sheet. Bake 300F 25 min.
