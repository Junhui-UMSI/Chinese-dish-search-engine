Apricot-Glazed Pears
pears, Cinnamon, Butter, Vanilla, Apple juice, apricot preserves, 
Peel pears, leaving them whole with stem intact. Place in slow cooker. Add  apple juice, vanilla, butter and cinnamon stick. Cook on high for 3-4  hours.    Remove pears with slotted spoon.  Place in individual dessert dishes. Heat  apricot preserves in small saucepan until melted. Top each pear with  preserves sauce.
