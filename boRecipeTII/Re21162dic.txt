Tyler Texas Chili
Ground round, (28 oz) tomatoes, Ground cumin, Garlic, Bell pepper; diced, Chili powder, (12 oz) tomato paste, Red pepper flakes, Canned jalapeno peppers, (28 oz) chili beans, Bay Leaves, Cayenne pepper, Onions, 
Brown beef, onions, and bell pepper.  Add garlic. When browned add rest of  the ingredients except beans and jalapenos. Simmer for 2 hours. Add beans  and jalapeno peppers and simmer for one hour longer.     NOTE: FOR HEART-HEALTHY CHILI, SUBSTITUTE GROUND TURKEY FOR BEEF IN THE  ABOVE RECIPES.
