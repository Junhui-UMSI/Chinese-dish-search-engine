Applesauce Cake
Applesauce, Egg, Salt, Allspice, Baking soda, Soft Shortening, Flour, Cloves, Cinnamon, Sugar, 
Mix shortening, sugar and egg.  Add the applesauce.  Mix flour with spices.  Add to applesauce mixture.  Stir in 1/2 cup chopped nuts.  Add 1 cup  raisins.  Pour into oblong cake pan.  Bake in a 350F oven for 45 to 50  minutes.  If you use a square dish bake for 35 to 40 minutes.  If deeper  bake longer.  Test for doneness with a toothpick.  From: Syds Cookbook.
