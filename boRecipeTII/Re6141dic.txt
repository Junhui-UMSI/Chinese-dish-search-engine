Pizza on the Grill Pt 2
See part 1, 
Close grill or cover pizzas loosely with tented tinfoil. Grill until  cheeses melt and dough is cooked through and browned, probably about 5  minutes. Transfer grilled pizzas to serving plates and repeat the grilling  process for the two remaining pizzas.
