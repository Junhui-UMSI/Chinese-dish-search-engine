Asparagus Vinagrette Salad
Olive or salad oil, Chopped pimento, Salt, Capers, Sugar, Garlic juice, Cayenne pepper, Chopped parsley, 15 oz. whole asparagus,drain, Paprika, Chopped shallots or onions, Tarragon Vinegar, 
Combine sugar, salt, cayenne pepper, paprika, and garlic juice in a bowl.  Add vinegar and oil slowly, beating constantly. Add onions or shallots,  parsley, pimento, and capers. Mix well. Pour over asparagus and refrigerate  overnite.
