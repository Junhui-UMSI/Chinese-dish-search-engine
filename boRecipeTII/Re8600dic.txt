Potatoes with Juniper Berries
Potatoes, peeled and coarsly, Black Pepper, Red bell pepper, seeded and, Crushed juniper berries, 
Place potatoes in a colander and rinse well under cold running water. Do  not drain, but place them in a skillet with other ingredients. Cook gently,  turning occasionally, for 1/2 hour.  Add water sparingly, as needed.
