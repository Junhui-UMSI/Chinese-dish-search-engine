Anticucho Style Grilled Fish
Fish Fillets(Shark, Tuna,, Pickled Jalapenos; plus 2 T., To 1/2 C Fresh Lime Juice, Soy sauce, Vegetable oil, Cracked black pepper, Fistful Of Fresh, Cloves Garlic; peeled, 
Combine and blend all ingredients, except fish, in blender until smooth.  Marinate fish for 30-40 minutes. Grill over medium hot coals until cooked  through. Baste with the reserved marinade several times during cooking.  Serve with lime wedges, avocado slices and pico de gallo. Can also used  this same marinade on boneless, skinless chicken breasts. Can marinate the  chicken breasts for a couple hours though, and then barbeque them like  fish.
