Prune Whip Cake
Flour, Shortening, Vanilla, Sugar, Allspice, Nutmeg, Salt, Baking soda, Prune juice, milk, Cinnamon, Baking Powder, Eggs, 
Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, soda,salt and spices. Add shortening, add  prune juice and milk, then vanilla. Mix well then add the eggs and mix  well. Pour into greased and floured tube pan or 13 x9 inch pan. Bake at 350  for 50 minutes
