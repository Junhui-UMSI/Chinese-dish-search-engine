All-Bran Refrigerator Rolls
Salt, Shortening, Yeast, Boiling water, Sugar, All-bran cereal, Flour, Eggs, 
Melt shortening into water. Add sugar, cereal & salt, then cool to  lukewarm. Soften yeast in 1 cup warm water then mix with eggs. Add to bran  mixture. Add flour & beat thoroughly. Cover & let rise. Punch down, cover &  place in refrigerator until ready to use. Form into rolls. Let rise. Bake  at 450 for 20 minutes. Will keep about a week. Dough is soft. Makes a  lighter roll if refrigerated before using.    MRS A.E. (PHYLLIS) SOLEM    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
