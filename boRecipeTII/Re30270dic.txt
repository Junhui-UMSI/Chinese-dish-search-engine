Banana Bun for on the Run!
Banana, Jelly, Lemon juice; optional, Peanut butter, Hot dog roll, 
Spread inside of hot dog roll with peanut butter and jelly.  Brush peeled  banana lightly with lemon juice (so it doesnt turn brown).  Place banana  in roll and off they go!
