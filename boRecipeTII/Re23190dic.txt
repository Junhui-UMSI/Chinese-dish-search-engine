Tuna Cheese Swirls
CHICKEN WITH RICE SOUP, FROZEN PEAS, GRATED CHEDDAR, MILK OR WATER, TUNA; DRAINED, QUICK MIX, 
PREHEAT OVEN TO 425. BUTTER A 1 1/2 QT CASSEROLE. COMBINE TUNA, PEAS AND  SOUP IN A MEDIUM BOWL. POUR INTO PREPARED CASSEROLE. IN SMALL BOWL, COMBINE  QUICK MIX AND MILK. STIR UNTIL BLENDED. ON LIGHTLY FLOURED SURFACE, KNEAD  DOUGH GENTLY 10 TIMES. SPRINKLE WITH GRATED CHEESE AND ROLL LIKE A JELLY  ROLL. SEAL EDGE. CUT INTO 1/2" SLICES. PLACE SLICES ON TOP OF CASSEROLE.  BAKE 15 TO 20 MINUTES, UNTIL BISCUITS ARE GOLDEN. (Rated:*** out of ****)    Recipe by: Make a Mix Cookbook    Posted to brand-name-recipes by Meg Antczak <meginny@frontiernet.net> on  Feb 03, 1998
