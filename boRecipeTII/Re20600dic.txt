Apple Citrus Refresher
Apple juice, Lime juice; freshly squeezed, Grapefruit juice, Ginger ale; each 12 oz/355 l, Orange juice, 
"An all-occasion beverage - fruity & effervescent. Make sure juices and  ginger ale are well chilled at the start."    In large pitcher or punch bowl combine apple, orange, grapefruit and lime  juices. Just before serving, pour in ginger ale. Serve over ice cubes in  tall glasses. MAKES: 8-10 SERVINGS SOURCE: _The Dave Nichol Cookbook_
