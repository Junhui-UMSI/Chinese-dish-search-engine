Anchovy-Garlic Dressing
Balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, Oli, Clove garlic mashed; (or 1, Lemon juice, Anchovy fillet chopped, 
Blend the first 5 ingredients . Wisk olive oil in slowly.    NOTES : This can be made in a blender or food processor, if desired.  It  will have a different texture,though.  Recipe by: Judy Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #235 by Judith Sobel  <jcs@mindspring.com> on Aug 20, 1997
