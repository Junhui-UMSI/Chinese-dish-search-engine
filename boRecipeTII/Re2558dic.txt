Peaches with Balsamic Vinegar and Black Pepper
fresh peaches, balsamic, Black Pepper, Sugar, 
Wash and drain peaches. Slice peaches into 1/2-inch thick slices and place  in a large mixing bowl. Add vinegar, pepper and sugar and gently toss to  coat. Divide among 4 martini glasses and serve.
