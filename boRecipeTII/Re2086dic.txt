Apple Cake
White wheat flour, Apples, Granulated white sugar, Cinnamon, Eggs, Granulated white sugar, Vegetable oil, Orange juice, Vanilla extract, Baking Powder, 
Mix the first 7 ingredients together and set aside. Peel and slice the  apples, and toss them with the remaining sugar and cinnamon. In a buttered  cake pan, alternate layers of the batter and the apples, starting with the  batter. Bake at 350F for 1 hour and 15 minutes.    Recipe by Mangia!
