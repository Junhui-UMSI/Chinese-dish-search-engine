Ravioli #2
Liquid, Garlic, Spaghetti sauce with, Salad oil, Ground beef, Tomato paste, Tomato sauce, Eggs; well beaten, Frozen chopped spinach, Pepper, Raw macaroni, Salt, Onions, Vegetable oil, Soft bread, 
Cook spinach as directed on package, drain liquid & add enough water to  make 1 cup (save liquid). Brown beef with onion & garlic in 1 Tbs oil. Add  spaghetti sauce, tomato paste, tomato sauce, salt & pepper to meat mixture  & simmer 10 minutes. Boil macaroni just until tender; drain & combine with  bread, eggs, reserved liquid & oil plus the spinach. Spread in a 9x13-inch  baking pan & cover with meat mixture. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes. Let stand  10 minutes before cutting into squares.    MRS J.T. (HARRIET) EVANS    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.
