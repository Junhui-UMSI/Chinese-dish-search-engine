Cecily Brownstones Buttermilk Batter
Egg, Buttermilk, Flour, Salt, Baking soda, 
Beat egg with buttermilk and stir in other ingredients. James Beard says  that this is the best onion-ring batter he has ever found. It can also be  used with most veggies; Beard suggests artichoke hearts, asparagus,  broccoli flowerets, Brussels sprouts, eggplant fingers or slices,  fiddleheads, green beans, mushrooms, pepper strips, snap peas, snow peas,  spinach leaves, sunchokes, zucchini strands.
