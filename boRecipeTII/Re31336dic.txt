Bar Cookies
Flour, Baking soda, Eggs, Nuts, Brown sugar, Butter, 
Mix eggs & brown sugar together. Add melted butter & cream well. Add flour  & soda a cup at a time. When mixed well, add nuts. Divide dough into 3  parts & shape into rolls with waxed paper. Chill well. Slice & bake as  desired in 350 degree oven. (Dough may be frozen.)    HELEN WEAVER    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
