Algerian Carrots
Hotsauce, Fresh mint leaves; chopped, Sugar, Carrots; scraped, slice, Garlic cloves; thinly sliced, Lemon; juice only, Light olive oil, Cumin seeds; toasted and, Salt, 
Cooks Note: Excellent served with poultry or whitefish.    1. Put the carrots in a steamer basket set over boiling water.  Steam for  about 5 minutes, until barely tender.  Reserve the cooking water and mix 5  tablespoons of it with the hot sauce.    2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  Add the garlic,  diluted hot sauce, lemon juice, cumin, salt and sugar.  Mix well.  Add the  carrots, then partially cover and cook over medium-low heat for about 10  minutes, until the liquid is reduced.    3. Stir in the mint and serve at once.
