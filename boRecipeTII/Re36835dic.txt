Strawberry Preserves #2
Strawberries, Sugar, 
Put strawberries & 1-1/2 cups sugar in large pan & boil 5 minutes. Add  remaining sugar; boil 10-15 minutes. Spoon into earthenware jar, skim off  foam, cover & let sit 24 hours. Stir occasionally. Put preserves in clean,  sterilized jars; seal. Never cook more than one quart of berries at one  time. Yields 2 pints.
