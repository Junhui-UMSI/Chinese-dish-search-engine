Quick and Healthy Chicken Stir-Fry, Lhj
Onion, Rice, Broccoli florets, Vegetable oil, Stir-fry sauce, chicken, Red pepper, Water, 
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and pepper. Cook  5 minutes. Stir in broccoli, water and stir-fry sauce. Cook until tender,  stirring occasionally, 5 minutes. Stir in chicken and heat through. Serve  with rice or couscous.    Prep time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 10 minutes    (C) Copyright 1997, Meredith Corporation, All Rights Reserved.    NOTES : This tasty chicken and vegetable stir-fry couldnt be easier--and  everything except the rice or couscous gets cooked in one skillet--so  cleanup is a breeze, too!
