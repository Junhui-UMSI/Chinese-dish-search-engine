Vegetable Dip #2
Mayonnaise, Garlic powder, cream cheese, Garlic, onions, Tabasco, Parsley flakes, bouillon, flavored, Boiling water, Pepper, 
Dissolve bouillon cubes in boiling water. Mix all ingredients together and  chill until serving time. Garnish with parsley flakes, if desired. Serve  with raw vegetables.    LISA CHAPMAN    (MRS.
