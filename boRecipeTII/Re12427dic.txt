Zuchini and Sausage Soup
Onion, Pepper, Basil, Celery, chopped, Zucchini, sliced, Salt, Sausage, Green pepper, chopped, Tomatoes, Oregano, Garlic, 
Brown the sausage, remove from skillet. Add garlic, celery, onion, zucchini  and gr pepper. Saute lightly, about 3 minutes. Add sausage, seasonings and  tomatoes. Simmer, covered for 30 minutes. If soup becomes to thick, add  chicken stock.       Enjoy, Sheryl
