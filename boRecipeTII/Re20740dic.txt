Apple Dessert Pancake
milk, Eggs Yolks, Baking Powder, Salt, Sugar, Egg whites, Cinnamon, Flour, Macintosh Apples, Sugar, Unsalted butter, 
Mix 2 tablespoons sugar and cinnamon.  Melt butter in 10" iron skillet.  Swirl sugar/cinnamon mixture over all.  Arrange apple slices in skillet and  cook 5 minutes over low heat.  Preheat oven to 400.  Mix flour, salt, egg  yolks, baking powder, and milk.  Beat egg whites with 3 tablespoon sugar  until soft peaked.  Fold into flour mixture.  Pour batter over apple slices  and bake 10 minutes.  Remove from pan by inverting on plate.
