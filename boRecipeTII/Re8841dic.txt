Praline Strips
Graham crackers, Brown sugar, Butter or margarine, Pecans, 
Line bottom of cookie sheet with crackers. In double boiler melt butter &  sugar. Bring to a boil for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Add nuts & pour  over crackers. Bake 8 minutes at 350. Cut into strips.    MRS. MICHAEL FIELDER    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
