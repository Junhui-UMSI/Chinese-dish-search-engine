Watkins Stawberry Pie
Sliced strawberries, Baked 9-inch pie shell, Water, Sugar, Unflavored gelatin, WATKINS Strawberry Punch, Flavor with WATKINS Vanilla, Concentrate, Extra whipped topping and, WATKINS Vanilla Dessert Mix, Strawberries for garnish, Sweetened whipped topping, 
In medium saucepan combine Vanilla Dessert Mix, sugar, gelatin and water.  Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a full boil.  Pour  into a bowl and add Stawberry Punch Concentrate;chill until thickened.*  Fold in whipped topping; blend well.  Stir in strawberries.  Pour into pie  shell and chill until set, 1 to 2 hours.  Garnish with additional whipped  topping and strawberries.    *Note from Watkins Kitchen: To hasten chilling, place bowl of pudding  mixture in large bowl of ice water, then stir until thickened.
