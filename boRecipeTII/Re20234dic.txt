Veal Birds (Kalf Kyckling)
Water, Butter, Chopped parsley, Cream, Bonelss veal steak, Salt and white pepper, 
Cut meat into serving size portions. Pound flat. Combine parsley and 4  tablespoon of the butter. Spread on veal. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Roll up each piece. Secure with picks. Brown in remaining butter. Add  water. Cover. Simmer for 1 hour. Add cream. Cover. Simmer for 30 minutes  but do not boil. Serve with baked potatoes. serves 6-8    Posted to Recipe Archive - 22 Sep 96    submitted by: LeiG@aol.com    Date: Sun, 8 Sep 1996 20:58:37 -0400
