Stick butter; softened, GARLIC BUTTER, Salt, (13 3/4-ounce) of artichoke, Lemon ; Juice of, Italian-seasoned bread, Chopped fresh parsley, Eggs, Oil for deep frying, Garlic powder, 
http://www.foodtv.com/midatl/reclist.htm    Drain artichoke hearts and cut each in half. Beat eggs. Dip artichoke  hearts in beaten egg and coat in bread crumbs. Deep fry for about 30  seconds until golden brown. Remove to serving dish and cover with Garlic  Butter.    Yield: 3 servings    Garlic Butter: Mix softened butter with all ingredients until blended.  Refrigerate for use with other vegetables.
