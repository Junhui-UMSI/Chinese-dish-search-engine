Angel Buns
Bisquick baking mix, Pack yeast, Sugar, Warm water (105-115), milk, 
Dissolve yeast & sugar in warm water in medium bowl. Mix in baking  mix & milk until dough forms. Gently smooth into a ball on surface  dusted with baking mix.  Knead 10 times. Roll 1/2" thick. Cut with 2"  biscuit cutter dipped in baking mix. Place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Let rise in warm place about 30 minutes or until soft & puffed. Heat  oven to 425. Bake 6-8 minutes or until golden brown.  For high  altitude: Let rise for 20 minutes. Heat oven to 450. Bake 5 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
