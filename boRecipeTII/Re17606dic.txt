Almond Joys
Coconut, Vanilla, Almonds, Margarine, Chocolate chips;, Powdered sugar, Eagle Brand, Stick; (1/4 lb.) paraffin, 
Melt margarine. Add Eagle brand milk, powdered sugar, package of coconut  and vanilla. Mix well and turn into a large cookie sheet. Refrigerate until  solid.    Melt wax and chocolate chips in double boiler. Cut candy in 1 inch squares  and dip individually into chocolate mixture. (Use a fork for dipping.)
