Beef Paprika "Stew"
Beef stock or bouillon, Salt, Red or green bell peppers, Caraway seeds, Dried marjoram, Sweet hungarian paprika, Ground beef, Black pepper to taste, Onions, Peeled canned tomatoes, Cooking oil, 
Chop onions. Seed and slice peppers into 1/4-inch strips. Heat oil in large  skillet. Add onions and peppers and cook over med-high heat until onions  are soft. Add beef, raise heat, and brown, stirring. Drain off excess fat.  When beef has browned, add bouillon, paprika, caraway seeds, and marjoram.  Add tomatoes, squeezing them in your hand to break them up. Season with  salt and pepper and cook until serving time.
