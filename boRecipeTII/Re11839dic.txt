Raw Potato Pancake
Egg, separated, Salt, Med Potato, raw, Flour, Baking Powder, 
Grate the potatoes and add the beaten egg yolks. Stir in the flour, baking  powder and salt and mix well. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites. Cook  by spoonfuls in heavy skillet in hot fat. Serve with apple sauce. Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
