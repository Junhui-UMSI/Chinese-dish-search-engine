Pumpkin Walnut Cookies Frosted
Salt, Butter/margarine, softened, Lemon extract, Pumpkin -cooked, mashed, milk, MAPLE FROSTING, Flour - all purpose, Vanilla extract, Eggs, Powdered sugar, sifted, Brown sugar, firmly, Pumpkin pie spice, Walnuts, chopped, Butter/margarine, softened, Baking Powder, Maple extract, 
cked Cream butter; gradually add brown sugar, beating well at medium speed  of an electric mixer. Add eggs, one at a time, beating after each addition.  Stir in pumpkin and flavorings. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and  pumpkin pie spice. Gradually add to creamed mixture, mixing well. Stir in  walnuts. Drop dough by teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto greased cookie  sheets. Bake at 375F for 12 minutes. Cool on wire racks. Frost with Maple  Frosting. Yield: 7 1/2 dozen. (Maple Frosting) Cream butter; gradually add  1 cup powdered sugar, beating well at medium speed of an electric mixer.  Add remaining sugar alternately with milk, beating until smooth enough to  spread. Add maple extract, and beat well. Yield: about 1 cup.
