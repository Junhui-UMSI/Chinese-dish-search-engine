White Enchiladas
Sour cream, milk, Monterey Jack, chopped green chiles  , Onion, tortilla chips, chicken, salt and pepper, Cream of chicken soup, 
Combine sour cream, onion, salt, pepper, chiles, soup & milk & mix well.  Layer half the tortilla chips, half the cream mixture, half the chicken &  half the cheese. Repeat layers. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.    JANE GLASS    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
