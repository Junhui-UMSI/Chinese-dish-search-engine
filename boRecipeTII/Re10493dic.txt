Quick Fruit Dressing
SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, JUICE; PINEAPPLE #3, SALAD DRESSING #2 1/2, 
1.  BLEND SUGAR WITH PINEAPPLE JUICE AT LOW SPEED IN MIXER BOWL.  BLEND IN  SALAD DRESSING AT LOW SPEED UNTIL JUST BLENDED.    2.  COVER; REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.    3.
