Turkey Chili
olive oil, Ground turkey, Tomato paste (6 oz), Self-reduced chicken broth, Black beans, rinsed and, Diced tomatoes in juice,, Black pepper, Frozen corn kernels, Garlic, Chili powder, Ground cumin, Onion, Chopped onion, for garnish, 
Over medium heat, saute onion in oil in Dutch oven until soft, about 5  minutes. Add turkey and cook, stirring with a wooden spoon to break up  large pieces, until no longer pink, about 7 minutes.    Stir in garlic, chili powder, pepper and cumin. Add beans, tomatoes  and their juice, tomato paste, broth and corn.    Simmer about eight minutes, or until thoroughly hot, stirring often.  Garnish with chopped onion.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
