Penoche
Brown sugar, Butter, Chopped dates, Pecans, Cream, Coconut, 
Combine sugar, cream, and butter.  Boil to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F).  Remove from fire.  Cool to room temperature. Beat until creamy. Add fruit,  coconut, and nuts. Continue beating until mixture will hold its shape. Pour  into well-buttered, shallow pan. Cut in squares. Myrtle Howell, Gillett,  WI.
