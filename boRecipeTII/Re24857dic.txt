Aunties Chicken Casserole
Cream of chicken soup, Macaroni, uncooked, Cheese, velveeta or preferen, Chicken, cooked, chopped, milk, Cream of mushroom soup, Eggs, hardboiled, chopped, 
Mix all the ingredients together and refrigerate overnight, be sure to  cover.  take out 1 hour before cooking. Bake 1 hour at 350.
