Barbecued Duck #1
Water, Salt, Mustard, Vinegar, Ducks, Ketchup, Sherry, Worcestershire, 
Soak ducks in vinegar, salt and enough water to cover for 2 hours. Pat dry  and season. Grease skins with oil; put in roasting pan, breast down. Cover  and cook at 325 for 45 minutes. Combine sherry, ketchup, mustard and  Worcestershire. Turn ducks over and pour on sauce. Cover, and bake at 425  until done. Baste every 30 minutes. You may have to add more sauce.
