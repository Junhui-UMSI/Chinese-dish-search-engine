A Rice Krispy Treat
miniature marshmallows, Corn oil margarine, Rice Krispie cereal, 
Date: Sun, 25 Feb 1996 22:41:09 -0500    From: "Vernon E. Phipps Jr." <cook4u@vivanet.com>    Melt margarine and marshmallows in large pot over low heat. Stir until  melted. Remove from heat. Add Rice Krispie cereal; stir until all cereal is  coated. Press the treat intoa greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan. Cool, cut into  squares or any shape you like.
