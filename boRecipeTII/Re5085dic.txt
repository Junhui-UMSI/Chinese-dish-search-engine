Pico De Gallo / Black Bean Soup
Onion chopped, Cooked black beans or black, Cloves garlic, minced (up to, Green chilies, minced, Salt to taste, Fresh lime juice, Fresh Cilantro, Tomatoes, peeled, seeded and, Minced jalapeno pepper, 
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients. Mix thoroughly and season to taste  with salt. Microwave until warm. This will keep the vegetables somewhat  crisp.
