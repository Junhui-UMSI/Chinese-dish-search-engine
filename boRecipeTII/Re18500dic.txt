Amaretto Fruit Parfait
Chilled; sparkling mineral, Amaretto, Fresh fruit chunks or whole, 
Spoon chunks of fresh fruit or whole berries into long-stemmed wineglasses.  Sprinkle with 1/4 teaspoon amaretto, if you wish, then cover the fruit with  chilled sparkling mineral water.
