Rabbit with Plums
Coarsely diced carrots, Cut up rabbit, Basil, Flour, Coarsely diced med onion, Sherry, Butter, Parsley, Pepper, Vegetable oil, Pitted plum, 
In large pot or Dutch oven, heat half the butter & oil. Brown meat.  Remove & keep warm. Add carrots & onion; saute until tender. Add  plums & sherry. Return meat to pot. Sprinkle with seasonings. Cover &  simmer 45 minutes. Remove meat & keep warm. Blend in remaining butter  & flour. Whisk into liquid a little at a time. Simmer until thick.  Pour over rabbit & serve.
