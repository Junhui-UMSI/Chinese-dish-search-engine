Applesauce Raisin Bread
Oats, Raisins, Whole-wheat flour, Applesauce, Whole eggs; lightly beaten, Nutmeg, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, Pecans, Salad oil, Salt, Cinnamon, 
Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Stir in  oats and brown sugar. Beat eggs. Blend eggs, oil and applesauce together.  Stir in dry ingredients, raisins and nuts. Pour into greased 9 x 5 x 3 inch  loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
