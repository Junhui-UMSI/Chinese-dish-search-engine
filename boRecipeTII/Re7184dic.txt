Pork and Chilli Soup
Black Pepper, Boneless lean pork; cubed, Dried hot red chillies;, Onion; sliced, Garlic, Corn kernels, Potatoes; peel and quarter, Vegetable oil, Water, Ground cumin, Salt, Lemon juice, Chopped green bell pepper, 
Heat the oil in a large soup pan and brown the pork. Add the onion, cumin,  salt, pepper and chilies and stir for 2 minutes. Add garlic and the water,  cover and simmer for 30 minutes. Add the potatoes and continue cooking till  they are tender. Mix in the corn kernels, green pepper and lemon juice and  reheat to boiling. Check the seasoning and ladle into soup bowls. Sprinkle  fresh, chopped parsley over each serving.  Serve with plenty of fresh bread, a slaw of cabbage, onion and red apple,  and beer.  This soup is really a hearty meal, but it is served in soup bowls, hence  its name. 1979. Reprinted in 1986.
