Bacon Wrapped Microwave Cheese Dogs
Hot dogs, Hot dog buns, Mustard, American cheese, Bacon, 
Place bacon on bacon rack. Cover with paper towel. Microwave on High for 3  1/2 minutes or until almost done. Starting 1/2-inch from the end, slit each  hot dog lengthwise. Cut cheese into 4 strips and place in hot dog buns.  Wrap bacon around hot dogs and secure with toothpicks. Drain grease from  bacon rack. Place hot dogs on rack. Cover with paper towel.    Microwave on Medium-High (70%) for 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 minutes. Spread mustard  on buns and place bacon wrapped hot dogs in each. Warm, if desired, 8  seconds each.
