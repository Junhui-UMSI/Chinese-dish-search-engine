Beef Brisket #2
Garlic powder, Liquid smoke, Mushroom soup (optional), Brisket, Celery salt, Onion powder, Worcestershire, 
Combine all ingredients, except brisket (for gravy) for marinade. Marinate  brisket overnight in refrigerator. Bring brisket to room temperature the  next morning and cook 5 hours at 300 degrees. For a good gravy, add  mushroom soup.    OVERNIGHT MARINATE    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
