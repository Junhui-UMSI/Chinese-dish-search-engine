Tomato, Shallot, and Cucumber Salad
Tomatoes (2 medium, Chopped tarragon, Salt and pepper, Cucumber, peeled, seeded and, Balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Peeled shallots,; cut in, 
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Toss the shallots with 1 tablespoon olive  oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 12 minutes. Meanwhile, place  tomatoes and cucumbers in a mixing bowl. Place shallots in a small bowl.  Mix in vinegar, tarragon, remaining olive oil and season. Toss with the  tomatoes and cucumbers.
