Pineapple-Buttermilk Salad
Sugar, Nuts, Unflavored gelatin, (3-oz) orange gelatin, (10-oz) whipped topping, Buttermilk, (20-oz) crushed pineapple, 
Combine pineapple & sugar in saucepan. Bring to a boil, stir. Remove from  heat & add gelatins. Fold in buttermilk, whipped topping & nuts. Chill  overnight.
