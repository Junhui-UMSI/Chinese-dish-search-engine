Persimmon Sorbet
Granulated sugar, Persimmon puree, Lemon juice; or lime juice, 
Separate the persimmon pulp from the fibrous threads by pressing through a  sieve with the back of a spoon. Blend the persimmon puree with the sugar  and juice. Freeze until firm, stirring once or twice. Makes about 1 quart.  Penny Halsey (ATBN65B).    Recipe by: Ice Cream!
