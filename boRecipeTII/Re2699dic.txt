Margarine; softened, Whipping cream, Flour, Yellow food coloring, CAKE, Bananas; ripe, mashed, Powdered sugar, milk, Bananas, thinly sliced, Creamy peanut butter, Eggs, Pills.+ Butter Cake Mix, FROSTING, GLAZE, Vanilla, Creamy peanut butter, GARNISH, Banana extract or vanilla, Pills. 1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour 13x9"pan. 2. In large bowl,  combine bananas and margarine; mix well (mixture will look slightly  curdled). Add peanut butter; beat well. Add whipping cream and eggs; beat  at high speed until smooth. Add cake mix and flour; beat at low speed until  moistened; beat 2 minutes at high speed. Pour into greased and floured pan.  3. Bake at 350 Degrees for 45 to 55 minutes or until toothpick inserted in  center comes out clean. Cool 15 minutes; remove from pan, if desired. Cool  completely. 4. In small bowl, combine all frosting ingredients; stir until  smooth and creamy. Frost cake. In small         04/21/92        6:44 PM bowl, combine all glaze ingredients; beat  until smooth. Drizzle glaze over frosting. Just before serving, garnish  with banana slices. Store in refrigerator.
