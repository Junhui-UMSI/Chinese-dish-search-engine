Apple-Sweet Potato Casserole
Sweet potatoes, Baking apples, (12-oz) corn; drained, Nutmeg, Patties vegetarian breakfast, Cinnamon, 
Peel, halve and cut sweet potato into 1/2-inch thick slices. Boil potato  until barely tender. Meanwhile peel, core, and slice apple into thin  slices. In a non-stick skillet, cook the veg. sausage until well browned  and crumbled. Mix together the cooked sweet potatoes, apples, cooked  sausage, corn, cinnamon and nutmeg. Transfer to a glass baking dish, cover  and bake in a 350 oven until the apples are tender (about 45 minutes).  Serve hot.    Recipe by: Lucinda J.
