Beefn Potato Bake
Black pepper, Garlic salt, Brown Gravy Mix, Frozen Mixed Vegetables;, Cheddar, Frozen Hash Brown Potatoes;, Water, Vegetable oil, Ground beef, Durkee French Fried Onions, Shredded cheese; (4-ozs), 
In a shallow 1-1/2-quart baking dish, combine potatoes, oil and pepper.  Firmly press mixture across the bottom, and up the sides of dish to form a  shell. Bake, uncovered, at 400` for 15 minutes.    Meanwhile, in a skillet, brown and drain ground beef. Stir in gravy mix,  water and garlic salt; bring to a boil. Add mixed vegetables; reduce heat  to medium, and cook 5 minutes. Stir in 1/2 cup cheese and 1/2 can of french  fried onions; place in potato shell. Bake, uncovered, at 350` for 15  minutes. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and onions. Bake 5 minutes longer.
