Twisty Beef Bake
Green Pepper, (14 1/2 oz.) whole tomatoes;, (3 oz.) french fried onions, Corkscrew macaroni; uncooked, (10 3/4 oz.) cream of, Seasoned salt, Ground beef, Cheddar cheese, 
Cook macaroni as directed on package; drain. Brown ground beef; drain fat.  Combine all ingredients except French fried onions. Pour half the mixture  into a greased 2 quart casserole. Top with 1/2 can of onions. Pour  remaining macaroni mixture over onions. Cover and bake at 350 for 30  minutes. Uncover, top with remaining onions and bake 5 minutes longer.  Makes 4-6 servings.
