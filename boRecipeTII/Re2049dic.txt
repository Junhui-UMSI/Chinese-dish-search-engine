Almond Raisin Fudge Sauce
Sugar, Whipping cream, Salt, Vanilla extract, Raisins; coarsely chopped, Chopped or slivered almonds;, Unsalted butter, Unsweetened Chocolate, Unsweetened cocoa powder, 
In a heavy small saucepan, melt butter and chocolate over low heat. Stir in  cocoa, sugar, cream, and salt. Bring slowly to a boil. Remove from heat and  add vanilla, raisins, and almonds. Serve warm or at room temperature.    Makes about 1-1/2 cups.    Recipe by: the California Culinary Academy    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by Pat Hanneman <kitpath@earthlink.net> on Sep  09, 1998,
