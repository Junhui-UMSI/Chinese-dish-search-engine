Apple Oatbran Scones
Golden Delicious apples,, Currants, Oatbran, Egg whites, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, 2 % milk, Sugar, Margarine, Cinnamon, 
In a large mixing bowl, mix together flour, sugar, baking powder and  cinnamon.  Use two knives to cut margarine into a dry mixture until  crumbly.  Stir in oatbran, grated apple, and currants. In a measuring cup,  whisk together 4 egg whites with milk.  Mix egg mixture into oatbran  mixture until moistened.  Grease large cookie sheet and spread dough so  that it is approximately 1 inch thick, 9 inches wide and 15 inches long.  Score the dough into at least 18 squares. Bake at 375 deg F for 25-30  minutes.  Best served warm with jam. Posted by Linda Davis
