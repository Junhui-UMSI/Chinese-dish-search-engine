Sweet Red Beans New Orleans Style
Kidney beans, Celery, Garlic, Butter, Ketchup, Dried parsley, Salt & pepper, Onion, Green pepper, Pork, Carrot, Water, 
Soak beans overnight.    Cook in large pot with water until beans start to  soften.  Saute onions, celery bell pepper, garlic, cubed meat and parsley.  Add to beans. Add carrots, butter and catsup. Cook on low heat for another  hour or until tender.  Remove 1 cup of beans and mash with fork or potato  masher.  Return to pot and continue to simmer untill thickened.   Serve with  hot steamed rice.
