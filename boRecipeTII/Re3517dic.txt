Pecan Pie Bars #3
(14-oz) Eagle Brand, Unsifted flour, Egg, (6-oz) Almond Brickle chips, Vanilla, Pecans, Butter, 
Combine flour and sugar; cut in butter. Press firmly on bottom of 13x9-inch  pan. Bake at 325 degrees for 15 minutes. Beat milk, egg and vanilla. Stir  in chips and nuts. Spread over crust. Bake 25 minutes. Cool. Chill  thoroughly. Cut into bars. Store covered in refrigerator.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
