Walter Mcilhennys Chili
Ground cumin, TABASCO pepper sauce, Hot cooked rice, Vegetable oil, Onions, Garlic, Water, Chopped green chilies;, Lean beef chuck; well, Chili powder, Salt, 
In 5-quart Dutch oven or saucepot, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add  beef, 1/3 at a time; cook until browned on all sides. Remove; set aside.  Add onion and garlic; stirring frequently, cook 5 minutes or until tender.  Stir in chili powder, cumin, salt and Tabasco pepper sauce; cook 1 minute.  Add water and chilies; bring to a boil. Return beef to Dutch oven. Reduce  heat and simmer 1 1/2 hours or until beef is tender. Serve over rice with  chopped onion, shredded cheese and sour cream, if desired.
