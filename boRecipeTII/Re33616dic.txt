Swedish Nut Cake
Walnuts, chopped, Vanilla, Butter, crushed pinapple, TOPPING, Vanilla, Flour, Walnuts, chopped, Sugar, Cream cheese, Powdered sugar, Baking soda, Eggs, 
Mix together the cake ingredients.  Pour into a 13x9 inch pan and bake at  350, 36 minutes.    Mix together topping ingredients and beat with a beater then spread on hot  cake.
