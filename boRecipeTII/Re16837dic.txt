All-American Egg Rolls
Egg, fresh garlic, cream cheese, Polish sausages, Cooking oil, Prepared mustard, parsely, milk, Egg roll wrappers, Real mayonnaise, 
Date: Mon, 6 May 1996 18:39:14 -0500    From: pickell@cyberspc.mb.ca (S.Pickell)    Heat oil in frying pan to medium-high, about 350 degrees.  Combine garlic,  mayonnaise, cheese, and mustard in blender until smooth. Remove from  blender. Cut each Polish sausage into two shorter halves and score  lengthwise.  Beat egg and milk with fork until smooth. Place each sausage  half at end of egg roll wrapper, add a dollop of mustard sauce and roll  sausage into wrapper, sealing ends with egg mixture. Fry in hot oil until  lightly browned on all sides. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve with  extra mustard sauce.
