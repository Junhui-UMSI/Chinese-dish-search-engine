Apple Cranberry Crisp
FILLING, (about 4 medium size),, Peeled, cored, And cut in bite-size, Fresh or frozen cranberries, TOPPING, Salt, Granulated sugar, Golden Delicious apples,, Granulated sugar, Ground cinnamon, Butter or margarine, cut in, 8 pieces, Chunks (5 cups), All-purpose flour, 
1. Heat oven to 350F. Lightly grease a shallow 2 quart baking dish.    2. Topping: Mix flour, sugar, cinnamon and salt in medium-size bowl. Cut in  butter with pastry blender or 2 knives until mixture resembles coarse  crumbs.    3. Filling: Put apples, cranberries and sugar into prepared dish. Toss  gently to mix.    4. Sprinkle with topping. Pat lightly with fingertips.    5. Bake about 1 hour or until top is golden and filling bubbles.
