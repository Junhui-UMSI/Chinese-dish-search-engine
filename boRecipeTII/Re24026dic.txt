Tostadas De Pollo Y Frijoles
Hot sauce, Plain green olives, chopped, (or refried beans), Cooked, mashed black beans, Green bell pepper, sliced, String beans, cook & cool, Green onions, diced, Head lettuce, shredded, Tomato, wedged, Chicken, shredded, Cheddar cheese, grated, Tortillas, 
Lay a tortilla on each plate; spread with a layer of beans. Lay chicken on  beans.  Toss together vegetables and cheese; and mound on top of chicken.  Sprinkle hot sauce on top.
