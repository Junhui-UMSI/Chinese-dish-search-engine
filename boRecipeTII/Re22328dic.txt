Turkey-Pasta Salad
Tomato; Cut Into 1/2-Inch, Reduced Calorie Creamy, Frozen Corn Kernels;, Cilantro Or Parsley; Chopped, Optional, Pace Preferred, Red Bell Pepper; Cut Into, Additional Picante Sauce;, Cooked Turkey Or Chicken, Wagon Wheel Or Spiral Pasta;, Whole Kernel Corn OR;, 1/2-Inch Chunks, Zucchini; Cut Into 1/2-Inch, Salt; Optional, Picante Sauce; Pace, 
Combine the pasta, turkey, corn, zucchini, tomato, red bell pepper, and  cilantro in a large bowl.  Combine the picante sauce, dressing and salt in  a small bowl, mixing well to blend. Pour the dressing over the pasta  mixture and toss gently to coat all of the ingredients. Refrigerate. Serve  with additional picante sauce, if desired.
