Wild Rice Waldorf Salad
Pineapple tidbits in own, Cooked wild rice, Chopped walnuts; toasted, Celery, Granny Smith apples; diced, Sour lean, Mayonnaise, Lemon juice, Red Delicious apples; diced, Sugar, 
Add wild rice to usual Waldorf Salad ingredients and you create a robust  rendition to serve as both salad and side dish.    Drain pineapple, reserving 1 tablespoon juice. Combine pineapple, wild  rice, apples, celery and walnuts in large bowl. Stir reserved pineapple  juice and remaining ingredients together until sugar is dissolved. Fold  dressing into salad. Refrigerate, covered, several hours. Amount: 8 cups.    Recipe by: The Best of Byerlys, vol 2    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Kathleen <schuller@ix.netcom.com> on Apr 07,  1998
