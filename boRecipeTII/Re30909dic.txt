Tangy Sweet Bbq Sauce
Cayenne; to taste, Lemon juice, Brown sugar, Salt, Margarine, Water, Vinegar, Mustard, Tabasco sauce; to taste, Worcestershire Sauce, Ketchup, Chili powder, Onion, 
Chop onion. Saute in margerine until lightly colored. Add all other  ingredients and simmer for 30 min. makes 7 cups.
