Pizza Rice Casserole
Tomato sauce, Onion, Pepper, Garlic salt, Cottage cheese, Sugar, Ground beef, Rice, cooked, Oregano, Parsley flakes, Salt, cheese, 
Cook rice and set aside. Brown ground beef and onion in large skillet. Add  tomato sauce, garlic salt, sugar, salt, pepper, oregano and parsley. Cover  and simmer 15 minutes. Combine cottage cheese and cooked rice. Put 1/3 of  the rice mixture in a buttered 2 qt. casserole. Top with 1/3 of the  meat-tomato sauce. Continue to alternate layers, ending with tomato sauce.  Sprinkle with shredded cheese. Bake at 325F for 30 minutes or until hot and  bubbly.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #463 by sewin <hammer@imag.net> on Feb 01,  1997.
