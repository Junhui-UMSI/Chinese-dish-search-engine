Pistachio Delight
Walnuts Chopped, Pistachio instant pudding,lg, Colored Mini Marshmallows, Cool Whip, Crushed Pineapple undrained, 
In large bowl mix pudding and cool whip together. Add pineapple with liquid  and mix. Add walnuts and marshmallows. Refrigerate for 8 hours before  serving. This is a very colorful and delicious salad or dessert. You will  get a lot of compliments.
