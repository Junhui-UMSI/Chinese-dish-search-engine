Peas and Peanuts Salad
Garlic salt, Sour cream, Worcestershire Sauce, Small redskin peanuts;, Mayonnaise, Frozen peas; (20 oz.), 
Defrost peas (run under hot water and drain). Place peas and peanuts in a  large bowl. In a small box mix garlic salt, Worcestershire sauce,  mayonnaise and sour cream together; pour over peas and peanuts and mix  well. Chill. Note: Better if made same day as served.
