The Lairds Shrimp
Lemon juice, Garlic, Finely minced onion, Salt and pepper, Butter, Dried basil, Johnnie Walker Red, Raw shrimp; peeled and, 
Heat butter in skillet. Add garlic and onion and cook over low heat until  translucent. Add shrimp, Johnnie Walker Red, lemon juice and basil. Saute  shrimp just until pink, stirring often. Be careful not to overcook. Add  salt and pepper to taste. Makes 2-3 servings.    Recipe by: Johnnie Walker Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #752 by  L979@aol.com on Aug 21, 1997
