Peanut Butter Pie
Cool Whip whipped topping, Chopped peanuts,optional, Powdered sugar, Peanut butter, milk, Cream cheese,softened, 9" graham cracker pie shell, 
Whip cream cheese in mixer bowl until light.Blend in peanut butter and  powdered sugar.Add milk,gradually,beating constantly.Fold in whipped  topping.Spoon into pie shell.Garnish with chopped peanuts,if desired.Freeze  until firm.Makes 6 to 8 servings.
