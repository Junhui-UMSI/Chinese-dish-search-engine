Tridents Special Health Shake
Honey, Sugar, Strawberries; Fresh, Whole Cantaloupe, Ice, Yogurt, Wheat germ, Papaya, Fresh, Fresh Mint Leaves, 
1. In blender, combine half of all ingredients and blend until smooth. Turn  into chilled pitcher.    2. Blend remainder of ingredients and add to pitcher. To serve, pour into  chilled glasses. Garnish with mint.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net   (The Trident, Sausalito, California.)    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 319 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 27, 1997
