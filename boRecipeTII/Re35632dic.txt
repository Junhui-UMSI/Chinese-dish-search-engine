Stufato Di Manzo
Dry red wine from, Carrots, sliced, in 1/16", Turnips, cut in, 1/2" cubes, Veal stock, Basic tomato sauce, olive oil, Spanish onion, medium dice, Unsalted butter, Beef chuck, 
ut beef into 2 by 3 inch cubes and season with salt and pepper.    In an 8 quart heavy bottom Dutch oven, heat oil and butter over medium heat  until foam subsides. Brown beef pieces 4 to 5 at a time until dark golden  brown, about 12 to 20 minutes. This step cannot be shortened, it is  essential! When meat is done, remove last pieces and add onions, carrots  and turnips and cook until brown bits on bottom of pan have softened and  loosened. Add stock, tomato sauce and wine and bring to a boil. Add beef  pieces and return to boil. Lower heat to simmer, cover and cook 1 1/2 to 2  hours until meat is very tender. Remove and serve with Risotto Alla  Milanese.    Yield: 4 servings    Recipe by: Molto Mario Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #566 by Sue  <suechef@sover.net> on Apr 14, 1997
