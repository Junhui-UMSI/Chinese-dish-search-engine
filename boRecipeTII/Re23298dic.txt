Tuna Sauce
Italian tuna packed in oil, salt and pepper, Can chopped peeled tomatoes, White wine, Garlic chopped, Butter, Fresh chopped parsley, 
Melt butter and tuna oil in pan. Sautee garlic for 2 min, until golden. Add  tuna and cook 5 min. Add tomatoes, wine and parsly, salt and pepper and  cook on low heat 30 min. Serve over cooked, drained pasta. (wrv)
