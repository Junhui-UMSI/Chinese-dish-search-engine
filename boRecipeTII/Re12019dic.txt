Zhulyen Kurinyi V Kokotnitsakh
Sour cream, Swiss cheese, Butter, Onion, Musrooms, Nutmeg, Chicken boneless & skinless, 
Mince the onion. Mince the mushrooms, and the chicken together. Cook the  onion in 4 T of melted butter. When onions are done add the  chicken-mushroom mixture and cook for 5 minutes aqdding salt & pepper to  taste. Remove frm the heat and stirin the sour cream and the nutmeg.  Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Spoon the chicken mixture into 4  ramekins and sprinkle with grated cheese. bake for 10 minutes the serve  hot.
