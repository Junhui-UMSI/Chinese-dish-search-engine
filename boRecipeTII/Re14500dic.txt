"Best" Apple Nut Pudding
Egg, Brown sugar, Vanilla, Flour; heaping tablespoons, Salt, Nutmeg, Nuts;chopped;walnuts, pecans, Apples; pared chopped fine, Baking Powder, 
Break egg into bowl, beat with sugar and vanilla. Add flour, baking powder,  salt, nutmeg, and blend well. Stir in nuts and chopped apple. Bake in  greased and floured 9-inch square baking dish or pan, in moderate oven  (350^) for 30 minutes.
