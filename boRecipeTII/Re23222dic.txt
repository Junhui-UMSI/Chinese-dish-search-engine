Tuna Jambalaya
Water; boiling, Fresh parsley, Salt, Strips bacon, cut 1-in piece, Onion, Thyme, Tomatoes; peeled and chopped, Bay leaf, Tuna; drained, Cloves garlic; minced (or 3), Pepper, Rice; raw, 
In large, heavy skillet cook the bacon until crisp, remove from pan and set  aside.  In same pan saute onion and garlic until tender. If bacon is very  fatty, drain off some of the grease.  Add bay leaf and thyme and cook  stirring constantly for 5 minutes over medium heat. Add tomatoes and cook  for 10 minutes. Add tuna and reserved bacon pieces. Season with salt and  pepper, stir in the rice, and add approximately 2 cups of boiling water to  reach 1 ince above the mixture. Cover the pan tightyly and simmer over low  heat until rice is tender (approximately 30 minutes). If there is too much  liquid, continue to simmer with cover removed. Sprinkle each serving  liberally with chopped fresh parsley.
