Strawberry/peach Baked Dessert
FRUIT BASE, Sugar, Peaches, Brown sugar, Strawberries, Whole wheat flour, Cinnamon, Lemon juice, TOPPING, Nutmeg, Butter, Dry oatmeat, Whole wheat flour, 
Combine and blend all ingredients for fruit base, put into 8" square glass  baking dish.    Make crumb mixture of topping ingredients and sprinkle over fruit.    Bake at 375 F for up to 35 minutes.  Check at about 25 minutes.    The topping should be light brown on top, do not burn.    Can be served with whipped cream, sour cream or yogurt on top.
