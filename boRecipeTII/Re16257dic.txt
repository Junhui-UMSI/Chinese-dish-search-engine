Watergate Cake
pistacio instant, 7-Up soft drink, white cake mix, milk, WATERGATE FROSTING, Vegetable oil, nuts, Dream Whip mix, Eggs, pistacio instant, 
From:    "Sharon H. Frye" <shfrye@PEN.K12.VA.US>    Date:    Sat, 13 Jul 1996 12:52:03 EDT  Combine and beat according to the directions on the box of came mix. Pour  into a greased/floured tube or Bundt cake pan. Bake in a preheated 350  degree oven for 35-40 minutes, or until pick inserted near center comes out  clean. Let cool 5 minutes in pan, then remove and cool completely. Frost  with Watergate frosting.    Watergate Frosting: Beat all ingredients together on high speed until  mixture reaches a spreading consistency. Top the watergate cake with the  frosting, and sprinkle with chopped nuts, if desired.    Store leftovercake in the refrigerator. This can also be baked in a sheet  cake pan, but baking time must be adjusted.    EAT-L Digest 12 July 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
