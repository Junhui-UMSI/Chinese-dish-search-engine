Popovers Variations
***  NON   NE *****, 
PICK-A-FLOUR POPOVERS: Prepare Popovers as directed, EXCEPT reduce  all-purpose flour to 2/3 cup and add 1/3 cup whole sheat or rye flour or  1/4 cup Masa Harina Tortilla Flour.    LEMON-POPPY SEED POPOVERS: Prepare Popovers as directed, EXCEPT add 1 Tbsp  poppy seed and 1 1/2 tsp finely shredded lemon or orange peel with the  flour and salt.    SPICED POPOVERS: Prepare Popovers as directed, EXCEPT add 2 tsp brown  sugar, 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon, 1/4 tsp ground ginger, and 1/8 tsp ground  cloves with the flour and salt.    BUTERMILK POPOVERS: Prepare Popovers as directed, EXCEPT substitute 1 cup  buttermilk for the milk.    GARLIC N CHIVE POPOVERS: Prepare Popovers as directed, EXCEPT add 1 Tbsp  snipped chives or parsley and 1/8 tsp garlic powder with the flour and  salt.
