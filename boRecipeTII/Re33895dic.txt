Sweet and Sour Vegetables
Firm tofu; cubed, 8-oz pineapple chunks in, Red or green bell pepper;, Shredded cabbage, Broccoli; chopped, Sweet and sour sauce;, 
Substitute a bag of cole-slaw mix. Use a sweet and sour "cooking sauce".    Heat sweet-and-sour sauce in large saucepan with chopped pepper and  broccoli; simmer about 5 minutes. Add tofu cubes, cabbage and pineapple  with its juice. Heat through an additional 3-5 minutes. Serve over brown  rice.    Serves 4, with 330 calories and 7 grams fat per serving.
