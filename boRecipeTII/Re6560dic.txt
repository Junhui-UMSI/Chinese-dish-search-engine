Poached Salmon Steaks
Salmon steaks; 1 to 1-1/2, White wine, Water, POACHING LIQUID, Onion, Salt, Carrot, Stalk celery, 
Put ingredients for poaching liquid into a large skillet and simmer 15-20  minutes.     Add salmon steaks to simmering liquid. Skillet should be large enough to  hold steaks in a single layer. Simmer 8-12 minutes or until fish is done  when fork pierces the flesh easily.     Remove steaks from liquid with large slotted spoon or pancake turner.  Serve with "Cucumber Sauce" (see recipe in this cookbook).
