Barbecued Meat Loaf
Cider vinegar, Ground chuck, Fresh bread crumbs, (8-oz) tomato sauce, Prepared mustard, Egg, Salt, Ketchup -or-, Brown sugar, Onion; minced, Pepper, 
Put beef, onion, bread crumbs, egg, salt, pepper and 1/2 cup ketchup in  bowl. Mix lightly until well blended. Transfer to loaf pan. Mix remaining  ketchup, sugar, mustard and vinegar; pour on top of loaf. Bake at 350 for 1  hour. Let stand 5 minutes before serving. To make bread crumbs, quarter 3  slices fresh white bread and put into blender. Cover and blend at high  speed about 10 seconds.
