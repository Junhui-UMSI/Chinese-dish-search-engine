Baked Hush Puppies
Yellow cornmeal, Flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Onion, Skim milk, Vegetable oil, Pepper, Egg, 
Combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder and salt in a medium bowl.  Make a well in the center of mixture.  Combine onion, mil, pepper and egg.  Add to dry ingredients, stirring just until moistened.    Spoon 1 tablespoon of batter into minature muffin pans coated with cooking spray.   Bake At 450 For 10 Minutes or until lightly browned.    Remove from pan immediately.
