Tomatoes, Green Pepper, and Cucumbers
Green pepper, Salt, tomatoes, fresh mint leaves, Sliced, Lemon juice, Parsley, olive oil, Green onions, Cucumbers, 
Place tomatoes, pepper, cucumber, parsley, mint (if used), and green onion  in a salad bowl. Beat together lemon juice, olive oil, and salt; pour over  vegetables, toss, and serve.    Serves 4.
