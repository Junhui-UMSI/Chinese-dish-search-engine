Tomato, Green Pepper, and Cucumber Salad
Green onions, Fresh Lemon Juice, Cucumbers, Salt, Parsley, fresh mint leaves, olive oil, Green bell pepper, Ripe tomatoes, Sliced, 
Place tomatoes, pepper, cucumber, parsley, mint (if used), and green onion  in a salad bowl. Beat together lemon juice, olive oil, and salt; pour over  vegetables, toss, and serve.
