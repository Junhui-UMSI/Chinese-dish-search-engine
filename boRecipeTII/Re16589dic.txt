Aioli
Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Lemon juice, Salt, Black Pepper, Garlic, Tabasco, Worcestershire Sauce, Egg yolks, Boiling water, 
1. Mix yolks, garlic, lemon, salt and pepper in a food processor. Blend for        3    minutes.    2. Dribble oil in until it begins to thicken and is beginning to blend.    3. Add the rest of the oil.    4. Add in seasonings to taste.    5. Finish with boiling water and refrigerate immediately.    Notes: For this tasty, tangy French-style garlic mayonnaise, use only  top-quality, extra virgin olive oil and fresh-squeezed lemon juice.
