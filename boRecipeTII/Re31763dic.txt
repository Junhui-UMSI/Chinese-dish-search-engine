Syrian Feta Spread
Dill; chopped, Garlic, Parsley, Ricotta cheese, Softened butter, Feta cheese, 
Process feta until finely crumbled. Add herbs and garlic and process. Add  ricotta and butter.  Blend until smooth.    Suggested Wine: New Land Vineyards 1994 Dry Riesling    NOTES : Chopped walnuts, black olives or scallions may be added.
