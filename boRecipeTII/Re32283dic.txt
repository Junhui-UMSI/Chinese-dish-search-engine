Barbeque Sauce Ala Jetton
Black pepper, Salt, Chili powder, Water, Paprika, Garlic, Onion, Cider vinegar, Stalks celery, chopped, Butter, Tomato ketchup, Bay Leaves, Sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Combine all ingredients and bring to a boil.  Simmer about 15  minutes. Remove from heat and strain.  This is a table sauce  to be served with beef, chicken or pork.  Do not cook things  in it.
