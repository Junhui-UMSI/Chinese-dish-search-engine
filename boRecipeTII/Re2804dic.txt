Peanut Butter Cookies #2
Peanut Butter (Creamy), Granulated sugar, Baking Powder, Brown Sugar (firmly packed), Egg, Flour, milk, Salt, Baking soda, Vanilla, Shortening, 
1.  Heat oven to 375xF.    2. Cream together; shortening, peanut butter, both sugars and vanilla. Beat  with electric mixer at medium speed for one minute. Add egg and milk. Beat  until well blended.    3.  Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt and add to creamed  mixture.  Mix well.    4.  Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten  in crisscross pattern with fork dipped in flour.  Bake for 8 to 10 minutes.  Cool 2 minutes then remove cookies from cookie sheet onto a cooling rack.    Makes 2 dozen cookies.
