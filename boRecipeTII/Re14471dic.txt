Whole Wheat-Rhubarb Pudding Cake
rhubarb, Sugar, Whipped cream, Baking Powder, milk, Cornstarch, Pecans, Boiling water, Margarine, All purpose flour, Ground cinnamon, Whole wheat flour, Sugar, Salt, Ground nutmeg, Sugar, 
Place the sliced rhubarb in a 13x9x2 inch baking dish. Sprinkle the 1/4 cup  sugar over the rhubarb. In a medium mixing bowl stir together the  all-purpose flour, the 1 cup sugar, whole wheat flour, chopped pecans,  baking powder, cinnamon, salt, and nutmeg. Add the milk and melted butter.  Stir till combined. Spoon the batter over the rhubarb and spread evenly. In  a medium mixing bowl stir together the 1 1/4 cups sugar and the cornstarch.  Add the boiling water and stir till sugar dissolves. Slowly pour the  mixture over the top of the batter. Bake in a 375 oven about 45 minutes or  till top tests done. Let cool for 30 minutes. Serve with whipped cream if  desired.    Recipe by: Better Homes & Gardens Farmers Market Cookbook    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #996 by L979 <L979@aol.com> on Jan 8, 1998
