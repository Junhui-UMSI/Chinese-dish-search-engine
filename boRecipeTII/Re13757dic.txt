Wild Rice W/almonds and Mushroom
Chicken broth, Slivered almonds, Unsalted butter, Minced green bell pepper, Fresh ground black pepper to, Fresh mushrooms; sliced thin, Salt, Uncooked wild rice, Clove garlic; finely minced, 
Preheat oven to 350. Melt butter in a large saucepan. Add mushrooms,  garlic, bell pepper, almonds & rice; cook, stirring, until mushrooms are  soft. Pour mixture into a large, shallow baking dish; stir in broth, salt &  pepper. Cover & bake about 1-1/2 hours or until rice is tender. Makes 6  servings.    From the <Hotter Than Hell!>, by Jane Butel, ISBN 0-89586-646-3  (0-89586-542-4 paperback).  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
