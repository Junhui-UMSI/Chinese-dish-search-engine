Persimmon Bar Cookies
Cinnamon, Cream, Eggs, Nuts, Powdered sugar, Baking Powder, Sugar, Salt, Margarine, FROSTING, Persimmon pulp, Flour, Baking soda, Vanilla, 
Combine salt, flour, baking powder and soda, and cinnamon. Set aside. Beat  eggs, add sugar gradually. Add remaining ingredients and blend. Spread in a  greased jelly roll pan 10 1/2 x 15 1/2 inch or 2 greased 9 x 9 inch pans.  Bake 30 to 40 minutes at 325 degrees. Frost and sprinkle with nuts if  desired. Cut into bars.
