Plastic Bag Ice Cream
Sugar, Salt (Rock?), Pint size Zip-lock Bag, Ice cubes, Gallon size Zip-lock Bag, milk, Vanilla, 
Put milk, sugar, and vanilla in the pint size bag and close. Put this bag  inside the inside the gallon size bag. Fill large bag with ice cubes and 6  ~ 7 tablespoons salt. Close tightly and shake vigorously for about 5  minutes or until ice cream is done.    From: Theresa Hart, 7/5/96 Press Enterprise    Note: Fruit and/or syrup can be added to the ice cream.
