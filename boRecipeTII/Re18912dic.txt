Angel Food Cake Ala Mode
Sugar, Grand Marnier, Slices angel food cake, Blueberries, Scoops vanilla ice cream, 
In a bowl mix berries with sugar and Grand Marnier.    On each plate, set a slice of cake and top it with a scoop  of ice cream. Spoon berry mixture over and around the cake.
