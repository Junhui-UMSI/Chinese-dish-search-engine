Apricot Salad
(20 oz.) crushed pineapple, (8 oz.) cream cheese;, Water, Jars; (4 3/4 oz.) apricot, (3 oz.) apricot flavored, Sugar, (14 oz.) sweetened condensed, 
Combine Jello, sugar, and water in small pan. Bring to a boil, stirring to  dissolve sugar and Jello. Remove from heat and stir in fruit. Set aside to  cool. Combine condensed milk and cream cheese.
