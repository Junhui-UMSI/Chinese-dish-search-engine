Very Hot Swedish Mustard
Cream, (2-oz) dry mustard, Egg yolks, Sugar, Brown sugar, Garlic vinegar, 
Mix all ingredients with a fork until smooth. Let stand overnight. This  mixture gets hotter with age. Use sparingly. Keep refrigerated. Makes about    1-1/3    cups.
