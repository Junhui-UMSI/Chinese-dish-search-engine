Beef Burgundy #1
(10.7-oz) cream of celery, (10.75-oz) cream of mushroom, Burgundy wine, (10-oz) cream of chicken, Onion soup mix, Beef; cubed, 
Mix soups and wine; add beef. Put in large casserole and bake uncovered at  300 for 3 hours. Serve over rice or noodles.
