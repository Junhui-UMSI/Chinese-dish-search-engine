Pjs Cinnamon Swirl Bread
Warm water, Active dry yeast; (instant), Cinnamon ; mixed with, Bread flour, Sugar, Powdered milk, Salt, Unsalted butter; cut into, Warm water, Sugar, 
Place ingredients, except cinnamon-sugar, into bread machine and run on  "dough" cycle.    When machine has completed cycle, place dough on lightly oiled surface and  let sit for 5 minutes. Roll into 8X15" rectangle, sprinkle with  cinnamon-sugar. Starting with short end, roll dough into tight log. Tuck  ends under.  Put loaf seam side down in lightly greased bread pan. Let rise  in warm place for 1-1.5 hours.    Bake bread in 375 oven for 35-40 minutes.  May need aluminum foil tent last  10 minutes of baking to keep it from getting too brown.
