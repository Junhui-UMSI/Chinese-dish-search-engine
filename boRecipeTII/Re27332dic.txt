Toad-In-The-Hole Cornmeal Muffins
WET MIX, Cocktail sausages, DRY MIX, Flour, Sugar, Fine cornmeal, milk, Melted margarine, Baking Powder, Egg, 
Reheat oven to 400F, prepare pans. Blend wet mix ingredients (except  sausages). Add dry mix previously combined until just blended. Two-thirds  fill the pans & pop a sausage into the middle of each muffin. The mixture  should be firm enough to hold up the sausages. Bake for 15-20 mins. Makes  15 muffins. These are great for picnics or school lunches.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
