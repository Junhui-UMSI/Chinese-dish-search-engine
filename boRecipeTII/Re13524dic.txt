Whoopie Pie (Filling)
Milk,, add up to 3 teaspoon, Butter, Vanilla, 
In electric mixer beat butter til fluffy, add confectioners sugar, vanilla  and milk. Beat til fluffy.    Spread in the middle of two whoopie pie cakes.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #496 by Steve Parks <CobraSon@concentric.net>  on Mar 3, 1997.
