Baked Curried Onions
Butter, Cayenne pepper, Onions; sliced, Paprika, Bread; cut in triangles and, milk, -(up to), Flour, Sharp cheese; grated, Bouillon cubes, Curry powder, Water, 
Place onions in a saucepan with water. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. In  a double boiler, melt the butter and stir in the flour and seasonings. Add  bouillon cubes and stir in milk gradually. Add cheese, saving a few  Tablespoons to spread on top. Stir constantly until mixture is very thick.  Place toast in a buttered 2 quart casserole dish and cover with drained  onions. Pour sauce over them and sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 350? for 20  to       30    minutes.  Yield: 6 servings.    MARGARET WINDSOR CLARK    (MRS.
