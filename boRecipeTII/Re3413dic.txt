Pecan Brown Bread
Buttermilk, Molasses, Whole-wheat flour, Salt, Pecans, Baking soda, 
Sift dry ingredients together, saving just a bit of flour to mix later with  the pecans. Add buttermilk and molasses. Mix reserved flour with pecans and  stir into batter.    Put into well-greased 9 by 5-inch bread pan. Bake in 300 degree oven about        1    hour or until it tests done.    Posted to Recipe Archive - 09 Feb 97 by ted by: ajewell@sound.net on Feb 9,  97.
