Peanut Butter and Jelly
Unbleached flour, Peanut butter, Skim milk, Applesauce -- at room, Egg white -- whipped, Grape or strawberry jelly, Salt, Temperature, Baking Powder, Granulated sugar, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray.  In a mixing bowl,  combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.  In another mixing bowl,  combine peanut butter, milk, applesauce, egg white, and jelly.  Mix dry  ingredients with wet ingredients just until moistened.  Bake for 25 to 30  minutes depending on pan size.
