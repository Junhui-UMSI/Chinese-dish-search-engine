Emerils Quick Horseradish Sauce
horseradish, White vinegar, Sugar, Sour cream, Salt and pepper, 
In a small saucepan combine horseradish, vinegar, sugar and bring to a  boil. Simmer until liquid has reduced by half. Cool slightly. In a small  bowl whisk sour cream to lighten. Add horseradish mixture, stir until well  combined and season to taste with salt and pepper. Formatted by  jayne@idt.net    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Jayne Oake <jayne@idt.net> on Aug 12, 1998
