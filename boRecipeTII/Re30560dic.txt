Taggarts Thanksgiving Cranberry Salad Supreme
Water -- boiling, Can, Raspberry jello mix, Juice, Whipping cream, Pkg, Minature marshmallows, Pkg, Cream cheese, Mayonnaise, Lemon jello mix, Pineapple -- crushed/in, Water -- boiling, Can, Whole berry cranberry sauce, 
Dissolve the raspberry jello mix into 1 cup of boiling water. Stir in the  cranberry sauce until well blended. Pour the raspberry/cranberry mixture  into the bottom of a 1 1/2 quart round mold or bowl and chill until the  mixture is set (about 1 hour). Dissolve the lemon jello mix into 1 cup of  boiling water and set aside. Beat together the cream cheese and mayonnaise  until smooth. Gradually add the dissolved lemon gelatin until well blended  and stir in the pineapple. Chill until partially set. Whip the cream until  stiff and fold into the partially set lemon mixture. Add the marshmallows  to the mixture and spread the mixture over the set raspberry/cranberry  mixture. Chill overnight.
