Taskebab
Onion,diced, salt and pepper, Oregano, olive oil, Light tomato sauce, Diced beef, Basil, Eggplant, peeled and diced, 
Fry beef and eggplant in oil in skillet (or eggplant may be deep-  fried).Prepare light tomato sauce.Combine with remaining ingredients.  bake,uncovered,on low heat about 45 minutes,as for stew,to allow sauce to  blend through meat and eggplant.Serves 4 to 5.
