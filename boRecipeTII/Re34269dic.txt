Bbq Lamb Shoulder
Catsup, Worcestershire Sauce, Pepper, Celery salt, Honey, Boned shoulder of lamb, 
Combine catsup, celery salt, Worcestershire, pepper & honey; mix well.  Place lamb on spit; brush with honey mixture. Cook on rotisserie 2  hours, brushing frequently with honey mixture during cooking.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
