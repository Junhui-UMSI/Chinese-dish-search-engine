American-English Trifle
nuts, Canned, Whipping cream, Jello, Vanilla pudding mix, Fruit juice, white cake, 
Combine jello with hot fruit juice. Crumble cake in dish. Add drained fruit  & pour slightly thickened jello on fruit. Prepare pudding mix. After jello  is set, top with pudding; add a layer of whipped cream & decorate with nuts  & cherries.
