Potato-Mushroom Soup with Apple
Scallions; sliced thinly, Apple, Rome Beauty; cored, Margarine, nondairy, Soy sauce, Thyme, dried, Pepper, Parsley, Italian, fresh;, Vegetable oil, Mushrooms; sliced, Onion, Vegetable stock, Potatoes; peeled and thinly, 
In a large saucepan, saute scallions and onion in margarine and oil over  medium-high heat for 2 minutes.   Add potatoes and mushrooms and saute 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Add  broth, soy sauce and pepper.   Cover and simmer 15 minutes, or until potatoes are tender. Add thyme and  taste for seasoning. Before serving, add parsley and apple. Serves 6.     Per serving: 111 cal; 2 g prot; 5 g fat; 15 g carb; 0 chol; 212 mg sod; 2  g fiber     From the files of DEEANNE
