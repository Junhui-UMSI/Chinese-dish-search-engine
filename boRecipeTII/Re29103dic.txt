Baked Quail
butter, Salt, Quail, Water, Pepper, Flour, 
Salt & pepper each quail. Put in paper sack with flour & toss to coat.  Butter bottom of iron skillet or Dutch oven & place quail in skillet. Pour  melted butter over quail, making sure it covers each one. Pour water in  bottom of skillet & put lid on top. Cook at 300 for 2 hours or longer. Add  more water if needed. Use water drippings to make gravy.    JEWELL GRIFFIN    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
