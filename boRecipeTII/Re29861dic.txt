Tennessee Mountain Stickies
Brown sugar, milk, Butter, Buttermilk baking mix, 
From: bobbi744@sojourn.com (Roberta Banghart)    Date: Wed, 03 Jul 1996 15:10:09 -0400  Recipe By: Recipe from The Nantucket Diet Murders by Virginia Rich    Melt butter in a heavy 10" skillet (an Iron Spider )  with brown  sugar.  Prepare dough, using 2 cup biscuit mix and milk. Turn out on floured board.  Knead lightly. Roll to rectangle about 8x12". Spread with most of sugar  mixture. Roll up jelly roll style and cut in 1" slices. Arrange in same  skillet and pour boiling water to barely cover. Bake, uncovered at 350? for  30 minutes until brown and bubbling. Serve hot or old in cereal or berry  bowls. Can serve with cream.    MC-Recipe Digest V1 #138    From the MasterCook recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
