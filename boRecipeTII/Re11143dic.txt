Raisin Catsup Cookies
Shortening, Flour, Egg, Sugar, Catsup, Chopped raisins, Salt, Baking soda, 
Cream shortening and sugar.  Add catsup and unbeaten egg. Beat thoroughly.  Add raisins.  Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking soda and salt. Add  to first mixture. Mix thoroughly. Turn onto lightly floured board. Knead  thoroughly.  Form into roll 2 inches in diameter. Chill overnight. Cut in  thin slices. Place on well-oiled baking sheet. Bake in hot oven (410 F)  about 10 minutes.  24 servings. The Household Searchlight
