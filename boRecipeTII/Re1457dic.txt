Father Georges Favorite Seafood Sin
Butter, Ribs celery; finely chopped, Onion, Bell peppers; finely chopped, Green onions with tops;, Lump crabmeat, Shrimp; deveined,, Crawfish tails, (8-oz) mushrooms; stems and, (12-oz) of nacho cheese, Jar pimentos; drained, Half-and-half, Bottled garlic, (8 ounces) egg noodles;, Flour, salt and pepper, Dried parsley, Paprika, 
From the Sun-Herald Cooks Exchange:    Prepare noodles according to package directions. Set aside. Saute celery,  onion, bell pepper, green onion and garlic in butter for 10 minutes. Add  shrimp, crabmeat, crawfish tails and mushrooms and saute for 10 minutes  more. Add flour and mix well. Stir in sauce, pimentoss and cream, mixing  well. Stir nacho cheese sauce into pimentos and cream, mixing well. Season  with Tony Chacheres Famous Creole Seasoning, add salt and pepper to taste.    Combine seafood mixture with cooked noodles. If sauce is too thin, add  additional cheese sauce and flour to thicken. Pour into a 12-by-1-inch  casserole dish. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, dried parsley and paprika; dot  lightly with butter. Bake at 400 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes.    Posted to CHILE-HEADS DIGEST by Judy Howle <howle@ebicom.net> on Sep 27,  1998,
