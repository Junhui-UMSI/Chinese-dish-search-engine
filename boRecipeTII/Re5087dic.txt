Pico De Gallo 2
Ground cumin, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Onion, Salt, Fresh cilantro, Tomatoes, Fresh lime juice, Garlic, Jalapeno chile pepper, fresh, 
Combine all ingredients in bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Cover; chill  at least 1 hour before serving.    Per serving: 48 Calories; 3g Fat (44% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 6g  Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 187mg Sodium    NOTES : Yield:  about 5 cups. Keeps well chilled.
