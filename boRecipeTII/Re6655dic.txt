Polenta Bread with Rosemary and Garlic
Dried Rosemary; (1 1/2 T), 1 LB LOAF:; (1 1/2 LB LOAF, Active Dry Yeast; (1 1/2 T), Yellow Cornmeal; (1/3 C), Bread Flour; (3 C), Water; Plus, Garlic Clove; Chopped, (1, Salt; (3/4 T), Water; (1 1/4 C), 
Slice thin and cut into triangles.  Toast lightly and serve alone or with  Italian-style salsa made with tomato, hot pepper and olives.  Or top with  grilled chicken or grilled vegetables for an open-faced sandwich.    Add all ingredients in order suggested by your bread machine manual, follow  the manufacturers directions and bake on basic bread setting.    This is very good.
