Tropical Ice
Undrained (20 oz.), Sugar, Can Maraschino Cherries --, Lemon juice, White wine, Crushed Pineapple --, Mashed Bananas, Ginger Ale, Orange juice, 
Combine first 6 ingredients in blender and freeze until firm. To serve  punch, partially thaw fruit mixture. Place in punch bowl and break into  chunks. Add ginger ale and white wine to taste.    Recipe By     :    From:                                 Date:
