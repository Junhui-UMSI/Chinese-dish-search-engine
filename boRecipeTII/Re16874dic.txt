All-Purpose Mix
MIX INGREDIENTS, All-Purpose Flour, milk, Sugar, Grated American cheese, FOR MUFFINS, milk, FOR PANCAKES, Mix, Mix, Paprika, Egg, Mix, FOR CHEESE BISCUITS, Mix, Crisco shortening, milk, milk, Baking Powder, FOR BISCUITS, Sugar, Eggs, Salt, 
Stir baking powder, salt & sugar into flour. Sift together 3 times into  large mixing bowl. Cut in shortening with pastry blender until mix is  consistency of cornmeal. Store in a tightly covered container at room  temperature. To measure mix, pile it lightly into a standard cup & level  off with a spatula. BISCUITS; Stir ingredients & roll as for other  biscuits. Bake at 450 until done. PANCAKES: Stir ingredients together &  grill as usual. CHEESE BISCUITS: Mix ingredients, bake as for biscuits.  MUFFINS:  Mix & bake at 425.
