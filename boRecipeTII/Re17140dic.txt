Virgin Pina Colada
Pineapple juice, Orange, Coconut cream, Crushed, Crushed pineapple, Maraschino cherry, 
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend at high setting until smooth  ~- about 30 seconds. Strain into highball or collins glass and garnish with  orange slice and cherry.    NOTE: Crushed pineapple juice canned in its own juice is preferable for a  less-sweet drink; use pineapple canned in syrup for a sweeter concoction.
