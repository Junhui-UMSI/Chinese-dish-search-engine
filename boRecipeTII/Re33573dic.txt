Swedish Cream with Berries
strawberries, unflavored gelatin, Sour cream, Cold water, Whipping cream, Sugar, Vanilla extract, 
Dissolve gelatin in water, let stand 5 minutes to soften. Place cream in  saucepan; add sugar and gelatin. Heat gently until of a creamy consistency,  stirring gently. Remove from heat and cool until thick. Place in  refrigerator 30 to 60 minutes to hasten thickening. When partially  thickened, fold in sour cream and vanilla. Pour into sherbet glasses,  leaving room for the berries. Chill 8 hours. Remove from refrigerator,  spoon berries over the top of the Swedish cream. Juice from the berries  adds flavor. This is lovely for a luncheon.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
