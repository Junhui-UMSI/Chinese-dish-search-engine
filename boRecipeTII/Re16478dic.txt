Wendys Peanut Butter Cookies
Baking soda, Granulated sugar, Egg, Butter, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, All purpose flour, Whole or crush peanuts;, Peannut butter; (smooth or, Salt, Shortening, 
1.In a large bowl, mix together sugars, peanut butter, shortening, butter  and egg. Stir well.    2.Mix together flour, baking soda, baking powder and slat, and slowly mix  into peanut butter mixture.    3.Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours    4.Shape dough into 1 1/4 inch balls and place about 3 inches apart on an  ugreased cookies sheet. Flatten in a criss cross pattern with a fork dipped  in flour.    5.Bake about 9 minutes, until just light brown. 6.Remove from oven, let  cool two minutes and remove from cookie sheet.    Notes: When baking cookies, place the cookie sheet with the cookies on the  centre rack in your oven, and a second cookie sheet on the rack below. This  makes for nice chewy cookies... Preheat oven to 375? Yield: 85 cookies  approx.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by RecipeLu <recipelu@geocities.com> on Feb 14,  1998
