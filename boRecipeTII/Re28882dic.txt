Thai Iced Coffee #2
Ground coffee, Crushed ice, Water, Sweetened; condensed milk, 
1.  In a 6 inch strainer, place a coffee filter.  Put the coffee into the  filter and place over a 2 quart pot.    2.  Bring the water to a boil and pour over the coffee.  Stir the water  continuously into the coffee filter.    3.  Let cool to lukewarm.  Pour into tall glasses filled with ice.    4.  Add about 1 inch of condensed milk and stir.  Serves 4.
