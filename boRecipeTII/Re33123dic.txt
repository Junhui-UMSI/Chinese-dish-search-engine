Basic Gazpacho
Red wine vinegar, Ripe tomatoes, peeled,;, Chopped jalapeno pepper, Toasted French bread, Lime; cut into slices, Garlic, Cucumber, peeled,; seeded, Chopped red pepper, Good olive oil, Tomato juice, Chopped red onion, Celery, Salt, pepper, ground, Chopped cilantro, 
Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor (except bread and  limes), and puree until smooth. Transfer to a large bowl and refrigerate  for 4 hours. Meanwhile prepare relish.
