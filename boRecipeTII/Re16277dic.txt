Watermelon and Pepper Sauce
Fresh lime juice, Adjusted to taste), Hot pepper sauce (can be, Cubed, seeded watermelon, Salt, 
Combine lime juice, salt & pepper sauce. Put watermellon cubes on serving  tray and juice mixture in a bowl in the middle of the watermelon cubes. Dip  watermelon cubes in juice.
