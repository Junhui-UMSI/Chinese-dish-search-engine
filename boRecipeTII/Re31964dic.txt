Taco Filling
TACO SEASONING MIX, Ground beef, Water, 
Brown ground beef in a med skillet over med high heat. Drain excess grease.  Add water and TACO SEASONING MIX. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes  stirring occasionally. Makes filling for 8 to 10 tacos.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 565 by Midian125  <Midian125@aol.com> on Jan 20, 1998
