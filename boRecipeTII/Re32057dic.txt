Barbecued Brisket
Salt and pepper to taste, Bottle (small) liquid smoke, Beef brisket whatever size, 
Rub salt and pepper into sach side.  Pour liquid smoke over each side of  brisket.  Wrap completely and place in large cooking pan. Bake in 200 F.  oven for 4-5 hrs., or until tender. Unwrap foil and drain. Pour on BBQ  sauce, over and under meat. Return wrapped brisket to oven for 45 min. When  serving, slice ACROSS grain.    SLOW COOKING IS THE SECRET FOR TENDER BRISKET.
