Sweet and Sour Sausage Kabobs
Bell pepper -- in 10 chunks, Polska kielbasa -- in 20, Red cooking apples -- in 20, Brown Sugar, Cider vinegar, Wedges, Cornstarch, Pieces, Orange Peel, Apple juice, 
Heat grill.  In small pan, combine all glaze ing, blending well.  Cook over  medium heat until mixture boils and thickens,  stirring constantly.  alternately thread sausage, apples, and peppers on 5 12" skewers Grill over  med hi gas heat or 4-6 inches from coals.  Cook 4 mins. Brush with glaze,  turn, brush again.  Cook 4 mins more.  Serve with remaining glaze.
