Quick Fruit Salad
3oz pkg, Mandarin oranges -- drained, Juice, Instant vanilla pudding, By, (with garnishing tool sold, 20oz can, Chunk pineapple -- with, 11oz can, Seedless grapes, Bananas -- sliced, Pampered Chef), 
Pour pineapple and juice into bowl.  Sprinkle dry pudding over it and stir.  Add drained oranges and grapes and refrigerate.  Just before serving, add  bananas.  Serves 6-8. Posted by DDMom.
