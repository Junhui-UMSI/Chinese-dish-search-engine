Beef Bulgogi
Soy sauce, Beef, Sugar, Saki, Peanut oil, Red pepper flakes, Sesame oil, Sesame salt, Fresh ground black pepper, Fresh ginger root, garlic, Scallion, Sesame seeds, 
Combine soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, sesame salt (toasted sesame seeds  ground with salt), pepper, scallions, garlic, ginger, saki and red pepper  flakes in a medium-size bowl. Add meat and toss. Marinate 30 minutes or  more.
