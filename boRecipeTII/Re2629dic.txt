Peanut Brittle Ice Cream Pie W/chocolate
Pecans, Vanilla ice cream, -(up to), Light cream, Salt, (7-oz) coconut, All-purpose flour, Stick margarine; melted, FILLING, Crushed peanut brittle, FUDGE SAUCE, Granulated sugar, Butter or margarine, Vanilla, Crust, Cocoa, Light corn syrup, 
CRUST: Mix flour with coconut and pecans; add melted margarine and stir.  Pat into 10-inch pie plate. Bake for 10-12 minutes until lightly browned at      350    degrees.     FILLING: Mix softened ice cream and peanut brittle. Place into cooled pie  shell. Freeze at least 6 hours.     FUDGE SAUCE: Mix first 5 ingredients in sauce pan. Bring to boil, boiling  for 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat, add salt and vanilla.  Cool and drizzle over whipped cream, atop pie. All steps may be done the  day before presentation.
