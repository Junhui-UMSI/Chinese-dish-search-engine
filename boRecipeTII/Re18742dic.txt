Anadama Bread #5
Salt, Molasses, White granulated sugar, Bread flour, Dry yeast (not rapid rise), Margerine, Corn meal, Boiling water, Egg, 
From:    "Sharon H. Frye" <shfrye@PEN.K12.VA.US>    Date:    Tue, 20 Aug 1996 18:15:08 EDT  Combine water, corn meal, molasses, margerine, and sugar in a bowl. Stir  until margerine dissolves. Cool to room temperature.    In bread machine basket place flour and salt. Add the egg. Pour the cooled  liquid on top.    Place bread basket in machine. Put yeast in yeast dispenser.  Select white  bread, sandwich mode. This will take four hours.    EAT-L Digest 19 August 96    From the EAT-L recipe list.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
