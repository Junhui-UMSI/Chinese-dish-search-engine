Trout Appetit with Remoulade Sauce
Horseradish mustard, Paprika, Catsup, Smoked trout, Garlic, Celery, Tarragon Vinegar, Cayenne pepper, Salad oil, Shallots, REMOULADE SAUCE, Salt, 
Remove skin and bones from fish.  Flake and place in cocktail glasses or  iced supreme bowls lined with lettuce.    Place all ingreds.  for Remoulade Sauce in blender and blend thoroughly.    Chill well before serving.    Smoked trout may be purchased at kosher deli.
