Tuna-Waldorf Salad Mold
(6 ounces) lemon-flavored, (6-1/2 ounces) starkist?, Low-fat milk, Reduced-calorie mayonnaise, Finely chopped celery, Finely chopped unpeeled, Boiling water, Apple juice, Chopped pecans or walnuts, 
In a large, shallow bowl dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Stir in apple  juice until blended. Cover; chill mixture about 30 minutes, or until the  consistency of unbeaten egg whites. Fold in tuna, apple, pecans and celery.  Spray a 4- or 5-cup mold (or 5 individual 1-cup molds) with aerosol  shortening; pour in mixture. Cover; chill several hours until set. For  dressing, in a small bowl stir together mayonnaise and milk; serve with  salad.    Notes: Make 4 main-dish or 8 side-dish servings.    Recipe by: StarKist?
