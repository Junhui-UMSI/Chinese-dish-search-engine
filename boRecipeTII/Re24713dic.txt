Au Gratin Potatoes and Ham
Thyme, potatoes, cubed cooked ham, milk, Condensed cream of mushroom, american cheese, Onion, 
In slow cooker, layer half each of the potatoes, onion, ham and cheese;  repeat layers. In small bowl, combine soup, milk and thyme; pour over top.  Cover; cook on high setting for 1 hour. Reduce to low setting; cook for 6  to 8 hours or until potatoes are tender. 7 (1 cup) servings.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1059 by "M. Hicks" <nitro_ii@email.msn.com>  on Jan 29, 1998
