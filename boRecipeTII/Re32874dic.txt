Sweet Potatoes with Apples
Butter, Sweet potatoes, Cinnamon, Apples, Brown sugar, 
Saute sliced apples in butter with cinnamon and brown sugar.  Place in  bottom of greased pan.  Cover with seasoned mashed sweet potatoes. Bake  until bbrowned on top.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V5 #019 by dandelion@edeneast.com on Jan 19,  1998
