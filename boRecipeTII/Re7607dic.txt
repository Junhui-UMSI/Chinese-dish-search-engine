Pork Steak Casserole
Cream of mushroom soup, Flour, pork steaks, milk, 
Servings: 1    Carrots sliced thin Onions sliced thin Potatoes sliced thin    Brown steaks well in frying pan. Remove meat - add flour, soup and milk to  fry pan and simmer a few minutes. layer in casserole the potatoes and  carrots and onions. Top with meat and season with salt and pepper. Top with  soup mixture and bake at 350 degrees about 1 1/ 2 hour or until done.
