Taco Casserole #1
Salt and pepper to taste, Chili powder, Hamburger, Doritos, tomato paste, Onion, kidney beans, taco shells, Taco seasoning mix, Cheddar cheese, Lettuce, Tomatoes, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a skillet, brown hamburger, drain. Add the  next 6 ingredients.  Cook for approximately 15 minutes.  Line a 9 x 13 inch  pan with crushed taco shells.  Pour above hamburger mixture over crushed  shells.  Place 1 pound of shredded cheese on top of mixture. Crush 1 small  package of Doritos, and place on top of cheese. Bake for 15 minutes. Top  with remaining Cheddar cheese, lettuce and tomatoes.
