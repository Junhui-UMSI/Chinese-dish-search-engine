Pear and Apricot Butter
Pears; peeled and cored, Sugar, Dried apricots, 
Place pears and apricots in a large saucepan. Add water and cook until  soft. Strain, and return to pan. Add sugar and cook until thickened,  stirring frequently. Pour into glass jars and seal.
