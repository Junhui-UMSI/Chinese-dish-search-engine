From: blkcat!Leti.Labell@uunet.uu.net (Leti Labell)    Date: Sun, 12 Dec 1993 11:31:08 -0500    From Family Circles _Christmas Helps and Holiday Baking. 1993    Toast nuts; place nuts in glass pie plate; microwave on High 4 minutes,  stirring once.  Let nuts cool.    Line an 8-inch square baking pan with foil; butter foil and set aside.    Combine butter, chocolate chips and milk in an 8-cup microwave safe bowl.  Microwave on high 1 1/2 to 2 minutes or until butter and chips melt. Remove  and stir with wooden spoon to blend.    Stir in sugar and beat well 2 or 3 minutes until smooth.  Stir in vanilla  and toasted nuts.    Spread fudge in prepared pan.  Cover and refrigerate 2 hours or until firm.    To serve, lift out foil and fudge; remove foil.  Place fudge on board and  cut into squares.  Decorate with nuts, if desired.    (I wrapped individual pieces in plastic wrap and froze them.)
