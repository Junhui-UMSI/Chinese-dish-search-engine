Peaches and Cream Cheesecake
milk, Sugar, Cinnamon, Sugar, LORI NORMAN RFHH52E, Peaches, sliced, canned, Flour, Salt, Eggs, Baking Powder, Vanilla Pudding, Cream cheese, Peach syrup, 
Combine first 6 ingredients, and beat 2 minutes at Medium speed. Pour into  a greased and floured 9x13-inch pan. Arrange peaches over the top of  batter. Combine cheese, sugar and peach syrup. Beat 2 minutes. Spoon over  peaches. Mix cinnamon and sugar together, and sprinkle over the top. Bake  at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes. Store in the refrigerator.
