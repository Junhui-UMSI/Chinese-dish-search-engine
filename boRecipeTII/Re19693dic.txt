Antipasto
Tuna, canned, Carrots, Mushrooms, Green peppers, cauliflower, Granulated sugar, Vegetable oil, Garlic, celery, ketchup, onion, Juice of 1 lemon, 
Finely chop peppers, onion, celery, carrots, garlic, mushrooms and  cauliflower; cook in hot oil for 5 minutes, mix well. Cook 5 minutes. Pour  into sterilzed jars, cool and refrigerate. When serving, place antipasto in  a pretty dish, surround with crackers. Note: Recipe is incomplete as it  doesnt say what to do with several        ingredients.
