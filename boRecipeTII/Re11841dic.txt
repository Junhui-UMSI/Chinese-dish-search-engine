Raw Tomato Sauce
Basil leaves, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, red pepper flakes, Salt to taste, Beefsteak tomatoes, garlic, 
Chop the tomatoes into 1/2-inch cubes. Reserving the liquid.    Place tomatoes into a bowl. Add the olive oil, sliced garlic, basil  chiffonade and optional red pepper flakes, if desired.    Mix loosely with spoon. Note: do not add the salt until the moment it is to  be eaten, or the liquid from the tomatoes will pour out and leave your  sauce flat and lifeless.    Yield: 3 cups    * To chiffonade any leafy vegetable or herb, loosely roll 5 or 6 leaves  like a cigar and slice across the roll into ribbons about 1/16-inch thick.
