Quick Beef and Vegetable Stir-Fry
Bag; (16-ounce) frozen mixed, Cornstarch, Vegetable oil, COLLEGE INN Beef Broth, Beef; (1-pound) top round, Soy sauce, A.1. SWEET & TANGY or, 
Preparation Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes    1. Mix beef, steak sauce, soy sauce and cornstarch until well coated. Drain  beef, reserving steak sauce mixture.    2. Cook and stir beef in hot oil in large skillet for 2 to 3 minutes. Add  vegetables, reserved steak sauce mixture and beef broth; heat to a boil.  Reduce heat, simmer 1 minute.
