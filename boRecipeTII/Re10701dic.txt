Quick Spanish Rice
Rice, Garlic powder, Cumin, stewed tomatoes, Oregano, chicken bouillon, Boil in a Bag rice, Butter, Chili powder, 
In medium saucepan combine first 7 ingredients. Simmer mixture for 10  minutes.  Stir in cooked rice. Garnish with green onions & serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
