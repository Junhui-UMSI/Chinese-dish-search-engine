Apples and Cranberries Pudding
Bread crumbs, Nutmeg, fresh, Brown sugar, Apples, Granny Smith, Cranberries, Butter, 
Source: From my mother with love & more. Family recipe.    Place 1/2 of the apples in greased baking dish. Sprinkle with 1/2 sugar and  nutmeg. Dot with 1/2 the butter. Add 1/2 the bread crumbs and cranberries.  Repeat: Ending with the crumbs. Dot with the butter and bake in pre heated  350F hot oven for 1 hour.    Serve hot with Lemon Pudding.
