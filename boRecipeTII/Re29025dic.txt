Baked Pineapple Chicken
Half chicken breasts, boned, Worcestershire Sauce, Lemon, thinly sliced, Cornstarch, DOLE Pineapple Chunks (20oz), Garlic clove, crushed, Rosemary, crushed, Dijon mustard, 
DRAIN pineapple, reserve juice. Combine reserved juice with garlic,  cornstarch, Wrocestershire sauce, mustard and rosemary. Arrange  chicken in shallow baking pan or broiler-proof dish, skin side up.  BROIL until browned. Stir sauce; pour over chicken. BAKE at 400F. 30  minutes. Arrange lemon and pineapple slices around chicken. Spoon  sauce from baking pan over all; continue baking 5 minutes. Garnish  with fresh rosemary or parsley, if desired. Prep Time: 10 minutes.  Bake Time: 40 minutes.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/tn-94q3.zip
