Apple Pie (Dehydrated Apples)
LEMON FRESH, SUGAR; GRANULATED 10 LB, STARCH EDIBLE CORN, BUTTER PRINT SURE, All Purpose Flour, SHORTENING; 3LB, APPLE SLICED #10, Salt, Water, Salt, NUTMEG GROUND, Water, WATER; COLD, CINNAMON GROUND 1 LB CN, 
PAN:  9-INCH PIE PAN                              TEMPERATUE:  425 F. OVEN  :    1.  SEE RECIPE NOS. IG002 AND I00100.    2.  COMBINE CANNED DEHYDRATED APPLES, WATER, GRANULATED SUGAR,  PREGELATINIZED STARCH, SALT, GROUND NUTMEG AND GROUND CINNAMON.  BRING  TO A BOIL; REDUCE HEAT; SIMMER 15 MINUTES OR UNTIL APPLES ARE TENDER.  REMOVE FROM HEAT.  DO NOT DRAIN; USE UNDRAINED APPLES IN STEP 5.    3.  FOLD APPLES, BUTTER OR MARGARINE, AND LEMON JUICE CAREFULLY INTO  THICKENED MIXTURE.  ADD WATER.    4.  POUR 3 1/2 CUPS FILLING INTO EACH UNBAKED PIE SHELL.    5.  BAKE 30 TO 35 MINUTES OR UNTIL LIGHTLY BROWNED.    6.  CUT 8 WEDGES PER PIE.  :    NOTE:  IN STEP 3, 6 OZ LEMONS A.P. (1 1/2 LEMONS) WILL YIELD 4 2/3  TBSP JUICE.
