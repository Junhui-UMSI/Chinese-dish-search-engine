Artichokes Dunks (Continued)
Half and half, Lemon juice, Sauce III, Egg yolks, Dry mustard, Butter, 
Place butter in a 2 cup measure. Microwave at high (100%) until melted, 45  seconds to 1 minute. Add lemon juice and egg yolks. Blend well. Beat in  half and half and mustard. Microwave at medium high (70%) until mixture  begins to thicken, 1 to 2 minutes. DO NOT BOIL Beat until smooth. Serve  warm.
