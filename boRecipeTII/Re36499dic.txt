Strawberries Alberto
Thin slices lemon, Ripe strawberries, Hulled and sliced, Sugar, Chianti, To, 
FAWN STREET, BALTIMORE. WINE: BOLLA AMARONE 1. Mix wine with the sugar and  lemon slices. Stir in the strawberries. Chill. Serve. Note: If the  strawberries are very ripe & sweet you may not need so much sugar.
