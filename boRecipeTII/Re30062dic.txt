Balsamic Glazed Chicken
Chicken breast halves, Orange marmalade, Balsamic vinegar, Black olives, olive oil, Lemon-pepper seasoning, GARNISH, Parsley, Orange wedges, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Mix vinegar, marmalade and oil and zap in  microwave 1 minute. Place chicken breasts in baking dish and brush with  mixture. Sprinkle with lemon-pepper. Bake 20 minutes. Baste again and bake  additional 20 minutes. Serve with garnish if desired.    Recipe by: Ethel Hofman Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest V97 #242 by Judith  Sobel <jcs@mindspring.com> on Sep 01, 1997
