Beef Jerky
Honey, Lemon juice, Salt, Soy sauce, Flank steak, Garlic, Pepper, 
Put steak in freezer for about half an hour, just until firm. Slice steak  across grain, about 1/4-inch thick. Combine remaining ingredients and  marinate steak strips in this for at least 2 hours. Place slices on rack in  pan and dry in oven at 150 degrees, 12 hours.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #1030 by Cynthea <Cynthea@aol.com> on Jan 22,  1998
