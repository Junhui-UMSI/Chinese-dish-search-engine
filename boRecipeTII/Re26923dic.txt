Baked Burgundy Beef
Cubed chuck roast, Burgundy, Fine dry bread crumbs, (10 1/2 oz.) condensed, Bay leaf, Onion soup, Buttered noodles, 
350 degree oven Time to prepare - 3 hours In casserole thoroughly combine  ingredients.Cover and bake 2 1/2 to 3 hours,until tender and sauce has  thickened.Serve over noodles.
