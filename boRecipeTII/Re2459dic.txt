Blushing Peaches
Peaches, Sugar, Lemon peel, grated, Currant jelly, Water, Lemon juice, 
Combine all ingredients except peaches in fry pan.  Cover and simmer 5  minutes until jelly is dissolved.    Peel and cut peaches in halves. Place cut side down in syrup in fry pan.  Cover and simmer 10 minutes, turn cut side up, and cook for additional 5  minutes.    Remove peaches from syrup to a dish.  Cook syrup, stirring constantly until  it begins to jell ( 2 drops run together in a sheet when mixture is dropped  form the edge of a spoon). Pour mixture over peaches. Serve hot.
