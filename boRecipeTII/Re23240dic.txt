Tuna Melt with Bacon and Swiss Cheese
Fat-free swiss cheese single, Low-fat bacon, White bread, naked tuna salad, Drained, Fat-free thousand island dressing, 
Recipe by: The Tuna Fish Gourmet Preparation Time: 0:10 Arrange bread in  pairs of a work surface. Spread thousand island dressing over all slices.  Top each piece of bread with a slice of cheese, folding th cheese in half  to fit if necessary. Place 2 bacon slices on 4 of the slices on the  opposite 4. Spread tuna salad on top of the 4 servings of bacon, the take  the opposite halves of the sandwiches and gently invert them on top of the  tuna halves.  Preheat a skillet over medium heat.  Lightly spread the fat  free spread over both sides of each sandwich. Place sandwiches in the  preheated skillet and cook until lightly toasted on bottom and flip and coo  3 minutes longer or until cheese has melted.
