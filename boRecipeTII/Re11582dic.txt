Raspberry Mint Honey
(4 ounces) fresh mint leaves, Frozen raspberries, Mild-flavored honey, such as, 
This recipe can be doubled or tripled. Use a large pan, since the honey  expands dramatically while simmering.    In a 3- to 4-quart pan, combine all ingredients over medium-high heat. Stir  occasionally until honey simmers, about 5 minutes. Simmer 20 minutes,  remove from heat and pour through a fine strainer; discard raspberry pulp  and mint leaves. Let flavored honey stand until completely cool; pour into  clean decorative jars. Store at room temperature up to 1 month.
