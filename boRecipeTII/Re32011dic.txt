Barbecue Slaw
Shredded red cabbage, Shredded carrots, Slaw dressing; Commercial or, Shredded bell peppers, Shredded green cabbage, Bermuda onion; thin sliced, Barbecue sauce, Hot pepper sauce, 
From:    LD Goss <ldgoss@METRONET.COM>    Date:    Mon, 15 Jul 1996 14:33:57 -0500  In large bowl, combine cabbages, onion, peppers and carrots.  In small  bowl, combine dressing, barbecue sauce and hot pepper sauce. Pour over  vegetables and toss lightly. Serve immediately.    NOTE:  Barbecue Slaw can also be used as a topping for grilled burgers and  chicken, as well as a flavorful side dish.    The Milwaukee Journal; and Prodigy Services.
