Turkey with Creole Mayonnaise
Vinegar or lemon juice, Thin slices roast turkey, Crusty rolls, split, Large dill pickles,thinly, Minced green onions, Black and cayenne peppers, Light mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, 
In small bowl, stir together mayonnaise, onion, mustard, vinegar, and black  and cayenne peppers to taste; spread on cut side of rolls. Top with turkey;  arrange pickles on top.  Sandwich with remaining halves. Makes 4 servings.
