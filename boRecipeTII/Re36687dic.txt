Strawberry Delight #1
Angel food cake, (8-oz) whipped topping, Whole Milk, Instant vanilla pudding, Vanilla ice cream, Strawberry gelatin, Frozen strawberries, 
Slice angel food cake & cover the bottom of a 9x12-inch pan. Mix instant  pudding with milk. Add vanilla ice cream & beat until smooth. Pour this  over cake. Dissolve strawberry gelatin in 1 cup hot water. Let cool  slightly & add frozen strawberries. Mix well & pour over vanilla mixture.  Top with layer of whipped topping. Refrigerate overnight.
