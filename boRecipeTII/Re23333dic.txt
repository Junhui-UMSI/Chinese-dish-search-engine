Tuna Tataki
Ginger, Tuna loin, Garlic, Spice powder, Black pepper, sauce, Sesame oil, Soy sauce, Peanut oil, 
Fresh loin of tuna without bloodlines, roll in cracked black pepper then  sear it in very hot cast iron pan without oil, cool down for better  slicing. Sauce: 1 cup peanut oil, 1 oz. sesame oil, 2 oz. soy sauce, 1/2 oz  grated garlic, 1/2 oz grated ginger, 5 spice powder to taste. Slice tuna  very thin, serve with sauce down and garnish with pickled ginger. A great  appetizer.
