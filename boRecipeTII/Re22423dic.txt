Turtle Pecan Pie
Semisweet chocolate morsels, Pecan halves, Chocolate wafer crumbs, Vanilla ice cream, Evaporated milk, Mini marshmallows, Butter, Salt, 
Combine chocolate crumbs and butter; press into a 9 inch deep dish pie  plate.  Freeze for 15 minutes.  Combine chocolate morsels and the next 3  ingredients in a saucepan.  Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until  thickened and smooth.  Remove from heat and set aside.  Spread 2 cups of  the ice cream into pieplate; cover with foil and freeze about 30 minutes.  Spread the remaining chocolate mixture over ice cream and top with the  pecans.  Cover again and freeze.  Let unthaw slightly at least 1/2 hour  before serving.
