Veal Stronganoff
Chicken bouillon cube, Salad oil, Veal Thinly sliced, Mushrooms, Paprika, Flour, Salt, Water, Sour cream, Pepper, Onion, 
From: HallieByrd <HallieByrd@aol.com>    Combine flour, salt and pepper, dip meat into it to coat lightly. Heat oil  in large skillet, quickly brown meat. Add onion and muchrooms. cook 5 min  Add water, bouillon and paprika, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat blend in  sour cream. Cover and simmer gently 15 min. Serve with noodles.
