Potato and Salmon Bisque
(7 1/2 ounces) salmon,, Idaho instant mashed potato, Can( l3 3/4 ounces) chicken, Chopped red pepper, Grated lemon peel or 1, Green onions, chopped, 
In microwave-safe 2-quart casserole combine salmon, broth, milk, green  onions, red pepper, lemon peel and pepper. Cover loosely with plastic wrap;  cook on High 5 to 6 minutes or until very hot. Stir in potatoes. Re-cover;  let stand 5 minutes. Cook on High 2 minutes if necessary to reheat    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
