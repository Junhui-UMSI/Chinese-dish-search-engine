Stuffed Peppers\clay Cooker
Onions, Eggs, Large green or red peppers, Salt, pepper, and nutmeg, Parsley, minced, Bread crumbs, Chopped bacon, Rice, Ground beef, 
Soak lid of clay cooker. Mix all ingredients and stuff peppers. Set stuffed  peppers into the cooker. Cover and put into cold oven. Turn to 425F and  bake for 60 minutes.
