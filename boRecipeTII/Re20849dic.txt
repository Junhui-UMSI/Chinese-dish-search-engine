Apple Kuchen Coffee Cake
Ground cinnamon, Apple-cinnamon or vanilla, Margarine or butter;, Sugar, Bisquick Original baking mix, Sugar, Egg, Apple; peeled and thinly, Egg, 
Prep Time: 17 min. Bake Time: 50 mins.    Heat oven to 350. Grease spring-form pan, 9x2 inches, or round pan, 9x1 1/2  inches.    Beat 1/2 cup sugar, the margarine and egg in medium bowl on low speed 30  seconds or until smooth. Beat in baking mix. Spread over bottom and 1 inch  up side of pan. Mix yogurt and egg until smooth. Stir in apple. Spoon over  batter in pan. Mix 1/4 cup sugar and the cinnamon: sprinkle over apple  mixture.    Bake 45 to 50 minutes or until center is set and crust is deep golden  brown. Cool 10 minutes; remove side of pan. Serve warm.          8    servings.  Posted to brand-name-recipes by Whimsey Stephens <whimsey@mindspring.com>  on Feb 03, 1998
