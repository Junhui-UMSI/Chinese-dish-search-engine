Cajun Chili
Rotel; diced chilis, Minestrone soup, Ranch style beans, Stewed tomatoes, Onion diced, Ground beef, 
In skillet brown ground beef and onions. Put beef, onion mixture in crock  pot and add all the remaining ingredients to crock pot. Set crock po on low  and simmer for 8 hrs.    When serving you can top with shredded cheese or fritoes!
