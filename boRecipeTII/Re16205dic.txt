Wassail Bowl
Apple brandy, Ground cloves, Cloves, Apple cider, Sugar, Ground nutmeg, Oranges; each stuck with, Ground cinnamon, 
Bake oranges for 1 hour at 325 degrees. Bring cider to boiling point. Place  baked oranges in wassail bowl & pour brandy over them. Sprinkle with sugar  & ignite the oranges. Extinguish flame by pouring hot cider into bowl.  Reserve 1 cup hot cider to mix with spices. When spices are dissolved, add  to boiling cider mixture.
