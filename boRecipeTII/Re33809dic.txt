Sweet and Sour Pasta Salad
drained, Red pepper; diced, Can EDEN Black Beans, EDEN Brown Rice Vinegar, Can EDEN Kidney Beans, EDEN Barley Malt Syrup, EDEN Kamut Spirals, Dried basil, drained, DRESSING, EDEN Shoyu, EDEN Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Green onions; diced, 
Cook Spirals as package directs. Rinse and drain. Add drained beans,  diced red pepper, green onions and basil. Prepare dressing. Mix into  salad and serve.
