Pork Booklets
1/2" thick Pork Tenderloin, Vegetable oil, Ham slices, Seasoned bread crumbs, Thick cheddar cheese slices, Egg, 
Pound pork with mallet until flattened to 1/4" thick. Top 6 cutlets with 1  cheese slice and 1 ham slice. Top with remaining 6 cutlets. Pound edges to  seal. Season egg with salt and pepper. Dip pork into egg. Coat completely  with breadcrumbs. Heat oil in medium skillet over medium-high heat. Add  pork and cook til browned, about 5 minutes per side.
