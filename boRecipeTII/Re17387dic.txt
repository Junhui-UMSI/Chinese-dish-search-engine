Walnut Creams
Sweetened condensed milk, Salt, Food coloring, Walnut pieces, Vanilla, 
DIRECTIONS: Mix well first 4 ingredients. Knead until smooth. Divide; color  half pink and half green. Shape in 3/4" balls, and press a piece of walnut  on each. Let stand until firm.    Source: Moms old magazine clippings- 1940s to 1970s    From: Sallie Austin
