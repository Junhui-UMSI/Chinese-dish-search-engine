Veggie Burgers
carrots, Ground cumin, salt and pepper, Olive oil, beets, Mixed dried dill, Quick-cooking rolled oats,, turnips, Water, hazelnuts, Rice, zucchini, Garlic, onions, olive oil, Mashed potatoes, 
Date: Sun, 19 May 96 06:26:36 PDT    From: eboyd@shentel.net    Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet. Add onions, carrots, turnips,  zucchini, beets, garlic, cumin and herbs. Saute until vegetables begin to  wilt, about 3 to 5 minutes. Allow to cool slightly.    Soak oats in water for 5 minutes, then drain and press out excess water.  Add to vegetable mixture along with potatoes, hazelnuts and rice. Season  with salt and pepper.    Form mixture into 8 patties. Grill patties on well oiled vegetable grill,  or saute in olive oil for 3 to 4 minutes on each side, until golden brown.    Per patty: 106 calories, 4 gm. fat, 0 mg. cholesterol, 158 mg. sodium.
