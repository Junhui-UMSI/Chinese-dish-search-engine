Pheasant Curry

From: joell@jolt.mpx.com.au (Joell Abbott)    Date: 9 Sep 1995 20:53:10 -0600  Pound together the coriander, cumin, sesame seeds, turmeric, chili peppers,  ginger and salt. Rub mixture into pheasant pieces. Melt the butter in a  casserole dish and brown onions in it. Add the pheasant and any left over  spices and brown. Stir in the water; cover and cook over low heat forty  five minutes or until tender. Cover for ten minutes.
