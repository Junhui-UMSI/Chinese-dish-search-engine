Pot Roast Dijon
Wine vinegar, (4-lb) pot roast or rump, olive oil, Water, Water, Dijon mustard; mixed with, Vegetable oil, Onion; thinly sliced, Garlic, Seasoning salt, Pepper, 
In a large bowl, marinate meat in onion, garlic, salt, pepper, oil and  vinegar. Marinate at least 2 hours, turning meat at least once. Drain and  save marinade. In a Dutch oven, heat vegetable oil, add meat and brown on  all sides. Add marinade and 1 cup of water, cover tightly and simmer for 3  or more hours until meat is tender. Add mustard to meat and cook until  liquid is reduced and sauce is thickened somewhat. To serve, carve meat in  thin slices and pour sauce over the slices. Yield: 8 to 10 servings.    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
