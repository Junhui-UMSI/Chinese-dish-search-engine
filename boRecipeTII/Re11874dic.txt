Real American Brownies
Chocolate, melted & cooled, Butter, 1 oz squares unsweetened, Vanilla, Eggs, Granulated sugar, Chopped walnuts, optional, All-purpose flour, sifted, 
In a medium saucepan, melt the butter and chocolate.  Remove from the  heat; stir in sugar.  Blend-in eggs, one at a time.  Add vanilla.  Stir in flour and nuts; mix well.  Spread in greased 8inch x 8inch x  2inch deep baking pan.  Bake at 350F for 30 minutes (be careful not  to over-bake) Cool 20 minutes before cutting into squares for serving.
