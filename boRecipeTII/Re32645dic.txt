Sweet Pepper Ravioli
Chopped parsley, More, Salt, Eggs -- at room temperature, Ricotta Cheese -- (we used, Peeled -- julienned, Red Peppers, Roasted &, Low fat), Eggs, Parmesan Cheese -- grated, Salt And Pepper -- to taste, All-Purpose Flour -- or, 
(Their recipe calls for you to place the flour, 3 eggs, peppers and salt in  a food processor and pulse until mixture forms a uniform-colored ball) Old-  fashioned method (or poor college student method) Puree the pepper ina  blender. Make a mound of the flour with a well in the center, add the salt.  Put the pepper and the eggs into the well and blend with a fork until that  becomes too dtoo difficult, then knead on a floured surface until smooth  and elastic and not too soft. If its too dry, add liquid, too sticky, add  flour.    In a bowl, beat ricotta cheese, add eggs (2), parsley, cheese, salt and  pepper and beat until blended.    Roll out the pasta dough according to instructions for ravioli rack (we did  it three times through a hand-cranked pasta machine) and fill with cheese  (@ 1 tsp, maybe less). To cook: boil in lightly salted water for about 3  minutes or until tender.  We served it with a standard pasta sauce (crushed  tomatos, paste, onions, peppers, lots o garlic, mustard powder, herbs...    Recipe By     : Jessica Chaiken <11JCHAIKEN@GALLUA.GALLAUDET.EDU>
