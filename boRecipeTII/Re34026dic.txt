Basil-Cumin-Fennel Potatoes

Heat some oil in a pan.  When hot, throw in the cumin and fennel seeds.  After a few seconds, put in the potatoes, along with the salt, black  pepper, and cayenne.  Fry the potatoes, stirring occasionally, until they  get crusty.  A minute before serving, add the basil. These can be eaten  plain, or wrapped in a flour tortilla with yogurt, or in pita bread with  yogurt.  If youre combining them with yogurt, the spices should be a bit  heavier, as the yogurt is fairly bland.    From: narad@nudibranch.asd.sgi.com (Chuck Narad).  rfvc Digest V94, Aug.  30, 1994.
