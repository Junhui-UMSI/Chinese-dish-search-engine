Bacon and Cheddar Pancakes
Unsalted butter; melted, Sugar, Buttermilk, Plain yogurt, Salt, Mild cheddar cheese; grated, Cornmeal, Cooked bacon; crumbled, Flour, Eggs, Baking soda, 
1. In large bowl, whisk together flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt and baking  soda.    2. In another bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, yogurt and butter. Whisk  into flour mixture just until combined.    3. Heat griddle over med heat (350F). Preheat oven to 200F if you want to  hold pancakes.    4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter, forming 4" rounds and sprinkle 1 tbsp of cheese  and bacon onto each pancake. Cook until golden, 1-2 mins on each side.  Serve with cheese and syrup.
