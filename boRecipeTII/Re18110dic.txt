Veggieburgers
Whole-wheat bread crumbs, Butter or margarine, Chopped red pepper, Green Pepper, Pepper, Shallots, chopped, Finely chopped spinach, Finely chopped pecans, Grated carrot, Chick-peas, drained and, Chopped fresh parsley, Celery, Mayonnaise, 
Combine chick-peas, pecans, bread crumbs, spinach, carrot, green and  red pepper, shallots, celery, parsley and mayonnaise. Shape into 6  patties.    Saute patties in butter, cooking until browned on both sides.    From Tuscon area newspapers, 1994, 3rd quarter, courtest Mike Orchekowski.
