Baked Potatoe
Onion, Potatoes, 
preheat oven to 400 degrees, wash and dry tatoes,. Cut the potatoe (but  only 3/4 through) into 4 slices. Slice onion into 1/4 inch slices then in  half, put onion inside potatoe slices. Rub potatoe with butter and wrap in  tin foil cook for one hour    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #214 by "Pollywog"  <hlewis@cci-29palms.com> on Sep 2, 1997
