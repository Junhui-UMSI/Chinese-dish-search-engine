Taco Compuesto
LEAN ground beef;, Part-skim mozzarella cheese;, Onion, Low-cal prosse cheese or;, Tomato; chopped, Seasoned salt; yak!!, Taco sauce, Green chili peppers; diced, Italian Salad dressing;, Taco shells;, Lettuce;, Garlic;, Chili powder;, 
In a large skillet cook ground beef, chopped onions, green chili  pepper; and minced garlic over medium heat till beef is brown.  Drain  off fat. Stir in chili powder.    In a mixing bowl combine chopped tomato, Italian salad dressing, and  seasoned salt. ( Salt is Salt no matter what) Spoon beef mixture into  taco shells. Top with tomato mixture, shredded lettuce, shredded  cheese, and taco sauce.  Makes 4 servings.  Time 20 min.
