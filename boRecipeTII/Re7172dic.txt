Pork and Bbq Sauce on Bun
Dry white wine or sherry, Hamburger buns -- buttered, Butter, Bbq sauce, Brown sugar, Onion -- sliced crosswise, Ketchup, Cook pork -- thinly sliced, ***BBQ Sauce***, Clove garllic -- minced, Soy sauce, Mustard, 
Stir sliced pork into bbq sauce. Cook over low heat until pork is hot.  Spoon pork with bbq sauce into hamburger buns. Garnish with dill pickles  and coleslaw.    BBQ sauce. Saute onion and garlic in butter in heavy saucepan for 3-4 min  or until onion is soft, stirring constantly. Add remaining ingredients and  heat to boiling. Simmer 5 min.
