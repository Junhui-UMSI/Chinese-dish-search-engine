Raisin-Oatmeal Brownies
Eggs; Large, Oats; Quick-cooking Rolled, Vanilla, Flour; Unbleached, Salt, Baking soda, Butter or margarine, Chocolate Chips; Semisweet, Brown Sugar, Raisins, 
Sprinkle 2 T of the oats over bottom of greased 9 X 9 X 2-inch baking pan;  set aside.  In saucepan melt chocolate and butter over low heat; stir  constantly.  Remove from heat; blend in sugar.  Beat in eggs and vanilla.  Stir together flour, remaining oats, raisins, soda and salt; stir into  chocolate mixture.  Spoon into prepared pan.  Bake at 325 degrees F. about  30 minutes.  Cool; cut into bars.    Makes 24 brownies.    From Better Homes & Gardens Homemade Cookies Cookbook, via The Home BBS.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/travel.zip
