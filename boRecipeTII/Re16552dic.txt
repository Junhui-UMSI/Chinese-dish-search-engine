After-The-Holidays Salad
Sliced greed onions, Drained, Celery, Seedless green peppers, Mayonnaise, Pineapple chunks -- well, Chopped chutney, Lettuce leaves -- optional, Curry powder, Diced cooked turkey, Dry roasted peanuts, Salt, Lime juice, 
In a mixing bowl, toss first six ingredients.  In a small bowl, combine all  remaining ingredients except lettuce. Pour over turkey and mix gently.  Chill. Serve on a bed of lettuce, if desired. Yields: 4 servings.    The fruit, nuts and seasonings give the salad a unique flavor.    Recipe By     :
