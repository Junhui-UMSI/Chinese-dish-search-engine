Traditional Potato Teigarch (Potato Pudding)
Egg, Onion, Salt, Baking Powder, Poultry or vegetable fat, Potatoes, Pepper, Flour, 
Wash, pare and finely grate the potatoes and the onion. Sift the dry  ingredients together, and add together with the egg and the fat.    Mix and pour into a greased 2 quart baking dish, dot with additional fat  and bake in a moderately hot oven (375) for 1 1/2 hours, until the top is  crusty brown.
