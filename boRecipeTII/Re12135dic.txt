Zucchini and Date Tea Loaf (Motts)
Unsweetened Apple Sauce, Lemon Peel, Sugar, All-purpose flour, Chopped dates, Ground nutmeg, Egg whites; lightly beaten, Ground cinnamon, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Shredded zucchini, 
Preheat oven to 350F. Spray 8x4 inch loaf pan with nonstick cooking spray.    In large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder  and nutmeg.    In small bowl, combine zucchini, apple sauce, egg whites and lemon peel.    Stir apple sauce mixture into flour mixture just until moistened. Fold in  dates. Spread batter into prepared pan.    Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out  clean. Cool in pan 10 minutes. Invert onto wire rack; turn right side up.  Cool completely. Cut into 12 slices.    Notes: Pantry: Motts Natural Apple Sauce.
