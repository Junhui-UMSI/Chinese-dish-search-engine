Pork Chop Lentil Bake
Vegetable oil, Pork chops (about 1 1/2 lb), Parsley; snipped, Garlic, Water, Instant chicken bouillon, Bay leaf, Dried Lentils, Carrot; chopped, Onion, 
In a 2 quart saucepan, cook onion and garlic in oil until onion is tender  but not brown. Add water, lentils, carrots, parsley, bouillon granules and  bay leaf; bring to boil. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer 30 minutes. Remove  pan from heat. Remove and discard bay leaf. Spoon lentil mixture into a 12  x 7 1/2 x 2" baking dish; arrange pork chops on top. Cover and bake in a  350 degree oven 25 minutes. Uncover; bake 15 minutes more.
