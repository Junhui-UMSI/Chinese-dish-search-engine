Peppy Snack Mix
Pretzel Sticks, Parmesan, Peanuts, Butter, Chili powder, Life Cereal, 
Preheat oven to 325F.  Combine cereal, pretzel sticks and peanuts. Add  butter and mix well.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and chili powder and  mix well.  Spread onto ungreased 10" X 15" jelly roll pan. Bake for 20  minutes stirring occasionally during baking.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
