Banana Bread Pudding/bananas Foster Sauce
pecans, Unsalted butter, Dark rum, Banana liqueur, Sugar, Vanilla, Vanilla, SAUCE, milk, Add:, Raisins, Brown sugar, Eggs, bananas, CUSTARD, Cinnamon, Ground nutmeg, Cinnamon, milk, Unsalted butter, bananas, Nutmeg, 
Mix all ingredients for custard. Pour over stale bread cubes in baking  dish. On top put raisins, pecans and dot with butter. Let sit 30 minutes.  Bake, covered with foil, at 300~ for 1 hour. Uncover and cook for 15  minutes more. SAUCE-Melt sugar in butter and add bananas and saute until  soft. Add nutmeg and cinnamon. Remove from heat and add rum and banana  liqueur. Return to flame and add vanilla. Arrange a scoop of pudding on a  dish, top with sauce and bananas.
