Picadillo Stuffing
Onion, olive oil, Raisins, Green pepper; chopped, Fresh tomato, Ground beef, Chicken Stock, Slivered almonds, Dry spanish sherry, Garlic, Capers, Raw white rice, salt and pepper, Spanish olives w/pimiento, 
In a large skillet saute onion, pepper and garlic in olive oil. Add tomato  and cook slowly until well blended. Brown meat in same skillet and cook  slowly with olives, raisins, and capers. Meanwhile, cook rice in stock  until done. Mix rice with other ingredients to form a moist,but not wet,  stuffing. Season with salt and pepper. Stuffs a 12-15 pound turkey.
