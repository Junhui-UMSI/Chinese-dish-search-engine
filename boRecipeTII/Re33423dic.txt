Basics Kasha
Kasha, Boiling water, Egg, Pepper, Salt, Butter, 
Basics for new cooks:    [Kasha: aka Buckwheat Groats]    In saucepan, combine kaska, egg, salt and pepper; cook over medium-low  heat, stirring, for 3 minutes or until dry and kernels separate. Stir in  water and butter; cover and cook, without stirring, for 10-12 minutes or  until water is absorbed. Fluff kasha with fork. Makes 1-1/2 cups, or 2  servings.
