Pesto Genovese
Garlic, To 3 - cups fresh basil, Pine nuts, olive oil, Salt, Parmesan cheese,grated, 
Cherry tomatoes for garnish    Place all ingredients except oil in a food processor or blender Process,  quickly, to a puree.  With machine running, gradually add oil until  absorbed.  Toss with 1 pound fresh, hot pasta (vermicelli, linguini, and so  forth).  Serve hot.  Garnish with cherry tomatoes.
