Polish Sausage and Scalloped Potatoes
Polish sausage, scalloped potatoes, 
Make potatoes according to directions, bake for 10 minutes. Remove from  oven and add chopped up sausage poking the pieces down into the potatoes.  Return to oven and bake until done and bubbly.
