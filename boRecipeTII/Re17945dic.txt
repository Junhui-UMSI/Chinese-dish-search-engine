Date: Wed, 6 Mar 96 13:35:21 EST    From: squeakn@pcnet.com    Pre heat oven to 300 degrees.  Line a cookie sheet with aluminum foil.  Butter & flour it. Combine almonds, sugar, flour and salt in food  processor. Process till fine. Transfer to bowl. Stir in egg whites and  almond extract. Drop batter by teaspoonful onto sheet 1-1/2 inches apart.  Bake 20 minutes or until dry. Let cool slightly on sheet then transfer to  rack.  Cool completely. Store in airtight container.
