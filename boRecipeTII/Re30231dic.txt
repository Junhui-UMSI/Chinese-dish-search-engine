Banana Bread #13
Brown sugar, Nutmeg, Coarsely chopped nuts, Vanilla, Eggs -or-, Flour (or 1/2 whole wheat, Quaker oats (cooks in 1 min), Bananas (very ripe; mashed), Ground ginger, Raisins (optional), Wheat germ (honey roasted is, Vegetable oil, Egg whites (if you prefer), Baking Powder, Cinnamon, 
From: ahrensm@alpha.hanover.edu (Marsha Ahrens)    Date: 20 Aug 1994 13:12:30 -0400    Preheat oven to 325 deg. F. Grease loaf pan. In large bowl, combine oil and  sugar until smooth. Add bananas, vanilla and eggs. In a medium bowl combine  flour, oats, wheat germ, baking powder and spices. Add to banana mixture;  stir until combined. Add raisins and/or nuts. Pour into prepared pan. Bake  1 hour and 10 mins or until wooden pick comes out clean. Turn immediately  onto a cooling rack. Recipe can be doubled or tripled without alteration.  Freezes well. To make mini loaves, reduce cooking time and check often for  doneness.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
