Pitcaithly Bannock
Mixed Candied Peel, Butter, Choped Almonds, Caster sugar, Flour, 
:       Set Oven to 325F/Gas 3. Grease a baking sheet. Sift the flour into  a bowl. Add the sugar and butter and rub in to form a dough. Add the  almonds and mix in the peel, making sure they are evenly distributed. Form  into a thick round on a lightly floured surface and prick all over with a  fork. Place on the sheet and bake for about 45-60 minutes.
