Raw Chow Chow
Mustard seed, Salt, Green tomatoes, Green peppers, Celery seed, Vinegar, Cabbage, Onions, Sugar, 
Combine, cabbage, green tomatoes, onions and green peppers with salt. Let  stand 3 hours and then drain.    Combine vinegar, sugar, mustard seeds and celery seeds. Let stand 3 hours.    Combine cabbage mixture and vinegar mixture then seal in jars.
