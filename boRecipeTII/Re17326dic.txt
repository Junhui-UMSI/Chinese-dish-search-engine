Walford Salad
Seedless green grapes;, Apple; diced, Walnuts;, Celery; sliced, Mayonnaise;, Dates;, Dry white wine;, Lettuce Leaves;, 
Pace celery, grapes, apple, dates, and walnuts into a bowl. Blend  mayonnaise with wine; pour into bowl. Stir to blend with celery, fruit and  walnuts.  Use slotted serving spoon to serve, shaking spoon slighty to  remove excess dressing.  Serve on to remove excess dressing.
