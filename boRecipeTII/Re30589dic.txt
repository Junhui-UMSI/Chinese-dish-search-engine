Tahini Dressing
Salt, Lemon juice, Green onion (with top), chop, Water, Tahini (sesame seed paste), Vegetable oil, Cl Garlic, crushed, 4 To 6 dr Red pepper sauce, 
Tahini is a popular food from the Middle East made from sesame seeds. Place  all ingredients in blender or food processor. Cover and blend on high  speed, or process, until mixture is smooth. ABOUT 1 CUP DRESSING; 75  CALORIES PER TABLESPOON
