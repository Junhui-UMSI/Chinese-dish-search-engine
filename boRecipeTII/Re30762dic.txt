Tandoori Style Chicken Breasts with Yogurt Cilantro Sauce
Yogurt cilantro sauce, Paprika, Ground coriander, Onion chopped coarsely, Tumeric, Lemon juice, Cascabel chile powder, TANDOORI MARINADE, Salt, Chicken breasts, Ancho chile powder, Ground cloves, Tandoori Marinade, Garlic cloves; chopped fine, Lime juice, Plain yogurt, Ground ginger, 
In a food processor, puree the onion and garlic. Process in the lemon and  lime juice, add spices and the yogurt and process to blend. Marinate the  chicken breasts in this mixture turning occasionally overnight in the  refrigerator.    Prepare a charcoal or wood fire and let it burn down to embers.    Remove the excess marinade and grill for 5 minutes on each side or until  done. Serve with YogurtCilantro sauce to taste.
