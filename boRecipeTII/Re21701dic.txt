Apple Tart
-(up to), Unbaked 10" deep-dish pie, Ground coriander, Salt, Flour, Sugar, Nutmeg, Margarine; streusel topping,, Sliced apples, Cinnamon, 
Date: Fri, 1 Mar 96 6:47:18 EST    submitted by: howle@ebicom.net  Combine sugar, flour, spices and salt.  Stir in apples.  Let stand 30  minutes.  Spoon into pastry shell; dot with margarine.  Sprinkle topping  over apples. Bake at 400 d. for 50-55 minutes. If it gets too brown, cover  loosely with foil.  Serve warm with ice cream.    Streusel topping:  1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar, 1 cup flour, 1/2 cup  margarine.  Mix or process until crumbly.
