Wild Rice Mingle
Water, Wild rice, (10-oz) beef consomme;, Regular grain white rice, Boiling water, Onion, Butter, Salt, Butter, Heavy cream, Salt, 
Wash wild rice and add to 2 cups salted boiling water, cover, reduce heat  and cook until tender, stirring occasionally.  (This may take as long as  1-1/2 hours; start checking after 1 hour.)     Saute onion in butter; stir in consomme and soup can of water. Bring to a  boil and add white rice and 1/2 teaspoon salt.  Cook for 20 to 25 minutes  or until liquid is completely gone.     Drain wild rice, add 1 tablespoon butter. Mix wild rice and regular grain  rice and place in a casserole and heat in 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.  Serves 8.
