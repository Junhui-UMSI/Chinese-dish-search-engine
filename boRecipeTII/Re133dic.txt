Koala Kookies
Butter, Powdered sugar, All-purpose flour, Walnuts, chopped, Powdered sugar, Vanilla, 
1. Mix butter and powdered sugar in a large bowl until smooth and creamy.  2. Add vanilla. Mix well.  3. Add flour and walnuts. Mix well.  4. Roll into small balls and flatten on a greased baking sheet.  5. Bake in a preheated 350-degree oven for 12 to 15 minutes.  6. When cookies are cool, sprinkle powdered sugar on top. Makes about 5 dozen.
