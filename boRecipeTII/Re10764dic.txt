Quick Vodka Sauce
Parm cheese, Vodka, spaghetti sauce, Oregano, Heavy cream, 
*Im fond of Paul Newmans, has a nice fresh taste...but there are some  really good ones out these days). --Mix all ing. and heat over a med flame,  stirring constantly so the mixture does not boil or curdle. --The sauce is  an orange/pink when you are done and the vodka is only a very light  flavoring that most can not name as vodka.
