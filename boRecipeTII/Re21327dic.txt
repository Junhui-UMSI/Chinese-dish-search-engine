Uncle Shels Rainy Day Sauce
Celery seed, Liquid Barbecue SmokeAE, Sweet hungarian paprika, Tomato sauce, Chipotle pepper -- minced, Lemon juice, Cloves -- ground, Molasses, Clove, Red pepper flakes, Coriander, Garlic -- minced, Brown sugar, Balsamic vinegar, Ginger, Cayenne, Red,white,green and black, Peppercorns -- crushed, 
Pour the tomato sauce into a heavy 3 qrt saucepan. Bring the heat up to  medium and start the sauce warming. Add the minced garlic, sugar, molasses,  balsamic vinegar, and spices and let the sauce warm until it just bubbles.  You may up the heat a little but dont let the sauce boil. When sauce  starts to bubble add the liquid smoke and the squeeeze of fresh lemon juice  and let sauce sit on low or warm until ready to use.
