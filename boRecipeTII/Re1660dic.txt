Carrot Donuts
Eggs, Brown sugar, Vegetable oil, MIX TOGETHER TO SOUR MILK, milk, Lemon juice, Sherry, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, carrots, White wheat flour, Salt, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Vanilla, orange rind, 
Mix all ingredients together well, roll out on floured board, cut and fry.  This dough is very sticky, but if you add too much extra flour they get too  heavy.    (Sour milk: use regular milk with lemon juice added - 1 tablespoon juice  per 1 cup milk)    Recipe by Mangia!
