Apricot Delight Babyfood Cake
Strained Apricots with, Yellow cake mix, Sugar, Crushed pineapple, drained, Vanilla instant pudding, Whipping cream, milk, 
Grease and flour a Bundt pan. Bake cake according to package directions.  Cool. Slice tube cake into three layers. Combine pudding, apricots, milk  and pineapple. Spread between the layers and on top of cake. Beat whipping  cream until stiff, gradually adding sugar. Ice sides and center hole of  cake with whipped cream. 12-16 servings
