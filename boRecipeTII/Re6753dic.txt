Polish Sausage with Cabbage
Salt, Head green cabbage; shredded, Beer water; (12 oz.), Head purple cabbage;, Fully cooked Polish sausage, Sugar, 
In large cooker or Dutch oven, combine the beer and enough water to cover  sausage; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and add sausage; simmer 8 to 10  minutes (do not boil sausage). Remove sausage. Add cabbage, sugar and salt  to cooking liquid. Mix well. Top with sausage. Cover and simmer for 10  minutes. Drain liquid before serving.
