Pound Cake Pyramids
Garnish (1/4 cup of any 3), Sara Lee? pound cake; (10.75, Hot fudge sauce; warm, 
Cut pound cake into 8 slices. Lay slices flat and cut each in half  diagonally to make 16 triangle pieces. Dip the long pointed end of each  piece halfway into the warm hot fudge sauce.
