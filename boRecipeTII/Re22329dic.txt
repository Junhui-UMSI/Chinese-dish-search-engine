Turkey-Potato Salad
Salt, Green pepper, chopped, Prepared mustard, Onion, Celery, chopped, Turkey, cooked, diced, Cooked diced potato, Salad dressing, 
2 servings of about 1 cup each 230 calories per serving    1. Mix turkey, celery, potato, onion, and green pepper.    2. Mix salad dressing, mustard, and salt. Stir lightly into turkey    3.
