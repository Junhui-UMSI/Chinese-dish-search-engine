Peanut Butter Balls #1
Butter, (13-oz) chocolate chips, Rice krispies cereal, Crunchy peanut butter, Vanilla, Block paraffin, 
Melt butter; add cereal, peanut butter, sugar and vanilla. Mix well; shape  into balls. Melt paraffin with chocolate chips. Dip balls into chocolate  and cool on waxed paper.
