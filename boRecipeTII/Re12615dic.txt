Yogurt Dressing for Fruit
Fresh Lemon Juice, Pineapple juice (optional), Low calorie cottage cheese, Honey, Plain low-fat yogurt, 
Place all ingredients in blender container and blend until smooth. Serve  over fruit platter or fruit salad. Calories: 11 per Tablespoon. Yield:    2-1/2    cups.    LOW CALORIE    JAN JOHNSON (MRS.
