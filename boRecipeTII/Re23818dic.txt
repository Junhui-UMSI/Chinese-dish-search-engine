Asian Chowder
Red snapper,; cut into, Salmon roe, Coconut milk, Fried wonton wrappers, Bok choy,; julienne, milk, olive oil, Julienne carrots, Water chestnuts,; thinly, Cloud mushrooms,; hydrated, Chiffonade of cilantro, Green Onions, Pinch ground chili pepper, Straw mushrooms, Sheet nori seaweed,; cut, Fish stock, Pinch black sesame seeds, 
In a large sauce pan, heat the olive oil. When the oil is hot, saut_ the  bok choy, carrots, water chestnuts, straw mushrooms and cloud mushrooms.  Saut_ for 3-4 minutes or until the vegetables wilt. Season with salt and  pepper. Stir in the snapper. Whisk in the fish stock, milk and coconut  milk. Bring the liquid up to a boil and reduce to a simmer. Simmer for  15-20 minutes. Season the chowder with the black sesame seeds and chili  pepper. Stir in the cilantro and seaweed. Bring the liquid back up to a  boil. Ladle the soup in a shallow bowl. Garnish the soup with the salmon  roe and green onions. Serve with the fried wonton wrappers.
