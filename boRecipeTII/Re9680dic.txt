Pumpkin Orange Cheesecake
Cream cheese, Pumpkin; (16 ounce) about 2, Eggs, Salt, Pumpkin pie spice, Margarine or butter; melted, Sweetened condensed milk;, Gingersnap cookie crumbs; 32, Orange liqueur OR orange, 
Preheat oven to 300 F. Combine crumbs nad margarine. Press firmly on bottom  and halfway up sides of a 9 springform pan. In large mixer bowl, beat  cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat in sweetened cindensed milk until  smooth. Beat in remaining ingredients. Pour into pan. Bake 1 hour and 15  minutes or until set. Cool. Chill. Garnish. Refrigerate leftovers.
