Peanut Butter Apple Muffins
Skim milk, Whole wheat flour, Margarine, Rolled oats, Eggs, Wheat Germ, Applesauce; Unsweetened, Baking soda, Peanut Butter; Chunky Style, Sugar, Vanilla, 
Stir the flour and baking soda together.  In a large bowl, beat the peanut  butter and margarine together until fluffy.  Add the sugar and vanilla.  Beat well.  Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.  Stir in the applesauce and milk.  Add the flour mixture to the peanut  butter mixture.  Stir in the oats and wheat germ.  Spoon into muffin tins  that have been sprayed with a non-stick spray.  Bake at 350 degrees F. for  15 to 20 minutes, or until done.  Cool in the muffin tin.
