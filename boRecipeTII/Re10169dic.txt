Quiche Lorraine Ala Katherine
Cooked Bacon; crumbled, Swiss Cheese; grated, 9" Quiche Pastry, Half-and-half, Rendered Bacon Fat; use, Parmesan cheese, Pepper, Onion, Eggs, Salt, 
SAute onion until tender. Place bacon, onion and cheeses in pie shell.  Blend together eggs, cream, salt and pepper. Pour over mixture in quiche  shell. Bake at 375F. for 45 minutes, or until mixture is firm and light  brown.    Source: Shelby Smith of Stinson Beach, Ca Typed by Katherine Smith  Kook-Net: The Shadow Zone IV - Stinson Beach, CA
