Potato Cakes - Teisennau Tatws
Self-raising flour, Sugar, Cinnamon or mixed spice, Potatoes, Butter, Milk or 1 egg, 
Peel and boil the potatoes in salted water. Drain the  potatoes and mix in all the ingredients. Turn on to a  floured board and shape into rounds about 1 inch  thick and cook in a fairly hot oven (400o F. or  Mark 6) for twenty minutes. Serve hot with bacon  or top with fried eggs.
