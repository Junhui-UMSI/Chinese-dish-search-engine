Watercress-Mushroom Salad
Fresh lime juice, Cloves (large) garlic;, Fresh rosemary, Head endive; separated, Bunch watercress; rinsed,, Fresh button mushrooms; very, Caribe (N.M. red chile), Fresh minced basil, Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Fresh minced tarragon, Fresh minced thyme, Dijon style mustard, 
Combine oil, garlic, herbs and mustard and let stand at least 30 minutes  (or longer) at room temperature while you prepare the salad ingredients.  Arrange watercress, endive & mushrooms in a wooden or glass bowl;  refrigerate until cold. To serve, sprinkle with caribe, then add dressing  and lace with lime juice. Toss and serve. Makes 4 servings.    From the <Hotter Than Hell!>, by Jane Butel, ISBN 0-89586-646-3  (0-89586-542-4 paperback).  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
