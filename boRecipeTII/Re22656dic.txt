Apricot Cream-Filled Waffles
(3-oz) cream cheese;, Honey, Canned apricot halves;, Hungry Jack Microwave Ready, Frozen waffles; toasted, 
In small bowl, combine cream cheese and honey; blend well. Stir in  apricots. Spoon and spread apricot mixture onto 4 waffles. Top with  remaining waffles. Serve with syrup.
