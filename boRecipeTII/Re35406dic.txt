Beef Embul
Cm cinnamon stick, Tamarind, White pepper, Water, Turmeric, Cm lemon grass, Beef, Salt, Chilli powder, 
Wash and cut beef into 2 cm cubes. Mix tamarind with water and remove  seeds.  Place tamarind water in a pan, add chilli powder, pepper powder,  salt, turmeric, cinnamon stick and lemon grass and mix well. Add beef cubes  and simmer over a medium heat until the meat is tender and the gravy has  thickened.   Remove  the cinnamon stick before serving. From "A taste of  Sri Lanka" by Indra Jayasekera, ISBN #962 224 010 0
