Tropical Fruit Fluff
Apricot preserves, Sour cream, milk, Flaked coconut, Walnuts, 
In small bowl combine sour cream, coconut, walnuts & apricot preserves. Mix  well. Stir in small amount of milk to make mixture of dipping consistency.  Chill. Top with chopped walnuts & additional coconut. Serve with chilled  fruits, such as seedless green grapes, melon balls, fresh pineapple wedges  & sliced bananas.
