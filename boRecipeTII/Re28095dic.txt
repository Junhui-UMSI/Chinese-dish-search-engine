Baked Cod
olive oil, Lemon pepper, Cod fillets, 
Cook: 10 min.    Preheat oven to 375F. Arrange fillets in a baking dish. Drizzle with oil  and season with salt and lemon pepper to taste. Bake 10-12 minutes or until  fish is opaque throughout.    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Feb 20, 1998
