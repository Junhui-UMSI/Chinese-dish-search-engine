Yellow Squash Casserole
Onions, Roll Ritz Cracker, Cheddar Cheese, Margarine, Yellow Squash, 
Melt margarine, add onions & saute. Crush roll of crackers and add to  cooked onion Immediately turn off burner, then stir; remove from heat.  Layer 2 qt. deep casserole dish with squash, then cracker mix, then cheese;  repeat ending with squash. Bake 350 F for 35 minutes uncovered, 10 - 15  additional minutes covered.
