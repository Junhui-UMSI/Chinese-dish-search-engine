Beef Patties Italian-Style
Dried oregano, crumbled, Ground beef, Salt to taste, Sliced cheese, provolone or, Green pepper, cut into strip, Spaghetti sauce (1 jar), Spaghetti (8oz), Fresh oregano, chopped or, Mozzarella or fontina, Onion, sliced, Fresh mushrooms, sliced, Clove of garlic, minced, 
Cook spaghetti. Meanwhile, shape ground beef into 4 oval patties about  1/2-inch thick. In large skillet, fry patties until browned on both sides.  Sprinkle with salt. Remove patties to platter. Add onion to drippings in  pan and cook until tender. Add garlic, mushrooms and peppers. Cook until  tender crisp. Drain off fat. Stir the oregano and spaghetti sauce into  vegetables and heat to boiling. Simmer for 1 minute. Return beef patties to  skillet. Top each with a cheese slice and heat through. Drain spaghetti  Place on plate. With pancake turner, transfer patties and sauce over  spaghetti. Garnish with fresh oregano, if desired. SERVES 4.
