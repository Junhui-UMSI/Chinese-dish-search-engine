Big and Soft Raisin-Oatmeal Cookies
Butter, Dark or light corn syrup, Sugar, Vanilla, Dark brown sugar, Eggs, Baking soda, Mace, Oats, Raisins, Smooth peanut butter, 
Beat butter in mixer until softened. Beat in peanut butter. Beat in  vanilla, corn syrup and both sugars until light and fluffy. Add eggs,  beating after each addition. Add baking soda and mace. Gradually add oats.  Stir in raisins. For each cookie, shape 1/4 c. dough into ball and place  four on cookie sheet. then press into 3" circle about 1.2" thick. Bake 350  for 16-17 min. Let stand on cookie sheets. 1 min. From LHJ, 10/90, page  198.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on Oct  20, 1998,
