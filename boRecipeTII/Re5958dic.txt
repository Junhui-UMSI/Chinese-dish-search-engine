Pistachio Raisin Bread
Honey, Pistachio nuts, Water, Heated to 80-85 deg, Bread flour, Whole wheat flour, Raisins, Vegetable oil, Salt, Yeast, 
Add first six ingredients to bread machine in order listed. Bang sides to  settle ingredients. Make well in center and add yeast. Set on Sweet cycle.  Add raisins and nuts at beep (or after first kneading). Makes a 2 pound  loaf.
