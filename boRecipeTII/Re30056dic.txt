Balsamic Dressing
Sage; minced, Balsamic vinegar, Pure olive oil, Basil leaves; minced, Garlic; pureed, Oregano; minced, Italian flat-leaf parsley;, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, 
1. Combine all & mix well.    2. season with salt & pepper to taste, let marinate for 24 hours in ice  box.    3. stir very well each time before using.    Excellent on baby greens!!
