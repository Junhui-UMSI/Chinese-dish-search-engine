Banana Split Sundae Cookies
Egg whites, Low-fat vanilla ice cream;, Fat-free caramel topping, Unbleached flour, Margarine, Baking soda, Semisweet chocolate chips, Vanilla, Brown Sugar, Bananas, Rolled oats, 
submitted by: matejka@bga.com (Anita A. Matejka, Texas, USA)    Recipe By : Bake It Better With Quaker Oats    Preheat oven to 350. In a mixing bowl, combine margarine, bananas, egg  whites, and vanilla. In another mixing bowl, combine rolled oats, flour,  sugar, baking soda, and chocolate chips. Mix dry ingredients with wet  ingredients just until moistened. Drop dough by 1/4 measuring cupfuls onto  unprepared baking sheet, about 3" apart. Spread dough to 3 1/2" in  diameter. Bake for 14 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool 1 minute on  baking sheet and remove to wire rack.    Per serving: 208 Calories; 7g Fat (29% calories from fat); 4g Protein; 35g  Carbohydrate; 1mg Cholesterol; 132mg Sodium    Serving Ideas : To serve, top each cookie a scoop of ice cream & topping.
