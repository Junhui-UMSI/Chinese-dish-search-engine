Very Lemony Broccoli Pasta
Garlic, Pasta, White wine, Ground pepper, Lemon, olive oil, Broccoli florets, Parmesan cheese, 
Cook pasta and steam broccoli. Grate the peel of the lemon and squeeze the  juice. Sautee garlic, then add lemon peel and pepper. Pour in the wine and  lemon juice and let cook for a little while, until some of the liquid  disappears. Add broccoli and let it get warm. Meanwhile, cook the pasta.  When it is done, drain and put back into the warm pan and mix immediately  with the sauce. Sprinkle with parmesan and serve!    Serves: 4    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Mar 04, 1998
