Whoopie Pies
Vegetable oil, Salt, Flour, Sugar, Baking cocoa, Buttermilk or sour milk, Hot water, Vanilla, Egg, Baking soda, 
In a bowl, blend in dry ingredients, then add remaining ingredients mix  with electric mixer til blended. Drop on cookie sheet by teaspoon.    Bake at 350 for 8 minutes or until done.    Remove from oven and allow to cool. While cakes cool, prepare fillijng.  When COOL, take one half (one cake) and spread filling on bottom, then take  one more half (one cake) and place on top of the first. (two halfs equal  one whoppie pie)    Serving Ideas : Serve with MILK    NOTES : Goes well at parties, or where finger food or snacks are used    Recipe by: Family    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #496 by Steve Parks <CobraSon@concentric.net>  on Mar 3, 1997.
