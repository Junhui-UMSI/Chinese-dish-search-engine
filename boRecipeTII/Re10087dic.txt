Queen Alexandras Sandwiches
MUSTARD BUTTER, Salt, Unsalted butter, Mild french mustard, Black pepper, TABASCO pepper sauce, Cress, chicken, Brown bread, Lemon juice, Mayonnaise, Roast lamb, mustard, 
To make the mustard butter, beat all the ingredients together to a smooth  paste. Store in a jar in the refrigerator.    Mix the chicken and mayonnaise with a fork, and season with salt, pepper  and Tabasco. Spread half the slices of bread with the mustard butter. Lay  slices of lamb or tongue on the bread and spread with the chicken mixture.  Add mustard and cress. Top with the remaining slices of bread to make  sandwiches, trim the crusts and cut into dainty squares.
