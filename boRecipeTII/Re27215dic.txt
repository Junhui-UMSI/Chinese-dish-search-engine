Tipsy Cake
Warm custard, Sherry, Jam, Measure whisky, Stale cake (pound, angel), Whipped cream, 
Break up the cake or cakes and gently mix the jam through -- the mixture  does not have to be completely even.  Place in a glass bowl.  Mix sherry  and whisky and sprinkle over cake.  Press down lightly.  Pour custard over  the cake, and chill.  Then spoon whipped cream over top and serve.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/irish.zip
