Three Bean Rice Salad
Rice, Red kidney beans, drained, Thinly sliced onion rings, Prepared Italian salad, Cut wax beans, drained, French cut green beans,, Salt, Pepper, Water, 
Mix beans, salad dressing, onion, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Set  aside to allow flavors to blend. Fold rice into bean mixture. Cover and  chill thoroughly. Serve on a bed of lettuce.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  May 16, 1998
