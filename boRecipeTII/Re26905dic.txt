Baked Brie
brie, Butter, Crescent rolls, 
Take two triangles of crescent roll dough and press them together to form a  square, repeat with other 6 triangles. Cut round of brie in half heigth  wise (you should have to rounds now). Form two squares of dough around each  round to completely cover. Brush with melted butter. Follow baking  instructions as given on crescent roll package.
