Plain French Omelet
Salt, milk, Vegetable oil, Black Pepper, Eggs, 
Heat oil in skillet; then tip skillet to grease sides. Beat eggs in mixer  at high speed, adding salt, milk and pepper. Pour into skillet and cook  over low heat until a film of cooked eggs forms in bottom of pan. Tip  skillet and lift this with spatula to let raw egg mixture to run under the  cooked. Continue this process until eggs are all cooked and bottom slightly  brown, about 10 minutes. This serves 4. You may add herbs, cheese, ham or  vegetables to vary.
