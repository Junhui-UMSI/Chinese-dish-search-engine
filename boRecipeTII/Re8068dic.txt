Potato Bacon Fritatta
Pepper, Bacon, Frozen potatoes with onions, Cheddar, Butter or margarine, Eggs, fresh tomato, GARNISHES, milk, Salt, 
Melt butter in 10 inch non stick skillet over medium heat, add frozen  potatoes and cook 10 minutes or until browned.    Combine eggs and next 3 ingredients, stirring with a wire wish and pour  over potatoes. As mixture starts to cook, gently lift edges of frittata  with a spatula and tilt pan so uncooked portion flows underneath. If center  remains uncooked, carefully turn frittata over and cook 5 minutes or until  center is done.    Place frittata on serving platter, and sprinkle with shredded cheese. Cover  with aluminum foil and let stand for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with bacon and  garnish if desired. Serve immediately.
