Sweet/sour Meatballs
Ginger, Vinegar, Salt, Shortening, Cornstarch, Brown Sugar, Ground beef, Pineapple tidbits; 13.5 oz, milk, | drained (reserve syrup), Cracker crumbs, Egg, Soy sauce, Onion; minced, Green bell pepper; chopped, 
Mix thoroughly beef, crumbs, onion, egg, salt, ginger and milk. Shape  mixture by rounded tablespoonfuls into balls. Melt shortening in large  skillet; brown and cook meatballs. Remove meatballs; keep warm. Pour fat  from skillet.    Mix cornstarch and sugar.  Stir in reserved pineapple syrup, vinegar and  soy sauce until smooth.  Poor into skillet; cook over medium heat, stirring  constantly, until mixture thickens and boils. Boil and stir 1 minute. Add  meatballs, pineapple tidbits and green pepper; heat through.
