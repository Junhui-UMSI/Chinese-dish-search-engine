Tomato and Brie Sauce
Cloves garlic; (crushed), Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Fresh basil; (chopped), Brie; (chopped, with rind, Linguine, Very ripe tomatoes; (peeled), 
Peel and chop tomatoes and garlic and mix with basil and oil. Add brie to  tomato mixture and Let stand at room temp. for 2 hours.Cook pasta and serve  hot with sauce. Heat from noodles will melt cheese and warm sauce.    Serves: 4    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Mar 04, 1998
