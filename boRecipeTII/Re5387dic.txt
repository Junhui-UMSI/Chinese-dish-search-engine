Pineapple Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting
Walnuts, Flour, Brown sugar, Crushed pineapple in juice  , Eggs, Baking soda, Butter, Sugar, FROSTING, CAKE, Cream cheese, Vanilla, Powdered sugar, 
Combine all cake ingredients and pour into an ungreased, 13x9 inch Pyrex  pan.  Bake at 350^ for 45-50 minutes. Combine frosting ingredients and  spread on cooled pineapple cake. ~Diana Rattray  Recipe By     : Berkshire Seasonings    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #255    Date: Wed, 23 Oct 1996 18:54:25 +0000    From: Patti McCoy <patti-will-mccoy@postoffice.worldnet.att.net>
