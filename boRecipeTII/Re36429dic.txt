Beer Catfish
Vegetable Oil, Self-Rising Flour, Garlic Powder, Beer, Catfish Fillets, salt and pepper, 
Combine 1 cup flour, beer, garlic salt, salt and pepper in a bowl. Mix  well.  Dip catfish strips in remaining flour and then in batter. Fry in hot  oil in a deep-fryer or skillet until golden brown. Drain on absorbent  towels.  Serve hot.    Serve with your favorite sauce.    Yield:  4 servings.
