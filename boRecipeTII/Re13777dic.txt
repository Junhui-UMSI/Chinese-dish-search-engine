Wild Rice with Thyme and Almonds
Water, olive oil, Shallot, peeled and chopped, Ground pepper, to taste, Almond slivers, blanched, Golden raisins, Lemon rind, grated, Fresh thyme, minced, WILD rice, washed, Salt, 
Combine wild rice, water and salt in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until tender, about 40 minutes. Uncover  and toss in remaining ingredients. Serve immediately.    Recipe by: NEW YORK TIMES    Sent to me by Jane Rosenberg-Coombs by jfcoombs@avana.net on 1 Fe, b 97.
