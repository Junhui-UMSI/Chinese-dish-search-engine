Thai Pasta Salad with Peanut Sauce
Ground red pepper, Chopped, Evaporated skim milk, Fresh ginger root -- grated, Green onion -- finely, Red onion -- finely chopped, Whole wheat spaghetti --, Lemon juice, Soy sauce, low sodium, Cooked, drained, hot, Brown sugar, Creamy peanut butter, 
1. Combine milk, peanut butter, red onion, lemon juice, sugar, soy sauce,  and  red pepper in medium saucepan.  Bring to a boil over high heat, stirring  constantly.  Boil 2 minutes, stirring constantly.  Reduce heat to  medium-low.  Add ginger; blend well.  Add spaghetti;  toss to coat.    2. Top servings evenly with green onion.  Serve.
