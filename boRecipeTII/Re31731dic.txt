Swordfish W/sun-Dried Tomatoes/olives/lemon
Swordfish steak, Marinated sun-dried tomatoes, Kalamata olives; pitted, Lemon; slice thin, 
Blend tomatoes with 8 pitted Kalamata olives. Lay lemon slice on the bottom  of baking dish. Place swordfish steak on the top and spread the sundried  tomato and olive mixture onto the swordfish. Bake for 17 to 20 minutes in a  preheated oven (350). (wrv)
