Vegetable Almondine Bake
Almonds; blanched, slivered, Wheat flakes, Curry, Thyme, Salt, Tomato; chopped, Sesame oil, Green pepper; chopped, Sage, Onion, Scallions; chopped, 
Preheat oven to 375 deg. Lightly grease 4 x 8 baking dish with sesame oil.  Cook wheat flakes in 12 ounces of water for 10 to 12 minutes. Saute  vegetables, almonds, thyme, sage, curry and salt in a skillet with sesame  oil for 5 minutes. Combine with wheat flakes and transfer to baking dish  and bake for 15 minutes.   Serves 2.
