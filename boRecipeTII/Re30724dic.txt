Tampas Own Fish Chowder
Fish fillets (snapper or, Chicken (skinles), Slices of bacon, Black pepper, Diced potatos, Dice celery, Lemon- juice of, Salt, Onions, Ground dill, Half & Half, Ground cumin, Ground ginger, Garlic minced, Boned and diced cooked, Grouper), Diced carrots, HOT water(not quite boiling), Paprika, 
Fry bacon & remove from pan. Add onions & garlic to drippings. Saute     until tender but not browned. Add the water, potatos, carrots, celery,  and     paprika. Cook until the potatos are almsot done. Cut fish into 1 inch     squares. Add seasonings and fish at the same time. Cook until fish  becomes     flaky. Pour in the Half & Half and stir well.     NOTE*....you may add 2 Tblspns of a dry white wine to this as well.
