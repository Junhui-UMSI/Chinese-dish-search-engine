Pepper Steak #4
Butter, Or, Water, Steak; cut in strips, Tomatoes; chopped, Onions; chopped, Soy sauce, Cornstarch, Garlic salt, -(up to), Green peppers; chopped, 
Brown steak in butter and simmer a few minutes. Remove meat from pan and  set aside. Add onions, pepper and tomatoes to pan, season with garlic salt,  and saute a few minutes. Return meat to pan. Meanwhile, combine cornstarch,  water and soy sauce. Stir into meat mixture and simmer until thickened.  Serve over coked rice.    From a collection of my mothers (Judy Hosey) recipe box which contained  lots of her favorite recipes, clippings, etc.  Downloaded from Glens MM  Recipe Archive, G Internet.
