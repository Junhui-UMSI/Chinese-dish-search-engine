Toffee Tira Mi Su
Frozen pound cake (10.75, Whipping; (heavy) cream, Strong coffee, Chocolate flavored syrup, Bars chocolate-covered, Cream cheese, 
1. Arrange cake slices in rectangular baking dish, 11 X 7 X 1", cutting  cake slices if necessary to cover bottom of dish. Drizzle coffee over cake.    2. Bead sugar, chocolate syrup and cream cheese in large bowl with electric  mixer on medium speed until smooth. Add whipping cream. Beat on medium  speed unti light and fluffy. Spread over cake. Sprinkle with candy.    3. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour, but no longer than 24 hours.
