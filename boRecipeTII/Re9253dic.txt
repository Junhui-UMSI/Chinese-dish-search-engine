Pudding Chillers
(4 oz) jello choc or vanilla, Cold milk, 
Pour milk into medium bowl. Add pudding and beat with wire whisk 2 minutes.  Spoon into six 5-oz paper or plastic cups. Insert sticks into each for a  handle. Freeze for 5 hours or until firm. Place bottom of cup under warm  running water for 15 seconds. Press firmly on bottom of cup to release.  Makes 6    Recipe by: Jello    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by "Chris or Terri Weidman"  <weedbear@infowest.com> on Apr 25, 1998
