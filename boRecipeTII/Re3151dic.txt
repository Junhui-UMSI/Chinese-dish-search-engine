Peanuts, Sugar Coated
On, Water, Granulated sugar, Raw shelled peanuts -- skins, 
Preheat oven at 300.  Dissolve sugar in water in a saucepan over medium  heat.  Add peanuts and continue to cook over medium heat, stirring  frequently.  Cook until peanuts are completely sugared (coated and no  syrup).  Pour on a unprepared baking sheet, separate peanuts with a fork.  Bake for 30 minutes, stirring at 10 minute intervals.  Makes 2 1/2 cups.    Recipe By     : Anita A. Matejka    From:                                 Date: 05/28    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
