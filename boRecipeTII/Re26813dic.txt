Baked Artichoke Hearts
Garlic butter, melted, Bread crumbs, 13-oz can whole artichoke, Pine nuts, toasted, Parmesan cheese, grated, Cream cheese -OR- Boursin, 
Open artichoke heart centers gently. Stuff with cream cheese. Dip into  butter, then mixture of Parmesan cheese and crumbs. Press pine nuts into  cheese. Place on foil-lined baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 30  minutes. May flavor cream cheese with any favorite herbs.    Approx per serving:  Cal 251; Prot 6 g; Carbo 11 g; Fiber <1 g; Fat 20g;                       72% Calories from Fat; Chol 49 mg; Sod 457 mg.
