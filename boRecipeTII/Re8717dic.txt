Poultry Flavoring Mix
Sage, Garlic powder, Turmeric, Salt, Black pepper, Marjoram, Rosemary, Onion powder, Nutritional yeast flakes, Paprika, Thyme, Ginger, Tarragon, Celery seed, 
Pulverize in a blender and store in a cool place.  For gravy, buzz ~1 Tb.  flavor mix and ~1 Tb. flour per 3 - 4 fl.oz. water in a blender depending  on desired thickness. Simmer or microwave, stirring periodically until  thickened. Thin with water as desired.  Good broth base -- make a veggie  chickenless soup or gravy with this.
