Tandoori Murgh (Tandoori Chicken)
Chicken, Ginger, Garlic, Plain yogurt, Salt, Cayenne pepper, Paprika, Garam masala, Coriander seeds, Cumin seeds, Lemon juice, 
* I use a blend of 1 nutmeg, 1 1/2 tsp green cardamom pods, 4 cinnamon  sticks, 1 Tbsp whole cloves, 1 tsp peppercorns & 1 tsp cumin seeds, all ground together    Skin the chicken, wash & pat dry with paper towel. Make  deep cuts on the surface with a knife. Mince or grate ginger and garlic.  Transfer to a small bowl and add the spices. Mix well, then rub the paste  into the chicken and let marinate several hours in refrigerate.   Cook  chicken on grill or in broiler (30 minutes for legs), basting with leftover  marinade and turning to ensure even cooking. Serve with a dash of lemon  juice.   Serves: 4-6 Source:_From Bengal to Punjab: The Cuisines of India_  posted by Anne MacLellan          :
