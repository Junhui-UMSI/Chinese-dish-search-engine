Fake Pareve Eclairs
Graham crackers, Boxes of pareve vanilla, Rice milk, Container frozen pareve, Chocolate chips, Margarine, Kahlua or rum or orange, 
Beat whippd cream; mix vanilla pudding with milk and fold in whipped cream;  put a layer of graham crackers on bottom of 9 by 13 pan; cover with layer  of pudding; cover with layer of graham crackers and rest of pudding. add  one more layer of graham crackers. melt chocolate with margarine and stir  in orange juice or liqueur and spread on top of graham crackers. chill  overnight or longer.
