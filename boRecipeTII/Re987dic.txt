Italian Oven Cheese Chowder
Basil, Zucchini; sliced, Onions; sliced, (15 1/2 oz) garbanzo beans, (1-lb) diced tomatoes, Butter; (or margarine), Dry white wine, Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Bay leaf, Grated Monterey Jack cheese, Grated Romano cheese, Whipping cream; (condensed, 
(The S.F. Chronicle)    Combine all ingredients except cheeses and cream in a 3-quart baking dish.  Cover. Bake in a 400 degree oven for one hour, stirring once half way  through. Stir in cheeses, then stir in cream. Bake 10 minutes longer. Makes        3    delicious quarts.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Linda Shapiro <lss@coconet.com> on Oct 05,  1998,
