Zucchini Stroganoff
Chervil or basil, Egg noodles, Sliced zucchini, Salt and pepper, Mushroom soup, Ground beef [turkey or lamb, Onions, Sour cream, 
Place ground meat and onion in 10" skillet.  Cook until meat loses pink  colour.  Spread over the meat mixture, the mushroom soup, noodles, zucchini  and seasonings.  Simmer covered until noodles are done.  Stir in sour cream  and warm through.    Serves 2 [generously]    Posted to Recipe Archive - 24 November 96    Date: Sun, 24 Nov 96 7:21:04 EST    submitted by: jmckerr@island.net
