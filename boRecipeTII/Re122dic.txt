Kips Country Bbq Sauce
Garlic powder, Tomato sauce, Red wine vinegar, Dark brown sugar, Celery seed, White pepper, Black pepper, Coarse black pepper, Coarse red pepper, Liquid smoke, Cayenne pepper, 
Put all ingridients into pan. Bring to boil for 2 minutes under high heat.    Turn heat to low and simmer 1/2 hour. Let cool and use cold. This is a dipping sauce. BBQ should be dipped into sauce every 15 min for grilling and every 45 min. for smoking.   Sauce can be put over chicken, ribs, pork, or beef and baked in oven as well.
