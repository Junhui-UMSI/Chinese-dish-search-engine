Strawberry Broil
Strawberries; halves, Nonfat sour cream, Brown Sugar, Cinnamon; ground, 
Put strawberries in an ungreased shallow 1 1/2 qt baking dish. Combine sour  cream and cinnamon; spoon over berries. Sprinkle with brown sugar. Broil  until bubbly, about 3-4 minutes. Serve over low-fat ice cream if desired.    Recipe by: Country Woman Magazine, 5/97 Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #670  by hurlbert <hurlbert@concentric.net> on Jul 13, 1997
