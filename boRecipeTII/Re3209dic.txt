Pear and Walnut Salad
Ripe pears, Lemon juice, Pear brandy; if available,, Walnuts, 
Peel, core and slice ripe pears and sprinkle with lemon juice. Pour over a  little pear brandy if available, or kirsch or white rum. At serving time,  sprinkle with chopped walnuts.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /FRUIT    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
