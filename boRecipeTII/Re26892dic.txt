Baked Beet Salad
Spanish onion, VINAIGRETTE, Vegetable oil, Beets, Dijon mustard, Salt, Fresh mint, Pepper, Cider vinegar, 
Remove stems and leaves from beets; wrap beets and onion separately in foil  to make 2 packages. Bake in 350F 180C oven for about 1 hour or until  vegetables are fork-tender.    Remove vegetables from foil; let stand for 15-20 minutes or until cool  enough to handle. Loosen skins from vegetables; slice onion into strips and  cube beets.    Vinaigrette: Meanwhile, in large bowl, whisk together oil, vinegar, mint,  mustard, salt and pepper.    Toss vegetables with vinaigrette: let stand at room temperature for 1 hour  or up to 4 hours. Toss lightly before serving.
