7 Layer Cookies
Butterscotch chips, Butter, Eagle Brand milk, Graham cracker crumbs, Chocolate chips, Coconut, Nuts, 
Melt butter in bottom of 9 x 13 inch cake pan. Press graham cracker crumbs  into pan. Pour can of Eagle Brand milk over graham cracker crumbs. Sprinkle  chocolate chips and butterscotch chips and nuts over mixture. Sprinkle  coconut on top. Bake at 350 degrees until coconut browns, (approximately 15  minutes).    busted by sooz    Recipe by: Key Gourmet    Posted to recipelu-digest by sooz <kirkland@gj.net> on Mar 19, 1998
