Cocoa Rum Balls
Vanilla wafer crumbs, Pecans, Powdered sugar, Light rum, Light corn syrup, Powdered sugar, 
In large bowl, combine crumbs, nuts, 3/4 cup powdered sugar and cocoa. Add  rum and corn syrup; blend well. Shape into 1-inch balls; roll in powdered  sugar. Store in airtight container 2 to 3 days to develop flavor. Reroll in  powdered sugar before serving. About 4 dozen balls.    * 1/2 cup orange juice plus 1 teaspoon freshly grated orange peel can be  substituted for rum.
