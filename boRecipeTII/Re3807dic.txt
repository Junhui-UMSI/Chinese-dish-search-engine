Pennsylvania Dutch Sandwich
ham, Sauerkraut, butter, Swiss cheese, Caraway seeds, Rye bread, Mayonnaise, 
Cut ham and cheese into julienne strips. Blend caraway seeds and  mayonnaise. Add ham and cheese. Stir to coat all pieces. Add sauerkraut.  Spread bread with butter or margarine. Spread half of bread slices with  ham-cheese mixture. Top with remaining bread slices. Wrap each sandwich  with plastic wrap or foil and refrigerate. Yield: 6 sandwiches.    CEILE FAULKNER (MRS. JIM)    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
