Barbecue Halibut Oriental
Garlic, Soy sauce, Orange juice, Chopped fresh parsley, Ketchup, Lemon juice, Halibut, Salad oil, Oregano, Pepper, 
Cut fish into fillets and place in a single layer in a shallow pan. Combine  the remaining ingredients, mix well and pour over the fish. Marinate for 30  minutes, turning the fillets once or twice to be sure they are well coated.  Grill fish, reserving the sauce to pour over the fillets before serving.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
