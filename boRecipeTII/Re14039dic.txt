White Chocolate Macadamia Brownies with Hot Fudge Sauce
Unbleached flour, Salt, Eggs, Granulated sugar, Imported white chocolate, Unsalted butter, Vanilla extract , Unsalted macadamia nuts, Imported white chocolate, 
Preheat over to 350F. Butter and flour 9x13 pan. Sift flour with salt into  bowl. Using electric mixer, beat eggs in large bowl until frothy. Add sugar 1 tablespoon at a time and beat until pale yellow and slowly dissolving  ribbon forms when beaters are lifted. Gently fold in melted chocolate  (mixture may lose volume). Add melted butter and vanilla and stir well  (mixture may look crudled). Stir in flour then fold in nuts and chopped chocolate. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake until toothpick inserted in  center comes out not quite clean, about 25 minutes; do not overbake. Cool in pan on rack, (Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Store at room temperature.)  Cut brownies into squares. Transfer to plates. Pour sauce over and serve.
