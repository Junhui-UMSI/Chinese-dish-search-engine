Warm Shrimp and Orange Salad (Mf)
Clov garlic, thinly sliced, Calamari cut into rings, Smoked mussels, Sherry wine vinegar, (up to, olive oil, Peeled and deveined shrimp,, Cooked great northern beans, Navel oranges, cut into, Mixed greens such as frisee,, Salt, 
Heat the olive oil in a skillet. Add the garlic, calamari and shrimp and  saute for 1 minute. Add the beans and saute for a minute. Add the vinegar  and immediately remove the skillet from the heat and season to taste with  salt and pepper.    Transfer the mixture to a bowl and add the smoked mussels and oranges and  toss. Set on a bed of mixed greens and serve at room temperature.
