Yogurt-Garlic Sauce
Garlic clove; crushed, Salt, Plain yogurt, 
With the side of a chefs knife, mash garlic and 1/4 tsp. salt together to  form a paste. Place in a bowl and stir in yogurt. Season with more salt to  taste.    Makes 1/2 cup.
