Baked String Beans
Hot water, Frozen string beans, Pepper, Bacon, Beef Bouillon, Garlic salt, 
Place beans in an ungreased 1-1/2 quart baking dish; set aside. Cook bacon  until crisp; remove with a slotted spoon. Crumble and set aside. Stir 1  tablespoon of drippings into beans. Dissolve bouillon in water, add garlic  salt and pepper. Pour over beans. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Top with bacon.
