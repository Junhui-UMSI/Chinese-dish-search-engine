Vanilla Poached Plums
Vanilla bean, plums, Lemon juice, Margarine, Sugar, 
Cut vanilla bean in half lengthwise, and scrape seeds into a small bowl;  discard bean. Add sugar and stir well.    Place plums in a 1-quart baking dish, add sugar mixture and lemon juice,  tossing gently to coat. Dot with margarine; cover and bake at 400F for 20  min or until plums are tender, stirring once.    Yield: 4 servings @ 112 calories each.
