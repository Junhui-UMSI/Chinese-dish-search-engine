Swiss Style Oats
Mixed dried fruit, chopped, Slivered almonds, toasted, Sea salt (optional), Honey, Water, AM Oat Flakes (rolled oats), AM Wheat Germ, 
Bring water to a boil; stir in fruit, salt, and honey.  Bring to boil  again.  Add oats; stir; lower heat to simmer.  Cover and simmer 15 minutes  (stir before serving).  Sprinkle each serving with 1-1/2 teaspoon wheat  germ and 1 tablespoon almonds.  Serve with milk.
