Austrian Liver Dumplings
Egg, Breadcrumbs, Butter, Hard roll OR, Large slice Italian bread, Salt and pepper to taste, Chopped fresh parsley, 
1>. Cut liver into chunks and place into blender until all meat has been  pureed. Soak the roll or bread in water until soft, squeeze out the surplus  moisture and finely mince or grate. Cream the butter with the other  ingredients, finally adding sufficient breadcrumbs to make the mixture firm  enough to be formed into small dumplings (less than an inch in diameter).  If the dumplings are to served with stew or soup, simmer in this for 5  minutes. They may also be cooked in stock and served with meat and gravy.
