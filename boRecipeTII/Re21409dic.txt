Updated Mincemeat
Cinnamon, Brandy, Unsalted butter or margarine, Candied orange peel, White pepper, Almonds, Mace, Candied lemon peel, Allspice, Raisins, Currants,, Citron, Chopped apples, Sultanas, 
PREHEAT OVEN TO 325F. Combine all ingredients and grind twice through the  finest blade of your meat grinder. Place in a lidded oven-proof pot and  place in the oven. Bake for 40 minutes. Remove from oven and stir in  brandy. Store the mincemeat in a cool place for at least 1 week before  using, or up to 6 months. Makes 2 Pies
