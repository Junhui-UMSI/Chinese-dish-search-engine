Wisconsin Cheese Muffin
Flour, Garlic powder, Milk or 1 cup beer, Baking Powder, Vegetable oil, Wisconsin cheddar cheese;, Egg, Salt, Sugar, 
Mix the dry stuff and the wet stuff in different bowls, then mix together  along with the cheese. Spoon into greased muffin tins and bake for 18 to 20  minutes at 400 degrees (f). When done let them sit for 5 minutes before  taking them out of the pan.
