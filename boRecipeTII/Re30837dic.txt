Tangy Cucumber and Mung Bean Sprout Salad
cucumbers, Mung bean sprouts, fresh pea greens, fresh waterchestnuts, 
2 Tbs. light soy sauce 1 Tbs. cider vinegar 1 Tbs. freshly minced garlic x  several splashes to 1 tsp. Tabasco sauce (no, really, it works!) 1 Tbs.  black bean sauce 1/4 tsp. fresh ground pepper    Peel the cucumber, slice it down the middle and remove the seeds. Cut each  half down the middle and slice these strips into bite sized pieces.    Toss cucumbers and mung bean sprouts into a large sealable container. Mix  the dressing separately and pour over the veggies.  Put the lid on the  container and shake it.  Put it in the fridge to marinate for 3 hours or  over night, shaking it whenever you think of it. :)    Just before serving add the pea greens and peeled, chopped waterchestnuts  and shake the whole thing together again.
