Pineapple Salsa (Hot, Hot, Hot!!!)
Minced fresh mint minced, Minced fresh cilantro, Chili pepper; minced finely, Very ripe pineapple; corred, Fresh lime juice, Red pepper; minced finely, 
I make this with 3 types of hot peppers; red, orange and green. This makes  it really pretty and colorful. The hotter the better. Serve with taco  chips. This can also be served with grilled scallops or baked ham  sandwiches.  Posted to recipelu-digest by QueenBerta@aol.com on Feb 5, 1998
