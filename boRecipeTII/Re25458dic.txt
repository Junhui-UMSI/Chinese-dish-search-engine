Tortilla Stack C/p
Green Onion; chopped, Dry Taco Seasoning Mix Up, Corn Tortillas; each cut in, Sour Cream, Lean Ground Beef (Up To; 1, Cheddar Cheese Soup;, Tomatoes; chopped, Avocado; peeled & chopped, Lettuce; shredded, 
Crumble 1/4 of ground beef into bottom of slow cooker. Top with 1/4 of  tortilla wedges.  In small bowl, combine undiluted soup and taco seasoning.  Spread 1/4 of soup mixture over tortillas in slow cooker. Sprinkle with 1/4  of tomatoes.  Repeat layering until all beef, tortillas, soup mixture and  tomatoes are used. Cover; cook on low- heat setting for 4 - 5 hours. To  serve, spoon mixture onto individual plates. Top each serving with sour  cream, lettuce, green onion, pepper and avocado.    Serves 6-7.    Per serving: 372 calories.  21 g. protien.  24 carbos. 21 grams fat. 67 mg  choleterol. 387 mg. sodium.
