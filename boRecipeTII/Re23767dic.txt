Artillery Punch #1
Brandy, Lemons; juice of, Bottles sparkling water, Sugar, -(up to), Sherry, Vodka, Rum, 
Cool brandy, rum, sherry & vodka and mix. Add sugar & lemon juice & cool  for 3-4 hours. Add cold sparkling water just prior to serving.    CPT KIRK J. CUNNINGHAM    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
