Stringbeans in Walnut Sauce
Red wine vinegar, Onion, Shelled walnuts, Salt, Fresh string beans, Chicken Stock, Water, Cilantro, Sweet paprika, Garlic, 
In mill or with a mortar & pestle, pulverize the walnuts into a paste.  Combine the stock, onions, garlic, paprika, vinegar, salt, walnut paste, &  cilantro. Mix thoroughly. Trim but do not cut the string beans. Bring the  water to boil, add the string beans and boil them for 10 minutes. Drain the  beans well. Add the beans to the walnut paste mixture and toss until coated  completely with the mixture. Serve at once.
