7-Up Bundt Cake
Flour, Eggs, 7 up (regular; not diet), Margarine, Sugar, Realemon, 
From: mark@alexr.demon.co.uk (Mark Alexander)    Date: Fri, 25 Nov 1994 16:40:31 GMT  [I recieved the following recipe from Kelly McGahey @carleton.ca, however  there wasnt a proper mail address on it and I thought people might be  interested so ive posted under my name.] [Mark - Co-Mod]    Heres one out of the "Carleton Cooks" community cookbook, this recipe  credited to Patrick OReilly.    Cream sugar and margarine and beat until light and fluffy.  Add eggs one at  a time, and beat well.  Add flour.  Beat in Realemon and 7 Up.  Pour batter  into well greased and floured Bundt pan.  Bake at 325F for 1 1/4 hours.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES ARCHIVES    /CAKES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
