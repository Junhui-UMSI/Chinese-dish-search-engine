Stuffed Flank Steak
mushrooms  , Grated Parmesan cheese, Dry Red Wine, bread stuffing, Flank steak, Sauce:, Green onions, Butter, Currant jelly, Salad oil, brown gravy mix, 
Combine bread stuffing with mushrooms and juice, butter, and cheese. Spread  over flank steak; roll up like jelly roll.  Fasten with skewers or string.  Pour oil in slow-cooking pot.  Roll steak in oil, coating all sides.    For sauce:  Prepare gravy mix according to package directions.  Pour gravy,  wine, and onions over meat.  Cover and cook on low 8 to 10 hours. Remove  meat from pot; slice.  Add jelly to sauce and stir until disolved. Serve  over meat.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
