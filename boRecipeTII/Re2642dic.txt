Peanut Butter n Chocolate Chip Cookies
Butter, Brown Sugar, Sugar, Peanut butter, Eggs, Vanilla, Flour, Baking soda, Chocolate chips, 
Heat oven to 350F.    Beat butter, sugars, and peanut butter in large bowl on medium speed until  light and fluffy.  Blend in eggs and vanilla.  Mix in flour and baking  soda.  Stir in chips.    Drop by rounded tablesppons onto ungreased cookie sheets.  Bake 10 to 12  minutes, or until lightly browned.
