Baked Ziti
Mozarella cheese, Noodles, Sausage, Prego Spaghetti Sauce, Hamburger, 
Mix hamburger and sausage together and make into balls and cook until done  and brown. Mix in prego sauce. Cook noodles according to package. Layer  noodles in bottom of casserole dish. Then spoon on sauce/meat mixture. Then  a layer of cheese(s). Continue to layer items until dish is full or you use  all the sauce and noodles. Bake in preheated oven at 325 degrees for 25-35  minutes until cheese is melted and golden. Posted to EAT-L Digest by  "Winona Y. Wentworth" <wyw@ATOMIC.NET> on Sep 2, 1997
