Pickled Okra #1
Salt, Cider vinegar, Clove garlic; chopped, Red pepper pods, Okra; washed and drained, Water, Dill seed, 
Boil water, salt and vinegar 5 minutes. Cool. Into each quart jar put okra,  3-4 cloves garlic, 1 red pepper pod and 1 teaspoon dill seed. Pour liquid  over okra and seal. Process.    NOTE:  Get a book on canning if unsure about sealing and processing.    MRS B.M.
