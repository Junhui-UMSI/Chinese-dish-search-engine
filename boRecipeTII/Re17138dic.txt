Virgin Mary
Salt -- to taste, Tomato juice, Pepper -- to taste, Lemon juice, Lime slice, Ice cubes, Worcestershire Sauce, Celery salt -- to taste, Cracked ice, Tabasco sauce, Celery stalk, 
Combine liquid ingredients and seasonings in a mixing glass. Shake with ice  and strain into a highball or Collins glass filled with ice cubes. Garnish  with celery stalk and wedge of lime.    Recipe By     : Joe Robertson    From:                                 Date:
