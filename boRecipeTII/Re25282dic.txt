Top Stove Meat Loaf
Black pepper, Ground chicken breast,, Water, White bread crumbs, Egg white; whipped, Prepared mustard, Fat-free american cheese, Tomato soup, condensed, Onions; chopped, olive oil, 
In a mixing bowl, combine chicken, bread crumbs, 1/4 cup tomato soup,  onions, egg white, and black pepper. Shape firmly into 2 loaves. In a  skillet, heat oil over medium  heat. Add loaves. Brown on both sides. Cook,  covered on low heat for 25 minutes. Stir in remaining tomato soup, water,  and mustard.  Top loaves with cheese slices. Cook, uncovered for 10  minutes.
