Almond Cheese Slices
Paprika, Lemon juice, Havarti cheese, Finely diced pimientos, Salt, Blanched almonds, Butter, Cream cheese, Worcestershire Sauce, 
Saute almonds in butter, then chop fine. In processor, blend cheeses  with remaining ingredients. Shape into a roll. Place almonds on a  piece of waxed paper. Roll cheese log in almonds to coat, wrapping  with wax paper. Refrigerate 2 hours. Remove paper & cut in even  slices.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
