Thai Vegetable Salad
Shredded carrots; scrubbed, Honey, Soy sauce; low sodium, see, Fresh Cilantro, Zucchini; julienned, Cornstarch, Shredded cabbage, Garlic, THAI DRESSING, Shredded bok choy, Water, White wine vinegar, Mung bean sprouts, Scallions; chopped, Peanut butter; natural, Crushed red pepper; flakes,, 
Make the dressing and allow to cool. Combine all of the vegetables in a  large bowl. Pour the dressing over and toss gently to mix well. Cover and  chill for an hour or two before serving. Serves 6: Per Serving with  Dressing: 60 cals, 1.5 g fat (0 mg chol); 96 mg sodium. Exchanges: 2  vegetables.    DRESSING -- combine water, vinegar, soy sauce, honey and cornstarch in a  saucepan. Bring to a boil and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture  thickens. Remove from heat. Stir in the peanut butter until smooth. Add the  remaining ingredients and stir until well combined. Makes 2.3 cup.    A Hearth Healthy Food Sponsored by the Loma Linda International Heart  Institute. Pantry: Angostura Brand makes a low sodium soy sauce.
