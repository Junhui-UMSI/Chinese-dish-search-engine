Peanut Butter Blosom Cookies
Biscuit baking mix, Eagle sweetened condensed milk, Vanilla extract, Egg, Peanut butter, Solid milk chocolate candy, 
Preheat oven to 350F.    In large mixer bowl beat sweetened condensed milk, peanut butter, egg and  vanilla until smooth.  Add biscut mix; mix well.    Chill at least 1 hour.    Shape into 1 inch balls. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.  Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned around edges. [do not  overbake]    Immediately press a candy drop in center of each cookie. Cool thoroughly  until candy drops are set. Store tightly covered at room temperature.
