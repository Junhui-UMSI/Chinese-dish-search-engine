Another Garlic Soup Recipe
olive oil, Garlic, peeled & minced, Croutons, Pepper, Onion, Vegetable stock, Tomatoes, 
Combine garlic & onion in a bowl.  Heat oil in a large pot. Add garlic &  onion & saute till soft.  Do not brown. Add chopped tomatoes & stir in  well.  After a few minutes, stir in stock & pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.  Add salt if desired.    Serve with croutons.    Source Unknown
