Pizza Sauce #07
Balsamic vinegar, Italian seasoning, Dried Oregano, Red wine, olive oil, tomato puree, Garlic, salt and pepper, Dried basil, 
Mix all ingredients and let sit at least 30 minutes and up to 1 day before  using, stirring occasionally, and refrigerated if allowed to sit more than  1 hour.
