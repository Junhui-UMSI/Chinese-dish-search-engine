Amaretto French Toast
Amaretto, French bread, Eggs, milk, Amaretto syrup, Amaretto butter, 
In shallow bowl mix all ingredients except bread.  Soak bread in mixture.  In large skillet, melt some butter. Drain bread. Cook in buttter until both  sides are golden brown. Serve immediately with Amaretto butter and Amaretto  Syrup.    NOTE: For a change, try Irish Cream instead of Amaretto.
