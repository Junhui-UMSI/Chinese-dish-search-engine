A Croque Monsieur Salad
THE SALAD, Butter, Grated Parmesan, Mixed greens (spinach; red, Garlic, Croutons, olive oil, Nasturtium flowers for, olive oil, Red wine vinegar, Lemon, Dijon mustard, Crumbled dried, Grinding of black pepper, Chopped fresh basil OR, Sugar, Julienne strips cooked ham, 
The Lemon-Basil Vinaigrette:    1.  Prepare the vinaigrette:  In a small bowl, whisk together all the  ingredients until smooth.  Set aside.    2.  Prepare the salad:  In a small skillet, heat the oil and butter over  low heat. Add the garlic and saute 10 seconds. Add the ham and saute for  another 10 seconds. Off the heat, add the grated cheese.    3.  Place the mixed greens in a salad bowl or plate and top with the ham  mixture.  Garnish with the nasturtium flowers and drizzle the vinaigrette  on top. Top with croutons. Serve this with a good crusty load of French  bread and a bottle of dry white French wine.    From:  LEFTOVERS by Kathy Gunst, Harper Perennial, New York. 1991.
