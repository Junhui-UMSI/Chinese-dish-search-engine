Bean and Beet Salad
Onions; cut in rings, Salt, French Cut Green Beans, Sliced Beets, Salad oil, Pepper, Vinegar, Sugar, Celery seed, 
Combine beans, beets and onion rings. Combine the next six ingredients and  pour over the obove. Marinate over night.    Source: Recipes Out of This World Women of St. Agnes Catholic Church  Charleston, WV 1969    billspa@icanect.net    Recipe by: Mrs. Eddie Wegmann  (school cook)    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest by Bill Spalding <billspa@icanect.net> on Mar  07, 1998
