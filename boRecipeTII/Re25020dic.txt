Tomato Sandwich Hors Doeuvres
Onion, Mayonnaise, Parsley, Deviled ham, Tomatoes, 
Combine the ham, mayonnaise and onion.  Rinse, dry and cut each tomato into  four 1/4 inch crosswise slices. Spread deviled ham mixture on half the  tomato slices and top each with a second tomato slice to make sandwiches.  Garnish with mayonnaise and a sprig of parsley and serve as a first course.
