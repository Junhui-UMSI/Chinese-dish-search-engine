Bacon Wrapped Chestnuts
Whole chestnuts, Bacon, Bar-b-que sauce, 
Drain one can whole chestnuts; take 1 pound of bacon and cut each strip in  half. Wrap each chestnut with the half strip of bacon and secure with a  toothpick. Place on an ungreased cookie sheet and bake at 350 til bacon is  done. Heat up bar-b-que sauce and gently place wrapped chestnuts in sauce,  crockpot is great for parties !!!
