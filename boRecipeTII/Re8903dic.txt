Premier White Cheesecake Tart
Chocolate wafer crumbs, Cherry pie filling, (optional), Pack Premier White baking, Vanilla, Eggs, (8 oz) packs softened cream, Sugar, Butter, Cheese, Bars, melted, 
Preheat oven to 375. In small bowl, combine chocolate wafer crmbs,  butter & 1/4 cup sugar. Press evenly onto bottom of 9" springform  pan. Refrigerate. In large bowl, beat cheese & 1/4 cup sugar until  smooth. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.  Blend in melted white chocolate & vanilla. Pour into prepared pan.  Bake 30-35 minutes until center is set. Cool completely. Loosen edge  from pan; refrigerate until serving time. Top with pie filling.
