Apple Date Muffins
Whole wheat flour, Cinnamon, Vegetable oil, Wheat bran, Egg, Dates; chopped, Low-fat milk, Apple; peeled, grated, All purpose flour, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, 
In a large bowl combine the whole wheat and all purpose flours, bran,  baking powder, baking soda and cinnamon. Stir in the dates. In another  bowl, beat the egg with sugar and oil until well mixed, then stir in the  milk and apple. Pour into the flour mixture and stir just enough to  moisten, being careful not to overmix.    Spoon into nonstick or paper-lined medium muffin tins, filling almost to  the top. Bake at 375 degrees (F) for about 20 minutes, or until firm to the  touch.
