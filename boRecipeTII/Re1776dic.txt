Chicken and Sausage Scarpariello
Boneless chicken breasts, Sweet Italian sausage, Red/green peppers, Onions, Salt and pepper, hot cherry peppers, Oregano, olive oil, 
Grill sausage and chicken breasts on a barbecue grill or broil in the oven,  until slightly less than fully cooked. In a large skillet, heat olive oil  and saute garlic, onions, green and red peppers, oregano, salt and pepper  until mostly cooked. Cut sausage and chicken into bite-size pieces. Combine  meats with peppers and onions. Add hot peppers and juice, stir, and  continue to cook 10-15 minutes or until chicken is fork tender.
