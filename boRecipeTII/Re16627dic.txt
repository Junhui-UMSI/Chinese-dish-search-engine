Ajilimojili (Garlic and Pepper Sauce)
Oil, olive, Peppers, red, hot; seeded, Garlic, Peppercorns, white, Peppers, bell, red; seeded, Lime juice, Salt, 
Pound peppers, peppercorns, garlic and salt in a mortar, or chop in  processor finely. Transfer to a bowl and beat in lime juice and oil. Served  with suckling pig.    MM and upload by DonW1948@aol.com / CBCC Submitted By DONW1948@AOL.COM On  SAT, 9 SEP 1995 204220 -0400
