Bandera Mexicana
Lime juice, Maraschino cherries, Seedless grapes; or lime, Tequila, Jicama, Crushed ice, Sugar, 
Fill six cocktail glasses with crushed ice.    With melon baller, scoop six balls from jicama. Put one jicama ball, one  grape (or lime wedge), and one cherry on each pick.    In a pitcher, combine sugar, lime juice, and tequila. Stir and pour into  cocktail glasses. Top each glass with "Mexican flag" garnish.
