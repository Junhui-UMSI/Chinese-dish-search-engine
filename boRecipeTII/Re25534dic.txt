Avocado Banana Spread
Lemon juice, Mashed ripe avocado, Mashed ripe banana, 
Blend ingredients in a food processor or blender. Heat to a boil in a  saucepan; turn to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until mixture  reaches desired thickness. Yields 1/2 cup.
