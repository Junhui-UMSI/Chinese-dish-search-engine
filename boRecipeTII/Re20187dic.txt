Vanilla-Scented Pineapple
Pineapple, fresh;, Pineapple juice;, *cals, *mg sodium, Agar-agar flakes;, ;water;, Vanilla extract;, *gm protein, *gm carbo, *gm fiber, Honey;, PER SERVING, *gm fat, 
Peel and core pineapple, then slice into 8 rings. Place one ring in each of  8 shallow dessert dishes. IN a saucepan, combine remaining ingredients;  bring to a boil. Simmer 5 minutes, stirring, until agar has completely  dissolved.  Divide sauce among dessert dishes. Chill until sauce if firm,  about 12 minutes. Serves 8.
