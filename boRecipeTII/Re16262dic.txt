Watergate Salad
Cool Whip, Mini marshmallows, pistachio Instant pudding, Walnuts, Maraschino cherries, crushed pineapple, 
Blend dry pudding mix into Cool Whip and mix well. Add crushed pineapple,  marshmallows and walnuts. Refrigerate about 2 hours.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 365 by "Pauline J.Pelletier"  <pjpellet@turbont.net> on Dec 13, 1997
