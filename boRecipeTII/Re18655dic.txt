Amish Friendship Bread
Salt, Cinnamon, Flour, Eggs, Baking Powder, Amish Starter, Vanilla, Vegetable oil, Baking soda, Sugar, 
***Do not use metal spoon ***Do not refrigerate dough!!    day 1 -- Get starter and do nothing day 2 -- Stir once each day with wooden  spoon day 3 -- Stir once each day with wooden spoon day 4 -- Stir once each  day with wooden spoon day 5 -- Add 1 cup flour, 1 cup sugar, 1 cup milk and  stir day 6 -- Stir once each day with wooden spoon day 7 -- Stir once each  day with wooden spoon day 8 -- Stir once each day with wooden spoon day 9  ~- Stir once each day with wooden spoon day 10 - Add 1 cup flour, 1 cup  sugar, 1 cup milk and stir.         pour into containers of 1 cup each and give to 3 friends         with copy of recipe (or 2 friends and keep 1 start for yourself)    To the remainder add:    2/3 cup oil          1 1/4 tsp baking powder 3 eggs 1/2 tsp baking soda 2  cups flour         1/2 tsp salt 1 cup sugar 2 tsp vanilla 2 tsp cinnamon    Pour into 2 well greased and sugared loaf pans.  Bake 40 to 50 min. at 350  degrees.  Cool 10 minutes before removing from pan.  The bread may be  frozen for a later date.
