Winter Green Salad
Lemon, Ranch dressing, Head nappa, Radishes, Seasoned salt, ground pepper, 
Cut cabbage into bite size pieces. Slice radishes and add to cabbage.  Squeeze lemon over all and sprinkle salad lightly with seasoned salt and  pepper. Add ranch dressing and toss. Posted to EAT-L Digest 25 Mar 97 by  Betsey <waterman@VT.EDU> on Mar 26, 1997
