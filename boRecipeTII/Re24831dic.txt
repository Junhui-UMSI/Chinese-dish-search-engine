Aunt Ruth Packards Potato Chip Cookies
Crushed potato chips, Flour, Sugar, Vanilla, Pecans, Sticks of butter, 
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix margarine and sugar and vanilla.  Add  crushed potato chips and nuts mixed with flour.  Roll into balls  about the size of a walnut and place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Flatten down with a glass that has been dipped into sugar.  Bake for  14-16 minutes.
