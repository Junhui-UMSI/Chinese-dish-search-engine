Tomatoe Soup
Frozen peas, Grated onion, Ground cloves, Long grain rice, Frozen corn, Juice from canned tomatoes, 
This is from the book "Choose to Lose"    Combine tomato juice, onion, cloves and rice and bring to a boil. Reduce  heat and simmer for about 25 minutes. Add peas and corn and cook about 5  minutes more.    Makes 8 one-cup servings    93 Total calories; 0 Fat calories    Each time you use canned tomatoes in a recipe and not the juice, save the  juice and freeze until you have enough for future soups.
