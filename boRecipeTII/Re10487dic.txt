Quick Fried Cabbage with Egg
Chile powder, Cabbage, Pepper (and salt if you, Onion, Peanut oil, Eggs, 
Finely slice the cabbage and onion.  Heat the oil in a pan and fry the  onion until soft. Add the cabbage, chile powder and seasoning and fry for 3  minutes.  Cover, reduce heat to low and fry for a further 10 minutes.  Beat  the eggs and pour them over the cabbage.  Stir until the eggs have set.
