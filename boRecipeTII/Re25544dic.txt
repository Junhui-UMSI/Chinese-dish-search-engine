Avocado Cilantro Soup
Lime juice, Plain Nonfat Yogurt, Ground red pepper, Garlic, Avocado, Curry powder, Skim Buttermilk, Fresh Cilantro Leaves, Scallions, 
In a food processor, combine scallions, cilantro and garlic; process until  finely chopped.  Add avocado; process until smooth. Add yogurt, buttermilk.  1/4 cup water, lime juice, curry powder and red pepper to food processor;  process until smooth.  Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour, until well  chilled.  Each 1/2 cup serving contains 115 calories, 5 gm fat, 104 mg  sodium and 3 mg cholesterol.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
