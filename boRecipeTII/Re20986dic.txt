Apple Pie
Sugar., Cinnamon., Salt., Butter or margarine., Nutmeg., Tart cooking, All-purpose flour., 
INSTRUCTIONS:Combine sugar,flour,cinnamon,nutmeg,lemon juice and salt;mix  with apples.    Divide pastry in half.Roll to 1/8 inch thickness and line a 9-inchpie  plate.Fill with apple mixture and dot with butter.Roll outremaining crust  and place on pie.Cut slits for steam to escapeand seal edges.Sprinkle with  sugar lightly.    Bake at 400^F for 50 minutes. YIELD:One 9-inch pie.    From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
