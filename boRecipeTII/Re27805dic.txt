The Tapia Familys Vegetable Stew with Chiles
Crookneck squash, sliced, Or 5 medium zucchini or, Can tomato sauce (8 oz), Garlic, Ears of corn, kernels cut, Hot italian sausage, Off the cob, Cheddar cheese, grated, Chiles, charred, seeded, and, Chopped, Flat red onion, chopped, Jalapeno chiles, seeded and, Ripe tomatoes, cored and, Anaheim or New Mexican green, Minced, Chopped, Black pepper, Monterey jack cheese, grated, 
Slice the sausage and fry over medium heat until browned.  Drain off  all but 2 T of the fat.  Saute the onion and garlic for 3 minutes.  Add the chiles, corn, tomatoes, sauce and zucchini.  Simmer for 45  minutes to 1 hour over medium heat with an occasional stir.  Sprinkle  the cheese over the top and simmer until melted.    You can prepare the stew in the morning and reheat it later with the  cheese for a covered dish supper, potluck or barbecue.  Serve with  warm flour tortillas.
