West Bend Pumpkin Nut Bread [Abm]
Brown sugar, Butter or marg, Salt, Pumpkin pie spice, Canned pumpkin, All purpose flour, Whole wheat flour, Plus 2tbsp Evaporated milk, Ground nuts; your choice, Active dry yeast, 
Add milk and pumpkin to the pan. Add flours, nuts, sugar, spice and salt to  the pan. Level dry ingredients. Place a 1/2 tb butter in each corner. Make  a well and add the yeast. Program for basic.    From the West Bend ABM manual. Posted by Jim Weller.  Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest V1 #453 by Jim Weller <jweller@ssimicro.com>  on Dec 14, 1997
