30-Min Herbed Pork Cutlets
Fresh thyme, Fresh oregano, Fast-fry pork cutlets, Fresh basil, Dry bread crumbs, Salt, Egg, Vegetable oil, Parmesan, Pepper, 
In shallow dish, lightly beat egg. In separate shallow dish, stir together  bread crumbs, basil, oregano, Parmesan, thyme, pepper and salt. Dip pork  into egg to coat well; press into bread crumb mixture, turning to coat all  over.    In large skillet, heat half of the oil. over medium heat; cook pork, in  batches and adding remaining oil if necessary, turning once, for 8-10  minutes or until just a hint of pink remains inside.
