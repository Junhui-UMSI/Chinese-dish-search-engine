Tangy Green Beans
Cans), Onion -- grated, Vinegar, Tarragon Vinegar, Parsley -- chopped, Salt, Paprika, String beans -- (15 1/2 oz, Butter, Bacon strips -- halved, Sauce:, 
Drain beans.  Wrap beans in bacon, about 6-7 beans in a piece of bacon.  Secure with toothpick.  Broil until bacon is done. Combine ingredients for  sauce and bring to a boil. Pour hot sauce over beans and serve.    Recipe By     : Telephone Pioneers - MS chapter
