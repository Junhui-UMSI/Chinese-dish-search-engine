Thai Pork Salad
Lime juice, Red onion strips, Red onion; cut into thin, Lightly packed cilantro, Salt, Grilled pork loin chops, Lightly salted roasted, Cayenne pepper, Cilantro sprigs, Vegetable oil, Lime wedges, Packaged coleslaw mix, Mint leaves; coarsely, 
Combine cabbage, cilantro and mint in large bowl; set aside. Cut pork chops  into 1/4 inch thick strips. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high  heat. Add pork, onion, nuts, salt, and cayenne pepper. Cook and stir 2  minutes; remove from heat. Stir in lime juice and sugar. Spoon pork mixture  over cabbage; toss well to coat. Garnish with lime wedges, onion strips and  cilantro. Makes 5 main dish servings.    Recipe by: Unknown Cooking Magazine Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #752 by  L979@aol.com on Aug 21, 1997
