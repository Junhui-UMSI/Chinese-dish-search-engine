Pistachio Bread
Instant pistachio pudding,, Box yellow cake mix, Eggs, Ground nutmeg, Ground cinnamon, Sour cream, Plus 2 tablespoons sugar,, Vegetable oil, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine cake and pudding mixes, 1/4 cup sugar,  oil, eggs and sour cream in bowl. Pour into two 8 1/2x4 1/2-inch greased  loaf pans. Combine nutmeg, cinnamon and remaining 2 tablespoons sugar in  small bowl and sprinkle over batter in pans. Bake in preheated oven 45-60  minutes or until breads test done. Makes 2 breads, 10 to 12 slices per  bread.    NOTES : Journal    Recipe by: Laurie Y Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #612 by  Aquasea221@aol.com on May 14, 1997
