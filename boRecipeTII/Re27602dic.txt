In a 9x13-inch baking pan, bake chicken at 350-degrees for 40 minutes.  (This seals in the juices ab takes the place of browning the chicken in  oil, reducing the amount of oil needed. It also will defat the chicken).  Meanwhile, in a Dutch oven casserole, cook together the next 8 ingredients  for 40 minutes. Add the chicken to the casserole, discarding the skin,  bones and fat in the pan. Continue cooking for 15 minutes, or until chicken  is tender. Add the potatoes and heat through. Sprinkle with parsley or  chives, if desired. Serves 6 as dinner and 12 as an accompaniment to  dinner.
