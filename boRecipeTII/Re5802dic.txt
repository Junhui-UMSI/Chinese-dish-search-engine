Pinto Bean Fudge
Chocolate chips, pinto beans, Sugar, Nuts, Canned milk, Mini marshmallows, Vanilla, 
Combine sugar and milk in kettle.  Boil 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Add  remaining ingredients and stir until marshmallows melt. Pour into buttered  pan; cool and cut into squares.    FROM:    CYNTHIA ALLDREDGE   (DWWJ72A)
