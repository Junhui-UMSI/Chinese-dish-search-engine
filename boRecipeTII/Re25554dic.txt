Avocado Dressing
Avocados, Medium, Ripe, Salt, Tomato, Peeled, Chopped, Garlic Clove, Crushed, Green Chiles, Diced, Onion, Chopped Fine, Lemon Or Lime Juice, 
Cut avocados in half. Remove seeds and skin. Put avocados, lemon/lime  juice, onion, garlic, green chiles and salt in blender and blend until well  mixed. Stir in tomato. Chill in refrigerator until ready to serve. This  dressing will keep its color because of the citrus juice. Serve as a  dressing for cooked or raw vegetables, such as cauliflower, carrots,  tomatoes, green beans or on any salad. From: Syds Cookbook.
