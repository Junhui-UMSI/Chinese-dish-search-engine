Baked Salmon Steaks
Salmon steaks, Butter, Lemon juice, Parsley, Onion, salt and pepper, Paprika, Assorted dried herbs, 
Melt butter -- add lemon juice and seasonings. Place fish in shallow  casserole, and pour lemon butter mixture over top. Bake at 350 for 25-30  minutes or until the fish flakes easily with a fork. Do not over cook.    Recipe by: Carol Mann    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by wsmann@juno.com (William S Mann) on Feb 14,  1998
