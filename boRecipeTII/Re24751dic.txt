Aunt Celestes Butter Cookies
Cream of tartar, Flour, Baking soda, Sugar, Butter, Vanilla, Egg, 
Cream butter and sugar. Beat egg and add to mixture. Set aside 10 minutes.  Add baking soda. Sift flour and cream of tartar together and add to  mixture. Drop by spoonful onto greased cookie sheet. Dip the bottom of a  glass in sugar and press down on cookies to flatten. Bake at 350 until  edges turn brown (8-10 minutes).
