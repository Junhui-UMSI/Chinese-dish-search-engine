Baked Chicken with Biscuits and Gravy
Cream of mushroom soup;, Mushroom stems and pieces  , Parsley; flakes, onion soup mix, Broiler-fryer chicken, milk, Water, Buttermilk baking mix, Paprika, 
Heat oven to 400 degrees F.    Place chicken skin sides up in ungreased baking pan, 13x9x2 inches.    Reserve 2 tablespoons onion soup mix (dry). Mix remaining soup mix, the  mushroom soup and water; pour over chicken.    Cover and bake 1 hour. Spoon mushrooms over chicken. Mix baking mix, milk,  reserved onion soup mix and the parsley flakes until soft dough forms; beat  vigorously 30 seconds.    Drop dough by spoonfuls onto chicken and gravy; sprinkle with paprika. Bake  uncovered until biscuits are light brown, about 12 minutes.
