Tomato Relish
Onions, Ripe tomatoes, Red peppers, Green peppers, Salt, Sugar, Green tomatoes, Vinegar, 
Chop green tomatoes.  Add salt.  Allow to stand overnight. Drain. Chop ripe  tomatoes, onions, and peppers. Add to green tomatoes. Add vinegar and  sugar.  Heat to boiling.  Simmer 1/2 hour, stirring frequently. Florence  Taft Eaton, Concord, MA.
