Beans n Rice Cha Cha Cha
Reserve bean stock, BASIC BEANS, Lemon juice, Dried beans (black, navy,, INGREDIENTS, Sliced onion, Chili powder, Mushrooms, Cooked basic black beans, Peeled, sliced celery, Garlic, (see following recipe), salt and pepper, Sliced carrots, Chopped cilantro, Canned diced green chiles, Cooked brown rice, Pinto, haricot, etc), olive oil, Onion, roughly sliced, Salt or to taste, 
1.  In large deep saucepan heat olive oil, and saute       garlic, onion, celery, carrots and chili powder,       until onion is translucent.     2.  Add chiles and mushrooms and saute 5 minues more.     3.  Stir in beans, bean stock and cilantro.  Season to       taste.     4.  Cover and simmer over low heat about 10 minutes,       stirring occasionally.     5.  Serve over rice.     DIRECTIONS FOR BASIC BEANS:     1.  Prepare beans for cooking as described in How       Cook Beans. (see next post)     2.  Cook covered, with onion and lemon juice, until       tender. Do not stir during cooking.     3.  Add salt to taste     4.  Set aside until next day.  They should be somewhat       soupy.
