Black Bean Enchiladas
Mole sauce; (1 jar), Water, Corn tortilias, Ricotta, Black beans; prepared, Cheddar cheese; grated, Tomatillo; cleaned and, 
1. In a sauce pan bring mole sauce and water to a boil. Simmer until  reduced by 1/4.    2. In a heavy skillet, heat tortillas one at a time on both sides. Using  tongs, dip tortillas into sauce.    3. Lay tortilla on counter and fill with 1/4 cup beans, two teaspoons  ricotta and one slice of tomatillo.    4. Roll tortillas up and place into baking dish. (Tortillas may be folded  if you prefer.)    5. Evenly pour remaining sauce over enchiladas. Top with Cheddar cheese and  bake at 350? for 30 minutes.
