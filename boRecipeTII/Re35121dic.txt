Beautiful Bagel Sandwiches
HORSERADISH SAUCE, Kiwifruit; peeled, thinly, Sour Cream, Alfalfa sprouts, Freshly grated horseradish, Bagels; sliced in half, Monterey Jack; sliced, Smoked salmon; thinly sliced, Mayonnaise, 
Prepare Horseradish Sauce by thoroughly mixing all three ingredients.  Spread layer of sauce on each bagel half. Sprinkle sprouts on each half,  then layer with cheese & salmon. Top each half with kiwifruit slices,  arranged in an overlapping ring. If warmed bagels are desired, heat before  adding kiwifruit. Makes 4 to 8 servings.    From the <Hotter Than Hell!>, by Jane Butel, ISBN 0-89586-646-3  (0-89586-542-4 paperback).  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
