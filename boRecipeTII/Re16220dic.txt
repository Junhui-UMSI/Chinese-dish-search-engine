Water Chestnut Hors Doeuvres
Brown sugar, Mustard, Worcestershire Sauce, Bacon, Whole Water Chestnuts, Butter, Soy sauce, 
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Wrap each water chestnut in 1/2 strip of  bacon. Secure each with a toothpick. Bake until the bacon is done (about 40  minutes). Remove to a chafing dish. Combine the remaining ingredients in a  small saucepan over low heat. Pour over the chestnuts. Keep warm.
