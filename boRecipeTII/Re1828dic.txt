Chicken Pizzaiola
Baking potatoes, quartered, Peas, drained, Onion, sliced, Mushrooms, drained, Can plum tomatoes, Salt/pepper, olive oil, Parmesan cheese, Oregano, Chicken parts, 
Arrange chicken, potatoes, peas, onion, and mushrooms in a roasting pan.  Pour tomatoes over top. Add salt, pepper, olive oil, cheese, and oregano.  Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes or  until chicken is fork tender.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by Paula <demoness@bellatlantic.net> on Sep  16, 1998
