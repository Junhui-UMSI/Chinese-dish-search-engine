Basic Mopping Sauce
Unsalted butter (try marg.), Ketchup, Strong coffee, Sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, Fresh ground pepper, Salt, 
source: Kansas City Barbeque Society Cookbook (1996) Ardie Davis    Yield 2-1/2 cups Combine all ingredients in saucepan. Simmer uncovered  stirring frequently, until the sauce thickens about 30 min.    Note: "Because of is low sugar content the sauce can be mopped onto beef or  poultry without fear of burning."
