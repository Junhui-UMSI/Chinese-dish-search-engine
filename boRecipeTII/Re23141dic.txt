Tuna and Caviar Parfait
TABASCO pepper sauce, salt and pepper, olive oil, Fresh ahi tuna; finely diced, Wasabi tobiko and/or osetra, Whipped cream; unsweetened, Finely chopped chives, Chives, GARNISH, 
In a well chilled bowl, mix tuna, Tabasco pepper sauce, chives, olive oil,  and salt and pepper together. In a parfait glass, alternate layers of tuna  mixture, wasabi tobiko and osetra caviar. Top with whipped cream and two  chives.
