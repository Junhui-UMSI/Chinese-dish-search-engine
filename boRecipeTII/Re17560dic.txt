Almond Crusted Sole
Almonds, sliced, Sole fillets, milk, Fresh lime, Paprika, Butter, Pepper, 
Sprinkle sole with pepper & paprika. Dip sole in milk; coat both  sides with almonds. Melt butter in skillet over med high heat.  Arrange fillets on bottom of pan, close together but not overlapping.  Fry about 5-7 minutes, turning once until almonds are golden brown on  both sides & fish flakes easily with fork. Use spatula to transfer to  serving platter. Arrange lime slices on top.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
