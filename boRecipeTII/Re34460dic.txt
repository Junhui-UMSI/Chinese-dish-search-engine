Bean Mexican Soup
Drained/rinsed, Kidney beans --, Tomatoes -- crushed/undraine, Drained/rinsed, 6 oz can, Onions -- chopped, 4 oz can, Turkey -- ground/cooked, 8 oz jar, 5 1/2 oz can  red beans --, Bell peppers -- chopped, Taco sauce, Taco seasoning mix, 
In a skillet, cook turkey, onions, and peppers until vegetables are tender  and meat is no longer pink. Add taco seasoning mix, taco sauce, beans, and  tomatoes. Simmer for 30 minutes.    Recipe By     : Anita A. Matejka    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
