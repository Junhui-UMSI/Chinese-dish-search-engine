Peanut ButterTopping for Waffles, or Pancakes
Peanut butter, water, 
Well,  These are just wonderful with the flavor of maple syrup. Simply  blend 3 or 4 tablespoons of peanut butter with an equal amount of hot tap  water. Stir with a table fork in a cup. When smooth, add it to your  favorite waffle or pancake batter recipe.
