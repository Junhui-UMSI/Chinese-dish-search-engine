Tortellini Toss
CHEESE TORTELLINI, olive oil, salt and pepper, YELLOW SQUASH, ONION, RED BELL PEPPER, GARLIC, MUSHROOMS, Grated Parmesan cheese, ZUCCHINI, Oregano, 
HEAT OLIVE OIL IN A LARGE SKILLET OVER HIGH HEAT. ADD ONION, ZUCCHINI,  SQUASH, AND BELL PEPPER. STIR IN MUSHROOMS, GARLIC, OREGANO, SALT AND  PEPPER. COOK AND STIR 7 MINS. OR UNTIL VEGETABLES ARE CRISP-TENDER. TOSS  WITH TORTELLINI AND PARMESAN CHEESE.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #300 by "Deborah K?hnen"  <DEBKUHNEN@classic.msn.com> on Nov 19, 97
