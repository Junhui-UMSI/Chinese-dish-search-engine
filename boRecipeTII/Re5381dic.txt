Pineapple Bundt Carrot Cake
Flour, Eggs, Baking soda, Baking Powder, Vegetable oil, Vanilla, Sugar, Salt, crushed pineapple, carrots, Cinnamon, Pecans, 
Mix all dry ingredients together. drain pineapple and reserve juice. add  juice to dry mixture. add eggs, oil and vanilla. Beat for 3 minutes. Stir  in carrots, pineapple and nuts. Bake in bundt pan 325 for 1 1/2 hours. cool  10 minutes in pan before removing. Sprinkle with powder sugar.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Robert Ellis" <rpearson@snowcrest.net> on  Feb 3, 1998
