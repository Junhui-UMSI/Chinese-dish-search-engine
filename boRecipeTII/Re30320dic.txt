Banana Chocolate Chip Bread
Semi sweet chocolate pieces, Bread flour, 11/2 lb loaf, milk, bananas, Salt, patti  vdrj67a, Bread machine yeast, Egg, Butter or margarine, 
Add ingredeints in order given by manufacturer, adding mashed bananas with  milk and chocolate with flour. Basic/white bread cycle. Light color  setting.    NOTE: How this bread turns out will depend on your breadmaker. Some  machines will make a smooth chocolate colored bread. Others will leave bits  of chocolate chips, and still others will give a marbled loaf.
