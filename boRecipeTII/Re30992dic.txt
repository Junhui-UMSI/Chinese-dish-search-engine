Banana Nut Bread, Memorable
1% low-fat buttermilk, Baking Powder, Grape-Nuts, Sugar; (1/2 to 1 cup), No-stick cokking spray, Light corn syrup, Flour, Egg Beaters 99% egg, Bananas; mashed, Vanilla, Baking soda, 
Mix all ingredients; bake in a large sprayed loaf pan at 350 degrees F. for  50-60 minutes. Do not over-bake. Test for doneness with toothpick.
