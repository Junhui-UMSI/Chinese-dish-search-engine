Banana Bread #12
All-purpose flour, Ground cinnamon, Baking Powder, Ripe bananas; peeled, mashed, Egg whites, Vanilla extract, Granulated sugar, Applesauce, Baking soda, 
From: Lisa Burnham <lburnham@golden.ncw.net>    Date: Fri, 1 Jul 1994 03:00:45 GMT    Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a 9x5 loaf pan with non-stick cooking  spray.  Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and cinnamon and set  aside. In a larger bowl, whisk together applesauce, sugar, egg whites,  banana and vanilla. Add flour mixture all at once and stir gently to blend.  Pour batter into prepared loaf pan and bake for 50 - 60 minutes or until  knife inserted comes out clean. Cool bread completely before slicing. 162  calories, .291 grams of fat per slice.    REC.FOOD.RECIPES    From rec.food.cooking archives.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
