Peanut Butter Cookies
AM Crunch Peanut Butter, Vanilla, AM Barley Flour, Honey, AM Brown Rice Flour, 
Stir peanut butter, honey and vanilla together.  Mix rice and barley flour  together and stir into mixture.  Roll into small balls.  On an oiled  cookie sheet, flatten the balls out fairly thin.  Bake 7-8 minutes at 350  F.  Let cool on the cookie sheet before removing.    Source: Arrowhead Mills "COOKIES" tri-fold  Reprinted by permission of Arrowhead Mills, Inc.
