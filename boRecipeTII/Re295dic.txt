Leah`s Ultimate Chocolate Brownies
Cocoa powder, Baking soda, Butter, Melted butter, Boiling water, Sugar, Eggs, Flour, Vanilla, Salt, Mini semisweet chocolate, 
Whenever there is a special occasion in school, my children are told and  not "asked" to bring these brownies to school for everyone.I really don`t  remember where the recipe is from, I`ve been making it for so long.    Preheat oven to 350. Grease or spray 9 x 13 pan. MIX BY HAND (do not use  mixer!!) Sift together cocoa & baking soda. Add 1/3 cup melted butter. Add  boiling water & mix until it thickens. Stir in sugar, eggs, & 1/3 cup  butter until smooth. Add flour, salt & vanilla & blend completely.DO NOT  BEAT! Stir in chocolate chips. Bake at 350` for 35 minutes. Cool completely  in pan on rack.    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "leah perez" <perezleah@hotmail.com> on Aug  02, 1998,
