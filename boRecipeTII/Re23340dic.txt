Tuna Twists
Butter, Tuna Chunks in Oil,, Garlic Clove, peeled and, Salt and pepper, Snipped Fresh Chives, Tricolour Pasta Twists, olive oil, Button Mushrooms, Wiped, 
Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil and add a tablespoon of olive  oil to prevent pasta from sticking. Add pasta twists, bring back to the  boil and simmer for about 10-12 min or al dente. Drain.    Meanwhile, heat the remaining oil and butter in a saucepan and add the  garlic and mushrooms. Cook for 2-3 minutes, add tuna and heat thoroughly,  season to taste.    Mix the pasta and the sauce together. Serve garnished with the chives    Source: Take-A-Break
