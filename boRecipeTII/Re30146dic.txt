Banana and Blueberry Bread (Corrected)
Equal; (sugar substitute), Cup brown sugar, Self raising; wholemeal, Ripe bananas, Vanilla, Cinnamon, (425g) of blueberries;, Raisins; (optional), 
Mash bananas, sugars and vanilla together. Add remaining ingredients  (blueberries last). Pour into sprayed loaf tin and bake for at least one  hour at 160 degrees C or 320 degrees F. Or use muffin tins and bake for  less time. Remove from pan and let cool before cutting.  Posted to fatfree digest by cctlg@cc.newcastle.edu.au (Terri Nowak) on Apr  23, 1998
