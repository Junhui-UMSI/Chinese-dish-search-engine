7-Up Cake
7UP, Butter, Lemon extract, Sugar, Flour, Eggs, 
Cream butter and sugar until smooth. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well  after each addition. Add flour, lemon extract, and 7UP; mix well. Pour into  greased angel food (or bundt) pan. Bake 325F for 1 hour and 15 minutes or  until done. Sprinkle with confectioners sugar.
