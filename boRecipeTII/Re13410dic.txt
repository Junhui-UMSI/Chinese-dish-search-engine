Yeast Biscuits #1
Sugar, shortening, Yeast, Warm water, Flour, Salt, 
Dissolve yeast in warm water; mix with dry ingredients. Add shortening and  mix. Roll out 1/4 inch thick; cut; dip in melted butter. Let rise for 1 1/2  hours and bake at 400 about 15 minutes.
