Apple Cream Leather
Cinnamon, Cloves, Nutmeg, Nonfat yogurt; (plain), Apples, 
Puree ingredients in food processor or blender until smooth. Place fruit  leather sheets in dehydrator trays. Spread puree over fruit leather sheets,  1/4" thick. Dry until leathery (pliable but not sticky).    Posted on GEnie Food & Wine RT Nov 08, 1993 by FW.PATTYE  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #740 by hurlbert <hurlbert@concentric.net> on  Aug 13, 1997
