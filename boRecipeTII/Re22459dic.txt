Tuscan Roast Pork Tenderloin
Salt, Black Pepper, Lemon slices; for garnish, Pork tenderloin; (10 to 12, Garlic, Fennel seeds; crushed, olive oil, 
Recipe By: LHJ ONLINE http://www.lhj.com    Heat oven to 475F. Mash garlic, fennel seeds, salt and pepper to a paste;  rub over tenderloin. Place in roasting pan; drizzle with oil. Roast 25  minutes, until meat thermometer inserted in center reaches 155F. Slice  thin diagonally. Garnish with lemon slices.    Youll love the subtle sweet flavor of this fennel-roasted pork.    Prep time: 5 minutes Cooking time: 25 minutes Degree of difficulty: Easy  Low-calorie    Makes 2 servings.
