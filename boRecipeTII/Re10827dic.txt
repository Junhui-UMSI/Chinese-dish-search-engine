Quinoa and Bean Soup
Eden Tamari, Eden Quinoa, Water, Paprika, Black pepper, Garlic, Eden Kidney Beans, canned, Eden Sauerkraut, canned, Eden Toasted Sesame Oil, Eden Shiitake Mushrooms, Onion, 
Soak Shiitake Mushrooms in 1 cup of water for 1 hour.  Drain and save  soaking water.  Discard stems and slice mushroom tops.  Puree beans in  blender or food processor and set aside.  Heat oil and saute garlic and  onions until onions are translucent.  Add 3 cups water, Shiitake Mushrooms  with soaking liquid, Sauerkraut and Quinoa.  Cook 30 minutes over low  heat.  Add paprika, black pepper to taste, tamari and pureed beans, then  simmer for 5-10 minutes.
