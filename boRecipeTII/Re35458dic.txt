Beef Jerkey
Soy sauce, Garlic powder, Pepper, Monosodium glutamate, Liquid smoke, Worcestershire Sauce, Seasoned salt, Onion powder, Steak flank, 
Trim off fat. Slice meat very thin with grain. Place meat in shallow pan.  Combine ingredients and pour over meat. Marinate 24 hours. Preheat oven at  125 degrees. Lay strips on oven racks in single layers. Place foil on  bottom to catch drip- pings. Leave oven door slightly open. Test  occasionally. Bake 8 to 12 hours until chewy.
