Basil Carrots
Margarine; melted, Salt;, Basil leaves; crushed, Carrots;, Butter, 
Slice carrots.  Simmer, covered, in salted water until tender, about  10 to 15 minutes; drain. Combine remain ingredients, toss with  carrots.
