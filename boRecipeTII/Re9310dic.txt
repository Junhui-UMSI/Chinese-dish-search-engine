Puff Pancake with Peaches and Sausage
Butter, Maple syrup, Ground nutmeg, Filling:, Flour, Peaches, milk, (16 oz) sliced drained, Beaten eggs, Sugar, Sausage, Salt, 
To make pancake, heat oven to 400. Place butter in 9" pie plate &  heat in oven until melted. Whisk together eggs, milk, flour, sugar &  salt until smooth. Pour into pie plate; do not stir. Bake 13-15  minutes or until golden. Meanwhile, cut sausage links into halves.  Place in large skillet & brown over medium heat, about 6 minutes.  Remove drippings. Add peach slices, syrup & nutmeg. Bring to a boil &  simmer until syrup has thickened & sausage & peaches are glazed.  Spoon filling into pancake & serve immediately.
