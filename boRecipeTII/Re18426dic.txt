Vickis Cream Cheese Balls
;sliced thin, ;8 oz each, ;jar, in small pieces, Armour Dried beef, Black olives, Cream cheese, ;diced, Green olives, ;diced, Accent, Green onions, 
Soften cream cheese.  Mix with cut up meat and onions and accent. Seperate  into thirds. in one third, mix black olives, the green in another third.  Form all three into balls. May roll "plain" third in nuts.    Source: Joan Mershon
