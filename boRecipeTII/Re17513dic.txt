Almond Coated Chicken with Orange
milk, Onions, SAUCE, Orange juice, Salt and pepper, Flour, Poultry seasoning, Eggs, Chicken breasts, Butter, (100g) almonds, Grated orange rind, Orange marmalade, 
Karen Haigh          6    servings    Crispy Chicken in a tasty sauce, easy to make.  http://dinnercoop.cs.cmu.edu/dinnercoop/Recipes/subjects/turkey.html    Dip chicken breasts in flour, egg, then almonds.  Melt a small amount of butter in a large nonstick frypan. Saute  coatedchicken breasts, one at a time, on both sides until golden and cooked  through, adding more butter to pan as needed. Set aside; keep warm.    Melt a little more butter in same frypan. Saute onion until tender. Blend  in 2 tablespoons. flour and poultry seasoning. Gradually stir in milk. Cook  and stir over medium heatuntil mixture boils and thickens. Remove from  heat. Stir in marmalade, orange rind, juice, salt and pepper.    To serve, pour sauce over breasts.
