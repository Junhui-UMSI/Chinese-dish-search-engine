Vegetable Millet Delight
Water, Onion, Coriander, Sesame oil, Red and green pepper;chopped, Millet; cooked, Salt, Mushrooms; chopped, 
Place cooked millet in blender with 16 oz water, coriander, salt, and oil.  Puree until smooth. Pour into saucepan over medium heat. Add chopped  vegetables, and cook for 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.     From DEEANNEs recipe files
