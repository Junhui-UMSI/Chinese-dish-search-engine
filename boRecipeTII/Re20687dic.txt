Apple Crisp 8
Flour, Nutmeg, Brown Sugar, Cinnamon, Brown Sugar, Granulated sugar, Lemon juice, Margarine, Granny Smith apples, Granulated sugar, TOPPING, Flour, cooking oats, 
Place apples in a large casserole dish.  Drizzle lemon juice over.  Combine  remaining ingredients for filling and toss with apples until thoroughly  coated. Combine topping ingredients and sprinkle over apples. Bake in a  preheated 350 degree oven for about 45 minutes, or until center is bubbly.
