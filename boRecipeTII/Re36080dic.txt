Beef Rellenos
Egg, Salt, Onion, minced, olive oil, Flour, Tomato sauce, Chili pepper, Garlic, Chili powder, Ground beef, 
In large skillet saute meat till light brown.  Drain excess fat; add  onion, garlic,chili powder and salt. Saute 3 minutes. Add tomato sauce and  simmer 10 minutes. If using fresh chilies, place under broiler about 8  minutes.  Split chilies down one side and remove skin. Remove seeds.Stuff  inside with meat mixture and roll in flour.Have ready a large skillet  filled with 1/4" oil. Separate eggs.  Beat whites stiff; beat yolks light.  Fold yolks into whites. Lightly dunk peppers in mixture;ease into hot oil  in skillet and cook until golden brown. Remove. Serve with hot sauce on the  side.
