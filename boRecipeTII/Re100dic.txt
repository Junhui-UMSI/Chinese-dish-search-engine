Killer Bread
Mayonnaise, Grated Parmesan, Garlic, Sourdough bread, Butter, Fresh Basil, Dried basil, 
Preheat broiler.    Mix mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese and garlic in a large bowl to blend.  Arrange bread, cut side up on a large cookie sheet.   Butter bread. Broil until crisp and brown.  Spread Parmesan mixture over cut sides of bread.  Broil until top is puffed and golden brown. Sprinkle with basil. Cut into wedges and serve.
