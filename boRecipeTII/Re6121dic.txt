Pizza Hot Dogs
mozzarella cheese, pizza sauce, Hot Dog buns, Hot Dogs, Grated Parmesan cheese, 
Split hot dogs and hot dog buns lengthwise.  Place buns on a cookie sheet.  Sprinkle mozzarela cheese over buns. Place split hot dog on each bun.  Spread pizza sauce over the hotdogs, sprinkle with parmesan cheese and  broil until cheese bubbles and the hot dogs are hot.    Gretchen Barthold
