Tomato Chili Sauce
Peanut oil, Ripe Medium Tomatoes, Minced Jalapeno Pepper, Salt to taste, Scallions, Thinly Sliced, Fresh Coriander, Chopped, Red wine vinegar, 
Blanch tomatoes in a large saucepan of boiling water for about 15 seconds.  Cool in bowl of ice water and slip off skins. Cut tomatoes crosswise in  half and squeeze out seeds; then finely chop with knife or in food  processor. Transfer to mixing bowl and whisk briefly. Whisk remaining  ingredients in small bowl until blended. Add to tomatoes and whisk  vigorously. Whisk again just before serving. Serve with grilled hamburgers,  fish, shrimp or chicken.
