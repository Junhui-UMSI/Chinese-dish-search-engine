Baked Party Ham with Bourbon and Pineapple
Dark brown sugar, Ham, Cloves, Pineapple chunks with juice, Marmalade, Bourbon, 
Preheat oven to 350f. Score ham in diamond pattern and insert cloves in the  crossed points and pat brown sugar over top. Place in shallow baking pan  and cook for 30 minutes. Heat pineapple, juice and marmalade together over  low heat. Remove pan from heat and add bourbon. Baste frequently for 1 1/2  hours.    NOTES : Serve with breads, mustards,etc.    Recipe by: Silver Palates New Basics    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #965 by Dan Taylor <dant69@idt.net> on Dec  18, 1997
