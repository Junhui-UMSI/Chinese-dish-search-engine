Peggy and Bruces Baked Chicken Breast Casserole
Garlic powder, Paprika, Chicken Breasts, Cayenne pepper, Thyme, Carrots, Medium Onions, Rosemary, PEI Potatoes, Parsley, Oregano, olive oil, 
1) Combine all seasonings. Cut vegetables into large chunks.    2) Put chicken breasts in casserole dish. Surround with vegetable chunks.     Brush olive oil generously on all poultry and vegetables.    3) Sprinkle spices on chicken only. Bake covered in a 350 F oven     approximately 1 hour. Uncover and continue to bake until vegetables are     crispy and the potatoes/chicken are browned.    For a spicy variation, dash some worcestershire sauce and/or tobasco over  the dish before baking.
