Vanilla Walnut Coffee Cake
Vanilla, Brown sugar, Salt, milk, Eggs, Butter, Bread flour, Yeast, Walnuts, Honey, Egg yolk, 
Adjust flour for your machine. 1 1/2 - 2 cups bread flour.    Scald the milk. Stir in the honey and butter. Lett cool to room temp. Add  the milk mixture and all remeaining ingredients except the brown sugar and  walnuts in the order suggested by your machines. At the beeper or end of  the first kneading cycle, add the brown sugar and walnuts. Eat warm or  cool.  Posted to EAT-L Digest  by LDGOSS <LDGOSS@PRODIGY.NET> on Jan 2, 1998
