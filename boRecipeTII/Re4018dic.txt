Peppered Yellow Squash
yellow squash, Red wine vinegar, Salt and coarse grnd black pepper, olive oil, 
Slice squash lengthwise into 1/4 inch slices. Dip squash in oil and  vinegar;  season  with salt and generously with pepper; grill 5 to 7  minutes, covered. Use barbeque basket if available. From Chuck Ozburn.
