Bayrischer Kartoffelsalat (Bavarian Potato Salad)
Onion, Pepper, Vegetable oil, Potatoes; *, Sugar, Chicken Broth; **, Lemon juice, Salt, 
*    Potatoes should be peeled and sliced 1/4-inch thick. ** Chicken broth  may be either home made or commercial. +++ Boil potatoes in broth with 1/4  t salt for 5 to 8 minutes, until tender. Drain. Toss warm potatoes with  vegetable oil and onions. Dissolve remaining 1/4 t salt and the sugar in  lemon juice.  Pour over potatoes. Marinate salad 1 to 2 hours before  serving.  Serve at room temperature.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdjaxxx.zip
