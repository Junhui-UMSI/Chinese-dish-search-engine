Acadian Crab Bake
Tomatoes, bread crumbs, Sour cream, milk, Green onions, crabmeat, cream cheese, Salt to taste, Rice, 
Combine cream cheese, sour cream and milk; whip until smooth. Fold in  green onions, rice and crabmeat. Turn mixture into a buttered 2 quart  casserole. Arrange tomato slices over casserole mixture. Sprinkle with  salt; top with bread crumbs. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 30  minutes.      Serves 6.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
