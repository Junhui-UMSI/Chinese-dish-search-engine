Wine-Glazed Baked Ham
orange rind, Ground allspice, Ground cloves, ham, Brown sugar, Dry Red Wine, 
Carefully remove rind from ham with sharp knife. Score ham fat in diamond  pattern. Place ham in shallow roasting pan. Combine brown sugar, wine,  orange rind and spices in a small saucepan. Bring to boiling; simmer 5 min.  Heat oven to 325*. Drizzle half the wine syrup over ham. Bake, basting  frequently with remaining syrup, until meat thermometer registers 130* F,  allowing about 14-17 min per pound. Remove ham from oven. Transfer ham to  heated platter. Garnish with cranberry relish in orange cups, if desired.    Posted to EAT-L Digest  by Shawn Zehnder Lea <slea@HIGHTOWERSERVICES.COM>  on Dec 11, 1997
