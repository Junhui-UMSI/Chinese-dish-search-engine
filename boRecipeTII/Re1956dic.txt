Chocolate Ice Cream
Eggs, Sugar, Half-and-half, Cream, whipping, chocolate syrup, Vanilla extract, Milk, 
Beat eggs with electric mixer at medium speed until frothy. Gradually add  sugar, beating until thick.  Add next 4 ingredients; mix well. Pour mixture  into freezer can of a 1-gallon hand-turned or electric freezer.    Add enough milk to fill can about 3/4 full.  Freeze according to  manufacturers instructions.  Let ripen at least 1 hour.    SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, August 1980. Typos by Nancy Coleman.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by "Rfm" <Robert-Miles@usa.net> on Aug 29, 98
