Western Fish Stew
Thyme, Lemon Crystals, Stick Cinnamon -- 1" pieces, Tomato Crystals, Water, ** Package Separately **, ** Package Together **, Garlic powder, Fish Fillet, Dried Onions, Chicken Bouillon Cube, Salt -- to taste, Beef Bouillon Cubes, 
1. Fillet the fish and cut into chunks. 2. Add everything except tomato and  fish to 4 or 5 cups of water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer  gently for 10 to 15 minutes. 3. Blend the tomato crystals with enough cool  water to form a smooth paste. Add slowly to the simmering broth. 4. Add  fish and cook until it flakes easily.    Serves 3 or 4.
