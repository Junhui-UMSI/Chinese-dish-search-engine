Baked Stuffed Flounder
Butter, Green Onions, garlic, Egg, Chopped parsley, Salt, pepper, red pepper, Progresso bread crumbs, Celery, Crabmeat, shrimp, 
Saute celery, onions and garlic in 1/2 stick melted butter. Add bread  crumbs, shrimp, crabmeat and parsley. Mix well. Season to taste with salt,  pepper and cayenne. Split thick side of each flounder, lentthwise and  crosswise, and loosen meat from bone of fish to form a pocket for stuffing.  Brush well with additional melted buttter, salt and pepper. Stuff pocket  with dressing.  Melt remaining 1/2 stick butter in shallow baking pan.  Place fish in pan.  Cover and bake at 375 F. for abt. 30 minutes. Remove  cover and bake additional 5 minutes.
