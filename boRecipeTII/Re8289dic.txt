Potato Paprikash (Hungarian Potato Paprikash)
Potatoes, Green peppers, water, Caraway seeds, Onions, Tomatoes, Cooking oil, Paprika, garlic, 
1  In a heavy-bottomed casserole, heat the oil and fry the onions and  garlic. 2 Add the caraway seeds, paprika, and water. 3 Add the potatoes,  peppers, and tomatoes. 4 Simmer for thirty minutes.    Difficulty: easy.  Time: 15 minutes preparation, 30 minutes cooking.  Precision: no need to measure.    Contributed by: Evelyn C.
