Amber Cucumber Relish
Mustard seed, Distilled white vinegar, Dry mustard, Cinnamon, White onions, Ground cloves, Cucumbers, Salt, Water to cover, Tumeric, 
Peel & chop cucumbers & onions. Add salt & enough water to cover. Let stand  1 hour. Drain off brine. Boil vinegar, sugar & spices together for 20  minutes. Add vegetables. Cook slowly until tender & all the ingredients  have become yellowish. Pack into hot, sterilized jars. Seal. Process in  boiling water bath for 10 minutes. Makes 4-5 pints.
