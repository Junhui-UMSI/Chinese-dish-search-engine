Peasant Style Fish
olive oil, Chopped Tomatoes, Salt and pepper, Balsamic vinegar, 8 ounce halibut fillet,;, Reduced beef stock, Cooked lentils, Chopped shallots, Sliced green olives, Chopped parsley,; for, 
Season fish fillet with salt and pepper. In a medium skillet heat 1  tablespoon oil until rippling. Add fish, skinned-side up and sear for 3  minutes. Add lentils, olives, tomatoes and shallots. Using a spoon gently  stir vegetables. Turn fish and cook for 2 minutes. Add vinegar and cook 30  seconds. Add stock, bring to a boil and reduce liquids by half. When fish  is tender and cooked through, transfer it to a dinner plate. Add remaining  olive oil, stirring quickly to work it into sauce. Taste and adjust  seasonings. Pour sauce over fish and garnish with parsley.
