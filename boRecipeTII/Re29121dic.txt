Baked Rhubarb
Water, Rhubarb; peeled if, Salt, -(up to), Sugar, 
From: Pat Gold <plgold@ix.netcom.com> (COLLECTION)    Date: 16 Jun 1995 12:39:57 +0100    Arrange the rhubarb in a 1-1/2 -quart casserole with a cover. Add the  water, sugar to taste, and salt. Cover tightly and bake at 350 degrees 20  to 25 minutes or until tender. Chill and serve. Cooked rhubarb is good  served with heavy cream or sour cream, witih fresh strawberries if you  like.
