Prophets Porridge, Quick Cooking
Cold water, Sea salt (optional), Cinnamon or nutmeg, AM Multi Blend Flour, Raisins or dried fruit, Honey, 
Mix all ingredients in a heavy saucepan.  Stir until lumps are gone.  Cook  over medium heat, stirring constantly, until porridge bubbles.  Turn off  heat.  Stir to desired thickness.  Serve plain or top with milk, fruit  butter or favorite topping.  Makes 3 large servings.
