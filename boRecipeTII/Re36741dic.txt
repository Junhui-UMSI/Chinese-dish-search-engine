Strawberry Ice Cream Sandwich
Fruit-sweetened strawberry, Sliced fresh strawberries, Reduced fat artificially, Fat-free whipped topping, 
Place one half of the ice cream sandwich on a serving plate. Spread 2  tablespoons of the strawberry jam on top. Place the other sandwich half on  top of the jam. Cover the top and sides evenly with the whipped topping.    Garnish with the sliced strawberries and serve immediately.
