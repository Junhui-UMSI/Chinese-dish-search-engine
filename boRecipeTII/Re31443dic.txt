Barbecue Ribs
Dark Rum, Brown Sugar, Firmly Packed, Catsup, Dry mustard, Spareribs Cut To Serve, Cloves Garlic, Crushed, Worcestershire Sauce, Soy sauce, Chili Sauce, Dash Pepper, 
Wrap ribs in double thickness of foil and bake for 1 1/2 hours at 350  degrees F.  Unwrap and drain drippings.  Combine all ingredients and pour  over ribs.  Marinate at room temperature for 1 hour.  Bake at 350 degrees  30 minutes, basting wiht sauce, or grill 30 minutes, 4 inches above coals  turning and basting.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/meats.zip
