Poppyseed Filling for Hanantaschen
Poppyseed, Nuts & raisins (opt.), Egg, Honey, Sugar, 
Wash poppyseed thoroughly in boiling water and drain. Spread thinly on  clean cloth and allow to dry overnight. Grind with finest blade of chopper  and mix together with remaining ingredients. Place tablespoonfull in center  of Hamantaschen.  If using prepared poppyseed filling, add 2 tbs. honey to  mixture.
