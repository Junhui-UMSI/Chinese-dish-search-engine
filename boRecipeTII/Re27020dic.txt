Carey's Four Bean Summer Salad
kidney beans  , Yellow wax bean  , garbanzo beans  , frozen French-cut  , Red onion, Green bell pepper, Red bell pepper, olive oil, Red wine vinegar, Italian dressing, Honey, Garlic, Dried basil, 
1. Drain all four Beans and strain then set aside in a large bowl. 2. Cut your onion and bell peppers add to the bowl. 3. In a seperate mixing bowl; combine oil, vinegar, italian dressing, honey, garlic, basil , whisk well,       combine with other ingredients in large bowl and mix well to coat, let marinade several hours or over night for best flavor.
