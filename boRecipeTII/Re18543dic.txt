Ambrosia
APPLE FRESH, PINEAPPLE SLICED #10, PEACHES SLICE #2 1/2, PEARS #10, COCONUT SWEETNED PRE, ORANGES FRESH, 
1.  DRAIN PEACHES AND PEARS.  RESERVE JUICES.  CUT FRUIT INTO 3/4 INCH  PIECES.    2.  COMBINE PINEAPPLE WITH PEACHES, PEARS AND JUICES FROM ALL FRUIT.    3.  ADD ORANGES.    4.  QUICKLY (TO PREVENT DISCOLORATION) COMBINE APPLES WITH FRUIT MIXTURE;  MIX THOROUGHLY.    5.  COVER, REFRIGERATE UNTIL READY TO SERVE.  :    NOTE:  1.  IN STEP 3, 4 LB 4 OZ FRESH ORANGES A.P. (11 ORANGES) WILL YIELD  3 LB PEELED ORANGES.    2.  IN STEP 4, 4 LB 2 OZ FRESH APPLES A.P. (11 APPLES) WILL YIELD  3 LB 8 OZ CORED APPLES.
