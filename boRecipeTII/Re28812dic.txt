Thai Chicken-Basil Sauce with Linguine (Light)
Carrot -- cut in 1/4" dice, Minced, Dice, Lime juice, Chicken broth, Fish sauce, Zucchini -- cut in 1/4", Grated fresh ginger, Soy sauce, Fresh basil -- chopped, Shallots -- minced, Jalapeno -- seeded and, Sugar, Fresh pasta, linguine, Ground chicken, Cloves garlic -- minced, 
Cook ground chicken, carrot, zucchini, shallots, chile, ginger and garlic  in  nonstick skillet brushed lightly with oil over medium heat until chicken  loses  its pink color, about 5 minutes. Drain off any liquid. Add stock, soy, fish  sauce, lime juice and sugar. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer  for 5 minutes. Stir in basil and cook until wilted. Keep warm. Cook pasta,  drain well and return to pot. Add sauce and toss well. Serve with  additional  fresh basil sprinkled over top if desired. Serves 4. Calories: 143, Fat: 5  grams per serving.
