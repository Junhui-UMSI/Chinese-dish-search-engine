Zucchini Relish
Lime juice, Pepper, Fresh Cilantro; Snipped, Salt, Zucchini; Shredded, Sugar, Vegetable Or Olive Oil, 
Mix all ingredients in glass or plastic bowl.  Cover and refrigerate at  least 1 hour. Makes about 1 1/4 cups relish.    Miscellaneous recipes from the collection of Mike Orchekowski.  File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mo-misc.zip
