Wild Rice and Mushroom Casserole
Liquid (mushroom juice plus, Bread crumbs, Flour, Mushrooms (size you desire), Butter, Butter, 
Cook rice as directed on box. While rice is cooking, prepare sauce. Melt  butter, saute mushrooms in butter and add flour. Brown slightly and add  liquid. Stir until smooth. Add to rice mixture as soon as rice is done.  Stir well. Top with bread crumbs and brown at 350 degrees.      Delicious with ham and sweet potatoes.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.
