7-Up Cake #1
Flour, Lemon extract, Butter or margarine, 7-Up, Eggs, Sugar, 
Cream sugar and shortening together; beat until light and fluffy. Add eggs  one at a time; beat well. Add flour; beat in extract and 7 Up. Pour into  well greased and floured bundt or tube pan. Bake at 325 degrees for 1 to    1-1/4    hours.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
