Pesto Popcorn Seasoning Mix
Dried parsley flakes;, Butter flavored sprinkles, Grated Parmesan cheese, Dried basil; crushed, Garlic powder, 
Combine butter-flavor sprinkles, Parmesan cheese, basil, parsley flakes,  and garlic powder in a small bowl. Give as a gift with unpopped popcorn.  (Seasoning mixture will coat about 10 cups of popped popcorn.) Store mix in  the refrigerator.    Recipe by: Holiday Cooking 1996    Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #839 by L979@aol.com on Oct 12, 1997
