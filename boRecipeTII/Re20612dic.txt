HEAT oven to 400 degrees. Grease square baking dish, 9x9x2-inches. Prepare  Streusel Topping; reserve.     MIX baking mix, milk, sugar and egg; beat vigorously 30 seconds. Spread  half of the batter in baking dish.  Arrange apple slices on batter;  sprinkle with half of the topping. Spread remaining batter over apple  slices; sprinkle with remaining topping. Sprinkle with nuts.     BAKE about 25 minutes or until tooth pick inserted in center comes out  clean; cool. Drizzle with Glaze.  8 servings.     Streusel Topping: Mix baking mix, brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg. Cut in  margarine with fork or pastry blender until mixture is crumbly.
