Yaasa (West Africa, Senegal)
Mustard; Heaping, Garlic, Plenty Of Cooked Rice, Whole Chicken; Cut In Pieces, Onions; Diced, salt and pepper, Onions; Sliced Thin, Chicken Bouillon Cube;, Lemons; Juiced,
