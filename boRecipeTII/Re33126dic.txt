Basic Granita Formula
Sugar, lemon juice, fruit juice, Water, 
In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar and water and bring to a boil,  stirring until the sugar dissolves. Let cool. Stir in the fruit juice and  lemon juice and pour into a chilled 13 by 9 inch metal baking dish. Place  in the freezer and freeze, stirring every 30 minutes until the mixture is  firm.
