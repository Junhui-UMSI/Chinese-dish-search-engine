Flavorageous Crispy Treats
Butter or margarine, Mini marshmallows, Kool-Aid, Crispy rice cereal, 
Grease a 15x10x1" pan. Microwave butter in large microwaveable bowl on high  45 sec. or till melted. Add marshmallows; toss to coat with butter.  Microwave on high 2 1/2 mins or till smooth when stirred, stirring every  minute. Stir in drink mix. Immediately add cereal, mix lightly until well  coated. Using greased spatula or wax paper, press into prepared pan. Cool.  Cut into desired shapes with cookie cutters or cut into squares.    TOP OF STOVE: Melt butter in 4 qt saucepan on low heat. Add marshmallows;  stir until marshmallows are melted and mixture is smooth. Remvoe from heat.  Continue as directed above.    NOTES : Courtney made this for her 5th grade class for Valentines Day.  Easy to make, very strong fruity taste because of the Kool-Aid.    Recipe by: advertisement    Posted to KitMailbox Digest  by Pat Hanneman <kitpath@earthlink.net> on Sep  01, 1998,
