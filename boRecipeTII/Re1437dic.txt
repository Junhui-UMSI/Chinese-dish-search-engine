Fagiolini Sottolio (Green Beans in Olive Oil)
Fresh green beans, White vinegar, Water, Dalt, Cloves garlic coarsly cut up, Whole black peppercorns, olive oil, 
Source The classic cuisine of the Italian jews by Edda Servi-Machlin volume  II    Trim grenn beans, wash and place in an non metalic saucepan with vinegar,  water and salt.    Bring to a boil, and cook uncovered for 5 minutes. Drain and cool  throughly. Add garlic and peppercorn and toss to combine. Place in a  plastic container pour olive oil over to cover. Store in the refrigerator  for a few days to 2 weeks    I cooked the beans longer, but it is a matter of taste.
