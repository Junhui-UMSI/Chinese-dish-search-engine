Zucchini Fries
Zucchini -- cut like french, Soy sauce, Vegetable oil, Frie, Onion salt, 
Heat oil and add zucchini, tossing gently until slightly tender. =  Sprinkle=20 with soy sauce and season with onion salt. Serve hot.
