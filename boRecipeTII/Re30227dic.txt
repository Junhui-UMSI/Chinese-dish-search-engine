Banana Bread #1
Sugar, Sour milk, Flour, Baking soda, Shortening, Pecans, Eggs, Ripe bananas;, crushed well, 
Cream shortening, add sugar. Cream again, then add crushed bananas to  creamed shortening and cream again. Beat all ingredients together.
