Persimmon Muffins
Persimmon pulp, Cinnamon, Walnuts, Nutmeg, Vegetable oil, Lemon extract, Flour, Raisins, Sugar, Baking soda, Salt, Baking Powder, 
Combine the first 9 ingredients. In a blender, blend pulp, oil and lemon  until just smooth and add to the flour mixture. Stir only until well mixed.  Pour into greased muffin pans, and bake at 350F for 25-30 minutes. Makes 12  large muffins. Lemon glaze may be added when cool.
