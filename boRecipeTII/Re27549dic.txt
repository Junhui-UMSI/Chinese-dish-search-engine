That Good Salad
Pepper, (1 pound each) romaine; torn, Swiss cheese; shredded, Fresh Lemon Juice, Tomatoes; chopped, Strips bacon; cooked and, Slivered almonds; toasted,, Parmesan cheese, Caesar salad croutons, Garlic, Vegetable oil, Salt, 
In a jar with tight-fitting lid, combine oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt and  pepper; cover and shake well. Chill. In a bowl, toss romaine, tomatoes,  Swiss cheese, almonds if desired , Parmesan cheese and bacon. Shake  dressing; pour over salad and toss. Add croutons and serve immediately.  Yield: 14 servings.    NOTES : "When a friend shared this recipe, it had a fancy French name.  Our  children can never remember it, so they say, Mom, please make that "that  good salad".  Now our friends and neighbors request it for potluck  dinners." Submitted by Betty Lamb, Orem, Utah.
