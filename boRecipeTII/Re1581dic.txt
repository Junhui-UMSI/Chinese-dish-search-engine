Butterscotch Pudding 1
Butter, Light brown sugar, Whipping cream, Milk, Egg, Flour, Salt, Vanilla extract, 
In a heavy 2-quart saucepan melt the butter, add the brown sugar, and stir  over low heat until the sugar and butter have melted together and bubble.  Slowly whisk in the cream and 1 c milk. Stir until smooth. Remove from the  heat. In a small bowl, beat together the eggs, flour, and 1 c milk. Add to  the sugar mixture. Add the salt, and return to the stove. Over medium heat,  stir constantly until thickened. While still over medium heat, beat with a  rotary hand mixer at medium speed for 3 minutes. Remove from the heat, stir  in the vanilla, and pour into a 2-quart casserole dish or 8 individual  pudding cups. Chill.
