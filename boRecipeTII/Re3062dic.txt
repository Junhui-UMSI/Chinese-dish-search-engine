Peanut Crispies
Butter or margarine, Baking soda, Rolled oats, Corn flakes, Shortening, Baking Powder, Salt, All-Purpose Flour, Egg; unbeaten, Vanilla, C&H Golden Brown Sugar, Salted peanuts, 
Melt shortening and butter over very low heat.  Remove from heat and stir  in sugar.  Add egg and vanilla; beat well.  Combine flour, soda, baking  powder, and salt; sift into sugar mixture and mix well.  Blend in oats,  corn flakes, and peanuts.  Shape dough into 1" balls.  Place 2" apart on  greased baking sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.    Makes 4 dozen 2" cookies.
