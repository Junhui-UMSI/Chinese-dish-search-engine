Beef and Beans
Prepared mustard, + 1 teaspoon instant minced, Molasses, Chili sauce, Salt, Pork and beans in tomato, Ground beef, 
Cook and stir ground beef in 10" skillet over medium heat on grill or camp  stove until light brown; drain. Stir in remaining ingredients. Heat until  bubbly, about 10 minutes.
