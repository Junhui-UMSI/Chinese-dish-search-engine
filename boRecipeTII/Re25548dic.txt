Avocado Crabmeat Salad
Artichoke hearts, Pickapepper sauce, Jalapeno cheese -- grated, Lemon wedges, Quartered, Crabmeat, Cheddar cheese -- grated, Lettuce with:, Hearts of palm, Basket cherry tomatoes, Mayonnaise, Hard boiled eggs --, 
Combine all ingredients and mix together. Add a little oil from the  artichokes as you mix.
