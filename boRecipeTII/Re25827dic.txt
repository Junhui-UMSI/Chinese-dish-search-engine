Baby Plum Cake
Jar baby food plums;, Nuts; chopped, Self-rising flour, Salad oil, Sugar, Cinnamon, -(up to), Eggs, 
Place dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Add oil, eggs & plums,  stirring well. Add nuts. Mix well, but do not beat. Bake in greased bundt  pan in a non-preheated oven at 325 for 1 hour.
