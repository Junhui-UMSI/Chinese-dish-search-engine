Vegetable Soup #6
Frozen mixed vegetables, Creamed soup of your choice, Top ramen noodles, 
Put some water in a sauce pan (~one cup). Add the noodles and spice  package. boil for three min. Mix the soup with one can full of milk or  water and add to the noodles. add the vegs and simmer until tender.  Its  ready.
