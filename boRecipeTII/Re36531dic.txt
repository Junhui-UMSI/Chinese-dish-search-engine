Strawberries W/champagne Sauce
Vanilla, Egg yolks, Champagne, Sugar, Whipping cream; stiffly, Cornstarch, Strawberries; washed and, milk, 
Combine yolks, sugar and cornstarch. Add milk slowly, stirring constantly.  Cook over medium heat until sauce coats a wooden spoon. Refrigerate until  cool. Add vanilla, then fold in whipping cream. Gently fold in champagne,  then serve over strawberries.    FEMME OSAGE    WEST FRONT STREET, ST. LOUIS.    From the <Micro Cookbook Collection of French Recipes>.
