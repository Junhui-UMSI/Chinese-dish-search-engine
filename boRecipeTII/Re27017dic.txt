Three-Bean Chili
Green Onions, Heinz HomeStyle Brown Gravy, Pinto or kidney beans, Shredded cheese, Garbanzo beans, drained, Chopped green chilies, Yogurt or sour cream, Chili powder, Tomatoes, Spicy Chili beans, 
Combine gravy, tomatoes and chili powder in 3-quart saucepan. Bring to a  boil, then stir in beans and chilies. Cover; simmer 15 minutes, stirring  occasionally.  Serve with desired toppings.
