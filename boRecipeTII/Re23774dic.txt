Arugala and Nectarine Salad
olive oil, Butter lettuce leaves, fresh, nectarines, Raspberry vinegar, Dijon-style mustard, Walnuts, Sugar, Arugula leaves, fresh, 
For the dressing: In a small bowl whisk together the olive oil, raspberry  vinegar, mustard, sugar, salt, and pepper. Set aside.    Place arugula, butter lettuce, and sliced nectarines in a large bowl. Pour  dressing over salad; toss gently to coat. Sprinkle with nuts. Makes 6 to 8  servings.    Cooks Notes: Tangy green arugula and sweet, juicy nectarine slices combine  for luscious contrasts in colors and flavors.    Source: From the article "Last Taste of Summer" by Renee Shepherd in the  Fall 1997 issue of Country Gardens Magazine. Renee says: "Its summers  grand finale, and your kitchen counter is overflowing with fresh-picked  produce. What can you do? Try this (these) new way(s) to savor your  gardens bounty."
