Topsy Turvy Lemon Pie
Cornstarch, 9-inch pie crust; deep dish, Lemon Rind, Eggs, Cheese Layer, Margarine; melted, Egg, Lemon Layer, Lemon juice, Sugar, Light cream cheese, Corn syrup, 
Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add egg and blend well. Spread  evenly in bottom of pie shell. Beat all ingredients for lemon layer  together until smooth. Pour over cheese layer. Bake at 350 for 50-55 mins.  or until set and golden. Cool on rack. Garnish with whipped cream if  desired.    Source Canadian Living Calender for 1998 Formatted for Mastercook by Carol  Floyd--c.floyd@arnprior.com    NOTES : When this pie is baking the cheese layer comes to the top and the  lemon layer goes to the bottom.
