Tropical Punch
Ginger ale, Water, pineapple juice, Sugar, frozen orange juice, frozen lemonade, Orange, 
Mix all ingredients but ginger ale. Refrigerate. Add ginger ale to punch  bowl before serving. Yield: 75 punch cups.    RUBY HAMPTON (MRS. F. BARNES)    DE WITT, AR    From <Traditions: A Taste of the Good Life>, by the Little Rock (AR) Junior  League.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
