Tangy Tomato Barbecue Sauce
Vegetable oil, Salt, Chili powder, Tabasco, Paprika, Worcestershire Sauce, (6-oz) tomato sauce, Garlic, Black pepper, Onion, Dry mustard, Lemon juice, Water, Cayenne, 
Combine all ingredients in saucepan & simmer for 20 minutes.    MRS RALPH A. (DENISE) RICHMOND    From the <The Bliss of Cooking Returns>, Fort Bliss Officers Wives Club,  Ft. Bliss, TX.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
