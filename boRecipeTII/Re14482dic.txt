Whole-Grain Wheat Berries (Vegan)
Whole-grain wheat, Salt, Water, 
In 2qt saucepan, boil water, stir in wheat, and return to boil. Reduce to  simmer, covered, for 2 hours, until almost all of the liquid has been  absorbed. Remove from heat, let stand for 15 minutes. Add salt and fluff  with a fork    Posted by "Rob Ryerson" <RYERSONRA@compctr.ccs.csus.edu> to the Fatfree  Digest [Volume 13 Issue 8] Dec. 8, 1994.
