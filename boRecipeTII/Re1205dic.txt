Crock Pot Cheese Potatoes
Potatoes, salt and pepper, margarine, mild Cheddar cheese, milk, 
Boil potatoes in skins until partially cooked; cool, peel, and grate.  Divide potatoes in 3 equal portions.  In a large round crockpot, put in a  layer of grated potatoes, salt and pepper, 1/2 stick of butter, cut into  pats, 1 cup shredded cheese.  Repeat this procedure two more times, then  pour milk over this. Cover and cook on MEDIUM heat for 2 1/2 hours, the  last 1/2 hour put on HIGH.
