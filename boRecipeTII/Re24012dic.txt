Tossed Snow Peas and Zucchini
Red wine vinegar, 1 Inch Diagonal Pieces, Pine Nuts Toasted, Minced Shallot, Cherry Tomatoes, Quartered, Pepper, Zucchini, Thinly Sliced, Safflower 0R Vegetable Oil, Fresh Snow Peas Cut Into, 
Place Peas in A Vegetable Steamer Over Hot Water. Cover, Steam 2 Min. OR  Until Crisp Tender. Drain & Rinse Under Cold Water. Combine Peas, Zucchini,  Tomatoes & Pine Nuts in A Medium Bowl. Combine Shallot, Vinegar, Oil &  Pepper; Mix Well With A Wire Whisk. Pour Over Vegetables & Toss Gently To  Mix Well. Cover & Chill Thoroughly.   (Fat 6.  Chol. 0.)
