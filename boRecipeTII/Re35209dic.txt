Beef and Cheese Log
finely chopped, Natural sharp Cheddar cheese, Cream cheese, finely shredded, Chopped pressed beef, Pecans; finely chopped, Salad dressing (mayonnaise), 
Mix salad dressing with softened cream cheese. Add Cheddar cheese and  beef.  Mix well.  Shape into a 10-inch log.  Roll in pecans.  Wrap in  waxed paper.  Chill.  Serve sliced with assorted crisp crackers.    Makes 40 1/4-inch slices    Calories per 1/4-inch slice without crackers: About 35    Source: FOOD -- by U.S. Department of Agriculture  Typed for you by Karen Mintzias    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/usdafood.zip
