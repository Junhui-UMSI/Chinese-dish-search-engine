Apple and Cheddar Corn Muffins Ww
Cornmeal, yellow, Flour, all-purpose, Sugar, granulated, Nutmeg, ground, Eggs, Baking powder, double-acting, Corn oil, Corn, whole-kernal, frozed, Cinnamon, ground, Apples, Cheese, Chedder, reduced-fat, 
Preheat oven to 400F. Line 12 2-1/2" muffin-pan cups with paper baking  cups; set aside. In large mixing bowl combine flour, cornmeal and baking  powder. Add apples, corn, cheese, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg, stirring to  combine. In small bowl beat together oil and eggs; stir into flour mixture.  Fill each baking cup with an equal amount of batter. Bake in middle of even  fro 15 minutes, until golded and tootpick comes out dry. Set pan on wire  rack and let cool.
