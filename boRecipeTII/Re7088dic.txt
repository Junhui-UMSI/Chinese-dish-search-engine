Poppy Seed Dressing #4
Paprika, Celery seeds, Dry mustard, Powdered sugar, Wesson oil, Salt, Vinegar plus, Poppy seeds, Vinegar, 
Mix sugar, mustard, salt and vinegar in mixing bowl. Heat 1-1/2 cups Wesson  oil and paprika. Cool to cold then add remaining Wesson oil. Beat as for  mayonnaise. Add poppy and celery seed and mix well. Store in refrigerator.  Use as needed.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
