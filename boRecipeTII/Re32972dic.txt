Sweetn Sour Cashew Chicken
cling peaches, Carrot, fresh ginger, Vegetable oil, Soy sauce, Cashews, Cornstarch, Sugar, Onion, Boned, skinned chicken Breast, 
Reserving 1/3 cup of syrup from peaches, cut slices in half. Blend reserved  syrup and Sweetn Sour sauce (bottled); set aside. Cut chicken into 1-inch  squares.  Combine cornstarch, soy sauce, ginger and sugar; stir in chicken.  Heat 1 Tb vegetable oil in wok or large skillet over high heat. Add chicken  mixture and stir-fry 4 minutes. Remove chicken to warm plate. Add second Tb  oil to wok, and stir in onion and carrot. Stir-fry until vegetables are  crisp/tender and add Sweetn Sour mixture and reserved cooked chicken.  Cook, stirring until chicken and vegetables are coated with sauce. Stir in  peaches and cashews; heat through. Serve immediately over rice.
