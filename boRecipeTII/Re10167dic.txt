Quiche Lorraine Five
Salt, Cayenne pepper, Bacon; (about 1/2 pound),, Shredded Swiss cheese;, Whipping cream; or light, Pastry for 9" pie, Eggs, Onion, Sugar, 
Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Prepare pastry. Sprinkle bacon, cheese and  onion in pastry-lined pie pan. Beat eggs slightly; beat in remaining  ingredients. Pour cream mixture into pie pan. Baket 15 minutes.    Reduce oven temperature to 300 degrees F. and bake 30 minutes longeror  until knife inserted 1 inch from edge comes out clean. Let stand 10 minutes  before cutting. Serve in wedges.    Yields 6 main-dish servings or 8 appetizer servings.    NOTES : Done this a lot of different ways: shrimp, Monterey Jack cheese and  green onions is our favorite. As Quiche Lorraine, Diabetic Exchanges are 2  lean meat, 7-1/2 fat.  Recipe by: Betty Crocker Cookbook, 1969    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #291 by SuenDoug <SuenDoug@aol.com> on Nov  6, 1997
