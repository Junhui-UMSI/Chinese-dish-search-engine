Pineapple Zucchini
Salt, Raisins, Egg whites -- whipped, Baking Powder, Vanilla, 8 oz can, Baking soda, Granulated sugar, Zucchini -- shredded, Cinnamon, Applesauce, Unbleached flour, Pineapple -- crushed/drained, 
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.  In a  mixing bowl, combine egg whites, applesauce, vanilla, zucchini , and  pineapple.  In another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking soda,  salt, baking powder, cinnamon, and raisins.  Mix wet ingredients with dry  ingredients just until moistened.  Pour into prepared baking pan(s).  Bake  for 1 hour depending on pan size.
