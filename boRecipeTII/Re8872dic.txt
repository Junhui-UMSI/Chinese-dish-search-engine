Prawn Baffad
Tomatoes, skinned and, Garlic, Peppercorns, Onions, chopped, Salt to taste, Vinegar to taste, Cummin seeds, Uncooked shelled prawns, Amount of tamarind dissolved, Dried kashmiri chillies, Ground turmeric, /2-inch    piece ginger, 
Grind together the chillies, cummin, peppercorns, garlic and ginger. Mix in  the turmeric.  Fry the onions in a little oil until golden brown. Add the  tomato and prawns.  Fry for a minute.  Add the masala, tamarind and  vinegar. Cover and cook until prawns are done.  From archives of rec.food.recipes    Date: Tue, 31 Jan 1995 08:20:10 GMT    From: trev@wg.icl.co.uk (Trevor Hall)
