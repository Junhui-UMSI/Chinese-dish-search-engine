Bread and Butter Pickles
Mustard seed, Celery seed, Green peppers; chopped, Turmeric, Vinegar, Salt, Cucumbers; medium-size,, Granulated sugar, Medium-sized onions; thinly, 
Gently mix together the cucumbers, onions, green peppers and salt. Cover  with crushed ice and let stand for 3 hours. Continue to add ice as it melts  so cucumbers become crisp and cold. Drain well. Meanwhile, combine sugar,  spices and vinegar in a large kettle and bring to a boil. Add drained  vegetables and heat to boiling point, but do not boil. Spoon into hot  steralized jars and seal. Makes 8 pints. The Best Of Amish Cooking by  Phyllis Pellman    Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by Nancy Berry <nlberry@prodigy.net> on Sep  25, 1998,
