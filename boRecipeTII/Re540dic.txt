Fresh Tomato Bruschetta
BREAD, Loaf italian bread; or, ; (6-oz), olive oil, TOPPING, Plum tomatoes; minced or, Salad tomato, Garlic, Minced basil, Minced thyme, olive oil, 
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Cut the bread into 6 even slices. Brush  each slice with olive oil and place onto a cookie sheet.    To prepare the topping, combine all ingredients.    Place the bread in the oven and toast for 1 to 2 minutes.    Remove the bread from the oven and spread a teaspoon of topping over each  slice. Serve immediately.
