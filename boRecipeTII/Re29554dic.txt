Tartar Sauce (Mf)
Mayonnaise, Packed fresh dill, Kosher dill pickle (4, Dijon or creole style, Chopped capers, Parsley leaves, Salt and tabasco sauce, Chopped fresh chives, 
Whisk the mayonnaise and mustard together and blend in the pickle and  capers. Finely chop the herbs and add them to the mayonnaise and season  with salt and Tabasco to taste.
