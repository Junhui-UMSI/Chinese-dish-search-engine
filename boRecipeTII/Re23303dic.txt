Tuna Souffle
milk, Pepper, Flour, Pinch of thyme, Flaked tuna, drained, Parmesan cheese, Garlic salt, Egg whites, well beaten, Mayonnaise, 
Mix together the first 4 ingredients; slowly add milk; then tuna. Fold in  beaten egg whites, then thyme.  Pour into a well-buttered 2 quart souffle  dish.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Bake at 350F for       45    minutes.  Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V3 #292    Date: Fri, 25 Oct 1996 11:39:42 +0100    From: "femorgan" <femorgan@iol.ie>
