Banana Oatmeal Bread
BANANA; 2 cups, BREAD FLOUR; 2 cups, BUTTER; 1 tablespoon, NONFAT DRY MILK; 1, CINNAMON; 1/2 teaspoon, EGG; 1, WHOLE WHEAT FLOUR; 1 cup, HONEY; 2 tablespoons, NUTMEG; 1/4 teaspoon, ACTIVE DRY YEAST; 2, ROLLED OATS; 1 cup, SOUR CREAM; 1/4 cup, SALT; 1 teaspoon, for Panasonic/National,, (for all machines), Ripe and sliced, 1pound loaf :, for Welbilt/DAK add 1T, 
Crust: Light Menu selection: Bake (light)    1.  Place all ingredients in bread pan, select Light Crust setting,    and press Start. 2. After the baking cycle ends, remove bread from pan,  place on cake rack, and allow to cool 1 hour before slicing.    >From: Ruth C Perry <perryrc@battelle.org>  From: Bread-Bakers Archives: ftp.best.com/pub/reggie/archives/bread/recipe
