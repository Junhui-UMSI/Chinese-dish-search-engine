Apple-Cinnamon Salad
Celery, Cold water, (3-oz) strawberry gelatin, Diced; peeled apples, Cinnamon, Pecans, Boiling water, 
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Add cold water & cinnamon & chill until  thickened. Fold in apples, celery & pecans. Spoon into a 6-cup mold or  bowl. Chill until firm.
