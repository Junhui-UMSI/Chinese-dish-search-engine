Swordfish Acapulco
Swordfish steaks -- cut into, Marinade-----, Mayonnaise, Vegetable oil, Salt and pepper, Hard-boiled egg, Fresh parsley -- chopped, Clove garlic -- crushed, Bite size p, Tomato puree -- paste, Onion -- finely chopped, Dry white wine, Thousand Island Sauce-----, 
Combine marinade ingredients; stir in fish. Cover and leave fish to  marinate in refrigerator for 2-3 hours. To make Thousand Island sauce chop  egg. Put all ingredients into a bowl; season to taste with salt and pepper  and mix together. Spoon into a serving dish. Before cooking the fish in hot  oil drain from marinade and arrange in a serving dish.
