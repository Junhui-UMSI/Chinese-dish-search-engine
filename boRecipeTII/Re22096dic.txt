Turkey Indienne
Water, Turkey; cooked, diced, Toasted almonds, Green Pepper, Vegetable oil, (4-oz) sliced mushrooms, Onion soup, Rice, Flour, 
Saute green pepper in oil. Blend in flour. Add onion soup & water. Cook  slowly until thickened, stirring constantly. Add turkey, mushrooms &  toasted almonds. Simmer 10 minutes & serve over cooked rice. Serves 4.    MRS. GORDON E. MC CARTY    From <Simply Southern>, the Desoto School Mothers Assn, Helena-West  Helena, AR 72390.
