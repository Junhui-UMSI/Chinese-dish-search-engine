Tvp "Saute"
Califlower, TVP, Water or veg. broth, Onions, Tomotoes, Broccoli, Rinsed/drained garbanzo, 
"Saute" onions in water or broth. Add TVP for about 10 minutes, then  gradually add remaining vegetables and cook approximately 5 minutes. This  is great over pasta. Also, liquid smoke adds flavor if you have some.    Posted by Michelle Dick, FATFREE listowner, while list was down.
