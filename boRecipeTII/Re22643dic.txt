Apricot Coffee Cake
Sugar, (15.25-oz) crushed, Eggs, milk, Ground nutmeg, Apricot preserves, Margarine or butter; melted, (8.5-oz) apricot halves;, Bisquick reduced fat baking, 
HEAT oven to 375 degrees. Grease 2 round pans, 9x1-1/2 inches.     MIX all ingredients except preserves thoroughly. Divide batter between  pans.     BAKE 20 to 22 minutes or until golden brown; cool completely. Place one  cake layer, rounded side down, on serving plate; spread with 1/3 cup  preserves. Top with second cake layer, rounded side up; spread with  remaining preserves. 8 servings.    From <Betty Crocker: Bisquick Classics and New Favorites>.  Downloaded from  G Internet, G Internet.
