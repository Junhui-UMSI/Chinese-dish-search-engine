Apricot Coconut Tartlets
Butter, Apricot jam, Flaked coconut, Pack (3 oz) cream cheese, Flour, Salt, Sugar, 
Mix shortening, cheese, sugar & salt. Blend in flour. Form into a  ball & chill at least 3 hours. Heat oven to 400. Roll out dough 1/8"  thick on cloth covered board. Cut with 2" cookie cutter; place on  ungreased sheet. Top each with 1/2 tsp. apricot jam. Bake about 10  minutes. Remove from oven; quickly sprinkle with coconut. Bake 2 more  minutes.
