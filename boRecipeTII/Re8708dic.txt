Poulet Formage
Heavy cream, Salt and pepper to taste, Bleu cheese, Chopped shallots, Grated Swiss cheese, Dijon mustard, Softened butter, Chicken; cut in pieces, Dry vermouth, 
saute chicken in butter, but do not brown! Add shallots and dry vermouth;  reduce liquid slightly. Add heavy cream (enough to almost cover), salt and  pepper. Bring to a boil. Put in oven and cover with oiled wax paper. Cook  approximately 15 minutes. Remove, strain sauce, reduce. Whip Swiss cheese,  bleu cheese, butter and mustard together. Pour over chicken and serve.
