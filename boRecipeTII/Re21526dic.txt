Apple Pie Filling (Freezer )
Filling for 3 pies:, Macintosh apples, Butter, pie shell, Nutmeg, Nutmeg, For 1 pie:, Sugar, Flour, Ginger, Butter, apples, 
Melt butter in heavy pot and add apples and all remaining ingredients.  Partially cook fruit over very low heat for 15 minutes, stirring now and  then with wooden spoon. Coool and dvide filling into 3 freezer containers.  Freeze until needed. To make one pie: Remove filling from one container and  defrost. Spoon pie filling into pie shell. Sprinkle with flour and dot with  butter. Cover with remaining pie dough and crimp edges togehter. Slash top  in several places to allow steam to escape, Bake in a 450 oven for 15  minutes. Lower heat and bake 30 minutes longer or until crust is nicely  browned.
