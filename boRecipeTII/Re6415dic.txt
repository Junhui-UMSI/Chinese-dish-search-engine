Plum Ice
Fresh orange juice, Honey, Lemons; Juiced, Plums, 
1. Put fruit and force pulp with juice through ricer or sieve.    2. Add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.    3. Put tray in lower compartment of freezing unit.    4. With temperature control at coldest point, freeze until partly frozen.    5. Remove to iced owl and beat with chilled rotary beater until light.    6. Return to tray and freeze until firm.    Makes 6 servings.    Recipe by: diane@keyway.net    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 199 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Nov 4, 1997
