Banana Bread 9
Dried dates, chopped, Baking Powder, Brown Sugar, Egg substitute, Salt, Egg beaten -or-, Very ripe medium sized, Skim milk (I like the 1%, All-purpose flour, Plus, Cinnamon, 
I just made this recipe two days ago and thought it was pretty good and a  significant drop in fat! It is taken from The Weight Watchers Complete  Cookbook and Program Basics, page 75, Macmillan-(I made couple of slight  modifications to this if you have the book).    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 8X4" loaf pan with cooking spray. In a  medium bowl, combine dry ingredients. Make a well in the middle of the dry  ingredients and add the egg, banana, and the milk. mix these slightly in  the middle, then blend with flour mixture until just mixed-Do not overmix!  Pour into loaf pan and bake about 1 hour or until toothpick inserted in the  middle comes out clean. Turn out onto rack to cool.    per serving (with the egg and the skim milk) 119 calories, 3 g. protein, 1  g. fat, 26 g. carbohydrate, 193 mg Sodium, 18 mg cholesterol, 1 g. dietary  fiber.
