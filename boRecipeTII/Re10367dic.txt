Quick Cherry Tomatoes
Reduced-calorie margarine, Cherry tomatoes, Fresh basil, 
In a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, saute tomatoes in melted  margarine just until heated through, about 3 minutes. Sprinkle with basil.  Cook and stir until basil is wilted, about 1 minute. Serve hot.    Per serving       22    calories, 0.8 g fat (27%)    Variation ^ Use a combination of basil, parsley, oregano and thyme, or dill  and parsley instead of all basil.
