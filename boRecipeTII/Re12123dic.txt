Zucchini and Bow Tie Pasta
farfalle pasta, Fresh basil, Garlic, romano cheese, Zucchinis, olive oil, 
Boil noodles In a wok or large pan, heat oil and sautee the garlic. Add the  zucchini and basil and cook until desired tenderness is reached. Drain  noodles. Stir in zucchini. Add cheese. Add salt & pepper for taste.    Serves: 4    Posted to recipelu-digest by molony <molony@scsn.net> on Mar 04, 1998
