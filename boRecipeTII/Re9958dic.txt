Pureed Peas
Fresh peas, Sweet butter, 
From "Babys First Helpings" by Chris Casson Madden ISBN: 0-385-19143-X    Formula, breast milk, or cooking water (about 8 teaspoons)    Boil or steam peas for 7 to 12 minutes or until tender. Drain and puree in  your food processor or blender with some of the steam water or formula or  breast milk until very smooth.  Remove from the blender and mash with  butter. Refrigerate or freeze leftovers in ice cube trays.    Makes 2-8 servings    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
