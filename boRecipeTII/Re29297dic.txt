Baked Stuffed Shrimp
milk, Fresh crab meat, Salt, Grated Parmesan cheese, Dry mustard, Onion, Butter, Flour, Mayonnaise, Worcestershire Sauce, Paprika, shrimp, Sherry wine, 
Clean and remove heads and shells from shrimp, leaving tails. Split shrimp  and open flat. Saute onion and pepper in 4 T. of butter until soft but not  brown. Add the crab meat, dry mustand, Worcestershire sauce, salt and  mayonnaise. Set aside. Make white sauce using remaining 2 T. butter, flour  and milk. Add to crab meat mixture along with sherry. Mix well, stuff the  butterflied shrimp with the crab meat and dot with extra butter. Sprinkle  lightly with Parmesan cheese and paprika. Arrange in a shallow baking pan  and bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes. Serves 4-6.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 195 by SallieSays@aol.com on Nov  4, 1997
