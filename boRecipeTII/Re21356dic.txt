Uncooked Tomato Sauce with Olives and Mozzarella
Mozzarella cheese; diced, Tomatoes; medium, Olives; black pitted & slic, Capers; drained, Oregano; dried, Oil; olive, Garlic cloves; large, mince, 
Fat grams    per serving:              Approx. Cook Time: none    Nobake but requires time to marinate. Combine sauce ingredients in  noncorrosive bowl, set aside for 30 minute or more, up to 2 hours.  Cook pasta and drain. Return empty pot to turned off burner, pour in  drained pasta and toss. Cover with lid for 1 minute to let cheese  soften. Uncover and serve.  Good for the height of tomato season on  hot days.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
