Peanut Butter Chocolate Rice Krispie Treats
Peanut butter, Sugar, Corn syrup, Rice Krispies, Semi-sweet chocolate, Butterscotch morsels, 
Bring sugar and syrup to a boil.  Add peanut butter and cook, stirring  until well blended.  Remove from heat and pour hot mixture over cereal. Mix  quickly and thoroughly.  Spread in a greased 9x12x2 inch pan. Sprinkle  butterscotch and chocolate morsels over top. Press morsels into bar mixture  lightly with spoon.  When cool, cut into bars and store at room  temperature.    Source:  Peanut Advisory Board, Tifton, Ga.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/kids.zip
