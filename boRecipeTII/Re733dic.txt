Grilled Lemon-Oregano Chicken Salad
MARINADE, Lemon Peel, Fresh Lemon Juice, olive oil, Chopped fresh oregano, Garlic, Salt, Boneless, skinless chicken, DRESSING, Mayonnaise, Buttermilk, Lemon Peel, Garlic, Salt, SALAD, Torn romaine lettuce leaves, Cherry tomatoes, Fresh Lemon Juice, 
1. Combine lemon peel, lemon juice, oil, oregano, garlic and salt in  resealable plastic storage bag. Add chicken, seal bag, turn to coat and  refrigerate 2 hours.    2. Make Dressing: Whisk together mayonnaise, buttermilk, lemon peel, lemon  juice, garlic and salt in medium bowl. Cover and refrigerate.    3. Meanwhile, heat grill. Grill chicken 5 to 6 minutes per side. Slice  thin.    4. Arrange lettuce, tomatoes and chicken on 4 plates. Drizzle dressing on  top.
