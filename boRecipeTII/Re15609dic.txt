A 20-Minute Chicken Parmesan
Grated Parmesan cheese, Chopped fresh parsley, Boneless and skinless, Spaghetti sauce, Shredded mozzarella cheese, Egg, slightly beaten, Seasoned bread crumbs, Butter or margarine, 
Using palm of hand flatten chicken to even thickness. Dip chicken into egg  then into crumbs to coat. In skillet over medium heat, in hot margarine,  brown chicken on both sides. Add sauce. Reduce heat. Cover; simmer 10  minutes. Sprinkle with cheeses and parsley. Cover; simmer 5 minutes or  until cheese melts.    Makes 4 servings.    From: Steve Herrick Source: [Prego]
