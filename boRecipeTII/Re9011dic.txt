Preventions White Bean Salad with Tuna
Cannellini beans, Sage; rubbed, Black Pepper, Freshly chopped thyme, Red wine vinegar, Anchovy paste, Sage; optional garnish, Baguette slices; optional, Vegetable broth, olive oil, Chopped fresh parsley, Garlic, Shallot; minced, Light tuna in water; low, 
1/> Rinse the canned beans gently under cool water and drain. 2/> Heat oil  in a medium skillet to medium high. Add garlic and saute until golden. Add  broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, reducing liquid  somewhat. Stir in herbs and anchovy paste. Continue simmering until liquid  is reduced to half. Add the rinsed beans and stir. 3/> Remove from heat and  allow to cool. Season with black pepper and stir in vinegar. 4/> Arrange  beans in a circle on a flat platter. Place mound of tuna in center of  circle and garnish with whole sage leaves, if desired.
