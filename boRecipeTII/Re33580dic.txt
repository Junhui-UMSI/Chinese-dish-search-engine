Swedish Fruit in Liqueur
Red currants, Light rum unsweetened, Blueberries, Raspberries, (or more) granulated sugar, Strawberries, Brandy, 
Swedish Fruit in Liqueur ************************    Remove stems and hulls from berries. Rinse, drain, and place in glass  serving bowl. Add sugar, brandy, and rum, stirring with a wooden spoon.  Taste and add more sugar, if needed. Marinate overnight in refrigerator.  Serve with whipped cream and a generous amount of sauce. Serves 6 to 8.
