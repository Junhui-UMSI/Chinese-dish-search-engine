Bean Patties
Whole wheat hamb. buns, Dry bread crumbs, Pinto beans, rinsed, drained, Pepper, Prepared horseradish, Shredded lowfat cheddar, Slices tomato, Chopped gr onions w/tops, Worcestershire, Lettuce leaves, HORSERADISH SAUCE, Egg white or 2T egg product, Plain nonfat yogurt, Salt, 
Spray 10 inch nonstick skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Mash beans in  med bowl.  Mix in cheese, bread crumbs, onions, worcestershire, pepper,  salt and egg white. Shape into 4 patties. Cook in skillet over med heat  about 10 mins, turning once until light brown. Serve on buns with  horseradish sauce, tomato and lettuce. Per serving--280 calories, 14 gr  protein, 5 grams fat, 0 cholesterol, 790 milligrams sodium, 3 grams  saturated fat, 2 grams unsaturated.
