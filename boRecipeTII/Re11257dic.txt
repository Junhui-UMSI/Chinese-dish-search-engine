Raita Burger Topping
Ground red pepper, Unpeeled cucumber, halved, Salt, Lengthwise, seeded & cut in, Chunks, Plain low fat yogurt, Onion, cut in chunks, Garlic clove, cut in half, Sugar, 
Chop onion & garlic in food processor or blender. Add cucumber &  continue processing until chopped fine. Scrape into bowl. Stir in  yogurt, sugar, salt & red pepper. Serve immediately or cover &  refrigerate up to 2 days.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
