Gazpacho Soup
Chopped Tomatoes, Peeled chopped cucumbers;, Green pepper; chopped, you, Cloves chopped garlic, Water, Vegetable oil, Wine vinegar, 
blend all in electric blender on chop. Make one day prior to allow it to  settle. When serving you can cut up small pieces of onion, tomotoes,  peppers, croutons, anything you want to add to the soup. Hope you all  enjoy.
