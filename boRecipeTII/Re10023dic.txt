Quail Gallo
olive oil, European style quail, Dry white wine, Salt, Pitted olives, Pepper, Brown bread crumbs, Garlic, Pinches oregano, Marinated artichoke hearts, 
Drain the oil from the artichokes and save the oil.  Place the wine and  olive oil in a saucepan, add artichokes, bring to a boil.  Remove from the  heat and remove the artichokes to cool, save liquid.  Season cavity of the  quail with salt and pepper.  To stuff, place the olive in the cavity first,  then the cooled artichoke heart, then plug with bread crumbs that have been  slightly moistened with wine.  Rub the outside of the birds with the oil  from the artichoke hearts and sprinkle oregano over each bird. Split garlic  and put in bottom of roasting pan with birds. Add wine and olive oil from  the saucepan. Place in a 450 degree oven for 5 minutes, reduce the heat to  350 degrees and cook until done, about 10 minutes, basting frequently with  oil and wine.
