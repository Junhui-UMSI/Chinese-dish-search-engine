Banana Fudge Pops
Banana; sliced, Orange/banana juice, Cocoa, Sugar, Evaporated skim milk, 
Combine banana & juice in blender or food processor. Blend until smooth.  Add sugar & cocoa & blend well. Add milk & blend. Pour mixture into 6 paper  cups (5 ounces each). Freeze about 1 hour. Insert popsicle sticks into  fudge pops. Cover & freeze until firm. Peel off cups to serve.    136 cal., .8 g fat 4.7%, 29 mg sodium  Recipe By     : Hersheys Fabulous Desserts    Posted to Digest eat-lf.v096.n213    Date: Fri, 08 Nov 1996 18:52:08 -0500    From: charles moffat <moffats@usaor.net>    NOTES : Plain orange juice can be substituted.
