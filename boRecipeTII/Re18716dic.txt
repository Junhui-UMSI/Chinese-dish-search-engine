Amys Diced Potatoes
Worcestershire Sauce, Bottle, Butter, liquid, Potato -- diced, 
Diced potatoes into 1/2 inch cubes. Place potatoes into a foil boat or an  aluminum pan. Sprinkle worcestershire sauce all over the potatoes. Sprinkle  butter over the potatoes. Sprinkle seasoned salt next. Cook til done over a  grill or in a smoker.
