Zip-Lock Bag Ice Cream
Vanilla, Cream, Zip-Lock Bag, Zip-Lock Bag, Sugar, Ice Cubes, Salt, 
Put cream, sugar, and vanilla into small bag and zip up to seal. In large  bag, put 1/4 c salt and half gallon ice cubes. Put the small bag into the  large bag and zip to seal. Shake bag back and forth until ice cream is  frozen. Should take about 3 minutes.
