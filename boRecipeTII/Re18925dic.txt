Angel Food Strawberry Dessert
strawberries, frozen, Water, Cool Whip, Strawberry jello, Angel food cake, 
Tear cake into bite size pieces and spread in 9 x 13 pan. Dissolve jello in  water. Add 2 packages frozen strawberries and stir until dissolved. Pour  over cake. Cover with Cool Whip and chill.
