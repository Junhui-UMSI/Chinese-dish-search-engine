Albondigas Meat Balls
Beef, lean, ground, Cornmeal, Onion; minced, Garlic, Basil leaves, dried; crumbl, Eggs, Pork sausage, Milk, whole, 
Mix together until well blended lean beef, pork sausage, cornmeal, milk,  egg, onion, garlic and basil.    Shape the mixture into about 3/4-inch balls.  If making ahead, return the  meatballs to bowl, cover, and chill up to 4 hours.    Adapted from Sunset Best of the West, Spring/Summer 1993. Downloaded from  Food & Wine Round Table of GEnie.
