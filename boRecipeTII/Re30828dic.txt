Tangy Chicken Salad
Grapes, red, halved, Curry powder, Yogurt, plain, Pineapple, chunks, drained, Chicken or turkey, chopped, Peanuts, dry roasted, Oats, uncooked, Celery, sliced, 
Stir curry into yogurt and add all ingredients except oats and peanuts. Add  those immediately before serving and mix. Makes 4 servings at 275 calories  each.
