Apple Crisp (Filling Prepared)
BUTTER PRINT SURE, PIE FIL APPLE #10, -, 
PAN SIZE 18 BY 26 SHEET PAN                    TEMPERATURE:  375 F. OVEN    1.  ARRANGE ABOUT 4 1/2 QT PREPARED PIE FILLING ON EACH 18 BY 26-IN SHEET  PAN.    2.  COMBINE OATMEAL COOKIE MIX WITH 1 LB SOFTENED BUTTER OR MARGARINE.    3.  SPRINKLE ABOUT 2 1/2 QT MIXTURE EVENLY OVER APPLES IN EACH PAN.    4.  BAKE 40 MINUTES OR UNTIL TOP IS LIGHTLY BROWNED.    5.  CUT 6 BY 9. SERVE WITH SERVING SPOON OR SPATULA.  \    NOTE:  IN STEP 4, IF CONVENCTION OVEN IS USED, BAKE AT 350 DEGREES F.  30  MINUTES OR UNTIL BUBBLING AND LIGHTLY BROWNED ON LOW FAN, OPEN VENT.
