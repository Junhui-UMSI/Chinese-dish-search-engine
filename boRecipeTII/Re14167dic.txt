White Gazpacho
Clove (small) garlic minced, Parsley, Salt, Sour cream, White vinegar, Yogurt, Cucumber peeled and diced, 
Date:    Sun, 16 Jun 1996 15:57:22 -0400    From:    Teresa Matchett <TLMVC@AOL.COM>    Recipe By: The Goldbecks Short-Order Cookbook  Combine cucumber, garlic, parseiy, salt and vinegar in a blender or food  processor and blend until smooth.  Blend in yogurt and sour cream until  evenly mixed. Chill before serving.    Notes: Tastes good with nonfat yogurt and nonfat sour cream    Serving Ideas: Top with croutons.
