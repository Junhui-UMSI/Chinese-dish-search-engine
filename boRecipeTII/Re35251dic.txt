Beef and Potato Casserole
Potatoes, Mushroom soup, Hamburger meat, Onions, Salt, Fat, 
Season meat with salt and brown in hot fat.  Slice potatoes and onions  thinly.  Make alternate layers of potatoes, onions and meat. Pour mushroom  soup over top and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
