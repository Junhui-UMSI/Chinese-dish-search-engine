Basic Meatballs
Egg, Ground beef, Fine bread crumbs, Pepper, Salt, Paprika, 
Mix together; shape into 12 balls. Arrange in circle in 9 x 10 pie plate.  Cover with wax paper. Microwave on high 9 to 12 minutes, rotating dish half  way after 5 minutes.    Variation 1 -- use 1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce and 1/4 cup chopped onion    Variation 2 -- 1 Tbsp. steak sauce and 1/4 Tbsp. garlic powder    Variation 3 -- 1 Tbsp. catsup and 1/4 tsp. prepared mustard    Posted to Bakery-Shoppe Digest by "CD" <poppy@connectcorp.net> on Apr 5,  1998
