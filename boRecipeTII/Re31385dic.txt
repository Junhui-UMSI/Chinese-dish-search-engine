Barbara Bushs Chocolate Chip Cookies
Sugar; brown firmly packed, Water; very hot, Flour & 2 tbsp; sifted, Sugar; granulated, Egg, Baking soda, Salt, Chocolate chips; semisweet, Vanilla, Butter, 
Preheat oven to 375F. Grease baking sheets with vegetable oil. Sift flour,  baking soda and salt on waxed paper. Beat butter, sugar and egg in large  bowl till fluffy, about 3 minutes. Beat in hot water and vanilla. Gradually  beat in flour mixture until blended and smooth. Drop dough by well rounded  teaspoonfuls on prepared baking sheets. Bake in 375F oven till golden,  about 10 minutes. Cook on sheets on wire rack to cool completely. Makes  about 3 dozen cookies  Posted to recipelu-digest by "Bunny" <layla696@ix.netcom.com> on Mar 5,  1998
