Alexis Smiths Corn Pudding
Corn, Green chili, Cheddar, milk, Salt, Egg, 
Prep Time: 10 minutes Bake Time: 1 hour Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes Oven Temp:350F / 180C / Gas mark 4  Preheat oven.  Mix ingredients in the order given. Turn into a greased 2 qt. baking  dish and set in a shallow pan of hot water.   Bake in oven for  about 1 hour, or until set.   Serve hot or cold. Great with barbecued chicken  or spare ribs or steak.
