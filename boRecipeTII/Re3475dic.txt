Pecan Kisses
Pecan halves, Brown sugar, Vanilla, Egg white, 
Beat egg white until it stands in peaks. Grad- ually add brown sugar and  vanilla. Fold in pecans. Coat well with mixture. Drop by teaspoon on  greased cookie sheet 1 inch apart. Bake at 250 for 30 minutes. Turn off  oven and let stand for 30 minutes longer. Freezes well.    MRS R.J.
