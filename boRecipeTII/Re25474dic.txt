Tortillas Snacks
Salt -- to taste, Margarine -- melted, Grated Parmesan cheese, Flour tortillas -- fresh, Chili powder, 
1. Brush tortillas with melted margarine. Sprinkle four of the tortillas  with chili powder and stack. Sprinkle the other 4 with the Parmesan cheese  and stack. Cut each stack into 4 wedges. 2. Spread the wedges on a large  ungreased baking sheet, using 2 sheets if necessary. 3. Place in preheated  350-degree oven for about 8-10 minutes. Turn 1-2 times. Watch carefully to  prevent overcooking. Remove tortillas and place in a serving basket.    Serve immediately.
