Prune Cookies
Shortening, Molasses, Cake flour, Cinnamon, Cooked prunes, Salt, Vanilla, Sugar, Baking soda, Eggs, well beaten, 
Drain prunes.  Remove pits.  Cut prunes into pieces. Cream shortening and  sugar.  Add molasses and eggs. Beat thoroughly. Sift flour, measure, and  sift with baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Add to first mixture. Beat  thoroughly.  Add prunes and flavoring. Mix thoroughly. Drop by teaspoonfuls  onto well-oiled baking sheet. Bake in hot oven (400 F) about 12 minutes. 15  servings.
