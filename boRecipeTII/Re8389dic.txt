Potato Skins with Black Beans and Salsa
Salsa, Fresh cilantro sprigs;, Baked potatoes, Low-fat sour cream, Nonfat black bean dip, Nonfat nacho dip, 
1. Preheat oven to 400F.    2. Halve baked potatoes lengthwise; scoop out pulp, leaving 1/4-inch pulp  attached to skin (avoid breaking skin). Place potato skins on large baking  sheet, skin-sides down; bake 5 minutes.    3. Fill each potato skin with 1 tablespoon bean dip and 1 tablespoon nacho  dip. Return to oven; bake 10 minutes. Remove from oven; let cool 5 minutes.  Top each potato with 1 tablespoon salsa and 1 tablespoon sour cream.  Garnish with cilantro. Serve hot.    Makes 12 servings. Preparation time: About 20 minute. Baking time: About 15  minutes. Standing time: About 5 minutes.    Per serving: About 99, 3 g pro, 19 g car, 1 g fat, 9% cal from fat, 3 mg  chol, 295 mg sod, 1 g fiber.
