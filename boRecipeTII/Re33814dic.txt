Sweet and Sour Poppy Seed Dressing
Onion juice, Vinegar, Vegetable oil, Dry mustard, Salt, Poppy seeds, Sugar, 
Mix sugar, dry mustard, salt and vinegar together. Add onion juice and mix  well. Gradually add oil, beating constantly. Add poppy seeds last; mix. Mix  well before serving. Take from refrigerator about 1/2 hour before serving.  Goes well on fruit or greens.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
