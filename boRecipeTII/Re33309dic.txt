Basic Sponge
Bread flour, Very Warm Water; 120 To 130, Sugar, Dry Yeast; (2 1/2 Tsp), 
Lightly spoon flour into a dry measuring cup; level with knife.    Place all the ingredients in a food processor, and process for 1 minute or  until well-blended. Spoon mixture into a medium bowl; cover and chill  sponge for 2 to 24 hours.
