Quick Bbq Sauce
Ketchup, Clove garlic -- finely, Chopped, Onion -- finely chopped, Lemon juice, Tabasco sauce -- to taste, Butter, Brown sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, Mustard, 
In a small saucepan, cook onion and garlic in butter intil tender. Add  remaining ingredients bring to boil. Reduce heat, simmer uncoverd 15 to 20  min. Use as a basting sauce for chicken, pork or beef. Can be made in  microwave.
