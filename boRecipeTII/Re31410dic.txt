Barbecue Beef Cups
Extra lean ground beef, Brown sugar, Biscuits, canned, Cheddar cheese, shredded, Onion; minced, Barbecue sauce, 
Brown hamburger; add sauce, onion and brown sugar. Simmer. In greased  muffin tin, put 1 biscuit in each cup and shape into a cup. Spoon hamburger  mixture into cups. Sprinkle cheddar cheese over. Bake at 400 degrees for 10  to 12 minutes.  Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #268    Date: Tue, 29 Oct 1996 09:57:45 -0800    From: Mary Ash <smile@ridgecrest.ca.us>
