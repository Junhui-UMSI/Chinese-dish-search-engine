Red and Black Bean Buffalo Chili
To 1 1/2 lbs ground, Chili powder, Fresh Cilantro, Crushed red pepper, Garlic, Red bell pepper; diced, Tomatoes; diced, Red onion; diced, Cooked black beans, Green bell pepper, Ground cumin, Honey; if desired, Ears sweet corn, Tamari sauce, Cooked red beans, Yellow bell pepper, 
Cook meat in large nonaluminum Dutch oven until no longer pink. Stir in  remaining ingredients except cilantro. Simmer gently, partially covered and  stirring frequently, 40 to 45 minutes.  Taste and adjust seasonings. Add  cilantro at serving time.     This is adapted from a recipe used at the Heartland Cafe on Chicagos  north side.  It uses dried, cooked beans although canned beans are a fine  short-order alternative. If desired, the chili can be served with grated  cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions, tortillas or crackers.     Chicago Tribune, 10/07/93.
