Wisconsin Cheese Soup
Sharp Cheddar cheese, Baking soda, chicken broth, Flour, red pepper, flakes, Butter, carrots, Chopped parsley, milk, Onions, 
(Biloxi MS Sun-Herald)    In a saucepan, melt butter; add onion, carrot and celery. Cook until  tender. Stir in flour; cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes until bubbly. Stir in  soda.    Gradually stir in milk and broth. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat aned simmer,  stirring until mixture thickens slightly, 10 to 15 minutes. Add cheese and  red pepper; stir just until melted and well-blended. Garnish with parsley.  Makes about 4 1/2 cups.
