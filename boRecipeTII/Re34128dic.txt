Batter Rolls
Granulated sugar, milk, Salt, Flour, Dry yeast, Shortening, Granulated sugar, Egg, Warm water, 
Disolve 1 tsp sugar in warm water in lrge bowl. Sprinkle in yeast. let  stand 10 minutes, then stir well. Combine milk, 1/4 cup sugar, shortening  and salt in saucepan. Heat until lukewarm and shortening is melted. Stir  well.  Add to yeast mixture.  Add 2 3/4 cups flour and beat vigorously with  wooden spoon or electric mixer until smooth. Gradually stir in remaining 1  c flour.  Batter will be soft.  Cover with tea towel. Let rise in warm  place until doubled about 1 hour. Stir down dough and let stand 10 minutes.  Fill greased muffin cups 1/2 full.  Let rise until doubled 45 minutes. Bake  at 375 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until golden. Turn out of pans  immediately. Serve warm and cool.  Makes about 18 rolls.
