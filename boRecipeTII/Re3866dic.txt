Pepper Catfish
Ground coriander, Garlic, Brown sugar, Whole peppercorns; crushed, Soy sauce, MARINADE, Catfish fillets or 1 lb., 
Mix marinade ingredients. Pour over fillets; cover and refrigerate at least  30 minutes; up to 4 hours. Grill over medium flame about 5 minutes per  side. May also be broiled.    Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 628 by "Joe"  <Broutek@worldnet.att.net> on Jan 29, 1998
