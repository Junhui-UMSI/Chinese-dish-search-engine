Quickie Mix
Instant nonfat dry milk, Baking Powder, Cream of tartar, Vegetable shortening, Salt, All purpose flour, Baking soda, 
In large bowl, sift together all the dry ingredients. Mix well. Cut in  shortening until evenly mixed.  Mixture will look like cornmeal. Put in a  large airtight container. Make 13 cups of mix. Use within 10 - 12 weeks.  Whole Wheat Mix: 4 1/4 c all purpose flour and 4 1/4 c. whole wheat flour  instead of 8 1/2 c ap flour.  Increase baking powder to 5 tablespoons.
