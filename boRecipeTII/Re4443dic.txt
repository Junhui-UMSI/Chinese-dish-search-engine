Pesto and Pasta
Rotelle pasta, Tomato sauce, Pesto, Cream, 
Cook Rotelle according to package directions. Drain and add 3 tablespoons  of pesto. Toss to coat. Set aside. In saucepan combine remaining pesto and  tomato sauce. Stir until mixed. Add cream and stir well. Pour over pasta  and serve.
