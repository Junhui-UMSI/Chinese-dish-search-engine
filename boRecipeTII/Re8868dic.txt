Prawn and Pork Toast
Prawns, cooked, Pork -- minced, lean, White bread slices -- day, Vegetable oil -- for frying, Egg, Old, Coriander leaves, Chinese dried prawns, Fish sauce -- to taste, Garlic, 
1. Finely chop the dried prawns (shrimps), coriander and garlic in food  processor.  Add fresh prawns and pork and grind to paste.    2. Add fish sauce and egg. Process until mixed.    3. Spread paste on bread. Cut crusts and slice into four triagles.    4. Heat oil for deep frying. Fry toast only four or five pcs at a time.  Slide into oil, filling downward and fry until golden brown, then flip over  and cook other side briefly.    5. remove toast and place on wire rack to drain.    6. Arrange toast on shredded lettuce with a small bowl of sweet and source  sauce.  Serve hot.
