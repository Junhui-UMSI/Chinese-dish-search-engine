Pork and Seaweed Soup
Sheets purple seaweed, Stock (see recipe) or water, Salt, Scallion, Soy sauce, Lean pork, Pepper, 
1. Tear seaweed in small squares and soak. Squeeze dry.       2. Slice pork thin. Mince scallion.       3. Bring stock to a boil. Add pork and simmer, covered, 5 to 8 minutes.       4. Add seaweed and simmer, covered, 5 minutes more.       5. Stir in soy sauce, salt and pepper. Garnish with minced scallion.       VARIATIONS: For the pork, substitute either smoked ham or raw chicken.       Add with pork either 6 dried shrimp (soaked) or 4 large fresh shrimp,  shelled, deveined and diced.
