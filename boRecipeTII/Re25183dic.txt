Tomatoes and Feta Cheese
SERVE, Cold watermelon slice, Feta cheese, Olive oil and salt to taste, Toasted bread slice, Diced tomatoes, 
Source : Bulgarian recipe. My fathers favorite summer dinner.    Crumble feta cheese, mix well with diced tomatoes, spread on a slice of  toasted bread. Olive oil and salt to taste. Serve with a cold watermelon  slice.  Posted to JEWISH-FOOD digest by "Viviane & Israel Barzel"  <i_barzel@netvision.net.il> on May 12, 1998
