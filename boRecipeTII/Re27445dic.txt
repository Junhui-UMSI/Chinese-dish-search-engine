Toffee Bars
Nuts; chopped, Brown sugar, Chocolate chips, Flour, Salt, Vanilla, Egg yolk, Butter, 
POWER: 50% .... TURN: Yes .... TIME: 8 min. per 6 servings Cream butter and  brown sugar. Add egg yolk, flour, vanilla and salt. Spread in 8x8 glass  baking dish. Microwave at 50% power for 7 to 8 minutes. Give 1/2 turn twice  during cooking. Sprinkle chocolate chips over top. Let chips soften for 2  minutes. Spread with knife over bars. Sprinkle with nuts. Cut into bars.
