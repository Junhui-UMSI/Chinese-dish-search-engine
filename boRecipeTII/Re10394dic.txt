Quick Chili W/homemade Chili Seasoning Mix
Tomatoes, Homemade chili seasoning mix, Lean ground beef, Kidney beans, 
Brown meat in a skillet over medium heat.  Drain excess fat.  Add beans,  tomatoes, and homemade chili seasoning mix.  Cover, reduce heat and simmer  for 10-15 minutes.    Makes 4-6 servings.
