Piperade Frittata
Chunky tomato sauce --, Salt, Parmesan cheese -- grated, Basil leaves -- (fresh), Pepper -- to taste, Piperade*, Chopped, olive oil, (optional), Eggs -- beaten, Cold water, Ham -- finely diced, 
Beat together eggs, water, cheese, salt and basil. Stir in piperade. Heat  oil in 10- to 12-inch nonstick skillet. Add ham;  saute over medium-high  heat for 2 minutes. Cook until set on bottom.  Reduce heat to medium. With  spatula, lift edges of fri ttata so uncooked egg runs underneath. Cook  until set, 1 to 2 minutes. Continue lifting and cooking until egg is  completely set and lightly browned on underside. Flip frittata and brown  top side, about 2 minutes.  Transfer to platter; dust with pepper. Cut into  4 wedges. Top with tomato sauce, if desired.    Recipe By     : MARIA CIANCI, Chronicle Staff Writer    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
