Strawberry Trifles
Topping, thawed, Strawberries, Sugar, Frozen nondairy whipped, Water, Unfilled lady fingers, 
Reserve 6 whole strawberries with stems for garnsih. Hull remaining  berries.  Process about half of the berries with sugar & water in  blender or food processor until almost pureed but still chunky.  Scrape into large bowl. Thinly slice remaining berries & stir into  berry mixture. Fold in whipping topping.  Divide mixture into 6  individual bowls. cut lady fingers in half, crosswise, & stick cut  end down into strawberry cream around edge of bowl. Garnish with  remaining berries.
