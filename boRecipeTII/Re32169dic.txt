Barbecued Pork Chops
Water, Dry mustard, Pork Chops, Chili sauce, Vinegar, Brown sugar, 
Combine ingredients and mix well.  Pour over seasoned chops  in glass baking dish, cover and bake at 400 F degrees for  1-1/2 hours, uncovering last 30 minutes.  Water may be added  to make gravy.  Delicious with ribs also.  From "Talk About Good" contributed by Susan Chandler Castille    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/meats.zip
