Peanut Butter and Jam Bars
Chunky peanut butter, All-purpose flour, Baking Powder, Strawberry preserves, Glaze, Unsalted butter, Powdered sugar, Hot water - + or -, Unsalted butter, Baking soda, Egg, Granulated sugar, Brown sugar, 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.    Well blend sugars, butter, peanut butter and egg in a large mixing bowl. By  hand stir in flour, baking soda and baking powder until well mixed. Set  aside 1 cup of the dough.    Press remaining dough onto the bottom of an ungreased 13x9x2-inch baking  pan. Spread strawberry preserves over dough.    Crumble the reserved dough, them generously sprinkle over the layer of  preserves.    Bake 20-25 minutes or until golden brown; cool completely. Drizzle with the  glaze. Cut into 2 x 1 1/4-inch bars.    GLAZE: Melt margarine in a double boiler over low heat. Stir in powdered  sugar and vanilla extract. Add water, 1 tsp at a time, beating until smooth  with a wish until thin enough to drizzle over the bars.
