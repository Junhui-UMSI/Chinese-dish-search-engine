Strawberry Cream Pie
9" Pastry Shell, Small Egg Yolks, Strawberries, Flour, Vanilla, milk, Firm Butter, Sugar, 
Bake and cool pastry shell.   Blend flour and sugar in heavy saucepan. Slowly stir in 1 cup milk until  smooth. Add rest of milk; cook and stir over direct heat until mixture  boils and thickens [be careful of boil-overs!].   Stir 1/2 cup of hot mixture into well-beaten egg yolks; stir together.  Pour back in saucepan; cook and stir 2 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in  vanilla and butter.   Pour half of mixture in pie shell. Layer strawberries; pour remaining  mixture on top. Cool and serve. Yields 6-8 servings.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmstrawb.zip
