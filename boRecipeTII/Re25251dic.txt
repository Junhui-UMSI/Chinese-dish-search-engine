Tonys Spaghetti Sauce
Bay leaf, Can Whole Plum Tomatoes --, Salt, Oregano, Fresh Parsley -- chopped, Black pepper, Clove Garlic -- minced, olive oil, Sugar, Tomato paste, Onion -- chopped, 
Cover bottom of deep heavy pot with olive oil. Cook onions and garlic until  soft.  Add tomatoes. Bring to a boil and add spices and seasonings. Simmer  1/2 hour, then add tomato paste and simmer for 2 hours, adding meatballs to  sauce last hour of cooking. Enough sauce and meatballs for 1 lb. thin  spaghetti, or four normal servings. Hearty eaters may eat more. Serve with  freshly grated Parmesan or Romano cheese, crusty bread, a green salad and  Chianti    Recipe By     :
