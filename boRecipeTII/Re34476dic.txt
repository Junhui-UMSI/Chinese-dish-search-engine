Bean Salad
Pepper, Sugar, Cider vinegar, Chick peas; drained, Salt, Onion; cut into rings, Yellow beans; drained, Green pepers; cut into rings, Kidney beans; drained, Green beans; drained, Vegetable oil, 
Mix first five ingredients together. Pour over remaining ingredients. This  salad tastes better if left to marinate overnight in the refrigerator. GOOD  TASTING SALAD! Posted to EAT-L Digest by pat hogberg  <p_hogberg@HOTMAIL.COM> on Dec 9, 1997
