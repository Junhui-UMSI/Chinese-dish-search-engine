Almond Gelatin Squares in Fruit
Pineapple Chunks, Seedless Red Grapes, Unflavored gelatin, Kiwi Fruit; peeled and, Orange; peeled,sectioned, Sugar, Nonfat Dry Milk, Boiling water, Ground cinnamon, Orange juice, Cold water, Almond extract, Pear; peeled,cored,cubed, 
In a small saucepan, combine the gelatin and cold water. Add the boiling  water and stir until the gelatin is completely dissolved. Place over medium  heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Gradually whisk in the dry  milk and sugar and return to a boil. Stir in the almond extract and  cinnamon. When the cinnamon is dissolved, pour the mixture into an 8 inch  square pan. Cover and refrigerate until firm, about 1 hour.    In a large bowl, combine the pear, grapes, kiwifruits, orange, pineapple  and orange juice. Gently toss to mix well.    To serve, cut the gelatin into 1 inch squares, add to the fruit and toss  gently. Divide among 4 individual bowls.    NOTES : Kiwifruits, oranges and pineapples are all excellent sources of  vitamin C.
