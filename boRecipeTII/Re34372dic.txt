Bean and Corn Salad
Salt, Honey Dijon mustard, Black Pepper, Cooked fresh corn kernels;, olive oil, Green beans; trimmed, Cider vinegar, (15 oz.) black beans;, Red onion; thinly sliced, 
Work Time: 15 minutes Total Time: 15 minutes    1. Cook green beans in boiling water until tender-crisp, 3 to 5 minutes.  Drain and rinse with cold water; drain.    2. Combine vinegar, oil, mustard, salt, and pepper in large bowl. Toss in  green beans, black beans, corn, and onion.
