Zippy Chili Dip
Pinto Beans, Drained And, Chili powder, Green Pepper, Ground beef, Salsa, 
Cook and stir ground beef in 10-inch skillet until brown; drain. Stir in  beans, salsa and chili powder. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly;  sprinkle with green pepper. Serve warm and, if desired, with tortilla  chips, sliced jicama and celery sticks. Typed by Syd Bigger.
