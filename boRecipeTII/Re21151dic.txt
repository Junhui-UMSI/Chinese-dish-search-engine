Two-Tomato Salsa
Chopped purple onion, Salt, Diced green bell pepper, Clove, minced garlic, olive oil, Unpeeled green tomato, Coarsely ground pepper, Diced unpeeled plum tomato, Finely chop jalapeno pepper, Finely chop fresh cilantro, Fresh lime juice, 
1.  Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Stir well. Serve at room      temperature or chilled.  Use a slotted spoon to serve with corn      tortilla wedges, poultry, or fish.  Yields 3 cups.
