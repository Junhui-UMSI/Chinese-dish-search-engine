Pungent Salt Substitute
Marjoram, Ground cumin, Summer savory, Basil, Thyme, Celery seed, Sage, 
Put ingredients into a blender, food processor or clean coffee grinder. (Or  crush with a mortar and pestle.) Blend well. Store in a glass container in  a cool, dry place. To prevent caking, add a few grains of rice.    From:  POLLYS POINTERS by Polly Fisher, San Antonio Express-News, 5/92  Posted by:  Karin Brewer, Cooking Echo, 6/92
