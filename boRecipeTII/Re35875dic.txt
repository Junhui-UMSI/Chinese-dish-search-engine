Stuffed Jalapenos (Massas)
Jalapenos, cut in half, l.w., White pepper, Onion, Cracker meal or bread crumbs, Butter, Fresh crab meat, Salt, Egg, slightly beaten, Celery, chopped, Garlic, chopped, 
Heat butter in a non-stick skillet and saute chopped onion, celery and  garlic until softened, about 3 minutes. Combine in bowl with crab, egg,  salt, pepper and cracker meal, and mix (dressing should stick together). If  too dry, add another egg. Stuff pepper halves liberally. Moisten stuffed  peppers by dipping in cool water. Roll in more cracker meal to coat. Deep  fry (or fry in skillet) in hot vegetable oil until golden brown. NOTE: To  parboil, drop peppers into boiling water for a minute or two      until semi-soft, then drain.
