Wax Beans with Mushrooms
Salt, Onion cut in thin strips, (6oz) sliced broiled, Butter, (9-oz) frozen cut wax beans, 
Saute onion in butter until golden. Drain mushrooms, reserving liquid. Add  mushrooms to onion. Cook beans according to directions, using mushroom  liquid instead of water and the salt. Drain, add to the onion mixture and  reheat.
