Apple Brandy Cake
Diced apples, Raisins (optional), Vanilla, Nuts (1/2 to 1 cup), Applejack (brandy), Vegetable oil, Baking soda, Flour, Eggs, Sugar, 
Combine flour, sugar, baking soda, nuts and raisins. Combine apples,  applejack, eggs, oil, and vanilla. Combine two mixtures (will be stiff).  Spread in a greased and floured tube pan. Bake 40-45 minutes (no  temperature given, sic).  Let stand 10 minutes. Pour two ounces of  applejack over the cake.
