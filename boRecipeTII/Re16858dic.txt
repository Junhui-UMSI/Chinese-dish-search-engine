All-Dressed Spaghetti
Mushrooms, sliced, Sweet green pepper, chopped, Vegetable oil, Onion, Canned tomato sauce, Salt, Pepper, Dried Oregano, Pepperoni, sliced, Spaghetti, 
In skillet, heat oil ove medium heat; cook onion, mushrooms, pepperoni,  green pepper and oregano, stirring often, for 5 minutes or until onion is  softened.    Add tomato sauce, salt and pepper; cook for 3 minutes or until boiling.    Meanwhile, in large pot of boiling salted water, cook spaghetti for 8-10  minutes or until tender but firm. Drain well and return to pot.    Add sauce; toss to coat well.
