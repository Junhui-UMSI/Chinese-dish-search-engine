Pound Cake #3
Butter, Flour, Lemon juice, (14-oz) sweetened condensed, TOPPING, Butter, Eggs, Powdered sugar, Butter flavoring, Sugar, 
Cream sugar & butter. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well between  additions. Add butter flavoring. Add sweetened condensed milk alternately  with flour. Bake in greased tube pan at 325 for 1 hour and 20-30 minutes.  TOPPING:  Melt butter. Add lemon juice & powdered sugar. Stir & drizzle  over cooled cake. (Rich & delicious.)
