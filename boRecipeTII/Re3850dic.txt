Pepper and Eggs
Egg whites, (large), Egg, (large), Salt,, Garlic, Mushroom, chopped, Potato, cooked, cubed, Black pepper, freshly ground, Green bell pepper, chopped, 
1. In small bowl, combine egg whites, egg, salt, and pepper.    2. In medium nonstick skillet, heat oil; add red and green bell peppers,  mushrooms, and garlic. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, 2  minutes. Add potato; cook until browned, about 2 minutes.    3. Pour in egg mixture; cook, stirring constantly, until eggs are just set,  about 1 minute.
