Pineapple-Graham Cracker Bars
Graham cracker crumbs, Packed light brown sugar, Black walnuts, Drained, Egg, Crushed pineapple, All-purpose flour, Margarine, 
1. Place softened margarine and brown sugar in a bowl and, using hand mixer  or wooden spoon, cream mixture until light and fluffy. 2. Beat in the egg.  Stir in sifted flour, crumbs, well-drained pineapple and nuts. 3. Pour into  a lightly greased 8 x 8 x 2-inch pan. Bake in preheated 350-degree oven  30-35 minutes or until brown and surface feels firm to the touch. 4. Let  cool completely in pan, then cut into 20 bars.
