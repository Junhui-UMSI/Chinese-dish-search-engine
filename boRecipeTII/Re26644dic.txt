Baileys - # 3
Evaporated milk, Irish whiskey, Almond extract, Coconut extract, Vodka, 
Method: Put in blender. Walk around table three times to age to perfection  [cute, huh?]. Blend. Keep refrigerated. From Bobbi Zee. This wont have as  strong an Irish whiskey flavor.
