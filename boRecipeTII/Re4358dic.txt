Persimmon Orange Delights
Salt, Butter or margarine, Baking Powder, Eggs, Nutmeg, Persimmon pulp, Nuts; coarsely chopped, Orange rind; grated, Cinnamon, Brown sugar; firmly packed, Mace or cloves, Flour, Raisins, Orange juice, Baking soda, 
Cream butter and sugar lightly, then add next four ingredients. Measure  flour and add soda, baking powder, spices and salt. Stir once together then  add to first mixture. Fold in nuts and raisins.    To make Drop cookies, drop by teaspoon on greased cookie sheet. Bake at 375  degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.    To make persimmon brownies, pour into a 13 x 9 inch greased pan. Bake at  350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes or until brownies start to leave sides of  pan. Cut in squares and serve a la mode with ice cream or sweetened whipped  cream sprinkled with grated orange rind.    Makes 3 dozen drop cookies, or 12 squares.
