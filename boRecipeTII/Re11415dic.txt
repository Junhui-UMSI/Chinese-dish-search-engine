Raspberries and Cream
Cream, Sugar, ICE CREAM COOKBOOK-----, vanilla ice cream, Fresh raspberries, 
Sugar the raspberries and then fold into the vanilla ice cream when it is  completed, but still soft. This method results in vanilla ice cream  containing whole fresh raspberries.
