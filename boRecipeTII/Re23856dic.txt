Asian Rice Salad
To 3 tablespoons rice or red, Green onions (white and, Sugar, Cloves garlic, minced (or, Chopped, 1 teaspoon bottled), Thin carrot slices, Wine vinegar, Leftover Asian rice pilaf, Thin celery slices, Optional:, Sliced water chestnuts,, Roasted peanuts, coarsely, Coarsely chopped, Oil (preferably peanut), Green tops), sliced, 
1. Fluff leftover rice pilaf with a fork.  Add garlic, water chestnuts,  carrots, celery, green onions and peanuts. 2. Combine oil, vinegar and  sugar in a small jar, cover and shake to combine. Pour over the salad and  serve at once.
