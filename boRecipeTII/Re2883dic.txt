Peanut Butter Fudge #11
Sugar, Peanut butter, milk, Butter, 
Combine sugar and milk in saucepan.  Cook over moderate heat to "soft  ball", stirring occaisionally to keep form sticking to the bottom of the  pan. Remove from heat. Beat in butter and peanut butter. Beat until it  holds it shape.[or arm falls off-JM] Pour into buttered dish to cool.
