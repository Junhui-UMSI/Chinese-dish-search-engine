Quick Beef and Cheese Burritos
Lean ground beef, Chunky salsa, Flour tortillas, black olives, Monterey jack cheese, tomato, lettuce, 
Brown the beef in a skillet over medium-high heat until no longer  pink. Pour off drippings.  Stir in salsa and cheese; heat until  cheese melts.    To serve, divide filling among tortillas.  Fold bottom edge of each  tortilla up over filling; fold sides to center, overlapping edges.
