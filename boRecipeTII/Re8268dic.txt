Potato Pancakes
Pepper, Butter, melted or margarine, Baking Powder, Onion, finely chopped, milk, Potatoes, grated, Salt, Eggs, All-purpose flour, 
Grate potatoes just before using to prevent discolouration. Combine all  ingredients in a bowl and beat until well blended then add grated potatoes.  Drop in spoonfuls in large amounts of hot fat in a fry pan. Spread out  flat. Fry on medium heat until crisp and golden brown on under side. Turn  and brown the other side. Serve with sour cream or apple sauce or both.
