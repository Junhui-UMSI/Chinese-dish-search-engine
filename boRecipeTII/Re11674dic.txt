Raspberry Vinaigrette with Gelatin
Garlic, Cold water, Lemon juice, Raspberry vinegar, Unflavored gelatin, Pepper, Brown sugar, Boiling water, 
Sprinkle gelatin over cold water and lemon juice in blender.  Let stand 2  minutes. Add boiling water & process on low until gelatin is dissolved,  about 2 minutes. Combine vinegar and sugar.  Mix well. Add vinegar & sugar  and the remaining ingredients to the gelatin and refrigerate for 2 hours.  Stir before serving.  Good for 50 days, covered and refrigerated.
