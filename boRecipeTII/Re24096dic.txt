Traditional Apple Butter
Cooking apples (about 16, Sugar, Chopped) -- chopped, Apple cider, Ground cloves, Ground cinnamon, 
Core and chop apples.  (Do not peel.)  Combine apples and cider in  slow-cooking pot.  Cover and cook on low for 10 to 12 hours or until apples  are mushy.  Puree in food mill or sieve.  Return pureed mixture to pot; add  sugar, cinnamon, and cloves.  Cover and cook on low one hour. Will keep  sseveral weeks in the refrigerator.  Or, if desired, pour into hot  sterilized jars and seal, or pour into freezer containers and freeze.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
