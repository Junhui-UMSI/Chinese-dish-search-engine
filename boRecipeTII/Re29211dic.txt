Baked Smoked Chicken Apple Sausage with Cider Sauce
Apple cider, lager beer, Chicken Stock, Brown Sugar, Cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, Aidells smoked sausage, 
Bring cider, lager and stock to a boil in a heavy saucepan over medium-high  heat. Boil about 8 minutes. Whisk sugar, vinegar and mustard in a small  bowl until reduced to 2 1/4 cups, stirring occasionally, about 6 minutes.    Preheat oven to 375 F. Place sausages in a baking dish and spoon over the  sauce. Bake until sausage is heated through, about 15 minutes. Serve.
