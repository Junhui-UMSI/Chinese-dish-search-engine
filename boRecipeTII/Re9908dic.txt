Punjabi Garam Masala
Black peppercorns, Green cardamom seeds, Coriander seeds, Black cardamom seeds, Inch stick cinnamon, Whole nutmeg, about, Cumin seeds, Cloves (up to 5), 
(if you want to make your own or it isnt available)    Toast coriander and cumin seeds lightly in a skillet.  Pour onto a plate  and allow to cool. Place into a grinder along with remaining ingredients.    Grind to a fine powder.  May be done in more than one batch.  Place in a  tightly sealed container and store in the *freezer*.
