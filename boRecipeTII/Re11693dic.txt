Raspberry Zinger Vinaigrette
Balsamic vinegar, Raspberry Zinger tea bags, White vinegar, Sugar, olive oil, 
Place the teabags and vinegar in a jar.  Steep the tea bags for 20 minutes.  Remove the bags, and gently squeeze the last bit of flavor from the bags  before discarding.  Add the remaining ingredients to the vinegar, and  shake.    Preparation Time: 20 minutes    Source: Celestial Seasonings "Cooking With Tea" by Jennifer & Mo Siegel    The Chefs Comments: "I just bought this nifty cookbook with all sorts of  recipes involving herbal teas (and regular teas as well).
