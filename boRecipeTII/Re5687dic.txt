Pineapple-Bran Whole-Wheat Muffins
Skim milk, Vegetable oil, Whole wheat flour, Egg, Brown sugar, 100% all-bran cereal, Can crushed pineapple w juic, Baking Powder, Salt, 
Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.  Beat the egg slightly.  Add cereal, milk, and oil to the egg.  Stir to combine.  Let stand for 2  min. or until the cereal has softened. Stir the pineapple, including the  juice, into the mixture.  Add flour mixture, stirring only until combined.   Spoon the batter evenly into a paper-lined muffin tin and bake at 400F  for about 25 min.  Serve warm.  Cal: 100, Fat: 1g.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmealnew.zip
