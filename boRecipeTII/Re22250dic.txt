Turkey Stuffing Bake
Pepperidge Farm? Cubed Herb, Coarsely chopped walnuts;, Cubed cooked turkey, (16 ounces) whole berry, (14 3/4 ounces), 
Prep Time: 15 Min. Cook Time: 20 Min.    1. Prepare stuffing according to package directions. Stir in cranberry  sauce and nuts.    2. In 3-quart oblong baking dish, spoon stuffing. Arrange turkey over  stuffing mixture. Pour gravy over turkey.    3. Bake at 400F. 20 minutes or until hot and bubbling. Makes 6 servings.
