Peanut Butter Cookies #3
(60 mL) creamy peanut butter, (30 mL) granulated sugar, (5 mL) vanilla extract, (375 mL) flour, Egg, (60 mL) water, Replacement, (5 mL) baking soda, (2 mL) baking powder, (60 mL) margarine, 
Cream together margarine, peanut butter and sugar replacements. add egg,  water and vanilla, beating until fluffy. Combine flour, baking soda and  baking powder in sifter and sift dry ingredients into creamed mixture. Stir  to blend completely. Chill thoroughly, at least 2 hours or overnight. Drop  by teaspoonfuls onto lightly greased cookie sheets, 2 to 3 in. (5 to 7 cm)  apart. Press flat with a floured bottom of a small glass. Bake at 375  degrees F (190 degrees C) for 12 to       15    minutes.
