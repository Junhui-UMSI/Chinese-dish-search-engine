Real Italian Spaghetti Sauce
olive oil, Snipped parsley, Garlic, Italian tomatoes, Italian tomatoes, Onions, Tomato paste, Tomato juice, Brown sugar, oregano, Pork, Bay leaf, MSG, Italian sausage, Parmesan cheese, Ground beef, Salt, thyme, 
Brown onion and garlic in olive oil. Brown ground beef, sausage and pork  after onions and garlic have been removed. Add remaining ingredients and  simmer 3 hours, or until sauce is thick.
