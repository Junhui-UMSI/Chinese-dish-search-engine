Basic Deep Fried Soft-Shell Crabs
Soft-shell crabs, Egg whites, Water, All-purpose flour, Oil for deep frying, salt and pepper, 
Prepare batter by whisking egg whites and water (50 percent egg whites, 50  percent water). Sift flour seasoned with salt and pepper. Dip crabs in  batter, drain then lightly dredge in flour. Shake to remove excess flour.  Deep fry at 375 F for about 3 minutes or until lightly browned and crispy.  Turn crabs often while cooking.
