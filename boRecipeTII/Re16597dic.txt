Aioli Sauce
Fresh Lemon Juice, Paprika, Egg yolks, olive oil, Salt, Cayenne pepper, garlic, 
Place all ingredients except olive oil in blender. Blend on low speed until  garlic is finely chopped, about 2 minutes. Blend in olive oil. Excellent on  fish.    From <A Taste of Louisiana>.  Downloaded from G Internet,  G Internet.
