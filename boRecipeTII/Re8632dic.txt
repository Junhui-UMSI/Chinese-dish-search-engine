Potlatch Salmon
Juniper berries; (about 50), Salt, garnish, Pepper, 8-oz. salmon steaks, Lemon wedges; or lime wedges, Mayonnaise, 
Lightly crush juniper berries.  Push 6-8 berries into each steak. Sprinkle  fish with salt and pepper.    Grill over coals 5-6 minutes on each side.  Garnish with lemon wedges and  mayonnaise.    Serves 6.    (Adapted from a recipe in "A Seafood Heritage From the Plains to the  Pacific", US Department of Commerce)    Recipes sent to me from Bill, wight@odc.net
