Chipotle Butter
Butter, chipotles, Lime juice, cilantro, 
By CATHY THOMAS The Orange County Register    In a food processor, blend half a cup (one stick) butter, one teaspoon  pureed chipotles, one teaspoon lime juice and one teaspoon minced cilantro.  Use plastic wrap to form into a tube and refrigerate. Serve on corn on the  cob and other vegetables.    Posted to CHILE-HEADS DIGEST by Leslie Duncan <duncan@vianet.on.ca> on Sep  24, 1998,
