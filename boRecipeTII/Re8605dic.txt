Potatoes with Parsley Sauce
Red skinned potatoes,, Firmly packed parsley, Scallions, chopped, Chopped cucumber, scrubbed, Salt and pepper to taste, Lemon, juiced, Minced dill, Poppy seeds for topping, olive oil, 
Cook potatoes in their skins until done but still firm. when cool enough to  handle, quarter them lengthwise & slice 1/2-inch thick. Place sauce  ingredients in a food processor & process till mixture is smooth & thick.  toss at once with the potatoes. Season to atste. Transfer to a shallow  serving dish & sprinkle with poppy seeds. Serve warm.
