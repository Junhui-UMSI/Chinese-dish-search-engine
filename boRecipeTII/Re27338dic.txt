Toast Hawaiian Style
White Bread, Boiled Ham, Gouda Cheese, Marachino cherries, Pineapples (Rings), 
1.Put a slice of ham, a slice of pineapple and a slice  of cheese on each slice of bread. 2.Bake in 200 C oven  until the cheese is melted. 3.Garnish with cherry and  serve at once. Translated by Brigitte Sealing
