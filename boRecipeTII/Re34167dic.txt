Bavarian Patties with Sauerkraut
Ground beef, Allspice, Bread crumbs; dry, Salt, Applesauce, Egg, Onion, (16-oz) sauerkraut; drained, 
Date: Thu, 29 Feb 1996 03:28:54 GMT    From: gemini@synapse.net (Georgette & Dave Burnside)    Mix all the ingredients together except the sauerkraut.  Shape the mixture  into 6 patties, each about 3/4-inch thick.  Brown the patties in a large  skillet over medium heat, turning once.  Drain off the excess fat.  Spoon  the sauerkraut onto the patties. Cover and simmer for about 15 minutes.
