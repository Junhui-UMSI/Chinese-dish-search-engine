Beef Squares
Oregano, Extra lean ground beef, Cheez whiz, Sausage meat, Garlic powder, 
Brown meats amd drain well. Add rest of ingredients and mix well. Spread on  cocktail breads and freeze on a cookie sheet. When frozen put in an  airtight container in layers separated by wax paper. To serve remove from  freezer and broil until bubbly.    Source Betty Sampson Formatted for Mastercook by Carol  Floyd--c.floyd@arnprior.com    NOTES : These are great to have in the freezer to serve unexpected guests.
