Pilaf Stuffed Chicken
Onion -- chopped, Pine nuts, Pepper, Butter -- melted, Rice, Currants, 3 lb., Yogurt, Chicken, Water, Salt, Butter or margarine, 
Fry onions in 1/4 cup butter in saucepan until transparent. Add pine nuts  and rice; saute until light brown. Add currants, 1 tablespoon salt and  water, a dash of pepper. Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer for 25 - 30  minutes, or until all the water is Cool before using as stuffing. Stuff  chicken with one cup of pilaf. Combine yogurt and salt; brush half over  Chicken. Bake at 400 F for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 F; bake 1 hour  more, basting with yogurt.
