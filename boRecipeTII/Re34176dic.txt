Bavarian Sausage Salad
Pickles, Knockwurst, Paprika, Salt, Vinegar, Sugar, Onion, Pepper, Mustard, Vegetable oil, Capers, Parsley, 
*  Mustard must be the strong Djon or Gulden Type.  ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++ ++++  Cut the knockwurst into small cubes.  Mince the pickles and onion. Mix  together the vinegar, mustard and oil.  Add salt, pepper, paprika and  sugar.  Adjust seasonings if desired.  Add the capers; mix well.  Stir in  the chopped knockwurst, pickles, and onions.  Just before serving, garnish  with chopped parsley.    Recipe By     :    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmdja006.zip
