Baking Powder Drop Biscuits
Baking Powder, Salt, Shortening, Buttermilk, Baking soda, A. P. flour, 
Sift flour with soda, salt and baking powder.  Mix in the shortening  with fingertips.  Add buttermilk and stir. Mix all into a soft dough.       Using a spoon, drop pieces of the dough about the size of a walnut onto  a greased baking sheet.  Leave 2 inches between each.       Bake in a preheated 400 F. oven for 10 min., or until browned.
