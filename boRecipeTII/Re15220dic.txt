Whipped Honey Butter
Honey, Lemon zest, Unsalted butter, 
Beat the butter until creamy with a wooden spoon, an electric mixer, or a  food processor. Add the honey and zest. Beat just until combined. Store,  covered, in the refrigerator until ready to use. To serve: Let stand 30  minutes at room temperature to soften.
