Wunder-Bar Cookies
Flour, Confectioners sugar, Butter, Vanilla, Light cream, 
Cream butter, sugar & vanilla. Stir in flour alternately with cream.  Chill dough thoroughly. Heat oven to 350. Shape into 1" balls. Place  on ungreased sheet. Bake 20 minutes.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
