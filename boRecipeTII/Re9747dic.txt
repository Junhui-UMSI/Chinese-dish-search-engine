Pumpkin Roll
Flour, Cream cheese, Pumpkin, Sugar, FILLING, Nutmeg, Vanilla, Eggs, Salt, Pecans, Sugar, Butter, Lemon juice, Cinnamon, Ginger, Baking Powder, 
Beat together 3 Eggs and 1 cup of sugar (5 min).  Fold in the pumpkin and  the lemon juice. Then fold in the flour, spices, salt, and baking powder.  Pour into a greased and floured jelly roll pan or lipped cookie sheet and  sprinkle with chopped nuts.  Bake at 350? for 15 minutes. Dust a kitchen  towel well with powdered sugar. Remove the cake roll from the pan and  immediately place on sugared towel. Roll up cake in the towel and let cool.  Unroll and spread filling on cake. Roll cake and filling into dessert roll.  Chill and cut into 1/2 inch slices.
