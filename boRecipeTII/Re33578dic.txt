Swedish Flatbread
Fennel seed, Rye flour, Salt, Butter or margarine, White flour, Baking soda, Buttermilk, Sugar, 
In a bowl, blend flour, sugar, salt and soda.  Cut in margarine until  mixture resembles fine crumbs.  Stir in buttermilk and add fennel seeds,  sing a fork, just until mixture holds together.  Shape into small balls and  roll on floured board to make very thin rounds four to five inches in  diameter.  Bake on ungreased sheets at 375F five minutes or until light  brown.
