Barbecue-Grilled Chicken
Instant minced onion, Worcestershire, Brown Sugar, , Chicken, Ketchup, White vinegar, 
Preheat the oven to 350F. Place the chicken in a 9" x 13" baking dish that  has been coated with nonstick cooking spray and bake for 25 minutes.  Meanwhile, in a medium-sized bowl, mix together the remaining ingredients.  Preheat the grill to medium heat. Coat the chicken with the sauce mixture  and place on the grill. Grill for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the juices run  clear and no pink remains, turning halfway through the grilling.
