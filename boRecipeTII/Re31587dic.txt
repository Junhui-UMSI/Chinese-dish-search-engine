Swiss "French" Salad Dressing
Stock, Vinegar, Cream, Egg, Sugar, Salt, Mustard, Fresh-ground pepper, olive oil, 
Blend together all the ingredients except the stock or water in a blender  or food processor until quite smooth. Add enough stock or water to give a  lightly coating consistency. Put in a screwtop jar and keep in the  refrigerator.  Use within 3-4 days.    (from A TASTE OF SWITZERLAND, Sue Style)
