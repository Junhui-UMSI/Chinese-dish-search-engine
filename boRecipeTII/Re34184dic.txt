Bay Country Crab Soup
Water, Onion, Maryland claw crabmeat;, Black pepper, Fresh or frozen mixed soup, Frozen mixed vegetables;, Stalks celery; chopped, Beef shin; bone-in, Salt, Cayenne pepper, 1 pound can tomatoes, Maryland regular crabmeat, 
Recipe by: The Maryland Seafood Cookbook.    Put first 8 ingredients in a large pot and simmer covered, until meat is  tender, about three hours. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer  covered until vegetables are done.
