Tangy Lemon Icing
Salt, Butter, Lemon Rind, Lemon juice, Yellow food coloring, Egg white, 
Blend half of sugar, egg white, salt, melted butter & lemon rind in  large bowl.  Add 1 Tbls. lemon juice. Blend remaining sugar into  mixture gradually. Stir in enough lemon juice to reach desired  consistency. Tint.    File ftp://ftp.idiscover.co.uk/pub/food/mealmaster/recipes/mmkah001.zip
