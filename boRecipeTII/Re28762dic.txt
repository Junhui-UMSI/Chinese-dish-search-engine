Tgi Fridays Soy Sauce Dressing
Peanut oil, hot mustard, WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A, Water, Soy sauce, , Green onion stems, Cider vinegar, Honey, 
Combine all ingredients in a jar with tight-fitting lid; shake the jar  vigorously to combine ingredients thoroughly. Keep refrigerated and covered  to use within a few weeks. Shake before using.
