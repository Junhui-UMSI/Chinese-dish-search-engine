Tender Pork Roast
Ground mustard, Soy sauce, Boneless pork roast (about, Sugar, 3 pounds), (8 oz.) tomato sauce, 
Cut roast in half; place in a 5 qt. slow cooker. Combine remaining  ingredients; pour over roast.  Cover and cook on    low for 8-9 hours or until a meat thermometer read 160?-170?. Remove  roast to a serving platter and keep warm. If desired, skim fat from pan  juices and thicken for gravy. Yield 8 serving. Typed in MMFormat by  cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Quick Cooking.
