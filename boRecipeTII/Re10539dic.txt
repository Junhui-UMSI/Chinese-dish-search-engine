Quick Lemon-Beef Stir Fry
Carrots, shredded, Lemon zest, finely minced, Lemon juice, fresh, Snow peas, Sugar, Salt, Water chestnuts, sm, sliced, White pepper, Soy sauce, Cornstarch, Beef flank steak, thinly slc, Garlic, Mushrooms, fresh, sliced, Red chili flakes, Hoisin sauce, 
Heat a wok or skillet. Add 1T vegetable oil. Add thinly sliced beef and  sautee until half cooked. Combine soy, hoisin, garlic, sugar, chili, lemon  and cornstarch into sauce and add to beef. When glazey, about 1 minute,  add salt & white pepper to taste and stir in vegetables. Stir-fry until  mushrooms and snow peas are cooked but still crunchy. Serve immediately  with plain rice.
