Zupa Wisniowa (Cherry Soup)
Sugar, Cooked egg noodles, if, Pitted dark sweet cherries, Lemon juice, Water (2 cups), Whipping cream, 
In a large saucepan, combine cherries and water. Bring to a boil over  medium heat. Puree cherries and water in a blender or food processor fitted  with a metal blade. Return mixture to saucepan. Add lemon juice to taste,  sugar and whipping cream. Gently stir until smooth. Serve hot over noodles,  if desired. Or serve chilled. Makes 6 servings    Variation: Substitute red tart cherries for sweet cherries. Do not add  lemon juice. Sweeten with sugar to taste.
