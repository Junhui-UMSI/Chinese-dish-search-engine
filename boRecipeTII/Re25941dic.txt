Bacon and Water Chestnut Hors D Oeuvres
Sugar, Water Chestnuts; drained, Soy sauce, Bacon strips; cut in thirds., 
Marinate water chestnuts in soy sauce for 30 minutes. If chestnuts are too  large cut into bite-size pieces. Roll each in sugar, the wrap in a piece of  bacon. Place on slotted surface of broiler pan and bake at 400 degrees for  approximately 20 minutes, turning once. Drain on paper towels.
