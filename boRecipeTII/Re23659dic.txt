Artichoke Dip #4
Parmesan cheese, Mayonnaise, Mozzarella cheese; shredded, Artichoke hearts; chopped, Cheddar cheese; shredded, Garlic powder, Mushrooms; sliced, 
Date: Thu, 14 Mar 1996 06:42:06 +1100    From: sherae@zeta.org.au (Sheri McRae)  Mix together all ingredients, except 1/4 cup parmesan cheese. Spread in a  8x11-inch cake pan. Sprinkle with remaining parmesan cheese. Bake at 350F  for about 30 minutes. Serve it with Ritz crackers.    Variation: Spread on bagel halves and broil until bubbly and golden.
