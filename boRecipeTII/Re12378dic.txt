Zucchini with Cheese
Zucchini, Dried oregano leaves, Olive or salad oil, Sour Cream, Salt, Grated Parmesan cheese, Pepper, All-purpose flour, Tomatoes; sliced, 
1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Lightly grease 8-by-8-by-2-inch baking dish. With  stiff brush, scrub zucchini well. Cut crosswise into 1/4-inch slices.    2. In medium bowl, combine flour with 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2teaspoon  oregano, and 1/8 teaspoon pepper. Toss zucchini slices in seasoned flour,  to coat them well.    3. Slowly heat oil in large, heavy skillet. Saute6 zucchini until golden  brown -- about 4 minutes on each side. Drain.    4. Cover bottom of baking dish with zucchini. Top with tomato slices.  Combine sour cream, rest of salt, oregano, and pepper, and spread evenly  over tomato slices. Then sprinkle with grated cheese.    5. Bake 30 to 35 minutes, or until cheese is melted and zucchini is tender.  Makes 6 servings.  Posted to recipelu-digest Volume 01 Number 370 by "Diane Geary"  <diane@keyway.net> on Dec 15, 1997
