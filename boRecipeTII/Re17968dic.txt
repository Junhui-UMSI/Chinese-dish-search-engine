Amaretto Carrots
Carrots, cut into, Amaretto, Ground ginger, or to taste, Sweet butter, Sliced unblanced almonds,, 
In a steamer set over simmering water steam the carrots, covered, for 10  minutes, or until they are just al dente. Quarter the carrots lengthwise.   In a skillet, cook the carrots in the butter over moderate heat, stirring,  for 3 minutes; stir in the Amaretto and the ginger, and cook the mixture,  covered, over low heat for 3 minutes.  Stir in the almonds and salt and  pepper to taste and simmer the mixture, covered for 1 minute. Serves 6 to  8.
