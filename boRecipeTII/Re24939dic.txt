Autumn Barley Stew
Onions; chopped in large, Barley, Dill weed, Brussels sprouts, Sweet potato; chunked, Potatoes, white; chunked, Parsley flakes, Oregano, Tomatoes; wedged, Tamari, low sodium, Vegetable broth, 
Place barley and water in large pot. Bring to a boil.   Add remaining ingedients, except the tomatoes.   Reduceheat, cover, and simmer about 50 minutes. Add the tomatoes. Cook an  additional 10 minutes. Serve hot.     Can be prepared in a slow cooker, but dont add the tomatoes until just  before serving.     From the files of DEEANNE
