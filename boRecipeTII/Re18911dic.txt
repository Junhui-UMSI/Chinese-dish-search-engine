Angel Food Cake (Mix)
CAKE ANG FD SEASONAL #275, 
PAN:  16 BY 4 1/2 BY 4 1/8-INCH LOAF-TYPE PAN    TEMPERATURE:  350 F. OVEN    1.  PREPARE MIX ACCORDING TO INSTRUCTIONS ON CONTAINER.    2.  POUR 1 LB 8 OZ BATTER EVENLY INTO EACH UNGREASED PAN.  DO NOT FILL MORE  THAN 2/3 FULL.    3.  BAKE 35-40 MINUTES OR UNTIL CRUST IS GOLDEN BROWN.  INVERT PANS;  COOL AT LEAST 1 HOUR.    4.  FROST IF DESIRED.  CUT 13 SLICES PER LOAF.
