Doritos Casserole
Ground beef, Onions, Chopped green chiles, Cream of mushroom or cream, Soup can milk, Bag Doritos tortilla chips, Grated cheese, 
Brown meat and onion until meat is done and onion is tender; drain. Add  chiles, soup, milk, and chips. Mix well. Pour into baking dish and top with  grated cheese. Bake at 325 degrees until heated throughly and cheese melts.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest  by IceTeaLuvr@aol.com on Aug 22, 1998,
