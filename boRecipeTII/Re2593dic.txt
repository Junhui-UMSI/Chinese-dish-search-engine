Peachy Pork Picante
Boneless cubed pork, Oil to brown pork, Jar of salsa, Peach preserves, Cooked rice for 4, Taco seasoning, 
Coat pork cubes with taco seasoning and brown in skillet. Add salsa and  peach preserves to skillet. Stir to mix well, cover and lower heat. Simmer  gently for 15 minutes. Serve over hot, cooked rice.    Source:  Victoria Magazine, March 1995, ad for "Pork - the Other white  meat".
