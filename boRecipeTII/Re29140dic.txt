Baked Rice with Green Chiles
MontereyJack Cheese, Rice, Cheddar Cheese, Sour Cream, Red Chiles; Ground, Green Chiles * , 
*    Use 2 4-oz cans of chopped green chiles which have been drained.  ~-------------------------------------------------------------------------  Heat the oven to 350 degrees F.  Mix all the ingredients in an ungreased  2-quart casserole.  Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes, or until done.    Miscellaneous recipes from the collection of Mike Orchekowski.
