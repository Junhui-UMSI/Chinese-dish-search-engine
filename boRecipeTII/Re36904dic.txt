Strawberry Sorbet
Fresh strawberries, Water, Orange juice, Sugar, 
In a small saucepan, combine water and sugar.  Stir over low heat until  sugar dissolves; bring to a boil.  Boil gently 5 minutes without stirring; set aside to cool. Wash  strawberries; remove and discard caps.  Puree strawberries in blender or food processor fitted with metal blade.  In a medium bowl, combine pureed strawberries, cooled syrup and orange  juice. Pour into ice-cream canister. Freeze in ice-cream maker according to  manufacturers directions.  Makes about 1 quart.  Per Serving: 90 calories - 0 prot. - 23 g. carb. - 0 fat - 0 chol. - 7  mg. calcium.
