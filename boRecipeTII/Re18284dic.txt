Venison-Beef Stew
Beef, Venison, Potatoes, diced, Butter, Worcestershire Sauce, Celery stalks, chopped, Onion, diced, Water, Beef bouillon cubes, Garlic, 8.5 oz. can sweet peas, 7.6 oz. cans stew starter, Kitchen Bouquet, Carrots, chopped, Bay leaf, 
Cut up venison and beef into bite size pieces.  Sprinkle with Adolphs meat tenderizer.  Let set for  10 minutes; then brown meat. Add water and rest of  ingredients and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover  and simmer for 1 1/2 hours or until meat and  vegetables are tender.  Use large 4-quart pot.
