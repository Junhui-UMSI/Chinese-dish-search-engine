Bacon Cheese Muffins
milk, Egg, Cheddar cheese; shredded, Baking Powder, Vegetable oil, Crunchy nugget cereal, Bacon; (12 slices), Flour, Sugar, 
In large skillet, cook bacon over medium-high heat until crisp. Drain,  reserving drippings. If necessary add oil to drippings to measure 1/3 cup.  In small bowl, combine dripping mixture, egg and milk; set aside. Crumble  bacon; set aside. In large bowl, combine flour, sugar and baking powder.  Make well in center. Add dripping-egg mixture all at once to flour mixture.  Stirring just until moistened. Batter should be lumpy. Fold in bacon,  cheese and cereal. Spoon into GREASED or PAPER-LINED 2 1/2" muffin cups,  filling about 3/4 full. Bake in preheated oven 400F degrees. Bake 15-20  minutes. Remove from pan and cool on wire rack.
