Thai Lemon Grass Soup with Shrimp
Scallions, Fresh cilantro, , shrimp, lemon grass, Very hot small red chilies, Thai fish sauce, Vegetable oil, Fresh lime juice, Unsweetened coconut milk, 
Peel shrimp, reserve shells and set both aside. Heat oil in stockpot, add  shrimp shells, & fry until they are bright pink. Add 8 cups water and bring  to boil and cook for 5 minutes.  Strain and return the broth to the pot  discarding the shrimp shells.  Add lemon grass, raw shrimp, Thai fish sauce  and lime juice, and reduce the heat to medium. Simmer for 5 minutes and  skim off any foam.  Just before serving, add the coconut milk, red chilis,  green onion and fresh cilantro.  Taste and adjust seasoning---you may like  more coconut milk or salt. Enjoy.
