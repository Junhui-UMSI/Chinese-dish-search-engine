Sweet and Sour Asparagus
Cloves, LISA CRAWLEY  TSPN00B-----, Fresh Asparagus, Water, White vinegar, Salad oil, Salt, Sugar, Celery seed, Sticks Cinnamon, 
Prepare and cook asparagus. Remove from pan; set aside. Combine remaining  ingred. in saucepan; bring to boil. Place asparagus in shallow glass dish;  pour vinegar mixture over. Cover and chill 24 hrs. Drain before serving.  Equal amts. of canned, green asparagus spears can be substituted for fresh  asparagus.
