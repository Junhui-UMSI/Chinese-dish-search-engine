Very Special Lemon Muffins
Salt, Flour, Sugar, Lemon Peel, Butter, Lemon juice, Baking Powder, Egg yolks, Egg whites, 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Combine sugar and butter and cream until  smooth. Add egg yolks and beat well. Sift dry ingredients together. Add  alternately to creamed mixture with lemon juice, mixing well after each  addition. DO NOT OVERMIX. Fold in the egg whites and lemon peel. Fill  greased miniature muffin tins 3/4 full and bake about 18 to 20 minutes.  Yield: 5 dozen miniature muffins.
