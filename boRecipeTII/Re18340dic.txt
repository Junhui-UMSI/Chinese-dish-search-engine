Vermont Spring Chicken
Butter, Maple syrup, Lemon Rind, Salt, Chopped almonds, Lemon juice, Pepper, Chicken,2-1/2to 3 lb, cut up, 
Place chicken pieces in shallow buttered baking dish. Mix remaining  ingredients and pour evenly over chicken. Bake uncovered 50-60 minutes at  4008F. Baste occasionally. Delicious on bed of rice.    Posted to MM-Recipes Digest V4 #092 by valerie@nbnet.nb.ca (valerie) on Apr  3, 1997
