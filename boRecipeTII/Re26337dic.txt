Tomato Aspic with Shrimp
wate, Mold, gelatin, Tomato/vegetable juice (V8), Lettuce, Fresh Lemon Juice, bay shrimp, Celery, 
1.  Soften gelatin in cold water Add vegetable juice. Stir. 2. Pour into  oiled mold.  3.  Chill until partially set. Fold in shrimp and celery. 4.  Run a thin knife around edge of mold to loosen it. Unmold on platter and  decorate with green olives, carrot peels or whatever. From Janet Mobley.
