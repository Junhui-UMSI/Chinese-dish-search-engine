Real Texas Chili
Cubed Chuck Steak, Vegtable oil, Medium Jalapeno Peppers, Raw Tomatoes, Black pepper, Green Bell Pepper, Chili powder, Salt, Medium Onions, 
In a food processor or by hand dice the Onions, Bell Pepper and the  Jalapeno peppers and set aside. Cut Chuck Steak in 1/2 inch Cubes or  smaller and set aside.  Cut tomatoes into pieces and set aside. Heat  Vegtable oil in an 8 quart Sauce Pan and add the diced Onions, Bell  Pepper and Jalapenos, saute till browned. Add the Cubed Chuck Steak  and sear untill Browned, add Tomatoes and spices.  Cover and simmer  untill Tomatoes are cooked down.    This will feed about 6 people as a meat dish.....
