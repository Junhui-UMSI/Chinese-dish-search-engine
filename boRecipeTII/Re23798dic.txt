Ash-E Jow (Iranian Barley Soup)
Onions, Water, Black Pepper, Dried barley, Dried green lentils, Dried mint, Olive oil, Turmeric, 
1) Put everything into a pot and then bring to a gentle boil.    2) Simmer for 1 1/4 hours, stirring occasionally.    3) Serve with feta cheese and salad.    Variations: Fry the onions, in the oil, before putting them in the pot. Add  1 cup of cooked chick peas or red kidney beans, a few minutes before  serving.
