Sweet Onion Quiche
Ham, diced, Onions, chopped, Eggs, Mild American cheese, grated, Pie crust, unbaked, Sour cream, Swiss cheese, grated, Crumbled bacon, Evaporated milk, Butter, Caraway seed cheese, grated, 
Melt butter in skillet; saute onions and ham until done. Place in bottom of  unbaked pie crust.  Mix cheese, milk, eggs, and sour cream and pour over  onion and ham mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes; garnish with  crumbled bacon.    Source: Rock Springs Sweet Onion Festival Cook Book 1992
