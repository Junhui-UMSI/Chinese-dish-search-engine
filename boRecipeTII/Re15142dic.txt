Wheat Free Shortbread
Rice syrup, Barley, AM Rice Flour, Unrefined vegetable oil, 
Mix all ingredients well to make a smooth dough.  Roll out 1/4 inch thick  and cut into desired shapes.  Bake on an ungreased cookie sheet for 17  minutes at 375 F.
