Baked Chicken Salad
cooked chicken, Pepper, Crushed potato chips, Onions, Salt, Tomato, sharp cheddar, Celery, Lemon juice, 
Combine chicken, celery, 1/2 cup cheese, onion, lemon juice, seasonings &  enough mayonnaise to moisten. Mix lightly. Spoon into a 1-1/2 quart  casserole & top with tomatoes. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes. Top with  remaining cheese & chips & continue baking until cheese is melted. Serves  6.
