Tuna Tacos
Green pepper, Onion, Cheddar cheese, Purchased taco sauce, Lettuce, Spaghetti sauce, Taco shells, Tomato, Tuna, Butter, Chili powder, 
In frying pan, melt butter and saute onion and green pepper until tender.  Add tuna, chili powder, spaghetti sauce and taco sauce and cook for 5  minutes. Place 1/4 of the tuna mixture in a taco shell and garnish with  lettuce, tomato and cheddar cheese. Repeat with remaining shells. A hit as  a snack or meal.
