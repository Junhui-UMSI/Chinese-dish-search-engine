Wassail Bowl
Allspice,whole, Orange,sliced,small,garnish, Cloves,whole, Lemon,sliced,small,garnish, White wine,dry(750ml btl), Cinnamon sticks,3" long, Apple cider/juice, Lemonade,frozen(6oz), Orange juice,frozen(6oz), Water, Brown sugar,packed, 
1. In 5-quart saucepan over high heat, heat apple cider, cinnamon sticks,  allspice, and cloves to boiling. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 15  minutes.   2. meanwhile, place orange and lemon slices in heat-safe 5-quart punch  bowl; set aside.   3. To cider mixture, add frozen orange juice concentrate, lemonade  concentrate, white wine, water, and brown sugar; over high heat, heat to  boiling. Pour hot mixture over fruit in bowl.
