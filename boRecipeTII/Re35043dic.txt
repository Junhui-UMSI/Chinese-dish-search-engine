Beans and Barley
Tabasco sauce; to taste, Navy beans; soaked, Pinto beans; uncooked, Carrot; chopped, Pearl barley, Prepared mustard, Vegetable broth, Mushrooms; chopped, Parsley; minced, Lentils; dried, Split peas; dried, Onion, 
Soak white and pinto beans overnight. Saute onion, mushrooms and carrot in  1 tablespoon of vegetable broth until tender. Add drained beans, vegetable  bouillon, mustard and parsely, bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer  45 minutes. Add split peas, lentils and barley. Cover and simmer another  hour until all beans are tender.
