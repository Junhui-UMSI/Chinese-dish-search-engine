Barbs Best Crisp
Flour, Rolled oats, fruit, butter, Water, Brown sugar, Cinnamon, Salt, 
Butter a 9x9"  baking pan.   Place sliced fruit in pan.  Works great with  any firm fleshed fruit like apples, peaches, plums - even oranges).  Add  water to fruit.  If the fruit is dry or wet, adjust water accordingly.    Mix melted butter or margarine, brown sugar, flour, oats, cinnamon and salt  together to make a coarse mixture.  Pat topping mix onto fruit, covering it  completely. Bake 1 hour at 350 oF.  Serve warm (with ice cream!).
