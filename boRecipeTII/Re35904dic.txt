Stuffed Mushrooms
Bread crumbs, Water, Salad oil, Mushrooms, fresh, Pork, spicy, Garlic clove, crushed, Bacon, 
Clean mushrooms and remove stems from caps. Brush caps with some oil and  place in a baking dish (13 by 9 inch for standard serving size). In  skillet, cook bacon (or sausage) until no longer pink; remove from heat.  Drain off all but 2 tbl* of drippings. Add bread crumbs and chopped stems.  Spoon crumb mixture into oiled mushroom cap. Pour water in bottom of baking  dish. Bake 20 minutes. *Quantity for standard serving size.
