Apricot Honey Chicken Poupon
Boned and skinned chicken, Dijon mustard; or honey, Apricot preserves, Ground ginger, 
Blend mustard, preserves and ginger. Brush some mustard mixture on chicken.  Grill or broil chicken for 6 to 8 minutes on each side, or until done,  brushing with mustard mixture frequently.
