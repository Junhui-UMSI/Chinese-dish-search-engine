Winter Pie
Finely ground carrot, Hot water, Cornstarch, Cloves, Coarsely chopped apple, Seedless raisins, Sugar, Nutmeg, Salt, Cinnamon, 
Combine all dry ingredients, and add to the raisins and carrot. Add water,  bring to a boil and simmer about 5 minutes. Then add apple. Bake this  mixture between two crusts for 25 minutes at 375F.
