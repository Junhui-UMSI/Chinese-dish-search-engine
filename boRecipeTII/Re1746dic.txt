Cheesy Zucchini Pizza
Tomatoes, unpeeled, Bacon, Zucchini, sliced, Onions, sliced, Pepper, American cheese slices, Salt, garlic salt, oregano, 
Place zucchini slices in a shallow baking dish.  Sprinkle with salt, garlic  salt, oregano and pepper.  Slice tomatoes and place over squash. Season the  same as zucchini.  Slice onions to form a layer and season as before. Layer  cheese slices on next.  Top with a layer of bacon. Bake uncovered for 45 to  55 minutes in a 350 degree oven.
