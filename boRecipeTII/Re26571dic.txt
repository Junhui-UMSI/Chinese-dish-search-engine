Bagel and Cream Cheese Morning Mix
Brown sugar, Reduced-fat cream cheese;, Margarine or butter;* melted, Wheat Chex? Cereal, Oatmeal CrispTM with Raisins, Cinnamon-raisin bagel chips;, Raisins, 
Recipe from "Chex Recipes"    Heat oven to 325?. Mix cereals, bagel chips and raisins in large bowl; set  aside. Microwave margarine, brown sugar and cream cheese in 2-cup  microwavable measuring cup on High 1 minute; stir to remove lumps. Pour  over cereal mixture in bowl, stirring until evenly coated. Spread in  ungreased jelly roll pan, 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1 inch. Bake 20 minutes,  stirring after 10 minutes. Cool completely. Store in airtight container or  plastic food-storage bag. 7 1/2 cups snack.    *Do not use spread or tub products.    1 serving (1/2 cup): 110 calories (35 calories from fat); 4 g fat (1.5 g  saturated fat); 5 mg cholesterol; 480 mg sodium; 15 g carbohydrate; 3 g  protein.
