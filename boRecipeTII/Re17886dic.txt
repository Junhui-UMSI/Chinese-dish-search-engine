Aloras Rhubarb Pie
FILLING, Baking Powder, Brown sugar, Sugar, Flour, CRUMBS, 8" unbaked pie shells, Eggs, Baking soda, Coarsely chopped rhubarb, Shortening, Flour, 
To prepare filling combine sugar, flour and eggs. Stir to form a thick  paste. Fold in rhubarb pieces.    Spoon into 2 unbaked pie shells (cut recipe by half if you only want to  make one pie)    To prepare crumbs combine all ingredients and mix well. Sprinkle evenly  over rhubarb mixture.    Bake at 400 degrees for 10 minutes, reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake  40-50 minutes more. Sent recipeLu 3/98  Posted to recipelu-digest by ncanty@juno.com (Nadia I Canty) on Mar 13,  1998
