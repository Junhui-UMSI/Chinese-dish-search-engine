Alligator Sausage and Crawfish Casserole
Brown, drain, Converted long-grain rice, Garlic -- minced, Butter or margarine, Black pepper, Crawfish tails, Creole seasoning, Chiles, Alligator sausage; crumble, Waldine Van Geffen VGHC42A, Onion Soup, Bay Leaves, Cn Rotelle tomatoes/w, Mushrooms -- sliced, Beef consomme, Green onions -- chopped, Smoked sausage -- sliced, 
Mix alligator sausage with remaining ingredients. Place in a covered  casserole or pan and cook in preheated 350~ oven for 1 hour. After 30  minutes, stir well and continue cooking for the remaining time. Let cool,  uncovered, for a few minutes and serve. Can serve 10-20.
