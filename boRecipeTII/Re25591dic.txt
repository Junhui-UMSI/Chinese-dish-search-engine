Avocado Salsa
Avocado,, diced, Onion, Minced cilantro, Lime, Juice of, Cayenne pepper, Sea salt, 
In a small bowl, mix the avocado, lime juice, onion, cilantro, salt, and  pepper. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator no more than 1  hour.
